5 interesting facts about Dangote's beef with Nigeria's north-central government, Kogi State

The Nigerian National Security Council has ordered the reopening the Dangote cement plant at Obajana, Kogi State.  The directive came as a result of security threats, protests, and a huge employment gap.  The Kogi State Government ordered the shutdown of the plant which lasted for 10 days. The largest cement conglomerate in Sub-Saharan Africa, Dangote Cement, and the Kogi State have been at each other's throats for close to two weeks now. The 10-day dispute between both parties was spurred upon by the State's rejection of the factory's legality.
