Oppo is all set to launch Oppo K10 5G in India on June 8. The phone is said to be the slimmest 5G smartphone in the given segment. Check details here. Oppo on Friday announced the launch date of its new addition to the K series lineup- Oppo K10 5G in the Indian market. Oppo K10 5G will be launching in India on June 8, 2022 at 12PM on Flipkart. Informing about the same Oppo India tweeted, "Faster, sleeker, stronger! The #OPPOK105G is filled to the brim with everything you need and more. Launching on 8th June, 12PM.
