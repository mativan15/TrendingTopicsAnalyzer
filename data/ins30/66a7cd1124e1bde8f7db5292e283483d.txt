Russia cuts gas supplies to Dutch state trader raising stakes in sanctions war
theguardian.com
sport
2022-05-31 13:47:55

Russia has further cut off gas supplies to Europe, after state energy giant Gazprom turned off the taps to a top Dutch trader, amid an intensification of the economic battle over Moscow's invasion of Ukraine.It follows the EU's overnight decision to place an embargo on most Russian oil imports as part of its financial sanctions against the Kremlin.EU leaders agree to partial embargo of Russian oil importsEU leaders said the ban would immediately impact 75% of Russian oil imports, rising to 90% of this trade by the end of the year.
