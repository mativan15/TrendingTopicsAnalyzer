Lulu Group to invest Rs 2500 crore in Uttar Pradesh across three new projects
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 15:00:00

AgenciesThis is the first such event in this huge format to be held in India after pandemic challenges and so far 80,000 crore worth of new projects have been committed in the state of UP.Abu Dhabi-headquartered Lulu Group will invest Rs 2500 crore in Uttar Pradesh in three new projects in the state - one Lulu Mall each in Varanasi and Prayagraj and Lulu Food Proecessing Hub in Greater Noida. UP launched their 3rd Investment Summit and ground breaking ceremony, which was inaugurated by PM Narendra Modi in the presence of CM Yogi Adityanath, Indian Defence Minister Rajnath Singh and other officials in Lucknow.
