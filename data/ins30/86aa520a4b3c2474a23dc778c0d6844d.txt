MONTREAL, Oct. 17, 2022 /CNW Telbec/ - Beiersdorf North America announced today the appointment of Florian Wolfram as General Manager, Beiersdorf Canada, effective October 1, 2022. In his new role, Florian reports to Mauricio Valdes, General Manager, North America and will be responsible for the Canadian business. He will lead Canada's Leadership Team and become a member of the North America Leadership Team.

Beiersdorf Canada Inc. (CNW Group/Beiersdorf Canada Inc.)

Florian has been with Beiersdorf for more than nine years in various sales & commercial capacities.
