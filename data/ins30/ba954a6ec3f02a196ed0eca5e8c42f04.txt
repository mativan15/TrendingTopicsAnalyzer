Mumbai: Ultimate Kho Kho, upcoming franchise-format kho kho league backed by 's Amit Burman, has secured Adani Group and GMR as two of its six team owners. While Adani Group purchased the Gujarat franchise, GMR bought the Telangana squad. "I am delighted to welcome the Adani Group and GMR onboard on our Ultimate Kho Kho journey," said Tenzing Niyogi, CEO of Ultimate Kho Kho. "This is certainly a strong step forward for Ultimate Kho Kho becoming a sports movement." Adani Sportsline, a part of the Adani Group, is associated with many sporting leagues, including the Gujarat franchise of the Pro Kabaddi League, the Indian Boxing League, and the UAE's upcoming T20 League.
