Tech Down on Earnings Trepidation -- Tech Roundup
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 20:48:00

Shares of technology companies fell amid trepidation ahead of earnings, and worries about the implications of a strong dollar. Shares of International Business Machines rose in late trading after the computer-services giant's quarterly earnings topped some investors' expectations. Tesla shares slipped after the electric car maker said a stronger dollar weighed on third-quarter earnings. Contract semiconductor manufacturing giant Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing is considering expanding its capacity in Japan as it seeks to diversify its production hubs to account for geopolitical risks, The Wall Street Journal reported.
