Binance is the world's biggest cryptocurrency exchange, handling $490 billion of spot trading volumes in March 2022.Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange, is launching its own venture capital fund.The company's venture arm, Binance Labs, said Wednesday it has raised $500 million for its debut start-up fund, securing backing from venture capital firms DST Global and Breyer Capital as well as unnamed family offices and corporations.Binance Labs plans to use the capital to invest in companies building "Web3.
