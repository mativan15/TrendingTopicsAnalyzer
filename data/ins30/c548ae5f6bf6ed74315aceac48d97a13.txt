 A Ludhiana court on Saturday discharged Congress councillor Gagandeep Sunny Bhalla in the alleged ₹2,000 foodgrain transportation scam. Doing a U-turn, the vigilance bureau informed the court that nothing was found to establish Bhalla's involvement in the case.
