Crypto in your pension pot? No thanks
irishtimes.com
business
2022-06-03 22:16:01

The State's funds sector, home to investment pots holding €4 trillion of assets for global investors as of the end of 2021, has had plenty to deal with in recent months as equity and bond markets have taken a hammering. But that hasn't stopped the industry from plotting to add a little more spice to your average investment fund.Irish Funds, the industry lobby group representing the world's third-largest domicile for investment funds (behind the US and Luxembourg), published a white paper this week pushing for funds marketed to small-time, or retail, investors to be allowed to put money into crypto 'assets' — among the most volatile bets around.
