SAN ANTONIO (Oct. 17, 2022) – The San Antonio Spurs today announced that they have waived guard Joe Wieskamp to finalize their opening night roster for the 2022-23 season. The Silver and Black tipoff the regular season on Wednesday, Oct. 19 against the Charlotte Hornets at the AT&T Center.Wieskamp appeared in 29 games for the Spurs, averaging 2.1 points in 7.1 minutes per game after being drafted by San Antonio with the 41st overall pick in the 2021 NBA Draft. He also played and started in 28 games for the Austin Spurs where he averaged 15.
