Country rebel Tanya Tucker returns to the spotlightTanya Tucker always challenged the standards of how women in country music were supposed to look and act. Now at 60, Tucker has returned to the spotlight with her first album of new songs in 17 years. (Aug. 29)Tanya Tucker's cackling laughter pauses. At the same time, she sips a potent blend of tequila and ruby red grapefruit juice (a signature "Tanyatini") while seated in a crowded upstairs lounge at Nashville's Belcourt Theater.The multi-award-winning Kathlyn Horan-directed documentary, "The Return of Tanya Tucker: Featuring Brandi Carlile," has just premiered to a standing ovation at the Nashville Film Festival.
