by Ishan ChaturvediRenewable energy is the future. With the current climate conditions and the deteriorating plant health, it is of paramount importance to try and reverse the change. Energy consumption is monumental in the world we live in today, especially going to digitization. As a country with a massive population, India can truly benefit from renewable energy. In healthcare specifically, we can pave the way for cost-optimized healthcare for the citizens of their country with renewable energy.
