ET Canada Drew Barrymore has confirmed she hasn't been in an 'intimate relationship' for years, and has no desire to be in one anytime soon.The 'Drew Barrymore Show' host was married to Will Kopelman from 2012 to 2016 and revealed on her personal blog that she hasn't had a sexual relationship since the split.Barrymore and Kopelman share two daughters together — Olive, 10, and Frankie, 8 — and the actress insisted her priority is the kids and has been since they were born.
