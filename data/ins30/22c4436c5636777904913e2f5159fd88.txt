How to Buy I Bonds

After inflation rose to a 40-year high in 2022, Series I savings bonds -- better known as "I Bonds" -- re-entered the mainstream conversation. In their latest release, I Bonds pay an annualized rate of 9.62%. Given persistent inflation, they will continue to pay competitive interest in the immediate future.This article will review where and how to purchase I Bonds and answer a number of frequently asked questions.



Image source: Getty Images

The most common way to buy I Bonds is to visit TreasuryDirect, the government website that allows for the purchase of government securities.
