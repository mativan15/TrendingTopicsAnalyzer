3 Power sector stocks that can impress you in Q2 earnings season
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 07:30:00

ETMarkets.comAmid an optimistic outlook for the sector, the brokerage sees 3 power stocks – CESC, NHPC and NTPC – to impress with better earnings in the Q2 period.As the economic activities in the country have picked up after the Covid-led disruption, the power sector is in strong demand and against this backdrop, Securities expects the power industry to report healthy earnings growth in 2QFY23. The brokerage sees the industry to log 19.4% Y-o-Y growth in earnings given the continued spur in power demand as well as merchant rates.
