Portsmouth is handing out $500 gift cards to low-income households this summer
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-03 17:37:00

The city of Portsmouth announced this week that it's using federal stimulus money to give out $500 gift cards to low-income households for the next two months.

From now until Aug. 1, eligible limited-income households in Portsmouth can apply for the one-time Visa gift card.

Eligible residents include those who meet the income criteria and typically don't receive assistance from the local Department of Social Services.

For a one-person household, the minimum income must be $25,536 and the maximum must be $34,487; for a family of two, the minimum is $34,488 and the maximum is $43,450; for three, the minimum is $43,452 and the maximum is $50,639; for a family of four or more, the minimum income must be $52,416 and the maximum at $55,057.
