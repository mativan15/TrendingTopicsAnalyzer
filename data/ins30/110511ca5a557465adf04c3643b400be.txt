Berlin, Germany, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) --    Tsuyoki Inu ($TINU), one of the most hyped and bullish projects on the BNB Smart Chain, with the goal of reaching unimaginable heights, was launched at 18:00 UTC May 27, 2022, after a successful presale on CookieSale. Tsuyoki Inu presale commenced on CookieSale launchpad on May 26, 2022 at 18:00 UTC and was set for 24 hours, but the hard cap was successfully filled in the first 10 minutes of the presale. The presale had a soft cap of 100 BNB, whereas a hard cap of 200 BNB.
