Indiewire Who else wants to say 'I do' to some dysfunctional family hilarity?In Prime Video's 'The People We Hate at the Wedding,' Ben Platt and Kristen Bell star as outrageous siblings who travel with their optimistic mother (Allison Janney) to the British wedding of their estranged half-sister (Cynthia Addai-Robinson). What could be a way to reconnect proves to only ignite their differences as chaos quickly ensues…and lands the core trio behind bars.'Eloise is our half-sister, which means that we can half-ass the relationship,' Platt says in the trailer, referencing the bride-to-be.
