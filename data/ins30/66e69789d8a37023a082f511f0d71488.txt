At least eight killed, 15 injured in boiler explosion at chemical factory in UP's HapurAt least nine workers died and 19 were injured when a boiler exploded at a chemical factory in western Uttar Pradesh's Hapur district on Saturday afternoon. The numbers were confirmed by Medha Roopam, District Magistrate, Hapur. The factory is located adjacent to a CNG pump. The impact of the explosion was so intense that the roofs of some of the factories located in the vicinity were damaged. It took three hours for the fire tenders to douse the blaze in the factory.
