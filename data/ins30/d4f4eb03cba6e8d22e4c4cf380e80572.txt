Published: 17:00 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 17:07 EDT, 18 October 2022 The Princess of Wales' mother Carole Middleton has launched her party paraphernalia business in the US  and used a cardboard cut-out of herself to help promote the new move.Party Pieces the company was founded by Kate Middleton's mother in 1987 when she was looking for inspiration for her daughter's fifth birthday party.She realised there was a gap in the market and set up the business that offers time-pressed parents and easy choice of imaginative parties for their children.
