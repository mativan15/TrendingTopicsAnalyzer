DEERFIELD, Ill., June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Surgalign Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: SRGA), a global medical technology company focused on elevating the standard of care by driving the evolution of digital health, today announced that it has granted restricted stock unit awards to five (5) employees, none of whom was an executive officer, as an inducement for them to accept employment with Surgalign, and representing the right to receive, in the aggregate, up to 27,877 shares of Surgalign common stock.
