Southern Utah University returns home against #20 Stephen F. Austin, aiming to snap a three-game losing streak.

Southern Utah (3-4, 1-2 in the WAC) football has lost three straight and the road to getting back into the win column on Saturday doesn't get any easier with #20 Stephen F. Austin entering Eccles Coliseum.

After a 3-1 start, Southern Utah has lost its last three games by a combined 12 points.

'Focus and effort is not our problem,' said head coach DeLane Fitzgerald. 'Our kids are focused, they fight their butts off, we just don't know how to finish.
