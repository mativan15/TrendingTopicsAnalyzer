ICYMI: Rep. Sherrill Holds Press Conference with Law Enforcement on Legislation to Combat Recent Auto Thefts
house.gov
news
2022-10-19 16:18:37

Madison, NJ–– Representative Mikie Sherrill (NJ-11) held a press conference this week with local law enforcement and elected officials to highlight the introduction of her new bipartisan bill, the Auto Theft Prevention Act. Read more about this legislation here. She was joined by state and local officials and representatives of law enforcement to highlight the collaborative work happening on this issue across New Jersey, including state legislation introduced by State Senators Richard Codey and Anthony Bucco.
