'Queen' takes corgis on royal day out ahead of Jubilee
mirror.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 14:01:37

GoBoat is offering guests the chance to feel like royalty to mark the Queen's Platinum Jubilee with a special butler service and a tasty afternoon teaIt's very rare that us regular folk get to experience what life is like for the rich and famous, in a world where money isn't an object and staff are always on hand to help with mundane tasks. But to mark the Queen's Platinum Jubilee this weekend, the bosses at GoBoat are rolling out the red carpet to offer the ultimate royal experience. People hiring out the eco-friendly boats will have their very on butler on board to serve drinks and food during their trip around some of London's best sights.
