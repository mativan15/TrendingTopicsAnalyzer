GRAND CAYMAN, Cayman Islands, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Greenlight Re Innovations ("GRI"), part of Greenlight Capital Re, Ltd. (NASDAQ: GLRE) ("Greenlight Re" or the "Company"), has announced an investment in Garentii GmbH ("Garentii"), a security deposit replacement insurance MGA and platform based in Germany.



Security deposits have long been the standard form of landlords' security against property damage and unpaid rent. However, security deposits represent an inefficient use of renters' capital and an administrative burden for landlords.
