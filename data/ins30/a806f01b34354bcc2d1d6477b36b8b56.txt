Variety - TV News 'The Rookie: Feds' has received a full season order at ABC, Variety has learned.With the pickup, the first season of the broadcast drama series will now run for a total of 22 episodes. The series debuted on Sept. 27. It airs Tuesdays on ABC.'The Rookie: Feds' is a spinoff of ABC's 'The Rookie.' 'Feds' originally aired as a two-part backdoor pilot during 'The Rookie's' fourth season. 'Feds' stars Niecy Nash-Betts as Simone Clark, the oldest rookie in the FBI.
