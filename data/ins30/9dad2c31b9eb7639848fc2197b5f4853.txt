Cooperatives included as buyers on government's e-marketplace portal
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 12:00:00

ET BureauGeM is a one stop government e-Market place where common user goods and services can be procured. GeM is dynamic, self sustaining and user friendly portal for making procurement by Government officers.Cooperatives can now use the GeM portal for their purchases after the Cabinet gave its go ahead to the move to include them on the portal. The move will strengthen the government's e-marketplace and provide an opportunity to a wide array of sellers to make their goods available to a wider buyer base.
