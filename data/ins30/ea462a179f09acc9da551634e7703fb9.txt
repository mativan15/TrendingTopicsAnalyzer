EU leaders are struggling to pass sanctions on Russian oil. Why?
euronews.com
news
2022-05-27 18:19:21

Doubt is beginning to fall on the European Union's ability to act as a unified bloc as member states still haven't agreed on a Russian oil embargo. The proposal to phase out imports of crude over the next six months, and refined oil by the end of the year, was put forward by Commission president Ursula von der Leyen weeks ago. Its fate remains uncertain.One of the major obstacles is Hungary. A number of logistical - as well as political - contingencies make it harder for Viktor Orbán's government to break free of reliance on Russian oil.
