The pound has sunk further into the red, with investors rattled by the resignation of Prime Minister Liz Truss.
Ms Truss's departure following a short but chaotic stint in Number 10 is being compounded by lacklustre retail sales figures and a jump in UK public borrowing to £20bn.
The gloomy economic data highlights the challenge facing Ms Truss's successor and the Chancellor.
The pound dropped as much as 1pc against the dollar to as low as $1.1123 before recovering slightly.
