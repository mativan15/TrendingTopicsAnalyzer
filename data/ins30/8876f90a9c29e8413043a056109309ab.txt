No political party would dream of electing its leader the way we elect a mayor.

A party leader needs support from a broad majority of the members, including those who would have preferred someone else. Successive rounds of voting ensure that such a majority emerges.

A mayor, too, should have to earn such broad support through a system of ranked-choice voting, an option removed for no good reason by the PC government in 2020.
