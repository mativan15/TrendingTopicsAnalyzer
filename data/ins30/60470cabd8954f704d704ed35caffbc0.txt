Tricida announces $125M debt facility
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 21:00:54

Tricida (NASDAQ:TCDA) on Wednesday announced a $125M debt facility with Hercules Capital (HTGC). Of the total amount, $100M will be available for drawdown at Tricida's (TCDA) option subject to meeting certain targets. $25M will be available for drawdown till Dec. 31, subject to announcement of positive data from the VALOR-CKD trial. Additional $25M will be available for drawdown until the earlier of 10 business days after the filing of the NDA for veverimer and Sept. 15, 2023.
