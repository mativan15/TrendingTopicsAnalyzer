Man sentenced to 16 years in prison for criticising Saudi Arabia on Twitter

Speaking publicly for the first time, his son has alleged that the Saudi government has tortured his father in prison and said that the US State Department mishandled the caseSaad Ibrahim Almadi is a 72-year-old joint Saudi-American citizenSaudi Arabia has sentenced a pensioner to 16 years in prison for tweets he posted which were critical of the regime. Saad Ibrahim Almadi, a 72-year-old joint Saudi-US citizen, was arrested in November 2021 upon landing in Riyadh for a two-week stay in his native country for a work and personal trip.
