Joe Raedle/Getty Images News The Update Crestwood (NYSE:CEQP) announced several simultaneous transactions on Wednesday (May 25th). They will buy Sendero Midstream, a gathering operator in the Delaware Basin with complementary assets to Crestwood's footprint for $600 million in cash. They also acquired former controlling shareholder First Reserve's 50% stake in a Permian JV for $320 million worth of units. The company stated that these deals were priced at 7x net twelve months EBITDA, implying about $130mm of incremental EBITDA.
