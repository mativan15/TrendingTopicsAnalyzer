Market sentiment worsened further because of a sharp rise in global crude oil prices, which add to upside inflation risks, strengthening the case for aggressive rate hikes by the Reserve Bank of India's MPC.

Bond yields and prices move inversely. A rise in yields, therefore, brings with it the risk of marked-to-market losses on bond portfolios and eats into banks' profitability.
