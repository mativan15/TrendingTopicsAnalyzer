Sir Michael Stoute's unbeaten colt heads to Epsom as the clear standard-setter for the 2022 Cazoo Derby on June 3. The event is part of the Queen's Platinum Jubilee celebrationsDesert Crown is the new Derby favouriteFavourite Desert Crown puts his unbeaten record on the line in the Cazoo Derby at Epsom. The event, part of the Queen's Platinum Jubilee celebrations, will see the colt bid to give trainer Sir Michael Stoute a sixth success in the flagship contest. The son of Nathaniel powered clear of what had looked on paper a decent Dante Stakes field at York, on just his second start.
