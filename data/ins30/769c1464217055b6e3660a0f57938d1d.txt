HUDSON, N.H., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Right Networks, a vertical cloud service provider, is pleased to announce the appointment of Karl Johnsen, CPA, as Chief Financial Officer. With more than 25 years of success leading prominent technology companies, Johnsen will help drive the company's expansion and market position as the only intelligent cloud purpose-built for accounting firms and professionals. 'We are excited to have Karl join our team. He will play a critical role in ensuring we continue to scale and that we're successfully reinvesting in our business for growth,' said Joel Hughes, CEO of Right Networks.
