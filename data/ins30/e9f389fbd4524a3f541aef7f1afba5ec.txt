A WOMAN has revealed how she cut her food shopping bills to just £85 a month - without even going inside a supermarket.
Caitlin Weich, 29, from Dublin, Ireland, swears by dumpster diving and using a food sharing app to slash her bills.
Caitlin Weich has slashed her food shop down to £85 per month by dumpster divingCredit: PA Real Life
The marketing manager has said she is saving so much money by dumpster diving that she is now able to save for a home.
She revealed that she and her lawyer boyfriend, Adam, 28 now eat like King's on a pauper's budget, while helping reduce food waste.
