BARK Shares Fall 7% After Co. Reports 4Q Results
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 22:33:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of BARK Inc. were trading lower in Tuesday's after-hours market, following the company's release of fourth-quarter financial results. At 6:12 p.m. ET, the company's shares were trading 7.2% lower, at $2.32 per share. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 3.85% loss, closing at $2.50 per share. The company said it had a fourth-quarter net loss of $36.7 million, compared to a net loss of $7.1 million in the year-ago period. For fiscal 2022, BARK said it had a net loss was $68.
