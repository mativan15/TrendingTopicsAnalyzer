Animal Rebellion protesters were filmed pouring milk on to the shop floor of Harrods on Saturday as they called for a transition to a 'plant-based food system'. Footage shows several protesters pouring milk, taken from the shelves, on to display cabinets in Harrods' food hall in Knightsbridge.The group co-ordinated the action in stores like Waitrose, Whole Foods and Marks and Spencer in London, Manchester, Norwich and Edinburgh just before 12pm.Skylar Sharples, an international development graduate from Bristol and one of the protesters at Harrods, said: 'Supporters of Animal Rebellion are back acting because (Prime Minister) Liz Truss and Ranil Jayawardena (Secretary of State for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs) are again deciding to ignore calls to start building a better future.
