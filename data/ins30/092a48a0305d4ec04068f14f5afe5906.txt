It won't have escaped your attention that air fryers have become incredibly popular this year, thanks to their ability to cook some meals more efficiently than other kitchen appliances.As well as packing the potential to lower your household energy bills when compared with conventional ovens, air fryers also use little or no oil to cook fried food, meaning healthier chips could be on your menu.Like most other kitchen appliances, air fryers vary in size, price, performance and abilities. Prices tend to start around the £60 mark, but can head up to more than £200 for the largest and most-capable examples.
