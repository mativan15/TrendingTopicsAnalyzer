Published: 11:40 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:50 EDT, 17 October 2022 Liz Truss looked on today as Jeremy Hunt declared he had to take a wrecking ball to her economic policies to ensure 'stability'.Appearing in the Commons with the PM by his side, the new Chancellor confirmed an extraordinary dismantling of the disastrous mini-Budget, two weeks ahead of schedule and barely three days after he was parachuted into the job.And he cautioned that 'eye-watering' decisions will be needed to balance the books, after ditching a host of measures including knocking 1p off the basic rate of tax from April.
