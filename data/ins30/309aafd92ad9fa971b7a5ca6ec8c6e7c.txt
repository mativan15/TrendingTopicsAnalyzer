Uttar Pradesh: RSS meet pays tributes to Mulayam Singh Yadav, others
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 15:45:00

PRAYAGRAJ: In a break from the usual, tributes were paid to Samajwadi Party patriarch Mulayam Singh Yadav on the opening day of Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh 's four-day national executive board meeting which began in Prayagraj on Sunday.Several top office-bearers of the organisation, including RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat, general secretary Dattatreya Hosabale and others participated in the meeting.Over 300 office-bearers, including sanghchalak, karyawah, pracharak and workers from all 11 regions (kshetra) and 45 provinces (prant) were also present in the meeting.
