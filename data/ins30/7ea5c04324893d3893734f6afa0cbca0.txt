PalletWorks not only integrates with packaging and labeling hardware on the plant floor, but also communicates with existing controls and ERP systems. MINNEAPOLIS (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Cybertrol Engineering, an industry-leading control and information systems integrator that develops, implements, and supports plantwide automation systems, industrial IT/OT networks, and manufacturing intelligence solutions, has announced the release of PalletWorks 2.0 tracking and labeling software. Cybertrol will showcase PalletWorks 2.
