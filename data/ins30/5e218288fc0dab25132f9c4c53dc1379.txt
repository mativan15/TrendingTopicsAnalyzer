No, it wasn't just an act.

The Danielle Smith who ran a leadership campaign heavy on conspiracies and light on reality is the same Danielle Smith who is now Alberta premier.

To wit, the now-notorious comment from her first news conference as premier last Tuesday, speaking about Albertans who refused to be vaccinated against COVID: 'They have been the most discriminated-against group that I've ever witnessed in my lifetime.'

That is Danielle Smith unvarnished, unapologetic and unaware. And there's a reason why she won't be pivoting to the centre anytime soon.
