A long-term commitment to the St. Louis region, the one-million-square-foot state-of-the-art manufacturing facility will deliver in-demand fiber cement product, local jobs, and increased local investmentCRYSTAL CITY, Mo., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- James Hardie, a world leader in fiber cement home siding and exterior design solutions, plans to build its newest manufacturing facility in Crystal City, Missouri. The new facility and James Hardie's partnership with local, state, and federal officials reflect the company's long-term commitment to Crystal City and the state of Missouri.
