New Omicron Sub-Variants Found: Government Is Now Monitoring Key Data
ndtv.com
economics
2022-10-19 07:55:34

New Delhi: Cases of coronavirus infection are dipping in India but at least five new sub-variants of the Omicron variant have been detected in different states over the past few days.So far, severe disease has not been reported due to these sub-variants, 'but many times the behaviour of the virus is not known, so it is important to be careful,' said top sources in the Health Ministry.The ministry is monitoring the rate of hospitalisation, ICU care need, and deaths. A meeting was held yesterday with different departments that monitor these data.
