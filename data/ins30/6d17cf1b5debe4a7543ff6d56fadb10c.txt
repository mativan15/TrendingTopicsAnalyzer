Elon Musk Reportedly Says Remote Work No Longer Acceptable For Tesla Employees
cnet.com
news
2022-06-01 14:19:32

Remote work is reportedly no longer acceptable for Telsa employees. CEO Elon Musk laid out the directive in an email on Tuesday to his executive staff, reported Fortune, citing a screenshot of the email shared on Twitter by Telsa shareholder Sam Nissim. "Anyone who wishes to do remote work must be in the office for a minimum (and I mean *minimum*) of 40 hours per week or depart Tesla," Musk wrote in the leaked email. "If there are particularly exceptional contributors for whom this is impossible, I will review and approve those exceptions directly.
