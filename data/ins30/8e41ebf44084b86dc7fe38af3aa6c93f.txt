DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--The 'UK: HnB, Oral Tobacco and Nicotine Pouch Regulation' report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.The Tobacco and Related Products Regulations 2016 introduced a special category of tobacco - novel tobacco product, which is defined as a product supplied after May 2014, different from tobacco, cigarettes, cigars, cigarillos, hand-rolled tobacco, pipe, or waterpipe tobacco, chewing, nasal or oral tobacco.By the end of 2022, a new tobacco control plan will be published.
