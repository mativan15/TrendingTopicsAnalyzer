Today in 1878, Thomas Edison established the Edison Electric Light Company in New York City. Today in 1962, the Cuban missile crisis was officially underway as President Kennedy was informed that reconnaissance photographs had revealed the presence of missile bases in Cuba. Today in 1965, students at the University of California at Berkeley began burning their draft cards to protest the war in Vietnam. Today in 1989, Wayne Gretzky of the Los Angeles Kings surpassed Gordie Howe's scoring record of 1,850 points, during a game against the Edmonton Oilers.
