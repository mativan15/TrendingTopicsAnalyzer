More than $225,000 still available in energy bill assistance for Duke Energy Indiana customers

2022-06-01 16:21:00

Qualifying customers can receive up to $300 toward their energy bills.

PLAINFIELD, Ind., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- More than $225,000 in financial assistance is still available to Duke Energy Indiana customers who may be struggling to pay their energy bills.
"Hoosiers are paying more at the grocery store and gas pump and may also be noticing higher energy bills, as rising fuel costs impact the price of electricity," said Stan Pinegar, president of Duke Energy Indiana. "We know that higher bills can be a hardship for many, and we want to connect our customers with financial assistance and resources to help.
