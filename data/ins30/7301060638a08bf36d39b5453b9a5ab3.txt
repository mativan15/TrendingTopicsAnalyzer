IANSRam Setu is all set to hit theatres on October 25. Competition comes from the Ajay Devgn, Sidharth Malhotra, and Rakul Preet Singh film 'Thank God'.The song Jai Shree Ram from the forthcoming Akshay Kumar movie Ram Setu is now available. The song, written, performed, and produced by Vikram Montrose with lyrics penned by Shekhar Astitwa, is an upbeat devotional song that fits in well with the film's so far-released promotional material. The video for the song makes extensive use of the same visual elements seen in the teaser.
