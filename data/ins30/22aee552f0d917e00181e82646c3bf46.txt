McCaffrey Improves 49ers' Title Odds
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-21 15:10:14

The San Francisco 49ers have gone all in on chasing a championship by trading a haul of draft picks for Christian McCaffrey. By adding the superstar running back, the 49ers have improved their chances of winning Super Bowl LVII.San Francisco's Super Bowl odds have gotten better at several sportsbooks in the immediate aftermath of the trade. The 49ers have +1400 odds to win the championship at BetMGM, FanDuel Sportsbook and Caesars Sportsbook.The 49ers had +1900 Super Bowl odds at FanDuel Thursday night.
