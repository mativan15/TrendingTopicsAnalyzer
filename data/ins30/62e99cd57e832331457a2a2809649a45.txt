New Healthcare Networking Platform Partners to Identify More Patients at Risk of Chronic Disease in Australia

BRISBANE, Australia, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- New health networking platform, Annuo™, has entered a working agreement with WeKo GP to assist with the identification and treatment of patients with chronic disease in Australia.



This key partnership will facilitate the management of people living with chronic disease by General Practice teams using WeKo and Annuo™.
