Moment plane fills with smoke leaving flyers 'unable to breathe for 25 mins'

2022-10-17 23:40:10

PASSENGERS were left in a panic after a plane cabin began filling with smoke mid air, leaving them struggling to breath. 
Travellers were left gasping for air for around 25 minutes and claimed they were told to "start praying" during the chaotic incident onboard a SpiceJet flight from Goa, India.
The SpiceJet aircraft was engulfed by smoke as terrified passengers gasped for airCredit: Jam Press/TIM
Emergency services were waiting on the runway as the flight made an emergency landingCredit: Jam Press Vid/TIM
The aircraft was forced to make an emergency landing at Hyderabad Airport shortly after departure last Wednesday at around 11pm.
