Pune: Five booked for killing worker and passing it off as hit-and-run
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 06:45:00

AgenciesRepresentative ImageFive men killed a casual worker, on October 10 and threw his body into a ditch in a bid to pass it off as a hit-and-run accident. The Uttamnagar police on Monday arrested five men on the charge of murdering 22-year-old Omkar Laxman Supekar. "Supekar and the five arrested men hail from Pangharun village near Uttamnagar. The assailants were Supekar's colleagues working as drivers and helpers with a sand mining unit in Pangharun along the Mutha river bed," TOI quoted senior inspector Sunil Jaitapurkar of the Uttamnagar police as saying.
