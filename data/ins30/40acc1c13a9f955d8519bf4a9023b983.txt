12 million at risk of breaking down and engine damage from fuel error
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

There are over 12 million 'red line runners' across the UK who let their fuel tank get as close to empty as possible before filling up. It is estimated that almost a third of drivers are more likely to do this as a result of the current cost of living crisis.Young drivers are the most likely to risk running out of fuel, with half (51 percent) of 'red line runners' aged under 35.Breakdown specialists Green Flag are warning drivers, saying they need to be careful, as running fuel gauges down can be a 'risky business'.
