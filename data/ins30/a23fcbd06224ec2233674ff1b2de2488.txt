Pensions experts on managing your funds amid soaring interest rates
express.co.uk
finance
2022-10-15 03:00:00

Struggling Britons may be tempted to dip into their pensions to help make end's meet amid the cost of living crisis, or be thinking about switching up their investments to take advantage of the high interest rates. Express.co.uk spoke to three financial experts to get their thoughts on how Britons could arrange their pensions and investments.Holly Mackay, CEO of Boring Money, said high interest rates will be most concerning for people drawing an income from their pensions today, or those close to retiring.
