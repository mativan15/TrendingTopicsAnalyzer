(Shutterstock)BELLEVILLE, NJ — The township of Belleville will be holding several spookily fun activities for Halloween 2022. Here is some info about each, via the municipal website.HALLOWEEN SPECTACULAR – The town will hold a Halloween Spectacular on Saturday, October 22, 2022 on the front lawn of Belleville High School from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. Attractions will include a pumpkin patch, pumpkin painting, Halloween characters, a costume contest and special guest, Jake the Snakeman and his reptile friends.
