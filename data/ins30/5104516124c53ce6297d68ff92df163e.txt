244 AIADMK cadres detained In Salem for taking out demo
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 06:45:00

Salem: At least 244 AIADMK cadres were detained on Wednesday for taking out demonstrations across the district to condemn the arrest of party interim secretary Edappadi K Palaniswami and other leaders in Chennai. AIADMK Salem urban district secretary and former MLA G Venkatachalam led a group of more than 170 party cadres gathered in front of the new bus stand and raised slogans against the ruling government. The city police detained all the cadres and lodged them in a marriage hall. They were released in the same evening.
