October 19, 2022 11:58 AM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kevin Shinick has been named showrunner for Exile Content Studio's Lil' Heroes animated television series, it was announced today by Daniel Eilemberg, co-founder and CEO of Lil' Heroes and Stephen Davis, Exile Kids and Animation Executive Producer, and former CCO of Hasbro, whose past credits include Transformers, My Little Pony and Power Rangers, among many others. Shinick joins NBA superstar Carmelo Anthony, who along with his producing partner and co-founder of Creative 7, Asani Swann, serve as executive producers on the series, with Anthony also voicing one of the characters.
