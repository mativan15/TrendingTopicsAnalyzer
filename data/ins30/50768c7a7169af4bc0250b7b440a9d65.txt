Sinclair & Rush, Inc. Acquires Indepak Corporation

ST. LOUIS (PRWEB) June 03, 2022 Sinclair & Rush, Inc. has successfully completed the acquisition of Indepak Corporation of Portland, Oregon, a thermoform manufacturer specializing in custom trays, blisters and clamshell packaging primarily for medical and food applications. Indepak also supplies world-class manufacturers with highly engineered thermoformed shipping trays used in the aerospace, defense and automotive industries. Sinclair & Rush, a leading manufacturer of clear plastic packaging products, significantly increases their thermoforming capacity and capabilities while also providing a manufacturing footprint on the west coast.
