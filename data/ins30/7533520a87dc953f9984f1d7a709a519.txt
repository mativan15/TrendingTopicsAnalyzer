USA: Tenants grapple with rent hikes amid overall inflation spike
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-04 17:15:00

LOS ANGELES: At a time when rising gasoline and food prices are already straining Americans' budgets, many apartment tenants are grappling with soaring rents.The Miami-Fort Lauderdale-West Palm Beach metropolitan area in Florida saw overall median rent soar over 50% in April from a year ago, to $3,045 a month, according to Realtor.com.The next biggest increase? The central Florida metropolitan area made up of Orlando, Kissimmee and Sanford, where the median rent jumped 32.9% from April last year to $1,927, the firm said.
