This is a crunch week for Liz Truss and her party
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 21:00:00

The new Chancellor Jeremy Hunt deployed his most emollient bedside manner at the weekend in a bid to reassure the markets, the country and the Tory party that the Government is back on track. But the message he is required to sell is politically hard to land.
Essentially, he is saying that the country is in a mess after 12 years of Conservative-led administrations and things are about to get even worse. Moreover, he acknowledged that the Prime Minister, in place for just five weeks, had made some egregious mistakes, which he is now required to undo.
