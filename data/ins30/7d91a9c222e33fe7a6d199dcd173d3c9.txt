Penalty kicks. Five-set tennis matches. Game-winning baskets. Tiebreakers. Sudden death overtime. Seeing someone win or lose by the thinnest margin is one of the most thrilling aspects of watching athletes compete. But that's not the goal on Caldera, the virtual island setting of 'Call of Duty: Warzone.' Popular 'Warzone' players on Twitch and YouTube do their best to seek out game lobbies filled with less skilled players — often called 'bot lobbies' — and completely dominate them.
