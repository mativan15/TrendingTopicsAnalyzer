IMI to Buy Bahr Modultechnik for EUR98 Mln Enterprise Value
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-30 06:18:00

By Joe Hoppe IMI PLC said Monday that it will buy electric linear motion specialist Bahr Modultechnik Holding GmbH for an enterprise value of 98 million euros ($105.2 million). The U.K. specialist-engineering company said that Bahr Modultechnik will become part of the industrial automation business unit within IMI Precision Engineering, with closing of the transaction expected by mid-June. IMI said the Luhden, Germany-based business will provide it with an electric actuation portfolio that is highly scalable in attractive markets.
