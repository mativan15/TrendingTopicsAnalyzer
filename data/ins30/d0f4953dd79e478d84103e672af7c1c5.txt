 Months after he failed to find a place in Yogi 2.0 government, former minister and Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) lawmaker Mahendra Singh was appointed party's election in-charge for Tripura where assembly polls are due early next year. BJP chief JP Nadda issued a letter clearing Singh's appointment as the election in-charge for Tripura with Rajya Sabha member Samir Oraon as co-incharge.
