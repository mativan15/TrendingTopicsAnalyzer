RRR in Japan: Ram Charan meets his fans ahead of film release
indiatimes.com

2022-10-20 09:30:00

RRR star Ram Charan and wife Upasana Kamineni are currently in Japan for the RRR promotions ahead of its premiere. The actor was spotted as he posed for pictures with his fans. The movie directed by SS Rajamouli will release in Japan on October 21. As part of the promotions, the makers organized a grand event in Japan. Director SS Rajamouli, Ram Charan, and Jr NTR will join the event and will likely watch the movie in the theatre with their fans.

Before flying to Japan, Ram Charan and his wife Upsana were spotted at the Hyderabad airport.
