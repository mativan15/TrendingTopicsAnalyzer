Editor's note: This is one in a series of stories looking at contested races in the Aurora area in the June 28 primary election.The Republican primary race for a seat on the Kane County Board in District 13 includes incumbent Todd Wallace and challengers Stephen Brown and Anthony Catella.Michael Linder is running unopposed in the Democratic primary in District 13.The primary election is set for June 28.Wallace, 46, of St. Charles, said he was appointed a year ago to the board and is now running for his first full term.
