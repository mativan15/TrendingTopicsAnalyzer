Published: 20:53 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 20:55 EDT, 17 October 2022 Daniel Craig stepped out in style on Monday as he attended a special screening and Q&A for his latest film Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery at The Curzon in Mayfair.The James Bond star, 54, cut a dapper figure in a brown double breasted blazer which he buttoned up over a smart black shirt. The actor rocked a pair of blue jeans and a pair of suede brown shoes, while arriving at a screening of the sequel to the original Knives Out mystery.
