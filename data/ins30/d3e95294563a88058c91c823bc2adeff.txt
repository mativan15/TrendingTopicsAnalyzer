By Australian Associated Press Published: 17:56 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 17:56 EDT, 19 October 2022 Former Labor leader and Western Australia governor Kim Beazley has been appointed to the Australian War Memorial Council. Mr Beazley will replace council chair Brendan Nelson, who will step down at the end of November to take up a role with Boeing in London. The former opposition leader is expected to fill the council vacancy for three years. Veterans' Affairs Minister Matt Keogh congratulated Mr Beazley on his appointment.
