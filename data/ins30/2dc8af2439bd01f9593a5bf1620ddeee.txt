Woman furious with ex for giving his daughter her name slammed
newsweek.com

2022-10-17 13:00:54

A woman was slammed online after admitting to being upset that her ex gave his daughter the same name as her.Choosing a baby name can be one of the most difficult decisions anyone has to make, although many would agree ones shared by an ex-partner would be off-limits.According to the Social Security Administration, the top boy's name in 2021 was Liam and the top girl's name last year was Olivia.But Mumsnet user LetMeSpeak found herself in the unusual position of sharing her name with her ex's daughter.
