Elon Musk has a new arch-enemy: the Fed
Insider

2022-10-21 07:15:00

Elon Musk has a new nemesis in the Federal Reserve.The Tesla CEO has slammed the US central bank for soldiering on with interest-rate hikes, despite what he considers growing signs the inflation threat is fading. The comments came after the electric carmaker blamed a historically strong dollar for a quarterly sales miss, and said the currency movements cost it $250 million in operating income."The Fed is raising rates more than they should," Musk said during the automaker's third-quarter earnings call on Wednesday.
