Published: 08:04 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 08:10 EDT, 18 October 2022 It continues to be the source of news and entertainment for millions, despite its flaws and setbacks. The public broadcaster that is known by billions of people across the world was formed on this day as a private organisation in 1922, by a group of leading wireless manufacturers. Back then, its famous initials stood for the British Broadcasting Company, rather than Corporation as it is now, and its first ever broadcast came the following month, on November 14, from London station '2LO'.
