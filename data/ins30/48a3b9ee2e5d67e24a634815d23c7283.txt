Citizens Financial Services to acquire HV Bancorp

tupungato/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Citizens Financial Services (NASDAQ:CZFS) is set to acquire HV Bancorp (NASDAQ:HVBC). The two companies have entered into a definitive merger agreement, pursuant to which HVBC shareholders will have the right to elect to receive for each share either $30.50 in cash or 0.400 shares of CZFS. The amount will come to ~$67.4M in aggregate. The transaction, approved by the boards of both the companies, will close in H1 of 2023, subject to regulatory approval, approval by HVBC shareholders and other customary closing conditions.
