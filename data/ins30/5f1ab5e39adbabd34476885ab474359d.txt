Published: 18:10 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 18:10 EDT, 4 June 2022 Desperate fish and chip shop bosses are to seek help from Norway amid the soaring cost of cod and haddock.The move comes after warnings that thousands of Britain's traditional takeaways could be forced out of business due to the sharp increase in prices.Up to 40 per cent of our favourite fish suppers originate in Russia, but Government plans for a levy on imports following the invasion of Ukraine mean we face paying more at the counter.
