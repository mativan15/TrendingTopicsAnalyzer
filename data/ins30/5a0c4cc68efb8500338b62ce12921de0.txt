October 18, 2022 09:17 AM Eastern Daylight Time PLANO, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tyler Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: TYL) announced that Yuba County, California, has successfully gone live with Tyler's Enterprise Permitting & Licensing solution following an on-time and under-budget implementation.



'Going live with Tyler's software has already brought many improvements to our county's permitting process, including more streamlined communication and the ability to respond much quicker to customer requests,' said Steve Oehler, business systems analyst, Yuba County.
