October 17, 2022 05:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time PLANO, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Diodes Incorporated (Nasdaq: DIOD) will host a conference call on Monday, November 7, 2022 at 4:00 p.m. Central Time (5:00 p.m. Eastern Time) to discuss its third quarter 2022 financial results.

Joining Dr. Keh-Shew Lu, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer of Diodes Incorporated, will be Brett Whitmire, Chief Financial Officer; Emily Yang, Senior Vice President of Worldwide Sales and Marketing; and Gary Yu, Senior Vice President of Business Groups.
