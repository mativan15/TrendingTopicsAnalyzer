Michigan State football offers 2023 NJ safety
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-21 14:14:51

The Michigan State coaching staff has sent out a significant offer to the 2023 recruiting class. New Jersey defensive back Saboor Karriem has received an offer from the Spartans coaching staff.
Karriem is a native of West Orange, New Jersey, and projects as a safety in college. Standing at 6-foot-3 and 180 pounds, Karriem possesses great size and length at the safety position. He is currently ranked as a 3-star prospect.
