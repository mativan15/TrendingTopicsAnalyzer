AgenciesRepresentative imageA low pressure area was formed in the Bay of Bengal on Thursday. The India Meteorological Department (IMD) has said that it is likely to intensify into a cyclonic storm over the next four days. The low pressure area, which has been formed over southeast and east-central Bay of Bengal, is likely to deepen into a depression by October 22 and into a cyclonic storm by October 24.
