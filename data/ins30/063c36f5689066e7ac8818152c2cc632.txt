ArtistGNDphotography/E+ via Getty Images iShares U.S. Energy ETF (NYSEARCA:IYE) is an exchange-traded fund that seeks to track the performance of its chosen benchmark index, the Russell 1000 Energy RIC 22.5/45 Capped Gross Index. IYE has a reported expense ratio of 0.41%. The fund had 37 holdings as of May 31, 2022. The benchmark index does not offer very informative information as to the portfolio's underlying profitability and productivity. Nevertheless, IYE is basically a large-cap energy stock portfolio.
