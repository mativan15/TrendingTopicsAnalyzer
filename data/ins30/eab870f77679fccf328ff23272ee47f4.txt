6:19 PM ETASHBURN, Va. -- Washington Commanders quarterback Taylor Heinicke said he's a different player than last season. Now he'll get a chance to prove just how much.When Heinicke starts against the Green Bay Packers on Sunday, he'll be able to draw upon his 2021 experience, when he started 15 games for the injured Ryan Fitzpatrick. Now he'll start for Carson Wentz, who fractured his right ring finger."I feel a lot more comfortable, a lot more confident," Heinicke said. "I was a little overwhelmed last year; it was my first time starting 15 games and it gets to you a little bit.
