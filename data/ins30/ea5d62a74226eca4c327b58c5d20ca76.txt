AgenciesCounterfeit notes detected for various denominations in the banking sectorThe Reserve Bank of India ascertained a 101.93% rise in fake notes of Rs 500 denomination and 54% for fake notes of Rs 2,000. The increase rate of counterfeit notes for Rs 10, Rs 20 and Rs 200 were 16.45%, 16.48% and 11.7% respectively. The RBI detected an increase of 10.7% in counterfeit notes of Indian denominations in FY22.
