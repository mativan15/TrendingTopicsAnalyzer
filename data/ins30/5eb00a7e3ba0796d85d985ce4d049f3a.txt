REUTERS/Toru Hanai/File PhotoRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comJune 1 (Reuters) - Panasonic Corp (6752.T) said on Wednesday it expected global car production to recover this fiscal year, but that the two-year long chip shortage will continue.The Japanese conglomerate, which makes batteries for Tesla Inc (TSLA.O), said last month it expected no growth in profits this business year, due to component shortages caused by COVID-19 lockdowns in China and rising material costs following Russia's invasion of Ukraine.
