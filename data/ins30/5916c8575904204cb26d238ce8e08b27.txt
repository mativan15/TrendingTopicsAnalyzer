Queen's pay rise for staff to help with cost of living crisis as Jubilee begins
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-06-02 14:32:30

Loyal members of staff whose pay has been frozen for two years will enjoy a boost in their earnings from this monthThe loyal members of staff for the Queen will get a pay rise Royal staff will enjoy a 5% pay rise as part of this weekend's Platinum Jubilee celebrations, to help them through the cost of living crisis. Members of staff who have previously had their pay frozen for two years will enjoy a welcome boost to their bank balances. The pay rise is in recognition of the employees' hard work during the pandemic.
