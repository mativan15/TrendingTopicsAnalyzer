Published: 19:47 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 19:55 EDT, 19 October 2022 Gianfranco Zola is not ruling out his former club Chelsea when it comes to the Premier League title race, despite a mixed start to the campaign.The Blues currently sit fourth in the league table after the opening 10 matches, despite being held to a goalless draw by Brentford in their latest fixture.They did, however, extend their unbeaten run to seven games under new boss Graham Potter at the Brentford Community Stadium. Chelsea currently sit fourth in the Premier League after picking up a 0-0 draw at Brentford Chelsea extended their unbeaten run to seven games under Graham Potter (pictured, centre) They still have work to do in order to get themselves back into the title race as they're seven points behind league leaders Arsenal while also remaining behind Manchester City and Tottenham Hotspur.
