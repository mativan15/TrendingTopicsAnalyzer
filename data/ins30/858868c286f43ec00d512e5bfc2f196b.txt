Ally Financial stock slides after big Q3 earnings miss reflects higher costs (NYSE:ALLY)
seekingalpha.com

2022-10-19 12:20:14

deepblue4you/E+ via Getty Images Ally Financial (NYSE:ALLY) stock dipped 7.7% in Wednesday premarket trading after its Q3 earnings and revenue fell short of Wall Street expectations as expenses increased and its provision for credit losses rose. Adjusted EPS of $1.12 missed the average analyst estimate of $1.73 and dropped from $1.76 from Q2 and $2.16 in the year-ago period. Similarly, revenue of $2.02B missed the $2.16B consensus and fell from $2.08B in Q2, but remained elevated from $1.99B a year before.
