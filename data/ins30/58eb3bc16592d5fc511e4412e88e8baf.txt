Back in 2017, Samsung introduced a feature called Dex which transformed your Android smartphone into a fully fledged desktop PC complete with mouse and keyboard support. It was - for many users - a life changer and provided with a compelling alternative to other desktop OS like Windows, Ubuntu or MacOS.Dex doesn't require any physical connection to work if you have the right Samsung smartphone, which paves the way to what I'd consider to be the holy grail of hybrid working: A smartphone that can replace your laptop/desktop and doesn't require any physical cable.
