Tricia Fitzmaurice joins Rancher Government Solutions (RGS) as Vice President of Sales. Tricia is a trusted leader in the Federal IT community bringing 27 years of experience serving the public sector, DoD, and Intelligence Community with prior leadership roles at RedHat and UiPath. She joins RGS following a series of contract wins and security validations, as the adoption of Kubernetes has accelerated in the federal market for application modernization, cloud native, and edge technologies.
