June 02, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Vince Holding Corp. (NYSE: VNCE), a leading global contemporary group, today announced that it plans to report its first quarter 2022 financial results pre-market on Thursday, June 9, 2022. The Company also plans to hold a conference call to discuss its financial results on the same day at 8:30 a.m. ET. During the conference call, the Company may answer questions concerning business and financial developments, trends and other business or financial matters.
