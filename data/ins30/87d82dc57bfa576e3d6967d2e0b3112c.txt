Image source, Scott FinneyImage caption, James Morrison appeared at the Tavern Inn in NewquaySinger James Morrison has performed at a local pub, after a chance meeting with a terminal cancer patient.The Broken Strings singer made his surprise appearance at the Tavern pub in Newquay, Cornwall - the town where he went to school - on Saturday. He had met Jay Day a week before, and asked if he could do anything to help his efforts to raise money for cancer wards at the Royal Cornwall Hospital.Mr Morrison said: "I just thought he was such a courageous guy.
