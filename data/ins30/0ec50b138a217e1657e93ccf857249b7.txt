FTC Eyes a Crackdown on Influencers and Advertisers Who Target Kids
gizmodo.com
news
2022-10-19 20:25:00

The Federal Trade Commission held an event Wednesday focused on the problem of 'stealth' advertising in content geared towards children, where it's difficult to differentiate between marketing and regular content. In the opening hours of the day-long event, the commission provided a glimpse into its thinking on the issue, and indicated that new regulatory efforts may be on the horizon.'When kids interact with digital media, they're exposed to an array of marketing practices that blur the line between advertising and entertainment.
