Dublin, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Advanced Materials for 3D Printing: Technologies and Global Markets" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.

Additive manufacturing or three-dimensional (3D) printing refers to the process of creating a 3D, solid object from a digital model by accumulating successive layers of material in numerous shapes.
