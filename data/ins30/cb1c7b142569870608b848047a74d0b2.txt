Delhivery stock hits lifetime low, down another 18% in early trade
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 05:20:33

Shares of new-age logistics firm Delhivery tanked another 18% on Friday morning on the BSE after it was down 15% on Thursday following muted growth outlook from the firm Delhivery shares are trading at Rs 386.70 per share - down nearly 18% compared to the previous close on BSE on Thursday. This is a record low for Delhivery's stock since it listed on the bourses earlier this year.Delhivery, in a stock exchange filing had said it is likely to see an adverse impact of high inflation and will clock moderate growth in shipments for the rest of the financial year.
