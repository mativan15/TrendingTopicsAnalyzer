Indiewire Abigail Breslin is shining a light on domestic violence.The 'Little Miss Sunshine' actress took to Instagram to honor Domestic Violence Awareness Month and share her personal history of surviving an abusive relationship.'As a Dv survivor, I felt compelled to write a little about my story,' Breslin posted. 'I was in a very abusive relationship for close to two years. It all started out perfectly, I was so in love. Unfortunately, my abuser took advantage of my innocence and naïveté and the relationship subsequently became violent.
