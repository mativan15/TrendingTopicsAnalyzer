Days after United States President Joe Biden caught the world's attention with his 'one of the most dangerous nations' comment on Pakistan, the US Department of State made a statement which seemed to be contradicting the commander-in-chief's remark.A "secure and prosperous" Pakistan has always been viewed by the US as critical to its interests, State Department's Deputy Spokesperson Vedant Patel was quoted as saying at a briefing by news agency PTI.
