May 27, 2022 02:18 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The law firm of Kirby McInerney LLP is investigating potential claims against Dentsply Sirona Inc. ('Dentsply' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: XRAY). The investigation concerns whether Dentsply has violated the federal securities laws and/or engaged in other unlawful business practices.



Dentsply is a dental equipment manufacturer and dental consumables producer that markets its products in over 120 countries.

On April 19, 2022, Dentsply stated that the Company's Chief Executive Officer had been terminated, effective immediately, and will 'cease to serve as a member of the Company's Board.
