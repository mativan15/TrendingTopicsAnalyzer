Fayette Woods, 26, says he needs two jobs to meet his financial goals, which include paying his wife's college tuition, saving to retire at age 60, and keeping up with his bills during a time of four-decade-high inflation.But Woods works both of his jobs during the same hours, and neither employer knows about the other."In this economy, you cannot get by with one income," Woods said. He requested that his real name and employers be kept anonymous for privacy reasons, but his identity is known to Insider.
