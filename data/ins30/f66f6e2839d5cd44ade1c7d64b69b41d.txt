Retailer Joann says inflation, supply-chain problems are taking a toll
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 20:58:00

Joann Inc. stock fell more than 15% in the after-hours session Thursday after the sewing and crafts-supplies retailer swung to a surprise quarterly loss and reported lower sales, saying that it faced inflation and 'challenged' supply chains. Joann JOAN, +3.81% said it lost $35.1 million, or 86 cents a share, in the first quarter, contrasting with earnings of $15.1 million, or 38 cents a share, in the same quarter last year. Revenue fell 13.3% to $498 million, with same-store sales declining 12.9%.
