With its stock down 31% over the past three months, it is easy to disregard CAE (TSE:CAE). Long-term fundamentals are usually what drive market outcomes, so it's worth paying close attention. In this article, we decided to focus on CAE's ROE.

Return on Equity or ROE is a test of how effectively a company is growing its value and managing investors' money. In simpler terms, it measures the profitability of a company in relation to shareholder's equity.
