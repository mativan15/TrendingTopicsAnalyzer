bodnarchuk/iStock via Getty Images Introduction On May 31, 2022, Gold Fields Limited (NYSE:GFI) announced a definitive agreement under which Gold Fields will acquire all of the outstanding common shares of Yamana Gold (AUY). Gold Fields shareholders will get 61% of the combined company if the deal is approved, and Yamana Gold shareholders will get the remaining 39%. The Transaction implies a valuation for Yamana of US$6.7 billion and represents a premium of 33.8% to the 10-day Volume-Weighted Average Price ('VWAP') of Yamana's Shares of US$ 5.
