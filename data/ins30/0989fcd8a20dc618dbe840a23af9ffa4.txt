In the run-up to the civic polls, Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Thursday said the people of Delhi have to decide whether they want to be  AAPnirbhar (dependent on AAP) or  Atmanirbhar (self-reliant). Mr. Shah made this comment while inaugurating a waste-to-energy plant in Tughlakabad's Tehkhand.Mr. Shah also alleged that the Delhi government had given 'stepmotherly' treatment to the Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) by 'not paying it the due amount of more than ₹40,000 crore'.
