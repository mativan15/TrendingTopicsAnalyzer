UK Braces for Sudden Return of Austerity as Hunt Demands Savings
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-18 19:19:47

(Bloomberg) -- Liz Truss's UK government has raced from massive tax cuts and stimulus to what is effectively austerity 2.0 in a matter of days.All government departments are being told to find savings, which outside of health and defense are expected to be as much as 15% of budgets, people familiar with the matter said. No options are off the table and new Chancellor of the Exchequer Jeremy Hunt has even refused to guarantee he will stick to a key Conservative Party manifesto pledge to raise the state pension in line with the highest of inflation, wages or 2.
