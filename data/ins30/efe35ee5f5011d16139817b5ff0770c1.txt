Published: Oct 18, 2022SACRAMENTO – Governor Gavin Newsom today announced the following appointments:
Jennifer L. Maguire, 49, of Elk Grove, has been appointed Director of the Financial Information System for California (FI$Cal), where she has been Acting Director since 2022. Maguire held several positions at the Financial Information System for California from 2012 to 2022, including Chief Deputy Director and Deputy Director of Administration. She held several positions at the California Department of Justice from 2005 to 2012, including Budget Officer, Staff Services Manager II and Staff Services Manager I.
