BJP did nothing to make Delhi garbage-free, says AAP
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 18:32:46

New Delhi Hours after Union home minister Amit Shah accused the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) of step-motherly treatment towards the erstwhile south, north and east Municipal Corporations of Delhi (MCDs), chief minister Arvind Kejriwal hit back, claiming that the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) was responsible for the landfill crisis in the city.Kejriwal said Shah has asked for three years to make Delhi garbage-free, while nothing has been done during the last 15 years when the BJP ruled the MCDs. 'Why should people trust you? You will not be able to do it.
