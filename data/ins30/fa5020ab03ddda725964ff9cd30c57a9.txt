Max Rashbrooke is a senior associate at the Institute for Governance and Policy Studies, Victoria University of Wellington-Te Herenga Waka. He writes a fortnightly column for Stuff. OPINION: I was in a Victoria University auditorium on Wednesday, listening to National MP Nicola Willis outline her party's resuscitation of 'social investment', when I turned and saw Bill English sitting a few rows back. His face wore its usual quietly confident smile, as it had when he was prime minister. Back to the future, then: for it was precisely English's ideas that Willis, the party's deputy leader, was reviving.
