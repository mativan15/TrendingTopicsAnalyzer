Davos 2022: The nature of the big transitions dominated sobering business and investor debates. The biggest worries are macro regime change, the mobilisation of transition finance, the crypto crash, and mergers and acquisitions taking advantage of sell offs. Is the current consensus – as in 2009 – too bleak, as business and policymakers respond earnestly to these transitions and risks? 

 The mood at Davos was the gloomiest since 2009. The war in Ukraine, surging inflation, tightening monetary policies, dismal markets, crypto unravelling, and a long economic hangover from COVID-19 are disorienting investors and business leaders.
