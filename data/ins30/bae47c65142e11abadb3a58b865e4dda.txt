Riverwalk Holdings launches Rs 150 crore fund to back early-stage companies
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 12:00:00

Jai Sumer Singh and Satveer Singh Thakral, co-founders, Riverwalk Holdings.NEW DELHI: Riverwalk Holdings, an early-stage India-focused venture capital fund, announced the launch of their Rs 150 crore fund. The fund is anchored by the Singapore based, Thakral Group and has been backed by various business houses across India and Singapore. Riverwalk has a sector agnostic investment approach with preferred sectors such as enterprise SaaS, fintech and consumer-tech and consumer brands. The fund will invest up to Rs 10 crore per company, and has already made four investments.
