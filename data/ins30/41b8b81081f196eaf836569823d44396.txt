Sense isn't always common, but when it comes to brain trauma in rugby, it's heartening that medical research is beginning to recognise and appreciate how the legacy of head injuries may vary from player to player and therefore pursue the need to continually refine the treatment process.Leinster number eight Caelan Doris confirmed as much in conversation ahead of Saturday's United Rugby Championship match against Munster at the Aviva Stadium. His concussion related issues are well documented, including taking four months off ahead of the 2021 Six Nations Championship.
