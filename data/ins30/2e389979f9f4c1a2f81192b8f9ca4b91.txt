We're all switching up our daily habits to keep our rising energy bills in check. Making responsible choices, like putting on a jumper instead of the heating or taking short showers instead of long baths. But you also need to think about long-term swaps to keep your costs lower this winter.Ditching old energy-zapping appliances for new smarter energy tech is the way to go if you want to keep the running costs of your household down. And with large kitchen appliances accounting for roughly 30% of annual energy consumption these are the household buys you need to focus on.
