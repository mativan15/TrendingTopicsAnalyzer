Kameleon007 Yesterday the bulls regained the ground lost on Friday with another enormous rally in the major market averages. The United Kingdom's new finance minister completely reversed the initial budget proposals that sent the country's bond yields soaring and the British pound plunging. The move returned stability to that market and weakened the U.S. dollar index. A weaker dollar fueled the stocks of U.S. multinationals that have seen profits take a hit from this year's dollar strength. Investors were also impressed with better-than-expected earnings from the banking sector, with Bank of America being the latest to beat on the top and bottom lines yesterday.
