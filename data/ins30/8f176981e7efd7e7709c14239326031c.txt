Labrador's instant reaction to new puppy melts hearts: "I teared up"
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-21 14:45:38

A Labrador named Billie has left the internet in tears after his owner shared a video of him interacting with his new puppy, documenting their friendship.The viral clip, shared on TikTok by Billiethelab_ on Monday, shows the excited dog instantly becoming best friends with the household's new puppy, acting like a big brother, greeting the puppy with lots of hugs and kisses.Many dog owners usually get a second dog mostly to keep the first dog company, and while most of them will welcome a new sibling, it is not always smooth sailing.
