Budget airline announces new direct flights to New York from the UK

news
2022-10-17 14:44:56

ANOTHER low cost airline has confirmed plans for direct flights between the UK and New York. 
Budget airline Norse Atlantic will be able to operate flights from London Gatwick to New York after being given a foreign air carrier permit.
Norse Atlantic will be able to fly direct to New York next yearCredit: Getty
The airline first started flights from London Gatwick to New York in August.
However, this included a stopover in Norway.
The new flights will be able to fly direct without the connection from summer 2023.
