Maharashtra: Proof shows Shiv Sena MP Sanjay Raut's money laundering role, says ED
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 06:00:00

MUMBAI: Opposing the bail plea of Sanjay Raut in a money laundering case related to Patra Chawl , the ED told the special court on Monday that the Shiv Sena MP had taken an active interest in the redevelopment project and was even present at meetings on it. The prosecution has completed its arguments.Raut, arrested on July 31, was produced before special judge M G Deshpande, who extended his judicial custody.Calling Raut an influential person, the ED submitted that the economic offences involving him were serious and investigations were on.
