20 October 2022 by Clayton Davis 
Variety - Film News Writer/director Rian Johnson beams with positivity when sitting down to chat at the Middleburg Film Festival. His latest film 'Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery' has been making the festival rounds, garnering critical acclaim and some Oscar buzz. But there's one thing that his fans are dying to know: will he ever make his proposed 'Star Wars' trilogy?'I have talked to Kathy [Kathleen Kennedy] about it and we're still talking about it,' Johnson tells Variety‘s Awards Circuit Podcast.
