Infosys wins IT transformation deal with manufacturing firm CIRCOR
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 11:45:00

Infosys has announced its collaboration with industrial products manufacturer CIRCOR International to transform The latter's IT infrastructure, service desk, and user support applications. The Bengaluru-based company did not disclose the tenure or size of the deal. As part of the strategic engagement, Infosys will work on transforming CIRCOR's IT landscape and modernize its IT infrastructure, an exchange filing said. Infosys will transform IT services for CIRCOR's business users by deploying managed IT services, improve processes, bring in agility into operations and will also modernize the local data centers and cloud landscapes, the filing added.
