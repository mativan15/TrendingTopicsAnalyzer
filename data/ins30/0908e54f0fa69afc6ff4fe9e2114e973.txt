Dhruv Vikram's film with director Mari Selvaraj to begin next year?
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-18 10:22:00

It was earlier announced that actor Dhruv Vikram will team up director Mari Selvaraj. While the shooting of the film was expected to begun much earlier, the latest is that the film will will kick off once director Mari Selvaraj completes the post production work of his upcoming film 'Maamannan' with Udhayanidhi Stalin. Buzz is that the film will begin early next year.The film is said to be a sports biopic based on the life of a Kabbadi player. Dhruv will be seen starring in this true life story of a sports man who clawed his way from the rural hinterland of Tamil Nadu to finally winning the nation's highest sports honour and winning gold for his country at the Asian Games.
