FTSE 100 falls as economic and political concerns temper sentiment

British Prime Minister Liz Truss. The FTSE 100 fell at the open as inflation and political jitters dimmed the mood. Photo: Daniel Leal/Reuters

Global stocks turned red as a market rout wiped out the gains made at the start of the week, and as soaring inflation has dimmed the investing outlook and raised concerns over higher interest rates.

Investors are also contending with a worsening economic and political turbulence in the UK as prime minister Liz Truss clings onto power after the failed mini-budget caused market turmoil.
