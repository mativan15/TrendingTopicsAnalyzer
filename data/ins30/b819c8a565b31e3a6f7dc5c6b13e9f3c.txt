Luis Lopes makes the difference as Aberdeen bounce back
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

The Aberdeen manager Jim Goodwin described his team's 2-0 win over Hearts as the 'perfect response' to their 4-0 defeat by Dundee United last weekend, as his team showed the type of character and fight that was absent at Tannadice.Two second half goals, the first from Luis Lopes and the second from Vicente Besuijen just five minutes later, was enough to secure an important win. 'You have to earn it,' Goodwin said. 'You have to fight hard and defend the way we defended because I will always believe we will score goals.
