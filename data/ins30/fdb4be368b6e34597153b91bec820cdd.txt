Woman accused of releasing swarm of bees on sheriff's deputies
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 23:09:00

A Massachusetts woman has been accused of releasing a swarm of bees on sheriff's deputies as they tried to serve her with an eviction notice. Rorie Woods, 55, is now facing multiple assault and battery charges following the incident at Memery Lane, Longmeadow.Ms Woods pleaded not guilty at her arraignment on October 12 at Springfield District Council.Court records cited in local reports revealed that Woods was released without bail.According to an official department report, Hampden County Sheriff's Department deputies went to a home in Longmeadow on October 12 and were met by protesters.
