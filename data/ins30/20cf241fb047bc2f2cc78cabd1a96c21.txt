Robert Way/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Alibaba Group (NYSE:BABA) stock price surged by almost 15% after it reported is March quarter results on May 26. The results beat both topline and bottom-line expectations. It's a surprise on one hand given China's COVID lockdown in key cities such as Shanghai during the past quarter. On the other hand, the better-than-expected results and large price really also seem logical given the events leading to the earnings report on May 26. As such, in this article, I will examine two of the key market events before reviewing earnings because I do not think earnings can be properly interpreted without a broader background.
