Kakao Sinks to Lowest Since May 2020 After Fire Disrupts Service
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-17 00:54:47

(Bloomberg) -- Kakao Corp. tumbled to the lowest since May 2020 after a fire at a data center disrupted services for the South Korean internet giant over the weekend.

Most Read from Bloomberg

The company's stock dropped as much as 9.5%, with it and shares of its affiliates among the biggest drags on the benchmark Kospi. Peer Naver Corp. slipped before recovering, while data-center operator SK Inc. slid as much as 6.6%, the most since July 2020.

The blaze halted messaging, ride-hailing, payment, gaming and other popular services for hours on Saturday.
