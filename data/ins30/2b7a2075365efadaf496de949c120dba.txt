Doctors find a developing foetus inside stomach of a 40-day old infant in Bihar
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 08:30:00

AgenciesThe case came to light when the 40 days old baby was bought for treatment in the Rahmania Medical Center. The parents bought the child to the hospital as he felt bloating, due to which the infant couldn't urinate properly. Thus, Dr Tabrez from the Rahmania Medical centre prescribed for a CT (computed tomography) scan to detect the cause of bloating and urine blockage. During the treatment, the doctors observed the developing foetus inside the infant's stomach. These results left everyone shocked, and soon, several tests were conducted to detect the baby's condition.
