Public vehicles to have tracking device, panic button
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 09:30:00

Location of vehicle is received and updated automatically on VLT & EA portal, so the real time location of vehicles can be monitored. The UT administration has directed the public service vehicles (maxi cab, motor cab, buses) registered in the city to install vehicle location tracking (VLT) device and emergency button (panic button).Ministry of road transport & highways (MoRTH) notified Motor Vehicle (Vehicle Location Tracking Device and Emergency Button) Order 2018 and made it mandatory that all public service vehicles are required to be fitted with VLT device and panic button under Rule 125(H) of Central Motor Vehicles Rules, 1989.
