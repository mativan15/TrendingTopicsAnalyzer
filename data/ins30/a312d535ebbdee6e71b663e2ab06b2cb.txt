The new factory, to focus on Ola Electric's upcoming electric car business, will be the second besides the EV maker's 500 acre facility inin Tamil Nadu where it has set up its Futurefactory, the world's largest two-wheeler factory.
 








Ola Electric is looking for lands to set up its electric car and battery cell plants in India. (Ola)
 

After successfully setting up its electric two-wheeler facility, Ola Electric is now gearing up to set up its second facility in the country. The Tamil Nadu-based electric two-wheeler manufacturer is now going to focus on its electric four-wheeler business as promised earlier.
