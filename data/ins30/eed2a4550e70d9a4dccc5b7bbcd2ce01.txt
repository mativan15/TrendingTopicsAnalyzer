Collider.com The DC Extended Universe has often struggled to find the right way to incorporate darkness into its films. The Dceu started a year after Christopher Nolan concluded his series of Batman films, and the attempts to bring a similar darkness to their properties have had mixed results. While this tone has worked when combined with humor, as with 2021's The Suicide Squad and Birds of Prey, the Dceu can often feel weighed down with the weight of this darkness.
