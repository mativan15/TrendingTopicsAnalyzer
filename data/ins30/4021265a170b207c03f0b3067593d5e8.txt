A mum who adopted two young boys has said she chose to change both of their names because she wasn't a fan of the car-themed monikers their birth mum had given themThe woman and her husband changed their sons' names immediately (stock photo) A mum who adopted two brothers into her care when they were both less than two-years-old has explained her decision to change their names - after their birth mum named them after car manufacturers. The woman, named Elizabeth, initially fostered the two boys with her husband Ed after having already welcomed seven biological children together.
