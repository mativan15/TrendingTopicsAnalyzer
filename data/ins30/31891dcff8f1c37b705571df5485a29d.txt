State board schools await textbooks two weeks into academic year

In-person classes for this academic year began on May 16. However, students enrolled in schools affiliated to the state board are yet to receive their textbooks. Teachers are using printed-out copies of online textbooks provided by the education department. School managements are worried the textbooks this year might be delayed than usual. 'We have not received the textbooks for any of the classes so far. The books were expected to arrive before the schools reopened.
