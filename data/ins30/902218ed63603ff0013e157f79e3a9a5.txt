Worldwide Hardcopy Peripherals Shipments Fell 12.3% Year Over Year in the First Quarter of 2022, According to IDC
businesswire.com
news
2022-06-01 00:00:00

June 01, 2022 04:14 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEEDHAM, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Worldwide shipments of hardcopy peripherals decreased 12.3% year over year to 22.4 million units in the first quarter of 2022 (1Q22). Shipment value was down 10.6% year over year during the quarter to $9.7 billion, according to new data from the International Data Corporation (IDC) Worldwide Quarterly Hardcopy Peripherals Tracker. Manufacturing constraints and logistics issues continued to be the main causes of the declining market.
