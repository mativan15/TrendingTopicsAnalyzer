No Matter Where You Live in Houston, a School of Science and Technology Campus Is Nearby
chron.com
news
2022-10-19 18:13:09

Access has long been a barrier to quality education, especially for those who depend upon public schools. As the 21st century job market continues to evolve towards positions that require a strong background in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM), our children's futures will be inexorably linked to whether they can access a quality STEM education. The good news for Houston parents is, no matter where you live in Houston, your child can go to a School of Science and Technology campus – including their new Online Academy – and receive the kind of education that will not only open doors for them later in life, but challenge and delight them now.
