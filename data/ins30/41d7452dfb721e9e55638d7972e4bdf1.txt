June 01, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time BIRMINGHAM, Ala.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--RFG Advisory 'RFG', an innovative and fast-growing platform for independent advisors today announced its new affiliation with RVA Wealth Management 'RVA', an independent advisory firm located in Glen Allen, Virginia. The affiliation marks RFG's third partnership in three weeks, following the addition of two new firms in Arizona and West Texas.



RVA Wealth Management's Founder and Wealth Manager David Koren, MBA, ChFC®, CLU® and his wife Robbie, an Operations Manager at RVA, lead a credentialed team of advisors and support staff, with over 60 years of combined experience in the financial services industry.
