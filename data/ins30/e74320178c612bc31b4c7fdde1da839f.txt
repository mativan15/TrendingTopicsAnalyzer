Tecno Spark 9 Pro has collaborated with Designworks, the design innovation studio for the BMW Group to get a new BMW-themed colourway. The Tecno Spark 9 Pro debuted earlier this year globally in several colors. And now, the mobile manufacturer has collaborated with Designworks, the design innovation studio for the BMW Group to design the Spark 9 Pro in a unique BMW-themed colourway. The handset is being designed for selected markets in Africa, Latin America, and the Middle East. The new colourway of Tecno Spark 9 Pro was inspired by an 'icy crystal talisman.
