Record of 23 Canadians on opening-night rosters as NBA season tips off
thespec.com
news
2022-10-18 19:26:37

A record 23 Canadians will be on opening-night rosters as the NBA tips off its regular season on Tuesday.It's the ninth consecutive season Canada is the second most represented country in the league, behind only the United States. The previous record of players for Canada was 18 from last season.Andrew Wiggins headlines the Canadian delegation fresh off an NBA title win with the Golden State Warriors. Wiggins, 27, made his first all-star team last season as one of the best two-way players in the league.
