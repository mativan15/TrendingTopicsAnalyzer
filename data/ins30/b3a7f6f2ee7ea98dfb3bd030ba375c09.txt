'Lyle, Lyle, Crocodile,' 'Halloween Ends' Debut Atop U.K. Box Office
variety.com
entertainment
2022-10-18 14:00:32

Sony's 'Lyle, Lyle, Crocodile,' the film adaptation of the children's books by Bernard Waber, featuring a singing crocodile, bowed at the U.K. and Ireland box office with £2.7 million ($3.1 million), according to numbers released by Comscore. Universal's 'Halloween Ends,' the final instalment in the horror trilogy of sequels to the original 1978 film, starring Jamie Lee Curtis, debuted in second place with £2.1 million. Another horror, Paramount's 'Smile,' collected £1.4 million in third position in its third weekend for a total of £7.
