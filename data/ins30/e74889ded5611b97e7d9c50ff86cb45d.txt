President Ram Nath Kovind's visit to Maghar matter of pride for Uttar Pradesh: CM Yogi Adityanath
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 06:00:00

GORAKHPUR: Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath visited Kabir Sthali at Maghar in Sant Kabir Nagar district on Sunday to take stock of preparations for President Ram Nath Kovind 's visit on June 5.The CM inspected the Kabir Chaura campus and directed the officials to complete the preparations within time and ensure proper security arrangements. The CM also paid his respects to Saint Kabir Das and said, "It is a matter of pride that President Ram Nath Kovind will be visiting this place and there should be proper preparations for welcoming him.
