How AR Helps Cops With Recapturing Fugitives
forbes.com
news
2022-06-02 12:30:00

In 2021, the US Marshals recaptured more than 84,000 fugitives. While that is an astounding figure and a credit to the US Marshal Service (USMS), one can imagine the sheer number of fugitives across the world when so many are recaptured in just one country. With everything that is going on in the current climate, law enforcement officials around the world have enough on their plate already. The added responsibility of re-catching those who are sly enough to escape high-security penitentiaries simply adds to the strain police officers are under.
