Vanessa Feltz had a very unusual defence for Suella Braverman as she departed Liz Truss' chaotic government.
The home secretary lashed out at the prime minister's ‘tumultuous' premiership as she departed the cabinet after just 43 days.
The politician said she was asked to resign after ‘sending an official document from her personal email to a trusted parliamentary colleague as part of policy engagement.
