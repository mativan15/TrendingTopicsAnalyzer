Image created by author using CanvaI know AI writing is the craze these days. I get it. The world belongs to content creators, and we're in a hurry to produce a lot with little effort and get paid six figures from stuff we didn't work for.The reason I'm not giving in to this craze is because of one simple thing: I like doing the work. Even if it brings me a little less money. I'm a slow AF writer. So, naturally, using AI would help me create more. But even so, I'll be happy to take a smaller paycheck than give up on the joy of creating something all by myself.
