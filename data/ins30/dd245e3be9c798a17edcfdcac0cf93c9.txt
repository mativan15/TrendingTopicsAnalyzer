 Waterloo Regional Police say that a teen girl was taken to hospital after a vehicle struck two cyclists in Kitchener on Wednesday night.
According to police, the collision occurred near Ottawa Street and Midland Drive in the Stanley Park area at around 8 p.m.They say two cyclists were crossing Ottawa Street when they were hit by a red Toyota SUV. One of the cyclists, a 13-year-old girl, was taken to an area hospital for non-life-threatening injuries.
