Texas school shooting: Uvalde begins to bury its dead in wake of school massacre
sky.com
news
2022-05-31 15:10:00

The first two of the 19 children killed in one of America's worst school shootings, are being remembered ahead of their burials.Visitation gatherings are being held for 10-year-olds Amerie Jo Garza and Maite Rodriguez at the two funeral homes in the small Texas community of Uvalde.
Over the next two-and-a-half weeks, people in the southwestern Texas town will say goodbye to 19 children and their two teachers, in one visitation, funeral and burial after another.All, were victims of high school dropout Salvador Ramos, who went on a murderous rampage at Robb Elementary School last Tuesday.
