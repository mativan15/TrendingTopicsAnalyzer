Wealth Advisory Firm Also Expands Offices in Washington, D.C.

Overland Park, Kan. --News Direct-- Mariner

Mariner Wealth Advisors, a national wealth advisory firm, today announced that it is set to acquire Boston, Mass.-based Taylor Wealth Management Partners, securing its second office in the Boston area. This acquisition is the latest in a series of moves to bolster its presence along the East Coast. Mariner Wealth Advisors is also expanding in the Washington, D.C. area with the addition of new Managing Director Bob Glass, who will oversee its recently established McLean, Va.
