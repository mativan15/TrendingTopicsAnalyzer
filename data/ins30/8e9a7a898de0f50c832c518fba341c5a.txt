Asian shares extend rally
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-19 02:25:16

Asian shares have mostly risen, with US corporate earnings aiding sentiment, while traders await British inflation readings for clues on how hawkish central banks need to be to fight inflation.

MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan edged up 0.2 per cent on Wednesday, but further gains were capped by slight falls in Chinese shares. China's mainland blue chips lost 0.2 per cent while Hong Kong's Hang Sang index fell 0.1 per cent.

Elsewhere, stocks tracked Wall Street higher. Japan's Nikkei advanced 0.
