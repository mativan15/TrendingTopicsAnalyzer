Student Loan Forgiveness Application Live: How to Apply Today
cnet.com
news
2022-10-18 21:15:02

The student loan debt forgiveness application is now open for borrowers, President Joe Biden announced Monday during a press briefing. Over the weekend, the government launched a beta version, and already millions of applications have been processed -- Biden said 8 million were "handled" over the weekend. The application will remain open until Dec. 31, 2023.It takes about two minutes to fill out the short form. You can apply for up to $20,000 in debt relief if you owe money on federal student loans and earned less than $125,000 a year or $250,000 as a head of household or married filing jointly in either 2020 or 2021.
