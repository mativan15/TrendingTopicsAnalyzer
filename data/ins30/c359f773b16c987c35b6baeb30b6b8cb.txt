Consumers, enthusiasts, and trade professionals alike will rely on Shine Score to effectively measure the beauty of their prized possessions. OCONOMOWOC, Wis. (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Lake Country is launching a first-of-its-kind mobile app to measure the surface gloss level of autos, RVs, and boats. Using cutting-edge technology, users can track and measure the results of finish-detailing, polishing, and paint correction on surfaces simply by snapping a photo and allowing the app to score their work.
