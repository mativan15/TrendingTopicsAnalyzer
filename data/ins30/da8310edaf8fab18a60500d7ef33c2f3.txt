October 20, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Life Science Cares Bay Area, a 501(c)3 nonprofit uniting the regional life sciences industry to address local poverty and social inequities through grants and volunteerism, will host its first annual Impact Festival and fundraiser on Thursday, November 3, 2022, from 4:00 pm to 7:00 pm at the Genentech campus in South San Francisco.



Ten percent of Bay Area residents live in poverty and half experience economic insecurity during the year.
