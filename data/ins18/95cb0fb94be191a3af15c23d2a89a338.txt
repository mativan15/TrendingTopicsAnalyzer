Wemade to launch Mir M: Vanguard and Vagabond in Korea on June 23

news
2022-05-31 17:10:00

Wemade to release its new mobile game Mir M on June 23 [WEMADE] Wemade will launch its new mobile game Mir M: Vanguard and Vagabond in Korea on June 23, the company said Tuesday. Mir M is the latest edition of the Mir Massively multiplayer online roleplaying game (MMORPG) franchise and one of the biggest titles to come from the company this year. The game provides similar action and battle features, but also focuses on the growth of each character's skillsets, according to Wemade. 'We will focus on the finishing touches to perfect the game until the day we start the official service,' said Chang Hyun-guk, the CEO of Wemade, in a press release.
