Leon's Furniture to Develop New Facility in Alberta
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 12:13:00

By Adriano Marchese Canadian retailer Leon's Furniture Ltd. said it will develop a new distribution center and corporate office in Alberta for one of its main business units. Leon's said Monday that the new 500,000-square-foot facility in the provincial capital of Edmonton will be for its business the Brick, and that it is expected to replace the current 365,000-square-foot facility in another part of the city. The new facility will be built on 28 acres of land that the company purchased, and it is a joint venture with land developer Qualico Properties, Leon's said.
