After a two-year hiatus, UAE residents are finally celebrating Diwali on a grand scale. With the festival all set to take place for five days in full swing, the UAE will experience this occasion with a host of fireworks displays and cultural programmes. To find out where to go for all the fun and festivity, take a look.1) Dubai Festival City Mall, DubaiA bespoke show is all set to take place with an elaborate display of laser, fire, water and other projections for all those who visit the area. To add to the festivities, a spectacular firework showcase will enchant the visitors on Sunday, October 23, 2022 at 8:30pm.
