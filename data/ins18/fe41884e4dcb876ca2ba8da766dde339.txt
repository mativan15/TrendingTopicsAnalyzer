June 01, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time MAYNARD, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Stratus Technologies, a global leader in simplified, protected, and autonomous Edge Computing platforms, today announced that it will present, exhibit, and demonstrate its Edge Computing platforms, including the 2nd Generation ztC Edge platform and pre-validated control architectures developed with Rockwell Automation and Schneider Electric at the 26th annual ARC Industry Forum, Accelerating Industrial Digital Transformation and Sustainability, taking place June 6-9 in Orlando, FL.
