You Can Plan a Cheaper Wedding
lifehacker.com
tech
2022-06-02 17:30:00

According to The Knot, the average wedding in 2021 cost $34,000, a steep price tag for a party that lasts all of four hours. If you're like me (meaning you also do not have $34,000 to spend on a wedding), you're probably thinking that there has to be a better way—a cheaper way—to celebrate your nuptials alongside your friends and family. And of course there is. There is a large online support network out there for people eschewing the influences of the marital industrial complex. These folks still want to throw themselves a party, but recognize there's a large gap between a courthouse wedding and a lavish ballroom affair, and they're eager to share their advice for how to make it happen on a much smaller budget.
