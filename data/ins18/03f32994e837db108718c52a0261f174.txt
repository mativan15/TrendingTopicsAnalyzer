Employee happiness is often dependent upon the work environment at each company. A workplace with a strong and supportive culture typically sees highly motivated and productive team members. Conversely, companies that don't have a healthy work environment–whether because they don't encourage a work-life balance or don't listen to the opinions of employees–will not see such good results. For leaders interested in increasing employee engagement, there are several ways you can rethink your company culture to further support your team.
