Collider.com With the rise in superhero media over the last decade or so, it seems as if production companies may be running out of strong comic book origins to work from when coming up with their latest big releases. All the great Marvel and DC comic book heroes have seemingly had their time in the spotlight, and, back in 2018, one of DC's greatest stories was finally brought to our screens, and is still a fan favorite. Debuting back in the 1964 DC comic book titled The Brave and the Bold, the Teen Titans originated as Kid Flash, Aqualad, and Robin who were all, as the name suggests, teenage sidekicks to the great Justice League heroes.
