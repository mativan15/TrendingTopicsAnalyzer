Meet 100 of the Bay Area's fastest-growing companies
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 22:43:00

This year's Fast 100 — the San Francisco Business Times' annual List of the region's Fastest-Growing Private Companies — come from all parts of the Bay Area and are a cross section of the industries that drive the local economy.

They include everything from Uphold, a platform for trading cryptocurrency assets, to Flynn Restaurant Group, one of the nation's largest restaurant franchisees.

As for their growth trajectory, this year's Fast 100 top out at a growth rate between 2019 and 2021 of more than 2,200%.
