By Reuters Published: 20:55 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 20:55 EDT, 14 October 2022 Retired manager Bruce Bochy reportedly met with Texas Rangers general manager Chris Young on Thursday in Nashville, Tenn., according to The Athletic. The Rangers are in the process of hiring a new manager and Young is familiar with Bochy. Young was a pitcher for the San Diego Padres in 2006 when Bochy was the manager of that club. It wasn't immediately known whether the 67-year-old Bochy will officially interview for the job.
