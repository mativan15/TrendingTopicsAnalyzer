4-year-old boy killed in crash that left father in critical condition in Polk County

A four-year-old boy was killed and his father was critically injured in a crash in Polk County Wednesday, according to the Georgia State Patrol.

Troopers said 25-year-old Luis Guzman-Baeza was driving on Cave Springs Road on wet roads when he went around a curve and lost control of his car.

The car went into a spin before rolling over and slamming into a tree, according to the crash report.

Guzman-Baeza's son, Luis Guzman-Cornejo, was ejected from the vehicle and died at the scene.
