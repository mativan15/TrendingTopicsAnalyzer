Billionaire Winklevoss twins to lay off 10% of Gemini employees, blaming 'crypto winter'
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 13:19:00

Billionaire twins Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss announced that 10% of jobs at their cryptocurrency exchange and custodian, Gemini Trust, would be eliminated. The memo was posted on the company's blog. The layoffs were a part of assessing the business amid 'turbulent market conditions that are likely to persist for some time,' they said.
