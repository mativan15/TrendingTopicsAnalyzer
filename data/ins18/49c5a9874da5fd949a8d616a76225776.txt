Indian shares seen opening higher as oil slips
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 14:30:00

Indian shares are set to open higher on Wednesday, taking cues from overnight gains on Wall Street as corporate earnings helped improve sentiment and oil prices fell on worries of higher U.S. supply.India's NSE stock futures listed on the Singapore exchange were up 0.14%, as of 0230 GMT. The MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan gained 0.04%, although they were capped by a fall in Chinese shares.The NSE Nifty 50 index settled 1.01% higher at 17,486.95 on Tuesday, and the S&P BSE Sensex climbed 0.
