It's time for a new Brock Lesnar.
No, I don't want the Beast Incarnate to drop his amazingly fun and refreshing cowboy persona. He should ride that until he's finally ready to retire because he's never been more entertaining and authentic. But Lesnar's role in WWE needs to change. Gone should be the days of him challenging for world titles regularly and an era where he teaches the occasional heel a lesson.
