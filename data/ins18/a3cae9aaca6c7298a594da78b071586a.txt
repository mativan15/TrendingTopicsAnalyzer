Progress has been made in the development for a new urgent care centre in Saskatoon.
The Government of Saskatchewan and Ahtahkakoop Cree Developments (ACD) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) to advance a new approach in the development of a new urgent care centre in Saskatoon.'Today is an exciting day for the government, for our partners at Ahtahkakoop, and for the residents of Saskatoon who are looking forward to this new urgent care centre,' said Health Minister Paul Merriman Monday.
