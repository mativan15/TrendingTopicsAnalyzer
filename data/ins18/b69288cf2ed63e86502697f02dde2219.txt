When it comes to Wall Street, it's often the case that a company's size says a lot about its long-term success. However, that isn't always something you can count on. When you look at shopping center real estate investment trusts (REITs) Federal Realty Investment Trust (FRT 0.90%) and Regency Centers (REG 1.59%), for example, you have to dig a little deeper to pick which one is the better landlord. Here's a quick look at some key metrics.
1. Portfolio size
Regency Centers is one of the largest strip mall REITs, with a portfolio that contains more than 400 properties.
