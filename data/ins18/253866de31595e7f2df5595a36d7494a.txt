Wall St set to jump at open as Goldman, J&J results lift earnings mood

Wall Street Published by Reuters By Ankika Biswas and Shreyashi Sanyal (Reuters) -U.S. stock index futures jumped on Tuesday as strong earnings from Goldman Sachs and Johnson & Johnson lifted hopes that upbeat corporate reports could soothe market worries of a potential recession due to rising inflation and interest rates. Goldman Sachs Group Inc gained 2.7% in premarket trading after reporting a better-than-expected quarterly profit as rising borrowing costs boosted net interest income, cushioning the blow from a slowdown in investment banking.
