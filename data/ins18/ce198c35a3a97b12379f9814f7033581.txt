A builder who was accused of murdering two women six months apart and then burnt their bodies described himself as a ‘psychopath with a conscience', a court heard.

Mark Brown is accused of killing Alex Morgan, 34, and Leah Ware, 33, who both disappeared in Hastings, East Sussex last year.Both women had been working as escorts and were registered with an online agency Mr Brown regularly used.
