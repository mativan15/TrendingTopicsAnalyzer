The board of The Western Union Company (NYSE:WU) has announced that it will pay a dividend of US$0.23 per share on the 30th of June. This means the annual payment is 5.3% of the current stock price, which is above the average for the industry.

Western Union's Dividend Is Well Covered By Earnings

Impressive dividend yields are good, but this doesn't matter much if the payments can't be sustained. Prior to this announcement, Western Union's dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings.
