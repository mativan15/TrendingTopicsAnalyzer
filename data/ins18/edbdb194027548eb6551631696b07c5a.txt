Caller threatens to kidnap, kill SBI chairman, blow up bank office 'if Rs 10 lakh loan not sanctioned
indiatimes.com

2022-10-15 07:45:00

AgenciesState Bank of India (FILE PHOTO)An unidentified caller allegedly threatened to kidnap and murder the chairman of the State Bank of India's (SBI) south Mumbai office. The caller also threatened to blow up the office. The reason for the threats was that the caller wanted a loan of Rs 10 lakh sanctioned to him, according to a police officer. The call was received at the office of the personal assistant of SBI chairman located in the Corporate Centre in Nariman Point area on Wednesday morning.
