Published: 07:36 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 07:36 EDT, 1 June 2022 A professional golfer at a 134-year-old course stole almost £50,000 from the club and kept money raised at two charity golf events for himself, a court heard today.Steven Hunter was head pro at the historic Royal Winchester Golf Club, Hampshire when he allegedly underreported money paid by visitors to play the course.The hearing was told he was caught out after club staff discovered 'consistent' discrepancies, totalling more than £45,000 over a period of almost two years.
