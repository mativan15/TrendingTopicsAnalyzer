getty In today's digital economy, the outcome of cloud migrations can have a profound long-term impact on business outcomes for organizations of all sizes. However, independent research suggests that few organizations fully realize the anticipated benefits from their migration effort as the journey to the cloud increases in complexity. In fact, one-third of cloud migrations fail outright, and only 1 in 4 organizations meet their migration deadlines.
