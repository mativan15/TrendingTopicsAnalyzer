Pakistan's armed forces are likely to be allocated $7.6 billion in the Budget for the next fiscal year, about ₹83 billion higher than the current year, a media report said on Sunday.Defence spending often comes under scrutiny at the time of the annou­nce­ment of the annual budget when allocati­ons for various sectors are earmarked.
