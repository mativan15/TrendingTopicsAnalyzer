ITC shares hit 5-year high ahead of Q2 results announcement
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 10:15:00

Shares of FMCG major ITC, which is scheduled to announce its September quarter earnings later in the day, today hit a fresh 5-year high of Rs 350.20. The stock's last high in 5 years was at Rs 349.50 hit on 23 September 2022. Having rallied over 59% year-to-date, ITC is the second-best Nifty performer in the last year period.

Out of the 31 analysts with coverage on the stock, 30 have buy ratings and one hold. Trendlyne data shows that the average target price on ITC is at Rs 347.20, which is 2.5% higher than the current market price.
