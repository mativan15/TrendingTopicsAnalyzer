iQOO Neo 6 launches in India as the first device in the iQOO Neo series. Check out the price and specs of this low-cost gaming smartphone. iQOO Neo 6 has been launched in India as the company's first Neo series device. The Neo series has long existed in China and has now come to India as low cost gaming device. The iQOO Neo 6 has been around in China already but the Indian variant is slightly different, especially with regards to the specifications. The iQOO Neo 6 is a 5G smartphone that comes with a flagship-grade Snapdragon 870 chip.
