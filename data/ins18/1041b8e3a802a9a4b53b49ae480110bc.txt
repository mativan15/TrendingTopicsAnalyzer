We often forget that smart phones are phones.
But because they are, they can take advantage of the phone infrastructure to deliver information that your device either makes hard to find or just doesn't have at all. You can even change settings easier via that infrastructure.
Plus, entering special codes in your phone dialer is just kind of fun. It makes you feel like a spy.
