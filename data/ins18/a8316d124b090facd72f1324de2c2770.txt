KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy iHeartMedia's Corey OlsonOct 17, 2022The U.S. Constitution reads "all men are created equal," but the Biden administration may want to change that to "all men are created equitable." The administration's push for equity is behind its latest race-based program, the Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA), which is "solely dedicated to...minority business enterprises." That means non-white. Which also means discrimination, according to Rick Esenberg, president of the Wisconsin Institute for Law & Liberty---which is challenging several of these race-based programs in court.
