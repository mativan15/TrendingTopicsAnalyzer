Rangers topple Lightning in Game 2 to take commanding 2-0 series lead

The Rangers have the two-time defending champions on the ropes. 
After overcoming series deficits in each of the first two rounds of the postseason, the Rangers are now on the other side of the coin. They carry a 2-0 series lead into Tampa Bay after their 3-2 win at Madison Square Garden on Friday night.

New York scored three unanswered after the the Lightning's Nikita Kucherov opened the scoring in the first period.
