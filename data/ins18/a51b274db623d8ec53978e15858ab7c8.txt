Senegal's Soccer Victory Is So Much More Than a Soccer Victory

The nation won its first-ever Africa Cup of Nations, giving Senegalese people the world over something to celebrate.Zohra Bensemra / ReutersThere are moments when the success of a sports team can transfix a nation. Such moments provide respite from difficult circumstances and can offer a sense of hope that permeates people's everyday lives. Senegal winning its first-ever Africa Cup of Nations yesterday in Cameroon is such a moment.Senegal is a country where soccer is everywhere. Take a walk along the beaches of Dakar, the capital city, and you will likely find a group of people playing.
