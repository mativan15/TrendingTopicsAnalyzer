Tynwald approves £18m fund to cut hospital waiting times

world
2022-10-20 10:23:50

Image caption, Manx Care has been tasked with cutting waiting times to an average of 18 weeksAn additional £18.3m will be spent on cutting Isle of Man hospital waiting lists after Tynwald approved the move.The money will be used to bring forward treatment in orthopaedics, general surgery and ophthalmology.Health Minister Rob Callister said the wait for procedures had "unfortunately grown to beyond what we would consider safe".Updates on the progress of the initiative must be published every quarter until the money runs out.
