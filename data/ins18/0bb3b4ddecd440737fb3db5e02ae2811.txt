Soho House opens two more homeware stores
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 23:01:00

The Membership Collective Group, which owns Soho House, plans to open two more of its Soho.Home.Studio homeware stores this summer.The outlets, in Westbourne Grove in west London and Melrose Avenue in Los Angeles, will double its store portfolio to four. Last year the group opened a store in New York and another on the King's Road in Chelsea, also in west London.The stores offer interior design services and a programme of workshops and events, as well as selling homeware.Andrew Carnie, president of the group, said: 'These two new locations reflect our confidence in the strength of our aspirational home and interiors business.
