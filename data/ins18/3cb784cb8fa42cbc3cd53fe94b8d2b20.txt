Optical illusion of "giant dog in a swimming pool" baffles internet
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-31 14:59:47

A picture of a sleeping dog has gone viral over the last few days, leaving the internet wondering whether or not it was a "giant dog sleeping in a pool."According to the dog's owner, who goes by the username Feeling-View-2394, the pooch is actually a normal-sized Akita sleeping in a canoe, with the angle of the picture giving the false impression that the dog is super-sized.In a further comment down the thread, they also said they had not realized that they had created an optical illusion: "Because I took the picture I never realized how confusing it could be.
