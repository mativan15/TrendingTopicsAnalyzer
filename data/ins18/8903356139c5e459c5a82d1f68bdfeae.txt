It looks like Williams-Sonoma, Inc. (NYSE:WSM) is about to go ex-dividend in the next four days. Typically, the ex-dividend date is one business day before the record date which is the date on which a company determines the shareholders eligible to receive a dividend. The ex-dividend date is important because any transaction on a stock needs to have been settled before the record date in order to be eligible for a dividend. Thus, you can purchase Williams-Sonoma's shares before the 20th of October in order to receive the dividend, which the company will pay on the 25th of November.
