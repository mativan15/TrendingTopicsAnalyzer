Travellers who have booked a cruise to the Bermuda Triangle are being told they will potentially receive a full refund of their ticket money if the ship disappears in the sea.We aren't talking about any place, we are talking about the Bermuda Triangle here.  Daily Mirror It is an approximately triangular area located off the southeastern coast of the US in the Atlantic Ocean, between Bermuda, Florida and Puerto Rico. Also known as Devil's Triangle, many ships and planes have been reported to have mysteriously disappeared in the region.
