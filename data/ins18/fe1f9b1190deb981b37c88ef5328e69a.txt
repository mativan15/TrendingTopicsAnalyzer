Online news bill C-18 would pump most funds into CBC and other broadcasters
theglobeandmail.com
politics
2022-10-20 01:41:12

A report has revealed the publicly funded CBC and other broadcasters would get the majority of the $329-million a year Bill C-18 would inject into the news industry if it becomes law.Fred Lum/the Globe and MailBroadcasters, including the publicly funded CBC, would get most of the $329-million a year the federal online news bill would inject into the news industry if it becomes law, according to a report by the Parliamentary Budget Officer (PBO).Papers and the online news media would get less than a quarter of the funding pumped into journalism by tech giants Facebook and Google, the report says.
