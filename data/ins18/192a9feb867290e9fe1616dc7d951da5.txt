Orphaned children have home bought for them after strangers donate $400k
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-30 15:33:54

Four children from Ireland, who recently became orphans, have been able to buy their family home thanks to the generosity of strangers.Patrick, 21, Jack, 14, Andrew, 8, and Conor, 5, lost both their parents to cancer in the space of a few months, according to their GoFundMe Page.Their mother, Elaine Clifford, was diagnosed with stage 4 cancer and died in August 2021. Their father, Padraig Clifford, died five months later in January. Both were from County Kerry in Ireland.The GoFundMe page highlighted that after the children's mother's death, Padraig Clifford found it difficult to deal with the loss.
