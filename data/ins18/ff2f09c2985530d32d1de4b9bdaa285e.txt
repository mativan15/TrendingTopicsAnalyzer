BOCA RATON, Fla, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sensus Healthcare, Inc. (Nasdaq: SRTS), a medical device company specializing in highly effective, non-invasive, minimally-invasive and cost-effective treatments for oncological and non-oncological conditions, announces the introduction of its portable Silk™ by Sensus hair removal system with blended wavelengths at the Fall Clinical Dermatology Conference, being held October 20-23 in Las Vegas. Silk by Sensus features a truly portable diode laser with 2000W power, a lightweight handpiece, super-cold cooling tip and high repetition rates.
