Amid rising inflation, what should investors look for when choosing dividend stocks?

With inflation high and the stock market full of volatility, dividends can be a bit of shelter from the storm for investors.

Dividends are payments made by publicly traded companies to their shareholders.

Beyond the dictionary definition, though, they can be an important tool for portfolio growth (particularly if you opt to reinvest your dividends to buy additional stock shares). Dividend payments can provide income for shareholders of all ages.
