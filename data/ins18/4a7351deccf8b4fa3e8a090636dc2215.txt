Binance's US arm turns to ex-FBI agent to head investigations team
fnlondon.com

2022-10-20 16:18:31

The US arm of Binance, the world's largest crypto exchange by trading volume, has hired former FBI agent BJ Kang as its first head of investigations. In his new role, Kang will work closely with law enforcement, regulators and other crypto companies, Binance.US said.
