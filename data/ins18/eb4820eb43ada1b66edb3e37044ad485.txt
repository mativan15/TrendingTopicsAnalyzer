Former NFL Player Antonio Dennard Shot To Death At 32
iheart.com
news
2022-10-17 18:55:37

Former NFL cornerback Antonio Dennard was shot and killed outside of a Pennsylvania bar early Sunday (October 16) morning, WFMZ reports. Dennard, 32, was pronounced dead at a Muhlenberg Township hospital at 3:15 a.m. after being shot at Legends Bar & Restaurant, the Berks County coroner's office confirmed. Local authorities are ruling Dennard's death as a homicide, WFMZ reports. Dennard had spent time with the Jacksonville Jaguars, New York Giants and, most recently, the Green Bay Packers during his NFL career.
