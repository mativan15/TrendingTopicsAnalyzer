PTIIn groundbreaking ceremony 3.0, a total of Rs 4,459 crore will be pumped into new MSMEs being set up in the state.Prime Minister Narendra Modi will be attending the third "groundbreaking" ceremony in Uttar Pradesh on Friday that aims to draw investments of over Rs 80 thousand crore into the state. Gautam Adani, Kumar Mangalam Birla, Sajjan Jindal, and Anant Ambani are among the bigwigs from the corporate world who are slated to attend the event. "The groundbreaking will see investment of Rs 80 thousand crore, which is a record," MSME Minister Nand Gopal Gupta Nandi told reporters here Thursday.
