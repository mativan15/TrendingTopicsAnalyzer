We Need to Build a Better Flu Shot
washingtonpost.com
news
2022-10-17 14:10:53

It's the time of year when US public health officials are practically begging people to go out and get a flu vaccine. This year, their message is particularly urgent: Protect yourself now because the country might be in for a bad flu season that coincides with a resurgence in Covid cases.Last week, I took their advice and got my shot (and my Covid booster at the same time). But I'm likely to be in the minority. A recent survey by the National Foundation for Infectious Diseases gauging flu vaccine attitudes in the US found that just 49% of Americans planned to get immunized this year.
