Scottish musician Lewis Capaldi's upcoming performance at the Exeter Westpoint was announced earlier this week as part of an arena tour of Europe. The 26-year-old Critic's Choice Brit Awards 2019 nominee will perform at Westpoint Arena on February 2 next year.
 The master of emotional songcraft rose to success by selling 10 million copies of his first album. Broken By Desire To Be Heavenly Sent, Capaldi's second album, will be released on May 19.
 
What are the ways to secure early entry to the 2023 Europe tour?Book a pre-order for Broken By Desire To Be Heavenly sent before 4 pm on October 25 in any format from the Lewis Capaldi Store to get a unique ticket presale code for the arena tour.
