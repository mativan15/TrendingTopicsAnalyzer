By Australian Associated Press Published: 00:10 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 01:50 EDT, 16 October 2022 The Western Bulldogs are on the verge of ending their AFLW finals drought after captain Ellie Blackburn led from the front in a hard-fought 15-point win over St Kilda. Blackburn (24 disposals, six tackles) and in-form ruck Alice Edmonds (38 hit-outs, nine tackles) were outstanding as the Bulldogs overcame inaccuracy in front of goal to post a 4.11 (35) to 3.2 (20) victory in Ballarat on Sunday.
