Tesla Steps Up Job Ads As Recession Clouds Gather

business
2022-10-17 10:36:41

The logo of Tesla is seen in Taipei, Taiwan August 11, 2017. 
While Elon Musk may be worried about the economy, job openings on Tesla's website suggest the world's most valuable car maker is hiring employees at a quick pace, in nearly every category of job.Tesla this week listed over 6,900 jobs on its career website, almost a 50% surge since mid-June, when Reuters began tracking the data. That compares to a 2022 peak of over 7,400 job ads in May, according to data similarly tracked by Thinknum Alternative Data and hedge fund Snow Bull Capital.
