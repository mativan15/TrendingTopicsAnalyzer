WiCyS is an important part of that effort, helping get people involved on the basis of how much they can help," said Craig Newmark, founder of craigslist. COOKEVILLE, Tenn. (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Craig Newmark Philanthropies (CNP) is the latest leader in cyber to join forces with Women in CyberSecurity (WiCyS) to support the Security Training Scholarship. WiCyS is proud to announce the support of CNP to further its' work to increase the number of women in cybersecurity and help advance their careers.
