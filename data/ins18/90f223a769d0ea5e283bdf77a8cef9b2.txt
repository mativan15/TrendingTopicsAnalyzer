NEW YORK, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--VICI Properties Inc. (NYSE: VICI) ("VICI," "VICI Properties" or the "Company"), an experiential real estate investment trust, announced today that it has entered into a delayed draw term loan facility for up to $200 million (the "Loan Facility") to fund the development of Canyon Ranch's newest wellness-resort offering, Canyon Ranch Austin in Austin, Texas. Canyon Ranch is a pioneer in the place-based wellness space with the mission to inspire a well way of life through integrative wellness guidance from world-class experts across a broad range of disciplines.
