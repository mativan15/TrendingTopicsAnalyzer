Jeremy Hunt: The low profile Remainer who became prime minister in all but name
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 20:27:00

For a man who spent nine uninterrupted years in the Cabinet, including six as Britain's longest-serving health secretary, Jeremy Hunt left surprisingly little impression on the public imagination.
For much of that period, the only two images of Mr Hunt that stuck in the mind were an enthusiastically rung bell snapping off in his hands live on TV and nearly braining a bystander, and the time he got confused about his wife's nationality.
While Michael Gove was the whirring brainbox sent in to transform underperforming departments with sweeping reforms, Mr Hunt was the safe pair of hands called on to steady the ship and clamp down on mishaps.
