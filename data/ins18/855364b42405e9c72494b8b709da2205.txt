Search underway for future site of Red Deer's permanent homeless shelter
cbc.ca
news
2022-05-27 23:37:00

Red Deer resident and business owner Tracy Chabot says the new permanent housing shelter must not go downtown, otherwise problems related to the temporary shelter will persist. (Submitted by Tracy Chabot)The City of Red Deer is encouraging residents, business owners and non-profit agencies that serve the community's homeless population to provide feedback as the city considers where to put a new homeless shelter — after council's earlier suggestion didn't go over well.Earlier this year, city officials proposed a downtown site, at 4934 54th Avenue.
