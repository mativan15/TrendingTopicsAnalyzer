Gaston County Schools' 28 valedictorians and salutatorians in 2022
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-05 16:27:32

Gaston County Schools has 28 students in the Class of 2022 who have achieved the top academic distinction of valedictorian or salutatorian.

The senior with the highest weighted grade point average (GPA) is selected valedictorian, and the senior with the second-highest weighted GPA is chosen salutatorian.

'We are proud to recognize and honor 28 outstanding seniors in the Class of 2022 as our valedictorians and salutatorians for Gaston County Schools,' Superintendent of Schools Jeffrey Booker said.
