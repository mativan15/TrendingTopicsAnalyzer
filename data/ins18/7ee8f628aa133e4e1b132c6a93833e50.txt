Alex Wong/Getty Images News If you're curious why Fannie Mae (OTCQB:FNMA) and Freddie Mac (OTCQB:FMCC) are in conservatorship, here's what happened. After the financial crisis, the government decided to take control of the GSEs to ensure that the market for agency mortgage-backed securities did not implode. Fannie and Freddie still have some things they need to do before they exit conservatorship. That includes improving their capital reserves and resolving legal claims against them by various stockholders.
