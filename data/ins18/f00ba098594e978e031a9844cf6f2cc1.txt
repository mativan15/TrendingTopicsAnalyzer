UK-based Planet Smart City inks pact to acquire architectural, engineering design co Politecna Europa
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 08:30:00

Getty Images(Representative image)UK-based affordable housing and advanced proptech solutions major Planet Smart City has entered into an agreement to acquire Politecna Europa, a company specialising in architectural and engineering design. The specialist knowledge of both companies will be applied to create intelligent neighbourhoods and homes by increasing their efficiency and maximizing the user experience of residents across its projects world over including India. Planet Idea, Planet Smart City's Competence Centre, and Politecna Europa are already involved in activities oriented in this direction.
