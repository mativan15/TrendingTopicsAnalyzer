Published: 19:35 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 19:36 EDT, 18 October 2022 Union barons yesterday threatened to bring the nation to a standstill in what critics claimed was an attempt to force the first 'general strike' in nearly 100 years.Militant bosses called for a co-ordinated 'uprising' to 'defeat the Government' as they addressed fringe events at the TUC conference in Brighton.They bragged they had 'already achieved' a general strike, reeling off a long list of workers expected to vote for walkouts as they promised to plunge the nation into 'chaos'.
