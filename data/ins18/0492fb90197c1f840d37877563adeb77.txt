'Union Jack flying!' UK pays 'tribute to Queen' with first-ever launch from British soil
express.co.uk
gaming
2022-06-03 09:01:00

Virgin Orbit will pay its respects to Her Majesty this summer when its Launcher One blasts satellites from Spaceport Cornwall into low-Earth orbit. The mission, dubbed Prometheus-2, will see Virgin Orbit's rocket taking off horizontally from a modified Boeing 747 jet to carry Cubesat satellites into space. These systems provide a test platform for monitoring radio signals such as GPS and sophisticated imaging and will be used by the Ministry of Defence.Defence Procurement Minister, Jeremy Quin said: 'Space technology is crucial for developing Defence capabilities and the launch of Prometheus-2 represents another important step forward for our homegrown space programme.
