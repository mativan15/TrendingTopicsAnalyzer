Organisation takes up mission to improve quality of teaching, make learning joyful in Arunachal Pradesh
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-19 16:31:39

Aiming to make learning joyful an organisation called Reach to Teach (RTT) Foundation, on Wednesday said it has taken up the mission to improve the quality of teaching in government schools in Arunachal Pradesh.
Reach to Teach Foundation, had inked a tripartite memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the state government and NITI Aayog in July this year for a large-scale transformation in school education.

The three-year partnership (2022-25) focused on the enhancement of the learning outcome of students across 3,000 plus government schools in the state, thereby reaching out to over two lakh children.
