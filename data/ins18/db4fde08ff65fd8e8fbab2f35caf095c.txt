Shares of Cloudflare (NET -1.59%) sank 35% in May, according to S&P Global Market Intelligence. The global cloud services provider posted its first-quarter earnings that were above analyst expectations; however, investors still decided to sell off the stock in the days and weeks following the report. We can also factor in the decline of the Nasdaq 100 and technology stocks, which continued last month.
