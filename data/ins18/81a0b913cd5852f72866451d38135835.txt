FIA accused of more 'outrageous' scandal than Red Bull's budget breach

The FIA are yet to decide on Red Bull's punishment (Image: GETTY)Former Dutch racing driver Allard Kalff believes the 'leak' from the FIA that Red Bull were in breach of last year's budget cap is even more 'outrageous' than the violation itself. Ahead of the FIA publishing their auditing findings for last year's £114million spending cap, reports emerged that Red Bull had overspent by around £10m.Rival teams were furious with both Mercedes and Ferrari insisting any breach gave Red Bull a significant advantage.
