Image source, Jonathan TaylorImage caption, Lawyer Jonathan Taylor spent a year stuck in Croatia fighting extradition to MonacoA court has dismissed an appeal against the decision to clear an oil industry whistleblower who spent more than two years fighting extradition to Monaco.Jonathan Taylor was arrested in Dubrovnik in July 2020 on an Interpol red licence, issued by Monaco, on bribery and corruption charges.He had previously exposed bribery at his old employer, Dutch oil firm SBM Offshore.Mr Taylor said the latest ruling had "given me back control of my life".
