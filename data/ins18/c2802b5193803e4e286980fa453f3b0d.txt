Thanks to the world's insatiable appetite for computer chips, semiconductor-testing equipment maker Lasertec saw sales and orders continue to grow over the past year. The Tokyo-based company, which sells to some of the world's biggest chipmakers, benefited from the remote work trend and the growing market for 5G smartphones and other devices.
