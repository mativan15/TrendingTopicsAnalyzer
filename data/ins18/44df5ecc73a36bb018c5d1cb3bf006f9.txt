PTIRelatives and family members of deceased teacher Rajni Bala mourn near her mortal remains, at their residence in Samba. Rajni was shot at allegedly by terrorists in the Kulgam district of Kashmir ValleyThe Congress has hit out at the Centre over rising cases of target killings, especially of Kashmiri Pandits and and other Hindus, in the Valley and demanded to know from the Modi government its strategy to curb terror strikes and to ensure safety of targeted social sections. AICC fielded Kashmiri Pandit Vivek Tankha, a Congress parliamentarian, at the official briefing to air the opposition party's concerns on targeted killings of Kashmiri Pandits and other Hindus and to demand safety and justice.
