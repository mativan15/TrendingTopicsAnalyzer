No penalty will be levied on people not wearing face masks in the national capital, the Delhi government announced on Thursday. The Delhi Disaster Management Authority (DDMA) has decided to withdraw the order that made wearing face mask in public places compulsory and those found violating the mandate were fined ₹500, according to Delhi's health and family welfare department.
