Colgate-Palmolive's (NYSE: CL) stock price rose just 1.32x from around $60 in 2018 end to $79 currently, primarily due to favorable changes in its P/S multiple. During this period, the company also witnessed a 12% rise in revenues and a 4% drop in the outstanding share count.
