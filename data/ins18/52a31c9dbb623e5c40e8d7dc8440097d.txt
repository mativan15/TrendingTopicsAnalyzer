The terms remake, re-release, remaster and reboot get thrown around a lot these days, often to the point of mass gamer confusion. Destroy All Humans! 2: Reprobed, with its absolutely appropriate and totally bonkers subtitle, seems to be the complete former: A from-the-ground-up remake with new graphics and new features. The original Destroy All Humans! 2, which starred everyone's favorite sarcastic cloned extraterrestrial Crypto, launched back in 2006 for both the PlayStation 2 and original Xbox.
