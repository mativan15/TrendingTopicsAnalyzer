Covid-19 and emergence of new-age asset light healthcare models reform hospital operations in India
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 10:39:05

Covid-19 brought a wave of change, right from shifting the way people think about their health to how the healthcare sector approaches healthcare. The pandemic clearly highlighted the gaps in our healthcare system and the pressing need for quick changes to prepare us for the future. Currently, even though the number of instances of Covid-19 have reduced, hospital management norms have been permanently altered and are now supported by a robust healthcare system that has been strengthened by increased testing, digital consultations, and social distancing norms.
