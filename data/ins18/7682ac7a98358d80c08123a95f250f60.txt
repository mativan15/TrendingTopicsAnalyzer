Amid deadly Russia drone strikes, the Ukraine government has developed a long-range capability drone. State-owned defence holding firm Ukroboronprom has said that the new drone is nearing completion. The company also released an image of what appeared to be part of the nosecone of a drone. With a weight of 75KG warhead, the Ukrainian version of the Kamikaze drone will have a range of 1,000 kms.
