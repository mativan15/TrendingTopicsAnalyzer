Acumera Expands Services and Market Reach by Acquiring Netsurion's Secure Edge Networking

AUSTIN, Texas and FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Acumera, a leading provider of software-as-a-service (SaaS) solutions for edge computing, network operations, visualization and security, today announced its acquisition of Netsurion's Secure Edge Networking business. Under the agreement, Acumera will acquire Netsurion's all-in-one managed networking, resilience, compliance and security solution, as well as the development staff, expertise and support personnel associated with Secure Edge Networking.
