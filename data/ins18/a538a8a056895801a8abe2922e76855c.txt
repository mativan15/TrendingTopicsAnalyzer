Crypto Price Today: Bitcoin holds $19K; Solana, Polygon, XRP & Uniswap crack up to 3%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 06:00:00

New Delhi: Crypto market remained range-bound on Wednesday as Bitcoin continued to hold above the $19,000 throughout early Asian trading hours. Crypto is locked into a consolidation mode and it will continue to remain there as investors are wary of Fed over-tightening and recession. Majority of the crypto tokens were trading lower on Wednesday. XRP, Cardano, Solana, Uniswap tumbled 3% each, whereas Ethereum and Shiba Inu dropped 2% each.

The global cryptocurrency market cap was trading mildly lower at $926.
