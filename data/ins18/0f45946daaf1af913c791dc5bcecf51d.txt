Takeaways are all well and good but I'm very big on figuring out how to make some of your favourites at home. It's less expensive, more healthy and, I find makes those treat occasions where you do splash out on takeaways – ie, not an ordinary Tuesday night – that much more special. This Chinese menu classic is surprisingly simple to make. The key is in that sugar-vinegar-tomato combo; balance that right, and everything else is a breeze. Get the kids involved in mixing and moulding the meatballs while you prep the vegetables.
