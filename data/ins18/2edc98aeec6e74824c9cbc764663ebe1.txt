October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--UserTesting (NYSE: USER), a leader in video-based human insight, today announced the winners of the illumi Awards at The Human Insight Summit (#THiS22). The Human Insight Summit is UserTesting's annual customer conference dedicated to helping organizations adapt their businesses to continual changes taking place, through the application of human insight. The event brings together some of the best minds in research, design, product, and marketing from around the globe from Fortune 500 companies to small and medium-sized businesses.
