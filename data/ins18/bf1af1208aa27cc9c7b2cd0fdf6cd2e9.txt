Calgary Flames suffer first loss as Tuch's hat trick leads Sabres to 6-3 win
globalnews.ca

2022-10-21 11:43:03

Led by strong individual performances including Ramus Dahlin's record-setting start to the season, the Buffalo Sabres have looked the part of a more formidable opponent than years past.
Dahlin scored for the fourth straight game — an NHL record for a defenceman to open the season — and Alex Tuch notched his first career hat trick as the Sabres handed the Calgary Flames their first loss of the season on Thursday night with a 6-3 victory.'It's crazy,' Dahlin said about his accomplishment.
