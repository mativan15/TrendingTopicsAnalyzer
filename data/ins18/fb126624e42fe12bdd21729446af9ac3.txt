Magellan Shares Fall as Its Funds Under Management Decline
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-06 00:49:00

By Alice Uribe SYDNEY--Magellan Financial Group Ltd. shares fell in early Monday trading after the company's funds under management declined in May. The Australian fund manager's shares were 9.4% lower at A$13.53. The stock is the poorest performing stock on the ASX 200 so far today. Magellan said its FUM declined from A$68.6 billion on April 29 to A$65.0 billion at the end of May. The company's retail FUM fell to A$23.6 billion on May 31, from A$24.8 billion at the end of April, while FUM in its institutional unit dropped to A$41.
