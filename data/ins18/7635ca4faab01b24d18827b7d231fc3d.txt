TYSON FURY claims he'll pay Piers Morgan £1million in COINS after losing a bet thanks to his retirement U-turn.
Fury "quit" after beating Dillian Whyte in April - but launches a comeback against fellow Brit Derek Chisora at Tottenham Hotspur stadium on December 3.
Tyson Fury says he'll honour a colourful bet with Piers MorganCredit: Reuters
Fury shows the money but its coins not notes he plans to dish out to Morgan after reversing his retirement decision
TV presenter Morgan was one of many to doubt the undefeated two-time world heavyweight champion would walk away from boxing while still in his prime and raking it in.
