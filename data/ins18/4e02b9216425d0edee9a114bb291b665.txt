ASU professor appointed to first federal sustainable purchasing committee in the US

2022-10-19 22:25:00

October 19, 2022 With discretionary spending totaling $1.6 trillion last fiscal year, the federal government has a lot of purchasing power. Spending those dollars sustainably may be key to helping the U.S. achieve its climate change goals. Nicole Darnall, a professor of management and public policy in Arizona State University's School of Sustainability in the College of Global Futures, is pointing the way as a member of the nation's first advisory committee focused on sustainable federal spending.
