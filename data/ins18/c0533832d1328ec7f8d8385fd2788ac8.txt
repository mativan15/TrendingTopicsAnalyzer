Upgraded Equipment is Projected to Reduce the Site's CO2 Emissions by 50%

WORCESTER, Mass., October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Saint-Gobain has started up operations at its new electrical powerhouse on its flagship manufacturing campus in Worcester, Massachusetts, which is projected to reduce the site's carbon dioxide emissions by 50%.
