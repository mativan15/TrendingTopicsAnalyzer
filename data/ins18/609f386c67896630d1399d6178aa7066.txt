Published: 07:13 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 07:15 EDT, 30 May 2022 This is the moment a driver smashed a £500,000 Ferrari supercar into a row of five parked vehicles before fleeing the scene.Home CCTV footage shows some of the cars - parked several metres apart on a residential street - collide with another in a split second as the Ferrari ploughs into them.The driver, dressed in a t-shirt and shorts, can be seen frantically trying both doors before clambering out of the driver seat window and scurrying away.
