By Stefan Krauss, SVP - General Manager Discrete Manufacturing Industries and Energy & Natural Resources As participating nations emerged from COP26 with an agreement to achieve net-zero by 2050, conversations around the ways and means of achieving this is set to gather steam. Consumers are no longer ignorant on matters of sustainability and demand change. Meanwhile, investors and financial institutions seek to put the onus back on businesses to make them more inclusive, socially responsible, and sustainable.
