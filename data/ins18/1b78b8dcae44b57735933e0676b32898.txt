Will study past reports, orders to decide on Vokkaliga quota: CM

With demand for 12% reservation for Vokkaligas gaining momentum, Karnataka chief minister Basavaraj Bommai on Tuesday said many communities have been seeking quota and that the state government will act based on several judgements and reports of the permanent backward class commission.Adichunchanagiri seer Nirmalanandanatha Swami on Monday said the Vokkaligas, who constitute about 16% of the population in state, need more than the existing 4% reservation.'I have already said this in the assembly.
