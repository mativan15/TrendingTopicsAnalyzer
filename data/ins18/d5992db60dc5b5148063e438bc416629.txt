Getting ESG right for the textile industry

Environmental, social and governance (ESG) commitments are top priority for all business stakeholders. Companies have been presented with clear and rising expectations from these stakeholders – from investors to customers – to be proactive while managing ESG risks and opportunities as part of their overall business strategy. In fact, this interest has reached a tipping point with the stakeholders demanding that business leaders improve sustainability practices that benefit not only the bottomline but also generate a wider societal impact.
