Malt whisky society gains foothold in South Korea
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 23:01:00

The Scotch Malt Whisky Society is expanding into South Korea via a partnership with the country's fourth largest liquor distributor.The Artisanal Spirits Company, owner of the society, has signed a franchise agreement with FJ Korea. It will be the society's first move into South Korea.FJ Korea was founded in 2006 and is said to have a presence at 2,000 on-trade venues, including in cities such as Seoul. It distributes brands including Bacardi, Dewars Scotch whisky, Bushmills Irish whiskey, Jose Cuervo tequila and Bombay Sapphire gin.
