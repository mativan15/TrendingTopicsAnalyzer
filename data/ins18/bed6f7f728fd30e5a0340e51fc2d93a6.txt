Ambuja Cements Q2 Results: Profit plunges on rising cost
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 11:00:00

BENGALURU - Indian conglomerate Adani Group-backed Ambuja Cements on Friday reported a 68.75% slump in September-quarter profit, hit by ballooning expenses.Standalone profit for the three months ended Sept.30 was Rs 138 crore ($16.68 million), compared with a profit of Rs 441 crore a year ago. Revenue rose to 13.38% to Rs 3670 crore.Analysts, on average, had expected the company to report a profit of Rs 470 crore, according to IBES data from Refinitiv.Overall expenses surged 32.25% to Rs 3546 crore, while sales volumes expanded 12% to 6.
