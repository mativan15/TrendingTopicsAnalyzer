More than half of UK homeowners claim property is a work in progress
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-06-01 11:32:54

The study of 2,000 property owners found almost half don't think their home will ever be ‘finished' – with some of the bigger tasks still in progress ranging from loft conversions to half-done home officesSiobhan Murphy has teamed up with Shawbrook BankMore than half of UK homeowners claim their property is a 'work in progress' - with an average of five DIY jobs currently languishing unfinished in homes across the country. Some of these on-going renovations include half painted walls and doors, incomplete fire pits or pizza ovens in the garden - and even entire kitchen overhauls which have fallen by the wayside.
