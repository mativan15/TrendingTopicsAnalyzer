The United States has agreed to supply Ukraine with advanced rocket launch systems to try to turn the tide of the war in the eastern Donbas region, where the city of Severodonetsk looks poised to fall to Russian forces.
Following desperate pleas from Volodymyr Zelensky, the Ukrainian president, Washington said it would be sending high-tech, medium-range rocket systems. Germany will supply Ukraine with the Iris-T air defence system and reportedly the Mars II multiple launch rocket system.
