Google, Netflix Under Scrutiny In South Korea Over Network Fees

Smartphone with Netflix logo is placed on a keyboard in this illustration taken April 19, 2022. 
South Korea's parliament saw heated debate on Friday over proposed legislation to make global content providers such as Netflix and Alphabet's Google pay South Korean network fees.The deliberations mirror attempts in Europe by some countries that want the European Commission to come up with legislation that ensures Big Tech firms partly finance telecoms infrastructure, as video streaming and other data usage surges.
