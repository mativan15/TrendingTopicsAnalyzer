Budapest's French-Hungarian high school is at the center of anti-Orban protests
lemonde.fr
news
2022-10-19 03:00:13

In front of the Ferenc-Kölcsey High School in Budapest, Hungary, on October 5, 2022. ANDI GALDI VINKO FOR "LE MONDE" She can no longer walk the streets of Budapest without Hungarians expressing their support: At 53 years old, Katalin Törley has just lost the position of French teacher that she held for 23 years at the Ferenc-Kölcsey High School, a prestigious and highly reputed establishment in the center of the Hungarian capital that awards a bilingual French-Hungarian baccalaureate. Her sudden sacking on October 5 came as such a shock in Hungary that she has become one of the main figures of the protests against Prime Minister Viktor Orban that have been shaking the Hungarian education system for several months.
