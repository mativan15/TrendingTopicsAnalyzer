By DAMIAN J. TROISE and ALEX VEIGA - AP Business Writers Stocks closed broadly higher again on Wall Street Tuesday, adding to weekly gains for major indexes that have been mired in a broad slump amid inflation and recession concerns.The S&P 500 rose 1.1%, with roughly 90% of the stocks in the benchmark index notching gains. The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 1.1% and the Nasdaq composite ended 0.9% higher.Trading was choppy, at one point pulling the Nasdaq into the red as technology stocks lost ground then rallied by the end of the day.
