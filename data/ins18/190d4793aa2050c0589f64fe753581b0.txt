AgenciesNetflix says that the payment information will not be transferred to the new account.Netflix has announced a new feature called 'Profile Transfer', which allows users to transfer their settings to a new account using the tool. Netflix profiles can now be transferred easily to a new user account, which means the settings, saved games, history, My List section, recommendations, and more can be handily transferred from an existing account to a new one. People using your account can switch to the new account for which they have paid their membership.
