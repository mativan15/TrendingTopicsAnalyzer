Samsung has slashed the prices of its 4K TVs and soundbars (Image: SAMSUNG)Samsung is running a limited-time-only sale that offers a tidy discount on its latest and greatest TVs. The South Korean tech giant is offering a 10 percent price cut on its wide range of TVs thanks to a new code you can use right now. This will slash the price of all TVs and soundbars available on the Samsung website.To take advantage of the discount you'll need to enter the JUBILEETV10 code at checkout.As you can probably gather, this voucher code has been launched in the run-up to the Queen's Platinum Jubilee.
