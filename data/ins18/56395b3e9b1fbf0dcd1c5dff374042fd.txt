National Herald case: ED issues fresh summons to Rahul Gandhi on June 13
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-03 06:43:10

The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Friday issued fresh summons to Congress leader Rahul Gandhi in the National Herald newspaper case. Gandhi has now been asked to appear before the ED on June 13.Gandhi was asked to appear on June 2 but the Congress MP from Wayanad sought a fresh date as he was out of India. "Rahul Gandhi is expected to be back home by June 5 after which he will seek another date from the ED for questioning in a money laundering case," Congress sources earlier told news agency ANI on condition of anonymity.
