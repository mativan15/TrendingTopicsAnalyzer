Sebi streamlines approval process for change in control of portfolio manager
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 14:15:00

Further, applications for fresh registration following change in control will be made to Sebi within six months from the date of prior approval.Capital markets regulator Sebi on Thursday streamlined the process of providing its approval to the proposed change in control of a portfolio manager. The new guidelines will be applicable from June 15, the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) said in a circular. Under this, the regulator has specified the procedure that needs to be followed by portfolio managers in matters which involve scheme(s) of arrangement which needs sanction of the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT).
