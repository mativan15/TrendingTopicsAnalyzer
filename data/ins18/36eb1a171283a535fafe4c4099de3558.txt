Does meditation work? Some scientists are sceptical
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 06:00:00

Once upon a time meditation was regarded as the prerogative of Buddhist monks, hippies and the Beatles. But the practice has moved far from its spiritual roots since the concept of mindfulness gained traction in the Seventies and the focus shifted from spiritual goals to more earthly endeavours.Mindfulness is on our phones, in our workplaces and in our wallets; the meditation app Headspace has been downloaded more than 65 million times and the global meditation industry will be worth an estimated $9 billion by 2027.
