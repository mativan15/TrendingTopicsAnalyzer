LOS GATOS, CA / ACCESSWIRE / October 20, 2022 / - Atomera Incorporated (NASDAQ:ATOM), a semiconductor materials and technology licensing company, announced today that it plans to release its third quarter 2022 financial results after the market closes on Wednesday, Nov. 9, 2022.

The company will host a live video Zoom webinar at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time (5:00 p.m. Eastern Time) on Wednesday, Nov. 9, 2022, to discuss the results. The live webinar can be accessed through Atomera's investor relations website at https://ir.
