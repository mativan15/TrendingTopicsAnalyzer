Shares of Telefonaktiebolaget LM Ericsson (ERIC -15.13%) fell hard on Thursday, following a third-quarter earnings report that fell short of market expectations. Ericsson's stock traded 15.1% lower at 1:20 p.m. ET, having fallen as much as 19.5% earlier in the day.
So what
The Swedish maker of telecom-grade networking equipment saw Q3 sales rise 21% year over year to SEK 68 billion ($6 billion). Organic sales, adjusted for currency exchange effects and the recently closed acquisition of cloud-based communications expert Vonage, increased by 3% from the year-ago period.
