October 20, 2022 02:45 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Sen. Ron Johnson (R-Wis.) for re-election to the U.S. Senate, and Reps. Scott Fitzgerald (R-Wis.), Mike Gallagher (R-Wis.), Glenn Grothman (R-Wis.), Bryan Steil (R-Wis.), and Tom Tiffany (R-Wis.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.
