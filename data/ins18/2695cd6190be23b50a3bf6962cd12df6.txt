Variety - TV News Italy's Lux Vide has unveiled first-look images of the second series of 'Good Morning Mom,' starring Raoul Bova and Maria Chiara Giannetta.The show, which debuted in 2021, sees Bova as a father who tries to keep his family together after his wife falls into a coma. But her presence, albeit a silent one, is still very important to everyone around her.The first season sold to multiple territories, including Hungary, Croatia, Brazil and Mena territories.
