Getty ImagesReligare Finvest has also proposed to offer 40% of the proceeds from the settlement it would receive from DBS Bank (earlier Lakshmi Vilas Bank). The bank had encashed ₹791 crore worth of fixed deposits of RFL in FY20. The matter is under litigation, with the finance company claiming the amount was misappropriated without its consent.Religare Finvest has proposed a one-time settlement of ₹2,300 crore against its unpaid borrowings to thwart possible bankruptcy proceedings by lenders, two people aware of the development told ET.
