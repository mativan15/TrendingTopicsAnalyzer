MP CM Shivraj Chouhan on May 30 took stock of preparations ahead of BJP Chief JP Nadda's visit to state. State BJP President VD Sharma accompanied him. BJP National President JP Nadda is scheduled to visit Madhya Pradesh from June 01 till June 03.
