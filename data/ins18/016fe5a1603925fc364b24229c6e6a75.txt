NASHVILLE (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 StaksHoops LLC and StaksTM, in partnership with Donald Hand Basketball (DHB), will be hosting their inaugural StaksHoops Basketball Combine this Saturday October 22, 2022, at U-Turn Sportsplex in Richmond, VA. The Combine is an invite-only event for 40 of the top high school basketball players on the East Coast to compete in an elite environment and showcase their skills for college recruitment. Participants will be put through several tests of athleticism, including the vertical jump and short shuttle sprint, as well as compete in position-specific drills, 1-on-1, and 5-on-5 games.
