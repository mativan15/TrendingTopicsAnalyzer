Billionaire philanthropist MacKenzie Scott gives Girl Scouts $85M
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-18 23:06:00

Philanthropist MacKenzie Scott has donated $84.5 million to Girl Scouts of the USA and 29 of its local councils.

The 110-year-old youth group announced the gift on Tuesday, calling it the largest donation from a single individual in its history.
