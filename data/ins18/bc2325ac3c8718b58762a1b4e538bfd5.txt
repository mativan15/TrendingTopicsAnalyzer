(Bloomberg) -- Chinese President Xi Jinping signaled no change in direction for two main risk factors dragging down China's economy -- strict Covid rules and housing market policies -- providing little lift to a worsening growth outlook.

Xi praised Covid Zero, his no-tolerance approach to containing infections, during a speech opening the 20th Communist Party congress in Beijing on Sunday, although he didn't reference the virus again in sections laying out plans for the future.
