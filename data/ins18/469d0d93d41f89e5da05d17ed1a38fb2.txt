The feature film "Pather Panchali" by renowned director Satyajit Ray has been named the best Indian movie of all time by the International Federation of Film Critics (FIPRESCI).

Following a survey held by the India branch of FIPRESCI, the top ten films in the Indian cinema history list have been revealed, with the 1955 movie taking the top spot.
