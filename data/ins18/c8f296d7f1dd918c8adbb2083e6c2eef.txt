June 01, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN RAMON, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Five9, Inc. (NASDAQ: FIVN), an industry-leading provider of the intelligent cloud contact center, announced today that Five9 Intelligent Virtual Agent (IVA) has been named a leader in the 2022 Opus Research 'Decision Makers' Guide to Enterprise Intelligent Assistants.' Five9 IVA leverages the latest AI technologies (natural language processing, sentiment analysis, speech recognition, text-to-speech) to deliver intuitive, effective self-service in the contact center.
