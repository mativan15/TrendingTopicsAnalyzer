Man beat roommate to death, left body in home for days: Police
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 11:46:17

A Detroit man is facing murder charges after allegedly beating his roommate to death and leaving the body inside the home for days.According to police, Corey Stacey Canty, 44, is facing charges of one count of premeditated first-degree murder.Local news outlet WDIV reported that Detroit police were called to a home on Fairview Street in the east side of the city at about 1:40 p.m. on October 10. According to the report, officers found the body of 43-year-old John Boble.Medical officials pronounced Boble dead at the scene and cited blunt force trauma as the cause of death.
