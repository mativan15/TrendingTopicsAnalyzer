October 20, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cynet, the world's first provider of an autonomous, end-to-end, fully automated extended detection and response (XDR) platform, today announced the launch of its newly optimized Global Partner Program, which offers channel sales organizations the ability to significantly increase profitability on their Cynet business. The updated program includes new pricing, discounts and resources aimed at streamlining the partner journey to reduce operational and buying costs.
