Devon head teacher brings in mother to help cash-strapped school

Image caption, Steve Hitchcock said: "Getting cleaners and lunchtime staff is really tricky because the pay is low"A head teacher said he had been forced to ask his mother and sister to help out due to a lack of staff.Steve Hitchcock, of St Peter's Primary in Budleigh Salterton, Devon, said he was losing some catering staff to supermarkets because they paid more."It does feel like something needs to give, enough is enough," he said.The Department for Education said it understood that schools were facing cost pressures and said they would benefit from the Energy Relief Scheme.
