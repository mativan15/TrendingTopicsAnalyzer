'He'll put me on the clock and ask me, like, ‘What's the sin of pi over three?' I'm sitting there like, ‘Geez,' " Miles said. 'It's all challenging, which I love. It builds our relationship.'Miles is adept when it comes to learning and applying a new high-level concept. Previously a standout running back for the Buccaneers (5-1), he switched to quarterback this year to fill a void.After racking up 157 carries for 1,539 yards, and 25 touchdowns a season ago, he has completed 63 percent of his passes, has thrown twice as many TDs as interceptions, and has a whopping 22 rushing scores in six games as a dual-threat star.
