Nine companies in the Middle East and India were presented with awards for demonstrating innovation and leadership

MUMBAI, India, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The 10th edition of Frost & Sullivan's Best Practices Awards for the Middle East and South Asia (MESA) region recognized nine companies demonstrating excellence, innovation, and leadership during a virtual awards ceremony held on October 19, 2022. The awards honored and acknowledged companies that have achieved significant growth and made a difference in their respective sectors.
