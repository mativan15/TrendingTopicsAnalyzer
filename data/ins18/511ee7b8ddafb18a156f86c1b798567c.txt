Bribery case: Jail superintendent in Haryana VB net after surrendering in court

A vigilance bureau spokesperson said Kumar was the Rewari jail superintendent, who used to look after the additional work of Narnaul prison, when it came to the fore that he used to demand bribe from inmates
Published on May 31, 2022 03:58 AM IST
 

 Haryana vigilance bureau (VB) has arrested jail superintendent Anil Kumar after he surrendered in the court complex in Narnaul district, an official said on Monday. Kumar had been absconding ever since he was accused of taking bribe and was declared proclaimed offender last month.
