"I'm excited to be opening a new clinic in Baltimore and am looking forward to future expansion and growth in my Mount Clare location; and for our company as a whole. - Dr. Zach Woods, DC BALTIMORE (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Mid-Atlantic Spinal Rehab & Chiropractic, a leading Chiropractic and Physical Therapy practice, today announced a new office location in the Baltimore, Maryland area. The new location; 1261 - D. West Pratt Street Baltimore, MD 21223 located inside Mount Clare Junction, will add to the company's Baltimore area clinics.
