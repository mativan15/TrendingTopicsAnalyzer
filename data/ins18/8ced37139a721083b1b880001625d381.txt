Former COO Of Balaji Telefilms Goes Missing In Kenya; Ekta Kapoor Urges Govt To Help
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 17:45:27

Former Balaji Telefilms COO Zulfiqar Khan and another Indian national, Mohamed Zaid Sami Kidwai, went missing in mid-July after leaving a famous club in Westlands at 11.53 pm. Several hours later, two Indian nationals and their driver Nicodemus Mwania, were reported missing. The trio has since been missing for over 70 days.  On Friday, producer Ekta Kapoor appealed to the Kenya Red Cross and the Indian government for support in locating Zulfikar Ahmad Khan, the former Chief Operating Officer (COO) of her company, Balaji Telefilms.
