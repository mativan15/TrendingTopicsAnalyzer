How boring stocks made D-Street investors richer since last Diwali
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 05:45:00

NEW DELHI: If you go by the sectoral performance on Dalal Street since last Diwali , then boring seems to be the new cool for investors. While many retail investors were obsessed with new-age and other tech stocks, smarter investors made money in boring sectors such as power, utilities and industrials.Since the last Diwali celebrations held on November 4, 2021, BSE Power has been the top sectoral performer with a 33.7% rally, followed by BSE Utilities at 24%, BSE Industrials at 14.92% and BSE Capital Goods at 12.
