ALPHARETTA, Ga. – Suburban voters in swing states around the country helped propel President Joe Biden to victory in 2020. That included Georgia, where voters also sent two Democrats to the U.S. Senate for the first time in years. Two years later, without former President Trump on the ballot — and without a Democratic backlash against him — it's an open question whether the suburbs will deliver enough votes to help Democrats win again.
