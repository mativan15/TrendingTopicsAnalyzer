Following are the UN Secretary-General António Guterres' remarks on the partnerships between India and the United Nations on India's seventy-fifth anniversary, at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, in Mumbai, today: I am delighted to be here with all of you.  This institution is 54 years old, and as it was said, 54 years ago, I was a student at a university in Lisbon, and my school was called — I will say in Portuguese, but you will understand — Instituto Superior Técnico.  And my dream at the time was to be a researcher in physics.
