Disney Dreamlight Valley has a lot of ingredients to find while foraging, digging, and more, and most of them are relatively easy to round up. However, in the case of Rich Soil, the game does very little to explain how to find this rare crafting material. In this guide, we'll share with you where you can score some--but expect to put in some work for it.How to get Rich SoilUnlike normal soil, which can be found by simply digging in most biomes, Rich Soil requires quite a bit more work and patience.
