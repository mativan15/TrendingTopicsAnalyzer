Bull return to D-St as Sensex rallies 632 pts; Nifty settles above 16,350 level
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 11:15:00

AgenciesBased on market capitalization of BSE-listed stocks, the last two sessions lifted investor wealth by Rs 4.81 lakh crore.Cheer returned to D-Street on Friday as bulls managed to run to this week's finish line on a positive note, trimming losses after a volatile week. The Sensex jumped 632.13 points or 1.17 per cent in a comeback rally to end the session at 54,884.66. Its broader peer, Nifty50, added 182.30 points or 1.13 per cent to close above the 16,350 mark, amid strength across global markets.
