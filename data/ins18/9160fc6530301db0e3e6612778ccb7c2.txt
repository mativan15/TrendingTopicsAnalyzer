By Kathryn Hardison CF Industries Holdings Inc. has appointed Erik Mayer as its vice president of clean ammonia growth as part of the company's clean energy initiatives. In the newly created role, Mr. Mayer will lead a team focused on the evaluation and development of the company's blue ammonia capacity expansion, the company said.
