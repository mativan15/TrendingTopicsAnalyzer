Jamie Fiore Higgins started her first job out of college honored to be one of the elite: an analyst at one of Wall Street's most respected firms, Goldman Sachs. Raised in a working-class family, she was in awe of the lavish dinners and luxurious lifestyle. But that awe soon turned to insecurity as she first witnessed, then became a part of, the toxic, abusive culture. The stories read like almost comical examples of egregious behavior from sensitivity training on an episode of The Office. Her boss once handed out bananas as he told his subordinates they were all monkeys he could and would easily replace.
