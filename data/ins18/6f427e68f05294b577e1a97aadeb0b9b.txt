After losing fourth place to Spurs in the final weeks of the Premier League season, the Gunners are preparing a big summer in the market.One player both clubs have been linked with is Manchester City forward Gabriel Jesus, who is out of contract at Eastlands in a year's time and could be available for a transfer.For Arsenal legend Merson, signing the Brazilian would represent a marked change in Arteta's strategy and present the club with a better chance of fighting Spurs.'Elite managers just have that little bit more know-how,' he wrote for the Daily Star.
