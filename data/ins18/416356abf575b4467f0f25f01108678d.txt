ATLANTA, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- MJV Innovation, a global consultancy on business transformation and digital has released its annual Digital Trends Report for 2023. The highly-anticipated research report examines the leading trends and ideas that will impact digital strategy in 2023 and beyond for companies.

MJV's report is trusted by Fortune 500 companies around the globe who harness the firm's services, including design thinking, business transformation and data design.

The 2023 report focuses on six segments of digital: discovery and research, software development, agile, CX management, prototyping and go-to-market, and big data literacy.
