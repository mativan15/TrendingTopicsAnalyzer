With Aussie companies reeling from major hacks that put hundreds of customers' personal information at risk, one expert has revealed the one thing they should be doing to safeguard their data.The head-scratching breach at Optus – where hackers claimed to have stolen the data of 10 million current and former customers before releasing the information of 10,000 Australians, then bizarrely backing down and apologising – kicked off an industry-wide panic as people questioned whether their details were really secure.
