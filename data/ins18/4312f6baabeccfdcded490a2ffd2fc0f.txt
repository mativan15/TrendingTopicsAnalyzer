Published: 19:11 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 19:11 EDT, 18 October 2022 Simon Cowell has revealed he is launching a new music project in the form of a Tik Tok-style app after putting the X Factor on pause.The music mogul, 63, has aided the careers of megastars One Direction, Jennifer Hudson and Kelly Clarkson and now hopes to launch the next generation.Simon has hopped on the Tik Tok trend and is taking his search for talent to a new app called StemDrop, which he's launching with producer Max Martin.
