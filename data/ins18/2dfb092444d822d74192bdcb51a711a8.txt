Mainstreet Equity Gets OK to Buy Back 10% of Public Float
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-30 11:15:00

By Adriano Marchese Canadian real-estate operating company Mainstreet Equity Corp. said Monday that it has received authorization to purchase up to 10% of its public float over the course of a one-year period. Mainstreet Equity said that the Toronto Stock Exchange has accepted its notice of intention to make a normal course issuer bid to buy back up to 477,109 shares. At Friday's closing price of 113.11 Canadian dollars, the equivalent of US$88.91, the value of the shares would be worth around C$54 million.
