 Below Deck star Captain Lee Rosbach has revealed that his fellow colleagues suggested he would ‘never work again' after taking part on the hugely successful series. The Bravo program has become one of the most popular reality TV shows on our screens, spawning three successful spin-offs with another one on the way. Since Captain Lee set sail in 2013, fans have tuned in to follow the antics of the crew on board as they balanced dealing with demanding charter guests and living in such close quarters with their castmates.
