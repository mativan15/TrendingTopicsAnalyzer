October 19, 2022 04:39 PM Eastern Daylight Time REDWOOD CITY, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--i2c Inc., a leading provider of digital payment and banking technology, today announced partnerships with Connect Financial, Miq, Plastk, and Viva Equity. These collaborations, according to the company, exemplify the power of modern issuing-processing technology in democratizing payment access and innovation across the digital financial ecosystem. Use cases enable crypto-backed wallets, cooperative pool funds, card access to underbanked segments, and various embedded financial services.
