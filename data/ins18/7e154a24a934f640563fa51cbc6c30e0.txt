Naga national flag is not negotiable: Nationalist Socialist Council of Nagalim (Isak-Muivah)
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 04:30:00

Nationalist Socialist Council of Nagalim (Isak-Muivah) said that Naga national flag is not negotiable. In its mouthpiece Nagalim voice, NSCN-IM stated, 'It is unthinkable for NSCN to accept the Naga national flag as a cultural flag as hinted by the Government of India. Naga National Flag that symbolizes Naga Political identity is not negotiable. "

The editorial of May issue of the 'Nagalim Voice' referring to the Framework Agreement signed in August 3, 2015, said that today, NSCN is watching how the Prime Minister Narendra Modi is going to handle Framework Agreement with NSCN and Naga people that he himself took so much pride and credit for that.
