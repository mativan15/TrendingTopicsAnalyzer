The next phase of the pandemic for retailers may be messy, first-quarter earnings reports and industry analysts indicate. In the past two weeks, retailers have shown that the anticipated, but hard to time, cooling in consumer demand for goods has arrived. And many retailers reported holding more inventory than they'd like — and the goods they have might not be the ones they need now.Simeon Gutman, an analyst at Morgan Stanley, called it a "perfect storm of overbuying, weather, inflation, and spending shifts.
