NEW DELHI: US-based telco software provider Mavenir is in discussions with Indian telecom operators, including cash-strapped Vodafone Idea, for securing 5G equipment deals. The company expects to commercially supply its made-in-India 4G and 5G Open Radio Access Network (Open RAN) radios in less than a quarter, according to a top executive of the company. 'These engagements are long drawn and the gestation period is usually around 9-12 months.
