iStockA bottom-up approach may be looked upon to reduce and monitor the cost in India.India is showing progress in every dimension of economic reforms. Its commitment to do so is evident from the scale undertaken at the pan-India level since 2014. Policies and schemes developed with the aim of Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) are underpinning India's 'development with all, and for all,' motto. Various reforms spanning across the business lifecycle have been implemented by Ministries and States. However, most of them focused on simplification of processes, or rationalisation, digitisation and elimination of compliances.
