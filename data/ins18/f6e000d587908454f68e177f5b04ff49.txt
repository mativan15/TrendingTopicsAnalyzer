UND to host pregame gathering before rivalry series with Minnesota
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-19 00:34:00

Oct. 18—MINNEAPOLIS — The UND Alumni Association and Foundation will host pregame parties for UND fans in Minneapolis before both of this weekend's rivalry games against the University of Minnesota.

The events will be held at Sally's Saloon, 700 Washington Ave., at 5 p.m. Friday and Saturday.

Admission is free and full drink and food menus will be available.

The games are set to begin at 7 p.m. in 3M Arena at Mariucci. Sally's is less than a half mile from the arena.

For questions, email events@UNDalumni.
