Sam Asghari, 28, gave an interview to GQ Magazine's Marisa Meltzer, where he opened up about his marriage proposal to Britney Spears, their recent miscarriage announcement and looking to the future.It is notable that this is the first time Asghari is opening up about his relationship with Spears, 40, to a major publication. Last November, he declined to even vaguely reference his mega-famous fiancé as a condition of being profiled by The New York Times.• On life with Spears: "I'm not a house husband," he says and laughs.
