DOUGLAS, Isle of Man, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The ‘financial heart attack' the UK is experiencing should be understood as a ‘moment of revelation,' with long-hidden costs of our energy systems being revealed, declared Chris Caldwell, CEO of energy generator United Renewables, in an article published today. ‘This is the hard truth: these costs – which seem to have appeared from nowhere - have been around for a long time,' he writes in regard to spiralling inflation, energy costs, and interest rates, ‘they just weren't priced in.
