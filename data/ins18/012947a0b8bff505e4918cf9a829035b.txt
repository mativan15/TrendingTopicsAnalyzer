What comes to your mind when speaking about offshoring? Have you ever made a decision about cooperation with an offshoring partner? What pitfalls does this process have? How can you get around them? All of this will be discussed in this column.When it comes to offshoring, there are usually several main benefits: cost-saving and speed of hiring. In fact, there are many more of them, such as transparency, 100 percent control over the team, ease of scaling up/down, and hiring tech talents while there's a lack of high-skilled specialists at the local market.
