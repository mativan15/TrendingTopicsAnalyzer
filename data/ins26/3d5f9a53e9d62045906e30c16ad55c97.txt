Jair Bolsonaro and Lula da Silva trade 'liar' insults in heated Brazilian election debate
sky.com
news
2022-10-17 10:23:00

Brazil's incumbent president Jair Bolsonaro and election challenger Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva have called each other a liar during a heated television debate.The term was used more than a dozen times by each of the candidates during the 90-minute TV Band encounter and comes before the presidential election's run-off on 30 October.
"You are a liar. You lie every day," Mr da Silva said during one exchange.Mr Bolsonaro said: "You can't come here to tell people these lies."Mr da Silva, who was previously president between 2003 and 2010 and who is mostly known as Lula, said the country needs a leader to "take care" of them.
