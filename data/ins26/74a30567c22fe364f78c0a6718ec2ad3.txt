Published: 04:03 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 04:06 EDT, 3 June 2022 British UFC heavyweight Tom Aspinall has fired back at former fighter Chael Sonnen for criticising his promotion skills as he rises through the ranks.Sonnen, who was famously outspoken throughout his iconic career in the sport, with some arguing that he talked his way into world title fights that he hadn't earned with his performances. The American has criticised Aspinall for not doing a better job of promoting himself in interviews on a recent interview, to which Aspinall has denied any talks that he needs to change his ways.
