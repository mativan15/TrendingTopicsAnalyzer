Julian Robertson's Family to Sell New Zealand Winery Business
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-19 03:20:42

Julian Robertson's family has agreed to sell its New Zealand winery business following the death of the renowned hedge fund manager.

Dry River Wines, located in Martinborough near the southern tip of New Zealand's North Island, will be sold to Wellington-based businessman Charlie Zheng, the Robertson family said in a statement. The purchase price was not revealed for the transaction, which is due to complete in the middle of next month.
