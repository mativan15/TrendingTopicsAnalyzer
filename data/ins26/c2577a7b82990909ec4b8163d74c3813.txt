Getting country house staff these days may be hard but keeping them is even harder. While we do not know the exact reason why Charlotte Vine, housekeeper of Chequers, left her job soon after Carrie Johnson became its new chatelaine, there are reports of a personality 'clash' (which a spokesperson for Johnson has denied). 
So how do you keep staff happy? What are the modern rules at a time when the old social conventions that governed households and their staff no longer exist? Let me dare to explain…   If you're lucky enough to stay in a house with staff, no chances can be taken when it comes to tipping.
