Apollo Hospital shares gain 1.26% as Sensex rises
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 08:45:00

Shares of the Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Ltd. traded at Rs 4344.8 on BSE at 01:34PM (IST) on Tuesday, up 1.26 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 3365.9 and a high of Rs 5930.7. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 70.62 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 61.44 per share and 11.55 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 4,385 shares changed hands on the counter till 01:34PM (IST). The stock commands a market value of Rs 62392.
