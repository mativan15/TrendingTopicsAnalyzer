NEWARK, Del: , Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- As per a recent market analysis by Future Market Insights (FMI), the global protective clothing market stands at US$ 10.92 Bn as of now and is expected to be worth US$ 17.6 Bn by the year 2030 at a CAGR of 6.% from 2022 to 2030. Workplace safety is of utmost importance in the manufacturing, food processing, construction, healthcare, and chemical verticals. Accidental injuries are amongst the leading causes of concern. Protective clothing, as the name suggests, is basically designed for protecting workers from numerous occupational fatalities like cut hazards, flame & fire, wear, dry particles, chemicals, and likewise.
