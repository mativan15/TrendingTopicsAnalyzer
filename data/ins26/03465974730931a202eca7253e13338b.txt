Published: 15:29 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 15:30 EDT, 20 October 2022 LONDON (AP) - Arsenal secured a spot in the Europa League knockout round after Granit Xhaka´s second-half goal was enough to earn a 1-0 win over PSV Eindhoven on Thursday that maintained the Gunners´ perfect record in the competition. Xhaka's well-struck volley in the 71st minute was the breakthrough Arsenal sought after failing to convert a number of chances thanks to a combination of wasteful finishing and good saves from goalkeeper Walter Benitez.
