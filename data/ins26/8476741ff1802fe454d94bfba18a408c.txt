'We're not taking a vacation right now': Ben Horowitz says Andreessen Horowitz has not slowed down its investing even as most growth VCs slam on the brakes
businessinsider.com
news
2022-06-03 13:23:30

The severe pullback in public markets has finally caught up to Silicon Valley, where most dealmaking has ground almost to a halt, especially at the later stages, multiple venture investors have told Insider in the past week.But the famed venture investor Ben Horowitz, a cofounder of Andreessen Horowitz, said investments in startups at A16z, as his firm is commonly known, had not slowed down."We're aware of the pricing changes and how things are going, but we're not taking a vacation right now," Horowitz told Insider.
