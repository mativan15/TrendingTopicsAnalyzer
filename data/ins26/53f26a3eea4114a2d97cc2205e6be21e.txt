Odisha Police nabs MD of Kolkata-based firm in chit fund case
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 13:30:00

BHUBANESWAR: The managing director of a Kolkata-based firm was arrested by the Odisha Police for allegedly duping investors in a ponzi scheme, a statement said on Tuesday.Jaideep Raha was nabbed from Kolkata on Monday on a complaint by Subash Chandra Pradhan, a resident of Puri district, it said.Chit fund companies Ambitious Diversified Projects Management and OAK India Multistate Credit Co-operative Society had illegally collected money by assuring investors of a high rate of interest, according to the complaint.
