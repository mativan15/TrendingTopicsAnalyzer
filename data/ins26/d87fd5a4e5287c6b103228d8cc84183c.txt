CHAMPAIGN — With 21 seconds to play and Illinois holding a 12-point lead over Minnesota, safety Sydney Brown came down with his first interception of the season. It was a sunny but chilly fall Saturday, and the 45,683 fans who bundled up to celebrate homecoming let out a roar loud enough to shake the college town.

Brown's heroics in the 26-14 victory weren't the only ones from his family on a day that he and his twin brother, Illini running back Chase Brown, were homecoming kings.
