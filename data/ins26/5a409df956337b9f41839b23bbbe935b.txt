Pakistan-based terrorist Rinda mobilising destitute youths in Punjab, reveals questioning of his associate
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 20:48:43

Pakistan-based gangster-turned-terrorist Harvinder Singh Rinda, in collusion with his henchman Canada-based gangster Lakhbir Singh alias Landa, has been mobilising destitute youths with no criminal background, to unleash terror activities in Punjab, questioning of three of his associates has revealed. The trio -- Balraj Singh of Bhikhiwind, Atish Kumar and Avinash Kumar of Sarhali village in Tarn Taran -- were nabbed with an AK-47 rifle, three pistols and 55 rounds of ammunition during a joint operation of Delhi Police, Punjab anti-gangster task force (AGTF) and the Amritsar police commissionerate on Friday.
