Why surging bills are bringing extra worry for people living with a health condition or disability

Surging living costs are a worry for many. But for people living with a health condition or disability, it can be an added stress on top of the extra expenses they are already having to pay in order to get by day-to-day.When budgets are finely balanced or stretched, extra financial strain may mean missing appointments, or living with pain rather than switching the heating on to help ease it.Having been diagnosed with a brain tumour in 2016, Julian Fiano says the situation with surging bills is adding to his stress and forcing him to make tough choices.
