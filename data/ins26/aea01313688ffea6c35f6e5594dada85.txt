[SK하이닉스] U.S. grants waiver to SK hynix for tech transfers to China 미국, SK하이닉스에 반도체 장비 중국 반입 금지 유예 Korea JoongAng Daily 1면 기사 Thursday, October 13, 2022 SK hynix has received a one-year waiver on U.S. restrictions related to the importing of certain semiconductor-related technologies into China. waiver: 면제, 포기 restriction: 제한 SK하이닉스는 특정 반도체 관련 기술을 중국으로 반입하는 것을 제한하는 수출통제 조치를 1년 유예받았다. With the permission in place, the world's secondlargest memory chipmaker will be able to transfer advanced equipment essential for the operations of two dynamic random-access memory (DRAM) factories in Wuxi.
