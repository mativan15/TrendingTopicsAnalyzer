These were the most expensive Milwaukee-area houses sold in September
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 17:57:00

While the overall Milwaukee-area housing market continued a relative slowdown last month amid interest rate increases, the local market for luxury residential properties remained strong in September.

The sale of the most expensive Milwaukee-area house sold so far in 2022 closed in September, as did sales of several other local, multimillion-dollar residential properties.

"The name of the game is premium lake property," Bruce Gallagher of Gallagher Lake Country Real Estate with Keller Williams recently told the Milwaukee Business Journal during a conversation about the local luxury market.
