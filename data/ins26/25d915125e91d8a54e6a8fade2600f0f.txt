It is our human nature to tinker with rules, as anyone who has argued over dubious interpretations of the game Monopoly will attest. Rule-breaking may hold the key to giving us an edge over machines that, for all their emerging capabilities, are tied to fixed directives. A close look at how mathematics comes to be demonstrates the power of departing from accepted convention, mathematician and author Junaid Mubeen writes in Mathematical Intelligence: A Story of Human Superiority Over Machines.How Humans have long been dreaming up concepts and creatures that exist outside our physical reality.
