4 signs that the Greater Philadelphia housing market is finally cooling
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 23:40:00

Real estate experts, along with homebuyers and sellers alike, have been anticipating a housing market decline following a historically hot stretch spurred by the Covid-19 pandemic.

With mortgage rates spiking to the highest they've been in a decade, the market is in the midst of change.

Parts of the Philadelphia housing market, like the Main Line, have stayed hot in recent months, and the region recently led the nation in bidding wars. Still, talk of a downturn continues to loom with many markets across the country already experiencing it.
