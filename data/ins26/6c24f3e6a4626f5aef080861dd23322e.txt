Joint Development Effort to Benefit SSD Manufacturers by Targeting Improvements in Real-World Robustness

LITTLETON, Mass., October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SANBlaze Technology Inc., a leading worldwide provider of advanced storage test and validation technologies, today announced that it is working closely with the University of New Hampshire InterOperability Lab (UNH-IOL) to produce OCP (Open Compute Project) test scripts, leveraging the combined experience of both teams in testing NVMe and advanced storage solutions.
