Saudi money has no lure for McIlroy as he tees it up at this week's Memorial
irishtimes.com
sport
2022-06-01 00:00:00

No temptation for Rory McIlroy to follow the lure of Saudi money. A number of players have had their heads turned by the wheelbarrows of green bucks on offer on the LIV start-up invitational tour – among them his good friend Graeme McDowell – but McIlroy, for one, is not lured by the extravagant prize funds.And, following on from failing to close the deal when in contention at the recent US PGA Championship, the Northern Irishman returns to tournament duty at this week's Memorial at Muirfield Village for a busy four-week stretch that has the US Open as its focal point.
