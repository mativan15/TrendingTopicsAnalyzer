Image source, Social mediaImage caption, Asra Panahi's death sparked protests in the north-western city of Ardabil on SaturdayA 15-year-old girl died in north-west Iran last week after she was beaten by security forces during a raid on her school, a teachers' union has alleged.Asra Panahi was one of several students injured in the incident in Ardabil, a statement posted by the Co-ordinating Council of Teachers Syndicates said.They were attacked by security forces when they refused to sing an anthem praising the supreme leader, it added.
