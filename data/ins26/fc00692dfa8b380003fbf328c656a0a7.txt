Former football powerbrokers face retrial calls
RT
news
2022-10-20 12:38:56

Swiss federal prosecutors have confirmed that they have filed an appeal to see the acquittals of ex-FIFA president Sepp Blatter and former UEFA president Michel Platini overturned.Switzerland's Federal Criminal Court cleared the pair of corruption charges in July following a seven-year investigation that froze them out of football.Prosecutors said at the time that they would examine the written verdict ahead of deciding whether to appeal against the two men, who once enjoyed major power in the sport.
