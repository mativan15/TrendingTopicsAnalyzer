Image caption, The exhibition has been curated by four local artistsAn art exhibition to give a "fresh and controversial perspective" on the Queen's Platinum Jubilee has opened.Off With Their Heads, includes paintings and installations asking questions about feminism and the monarchy.Works are based on local landscapes, children's stories, and personal experiences throughout the Queen's 70 years on the throne. Four local artists have curated the show, which is based in Nailsworth.Image caption, Artist Charlotte Miles shows the Queen with a "modern" twistThe exhibition said it "aims to honour the Queen's reign from a fresh and controversial perspective".
