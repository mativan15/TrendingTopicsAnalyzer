Vizag Airport Emerges As Nerve Centre Of Ap Politics
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 07:15:00

VISAKHAPATNAM: Visakhapatnam airport has been in the news for all the wrong reasons with regard to political developments of Andhra Pradesh for the last several years. The airport, which is located some 10 kilometres away from the core city, witnessed several key moments in the last five years, which might have partly shaped or reshaped the political arena of the state.When he was in the Opposition, chief minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy staged a protest on the tarmac itself when the city police prevented him from coming out of the airport in January 2017.
