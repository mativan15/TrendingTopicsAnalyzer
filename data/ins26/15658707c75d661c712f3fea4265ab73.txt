October 19, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Morgan Stanley at Work today released the latest findings from its second annual State of the Workplace Financial Benefits Study focused on the financial benefits, perceptions, and role of equity compensation among employers and employees in the workplace.




The perceived importance of equity compensation increased from 2021: 95% of HR leaders (up from 92% in 2021) and 80% of employees (up from 75% in 2021) agree that equity compensation and stock ownership is the most effective way to motivate employees and keep them engaged.
