Jubilant FoodWorks net profit up 11.3% in Q4, names new CEO
indiatimes.com
food
2022-05-30 19:30:00

New Delhi: Food services company (JFL), operator of Domino's Pizza and Dunkin' Donuts, reported an 11.3% growth in standalone net profit to ₹116.1 crore for the fourth quarter ended March 2022, compared to ₹104.3 crore in the corresponding year-ago quarter. It said revenue from operations grew 12.9% to ₹1,157.9 crore. The company also named Sameer Khetarpal as chief executive on Monday. Khetarpal, who moves from Amazon, succeeds Pratik Pota who stepped down in March.
 He will take charge as chief effective September 5, 2022.
