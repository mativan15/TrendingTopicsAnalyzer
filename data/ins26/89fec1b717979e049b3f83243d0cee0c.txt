One AI, Language AI startup, will enable seamless integration into developer workflows with use-case ready API
SACRAMENTO, Calif., May 30, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- One AI, an NLP-as-a-service startup, empowering developers to embed Language AI in their products, officially launched today. One AI has raised $8M in seed funding from leading angel investors and VCs, including Ariel Maislos, Tech Aviv, Tomer Wiengarten, CEO of Sentinel One.
One AI allows developers to seamlessly embed language comprehension into their projects, transforming texts from any source into structured data, with no training data or machine learning knowledge required.
