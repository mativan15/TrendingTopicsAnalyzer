Hundreds everywhere as former Test bats star in the Sheffield Shield

news
2022-10-19 07:03:00

A powerhouse display from Victorian captain Peter Handscomb outdid teammate Marcus Harris who extend a seven-summer streak of centuries as the pair went big in a Sheffield Shield run-fest at the Junction Oval. Harris has never gone through a Sheffield Shield season for Victoria without scoring a century and that streak extended in to a seventh summer on a day which featured dual-hundred makers.The prolific left-handed opener was outdone by his skipper Handscomb who scored a second-straight Shield ton and then went on with it scoring 174 not out to provide national selectors with more evidence of how deep Australia's Test-quality batting depth runs.
