Human Rights Expert: Why Everyone Should Care About Coercive Psychiatry
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-02 15:30:24

The COVID-19 pandemic brought more awareness to mental health and less stigma associated with those who seek treatment. As the world attempted to navigate one mental health crisis, another has been happening under our noses for years. In 2021, the World Health Organization released a 296-page report which called for countries to prohibit "coercive practices such as forced admission and treatment, seclusion and restraint, as well as the administering of antipsychotic medication, electroconvulsive therapy (ECT) and psychosurgery without informed consent.
