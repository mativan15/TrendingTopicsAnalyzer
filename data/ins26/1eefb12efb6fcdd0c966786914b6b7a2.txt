Published: 20:13 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 20:13 EDT, 5 June 2022 Minjee Lee of Australia shot an even-par 71 on Sunday and cruised to a four-shot victory while winning the U.S. Women's Open at Pine Needles Lodge & Golf Club in Southern Pines, N.C. Lee finished at 13-under 271 while comfortably outdistancing Mina Harigae and winning her second career major title. Her total set a U.S. Women's Open record, bettering the mark of 272 shared by Annika Sorenstam (1996 at Pine Needles), Juli Inkster (1999 at Old Waverly) and In Gee Chun (2015 at Lancaster).
