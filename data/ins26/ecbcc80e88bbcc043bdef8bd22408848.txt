ITASCA, Ill.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Teammates from across AIT Worldwide Logistics' North American region came together to raise money for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital® this year. Including the company's promised match, AIT successfully exceeded its $60,000 goal for a total donation of $68,195.

'The fight to end cancer is a cause that's close to my heart, personally, and to the hearts of so many of us here at AIT,' said AIT Worldwide Logistics Executive Chairman and CEO, Vaughn Moore.
