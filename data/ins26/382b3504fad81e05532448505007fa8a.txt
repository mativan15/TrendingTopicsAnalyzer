Agra: Vegetable seller stabbed to death for objecting to man drinking near cart
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 02:15:00

AGRA: A 32-year-old vegetable seller was stabbed to death with a knife after he objected to a man's drinking alcohol near his cart, police said on Monday. The incident occurred late in the evening on Sunday at Char Sayeed locality under the Itmad-ud-daulah police station area of Agra district, said officials.Manoj Diwakar, 28, along with four other men took a sharp object from the cart and stabbed the vendor, identified as Ravi Kumar, in his chest at around 10 am, said family members of the victim.
