LONDON, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Internet of Things (IoT) is one of the most fascinating advancements in the technology sector as it creates new headways for innovation, collaboration, and growth. From everyday gadgets to space tech, IoT holds immense value for virtually all aspects of modern life. However, new developments in the cyberattack landscape, coupled with the rising volumes of data exchange between devices, the chances of exploits and attacks in IoT-enabled systems has dramatically surged over the past few years.
