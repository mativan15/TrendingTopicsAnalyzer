A 42-year-old firefighter was placed under suspension after seeking money from a shop owner for the Deepavali festival on Monday.V. Thirumugam (42) is a firefighter attached to Namakkal Fire and Rescue Service. Recently, he approached a shop at Rasipuram seeking money for the Deepavali festival, and the shop workers asked him to call their owner.But he called to a wrong number, and the person who spoke on the other side, V. Nalvinai Selvan, blamed the firefighter for seeking money.
