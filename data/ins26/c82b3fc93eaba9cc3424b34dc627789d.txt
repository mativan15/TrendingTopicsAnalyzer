Published: 10:09 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 10:09 EDT, 17 October 2022 Despite an intensifying rivalry with the West and a slowing economy, Chinese President Xi Jinping has doubled down on his policies Chinese President Xi Jinping doubled down on his policies at the opening of the Communist Party Congress, pledging continuity on cornerstones of his decade in power as he seeks to secure a historic third term, analysts say. Since coming to office, Xi has transformed China by personalising and centralising power, cracking down on corruption and dissent, pushing a more assertive foreign policy and promoting domestic self-reliance in technology and the economy.
