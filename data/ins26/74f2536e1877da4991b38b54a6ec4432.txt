Retailers face £3bn bill for business rates rise
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 11:00:00

Business rates bills could rise by as much as £3 billion next April, experts have warned, piling additional costs on companies struggling in the face of rising prices.The commercial property tax is linked to September inflation data, which was today shown to have hit 10.1 per cent. The last time rates rose by a similar figure was 1991.'Uniform' business rates — part of a 'multiplier' used to calculate companies' rates bills — were frozen by then chancellor Rishi Sunak at the last two autumn budgets.
