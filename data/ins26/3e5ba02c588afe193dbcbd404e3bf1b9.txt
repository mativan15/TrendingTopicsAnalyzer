Collider.com She may be used to being the leader, but a new clip released ahead of the premiere of Enola Holmes 2 reveals that the titular character will need to put her trust in others if she wants to solve a case. In the teaser, Enola (Millie Bobby Brown) is keeping an eye out for the suspect in a murder plot when she realizes she'll need to roll up her sleeves and get her hands dirty in the worst way imaginable - ballroom dancing. A tomboy at heart, the young sleuth has no experience when it comes to cutting it up on the dancefloor, but when she spots her friend and crush Tewkesbury (Louis Partridge), she sees the opportunity to knock out two birds with one stone.
