As Cantonese dwindles, teachers preserve it for future generations
csmonitor.com
economics
2022-10-21 13:30:06

Three decades ago, finding opportunities to learn Cantonese in San Francisco wasn't hard. But today in the city that's drawn Cantonese speakers from South China for over 150 years, there's fear that political and social upheaval are diminishing a language that is a cultural touchstone.The Chinese government's push for wider use of Mandarin – already the national language, spoken by 1 billion people – along with the country's changing migration patterns have contributed to an undeniable shift away from Cantonese.
