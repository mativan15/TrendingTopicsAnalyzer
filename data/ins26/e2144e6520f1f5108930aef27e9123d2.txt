By Reuters Published: 11:10 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 11:10 EDT, 16 October 2022 Milwaukee Bucks guard/forward Pat Connaughton will be sidelined for approximately three weeks with a right calf strain. He missed the last two preseason games with soreness. An MRI on Friday revealed the extent of the injury. The Bucks tip off the regular season Thursday against the host Philadelphia 76ers.
