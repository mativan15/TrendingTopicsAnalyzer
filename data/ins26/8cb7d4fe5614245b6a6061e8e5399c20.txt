 Canada's main stock index was down nearly 100 points in late-morning trading as gains in the energy sector were more than offset by losses led by the financial and technology sectors.
The S&P/TSX composite index down 89.79 points at 18,708.39.In New York, the Dow Jones industrial average was up 65.58 points at 30,589.
