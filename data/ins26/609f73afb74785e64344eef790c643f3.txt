Be Ready to Pounce on Homebuilding Stocks the Next Time They Drop, Jim Cramer Says

CNBC's Jim Cramer said Thursday that despite conventional wisdom, he believes investors should pick up shares of homebuilding stocks the next time they plummet. "I believe these stocks will drop again, but when they do ... I think you've got to pounce," the "Mad Money" host said.
