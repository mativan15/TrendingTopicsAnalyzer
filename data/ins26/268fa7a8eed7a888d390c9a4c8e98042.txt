(NASDAQ:REYN), we can see that shares, for the most part, have traded in a range (with the exception of this year's June lows) between approximately $26 a share and $31 a share. Dividend growth stocks that trade within a narrow range attract income-oriented investors because of the potential of compounding one's gains at a much faster clip. Furthermore, rangebound stocks also offer the income-orientated investor the opportunity to sell covered calls against their long stock positions in order to earn more income on top of the dividend.
