LAFAYETTE, La., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Home Bancorp, Inc. (Nasdaq: "HBCP") (the "Company"), the parent company for Home Bank, N.A. (the "Bank") (www.home24bank.com), reported financial results for the third quarter of 2022. For the quarter, the Company reported net income of $10.4 million, or $1.28 per diluted common share ("diluted EPS"), up $2.0 million from $8.5 million, or $1.03 diluted EPS, for the second quarter of 2022. The third and second quarters of 2022 include merger expenses, net of taxes totaling $41,000 and $1.
