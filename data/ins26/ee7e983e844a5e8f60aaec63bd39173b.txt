Graeme McDowell won the US Open in 2010 and has represented Europe at four Ryder CupsGraeme McDowell says it "wouldn't be healthy" if players were banned from golf for competing in next week's $25m Saudi-funded LIV Golf Invitational.The PGA Tour and DP World Tour rejected requests for waivers from players who want to play and has threatened to ban those that flout the rules."I don't agree with it, I hope they make the right decisions," the Northern Irishman told BBC Radio Newcastle."If the big tours in the world start banning players its just not healthy for the sport.
