Indiewire Television For better or worse, 'The Handmaid's Tale' just dropped one of its most scream-worthy episodes ever.Season 5's 'No Man's Land,' written by Rachel Shukert and directed by Natalia Leite, focuses on one location and its characters: June (Elisabeth Moss) and Serena (Yvonna Strahovski), who just went into labor somewhere between Gilead and Canada. There's a lot to scream about in this episode — especially if you're Serena — from the tense birthing sequence to the shifting dynamics between these two women and what it all means.
