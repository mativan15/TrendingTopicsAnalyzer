The St. Martin Parish Sheriff's Office is investigating the death of a 37-year-old Houston man who was found dead with a gunshot wound in the back seat of a car at the 1100 block of Evangeline Thruway.

The sheriff's office said that deputies found Brady Paul Mayeaux early Saturday morning after receiving a 911 call reporting a male passenger in a car with a gunshot wound.
