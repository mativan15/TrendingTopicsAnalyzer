Published: 06:07 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 06:12 EDT, 1 June 2022 New Love Island contestant Indiyah Polack has a failed hairdressing business that was given the chop little more than two years after launching. The 23-year-old model, recently confirmed for the eighth series of the ITV2 show, lost the London based Junkie Colours business by failing to submit her first year's accounts.The dissolved company officially launched in 2018 and was listed as offering ‘hairdressing, custom coloured wigs and other beauty treatments.
