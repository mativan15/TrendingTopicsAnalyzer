 Cryptocurrency markets have had a rough time in 2022, so you may be questioning the safety and security of this bold new asset class.
Bitcoin (BTC) is down nearly 60% on the year. Meanwhile, because of the lack of legal framework, crypto crimes are on the rise. In the first quarter of this year alone, the Federal Trade Commission reported that $329 million in crypto was reported as lost to cryptocurrency fraud.
