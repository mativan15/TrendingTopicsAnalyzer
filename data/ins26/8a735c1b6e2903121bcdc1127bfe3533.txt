Dame Deborah James has been blown away by the fact her T-shirt has raised more than 500K towards the Bowel Babe fund.

As of Saturday, the Bowelbabe Fund for Cancer Research UK had reached more than six and a half million pounds in total.

The T-shirt, which has the phrase Rebellious Hope printed across the front, is part of her In The Style collection she has launched to raise money for charity.
