Monumental Sports names Friday Abernethy new GM of NBC Sports Washington
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 12:25:00

A month after Monumental Sports & Entertainment closed its acquisition of NBC Sports Washington from NBCUniversal, the D.C. sports conglomerate is getting to work on refashioning the regional sports network.

Monumental, the Ted Leonsis -led entity that owns the Washington Wizards, Capitals, Mystics and Capital City Go-Go, on Thursday announced the hiring of veteran media executive Friday Abernethy as the new general manager of the network. She fills the role previously held by Jackie Bradford, who remains president and general manager of NBC 4 and Telemundo Washington.
