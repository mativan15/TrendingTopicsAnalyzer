Omidyar Network backs D2C home building material firm Mistry.Store
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 09:00:00

Home interior building material platform Mistry.Store raised $2 million in seed funding, led by Omidyar Network India, an investment firm focused on social impact. The round also saw Waveform VC and Bharat Founders backing the startup along with angel investors like Oyo's Maninder Gulati, Cars24 founder Vikram Chopra and Swiggy CEO Rohit Kapoor among others. Founded by Vaibhav Poddar and Bhanu Mahajan in 2022, Mistry.Store works as an online store for building materials and home interiors, like wood, plumbing, hardware and paint for homeowners.
