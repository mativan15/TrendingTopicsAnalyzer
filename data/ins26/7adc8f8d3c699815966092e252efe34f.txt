REUTERS/Lee Jae-WonRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comJune 1 (Reuters) - Roper Technologies Inc (ROP.N) said on Wednesday it would sell a majority stake in its industrial business to private equity firm Clayton Dubilier & Rice LLC for $2.6 billion as it seeks to focus on its software business.The industrial solutions and software firm will keep a 49% stake in the business which will be spun off into a standalone company, it said in a statement."Selling a majority interest in these industrial businesses will provide Roper with significant upfront cash, while maintaining the ability to receive additional cash proceeds from the future exit of our minority interest," Chief Executive Officer Neil Hunn said.
