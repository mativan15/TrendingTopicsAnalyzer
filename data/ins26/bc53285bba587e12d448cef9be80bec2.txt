Closed-loop system for recycling batteries in China set up by BMW
chinadaily.com.cn
news
2022-05-30 00:00:00

By ZHANG DANDAN | China Daily | Updated: 2022-05-30 09:04 The booth of BMW in Chengdu auto show, on Aug 29, 2021. [Photo/IC] BMW said it will take the lead in realizing closed-loop recycling of China-made electric vehicles' power batteries after the German automaker announced its partnership with Huayou, a tech company specializing in battery recycling based in Zhejiang province, on Wednesday. According to the bilateral agreement, BMW and Huayou will cooperate in power battery recycling and reuse in an echelon network, which is expected to be conducive to ecological conservation and the improvement of resource utilization rates.
