JimVallee As Friday's price action makes clear, investors are desperate to believe that the Fed is going to cut rates again, allowing the era of cheap money to continue and bolstering the earnings of highly leveraged companies. Investors Hear What They Want To Hear - Not What Fed Speakers Say Each time any sentence of any Fed speaker can be interpreted as a sign that a pivot is coming bond yields drop and exuberant investors bid up the prices of stocks again, especially those of Tech stocks, which are more than others dependent on the availability of cheaply borrowed money and stocks with very slow earnings growth whose only appeal is the dividends they pay.
