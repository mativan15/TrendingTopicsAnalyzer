No. 8 Tennessee (5-0, 2-0 SEC) will host No. 1 Alabama (6-0, 3-0) Saturday at Neyland Stadium.

Saturday will mark the 20th time the Vols have played on Oct. 15.
Tennessee has a 9-8-2 record in games played on this date.

Tennessee first played on this date in 1892, recording a 25-0 victory against Maryville College.
