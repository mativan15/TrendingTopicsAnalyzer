Oct. 17—DICKINSON — Spooky times loom over the Western Edge. Several entities throughout the community are working hard to maximize Halloween fun. They include the West River Ice Center, Dickinson Area Chamber of Commerce and the Dickinson Public Library.

The West River Ice Center will host their Ice Scream Halloween Party from 7 to 9 p.m. on Saturday, Oct. 29. Admission is free but skate rental is $2, with limited sizes and availability.

Pizza will be available on a first come, first serve basis.
