HOUSTON, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Crown Castle Inc. (NYSE: CCI) ("Crown Castle") announced today that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $1.565 per common share. The quarterly dividend will be payable on December 30, 2022 to common stockholders of record at the close of business on December 15, 2022. Future dividends are subject to the approval of Crown Castle's Board of Directors.
