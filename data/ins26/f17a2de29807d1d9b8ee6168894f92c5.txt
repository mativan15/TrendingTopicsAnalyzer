Former player Anson Carter part owner of Atlanta ECHL team
espn.com
news
2022-10-18 20:10:00

4:10 PM ETFormer NHL forward Anson Carter has become a minority owner of the ECHL's Atlanta Gladiators, the team announced Tuesday.Carter, who played for eight NHL franchises over his career, is part of a two-person ownership group that also features Atlanta businessman Alex Campbell. Campbell, who will be the majority owner of the team, and Carter were unanimously approved by the ECHL board of governors, according to a release issued by the Gladiators."I am excited to be a part of professional hockey here in my adopted hometown," the Canadian-born Carter said in a statement.
