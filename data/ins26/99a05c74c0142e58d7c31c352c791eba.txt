Only back in 2018, people suggested the growth of remote working was grinding to a halt, with only 6 percent of the UK workforce operating remotely on a regular basis. What's more, remote working was only typically available to a select few, namely those working in the tech sector, where the parameters and expectations were clear and obvious.The rest of the UK workforce simply didn't have the opportunity to explore remote working, either because their employers either didn't see it as a feasible option, or thought it would limit staff productivity.
