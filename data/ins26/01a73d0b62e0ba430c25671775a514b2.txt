European Central Bank's President Christine Lagarde wants regulation of cryptocurrenciesChristine Lagarde, the President of the European Central Bank, stated that cryptocurrencies were 'based on nothing' and should be regulated to prevent people from risking their life savings on them. Lagarde expressed her concern about those unaware of the risks, and might everything. She also encouraged governments all around the world to take steps to protect inexperienced investors who make significant bets on digital assets.
