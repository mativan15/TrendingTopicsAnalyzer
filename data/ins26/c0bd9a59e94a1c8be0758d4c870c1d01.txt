Anoop, autorickshaw driver from Kerala, became an overnight sensation when he became the winner of a jackpot lottery draw on Onam. For the first time, the winner of the biggest prize can be seen making an entry to a Malayalam TV show.In the upcoming episode of the popular show, 'Bzinga Family Festival', Anoop , along with his family, can be seen as contestants. In the teaser that surfaced online, he shared the hardships he faced after the big win."I really wished I had only won a second or third prize," he says in the video.
