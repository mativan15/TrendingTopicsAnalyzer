In re-rebrand, Suffolk Construction returns to its roots

2022-05-27 14:14:37

On the cusp of its 40th anniversary as a company — and a little over two years after a rebranding didn't quite get the traction that its leadership was hoping for — Boston-based construction giant Suffolk is returning to its red, white and blue roots.Workers at Suffolk job sites this week began swapping out the contractor's pandemic-era light blue and purple branding with a revived version of its original namesake: Suffolk's name in bold all-caps white block letters spread over two color blocks of deep blue and red.
