Hormel Foods Lowers High End of 2022 Earnings Guidance
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 11:00:00

By Will Feuer Hormel Foods Corp. lowered the high end of its 2022 earnings guidance range while reiterating its sales forecast as the food producer navigates logistics challenges, avian influenza and Covid-19 restrictions in China. The Austin, Minn.-based company now expects full-year earnings of $1.87 a share to $1.97 a share. The company previously expected earnings of $1.87 a share to $2.03 a share. Chief Executive Jim Snee said robust demand across Hormel Foods' retail, foodservice and international channels as well as pricing action will help the company hit its sales guidance.
