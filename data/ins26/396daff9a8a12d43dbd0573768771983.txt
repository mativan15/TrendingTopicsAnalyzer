Meritor Wins Three Gold Telly Awards for 14Xe™ Electric Powertrain Commercial

Top Tier Recognition for Excellence in VideoTROY, Mich., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Meritor, Inc. (NYSE: MTOR) announced that The Road Ahead, a video production created to showcase the company's 14Xe™ electric powertrain, was recognized with three gold awards in the 43rd Annual Telly Awards.The Telly Awards honor excellence in video and television across all screens and is judged by leaders from video platforms, television, streaming networks and production companies. The Road Ahead won in the following categories:Online Commercials – AutomotiveOnline Commercials – Music/JingleOnline Commercials – Use of 3D AnimationThe Road Ahead can be viewed on Meritor.
