Noida: 2 record couple in hotel room in extortion attempt, arrested
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 02:30:00

NOIDA: Police arrested two men on Friday for allegedly recording private moments of a couple in a hotel room and trying to extort money by threatening to release the footage.Police have identified the accused as Vishnu Singh , a resident of Noida, and Abdul Wahab, a native of Bihar, who lived in Khora, Ghaziabad. During investigation into the case, police arrested two other people involved in separate cheating and fraud cases. One of them, Pankaj Kumar, a native of Gonda and resident of Chijarishi village in Noida, allegedly duped people on the pretext of providing jobs and also sold activated SIMs to criminals.
