October 18, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Coherent, a global no-code software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform quickly becoming the ubiquitous standard for transforming business logic from spreadsheets into enterprise-grade code, today announced new leadership for its Asia-Pacific (APAC) region, three new C-suite leadership hires, geographic expansion in locations across 15 U.S. states and Canada, and four new partnerships. Coherent also experienced its best performing financial quarter in the company's history, which has been fuelled by the need to turn spreadsheets into a superpower.
