"Bridgerton" star Ruby Barker said she's "happy to be alive" after being released from the hospital where she received mental health treatment.The actress, who plays Marina Crane (née Thompson) on the breakout Netflix series, posted a video on Monday to Instagram thanking healthcare workers, the media, her more than 230,000 followers, along with "everyone at Bridgerton" and "Shondaland" for their support after she shared that she had been hospitalized in a previous post last week.  "You have all been incredible; like literally.
