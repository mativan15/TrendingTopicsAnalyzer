S&P 500 gains over 4% for the week amid hopes of Fed easing up on rate-hiking spree
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-21 21:18:08

franckreporter/iStock via Getty Images The S&P 500 (SP500) gained 4.7% for the week, with the benchmark index ending in the green in three sessions out of five, as sentiment was supported by hopes that the Federal Reserve may slow down its rate-hiking campaign. A Wall Street Journal story, whose author is known to be closely briefed on FOMC thinking, said there may be talks on slowing rate hikes at the next meet. San Francisco Fed President Mary Daly also said the Fed should consider reducing size of rate hikes now.
