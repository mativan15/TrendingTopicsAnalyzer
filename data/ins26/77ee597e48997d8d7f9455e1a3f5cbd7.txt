CEO at CloudSphere. getty One of the biggest C-suite mandates today is to keep a finger on the pulse of business conditions to safeguard operations and manage risk. And while there are many candidates for that senior-level attention, data privacy has become one of the most consequential factors affecting business conditions today. An enterprise's relationship with data privacy is a highly regulated environment of ever-changing laws and growing impacts. As technology solutions advance, the data that runs through enterprise systems can be increasingly leveraged for business insights, as long as the risk from breaches or noncompliant use of that data can be avoided.
