HDFC corners 15% of CLSS housing market with Rs 67k crore loans, Rs 7,200 crore in subsidy payout

2022-10-20 07:00:00

AgenciesThe 45-year-old Corporation is awaiting a reverse merger with its subsidiary HDFC Bank.With over Rs 7,200 crore in subsidy disbursals, the largest pure-play mortgage lender HDFC has cornered over 15 per cent of the credit-linked subsidy scheme for affordable housing since its launch in June 2016. The 45-year-old Corporation, which is awaiting a reverse merger with its subsidiary HDFC Bank, has also won the best housing finance company award from the government for this for the third time this year.
