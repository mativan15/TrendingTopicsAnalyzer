Spencer Platt/Getty Images News SL Green Realty (NYSE:SLG) stock dropped 3.8% in Wednesday after-hours trading after the largest office landlord in Manhattan saw its same-store cash net operating income drop from a year ago. The office REIT's Q3 FFO per share of $1.66 just skims past the $1.65 Visible Alpha consensus and declined from $1.87 in Q2 and from $1.78 in Q3 2021. Total revenue of $212.5M, topping the $156.4M Visible Alpha consensus, increased from $201.4M in the prior quarter and from $205.
