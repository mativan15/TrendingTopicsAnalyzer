Mighty dollar sinks all, rupee breaches 83
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 00:45:00

The one-month Bloomberg option volatility index surged 46 basis points to 6.85%.Mumbai: The rupee breached the 83 mark on its way to hitting a new lifetime low against the US dollar amid an unrelenting surge in the world's reserve currency that hasn't spared even its traditional proxies, including the yen and the pound. The rupee Wednesday slid to a new low of 83.02 amid minimal intervention by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). The local unit lost 0.80% against the US dollar, showed Bloomberg data compiled by ETIG.
