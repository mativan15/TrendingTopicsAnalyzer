Microsoft is Winning the War for Talent by Paying $163,000 for Top College Engineers
inc.com
business
2022-06-03 10:01:00

We all know it: money makes the world go round. Yet it's ironically a business faux pas for both employers and job seekers to admit this. In a new, bold move to attract top talent, Microsoft Corp isn't beating around the bush: come work for us, and you'll be earning $163,000 as a 22 year-old fresh out of college.For a business leader, being so explicit about compensation might be concerning. Doesn't this mean you'll attract those just in it for the money, those without real interest in the organization or passion for the job? Economics research tells us this is false.
