In run-up to investors' summit-23, CM Yogi meets USISPF delegation
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 19:28:22

Lucknow: A day after meeting India's ambassadors to 15 countries, chief minister Yogi Adityanath met a 30-member of delegation of US India Strategic Partnership Forum (USISPF) here on Tuesday, to step up efforts to attract investment to Uttar Pradesh at the Global Investors' Summit-2023 (GIS-2023) proposed to be organized in Lucknow from February 10 to 12 next year. 'The GIS-2023 is an excellent opportunity for investors and an occasion to strengthen bilateral trade relations between India and the USA,' said Yogi while interacting with the USISPF delegation here on Tuesday amid state government's efforts to make the state a global hub for industrial investment.
