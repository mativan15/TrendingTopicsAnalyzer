Charlotte giggles as she shows George how to conduct the orchestra (Image: PA Images)On their first official engagement in Wales, George, eight, and Charlotte, seven, took centre stage at Cardiff Castle as they joined the concert preparations, trying out a sound-mixing desk and conducting an orchestra.Encouraged by the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge, the children took temporary charge of the sound and light for the main stage, looking thrilled as they changed the levels while a choir sang live before them.
