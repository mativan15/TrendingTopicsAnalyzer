ASHA workers: Struggle for survival in shadows outside WHO spotlight
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 10:45:00

Come rain, sun, hail or indeed a contagion, the band of women who form the bedrock of India's rural healthcare system slog on, anonymous, underpaid and mostly unsung - until last week when the WHO honoured them for their efforts in controlling Covid. For the 10 lakh Accredited Social Health Activists (ASHA) volunteers who administer medicines, vaccines, first aid, offer health advice and a myriad other services across the length and breadth of India, the WHO recognition is just the global spotlight they need.
