October 18, 2022 09:03 AM Eastern Daylight Time RESTON, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Transaction Network Services (TNS) today announced that Assurance IQ, a direct-to-consumer platform that transforms the insurance buying experience for individuals, has deployed TNS Enterprise Branded Calling to facilitate an improved customer experience. Following TNS deployment, Assurance IQ saw a 25% increase in the likelihood that a customer who received a call will continue to explore financial solutions with Assurance IQ or one of its partners.
