ReutersThe central bank transferred 69.4% less to the government last fiscal at Rs 30,307.45 crore, against Rs 99,122 crore in 2020-21.The Reserve 's active trading in the market helped it avoid seeking capital from the government to meet prescribed levels, analysts said. If not for its sell/buy swaps in the currency market and conversion of revaluation gains into realised profits, the central bank's capital levels might have fallen below those prescribed by the Bimal Jalan committee, they said.

The RBI reduced its balance sheet by over Rs 1.
