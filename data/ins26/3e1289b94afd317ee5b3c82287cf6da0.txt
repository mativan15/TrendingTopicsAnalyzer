When is the last application date for UGC NET December 2021, June 2022 cycles?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 09:45:00

UGC NET, or the National Eligibility Test of the University Grants Commission, is held twice a year, once in June and once in December. But last year, it didn't happen in December. Thus, this year, it has merged with the June exam, and for that, registration windows are extended till 30th May 2022. So, all the aspirants who have not filled the form but want to appear for it can do this now. For that, you need to log on to ugcnet.nta.nic.in. The window for applying for the UGC NET was closed earlier as the last date of application was 20th May 2022.
