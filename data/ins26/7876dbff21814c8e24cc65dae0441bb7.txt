AARP Iowa shares tips on avoiding student loan debt forgiveness scammers

Photo: Getty Images(Des Moines, IA) -- AARP Iowa is sharing tips on how not to get scammed while looking for student loan debt relief. "With thousands of Iowans eligible for student debt relief, it is essential for borrowers to be alert for scams that could dupe them into sharing their personal information or money with fraudsters posing as legitimate government programs," said AARP Iowa State Director Brad Anderson. He says debt relief applicants should know the following: Apply only at StudentAid.
