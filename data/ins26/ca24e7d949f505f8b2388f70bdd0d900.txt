Sidhu Moose Wala murder accused Deepak Tinu, who is a close aide of Gangster Lawrence Bishnoi, recently escaped police custody in the Mansa district of Punjab. According to PTI, the Delhi Police carried out extensive operations at different places in the country in order to nab him. Accused Deepak Tinu kept changing his hideout after escaping custody on October 1.  web screen grabHowever, the Delhi police finally succeeded in capturing him on October 19 (Wednesday) as revealed by Special Commissioner of Police (Special Cell) HGS Dhaliwal.
