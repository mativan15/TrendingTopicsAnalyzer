Des Cahill steps down as host of The Sunday Game
irishtimes.com
sport
2022-10-17 00:00:00

Des Cahill has announced that he is to step away from The Sunday Game after 15 seasons as host of RTÉ's Sunday night GAA highlights programme.In a statement, he said: 'The reason I want a change is very straightforward - I want to go to live games again!'It's been a privilege to host The Sunday Game for the past 15 seasons, but I miss the sense of fun and anticipation as you make your way to a match, the feeling of tension and excitement, the roar of the crowd.'The presenter will now take up the role of host on both Saturday Sport and Sunday Sport on RTÉ Radio 1.
