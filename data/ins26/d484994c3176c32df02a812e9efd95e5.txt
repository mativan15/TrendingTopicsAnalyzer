SRP Ranks No. 4588 With Three-Year Revenue Growth of 95% Percent
SHREVEPORT, La., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- For the sixth time since 2012, SRP Environmental (SRP) has been recognized on the annual Inc. 5000 list, placing 4588 on the most prestigious ranking of the fastest-growing private companies in America. Previous rankings include No. 2122 (2021), No. 1948 (2019), No. 4225 (2014), No. 3381 (2013), and No. 2465 (2012). To put this in perspective, only 58% of the companies on the list are repeat honorees.
