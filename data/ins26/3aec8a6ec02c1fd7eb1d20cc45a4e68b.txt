Hewlett Packard Enterprise tumble leads enterprise IT stocks lower (NYSE:HPE)
seekingalpha.com

2022-06-02 14:54:57

JasonDoiy/iStock Unreleased via Getty Images Hewlett Packard Enterprise (NYSE:HPE) shares dropped more than 7% on Thursday after the enterprise IT company posted second-quarter results that missed expectations and said its adjusted earnings for 2022 would be below estimates, leading other enterprise IT stocks lower. For the period ending April 30, Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) earned an adjusted 44 cents per share on $6.7B in sales, compared to estimates of 45 cents in earnings and $6.8B in sales.
