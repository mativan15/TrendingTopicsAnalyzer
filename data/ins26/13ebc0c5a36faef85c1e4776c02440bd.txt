Already the engineering inputs for the Ocean SUV are from Fisker's Hyderabad-based technology development centre.New Delhi: Elon Musk is still dilly-dallying on his India plan for the manufacturing of Tesla's world-leading electric cars here. But, the new entrant to the global electrification market, Henrik Fisker, and his California-based Fisker Inc, which will roll out its first model named Ocean SUV on November 17, 2022, has well-defined and evolving India-centric plans. They involve product engineering and development, marketing, platform sharing, and joint venture manufacturing, sales and servicing in due course.
