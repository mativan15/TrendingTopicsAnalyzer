NexGen Hosts Event at World's First GaN-on-GaN Fab to Promote CHIPS Act and U.S.-Based Semiconductor ManufacturingSANTA CLARA, Calif., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- NexGen Power Systems, Inc., the world's leading designer, developer and manufacturer of innovative power conversion systems using its revolutionary Vertical GaN™ semiconductor technology, hosted New York Senator Chuck Schumer and New York State officials at its Syracuse fabrication facility (FAB-1) to promote U.S.-based semiconductor manufacturing and global technology leadership.
