More Video Patrick Gillespie, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- Software company Globant SA is partnering with FIFA, the global soccer governing body, on its new streaming platform ahead of the World Cup next month, deepening its push into sports and entertainment.Globant will create new features and experiences on FIFA+, the online streaming service for football's governing body launched in April. The company will also sponsor the FIFA Women's World Cup in Australia and New Zealand next year, among other FIFA events.
