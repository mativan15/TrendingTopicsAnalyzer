The company is making world-class, community-based cancer care more accessible to Texas residents

AUSTIN, Texas, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The Oncology Institute, Inc. (NASDAQ: TOI), one of the largest value-based oncology groups in the United States, is expanding its footprint into Austin, Texas with the opening of two new specialty cancer clinics. The first clinic opened to residents on May 31 and offers comprehensive medical oncology services designed to expand access to state-of-the-art oncology care for area patients.
