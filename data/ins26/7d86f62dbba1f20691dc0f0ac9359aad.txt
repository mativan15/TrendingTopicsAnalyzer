 York Regional Police say they are looking to identify a suspect wanted in connection with a hate-motivated incident reported at a mosque in Thornhill, Ont.
Police said on Oct. 14 they were called to Imam Mahdi Islamic Centre on Bayview Avenue for reports of property damage.An investigation into the incident revealed graffiti was spray painted the night before on Oct. 13.Investigators alleged a man spray painted three areas of the mosque with 'derogatory, anti-Iranian language, written in Farsi, aimed towards the Iranian government.
