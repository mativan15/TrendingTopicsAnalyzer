October 18, 2022 10:04 AM Eastern Daylight Time BERWYN, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Red Spruce Capital, a privately held Registered Investment Advisor, announced today the hiring of Raymond J. McCaffrey, CFA, as a Portfolio Manager. McCaffrey will directly manage all aspects of investment research, selection, and management for client portfolios. He joins Red Spruce's Portfolio Management team of Amish Desai, Mark Kennedy, Rahul Shah, and Mark Vasquez.
