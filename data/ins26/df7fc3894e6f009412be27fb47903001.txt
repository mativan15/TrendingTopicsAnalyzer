Despite claiming the title of the world's bastion of freedom, the U.S. doesn't always feel so free. It's no secret that government and corporate surveillance in this country has gotten a little out of hand in recent years—and, according to some studies, Americans consistently rank as some of the world's most surveilled people. One study published last year showed that America actually ranks #2 in surveillance globally—and another from several years back found that several U.S. cities ranked as some of the most spied upon in the world.
