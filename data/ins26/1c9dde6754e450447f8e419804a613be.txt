October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--On Saturday, Oct. 8, Alabama State University celebrated both a homecoming game and a large gift. Courtney Stewart, an Atlanta native and ASU alumnus (2003 graduate), gave $100,000 to his alma mater. As a former communications major, the money will be largely dedicated to scholarships for communication students and multiple communication department enhancements.

'It's so important to give back, and it's a blessing and an honor to be able to do that and help support the growth of HBCUs and prepare the current students with resources and an education to thrive after college,' said Stewart.
