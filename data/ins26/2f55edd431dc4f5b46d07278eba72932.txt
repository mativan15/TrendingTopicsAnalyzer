These 5 States Are Voting on Marijuana Legalization on Election Day
cnet.com
news
2022-10-21 17:30:02

Election Day is right around the corner and, in addition to key political races, many lawmakers are keenly tracking marijuana legalization initiatives on the ballot in several states.The topic of legal weed -- both on the state and national level -- has come back into focus as President Joe Biden pardoned all federal marijuana possession convictions this month."Just as no one should be in a federal prison solely due to the possession of marijuana, no one should be in a local jail or state prison for that reason, either," Biden said in a statement.
