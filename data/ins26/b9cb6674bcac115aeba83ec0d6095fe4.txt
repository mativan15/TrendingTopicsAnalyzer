Sovereign Gold Bond: Premature redemption of SGB 2017–18 Series IV fixed at this price
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 06:00:00

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) announced that the redemption price for SGB 2017–18 Series IV due on October 21, 2022 will be Rs 5,028 per unit of Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB). The RBI made this announcement via a notification on October 20, 2022. The calculation is based on the simple average of closing gold price for three business days, i.e., October 18–20, 2022. 'The simple average of the closing gold price for the previous three business days, as reported by the India Bullion and Jewellers Association Ltd.
