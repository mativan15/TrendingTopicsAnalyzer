Jack Bienvenue got inked at Hotline Tattoo in Hyannis by artist Taylor Lindley back in March.Courtesy Jack BienvenueThe Celtics are heading to the NBA finals, and Boston is ready to do battle in pursuit of another championship title. But nobody's manifesting a 2022 win quite like 18-year-old Jack Bienvenue.The senior at Cape Cod Tech decided to get a tattoo of a championship banner on his arm that reads: 'Boston Celtics 2022 World Champions.'It's his first tattoo — and he got it back on March 21.
