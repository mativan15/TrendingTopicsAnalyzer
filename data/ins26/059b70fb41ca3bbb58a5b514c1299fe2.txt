Autodesk's (ADSK 0.25%) stock price jumped 10% on May 27 after the software company posted its fiscal year 2023 first-quarter results (for the period that ended April 30). Its quarterly revenue rose 18% year over year to $1.17 billion, which beat analysts' estimates by $20 million. Its adjusted earnings increased 39% to $1.43 per share, which also surpassed the consensus forecast by $0.09.
