NEW YORK, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Mediaocean (www.Mediaocean.com), the mission-critical platform for omnichannel advertising, has released its H1 2022 Market Report. The report underscores the biggest issues facing marketers, which include the adoption of emerging identity solutions as well as cross-channel planning and measurement. Most notably, the findings of the new study show a clear concern about how the advertising industry will adopt privacy-friendly identity solutions in a post-cookie environment.
