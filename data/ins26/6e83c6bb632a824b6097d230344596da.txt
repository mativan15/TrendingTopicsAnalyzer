October 21, 2022 04:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nuveen Emerging Markets Debt 2022 Target Term Fund (NYSE: JEMD) today announced new details concerning its liquidation. Consistent with its investment objectives and organizational documents, the fund plans to terminate its existence and liquidate on or about December 1, 2022.

As the fund approaches liquidation, its common shares will continue trading on the New York Stock Exchange through November 25, 2022 and will be suspended from trading before the open of trading on November 28, 2022.
