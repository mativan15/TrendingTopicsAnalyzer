'Tennis is not the easiest sport for parents because it's an individual sport and with team sports you can always carpool. Tennis is pretty much all on you, all on the family.'Now a senior co-captain and first singles player at Brookline High, Caroline is ranked No. 1 in the state, third in New England, and 55th nationally among girls 18-and-under, according to USTA rankings.She's ready to lead the Warriors (13-1) at the Division 1 state tournament in the final matches of her undefeated high school career before joining the women's team at the University of Denver.
