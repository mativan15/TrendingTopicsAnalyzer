WFH or return to office? More companies are leaving the decision to their teams
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 20:15:00

To work from home or to work from the office? At some companies, neither the human resources department nor business leaders are dictating where employees function from. Companies are increasingly decentralising this decision and letting individual teams decide. Companies such as Microsoft, Accenture, Deloitte and RPG Group among others are redefining flexibility. In the longer term, such team agreements give people flexibility and empowerment
