Since its launch a year ago today, live entertainment and cashless tipping app PickleJar has helped artists earn more than $1.2 million. Over 2,500 artists and creators are on the platform and every penny earned through fan-funded support and transactions goes directly into their pockets. PickleJar CEO and Founder Jeff James and President and Founder Kristian Barowsky are longtime friends and business partners.
