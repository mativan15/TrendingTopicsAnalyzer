Henry Slade, Elliot Daly, Joe Marler and Danny Care are all left out but Harlequins' try machine Cadan Murley has received a first call-up from head coach Eddie JonesMurley scored two tries for Quins against Leicester and has a Premiership joint-leading seven this seasonEddie Jones has left Henry Slade, Elliot Daly, Joe Marler and Danny Care out of his England autumn squad. The Red Rose boss named a 36-strong squad this morning which includes the Premiership's joint top try scorers Max Malins and Cadan Murley.
