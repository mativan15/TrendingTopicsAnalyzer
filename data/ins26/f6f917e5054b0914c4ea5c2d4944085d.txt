Democratic congressman proposes 1,000% tax on certain firearms
foxbusiness.com
politics
2022-06-06 01:32:38

A U.S. House Democrat is proposing a federal 1,000% excise tax on assault weapons, and wants to pass it through reconciliation. Rep. Donald Beyer, D-Va., is introducing the bill which would tax all AR-15-style weapons at 1,000%, according to Business Insider.Beyer, a member of the House Ways and Means Committee and chairman of the Joint Economic Committee, told Business Insider that the measure is "sensible gun control.""What it's intended to do is provide another creative pathway to actually make some sensible gun control happen," Beyer said.
