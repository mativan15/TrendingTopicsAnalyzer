In reversal, Elon Musk says SpaceX will continue funding Starlink in Ukraine
cbsnews.com
news
2022-10-15 19:14:07

October 15, 2022 / 3:14 PM / AFP Elon Musk on Saturday announced that his company would continue to pay for Starlink satellite internet in war-torn Ukraine, a day after suggesting he cannot keep funding the project."The hell with it," the world's richest man wrote on Twitter. "Even though Starlink is still losing money & other companies are getting billions of taxpayer $, we'll just keep funding Ukraine govt for free."Musk had said on Friday that SpaceX would not be able to pay for Starlink in Ukraine indefinitely.
