Progressive Conservative candidate Kevin Holland addresses supporters at the Whitewater Golf Club on Thursday night after winning the Thunder Bay-Atikokan riding. (Jon Thompson/CBC)Thunder Bay-Atikokan voters will have a voice within Ontario's second consecutive majority Progressive Conservative government.Kevin Holland collected 36 per cent of the vote to beat one-term NDP incumbent, Judith Monteith-Farrell in Thursday night's provincial election. Holland's win ends a generation-long drought for the Tories in the riding.
