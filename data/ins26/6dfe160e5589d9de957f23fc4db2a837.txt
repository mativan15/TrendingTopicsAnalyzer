Breakups are never easy and living in fear your partner may be about to break up with you is even worse.A therapist and relationship expert has decoded some of the signs that mean your partner might be about to break up with you.Sometimes our partner - be it a girlfriend, boyfriend, wife, or husband - might change their behaviour.These changes in behaviour could be a sign your significant other is about to break up with you according to therapist and best-selling author, Marisa Peer.Marisa has worked for 30 years with thousands of clients and now she offers coaching on relationships, sex, anxiety, and wedding stress, among other things.
