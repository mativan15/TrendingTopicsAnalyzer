Browns RG Wyatt Teller to miss Sunday's game against Ravens
espn.com
news
2022-10-21 16:47:00

12:47 PM ETBEREA, Ohio -- Cleveland Browns All-Pro right guard Wyatt Teller will miss Sunday's game against the Baltimore Ravens because of a left calf strain.Teller missed three games in 2020 with a similar calf injury. He suffered the injury during New England's 38-15 victory over the Browns this past Sunday. He's been wearing a boot around the Browns' practice facility this week.Teller's injury is a blow to a Browns running game that leads the NFL with 1,032 yards. Hjalte Froholdt is expected to start in his place.
