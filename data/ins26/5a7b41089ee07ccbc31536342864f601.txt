English Finnish Municipality Finance PlcStock exchange release 19 October 2022 at 10:00 am (EEST) Municipality Finance issues a USD 25 million tap under its MTN programme On 20 October 2022 Municipality Finance Plc issues a new tranche in an amount of USD 25 million to an existing series of notes issued on 15 July 2021. With the new tranche, the aggregate nominal amount of the notes is USD 585 million. The maturity date of the notes is 15 July 2026. The notes bear interest at a floating rate equal to Compounded SOFR plus 100 basis points per annum.
