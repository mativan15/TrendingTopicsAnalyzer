In this week's Restaurant Roundup, the Cordùas' new concept is nearly ready to open in the Ion, Niko Niko's and El Tiempo are expanding, and a new pickleball concept is coming to the Webster entertainment district being developed next to the future Great Wolf Lodge.

Cordùas' new concept to open in the Ion this fall

Chef David Cordúa's new concept, The Lymbar, is slated to open this fall in the Ion.

Construction is nearly complete on the all-day neighborhood craft cocktail bar and restaurant, which will be in the Ion's jewel box corner suite at 4201 Main St.
