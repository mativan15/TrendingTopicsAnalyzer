Elon Musk says Starship needs work to send SpaceX Starlink 2.0 satellites into orbit
zdnet.com
news
2022-06-01 02:14:23

Image: Getty ImagesElon Musk has admitting he is banking on Starship, a launch rocket currently in development, to get SpaceX's next generation Starlink satellites into orbit."We need Starship to work and to fly frequently, or Starlink 2.0 will be stuck on the ground," the tech billionaire told YouTube show Everyday Astronaut.He explained that sending Starlink 2.0 into orbit with the company's Falcon 9 rockets, which were used to send the first generation of Starlink satellites into space, is not plausible.
