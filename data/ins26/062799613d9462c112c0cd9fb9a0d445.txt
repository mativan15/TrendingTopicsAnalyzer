BJP demands white paper on funds allocated and spent in Munugode

news
2022-10-16 17:50:39

Rajya Sabha member and national OBC president K. Laxman has demanded the TRS government to release a white paper on the funds allocated for various welfare schemes and spent for the development of Munugode Assembly constituency since 2014.He said this while participating in an election campaign in support of the party candidate K. Rajgopal Reddy on Sunday.The BJP leader accused the government of having double standards in pouring in funds during the time of bypolls and later neglecting the constituency.
