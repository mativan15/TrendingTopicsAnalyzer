The government Wednesday appointed one Judge and an additional Judge to the Delhi High Court. Advocate Anish Dayal was appointed as Judge while Advocate Amit Sharma was appointed as additional Judge of the Delhi High Court. Anish Dayal was designated as a Senior Advocate in 2021 and practised primarily at the Supreme Court and the High Court of Delhi. He had his own civil, commercial and corporate litigation and arbitration practice for over 26 years. He had enrolled as an advocate with the Delhi Bar Council in 1995.
