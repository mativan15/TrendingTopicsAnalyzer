'Money Heist: Korea - Joint Economic Area', a spinoff of hugely popular Spanish series Money Heist (La Casa de Papel), releases its official teaser on Thursday, ahead of its June 24 release date.According to reports, the original crime thriller, written by Alex Pina , lasted five seasons and followed a bunch of criminals who carried out a series of heists.The Korean version, executive produced by Pina, is set against the backdrop of North and South Korea's reunification and focuses on The Professor (Yoo Ji-tae), who approaches Tokyo (Jun Jong-seo) with a plot to steal 4 trillion won from the Korea Unified Mint The series is directed by Kim Hong-sun and written by Ryu Yong-jae, Kim Hwan-chae, and Choe Sung-jun, and was initially announced in November 2020.
