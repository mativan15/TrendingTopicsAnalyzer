(From Beecher & Bennett Funeral Home)NEW HAVEN, CT - (From Beecher & Bennett Funeral Home) Julia (Hammer) Knight, age 51, of New Haven, passed away at her home after an almost 2-year leukemia battle with her loving family beside her on May 24, 2022. Born in Louisville, KY on November 22, 1970, Julia is survived by her husband and best friend, Daniel M. Knight; her 2 beautiful adult children, Audrey and Winston Knight; her parents, David and Karen (Wallace) Hammer; and her siblings, Jane, Sam, Matt and Ann.
