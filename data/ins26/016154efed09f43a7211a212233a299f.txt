Indus Water Treaty: Officials from India, Pakistan hold talks
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 18:00:00

Officials from India and Pakistan have discussed a wide range of water-related issues during a meeting as part of the Permanent Commission on Indus Waters (PCIW), the Foreign Office said on Tuesday. A six-member delegation led by Pakistan Commissioner for Indus Waters Syed Muhammad Mehar Ali Shah attended the 118th meeting of the Pakistan-India Permanent Indus Commission (PIC) in New Delhi from May 30-31, it said. The Indian delegation was headed by the Indian Commissioner for Indus Waters, A K Pal.
