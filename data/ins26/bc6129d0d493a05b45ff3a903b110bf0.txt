Haryana is the best-governed, Khattar cites Public Affairs Index Report- 2022



 




Read this news in brief form


 

 

Chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar on Saturday said that Haryana has been ranked the best-governed state in the Public Affairs Index Report, 2022, prepared by Bengaluru-based think tank Public Affairs Center (PAC). The CM was addressing a public gathering in Naya Gaon village in Gurugram. Haryana ranked at the top among other large states in the report which was released on Friday.
