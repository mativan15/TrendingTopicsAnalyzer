Immersion Sues Meta Over Alleged Patent Infringement
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-27 12:25:00

By Will Feuer Immersion Corp. said it filed a complaint against Meta Platforms Inc., alleging that the Facebook parent company's augmented and virtual reality systems, including the Meta Quest 2 headset, infringe on six patents owned by Immersion. The complaint was filed in the U.S. District Court for the Western District of Texas, Immersion said. Immersion, which is focused on haptic technologies, also called 3-D touch, said it is seeking to stop Meta from further alleged infringement of the patents.
