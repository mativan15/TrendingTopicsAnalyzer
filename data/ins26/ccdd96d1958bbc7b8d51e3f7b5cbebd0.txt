Eli Lilly stock pulls back from record high after disclosing sales by largest shareholder
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 19:05:57

Shares of Eli Lilly & Co. LLY, -3.72% fell 4.0% in afternoon trading Tuesday, to pullback from the previous session's record close, after the drug maker disclosed that its largest shareholder sold nearly $64 million worth of its shares. In a Form 4 filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the company said Lilly Endowment Inc., a private philanthropic foundation, sold 200,000 Eli Lilly shares in the open market on May 27. The sales were executed in a series of trades ranging from $314.44 to $323.
