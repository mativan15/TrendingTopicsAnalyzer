Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Pharma index advances 0.52%
indiatimes.com

2022-10-20 11:00:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Pharma index closed on a positive note on Thursday.Shares of Cipla(up 1.32 per cent), Ipca Laboratories(up 1.28 per cent), Divis Laboratories(up 1.22 per cent), Torrent Pharmaceuticals(up 1.04 per cent) and Zydus Lifesciences(up 0.99 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack.On the other hand, Granules India(down 2.29 per cent), Strides Pharma Science(down 1.81 per cent), Alembic Pharmaceuticals(down 1.72 per cent), Lupin(down 1.37 per cent) and Pfizer(down 0.45 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.
