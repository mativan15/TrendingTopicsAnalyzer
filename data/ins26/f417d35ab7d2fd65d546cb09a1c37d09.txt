(VERI)By: Manshi Mamtora, CFA Kyro Digital, a platform for Web3 Builders collaborates with Veritone (NASDAQ:VERI), creator of aiWARE, a hyper-expansive enterprise AI platform. The partnership will make the experience smoother for Veritone clients to use the Kyro Platform, Builder and API to quickly create and deploy powerful new Web3 business applications that accelerate today's blockchain transformation. As part of the collaboration, Kyro is launching a Marketplace with Olympic athlete Cody Miller at CodyMillerNFT.
