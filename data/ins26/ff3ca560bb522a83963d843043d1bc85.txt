Wales welcomes Ukraine to Cardiff City Stadium in Cardiff, Wales Sunday. Kickoff is scheduled for noon ET (ESPN). Below, we preview the Wales vs. Ukraine odds and lines, and make our best World Cup bets, picks and predictions.
The winner of this match will officially join the England-led Group B. USA and Iran are also in the group. Ukraine beat Scotland 3-1 in Glasglow Wednesday to earn a chance to win one more game for a berth in Qatar.
Wales is ranked 18th in the FIFA rankings, while Ukraine sits 27th.
