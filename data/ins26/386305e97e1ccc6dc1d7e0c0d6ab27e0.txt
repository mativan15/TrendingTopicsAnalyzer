Passport Office delays mean missed holidays and heartache for Times readers
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 21:30:00

Last week The Times reported that staff had warned the backlog at the Passport Office would only get worse amid 'rock-bottom morale' and 'creaking' IT systems.Almost 200 frustrated readers wrote in with tales of lost passports, missed business trips and summer holidays put at risk.Jonathan Lowe, 67, spent weeks arguing with the Passport Office after trying to renew his British passport. He was required to submit his Irish passport alongside the online application. It was not until a month later that he was told that the application was held up because the third of his forenames was not shown on his Irish passport.
