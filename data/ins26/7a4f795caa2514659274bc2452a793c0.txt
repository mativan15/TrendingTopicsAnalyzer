Wevr has made stellar virtual reality games like the ocean simulation TheBlu. And now it is expanding to make Virtual Studio, a cloud-based platform to help people make next-generation games. The Wevr Virtual Studio is a tool in early access that game teams can use to stand up new projects. Neville Spiteri, CEO of Wevr, describes it as a purpose-built platform that lets interactive creators and game developers use real-time game engines like Unity and Unreal.
