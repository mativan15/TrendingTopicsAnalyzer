UK consumers shun physical wallets in favor of contactless and mobile payments
Insider
business
2022-05-31 14:06:10

Breaking it down: More than three-quarters (77%) of consumers in the UK have swapped their physical wallets for mobile payments, according to Marqeta research. Insider Intelligence 96% of surveyed consumers used contactless payments in the last year.61% of consumers feel confident enough with contactless payments to leave their wallet at home and just take their phone—that figure rises to 77% for Gen Zers.64% prefer to use their mobile phones to pay because it has more built-in security features.
