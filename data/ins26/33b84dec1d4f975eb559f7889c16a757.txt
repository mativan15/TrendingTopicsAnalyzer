Assam govt announces Rs 50-crore package for flood-landslide hit Dima Hasao district
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 05:15:00

ANIAssam Chief Minister Dr. Himanta Biswa Sarma visits the landslide-hit areas, in Dima HasaoTo give relief to flood and landslide affected families and immediate restoration of different sectors in Dima Hasao district, the state government has announced an immediate package of Rs. 50 crores. Under this package, Rs. 30 crore will be provided for immediate reconstruction of critical roads. On the other hand, the state government will provide Rs. 2 lakh each to 590 families to reconstruct their damaged houses.
