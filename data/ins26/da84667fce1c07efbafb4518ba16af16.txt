TORONTO, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TRX Gold Corporation (TSX:TNX) (NYSE MKT:TRX) (the 'Company' or 'TRX Gold') is pleased to announce that effective today at market open, the Company will begin trading as TRX Gold Corporation. The Company's common shares (the 'Common Shares') will continue trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange (the 'TSX') under its current ticker symbol 'TNX' and on the New York Stock Exchange (the 'NYSE') under its current ticker symbol 'TRX'. The new CUSIP of the Common Shares is 87283P109 and the new ISIN is CA87283P1099.
