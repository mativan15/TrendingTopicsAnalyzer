May 31, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time PARIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Orange Business Services is forging ahead in IT performance monitoring with the launch of Service Manage-Watch, a global supervision solution for network services and applications for both Orange-provided and third-party services. It is designed to ensure that connectivity and security at the edge, applications, equipment, and user experience are all performing at optimal levels to meet customers' business needs.



Enterprise information systems rely on a multitude of interconnected services from various suppliers.
