mbbirdy/E+ via Getty Images Stocks lost ground on Friday, as a hotter-than-expected employment report raised concerns that the Federal Reserve would have a green light to extend its rate-hiking campaign longer than previously thought. Investors also respond to a pessimistic economic forecast from Tesla boss Elon Musk. The slide, which gave back most of the gains posted during the previous session, ended an uncertain week for Wall Street, as investors gave back some of the recovery posted in the previous week.
