The newly named premier-designate in British Columbia, David Eby, has a roots in Waterloo region. The Kitchener native's swearing in ceremony was Friday afternoon. 'I want to deliver for people in very real ways,' Eby said at the ceremony. 'I can't wait to get started' His run to party leadership comes after Anjali Appadurai, his lone challenger, was disqualified from the race over collusion involving a third party. Eby, a former housing minister and attorney general, said his first 100 days in office will focus on affordable housing, clean energy and healthcare.
