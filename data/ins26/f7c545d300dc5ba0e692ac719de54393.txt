VICTORIA, Seychelles–(BUSINESS WIRE)–June 1, 2022–
KuCoin, a leading global cryptocurrency trading platform, is launching its brand-new decentralized product – KuCoin Wallet, with its website going live for users to access the service starting June 1st. The official app of KuCoin Wallet is also expected to launch later in June. Open beta app testing is expected to become available in the coming days. The launch of KuCoin Wallet is a significant endeavor for the KuCoin ecosystem in its effort to expand Web 3.
