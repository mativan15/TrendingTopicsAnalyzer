Northgate: Chester's long wait for market ends at last

news
2022-10-21 19:20:11

Image caption, The new site features 40 market stallsAfter many years of ups and downs, setbacks and scaled down plans, part of the long-running Northgate project in Chester will finally soon be here.The plans have been in the works for about 20 years and what the scheme would include has changed in that time.The first phase will include a new market to replace the ageing 1960s building it is currently housed in.The old market closed its doors on 15 October to give the traders time to relocate to the new building.
