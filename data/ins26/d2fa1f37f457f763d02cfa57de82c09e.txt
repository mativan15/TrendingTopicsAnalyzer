Netflix's Wednesday says she did her best not to act like Christina Ricci.Speaking with Interview Magazine, actress Jenna Ortega explained why the new live-action interpretation of Wednesday Addams stands on its own two feet.'It's very different, but [Christina Ricci is] who people see as Wednesday, and that's just the truth,' she said. 'I feel like the script was very reminiscent of '90s Wednesday. It was really important to me that I wasn't doing a knockoff of [Ricci's] performance, and it was different.
