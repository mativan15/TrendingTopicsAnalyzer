VANCOUVER, British Columbia, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Diversified Royalty Corp. (TSX: DIV; DIV.DB and DIV.DB.A) (the 'Corporation' or 'DIV') is pleased to announce that its board of directors has approved a cash dividend of $0.01833 per common share for the period of June 1, 2022 to June 30, 2022, which is equal to $0.22 per common share on an annualized basis. The dividend will be paid on June 30, 2022 to shareholders of record as of the close of business on June 15, 2022. Annual General Meeting The Corporation will be holding its Annual General Meeting of shareholders at 9:00 am (Pacific time) on Tuesday, June 28, 2022 (the 'Meeting').
