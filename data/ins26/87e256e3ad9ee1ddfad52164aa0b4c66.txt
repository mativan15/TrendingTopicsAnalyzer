Published: 11:56 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:56 EDT, 17 October 2022 Two flatmates who scammed a woman out of £612,000 by pretending to be a doctor in love with her as part of an online romance fraud scheme have been jailed.James Olagbaiye and Adesola Adebayo, both from Newham, east London, were part of an international organised crime group based in the UK and Nigeria and scammed multiple women and a man out of a total of £674,000, a court heard. Five victims in the USA, Switzerland, and different parts of the UK were identified during the investigation, including one woman who believed her online romance was with a British doctor living in London.
