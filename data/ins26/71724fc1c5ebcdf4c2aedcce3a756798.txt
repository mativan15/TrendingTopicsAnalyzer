Published: 00:27 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 02:17 EDT, 30 May 2022 By Max Hunder and Mari Saito KYIV/KHARKIV, Ukraine, May 30 (Reuters) - Russian troops entered the outskirts of the Ukrainian city of Sievierodonetsk, a regional governor said on Monday, describing the fighting as "very fierce" in a city that become a key objective for Moscow's offensive in the Donbas. Shelling killed two civilians and wounded five others as Russian troops advanced into the city's southeastern and northeastern areas fringes, Luhansk region Governor Serhiy Gaidai said.
