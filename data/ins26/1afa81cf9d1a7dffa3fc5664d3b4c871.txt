As things seem to be getting more and more back to pre-pandemic normalcy, so is the desire to celebrate halloween and increase spending on the holiday. For the first time in years, people are starting to get back to in-person events, and feel comfortable taking their children door-to-door for trick-or-treating. Vaccines and other safety measures have given some level of comfort to consumers resuming many of their pre-pandemic activities.
