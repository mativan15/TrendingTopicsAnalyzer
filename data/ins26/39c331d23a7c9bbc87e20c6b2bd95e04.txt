June 03, 2022 04:04 PM Eastern Daylight Time DOWNERS GROVE, Ill.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--InvenTrust Properties Corp. ('InvenTrust' or the 'Company') (NYSE: IVT) today announced that it has signed a definitive note purchase agreement providing for a private placement of $250 million senior unsecured notes of which $150 million are designated as 5.07% Series A senior notes due August 11, 2029, and $100 million are designated as 5.20% Series B senior notes due August 11, 2032 (collectively, the 'Notes'). Combined, the Notes are expected to have a weighted average tenor of approximately 8.
