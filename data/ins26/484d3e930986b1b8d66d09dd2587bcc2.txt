What to watch today: Stock futures are lower as Wall Street awaits May jobs report
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-03 12:15:29

Stock futures were down Friday morning, as Wall Street awaits the release of May's jobs report. On Thursday, all three major U.S. stock indexes posted strong gains, snapping two-day losing streaks and putting them in positive territory for the week. The Dow Jones Industrial Average advanced 435 points, or 1.3%, after being down more than 300 points at session lows. The S&P 500 added 1.8%, while the tech-heavy Nasdaq Composite outperformed, rising 2.7%. (CNBC) Wall Street is closely anticipating May's jobs data, which the Labor Department is scheduled to release at 8:30 a.
