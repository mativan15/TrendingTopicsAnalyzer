Calvin Klein parent PVH stock gains more than 4% after better quarterly sales
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 21:01:02

PVH Corp. PVH, +0.34% rose more than 4% in the extended session Wednesday after the parent company of Calvin Klein and Tommy Hilfiger reported better-than-expected quarterly sales. PVH said it earned $133 million, or $1.94 a share, in the first quarter, compared with $100 million, or $1.38 a share, in the year-ago period. Revenue rose 2% to $2.12 billion, reflecting "continued momentum" in Europe, the company said. FactSet consensus called for EPS of $1.61 a share on sales of $2.09 billion. There were ongoing supply-chain and "logistics disruptions" globally, as well as the impact of the COVID-related shutdowns in China, the retailer said.
