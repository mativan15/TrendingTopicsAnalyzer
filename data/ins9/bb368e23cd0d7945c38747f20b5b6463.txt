An Extended Managed Services Contract Will Create a Data-Driven Organization to Improve Customer Experience, Increase Revenue and Lower Operating Costs

WALTHAM, Mass., October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Netcracker Technology announced today that Vodafone in Oman has added analytics, DevOps processes and an integration layer to its ongoing engagement with Netcracker. This latest expansion to the partnership will further add to the operator's data-driven capabilities, allowing it to grow and enhance its business and deliver an improved experience for customers.
