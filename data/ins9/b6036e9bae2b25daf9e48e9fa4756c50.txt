UFC president Dana White has ruled out the idea of crossing into boxing, saying the sport is a 'broken business'.White played a role in Conor McGregor's boxing bout with Floyd Mayweather in 2017, but the American has not since delved back into the sport.Now, another UFC champion is keen to box, with heavyweight title holder Francis Ngannou having expressed a desire to take on Tyson Fury.White has played down the likelihood of allowing Ngannou to cross over into boxing, and the American has now also hit out at the sport in general.
