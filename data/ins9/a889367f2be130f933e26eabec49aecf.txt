An unconventional path

Dedren Snead '12 is many things. He's an international businessman, a tech whiz, an artist and an entrepreneur. He has lived and worked in places like Japan, China and Korea. He has a knack for learning both foreign and computer languages quickly, and he is always coming up with big, bold ideas.It may seem unlikely that such a worldly person was born and raised in the tiny town of Snow Hill, North Carolina. Perhaps even more unlikely for Snead as a young person was the idea of attending North Carolina's flagship university.
