St James's Palace21st October, 2022The Princess Royal, Patron, Maritime UK, this morning launched the National Centre for Coastal Autonomy at Plymouth Marine Laboratory, Prospect Place, Plymouth, and was received by His Majesty's Lord-Lieutenant of Devon (Mr David Fursdon).Her Royal Highness this afternoon visited Goonhilly Earth Station, Goonhilly Downs, Helston, and was received by His Majesty's Lord-Lieutenant of Cornwall (Colonel Edward Bolitho).The Princess Royal later visited Spaceport Cornwall's integration facility at Cornwall Airport Newquay, St Mawgan, Newquay.
