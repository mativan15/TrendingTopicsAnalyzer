June 01, 2022 02:43 PM Eastern Daylight Time WALL, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--New Jersey Natural Gas (NJNG), a regulated subsidiary of New Jersey Resources (NYSE: NJR), today submitted its annual Basic Gas Supply Service (BGSS) filing to the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities (BPU) seeking to adjust its periodic BGSS rates for residential and small commercial customers primarily to reflect a significant rise in wholesale market prices for natural gas.



For the typical heating customer using 100 therms a month, the proposed increase related to the BGSS rate is 12.
