InHome Therapy Wraps Up Q1 With Impressive Nationwide Growth and Activity

PHILADELPHIA (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 InHome Therapy has wrapped up the first quarter of 2022 with 48 new therapists, two additions to its executive leadership team and four new markets entered, including Western Florida, California, Indiana and Ohio. In March, the company announced the acquisition of Contract Therapy Services, Inc., a therapy company that provides contract physical, occupational and speech therapy, and registered dietitian services in Fort Myers and Naples, Florida, and Los Angeles, California, with a particular expertise in speech.
