Sabres forward Alex Tuch, left, shoots the puck past Flames goalie Dan Vladar during the third period of a 6-3 win on Thursday night at Scotiabank Saddledome in Calgary. (Jeff McIntosh/The Canadian Press)Alex Tuch notched his first career hat trick and the Buffalo Sabres handed the Calgary Flames their first loss of the season on Thursday night with a 6-3 victory.Dylan Cozens, Rasmus Dahlin and Casey Mittelstadt also scored for Buffalo (3-1-0) which completed a sweep of Alberta having beaten the Edmonton Oilers 4-2 on Tuesday.
