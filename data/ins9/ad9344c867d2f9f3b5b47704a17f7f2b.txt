Montreal Canadiens forward Kirby Dach scores the game-winning goal during a 3-2 overtime victory against the Pittsburgh Penguins on Monday. (Paul Chiasson/The Canadian Press)The Montreal Canadiens will have a lot of growing pains this season retooling with a young squad.However, the Habs found a way Monday night to please their fans by maintaining an undefeated home record through their first two NHL games.Martin St. Louis' side rallied in the third period to force overtime and then snatched a 3-2 win from the Pittsburgh Penguins at the Bell Centre.
