McGregor had previously suggested he would demand an immediate title shot against Oliveira upon his UFC return, but it now appears Alexander Volkanovski will be the challengerCian Cowley opens up about Conor McGregor's first spar back UFC president Dana White expects Alexander Volkanovski to challenge Charles Oliveira if he becomes champion again - ruling out a fight with Conor McGregor.
