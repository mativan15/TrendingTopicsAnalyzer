Minimum wage bill, wage-freeze repeal among bills expected to pass before Manitoba Legislature break
cbc.ca
news
2022-06-01 22:30:00

Manitoba's spring legislative session is expected to wrap up Wednesday evening. (Tyson Koschik/CBC)Politicians at the Manitoba Legislature are expected to pass a number of bills into law this evening before starting their summer break.Among the bills expected to go to a final vote is one that would let the government raise the minimum wage above the $12.35 an hour that is scheduled to take effect in October.The Progressive Conservative government has said it wants to remain competitive with other provinces and will determine a new minimum wage after consulting business and labour representatives.
