Advanced Oncotherapy Raises GBP6 Mln via Premium Subscription by Shareholder
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 06:40:00

By Ian Walker Advanced Oncotherapy PLC said Tuesday that it is raising 6 million pounds ($6.8 million) via a premium share subscription by one of its largest shareholders, and will use the money to support its operations. The U.K.-based developer of therapy systems for cancer treatment has issued 24 million new ordinary shares at 25 pence each to Odey Asset Management LLP. The price is a 12% premium to its closing price of 22.25 pence on Monday. The subscription shares carry warrants entitling Odey Asset Management the chance to buy 24 million shares at 25 pence through to Oct.
