The $750 million Aspiria mixed-use development in Overland Park is the largest multitenant campus office in the Kansas City area, and it continues to grow.

The owner of the former Sprint Corp. campus, Occidental Management Inc., aims to turn the 207-acre property southwest of 115th Street and Nall Avenue into a live-work-play destination spanning 6.34 million square feet, including 3.97 million square feet of office in 17 of its existing buildings.
