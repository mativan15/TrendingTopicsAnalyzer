IIT-Delhi Student Becomes ‘World's Top Coder', Wins Biggest Coding Contest And $10,000
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 20:15:53

IIT-Delhi (Indian Institute of Technology) student Kalash Gupta has won Season 10 of TCS CodeVita, the global coding competition. Gupta, a third-year computer science and engineering student from IIT-D, was one of the participants among 100,000 contestants from 87 countries, said a press release issued by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). IIT Delhi websiteKalash Gupta had also secured the 3rd rank nationwide in the Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) for admission to the IITs in 2018. He topped the entire Delhi zone.
