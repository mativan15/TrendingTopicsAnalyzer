American Express, Utah Jazz and VivintArena Announce New Partnership
nba.com
news
2022-10-17 16:04:26

American Express, the Utah Jazz, and Vivint Arena announced today a new sponsorship agreement making American Express the Official Payments Partner of the Jazz and Vivint Arena, with an emphasis on creating unforgettable customer experiences through unprecedented service, access, and value. The multi-year partnership also designates American Express as the Official Payment Systems Provider for all events at Vivint Arena, the 18,206-seat downtown facility that attracts more than 100 shows, concerts, sporting events, and NBA games annually.
