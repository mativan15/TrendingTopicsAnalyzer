CCAGW PAC Endorses Seven Ohio Representatives for Re-election

October 19, 2022 04:10 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Reps. Troy Balderson (R-Ohio), Mike Carey (R-Ohio), Steve Chabot (R-Ohio), Warren Davidson (R-Ohio), Jim Jordan (R-Ohio), Bob Latta (R-Ohio), and Brad Wenstrup (R-Ohio) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2019 Congressional Ratings.
