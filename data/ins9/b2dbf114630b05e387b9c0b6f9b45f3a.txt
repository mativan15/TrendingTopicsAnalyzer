While a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) can be invaluable to a company with regards to safety and cybersecurity, some smaller enterprises may want to look into a Virtual CISO (vCISO) to assist with cutting down on expenses. Michael Gray, Chief Technology Officer at Thrive, compared the positions and outlined the differences for companies looking to invest in this type of role. A virtual CISO is that of an independent or contracted employee, who fills the role of a CISO but is not employed full time.
