Bonds surge and sterling rallies as Hunt brings forward fiscal statement
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-17 00:00:00

UK government bonds surged and the pound rallied on investors' expectations the government could further depart from the expansive fiscal strategy that plunged markets into turmoil.Yields on 30-year and 10-year government bonds — also known as gilts — tumbled by around 8 per cent in early trading as a statement by the chancellor of the exchequer, Jeremy Hunt that he will bring forward a fiscal statement to later on Monday morning soothed volatile markets in advance of the medium-term fiscal plan slated for the end of the month.
