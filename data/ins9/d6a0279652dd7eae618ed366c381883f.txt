Melissa Kopka/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Thesis Raytheon Technologies (NYSE:RTX) reported strong Q1 2022 results on April 26, 2022. Both topline and bottom line reported healthy growth amid supply chain disruptions and ceasing of defense business with Russia. Sales came in at $15.7 billion, representing a 3 percent YoY growth and a 4 percent organic growth. GAAP EPS came in at $0.74, representing a 45 percent increase YoY, although note that $0.41 of EPS was due to acquisition accounting adjustments and net significant and/or non-recurring charges.
