Stray dog menace: Inform progress of action, HC tells Kerala govt
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 18:30:00

KOCHI: The Kerala high court has ordered the state government to inform the progress in the steps taken to address the issue of stray dog menace in the state.A division bench comprising justices AK Jayasankaran Nambiar and Gopinath P directed, 'Taking note of the urgency which prompted the issuance of the Government Order on 15-09-2022 and the fact that a month has elapsed since then, we would require the Government to furnish the aforesaid report positively by the next date of hearing, 04-11-2022.
