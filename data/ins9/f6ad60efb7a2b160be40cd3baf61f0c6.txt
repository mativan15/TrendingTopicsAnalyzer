Locals upset by riot in Northland's Paihia on Saturday night
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-28 23:12:45

Up to 50 people, some armed with baseball bats and outdoor furniture, were involved in a fight in Northland's Paihia on Saturday evening. There were no serious injuries in the incident but Bay of Islands locals are appealing for those involved in the riot to care more about the community. Do you know more? Email aucklandnewsroom@stuff.co.nz Police received a number of calls from 7.30pm on Saturday about a group fighting on Williams Rd, originally starting at a business then spilling out onto the road.
