Sudha Bharadwaj, a human rights lawyer and trade unionist, recently opened up about spending time with Rhea Chakraborty in Byculla jail during the actress' trial in the drugs-related case after the demise of Sushant Singh Rajput.In an interview with a news portal, Sudha revealed that although Rhea had faced media trial and criticism on social media after the death of Sushant, she took it all sportingly, and was very friendly with inmates in Byculla jail during her time there.
