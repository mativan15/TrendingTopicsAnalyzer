BT Sport has been the home of all UFC related content in the UK since 2013 and the promotion will continue to air on the network after agreeing a multi-year extensionKhabib makes UFC title demand after Charles Oliveira's latest win BT Sport will remain the place for UK fight fans to watch their UFC content as the promotion has extended their broadcast rights deal with the network. Fans have been able to watch UFC on BT Sport since 2013. Terms of the new agreement means fans can continue to watch all pay-per-view and fight night events, plus shows such as UFC Countdown and UFC Connected on BT Sport.
