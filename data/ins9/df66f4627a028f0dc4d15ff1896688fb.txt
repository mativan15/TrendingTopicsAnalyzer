The 24/7 Automated Pizzeria Adds Chief Development Officer as Rapid Expansion ContinuesTORONTO, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- PizzaForno, one of North America's only automated pizzerias, appoints Travis Edmonson, former Vice President of Franchise Development at Mr. Gatti's Pizza and former Vice President of Franchise Sales & Development at Marco's Pizza, as Chief Development Officer.Edmonson joins as the second former Marco's Pizza business leader this year to get behind this 24/7 digital pizzeria designed to combat labor shortages, food costs and rent rising.
