PerezBy @justPEREZplayJun 1, 2022Connecticut's favorite brunch spot, Place 2 Be has now expanded into Western Mass. Their new location has officially opened in Springfield, right by the Basketball Hall of Fame in the former Samuel's Tavern space. MassLive Reporter Heather Morrison gave a sneak peek of the beautiful new location with owner Gina Luari here. When it comes to the franchise, Gina plans to add another location in New Haven, several new restaurant concepts in Hartford and plans to go NATIONWIDE in the future.
