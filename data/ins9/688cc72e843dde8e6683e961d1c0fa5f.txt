Published: 22:37 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 22:37 EDT, 14 October 2022 Jesse Williams has reportedly asked to not attend an in-person deposition regarding a lawsuit following a car accident that took place two years earlier.The Cabin In The Woods star, 41, who has been cast to reprise his role in the upcoming Broadway play titled, Take Me Out, has fears of contracting the Covid virus if he is put in a crowded court room, according to TMZ.The actor previously admitted to rear-ending driver, Paula Bruce's, vehicle in January 2020.
