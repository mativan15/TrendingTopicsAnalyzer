SMITHFIELD, N.C., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- KS Bancorp, Inc. (the 'Company') (OTCBB: KSBI), parent company of KS Bank, Inc. (the 'Bank'), announced unaudited results for the third quarter of 2022. The Company reported net income of $2.1 million or $1.86 per diluted share, for the three months ended September 30, 2022, an increase of 22.4% compared to net income of $1.7 million or $1.52 per diluted share, for the three months ended September 30, 2021.   Net interest income before the provision for loan losses for the three months ended September 30, 2022 was $5.
