Commissioners Say Deal To Prepare Financial Reports Will Save County Money
iheart.com
news
2022-10-18 21:45:00

Photo: Getty ImagesThe Richland County Commissioners have agreed to a contract with a company out of Westerville that will help prepare financial reports and save the county money. According to RichlandSource.com, the 3 year deal with Julian and Grube for just over $85,000 dollars. The company has been around since 1995 and works with over 500 Government entities in Ohio. The contract will see them put together financial reports that are used in audits and grant applications.
