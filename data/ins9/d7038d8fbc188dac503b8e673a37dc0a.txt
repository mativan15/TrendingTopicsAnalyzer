By Reuters Published: 17:03 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 17:03 EDT, 31 May 2022 JOHANNESBURG, May 31 (Reuters) - South African aviation company Comair, a franchise partner of British Airways, said on Tuesday it had suspended all flights until it secures additional funding. The company, which is under a local form of bankruptcy protection called "business rescue," said in a statement that efforts to raise the necessary capital were under way and there was reason to believe funding could be secured.
