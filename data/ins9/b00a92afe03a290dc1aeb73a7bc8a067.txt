VANCOUVER, British Columbia, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Trailbreaker Resources Ltd. (TBK.V) ('Trailbreaker' or 'the Company') announces that subject to all regulatory approvals, the Company has granted 785,000 incentive stock options to directors, officers, and certain consultants of the Company. All Options issued pursuant to this grant shall vest immediately, have a term of 5 years and are exercisable at $0.24.
