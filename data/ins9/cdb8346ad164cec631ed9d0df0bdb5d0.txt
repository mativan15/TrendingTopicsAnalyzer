October 20, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Knightscope, Inc. (Nasdaq: KSCP) ('Knightscope' or the 'Company'), a leading developer of autonomous security robots, today announced it has completed its acquisition of CASE Emergency Systems ('CASE').

'We look forward to collaboratively working with our new incoming clients to overhaul our Nation's public safety infrastructure beginning with the 7,000+ blue light towers and emergency call box devices already in network.
