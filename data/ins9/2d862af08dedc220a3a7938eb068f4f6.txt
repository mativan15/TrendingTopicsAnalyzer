It's no coincidence that the last two years of uncertainty and angst have led us to recognize top, impactful leaders in some of the hardest-hit industries through the pandemic — restaurants in 2020, health care in 2021.

This year is no different.

In the days after Covid checked in, we saw the hospitality universe rapidly, agonizingly disintegrate. Hotels suffered through empty rooms, employee furloughs and eradicated revenue. For years, they struggled to lure folks through their doors, offering new perks like keyless phone entry, contactless concierges and deep cleanings.
