Indian digital payments firm PhonePe is in talks to raise funds in a round led by General Atlantic at a valuation of $12 billion, financial news website Moneycontrol reported on Friday, citing people aware of the matter.U.S. private equity group General Atlantic is likely to lead the round with an investment of $450 million to $500 million, the report said.
