LAS VEGAS & ABERDEEN, Scotland, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Global Oracle partner Inoapps today announced a slate of new launches at Oracle Cloud World 2022. From dedicated, industry-specific cloud solutions and a focused growth offering, to innovations based on the Oracle Application Express (APEX), Oracle Visual Builder and Oracle ME (my experience) platforms, as well as significant advancements in how to optimize your Oracle technical infrastructure, Inoapps has unveiled a compelling set of advances that will provide significant enhancements for customers and the wider Oracle ecosystem.
