Northern New South Wales farmers see their wettest conditions in decades as rain keeps falling
abc.net.au
news
2022-06-02 05:01:10

Farmers throughout parts of Northern New South Wales are seeing some of their wettest conditions in decades, as winter brings more rain and the added pressure of frost.Farmers and agronomists say the New England region is the wettest they have ever seen itLivestock producers are facing animal health issues and slowed pasture growth with the saturated soilsSheep graziers are warned to keep lambs warm and dry with more wet and cold conditions on the wayAcross the New England region paddocks are boggy, pastures are struggling under the extra moisture, and stock cannot find many warm, dry spots.
