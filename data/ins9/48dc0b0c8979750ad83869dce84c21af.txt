Edinburgh's beloved Hogmanay is making a return for the first time since the Covid-19 pandemic began. The three-day street party will return to the streets of the Scottish capital for the first time since 2019, and to mark its return, the organisers are promising attendees a New Year's Eve celebration to rememberAround 30,000 people are expected to descend on Edinburgh for a weekend days of live music, fireworks, and revelry. Find out below how you can get involved in the return of Hogmanay.The world-famous street party is set to kick off the three days of merriment at 8 pm on Friday, December 30, 2022.
