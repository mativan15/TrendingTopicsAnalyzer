DPR for Indian Coast Guard Academy in Mangaluru getting ready
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 17:00:00

MANGALURU: If everything goes as per plan, the proposed state-of-the-art Indian Coast Guard Academy project at Kenjar village is likely to be ready within two years, after a detailed project report (DPR) is prepared.Inspector General Manoj Vasant Baadkar , Commander, Coast Guard Region (West), said that the Coast Guard would like to start the academy in one or two years.Baadkar was interacting with media persons on board ICGS Varaha, during his maiden visit to the Coast Guard Headquarters, Karnataka, after taking over command of the Coast Guard Region (West), on Saturday.
