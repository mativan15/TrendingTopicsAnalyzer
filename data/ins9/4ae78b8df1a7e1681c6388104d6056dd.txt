Elon Musk faced SEC questions over his timing in disclosing Twitter stake
cnn.com
tech
2022-05-27 16:56:46

New York (CNN Business)Elon Musk has faced questions from the US Securities and Exchange Commission about how and when he disclosed his large stake in Twitter last month, prior to agreeing to buy the social media company.On April 4, the same day that Musk initially revealed he had bought up a more than 9% stake in Twitter (TWTR) and become the company's largest shareholder, the SEC sent him a letter asking why he appeared to have delayed disclosing his stake in an apparent violation of securities law.
