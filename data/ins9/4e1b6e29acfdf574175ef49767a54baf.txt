WA government accelerates green hydrogen plans near Geraldton as global players dig in
abc.net.au
science
2022-10-18 05:56:04

The development of a hydrogen hub north of Geraldton is being fast tracked by the West Australian government after receiving significant interest from major global producers. Western Australia's potential to create green hydrogen has attracted global interestThe WA government planned to allocate land at Oakajee to six companiesThe state's hydrogen minister hoped to see some projects up and running by 2024WA Hydrogen Minister Alannah MacTiernan said the government would tip $5.5 million into feasibility and planning works for the Oakajee Strategic Industrial Area (SIA).
