Founder of 2021 "SEO Agency of the Year" finalist LSEO.com. Serial Entrepreneur. Best-Selling Author (over 100,000 books sold). getty Size definitely matters when it comes to marketing. I've heard it said that marketing is marketing, period, and that it doesn't matter if you're a large enterprise business or a small- to medium-sized business (SMB). At the end of the day, you still need to put your company out there. You need to create a brand, make people aware of it, and engage with the consumers who want what you're selling.
