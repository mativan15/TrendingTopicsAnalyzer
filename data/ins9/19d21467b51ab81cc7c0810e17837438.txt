Tata Cliq CEO Vikas Purohit quits
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 15:15:00

Vikas Purohit chief executive officer of Tata Cliq has quit and his resignation was announced at a recent board meet, officials close to the development said. Purohit is understood to have signed on an offer from a leading MNC in a non competing sector to head its India business, it is learnt. As part of a recent group strategy, Tata Cliq is being integrated with Tata Neu super app to made a subsidiary of Tata Digital . The integration was part of the group plan to consolidate its major ecommerce ventures under this entity.
