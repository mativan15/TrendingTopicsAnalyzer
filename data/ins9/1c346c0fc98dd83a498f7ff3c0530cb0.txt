By CBS New York Team Updated on: June 2, 2022 / 6:22 PM / CBS New York AG Tish James warns of cryptocurrency investing risks AG Tish James warns of cryptocurrency investing risks 00:31 NEW YORK -- There's a new warning from New York Attorney General Letitia James about the risk of investing in cryptocurrencies.In a statement, the Attorney General said she wanted to remind New Yorkers that even well-known cryptocurrencies can crash and wipe away investments in "the blink of an eye."Her warning comes after the crypto market took a steep dive in May, resulting in billions of dollars in losses.
