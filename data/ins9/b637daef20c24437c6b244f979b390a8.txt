Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Cornwall Council said alternative cash support will be offered to familiesEligible children will no longer receive free school meal vouchers out of term time in Cornwall.The council has decided not to extend the holiday food voucher scheme, that was set up to help low income families during the coronavirus pandemic.The government stopped providing money for the meals in 2020, with Cornwall Council among local authorities to fund vouchers through Covid recovery grants.
