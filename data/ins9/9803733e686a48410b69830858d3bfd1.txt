Joe Gebbia's commencement speech at Brookwood High school in Georgia contained the usual flourishes about following your dreams and never giving up. But the Airbnb cofounder, now worth an estimated $7.8 billion, ended his speech with a surprise: each graduating student would get a handful of Airbnb stock. 'I would like to give you a piece of my dream to help inspire yours and let you know that it is possible,' Gebbia said during his speech Wednesday night.
