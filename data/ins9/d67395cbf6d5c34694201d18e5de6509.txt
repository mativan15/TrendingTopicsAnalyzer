The battle for the future of MAGA is playing out in the Arizona Republican Senate primary, and while Donald Trump looms large, there's another big figure at play: tech billionaire Peter Thiel, who's pumped millions of dollars into the Blake Masters campaign.Following the victory of Thiel-backed Republican J.D. Vance in Ohio, Masters, the 35-year-old who co-wrote Thiel's best-selling book, 'Zero to One,' is running to realign the Republican Party with a hard-line nationalism.'Despite Trump's four years in the White House and his enduring dominance over the GOP, Trumpism as an ideology is still largely inchoate,' writes Hank Stephenson in this sweeping profile of the would-be Millenial heir to MAGA.
