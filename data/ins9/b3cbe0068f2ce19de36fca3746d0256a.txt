Oct 20, 2022 6:37 PM

David Adelman has agreed to purchase a significant portion of the 10 percent stake of the Philadelphia 76ers owned by Michael Rubin. Adelman is a Philadelphia-area native and is also leading development of the Sixers' new arena.
