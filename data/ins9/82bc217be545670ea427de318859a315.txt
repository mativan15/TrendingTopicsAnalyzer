One day maybe bonds will get back to fulfilling their old role of delivering large amounts of steady, low-risk income and stabilizing portfolios in tough times. Until then, investors will have to get creative.Since the middle of 2018, billions of dollars have flowed into a group of exchange-traded funds known as defined outcome, structured, or buffered ETFs. Investors who buy them are making a very calculated trade, giving up a shot at potential gains to limit possible losses. The tighter the downside caps, the less upside investors are able to get.
