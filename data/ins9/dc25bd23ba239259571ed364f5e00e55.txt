Archaeologists stunned by UK medieval village: 'So grisly you won't believe it'
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 12:25:27

Up and down Britain are the remains of medieval splendours, from Warwick, one of England's oldest such settlements, to Oxford, Chester to York, and Ludlow to Durham. Each locality holds snippets of the past which attract millions of visitors from around the world each year. While these towns and cities maintain at least some parts of their medieval histories, many of their buildings, walls, monuments and taverns have either been upgraded or eclipsed by new developments.There is one settlement, however, that remains almost untouched, although is difficult to spot from ground level.
