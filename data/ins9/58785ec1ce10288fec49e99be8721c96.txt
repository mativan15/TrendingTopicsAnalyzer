NEW YORK, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Gainey McKenna & Egleston announces that a class action lawsuit has been filed against Enservco Corporation ('Enservco' or the 'Company') (NYSE: ENSV) in the United States District Court for the District of Colorado on behalf of investors who purchased Enservco stock between May 13, 2021 and April 18, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'). The Complaint alleges that Defendants made materially false and misleading statements regarding the Company's business, operations, and compliance policies.
