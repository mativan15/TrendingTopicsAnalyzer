Mullen Automotive Stock Soars on ELMS Bankruptcy Deal
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 16:07:00

By Will Feuer Mullen Automotive Inc. shares surged more than 50% after the electric-vehicle startup said it gained court approval to buy Electric Last Mile Solutions Inc.'s assets out of bankruptcy. The stock leaped 53% to 33 cents a share. It's still down 94% in 2022. Mullen said it expects to pay all cash for the assets and didn't disclose the price of the deal. The assets include Electric Last Mile Solutions' factory in Mishawaka, Ind., and all inventory and intellectual property. The plant is capable of producing 50,000 vehicles a year, the company said.
