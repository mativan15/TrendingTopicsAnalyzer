Hollywood star Adam Sandler and filmmaker duo Josh and Benny Safdie are reuniting for a follow-up to their 2019 critical hit 'Uncut Gems'. According to an entertainment news outlet, Netflix has come aboard to produce and distribute the project.The details of the new movie have been kept under wraps but the team plans to shoot it in 2023.Sandler told in a recent interview that the new movie will be just as intense as 'Uncut Gems'."Once that Safdie Brothers movie starts - and I love those guys, I know we're going to dedicate ourselves into working our asses off and making sure it's as good as it can be, and I know that takes a lot of time.
