Englewood mapping tech company signs $3M deal with Czech insurers
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 20:26:00

Insurance companies in the Czech Republic are turning toward a Colorado-developed technology to help them evaluate flood risks and more accurately underwrite properties.

Multiple members of the Czech Association of Insurers signed a multiyear contract with Englewood-based Intermap Technologies (OTCQX: ITMSF) for $3.1 million for the company's flood-risk software, Aquarius RMA, Intermap announced Monday. The association underwrites 98% of the total insurance premiums in the Czech Republic.

Intermap makes technology to collect, analyze and use geospatial information for a variety of purposes, including insurance underwriting, infrastructure planning and natural resources development.
