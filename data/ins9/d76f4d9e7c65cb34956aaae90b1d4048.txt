 Many people could achieve a couple of retirement finance goals by adding a qualified longevity annuity contract (QLAC) to their IRAs. QLACs were created in regulations issued by the IRS in 2014. They're an IRS-approved way to secure a lifetime stream of income from your IRA, ensuring you never run out of income during retirement. They also can reduce required minimum distributions (RMDs) for several years. Some people use them to fund long-term care that might be needed later in retirement.
