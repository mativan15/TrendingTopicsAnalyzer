GAIL shares up 0.7% as Nifty gains
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 07:30:00

Shares of GAIL (India) Ltd. gained 0.7 per cent to Rs 143.8 in Monday's trade. It hit a high of Rs 146.0 and low of Rs 141.55 during the session. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 147.56, while the 50-DMA was at Rs 155.71. If a stock trades above 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is upward. On the other hand, if the stock trades below 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it is considered a bearish trend and if trades between these averages, then it suggests the stock can go either way.
