HDFC Life Q2 Results: Profit rises 19% YoY to Rs 326 crore, misses estimates
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 09:00:00

Co on Friday reported a 19% year-on-year (YoY) rise in net profit for the quarter ended September 2022 to Rs 326 crore. This was lower than ET NOW poll estimate of Rs 345 crore.Net premium income for the quarter rose nearly 15% on year to Rs 13,111 crore.As on September 30, the insurance firm's solvency ratio stood at 210%, compared with 183% a quarter ago.The company continued to maintain a steady growth trajectory, with annualized premium equivalent (APE) growing by 11% in April-September, excluding Exide Life.
