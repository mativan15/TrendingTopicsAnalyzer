SC dismisses TMC MLA's plea against arrest in job scam
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 03:45:00

NEW DELHI: The Supreme Court on Thursday dismissed the plea of Trinamool Congress ( TMC ) functionary and former chairman of the West Bengal Board of Primary Education, Manik Bhattacharya , challenging his arrest by Enforcement Directorate in a money laundering case in the teacher recruitment scam.The bench of Justices Aniruddha Bose and Vikram Nath refused to grant him relief and turned down his plea. Bhattacharya, in his plea, said that he was arrested by ED when the protection granted by the Court in the CBI case was operative.
