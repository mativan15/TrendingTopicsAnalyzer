By Reuters Published: 12:00 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 12:00 EDT, 17 October 2022 The Portland Trail Blazers agreed to a four-year, $28 million extension with forward Nassir Little, ESPN reported. Little and the 2019 draft class faced a deadline of Monday at 6 p.m. ET to sign a contract extension. He set career highs with 9.8 points, 5.6 rebounds and 1.3 assists in 42 games (23 starts) in 2021-22. Little has averaged 5.8 points, 3.4 rebounds and 16.7 minutes in 138 games (30 starts) since Portland drafted him in the first round (25th overall) in 2019 out of North Carolina.
