Delhi University releases simulated list for undergraduate admissions, two days to change choices
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 06:30:00

The University of Delhi (DU) has released the first simulated list for undergraduate programmes on its website. The list is for the Undergraduate Admissions 2022-23. Candidates who have registered for the DU Admissions 2022 can download the list from the official website, du.ac.in or on admissions.uod.ac.in. As per the schedule, after the release of the simulated list, Delhi University will release the first merit list. Candidates will be given the chance the to edit their choices before the allotment procedure.
