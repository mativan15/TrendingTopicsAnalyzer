June 02, 2022 03:47 PM Eastern Daylight Time FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ShipMonk, a leading 3PL fulfillment service announced the launch of a new strategic partnership and direct integration with Gorgias, a leading help desk platform for high-growth eCommerce brands. This partnership is designed to provide a fully integrated software and fulfillment platform with customer service solutions, focusing on complete transparency and ease of use for both the client and end-consumer.

Leveraging both ShipMonk's 3PL platform with Gorgias' support platform, the combined offering helps merchants optimize critical aspects of operations.
