The dispute is taking place at the Atomic Weapons Establishment in Aldermaston, BerksThe wage hike dispute is taking place at the Atomic Weapons Establishment, AldermastonWorkers at Britain's top secret nuclear weapons base could go on strike in a row over pay. Staff at the Atomic Weapons Establishment in Aldermaston, Berks, are being balloted on whether to stage industrial action. Employees at the research facility are responsible for the design, manufacture and support of warheads for the UK's nuclear weapons, carried by the Trident missile system on board Royal Navy Vanguard submarines.
