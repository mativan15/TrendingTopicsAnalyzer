RT.com 5th June 2022, 20:13 GMT+10The Tesla owner's fortune shrank by nearly $17 billion in one day after his plans to cut jobs were leakedThe world's richest man, Elon Musk, saw his fortune shrink by $16.9 billion on Friday, Forbes reports.Earlier this week, reports emerged of Musk's plans to lay off 10% of Tesla's employees and suspend hiring, citing the CEO's leaked letter to senior management. As a result, Tesla shares dropped 9.2%, bringing his fortune down as well.According to the media outlet, his fortune is now estimated at $216.
