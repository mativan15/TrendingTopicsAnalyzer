The Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA) of South Africa has announced that crypto assets will now be classified as financial products. This means that these assets will be subject to regulation by the FSCA. Crypto assets are now officially regulated in South Africa. The Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA) has classified them as 'a digital representation of value' and mandated that they be treated as financial products from the publication date.
