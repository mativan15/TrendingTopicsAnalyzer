Big banks all aboard amid stability drive, increasing liquidity

By JIANG XUEQING | China Daily | Updated: 2022-06-02 10:09 CCB's booth is seen during a services expo in Beijing in September. [Photo provided to China Daily] Several large and medium-sized banks in China made arrangements recently to ramp up credit issuance in an effort to stabilize the economy and keep economic activity within a reasonable range. Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, the country's largest State-owned commercial lender by assets, will implement policies launched by central and local governments, make full use of various policy instruments and financial products, and focus on key regions, key fields, key industries and weak links in the economy.
