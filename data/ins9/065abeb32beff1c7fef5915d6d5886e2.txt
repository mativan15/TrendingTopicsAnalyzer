By Pierre Bertrand ABB Ltd is scheduled to report third-quarter results on Thursday. Here's what you need to know: REVENUE FORECAST: The Swiss industrial company is expected to report $7.38 billion in third-quarter revenue according to company-provided analysts' consensus, a roughly 5% increase compared with the same period a year prior. NET INCOME: Net income for the quarter to the end of September is expected at $444 million, according to the consensus, down nearly 32% compared with the $652 million in the prior-year period.
