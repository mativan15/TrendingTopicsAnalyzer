Washington raises jailing of US citizen with Saudis
irishtimes.com
world
2022-10-19 00:00:00

The US State Department confirmed a Saudi-American citizen has been jailed in Saudi Arabia and said Washington has repeatedly raised its concerns over his detention.Saudi Arabia in early October handed a 16-year prison sentence to Saudi-American Saad Ibrahim Almadi, the Washington Post reported late on Monday, over his tweets critical of the Saudi government.Almadi was charged with 'harboring a terrorist ideology, trying to destabilise the Kingdom, as well as supporting and funding terrorism,' the Post reported.
