LIC slides 3% as it reports 17% YoY fall in Q4 profit
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 06:15:00

AgenciesLIC's share price fell to a low of Rs 810 as against Rs 837.05 at the previous close on the BSE. The stock was last trading 2.3 per cent lower.Shares of Life Insurance Corporation of India () declined 3.2 per cent in early trade on Tuesday as the insurance behemoth posted a 17 per cent decline in consolidated net profit to Rs 2,409 crore for the fourth quarter ended March 2022. The insurer had earned a profit of Rs 2,917 crore in the same quarter a year ago. The country's largest insurer, whose debut on the bourses earlier this month marked India's largest ever initial public offering, said that its net premium income stood at Rs 1,44,158.
