Vaishali Takkar death case: Ex-boyfriend Rahul Navlani gets arrested for abetting the actor's suicide
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-20 05:38:50

In the latest development in actress Vaishali Takkar's suicide case, her ex-boyfriend and neighbour Rahul Navlani was arrested by the police. Indore Police Commissioner while speaking with anews channel said, "The accused was a neighbour so he knew about the goings on and managed to escape...Teams were sent to different states, we even announced a reward. Lookout circular was issued. He is being questioned." In her suicide note, Vaishali demanded punishment for Rahul and his family for mentally harassing her for 2.
