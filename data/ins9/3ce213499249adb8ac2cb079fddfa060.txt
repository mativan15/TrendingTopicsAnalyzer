Washington D.C., Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) --

WHAT: SBA's Elevating Small Business Series Webinar: Celebrating National Disability Employment Awareness Month.

In celebration of National Disability Employment Awareness Month, the U.S. Small Business Administration will host a live online small business roundtable with Shari Thomas, Chief Diversity Officer, SBA's Office of Diversity, Inclusion and Civil Rights; and small business owners Suzanne Foley of Port City Pretzels in Portsmouth, N.H.
