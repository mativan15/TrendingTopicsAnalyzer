June 02, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time PHILADELPHIA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AmeriHealth Caritas, a national leader in Medicaid managed care and other solutions for those most in need, announced that two of its leaders were named to Modern Healthcare's Top Diversity and Top Emerging Leaders lists. Yavar Moghimi, M.D., chief psychiatric medical officer for AmeriHealth Caritas District of Columbia, was named a Top Diversity Leader. Mairead McInerney, corporate director of population health-wellness coordination and member experience, was honored as one of Modern Healthcare's Top Emerging Leaders.
