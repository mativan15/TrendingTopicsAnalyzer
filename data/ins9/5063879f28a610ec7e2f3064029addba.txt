Published: 17:01 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 17:01 EDT, 4 June 2022 His career and reputation were resting on a knife-edge, but as a US jury pondered the lurid accusations of domestic abuse surrounding Johnny Depp, the star was already out celebrating, The Mail on Sunday can reveal.On Tuesday night, hours before the vicious libel battle with ex-wife Amber Heard drew to a dramatic finale, the Hollywood A-lister threw a lavish party in London that had all the hallmarks of a victory bash.Revellers at the exclusive members' club Laylow in trendy Notting Hill – including Depp's star witness, supermodel Kate Moss – racked up a bill thought to be in the region of £250,000 as drinking and dancing to 'really hard techno' continued until 5am.
