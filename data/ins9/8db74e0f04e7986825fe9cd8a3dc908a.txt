After NCT DREAM hit a career milestone with two nights in South Korea's largest concert venue, the K-pop boy band is sharing the experience with fans worldwide through their first-ever movie.

NCT DREAM THE MOVIE : In a DREAM will be released worldwide this November. The feature film will spotlight the group on stage and backstage at their Olympic Stadium show in Seoul from September earlier this year. Fans will also get to see the preparations that went into the concert and interviews with all seven members of NCT DREAM.
