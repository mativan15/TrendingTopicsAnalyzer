Anticipating the rejection of nomination papers filed by Uddhav Thackeray-led Shiv Sena faction's Rutuja Latke for the Andheri (East) Assembly by-election, the party's Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) councillor Sandeep Raju Naik too have filed election papers to meet any eventuality. With this, the total number of nominations filed for the forthcoming elections has reached 25, which includes two sets by Bharatiya Janata Party candidate Murji Patel and eight independent candidates.'We are afraid of any mischief played by the other group (Eknath Shinde-BJP) as they are now running the government.
