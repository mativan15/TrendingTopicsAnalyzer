How a former U.S. Marine jailed in Venezuela for 2 years fought for his freedom

news
2022-10-21 20:49:53

WASHINGTON

At Venezuela's most notorious prison, in a section known as the 'House of Dreams,' military intelligence officers torture inmates in the hope of breaking their spirit. But Matthew Heath refused to break.

Nearly two years into his detention, the American Marine Corps veteran was still resisting his captors, breaking cameras and microphones that littered prison corners and talking back to his guards. Early on he had refused to attend sham court hearings in protest of his prison conditions.
