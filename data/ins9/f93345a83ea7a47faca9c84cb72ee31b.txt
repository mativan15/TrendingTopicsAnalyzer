We've seen the en masse 'Great Resignation,' with employees reassessing their careers and making bolder-then-usual moves to new jobs. They are not alone — their employers are also re-assessing their goals and purpose. Call it the 'great dispersion,' that's prompting leaders in every industry to question and reassess their business paths. That's the word from Scott Galloway, author and professor of marketing at New York University, speaking at Infosys' recent Cobalt event, where he provided his predictions on going forward into the digital — or post-digital — 2020s.
