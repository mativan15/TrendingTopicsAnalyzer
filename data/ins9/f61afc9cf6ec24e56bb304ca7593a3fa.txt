Truss has proved herself to be a poor imitation of Mrs Thatcher
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 17:32:00

For some years now Liz Truss has claimed the mantle of Margaret Thatcher, appealing to Conservatives who rightly believe that we need a champion of enterprise more than ever. It carried particular resonance with me, as one of the few people left who started their own business in the sixties and worked and suffered throughout the whole of the depressing, strike-bound seventies, until we were all rescued by Mrs Thatcher.
I then spent the eighties in government, serving Mrs Thatcher. When I returned to the world of business, it was transformed.
