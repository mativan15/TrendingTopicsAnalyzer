ReWalk Robotics stock rises on $8M stock repurchase plan (NASDAQ:RWLK)
seekingalpha.com
business
2022-06-02 12:14:42

ReWalk Robotics (NASDAQ:RWLK) has announced a stock repurchase program to buyback up to $8M of its ordinary shares.
The program is subject to receipt of court approval, and ReWalk has filed a motion requesting permission to make purchases for a period of six months following the date of the court's authorization.
RWLK shares up 10% pre-market
