Mum amazed to give birth to twins born in different months

2022-10-21 08:25:00

Hayley Burgess is pictured with her newborn Charlie (Image: Supplied)A mother gave birth to premature twins in two different months.Hayley Burgess, 34, welcomed babies Charlie and Layla nine weeks early. She gave birth to Charlie at 11.30pm on September 30 and shortly after, Layla was born just after midnight, meaning her birthday will be on October 1, a different month.Hayley, from Birkenhead, Merseyside, said: "I came in at 9.10pm and by 12.10am in the morning, I'd had both of them. The midwife was great, she stayed up with me all night speaking to me.
