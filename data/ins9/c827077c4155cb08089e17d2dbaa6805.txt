AgenciesGail is India's leading gas trading, transmission and petrochemicals company with 70% share of the gas transmission marketLenders have issued a letter of intent to , giving a go-ahead to the company to take over the debt-laden JBF Petrochemicals through the insolvency process.
 The letter of intent was issued on Sunday, following which has to deposit 10% of its bid value or about Rs 200 crore as bank guarantee by Wednesday, said people familiar with the process. The takeover of Petrochemicals by Gail will be a rare instance of a public sector enterprise buying out a distressed company since the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code came into force in 2016.
