Conestoga College to open creative industries campus in downtown Kitchener
globalnews.ca
news
2022-05-27 19:28:51

The City of Kitchener and Conestoga College say they are teaming up to expand the post-secondary institution's school of creative industries.
The city says the expansion will make a number of innovative programs available to students, including game art, artificial intelligence in design, visual design, special effects, virtual reality merchandising, motion graphics and esport.It believes that this will attract new talent to Kitchener for the area's burgeoning tech industry as Conestoga doubles the number of program offerings across various fields.
