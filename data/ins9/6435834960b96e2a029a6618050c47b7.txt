Tech Flat on Appetite for Mega Cap Stocks -- Tech Roundup
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 21:15:00

Shares of technology companies were flat as mega cap stocks held recent gains. Shares of electric-car maker Tesla fell sharply as the automaker cut its full-year sales projections, reflecting, in part, changes to vehicle-shipping patterns.
