The institutions have been putting pressure on Whirlpool (NYSE: WHR) stock and that may not change now the Q3 results are in. The company's report and outlook confirm the notion of a slowdown in the housing sector that will have repercussions for more than just Whirlpool. As for the institutions, they've been net sellers for the last two quarters going into Q4 as well and the activity has been on the heavy side. Selling in Q3 alone outpaced buying by an amount equal to 3% of the market cap with shares trading near $131 and this pace may continue.
