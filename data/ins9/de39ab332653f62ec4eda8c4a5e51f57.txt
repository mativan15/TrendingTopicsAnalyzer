October 19, 2022 04:01 PM Eastern Daylight Time PHOENIX--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Knight-Swift Transportation Holdings Inc. (NYSE:KNX), one of North America's largest and most diversified freight transportation companies, has released its earnings for the quarter ended September 30, 2022. The release is currently available on the Knight-Swift investor relations website: https://investor.knight-swift.com/ and will be filed with the SEC on a Form 8-K.

The company will hold a conference call this afternoon at 4:30 PM EDT to further discuss its results of operations for the quarter ended September 30, 2022.
