By Madeline Bartos May 31, 2022 / 12:57 PM / CBS Pittsburgh CBS News Pittsburgh Live ALIQUIPPA, Pa. (KDKA) - Three Beaver County Jail corrections officers are accused of selling drugs and contraband to inmates. Raphael Abercrombie, Richard Suman and Erin Huff are facing charges after the warden asked police to investigate reports of correction officers bringing contraband into the jail. According to the criminal complaints, the warden told police they had confiscated needles, lighters and tobacco from inmates and several had tested positive for drugs.
