Price in flux: Inflation compels hikes from Portland's small businesses
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 20:48:00

The hardships of 2020 are hardly past, and small businesses now face another setback: inflation.

Supplies, labor, food, gas, rent, and interest rates are all higher. Small businesses' prices for their goods and services? Two out of three have raised prices during the past 12 months to meet these rising expenses, while others wait to see how long they can hold out, often out of concern for their customers.

Price increases are a necessity of running a small business. It's not just that costs have gone up: Business has fundamentally changed.
