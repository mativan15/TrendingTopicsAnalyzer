By Laurence Brahm | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-21 10:57 The fifth session of the 13th National People's Congress opens at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing, March 5, 2022. [Photo/Xinhua] General Secretary Xi Jinping's speech at the opening ceremony of the 20th CPC National Congress set forth a long-term strategic blueprint for China to navigate its own development in a rapidly changing and extremely volatile and unpredictable world. After a clear review of China's stages of reforms since its opening-up in 1979, Xi acknowledged past accomplishments, but recognized the challenges of our present time and the systemic global risks of the future.
