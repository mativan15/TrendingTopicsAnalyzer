Over a month passes with no sign of Arunachal natives who went missing at Chaglagam circle
indiatimes.com

2022-10-15 15:15:00

IANSTwo Arunachal youths missing from India-China border for 52 daysIt's more than a month two natives of Arunachal Pradesh who are residents of Duiliang village under Hayuliang-Goiliang circle of Anjaw district of Arunachal Pradesh have been missing from Chaglagam circle in Indo-China border. The duo Bateilum Tikro and his friend Bayingso Manyu is missing since August 24 last. 
Dishanso Chikro, younger brother of Tikro in a letter to Hayuliang assembly constituency MLA Dasanglu Pul stated that they left their village on August 19 in search of herb at high altitude hills along the line of actual control (LAC).
