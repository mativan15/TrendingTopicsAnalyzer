Dublin, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Crowd Analytics Market By Solutions, By Deployment Mode, By Application, By End User: Global Opportunity Analysis and Industry Forecast, 2020-2030" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.

Process of evaluating data on the natural movement of people or objects is known as crowd analytics.
