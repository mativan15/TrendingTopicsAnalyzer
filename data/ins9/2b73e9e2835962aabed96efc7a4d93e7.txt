Suella Braverman has resigned as home secretary, criticising prime minister Liz Truss while explaining why she has gone.The popular figure among the Tory right told Ms Truss she had made a "technical infringement" of the rules by sending an official document from a personal email and was now taking responsibility.
This is her letter in full:Dear Prime Minister,It is with the greatest regret that I am choosing to tender my resignation.
Earlier today, I sent an official document from my personal email to a trusted parliamentary colleague as part of policy engagement, and with the aim of garnering support for government policy on migration.
