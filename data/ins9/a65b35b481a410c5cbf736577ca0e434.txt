This is the most opportune time to get into a business in India: Anupriya Patel
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 05:30:00

PTIAnupriya Patel added that the portal would help SMEs and startups to leverage the ecommerce platform and join India's exports in the march towards becoming self-reliant.Calling this the most opportune time to get into a business in India, Anupriya Patel, Union Minister of State for Commerce and Industry, said a lot of efforts were being made to create better opportunities for businesses. Adding how India had implemented the Free Trade Agreement (FTA) with the UAE, she said FTAs with others such as the UK and the EU were being expedited.
