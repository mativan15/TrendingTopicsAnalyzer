By Mary Mrad For Daily Mail Australia Published: 21:46 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 21:54 EDT, 5 June 2022 Tom Hanks was the star of the show at Baz Luhrmann's Elvis premiere in Sydney and the Gold Coast over the weekend. And on Monday, the actor, 65, who plays Elvis' manager Colonel Tom Parker in the movie, jetted out of Sydney Airport after attending the premiere.The Philadelphia Oscar winner kept a low profile in sunglasses and a face mask as he arrived to the airport.
