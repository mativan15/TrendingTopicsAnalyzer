(Source photos by Getty Images and Yuki Kohara) KIM JAEWON, Nikkei staff writerOctober 19, 2022 12:30 JST | South KoreaSEOUL -- Namkoong Whon, one of South Korean internet giant Kakao's two CEOs, resigned on Wednesday as the company faced harsh criticism from the government and public over the handling of a fire at a data center over the weekend that caused a massive service outage and led investors to dump shares.
