Berlusconi 'rekindles' Putin friendship with 'sweet letter' and gifts
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 22:29:00

'I have rekindled relations with President Putin. For my birthday he sent me 20 bottles of vodka and a very sweet letter,' Mr Berlusconi is heard to say in the audio, which was released by La Presse news agency. It was said to have been recorded on Tuesday.
'I responded with bottles of Lambrusco and an equally lovely letter. I was declared the number one among his five true friends.'
Mr Berlusconi, who turned 86 on September 29, said he was deeply worried by the progress of the war.
'Russian ministers have said on many occasions that we are at war with them because we are supplying arms and money to Ukraine.
