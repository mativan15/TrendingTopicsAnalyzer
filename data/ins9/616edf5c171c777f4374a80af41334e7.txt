Jharkhand CM unveils 42 projects in Chatra, lays stones of 109

2022-10-20 08:15:00

CHATRA: Continuing with his tours to districts as part of the ' Aapki Yojana , Aapki Sarkar , Aapke Dwar ' campaign, chief minister Hemant Soren on Wednesday visited Chatra, where he laid foundation stones and inaugurated projects worth Rs 220 crore.Out of these, foundation stones for 109 projects worth Rs 187 crore were laid and 42 projects were inaugurated.He also announced that a new collectorate building and a dairy plant will be set up in the district soon.CM Soren said, "The government is working 24X7 to sort out issues of the people.
