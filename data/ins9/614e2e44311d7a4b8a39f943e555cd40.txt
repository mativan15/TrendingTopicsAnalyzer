2022-05-30 06:59:00

After two years of border closures replete with a host of quarantine and travel restrictions, free travel has finally returned, to most places, for the vaccinated. Those who choose to travel now will also benefit from many destinations' removal of testing requirements, making at least some trips more convenient and less expensive than they were. Reservations have surged to the point that Booking.com said April 2022 was the first month in which global room bookings exceeded 2019 levels. Still, health protocols remain in many nations and the rules continue to shift.
