June 02, 2022 03:18 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Class: Shareholder rights law firm Robbins LLP informs investors that a shareholder filed a class action on behalf of all persons and entities that purchased or otherwise acquired IonQ (NYSE: IOQ) securities between March 30, 201 and May 2, 2022, for violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. IonQ purports to "develop quantum computers designed to solve the world's most complex problems."
