Erik ten Hag still wants Man Utd to bid for Frenkie de Jong (Image: GETTY)Reports claim Erik ten Hag wants Manchester United to try their luck with a formal bid for Frenkie de Jong - despite the midfielder confirming his desire to stay at Barcelona this summer. The Dutch international has now outlined his plan to stay in Catalonia next season following several weeks of intense transfer speculation.De Jong is considered to be one of Ten Hag's top transfer targets but as of now, he does not want to jump ship and leave Barcelona.
