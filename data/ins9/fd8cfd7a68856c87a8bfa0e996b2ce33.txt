William H. Macy Joins ‘The Conners' Season 5 as Guest Star
variety.com
news
2022-10-20 16:00:00

William H. Macy has been cast as a guest star in Season 5 of 'The Conners.' He will play Smitty, one of Dan's (John Goodman) best buds from high school, who pays a visit to Lanford. Macy is known for starring in films such as 'Magnolia,' 'Boogie Nights,' 'Jurassic Park III' and Fargo. His most prominent TV credit was starring as Frank Gallagher for all 11 seasons of 'Shameless' on Showtime, for which he earned six lead comedy actor Emmy nominations. His other small screen projects including 'ER' and 'Door to Door' — which won him an Emmy — and voice roles on 'Curious George' and 'The Lionhearts.
