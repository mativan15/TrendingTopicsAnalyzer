Man duped of 1.5cr in cryptocurrency fraud
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-29 03:15:00

Mumbai: A Malabar Hill resident has been duped of Rs 1.53 crore by cyberfraudsters promising good returns on investment in cryptocurrency via a fake website. The complainant, who works in financial accounts, lodged an FIR of cheating and forgery at Malabar Hill police station. He told the police that in 2018, while he was in Australia, he had opened an account on a crypto exchange trading platform and had transferred money in AUD. He had also opened an account on another crypto exchange platform.
