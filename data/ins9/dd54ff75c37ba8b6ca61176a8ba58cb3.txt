 Image: Stock.adobe.com A largely unpredictable post-pandemic housing market is cooling, and a drop in demand means the beginning of a housing correction, according to Moody's Analytics chief economist Mark Zandi. New home sales in April and May dropped 19% to their lowest level since 2020, and sellers are responding. Roughly 19% of all home listings experienced a price reduction in the last month, causing home price growth to plateau after consecutive gains during the pandemic.
