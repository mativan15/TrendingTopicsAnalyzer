May 31, 2022 08:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time IRVINE, Calif--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jackie Dowd has joined Alliant Insurance Services as Senior Vice President within its Employee Benefits Group in New York. Dowd joins Alliant with an extensive background working with complex employer groups and will design and implement strategic employee benefits solutions to enhance business outcomes and increase productivity for a diverse client base.



'Jackie's experience delivering multifaceted benefits solutions for clients across a broad range of industries will serve as a powerful resource to our team and our clients,' said Kevin Overbey, President, Alliant Employee Benefits.
