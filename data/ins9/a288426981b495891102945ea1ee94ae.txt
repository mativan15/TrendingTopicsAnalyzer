ReutersThe death toll in the present wave of flood has reached 38 with five people died in landslides and rest in floods.Guwahati: Flood situation in Assam has improved, with only five districts in the state reeling under flood now. Pre monsoon flooding had affected a large part of Assam. The death toll in the present wave of flood has reached 38 with five people died in landslides and rest in floods. Embankments, roads, bridges and other infrastructure have been damaged by floodwaters in Cachar, Dima Hasao and Udalguri.
