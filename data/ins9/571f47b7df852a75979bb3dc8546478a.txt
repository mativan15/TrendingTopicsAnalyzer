Mohawk to Buy Vitromex Ceramic Tile Business for $293 Million >MHK
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 12:41:00

By Colin Kellaher Mohawk Industries Inc. on Friday said it agreed to buy the Vitromex ceramic-tile business from Mexico's Grupo Industrial Saltillo for about $293 million in cash. Mohawk, a Calhoun, Ga., maker of residential and commercial flooring products, said Vitromex has four manufacturing plants in Mexico and generated revenue of about $204 million last year. Mohawk, with 2021 sales topping $11 billion, said it expects to complete the acquisition in the second half of the year. Write to Colin Kellaher at colin.
