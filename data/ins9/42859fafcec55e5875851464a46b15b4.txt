Prosecutors' corruption investigation increasingly aimed at opposition leader SEOUL, Oct. 20 (Yonhap) -- The prosecution's yearlong investigation into alleged corruption in land development projects south of Seoul appears to be heading toward opposition leader Lee Jae-myung's presidential election campaign funds, political watchers said Thursday. The move appears to be palpable following Wednesday's detention of a Democratic Party (DP) politician known as one of Lee's closest aides.
