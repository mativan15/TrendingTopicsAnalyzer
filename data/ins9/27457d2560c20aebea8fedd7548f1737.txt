PEARL RIVER, N.Y., October 21, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Acorda Therapeutics, Inc. (Nasdaq: ACOR) will hold a virtual, video Q&A with its CEO, Ron Cohen, M.D., on Friday, October 28 at 1:00pm ET, 10:00am PT.

The call will review the items on the ballot for Acorda's Special Meeting of Shareholders on November 4, 2022. Participants will be able to submit questions to Dr. Cohen, which he will address live.
