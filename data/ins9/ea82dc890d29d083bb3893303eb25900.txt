If you're an adult who loves dressing up for Halloween, a large part of the appeal is the look of approval and delight on the faces of fellow partygoers when you see them recognize the reference behind your costume. Dressing up as a moment or figure in pop culture, whether it's Pizza Rat or the late Queen Elizabeth II, also makes for an easy conversation starter at Halloween parties — and if your costume is well executed, it might even win you a prize. So as we do every year, we've put together a list of the best pop-culture costume ideas of 2022 from the most glamorous of pregnant celebrities to everyone's favorite short-king chef, complete with the products you'll need to pull them off.
