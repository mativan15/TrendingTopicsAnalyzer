Published October 19, 2022 9:24am EDT If you're a renter, your personal possessions are just as important as a homeowner's, but did you know they may not be covered? Your landlord likely has a policy on your unit, but that policy doesn't protect your belongings, your liability, or provide you with a place to live if your unit is damaged. Renters insurance is an important insurance policy that protects your belongings, can limit your liability, and can even provide you money to stay elsewhere if your current place is uninhabitable.
