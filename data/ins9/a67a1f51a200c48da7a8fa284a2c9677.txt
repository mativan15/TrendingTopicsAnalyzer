Outrage as Costa Coffee increases prices by an average of 14p

Published: 08:34 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 08:45 EDT, 3 June 2022 Social media users have expressed outrage after Costa Coffee increased the price of its drinks for a second time in six months. Drinks such as a flat white have increased from £2.95 to £3.30 while the popular medium skinny latte has risen from £3.10 to £3.35. A small cappuccino has increased from £3.15 to £3.50. The retailer said the price of drinks can vary from store to store. Also the increase depends on the type of drink and its size.
