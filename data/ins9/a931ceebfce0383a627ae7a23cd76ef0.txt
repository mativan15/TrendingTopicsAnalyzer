TULSA, Okla., October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cherokee Federal, a division of Cherokee Nation Businesses (CNB), today announced the hiring of Clint Bickett as Chief Operating Officer.

Cherokee Federal hires Clint Bickett as Chief Operating Officer to support the organization's aggressive growth plans. (Photo: Business Wire)

With more than 20 years of c-suite experience, Bickett is well poised to support Cherokee Federal's aggressive growth plans, delivery of best-in-class solutions and overall expansion within the federal marketplace.
