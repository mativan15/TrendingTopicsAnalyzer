VANCOUVER, BC / ACCESSWIRE / June 2, 2022 / AMPD Ventures Inc. ("AMPD" or the "Company"), (CSE:AMPD);(OTCQB:AMPDF);(FRA:2Q0) subsidiary Departure Lounge Inc., a Vancouver, BC-based company offering a range of Metaverse-related technologies and creative services ("Departure Lounge") is pleased to announce that it has entered into a representation and reseller agreement with ARwall, a Los Angeles-based provider of LED wall virtual production and immersive XR technologies and software ("ARwall").

Announcement Highlights

ARwall is a mixed reality and augmented reality company offering services in motion picture virtual production effects, interactive experiences, and location-based amusements.
