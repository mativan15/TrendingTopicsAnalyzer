WSJ flags well-timed trades in Washington that coincided with the pandemic
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-20 09:52:23

honglouwawa More investigative journalism from the Wall Street Journal is reporting on how federal officials working on the government response to COVID-19 made some "well-timed financial trades" when markets tanked and rallied at the beginning of the pandemic. In fact, March 2020 was the most active for trading by officials across the federal government, including the Department of Health and Human Services, while some officials even started trading in January 2020, when the U.S. public was largely unaware of the threat posed by the coronavirus.
