Inflation Is High, Why Do Republicans Want You to Pay More for Medicine?
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 11:07:01

Americans just won the long-fought battle to finally allow Medicare to negotiate the soaring cost of prescription drugs this August, but extreme Republicans are already working to put money back in Big Pharma's pocket by reversing this huge achievement. Just recently, Senators Mike Lee of Utah, Marco Rubio of Florida, James Lankford of Oklahoma, and Cynthia Lummis of Wyoming introduced a billto kill Medicare negotiation of drug prices.This is not what Americans want. In fact, every time we've hosted town hall meetings over the last few years, we've heard from constituents about the struggle to afford lifesaving prescription drugs and even the difficult choices they face between taking their medicine as directed or paying for rent or food.
