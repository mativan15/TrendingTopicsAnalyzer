Shutterstock.comRSI has turned north from the 60 level, confirming bullishness.NEW DELHI: The Nifty Auto index traded positive around 10:39AM(IST)on Tuesday in an upbeat market. TVS Motor Company(up 3.51 per cent), Mahindra & Mahindra(up 2.76 per cent), Tata Motors(up 1.62 per cent), Maruti Suzuki(up 1.41 per cent) and Hero MotoCorp(up 1.38 per cent) were among the top gainers. Kubota(down 0.14 per cent) were the top losers on the index. The Nifty Auto index was up 1.46 per cent at 12745.9 at the time of writing this report.
