Red Wing Shoe Co. names Allison Gettings as first woman CEO
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 16:53:00

Red Wing Shoe Co. Inc. announced Thursday that Allison Gettings will be the footwear manufacturer's next CEO.

The Red Wing, Minnesota-based company's board of directors' selected Gettings, currently the company's president, to take over for the soon-retiring CEO Mark Urdahl. Gettings, who will also continue on as president, will assume the new role Jan. 1.

Urdahl has served as CEO since 2016. His successor will be Red Wing Shoe's 10th CEO and first woman to lead the 117-year-old company. Gettings has worked for the company a total of 14 years, beginning in 2006.
