Cryptos and blockchains to shape global payments scene going forward : Alexander Mamasidikov
indiatimes.com
business
2022-06-03 03:45:00

The new age financial sector is going through a dynamic transformation as the existing system is not enough to satisfy the growing customer demand. A number of people have diverted towards crypto assets which are acknowledging these needs.It is a popular opinion among the fanatics that cryptos and blockchain will play a prominent role in the future of payments as the financial market reaches the next step in its evolution. Alexander Mamasidikov, co-founder of MinePlex, a new-generation mobile digital bank that utilises crypto in its services, shared his opinion on this notion among others.
