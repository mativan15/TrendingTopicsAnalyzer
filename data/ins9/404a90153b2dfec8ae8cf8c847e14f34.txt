October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time OSHKOSH, Wis.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--When the Tempe Black Employee Alliance (TBEA), an affiliate of the City of Tempe, wanted to raise awareness for its organization, it decided to go with reusable grocery totes. The practical gifts helped build brand awareness at a local community fair. The organization is among a growing number focusing on sustainability initiatives. That's the cover story in this issue of amplify®, a digital magazine published quarterly by promotional products retailer 4imprint.
