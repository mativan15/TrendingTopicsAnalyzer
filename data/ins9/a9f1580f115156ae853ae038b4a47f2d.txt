Tyler Weitzman, Co-Founder of Speechify, accepted into Forbes Business Council
prweb.com
tech
2022-10-15 00:00:00

LONDON (PRWEB) October 15, 2022 Tyler Weitzman, Co-Founder of Speechify, the #1 text-to-speech app on iOS and Google Chrome, has been accepted into the Forbes Business Council, the foremost growth and networking organization for successful business owners and leaders worldwide. Tyler Weitzman was vetted and selected by a review committee based on the depth and diversity of his experience. Criteria for acceptance include a track record of successfully impacting business growth metrics, as well as personal and professional achievements and honors.
