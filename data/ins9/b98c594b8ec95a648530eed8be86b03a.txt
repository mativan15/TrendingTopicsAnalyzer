FREMONT, Calif., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Amprius Technologies, Inc. ("Amprius") (NYSE: AMPX), a leader in ultra-high energy density lithium-ion batteries with its Silicon Nanowire Anode Platform, today announced that the company received the first large-scale anode production machine from centrotherm, which it plans to install at its current Fremont location. This machine is expected to increase Amprius' capacity for silicon anode production to approximately 2 MWhs, or ten times its current capacity, by year-end 2023.
