CHARLOTTE, N.C., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- LPL Financial LLC announced today that financial advisor Chris Albright of Astra Wealth Advisors has joined LPL Financial's broker-dealer, RIA and custodial platforms. He reported having served approximately $192 million in advisory, brokerage and retirement assets*, and joins LPL from Wells Fargo Financial Advisors Network. With the move, Albright will partner with current LPL affiliated advisor Kimberly Santarsiero in an effort to strengthen his practice and expand services to clients.
