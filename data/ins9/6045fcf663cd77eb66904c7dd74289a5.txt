Published: 11:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 11:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 CLEMSON, S.C. (AP) - Clemson coach Brad Brownell looks around at ACC men's basketball meetings and sees fewer and fewer faces who've been doing it as long as him. Brownell is entering his 13th season with the Tigers - only Leonard Hamilton of Florida State and Tony Bennett of Virginia have held positions in the Atlantic Coast Conference longer - and hopes the season doesn't become a game-by-game referendum on his job status.
