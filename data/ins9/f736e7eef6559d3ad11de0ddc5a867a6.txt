Inpixon Shares Drop 38% After Direct Offering Prices
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 16:01:00

By Chris Wack Inpixon shares were down 38%, to $5.65, after the company said it has entered into a securities purchase agreement with a single institutional investor. The investor will buy $15 million of Inpixon shares and warrants to buy stock in a registered direct offering priced at-the-market under Nasdaq rules. The combined purchase price for one share of common stock and associated warrants will be $5.85, and the combined purchase price for the pre-funded warrants and associated warrants will be $5.
