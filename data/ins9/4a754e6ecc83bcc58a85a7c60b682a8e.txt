Haas F1 boss criticizes Mick Schumacher for costing the team money, but says the driver still has a shot to keep his job
insider.com
news
2022-10-17 20:04:47

Mick Schumacher still has a shot to keep his spot in Formula One, but only if he can figure out how to start delivering some points over the season's final four races.Haas F1 team owner Gene Haas was asked about Schumacher's future with the team before Sunday's NASCAR playoff race in Las Vegas. While Haas was critical of Schumacher's performance to date, he said the driver still controls his future in F1."We're just waiting," Haas told the Associated Press. "We need Mick to bring some points, and we're trying to give him as much time as possible to see what he can do.
