Mikel Arteta has sent a message of support to Arsenal's on-loan midfielder Marcelo Flores following his struggles at Real Oviedo this season.

The 19-year-old joined Arsenal's academy in 2019 and is regarded as one of their brightest prospects for the future.
Despite not making a first-team appearance for Arsenal, Flores already has three caps for Mexico's national team and had been pushing for a place in their World Cup squad.
But Flores has endured a tough start to his loan spell with Real Oviedo in Spain's Segunda Division.
