CAE: Fiscal Q4 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-05-31 21:51:40

ST-LAURENT, Quebec (AP) _ CAE Inc. (CAE) on Tuesday reported fiscal fourth-quarter earnings of $43.5 million.On a per-share basis, the St-Laurent, Quebec-based company said it had net income of 13 cents. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs, were 23 cents per share. The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of eight analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 18 cents per share. The civil and military flight simulator company posted revenue of $754 million in the period, which also topped Street forecasts.
