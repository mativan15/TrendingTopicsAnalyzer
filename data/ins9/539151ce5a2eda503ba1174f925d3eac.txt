MoneyWatch: Managing Your Money By Matt Richardson October 18, 2022 / 12:46 PM / CBS News Even with a rise in mortgage interest rates, many homeowners in the United States would still benefit from refinancing their loans. Getty Images/iStockphoto For homeowners looking to reduce their expenses and save money, a refinance is often one of their most attractive options. By simply taking out a new loan to replace the existing mortgage you can, in theory, get a lower interest rate, thus lowering your monthly payment.
