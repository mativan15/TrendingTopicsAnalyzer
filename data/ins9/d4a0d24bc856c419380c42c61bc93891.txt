By Eve Buckland For Mailonline Published: 08:38 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 08:42 EDT, 1 June 2022 Liam Payne has declared welcoming son Bear with ex Cheryl 'ruined their relationship but  for all the right reasons' in his most candid chat yet.The former One Direction hitmaker, 28, and the Girls Aloud star, 38, welcomed Bear in 2017 but split the following year following a two year romance.Speaking on  Logan Paul's Impaulsive podcast, the Strip That Down singer said he originally struggled with becoming a father at the tender age of 23, and detailed how Cheryl haemorrhaged 'badly' while giving birth.
