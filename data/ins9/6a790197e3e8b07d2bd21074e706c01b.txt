FACEBOOK has left its users baffled after seemingly removing a popular feature over the weekend.
The dark mode in Facebook for iOS – a display setting that protects the eyes – has been down since at least Saturday.
WhatsApp has its own Dark Mode display settingCredit: WhatsApp
According to 9to5Mac, the problem is likely a bug. Facebook's parent company Meta has not provided an explanation for the issue.
The dark mode of other Meta apps such as Messenger, Instagram and WhatsApp are working as normal.
