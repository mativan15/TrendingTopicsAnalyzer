Anthony Albanese 'concerned' about delays on Australia-UK trade deal following resignation of Liz Truss
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-21 04:53:52

Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has expressed concern the political turmoil in the UK could delay a long-awaited free trade agreement between Australia and Britain. Mr Albanese says he wants to fast-track the trade deal to conclude it before the end of the yearThe deal was set to be one of the first new trade deals since the UK left the EUA new British prime minister is expected to be finalised by the end of next weekFollowing the resignation of UK Prime Minister Liz Truss after just 45 days in office, the shortest tenure in British history, Mr Albanese said it would be a difficult time for the outgoing leader.
