Cuemath raises $57 million funding led by Alpha Wave
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 09:45:00

Bengaluru: Math tutoring startup Cuemath has raised $57 million in a round led by US-based Alpha Wave Global at a valuation of $407 million, which the startup claims has doubled since its last funding round in December 2020. The company plans to use the funding to strengthen its product outcomes and for acquisitions and partnerships. Currently present in more than 70 countries, Cuemath aims to expand to over 100 countries by FY 2023. 'We plan to expand and strengthen footprint in North America, APAC, the UK, Europe, Middle East and capture markets in Africa and South America,' the startup said in a statement on Wednesday.
