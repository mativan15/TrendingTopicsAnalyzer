LA Clippers Launch ClipperVision, With Six Different Live Streams Available Direct-To-Consumer
nba.com

2022-10-17 12:00:07

ClipperVision will bring 70+ games directly to fans without a pay-TV subscription, with first stream Oct. 22Streams will include ClipperVision en Español, ClipperVision in Korean, and BallerVision, which will feature live commentary from some of Clipper Nation's biggest personalitiesLos Angeles, CA - In a first for the NBA, the LA Clippers will launch a direct-to-consumer streaming platform, ClipperVision, that will stream more than 70 games live in-market in its first season. ClipperVision brings cutting-edge technology to fans to enhance the live game-watching experience through augmented reality and alternate-language commentary options.
