Is Sustainability Just a PR Stunt?
chron.com
news
2022-06-02 18:30:00

If you check out any announcement at leading businesses these days, there will be some mention of sustainability, and probably DEI. These are two really hot issues in the corporate world, with companies coming under scrutiny if they are not doing enough to become a more inclusive and responsible company. This is because there is now a lot more focus on a company's CSR and ESG. If you're not entirely familiar with these two terms, let me briefly cover those. CSR looks at how companies make an impact on society, which can include the economy, the environment and people in general.
