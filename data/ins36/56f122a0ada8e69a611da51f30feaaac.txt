Global K-pop member, Jungkook, was spotted at a musical festival in Seoul interacting with his fans. A fan took to her Instagram stories to give a detailed description of her experience interacting with the star. The netizen explained that while she was watching a performance, she noticed that the person seated behind her was humming the melody to the song beautifully. When she looked back, she noted that the person bore a striking resemblance to Jungkook. Upon being unsure she asked her friend who suggested she looked at his hand tattoo to confirm if he was indeed Jungkook.
