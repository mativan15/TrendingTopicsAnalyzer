KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy Nikki CourtneyJun 3, 2022The ongoing shortage of workers in the US is making robots a more attractive option for businesses. Robot sales jumped 40% in the first quarter this year, according to the Association of Advancing Automation. The irony, business consultant Garrison Wynn points out, is that the developing business in robots that do mundane tasks human find boringly repetitive is creating jobs for humans. We don't have a robot shortage, we have a worker shortage.
