American Express says spending remains strong as earnings top expectations
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 11:05:00

Though acknowledging 'mixed signals' in the economy, American Express Co. once again cited strong spending trends as the financial services giant posted better-than-expected earnings for its latest quarter. The company logged third-quarter net income of $1.88 billion, or $2.47 a share, up from $1.83 billion, or $2.27 a share, in the year-prior quarter. The FactSet consensus was for $2.40 a share in earnings. American Express AXP, -1.08% notched $13.56 billion in total revenues net of interest expense for the period, coming in above the $13.
