DuPont™ Tedlar® Joins the Metal Construction Association
prweb.com
news
2022-05-31 13:30:00

'Tedlar® has over 60 years of proven performance in protecting surfaces around the world. We look forward to working closely with MCA to increase the use of metal in construction industry.' said Matt Urfali, Sales & Marketing Director of the DuPont Tedlar® business WILMINGTON, Del. (PRWEB) May 31, 2022 DuPont (NYSE:DD) announced that its Tedlar® business become a member of the Metal Construction Association (MCA). The MCA is an organization of manufacturers and suppliers whose metal products are used in structures throughout the world.
