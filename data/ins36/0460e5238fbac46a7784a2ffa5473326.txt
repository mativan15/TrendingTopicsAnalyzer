A Hydro Quebec worker walks back to his truck after inspecting a substation in Delson, Quebec, on Oct. 12, 2022.Christinne Muschi/The Globe and MailThe Quebec government is creating a super-ministry of the economy that will assume oversight over energy matters, setting up a potential clash with the senior leadership of power giant Hydro-Québec over how best to use the province's immense hydropower resources in the months ahead.After a crushing victory in the Oct. 3 provincial election, Premier François Legault unveiled his new cabinet Thursday and named Pierre Fitzgibbon minister for the economy and innovation.
