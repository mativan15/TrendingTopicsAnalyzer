LA Local NewsBy City News ServiceJun 5, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesVAN NUYS (CNS) - A white pickup crashed into a commercial building in Van Nuys Saturday afternoon, damaging the building. The crash was reported at 2:48 p.m. in the 13700 block of Victory Boulevard, at Woodman Avenue, according to the Los Angeles Fire Department's Nicholas Prange. Firefighters were called to the crash but did not transport any injured people to hospitals. A news videographer at the scene said the business was closed at the time.
