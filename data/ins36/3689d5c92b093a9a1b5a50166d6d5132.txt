Verizon's +play adds NBA streaming services

NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Verizon today announced that NBA League Pass, the league's premium live game subscription service, and NBA TV, the league's premium offering of 24/7 NBA content, are the latest additions to +play, a first-of-its-kind platform for consumers to discover, purchase and manage some of their favorite subscriptions – all in one place.With the NBA's direct-to-consumer subscription services in +play, Verizon customers will be able to seamlessly access and manage their subscriptions to both NBA League Pass and NBA TV, as well as exclusive offers from the many services available through +play – all on the network America relies on.
