State of the Pittsburgh Innovation District event has panelists discuss region's ongoing startup challenges
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 19:01:00

While Pittsburgh's local tech startup scene has been the subject of great fanfare and celebration in recent years amid a rise in funding, there's still plenty of work to be done to ensure the region remains competitive for aspiring startup founders who are looking for a place to build their companies.

That was one of the main takeaways from a panel discussion on Friday hosted by InnovatePGH, a public-private partnership based in Oakland that's looking to advance and champion the region's startup scene.
