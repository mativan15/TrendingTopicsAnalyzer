(Representational)Guwahati: Assam Police have arrested five people in connection with two ATM fraud cases and recovered 68 ATM cards. Police have also seized three vehicles.According to police, the arrested persons had fraudulently changed the ATM cards of unknown persons to withdraw money.Sudhakar Singh, Deputy Commissioner of Police (DCP), Guwahati City Police said that two cases of ATM fraud were registered on October 4 and 5.
