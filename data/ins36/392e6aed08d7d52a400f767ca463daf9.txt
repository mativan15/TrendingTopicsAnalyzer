Published: 23:44 EDT, 28 May 2022 | Updated: 23:50 EDT, 28 May 2022 A multi-millionaire businessman dragged his ex-wife's closest friends into a WhatsApp group and bombarded them with a 'degrading' image of her. Rennie De Maria, 54, who made millions through an insurance broking empire, pleaded guilty to circulating an intimate image of his former wife to some of her closest pals.De Maria, who lives in a mansion with a private helipad and three hole golf course, was rebuked for his 'sheer gutlessness' at Mansfield Magistrates' Court.
