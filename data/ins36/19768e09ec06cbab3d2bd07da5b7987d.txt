AgenciesFormer James Bond star Pierce Brosnan was wearing his wedding ring while shooting for the new superhero film Black Adam based on DC characters.As former James Bond star Pierce Brosnan chose to wear his wedding ring while shooting for his upcoming movie Black Adam, he explained that it was because it seemed proper. During an interview with the media, Brosnan, 69, described the ring in the form of a watch; an inscription on the watch talks of time travelling on the wings of love. Pierce Brosnan added that wearing the wedding ring was a tribute to his wife of 21 years, Keely Shaye Brosnan.
