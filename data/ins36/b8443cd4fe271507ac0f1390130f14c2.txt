Alan Wake Remastered Gets Surprise-Release Today On Switch With 20% Discount
gamespot.com
gaming
2022-10-20 14:41:53

Alan Wake Remastered has surprise-launched on Nintendo Switch, and it debuts with a nice discount. Remedy Entertainment had confirmed the game was coming this fall, but never gave a date. Releasing today, just in time for the spooky season, makes a lot of sense.The 20% off launch discount is a nice treat, too, bringing the game down from $30 to $24. This includes the base game and two expansions: The Signal and The Writer. Check out a trailer below.Alan Wake Remastered was originally released in October 2021 for Xbox Series X|S, PS5, and PC, as well as PS4 and Xbox One.
