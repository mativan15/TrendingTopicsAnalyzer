The Alabama Crimson Tide women's soccer team has officially claimed the No. 1 ranking after defeating No. 7 Arkansas on Sunday 2-1.
The Tide jumped the former No. 1 UCLA Bruins after they fell to Stanford 1-0 over the weekend.
