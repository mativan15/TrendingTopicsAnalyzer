Piramal Pharma shares hit 5% lower circuit after listing
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 06:00:00

Shares of ' pharma entity dipped 5% to hit its lower circuit at Rs 191.75 per share after opening at Rs 201.8 on the BSE in Wednesday's trade. Earlier last week, the company obtained a go-ahead from the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) for the listing of its equity shares. Furthermore, the listing of the stock is well in line with the company's plan to list it by the third quarter of the current fiscal year.

According to the circular by BSE, the securities of Piramal Pharma will be admitted to trading in the T group of securities.
