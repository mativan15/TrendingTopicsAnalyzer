If you had $1 million to invest in a better workplace, how would you spend it? For factory workers at C.H.I. Overhead Doors, the answer was air conditioning.As part of its investment in C.H.I., KKR & Co. set aside about $1 million each year and asked its hourly workforce to decide how to spend it to improve their day-to-day experience. 'We said, ‘We'll follow your lead, wherever you want to go, we will spend it.
