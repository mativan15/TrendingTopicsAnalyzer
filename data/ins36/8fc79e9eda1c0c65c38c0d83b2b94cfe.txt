TV show ‘Mithai', which is lagging in terms of TRPs these days and couldn't make it to the top 5 club in the previous week, seems to be ready to dish out some high-octane drama. The upcoming episodes will feature some interesting twists as male protagonist Siddhartha's arch-enemy Aditya Agarwal is raging with fury. He wants to avenge the death of his younger brother Omi , who lost his life while trying to kill Siddhartha. Although the latter is innocent, Aditya blames Sid for his brother's death and he is seeking vengeance.
