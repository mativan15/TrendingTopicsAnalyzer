By Reuters Published: 14:24 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 14:24 EDT, 19 October 2022 The New York Yankees made three changes to their American League Championship Series roster on Wednesday, including removing injured outfielder Aaron Hicks. The Yankees added right-hander Frankie Montas, infielder Oswald Peraza and right-hander Greg Weissert. Hicks is out for the rest of the postseason and beyond after injuring his knee in Tuesday night's Game 5 of the AL Division Series. He'll be sidelined for six weeks.
