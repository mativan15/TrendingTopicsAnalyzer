Warning over shocking crypto trend

2022-06-06 01:20:00

The consumer watchdog has revealed Australians are racking up staggering losses as fraudsters increasingly target a particular asset.Crypto scammers stole nearly $1m a day from Aussie investors in the first four months of the year, prompting the consumer watchdog to sound the alarm. The Australian Competition and Consumer Commission on Monday announced cryptocurrency fraudsters had been busy since January and were the main reason Australians had more than doubled their total scam losses compared to the same time in 2021.
