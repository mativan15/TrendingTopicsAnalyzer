Konami officially revealed three new game in the Silent Hill series in a special event today: Silent Hill: Ascension, Silent Hill: Townfall and Silent Hill: F. This is in addition to the remake of Silent Hill 2 it also announced, as well as a new movie. We don't yet know the release dates of any of the games (except Ascension) but they appear to be launching on PlayStation consoles.
