WWE Hall of Fame wrestler Kevin Nash's son dead at 26
nypost.com
news
2022-10-20 19:59:22

Tristen Nash — the son of WWE Hall of Fame wrestler Kevin Nash — has reportedly died at the age of 26.
The news of Nash's death was initially broken by a reporter from fightful.com, who also had a statement from the family regarding their son's death. 
'On behalf of Kevin and Tamara Nash, I have to unfortunately report that their son Tristen Nash has tragically passed away at the age of 26,' reads the statement.
