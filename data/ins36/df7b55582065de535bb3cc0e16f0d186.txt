Getty ImagesThree days ago, the movie ‘F3' which is Varun Tej and Victory Venkatesh's hilarious family entertainer 'F3' hit the big screens. This film was very well received and it managed to gross Rs 63.5 Cr globally in just three days of its release. This film is produced by Dil Raju and is directed by Anil Ravipudi. The film ‘F3' is made under the banner of Sri Venkateswara Creations and the music of the film is by Devi Sri Prasad. The film is reportedly receiving a unanimously positive reaction and thus, is continuing to post big numbers.
