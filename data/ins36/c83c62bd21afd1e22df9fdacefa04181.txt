ANIIn his speech, the Prime Minister said he stands by the commitment he had made to the people in 2014 when he assumed office.Reiterating his pledge to work tirelessly for good governance and welfare of the poor, Prime Minister Narendra Modi emphasised on completion of eight years in office that empowerment of all would be achieved only through ending discrimination and appeasement of some sections of society. Addressing a public meeting in Shimla to mark eight years in office, the prime minister said, "We have taken the initiative to make 100% benefit reach 100% of the beneficiaries.
