ORLANDO, Fla. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Aviation Performance Solutions (APS) announced today that its Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Paul BJ Ransbury, was named as Chairman of the NBAA Safety Committee yesterday at the National Business Aviation Association's Business Aviation Convention & Exhibition (NBAA-BACE) in Orlando, Florida.
