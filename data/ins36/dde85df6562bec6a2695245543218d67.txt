Pm Has Given Huge Funds To State: Min

2022-06-01 06:15:00

BHUBANESWAR: Union education minister Dharmendra Pradhan on Tuesday asked people to come forward to fulfil the vision Prime Minister Narendra Modi has for Odisha.Speaking at a public meeting at Mahanga in Cuttack district to mark eight years of the Modi government, the minister said nobody has taken the responsibility of Odisha's development the way Modi has done.'From road and rail to industrial infrastructure, Modi has been giving huge funds to Odisha. However, money sent by the Centre is not being properly used by the state government.
