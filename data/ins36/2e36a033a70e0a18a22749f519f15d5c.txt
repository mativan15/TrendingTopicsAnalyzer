MARVEL Snap is an all-new free-to-play card game from the game director behind Hearthstone, Ben Brode.
Many former developers who worked on Hearthstone, one of the world's most popular and competitive card games, have since moved to work on the upcoming Marvel title.
Card variants are available for early adopters.Credit: Marvel
With a seasoned and reputable team behind the new game, fans have been eager to try out Marvel Snap.
From trailers we know that it is a collectible and competitive card builder, where you gather cards featuring different Marvel heroes.
