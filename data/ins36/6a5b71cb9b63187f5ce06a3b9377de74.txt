Industry insiders say the biggest risk of Broadcom's $61 billion acquisition of VMware is a 'brain drain' from a talent exodus
businessinsider.com
news
2022-06-03 12:00:00

Any deal as big as Broadcom's planned $61 billion acquisition of the software maker VMware comes with challenges. While many analysts expect the deal to pass regulatory scrutiny, the biggest risk for Broadcom may be a VMware talent exodus after the acquisition, analysts and customers say.Broadcom said in a conference call on May 26 that it was acquiring VMware in large part because of its team. VMware's chief people officer, Betsy Sutter, also told employees at a town hall that day that Broadcom is "very interested in our talent.
