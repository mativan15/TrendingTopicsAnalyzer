 A woman walks past a decoration with the words "Joyous Welcoming of the 20th Congress" in Beijing, China, October 11, 2022. © Ng Han Guan, AP The Chinese Communist Party (CCP) Congress kicks off in Beijing on Sunday – an event expected to confirm Xi Jinping as China's first leader since Mao Zedong to serve three successive terms. Decisions unveiled at the congress – especially the fate of Prime Minister Li Keqiang – will indicate whether Xi plans to continue amassing power and implementing contentious economic policies.
