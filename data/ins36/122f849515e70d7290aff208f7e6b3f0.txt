CHARLOTTESVILLE, Va., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Duke's Mayonnaise is partnering with Champion Brewing Company to produce the perfect brew to pair with their fan's favorite southern college football tradition – tailgating. This is the second collaboration between the two Virginia-based brands. In 2021, they launched Family Recipe, a lager that perfectly pairs with the south's favorite sandwich – a mouthwatering, Duke's-slathered BLT.
