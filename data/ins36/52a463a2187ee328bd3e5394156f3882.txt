Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu has revealed she is set to embrace her natural beauty after Love Island, by going back to her natural hair colour and ditching her lip fillers. 
The reality star, 28, and boyfriend Davide Sanclimenti, 27, emerged victorious from Love Island 2022, and despite dominating this year's series, Ekin-Su revealed that she wants to switch up her look after seeing herself on screen.
