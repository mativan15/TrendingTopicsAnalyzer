How Covid-19 boosted small town confidence in health tech, local docs
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 10:15:31

The approach towards healthcare has been reimagined in India's hinterlands, particularly post the pandemic scare. The citizens are now considering healthcare as a priority, and not as a secondary need any longer. This is a paradigm shift from the late-stage treatment in the pre-covid era. Furthermore, there has been an increase in the level of awareness of diseases, symptoms, prevention and the recognition of formal medical treatment, rather than through home remedies. There is also increased adoption of testing and diagnosis, with or without doctor's recommendations and follow up treatments, basis these tests.
