BOSTON, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Block & Leviton is investigating BRP Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: BRP) for potential securities law violations. Investors who have lost money in their BRP Group, Inc. investment should contact the firm to learn more about how they might recover those losses. For more details, visit https://www.blockleviton.com/cases/brp. What is this all about? On September 13, 2022, NINGI Research issued a short report alleging that 'BRP has doctored its organic growth rate to beat analysts' estimates,' and 'the company misled investors by presenting inorganic revenue as organic revenue through a self-proclaimed separate agreement with an affiliate.
