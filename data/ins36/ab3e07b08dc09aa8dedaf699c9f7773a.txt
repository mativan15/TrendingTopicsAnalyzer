How Miguel Almirón put himself at forefront of Eddie Howe's Newcastle revolution
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 16:00:00

With five goals to his name, Newcastle United's Miguel Almirón has surpassed his best goal scoring return in a Premier League season. He is thriving for Eddie Howe, with the freedom to express himself on the pitch. After Wednesday night's winner against Everton, the 28-year-old already has two contenders for goal of the season.The frustration with Almirón has always been his consistency. He has shown glimpses, but never enough to get a solid run of games. Newcastle fans craved the brilliance that he showed for MLS club Atlanta United in 2018, the season before he moved to Tyneside, when he scored 12 times and assisted 11.
