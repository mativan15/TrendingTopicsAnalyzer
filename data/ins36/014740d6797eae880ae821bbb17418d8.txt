Getty ImagesRBSE 2022 result of class 12 for all three streams, commerce, arts, and science, can come out any time soon, said the chief of the BSER or Board of Secondary Education Rajasthan. Nearly 10 lakh students appeared for the senior secondary exam of the Rajasthan Board from 24th March to 26th April, and almost 6068 centers were there throughout Rajasthan. Though the date and time of the result are not known, students can see the same on the authorized website of the Rajasthan Board. They can either click on rajresults.
