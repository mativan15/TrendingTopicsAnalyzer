Jeremy Hunt: We will reverse almost all mini-budget tax cuts

2022-10-17 11:58:37

Media caption, Watch: Chancellor scraps nearly all of PM's tax plansThe government will reverse almost all the tax cuts it announced last month, Chancellor Jeremy Hunt has said.But Mr Hunt said the cuts to stamp duty paid on house purchases and the scrapping of the National Insurance rise would continue. Mr Hunt said economic growth required "confidence and stability", adding that the UK "will always pay its way".Among the measures to be reversed are plans to cut the basic rate of income tax from 20p to 19p from April.
