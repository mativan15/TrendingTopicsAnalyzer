Minnesota-based pet food company Tuffy's expands with purchase of Chaska business
startribune.com
news
2022-06-01 13:34:48

Tuffy's Treat Co. continues to grow, announcing that it is buying a Chaska-based pet treat business. Tuffy's was already an investor in Finley's Barkery. Terms of the deal were not disclosed Finley's Barkery, with a mission to employ people with disabilities as in-store ambassadors, began in a classroom in 2010 and has grown rapidly in recent years. "We are deeply humbled that what began as a dream to help those with disabilities will now be part of a company that shares our passion for pets and people.
