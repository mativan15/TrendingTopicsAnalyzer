Fresno, California --News Direct-- Bluewhite

Blue White Robotics was excited to officially announce their new company rebranding and expansion initiatives today, at FIRA USA. Leading the company's rebranding efforts and focus on the future, Blue White Robotics will now be known as Bluewhite, which was a change that came naturally for the company.

Hand in hand with their name change, the company also teased a variety of new initiatives, at FIRA, focused on transforming Bluewhite over the next five years.
