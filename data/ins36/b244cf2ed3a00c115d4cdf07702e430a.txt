2022 is proving to be a difficult year for many tech companies. One of the most prominent issues many of them are facing is slowing growth in the face of tough year-ago comparisons.
Even some of the biggest names in tech are facing challenges. Alphabet's growth has slowed significantly; Meta Platforms paused hiring as top-line growth turned negative; and Microsoft laid off employees. But somehow, The Trade Desk's (TTD 2.26%) second-quarter revenue soared.
The data-driven ad-buying platform company's management team guided for strong growth in Q3, too.
