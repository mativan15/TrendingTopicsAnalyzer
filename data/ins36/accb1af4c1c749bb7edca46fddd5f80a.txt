Sean Pavone/iStock via Getty Images Melco Resorts & Entertainment (NASDAQ:MLCO) was downgraded by Credit Suisse as a Zero-COVID policy and a weakening consumer likely impact results. 'Post 3Q, the gradual e-Visa and packaged tour resumption and slight normalization of Covid-19 policy (post Party Congress) should help drive sequential improvement,' the bank's analysts wrote. 'That said, the drag from lower player quality (on weaker macro), China's 'zero-Covid' policy, and efforts in anti-cross-border gambling remain.
