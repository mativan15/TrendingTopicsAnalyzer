Microsoft Says It Won't Fight Unions, Will Employees Call Their Bluff?
gizmodo.com
tech
2022-06-03 17:25:00

The big players at Microsoft say they'll hold back their foot from crushing the little guy if they decide to go the way of organized labor, though it seems the company would rather first exhaust any other avenue than sitting down for collective bargaining.In a Thursday blog post, company President Brad Smith said that with the multitude of unionization campaigns happening across the U.S., Microsoft might be next. Smith shared that the tech monolith recognizes 'that employees have a legal right to choose whether to form or join a union' and that they don't believe they'd benefit from resisting 'lawful employee efforts' to unionize.
