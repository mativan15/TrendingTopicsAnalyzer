Kelly Moran, 30, an office manager, and Stephen Hewitt, 32, head of broker channel at Iwoca, a small business lender, were married on October 3, 2021, at Gaynes Park in Epping, Essex.Kelly and Stephen's wedding photographs are particularly special. As well as a treasured record of the day they got married, they are a precious memory of a joyful occasion shared with his father, Martin, who died unexpectedly a few months later.The couple had postponed their wedding three times and waited a year and a half to get married because of Covid.
