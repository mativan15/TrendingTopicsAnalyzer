Intuitive Surgical stock rallies more than 10% following earnings beat
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 15:23:42

Intuitive Surgical Inc. ISRG, +11.49% shares rallied Wednesday after the surgical robotics company's quarterly results topped Wall Street estimates. Intuitive Surgical shares rose more than 12% to an intraday high of $220.98 Wednesday Late Tuesday, the company reported third-quarter net income of $324 million, or 90 cents a share, compared with $380.5 million, or $1.04 a share, in the year-ago period. Adjusted earnings, which exclude stock-based compensation expenses and other items, were flat at $1.
