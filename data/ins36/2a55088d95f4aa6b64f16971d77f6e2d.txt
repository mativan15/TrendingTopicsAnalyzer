October 21, 2022 06:45 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SurePoint Technologies, the leading cloud provider of financial and practice management solutions for law firms, announced today the launch of its latest innovation, Coyote Analytics Cloud.



The new cloud-hosted platform provides legal professionals with the ability to personalize workflows by leveraging a robust suite of user empowerment capabilities while working anytime and anywhere. The system enables law firms to optimize profitability and business operations and includes financial management, payment processing, mobile timekeeping, time management, and innovative business intelligence capabilities for data-driven strategic decision-making.
