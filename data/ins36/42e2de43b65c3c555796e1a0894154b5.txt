Leon Mann, from Lincolnshire, took 50 per cent deposits upfront - including from a cancer patient - and then spend the cash on online gambling sitesLeon Mann was sentenced in Lincoln Crown Court today A roofer has been fined £10,000 for feeding his gambling habit with customer deposits, without completing the work promised. Leon Mann, from Gainsborough in Lincolnshire, was also handed down a 16-month suspended sentence, Lincolnshire Live reports. He admitted 12 offences following a nine-month investigation by Lincolnshire Trading Standards.
