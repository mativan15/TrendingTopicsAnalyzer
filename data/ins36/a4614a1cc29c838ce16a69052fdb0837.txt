The Tiny Digital Factory announced it is making Infinite Drive as the 'first real driving metaverse.' The mobile game will use non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and Web3 technology.The Montreal, Canada, and Lyon, France-based game studio has 35 people and it has longstanding relationships with carmakers. It said the free-to-play game already will have more than 100 cars licensed by the end of the year.
