Concor shares up 2.5% as Nifty gains
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 06:00:00

Analysts see 15,900 to continue to pose as the immediate resistance. Shares of Container Corporation of India Ltd. gained 2.5 per cent to Rs 674.2 in Monday's trade. It hit a high of Rs 677.3 and low of Rs 658.4 during the session. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 650.61, while the 50-DMA was at Rs 644.09. If a stock trades above 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is upward. On the other hand, if the stock trades below 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it is considered a bearish trend and if trades between these averages, then it suggests the stock can go either way.
