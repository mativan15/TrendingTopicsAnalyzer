Published: 05:25 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 05:25 EDT, 22 October 2022 A Big Brother winner who won £50,000 on the reality show has said he is struggling to keep his bakery open after his energy bills quadrupled from £400 to £1,600 a month. Luke Anderson, 41, said he thought the energy bill for his North Wales bakery, which he owns with his wife Jamie-Lee Anderson, was a typo because it was so huge.The huge increase in energy bills will means they will need to earn £50 every day just to cover this one cost - not including other running costs.
