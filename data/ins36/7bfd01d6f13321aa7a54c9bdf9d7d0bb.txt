October 17, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Choice Properties Real Estate Investment Trust ('Choice Properties') (TSX: CHP.UN) announced today that the trustees of Choice Properties have declared a cash distribution for the month of October, 2022 of $0.061667 per trust unit, representing $0.74 per trust unit on an annualized basis, payable on November 15, 2022 to Unitholders of record at the close of business on October 31, 2022.

About Choice Properties Real Estate Investment Trust

Choice Properties is a leading Real Estate Investment Trust that creates enduring value through the ownership, operation and development of high-quality commercial and residential properties.
