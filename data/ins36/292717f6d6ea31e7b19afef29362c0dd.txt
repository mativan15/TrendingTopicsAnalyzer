African reporter who heckled Psaki says payments firm axed site's subscription income
nypost.com
news
2022-06-01 00:02:47

The African journalist who interrupted Jen Psaki's final White House press briefing earlier this month says the financial processing company Stripe is cutting off his publication's income. 
Today News Africa correspondent Simon Ateba provided The Post with copies of a message informing him that his website's offer of a 'lifetime' subscription — for $1,490 — violated Stripe company policies and therefore he'd have to start from scratch with another payment processor.
