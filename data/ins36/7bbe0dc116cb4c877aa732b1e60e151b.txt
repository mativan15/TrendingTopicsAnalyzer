ET Canada Nearly two and a half years after Josh O'Connor declared 'The Crown' would not include Prince Charles‘ infamous 'Tampongate' phone call with Camilla Parker Bowles, it's now been confirmed it actually is happening, and it's O'Connor's successor, Dominic West, who spilled the tea.West, who is playing the younger version of King Charles III in the upcoming seasons 5 of the Netflix show, opened up to Entertainment Weekly about how he felt about the ordeal while confirming to the outlet that the 1993 phone call will make it in the show.
