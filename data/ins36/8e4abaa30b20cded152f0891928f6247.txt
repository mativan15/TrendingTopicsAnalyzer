The BJP is going for micro-management of the Assembly seats in Gujarat by roping in its national office-bearers and experienced poll managers from various states and assigning them a district each, sources said on Sunday. Besides those from neighbouring Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh, the central leadership of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) has also given responsibilities to leaders from Uttar Pradesh for managing its election campaign in Gujarat, the sources in the saffron party told PTI.
