I travel with my mom and dad, who are in their late 50s and early 60s, multiple times each year, most recently hiking Machu Picchu with my mom. (Photo: Megan duBois)Travel is something that brings people together to learn about new places and cultures ... and about each other. For many young adults who've yet to start a family (or do not want children), that means continuing to travel with their mom and dad. These grandchildren-free trips have a great deal of perks, freeing up lots of space on the itinerary for big adventures that are not dependent on nap times and diaper changes.
