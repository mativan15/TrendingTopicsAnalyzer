SHANGHAI, China, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Baozun Inc. (NASDAQ: BZUN and HKEX: 9991) ('Baozun' or the 'Company'), the leading brand e-commerce service partner that helps brands execute their e-commerce strategies in China, announced today that it will hold its 2022 annual general meeting of shareholders on June 27, 2022, at 2:00 p.m., Singapore Time (or 2:00 a.m. on June 27, 2022, New York Time). The meeting will be held at 7 Temasek Boulevard, Level 32 Suntec Tower One, Singapore 038987.



Copies of the notice of 2022 annual general meeting, which sets forth the resolutions to be submitted to shareholder approval at the meeting, along with proxy statement, proxy form and voting card form are available on the Company's investor relations website at http://ir.
