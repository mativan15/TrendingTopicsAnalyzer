Many social media users including even actors and some critics accuse movie makers of buying theater tickets themselves to prove a movie is hitAmid many debacles Bollywood is going through including dismal box office collections in recent years, Brahmastra's revenues, which defeated the calls of boycott, has offered a sliver of hope for the country's estimated 240-billion-rupees movie industry. The film is estimated to have entered the Rs 400 crore club from box office collections and is a hit after a rough patch for an industry where only a few films helped producers ring the cash register in recent couple of years.
