Fuller Smith & Turner Agrees to New GBP200 Mln Debt Facility
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 08:03:00

By Michael Susin Fuller Smith & Turner PLC said Wednesday that it has borrowed 200 million pounds ($252.1 million) after it successfully completed the refinancing of its previous debt facility. The U.K. pubs-and-hotels business said the new debt consists of a GBP90 million loan and a GBP110 million revolving credit facility provided by seven banks. The new facilities have a maturity date of end of May 2026 and an option to extend by a further year. Shares at 0742 GMT were up 30.0 pence, or 5.4%, at 582.
