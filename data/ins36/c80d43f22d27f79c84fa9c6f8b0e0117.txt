Retired manager of UP Cooperative Bank under lens for online attempt to siphon off ₹146 cr
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 16:18:42

LUCKNOW: The role of a retired manager of the Uttar Pradesh Cooperative Bank (UPCB) has come under the scanner in the ongoing investigation into the failed online attempt to withdraw ₹146 crore from the official account of the bank. The suspect along with an aide was taken into custody for interrogation on Tuesday. Besides, 10 staffers have been suspended by the bank for lapses. 'Former bank manager RS Dubey and his aide Ganga Sagar Chauhan are being quizzed about the incident,' said Triveni Singh, SP, UP Cyber Crime Cell.
