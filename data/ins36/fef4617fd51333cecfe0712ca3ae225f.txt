The Mercedes-AMG ONE is a $2 million F1 car for the road
foxnews.com
news
2022-06-01 17:27:08

Mercedes-Benz has turned Formula One into a stock car racing series.The automaker has unveiled the Mercedes-AMG ONE, a supercar powered by a street-legal version of the engine used in its championship-winning F1 cars.Mercedes-AMG is Mercedes-Benz's performance and racing division.The two-seat coupe features a 1.6-liter V6 with an electric motor-assisted turbo mounted amidships. Only 275 Mercedes-AMG ONE cars will be built. (Mercedes-Benz)It also has another electric motor between the engine and a 7-speed transmission, and two more powering the front wheels.
