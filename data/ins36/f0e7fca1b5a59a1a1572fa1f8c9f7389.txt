No Uttar Pradesh madrassa got accreditation in 7 years, say officers
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 06:00:00

'Govt should have opened doors for registration of new madrassas'

AGRA: While the sword is hanging on over 6,000 unregistered madrassas in UP, officers in the state government have said no madrassa was given a registration certificate in the last seven years.The submission date of the survey report of unregistered madrassas has now been extended till November 15, a senior official said on Monday. "We are working on it, and once the survey is finished, we will disclose the policy on madrassas. But I can say that no illegal madrassa is being operated in UP.
