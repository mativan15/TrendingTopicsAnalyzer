By Reuters Published: 11:53 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 11:53 EDT, 31 May 2022 MILAN, May 31 (Reuters) - Telecom Italia (TIM) has attached an enterprise value of around 20 billion euros ($21.5 billion) to its landline grid which the former phone monopoly is considering selling as part of plan to create a national fibre network controlled by state lender CDP, two sources said on Tuesday. The plan, sketched out in a preliminary agreement on Sunday, aims to combine TIM's fixed network with that of CDP-controlled broadband rival Open Fiber.
