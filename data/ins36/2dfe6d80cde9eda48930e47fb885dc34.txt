U.S. stocks book big gains Monday, S&P 500 records best day in two weeks
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 20:02:11

U.S. stocks closed sharply higher Monday as quarterly corporate earnings picked up steam and the S&P 500 recorded its best daily percentage gain since Oct. 4, according to FactSet data. The Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +1.86% gained about 550 points, or 1.9%, ending near 30,188, while the S&P 500 index SPX, +2.65% advanced 2.7%.
