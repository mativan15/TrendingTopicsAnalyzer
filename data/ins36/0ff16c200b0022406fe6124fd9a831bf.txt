PTINew Delhi: Odisha Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik with Biju Janata Dal (BJD) MPs during the second part of Budget Session at Parliament House, in New Delhi. (PTI Photo/Shahbaz Khan) BJD has nominated 3 newcomers namely, Sulata Deo, Manas Ranjan Mangaraj, Niranjan Bishi who would be Rajya Sabha members of parliament from July 1st, 2022. Apart from them, Sasmit Patra would be in his second innings having represented Odisha in the previous term as the Chief Whip of the BJD in the Upper House. This announcement from the BJD was made on the occasion of the 3rd anniversary of the 5th Tenure of the Navin Patnaik Government.
