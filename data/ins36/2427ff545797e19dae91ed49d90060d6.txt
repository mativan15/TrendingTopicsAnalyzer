Some of the food products that will be available in the new Asda "Just Essentials" range include fresh meat, fish and poultry, bakery, frozen and cupboard staplesAsda has unveiled its new Just Essentials rangeAsda has finally launched its new 'Just Essentials' budget range as shoppers continue to struggle against the cost of living crisis. The new range replaces its Smart Price collection and consists of 293 products. Some of the food products that will be available include fresh meat, fish and poultry, bakery, frozen and cupboard staples.
