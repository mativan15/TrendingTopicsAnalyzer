Meats by Linz plans move to Hammond
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-05-31 19:32:47

Fred Linz became emotional Tuesday when he started to thank the people who helped grow the fourth-generation business Meats by Linz from about 30 employees just 30 years ago to more than 300 today.Linz was in Hammond Tuesday to break ground on a new meat processing facility for its premium products that are shipped to restaurants around the world. The company CEO was joined by family, employees and city officials including Hammond Mayor Tom McDermott.Linz said his family got its start in Hammond; his grandfather at Goldblatt's, where he first dreamed of opening a butcher shop, and his grandmother at Queen Anne Candy Co.
