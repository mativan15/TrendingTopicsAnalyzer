SAN MATEO, Calif., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Incorta, the platform for real-time operational analytics on raw business data, today announced new capabilities and enhancements, including Delta Sharing compatibility and several new data apps. With these latest updates, Incorta is making it even faster and easier for customers to get operational data from source systems and make it ready for business analysis. The newly released data apps offer prebuilt business schemas and dashboards for Oracle EBS, Oracle ERP and EPM Clouds, NetSuite, SAP, and others.
