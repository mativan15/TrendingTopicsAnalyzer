Retailers are overstocked in 2022 and that's good for you
beaumontenterprise.com
news
2022-06-01 18:05:46

With Memorial Day, and its expansive sales, over, you'd expect that retailers have had enough of giving away their stuff for much cheaper than retail — Well, interestingly enough, there's been some murmuring that not all is sitting easy.Usually, retailers tend to sit on their products until specific days of the year, such as Prime Day or Black Friday, with deals happening afterward being mostly to compensate for overstock (in fact, Cyber Monday is almost wholly dedicated to getting rid of extra stock retailers were expecting to sell during Black Friday).
