October 19, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHARLOTTE, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Truvant, a leading global packaging services company, today announced that Colleen Soukup has been elected to the company's Board of Directors.



Mrs. Soukup recently retired from General Mills, Inc. after a 39-year career with the Minnesota-based multi-national food company. Her last position upon retirement was Chief Procurement Officer. In this role, Mrs. Soukup led over a 300-person global procurement team with a spend of over $10B and also led the transformation of General Mills' external supply chain strategy to deliver significant growth and record productivity for the organization.
