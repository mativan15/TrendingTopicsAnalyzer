How heat pumps can save businesses money – DW – 10/19/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-19 10:50:55

From cheesemakers to cement manufacturers, companies need to heat and cool to produce their wares. Oil and gas are the go-to fuels for industry. Climate reasons aside, with these fossil fuels so cheap for so long, industries had little incentive to install more sustainable technologies.   But the war in Ukraine and a sharp spike in gas prices are forcing businesses to rethink their energy sources. That's where heat pumps come in. Manufacturers of the climate-friendly heating systems say they're getting more and more inquiries from businesses.
