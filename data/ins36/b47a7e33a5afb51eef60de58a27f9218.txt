10-year fixed-rate private student loans dip after two-week upward trend
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-06-01 15:44:41

Average private student loan rates for borrowers with credit scores of 720 or higher who used the Credible marketplace to take out student loans fell for 10-year fixed rates and rose for 5-year variable rates during the week of May 23, 2022: 10-year fixed rate: 6.04%, down from 6.42% the week before, -0.385-year variable rate: 4.33%, up from 4.15% the week before, +0.18Through Credible, you can compare private student loan rates from multiple lenders without affecting your credit score. Rates for a 10-year fixed-rate private student loan fell after climbing for two weeks in a row, while 5-year variable-rate loans edged up.
