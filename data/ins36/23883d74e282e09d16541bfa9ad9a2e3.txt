Denmark Open: Lakshya Sen loses in quarterfinals, Indian challenge ends
thehindu.com
sport
2022-10-22 02:56:53

Commonwealth Games champion Lakshya Sen suffered a straight-games loss to Japan's Kodai Naraoka in the men's singles quarterfinal to bring down curtains on India's campaign at the Denmark Open Super 750 tournament in Odense.Sen, 2021 world championships bronze medallist, lost 17-21, 12-21 to Naraoka, a bronze medallist at the 2018 Youth Olympics, in a lop-sided contest on Friday night.The two have played thrice in international circuit with the Japanese winning twice. Interestingly, it was Sen, who had defeated him in the 2018 Youth Olympics semifinals, their last meeting.
