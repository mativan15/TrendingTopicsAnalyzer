Denver health tech company CirrusMD names new CEO
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 13:31:00

CirrusMD, a Denver-based virtual health care startup that saw substantial growth during the pandemic, has named a new president and chief executive officer.

Jamie Hall, formerly the chief commercial officer at Transcarent as well as president and CEO at BridgeHealth before it was acquired, joins the CirrusMD team with 30 years of experience in health care, according to a news release. Hall will step into his role following the exit of interim CEO Karl Maier, who also serves as executive chairman of the board.
