TORONTO, May 31, 2022 /CNW/ - A Hearing Panel of the Prairie Regional Council of the Mutual Fund Dealers Association of Canada ("MFDA") has issued its Reasons for Decision dated May 30, 2022 ("Reasons for Decision"), in connection with a disciplinary hearing held electronically by videoconference in Edmonton, Alberta on March 29, 2022, in the matter of Fangzhou Du (the "Respondent").

In its Reasons for Decision, the Hearing Panel outlined its findings of misconduct and confirmed the sanctions imposed on the Respondent.
