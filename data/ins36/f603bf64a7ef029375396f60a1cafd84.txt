All you need to know about fantasy gaming? Why is India seeing a rise in this sector

In India, the gaming industry has grown significantly during the previous two years. According to Statista, India had a gaming population of 365 million in 2020, which was expected to grow to 510 million by the end of 2022. Based on these data, it is easy to see how beneficial these two years have been for India's gaming business. In this trend gaming business, a huge portion of our massive population resorted to fantasy games.
A fantasy sport is a form of game, commonly played on the Internet, in which participants assemble imagined, or virtual teams made up of proxies for genuine professional athletes.
