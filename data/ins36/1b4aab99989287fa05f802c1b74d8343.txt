Game Room Robbed, Suspect Arrested
iheart.com
news
2022-05-31 12:45:00

Beaumont police have arrested a man for aggravated robbery, felon in possession of a firearm, and city warrants following the robbery of a game room in the 3600 block of Stagg Drive. 30- year old Roderick McKinney was still inside the game room when police arrived on the scene yesterday morning. Witnesses say McKinney entered the store, pulled a gun, and demanded money from an employee.
