Bloomberg | | Posted by Lingamgunta Nirmitha Rao





BMW AG will stop manufacturing its electric Minis in the UK and move production to factories in China, dealing yet another blow to Britain's ambitions in battery-powered vehicles, the Times reported.The German car manufacturer builds around 40,000 electric, Mini vehicles a year at its factory outside Oxford but will call a halt by the end of next year, the newspaper said on Saturday, citing an executive who leads the Mini brand.
