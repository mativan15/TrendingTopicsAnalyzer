NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- MiNK Therapeutics, Inc., a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company pioneering the discovery, development, and commercialization of allogeneic, off-the-shelf, invariant natural killer T (iNKT) cell therapies to treat cancer and other immune-mediated diseases, announced it will release its third quarter 2022 financial results before the market opens on Thursday, November 3rd, 2022. MiNK leadership will host a conference call and webcast at 8:30 a.m. ET that same day.
