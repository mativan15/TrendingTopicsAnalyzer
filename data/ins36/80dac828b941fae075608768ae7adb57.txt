Nothing is Impossible; Epic Ascent Achieved in 8 Days, 23 Hours and 10 Minutes

HONG KONG, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- EquitiesFirst, the global asset-based financing corporation, today celebrated the record-breaking, successful 2022 summit of Everest by legendary 14 Peaks' mountaineer Nimsdai Purja MBE ("Nims").

Photo: Mingma Sherpa. Copyright Nimsdai.

Between May 7 and May 16 2022, Nims set two new World Records. The first record is for speed. Nims summited Everest, Lhotse and Kanchenjunga, in eight days, 23 hours and 10 minutes without oxygen.
