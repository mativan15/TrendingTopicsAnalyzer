May 31—Wednesday, June 1

The Berks Family Business Alliance (FBA) will host its annual Summer Symposium, "The 7 Principles of a Healthy Business," from 7:30 to 9:30 a.m. at the DoubleTree by Hilton Reading, 701 Penn St., Reading. Join Steve Essig, third-generation president of Essig Plumbing & Heating, as he shares the principles that have contributed to success and stability in his business. In addition, he will give his firsthand account of navigating family business complexities — the ups and downs, struggles and accomplishments.
