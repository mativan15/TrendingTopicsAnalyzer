Office of Iran's Sports Ministry/WANA (West Asia News Agency)/Handout via REUTERS PARIS, Oct 19 (Reuters) - Street protests are not a good strategy for opposing Iran's government, an Iranian female boxer in exile said after climber Elnaz Rekabi - who caused controversy by competing in an international contest without a headscarf - returned home to cheering supporters.
