June 01, 2022 03:21 PM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has removed from under review with developing implications and affirmed the Financial Strength Rating of A- (Excellent) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Ratings (Long-Term ICR) of 'a-' (Excellent) of Sutton Specialty Insurance Company (Sutton Specialty) (Oklahoma City, OK), Sutton National Insurance Company (Sutton National) (Oklahoma City, OK), Merit Life Insurance Co. (Merit) (Austin, TX) and 777 Re. Ltd. (Hamilton, Bermuda).
