16 October 2022 by Marta Balaga 
Variety - TV News Palestine's Fresco Films has boarded Tamara Erde and Mathias Énard's TV one-off 'Breaking Bread,' with France's Haut et Court Doc already locked as the main producer. Tony Copti will produce alongside Emma Lepers.Attempting to explore the Israeli-Palestinian conflict through food, 'Breaking Bread' will try to answer one question: Does gastronomy separate us or bring us together?Clocking in at 52 minutes, the documentary – to be shot in Arabic, Hebrew and French – will go into production in the spring of 2023.
