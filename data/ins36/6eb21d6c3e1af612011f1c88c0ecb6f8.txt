HFCL jumps 3% as telecom gear maker wins Rs 237 crore order
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 05:30:00

AgenciesHFCL's revenue from operations in the reported quarter also declined by about 15 per cent to Rs 1,182.98 crore from Rs 1,391.4 crore in the March 2021 quarter. Shares of climbed 2.9 per cent in early trade on Wednesday as the domestic maker of telecom gear said that it has secured an order worth Rs 237.25 crore from a private telecom operator for supplying optical fibre cables. The company, however, did not disclose the name of the customer. HFCL's share price rose to a high of Rs 64.85 as against Rs 63 at the previous close on the BSE.
