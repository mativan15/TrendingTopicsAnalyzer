We are delighted that we were selected to present our clinical validation data and clinical-grade metagenomics tool that enables more accurate detection of pathogens in urine samples compared to culture. NEW YORK (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Biotia, Inc., a health-tech company fighting infectious disease through the power of genomics and artificial intelligence, announced today that it will be presenting at IDWeek 2022. The presentation, titled 'Clinical-Grade Metagenomics in Urinary Tract Infections: Improving Performance of Next-Generation Sequencing Assays Using Internal Controls and Machine Learning,' will be delivered on October 20, from 12:15 to 1:30 p.
