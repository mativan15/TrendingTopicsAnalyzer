The U.K. retail industry has reacted with dismay after the reversal of a tax free Government policy that it says could cost the sector $1.36 billion. More to the point, it's a policy that was mysteriously scrapped as part of Brexit on January 1 2021, but was set to be reintroduced after an announcement made little more than three weeks ago.
