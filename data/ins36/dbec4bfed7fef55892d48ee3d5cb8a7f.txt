Image source, Devon and Cornwall PoliceImage caption, Lee Chugg and Jodie Swannick carried out a "brutal" attack, a judge saidA man and a woman who murdered a charity shop volunteer with learning disabilities have been jailed for life. Lee Chugg and Jodie Swannick kicked and stamped on 60-year-old Stephen Fogg at his home in Wembury, near Plymouth in December while looking for alcohol or money to steal.The drug addicts denied murder but were found guilty at an earlier trial at Exeter Crown Court.
