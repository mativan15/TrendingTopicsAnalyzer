Ally Financial 3Q Profit Falls on Credit-Loss Provision, Impairment Charge
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 11:49:00

By Will Feuer Ally Financial Inc. reported a drop in third-quarter earnings as the company provisioned more for credit losses and booked an impairment charge related to its mortgage business. The financial-services company posted net income of $272 million, down from $683 million a year earlier. Earnings were 88 cents a share, compared with $1.40 a share. Stripping out one-time items, including fair-value adjustments of securities, adjusted earnings were $1.12 a share. Analysts surveyed by FactSet were looking for adjusted earnings of $1.
