Suspect in Hamilton kidnapping of Faqir Ali arrested, charged
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-20 18:22:44

A 27-year-old man is facing a charge in connection with a Mount Hope homicide and abduction investigation dating back to 2021.
Hamilton Police say the man was arrested on Wednesday 'without incident' and charged with a single count of adult kidnapping.Faqir Ali was abducted and one of his two sons was killed during an attack at a residence on Glancaster Road near Dickenson Road shortly before 3 a.m. on Sept. 16, 2021.Police said two men in their 20s — Ali's sons — were found at the home suffering from gunshot wounds with Hasnain (Nano) Ali succumbing to his injuries in hospital.
