Let's talk about sustainable finance this World Investor Week
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 09:22:43

The World Investor Week, which commenced on Oct 10, is a global campaign for raising awareness about the importance of investor education and protection. It is an initiative of the International Organization of Securities Commission, which is an international body that brings together the world's securities regulators and is recognized as the global standard setter for the securities sector.This year, one of the areas of focus at World Investor Week is sustainable finance. The term sustainable finance has become a common occurrence in investment parlance.
