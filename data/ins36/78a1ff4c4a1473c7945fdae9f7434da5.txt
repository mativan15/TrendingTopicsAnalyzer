The mission of JP Morgan Chase &Co (JPM) is to 'be the most respected financial services firm in the world.' In many ways, that is already 'mission accomplished.' JPM is the world's largest bank by market capitalization. It is the fifth-largest bank in the world in terms of total assets—almost US$4 trillion. In the 2008 financial crisis, it was JPM that the U.S. government turned to for help. Just a few words from its silver-haired chairman and CEO Jamie Dimon can lift the entire stock market—albeit briefly.
