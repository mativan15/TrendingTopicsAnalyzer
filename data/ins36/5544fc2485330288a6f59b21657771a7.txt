West White Rose agreement under scrutiny by opposition parties

NDP interim leader Jim Dinn said the resumption of the West White Rose project is good news for workers, but called on the provincial government to make plans for transitioning away from fossil fuels. (Mark Quinn/CBC)The opposition parties say the resumption of the West White Rose oil project is good for Newfoundland and Labrador workers — but they have questions about the agreement between the provincial government and the companies behind the project.In a statement on Tuesday, Cenovus said it has been able to "de-risk" its stake in the project over the past 16 months.
