Hero Motocorp skids 4% as supply roadblocks compel delay of first EV 2-wheeler launch
indiatimes.com

2022-06-02 05:15:00

AgenciesHero Motocorp's scrip fell to a low of Rs 2,640 as against Rs 2,758.55 at the previous close on the BSE. The stock was last trading 2.4 per cent lower.Shares of slid 4.3 per cent in early trade on Thursday after the two-wheeler giant deferred the launch of its first electric two-wheeler on account of supply constraints amid geopolitical tensions. The automobile major had earlier announced that its EV, under the 'Vida' brand, would be launched in July.
