Mallikarjun Kharge joins Rahul Gandhi in 'Bharat Jodo Yatra' in Karnataka
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 09:30:00

IANSAs the October 17 polling day draws closer, Kharge is the hot favourite to win due to the AICC establishment's perceived patronage for his candidature. The Congress presidential candidate Mallikarjun Kharge on Saturday joined Rahul Gandhi and other leaders in the ongoing 'Bharat Jodo Yatra' in the district. Kharge, who had served as the union minister and as the Congress leader in the Lok Sabha, also took part in the mega public meeting here to celebrate the yatra reaching the 1,000-km milestone.
