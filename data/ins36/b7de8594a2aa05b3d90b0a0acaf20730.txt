In its bid to enhance domestic connectivity, IndiGo has announced the launch of eight new exclusive flights on the Bhopal-Udaipur, Ahmedabad-Jammu, Ranchi Bhubaneswar, and Indore-Chandigarh routes in its winter schedule for 2022. Out of these new connections, Bhopal-Udaipur flight will be a regional connectivity scheme (RCS) route and will increase accessibility between the states.

These destinations are known for their breath-taking scenic tourist locations and serve as manufacturing centres of various industrial goods.
