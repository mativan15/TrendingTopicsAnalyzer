ATLANTA (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 Frazier & Deeter (FD), one of the nation's Top 50 accounting and advisory firms, announced today that Chalice Beam, HITRUST CCSFP, CCSK, CDPSE, has joined the firm as HITRUST Director. 'FD's focus on quality, innovation, value and the customer experience has resulted in explosive growth in the HITRUST marketplace,' said Andrew Hicks, FD's National HITRUST Practice Leader.
