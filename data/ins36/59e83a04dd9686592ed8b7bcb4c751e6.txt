They will cuddle you, play with you and, of course, resemble you. They will require minimal resources and will cost next to nothing to bring up. If these sound like ideal children to you, be warned: what has just been described is a virtual child. These metaverse-hosted digital babies, an artificial intelligence expert has predicted, will be commonplace in 50 years.An expert claims overpopulation will prompt society to raise digital children. Catriona Campbell, one of the UK's leading authorities in AI and emerging and disruptive technologies, made the eyebrow-raising prediction in a book published this week.
