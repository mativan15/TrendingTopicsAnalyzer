AgenciesJangiri Madhumitha, a Bigg Boss personality, has given birth to her first child on Sunday, May 29. She was pregnant with Moses Joel, her cousin and an assistant director in the Tamil film industry. Jangiri Madhumitha and Moses Joel married at a prominent Chennai hotel in 2019. Actors and politicians were present during the wedding ceremony. Madhumitha is one of Tamil cinema's most well-known female comedians. She has been in a number of renowned films before becoming well-known for her performance in the movie "A Stone is a Glass", in which she co-starred alongside Santhanam.
