Published: 12:53 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 12:53 EDT, 2 June 2022 By Anisha Sircar and Devik Jain June 2 (Reuters) - U.S. stock indexes rose in choppy trading on Thursday led by Tesla and other growth stocks, with investor focus on Microsoft's forecast cut and comments from a Federal Reserve official ahead of a key jobs report due on Friday. Six of the 11 major S&P sectors advanced. Consumer discretionary and materials were the top gainers, up 1.9% and 2.1%, respectively. Chipmakers added 2.0%, with Nvidia Corp climbing 5.
