Haryana best-governed in big states: Public Affairs Index 2022
 

: Haryana has emerged as the best-governed among 18 big states of the country, according to the Public Affairs Index 2022 prepared by a Bengaluru-based think tank. A Haryana government spokesperson said that the state has topped among the major states with a score of 0.6948, followed by Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Chhattisgarh, Punjab and Karnataka. Chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar said that this could become possible because of the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
