ManpowerGroup stock jumps after profit beat, as labor demand remains 'solid'
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 11:49:13

Shares of ManpowerGroup Inc. MAN, -2.73% jumped 3.8% in premarket trading Thursday, after the staffing services company reported third-quarter profit that beat expectations even as revenue fell a bit short, as demand for labor remained "solid." Net income rose to $111.3 million, or $2.13 a share, from $97.7 million, or $1.77 a share, in the year-ago period. Excluding nonrecurring items, such as integration costs related to the U.S. Experis acquisition, adjusted earnings per share came to $2.21, above the FactSet consensus of $2.
