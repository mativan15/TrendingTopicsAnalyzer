Founder of LifeUSA, retired Chairman & CEO of Allianz Life of North American, Senior Strategy Consultant to All Things Annuity. getty The Federal Reserve bank has begun to implement what it has signaled will be a series of interest rate increases. The objective of these rate increases is to tamp down rapidly rising inflation. While the intent of the Fed's action is commendable, it has not indicated when, how many or how high these interest rate hikes may be. The result is an environment of volatile and uncertain interest rates that make it difficult to make prudent investment or saving decisions.
