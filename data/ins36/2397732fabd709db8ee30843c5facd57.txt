NEW YORK, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Value Line, Inc. (NASDAQ: VALU) announced today that its Board of Directors declared on October 21, 2022 a quarterly cash dividend of $0.25 per common share, payable on November 10, 2022, to stockholders of record on October 31, 2022. The Company has 9,458,837 shares of common stock outstanding as of October 21, 2022. The Board of Directors also on October 21, 2022 approved a renewal of the share repurchase program, effective immediately, allowing the repurchase of shares from time to time, up to an aggregate amount of $3,000,000.
