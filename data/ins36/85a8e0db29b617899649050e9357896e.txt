(PRWEB) October 19, 2022 In a continuing effort to diversify its educational offerings, Manhattanville College will launch a portfolio of online degrees supported by Academic Partnerships(AP), beginning in January 2023. The new online undergraduate and graduate level programs will complement Manhattanville's existing business and nursing programs designed to appeal to students who are seeking alternative options to a traditional college education.
