Woman suffers brain injury after homeless man attacks her with gardening shears

A 24-year-old woman in Los Angeles, in the US, suffered brain damaged after an alleged attack by a man with gardening shears. Kyli Watts was out walking when she was set upon on October 10Kyli Watts, 24, was attacked in the North Hollywood area of Los AngelesA woman is recovering from a brain injury after she was stabbed in the head with gardening shears in an attack allegedly carried out by a homeless man. Kyli Watts, 24, was the victim of what reports say was an unprovoked attack when the four-inch blades struck her head while she was walking down a North Hollywood street on October 10.
