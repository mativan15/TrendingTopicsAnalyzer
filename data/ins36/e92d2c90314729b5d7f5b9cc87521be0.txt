October 19, 2022 11:04 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK & PALO ALTO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Centana Growth Partners, a specialized growth equity firm, announced today the promotion of Matthew Alfieri to Partner.



'Matt has made significant contributions to Centana and our growing portfolio, and his work on many successful investments has justified our confidence in him,' said Ben Cukier, Partner.

Matt Alfieri joined Centana's NYC office in 2017 as a Vice President on the investment team and was promoted to Principal in 2021.
