Published: 16:10 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 16:10 EDT, 16 October 2022 Jacinda Ardern is refusing to buy into claims from her former deputy her Labour government has lost its way, dismissing provocative attacks as "attempts to get a headline". Winston Peters, the 77-year-old mainstay of New Zealand politics, is priming himself for another comeback as his self-styled force, New Zealand First, prepares for next year's election. This time around, he will be campaigning against the woman he put into power.
