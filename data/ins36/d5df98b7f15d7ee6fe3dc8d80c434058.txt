Crops have been damaged and dairy farmers left without electricity as major flooding moves through farmland in northern Victoria.  Farm land across northern Victoria is submerged by floodwaterDairy farms have been left without electricity and crops are submergedMore than 50mm of rain is forecast for some parts of the state later this weekThere was optimism floodwater around Shepparton had peaked at a lower level than expected, however a forecast predicting more rain later in the week has farmers concerned.
