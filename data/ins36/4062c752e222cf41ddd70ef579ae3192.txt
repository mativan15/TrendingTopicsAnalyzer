Wall Street stocks end with weekly gains for first time in two months
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-28 03:09:08

Wall Street stocks rallied Friday following signs of moderating inflation, finishing with weekly gains for the first time in about two months.The Dow Jones Industrial Average climbed 1.8 percent, or about 575 points, to 33,212.96, a weekly increase of 6.2 percent.The broad-based S&P 500 gained 2.5 percent to 4,158.24, while the tech-rich Nasdaq Composite Index jumped 3.3 percent to 12,131.13.It was the first weekly gain for the Dow in eight weeks and the first for the S&P 500 and Nasdaq in seven weeks.
