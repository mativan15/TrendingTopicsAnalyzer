Cramer's Week Ahead: Watch for Consumer Trends to Gauge the State of Inflation
nbcsandiego.com
business
2022-06-03 22:45:00

CNBC's Jim Cramer said Friday that any signs of consumer negativity next week will be a welcome sign for the Federal Reserve in its fight against inflation. "I know it's a total drag to hope for negativity, but that's what we need right now" to bring inflation down, he said. "I bet we'll find that confidence is falling rapidly."
