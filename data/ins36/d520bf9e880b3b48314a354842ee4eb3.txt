Coal India to import coal for supply to states
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-28 15:00:00

State-run will import coal for utilities run by state governments and private companies, according to a federal power ministry letter reviewed by Reuters. India is expected to face a wider coal shortage during the quarter ending September as higher power demand worsens the risk of widespread power outages.
