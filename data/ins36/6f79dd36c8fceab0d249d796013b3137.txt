AgenciesMaharashtra reports 1,357 COVID-19 cases, one deathMaharashtra on Saturday reported 1,357 new coronavirus infections and one pandemic-related death, the state health department said. The state had recorded 1,134 new infections and three deaths the day before. This is the third consecutive day when the state reported more than 1,000 cases. The state has 5,888 active cases now. The caseload in the state increased to 78,91,703 while death toll reached 1,47,865. As many as 595 people recovered during the day, which raised the tally of recovered patients to 77,37,950.
