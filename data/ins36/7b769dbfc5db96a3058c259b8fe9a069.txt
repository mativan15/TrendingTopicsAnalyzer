Tennessee baseball is a win away from a second straight super regional appearance.

The No. 1 Vols (55-7) face No. 2 Georgia Tech on Sunday (7 p.m. ET, ESPNU) at Lindsey Nelson Stadium. Tennessee advances to the super regionals with a win. If UT loses, it will play the Yellow Jackets on Monday for a winner-take-all game.

UT is 2-0 in the Knoxville Regional after trouncing No. 4 Alabama State 10-0 on Friday and edging No. 3 Campbell 12-7 on Saturday.

The Vols set a program record for wins in a season by beating Campbell.
