North councils spend millions to get Levelling Up grants

2022-10-16 08:10:15

Image caption, Towns Fund money will help train young people in Hartlepool civil engineeringCouncils in the north of England have spent millions of pounds preparing bids for government Levelling Up grants.Freedom of Information requests by BBC News reveal 22 councils in the North East and Cumbria spent a total of more than £4.6m.The "time-consuming" process of bidding for competitive funds was criticised by academics last week.The government said the area's £37 per person Levelling Up funding was "the second highest for an English region".
