NEW ORLEANS, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Former Attorney General of Louisiana Charles C. Foti, Jr., Esq. and the law firm of Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ("KSF") are investigating the proposed sale of Steward Health Care System to CareMax, Inc. ("the Company") (NasdaqGS: CMAX). Under the terms of the proposed transaction CareMax will pay $25 million in cash and issue 23.5 million shares of CareMax's Class A common stock to the equityholders of Steward at closing, subject to customary adjustments. In addition, CareMax will fund a Medicare receivable to Steward covering accounts receivable related to 2021 and the pre-close period of 2022.
