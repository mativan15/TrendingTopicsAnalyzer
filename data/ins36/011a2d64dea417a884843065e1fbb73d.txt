Action Against Moonlighting Can Ruin A Person's Career, Says TCS COO
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 09:17:06

At a time when IT giants such as Wipro and Infosys have not only spoken against moonlighting but even fired employees for the same, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) Chief Operating Officer has put forth his views on moonlighting. TCS COO N. Ganapathy Subramaniam told PTI that action against moonlighting can ruin a person's career and, hence, it is important to show empathy while dealing with the issue.   TCSHe said, "Nothing prevents the country's biggest IT services exporter from taking action against employees for moonlighting when it has evidence because it is a part of the service agreement, but young kids will have to be dissuaded.
