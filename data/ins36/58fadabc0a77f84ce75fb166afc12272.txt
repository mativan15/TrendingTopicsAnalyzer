Netball Australia stands by Hancock Prospecting sponsorship despite player backlash
theguardian.com
sport
2022-10-17 21:35:43

Netball Australia has said it is an 'absolute priority' to address and resolve any concerns raised by Diamonds players amid a backlash over a new sponsorship partnership with Hancock Prospecting.But the cash-strapped organisation will not turn its back on Gina Rinehart's mining company and reinforced its support for the multimillion-dollar deal announced last month.The partnership has caused controversy, with Indigenous player Donnell Wallam reported to have raised concerns over Hancock's record on Indigenous issues.
