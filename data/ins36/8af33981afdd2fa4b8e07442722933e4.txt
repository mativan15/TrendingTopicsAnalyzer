Police investigate alleged criminal damage to Man City bus
thehindu.com
sport
2022-10-19 11:37:12

Police have confirmed they have received a complaint of alleged criminal damage to Manchester City's bus following the team's 1-0 Premier League defeat to Liverpool at Anfield on Sunday.An image showed damage to the windscreen of the bus, which City said was caused by an object being thrown from the street.Merseyside Police on Wednesday appealed for further information over the incident.'We have now received a report of an alleged criminal damage of the Manchester City team coach following the Liverpool and Manchester City game on Sunday, 16 October,' the statement said.
