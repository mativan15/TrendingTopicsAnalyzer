A screenshot of Rsquare's Singapore database (Rsquare)South Korean real estate platform Rsquare said Thursday it has collected over 65,000 pieces of commercial real estate data in Singapore, officially launching its business in the major Asia-Pacific hub.The company has compiled both quantitative and qualitative data on office buildings, hotels, distribution centers, factories and other commercial buildings in Singapore's principal districts.'Just like in South Korea and Vietnam, our employees went on foot, visited office buildings and interviewed the owners.
