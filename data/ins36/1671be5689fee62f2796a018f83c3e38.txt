Non-compete clause: union may pursue complaint against Infosys with state authorities
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 14:15:00

Bengaluru: The Pune-based labour union may now pursue its complaint against Infosys on the non-compete clauses in the IT company's offer letters with Maharashtra state's labour ministry as discussions with the Central Labour Ministry hit a dead-end. "A follow-up hearing for joint discussions was held on Thursday in New Delhi and it seems like the issue cannot be resolved amicably as Infosys' team did not turn up again. Also, it appears as the central government may not have the powers to take appropriate action in this case despite labour being a concurrent subject.
