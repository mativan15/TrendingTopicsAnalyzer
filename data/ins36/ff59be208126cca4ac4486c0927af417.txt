Austin, TX (May 31, 2022) – Major League Pickleball (MLP), the elite pickleball league showcasing the world's best professional pickleball players in a unique mixed-gender team format, today announced its full line-up of teams, owners, events, and sponsors, as it prepares to kickstart its second season at MLP 2022 Austin from June 3rd-5th.
