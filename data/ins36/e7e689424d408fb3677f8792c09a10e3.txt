Comporium Promotes Brian Hovis to Vice President of Business Sales

October 19, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ROCK HILL, S.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Comporium is pleased to announce that Brian Hovis has been promoted to Vice President of Business Sales. Hovis now will be responsible for executing the company's business sales strategy and supplying oversight of daily business sales operations for all of Comporium's service areas. Brian also will be taking on the new responsibility of managing the Media Sales teams to further develop and grow their media offerings to business customers.
