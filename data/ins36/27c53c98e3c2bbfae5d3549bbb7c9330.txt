As the Kannada movie 'Kantara' continues to receive love from the audiences, it seems that the Hindi dubbed version of the film has also struck a chord. Going by the box office numbers, the film is earning big bucks and maintaining a strong foothold at the ticket window.The Rishab Shetty starrer which ended its first weekend with a collection of Rs 7.50 crore nett, saw good growth in numbers during the weekdays. According to boxofficeindia.com, 'Kantara' saw a jump in numbers on its first Tuesday.
