Nagaland lottery results to be released today
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 16:00:00

The Nagaland Lottery today results will be out at 8 PM today, i.e. May 28. After the declaration of results, they will be available on the official website. The Nagaland State Lotteries May 27 results were declared yesterday on http://www.nagalandlotteries.com/. Nagaland is amongst the 13 states of India that are allowed to host a legal lottery. The evening lottery Sambad is a popular one. You can try out your luck to win a massive amount by buying a ticket for just INR 6. The body responsible for arranging and managing the Nagaland Lottery is the Directorate of Nagaland State Lotteries.
