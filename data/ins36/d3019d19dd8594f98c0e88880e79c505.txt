Hunter Yancey is graduating from Pleasant Local High School Sunday. He will attend Ohio State Marion in the fall and plans to transfer to the Columbus campus the following spring.

For the Pleasant High School graduating senior who is already building his own video production company, iHunter Productions, with real-world clients, it seemingly was.
