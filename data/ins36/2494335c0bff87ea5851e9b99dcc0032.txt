Is The School For Good And Evil based on a book?
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 22:42:34

The School For Good And Evil is a new fantasy film that has been released on Netflix.
The fairytale movie – directed by Paul Feig – follows best friends Sophie (Sophia Anne Caruso) and Agatha (Sofia Wylie) who are kidnapped and taken to the titular school.
After their fortunes are reversed, the duo tries to figure out a way to return home. In doing so, their friendship is put to the test.
Since it was launched on the streaming platform, viewers have been wondering whether the film is based on a book.
