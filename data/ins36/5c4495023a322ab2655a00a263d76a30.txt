Southbank boss Mark Ball: 'One play changed my life'
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 12:28:00

Who at the start of this year would have wanted to take over a leading arts institution? The sector was still on its knees following the ravages of the pandemic. Most institutions had incurred massive holes in their finances. The public were coming out of lockdown with the timidity of moles from holes. Tourism wasn't anticipated to get back to normal until the middle of the decade. You'd have to have been mad, surely, or at the very least, foolhardy.
Step forward Mark Ball, 54, who in January became the Southbank's new artistic director at a pivotal moment in its fortunes.
