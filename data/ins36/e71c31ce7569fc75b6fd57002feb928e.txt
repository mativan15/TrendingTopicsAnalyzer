Korean Park Hang-seo to end 5-year coaching stint with Vietnam nat'l teamBy Yoo Jee-ho SEOUL, Oct. 17 (Yonhap) -- South Korean football coach Park Hang-seo's successful tenure with the Vietnamese men's national team will come to an end early next year, the country's football governing body announced Monday. In a statement, the Vietnam Football Federation (VFF) said it has come to "a mutual agreement" with Park to end their journey after the end of Park's current deal on Jan. 31, 2023. Park's final tournament with Vietnam will be the ASEAN Football Federation (AFF) Championship in December, the VFF added.
