New York, NY and Oakville, ON, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- For many, a team jersey is the first thing that connects us to our favorite athlete. We wear it to show support for our teams, the player and to showcase a loyalty that often lasts a lifetime. That's why, to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the landmark ruling that changed American education (and sport), Six Star Pro Nutrition® (www.sixstarpro.com), 'The Athlete's Choice' and a leading maker of scientifically formulated premium supplements, announced today that the brand will give away 50 jerseys of female athletes during the month of June.
