REUTERS/Henry Nicholls/File PhotoLONDON, Oct 16 (Reuters) - Britain's new finance minister Jeremy Hunt said he would show investors that he will repair the country's public finances after the original economic plans of Prime Minister Liz Truss triggered a bond market rout."No government can control the markets. No Chancellor should seek to do that," Hunt told BBC television in an interview broadcast on Sunday.
