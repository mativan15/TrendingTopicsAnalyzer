Two suspects arrested for killing 2 Ole Miss students
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 17:00:00

The two persons accused of killing an Ole Miss student and causing injury to another have been arrested. Officials have identified the two suspects as Tristan Holland and Seth Rokitka. Both the accused are of Collierville. Police, on October 16, stated that Tristan Holland was arrested for Accessory After the Fact, and he will be facing extradition back to Oxford.
 This morning at 8:30 AM, Seth Rokitka was taken into custody. His truck was found wrecked in Marshall County. It has since been recovered and impounded.
