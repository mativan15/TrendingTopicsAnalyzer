June 01, 2022 04:46 PM Eastern Daylight Time HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Magnolia Oil & Gas Corporation (NYSE: MGY) ('Magnolia' or the 'Company'), today announced the proposed underwritten block trade (the 'Offering') of 7,500,000 shares of the Company's Class A common stock (the 'Class A Common Stock') by certain affiliates of EnerVest, Ltd. (the 'Selling Stockholders'). Magnolia will not sell any shares of its Class A Common Stock in the Offering and will not receive any proceeds from the sale by the Selling Stockholders of shares of Class A Common Stock.
