Published: 18:50 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 18:50 EDT, 15 October 2022 An unexpected alliance has formed between conservative parents and Arab Americans in Dearborn, Michigan.The groups have come together to stand up against the public school boards that are supplying their children with 'explicit' LGBTQ reading material.Reports indicate a school board meeting in Dearborn, Michigan was disrupted last week when hundreds of protestors denouncing books with LGBTQ content poured into the Dearborn Public Schools administration building.
