Diana Kapp likes Type 2 Fun, the kind of fun that is miserable when you are doing it, but with some distance, you remember it as satisfying. Not everyone looks for fun that isn't fun, but that is what she cares about. "I just love physical challenge," Kapp says. "I love it when it's hard." She says that at the Mount Baldy ski resort in the San Gabriel Mountains, most of the mountain bikers carry their bikes up in the chairlift and then ride down. Kapp does it the opposite way: She rides her bike up, and then she chairlifts down.
