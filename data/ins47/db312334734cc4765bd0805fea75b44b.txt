Youth beaten to death in public view, brothers held in Delhi
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-05 06:15:00

NEW DELHI: A 28-year-old man was murdered in full public view by two brothers in northwest Delhi 's Adarsh Nagar area on Friday. The reason is said to be a monetary dispute . Police have arrested both the accused The murder was captured on a CCTV camera. In the footage, it can be seen that a man, wearing a t-shirt and shorts, was walking when one of the attackers came running and started a fist fight. Another person, who came on a scooty, joined the attack. Later, another man tried to intervene and removed one of the attackers.
