Korean stocks open slightly lower Friday
naver.com
news
2022-10-21 11:34:00

Electronic display boards at Hana Bank in central Seoul show stock and foreign exchange markets Friday morning. [YONHAP] Stocks opened slightly lower Friday as weak export data fanned concerns about an economic slowdown. The benchmark Kospi had slipped 0.85 points, or 0.04 percent, to 2,217.24 points as of 9:15 a.m. Korea's exports in the first 20 days of October contracted 5.5 percent from the previous year, indicating that monthly outbound shipments could decrease on-year for the first time in two years.
