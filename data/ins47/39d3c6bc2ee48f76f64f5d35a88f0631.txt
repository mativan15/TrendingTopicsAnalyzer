Cannabis marketplace Leafly reduces head count by 56 people
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 15:37:20

Leafly Inc. LFLY, +2.60% is eliminating about a fifth of its jobs as it moves to generate cost savings. The cannabis marketplace said it's cutting 56 positions, or 21% of its workforce, through layoffs and attrition. Leafly will book a $500,000 charge in the fourth quarter for expenses related to the move.
