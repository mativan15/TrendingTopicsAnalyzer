Live commerce startup Shopr.tv raises $1.7 million from Y-Combinator, Kunal Shah
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 11:45:00

Shopr.TV foundersShopr.tv, a live commerce startup, has raised $1.7 million in funding from institutional investors like Beenext and Y-Combinator, along with angel investors, including Cred's Kunal Shah. The startup, founded in 2021 by entrepreneurs Akhil Suhag, Akshay Suhag, Mukul Anand, and Tushar Dhara, said it will invest the money in its technology, product, and team. Live commerce is a relatively new trend in the e-commerce industry where the customer can buy a product in real-time while interacting with the seller on a live stream through chats and reactions.
