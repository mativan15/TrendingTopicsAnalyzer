Prop 30: Taxing the rich to fund EVs divides California progressives
cbsnews.com
news
2022-10-17 05:30:00

SACRAMENTO -- A California ballot measure that would tax the rich to help put more electric cars on the road may seem tailor-made to win support from Democrats in a state known for climate leadership but Proposition 30 has one notable opponent: Gov. Gavin Newsom. That's put the Democratic governor on the opposite side of his own party and against his traditional environmental allies.The proposition before voters would add a 1.75% tax on personal income of more than $2 million, or fewer than 43,000 people.
