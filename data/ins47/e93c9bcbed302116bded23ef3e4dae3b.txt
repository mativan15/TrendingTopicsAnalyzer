Affiliates of Phoenix Investors to Break Ground at Beloit Gateway Business Park

Company to construct a state-of-the-art industrial facility. 
BELOIT, Wis., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- An affiliate of Phoenix Investors ("Phoenix"), a national private commercial real estate firm headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, has acquired a 56.2-acre site within the Gateway Business Park in Beloit, Wisconsin. Phoenix selected general contracting firm Corporate Contractors, Inc (CCI) to manage and provide construction services to build a 500,000-square-foot speculative industrial warehouse intended for a future tenant.
