Dream Fulfilled For Imelda Marcos As Family Reclaims Presidency
ibtimes.com
news
2022-05-27 04:18:32

Decades after fleeing the Philippines with bags and boxes stuffed with jewels, gold and cash, former first lady Imelda Marcos has achieved her ultimate ambition: her son winning the presidency.The frail but flamboyant 92-year-old matriarch has been the driving force in the family's comeback from exile to the peak of power in the May 9 polls.President-elect Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos has described his mother as "the supreme politician in the family", one who wields a "very big influence". As first lady, Imelda gained celebrity status in the Philippines and abroad, but she also inspired the derisive adjective 'Imeldific' for her excess Photo: AFP / STR Known for her lavish spending, Imelda symbolises the greed of her husband Ferdinand Marcos's dictatorship, which ended in 1986 after a popular revolt ousted him.
