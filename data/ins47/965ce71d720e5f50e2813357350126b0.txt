India among very few standout performers in a world of uncertainties: FM Sitharaman
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 02:45:00

PTIUnion Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman (File Pic)In a world of uncertainties, India is one of the very few standout performers, Union Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman said on Friday, a day after the International Monetary Fund described the country as a bright spot in a global economy which is facing an imminent recession. Sitharaman was addressing the International Monetary Finance Committee during the ongoing annual meeting of the World Bank and the IMF. 
"In a world of uncertainties, India is one of the very few standout performers," the minister said.
