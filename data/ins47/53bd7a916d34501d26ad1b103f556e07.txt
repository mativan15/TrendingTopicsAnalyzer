Xinhua | Updated: 2022-06-02 18:51 Workers are busy at the production line at SAIC Motor's Lingang plant in Shanghai, on April 23, 2022. [Photo/VCG] BEIJING -- China's Ministry of Industry and Information Technology on Thursday pledged to strengthen support for the industrial economy despite mounting downward pressure, as part of efforts to stabilize the country's economic fundamentals. The implementation of the country's package of measures, such as tax and fee cuts, should be accelerated to help enterprises tide over their difficulties as early as possible, said Tao Qing, a ministry official.
