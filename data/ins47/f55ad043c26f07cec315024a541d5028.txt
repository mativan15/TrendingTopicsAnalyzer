Divided Loyalties In Ukraine City As Russia Presses Assault
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-19 05:36:30

In the eastern city of Bakhmut, the few remaining residents stock up on food and clothes for the winter to the sounds of artillery
Patriotic messages stating the fact that "Bakhmut is Ukraine" are scrawled on monuments all around this frontline city.But not everybody agrees that it should be so.The subject is up for debate at the local market, where the city's few remaining residents stock up on food and clothes for the winter to the sounds of artillery.One shopper, Yulia, said she believed Ukrainian forces bombed cities that were about to be captured by Russia -- repeating a conspiracy theory popular on social media.
