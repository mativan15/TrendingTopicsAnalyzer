China's zero-Covid policy has become one of the reasons for growing dissatisfaction. (File)Beijing: Ahead of the result of the 20th National Congress meeting, the protest against Chinese President Xi Jinping showed how dissatisfaction has gripped the society and especially in the wake of its draconian zero-Covid policy.Just before the beginning of the once-in-five-year National Congress in Beijing, people protested against the country's authoritarian system on October 13, The Geneva Daily reported.
