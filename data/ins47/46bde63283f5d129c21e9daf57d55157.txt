Queen Elizabeth II's demise ignited a juggle in the British Royal Family. Many royals have been appointed to new roles, and one of them is the Queen's daughter-in-law, Sarah Ferguson, Duchess of York. Sarah is the former wife of Prince Andrew, the younger brother of King Charles III. According to reports, the deceased Queen Elizabeth II's most beloved corgis, Sandy and Muick, now belong in the custody of Sarah and Andrew. The two were the most treasured pets of the Queen. Sarah shared the pictures of her having a good time with the canines to mark her 63rd birthday on October 15.
