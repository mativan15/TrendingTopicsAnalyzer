(RTTNews) - Retail major Walmart announced its plans to build four new high-tech fulfillment centers in the United States with each creating more than 1,000 new jobs. The company said it will open centers in Joliet, Illinois; Greencastle, Pennsylvania; and McCordsville, Indiana. Meanwhile, the fourth center is yet to be announced. In Joliet, Illinois, 45 miles southeast of Chicago, the new 1.1 million square-foot facility is set to open in summer 2022.
