Can Europe realize its tech ecosystem potential and close the gap with Silicon Valley? Photo by Léonard Cotte on Unsplash 
Given its size and economic might, Europe's story around technology continues to be one of unrealized potential. We have always had the talent, but we haven't always done a good job of retaining the talent or monetizing our innovations. However, with startup valuations skyrocketing, record numbers of unicorns, and ever-increasing volumes of capital flowing into the European tech ecosystem, Europe is finally making its presence felt on the world stage.
