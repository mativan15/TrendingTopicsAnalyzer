Was British Rail really so awful? In a word, yes
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 11:00:00

Christian Wolmar is described by his publishers as Britain's foremost transport journalist, and no doubt he is. Journalism about transport is always of some interest, whether it is the news that the people we hope will be driving our trains will be on strike, or that a much-touted new service is behind schedule, or that a new road is about to concrete over a vast part of our countryside, but such things rarely improve one's spirits. Nor, one might expect, would a history of British Rail such as Wolmar has written; and one would be right.
