While it is true that the number of units of a given cryptocurrency is limited, there appears to be no limit on the number of currencies that can be developed. The number of cryptos has risen by 70% in the last year. The crypto development is reminiscent of the periods in which independent American banks each issued their own currencies. This ended in an implosion which only the strong survived. Thus far, 2022 has brought lower prices. Price has been moving sideways recently in what might be seen as the building of a base.
