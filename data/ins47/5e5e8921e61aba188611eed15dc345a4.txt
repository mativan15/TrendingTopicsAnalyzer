Published: 07:18 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 07:24 EDT, 27 May 2022 A police officer has been sacked after going to collect a mat for his new hot tub at Homebase instead of responding to an urgent call to assist a suicidal teenager.When the emergency call came in, PC Tristan Hankins told dispatchers he would be free to respond 'very, very shortly' and chose to continue with his errand, drive off his patch and collect the mat.It was found that the officer arrived at the scene up to 10 minutes later than he would have done, had he not ran his errand.
