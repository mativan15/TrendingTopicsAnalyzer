If you've been sleeping with the lights on after binging terrifying true crime shows, you're not alone.It seems everyone - including Hollywood's biggest stars - has become obsessed with true crime as streaming platform Netflix and more release shows, podcasts and movies about real life mysteries.Fox News Digital spoke to psychotherapist Dr. Robi Ludwig who explained why society, and celebrities, are so fascinated by these horrific real life crimes."I think human beings, first of all, love a mystery and we also love extremes," Ludwig explained.
