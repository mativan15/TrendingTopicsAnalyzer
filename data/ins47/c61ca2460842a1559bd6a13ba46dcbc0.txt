Cormac Boylan immediately fell in love with the classic car (Image: Submitted)A young petrolhead has rescued an abandoned Rolls Royce from a car park and has plans to restore it to its former glory.Cormac Boylan claims it will be better to spend thousands of pounds on restoring the classic car than on a girlfriend "you no longer love".The 22-year-old is already known as "Mr Rolls Royce" among friends after purchasing his first at the age of 20, and now he has added a second to his garage, reports Leicestershire Live.
