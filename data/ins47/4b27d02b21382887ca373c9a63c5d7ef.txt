Ascend Elements Begins Construction of Apex 1 in Southwestern Kentucky

2022-10-21 01:15:00

140-acre facility and campus will produce sustainable EV battery precursor and cathode active material for 250,000 electric vehicles per yearHOPKINSVILLE, Ky., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ascend Elements began construction of its largest electric vehicle battery recycling and engineered materials manufacturing facility with a ceremonial groundbreaking today. Leaders of the fast-growing startup were joined by Gov. Andy Beshear, local business and community leaders, and EV battery industry executives during the midday event in Hopkinsville, Ky.
