Apple Announces New iPad With USB-C and Magic Keyboard Support
ign.com
news
2022-10-18 16:04:26

Apple has announced a new model refresh of its entry-level iPad, which now includes support for USB-C charging and a significant overhaul to the tablet's design.

The 10th-generation iPad Pro features a 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display with flatter edges, bearing some similarities with the last two generations of the iPad Air or the most recent iPad Mini refresh. Instead of the front camera placed on the top of the display, the latest iPad now moves the camera to the longer side.
