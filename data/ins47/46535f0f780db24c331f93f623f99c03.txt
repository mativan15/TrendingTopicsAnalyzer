OKX is empowering investors by providing them with more trading opportunities

SEYCHELLES, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- OKX , the world's second largest cryptocurrency exchange, today announced that it has launched BTC/USDC and ETH/USDC perpetual contracts.



USDC is considered the world's safest, most reliable and most transparent stablecoin. In recent years, OKX has been expanding support for USDC and other related products. OKX now offers the most USDC pairs of any crypto exchange, with 126 USDC trading pairs and 1 USDC margin pairs.
