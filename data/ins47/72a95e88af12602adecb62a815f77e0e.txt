Conmed Corp. Shares Fall 10%, Plans Private Offering of Convertible Sr Notes
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 20:57:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Conmed Corp. are trading lower in Tuesday's late session, after the company said it plans to offer convertible senior notes in a private offering. The medical technology company plans to offer $600 million of convertible senior notes due in 2027. At 4:19 p.m. ET, shares had fallen 10.3% to trade at $106. The stock ended the day's regular session with a 0.26% gain, closing at $116.29. The stock touched a 52-week low of $107.08 on May 10. Write to Stephen Nakrosis at stephen.
