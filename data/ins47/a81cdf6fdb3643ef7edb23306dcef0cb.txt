Several of the largest banks and credit unions in the Albany area announced new executive hires and new board directors within the last month.

Meet the recent hires and new board members at TrustCo Bank, Sunmark Credit Union and more.

Sunmark hires chief risk officer

Sunmark Credit Union has hired Vik Muktavaram as chief risk officer.

In this role, Muktavaram will oversee financial and operational risk management; regulatory management; vendor management; legal affairs; fraud and loss mitigation; security; and quality control.
