Naperville Central's Michael Umbright stayed in the moment Saturday.The sophomore infielder felt playing composed and poised gave him the best chance to ignite a rally in the fifth inning for the Redhawks. And that's exactly what he did.'We never give up on the game,' said Umbright, whose RBI single in the bottom of the fifth helped close a four-run deficit. 'We hit the ball hard, but the game didn't go our way.'On a day of upsets in the Romeoville Sectional, Naperville Central's quest for a Class 4A regional title ended with a 5-3 loss to unheralded Oswego at Waubonsie Valley.
