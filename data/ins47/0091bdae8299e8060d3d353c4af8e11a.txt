Financials Down Amid Stagflation Fears -- Financials Roundup
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 20:45:00

Shares of banks and other financial institutions fell sharply as global markets were buffeted by stagflation fears. U.K. consumer-price inflation topped 10% in September, spurring concerns about a prolonged era of slowing growth and rising inflation. The yield on the two-year Treasury rose to the highest level since 2007. "A combination of persistent inflation, rising rates, falling growth estimates, and heightened financial stress" is weighing on the stock market, said Mark Haefele, chief investment officer at money manager UBS Global Wealth Management, in a note to clients.
