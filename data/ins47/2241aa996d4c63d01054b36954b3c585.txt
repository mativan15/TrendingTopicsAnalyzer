Chancellor to reveal parts of his medium-term fiscal plan today, Treasury says

Chancellor Jeremy Hunt will reveal parts of his medium-term fiscal plan later today - two weeks earlier than planned.The Treasury said the chancellor, who was installed on Friday, was fast-tracking the plans, which will be released in full on 31 October.
It said the decision followed conversations with Prime Minister Liz Truss over the weekend and a meeting with the governor of the Bank of England and the head of the Debt Management Office on Sunday night.Politics latest: More U-turns expected on mini-budgetMr Hunt is set to make a statement at 11am before addressing the House of Commons at around 3.
