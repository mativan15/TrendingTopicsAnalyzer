Barramundi is a native Australian fish but 60 per cent of stock we eat is imported
brisbanenews.net
news
2022-10-20 21:25:57

North Queensland barramundi farmers are backing a bill that would see internationally imported seafood labelled on menus across the state.The Country of Origin Labelling Bill would require restaurant owners to label imported seafood dishesNorth Queensland barramundi farmers say the labels would help boost demand for local seafoodThe federal government has indicated it wants to implement country of origin labelling at a national levelDespite being a native fish, not all barramundi Australians consume comes from local waters.
