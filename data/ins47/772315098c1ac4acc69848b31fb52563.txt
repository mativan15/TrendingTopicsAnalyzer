ADIA, Brookfield, KKR and TPG to separately invest Rs 4,040 cr in UPL
indiatimes.com
economics

UPL on Friday announced that Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA), Brookfield, KKR and TPG will be separately investing Rs 4,040 crore, or US$ 500 million for minority stakes in distinct pure play business platforms. A wholly owned subsidiary of the ADIA, Brookfield and TPG will invest Rs 1,580 crore, or US$ 200 million for a 9.09% stake in UPL SAS - India Agtech Platform at an equity valuation of around Rs 17,380 crore, or US$ 2.2 billion, the company said in a statement.
