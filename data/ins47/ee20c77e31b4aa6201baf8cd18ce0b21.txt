Edtech unicorn Eruditus lays off employees to focus on profitability
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 13:30:00

AgenciesThe company turned unicorn in 2021 when it raised $650 million at the valuation of $3.2 billion. In March, Eruditus raised $350 million in debt funds from the Canada Pension Plan Investment Board, a global investment management company.Ed-tech platform Eruditus Group, which is valued USD 3.2 billion, is laying off a section of its employees, India and globally, as a part of its business restructuring to focus on profitability. Eruditus Group owns Eruditus Executive Education and Emeritus.
