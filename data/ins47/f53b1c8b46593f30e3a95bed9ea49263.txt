Prime Video Launches Live-Streamed Concert Series Amazon Music Live
adweek.com
news
2022-10-18 17:23:48

Prime Video is expanding its livestreaming lineup with the launch of a new weekly live-streamed concert series called Amazon Music Live.
The concert series will air on the streamer following Thursday Night Football.
It premieres on Oct. 23 with musical guest Lil Baby, followed by three-time Grammy winner Megan Thee Stallion on Nov. 3 and country music star Kane Brown on Nov. 10. More performances will be announced in the coming weeks.
'Whether you're a sports fan waiting for the first football game of the week, or a music fan staying up late to hear the latest drop from your favorite artist, Thursday is the biggest night for entertainment,' Kirdis Postelle, global head of artist marketing of Amazon Music, said in a statement.
