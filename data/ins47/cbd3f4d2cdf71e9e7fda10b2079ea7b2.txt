Timberwolves unveil food, beverage program at Target Center for 2022-2023 season
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 19:09:00

Jester Concept's Butcher & the Boar revival and St. Paul mainstay El Burrito Mercado are among the new recruits for the lineup of Target Center concessionaires.

The food and beverage program for the 2022-2023 Minnesota Timberwolves season was unveiled Monday ahead of the team's home opener Wednesday against the Oklahoma City Thunder. The announcement highlighted several new food options, including a stand from plant-based Minneapolis startup Wicked Kitchen, as well as many returning fan favorites, plus a new premium wine program.
