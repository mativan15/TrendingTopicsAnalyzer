YICHANG, China, Oct. 16, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Angel Yeast, (SH600298), the world's leading yeast manufacturer, has announced it aims to play a larger role in increasing people's access to nutritious food, and making food systems more inclusive and sustainable, as part of events to commemorate World Food Day.
