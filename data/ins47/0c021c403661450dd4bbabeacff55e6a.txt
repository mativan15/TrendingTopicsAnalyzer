Families with extra needs deserve public supportGETTY IMAGESNo one could remain unmoved by James Coney's searingly honest article 'I'm a middle-class benefit claimant and I'm terrified' (Money, last week). It is convenient to imagine that 'the vulnerable' are a different species who are not, and never could be, part of our world or class, but through a simple twist of fate any of us can become vulnerable or a carer.The mismanagement and underfunding of social care is truly understood only by people who, like Coney, have to grapple with it constantly; but some recognition that we are all in this together would be a welcome step change.
