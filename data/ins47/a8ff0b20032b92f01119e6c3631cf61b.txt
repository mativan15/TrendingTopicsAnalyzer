MANSFIELD, Pa. and DOYLESTOWN, Pa., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Citizens Financial Services, Inc. (NASDAQ: CZFS) (the "Company" or "CZFS"), the holding company for First Citizens Community Bank ("FCCB"), and HV Bancorp, Inc. (NASDAQ: HVBC) ("HVBC"), the holding company for Huntingdon Valley Bank ("HVB"), are pleased to jointly announce entry into a definitive agreement (the "Agreement") pursuant to which HVBC will merge with and into CZFS.
