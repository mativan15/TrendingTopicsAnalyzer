U.S. stock market rebound will run out of steam, Wall Street's most vocal bear says
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 18:18:00

Morgan Stanley's Mike Wilson landed a career-defining call by warning clients, and anybody else who would listen, about the brutality that global stocks have faced this year. Given this recent track record, and with U.S. stocks giving back some of last week's rebound on Tuesday, it's probably worth taking a look at what Wilson and his team are saying about the latest rebound in equities after the main U.S. benchmarks broke a historic losing streak last week. For the Dow Jones Industrial Average at least the eight week slide that ended last week was the longest since the administration of President Warren Harding.
