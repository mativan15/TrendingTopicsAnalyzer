Cardiff Blues scrum-half Tomos Williams has scored 18 tries in 102 matches for his regionWales scrum-half Tomos Williams has signed a new "long-term" contract to remain with Cardiff Blues.Williams, 26, capped 22 times by Wales, was out of contract at the end of the season, with Cardiff Blues stating he attracted "widespread interest" from other teams."Retaining Tomos was important for us," said director of rugby Dai Young."We have managed to keep all of the players we wanted in what has been a difficult year.
