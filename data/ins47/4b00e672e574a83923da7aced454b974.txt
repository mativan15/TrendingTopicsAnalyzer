October 19, 2022 04:45 PM Eastern Daylight Time LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Regulatory News:

Pershing Square Holdings, Ltd. (LN:PSH) (LN:PSHD) (NA:PSH) today released its regular weekly Net Asset Value ('NAV') and performance returns on its website, https://pershingsquareholdings.com/performance/net-asset-value-and-returns/. The NAV and returns were computed as of the close of business on Tuesday, 18 October 2022.

PSH NAV per share as of close of business on 18 October 2022 was 47.81 USD / 42.22 GBP and year-to-date performance was -15.
