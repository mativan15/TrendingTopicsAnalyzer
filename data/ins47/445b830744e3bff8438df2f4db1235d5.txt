Flexing and fundraising: Classes raise money for displaced Ukrainians settling in Manitoba
cbc.ca
news
2022-06-04 19:27:00

A group of people took part in a donation-based strength class on Saturday, the proceeds of which will go to Ukrainians settling in Manitoba. (Anne-Louise Michel/Radio-Canada)Newcomers to Manitoba were among a number of Winnipeggers who took part in an active fundraiser for displaced Ukrainians on Saturday.Altea Active, a gym in Winnipeg's Bridgwater neighbourhood, teamed up with the local chapter of the Ukrainian Canadian Congress to offer donation-based yoga, strength and cycling classes as well as a barbecue and raffles.
