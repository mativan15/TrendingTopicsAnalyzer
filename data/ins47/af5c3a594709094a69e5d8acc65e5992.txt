Many factors force businesses to be flexible, nimble, and, above all, innovative. In today's business landscape, it is imperative to innovate on a regular basis. And it doesn't just happen out of the thin air; it must be planned for, budgeted for, and executed.That's why it's critical that senior executives prioritize these digital investments. This panel discussion, sponsored by SAP, Fast Company, and Inc. features Greg Petraetis, managing director, SAP, and Kevin Hettrich, CFO of QuantumScape. They share how organizations should embrace innovation and take the next step on a digital transformation journey, keeping them a step ahead of the competition and ready for the future.
