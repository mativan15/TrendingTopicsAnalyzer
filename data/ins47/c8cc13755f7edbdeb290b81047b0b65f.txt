Rescue plan sought for virtual training group Clarity Engagement Solutions
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-17 04:00:00

Clarity Engagement Solutions, a Dublin-based performance consulting business that provides virtual training to global pharmaceutical companies, has moved to appoint an adviser to work out a rescue plan for the troubled company under a new process for small and micro companies.Clarity Learning Systems, trading as Clarity Engagement and C3H, has appointed Declan Hanly of Crowe Ireland to restructure debts under the Small Companies Administration Process (Scarp) unveiled last year as a cost-effective alternative to the examinership process.
