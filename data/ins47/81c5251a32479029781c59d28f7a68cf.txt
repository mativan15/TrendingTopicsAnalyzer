BIT Mining Q1 revenue falls sharply after China's crypto ban (Cryptocurrency:BTCM-USD)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-27 12:52:36

peshkov/iStock via Getty Images BIT Mining (NYSE:BTCM) Q1 revenue on Friday has nosedived from the fourth quarter, hindered by its mining pool business after China banned cryptocurrency transactions last year. Moreover, revenue of $296.68M cratered from $497.78M in Q4 2021, but remains elevated from just $2.6M in the year-ago period. Operating expenses were $298.8M in Q1, compared with $501.6M in Q4 2021 and $3.9M in Q1 2021. Overall, the crypto miner posted a net loss of $3.75M in Q1, improving from -$17.
