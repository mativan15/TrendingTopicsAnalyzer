Jun. 2—WILLMAR — Willmar Senior High School has named students of the month for May. Each month, one student is selected from each of the departments based on criteria established by the departments.

The students, their grade and department are Amelia Reed, 12, Ag Technology; Alexis Flaten, 11, Business; Yahely Guillen Martinez, 10, English Language Arts; Fernanda Ossa Letelier, 10, Math; Ayub Dahir, 9, Multilingual; Brooklyn Mueske, 12, Music; Nina Dawson, 12, Physical Education & Health; Eden Winckler, 9, Science; Enrique Valladraez, 10, Social Studies; Olivia Burgher, 10, Studio Art; Carson Ammerman, 12, Successful Life Strategies; Isaiah Iedema, 11, Technology Education and Samuel Raitz, 11, World Languages.
