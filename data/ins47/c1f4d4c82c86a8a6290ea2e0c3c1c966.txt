getty The Covid-19 pandemic has forced healthcare organizations to rapidly adapt and change the way they operate. In today's world, transformed by the pandemic, it is crucial for healthcare businesses to continue to evolve to meet the needs of providers and patients and stay ahead of the curve. Healthcare companies weathered the pandemic differently based on whether they decided to pivot resources to support Covid-19 or stay focused on their primary business without wavering.
