Yamana Gold stock soars after Gold Fields buyout deal valuing the miner at $6.7 billion
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 13:14:02

The U.S.-listed shares of Yamana Gold Inc. AUY, -0.96% YRI, +2.58% soared 15.3% in premarket trading Tuesday, after the Canada-based gold and silver miner announced an agreement to be acquired by Gold Fields Ltd. GFI, +4.10% in a stock deal that values Yamana at about $6.7 billion. Under terms of the deal, all Yamana shares will be exchanged for 0.6 Gold Field shares, which based on Friday's closing prices would value Yamana's stock at $7.32 each, a 41.6% premium. Gold Fields' stock slumped 12.6% in premarket trading.
