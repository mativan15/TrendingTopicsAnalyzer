REUTERS/Andrew Kelly/FilesRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comJune 2 (Reuters) - The U.S. CFTC filed a federal lawsuit in New York accusing Gemini Trust Co of making false and misleading statements concerning a bitcoin futures contract the firm was pursuing in 2017.The agency contends that Gemini, a crypto exchange led by Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss, violated federal laws governing commodities, and is seeking civil fines and other remedies.
