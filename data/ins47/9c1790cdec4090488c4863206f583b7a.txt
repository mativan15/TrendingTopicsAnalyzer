Liverpool legend Steven Gerrard was dismissed by Aston Villa on Thursday night after his struggling team won just two of their first 11 Premier League games this seasonSteven Gerrard was tipped to be the next Liverpool managerIt wasn't supposed to be like this. For years, Steven Gerrard was billed as Jurgen Klopp's successor at Liverpool. First came his spell in the Reds' academy, then he led Rangers to a first Scottish Premiership title in a decade. It seemed as if success at Aston Villa was the next logical step in Gerrard's journey to becoming a Merseyside manager, yet that stone has crumbled beneath him.
