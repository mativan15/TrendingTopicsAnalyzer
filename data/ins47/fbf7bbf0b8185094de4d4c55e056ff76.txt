2022-10-17 15:45:00

APVolodymyr Zelenskyy to publish book of wartime speeches in DecemberA collection of wartime speeches by Volodymyr Zelenskyy, featuring an introduction written by the Ukrainian president, will be published Dec 6. Crown, a Penguin Random House division, announced Monday that the book will be called "A Message from Ukraine" and will also include a preface by Arkady Ostrovsky, the Economist's editor for Russia and Eastern Europe . "Supporting Ukraine is not a trend, a meme or a viral challenge," Zelenskyy said in a statement released by Crown.
