Linus Idahosa is building the much-needed capacity for Africa's creative industry through the Del-York Group
businessinsider.com
news
2022-06-01 09:04:47

Founded in 2007 by Mr. Idahosa, Del-York Group is a conglomerate consisting of companies operating in media & communication, international public relations, specialised infrastructure development, education, and technology, etc. Del-York International, which is the strategic communication and creative agency arm of the conglomerate, was specifically established for the sole purpose of closing the talent gap in Africa's creative industry; through the cultivation of creative products and talents in Africa.
