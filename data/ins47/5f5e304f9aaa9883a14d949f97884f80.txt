FORT WAYNE, Ind., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Fort Wayne, Indiana-based Allied Payment Network, Inc. (Allied), the industry's most progressive provider of online and mobile money movement services to banks and credit unions, announced today that it has for the third consecutive year been named one of the 2022 Best Places to Work in Financial Technology. The awards program was created in 2017 and is a project of Arizent and Best Companies Group to identify, recognize and honor the best employers in the financial technology industry.
