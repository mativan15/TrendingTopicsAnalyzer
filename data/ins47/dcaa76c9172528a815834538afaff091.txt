Naver's founding member Kim Jeong-ho to replace Kakao founder at Brian Impact foundation



Kakao founder Kim Beom-su (left) and Bear.Better. CEO Kim Jeong-ho (Kakao, Brian Impact foundation)Kakao founder Kim Beom-su has stepped down as chairman at the Brian Impact foundation he established last year for philanthropic projects, the nonprofit said Friday.With the announcement, Kim has resigned from all chairman posts including his resignation as Kakao chairman of the board in March. Kim will keep his position as the head of the Kakao Future Initiative Center.
