October 20, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SCOTTSDALE, Ariz.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Act!, a leading customer relationship management (CRM) and marketing automation platform provider for small businesses, today announced the appointment of Jim Campbell as chief financial and operations officer. In his role, Campbell will be responsible for directing all operations, financial and corporate planning matters as well as business systems, project management office, legal and risk management functions.



Campbell is an accomplished operating executive who has spent the last 15+ years serving the private equity portfolio sector, leading expedited change for various B2B and SaaS companies.
