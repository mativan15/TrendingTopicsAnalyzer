The Future of the Stock Market: Why Chase CEO Jamie Dimon Warns 'Brace Yourself' for Economic 'Hurricane'
stamfordadvocate.com
business
2022-06-02 16:30:00

The economy has faced a whirlwind of recent pressures contributing to investment uncertainty and steep falls in the stock market. The market losses at the hand of factors like inflation, the conflict in Russia and Ukraine and rising interest rates has prompted predictions as to what exactly comes next for the global economy as these opposing factors reach their boiling point. JP Morgan Chase CEO, Jamie Dimon, shared a frank warning that 'you better brace yourself' for an economic 'hurricane.'Dimon argues that while the current economic landscape isn't as threatening as the conditions that led to the Great Recession, there is certainly an inevitable whirlwind that may lead to unfavorable outcomes for the larger economy.
