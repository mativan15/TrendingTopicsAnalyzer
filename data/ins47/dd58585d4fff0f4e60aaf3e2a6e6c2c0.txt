India pushes for $7.7 bln value for IDBI Bank in stake sale- Bloomberg
reuters.com
world
2022-10-21 06:03:31

BENGALURU, Oct 21 (Reuters) - India is seeking a valuation of around 640 billion rupees ($7.73 billion) for state-owned IDBI Bank Ltd (IDBI.NS), Bloomberg News reported on Friday, as part of the government's stake-sale in the lender.Earlier this month, India had invited bids for a 60.72% stake in the bank, which is 45.48% owned by the government and 49.24% by state-owned Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) (LIFI.NS).Potential investors, ranging from domestic and foreign banks to non-banking financial firms and private equity funds, have shown initial interest, the report said on Friday, citing a person familiar with the matter.
