Union commerce minister Piyush Goyal on Thursday said he believes that whoever comes into the government in the United Kingdom will be wanting to engage with India. And India, on the other hand, will wait and watch the political developments in Britain following Liz Truss's resignation on Thursday. Liz Truss will stay in charge until her successor is elected by the Tory party.
