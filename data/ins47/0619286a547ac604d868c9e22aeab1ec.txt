October 18, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Playmaker Capital Inc. (TSXV: PMKR) ('Playmaker') the digital sports media company that delivers authentic content experiences through its portfolio of sports media and technology brands, announced today that it has acquired Wedge Traffic Limited dba Wedge ('Wedge') to enhance its iGaming and affiliate presence in the U.S. market. Wedge's technical and affiliation expertise, combined with Playmaker's ecosystem of highly engaged sports fans, will create significant value for online sportsbooks and casino operators that are vying to acquire and retain customers, and facilitate a safe and fulfilling iGaming user experience.
