Getty Images/Getty Images News Thesis On October 18th BYD (OTCPK:BYDDF) stock jumped as much as 7% in the Hong Kong trading session (Reference: 1211 BYD) after the company reported that net income for Q3 2022 could almost quadruple versus the same period one year prior, citing strong consumer demand. Notably, BYD's upbeat forecast comes at a time when global investor sentiment towards China-based equities turns ultra-cautious, and at a time when the company's most famous western investor - Warren Buffet - is reportedly selling down his stake.
