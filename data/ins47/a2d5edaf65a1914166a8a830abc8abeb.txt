What business can learn from Russia, Ukraine and the semiconductor shortage
venturebeat.com
news
2022-06-01 20:07:00

Unless you've been living under a rock, you may have heard the global supply chain has experienced some issues over the past few years. Labor shortages, ships running aground in key waterways and rising costs have all taken turns dominating the headlines, thrusting complex systems we largely take for granted into the light for all to see. As we move into a post-pandemic reality, it might seem like those problems will start to subside and slowly return to 'normal,' but the global economy is still squarely feeling the impact of the pandemic and it will be a slow crawl to get back to where we were in 2019.
