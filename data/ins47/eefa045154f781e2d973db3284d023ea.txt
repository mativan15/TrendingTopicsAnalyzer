Person uses fallen officer's name to scam people in Port Orange, police say
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 11:57:47

Port Orange police said someone is trying to scam people in the name of a fallen police officer.

Officer Jason Raynor was fatally shot during a traffic stop in 2021.

Police said someone is going door to door trying to use Raynor's name to raise funds.
