October 17, 2022 4:37:15 pm October 17, 2022 4:37:15 pmAAP Rajya Sabha MP Sanjay Singh was detained along with other AAP leaders and workers as they protested outside CBI headquarters against Delhi Deputy CM Manish Sisodia being summoned. (Express photo by Praveen Khanna)The party leaders and workers were holding a sit-in protest outside the CBI office. (Express photo by Praveen Khanna)CBI has summoned Sisodia in connection with the now-withdrawn Delhi Excise Policy 2021-22. (Express photo by Praveen Khanna)"We are sitting on a peaceful protest but Modi's police is dragging and harrassing woman and senior leaders," said Sanjay Singh.
