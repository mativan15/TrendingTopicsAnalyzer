Wisconsin lands Crystal Ball Prediction for 2023 four-star CB Jace Arnold
usatoday.com
news
2022-06-01 17:56:25

On Wednesday, 247Sports writer Evan Flood gave his Crystal Ball Prediction for 2023 cornerback Jace Arnold out of Marietta, Georgia to commit to the University of Wisconsin.
Per the 247Sports composite, Arnold is the No. 443 overall prospect in the 2023 recruiting class, the No. 46 CB in the country, and the No. 40 player in the state of Georgia.
The Marietta High School standout currently holds offers from Wisconsin, Vanderbilt, North Carolina, East Carolina, Florida, Georgia, Georgia State, Georgia Tech, Louisville, Mississippi State, Ole Miss, and Tennessee.
