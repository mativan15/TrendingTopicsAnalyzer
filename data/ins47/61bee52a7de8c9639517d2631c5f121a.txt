(Disney+ via AP)One of Hollywood's most successful franchises, Star Wars, has come openly to support Moses Ingram over the issue of racism. Ingram is the new addition to the franchise, and she has played the role of Inquisitor Reva in the recent release and a much-awaited series "Obi-Wan Kenobi." The web series belongs to Disney+, and its first episode aired on May 27, 2022. Although the web series garnered much praise and appreciation from its fans, the success was marred by the recent events where Moses Ingram faced racial abuse on her debut.
