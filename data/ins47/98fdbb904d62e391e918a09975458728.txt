By Reuters Published: 22:54 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 22:54 EDT, 19 October 2022 BENGALURU, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Indian shares may open lower on Thursday, after four straight sessions of gains, following broader dour sentiment for risky assets as U.S. Treasury yields surged on fears that the Federal Reserve will remain on its aggressive rate-hike path. India's NSE stock futures listed on the Singapore exchange were down 0.81%, as of 0246 GMT. The MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan was down 1.
