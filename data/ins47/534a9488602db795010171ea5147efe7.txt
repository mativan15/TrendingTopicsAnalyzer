Smiths News Secures Contract Renewal With Associated Newspapers
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 06:28:00

By Joe Hoppe Smiths News PLC said Wednesday that it has secured a long-term contract with Associated Newspapers Ltd. for all of Smiths's current U.K. distribution territories through 2029, representing first of the major newspaper publisher contract renewals. The newspaper and magazine wholesaler company said the contract represents revenue of 155 million pounds ($175.5 million) a year. The agreement with the publisher of the "Daily Mail," "The Mail on Sunday" and the "i" newspapers represents the first of the major newspaper publisher contract renewals.
