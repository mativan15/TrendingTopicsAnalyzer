 Waterloo Regional Police say a person was threatened by a stranger with a knife in Wilmot on Sunday night.
According to police, the victim was sitting in their car when the stranger came at them holding a knife at around 7:30 p.m. near Notre Dame Drive and Deerfield Avenue in Petersburg.Officers arrived at the scene a short time later and a man was taken into custody.
