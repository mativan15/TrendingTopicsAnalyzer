Kolkata: ED arrests Rumen Agarwal in gaming app fraud case
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 15:45:00

KOLKATA: Rumen Agarwal, close aide of Amir Khan -- prime accused in the gaming app fraud case -- was sent to Enforcement Directorate (ED) custody till October 28.Agarwal was arrested by the central investigating agency on Thursday night after being questioned for hours.The central investigating agency alleged that he was involved in siphoning off the ill gotten money and involved in routing the amount. He was arrested under Section 3 of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002.On Thursday, the agency carried out raids at the residence of Agarwal.
