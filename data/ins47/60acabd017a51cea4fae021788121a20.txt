The idea behind BU's tech fest was to strengthen the student tech community, say students

Zenevia, the tech fest of Bennett University , was held from 17-19 October, along with Uphoria , the cultural fest. The idea behind the fest was to strengthen the student tech community. It was a mix of fun and holistic learning for the students to showcase their ideas and explore the tech domain. The students also got to interact with industry experts and compete against each other in fun tech-based contests.
