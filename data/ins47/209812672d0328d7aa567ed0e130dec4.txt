India records marginal dip in daily Covid tally with 3,962 new cases
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 05:15:00

ReutersThe daily positivity rate was recorded at 0.89 per cent.India logged a single-day rise of 3,962 coronavirus infections and 26 fatalities, pushing the overall tally to 4,31,72,547 cases and 5,24,677 deaths, according to Union Health Ministry data updated on Saturday. The data updated at 8 am also stated that the number of active cases in the country increased by 1,239 in 24 hours to stand at 22,416. Of the 26 fresh fatalities, six were recorded in the last 24 hours and 20 reconciled by Kerala.
