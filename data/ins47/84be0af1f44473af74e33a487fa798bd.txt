NEW BRAUNFELS, Texas, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TaskUs, Inc. (NASDAQ: TASK), a global provider of outsourced digital services and next-generation customer experience to fast growing technology companies, was recognized as a Major Contender on Everest Group's PEAK Matrix® for Trust and Safety – Content Moderation Provider 2022.According to Everest Group, TaskUs stands out through its well-being management offerings, investment in research, and recent development of TaskVerse, a centralized platform for the gig workforce to complete tasks from anywhere in the world.
