These 20 cities will pay you to move there. Here's how it's working.
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 16:40:00

Cities are increasingly luring remote workers with cash, down-payment assistance and free coworking spots as part of a new wave of economic development efforts.

But they are also getting creative. Topeka, Kansas is offering $1,000 in free Jimmy John's sandwiches. Noblesville, Indiana, showcases unlimited golf memberships and coffee with the mayor. Stillwater, Oklahoma, is giving workers their caffeine fix with free coffee for a year. Greensburg, Indiana, is throwing in 'grandparents on demand' who can help babysit your kids.
