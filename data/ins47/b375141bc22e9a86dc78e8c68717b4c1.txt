Ramanatha Brahmanapalli, a software engineer working in the US, spent all his earnings to make a Kannada movie. Ramanatha, who hails from Brahmanapalli in Ballary, was working in the US till 2018. Then he returned to his hometown to make the film Nahi Jnanena Sadrusham. The movie's tag is -- Education with entertainment. 'It is my passion towards filmmaking that made me come back to Karnataka. My objective is to convey a message on the importance of education and the need for innovative methods to teach students in classrooms,'' said Ramanatha.
