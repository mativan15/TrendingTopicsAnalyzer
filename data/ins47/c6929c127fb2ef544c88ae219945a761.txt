By Reuters Published: 08:39 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 08:39 EDT, 18 October 2022 Oct 18 (Reuters) - Sri Lankan shares closed lower for the third straight session on Tuesday, hit by losses in industrial and communication service stocks. * The CSE All-Share index settled down 1.31% at 8,866.05. * Sri Lanka approved legislation on Tuesday to let companies from oil-producing nations import and sell fuel as it attempts to cope with a steep oil bill that deepened its worst financial crisis in decades.
