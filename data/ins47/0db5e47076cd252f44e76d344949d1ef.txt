October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Ouster, Inc. (NYSE: OUST), a leading provider of high-resolution digital lidar sensors for the automotive, industrial, robotics, and smart infrastructure industries, announced that it will report its financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2022 after the market closes on Monday, November 7, 2022 and host a conference call that day at 5:00 p.m. ET to discuss its results.

Registration for the conference call can be completed by visiting the following website prior to, or on the day of, the conference call: https://conferencingportals.
