Published: 16:36 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 16:36 EDT, 2 June 2022 Dame Deborah James has revealed that her collaborations over the past few weeks will raise over £1million for her BowelBabe Fund. The 40-year-old, who is receiving end-of-life care at her parents home in Woking after living with stage four bowel cancer since she was diagnosed in December 2016, announced on Instagram that the extra money will provide her fund with another year of funding.She said: 'I'm so chuffed that over the weeks (albeit whilst I've been navigating this end of life care thing!), in order to up funds into @bowelbabefun the collaborations (T-shirt, Book, Rose, Bag) will end up bringing in over £1million extra for the fund which will give us a another year of funding.
