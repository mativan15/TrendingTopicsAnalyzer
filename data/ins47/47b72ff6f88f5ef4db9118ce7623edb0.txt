MPs who want Boris Johnson back should be careful what they wish for
independent.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 10:57:07

'I never dreamed she [Liz Truss] would be so bad at it,' one cabinet minister who joined the Gadarene rush to support Truss for the Tory leadership told me ruefully.Nor did I. I thought Truss, the rebel never without a cause, would temper her revolutionary zeal when she got to Downing Street. Instead, she opted for all or nothing, and quickly ended up with nothing. She launched an experiment on the British people with the theories her favoured right-wing libertarian think tanks had peddled for years, with little expectation they would ever be introduced in one go.
