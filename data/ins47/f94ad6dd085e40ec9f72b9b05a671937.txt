For the third year in a row, Mambu is recognized for its SaaS cloud banking platform

MIAMI, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CB Insights named Mambu, a leading SaaS cloud banking platform, to its fifth-annual Fintech 250 list for the third year in a row. The list recognizes the 250 most promising private fintech companies in the world. Mambu was recognized in the core banking and infrastructure category.
