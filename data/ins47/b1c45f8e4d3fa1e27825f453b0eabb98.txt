British Prime Minister Liz Truss Resigns After Only 44 Days

news
2022-10-20 17:23:04

Truss came to office amid extremely difficult circumstances. Queen Elizabeth II died within a few days of her taking over from Johnson. That removed the promise of any new leadership "bounce," as the nation was plunged into an official period of mourning. Overseeing the transition to a new monarch only added to the plethora of thorny challenges affecting the government, including the war in Ukraine and the threat of Scottish secession, as well as the severe energy and inflation crises. But if any observers expected caution from Truss, they were rapidly corrected.
