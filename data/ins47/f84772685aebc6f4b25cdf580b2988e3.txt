Published: 22:13 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 22:13 EDT, 30 May 2022 By Milounee Purohit and Vivek Mishra BENGALURU, May 31 (Reuters) - Indian house prices are set to accelerate this year to a pace not seen in half a decade, according to a Reuters poll of property analysts who also warned that higher interest rates will crimp affordability, especially for first-time buyers. A lack of demand has kept India's property market relatively quiet during the pandemic compared with other world markets that rocketed higher as households scrambled to buy more living space.
