Editor's Note: This is one of the 11 winners of this year's Deals of the Year Awards, honoring the most influential mergers and acquisitions for the region. We're rolling out one deal a day until the Oct. 26 event celebrating the deals. For details on the other winners, click here.

The deal: Alora Pharmaceuticals LLC, parent company of six specialty pharmaceutical and pharmaceutical manufacturing companies, bought life science company Osmotica Pharmaceuticals plc. in August 2021.

Purchase price: $111 million

How we got here: Alpharetta-based Alora bought Osmotica's legacy business, which includes subsidiaries Osmotica Pharmaceutical US LLC, Vertical Pharmaceuticals, LLC, Trigen Laboratories, LLC and its Marietta manufacturing facility.
