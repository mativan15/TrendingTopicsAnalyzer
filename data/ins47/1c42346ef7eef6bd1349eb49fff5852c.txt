IRVINE, Calif. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Umberg/Zipser LLP, a premiere business litigation and intellectual property law firm located in Irvine, CA, has again been named an honoree of The Civic 50 OC by OneOC and Points of Light, the world's largest organization dedicated to volunteer service. The award recognizes Umberg/Zipser as one of the 50 most community-minded companies in Orange County, as determined by an independently administered and scored survey. The Civic 50 OC initiative, modeled after Points of Light's national program, provides a standard for superior corporate citizenship, and highlights how companies can use their time, skills, and resources to drive social impact in their company and communities.
