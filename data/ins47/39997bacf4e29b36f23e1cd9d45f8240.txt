Image caption, Chips over their shoulders - visitors gathered on MondayOne minute, it was business as usual for a West Midlands chip shop.The next, TikTok had normality all wrapped up; battering the everyday into submission, and - oh my cod - turning Binley Mega Chippy into an online star.Now devotees and converts from across the UK are visiting the Coventry takeaway in a pickled egg pilgrimage and saveloy sojourn.They are among the hundreds of thousands to have watched TikTok videos praising the premises, with a song about it also ensuring the business is no small fry.
