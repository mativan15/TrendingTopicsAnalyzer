S&P 500 Futures Rise Following Last Week's Bounce
nbcnewyork.com
business
2022-05-30 23:15:00

S&P 500 futures rose on Monday night, as Wall Street tried to build on last week's momentum. Dow Jones Industrial Average futures gained 21 points, or 0.1%. S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 futures climbed 0.3% and 0.7%, respectively. The U.S. stock market didn't open Monday due to the Memorial Day holiday. Those moves follow the best weekly gains for the Dow and S&P 500 since November 2020. The Dow closed up 6.2% for the week, ending an eight-week losing streak. The S&P 500 gained 6.5%, and the Nasdaq gained 6.
