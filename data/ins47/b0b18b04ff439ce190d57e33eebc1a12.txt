RIL Q2 net remains flat at ₹15,512 crore on volatility in oil-to-chemicals business
.
news
2022-10-21 15:04:36

Reliance Industries Ltd. (RIL) reported flat consolidated net profit at ₹15,512 crore for the second quarter on account of volatility in the Oil to Chemicals (O2C) business. Net profit came in at ₹15,479 crore a year earlier.Revenue grew 32.4% to ₹253,497 crore with ‘strong traction' in the consumer businesses and on higher oil prices.The company said the earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) in the O2C segment was impacted by weak downstream chemical margins and introduction of special additional excise duty (SAED), partially offset by firm transportation fuel margins.
