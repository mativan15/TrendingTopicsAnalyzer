Ontario hospitals continue to deal with significant staffing shortages
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-21 19:42:19

Increased wait times, temporary emergency room closures and staffing shortages continues to be a major issue at hospitals across Ontario.
Many hospitals are operating at critical staffing levels while dealing with an increase in COVID-19, influenza and respiratory virus cases this fall.Peterborough Regional Health Centre (PRHC) hasn't had to close its emergency department, but officials tell Global News that staffing levels are often low while some members are off sick and approximately 140 full-time positions hospital-wide remain unfilled.
