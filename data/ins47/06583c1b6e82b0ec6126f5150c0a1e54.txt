There's certainly no shortage of choices for businesses and consumers searching for a new bank or credit card. Fortunately, you don't have to settle for a 'one size fits all' solution—there are distinct differences in the products and services offered by the wide variety of banks and credit cards in today's marketplace. Many customers may simply choose the first bank or credit card that stands out to them, but there are a lot of factors to consider, from services to fees to accessibility—and more.
