October 17, 2022 08:39 AM Eastern Daylight Time ROCKWALL, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Following the acquisition of GCP Applied Technologies by Saint-Gobain, GCP's Specialty Construction Chemicals (SCC) segment will integrate with CHRYSO within the new Construction Chemicals Business Unit, part of the High-Performance Solutions division of Saint-Gobain.

Steve Williams, formerly President of Chryso North America, has been appointed President of the Construction Chemicals Business Unit for North America and will run the combined North American business.
