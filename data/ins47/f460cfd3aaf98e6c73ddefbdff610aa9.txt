June 02, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--EngageSmart, Inc. (NYSE: ESMT), a leading provider of vertically tailored customer engagement software and integrated payments solutions, today announced that company executives will participate in the William Blair 42nd Annual Growth Stock Conference, taking place in Chicago, Illinois. Chief Executive Officer Bob Bennett will be presenting on Thursday, June 9, 2022, at 10:40 am CT / 11:40 am ET. Management will conduct meetings with investors at the conference on June 9, 2022.
