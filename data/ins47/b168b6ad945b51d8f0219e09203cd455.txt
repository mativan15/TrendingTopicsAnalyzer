Israeli Defence Minister Gantz meets India's top leadership; seeks cooperation in defence and other fields
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 18:00:00

Israeli Defence Minister Benny Gantz on Thursday called on Prime Minister Narendra Modi and sought cooperation between the two countries in defence and other fields to expand their capabilities to "contend with developing challenges", the minister's office said here. "We have a great opportunity to deepen defence cooperation between our countries and to build on our shared values in order to contribute to global stability," Gantz said during his meeting with the prime minister in New Delhi. During the meeting, Gantz emphasised the opportunity to invest further in defence cooperation between the two countries.
