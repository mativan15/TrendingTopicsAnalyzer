Product Management is the practice of strategically guiding the development, improvement, and support of a product through its lifecycle. A thorough understanding and application of product management principles, strategies, frameworks, tools, and techniques; helps deliver the perfect product experience at every stage of the user journey in alignment with strategic business goals. A product manager is pivotal to the success of an organisation and is among the most sought after globally. According to TeamLease Digital, besides the niche tech industry, there is a significant demand for product managers across BFSI, automobile, retail, pharmaceutical, entertainment, FMCG, logistics, and supply chain industries.
