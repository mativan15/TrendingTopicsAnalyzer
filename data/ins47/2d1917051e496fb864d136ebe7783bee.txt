Watch: Tamil Nadu CM MK Stalin stops convoy to help road accident victim

CHENNAI: Tamil Nadu chief minister M K Stalin on Friday came to the rescue of an accident victim who was lying on the Anna Salai in Chennai after falling off a two-wheeler.Arulraj of Chrompet was riding on his two-wheeler near DMS when he lost control and fell down. He sustained injuries on his head.The chief minister, who was on his way to the secretariat, stopped his convoy, got down from his vehicle, got the accident victim placed in an autorickshaw and sent him to Royapettah Government Hospital.
