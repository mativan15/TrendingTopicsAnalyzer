As soon as an actress puts on a few extra kilos, faceless trolls start attacking her. Time and again, many actresses have opened up about getting fat-shamed and have shared how they were affected by it. From Nimrat Kaur and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan to Ileana D'Cruz and Sonakshi Sinha, we've seen actresses giving it back to trolls with befitting replies from time to time.And now, actress Kajol is being trolled for gaining some weight and netizens have lots to say. A video of the actress was shared by photographer Viral Bhayani on Instagram in which Kajol could be seen walking out from her Pilates class.
