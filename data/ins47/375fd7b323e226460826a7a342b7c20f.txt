The new owners are in place after a 13-week saga in which the government sanctioned Roman Abramovich and a special operating licence placed stringent conditions on the club After 13 weeks of twists and turns, Chelsea Football Club is under new ownership. A consortium led by Todd Boehly has taken over from sanctioned Russian billionaire Roman Abramovich and the government restrictions that threatened to derail their season have been lifted. Boehly comes with a solid track record in sports ownership having already revived the fortunes of the LA Dodgers baseball team.
