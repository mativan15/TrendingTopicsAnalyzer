Published: 13:54 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 13:54 EDT, 19 October 2022 By Svea Herbst-Bayliss Oct 19 (Reuters) - Irenic Capital Management, a hedge fund that owns a 1.5% stake in Capricorn Energy Plc, asked the natural gas producer on Wednesday to abandon its sale to Israel's NewMed Energy LP in favor of selling its assets piecemeal. Irenic's intervention challenges a deal that would create a London and Tel Aviv-listed gas giant, at a time when European countries are looking for such companies to help with a supply crunch caused by Russia's invasion of Ukraine.
