Borregaard ASA: EBITDA1 of NOK 434 million in the 3rd quarter
globenewswire.com
news
2022-10-21 00:00:00

Borregaard's operating revenues reached NOK 1,752 million (NOK 1,429 million)2 in the 3rd quarter of 2022. EBITDA increased to NOK 434 million (NOK 391 million). The result improved in all business areas. The net currency impact on EBITDA was positive compared with the 3rd quarter of 2021. Increased sales prices and a strong product mix were the main reasons for the EBITDA improvement in BioSolutions and BioMaterials. For Fine Chemicals, the result improvement was due to increased sales prices and higher volume.
