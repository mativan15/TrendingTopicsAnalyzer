Warning to Aldi shoppers as halloween Specialbuys pulled from sale
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 14:30:00

Aldi is a popular budget supermarket chain in the UK (Image: Getty)Aldi has recalled two Specialbuy products and warned shoppers not to use them.The budget supermarket urged customers to return the make of ceramic wax burners and tealight houses, including special halloween-themed versions.They were availble to purchase online from Sunday September 25, and in store on Thursday September 29.But now Aldi, which has more than 900 supermarkets in the UK, is recalling the item "as a precautionary measure".
