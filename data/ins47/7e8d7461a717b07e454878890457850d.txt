Grant Shapps set to replace Suella Braverman as home secretary
sky.com
news
2022-10-19 16:22:00

Grant Shapps is set to replace Suella Braverman as home secretary after she said she resigned from her role.The former transport secretary, who backed Rishi Sunak in the leadership race and has been critical of some of Ms Truss' policies, is expected to be confirmed as her replacement.
Ms Braverman was only appointed to the role 43 days ago by new prime minister Liz Truss.Sky News understands from a government source that Ms Braverman's departure is "not over a policy disagreement" but an "honest mistake".
