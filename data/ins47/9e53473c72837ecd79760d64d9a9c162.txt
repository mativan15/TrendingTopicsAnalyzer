If US Ends Public Health Emergency COVID Costs to Go Up for Many
medscape.com
news
2022-10-20 23:45:00

Editor's note: Find the latest COVID-19 news and guidance in Medscape's Coronavirus Resource Center. With the end of the federal government's public health emergency (PHE) looming in 2023, many Americans will soon have to pay some or all of the cost of vaccines, treatments, and tests for COVID-19, according to a new report from the Kaiser Family Foundation (KFF). Moreover, if Congress doesn't pass a supplemental emergency funding request from the Biden administration, the federal government will soon deplete its supplies of COVID-19 vaccines, treatments, and tests, according to the report.
