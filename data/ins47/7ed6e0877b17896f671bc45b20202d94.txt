$8 million in additional funding from Breakthrough Prize Foundation will support Flexport.org's work bringing food and supplies to refugees in Ukraine, Pakistan and Horn of Africa
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Tech for Refugees Initiative and Flexport.org today announced an expansion of their humanitarian efforts to help refugees, with an increase in current efforts to support refugees from the war in Ukraine, in addition to new funding for emergencies in Pakistan and the Horn of Africa.
