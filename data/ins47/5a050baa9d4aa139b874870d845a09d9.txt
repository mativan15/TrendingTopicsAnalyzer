Cyber Consultants is a Business Reporter clientBusiness Reporter: What is a cyber security strategy and why is it important?A cyber security strategy that is clearly aligned to a business not only helps protect against threats such as ransomware and data breaches but is also paramount to a business being prepared should such an event take place. This preparedness alone can significantly reduce the cost implications to a business but also reduce the reputational damage it might incur. Research has shown that a business that understands its risks and is well prepared not only responds quicker and more effectively to a breach, but it can also reduce the costs by 34 percent.
