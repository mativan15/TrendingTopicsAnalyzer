June 03, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO & FORT WORTH, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--TPG Inc. (NASDAQ: TPG), a leading global alternative asset management firm, announced today that Jon Winkelried, Chief Executive Officer, is scheduled to present at the Morgan Stanley U.S. Financials, Payments & CRE Conference on Wednesday, June 15, 2022 at 8:45 a.m. ET.

A live audio webcast of the discussion will be available on the Investor Relations section of TPG's website at shareholders.tpg.com/events-presentations/events.
