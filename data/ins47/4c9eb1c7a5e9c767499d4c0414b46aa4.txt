Air NZ $1.2b rights offer was a test of how Sharesies serves its members
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-29 17:00:00

There they were, tens of thousands of Sharesies investors holding shares in Air New Zealand, when a simple stock suddenly became part of something so complicated it tripped up even stock exchange operator NZX. How well-equipped were they to navigate the $1.2 billion capital raising, which had been on the cards for more than a year but which materialised in an unexpected form? The two-for-one rights issue has been criticised for being overly complex given the high number of retail investors in the airline, that is, investors who pony up with their own money in contrast to institutions, which invest other people's money.
