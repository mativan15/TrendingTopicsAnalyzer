Complete Coverage of UEFA Nations League™ Will Stream on FuboTV Via Fubo Sports Network and FOX Sports

NEW YORK, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--FuboTV Inc. (NYSE: FUBO), the leading sports-first live TV streaming platform, announced today it is expanding its distribution of four Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) Nations League™ matches in June to Pay-Per-View (PPV). The matches will continue to stream live on FuboTV as previously announced.

FuboTV's exclusive English language streams of four UEFA Nations League™ matches - all featuring England - will be available to customers of DIRECTV, DISH and all major cable companies via iN Demand.
