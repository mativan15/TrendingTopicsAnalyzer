CDNA INVESTOR DEADLINE: Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd Announces that CareDx, Inc. Investors with Substantial Losses Have Opportunity to Lead Class Action Lawsuit

SAN DIEGO, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The law firm of Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP announces that purchasers of CareDx, Inc. (NASDAQ: CDNA) common stock between February 24, 2021 and May 5, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period') have until July 22, 2022 to seek appointment as lead plaintiff in Plumbers & Pipefitters Local Union #295 Pension Fund v. CareDx, Inc., No. 22-cv-03023. Pending in the Northern District of California, the CareDx class action lawsuit charges CareDx and certain of its top executive officers with violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
