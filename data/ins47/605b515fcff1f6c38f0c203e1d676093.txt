Hitachi Energy India Results: Profit up 31% to Rs 52 cr in March quarter

AgenciesThe company follows the January to December fiscal year.New Delhi: Hitachi Energy India on Friday posted an over 31 per cent rise in net profit to Rs 51.68 crore for the March quarter, mainly on the back of higher revenues. The company had posted a net profit of Rs 39.44 crore in the quarter ended March 31, 2021, a BSE filing showed. Total income in the quarter under review rose to Rs 1,142.13 crore from Rs 1,023.77 crore in the year-ago period. The company follows the January to December fiscal year.
