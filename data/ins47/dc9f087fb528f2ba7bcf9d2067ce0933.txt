Washington, D.C. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 The National Association of Mortgage Brokers (NAMB), the voice of the mortgage industry representing the interests of mortgage professionals and home buyers since 1973 was pleased to welcome over 2,100 industry professionals who enjoyed over 115 exhibitors and sponsors within its sold-out exhibit hall, several noted keynote speakers, educational opportunities and exciting award functions during NAMB National 2022. 'NAMB National was a tremendous success,' said Ernest Jones Jr.
