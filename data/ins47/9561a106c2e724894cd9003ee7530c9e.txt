Preppy Girl Hair Is Having a Moment
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-18 17:15:30

Trust TikTok to coin a new hair trend. Heading into autumn, the #preppyaesthetic is trending with crisp-collared button downs, blazers and loafers. And, Preppy Girl Hair is the perfect companion.

Prepare to start seeing more polished ponytails, neat ballerina buns and a whole ton of accessories to maximize on the trend when it comes to beauty choices, too. Because where would a preppy girl be without hair bows, headbands, barrettes, clips and slides?

As for inspo, Gossip Girl has generated over 7 billion views on the app thanks to its throwback Y2K nostalgia offering up a whole mine of lewks to revisit with a 2022 spin.
