AmerisourceBergen sets $1 billion stock buyback program
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 10:39:08

AmerisourceBergen Corp. ABC, -1.32% said Wednesday that it set a new $1 billion stock repurchase program. That represents 3.1% of the pharmaceutical and health care products company's market capitalization of $32.42 billion at Tuesday's stock closing price. As of May 31, the company said it had $321 million remaining from the buyback program it set in May 2020. The company also reiterated its fiscal 2022 guidance for earnings per share of $10.80 to $11.05, which compares with the current FactSet consensus of $10.
