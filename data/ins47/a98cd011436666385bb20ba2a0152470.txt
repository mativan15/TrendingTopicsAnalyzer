By Patrick Damp October 16, 2022 / 11:19 AM / CBS Pittsburgh Night market returns with autumn theme Night market returns with autumn theme 00:41 PITTSBURGH (KDKA) - The Downtown Pittsburgh Night Market has finally made its fall transition. Autumn-themed decorations and products were just some of the items sold by local vendors in the pop-up shop in Market Square. Some business owners said the community's support is greatly appreciated, especially when they're working hard to grow or expand their businesses.
