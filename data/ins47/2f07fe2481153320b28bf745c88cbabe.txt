AgenciesThe results of UKPSC PCS Prelims have been declared today by the Uttarakhand Public Service Commission. Candidates can check it on the official website of the commission ukpsc.gov.in. People who have given the test for Upper Subordinate Service or State Civil Service can check the result on the given site. The prelims exam was held on 3rd April 2022. The people who will qualify for the preliminary exam can further become eligible to give the Mains. The tentative date of the main test is from August 20,2022 to August 24.
