My entry into writing in tech started during my junior year of college. I just switched from majoring in biochemistry and public health to studying human-centered design and engineering and history at the University of Washington.I had considered pursuing tech roles after college but knew nothing about what was available outside software engineering and program management.I loved writing and was an avid college journalist. Somewhere between early-morning interviews and late-night transcription sessions, I began to see myself as a writer who wanted to empower underrepresented communities in media.
