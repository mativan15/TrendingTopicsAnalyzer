RIYADH, Saudi Arabia , Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The second edition of NEOM AI Challenge, launched by the Saudi Data & AI Authority (SDAIA) in partnership with NEOM, continues to welcome those wishing to compete in the Challenge until the 10th of November. NEOM Challenge aims to support innovators and entrepreneurs and build local and international capacities in small and medium enterprises worldwide to push them to new heights in innovation.
This Challenge comes within the framework of SDAIA's efforts to unlock the value of data as a national asset, as well as to contribute to realizing Vision 2030's objectives.
