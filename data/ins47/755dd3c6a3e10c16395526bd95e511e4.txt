Assam Direct Recruitment 2022: Results for Grade 4 to be released soon
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 06:00:00

While the Assam Board of Secondary Education was expected to declare the Direct Recruitment Result 2022 for Grade 4 by today, it has yet to be released as the official website has crashed. According to media reports, the result was going to be released on 19th October, but the board has not released any information about the release date and time of the result. Once the results are released, candidates will be able to check their result on the official website, sebaonline.org.

Assam's Chief Minister, Himanta Biswa Sarma took to Twitter to share the announcement of the Assam SLRC Result today.
