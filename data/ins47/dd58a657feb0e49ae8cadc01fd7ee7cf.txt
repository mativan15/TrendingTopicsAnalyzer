Korea's stock market opens low but closes high Monday
naver.com
news
2022-10-17 17:20:00

A screen in Hana Bank's trading room in central Seoul shows the Kospi closing at 2,219.71 points on Monday, up 7.16 points, or 0.32 percent, from the previous trading day. [NEWS1] Korea's stock market advanced for a second consecutive session on Monday as investors went on the hunt for undervalued stocks. The won fell against the dollar. After choppy trading, the benchmark Kospi added 7.16 points, or 0.32 percent, to close at 2,219.71 points. Trading volume was moderate at about 487 million shares worth some 6.
