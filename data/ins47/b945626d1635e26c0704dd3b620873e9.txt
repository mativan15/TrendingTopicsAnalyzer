Steven Gerrard's sacking leaves him further from Liverpool destiny than ever
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-21 16:30:44

The 2018 documentary charting Steven Gerrard's routinely astonishing life and times had sadness as a backdrop. Gerrard was – and presumably still is – scarred by events of April 2014, when that slip against Chelsea turned the Premier League title tide against his beloved Liverpool. 'There's not a day that doesn't go by that I don't think about what if that didn't happen,' Gerrard said. One got the impression lows lingered for far longer than epic highs.We were entitled to believe there would be redemption.
