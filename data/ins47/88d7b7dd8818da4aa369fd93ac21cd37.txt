Pak court grants pre-arrest bail to Imran Khan till June 25 in 'Azadi March' cases
indiatimes.com

2022-06-02 12:00:00

PTIOusted Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan gives the victory sign to supporters as he leads a march.A Pakistani court on Thursday granted a three-week pre-arrest bail to ousted prime minister Imran Khan in 14 cases registered against him in connection with the arson and vandalism by his supporters during the recent Azadi march organised by his Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party, according to media reports. The Peshawar High Court (PHC) granted Khan the bail to the 69-year-old former prime minister against a surety of Rs 50,000, the Geo News reported.
