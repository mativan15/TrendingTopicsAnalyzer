Kangana Ranaut to play theatre superstar Noti Binodini in Pradeep Sarkar directorial
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-19 05:49:46

As Kangana Ranaut readies up for the release of Indira Gandhi biopic, she has announced her next big outing. On Wednesday, the actor announced that she has signed up to play Noti Bondini, one of the most iconic theatre superstars from Bengal.
The mega-budget film will be helmed by Parineeti fame Pradeep Sarkar. It's written by Prakash Kapadia, who has films like Tanhaji, Padmavat, Devdas and Black to his credit. There was a buzz in early 2020 that Aishwarya Rai will play the role in the biopic.
Expressing her excitement over the new project, Kangana shared in a statement, 'I am a very big fan of Pradeep Sarkaar ji and very happy for this opportunity.
