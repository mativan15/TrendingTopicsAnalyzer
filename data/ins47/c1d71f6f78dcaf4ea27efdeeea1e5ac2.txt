Published: 09:28 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 09:58 EDT, 16 October 2022 A rude resident who was parking nine cars on their street had to pay a hefty price for their misdeeds after a petty neighbor sought revenge.  The poster, believed to be from the US, revealed on Reddit that they had grown increasingly disgruntled with their neighbors, who had nine cars and parked none on their driveway, preferring to line them up along the street and taking space from other residents.Eager to punish the offender, they got their revenge by tipping off the local authority about illegal building on their property after noticing that the neighbor had built a second unit on their plot in record time.
