Former Congress president Rahul Gandhi has termed the three regional parties in Andhra Pradesh — the YSRCP, the TDP, and the JSP — as corporate companies, as 'there is no internal democracy in them and are largely under dictatorship.'When asked whether the Congress would enter into an alliance with the TDP either in Telangana or in Andhra Pradesh as it did in the previous elections, Mr. Rahul Gandhi told the media here on Wednesday that 'the State units of the party will decide on the alliances when the time comes.
