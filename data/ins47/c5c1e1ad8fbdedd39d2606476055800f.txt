REUTERS/Arnd WiegmannLONDON, Oct 21 (Reuters) - JPMorgan has hired one of Deutsche Bank's (DBKGn.DE) most senior dealmakers, Richard Sheppard, to strengthen its UK investment banking business and help British companies navigate the challenges caused by market turmoil and rising energy costs.Sheppard will join the Wall Street bank in November as co-head of its UK investment banking business sharing responsibilities with Charlie Jacobs, according to a memo seen by Reuters.
