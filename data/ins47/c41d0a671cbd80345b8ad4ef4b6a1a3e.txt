October 18, 2022 08:07 AM Eastern Daylight Time ORLANDO, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Designed and manufactured by Cirrus Aircraft, the G2+ Vision Jet® is the ultimate business asset as an efficient Part 135 charter aircraft. The award-winning Vision Jet features an extensive portfolio of business use cases for missions between 200 to 1,000 nautical miles at the lowest operating cost for a business jet.



The Vision Jet is single-pilot certified or can be flown with two pilots and five passengers, depending on mission requirements.
