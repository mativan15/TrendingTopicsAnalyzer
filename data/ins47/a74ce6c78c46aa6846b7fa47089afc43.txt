Josiah Myers was depressed. After taking a job in the corporate world, he found himself a walking contradiction. The clothes he was wearing, having to shave every morning, going through the sort of daily grind that didn't fit his personality or long-term goals.

Then he heard about a fairly new concept at the time called Airbnb and decided to use the service for one of his Short North rental properties. He refurbished the space and started offering it on the short-term rental platform.

'The first month, I ended up tripling my income,' Myers said.
