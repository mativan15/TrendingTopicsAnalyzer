NEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- EXL [NASDAQ: EXLS], a leading data analytics and digital operations and solutions company, today announced that it will be holding an Investor and Analyst Day on Wednesday, November 16, 2022.This event will be hosted by Rohit Kapoor, Vice Chairman and CEO, along with members of the EXL executive leadership team. The focus of the event will be on the evolution of EXL's data-led enterprise strategy, including detailed examples of how the company is harnessing structured and unstructured data to help its clients create a sustainable business advantage.
