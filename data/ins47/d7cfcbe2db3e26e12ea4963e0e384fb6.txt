AgenciesImage used for representative purpose onlyThe firefighters of Leeds city in England, have managed to put out a huge fire that broke out at a multi-storey building in the city centre. Many buildings, which are located in the city centre, are also evacuated. The city centre building caught fire on Saturday evening, at approx. 45 minutes past 7 pm. The fire started on the top three floors of the building. The building was reportedly worn out. 
The emergency services came to the spot as quickly as possible and still tackled the fire hours later.
