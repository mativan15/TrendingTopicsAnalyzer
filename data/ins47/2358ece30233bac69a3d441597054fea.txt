Tesla owners love their cars. Elon Musk? Not as much.
expressnews.com
news
2022-06-04 12:31:11

Earl and Lindsey Banning love, love, love their Tesla, a common sentiment among the Teslarati. Owners don't tend to idle in neutral.But when it comes to Tesla CEO, Space X commander, Twitter behemoth and wannabe overlord Elon Musk, the couple in Dayton, Ohio, diverges like a fork in the interstate. "I find him deeply problematic," says Lindsey, 46, a clinical psychologist. "He is an attention-seeking person." Musk's tweets, which seem to have revved into overdrive since he announced a $44 billion hostile takeover bid of Twitter in April, make her "uncomfortable and anxious.
