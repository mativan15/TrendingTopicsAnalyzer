June 03, 2022 05:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Glancy Prongay & Murray LLP ('GPM'), a leading national shareholder rights law firm, announces that a class action lawsuit has been filed on behalf of investors who purchased or otherwise acquired Dentsply Sirona Inc. ('Dentsply' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: XRAY) common stock between June 9, 2021 and May 9, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'). Dentsply investors have until August 1, 2022 to file a lead plaintiff motion.
