Amazon CEO of Worldwide Consumer Dave Clark to resign July 1
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-06-03 17:49:08

Amazon's CEO of Worldwide Consumer Dave Clark will resign on July 1 after 23 years at the e-commerce behemoth. "As much as I have loved the ride, it is time for me to say goodbye to start a new journey," Clark said in an email to staff shared on Twitter Friday. "For some time, I have discussed my intent to transition out of Amazon with my family and others close to me, but I wanted to ensure that teams were set up for success. I feel confident that time is now."Clark has served as CEO of Worldwide Consumer since January 2021, overseeing the company's massive retail business.
