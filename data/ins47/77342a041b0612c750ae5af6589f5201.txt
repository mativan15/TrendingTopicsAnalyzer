While watching Tuesday night's Illinois gubernatorial debate, I noticed one glaring difference between Gov. J.B. Pritzker and Republican challenger Darren Bailey. While all politicians and future politicians are taught the 'spin technique' — not answering a question asked of you but spinning your answer to criticize the record of your opponent — I immediately questioned Bailey's ability to break down and answer an inquiry.

When you are the challenger trying to unseat an incumbent, your job in a debate is to clearly articulate your platform, not hurl criticisms back at your opponent.
