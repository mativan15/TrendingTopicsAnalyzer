To receive Google's Diwali surprise, open the search engine's browser.

Type 'Diwali' in the search box and hit enter

You can also search with another keyword, 'Diwali 2022'

The results page will open with Diwali along with a diya appearing as the top search

Now click on diya

Several lamps will now light-up your screen

Your cursor will also appear as a diya

The festival of lights, Diwali, is just around the corner.
