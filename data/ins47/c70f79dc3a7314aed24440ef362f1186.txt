How much of England is protected for nature? UK makes little progress on 30x30 target

2022-10-19 14:27:18

The Government said it aims to protect at least 30 per cent of land and sea in England by 2030, as part of the 30x30 pledge.However, a new study from the Wildlife and Countryside Link (WCL) has revealed that the Government has made 'little progress' in achieving that aim.The new report claims that the protection of land for nature in England has barely increased since 2021.WCL chief executive Richard Benwell said: 'Unfortunately, our figures show that, in the race to halt nature's decline by 2030, the Government is limping backwards.
