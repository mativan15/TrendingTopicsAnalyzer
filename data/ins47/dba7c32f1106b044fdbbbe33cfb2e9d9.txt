Two D-Street veterans scrap this underperformer after 50% fall in a year!
indiatimes.com

2022-10-19 07:15:00

New Delhi: Amid the portfolio rejig during the September 2022 quarter, seasoned investors have exited Paras Defence and Space Technologies, despite the overall sector being the season's flavour for the ongoing calendar year. The stock made a stellar debut at the bourses and delivered multibagger returns. However, ace investors including Sunil Singhania and Mukul Agrawal scrapped the counter from their portfolios.

According to the latest shareholding data, filed by the company as of September 30, 2022, names of both the investors or their funds were not on the shareholders' lists.
