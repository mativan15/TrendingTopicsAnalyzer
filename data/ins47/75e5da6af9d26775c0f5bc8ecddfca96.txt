US, Canada send armored vehicles to Haiti to fight gangs
dw.com
news
2022-10-16 03:00:00

The US and Canada sent armored vehicles and other supplies to Haiti to help police fight a powerful gang. Lack of government control in much of the country is increasing the risks from a cholera outbreak.

A police officer prevents protesters from entering a hotel that was looted in Port-au-Prince
The US and Canada on Saturday delivered security equipment to Haiti, including armored vehicles, to help the impoverished country deal with the spreading security and health crises.
US and Canadian air force planes landed in Port-au-Prince carrying "vital Haitian government-purchased security equipment, including tactical and armored vehicles," a joint statement from the two governments said.
