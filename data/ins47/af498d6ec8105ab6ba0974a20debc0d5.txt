Albany Tech will host 'test' night court session
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-15 21:16:00

Oct. 15—ALBANY — The city of Albany's Municipal Court and Community Court partner, Albany Technical College, will pilot a night court session at ATC on Thursday at 6 p.m.

ATC Dean of Academic Affairs Kenneth Singleton said he will be pleased to open the doors of the college for the night court test.

"Being physically located in a section of our community that would benefit from the court's Albany Works! program, we were delighted to offer the use of our Moot Court classroom for the planned night court session," Singleton said in a news release.
