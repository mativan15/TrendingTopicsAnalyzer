Prime Minister Justin Trudeau speaks with reporters before attending caucus on Parliament Hill in Ottawa on Oct. 19.Adrian Wyld/The Canadian PressPeople who receive the GST rebate can expect to see an extra cheque this year that will double the amount of the benefit for the next six months, after the Liberal government's Bill C-30 became law on Tuesday.The Senate passed the bill on Tuesday less than a month after the legislation targeting the cost-of-living crisis was introduced in the House of Commons.
