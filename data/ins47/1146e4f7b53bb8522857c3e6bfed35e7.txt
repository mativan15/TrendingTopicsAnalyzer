WASHINGTON, October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Reps. Carol Miller (R-W.Va.) and Alex Mooney (R-W.Va.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.

CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Reps. Miller and Mooney are both a lifetime "Taxpayer Hero" with lifetime ratings of 96 percent. They both earned the title of "Taxpayer Super Hero" in 2021 with a perfect 100 percent rating.
