Provident Financial Sees 2022 in Line With Market Expectations Despite Backdrop
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 06:34:00

By Michael Susin Provident Financial PLC said Wednesday that its performance for the full year is in line with market views, reflecting its shift to lower-risk costumers and despite the current market backdrop. The U.K. consumer-finance company said its performance throughout the third quarter of 2022 and into October were in line with the board's views. The company said it continues to see attractive opportunities for growth, but that it will continue to have a prudent approach to credit risk management and to focus on cost control.
