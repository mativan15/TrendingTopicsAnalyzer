Purpose-Driven Companies Evolve Faster Than Others
forbes.com
news
2022-10-21 20:00:00

Does your company have a purpose beyond profit? It may seem an irrelevant question, but the answer can vary depending on who you ask. 79% of business leaders surveyed by PwC believe that an organization's purpose is central to business success, yet 68% shared that purpose is not used as a guidepost in leadership decision making processes within their organization. According to the same PwC study, millennials who have a strong connection to the purpose of their organization are 5.3 times more likely to stay.
