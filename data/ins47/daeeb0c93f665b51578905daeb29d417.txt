Argo Visa Co-Founder & BDV Solutions COO Chris Richardson Named Riley Institute Fellow

GREENVILLE, S.C. (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 BDV Solutions, a tech-enabled, turnkey provider of labor shortage solutions to U.S. employers and employment-based immigration services to foreign nationals, and Argo Visa, the world's first company connecting visa applicants with former U.S. visa officers who prepare them for a successful visa interview, today announced that Chris Richardson, an officer of both companies, was named a Fellow of The Riley Institute at Furman University. He joins 42 fellow Lowcountry professionals nominated by alumni of the Institute's Diversity Leaders Initiative (DLI) in the 16th class of the DLI.
