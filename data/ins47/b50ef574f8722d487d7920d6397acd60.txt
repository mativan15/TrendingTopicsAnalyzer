The 36 th ‘District Formation Day' was observed here on Thursday. To commemorate the district formation day, all government offices were cleaned and decorated as instructed by District Collector K. Senthil Raj, who participated in the cleaning operation held on the Collectorate premises. Saplings were planted on the premises of the government offices. After planting the saplings on the Thoothukudi Taluk Office campus, the Collector inaugurated the drivers' waiting room there.
