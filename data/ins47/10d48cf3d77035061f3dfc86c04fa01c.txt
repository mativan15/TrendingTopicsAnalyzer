'Overzealous' Lidl security guard broke shoplifter's leg

Leandro Da Silva broke a shoplifter's leg after tackling him (Image: Daily Record)A Lidl security guard has appeared in court after he broke a shoplifter's leg when he tackled him to the ground.Leandro Da Silva kneeled on the culprit's body to restrain him before spotting a second thief and piling her on top of the man, leaving him in agony.The city's sheriff court heard the 28-year-old had received no training from his security firm before being stationed in the Lidl, reports The Daily Record.The court heard Da Silva was informed by his bosses that his job was to "stop shoplifters and apprehend them".
