2022 Colorado Inno Awards winner Fluid Truck focuses on tech to face supply chain issues
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 23:25:00

Fluid Truck is the winner of the 2022 Colorado Inno award in the green tech category.

Fluid Truck's peer-to-peer truck-sharing platform gives individuals and businesses access to the larger vehicles they need to get the job done, whether that job is moving to a new home or delivering products to customers.
