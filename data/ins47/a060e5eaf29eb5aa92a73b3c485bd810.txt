2022-06-04 13:52:44

The Saratoga Historical Foundation was set to break ground for its new blacksmith exhibit on Saturday, June 4. The exhibit will open will demonstrate the work a blacksmith of the 1850s would have handled. Interactivity and sound effects will add to the ambience. Also planned is a nonfunctional outhouse as part of the turn-of-the-century theme of all the structures in the Saratoga Historical Park.
The blacksmith exhibit, along with the the McWilliams House, one-room schoolhouse and the museum, are used to educate visitors about pioneer living as well as Saratoga's history.
