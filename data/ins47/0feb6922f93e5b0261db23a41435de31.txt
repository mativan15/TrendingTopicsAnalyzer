Hardik Patel to join BJP tomorrow
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 18:45:00

PTIFormer Congress leader Hardik Patel.After a bitter parting with the Congress last week after calling the party a hater of Hindus, a vocal critic of the BJP in the 2017 Gujarat assembly elections and the face of Patidar protests, Hardik Patel, is all set to join the BJP on Thursday in the presence of state BJP president CR Patil. Known for his fiery politics against the BJP and packed rallies that pushed the BJP to a difficult position in the 2017 polls, Patel's likely induction into the BJP has evoked mixed reactions in the party.
