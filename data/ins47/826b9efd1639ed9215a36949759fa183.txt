Insurers ‘must pass on reform savings to consumers'
irishtimes.com
politics
2022-10-19 00:00:00

Insurers must broaden their risk appetite and pass on savings generated by Government reforms of the sector to consumers, the Minister responsible for financial services said.Seán Fleming, Minister of State for Financial Services, sits on the Cabinet committee subgroup on insurance reform — with reform of the sector and decreasing prices for consumers set as a priority for the Government when it was formed in 2020.On Wednesday morning, Mr Fleming unveiled a report by PwC and Insurance Ireland on the market.
