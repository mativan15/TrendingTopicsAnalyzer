Impac Mortgage stockholders approve preferred stock exchange offers (NYSE:IMH)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-21 20:52:57

designer491 Impac Mortgage Holdings (NYSE:IMH) said holder of its common stock approved proposals that allow the closing of its previously announced offers to exchange some of its preferred stock for stock and warrants. As the company's exchange offers expired on 11:59 PM on Thursday, holders of ~69.23% of outstanding shares of its 9.375% series B preferred stock and holders of ~67.59% of 9.125% series C preferred stock tendered their shares for the exchange. In addition some 1,300 shares of series C preferred that remain subject to guaranteed delivery procedures were tendered before the expiration date.
