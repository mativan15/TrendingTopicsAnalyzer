Soccer Football - Serie A - Lazio v Udinese - Stadio Olimpico, Rome, Italy - October 16, 2022 Lazio's Ciro Immobile comes off after sustaining an injury REUTERS/Alberto LingriaOct 19 (Reuters) - Serie A's joint top scorer Ciro Immobile has suffered a medium-grade injury to his left hamstring, his club Lazio said on Wednesday after the Italy international underwent tests.Lazio did not mention the timeline of his recovery but Italian newspaper Gazetta dello Sport reported that Immobile will be out until January 2023.
