KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy iHeartMedia's Corey OlsonOct 20, 2022When it comes to inflation, talk is cheap for the Biden administration. After spending most of last year downplaying rising prices and calling inflation "transitory," the White House finally acknowledged the problem earlier this year, calling inflation the president's "number one issue." But months after that, on top of multiple interest rate hikes from the Federal Reserve (and more to come), the annual inflation rate stubbornly remains at a 40-year high of 8.
