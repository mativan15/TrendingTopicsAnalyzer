Treasury yields pull back from multiyear highs after U.K. tax U-turn
Copyright 2022, MarketWatch, Inc.
finance
2022-10-17 12:01:00

Yields on U.S. Treasurys retreated Monday as investors expressed relief over the British government's decision to rescind proposed tax cuts that had sparked chaos in U.K. government bonds.What yields are doing The yield on the 2-year Treasury note TMUBMUSD02Y, 4.443% fell to 4.435%, down from 4.507% at 3 p.m. Eastern on Friday. The 2-year's Friday finish was the highest since Aug. 8, 2007, based on 3 p.m. figures, according to Dow Jones Market Data. Yields and debt prices move opposite each other.
