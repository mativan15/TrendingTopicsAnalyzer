June 01, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time BELLEVUE, Wash.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Neville Ray, president of technology of T-Mobile US, Inc. (NASDAQ: TMUS), will present and provide a business update on Friday, June 3, 2022 at 11:00 a.m. Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) at the UBS Future of 5G Event.

A live webcast of the virtual event will be available on the Company's Investor Relations website at http://investor.t-mobile.com. An on-demand replay will be available shortly after the conclusion of the presentation.
