Economy tops abortion as key issue before US midterm elections
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

Republican candidates are gaining support less than three weeks before the American midterm elections as voters worry about the economy.The party's candidates for the House of Representatives and the Senate are attempting to prise Congress from the Democrats. They are targeting President Biden's economic record amid fears of recession, inflation at a 40-year-high and a volatile stock market.According to the latest polls, the economy has pulled ahead of abortion, democracy and immigration as the issue most on voters' minds.
