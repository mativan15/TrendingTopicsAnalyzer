Image source, Miles DavisImage caption, Birmingham City Council has been ordered to improve its proceduresA family will be refunded for 11 years of care home fees which their local authority charged incorrectly.The Local Government and Social Care Ombudsman also told Birmingham City Council to improve its procedures.The ombudsman stepped in after the family complained about being charged a "top-up" fee after their mother moved to a care home in 2011.The council said it "apologised unreservedly" and would give extra training to staff.
