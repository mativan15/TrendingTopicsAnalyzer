Musk announces excitement at closing overpriced Twitter deal
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 04:59:00

Despite the Space X founder's attempts to go back on his deal with Twitter in May, he has now expressed his excitement at closing the deal by October 28. The tech giant became the biggest shareholder of Twitter in April when he got a seat on the company board.Twitter shares subsequently surged when it was reported that he bought a 9.2 percent stake which was work almost $3billion, after which he made many suggestions of changes for the platform.Within two weeks, Musk offered to buy the platform in the interest of free speech, telling Twitter chairman Bret Taylor: 'Since making my investment, I now realise the company will neither thrive nor serve this societal imperative in its current form.
