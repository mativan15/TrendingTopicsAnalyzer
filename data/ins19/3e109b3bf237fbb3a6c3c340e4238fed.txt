On Supporting a Peaceful Transition in Chad
state.gov
news
2022-10-20 20:04:45

The United States condemns the violence in Chad involving clashes between security forces and demonstrators protesting the extension of Chad's original 18-month transition period, which expires today. We are deeply concerned by reports of casualties and urge all parties to deescalate the situation and exercise restraint.
