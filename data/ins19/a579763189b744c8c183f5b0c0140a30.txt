CCP-linked Chinese companies kept personal data of Indians
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 01:15:00

Chinese companies being investigated in India for tax evasion and dodgy finances were reportedly gathering the personal data of a large number of individuals, and the alleged real beneficiaries were senior members of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP). National security concerns over such access are exacerbated by the fact that Beijing can harness much of this data as Chinese companies are bound by data disclosure norms in accordance with their domestic laws. Many Chinese companies trace their ownership to senior members of the Communist Party, ET has reliably learnt.
