According to a survey, 69% of Americans plan to travel this summer, but rising gas prices and inflation now top their list of concerns, overtaking Covid. The national average for unleaded gas hit another new high Tuesday. After more than two years of largely staying home due to the pandemic, most Americans are ready to hit the road. Yet inflation and record-breaking gasoline prices are weighing on would-be vacationers, even more than Covid concerns, according to a report by Morning Consult.
