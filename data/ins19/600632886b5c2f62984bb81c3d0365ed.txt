Published: 05:58 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 05:58 EDT, 20 October 2022 Whether Tom Rogic plays at the World Cup in Qatar is the question many anxious Socceroos fans want answered.At 29, Rogic is arguably at the peak of his football powers.But after not featuring in crucial World Cup qualifying wins over the UAE and Peru due to 'personal reasons' in June, the gifted attacking midfielder appears to be on the outer at the worst possible time when it comes to national selection. Coach Graham Arnold confirmed to Daily Mail Australia Rogic will only have a small window to impress - at club level - after he was overlooked for the two recent friendlies versus New Zealand.
