CBI summons Sisodia in excise policy probe
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-16 17:57:44

New Delhi The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) has summoned Delhi deputy chief minister Manish Sisodia for questioning on Monday over alleged corruption in the formulation and implementation of the Delhi government's now-withdrawn excise policy 2021-22, people familiar with the matter said on Sunday.The probe agency has asked the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader to appear before it at its headquarters at 11am on Monday, they said.Reacting to the summons, Sisodia said he will provide 'full cooperation'.
