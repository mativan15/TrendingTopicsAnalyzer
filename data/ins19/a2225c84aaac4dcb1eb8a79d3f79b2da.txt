CHARLES Siebert has died at 84 due to Covid-related pneumonia, his family said.
The actor, known for his role in CBS drama Trapper John, MD, succumbed to the illness at University of California San Francisco Medical Center, his daughter told The Hollywood Reporter.

Charles Siebert was born on March 9, 1938, in Kenosha, Wisconsin.
He studied acting at university before eventually appearing on Broadway in 1967.
