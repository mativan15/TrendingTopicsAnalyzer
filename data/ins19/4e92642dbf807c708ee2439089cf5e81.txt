AgenciesRepresentative ImageIndia's GDP grew by 4.1% in the fourth quarter of FY22. Meanwhile, the overall growth recorded for the financial year 2021-22 is estimated at 8.7% as against the contraction of 6.6% in FY21. The estimate of 8.7% for FY22 is the highest in 22 years in terms of back series data. The nominal GDP grew by 19.5% in the financial year 2021-22. GVA for FY22 came in at 8.1% compared to a contraction of 4.8% in the preceding fiscal. Contact-intensive sectors like trade, hotels, and transport grew by 11.
