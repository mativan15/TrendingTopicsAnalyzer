American Airlines stock slips after revenue growth outlook raised but capacity view trimmed
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 11:53:51

Shares of American Airlines Group Inc. AAL, +0.98% fell 1.0% in premarket trading Friday, after the air carrier raised its revenue growth outlook but trimmed its capacity guidance and raised its fuel-cost estimate. The company now expects second-quarter revenue to be up 11% to 13% from the same period in pre-pandemic 2019, compared with previous guidance of up 6% to 8%. For capacity, the company now expects available seat miles to be down 7% to 8% from 2019 levels versus previous expectations of a decline of 6% to 8%.
