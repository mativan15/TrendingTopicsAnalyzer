FORGET websites, the next time you buy a Dyson product it could be in the metaverse.
The British firm has already built a virtual reality experience for people to explore its popular hair dryers, straighteners and stylers but that's just the beginning.
Dyson's current VR experienceCredit: Dyson
A senior official for the company has revealed to The Sun that they are already thinking about the metaverse.
Sean Newmarch, Global Ecommerce Director at Dyson, envisions not only for people to be able to virtually walk in and get hands on with vacuum cleaners, hairdryers and other well-known products.
