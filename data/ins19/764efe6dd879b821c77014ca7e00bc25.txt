Pekic/E+ via Getty Images FinVolution (NYSE:FINV) stock is jumping 7% in Wednesday late morning trading after the company posted Q1 earnings and reiterated its full-year loan volume target of RMB 17.5B-RMB 180B. Citigroup analyst Julia Cheung, though, upgraded the Chinese fintech to Buy on its improved take rate outlook. Management guided a net take rate of ~3.7%-3.8% for FY2022 on the back of 24% loan pricing, up from its previous outlook of 3.6% take rate and 23% loan pricing, the analyst said.
