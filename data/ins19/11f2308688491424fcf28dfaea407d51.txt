PTISpecial train arrives at New Delhi Railway Station.The Railways earned Rs 17,526.48 crore from special train services in 2021-22, which is almost 45 per cent of the total passenger revenue in the financial year, an RTI reply has revealed. The Railways ran only special train services when it resumed passenger train operations post the coronavirus pandemic in May 2020 in a bid to discourage people from travelling in the pandemic. According to the reply to an RTI application filed by Chandra Shekhar Gaur, the Railways said that in the pre-pandemic year of 2019-2020, it earned Rs 804.
