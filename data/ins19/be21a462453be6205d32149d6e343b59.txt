Lauv Announces The Lauvstream: A Live Music & Meditation Experience in Partnership with BetterHelp, Powered by Kiswe and Produced by Mercury Studios

New Jersey (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Lauv has announced a one-night-only digital event to celebrate his most recent album All 4 Nothing, in partnership with BetterHelp, powered by Kiswe, the interactive video company. The Lauvstream: A Live Music & Meditation Experience will take fans through an interactive journey exploring mindfulness, self-care and the power of vulnerability. The event includes an intimate and heartfelt live band performance that showcases songs from Lauv's latest album like never before, alongside some of his much loved million-selling hits.
