MELANIE Pillman was the widow of wrestling legend Brian Pillman, and mother to current wrestler Brian Pillman Jr. 
She and Brian Jr had a tumultuous relationship but were working to mend it when she died on June 1, 2022, at the age of 56. 
Brian Pillman Jr is the son of Melanie and Brian PillmanCredit: Instagram Melanie Pillman was the widow of Brian Pillman, known as the Loose Cannon, who died in 1997 at the age of 35. 
Recently, she was a common figure on son Brian Jr's twitch streaming channel, where she answered questions about her late husband's career and cheered on her son's career.
