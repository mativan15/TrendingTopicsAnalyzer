Bus driver among three held for attempting to rob finance firm

A gang of three, including a private bus driver, were arrested by the Rajagopalnagar police on Thursday for allegedly attempting to rob Manappuram finance firm on May 26.The accused, identified as Kumar, Nagaraj and Bahaddur Singh, broke open the shutter to gain entry into the firm and tried to break open the safe locker using a gas cutter after disconnecting the CCTV, when the branch manager got an alert and informed the police. By the time the police reached the spot, the accused had dumped their plan and escaped.
