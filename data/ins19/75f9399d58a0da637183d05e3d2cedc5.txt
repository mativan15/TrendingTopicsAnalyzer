iStock(Representational image) (WCL) on Friday said its associate company has bagged a contract valuing Saudi Riyal 490 million (about Rs 1,000 crore) to supply steel pipes in Saudi Arabia. The project is in line with the Saudi Arabia government's aim to expand the kingdom's water pipeline infrastructure through investment from the private sector, WCL said in a statement. "Associate company East Pipes Integrated Company for Industry (EPIC) has signed a contract with Saline Water Conversion Corporation (SWCC) valued at SAR 490 million.
