Mining, tech stocks drive ASX 200 higher
smh.com.au
business
2022-06-03 06:41:49

Stock and bond markets across China, Hong Kong and Taiwan were closed on Friday for a public holiday.Australia's energy sector was the best performer for the week with the oil price holding gains in Asia Friday - and set for a sixth weekly advance - after a keenly anticipated OPEC+ supply meeting delivered only a modest increase in output. Reports on Thursday suggested the Saudis were promising to pump significantly more oil, and Russia might be exempted from the alliance's monthly supply agreements.
