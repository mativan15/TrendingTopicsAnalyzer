Volvo Cars joins SteelZero initiative for fossil-free steel
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-31 16:30:00

Last year, the carmaker announced a collaboration with Swedish steelmaker SSAB to jointly explore the development of fossil-free, high-quality steel for use in the automotive industry through SSAB's HYBRIT initiative.New Delhi: Swedish carmaker Volvo Cars on Tuesday signed an agreement with SteelZero committing itself to stringent CO2-based steel sourcing requirements by 2030. By 2050, all the steel it sources should be net-zero steel, which is in line with the company's ambition to be climate neutral by 2040.
