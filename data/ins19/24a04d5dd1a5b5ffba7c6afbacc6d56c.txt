Research by Tesco has found that almost one in five (17 percent) of people are feeling more financial pressure this Christmas compared to previous years. To help the nation spend less, Tesco has partnered with money-saving gurus Ricky and Naomi Willis - who blog under the name Skint Dad - and have asked them to share their seasonal savings tips.
