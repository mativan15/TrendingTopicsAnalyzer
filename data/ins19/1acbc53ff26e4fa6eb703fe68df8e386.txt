CONCORD, Mass., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- reacHIRE, committed to supporting people returning to the workplace through returnship programs and technology solutions, today announced that the company has been named to the Boston Business Journal's Fast 50 list, which represents the 50 fastest-growing companies in the state. 'Despite the challenges of the last two years, there has been a growing focus on the relationship between employer and employee, and reacHIRE sits at the center of that conversation,' said reacHIRE CEO Addie Swartz.
