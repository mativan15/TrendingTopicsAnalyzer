By Reuters Published: 12:49 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 12:49 EDT, 20 October 2022 By Christoph Steitz, Andres Gonzalez and Emma-Victoria Farr FRANKFURT/MADRID, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Morgan Stanley's infrastructure investment arm is weighing a sale of a 40% stake in German wind and solar power project developer PNE AG after being approached by potential suitors, three people familiar with the matter said. Morgan Stanley Infrastructure Partners (MSIP) acquired the PNE stake following a failed takeover attempt in 2020.
