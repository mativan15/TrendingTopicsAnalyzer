The penalty for not wearing masks in public places in the national capital has officially been lifted as the Delhi Government on Thursday issued an order withdrawing the Rs 500 fine imposed under the Epidemic Act, to prevent the spread of COVID-19.The Delhi Disaster Management Authority (DDMA) had last month decided to stop levying the penalty after September 30, citing the fall in new COVID-19 infections.
