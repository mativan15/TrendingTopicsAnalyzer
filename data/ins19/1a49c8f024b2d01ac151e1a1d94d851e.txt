Smart tips to avoid loan app scams
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 05:37:51

Over the past 2 years, illegal & unregistered loan apps have been on the rise with fraudsters trying to scam people by offering them money.The usual modus operandi of these apps is to offer money to those in need and then extort more wealth out of them and harass the victims.The rampant rise of fraud loan apps has compelled the RBI to prepare a white list of legal apps. Here are some ways to avoid fraud.You should always double-check if the bank or NBFC is registered with the RBI. You can check it in the central bank's website.
