Mullen completes purchase of Electric Last Mile manufacturing plant and assets
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-19 12:25:02

Mario Tama/Getty Images News Mullen Automotive (NASDAQ:MULN) has purchased the assets of bankrupt electric vehicle (EV) company Electric Last Mile Solutions (ELMS), including its manufacturing plant, inventory and intellectual property. The plant in Mishawaka, Indiana will run retail and commercial EV production lines for Mullen. It can produce up to 50,000 vehicles annually. The assets will also help Mullen speed up the production and commercialization of the Mullen FIVE and Bollinger B1, B2 retail vehicles by 12 months.
