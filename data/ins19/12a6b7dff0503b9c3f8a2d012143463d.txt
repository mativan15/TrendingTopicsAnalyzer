Day after b'day, CM to meet Hyd investors
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 05:45:00

Bhubaneswar: Chief minister Naveen Patnaik will leave for Hyderabad on his birthday on Sunday to attend an investors' meet on Monday, officials said. A high-level delegation led by Naveen would be hosting on one-on-one government to business meetings on Monday.In the evening, Odisha Investors' Meet will be held at a hotel where the chief minister will interact with investors and apprise them about the Make in Odisha Conclave , scheduled from November 30 to December 4, and invite them to attend the event.
