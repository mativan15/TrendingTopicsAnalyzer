What and Where?The T20 World Cup, the global showpiece for cricket's shortest format, runs in Australia from October 16th to November 13th. Ireland start their tournament with three games in Hobart. If they do well, they move elsewhere in the country depending on where they finish in their group.This sounds familiar ...That's because it is. The previous iteration of the tournament happened just last year in the UAE. The time frame between T20 World Cups has never been regimented, unlike other tournaments and other sports, but Covid made what was already a fairly random system even more complicated.
