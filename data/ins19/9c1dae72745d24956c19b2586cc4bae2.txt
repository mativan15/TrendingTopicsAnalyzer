October 17, 2022 08:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO & SAN MATEO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Delos Insurance Solutions (Delos), a technology-powered insurance provider, and Guidewire (NYSE: GWRE) announced that Delos selected InsuranceNow to increase agent digital engagement for business growth. The company plans to implement InsuranceNow to its homeowners line of business in the state of California. Guidewire PartnerConnect Consulting member Exavalu will be leading the implementation project.
