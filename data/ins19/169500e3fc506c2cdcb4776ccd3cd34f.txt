The year 2022 has not been as good as 2021 for Indian equities as global central banks put an end to easy money by withdrawing excess liquidity in order to bring down sticky inflation. This roiled sentiment and saw foreign institutional investors exit Indian equities in a big way. But the community that remained unperturbed by the market volatility are retail investors.
