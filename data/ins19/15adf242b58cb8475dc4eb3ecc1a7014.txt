Soho Square Exits Investment in Technical Fire Safety Group to svt Group

LONDON, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Soho Square Capital LLP ("Soho Square"), an investment firm focused on serving the financing needs of established and growing UK and European SMEs, has exited its investment in Technical Fire Safety Group Ltd ("TFSG") to svt Holdings GmbH ("svt").

TFSG has grown to become Europe's market-leading independent fire safety glass specialist, producing, processing and supplying mission critical fire-rated products to over 1,300 customers across 40+ countries, in both the construction and refurbishment markets.
