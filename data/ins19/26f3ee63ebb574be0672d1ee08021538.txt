Raiders playoff chances fall after Week 6 bye week
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-18 06:32:53

The Raiders enjoyed a Week 6 bye week as they saw several AFC contenders lose. But how much did that actually impact their chances of making the playoffs? The answer might surprise you.
According to FiveThirtyEight, the Raiders saw their playoff chances dip from 18 percent going into Week 6 to 16 percent. That is mostly due to other teams just getting some wins, like the Jets, Steelers, Chargers, Patriots, etc.
