Aussies nationwide have been left scratching their heads after iconic snack brand Ryvita mysteriously disappeared from Woolworths and Coles shelves over the past few weeks.

Fans of Ryvita's rye-based crispbread have noticed that their local Woolworths and Coles stores have stopped selling it altogether, and are desperate to find out why.
