$1.2 million Chick-fil-A scheduled to open in Cibolo next year

Editor's note: This story was published through a partnership between the San Antonio Business Journal and KSAT.

Chicken lovers rejoice! A $1.2 million Chick-fil-A is setting down roots just northeast of San Antonio in Cibolo.

The restaurant chain is scheduled to begin construction on July 10, 2023, and will be completed by December 14, 2023, according to a Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation filing.

The 4,876-square-foot eatery will be at Cibolo Valley Drive and Borgfeld Road in Cibolo.
