Published: 09:35 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 09:35 EDT, 21 October 2022 Connor McDiarmid has declared war against childhood cancer after losing his best friend to the disease when he was just eight years old.Speaking to FEMAIL, the 10-year-old said he 'never wants another child to die from cancer' or for other kids to go through the grief of losing a friend.Blake Moulds was diagnosed with leukaemia in February 2021, after his grandmother and legal guardian, Janelle, noticed bruises forming over his legs after the first day back to school.
