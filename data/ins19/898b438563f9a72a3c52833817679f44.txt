YOUR iPhone has a clever trick that lets you set any TikTok video as your wallpaper.
It's easy to do and doesn't cost a penny.
Your iPhone has a hidden wallpaper trick that you'll loveCredit: TikTok / @heimadigital / Apple
If you didn't realise, you can set Live Wallpapers on your iPhone Home Screen.
These are special wallpapers that move around when you hold your finger on them.
If you've ever set a Live Photo that you've snapped as your wallpaper, you may have noticed it already.
But you can also convert any TikTok clip into a Live Wallpaper too.
