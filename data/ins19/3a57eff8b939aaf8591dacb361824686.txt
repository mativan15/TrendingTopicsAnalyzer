Noida: 2 Chinese men held for Rs 107 crore fraud via app
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 03:30:00

NOIDA: In a joint operation, the UP Special Task Force and Noida police on Saturday arrested two Chinese nationals for allegedly cheating hundreds of Indians of more than Rs 100 crore through schemes that mirrored a Ponzi.The scam has at least one common link with a luxury club an illegally staying Chinese national had been running since 2020 that was busted this July. Ravi Natwarlal Thakkar , one of the Indians associated with this Ponzi fraud, was the Indian partner of the Chinese man who ran the club.
