Disney Plus vs Netflix: Obi-Wan Kenobi faces off against Stranger Things in streaming clash

Millions of us will be watching two sci-fi giants go head to head tonight (Friday) in a historic streaming battle between Disney Plus and Netflix - one of the biggest duels the market has yet seen.

In the blue corner we have Disney's hugely-anticipated Star Wars series Obi-Wan Kenobi, while in the red corner there's Netflix's equally long-awaited season four of its hit show Stranger Things.
