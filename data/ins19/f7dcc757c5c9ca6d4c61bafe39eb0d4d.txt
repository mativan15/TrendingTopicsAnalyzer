Chief Revenue Officer at Wrike

Brian Clark joins leading work management company Wrike as CRO. He will be responsible for doubling Wrike's ARR and expanding its global sales strategy. Clark comes from InMoment, where he tripled the business as CRO. Prior to that, he was one of the first employees and CRO of SiriusDecisions, where he played a pivotal role in shaping the GTM sales and marketing strategies that drove market leadership and significant revenue growth, ultimately leading to an acquisition by Forrester Research.
