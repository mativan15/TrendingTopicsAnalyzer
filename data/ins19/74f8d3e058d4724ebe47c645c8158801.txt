Textron Aviation announces order for up to 14 jets at NBAA
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 14:58:00

Textron Aviation on Tuesday announced an order for up to 14 Cessna Citation business jets at the National Business Aviation Association convention in Orlando.

The order comes from private travel company flyExclusive, which will add eight Citation XLS Gen2 jets and up to six Citation Longitude jets to its fleet through the deal.

The aircraft have a combined value of more than $300 million at previously published prices.

Deliveries of the XLS Gen2 — an upgraded model that that entered service in May — will take place in 2024.
