REDWOOD CITY, Calif., Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Codexis, Inc. (Nasdaq: CDXS), a leading enzyme engineering company enabling the promise of synthetic biology, today announced that it will report its financial results for the third quarter of 2022 on Thursday, November 3, 2022, following the close of market. Codexis management will host a conference call and webcast at 4:30 p.m. Eastern Time to discuss the Company's financial results and provide a business update.



Participants may access the live webcast on the Codexis Investor Relations website, where it will be archived for 90 days.
