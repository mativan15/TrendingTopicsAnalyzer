Image caption, The scenic view from the new location of Falmouth Oyster FestivalThe new waterfront location for this year's Oyster Festival in Falmouth has been deemed a success by organisers.It comes as droves of people travelled to Falmouth as the festival got into full swing on Saturday.About 10,000 oysters were expected to be eaten over the course of the weekend at the event's new waterfront location in Church Street car park.The annual festival celebrates the native Fal Oyster and the start of the dredging season.
