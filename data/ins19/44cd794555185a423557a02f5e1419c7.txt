WESTFIELD, Mass., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Western New England Bancorp, Inc. (the 'Company' or 'WNEB') (NasdaqGS: WNEB), the holding company for Westfield Bank (the 'Bank'), announced that on October 13, 2022, the Company completed its 2.4 million stock repurchase plan ('2021 Plan') at an average price per share of $8.40. The 2021 Plan was authorized on April 27, 2021. On July 26, 2022, the Board of Directors of the Company authorized a new stock repurchase plan, pursuant to which the Company may repurchase up to 1.
