Things seem to be going from bad to worse for those who invested in insurance giant LIC's IPO. After a disappointing debut on listing day In May 2022, LIC's shares continued to bleed and reach new all-time lows in the past 4 months, with the first instance being barely a month after the IPO, i.e., in June.And today is no exception. LIC shares have again hit an all-time low mark today. shutterstockLife Insurance Corporation of India's shares slumped 2% to a new record low of Rs 588 on both the NSE and BSE during intra-day trading today, and closed at Rs 588.
