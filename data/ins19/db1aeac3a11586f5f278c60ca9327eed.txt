 The Punjab and Haryana high court has sought response from the central government on a plea challenging the Army's decision to reserve 90% vacancies in the Army Dental Corps (ADC) for males. The high court bench of justice GS Sandhawalia and justice Jagmohan Bansal while seeking response by December 13 also directed that the petitioner woman be interviewed provisionally.
