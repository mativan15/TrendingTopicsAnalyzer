Stock futures are slightly higher ahead of May's jobs report
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-02 22:18:16

Trader on the floor of the NYSE, June 1, 2022.Stock futures rose Thursday evening as investors focus their attention on more jobs data due out Friday morning.Futures tied to the Dow Jones Industrial Average were up about 78 points. S&P 500 futures and Nasdaq 100 futures were both higher by 0.3%.In regular trading Thursday, all three major indexes snapped two-day losing streaks, putting them on pace for a winning week. The Dow added 435.05 points, or 1.3%. The S&P 500 gained 1.8% and the Nasdaq Composite advanced 2.
