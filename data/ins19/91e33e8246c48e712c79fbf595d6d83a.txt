Vax proof not must to visit govt offices
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 06:15:00

Vadodara: With Covid-19 cases in the state remaining on the lower side, the state government has decided to allow into government office people not carrying Covid-19 vaccination certificates or self-declarations stating that they were vaccinated. Earlier, it had made the certificates or self-declaration mandatory for visitors to the government or related offices.The circular asking officials to ensure that those visiting government offices were vaccinated was brought out on December 31 last year.
