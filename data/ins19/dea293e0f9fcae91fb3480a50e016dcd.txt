One of the constants in Dungeons & Dragons games, across every edition, is Monty Python. Quote one of the influential troupe's shows, films or audio recordings and there's always a chance you'll derail the scene as everyone around the table fires off their favorite jokes. The hard part for the Dungeon Master is getting the game back on track.
