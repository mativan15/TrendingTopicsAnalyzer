Did Dr. Oz Win the Nobel Prize in Medicine?
snopes.com
science
2022-10-19 18:16:38

Television personality Dr. Mehmet Oz won a Nobel Prize in medicine for a 'memory drug.' Dr. Mehmet Oz, television personality and 2022 Republican candidate for U.S. Senate, has had a colorful career, but winning a Nobel Prize is not among his achievements. Since 2021, online articles and posts claim he won the Nobel Prize for medicine, including this post that wonders if it was for a 'memory pill.' The 'memory drug' in question could be referring to a number of instances where 'The Dr. Oz Show' and Oz himself promoted brain supplements meant to enhance memory.
