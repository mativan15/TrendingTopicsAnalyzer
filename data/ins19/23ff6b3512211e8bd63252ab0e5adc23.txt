When Sara Ali Khan danced on the street and people thought she was a beggar

news
2022-05-27 11:19:26

Sara Ali Khan might belong to the Pataudi family and be among the most sought after actors from the younger lot today, but there was a time when she was mistaken for a beggar. This, and several other candid confessions by Sara at various occasions over the years have kept her image relatable to fans.
Sara had sometime back, revealed her beggar story during an interview, the video of which went viral in 2020. Sara shared that the incident happened when she was out shopping with her parents, Saif Ali Khan and Amrita Singh.
