'We are so proud of Hilary and Anelia for their recognition as two of our best female consultants in the industry." DAYTON, Ohio (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 Centric Consulting, an international business and technology consulting firm, announced two of its Chicago-based consultants, Hilary Lee and Anelia Schmitt, have been honored by Consulting Magazine with the Women Leaders in Technology 2022 Award. Now in its third year, the awards acknowledge contributions made by women in the technology consulting profession.
