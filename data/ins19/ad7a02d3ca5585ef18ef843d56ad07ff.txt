Man who won $10m on lottery is sentenced to life in prison for girlfriend's murder

2022-05-30 19:07:27

A man who won $10m on a scratch-off lottery ticket back in 2017 has been sentenced to life in prison for shooting his girlfriend in the back of the head while she slept.Michael Hill, 54, was sentenced on Friday to life without the possibility of parole for the first-degree murder of Keonna Graham, 24, in 2020.The body of Ms Graham was discovered by a maid inside a hotel room in the SureStay Hotel in Shallotte, North Carolina, on 20 July 2020.The 24-year-old had suffered a single gunshot wound to the back of the head.
