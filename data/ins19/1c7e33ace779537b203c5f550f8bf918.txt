AIkido Pharma Approves Reverse Stock Split; Stock Falls
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 20:59:00

By Will Feuer Shares of AIkido Pharma Inc. fell in after-hours trading after the company said its board of directors has approved a reverse stock split at a ratio of 1 for 17 in an effort to gain listing compliance with The Nasdaq Capital Market. The reverse split will become effective at 12:01 a.m. Eastern time on June 7, the company said, and the stock will open for trading on a post-split basis the same day. The reverse split was approved by shareholders at the company's annual meeting on May 20.
