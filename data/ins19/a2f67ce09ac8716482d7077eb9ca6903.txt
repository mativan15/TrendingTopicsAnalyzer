Long-time A-J newsroom leader Burle Pettit dies
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-29 21:34:46

Burle Pettit, whose eloquent writing and effective leadership entertained Avalanche-Journal readers and influenced its journalists for more than four decades, died early Sunday. He was 87.

Pettit

Pettit joined the newspaper as a sports writer in 1960 and steadily climbed the newsroom's organizational ladder, culminating in his being appointed editor Jan. 1, 1995. He retired from that position five years later. Following retirement, he was named the paper's editor emeritus with his name appearing on the A-J opinion page masthead.
