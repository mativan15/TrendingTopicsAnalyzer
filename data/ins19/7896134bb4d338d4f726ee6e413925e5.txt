LONDON, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Innovation scale-up  Wazoku  has become one of the global B2B technology companies chosen as a  TrustRadius 2022 Tech Cares award winner TrustRadius provides verified, peer-based guidance and research to help business leaders to find and select the right software for their needs. Each year it publishes its Tech Cares awards list, recognising the B2B companies it feels demonstrated a strong commitment to corporate social responsibility (CSR) programmes that enable employees and local communities to live in a more sustainable and equitable world.
