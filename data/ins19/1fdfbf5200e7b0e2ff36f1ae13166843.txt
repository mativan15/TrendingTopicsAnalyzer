Tata Neu has big plans for financial services, says chief executive Pratik Pal
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 18:45:00

Chennai: Tata Neu has 'big plans' surrounding financial services because that is always the biggest money-making segment, Tata Digital chief executive Pratik Pal said. "So, you will see us doing insurance and investments ... (We have) very big plans," Pal said on Saturday. As per Tata Digital's website, the super app of the Tata Group currently enables users to earn rewards on all credit card spending, as well as get instant access to funds like personal loans and credit lines.

"It also allows them to protect their assets and grow their wealth with custom insurance plans created specifically for them.
