A MAN has told how he has been left with a terrible tattoo after deciding to get an inspirational inking across his arm.
He opted to get the motivational motto on his left forearm as a daily reminder to persevere - but everyone has been pointing out the same issue.
The embarrassing tattoo has been left open to interpretation among Reddit usersCredit: Reddit
The client wanted the phrase "life is tough, but so are you" scribed upon his skin, to encourage him to overcome adversity. 
But his moving message was unfortunately warped by the tattoo artist, distorting the entire expression and making it illegible.
