Robert Stravinsky Launches Scholarship for Healthcare Students

2022-10-20 15:41:00

WALTHAM, Mass., Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Students entering a healthcare school can now apply for the Robert Stravinsky scholarship for healthcare students. Students who are currently enrolled in a certified university in the United States of America and studying on the road to becoming healthcare providers can apply for the scholarship. High school students who will attend university in pursuit of a medical degree can also apply. The scholarship will be awarded based on an essay competition.
