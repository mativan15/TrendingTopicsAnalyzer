The UK's newly appointed finance minister Jeremy Hunt will lay out details of the Truss government's tax plans Monday, seen as a move to try to settle markets still jittery about their impact on the economy.The Treasury chief is speeding up the announcement of some tax measures by two weeks, after a series of abrupt U-turns by the government on its mini-budget failed to calm worries about its economic credibility.The British pound was up 0.74% at $1.1266 early Monday, having touched $1.1305 in the wake of the news.
