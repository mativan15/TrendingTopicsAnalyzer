Britain needs stability and a united leadership
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 23:01:00

Coming less than a week after former Chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng was unceremoniously sacked, the latest evidence of divisions within the Government will cause dismay to many. At a time when the nation faces huge challenges, people are crying out for a stable government which has a plan for Britain and the authority to see it through.Ms Braverman used her resignation letter to express concerns about the direction of the Government, highlighting divisions within the Conservative Party.It appears she was particularly concerned about proposals to allow more migrants into the UK, which some will feel run contrary to promises that Brexit freedoms would allow us control of our borders.
