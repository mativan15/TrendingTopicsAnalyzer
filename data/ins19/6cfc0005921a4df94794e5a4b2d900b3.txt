(Bloomberg) -- For episode 6 of the Zero podcast, Bloomberg Green's Akshat Rathi interviews Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau about Canada's emissions-reduction targets, whether the war in Ukraine will accelerate the clean-energy transition, and how he squares Canada's climate pledges with its economic reliance on oil and gas. This conversation was recorded live at the National Arts Centre in Ottawa at an event hosted by the Canadian Climate Institute and the Net Zero Advisory Body.
