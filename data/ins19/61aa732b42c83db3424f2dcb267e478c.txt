Landmark NJ funeral home featured in ‘The Sopranos' may be demolished
nypost.com
news
2022-10-18 17:02:45

The historic New Jersey funeral home were mobsters wound up after getting whacked in 'The Sopranos' is facing a hit job itself. 
The Irvine-Cozzarelli Memorial Home on Washington Avenue — which was discussed and featured in numerous episodes of the HBO Mafia drama — could be demolished as part of a redevelopment plan recently approved by Belleville officials, NJ.com reported. 
It's a controversial move, as the building on Washington Avenue where the storied business is located dates back to 1885 — and the local historical society is doesn't want to see it fitted for cement shoes.
