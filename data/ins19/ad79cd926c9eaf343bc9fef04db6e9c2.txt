October 21, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MEXICO CITY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best will sponsor and participate at the Cumbre Seguros Masivos 2022, which takes place Oct. 25-27, 2022, in Miami.

Carlos De la Torre, senior director of operations and business development for AM Best in Latin America, will give a presentation, titled, 'AM Best's Vision of Embedded Insurance,' at 11:35 a.m. (EDT) on Wednesday, Oct. 26. During the presentation, De la Torre will discuss the defining characteristics of embedded insurance and how it can be used to help close the insurance protection gap.
