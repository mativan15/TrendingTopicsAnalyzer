Less than a month before the 2022 midterm election, a new Des Moines Register/Mediacom Iowa Poll will share the results of a head-to-head matchup between U.S. Sen. Chuck Grassley, a Republican, and retired Navy Admiral Mike Franken, a Democrat.

The first results from the October Iowa Poll will be published online at 6 p.m. Saturday at DesMoinesRegister.com. The results will also be published in the Des Moines Sunday Register.
