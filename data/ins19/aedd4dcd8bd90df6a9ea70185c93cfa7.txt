Hollywood Examines Future of Storytelling at View Conference
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-10-20 16:16:08

The potential for advances in technology to help create and share new types of stories, as well as diversity and inclusion, were key themes during a discussion of the future of storytelling at the View VFX and animation conference, which is taking place this week in person in Turin, Italy, and virtually via a live stream. Speakers including Lucasfilm and ILM's creative head Rob Bredow discussed everything from VR and AR experiences to new forms of entertainment such as Walt Disney World's Star Wars: Galactic Starcruiser experience.
