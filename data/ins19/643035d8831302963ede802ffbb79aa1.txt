David Ramos/Getty Images News Investment Thesis International Business Machines (NYSE:IBM) demonstrated resilience during the market selloff, reported topline growth of 11% (constant currency), and raised 2022 revenue guidance earlier this month. It is easy to draw a causal effect between the first and the latter, postulating a successful turnaround plan. In my view, IBM's ticker performance mostly mirrors external market trends instead of a fundamental change in its market position, the root cause of its abysmal performance over the years.
