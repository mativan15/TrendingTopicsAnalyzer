Does it finally make sense to buy an annuity?
thetimes.co.uk

The 52 per cent rise in annuity rates since January is making them a viable retirement strategy for the first time in years.These insurance products were once obligatory for savers looking to take money out of their pension. But a combination of rock-bottom rates and greater freedom over what you can do with their pots has pushed annuities out of favour.
