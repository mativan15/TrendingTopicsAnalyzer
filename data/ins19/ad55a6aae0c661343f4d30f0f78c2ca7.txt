 Greener pastures? Upstate New York has long been a destination for the crypto mining industry due to the region's abundance of hydroelectric power, favorable climate for cooling rigs and the cheapest electricity prices in the Northeast. In recent years, many shuttered power plants with unused electric infrastructure have been converted into mining centers, even before the exodus from China in 2021. However, the industry's foothold in New York could be coming to an end following a bill that passed early Friday in the New York State Senate.
