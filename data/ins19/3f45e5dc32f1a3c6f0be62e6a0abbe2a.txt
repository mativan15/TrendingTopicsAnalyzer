After the first song ‘Chad Gayi Chad Gayi' and the poster release, the makers of ‘Oye Makhna' have now hooked the audience with the trailer of the movie. Starring Ammy Virk, Guggu Gill, Tania, and Sidhika Sharma, the movie is a comedy entertainer with a twist of a love triangle.We know that everyone sacrifices their life to find their true love, even if they have to adopt any wrong, absurd or strange method for him/her, they do not leave any stone unturned. Similarly, in the movie 'Oye Makhna', Makhan (Ammy Virk) falls in love with Tania just by looking at her eyes.
