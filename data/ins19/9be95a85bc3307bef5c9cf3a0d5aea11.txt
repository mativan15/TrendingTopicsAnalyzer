Melody and her letter, disabled sister Elisa, dad Dan and mum Maiju (Image: Steve Reigate)Her heartfelt letter asks ministers to support her parents who pay sky-high energy rates to run the 11 devices nine-year-old Elisa relies on. The Bournemouth family have cut right back but Melody wrote: 'We don't have enough money. Mummy and Daddy are worried because everything is getting more expensive.'Elisa has cerebral palsy and needs around-the-clock care, so her medical equipment consumes a lot of power.
