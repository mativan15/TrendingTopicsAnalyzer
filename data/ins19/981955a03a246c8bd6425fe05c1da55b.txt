Preferred Bank: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

news
2022-10-19 20:10:10

Business

LOS ANGELES

Preferred Bank (PFBC) on Wednesday reported third-quarter earnings of $35.2 million.

The bank, based in Los Angeles, said it had earnings of $2.40 per share.

The results beat Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of four analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.12 per share.

The independent commercial bank posted revenue of $80.6 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $69 million, also exceeding Street forecasts.
