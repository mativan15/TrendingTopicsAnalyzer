October 19, 2022 12:16 PM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sol-Millennium Inc., a medical device company and innovator in medication delivery and specimen collection, announced it has received a new 510(k) clearance from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for its proprietary Sol-Guard® Safety Pull Button Blood Collection Set, the newest safety device in the company's rapidly expanding blood collection portfolio. The Sol-Guard® Safety Pull Button Blood Collection Set features spring-activated needle retraction technology, designed to minimize the risk of accidental needlesticks and exposure to blood-borne pathogens.
