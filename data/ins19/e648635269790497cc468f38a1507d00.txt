Samsung discussed working with Intel on semiconductor pact: report (NASDAQ:INTC)

JHVEPhoto/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Samsung (OTC:SSNLF) said on Tuesday that its Vice Chairman Lee Jae-yong recently met with Intel (NASDAQ:INTC) Chief Executive Pat Gelsinger on a number of issues, including a potential deal to produce more semiconductors, amid the global supply chain crunch, according to Nikkei Asia. The two leaders met on Monday at Samsung's (OTC:SSNLF) offices in Seoul, South Korea and discussed product diversification, next-generation semiconductors and strengthening the global supply chain.
