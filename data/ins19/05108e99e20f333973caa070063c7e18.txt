298 of 300 votes from Jharkhand polled in favour of Mallikarjun Kharge
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 08:15:00

RANCHI: The mood at the state Congress headquarters in Ranchi turned jubilant as party workers welcomed the appointment of the new national president of the party on Wednesday by beating 'dhol' and 'nagadas' and bursting crackers.Kharge was pitted against Shashi Tharoor for AICC president's post in this election that was held after over two decades to choose a person to helm the party from outside the Gandhi family. Voting was held on Monday and counting took place in Delhi on Wednesday. From Jharkhand, 300 out of 319 were polled.
