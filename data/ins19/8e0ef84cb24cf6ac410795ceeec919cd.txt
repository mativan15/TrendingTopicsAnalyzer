Published: 03:22 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 03:29 EDT, 16 October 2022 Justin Hemmes is expanding his empire - by buying a petrol station. The billionaire pub baron, 50, has paid $10.15 million for a 840sq m petrol station site next door to his Merivale venue, The Vic on the Park, in Sydney's Marrickville. Hemmes purchased The Vic on the Park in 2017 for $22 million and appears to be expanding the venue into the adjoining lot.  Justin Hemmes is expanding his empire - by buying a petrol station.
