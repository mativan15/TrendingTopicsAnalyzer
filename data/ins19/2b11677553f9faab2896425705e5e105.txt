Asda takes battle to rivals with convenience store launches

news
2022-10-18 09:02:43

Britain's third largest supermarket Asda is to enter the convenience store space setting a challenge to rivals Tesco, Sainsbury's and Morrisons.The stores will open under a new ‘Asda Express' brand in an attempt to reach local communities in Tottenham Hale in the capital and Sutton Coldfield in Birmingham. The Birmingham store will open on November 8 and London on 6 December in the run up to Christmas creating 37 jobs. The supermarket is currently in the process of acquiring 132 convenience stores from the Co-op Group.
