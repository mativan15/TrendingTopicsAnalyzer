Spirit Airlines shareholders approve JetBlue buyout
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 15:36:00

Spirit Airlines stockholders appear to have approved the acquisition from rival JetBlue Airways Corp. (Nasdaq: JBLU) in a deal that would create the nation's fifth-largest carrier.

On Wednesday, Miramar-based Spirit (NYSE: SAVE) said holders of more than 50% of the outstanding shares of its common stock have voted in favor of the $3.8 billion transaction. Should the agreement secure regulatory approvals, the deal would be expected to close by the first half of 2024.
