AMITYVILLE, N.Y., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- NAPCO Security Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: NSSC), one of the leading designers and manufacturers of high-tech electronic security devices, wireless recurring communication services for intrusion, fire alarm, access control and locking systems as well as a leading provider of school safety solutions today announced that management will be presenting at the Stifel 2022 Cross Sector Insight Conference in Boston, MA.
NAPCO management is scheduled to present at 9:45AM ET on Tuesday June 7, 2022.
