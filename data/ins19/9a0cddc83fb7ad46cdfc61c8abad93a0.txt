UNIVERSITY PARK, Pa. — Pennsylvania's role as a battleground state in the 2022 midterm elections is the focus of WPSU's 'Conversations Live' on Thursday, Oct. 20. 
'Conversations Live: 2022 Midterm Elections' will air at 8 p.m. on WPSU-TV and WPSU-FM, stream online at live.wpsu.org and be available on-demand on WPSU's YouTube channel and the PBS streaming app.   
Community members can ask questions during the show by calling 1-800-543-8242. Questions can be submitted ahead of time by emailing connect@wpsu.
