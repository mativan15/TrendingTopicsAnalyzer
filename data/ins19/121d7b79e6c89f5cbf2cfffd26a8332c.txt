Argyle Studio was initially designed as a temporary pop-up shop to showcase diverse Pittsburgh artists. After a year of building connections, however, the dynamic Forbes Avenue retail space seems to be here to stay. "The mission is to bridge, build and transform," said Christine Bethea, an award-winning mixed media artist and current president of Women of Visions, a collective of Black female artists in Pittsburgh founded in 1981.
