Glancy Prongay & Murray LLP Reminds Investors of Looming Deadline in the Class Action Lawsuit Against Opendoor Technologies Inc. (OPEN)

October 17, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Glancy Prongay & Murray LLP ('GPM') reminds investors of the upcoming December 6, 2022 deadline to file a lead plaintiff motion in the class action filed on behalf of Opendoor Technologies Inc. ('Opendoor' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: OPEN) investors who purchased: (a) securities between December 21, 2020 and September 16, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'); and/or (b) common stock pursuant and/or traceable to the Offering Documents issued in connection with the business combination between the Company and Opendoor Labs Inc.
