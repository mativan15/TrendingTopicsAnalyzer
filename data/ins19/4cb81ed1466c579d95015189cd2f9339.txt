Huang Evan/iStock via Getty Images Introduction Major U.S. retailers Walmart Inc. (NYSE:WMT) and Target Corp. (NYSE:TGT) reported their first quarter earnings on May 17 and May 18, 2022 respectively. Walmart's shares declined sharply due to profit margin pressures and now expected earnings per share (EPS) of -1% for the full year. The sell-off in the stock intensified as a result of Target's abysmal report. Management cited unexpectedly high costs as the main reason for profitability well below expectations.
