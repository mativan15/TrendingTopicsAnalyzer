JTL Construction sells its building in Garfield Heights
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-20 18:48:00

An industrial property currently home to JTL Construction Co. and car repair shop Rivian Service Center has been sold in Garfield Heights.

The buyer, 9601 Polar Star LLC, is a company registered to a post office box in Babbitt, Minnesota. Cuyahoga County records do not list a sale price for the transaction, but reveal Polar Star entered a $1.8 million mortgage on the property with Key Community Bank.

Ethan De Naray, a finance broker, is listed as a managing member of the 9601 Polar Star LLC in county records.
