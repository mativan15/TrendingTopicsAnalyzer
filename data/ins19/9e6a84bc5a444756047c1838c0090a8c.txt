Analysts see 15,900 to continue to pose as the immediate resistance. Shares of Bharat Forge Ltd. fell 0.22 per cent to Rs 787.1 in Friday's session as of 12:23PM (IST) even as the equity benchmark Sensex traded 324.29 points higher at 59527.19. Earlier in the day, the stock witnessed a gap up start to the session. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 848.0 and a 52-week low of Rs 595.85 on NSE. Around 13969 shares changed hands on the counter till 12:23PM (IST). The stock opened at Rs 792.
