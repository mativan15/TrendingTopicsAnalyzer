The wind and rain have passed, and the sun may be out.

But, in the aftermath of Hurricane Ian, it can be hard to figure out where to start and how to resume business as usual. Roll out the welcome mat and get your customers back faster. Here is a three-step plan to reconnect with your customers.

Why do you need a plan to reconnect with your customers? As someone who has seen my fair share of storms living along the Gulf Coast, I know that in the days following a storm, accurate information is hard to find.
