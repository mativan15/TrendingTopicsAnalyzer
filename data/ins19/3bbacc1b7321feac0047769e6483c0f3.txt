EVERETT, Wash., June 03, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Fortive Corporation ("Fortive") (NYSE: FTV) today announced that Senior Vice President and General Counsel, Peter C. Underwood, will be presenting at the Cowen Sustainability and Energy Transition Conference on Wednesday, June 8, 2022 at 11:10 a.m. ET. The audio will be simultaneously webcast and will be archived on www.fortive.com.
