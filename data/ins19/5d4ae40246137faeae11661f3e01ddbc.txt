Axis Bank shares hit fresh record high on stellar Q2 show
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 06:30:00

Shares of private sector lenderrallied 6.5% to Rs 879.95, its record high, in Friday's intraday trade on BSE after the bank reported a 70% year-on-year (YoY) growth in net profit to Rs 5,330 crore for the quarter ended September 2022.The net profit of the bank was above the ET Now poll of Rs 4,400 crore.The net interest income of Axis Bank grew by 31% on the year to Rs 10,360 crore. The strong growth in the bottom line was on the back of lower provisions.Provisions during the quarter dropped to Rs 550 crore from Rs 1,735 crore a year ago.
