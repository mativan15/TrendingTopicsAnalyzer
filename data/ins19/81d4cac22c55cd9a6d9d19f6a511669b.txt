Turbulent Markets Jolt Currency Hedge Funds From Decade-long Slumber

2022-10-18 06:12:37

A picture illustration shows U.S. 100-dollar bank notes taken in Tokyo August 2, 2011. 
Veteran hedge fund manager John Taylor describes one of his favourite Gary Larson cartoons, where one vulture sitting on an animal carcass says to another as more descend: "good friends flying in from all over...this is the best of times".There is, he says jokingly, a resemblance to the hedge funds currently circling foreign exchange markets, where a sudden rise in volatility offers to boost returns for the few specialist investors who survived the decade-long period of calm that forced many from the sector.
