The Champions League final is supposed to be European football's pinnacle: a global event. But did it feel like that? I'm not sure it did, at least not in the UK for the neutral football fan watching on TV.

No doubt it looked impressive, a massive full stadium always does, but the French authorities and UEFA once again showed they can't organise a big game. The scenes outside of the ground were, for a while, very troubling. Inevitably, they tried to cover up their errors by laying the blame on Liverpool fans, despite Merseyside police saying fan behaviour was exemplary.
