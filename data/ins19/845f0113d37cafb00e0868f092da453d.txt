Come Tour Secaucus' Haunted High School, Put On By Students
patch.com
news
2022-10-21 14:41:00

(Secaucus school district)SECAUCUS, NJ - As they do every year, Secaucus high school students will put on a haunted high school.The haunted high school will be 4-7 p.m. next Saturday, Oct. 29 and the theme this year is "Legends of Horror." Secaucus high school students will re-create some of the most beloved horror movies of all time, from Stephen King's "The Shining," to "Friday the 13th" and "Nightmare on Elm Street."It is $5 per person admission and $2 for re-entry. They will also have trick-or-treating tables set up outside the high school with candy, games and crafts for children and food available for sale.
