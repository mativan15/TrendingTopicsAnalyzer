By Yang Yang | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-05-30 17:45 A view of the central business district in Beijing on May 29, 2022. [Photo/VCG] International enterprises are maintaining a positive outlook on China's business environment, the CCTV app reported on Monday. The surveyed enterprises have generally high satisfaction with the country's macroeconomic and regulatory policies, according to a report on China's business environment in Q1 2022 by China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.
