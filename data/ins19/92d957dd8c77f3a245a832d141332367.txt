SAN FRANCISCO, CA / ACCESSWIRE / June 2, 2022 / Wikisoft Corp. (OTCQB:WSFT) today announced that along with the acquisition of its controlling shares by Emergency Response Technologies, a new Board of Directors and Executive Officers are being put in place with immediate effect. The company is exploring and taking on new growth opportunities which will involve a reorganization of the operating business and a change in focus.

WSFT anticipates that it will simultaneously implement the necessary changes and take on its first growth opportunity.
