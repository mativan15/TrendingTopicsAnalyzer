Benchmark indices continued their rally for the fifth day in a row on October 20 amid fag-end recovery in equities triggered by buying in IT counters. Bouncing back from its early fall, the 30-share BSE Sensex climbed 95.71 points or 0.16% to settle at 59,202.90. During the day, it hit a high of 59,273.85 and a low of 58,791.28. Similarly, the broader NSE Nifty ended 51.70 points or 0.30% higher at 17,563.95. From the Sensex pack, HCL Technologies, Tech Mahindra, NTPC, PowerGrid, Bajaj Finserv, Nestle, Bharti Airtel, Tata Consultancy Services and Infosys were among the winners.
