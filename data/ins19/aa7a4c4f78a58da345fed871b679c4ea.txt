Apple is reportedly slashing its iPhone 14 Plus production amid concerns of weak demand
Insider
business
2022-10-18 19:41:39

Apple is slashing production of its iPhone 14 Plus, according to a new report from The Information's Wayne Ma.The publication cited two people familiar with the company's supply chain. The tech company has had at least one manufacturer in China stop production of parts for the "Plus" smartphone model, one source told the publication. The individual said the company stopped production at the facility to better gauge demand for the product.Apple had just announced the new lineup of smartphones on September 7.
