Katy, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Katy, Texas - 24/7 Plumbing Co. LP's owner Trent Cooke is pleased to announce a milestone of thirty years in the industry. The Katy plumber professionals are trained extensively, licensed, and insured in order to ensure customers quality services and products. The team of professionals provide repairs, maintenance, and new installations. The company offers both commercial and residential customers the same high level of service in all areas of plumbing. 24/7 Plumbing Co.
