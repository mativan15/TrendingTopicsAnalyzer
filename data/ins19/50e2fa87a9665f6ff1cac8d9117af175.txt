Published: 10:49 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 10:51 EDT, 21 October 2022  Virginia Democratic Rep. Elaine Luria accused her Republican rival of wanting to kiss House Minority Leader McCarthy's 'feet, ring and every other thing' – in a slam her rival called a 'disgusting' attack.Luria, who won a GOP-leaning district in 2018, is in a tight race with her Republican rival, Jen Kiggans, a state senator.The two faced off in a debate in Smithfield, Va., where Luria, a member of the House January 6 committee, accused Republicans of wanting high gas prices as a political issue.
