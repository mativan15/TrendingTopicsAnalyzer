May 31, 2022 06:07 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Global Retail Data and Analytics leader, Intelligence Node, announced a strategic partnership with BARE International to expand current online data capabilities to include in-store collected pricing and assortment data and insights. With this partnership, Intelligence Node aims to be a partner of choice for retailers and brands looking for the most comprehensive, data-rich perspective of the consumer buying journey across in-store and online touchpoints.
