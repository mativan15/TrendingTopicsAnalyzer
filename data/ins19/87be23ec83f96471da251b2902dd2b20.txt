(Photo courtesy of Jollibee Foods) CLIFF VENZON, Nikkei staff writerOctober 21, 2022 12:17 JST | PhilippinesMANILA -- Jollibee Foods aims to double its business in the next five years by aggressively growing in the U.S. and China, the company's president and CEO told Nikkei Asia, as the Philippine fast-food group famous for its Chickenjoy and Jolly Spaghetti plots a post-pandemic expansion.
