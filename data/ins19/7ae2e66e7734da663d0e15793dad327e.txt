Fans of McDonald's will be able to get their hands on two new burgers, a new flavour of McFlurry, and two sides from today - but they won't all be sticking aroundMcDonald's fans will be able to tuck into new menu items from today (stock photo) If you were thinking about treating yourself to a cheeky McDonald's tonight, here's your sign to do so - as there's a whole host of brand-new menu items to try that make the perfect excuse for skipping your veggies for one day. From today, the fast-food giant is officially serving the new McCrispy burger - which marks the first time in 15 years a new chicken burger has been made a permanent addition to the menu - alongside four other delicious additions ranging from breakfast snacks to sweet treats.
