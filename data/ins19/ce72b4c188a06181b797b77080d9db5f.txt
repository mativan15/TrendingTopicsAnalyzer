Published: 06:34 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 06:34 EDT, 20 October 2022 Sydney's iconic Idlemere mansion - built in 1880 - is on the market for the first time in more than two decades, with an asking price of between $45million and $50million.But with the fate of the high end market next year unknown, the city's wealthiest property owners are on tenterhooks and the waterfront Lavender Bay property is carrying a lot on its shoulders.As the end of the year looms after a bumpy start to the spring selling season, Idlemere carries the hopes and expectations of the entire prestige property market, which is feeling the pinch with falling prices and a lack of availability.
