Tanvi is a well-known businesswoman who owns a boutique in Delhi. She has consumers all across the country, and she gets the majority of her business orders through an online platform. Her business has grown significantly in recent years. And in order to keep up, she plans to open a second store but in a different city. However, due to a liquidity crunch, she has been deferring her intentions for a long time. But recently, her friend told her about business loans for women. Tanvi is now interested to learn more about this product's features and application process.
