Most small firms fear long-term fallout from UK's cost of living crisis
theguardian.com
sport
2022-05-29 23:01:13

Three-quarters of small and medium-sized companies are worried about the long-term impact the cost of living crisis, soaring energy bills and rising inflation will have on their business, a survey has found.Just over half (51%) of SMEs said they were concerned that rocketing prices would dent consumer spending, in response to Barclays' SME Barometer, a quarterly survey of business sentiment conducted for the bank.Rising energy bills and higher raw material costs are putting pressure on businesses and creating a challenging trading environment.
