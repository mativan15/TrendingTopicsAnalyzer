Tornado Cash winds pick up: Coin Center sues OFAC over privacy concernsCoin Center – a crypto policy not-for-profit organisation – has brought proceedings against the US Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC). Coin Center claims that OFAC unlawfully overreached its authority when it criminalised interactions with Tornado Cash – a privacy enabling Ethereum coin mixing facility which had been used by bad actors.The action follows Coin Center's threat raised immediately after the ban that it would challenge the OFAC decision in Court.
