Eventbrite raises second quarter's guidance on subscribers' growth outlook (NYSE:EB)

finance
2022-06-02 12:48:25

Eventbrite (NYSE:EB) has announced the financial outlook raise for second quarter ahead of its investor and analyst day event on Thursday. Q2 2022 Updated Guidance: The company said it now expects second quarter's revenue to be in the range of $64-$66M, vs. prior outlook range of $60-$63M, vs. current consensus of $62.70M. The company also expects Adjusted EBITDA to be positive for the quarter. 'The world is coming back to in-person experiences, and Eventbrite is leading the way. We are channeling strong consumer demand toward the events on our platform, and many Eventbrite creators are seeing record success.
