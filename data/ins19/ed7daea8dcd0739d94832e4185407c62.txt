Image source, ShutterstockImage caption, Andrew Bailey said Bank officials would "not hesitate to raise interest rates to meet the inflation target"The governor of the Bank of England has warned interest rates may need to rise by more than previously expected.Speaking in Washington, Andrew Bailey said "inflationary pressures" meant a "stronger response" could be needed from the Bank than thought in August.The next rate rise decision is on 3 November, days after the government lays out its economic plans.
