Old Bag of Nails team working with Union Cafe, Axis Nightclub
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 15:34:00

Two longtime Central Ohio restaurant groups are teaming up.

The Old Bag of Nails is now working with the Union Café and Axis Nightclub. While the terms of the partnership have not been disclosed, representatives of the bar chain confirmed a new business relationship exists.

'The Old Bag of Nails Pub management team has joined together with the team at Union Café & Axis Nightclub in the Short North,' Old Bag of Nails said in a statement. 'We look forward to continuing to support their business models and expand the offerings of both units.
