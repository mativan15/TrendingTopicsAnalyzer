JioMart strategy is to stay away from deep discounting: Sources
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 19:45:00

AgenciesThis festive season, the assortment has already gone up from 30,000 products last year to 2.5 million products with around 16,000 third-party sellers, they said.Reliance Retail is not going to burn cash by indulging in deep online discounting to drive traffic and sales for its e-commerce platform JioMart which is being turned into a marketplace. The company wants to make the platform profitable for almost every single transaction, two persons aware of the plans said. The company is also going to onboard hundreds of thousands of small and medium businesses as sellers on JioMart and launch an incubation programme for direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands to help them scale up by providing marketing and distribution support with an aim to make JioMart the country's largest marketplace over the next couple of years, they said.
