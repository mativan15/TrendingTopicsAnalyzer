[Photo/VCG] BEIJING -- Cash dividends that Chinese listed firms planned to dish out continued to rise in 2021 as regulators urged listed firms to reward investors. China's A-share listed firms proposed cash dividends of over 1.5 trillion yuan ($225 billion), up 1.4 percent and 13.6 percent compared with actual dividends paid in 2020 and 2019, respectively, the China Association for Public Companies said Wednesday.
