ANN ARBOR, MI / ACCESSWIRE / June 2, 2022 / Zomedica Corp. (NYSE American:ZOM) ("Zomedica" or "Company"), a veterinary health company offering point-of-care diagnostics and therapeutic products for companion animals, announced that it has appointed Karen DeHaan-Fullerton as its General Counsel and Corporate Secretary. She joined the Zomedica team on May 31, 2022.

Ms. DeHaan-Fullerton joins the company with more than 20 years of corporate legal experience, including advising and leading companies through transformation and acquisition activities.
