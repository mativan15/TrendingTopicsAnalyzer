Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu Launches 'India-Qatar Start-Up Bridge'
ndtv.com
news
2022-06-05 18:33:57

Leading businessmen from both India and Qatar participated in the business forum.Doha: Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu on Sunday launched the India-Qatar Start-UP bridge, a joint initiative that aims to link the start-up ecosystems of the two countries and help their economies mutually.The joint initiative was launched as M Venkaiah Naidu addressed the members of the business community at the India-Qatar Business Forum here.The start-up initiative is likely to boost the business sectors mutually.
