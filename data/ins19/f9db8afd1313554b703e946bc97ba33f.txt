iStockGMDC's revenue from mining stood at Rs 484.75 crore, rising 21.33% from the same period last yearShares of state-owned minerals and lignite mining company Gujarat Mineral Development Corporation (GMDC) rallied 5.2% to Rs 148.7 after the company reported a consolidated net profit of Rs 151.22 crore in Q2 FY23, rising 273% from the quarter ended September 2021. The company's revenue from operations grew 19.26% to Rs 538.92 crore in the second quarter of the current fiscal year, as against Rs 451.
