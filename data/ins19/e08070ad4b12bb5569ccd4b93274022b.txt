Department stores like Macy's and Nordstrom are bucking gloomy retail trends, and it shows inflation hasn't slowed spending for a large swath of Americans
Insider
business
2022-05-27 11:30:00

Record inflation may be hitting some Americans' finances hard, but higher-income shoppers haven't slowed down — and it's giving some department stores the upper hand. Amid a dreary few weeks of retail earnings, Nordstrom and Macy's were a bright spot, reporting strong first-quarter earnings despite high inflation and fears of an impending recession . Nordstrom's first-quarter sales exceeded analyst expectations and the retailer boosted its full-year financial outlook; Macy's raised its profit outlook for the year after reporting first-quarter results that surpassed Wall Street estimates.
