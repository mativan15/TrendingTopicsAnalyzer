Monarch Casino & Resort Stock Climbs 16% After Record Quarterly Results
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 14:49:00

By Sabela Ojea Shares of Monarch Casino & Resort Inc. on Thursday climbed a day after the company reported a record earnings for the third quarter, marked by earnings and revenue growth. At 10:30 EDT, the shares are up 16% at $71.49. The hotel operator, whose stock recommendation was earlier on Thursday raised to buy from hold by Stifel, said net income rose to $27.5 million, or $1.41 a share, compared with $22.3 million, or $1.15 a share, a year earlier. The company's revenue increased 20% to $133.
