Upscale, Fast Casual Dining Powerhouse with House-Crafted Menu set to Open in Moreno Valley
MORENO VALLEY, Calif., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Capriotti's Sandwich Shop, known for its award-winning, hand-crafted cheesesteaks, turkey subs and more, will debut a new location in Moreno Valley at 12831 Moreno Beach Dr. on June 4. Capriotti's brings the Moreno Valley community its 45-year tradition of slow-roasting whole, all-natural turkeys in-house and hand-pulling them every morning and other favorites like the made-from-scratch meatballs using premium, fresh ingredients.
