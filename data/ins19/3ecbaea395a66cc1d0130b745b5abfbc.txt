Web3 Infra token Pocket get to be listed on global exchange KuCoin
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 12:45:00

Data relays and blockchain infrastructure provider Pocket Network said that its native token $POKT will be listed on decentralized exchange KuCoin. $POKT trading will be enabled on KuCoin on October 19th, with a trading competition also taking place through October 26, 2022. $POKT is also listed on a number of other global exchanges, including Huobi, Gate.io, Okcoin, MEXC, BTCEX, Btok, Bybit, ZB, Bitget, and Bitrue.

The utility token claims to enable a two-sided, permissionless marketplace where app developers can access blockchain data via Pocket Network by staking $POKT tokens.
