Country should be proud of Satyendar Jain, he should be awarded ‘Padma Vibhushan': Kejriwal
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-01 09:57:18

Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Wednesday said Satyendar Jain, arrested by the Enforcement Directorate, should be awarded the Padma Vibhushan for giving the ‘mohalla clinics' model that treats people free of cost.
Kejriwal defended Jain as a 'hardcore honest and patriot' person who was being framed in a 'false case' and hoped the minister will come out clean after the ED probe.
