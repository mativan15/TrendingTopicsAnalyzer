One of lutruwita/Tasmania's newest stages is playing host to its oldest stories. A Tasmanian Aboriginal dance company is bringing its stories to a theatre main stage for the first timeMembers have spent two decades working to reclaim traditional dance practices The work Tuylupa touches on stories that are 10,000 years old *pakana kanaplila is a Tasmanian Aboriginal traditional/contemporary dance troupe. The performers are bringing their latest work, Tuylupa, to the Theatre Royal Studio in nipaluna/Hobart.
