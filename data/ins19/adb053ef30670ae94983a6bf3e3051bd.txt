VistaJet boss Thomas Flohr: My luxury jets are boosting Britain

2022-10-15 23:01:00

Fancy eating your favourite Nobu dish on a private jet? Need your favourite brand of mineral water in-flight? Or perhaps you don't want to be disturbed by the cabin crew? All this is possible with VistaJet, the private-plane giant whose regulars include Taylor Swift, Victoria Beckham and footballer Paul Pogba — all of whom have been photographed climbing out of one of its silver and red aircraft.This is the world of the super rich — although VistaJet's founder and chairman, the Swiss tycoon Thomas Flohr, bristles at any suggestion that private air travel is largely the domain of celebrities.
