Some electric vehicles 'already 25 percent cheaper than diesel' despite charging concerns
businesstelegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 03:00:00

According to new data, almost half of fleet managers across Europe believe the trucking industry is the last road vehicle to join the electric transition. One of the main reasons for this is largely down to the challenges surrounding charging infrastructure in the UK and abroad.


The inefficiencies related to electric vehicle schemes introduced by local governments were found to be a source of this problem, with 41 percent of fleet managers demanding more accessible and affordable public charging.
