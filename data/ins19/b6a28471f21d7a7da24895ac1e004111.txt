October 17, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Zenyth Partners ('Zenyth'), a healthcare-focused private equity firm specializing in consumer-facing and tech-enabled healthcare services, announced today the successful completion of a transformative continuation fund transaction. The continuation fund is composed of both secondary and primary capital commitments, co-led by funds managed by BlackRock ('BlackRock Funds') and Manulife Investment Management with support from Newbury Partners, which funded the purchase of minority stakes in three portfolio companies (the 'Sale Portfolio') from Zenyth Partners, LP, a 2018 vintage permanent capital vehicle.
