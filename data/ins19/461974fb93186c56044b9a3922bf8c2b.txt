3 Painful Crypto Investment Lessons From The Merge
fool.com
news
2022-10-18 10:15:00

It may still be too early to tell, but The Merge has thus far been disappointing from an investment perspective. This technological upgrade from Ethereum (ETH 0.72%) was one of the most hyped events of the year, but the price of Ethereum has not responded as planned. The crypto is down nearly 65% for the year and an eye-popping 20% since The Merge took place on Sept. 15.
One major takeaway from The Merge is that the overall macroeconomic environment is going to far outweigh any news from a crypto, no matter how positive.
