Rates on these types of HELOCs just fell to their lowest level in 7 weeks
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 10:00:00

Getty Images/iStockphoto Home equity line of credit (HELOC) rates for loans with a 10-year repayment period remained at 4.74% for the second straight week, according to Bankrate data from the week ending May 31. Meanwhile, rates on HELOCs with a 20-year repayment period dipped to 5.57%, the lowest they've been in seven weeks. Though rates, in general, have been inching upwards, HELOCs remain one of the more affordable loan types for those who qualify (see the lowest rates you may qualify for here).
