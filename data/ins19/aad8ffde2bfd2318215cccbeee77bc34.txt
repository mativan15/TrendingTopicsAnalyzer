Insiders at Lockheed Martin Corporation (NYSE:LMT) sold US$13m worth of stock at an average price of US$433 a share over the past year, making the most of their investment. The company's market worth decreased by US$3.9b over the past week after the stock price dropped 3.6%, although insiders were able to minimize their losses

While we would never suggest that investors should base their decisions solely on what the directors of a company have been doing, logic dictates you should pay some attention to whether insiders are buying or selling shares.
