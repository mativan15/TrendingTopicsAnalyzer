Clever tyre pressure technique can help slash fuel consumption
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 07:01:00

New research has found that 28 percent of drivers surveyed only check their tyre pressure when the warning lights comes up on the dashboard. Around one in 10 motorists surveyed do not have a tyre pressure warning light in their vehicle.Esso, Kwik Fit and Michelin have teamed up to help drivers tackle the problem as part of Tyre Safety Month.Through the collaboration, Esso is offering UK drivers free tyre checks at 70 events this October.They want to raise awareness of the importance of correctly inflated tyres and help drivers increase their fuel efficiency.
