ETtechNASA last year selected the Musk-run SpaceX to continue development of the first commercial human lander that will safely carry the next two American astronauts to the lunar surface.US President Joe Biden has wished Elon Musk "lots of luck" for his trip to the Moon, as the Tesla CEO expressed "super bad feeling" about the economy and decided to cut its workforce by 10 per cent. When asked about Musk's pessimistic attitude toward the economy during a press briefing at the White House late Friday, Biden replied: "Lots of luck on his trip to the Moon.
