OHSAA football rankings
iheart.com
news
2022-10-19 12:52:00

Heading into the last Friday of the regular season for high school football, a number of area teams have clinched playoff berths. The OHSAA Tuesday released it's weekly computer rankings, the top 16 teams per region qualify for the post-season. In Division III, Chillicothe, Washington Court House, Miami Trace, and Jackson all are post-season bound. Hillsboro can get in with a win. In Division IV, Unioto has clinched a first round home game, Waverly needs a Friday win to get in, while Logan Elm has secured a berth.
