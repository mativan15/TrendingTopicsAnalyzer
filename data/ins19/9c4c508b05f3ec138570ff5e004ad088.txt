The Wrap On just the second day of testimony in the Danny Masterson rape trial, Judge Charlaine F. Olmeda called a brief recess barely an hour into the testimony of a witness who became overwhelmed with emotion as she detailed how she thought she was 'going to die' during an encounter with the 'That 70s show' star that included aggressive, violent behavior and gun play.Jane Doe No. 1, known for purposes of trial as 'Jb,' alleged she was raped on April 25, 2003, at the actor's Los Angeles home – the second time she says he assaulted her there.
