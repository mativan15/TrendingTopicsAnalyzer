Published: 05:40 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 05:55 EDT, 18 October 2022 ASDA is opening 30 new convenience stores, creating 500 new jobs, the supermarket announced today. The company plans to open its first two standalone convenience stores before Christmas, under a new 'Asda Express' brand.They will stock a range of hot and cold ‘food for now' options from Asda and partner brands including Leon, plus ‘food for later' choices such as ready meals and a selection of £10 meal deals for two people.
