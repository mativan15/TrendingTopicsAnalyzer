Britons resort to sharing baths to save money on energy bils
express.co.uk
finance
2022-10-21 06:01:00

The UK's inflation rate returned to a 40-year high of 10.1 percent in October despite a slight drop last month, according to latest figures from the Office for National Statistics (ONS).Those figures will come as little surprise to people who are feeling the pinch every day when it comes to rising food prices and soaring bills.Express.co.uk spoke to Bristol residents and asked them how the cost of living crisis is affecting them personally.One resident, 55-year-old Nigel Pedley, said his bills have risen by hundreds of pounds a month and his family have resorted to sharing baths.
