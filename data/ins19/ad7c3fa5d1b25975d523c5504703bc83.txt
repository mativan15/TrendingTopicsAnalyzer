June 02, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON & DETROIT--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Realtime Robotics, the leader in autonomous motion planning for industrial robots, today detailed its presence at the upcoming Automate 2022 event, taking place at the Huntington Place Convention Center in Detroit, MI, from June 6-9, 2022.

Realtime Robotics' innovative motion planning and collision avoidance software, RapidPlan, will be on display in demo cells located at Kawasaki Robotics Inc. booth #2332 and Mitsubishi Electric Automation booth #1023.
