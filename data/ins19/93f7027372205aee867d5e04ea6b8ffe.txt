Taylor Swift Midnights review: Clever, eclectic and addictive
brisbanetimes.com.au
news
2022-10-21 04:00:00

The atmospheric Snow on the Beach, featuring Lana Del Rey, bridges the worlds between electronica and Swift's more recent folk-driven sound.LoadingThe key track here, tapping into the same synth-pop aesthetic, is the infectious Anti-Hero, which is the frankest Swift has ever been, lyrically, about her insecurities and mental health struggles, explicitly naming depression and describing depersonalisation. There are some odd lyrics (bafflingly, 'sometimes I feel like everybody is a sexy baby'), but it's a plunge into a state of mind we've previously not been wholly privy to.
