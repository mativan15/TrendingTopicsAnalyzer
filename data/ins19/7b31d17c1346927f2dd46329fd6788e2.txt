June 02, 2022 08:34 AM Eastern Daylight Time GREENWICH, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Fieldpoint Private, the fast-growing private banking firm serving entrepreneurs, families, businesses and institutions, announced four additions to its client-facing team in the Atlanta, Orlando-Winter Park and New York City offices. The firm welcomed Tas Denman and Viviana Frias-Diaz as Managing Directors and Senior Private Bankers, Derek Grayson as Managing Director and Senior Commercial Banker and Robert Pirson as Managing Director and Commercial Banker.
