Awareness sessions on internet usage
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:30:00

Bhubaneswar: In order to tackle the challenges posed by cyber criminals for children, the commissionerate police has decided to launch a series of awareness sessions educating students on the responsible use of social media.Police personnel and cyber experts will visit educational institutions and educate students about cyber safety.'Cybercrime is a borderless and contactless offence where the scammers work clandestinely. We will sensitise school and college students about the responsible use of social media and other digital platforms,' commissioner of police Saumendra Priyadarshi said.
