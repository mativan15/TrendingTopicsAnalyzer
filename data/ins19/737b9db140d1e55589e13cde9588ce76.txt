AgenciesBansal, who spent 25 years at the Swedish telecom network company, was most recently its president for Europe and Latin America.Mumbai | New Delhi: Adani Airport Holdings has again rejigged its top management, naming Ericsson veteran Arun Bansal its chief executive, said several people close to the matter. Bansal, who spent 25 years at the Swedish telecom network company, was most recently its president for Europe and Latin America. 'The appointment will help strengthen digital transformation and the business growth agenda,' Adani Group chief HR officer Vikram Tandon wrote in an internal note, a copy of which ET has seen.
