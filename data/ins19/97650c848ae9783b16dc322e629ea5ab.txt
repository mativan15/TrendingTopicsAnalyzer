The NBA is back. After a busy offseason that saw plenty of drama (particularly in Los Angeles, Boston, Brooklyn and Utah), professional basketball action is heading off of Twitter and back onto the hardwood. The 2022-23 regular season is an 82-game marathon starting in October and running through the holidays and Christmas Day games, the All-Star Game in February and concluding next spring, when the NBA playoffs begin their own mini-marathon that ends in June with the NBA Finals.That's a lot of awesome basketball, and all of it is streaming live.
