Partial building collapse at Old School Warehouse
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-27 13:49:00

May 27—MONROEVILLE — An empty portion of the Old School Warehouse surplus store in Monroeville — the former Faulhaber bicycle seat factory — collapsed late Thursday night.

A middle unused section of the building, located at 21 N. Hamilton Street, caved in.

On its Facebook page, the store posted the following late Thursday night:

"Tonight we had a vacant unused part of the building collapse. Thankfully everyone is OK. We were planning on taking it down, but the recents rains beat us to it. Thanks to all the emergency personnel who quickly responded.
