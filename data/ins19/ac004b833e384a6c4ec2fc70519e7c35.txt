As we shiver through the first weekend of winter, it's the perfect time to cosy up with some hearty warm food. Juliet Speedy reports. Paul Howells is famous for his soups. The head chef and co-owner of Christchurch's Twenty Seven Steps also loves winter cooking. 'We spend a lot of time on our soups and braises, cassoulets and ragouts, it's a real labour of love for us. We're good at that winter cooking, we put a lot of heart and soul into it.' When I call his partner and restaurant co-owner Emma Mettrick and tell her I'm after a great winter dish, she replies: 'Paul's soups are incredible, over 15 years later they still blow me away.
