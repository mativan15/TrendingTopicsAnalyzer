Glasgow and Ulster matches postponed after stomach bug outbreak
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 15:00:00

Games involving Glasgow Warriors and Ulster, who were due to play South African opposition this weekend, have been postponed due to outbreaks of gastroenteritis in both camps.Glasgow were scheduled to take on the Lions in South Africa, while Ulster were set to face the Sharks in Durban. Glasgow played the Sharks last weekend while Ulster took on the Lions.Although information is limited, it is understood the two squads did not use the same hotel, although a common source of the illnesses still seems likely.
