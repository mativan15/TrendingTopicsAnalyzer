NEW YORK, Oct. 14, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Pomerantz LLP is investigating claims on behalf of investors of Block, Inc. ('Block' or the 'Company') (NYSE: SQ). Such investors are advised to contact Robert S. Willoughby at newaction@pomlaw.com or 888-476-6529, ext. 7980. The investigation concerns whether Block and certain of its officers and/or directors have engaged in securities fraud or other unlawful business practices.
