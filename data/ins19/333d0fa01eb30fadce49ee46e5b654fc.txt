SINGAPORE, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- XT.COM has listed Evolution Data (ELDT) in the Main Zone and the ELDT/USDT trading pair was opened to trading at 2022-October-19 7:00 (UTC).Users can deposit ELDT for trading at 2022-October-18 7:00(UTC)Withdrawals for ELDT will open at 2022-October-20 7:00 (UTC)About ELDTThe ELDT Token is a BEP-20 standardized token deployed on the Binance Smart Chain and plays an integral role as the key currency of the ELDT ecosystem. ELDT Tokens are used to pay participants and sponsors  who contribute to the development of data accumulation via surveys.
