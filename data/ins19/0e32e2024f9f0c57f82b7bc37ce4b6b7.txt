By Australian Associated Press Published: 12:36 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:36 EDT, 21 October 2022 Striker Adam Le Fondre is eager to share a slice of history with new teammate Robert Mak, who could become just the second man to score a goal in each of his first three A-League appearances for Sydney FC. Winger Mak scored Sydney's first brace of the season in the opening-round loss to Melbourne Victory and backed it up with a shot from outside the box in the defeat of reigning champions Western United last week.
