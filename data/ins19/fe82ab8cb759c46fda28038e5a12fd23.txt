Michael Shannon, Kiersey Clemons, Barbie Ferreira to Star in Drag Comedy ‘The Young King'
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-10-21 15:01:51

Michael Shannon is set to appear in drag comedy drama The Young King alongside Justice League‘s Kiersey Clemons and Euphoria breakout and Nope star Barbie Ferreira. The film, set in the drag king scene of '90s Las Vegas, will mark the feature directorial debut of Larin Sullivan (Anatomy: Soul, Dive), who also wrote the script, and has landed Justin Tranter (Happiest Season, A Star Is Born), the Grammy- and Golden Globe-nominated songwriter known for his work with Britney Spears, Lady Gaga, Justin Bieber and many others, as executive music producer.
