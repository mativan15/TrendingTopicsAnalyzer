Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, The 'Freedom Convoy' protests earlier this year gridlocked Ottawa's city centre with hundreds of heavy trucks.The mayor of Canada's national capital said officials failed to act fast enough to curb protests that had paralysed his city earlier this year. Jim Watson was testifying at a public inquiry looking into whether it was warranted for Canada to invoke emergency powers to end the protests.The so-called Freedom Convoy protests began at the end of January and gridlocked Ottawa for three weeks.
