Nora Fatehi is known for her swift moves and entertaining acts on the big screen. The actress often performs at packed shows and was scheduled to be a part of an event in Dhaka this week, but the same has been called off.The actress was to make an appearance at a women entrepreneur awards ceremony today, but the Ministry of Culture of Bangladesh cancelled the same to maintain the balance of foreign exchange reserves in the country. In its notice, the Ministry of Culture reportedly stated that in view of Bangladesh Bank's memorandum to maintain the foreign currency reserves in the country, Nora cannot be allowed to perform at the event which was hosted by Women Leadership Corporation.
