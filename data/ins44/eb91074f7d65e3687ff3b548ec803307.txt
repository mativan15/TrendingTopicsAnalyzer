By Brian Maass October 20, 2022 / 10:00 PM / CBS Colorado Peatinna Biggs, 46, a woman with developmental disabilities, is opening up about the Colorado sheriff who she says sexually molested her, and the $8.25 million federal jury verdict she won earlier this month.
