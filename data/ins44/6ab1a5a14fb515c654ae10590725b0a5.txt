REGINA, Saskatchewan, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Information Services Corporation (TSX:ISV) ('ISC' or the 'Company') announced today that it has acquired all of the issued and outstanding shares of Reamined Systems Inc. ('Reamined'), a recognized leader in providing property tax management infrastructure and services in the Province of Ontario, for $45.9 million, subject to customary purchase price adjustments. The purchase price is consistent with the Company's previous acquisition multiples and the business will become part of ISC's Registry Operations segment.
