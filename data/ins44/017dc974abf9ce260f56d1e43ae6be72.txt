Rhea Chakraborty danced with inmates and distributed sweets on her last day in jail, reveals lawyer-activist Sudha Bharadwaj
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-19 03:42:38

Rhea Chakraborty spent nearly a month in jail on drug-related charges after the Narcotics Control Bureau suspected a drug angle in her actor-boyfriend Sushant Singh Rajput's death case. Now, a human rights lawyer and activist Sudha Bharadwaj recalled the actress's time in jail and revealed she even grooved with her jail inmates on the last day. During a conversation with a portal, the lawyer said, 'Rhea took it very sportingly. When she left, she had some money remaining in her account, so for all the barracks she asked for sweets to be given, and everybody came down to wish her goodbye.
