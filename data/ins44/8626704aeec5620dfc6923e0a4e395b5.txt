[Photo/Huawei] According to a document that United States Federal Communications Commission Chairwoman Jessica Rosenworcel circulated to the three other commissioners last week, the FCC will ban approvals of new telecommunications equipment from China's Huawei Technologies and ZTE in the US on national security grounds. Without equipment authorizations from the FCC, the two companies will not be able to sell new equipment in the US.
