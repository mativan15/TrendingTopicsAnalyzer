Sparks hiring Sun's Curt Miller as coach, source confirms
espn.com
news
2022-10-21 18:22:00

2:22 PM ETComing off a WNBA Finals appearance, Curt Miller is leaving the Connecticut Sun and will be the next coach of the Los Angeles Sparks, a source confirmed to ESPN on Friday.Miller, a former Sparks assistant coach, had been with the Sun since 2016 and led them to the WNBA Finals in 2019 and 2022, when they lost to the Las Vegas Aces. Connecticut had made the playoffs the past six years of Miller's seven-year tenure.Miller's move was first reported by Just Women's Sports.Miller, 54, was a collegiate coach from 1991 to 2014, including as a head coach at Bowling Green and Indiana.
