Reality show Nannamma Superstar is all set to return to the small screen with a brand new season. The reality show, which features mother and child duo as a pair, is gearing up for its second season which will be premiering today (October 15) on a leading Kannada General Entertainment Channel (GEC).Interestingly, unlike the premiere season which featured celebrity mommy and kids, the upcoming season of Nannamma Superstar is opening its doors to commoners. The team has already finalised the contestants for the upcoming season of the reality show.
