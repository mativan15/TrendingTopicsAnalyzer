Sprinters reaping the benefits of changing distances has been a highlight of the 2022 athletics season.The USA's Fred Kerley famously moved down from 400m all the way to 100m and won gold on home soil at the World Athletics Championships in Oregon. The same can be said of Jamaica's 200m star Shericka Jackson who became the second-fastest women's 200m runner of all time at the Worlds, while still considering herself first and foremost a 400m specialist.However, when it comes to Team GB's Charlie Dobson - the 2018 U20 world 200m silver medallist - the talk on the circuit is all about him heading in the other direction.
