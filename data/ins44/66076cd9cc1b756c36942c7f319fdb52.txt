Actor Emma Watson has opened up about bonding with her fellow "Harry Potter" star Tom Felton. Emma Watson discussed their friendship ahead of Tom Felton's memoir hitting the stores. Emma Watson stated her relationship with Tom Felton did not start well, which people would also read in the book titled "Beyond the Wand: The Magic & Mayhem of Growing Up a Wizard." Emma Watson, now 32, said that despite the rocky beginning, Emma Watson and 35-year-old Tom Felton have grown to be dear friends and 'soulmates'.
