Former F1 champion Jenson Button blames McLaren boss for Ricciardo's performance
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 13:07:00

In terms of performance, the world would agree that Daniel Ricciardo is undergoing a challenging period. His second season with McLaren has not gone well so far. In this nightmare situation of Ricciardo, the former world champion Jenson Button has accused his boss of doing even more damage. Daniel Ricciardo became the Aussie with 32 podium finishes over 11 years by winning eight races in his career. Unfortunately, such an impressive record of the 32-year-old F1 driver would no longer be spared by the critics if he won't turn his fortunes around.
