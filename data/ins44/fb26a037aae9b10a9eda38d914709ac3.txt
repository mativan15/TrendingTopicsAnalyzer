Advaith India Foundation launches services in J'khand
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 04:30:00

Ranchi: Bangalore-based not for profit organisation, Advaith India Foundation, will start its services in Jharkhand.To begin with, it will distribute books of general awareness and other competitive exams to students of classes VIII to XII of government school on BIT Mesra campus.President of the foundation, Pushpalatha MS, said in a press conference held here on Wednesday that books would be distributed to the students on Friday.The foundation will also visit Telengadih, a Biroh colony of Tamar, on Friday to promote the importance of skill development programmes which will help candidates secure a job.
