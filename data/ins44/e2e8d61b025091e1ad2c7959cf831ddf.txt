Will Bring Big Positive Change: Prime Minister After Amit Shah Releases Hindi MBBS Textbooks In Madhya Pradesh
ndtv.com
economics
2022-10-17 04:59:07

New Delhi: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday hailed the beginning of the initiative of the Madhya Pradesh government to impart medical education in Hindi and said this will bring a very big positive change in the country. Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Sunday released textbooks in Hindi of three subjects for MBBS students as part of an ambitious project of the Madhya Pradesh government to impart medical education in that language. Terming it an important day in history, Amit Shah said Madhya Pradesh has become the first state in the country to start the MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) course in Hindi.
