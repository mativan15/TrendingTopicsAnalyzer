Cruise passengers warn guests which ship decks to avoid
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 03:01:00

On a cruise ship, passengers can opt for a luxury suite or save money with a cheaper interior cabin. But guests will also need to think about which deck they'd like to stay on.A new cruise passenger asked experienced cruisers which decks they might want to avoid due to noise.A guest said: 'Cabins below an open deck. The crew started washing down and then dragging furniture around at about 5am on one cruise we were on.'Open cruise decks are decks where facilities or activities are such as the swimming pool, slide or bars.
