The Wrap Michelle Monaghan has signed on to co-star alongside Mark Wahlberg in Apple and Skydance's new action-comedy film 'The Family Plan.'Wahlberg is set to produce through his Municipal Pictures banner, with Simon Cellan Jones directing from a script by David Coggeshall. The film follows a suburban father who goes on the run with his family when his past catches up to him.'The Family Plan' is also produced by Municipal Pictures' Stephen Levinson, along with Skydance's David Ellison, Dana Goldberg, and Don Granger.
