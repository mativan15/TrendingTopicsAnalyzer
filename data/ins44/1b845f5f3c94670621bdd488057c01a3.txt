On Friday's Tipping Point, ITV host Ben Shephard welcomed three new contestants onto the show. Simon, Gloria and Noah had to compete against each other as they tried to knock as many counters down into the machine as they could. The player with the most money in their bank would then go on to the final in the hopes of winning the £10,000 jackpot.In the first round, Simon stormed into the lead after he won £700 in his bank.Gloria was close behind him with £200, while Noah was in last place with just £100.
