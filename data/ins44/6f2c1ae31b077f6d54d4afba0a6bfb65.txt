Getty ImagesDrones have the potential to be at the centre of a technology-led transformation of Indian agricultureDrones, or the unnamed aerial vehicles, are increasingly being used by various sectors ranging from personal use to war purposes, but they can also add to economic output of a country. Drones have the potential to be at the center of a technology-led transformation of Indian agriculture and boost the country's gross domestic product by 1-1.5 percent while adding at least five lakh jobs in the coming years, the World Economic Forum said in a report.
