Published: 07:59 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 08:13 EDT, 30 May 2022 Prince Charles goddaughter India Hicks has shared photos of her second charity visit to help Ukrainians whose lives have been devastated by the war. India , 54, who lives on Harbour Island in the Bahamas with her husband David Flint Wood and their five children , told her 325,000 followers last week that she had travelled to the country to help communities impacted by Russian attacks. It comes two months after she travelled to Poland to help Ukrainian refugees.
