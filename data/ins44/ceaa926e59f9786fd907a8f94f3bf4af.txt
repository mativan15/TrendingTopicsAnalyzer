Deutsche Bank, DWS Raided Over Allegations of Greenwashing
bqprime.com

2022-05-31 13:33:01

(Bloomberg) -- Deutsche Bank AG and its asset management unit had their Frankfurt offices raided by police, adding to the legal headaches facing Germany's largest lender.Law enforcement officials on Tuesday morning entered the twin towers where Germany's largest lender is headquartered, as well as the nearby premises of DWS Group, according to a statement from the prosecutor that confirmed an earlier Bloomberg report. The search is related to accusations of greenwashing against the asset manager.
