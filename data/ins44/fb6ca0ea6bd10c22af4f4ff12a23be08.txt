National Bank of Rwanda grants Virtual Pay authorisation to operate as a Payment Service Provider (PSP).
businessinsider.com
news
2022-06-03 08:47:08

The green light to operate as a PSP in Rwanda will offer the company's clients and merchants an opportunity to have greater convenience and safety in their transactions, as they keep in step with the increasingly digital shift being experienced across the globe. About Virtual Pay International Virtual Pay International Limited (Virtual Pay) is a leading Pan-African Payment Service Provider renowned for bespoke payment solutions that serve a global clientele. Launched in 2019, Virtual Pay supports businesses to simply, securely and seamlessly receive cashless payments from their clients through cards and mobile money.
