ET Canada Jada Pinkett Smith and Sheree Zampino, Will Smith's ex-wife, talk it out on the latest episode of 'Red Table Talk'.The pair are joined by Jada's mother Adrienne Banfield-Norris as they discuss how things got off to a bit of a tricky start with them, given that Will only divorced Sheree two years before he and Jada tied the knot.Sheree was married to the actor from 1992 to 1995 and they share son Trey, 29, together.Jada recalls how Sheree once came into her and Will's master bedroom without asking.
