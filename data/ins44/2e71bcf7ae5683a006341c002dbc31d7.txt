Tesla Fair Fund distributions established to pay 'harmed' investors
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 14:30:50

The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission said Friday that distributions of the Tesla Fair Fund has been established, to be paid out to "harmed" Tesla Inc. TSLA, -7.45% investors. The Fund was established after Tesla and Chief Executive Elon Musk agreed to pay $40 million to settle an SEC suit alleging that Musk's tweet that he had "funding secured" to take Tesla private was false and misleading.
