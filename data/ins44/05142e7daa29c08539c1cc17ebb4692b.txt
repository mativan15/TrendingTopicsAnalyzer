Lincoln Electric Increases Qtr Dividend to 64c/Shr
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 20:37:00

By Denny Jacob Lincoln Electric Holdings Inc. on Tuesday said the board raised its quarterly cash dividend about 14% to 64 cents a share from 56 cents. The manufacturer said the dividend is payable on Jan. 13, 2023 to shareholders of record at the close of business on Dec. 31. "Our dividend increase reflects the strong execution of our Higher Standard 2025 Strategy and our confidence in the strength of future cash flow generation through the cycle," said Chief Executive Christopher Mapes.
