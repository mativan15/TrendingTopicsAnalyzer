(CBC)CBC Vancouver's Gloria Macarenko returns as host of the 65 Roses Gala, an annual event in support of Cystic Fibrosis Canada, November 19 at the Fairmont Waterfront Hotel. Cystic fibrosis is one of the most common genetic diseases affecting young Canadians, and for more than 60 years, Cystic Fibrosis Canada has been helping Canadians with cystic fibrosis by funding research, advocacy and clinical care. The intimate black tie event will bring together supporters of the Canadian cystic fibrosis community including prominent philanthropists, corporate leaders and entrepreneurs to raise funds to support the organization's work and mission – to find a cure for the disease.
