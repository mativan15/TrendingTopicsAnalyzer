Mutual fund houses are busy launching Fixed Maturity Plans or FMPs. Around 25 new FMPs have been launched by mutual fund houses in this year so far. These 25 FMPs have garnered Rs 6.634 crore, almost double from 2021. Three new FMPs are open in the market at the moment: ABSL FTP-Sr TY (76 D), Invesco India FMP Sr 35-Plan C, Invesco India FMP Sr 35-Plan D.
