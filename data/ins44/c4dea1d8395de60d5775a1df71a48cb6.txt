BKU holds rally against govt direction to not use tractors for ferrying passengers
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-17 12:44:25

MEERUT: To oppose the state government's direction against using tractor trolleys for ferrying passengers, hundreds of farmers took out a tractor rally from the Bharatiya Kisan Union (BKU) headquarters in Muzaffarnagar's Sisauli to the collectorate in Shamli.The rally, held on Monday, was led by BKU leader Rakesh Tikait, who asked farmers to fight against the government with 'Tractor and Twitter'. He said, 'Tractor is a multipurpose mode of transport in the rural belt. It is the backbone of farming and rural life and a symbol of our pride.
