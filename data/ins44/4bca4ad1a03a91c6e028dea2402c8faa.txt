34 Montgomery-area softball players named to ASWA All-State teams
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-05 10:01:15

Thirty-four Montgomery-area softball players were named All-State in their respective classifications by the Alabama Sports Writers Association, with nine earning spots on the first team. The teams were released Sunday.

Brewbaker Tech's Taniyah Brown; Hooper Academy's Kaylee Midkiff and Victoria Hagemann; Lowndes Academy's Ava Casey and Haley Briggs; Prattville Christian's Sarah Williams; Tallassee's Belle Haynes and Chloe Baynes; and Wetumpka's Mya Holt are first-team selections.

River Region second-teamers are: Edgewood Academy's Anna Guillot, Lily Stubbs and Lindsey Brown; Elmore County's Anna Catherine Segrest and Aubrey Allen; Hooper Academy's Mary Frances Bressler; LAMP's Caitlin Russell; Macon-East's Jamya Powell and Kylee Smith; Montgomery Catholic's Auburn Wilcoxson; Prattville Christian's Alivia Messick; Tallassee's Avary Lumpkin; and Wetumpka's Ashlynn Campbell.
