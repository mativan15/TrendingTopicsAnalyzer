The Yankees and the Cleveland Guardians will finally get a chance to face off in Game 5 on Tuesday afternoon after their first attempt at the game was rained out on Monday night. A trip to the American League Championship Series is on the line, with the winner of Tuesday's game heading straight to Houston to face the Astros on Wednesday.
