Completed renovations will enable residents to enjoy beautiful, affordable homes for years to come

Partnerships with over a dozen community organizations provide services to residents

GAINESVILLE, Fla., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Fairstead, a purpose-driven, vertically integrated real estate company committed to sustainable development and the creation and preservation of high-quality housing, and officials from Alachua County today celebrated the completion of renovations of Sweetwater Square in Gainesville, Florida with a Community Day party for residents and ribbon cutting.
