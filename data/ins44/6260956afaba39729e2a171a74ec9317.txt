Drowning people, contrary to popular belief, do not kick and flail as they go under.

They are too busy trying to survive to cry out for help.

This was evidenced by waterlogged British Prime Minister Liz Truss, who somehow rose to her two feet in the House of Commons on Wednesday and declared: 'I am a fighter and not a quitter.'

Less than two months after being elected to replace Boris Johnson as leader of Britain's Conservative Party, Truss is in grave danger of going down in a wave of popular and political discontent.
