Getty ImagesNifty moved in a tight range of 82 points, and ended slightly below the psychologically crucial level of 15,700. NEW DELHI: The Nifty IT index closed on a positive note on Friday. Shares of Infosys(up 0.91 per cent), Mindtree(up 0.68 per cent), Tata Consultancy(up 0.5 per cent), HCL Technologies(up 0.32 per cent) and Wipro(up 0.28 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack. On the other hand, Coforge(down 2.08 per cent), L&T Technology(down 0.97 per cent) and MPhasis(down 0.64 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.
