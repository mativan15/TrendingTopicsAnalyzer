This food festival is sure to excite your taste buds as well as highlight local chefs and live entertainersHOUSTON, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Houston's iconic fried chicken showdown, Fried Chicken Fest Houston is back for a 3rd year of fun, food, and good vibes on November 05, 2022, from 2:00 PM - 8:00 PM (VIP ticket entry at 1:00 PM). This event will invite members of the surrounding community to gather for a day of foodie fun at IAG Icehouse located at 6767 Portwest Drive, Houston, TX 77024 where they will try dishes from Houston favorites like Max's Wine Dive, Stuff'd Wings, and the World-Famous Turkey Leg Hut Food Truck.
