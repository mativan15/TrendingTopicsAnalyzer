Sathyaraj or as most millennials and movie buffs refer to him, Kattappa from Baahubali has an astonishing story of achieving his dreams in the film industry. Believe it or not, he never wanted to be an actor, he was just content with working for films. But as destiny would have it, the acting talent was noticed and Sathyaraj's hard work and luck paid off in equal measure. He made a mark in Tamil films playing a villain's henchman, slowly graduating to the main antagonist and then even making an impact as the leading man.
