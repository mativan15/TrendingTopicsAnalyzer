How do you picture an entrepreneur? In a recent survey by Cox Business, small business owners listed their top three entrepreneurial idols as Steve Jobs, Ben Franklin and Walt Disney DIS . But another part of that survey may be more revealing. Roughly two-thirds of the respondents said the desire to be their own boss motivated their entrepreneurial pursuits. This sense of autonomy has come to embody the rugged individualist persona often associated with the entrepreneurial spirit. 'Following a process that does not make sense to me has a strong tendency to undermine my motivation,' says Thibaud Clement, CEO and Co-founder of Loomly in Los Angeles.
