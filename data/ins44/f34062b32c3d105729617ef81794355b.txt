Contingency plan: Planning for the unexpected and ensuring business continuity
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-28 07:45:50

All things are only transitory. The Covid-19 pandemic has emphasised the fact that life is uncertain. While families were grappling with the sudden illness or loss of loved ones, it also created a big vacuum and put tremendous pressure on businesses.
It emerged from a survey held in March 2021 that family business owners didn't have contingency plans in place to deal with an unexpected event.
The sudden demise of the founder or identifying a successor who does not have the vision, experience or the skillset to drive the business etc.
