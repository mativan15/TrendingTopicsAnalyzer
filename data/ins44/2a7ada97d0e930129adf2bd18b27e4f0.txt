VANCOUVER, British Columbia, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Liquid Media Group Ltd. (the 'Company', 'Liquid Media' or 'Liquid') (Nasdaq: YVR) announces financial results from its third quarter for fiscal year 2022 ended August 31, 2022. Financial highlights from the quarter and corporate highlights to date include: Revenues of $1,533,893 for Q3 2022, compared to $5,365 in the same period in the prior year (increase of 28,491%)Gross profit for Q3 2022 increased to $512,909, from $496,618 for the prior quarter (increase of 3%) and gross loss of $46,562 for Q3 2021Operating expenses for the three-month period were $2.
