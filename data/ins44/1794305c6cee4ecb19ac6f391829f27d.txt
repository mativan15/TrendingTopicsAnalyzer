NEW YORK , June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Datadog, Inc. (NASDAQ: DDOG), the monitoring and security platform for cloud applications, today announced the appointment of Citigroup's (NYSE: C) Titi Cole to its Board of Directors. Cole is Citigroup's CEO of Legacy Franchises, overseeing the bank's consumer business in Asia, Europe, Middle East, Africa and Mexico. She is a member of Citigroup's Executive Management Team and also serves as a Member of the Board of Grupo Financiero Citibanamex, S.A. de C.
