Wood Group to Sell Built Environment Business to WSP for $1.9 Bln -- Update
einnews.com
world
2022-06-01 07:54:00

By Jaime Llinares Taboada John Wood Group PLC said Wednesday that it has agreed to sell its Built Environment consulting business to Canadian company WSP Global Inc. for $1.9 billion. The U.K. engineering and consulting business said the sale delivers significant value for its shareholders and enhances its financial flexibility. The primary and immediate use of the money will be to reduce net debt and strengthen its balance sheet, the company said. Wood had net debt of $1.4 billion as of Dec. 31.
