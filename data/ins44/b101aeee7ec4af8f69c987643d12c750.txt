Posted on May 28, 2022 by Zara Ali By Jessica Buxbaum Source OCCUPIED EAST JERUSALEM — On April 29, Inès Abdel Razek woke up to 80 new Twitter followers. 'These accounts were following the exact same people that were tweeting about Palestine, but from France or Francophone accounts that work on Palestine,' Razek said of her new followers. The advocacy director of Rābet, the Palestine Institute for Public Diplomacy's digital platform, became wary of the issue after Abier Khateb, a grants manager at Open Society Foundations, reported mass followings as well.
