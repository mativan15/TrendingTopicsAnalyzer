New Delhi: Shares of zoomed as much as 12 per cent in early trade on Monday after the company's demerger scheme was approved by the Chandigarh bench of the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT). According to the regulatory filing by the company, as many as seven transferor companies would be amalgamated into a single transferee company, Kamdhenu and the paint business would be demerged. Kamdhenu Colour and Coatings Limited will be listed separately as the second resulting company, the building material maker's regulatory filing added.
