REUTERS/Dado Ruvic/Illustration/File PhotoGalp Energia SGPS SALISBON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Portugal's oil and gas company Galp Energia said on Monday it may face additional sourcing disruptions after Nigeria LNG declared force majeure due to a widespread flooding.Galp Energia said in a statement it had received a notice from Nigeria LNG (NLNG), its main natural gas supplier, about the force majeure but said "no information was provided to support an assessment of potential impacts".
