Tulip Infratech, a Gurgaon-based real estate developer, will invest Rs 3,000 crore to revive a stuck project and develop about six million sq ft of residential and commercial area on the prime Golf Course road on the land it has acquired from . The project will help about 200 homebuyers, who were stuck since 2004. The lenders, that have taken over the 19-acre land from Vipul due to default on payments, restructured Rs 412 crore debt and transferred the project to Tulip Infratech. 
ET had reported about the transaction in March 2021.
