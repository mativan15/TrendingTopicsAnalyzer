As the market pulls back in fits and starts, a normal pattern in a bear market, investors should have a wish list of companies that they want to buy at lower prices. Three good dividend names to include on that list today are real estate investment trust (REIT) giant Realty Income (O 0.28%), integrated energy powerhouse TotalEnergies (TTE 0.64%), and U.S. utility Dominion Energy (D 1.74%). Here's a quick look at why each is worth looking at right now.
1. Big, reliable, and high yielding
REIT Realty Income owns net-lease properties, which means the occupants of its single-tenant buildings are responsible for most of the operating costs of the assets they occupy.
