CBS' hit courtroom drama Bull came to an end on Thursday after premiering the final episode of its sixth and final season. Ahead of its release, star Michael Weatherly took to Twitter to say goodbye to fans after 22 years on TV, as he hinted at an NCIS reunion.After premiering in 2016, Bull became a huge success for CBS as fans were excited to see Weatherly in a legal drama, following his departure from NCIS.The actor portrayed Dr Jason Bull, a psychologist and trial science expert.Alongside his team at his consulting firm, they would help their clients choose the right jurors as well as the best argument to ensure a courtroom victory.
