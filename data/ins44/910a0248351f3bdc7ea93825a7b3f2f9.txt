Oyo adds over 1,250 new corporate clients since March
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 10:30:00

New Delhi: Travel tech firm Oyo on Wednesday said it has added more than 1,250 corporate clients in the last three months, indicating that business travel in India may be rebounding post lifting of COVID-induced travel restrictions. The recovery is led by small and medium enterprises, traditional business houses and conglomerates, startups, travel management companies and film production houses, which have emerged as a key customer category for Oyo in recent times. The list of top cities searched and booked by corporates include Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Kolkata, Pune and Delhi, Oyo stated.
