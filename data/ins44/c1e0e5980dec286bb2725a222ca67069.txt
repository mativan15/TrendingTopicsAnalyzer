EASTENDERS star, Stacey Slater will be rushed to hospital in upcoming episodes after collapsing with suspected sepsis. 
The news comes after fans witnessed Jean push Stacey into a bar causing her to injure her leg.
Stacey refused to get treatment for the cut on her leg as she feared it would get Jean into trouble. 
However, as the days go on the wound becomes infected and she ends up collapsing and being rushed to hospital.
Kheerat goes with her and is shocked to hear from the doctors that Stacey is seriously ill and may have sepsis.
