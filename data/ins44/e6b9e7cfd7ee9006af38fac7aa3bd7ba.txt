Milan secured the win in the 81st minute when Rebic found Tonali alone in the box with a perfect ball across and he had no trouble finishing past Montipo.
Sandro Tonali's late goal sealed a 2-1 win for Italian champion AC Milan at Hellas Verona in Serie A on Sunday.The visitors went ahead after nine minutes when Verona's Miguel Veloso deflected Rafael Leao's cross into his own net.
