I didn't vote in 2016 election. Now I'm hoping young liberal voters don't make my mistake.
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-20 10:00:23

opinionI keep hearing, and polling suggests, that young voters are expected to skip the midterm elections on Nov. 8. But if young Democratic, progressive and independent voters worry about reproductive health care, student debt relief, the climate crisis and the economy, they should find their way to a ballot before all of that gets targeted by Republicans.And for all those reasons and more – I'm optimistic young people will show up to vote. I offer a hard-earned lesson about why voting is so important, even when you don't  agree with the choices on the ballot.
