The 'Rust' settlement that will allow production to resume on the Spaghetti Western Alec Baldwin-led movie, has triggered complicated feelings among the film's crew.Some members of the crew have said they would not return to the project, but others have supported the decision.The producers announced recently that they plan to resume production in January, more than a year after Alec Baldwin shot and killed cinematographer Halyna Hutchins while preparing for a scene at a location near Santa Fe, New Mexico.
