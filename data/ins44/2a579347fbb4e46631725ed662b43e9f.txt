(EAR)By: Anuron Mitra, SA News Editor Diego Thomazini/iStock via Getty Images Shares of Eargo (NASDAQ:EAR) added 8.5% to $1.40 in Tuesday morning trade, after the hearing aids maker said it will continue to be listed on Nasdaq after becoming up-to-date with all its pending filing obligations with the U.S. SEC. EAR said on May 24 it filed its quarterly report in a 10-Q filing for the March quarter 2022, which meant the company was no longer delinquent in its filings.
