Early monsoon brings hopes of cooling food inflation
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 01:30:00

APAdvance of the southwest monsoon over the Indian mainland is marked by its onset over Kerala, beginning the four-month rainy season The southwest monsoon arrived in Kerala on Saturday, three days earlier than normal, the India Meteorological Department (IMD) said, raising expectations that good rain this year will temper food inflation. Rain will also provide relief from the intense summer heat that has hit the wheat crop and triggered a power crisis as temperatures jumped to record highs in north India in March and April.
