HUNTINGDON, Pa. - June 1, 2022 - ( Newswire.com )W.D. Strategies, LLC, a Pennsylvania-based business consultancy and Wix Partner, will present their experience utilizing Wix's Branded App technology during a live webinar for other Partners. The webinar will take place on June 7, 11 a.m. EDT on YouTube. Joey DiGangi, W.D.'s COO and co-founder, will share client success stories and best practices with the community.The Branded App is a toolkit introduced by Wix in July 2021 that enables Wix website owners and developers to create native apps for Apple's App Store and Google Play.
