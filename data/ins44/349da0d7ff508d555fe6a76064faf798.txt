October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TYSONS, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Maximus (NYSE: MMS), a leading provider of government services worldwide, will issue a press release with its financial results for the three months and fiscal year ended September 30, 2022, after market close on Monday, November 21, and will host a conference call the following day at 9:00 a.m. ET on Tuesday, November 22.

Shareholders are invited to submit questions for management's consideration by emailing IR@maximus.
