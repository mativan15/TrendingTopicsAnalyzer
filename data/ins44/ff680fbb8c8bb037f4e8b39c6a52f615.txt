Leading telecom operator Reliance Jio has announced three new JioFi postpaid plans and its 4G JioFi wireless hotspot. Priced at Rs.249, Rs.299 and Rs.349, you can pick these plans when buying the JioFi wireless hotspot device. The JioFi device is a wireless dongle that you can connect anywhere to get 4G connectivity. A portable WiFi device, the JioFi dongle is available free of cost under the offer. However, it is offered on a returnable basis, which means that you would have to return the device when no longer needed.
