Ambitious plan to create 3,000 homes announced in Nunavut
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-18 16:30:00

Lorne Kusugak is the MLA for Rankin Inlet South, and serves as Nunavut's minister of housing. Two organizations have announced a plan to make 3,000 homes in the territory by 2030. Kusugak says he's 'humbled' by how quickly a plan for more housing has come together. (Jacqueline McKay/CBC)An ambitious housing plan for Nunavut was laid out on Tuesday.Two organizations — the government-operated Nunavut Housing Corporation (NHC) and Inuit-owned NCC Development Limited (NCCD) — announced a plan to make 3,000 homes by 2030.
