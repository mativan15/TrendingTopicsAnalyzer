By Lin Shujuan in Shanghai | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-19 15:58 Share - WeChat Zhao Jiaming, member of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai Municipal Party Committee and head of the publicity department, speaks during a news conference, Oct 18, 2022. [Photo/Xinhua] Shanghai has always adhered to a people-centric approach in its quest to become "a city for the people" as it is aligned with General Secretary Xi Jinping's call for cities to be built by the people and for the people, said Zhao Jiaming, spokesman of the Shanghai delegation, to the ongoing 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, at a news conference on Tuesday.
