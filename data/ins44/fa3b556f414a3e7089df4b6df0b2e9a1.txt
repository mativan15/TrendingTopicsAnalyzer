Co-CEO at Fluree, the scalable semantic graph database backed by blockchain technology. getty Some changes happen suddenly. Rumors and gossip pop up with head-scratching speed. Lottery wins, fires, floods and the latest cryptocurrency trend catch us unaware. Other times, the world shifts in small ways, one thing after another. A few years later, reality looks so different that we can hardly imagine the 'before.' Like Web 2.0 before it, Web3 is creating small shifts that, in the end, will define a new kind of reality.
