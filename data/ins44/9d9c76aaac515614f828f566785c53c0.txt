The move comes after a report said up to 30 former pilots had gone to train members of the Chinese army.The British government is taking measures to prevent China from recruiting serving and former Armed Forces pilots to train army personnel in China, according to the UK Ministry of Defence.
'We are taking decisive steps to stop Chinese recruitment schemes attempting to headhunt serving and former UK Armed Forces pilots to train People's Liberation Army personnel in the People's Republic of China,' a spokesperson said in a statement on Tuesday.
