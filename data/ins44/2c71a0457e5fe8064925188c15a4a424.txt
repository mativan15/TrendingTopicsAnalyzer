Getty ImagesIndia 10-year bond yield rose 0.05 per cent to 6.03 after trading in 6.02-6.04 range.Shares of Bharat Forge Ltd. gained 1.48 per cent to Rs 758.65 in Monday's trade. It hit a high of Rs 758.8 and low of Rs 743.0 during the session. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 705.0, while the 50-DMA was at Rs 746.23. If a stock trades above 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is upward. On the other hand, if the stock trades below 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it is considered a bearish trend and if trades between these averages, then it suggests the stock can go either way.
