Premier League fan Aaron Judge brought the badge kiss to baseball
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-19 10:53:55

Aaron Judge has transported the badge kiss celebration from soccer to baseball.
After hitting a key home run for the New York Yankees against the Cleveland Guardians in Game 5 of the ALDS, Judge was seen in the dugout kissing the Yankee logo on his jersey.
The Yankees would go on to win the game 5-1, advancing to the ALCS to face the Houston Astros.
When asked about the logo kiss after the game, Judge said the celebration came directly from his Premier League fandom.
