N.B. man runs Calgary Marathon to honour his mother's spirit
cbc.ca
news
2022-05-29 17:52:00

Matt Eagles raised over $2,000 for Ovarian Cancer Canada by running the Calgary Marathon on Sunday. (Mrinali Anchan/CBC)It was a grueling day for Matt Eagles at the Calgary Marathon in Stampede Park on Sunday. But the memories of his mother helped him get to the finish line after 42.2 kilometres."I miss her every day," said Eagles. "I certainly could feel her presence with me."Eagles, who grew up in Fredericton, is a neurosurgery resident in the department of clinical neurosciences at the University of Calgary.
