Is the transfer portal concentrating the riches of college football in the hands of a few elite programs, or is this the pathway toward spreading talent more widely across the sport? Maybe both?
Ari Wasserman of The Athletic tackled this question:
On the surface, you'd think that the presence of the transfer portal would result in talent being more evenly distributed across the board. All of those players stuck behind stars at Alabama, Georgia, Clemson and Ohio State now have the opportunity to leave and become eligible immediately.
