Yes To Moonlighting? Infosys Allows Employees To Take Up 'External' Gig Work
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 06:42:29

Nearly a month after its strict 'No two-timing, no moonlighting' message to employees, IT giant Infosys seems to have adopted a more accommodative stance towards moonlighting, or gig work.Infosys has sent an internal communication to employees allowing them to take up gig work outside their day jobs, but with consent from their managers, and on condition that it would not impact their work at Infosys nor conflict with anything Infosys does, as per the TOI report. shutterstockThe message reads: "Any employee, who wishes to take up gig work may do so with the prior consent of their manager and BP (business partner)-HR in their personal time, for establishments that do not compete with Infosys or Infosys's clients.
