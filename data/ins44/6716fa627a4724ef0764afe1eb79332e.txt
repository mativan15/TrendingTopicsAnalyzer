Wesfarmers puts focus on chemicals, energy division as retail falters
smh.com.au
business
2022-06-02 06:19:48

Retail powerhouse Wesfarmers has highlighted the performance of its oft-overlooked chemicals and digital businesses amid warnings of a retail downturn, as shoppers struggle with the rising cost of living.On Thursday, the Perth-based business held an investor day where the company's iconic retail brands - Bunnings, Kmart and Officeworks - took somewhat of a back seat, with Wesfarmers' WesCEF chemicals, energy and fertilisers business the first division pitched to shareholders.Wesfarmers CEO Rob Scott said the company has not yet seen a slowdown in consumer spending.
