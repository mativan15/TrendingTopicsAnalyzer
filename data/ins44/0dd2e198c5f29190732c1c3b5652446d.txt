SINGAPORE, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--MetricStream, the global market leader in integrated risk management (IRM) and governance, risk, and compliance (GRC), announced its latest product release, focused on accelerating GRC program performance. This includes a faster, safer, easier, and more personalized Connected GRC experience. The modernized, purpose-built low-code/no-code platform empowers customers to easily personalize and configure products to their unique needs. New APIs help connect MetricStream with external systems and enrich GRC insights to accelerate decision making.
