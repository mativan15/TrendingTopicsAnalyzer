Commerce: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

business
2022-10-18 10:06:30

KANSAS CITY, Mo.

Commerce Bancshares Inc. (CBSH) on Tuesday reported third-quarter profit of $122.8 million.

The bank, based in Kansas City, Missouri, said it had earnings of $1.02 per share.

The results did not meet Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of six analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.04 per share.

The bank holding company posted revenue of $401.2 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $384.9 million, beating Street forecasts.
