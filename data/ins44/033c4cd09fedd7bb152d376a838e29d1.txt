Meet HBJ's 2022 Women Who Mean Business honorees
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 19:46:00

The Houston Business Journal has named more than 100 women as honorees for the 2022 Women Who Mean Business Awards, recognizing women in leadership roles who have demonstrated excellence in their careers and community.

The awards honor women across 12 industry categories plus the Woman to Watch category. There is also a Lifetime Achievement Award recipient. The criteria for selection included career achievement, contribution to company and city success, community involvement and leadership.

All of the honorees were recognized during a sold-out awards luncheon on Oct.
