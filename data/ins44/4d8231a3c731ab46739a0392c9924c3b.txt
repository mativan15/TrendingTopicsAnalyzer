New York (CNN Business)LeBron James is a billionaire, marking the first time an active NBA player has achieved that milestone, according to one calculation.Forbes said that the Los Angeles Lakers star has "maximized his business," generating more than $1.2 billion in pretax earnings. His NBA salary from three different teams during the past 19 years has amounted to $383 million, and James has "raked in upwards of $900 million in income from endorsements and other business ventures," the magazine said.
