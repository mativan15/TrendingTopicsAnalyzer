Raptors' Pascal Siakam raring to go after late start last season
thestar.com
news
2022-10-16 00:00:00

Everyone wants to get the NBA regular season started after weeks of training camp tedium and games that are little more than low-intensity training sessions,

That is especially true for Pascal Siakam, who remembers how hard last season was and how special Wednesday night's Raptors home opener against the Cleveland Cavaliers will be for him.

'I'm starting the season at the point where I want to start and not having to play catch-up,' Siakam said after the Raptors practised Sunday afternoon. 'Obviously we still have to get back (to) game speed and pre-season isn't the same as the regular season, so you have to adjust, but I get a head start compared to last year.
