By Adriano Marchese Shares of Canadian mass media company Corus Entertainment Inc. fell in early trading on Friday after the company reported lower advertising dollars swung the company to a loss in its fiscal fourth quarter. At 9:36 a.m. ET, the shares were trading nearly 12% lower at C$1.97. For the period ended Aug. 31, Corus reported a loss of 367.1 million Canadian dollars (US$266.7 million), or C$1.82 a share, compared with a profit of C$19.9 million. or C$0.10 a share a year ago.
