Pakistan: Imran Khan blames Sharif govt for rising terrorism in Swat valley
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 03:30:00

AgenciesFormer Pakistan PM Imran Khan (File Pic)Former Pakistan Prime Minister and Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) chief Imran Khan on Thursday blamed the coalition government of Shehbaz Sharif for rising terrorism in the scenic mountainous valley of Swat. In his three speeches delivered in Takht Bhai, Charsadda and Rawalpindi, Khan said that people had taken to the streets in Swat against the rising violence in the valley, especially, after a school van was attacked leaving the driver dead and two children injured earlier this week, reported The Express Tribune.
