October 21, 2022 04:35 PM Eastern Daylight Time PLANO, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Finance of America Companies Inc. (NYSE: FOA) (the 'Company' or 'FOA'), a leading specialty finance solutions platform, today announced its decision to discontinue the operations of its forward mortgage originations segment across both retail and wholesale channels by the end of the year.

The Finance of America Strategy

FOA was created to provide pathways for consumers to achieve greater financial freedom through the use of the equity in their home.
