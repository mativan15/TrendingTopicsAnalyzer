The only Indian batter with two triple-hundreds in test cricket, Virender Sehwag, is celebrating his 44th birthday today (October 20). After his swashbuckling stint on the cricket field, Sehwag is on his way to creating fresh records by entertaining his fans and followers with his rib-tickling posts on various issues on social media Since he retired from active cricket, Sehwag has turned into a social media sensation, shooting hilarious like his inimitable batting style. Fans eagerly wait for his unique life tips under the series Viru Ke Funde.
