Several state police authorities have warned netizens against falling prey to hoax calls offering 5G services.
 

Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the 5G services in India on October 1 at the India Mobile Congress. The event witnessed telecom giants like Reliance Jio and Bharti Airtel sharing their vision to take 5G services to every nook and corner of the country.Reliance Jio rolled out True 5G in select cities of the country on October 5. The 5G services were rolled out in Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata and Varanasi for Jio users by invitation.
