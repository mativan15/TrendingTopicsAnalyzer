June 5 of every year is World Environment Day, a global day of sustainability awareness created by the UN Environment Programme (UNEP) with a unique theme each year. This year's theme, marking the 50th anniversary of the World Environment Day tradition, is 'Only One Earth'. In response to the growing climate crisis faced by the entire world, the international community is embarking on initiatives to help preserve the environment. Samsung Electronics considers sustainability to be a key business priority, and accordingly, builds eco-conscious efforts into every aspect of its products' lifecycles across all the various business areas in which it operates.
