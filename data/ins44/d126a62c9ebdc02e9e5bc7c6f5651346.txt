Kraft Heinz A Top Socially Responsible Dividend Stock With 4.3% Yield
forbes.com
news
2022-05-27 12:53:07

Kraft Heinz has been named a Top Socially Responsible Dividend Stock by Dividend Channel, signifying a stock with above-average ''DividendRank'' statistics including a strong 4.3% yield, as well as being recognized by prominent asset managers as being a socially responsible investment, through analysis of social and environmental criteria. Environmental criteria include considerations like the environmental impact of the company's products and services, as well as the company's efficiency in terms of its use of energy and resources.
