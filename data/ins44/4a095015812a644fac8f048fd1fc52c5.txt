These Bucs are a long way from the Super Bowl
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-16 22:23:53

Two years ago, the Tampa Bay Buccaneers rode an eight-game winning streak all the way to a Lombardi Trophy, winning their last four regular-season games and three straight playoff games on the road before becoming the first team in NFL history to win the Super Bowl in their home stadium.
Watching them play Sunday, it's clear just how far removed this year's Bucs are from that championship team.
