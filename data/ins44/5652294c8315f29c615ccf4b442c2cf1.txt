The first time Neha Samar received a handwritten note in her letterbox asking if she would be willing to sell her home, she threw it in the bin and forgot about it. 'Shepparton has a bad housing need at the moment, but it's so annoying.'Agents are turning to increasingly frenzied measures to hunt for sellers as lower supply, falling house prices and rapid interest rate rises dissuade punters from entering the market.
