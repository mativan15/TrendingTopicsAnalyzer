In some states, losing a law license — or getting "disbarred" — is forever.

In Wisconsin, a disbarred lawyer can petition to have a revoked license reinstated after five years. And many do, even those who've been convicted of felonies.

At least three Supreme Court justices support removing that option in the most egregious cases.

"I believe that when it comes to lawyer discipline, courts should say what they mean and mean what they say," wrote Chief Justice Annette Ziegler.
