Man Held For Withdrawing Cash From Acct Of Deceased
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 07:45:00

VADODARA: The cybercrime sleuths on Friday arrested a man for withdrawing money from the bank accounts of a deceased person. Kuleen Kumar Dobariya was nabbed after police got information about him during investigations. The police found two debit cards from Dobariya who is a resident of Ellora Park. Kabir Mirpuri had recently filed a complaint at cybercrime police station after he learnt that someone had withdrawn over Rs 12 lakh from his late father Jaikishan 's two bank accounts. The cybercrime sleuths got CCTV footage of the ATM booths from where Dobariya had withdrawn the money and it helped them in tracking down the accused.
