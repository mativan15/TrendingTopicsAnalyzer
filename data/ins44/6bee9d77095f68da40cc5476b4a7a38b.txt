Image source, GoogleImage caption, Staff at City College, Plymouth have walked out in a dispute over payA three-day staff strike is taking place at two Cornwall and Devon colleges over a pay offer.Members of the University and College Union (UCU) at City College Plymouth and Truro and Penwith College are striking from 18 to 20 October.A 2.5% pay recommendation from an employer body has been called an "insult" by the UCU.The Association of Colleges said leaders were doing all that they could to raise wages.
