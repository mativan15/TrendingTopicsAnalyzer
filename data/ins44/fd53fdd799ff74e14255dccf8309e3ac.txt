CALL of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 is holding a secret celebrity tournament in London to celebrate the game's launch on October 28.
Two teams of celebrities and Call of Duty streamers will take part in the event, including England's national goalie, Jordan Pickford.
It only a week until the full game's release.Credit: Activision
The event is set to take place in a secret location in London on the day before launch, featuring a number of different celebrities.
Pickford is headlining, though cult favourite and former player Adebayo Akinfenwa, known as The Beast, will also take part.
