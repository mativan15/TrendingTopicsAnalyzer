WEST HOLLYWOOD, Calif., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Digital commerce pioneer Oro, Inc., today announced that OroCommerce, the premier open-source digital commerce platform built for manufacturers, distributors, and suppliers, has been ranked second highest for the B2B eCommerce Use Case in 2022 Gartner Critical Capabilities for Digital Commerce Platforms report.
Gartner, a company that delivers actionable, objective insight to its executive and their teams, publishes its Critical Capabilities report to offer business buyers deeper insight into the capability and suitability of providers' IT products and services based on specific or customized use cases.
