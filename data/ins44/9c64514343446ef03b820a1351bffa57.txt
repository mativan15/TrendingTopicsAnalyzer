Baaeed loses unbeaten record in last race of career
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 15:46:52

Bay Bridge held off Adayar and My Prospero in the closing stages to claim the Champion StakesBaaeed's career and unbeaten run ended with defeat in the Champion Stakes as Bay Bridge triumphed at Ascot.The four-year-old, Europe's top-rated horse, was denied a 11th successive win in his final race on British Champions Day.The odds-on favourite did not threaten and finished fourth as Richard Kingscote steered the Sir Michael Stoute-trained Bay Bridge to victory."We thought the favourite was unbeatable," Stoute told ITV Racing.
