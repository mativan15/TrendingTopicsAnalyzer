City Union Bank Q4 net profit surges 88% to ₹209 crore
thehindu.com
news
2022-05-27 14:38:31

Private sector lender City Union Bank has reported an 88% growth in its standalone net profit for the fourth quarter ended March to ₹209 crore over the year-earlier period.Total income rose to ₹1,253 crore from ₹1,135 crore. Net interest income rose to ₹501 crore from ₹429 crore, it said in a regulatory filing.Net interest margin increased to 4.01% from 3.72%, the bank said in a presentation.
Gross non-performing assets declined to 4.70% from 5.11% and net NPA to 2.95% from 2.97%. Provisions rose to ₹231 crore from ₹188 crore.
