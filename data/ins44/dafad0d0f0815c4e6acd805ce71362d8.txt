Digitalbox to Acquire TVGuide.co.uk Assets for GBP550,000 -- Deal Digest
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-30 07:21:00

By Joe Hoppe ACQUIRER: Digitalbox PLC, a London-listed digital-media company which owns websites like Entertainment Daily, the Daily Mash, and the Tab. SELLER: TVGuide.co.uk Ltd., a dedicated interactive U.K. television listings brand. TARGET: TVGuide.co.uk's web and digital assets. PRICE: 550,000 pounds ($694,760). FINANCING: Completion is conditional on the satisfactory upgrade by TVGuide of its website. GBP20,000 is payable immediately on exchange to trigger redevelopment, with a further GBP350,000 payable on completion, and a deferred GBP180,000 payment four months from the completion date.
