Making Sensex weaker, Rs 27,000 crore of FII money flew away after Fed's last rate hike
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 05:30:00

NEW DELHI: After September 21 this year when US Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell hiked interest rates by 75 basis points for the third consecutive time to tame decades-high inflation, Sensex has lost over 1,500 points while FIIs have withdrawn over Rs 27,400 crore from Dalal Street. Barring July and August, FIIs have been net sellers on Indian stock exchanges in all months of the calendar year 2022. The total outflow on a YTD basis stood at Rs 176,247 crore. FIIs have been net buyers on Dalal Street only on 3 occasions since the Fed hike, shows market data.
