Guggenheim reiterates Netflix as Buy, modeling advertising revenue boost (NASDAQ:NFLX)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-02 19:29:57

Mario Tama/Getty Images News Guggenheim has reiterated its Buy rating on Netflix (NASDAQ:NFLX), noting that the new plan to add an advertising-supported tier raises the long-term revenue outlook. Its reduced its price target, though, expecting higher near-term costs from the ads rollout. Analyst Michael Morris is assuming a domestic Netflix launch of ad support in the first quarter of 2023, with a global rollout in the two years following that - EMEA in late 2023, Latin America in 2024, and Asia Pacific in 2025.
