DaveAlan/iStock Unreleased via Getty Images Spirit (NYSE:SAVE) shareholders should reject Frontier Group's (NASDAQ:ULCC) takeover offer, proxy adviser ISS recommends. Jetblue's (NASDAQ:JBLU) offer for Spirit is financially better than Frontier's (ULCC), though bought deals have antitrust risks, according to traders, according to an ISS report issued on Tuesday. JetBlue (JBLU) and Frontier (ULCC) have been waging a battle for Spirit (SAVE) over the last few months after Frontier originally agreed to a acquire the ultra-low-cost carrier.
