Travelers stock up 1.3% premarket after Q3 profit and revenue beat
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 11:18:56

Travelers Cos. Inc. shares TRV, +1.20% rose 1.3% in premarket trade Wednesday, after the property & casualty insurer topped profit and revenue estimates for the third quarter. The company posted net income of $454 million, or $1.89 a share, for the quarter, down 31% from $662 million, or $2.62 a share, in the year-earlier period.
