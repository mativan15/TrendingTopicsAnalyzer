Milford, NH, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The annual MedTech Conference serves to facilitate the exchange of ideas while fostering partnerships and catalyzing innovations that lead to a healthier world. This year, the conference is being held in Boston, MA at the Convention and Expo Center, October 24-26 and Cirtronics is exhibiting at Booth #315 Exhibit Hall level C.

This conference is a vital platform to connect and collaborate. Medical technology, medical devices, and lab equipment all require high levels of quality, attention, and focus.
