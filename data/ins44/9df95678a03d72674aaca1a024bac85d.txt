Owners of Winnipeg convenience store assaulted, 1 seriously, after confronting thieves
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-20 04:06:00

The owners of this convenience store were assaulted on Saturday, police say. Their son, Jonathan Lim, says his father has a brain injury following the assault, and both his parents will be retiring after 20 years in business. (Rachel Bergen/CBC)The couple that owns a convenience store in Winnipeg's West Broadway neighbourhood were assaulted and one was left with swelling in his brain after trying to stop a group of people from stealing, their son said."My dad was trying to stop some shoplifting in the store because it's become an issue and he just got jumped by a bunch of guys and girls outside the store," said Jonathan Lim, who rushed to Winnipeg from his home in Vancouver upon hearing of the assault on Saturday.
