Brian Haines, Chief Strategy Officer, FM:Systems. Getty Every organization comes to a point in which dated technologies no longer serve their originally intended purpose and simply must be upgraded. Inevitable as it is, it can be daunting and time-consuming to lead an IT team to carefully determine where and when improvements need to be made. The arrival of Covid-19, however, has fast-tracked this entire process, as organizations have had to quickly both choose and deploy new systems, equipment and applications better suited for remote work.
