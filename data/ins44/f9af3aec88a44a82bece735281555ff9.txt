The Strother Room coworking space offers a new, old-fashioned kind of work lounge
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 17:17:00

For generations, business has been done over a drink and a cigar. One entrepreneur in downtown Lee's Summit hopes to carry on that tradition with a new club-like coworking space.

The Strother Room, a work lounge at 310 SW Main St., is a coworking space with a Churchill-esque vibe. The brainchild of Levi Horseman, the space is filled with warm tones, craftsman-inspired textures, soft lighting and an old-fashioned approach for modern professionals.

Entering the second-floor space, visitors are taken back to a time when men and women 'understood a little self-indulgence went a long way to remedy a long day's work.
