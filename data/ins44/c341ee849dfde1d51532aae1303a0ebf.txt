After years of anticipation and a 10,000-person waiting list, LYMA Laser, the world's first at-home device featuring medical-grade laser technology, finally received FDA approval and launched in the U.S. on May 16. While beauty tech has boomed in recent years, especially with consumers wanting to take even more control over their skincare and wellness regimens, LYMA has established itself as an industry trailblazer by building its reputation the old-fashioned way: upon science and data.
