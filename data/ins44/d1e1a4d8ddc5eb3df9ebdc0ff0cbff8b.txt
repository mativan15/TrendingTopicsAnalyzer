Cryptocurrency taxation is subject to tremendous uncertainties. This article can't come close to covering all the relevant issues, but it highlights a few key ones.The first question is fundamental: When a customer deposits crypto with an exchange, does the customer continue to own the crypto, or does the exchange own the crypto? The answer, from both a tax and non-tax perspective, is that it depends.
