Kerala University Senate Members Removal: Action On Governor Arif Mohammed Khan Ruled Out
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-20 13:31:01

Thiruvananthapuram: The Left Democratic Front (LDF) government is not going to take steps with regard to Governor Arif Mohammed Khan's decision as Chancellor of universities to remove the 15 members of the Senate of the Kerala University, State Higher Education Minister R Bindu said on Thursday. He said the government has no relation to that decision. "Government has got nothing to do with it. It has no direct relation to the action taken by the Governor. It is for the university and the affected members of the Senate to decide the further course of action," she told PTI.
