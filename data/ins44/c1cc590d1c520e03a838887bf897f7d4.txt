19 October 2022 by Marcus Jones 
Indiewire One of the last big questions surrounding this year's Oscar acting categories has been answered: 'Women Talking' star Rooney Mara will be campaigned for Best Actress, sources close to the campaign confirmed to IndieWire.As there are no official rules from the Academy of Motion Picture Arts & Sciences concerning which actor goes into what categories — it's more of an honor system instead — this year in particular has seen lead and/or supporting actors moving all over the place in an effort to be campaigned where they will most likely receive an Oscar nomination.
