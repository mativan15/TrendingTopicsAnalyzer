The Maryland Department of Natural Resources continues to stock trout for the fall season. The week of Oct. 2, DNR stocked a total of 7,700 trout throughout the state, that included the waters of Cunningham Lake in Frederick County, Morgan Run in Carroll County, and Greenbrier Lake in Washington County. Around Oct. 11, another total of 2,900 trout were stocked in waters around the state, including the South Branch Patapsco River in Carroll County and Blairs Valley Lake in Washington County.
