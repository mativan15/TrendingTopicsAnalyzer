Do you have a Vivo smartphone? Software update to enable 5G coming this month
hindustantimes.com
tech
2022-10-19 21:01:40

Chinese smartphone manufacturer Vivo will release software updates this month to make most of its 5G-enabled handsets compatible with both standalone and non-standalone 5G networks, a company executive has announced.'More than six of our smartphones support the 5G standalone network. Most of our smartphones are compatible with NSA (non-standalone network). We will release software updates this month which will make most of our smartphones compatible with 5G standalone as well,' said Paigham Danish, business strategy head at Vivo India.
