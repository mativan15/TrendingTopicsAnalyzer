Fogarty, CFA; & Vinay Thapar, CFA Growth stocks are under acute pressure as rising interest rates change the dynamics that drive equity valuations. But market volatility shouldn't distract investors. We believe companies that can deliver sustainable growth in a sluggish economy will ultimately be prized for their business benefits and investment return potential. Market corrections are always painful, but investors in growth equities have suffered a particularly harsh blow this year.
