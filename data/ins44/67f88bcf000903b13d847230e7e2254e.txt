American Woodmark to increase stock kitchen, bath cabinet capacity for east coast markets
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-18 20:41:40

American Woodmark (NASDAQ:AMWD) said it will increase its stock kitchen and bath cabinet capacity for east coast markets.
The project is expected to represent a capital investment of ~$65M over the next two years and result in the creation of more than 500 new jobs.
The funding for the investment will be made from cash on hand and from revolving credit facility borrowings.
