Samsung UK Reveals the 2022 Finalists of its ‘Solve for Tomorrow' Competition
samsung.com
news
2022-06-01 15:37:30

LONDON, UK – 1 June 2022 – Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd is excited to unveil the five finalists of its youth tech competition ‘Solve for Tomorrow' – an alternative learning programme designed to inspire and support a diverse cohort of young people to share their passions outside of the traditional education system. Delivered in partnership with Digital Catapult, the UK's leading advanced digital technology innovation centre, the competition aims to find, fuel and celebrate the next generation of tech entrepreneurs by empowering participants with the confidence, connections and capabilities to realise their ideas, careers and ambitions.
