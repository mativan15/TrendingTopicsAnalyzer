The NSW Premier has vowed to go it alone on the $2 billion project to raise the Warragamba Dam wall even if the federal government doesn't split the cost.Mr Perrottet said environment impact statements for the government were still being worked on.Prime minister Anthony Albanese said the NSW Premier had not "advocated" for the project with himBut Mr Perrottet said federal governments regularly skimp on NSW  investment funding Dominic Perrottet said the state would wear the cost if the Commonwealth doesn't come to the table, despite indicating earlier this year that the state would be unable to fund the project alone.
