The Cannes Film Festival concluded recently with much fanfare, seeing a fantastic medley of some of the most captivating and widely acclaimed models, actors, and actresses from around the world. Behind the stunningly beautiful look of renowned actress Urvashi Rautela at the Cannes Film Festival was celebrity makeup artist Divya Behl. Rautela's two outfits, one a stunning black gown and another an angelic white dress, were perfectly complemented by the pink-and-peach makeup done by Behl.
