The hotly anticipated media startup Semafor has launched, creating the 'Semaform' article structure to separate news from opinion
businessinsider.com
news
2022-10-18 10:05:00

Semafor, the hotly anticipated media startup that grabbed industry attention before it even launched, is finally here.Led by former Bloomberg Media CEO Justin Smith and former New York Times media columnist and BuzzFeed News editor-in-chief Ben Smith, the outlet began publishing Tuesday morning with the lofty ambition of "solving certain significant news consumer frustrations," Justin Smith told Insider."The top of the list is polarization, bias, and social media distortion. Not far thereafter is information overload and of course the increasing blending of news and opinion," he said.
