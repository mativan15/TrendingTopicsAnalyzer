Chinmayi Sripada puts an end to 'surrogacy rumours' with a selfie of her 32-week-pregnant self; see pic
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-18 05:40:00

Eminent playback singer-TV judge Chinmayi Sripada has indeed drew curtains over the 'surrogacy' rumours with a selfie of herself while she was 32-week-pregnant. She took to her Instagram handle to share what she claims the 'only' selfie she took during her pregnancy and wrote, "The only selfie I took throughout pregnancy. The only.
Here at almost 32 weeks (sic)"

 For the unversed, ever since Chinmayi delivered twins in June, there have been many queries asking if she had kids through surrogacy and also rumours over the same.
