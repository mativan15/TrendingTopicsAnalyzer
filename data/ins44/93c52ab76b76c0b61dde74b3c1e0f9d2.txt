Tata Consumer Products Ltd. (TCPL) on Thursday reported a 36.25% rise in consolidated net profit to ₹389.43 crore for the second quarter ended September 30, led by strong growth in the domestic market. The company had posted a net profit of ₹285.80 crore for the July-September period a year ago, TCPL, earlier known as Tata Global Beverages Ltd., said in a BSE filing. Revenue from operations climbed 10.87% to ₹3,363.05 crore during the quarter under review as against ₹3,033.12 crore in the corresponding period last year.
