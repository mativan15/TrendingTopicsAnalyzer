Part 3 of the TED Radio Hour episode Humor Us. Jennifer Aaker and Naomi Bagdonas teach a class at Stanford's business school called 'Humor: Serious Business.' They say humor is vastly undervalued in business and is key to good leadership. About Jennifer Aaker Jennifer Aaker is a behavioral scientist and a professor at the Stanford Graduate School of Business. Aaker got her bachelor's in psychology at the University of California, Berkeley and earned her Ph.D. in marketing and psychology from the Stanford Graduate School of Business.
