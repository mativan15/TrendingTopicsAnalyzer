Cleveland Cavaliers hire new chief communications officer
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-20 21:48:00

The Cleveland Cavaliers have named a new chief communications officer: Susan Oguche.

Oguche, who comes to the Cavaliers from Nike, also will be executive vice president and chief communications officer for Rock Entertainment Group and Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse, the team said in a Thursday statement.

She replaces Tad Carper, who went to work for the Dallas Cowboys football team in August, according to NBC Sports.

Oguche, who goes by the name "Susan Onechojo O." in her LinkedIn profile, will be responsible for communications strategy and public affairs across the Rock Entertainment Group family of sports and entertainment properties, the Cavs said.
