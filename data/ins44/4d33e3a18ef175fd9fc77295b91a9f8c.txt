CLEVELAND (AP) — Guardians rookie infielder Gabriel Árias will make just his second career major league start at first base Saturday night in Game 3 of the AL Division Series against the New York Yankees.Árias will replace Josh Naylor, who came up hobbling in Game 2 after beating out an infield single in Cleveland's 4-2 win on Friday. Naylor came back this season after suffering a grotesque break of his right ankle last season.Naylor will be Cleveland's designated hitter in Game 3. The best-of-five series is tied at 1-all.
