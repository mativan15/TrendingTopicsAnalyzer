Crypto Giant Begins Rescinding Offers Amid Hiring Freeze as Market Continues to Tumble
chron.com
news
2022-06-03 20:45:31

It seems to be the season of mass layoffs and red alarms sounding for many industries, especially in the world of crypto.Popular crypto exchange platform Coinbase has not been immune to this, announcing two weeks ago that the company would begin slowing down its hiring process due to volatile conditions in the market. As of a new memo penned on Friday, it was announced that the company would be continuing this hiring freeze for an indeterminate amount of time while also beginning to rescind certain employment offers that have already been made.
