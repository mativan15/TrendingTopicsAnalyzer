Samvat 2078 leaves D-Street investors richer by Rs 11 lakh crore

ETMarkets.comThe headline index, Sensex, ended the Hindu accounting year Samvat 2078 on a weak noteEven as the headline index, Sensex, ended the Hindu accounting year Samvat 2078 on a weak note with a loss of 465 points, Dalal Street investors were left richer by Rs 11.3 lakh crore. The market capitalisation of all BSE-listed companies increased by Rs 11.3 lakh crore to Rs 274.4 lakh crore in the year. While the Sensex ended 465 points lower at 59,307.15 in Samvat 2078, Nifty ended 253 points lower at 17,576.
