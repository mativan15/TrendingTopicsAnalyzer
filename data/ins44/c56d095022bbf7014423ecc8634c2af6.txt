Imagine that you or your family experience a real emergency. Maybe your partner is having a heart attack. Maybe your kitchen is on fire. Maybe someone is trying to break in. You call 911. First responders arrive quickly and provide critical help. These things happen hundreds of times every day here in Chicago. Call centers in compliance with current standards answer 90% of 911 calls within 15 seconds and 95% within 20 seconds. We expect that rapid response. We can easily take it for granted.We shouldn't.
