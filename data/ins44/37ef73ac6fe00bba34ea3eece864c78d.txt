The Yossarian type: A subset of the self-obsessed have been proven right — mosquitoes do target them above others

2022-10-20 22:54:15

Everyone knows at least one person who takes everything a little too personally. Even a mosquito bite is attributed by such people to having particularly 'sweet blood'. Even the less-than-intelligent vampiric insect is given malicious powers of discretion by the self-obsessed. Call it the Yossarian phenomenon. The main character in Catch-22 — an American fighter pilot during World War II — refuses to fly a mission. There are, after all, thousands of Germans trying to kill him. 'But they're trying to kill everyone!', his commander insists.
