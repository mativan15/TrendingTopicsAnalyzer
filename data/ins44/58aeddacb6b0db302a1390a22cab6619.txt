Digital twins are moving into the mainstream

To meet ever more complex challenges in operations, customer demand, compliance and sustainability, businesses across a broad spectrum of industries are increasingly turning to digital twins. A new CapGemini study of 1,000 organizations of companies in life sciences, consumer products, energy and utilities, discrete manufacturing and infrastructure operators found that 80% of them are employing digital twins to develop new products, improve operational efficiencies, increase worker safety and meet sustainability targets.
