Photo: Courtesy of All Elite WrestlingThe status of one of All Elite Wrestling's biggest stars' status remains in question hours before the company's Double of Nothing pay-per-view event on Sunday (May 29) night. Maxwell Jacob Friedman, more commonly known by his initials, MJF, missed a scheduled appearance at AEW Fan Fest on Saturday (May 28) afternoon and was reported to have booked a flight out of Las Vegas, Fightful Select reported, later confirming that the Long Island native did not get on the flight, but his status for the event remained uncertain.
