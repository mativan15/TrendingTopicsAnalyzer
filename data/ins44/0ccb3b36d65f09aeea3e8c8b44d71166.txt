June 02, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time RESTON, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tellius, the AI-driven decision intelligence platform, today announced the appointments of Hardik Chheda to Chief Product Officer and John Aven to Head of Engineering. Both Chheda and Aven will focus on expanding capabilities for Tellius Decision Intelligence to accelerate how business and analyst teams uncover diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive insights from across data sources to inform decision making.



Chheda brings with him over a decade of experience in data analytics, including executive and product leadership roles at OmniSci, Infor, and Oracle.
