REUTERS/Yves Herman/File PhotoIn May, Eurozone inflation reached a new high of 8.1 per cent, owing to rising oil and food prices, which were pushed partly by Russia's war in Ukraine. The latest figures were released Tuesday by the European Union statistics agency, Eurostat, according to which the annual inflation in the 19 nations that use the euro currency surpassed the previous high of 7.
