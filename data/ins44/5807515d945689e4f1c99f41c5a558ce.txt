Ex-ministers ‘swanning around' Leinster House as lobbyists should lose access, Dáil hears
irishtimes.com
politics
2022-10-20 18:16:03

A call has been made for former TDs and Senators who become lobbyists to lose their automatic right of access to Leinster House.Social Democrats co-leader Róisín Shortall said 'the revolving door between politics and private business' had to end.She said 'I see it on a regular basis on budget day with various ex-Members getting the ear of the Minister. That needs to stop.'Oireachtas members retain the right when they leave or lose their seat to have unlimited access to Leinster House, including the members' and public bars.
