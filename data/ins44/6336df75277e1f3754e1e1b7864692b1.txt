Vadodara: Insta accounts hacked, used in crypto scams
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 02:15:00

Cyber experts, cops getting 3-4 calls of Insta fraud daily

VADODARA: Got a message on your Instagram offering fees for promoting a product? Don't click on the link in haste. For, you may lose your account and become a victim of cyber fraud.Hackers sitting on foreign shores have come up with a new trick to con Instagram users, especially the influencers having a huge following in Gujarat.They aren't duping just influencers but also their followers. They take control of the hacked account and start posting details of advantages of cryptocurrency investment and a few links for investing.
