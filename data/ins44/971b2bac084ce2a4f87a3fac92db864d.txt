Today's Mortgage Rates & Trends, June 3, 2022
thebalance.com
news
2022-06-03 19:37:02

The average rate offered for a 30-year fixed mortgage rose to its highest in more than a week, while the 15-year fell.
 
The average rate offered for a conventional 30-year fixed mortgage climbed to 5.71% from 5.68% the previous business day, while the average 15-year rate fell to 4.74% from 4.77% the previous business day. 
 
Rates have leveled off some after spiking in April to as high as 6.19% and 5.26%, respectively—the highest levels we'd seen since at least 2019 (and likely much farther back.
