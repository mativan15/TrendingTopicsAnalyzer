seekingalpha.com

2022-10-18 11:59:15

Dan Totilca Goldman Sachs (NYSE:GS) stock gained 2.8% in Tuesday premarket trading after the Wall Street bank announced an organizational realignment along with its Q3 earnings and net revenue that easily exceeded consensus estimates. The company's new operating segments are: Asset & Wealth Management; Global Banking & Markets; and Platform Solutions, a business that delivers digital financial services to the bank's clients. The new structure is designed to help the company to operate more efficiently, it said.
