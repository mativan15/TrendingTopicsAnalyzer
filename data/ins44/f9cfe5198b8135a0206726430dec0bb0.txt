June 02, 2022 01:10 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Manufacturers Bank is pleased to announce the election of Judith Posnikoff, PhD, to its Board of Directors. Dr. Posnikoff is a seasoned investment professional with extensive portfolio management, asset allocation, and portfolio construction experience as well as experience in compliance, operations, and legal issues related to private fund investing. She is a co-founder and managing partner at Martlet Asset Management, LLC in Newport Beach, California.
