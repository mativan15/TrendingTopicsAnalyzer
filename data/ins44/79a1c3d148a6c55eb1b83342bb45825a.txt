Freebee provides free, on-demand ride-hailing using electric vehicles to app users. 
The service is financed by local governments and entities, such as corporate business parks and universities, to cover defined areas or specific routes. 
The investment will help Freebee target expansion in the US beyond its base in Miami, Florida.
HOUSTON, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- bp ventures has invested $6 million in all-electric ride-hailing business, Freebee, a Miami-based company that provides free, on-demand transportation to local communities through their mobile app Ride Freebee.
