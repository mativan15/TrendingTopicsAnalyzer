MADISON, Wis., October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Navitus Health Solutions, a pharmacy benefit manager (PBM) committed to furthering access and affordability of important medications for the people who need them, announced the promotion of Cory Super, PharmD, to Vice President of Sales.

"Throughout his career, and in the short time Cory has been at Navitus, he has continuously reset the bar for sales goals and now leads an equally-ambitious team to industry-leading accomplishments," said Diana Gibson Pace, Chief Growth Officer of Navitus.
