Oct. 18, 2022 12:39 PM ETInpixon (INPX)By: Preeti Singh, SA News Editor Inpixon (NASDAQ:INPX) shares fell 41% on Tuesday after the indoor intelligence company priced its offering of ~$15M of its shares of common stock (or pre-funded warrants in lieu thereof) and warrants to purchase common stock. The company entered into a securities purchase agreement with a single institutional investor to sell 2,564,102 shares of common stock (or pre-funded warrants in lieu thereof) and warrants to purchase up to an aggregate of 3,846,153 shares of common stock.
