Bird, the troubled Sequoia-backed scooter company, is exiting markets and adding balance sheet risk
fortune.com
news
2022-10-19 11:10:22

One might say that Bird is no longer flying high. Bird was one of a handful of unprofitable electric-powered mobility companies that sailed into the public markets during the SPAC-hype of 2020 and 2021 on the promise of a greener future, reaping fresh capital from public investors and, at the outset at least, offering VC backers plump returns for their LPs. Bird—backed by prominent venture capitalists including Sequoia Capital's top captain Roelof Botha, Craft Ventures, Fidelity, and Valor Equity Partners—joined in on the fun.
