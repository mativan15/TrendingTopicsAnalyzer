Huge, unusually powerful explosion in space just detected by scientists
mashable.com
news
2022-10-15 09:00:00

In space, things frequently go boom.And recently, on Oct. 19, astronomers observed an extraordinarily colossal boom. NASA's Swift Observatory, which is specifically designed to spot the most powerful known explosions in the universe today — called gamma-ray bursts — detected an extremely strong such burst. Something wildly potent must produce these jets of energy that travel through space, and scientists say they're caused by the collapse and explosion of enormous stars, events called supernovae.
