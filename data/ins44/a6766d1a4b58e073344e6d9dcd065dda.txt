Individual investors account for 49% of Develop Global Limited's (ASX:DVP) ownership, while insiders account for 19%
yahoo.com

2022-10-19 01:03:50

A look at the shareholders of Develop Global Limited (ASX:DVP) can tell us which group is most powerful. The group holding the most number of shares in the company, around 49% to be precise, is individual investors. That is, the group stands to benefit the most if the stock rises (or lose the most if there is a downturn).

And individual insiders on the other hand have a 19% ownership in the company. Institutions often own shares in more established companies, while it's not unusual to see insiders own a fair bit of smaller companies.
