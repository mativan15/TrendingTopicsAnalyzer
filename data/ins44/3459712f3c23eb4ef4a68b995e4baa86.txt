No tech sector appears safe from the spring swoon

news
2022-05-26 01:35:00

Nvidia Corp., Nutanix Inc. and Snowflake Inc. all gave earnings forecasts that were below analysts' expectations on Wednesday, the latest in a batch of tech companies across sectors to give warnings about changing macroeconomic conditions in the spring. Lowered forecasts have struck across tech sectors, from social-media company Snap Inc. SNAP, +5.20% to chip maker Nvidia NVDA, +5.38% ; from cloud-software companies like Nutanix NTNX, +2.30% and Snowflake Inc. SNOW, +2.45% to networking giant Cisco Systems Inc.
