Cadaver dogs called as car feared buried since 1990s found at mansion: Cops
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-21 11:32:46

Cadaver dogs were summoned to an Atherton, California mansion after a car was discovered buried on the grounds surrounding the property.Police said that upon initial investigations of the scene around the car, the cadaver dogs "made a slight notification of possible human remains."The Atherton Police Department said in a statement that officers were called to the residence on the 300 block of Stockbridge Avenue at around 8:50 a.m. PDT on Thursday after the vehicle was uncovered by landscapers working on a project at the home.
