Firstrade launches a cash award offer of up to $4,000 USD for new account deposits in the US.

Firstrade Securities Inc., one of the first online brokerage firms to offer $0 commission, has announced a cash bonus award in which customers who open a new account can earn from $50 to $4,000 USD in cash bonuses. New accounts funded with a deposit between $5,000 to $1,500,000 are eligible to receive the cash bonus detailed in the new offer, available to all new customers across the US.
