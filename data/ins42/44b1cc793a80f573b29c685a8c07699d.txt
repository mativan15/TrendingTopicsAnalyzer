(NYSE: FCN) today announced the appointment of Susan Hartman as a Senior Managing Director in the Risk and Investigations practice within the firm's Forensic and Litigation Consulting segment. Ms. Hartman, who is based in Dallas, brings more than 20 years of professional services experience in finance and accounting, including valuations, damages calculations, investigations, regulatory compliance and internal controls.
