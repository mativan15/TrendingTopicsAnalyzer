Etsy can go up 54% from here as the online marketplace becomes a "top-of-mind" destination, JMP says
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-01 09:51:16

Investors should consider buying into Etsy , as the stock could see big gains going forward, according to JMP Securities. Analyst Nicholas Jones initiated coverage of Etsy with a market outperform rating, saying in a Wednesday note that the e-commerce company is attracting more repeat buyers during the pandemic. "ETSY's platform has been one of the top performers throughout and exiting the pandemic," Jones wrote. "We view its increase in brand awareness as lasting as opposed to transitory and see plenty of opportunity for ETSY to continue driving GMV growth through further improvement of brand awareness, geographic expansion, and technology investments.
