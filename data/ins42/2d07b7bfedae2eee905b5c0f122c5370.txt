Image caption, The market town lies between the Yorkshire Dales and North York Moors national parksRishi Sunak is back in the frame for high office as the Conservatives seek a new leader to follow Liz Truss as prime minister. The former chancellor's resignation in July was one of the catalysts which hastened the end of Boris Johnson's premiership.The BBC's Chloe Laversuch went to the North Yorkshire market town of Bedale in Mr Sunak's Richmond constituency to find out what his constituents think about their MP's chances of making it to Number 10.
