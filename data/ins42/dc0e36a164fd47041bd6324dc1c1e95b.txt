Salesforce stock rallies following raised outlook, with stronger dollar seen as only major headwind
marketwatch.com
business
2022-05-31 20:24:00

Salesforce Inc. shares rallied in the extended session Tuesday after the cloud-based customer-relationship-management company said business remained strong amid the current rocky economic landscape and hiked its earnings forecast for the year.Prior to the report, some analysts were concerned that reports of a slowdown in capital spending would be reflected in Salesforce's CRM, -2.94% results and outlook, but the only headwind the company reported in its conference call with analysts was a substantial rise in the dollar.
