 Four top Coinbase Global Inc. officials have collectively pocketed more than $1 billion by selling shares since the cryptocurrency exchange's public listing last spring, a period in which the company's shares have declined some 80%. Co-founders Brian Armstrong and Fred Ehrsam, as well as President and Chief Operating Officer Emilie Choi and Chief Product Officer Surojit Chatterjee, together netted about $1.
