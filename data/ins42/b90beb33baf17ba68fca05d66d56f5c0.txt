(The Conversation is an independent and nonprofit source of news, analysis and commentary from academic experts.)Luke Perry, Utica University(THE CONVERSATION) Mitt Romney has been one of few prominent Republicans to criticize Donald Trump, beginning with the 2016 presidential campaign. He did so again recently in response to Trump's critical comments about John McCain. 'I can't understand why the President would, once again, disparage a man as exemplary as my friend John McCain,' Romney tweeted.
