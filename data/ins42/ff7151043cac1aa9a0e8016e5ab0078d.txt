Paul Ryan-backed SPAC ENPC approves deal to create new oil and gas company (NYSE:ENPC)
seekingalpha.com
economics
2022-10-21 16:37:49

zimmytws Shareholders of SPAC Executive Network Partnering Corp. (NYSE:ENPC), which is chaired by former US House Speaker Paul Ryan, have voted in favor of a business combination with oil and gas asset holder Grey Rock Investment Partners that pegs the enterprise value of the combined company at around $1.3B. ENPC disclosed the voting results in an SEC filing. The combined company is expected to be named Granite Ridge Resources, with shares listed on NYSE under the symbol GRNT. Grey Rock and ENPC announced plans to combine in May.
