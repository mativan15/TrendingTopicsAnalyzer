Germany extends lifetime of all 3 remaining nuclear plants
dw.com
news
2022-10-17 17:30:00

Olaf Scholz's three-party coalition wrangled over nuclear power but the German Chancellor decided that the plants should keep running through winter.

Nuclear power is a touchy political topic in Germany, even as energy shortages loom
Chancellor Olaf Scholz on Monday ordered Germany's three remaining nuclear to remain in operation until April to fend off a possible energy crunch.
The German leader has asked the economy, environment and finance ministries to create the legal basis for the plants to remain open.
