New York, USA, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to Strategic Market Research, the Global Drug Delivery Devices Market size in 2021 was USD 239.2 billion, and by 2030 it will be USD 340.45 billion, with a 7.1% CAGR. The formulations, strategies, and techniques used to deliver chemical substances or drugs inside the body are referred to as drug delivery. This is used to treat, diagnose, cure, or prevent several diseases, including diabetes, gastrointestinal disorders, bacterial and viral infections, and allergies.
