Dublin, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Global Ammonia Market Report and Forecast 2022-2027" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.



According to the report, the global ammonia market attained a volume of around USD 191 MMT in 2021. Aided by the rising application of the product in industries such as pharmaceutical and agriculture, the market is projected to grow further at a CAGR of 1.90% in the forecast period to reach a volume of about USD 214.04 MMT by 2027.
