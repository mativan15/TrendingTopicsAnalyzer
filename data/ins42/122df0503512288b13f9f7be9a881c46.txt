By Australian Associated Press Published: 17:30 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 17:30 EDT, 18 October 2022 Renewable energy projects in Tasmania and Victoria have been fast-tracked in a bid to bring down power prices. The federal government has signed partnerships with the Victorian and Tasmanian governments to jointly fund the Marinus Link, a multi-billion dollar power project connecting the two states. The government also announced $1.5 billion of funding for renewable energy zone projects in Victoria, which include offshore wind.
