I happened to start writing this column while I was in the city of Barcelona, which has a square dedicated to George Orwell, author of both Homage to Catalonia and 1984. The latter book is a reminder of the totalitarian society that could emerge if we allow one person too much power. This isn't true for just politicians and governments. It's also true for technology and gaming. I read Orwell's books decades ago, and I see the warnings he offered us as relevant as ever.
