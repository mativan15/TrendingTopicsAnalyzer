HMSI inaugurated its premium big bike business vertical.New Delhi: Honda Motorcycle & Scooter India (HMSI) inaugurated its premium big bike business vertical, - Honda BigWing in New Delhi, on Friday.Atsushi Ogata, Managing Director, President, and CEO,HMSI,said, 'Our focus is on expansion of Honda BigWing (Honda's exclusive premium motorcycle network) to deliver a truly differentiated immersive experience closer to customers. Today, we are delighted to inaugurate BigWing at Lajpat Nagar in New Delhi.
