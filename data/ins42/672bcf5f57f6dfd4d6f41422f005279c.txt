May 27, 2022 04:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AVer Information Inc., the award-winning provider of video collaboration and education technology solutions, is excited to announce a technology partnership with Yamaha. With AVer's industry-leading cameras and Yamaha's high-quality microphones, the collaboration provides a smart solution to further enhance virtual connectivity and video presentation experiences.



'AVer is proud to align with Yamaha, a well-respected leader in the industry with strong values in innovation and excellence, to deliver a seamless user experience in both professional and educational environments.
