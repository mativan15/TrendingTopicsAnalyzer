Fintech acknowledged by Arizent for balanced, flexible workplace, dedication to employee advancement

SPRINGDALE, Ark., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Teslar Software, provider of lending process automation tools for community financial institutions, has been named a 2022 Best Place to Work in Financial Technology by Arizent and Best Companies Group.

Fintechs were selected for the list based on an evaluation of workplace policies, practices, philosophy, systems and demographics, and an employee survey rating experience.
