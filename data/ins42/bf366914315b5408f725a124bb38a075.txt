Delhi records 343 fresh COVID-19 cases, positivity rate 1.91%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 18:45:00

Delhi on Sunday logged 343 fresh COVID-19 cases with a positivity rate of 1.91 per cent while no new death was reported due to the viral disease, according to data shared by the city health department. With this, the national capital's COVID-19 case tally increased to 19,08,730 while the death toll stood at 26,212. A total of 17,917 COVID-19 tests were conducted in Delhi the previous day, the department said in its latest bulletin. On Saturday, the capital had logged 405 fresh COVID-19 cases with a positivity rate of 2.
