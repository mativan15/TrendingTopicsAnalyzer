MarinHealth CEO David Klein, MD, Featured Speaker at San Francisco Business Times' 'Future of Healthcare' Event on Tuesday, June 7

GREENBRAE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--MarinHealth today announced that CEO David Klein, MD, MBA, will be speaking at the San Francisco Business Times' upcoming event on the Future of Healthcare, which is being held at the Hilton Union Square on Tuesday, June 7th. The in-person panel discussion will focus on innovations shaping the healthcare industry. Details for registering are included below, including an option for people who would like to attend the event virtually.

Dr. Klein will be joined on stage by other distinguished guests including Suresh Gunasekran, MBA, President & CEO of UCSF Health; David Joyner, CEO of Hill Physicians Medical Group; Carrie Owen Plietz, President, Northern California Kaiser Foundation Health Plans & Hospitals; Rajeev Ronank, President, Digital Platforms at Anthem; Myoung Cha, President of Omnichannel Care & Chief Strategy Officer at Carbon Health; and Gyre Renwick, Chief Operating Officer at Modern Health.
