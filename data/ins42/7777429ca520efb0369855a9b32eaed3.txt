Originally founded in 1909 by solo entrepreneur Morris Rosenberg, ABM IPO'd in 1965 and is now a leading, diversified services company. It's now a very mature business in a generally slow-growth industry. They have grown mostly through acquisition and diversified their line of facilities services.
