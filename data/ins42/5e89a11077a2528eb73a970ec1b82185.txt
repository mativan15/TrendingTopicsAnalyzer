25 FCS freshmen selected to Jerry Rice Award Watch List
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 15:28:32

(Stats Perform) - Twenty-five FCS players were named on Thursday to the watch list of the 2022 Stats Perform Jerry Rice Award, which is presented to the national freshman of the year in college football's Division I subdivision.

The Rice Award, now in its 12th season, is named after legendary Hall of Fame wide receiver Jerry Rice. Past recipients include Los Angeles Rams wide receiver Cooper Kupp (Eastern Washington), Miami Dolphins running back Chase Edmonds (Fordham) and San Francisco 49ers quarterback Trey Lance (North Dakota State).
