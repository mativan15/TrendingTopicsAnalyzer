China Green Agriculture receives non-compliance letter from NYSE
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-20 20:48:01

China Green Agriculture (NYSE:CGA) said it had received a notice from the New York Stock Exchange that it was not in compliance with the listing requirements as a result of its failure to file its Annual Report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended June 30, 2022. The extension period expired on October 13. The Company said it is unable to meet the filing deadline due to the impacts related to COVID-19. The Company is currently working closely with its principal accounting firm to complete the filing of its 2022 Form 10-K as soon as reasonably practicable.
