The reversal of decades of interest rate declines has revealed an ugly side to owning bonds, but let's not get too caught up in appearances.Bonds are still the Number One way to protect portfolios from sharp stock market declines. You can see this in recent one-month comparative returns for stocks and bonds. Globeinvestor data shows that the FTSE Canada Universe Bond Index was up 0.2 per cent while the S&P/TSX composite index fell 2.7 per cent, the S&P 500 fell 7.4 per cent and Nasdaq fell 12.1 per cent.
