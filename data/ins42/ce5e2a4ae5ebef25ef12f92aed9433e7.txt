 Investigators with Wellington County OPP have released the identity of the woman killed in the 2-vehicle crash Sunday's in Guelph/Eramosa Township.
She is 38-year-old Hema Priya Loganathan of Cambridge.Three other occupants were in that same vehicle and taken to a trauma centre.OPP were called to an area on Wellington Rd. 32 at Fife Rd. west of Guelph around 6:30 p.m. Sunday. Emergency crews arrived to find a pick-up truck and a sedan had collided.
