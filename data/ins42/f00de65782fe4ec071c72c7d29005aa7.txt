When the crypto market was generally sluggish over the first quarter, some investors turned to nonfungible tokens, or NFTs, which outperformed most other digital assets at the time, in search of a haven. However, the bets soon turned sour. Prices of major NFT collections have crashed, with popular ones losing over 50% over the past month amid broad selloffs of risk assets, as investors are concerned about heated inflation, signs of a softening economy and the Federal Reserve's tightening of its monetary policy.
