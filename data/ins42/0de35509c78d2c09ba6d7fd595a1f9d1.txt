Teaser pictures from the upcoming drama "Maestro," starring Bradley Cooper and Carey Mulligan, are released by Netflix to the public. On Monday, the streaming platform posted four photos from the set of "Maestro" on social media. In the pictures, a young Bernstein played by Cooper can be seen along with his wife Felicia played by Mulligan. Cooper is also seen as an old Bernstein conducting an orchestra in one of the stills. What is the film's plot?
This new movie is based on the life of iconic composer Leonard Bernstein.
