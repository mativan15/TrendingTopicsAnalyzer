Saisha Shinde Fulfills Her Promise To Donate Half Her 'Lock Upp' Income For Trans Community
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 18:35:16

Ace designer Saisha Shinde, who was last seen on the reality show Lock Upp has fulfilled her promise of donating half of her income from the show to the LGBTQI+ organizations. In a recent post on Instagram, Saisha shared pictures from her recent visit to a foundation.  InstagramIn a detailed note, Saisha mentioned that she wouldn't just be donating half of her earnings but would be contributing on a monthly basis as she wants to be a part of their lives daily.  Instagram/Saisha ShindeSaisha shared the post with her fans after visiting a Trans-led organization that works with young transpersonal to provide shelter, education, employment, skill-building, and counseling support.
