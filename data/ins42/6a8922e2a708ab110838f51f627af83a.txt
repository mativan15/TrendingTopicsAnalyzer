How Jürgen Klopp can make one of Liverpool's best sides better

At a sports science conference a few weeks ago I got chatting to a couple of Spaniards, who have an analytics company that uses new metrics and visuals to break down the game. They were trying to roll their platform out into the UK but said that one Premier League club — Liverpool — was already on top of this stuff.I'd heard it before. A year ago, from someone developing a business based around bringing artificial intelligence tools to help recruitment. From deploying neuroscience to help with penalties, to employing a doctor of theoretical physics to help assess players, Liverpool are on top of a lot of things.
