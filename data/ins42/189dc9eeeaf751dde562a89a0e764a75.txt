London, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Hoping Club announced that it has recently invested in a new investment company in Singapore and has set up group offices in Singapore and Southeast Asia, and is expected to hold its annual group meeting at the end of the year, the first time Hoping Club has held a meeting in Asia.

Headquartered in Wall Street, New York, USA, Hoping Club is known as the world's financial center, with the world's most experienced investment institutions and the most stimulating competition.
