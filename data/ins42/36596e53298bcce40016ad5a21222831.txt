Kolkata police unearth firearm manufacturing unit in Jharkhand
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 10:17:28

The Special Task Force (STF) of the Kolkata police busted an improvised firearm manufacturing unit that was being operated in a concealed underground chamber in a house at Jamtara in Jharkhand on Friday, officials said. Police have already arrested four persons and seized a huge cache of firearms and unfinished products and machinery, which were used to manufacture the firearms. On Wednesday, the police arrested four persons, including a 40-year-old Munger-based arms dealer Md Imtiyaz. He also used to deal with counterfeit currency notes.
