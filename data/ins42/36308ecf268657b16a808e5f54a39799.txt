October 20, 2022 08:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time IRVINE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Skyworks Solutions, Inc. (Nasdaq: SWKS), an innovator of high-performance analog and mixed signal semiconductors connecting people, places and things, will conduct a conference call with analysts to discuss its fourth quarter and fiscal year 2022 results and business outlook on Nov. 3, 2022, at 4:30 p.m. EDT.

After the close of the market on Nov. 3, and prior to the conference call, Skyworks will issue a copy of the earnings press release via Business Wire.
