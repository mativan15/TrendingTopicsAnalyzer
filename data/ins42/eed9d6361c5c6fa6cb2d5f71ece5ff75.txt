Asian Stocks Set for Cautious Open as Yields Climb: Markets Wrap
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-20 22:40:47

(Bloomberg) -- Stocks in Asia look poised for a cautious open after another down day for US equities, with Treasury yields at the highest level since the global financial crisis.Futures for Japanese shares were mixed while those for Australia were down and Hong Kong contracts pointed to small gains. Wariness around economic challenges saw the S&P 500 swing from a gain of more than 1% to a loss of almost the same.Hawkish remarks from Federal Reserve officials and swaps pricing in a 5% peak policy rate in 2023 should continue to support the dollar against its major peers and emerging-market currencies.
