PTIThere was also a plan to construct helipads every 200-300 km along the highways and talks are on with the Union Surface Transport Ministry, Scindia said.Helipads will be constructed in every district of the country for providing help swiftly when incidents like chopper crash take place, Union Civil Aviation Minister Jyotiraditya Scindia said here on Thursday. Discussions were going on with the state governments in this regard, he told reporters. "The recent incident of a helicopter crash in Kedarnath is unfortunate and work is on to provide immediate relief during such accidents.
