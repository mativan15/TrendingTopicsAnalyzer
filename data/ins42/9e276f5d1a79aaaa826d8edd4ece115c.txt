League of Legends and Valorant developer Riot Games has acquired Wargaming's Sydney studio. The newly rebranded Riot Sydney will leverage the development team's expertise in live service games infrastructure. Riot focuses on live service titles, so an acquisition from a developer with a similar free-to-play model makes sense.
