Election: San Jose mayoral candidates make final push amid attack ads, historic spending
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-06-05 13:21:12

With just three days left to make their case for becoming mayor of the nation's 10th largest city, the top candidates and their supporters are making a final push to round up votes and in some instances take last shots at each other.
Fighting to take the seat San Jose Mayor Sam Liccardo will vacate when he terms out at the end of the year are: Santa Clara County Supervisor Cindy Chavez, San Jose council members Matt Mahan, Dev Davis and Raul Peralez, retired San Jose police officer Jim Spence and students Travis Hill and Marshall Woodmansee.
