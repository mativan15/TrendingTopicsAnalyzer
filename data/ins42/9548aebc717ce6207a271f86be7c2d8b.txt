AgenciesTata-owned Air India has received over 1,752 applications for pilots and 72,000 applications for cabin staff in the last two months. The airline, which has aggressive expansion plans, also revealed that it got over 25,000 application in just a week from postgraduates in management with three years of experience in ground-based business jobs. Furthermore, the airline received over 2,000 applications for various positions at a new technology center in Kerala, including developers, architects, cyber security specialists, program managers, and UX visual designers.
