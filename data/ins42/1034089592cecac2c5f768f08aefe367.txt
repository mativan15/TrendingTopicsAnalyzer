AgenciesThe Maharashtra government has directed all its departments to not carry out transfer of officials till June 30.The Maharashtra government has directed all its departments to not carry out transfer of officials till June 30. An order issued by the state government's general administration department on Friday said essential transfers due to administrative reasons can be done only after permission is taken from Chief Minister Uddhav Thackeray.
