David Becker/Getty Images News Just a little over a year ago we discussed Alibaba (NYSE:BABA) in an article titled: Alibaba: Is The Reward Worth The Risk? In that piece we concluded: on one hand, BABA appears to be a slam dunk strong buy with all the blocks checked... On the other hand, Chinese regulators' hands are all over this company right now and we are unsure if there are any more major blows headed BABA's way. Furthermore, its founder has fallen from grace in Chinese society just as e-commerce competition is increasing in the country.
