A dog standing up for his new puppy sibling has the internet in hysterics.In a video shared to TikTok on September 29 by user @sammurr0214 Levi the cocker spaniel can be seen jumping in to defend his tiny sister Olive, who is being told off for shredding a paper towel.TikTokers loved Levi and his big-brother energy, with the clip receiving 8.8 million views and more than 2 million likes.
