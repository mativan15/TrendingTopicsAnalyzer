Giorgia Meloni faces economic storm as she prepares to take helm in Italy
ft.com
news
2022-10-19 04:00:44

Until midsummer, Italian ceramics company Saxa Gres was on a high. It had record first-half sales of €50mn — up from €43mn for all of 2021 — as demand for its cobblestones and faux stone paving slabs surged in the wake of the post-coronavirus construction recovery. But in July, Francesco Borgomeo, the company's president, shut down its three kilns and furloughed 500 employees as soaring gas prices made production economically unviable. 'I had no other option,' he said. 'I had to protect the company.
