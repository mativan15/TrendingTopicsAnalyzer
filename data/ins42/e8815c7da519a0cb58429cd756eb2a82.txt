GoGame Announces Investment in Slime Royale
globenewswire.com

2022-05-30 00:00:00

CHIBA, Japan, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- goGame recently announced an investment in NFT Game: Slime Royale - the NFT game with Action RPG style, adorable 3D graphics and a balanced economic system. Although the crypto market is undergoing a difficult period, Slime Royale still won over hundreds of candidates and received a generous investment from goGame. CEO & Founder of goGame, Mr. David Ng speaks highly of Slime Royale and shared their reasons of investing in Slime Royale, "We strongly believe in Slime Royale team's ability to create fun and high-quality games and we are particularly impressed by Slime Royale's ability to create a robust game economy management system that aims to balance and maintain the coin value for a longer period of time, which is not seen in other live games in the market so far.
