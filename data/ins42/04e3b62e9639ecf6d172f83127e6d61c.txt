Charlotte area's largest private companies led by Hendrick Automotive, ATD, Alex Lee
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 17:52:00

The most recent Charlotte Business Journal ranks locally based private companies by 2021 revenue. The 50 companies on the list represent a range of industries, including automotive dealership groups, retailers, homebuilders and general contractors.

Hendrick Automotive Group tops the list with 2021 revenue of $11 billion. Hendrick Automotive has about 261 local employees and about 10,800 total in the company. It is followed by Huntersville-based American Tire Distributors Inc., with revenue estimated at $5 billion in 2021.
