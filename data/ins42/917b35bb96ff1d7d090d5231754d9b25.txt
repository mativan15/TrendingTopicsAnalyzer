Tesla stock falls 5% after missing revenue expectations
msn.com
news
2022-10-19 20:10:23

Electric car giant Tesla (TSLA), reported its Q3 earnings after the bell on Wednesday, missing analysts' expectations on revenue, but slightly beating on earnings. Here are the most important numbers from the report. Q3 Revenue: $21.45 billion versus $22.09 billion expected Q3 Adjusted EPS: $1.05 versus $1.01 expected Shares of the automaker fell more than 5% following the announcement. The company says it still expects to see 50% average annual growth rate on vehicle deliveries for the year. The automaker also said it believes it has enough liquidity to continue to build out its roadmap.
