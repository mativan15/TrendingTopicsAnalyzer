Jun. 01, 2022 4:37 AM ETNIOBy: Gaurav Batavia, SA News Editor Andy Feng/iStock Editorial via Getty Images NIO (NIO) added 34 battery swap stations, 37 supercharging stations, 20 destination charging stations and 10,000 third-party charging piles to its charging map in May despite lockdown in shutdown, where COVID-19 caused temporary closure of business. The company battery swap stations now totals 949 in May, with 245 of them located along highways, according to information it shared on its mobile app.
