May 27, 2022 03:50 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hess Corporation (NYSE: HES) announced today that John Hess, Chief Executive Officer, will participate in a fireside chat at Bernstein's 38th Annual Strategic Decisions Conference on Thursday, June 2, 2022, at 10:00 a.m. Eastern Time.

A live audio webcast and a replay of the presentation will be accessible via Hess Corporation's website.

Hess Corporation is a leading global independent energy company engaged in the exploration and production of crude oil and natural gas.
