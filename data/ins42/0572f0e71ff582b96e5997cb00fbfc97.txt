Prairie Gardens Adventure Farm struggles to move produce after reopening in Sturgeon County
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-22 02:16:26

This October has seen warmer weather than usual and people have been taking advantage of it — but one popular U-pick farm hasn't seen those benefits.
Prairie Gardens & Adventure Farm has all of its pumpkins on display — but this late into the season, many should have already been purchased and taken home.That hasn't happened because it's been a bumpy season at the farm near Bon Accord, about 25 kilometres north of Edmonton.
