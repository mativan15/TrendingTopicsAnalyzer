October 19, 2022 07:58 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Grayling, the world's local communications firm, announced the appointments of Robert 'Bob' Conrad as Executive Vice President and Alla Shkiler as Senior Vice President. They will provide senior counsel to U.S. and international clients on confronting societal challenges and articulating how they are doing so.



Based in Washington, D.C., their appointments bolster Grayling's public and corporate affairs capabilities in the region.
