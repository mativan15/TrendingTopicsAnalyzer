TORONTO, May 30, 2022 /CNW/ - SiriusXM Canada, the country's leading audio entertainment company, is pleased to announce that Michelle Mearns has joined as Vice President of Programming and Operations, reporting to CEO Mark Redmond. She will be responsible for the development and production of all Canadian programming as well as technical operations.

"Michelle is a respected leader in the industry and is going to bring an experienced and fresh perspective to our business," said Redmond.
