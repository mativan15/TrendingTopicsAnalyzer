New Delhi: The pandemic brought the urgent need for enterprises across the spectrum to adopt new and emerging technologies. The global banking sector has been at the forefront in investing in the latest technologies like AI/ML, advanced analytics and Metaverse.NatWest, a major retail and commercial bank in the UK, has made strong progress over the last 4-5 years embracing technologies like private and public Cloud, RPA tools, AI/ML, Blockchain and Metaverse.According to Amit Sakhuja, Head of Risk Analytics, Technology, Natwest group, will further unlock value for its customers, while ensuring that these new ways of doing things have consumer protection in mind.
