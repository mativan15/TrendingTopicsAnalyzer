May 31, 2022 05:45 PM Eastern Daylight Time BENTONVILLE, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Walmart Inc. (NYSE: WMT) will hold its Associate Celebration on Friday, June 3, 2022. Following this meeting, the company will host a Question-and-Answer session with the investment community. The session will be led by Walmart President and CEO Doug McMillon and will begin at approximately 12:30 p.m. CDT. The event will be webcast live and accessible by logging onto https://corporate.walmart.com/newsroom/financial-events and selecting the 2022 Q&A Session with the Investment Community event.
