By Prakash VLMCC visits site, prepares plan, and asks for funding to put end to overflowing of Mariyappana Lake There is a growing focus on rejuvenating rajakaluves as many neighbourhoods in Mysuru and Bengaluru are getting flooded due to either their shrinkage or encroachment. Now, the Mysuru City Corporation (MCC) has decided to find a permanent solution to the overflowing of Mariyappana Lake.
 The city corporation is clearing the encroachment of the rajakaluve that connects Mariyappana Lake and Lingambudhi Lake.
