TORONTO, Oct. 19, 2022 /CNW/ - Today, CIBC celebrates two exceptional leaders on being named recipients of this year's Women's Executive Network (WXN) Canada's Most Powerful Women: Top 100 Awards.

CIBC Logo (CNW Group/CIBC)

Kikelomo Lawal, Executive Vice-President and Chief Legal Officer was selected in the Professionals Award category. For more than 25 years, Kikelomo has worked as a lawyer both on Wall Street and Bay Street, as in-house counsel, and currently leads our global Administration team, while overseeing our enterprise-wide Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) strategy.
