Best Convertible for 2022

I 100% stand by my belief that convertibles make excellent cars. They're stylish, irresistibly fun to drive and it's a shame that they're not more popular. The Mazda Miata that I drive continually surprises me with how much it has to offer with its tiny package and nonfixed roof.These drop-top machines are fun, often boast zippy powertrains and are just plain different compared to your average car. Yes, sometimes they leak when you go through the car wash and keeping a soft top clean is a major pain, but that's all a small price to pay for the eternal grin I have on my face whenever I get behind the wheel of a drop-top.
