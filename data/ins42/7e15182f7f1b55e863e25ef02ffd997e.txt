3 Ways Leaders Must Give Meaning To Work In The Office
inc.com
business
2022-06-03 14:11:54

With roaring inflation and low unemployment, business leaders are struggling to get their people to return to the office -- in the U.S., in April, office occupancy rates seemed to have peaked at 43% according to Bloomberg. After all, why would employees want to burn through a $100 tank of gas twice a week and suffer in heavy traffic when they could work from home?To be sure, people who make things or can only do their jobs by serving people in person must work in close proximity. However, for workers who do not need to be in-person, the survival of companies during the pandemic demonstrates that many businesses could operate without offices.
