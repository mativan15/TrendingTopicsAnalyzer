ZHU HAIPENG/FOR CHINA DAILY US balance sheet trimming effects possible to be offset with growth The US Federal Reserve's balance sheet reduction, starting from Wednesday, could have a spillover effect of eroding global asset prices and driving speculative money back to the United States, including some from China, experts said on Tuesday.
