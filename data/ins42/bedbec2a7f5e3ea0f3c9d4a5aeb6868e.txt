The Premier League giants were due to face off in the midweek fixtures but will now be the only fixture that fails to go ahead on Matchday 12 - and Mirror Football explains whyArsenal's much-anticipated Premier League clash with Manchester City on October 19 is no longer going ahead - but all the other 18 top fight clubs will take part in the midweek round of fixtures. With the 2022 World Cup in Qatar taking place next month, the Premier League fixture schedule has become more hectic than ever throughout October, with Matchday 12 representing the second round of midweek games already this season - but the Gunners and the Citizens will not be involved.
