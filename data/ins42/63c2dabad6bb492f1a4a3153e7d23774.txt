SURVEY: Small businesses expect a recession, but few are prepared
prnewswire.com
news
2022-10-20 13:00:00

As majority of owners look to cut costs, roughly half are considering axing insurance – a dangerous business move
Highlights:

According to the latest Nationwide Agency Forward survey, 70% of small business owners expect a recession within the next 6 months, but significantly fewer feel prepared to weather it.
More than half are considering where they can cut costs – some business owners are contemplating changes that could impact the jobs market.
A risky business play, about half of business owners surveyed said they would consider decreasing current insurance coverage to reduce operational expenses.
