West Bengal: Forward Bloc ex-MLA Victor joins Congress
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-17 23:01:23

FORMER FORWARD Bloc MLA from North Bengal, Ali Imran Ramz (Victor) on Monday joined the Congress in the presence of state Congress president Adhir Chowdhury.
Victor reached Vidhan Bhavan around 2 pm on Monday. He was accompanied by his uncle and former minister and Forward Blok leader Hafiz Alam Sairani. Sairani is also likely to join the Congress in future, party sources claimed.
Forward Bloc is a partner of the Left Front. Asked why he switched loyalties, Victor said, 'I did not shift from left to right.
