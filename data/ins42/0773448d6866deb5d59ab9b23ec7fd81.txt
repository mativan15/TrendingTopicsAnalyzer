Man City conversation about Liverpool fans in dressing room speaks volumes
irishmirror.ie
news
2022-10-22 10:02:26

Manchester City defender Manuel Akanji has revealed he was stunned by the lack of atmosphere at Anfield ahead of kick-off before their recent clash with Liverpool - only to be shocked by the noise when the game kicked off. City have enjoyed some sensational battles with Jurgen Klopp's side both in Merseyside and in Manchester down the years, but do not boast the best record when it comes to games on the Reds' stomping ground. Last weekend's meeting between the two teams marked the first time Akanji has featured in this fixture.
