Giorgia Meloni to be sworn in this weekend as Italy's first female prime minister
irishtimes.com
world
2022-10-21 00:00:00

Right-wing politician Giorgia Meloni is to become the first woman to lead Italy as the euro zone's third-largest economy wrestles with a severe energy crisis because of Russia's invasion of Ukraine.Ms Meloni, whose party won the largest share of the vote in elections last month, succeeds Mario Draghi, whose coalition collapsed in July. The chief of Brothers of Italy, a party with post-fascist roots, will be formally sworn in as Italy's prime minister during a ceremony on Saturday.'We're ready to give this nation a government as quickly as possible,' Ms Meloni said after a meeting on Friday morning with President Sergio Mattarella, who later invited her to form a cabinet.
