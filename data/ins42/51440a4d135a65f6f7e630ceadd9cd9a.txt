Motilal Oswal invests Rs 260 crore in Urbanrise-Alliance Group
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 11:45:00

Private equity fund, Motilal Oswal has invested Rs 260 cr.in Urbanrise-Alliance Group, Chennai-based developer for its three upcoming residential projects. The funds were raised towards three upcoming projects - 30 acres villa project on Kanakapura Road, Bangalore, the 7 acres high-rise apartment project in Whitefield, Bangalore and the 17.5 acres of high-rise apartment project in Nizampet, Hyderabad. 
Manoj Namburu, Chairman and Managing Director of Urbanrise and Alliance Group, said, ' This has been possible due to our track record of promptly repaying every single of our loans taken over the last 18 years and even prepaying loans ahead of the loan tenure.
