Tamil Nadu Finance Minister Palanivel Thiaga Rajan presented the first supplementary estimates for ₹3,795.72 crore for 2022-23 in the Assembly on Tuesday.He said the government had sanctioned the amount and of it, ₹500 crore would go to the State Transport Undertakings (STUs) as a share capital assistance to create assets in the transport sector and ₹550 crore to Tamil Nadu Urban Finance and Infrastructure Development Corporation (TUFIDCO) for upgrading roads. The Minister said the Greater Chennai Corporation and the Directorate of Municipal Administration would get ₹373.
