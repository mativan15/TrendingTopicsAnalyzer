Apple gears up for WWDC 2022: From iOS 16 to macOS, here's what to expect
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-27 18:45:00

Apple is all set to kickstart its much-awaited Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) on June 6. The company has made the program available to over 30 million Apple developers through various online events that are free. In the run-up to the grand tech event, Apple unveiled its lineup of products that will be introduced at the WWDC. The free for all developers event will begin with a keynote followed by a series of events. The conference will help developers to learn about the latest technologies, tools, and frameworks that will be introduced on iOS, macOS, iPadOS, watchOS, and tvOS.
