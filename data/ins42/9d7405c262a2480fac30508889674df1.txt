Teacher at private Winnipeg Christian school on leave pending investigation into conduct
cbc.ca
news
2022-06-01 00:29:00

The board chairperson of The King's School in Winnipeg says allegations of inappropriate behaviour by a teacher were made earlier this year and are being investigated. (The King's School/Facebook)A private Christian school in Winnipeg has placed one of its teachers on leave while an investigation is conducted into the teacher's conduct at school.The move follows allegations of inappropriate behaviour that were made earlier this year about the teacher at The King's School — a school on Gateway Road in northeast Winnipeg that is affiliated with Gateway Church — the school's board chairperson says.
