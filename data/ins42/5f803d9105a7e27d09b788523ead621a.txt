[Photo by Zhang Kaixin/For China Daily] BEIJING -- Some 110.36 billion yuan ($15.52 billion) of treasury bonds were issued on the Beijing Stock Exchange in September, according to data from the bourse. The Beijing bourse started treasury bond sales on September 2. The launch of treasury bonds is a key move to improve the bourse's market functions and promote its high-quality development, which is also conducive to giving the capital market an improved role in serving the real economy, the bourse said.
