Gunshots and explosions as fire tears through Iran's notorious Evin prison

news
2022-10-16 05:46:49

Gunshots and explosions were heard as a huge fire broke out in Iran most notorious prison used to house political prisoners and anti-government activists. 
 

 © Provided by The Telegraph Flames and thick smoke rising from the Evin prison, in the northwest of the Iranian capital Tehran


As the Iranian regime faces mass protests over the death of a young woman at the hands of the morality police, a huge blaze engulfed Tehran's Evin prison, where British prisoner Nazanin Zaghari-Ratcliffe was previously held.
