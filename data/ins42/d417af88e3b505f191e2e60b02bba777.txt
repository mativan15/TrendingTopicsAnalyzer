One of the biggest challenges researchers face when building artificial intelligence (AI) is predicting what actions will be taken by a machine.This issue is very important, as decisions made by humans are far more advanced than those made by machines. For example, a human pilot would not enter a flight simulator and start flying an airplane; nor would he enter a motorboat and begin swimming through the water.The pilot would get in the boat or plane and fly or swim because these devices have engines that provide power for them to move.
