This past week, the SHIB army lost its commander when Ryoshi—the pseudonymous founder of the heralded cryptocurrency meme coin, Shiba Inu—mysteriously vanished from social media.It was not without a trace. A profile apparently belonging to Ryoshi left two cryptic blog posts on Medium, totaling only five sentences, which seemed to bid farewell to Shiba Inu fans.'Every Shibarmy is Ryoshi. It does not represent someone, but the glory of Shibarmy! . . . Take the SHIBA and journey upwards frens,' the profile wrote, using meme jargon for the word 'friends.
