185-Year-Old P&G Continues To Change With The Times
forbes.com
news
2022-06-02 11:30:00

P&G got its start in 1837 when William Procter, a British candlemaker, and James Gamble, an Irish soap-maker, merged their respective businesses in Cincinnati to create Procter & Gamble. Today, while not quite 200 years old, the company shines a light on the critical importance of continually embracing change, particularly as it pertains to the evolution of an organization's employee experience. A case in point is the introduction of P&G + Me, a refreshed way for the company to define its employee value proposition.
