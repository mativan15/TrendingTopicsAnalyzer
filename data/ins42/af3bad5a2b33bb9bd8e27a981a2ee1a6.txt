Maharashtra records 536 COVID-19 cases, no deaths; active cases cross 2,500
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 15:00:00

AgenciesThe case fatality rate in Maharashtra is 1.87 per cent.Maharashtra on Friday recorded 536 new coronavirus infections -- the highest one-day rise in cases since March 3 -- but zero pandemic-related fatalities, the health department said. The tally of active cases crossed the 2,500-mark, it said. On March 3, the state had recorded 544 new infections. The state's COVID-19 caseload rose to 78,84,865 while death toll stood unchanged at 1,47,858. 
The case fatality rate in Maharashtra is 1.87 per cent.
