Newcastle artist creates huge floor mural on disused quayside bus lane

2022-10-15 11:50:22

Image caption, Alex Mulholland said it was "just a bit of colour, a bit of fun"A street artist has created a huge, 90-metre (300ft) floor mural along a city's quayside.Alex Mulholland painted the work on the disused bus lane by the River Tyne over three weeks.The mural is "the longest pavement art installation in Newcastle and to our knowledge in the UK", business development agency NE1 said.Mr Mulholland said it was a "bit of a pinch me moment" to have his work "in front of the best view in the world".
