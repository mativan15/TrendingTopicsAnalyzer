'Act East', 'Neighbourhood First' policies to have impact beyond South Asia: S Jaishankar
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 11:30:00

PTIEAM addresses the inauguration of 3rd Natural Allies in Development and Interdependence (NADI) - Asian Confluence River conclave 2022, in Guwahati.External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar on Saturday said that the coming together of 'Act East' and 'Neighbourhood First' policies will have an enormous reinforcing impact for the country beyond the confines of South Asia. Addressing the Natural Allies in Development and Interdependence (NADI) conclave here, Jaishankar said the realisation of this is evident from the potential of BIMSTEC on the Bay of Bengal region.
