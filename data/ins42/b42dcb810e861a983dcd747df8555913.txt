getty When it comes to demonstrating empathy, there's something to be said about the phrase, 'Walk a mile in someone else's shoes.' Many of us have been told this at some point and hopefully changed for the better. I believe that the best innovators lead by example and address problems at the root, which is why this concept is fundamental for business leaders to understand figuratively and literally.
