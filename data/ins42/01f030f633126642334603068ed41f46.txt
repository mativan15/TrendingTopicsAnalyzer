John Moore/Getty Images News As a dividend growth investor, I constantly seek new income-producing investments to increase my income stream. Sometimes I allocate capital to existing positions, and other times I acquire shares in new companies to increase my diversification. The current bear market can be an opportunity to gain more income for a more attractive price. One elusive segment for many dividend growth investors is the basic materials sector. Companies there tend to rely heavily on the prices of commodities and become highly profitable when commodity prices are up but may lose money during recessions when demand is low.
