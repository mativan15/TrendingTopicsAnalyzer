Tamil Nadu: Civic workers to go on indefinite strike from October 25
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 06:45:00

COIMBATORE: Conservancy workers engaged on contract by the municipal corporation announced an indefinite strike from October 25 after the council reportedly failed to pass a resolution to hike their wages.The council on Wednesday passed a special resolution to send a recommendation to the state government to increase the wages, much to the chagrin of the workers, who were expecting immediate steps to hike wages."We expected the council to pass a resolution to increase the wages from Rs 330 to Rs 721, but it passed a resolution to make recommendations to the state government.
