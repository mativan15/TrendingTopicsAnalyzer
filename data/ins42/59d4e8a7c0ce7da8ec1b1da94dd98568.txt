Two Games- Two Wins To Start Providence Bruin Season
iheart.com
news
2022-10-17 18:15:00

The Providence Bruins are off to a good start opening the 2022-23 hockey season with an impressive 3-1 win over the Bridgeport Islanders on Friday night. Providence scored all their goals in the first period and within a three minute span. Saturday night, they traveled to Springfield to take on the division rival Springfield Thunderbirds. The Bruins took the Sunday afternoon game 4-3 over The Thunderbirds. After a 1 all tie in the first period, they scored three times in the second period and then played solid defense the rest of the way.
