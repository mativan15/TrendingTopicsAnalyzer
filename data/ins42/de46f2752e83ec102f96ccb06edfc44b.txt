By Reuters Published: 14:40 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 14:40 EDT, 16 October 2022 PARIS, Oct 16 (Reuters) - The French government is set to pass the 2023 budget bill using special constitutional powers that would allow it to bypass a vote in parliament, Prime Minister Elisabeth Borne said on Sunday. The government will likely use the procedure, under article 49.3 of the constitution, Borne said in an interview on French TV channel TF1, without specifying a date but ruling out its use on Monday.
