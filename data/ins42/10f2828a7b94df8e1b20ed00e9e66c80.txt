BEIJING & CAMBRIDGE, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--CANbridge Pharmaceuticals Inc. (HKEX:1228), a China-based global biopharmaceutical company committed to the research, development and commercialization of transformative rare disease and rare oncology therapies, announced that it will participate in and present at the Jefferies Global Healthcare Conference 2022 on Friday, June 10, 2022 at 12:45 PM ET.A live webcast of will be available on the 'Presentations & Webcasts' located in the Investor page of the Company's website.
