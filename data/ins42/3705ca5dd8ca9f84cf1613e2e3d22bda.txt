As the effects of climate change become more evident and widely accepted and environmental, social and governance issues — usually shortened to ESG — become more important to investors, many corporate giants are making pledges to reach net-zero emissions by certain dates.

We've got a couple of stories highlighting this trend today. FedEx Corp. is planning to build out electric vehicle charging infrastructure as it expands its own EV fleet. And our top story today concerns a multibillion-dollar acquisition of a renewable natural gas company as a key component of BP PLC's own net-zero ambitions.
