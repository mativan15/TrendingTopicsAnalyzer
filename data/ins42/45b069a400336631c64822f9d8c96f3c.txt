Japan's Sojitz Corporation sells 12.8 crore shares in Samvardhana Motherson
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 18:00:00

ETMarkets.comSamvardhana Motherson came into existence as a result of proposed restructuring in erstwhile Motherson SumiSojitz Corporation sold 12.8 crore equity shares or 2.83% stake in Samvardhana Motherson on Tuesday, showed BSE bulk deals data. The transaction was executed at an average traded price of Rs 64.53. This was at a significant discount in comparison with the previous day's closing price of Rs 69.2 per share. As of the quarter ended June, the entity held 3.46% stake or 15,61,25,499 shares in the company.
