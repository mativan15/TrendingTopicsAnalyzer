Advertising has been around forever, and it's pretty good at evolving to find potential customers where they like to gather. That is a big part of why advertising budgets are steadily going from old-fashioned mediums like print and network television to digital ones like streaming and the internet. Streaming company Roku (ROKU -4.00%) and ad-tech platform The Trade Desk (TTD -2.06%) have differences, but digital advertising sits at the core of what they both do.
The core for these two companies is facing some macroeconomic pressure at the moment as inflation slows consumer spending and rapidly rising interest rates lower short-term advertising forecasts.
