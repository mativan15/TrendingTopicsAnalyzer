Asia stocks edge higher, dollar in the doldrums
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 02:15:00

MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan fell 0.95%, Australian shares slid 1.53% and Japan's Nikkei stock index fell 2.08%.Asian stocks followed Wall Street higher on Monday while the dollar was pinned near five-week lows as investors wagered on an eventual slowdown in U.S. monetary tightening, albeit after sharp hikes in June and July. Helping to mellow the mood was news that Shanghai authorities will cancel many conditions for businesses to resume work from Wednesday, easing a city-wide lock down that began two months ago.
