PTILucknow: Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath with Bollywood actors Akshay Kumar, Manushi Chhillar and director Chandraprakash Dwivedi after a special screening of the movie 'Samrat Prithviraj' at Lok Bhawan in Lucknow. (PTI Photo/Nand Kumar) (Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath, who attended a special screening of period action drama "Samrat Prithviraj" with his cabinet colleagues, on Thursday declared the movie tax-free in the state. The screening of the movie was held at Lok Bhawan here, and Bollywood star Akshay Kumar, female lead Manushi Chillar and director Chandra Prakash Dwivedi were in attendance.
