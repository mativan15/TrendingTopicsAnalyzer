Crypto Platforms Must Be Licensed In 2023 To Operate Legally: South African Financial Regulator
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-20 10:55:48

"We are not legitimising crypto assets," Eugene Du Toit said.(Representational)Johannesburg: Cryptocurrency financial companies in South Africa will need to apply for a licence between June 1 and Nov. 20, 2023, in order to operate legally, the country's financial conduct regulator said on Thursday.A declaration on Wednesday that crypto assets are financial products does not mean that they are legal tender, Eugene Du Toit, head of the Financial Sector Conduct Authority's Regulatory Frameworks Department, said at a press conference.
