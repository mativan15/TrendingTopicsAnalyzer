The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Monday arrested Delhi Power Minister Satyendar Jain in a money-laundering case. The case is based on a CBI first information report lodged against the AAP leader in 2017 under the Prevention of Corruption Act where he was accused of having laundered money through four companies allegedly linked to him.
