CHICAGO (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 CSG Government Solutions, a national leader in government program modernization, today announced that it has been selected to provide Project Management Office and Organizational Change Management services for the Connecticut Department of Children and Families' (DCF) Comprehensive Child Welfare Information System (CCWIS) implementation.
