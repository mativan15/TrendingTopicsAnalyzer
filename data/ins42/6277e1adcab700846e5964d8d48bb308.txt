Zuckerberg's Metaverse Bet Is More Than Experimental
fool.com
news
2022-10-21 09:31:00

Pretty much everything about Meta Platforms (META -1.28%) and CEO Mark Zuckerberg is divisive these days. So it comes as no surprise that the company's new premium virtual reality (VR) headset, Meta Quest Pro, was received with disdain by many in the media. Hating on VR, the metaverse, and the company's attempt to build a 3D app future is an easy way to get clicks -- especially from those who are unsure of what the metaverse even is in the first place. 
But that's really a shame, because Zuckerberg isn't pursuing the metaverse just for kicks.
