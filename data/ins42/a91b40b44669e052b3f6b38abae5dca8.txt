Gary 'Spike' O'Sullivan has boxed with the likes of Chris Eubank Jr, Jaime Munguia and Billy Joe Saunders, and has a chance to become world champion against Erislandy Lara tomorrow night

Irish boxer Gary 'Spike' O'Sullivan thinks Conor McGregor would be afraid to call him out, even if he brings home a world title tomorrow night.

O'Sullivan is one of the country's most prolific boxers in recent years, and has only suffered defeat at the hands of world champions Billy Joe Saunders, Jaime Munguia and David Lemieux, as well as Chris Eubank Jr.
