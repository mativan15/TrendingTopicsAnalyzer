"Nature's Wild Ideas" is a love letter to nature—complete with a deep message of conservation. Lobster eyes inspired an X-ray telescope that is currently on a satellite sailing through space toward Mercury. Blue mussels made the ideal source of inspiration for a non-toxic glue. If we are to continue learning from the creatures around us, we must protect their untamed homelands.
