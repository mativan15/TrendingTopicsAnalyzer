County Schools, Businesses, Government Agencies to Partake in Quake Drill

2022-10-20 15:00:00

LOS ANGELES (CNS) - People in government offices, businesses and schools throughout Los Angeles County will stop everything for a minute Thursday to "drop, cover and hold on" during a statewide earthquake preparedness drill, now in its 14th year. The Great California ShakeOut of 2022 is scheduled for 10:20 a.m. Thursday. "What we do to prepare now, before the this big earthquake, will determine how well we can survive and recover," according to a statement posted to ShakeOut.org. "Great ShakeOut Earthquake drills are annual opportunities to learn and practice earthquake safety with millions of people.
