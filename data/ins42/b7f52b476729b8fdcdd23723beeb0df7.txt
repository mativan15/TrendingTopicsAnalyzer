By WCCO Staff October 20, 2022 / 1:34 PM / CBS Minnesota WABASHA COUNTY, Minn. -- Prosecutors sentenced a 39-year-old man to nearly six years in prison for criminal sexual conduct involving minors, Wabasha County Attorney Karrie Kelly announced Thursday.John Trainor, of Millville, was found guilty of two counts of criminal sexual conduct in the third degree and one count of the use of a minor in a sexual performance.During a hearing plea, Trainor admitted that in 2019 he gave a 15-year-old girl alcohol before having sex with her in his car.
