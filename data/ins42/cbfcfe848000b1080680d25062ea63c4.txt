‘Gentle density': Queensland premier highlights proposed solution to housing crisis
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-20 07:47:06

The eponymous home of the Queenslander is one of space, light and air – sprawling timber houses with wide verandas surrounded by lawn and gardens.The good people of the sunshine state, however, have been told they will need to embrace more dense forms of living if they want the state to navigate a path out of its housing crisis.But don't worry, they're told, it will be gentle.In her closing speech to the affordable housing summit on Thursday, the state's premier, Annastacia Palaszczuk, said the key words of the day were 'gentle density'.
