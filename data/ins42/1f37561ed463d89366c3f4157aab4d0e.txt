Dog parade, pumpkin decorating at Oaktober Harvest festival
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-10-16 23:13:49

The Oakley Recreation Department has revamped its annual Oaktober Harvest Festival for more family fun.
The 14th annual event is set for Oct. 22 from noon to 4 p.m. in Civic Center Plaza., 3231 Main St.
This year the fall festival will include beer and wine, a family-friendly costume parade, the iconic canine parade, a pumpkin-decorating contest, and more.
Stop by Civic Center Plaza to celebrate the fall season with more than 30 vendors, food trucks, and more Oaktober Harvest Festival entertainment.
