(AP Photo/Steve Helber) (Steve Helber / Associated Press)Women's golf is on the rise, a momentum swing not lost on fans and sponsors.With an abundance of emerging stars gaining notoriety, the sport is drawing more attention — and sponsorships — in the post-COVID era. Tickets sales for this week's U.S. Women's Open are up more than 50% over the three-year, pre-COVID average, according to the USGA.
