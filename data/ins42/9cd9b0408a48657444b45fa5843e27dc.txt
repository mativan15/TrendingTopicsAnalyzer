Do I Lose Home Equity After Refinancing?
cnet.com
tech
2022-10-18 18:16:00

Though refinancing won't reduce your house's value or your home equity but there are a number of factors that require careful consideration before undertaking one. In recent years, homeowners have been able to take advantage of record-low interest rates, and data from Bankrate shows that around 25% of homeowners refinanced their mortgages in 2020 and 2021. But things have changed since then, as a series of Federal interest rate hikes have nudged mortgage and refinance rates higher. In the current environment, it's crucial to take a hard look at your home equity, your home's value and all closing costs and fees before making the decision to refinance.
