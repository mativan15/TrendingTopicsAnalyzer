June 02, 2022 10:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time FORT LEE, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cross River Bank (Cross River), a technology infrastructure provider that offers embedded financial solutions, today announced that it has once again been named one of the 2022 Best Places to Work in Financial Technology for the 5th straight year. A project of Arizent and Best Companies Group, the awards program was created in 2017 and designed to identify, recognize and honor the best employers in the financial technology industry.
