'Sex-to-Earn' Startup Thinks People Will Fuck for Crypto
vice.com
news
2022-06-01 13:00:00

The Web3 boom has generated a plethora of complex token-based business models that promise riches: From play-to-earn, to move-to-earn, and now sex-to-earn. A cryptocurrency startup called SEXN (most likely a play on the popular move-to-earn running app STEPN) is hoping to convince people that sex-to-earn is not only functionally possible—through a series of biometric monitors, apps, and NFTs—but profitable. SEXN offers 'two of the indispensable things that humans love most: sex and money,' according to the company's Twitter bio.
