Published: 17:38 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 17:42 EDT, 16 October 2022 A quarter of a million smart meters made by a firm linked to the Chinese government have been installed in UK homes, sparking fears Beijing could ‘destroy' the national grid.At least three major UK energy suppliers have struck deals with Kaifa Technology UK, which is controlled by a subsidiary of the state-owned China Electronics Corporation (CEC).There are already 250,000 Kaifa meters in homes across the country, according to industry figures obtained by the Daily Mail.
