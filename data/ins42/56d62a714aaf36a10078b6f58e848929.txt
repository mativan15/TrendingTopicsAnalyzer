TORONTO, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- FAX Capital Corp. (TSX: FXC) ('FAX' or the 'Company') today announced that it has filed its management information circular (the 'Circular') and related meeting materials for the annual general and special meeting (the 'Meeting') of holders of subordinate voting shares and multiple voting shares of the Company (the 'Shareholders') to be held on June 24, 2022. At the Meeting, Shareholders will be asked to consider, among other things, the proposed acquisition, indirectly through an acquisition company, by Fax Investments Inc.
