ServisFirst Bancshares Fall After Earnings Miss
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 15:17:00

By Robb M. Stewart ServisFirst Bancshares Inc. dropped Tuesday after the company's quarterly earnings fell short of expectations. In morning trading, the shares were 12% lower, at $76.53. The stock has now fallen almost 10% so far this year. The Alabama-based bank holding company late Monday reported third-quarter earnings of $1.17 a share, up 22% on a year earlier but below the $1.23 consensus forecast of analysts polled by FactSet. Net interest income was $126.4 million for the quarter, compared with $96.
