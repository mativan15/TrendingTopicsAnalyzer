October 21, 2022 12:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time WATERLOO, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Odyssey Interactive, an independent game development studio founded by former Riot Games leads on League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics, has raised $19 million in a series A round led by Makers Fund, with participation by Anthos Capital, The Mini Fund, Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), and angel investor Mitch Lasky. Odyssey Interactive's debut title Omega Strikers, a free-to-play competitive 3v3 knockout striker, has earned acclaim from players globally for its PC Open Beta which launched in September 2022.
