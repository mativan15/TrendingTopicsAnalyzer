The wait is over, Unsolved Mysteries Season 3, known as Unsolved Mysteries Volume 3 has arrived on Netflix.Unsolved Mysteries is long-running documentary series that aired on NBC and CBS from 1987 until 2002. For Netflix, its original creators, John Cosgrove and Terry Dunn Meurer, are back with a modern take on the much-loved series, following the success of volumes 1 and 2 which aired in July and October 2020.The Netflix adaption is also produced by 21 Laps Entertainment, the producers of Stranger Things.
