Obama to campaign for Mandela Barnes in tough Wisconsin Senate race
nbcnews.com
news
2022-10-17 13:26:05

Former President Barack Obama is heading to Wisconsin later this month to help boost Senate candidate Mandela Barnes, a Democrat challenging GOP Sen. Ron Johnson, as well Gov. Tony Evers' re-election bid.The joint event, set for Oct. 29 in Milwaukee, will also promote other Democrats on the November ballot, including Attorney General Josh Kaul and Rep. Gwen Moore. NBC News was first to report the news that Obama would campaign in Wisconsin.'From restoring access to reproductive health care to defending democracy and Wisconsinites' right to vote, the stakes couldn't be higher,' the Wisconsin Democratic Party said in a statement on Friday.
