Finance Minister Katy Gallagher revealed she had been briefed on the East West Link and the Perth Freight Link projects and the cash for both is listed in the budget as contingent liabilities. A decision on both would have to be made after talks with the relevant premiers.Gallagher said the 'huge' infrastructure program, worth $120 billion over 10 years, had to be examined closely.
