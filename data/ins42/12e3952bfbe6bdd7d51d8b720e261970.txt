DOYLESTOWN, Pa., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- On Friday, October 21st, a group of four endurance athletes will embark on a mission to run 50 miles of the Grand Canyon, from the South Rim to North Rim and back again, to raise money and awareness for Travis Manion Foundation (TMF), one of the nation's leading veteran service organizations.
