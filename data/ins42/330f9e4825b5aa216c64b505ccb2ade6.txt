With food prices soaring, consumers on a budget need to be extra resourceful when shopping for groceries. Clipping coupons is still a useful trick, as is shopping with a list to resist impulse buys and enrolling in the store's loyalty rewards program. Another proven practice is to buy certain items in bulk -- like frozen vegetables and canned foods.
