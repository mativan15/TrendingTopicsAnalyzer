The Eagles were plus-3 in the turnover margin during a 26-17 win over the Cowboys that pushed them to 6-0 heading into their bye week

PHILADELPHIA - The Eagles moved to 6-0 for just the third time in their history by outlasting the Dallas Cowboys, 26-17, at Lincoln Financial Field on a Sunday night primetime stage.

What looked like it was going to be a laugher early after Philadelphia jumped out to a 20-0 lead, had some tense moments in the second half before the Eagles restored order on what is starting to become a trademark - a methodical, clock-draining drive in the fourth quarter.
