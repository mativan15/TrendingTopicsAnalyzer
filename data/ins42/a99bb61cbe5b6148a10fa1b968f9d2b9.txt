Why is the online gaming industry booming in India?
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 14:49:43

Online gaming has seen unprecedented growth in the number of users and time spent post covid. Gone are the days when gaming was restricted to consoles, video game centres or desktops that too in the top-tier cities of the country. Today rapid democratisation of video games is led by the adoption of gaming on affordable mobile phones and the internet. Mobile engagement in India is much ahead not just for the gaming industry but in other sectors as well.
According to a recent report by BCG, the gaming industry in India is growing at a CAGR of 38% and is expected to triple to $5Bn+ in mobile gaming revenues by the year 2025.
