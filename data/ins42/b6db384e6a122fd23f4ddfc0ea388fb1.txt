happyphoton/iStock via Getty Images ZIM Integrated Shipping Services (NYSE:ZIM) has been a massive outperformer since its IPO in 2021 and has been hyped multiple times for its outstanding dividend. Although the company had a favorable business environment as a consequence of the pandemic, this may be coming to an end, and institutional investors have been dumping the stock for the past few months, while the stock's short ratio significantly increased. ZIM is not a stock I want to buy in these circumstances and as I will show in this article, the likelihood of lower lows is overshadowing the short-term chances, which leads me to rate the stock as a higher-risk hold position.
