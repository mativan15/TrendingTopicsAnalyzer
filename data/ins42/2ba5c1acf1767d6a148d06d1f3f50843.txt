Anheuser-Busch is in the midst of a two-year, $1 billion investment designed to expand its U.S. operations, enable sustainable innovations, stimulate economic growth and strengthen its connection to local communities.

The plan includes significant upgrades to many of the company's more than 120 facilities around the U.S., where they produce, package and distribute many of the popular beers you've heard of such as Budweiser, Michelob Ultra, Stella and craft beer brands like Goose Island and Kona.
