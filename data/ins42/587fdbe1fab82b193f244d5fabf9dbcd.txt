When you attack firearms, you attack small businesses
washingtontimes.com
news
2022-06-02 22:30:00

OPINION: Over the past decade, I've spoken at the Shot Show in Las Vegas about a half-dozen times. Ever been there? It's quite a spectacle. The conference — which is next scheduled to take place in January 2023 — attracts tens of thousands of visitors. Are these gun nuts? Crazy right-wingers? Fox News devotees? Maybe there are a few. But the great majority of people are small business owners and employees that make their livelihoods from the firearms industry.   Take a walk around and you'll see what I mean.
