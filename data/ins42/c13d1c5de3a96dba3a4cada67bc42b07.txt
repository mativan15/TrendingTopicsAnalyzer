Magic Eden Launches $1 Million Hackathon to Innovate on NFT Creator Monetization and Royalty Tools

SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 14, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- In response to the growing trend away from static creator royalties, Magic Eden, the leading cross-chain NFT platform, today announced that it would be introducing a Creator Monetization Hackathon with up to $1M investment to support the development of pro-NFT royalties and alternative monetization tools on Solana.

Magic Eden (PRNewsfoto/Magic Eden)

As a platform committed to equally serving both collectors and creators, Magic Eden is ready to offer resourcing that will allow creators to contend with a rapidly changing market.
