Accused Sullivan County Child Killer Fires Her Attorney
patch.com
news
2022-10-21 19:29:20

(Tennessee Lookout)By Jamie Satterfield, Tennessee LookoutOctober 21, 2022With her trial looming, a Sullivan County teenaged mom accused of stuffing her 15-month-old daughter inside a trash can where she smothered to death has fired her court-appointed attorney.Sullivan County Criminal Court Judge James Goodwin on Friday allowed Megan Boswell's current attorney to withdraw from her case and appointed a new attorney. The move will delay her Feb. 6, 2023, trial.Boswell sought to fire attorney Brad Sproles at a hearing last month, complaining he was refusing to summon witnesses she believed would help her case.
