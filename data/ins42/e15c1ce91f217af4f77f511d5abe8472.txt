Doral Square

Doral Square, at the southeast corner of Doral Boulevard and 87th Avenue, is now fully leased following several new deals, according to Terra, its developer.

The new tenants include Home Goods (25,800 square feet), Pet Supermarket (5,846 square feet), Paradise Grills (5,244 square feet), One Medical (4,63 square feet), First Watch (3,750 square feet), Nacion Sushi (3,583 square feet), El Toro Loco (2,342 square feet), Lima Bakery (1,475 square feet), European Wax Center (1,435 square feet), Jersey's Mike (1,290 square feet) and AT&T (1,290 square feet).
