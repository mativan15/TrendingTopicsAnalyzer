FEATURES Nekhei Naatza: Israeli Punks Against the State By Yoni Kroll · October 18, 2022 In some ways, not a lot has changed in Israel over the past three decades. The threat of regional war is still constant. Israel still occupies the West Bank and Gaza Strip—land that belongs to the people of Palestine. The Palestinian response to living under this military occupation continues, and violence turns to violence turns to violence. Since the first intifada—Arabic for 'uprising'—started in 1987, more than 12,000 Palestinians and 1,500 Israelis have been killed, with many more thousands injured.
