DUBLIN, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Trade Republic, the largest savings platform in Europe, today launches in the ROI. Residents of Ireland can now use the Trade Republic app or website to jump-start their wealth creation journeys with secure, easy and commission-free access to capital markets.

Trade Republic does not charge its customers any order commission, there is just one euro of external fees to cover clearing and settlement, regardless of the size of the order. Savings plans for stocks and ETFs are offered completely free of charge.
