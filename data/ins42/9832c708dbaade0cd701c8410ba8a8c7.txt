Laredo Petroleum Shares Rise 13% After Board Authorizes Share Buyback
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 14:24:00

By Chris Wack Laredo Petroleum Inc. shares rose 13% to $94.95 after the company said its board of directors authorized a $200 million share repurchase program. The company said the two-year program is authorized through May 27, 2024. Laredo also updated its debt repayment target to $700 million by the end of 2023. The company said it expects to generate $900 million of free cash flow through the end of next year. Laredo said it intends to fund repurchases from available working capital and cash provided by operating activities.
