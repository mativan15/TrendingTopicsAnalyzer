> Life Four Young Pakistani entrepreneurs have been included in the Forbes' '30 under 30' Asia list 2022. The moment of immense pride urges us to share their story with other Pakistani's! Zain Ahmed, Dur-e-Aziz Amna, Muhammad Saad, and Shawana Shah are featured in the business journal for their contributions to e-commerce, enterprise technology, and other industries. Entrepreneurs have been recognized in the categories of art and style, industry and manufacturing, media and marketing, and social effect.
