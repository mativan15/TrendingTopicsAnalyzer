Love Island's Shaughna teases mystery man's identity in sweet pregnancy post

2022-10-20 06:44:22

Love Island star Shaughna Phillips made the surprise announcement she is expecting her first child on Instagram on Wednesday night. The TV personality, 28, who shot to fame when she appeared on the winter series of Love Island in 2020, kept fans guessing about the identity of her mystery boyfriend who was missing from her pregnancy reveal. But Shaughna's eagle-eyed followers noticed her ultrasound scan included her partner's name Billy as the reality star filmed the heart-warming appointment. Above the scan of their baby were the words: "Shaughna and Billy's baby".
