This week, Terra relaunched its Luna token after the dramatic implosion of its cryptocurrency and the associated stablecoin. The new token was airdropped to holders of the previous coin, which has now been renamed Luna Classic. But simply dropping a new coin into customer wallets doesn't undo the tens of billions of dollars worth of damage done by the ecosystem's collapse.
