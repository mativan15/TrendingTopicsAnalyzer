DBUs to boost RBI's financial inclusion efforts: Das
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 03:00:00

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is focused on facilitating technology and innovation in the banking sector for the benefit of taking financial services to the masses, Governor Shaktikanta Das said Sunday. "The establishment of DBUs (Digital Banking Units) is a step to further augment the digital infrastructure in the country," Governor Das said at the launch of 75 DBUs to mark 75 years of independence. "This will act as an enabler in the digital ecosystem and will improve customer experience by facilitating seamless banking transactions.
