TALLINN, Estonia, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The team behind the Zunami Protocol has announced the successful launch of its native $UZD stablecoin. The fully decentralized UZD stablecoin is backed by stable LP tokens from Curve Finance, Convex, Stake DAO, and Goldfinch. By relying on the Zunami Protocol DAO Strategy, users can enjoy high aggregator native APY thanks to the application of the auto-compounding and rebalancing mechanism.The Zunami Protocol is the first cross-chain yield aggregator that works with stablecoins and helps users get the highest rate on the market for providing liquidity in stablecoins.
