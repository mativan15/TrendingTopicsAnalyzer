Roparun 2022Participants in the Roparun prior to the start of the Roparun in Enschede, Netherlands, 04 June 2022. The teams will depart from Twente Airport to cover more than five hundred kilometers in relay form. In two days they will finish on the Rotterdam Coolsingel. The teams use this to raise money for supportive care for people with cancer. EPA/Marco de Swart
