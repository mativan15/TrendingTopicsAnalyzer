Fern Britton started her career in television on local stations before she become the BBC's youngest ever national news presenter; she also hosted iconic shows including Ready Steady Cook. She moved over to the This Morning sofa on ITV where she stayed for 10 years. Latterly, she became a novelist. She lives near Padstow in Cornwall with her daughter Winnie.
How did your childhood influence your attitude to money?
My mum, Ruth, was a very good housekeeper. She had to be. My dad [actor Tony Britton] left her before I was born, so she brought up me and my elder sister, Cherry, alone.
