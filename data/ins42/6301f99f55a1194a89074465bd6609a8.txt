October 20, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SEATTLE--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Workflow Labs, a company focused on helping eCommerce businesses realize their full potential through automated software solutions, today announces the launch of the first phase of its HelpDesk software. HelpDesk, an innovative eCommerce workflow management tool, is an easy-to-use dashboard that will allow businesses operating on the Amazon eCommerce platform to reduce time spent on repetitive tasks, creating more time for strategic growth and scalability.
