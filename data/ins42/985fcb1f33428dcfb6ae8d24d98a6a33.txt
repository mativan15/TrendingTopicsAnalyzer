Sky Glass cashback ends this month (Image: SKY GETTY)Sky is handing out £100 to anyone who buys its custom-designed Sky Glass television this month. The satellite firm will bundle a pre-paid Mastercard with £100 to spend anywhere you want. Sky Glass – a custom-designed 4K QLED TV with everything you need to stream live channels, on-demand boxsets, Netflix and other streaming platforms – launched last year and starts from £13 a month for a 43-inch model. The £100 giveaway is easily one of the best Sky TV deals available .
