Wellington County clerk set to retire after long career in municipal government
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-15 09:01:34

Wellington County Clerk Donna Bryce is retiring after 27 years in public service.
She announced her plans at the Sept. 29 County Council meeting.Bryce has worked at the County of Wellington for the last 12 years. Before that, she was at the Region of Peel and at the Legislative Assembly of Ontario.'I see it as such a privilege to be the county clerk and work with all the members,' said Bryce. 'We worked really hard to develop good relationships between Council and staff.'Warden Kelly Linton said in a statement that, 'Donna has always set an incredibly high standard for herself and her employees.
