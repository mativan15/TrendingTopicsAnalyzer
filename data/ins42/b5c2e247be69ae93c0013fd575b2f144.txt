Political temperatures are rising in Haryana with the ruling BJP, the Aam Aadmi Party, and the Congress holding rallies in the state on Sunday, ahead of the civic polls in June. While the chief minister is holding a rally in Sirsa, Arvind Kejriwal is eyening an expansion of the footprint with the mega Kurukshetra event. Former chief minister Bhupinder Singh Hooda of the Congress is in Fatehabad to touch base with locals.
