October 21, 2022 04:45 PM Eastern Daylight Time PALO ALTO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Corner Growth Acquisition Corp. 2 (NASDAQ: TRONU, TRON, TRONW) ('Corner Growth' or the 'Company') today launched a fixed price tender offer (the 'Tender Offer') to purchase and redeem its Class A Ordinary Shares (the 'Shares') at a purchase price of $10.21 per share. On October 20, 2022, the closing price of the Shares was $10.14 per share. The tender offer commenced today and is expected to expire at 5:00 p.m., New York City Time, on December 5, 2022, unless the offer is extended.
