AgenciesThe Apple TV 4K (2022) comes at a starting price of Rs 14,900.The Cupertino-based tech giant, Apple, has unveiled its premium tv console Apple TV 4K (2022) in India. The new device comes at a starting price of Rs 14,900. The latest device from Apple comes with the latest tvOS platform and claims to have better performance and specifications. The all-new Apple TV 4K also comes with HDR10+ for dynamic range content along with Dolby Vision and HDR10. The earlier version of the device only supported Dolby Vision and HDR10.
