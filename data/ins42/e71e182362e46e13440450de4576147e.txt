John Bolton's Russia regime-change fantasy has little basis in reality
RT
news
2022-10-17 18:59:41

John Bolton, the former National Security Advisor for President Donald Trump, recently published an article titled ‘Putin must go: Now is the time for regime change in Russia.'One must keep in mind that these words come from a man who famously commented, while speaking in February 1994 at an event in New York called the ‘Global Structures Convocation,' that the 'Secretariat building in New York has 38 stories. If it lost ten stories, it wouldn't make a bit of difference.'Bolton lambasts the fact that, according to the White House, President Joe Biden wasn't really serious about regime change in Russia when, during remarks made in Poland last March, he blurted out 'For God's sake, this man cannot remain in power.
