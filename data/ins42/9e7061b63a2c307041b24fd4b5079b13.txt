Raises Dividend to $9.80 Per Share_________ MIAMI, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Watsco, Inc. (NYSE: WSO) announced record operating results for the third quarter and nine-month periods ended September 30, 2022 and provided commentary on trends, growth opportunities, technology innovation and financial strength. Watsco also announced that its Board of Directors approved an 11% increase in its annual dividend to $9.80 per share on each outstanding share of its Common and Class B common stock to be reflected in the Company's quarterly dividend payment in January 2023.
