June 02, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ALISO VIEJO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Syxsense, a global leader in IT and security management solutions, today announced that Syxsense Secure has won the People's Choice Stevie Award for Best Endpoint Security Management Solution in the 2022 American Business Awards. More than 46,000 people voted for their favorite products this year, and winners will be celebrated during a gala awards banquet on Monday, June 13 in New York City.



'Syxsense Secure is the world's first IT management and security solution that combines vulnerability scanning, patch management, and endpoint security into a single cloud console.
