House Republicans divided over aid to Ukraine ahead of midterms
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 01:52:00

The Republican leaders in the House of Representatives claimed that Congress would not 'write a blank cheque to Ukraine' once his party comes to the power after next month's midterm elections, a news report has stated. The statement has instilled fears in Kyiv of a possible cut-off in the flow of military equipment.Kevin McCarthy, the House minority leader, told the Punchbowl News website on Tuesday: 'I think people are going to be sitting in a recession and they're not going to write a blank cheque to Ukraine.
