Updated: 13 Oct 2022, 12:12 PM ISTThe online tuition and training industry has turned into a gigantic earning machine for teachers and communicators. Mathematics, physics, chemistry and biology teachers are in very high demand, as well as accountancy and business studies. Corporate trainers are also in high demand. Quick money: Conducting online classes requires some basic training. Edutechs pay well so online classes are flourishing. A teacher can make roughly Rs.2,000-3,000 per class, while corporate trainers can earn up to Rs 5,000-10,000 per session.
