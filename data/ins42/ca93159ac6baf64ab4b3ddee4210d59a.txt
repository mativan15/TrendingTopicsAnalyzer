Woman gets attacked on NYC subway, no one steps up to help her
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 09:45:00

A man on the New York City Subway assaulted a woman while on the train. The police have not yet managed to locate the assaulter. The incident was captured on video in which the man is wearing a white hoodie as he makes his way through the train. He keeps swearing as he proceeds to sit down in a seat. The passengers nearby the man successfully slide away on seeing him get seated. But, the woman right next to him is unable to do so. She attempts to leave, but the man reaches for her hair, grabs it and then pulls her back down to sit next to him.
