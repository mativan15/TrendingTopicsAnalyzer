Governor Hochul, the Port Authority of New York & New Jersey and Delta Air Lines Announce Opening of Delta's New Terminal C at LaGuardia Airport, Bringing a Whole New LGA Near Completion 
 Governor Kathy Hochul, Port Authority of New York and New Jersey Executive Director Rick Cotton and Delta Air Lines CEO Ed Bastian today announced the opening of Delta's new Terminal C, which marks the substantial completion of the $8 billion whole new LaGuardia Airport just six years after beginning construction.
