(Photo by Jordan Strauss/Invision/AP Images)Ever since the Covid-19 crisis hit the world, movie theatres suffered greatly. Lockdown was imposed in most countries and because of that, theatres remained shut for a long time. 2022, however, has brought some cheer for theatre owners and exhibitors across the globe with many films doing well at the box office. Recently, Marvel's Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness raced past all films released globally and emerged as the biggest money-spinner this year.
