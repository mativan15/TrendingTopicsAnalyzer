Navigator launches $50M stock repurchase plan
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-18 12:46:29

Navigator Holdings (NYSE:NVGS) has launched a stock repurchase plan to buyback up to $50M of its common stock.
The plan does not oblige Navigator (NVGS) to repurchase any of its shares and it may be suspended, discontinued or modified by the Company at any time, for any reason.
