Reaffirming the Commitment to the Region and Marking the Continuation of its U.S. Custody Offering and Strategic Build-Out

NEW YORK, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BNP Paribas, a premier global bank, celebrates today the ten year anniversary of BNP Paribas Securities Services in the United States. This year marks the continuation of BNP Paribas Securities Services U.S. custody offering and the strategic build-out of the business in the U.S. as well as reaffirms the business' commitment to the region.
