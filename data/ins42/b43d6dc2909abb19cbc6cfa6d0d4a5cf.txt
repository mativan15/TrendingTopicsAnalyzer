Earlier this month, Jet Airways' air operator certificate was revalidated by the aviation regulator DGCA.Insolvency appellate tribunal NCLAT on Monday said the implementation of Jalan-Kalrock consortium resolution plan for would be subject to the outcome of its order over the appeals filed before it. While hearing a batch of petitions against the NCLT order approving Jet Airways' resolution plan, a two-member bench of National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT) said they would have to abide by the results of the appeals filed before it.
