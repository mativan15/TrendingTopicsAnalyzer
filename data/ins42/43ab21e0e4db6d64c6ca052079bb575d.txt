After a draw bruising and breathtaking in equal measure, it fit that only nine players fetched up for Brazil's training session on Saturday evening deep inside a police battalion complex here. Among them was attacking midfielder Juju Harris who started the second half in the 1-1 draw against the USA.Harris plays in the USA, the gold standard in the women's game, for Florida United. Also in the squad of 21 is midfielder Lara Dantas, who started against USA and hence skipped the session, who represents IMG Academy in USA.
