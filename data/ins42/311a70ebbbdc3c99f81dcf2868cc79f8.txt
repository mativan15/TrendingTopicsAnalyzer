AgenciesAayush Sharma with Arpita KhanAayush Sharma recently talked about how people mocked him after he wed Arpita Khan. Despite having two films under his belt, he claims he still finds it challenging to forge his own identity because people often refer to him as Arpita Khan's husband or Salman Khan's brother-in-law. The Loveyatri debutant revealed that no one paid attention to his social media posts, whether they were about the Mumbai weather or a film review. But it all changed after he married Arpita.
