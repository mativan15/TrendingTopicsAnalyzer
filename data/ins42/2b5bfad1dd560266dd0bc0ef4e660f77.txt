May 31, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Floor & Decor Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: FND) today announced that the Company is scheduled to present at the Baird 2022 Global Consumer, Technology & Services Conference on Tuesday, June 7, 2022, at 1:25 p.m. Eastern Time.

The audio portion of the presentation will be webcast live over the internet and can be accessed on the Company's Investor Relations website, ir.flooranddecor.com. An online archive will be available on that site following the presentation.
