16 October 2022 by John Hopewell 
Variety - TV News Fremantle has acquired international distribution rights to 'East Side,' the latest series from 'Shtisel' producer Abot Hameiri, starring Yehuda Levi, a Series Mania 2022 best actor winner for 'Fire Dance.'Taking 100 ownership of Abot Hameiri last year, Fremantle, which also co-financed the series, will bring 'East Side' onto the market at this next week's Mipcom trade fair and conference in Cannes.To debut on Israel's Kan 11 channel, 'East Side' turns on Momi, a former Israeli secret service agent hired to take over a Palestinian neighbourhood, one home at a time.
