jasonbennee/iStock via Getty Images Thesis Sigma Lithium Corporation (NASDAQ:SGML) is a Canadian-based lithium miner whose only asset is the 100%-owned Grota do Cirilo project in Minas Gerais, Brazil. The company is a pre-production miner that's in the process of completing the first of a three-phase project and is on track to begin production in the fourth quarter of this year. It also recently announced an increase to its estimated total mineral resource, bringing the amount to a very impressive 58.
