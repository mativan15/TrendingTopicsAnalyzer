HALLOWEEN is right around the corner but there's no need to spend a bomb on decorations when you've got a bin bag lying around.
We all know a savvy friend who used the odd refuse sack to make a fancy dress costume, but one TikTok mum has yet another use for the essential household item and it's saved her a fortune over the spooky season.
The penny-saving mum gave followers a step-by-step guideCredit: Tiktok/@budgetingmum
A parent-of-two, who calls herself The Budgeting Mum, impressed over two million viewers with her quick and clever way to make a spiderweb from a bin bag.
