Indian shares rise 1% as RBI eases inflation fears
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 14:30:00

The RBI should pause interest rate hikes, despite unacceptably high inflation, to avoid stalling a recovery in economic growth, monetary policy committee member Jayant Varma told Reuters in an interview on Monday.Indian shares jumped to three-week highs in early trade on Tuesday, as rate hikes fears were soothed after the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) said inflation looked set to ease and as risk sentiment improved globally on Britain's fiscal policy U-turn.The NSE Nifty 50 index rose 1.02% to 17,488.
