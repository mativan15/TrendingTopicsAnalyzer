Mahendra Singh Dhoni, Virat Kohli to mark entry into NFT space with FanCraze

AgenciesFanCraze's other marquee partners include Rohit Sharma, Hardik Pandya, Suryakumar Yadav, Ravindra Jadeja and Shikhar Dhawan, as well as IPL teams such as Chennai Super Kings and Delhi Capitals.Former Team India captains and two of the most popular cricketers Mahendra Singh Dhoni and Virat Kohli are set to launch their personal digital collectibles, or NFTs (non-fungible tokens), during the upcoming T20 World Cup. Both Dhoni and Kohli have entered into exclusive partnership with Web 3.0 company FanCraze to launch their player cards on FanCraze platform.
