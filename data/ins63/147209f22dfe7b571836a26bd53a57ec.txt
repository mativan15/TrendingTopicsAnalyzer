The Channel 4 presenter Krishnan Guru-Murthy has been taken off air for a week after referring to a Tory MP by a four-letter word.The news anchor, 52, was overheard saying 'what a c***' after his exchange with Baker, the Northern Ireland minister, in Downing Street last night.He made the remark, which was not broadcast on television but picked up on a livestream, after clashing with Baker while discussing Liz Truss's government and last night's chaotic fracking vote.A clip taken from a live feed outside No 10 — not from the channel's broadcast — later appeared online.
