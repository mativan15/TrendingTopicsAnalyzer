June 01, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Mitek (NASDAQ: MITK, www.miteksystems.com), a global leader in digital identity (ID) and fraud prevention solutions, today announced that CEO Max Carnecchia is scheduled to present at the 42nd Annual Growth Stock Conference in Chicago on Tuesday, June 7th at 9:20 a.m. Central Time, with virtual one-on-one meetings with investors held throughout the day.

The presentation will be webcast live and available for replay via the investor relations section of the company's website at https://investors.
