Stock market news live updates: Stocks rally as markets aim to snap weeks-long sell-off

2022-05-26 19:24:01

A rally in U.S. stocks gained momentum Thursday, placing markets on track to snap a 7-week losing streak.The S&P 500 surged 2%, and the Dow Jones Industrial Average gained 550 points, or 1.8%. The Nasdaq Composite jumped 2.5% as the index attempts to claw its way out of bear market territory.Strong retail earnings Thursday morning spurred markets early in the session, with Macy's (M) rising 18% in intraday trading after the retail giant raised its profit outlook in an upside surprise to investors weighing a slew of downward forecast revisions from peers.
