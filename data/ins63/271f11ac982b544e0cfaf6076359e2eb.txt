HOUSTON, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Oil States International, Inc. (NYSE:OIS) announced today that it has scheduled its third quarter 2022 earnings conference call for Friday, October 28, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. Central Daylight Time. During the call, Oil States will discuss the results for the quarter ended September 30, 2022, which are expected to be released on Thursday, October 27, 2022, after the markets close.
