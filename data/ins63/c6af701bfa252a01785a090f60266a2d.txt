TORONTO, Oct. 18, 2022 /CNW/ - Scotiabank is proud to be recognized as one of the top 25 World's Best Workplaces™ by Great Place to Work®. Scotiabank is the only Canadian headquartered company and only financial institution to be recognized in the ranking. Earlier this year, Scotiabank announced it had earned the recognition in several countries where it operates, placing on the Best Workplaces™ lists in Canada, Trinidad & Tobago, Panama, Colombia, Uruguay, Mexico, Dominican Republic, Barbados and Jamaica.
