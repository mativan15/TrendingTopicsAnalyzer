Two-time Olympian Cayla Barnes brings key experience to her sixth year at Boston College
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-10-20 00:16:35

Sometimes, it feels like Cayla Barnes' college career has gone by as quickly as she skates. Then there are other days where the opposite is true.'I do feel like I have been in college for a long time,' said Boston College women's hockey's senior captain, a two-time Olympian.One of USA Hockey's young superstars, Barnes is a rare player who had her college career interrupted by two different Olympic calls. But the defensewoman is finally back, and the Eagles are excited to see what she can do in her redshirt senior season.
