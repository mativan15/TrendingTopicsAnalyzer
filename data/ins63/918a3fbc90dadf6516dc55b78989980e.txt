Hazal Ak/iStock via Getty Images MSP Recovery (MSPR) shares tumbled Friday, marking the end of a highly volatile week for the newly public company's stock. Shares of the payment recovery company opened at $2.35. They closed at $1.74, down 32% from Thursday' close. MSPR went public on Tuesday through a merger deal with SPAC Lionheart Acquisition Corp. II that pegged the combined company with an enterprise value of around $32.6B. The shares closed at $5.06 on Tuesday, down 53% from their pre-merger close of $10.
