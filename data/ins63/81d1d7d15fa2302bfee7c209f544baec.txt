jetcityimage Regions Financial (NYSE:RF) Q3 earnings, excluding notable items, trailed the consensus as expenses and the provision for credit losses rose from the previous quarter and the year-ago periods, according to its results posted Friday. For 2022 guidance, the bank sees increased loan and net interest income growth boosting adjusted revenue growth. 2022 average loan balance guidance growth of ~9% Y/Y vs. previous guidance of ~8% growth. Expects net interest income growth of 20%-22%, vs. prior guidance of ~16%-18%; excluding PPP loans, it expects NII to rise 23%-25% versus its prior view of +19%-21%.
