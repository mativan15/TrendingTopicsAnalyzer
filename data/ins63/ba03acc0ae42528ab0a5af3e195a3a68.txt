By Associated Press Published: 20:14 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 20:14 EDT, 20 October 2022 MEXICO CITY (AP) - Mexico City announced Thursday it is bringing attempted murder charges against a subway train driver for allegedly driving subway cars while drunk. The city government said the subway system, known as the Metro, also brought felony charges of "attacks on transportation" against the man. The incident occurred Tuesday, when the driver pulled into a station and opened the doors on the wrong side of the cars.
