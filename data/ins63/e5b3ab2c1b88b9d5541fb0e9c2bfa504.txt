Getty ImagesNifty moved in a tight range of 82 points, and ended slightly below the psychologically crucial level of 15,700. NEW DELHI: The Nifty IT index closed on a positive note on Thursday. Shares of L&T Technology(up 3.4 per cent), Larsen & Toubro Infotech(up 3.1 per cent), MPhasis(up 2.18 per cent), HCL Technologies(up 2.14 per cent) and Tata Consultancy(up 2.02 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack. On the other hand, Tech Mahindra(down 0.05 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.
