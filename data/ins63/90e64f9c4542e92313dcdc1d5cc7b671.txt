Starry Group Shares Drop 21%, Hit 52-Week Low
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 16:48:00

By Chris Wack Starry Group Holdings Inc. shares were down 21% at 87 cents one day after the company said it is putting cost-cutting measures in place to conserve capital and improve its runway as it explores all strategic options. The company said these measures include a 50% reduction of its workforce and a freeze on hiring and nonessential expenditures. The stock hit its 52-week low of 82 cents earlier in the session, and fell 17% on Thursday.
