UK PM Truss Battles To Stay In Power After Tax Reforms Trashed
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-18 09:42:32

New finance minister Hunt told parliament Truss had agreed to reverse almost all the tax measures announced three weeks ago as the grim faced premier looked on
British Prime Minister Liz Truss on Tuesday battled to stabilise her position after an economic crash forced her into humiliating U-turns on tax reforms, putting her future as leader in doubt."It's hard to conceive of a more serious political and economic crisis in recent times than that which Britain now faces," right-wing broadsheet The Daily Telegraph wrote in an editorial.
