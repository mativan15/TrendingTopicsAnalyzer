Britons nearing retirement risk 'difficult future' if markets fall
express.co.uk
finance
2022-10-15 18:21:00

Amid market turmoil, the Bank of England has been forced to step in with support designed to help pension funds. Andrew Bailey, the governor of the central bank, stepped in to offer an emergency 13-day bond-buying programme, that ended yesterday.Pension savers are anxiously waiting, as the subsequent sell-off in UK Government bonds, known as gilts, has triggered a pensions meltdown.As the state of the market remains unclear, anyone cashing in investments will have seen them recently fall in value, meaning less cash for their future.
