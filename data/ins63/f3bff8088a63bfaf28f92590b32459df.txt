Energy Up as Earnings, Economic Data Offset Commodity Price Drop -- Energy Roundup
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 20:04:00

Shares of energy companies ticked up as upbeat earnings and economic reports offset a retreat in commodity prices. The Russian government blocked Exxon's efforts to sell its 30% stake in the Sakhalin-1 venture in Russia's Far East for months, and now wiped out Exxon's stake entirely. BP shares rose after the British oil major agreed to buy U.S. biogas producer Archaea Energy for $3.3 billion, plus debt, in a bet on growing demand from customers for renewable fuel. Natural-gas futures fell 4% to $5.
