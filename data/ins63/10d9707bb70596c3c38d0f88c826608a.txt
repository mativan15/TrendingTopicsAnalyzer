EXCLUSIVE: 22-year old award-winning animator, environmental activist and multi-hyphenate phenom Maya Penn announced her upcoming animated short film ASALI: Power of The Pollinators. The short is an original project from Penn's own purpose-driven premium animation studio Upenndo! Productions and is set to be executive produced by Viola Davis, Julius Tennon, and JuVee Productions.

ASALI: Power of The Pollinators is an animated action-adventure short film that follows a colorful group of pollinators and an environmental scientist as they unravel a trail of secrets to discover if they can save their home from a monstrous and deadly force.
