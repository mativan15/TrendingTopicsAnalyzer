REUTERS/Edgard GarridoMEXICO CITY, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Mexico's finance ministry has been tasked with refinancing the country's debt, especially bonds maturing in 2025, in order to help ease the financial burden on the next administration when it takes office, a senior official said.Some 40% of the 2025 debt profile has already been refinanced and that number could increase to 70% or 80%, Deputy Finance Minister Gabriel Yorio told Reuters in an interview at his offices late on Wednesday.
