Wolfsburg and Bayern primed to deliver Champions League – DW – 10/18/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-18 13:39:14

Wolfsburg and Bayern Munich remain the teams to beat in the Bundesliga, but this season both are presented with a huge opportunity to show the same is true in the Champions League (UWCL). Eight-time winners and defending champions Lyon pose an obvious threat, but with Barcelona signing European champions Lucy Bronze and Keira Walsh and PSG are equally primed to challenge, there is no clear favorite. And with seven of the 16 teams in this season's group stages different than last season, it's clear that competition is increasing across the continent.
