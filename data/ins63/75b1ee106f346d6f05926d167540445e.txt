AgenciesNaraka Chaturdashi PujaChhoti Diwali is known by many names in several areas of the country, like Roop Chaudas, Kali Chaudas, Naraka Nivaran Chaturdashi, Narak Chaudas, and Bhoot Chaturdashi. Naraka Chaturdashi, also known as Chhoti Diwali, is a Hindu festival that falls on the second day of the five-day-long Diwali celebrations. It falls on Krishna Paksha's fourteenth day in the Hindu month of Kartika. It is believed that Lord Krishna and Satyabhama killed Nakasura on this day.

Significance Of Naraka Chaturdashi
According to the famous legends of Hindu mythology, Narakasura, a mythical demon, tried to invade heaven and earth.
