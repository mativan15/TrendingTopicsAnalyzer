Euro zone economies have no choice but to tackle the supply shock together
irishtimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 00:00:00

The economic challenges facing the euro zone are not the same as those facing the US. On balance, however, they are even more difficult.The euro zone economy is not suffering from overheating of domestic demand to the same extent as the US. This should make the task of monetary policy easier for the European Central Bank than for the Federal Reserve. But the supply shock buffeting the euro zone is far bigger, with a huge rise in the price of energy, especially gas, after Russia's invasion of Ukraine.
