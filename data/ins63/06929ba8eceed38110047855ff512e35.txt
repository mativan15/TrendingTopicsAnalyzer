Head of Tech Company gravity&storm Wins Business Worldwide Magazine CEO Award
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-01 08:00:00

LONDON, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The CEO of Berlin based company gravity&storm has been named in the 2022 Business Worldwide Magazine CEO Awards. Naira Danielyan was the outright winner in her category and has been named 'Digital Transformation CEO of the Year - Germany'

The aim of the awards is to celebrate leading executives for their successes in their fields, across a wide range of industries. Unlike other business awards that focus on companies as a whole, here the spotlight is on the personalities that lead them.
