How to clean a laptop
sfgate.com
news
2022-10-17 18:40:40

Crumbs, pet hair, germs, oils, dust, dead skin cells: Your trusty laptop is routinely subjected to some pretty nasty stuff (and that's not counting any deep dives into the dark corners of the internet).

But your device deserves more routine care than a cursory swipe across the screen every now and then. Indeed, cleaning your laptop on a regular basis is a good idea for a couple of reasons. First of all, who wants to be tapping away on a grime-encrusted keyboard everyday? And second, keeping your laptop clean means its function will improve significantly, especially when it comes to integral parts like the fan that gathers lots of dust.
