The 75th great annual festival, Cannes Film Festival 2022, winds down this Saturday. Concurrently, some unexpected superstars were due to appear in the last few. After the party, Kylie Minogue, an Australian singer and actress, joined the famed Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) mixed-martial artist (MMA), Conor McGregor. 
McGregor and Minogue looked happy at Cannes
The Notorious liked to be the center of attention at all times, and he did so when visiting the famous film festival. McGregor was seen grinning and joking with Minogue, 53, as he placed his arms around her.
