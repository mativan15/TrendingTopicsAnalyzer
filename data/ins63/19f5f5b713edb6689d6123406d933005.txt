The inputs available to the High Court prima facie showed that non-desilting of Perandoor canal had contributed to the large-scale flooding in Kochi last week.
| Photo Credit: R.K. NITHIN
Three days into the desilting of Perandoor Canal, the Kochi Corporation hopes to complete the work within a fortnight.Justice Devan Ramachandran of the Kerala High Court had the other day, while asking the civic administration to complete the work, noted that there was nothing on record to show how the canal was desilted after 2018.
