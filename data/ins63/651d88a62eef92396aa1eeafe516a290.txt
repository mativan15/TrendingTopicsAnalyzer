Blind walker tackling Wolds Way for guide dogs
bbc.com
news
2022-10-18 16:15:40

A blind man is walking the 79-mile (127km) Yorkshire Wolds Way to raise money for a guide dogs charity.Andrew Elliker-Reeve lost his sight two years ago after having elective surgery to help the blood flow to his legs.He set off on Tuesday alongside volunteers helping to guide him along the way and aims to cover up to 12 miles (19km) a day.
