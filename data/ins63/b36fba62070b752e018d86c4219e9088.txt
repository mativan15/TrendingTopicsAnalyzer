Infosys raises CEO Salil Parekh's salary by 88% to Rs 80 crore
indiatimes.com
business
2022-05-27 04:00:00

BENGALURU: Infosys has raised CEO Salil Parekh 's compensation by a massive 88% to Rs 79.8 crore (more than $10 million), a reward for the industry-leading performance of the company, and the phenomenal increase in shareholder value during his tenure. The revision comes alongside the extension of Parekh's tenure by five years.Most of the increase is in performance-based rewards (cash and stocks). This has risen by 110%, to Rs 68.8 crore. Fixed compensation has risen by 13% to Rs 11 crore.The company's annual report for 2021-22, which has just been released, said the overhaul in the CEO compensation was undertaken also to benchmark it with those of peers like Accenture, IBM, TCS, Capgemini, Cognizant, DXC and others.
