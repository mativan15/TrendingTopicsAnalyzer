Shareholders in Great Panther Mining (TSE:GPR) are in the red if they invested five years ago
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-04 14:00:40

We're definitely into long term investing, but some companies are simply bad investments over any time frame. We really hate to see fellow investors lose their hard-earned money. Anyone who held Great Panther Mining Limited (TSE:GPR) for five years would be nursing their metaphorical wounds since the share price dropped 86% in that time. We also note that the stock has performed poorly over the last year, with the share price down 72%. The falls have accelerated recently, with the share price down 28% in the last three months.
