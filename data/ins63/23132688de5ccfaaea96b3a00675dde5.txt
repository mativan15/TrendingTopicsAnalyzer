Webtoons and Manhwas are famous forms of Asian entertainment. Should add that many shows based on these aesthetic masterpieces are enormous successes. Netflix is trying this trick by adapting the most popular Naver Webtoon: Lookism, marking the streaming giant's first foray into animation. 
Many popular webtoons are converted into shows, with Business Proposal and True Beauty being two of the most well-known. Lookism, on the other hand, is the first original animated adaptation by Netflix of a webtoon.
