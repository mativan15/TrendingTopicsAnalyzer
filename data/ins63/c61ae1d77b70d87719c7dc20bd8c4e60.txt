Michelle Williams to Receive Gotham Awards Performer Tribute
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-10-20 13:00:00

Michelle Williams is set to be honored with a performer tribute at the 2022 Gotham Awards. The four-time Oscar nominee stars in Steven Spielberg's new film, The Fabelmans, based on the acclaimed director's own childhood. In the movie, which is set to hit theaters next month after winning the People's Choice audience award at the 2022 Toronto International Film Festival, Williams plays Mitzi Fabelman, the artistic mom of an aspiring filmmaker. Williams won an Emmy, Golden Globe, Critics Choice Award and Screen Actors Guild Award for her role in the FX limited series Fosse/Verdon.
