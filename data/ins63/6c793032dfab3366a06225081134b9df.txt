Woburn, Massachusetts, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- NEWPRO Home Solutions ('NEWPRO'), today announced its partnership with Renovo Home Partners ('Renovo'), a Direct-to-Consumer platform based in Dallas, Texas providing home remodeling products and services. Renovo was formed in late 2021 by the combination of its partnership with Dreamstyle Remodeling, Alure Home Improvements, and Remodel USA. Angle Advisors served as exclusive financial advisor to NEWPRO. Founded in 1945, NEWPRO has grown into one of the largest and most trusted home remodelers in New England.
