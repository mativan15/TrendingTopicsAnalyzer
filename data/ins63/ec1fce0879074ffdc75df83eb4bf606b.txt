Published: 23:34 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 23:34 EDT, 31 May 2022 The Nanny is arguably one of the most popular TV sitcoms of all time.And one of the regions that it was most successful in was Australia, where it's enjoyed a huge following for decades.The series has now spawned a children's book N Is for the Nanny, which was penned by Australian author Rebecca Kelly and benefits Fran Drescher's Cancer Schmancer charity. 'The Nanny is wildly popular in Australia!' Fran Drescher has discussed the enduring popularity of The Nanny in AustraliaSpeaking to the Hot Takes and Deep Dives podcast, Fran, 64, explained how the show's popularity Down Under led her to collaborate with Rebecca on the book.
