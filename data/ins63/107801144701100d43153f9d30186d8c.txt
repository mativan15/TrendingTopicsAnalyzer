October 19, 2022 12:13 PM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has assigned a Financial Strength Rating of A- (Excellent) and a Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of 'a-' (Excellent) to Research Insurance Company Limited (RICL) (Bermuda). The outlook assigned to these Credit Ratings (ratings) is stable. RICL is a captive insurance company wholly owned by Battelle Memorial Institute (BMI), an Ohio-based nonprofit corporation and provider of technology-based research, management, commercialization and educational services to government and industry.
