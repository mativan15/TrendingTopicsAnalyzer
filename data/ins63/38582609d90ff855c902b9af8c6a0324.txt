Restoring biodiversity needs more than just a list
stuff.co.nz
politics
2022-06-01 01:06:47

When the Government released its Te Mana o te Taiao Biodiversity Implementation Plan recently, it was done with a promise of collective action to achieve the goals set out in the 2020 Biodiversity Strategy. In reality though, this new document lacks the teeth it needs to tackle one of the biggest challenges we face as a country. Biodiversity - the variety of living things on earth - is vital for functioning ecosystems that supply the oxygen, clean air and water we need to survive. New Zealand's biodiversity is in a state of decline.
