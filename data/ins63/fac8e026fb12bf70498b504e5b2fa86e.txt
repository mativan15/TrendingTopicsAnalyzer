Three killed in separate accidents in Dindigul district

A 72-year-old man was killed in a road accident near Kamaraj Bus Stand here on Monday. According to Dindigul Town North police, the deceased was identified as A. Palanisami of Paraipatti near here. Preliminary investigation revealed that M. Subbaiya, 53, was at the wheel of a TNSTC bus which hit the senior pedestrian. Palanisami sustained injuries and was shifted to Dindigul Government Medical College Hospital where he died. G. Sankar, 50, of Aruppukottai in Virudhunagar district was killed as the bike he was riding on collided with a pickup van at Kanavaipatti junction in Batlagundu on Monday evening.
