Bloomberg Economists See 100% Chance of a Recession Within a Year
fool.com
news
2022-10-18 14:41:33

According to the Bloomberg Economics probability model, there's now a 100% chance we'll see a recession in the next 12 months. The Bloomberg team isn't the only one predicting economic difficulties; many economists have been warning about a recession for several months. However, Bloomberg is unusual in being so certain. Most other predictions are couched in language of it being a strong likelihood rather than a 100% possibility.Bloomberg's 100% recession warningThe Bloomberg Economics model uses 13 different macroeconomic and financial indicators and to predict the chances of a recession at various points in the coming two years.
