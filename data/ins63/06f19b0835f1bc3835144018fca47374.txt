Tenby: School field hospitality plan scrapped after outcry

news
2022-05-28 15:55:42

Image caption, The events would have been held on the school fields over the summer holidayPlans to host pop-up hospitality events on school playing fields have been ditched after an outcry by residents. An application for licensed events at Ysgol Greenhill, Tenby, Pembrokeshire, was opposed by the town council over concerns about noise.Swansea-based Pop-Ups Limited offered to run events over the summer holiday up to 23:00 BST, and pay the school.But the school decided to pull the plans after concerns were raised about the site's suitability.
