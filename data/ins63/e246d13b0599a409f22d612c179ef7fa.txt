ENGLEWOOD, Colo., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- WOW! Internet, Cable & Phone (NYSE: WOW), a leading broadband provider in the United States, announced today it will host a webcast and conference call on Thursday, November 3, 2022, at 8:00 a.m. ET to discuss financial and operating results for the third quarter 2022. WOW! will issue a news release reporting its results earlier that morning.
The conference call will be broadcast live on the company's investor relations website at ir.wowway.com. Those parties interested in participating via telephone should dial (888) 330-3556 with the conference ID number 4844814.
