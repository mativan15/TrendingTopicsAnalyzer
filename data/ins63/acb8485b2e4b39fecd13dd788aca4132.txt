--News Direct-- TAIPEI, TAIWAN - Media OutReach - 1 June 2022 - Taiwan's IDB (Industrial Development Bureau) 'AI Life' Pavilion championed artificial intelligence at the center of Computex 2022, celebrating the IT trade show's first in-person exhibition after two years of virtual events. The Pavilion featured thirteen Taiwanese tech firms, showcasing their latest AI-driven products and solutions.13 Taiwanese technology companies exhibit on the IDB AI Life Pavilion at Computex 2022Signage above the Pavilion read, 'My AI, my dAIly life', broadcasting the Pavilion's purpose - to shed light on the behind-the-scenes role of AI in our daily lives.
