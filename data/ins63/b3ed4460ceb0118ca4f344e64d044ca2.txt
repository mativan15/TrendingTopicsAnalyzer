BOUNCING BACK
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 10:00:00

In October 2020, as the pandemic tore through the country and its industries, Textron Aviation was months removed from more than 1,000 layoffs in Wichita. Business jet deliveries were halved from the year before.

Just two years later, the maker of Beechcraft and Cessna piston, turboprop and jet aircraft hit the industry's showcase event this week on a trajectory few, if any, saw coming this quickly.

The Textron Inc. division went to the National Business Aviation Association convention in Orlando earlier this week riding a wave local hiring, facility expansion and continued customer demand.
