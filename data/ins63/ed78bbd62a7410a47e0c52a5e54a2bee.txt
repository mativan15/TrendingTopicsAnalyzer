By Huang Haiyan | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-22 08:59 Li Min/China Daily China will launch extensive public fitness initiatives, improve physical education for young people, promote all-around development of recreational and competitive sports, and move faster to build China into a country strong in sports, said the report to the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China on Oct 16. During the past decade, China has made unprecedented efforts to become a leading global sports power.
