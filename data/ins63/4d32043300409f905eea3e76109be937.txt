By Sarah-Grace Mankarious, CNN She's been photographed, painted and graffitied. She's been struck on coins, cast in iron, silk-screened onto T-shirts and fired onto plates. There's even a Barbie doll in her likeness. At 96 years old, the longest-serving monarch in Britain's history celebrates seven decades on the throne this year.
