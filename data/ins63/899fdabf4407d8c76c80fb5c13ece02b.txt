LOUISVILLE, Ky.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--LLFlex, one of the world's leading suppliers of specialized packaging materials and industrial laminates, has named Thomas Bowden Chief Executive Officer and Tamra Koshewa Chief Financial Officer effective immediately. Both executives have extensive experience in manufacturing and packaging companies.This press release features multimedia.
