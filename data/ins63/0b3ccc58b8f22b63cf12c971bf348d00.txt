Former Navy General Counsel and Federal Prosecutor Bob Sander Joins Ward & Berry
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 19:08:15

Ward & Berry, PLLC is pleased to welcome The Honorable Robert J. ('Bob') Sander, the Former General Counsel of the Navy, as its newest Partner. In addition to being General Counsel of the Navy, Bob has served as: • Principal Deputy General Counsel of the Army/Senior Official Performing the Duties of (i.e. Acting) General Counsel of the Army; • Senior Associate General Counsel of the Office of the Director of National Intelligence; • both a Federal and Local Prosecutor; and • Lieutenant Colonel in the Army Reserve Judge Advocate Generals ('JAG') Corps.
