Noida: Closed due to Covid, hotel leased by Chinese national used for fraud
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 03:30:00

NOIDA: Investigation into the cyber fraud orchestrated by two Chinese nationals has revealed that the duo had duped over 5,000 Indian nationals of several hundred crores in the past four years through schemes that mirrored a Ponzi, TOI has learnt.Moreover, the Greater Noida hotel from where the accused-Feng Chenjin, a resident of Jiangxi (Ganzhou), and Huang Kuan, a resident of Guangxi in southern China-were nabbed on Saturday by a joint team of the UP Special Task Force and Noida police, was taken on lease for five years by another Chinese national in June 2020.
