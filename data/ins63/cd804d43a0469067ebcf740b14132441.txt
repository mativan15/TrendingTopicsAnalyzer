Success of NEP 2020 outcome hinges on proper implementation, say former Vice-Chancellors
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-15 12:51:34

The National Education Policy 2020 being implemented in the State may run into teething problems but the realisation of the outcome envisaged under the policy hinges on its proper implementation. It was also imperative for the government to increase the GDP spending on higher educationThis was the opinion of former Vice-Chancellors of various universities who participated in a review and orientation meeting held here on Saturday.‘'The Orientation Programme for Students and Faculty in the Selection of Multidisciplinary Courses under NEP-2020 – Reported Lapses and Confusion in its Implementation and Possible Remedies'' was organised by the Forum of Former Vice-Chancellors of Karnataka (FVCK) and University of Mysore Alumni Association.
