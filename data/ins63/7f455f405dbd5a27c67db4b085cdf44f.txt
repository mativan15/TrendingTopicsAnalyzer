Protect your PIN: Improve personal and systemic vigilance, stop cybercrime from blotting India's rapid digital transition
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 20:50:07

Phishing attacks are not a new phenomenon. But the rapid penetration of smartphones and online banking and payment systems without a matching leap in awareness of their risks, seems to have given the scamsters a worrying edge over consumers. Many of them feel compelled to respond to WhatsApp and SMS messages and phone calls when these threaten to disconnect a key service or promise attractive rewards. That the voice on the other end is armed with a lot of personal information makes it harder to doubt the caller.
