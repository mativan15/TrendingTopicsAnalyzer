Published: 09:14 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 09:14 EDT, 20 October 2022 Former PM Berlusconi long-time friend of Putin * Rightist Meloni tries to build international trust * Meloni govt expected to be installed in coming days By Angelo Amante ROME, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Former Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi's pro-Russia statements are likely to complicate Giorgia Meloni's efforts to establish international credibility for the government she is set to lead, analysts say. Berlusconi is a long-time friend of Russian President Vladimir Putin and this week reiterated his sympathy for him, accusing Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskiy of triggering the war.
