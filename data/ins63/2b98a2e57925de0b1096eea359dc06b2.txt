US Women's Open: Friday 5am (NZ time) Where: Pine Needles Golf Club, North Carolina Live coverage: Sky Sport 6 Lydia Ko wants to ride the wave of Kiwi success currently being floated by herself, Ryan Fox and Steven Alker as well as inspiring the next generation, when she tees off at Pine Needles. Ko heads into this week's US Women's Open, which has been given a mammoth prizemoney injection, on the back of a solid season of her own. But the form of Alker on the PGA Tour Champions and Fox on the DP World Tour has not gone unnoticed by the former world No 1.
