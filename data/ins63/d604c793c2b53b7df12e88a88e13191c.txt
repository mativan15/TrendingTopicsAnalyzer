WestInvest funding for heart of Penrith
nsw.gov.au
news
2022-10-21 01:06:08

Friday, 21 October 2022 Penrith residents can soon enjoy a new city centre in St Marys and a huge new recreation precinct thanks to the $5 billion WestInvest Program. Premier Dominic Perrottet, Treasurer Matt Kean, Minister for Western Sydney David Elliott and Minister for Local Government Wendy Tuckerman today announced more than $28 million in WestInvest funding for Penrith: $21 million for the St Marys City Heart and Entertainment Canopy project to deliver a new civic park and state of the art performance centre$7 million towards the $41 million Gipps Street Recreation Precinct project to transform a 32-hectare former landfill site in Claremont Meadows into a multipurpose open space facility for the community.
