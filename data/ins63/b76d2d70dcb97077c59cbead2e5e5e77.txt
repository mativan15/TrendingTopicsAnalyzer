ASX bounces back, up 0.8pct at noon
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-03 02:16:18

The local share market is bouncing back from Thursday's losses with broad gains this morning.

The benchmark S&P / ASX200 index was up 55.6 to 7,231.5 at noon, a gain of 0.77 per cent. The broader All Ordinaries was up 65.1 points to 7,465.9, a 0.88 per cent rise.

Every sector was up except financials, telecom services and utilities, with tech and mining both up by around two and a half per cent.

BHP had risen 2.4 per cent to $46.72, Fortescue was up 3.8 per cent to $21.40 and Rio Tinto had gained 2.
