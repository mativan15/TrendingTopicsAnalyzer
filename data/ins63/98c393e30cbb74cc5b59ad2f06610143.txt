Published: 11:49 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:49 EDT, 17 October 2022 KANSAS CITY, Mo. (AP) - Patrick Mahomes expects to score every time he touches the ball. He expects to win every time he steps on the field. So when the Chiefs quarterback threw an interception in the final minute Sunday, allowing the Bills to escape with a 24-20 victory over Kansas City, there was a quiet sense of shock inside Arrowhead Stadium - as if Chiefs fans, who had grown so accustomed to winning, couldn't believe Mahomes and Co.
