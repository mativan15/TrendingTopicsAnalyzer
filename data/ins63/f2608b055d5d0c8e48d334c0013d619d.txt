The pound surges on Liz Truss' resignation as U.K. prime minister
fortune.com
news
2022-10-20 12:51:03

US stock futures fluctuated as investors assessed resilient company earnings against an unexpected drop in jobless claims. The pound was firmer and UK bonds climbed as Liz Truss announced she was stepping down as UK prime minister. S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 contracts were little changed as AT&T Inc. and American Airlines Group Inc. climbed in premarket trading after beating estimates Tesla Inc., however, dropped after the electric-vehicle maker's miss on sales. Oil stocks and luxury names outperformed in Europe.
