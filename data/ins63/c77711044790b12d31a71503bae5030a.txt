Good Times Restaurants Reports Fourth Fiscal Quarter Same Store Sales

2022-10-18 00:00:00

GOLDEN, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Good Times Restaurants Inc. (Nasdaq: GTIM), operator of Bad Daddy's Burger Bar and Good Times Burgers & Frozen Custard, today announced that same store sales1 for its fourth fiscal quarter ended September 27, 2022 increased 5.9% for its Good Times brand and increased 3.7% for its Bad Daddy's brand, and that average weekly sales2 were $29,896 and $49,945 for its Good Times brand and for its Bad Daddy's brand, respectively, for the fourth fiscal quarter. For the full fiscal year, same store sales increased 1.
