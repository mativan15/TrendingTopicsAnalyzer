By Reuters Published: 11:05 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 11:05 EDT, 16 October 2022 Oct 16 (Reuters) - Olympique Lyonnais extended their winless run in Ligue 1 to six matches after they suffered a 3-2 defeat at Stade Rennais in their first match under new coach Laurent Blanc on Sunday. Martin Terrier scored twice for Rennes, who registered their third straight league win and moved two spots up to fifth in the standings on 21 points after 11 matches. Lyon, who appointed former France defender Blanc to replace Peter Bosz, have lost five of their last six games and slipped to 10th on 14 points.
