Verizon, Razer Unveil 'world's First 5G Gaming Device; Coming In January
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-16 05:45:37

(RTTNews) - Verizon and Razer unveiled the world's first 5G gaming device, "the Razer Edge 5G". The device is coming in January 2023, but pricing is currently unknown. Verizon noted that the 5G handheld console, which equipped with the world's most advanced display, powered by the latest Snapdragon G3xFort Gen 1 Gaming Platform, is running on Verizon 5G Ultra Wideband, the 5G network for gaming. Gamers no longer need to be wired to their home network or endlessly searching for a reliable Wi-Fi hotspot when out and about.
