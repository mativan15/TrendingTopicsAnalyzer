Published: 13:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 13:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 A gangster rapper has been jailed alongside two others for running a huge heroin and cocaine dealing empire.Rayal Eastwood, stage name 'Champaz', used the encrypted EncroChat network to set up large drug deals across Birmingham and London before spending the money on flash motors.The 38-year-old portrayed himself on social media as a singer, rapper, and producer who creates 'some of the most controversial lyrics in dancehall scenery'.
