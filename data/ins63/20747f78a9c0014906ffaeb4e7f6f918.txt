EU plans aid to Ukraine of 1.5 billion euros per month
indiatimes.com
world
2022-10-21 15:45:00

BRUSSELS: The European Union is drawing up plans to provide Ukraine with 1.5 billion euros ($1.46 billion) in economic aid per month next year, EU chief Ursula von der Leyen said Friday.Speaking after an EU leaders' summit, von der Leyen said Ukraine had requested that international donors provide "a stable, reliable and predictable flow" of macroeconomic support.Ukraine's economy has been shattered by Russia's eight-month-old invasion, and Kyiv estimates that it needs at least four billion euros in grants or loans per month to ensure basic government functions.
