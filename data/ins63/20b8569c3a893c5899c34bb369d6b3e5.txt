October 18, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Adobe (Nasdaq:ADBE) today will host a financial analyst meeting with investors and financial analysts at its Adobe MAX Creativity Conference. Adobe's executive team will share the company's growth strategy across its creativity, digital document and customer experience management businesses.

Adobe is reaffirming its current fourth quarter fiscal year 2022 financial targets and is also providing preliminary financial targets for fiscal year 2023.
