Party organisation should not slow down: BJP general secretary D Purandeshwari
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 14:00:00

LUCKNOW: BJP national general D Purandeshwari said on Monday that a `Sashakt' (empowered) `Mandal' (division) comprising active booths was the basic mantra of the party's organizational structure.Addressing the state office bearers UP BJP headquarters , Purandeshwari asserted that the party should not slow down the process expansion despite becoming perceptibly omnipresent in the entire country under the leadership of PM Narendra Modi "We have to work to connect people of every section and section of the society with the party by carrying forward the party's policy of Sabka Saath-Sabka Vikas and Sabka Vishwas," she said at a meeting which had the attendance of UP in-charge and National Vice President of the party, Radha Mohan Singh , state president Bhupendra Singh Chaudhary and State General Secretary (Organization) Dharampal Singh.
