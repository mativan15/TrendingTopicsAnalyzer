October 17, 2022 09:25 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Lyft, Inc. (Nasdaq: LYFT) (the 'Company' or 'Lyft') will release financial results for the third fiscal quarter of 2022 after the close of the market on Monday, November 7, 2022.

On the same day, Lyft will host a conference call at 1:30 p.m. Pacific Time (4:30 p.m. Eastern Time) to discuss these financial results and business highlights. To listen to a live audio webcast, please visit the Company's Investor Relations page at https://investor.
