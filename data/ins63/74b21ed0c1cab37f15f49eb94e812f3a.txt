May 31, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time PORTLAND, Ore.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NuScale Power Corporation ('NuScale' or the 'Company') (NYSE: SMR), the industry-leading provider of proprietary and innovative advanced small modular nuclear reactor ('SMR') technology, today announced that it will host a Company Update call for investors and analysts on Friday, June 3, at 11:00 a.m. Eastern Time. President and Chief Executive Officer, John Hopkins, and Chief Financial Officer and Chief Strategy Officer, Chris Colbert, will provide commentary on recent business highlights, reaffirm financial projections, and take questions from analysts.
