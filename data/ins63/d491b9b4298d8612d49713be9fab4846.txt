Another downtown exit: Legal publication heads west
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 21:31:00

Missouri Lawyers Media said Friday that it has moved its eastern Missouri office from downtown St. Louis to Fenton, adding to the number of recognizable tenants that have left or plan to leave downtown.

The publisher of the Missouri Lawyers Weekly newspaper, as well as five daily records newspapers around the state, said it had operated from the Security Building downtown, 319 N. Fourth St., since 2007.

It said it is now in suite 103 at 1400 S. Highway Drive, The 1400 Building on the Maritz campus that is owned by a Maritz development company.
