Gautam Adani plans flagship firm's first retail bond
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 11:15:00

AgenciesGautam Adani, chairman & founder of Adani GroupThe flagship company of Asia's richest person Gautam Adani is planning a maiden bond sale to individual investors, after the coal-to-ports conglomerate expanded in the past few years into everything from data centers to cement, media and alumina. Adani Enterprises Ltd has proposed a 10-billion-rupee ($121 million) public sale of bonds, according to a statement from Care Ratings, which assigned the potential issuance an A+ score.

The Indian tycoon has been on an acquisition spree this year across sectors as he rapidly seeks to diversify.
