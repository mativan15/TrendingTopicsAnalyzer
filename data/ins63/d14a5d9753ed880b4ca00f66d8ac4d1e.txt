Hospitality Roundup: Fresh Del Monte buys 39% stake in tech startup
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-17 11:08:00

Fresh Del Monte Produce announced it purchased a 39% stake in Decapolis, a startup that provides blockchain-based tech solutions for the food industry.

The Coral Gables-based company (NYSE: FDP) did not disclose how much it invested in the Jordan- and U.K.-based company that provides safety and traceability technology.

But Fresh Del Monte, a leader in the production and distribution of fresh fruits and vegetables, said the investment was in line with its technology-driven mission to offer innovative solutions for its products and services, while providing sustainable options.
