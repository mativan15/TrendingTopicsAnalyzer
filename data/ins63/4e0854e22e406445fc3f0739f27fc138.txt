Mom of preemie twins sends message to Biden as baby formula shortage persist: 'Where are you? Wake up!'
msn.com
news
2022-10-19 12:15:00

Parents nationwide are still struggling to find baby formula months after panic first ensued, and one mother is sharing her message for the Biden administration over the crisis.  Louisiana mother Amber Bergeron joined "Fox & Friends First" Wednesday to discuss her struggle to find baby formula for her premature twins and the Biden administration's failure in handling the shortage. "The conclusion that I have come to is the American people need to remember this," Bergeron told co-host Todd Piro. "Remember that the children, our future, was not important to this administration.
