GrowthSpace Opens UK Operations, Hires Sales Director to Build European Presence

NEW YORK, and TEL AVIV, Israel, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- GrowthSpace, the world's first outcome-driven talent development platform, announced today that the company is expanding its activities to the UK and Europe, having hired Nick Koutris, formerly of Coachhub and LinkedIn, as the Director of Sales for the region.
The expansion is fuelled by the recently announced $25 million in Series B funding led by Zeev Ventures with existing investors M12 (Microsoft's venture fund) and Vertex Ventures participating, bringing the total amount raised by the company to $44 million.
