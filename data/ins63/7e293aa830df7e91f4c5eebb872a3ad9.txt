A NEWLYWED woman has shown how to look stunning at your wedding while on a budget with not just one, but two dresses.
Posting to social media, Mari Verd shared how she lucked out on two elegant and affordable Shein buys for her ceremony.
A woman has shared two gorgeous Shein dress buys that she wore at her wedding (stock photo)Credit: Getty
"My inexpensive dresses were under $50 from Shein," Verd wrote in a TikTok video.
"LOVED BOTH OF MY WEDDING DRESSES," she added in her caption.
Her first dress, the Missord Mermaid Hem Sequin Prom Dress, retailed for $46 when she purchased it.
