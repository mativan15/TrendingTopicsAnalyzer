Every investor in Lloyds Banking Group plc (LON:LLOY) should be aware of the most powerful shareholder groups. The group holding the most number of shares in the company, around 84% to be precise, is institutions. That is, the group stands to benefit the most if the stock rises (or lose the most if there is a downturn).

Institutional investors would probably welcome last week's 3.9% increase in share prices after a year of 13% losses as a sign that returns are likely to begin trending higher.
