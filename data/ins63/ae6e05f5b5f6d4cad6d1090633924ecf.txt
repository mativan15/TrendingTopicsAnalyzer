The share price of Bharat . dropped 1.58 per cent to Rs 695.65 at 01:49PM (IST) in Wednesday's trade. The scrip has hit a high of Rs 712.15 and low of Rs 694.85 so far during the session. It traded at a price-to-earnings multiple of 29.99 and a price-to-book ratio of 4.97. A higher price-to-earnings ratio shows investors are willing to pay a higher price for per rupee earnings given by the stock because of better future growth expectations.
