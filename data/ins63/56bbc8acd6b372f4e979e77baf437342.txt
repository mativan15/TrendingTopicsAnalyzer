SILVER SPRING, Md. (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 CLEO TV's welcomes new series WHAT'S COOKING? to its "Design & Dine" Wednesday nights. The premiere season kicks off in Atlanta, GA, known as the "Black Mecca". Hosted by Celebrity Comedian and Personality Pretty Vee, featuring Willie Moore Jr. in a fun segment called 'Something to Chew On', WHAT'S COOKING? highlights an interesting mix of culinary talent. The show format will spotlight chefs and restaurants in ATL by making their signature dishes and sharing their backstories.
