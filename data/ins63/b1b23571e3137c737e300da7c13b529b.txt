NEW YORK, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--INDUS Realty Trust, Inc. (Nasdaq: INDT) ("INDUS" or the "Company"), a U.S. based industrial/logistics REIT, announced today that it will be meeting with investors and others at Nareit's REITweek 2022 Investor Conference, to be held June 7th through June 9th at the New York Hilton Midtown Hotel in New York City. Management will also deliver a presentation on Tuesday, June 7th from 3:00 pm to 3:30 pm Eastern Time.
