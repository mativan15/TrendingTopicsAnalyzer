TVLine.com As We See It, Parenthood creator Jason Katims' dramedy about roommates living on the autism spectrum, will not be returning for Season 2 on Prime Video, TVLine has learned.Touted as 'Katims' best series since Parenthood' in TVLine's Best Shows From the First Half of 2022, the eight-episode series told the story of three twentysomething roommates living on the autism spectrum as they attempt to 'get and keep jobs, make friends, fall in love and navigate a world that eludes them.
