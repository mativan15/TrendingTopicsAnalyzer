KaylaBy Kayla ThomasJun 2, 2022It was just announced that Chipotle is joining the cryptocurrency club. Soon you can pay for your burritos and burrito bowls through a digital payment platform called Flexa. According to Coin Desk, the Mexican restaurant joins a list of other restaurants like Subway, Starbucks and Pizza Hut that accepts cryptocurrency. To celebrate this huge announcement. Chipotle is giving all of its customers a 10 percent discount on payments made with crypto.
