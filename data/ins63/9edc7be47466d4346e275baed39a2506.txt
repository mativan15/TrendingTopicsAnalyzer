REUTERS/Dado Ruvic/Illustration/NEW YORK, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Pfizer Inc expects to charge about $110 to $130 per dose for its COVID-19 vaccine after the United States government's current program expires, Pfizer executive Angela Lukin said on a call with analysts.Lukin said she expects the vaccine will be made available with no co-pay to people who have private insurance or government paid insurance.
