Power Grid Corporation acquires MTL for over Rs 9 crore
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 10:32:31

State-owned Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (PGCIL) on Tuesday said it has acquired Mohanlalganj Transmission Limited in Uttar Pradesh for over Rs 9 crore. Incorporate in June 2021, Mohanlalganj Transmission Limited ( MTL ) is the project SPV (special purpose vehicle) to establish an intra-state transmission system, PGCIL said in a BSE filing."...it is informed that PGCIL pursuant to its selection as the successful bidder under tariff-based competitive bidding, has on May 30, 2022, acquired MTL," it added.
