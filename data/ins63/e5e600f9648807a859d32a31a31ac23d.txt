We told you back in April about the return of Taco Bell's Mexican Pizza after a 'hiatus' back in 2020, but apparently…it's going away AGAIN! What gives? High demand. In a purported letter to employees that was posted on Reddit it's not coming back anytime soon. 'The Mexican Pizza will return later this year,' the letter offers. 'You'll hear more from us as that date approaches… we're working diligently with our restaurant and suppliers to get more back in the hands and stomachs of our biggest fans.
