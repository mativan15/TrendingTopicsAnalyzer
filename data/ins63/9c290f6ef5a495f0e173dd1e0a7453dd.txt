How exposure to climate risks make supply chain vulnerable

2022-10-20 07:35:01

Global economic production mainly depends on a network of interlinked supply chains, ensuring the circulation and exchange of products (from essential to luxury goods) are done efficiently. Circulation of products across the globe is crucial for realizing adequate surplus demands to carry on a business, which is possible through the interdependence of multiple supply chains worldwide. Therefore, the significance of studying and analyzing the effectiveness of supply chains under myriad circumstantial hindrances has been discerned over the years.
