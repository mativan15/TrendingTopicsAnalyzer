Miami-based EVI Industries makes deal to acquire Alabama laundry biz
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 18:42:00

A Miami-based corporation that specializes in distributing commercial laundry and dry cleaning equipment is contracted to purchase yet another laundry business.

EVI Industries (NYSE: EVI) announced it has an agreement to acquire Wholesale Commercial Laundry (WCL) Equipment SE, an Alabama-based distributor of equipment for industrial laundries and laundromats in Alabama, Georgia, Mississippi, Western Tennessee, and the Florida Panhandle. The purchase price was not disclosed.

In a release, EVI chairman and CEO Henry Nahmad, said the acquisition is part of the corporation's long-term growth strategy.
