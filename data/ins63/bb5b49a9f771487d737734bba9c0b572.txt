An MoU is inked with Proxtera to jointly create and establish an open framework for all participating financial institutions, in addition to serving financing needs of SMEs as Proxtera's first participating digital wholesale bank in Singapore.

SINGAPORE, June 06, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ANEXT Bank, a digital wholesale bank incorporated in Singapore and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Ant Group, announced its soft launch today. The soft launch follows its receipt of MAS' approval to commence business on 2 June 2022.
