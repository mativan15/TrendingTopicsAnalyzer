Battery companies are desperate for talent to meet the demand they're seeing from electric vehicle automakers — and some are paying big bucks to attract workers.Battery companies are racing to get critical materials like lithium and nickel. They need to cobble together domestic sourcing plans to qualify for the tax credits set forth in this summer's Inflation Reduction Act. They have to experiment to find the best battery mix. And they need a plan to recycle these batteries. That all requires scores and scores of workers.
