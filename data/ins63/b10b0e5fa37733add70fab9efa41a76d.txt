An influencer shares his 1-page media kit and explains how he's been successful pitching brands by keeping it 'short and sweet'
businessinsider.com
news
2022-05-27 10:00:00

In the competitive and fast-moving travel and creator industry, you might think you have to oversell yourself to stand out to attract brand deals.But one influencer told Insider that he believes a media kit should be an "elevator pitch," and not a full presentation of yourself.Eric Stoen, 50, of the Instagram account @travelbabbo, has amassed over 331,000 followers in the eight years he's been traveling and posting professionally. And he uses a one-page media kit to pitch hotels and potential clients.
