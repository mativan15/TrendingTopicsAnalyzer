YES Bank Q2 preview: NII may grow 25-30% YoY; analysts divided over bottomline
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 09:30:00

New Delhi: is likely to report a 25-30% growth in net interest income (NII) for the September quarter although analysts are mixed over the private lender's bottomline projections. The private sector lender posted an 11.6% year-on-year (YoY) growth in the loans and advances to Rs 1.92 lakh crore in Q2FY23. The lender's deposits stood at Rs 2 lakh crore as on September 30, up 13.2% YoY. Nuvama Institutional Equities expects a 19.3% improvement on a YoY basis in the CASA ratio, with a net interest income (NII) at Rs 1,890 crore, up 25.
