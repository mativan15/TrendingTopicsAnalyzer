REUTERS/Henry NichollsLONDON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Britain's new finance minister on Monday jettisoned most of Prime Minister Liz Truss' raft of fiscal measures, saying it was "not right" to borrow money to fund tax cuts, sending long-dated gilt prices sharply higher.The pound soared by as much as 1.4% to a session high of $1.
