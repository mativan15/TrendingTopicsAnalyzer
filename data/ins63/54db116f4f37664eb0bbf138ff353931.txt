Wilmington, Delaware, United States, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Wilmington, May 22, 2022. Xeniia (ξενία) meaning guest hospitality in greek will be the token representing HospoWise and will be the company's representation of the hospitality sector.  A series of new products are being built to serve the hospitality industry and replace existing systems and tech that is currently not working in the best interests of the individuals who work within the hospitality industry. These products will benefit businesses related to the industry concerned.
