By Lisa Petrillo October 21, 2022 / 10:35 AM / CBS Miami MIAMI -- A little bit of Brooklyn has landed in the heart of Brickell at DC Pie, which is located just under the metro mover.The restaurant is named after owner Dominic Cavagnuolo, who was inspired by his childhood Sunday dinners at the homes of his Italian grandmothers."I'm born and raised in Brooklyn," he said. "I was accustomed to eating great pizza every day. I wanted to take the ingredients for simplicity from my two Italian grandmothers and incorporate what I'm accustomed to eating as a kid, into thin crust brick oven pizza.
