APChinese manufacturing activity started to rebound in May after the government eased anti-virus restrictions that shut down Shanghai and other industrial centers, an official survey showed Tuesday.The contraction gripping China's manufacturing sector slowed in May, data showed Tuesday, as some factories gradually resumed work after officials eased some Covid lockdown measures. The Purchasing Managers' Index (PMI) -- a key gauge of manufacturing activity -- edged up to 49.6, officials said, from April's 47.
