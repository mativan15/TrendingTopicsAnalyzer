Epic Games Store Has Fallout 3 Free For This Week
ign.com
gaming
2022-10-21 08:45:20

The Epic Games Store has revealed its free games for this week. You can get Fallout 3: Game of the Year Edition and Evoland Legendary Edition from the store. Fallout 3 and Evoland are available for free on the Epic Games Store until October 27, 8:30 PM IST. Fallout 3: Game of the Year Edition Bethesda Game Studios' Fallout 3 came out in 2008 and is the first game in the Fallout series to use 3D graphics and real-time combat. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world where every minute is a fight for survival.
