Reduces board size to 12

CINCINNATI, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Cincinnati Financial Corporation (Nasdaq: CINF) announced today that the board of directors elected Dirk J. Debbink as the new lead independent director and chair of the nominating committee and added him to the executive committee, following the death of former lead independent director William Bahl. At the same time, the board reduced its size to 12 members.

Debbink has served on the Cincinnati Financial board since 2012, most recently as a member of the audit and nominating committees.
