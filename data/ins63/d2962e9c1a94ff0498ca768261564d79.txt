Horror as Brit mum dies after being struck by lightning on holiday

2022-10-20 10:32:00

A bright lightning bolt strikes (stock image) (Image: Getty)A mother died after she was struck by lightning while on holiday with her two children, an inquest heard.Daniella DiMambro suffered a cardiac arrest after being struck by lightning on a beach near Split-Dalmatia, Croatia, during her break. Ms DiMambro's inquest yesterday heard it is likely a freak lightning strike entered through her right ear.A bystander gave her CPR but the mum-of-two sustained hypoxic brain injuries (brain injuries which form due to a restriction on the oxygen being supplied to the brain).
