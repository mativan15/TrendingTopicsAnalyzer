HONG KONG, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Meituan (HKG: 3690) (the "Company"), China's leading e-commerce platform for services, today announced the unaudited consolidated results of the Company for the three months ended March 31, 2022.
Company Financial Highlights
During this quarter, our revenues increased by 25% to RMB46.3 billion from RMB37.0 billion for the same period of 2021. Our food delivery and in-store, hotel & travel segments achieved an aggregate operating profit of RMB5.1 billion for the first quarter of 2022, up from RMB3.
