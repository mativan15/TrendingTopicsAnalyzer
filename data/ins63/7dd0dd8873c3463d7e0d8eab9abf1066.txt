Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Auto index falls 0.27%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 12:30:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Auto index closed on a negative note on Friday.Shares of MRF(up 2.44 per cent), Mahindra & Mahindra(up 0.54 per cent) and Hero MotoCorp(up 0.17 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack.On the other hand, TIINDIA(down 3.08 per cent), Sona BLW Precision Forgings(down 1.4 per cent), Bosch(down 1.38 per cent), Bharat Forge(down 1.37 per cent) and Ashok Leyland(down 1.21 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.The Nifty Auto index closed 0.27 per cent down at 12695.
