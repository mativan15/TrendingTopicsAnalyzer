Cineflix Rights Presells ‘Last King of The Cross' to Sky
variety.com
news
2022-10-17 06:40:32

Cineflix Rights has pre-sold Helium's ten-part Australian crime drama 'Last King of The Cross' to Sky.  Starring Tim Roth, the series will air exclusively in all Sky territories in Italy, the UK, Ireland and Germany, the company has announced.  Inspired by John Ibrahim's best-selling autobiography, which follows his rise from a poverty-stricken immigrant to Australia's nightclub kingpin, 'Last King of The Cross' tells the story of two brothers who lose each other in their ascent to power. Set in Sydney's Kings Cross in the 1980s and 1990s, the cast includes Lincoln Younes ('Grand Hotel,' 'Tangle'), Claude Jabbour ('Eden,' 'Stateless'), and Callan Mulvey ('300: Rise of an Empire').
