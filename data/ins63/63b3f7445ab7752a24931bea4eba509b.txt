Photo: Getty ImagesThe Houston Astros will face the New York Yankees in the American League Championship Series. The Yankees took down the Cleveland Guardians 5-1 on Tuesday night in the Bronx to win the American League Division Series in five games. Game One of the best-of-seven series gets underway tonight at Minute Maid Park. The winner of the ALCS will advance to the World Series.
