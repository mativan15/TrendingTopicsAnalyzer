No gloom at Bloom despite concerns over rising production costs
irishtimes.com
news
2022-06-05 22:16:28

There was an air of upbeat defiance among the producers populating the food hall at Bloom on Sunday afternoon as they presented themselves to the public in weather and economic conditions which could best be described as miserable.Talk of rising prices, increased costs and increasingly intense pressure on supply chains was never too far away but the artisans and the food adventurers who had set up stall were still glad to be out meeting people face to face and selling everything from beetroot juice to bangers and nut butters.
