Brightwood Capital Advisors and PhenixFIN Lead Financing to Support CCP Capital Strategies' Acquisition of Wireless Maritime Services

news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Brightwood Capital Advisors ('Brightwood'), a leading private credit firm, and PhenixFIN Corporation ('PhenixFIN'), a publicly traded business development company, announced today that they have provided debt and equity financing to support CCP Capital Strategies' ('CCP') acquisition of 100 percent of Wireless Maritime Services, LLC ('WMS') stock from AT&T and Anuvu.

Formed in 2005 and headquartered in Miramar, Florida, WMS provides maritime cellular networks enabling secure, high-performance connectivity at sea.
