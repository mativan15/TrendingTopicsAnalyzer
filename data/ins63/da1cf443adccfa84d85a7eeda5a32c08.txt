A man has been charged with kidnap after two people were robbed at knife point in their car before being forced to drive to a cashpoint and withdraw money. Thames Valley Police says the incident, which saw one of the victims assaulted, happened in Ladygrove, Didcot, Oxfordshire on 15 October. Robert Bennett, 44, of Kibble Close, Didcot was remanded in custody to appear at Oxford Crown Court on 11 November. Mr Bennett is charged with two counts of kidnap, two counts of false imprisonment and one count each of robbery, attempted robbery and assault.
