Students are overwhelmingly in favor of continuing with hybrid learning, according to a global survey. For many students, the Covid-19 pandemic was their first sustained experience of online learning, as lockdowns forced universities around the world to cancel face-to-face teaching. And having got a taste for it, it seems the vast majority of students are keen to continue to embrace the benefits of online teaching.
