By Kosaku Narioka Kotak Mahindra Bank Ltd. is scheduled to report its second-quarter results on Saturday. Here's what you need to know: NET PROFIT FORECAST: The company is expected to post a 17% rise in net profit from a year earlier to 23.68 billion rupees ($285.9 million) on a standalone basis for the quarter ended Sept. 30, according to a poll of analysts by FactSet. That compares with INR20.32 billion a year earlier. WHAT TO WATCH: --SEGMENT RESULTS: Investors will be focusing on the performance of key segments.
