Trauma Is the Best Comedy Material. But April 7 Was Not Funny.
nytimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:28:31

I was eating a burger on Dizengoff Street in busy central Tel Aviv one night when I found myself fleeing a Palestinian shooter who had opened fire at a bar nearby. Since that day, I have imagined getting killed constantly. My therapist tells me I have PTSD.

When comedians encounter trauma, we typically hear an internal voice telling us: Good, more material. I heard that voice when the Covid-19 pandemic derailed the prestigious U.S.-based fellowship I was enrolled in and sent me home to Israel; I heard it when Covid made me terribly sick.
