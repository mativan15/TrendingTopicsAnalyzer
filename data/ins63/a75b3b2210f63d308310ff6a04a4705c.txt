VC fund Beenext tells startup founders how to adapt to the funding winter
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 06:00:00

ETtechBeenext founder and CEO Teruhide SatoBengaluru: Singapore-based early-stage investor Beenext which has backed companies like BharatPe, Open, Jupiter, among others, has joined the growing list of venture capital funds to issue cautionary notes to their portfolio companies asking them to reset and adapt to the current market downturn. Beenext, which is focussed on fintech investments has asked all founders to work together with their existing shareholders, revise budgets across marketing and headcount, while checking for availability of additional capital.
