Published: 04:11 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 04:11 EDT, 22 October 2022 Five Australian women allegedly strip searched at gunpoint at Doha Airport after a baby was found in a bathroom have sued the Qatari government for assault and battery.Hundreds of women were forcibly removed from aircraft at Doha on October 2, 2020 as officials searched for the mother of a newborn baby in a bathroom at the terminal.The women were removed by armed guards and many say they were forced to conduct non-consensual gynaecological or intimate physical examinations.
