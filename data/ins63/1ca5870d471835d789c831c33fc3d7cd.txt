‘I think that's the case': Cabinet minister refuses to say Liz Truss will survive

A Cabinet minister refused to predict Liz Truss will survive in No 10 until the general election – saying 'at the moment, I think that's the case'.Anne-Marie Trevelyan laid bare the deepening crisis in Downing Street – following the breakdown of discipline in the fracking vote – which has led some Tory MPs to call for the prime minister to be forced out today.Asked if Ms Truss would still be the Conservative leader when the election is fought, the transport secretary she believed she would be 'at the moment'.
