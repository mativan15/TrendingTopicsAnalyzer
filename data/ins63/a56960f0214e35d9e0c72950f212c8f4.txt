Gov. Noem: S.D. will sue if USDA withholds lunch money over transgender sports debate
washingtontimes.com
news
2022-06-03 13:30:00

South Dakota Gov. Kristi Noem is threatening to sue over new Biden administration rules that could withhold school meal funding from states that are deemed to be discriminating against students based on sexual orientation and gender identity. Ms. Noem's state is among more than a dozen to ban transgender athletes from girls' sports, raising questions about whether lunch money will be withheld once the state ban takes effect July 1.
