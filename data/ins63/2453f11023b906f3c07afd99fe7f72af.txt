The move comes amid fears of an energy shortage over winter.Germany's Cabinet on Wednesday approved an executive decision by Chancellor Olaf Scholz to keep the country's three remaining nuclear plants operating beyond an end date set by the government of former Chancellor Angela Merkel and originally confirmed by the current ruling coalition.
