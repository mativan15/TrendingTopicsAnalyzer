When she turned professional at 17 in December 2014, companies salivating for space on the Brooke Henderson billboard were aggressive in their offers. They had been circling for a while.The Smiths Falls, Ont., golfer was not only pegged as a future star because of her sterling amateur resumé, but she was viewed as someone with whom companies would be smart to align because of her personality and charm. She was young, athletic and talented, sure, but she was also wholesome, likable and photogenic.
