IBM Has to Pay BMC $1.6 Billion for Poaching AT&T Account

(Bloomberg) -- International Business Machines Corp. must pay $1.6 billion to BMC Software Inc. for swapping in its own software while servicing their mutual client, a federal judge ruled.

Most Read from Bloomberg

US District Judge Gray Miller in Houston on Monday rejected IBM's claim that the mutual client, AT&T Corp., opted to switch software products on its own. Miller awarded damages based on his earlier determination that IBM's role in the decision to dump BMC 'smacked of intentional wrongdoing.
