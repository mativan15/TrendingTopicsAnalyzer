HEN parties were left hundreds of pounds out of pocket last night after Butlers in the Buff went bust.
Owner Stacey Bramhall, 42, has called time on the UK stripping service after 20 years.

She said it had fallen foul of the cost of living crisis and the pandemic.
Stacey said: 'It's absolutely heartbreaking. It's not what we wanted to happen at all.
'We've been doing this for 20 years and I've lost my livelihood.
