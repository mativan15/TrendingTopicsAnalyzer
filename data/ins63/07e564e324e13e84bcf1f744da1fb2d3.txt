Genesis BBQ chicken to be offered at London East Asia Film Festival
naver.com
news
2022-10-19 18:12:00

A promotional image for bbq Night, which will be hosted by Genesis BBQ on Thursday, during the London East Asia Film Festival [GENESIS BBQ] Genesis BBQ will showcase its fried chicken at the London East Asia Film Festival, which runs through Oct. 30. At the opening gala of the film festival on Wednesday, director and actor Lee Jung-jae will hold a Q. and A. session for his new film 'Hunt.' During the event, Genesis BBQ will offer six varieties of its chicken, including the gold olive chicken and black pepper chicken.
