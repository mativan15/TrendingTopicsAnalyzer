APIndian Prime Minister Narendra ModiIn an attempt to address the futures of children who lost their parents to the COVID-19 pandemic, Prime Minister Narendra Modi has launched the PM CARES for Children programme, under which children orphaned by the coronavirus will receive benefits including a scholarship. "I know how difficult the situation is for people who have lost their family members during the Covid-19 pandemic. This program is for the children who lost their parents during the pandemic.
