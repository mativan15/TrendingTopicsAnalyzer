Scheffler and Spieth added to JP McManus Pro-Am field
irishtimes.com
sport
2022-05-31 00:00:00

World number one Scottie Scheffler and three-time Major winner Jordan Spieth are among the latest names confirmed for the 2022 JP McManus Pro-Am at Adare Manor in July.The American PGA Tour stars will be joined in the field by Rickie Fowler, Patrick Cantlay, Jason Kokrak, Sam Burns and Tom Hoge. Nine of the current top 11 players in the world golf ranking are now playing at the event on July 4-5.Already confirmed for the tournament in Limerick were a host of top professionals, including Tiger Woods, Rory McIlroy, Dustin Johnson, Jon Rahm, Leona Maguire, Brooks Koepka and Shane Lowry.
