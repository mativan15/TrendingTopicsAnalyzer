Share price of Zee Ent. jumps as Sensex gains 687.16 points
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 05:00:00

The share price of Zee Entertainment Enterprises Ltd. gained 5.22 per cent to Rs 277.05 at 10:00AM (IST) in Tuesday's trade. The scrip has hit a high of Rs 279.75 and low of Rs 271.3 so far during the session. The stock had closed at Rs 263.3 in the previous session. The counter has had a total traded volume of 719335 shares so far with a value of Rs 19.81 crore on the NSE. It traded at a price-to-earnings multiple of 30.7 and a price-to-book ratio of 2.55. A higher P/E ratio shows investors are willing to pay a higher price for per rupee earnings given by the stock because of better future growth expectations.
