American fashion company Ralph Lauren (RL) on Thursday apologized for selling clothing that copied indigenous Mexican designs.In an Instagram post on Thursday, the wife of Mexican President Andrés Manuel López Obrador, Beatriz Gutiérrez Müller, shared a photo of a Lauren by Ralph Lauren long-sleeve cardigan. In the post, Gutiérrez Müller writes that by copying traditional cultural designs, Ralph Lauren is "committing plagiarism" and "plagiarism is illegal and immoral.
