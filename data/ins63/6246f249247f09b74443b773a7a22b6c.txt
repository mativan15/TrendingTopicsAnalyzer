Contact, it's what makes us love football, and it's also the most challenging portion of the sport to accurately represent in a gridiron video game. When Madden 23 drops for multiple platforms on August 19, it will introduce a new gameplay concept called FieldSense. While not entirely related to collision detection, the new tech is designed to make contact more realistic and fun in Madden 23. I had an opportunity to sit down with gameplay producer Clint Oldenburg and creative director Connor Dougan to discuss gameplay and other aspects of the next version of Madden.
