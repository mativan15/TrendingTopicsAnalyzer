Poirier decided to skip a fight for the world title in order to take a third fight with McGregor last summer, and believes it was the right direction for he and his familyConor McGregor drops out of richest athletes list after being No.1 last yearDustin Poirier has claimed that his three fights with Conor McGregor earned him enough money for his family to have "financial freedom".
