Cincinnati's CinRx Pharma names Lonza's Dr. Gavin Samuels chief business officer
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 17:01:00

A Cincinnati-based biotech portfolio company looking to accelerate the development of new medicines is expanding its C-suite with a former physician who's served stints at Lonza, Merck and Pfizer.

Madisonville-based CinRx Pharma said Tuesday Dr. Gavin Samuels has assumed the role of chief business officer, a new position for the company. With the hire, Samuels becomes the fifth member of the CinRx leadership team.

In the position, Samuels will be responsible for leading corporate, strategic and business development and transactions for the CinRx portfolio.
