October 18, 2022 05:54 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Rep. Ted Budd (R-N.C.) for U.S. Senate, and Reps. Dan Bishop (R-N.C.), Virginia Foxx (R-N.C.), Richard Hudson (R-N.C.), Patrick McHenry (R-N.C.), Greg Murphy (R-N.C.), and David Rouzer (R-N.C.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.
