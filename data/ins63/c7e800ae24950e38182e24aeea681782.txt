IIFCL open to raise fund via green bonds to finance renewal energy projects
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-28 01:27:00

New Delhi: State-owned India Infrastructure Finance Company Ltd (IIFCL) on Friday said it is open to explore green bond route to raise funds to finance renewal energy sector projects. "Our subsidiary called IIFCL Projects is a certifying agent for green bonds for India. So, we are already into the system. The question is how it will help us further the green initiative by issuing bonds. As long as the company is getting suitable funds to invest in such bonds of IIFCL, we would definitely be open to that," IIFCL managing director P R Jaishankar said.
