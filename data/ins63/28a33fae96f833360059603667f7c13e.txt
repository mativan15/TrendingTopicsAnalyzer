NewsBusiness NewsIndia Business NewsAgarbatti sales ease as WFO startsIt's usually during a rough patch that people become more spiritual. During the pandemic, when people were working from home, there were multiple occasions of consumption of agarbattis (incense sticks) in a household, which led to a surge in demand for these. During those times, the agarbatti industry, according to experts, witnessed a growth of 30%. Now, with people returning to offices, the growth in the largely unorganised sector is back in single digits.
