Mother stabs daughter to death in Texas park: Police
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 08:12:57

A mother is alleged to have stabbed her daughter to death at a Texas park, according to police.The Harris County Sheriff's Office (HCSO) said a mother claimed to have killed her child at Spring Creek Park in Tomball on Sunday.According to Sheriff Ed Gonzalez, preliminary information suggested the child—possibly 5 or 6 years old—was stabbed by her mother and that the victim was transported to a nearby hospital shortly afterward.
