Top Amazon retail executive resigns less than two years after being named CEO
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 18:25:00

Dave Clark, who has spearheaded Amazon.com Inc.'s world-wide consumer business since the start of 2021, is resigning. The 23-year Amazon AMZN, -2.45% veteran, who was promoted to chief executive of the company's consumer organization to oversee massive expansion of warehouse and logistics operations last year, will resign July 1, Amazon announced in a regulatory filing Friday. In a tweet, Clark said it was 'time for me to say goodbye and start a new journey.' Amazon did not name a replacement for Clark.
