Kin raise issues with CMCH death certificate
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 08:15:00

Coimbatore: A section of people on Sunday complained that the Coimbatore Medical College and Hospital ( CMCH ) was not including the names of immediate relatives of the deceased in the death certificate, making it difficult for them to avail government welfare assistance or obtain documents.A 29-year-old man from Tirupur said he was running from pillar to post to make changes to his father's death certificate, as the same was not containing the names of his grandmother and mother. 'As the certificate does not have my mother's name, we are unable to obtain the legal heir certificate from the government.
