David Smith is an Associate Professor in American Politics and Foreign Policy at the US Studies Centre, University of Sydney EXPLAINED: Midterm elections in the United States elect the House of Representatives, a third of the Senate, and thousands of state legislative and executive offices. For all their magnitude and importance, these elections attract far less attention than presidential elections and have much lower turnout. But the November 8 2022 midterms, taking place in one of the most closely divided Congresses in history, could have far-reaching consequences.
