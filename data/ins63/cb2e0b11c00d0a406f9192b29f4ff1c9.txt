The results of the class 10 final exam for the arts and commerce stream are expected to be declared by the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSHSEB). As per the latest updates, students can expect their results by the 15th of June on the official website of the board. An official date has not been declared yet. The Gujarat board Class 10 result can be checked on gseb.org and gsebeservice.com. The results for the science stream have already been declared on May 12th. The GSEB Class 10 Board exams started on the 28th of March 2022 and ended on the 9th of April.
