Announcement from Business Editor Lori Montgomery, Deputy Business Editor Damian Paletta and Technology Editor Christina Passariello: We are thrilled to announce that Evelyn Larrubia is joining The Post as investigative editor for technology and business, leading a team of reporters who deliver revelatory journalism about the influence of money and technology on our government and our world.

Evelyn brings years of experience editing accountability and investigative journalism, often built on creative analysis of original data.
