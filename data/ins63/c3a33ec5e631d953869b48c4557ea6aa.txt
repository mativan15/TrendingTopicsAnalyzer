There is one thing missing from Tottenham Hotspur's impressive £1 billion ($1.12bn) home – a name. Since the 62,850-capacity stadium opened three-and-a-half years ago, the London club has been looking for a naming rights partner for what is currently 'Tottenham Hotspur Stadium'. Speaking in 2019, Spurs chairman Daniel Levy said there would only be a naming rights deal 'if we get the right brand, in the right sector, on the right money.
