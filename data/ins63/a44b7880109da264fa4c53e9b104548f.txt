New Jersey Bill Could Ban In-Car Subscriptions
cnet.com
news
2022-10-21 16:04:33

You may remember the brouhaha that broiled up after BMW first started offering subscriptions for items like heated seats -- outside the US, mind you. But if you don't, and the idea of paying more money for something already built into your car gets your own soup a-steamin', this proposed New Jersey bill could be right up your alley.State legislators in New Jersey have proposed a bill that would, as its synopsis states, "[Prohibit a] motor vehicle manufacturer or dealer from requiring subscription for certain motor vehicle features.
