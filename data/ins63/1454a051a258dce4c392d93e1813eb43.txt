The cost of living: Can I afford to have a baby?
smh.com.au
news
2022-05-30 19:00:00

The moment I realised I wanted to become a mother was also the moment it occurred to me that I wasn't in a financial position to afford a baby. In some abstract way, I've always figured I'd eventually have a baby. But it's only been recently, as I hurl towards the end of my twenties, that I've felt that longing.But these feelings are overshadowed by my anxiety about money. I am a 28-year-old middle-income earner living in Sydney, bringing in $65,000 a year in one of the most expensive cities in the world.
