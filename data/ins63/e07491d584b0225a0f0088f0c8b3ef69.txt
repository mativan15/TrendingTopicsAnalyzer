Greenwich LifeSciences Shares Rise 11% After CEO Increases Stake
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 17:51:00

By Chris Wack Greenwich LifeSciences Inc. shares were up 11% at $8.73 after a Form 4 filed to the Securities and Exchange Commission showed that Chief Executive Patel Snehal has raised his stake in the company. The company said in the filing that Mr. Snehal bought 6,800 shares on Thursday, at $7.38 a share, for a total value of $50,000. Mr. Snehal has direct ownership of 1.2 million shares as of Friday. He has made multiple stock purchases since the beginning of the year. Greenwich LifeSciences shares hit their 52-week low of $6.
