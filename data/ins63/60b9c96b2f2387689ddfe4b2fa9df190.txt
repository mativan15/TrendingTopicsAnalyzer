Generali is said to weigh takeover of Guggenheim asset management unit for $4B
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-21 19:44:29

Maksim Labkouski Assicurazioni Generali (OTCPK:ARZGF) (OTCPK:ARZGY), the biggest insurer in Italy, is progressing in its discussions to buy the asset management business of U.S.-based Guggenheim Partners, in a deal that could be valued at as much as $4B, Bloomberg reported Friday, citing people with knowledge on the situation. Generali (OTCPK:ARZGY) was said to have first weighed the acquisition of Guggenheim's asset management division to expand its asset arm at the end of September, when the stock climbed over 4% as a result of the news.
