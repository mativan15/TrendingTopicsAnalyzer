Record PLC

Record Plc engages in the provision of currency management services. Its products include passive and active hedging, and currency for return. It offers cash, collateral, tenor, and liquidity management; spot execution; transaction cost analysis; tenor management; asset-backed hedging; and cross currency liquidity. The company was founded by Neil Peter Record in 1983 and is headquartered in Windsor, the United Kingdom.
