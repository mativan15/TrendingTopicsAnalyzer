2022-10-15 15:04:00

A 53-year-old manufacturing company in Raleigh has been acquired by an investment startup in a move both companies say will create local jobs.

Three-year-old Rise Capital has bought out Sheet Metal Technologies (SMT). Sheet CEO Susan Rothecker said the deal, which closed Friday, will help the longtime Raleigh manufacturer scale up.

It's a point Harry Smith, former Flanders CEO and co-founder of Rise Capital, doubled down on in an interview. Smith said that not only will all 150 of SMT's current employees still have jobs under Rise, 'but our plan is to make it 300 employees.
