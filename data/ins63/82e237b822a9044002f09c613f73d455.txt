Here is all you need to know about the Ligue 1 clash between Paris Saint Germain and Olympique de Marseille on Monday.
League leader PSG will take on third placed Olympique de Marseille in Ligue 1 on Monday at the Parc des Princes stadium, Paris. Unbeaten since the start of this year's league campaign, the Parisians will hunt for a win that will further solidify their spot at the top of the league table.
