HP Q2 Profit, Revenues Beat Street View

(RTTNews) - HP Inc. (HPQ), the PC and printer business of the former Hewlett-Packard Co., on Tuesday reported results for the second quarter, with both profit and revenues beating Wall Street analysts' estimates, driven by strong demand for computers. HP's second-quarter profit was $1.00 billion or $0.94 per share, compared with net income of $1.23 billion or $0.98 per share last year. Adjusted earnings were $1.08 per share, compared with $0.93 per share in the year-ago quarter. Analysts polled by Thomson Reuters expected earnings of $1.
