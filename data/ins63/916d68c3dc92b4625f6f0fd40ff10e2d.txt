Dwayne Johnson turns heads in shiny suit at UK premiere for Black Adam
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 21:45:48

Stars of DC Comics' latest blockbuster Black Adam took to the red carpet for the movie's premiere in London's Leicester Square.
Dwayne Johnson – who stars as the titular lead – smiled from ear to ear while rocking a bold purple tuxedo as he joined co-stars Pierce Brosnan, Sarah Shahi and Noah Centineo.
Former 007 frontman Pierce donned various shades of blue in his formal get-up, while To All The Boys I Loved Before star Noah rocked a smart casual look.
Love Island winners Ekin-su Culculoglu, boyfriend Davide Sanclimenti, comedian Jack Whitehall and presenter Kate Garraway – who stayed true to the theme of the movie and wore a hot pink cape.
