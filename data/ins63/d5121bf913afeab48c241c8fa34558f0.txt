 Middlesex OPP are investigating a sudden death in Weldon Park after a body was located early Monday morning.
Just before 6:15 a.m., police received a report of a sudden death at 21466 Richmond Street in Arva. The deceased has not been identified, pending next-of-kin notification.However, investigators determined that the death is not suspicious.Weldon Park has been closed to the public as police continue the investigation.
