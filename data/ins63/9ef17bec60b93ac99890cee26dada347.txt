We'll win Munugode bypoll with big majority, claims Union Minister Bhupender Yadav
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-19 12:37:36

Union Minister for Labour, Employment and Environment Bhupender Yadav expressed the confidence that the BJP will win the Munugode bypoll with a big majority and also the Assembly polls later to form a democratic and people's government by the ‘lotus' (BJP symbol) on Wednesday.'People of Telangana are yearning for a change as the objective for which the separate state has been formed remains unfulfilled. This was discernible during my last visit to the constituency and also from the response to our TS party president Bandi Sanjay Kumar's ‘padayatra'.
