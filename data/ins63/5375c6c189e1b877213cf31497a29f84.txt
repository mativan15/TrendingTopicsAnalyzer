British farms could be the next tech-led revolution
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

Pretty much everyone involved in the endless debate about Britain's economic future agrees that better use of technology is critical to delivering higher growth. Less obvious is the fact that some of that transformative tech could be found hanging around a cow's neck.Whatever its other misjudgments, the Treasury's growth plan last month was right to identify agricultural productivity as an important area for improvement.Though farming can look like a traditional, even timeless activity, around the world many farms are changing, using better kit and management techniques to become more efficient.
