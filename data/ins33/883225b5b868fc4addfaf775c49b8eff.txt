Published: 03:29 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 05:30 EDT, 21 October 2022 WARSAW, Poland (AP) - Two stories beneath a modern steel production plant on Warsaw's northern edge lies an untouched Cold War relic: a shelter containing gas masks, stretchers, first aid kits and other items meant to help civil defense leaders survive and guide rescue operations in case of nuclear attack or other disasters. A map of Europe on a wall still shows the Soviet Union - and no independent Ukraine. Old boots and jackets give off a musty odor.
