Tellurian stock slides on plans to sell $500M senior convertible notes

finance
2022-06-01 12:20:20

Tellurian (NYSE:TELL) has entered into definitive agreements to sell $500M of senior secured convertible notes, expiring May 1, 2025 and bearing 6.0% per annum interest.The notes will be convertible into shares of Tellurian (TELL) common stock at an initial conversion price of $5.724.TELL shares down ~4% pre-market
