Officials rebut claims that Russian troops have surrounded the city of Severodonetsk in eastern Ukraine
cnn.com
news
2022-05-27 04:00:54

Ukraine's President said the situation in Donbas is "very difficult" as Russia ramps up firepower and manpower, but vowed the region would be "Ukrainian again." Ukrainian officials denied claims that Severodonetsk in embattled Luhansk is surrounded by Russian forces, but acknowledged Russia holds one part of the city.The US Defense Department said it is "mindful and aware" of Ukraine's request for multiple-launch rocket systems, but "decisions … haven't been made yet." CNN reported Thursday the US was preparing to send the weapons as part of a larger package of assistance.
