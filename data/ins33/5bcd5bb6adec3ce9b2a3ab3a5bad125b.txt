Do we have to separate you two? Best, worst companion plants

2022-05-31 13:29:00

We gardeners spend so much time stressing over what our plants need that we often forget they grew just fine for millennia without us. Suppose we step back from our self-aggrandizing human nature and take our cues from actual nature instead?That would save time, money and the environment by reducing the need for fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides -- even garden stakes. Many plants co-exist in either symbiotic or non-complementary relationships. Native Americans noticed this centuries ago, and used their findings to take advantage of plants' natural inclinations.
