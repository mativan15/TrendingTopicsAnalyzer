Matt Turner has revealed he became an Arsenal fan as they were the first team he picked on the video game FIFA.Turner, who joined Arsenal this summer from MLS side New England Revolution for around £7million, revealed his delight at the time when he made the move.The goalkeeper used to get up in the early hours of the morning to watch Arsenal games and he has now explained where his love for the team came.'I mean it's not really the best story ever about supporting Arsenal,' said Turner.'It was after the World Cup in 2010 I bought my first game of FIFA and when it says, ‘What club do you want to support or select?'.
