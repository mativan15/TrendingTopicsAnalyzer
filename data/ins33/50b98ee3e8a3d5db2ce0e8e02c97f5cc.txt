The Johnny Depp, Amber Heard Trial Verdict is In: What to Know
cnet.com
news
2022-06-01 22:24:00

A jury on Wednesday found both Johnny Depp and Amber Heard liable for defamation but awarded more money to Depp. The decision follows a highly publicized trial that took place in Fairfax, Virginia, and spanned several weeks.Depp sued ex-wife Heard for defamation over a 2018 op-ed she wrote for The Washington Post, seeking $50 million in damages. On Wednesday, a jury found Depp had been defamed by Heard in the piece and awarded him $10 million in compensatory damages and $5 million in punitive damages.
