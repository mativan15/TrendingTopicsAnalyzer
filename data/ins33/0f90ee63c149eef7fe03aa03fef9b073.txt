June 02, 2022 08:16 AM Eastern Daylight Time ALPHARETTA, Ga.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bakkt Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: BKKT), a trusted digital asset platform that enables consumers to buy, sell, send and spend a range of digital assets, announced today it will participate in the Piper Sandler Global Exchange & FinTech Conference. Management will attend the conference, which will include a fireside chat with Gavin Michael, Chief Executive Officer, on June 9th, at 2:30PM ET.
