Halloween event to promote sales and tourism in BangkokA shopper looks on as he walks past a staff member wearing a dread costume during a promotion event to celebrate Halloween at the Seacon Square Srinagarindra shopping mall in Bangkok, Thailand, 21 October 2022. Participants, vendors and staff members wear horror make-up and dread costumes to haunt and entertain shoppers in the Halloween event at the shopping mall aimed at boosting their sales and promote tourism in an effort to recover the business after suffering from the prolonged COVID-19 coronavirus pandemic.
