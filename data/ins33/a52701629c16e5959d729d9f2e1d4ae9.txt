The Great Resignation is spurring the young and talented to climate careers

2022-06-01 18:49:00

In Silicon Valley, few principles are as foundational and accepted as Moore's Law, the observation that as the capabilities of our computers increase, the costs will decrease. We are seeing this principle in action once more as enormous human talent and unprecedented capital stream into climate tech. Thankfully, the ability of the private sector to take a leading role in accelerating and scaling climate solutions is completely different from ten, five or even two years ago. We are finally in a position to drive necessary innovation because six key market forces have aligned to create optimal investment conditions to save our planet.
