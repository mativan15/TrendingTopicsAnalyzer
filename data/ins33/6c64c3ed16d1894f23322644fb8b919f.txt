20th Congress: Galwan Valley clash clip shown at key China meet opening

BEIJING: The Chinese military commander injured in the Galwan Valley clash with Indian troops in June, 2020 attended the opening of the Communist Party of China (CPC) 20th national congress at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing on Sunday.Qi Fabao was one of the 304 delegates from the People's Liberation Army (PLA) and People's Armed Police chosen to attend the all-important meeting of the party.The Congress was inaugurated by President Xi Jinping who read out a lengthy report, addressing the nearly 2,300 delegates and invitees at the opening.
