The countdown to Pride Month will be celebrated nationwide through a digital telethon program simulcast across Instagram, TikTok, Youtube, and on Inviz.tv.LOS ANGELES, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Inviz.tv, an Invisible Narratives company, today announces the first-ever "Pride Eve" celebration, an annual holiday to celebrate and countdown to Pride Month.To launch the first-ever Pride Eve, Inviz.tv has partnered with over 100 creators, influencers, and artists, reaching an audience of millions, to establish one day for members of the LGBTQ+ community and allies to come together - no matter where they are located - to celebrate Pride as one.
