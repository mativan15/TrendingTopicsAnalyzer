Physician Services is the leading strategy into key markets of Washington, Oregon and FloridaNEWARK, Del., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Asenda Health Inc. (the 'Company'), through its wholly owned subsidiary Kater Medical, has acquired a controlling 61% interest in Washington based Post Acute Healthcare ('Post Acute') with commitment to purchase the remaining 39% over the next two years. Post Acute is an outsourced physician services business currently conducting 3,000 monthly patient encounters through contracts with 18 long-term care and skilled nursing facilities in Washington and Oregon.
