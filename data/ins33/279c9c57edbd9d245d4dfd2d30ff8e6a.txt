Harrisburg, PA, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Today, Pace-O-Matic announced that its attorneys negotiated a settlement that includes the return of its skill games, related equipment and money seized without qualification. This property was wrongfully seized during raids conducted by the Pennsylvania Bureau of Liquor Control Enforcement and Clearfield County District Attorney's office in September of 2021.
