Man arrested on charge of violating bail conditions

The Chengamanad police on Saturday arrested a man on the charge of illegal fund collection in violation of his bail conditions. The arrested was identified as Vinu Vikraman, 29, of Nedumbassery. He is accused of trespassing into a park selling construction materials with two other accomplices, threatening the workers and demanding money from the owner. They also allegedly threatened to kill the owner if he declined to pay the money or informed the police.
The arrested is a key accused in the murder of a man at Athani in 2019.
