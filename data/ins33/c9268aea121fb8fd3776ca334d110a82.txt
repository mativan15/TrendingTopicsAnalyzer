*This episode originally aired on June 5, 2019. We have updated this story to ask Safaneh Mohaghegh Neyshabouri, a former PhD student who is now an assistant professor, to respond to the current situation in Iran.Unprecedented mass demonstrations continue to erupt across Iran after the death of a Mahsa Amini. The 22-year old Kurdish woman died while detained by Iran's 'morality police' in Tehran. "Women are spearheading the current movement," said Safaneh Mohaghegh Neyshabouri, assistant professor of gender and sexuality studies and Muslim cultures at the University of Calgary.
