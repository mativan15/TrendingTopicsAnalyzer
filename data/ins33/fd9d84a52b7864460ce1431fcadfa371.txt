October 17, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time DENVER--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Funding Circle, the leading online platform for small business loans, announced today that it is now operational in Nevada, allowing it to provide the state's more than 280,000 small businesses with much-needed access to capital. With operations live in Nevada, Funding Circle now offers loans to businesses in all 50 states and Washington, D.C.



In Nevada, securing capital funding has historically been very difficult for small businesses.
