jroballo/iStock via Getty Images Written by Nick Ackerman. This article was originally published to Cash Builder Opportunities members on May 28th, 2022. AT&T (NYSE:T) has rewarded us again. Not as shareholders but as option sellers. AT&T (and Verizon (VZ)) are often great examples of what conservative option sellers are looking for. Historically, they offered low volatility - though lately, they haven't been quite the defensive companies we have seen historically. The volatility of 2022 has been hitting these telecoms.
