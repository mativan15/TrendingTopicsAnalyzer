Double Upgrade For Iamgold After Rosebel Sale: These Assets Could Be Next, Analyst Says
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-19 18:42:33

Iamgold Corp. (NYSE:IAG) sold a 95% interest in Rosebel Gold Mines N.V. to Zijin Mining of China at a price that is 'well above" the carrying value of $177 million. The deal represents a significant move in addressing the funding gap for the development of the Côté Gold mine, according to Bank of America Securities. The Analyst: Lawson Winder upgraded the rating for Iamgold from Underperform to Buy, while raising the price target from $1.20 to $1.65. The Thesis: The sale adds $360 million of cash to the company's liquidity of $453 million in cash and $349 million available on credit facility, Winder said in the upgrade note.
