October 18, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time FAYETTEVILLE, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--White River Energy Corp ('White River') (OTC: WTRV), formerly (OTC: FRTM), today announced that the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority ('FINRA') has approved the company's requested change in its ticker symbol from FRTM to WTRV. White River commenced trading under this new ticker symbol on October 18, 2022.

About White River Energy Corp

White River is a vertically integrated energy company with oil and gas exploration, production, and drilling operations on over 30,000 cumulative acres of active mineral leases in Louisiana and Mississippi.
