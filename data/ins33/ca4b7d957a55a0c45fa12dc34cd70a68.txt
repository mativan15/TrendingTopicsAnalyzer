ScreenDaily CEO Jennifer Mullin and group COO Andrea Scrosati address scripted push in Mipcom keynote.Fremantle's senior leadership has stressed the 'great opportunity' and potential for 'enormous upside' from investing in feature films.In a keynote Mipcom interview, Fremantle CEO Jennifer Mullin and group COO Andrea Scrosati talked up the company's growth in scripted TV and film.Fremantle has invested heavily in the sector in recent years, acquiring companies such as Ireland and UK's Element Pictures and Italy's Lux Vide and Wildside.
