Image caption, Nadhim Zahawi had been at the University of Warwick to speak to its Conservative AssociationEducation Secretary Nadhim Zahawi had to be escorted off a university campus by security guards after being heckled by LGBT+ protesters.The Conservative MP for Stratford-upon-Avon had been at the University of Warwick on Friday to talk at the university's Conservative Association.Members of the university's Warwick Pride group shouted "Tory scum" outside and waved flags and placards.The group were protesting over comments made by Mr Zahawi.
