REUTERS/Lucas Jackson/FilesOct 20 (Reuters) - Dow Inc (DOW.N) on Thursday posted a 56% fall in third-quarter profit, as the chemicals giant took a hit from soaring energy prices and a slower-than-expected recovery in demand.Dow's chemicals, used in industries ranging from automobiles and food packaging to electronics, are seeing higher energy costs, especially in Europe, as well as lower demand as consumers tighten purse strings amid a global economic slowdown.
