Magnopus is a Business Reporter clientThere's a lot of talk about what the metaverse could, should or will be, but the real question is: how can businesses build in the metaverse today?One of the biggest opportunities for your business strategy right now is to create entirely new virtual expressions of brand and customer experience. But many businesses' first forays into the metaverse have started with spatial and operational replication. They build a three-dimensional version, or digital twin, of their store on a digital platform such as Roblox, for example.
