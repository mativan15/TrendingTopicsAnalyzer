Illustration: Dan Page Investors are pouring billions of dollars into annuities, as worries about stagflation and a U.S. recession whipsaw the stock and bond markets. These complex financial tools, a mix of insurance and investment, come in a variety of product types and are sold as a way to safeguard retirement assets until they can be tapped for a stream of income.
