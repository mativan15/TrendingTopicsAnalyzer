Rupee gains 2 paise to 77.59 against US dollar in early trade
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 07:00:00

In the previous session, the rupee settled at 77.61 against the American currency.Mumbai: The rupee appreciated 2 paise to 77.59 against the US dollar in the opening trade on Friday, supported by heavy buying in domestic equities and a weak greenback in the overseas markets. However, rising global crude prices and foreign fund outflows capped the rupee's gain, forex traders said. At the interbank foreign exchange, the rupee opened at 77.60 against the American dollar and then inched up to quote at 77.
