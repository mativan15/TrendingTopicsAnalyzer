New dividend-based infrastructure ETF may be available soon
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-18 12:53:17

JaCZhou BNY Mellon intends to launch the BNY Mellon Global Infrastructure Income ETF, a fund meant to focus on dividend-paying infrastructure companies. Per the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the fund seeks to capitalize on infrastructure-related stocks that offer a combination of cash flow stability, sustainable business models, dividend payment potential and quality valuation metrics. Moreover, according to the prospectus, 'infrastructure refers to the structures, networks, systems and facilities necessary for the operation, function, growth or development of a society or economy.
