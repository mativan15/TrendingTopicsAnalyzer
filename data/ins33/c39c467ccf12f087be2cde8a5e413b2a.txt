PNC Bank in Woodmere Held Up, Suspect At Large, PNC Offering a Reward
iheart.com
news
2022-10-17 17:40:00

WOODMERE - Police say on Saturday around 10 am, a man entered the PNC bank on Chargin Blvd in Woodmere, Ohio. That man handed the bank teller a note that demanded money, the teller gave an unknown amount of cash, and that man left the bank.

Police and PNC bank are looking for that man.

The suspect is an African American man who stands around 6'1". The suspect, pictured below, was last seen wearing a black durag, a blue covid mask, black pants, a long sleeve black shirt, and a gray vest.
