17 October 2022 by Drew Tinnin 
Slash Film Director David Bruckner is on a roll. After helming the critically lauded haunter "Night House," starring the spectacular Rebecca Hall as a widow discovering a dark secret, Bruckner is currently enjoying the overwhelmingly positive reviews for "Hellraiser" on Hulu, his re-introduction to Clive Barker's perversely alluring universe. With the newly announced "V/H/S/85," he's also returning to the found footage anthology franchise that helped make him a writer and director to watch.
