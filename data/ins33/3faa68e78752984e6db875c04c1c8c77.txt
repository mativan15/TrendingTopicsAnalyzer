HC seeks status of State govt.'s move to enact law against superstitious practices

2022-10-18 13:07:03

A Division Bench of the Kerala High Court on October 18 asked the State government to inform the Court about the status of the steps taken to enact a law for preventing and eradicating evil practices, including sorcery and black magic.The Bench passed the directive on public interest litigation (PIL) filed by the Kerala Yukthivadi Sanghom, seeking a directive to the State government to implement the recommendation of the State Law Reforms Commission to enact a law against evil practices.When the petition came up for hearing, the State government submitted that it had already taken steps to bring such a law.
