Goldy Brar, a Canadian gangster, allegedly claimed responsibility for the murder of Punjabi artist and Congress politician Sidhu Moose Wala in a Facebook post on Sunday evening. Goldy Brar is a close ally of gang leader Lawrence Bishnoi, who is also suspected of murdering the singer. According to a report by India Today, Punjab Police has compiled a dossier on Goldy Brar on Monday. The dossier indicates that Goldy Brar's full name is Satwinderjit Singh. Satwinderjit Singh, alias Goldy Brar, is a BA graduate who was born in 1994.
