Australia has recorded a second consecutive defeat in the Constellation Cup, with New Zealand triumphing 52-48 in Tauranga.The Diamonds led 25-24 at half-time but were outplayed in the final two quartersThe Silver Ferns hold a 2-0 series leadThe third and fourth Constellation Cup Tests will be played in AustraliaThe Diamonds held one-point leads at quarter-time and half-time, before the Silver Ferns took control of the match in the third term.The home side led 39-34 at the final break, before doing enough to keep their noses in front in the fourth quarter.
