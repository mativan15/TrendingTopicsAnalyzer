IDC Maintains Its Forecast for the Worldwide IT and Business Services Market Despite a Looming Recession
businesswire.com

2022-10-21 00:00:00

October 21, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEEDHAM, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Worldwide IT and business services revenue is expected to grow (in constant currency) 5.7% this year and 5.2% in 2023, according to the International Data Corporation (IDC) Worldwide Semiannual Services Tracker. In nominal dollar denominated revenue based on today's exchange rate, the market will grow by 2% this year, due to currency headwinds.



The 2022 market growth represents a slight increase of 12 basis points from IDC's April 2022 forecast.
