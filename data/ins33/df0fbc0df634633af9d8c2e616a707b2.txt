U-M faculty and staff who are among the estimated 154,000 Michigan residents eligible for federal public-service loan forgiveness of federal student loan debt now have until Oct. 21 to apply to the Temporary Expanded Public Service Loan Forgiveness program to get credit for payments that previously didn't qualify. View the Federal Student Aid website at studentaid.gov for details and eligibility requirements.
