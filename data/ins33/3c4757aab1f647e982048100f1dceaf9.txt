Dollar heads toward monthly loss as investors scale back bets
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-05-30 06:25:25

The dollar suffered losses last week and was headed toward its first monthly decline in five months on Monday as investors have eased their bets that rising U.S. rates will result in additional gains. Concerns about a global recession have begun to wane a little as well.Trade was expected to be lessened through Monday as U.S. stock and bond markets close for Memorial Day.
