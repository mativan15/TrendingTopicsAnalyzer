Dow jumps more than 500 points as bond yields fall after reports Fed may shift to smaller rate hikes after November
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 09:21:00

U.S. stocks were trading sharply higher Friday as investors weighed a story from the Wall Street Journal and comments from Federal Reserve officials suggesting that the central bank might shift to smaller interest-rate rises after its November meeting. Investors were also coping with intraday volatility in stocks as more than $1.7 trillion in notional value of options on stocks, indexes and exchange-traded funds have expired, or will expire, on Friday, according to Goldman Sachs. The Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +1.
