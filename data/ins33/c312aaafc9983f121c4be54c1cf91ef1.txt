Published: 14:49 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 14:52 EDT, 17 October 2022 During his presidency, Donald Trump's hotels charged Secret Service agents more than five times the government rate while they were tasked with protecting the former president, according to a new report by the House Oversight Committee.Beginning in January 2017 through Sept. 15, 2021 the Secret Service obtained more than 40 waivers to spend beyond the recommended per diem rates at Trump properties to protect the Trump family during and after his presidency.
