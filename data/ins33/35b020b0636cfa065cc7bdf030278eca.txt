A mum has been left furious after her neighbour's two teenagers began playing loud music for 12 hours a day while home from school - distracting her from her workThe mum has been left furious by the noise (stock photo) If you're still working from home, you'll know how irritating outside noises can be - from neighbours completing DIY projects to dogs barking, and even the noise of the bin men coming to empty your wheelie bin. But one mum has been left particularly outraged after her neighbour's two teenagers began "blasting" music from their house every single day for 12 hours at a time, meaning she can't get any work done.
