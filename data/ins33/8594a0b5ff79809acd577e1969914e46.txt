The BJP declared its first list of 62 candidates for the Himachal Pradesh assembly election on Wednesday and fielded chief minister Jairam Thakur from Seraj seat in Mandi. The party has dropped 11 MLAs, including one minister, and given ticket to 19 new faces in this election, which is expected to be a direct contest between the BJP and the Congress. Thakur filed his nomination papers on Wednesday. 
The BJP's central election committee (CEC) held a meeting on Tuesday evening to discuss the candidates.
