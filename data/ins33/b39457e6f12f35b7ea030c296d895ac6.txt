Stock Futures Slip After Major Averages Register Two Days of Losses
nbcsandiego.com
business
2022-10-20 23:00:00

Stock futures are down as investors came out of a tumultuous day with bond yields rising and wide-ranging corporate earnings. Futures for the Nasdaq 100 slipped 0.6%, while futures linked to the Dow Jones Industrial Average lost 42 points or 0.1%. S&P 500 futures dropped about 0.4%. The indexes saw a second consecutive day of slides during regular trading, with the Dow shedding 90.22 points, or 0.3%. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq Composite were down 0.8% and and 0.6%, respectively. It was a day that started on better footing for the Dow, which hit nearly 400 points at session highs, but rising Treasury yields slashed threw cold water on stocks.
