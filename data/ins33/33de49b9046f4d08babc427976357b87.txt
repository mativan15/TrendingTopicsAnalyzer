'Maja Ma' has been the talk of the "internet" since it premiered on October 6th. The film has been directed by Anand Tiwari of "Bandish Bandits" fame and stars Gajraj Rao, Ritwik Bhowmik, Rajit Kapur, Simone Singh, Sheeba Chaddha, and Srishti Shrivastava. ScreengrabWhile there were many mixed reactions to the film, Madhuri Dixit and Simone Singh's portrayals of queer characters have been lauded by everyone. The movie took everyone by surprise when the lead character in a mainstream film was shown coming out of the closet after decades of being married and having kids.
