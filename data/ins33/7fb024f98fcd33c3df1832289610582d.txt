Actor-filmmaker-producer Prithviraj Sukumaran celebrates his 40th birthday today (Oct 16). The multifaceted talent who ventured into showbiz as an actor with the 2002 Malayalam film ‘Nandanam' shortly spread his wings to other industries including Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi. In his illustrious career spanning over two decades, Prithviraj Sukumaran has made notable contributions not only as an actor but also to the entertainment industry, especially Malayalam. The young star has multiple projects in various languages lined up for release and on this special day, let's take a look at the most awaited upcoming films of Prithviraj Sukumaran.
