10 Toronto-area theatre experiences you can't miss this summer
thestar.com
news
2022-05-28 11:00:00

Record heatwaves, shortages of fans and air conditioners in big-box retailers, and a rush of folks high-tailing it to cottage country can mean only one thing: summer has hit Southern Ontario.It's a welcome shift in the performance sector. The last few months have been strange ones for theatre in the GTA and beyond: there have been a plethora of conflicting opening nights, for instance, and numerous shows have had to cut their runs short due to COVID cases within casts and creative teams. But despite the chaos of the last few months, on their way are warmer weather and a powerful new season of theatre.
