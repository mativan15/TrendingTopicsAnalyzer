Pune: The Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB) sleuths have booked assistant police inspector (API) Harshada Balasaheb Dagade and police constable Abhijit Vitthal Palke, both attached with Kondhwa police station for demanding a bribe of ₹50,000. According to the ACB sleuths Palke had demanded the bribe money from the complainant for helping by not arresting his family members in a case registered against them while Dagade assisted him in the crime.
