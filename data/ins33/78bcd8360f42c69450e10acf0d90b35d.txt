(NOAA via AP)As the Atlantic hurricane season approaches, a new poll shows an increasing number of Floridians refuse to leave their homes when a hurricane threatens. While more Florida residents are becoming concerned about this year's hurricane season, a new survey found there are still a lot of people without an emergency plan for the season.
