AgenciesApple seems to be ready for the big switch to type C with its iPhone 15 lineup. Apple revealed its current lineup of iPhone 14 on the 6 September, 2022, but rumours about the new series have already started to spread. Now a new report has claimed that Apple is finally switching to Type C charing port with the iPhone 15 series. The trillion-dollar company will likely include a type C charging port instead of its 10-year-old lighting port with its next lineup of iPhone 15 in Europe. As per reports, the alpha testing of USB type-C charging ports on new iPhones is already underway.
