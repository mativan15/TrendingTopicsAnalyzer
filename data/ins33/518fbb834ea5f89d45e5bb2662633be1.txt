A 14-year-old girl caused a stir at a north Florida middle school when she handed out more than $10,000 that she is accused of stealing from her grandmotherMarion County deputies responded to Lake Weir Middle School in Summerfield on Thursday after reports that she gave classmates hundreds of dollars each The girl said she had been given the money by an unknown former student who wanted the money disseminated By Friday, officials had recovered about $700 of the money that had been given to other studentsThe girl was arrested and charged with grand theft By Dailymail.
