Members of the Forbes Under 30 community mentored 11 early-stage Middle Eastern startups Wednesday as part of a pitch competition at the 2022 Forbes Under 30 Summit EMEA, co-hosted by Jerusalem Venture Partners. Erez Elmanuzir 
Less than 24 hours after Israel and the United Arab Emirates signed a monumental free-trade agreement, hundreds of young entrepreneurs gathered in Jerusalem to pitch peaceful solutions of their own. 
Situated under the vibrant cherry trees at Margalit Startup City, one of Startup Nation's signature innovation hubs, members of the Forbes Under 30 community mentored 11 early-stage Middle Eastern startups Wednesday as part of a pitch competition at the 2022 Forbes Under 30 Summit EMEA, co-hosted by venture capital firm Jerusalem Venture Partners.
