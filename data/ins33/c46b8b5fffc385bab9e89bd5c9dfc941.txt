A woman has heaped praise on a bloke she recently went on a date with after he took steps to make sure she felt 'safe' with one drink request he made to their waiterThe woman praised her date for his actions (stock photo) For many women, going on a first date with a total stranger can be a daunting experience, especially if you've met them online and you have no idea who they really are as a person. And while so many of us may feel anxious in those situations, one woman on Reddit has expressed her shock after a man she went on a date with went above and beyond to make sure she felt "safe" - with one simple request to their waiter.
