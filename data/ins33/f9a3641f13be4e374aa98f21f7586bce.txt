Despite my feeling disillusioned about writing for Seeking Alpha, I decided it was time to at least offer an update to my readers. Better late than never, I suppose. I have spent the past couple of years focused on other endeavors, including updating my e-book and e-course Perpetual Income With Closed-End Funds.
