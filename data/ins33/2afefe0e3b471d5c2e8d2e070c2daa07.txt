The state government will expand its container deposit scheme, with the 'return and earn' program set to include wine casks and bottles and other large bottles.The government has opened community consultation on the scheme's expansion, which it claims will result in an extra 400 million eligible bottles recycled each year.A return and earn sorting facility in Sydney's west.Credit:Quentin Jones 'The scheme expansion would boost recycling rates, reduce landfill and supercharge our push towards a circular economy in NSW,' said Environment Minister James Griffin in a media release on Saturday.
