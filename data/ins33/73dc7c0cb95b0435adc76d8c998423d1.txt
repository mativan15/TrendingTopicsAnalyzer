Quarterly Revenue Increases 55% Year Over Year

TORONTO, ON / ACCESSWIRE / May 30, 2022 / EQ Inc. (TSXV:EQ) ("EQ Works" or the "Company"), a leader in geospatial data and artificial intelligence driven software, announced its financial results today for the first quarter ended March 31, 2022.

Announcing it's highest first quarter revenue in over a decade, EQ reported revenue of over $2.7 million, a 55% increase when compared to the first quarter of 2021. This revenue growth was the result of new data engagements and the Company's continued focus on key verticals, including financial services, automotive, and retail.
