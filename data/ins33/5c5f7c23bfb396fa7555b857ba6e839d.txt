October 20, 2022 10:59 AM Eastern Daylight Time PLANO, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NTT DATA, a global digital business and IT services leader, today announced its sponsorship of an historic effort by explorer Robert Swan, OBE, to complete his walk across the full breadth of Antarctica while demonstrating sustainable technologies and renewable energy.



Swan is the first person to walk to the North and South Poles, and he now seeks to complete his goal of walking across the entire Antarctic landmass.
