Don't Give These Recalled Similac and Pedialyte Products to Your Kids

tech
2022-10-17 16:00:00

Although the situation isn't as dire as it was earlier this year, the unprecedented baby formula shortage has yet to be resolved. And if you have managed to get your hands on some, you're going to want to check the label: Abbott Labs recently issued a voluntary recall for several of its Similac brand formula products, as well as certain lots of its Pedialyte Electrolyte Solution, according to an announcement from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Here's what to know.Why is formula being recalled now?It's been a rough year for parents and caregivers who rely on formula to feed their children.
