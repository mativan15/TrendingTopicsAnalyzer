Former Japan and Celtic star Shunsuke Nakamura to retire at end of year
japantimes.co.jp
news
2022-10-17 00:00:21

Former Japan and Celtic star Shunsuke Nakamura, currently at Yokohama FC in the J. League second division, is set to retire at the end of the season, Kyodo News learned Monday.
The 44-year-old midfielder, known for his deadly set-piece deliveries with his left foot, played 98 games for Japan, scoring 24 goals, and was part of the 2006 and 2010 World Cup squads.
