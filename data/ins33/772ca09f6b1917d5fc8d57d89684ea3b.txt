MACKENZIE Scott has been in the public eye since she tied the knot with Amazon founder Jeff Bezos.
While she has kept a relatively low profile, her high-profile divorce threw her back into the spotlight, causing many to wonder more about her personal life.
 MacKenzie Scott is a popular philanthropistCredit: Getty MacKenzie Scott is a novelist and philanthropist who was born on April 7, 1970, in San Francisco, California.
Over the years, she has published several books, including The Testing of Luther Albright (2005) and Traps (2013).
