Melrose sale leaves focus on GKN businesses
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-03 23:01:00

The industrial turnaround specialist that bought GKN in 2018 after an acrimonious £8 billion bid battle has sold the remaining business from its previous big deal.Melrose Industries has agreed to sell Ergotron, an American maker of ergonomic desks and stands for computer screens, for £512 million to funds managed by the Sterling Group, a private equity firm. The deal marks the final disposal from Melrose's purchase of Nortek, an industrial conglomerate, in 2016.The London-listed Melrose has a 'buy, improve, sell' business model, snapping up underperforming engineering and industrial companies and selling them on for a profit.
