Salman Khan, who recently hosted the International Indian Film Academy Awards (IIFA) in Abu Dhabi, got emotional at the event as he recalled his days of struggle in the film industry. Salman revealed that he had no work after his hit film Maine Pyar Kiya, as his co-star Bhagyashree allegedly took all the credit for it.
