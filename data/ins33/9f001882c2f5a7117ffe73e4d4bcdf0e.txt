Follow up to the award winning CG3 and CG23, the new models introduce a new appearance, refined performance, and lower price available for purchase starting October 2022LOS ANGELES, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Effective Friday Oct. 21, 2022, RSL Speaker Systems will begin shipping the CG3 & CG23 "M" series loudspeakers. This new series builds upon RSL's award-winning heritage of the CG3 and CG23 compact bookshelf speakers originally released in 2016. These new models were developed in direct response to current economic factors to offer an even more enjoyable listening experience at a lower price.
