October 20, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MIAMI--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Latin Recording Academy® announced that Mexican muralist Quetzal Fuerte has been selected as the official artist of the 23rd Annual Latin GRAMMY Awards®.



Approaching this year's official Latin GRAMMY artwork with a fresh lens, Quetzal's mural for The Latin Academy reflects his distinct signature style while merging a unique perspective showcasing the energy of Latin music through the use of colors. The mural is on display in Morelia, Mexico and a sister piece will soon be created at the GRAMMY Museum® in Los Angeles in November, in an effort to provide access to art and local enthusiasm for the upcoming Latin GRAMMYs.
