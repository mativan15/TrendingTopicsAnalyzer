Inpixon Surges 70% on Heavy Volume
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 18:23:00

By Josh Beckerman Shares of "indoor intelligence" company Inpixon were recently up 70% to $8.12 on volume of more than 22.1 million shares, compared with a 65-day average of 68,393. Inpixon didn't appear to issue any press releases or securities filings Monday. The company's technology is used for purposes like navigation, space utilization, asset tracking and collision avoidance at places including offices, factories, warehouses, mines and malls.
