Goodwin Appoints Konstantin Shishkin Chief Communications Officer

June 02, 2022 11:20 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Goodwin, the global law firm serving innovators and investors, today announced that Konstantin Shishkin has been appointed as the firm's first Chief Communications Officer (CCO). As CCO, Shishkin, who previously served as the firm's Managing Director of Marketing and Communications, will oversee all of the creative marketing and communications strategies for the firm and its clients. He reports directly to Goodwin's Chief Operating Officer, Michael Caplan.
