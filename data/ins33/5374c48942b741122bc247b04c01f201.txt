The cost of going to a game at Wrigley Field has steadily increased for at least the last three decades. In 2022, a Cubs game cost the average fan $364.83. Only a game at Fenway Park was more expensive. But on Wednesday, the Cubs announced that the average price of season ticket packages was going down 5.
