NFL Players Association executive director DeMaurice Smith, whose time in the job is moving toward a conclusion, has experienced a major drop in compensation from one fiscal year to the next.

Via Liz Mullen of Sports Business Journal, the NFLPA's most recent financial statement for the fiscal year ending February 28, 2022 shows that Smith's salary fell from $4.462 million in the year ending February 28, 2021 to $2.
