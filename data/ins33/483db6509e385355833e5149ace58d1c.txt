Trump's anger ricochets through Colorado Senate race
washingtonpost.com
politics
2022-10-22 10:00:07

CASTLE ROCK, Colo. — Barbara Hildebrand left the rally for Senate candidate Joe O'Dea and other Republicans here this week in a cloud of frustration.'I'm really upset about what [O'Dea] said about Trump,' Hildebrand said as she made a beeline for the door. 'I'm an ultra-MAGA … Hasn't he seen the rallies that Trump has? I mean, those are a lot of people. And he's alienated them.'With just three weeks before the midterm elections, former president Donald Trump this week inserted himself into the Senate race in this blue-trending mountain state — upsetting the delicate balance between Colorado's GOP base and its pro-abortion Senate candidate who seems tailor-made to appeal to independents.
