October 17, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TYSONS, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Maximus (NYSE:MMS), a leading employer and provider of government services worldwide, today announced that Dr. Arvenita W. Cherry, the company's Vice President for Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DE&I), has been named the new President of the Maximus Foundation. Dr. Cherry has stepped into this leadership role effective October 1.
