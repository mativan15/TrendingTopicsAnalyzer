NSDC and IIT Mandi partner to provide certification program in Data Science and Machine Learning
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 09:45:00

The National Skills Development Corporation and IIT Mandi have entered into a strategic partnership to provide certification program in Data Science and Machine Learning to aspiring candidates. The aim is to build a skilled workforce of data analysts, data managers, data scientists, business analysts, business intelligence experts and data analytics consultants. The program will begin from November 1, 2022. Under the partnership, two certification programs will be available for the candidates, including six-month certification and nine-month long advance certification program in modern technologies to make India's youth future ready, NSDC said in a statement on Wednesday.
