ArtistGNDphotography Leading Swiss specialty chemicals company Sika's (OTCPK:SXYAY) recent investor day event outlined a clear path to achieving its near to mid-term targets, comprising an annual sales increase of 6-8%, 15-18% EBIT margin, >25% ROCE, and an >10% cash flow margin. In addition, the company has doubled down on net zero, aiming to reduce scope 1 and 2 emissions by 42% in 2032 and an impressive 90% by 2050. Similarly, scope 3 emissions are targeted to see a 25% reduction by 2032 and a 90% reduction by 2050.
