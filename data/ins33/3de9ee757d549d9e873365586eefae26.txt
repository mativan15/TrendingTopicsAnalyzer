TV Insider Freddie Prinze Jr. is making his rom-com return with Netflix‘s upcoming holiday flick, Christmas With You, which is set to premiere Thursday, November 17 on the streaming platform. In anticipation of the film's debut, Netflix has unveiled the first trailer featuring Prinze Jr. alongside costar Aimee Garcia (Lucifer). Christmas With You centers around pop star Angelina (Garcia) who has burnout in her career. Eager to escape the feeling, Angelina decides to grant a young fan's wish.
