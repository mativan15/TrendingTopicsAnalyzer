Democrat Josh Riley is running against Republican Marc Molinaro in New York's 19th Congressional District.

The 19th District stretches from the state's south-central region to the Massachusetts state line.

Molinaro was defeated by now-Rep. Pat Ryan in a 2022 special election to serve the remainder of former Rep. Antonio Delgado's term.
