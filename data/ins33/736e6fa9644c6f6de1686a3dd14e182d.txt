Asda will open its new 'Asda Express' convenience stores with the first shop opening in Sutton Coldfield on November 8 and Tottenham Hale, London, on December 6Asda will open its first two Express stores this yearAsda has announced plans to open its first standalone convenience stores - and they'll be up and running before Christmas. The new format will be called 'Asda Express' with stores opening in Sutton Coldfield on November 8 and Tottenham Hale, London, on December 6. Asda said the openings will create 37 new jobs.
