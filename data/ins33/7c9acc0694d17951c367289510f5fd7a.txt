Ja Morant finished three points shy of his career-high of 52 for the Memphis GrizzliesJa Morant scored a game-high 49 points as the Memphis Grizzlies won 129-122 at the Houston Rockets on Friday.Memphis trailed by 16 in the second quarter before taking the lead in the third, during which Morant scored 19 points, making eight of his nine shots.He added a game-high eight assists and blocked two shots in the fourth quarter as Houston kept things close.Meanwhile, defending champions Golden State Warriors had their first loss of the season against the Denver Nuggets.
