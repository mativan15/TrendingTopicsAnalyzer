Sri Lankan PM Wickremesinghe makes special reference to India while advocating more powers to Parliament
indiatimes.com

2022-05-29 17:30:00

AgenciesRanil WickremesingheSri Lankan Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe on Sunday made a special reference to India while advocating strengthening of the existing laws to give more powers to Parliament. In his special televised statement on the current constitutional reforms, Wickremesinghe said there is a need to change the structure of Parliament and create a new system by combining the existing system of Parliament or the Westminster system and the system of State Councils. "First of all, the existing laws need to be strengthened in order to give those powers to Parliament in the exercise of monetary powers.
