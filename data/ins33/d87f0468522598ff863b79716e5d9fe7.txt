The Founder of the Atlanta-based fashion and swimwear brand wishes to call attention to its stated philosophy that every woman deserves to look and feel their best without having to spend too much money on clothes

ATLANTA, GA / ACCESSWIRE / June 1, 2022 / When entrepreneur and designer Justina McKee launched her fashion and swimwear brand Matte Collection in 2017, she did so with two core principles in mind. The first was that all of the brand's garments ought to be made for women by women, while the second principle was that every woman deserves to look and feel their best without having to spend too much money on clothes.
