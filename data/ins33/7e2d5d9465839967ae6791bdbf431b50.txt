Gujarat high court seeks government's affidavit on defunct online portal for RTI
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 14:30:00

AHMEDABAD: The Gujarat high court on Thursday ordered the state government to file an affidavit and state whether its online portal for Right to Information Act (RTI) applications is properly functioning.In 2018, advocate Bandish Soparkar filed a PIL demanding the online portal for RTI application and information dissemination in line with the portals being maintained by various instrumentalities of the central government. A year later, another lawyer Sandip Munjyasara also joined the cause and filed another PIL in this regard.
