Entertainer charged in global crime sting gets continued suppression
Stuff Limited.

2022-06-02 23:50:39

An entertainer charged in a global sting on organised crime has successfully appealed to keep his name secret. The entertainer was arrested last June with about 40 others across the country. He's set to go to trial in May 2023. After previously losing name suppression at the High Court, he appealed the Court of Appeal.
