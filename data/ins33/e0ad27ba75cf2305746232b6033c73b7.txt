No country better prepared for independence, claims Sturgeon
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

An independent Scotland will apply to rejoin the European Union and redesign the energy market, according to an economic paper published today.The Scottish government prospectus is expected to set out proposals on currency and border arrangements, alongside a migration policy designed to boost the working population.The plan would demonstrate no country in history has been 'better prepared to become independent', according to Nicola Sturgeon.The first minister said the debate on independence is not about 'change -versus status quo', adding: 'Scotland cannot afford to live under Westminster control any longer.
