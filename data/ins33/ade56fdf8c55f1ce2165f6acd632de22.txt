NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Dan Rossi, known as The New York Hot Dog King, has written a memoir called The New York Hot Dog King: From Rags to Riches to Less Than Rags. Dan is a local legend and has an incredible story. He currently owns a food cart in front of The Met and has been in the media for many years after being the largest food cart manufacturer in New York City's history.
