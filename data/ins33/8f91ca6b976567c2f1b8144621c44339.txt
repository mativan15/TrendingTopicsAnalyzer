The Irish Times view on the legacy of Brexit
irishtimes.com
politics
2022-10-21 20:00:00

A new assessment of Britain's trade losses since Brexit underlines the severity of the economic challenge facing the successor to Liz Truss. This calls for candour rather than bluster as the Conservatives weigh their options between likely candidates Rishi Sunak, Boris Johnson and Penny Mordaunt. But there has been little enough of that in the endless Tory drama. Six long years of chaos since the fateful vote to take the UK out of the EU, Brexit remains the defining schism in British politics. For all the argument, one thing is clear: the promised economic dividend remains elusive.
