 Sports fans are increasingly have to search for what options are available for viewing their favorite games, as streaming media giants like Apple AAPL and Amazon AMZN .com have been bidding against the major cable and broadcast network owners for sports rights. Amazon on 10/18 announced it added another NFL game to its package starting next year which will debut the day after Thanksgiving, the biggest shopping day of the year, Black Friday.
