Berlin Democrat Denise McNair aims to unseat state Rep. Donna Veach, R-Berlin, for the 30th House District. (Courtesy of Denise McNair)The 2022 election is heating up in Connecticut and there are plenty of races with candidates eager to serve in elected office.Eyes are primarily focused on the gubernatorial election, but every state representative and senate seat is up for grabs. All five of Connecticut's congressional seats are up for grabs as well. There are 151 seats in the state House of Representatives and 36 in the state Senate.
