Elon Musk tells Tesla staff they can work remotely – after doing 40 hours in the office
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 08:49:00

Tesla's attitude to remote work has seen its Shanghai staff sleeping on their factory floor amid efforts to restart production during a Covid lockdown in April. Local authorities had decreed that commuting had to end while public health officials struggled with a Covid outbreak in the city.
Tech companies initially began cracking down on remote work last summer before a movement dubbed the ‘Great Resignation' began forcing employers back to the negotiating table as staff quit in favour of jobs with better working conditions.
