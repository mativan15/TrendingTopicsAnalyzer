When interacting with a customer, the team should ask themselves, 'What would the most ... [+] customer-obsessed company in the world do?' getty 
'A customer-obsessed organization starts with knowing what the customer wants.' That's the message from Jeffrey Mezger, chairman, CEO and president of KB Home, a homebuilding company founded in 1957. Mezger joined the company in 2006 and has the lofty goal of being the most customer-obsessed homebuilder on the planet. I had the opportunity to interview him for Amazing Business Radio, and he shared some of his business philosophy—and his obsession—about customer service and experience.
