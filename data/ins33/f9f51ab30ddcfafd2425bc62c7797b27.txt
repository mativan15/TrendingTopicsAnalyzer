The owner of seven holiday homes confirmed he will keep the money which will go to every household in the UK to help Brits save £400 off their energy billsOwner of seven homes says he'll keep energy rebate An owner of seven homes who is set to receive £2,800 from Chancellor Rishi Sunak' s energy rebate has said he'll keep the cash. Craig, an owner of seven holiday lets, admitted he will keep the money which will go to every household in the UK to help Brits save £400 off their energy bills. The energy rebate is to help towards the cost of living crisis after the government introduced a temporary 25 per cent windfall tax on oil and gas companies to fund the cash.
