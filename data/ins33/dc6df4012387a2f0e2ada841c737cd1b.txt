June 01, 2022 05:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time GUANGZHOU, China--(BUSINESS WIRE)--XPeng Inc. ('XPeng' or the 'Company', NYSE: XPEV and HKEX: 9868), a leading Chinese smart electric vehicle ('Smart EV') company, today announced its vehicle delivery results for May 2022.

XPeng delivered 10,125 Smart EVs in May 2022, representing a 78% increase year-over-year. The May deliveries consisted of 4,224 P7 smart sports sedans, 3,686 P5 smart family sedans, as well as 2,215 G3 smart compact SUVs.

As of May 31, 2022, year-to-date total deliveries reached 53,688, representing a 122% increase year-over-year.
