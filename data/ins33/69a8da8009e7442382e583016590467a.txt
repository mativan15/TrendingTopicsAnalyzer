MIDLAND, N.C., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Aberdeen Carolina and Western Railway (ACWR) is excited to announce the opening of the Midland Logistics Park in Cabarrus County, NC. The Midland Logistics Park is a 70-acre development anchored by the ACWR's Midland Transload Yard and includes three additional properties available for prospective rail customers.
