June 01, 2022 09:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bragar Eagel & Squire, P.C., a nationally recognized stockholder rights law firm, announces that a class action lawsuit has been filed against IonQ, Inc. ('IonQ' or the 'Company') (NYSE: IONQ) in the United States District Court for the District of Maryland on behalf of all persons and entities who purchased or otherwise acquired IonQ securities between March 20, 2021 and May 2, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period').
