Why is Gambia pushing women out of politics? – DW – 04/21/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-18 12:45:00

In Gambia, only five women made it to the 58-member Parliament after recent elections. Despite women making up more than half of the Gambian population, they account for less than 9% of lawmakers. The erosion of women's participation in national politics, has angered gender activists who are determined to turn this tide. Listen to this week's 77 Percent to find out more.
