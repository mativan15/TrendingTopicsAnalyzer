Klaus Vedfelt/DigitalVision via Getty Images Yesterday started on a sour note with lower guidance from Microsoft (MSFT) before the market opened. The company will fall short of estimates on the top and bottom lines due to foreign exchange headwinds. That turned what had been pre-market gains into losses at the open. That was followed by analyst downgrades of social networks Pinterest (PINS) and Snap (SNAP), due to concerns about ad market weakness, which was like throwing fuel on the fire for the technology sector.
