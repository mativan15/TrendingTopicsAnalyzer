Many New Zealand Jews have been harassed over Zionism
jpost.com
news
2022-10-15 03:53:00

New Zealand Jews are saying that the most common form of antisemitism they experience is related to Israel and to anti-Zionism, according to a local human rights group "Humanity Matters."

Overall, 65% of New Zealand Jews said that they experienced antisemitism on social media in the last three years.

The report aims to explore the Jewish experience in New Zealand and to understand the unique challenges facing the relatively small Jewish community.

What did the study find?

52% of the Jewish people living in New Zealand said that they encountered antisemitic misinformation or disinformation online that was either directly related to Israel or to anti-Zionism during the last 12 months, according to the report.
