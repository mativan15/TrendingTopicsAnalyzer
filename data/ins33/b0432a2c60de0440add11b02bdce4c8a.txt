Under CM Yogi, U.P. has become favourite place for foreign investors: Nandi
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-30 17:02:55

Uttar Pradesh industrial development minister Nand Gopal Gupta 'Nandi' has said the state government is working to make the third ground breaking ceremony to be held in Lucknow on June 3 a grand success. In a press statement on Monday, he said, 'Under the leadership of chief minister Yogi Adityanath, Uttar Pradesh has become a favourite destination for foreign investors. The state government has taken several measures to promote industrial investment due to which UP is attracting several foreign companies that are eager to invest in the state.
