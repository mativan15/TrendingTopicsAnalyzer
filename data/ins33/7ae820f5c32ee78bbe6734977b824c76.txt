MINNEAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--You asked for it, now we are delivering. Today, FaZe Clan Inc. ('FaZe Clan') and Totino's™ Pizza Rolls ™ brand pizza snacks are teaming up to drop one of fans' top requested Totino's Pizza Roll flavors: FaZe Clan Buffalo Chicken. The new snack is the latest iteration of the multi-year partnership between FaZe Clan, the lifestyle and media platform rooted in gaming and youth culture, and Totino's™ Pizza Rolls™, the delicious, poppable and baked-to-a-golden-finish hot snack.
