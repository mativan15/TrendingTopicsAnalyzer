AMC jumps 10% after the meme-stock darling says record weekend for Top Gun sequel drove huge increase in attendance at theaters
Insider Inc.
business
2022-05-31 13:25:14

The long-awaited release of the sequel to Top Gun sent shares of AMC Entertainment soaring on Tuesday after the movie broke box office records.AMC Entertainment stock jumped as much as 10% as investors sized up the $156 million haul Top Gun: Maverick delivered for US movie theaters over the long weekend, which is a Memorial Day weekend record.The movie was originally slated for a 2020 release, but was delayed by two years due to the COVID-19 pandemic. That delay translated into strong pent-up demand.
