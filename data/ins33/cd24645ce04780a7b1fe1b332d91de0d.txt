Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Realty index advances 0.41% in an upbeat market
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 05:00:11

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Realty index traded positive around 10:30AM(IST)on Wednesday in an upbeat market.Prestige Estates Projects(up 2.1 per cent), Oberoi Realty(up 1.64 per cent), DLF(up 1.6 per cent), Indiabulls Real Estate(up 1.57 per cent) and Godrej Properties(up 0.87 per cent) were among the top gainers.Macrotech Developers(down 2.43 per cent), Phoenix Mills(down 1.3 per cent) and Brigade Enterprises(down 1.07 per cent) were the top losers on the index.The Nifty Realty index was up 0.41 per cent at 427.
