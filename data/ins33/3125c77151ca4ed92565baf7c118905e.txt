October 20, 2022 03:02 PM Eastern Daylight Time WEST CHESTER, Ohio--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Lithko Contracting ('Lithko'), a leading national concrete contractor, received recognition as the number two concrete contractor in Engineering News-Record's Top 600 Specialty Contractors issue.



Lithko posted an extraordinary 56% growth from the prior year.

Lithko Leadership knows this phenomenal expansion was the culmination of the company's hardworking teams throughout the country combined with the recent strategic acquisition of four companies.
