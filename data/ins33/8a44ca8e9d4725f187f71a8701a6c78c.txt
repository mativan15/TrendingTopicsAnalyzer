The BBC's Children in Need is back again for another year, and the Rickshaw Relay has also made a reappearance. On Monday October 17, the team will be cycling through Sunderland, before finishing their day's journey in Durham. Countryfile presenter Matt Baker MBE will also return, and he joins a team of 'five incredibly inspiring young people, all of whom have been supported by BBC Children in Need funded projects', the BBC Children in Need website explains.The team will take turns to travel part of the journey with the challenge ending on Friday, October 21.
