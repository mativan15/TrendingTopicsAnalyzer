Gordon Sparks, BBC Radio Devon host, dies of cancer aged 61
express.co.uk
entertainment
2022-10-17 06:36:00

Gordon Sparks, whose work included commentating on Plymouth Argyle, died on Friday aged 61 after a battle with cancer. The well-known BBC Radio Devon presenter revealed prior to his death that he had been moved to a hospice.Gordon had taken to Twitter the day before his death to update his friends and colleagues on his worsening condition.He said: "Huge thanks to the nurses on Brent ward at @UHP_NHS for looking after me over the last week."Have moved to @StLukesPlymouth. After introduction, slept most of the afternoon.
