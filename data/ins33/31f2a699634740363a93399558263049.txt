NEW YORK, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pagaya, a global, data-driven financial technology company reshaping the future of finance, today announced Ashok Vaswani as its President. This additional key hire is a pivotal next step to continue the company's recent growth and expected ascent to becoming a publicly traded company in June.

"We are excited to welcome Ashok as President of Pagaya. He has a proven track record of building and growing businesses at international scale," said Gal Krubiner, Pagaya's Co-founder and CEO.
