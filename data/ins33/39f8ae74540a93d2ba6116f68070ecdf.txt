Britons skipping meals to save money poll
theuknews.com
news
2022-10-20 21:45:00

People are reportedly eating less healthily and less often, preferably at a discount, due to the rising cost of livingMillions of British households are being forced to skip meals or buy cheaper food due to the rising cost of basic products, a consumer watchdog group has said, citing a new poll.More than eight in 10 people in the UK (85%) are changing their eating habits in response to the rising cost of living, consumer champion Which? said on Thursday. The most popular way of coping with inflation is to seek promotional offers, which is what more than half (55%) of the respondents reported doing.
