May 30, 2022 02:00 ET | Source: Nordecon Nordecon Tallinn, ESTONIA English Estonian AS Nordecon group company Embach Ehitus OÜ entered into contract with Kaarsilla Kinnisvara OÜ for the design and construction of the commercial building located in Kandiküla, Tartu. The building to be built will be mainly a one-storey, partly two-storey building with a closed net area of 4,560 sqm, and it will be completed in September 2023.
