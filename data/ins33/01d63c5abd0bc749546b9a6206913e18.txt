Big Tech ramps up efforts to quash milestone antitrust bill
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 04:15:00

By David Shepardson and Diane Bartz WASHINGTON: Big tech has launched a media blitz aimed at derailing one of the most ground-breaking bipartisan bills in the U.S. that would bar tech companies, like Amazon.com Inc , from giving preference to their own businesses on their websites. Amazon lambasted the bill on Wednesday saying in a blog that the bill "jeopardizes two of the things American consumers love most about Amazon: the vast selection and low prices made possible by opening our store to third-party selling partners, and the promise of fast, free shipping through Amazon Prime.
