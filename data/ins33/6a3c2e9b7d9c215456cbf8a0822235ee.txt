By Reuters Published: 22:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 22:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 SHANGHAI, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Chinese chip maker Yangtze Memory Technologies Co (YMTC) Ltd denied a media report that it had participated in emergency meetings convened by China's industry ministry to discuss the impact of U.S. sanctions. The company in a statement late on Thursay described the report as "false and sinister," adding that the report had damaged its corporate image and would have serious adverse effects on the semiconductor industry environment at home and abroad.
