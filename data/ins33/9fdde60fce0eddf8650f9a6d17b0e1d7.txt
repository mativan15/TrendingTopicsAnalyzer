What are modern enterprises doing to build productive teams?
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-27 15:33:05

In the age of divided teams and virtual workspaces, modern enterprises are scrambling to solve the productivity puzzle. The outbreak of the coronavirus pandemic compelled organizations and workforces alike to adapt to new-age digital business practices such as digital marketing and adjust to distributed forms of work such as remote and hybrid teams. But at a time when burnout rates are through the roof and employee engagement has plummeted to the bottom, how can leaders and teams be functionally productive.
