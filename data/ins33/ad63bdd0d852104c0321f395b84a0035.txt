With deal flow drying up and clients nervous, small and medium sized firms are likely to seek safety in numbers.Speculation about tie-ups are bound to extend to Numis, Cenkos and perhaps Peel Hunt.All have seen fees fall and are under pressure to cut jobs.Panmure, one of the remaining old style broker names, came under the control of Diamond and the Qatari royal family back in 2017.It was hoped his network of middle east contacts and the skill of CEO Rich Richie, a former Barclays colleague, would revive the firm.
