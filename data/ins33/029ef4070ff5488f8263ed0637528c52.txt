October 17, 2022 07:08 AM Eastern Daylight Time LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BackupVault, a provider of online backup specialising in the SME market, found that a large majority of their clients did not know why external backup of cloud applications was so vital. BackupVault founder and CEO Rob Stevenson explains: 'A staggering 93% of our SME clients didn't know that their cyber-insurance was only effective if they had external backup in place until we told them, and 8 in 10 were not aware of the Shared Responsibility Model used by Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) services when they came to us.
