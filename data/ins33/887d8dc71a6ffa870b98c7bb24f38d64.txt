The UNC basketball program will soon find out their fate for a big transfer target according to a report on Friday.
Inside Carolina's Sherrell McMillan is reporting that former Baylor forward Matthew Mayer is set to make his decision sooner than expected. Mayer will reportedly decide on his list of four finalists sometime within the next week.

Mayer is down to four schools, he announced on Thursday, focusing on UNC, Illinois, Memphis and Texas Tech. He recently withdrew his name from the NBA Draft with the decision to return to college and transfer.
