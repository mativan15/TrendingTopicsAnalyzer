bluebay2014 By Brian Manby, CFA Not that you need a reminder, but 2022 has been one of the most volatile years on record. With most major equity indexes in bear market territory, investors have struggled to find relief from the pressure of rising interest rates and record inflation stubbornly weighing on markets. Cratering markets require creative solutions to keep portfolios afloat, which is why now is an opportune time to revisit the WisdomTree Target Range Fund (GTR), one year after its launch.
