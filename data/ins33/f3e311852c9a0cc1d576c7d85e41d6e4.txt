We plan to add 2 GW capacity every year: Shivanand Nimbargi, MD and CEO, Ayana Renewable Power
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-30 09:15:00

New Delhi: Ayana Renewable Power, a Bengaluru-based clean energy firm, plans to have a 10-GW portfolio by 2025 through greenfield and acquisitions with an equity commitment of $720 million from their shareholders – NIIF, BII and GGEF – Shivanand Nimbargi, managing director and chief executive officer of the firm tells ETEnergyWorld in an exclusive interview. Edited excerpts:What are your expansion plans for FY23 and how much investment are you targeting for this period? Our aim is to add 2 gigawatt (GW) of renewable power capacity every year, which includes a combination of wind, solar, hybrid, peak power and round-the-clock energy access projects.
