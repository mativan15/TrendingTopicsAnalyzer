'Super handy' car packing tip could risk of avoid fines and points
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 06:45:00

There are plenty of times when you may need to pack lots of your belongings into your car, however, motivating experts from Car Money point out the importance of doing this correctly. Should any of the luggage block a crucial viewpoint, drivers could find themselves being pulled over by the police.The experts explained: "When packing any luggage into a car, be sure to avoid blocking the rearview window with items as this is dangerous when driving and may be breaching the law."Drivers found to be travelling with an obstructed window can be hit with a £50 fixed penalty notice, and more seriously, may be fined up to £2,500, receive three penalty points and given a total driving ban if the vehicle being driven is in a dangerous condition, according to Gov.
