Murray isn't old. He's also not going anywhere anytime soon; the Arizona Cardinals signed him to a five-year, $230 million contract with $189.5 million guaranteed back in July. For all intents and purposes, Arizona's got him on the roster through at least 2027.
This is not the boon the Cardinals hoped it would be. Murray, known for his hot starts as a pro before cooling off each winter, has instead opted to start his 2022 looking terrible instead of finishing the season that way.
