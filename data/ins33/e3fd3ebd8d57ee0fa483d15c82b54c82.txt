Jetlinerimages Airline stocks were among the few that rebounded this week on positive forecast for the sector. For the week ending Oct. 14, two out five industrial stocks (in this segment, four if we count out of 10) were airlines. Despite growing concerns on inflation and its impact on consumer spending, travel is among the last budget cuts being forwarded by consumers, according to industry executives and experts. On the broader economic front, the U.S. Federal Reserve hinted at maintaining its hawkish stance and officials acknowledged that their rate hike path will weigh on economic activity in coming months and years.
