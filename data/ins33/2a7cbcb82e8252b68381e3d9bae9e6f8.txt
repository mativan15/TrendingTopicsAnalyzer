Rockville, MD, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global commercial photography market size is estimated at US$ 5.2 billion in 2022 and the market is expected to evolve at a CAGR of 2.2% during the forecast years of 2022-2032, according to the latest study by Fact.MR, a market research and competitive intelligence provider. The surging e-Commerce industry across the world is a prominent factor driving the demand for commercial photography. Product photography has tremendous demand in the e-Commerce industry to enhance brand awareness and drive consumer attention to products.
