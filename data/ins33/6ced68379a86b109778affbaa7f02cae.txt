Hamilton police charge 4th person in deadly Mount Hope home invasion
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-21 22:45:00

Hamilton police have arrested a fourth person after a deadly home invasion and kidnapping last year.In the early hours of Sept. 16, 2021, according to police, Hasnain (Nano) Ali, 21, and his brother were shot while trying to protect their father from kidnappers at their home near Glancaster and Dickenson roads in Mount Hope.Police said the kidnappers took off in an SUV with 63-year-old father Faqir Ali, who was later found severely beaten near a lift bridge on Beach Boulevard.His wife and daughter were in the home too, but weren't harmed.
