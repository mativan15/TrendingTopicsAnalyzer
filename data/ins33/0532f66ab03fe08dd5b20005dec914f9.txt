My role in Head Bush helped push my limits: Raghu Mukherjee
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-15 11:31:00

Head Bush, directed by Shoonya, set to hit the screens on Friday, is actor Raghu Mukherjee's first periodic film. In it, he is essaying the powerful role of M D Nataraj, the son-in-law of the late Chief Minister D Devaraj Urs. The project is based on the autobiography My Days in the Underworld , penned by Agni Shridhar."MD Nataraj, called MDN, is an aspiring politician and a loyalist to his father-in-law. He created a sort of revolution in the 1970's period, and had a say in policies during the emergency.
