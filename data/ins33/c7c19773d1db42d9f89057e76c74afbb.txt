Help (mostly) wanted: A diverging job market boosts some workers' prospects and puts others on notice
cnbc.com
economics
2022-05-29 11:00:01

A help wanted sign is displayed in the window of a Brooklyn, New York business.Cracks are forming in the U.S. labor market as some companies look to curb hiring while others are desperate for employees.Microsoft, Twitter, Wayfair, Snap and Facebook-parent Meta recently announced they plan to be more conservative about adding new employees. Peloton and Netflix announced layoffs as demand for their products slowed, and online car seller Carvana cut its workforce as it faces inflation and a cratering stock price.
