'Effort to taint country's image': Centre on India's low ranking in Global Hunger Index
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 14:15:00

(With inputs from agencies)

NEW DELHI: The Centre on Saturday slammed the Global Hunger Index 2022, which has ranked India 107 out of 121 countries, as being "an erroneous measure of hunger ... and suffers from serious methodological issues".In a statement, the Centre said that "a consistent effort is yet again visible to taint India's image ... misinformation seems to be the hallmark of the annually released Global Hunger Index". It went on to list the various measures taken by the government to ensure food security.
