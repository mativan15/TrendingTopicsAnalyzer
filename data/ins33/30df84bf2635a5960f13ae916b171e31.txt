ROCKFORD, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--Wolverine World Wide, Inc. (NYSE: WWW) today announced that Michael Stornant, Executive Vice President & Chief Financial Officer, will participate in a fireside chat at the 2022 Baird Global Consumer, Technology and Services Conference in New York City on Tuesday, June 7, 2022. The presentation will begin at 3:10 pm Eastern Time.There will be a live audio webcast of the presentation and a replay will be available for 90 days following the presentation on the Events page of the Investor Relations section of the Company's website at Investor Relations | Wolverine Worldwide (gcs-web.
