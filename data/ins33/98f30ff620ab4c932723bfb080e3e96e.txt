Lakers hire Bucks assistant Darvin Ham as coach
latimes.com
news
2022-05-28 00:51:22

The Los Angeles Lakers have hired Darvin Ham to be their next head coach, sources have told The Times.Ham, a first-time head coach, has been a longtime NBA assistant following his eight-year NBA playing career, which included a championship in 2004 with the Detroit Pistons, who beat the Lakers in the Finals.He began his NBA coaching career with the Lakers as a player development coach in 2011 on the staff of coach Mike Brown.Ham, 48, has earned respect around the NBA as a well-rounded communicator on Mike Budenholzer's staffs in Atlanta and Milwaukee.
