2022-05-28 12:31:57

A coach bought by a Liverpool supporter to transport dozens of fellow fans to Paris for just £1 each has arrived in the city ahead of the Champions League final.The Reds will take on 13-time winners Real Madrid at the Stade de France on Saturday, with many fans facing huge fees to get to the city.Simon Wilson, a British vlogger, from Wrexham, bought the cheapest coach he could find for £5,000 to take as many fans there to avoid the 'extortionate' travel fees.Just £1 each meant around 50 supporters could go from Anfield to the French capital in time for the game.
