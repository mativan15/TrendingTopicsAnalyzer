SpaceX to no longer fund free internet to Ukraine in blow to Kyiv
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 04:27:00

Documents obtained by CNN have revealed that SpaceX has said it can no longer afford to donate free internet to Ukraine unless the US Government help funds the programme. The director of government at Space X wrote to the Pentagon in September: 'We are not in a position to further donate terminals to Ukraine, or fund the existing terminals for an indefinite period of time."
