Chris Sommers spent the better part of the last decade in the restaurant business & still owns restaurants in St. Louis & Washington, DC, with franchises in Texas & the Middle East. He now spends the bulk of his time on his latest venture, Accrue, a financial technology company he founded in 2020, and sold last year. He remains Chief Product Officer of the company, which sells lending & account opening software to community banks. Accrue was initially funded by Block founder, Jim McKelvey.
