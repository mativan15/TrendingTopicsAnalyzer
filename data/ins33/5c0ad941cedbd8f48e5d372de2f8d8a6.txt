It is a nice Safeway, located on 14th Street in Southeast Washington; indeed, it is going to great pains to be nice. This grocery store has an oyster bar. You can get gelato scooped to order. Ask employees where to find an item, and they will leave what they're stocking to escort you there. Nice.

This is dispatch No. 98,742 from the files of why we can't have nice things. The fact is, something is very wrong here. It is something you discover as soon as you enter the store.
