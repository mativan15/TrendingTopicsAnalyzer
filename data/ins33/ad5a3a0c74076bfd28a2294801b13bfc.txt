Conn's CEO steps down after year in role, company expects decline in Q3 revenue
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 18:27:00

Conn's Inc. (Nasdaq: CONN), a specialty home goods retailer based in The Woodlands, announced Oct. 18 President and CEO Chandra Holt is stepping down effective immediately.

Holt began her tenure on Aug. 9, 2021, replacing former President and CEO Norm Miller, who had served in the position since September 2015. Prior to Conn's, Holt held various positions with Walmart Inc. (NYSE: WLMT), Walgreens (Nasdaq: WBA) and Target (NYSE: TGT).

Miller, who most recently served as executive chairman of the company from August 2021 to April 2022, will take over as interim president and CEO.
