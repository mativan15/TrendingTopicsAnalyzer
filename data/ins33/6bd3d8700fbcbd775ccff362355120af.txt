18 October 2022 by Brian Welk 
The Wrap 'Twisters,' a sequel to the 1996 blockbuster 'Twister,' is in development at Universal Pictures and Amblin Entertainment and is gathering strength after the studio has attached 'The Revenant' screenwriter Mark L. Smith to write the script, an individual with knowledge of the project told TheWrap.News of a sequel to 'Twister' first broke in 2020 when 'Top Gun: Maverick' filmmaker Joseph Kosinski was in talks to direct, but he has since exited the project, and the individual adds that the studio is currently meeting with other directors in the hopes the film can begin shooting by this spring.
