Companies say they're facing a number of challenges including staff shortages, air traffic control restrictions, runway works and airport handling delays.So what are your rights if your flight is cancelled?If you're flying from a UK airport on any airline, arriving at a UK airport on an EU or UK airline, or arriving at an airport in the EU on a UK airline, you have a number of rights under UK law.
