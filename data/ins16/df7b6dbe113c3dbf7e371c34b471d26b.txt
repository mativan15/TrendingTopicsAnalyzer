Dartford crossing closed after two 'Just Stop Oil' activists climb Queen Elizabeth II bridge
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:00:00

ReutersActivists of "Just Stop Oil" climbed onto the cables of the Queen Elizabeth bridge connecting Essex and Kent and closed the bridge. Cops confirmed that the Dartford Crossing was closed off for traffic as the incident happened between 3 to 4 in the morning. Two people were seen scaling the bridge in the pictures that went viral. It caused a lot of delay for the ongoing traffic. The two people were identified as Londoners. One of them is the architect of the bridge, Morgan Trowland,39 and the other person is a teacher, Marcus,33.
