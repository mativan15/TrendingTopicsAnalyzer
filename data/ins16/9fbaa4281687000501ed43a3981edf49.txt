Just Stop Oil protesters arrested after Harrods sprayed with orange paint
theguardian.com
sport
2022-10-20 13:58:20

Just Stop Oil protesters have sprayed orange paint on the front of Harrods in central London as they continue to call on the government to end all 'new oil and gas'.About 20 demonstrators gathered outside the department store in Knightsbridge at about 9am on Thursday for a 20th day of disruption to the capital in a row.A video shared on Just Stop Oil's Twitter account showed two protesters spraying paint on the windows of Harrods. A second clip appeared to show the pair being taken inside the store by security guards.
