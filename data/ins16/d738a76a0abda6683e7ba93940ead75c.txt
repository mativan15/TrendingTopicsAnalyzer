With its stock down 7.2% over the past month, it is easy to disregard Agnico Eagle Mines (NYSE:AEM). However, stock prices are usually driven by a company's financials over the long term, which in this case look pretty respectable. In this article, we decided to focus on Agnico Eagle Mines' ROE.

Return on equity or ROE is an important factor to be considered by a shareholder because it tells them how effectively their capital is being reinvested. Put another way, it reveals the company's success at turning shareholder investments into profits.
