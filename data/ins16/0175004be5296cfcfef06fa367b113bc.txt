Feds: Business owner devised 'kickback scheme' to secure NKY construction contract
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 19:59:49

The owner of a South Carolina architecture, engineering and construction firm is facing allegations of offering kickbacks to ensure his company received the contract for a Gallatin County steel mill expansion project, court records show.

Tony Berenyi was indicted Thursday on charges of conspiracy to commit honest services wire fraud, wire fraud, money laundering and engaging in monetary transactions in criminally derived property, according to court documents filed in federal court in Covington.
