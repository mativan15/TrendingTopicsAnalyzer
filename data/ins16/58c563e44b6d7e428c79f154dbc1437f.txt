KKR Real Estate Finance Trust prices public stock offering
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-03 14:11:05

KKR Real Estate Finance Trust (NYSE:KREF) on Thursday priced a public offering of 7M shares. The offering consists of 2.75M primary shares to be issued and sold by KREF, and 4.25M secondary shares to be sold by KKR REFT - a subsidiary of KKR (KKR). Estimated gross proceeds of the offering are ~$54.2M to KREF (or $74.9M if underwriters exercise their overallotment option) and ~$83.7M to the KKR stockholder. KREF intends to use the net proceeds it receives from the offering to originate, acquire and finance target assets, and for general corporate purposes.
