All of CBC Hamilton's election coverage can be found here.Mayoral candidate Andrea Horwath visited CBC Hamilton on Oct. 4, and spoke about the need to build more transit to help businesses on the outskirts of the city attract workers, among other reasons. As mayor, she would also support the Hamilton is Home plan that would build 3,000 affordable housing units in three years. She also fielded questions on her involvement in the local community and her support for maintaining police service levels.
