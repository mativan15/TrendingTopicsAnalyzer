Alec Baldwin posts photo of Halyna Hutchins on Rust set one year since shooting
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-10-22 07:12:51

Alec Baldwin has shared a photo of Halyna Hutchins as he reflected on the tragedy on the one year anniversary of the Rust shooting. Cinematographer and mother-of-one Halyna was fatally shot with a prop gun Alec Baldwin was using in October last year on the New Mexico film set. Film director Joel Souza was also injured in the incident. Lead star in the movie Alec marked the first anniversary on Instagram by posting a photo of Halyna, ahead of the final police report into the incident. Alec shared the pic of Halyna looking into a camera while on set.
