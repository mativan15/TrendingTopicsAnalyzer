Banking as a service: The future of finance

The era of digital revolution propelled by the pandemic has left almost every industry and sector grappling with waves of transformation. The ways in which customers engage with brands and services across the globe has changed drastically – and like everyone else, the banking sector must adapt.
A recent survey report highlights that an estimated 205 million Indian adults already have a digital-only bank account. And this number is expected to grow to 397 million within the next five years 1 . This shows how customers today demand evolved and integrated services at every step of the way.
