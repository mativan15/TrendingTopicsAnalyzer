Fact Check: Did Marco Rubio Skip Senate Vote on Hurricane Ian Relief?
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 14:06:43

The damage and economic losses caused by Hurricane Ian may exceed $200 billion as Florida continues to deal with the fallout from the natural disaster. Among the worst hurricanes Florida has ever seen, Ian left much of the state underwater, and rescue efforts are ongoing. Yet amid such tragedy and devastation, reports emerged recently suggesting that Senator Marco Rubio (R-FL) hadn't shown up to vote for a bill to open funding.
Republican senator for Florida Marco Rubio has been called out for his apparent absence during a senate bill vote that would have opened up funding for Hurricane Ian.
