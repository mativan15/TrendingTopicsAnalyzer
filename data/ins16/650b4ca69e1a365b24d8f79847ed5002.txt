UPPER MARLBORO, Md. (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 The grand opening of Liberty Sports Park, the Mid-Atlantic Region's premier field sports vacation destination, will take place on Sunday, Oct. 23, at 11 a.m. at the facility, located at 220 Prince George's Boulevard in Upper Marlboro, Md. Liberty Sports Park features 10 lit fields – six turf and four grass – available for youth and adult league, club and other soccer, lacrosse, field hockey, football and rugby programs, as well as Prince George's County Public Schools, Prince George's Community College and the Prince George's County Boys and Girls Club.
