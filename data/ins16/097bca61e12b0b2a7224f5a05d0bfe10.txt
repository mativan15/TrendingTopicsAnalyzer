Collider.com If you looked outside your window today, you may have noticed the sun shining a little brighter than usual. You might have noticed that the people that have passed you by are acting a little more cheerful as well, giving you and your outlook on the world in a more positive light. This isn't due to some sudden change in the autumn-time, or even from a sudden increase in the marketing budget for Smile. Dear reader, it is Chainsaw Man week, the long-awaited time of year when the anime adaptation of the highly-regarded Manga by Tatsuki Fujimoto slices its way into our television sets, and the world is simply doing a little better because of it.
