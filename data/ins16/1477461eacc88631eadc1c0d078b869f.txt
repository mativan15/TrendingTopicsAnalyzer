October 19, 2022 05:10 PM Eastern Daylight Time DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Celanese Corporation (NYSE: CE), a global chemical and specialty materials company, today declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.70 per share on its common stock, payable on November 14, 2022 to stockholders of record as of October 31, 2022.



The board of directors of Celanese has approved a 3 percent increase in the company's quarterly common stock cash dividend which increases the quarterly dividend from $0.68 to $0.70 per share of common stock.
