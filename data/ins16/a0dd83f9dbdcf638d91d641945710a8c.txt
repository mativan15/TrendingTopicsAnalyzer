Homelessness, housing and climate change are among some of the top reasons candidates for council in Kitchener are running in this year's municipal election.Three of the city's 10 councillors are not seeking re-election, which means there will be some new faces around the council horseshoe this fall. Those not seeking re-election are:Ward 3 Coun. John Gazzola.Ward 5 Coun. Kelly Galloway-Sealock.Ward 10 Coun. Sarah Marsh.The municipal election will be held on Monday and to help people in Kitchener get to know their candidates, CBC Kitchener-Waterloo asked all candidates to answer a survey that listed their top three reasons for running.
