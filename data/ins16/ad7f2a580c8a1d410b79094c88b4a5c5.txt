Mike Ashley's Frasers Group buys Missguided out of administration in £20m deal
sky.com
gaming
2022-06-01 06:55:44

Frasers Group, the shopping empire founded by Mike Ashley that owns House of Fraser and Sports Direct, has swooped in to save Missguided from collapse.The company has acquired the embattled online retailer for £20m, saving 147 jobs in the process.
However 87 members of staff were made redundant when the company collapsed on Monday.Frasers Group has bought all of the related intellectual property of the women's fashion brand - but Missguided's clothing suppliers say they are still owed millions of pounds.
