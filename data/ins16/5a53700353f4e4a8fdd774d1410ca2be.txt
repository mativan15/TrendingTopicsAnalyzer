Indus Towers shares rise 1.45% as Nifty gains
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 05:15:00

ReutersNifty continues to climb a wall of worry as it looks to be in the final leg of its rally before heading for a correction.Shares of Indus Towers Ltd. traded 1.45 per cent up at Rs 192.55 on Wednesday at around 10:15AM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex advanced 313.92 points to 59274.52. As many as 98,791 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 1.91 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 313.4 and a 52-week low of Rs 181.15. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 216.
