Exact times National Grid could impose winter blackouts

news
2022-10-20 08:27:00

National Grid has started planning for winter 2022, with energy demand to spike considerably in the coming months. Officials have recently laid out the utility firm's plans for supply shortages as the western world continues to wean itself off Russian gas. Among the worst case scenarios is a plan for forced blackouts to conserve the country's energy network, and National Grid has revealed the prospective timings for this possibility.Earlier this week, National Grid's chief executive said British households could face blackouts.
