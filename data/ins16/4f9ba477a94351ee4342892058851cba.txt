getty So many people are hustling to become the next big tech entrepreneur—but not everyone knows that about 90% of startups fail. 10% of startups fail within the first year. Across all industries, startup failure rates seem to be close to the same. Failure is most common for startups during years two through five, with 70% falling into this category. The main question: Why do all those startups fail? According to the startup founders, reasons for failure include money running out, being in the wrong market, a lack of research, bad partnerships, ineffective marketing, not having the right team, etc.
