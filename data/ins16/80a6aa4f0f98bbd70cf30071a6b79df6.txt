The Texas Tech Red Raiders (3-3) will host the West Virginia Mountaineers (3-3) on Saturday at 3:00 PM ET.
The Red Raiders enter this matchup after a 41-31 loss to the Oklahoma State Cowboys in their most recent game. The Mountaineers are coming off of a 43-40 win over the Baylor Bears. Behren Morton threw for 379 yards, with two touchdowns and one interception on 39-of-62 passing (62.9%) for the Raiders in that matchup against the Cowboys. He also added 16 carries for 46 yards and one rushing touchdown.
