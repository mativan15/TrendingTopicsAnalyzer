Hebner, a Real Estate Finance and Accounting Veteran with a Passion for Fostering Equity and Innovation, joins Fairstead's Leadership Team to Support the Company's Growth

NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Fairstead, a national purpose-driven, vertically integrated real estate company, today announced that real estate industry veteran Sally Hebner has joined the company as its Chief Financial Officer. Hebner has three decades of experience in real estate accounting and finance and most recently served as the Chief Financial Officer of Enterprise Community Partners, the leading national non-profit housing provider, which she joined in 2005.
