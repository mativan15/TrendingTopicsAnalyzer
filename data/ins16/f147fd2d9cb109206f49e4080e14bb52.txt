[Photo/Xinhua] WASHINGTON - A US congressional panel released a new report on Monday detailing efforts by the Trump administration to politicize federal response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The House Select Subcommittee on the Coronavirus Crisis accused the White House under Donald Trump of undermining "public health to benefit the former president's political goals.
