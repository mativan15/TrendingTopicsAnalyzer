Published: 03:41 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 04:08 EDT, 18 October 2022 Sadio Mane was a proud recipient of the first Socrates Award at the Ballon d'Or awards ceremony on Monday night.The award has been established to recognise accomplishments off the pitch, and Mane was announced as the winner following his charity work in his homeland of Senegal.The Bayern Munich forward accepted the gong in front of a giant screen showing him greeting people on his visits to Senegal, and he expressed his pride at winning the award on stage.
