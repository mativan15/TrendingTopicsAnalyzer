WNBA Coach of Year Curt Miller joins LA Sparks

October 21, 2022 / 6:52 PM / AP Two-time WNBA Coach of the Year Curt Miller is the new coach of the Los Angeles Sparks after seven seasons with the Connecticut Sun, the team said Friday.Miller, also the general manager for the past six years, led the Sun to two trips to the WNBA finals, including this year when they lost to the Las Vegas Aces in four games in the best-of-five series. He was an assistant for the Sparks in 2015 before going to Connecticut."It was an exciting time, such fond memories of my 2015 experience," Miller said.
