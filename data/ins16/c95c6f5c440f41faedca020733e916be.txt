Big decisions ahead for the new gaming committee: The future is now!
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 11:00:14

The Government's decision to set up a committee to regulate online gaming, with a ministry to oversee it, is a welcome move that highlights the Government's commitment towards ensuring a supportive policy framework for tech-led innovation and entrepreneurship in India. This move has been celebrated by the industry, which has been seeking the involvement of the centre for clearly-defined, ubiquitous and uniform policies and regulations for a couple years now. While some state governments have attempted to ban and harshly regulate online gaming, the central government and honourable courts have repeatedly taken measures that reaffirm their support for the promising industry.
