B.A. Sweetie Candy buys chocolate and popcorn maker Olympia Candy
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-20 19:07:00

B.A. Sweetie Candy Co. has acquired chocolate and popcorn manufacturer Olympia Candy, including its manufacturing facility in Strongsville, Ohio, for an undisclosed amount.

Cleveland-based B.A. Sweetie Candy Co. said Olympia's prior owner, Bob McGrath, will continue to manage the new Sweeties Olympia Treats Co. McGrath also will head the popcorn manufacturing division of B.A. Sweetie's popcorn brands, including All American, Campbell's Sweet Factory, Humphrey Popcorn and Olympia in the company's new Sweeties Popcorn Kitchen on Brookpark Road.
