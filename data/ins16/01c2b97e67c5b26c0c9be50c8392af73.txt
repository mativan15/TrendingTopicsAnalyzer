Mohamed Salah is seemingly prepared to join another Premier League club (Image: GETTY)Mohamed Salah would prefer to join another Premier League club if he ends up leaving Liverpool upon the expiry of his contract, according to reports. The Egyptian, who shared this season's Golden Boot with Tottenham Son Heung-min, is still yet to agree terms over a new deal meaning his contract is due to expire in the summer of 2023.Salah has already confirmed he'll be staying at Liverpool next season whatever happens but the longer the saga rumbles on, the more likely the 29-year-old looks to leave.
