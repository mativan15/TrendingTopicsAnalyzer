Cathie Wood's ARK bought $44 million Nvidia shares after the gaming firm beat earnings
Insider
business
2022-05-27 15:39:57

Cathie Wood's ARK Invest bought almost $44 million in shares of Nvidia on Thursday, according to a trading update, after the tech company reported its first-quarter earnings.ARK's purchase of 245,286 Nvidia shares at Thursday's closing price of $178.51 were worth $43.7 million.Nvidia's earnings report showed stronger than expected revenues, but the chip maker warned that tough macroeconomic conditions would reduce its sales. The company said the Russia-Ukraine war and COVID restrictions in China would have a $500 million hit on its revenue for the current quarter.
