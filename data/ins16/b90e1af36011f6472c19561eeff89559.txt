Tata Elxsi falls over 13% in 2 days. What's bothering investors?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 08:45:00

Shares of leading design and technology services provider have fallen over 13% to Rs 7,364 in the last two trading sessions after the company announced its second-quarter results. In the September quarter of FY23, the company's net profit declined by 5.65% as compared to the first quarter of 2022. Whereas, the profit before tax (PBT) of Tata Elxsi stood at Rs 219.17 crore, registering a decline of 3.9% quarter-on-quarter (QoQ) and a growth of 28.2% year-on-year (YoY). For the quarter that ended September 30, 2022, the company reported Rs 763 crore of revenue from operations, a growth of 5 per cent QoQ and 28 per cent YoY while profits after tax grew 39 per cent YoY.
