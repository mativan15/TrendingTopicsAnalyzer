NOIDA: The police have arrested 16 people, including 10 women for allegedly running a fake call centre in Noida. They allegedly cheated a retired general manager of a leading public sector undertaking (PSU) in the city of Rs 1.6 crore on the pretext of renewing his insurance.

The arrests were made after 61-year-old Sudama Prasad Kushwaha, a resident of Sector 75, filed a complaint at Sector 113 police station on May 21 this year. According to him, the accused had duped him of Rs 1.
