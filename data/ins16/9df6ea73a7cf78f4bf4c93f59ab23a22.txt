The Duchess of York Sarah Ferguson has provided a heartwarming update on the corgis. She and her ex-husband Prince Andrew have been taking care of the corgis following the demise of Queen Elizabeth II. The wake of the terrible passing of our longest-reigning king last month revealed that the duo would face the dogs. On Saturday morning, the Duchess of York Sarah Ferguson, shared a photo of herself with the two corgis playing in the sun on Instagram. She captioned the image: The gifts that keep giving.
