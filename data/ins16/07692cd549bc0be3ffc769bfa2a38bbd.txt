Suspect of armed robbery and assault at Dollar General still on the loose, police say
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-27 15:06:47

Cornwall Police are trying to identify a man, still on the loose, who assaulted a clerk and tried to rob the Dollar General in West Cornwall Township Wednesday.

A white man entered the store, at 2520 Quentin Road, brandishing a large hand gun, which he pointed at the store clerk and demanded money, police said. Officers responded to the armed robbery at 8:40 p.m.

"The suspect proceeded to assault the store clerk with the pistol causing injury to the clerk," Police Chief Brett Hopkins said in a statement.
