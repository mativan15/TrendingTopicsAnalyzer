Not Supplied Credit: News Corp AustraliaMashuka TabassumNCA NewsWireFootage of a truck stealing a mini excavator on the Sunshine Coast was released by Queensland police on Wednesday.The mini excavator was stolen from the fenced yard of a Cordwell Rd business at Yandina at around 2am on Sunday, September 4.A mini E20 excavator with Queensland registration 80705C and a trailer with Queensland registration 414UDD were stolen.Detectives appealed for anyone who has seen the excavator, or who recognises the truck towing them, to contact police.
