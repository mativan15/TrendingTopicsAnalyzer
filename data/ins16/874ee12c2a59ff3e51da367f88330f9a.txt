Visit our dedicated hub for coverage of the 2022 midterm elections, and explore our statistical model of the race to control Congress.IT WOULDN'T BE an election year in America without fearmongering campaign ads. Some candidates have elevated them to an art form. A recent one for Christine Drazan, the Republican running for governor in Oregon, opens with a woman recounting how she was held at knifepoint by a homeless man in Portland. It was only thanks to the police that she survived, she tells the camera, before warning that Kate Brown, the current Democratic governor, and Tina Kotek, the Democratic candidate to replace her, are 'releasing criminals' and making Oregonians 'less safe'.
