RT.com 1st June 2022, 21:13 GMT+10Novak Djokovic was not happy with the scheduling of his French Open classic against rival Rafael NadalNovak Djokovic's exit from the French Open wasn't without a bit of customary drama after the Serbian ace criticized event organizers for allowing his marathon match with rival Rafael Nadal to extend past 1am local time.The world number one came out on the losing end of a four-hour epic against clay court specialist Nadal, losing by a score of 2-6, 6-4, 2-6, 6-7(4) in a match which began in May but ended in June.
