Queens Park Rangers went top of the Championship thanks to Lydon Dykes' brace in a 3-0 win over 10-man Cardiff.On the day Wolves got set to make an approach for manager Michael Beale, Dykes struck a 19th-minute penalty after Jack Simpson was shown a straight red card for bringing down Sinclair Armstrong, then doubled his side's lead before the break.Kenneth Paal added a third to make it four wins from five for Rangers, with Beale admitting 'it would be hard to walk away from QPR right now and especially this young group.
