U.S. IPO Weekly Recap: No IPOs Before The Memorial Day Weekend
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-28 06:35:00

Mikko Lemola/iStock via Getty Images There were no pricings ahead of the long holiday weekend, the first week with no IPOs or SPACs in over two years, by Renaissance Capital's standards. Normally, the IPO market is gearing up for a wave of post-Memorial Day launches, but new filings have been scarce in recent weeks. The pipeline got a boost from minerals exploration company Ivanhoe Electric (IE), which filed to raise $200 million in the first major filing since April. Singapore-based investment bank Boustead Wavefront (BOUW) also filed, planning to raise $15 million.
