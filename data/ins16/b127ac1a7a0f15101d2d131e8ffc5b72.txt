By Reuters Published: 01:38 EDT, 6 June 2022 | Updated: 01:38 EDT, 6 June 2022 By Chandini Monnappa BENGALURU, June 6 (Reuters) - Indian shares edged down on Monday dragged by technology stocks, while global investor mood turned cautious ahead of key U.S. economic growth data and central bank rate decisions in Europe and India this week. The NSE Nifty 50 index slipped 0.47% to 16,504.60, as of 0510 GMT, while the S&P BSE Sensex fell 0.49% to 55,484.61.
