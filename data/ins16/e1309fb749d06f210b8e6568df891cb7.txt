I love vacations, just love them. Especially the way vacations are nowadays. The first day sets the tone. You check into the hotel or resort and they put you on the clock immediately. 'Sir buffet breakfast will be served every day from 6.30 to 10 am.' I don't quite remember the last time we took a family vacation out of Pune. It was three maybe four years ago. It's become habit now to blame everything on Covid but that's not true and especially because I know quite a few people who travelled in spite of the pandemic.
