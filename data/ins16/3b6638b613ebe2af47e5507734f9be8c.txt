By Reuters Published: 05:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 05:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 LONDON, Oct 18 (Reuters) - The Bank of England said on Tuesday that a report in the Financial Times about a new delay to the start of its sales of government bonds was inaccurate. "This morning's FT report that the BoE has decided to delay MPC gilt sales ('QT') is inaccurate," a spokesperson for the central bank said. Earlier, the Financial Times said the BoE was likely to push back the start of its sales of government bonds from its latest scheduled date of Oct.
