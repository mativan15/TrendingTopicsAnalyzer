Report: Brooklyn Nets have hired a new head athletic trainer
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-17 20:01:55

With the NBA preseason coming to an end, the Brooklyn Nets have ended the exhibition schedule with a .500 record, bringing home two wins in their final games.
The Nets have signed the head athletic trainer for the New York Football Club and former US Olympic trainer Jonathan Filipe to the franchise. Filipe announced his addition by adding the job to his LinkedIn profile.
The Nets organization is utilizing the remaining time before the regular season to solidify their front office and staff positions that will put them in a winning mindset.
