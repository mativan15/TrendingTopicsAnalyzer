Minco Silver Purchase Stake in Chinese Cement Company Sichuan Hexie Shuangma
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 10:34:00

By Adriano Marchese Canadian-based silver mining company Minco Silver Corp. said Wednesday that it will acquire a stake of less than 1% in Chinese cement manufacturing company Sichuan Hexie Shuangma Co. using spare cash it didn't foresee needing in the near future. Minco said that its China-based subsidiary, Minco Mining (China) Co., has entered into a transfer agreement and will acquire an interest in the Tianjin Saikehuan Enterprise Management Center LP from Tianjin Huaxin Anneng Management Consulting Partnership LP for 119,762,699 Chinese yuan, the equivalent of US$16.
