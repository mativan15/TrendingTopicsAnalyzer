MENLO PARK, Calif., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- PacBio (NASDAQ: PACB), a leading developer of high-quality, highly accurate sequencing solutions, today announced the creation of the Co nsortium for Lo ng R ead S equencing (CoLoRS) that aims to accelerate the utility of long-read human genome datasets. CoLoRS is an open coalition of international researchers focused on creating a comprehensive database of frequency information for all classes of human variation identified using long-read human whole-genome sequencing.
