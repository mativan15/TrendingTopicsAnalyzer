NEW DELHI: Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) is likely to report a 17-40 per cent jump in standalone profit for the March quarter on a 25-30 per cent jump in sales. Margins are seen contracting at least 200 basis points YoY. Emkay Global sees standalone profit for M&M rising 17.5 per cent YoY to Rs 1,178.10 crore from Rs 1,002 crore in the year-ago quarter. It sees sales rising 29.5 per cent YoY to Rs 17,270.50 crore from Rs 13,338.20 crore. Ebitda margin is seen compressing 246 basis points YoY to 12.2 from 14.
