Naga political groups to form Council of Naga Relationships and Cooperation
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 14:30:00

PTIThuingaleng Muivah, general secretary of National Socialist Council of Nagalim (NSCN-IM).Naga national political groups have agreed to form the Council of Naga Relationships and Cooperation to explore, at the earliest, realistic ways for Nagas to move forward on the basis of Naga historical and political rights. The council will be led by the Convenor of Naga National Political Groups (NNPGs)and Chairman of National Socialist Council of Nagalim (Isak-Muivah). In a statement the groups stated in response to the Naga people's yearning for reconciliation and unity in purpose, NNPGs and the NSCN-IM, with a renewed spirit of commitment met in Kolkata on October 17 and 18, 2022 under the initiative of the Forum for Naga Reconciliation (FNR).
