David Ramos/Getty Images News Thesis and Background International Business Machines (NYSE:IBM) faces mixed signals ahead. On the positive side, it finished 2021 with better-than-expected results. Momentum from key segments continued. Hybrid cloud adoption kept its growth. Software and consulting segments also maintained a healthy margin and healthy backlog. The consulting segment saw a 40% increase in bookings, and book-to-bill remained at a solid 1.1 for the quarter and over the last year.
