Delhi: Ghazipur slaughterhouse closure may lead to rise in meat prices, say traders
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 12:45:00

NEW DELHI: Delhi's sole slaughterhouse in Ghazipur has been shut for last few days for allegedly not complying with environmental norms and traders fear that the closure may trigger meat shortage leading to increase in its prices.According to officials of the Municipal Corporation of Delhi, the slaughterhouse was shut on May 30 on the directions from the Delhi Pollution Control Committee following National Green Tribunal's directives. Ghazipur slaughterhouse caters to the meat requirements of larger part of the city.
