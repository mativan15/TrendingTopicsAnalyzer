Midmarket organizations are primed for growth with digital transformation
thestar.com
news
2022-10-21 15:40:00

While small and medium-sized businesses are the backbone of the Canadian economy, they hesitate to commit to larger capital IT investments, opting to focus on growing their business. This is no longer the best approach as technology has evolved such that it is the foundation for enabling the strategic vision and business outcomes mid-sized organizations are prioritizing.

Midmarket businesses are primed to take advantage of the opportunities that digital transformations can bring them. In fact, it is becoming a strategic differentiator for them.
