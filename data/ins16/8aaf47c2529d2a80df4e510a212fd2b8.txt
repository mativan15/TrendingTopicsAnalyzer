LDF govt. plans to build 63 lakh sq. ft. of IT space, 67,000 jobs during its tenure: Chief Minister

The Left Democratic Front (LDF) government is aiming at constructing 63 lakh square foot of IT space and create 67,000 job opportunities during its current tenure, Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan has said. Speaking after inaugurating the 25th anniversary celebrations of IBS Software here on Wednesday, he said that since 2016, Kerala's total IT space had been expanded by 46.4 lakh square foot, creating 45,896 additional IT jobs. He said that over the past six years, the number of start-ups in Kerala had risen from a mere 300 to a massive 3,900.
