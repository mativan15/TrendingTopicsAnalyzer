[SEOUL METROPOLITAN GOVERNMENT] The Seoul Friendship Festival, the city's largest multicultural festival, is returning after two years. The Seoul Metropolitan Government announced Monday that it will host the Seoul Friendship Festival 2022 this weekend at Seoul Plaza, Mugyo-ro and Cheonggyecheon-ro with 46 participating embassies. The festival was first held in 1996, so marks its 25th edition this year. The 2020 event was canceled and the 2021 festival held online due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
