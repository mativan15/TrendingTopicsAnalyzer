When Pennsylvania Attorney General and Gubernatorial Candidate Josh Shapiro expressed his support for Lifeline Scholarships last month, many were shocked, and some people were just angry. One activist for traditional public schools even said that 'Real Dems Don't Support Vouchers.' We beg to differ. Between the two of us, we have more than a century of experience working to rectify the most stubborn inequities in American society, particularly for young Black and Brown people.
