Oct. 17—ANNUAL WOMAN OF ACHIEVEMENT DINNER — Uptown Midland Business and Professional Women is honoring Kathleen Kirwan-Haynie, senior life executive director and special needs advocate, as the 2022 Woman of Achievement at a dinner at 6 p.m. today at the Petroleum Club, 501 W. Wall St., Midland.

The annual event celebrates National Business Women's Week by honoring the contributions of working women, as well as the employers who support working women and their families.

Tickets start at $65 per person.
