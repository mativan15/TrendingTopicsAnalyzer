Bengaluru might be India's tech hub, but the city is also famous for its traffic. The long traffic snarls are a constant theme and residents continue to struggle on a daily basis to reach their destinations. Not that we needed any more reminding of the issue of traffic in Bengaluru, but one man has perfectly summed up the situation.   PTIA Twitter user named Shrikant took a sly dig at the city's traffic and we are sure everyone will find it relatable. In his witty post, Shrikant claimed that his friend is planning to sell the third, fourth and fifth gear of his car.
