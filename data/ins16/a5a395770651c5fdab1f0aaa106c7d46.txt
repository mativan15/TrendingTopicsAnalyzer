Liz Truss is to resign as prime minister after just six weeks, Sky News understands.Her time in office has been dominated by market chaos prompted by the mini-budget that was announced by former chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng last month.
Despite sacking Mr Kwarteng last week and reversing almost all the unfunded tax cuts that had been proposed, the prime minister's position had continued to come under pressure.Follow the latest politics reaction liveSome Tory MPs had publicly called for her resignation, but many others had privately suggested her time was up.
