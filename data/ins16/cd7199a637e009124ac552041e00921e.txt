Aayush Sharma says people still address him as ‘Salman Khan ka jija' as he recalls getting trolled after marrying Arpita Khan
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 02:59:47

Aayush Sharma got married to Salman Khan's sister Arpita Khan in 2014. And with this, the actor also came on the radar of trolls. Recently, the film 'Loveyatri' actor recalled getting trolled mercilessly post-wedding and revealed people still address him as ‘Salman Khan ka jija.' During a conversation with a portal, Aayush said that while some people said he married Arpita for money, others felt he did so to become an actor. However, he admitted that despite not being a star kid, he didn't have to struggle like an outsider.
