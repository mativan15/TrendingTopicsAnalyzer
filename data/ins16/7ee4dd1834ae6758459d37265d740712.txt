West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee wished quick recovery for Shah Rukh Khan as reports suggested that he has tested positive for Covid-19. Banerjee wrote on Twitter, 'Just came to know that our Brand Ambassador Shahrukh Khan has been detected covid positive. Pray fastest recovery for the superstar. Get well Shahrukh! Spring back asap!' Shah Rukh and his representatives are yet to confirm the reports.
The news comes days after Shah Rukh announced his pan-India film Jawan, to be directed by Atlee.
