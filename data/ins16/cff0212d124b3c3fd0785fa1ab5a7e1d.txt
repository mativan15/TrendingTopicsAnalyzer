Beware storm season hype

As predictable as the hurricane season is the media's hype that follows it. Seasoned Floridians leaned long ago to ignore most of the broadcast and printed materials regarding the much-too-often-inaccurate predictions of these purported storms. We have become complacent and mistrusting by the continuous supply of misinformation. New residents to the state will yield to the hype until they also become aware of the hysteria created by this false narrative of doom and gloom.
