Biden calls Truss's economic policies 'a mistake'
msn.com
world
2022-10-16 15:45:00

The president of the United States, Joe Biden, has called Liz Truss's original economic policies "a mistake". In an unusual intervention, he said the economic turmoil that followed the government's mini-budget had been "predictable".Speaking at an ice cream shop in Oregon, Mr Biden was asked by reporters about the prime minister's attempt to boost growth with unfunded tax cuts - what the reporter called the "trickle-down plan that she has had to back away from". The president replied: "Well, it's predictable.
