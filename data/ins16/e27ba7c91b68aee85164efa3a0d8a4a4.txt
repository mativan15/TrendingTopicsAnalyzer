KKR & Co, Brookfield and existing investors TPG and Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA) are investing $500 million in two wholly owned subsidiaries of UPL Ltd, an agrochemicals, seeds and crop protection company. UPL announced Friday that KKR is investing ₹2,460 crore ($300 million) in Advanta Enterprises, which specialises in seeds technology, for a 13.33% stake, valuing the unit at ₹18,450 crore ($2.25 billion).
