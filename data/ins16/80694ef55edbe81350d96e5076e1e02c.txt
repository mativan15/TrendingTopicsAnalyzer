ET Intelligence Group: Consumer-facing businesses, such as retail and phone services, are increasingly making up a disproportionate share of operating profits at India's biggest company by value, Reliance Industries (RIL). In the second quarter, these verticals contributed nearly half to RIL's operating profit, although their collective revenue share is about a third of the total.
