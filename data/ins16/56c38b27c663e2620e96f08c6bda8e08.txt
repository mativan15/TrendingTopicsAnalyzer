Premier League champions say their team bus' windscreen was cracked after fiery evening in which Pep Guardiola had also been targeted with coinsMan City allege that this damage was caused as their bus left Anfield on Sunday evening Manchester City claim the windscreen of their team bus was damaged as it left Anfield following the 1-0 defeat by Liverpool on Sunday. The incident has further heightened tension between the clubs after their hosts claimed some parts of the away section was vandalised by visiting fans, who also allegedly sang inflammatory and offensive chants.
