Relief for Richarlison as calf injury will not rule him out of World Cup
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 23:01:00

Richarlison is set to be fit for the World Cup, Antonio Conte, the Tottenham Hotspur head coach, has confirmed.The Brazil forward was in tears after being forced off in the 52nd minute of Saturday's 2-0 win over Everton with a calf injury. But a scan has revealed that the injury is 'not serious' and while the 25-year-old will miss Wednesday's trip to Manchester United, he should return for Spurs before the Premier League pauses on November 13 for the tournament in Qatar.'It's an injury but not so serious,' Conte said.
