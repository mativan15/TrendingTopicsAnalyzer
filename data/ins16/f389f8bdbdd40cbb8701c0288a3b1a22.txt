Image source, Northumbria PoliceImage caption, Police praised bank staff for "trusting their instincts"Bank staff have been praised for protecting a pensioner who was falling victim to an alleged £8,000 roofing fraud.The man, aged in his 70s, had taken out £5,000 that week and Lloyds Bank staff in South Shields became suspicious when he tried to withdraw £3,000 more.He told them roofers working on his home urgently needed more money to complete work as it was going to rain.Police inspected the roof and only found one tile missing and one loose.
