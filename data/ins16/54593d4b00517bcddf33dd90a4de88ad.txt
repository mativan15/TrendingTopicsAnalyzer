(Bloomberg) -- Australia's economy expanded faster than forecast last quarter as households stepped up spending, bolstering the case for an outsized 40-basis-point interest rate hike next week.

Most Read from Bloomberg

Gross domestic product advanced 0.8% from the final three months of last year, taking the annual pace to 3.3%, government data showed Wednesday. That was higher than estimates and outpaced the pre-pandemic average of around 2%.

In response to the stronger result, the three-year government bond yield jumped to 2.
