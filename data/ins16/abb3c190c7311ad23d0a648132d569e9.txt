After being cut in two last year, Wellington's iconic foodie festival Visa Wellington on a Plate​ is back with a vengeance and some big changes announced. This year's theme, ‘'A State Of Flux'', took stock of the past two years of Covid-forced fluidity, recognising that nothing was constant, said festival director and chief executive Sarah Meikle​. That included the festival itself, which opens with a new event focused on sustainable seafood and news that the festival will be split into two parts from 2023.
