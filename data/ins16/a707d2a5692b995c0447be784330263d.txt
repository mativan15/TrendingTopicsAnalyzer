Irani national arrested for extortion attempt
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-17 19:03:22

An Irani national has been arrested by unit 2 of the crime branch of Pune city police for attempting to extort Rs40 lakh from a businesswoman to withdraw a case filed against her son by an Irani girl. The accused, identified as Farzad Mohamad Riza Fakhabadi, 31, a resident of Kondhwa, is a member of the Irani group and is here on business visa. According to the police, Fakhabadi is the leader of the Irani group in Pune, and works for the Irani community. As per the complaint filed by the businesswoman, Sapana Morarjee, Fakhabadi demanded Rs40 lakh to withdraw a case filed against her son, Dhanraj Morarjee, for allegedly threatening an Irani girl.
