In full: Jeremy Hunt's speech scrapping ‘almost all' of the mini-budget

Chancellor Jeremy Hunt, only four days into the job, has ripped up predecessor Kwasi Kwarteng's mini-Budget and abandoned the key tenets of Liz Truss' economic agenda.
Mr Hunt reversed "almost all" of the tax plans announced on September 23 in a pre-recorded statement released on Monday morning, saying the U-turn would help delivery "stability" after weeks of market turmoil.

A central responsibility for any government is to do what is necessary for economic stability.
