Ben Hall in KyivNine out of ten Ukrainians consider victory over Russia to mean regaining all territory lost since 2014, including Crimea, according to a new opinion poll by Gallup.Two-thirds of Ukrainians say Kyiv should keep fighting until it wins the war, while 26 per cent want it to negotiate an end to the fighting as soon as possible, the survey found.The poll results underscore the Ukrainian government's determination to liberate all of its territories before engaging in peace talks with Moscow.
