Albertsons stock gains after profit, sales rise above expectations
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 12:40:32

Shares of Albertsons Companies Inc. ACI, +0.84% rose 1.0% in premarket trading Tuesday, after the grocery chain, which has agreed to be acquired by Kroger Co. KR, -2.43%, reported fiscal second-quarter profit and sales that rose above expectations, even as gross margin decreased. Net income for the quarter to Sept. 10 rose to $342.7 million, or 59 cents a share, from $295.2 million, or 52 cents a share, in the year-ago period. Excluding nonrecurring items, adjusted earnings per share of 72 cents beat the FactSet consensus of 65 cents.
