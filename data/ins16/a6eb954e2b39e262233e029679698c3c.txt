Police seizes Rs 2 crore from businessman's car in Bengal's Howrah
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 16:15:00

PTIKolkata Police personnel at work.Cash worth Rs 2 crore and a huge amount of gold and diamond ornaments have been recovered from a private car, owned by a businessman, inside a posh residential complex's parking area at Shibpur in Howrah, by Kolkata Police in connection with a bank fraud case. Apart from this recovery, two bank accounts with Rs 20 crore cash have been freezed. Sailesh Pande, a businessman, and his brother Rajesh Pande were residents of the residential apartment, who had accounts in a branch of Canara Bank in South 24 district's Narendrapur.
