The free transfer will offically go through on July 1 ahead of the Croatian winger linking up with Antonio Conte, the pair having won the Scudetto together at the Giuseppe Meazza two seasons ago.Inter admitted on Monday that they had lost the battle to keep Perisic, who is said to want a new challenge at Tottenham. He flew into London to complete a medical on Monday before today's announcement.Spurs will also sign Fraser Forster on a free transfer from Southampton when his contract at St Mary's is up at the end of next month.
