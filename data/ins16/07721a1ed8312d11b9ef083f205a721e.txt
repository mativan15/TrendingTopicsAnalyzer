SAN FRANCISCO, June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Newfront, a tech-enabled, full-service brokerage based in San Francisco, CA, welcomes Judy McLaughlin as Strategic Benefits Consultant. Judy joins Newfront with three decades of experience in the industry, most recently as Senior Vice President, Client Advocate at Willis Towers Watson."Judy has a great reputation, and her customer service philosophy aligns with our mission of putting clients at the very center of all that we do," said Newfront President Brian Hetherington.
