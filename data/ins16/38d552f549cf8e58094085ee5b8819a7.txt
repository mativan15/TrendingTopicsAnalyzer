SSRGA acquires Turek Roth Grossman
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-17 18:39:00

Law firm Schwartz Sladkus Reich Greenberg Atlas LLP, also known as SSRGA, has acquired Turek Roth Grossman, a New York-based law firm focused on real estate, business and litigation.

SSRGA's acquisition comes as the firm increased its footprint by 12,000 square feet last month at 444 Madison, between East 26th and 27th streets. The firm's real estate footprint now totals 45,000 square feet.

Allen Turek, Jeremy Roth, Jason Grossman and Gaddi Goren of Turek Roth Grossman will become partners in SSRGA.
