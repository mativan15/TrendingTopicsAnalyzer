CryptocurrencyOn the MoveMay 31, 2022 3:59 PM ETBTC, BTC-USDBy: Liz Kiesche, SA News Editor Marco Bello/Getty Images News MicroStrategy (MSTR) stock is soaring 19% in late Tuesday trading after bitcoin (BTC-USD) rises 3.2% to break above $31K for the first time since May 16 and stemming a nine-week decline. The software company's stock is affected by the crypto's price moves because MicroStrategy (MSTR) holds 129,200 bitcoins (BTC-USD) on its balance sheet, making it the world's largest publicly traded corporate owner of the cryptocurrency.
