Analysis: Liz Truss is in big trouble after her biggest U-turn yet
ctvnews.ca
world
2022-10-15 14:59:52

Liz Truss has sacrificed her finance minister and closest political ally just weeks into her premiership in order to save her own skin. On Friday morning, Britain's Chancellor of the Exchequer, Kwasi Kwarteng, was summoned a day early back to London from the United States straight to Downing Street, where he was relieved of his duties. The move came three weeks after Kwarteng announced a controversial mini-budget full of unfunded tax-cutting measures that sent financial markets into meltdown. At one point, the pound sank to its lowest level against the dollar in decades.
