This plastic self-destructs within two years
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 13:44:14

A London-based startup has developed a new, low-cost additive that makes plastics self-destruct if they escape into the environment. By adding a sprinkling of Polymateria's "special sauce" at the point of manufacture, strong, recyclable plastics can be programmed to incorporate a biodegradable Plan B.The United Nations has estimated that 400 million tons of plastic waste are produced every year. Thirty-two percent of this plastic escapes collection systems and ends up in nature, with millions of tons entering the ocean every year.
