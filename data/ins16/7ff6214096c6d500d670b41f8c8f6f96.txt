Semi-annual trunk sale in Kelowna this weekend
globalnews.ca
news
2022-05-27 23:22:53

Love hunting for deals? A trunk sale in Kelowna this weekend may offer some thrilling finds.
Organized by the Regional Waste Reduction Office, the semi-annual Trunk Sale will run from 8 a.m. to noon on Saturday at Okanagan College on KLO Road.The waste reduction office says it's the largest garage sale in the Central Okanagan, with more than 150 vendors selling 'stuff straight from the trunks of their vehicles.''The trunk sale model is unique in these parts: A mega garage sale from the backs of vehicles, one convenient location, tons of bargains,' said Rae Stewart, a waste reduction facilitator with the regional district.
