Shehan Karunatilaka, a writer from Sri Lanka, won the 2022 Booker Prize for Fiction for his metaphysical thriller "The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida". The novel was praised for its scope and the humorous daring of its narrative tactics. This is the story of a war photographer, who has "Seven Moons" to try and unravel the mystery surrounding his passing and aid in the disclosure of a collection of images that will shock war-torn Sri Lanka.
