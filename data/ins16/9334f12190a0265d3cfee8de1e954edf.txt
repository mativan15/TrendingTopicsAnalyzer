AgenciesThe film has been running almost non-stop at the Maratha Mandir theatre in Mumbai since 1995. How many times are you willing to re-watch your favourite movie at the theatres? Twice? Thrice? Will you go watch the same movie every year? That's what the fans of ‘DDLJ' aka the glorious ‘Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge' have been doing for the past 27 years or so. Ever since its release in 1995, fans have shown up at the Maratha Mandir film theatre in Mumbai without fail to catch the matinee show at 11:30 am.
