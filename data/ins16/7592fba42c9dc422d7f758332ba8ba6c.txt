Paris, France, May 27, 2022 – The 2022 edition of the AFRICA CEO FORUM, the largest annual event dedicated to private sector development in Africa, will be held on June 13-14, 2022, in Abidjan, Côte d'Ivoire. At a time when the world is recovering and rebuilding from COVID-19, and 10 years after its first edition, the event will focus on proposing new routes for African growth. Vaccine production, disruption of supply chains, digitalization of economies, energy transition and the health crisis brought on by COVID-19 have accelerated the transformation of economies towards new models.
