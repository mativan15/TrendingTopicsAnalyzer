 A Kelowna home suffered extreme damage Thursday night when a fire was sparked, but officials say there were no injuries.
The blaze that fire officials have now deemed accidental in nature broke out at around 7:30 p.m. inside a house in the 1900 block of McDougall Street. When firefighters arrived, they could see flames in the area of the roof.'It was confirmed by the homeowner that no one was inside the structure.
