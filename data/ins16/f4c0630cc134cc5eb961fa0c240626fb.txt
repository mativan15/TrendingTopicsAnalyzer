NASHUA, N.H., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Dresner Advisory Services today published the 2022 Financial Consolidation, Close Management, and Financial Reporting Market Study, part of its Wisdom of Crowds® series of research. Financial consolidation, close management, and financial reporting solutions are closely related to enterprise performance management (EPM). Financial consolidation systems combine and aggregate financial data from multiple operating entities to produce an overall consolidated financial view of the group's operations.
