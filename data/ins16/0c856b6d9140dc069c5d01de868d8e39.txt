Queen Letizia stepped out today in Berlin (Image: GETTY)Queen Letizia, 50, and King Felippe, 54, along with the Berlin Mayor Franziska Giffey, walked across Pariser Platz square today in front of the landmark Brandenburg Gate in central Berlin. The Queen braved the rain and carried a large umbrella for the occasion.Letizia opted for a Carolina Herrera Belted Coat for today's occasion, which looked very similar to a camel coat worn by Princess Eugenie on Monday.The Queen has been seen wearing this coat since 2017, and she wore it over her shoulders as opposed to putting her arms through the sleeves.
