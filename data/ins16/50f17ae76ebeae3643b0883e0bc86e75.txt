Investors' love affair with tech darlings turns sour
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 15:00:00

Investors switched off Netflix almost as fast as its former subscribers. Twitter's wings have been clipped despite its agreed $44 billion sale to the world's richest man. Even Apple — no longer the world's largest public company — has fallen from the top of the tree.American technology companies are battening down the hatches as their shares tumble. Sequoia Capital, one of Silicon Valley's leading venture capital firms, has described the current situation as a 'crucible moment'.This week, it was Snap's turn to crackle and pop.
