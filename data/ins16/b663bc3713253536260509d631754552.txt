One of the biggest occasions for an individual, who has had the privilege of getting educated, is the time when their board examination results are announced. Most people in India get to experience this moment twice. The first time they experience this is when their Class 10 results are announced and the second time they go through a roller-coaster of emotions when their Class 12 board exam results are scheduled to be out. Waiting for one's board exam results is both an exciting and unnerving experience.
