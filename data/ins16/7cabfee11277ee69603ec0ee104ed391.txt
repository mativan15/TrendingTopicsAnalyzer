All Blacks star Ardie Savea to take post-Rugby World Cup sabbatical in 2024
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-17 05:21:15

All Blacks star Ardie Savea is set to take a sabbatical in 2024 to play overseas. Savea is heading to Japan to play for the Kobelco Kobe Steelers, before returning to New Zealand in June. Savea, who has played 67 tests, remains contracted with New Zealand Rugby until the end of the 2025 season. He re-committed to the Hurricanes and NZR late last year, where a sabbatical was part of his contract extension.
