Apple announces new-look iPad and upgraded iPad Pro
Copyright 2022, ESI Media Ltd
news
2022-10-18 16:07:19

Apple has unveiled a redesigned version of the iPad alongside an update to its top-of-the-range iPad Pro line-up in its latest round of new gadget announcements.The tech giant's new entry-level iPad model has seen the home button removed, the front-facing camera moved to the longer side of the device and the lightning port changed to the more widely used USB-C connection.It is joined in the line-up by an upgraded version of the iPad Pro, which includes Apple's high-powered, own-made M2 chip for faster performance.
