ATHENS, Greece, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Imperial Petroleum Inc. is a ship-owning company providing petroleum product, crude oil and drybulk seaborne transportation services, announced today that it will release its third quarter and nine months' financial results for the period ended September 30, 2022 before the market opens in New York on October 24, 2022. On October 24, 2022 at 11:00 am ET, the company's management will host a conference call to discuss the results and the company's operations and outlook.
