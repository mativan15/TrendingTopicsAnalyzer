MIAMI, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Global Crossing Airlines Group, Inc. (JET: NEO; JET.B: NEO; JETMF: OTCQB) (the 'Company' or 'GlobalX') is pleased to announce the start of Phase 2 of the Colombia AOC (Air Operators Certificate) Certification Process by GlobalX-Colombia. Initial approval from the Colombian CAA to start the formal certification was received in June of this year. GlobalX-Colombia is a 100% owned subsidiary of GlobalX and is led by Roberto Held, former CFO of Avianca and now a senior advisor to GlobalX who will become CEO of the subsidiary upon final certification.
