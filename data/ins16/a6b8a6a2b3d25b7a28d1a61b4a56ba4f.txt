Yagan Square 'ghost town' in heart of Perth CBD to undergo $8.4 million overhaul
abc.net.au
business
2022-10-16 06:42:32

The WA government will spend millions of dollars on a five-level hospitality venue in an effort to breathe life into the embattled Yagan Square in the heart of Perth's nightlife precinct. The Market Hall precinct was described as a "ghost town" by previous tenantsIt will undergo an $8.4 million overhaul to attract more people to the cityThe state government has committed $5.4 million to the projectHospitality group Nokturnl has signed a 30-year lease to redevelop the Market Hall precinct, which was described by previous food vendors last year as a "white elephant" when it failed to live up to expectations as the crowing jewel of the CityLink project.
