In Kyiv, the home front is back and winter is looming
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-21 14:49:13

For some it was a rude awakening. Those arriving into Kyiv's busy central station on Monday morning suddenly found themselves in the middle of an unexpected and unwanted drone war; hammered by the sounds of panicked last-ditch gunfire from the ground that failed to stop five terrifying explosions in two hours.It was the second Monday in a row the centre of Kyiv had been targeted, and the first time the capital was hit by Iranian-made Shahed-136 drones. The Russians had been aiming for a power plant a block from the station, but instead hit buildings and people elsewhere.
