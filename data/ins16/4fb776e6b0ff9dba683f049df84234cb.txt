October 20, 2022 08:32 PM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AnalytixInsight Inc. ('AnalytixInsight', or the 'Company') (TSX-V: ALY; OTCQB: ATIXF), a financial content and enterprise software solutions provider, is pleased to announce today that Scott Gardner has been appointed as a director of the Company, effective immediately.



Mr. Gardner has been the Chief Investment Officer Chief Investment Officer ('CIO') of Torrent Capital Inc., a Halifax based investment company, ('Torrent') since its formation in February 2017.
