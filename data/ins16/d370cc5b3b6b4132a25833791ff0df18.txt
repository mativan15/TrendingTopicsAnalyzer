Published: 01:29 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 01:29 EDT, 27 May 2022 'Today, in my capacity as interim Minister for Home Affairs, I exercised my power under section 195A of the Migration Act 1958 to intervene in the case of the Murugappan family.'The effect of my intervention enables the family to return to Biloela, where they can reside lawfully in the community on bridging visas while they work towards the resolution of their immigration status, in accordance with Australian law.'I have spoken to the family and wished them well for their return.
