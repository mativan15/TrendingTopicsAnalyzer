Boris Johnson removes instruction to ministers to 'uphold the very highest standards of propriety' in new Ministerial Code foreword
Insider
business
2022-05-27 13:41:14

Prime Minister Boris Johnson has removed an instruction to ministers in his foreword to the Ministerial Code, the handbook of rules for how ministers should behave, to "uphold the very highest standards of propriety."The updated foreword came alongside a minimally changed edition of the Code, the first update since the first edition released under Johnson's premiership in August 2019.In Johnson's first foreword, he stressed how ministers must "win back the trust of the British people.""We must uphold the very highest standards of propriety — and this code sets out how we must do so.
