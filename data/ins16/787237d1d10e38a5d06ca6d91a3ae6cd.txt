May 27, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--System1, Inc. (NYSE: SST) ('System1' or the 'Company'), an omnichannel customer acquisition marketing platform, announced that the Company will be participating in the BofA Securities Global Technology Conference to be held at The Ritz-Carlton Hotel, San Francisco, CA on June 7, 2022.

Michael Blend, System1's Chief Executive Officer & Co-Founder, will participate in a panel discussion titled Adtech in a Post-IDFA World at 2:20pm PT on June 7, 2022.
