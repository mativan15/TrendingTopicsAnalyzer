New York Attorney General Warns of Risks in Crypto Investment
wsj.com
news
2022-06-02 23:30:00

Risk & Compliance Journal Photo: MIKE SEGAR/REUTERS New York's attorney general said the cryptocurrency market contains myriad risks for investors, from wild price swings to potential losses from hacks or fraud. Letitia James reminded New Yorkers of the risks of investing in virtual currency in a guidance note published Thursday. She said investors have lost hundreds of billions of dollars in crypto investments in the market turmoil last month and said even legitimate virtual currency assets are subject to speculative bubbles and security issues.
