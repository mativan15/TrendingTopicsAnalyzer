iStockIn the 2018 Nagaland Assembly election, the NDPP won 16 seats while BJP got 12 to form the alliance government.Nagaland BJP president Temjen Imna Along asserted that the party's national leaders are committed to the current alliance with NDPP but its continuation for the 2023 state elections would be announced at the right time. The Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party (NDPP) and BJP had announced a pre-poll alliance before the 2018 Nagaland Assembly elections and contested the elections on a seat-sharing basis of 40:20.
