Domestic gas price hike to help upstream players: Should you invest?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 03:15:00

The government has increased domestic APM (administered price mechanism) gas prices by 40% to $8.57/mmbtu from $6.1/mmbtu. The new prices will be applicable for the second half of 2022-23. In addition, the ceiling price for high-pressure high-temperature fields has also been raised to $12.5/mmbtu from $9.9/mmbtu.
