ET Canada On Wednesday, Country Music Television announced the stacked line-up for its final 'Next Women of Country' showcase of the year, taking place on Nov. 8.The event, in partnership with City Winery Nashville, will honour country star Loretta Lynn, who peacefully passed away earlier this month.'CMT Next Women of Country: Celebrating the Songs of Loretta Lynn', presented by 'CMT Night Out Nashville' and Change The Conversation, will pay tribute to the late icon's legendary catalog, with Lynn's songs performed by a special group of female artists.
