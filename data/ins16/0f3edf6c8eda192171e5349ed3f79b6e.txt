Covid Lockdown is over - so why are College and Graduate School enrollments declining? U S Colleges are seeing this year well over half a million fewer students than last year. Higher education expert Dr. Marty Nemko says many college prospects are just not seeing the value. "The costs continue to go up well in excess inflation rates even though the classes you're paying for are mostly remote!" And Dr. Nemko says in many industries, a bachelors degree isn't heralded like it used to be. "Employers are increasingly recognizing, "What we need is a person who can think, who is responsible, and who is ethical.
