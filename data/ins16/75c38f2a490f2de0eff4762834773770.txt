October 19, 2022 11:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Calix, Inc. (NYSE: CALX) paid tribute to their partner community by presenting the 2022 Partner Innovations Awards at ConneXions 2022. The Calix Partner Innovations Awards are given at ConneXions to Calix channel and solution partners who have made extraordinary contributions throughout the year. This year's awards honor partners who have simplified service delivery and helped build and grow new broadband businesses.
