Certificates issued for 1.5 MT wheat export after ban
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 20:00:00

The Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) has issued registration certificates for 1.5 million tonnes of wheat to exporters having valid letter of credit, after the grain exports was banned on May 13. At an event organised by the Federation of Indian Export Organisations (FIEO), DGFT Santosh Kumar Sarangi said that some applications are pending and are being scrutinised. 
The government has allowed wheat shipments for which irrevocable letters of credit (L/C) were issued on or before May 13, when the ban of the export of the foodgrain was imposed.
