City sees 31st cadaver organ donation
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-17 18:27:46

Mumbai: The city witnessed its 31st cadaver organ donation after the relatives of a 57-year-old brain-dead woman donated her liver and kidneys on October 17. The woman was admitted to Global Hospital-Parel.While the donation gave a new lease of life to three patients, Zonal Transplant Coordination Committee (ZTCC) has now formed a group for neurosurgeons and neurologists in the city to boost the cadaver organ donation programme further.Dr Bharat Shah, general secretary, ZTCC said, 'They are the ones who also identify brain-dead patients.
