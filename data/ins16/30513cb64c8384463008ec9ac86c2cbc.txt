Covid-19: Mumbai sees 318 cases, no death; active tally now 2,238
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 15:00:00

Mumbai on Monday reported 318 COVID-19 cases, the fifth day in a row when the addition to the tally was above 300, taking the infection caseload here to 10,65,296, while the death toll stood unchanged at 19,566, a civic official said. The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation official said 298 of the 318 new cases were asymptomatic, while just three of the 20 persons hospitalised needed oxygen support. 
He also said only 98 of the 24,472 hospital beds earmarked for COVID-19 treatment are currently occupied.
