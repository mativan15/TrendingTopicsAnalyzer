Hayat Brown, a leading engineering and advisory firm, has named Shawn Dolan as Senior Vice President of Business Development. Shawn has nearly 20 years of experience developing, managing, and implementing major engineering, infrastructure, and management programs for federal agencies and state governments. Shawn will be responsible for growing the firm's federal sector business, leveraging its engineering, design, construction management, regulatory support, and permitting services.
