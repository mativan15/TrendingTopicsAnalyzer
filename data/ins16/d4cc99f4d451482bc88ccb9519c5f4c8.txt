Suella Braverman has departed role as home secretary
sky.com
news
2022-10-19 16:23:00

Home Secretary Suella Braverman has departed the government, a source from the department has confirmed.She was only appointed to the role 43 days ago by new prime minister Liz Truss.It follows the sacking of chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng last Friday and the axing of the majority of the government's mini-budget on Monday by new Chancellor Jeremy Hunt.
