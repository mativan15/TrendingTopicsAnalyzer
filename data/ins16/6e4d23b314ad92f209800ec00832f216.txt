Low-pressure area over Andaman sea intensifies, cyclone likely to be formed by October 24
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-22 09:30:00

The name 'Sitrang' has been proposed by Thailand for the possible cyclonic storm.A low-pressure area over the north Andaman sea has intensified into a depression, around 1,460 km south-southeast of Sagar Island in West Bengal. It is expected to move northwestward and intensify further into a deep depression over east-central and adjoining southeast Bay of Bengal by October 23 morning. 
It is expected to take the shape of a cyclonic storm by October 24, the IMD said in its bulletin. The weather system moved west-northwestward and concentrated into a depression on Saturday at 8.
