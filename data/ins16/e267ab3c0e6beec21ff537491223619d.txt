Mapleton Board Approves To Allow 5G Cell Tower To Be Built On School Land
iheart.com
news
2022-10-18 21:45:00

Photo: Getty ImagesIn Ashland County, the Mapleton School Board of Education has agreed to a deal that will see a 5G cell phone tower built on school property. RichlandSource.com reports, the deal is with Arcadia Towers, who will now work with cell carriers for a lease contract, which will see the school district receive a portion of money. The decision on where the tower will be located is up to the school district.
