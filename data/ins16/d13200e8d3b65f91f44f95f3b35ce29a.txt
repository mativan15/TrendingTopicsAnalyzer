Covid In China: China once boasted of a robust defence against COVID-19 pandemic.Beijing: In order to stabilize the nation's bleeding economy and avoid negative growth in the April-June quarter, Chinese premier Li Keqiang has been brought back at the forefront to revive the economyThe Chinese economy is reeling from the double whammy of a strict zero-COVID policy and President Xi Jinping's crackdown on the property and tech industries.As the Chinese economy contracted by 6.8 per cent in real terms in the January-March quarter of 2020, Xi Jinping had no other option but to tap Premier Li to stop the economic repression that he had largely sidelined throughout their decade in office, Nikkei Asia reported.
