Rasipuram man, his mother convicted for dowry death
thehindu.com
news
2022-06-04 08:07:07

An SC/ST Special Court has convicted a casual labourer and his mother for causing the death of his wife by harassing her for dowry. They were sentenced to seven years of imprisonment for abetment of suicide.According to the prosecution, the first accused R. Ramachandran (32), a resident of Rasipuram married Udhayatharagai (23) in 2013. She died by suicide in June 2014 at their residence following a quarrel with her husband. Her father had filed a complaint with Rasipuram police against her husband and his mother R.
