Facebook's chief operating officer Sheryl Sandberg announced she will be leaving the social network in the fall to pursue philanthropic work. Her announcement has come at a tough time for the company that is facing an uncertain future and fierce competition.Sandberg joined Facebook when it was just a start-up and its founder was only 23 years old. A Harvard-educated executive, she helped pave the path for Facebook's multi-billion-dollar advertising empire. And she did succeed, and make the social network a colossus.
