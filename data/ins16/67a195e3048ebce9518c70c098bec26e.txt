Attendance Allowance claimants should have started to receive their £150 cost of living support. The support package introduced by the Government also includes the Energy Price Guarantee and cost of living payments totalling £650.Britons claiming Attendance Allowance are part of the six million disabled people in the UK getting those one-off support.Anyone receiving disability benefits may be eligible for the payment.These benefits include a Disability Living Allowance, Personal Independence Payment, Attendance Allowance, Scottish disability benefits (adult and child), Armed Forces Independence Payment, Constant Attendance Allowance and War Pension Mobility Supplement.
