Shanghai enters post-lockdown life, but China's COVID-zero policy will haunt the younger generation
abc.net.au
economics
2022-06-02 18:58:49

While Shanghai residents head out into the streets to celebrate the city's reopening, Yuchen Yang and her peers are still unsure when their lockdown will end.The 22-year-old Shanghai university student has stayed on campus since her university enacted its COVID-19 bubble in March.During Shanghai's COVID lockdown, the city's 64 universities closed their campuses, with around 740,000 students trapped inside their dormitories. Many of them were given limited time to leave their rooms throughout the day.
