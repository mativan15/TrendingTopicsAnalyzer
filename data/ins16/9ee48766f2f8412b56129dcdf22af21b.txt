Texas LT Kelvin Banks among On3's true freshmen standouts in Week 7
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-18 22:07:42

True freshman left tackle Kelvin Banks has played absolutely fantastic for Texas this season.

The former five-star recruit in the 2022 recruiting class has looked like a seasoned veteran against some of the top pass rushers in the country.

His performance against a solid Iowa State front earned him a spot on On3's five true freshmen standouts of Week 7.

Kelvin Banks has manned the left tackle spot for Texas all season and had one of his better games to date in the Longhorns' 24-21 win over Iowa State.
