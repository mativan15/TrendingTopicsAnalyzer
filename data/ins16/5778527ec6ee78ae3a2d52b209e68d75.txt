Published: 17:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 17:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 Eddie Howe believes Cristiano Ronaldo's second stint at Manchester United has been a success as opposed to failure, but says Newcastle United - one of the few clubs who could afford to sign him - would not be interested in the five-time Ballon d'Or winner.The Magpies travel to Old Trafford this afternoon with the Portuguese forward having scored his 700th club career goal in last Sunday's 2-1 win at Everton.Ronaldo netted twice against Newcastle in last season's reverse fixture on what was his second debut for the club.
