National Conservatism has no coherent, workable plan for America
substack.com
news
2022-10-20 02:55:27

In the late 2010s, American conservatism changed. A series of failures and rejections had made the old Reaganite conservatism — which came to prominence in the late 70s — untenable. The financial crisis and Great Recession had soured people on tax cuts and deregulation as a path to prosperity, the Iraq War discredited muscular interventionist neoconservatism, and the secularization of the American populace and the broad acceptance of gay marriage diminished the political power of conservative Christianity.
