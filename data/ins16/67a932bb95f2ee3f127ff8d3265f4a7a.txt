David Cox Promoted to President of JK Moving, Heading Up Day-to-Day Operations of Nation's Largest Privately Held Moving Company

tech
2022-05-27 13:32:49

STERLING, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 27, 2022--JK Moving Services has promoted David Cox to president. Cox will assume responsibility for day-to-day operations of the nation's largest independently owned moving and storage company. Chuck Kuhn will remain CEO of the company he founded 40 years ago.'I am excited to announce the promotion of David Cox to president of JK Moving Services. He is a natural leader and his industry knowledge, focus on technology and innovation, as well as keen business sense have helped our company grow even during the global pandemic,' explained Chuck Kuhn, CEO, JK Moving Services.
