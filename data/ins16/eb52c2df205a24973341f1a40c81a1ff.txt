OLDWICK, N.J., June 03, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has affirmed the Financial Strength Rating of A- (Excellent) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of "a-" (Excellent) of Quanta Insurance Company, Inc. (QIC) (Houston, TX). The outlook of these Credit Ratings (ratings) is stable.

The ratings reflect QIC's balance sheet strength, which AM Best assesses as very strong, as well as its adequate operating performance, neutral business profile and appropriate enterprise risk management.

QIC is a single parent captive of Quanta Services, Inc.
