 A Winnipeg Woman's life hangs in the balance after a car crash early Sunday morning, police say.
At 4:30 a.m. police went to the intersection of Sargent Avenue and Empress Street after multiple collision reports.One of the vehicles was occupied by a 45-year-old man and an 81-year-old woman.
