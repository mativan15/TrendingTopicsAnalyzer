June 03, 2022 05:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cabot Corporation (NYSE: CBT) announced today that Sean Keohane, President and Chief Executive Officer, will present at the Deutsche Bank Global Materials Conference in New York on Thursday, June 9, 2022 at 10:30 am (ET).

A live webcast of the presentation, along with the slides and replay will be available on the investor section of Cabot's website http://investor.cabot-corp.com and at https://kvgo.com/db-global-materials/cabot-june-2022

About Cabot Corporation
Cabot Corporation (NYSE: CBT) is a global specialty chemicals and performance materials company headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts.
