The Congress will get its first non-Gandhi chief in more than two decades as the result of the Congress presidential election will be declared at the party headquarters on Wednesday. The winner between senior leaders Mallikarjun Kharge and Shashi Tharoor will succeed Sonia Gandhi as the party's next president.Congress MP Tharoor, who actively campaigned for the race, has already said there are no ideological differences with Kharge. Tharoor also made it clear that the election was not for any personal gain, but "for the betterment of the party and by extension, the country".
