No. 1 Vols blast Hornets in NCAA opener

2022-06-04 03:19:40

KNOXVILLE — The No. 1 overall seed in the NCAA baseball tournament put on an offensive and defensive show Friday.Jordan Beck hit two of Tennessee's five homers and Blake Tidwell allowed three hits and struck out seven in 7 2/3 innings of a 10-0 victory over Alabama State in the Knoxville Regional.The Vols (54-7) will play Campbell on Saturday in what could be a slugfest. The Camels hit four homers in their 15-8 win over Georgia Tech on Friday and have gone deep in 20 straight games.Winners of nine straight, the Vols extended their home-run record to 146.
