‘Not double-engine, but Gujarat wants…': On Modi turf, Kejriwal seeks a chance
sumanasa.com
news
2022-10-16 17:12:25

Delhi chief minister and Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) national convenor Arvind Kejriwal on Sunday poll-bound Gujarat does not want a ‘double-engine' government, an oft-repeated term by the ruling saffron camp to stress the benefits of having the BJP in power in states as well as at the Centre. He said Gujarat now wants a ‘new engine' government as the double-engine one has become old.Addressing a rally in Bhavnagar city in the poll-bound state, Kejriwal said, 'They say 'double engine ki sarkaar' (a double-engine government).
