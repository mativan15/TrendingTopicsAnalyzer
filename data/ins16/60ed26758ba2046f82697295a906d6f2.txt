Published: 12:00 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 12:06 EDT, 31 May 2022 It's one of the more unusual overnight TikTok sensations: a fish and chips shop in the Midlands.Now staff at Binley Mega Chippy in Coventry are inundated with tourists pouring into the shop from as far afield as Portugal and Australia after a catchy music video was posted on TikTok.Its owner Kamal Gandhi said he had 'no idea' why his store had seen record numbers of customers and has had to order almost double the amount of stock to keep up with demand.
