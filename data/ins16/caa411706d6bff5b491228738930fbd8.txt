May 27, 2022 05:06 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--On May 27, 2022, BNY Mellon Alcentra Global Multi-Strategy Credit Fund, Inc. (Ticker: XALCX) today declared a distribution of $1.75 per share of common stock, payable on June 28, 2022 to shareholders of record at the close of business on June 13, 2022. The ex-dividend date is June 10, 2022. The previous dividend declared in February was $1.75 per share of common stock.

The Fund intends to distribute all or a portion of its net investment income to common shareholders on a quarterly basis.
