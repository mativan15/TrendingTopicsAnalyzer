WEI DONGSHENG/FOR CHINA DAILY The State Administration for Market Regulation issued a document for enforcing market regulation laws on Oct 8, which sets clear, detailed standards for various levels of market regulation bureaus in their law enforcement activities. For businesses, the market regulation bureau is perhaps one of the most frequent go-to departments.
