Tyre industry to need big tech investments to get electric-ready
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 04:45:00

Tyre experts say the biggest spends will be on new materials that will reduce noise, rolling resistance and wear and tear.Chennai: Tyre companies will need to invest 'millions of dollars' in tech spend as India's auto industry shifts towards electric vehicles which have zero noise and vibration, much higher torque and are much heavier than petrol or diesel vehicles. Tyre companies are upping their R&D spend as they get ready to not only tweak existing tyres for EV but also make ground up new products for the born electric lineups being developed right now.
