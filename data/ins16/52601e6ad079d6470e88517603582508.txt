May 30, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tokens.com Corp. (NEO Exchange Canada: COIN) (Frankfurt Stock Exchange: 76M) (OTCQB US: SMURF) ('Tokens.com' or 'the Company'), a publicly-traded company that invests in Web3 crypto assets and businesses linked to the Metaverse and NFTs, is pleased to share that its subsidiary, Hulk Labs, has made several strategic hires and also acquired new gaming assets.



Hulk Labs has added James Hewitt, a P2E gaming expert and Alice Huang, Head of Operations, to the team.
