REUTERS/Anindito MukherjeeBUENOS AIRES, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Appliance maker Whirlpool Corp (WHR.N) is set to inaugurate a new factory in Buenos Aires after a $52 million investment, with plans to export more than 70% of the local production, Joao Carlos Brega, the firm's Latin America chief, said.The plant, which will employ some 400 workers, will produce 300,000 washing machines each year and export $50 million worth of products around Latin America, mainly to Brazil.
