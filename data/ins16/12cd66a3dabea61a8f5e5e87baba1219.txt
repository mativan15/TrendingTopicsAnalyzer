National Open Athletics Championships 2022: Jyothi Yarraji resets 100m hurdles national record for the fourth time this year
olympics.com
news
2022-10-17 16:05:32

Jyothi Yarraji bettered her own women's 100m hurdles national record with a 12.82m run to win the gold medal at the National Open Athletics Championships 2022 in Bengaluru on Monday.This was the fourth-time this year that Jyothi Yarraji has reset India's 100m hurdles national record. After being denied the NR multiple times due to wind assistance and other factors, the Andhra Pradesh-born runner first took possession of the mark in May 2020, clocking 13.23s at the the Cyprus International athletics meet in Limassol.
