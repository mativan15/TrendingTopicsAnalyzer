NewsCity Newsbhubaneswar NewsModest CM keeps people close to heartBhubaneswar: By the time Naveen Patnaik goes to polls in 2024, he would be just 81 days short of setting a new record as Indian's longest serving chief minister, beating Pawan Kumar Chamling, who governed Sikkim for more than 24 years.The 75-year-old Naveen, who will complete three years in office in his fifth successive term as chief minister on Sunday, would be in power for 8,851 days when his current mandate ends in May 2024. Chamling was in power for 8,931 days, between 1994 and 2019.
