Yakshagana timings: Patrons to hold padayatra to temple
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 06:45:00

MANGALURU: Patrons of Kateel Mela in the coastal district have decided to submit a memorandum to chief minister Basavaraj Bommai seeking the relaxation of rules allowing yakshagana performances throughout the night as per traditions.Patrons of Kateel Mela have also decided to seek Kateel temple deity's blessings by holding a padayatra – ‘Ammanedege Namma Nadige'. The century-old Kateel Durgaparameshwari Prasadita Yakshagana Mandali , popularly known as Kateel Mela, has six troupes presenting yakshagana ‘thirugata', where shows begin in the evening and conclude the next morning.
