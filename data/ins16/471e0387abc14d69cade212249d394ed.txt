 A man has been arrested in connection with a shooting at a nightclub in Mississauga, Ont., police say.
Peel Regional Police said on June 5, at around 3 a.m., officers received a report of a shooting at HER Nightclub in the Eglinton Avenue and Creditview area.Police said a shooting occurred after a 'dispute between multiple people.
