AgenciesThe shares were sold in open market sale in the ordinary course of transaction, which is an investment function.Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) has informed that it has sold share worth Rs 700.79 crore of hydro power generator NHPC between March and October this year. After the sale of NHPC shares, LIC's shareholding in the company has come down to 5.2 per cent. The Country's largest insurer said that its shareholding in NHPC has diluted from 72,62,11,315 to 52,26,11,195 equity shares, decreasing the shareholding from 7.
