India's finance ministry seeks industry suggestions on tax changes for FY24 Union Budget
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 15:00:00

AgenciesFinance ministry has asked industry inputs on taxes for federal budgetIndia's finance ministry has asked for suggestions from industry and trade associations regarding the country's direct and indirect taxes, as it kickstarted stakeholder consultation for the federal budget for the next fiscal year. The industry's demands will need to be backed up with justifications, which the finance ministry may include in the budget if they find merit in them, the ministry said. Industry stakeholders must submit suggestions for tweaks in the duty structure, rates and broadening of tax base on both direct and indirect taxes by November 5.
