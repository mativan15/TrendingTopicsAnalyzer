Bollywood's current couple goals are undoubtedly actors Katrina Kaif and Vicky Kaushal. The duo who got married in December 2021 just took over the internet by storm as soon as the news of their wedding broke in the media. Katrina is currently on a spree promoting her upcoming release Phone Bhoot and we wonder why she is all hail about her husband Vicky like never before. SiasatKatrina first discovered Vicky in the trailer for filmmaker Anurag Kashyap's 2018 romantic drama, Manmarziyaan."I remember (producer) Aanand L Rai showing me a promo of Manmarziyaan and I was like, ‘Who is this guy?!' At that point I just found it… Wow! He was so effortless and raw.
