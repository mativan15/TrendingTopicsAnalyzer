Delhi reports 118 new Covid-19 cases, no deaths
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 19:45:00

NEW DELHI: Delhi recorded 118 fresh Covid cases with a positivity rate of 1.77 per cent on Thursday, according to data shared by the health department.No Covid-related fatality was reported in the city. With these new cases, the capital's infection tally has climbed to 20,05,329 while the death toll stands at 26,506, the department said in its latest bulletin.The new cases were detected from 6,652 tests conducted the previous day, it said.Meanwhile, the Delhi government on Thursday issued an order withdrawing the Rs 500 fine for not wearing masks in public places in the national capital.
