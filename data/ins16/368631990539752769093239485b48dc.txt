REUTERS/Mario Anzuoni (UNITED STATES - Tags: BUSINESS)Oct 18 (Reuters) - Daniel Loeb's Third Point has built a stake in toothpaste maker Colgate-Palmolive Co (CL.N), with the activist investor seeing value in a potential spin off of the Hill's Pet Nutrition business, CNBC reported on Tuesday."Hill's could deliver even faster growth and better margins if it were a stand-alone business," Loeb says in a forthcoming investor letter, according to the report which cited sources.
