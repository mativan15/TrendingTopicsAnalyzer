Medical alert systems can be life-saving devices. Fortunately, there are several ways to get free or discounted systems. BSIP/Universal Images Group Medical alert systems can be life-saving devices. With the push of a button, you can get help during a medical emergency, such as by having a dispatcher send an ambulance or notify loved ones. Some systems even have automated features, like fall detection, so that you can get help even when you can't physically call for it. While these systems can be very important, they're not always cheap.
