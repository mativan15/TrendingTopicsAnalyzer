The Indian equity market continued to move northwards for the third consecutive week as investors continued to assess new data on inflation and economic activity. Benchmark indices - BSE Sensex and Nifty50 - gained up to 2 per cent during the week. BSE Midcap index gained more than a per cent, whereas index gauged to smallcap stocks rallied 3 per cent.
