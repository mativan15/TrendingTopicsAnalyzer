"Immediately no": Mental health expert shares red flags on dating profiles
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-02 15:45:38

In a now-viral TikTok video, a mental health expert discussed what would cause her to not match with someone on a dating app.Karli Kucko, LPC-A shared the video to her account @karli.k.counseling where it garnered more than 100,000 views. Her video, which discussed seeing people making demands on their profile, was the first of several that touched on behaviors that would cause her to "swipe left."Viewers found these points helpful, and many shared what they considered to be red flags when they peruse dating apps themselves.
