By Taylor LinzinmeirJune 1, 2022Photo: Franklin County Sheriff's OfficeA federal jury recently found an Ohio man guilty for his involvement in a romance scammer ring, according to NBC 4. Seth Nyamekye, a 39-year-old man from Canal Winchester, scammed from victims on dating websites out of over $1 million, according to the U.S. Attorney General's office. He was convicted of 35 counts of money laundering for acting as a middle man for the scammer ring. According to the U.S. Attorney General's office, the scammer ring would use several fake accounts on dating websites from at least June 2016 to February 2018 to connect with men and women around the country.
