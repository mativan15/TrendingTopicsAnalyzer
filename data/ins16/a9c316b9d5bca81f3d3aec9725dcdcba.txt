New entity will deliver member companies enhanced resources and solutions and new opportunities for growth and expansion

PAOLI, Penn. and CLEARWATER, Fla. --News Direct-- AmeriLife

AmeriLife Group, LLC ('AmeriLife'), a national leader in developing, marketing, and distributing life and health insurance, annuities and retirement planning solutions, announced today that it has entered into a joint venture with AIMCOR Group LLC ('AIMCOR') to launch AIMCOR Consolidated, LLC (d/b/a ICON). Per the agreement, terms of the deal were not disclosed.
