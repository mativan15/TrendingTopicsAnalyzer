Did You Know Pan Nalin sold his home to make 'Chhello Show'?
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-17 14:22:00

'Chhello Show ', India's official entry for the Oscars 2023, was released in Indian cinema on October 14. Pan Nalin's directorial debut is the first Gujarati feature to be selected for the Tribeca Film Festival showcase, and it was also screened at Spain's Valladolid International Film Festival, where it won the top prize, the Golden Spike for Best Film. Pan Nalin revealed in an exclusive interview with a media source that he sold his home to make the film. Pan Nalin began working on the film in 2018 and it was ready in around three years.
