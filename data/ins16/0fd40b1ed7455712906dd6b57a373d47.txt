LUXEMBOURG, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Arrival (NASDAQ: ARVL), inventor of a unique new method of design and production of equitable electric vehicles (EVs) by local Microfactories, today announced that physical type approval testing is now complete for the Van, and Arrival expects to receive official paperwork over the coming weeks for European Whole Vehicle Type Approval (EUWVTA).



"This is another milestone achievement for Arrival following the recent certification of our Bus, and is a testimony to our innovative technologies and our unique new method of design and production of electric vehicles.
