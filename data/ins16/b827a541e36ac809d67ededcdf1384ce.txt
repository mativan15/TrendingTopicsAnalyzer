AgenciesThe iPhone comes with an 8GB storage and 2MP camera. Apple's iPhone, which was launched in 2007, has become one of the most popular smartphones in the world. The iOS device commands a legion of fans worldwide. It all began in 2007 when Steve Jobs introduced the maiden Apple smartphone to the world. Fast forward 2022, an unopened and factory-sealed first generation iPhone has been reportedly sold for over USD 39,000 (about Rs 32 lakh) by LCG Auctions. The Apple iPhone was launched at a price of USD 599 (about Rs 49,719) in 2007.
