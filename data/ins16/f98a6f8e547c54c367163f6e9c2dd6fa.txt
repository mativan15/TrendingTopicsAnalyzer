You're a first-time founder and you've never run a company before. You've already raised significant capital, and it's becoming clear to your board that your current leadership — who gave their blood, sweat, and tears to get you to this point — aren't the same people who will take your company to scale. You need to find a few executives who have done this before. Extensive experience is table stakes, but how will you know someone is right for your company outside of their track record?Of course, there are considerable risks.
