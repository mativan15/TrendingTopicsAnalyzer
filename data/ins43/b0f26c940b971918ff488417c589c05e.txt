(Universal Pictures via AP)Like we needed any additional proof, but the breezy new romantic comedy "Ticket to Paradise" confirms that Julia Roberts and George Clooney still look great in the air, on dry land or out at sea; wearing formalwear, swimsuits and wetsuits; bickering, bantering and burying the hatchet.A sleepless night in a humid jungle cannot defeat Roberts' iconic hair or mess with Clooney's perfectly maintained scruff.
