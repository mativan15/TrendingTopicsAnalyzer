Disgraced surgeon Ian Paterson is pictured (Image: SWNS)A coroner will open a further 13 inquests into the deaths of patients of a butcher surgeon who carried out a raft of unnecessary operations in NHS and private hospitals.Ian Paterson was jailed for 20 years for wounding after a court heard the shameless surgeon betrayed the best interests of his patients, exaggerating and inventing cancer risks. A court heard the 62-year-old man was employed by the Heart of England NHS Foundation Trust and also practised in the independent sector at two private hospitals in West Midlands.
