Oppose imposition of Hindi, support DMK and fight for State's rights: Thirumavalavan tells party MLAs, elected members
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-18 16:34:24

Viduthalai Chiruthaigal Katchi Members of Legislative Assembly and the Member of Parliament on Monday last held a consultative meeting with party president Thol. Thirumavalavan, also an MP, to discuss the issues that need to be raised and addressed in the Assembly and the Parliament. Speaking to The Hindu, Sinthanai Selvan, VCK's floor leader in the Assembly, said Mr. Thirumavalavan urged the members to discuss about the need for the government to use the funds meant to implement various policies for the development of the Scheduled Caste /Scheduled Tribe community, which has not been utilised efficiently in the previous [AIADMK] regime.
