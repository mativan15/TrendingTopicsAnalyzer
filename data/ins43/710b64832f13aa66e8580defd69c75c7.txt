‘John Wick' filmmaker Chad Stahelski to direct ‘Black Samurai' movie for Netflix

2022-10-21 08:01:06

Chad Stahelski and Keanu Reeves
Chad Stahelski, the director of Keanu Reeves-led "John Wick" franchise, will tackle the feature adaptation of "Black Samurai" novel for Netflix.Writer Leigh Dana Jackson will adapt the novel series, penned by famed author Marc Olden, the streaming service said in a press release."Black Samurai" follows an American soldier in Japan who learns the ways of the most powerful martial arts, and becomes The Black Samurai, embarking on a mission to stop dangerous forces who threaten everything he holds dear.
