MISSION, Kan., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- (Family Features) Americans should brace for a potentially cold and wet winter, according to NOAA and this year's Farmer's Almanac Winter Outlook. That means now is a perfect opportunity to think about preparing your family and home for the colder months ahead.
