PM: Will bring back maritime glory of Dholavira & Lothal
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 23:45:00

NEW DELHI: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday slammed the earlier governments for ignoring the rich maritime history of India while highlighting the focus of the current regime to bring back the glory of Dholavira and Lothal in Gujarat.While chairing an online review of the progress of the upcoming National Maritime Heritage Complex (NMHC) at Lothal, Modi said, "We have several rich saga of our history, which were ignored and no effort was made to preserve them and take them to the new generation.
