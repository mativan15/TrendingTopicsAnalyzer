NAXO founders were first-movers in the crypto-investigative space 
NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- NAXO announced its official launch as an elite cybersecurity investigations firm focused on cryptocurrency and other emerging technologies. Co-founded by former law enforcement agents, computer scientists, and security researchers who led the takedown of the notorious "Silk Road" marketplace, NAXO offers the expertise and agility necessary to keep pace with the evolving tactics of cybercriminals.
