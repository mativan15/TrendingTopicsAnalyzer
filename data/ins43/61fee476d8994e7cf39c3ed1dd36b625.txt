Outlook for U.S. Economy Tumbles to 11-Year Low as Fear of Recession Grows, AICPA Survey Finds

2022-06-02 00:00:00

June 02, 2022 07:26 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Inflation worries, a tight labor market and global fallout from the Russo-Ukraine war have dropped business executives' view of U.S. economic prospects to its lowest level since 2011, according to the second-quarter AICPA Economic Outlook Survey. The survey polls chief executive officers, chief financial officers, controllers and other certified public accountants in U.S. companies who hold executive and senior management accounting roles.
