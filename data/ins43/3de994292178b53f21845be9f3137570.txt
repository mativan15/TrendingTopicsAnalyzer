Odisha: 20 illegal cracker units busted in Ganjam, one held
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:30:00

BERHAMPUR: Ahead of Diwali, police on Thursday busted at least 20 illegal firecracker manufacturing units at Nalabanta , known as Sivakasi of Odisha , under Aska police station in Ganjam district and seized huge quantities of crackers , including a truck-load, worth around Rs 45 lakh.While the manufactures managed to escape, the police arrested one Hrudayananda Rout of Tigiria, who was coming to Cuttack to deliver the truck-load of crackers."We have seized huge quantities of different types of crackers from the manufacturing units," said PK Sahoo, inspector-in-charge, Aska police station.
