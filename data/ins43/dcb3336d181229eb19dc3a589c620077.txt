Many hurt in US tech war against China: America's determination will deeply affect China but also companies around the world
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 20:40:36

The US has taken firm action to cripple China's supercomputer and artificial intelligence industry. It has banned the supply of software, machines and semiconductor chips that power the supercomputers running AI applications.
The new US export control rules announced on October 7 also block chip makers worldwide from developing advanced chips for China.
