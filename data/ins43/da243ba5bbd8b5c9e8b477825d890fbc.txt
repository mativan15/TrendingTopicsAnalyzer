October 18, 2022 11:32 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KBRA releases a Day 1 recap of the ABS East 2022 conference held in Miami on October 17-19.

This year's schedule deviated from previous years, with the conference beginning on a Monday afternoon, rather than over the weekend. Day 1 was allocated almost entirely to breakout panel sessions, with topics ranging from CLO, ABS, and RMBS market outlooks, to more asset-specific topics.
