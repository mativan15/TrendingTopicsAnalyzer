Ukraine war: Eurovision trophy sold to buy drones for Ukraine
bbc.co.uk
world
2022-05-30 03:43:21

By Patrick JacksonBBC NewsImage caption, On stage with the trophy in TurinKalush Orchestra, the Ukrainian band which won this year's Eurovision Song Contest, have sold their trophy for $900,000 (£712,000; €838,000) to raise money for the war in Ukraine. The crystal microphone was auctioned on Facebook, with the aim of buying drones for Ukraine's military. The sale coincided with the band's appearance at a charity concert at Berlin's Brandenburg Gate.It aimed to raise money for medical care and supplies.
