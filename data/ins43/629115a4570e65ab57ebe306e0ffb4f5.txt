17 Oct 2022, 06:45 PM ISTIndian equity indices ended higher for a second straight session on Monday, lifted by banking and financial stocks. The S&P BSE Sensex rose 491 points to settle at 58,411, while Nifty50 closed above the 17,300 mark.'On the daily chart, the index formed a piercing line pattern, suggesting a bullish reversal. The trend and momentum remain positive for the short term. Over the short term, the index is likely to go towards 17,500/17,700. On the lower end, support is visible at 17,100,' Rupak De, Senior Technical Analyst at LKP Securities, said.
