Transcard and Sage Intacct share a common goal of helping businesses overcome the barriers to frictionless financial processes CHATTANOOGA, Tenn. (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 SMART Hub is a first-of-its-kind payment portal that digitizes and simplifies the way that businesses make, receive, and manage payments, and access payment insights. The portal also includes a built-in onboarding module that guides Sage customers through the process of setting up the application. 'Transcard is excited to work with the Sage Intacct team to deliver a frictionless, digital payments experience for Sage Intacct customers,' said Transcard CEO Greg Bloh.
