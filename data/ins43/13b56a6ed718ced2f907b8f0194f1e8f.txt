Wages increase: A Macron taboo challenged by inflation
lemonde.fr
news
2022-10-20 09:28:28

Was the French government slow to spot the simmering conflict in oil refineries? Or did it simply follow President Emmanuel Macron's doctrine since 2017, consisting of keeping a distance from wage talks and leaving it to businesses? "Would you find it normal, in a democracy like France, to have the minister of the economy participating in wage negotiations between a private company and unions?" asked Economy Minister Bruno Le Maire, when questioned on this point on Monday, October 17, on RMC. "That's not our role at all," he said.
