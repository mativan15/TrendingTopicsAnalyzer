Uran says farewell at Crown Point State of City event
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-06-01 21:33:17

Mayor David Uran gave his final 2022 State of the City address at the Franciscan Health Pavilion at Bulldog Park Tuesday evening.The mayor spoke before a lively crowd, saying farewell after 14 years as mayor. Uran is leaving to take a new job as Executive Director of the South Shore Convention and Visitors Bureau.Uran credited the Crown Point Police Department for doing a stellar job of protecting the city's almost 34,000 residents, as well as praising the efforts of every other city department from City Court and the Fire Department to the Board of Zoning and Appeals and the Legal Department.
