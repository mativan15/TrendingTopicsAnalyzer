Published: 07:25 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 07:25 EDT, 21 October 2022 The next time a dreaded work meeting comes up, it's worth telling yourself you'll enjoy it.That's because, in a kind of self-fulfilling prophecy, simply expecting a lecture or presentation to be boring will make you feel more bored, a study suggests.Researchers from the University of Hong Kong suggest we may pay less attention to a talk we think will be boring, and this lack of engagement ultimately makes us feel more bored.
