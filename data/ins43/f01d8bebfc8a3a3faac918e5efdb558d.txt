DUBAI, Arab Emirates, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- At theÂ Global Blockchain Convention in Dubai yesterday, Centbee announced that consumers living in the UK can send digital cash via the blockchain to their families back home in West Africa. Co-CEO Lorien Gamaroff described the new feature enabling consumers to send digital cash to a recipient's MTN, Vodafone, Tigo or Airtel mobile money wallet or their bank account. The service will be available for people in the United Kingdom to send money to West Africa, including Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Mali, Niger and Benin.
