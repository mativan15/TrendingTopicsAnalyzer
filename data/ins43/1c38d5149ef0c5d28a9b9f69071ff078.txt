By Justin Taylor

Oct. 21, 2022

Let's get the bad news out of the way first: T.C. Boyle's I WALK BETWEEN THE RAINDROPS: Stories (Ecco, 272 pp., $28.99) is a train wreck of telegraphed punches, labored jokes and liberal sanctimony passing itself off as satire. The turgid, sloppy prose reads as though it was composed with autocomplete enabled.
