Westford, USA, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Initially, fingerprint sensors were only used by certain high-end users. However, with the increasing popularity of mobile devices and the increase in hacking attempts, businesses and individuals have started to adopt this technology more widely. Not only is it more secure than traditional passwords, but it also eliminates the need for you to remember multiple digits! This growth of the global fingerprint sensor market is attributed to increasing demand for secure identification and authentication of individuals as well as increased adoption of mobile payment platforms.
