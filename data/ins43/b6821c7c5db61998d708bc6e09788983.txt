Geodrill Plans to Buy Back Up to 5% of Public Float
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 11:19:00

By Adriano Marchese Canadian exploration drilling company Geodrill Ltd. said on Friday that it intends to repurchase up to 5% of the company's issued and outstanding ordinary shares over the course of one year. Geodrill said that the Toronto Stock Exchange has accepted its notice of intention to renew its normal course issuer bid to purchase up to about 2.3 million shares. At Thursday's closing price of 2.41 Canadian dollars (US$1.92), the total value of the shares that could be repurchased would be worth about C$5.
