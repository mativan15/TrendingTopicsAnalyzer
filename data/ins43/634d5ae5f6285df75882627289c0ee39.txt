Ajsu-P chief slams state
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 05:45:00

Ranchi: Ajsu-P president Sudesh Mahto on Tuesday said the state government lacks vision and commitment for Jharkhand 's pressent and future.Addressing the Kolhan divisional-level workers' meet at Chandil Dam, Mahto slammed the Hemant Soren government for 'not framing any road map for the growth of the state'.'The government has failed to fulfil its promises to people. Basic amenities like education, medicines and justice are out of the reach of the common people,' he added. Ajsu-P has set a target to enrol one lakh active workers and 10 lakh general members in the party, Mahto said.
