Changes to wine industry's return and earn scheme leave frustrated growers fretting about 'big, hairy tax'
abc.net.au
food
2022-10-20 03:33:51

New South Wales wine growers say a plan to include wine bottles in the state's Return and Earn scheme is causing heartache and angst as they face a new "big, hairy tax" on their businesses.The NSW government plans to include wine bottles in the return and earn schemeThe wine industry says it will create an added cost for growersThe NSW Environment Minister says the government welcomes consultation with the industryThe state government has proposed to include more beverage containers between 150mL and 3 litres in the scheme, including glass wine and spirit bottles.
