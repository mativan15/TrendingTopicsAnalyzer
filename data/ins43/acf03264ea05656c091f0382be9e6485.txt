Private equity investments in retail real estate down 63% to 303$ million in Jan-Sep: Report
hindustantimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 07:51:04

Private equity investments in retail real estate space fell 63 per cent to USD 303 million during January-September as investors were concerned about possible impact of high inflation on consumption, according to Knight Frank.The private equity investments in retail stood at USD 817 million in the corresponding period of the last year."Investors avoided the retail sector due to concerns about the potential negative effects a high inflation environment would have on the sector," real estate consultant Knight Frank India said in a report 'Trends in Private Equity Investments in India (9Month 2022).
