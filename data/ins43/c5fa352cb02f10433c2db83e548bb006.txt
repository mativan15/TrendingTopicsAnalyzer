Can ESG pass the bullshitometer?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 05:15:00

Tomorrow marks 50 years since the establishment of the UN Environment Programme (UNEP), essentially the start of the global environmental movement. As climate change assumes priority, norms pertaining to environmental, social and (corporate) governance (ESG) norms are emerging as one of the prerequisites of investing. However, the concept has started facing criticism globally. Companies are rated on their ESG work based on the disclosures they make in their public documents and sustainability reports.
