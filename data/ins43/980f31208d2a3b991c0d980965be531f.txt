WILMINGTON, Del., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Avelo Airlines announced today that it will open the airline's fourth base at Wilmington Airport (ILG) next February. The new base will unlock a new era of affordable, convenient and reliable air service for the Delaware Valley, encompassing portions of four states, including Delaware, Southeastern Pennsylvania / Philadelphia, South Jersey and the northern Eastern Shore of Maryland.
