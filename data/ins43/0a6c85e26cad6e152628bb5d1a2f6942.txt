Getty Images/iStockphotoIn its mission "to reduce the death rate of small businesses," fintech services provider Nav Technologies is partnering with Marcus by Goldman Sachs to provide small business owners "financially smart" lines of credit with fixed rates, no origination or maintenance fees, and a wholly digital experience. Through this launch, Nav, which incorporates an AI and machine learning platform to present small business owners with curated financing options, said it's "expanding its commitment to providing the tools that will make financing simple for entrepreneurs and business owners," according to the company's announcement.
