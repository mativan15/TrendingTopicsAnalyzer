ASX down 0.9 pct at noon, led by tech drop
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-02 02:22:05

The Australian share market is lower across every sector except utilities and energy, with the latter gaining ground despite a drop in oil prices overnight.

The benchmark S&P/ASX200 index had fallen 61.6 points, or 0.85 per cent, to 7,172.4 at noon on Thursday, while the broader All Ordinaries was down 62.1 points, or 0.83 per cent, to 7,400.8.

Woodside Energy was up 4.6 per cent to $31.59 after JP Morgan on Wednesday night handled a huge $1.1 billion block trade in Woodside shares.
