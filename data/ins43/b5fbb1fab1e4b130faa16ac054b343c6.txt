(Shutterstock)ROXBURY TOWNSHIP, NJ — A Morris County man who ran a construction business was indicted Thursday on 12 counts involving fraudulent practices, according to the U.S. Department of Justice. Zeki Donuk concealed checks made payable to his company, instead cashing them and failing to report them to tax officials, authorities said.Donuk, of Landing, operated Titan Builders LLC — later called Titan Steel Construction LLC — according to the indictment.
