Blaze Burns Historic Maple Grove Village Hall​

The Maple Grove Village Hall is located at Lakeview Knolls Park​. It was used for local government from 1939 until 1975. (Google Streetview)MAPLE GROVE, MN — A fire significantly damaged the historic Maple Grove Village Hall Tuesday night.Crews responded to the building at about 9:15 p.m., KARE 11 reported. The fire was found burning at the back building, according to Fox 9.By 10:50 p.m., the fire crews had knocked down the blaze.The origin and cause of the fire are not yet known, but authorities have launched an investigation into the blaze.
