Vancouver, British Columbia--(Newsfile Corp. - October 17, 2022) - Resouro Gold Inc. (TSXV: RAU) ("Resouro" or the "Company") is pleased to announce that Ms. Sandra Evans, CPA, CGA (Alberta) of Grove Corporate Services Ltd. ("Grove") has been appointed as Chief Financial Officer of the Company, subject to approval by the TSX Venture Exchange (the "Exchange"). Ms. Evans has over 30 years of experience in the energy, mining and manufacturing industries, both domestically and internationally. She has held senior accounting roles in several highly successful international exploration companies and brings a hands-on approach to budget management and public reporting.
