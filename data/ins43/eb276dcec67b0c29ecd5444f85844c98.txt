A Tennessee lineman vomited on the field against Alabama, then used the moment to intimidate his opponents

The Tennessee Volunteers gutted out a massive 52-49 upset against the mighty Alabama Crimson Tide to secure their first victory of the rivalry in 16 years.And one Vols player took "gutting it out" to a whole new level.With his team down seven midway through the fourth quarter of Saturday's thriller at Knoxville's Neyland Stadium, Tennessee offensive lineman Jeremiah Crawford casually leaned out of the huddle and vomited on his home turf. But instead of taking the moment to step away from the gridiron or even wipe his mouth, the 6-foot-5, 315-pound senior simply lifted his head and nodded towards his opponents across the line of scrimmage, as if to say, "You don't want to mess with this.
