(Image courtesy: iStock)NEW DELHI: Mobile gaming in India has grown at a healthy rate of 16% in the post-Covid period, riding on the pandemic-induced spike along with the availability of powerful smartphones across the mid-and low-price tiers, according to findings released by CyberMedia Research (CMR) on Wednesday. 'Our gaming intelligence attributes this spike in hardcore and enthusiast gaming to powerful smartphones across the mid and lower price tiers,' said Prabhu Ram, Head-Industry Intelligence Group (IIG), CyberMedia Research.
