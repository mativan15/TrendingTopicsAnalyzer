Road to Retirement: Batten down the hatches
denverpost.com

2022-06-05 12:00:51

Well, here we are on the cusp of another bear market. If the S&P 500 falls a few more percentage points from the time of the writing of this column, we'll be in our fourth bear market in 22 years. Bear markets are generally defined as declines of 20% or more.
We can debate until the cows come home why we are in this situation, what needs to be done to fix it, and when it will get corrected. But nobody knows how this process will proceed and what's in store for markets as the Federal Reserve ramps up its inflation-fighting activity.
