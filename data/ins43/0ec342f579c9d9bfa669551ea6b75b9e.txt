Nissan looking to drive in global products into Indian market
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 10:15:00

Japanese automaker Nissan is looking to drive in its global sports utility vehicles, including X-Trail into the Indian market, as it looks to strengthen its presence in the country, a top company official said on Tuesday. The company, which currently sells models like Magnite and Kicks in the country, is looking to drive in models like X-Trail, Juke and Qashqai into the country. On Tuesday, the automaker announced that it is studying the feasibility of X-Trail and Qashqai for the Indian market. Testing has already begun for the X-Trail and Qashqai in the Indian road conditions.
