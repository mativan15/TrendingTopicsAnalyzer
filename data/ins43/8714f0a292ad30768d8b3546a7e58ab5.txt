Vancouver-based Rock Tech signs lithium supply deal with Mercedes-Benz
theglobeandmail.com
business
2022-10-20 22:29:10

German automaker Mercedes-Benz and German-Canadian company Rock Tech Lithium signed an agreement Thursday for the future supply of lithium hydroxide, the key input material for electric car batteries.Vancouver-based Rock Tech is committed to supplying Mercedes's battery suppliers with 10,000 tonnes of the material annually starting in 2026. Some of the lithium ore will come from Canada. According to the company, the annual supply volume could be sufficient to supply around 150,000 electric vehicles with high-performance batteries.
