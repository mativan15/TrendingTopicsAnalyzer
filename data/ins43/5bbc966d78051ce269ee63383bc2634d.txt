Jimmie Johnson wants to join an elite category on Sunday. His eyes are set on hoisting the Borg Warner Trophy after Sunday's 106th running of the Indianapolis 500. It's a rare opportunity for Johnson to join AJ Foyt and Mario Andretti as the only drivers to win this prestigious event and the Daytona 500.
