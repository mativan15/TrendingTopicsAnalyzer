This is what homes were like in 1952 at the time of the Queen's coronation
metro.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 11:44:00

In 1952 the home was very much the haven of the housewife — women who seldom worked in paid jobs after marriage and took huge pride in their most personal domain: particularly the kitchen. 
By that time, servants had for the most part become a thing of the past, but many middle-class married women had home ‘help' who popped in a few times a week — or even on a daily basis — to assist with cleaning or perhaps the more tedious jobs, such as polishing the silver.
Here, with the assistance of Robert Opie from the Museum of Brands, we take a look at the styles and trends of the time which continue to influence design today.
