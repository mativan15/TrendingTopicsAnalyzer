The neon-blue coin hovered above a lunar landscape, five words etched along its perimeter: 'financial power for the people.'Wealthsimple, a Toronto-based financial services company, circulated the animation in an email to its members in February alongside an exciting announcement: a buzzy cryptocurrency called TerraUSD was officially available to buy through Wealthsimple's trading app.'Earth to crypto explorers: Terra (LUNA) has landed,' the company wrote.Since August 2020, when Wealthsimple launched Canada's first regulated cryptocurrency exchange, this kind of announcement has become routine for the company.
