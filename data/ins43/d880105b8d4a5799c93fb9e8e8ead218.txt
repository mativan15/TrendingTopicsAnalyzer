Trade unionism is a long-term struggle. It is not only the CGT trade union activists, who marched the streets of Paris on Monday, October 17, who are of this opinion. Christian Smalls largely shares this view. He was fired by Amazon in 2020 for having sparked a protest among employees about sanitary conditions in the midst of the Covid-19 wave. Mr. Smalls is a former rapper with an offbeat look who founded the Amazon Labor Union in 2021.
