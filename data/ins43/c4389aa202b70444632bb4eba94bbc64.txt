Image source, SOPA ImagesImage caption, RMT general secretary Mick Lynch on a picket line during a strike this summer.Rail workers in the RMT union are voting on whether to continue strike action for a further six months.The ballot closes on 15 November and is part of an ongoing dispute over pay, job security and working conditions.RMT members at 15 train companies and Network Rail have taken part in eight nationwide strike days since June. No more are currently scheduled. Network Rail is preparing to make a revised offer to unions.
