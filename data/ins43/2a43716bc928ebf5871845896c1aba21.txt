Jets rookie Sauce Gardner celebrated an upset win over the Packers by dancing in a cheese head hat at Lambeau Field

2022-10-17 03:54:22

The New York Jets stunned the Green Bay faithful on Sunday, traveling to Lambeau Field to trounce the Packers 27-10.Once again, it was the Jets' young stars that shined the brightest for New York, with rookie running back Breece Hall going off for 116 yards on the ground and a score and cornerback Sauce Gardner helping to hold Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers to just 210 passing yards.When Hall broke free for a touchdown run, he found some Jets fans sitting near the end zone and got to do his very own Lambeau Leap.
