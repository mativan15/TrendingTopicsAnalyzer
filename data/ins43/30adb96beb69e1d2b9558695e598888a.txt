CAL at COLORADO
Records: Cal 3-2, 1-1 in the Pac-12; Colorado 0-5, 0-2 in Pac-12
Kickoff: 11 a.m. PT Saturday at Folsom Field in Boulder, Colorado
TV: Pac-12 Networks Radio: KGO (810 AM)
Series history: Cal leads 7-4 and has won five of seven meetings since CU joined the Pac-12. The Bears have won the past two meetings, including a 26-3 victory last season at Berkeley.
Cal storylines: The Bears, one of just five Pac-12 teams with no more than a single conference defeat, can remain in the upper division of the conference standings with a victory.
