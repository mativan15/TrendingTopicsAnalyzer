TORONTO (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Interactive Response Technology (IRT) systems have been a mainstay for clinical trials for more than two decades. During that time, trials and systems have advanced at a rapid pace, creating new possibilities for greater supply efficiency. But what do those advancements really mean for sponsors and how to decide where to make changes that will make a difference? In this webinar, hear first-hand accounts from clinical trial supply experts with deep expertise in IRT, forecasting and optimization, and clinical trial management.
