TriBridge Residential sells Optimist Park apartment property for $10.5M
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 18:29:00

TriBridge Residential has sold 300 Optimist Park, a 48-unit multifamily building, for more than $10 million.

Northmarq, a commercial real estate firm out of Minnesota, announced today that its Carolinas investment sales team brokered the $10.55 million sale of the three-story apartment building at 300 Parkwood Ave. on behalf of Tribridge.

The buyer is listed as Optimist Prime LLC, and the sale closed on Oct. 14, according to Mecklenburg County real estate records.

300 Optimist Park delivered in spring 2018.
