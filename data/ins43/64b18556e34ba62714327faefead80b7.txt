Amit Shah meets Sunil Jakhar in Panchkula
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 06:15:00

PTIUnion home minister Amit Shah (File Pic)Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Saturday met Sunil Jakhar, who was recently inducted into the Bharatiya Janata Party, at the latter's residence in Panchkula. Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar and Union Minister Anurag Thakur were also present on the occasion. Sunil Jakhar, whose three generations of his family had served the Congress, left the grand old party last month. This is the first meeting between the Home Minister and Jakhar after the latter's induction into the party on May 19 in the presence of JP Nadda.
