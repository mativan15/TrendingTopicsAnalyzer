Ray Romano Feature Directorial Debut ‘Somewhere in Queens' Acquired By Lionsgate & Roadside Attractions
deadline.com
entertainment
2022-10-18 19:00:00

Lionsgate




Lionsgate and Roadside Attractions have picked up Ray Romano's feature directorial debut Somewhere in Queens, which previously had a world premiere at the Tribeca Film Festival. The three-time Emmy winner also co-wrote, stars and produced the film. Roadside plans to give Somewhere in Queens a theatrical release next year. The pic currently stands at 100% on Rotten Tomatoes.

	In the film, Leo Russo (Romano) lives a simple life in Queens, New York with his wife Angela (Laurie Metcalf), their shy but talented son 'Sticks' (Jacob Ward), and Leo's close-knit network of Italian-American relatives and neighborhood friends.
