Bank credit to NBFCs rises 10%to Rs 10.5 lakh crore in FY22: Report
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 07:45:00

Bank credit to NBFCs grew in double digit in FY22 with outstanding bank credit to them rising by 10.4 per cent to Rs 10.5 lakh crore on the back of improvement in overall economic activities and banks' renewed focus on the NBFC sector following improvement in their balance sheets, says a report. In absolute terms, according to an analysis by , bank credit to non-banking financial companies (NBFCs) expanded by Rs 99,000 crore in FY22, from FY21. The number does not include liquidity given by banks to them through securitization route (direct assignment and pass-through certificates) and also banks' investments in NBFCs' debt instruments.
