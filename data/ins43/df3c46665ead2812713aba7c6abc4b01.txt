Presidential Heating & Air Conditioning, Inc. celebrates 40 years in business. Our mission–honesty, superior service, and quality installation...is why we still serve many of the same customers from our first year in business. GAITHERSBURG, Md. (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 Presidential Heating & Air Conditioning, Inc.—Montgomery County's premier heating and air conditioning company—is celebrating its 40th year in business. A major milestone for this award-winning, family owned and operated, business. 'Our mission has guided our company success—honesty, superior customer service, and the highest quality system installation around,' said Wendell Nixon, Founder and President.
