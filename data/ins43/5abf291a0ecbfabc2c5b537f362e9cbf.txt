Jury begins deliberations in case against 'Steele dossier' 'collector'
go.com
news
2022-10-18 00:37:13

Igor Danchenko is accused of misleading FBI agents about his sources.A federal jury in Alexandria, Virginia, is deliberating the fate of Igor Danchenko, the Russian national accused of lying to federal investigators about information he collected in 2016 for former British intelligence officer Christopher Steele that was compiled in Steele's now-infamous "dossier" detailing Donald Trump's alleged ties to Russia.A Washington-based think tank analyst, Danchenko was hired by Steele to contribute intelligence information to Steele's research.
