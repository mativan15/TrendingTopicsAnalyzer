European Shares Tumble As Bond Yields Rise Again
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-21 11:55:08

(RTTNews) - European stocks tumbled on Friday amid worries about persistent inflation and the Federal Reserve's aggressive rate-hike cycle. U.S. 10-year Treasury yields rose to a new 14-year high of 4.272 percent after Philadelphia Fed President Patrick Harker said that the central bank was not done yet with rate hikes. Germany's 10-year bond yields hit a fresh 11-year high amid expectations that the European Central Bank (ECB) will raise interest rates sharply again next week. The pan European Stoxx 600 was down 1.
