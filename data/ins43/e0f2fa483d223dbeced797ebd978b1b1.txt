A couple have been referred to as 'manipulative' for refusing to serve an all-vegan menu at their wedding - but they only have one vegan guest attending the special dayThe woman didn't think she was in the wrong for not wanting to make her whole wedding vegan-friendly (Stock Image)A couple have been left heartbroken after a family argument was started because of the menu at their wedding. The pair had decided that they were going to have predominantly meaty options on their big day, but the one vegan guest attending was left put out by their choice of chicken and steak, and referred to the bride-to-be as 'controlling' and 'manipulative' for not making the entire day vegan-friendly.
