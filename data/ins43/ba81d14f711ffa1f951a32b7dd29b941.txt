PHOENIX, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Comerica Bank and Iron Mountain Incorporated, the global leader in storage and information management services, will securely shred sensitive paper documents for free as part of Shred Day Phoenix from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. on Saturday, Oct. 29 in the Capitol Mall Area at West Jefferson Avenue and 17th Avenue. The event also will help fight hunger -- St. Mary's Food Bank will be on-site collecting cash donations as well as nonperishable food items.
