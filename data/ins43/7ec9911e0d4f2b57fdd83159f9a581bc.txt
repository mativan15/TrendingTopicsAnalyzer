King Charles 'returns to Balmoral to thank soldiers who guarded over late Queen'
mirror.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 01:41:27

The King has reportedly returned to Balmoral to thank the soldiers in person who guarded over the Queen following her death before her body was taken to London for a state funeralCharles attended a church service at Crathie Kirk on SundayKing Charles has returned to Balmoral so that he could thank the soldiers who guarded over the late Queen at her favourite Scottish retreat, it is reported. As members of the Royal Family were told to head to Balmoral due to the Queen's deteriorating health, Charles was one of only two who managed to get there in time before she died on September 8.
