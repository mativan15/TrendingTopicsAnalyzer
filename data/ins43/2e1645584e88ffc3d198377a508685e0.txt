High Tide Inc. (HITI) Closes Acquisition of Bud Heaven, Adding Two Established Cannabis Retail Stores
marijuanastocks.com
finance
2022-06-01 13:03:47

High Tide Inc. ('High Tide' or the 'Company') (Nasdaq: HITI) (TSXV: HITI) (FSE: 2LYA), a leading retail-focused cannabis company with bricks-and-mortar as well as global e-commerce assets, is pleased to announce that further to its press release dated March 30, 2022, the Company has completed its acquisition (the 'Acquisition') of 100% of the equity interest of Livonit Foods Inc. operating as Bud Heaven ('Bud Heaven') which operates two retail cannabis stores in Ontario (the 'Stores') for CAD$2.8 Million (the 'Transaction').
