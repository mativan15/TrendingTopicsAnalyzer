OKC Blue acquires two 2022 second-round picks from Iowa Wolves for Justin Jaworski
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-21 13:00:43

The OKC Blue continues to wheel and deal under general manager Nazr Mohammed.
The Blue — the Oklahoma City Thunder's G League affiliate — announced on Friday that they have acquired two second-round picks from the Iowa Wolves in the 2022 NBA G League Draft in exchange for the rights of  Justin Jaworski.
The Iowa Wolves are the G League affiliate of the Minnesota Timberwolves.
The Blue received the 41st and 42nd overall picks and will now have five total selections in Saturday's NBA G League Draft, including the fifth, eighth and 66th picks.
