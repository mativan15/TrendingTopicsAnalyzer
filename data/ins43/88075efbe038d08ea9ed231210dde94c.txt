AgenciesShe screamed into our hearts through her first cinematic appearance in Jalwa opposite Naseeruddin Shah and then, she became famous. Living under the flashlights of stardom has been 'an old shirt' so to speak of Archana Puran Singh. Today she is a mother of 2 happy sons and her marriage to Parmeet now runs for three decades. Well, their meeting must have had its share of ups and downs she claims, but their age difference has never been highlighted as an issue post marriage. Many a relative had advised her against tying the knot due to Parmeet being seven years younger at that time.
