Link via Bangladesh, Myanmar to help strengthen IPEF too: S Jaishankar
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 18:15:00

PTIGuwahati: External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar addresses the inauguration of 3rd Natural Allies in Development and Interdependence (NADI) - Asian Confluence River conclave 2022, in Guwahati. External affairs minister S Jaishankar on Saturday said India is looking at enhancing connectivity with Myanmar and Bangladesh which will not only help build a strong partnership with ASEAN countries but also make a difference to the Indo-Pacific Economic Framework (IPEF). Reference to the IPEF comes days after a landmark agreement was signed by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, President Joe Biden and 11 other nations at a summit in Japan.
