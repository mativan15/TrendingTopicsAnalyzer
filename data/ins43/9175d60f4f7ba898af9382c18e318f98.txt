By YANG CHENG in Tianjin | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-20 08:53 A containership is berthed at the zero-carbon smart terminal in Tianjin Port on Oct 13. [Photo/Xinhua] Tianjin Port said, as of Oct 13, its zero-carbon smart terminal in Beijiang port area has handled 1 million twenty-foot equivalent units (TEUs) of containers since the operation began in October last year. "It took less than three years to not only finish construction of the intelligent and green port but also achieve a record container throughput of 1 million TEUs," said Yang Rong, general manager of the No 2 Container Dock Co Ltd, Tianjin Port Group.
