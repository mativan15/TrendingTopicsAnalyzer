iStockRepresentative ImageAfter clarifications by National Restaurant Association of India (NRAI) concerning the service charge issue, hospitality industry association Federation of Hotel & Restaurant Associations of India (FHRAI) has clarified that a service charge, like any other charge collected by an establishment, is a part of the 'invitation' offered by the restaurant to the potential customers. The association said it is for the customers to decide whether they wish to patronise the said restaurant or not.
