D Gukesh: Youngest to beat World Chess Champion Magnus Carlsen
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 06:15:00

Indian Grandmaster Dommaraju Gukesh has defeated Magnus Carlsen in the ninth round of the preliminary phase of the Aimchess Rapid online chess tournament. The 16-year old from Chennai beat the world No.1 with white pieces in 29 moves early on Monday to move up to third position in the tournament. Gukesh became the latest Indian teen to defeat Carlsen - World Chess Champion since 2013 - in recent times. A day earlier 19-year old compatriot Arjun Erigaisi posted a maiden win over the world champion.
