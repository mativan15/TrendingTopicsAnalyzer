Charlotte, N.C., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Akoustis Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: AKTS) ('Akoustis' or the 'Company'), an integrated device manufacturer (IDM) of patented bulk acoustic wave (BAW) high-band RF filters for mobile and other wireless applications, announced today that it is ramping production with Aruba , a Hewlett Packard Enterprise company's new suite of leading Wi-Fi 6E enterprise Access Points (AP).
