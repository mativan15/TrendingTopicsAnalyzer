Published: 07:35 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 09:08 EDT, 17 October 2022 Manchester City feel Liverpool boss Jurgen Klopp 'irresponsibly' inflamed tensions before Sunday's clash at Anfield after trouble broke out.City boss Pep Guardiola was targeted with coins on the touchline while City also allege that their team bus was hit by an object as they left Anfield.The Times report that City were left totally unimpressed by Klopp's remarks in his pre-match press conference, with belief in Manchester that he inflamed animosity ahead of a fixture that has a history of incidents.
