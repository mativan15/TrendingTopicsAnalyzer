BREA, Calif., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- via InvestorWire -- Mullen Automotive, Inc. (NASDAQ: MULN) ('Mullen' or the 'Company'), an emerging electric vehicle ('EV') manufacturer, announces today that David Michery, CEO and chairman of Mullen Automotive, will be featured on Benzinga's All Access Series on Friday, June 3, 2022, at 11 a.m. Eastern time.  Benzinga's All Access show is a hub for discovering the latest trending stocks and investment opportunities and where individual investors get front-row access to live CEO interviews, due diligence presentations and Q&As with company leadership.
