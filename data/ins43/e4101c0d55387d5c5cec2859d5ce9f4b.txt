Covid 19: 14,311 new cases over the past week, 10 Covid-attributed deaths
stuff.co.nz
science
2022-10-17 00:34:01

There have been 14,311 new Covid-19 cases reported across New Zealand in the past week – up from 11,205 last week. The seven-day rolling average of new daily cases is 2041, an increase on last week when it was 1598. As of midnight on Sunday, there were 185 people in hospitals with the virus – two of whom were in an intensive care or high dependency care unit. There had been 10 Covid-attributed deaths over the past week – seven caused by Covid and three that had Covid as a contributing cause.
