Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz lead loved-up couples at Academy Museum Gala
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 10:40:06

Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz were all about the PDA as they hit the red carpet at the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures Gala alongside other celebrity couples last night. 
A-listers including Julia Roberts, Tilda Swinton and Selena Gomez flocked to the swanky event in Los Angeles on Sunday to celebrate those who have made exceptional contributions to cinema from actors to directors and producers. 
Many celebrities arrived coupled up with their other halves, including Brooklyn and Nicola who rocked up in matching all-black outfits.
