So what
This morning, Piper Sandler downgraded the stock on concerns about the economy in general, and about DRAM prices in particular.
DRAM prices are dropping, the analyst warned, and Micron has an "oversized [55%] exposure to mobile, PCs, and other consumer end-markets" that use this form of computer memory.
