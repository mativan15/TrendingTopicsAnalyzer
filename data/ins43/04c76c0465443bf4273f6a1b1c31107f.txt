Live Nation Entertainment Schedules Third Quarter 2022 Earnings Release And Teleconference
prnewswire.com
news
2022-10-20 20:15:00

LOS ANGELES, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Live Nation Entertainment, Inc. (NYSE: LYV), the world's leading live entertainment company, will release its third quarter financial results after market hours on Thursday, November 3, 2022. Michael Rapino, Live Nation Entertainment's President and Chief Executive Officer, will host a teleconference that day at 2:00 p.m. PT (5:00 p.m. ET) to discuss the company's financial performance, operational outlook, and other forward-looking matters.
A live webcast of the call will be accessible from the "News / Events" section of the company's website at investors.
