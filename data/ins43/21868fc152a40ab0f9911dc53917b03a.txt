MarsBarsIt's safe to assume that most investors of REITs are there for the income. This asset class excels at that, as it's incentivized to pay out 90% of its taxable earnings to avoid being taxed at the corporate level. This enables more shareholder returns and it's no surprise that REITs have a higher average yield than that of the broader market. No two REITs are alike, however, as some have higher dividend growth track records than others. One of the common complaints of STAG Industrial (NYSE:STAG) that I see in comment streams is its lack of meaningful dividend growth.
