Quanex Building Raises Full-Year Guidance, Shares Rise 9%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 22:18:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Quanex Building Products Corp. were higher in Thursday's late market, after the company released second-quarter financial results and said it was raising its full-year guidance. At 5:47 p.m. ET, the company's shares were trading 9.55% higher, at $22.72 each. Volume in the late session topped 186,000 shares. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 2.42% gain, closing at $20.74 per share. After the bell, Quanex reported earnings per share of 80 cents on sales of $322.
