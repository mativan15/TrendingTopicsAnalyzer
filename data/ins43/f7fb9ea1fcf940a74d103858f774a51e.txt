indiatimes.com
business
2022-05-31 16:31:33

NEW DELHI: A lobby group including Apple and other technology giants operating in India called out the country's authorities for misunderstanding how patent fees work, following local officials' dispute with Xiaomi Corp.In a letter to government, the India Cellular and Electronics Association urged the Centre to intervene and accused the country's enforcement agencies of a 'lack of understanding' of royalty payments in the technology industry.The anti-money-laundering agency is accusing Xiaomi of moving money out of the country by falsely claiming it was for patent-fee payments.
