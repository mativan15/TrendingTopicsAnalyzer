Original Wordle is dead, long live NYT Wordle
mashable.com
entertainment
2022-06-05 17:30:51

Wordle is dead, long live Wordle. New York Times Games announced via tweet that game creator Josh Wardle's website will no longer host a playable version of Wardle's megahit word-guessing game Wordle, and will now be fully migrating to the New York Times' own page starting June 9, 2022.
Tweet may have been deleted

The clock for the original Wordle link has been ticking since the gaming wordsmiths at the New York Times acquired Wordle for an undisclosed seven-figure amount, promising at the time that Wordle would "initially remain free.
