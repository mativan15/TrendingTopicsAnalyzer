Getty ImagesRetail and banking stocks rose 0.5 per cent and 0.7 per cent, respectively.Shares of Berger Paints (India) Ltd. fell 2.43 per cent to Rs 599.55 at 12:58PM hours (IST) on Wednesday. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up start to the session. As per data available on BSE, total traded quantity on the counter stood at 22,348 shares with a turnover of Rs 1.35 crore till 12:58PM (IST). The stock traded at a price-to-earnings (P/E) multiple of 70.01, while the price-to-book value ratio stood at 17.
