Tippie team wins essay contest at Eller Ethics Case Competition, second place overall
uiowa.edu
news
2022-10-17 18:58:47

Tippie students Paul Eikenberry and Isabelle Wilcox's essay took first place at the 2022 Collegiate Eller Ethics Case Competition. They also won second place overall in the case competition. They will both receive a new laptop for their case competition placement and $100 each for the essay contest win. 
Gabrielle Kilpatrick, Isabelle Wilcox, and Paul Eikenberry after their win. The team of Tippie students—Eikenberry, Wilcox, and Gabrielle Kilpatrick, who served as an alternate and provided important research support to the team—traveled to Tucson, Ariz.
