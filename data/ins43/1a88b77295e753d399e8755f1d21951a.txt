NEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- REcolorado, one of the largest MLSs in the nation, and Perchwell, the company behind the premier data and workflow technology platform in residential real estate, announced plans to roll out Perchwell's flagship MLS technology platform to REcolorado's 27,000+ subscribers in 2023.

Perchwell is the premier data and workflow platform for the residential real estate industry. (PRNewsfoto/Perchwell)

Perchwell is an end-to-end technology solution with web and mobile apps that power real estate agents' core workflows.
