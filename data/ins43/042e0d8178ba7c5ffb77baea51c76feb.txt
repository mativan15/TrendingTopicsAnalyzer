Xi's Vow on Wealth Regulation Ignites Calls for Taxing the Rich
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-20 03:40:47

(Bloomberg) -- Chinese President Xi Jinping's pledge to regulate wealth has stirred speculation Beijing may impose property and inheritance taxes on the wealthy in pursuit of its 'common prosperity' goal. Several government-linked economists have highlighted the possibility of major tax reform in recent days. Liu Yuanchun, the president of the Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, advocated this week for the promotion of real estate taxes. Su Hainan, a researcher at the China Association for Labor Studies, suggested capital gains should be taxed properly given how the 'overly fast amassing of wealth by a few people' has triggered 'discussions in society.
