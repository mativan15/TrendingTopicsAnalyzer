Germany will pay the price for its bizarre love affair with dictatorships
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 09:00:00

Despite apparently viewing itself as a bastion of the free world, Germany is having a hard time ending its love affair with dictatorships. We have all noticed its ambivalence toward the war in Ukraine, even if it has ultimately been dragged kicking and screaming along the path of righteousness by Nato. But Berlin is still cleaving to its other bad love: the malignant regime that rules in Beijing.
When it took office, Germany's current coalition officially agreed to harden the line on Beijing. But since then, Chancellor Olaf Scholz seems to have done everything in his power to undermine that stance.
