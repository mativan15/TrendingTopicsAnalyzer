October 20, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time HOBOKEN, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NICE (Nasdaq: NICE) today announced that NICE has received the highest scores for Digital Customer Service Center, the newest use case included in the 2022 Gartner Critical Capabilities for Contact Center Service research report. Giving this year's digital addition context, the Gartner report says, 'We have also added a digital customer service center use case to address the growing need for managing digital channels as a primary interface.
