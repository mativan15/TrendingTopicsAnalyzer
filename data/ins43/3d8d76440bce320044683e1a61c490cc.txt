One of Australia's most-celebrated authors has decided to give away $50,000 in prize money instead of spending the cash on "incontinence pads".Mr Keneally said the award was confirmation that people believed he was "still a novelist"He has won countless awards including the Man Booker Prize and the Miles Franklin AwardHis latest novel Corporal Hitler's Pistol is set in his hometown of KempseyThomas Keneally turned 87 this month and has enjoyed a decorated writing career as the author of Schindler's Ark — the book adapted to Steven Spielberg's film Schindler's List — along with many other award-winning novels.
