A scathing report by the House of Commons Justice Committee say there is 'inadequate' focus from police forces as cases skyrocket, and called on companies to face prosecution if they fail to prevent fraudExperts believe around 4.6 million people fall victim to fraud each year (file image)Hundreds of thousands of fraud victims are being failed every year as the justice system fails to deal with an "epidemic" of crime, a scathing report warns today. MPs say that although fraud accounts for 40% of crimes, just 2% of police funding is dedicated to tackling it, while a national reporting centre has proven itself "unfit for purpose".
