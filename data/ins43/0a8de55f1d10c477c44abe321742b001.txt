Outmin raises €1.5m in seed funding round
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-20 13:03:40

Outmin, a real-time accounting solution for SMEs, has raised €1.5 million in a seed funding round that will support the company's expansion.The round was led by Middlegame Ventures with participation by Fuel Ventures, KellySan Enterprises Ltd and several strategic angel investors. The money will be used to further develop its technology that aims to save clients time and money on their financial admin, and minimise its distraction.The company plans to open a UK office and will double its headcount, currently at 28, in the next 12 months.
