FOXBOROUGH — Patriots coach Bill Belichick was still mum about whether Mac Jones will be healthy enough to return to the field Monday night against the Chicago Bears and whether the quarterback would start if available.Jones, who suffered a high ankle sprain on the Patriots' final offensive play in Week 3 against Baltimore, was a limited participant in Friday's practice. But he said his recovery is coming along well.'I think it feels pretty good,' Jones said. 'Just trying to work through all the stuff to be able to play an NFL football game.
