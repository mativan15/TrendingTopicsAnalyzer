LABEL PROFILE Carl Craig Celebrates Thirty Years on Planet E By Andrew Parks · May 27, 2022 When Carl Craig first told Derrick May he was going to launch his own label in the early ‘90s, the Detroit techno don got straight to the point. 'Derrick told me, ‘Don't do it,'' Craig says. 'I took it as tough love. But just because somebody tells you that you shouldn't do something doesn't mean that they are correct. Even though Derrick had his finger on the pulse of a lot of stuff, I didn't agree with his angle.
