Intel's Mobileye IPO Looks Desperate and Needy
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-18 14:56:38

Going public in the middle of a market meltdown, at a deep discount to recent valuations, sends bad signals to investors

By

The tech-heavy Nasqaq Composite has lost a third of its value, public-offering volume has plummeted over 90%, and the majority of last year's IPO crop are underwater. All the signs are there for any right-minded chief executive to hold off on a share sale. Yet Intel Corp. is bringing Mobileye Global Inc. to market, making CEO Pat Gelsinger look desperate.

Five years after buying and delisting the company for $15.
