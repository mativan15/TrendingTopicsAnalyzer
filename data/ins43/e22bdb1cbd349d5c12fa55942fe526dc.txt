Faraday Future interim CFO Becky Roof resigns
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 21:45:00

Faraday Future Intelligent Electric Inc. said its interim chief financial officer, Becky Roof, is resigning after helping the company complete key filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission and fundraising milestones.

Gardena, California-based Faraday Future (Nasdaq: FFIE) said Roof has agreed to assist the electric vehicle startup ensure an orderly transition.

Faraday Future hired Roof and AP Services LLC, an affiliate of AlixPartners LLP, in March to strengthen its financial team, help the company return to timely filing status with the SEC and lead its fundraising process.
