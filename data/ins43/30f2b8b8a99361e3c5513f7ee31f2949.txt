DAVIDSON, N.C., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Curtiss-Wright Corporation (NYSE: CW) today announced that Lynn M. Bamford, Chair and Chief Executive Officer, and K. Christopher Farkas, Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, will participate in the Stifel 2022 Cross Sector Insight Conference. A fireside chat discussion and simultaneous webcast will take place on Wednesday, June 8, 2022, at 9:10 am ET. There will be no formal presentation at this event.
