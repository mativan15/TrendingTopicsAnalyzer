Plane crazy: Star's bowling balls are lost on flight before international debut
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-02 23:01:00

The player-manager of Ireland's international bowling team faces the prospect of playing at the European Championships today without his custom-made balls after his luggage was misplaced on the journey.Anthony Byrne was hoping to lead his squad to the perfect strike, but upon landing in Frankfurt airport on Monday he realised he had made the journey without some important items.Byrne, a 32-year-old engineer from Coolock in Dublin, is competing at a major bowling championship with the Ireland team for the first time.
