I wrote a column recently about the Nigerian Letter Scam in which I noted that actual losses to it are low, but that the same crooks perpetrate far more lucrative scams. The FBI and partner agencies across the globe have announced the results of their latest crackdown on one of the most financially devastating crimes.

Business Email Compromise (BEC) scams usually target finance and human resources employees in companies that work with foreign suppliers and/or regularly send wire transfers. The crooks impersonate high-level executives, business partners, or vendors and convince the employees to send them money via wire transfer or check or to divulge confidential employee information such as employee tax records.
