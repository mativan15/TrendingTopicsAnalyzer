The Peripheral is Prime Video's new sci-fi drama that is based on the novel of the same name by William Gibson. The series takes place between the years of 2032 to 2099. It stars Chloë Grace Moretz as Flynne Fisher and Jack Reynor as her Marine veteran brother, Burton. The show revolves around the two unknowingly getting involved in dangerous crimes through simulation games that are way too real. On a quest to earn money, the perils of technology makes them realize that there is too much on the line to lose.
