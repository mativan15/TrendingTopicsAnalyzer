The Scripps National Spelling Bee finals are here.
nytimes.com
news
2022-06-02 19:17:32

Followers of the Scripps National Spelling Bee know that the champion or co-champions win a $50,000 cash prize.But other spellers, from participants to the national finalists, don't leave the competition empty-handed either, receiving a variety of awards and prizes from the Bee and its sponsors.In addition to the $50,000 prize, the top speller takes home a commemorative medal and the Scripps Cup, the event's official trophy. Merriam-Webster, the Bee's dictionary partner, also presents the winner a $2,500 cash prize.
