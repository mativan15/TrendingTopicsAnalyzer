Google Pixel 7 Pro Review: The new flagship takes the Pixel further than before
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-10-21 22:46:52

Tech giant Google has released its latest series of Pixel smartphones with the Pixel 7 and Pixel 7 Pro, and while flagships have been part of the Pixel range since its inception, it only feels like they hit their stride last year with the Pixel 6 Pro. The Pixel 6 Pro finally offered a more premium experience for users looking for a higher resolution screen, a bigger device, more RAM and higher battery capacity among a few other upgrades. The Pixel 7 Pro is the latest and greatest in their flagship line that brings the new advancements of the Pixel 7 to a high-end, flagship model, offering that larger frame and beefed-up hardware.
