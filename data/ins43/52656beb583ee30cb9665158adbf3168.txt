Chess player at center of St. Louis cheating scandal files defamation suit
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 18:40:00

The chess player who says he was accused of cheating at a St. Louis tournament has filed a defamation suit against his opponent and a pair of companies, seeking at least $100 million.

The allegations against 19-year-old Hans Niemann created what the Washington Post described as 'a storm in the chess world' and come as St. Louis in recent years has become a hub for competitive chess.
