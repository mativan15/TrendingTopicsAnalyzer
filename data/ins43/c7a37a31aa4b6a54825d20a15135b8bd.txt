With recent price increases, loan interest rate hikes and rising oil prices, the average payment for owning a car has swelled. Major carmakers in India like , Kia, , MG Motor, Hyundai and Toyota have already increased the prices of cars twice this year, with many companies also delaying the delivery of their vehicles by several months due to the rise in demand.
