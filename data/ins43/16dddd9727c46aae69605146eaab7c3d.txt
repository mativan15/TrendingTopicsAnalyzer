Dublin, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Live Cell Imaging Market - Global Outlook & Forecast Market 2022-2027" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.



Live cell imaging is one of the popular techniques for studying live cells and investigating the biological processes in real-time. It utilizes time-lapse microscopy and careful preparation of living cell environments that have made it easier to observe cell-to-cell interactions and study the behavior of single cells and related changes within the cell.
