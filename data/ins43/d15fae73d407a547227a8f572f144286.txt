By Reuters Published: 23:24 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 23:24 EDT, 20 October 2022 Brennan Armstrong became Virginia's career passing touchdowns leader and he ran for another score to lead the Cavaliers to a 16-9 victory over Georgia Tech on Thursday in Atlanta. Armstrong, who completed 20 of 35 passes for 255 yards, threw his school-record 57th career touchdown pass with a 44-yard strike to Dontayvion Wicks midway through the second quarter. The score gave Virginia (3-4, 1-3 ACC) a 13-6 lead it wouldn't relinquish.
