By Reuters Published: 00:39 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 00:39 EDT, 21 October 2022 By Dharamraj Dhutia MUMBAI, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Indian government bond yields were higher for a fourth straight session on Friday, tracking a fierce upward trajectory in U.S. yields, while additional supply via the weekly debt auction further weighed on sentiment. The benchmark Indian 10-year government bond yield was at 7.5121% as of 0435 GMT. The yield had ended at 7.4767% on Thursday. It has risen by seven basis points in three sessions.
