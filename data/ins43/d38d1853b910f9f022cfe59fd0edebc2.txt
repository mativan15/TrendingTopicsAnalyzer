The mind is a powerful thing. Yet sometimes, it needs some support, especially when trying to make sense of difficult concepts, struggling through creator's block or managing complex projects. Mind mapping is the perfect solution. Mind mapping is a process involving mapping the connections between core concepts. For example, each map starts with one central idea node, such as a new project, new service, idea or problem. Secondary idea or concept nodes are then linked to the central node using a maplike structure.
