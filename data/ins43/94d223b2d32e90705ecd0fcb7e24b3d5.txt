Deputies: Foul play not suspected in death of man found near Deltona trail
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-01 19:43:04

Volusia County Sheriff's deputies said they are working to determine how a man ended up dead near a trail in Deltona over the weekend.

Investigators said they do not suspect foul play in the death of Nathaniel Moss, 36, of Deltona, whose body was found near DeBary and Jacob Brock avenues on Monday.

Moss's body was discovered at 11:07 a.m. by a Titusville couple who were riding their bicycles on the East Central Regional Rail Trail, according to a Volusia County Sheriff's Office report.
