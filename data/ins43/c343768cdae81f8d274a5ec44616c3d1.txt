United Nations Communications Vital More than Ever in Face of Multiple Crises, Fourth Committee Hears, as Speakers Debate Information
un.org
news
2022-10-20 01:29:42

The work of the Department of Global Communications is as critical as ever as the international community grapples with the manifold challenges of a global pandemic, the negative impacts of climate change and a resurgence of conflict, the Fourth Committee (Special Political and Decolonization) heard today as it took up questions relating to information. Those 'three Cs' — namely, COVID‑19, climate and conflict – have been at the heart of the United Nations' communications efforts this year, Melissa Fleming, Under-Secretary-General for Global Communications, told the Committee.
