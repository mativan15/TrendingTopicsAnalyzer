Trai defers implementation of broadcast regulations again
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 15:00:00

AgenciesThose who already had RIOs in line with NTO 2.0 can update and submit new ones by August 31st.The Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (Trai) has once again extended the deadline for the implementation of the new broadcast sector tariff regulations —commonly known as NTO 2.0 — by almost six months, to November 30th. The new channel and bouquet pricing for consumers will now take effect from November 30th, instead of the earlier announced June 1st, according to the revised schedules. The move is in line with the ongoing consultation process on the issues related to the new regulatory framework for broadcasting and cable services, for which stakeholders have been asked to submit comments and counter-comments by June 6th and June 13th, respectively.
