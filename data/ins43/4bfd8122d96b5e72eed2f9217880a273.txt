IN10 Media taps former Sony executive Vivek Krishnani to foray into film production business
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 06:45:00

Prior to this, Krishnani served as MD of Sony Pictures Films India and was instrumental in building the local films slate for production, acquisition, and distribution as well as overseeing revenue maximisation of the Hollywood releases.Anand Mahindra and Aditya Pittie-backed IN10 Media Network is foraying into film production business and has roped in Vivek Krishnani as the chief executive for the soon-to-be-launched division. Krishnani comes with over 25 years of experience across films, television, radio, and advertising.
