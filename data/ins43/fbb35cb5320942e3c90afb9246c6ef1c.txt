UN Rights Chief Urges End to Deadly Crackdown on Chad Protesters

Voice of America 22nd October 2022, 05:06 GMT+11Geneva - The U.N.'s chief human rights official, Volker Tuerk, is calling for an end to the use of lethal force by Chadian security forces who reportedly killed and wounded dozens of protesters Thursday.Violent demonstrations broke out in several cities Thursday, protesting the decision by Chad's military government to delay by two years the handover of power to civilian rule.Chadian authorities report some 50 people have been killed and nearly 300 injured in clashes between security forces and protesters.
