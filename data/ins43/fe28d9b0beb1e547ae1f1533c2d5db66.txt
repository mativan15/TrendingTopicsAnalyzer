June 02, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time LITTLE ROCK, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Buddy Bayer, president of the Windstream Enterprise and Wholesale organization, today announced that Michael Fiacco, formerly senior vice president for the east region and financial services vertical at Lumen Technologies, has been named chief revenue officer for Windstream Enterprise in order to further enhance its commitment to technological innovation and superior customer service.

'Our customers' needs are evolving as the market accelerates its digital transformation, and Windstream Enterprise and Wholesale is evolving alongside them to ensure that we are delivering the leading-edge products and services they need to succeed,' Bayer said.
