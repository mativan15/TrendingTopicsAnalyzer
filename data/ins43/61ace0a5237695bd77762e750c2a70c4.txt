1. Nepotism Exists In All Industries, Not Just Bollywood: Ananya Panday Without hesitating to accept being a product of nepotism, Ananya Panday said, "The fact that I can meet a Karan Johar is something that is available to me because of my parents. But I also believe you can have access but what you do in that room is purely dependent on your talent. If you have access and don't have the talent to back it up, people won't invest their money in you. Having said that, I do believe nepotism exists and it exists in all industries, not just Bollywood.
