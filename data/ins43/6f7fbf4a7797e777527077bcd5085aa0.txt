Yoon says global challenges should be overcome with Olympic spirit
naver.com
news
2022-10-19 14:16:00

President Yoon Suk-yeol (Yonhap)President Yoon Suk-yeol said on Wednesday that the spirit of freedom and solidarity pursued by the Korean government is no different from the spirit of the Olympics, adding that global challenges should be overcome with the Olympic spirit.'We will do our best to bring the spirit of freedom and solidarity into the overall sports field and do our best to contribute to the international community through sports,' Yoon said in a keynote speech at the 26th General Assembly of the Association of National Olympic Committees in Seoul.
