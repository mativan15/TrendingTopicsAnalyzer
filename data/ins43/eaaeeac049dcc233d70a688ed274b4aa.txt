A major Army training exercise was conducted by various units and formations of Ambala-based Kharga Corps in the Mahajan Area from October 2 to 18. During the exercise, the validation of latest operational concepts such as attack helicopters as maneuvering arm of ground forces, deep interdiction across adversary's obstacle systems, fighting in semi-developed terrain, use of hi-tech drones for tactical ISR (intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance) as force multipliers, sustained operations in desert terrain by use of polymer track ways and heliborne operations were conducted successfully.
