David Lammy says Tories have made UK economy 'an international punchline'
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 16:05:50

Labour has claimed that Tory policies – and their failure to deliver them – has made the UK economy a joke on the world stage.
It comes after US President Joe Biden spoke publicly about his view that the ‘mini' budget delivered by the new government was a ‘mistake'. 
Shadow foreign secretary David Lammy said: ‘As well as crashing the economy, Liz Truss's humiliating U-turns have made Britain's economy an international punchline.
‘President Biden knows the dangerous folly of trickle-down economics.
