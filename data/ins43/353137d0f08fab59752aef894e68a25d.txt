Prime Minister Narendra Modi is expected to be the chief guest at a Ram Leela celebration in Ayodhya, which will feature a special Russsian version of the epic to be performed by a team from Moscow. Government sources have confirmed that Mr. Modi will be in Ayodhya on October 23 where he is slated to perform pooja and other rituals of the deity Ram Lalla Virajman, and also inspect the Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra site where the Ram Janmabhoomi temple is coming up.
