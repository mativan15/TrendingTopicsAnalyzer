Published on Oct 19, 2022 10:59 AM IST China is poaching former UK military pilots to train its PLA personnel. According to a BBC report, up to 30 ex-pilots are believed to be training the Chinese PLA. The report also suggested that Beijing is giving huge amounts of money to former UK military pilots to pass on their expertise on how western aircraft and pilots operate. The UK Defence Ministry issued a warning to its former military pilots working against the Chinese. Watch this video to know more.
