How to Hold Foreign Corporate Insiders Accountable
upenn.edu
news
2022-05-31 17:30:00

A research paper on insider trading by experts at Wharton and New York University has set the stage for a bill that seeks to reform disclosure requirements by insiders at foreign firms listed in the U.S. The paper, titled 'Holding Foreign Insiders Accountable,' published in April, prompted Sen. John Kennedy (R-La.) in early May to push for a law with the same title. A loophole in the law allowed insiders at foreign firms listed in the U.S., especially those from China and Russia, to avoid losses totaling $11.
