Portland, OR, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to the report published by Allied Market Research, the global pipeline monitoring systems market generated $13.86 billion in 2020, and is estimated to reach $29.11 billion by 2030, witnessing a CAGR of 7.6% from 2021 to 2030. The report offers a detailed analysis of changing market trends, top segments, key investment pockets, value chain, regional landscape, and competitive scenario. Rise in leakage problems, increase in use of petroleum products, and surge in oil & gas plants drive the growth of the global pipeline monitoring systems market.
