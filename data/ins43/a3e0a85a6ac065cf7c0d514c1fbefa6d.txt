Why food inflation may have some way to go
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-28 23:01:00

Food prices are the polar opposite of stock prices. Nobody really pays much attention to shares when they rise, but when they fall it's TV news. Equally, Morning Ireland has never headlined on falling food prices.Yet if one drills down into the Central Statistics Office (CSO) consumer price index for April, the vast majority of food stuffs are still below December 2016 prices, despite recent increases.The single biggest food price increase on the CSO index, by a very large margin, is not flour, bread or pasta.
