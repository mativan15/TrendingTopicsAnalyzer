June 03, 2022 04:38 PM Eastern Daylight Time GRAND RAPIDS, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Northern Trust has appointed Alison Mast Senior Managing Director and President of Western Michigan. Mast will be responsible for leading Northern Trust's Grand Rapids-based team in the delivery of holistic advice and outstanding client service, while seeking to accelerate the growth of the firm's wealth management business in the Western Michigan Region.



Alison joined Northern Trust in 2019 as the Head of Strategy for Wealth Management, working closely with practice and market leadership to identify, prioritize and implement strategic initiatives across the business.
