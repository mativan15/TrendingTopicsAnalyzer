3 ‘tech-savvy' men helped retd manager steal from co-op bank: Lucknow Police
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 18:27:21

LUCKNOW: Apart from a retired manager, three ‘tech-savvy' youngsters are also suspected to have hacked into the server of Uttar Pradesh Cooperative Bank (UPCB) and tried to steal ₹146 crore from the bank on Saturday. The police's cybercrime team has gained access to the CCTV footage from bank headquarters in Hazratganj, in which the three men could be seen. The retired bank manager, RS Dubey, and one of his associates, Ganga Sagar Chauhan, were already in police custody and being quizzed about their involvement in the crime.
