Meta documents show main metaverse is losing users and falling short of goals, report says
msn.com
tech
2022-10-15 17:00:00

Horizon Worlds, Meta's flagship metaverse for consumers, is failing to meet internal performance expectations, according to internal documents reviewed by The Wall Street Journal. Meta initially aimed to reach 500,000 monthly active users in Horizon Worlds by the end of the year, but the current figure is less than 200,000, according to the report.
