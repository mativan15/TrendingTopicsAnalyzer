Alexis Bledel has shockingly announced her departure from The Handmaid's Tale, which has begun filming its fifth season'After much thought, I felt I had to step away from The Handmaid's Tale at this time,' the 40-year-old actress told Variety Adapted from a 1985 novel by Margaret Atwood, the show takes place in a patriarchal dystopia called Gilead founded on what used to be the United States 'Handmaids' are the women forced under veils and subjected to a form of slavery in which they are repeatedly raped for the purpose of producing children Alexis plays Emily Malek, who was a Handmaid under the name of Ofglen until she escaped to Canada and who now works for the Mayday resistance The show has passed up the events of the Margaret Atwood novel, so the writers have a level of freedom to shape the plot around Alexis' departureBy Sameer Suri For Dailymail.
