Kevin McCarthy Sounds a Whole Lot Like the the Far-Right Wing of the GOP on Ukraine
vanityfair.com
news
2022-10-20 16:06:28

The Biden administration is concerned about what a Republican majority could mean for the war in Ukraine — and for good reason. Speaking to Punchbowl News this week, Kevin McCarthy suggested his party could hold up aid to the embattled country should Republicans take back the House, warning that the GOP would be disinclined to 'write a blank check to Ukraine.''They just won't do it,' the minority leader told the outlet, citing the economy and 'things [the administration] is not doing domestically' as potential reasons Republicans could hold up military aid.
