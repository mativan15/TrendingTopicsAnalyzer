IT'S not often that The Chase's Bradley Walsh is one-upped with a good joke, but one of Monday night's contestants left him howling with laughter with his cheeky quip. 
On the latest episode of the game show favourite, four new hopefuls prepared to face off against Anne 'The Governness' Hegerty. 
Bradley found himself cackling over Guy's cheeky jokeCredit: ITV
Guy said he intended to go skiing - and 'might bring the wife and kids' tooCredit: ITV
Electrician Guy was the third up to the plate, and when Bradley asked what he planned to do with the prize money should the team win, he revealed he intended to use it to go on a skiing holiday.
