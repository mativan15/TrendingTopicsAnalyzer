The Embassy of Japan, New Delhi, has filed an application seeking Geographical Indication (GI) tag for nihonshu/Japanese sake, an alcoholic beverage. It is learnt that this is the first time a product from Japan has filed for a tag at the Geographical Indication Registry here.According to details provided in the filing, in Japan, nihonshu is regarded as a special and valuable beverage made from fermenting rice.
