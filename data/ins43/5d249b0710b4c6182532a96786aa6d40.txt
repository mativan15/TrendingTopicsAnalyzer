New York, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Bit Origin Limited (NASDAQ: BTOG) ("Bit Origin" or the "Company"), an emerging growth company engaged in crypto mining business with diversified expansion strategies, today announced the pricing of an underwritten public offering of 9,803,922 ordinary shares, at an offering price of $0.51 per ordinary share. The gross proceeds to the Company are expected to be approximately $5 million, before underwriting discounts and commissions, along with other estimated offering expenses.
