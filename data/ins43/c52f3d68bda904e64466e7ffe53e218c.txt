Pensacola businessman alleged to be 'leader' in HIV drug counterfeit ring, lawsuit says
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-18 14:34:57

A Pensacola businessman was named as a defendant in a sprawling lawsuit seeking to halt an alleged expansive criminal HIV drug counterfeiting network.

Last month, Gilead Sciences named John Levitan, along with more than 50 other defendants, in a lawsuit that alleges that Levitan was a "leader" in a network that sold more than $230 million in counterfeit HIV drugs.
