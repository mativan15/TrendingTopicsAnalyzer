October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUGUSTA, Ga.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Conceal, the leader in Zero Trust isolation and ransomware prevention technology, today announced it is expanding into the Australian and New Zealand markets through a strategic partnership with Virtual Graffiti, a BlueAlly Company and leading provider of IT solutions focused on IT security, network, storage and cloud.



'We've partnered with Conceal because traditional endpoint defense solutions like antivirus are not enough,' said Thad Legg, General Manager at Virtual Graffiti.
