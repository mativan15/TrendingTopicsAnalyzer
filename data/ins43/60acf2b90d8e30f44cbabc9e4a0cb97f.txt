Friday night's game between the Blue Jays and Angels -- a matchup featuring Los Angeles' Shohei Ohtani and Toronto's Vladimir Guerrero Jr., the top two finishers in 2021 AL MVP voting -- will air exclusively on Apple TV+. It will not be available on your local cable provider or MLB.TV.
First pitch is scheduled for 9:38 p.m. ET/6:38 p.m. PT.
For more information about how to access "Friday Night Baseball" on Apple TV+, visit the Apple Knowledge Base.
