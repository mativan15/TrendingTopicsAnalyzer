May 31, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time COXSACKIE, N.Y.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The National Bank of Coxsackie announced today that Angela (Fereshteh) Osier joined National Bank of Coxsackie as its newest Business Development Officer in late April of this year.



Osier comes to National Bank of Coxsackie with over 10 years of experience in the Banking Industry. She joins NBC from Berkshire Bank, where she was a Branch Manager in the Malta area. Her experience prior to Berkshire Bank includes positions at Key Bank and Citizens Bank.
