Exclusive: Ryan had been paying too much council tax for four years after it turned out his home was placed in too high of a banding - here, he explains how to challenge your bandIt is possible to challenge you're council tax if you think you're in the wrong band A dad has explained how he won back £910 in council tax by challenging his banding. Ryan, 34, who lives in Manchester, had been paying too much council tax for over four years. His property was classed as Band B but after he challenged this, it was moved down to Band A.
