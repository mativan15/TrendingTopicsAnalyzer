Vancouver, British Columbia--(Newsfile Corp. - October 20, 2022) - PreveCeutical Medical Inc. (CSE: PREV) (OTCQB: PRVCF) (FSE: 18H) (the "Company" or "PreveCeutical") , announces the appointment of Ms. Kathleen Rotika ("Kathy") to the Company's board of directors (the "Board").

Kathy is a CPA and Principal of Somerset CPA's P.C. She is a finance, operations, and strategy-focused executive with extensive experience with large medical groups. Her experience includes financial performance improvement, business development, operational process improvement, information reporting/analytics, treasury management, budgeting and payor contracting/relations.
