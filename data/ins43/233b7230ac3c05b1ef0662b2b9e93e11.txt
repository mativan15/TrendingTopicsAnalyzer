Nowadays, the market for cross-platform app development is growing as we speak. Because cross-platform applications are more effective at reaching a wider audience than single-platform apps, as well as taking less time and money to build, they are less expensive. At present, many cross-platform frameworks are available on the market and all of them offer their own features and benefits, but so far, React Native has proven to be more beneficial in many ways. It is maintaining its position at the top in cross-platform app development as always.
