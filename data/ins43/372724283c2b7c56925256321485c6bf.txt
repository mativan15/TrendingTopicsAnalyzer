Opinion: A California windfall profits tax would curb gas-price gouging
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-10-15 12:00:33

Ever since 2015, Californians have been on a roller-coaster ride with gasoline prices consistently a dollar higher than U.S. gas prices.
But the recent steep climb to a $2.60 more per gallon than U.S. prices is so unprecedented that Gov. Newsom rightly called a special legislative session to enact a windfall profits tax.
The governor correctly sees the forest through the trees. Gas prices in California are not set by some magic market mechanism.
