June 03, 2022 09:25 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) has awarded Concurrent Technologies Corporation (CTC) a prime contract worth a potential $15 million to demonstrate the practicality of producing obsolete critical castings through 3D printed sand-casting mold technology. In addition, the project will further develop the science and technology of 3D printed sand-casting molds. CTC will work with subcontractor ExOne, a subsidiary of Desktop Metal (NYSE: DM), as well as the University of Northern Iowa, DLA, and metal casting foundries on the project.
