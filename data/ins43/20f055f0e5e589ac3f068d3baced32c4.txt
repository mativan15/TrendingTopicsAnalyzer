Bernstein says Musk was 'curt' and 'almost dismissive' on earnings call, sees stock dropping nearly 30%
cnbc.com
news
2022-10-20 12:18:54

Tesla 's quarterly revenue came in below expectations , and Elon Musk's remarks on the earnings call also seemed to upset a top tech analyst on Wall Street. Toni Sacconaghi, Bernstein senior research analyst who has a underperform rating on the EV player, said he's not pleased with the Tesla CEO's performance on the call. "Aside from the financials, the earnings call didn't sit well with us," Sacconaghi said in a note Thursday. "Answers to many questions on the earnings call were curt and almost dismissive, with CEO Musk instead repeatedly making very bold prognostications about Tesla's future and capabilities.
