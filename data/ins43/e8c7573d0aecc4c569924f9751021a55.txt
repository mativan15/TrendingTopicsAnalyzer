Oct. 14—COVENTRY — Charlotte Jordan had 13 kills, two digs, six service points and an ace to help lift Coventry to a 25- 6, 25-15, 26-24 North Central Connecticut Conference sweep over Canton on Wednesday at Matthew J. Hurlock Gymnasium.

Abby Polo added 28 assists, five kills, 10 digs, two aces and six service points for the Patriots (9-4 overall, 9- 1 league).

Lindsey Harrington finished with eight kills and 11 digs while Molly Bushnell won 12 service points with four aces and nine digs for Coventry.
