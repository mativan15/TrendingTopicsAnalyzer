Cinema halls rake in over Rs 4,000 crore in 4 months, eye blockbuster 2022
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 19:30:00

ThinkStock Photos2022 is anticipated to be the best year ever for the movie exhibition sector.India's gross box office statistics reached ₹4,002 crore in the four months to April, signalling the best-ever performance post Covid despite the impact of the third wave and closure of a number of cinemas. It was also the first time that the monthly average box office exceeded ₹1,000 crore, thanks to films such as K.G.F: Chapter 2, RRR, The Kashmir Files, Beast, Gangubai Kathiawadi and Valimai. According to a sectorial report from GroupM and Ormax Media, 2022 is anticipated to be the best year ever for the movie exhibition sector, with a domestic box office of ₹12,515 crore, surpassing the previous record set in 2019 by ₹1,567 crore.
