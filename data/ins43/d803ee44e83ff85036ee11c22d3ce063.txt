(Shutterstock)NEW JERSEY — A newly introduced gun measure would make New Jersey's concealed-carry laws "the toughest in the nation," according to Democrats in the State Legislature. The bill would expand background checks, add requirements for obtaining a concealed-carry permit, and criminalize carrying a gun in certain areas.The legislation, introduced last week, would also require license holders to purchase liability insurance and require training — even for those who already have a permit to carry a handgun.
