NAMIC teams up with The Risk Institute at The Ohio State University Max M. Fisher College of Business on national surveyINDIANAPOLIS, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Homeowners in hurricane-prone coastal states and around the country believe severe weather is increasing and that their property is at growing risk, but they aren't confident in how to better protect their properties or the role insurance plays in that protection. Those are among the key findings from a new survey by the National Association of Mutual Insurance Companies.
