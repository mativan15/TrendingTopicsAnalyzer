Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Halls like this one in Knodishall, Suffolk, typically provide a focal point for different aspects of village lifeVillage halls in need of a facelift can apply to share a £3m fund marking the Queen's Platinum Jubilee. The money will be divided between 125 halls in England to pay for renovations like wi-fi, building extensions and modernising facilities.Rural affairs minister Lord Benyon described the venues as "the centrepiece of rural life".The aim is to build on schemes that helped commemorate Queen Victoria and King George V.
