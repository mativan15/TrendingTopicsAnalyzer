KANSAS CITY, Mo., Oct. 15, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Standard Wellness ("Standard" or "the Company") has announced that its sister company The Forest will open its first medical marijuana dispensary in Missouri in the Westport historic neighborhood of Kansas City. The Forest Kansas City ("the Forest") will begin serving patients from its location at 706 Westport on October 7. The dispensary will have hours of operation of 10 AM – 7PM on Monday through Saturday and 10AM – 6PM on Sundays."We are excited to expand access to medical cannabis to patients in Missouri and build strong relationships in the Kansas City community," said Devin Mullins, General Manager of The Forest.
