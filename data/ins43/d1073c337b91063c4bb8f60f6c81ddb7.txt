There's a clear reason why Lululemon Athletica (LULU 3.07%) is attracting attention right now. In an environment of rising inflation and supply chain troubles, retail isn't the easiest business. But Lululemon is showing it can handle the headwinds -- and grow.
In fact, the seller of yoga-inspired apparel this year has even reached some targets of its Power of Three growth plan early. And it's launched an all-new plan to take it to the next level. But Lululemon shares haven't climbed along with this good news.
