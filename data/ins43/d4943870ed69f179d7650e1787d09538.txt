Published: 06:11 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 06:11 EDT, 31 May 2022 People pay tribute to Punjabi hip hop star Sidhu Moose Wala, who was shot dead by unidentified assailants Thousands of mourners gathered Tuesday for the cremation of Indian hip hop star Sidhu Moose Wala, whose murder at the weekend shocked fans at home and in Punjabi communities from Canada to Britain. Moose Wala -- whose real name is Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu -- was shot dead in his car by unidentified assailants in the northern state of Punjab on Sunday.
