Marti157900/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Conn's (NASDAQ:CONN) shares slid nearly 20% in premarket trading on Tuesday after the sudden departure of CEO Chandra Holt. The Texas-based specialty retailer announced on Tuesday that Holt would step down from her role as CEO and resign her seat on the board, effective immediately. Norman L. Miller will serve as interim CEO, returning to a role he held from September 2015 to August 2021 when he was succeeded by Holt. 'The Board is pleased to welcome Norm back as Interim President and Chief Executive Officer.
