PM likely to inaugurate greenfield airport in Itanagar and Kameng hydroelectric power project in Arunachal Pradesh
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 14:30:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi is likely to inaugurate the greenfield airport in Itanagar and 600 mw generation capacity Kameng hydroelectric power project in Arunachal Pradesh. A senior official in Arunachal Pradesh government who does not want to be named told ET, " The Prime Minister is likely to visit Arunachal Pradesh on October 27 or 28 and his tour schedule has not yet been finalised". Modi is also likely to visit Tripura. Modi has recently assured Arunachal Pradesh chief minister Pema Khandu that he will visit the state to dedicate to the nation two projects, the Greenfield airport and a power project.
