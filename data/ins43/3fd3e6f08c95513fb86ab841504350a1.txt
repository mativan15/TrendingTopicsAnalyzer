Published: 03:02 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 03:13 EDT, 16 October 2022 New Chancellor Jeremy Hunt has been accused of leading a 'silent coup' after he effectively took control of the Government and ditched the last remaining major plank of Liz Truss's new economic plan.Amid claims that Tories are still plotting to replace the Prime Minister, Mr Hunt is expected to follow up her Friday climbdown on increasing Corporation Tax by axing the mini-Budget's plan to cut the basic rate of income tax by 1p to 19p.
