CBS Sports has updated their bowl projections for the 2022 college football postseason, with the Florida Gators projected to play the Baylor Bears in the Liberty Bowl.
The Liberty Bowl is currently scheduled to be played on Dec. 28 in Memphis, Tennessee, with kickoff set for 5:30 p.m. EST.

The Gators are coming off a 45-35 loss to LSU, the Orange and Blue's fourth straight loss in the rivalry. Offensively, there were many positive signs, with Anthony Richardson opening the game with a 51-yard touchdown pass and adding a third quarter, 81-yard scramble that resulted in a touchdown.
