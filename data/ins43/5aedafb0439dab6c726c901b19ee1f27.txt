'Waived off farmers' loan in Chhattisgarh in 2 hrs after forming govt': CM Baghel in HP

Source: ANI

Chhattisgarh Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel while campaigning for Congress in Himachal Pradesh said that his government in Chhattisgarh waived off farmers' loan in 2 hrs after forming government in the state. Addressing an event in Solan, he said, 'The BJP govt works to pull out money from your pockets, by increasing the prices of petrol, diesel, PNG. Now they've imposed GST as well, 5% on 'Rotis' & 18% on 'Paranthas'. Make Congress win in Himachal by a 3/4th majority.
