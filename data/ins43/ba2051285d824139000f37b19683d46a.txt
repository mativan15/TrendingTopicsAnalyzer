Charles may get dragged into political turmoil, warns polling expert
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

Liz Truss's successor must try to 'enjoy the next two years' as the Conservatives are likely to lose the next election, according to Scotland's leading polls expert.Sir John Curtice, professor of politics at Strathclyde University, said the Conservatives were now in terminal decline following Truss's resignation after just 44 days.He said Rishi Sunak, the former chancellor whose resignation spelt the end of Boris Johnson, and Penny Mordaunt, a possible unity candidate, were the frontrunners to become the next prime minister.
