Prada Names Roberto Massardi as Chief Business Development Officer
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 11:46:00

By Pierre Bertrand Prada SPA said Wednesday that Roberto Massardi has been appointed Chief Business Development Officer and has been in the role since May. Mr. Massardi is responsible for the fashion group's strategic development by assessing new business opportunities, Prada said. He has worked for the Prada group in the past, and has also held roles at Pirelli & C. SpA and Stone Island, the company said. Write to Pierre Bertrand at pierre.bertrand@wsj.com
