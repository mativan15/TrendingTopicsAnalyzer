ByBlack Conference WASHINGTON (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Today, the U.S. Black Chambers, Inc. (USBC) kicked off their annual ByBlack Conference 'The Color of Commerce' at the MGM National Harbor Resort & Casino taking place October 19-21, 2022. ByBlack Conference will feature over 50 speakers, 20 mainstage sessions, 3 evening experiences, and various masterclasses with topics ranging from public relations to mental health care. Attendees will have the opportunity to learn from some of the most influential leaders in Black business, as well as network with other attendees who are interested in taking their businesses to the next level.
