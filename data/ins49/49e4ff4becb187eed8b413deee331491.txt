3 executives to leave KFC due rising interest rates: REPORT
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 10:16:00

Senior-level KFC executives are leaving the company due to rising interest rates on the company's pension plan, according to a report from the Wall Street Journal.

The Journal reviewed internal messages between employees and franchisees, which stated that KFC's U.S. chief operating officer, chief financial officer and national field operations director all had announced their retirement by the end of this month. KFC is the largest division of Louisville-based Yum Brands Inc. (NYSE: YUM).

The executives are Monica Rothgery, COO; Trip Vorholt, CFO; and Jeff Griffin, director of national field operations.
