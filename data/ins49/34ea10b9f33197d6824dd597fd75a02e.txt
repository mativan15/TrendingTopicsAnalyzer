Published: 00:55 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 01:29 EDT, 16 October 2022 SAN JOSE, Calif. (AP) - Sam Lafferty scored short-handed goals on back-to-back penalties in the second period to give coach Luke Richardson his first career win in the Chicago Blackhawks' 5-2 victory over the San Jose Sharks on Saturday night. "Just kind of good bounces," Lafferty said. "Right place, right time and good work by all the PK guys." Jonathan Toews also scored in the second period as the Blackhawks scored three times in a span of 3:14 to erase a two-goal deficit.
