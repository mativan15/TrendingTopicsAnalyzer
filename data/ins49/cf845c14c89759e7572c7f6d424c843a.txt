Red Wings' Jakub Vrana enters player assistance program
espn.com
sport
2022-10-19 23:03:00

7:03 PM ETDetroit Red Wings forward Jakub Vrana has been placed in the NHL and NHLPA's player assistance program.The league entities released a joint statement on Wednesday updating Vrana's status as he enters their protocols, which are designed to help players with a range of matters from mental health to substance abuse.Vrana will be unavailable to the Red Wings for an "indefinite period" while receiving necessary care, the NHL and union said. He will continue to be paid and can return to the ice once cleared by the program's administrators.
