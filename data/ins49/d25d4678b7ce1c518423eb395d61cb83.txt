Published: 16:36 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 16:36 EDT, 20 October 2022 LANDOVER, Md. (AP) - Aaron Rodgers walked into the locker room after a practice this week and felt a lively vibe that belied a rare Green Bay Packers losing streak. They've lost two in a row during the same regular season for the first time since Matt LaFleur took over as coach in 2019. Green Bay has the chance to end the skid at the Washington Commanders on Sunday. "We just want to get back out there," Rodgers said. "The beauty of this league is you get another chance.
