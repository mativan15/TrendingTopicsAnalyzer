India will be first inbound market to recover to 2019 levels for Australia, says Tourism Australia
indiatimes.com
travel
2022-10-20 14:00:00

PTIA passenger arrives on an early morning flight at Sydney Airport.Among all the global markets, India will be the first inbound market in the world to recover to 2019 levels for Australia. Indian arrivals to Australia for the period April to August 2022 were at 86% of pre-Covid levels, and around 18,000- 20,000 spectators from India are expected for the upcoming ICC Men's T20 World Cup, said Nishikant Kashikar, country manager, India and Gulf at Tourism Australia. India ranked as the number two inbound market for Australia after New Zealand since the reopening of Australian borders as well as from January to August this year.
