Mighty Tigers stun Ansonia
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-01 03:59:00

May 31—DERBY — Brody Belanger threw a complete- game 6- hitter and Lucas Tarbell hit a 2- run double in the top of the seventh inning when Windham Tech scored four times in a 4-1 CIAC Class M state tournament qualifying- round baseball victory over Ansonia on Saturday.

Trailing 1- 0 entering the seventh, Tyler Tashea and Ben Gardner each singled before Tarbell's double into the gap put the 34th- seeded Mighty Tigers (9-12) ahead 2-1. Dylan McMahon moved Tarbell to third with a sacrifice bunt and Danny Meikle followed with a double for a 3- 1 lead.
