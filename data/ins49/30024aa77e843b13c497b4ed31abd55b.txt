‘One in four skipping meals' Brits forced to rely on ‘cheap calories' as food crisis bites
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-28 09:00:00

The Ipsos poll was conducted with 2,061 people aged between 16 and 75 in Great Britain between May 11 and May 12, and revealed that 51 percent of people were 'very concerned' over the cost of living. With inflation and the cost of food on the rise, people around the country have been forced to look into their weekly spending habits, with food shopping costs having to be slashed. This has led to Brits becoming 'concerned' for their diets, but experts have revealed some handy tips for creating healthy meals on a low budget.
