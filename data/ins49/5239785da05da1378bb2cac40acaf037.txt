ASML To Appoint Wayne Allan as Executive Vice President
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 05:37:00

By Michael Susin ASML Holding NV said Wednesday that it will appoint Wayne Allan as executive vice president and chief strategic sourcing and procurement officer, subject to general meeting approval on April 26. The Dutch semiconductor-equipment maker said that Mr. Allan will be responsible for sourcing and supply chain organization, a new position in the board. "The appointment of Wayne Allan to the board of management reflects the importance of our sourcing and supply chain organization in our ability to execute our strategy.
