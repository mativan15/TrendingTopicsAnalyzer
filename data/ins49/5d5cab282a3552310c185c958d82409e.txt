Air pollution is not just a London issue. The pressing need to cut harmful transport emissions is one of the most urgent challenges of our era and shared by cities across Europe.Without fast action to transform the way we move around in our cities, the worst impacts of climate change and air pollution only become more likely. As we all look for solutions to clean up urban transport, shared, electric mobility presents itself as a sustainable fix to these problems.Encouraging drivers who drive the most miles to switch to electric vehicles is a critical part of this journey.
