Gold prices hit 1-month high as dollar weakens, set for weekly gain
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 02:15:00

June 3: Gold prices scaled a one-month peak on Friday, supported by a lower U.S. dollar, which also put bullion on track for a third straight weekly gain. 
FUNDAMENTALS
* Spot gold was up 0.2% at $1,871.69 per ounce, as of 0048 GMT, its highest since May 9. Gold prices have risen about 1% this week. * U.S. gold futures were up 0.3% to $1,875.90. * Gold prices rose over 1% on Thursday supported by a dip in the dollar and data showing U.S. private payrolls rose less than expected last month. * Signs of an economic crisis can be supportive for gold demand, as investors consider it as a safe-haven asset.
