Vols No. 1 in USA TODAY Sports baseball coaches poll for tenth straight week
usatoday.com
news
2022-05-30 18:44:31

Tennessee (53-7, 25-5 SEC) concluded Southeastern Conference Tournament play Sunday in Hoover, Alabama.
Tennessee defeated Florida, 8-5, at Hoover Metropolitan Stadium in the SEC Tournament championship game.
The Vols won the SEC regular season and tournament championship in 2022.
Following the SEC Tournament, the latest USA TODAY Sports baseball coaches poll was released Monday.
The Vols are ranked No. 1 for the tenth consecutive week.
