Nestle Expands Coffee Business, Lifts 2022 Organic Growth Guidance
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 07:25:00

By Giulia Petroni Nestle SA said Wednesday that it would acquire Seattle's Best Coffee brand from Starbucks Corp. in a move to bolster its U.S. coffee business, and lifted sales guidance after price increases in the first nine months of the year. The Swiss food and beverage giant plans to acquire the brand for an undisclosed sum, expanding its coffee portfolio. Nestle has a robust presence in the world coffee sector, with brands like Nescafe, Nespresso and Blue Bottle. In the first nine months, group sales rose to 69.
