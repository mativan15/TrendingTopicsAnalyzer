Flood recovery grant fraud battle slows cash flow for those who desperately need it
abc.net.au
business
2022-06-05 19:59:20

People trying to eke out a road to recovery after this year's floods say they've applied for government grants only to find fraudsters have beaten them to the punch.Businesses and residents in northern New South Wales say their details have been used for fraudulent grant applicationsThere are concerns fraud prevention is stopping people in need from accessing key recovery grantsService NSW has rejected 50 per cent of flood recovery grant applicationsAndrea Bonotto's burger joint in Lismore's CBD was left in ruins after floodwaters receded, so he decided to open a new business on higher ground.
