Fears over government's data protection plan
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 23:01:00

Britain's data protection regime is set to be overhauled, ministers announced in last month's Queen's Speech. The Data Protection Reform Bill is intended to realise the government's stated intention of creating a more business-friendly system that promotes growth and innovation.However, if the bill results in significant divergence from the EU regime, the UK risks losing the adequacy finding made by the European Commission last year.That finding enabled the free-flow of personal data from the EU to continue after the UK formally left the bloc.
