MANHATTAN BEACH, Calif., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Satyagraha Alliance, the social corporate responsibility arm of Matern Law Group, has announced the launch of their new microloan program. The program was created to empower and support underserved business owners and ambitious entrepreneurs in California who are unable to secure financing through conventional loans.  
The Satyagraha Alliance will be offering an affordable loan product for aspiring or established entrepreneurs to use in their start-up or existing business.
