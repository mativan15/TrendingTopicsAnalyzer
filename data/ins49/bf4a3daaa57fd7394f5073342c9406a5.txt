Legends Acquire Returning Player Rights To Tyler Hall, Send Feron Hunt To Westchester
nba.com
news
2022-10-17 15:30:00

FRISCO, TX, (Oct. 14, 2022) - The Texas Legends have acquired the returning player rights to Tyler Hall as well as a 2023 2nd round pick in a trade with the Westchester Knicks.  In return, Texas sends the returning rights to Feron Hunt.

Hall (6-5, 210, Montana State) saw action in two games with the Mavericks in NBA preseason averaging 3.0 points, 2.0 rebounds in 12.3 minutes per game.  Hall spent the last three seasons with the Westchester Knicks averaging 11.8 points, 3.8 rebounds, and 2.8 assists across 89 games (66 starts).
