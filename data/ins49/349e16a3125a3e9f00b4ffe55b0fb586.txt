Lured back into business by the fun of the fair
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 10:00:00

For many the fairground conjures images of hook-a-duck, the waltzers, candyfloss and perhaps even the odd pick-pocket. For the Instagram generation, though, there is a new fairground concept in town: Fairgame, an indoor adults-only playground where they can drink cocktails, graze on prosecco-infused popcorn and play games sexed up for the tech-savvy generation Z.It is the brainchild of Richard Hilton, the founder of Gymbox, a gym chain he started in 2004 with the aim of making exercise more fun. Gyms and indoor fairgrounds have more in common than one might think, he said.
