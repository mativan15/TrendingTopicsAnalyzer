June 02, 2022 02:37 PM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CRG, a national real estate development and investment firm, has announced the successful closing of U.S. Logistics Fund II (USLF II). The fund and its co-investment vehicles expect to deliver $1.5 billion of new state-of-the-art warehouse and distribution industrial facilities for e-commerce and other users across key logistics markets throughout the United States over the next two years.



CRG raised $300 million through USLF II, reaching the fund's cap three months ahead of schedule, and co-investment vehicles are expected to provide an additional $150 million of equity.
