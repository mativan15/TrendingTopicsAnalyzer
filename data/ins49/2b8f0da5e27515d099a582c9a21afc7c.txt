With six days left for Deepavali, most of the textile shops witnessed brisk sales here on Tuesday.Over 3,000 shops selling textile products function at Ashokapuram, near Central Theatre and at the E.K.M. Abdul Gani Textile Market (Gani Market) at Panneerselvam Park in the city. These weekly textile shops start functioning from Monday night and conclude on Tuesday evening. Both wholesale and retail sales are undertaken in these shops that usually witness brisk sales ahead of festival seasons.
