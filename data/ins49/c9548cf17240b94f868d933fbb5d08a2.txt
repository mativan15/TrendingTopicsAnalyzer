The Conservatives can change PM, but not the economic hole they have dug
sky.com
news
2022-10-20 16:54:00

The final straw for Liz Truss's premiership was a collapse of confidence among Conservative MPs, but the underlying cause is an economic crisis she first ignored, then deepened, and will now define her successor's time in office.The Conservatives are about to discover that they can change leaders but not the economic hole they have dug, or the ideological splits that did much of the work.
Ms Truss got into Number 10 on the promise of "growth growth growth", to the delight of small-state free marketeer colleagues who coalesced around her candidacy only at the last moment.
