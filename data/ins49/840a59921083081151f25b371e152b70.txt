October 19, 2022 05:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time SOUTHPORT, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sturm, Ruger & Company, Inc. (NYSE-RGR) will announce its financial results for the third quarter 2022 and file its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q on Wednesday, November 2, 2022, after the close of the stock market.

On Thursday, November 3, 2022, Sturm, Ruger will host a webcast at 9:00 a.m. ET to discuss the third quarter operating results. Interested parties can listen to the webcast via this link or by visiting Ruger.
