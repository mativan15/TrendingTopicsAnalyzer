October 21, 2022 11:16 IST Union Home Minister Amit Shah paying tributes at the 'Police Commemoration Day', in New Delhi on October 21, 2022. | Photo Credit: Twitter/@AmitShah Union Home Minister Amit Shah on October 21, 2022 said most of the "hotspots" in the country are almost free from anti-national activities now and the situation in major security theatres has improved over the last eight years of the Narendra Modi government.
He said more than 70% decline has been recorded in incidents of violence that were reported from insurgency-hit areas of the Northeast during this time period and this is an indicator of a "happy Northeast".
