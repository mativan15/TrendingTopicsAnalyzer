Two former political adversaries who have run the gauntlet, from scandal and scrutiny to mental health challenges, have found a common cause fighting against climate change, writes Sonya Holm. The bumpy road of politics has led two former Palmerston North MPs from opposite sides of the divide to unexpectedly join forces to fight for the environment. Jono Naylor was the mayor of Palmerston North for seven years and abandoned the role in 2014 to go head-to-head with then-incumbent MP Iain Lees-Galloway for the local role.
