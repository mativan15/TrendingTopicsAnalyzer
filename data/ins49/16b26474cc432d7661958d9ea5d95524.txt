Jamie Dimon, the CEO of JPMorgan and one of the most respected Wall Street leaders, gave a stern warning to investors. He advised people to prepare for an upcoming economic 'hurricane.' At an investor conference on Wednesday, Dimon said, 'That hurricane is right out there down the road coming our way.' The chief executive added, 'We don't know if it's a minor one or Superstorm Sandy. You better brace yourself.' Dimon alluded to Hurricane Sandy, a storm National Geographic referred to as a 'raging freak of nature.
