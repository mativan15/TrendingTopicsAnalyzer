Japan eyes kamikaze drones against invasion media
asiabulletin.com
news
2022-10-17 07:15:00

Tokyo reportedly seeks to deploy hundreds of attack drones to its remote islandsThe Japanese military has devised a plan to use kamikaze drones to strike enemy landing forces and vessels should there be an invasion of the nation's remote territories, such as the Nansei Islands, according to local media reports.Initially, the military wants to deploy a limited number of unspecified types of US and other foreign-made loitering munitions, until the Japanese defense industry comes up with a viable domestic alternative, the Japan Times reported on Sunday.
