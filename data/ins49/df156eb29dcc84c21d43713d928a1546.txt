October 20, 2022 01:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Videojet Technologies, a global leader in coding, marking and printing solutions, will be displaying its latest product innovations in Pack Expo booth S-2042 and within 50+ OEM partner booths at McCormick Place in Chicago, October 23-26, 2022. Videojet will demonstrate the latest advancements in continuous inkjet printers, lasers, thermal transfer over printers, thermal inkjet printers, case coders and advanced connectivity options and tools.
