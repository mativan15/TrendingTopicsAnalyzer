Larry Magid: Shortcuts make Mac and Windows easier to use
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-10-20 15:00:08

Regardless of whether you're using a Windows PC or an Apple Macintosh, you may be working harder than you need to be if you're not using shortcuts. Shortcuts are keyboard combinations that you can use to perform a task that might otherwise require accessing a menu. The bad news about shortcuts is that they're sometimes harder to learn or remember because they may not be as intuitive or obvious as selecting a menu. The good news is that they're faster and easier to use and especially helpful if it's a command that you use frequently.
