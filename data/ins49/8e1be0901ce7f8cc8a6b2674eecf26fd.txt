Published: 04:20 EDT, 28 May 2022 | Updated: 04:21 EDT, 28 May 2022 Skincare experts have revealed how certain professions are more likely to suffer from problem skin than others. Yoga teachers and personal trainers have the best skin, according to the research conducted by UK online beauty and cosmetics company Beauty Pie. The nature of their jobs means they are likely to have an overall healthy lifestyle with regular sleep and exercise and a nutritious, balanced diet, which promotes glowing skin.
