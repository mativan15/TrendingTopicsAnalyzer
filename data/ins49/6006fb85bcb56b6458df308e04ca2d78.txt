Changes in the funding of a popular Texas A&M AgriLife Extension Service project for active duty and veteran military members who want to become involved in production agriculture will allow the program to further improve and expand.     The Battleground to Breaking Ground program is for active duty and veteran military members and others interested in production agriculture.
