Despite two quarters of profit, Srei group's net worth "fully eroded"
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 14:30:00

AgenciesThe group netted Rs 70 crore in the December quarter while suffering a Rs 3555 crore loss in the fourth quarter of FY21. The net loss for FY22 was Rs 2545 crore compared with a loss of Rs 7338 crore in the preceding fiscal.Kolkata: The Srei group has eroded its net worth fully after facing two consecutive years of net losses even as the group reported Rs 325 crore of consolidated net profit in the fourth quarter ending March 31, riding on the recovery of loans and sharp fall in expenses. Consequently, it has not been able to comply with various regulatory ratios and limits.
