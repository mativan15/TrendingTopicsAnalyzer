BOSTON (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 BlueConic, the market-leading pure-play customer data platform (CDP), was named a winner of a People's Choice Stevie® Award for Favorite New Products in The 20th Annual American Business Awards®. The People's Choice Stevie Awards for Favorite New Products are a feature of The American Business Awards in which the general public can vote for their favorite new products and services of the year.
