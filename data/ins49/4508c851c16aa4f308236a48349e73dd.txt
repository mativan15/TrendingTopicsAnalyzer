Freight train from Ningxia heads for Hungary

By Hu Dongmei in Yinchuan and Ye Zizhen  | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-05-27 15:42 The first China-Europe freight train from Yinchuan, Ningxia Hui autonomous region, departed on Thursday for Budapest, Hungary. It will arrive in 21 days. [Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn] A freight train loaded with 50 containers departed Yinchuan, Ningxia Hui autonomous region, on Thursday morning, bound for Budapest, Hungary. It was the first China-Europe freight train departing from Ningxia. It is expected to reach its destination in 21 days, passing through Kazakhstan, Russia, Belarus, Poland and Hungary, a trip of 8,500 kilometers.
