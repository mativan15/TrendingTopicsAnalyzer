The growth of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology is something about which a great amount is written, but in terms of everyday business activities specifically, what does this mean? In this blog, we'll look at some examples of AI-driven technology that is having a dramatic impact on how customer service teams interact with customers.This blog will consider the obstacles that need to be overcome to deliver on these three pillars of customer service, how AI is able to help, and how this is driving change in the CCaaS space.
