To add a dose of glamour and entertainment, actors Sidharth Malhotra and Rakul Preet Singh will be seen joining Bollywood superstar Salman Khan on 'Shanivaar Ka Vaar'.The rivalry between housemates Nimrit Kaur Ahluwalia and Priyanka Chahar Choudhary is underlined to the hilt in a task assigned by host Salman.A weighing scale is placed before the duo and the rest of the contestants to vote by dropping weight in the favour of their preferred contestant while also specifying their reason.
