AgenciesRepresentative ImageHeavy rains across Bengaluru on Wednesday lead to collapse of a wall near the Majestic that resulted in damaging several four-wheelers parked on the road. Incessant rains battered Bengaluru Wednesday, with downpours resulting in waterlogging in several parts of the city. 
 The India Meteorological Department (IMD) had already issued a yellow alert in the city.

Karnataka | Some vehicles were damaged after a wall collapsed due to heavy rains near Majestic in Bengaluru city.
