October 17, 2022 02:03 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Reps. Tom Emmer (R-Minn.) and Michelle Fischbach (R-Minn.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Rep. Emmer is a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero,' with a rating of 85 percent. Rep. Fischbach scored a perfect 100 percent in 2021 and has a lifetime rating of 100 percent, making her a 'Taxpayer Super Hero.
