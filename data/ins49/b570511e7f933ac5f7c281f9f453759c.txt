Just as a disheartening incident of a body of an old woman partially eaten by stray dogs at the holy place of Deval Ganagapur in Afzalpur taluk of Kalaburagi district came to light, Chief Executive Officer of Kalaburagi Zilla Panchayat Girish Badole rushed to the village on Tuesday. He met priests at the Dattatreya Temple and enquired about the incident.
