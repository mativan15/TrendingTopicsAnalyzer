Serves 4-6 This one-pot dish is fast and simple to cook, and despite the short cooking time, it is full of flavour. I like to cut a chicken into 8 pieces (your butcher can do this for you), or you can use chicken pieces. I have used Spanish flavours, but kept them subtle so as not to overpower the flavour of the chicken.
