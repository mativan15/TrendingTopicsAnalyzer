Published: 19:05 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 19:05 EDT, 27 May 2022 Having robots for colleagues in the near future will make Aussie businesses work faster, smarter and simpler. And the coffee rounds will be cheaper too. Artificial intelligence (AI) is already very much a mainstay of the modern economy with the average Australian interacting with it more than 100 times a day. This could range from more proactive interactions - chatting with virtual messenger bots or answering questions online - to things like looking up digital maps or receiving targeting recommendations for products and services.
