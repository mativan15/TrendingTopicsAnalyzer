EMX by Big Village Collaborates with Kroger Precision Marketing to Improve Consumer Reach

The collaboration makes Kroger first-party retail data available to advertisers through EMX's Data Connected Marketplace via Private Marketplaces NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- EMX by Big Village, the leading Premium SSP and end-to-end programmatic technology group focused on the planning, buying, and measurement of CTV and omnichannel media, today announced it is approved by Kroger Precision Marketing (KPM), the retail media business of The Kroger Co., to make Kroger retail sales data available to advertisers within its SSP through EMX's Data Connected Private Marketplace (DCPMP) offering.
