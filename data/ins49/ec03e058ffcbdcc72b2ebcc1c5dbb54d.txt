NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Recruiter.com Group Inc. (Nasdaq:RCRT), an on-demand recruiting platform providing flexible talent acquisition solutions that scale from startups to the Fortune 100, today announced a new Shareholder Rewards Program exclusively through TiiCKER.
Shareholders owning Recruiter.com stock can redeem perks simply for owning shares. Perks are available at TiiCKER.com/RCRT or in the TiiCKER app and can be redeemed when a shareholder creates an account and connects any brokerage account to verify their stock ownership.
