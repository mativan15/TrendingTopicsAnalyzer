(Bloomberg) -- It's the $4 billion question. Or $9 billion, depending on who's estimating.How Credit Suisse Group AG will finance a reshaping that will win back investors' confidence -- and what that figure is -- is the biggest of many unknowns hanging over the bank in the final days before its plan is unveiled.What's confirmed is that Credit Suisse is looking for a buyer for all or part of its lucrative but capital-intensive securitized products group, and that it's trying to sell an almost 200-year-old luxury hotel in the heart of Zurich.
