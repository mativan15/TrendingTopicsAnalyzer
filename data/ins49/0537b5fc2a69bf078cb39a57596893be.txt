Tesla Shares Dip on Elon Musk's Plans to Cut Workforce
nbcconnecticut.com
business
2022-06-03 13:10:53

Tesla shares dipped 8.5% on Friday after news that CEO Elon Musk plans a hiring freeze and 10% job cuts at his electric vehicle and renewable energy company. Cowen equity researchers lowered their Tesla vehicle delivery estimates on Friday, too citing the impacts of stringent Covid lockdowns that hampered production at Tesla's Shanghai factory.
