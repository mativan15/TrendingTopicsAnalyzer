Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Folk artists performing in front of a polling station during the 2022 presidential electionThe Turkmen government has an absolute monopoly of the media. The authorities monitor media outlets, control printing presses, block websites, monitor internet use and lay down editorial policies. Reporters Without Borders has called Turkmenistan "an ever-expanding news black hole"."Journalists have been arrested, tortured, physically attacked, or otherwise forced to stop working," the group says.
