The SC amends its 2016 order and approves the NEET-PG schedule for the academic year 2022–2023

In light of the postponement of the start of the previous two academic years due to the onset of Covid-19, the Supreme Court modified its 2016 order on Friday and approved the academic calendar for postgraduate medical courses proposed by the National Medical Commission for the academic year 2022–23. According to the approved academic calendar, the first round of counselling for all-India quota and deemed central institutes under the central counselling in the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test was held from September 15 to 27.
