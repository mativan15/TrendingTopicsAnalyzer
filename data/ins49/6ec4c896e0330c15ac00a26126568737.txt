Two people die in Glendenning house fire, police establish crime scene

Here's what you need to know today.Two people killed in house fire The fire burned for hours. (ABC News) A firefighter works to put out the blaze.(ABC News)Two people have died in a house fire in Sydney's west this morning. Emergency services were called to Glendenning at 4:45am amid reports a house was on fire. A woman was rescued from the flames and paramedics commenced CPR. Despite efforts to revive her, she died at the scene.A second person died inside the building.Police have established a crime scene and investigations into the cause of the fire are underway.
