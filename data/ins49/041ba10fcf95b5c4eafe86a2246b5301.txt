By Reuters Published: 11:45 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:45 EDT, 17 October 2022 The Denver Broncos are activating All-Pro safety Justin Simmons for Monday night's game against the Los Angeles Chargers, NFL Network reported. Simmons has missed the past four games with an injured quad. Denver designated him for a return from injured reserve last week. He was hurt in the Broncos' season opener at Seattle. Simmons, 28, had played in 66 straight games prior to the injury. Simmons finished Week 1 with nine tackles and a fumble recovery.
