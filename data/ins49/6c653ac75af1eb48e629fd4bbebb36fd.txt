October 17, 2022 06:17 PM Eastern Daylight Time SOUTHFIELD, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sterling Bancorp, Inc. (the 'Company') (NASDAQ: SBT), the thrift holding company for Sterling Bank and Trust, F.S.B., Southfield, Michigan (the 'Bank'), today announced that it expects to issue its financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2022 prior to market open on Monday, October 31, 2022.

The Company will also host a conference call and webcast on Monday, October 31, 2022 at 11 a.m. (ET) to discuss its financial results.
