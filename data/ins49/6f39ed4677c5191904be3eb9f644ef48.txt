Oil prices surge, gas hits new high after E.U. cracks down on Russia
startribune.com
news
2022-05-31 22:21:11

Americans filling their gas tanks are feeling intensifying economic reverberations from the war in Ukraine. Fuel prices set a record Tuesday after the European Union agreed to a partial ban on Russian oil, the bloc's most significant economic move yet to punish Moscow for the unprovoked invasion. The U.S. average for a gallon of gasoline now stands at $4.62, according to AAA; that's 52% higher than last year and an inflationary pressure point that can choke consumer spending and flatten economic growth.
