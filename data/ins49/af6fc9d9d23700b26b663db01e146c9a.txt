 TORONTO – The Toronto Maple Leafs have placed veteran defenceman Jake Muzzin on injured reserve, the team announced Wednesday.
The 33-year-old left Monday's 4-2 home loss to Arizona with a neck injury after colliding with Coyotes winger Clayton Keller in the second period.Muzzin, who missed the start of training camp with back discomfort and has a concussion history, will miss at least the next three games — Thursday at home against Dallas, Saturday in Winnipeg and Monday in Vegas.
