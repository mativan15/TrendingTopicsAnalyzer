Looking for the iPhone 14 launch date? Shockingly, leaks suggest there might be more than ONE
hindustantimes.com
tech
2022-05-31 04:27:16

The iPhone 14 launch date has been a deeply intriguing topic of discussion among fans amid all the supply chain disruptions Apple has been facing. But new leaks suggest that the iPhone 14 launch might have a split release, just like the iPhone 12. The iPhone 14 launch dates have been under doubt ever since the key Apple suppliers in China began facing reopening delays due to the COVID-19 pandemic lockdowns. Even as many sources believe that the suppliers might be able to make up for lost time by working round the clock, it is expected that the manufacturing timelines might suffer a delay.
