Milpitas Post editorial: Vigorous community conversation needs to get underway regarding the future of high school education in Milpitas
mercurynews.com
news
2022-05-31 04:02:07

The need for community involvement in the decisions the Milpitas Unified School District board will be making soon is very clear. Less so is the path to get such a conversation going. The Milpitas Post's Aliyah Mohammed, over the past several weeks, has outlined extensively some of the choices that have been presented to our school trustees. In her reporting, she has noted that board members need and want more response from the public on this issue.
Our single comprehensive high school, Milpitas High, which was built for 1,800 students 45 years ago, now houses more than 3,100.
