ANIIn the March-April polls to the 126-member Assam Assembly, the BJP had secured 60 seats. Assam chief minister Himanta Biswa Sarma said on Thursday that he will expand his council of ministers on June 9. This will be the first expansion of the one-year-old Himanta Biswa Sarma led government in Assam. Sarma told media persons, 'There will be a minor induction with one minister from KarbiAnglong will be inducted in the council of ministers on June 9.' Presently four berths are vacant in the council of ministers.
