Cardano leads increases as largest cryptocurrencies start mixed
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 14:00:00

The largest cryptocurrencies were mixed during morning trading on Tuesday, with Cardano ADAUSD, +11.89% seeing the biggest move, jumping 13.14% to 62 cents. Polkadot DOTUSD, -4.55% led the decreases with a 3.55% drop to $10.14. Four other cryptocurrencies saw increases Tuesday. Uniswap UNIUSD, +1.28% rose 1.75% to $5.63, and Bitcoin Cash BCHUSD, +0.37% climbed 1.39% to $194.56. Ripple XRPUSD, -0.12% and Bitcoin BTCUSD, -0.00% rounded out the increases, ticking up 0.38% to 41 cents and 0.19% to $31,330.
