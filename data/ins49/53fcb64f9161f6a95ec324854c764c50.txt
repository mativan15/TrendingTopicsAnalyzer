PHILADELPHIA, Oct. 16, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kaskela Law LLC announces that it is investigating CorMedix Inc. ('CorMedix' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: CRMD) on behalf of the Company's long-term investors.Recently an amended securities fraud complaint was filed against CorMedix on behalf of certain investors who purchased shares of the Company's common stock between October 16, 2019 and September 6, 2021. According to the complaint, during that time period CorMedix and certain of the Company's executive officers issued a series of materially false and misleading statements to investors about the Company's business, operations and prospects.
