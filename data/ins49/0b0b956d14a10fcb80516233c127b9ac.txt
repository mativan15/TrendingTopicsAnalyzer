May 27, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--American Express Global Business Travel ('Amex GBT' or the 'Company'), the world's leading B2B travel platform, today announced that it will begin trading as a public company on the New York Stock Exchange ('NYSE') under ticker symbol 'GBTG' on May 31, 2022.

Paul Abbott, Amex GBT's Chief Executive Officer, said: 'Today marks a significant milestone in the business travel industry recovery and for Amex GBT.
