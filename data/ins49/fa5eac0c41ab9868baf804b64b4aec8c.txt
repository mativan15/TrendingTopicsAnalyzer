Sutter Health to pay $13 million to settle federal claims over health plan billing
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 13:12:00

Sutter Health will have to pay $13 million to settle allegations brought by federal prosecutors over billing of government health programs.

The U.S. Attorney's Office in San Francisco announced the settlement this week. Prosecutors had alleged that one of Sacramento-based Sutter Health's Bay Area hospitals billed insurance, including government health plans, for lab tests that were actually done by a third party.
