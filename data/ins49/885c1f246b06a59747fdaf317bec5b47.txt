Sensex, Nifty rise amid firm global cues; IT stocks lead gains

AgenciesTech Mahindra rose 2.85 per cent to Rs 1,110.85. HCL Tech advanced 2.53 per cent to Rs 1,005.90. Infosys gained 2.10 per cent to Rs 1,459.30. NEW DELHI: Benchmark stock indices opened higher on Friday, tracking an overnight rise in US stocks and an ease in oil prices from a two-week high. Gains were led mainly by IT shares, with HCL Tech, , Infosys and Wipro remaining the top four Sensex gainers. Financial stocks also gained. At 9.27 am, the BSE Sensex was trading 435.93 points or 0.80 per cent higher at 54,688.
