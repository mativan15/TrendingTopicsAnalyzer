Published: 21:35 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 21:35 EDT, 15 October 2022 Greek mythology is littered with tall tales and battles against the odds, but the story of the country's Rugby League World Cup team might just be one of the most extraordinary. It is a tale that involves arrests, midnight matches and Facebook posts advertising fake qualifying games. As head coach Steve Georgallis told AAP ahead of their first World Cup game against France in Doncaster on Monday (Tuesday AEDT), Greece's story is an example of "sports politics at its worst".
