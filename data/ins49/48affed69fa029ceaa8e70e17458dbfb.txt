October 18, 2022 08:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Capstone Green Energy Corporation (NASDAQ: CGRN) will hold its quarterly conference call to discuss its second quarter fiscal year 2023 financial results on Monday, November 14, 2022 at 1:45 p.m. Pacific Time (4:45 p.m. Eastern Time).

At the end of the live conference call, Capstone Management will host a question-and-answer session to provide an opportunity for financial analysts to ask questions. Investors and interested individuals are invited to listen to the webcast by logging on to the Company's investor relations webpage.
