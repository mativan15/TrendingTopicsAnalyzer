My latest money-saving ruse: taking in a lodger
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 23:01:00

I grew up in a big house with a succession of lodgers. Over the years about 20 people passed through our doors to live with my mum, my sister and me.Always recommended by friends of friends, they were largely sensible young people who kept to themselves, had a shelf in the fridge and never brought anyone home without asking first. The worst it ever seemed to get was when one lodger ran the washing machine to clean just one T-shirt or the occasional time the odd bottle of wine vanished from the basement.
