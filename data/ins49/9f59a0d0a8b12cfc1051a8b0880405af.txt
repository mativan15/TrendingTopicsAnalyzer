Why fake crypto investment opportunities top billion dollar scam list
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-03 22:42:13

Cryptocurrency investment scams are currently on the rise, according to a new report from the Federal Trade Commission (FTC).The report, published on Thursday, states that since last year, more than 40,000 Americans have reported losing "over $1 billion in crypto to scams," with a majority of scammers using Bitcoin, the world's most popular cryptocurrency, followed by Tether and Ether. According to the report, the number of reported cryptocurrency losses last year "were nearly sixty times what they were in 2018.
