Florida NewsBy Will AlthoffMay 28, 2022It's time to stock up on those hurricane supplies. Florida's Disaster Preparedness Sales Tax Holiday is underway and Governor Ron DeSantis urging Floridians to get prepared for the upcoming Atlantic Hurricane season. Flashlights, radios, batteries, generators and more can all be purchased tax free. Atlantic Hurricane Season kicks off Wednesday.
