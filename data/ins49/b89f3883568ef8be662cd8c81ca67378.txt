Terri Lampe Melcher has been named vice president of workforce development and continuing education for Reading Area Community College (RACC). In this position, Melcher will lead workforce development efforts at RACC, including customized training in business and industry; healthcare, nonprofit and government sectors; workforce development programs, professional/career training programs; and small business/entrepreneurial development programs. She will work with the president and senior leadership team to determine a vision of growth to train the workforce of Berks County and beyond.
