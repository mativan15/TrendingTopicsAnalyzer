Newly Relocated Industry Show to Welcome Tech Talent to Connect with Top AI and IoT Companies

AUSTIN, Texas, October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the AI Summit and IoT World Austin conference announced a new slate of programming, speakers and talks focused on the trends of Smart Mobility & Connected Transportation, AI is Coming Down to Earth, and Quantum is Getting Real. Taking place at the Austin Convention Center November 2 - 3 and featuring 150 speakers from more than 100 companies and 10 countries, this global event celebrates the convergence of AI and IoT - two transformative technologies used in everyday life by everyone.
