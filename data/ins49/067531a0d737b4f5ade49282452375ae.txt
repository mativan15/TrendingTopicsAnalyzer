Elizabeth Debicki opened up about the moment she recreated Princess Diana's iconic 'revenge dress.'The 32-year-old Australian actress is set to take over the role of the late Princess of Wales from Emma Corrin in the forthcoming season of The Crown. As such, she recreated one of the best-known moments in fashion of the last several decades.In 1994, Princess Diana wore an off-the-shoulder black gown to a Vanity Fair party at London's Serpentine Gallery. The party coincided with news reports fixating on her ex husband, King Charles III, and his confession to having an affair with Camilla Parker-Bowles in a TV interview.
