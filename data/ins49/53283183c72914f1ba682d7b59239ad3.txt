Oct. 18—OXFORD — For all the good things No. 7 Ole Miss is doing this season, tackling is one that could stand to use some improvement.

After solid tackling grades in the first four games of the season, Pro Football Focus has given the Rebels (7-0, 3-0 SEC) tackling grades of 44.9, 56.5 and 54.2 against Kentucky, Vanderbilt and Auburn, respectively. Their previous low was 65.2 against Tulsa. The rating is on a scale of 1-100.

Ole Miss missed 16 tackles against the Tigers and has 49 missed tackles total the last three weeks.
