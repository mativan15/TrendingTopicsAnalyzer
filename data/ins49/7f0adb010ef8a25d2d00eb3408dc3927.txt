Is It Time to Invest in China's Internet Giants? Two Strategists Weigh in
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-20 01:49:02

Investing in China's technology giants may seem like a risky move to some investors, but one analyst says valuations are "extremely cheap" and China tech buys are an obvious choice now. Tencent and Alibaba are "extremely strong companies," according to Anand Batepati, portfolio manager at GFM Focus Investing. But Gil Luria, technology strategist at D.A. Davidson, said investors should avoid Chinese big tech stocks because their overseas expansion could be affected as the country is headed toward an "isolationist path.
