CHICAGO and VANCOUVER, British Columbia, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Green Thumb Industries Inc. ('Green Thumb') (CSE: GTII) (OTCQX: GTBIF), a leading national cannabis consumer packaged goods company and owner of RISE Dispensaries, opened applications for the third round of its Good Green Grant Program. 'The Good Green Grant Program was born out of the desire to reinvest cannabis funds back into the community and create opportunities for nonprofit organizations who are doing the groundwork to create real and sustained progress against the War on Drugs,' said Green Thumb Founder and Chief Executive Officer Ben Kovler.
