>>Home-Building Contractor Accused of Taking Money from Clients  (Cumberland County, PA) --  A contractor from Dickinson Township is facing charges that he stole from clients in his custom home-building business. The Cumberland County District Attorney's Office says Timothy Hoffman of Hoffman Custom Contracting has been charged with five counts of theft. He reportedly required people to pay him deposits up front for his work then never completed the work. The D-A's office says many of his victims were elderly residents.
