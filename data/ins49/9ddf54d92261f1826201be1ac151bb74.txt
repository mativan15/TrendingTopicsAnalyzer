AFP via Getty Images For 10-year fixed rate loans, the average student loan refinancing rate held steady at 4.98% for the second straight week. And average rates on 5-year variable-rate loans hit 4.64%, up from 3.53% the week prior, according to the latest rates from Credible for the week ending May 30.
