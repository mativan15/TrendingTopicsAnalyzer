GoNetspeed Coverage Area of Old Orchard Beach 'The importance of high speed, reliable Internet access has never been more evident than it is right now,' commented GoNetspeed CEO Richard Clark. OLD ORCHARD BEACH, Maine (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 GoNetspeed, a high-speed fiber to the premises Internet provider, has completed its fiber Internet build in Old Orchard Beach. GoNetspeed invested over $1.8 Million into the Old Orchard Beach community with their latest build. Service is now available to over 3,000 locations in Old Orchard Beach, spanning 27 miles.
