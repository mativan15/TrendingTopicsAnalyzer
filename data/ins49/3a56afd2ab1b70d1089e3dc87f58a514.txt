Person in serious condition after Christchurch assault
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-18 06:20:12

A person has been taken to hospital in a serious condition after an assault in the heart of the Christchurch central business district. Police were notified of an assault on Cashel St at 6.20pm. At least 10 officers could be seen at the scene, and part of Cashel St was cordoned off near the Colombo St intersection. It is believed the victim was chased by a group of people before he was allegedly assaulted. A police spokesperson said one person was taken to hospital in a serious condition. More to come.
