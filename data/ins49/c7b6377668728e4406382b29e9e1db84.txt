NCLT has directed HDFC to convene a shareholders' meeting on the HDFC-HDFC Bank merger plan. Why is this meeting important and what should one expect from it? Read on. (Source: ET NOW | Elara Sec)The merger is expected to be completed earlier than expected i.e. by Q2FY24.Approvals are already granted by stock exchanges, RBI and CCI. NCLT nod was the last major approval left which has now come in.If shareholders approve the merger, then HDFC will get excluded from Nifty50. According to some estimates, HDFC might exit Nifty in December this year or January 2023.
