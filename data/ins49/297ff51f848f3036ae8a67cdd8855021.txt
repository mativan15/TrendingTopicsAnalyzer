Geordie Shore star Chloe Ferry has expressed her utter devastation after being forced to leave a flight to Los Angeles, breaking down in tears in a series of videos shared on social media.
On Instagram – where the reality star boasts 3.8million followers – she excitedly shared posts on her story in the lead-up to her trip to LA, which she explained was for a work opportunity.
Chloe and her boyfriend, Johnny Wilbo, made it onto the aircraft, as she shared a video of her legs stretched out in front of her in her business class seat, just below her TV screen.
