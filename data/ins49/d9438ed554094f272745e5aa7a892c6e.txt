SANTA BARBARA, Calif., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sientra, Inc. (NASDAQ: SIEN) ('Sientra' or the 'Company'), a medical aesthetics company focused on enhancing lives by advancing the art of plastic surgery, today announced that it intends to offer and sell in an underwritten public offering, subject to market and other conditions, shares of common stock (or common stock equivalents). Each share of common stock (or common stock equivalent) will be sold with a warrant to purchase one share of the Company's common stock.
