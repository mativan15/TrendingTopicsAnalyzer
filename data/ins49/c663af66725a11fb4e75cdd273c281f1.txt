Texas infielder Trey Faltine (0) celebrates a strikeout while running back to the dugout during the NCAA regional playoff game against Louisiana Tech at Disch-Falk Field in Austin, Texas on June 4, 2022.

For much of Saturday night, a record crowd at UFCU Disch-Falk Field waited.

And waited.

And waited.

Those fans eventually got to root, root, root on the home team after the seventh-inning stretch, though. Tristan Stevens and a surprising string of singles led Texas to a 5-2 win over Louisiana Tech in an NCAA Tournament regional game.
