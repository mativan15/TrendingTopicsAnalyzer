Dr. Thyagi Ponnamperuma will describe different types of trauma exposures of adolescents and risks and resilient factors of trauma related mental health problems including posttraumatic stress disorder, emotional and behavioral problems, and impaired daily functioning. Bio: Thyagi Ponnamperuma, PhD (Maastricht, NL), MPhil (Maastricht, NL), DFM (Colombo, SL), MBBS (Ruhuna, SL) currently serves as Professor in Community Medicine, Faculty of Medicine at the University of Ruhuna in Sri Lanka. Dr. Ponnamperuma is a member of the National Science Foundation, Government Medical Offices Association, Galle Medical Association, Sri Lanka Medical Council, and the Sri Lanka Medical Association.
