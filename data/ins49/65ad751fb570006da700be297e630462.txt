The year 2022 has started satisfactorily for FirstFarms, and we can see progress in several of our operating business units, while the contribution ratio has also increased.

'However, the markets are still characterised by great uncertainty. Prices can i.e. fluctuate dramatically from day to day. We do, however, see an upward trend on both milk and pig prices, and the crop prices continue to set new records.
