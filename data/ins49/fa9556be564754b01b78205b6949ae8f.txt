The story also touches on how quickly technology evolves and how the advancements have made the life of humans far more enjoyable, especially over the last 50 years. MIAMI, Fla. (PRWEB) May 31, 2022 Ernest Cicogna has made his authorial debut with 'Imagine,' a gripping, touching, and memorable, story introducing the Stork family, a young American family living and adapting to life in an ever-changing futuristic world.
