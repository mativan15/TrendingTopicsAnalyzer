A woman has claimed you don't need a tumble dryer to dry your towels this winter - as you can speed up the drying process with an extra step in your washing machineSome items can take too long to dry if you don't use a tumble dryer (stock photo)We're all looking for ways we can cut back on our energy use this winter amid the cost of living crisis, and for many of us, that will mean not using our tumble dryer as often when we want to get our washing dry. And while using clothes horses and radiators works well for smaller items like t-shirts, trying to get bigger and heavier things such as towels dry will take a long time - and could potentially leave your house smelling damp as the water slowly evaporates.
