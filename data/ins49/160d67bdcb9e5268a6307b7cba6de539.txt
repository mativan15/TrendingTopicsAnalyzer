By LIA ZHU in San Francisco | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-05-27 17:28 US Secretary of State Antony Blinken speaks during a reception celebrating Eid in Washington, D.C., US May 26, 2022. [Photo/Agencies] US Secretary of State Antony Blinken on Thursday welcomed Chinese students for their contributions, but whether the White House will truly open the door remains to be seen, an expert said. The United States is "lucky" to have Chinese students who "have enriched our communities", said Blinken in a speech outlining the government's approach toward China at George Washington University.
