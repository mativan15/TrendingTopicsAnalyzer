Devon and Cornwall grassroots sports clubs struggle with rising costs
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 05:27:17

Image caption, Elburton Villa FC chairman Kevin Treeby says the cost of living crisis has put significant pressure on grassroots sportSome sports clubs in Devon and Cornwall say they are struggling with soaring prices.Kick-off times are starting earlier to avoid turning on flood-lights and clubs are battling price increases in transport, maintenance and food.Plymouth's Elburton Villa FC said every game was a struggle to pay officials and cover the players' expenses.The club said families were also in "dire straits for money" and spectator numbers were in decline.
