October 19, 2022 06:57 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Travelers Companies, Inc. today reported net income of $454 million, or $1.89 per diluted share, for the quarter ended September 30, 2022, compared to $662 million, or $2.62 per diluted share, in the prior year quarter. Core income in the current quarter was $526 million, or $2.20 per diluted share, compared to $655 million, or $2.60 per diluted share, in the prior year quarter.
