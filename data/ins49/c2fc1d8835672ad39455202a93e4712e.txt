Liverpool are 11 points behind leaders Arsenal in the Premier League but a victory over Manchester City last weekend has given the Reds renewed optimism for their seasonJurgen Klopp feels Darwin Nunez is reaching his best formJurgen Klopp has a message to Liverpool fans on his side's title quest: 'Please don't think we have no ambitions.' The Reds boss has repeatedly suggested in the past few weeks that his side are out of the race for the Premier League trophy. But after three wins in a week, and two big domestic victories which have moved them to within four points of the top four, and seven behind Champions Manchester City, Klopp admitted that he wished he could turn back time, and say differently.
