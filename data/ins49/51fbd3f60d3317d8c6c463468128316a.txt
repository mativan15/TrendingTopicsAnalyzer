Thailand celebrates 135 years of diplomatic ties with Japan
nhk.or.jp
news
2022-10-21 12:00:00

More than 100 Thai and Japanese musicians are taking part in a festival to promote mutual friendship between their two countries. The event was held to commemorate the 135th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Bangkok and Tokyo. The three-day festival kicked off Friday in a popular tourist shopping mall in the capital. It is one of many commemorative events the two countries have been holding this year, including business and cultural exchanges. The event's organizer Yupharet Eakturapakal said, "I think tourism, food, music, entertainment and all of the soft power are very important for the both countries.
