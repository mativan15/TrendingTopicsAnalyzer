Ted White, Jason Voorhees Actor, Died at 96

Actor Ted White passed away on Friday at the age of 96, reports Bloody Disgusting. In horror circles, White is best known for playing slasher icon Jason Voorhees in 1984's Friday the 13th: The Final Chapter.The news came from film producer Sean Clark, who was informed that White passed peacefully in his sleep. 'I've had the pleasure of knowing him and working with him for close to 20 years. He was my adopted grandpa and hands down the best storyteller you could have ever met,' wrote Clark. 'I am going to miss you immensely and those steak dinners we always had.
