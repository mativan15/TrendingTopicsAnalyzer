2022-10-22 02:47:22

Chuck Adamopoulos is a business teacher at Central Catholic High School. On the field, his Raiders took care of business Friday night in their Merrimack Valley Conference matchup against Haverhill.With a 42-21 victory, Adamopoulos became the 57th football coach in Massachusetts history to reach 200 career wins, and the third this season, joining Shawsheen's Al Costabile and Stoneham's Bob Almeida. After coaching at Pentucket, his alma mater, for two seasons, Adamopoulos has spent 39 years in the Raider program.
