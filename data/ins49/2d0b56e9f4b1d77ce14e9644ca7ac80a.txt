Radico Khaitan Q4 net profit down 31.8% to Rs 50.15 cr, Sales up 9.43% to Rs 3,224.45 cr
indiatimes.com
food
2022-05-31 04:30:00

New Delhi: Liquor maker Radico Khaitan Ltd on Monday reported a 31.79 per cent decline in its consolidated net profit to Rs 50.15 crore for the fourth quarter ended March 2022 on account of continued commodity inflation. The company had posted a consolidated net profit of Rs 73.53 crore in the January-March quarter a year ago, Radico Khaitan said in a BSE filing. However, its revenue from operations was up 9.43 per cent to Rs 3,224.45 crore during the quarter under review as against Rs 2,946.43 crore in the corresponding quarter of the previous fiscal.
