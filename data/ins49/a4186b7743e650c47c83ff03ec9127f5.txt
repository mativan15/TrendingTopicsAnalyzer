Ethereum Foundation founder and Metametaverse CEO Joel Dietz speaks Wednesday at the Korea Herald Biz Forum, held at the Four Seasons Hotel Seoul.The future of the metaverse will include telepathy, predicting the future and hyper learning, Ethereum Foundation founding member and CEO of the metaverse-building platform MetaMetaverse Joel Dietz said Wednesday at the Korea Herald Biz Forum held at the Four Seasons Hotel Seoul.'I am going to make some bold claims,' Dietz said as he introduced three of his futurist visions of the metaverse.
