From starting up to scaling up, business founders are on a constant learning curve, navigating change and developing new skills to get their business to the next growth stage. Having external support from a mentor with shared experiences can make a huge difference in their success. And yet, many of those new to business ownership are unaware of the value of mentoring, as research from Virgin StartUp has revealed. Their recent study found that 75% of new founders have used a business mentor, with 56% getting advice from friends or family and 39% using a professional mentor.
