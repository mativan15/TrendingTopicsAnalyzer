Delhi: 10 arrested in kidney transplant racket
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-01 20:16:13

NEW DELHI: An illegal kidney transplant racket has been busted with the arrest of 10 people including a Delhi-based doctor, police said on Wednesday, and added that at least 12 illegal transplants have been conducted by members of the gang in the last six months. Police said the gang had set up a medical unit in Sonipat district's Guhana to conduct the transplant surgeries. Investigators said that the gang members lured poor people to sell their kidneys for ₹3-4 lakh and sold the organs to rich receivers for ₹30-40 lakh.
