Malvern fintech Cantaloupe staves off delisting by filing 10-K annual report
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 16:14:00

Malvern-based fintech Cantaloupe has filed its Form 10-K annual report a little more than two weeks after receiving notice from the Nasdaq Stock Market that it was no longer in compliance with listing rules for failing to do so within the required time.

Cantaloupe (NASDAQ: CTLP), a digital payments and software services company focused on cashless and mobile transactions in the self-serve retail markets — such as vending machines — has a fiscal year that ends on June 30.
