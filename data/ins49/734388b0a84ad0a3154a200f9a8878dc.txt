Today we face difficult times, but just look at how far we have come
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 21:00:00

We are told by the media that these are extraordinary times. I don't disagree. It's undeniable that referendums, Covid, the war in Ukraine, the recent death of Her Late Majesty the Queen and now the volatility of the markets have shaken us. They've left our compass spinning. Our country needs stability, not a soap opera.
Through every recent event, we reaffirmed and remembered the best of ourselves, as individuals and as a nation. This land of ours is full of stories of those in our communities - national or local, large and small - that have risen to challenges.
