Denmark's largest energy company said Russia cut off its gas supply on June 1 because it refused to pay in rubles, the latest escalation over European energy amid the war in Ukraine.Russia previously halted natural gas supplies to Finland, Poland and Bulgaria for refusing a demand to pay in rubles.
