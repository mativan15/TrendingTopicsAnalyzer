Antietam Valley Recreation & Community Center Pool: 905 Byram St., Lower Alsace Township, 610-779-5240. Apply online and use Paypal or call to make an appointment to join in person, Membership prices range from $125 for a single senior age 60 or older to $375 for a family membership, which includes up to two adults and two children.
