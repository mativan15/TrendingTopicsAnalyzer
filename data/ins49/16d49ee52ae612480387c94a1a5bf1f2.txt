Jonathan Brownlee has said of his Olympic chances 'if I can be genuinely competitive on the start line in Paris, I want to be there', as he was made an MBE by the Princess Royal at Windsor Castle.The younger of the two Brownlee brothers, Jonathan is the only triathlete to obtain a bronze, silver and gold at Olympic Games, finally becoming champion in the mixed relay event at Tokyo 2020.He told the PA news agency: 'I never thought I'd be in this situation now with the Olympic bronze, silver and gold medal and again, in three different Olympics.
