June 01, 2022 10:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time PHILADELPHIA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Comcast Corporation today announced the preliminary results of its 2022 Annual Meeting of Shareholders held this morning. Final voting results will be filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission on a Form 8-K within four business days.

Company Proposals


Election of Directors: Shareholders elected all nominees to Comcast's Board of Directors for one-year terms.



Advisory Vote to Approve Executive Compensation: Shareholders voted in favor of this proposal.
