One in seven Brits skipping meals and going without food, warns new poll

2022-10-18 21:30:00

TUC General Secretary Frances O'Grady said 'no one should have to worry about putting food on the table or heating their homes' as a new poll found 14% of Brits were cutting back on food14% of people are cutting back on food to make ends meet, a TUC poll has foundOne in seven people in the UK are skipping meals or going hungry as Brits face a "cost of living emergency". A new mega poll for the TUC reveals today that 14% of people are cutting back on food to make ends meet, rising to 20% and above in nearly 50 constituencies.
