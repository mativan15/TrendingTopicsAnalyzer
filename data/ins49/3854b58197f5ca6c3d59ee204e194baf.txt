Little-known speed bump trick slashes fuel consumption
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 13:00:00

Every driver knows that speed bumps can be a menace, especially when fuel prices remain high. The constant slowing down and acceleration negatively affect fuel consumption and can lead to motorists spending much more money on petrol and diesel.However, there is a trick that allows drivers to improve their car's fuel economy while driving on roads filled with the speed-limiting obstacles.According to experts at Euro Car Parts, many motorists are unaware that constantly driving at one speed through speed bump-filled roads can massively improve fuel economy.
