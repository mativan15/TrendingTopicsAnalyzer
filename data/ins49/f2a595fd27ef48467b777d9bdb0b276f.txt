Apples & Oranges but the petite iPad is still bigger than top IT Indian firms

The world's most valuable tech company Apple earns more revenue from its smallest product segment- iPads - than India's largest technology companies overall. An analysis of the January-March quarter numbers of these companies show that although Apple has reported a slippery performance in the iPad segment in recent months, it is still larger than the businesses of Indian technology behemoths.
