Costa reveals Christmas menu for 2022
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 11:20:29

It's coming up to that time of year, when Mariah Carey's whistle note comes out of hibernation and toy shops turn into present-buying battlegrounds.
The run-up to Christmas also means supermarkets begin to stock up on festive goodies, and coffee shops say goodbye to pumpkin spice lattes in favour of yuletide specialities.
Costa has just released its Christmas food and drinks menu for 2022, with a host of seasonal favourites alongside exciting new additions to get you in the spirit.
For the first time, the chain has teamed up with Toblerone to showcase two brand new festive drinks.
