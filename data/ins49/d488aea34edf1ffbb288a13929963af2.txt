If you feel suicidal or know someone in distress, please reach out to them with kindness or reach out to local emergency services, helplines, and mental health NGOs)TV actress Vaishali Takkar died by suicide by hanging herself at her residence in Indore. A suicide note was also recovered from the spot, suggesting that she was stressed and harassed by her neighbor, businessman Rahul Navlani.
The actor had always wished to donate her eyes after her death.
