Ramsey, NJ, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Konica Minolta Business Solutions U.S.A., Inc. (Konica Minolta), a leader in industrial and commercial printing and packaging solutions, today announced a new label press, the AccurioLabel 400. As the company accelerates its presence in the label, packaging and embellishment markets, the new machine will target mid- to high-volume label converters, commercial printers, and large brand owners and provide affordable, top-quality digital label printing.The first technology preview of the AccurioLabel 400 was held yesterday at Konica Minolta's European Labels and Packaging Exposition at its offices in Madrid, Spain.
