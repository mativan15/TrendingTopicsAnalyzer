October 20, 2022 10:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time PITTSBURGH--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Seegrid Corporation, the leader in autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for material handling, today announced a strategic partnership with Koops Automation Systems, a leading systems integrator specializing in industrial automation for manufacturing environments. The collaboration agreement enables Koops to bolster their portfolio by introducing their customers to the full suite of Seegrid Palion™ autonomous mobile robots and Fleet Central™ enterprise software solutions.
