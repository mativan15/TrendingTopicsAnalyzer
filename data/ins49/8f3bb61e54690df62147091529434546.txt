Pa. GOP Gov. nominee Doug Mastriano cooperating with Jan. 6 Committee
nbcnews.com
news
2022-06-02 17:39:38

Doug Mastriano, the Republican gubernatorial nominee in Pennsylvania, has provided documents to the House select committee investigating the Jan. 6 attack on the Capitol and has agreed to sit for a voluntary interview, his attorney confirmed to NBC News."We provided everything we found that was not part of his legislative functions," Mastriano's lawyer, Tim Parlatore, told NBC News. "My client has nothing to hide."Parlatore added that Mastriano's interview has yet to be scheduled. His cooperation with the committee probing the Capitol riot was first reported by Politico.
