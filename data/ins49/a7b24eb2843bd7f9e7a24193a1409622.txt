But a long time ago I quit eating any creature prepared by plunging it into boiling water. The old lie that they don't really feel it didn't work on me anymore. Of course they felt pain. Why else did they struggle so hard to climb out of the pot?That magnificent, intelligent, social animals such as whales, including devoted mothers, suffer cruelly for months or years before slowly dying and then sinking, wrapped in polypropylene rope, in order to offer a greater variety to people bored with the bounty already in their supermarkets just confirms my instincts.
