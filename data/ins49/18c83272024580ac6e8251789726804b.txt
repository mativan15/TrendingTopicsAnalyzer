REUTERS/Janine Costa/File PhotoRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comLIMA, May 31 (Reuters) - Peruvian state oil company Petroperu said on Tuesday it would present the long-delayed audit of its 2021 financial statements by September, following approval from its bondholders.Petroperu was supposed to present the paperwork by May of this year but failed to do so when accounting firm PwC declined to carry out the audit in the middle of a corporate governance crisis at the oil company.
