Progressive Conservative candidate Brian Riddell elected in Cambridge
globalnews.ca
news
2022-06-03 03:29:55

Residents of Cambridge elected Progressive Conservative candidate Brian Riddell as its new Member of Provincial Parliament, while also bidding adieu to former MP Belinda Karahalios.
Karahalios was elected as a Progressive Conservative candidate four years ago but was booted from the party in 2018.She would then form the New Blue Party, alongside husband Jim Karahalios, who lost in the riding of Kitchener Hespeler on Thursday evening.
