October 18, 2022 07:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time ISTANBUL--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Turkish mobile games studio Magic Games, on a mission to define a new genre of casual, community-driven multiplayer puzzle games, has raised more than $5 million in seed funding led by Makers Fund, with participation by Firat Ileri, Hummingbird Ventures. Founded by former Peak Games leaders Çağrı Öztürk, Rüya Çelikcan and Mert Kasar, and joined by senior industry leaders Murat Özçelik, Anıl Sert and Orhan Tuç, the team draws from deep experience creating top-grossing mobile puzzle games including Toy Blast, Toon Blast and Star Blast.
