AgenciesFILE PHOTO: A worker harvests grapes at the Domaine Pinson vineyard in Chablis, France, September 21, 2021.Nashik-based Sula Vineyards is likely to file its documents with the market regulator Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) for an initial public offering soon, said banking sources. The IPO size could be in the range of ₹1,200 crore to ₹1,400 crore, they said. The company has appointed Capital, CLSA, and IIFL as the bankers for the upcoming IPO, which will comprise a fresh issue of shares and an offer for sale from some existing investors, said banking sources.
