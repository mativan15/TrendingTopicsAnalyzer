Apple's new software iPadOS 16 likely to turn iPad into a laptop
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 12:45:00

Apple is planning to bring significant changes to the iPad's software in a push to make the device more like a laptop and less like a smartphone.Apple's planning some big updates for the iPad and the announcement will be made at the tech giant's annual event, WWDC 2022, which is all set to kick off next week. According to reports, Apple is planning to bring significant changes to the iPad's software in a push to make the device more like a laptop and less like a smartphone. Things could change for the better with the upcoming iPadOS16 which will introduce new features to make the iPad more compatible for multi-tasking, just like a laptop or a desktop.
