Section 144 imposed in Karnataka's Mandya over VHP rally
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 06:15:00

AgenciesLocal MLA Bharath Shetty demanded an archaeological survey into the matter.In wake of the 'Srirangapatna Chalo' call given by Vishva Hindu Parishad (VHP) for today, Section 144 CrPC has been imposed in the area for twelve hours, between 6:00 AM and 6:00 PM, informed the officials on Saturday. "Over 500 police personnel deployed, 4 check posts installed. The weekly market has been postponed and liquor sale has been banned around a 5-kilometre radius in the area," said S Ashwathi, Deputy Commissioner Police.
