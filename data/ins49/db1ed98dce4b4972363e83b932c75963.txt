Murphy rules out assault weapons ban, new background checks in Senate proposal
nbcnews.com
news
2022-06-05 16:01:59

WASHINGTON — Sen. Chris Murphy, who is helping lead Senate talks on gun control, said lawmakers don't plan to bring any bill to the floor that would ban assault weapons or include comprehensive background checks, but are actively working on legislation that would include a range of other measures. 'We're not going to put a piece of legislation on the table that's going to ban assault weapons, or we're not going to pass comprehensive background checks,' Murphy, D-Conn, said Sunday on CNN's "State of the Union.
