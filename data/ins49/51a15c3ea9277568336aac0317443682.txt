BHU students protest against fee hike, but university denies change
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 06:30:00

Varanasi: While students of Banaras Hindu University BHU ) have been protesting against the fee hike for the last three days, the university claimed that there is no increase in the fee structure.A group of students associated with the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) on Monday expressed their protest against the fee hike by tying black bands on their mouths and also staged demonstration near the Vice-Chancellor's residence on the campus.Meanwhile, the university categorically denied the fee hike.
