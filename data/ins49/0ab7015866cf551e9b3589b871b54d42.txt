Three lessons for Tories from this debacle
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

Let's assume the Conservatives will be out of office at the next election. That's the view of pretty much every Tory MP I know. Some aren't even particularly sad about this. Twelve years in office and one month of Liz Truss have crushed the Tory will to govern.Some dream their spell in opposition will be short. They imagine Sir Keir Starmer, with a small or non-existent majority, struggling with the fiscal hangover this government is likely to leave.Governing Britain will be a dreadful ordeal in the next parliament.
