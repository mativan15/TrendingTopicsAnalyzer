Carlsbad, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- (CARLSBAD, Calif. Oct. 17, 2022) - Although the real estate market is rapidly changing, it does not have to 'be a crisis,' but rather can be an 'incredible opportunity for those who want it,' according to real estate industry expert Brian Buffini, founder and chairman of Buffini & Company. During a recent episode of his popular podcast, 'It's a Good Life,' Buffini interviewed three resident experts at Buffini & Company about their thoughts on the current state of the real estate market.
