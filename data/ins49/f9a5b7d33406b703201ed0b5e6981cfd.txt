BJP and AAP engage in war of words over Satyendar Jain
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 18:45:00

ANIJain, who holds various portfolios, including health, home and power in the Kejriwal government, was arrested by the ED in a case of money laundering on Monday.BJP on Wednesday went all out in attacking Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal after he defended the arrest of his minister Satyendar Jain by the Enforcement Directorate while the AAP claimed that these charges were being brought out now as the Centre was trying to divert attention from the killings of Kashmiri Pandits in the Valley. Union minister Smriti Irani on Wednesday presented details linking Jain to money laundering cases.
