Himachal Pradesh Election: BJP releases list of remaining six candidates

IANSHINDIHimachal Pradesh Elections - BJP released the first list of 62 candidates.The ruling BJP in Himachal Pradesh on Thursday released the list of remaining six candidates for the November 12 assembly polls. Five-time BJP MLA Ravinder Singh Ravi, a confidant of two-time chief minister Prem Kumar Dhumal, who was denied the party ticket, would contest from Jawalamukhi, while sitting MLA from Jawalamukhi Ramesh Dhawala has been given ticket from Dehra and Hoshiyaar Singh, who won as an Independent from Dehra in 2017 assembly polls and joined the BJP, has been denied the ticket.
