 European markets were lower on Friday as political chaos in the U.K. continues following the resignation of Prime Minister Liz Truss. A leadership contest will now take place over the next week. Meanwhile, EU leaders are still debating how to tackle the bloc's energy crisis as they meet in Brussels, after Germany gave the green light for discussions around a price cap.
