Europe's scramble for gas could cause the next energy shortage
cnn.com
energy
2022-05-30 09:44:50

London (CNN Business)Europe's scramble to find alternatives to Russia's natural gas is pushing the world to the brink of a winter energy shortage, with the worst effects likely to be felt in poorer economies in Asia.Since Russia invaded Ukraine, the European Union has tried to reduce its reliance on Moscow for energy. EU countries have been racing to buy more liquefied natural gas (LNG), an attractive alternative to Russian gas because it can be shipped on tankers, rather than delivered via pipelines.
