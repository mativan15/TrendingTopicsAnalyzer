I am the Chief Growth Officer & GM at Lendio Inc, the online marketplace for small business financing. 
getty It's time to rethink 'winning.' The definition itself isn't the problem—to win is to achieve success, to emerge victorious. The problem lies in how winning is so often perceived in the business world as the closing of a deal. For me, securing the deal alone isn't winning. It's just one part of the game. And after negotiating dozens of high-profile partnerships, I realize that sometimes, letting go of a deal is actually the win—particularly when the alternative is winning a deal that just isn't right.
