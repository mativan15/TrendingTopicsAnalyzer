October 21, 2022 05:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time WIXOM, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--XL Fleet Corp. (NYSE: XL) ('XL Fleet' or the 'Company'), a provider of subscription-based services that make it easy for homeowners and small businesses to own and maintain rooftop solar and battery storage, today announced that on October 20, 2022, it received a notice from the New York Stock Exchange ('NYSE'), notifying the Company that it is out of compliance with the NYSE's price criteria for continued listing standards because, as of October 19, 2022, the average closing price of the Company's common stock was less than $1.
