To survive in this economic climate, Democrats must fight monopolies, not flirt with them
thehill.com
news
2022-06-03 15:00:00

The Democrats' only chance for success in this challenging year is to run as the party willing to take on greedy, monopolistic corporations and corporate CEOs, so that we can improve the lives of working and middle-class families. 
Polling and focus groups of working-class voters commissioned over the last couple of months by my organization, American Family Voices, found them cynical about both political parties. They believe that Republicans are in bed with big corporations and their CEOs, and that Democrats are too weak to stand up to them.
