The Hollywood Reporter - Movie News Click here to read the full article.Netflix has ordered up a second season of its breakout Australian YA series Heartbreak High. Produced by Fremantle Australia and NewBe for Netflix, Heartbreak High‘s cast and creators will soon reassemble in Sydney for pre-production and filming.The show is a reimagining of a classic Australian series that ran for seven seasons in the 1990s and was eventually broadcast in over 70 countries.
