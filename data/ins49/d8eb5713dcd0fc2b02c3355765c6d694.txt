Private student loan interest rates soar for both 5- and 10-year loans
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-10-19 18:13:32

During the week of Oct. 10, 2022, average private student loan rates rose significantly for borrowers with credit scores of 720 or higher who used the Credible marketplace to take out 10-year fixed-rate loans and 5-year variable-rate loans. 10-year fixed rate: 7.39%, up from 6.37% the week before, +1.025-year variable rate: 8.87%, up from 7.97% the week before, +0.90Through Credible, you can compare private student loan rates from multiple lenders without affecting your credit score. Private student loan interest rates surged this week for both 10-year fixed-rate loans and 5-year variable-rate loans.
