Take Five: What we've learnt about the cheapest prices of food
stuff.co.nz

2022-06-02 17:00:00

Over the last four days, we have been searching for the cheapest grocery items across Aotearoa. In conversation with readers on our live blog, and via reporting from teams around the country, we've looked at everything from the price of cheese to how much we are paying for toilet paper. Stats NZ's Food Price Index shows the cost of food has risen by 6.4% compared to this time last year, and there is no sign of an end to the climb. Here's what we've found in our hunt for the cheapest day-to-day grocery items.
