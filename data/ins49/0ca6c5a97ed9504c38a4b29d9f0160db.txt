Loved ones of Maurice Cardinal and Jacob Sansom want them remembered for who they were – playful and supportive family men – and not how they died. On Tuesday, two people were found guilty in the deaths of both Metis men, who were shot and killed in rural Alberta in 2020. Cardinal and Sansom had set out on a hunting trip to harvest moose for their families. On Wednesday, Sansom's widow Sarah opened up to CTV News Edmonton about the night before he died. "Jake was laying down, I was sitting in my rocking chair, and the three kids were sitting on him.
