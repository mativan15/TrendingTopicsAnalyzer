Josef Mohyla/E+ via Getty Images Buying into a company on the cheap is a great way to generate attractive returns, especially if you are patient enough to wait out volatility. But there is a big difference between buying shares at a discount and buying shares in a company that deserves to trade at a discount. In the first case, you are acquiring stock in something that is fundamentally undervalued. And in the second, you are purchasing stock in a company that is being punished by the market because of a lack of consistency in performance or just overall bad performance.
