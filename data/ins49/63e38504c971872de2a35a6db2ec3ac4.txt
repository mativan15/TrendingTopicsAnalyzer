Anatoly Morozov/iStock via Getty Images Controversy over allegations of forced labor in Xinjiang, China almost brought Daqo New Energy (NYSE:DQ) on the verge of potential US sanctions in 2021. The company manufactures and sells polysilicon to photovoltaic factories in China and around the world. The solar industry has grown over the past half-decade as China rushes to achieve carbon-neutrality before 2060. China plans to add at least 570 GW of wind and solar power from 2021 to 2025 in its 14th five-year plan.
