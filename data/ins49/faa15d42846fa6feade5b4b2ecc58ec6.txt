English French MONTREAL, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Dominion Water Reserves Corp. ('Dominion Water' or the 'Corporation') (CSE: DWR), announced today that its official corporate name will change to 'Prime Drink Group Corp' on the Canadian Securities Exchange and on other corporate materials to reflect the Corporation's intent of diversifying its investment portfolio beyond the water industry. Following receipt of all regulatory approvals, the Corporation expects that its common shares will commence trading under its new name and new stock symbol 'PRME' on or about November 21, 2022.
