Over 2 Lakh Expected In Vcci Expo
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 07:45:00

VADODARA: The Vadodara Chamber of Commerce and Industry (VCCI) will organize its 11th national industrial exhibition, VCCI Expo 2023 , from January 23 to January 30, 2023 at Navlakhi Palace ground.Himanshu Patel , senior vice-president of VCCI and chairman of VCCI Expo 2023, told reporters on Saturday that two lakh representatives from various industries will be visiting the expo.The expo is being organised with support of the Ministry of Micro Small & Medium Enterprise, Government of India, NSIC and Industries Commissionerate of Gujarat government.
