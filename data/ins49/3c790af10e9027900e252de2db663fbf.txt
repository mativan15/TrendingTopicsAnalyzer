Published: 00:55 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 00:55 EDT, 2 June 2022 Senior management at the Star Sydney were seemingly in the dark about the casino's ongoing operations with a controversial junket operator. Lawyers assisting the NSW gaming authority inquiry are closing their case after examining claims that ASX-listed Star Entertainment enabled suspected money laundering, organised crime, fraud and foreign interference at its Sydney casino. The inquiry heard the relationship with junket operator Kuan Koi continued despite the board being warned the company had not been able to verify whether it was following the law.
