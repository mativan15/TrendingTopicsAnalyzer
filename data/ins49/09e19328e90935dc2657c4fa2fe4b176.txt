Design Core Detroit Restructured, New Leadership Announced
union-bulletin.com
news
2022-05-31 14:34:34

Detroit, Michigan, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Design Core Detroit announced that Bonnie Fahoome and Kiana Wenzell have been promoted to Co-Executive Directors. They succeed Olga Stella who has served in that role since 2016, Stella will continue to provide oversight to the organization in her role as Vice President of Strategy and Communication at the College for Creative Studies (CCS), of which Design Core is a part. Fahoome and Wenzell have both held leadership positions in the organization for several years.
