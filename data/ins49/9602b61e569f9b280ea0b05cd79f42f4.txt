AgenciesGoyal and Sitharaman have been given another term from the same slot they were vacating in Maharashtra and Karnataka, respectively.Former UP BJP chief Laxmikant Bajpai and Radhamohan Agarwal, the Gorakhpur Sadar MLA who was replaced by chief minister Yogi Adityanath as candidate in the recent assembly elections, were declared nominees by the party for the Rajya Sabha elections. Union ministers Nirmala Sitharaman (Karnataka) and Piyush Goyal (Maharashtra) figure in the BJP list of 16 announced on Sunday.
