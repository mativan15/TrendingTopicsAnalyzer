First, our focus has decidedly shifted to enhancing our economy's productivity. Traditionally, we were known as low productivity economy. In recent years, we have leveraged digitisation to increase productivity significantly. Some of the critical areas for example are:

Almost 10% of the total retail commerce has moved to ecommerce in less than a decade

Use of AI is expected to improve agriculture yield by at least 15%

In the logistics sector, which contributes app 5% of our GDP, we are working to reduce logistics costs by nearly half by using various technology interventions.
