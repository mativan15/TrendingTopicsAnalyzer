Have you ever thought about starting a career in content creation? If you have, then the question comes, which one should I do? A blog? Or a Vlog? There are many factors to consider in comparing a blog vs. a vlog. And perhaps the most important one is: which one earns more money? A blog or a vlog? However, there are other considerations besides money. 
You should see which one suits your skill set better. Which one do you find the easiest? After all, both blogging and vlogging are full-time jobs and take up a large portion of your time.
