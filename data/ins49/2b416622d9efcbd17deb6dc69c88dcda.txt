Shanghai will ease COVID restrictions on 1 June. Image: MicrosoftChina's commercial capital, Shanghai, will this week ease pandemic restrictions that have hampered production at key suppliers of US tech giants.The city's municipal government announced that residents in areas deemed low risk will be free to move within the city, use public transport and drive on roads from midnight on June 1.Tesla, Apple and Cisco are among US firms that have been caught out by China's COVID-19 restrictions under its controversial zero-COVID policy.
