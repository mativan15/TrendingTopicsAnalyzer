AgenciesAt least 40 workers are dead in a coal mine explosion in Turkey. The rescue operation is still underway to recover the dozens trapped inside the mine, according to reports. TTK Amasra Muessese Mudurlugu mine, where the explosion happened on Friday evening, is situated in the Bartin coastal province of the Black Sea. It is a state-owned mine where 110 miners work. Interior Minister Suleyman Soylu conveyed on Saturday that the total number of confirmed deaths was 40, and the number of injured and hospitalised miners was 11.
