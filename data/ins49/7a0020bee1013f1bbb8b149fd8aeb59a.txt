Utah Jazz Sign Tyler Cook, Frank Jackson, and Isaiah Miller
nba.com
news
2022-10-15 21:31:01

The Utah Jazz have signed forward Tyler Cook, and guards Frank Jackson and Isaiah Miller. Per team policy, terms of the deal were not released.Cook (6-8, 255, Iowa) is entering his fourth NBA season, owning career averages of 3.8 points on 65.4 percent from the field, and 2.5 rebounds in 65 games (three starts) with Cleveland, Denver, Detroit, Brooklyn, and Chicago. In 2021-22 he played in 20 games (two starts) with the Bulls, averaging 3.4 points and 2.7 rebounds in 10.0 minutes per contest. He also appeared in 17 games (16 starts) with the Windy City Bulls of the NBA G League, averaging 17.
