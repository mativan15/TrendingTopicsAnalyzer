He uses all forms of digital research, verification tools and methods of forensic analysis to expose manipulation and deception. In fact checks for social media, TV and online, Joscha Weber and his team present the verification of current topics - often with the support of DW's specialist and regional editors. He is also a trainer for fact-checking and digital research techniques. He previously worked as a freelance journalist for WDR and dpa, among others, while studying communication, politics and history in Germany and France.
