By Wang Diankai | China Daily | Updated: 2022-05-31 07:28 [MA XUEJING/CHINA DAILY] The central authorities' recent official document to accelerate the construction of a national unified market said it is necessary to expedite the establishment of a unified national market system and rules, eliminate protectionism and market segmentation, clear the bottlenecks restricting the economic cycle, promote the smooth flow of commodity factors and resources on a larger scale, and accelerate the construction of an efficient, standardized, fair, and fully open national unified market.
