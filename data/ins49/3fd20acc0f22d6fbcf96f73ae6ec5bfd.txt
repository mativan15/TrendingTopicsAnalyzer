FORT COLLINS, CO / ACCESSWIRE / October 17, 2022 / Statera Biopharma, Inc. (NASDAQ:STAB) (the "Company" or "Statera Biopharma"), a biopharmaceutical company creating next-generation immune therapies that focus on immune restoration and homeostasis, today announced the signing of a binding Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") with Holobeam Technologies, Inc. ("Holobeam"). Holobeam is the world leader in creating the next generation of holographic medical imaging and therapeutic technologies for the early detection and treatment of cancer and other diseases using their newly patented Holographic Energy Teleportation (HET) technology.
