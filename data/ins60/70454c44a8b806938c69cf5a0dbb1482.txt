Payal Rajput, who works predominantly in Telugu and Punjabi films, credited Kareena Kapoor for getting her many ads and even her first show. The actor said she's used Kareena's ‘Sikhni hoon mein Bhatinda ki' monologue in many auditions. Payal will soon be seen in the upcoming Telugu film Ginna, scheduled to be released on October 21. The film also features Vishnu Manchu and Sunny Leone.
