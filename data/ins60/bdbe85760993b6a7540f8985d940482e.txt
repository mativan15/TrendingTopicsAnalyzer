The trial of Guy Sebastian's ex-manager has been stopped in its tracks again after another juror became ill.Titus Day, 49, is on trial in the NSW District Court, accused of fraudulently embezzling about $900,000 in payments that the singer was allegedly due to be paid on 50 occasions.When the jury returned to continue the trial on Monday, Judge Tim Gartelmann SC said they would be sent home again because one of them was missing.Guy Sebastian and Titus Day parted ways in 2017 after a bitter relationship breakdown.
