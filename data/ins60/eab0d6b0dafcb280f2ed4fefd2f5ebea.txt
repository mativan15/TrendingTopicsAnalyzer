Italy's first female PM officially appointed
europesun.com
news
2022-10-21 22:30:00

Giorgia Meloni has now been tasked by the country's president to form new cabinetItaly's President Sergio Mattarella on Friday gave the country's new Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni the mandate to form a government. The new cabinet is expected to be sworn in on Saturday.The new coalition government is set to be formed by several right-wing parties, including Meloni's Brothers of Italy (FdI), Matteo Salvini's League party and the Forza Italia party, led by the veteran politician and the country's ex-PM Silvio Berlusconi.
