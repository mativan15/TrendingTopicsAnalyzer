Beyond the uncertainty surrounding COVID, Chinese companies listed in the U.S. face the risk of delisting over whether oversight is granted to an American accounting regulator. JACK STONE TRUITT, Nikkei staff writerJune 1, 2022 06:54 JST | ChinaNEW YORK -- A week after a vote on the most prominent delisting yet of a Chinese company on an American exchange, shares in China's tech sector continue to rally as investors appear to be bullish on Chinese equities following months of uncertainty over regulation and COVID-19 lockdowns.
