ET Canada Anyone who's ever gazed with envy at the flawless complexion of Jared Leto can now enjoy his skincare secrets, thanks to the recent launch of Twentynine Palms, the 'House of Gucci' star's new beauty line.Speaking with Vogue, the 50-year-old actor opened up about why he decided to launch his own beauty brand, an 11-piece line of gender-neutral products for the face, body and hair.'I know I'm a student here, but I think that's the best place to be,' he explained.
