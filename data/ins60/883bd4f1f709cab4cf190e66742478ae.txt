HERNDON, Va., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Beacon (Nasdaq: BECN) (the "Company") announced today that its earnings results for the quarter ended September 30, 2022 will be released after market close on Thursday, November 3, 2022. The Company will host a conference call and webcast to discuss its financial results at 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time that same day.
