Source: ANI

The beneficiaries have started receiving amount under Dalit Bandhu Scheme in Hyderabad of Telangana. The scheme aims to empower Dalits and lift them out of poverty. As an effective policy, the Dalit Bandhu scheme aims to provide employment, self-esteem, and development to Dalits. No bank linkage and collateral security are required to start their business. For starting any business activity, a sum of Rs 10,00,000 is transferred into the beneficiary's account.
