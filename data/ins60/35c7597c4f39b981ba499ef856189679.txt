Billionaire American technology entrepreneur Peter Thiel was born in Germany and holds US and New Zealand passports. Photo / Getty ImagesObtaining citizenship in Malta would provide another passport for Peter Thiel, who is one of the largest individual donors for the US midterm elections. At the end of a narrow road, past crushed beer cans and the remnants of a chain-link fence, a weathered sandstone building overlooks the Mediterranean coast. The British tourist who answered the door of a third-floor apartment had no idea she was staying at the residence of one of the world's richest men.
