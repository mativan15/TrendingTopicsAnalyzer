May 31, 2022 06:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BitNile Holdings, Inc. (NYSE American: NILE), a diversified holding company ('BitNile' or the 'Company'), announced today the pricing of its public offering of 123,423 shares of its 13.00% Series D Cumulative Redeemable Perpetual Preferred Stock (the "Series D Preferred Stock") at a price to the public of $25.00 per share. Gross proceeds from the offering are expected to be approximately $3.08 million, before deducting offering expenses.
