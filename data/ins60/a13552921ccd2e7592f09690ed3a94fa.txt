SAN DIEGO, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The Shareholders Foundation, Inc. announced that a deadline is coming up on June 13, 2022, in the lawsuit that was filed for certain investors in NASDAQ: SDIG shares. Investors with losses more than $100,000 in shares of Stronghold Digital Mining, Inc. (NASDAQ: SDIG) have certain options and there are short and strict deadlines running. Deadline: June 13, 2022. NASDAQ: SDIG investors should contact the Shareholders Foundation at mail@shareholdersfoundation.
