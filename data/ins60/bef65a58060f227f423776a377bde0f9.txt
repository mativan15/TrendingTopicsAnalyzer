GURNEE, Ill. (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 BrightStar Care, the leading nationwide home care and medical staffing franchise known for providing the highest standard of quality in-home care, announced today the acquisition of four new BrightStar Owned (BSO) locations bringing its number of company-owned agencies to 20. The latest four locations added to the BrightStar Owned umbrella are located in Arizona and South Carolina, bringing the brand's company-owned footprint coast to coast. BrightStar Owned acquired these four locations from former BrightStar Care franchisees Andy Ray and Frank Sanders.
