In the wake of some of its spokespersons and leaders speaking against Prophet Mohammad and making communal statements, BJP issued a statement on Sunday stating that the party respects all religions and does not support remarks against any religion or religious leader. In a statement issued by BJP General Secretary Arun Singh, the party said, 'During the thousands of years of the history of India every religion has blossomed and flourished. BJP respects all religions. BJP strongly denounces insult of any religious personalities of any religion.
