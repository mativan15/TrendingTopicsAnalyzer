Nvidia (NVDA -0.74%) posted its first-quarter earnings report on May 25. The chipmaker's revenue rose 46% year over year to $8.29 billion, which beat analysts' expectations by $190 million. Its adjusted net income increased 49% to $3.44 billion, or $1.36 per share, which also cleared the consensus forecast by seven cents.
Nvidia's stock price advanced slightly after that big earnings beat, but it's still down about 36% for the year. Is it too late to buy Nvidia as a turnaround play in this turbulent market?
Why the market turned against Nvidia
