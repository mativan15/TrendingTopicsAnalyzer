More Video Richard Henderson and Georgina Mckay, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- More of Australia's largest businesses are using excess capital from high commodity prices and a dearth of mergers and acquisitions to snap up their own shares.So far this year, 40 companies in the S&P/ASX 300 Index have announced share buybacks, marking a 74% jump from 2021, according to data from UBS Group AG. The increasing breadth of repurchases reflects extra profits from mining and energy firms, which are among the winners from surging commodity prices resulting from the war in Ukraine.
