Margaret Thatcher statue unveiled in her home town days after memorial was vandalised by protesters
msn.com
news
2022-05-31 18:52:13

A statue of Baroness Margaret Thatcher has been officially unveiled in her home town days after it was vandalised with paint and egged by protesters.The £300,000 memorial to the former Prime Minister had been guarded by fences prior to its official opening in Grantham, Lincolnshire, today. It was lowered onto a 10ft-high granite plinth on May 15, and in the two weeks since has been targeted by vandals.
