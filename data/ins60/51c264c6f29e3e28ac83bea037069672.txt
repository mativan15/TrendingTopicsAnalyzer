Americans Lost $1 Billion to Crypto Scams Last Year, Feds Say
gizmodo.com
business
2022-06-04 11:30:00

Investing in Web3, the supposedly 'transformative' environment of blockchain and cryptocurrency technologies, continues to be a revolutionary new way to lose a whole lot of money. A study from the Federal Trade Commission released Friday shows that Americans have lost over $1 billion to crypto-related scams since January 2021. In raw numbers, that's over 46,000 people who say they have been ripped off—usually with the promise of a fake 'investment opportunity' in some sort of Web3-related nonsense.
