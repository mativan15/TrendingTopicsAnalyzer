NEW YORK (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 The Software Report is pleased to announce The Top 50 SaaS CEOs of 2022. The leaders on this year's list represent companies of every size, from those with thousands (or 10,000+) employees, to those with fewer than 50. They also run the gamut from enterprise resource planning software to design and publishing, hospitality management, and transportation and logistics. What these awardees have in common is their expert leadership of some of the most transformative, impactful SaaS companies in the industry.
