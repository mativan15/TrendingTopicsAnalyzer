Pensions: Liz Truss commits to triple lock after concern from Tory MPs
bbc.com
news
2022-10-19 15:57:50

Media caption, Liz Truss: I am completely committed to the triple lock on pensionsLiz Truss has said she is committed to increasing pensions in line with prices, after some Tory MPs raised concerns the pledge could be ditched. On Tuesday, the PM's spokesman said she was "not making any commitments", when asked about the triple lock pledge. The triple lock means state pension payments rise by whatever is higher - inflation, average earnings or 2.5%.It came after one Tory MP said pensioners should not be "paying the price for the cost-of-living crisis".
