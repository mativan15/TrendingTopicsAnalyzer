Tennessee has become college football's best story under Josh Heupel
nypost.com
news
2022-10-16 16:09:28

The signs were there from the beginning, all the way back to the start of last season.
Tennessee began putting up big offensive numbers, numbers that hadn't been seen recently in Knoxville. The Volunteers went from the 109th-ranked scoring offense in 2020 to No. 7 last year. They had a four-win turnaround and reached a bowl game for just the second time in five years.
It was obvious then that Tennessee had found a coach after misfiring so many times going back to the days when Phillip Fulmer strode the sideline.
