More upside in RIL, HDFC Life; Ultratech may see more weakness: Santosh Meena
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 07:45:00

Benchmark equity indices ended the week on a positive note, thanks to buying during the early half of the week. However, the gains were cut short by reversal on Friday as traders dumped financial stocks ahead of the RBI meeting. It was the third consecutive week of gain for the Indian equity market, led by the recovery in global markets, eased down US bond yields and mild drops in the dollar index. On the other hand, regular profit booking limits the gains. Santosh Meena, Head of Research, , said value buying in some beaten-down heavyweights by domestic investors and short covering also helped the market to move higher.
