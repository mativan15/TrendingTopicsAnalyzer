At the MarketWatch Best New Ideas in Money Festival on Sept. 21, Michael Saylor renewed his epic projections for Bitcoin. MicroStrategy's founder and executive chairman predicted that the flagship cryptocurrency would regain its peak of almost $70,000 within four years and hit $500,000 within a decade en route to 'go[ing] to the value of gold.' It's no wonder the flamboyant promoter is talking his book.
