Kashmiri Pandit killed by militants in Shopian

AgenciesInitial probe suggested that one militant was involved in the attack, Kumar said.Unidentified gunmen killed a Kashmiri pandit, Puran Krishan Bhat, in Shopian district of southern Kashmir on Saturday morning. The Jammu and Kashmir police said one militant was involved in the incident and a proxy militant outfit, Kashmir Freedom Fighters has claimed responsibility of the attack.

The gunmen opened fire at Bhat at his residence in Choudhry Gundh area of Shopian. He was immediately shifted to hospital where he succumbed to injuries.
