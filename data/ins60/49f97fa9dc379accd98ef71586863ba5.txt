Abhishek Bachchan, Amit Sadh, Nithya Menen, Sayami Kher, and Ivana Kaur reprise their roles in the new season, while Naveen Kasturia is the new addition to the cast

The second season of the Abhishek Bachchan-starrer Breathe: Into the Shadows will arrive on Prime Video on November 9, the streamer said on Monday.

Besides Abhishek, the new chapter of the psychological thriller series will see Amit Sadh, Nithya Menen, Sayami Kher, and Ivana Kaur reprise their respective roles.
