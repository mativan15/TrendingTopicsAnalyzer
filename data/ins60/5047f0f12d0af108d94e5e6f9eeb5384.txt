In tight Oregon governor's race, GOP's Drazan attacks Democrat Kotek in new ad
nbcnews.com
news
2022-10-17 14:30:00

Christine Drazan, the Republican nominee in the tight three-way race for governor in Oregon, is ratcheting up attacks on Democrat Tina Kotek on homelessness and education in a new ad that will launch later Monday.The 30-second ad, shared first with NBC News ahead of its release, will run statewide on television and on digital. It is part of a weekly ad buy for more than $1 million, Drazan campaign spokesperson John Burke said.'Leaders like Tina Kotek and Kate Brown have let us down,' Drazan says in the ad in a direct-to-camera appeal.
