No Fine For Breaking Traffic Rules During Diwali, Says Gujarat Government

2022-10-22 07:15:00

Gujarat announced there will not be any fine for violating traffic rules this Diwali (Representational)Surat, Gujarat: The Gujarat government has announced that people will not be fined for violation of traffic rules during the Diwali festival this year.Gujarat Minister of State for Home Harsh Sanghavi made this announcement in Surat on Friday.Addressing a gathering, he said that in view of the Diwali festival, traffic police in Gujarat will not collect any fine from the violators till October 27.
