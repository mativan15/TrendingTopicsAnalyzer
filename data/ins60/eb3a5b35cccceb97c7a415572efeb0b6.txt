Don't worry. This is not another column fretting about the politics of our soccer team's post-match singsong. Nobody here will be asking the women's team to flay themselves with sharpened poppies from now until Remembrance Sunday. But the story does remind us how rapidly any perceived offence can now get halfway around the world. We have technology to blame. We have ourselves to blame. One yearns to describe the current craze for self-surveillance as 'Orwellian', but that author never imagined anything so unlikely.
