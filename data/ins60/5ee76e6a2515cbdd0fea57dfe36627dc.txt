LMF Acquisition Opportunities, Inc. Stockholders Approve Previously Announced Business Combination with SeaStar Medical, Inc.

DENVER and TAMPA, Fla., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- LMF Acquisition Opportunities, Inc. (NASDAQ: LMAO) (LMAO), a special purpose acquisition company sponsored by LM Funding America, Inc. (NASDAQ: LMFA), and SeaStar Medical, Inc., a medical technology company developing proprietary solutions to reduce the consequences of hyperinflammation on vital organs, today announced that LMAO stockholders voted to approve the previously proposed business combination with SeaStar Medical at LMAO's special meeting of LMAO stockholders (the 'Special Meeting') held earlier today, October 18, 2022.
