A senior Tory has accused the government of looking "like libertarian jihadists" and treating the country as "laboratory mice" over the past few weeks.Robert Halfon, former deputy chairman of the Conservative Party and an education minister under Theresa May, said he believes Liz Truss needs to apologise to the public for the economic turmoil caused by the mini-budget three weeks ago.
He told Sky News' Sophy Ridge on Sunday programme: "I worry that over the past few weeks the government has looked like libertarian jihadists and treated the whole country as laboratory mice on which to carry out an ultra-free market experiment.
