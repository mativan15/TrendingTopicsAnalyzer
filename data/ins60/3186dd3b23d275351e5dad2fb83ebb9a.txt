WALLA WALLA, Wash., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Wilkinson Baking Company ("WBC"), creator of the world's first fully-automated commercial bread baking robot, announced the close of its fully subscribed $3.0m seed round. The round was led by prominent Washington investor Ken Peterson of Columbia Ventures Corporation ("CVC"), along with Rich Product Ventures ("RPV").
