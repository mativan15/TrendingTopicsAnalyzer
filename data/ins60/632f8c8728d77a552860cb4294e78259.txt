Best Tampa Bay Buccaneer CoverageBy Jason BarringerOct 18, 2022VIA TAMPA BAY BUCCANEERS COMMUNICATIONS The Tampa Bay Buccaneers today placed safety Logan Ryan on Injured Reserve and released tackle Justin Skule from the practice squad. Ryan has appeared in four games for Tampa Bay this season, making two starts, recording nine tackles, two passes defensed, one interception, one forced fumble and one fumble recovery. He is one of just four players through Week 6 with an interception, forced fumble and fumble recovery.
