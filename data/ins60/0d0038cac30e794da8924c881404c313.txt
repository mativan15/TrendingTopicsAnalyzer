Residents report tremors as earthquake hits Shropshire
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 16:01:20

The British Geological Survey confirmed an earthquake on Monday afternoon near Shrewsbury, Shropshire (BGS)

People in Shropshire reported feeling the effects of a "sizeable" earthquake on Monday afternoon.

The tremor was felt around 3.35pm and was preceded by a rumbling sound.

Residents in the villages of Prees and Wem, as well as the city of Shrewsbury, Shropshire, posted on Twitter after feeling movement beneath the ground.

The British Geological Survey (BSG) confirmed the earthquake measured a magnitude of 3.
