Mayank Prajapati, Hemanth Kommu to represent India at Global Esports Games 2022
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-18 08:53:25

Top Street Fighter V athlete Mayank Prajapati and reigning eFootball national champion Hemanth Kommu will represent India in the world finals of the Global Esports Games 2022, scheduled to take place in Istanbul from December 14 to 18.Reigning national champions Mayank and Hemanth have received direct entries for the prestigious event. India's DOTA2 team, on the other hand, couldn't make it to the world finals after they crashed out of the Asia-Pacific region qualifiers.Mayank, popularly known as MiKeYROG, clinched the title of National Esports Championships (NESC) 2022, conducted by Esports Federation of India (ESFI) in April this year and will also represent India at the Asian Games which is scheduled next year.
