Honda Aircraft introduces new Elite II model and avionics enhancements
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 11:39:00

Honda Aircraft Company, based at Piedmont-Triad International Airport, introduced a new model of aircraft on Monday, the HondaJet Elite II, which the company says is the fastest, highest and farthest-flying aircraft in its class.

Key new features include a range of 1,547 nautical miles, increased fuel capacity, increased gross weight to a maximum takeoff limit of 11,100 pounds, and new ground spoiler to optimize takeoff and landing performance.

Honda Aircraft (NYSE: HMC) also announced rollout in 2023 of two major avionics features, Autothrottle and Autoland, which will be available next year on the Elite II.
