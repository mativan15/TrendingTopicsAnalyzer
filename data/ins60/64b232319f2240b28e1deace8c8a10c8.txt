IOG Shuffles Management, Rupert Newall to Replace Andrew Hockey as CEO
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 08:30:00

By Joe Hoppe IOG PLC said Thursday that it has reshuffled its board, taking on a new chief executive officer, chief operating officer and chief financial officer. The energy company said CEO Andrew Hockey will step down and retire with immediate effect, with Rupert Newall taking over the role. David Gibson will step down as chief operating officer with Dougie Scott taking over that position subject to due diligence, and John Arthur has been named chief financial officer, a non-board position.
