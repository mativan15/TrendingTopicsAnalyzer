‘Unprecedented' 30m euro package aims to give postmasters certainty
msn.com
finance
2022-06-01 19:00:00

A 'one-off' Government funding package for the post office network worth 30 million euro over three years was announced on Wednesday. 
 

 © Niall Carson Minister of State for International and Road Transport and Logistics, Hildegarde Naughton (Niall Carson/PA)


The minister of state with responsibility for the sector hailed the 'hard-fought for' package and appealed to the public to use their post office more often.This is the first time an Irish Government has provided direct financial support for postmasters.
