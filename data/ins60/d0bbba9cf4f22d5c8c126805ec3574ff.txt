October 18, 2022 04:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time GULFPORT, Miss.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hancock Whitney Corporation (Nasdaq: HWC) today announced its financial results for the third quarter of 2022. Net income for the third quarter of 2022 totaled $135.4 million, or $1.55 per diluted common share (EPS), compared to $121.4 million, or $1.38 per diluted common share, in the second quarter of 2022. The company reported net income for the third quarter of 2021 of $129.6 million, or $1.46 per diluted common share.
