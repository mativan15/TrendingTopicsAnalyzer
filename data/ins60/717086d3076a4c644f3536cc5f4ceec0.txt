Why remote work will lead to greater success

Environment affects modes of work. And that's why the recent shift to remote work is so consequential.Until the COVID-19 pandemic arrived in 2020, nearly all the conversations about office design centered around collaboration. This was especially true in the tech industry. Companies (ranging from scrappy startups to industry giants like Apple, Google, and Facebook) innovated with casual meeting spaces, extensive break areas, and open office plans.Collaboration was king. And then COVID-19 happened.
