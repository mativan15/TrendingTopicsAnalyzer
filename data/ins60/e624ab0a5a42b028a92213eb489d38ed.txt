US imposes sanctions on luxury yacht firm linked to Putin
theguardian.com
sport
2022-06-02 18:11:30

The US government has imposed sanctions on a close Putin associate as well as a yacht management firm and its owners, describing them as part of a system that allows Russian president Vladimir Putin and his elite to 'move money and anonymously make use of luxury assets around the globe'.In a statement, the treasury department said that it was sanctioning the Monaco-based Imperial Yachts, and its owner, Moscow-born Evgeniy Kochman.According to the treasury department, Kochman's company 'provides yacht-related services to Russia's elites, including those in President Putin's inner circle'.
