Company continues to deliver the best experience and lowest rates in the industry

AUSTIN, Texas, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Auctane, the leader in shipping and fulfillment software solutions that operates trusted brands such as ShipStation, Stamps.com, ShippingEasy, ShipEngine, ShipWorks, Metapack, and Endicia, today announced a newly expanded enterprise-wide agreement with the United States Postal Service (USPS). Through the updated agreement, which encompasses Auctane's entire family of brands, the company will continue to deliver the lowest rates in the industry and further expand its savings and benefits to its customers.
