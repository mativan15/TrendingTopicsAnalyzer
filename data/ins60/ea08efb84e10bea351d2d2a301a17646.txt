Updated: 04 Jun 2022, 12:18 PM ISTGold is a valuable investment that comes in useful when cash is needed quickly. Indians connect with gold with a deep sense of personal belonging, leading them to accumulate jewellery, coins, and bars made of it. Family-owned gold assets are frequently pledged to banks and formal money lending institutions when cash is needed immediately. Until the loan is fully returned, the gold acts as collateral with the bank.The gold loan amount and interest rate vary with the bank and gold depending on tenure.
