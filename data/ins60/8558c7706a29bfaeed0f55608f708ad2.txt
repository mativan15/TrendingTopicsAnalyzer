Christa CooperJun 1, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesAre you one of those people who can't watch a movie at a theater WITHOUT ordering popcorn? Well, brace yourself. The "Wall Street Journal" reports that movie theater chains are worried that "supply chain issues" may lead to a POPCORN SHORTAGE later this summer. An executive from a popcorn supplier says "Supply will be tight" . . . partially because they're having to pay farmers more to keep growing popcorn, rather than switching to more lucrative crops like soybeans.
