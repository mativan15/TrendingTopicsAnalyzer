Published: 10:47 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 10:51 EDT, 4 June 2022 A freight company manager is at his breaking point after being forced to spend an extra $25,000 per month on petrol then he was at the start of the year amid rising fuel costs.Tristan Wernhard runs AA Plus Driving Training and Transport, which utilises a fleet of six trucks to service NSW, South Australia, Queensland and Victoria. The Western Sydney-based truck driver is now fearing he'll have to shut down his business altogether as he battles to keep up with the cost of fuel and tolls.
