Kevin Durant to produce 'NYC Point Gods' documentary
usatoday.com
news
2022-06-02 11:58:47

Kevin Durant is set to produce a documentary analyzing the impact of New York City point guards in the 1980s and 90s.
The documentary will be focused on New York City high school and playground icons such as Rafer Alston, Kenny Anderson, Mark Jackson, Stephon Marbury, God Shammgod, Kenny Smith, Rod Strickland, and Dwayne 'Pearl' Washington.
