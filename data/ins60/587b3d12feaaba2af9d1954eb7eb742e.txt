The booming economy, not the 2017 tax act, is fueling corporate tax receipts
brookings.edu
news
2022-06-03 13:30:08

Corporate tax revenues boomed in 2021 and some supporters of the 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA) argue that the big tax reductions in the bill deserve the credit (Wall Street Journal, Goodspeed and Hassett). But there is a much better explanation: Last year's strong economic growth, high inflation, and pandemic-related relief legislation increased both corporate profits and the taxes business paid.
