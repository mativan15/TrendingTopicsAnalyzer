"Bad precedent": Dad applauded for not covering wife's part of college fund
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-03 00:45:39

Members of a popular internet forum were quick to support one father who said he refused to cover for his wife after she was unable to contribute to their children's college fund.In a viral Reddit post published on r/AmITheA**hole, Redditor u/financialwoes11 (otherwise referred to as the original poster, or OP) said he and his wife each contribute $200 per month to college funds for their two kids and revealed how a recent shopping spree put one half of that monthly contribution in jeopardy.Titled, "[Am I the a**hole] for not contributing my wife's half to our kid's college savings account?" the post has received nearly 5,000 votes and 1,000 comments in the last day.
