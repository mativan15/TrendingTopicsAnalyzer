Long period of slavery, indifference made us forget our maritime heritage: PM Modi

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on October 18 said the "long period of slavery" and indifference made us forget or overlook India's rich maritime history.Speaking after reviewing, through video conference, the construction of the National Maritime Heritage Complex at Lothal in Gujarat's Ahmedabad district, he said it will act as a centre for learning this history.The complex at Lothal represents "our resolve to celebrate India's rich maritime history," Mr. Modi tweeted later."Centuries ago, India's trade was spread over a large part of the globe.
