MicroStockHub/E+ via Getty Images Originally posted on May 24 Bitcoin has been relatively flat in the past couple of weeks in the wake of the TerraUSD (UST-USD) blowup. That is also in spite of further declines for equities which have been highly correlated to cryptos lately, especially on an intraday basis. As the two largest cryptos - Bitcoin and Ethereum - have traded more or less sideways, speculators appear to be getting a little more bullish. In Monday night's Closer we highlighted how positioning data from the CFTC's Commitments of Traders report showed speculators are positioned net long (meaning there is a higher share of open interest that are long the future than short) in Ethereum and Bitcoin futures.
