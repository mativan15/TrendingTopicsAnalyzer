MEDFORD, Ore., October 21, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PBCO Financial Corporation (OTC PINK: PBCO), the holding company (Company) of People's Bank of Commerce (Bank), announced today its financial results for the 3rd quarter 2022.

Highlights

Third quarter net income of $3.0 million, or $0.59 per diluted share

Steelhead gross factoring revenue of $1.9 million, a 6.2% increase from Q3 2021

Core earnings (excluding PPP fee income) up 73.9% from Q3 2021

Investment securities increased $42.6 million, or 20.
