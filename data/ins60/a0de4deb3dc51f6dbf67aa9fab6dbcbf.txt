(Edward Berthelot / GC Images)If there's one business that Kanye West would be most upset to leave his orbit, it's likely Balenciaga.The high-fashion firm and its creative director Demna have long collaborated with and inspired West in his apparel-industry ventures, including a cosign on West's now-scuttled deal with retail apparel giant the Gap and a slot for West to walk the runway in Balenciaga's Paris Fashion Week show this month.
