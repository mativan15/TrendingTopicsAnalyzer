First-time Chain Snatcher Breaks Legs
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 05:45:00

COIMBATORE: A debt-ridden Vinayagapuram resident, who resorted to chain snatching to solve his financial problems, broke his legs in the maiden attempt.The man, identified as Muthu Balaji, 34, used to work as a lathe operator, police said. After losing some hard-earned money in the stock market, he took a loan to buy more shares and lost that also. As he struggled to make ends meet, his wife left for her parents' home with their three-year-old daughter.On Thursday night, Muthu snatched a two-sovereign gold chain from Kanchana, 60, of Amman Nagar at Vinayagapuram.
