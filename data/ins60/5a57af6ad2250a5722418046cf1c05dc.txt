Stock Futures Slip, Oil Prices Jump
wsj.com
news
2022-05-31 07:39:00

Photo: Courtney Crow/Zuma Press May 31, 2022 3:39 am ET 
 U.S. stock futures edged down and crude prices extended recent gains after the European Union pledged to curb its purchases of oil from Russia. S&P 500 futures fell 0.3% and Dow Jones Industrial Average futures declined 0.3%. The contracts don't necessarily predict movements after the markets open.
