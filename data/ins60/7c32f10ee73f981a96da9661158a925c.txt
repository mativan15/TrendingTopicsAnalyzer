Breitling Super Chronomat Origins watch with ethically sourced gold and lab-grown diamonds.Breitling In an effort to strengthen its commitment to sustainability, transparency and traceability in the supply chain, Swiss watch brand Breitling unveils the newest Super Chronomat Automatic 38 Origins watch line that packs a good dose of 'Better' into it. By teaming with the Swiss Better Gold Association for properly sourced artisanal gold, and by using lab grown diamonds for the bezels of these watches, Breitling's newest line is its first-ever traceable watch.
