The best golf sweaters come out of your closet during those seasons that either tease incoming summer temperatures or prepare to wave farewell to warm weather rounds. Whether autumn is squeezing every last round of golf out of the year before winter snows out the courses, or spring pushes you out onto the links before there's any real chance of snagging a golfer's tan, that's when a good golf sweater is essential. While you can still get your hands on thicker and heavier athletic sweaters more in keeping with traditional looks, today's golf wear manufacturers employ materials that will keep you just as warm with a lot less weight and restriction of movement.
