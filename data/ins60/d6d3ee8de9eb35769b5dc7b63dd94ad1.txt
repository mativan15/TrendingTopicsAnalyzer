These ‘margin leader' stocks have strength in a recession, Jefferies says
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-03 19:08:16

Recession fears are haunting investors, but these "margin leaders" could be worth adding to your portfolio in a slowing economy, Jefferies found. Markets have come under pressure this year as the Federal Reserve proceeds on its first rate-hiking cycle in more than three years in an effort to curb record inflation . "With growth stocks facing significant headwinds amid rising rates, topline leaders are underperforming," wrote Desh Peramunetilleke, global head of microstrategy at Jefferies, in a Tuesday note.
