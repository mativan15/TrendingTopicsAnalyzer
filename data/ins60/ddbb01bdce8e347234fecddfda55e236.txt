St. James city council accepts $150k grant to improve police de-escalation

By WCCO Staff October 21, 2022 / 10:25 AM / CBS Minnesota ST. JAMES, Minn. – On Tuesday, October 18, the St James City Council voted unanimously to accept a $150k grant from the United States Department of Justice (DOJ). The grant will allow the St. James Police Department to purchase equipment to train officers in training topics such as de-escalation, autism awareness, and effective communication with the public. According to the St. James Police Department, they intend to purchase a VirTra V-180 Training Simulator System with the grant money.
