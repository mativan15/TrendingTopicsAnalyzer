I paid £85 for airport parking only to be told it 'didn't EXIST'

A HOLIDAYMAKER was left perplexed and seriously out of pocket after paying £85 for an airport parking service that "didn't exist".
David Wright, 72, was set to make his regular trip to Limoges in France last month - until he was met with a nasty surprise.
David was told by staff the meet and greet service he'd booked through Ryanair didn't actually existCredit: NCJ MEDIA
David was forced to shell out an eye-watering £229 to leave their Hyundai in the airport's long-stay parkingCredit: NCJ MEDIA
To his horror, the retired researcher was told by staff at Leeds Bradford airport that the meet and greet service he'd booked through budget airline Ryanair didn't actually exist.
