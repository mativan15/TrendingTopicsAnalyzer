By Reuters Published: 04:37 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 04:37 EDT, 27 May 2022 May 27 (Reuters) - Hong Kong stocks jumped nearly 3% on Friday, led by tech shares following forecast-beating results from Alibaba Group and Baidu, while comments by a U.S. official on relations with China were interpreted by some as positive. ** Also lifting the market were expectations that Beijing will implement more measures to revive growth. ** Hong Kong's Hang Seng Index gained 2.9%, while the Hang Seng Tech Index surged 3.
