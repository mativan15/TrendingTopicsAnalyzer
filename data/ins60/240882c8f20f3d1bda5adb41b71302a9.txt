Published: 01:44 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 01:44 EDT, 30 May 2022 Lachie Gill was crowned the winner of The Voice Australia for 2022 during Sunday night's grand finale episode.And on Monday, the 24-year-old P.E. teacher revealed that he is planning to use the $100,000 cash prize to purchase a home or invest into his music career.'I'll never be the person to buy jewellery or a car. I'll make sure it goes somewhere where it will benefit me for my future,' Lachie told Daily Mail Australia. Revealed: The Voice Australia winner Lachie Gill (pictured) has revealed how he plans to spend his $100K prize money '[I'll either] invest it into the music stuff or start looking at a house,' he explained.
