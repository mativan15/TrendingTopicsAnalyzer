Young man blackmailed for money ends life
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 07:45:00

Vadodara: A young man's death has sparked a row after his father claimed that he had committed suicide. Mayur Patel , a resident of Fatehpura , was rushed to the hospital after he complained of uneasiness at home on Thursday.While Patel died during the treatment, his friend Javed Garasia approached the deceased's family members. Garasia told them that Patel was being blackmailed and threatened to return money by a couple.Patel had already given Rs 4.50 lakh to the couple but they were demanding more, Garasia claimed.
