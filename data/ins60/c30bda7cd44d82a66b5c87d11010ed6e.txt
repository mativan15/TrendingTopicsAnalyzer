Punjab CM digs in heels, says PAU V-C Gosal appointed as per rules
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 12:04:24

Two days after Punjab governor Banwarilal Purohit sought the removal of Satbir Singh Gosal as the vice-chancellor of Punjab Agricultural University, Ludhiana, terming his appointment 'totally illegal', a defiant chief minister Bhagwant Mann wrote back, saying the norms and procedures had been followed. Also read: Punjab CM Bhagwant Mann appears in Mansa court in defamation caseIn the letter to Purohit on Thursday, Mann reiterated that Gosal's appointment was carried out according to the Haryana and Punjab Agricultural University Act, 1970, by the university's board.
