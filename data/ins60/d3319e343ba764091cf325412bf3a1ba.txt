Back in 2018, Australia got a treat: a 15-piece band of Jamaican and Cuban musicians, flown around the world as an unlikely supergroup.It was the dream project of Jake Savona, a producer, arranger, keyboardist and DJ from Melbourne now living in Byron Bay: the live version of his cross-cultural fusion album, Mista Savona Presents Havana Meets Kingston.Jake Savona with his Jamaican/Cuban supergroup, Havana Meets Kingston.Credit:Lara MerringtonAnd now he's determined to do it again. Only this time, he wants to go all the way to Carnegie Hall.
