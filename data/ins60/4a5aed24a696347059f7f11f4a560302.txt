Published: 04:03 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 04:06 EDT, 22 October 2022 Newcastle striker Callum Wilson believes it will be Tottenham feeling the pressure when his side take on on the north London club on Sunday.The Magpies have lost just one of their opening 11 league games this term, and are unbeaten in their last six - a run that has moved them to within two points of the top four.Over the past week, Newcastle have picked up four points from games against Manchester United and Everton, while Spurs are coming off the back of a defeat to the Red Devils in midweek.
