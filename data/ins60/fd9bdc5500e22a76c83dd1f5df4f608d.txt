Twitter shares slump after report that the U.S. mulls national-security reviews for some of Elon Musk's ventures
Copyright 2022, MarketWatch, Inc.
news
2022-10-21 03:50:00

Shares of Twitter plunged in premarket trade on Friday after a report Biden administration officials are considering subjecting some of Elon Musk's ventures to national-security reviews. Twitter TWTR, +1.18% shares plunged 9% to $47.64 in premarket trade, below the $54.20 per share buyout price. Bloomberg News reported late Thursday that some U.S. officials have become concerned in recent weeks by Musk's Russia-friendly tweets and his threat to cut off Starlink satellite internet service to Ukraine.
