Fact check: Image shows commemorative medal, not Israeli 'shekel' predicting 9/11
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-17 22:11:03

Social media users are sharing an image they claim shows an old Israeli coin that predicted the Sept. 11, 2001, terrorist attacks. "1968 Israeli 'shekel' 33 years before 9/11/2001," reads the caption of one Oct. 11 Facebook post, which features an image of a gold coin that is inscribed with Hebrew as well as depictions of a plane, a tank and a scroll.An Oct. 12 version of the post written in Hungarian garnered more than 200 shares on its first day, while a version from July accumulated more than 700 likes.
