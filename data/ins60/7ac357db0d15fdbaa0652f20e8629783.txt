The last two years have been a perfect storm of challenges for Lindsay's downtown businesses, but just as the end has come into view, it has been pushed further into the future. Notwithstanding the May 21 storm, which resulted in power outages for thousands of businesses and residences across the Kawarthas, downtown shops have struggled with a combination of COVID-19 closures, restrictions and supply chain difficulties on top of construction. Some work has had to be redone due to product deficiencies.
