Salesforce Shares Higher On Demand Optimism
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 16:12:00

By Michael Dabaie Salesforce shares rose 10% to $175.86 after the company's first quarter beat analyst views and it expressed optimism on demand. The customer relationship management company on Tuesday reported first quarter revenue increased to $7.41 billion, a bit above FactSet consensus for $7.38 billion. Adjusted profit of 98 cents a share beat the FactSet consensus for 94 cents. The company cut its fiscal year guidance to $31.7 billion to $31.8 billion, from an earlier forecast of $32 billion to $32.
