Here are the best USB-C to Ethernet adapters for your MacBook this 2022. Photo: Dmitry Chernyshov/Unsplash (CC0) In its goal to make MacBooks lighter and slimmer, Apple made the unfortunate decision of killing almost all its ports, save for the USB-C ports, which have since been the only available option for Mac users since 2015.That all changed this 2022, as Apple decided to listen to its customers and bring back the legacy ports with the release of the new MacBook M1 Pro and M1 Max.However, not everyone has the money for these new Macs, so it's highly likely that there are still people making do with the USB-C ports.
