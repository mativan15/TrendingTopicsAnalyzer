FUZHOU, China, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- E-Home Household Service Holdings Limited (Nasdaq: EJH) (the "Company" or "E-Home"), a provider of integrated household services in China, today announced the Company has been awarded RMB18 million new contracts during April-May to provide property cleaning services for neighborhood residences. The Company signed RMB5 million of the service contracts and Putian YouYou Cleaning Co., Ltd. ('YouYou'), a subsidiary of the Company, was awarded RMB13 million of the new business.
