Buying your own house is an important milestone in one's life stage cycle. To attain this milestone, one needs to have both monetary robustness and future earnings visibility to provision this critical yet fundamental investment. In procuring this substantial financial acquisition of a dream life asset, it is natural to be apprehensive about making a high ticket miscalculation. It is in this context one needs to scrutinize whether with rising inflation and the subsequent interest rate hike already raising cost of living, is it really viable and the right time for home buyers to go ahead with their purchase?
