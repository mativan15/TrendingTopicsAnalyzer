Girl, 6, drowns at beach in Memorial Day tragedy
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-31 09:03:54

A 6-year-old girl has died after she drowned at a Michigan beach on Memorial Day, according to police.Police arrived at Martindale Beach at the Kensington Metropark, in Milford Township, after the child was reported missing at about 5:30 p.m. local time on Monday.When police started their investigation, they concluded she could be in the water and requested assistance from a dive team, according to ABC affiliate WXYZ.Several people in the area volunteered to help and formed a human chain in a bid to locate the girl.
