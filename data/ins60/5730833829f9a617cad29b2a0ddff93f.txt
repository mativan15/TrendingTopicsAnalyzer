Gujarat Titans celebrate maiden IPL title with roadshow
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-30 13:56:47

The Gujarat Titans team held a roadshow at Sabarmati Riverfront in Ahmedabad on Monday celebrating their victory in IPL cricket final. Captain Hardik Pandya was instrumental with the ball on Sunday night to hand the new franchise their maiden IPL trophy.
'This title is going to be a special one because we talked about creating a legacy. The coming generations will talk about it,' Hardik had said after the match.
