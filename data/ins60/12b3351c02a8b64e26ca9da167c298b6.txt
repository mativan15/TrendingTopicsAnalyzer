Published: 17:31 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 17:32 EDT, 21 October 2022 NBCUniversal is considering offering early retirement packages to its employees in another effort to reduce companywide spending.The conglomerate is expected to offer employees throughout the company who have worked for more than 10 years and are age 57 or older, which represents 2 percent of its 74,000-member staff.As part of the package, the network will offer 12 to 18 months of pay and will vest restricted stock units, according to The Hollywood Reporter.
