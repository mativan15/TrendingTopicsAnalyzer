HackerEarth and Coinbase Cloud Join Forces to Host "BUIDL the Future: A Global Hack for Web3"

2022-10-18 17:00:00

Hackathon will unite the global web3 developer community to drive education and innovation in crypto

SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- HackerEarth, a leading solutions provider for remote developer assessments, interviews, and upskilling, today announced its Hackathon in collaboration with Coinbase Cloud, which provides web3 APIs, services and infrastructure to power the next generation of software builders. These two industry leaders are hosting "BUIDL the Future" to rally the global web3 developer community behind innovative projects for the next generation of crypto users.
