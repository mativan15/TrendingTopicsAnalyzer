Fox Shares Slide, News Corp Shares Gain on Possible Merger
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 14:31:00

By Dean Seal Shares of Fox Corp. slid while shares of News Corp climbed in early trading Monday following reports that Rupert Murdoch has proposed a recombination of the companies just nine years after they were separated. Fox shares fell 8%, to a 52-week low of $26.81, before recovering some within the first hour of trading, while News Corp shares advanced 7.5%, to $16.77. The Wall Street Journal reported after the bell on Friday that special board committees have recently been established by both companies to study a possible deal and evaluate potential financial terms, according to people familiar with the situation.
