Homeownership feels like it's becoming an ever more distant pipe dream. In the current climate, the cost of pretty much everything is increasing: rent, electricity, gas, petrol and mortgages, which, when added together, are making it more difficult than ever for those of us with aspirations to get on the housing ladder.When you first delve into the property market and look at the costs involved it can be quite daunting . There are many hurdles to overcome for first-time buyers, but one of the biggest barriers is to save a deposit towards your first purchase.
