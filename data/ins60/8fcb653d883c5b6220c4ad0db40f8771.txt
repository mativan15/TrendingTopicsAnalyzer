What if Apple made an e-bike?
seattletimes.com
business
2022-10-17 06:00:10

For seven years, Horace Dediu has been thinking about one idea almost every day. A longtime handset industry analyst and avid Apple follower, Dediu, 54, also popularized the term 'micromobility' to describe a growing array of small, non-car electric vehicles. His obsession is at the intersection of all those interests: Dediu is convinced that Apple should make an e-bike, or something like it.

'I fundamentally believe there's no better product for Apple in mobility than micromobility,' Dediu says.
