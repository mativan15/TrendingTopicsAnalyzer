June 01, 2022 04:59 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nextdoor Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: KIND), the neighborhood network, today announced that its Board of Directors has approved a share repurchase program with authorization to purchase up to $100 million of its Class A common stock through June 30, 2024.

'We are committed to strategically deploying capital to drive long-term value for stockholders. We believe that the current macroeconomic environment, combined with the strength of our balance sheet, presents an attractive buying opportunity for our stock.
