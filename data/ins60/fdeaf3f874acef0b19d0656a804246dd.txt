The Breakfast Index Shows Fry-Up Costs 20% More Than Last Year
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-20 04:16:47

(Bloomberg) -- A full-English fry-up is now 20% more expensive than a year ago in a stark example of how rampant food price inflation is chipping away at household budgets.Just a day after Britain's inflation level hit double digits once more, the latest Bloomberg Breakfast Index shows how every ingredient is now much more costly than a year ago.Coffee, which experienced the biggest uplift in prices in September, is about 22% dearer than the same time last year. All the ingredients that make up a full English, except mushrooms, have similarly experienced double-digit price inflation over the past year.
