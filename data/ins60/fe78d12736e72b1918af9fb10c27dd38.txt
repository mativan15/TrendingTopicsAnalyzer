AgenciesPrime Minister Narendra Modi (File Pic)Prime Minister Narendra Modi will launch the national portal for credit-linked government schemes - Jan Samarth Portal on Monday. The portal will be launched during the 'Iconic Week Celebrations' of the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Corporate Affairs on Monday at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi. 
 This week is being celebrated as part of the 'Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav' (AKAM) between June 6-11.
 According to Prime Minister's Office (PMO), Jan Samarth Portal is a one-stop digital portal linking government credit schemes.
