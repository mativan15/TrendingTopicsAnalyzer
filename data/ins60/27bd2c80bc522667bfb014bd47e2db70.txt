Groups Urge U.S. To Probe 'Loot Box' On Electronic Arts Video Game
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-02 10:04:40

Consumer advocates on Thursday urged U.S. regulators to investigate video game maker Electronic Arts Inc for the misleading use of a digital "loot box" that "aggressively" urges players to spend more money while playing a popular soccer game.The groups Fairplay, Center for Digital Democracy and 13 other organizations urged the Federal Trade Commission to probe the EA game "FIFA: Ultimate Team".In the game, players build a soccer team using avatars of real players, and compete against other teams.
