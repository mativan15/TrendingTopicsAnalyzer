Change isn't always easy. Not even for two-time NASCAR Cup Series champion Kyle Busch.

Even in NASCAR, a sport with such deeply rooted history and tradition, it is rare for a driver to spend 15 years with a single organization. Busch had become synonymous with the Joe Gibbs Racing brand and the bright yellow M&M's Toyota Camry that even the most casual fans could recognize anywhere.
