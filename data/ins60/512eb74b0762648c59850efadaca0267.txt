Fans vow continued support for BTS' second chapter after military service SEOUL, Oct. 17 (Yonhap) -- Ardent fans of K-pop supergroup BTS were shocked Monday by the announcement of the members' plan to fulfill their mandatory military service, but accepted what was coming and promised to remain their ARMY during the transition period. Known as ARMY (Adorable Representative MC for Youth), BTS' global fan base of millions of loyal followers is powerful enough to create major waves and has helped the group break music industry records since its debut in 2013.
