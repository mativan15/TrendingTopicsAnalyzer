(PRWEB) October 18, 2022 The Law Offices of Allen Sawyer announces that California criminal defense attorney Allen Sawyer has succeeded in getting a felony reduced to a misdemeanor and then having that misdemeanor expunged from the record of former Stockton Mayor Anthony Silva, who was originally convicted in 2019 after pleading no contest to a felony conflict of interest charge (Case # STK-CR-FE-2017-2704).
