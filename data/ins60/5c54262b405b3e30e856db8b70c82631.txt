WHAT A DREAM 16:56, 18 Oct 2022Updated: 17:01, 18 Oct 2022MORE than 190 kids set off on a holiday of a lifetime yesterday as part of a scheme between British Airways and UK-based charity Dreamflight. 
Supporting kids aged between eight and 14 with a serious illness or disability, Dreamflight was co-founded by former British Airways' cabin crew member, Patricia Pearce MBE.
Since the first flight in 1987, more than 6200 children have flown on DreamflightCredit: PA
