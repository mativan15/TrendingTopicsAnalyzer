Stock Market Today: Dow, Nasdaq Up Sharply
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 15:22:46

U.S. stocks rose sharply in early trading Monday ahead of key corporate earnings reports this week, including several closely watched technology companies.The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 468.46 points, or 1.58%, to 30,103.29.
The S&P 500 rose 87.82 points, or 2.45%, to 3,670.89, and the Nasdaq
Composite was up 315.56 points, or 3.06%, to 10,636.95.Netflix (NFLX), Tesla (TSLA) and IBM (IBM) are each scheduled to report
quarterly earnings this week. So are Johnson & Johnson (JNJ), United Airlines
(UAL), AT&T (T) and Verizon (VZ).
