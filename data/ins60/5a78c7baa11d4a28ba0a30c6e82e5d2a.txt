October 17, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LITTLETON, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Norgren, a world leader in motion control and fluid technology, will be demonstrating its most recent solutions for packaging at Pack Expo International 2022 (October 23 - 26) at McCormick Place in Chicago, Booth S-1650. In addition, Norgren will be showcasing its latest acquisitions, including Bimba and Bahr Modultechnik. Demonstrations will feature IO-Link-enabled pneumatic products, as well as a wide array of pneumatic and electric actuators.
