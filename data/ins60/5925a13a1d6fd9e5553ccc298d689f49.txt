SAN JOSE, Calif., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Eargo, Inc. (Nasdaq: EAR), a medical device company on a mission to improve the quality of life for people with hearing loss, today announced it will present at the William Blair 42nd Annual Growth Stock Conference. Christian Gormsen, Eargo's President and Chief Executive Officer, is scheduled to present on Thursday, June 9th at 11:40 a.m. Eastern Time (8:40 a.m. Pacific Time).The live web cast of Eargo's presentation can be accessed at www.eargo.
