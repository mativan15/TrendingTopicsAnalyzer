China's economy showed a significant rebound in the third quarter and employment was largely stable, a senior official at the state planner said on Monday, even as the economy still faces challenges and difficulties.Policymakers have rolled a flurry of measures to bolster the world's second-largest economy, which narrowly escaped a contraction in the second quarter, as stringent COVID curbs and a deepening property slump weigh on the outlook.
