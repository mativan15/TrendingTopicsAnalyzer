The most expensive midterm race in the country doesn't even feature candidates
nbcnews.com
news
2022-10-18 18:45:00

All eyes are on Georgia and Pennsylvania's pivotal Senate races this year, but those races currently rank second and third in ad spending nationally, according to AdImpact. California's Proposition 27, a question about legalizing sports betting in the state, has spurred over $215 million in ad spending so far this cycle, with millions booked for the next three weeks leading up to Election Day.By comparison, $184 million has been spent in Georgia's Senate race so far, while $176 million has been spent in Pennsylvania's Senate race, including primary and general election ad spending.
