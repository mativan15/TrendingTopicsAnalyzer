Joyce Sims no more, dies at 63: Remembering 'Come Into My Life' singer
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 14:30:00

AgenciesChelsea Football Club worker CJ Carlos announced on social media that the singer Joyce Sims passed away on October 15 at age 63. There is no disclosure of the cause of her death. The singer's biggest hit, Come Into My Life, was in the top 10 on the UK Singles Chart and the US Billboard R&B Chart in 1987. She also released the albums Lifetime Love, Walk Away, and Looking For Love. Angie Stone and Snoop Dogg have sampled her work. She was the writer of the tunes for the movie Species (1995).
