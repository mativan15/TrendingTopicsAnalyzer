Published: 12:35 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 12:35 EDT, 18 October 2022  Jay-Z is suing business partner and alcohol giant Bacardi as he wants 'financial clarity' regarding his top-shelf cognac brand D'Usse.The 52-year-old mogul has filed a lawsuit against the major spirits company as he wants to know how much his alcohol line is making as they are 50/50 partners.TMZ Hip Hop obtained documents on Tuesday which show that his company SC liquor had demanded for financial clarity when it comes to D'Usse.
