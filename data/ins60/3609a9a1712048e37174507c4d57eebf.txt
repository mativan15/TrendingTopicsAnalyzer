Russia cutting off natural gas to Denmark, company says

2022-06-01 08:51:59

APRussian President Vladimir Putin declared in the wake of Western sanctions that "unfriendly foreign buyers" needed to open two accounts with state-owned Gazprombank, one to pay in euros and dollars as specified in contracts and another in rubles.Denmark's largest energy company said Russia is cutting off its gas supply as of Wednesday because it refused to pay in rubles, the latest escalation over European energy amid the war in Ukraine. Russia previously halted natural gas supplies to Finland, Poland and Bulgaria for refusing a demand to pay in rubles.
