By Vanessa Murdock June 4, 2022 / 9:21 PM / CBS New York NEW YORK -- Hundreds of people are taking part in an overnight walk in Manhattan in the fight against suicide.The Out of the Darkness Walk kicked off from the Intrepid Museum on the West Side on Saturday night.As CBS2's Vanessa Murdock reports, the walk takes place in one night with one goal -- stop suicide."This is for a brother, my brother Jonathan," said a participant named Ben.Ten years ago, Ben lost his brother to suicide. Saturday marks his second Out of the Darkness Walk and says his team raised more than $35,000 for the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention.
