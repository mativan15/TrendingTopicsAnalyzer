Nunez earns points for Liverpool, Man Utd outclass Tottenham

2022-10-20 03:41:00

LIVERPOOL, England, Oct 19 (Reuters) - Liverpool maintained their new-found momentum as Darwin Nunez scored the only goal in a 1-0 victory over West Ham United in the Premier League, but high-flying Tottenham Hotspur suffered a reality check with a 2-0 loss at Manchester United on Wednesday.Chelsea's run of four successive league wins came to an end as they were held to a 0-0 draw at Brentford.Impressive Newcastle United bolstered their place in the top-six with a 1-0 victory at home to struggling Everton.
