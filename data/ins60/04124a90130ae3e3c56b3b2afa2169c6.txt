getty I know you've heard the internet gurus dozens of times. Create content! Hire a videographer! Get on social media! Though the advice is sound and the suggestion is truly rooted in your company's best interest, content creation can be daunting. There are countless ways to do it, trends to follow and dozens and dozens of "authorities" on the subject all over social media. 
The purpose of this article is to break down simple and actionable steps to start making content creation a routine in your company's day-to-day activities.
