Ninoon Introduction Global Medical REIT (NYSE:GMRE) is a real estate investment trust that owns a portfolio of medical office buildings and small-to medium sized healthcare facilities located in secondary markets and suburbs of primary markets that provide services needed for an aging population such as cardiovascular treatment, rehabilitation, eye surgery, gastroenterology, oncology treatment and orthopedics. The company believes these facilities and markets are typically overlooked by larger REITs in the sector.
