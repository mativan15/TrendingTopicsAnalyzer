White House trying to fight inflation is like having an ‘arsonist trying to shut out a fire,' economist says

During an interview on FOX Business' "Mornings with Maria" former White House Council of Economic Advisers Chair Tomas Philipson slammed the Biden administration for their attempt to fight inflation, comparing it to an "arsonist trying to shut out a fire." Former White House economist argued that the White House is "actually almost trying to increase inflation" on FOX Business' "Mornings with Maria."  ((AP Photo/Patrick Semansky) / AP Images)TOMAS PHILIPSON: Getting the White House to fight inflation at this point is like having an arsonist trying to shut out a fire.
