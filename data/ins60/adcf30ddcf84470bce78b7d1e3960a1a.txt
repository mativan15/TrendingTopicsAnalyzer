I recently misplaced two 14-year-old girls. We were at Sakura-Con, an anime convention in Seattle. Two of the three teens in our group went, with permission, to buy food. They didn't come back. The Seattle Convention Center was packed with tens of thousands of people, most dressed like characters from Demon Slayer.It was a frantic hour-plus before they returned, apologetically (phone had no data; convention center had no Wi-Fi; lost track of time) after I already had played through every hellish scenario in my mind.
