For nearly a decade, discussions around student success and the best avenues to attain it have been highly debated in nearly every corner of the higher education ecosystem. Depending on the viewpoint, the definition of 'success' and its associated measurement varies quite dramatically.
But ultimately a learner's degree completion and professional growth are the best measures to a learner's success and, regrettably, completion rates are continuing to fall short. In 2021, only 62.2 percent of students in India had completed a bachelor's degree within six years.
