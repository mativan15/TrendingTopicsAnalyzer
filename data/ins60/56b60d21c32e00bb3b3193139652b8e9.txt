Ra Medical Shares Rise 31%, Company Completes $7.6M at-the-Market Facility
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 22:13:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Ra Medical Systems, Inc. were higher in Monday's late trading session after the company said it completed an at-the-market facility that raised gross proceeds of $7.6 million. Will McGuire, the company's chief executive, said proceeds will help the company meet the conditions of the proposed merger with Catheter Precision. At 5:56 p.m. EDT, the company's shares had risen 31.01% to trade at $7.14 per share. Volume in the after-hours market topped 2.4 million shares.
