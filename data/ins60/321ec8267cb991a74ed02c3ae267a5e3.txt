TherapeuticsMD Shares Soar After Acquisition Agreement
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 14:07:00

By Kathryn Hardison TherapeuticsMD Inc. shares soared more than 350% on Tuesday after the company said it will be acquired by private-equity firm EW Healthcare Partners for $177 million. An affiliate of EW will pay $10 a share in cash for the women's healthcare company. The deal is expected to completed by July 13. TherapeuticsMD shares closed at $2.14 on Friday. "We have a deep appreciation for EW Healthcare Partners's depth of expertise and track record and know they will bring an incredible value of knowledge and strategic guidance," TherapeuticsMD CEO Hugh O'Dowd said in a prepared statement.
