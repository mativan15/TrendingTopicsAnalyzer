Gautam Budh Nagar admin asks Noida authority to demolish illegal construction in Sector 48



 




Read this news in brief form


 

 

NOIDA: The Gautam Budh Nagar district administration has written to the Noida authority, asking them to raze multiple illegal buildings constructed on enemy property land in Noida's Barola Sector 48. The administration said that once freed, the land will be handed over to the ministry of home affairs which is the owner of these properties.
