October 19, 2022 09:04 AM Eastern Daylight Time SOMERSET, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--1Kosmos, the only company that unifies identity proofing and passwordless authentication, today announced it has received a System and Organization Controls (SOC) 2 Type II certification. This certification validates that 1Kosmos, which provides identity proofing and passwordless as a cloud service, maintains effective system level controls that ensure the security of its platform.



This is the latest in a series of certifications the company has received including support for the FIDO Alliance FIDO2 for strong and multi factor authentication, Kantara Initiative NIST SP 800-63 rev.
