October 19, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOULDER, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Vision Ridge Partners ('Vision Ridge' or the 'Firm'), a sustainable real assets investor, today announced that Jules Kortenhorst, former Chief Executive Officer of RMI (formerly known as Rocky Mountain Institute), will join the Firm as a Partner, effective January 9, 2023. Mr. Kortenhorst will focus on continuing to scale and strengthen Vision Ridge's asset management and governance capabilities in support of its portfolio.
