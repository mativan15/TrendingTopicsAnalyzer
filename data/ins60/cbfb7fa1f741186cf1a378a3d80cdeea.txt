Published: 12:33 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 12:44 EDT, 31 May 2022 Council killjoys are attempting to ban a businessman from hosting a charity party to mark the Queen's Platinum Jubilee - despite the event being held in his garden.Community-minded Drew Cockton informed local officials about his plan to toast the Queen's 70th year on the throne with an event at his seven-bedroom home in Wirral, Merseyside.Alongside gathering friends and neighbours to raise a glass to Her Majesty, the 35-year-old entrepreneur had hoped to raise money for The Clatterbridge Cancer Charity.
