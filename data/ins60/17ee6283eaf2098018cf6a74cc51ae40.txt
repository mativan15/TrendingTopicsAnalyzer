Alphabet, Meta had highest-paid median workers in 2021: Report
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 07:00:00

San Francisco: The median compensation for most S&P 500 corporations increased last year as the economy grew and the job market tightened, according to a Wall Street Journal analysis.The report mentioned that Google parent Alphabet and Facebook's Meta Platforms had the highest-paid median workers, at almost $300,000. They were among nearly 150 companies that said their median employee earned more than $100,000.Auto-parts supplier Aptiv PLC had the lowest-paid median worker in the S&P 500 -- a full-time factory worker in Mexico who earned less than $7,500 last year.
