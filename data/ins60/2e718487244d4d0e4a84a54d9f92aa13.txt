Twitter hit by fears of national security review
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 16:00:00

Shares in Twitter fell sharply on reports that US government officials are discussing whether to subject some of Elon Musk's business interests to national security reviews, including the billionaire's $44 billion takeover of the social media platform.The reports have emerged one week before Musk, who is also head of Tesla, the electric car maker, and SpaceX, the space and satellite group, is required to complete the acquisition if he is to avoid a trial over his previous efforts to terminate the Twitter deal.
