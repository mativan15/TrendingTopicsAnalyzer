Demanding immediate disbursal of funds for 100days work under MNREGA, Trinamool Congress has organized two-day state-wide protests in West Bengal. From block level to the cities, Trinamool took out protest rallies led by several leaders. West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee has expressed her resentment several times over Centre withholding central funds and claimed that funds for MNREGA and PW Awas Yojana has not been disbursed from last year's December. 
Kunal Ghosh, state spokesperson of Trinamool Congress, said, 'The BJP is acting with political vendetta after losing in 2021 assembly polls and other elections.
