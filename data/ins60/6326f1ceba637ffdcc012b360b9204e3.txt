People must continue to use masks in busy areas, especially pregnant women and the elderly, experts said on Thursday, hours after the Delhi government lifted the Rs 500 fine for not wearing them in public places. Doctors at the city's top government and private hospitals agreed that people should exercise "extra caution" because, while cases in Delhi are low, a new variant of COVID-19 that is highly communicable is being reported.
