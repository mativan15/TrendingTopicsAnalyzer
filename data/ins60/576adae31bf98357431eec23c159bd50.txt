IANSPrime Minister Narendra ModiPrime Minister Narendra Modi is likely to visit Tripura on October 27. Tripura is going to assembly polls early next year. A senior leader in state BJP who does not want to be named told ET, 'It will be a one-day programme.' In May this year Manik Saha became chief minister replacing Deb in a sudden political development. Tripura is witnessing a series of political clashes and political violence. Political parties had to approach the court. Ruling BJP plans to go door to door with a report card of the first BJP led government in the state.
