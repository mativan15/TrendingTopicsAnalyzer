A Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan awardee, educationist, author, visionary, philanthropist, and one of the greatest scientists India has ever produced, APJ Kalam's legacy speaks for itself.Apart from being India's eminent scientist, Dr. Kalam was also known for his utmost simplicity and humanity. Moreover, his qualities like kindness and empathy make Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam a true gentleman. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam has always been known for his knowledge and kindness.
An aerospace scientist who also served as the 11th President of India (from 2002 to 2007) and was widely regarded as the 'People's President'.
