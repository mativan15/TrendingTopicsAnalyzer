Published: 09:24 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 11:09 EDT, 21 October 2022 Former Trump advisor Steve Bannon arrives at federal court in Washington to be sentenced -- Bannon was found guilty of two counts of contempt of Congress Donald Trump's former aide Steve Bannon was sentenced Friday to four months in prison for refusing to testify in the congressional probe of the January 6, 2021 attack on the Capitol. One of the masterminds behind Trump's 2016 presidential campaign and victory, Bannon was found guilty on two counts of contempt of Congress for defying a subpoena to testify over the riot by the former president's supporters.
