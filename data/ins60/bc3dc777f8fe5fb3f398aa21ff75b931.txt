Avenue Supermarts Q2 Results: Profit rises 63% YoY to Rs 730 crore; revenue jumps 36%

Agencies Revenue for the quarter rose nearly 36 per cent on year to Rs. 10385 crores. on Saturday reported a nearly 63 per cent YoY rise in net profit for the September quarter to Rs. 730.48 crores. Revenue for the quarter rose nearly 36 per cent on year to Rs. 10385 crores. The earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization (EBITDA) for the quarter rose more than 33 per cent on year to Rs. 895 crores. However, operating margin dipped by 14 basis points to 8.62 per cent.

The fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) and staples segment of the business performed better than general merchandise and apparel segments, the company said in a release.
