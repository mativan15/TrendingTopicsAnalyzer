Microsoft stock sinks after profit and sales outlook lowered due to 'unfavorable' currency moves
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 13:28:01

Shares of Microsoft Corp. MSFT, -3.07% sank 2.5% in premarket trading Thursday, after the software behemoth lowered its fiscal fourth-quarter guidance for profit and revenue to reflect and "unfavorable" currency impact. The company revised its earnings-per-share estimate for the quarter to June 30 to $2.24 to $2.32 from $2.28 from $2.35, with the new guidance below the FactSet EPS consensus of $2.33. The revenue outlook was lowered to $51.94 billion to $52.74 billion from $52.40 billion to $53.2 billion, with the new range below the FactSet consensus of $52.
