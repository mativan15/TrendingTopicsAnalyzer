ILKAY GUNDOGAN is a shock target for Turkish club Galatasaray.
The Germany international midfielder has less than a year left on his contract.
Galatasaray are eyeing Man City star Ilkay Gundogan, who is out of contract next yearCredit: Rex
City skipper Gundogan admitted in 2020 he would like to play in Turkey — the country of his descent — or the US once he leaves England.
But the 31-year-old has not ruled out staying if an offer was made.
However, his new wife Sara spoke last week about how she struggles to find good restaurants in the Manchester area.
