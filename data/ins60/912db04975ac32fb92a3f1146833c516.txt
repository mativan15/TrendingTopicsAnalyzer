What are the Boston Celtics' weaknesses heading into the 2022-23 NBA season?
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-19 06:00:16

Depending on who you talk to, the Boston Celtics are looking at anything from a near-certain banner to hang this summer to a second-round exit in the 2023 NBA Playoffs, one or the other being presumably isomorphic with the glass-half-full and glass-half-empty crowds.
But which version of the team might we actually encounter on the court, playing games that count? More importantly, what are the weak points of a squad lauded for having one of the better offseasons adding talent to last season's second-best roster by default? By now we are all quite familiar with what the Celtics' strengths are — how about where they are weak?
Such a task was accomplished by HoopsHype's Yossi Gozlan in a recent season preview of the team — let's take a look at some of what the HoopsHype expert had to say about what issues the Celtics will need to overcome if they are to achieve their goals of winning it all in 2023.
