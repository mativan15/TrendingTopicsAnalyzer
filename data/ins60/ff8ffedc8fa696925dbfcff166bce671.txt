Industrials Down Sharply After Union Pacific, Dow Earnings -- Industrials Roundup
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 20:35:00

Shares of industrial and transportation companies fell sharply after lackluster earnings from some industrial bellwethers. Shares of railroad Union Pacific fell after rising costs of equipment, fuel and labor offset increased freight volumes. Chemicals giant Dow, which sells plastics and other chemical products to a wide range of industrial customers, posted a retreat in third-quarter sales and earnings. The Philadelphia Federal Reserve's gauge of regional business activity inched up to negative 8.
