It was, perhaps, more straight out of The Thick of It than Compton, yet Nadine Dorries seemed undeterred.In a move that has elicited a somewhat mixed reaction, the culture secretary has launched a policy explanation of the government's online safety bill in the form of a 41-second rap on TikTok.'It's effectively a framework to protect internet users / From scams, illegal content and anonymous abusers,' rhymes Dorries about the legislation that will require tech companies to protect children and adults from harmful online content.
