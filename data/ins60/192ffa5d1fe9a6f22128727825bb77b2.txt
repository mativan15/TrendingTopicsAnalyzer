Italy Opens Talks On New Government As Coalition Spars Over Ukraine
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 11:57:31

Far-right leader Giorgia Meloni, 45, is expected to be named Italy's first woman prime minister following consultations that began Thursday
Italy's president began consultations Thursday on forming a new government following the victory of far-right leader Giorgia Meloni in elections last month, as tensions over Ukraine threatened the unity of her coalition.Meloni, 45, is expected to be named Italy's first woman prime minister following the talks, which are set to wrap up Friday, after her post-fascist Brothers of Italy party came top in September 25 polls.
