Xiaomi will soon launch Mi Band 7 Pro, which is rumoured to be releasing alongside the Xiaomi 12 Ultra.The tech company Xiaomi last week announced the Mi Band 7, which will boast of a larger display with 25 per cent more viewable space along with Always-On feature. However, the smartwatch will not come packed with a built-in GPS, which is already a part of an upgrade in the related Redmi Smart Band Pro. 
But according to reports, Xiaomi will soon launch the Mi Band 7 Pro, which is rumoured to be releasing alongside the Xiaomi 12 Ultra.
