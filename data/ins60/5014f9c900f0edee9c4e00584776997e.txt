Ajit Doval meets Russian counterpart, discusses current state of affairs
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 18:45:00

New Delhi: National security advisor (NSA) A K Doval on Friday met his Russian counterpart Gen Nikolai Patrushev in Dushanbe in what was their first meeting since Moscow launched military attack on Ukraine in February. The two officials are understood to have discussed current state of affairs as well as strategic partnership and terrorism in the Afghanistan-Pakistan region. Doval met Patrushev in Dushanbe on the sidelines of the fourth Regional Security Dialogue on Afghanistan led by the NSAs of Tajikistan, India, Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Iran, Kyrgyzstan and China, ET has learnt.
