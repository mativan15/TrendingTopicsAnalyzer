Australia's Link Administration plans to divest UK-based Link Fund Solutions

finance
2022-10-19 22:04:10

Share registry firm Link
Administration Holdings Ltd said on Thursday it will
explore options to divest its Link Fund Solutions business,
months after Britain's financial regulator said it may fine the
unit 50 million pounds ($56.10 million) in a probe relating to a
now-defunct fund.
 ($1 = 0.8913 pounds)
