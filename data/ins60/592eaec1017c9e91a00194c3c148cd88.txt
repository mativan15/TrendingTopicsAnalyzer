Floor & Decor Launches the Grand Opening of its Parkville, Maryland Store on June 2, 2022

2022-06-01 00:00:00

June 01, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Floor & Decor (NYSE: FND) a leading specialty retailer of hard-surface flooring, will expand its nationwide footprint when it opens the doors to its newest location in Parkville, Maryland on June 2, 2022. The 85,000 square-foot Floor & Decor warehouse store and design center will open with a team of about 50 full-time and part-time associates led by Daniel Avallone, the new store's Chief Executive Merchant.



'Floor & Decor is thrilled to open our doors in Parkville,' said Avallone.
