When the pandemic isolated nearly everyone at home, our fascination with forebears intensified. That was especially true after Covid-19 felled many older family members, says Deborah Liu, CEO of Ancestry.com. 'The people we lost during Covid-19 are a stark reminder of how important our family stories are, and why we should preserve their memories while we still can,'' adds Liu, a Silicon Valley veteran who's also the daughter of Chinese immigrants and a mother of three.
