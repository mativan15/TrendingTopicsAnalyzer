The South Taranaki mum who waited 40 years to become a racing car driver
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-16 16:00:00

Despite a lifetime involved in stock car racing, it took Sallie Barr 40 years to finally get behind the wheel, but it was worth the wait. 'It was really exciting, it certainly got the adrenalin and heart pumping,' said the Hāwera woman, who drives a mini stock – a non-contact class of stock car. 'It is very different feeling out on the track than it is watching.' The move into the driver's seat brought to a conclusion the 50-year-old's lifelong dream to drive the dirt track cars, joining her husband and two daughters in their passion for the sport.
