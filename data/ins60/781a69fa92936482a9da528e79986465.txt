OSLO, Norway, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by Kalera S.A. (the "Company") (Euronext Growth Oslo: KAL) on 31 May 2022 regarding the merger between the Company and Kalera AS (the "Merger"), and the merger consideration shares issued by the Company in connection with the Merger. As disclosed in the abovementioned announcement, a total of 105,719,452 new shares were issued by the Company to deliver merger consideration shares to the shareholders of Kalera AS.
