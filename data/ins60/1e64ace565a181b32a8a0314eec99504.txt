Pound rises 0.5% in Asia trade following Liz Truss' economic changes
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 06:18:00

The pound has risen higher against the dollar in early Asia trading after Liz Truss reversed some economic plans that had caused instability. On Monday morning, the sterling pound gained around 0.5 percent to trade above $1.12 in trade in Asia.On Friday, Mr Kwarteng was sacked by Ms Truss after 38 days in office, as his mini-budget economic plan caused economic instability as the cost of Government borrowing and mortgages rose.It led to the Bank of England stepping in three times in an unprecedented move to protect pensions.
