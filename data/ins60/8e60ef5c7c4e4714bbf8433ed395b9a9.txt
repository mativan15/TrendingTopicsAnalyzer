MSMEs can continue to avail non-tax benefits post re-classification: Govt
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 09:45:00

Non-tax benefits include benefits of various government schemes, including Public Procurement Policy, Delayed Payments, etc.NEW DELHI: Micro, small and medium enterprises will continue to avail all non-tax benefits in their respective categories post re-classification for three years, the government said on Wednesday. The Ministry of MSME has notified that in case of re-classification in terms of investment in plant and machinery or equipment or turnover or both, and consequent re-classification, an enterprise shall continue to avail of all non-tax benefits of the category it was in before the re-classification for three years from the date of the such upward change.
