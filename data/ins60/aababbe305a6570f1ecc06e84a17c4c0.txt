Energy bills forecast to rise above £4,000 in April
bbc.com
business
2022-10-18 09:16:18

Image source, Getty ImagesTypical household energy bills could reach £4,347 a year from April after the government said it would scale back support, an analyst has estimated.The forecast from Cornwall Insight comes after the chancellor said the energy bill cap, which had been due to last for two years, would end in April.The government said the most vulnerable would continue to be protected from soaring energy prices.The forecasts could change depending on movements in wholesale energy prices.The new Chancellor, Jeremy Hunt, announced the change to the energy price support as part of a package of measures designed to save money after the government's mini-budget left a big projected hole in the public finances.
