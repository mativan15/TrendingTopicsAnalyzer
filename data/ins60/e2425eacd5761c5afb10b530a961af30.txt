ONEOK Inc. stock outperforms market despite losses on the day
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 21:12:00

Shares of ONEOK Inc. OKE slipped 0.21% to $67.27 Friday, on what proved to be an all-around poor trading session for the stock market, with the S&P 500 Index SPX falling 1.63% to 4,108.54 and Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA falling 1.05% to 32,899.70. The stock's fall snapped a two-day winning streak. ONEOK Inc. closed $7.80 short of its 52-week high ($75.07), which the company achieved on April 21st. Despite its losses, the stock outperformed some of its competitors Friday, as Sempra SRE fell 1.
