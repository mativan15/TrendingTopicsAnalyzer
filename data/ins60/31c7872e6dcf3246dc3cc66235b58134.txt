Tristen Nash, the son of WWE Hall of Famer Kevin Nash, tragically passed away at the age of 26 on Friday. On Thursday, October 20, reporter Sean Ross Sapp tweeted that he had to regretfully announce that Kevin Nash and Tamara Nash's son Tristen Nash had tragically departed at a very young age, 26. Tristen Nash only began working on Kevin's new show lately, and the two enjoyed their time together. The reporter requested the public to respect the Nash family's privacy in this hour of despair.

Before his unexpected passing, the 63-year-old professional wrestler and his wife Tamara Nash birthed Tristen in the month of June of 1996 and raved about parenthood.
