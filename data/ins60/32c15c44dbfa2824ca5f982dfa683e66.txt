 Calgary Fire Department crews were quick to act after a fire broke out at a duplex in the northwest community of Bowness.
Calgary police tell Global News a call came in around 5:07 a.m. for reports of smoke coming from a basement at 4304 73 St. NW. People from the home along with others who lived adjacent to the house were evacuated.As of 5:30 a.m. firefighters had the fire under control.
