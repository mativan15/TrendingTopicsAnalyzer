Two Massachusetts cities ranked among the Top 20 best places to live in U.S.
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-17 20:11:18

For those looking to settle down, there's fewer places better than the Bay State, according to a new ranking.

According to the online financial magazine, Money, both Somerville and Milton are two of the country's most desirable locations, ranking seventh and seventeenth on their Top 50 list of the greatest cities to live in the United States.

Money cites Somerville's proximity to Boston, low crime rates and great education as the hallmarks of a great city.

'For residents, Somerville offers more than just a wide array of activities.
