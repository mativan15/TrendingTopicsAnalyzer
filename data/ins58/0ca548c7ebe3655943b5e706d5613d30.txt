VANCOUVER, British Columbia, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Standard Lithium Ltd. ('Standard Lithium' or the 'Company') (TSXV: SLI) (NYSE American: SLI) (FRA: S5L), an innovative technology and lithium project development company, has appointed PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP (PWC) as its new independent registered public accounting firm, effective October 17, 2022. The appointment of PWC has been approved by both the audit committee and the Board of Directors (the 'Board') of the Company.
