Trend-following hedge funds are capitalising on market disruption and geopolitical unrest, with funds such as Graham Capital Management, Aspect Capital, AlphaSimplex and AQR Capital Management all near or over 40% higher for the year.Such funds profit by buying or selling when the markets make big moves and by riding that move until it fizzles out or the market changes direction.
