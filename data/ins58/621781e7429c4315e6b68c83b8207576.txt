Indian Banks' Association re-elects PNB's AK Goel as chairman
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-22 09:45:00

Agencies A K Goel, managing director and CEO of Punjab National Bank Indian Banks' Association reelected A K Goel, managing director and CEO of Punjab National Bank as its new chairman, who will serve the industry body for the financial year 2022-23. The reelection was one of the rare occasions in history when an incumbent IBA chairman obtained a second consecutive term.

Goel was supposedly a unanimous candidate, said a banking source. 'Goel will be able to coordinate with the government better sitting in Delhi,' the person said.
