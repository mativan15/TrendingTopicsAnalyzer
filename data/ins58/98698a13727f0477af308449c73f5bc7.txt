Ranvir Shorey isn't afraid of speaking his mind. His new film Mid Day Meeal has just released and he reveals that it's a film unlike anything he's done before. He also confesses that not having enough lead roles in films has bothered him. And with the same candour, he reveals that Bollywood 's filmmakers should make better films to ensure box office returns. All that and more revelations in this no-holds-barred conversation. Excerpts...Mid Day Meeal is a film that has a social theme at its core but it also has a very good screenplay.
