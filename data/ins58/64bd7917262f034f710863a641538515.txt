Khandeshi Movies enters crypto partnership with NFT player XCAD
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 11:15:00

New Delhi: Khandeshi Movies and XCAD have partnered in a multi-year deal to explore new opportunities in the Watch-to-Earn model. XCAD Network has its crypto token, whereas Khandeshi Movies creates short films on YouTube. XCAD Network provides creators with DeFi tools to create an incentivized economy by tokenizing the engagement of each creator's audience and their content. XCAD Network is also home to an exclusive NFT Marketplace for creators.

With a subscriber base of 32.4 million, Khandeshi Movies provide a mixture of comedy movies, short films and gigs, which gives their online community an opportunity to showcase their talent.
