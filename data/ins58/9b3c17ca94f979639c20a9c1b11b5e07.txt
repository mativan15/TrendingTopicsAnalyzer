Walldorf, 17 October, 2022 Ekotechnika AG (Primary Market; ISIN: DE000A161234), the German holding company of the largest dealer of international agricultural machinery in Russia, has revised its sales and earnings forecast after the preliminary figures for the past financial year 2021/2022 (30 September) had become available.
