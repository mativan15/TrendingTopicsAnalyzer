By Elizabeth Elkind, Politics Reporter For Dailymail.Com Published: 12:04 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 12:14 EDT, 31 May 2022 Former Hillary Clinton campaign attorney Michael Sussmann was found not guilty on Tuesday in the first trial of Special Counsel John Durham's probe into the federal government's actions investigating Donald Trump's 2016 presidential campaign.Sussmann was charged with making a false statement to the FBI.It's the only case that has gone to trial in Durham's three-year inquiry into the origins of Robert Mueller's probe looking into allegations that Trump's 2016 campaign was aided to victory by Russia.
