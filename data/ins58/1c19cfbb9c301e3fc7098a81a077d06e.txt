Haryana Sikh Gurdwara (Management) Act: SGPC files two review petitions in Supreme Court
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 23:11:41

The Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee (SGPC) has filed two review petitions in the Supreme Court against its recent judgment upholding validity of Haryana Sikh Gurdwara (Management) Act that validates separate management committee for the historic gurdwaras in Haryana. As per its prior announcement, one petition has been filed by it as an organisation. It has got a second petition filed by Harbhajan Singh Masanan, an SGPC member from Haryana's Kurukshetra. The information was divulged by SGPC president Harjinder Singh Dhami during a press conference held at its head office here after he chaired a meeting of dharam parchar wing of the gurdwara body.
