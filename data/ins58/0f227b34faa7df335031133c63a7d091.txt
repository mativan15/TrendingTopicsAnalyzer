AgenciesAnother list of BJP candidates for the remaining seats from different states is expected on Monday.Former Uttar Pradesh BJP President Laxmikant Bajpai and Radhamohan Agarwal, the sitting veteran MLA from Gorakhpur Sadar seat who was replaced by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath as the candidate in the recent Assembly elections, were declared candidates by the party for the Rajya Sabha elections. Union Ministers Nirmala Sitharaman (Karnataka) and Piyush Goyal (Maharashtra) figure in the BJP list of 16 names announced on Sunday.
