Why Constellation Brands Remains Bullish on Canopy Growth
fool.com
news
2022-10-20 10:45:00

It has been five years since alcoholic beverage maker Constellation Brands (STZ -1.43%) first invested in pot producer Canopy Growth (CGC -6.56%). In 2017, it paid $190 million for take a 10% stake in the business. The following year it invested another $4 billion. Today, Constellation Brands owns 38.6% of the cannabis business. However, Canopy Growth hasn't made for a great investment thus far -- its top and bottom lines have struggled, and its share price has collapsed.
But despite the adversity, Constellation Brands remains committed to the business, even after recently incurring a major impairment charge against its investment.
