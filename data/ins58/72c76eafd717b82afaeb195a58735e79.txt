Once a GOP gimme, race for Colorado Springs' HD 16 has Dems feeling hopeful
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-15 11:42:00

Oct. 15—When Stephanie Vigil took her first swing at a seat in the Colorado House of Representatives two years ago, she realized she probably didn't have enough of the home team on her side — yet.

Like much of Colorado, House District 16's biggest group of voters identified as "unaffiliated," but HD 16 hadn't sent a Democrat to Denver for as long as anyone could remember.

It didn't again in 2020, but not by a landslide.

"We put a pretty good dent in the numbers," said Vigil, whose GOP opponent, Andy Pico, drew 54.
