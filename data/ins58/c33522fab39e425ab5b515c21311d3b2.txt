Haryana to expedite implementation of 5G spectrum: CS
 

: The Haryana government will step up the deployment work of telegraph infrastructure, including 5G small cells and aerial optical fibre cable (OFC), chief secretary Sanjeev Kaushal said on Wednesday. Reviewing the implementation of 5G spectrum initiative, Kaushal said that besides providing advance telecom services to people of the state, the 5G service telecom services will go beyond smart facilities, smart classrooms and as well as smart teachings.
