June 03, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TAMPA, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--After a career spanning more than 40 years and including 34 years with the Coca-Cola System, Duane Still will retire from his position as Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, Coca-Cola Beverages Florida, LLC (Coke Florida) on July 1, 2022.



Duane joined Coke Florida as CFO in May 2019 after retiring from CSM Bakery Solutions. Before joining CSM Bakery Solutions, Duane spent over 30 years with the Coca-Cola System, including multiple North America and international operating unit CFO roles, culminating with the CFO role of the North America Group, a $21 billion operating unit of The Coca-Cola Company.
