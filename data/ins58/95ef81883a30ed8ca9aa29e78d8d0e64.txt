US Sen. Dick Durbin in Elgin to announce $250,000 in federal funding for Fox River environmental restoration study
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-06-01 22:23:17

With the Kimball Street dam serving as a backdrop, U.S. Sen. Dick Durbin stood on Elgin's Walton Island Wednesday to announce $250,000 in federal funding to complete a long-in-the-works feasibility study on the environmental restoration of the Fox River.'Over the past 100 years, the Fox River has struggled with pollution issues stemming from years of wastewater discharge and untreated runoff,' Durbin said. 'Despite significant improvement in water quality over the past 20 years, there is still much work to be done to restore the habitat of the Fox River.
