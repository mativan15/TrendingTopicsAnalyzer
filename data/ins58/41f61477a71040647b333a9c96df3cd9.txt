In the flesh, Truss gives more than a stock answer
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 20:00:00

In these days of rampant Trussophobia I've been searching for something, anything, positive to say about our prime minister. Well, a little story has come my way that runs counter to the scathing tide, and suggests flashes of wit and warmth. A Derbyshire farmer friend — let's call him Jack — was part of a small delegation taken to meet and talk to the PM at Downing Street. Jack's a bold and plain-speaking sort: he it was who, when I corrected him on his pronunciation of 'reservoir' and asked him how, for example, he'd pronounce 'abattoir', replied 'slaughter'ouse'.
