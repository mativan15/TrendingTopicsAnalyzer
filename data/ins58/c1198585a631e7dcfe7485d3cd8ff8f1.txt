Koreans added to MLB team for exhibition games on home soilBy Yoo Jee-ho SEOUL, Oct. 20 (Yonhap) -- Three South Korean players have been added to the major league team for fall exhibition games to be played on their home soil. Major League Baseball (MLB) on Thursday announced seven new players to their squad ahead of the "MLB World Tour: Korea Series," with games set for Nov. 11 and 12 in the southeastern city of Busan and for Nov. 14 and 15 in Seoul. The new group includes Tampa Bay Rays first baseman Choi Ji-man and two Pittsburgh Pirates players, Park Hoy-jun and Bae Ji-hwan.
