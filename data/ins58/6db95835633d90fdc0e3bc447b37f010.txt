Record PLC Assets Under Management Rose on Quarter Despite Market Volatility
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 08:02:00

By Kyle Morris Record PLC said Friday that it booked assets under management equivalents net inflows of $6.6 billion during the third quarter and performance fees of 500,000 pounds ($561,950). The currency and asset manager said that AUME as at Sept. 30 was $80.8 billion compared with $77.9 billion at June 30. "Sustained uncertainty in financial markets continues to have only a limited impact on the value of some clients' underlying portfolios in our traditional business for currency products and services," Chief Executive Leslie Hill said.
