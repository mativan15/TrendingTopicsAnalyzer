Olivier Le Moal Private equity markets have seen a surge of interest in impact investing in recent years. In a recent survey by Pitchbook, 65% of respondents managing external money said that they had offered impact strategies to their clients, up from 57% in 2021. However, challenges around transparency, reporting and access to opportunities are common. Here's how a fund of funds approach can help. Minimizing the negative societal consequences of an investment is no longer just a nice-to-have. Investors today are increasingly prioritizing positive and measurable environmental, governance or social outcomes alongside financial gains.
