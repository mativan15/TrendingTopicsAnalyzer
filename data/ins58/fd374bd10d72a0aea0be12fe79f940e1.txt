KANSAS CITY, Mo., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Foxpoint, a leading tech-enabled logistics platform and holding company for OTR leasing, announces the promotion of Joshua Krause to Chief Operating Officer to continue driving business initiatives forward.'Josh's growth-focused mindset and automotive industry experience enabled him to overhaul the Sales and Marketing functions of our business to increase efficiencies and customer engagement,' said Jeff Dema, Foxpoint Chief Executive Officer. 'We are pleased to promote Josh to COO and know his passion for optimization and operational excellence will continue to drive our business forward.
