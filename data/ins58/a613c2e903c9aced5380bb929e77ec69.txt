Just like consumer credit cards that provide rewards for spending on travel, gas stations, grocery stores or restaurants, business credit cards can also give back, usually for spending on categories like office supplies, advertising, phone service or other common business expenses.When selecting a business credit card, you'll want to examine your expenditures and cash flow -- how much and where you spend money will help determine which card will work best for you. You'll also want a credit card that lets you add as many employees as you need to be authorized users.
