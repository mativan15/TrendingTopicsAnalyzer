The trial of two brothers charged in the car-bomb assassination of a Maltese journalist Daphne Caruana Galizia (pictured) who investigated corruption in the tiny island nation began on Oct. 14, nearly five years after the slaying that sent shockwaves across Europe.Jon Borg/The Associated PressMalta on Sunday marked the fifth anniversary of the car bomb slaying of investigative journalist Daphne Caruana Galizia with calls for justice, just two days after two key suspects reversed course and pleaded guilty to murder on the first day of their trial.
