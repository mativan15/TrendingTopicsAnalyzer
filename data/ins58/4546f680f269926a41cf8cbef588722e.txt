LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--Glancy Prongay & Murray LLP ('GPM'), announces that it has filed a class action lawsuit in the United States District Court for the District of Maryland, captioned Leacock v. IonQ, Inc., et al., Case No. 22-cv-1306, on behalf of persons and entities that purchased or otherwise acquired IonQ, Inc. ('IonQ' or the 'Company') (NYSE: IONQ ) securities between March 30, 2021 and May 2, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'). Plaintiff pursues claims under Sections 10(b) and 20(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the 'Exchange Act').
