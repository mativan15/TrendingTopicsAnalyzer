Vivo's sub-brand iQOO launches Neo 7 smartphone with 120W fast charging
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 08:00:00

Vivo's sub-brand iQOO has announced the launch of a new smartphone called Neo 7. The handset has a self-developed, sophisticated temperature detection technology and nine temperature sensors. According to the company, temperature sensors can detect and monitor the temperature in real time. The iQoo Neo 7 is available in black, orange, and blue. The smartphone has a starting price of 2,699 yuan (Rs 30,890). The smartphone will be available in China beginning on October 31. The company has not yet released any information on the smartphone's debut in India.
