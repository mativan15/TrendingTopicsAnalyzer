SEOUL, South Korea, May 26, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- With the recent appointment of their new President, the excitement and development around the crypto space in South Korea have continued to grow. One of the blockchain-based digital assets reaching new heights is Nagaya (NGY), which recently achieved a new milestone by being honored with the Grand Prize in the field of Nickel Industry (IT Digital Asset) at the 25th Jang Young-sil International Science and Culture Award, held on 22 April 2022. The Jang Young-sil International Science and Culture Awards ceremony was first held in March 1999 to honor Jang Young-sil, one of the greatest inventors in South Korean history.
