Take Two Interactive acquires mobile gaming giant Zynga for $12.7 billion
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-27 03:45:00

Take Two Interactive, Inc. has announced that it has completed the acquisition of mobile games giant Zynga $12.7 billion. This deal was first announced in January and it will bring Zynga's popular games like -- Farmville and Words With Friends under Take-Two ownership. The terms of this merger deal have also been disclosed, under which each Zynga shareholder has received $3.50 in cash and 0.0406 shares of Take-Two common stock per share of Zynga common stock.
