Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Auto index advances 0.07%

2022-10-20 11:57:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Auto index closed on a positive note on Thursday.Shares of MRF(up 1.23 per cent), Balkrishna Industries(up 1.18 per cent), Eicher Motors(up 1.08 per cent), Bharat Forge(up 1.0 per cent) and Bajaj Auto(up 0.98 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack.On the other hand, TVS Motor Company(down 2.49 per cent), Ashok Leyland(down 1.43 per cent), TIINDIA(down 1.39 per cent), Sona BLW Precision Forgings(down 0.28 per cent) and Tata Motors(down 0.24 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.
