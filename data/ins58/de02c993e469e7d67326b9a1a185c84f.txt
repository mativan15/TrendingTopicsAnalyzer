Anthony Albanese is wasting no time racking up the air miles on 'Toto 1″ – as the Prime Ministerial plane now goes by. After jetting to Tokyo for the Quad Leaders' summit within days of Labor's election victory (with votes still being counted), the Prime Minister is now in Jakarta where he hopes to strengthen ties with Indonesian President Joko Widodo.Albanese and Foreign Minister Penny Wong's relentless touring schedule is a sign the government hopes to re-establish relationships it believes were left to wither under the Morrison government.
