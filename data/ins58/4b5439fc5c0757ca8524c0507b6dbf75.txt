Lowe's raises dividend, to boost implied yield above 2%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-27 13:08:19

Shares of Lowe's Companies LOW, +1.51% rose 0.8% in premarket trading Friday, after the home improvement retailer raised its dividend by 31.3%. The new quarterly dividend of $1.05 a share, up from 80 cents, will be payable Aug. 3 to shareholders of record on July 21. Based on Thursday's stock closing price of $195.53, the new annual dividend rate implies a dividend yield of 2.15%, which compares with the yield for rival Home Depot Inc.'s stock HD, +0.81% of 2.51% and the implied yield for the S&P 500 SPX, +0.
