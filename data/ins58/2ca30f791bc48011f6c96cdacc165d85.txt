October 20, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Wayfair Inc. (NYSE:W), one of the world's largest destinations for the home, today announced its expanded Shop Sustainably offering, which allows shoppers to easily find sustainable products that adhere to a wide-variety of third-party certifications. With more than 50 third-party sustainability certifications to-date, Wayfair is home to the largest variety of sustainability certifications featured by a home retailer.



Customers can now shop more than 12,000 Shop Sustainably products on Wayfair and across Wayfair's family of brands by filtering for categories such as water-efficient, nontoxic, cruelty-free, fair trade, and more.
