Analysts see 15,900 to continue to pose as the immediate resistance. The share price of Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Ltd. dropped 0.86 per cent to Rs 384.1 at 02:11PM (IST) in Wednesday's trade. The stock has hit a high of Rs 387.55 and low of Rs 381.65 so far during the session. The stock had closed at Rs 387.45 in the previous session. The counter has had a total traded volume of 8824 shares so far with a value of Rs 0.34 crore on the NSE. It traded at a price-to-earnings multiple of 40.
