PTINew Delhi: Union Minister for Commerce and Industry Piyush Goyal with Minister of Commerce, Industry and Investment Promotion of Oman, Qais Bin Mohammed Al Yousef during the 10th session of the India-Oman Joint Commission Meeting, in New Delhi. (PTI Photo/Arun Sharma)(Government and business leaders in India and the UAE have held a series of high-level meetings focused on opportunities within the energy transition and industrial growth. The meetings were held on the sidelines of UAE Climate Envoy and Minister of Industry and Advanced Technology Dr Sultan Al Jaber's visit to New Delhi on Thursday, UAE's official news agency Wam said.
