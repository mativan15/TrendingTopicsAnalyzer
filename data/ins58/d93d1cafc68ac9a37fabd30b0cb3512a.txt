NEW YORK, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global mastopexy market size is projected to arrive at a value of around USD 2,175 Million by 2030, with a CAGR of above 5.8% from 2022 to 2030. According to the Aesthetic Society Organization, a mastopexy (breast lift) is an aesthetic operation that elevates or repositions loose, drooping breasts while also improving the shape and size of the breasts. Thousands of women have effective breast lift procedures each year. The surge in medical tourism fuels the global mastopexy market The surge in medical tourism is one of the factors driving the market growth.
