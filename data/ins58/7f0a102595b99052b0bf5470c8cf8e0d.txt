Why Shares of American Express Are Falling This Morning
fool.com
news
2022-10-21 14:00:55

Shares of credit card and payments company American Express (AXP -4.58%) traded roughly 4.7% lower as of 9:37 a.m. ET today after the company reported earnings for the third quarter of the year.
So what
American Express reported diluted earnings per share (EPS) of $2.47 on total revenue of about $13.56 billion for the third quarter. Earnings beat expectations, while revenue was roughly in line.
Total network volume of $394 billion fell slightly from the previous quarter but is up 19% year over year.
