Tool visualizes the public health impact of the discriminatory practice of 'redlining'
phys.org
business
2022-06-03 14:25:03

In this map, the most poorly rated neighborhoods under the Home Owners' Loan Corporation (outlined in red) are shown to experience the highest exposures to diesel particulate matter, whereas the highest rated neighborhoods under the Home Owners' Loan Corporation (outlined in green) experience among the lowest exposures to diesel particulate matter. Credit: Environmental Racism: A Tool for Exploring the Enduring Legacy of Redlining on Urban Environments 
The long-term, systemic consequences of structural racism can be challenging to see, quantify, and communicate.
