June 01, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time IRVINE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--VIZIO Holding Corp. (NYSE: VZIO) today announced that Adam Townsend, Chief Financial Officer, will participate in a fireside chat at the BofA Securities 2022 Global Technology Conference on June 7, 2022 at 11:00 a.m. PT.

A live webcast will be available in the events section of VIZIO's Investor Relations website at https://investors.vizio.com/events. A replay of the webcast will also be available following the event.
