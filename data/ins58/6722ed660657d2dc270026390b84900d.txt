Florida Panthers defenceman Aaron Ekblad during a game against the Boston Bruins, at TD Garden, in Boston, on Oct. 17.Maddie Meyer/Getty ImagesThe Florida Panthers will be without top defenceman Aaron Ekblad until at least mid-November, and quite possibly longer, after the team putting him on the long-term injured reserve list with a groin injury.Players on the LTIR list must miss at least 10 games and 24 days; the Panthers have 11 games in that 24-day window, so Ekblad is out for at least that many.
