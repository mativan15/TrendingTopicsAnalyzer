Those who are out looking for firecrackers this year are returning with no luck as their usual shopping hubs were found without traders. With several government restrictions, the sales of firecrackers have plummeted by almost 80% in the city, according to dealers. With increased demand and short supply, the prices of firecrackers have also gone up by 40-50%. New varieties of crackers like Cheers, Cracking king, Singing birds, break dance, rocket Cyclone have hit the markets this year.
