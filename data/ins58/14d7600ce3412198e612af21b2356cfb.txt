Published: 02:29 EDT, 28 May 2022 | Updated: 02:29 EDT, 28 May 2022 The son of legendary AFL footy news man Mike Sheahan faces a stint behind bars after stealing an Uber driver's vehicle in a terrifying attack. Daily Mail Australia can reveal Tony Sheahan, 45, was almost four times the legal limit to drive when he hijacked Uber driver Janaka Pathirana's car at Bondi Beach, east of Sydney, just before Christmas. The gossip columnist did not even have a licence when he decided to take off in the hard-working driver's Holden Captiva.
