Corporate earnings continued to remain healthy in 4QFY22 and instilled a ray of hope, amid the grim situation plagued by various disruptions. Earnings for 4QFY22 were in line with MOFSL expectations, despite the global and domestic economic environments grappling with challenges like geopolitical conflict, a surge in energy prices, raw material inflation, chip shortage, monetary tightening, etc. While the aggregate growth appeared impressive, it was hardly broad-based and driven only by three sectors: BFSI, O&G, and Metals.
