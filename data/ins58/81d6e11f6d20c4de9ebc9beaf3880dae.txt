Meghan's Paris Hilton interview revealed relatable "stories of triumph"
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-20 11:05:24

Meghan Markle's recent interview with Paris Hilton on the sixth episode of her hit solo podcast Archetypes revealed an example of "stories of triumph" that "anyone is able to relate to," furthering the duchess's goal of "giving women a voice," a podcasting expert told Newsweek.The royal's interview with Hilton formed the central basis of the podcast episode titled "Breaking Down the Bimbo," in which the pair unpicked the public persona that the star revealed she had adopted as a result of time spent in an abusive school environment and negative media attention.
