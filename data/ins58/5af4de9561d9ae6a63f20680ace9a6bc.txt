Sundry Photography/iStock Editorial via Getty Images CrowdStrike (NASDAQ:CRWD) is scheduled to announce Q1 earnings results on Thursday, June 2nd, after market close. The cybersecurity firm posted Q4 earnings and outlook that topped Wall Street expectations. Annual recurring revenue (ARR) came in at $1.73B, up 65% year-over-year, topping analysts' expectations for $1.68B. At the time, CrowdStrike posted a guidance of $1.03-$1.13 in EPS and $2.13B-$2.16B in revenue for FY23. The firm is poised to continue to benefit from the ongoing Russia-Ukraine crisis, with Wedbush Securities analyst Dan Ives suggesting "a golden age for the cybersecurity sector" in the months and years ahead.
