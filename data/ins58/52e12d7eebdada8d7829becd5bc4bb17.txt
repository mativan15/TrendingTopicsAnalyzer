Even in a bear market, Nick Maggiulli thinks you should Just Keep Buying. That's the title of his recently released book, based in part on his popular blog, Of Dollars and Data. "I started writing one blog post a week in early 2017 as a New Year's resolution," says Maggiulli, who graduated from Stanford University with an Economics degree. "It's the only one I've ever kept. I just kept writing, and now I'm on week 293.
