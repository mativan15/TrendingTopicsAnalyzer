Published: 08:14 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 08:15 EDT, 20 October 2022 FAYETTEVILLE, Ark. (AP) - With two consecutive Elite Eight appearances in tow, Arkansas´ hopes for the Final Four aren´t just a pipe dream. Coach Eric Musselman, heading into his fourth season in charge of the Razorbacks, has two returning players from last year´s rotation, only one of which is a starter in guard Devo Davis. But he pulled in the No. 2 class in the nation, headlined by projected lottery pick and first-team preseason All-SEC guard Nick Smith Jr.
