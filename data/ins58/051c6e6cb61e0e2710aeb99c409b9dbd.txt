Trehan Home Developers to develop 1.3 lakh sq ft of retail area in Alwar
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 07:30:00

Realty firm Trehan Home Developers Private Limited (THD) will invest about Rs 35 crore to develop a shopping mall at Alwar in Rajasthan as demand for retail spaces in tier 2 towns have increased. The construction work in the shopping mall 'Urban Square', comprising a leasable area of 1.3 lakh square feet, will be completed by March next year. This will be the largest mall at Alwar.
