5 Tips to Lower Your Air Conditioning Bill While You're Away on Vacation
cnet.com
news
2022-05-31 11:00:17

When you leave for a summer trip, you might be tempted to shut down the air conditioning to save some money on your electricity bill. But as outdoor temperatures soar, this can result in an overheated, humid home that you'll dread returning to. You don't have to spend a fortune to keep your house cool. With a little help from technology ranging from window shades to smart thermostats -- and a minimum of planning -- you can switch off the lights and enjoy your vacation without worrying about energy prices.
