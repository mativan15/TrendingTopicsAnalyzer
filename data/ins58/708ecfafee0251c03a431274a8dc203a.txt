Bands pan Taylor Swift's "Midnights," but is she to blame for vinyl delays?
newsweek.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 23:56:58

Taylor Swift's latest album, Midnights, came out Friday, and it shipped in a number of different colored versions across formats—including four marbled vinyl versions in "Moonstone Blue," "Blood Moon" red, "Mahogany" and "Jade Green." A lavender edition is also available at Target stores.However, many indie bands have faced delays of a year or more getting their albums pressed—and some have accused larger acts like Swift of jumping the queue. But is Swift to blame for the delays in getting records pressed?Late Thursday night, Damon Krukowski, of the bands Damon & Naomi and Galaxie 500, made a viral tweet, calling out Swift's multiple vinyl editions.
