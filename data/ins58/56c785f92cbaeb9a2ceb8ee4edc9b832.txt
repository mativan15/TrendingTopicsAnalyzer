Published: 16:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 16:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 TEMPE, Ariz. (AP) - Bobby Hurley had built Arizona State into a contender, taking the program to the cusp of a third straight NCAA Tournament in 2020. The pandemic cut that bid short and the Sun Devils haven't been the same since. That could change this season, a pivotal one for Hurley's tenure in the desert. Adding key transfers to a solid core of returning players, Arizona State has the type of roster that could earn the Sun Devils their first NCAA Tournament berth since 2019.
