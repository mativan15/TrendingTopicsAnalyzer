Brits face uncertain future as Truss quits, too – DW – 10/21/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-21 19:41:41

Westminster is braced for yet another whirlwind week after the chaotic demise of Prime Minister Liz Truss, who tendered her resignation on Thursday after just six weeks in office. The Conservative Party is gearing up to choose a new leader by October 28. The next prime minister would be Britain's third this year and the fifth in six years.  Political commentators may be excited to watch as all-too-familiar front-runners vie yet again for the top job in British politics. But all jokes aside about the livestreamed lettuce in a wig that managed to outlast Truss, the country is in dangerous waters: It faces a cost of living crisis and serious financial turmoil, for a start.
