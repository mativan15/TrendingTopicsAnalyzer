President Xi Jinping just delivered China's most authoritative policy blueprint for the next five years at a Communist Party congress where he's set to clinch a third term in power.

During a nearly two hour-speech, Xi laid out the ruling party's priorities on everything from Covid Zero to its ambitions on Taiwan and goals for tech sufficiency.
