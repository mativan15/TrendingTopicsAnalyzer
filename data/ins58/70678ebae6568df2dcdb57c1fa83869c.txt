REUTERS/Anushree Fadnavis/File PhotoNEW DELHI, Oct 20 (Reuters) - India's capital market regulator approved the government's proposal to convert dues of over $1.92 billion by telecom operator Vodafone Idea (VODA.NS) to equity, two senior government officials said on Thursday.Last year, India had approved a rescue package for debt-strapped telecom companies that allowed them to convert interest on deferred adjusted gross revenue owed to the government into equity.
