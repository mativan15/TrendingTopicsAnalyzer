Variety - Film News Gene Levy, former V.P. of Fox Pictures and producer of over 40 films and television series, died on Oct. 15 in Las Vegas. He was 84.Levy produced an array of films spanning over five decades, including 'The Bachelor,' 'Waterworld,' 'Unlawful Entry,' 'Streets of Fire,' 'Brewster's Millions,' 'Blood Voyage,' 'Hysterical,' 'Judgement Night' and 'Trojan War.'
