October 17, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time GLENDALE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Loop Media, Inc. ('Loop Media' or 'Loop') (NYSE American: LPTV), a leading multichannel streaming platform that provides curated music video and branded entertainment channels for businesses and consumers, announced today that Chief Revenue Officer Bob Gruters will appear on a discussion panel as part of Advertising Week in New York City, on Tuesday, October 18 from 1:15pm-1:45pm on the Insights Arena Stage at 200 Broome Street.
