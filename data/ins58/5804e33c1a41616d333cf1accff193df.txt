A FURIOUS mum has slammed teachers who chopped up her teen daughter's false eyelashes and made her a laughing stock in front of her friends. 
Martina Cantor, 37,  has hit out at Harris Academy Beckenham, in south-east London, after her daughter Rhenne was pulled aside for wearing false eyelashes. 
Teachers at Martina Cantor's school cut her daughter Rhenne's eyelashes
The-mum-of-three says they had a big chunk taken out of the middle
Rhenne wears fake eyelashes sometimes because her natural ones aren't very long
The mum-of-three says teachers took a pair of scissors to her daughter's lashes after a teacher spotted they were glued to her real ones.
