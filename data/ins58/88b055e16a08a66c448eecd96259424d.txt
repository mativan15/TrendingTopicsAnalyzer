It also asked Google to provide fair access to all stakeholders (Illustration: Rahul Awasthi)A Rs 1,337.76-crore penalty on Google, and a Rs 392-crore fine on travel and hospitality sites MakeMyTrip, GoIbibo and Oyo are the latest moves in the Competition Commission of India's (CCI) crackdown on tech companies as the antitrust watchdog looks to rein in anti-competitive practices in the sector.
