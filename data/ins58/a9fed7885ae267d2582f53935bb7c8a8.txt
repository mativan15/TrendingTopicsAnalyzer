Published: 06:08 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 06:08 EDT, 30 May 2022 Barcelona's planned summer rebuild has hit a snag with the club reportedly unable to register free signings Andreas Christensen and Franck Kessie. With Barcelona looking to embark on a summer rebuild under the promising tutelage of Xavi Hernandez, the club has looked to move early in the transfer market snapping up value where possible. However, owing to their present financial situation, the club is unable to register two players they had believed they had signed.
