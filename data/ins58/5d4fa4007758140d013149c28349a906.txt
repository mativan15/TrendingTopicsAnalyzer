'The best team we've played': Ashland wary of dangerous Tiffin in G-MAC football showdown

The Ashland University football team has proven itself throughout a six-game winning streak to start the season. Now comes the biggest test so far.

The Eagles will play host to Tiffin in their 98th homecoming game at 1 p.m. Saturday with the outright lead in the Great Midwest Athletic Conference on the line. AU is 6-0 overall, while Tiffin has suffered two close losses in a 4-2 season. Both teams are 4-0 in the G-MAC.
