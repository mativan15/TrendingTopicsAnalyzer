Varun Beverages, ABB, Page Inds, Tata Elxsi tipped to be large-caps in AMFI list
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 05:00:00

As per the Amfi, a large-cap stock is defined as 1-100 in rank based on the six months average market capitalisation, while 101-250 stocks are classified as midcaps.Mumbai: Varun Beverages, ABB, Tata Elxsi, and Page Industries are likely to find a place in the large-cap category in the Association of Mutual Funds in India's (AMFI's) semi-annual re-categorisation. Timken, Metro Brands, Blue Dart Express, and Fine Organic are expected to enter the mid-cap category, according to Nuvama Alternative & Quantitative Research.
