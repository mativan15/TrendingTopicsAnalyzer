RH: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-06-02 20:33:13

CORTE MADERA, Calif. (AP) _ RH (RH) on Thursday reported fiscal first-quarter profit of $200.7 million. On a per-share basis, the Corte Madera, California-based company said it had net income of $12.16. Earnings, adjusted for one-time items, were $7.78 per share. The results beat Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of nine analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $5.46 per share. The furniture and housewares company posted revenue of $957.3 million in the period, also beating Street forecasts.
