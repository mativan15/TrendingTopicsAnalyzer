Alberta Health Minister Jason Copping addresses the media in Edmonton on Sept. 21, 2021.JASON FRANSON/The Canadian PressThe Alberta government has approved a bid by the Enoch Cree Nation near Edmonton to build a private clinic to perform thousands of publicly covered hip and knee surgeries.Health Minister Jason Copping says the clinic is to be built by the middle of next year to reduce a backlog of orthopedic operations in and around the capital.The clinic will be a partnership between the Enoch Cree and Surgical Centres Inc.
