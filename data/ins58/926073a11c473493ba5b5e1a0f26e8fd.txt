October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Shake Shack Inc. ('Shake Shack' or the 'Company') (NYSE:SHAK) today announced the appointment of Lori George Billingsley to its Board of Directors, effective October 17, 2022. Billingsley has been named to the Nominating & Corporate Governance Committee of the Board. She will become the eleventh member of Shake Shack's Board of Directors, following Tristan Walker's appointment in 2020.



'We are pleased to name Lori to Shake Shack's Board of Directors,' said Randy Garutti, Shake Shack CEO.
