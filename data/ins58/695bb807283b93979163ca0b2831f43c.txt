June 01, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Q4 Inc. (TSX: QFOR) ('Q4'), a leading capital markets communications platform provider, is excited to announce the launch of Q4 Capital Connect™, a revolutionary, scalable platform built to streamline workflows and provide data-driven strategic insights across the capital markets.



Q4 Capital Connect™ enables public companies across all industries and market caps to seamlessly launch and measure the impact of a complete investor relations strategy.
