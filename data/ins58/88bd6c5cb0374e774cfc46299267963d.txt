By Reuters Published: 21:29 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 21:29 EDT, 19 October 2022 SYDNEY, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Australian sailor Mat Belcher, who won two gold medals and a silver in the 470 class over three Summer Games, has decided not to compete at the 2024 Paris Olympics and announced his retirement on Thursday. The eight-times 470 world champion considered having a crack at a fourth Summer Games, when the event will be mixed sex for the first time, but instead called time on his 22-year career with the Australian sailing team.
