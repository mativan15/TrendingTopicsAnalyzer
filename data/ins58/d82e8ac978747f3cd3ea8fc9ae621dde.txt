Published: 06:26 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 06:27 EDT, 30 May 2022 A waitress with PTSD has won almost £20,000 after she was harshly sacked by her boss while on maternity leave.Kirsty Blyth said she visited Black Rooster Peri Peri restaurant in Coatbridge, near Glasgow, with her nine-week-old baby only to be handed a letter telling her she had been sacked. The 26-year-old was given her job back after launching legal action, but in the end her boss told her she would not be allowed to return to work.
