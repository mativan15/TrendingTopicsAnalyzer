KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy iHeartMedia's Corey OlsonOct 18, 2022While the Biden border crisis is costing American taxpayers billions, it's doing just the opposite for Mexican drug cartels. The criminal cartels that once thrived on the drug trade are now finding the human trade just as lucrative, thanks to the millions of foreigners rushing to take advantage of Biden's open border policy. "More than five million people have crossed through their territory, and have been charged anywhere from $2,500 per head, to as much as 9, 10, 11, or $12,000 a head," says Todd Bensman, senior fellow at the Center for Immigration Studies.
