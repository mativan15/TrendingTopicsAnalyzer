[Photo/Xinhua] Eyes now on National Congress to show where country's path lies There is nothing quite like a crisis to expose the weaknesses in systems and societies. Last year, a number of countries promised to lessen reliance on fossil fuels and announced ambitious net-zero targets. The backpedaling on many of these commitments has been furious ever since the Russia-Ukraine conflict triggered a dip in supplies and a hike in natural gas prices.
