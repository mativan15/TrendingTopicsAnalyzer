Canton Fair helps stabilize industrial and supply chains

By Yang Yang | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-17 13:25 The 132nd session of the Canton Fair kicks off online on Oct 15, attracting over 35,000 domestic and overseas companies, and uploading over 3.06 million pieces of exhibits in Guangzhou, South China's Guangdong province. [Photo/Xinhua] The expanded and upgraded virtual Canton Fair, or China Import and Export Fair, helps stabilize industrial and supply chains for China and the world, China News Network reported on Sunday. As the essential exhibition, matchmaking and trading platform of the whole industrial chain for products "Made in China", the 132nd session of Canton Fair will continue to contribute to the stability of the industrial and supply chains in China and the world, the report said.
