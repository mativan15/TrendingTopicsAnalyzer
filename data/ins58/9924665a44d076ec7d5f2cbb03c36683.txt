Believe it or not, there are some stocks that are actually up in 2022, particularly in the energy sector where rising oil prices have been a catalyst. However, one stock that could end up being an even bigger beneficiary of the 2022 bear market is Berkshire Hathaway (BRK.A 0.66%)(BRK.B 0.88%).
To be sure, Berkshire hasn't been spared in the recent market volatility. While shares are slightly higher for the year, Berkshire is about 13% below its highs. But there are a few good reasons to believe Berkshire will emerge from this downturn a stronger company than ever.
