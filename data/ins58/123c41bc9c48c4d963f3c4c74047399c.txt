With the BBC celebrating 100 years of broadcasting, here is a timeline of key moments:– 1922The British Broadcasting Company, as the BBC was initially called, was formed on October 18 1922 by a group of leading wireless manufacturers, including Marconi.– 1936The BBC was the first broadcaster in the world to provide a high definition television service starting on November 2.– 1942Desert Island Discs launches with broadcaster Roy Plomley who presented the first edition on January 29 with comedian Vic Oliver.
