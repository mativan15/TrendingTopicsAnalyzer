 Researchers at the Indian Institute of Technology, Mandi, claim to have developed cooperative spectrum sensors that will provide state-of-the-art solutions in telecommunication technology and enhance the reusability of radio-frequency spectrum to meet the growing demands of data communication in the future 5G and 6G wireless communication applications.
