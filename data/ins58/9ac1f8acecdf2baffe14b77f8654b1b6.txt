ICYMI: Secretary Granholm Visited Tampa to Tout the Benefits of Clean Energy Innovation and Deployment
energy.gov
energy
2022-06-03 21:12:29

WASHINGTON, D.C. — Yesterday, U.S. Secretary of Energy Jennifer M. Granholm traveled to Tampa, Florida to highlight how clean energy infrastructure is critical to lowering costs for Americans and reiterate the importance of getting novel technologies to the domestic marketplace to reduce our reliance on volatile energy sources.  Secretary Granholm toured the Hillsborough River Dam with U.S. Representative Kathy Castor (FL-14) and Tampa Mayor Jane Castor where Mayor Castor announced plans to build two hydroelectric turbines on the river as part of the city's clean energy deployment.
