A 39-year-old man fell into the water at Devil's Lake while getting ready to go tubing with his family. As per the office of the Lenawee Country Sheriff, two families from Ohio were spending the Memorial Day weekend at a cottage that they had rented near the lake. The missing man was getting ready to go tubing with his daughter just before he fell into the lake at around 2 pm. Authorities said that although his daughter was wearing a life jacket, the man was not wearing one and did not resurface after he went underwater after falling off the tube.
