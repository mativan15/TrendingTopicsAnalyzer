Credit Suisse to Pay $495M in US Settlement
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 06:31:00

By Pierre Bertrand Credit Suisse Group AG said Monday that is has reached a $495 million settlement with the New Jersey Attorney General regarding transactions related to its residential mortgage-backed securities business. Under the agreement, the Swiss lender will make the one-time payment resolving claims made in 2013 by the eastern U.S. state that the company had made false and misleading statements about the quality of more than $10 billion of residential mortgage-backed securities. The New Jersey Attorney General had alleged $3 billion in damages, Credit Suisse said.
