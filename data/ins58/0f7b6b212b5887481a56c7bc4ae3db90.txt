Email is an essential tool for digital marketers, despite the proliferation of new channels. Beyond direct advertising, email gives marketers unique opportunities to engage customers with newsletters, surveys, and special offers. It's a proven method of increasing customer acquisition, retention, and engagement. But to succeed at email marketing means being strategic, and for that, you'll need tools to help you compose, send, and track email, plus integrate email campaign data with your other backend systems.
