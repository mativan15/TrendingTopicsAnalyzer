Economist Impact's second annual Sustainability Week US looks at how the promise of a green future can be kept

Over 150 leading figures from across the sustainability community, from business to intergovernmental organisations, investors, national governments, central bankers and civil society, are confirmed to speak at this year's event. Anita Bhatia, assistant secretary-general of the United Nations and deputy executive director for UN Women, will give her keynote remarks to open the virtual event at 9:45 a.m. ET, Monday, June 6th. Tuesday's in-person event will kick-off with a fireside chat at 8:35 a.m.
