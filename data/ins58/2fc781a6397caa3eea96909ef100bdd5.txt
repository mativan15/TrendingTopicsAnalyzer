Travel firm takes tourists for a ride, pays the price; district commission asks it to refund Rs 2.97 lakh
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-15 22:24:12

The District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission of Chandigarh has directed a travel firm to pay Rs 2.97 lakh to two complainants, whose tour was cancelled due to the outbreak of Covid-19 in March 2020, but the firm did not refund the money.
V K Meena of Mandi (Himachal Pradesh) alleged in his complaint that he had availed of a tour package from travel firm Yatra.com for three people for five days and four-night stay at Andaman Islands. This included airline tickets, hotel stay, travel arrangements, etc.
