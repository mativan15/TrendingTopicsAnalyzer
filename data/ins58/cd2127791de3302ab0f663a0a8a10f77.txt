NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The "Tubes and Cores Market in North America by End-user and Geography - Forecast and Analysis 2022-2026" report has been added to Technavio's offering. Technavio has considered various factors including the consumer adoption rate, revenue generated by vendors, growth in the packaging industry, per capita income, and commodity price fluctuation among others to estimate the size of the market.
