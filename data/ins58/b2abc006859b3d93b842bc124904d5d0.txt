The Carolina Panthers were already bad, which explains why they fired Matt Rhule earlier in the month.
They are now even worse with the Christian McCaffrey trade.
But that's the idea! When you're terrible in the NFL, especially when you don't have a franchise quarterback waiting in the wings, you have to tank and accrue as much draft capital as you can to reboot the team.
Now that McCaffrey and Robbie Anderson were dealt, we looked over the Panthers roster and identified a few other players the front office should deal to continue the tank job.
