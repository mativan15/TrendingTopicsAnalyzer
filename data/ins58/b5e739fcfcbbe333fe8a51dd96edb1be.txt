Salary in certain sectors to reach pre-pandemic level by fiscal-end: TeamLease HRTech CEO
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 12:30:00

Salary level in certain sectors are expected to be restored to the pre-pandemic mark by the end of this financial year aided by the recovery in economic activities, according to TeamLease HRTech CEO Sumit Sabharwal said. Hit by the pandemic, organisations across sectors resorted to pay cuts and layoffs to reduce their labour costs, Sabharwal said.
