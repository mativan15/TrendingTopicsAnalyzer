The Company filed on SEDAR its financial statements ("FS") and related management discussion and analysis ("MD&A") for first quarter results ending March 31, 2022 ("Q1 2022").

Highlights:

Concierge Medical generated $207,624 in revenue

As of March 31, 2022, the Company acquired over $1 million in Cash, an increase of 396% since December 31, 2021

As of March 31, 2022, the Company holds over $11 million in total assets, an increase of 145% since December 31, 2021

2022 Financial Summary

Three Months Ended March 31, 2022
