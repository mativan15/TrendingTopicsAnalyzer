The UK's finance minister Jeremy Hunt will lay out details of some of the government's tax plans Monday.

The policy details come two weeks early, after abrupt tax U-turns and the sacking of Hunt's predecessor.

The pound rose Monday, after weeks where the tax plans roiled markets as investors fretted about stability.

The UK's newly appointed finance minister Jeremy Hunt will lay out details of the Truss government's tax plans Monday, seen as a move to try to settle markets still jittery about their impact on the economy.
