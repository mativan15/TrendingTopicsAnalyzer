MLS enjoys record breaking viewership as Apple prepare to claim broadcast rights

Fans have tuned in to watch Major League Soccer more regular during 2022, and Apple will be hoping to continue that trend as they take on the broadcasting rights from next seasonStars such as Gareth Bale and Giorgio Chiellini have helped the MLS growMajor League Soccer has seen domestic views soar this season, recording their best figures since 2007. During the 2022 regular season, an average of 343,000 people have tuned in across both ABC and ESPN networks, a 16 percent rise from the 295,000 recorded last year.
