US Factories Operating at Full Steam Are Risking More Inflation
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-18 16:04:47

(Bloomberg) -- US factory utilization last month matched the highest level in more than two decades, raising the risk that inflationary pressures will remain elevated in the near term.The capacity utilization rate for manufacturers rose to 80% in September, up from 79.7% a month earlier, Federal Reserve data showed Tuesday. The increase coincided with a second-straight 0.4% gain in factory output during the month.Utilization rates are a measure of operating efficiency, illustrating a relationship between actual production and its full potential.
