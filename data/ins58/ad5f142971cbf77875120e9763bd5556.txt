Elon Musk to staff: Return to office or ‘pretend to work' elsewhere
euronews.com
economics
2022-06-02 11:30:25

Tesla CEO Elon Musk has asked employees to return to the office full-time or leave the company, according to an email sent to staff."Everyone at Tesla is required to spend a minimum of 40 hours in the office per week," Musk wrote in the email sent on Tuesday night, which was seen by Reuters. "If you don't show up, we will assume you have resigned"."The more senior you are, the more visible must be your presence,' Musk wrote. 'That is why I lived in the factory so much - so that those on the line could see me working alongside them.
