Wolves 600179023 Matt Lloyd will get a promotion in title as he moves from Florida to Minnesota.  By Chris Hine Star Tribune June 3, 2022 — 3:53pm 
 Matt Lloyd, the vice president of basketball operations for the Orlando Magic, is in line to become a senior vice president of basketball operations for the Timberwolves. Lloyd has been with the Magic since 2012 and would be the first major addition to new Wolves president of basketball operations Tim Connelly's front office, ESPN reported on Friday.
