As Truss admitted defeat, her potential replacements were scattered far and wide
telegraph.co.uk

2022-10-20 17:25:00

Liz Truss's parting words were, like her tenure, brief. Are resignation speeches in proportion to time served? It took less than three minutes for her to break George Canning's record as the shortest-serving prime minister in history. She'd been outlived by the (only slightly wilted) Daily Star lettuce and even her own leadership campaign.
Other things that survived PM Truss include various perishables in my fridge, Boris Johnson's most recent vow of marital fidelity and the roadworks on the Harrow Road.
