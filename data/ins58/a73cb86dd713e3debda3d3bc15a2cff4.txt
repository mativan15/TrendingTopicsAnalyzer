 Guelph Police say a pair of commercial break-ins over the weekend are related.
They were called to a business on Saturday morning on Silvercreek Parkway at Woodlawn Road after staff found a glass front door smashed.While it is not believed the suspect gained entry, police say damage is pegged at around $5,000.
