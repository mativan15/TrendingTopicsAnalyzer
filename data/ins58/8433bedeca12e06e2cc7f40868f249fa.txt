Time to ditch petrol? Electric vehicles now more affordable and available
smh.com.au
finance
2022-10-17 22:29:45

Electric vehicles (EVs) are famously quick off the mark. However, when it comes to going electric, Australia has not been. But we're finally putting our foot down.

September was a watershed month: Tesla outsold Volkswagen for the first time ever; an Aussie billionaire invested $10 million in a company that's importing second-hand EVs in bulk; and the federal government released an overdue strategy to get more EVs delivered down under.

About 4 in 10 Australians say their next car will be electric or hybrid.
