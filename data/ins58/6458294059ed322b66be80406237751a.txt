Source: TOI.in

The Visakha Garjana rally was held amidst heavy downpour in Visakhapatnam on October 15. This was yet another rally, attended by enormous number of individuals from all walks of life, in support of the state government of Andhra Pradesh's three-capital plan and the idea of decentralisation. Such rallies are held almost every week in the city on the issue of the three capitals. The rally call was given by Joint action committee with majority of the gathering was allegedly done by YSRCP ministers who targeted Telugu Desam Party (TDP) during their speeches.
