Piramal Pharma gets locked in lower circuit
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 02:30:00

Piramal Pharma, recently demerged from , was listed on Wednesday. Its shares dropped 5% — the lowest permissible trading limit of the day — to close at 191.75 on the BSE. The stock was listed at 201.80 and fell 5% in the first 10 minutes of trade. Nearly 5.9 million shares exchanged hands on the BSE and NSE before the stock froze at the lowest designated price for Wednesday. The closing price is roughly a 10% discount to the fair value price assigned by some of the domestic brokerages. 'Piramal Pharma has been facing business headwinds over the past 15-18 months,' said Motilal Oswal Financial Services analyst Tushar Manudhane in a client note.
