iStockSnowman Logistics will provide distribution and consolidation services, including sourcing on behalf of its customers, in addition to the existing bouquet of 3PL services of transportation and warehousing.MUMBAI: Snowman Logistics Limited has launched of Fifth-Party Logistics or 5PL services in India and has become the first company to introduce this service in the cold chain logistics and supply chain management sector in the country. The need for 5PL services has grown incrementally in the last couple of years due to the development of e-commerce businesses, the rise in international trade activities, and the increased complexities in supply chain.
