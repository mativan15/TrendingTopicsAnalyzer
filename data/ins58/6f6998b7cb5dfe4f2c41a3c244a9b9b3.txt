By Christopher Baker October 16, 2022 / 10:32 PM / CBS Sacramento Sacramento County Jail on lockdown after officers exposed to Fentanyl Sacramento County Jail on lockdown after officers exposed to Fentanyl 00:28 SACRAMENTO — The Sacramento County Jail has been put on lockdown after officers were exposed to Fentanyl.According to a County Jail representative, two Sacramento Police officers were exposed to Fentanyl on money while booking a suspect. A jail officer came over with Narcan to help the police officers but began to feel dizzy.
