 Those who were in downtown Kitchener on Wednesday may have noticed that a number of trees were being taken down by city workers on King Street.
A spokesperson for Kitchener says that the city is removing unhealthy trees while they are doing other work in the downtown core. They said the trees will be replaced as further work continues in the area.
