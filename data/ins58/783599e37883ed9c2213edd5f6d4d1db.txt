Shares of video gaming platform operator Roblox (RBLX -1.64%) spiked by 16.4% this week, according to data provided by S&P Global Market Intelligence, after the company released data for September showing significant year-over-year growth in number of users and user engagement.
Roblox stock has been beaten down over the past year, and investors greeted the positive news from the video game company enthusiastically.
