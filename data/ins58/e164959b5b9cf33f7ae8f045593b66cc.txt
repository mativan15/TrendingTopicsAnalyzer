Kwasi Kwarteng failed to brief Bank of England about risks from mini-budget, MPs told
msn.com
news
2022-10-19 15:38:08

The Bank of England was not briefed properly about the disastrous mini-budget that forced it to launch a £65bn rescue of pension funds, it has told MPs. 
 

 © AP Kwasi Kwarteng


Its deputy governor said the Bank would have warned then-chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng if it believed there was a threat to 'financial stability' – but was unaware of the full scale of tax cuts to be unleashed. 'We did not have a full briefing of the package the night before,' Sir Jon Cunliffe told the Commons treasury committee, of the 23 September 'fiscal event'.
