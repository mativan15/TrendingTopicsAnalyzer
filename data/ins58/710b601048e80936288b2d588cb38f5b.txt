NN Inc. closing California facility as part of strategic shift, effort to improve cost structure
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 17:52:00

NN Inc. is closing a manufacturing facility on the West Coast as part of its shift in focus to growth opportunities in the electric vehicle and electric infrastructure markets. The Charlotte-based diversified industrial company said the move also is tied to ongoing efforts to optimize its manufacturing footprint and improve profitably and cash-flow generation.

The facility in Irvine, California, is one of five plants the company has started shutting down this year. Each closure is expected to be complete by March, NN said in a news release on Oct.
