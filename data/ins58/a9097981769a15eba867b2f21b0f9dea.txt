Photo Italia LLC/iStock Editorial via Getty ImagesEarnings of Arrow Financial Corporation (NASDAQ:AROW) will benefit from decent loan growth this year. On the other hand, the acceleration of loan growth will lead to a higher net provision expense this year compared to last year, which will hurt earnings. Meanwhile, the net interest margin will likely remain stable this year because the benefits of rate hikes will be felt with a lag. Overall, I'm expecting Arrow Financial to report earnings of $3.
