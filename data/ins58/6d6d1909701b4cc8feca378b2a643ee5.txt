Bitdeer has been selected as a winner of the Leader award from SourceForge, the world's largest software reviews and comparison website.

SINGAPORE, May 30, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SourceForge, the world's largest software review and comparison website, today announced that Bitdeer has been awarded as a Spring 2022 Leader in the filed of crypto mining services. This award recognizes exceptional companies and products with outstanding user reviews that puts them in the top fifth percentile of highly reviewed products on SourceForge.
