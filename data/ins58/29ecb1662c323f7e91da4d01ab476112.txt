NEW ORLEANS, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Former Attorney General of Louisiana, Charles C. Foti, Jr., Esq., a partner at the law firm of Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ("KSF"), announces that KSF has commenced an investigation into Tilray, Inc. (NasdaqGS: TLRY).
In January 2019, the Company entered into a significant marketing agreement with Authentic Brands Group LLC ('ABG') in which, among other things, the Company would expand globally as ABG's preferred cannibas supplier, receiving up to almost half of the net revenue and a guaranteed $10M annual payment.
