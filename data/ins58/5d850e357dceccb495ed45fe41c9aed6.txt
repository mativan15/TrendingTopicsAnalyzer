Tory MP 'fed up with soap drama' as Braverman forced out of top job

Liz Truss's embattled premiership has been rocked by the departure of Suella Braverman as home secretary. A Home Office source confirmed that Ms Braverman was out after the Prime Minister made a last-minute cancellation of a trip out of Westminster on Wednesday. Ms Braverman is a figure-head of the right in the party and the exit of a former Tory leadership candidate will create further challenges for Ms Truss as she struggles to maintain her grip on power.Speaking to LBC, Bob Seely said: "I actually just want to apologise, I am really getting fed up with this soap drama as much as your listeners are.
