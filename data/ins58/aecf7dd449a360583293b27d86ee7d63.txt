The board of NetApp, Inc. (NASDAQ:NTAP) has announced that it will pay a dividend of US$0.50 per share on the 27th of July. The dividend yield will be 2.8% based on this payment which is still above the industry average.

NetApp's Payment Has Solid Earnings Coverage

We like to see robust dividend yields, but that doesn't matter if the payment isn't sustainable. Prior to this announcement, NetApp's dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings.
