SAN FRANCISCO, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Ahead of the summer season, Instawork, the leading platform for connecting businesses with hourly workers, is announcing new flexible work opportunities in Los Angeles and Orange County as a direct response to thousands of businesses in great need of skilled workers.  Last year, as Americans began to receive their COVID vaccinations, Los Angeles' tourism and hospitality industry rebounded from its pandemic slump to welcome 40 million people to the area.
