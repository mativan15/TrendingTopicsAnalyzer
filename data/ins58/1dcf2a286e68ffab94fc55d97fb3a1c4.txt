Karnataka invites IT minister Ashwini Vaishnaw to open tech summit
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 15:30:00

Karnataka IT/BT Minister CN Ashwath Narayan on Tuesday met Union IT/BT Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw in New Delhi and invited him to open the silver jubilee edition of the three-day Bengaluru Technological Summit (BTS 2022) beginning November 16. He also briefed the Union Minister about the highlights of the BTS, which is acclaimed as the flagship tech event of the country. Additional Chief Secretary (IT/BT) EV Ramana Reddy and IT/BT director Meena Nagaraj were present on the occasion. Narayan also met senior BJP national general secretary Arun Singh and invited him to attend the unveiling ceremony of the 108-foot statue of Kempe Gowda, the architect of modern Bengaluru, by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on November 11.
