The current labour shortage is just a hint of problems to come, with a lack of skilled tradespeople and digital workers imperiling infrastructure construction and the shift to a greener economy.Aaron Vincent Elkaim/The Canadian PressThe Ontario economy is sailing along smoothly, with the rebound from the pandemic recession well under way.Two years after the dizzying economic whirlwind of the first lockdown, the jobs market is surging, and employers are now complaining of a shortage of workers. The province's once-enormous budget deficit has contracted sharply, and revenues are growing quickly, in part propelled by the effects of inflation.
