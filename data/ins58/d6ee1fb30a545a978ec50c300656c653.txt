The war in Ukraine has become caught up in a struggle over whether its objective is military defeat and probable regime change for Russia, or a peace deal which will recognise Ukraine's huge military achievements but freeze over the current dangerous escalation towards the possible use of nuclear weapons.Those who support victory over Russia are inspired by the Ukrainian resistance to imperialist invasion and believe that the Ukrainian leadership should determine the conditions for a settlement. Their position is exemplified in a Washington Post report on October 12th dealing with the options facing the Biden administration:'Privately US officials say neither Russia nor Ukraine is capable of winning the war outright, but they have ruled out the idea of pushing or even nudging Ukraine to the negotiating table.
