Davis Webb, NFL backup to the stars, sees Daniel Jones' best year coming

College teammate of Patrick Mahomes?  Check.
As an NFL rookie, teammate of Eli Manning?  Check.
The past three years, a teammate of Josh Allen?  Check.
Davis Webb has been in the same meeting room with three greats at the quarterback position, a room he now shares with Daniel Jones in Webb's second go-round with the Giants. Webb's eyes are more educated than most, as he declined the position of quarterback coach with the Bills for the 2022 season to keep his playing career alive for as long as possible.
