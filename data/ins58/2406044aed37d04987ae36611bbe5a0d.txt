Variety - TV News Dot Da Genius spent many years in the early 2000s grinding it out in New York City, living with and producing music for his close friend Scott Mescudi, better known as his hip-hop alias Kid Cudi. He was behind the boards on 'Day ‘n' Nite,' which launched Cudi into the mainstream in 2008 with his debut mixtape 'A Kid Named Cudi.'Dot and the 38-year-old rapper have since maintained a long creative partnership, still trusting one another to this day to explore new artistic mediums together.
