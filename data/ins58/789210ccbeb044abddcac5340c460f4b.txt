U.S. wins latest legal battle to seize Russian superyacht in Fiji
latimes.com
world

WELLINGTON, New Zealand — The U.S. on Friday won the latest round of a legal battle to seize a $325-million Russian-owned superyacht in Fiji, with the case now appearing headed for the Pacific nation's top court. The case has highlighted the thorny legal ground the U.S. finds itself on as it tries to seize assets of Russian oligarchs around the world.
