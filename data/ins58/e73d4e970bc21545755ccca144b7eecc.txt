MongoDB Shares Higher After Guidance Raised, 1Q Beat
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 17:22:00

By Michael Dabaie MongoDB Inc. shares were up 18% to $286.41 in afternoon trading Thursday after the company boosted guidance and reported earnings above analyst views. The database platform company after the bell Wednesday reported first-quarter revenue was up 57% to $285.4 million, beating the FactSet consensus forecast for $268.6 million. The loss per share was $1.14, versus a loss of $1.04 a year earlier. Adjusted EPS came to 20 cents, better than the FactSet consensus for an adjusted loss of 8 cents.
