SAN FRANCISCO, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Tempo Automation, a leading software-accelerated electronics manufacturer, today announced The Globee® Awards, organizers of the world's premier business awards programs and business ranking lists, has named Tempo a winner in the 2022 Disruptor Company Awards. Tempo won the Gold Globee in the Electronics and Semiconductor category and was named one of 10 overall Grand Trophy Winners across all categories. Tempo was honored for transforming electronics manufacturing with its unique automated software manufacturing platform, which optimizes the complex process of printed circuit board manufacturing and assembly.
