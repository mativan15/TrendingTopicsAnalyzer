New York Attorney General Letitia "Tish" James is poised to give Donald Trump a Memorial Day-worthy grilling, thanks to a court ruling on Thursday ordering that he, Ivanka Trump, and Donald Trump, Jr. all sit for sworn depositions. But as James winds down her three-year probe into the Trump Organization — the former president's New York-based hotel, real estate, and golf resort empire — James appears to have greater pyrotechnics in store.Trump doesn't appear to be facing criminal charges. James distanced herself from that idea in a filing two months ago.
