GRAPEVINE, Texas, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--GameStop Corp. (NYSE: GME) ("GameStop" or the "Company") today released financial results for the first quarter ended April 30, 2022. The Company's condensed and consolidated financial statements, including GAAP and non-GAAP results, are below. The Company's Form 10-Q and supplemental information can be found at http://investor.GameStop.com.

FIRST QUARTER OVERVIEW

Net sales were $1.378 billion for the quarter, compared to $1.277 billion in the prior year's first quarter.
