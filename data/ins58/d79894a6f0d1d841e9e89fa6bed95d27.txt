Some of the big-name players from the 2019 NBA draft got their payday — Zion Williamson, Ja Morant, Tyler Herro, Jordan Poole — but many more are now playing for their next contract and are headed to restricted free agency next summer.

What follows is a list of players headed to NBA restricted free agency next summer. That doesn't mean they are on the move, the team with their rights can match any offer (ask Deandre Ayton about that), but another team could try to come in over the top and try to poach them.
