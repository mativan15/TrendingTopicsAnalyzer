The United Kingdom's Imperial War Museum in London recently launched an exhibit that explores what video games can tell us about war. War Games: Real Conflicts | Virtual Worlds | Extreme Entertainment delves into the medium of games, challenging perceptions of how video games interpret stories about war and conflict through a series of titles which, over the last 40 years, have reflected events from the First World War to the present.
