Electric cars in this country face battery challenge: Report

Electric cars in the UK are expected to grow to three million by 2035.
 








UK needs to have a strong battery supply chain immediately to support its EV industry. (BMW)
 

Britain risks falling behind in the race to establish an electric car industry as it is lacking battery factories in a short span, claims Reuters in a report. The report points out that the automakers in the UK need to ramp up production of battery-powered electric vehicles and also build a strong battery manufacturing ecosystem as well.
