French government repeats Parliament-overruling procedure on social security budget

Prime Minister Elisabeth Borne at the Assemblée Nationale in Paris on October 19, 2022. JULIEN MUGUET POUR « LE MONDE » Article 49.3 of the French Constitution, which allows for the government to force legislation through Parliament without a vote, was triggered twice in 24 hours. On Thursday, October 20, Prime Minister Elisabeth Borne, arrived at the Assemblée Nationale at 7:45 pm to once again activate the procedure. Just a day before, she had used it for the first time during deliberations on the state budget.
