Renter Elizabeth Clark knows first hand the financial pain and anxiety of moving without a bond return. The Residential Tenancies Authority says in 2021/22 tenants and residents were refunded an average of 74.9 per cent of their bondsTenants Queensland says cost of living and rent increases have made it "very challenging" for people waiting for their bond It wants the state government to introduce a bond transfer system to help vulnerable renters who move and are still waiting for their old bond"We had no bond to move to another place, so I had to ask my mum and dad if we could borrow the money," Ms Clark said.
