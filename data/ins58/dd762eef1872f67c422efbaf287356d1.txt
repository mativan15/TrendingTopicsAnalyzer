15 amazing all-inclusive winter sun holidays
telegraph.co.uk

2022-10-16 09:00:00

It was with some unease that I checked into an all-inclusive wellness retreat in St Lucia, despite the dreamy palm trees swaying outside. Being tethered to one resort for a week wasn't my usual idea of travel fun. But then I saw the menus. First the ones featuring bottomless cocktails, smoothies, steaks and sushi. Then the overflowing menu of activities: Morning Stretch, Beach Boot Camp, Hatha Yoga, Archery, Spin, Sailing, Jive… And all free. Later, lounging with an icy Piton beer, muscles tingling from my daily massage (included), I figured there were worse places to be 'stuck'.
