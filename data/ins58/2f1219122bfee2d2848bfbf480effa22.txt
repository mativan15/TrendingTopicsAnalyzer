Hungary explores investment opportunities in water, agriculture and food processing, and S & T sectors
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 11:00:00

Hungary has been among India's steadfast partners in Central Europe and the two on Friday agreed to further explore opportunities in various sectors including water, agriculture, food processing, health and S&T. The Hungarian side also signed the International Solar Alliance Framework Agreement coinciding with the visit of its Foreign Affairs and Trade Minister Péter Szijjártó. He was accompanied by a high level business delegation.

Foreign Ministers S Jaishankar and Szijjártó, over delegation level talks, discussed the entire gamut of bilateral relations, including political, economic, cultural and people to people ties.
