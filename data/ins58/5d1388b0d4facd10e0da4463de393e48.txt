BJP to expose parties resorting to short-term politics, says Rajeev Chandrasekhar
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-18 22:37:06

In the upcoming Assembly elections, the Bhara-tiya Janata Party (BJP) would 'expose the parties resorting to short term politics' in Gujarat, said Rajeev Chandrasekhar, Minister of State for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship and Electronics and Information Technology, in Vadodara Tuesday.
Speaking on the sidelines of an event, Chandrasekhar said that the 'vision of PM Narendra Modi' was going to lead the country to a 'new decade'.
On the presence of the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) in Gujarat, Chandrasekhar said, 'Our work and the vision of our leader, Narendra Modi, has brought a change in the country and in Gujarat.
