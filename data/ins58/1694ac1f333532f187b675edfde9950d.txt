Trellix Finds Workforce Shortage Impacts 85% of Organizations' Cybersecurity Posture
apnews.com
tech
2022-06-01 04:02:52

SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--Trellix, the cybersecurity company delivering the future of extended detection and response (XDR), conducted new research into the talent shortage afflicting the cybersecurity industry. Among the key findings, 85% of those surveyed believe the workforce shortage is impacting their organizations' abilities to secure increasingly complex information systems and networks. Of the current workforce, 30% plan to change professions in the future.This press release features multimedia.
