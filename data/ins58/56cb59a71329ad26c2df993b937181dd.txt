Former Stanford standout Christian McCaffrey is returning to the Bay Area after the 49ers acquired him via trade from the Carolina Panthers Thursday night.
San Francisco traded second-, third- and fourth-round picks in the 2023 NFL Draft, as well as a fifth-rounder in 2024 to Carolina in exchange for McCaffrey, who is expected to fly to the Bay Area Friday and join the team, though it is not clear yet whether he will make the quick turnaround to play Sunday against the Chiefs.
Before he arrives, let's take a look at some of his biggest games during his first Northern California stint.
