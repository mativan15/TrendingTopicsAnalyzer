Liz Truss is trying to channel Margaret Thatcher, but why is it not working?

world
2022-10-18 02:01:13

Britain is facing a tough winter ahead. Energy prices are rocketing, the pound has fallen relative to the dollar, mortgage rates have climbed sharply, and the National Health Service is under strain. Industrial action has ground trains to a halt and slowed down Royal Mail delivery. For older Britons, this is all eerily reminiscent of the problems Britain faced during the 'Winter of Discontent' in 1978 and 1979. And new UK Prime Minister Liz Truss is trying to pattern herself on a political figure who rose to prominence during that period: Margaret Thatcher.
