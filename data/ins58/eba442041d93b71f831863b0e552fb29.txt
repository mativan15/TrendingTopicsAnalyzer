NEW DELHI: The March quarter earnings season was a mixed bag for India Inc. with many companies facing input cost pressure that hit their bottomlines. However, there were many who managed to come back in the black during the quarter. A cursory at the data shows there are at least 16 companies that reported profit compared to a loss in the same quarter a year ago.
