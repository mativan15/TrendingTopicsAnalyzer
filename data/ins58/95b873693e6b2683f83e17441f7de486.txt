WESTMINSTER, Colo., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TaskRay, a leading provider of a collaborative work management solution designed for managing post-sale processes, today announced that it has successfully obtained the System and Organization Control (SOC) 2 Type II certification.  This achievement attests to TaskRay's commitment to follow, reinforce, and maintain the strictest controls over time in order to ensure and deliver the highest security and quality of services to its customers. The SOC 2 Type II certification confirms TaskRay implements and aligns with the most stringent security and availability measures as set by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA).
