October 19, 2022 09:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time HONG KONG--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Valuence Group offers life-changing value tailored to all stakeholders, encouraging every person in the world to live true to yourself. Valuence International Ltd. (Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China; Susumu Muguruma, Representative) has announced that the company has changed all overseas Nanboya locations to the ALLU trade name.

Strengthening Retail Sales and Marketing Overseas
Since opening its first office in Hong Kong in July 2019, Valuence has continued to expand its presence overseas.
