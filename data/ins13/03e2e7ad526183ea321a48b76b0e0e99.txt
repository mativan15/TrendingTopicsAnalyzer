LeBron James, the basketball legend with four NBA championships, three Olympic medals and 18 NBA All-Star appearances under his belt, is the first active NBA player to achieve billionaire status. Forbes reported Thursday that following an incredibly lucrative year — raking in just over $121 million – James has officially become a billionaire.
