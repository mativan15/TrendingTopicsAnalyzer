By Reuters Published: 10:40 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 12:05 EDT, 17 October 2022 NEW YORK, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Kevin Spacey took the witness stand on Monday to testify in his own defense in a trial over a civil lawsuit brought by actor Anthony Rapp, who says the Oscar-winning star made an unwanted sexual advance in 1986 when Rapp was 14. Rapp, now 50, testified earlier this month that an intoxicated Spacey - then 26 and acting on Broadway - climbed on top of him at a party at his Manhattan apartment.
