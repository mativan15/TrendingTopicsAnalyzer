Bob DelmontOct 21, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesPhoto Credit: Getty Images Here's today's feel-good story: Isaac Winfield is an 11-year-old that has used his birthday money to help donate food to the needy in his area. Since 2020, the operation has grown so much that Isaac's parents bought a shed so they could store the hundreds of donated items. 'We'd always donated to food banks, and during the pandemic he would take food parcels into school which would be given to people in need,' said his mom Claire.
