EA Kills Off Servers For These Classic Games
ign.com
news
2022-10-18 06:24:16

Electronic Arts will be shutting down online services for a few games in the coming weeks. Between October 20 and January 19, online services for 10 games will be discontinued. Games such as Army of Two: The 40th Day, Army of Two: The Devil's Cartel, Command & Conquer: Red Alert 3, Command & Conquer 3: Tiberium Wars, Gatling Gears, Onrush, Mercenaries 2, Mirror's Edge, NBA Jam On Fire Edition, and Shank 2 will all be affected. These games' online services will be discontinued on the following dates: October 20: Army of Two: The 40th Day and Army of Two: The Devil's Cartel November 9: Command & Conquer: Red Alert 3, Command & Conquer 3: Tiberium Wars, and Mercenaries 2 November 30: Onrush January 19: Gatling Gears, Mirror's Edge, NBA Jam On Fire Edition, and Shank 2
 While the announcement will without a doubt disappoint some gamers, it's unclear how many are still playing any of these games.
