Click here to read the full article.A decade after Robert Downey Jr. earned 50 million for the first Avengers, stepping into a superhero costume remains one of the last ways for an actor to earn a major payday. With rare exception, even A+ stars aren't making what they used to, as first-dollar-gross deals and massive backends have become little more than Hollywood lore.And, more often than not, iconic characters like Spider-Man and Batman are seen by studios as more important and valuable than the famous movie stars who are playing them.
