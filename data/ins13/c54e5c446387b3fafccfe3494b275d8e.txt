AgenciesMazhar Mohammad of Chartviewindia.in said technical indicators are turning mixed as the weekly MACD chart has generated a 'buy' signal, giving an edge to the bulls.Shares of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. traded 3.49 per cent up at Rs 235.7 at around 10:15AM (IST) on Wednesday. The stock had closed at Rs 227.75 in the previous session. Equity benchmark Sensex traded 18.44 points lower at 55547.97 around the same time. The scrip has declined 16.26 per cent in the last one year till date, while the 30-share index has gained 8.
