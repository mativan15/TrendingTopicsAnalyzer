Ambuja Cements allots 47.74 cr warrants to Adani family firm; raises Rs 5,000 crore
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 09:00:00

New Delhi, on Tuesday said it has issued 47.74 crore warrants to Adani family firm Harmonia Trade and Investment and raised Rs 5,000 crore. Ambuja Cement, now a part of Adani Cement, has issued 47.74 crore warrants at a price band of Rs 418.87, said a regulatory filing by the company. As per the terms and conditions, the Adani group family has contributed Rs 5,000.15 crore, which is "25 per cent of the Limited issued price", it added. "....We would like to inform you that, the Finance Committee of Directors of the Company, at its meeting held today approved allotment of the warrants of the Company by way of the private placement on a preferential issue basis to Harmonia Trade and Investment Ltd," Ambuja said.
