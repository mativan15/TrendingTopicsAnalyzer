National Real Estate Firm Evernest Acquires Denver Property Management Business
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-06-02 15:00:00

DENVER, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Evernest, a national, full-service real estate and property management firm, announced today that they have acquired Denver-based Cobalt Creek Property Management.The acquisition adds approximately 100 units to Evernest's Denver portfolio, and boosts the firm's overall properties managed to more than 7,000 nation-wide."It's no secret that Denver is a red-hot market for real estate investors," said Matthew Whitaker, Evernest Founder and CEO. "Luckily, we have an established team of professionals already living and working with owners in the area, so Cobalt Creek Property Management clients will be integrated into the Evernest family very quickly.
