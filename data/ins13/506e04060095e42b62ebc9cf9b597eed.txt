"Tesla Will Be Worth More Than Apple & Saudi Aramco Combined" Predicts Elon Musk
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:16:40

In what is being seen as a bold prediction by the world's richest person, Elon Musk has said that he sees Tesla becoming bigger than the combined valuation of Apple and Saudi Aramco one day. And no, this is not the first time he has said so!  After releasing Tesla's quarterly results a couple of days back on Wednesday, Musk discussed Tesla's valuation. indiatimesAs per the Apple Insider report, "A while ago," Musk said, "I said on an earnings call that I thought it was possible for Tesla to be worth more than Apple, which was worth (around) $700 billion at that time.
