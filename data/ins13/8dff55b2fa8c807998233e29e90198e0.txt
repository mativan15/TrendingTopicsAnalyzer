Eyemart Express Names Mike Nuzzo as New CEO

2022-10-19 00:00:00

October 19, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--National optical retailer Eyemart Express ('the Company') today announced that it has appointed Mike Nuzzo to serve as Chief Executive Officer, effective immediately. Nuzzo will leverage his seasoned retail management experience to lead the Company's next phase of growth. He succeeds interim CEO Jason Shanks, who will return to his roles as General Counsel and Head of Managed Vision Care Operations.



"I'm thrilled to join Eyemart Express as the demand for high quality glasses and ophthalmic care for adults and children continues to remain strong in North America,' said Nuzzo.
