Accenture plc ACN yesterday announced that it has completed the acquisition of Brussels-based Greenfish, an independent engineering and advisory company. The deal was initially announced on Apr 20, 2022. Financial terms of the deal have been kept under wraps.

Greenfish offers sustainability consultancy services and engineering solutions to boost sustainable business transformation. Founded in 2010, the company has offices in Belgium, France and the Netherlands.
