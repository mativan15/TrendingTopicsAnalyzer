Republican politician brandishes handguns during debate on gun control

A Republican legislator has brandished his guns during a debate over gun control in the US House of Representatives.Republican Representative Greg Steube, who attended the committee meeting virtually from his Florida home, said the legislation would ban various handguns.
The second-term congressman held up each of his four guns for the committee to see, describing one as "a gun I carry every single day to protect myself, my family, my wife, my home".Texas Democrat Sheila Jackson Lee said: "I hope to God that is not loaded.
