NotedDC — Biden leans in on abortion despite warning signs
thehill.com
news
2022-10-18 23:56:10

President Biden is promising Democrats he'll introduce legislation to codify abortion rights as his first proposal of the new year if they elect enough Democrats to keep control of the House and Senate.

'If you care about the right to choose, then you gotta vote. That's why these midterm elections are so critical … if we do that, here's the promise I make to you and the American people: The first bill I will send to the Congress will be to codify Roe,' Biden said during a speech in Washington, D.
