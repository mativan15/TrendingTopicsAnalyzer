Liverpool manager Juergen Klopp said he was delighted with forward Mohamed Salah's display in a more central role after the Egyptian's goal sealed a 1-0 Premier League victory over champion Manchester City on Sunday.Salah raced away from Joao Cancelo to score and give his side a much-needed win after it came into the encounter having suffered its worst start to a Premier League season after eight games since 2012-13.'Absolutely outstanding,' Klopp told reporters.
