LOOK: Mike Norvell uses helicopter to drop in on top running back

news
2022-10-21 05:22:00

Florida State is in the midst of its second BYE week of the season. The Seminoles' coaching staff is utilizing the extra time over the next few days to hit the recruiting trail. In the process, the staff has been visiting top prospects and putting out a ton of new offers.

On Thursday, head coach Mike Norvell arrived at Edgewater High School in style. Norvell dropped in on a helicopter to check out the program and get an eye on four-star running back Cedric Baxter Jr. He made an impression in the process.
