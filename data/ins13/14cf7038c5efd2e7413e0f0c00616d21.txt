Pune, India, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global Business Spend Management (BSM) software market size was valued at USD 17.07 billion in 2021 and is projected to reach a valuation of USD 15.68 billion in 2022. The market is expected to reach USD 39.05 billion by 2029 with a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. Increasing adoption and integration of cloud-based solution and AI is anticipated to drive market growth. Increasing usage of spend analytics in businesses is expected to propel the market.
