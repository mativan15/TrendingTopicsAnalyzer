ANI Trinamool Congress (TMC) leader Anubrata MondalTrinamool Congress leader Anubrata Mondal was quizzed for more than five hours after he appeared before the Central Bureau of Investigations (CBI) in Kolkata on Thursday in connection with post-poll violence and murder of a BJP worker in Birbhum district. Anubrata appeared before the CBI today after the agency's fifth summon. 'Anubrata was called in connection with a post-poll violence case. He was unwell, so he could not appear earlier,' Anubrata's lawyer told mediapersons.
