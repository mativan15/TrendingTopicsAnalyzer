The nearshore and offshore company has expanded and increased operations around the globeCORAL SPRINGS, Fla., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Lean Solutions Group (LSG), a rapidly growing nearshore and offshore services provider, is celebrating its tenth anniversary. In the last decade, Lean Solutions Group has partnered with more than 500 U.S.-based companies, and expanded service offerings to include staffing, technology, marketing, sales, and business process outsourcing services. While the company began as a nearshore provider to the transportation and logistics industry, it increased its offerings for other sectors including retail, warehousing, and healthcare.
