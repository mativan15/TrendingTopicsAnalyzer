Society Pass Shares Rise 12% After Buying Gorilla Networks
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 14:12:00

By Chris Wack Society Pass Inc. shares were up 12%, to $2.35, after the company said it bought Singapore-based Gorilla Networks Pte Ltd., a next generation Web3-enabled mobile virtual network operator. Established in Singapore in 2019, Gorilla uses Web3 technology to operate an MVNO for its users in Southeast Asia, with network coverage to more than 160 countries. Society Pass said that with a rebound of the travel market throughout Southeast Asia in the second and third quarters of 2022, Gorilla expects to increase its customer base by marketing to the region's business and leisure travellers with its user-friendly technology platform.
