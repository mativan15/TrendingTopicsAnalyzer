Paul Merson believes Wilfried Zaha should consider staying at Crystal Palace (Image: GETTY)Paul Merson believes Wilfried Zaha should think twice before running down his contract at Crystal Palace in pursuit of a Big Six move because the grass is not always greener. The Ivory Coast international knows it himself following a tough spell at Manchester United in his early 20s which eventually saw him seal a swift return to south east London with his boyhood club.Zaha is out of contract next summer meaning he will be free to negotiate with foreign clubs as soon as January.
