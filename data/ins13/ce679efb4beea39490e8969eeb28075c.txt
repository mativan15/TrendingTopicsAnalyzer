Accenture, Atlassian In Strategic Partnership To Help Organizations Gain Enterprise Agility
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-17 11:18:00

(RTTNews) - Professional services company Accenture plc (ACN) announced Monday a strategic partnership with Atlassian (TEAM) to help organizations achieve greater enterprise agility. Under the deal, an Accenture Atlassian Center of Excellence will provide coaching, education, agile practice disciplines, and platform enablement to clients around the world. Accenture and Atlassian will support clients on every step of the enterprise agility journey. With the partnership, the companies could help organizations drive more value from technology investments, improve customer and employee experiences, embrace change and create new business value with enterprise agility services.
