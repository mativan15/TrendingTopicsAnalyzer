SS&C Europe Upgrades Financial Services Offering with Napier's AI-enhanced Compliance Solutions

LONDON, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Napier, provider of leading anti-financial crime compliance solutions, has announced that the financial services firm SS&C Europe will be upgrading its offering with Napier's AI-enhanced financial crime risk management platform, Napier Continuum, which includes Client Screening and Transaction Monitoring tools. SS&C Europe is the UK's largest fund administrator.
