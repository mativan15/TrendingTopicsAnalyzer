GUWAHATIAn Assam-based NGO has filed a first information report against information technology firm Wipro and the National Register of Citizens (NRC) Directorate for allegedly misappropriating a 'huge amount of public money' in the exercise to update the NRC.The updating of the NRC of 1951 in the State was based on a 2009 petition filed by the NGO named Assam Public Works.The NGO's president, Aabhijeet Sharma said his organisation had been monitoring the process of updating the NRC since its inception.
