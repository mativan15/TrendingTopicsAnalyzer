Team India face Pakistan in their opening match of T20 World Cup at the Melbourne Cricket Ground on October 23. India head into the tournament, with high hopes of ending their 15-year drought and claiming their second title thanks to a side packed with talent with both bat and ball. Ahead of their crunch game against Pakistan, the Rohit Sharma-led side met the Governor of Victoria, Linda Dessau AC, and other notable dignitaries at the Government House in Melbourne.The BCCI and the Governor of Victoria shared photos of Team India's visit to the Government House.
