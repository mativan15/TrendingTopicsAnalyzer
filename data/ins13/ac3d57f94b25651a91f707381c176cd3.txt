Valentine In The MorningBy Valerie Hernandez May 27, 2022Photo: Getty Images North AmericaAccording to Sports Business Journal (SBJ), the National Football League is launching it's own streaming service. NFL Plus is expected to come out in July and will primarily focus on providing access to live games on mobile phones and tablets. The service is expected to cost about $5 per month, but the pricing structure may change. Other content such as radio, podcasts and various team-related content could also feature.
