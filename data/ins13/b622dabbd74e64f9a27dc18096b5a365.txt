Drug major on Friday said its subsidiary has acquired the Uractiv portfolio from Fiterman Pharma in Romania. The company's unit - SC Terapia SA, Romania - has acquired the Uractiv portfolio, which comprises food supplements, including minerals, vitamins and adjuvants; cosmetics and medical devices used for maintaining urinary tract health. The Mumbai-based drugmaker, however, did not disclose the financial details of the deal. Uractiv is the leading brand in its category in Romania, trusted by consumers for more than 10 years, Sun Pharma said in a statement.
