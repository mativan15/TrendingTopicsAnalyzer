Story: Ammu (Aishwarya Lekshmi), a young girl from a regular household, gets married to her next-door neighbour and inspector, Ravindranath, aka Ravi (Naveen Chandra), without knowing what she is getting into. A few days into what looks like a fairytale marriage, Ravi's true colours start unfolding. Despite knowing she is in a toxic and abusive relationship, Ammu struggles to break away from her manipulative husband. But will she muster enough strength and courage to stand up for herself?Review: The movie begins with a little girl questioning Ammu about her decision to marry Ravi, for which she doesn't have concrete answers.
