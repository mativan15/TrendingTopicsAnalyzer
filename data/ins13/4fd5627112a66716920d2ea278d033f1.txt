Getty ImagesNifty moved in a tight range of 82 points, and ended slightly below the psychologically crucial level of 15,700. Shares of the Berger Paints (India) Ltd. traded at Rs 585.5 on BSE at 01:55PM (IST) on Friday, down 5.34 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 543.85 and a high of Rs 813.05. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap down opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 60.24 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 9.74 per share and 17.31 times its book value, as per BSE data.
