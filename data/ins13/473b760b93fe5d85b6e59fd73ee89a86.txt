Ecuadorian naval Capt. Isiais Bodero Mala is on the front lines of protecting the Galapagos marine reserve, home to some of the world's greatest biodiversity, from illegal fishing. Ecuador's security forces are working with Canadian technology firms to fight back against environmental crimes. (Philippe Le Billon/CBC News - image credit)

From a naval command centre perched on the coast of Ecuador's Galapagos Islands, Capt. Isiais Bodero Mala surveyed incoming satellite feeds tracking fishing vessels circling one of the world's most biodiverse places.
