"SAE's Government/Industry Meeting is unique in that it takes an open-forum approach to exploring current areas of critical importance to the mobility industry, including safety, emissions, fuel efficiency, and automated vehicles.' WARRENDALE, Pa. (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 SAE International's annual Government/Industry Meeting (GIM) will take place in Washington, D.C, Jan. 17-19, 2023. Co-located with the Washington, D.C., Auto Show, this highly regarded annual conference connects government policy and regulatory makers with automotive professionals to discuss critical issues impacting the mobility industry.
