Image caption, The woman who made the claims arrived in the North East under the Homes for Ukraine scheme (image posed by an actor)A North East woman who invited a Ukrainian mother and son into her home says she has been badly let down by the experience and warns others thinking of doing so to be cautious.The woman, who does not want her name to be released, claims she was falsely accused of exploiting the refugees.But hundreds of pounds of her own money were spent on food and gifts and she even funded their flights, she said.
