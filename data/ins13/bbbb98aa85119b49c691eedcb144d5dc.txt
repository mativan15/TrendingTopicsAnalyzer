Swimmer selected for Pan Pacific Master games
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 05:30:00

Mangaluru: Sandesh Thingayalaya has been selected for Pan Pacific Master Games in swimming which will be held in Australia from November 1.We One Aqua Centre in St Aloysius Swimming Pool director Naveen told reporters on Wednesday that Sandesh is the swimming coach at the Centre in St Aloysius Swimming Pool. He had won six gold medals in the national level swimming contest.Sandesh, 45, said he will be taking part in freestyle, backstroke in swimming, medley relay and other events in Australia. 'I hail from Mogaveera community and have experience in swimming in the sea,' he said.
