October 20, 2022 07:20 PM Eastern Daylight Time MILL VALLEY, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Four Corners Property Trust (NYSE: FCPT), a real estate investment trust (REIT) primarily engaged in the ownership and acquisition of high-quality, net-leased restaurant and retail properties ('FCPT' or the 'Company'), announced today that Eric S. Hirschhorn has resigned from his role as an independent director of the Company's Board of Directors ('Board') to focus on other business endeavors.
