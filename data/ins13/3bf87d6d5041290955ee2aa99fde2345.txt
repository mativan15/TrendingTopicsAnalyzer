Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen may be left angry following update
express.co.uk
sport
2022-10-16 06:10:00

Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen may be angry after Saudi Arabia's sports minister HRH Abdulaziz Bin Turki Al-Faisal opened the door to two races taking place in the country in the future. The two stars have been outspoken against racing in Saudi Arabia, with the Brit previously admitting he could not wait to leave after this season's race.Al-Faisal admitted that the prospect of two races taking place in Saudi Arabia was something that they will discuss with F1. 'We really see the benefits of having these events for the Kingdom,' Al-Faisal said.
