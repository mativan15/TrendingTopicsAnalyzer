Home Politics, Law & Government Banking & Business marketing, the sum of activities involved in directing the flow of goods and services from producers to consumers.Marketing's principal function is to promote and facilitate exchange. Through marketing, individuals and groups obtain what they need and want by exchanging products and services with other parties. Such a process can occur only when there are at least two parties, each of whom has something to offer. In addition, exchange cannot occur unless the parties are able to communicate about and to deliver what they offer.
