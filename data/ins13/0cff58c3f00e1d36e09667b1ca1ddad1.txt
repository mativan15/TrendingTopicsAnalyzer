October 19, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN JUAN, Puerto Rico--(BUSINESS WIRE)--EVERTEC, Inc. (NYSE: EVTC) ('Evertec' or the 'Company') today announced that it will host a conference call and webcast on Wednesday, November 2, 2022 at 4:30 p.m. ET to review third quarter financial results. Participating on the call will be Mac Schuessler, President and Chief Executive Officer, and Joaquin Castrillo, Chief Financial Officer. A press release with third quarter 2022 financial results will be issued shortly after the market closes on Wednesday, November 2, 2022.
