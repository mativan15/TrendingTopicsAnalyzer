It seems like no one is immune to the downfall of the market, not even crypto billionaires Cameron and Tyler Winklevoss, who founded the crypto exchange platform Gemini which is currently worth an estimated $7.1 billion. The CEO and President penned a letter to Gemini employees on Thursday (whom they call 'astronauts') to inform their team that they would be cutting 10% of their total workforce due to what they are calling the 'crypto winter.'
