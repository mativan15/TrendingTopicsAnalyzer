PORTLAND, Ore. (PRWEB) June 03, 2022 Mazda has selected Adpearance, a digital marketing company, as a certified new provider for dealers in the Mazda Digital Certified Program for the Sales Digital Advertising, Parts & Service Digital Advertising, and Cars for Sale Programs. 'Adpearance is excited to partner with Mazda dealers across the country to achieve our shared goal of improving digital advertising performance with technology-driven solutions and unsurpassed execution,' said Aaron James, Adpearance CEO and Co-Founder.
