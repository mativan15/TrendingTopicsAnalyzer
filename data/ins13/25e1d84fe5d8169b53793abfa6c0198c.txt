By Reuters Published: 13:29 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 13:29 EDT, 20 October 2022 By Emma-Victoria Farr FRANKFURT, Oct 20 (Reuters) - German sporting goods maker Adidas on Thursday cut its full-year guidance, citing weaker expectations for China, lower demand in major Western markets and one-off expenses related to its exit from the Russian market. The company now expects currency-neutral revenues to grow by a mid-single-digit percentage in 2022, down from a mid- to high-single-digit percentage forecast previously, while its operating margin is seen at 4%, down from 7%.
