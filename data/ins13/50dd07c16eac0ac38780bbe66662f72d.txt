How not to fall prey to common cryptocurrency frauds:

KOLKATA: The next time you find yourself being added into an unknown WhatsApp group dealing with cryptocurrency without your prior knowledge, be very warned. The CID and city police have taken to Twitter to warn netizens of being tricked by cheats posing as cryptocurrency experts after noticing cases where youngsters trying to deal with cryptocurrency fell in this trap.'Cryptocurrency is a raging issue. It includes benefits but often fraudsters add us to Whatsapp/Telegram groups without our permission, wherein people are seen rejoicing for their profit.
