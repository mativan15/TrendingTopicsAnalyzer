PALO ALTO, Calif (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Constellation Research announced the appointment of Ray Jan as vice president and chief operating officer to manage daily operations, grow domestic and global sales, and lead business development. The appointment of Jan reinforces Constellation's ongoing commitment to excel globally as a top contender in the technology research and advisory space and to ensure the success of advancements in sales and business operations.
