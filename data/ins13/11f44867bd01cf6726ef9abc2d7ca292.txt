30 October 1922At the moment when Mussolini, the leader of the Italian fascisti, was seizing control of the country by force, the government's authority has been placed in his hands by the king, who yesterday asked him to form a cabinet.On Saturday, while Mussolini ordered a general mobilisation of his more or less armed force of nearly half a million men, the cabinet decided upon measures of resistance – with certain reservations. The military were ordered to hold Rome against attack, but to give some latitude to the revolutionaries in the provinces.
