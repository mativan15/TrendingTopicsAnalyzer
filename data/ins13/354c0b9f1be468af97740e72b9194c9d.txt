godda: Bjp Mp: Ed To Call Cabinet Ministers For Questioning
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 03:30:00

Ranchi: BJP MP from Godda Nishikant Dubey , on Tuesday claimed that the enforcement directorate (ED) has now decided to call the state cabinet ministers to give depositions on the various policies of the government. In a tweet on Tuesday, Dubey said ED would call all members of the cabinet in a bid to understand the policies related to mining, liquor, transport and posting, smart city and industry.He further said efforts were made to seek a view of the ministers but none of them could be contacted.
