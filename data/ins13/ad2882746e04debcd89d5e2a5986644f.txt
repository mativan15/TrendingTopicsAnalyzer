Yesterday, Amazon shut down Fabric.com, a business it acquired back in 2008 to bolster its craft and hobby materials sales.

Fabric.com has been selling fabric since 1993(Opens in a new window) when former owner Phoenix Textile Group set it up to sell by-the-yard fabrics direct to consumers. It clearly worked as the Amazon acquisition proved 15 years later, but recently vendors working with the site noticed problems with the business.
