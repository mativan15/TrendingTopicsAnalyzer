Amerisourcebergen Authorizes Up To $1 Billion in Buybacks
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 10:50:00

By Will Feuer Amerisourcebergen Corp. said its board has authorized a new share buyback program allowing up to $1 billion in repurchases. The company previously authorized a program of up to $500 million in May 2020, and as of Tuesday, the company had $321 million remaining under that authorization. Amerisourcebergen reiterated its fiscal 2022 guidance, saying it doesn't include any potential benefit from the new buyback authorization. Shares of the pharmaceutical company have held up better than the overall market.
