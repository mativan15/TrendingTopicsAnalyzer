Sebi allows virtual annual meetings for InvITs, REITs till end of Dec
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 15:00:00

AgenciesThe circular came after Sebi received representations from REITs/InvITs to further extend the facility to conduct annual meetings and other meetings of unitholders through Video-Conferencing (VC) or through Other Audio-Visual Means (OAVM).Markets regulator Sebi on Friday allowed Infrastructure Investment Trusts (InvITs) and Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) to conduct annual meetings of their unitholders and other meetings through video-conferencing and other audio-visual means till the end of December.
