Italy's Meloni Meets President, Set To Become Prime Minister

Brothers of Italy leader Giorgia Meloni arrives for a meeting with Italian President Sergio Mattarella at the Quirinale Palace in Rome, Italy October 21, 2022. 
Giorgia Meloni, set to become Italy's next prime minister, met President Sergio Mattarella on Friday to discuss the formation of a new government against the backdrop of tumult within her right-wing coalition.Meloni, head of the nationalist Brothers of Italy party, entered the president's palace alongside her main allies -- Matteo Salvini, who leads the far-right League, and Silvio Berlusconi, founder of the conservative Forza Italia party.
