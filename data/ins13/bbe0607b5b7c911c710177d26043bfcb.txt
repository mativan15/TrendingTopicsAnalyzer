Criss Angel's botched magic trick left 'Pony' singer Ginuwine unconscious, read what happened
indiatimes.com

Ginuwine underwent a troublesome situation after Criss Angel performed a magic trick during rehearsals of the show "Magic with the Stars", Ginuwine went underwater holding his breath in a glass cube when he banged the sides of the glass cube signaling trouble. A few people gathered to help the R&B star as they pulled him out before he passed out. According to reports, an ambulance was called and Ginuwine was carted off while still unconscious. Reports suggest he was trying to overcome his fear of staying underwater, where he couldn't hold his breath for over 15 seconds.
