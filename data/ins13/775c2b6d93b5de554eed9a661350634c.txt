Reliance Capital to be divided into four entities? New proposal surprises many
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 15:45:00

The RBI had superseded the board of RCL following payment defaults and serious governance issues, on November 29, 2021.In a move that came as a big surprise to many, Reliance Capital's administrator has proposed to divide the company into four core investment companies (CICs), changing its very structure. The bidding process for the debt-ridden company is already at an advanced stage. There are four companies in the fray for RCAP's General Insurance and Life Insurance ventures. These are: Piramal, Zurich, Advent Private Equity and Aditya Birla Capital.
