We are about to celebrate an important birthday: The Sunday Times is turning 200.The paper was founded in 1822 by Henry White, who set out to expose the 'secret springs' by which government was enacted. Over the past two centuries we have done that and more, revealing Kim Philby's Soviet treachery, the thalidomide scandal, the truth of Princess Diana's dysfunctional relationship with Prince Charles, Lance Armstrong's doping and more recently the government's chaotic handling of the early days of the pandemic.
