Samvardhana Motherson tanks 7%, hits fresh 52-week low; here's why
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 06:15:00

New Delhi: Shares of Samvardhana Motherson International (SAMIL) led the loser pack during the early trading on Tuesday following a bulk deal on the counter.Japan-based Sojitz Corp had announced that it would sell a 1.9% stake in the midcap auto component major Samvardhana Motherson through a block deal.The floor price for the deal was set at Rs 64.36 per equity share, a discount of 7% from its previous close. The scrip settled at Rs 69.20 on Monday on BSE.At the given price, the deal size for Sojitz Corp share sale in Samvardhana Motherson has been fixed at Rs 824 crore.
