INDIANAPOLIS, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Dronedek, known for its "mailbox of the future," today announced its technical integration partnership with commercial drone solutions developer, A2Z Drone Delivery, the ninth organization to join Dronedek's growing "delivery ecosystem of the future" this year.For safe and reliable drone delivery to reach scale, carriers must be part of a wider network of supporting elements - an ecosystem - delivering to devices, like Dronedek smart mailboxes, that can securely receive and store those deliveries, says Dronedek's CEO and Founder Dan O'Toole.
