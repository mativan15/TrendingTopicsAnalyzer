Musk Says Tesla Staff Can ‘Pretend To Work Somewhere Else' Following Leaked Emails Ending Remote Work
forbes.com
news
2022-06-01 14:05:12

Tesla CEO Elon Musk apparently sent an email to the electric car company's executive staff—was shared on social media on Wednesday—that nixed working from home, saying anyone wishing to work remotely must work from the office 'for a minimum (and I mean *minimum*) of 40 hours a week,' or they must depart the company.
