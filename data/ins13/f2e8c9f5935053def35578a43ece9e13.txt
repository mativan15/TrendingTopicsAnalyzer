Wakefield & Associates Acquires Choice Recovery to Expand its Geographic Footprint

AURORA, Colo., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Wakefield & Associates, an industry-leading collections and healthcare revenue cycle solutions company, today announces it has acquired the debt collection business of Choice Recovery, Inc., an Ohio-based nationally recognized collections provider. The acquisition of Choice Recovery will further grow Wakefield's geographical presence in the Great Lakes region and enhance its ability to provide a full suite of revenue cycle management services to a wide scope of healthcare industry clients.
