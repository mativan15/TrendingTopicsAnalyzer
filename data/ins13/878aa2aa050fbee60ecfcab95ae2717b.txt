Can you afford a 'second act' after retirement? Here are some questions to ask yourself
cnbc.com
news
2022-10-15 13:00:01

Key Points

With median retirement savings of just $134,000 per person in 2019, Americans aged 55 to 64 can expect a big drop in living standards after they retire, even after Social Security benefits are added in.
Many who are more secure from a financial perspective will pursue second careers based on personal interests.
Some may be forced to just survive on the reduced income, while others still may not be able to afford switching to a job they're more passionate about until they've managed to save up more money in their better-paying position.
