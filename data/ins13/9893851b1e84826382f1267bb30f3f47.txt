 Karnataka chief minister Basavaraj Bommai on Sunday said Congress leader Rahul Gandhi might have developed 'selective amnesia' and hence details of corruption during his party's regime in the State would be sent to him.The chief minister was reacting to Rahul Gandhi's statement in Ballari on Saturday claiming that only people having money get jobs in Karnataka.
