Allu Arjun's fans are now celebrating Diwali in Pushpa style with 'Pushpa Anaar'
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-22 08:00:00

It's been almost a year since Allu Arjun's 'Pushpa: The Rise 'was released, but till now people are going gaga over the film. After creating Pushpa-inspired Ganesha idols during Ganesh Chaturthi, his fans are now celebrating Diwali in Pushpa style. And what's better than to celebrate Diwali with Pushpa firecrackers. Reportedly, a lot of shops in Mumbai are selling "anaar"crackers with posters of Allu Arjun on the packets. As expected, those Pushpa Anaars are selling like hot cakes. Several images and videos of Pushpaa-inspired crackers have also surfaced online.
