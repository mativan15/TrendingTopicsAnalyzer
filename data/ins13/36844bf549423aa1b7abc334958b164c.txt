BEIJING, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- A news report from chinadaily.com.cn:

Wuhan East Lake High-tech Zone has more than 4,000 national high-tech enterprises and is the fastest economic growing area in Wuhan. This is the symbolic building of Donghu High-tech Zone, its shape is similar to a horseshoe lotus.
