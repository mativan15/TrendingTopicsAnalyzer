JARROD BOWEN fumed at referee Peter Bankes after appearing to be inadvertently blocked by the official in the build-up to Southampton's opener.
The West Ham ace tried to close down goalscorer Romain Perraud, only for Bankes to get in his way.
Peter Bankes appeared to get in Jarrod Bowen's way
The England star was furious with the official
Bowen, 25, immediately turned around to the whistler to protest... only for Perraud to fire the ball into the net.
The England international was furious with Bankes, but his protestations fell on deaf ears.
