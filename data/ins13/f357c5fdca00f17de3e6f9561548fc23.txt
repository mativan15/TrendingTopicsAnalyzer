 The City of Kingston, Ont., is boasting its most recent credit score.
The eastern Ontario city has been given a 'stellar' AA+ credit rating from S&P Global Rankings. It was also given a stable outlook.The city attributes its strong performance to its 'strong financial management practices and economic diversity.''We are very pleased to once again receive this positive news. A strong credit rating is not only an indicator of our ability to meet our financial obligations, but it is an important validation of the City's fiscal policies and practices,' Desiree Kennedy, Chief Financial Officer and City Treasurer, said.
