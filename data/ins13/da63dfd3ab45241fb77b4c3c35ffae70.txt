B-Town celebrities on October 19 had to choose between two Diwali parties - one by Ramesh Taurani and another by Kriti Sanon. Actress Kriti Sanon hosted a party for her close family and friends. Actor Varun Dhawan and his wife, Natasha Dalal, attended Kriti's bash. Vicky Kaushal, Kartik Aaryan, Ananya Panday, Neha Dhupia, Karan Johar, Nushratt Bharuccha, Angad Bedi, Tahira Kashyap, Vaani Kapoor, Kunal Khemu, Soha Ali Khan, and Rajkumar Rao were among other guests that showed up at the party. 
Kriti Sanon donned a stunning lehenga for the party while her mother, Geeta Sanon, and her sister, Nupur Sanon, were alongside her.
