The City of Calgary and the owners of the Calgary Flames have argreed to restart negotiations for the construction of a new arena to replace the Saddledome. (Dialog Architecture, Robson Fletcher/CBC)Arena talks in Calgary are back on.The city and Calgary Flames owners confirmed Wednesday negotiations will begin again for a new sports, arts and entertainment building.Their previous deal agreed upon in 2019 collapsed late last year when the estimated cost of the project rose and the Flames withdrew.
