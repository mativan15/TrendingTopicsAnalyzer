Vade Lands $30 Million in New Funding Round

Latest investment from Tikehau Ace Capital, Bpifrance and Auriga Partners will propel a new chapter of company growth.
SAN FRANCISCO, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Vade, an international pioneer in threat detection and response that already monitors one billion email messages worldwide, has announced that it has raised €28 million (~$30 million) from Tikehau Ace Capital, the French government, through French Tech Souveraineté, which is part of France 2030, led by the General Secretariat for Investment and operated by Bpifrance, and Auriga Partners.
