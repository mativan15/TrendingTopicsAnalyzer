Essex schools head says rising costs affecting children

Image source, Eveleigh Link Academy TrustImage caption, Joseph Figg said: "It keeps me awake at night worrying that I won't be able to keep all those services going, and those staff in a job"The head of seven primary schools said children were suffering due to rising energy bills and staff costs which left him feeling "out of control".Fuel bills at Eveleigh Link Academy Trust in Essex went up this year to nearly £300,000 from £115,000, it said.Chief executive Joseph Figg said it was "a massive weight on my shoulders because of the impact on the children".
