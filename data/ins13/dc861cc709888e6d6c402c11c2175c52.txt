Company Provides Critical Infrastructure to Emerging Agriculture MarketsTULSA, OK / ACCESSWIRE / June 2, 2022 / INDUSTRY SOURCE CONSULTING, INC. (OTC PINK:INSO) announced today that Tulsa, OK based Yields Management Group, Inc. has acquired the controlling interest in the Company. The Company is working with The Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA) and the State of Wyoming to officially change the name and trading symbol for the Company. The new name of the Company is Yields Management Group, Inc.
