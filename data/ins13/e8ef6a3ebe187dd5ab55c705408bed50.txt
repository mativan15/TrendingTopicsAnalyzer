Being in debt can be stressful no matter what. But it may end up being an exceptionally stressful thing in 2023.The reason? The Federal Reserve has been hiking up interest rates in an attempt to cool the pace of inflation. And so if you have debt with a variable interest rate attached to it, like a credit card balance, your rate might soar in 2023, thereby costing you more money. Similarly, if you first take on credit card debt in 2023, you might end up paying a lot of interest on it from the start.
