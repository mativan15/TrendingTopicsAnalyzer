Scottish business leaders demand stability from next PM
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

The abrupt departure of Liz Truss from Downing Street has 'exacerbated' uncertainty about the UK's prospects, Scottish business leaders have warned.Trade bodies and business representatives have called for an end to the 'turmoil' which has engulfed Westminster over recent weeks.They also want clarity on what support will be provided by the UK government in the face of inflation at a 40-year-high and soaring energy costs which are threatening the financial stability of many firms.The scrutiny on the fiscal statement planned by Jeremy Hunt, the chancellor, will now be even more intense.
