Forest dept to cull blue bulls, wild boars at Darbhanga airport
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 16:35:21

Forest department is working on a comprehensive plan to rope in professional shooters to cull blue bulls, also known as nilgai or ghorparas, and wild pigs inhabiting the extensive thick bushes at Darbhanga airport, said a senior forest department official on Saturday. The burgeoning number of the antelope has frustrated forest department's efforts to trap and relocate them within a specified time frame, officials said. 'Getting blue bulls and wild boars off the airfield is critical for the safety of flight operations.
