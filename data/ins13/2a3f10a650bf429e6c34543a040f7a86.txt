Infineon's innovation center resurrects knowledge venture capital

Presented by Infineon Infineon's Silicon Valley Innovation Center's (SVIC) collaboration model provides young partner companies with quick access to technical expertise, supporting anything from joint R&D for IP development to guidance on designing products with manufacturability in mind.   It's been a little more than 50 years since Arthur Rock began funding and guiding a small company called Intel — and then formalized the practice of investing in technology companies and shepherding them to success.
