Strategic partnership launches WSX.TV to live-stream the mega motorsport series worldwide

NEW YORK, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ViewLift, a global leader in end-to-end streaming and OTT solutions, today announced a strategic alliance with SX Global to power the streaming channel WSX.TV as the global home of the FIM World Supercross Championship series. This partnership will help SX Global increase the worldwide viewership and fanbase beyond its current linear broadcast distribution. WSX.TV next will showcase races from Melbourne, Australia on October 21st and 22nd.
