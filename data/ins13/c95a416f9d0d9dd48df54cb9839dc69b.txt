Two young men stabbed to death in separate incidents in London as capital prepares Jubilee Weekend
msn.com
news
2022-06-02 01:06:16

Two young men have been stabbed to death in separate incident in London as the capital prepared for the four-day Platinum Jubilee bank holiday.Police were first called to reports of a stabbing in Neasden, north west London, at around 6.30pm.A man believed to be in his mid-20s was found injured in the street and, despite the best efforts of paramedics, was pronounced dead at the scene around 45 minutes later.A police cordon has been erected around Henderson Close and a number of local roads have been closed to traffic.
