Dominion Energy Acquires Second Energy Storage Project from East Point Energy

October 18, 2022 10:12 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHARLOTTESVILLE, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--East Point Energy, an energy storage development firm, announced the sale of the Shands Energy Storage project to Dominion Energy Virginia, one of the nation's largest utilities. At 15.7 megawatts(MW)/62.8 megawatt-hours (MWh), Shands will be one of the largest standalone storage projects in Virginia when it becomes operational in 2023. Located in Sussex County, Shands will enhance local grid reliability and serve as an essential component of Dominion Energy's 100% clean energy future.
