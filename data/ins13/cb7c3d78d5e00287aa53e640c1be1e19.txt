Unleashing a fresh attack against Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Leader of the Opposition in the Assembly Siddaramaiah said the Modi-government had pushed the India into a debt trap by borrowing ₹100 lakh crores from international financial institutions in the last eight years. 'When Mr. Modi assumed power as Prime Minister in 2014, India's debt was around ₹53,000 crores and now it has swelled to ₹155 lakh crores.
