The Delhi High Court has permitted Bollywood actor Jacqueline Fernandez, against whom a lookout notice was issued by the Enforcement Directorate in connection with an alleged money laundering case, to travel abroad for IIFA awards with certain conditions.Justice Sudhir Kumar Jain directed the actor to submit a fixed deposit receipt (FDR) of ₹1 crore and give an undertaking that in case she does not return, it shall be forfeited along with a surety of ₹1 crore.
