Automating business for Covid-19 continuity
techradar.com
tech
2022-10-17 13:23:33

The Covid-19 pandemic has triggered more change than any other event we lived through so far. Some industry leaders have already said that their customers went through more digital transformation in the last two months than in the last two years. IT automation is one of the key technologies fuelling that digital transformation. The current crisis has put the business need for automation in the spotlight. Without semi-automated warehouses and distribution centres, it would be impossible for businesses like Amazon and Ocado to get essential goods to consumers at the scale those goods are being purchased.
