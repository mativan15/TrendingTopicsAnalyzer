Binance Labs, the company's venture arm, has raised $500 million for a fund dedicated to investing in Web3 start-ups. Web3 is a movement in tech that aims to create a new version of the internet based on blockchain technology. The company's venture arm, Binance Labs, said Wednesday it has raised $500 million for its debut start-up fund, securing backing from venture capital firms DST Global and Breyer Capital as well as unnamed family offices and corporations.
