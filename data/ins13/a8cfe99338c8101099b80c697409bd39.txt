California-based company and participating distributors provide relief through discounts and expedited shipping on wall-mountable batteries and portable emergency power kits.

Oxnard, CA, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Given the volatile and unpredictable scale of recent storms, SimpliPhi Power – now part of Briggs and Stratton – is providing reduced pricing on wall-mountable PHI 3.8 kWh Battery, Big Genny Emergency Power Kit, and Little Genny Power Kit through October 31, 2022.

Customer-sited battery storage is a critical solution that creates reliable backup power in the face of growing threats to the centralized grid, whether at home, at work, or on-the-go during evacuations.
