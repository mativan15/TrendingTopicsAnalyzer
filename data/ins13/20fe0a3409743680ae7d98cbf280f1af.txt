When it comes to red carpet fashion, there's nowhere quite like Cannes to dial things up to the max. Whether it's Hollywood actors descending on the city to promote their latest arthouse film, French cinema legends making a cameo appearance in sleek Gallic style, or models hitting La Croisette in couture while in town for a charity gala, the order of the day is a style spectacle on the grandest scale.While the festival's jury might be there to weigh in on the films, that's not to say they can't have a little fun with their fashion, too.
