The staff who work at the factory in Glasgow will walk out on Monday, October 31 and will stay on strike for up to seven days unless there is a resolution50 coffin makers set to go on strikeFifty workers at the UK's only coffin-manufacturing hub are to go on strike this month for seven days. The craft workers at the Co-op Funeralcare factory in Glasgow have rejected a pay offer, which they say is a real-terms cut. The staff who work at the Shieldhall facility will walk out on Monday, October 31 and will stay on strike for up to seven days unless there is a resolution.
