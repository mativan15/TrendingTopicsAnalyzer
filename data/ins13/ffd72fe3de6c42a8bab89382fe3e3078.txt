Elvis Wedding Crackdown Leaves Las Vegas All Shook Up
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-02 19:51:15

Every year thousands of visitors to Las Vegas can't help falling in love -- at least long enough to get married by an Elvis impersonator.But the company that controls the rights to the King's likeness has sparked outrage in Sin City by cracking down on dozens of wedding chapels offering Elvis-themed nuptials.Authentic Brands Group, which bought a controlling stake in Elvis Presley's estate in 2013, last month sent cease-and-desist letters to companies offering the kitschy weddings.The move triggered angry responses from Elvis impersonators, chapel owners, and even the mayor of Las Vegas.
