At times like this, don't be scared to say ‘I don't know'
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

Nobody knows anything pinged out in bold capitals on the first slide of our presentation. I sat, aged 21, feeling very out of place in a boardroom in New Zealand as I watched my first-ever business meeting kicking off. I was working for a strategy consultancy and my boss used this statement to declare how our client should approach the strange new world forming at the intersection of business and the web.I was flabbergasted when he showed me the presentation in advance — and as he clicked onto this first page, I was still reeling from how much we were being paid for this great insight.
