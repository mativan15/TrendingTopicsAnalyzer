Against investors' advice, Jason Goldberg launched a Web3 development startup in the middle of the crypto winter. Here's how he raised $3 million anyway.
businessinsider.com
news
2022-10-20 11:00:00

Jason Goldberg cofounded his Web3 development startup, Airstack, in May, just as crypto markets began to unravel. When he first set out to raise funding, investors advised him to take the summer off.Goldberg, a repeat founder whose past startups include Social Median, Fab, and Hem, decided not to heed that advice. Instead, he and his cofounders — Deepesh Nath, Sarvesh Jain, Ignas Peciura, and Vysakh Nair — pushed full steam ahead to build a prototype of Airstack's platform.Following their guts ended up paying off.
