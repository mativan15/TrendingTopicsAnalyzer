By Australian Associated Press Published: 01:46 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 01:46 EDT, 21 October 2022 Nineteen jailed terrorists are due for release from custody in Australia in the next five years. The latest annual report by the Australian Federal Police noted there were 55 people serving jail time for terrorism offences. The AFP monitors what are known as "high-risk terrorism offenders" - people who have served their sentence but are assessed as posing an unacceptable risk of future terrorist conduct.
