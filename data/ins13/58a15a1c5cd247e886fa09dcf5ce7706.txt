But this move lacks decisiveness due to lingering issues like inflation, geopolitical tension, etc.NEW DELHI: After a gap-up opening on Monday, domestic indices struggled for the rest of the week but eventually managed to maintain the gains. Sensex and Nifty closed the week with gains of more than a per cent. Analysts said two factors were responsible for the bounce in the market after a lacklustre few weeks -- a reduction in FIIs selling due to good earnings in the US and the maintenance of domestic inflows.
