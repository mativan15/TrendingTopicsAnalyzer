Match PreviewHost India will play for pride, while for a few others, there's a lot at stake in the final Group A and Group B games of the 2022 FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup with three quarterfinal spots still to fight for. India and Brazil from Group A and Nigeria and Chile from Group B will be in action at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar. The other four teams from the same groups (USA, Morocco – Group A and Germany, New Zealand – Group B) will play their matches at the Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in Margao, Goa.
