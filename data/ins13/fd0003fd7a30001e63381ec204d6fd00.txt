A solo hiker said she was "charged and gored" by a bison in Texas on Oct. 2. She recorded the encounter and posted the video to TikTok. Getty Images As she lay bleeding on the ground, Rebecca Clark knew her experience would make a great TikTok.
While on a solo hike at Caprock Canyons State Park and Trailway in Texas, Clark encountered a group of wild bison.
As the bison crossed the dirt trail in front of her on Oct. 4, Clark recorded video of the hulking animals on her phone.
