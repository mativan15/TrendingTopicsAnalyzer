October 20, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time RICHMOND, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Apple Hospitality REIT, Inc. (NYSE: APLE) (the 'Company' or 'Apple Hospitality') today announced that its Board of Directors authorized an increase in the Company's regular monthly cash distributions to $0.08 per common share, up from $0.07 per common share, and declared a monthly cash distribution of $0.08 per common share for the month of November 2022. The distribution is payable on November 15, 2022, to shareholders of record as of November 2, 2022.
