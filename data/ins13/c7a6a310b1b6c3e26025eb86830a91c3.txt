NAVI MUMBAI: The NRI Coastal police in Navi Mumbai on Wednesday arrested two brothers from UP's Meerut for attempting to kill their sister at her rental flat in Ulwe.

The 28-year-old bar girl was found unconscious at her Ulwe flat with injuries on her face and strangulation marks on her neck on May 26 around 7.30pm.

The accused have been identified as Shahrukh Khan (22) and Salman Khan (20).

Ravindra Patil, senior inspector of NRI coastal police station said, "The injured woman Sultana Khan's mobile call data record and tip-off by informers provided us vital lead that Sultana's two brothers had come from Meerut to her Ulwe flat on May 26 early morning.
