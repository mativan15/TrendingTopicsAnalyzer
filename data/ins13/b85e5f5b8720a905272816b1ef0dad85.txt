Chemical maker TPC defends bankruptcy financing amid bondholder battle
reuters.com
news
2022-06-02 22:51:35

(Reuters) - Texas petrochemical producer TPC Group defended its proposed bankruptcy financing in Delaware bankruptcy court on Thursday as groups of bondholders squared off over the company's proposed restructuring.TPC filed for Chapter 11 protection on Wednesday after struggling with costs and legal claims related to a 2019 explosion and fire at its Port Neches, Texas, manufacturing plant. It also cited the COVID-19 pandemic, a severe winter storm in Texas and a series of equipment failures as reasons for its bankruptcy.
