Popular actress Sobhita Dhulipala dazzled the ramp as the showstopper for Logitech X Saaksha & Kinni.NEW DELHI, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The association of Logitech with designers Saaksha and Kinni for the collection 'Prism' was a celebration of style and technology with geometry, colour and mirrors forming a perfect amalgamation at the Lakmé Fashion Week in partnership with FDCI. In a creative confluence of fashion and technology, popular actress and fashion icon Sobhita Dhulipala walked the ramp as the showstopper for Logitech X Saaksha & Kinni.
