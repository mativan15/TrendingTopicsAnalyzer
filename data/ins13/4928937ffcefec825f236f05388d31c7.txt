Move to spend £1.6m on healthier school meals
msn.com
science
2022-10-15 09:15:00

The Government of Jersey has proposed spending £1.6m on healthier school meals for primary school children. 
 

 © PA Media The government said it wanted to provide "as many children as possible" with healthy meals


The funding has been lodged as part of the government plan, following a pilot launched by the government in 2019. If approved by the States Assembly, the pilot will expand from five primary schools to all government-run primary schools.The Government of Jersey said funding would also cover the cost of "creating facilities to store and serve meals".
