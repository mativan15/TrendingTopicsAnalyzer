October 18, 2022 11:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--HackNotice, the leading threat awareness platform, now accepts cryptocurrency as a payment method. The new shift to accepting digital currencies allows security and privacy-conscious businesses to make safe, easy, and quick transactions. The company accepts two different types of digital currencies, Monero and Bitcoin.

At visionary companies, digital currencies are quickly replacing traditional payment methods because of their ease of use, better protection, and anonymous nature of transactions.
