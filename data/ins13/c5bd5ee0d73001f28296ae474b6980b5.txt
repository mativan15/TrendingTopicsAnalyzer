Microsoft Announces Layoffs After Earnings Decline
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-18 19:15:59

Microsoft Corp. (MSFT) announced layoffs for nearly 1,000 workers after its earnings dipped. The Redmond, Washington-based company said in July that it planned to cut a number of roles, representing less than 1% of its 200,000-plus employees.The company has not explicitly stated how many staff would be impacted with the latest downsizing push. However, nearly 1,000 employees are said to be affected, Axios reported."Like all companies, we evaluate our business priorities on a regular basis, and make structural adjustments accordingly.
