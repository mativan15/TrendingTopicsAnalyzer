Motovolt Mobility in talks with online food, grocery delivery firms to develop their fleet
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 14:30:00

The planned e-scooters can have a speed of 25 kmph for which motor vehicle registration and licences are mandatory.Electric cycle manufacturer Motovolt Mobility is in talks with various online food and grocery delivery firms to help develop their fleet size across the country, a company official said on Wednesday. It is also in discussion with a few private-equity (PE) players to raise Rs 100 crore in the next financial year, he said. "Talks are on with online delivery firms like Swiggy, Zomato and Zepto for supplying products like e-bikes to help develop their fleet," Motovolt Mobility founder and CEO Tushar Choudhary told reporters here.
