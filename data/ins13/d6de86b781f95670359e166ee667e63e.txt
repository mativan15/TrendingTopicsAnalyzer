Shay DiddyBy @ShayDiddyJun 2, 2022After being pulled over, Desiigner had an ugly discussion with cops, which resulted in a shouting brawl before things were straightened out. The incident occurred in Los Angeles on Wednesday after the "Panda" rapper was pulled over for driving without license plates and tinted windows, according to law enforcement sources. Desiigner allegedly got out of his car and approached the officer who had pulled him over. The police pulls his pepper spray, which sets Desiigner off, letting the cop know he doesn't represent a threat and there's no need to use force.
