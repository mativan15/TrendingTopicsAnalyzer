Published: 20:11 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 20:26 EDT, 30 May 2022 A French man who owns two galleries on Palm Beach's exclusive Worth Avenue has been charged with selling fake paintings from some of the most renowned artists to unsuspecting buyers.Daniel Elie Bouaziz, who owns the Danieli Fine Art Gallery and Galerie Daniel, is accused of selling fake Banksy, Jean-Michael Basquiat and Ray Lichtenstein paintings for hundreds of thousands of dollars - despite buying the replicas for just hundreds.
