Variety - Film News Charlie Cox is Daredevil once more. After reviving the character for a brief cameo in 'Spider-Man: No Way Home,' Cox officially debuted his fan favorite superhero in the Marvel Cinematic Universe in a few episodes of the Disney+ series 'She-Hulk: Attorney at Law.' Next up, Cox will lead the 18-episode limited series 'Daredevil: Born Again.' Speaking to Marvel.com, the actor said he owes his entire career to the fan-driven 'Save Daredevil' campaign on social media.
