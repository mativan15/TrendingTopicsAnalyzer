BDO executives to lead discussion panels on business and manufacturing strategies, technology for cell and gene therapy

BOSTON, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BDO's BioProcess Technology Group (BPTG), the world leader in providing strategic, technical, regulatory, and business consulting services to the biopharmaceutical industry, will be presenting at the upcoming 2022 BIO International Convention, June 13-16, 2022 in San Diego. The annual convention, hosted by Biotechnology Innovation Organization (BIO), is the world's largest industry gathering and has returned live and in person for the first time since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic.
