Published: 02:51 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 03:08 EDT, 27 May 2022 The cost of living crisis is hitting many families hard, but high earners who are still able to splash out on luxuries have revealed what it is they spend their cash on every month. In an eye-opening thread on the parenting website Mumsnet and unnamed woman, 27, who lives an hour outside London, explained that she and her doctor husband, 29, earn just under £140,000 a year combined. Her post entitled: 'High earners - how do you spend your salary?' explained that they contribute to their pensions and give 10 per cent a month of their earnings to charity, but wanted to know if they could be allocating their income in a better way.
