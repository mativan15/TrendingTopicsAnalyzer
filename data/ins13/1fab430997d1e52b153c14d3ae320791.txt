Richard Hammond's Workshop premiered last year with series one and saw The Grand Tour star taking the first steps in a dream of his - to revamp classic cars. The Discovery+ series enticed viewers to tune in to watch the former Top Gear legend taking on a new project. Speaking exclusively to Express.co.uk about series two, which is available now, Richard talked about some of the more emotional moments that come with running the workshop.One year into the business, series two of Richard Hammond's Workshop shows the car expert struggling with the finances of the company.
