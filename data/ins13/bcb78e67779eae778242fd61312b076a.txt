By Dominic Chopping STOCKHOLM--Svenska Handelsbanken on Wednesday posted a third-quarter net profit that beat expectations after net interest income rose more than expected while fees and commissions remained stable at a high level despite falling stock markets. The Swedish bank reported net profit of 5.72 billion Swedish kronor ($515.8 million) compared with SEK4.73 billion a year earlier, and a FactSet consensus forecast of SEK5.02 billion. Net interest income rose to SEK9.58 billion from SEK7.
