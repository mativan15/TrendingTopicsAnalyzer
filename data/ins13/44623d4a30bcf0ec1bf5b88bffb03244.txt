October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Certa, a leading no-code automation platform for procurement and compliance, today announced it has been named a Top 50 Procurement Provider to Watch by Spend Matters, the proprietary tech-enabled data platform that drives and optimizes the strategic technology procurement decisions across the global procurement, finance, and supply chain technology ecosystem.



Now in its ninth year of publication, the 50 Providers to Watch list recognizes the fast-rising companies in the procurement and supply chain market.
