Initial group of UN Global Compact Patron and Sponsor companies announced ahead of Leaders Summit 2022

New York, USA, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The United Nations Global Compact is pleased to announce its initial group of Patron and Sponsor companies for their ongoing commitment to the Ten Principles of the UN Global Compact and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The 16 UN Global Compact Patron and Sponsor companies recognized today are highly engaged participants of the world's largest corporate sustainability initiative and represent different regions of the world and different industrial sectors.
