World Health Summit 2022German Chancellor Olaf Scholz delivers a speech at the World Health Summit 2022 in Berlin, Germany, 16 October 2022. A number of topics will be addressed, including climate change and health, pandemic preparedness, sustainable health systems and the role of the G7 and G20 in global health. Over 20 government officials and more than 300 speakers from all regions of the world and sectors of society are expected to attend. The summit takes place from 16 to 18 October. EPA/HA
