ReutersNottingham Forest's James Garner, Brennan Johnson, Keinan Davis and Djed Spence celebrate after winning the Championship Play-Off Final Action Images via Reuters/Andrew BoyersNottingham Forest won the Championship play-off final against Huddersfield 1-0 on Sunday to clinch promotion to the League for the first time in the long period of 23 years. The most lucrative football match in world history was won by Steve Cooper's team after Levi Colwill's own goal that was made late in the first half in Wembley.
