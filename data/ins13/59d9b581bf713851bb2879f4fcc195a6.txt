Info Edge bets on Housing.com founder Rahul Yadav's latest startup
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 16:30:00

Bengaluru: Sanjeev Bikhchandani-ledInfo Edge has been investing in Housing.com founder Rahul Yadav's latest startup, Broker Network. Operated by parent 4B Networks, it provides a technology platform to help brokers connect with properties, buyers and other brokers. According to recent filings with the Indian exchanges, invested Rs 137.12 crore in 4B Networks in March, through its subsidiary Allcheckdeals India (ACD), and has been investing in the company over multiple tranches. It invested Rs 9 crore in February and Rs 18 crore in December 2021.
