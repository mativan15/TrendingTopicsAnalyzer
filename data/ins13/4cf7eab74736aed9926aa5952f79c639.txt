Consensus EPS estimate is $3.07 and consensus revenue estimate is $6.41B (+15.1% Y/Y). Over the last 1 year, UNP has beaten EPS estimates 75% of the time and revenue estimates 50% of the time. Over the last 3 months, EPS estimates have seen 9 upward revisions and 13 downward. Revenue estimates have seen 18 upward revisions. UBS is positive on the rails sector ahead of earnings as the fuel benefit to operating income could be 2-3x larger than the tailwind seen in Q2.
