Annrai O'Toole's Utmost acquired by US-based Beeline
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-19 13:17:22

Utmost, the company that was cofounded by Irish tech veteran Annrai O'Toole, has been acquired by US based Beeline for an undisclosed sum.The Irish company, which has offices in Dublin and San Francisco, helps businesses to manage individuals who work for but are not directly employed by companies, including contractors, freelancers, vendors and consultants. Founded in 2018, Utmost's technology allows customers to view, in real time, both employee and non-employee workforces by location and reporting structure, helping them to manage and optimise their total workforce.
