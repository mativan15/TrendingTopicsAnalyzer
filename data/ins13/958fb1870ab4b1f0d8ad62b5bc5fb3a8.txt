We are committed to enhance our existing wholesale capabilities in India: Metro spokesperson
indiatimes.com
business
2022-06-02 02:44:00

New Delhi: Amid reports of Metro Cash and Carry, the India business of German wholesale retailer Metro AG, wrapping up operations, the company's spokesperson has said the Indian market has enormous potential and the retailer is committed to enhance its capabilities in the country.'The Indian market has enormous potential for wholesale and we are committed to enhance Metro's existing wholesale capabilities. Metro India business is doing well and we have seen a big jump in our EBITDA for FY 21 vs FY 20,' said a spokesperson of Metro India.
