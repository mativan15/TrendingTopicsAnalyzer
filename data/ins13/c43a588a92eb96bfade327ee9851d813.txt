Meet 11 people leading the aviation industry's push to slash emissions and ditch fossil fuels
businessinsider.com
news
2022-05-31 09:50:00

The global aviation industry is rushing to lower carbon emissions caused by air travel to meet ever stricter emissions standards, reduce fuel costs, and appeal to climate-conscious customers. Experts have estimated that aviation is responsible for nearly 2% of global greenhouse-gas emissions and 2.5% of carbon-dioxide emissions.Some aviation giants, like United, are signing purchase agreements to buy electric aircraft. Others, like Boeing, are buying sustainable aviation fuel to deliver to their customers.
