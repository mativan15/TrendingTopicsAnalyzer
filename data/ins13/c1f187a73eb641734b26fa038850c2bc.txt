Like its peers, American Airlines reported strong third quarter demand that is continuing into the current quarter. 'Demand is strong,' CEO Robert Isom said Thursday morning on Squawk Box. 'We're really pleased (with) profitability two quarters in a row, projecting a third. It's all fueled by a terrific demand environment' for leisure, international and business travel.
