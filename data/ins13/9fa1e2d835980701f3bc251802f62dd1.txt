‘EY India's Advisory Biz will Log Billion Dollars in Revenue Even After Split'
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 03:15:00

EY India's advisory business will register a billion dollars in revenue this financial year ending June 2023 even after the audit-advisory split, and the firm's employee strength in the country will cross 100,000 by then, said Rajiv Memani , chairman and managing partner. In an exclusive chat with Vinod Mahanta, Memani gets down to the brass tacks on the impending split at EY India even as the professional services firm — the first to log a billion dollars in revenue in the country — takes its first steps globally toward a formal separation of the two revenue streams.
