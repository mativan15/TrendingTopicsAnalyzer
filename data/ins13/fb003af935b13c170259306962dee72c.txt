Navigating Uncharted Territory: How to Craft an Employee-Friendly Return to the Office
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-03 14:37:10

As organizations plot a return-to-work plan that balances the needs of the business with the shifting priorities and expectations of their workforce, they are finding themselves in uncharted territory. In the move to mobilize and bring back employees to the office, they must bridge what can be a sizable gap between what employees prefer and what their employers envision.American Express, for instance, began bringing its New York City employees back to the office in March, based on the assumption that working virtually "will remain a lasting fixture of how we operate going forward.
