Unity, love, and harmony are the defining characters of the people of Karnataka and India and cannot be eliminated even if right-wing groups make attempts over the next 50 years to remove them, said Congress leader and MP Rahul Gandhi.He was addressing a massive public rally in Ballari in North Karnataka as part of Bharat Jodo Yatra, which on Saturday completed 1,000 kilometre of 3,570-km march launched in Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, on September 07, 2022.
