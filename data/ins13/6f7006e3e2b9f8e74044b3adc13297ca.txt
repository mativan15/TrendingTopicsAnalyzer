Hundreds of people are evacuated from Whitehall 'and Downing Street after suspicious package found'
msn.com
news
2022-10-18 11:12:47

A suspicious package has been found near Downing Street causing police to shut down Whitehall, according to reports.Government buildings along Whitehall, central London, are being emptied, a government official said.Hundreds of people were seen gathered at Horse Guards Parade.A policeman at the scene confirmed a road near Downing Street where Prime Minister Liz Truss's office and residence is closed due to reports of a suspicious package - which will be assessed by specialist officers. Whitehall, between the Houses of Parliament and Trafalgar Square, is also home to several government departments including the foreign ministry, the Cabinet Office and the Ministry of Defence.
