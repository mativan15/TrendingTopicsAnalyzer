The 2022 Trends in Investing Survey also shows advisers may be hedging on crypto

DENVER, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Over the past four years, financial adviser use/recommendation of environmental, social, and governance (ESG) investing strategies have remained consistent. Still, that usage could see a downward swing over the next 12 months, according to the 2022 Trends in Investing Survey, conducted by the Journal of Financial Planning and the Financial Planning Association® (FPA®), the leading membership organization for Certified Financial Planner™ professionals.
