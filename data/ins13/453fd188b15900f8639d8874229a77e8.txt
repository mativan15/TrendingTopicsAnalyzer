After his last TV show, Rang Jaun Tere Rang Mein (RJTRM), which he joined midway in August, and the news of it shutting surfaced a month later, Reyaansh Vir Chadha has now decided that he will not enter an ongoing daily soap. He says, 'I will avoid joining an ongoing daily soap. A new show doesn't deviate from the original storyline overnight and its characters get ample time to forge a connection with the audience. However, in an ongoing show, the team is already buckling under the pressure to rake in the numbers and fight other insecurities.
