Image caption, More than 100 residents got together for the street party in PinnerA generous gift of funds was split between a food bank and a Jubilee street party in Pinner, north-west London.Kara Heward, one of the organisers, said a resident had donated the fee from some filming in their house to the community.About 120 residents from the Chase got together to celebrate the Queen's Platinum jubilee.They enjoyed food, drink and games including a wheelbarrow race.Image caption, Organiser Kara Heward said half the money had been donated to a local food bankMs Heward said celebrating the jubilee held a personal significance for her due to the symbolism of the royal family.
