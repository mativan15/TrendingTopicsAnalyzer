October 18, 2022 09:56 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN MATEO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SnapLogic, a leader in intelligent integration and enterprise automation, today announced the launch of its global Integreat Tour, which will visit eight cities worldwide with the goal of helping attendees drive the greatest possible value out of their data integrations, automation initiatives, and digital transformation efforts.



The tour will begin in November with stops in Frankfurt, Paris, London, New York, Dallas, Menlo Park, and finish up in early 2023 in Sydney and Singapore.
