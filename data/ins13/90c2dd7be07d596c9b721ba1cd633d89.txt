Slipstream is a 60-metre superyacht available to charter with BurgessBurgess
It takes a certain formula to make a charter yacht popular year after year, particularly among families. Owners can go overboard with lots of bells and whistles, and amazing yet OTT design, but sometimes it's best to stick to the foundations of what guests really want. Slipstream is the most successful charter yacht in the 60-metre range under management by Burgess. Considering Burgess has a vast catalogue of incredible yachts on its books – including Aquila and Carpe Diem – that's quite the accolade.
