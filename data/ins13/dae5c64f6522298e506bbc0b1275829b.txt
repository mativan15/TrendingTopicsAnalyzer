Published: 05:51 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 05:52 EDT, 27 May 2022 Lionel Messi wants Joan Laporta to stop talking about him after the Barcelona president likened signing for PSG to slavery, according to a report.PSG icon Messi's father, Jorge, told Laporta to stop talking about the Argentina captain in a conversation described as 'cordial' two months ago, Cadena SER said.Laporta, whose first spell in charge of Barcelona saw the club win the Champions League twice, has now accused players of being motivated purely by finances when they join the Ligue 1 winners.
