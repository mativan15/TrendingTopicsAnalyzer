Some dramatic and dark storylines have gripped Emmerdale since it first hit screens 50 years ago, usually at the hands of some truly evil characters. Most recently, serial killer Meena Jutla (played by Paige Sandhu) wreaked havoc on the village by bumping off multiple residents before she was finally caught. However, some previous characters could probably give her a run for her money in claiming the title of ultimate villain. Who will be voted as the worst person to grace the soap?Love them or hate them, villains are an important part of all soaps.
