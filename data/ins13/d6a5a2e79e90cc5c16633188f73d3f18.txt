Collider.com Jesse Williams is making a big return to the long-running medical drama Grey's Anatomy. He will return as director and guest star in the fifth episode of the show's 19th season. The episode, which will air on Nov. 3, is called 'When I Get to the Border', and features Williams' return as Dr. Jackson Avery. Williams has played Jackson since 2009, and over the course of 12 seasons, fans watched him develop from inexperienced intern to successful plastic surgeon, and eventually a loving father.
