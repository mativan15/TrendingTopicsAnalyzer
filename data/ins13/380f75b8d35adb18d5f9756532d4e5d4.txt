ARLINGTON, Va., Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Today, Radio America released the official trailer previewing its new podcast, Dark Wars: The Border, set to premiere on Oct. 25, exactly two weeks before Election Day. Hosted by award-winning investigative journalist Sara Carter, the podcast follows Carter on her perilous journey to expose how the porous U.S.-Mexico border has facilitated a deadly trail from America's foreign adversaries to your hometown; with cartels, slavery, and death in between.
