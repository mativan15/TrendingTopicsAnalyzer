David Moyes' West Ham make the relatively short trip to the south coast to take on Ralph Hasenhuttl's Southampton for a big clash in the bottom half of the Premier League West Ham United put European duties to one side as they make a return to Premier League action with a trip to face Southampton on Sunday. The Hammers have begun to find their stride in recent weeks with consecutive top flight victories over Wolves and Fulham, while they secured their place in the knockout stages of the Europa Conference League on Thursday with a 2-1 victory over Anderlecht.
