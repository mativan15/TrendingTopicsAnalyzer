Low-pressure area over sea intensifies into depression, cyclone likely to be formed by October 24
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-22 09:22:49

A low-pressure area over the north Andaman sea that on October 22, 2022 intensified into a depression, around 1,460 km south-southeast of Sagar Island in West Bengal, is expected to take the shape of a cyclonic storm by October 24, the IMD said in its bulletin.The weather system moved west-northwestward and concentrated into a depression on October 22, 2022 at 8.30 am, close to west of Andaman Islands, the bulletin said."It is expected to move northwestward and intensify further into a deep depression over east-central and adjoining southeast Bay of Bengal by October 23 morning.
