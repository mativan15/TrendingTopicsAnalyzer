Pune, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global ceramic sanitary ware market size is anticipated to hit USD 74.52 billion by 2028 and exhibit a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period. The increasing construction activities due to rising urbanization, industrialization, and the growing trend of smart homes is projected to boost the market growth.
