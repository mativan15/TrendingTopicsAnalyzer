Bray Wyatt left WWE fans in tears as he brought SmackDown to a close with an emotional speech before a gut punch of a twist.
The former world champion – who made a sensational return at Extreme Rules last weekend – appeared essentially out of character as Windham Rotunda for the first time and offered a heartbreaking insight into the ‘past year of his life', including dealing with the death of Brodie Lee and getting fired by the company.
