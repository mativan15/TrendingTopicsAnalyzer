NEW YORK, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- My Money My Future, a leading fintech for financial inclusion, and Black & Brown Founders, a non-profit focused on tech entrepreneurship, announced today a new initiative to educate and activate one million Black & Latino investors. The THRIVE initiative will focus on increasing access to investor education and advancing wealth building in underserved communities.
