ETtechThe company is in the process of amalgamating Mindtree with itself, which was approved by its shareholders and unsecured creditors on August 10.New Delhi: IT company Infotech on Saturday posted an about 23 per cent increase in its consolidated net profit at Rs 679.8 crore for the quarter ended September 2022. The company had registered a net profit of Rs 551.7 crore in the same period a year ago. 
 The consolidated revenue from operations of Larsen and Toubro Infotech (LTI) grew by 28.39 per cent to Rs 4,836.
