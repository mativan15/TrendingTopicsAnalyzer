Concor shares fall 4.73% as Nifty drops
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 08:45:00

Shares of Container Corporation of India Ltd. traded 4.73 per cent down at Rs 734.65 on Thursday at around 01:44PM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex declined 143.09 points to 58964.1. As many as 60,239 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 4.54 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 781.95 and a 52-week low of Rs 554.1. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 656.64, while 50-DMA was at Rs 711.38. If a stock trades above 50 DMA and 200 DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is up.
