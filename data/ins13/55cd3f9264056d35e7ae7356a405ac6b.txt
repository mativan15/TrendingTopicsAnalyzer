Here we go again. The hedge fund, Macellum Advisors, is making a third attempt to overhaul Kohl's board and is advising the company there will be another proxy fight if the company fails to immediately address the issue. The attack is directed at board chairman Peter Boneparth and board members Stephanie Streeter, John Schlifske, and Jonas Prising. In the opinion of Jonathan Duskin, Macellum's CEO, they failed to 'adequately support Kohl's senior management.
