Vet warns of dangerous Halloween TikTok trend that can lead to distress in dogs

2022-10-18 15:08:43

A vet has issued a warning to pet owners about dressing their dogs up this Halloween after a TikTok video went viral showing a dog wearing false teeth, which can be 'incredibly dangerous and distressing'Halloween can be a distressing time for pets (stock image) As much as we love dressing up and eating all the festive foods at Halloween, the same cannot be said for our four-legged friends. This time of year can pose significant dangers to our beloved pets, from the toxic ingredients in treats to the uncomfortable nature of fancy dress.
