lifelong learning getty If you've been working for a couple decades (or more), you may find yourself facing a career lull, and thinking: Argh! I've been doing the same thing for so long! I want something new! That frustrated feeling may be a sign that it's time for an educational reboot. Lifelong learning—a term that covers an array of actions—isn't only for those with years of experience and repetition fatigue. Restocking your mental toolkit can advance your career and enhance your life at any age, whether you're a recent grad interested in a field different from your major, a mid-career professional, or a grandparent watching your first grandchild head to college.
