Americans can ridicule Liz Truss. But our own governing problems go much deeper.

2022-10-21 00:15:00

It's easy for Americans to look to the United Kingdom and compare fortunes, considering how closely tied together our countries are in history and heritage. Leaders of our two nations even refer to the two countries' 'Special Relationship' in tones where you can hear the capitalizations spoken out loud. Those comparisons are in vogue again this week since Liz Truss' resignation after six weeks as prime minister of the United Kingdom, leaving her Conservative political party and Britain's economy in disarray.
