Telesilla Kotsi is an assistant professor of operations management at the Fisher College of Business. She joined the Management Sciences department after receiving her PhD from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. Telesilla combines field research with rigorous analytical methods to study challenges that nonprofit organizations face. For example, in her current work, she uses game theory, dynamic programming, and econometrics. Nonprofit operations management is an important area of research, given the nonprofits' contribution to the US economy is larger than the individual contributions in all but three states.
