May 31, 2022 01:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CONCORD, Calif. & LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--To decarbonise buildings and help advance Net-Zero initiatives, US-based Buildings IOT has launched operations in the UK to serve the unique needs of building stakeholders in Europe. Buildings IOT will market its IOT Jetstream smart building platform for building owners, managers, investors, contractors, and systems integrators located in the UK and across Europe.



Buildings IOT's UK operation is led by Les Russell, Director of Business Development.
