Image source, Msc1photographyImage caption, As well as winning the overall award, the museum in Sheffield also took home the prize for the Best Small MuseumA museum which faced closure after the Covid lockdowns but was saved by a crowdfunding campaign has been named the UK's best Family Friendly Museum.The National Emergency Services Museum in Sheffield was praised by judges for "outstanding community work, hands-on galleries and welcoming staff".It displays many historical vehicles, including a fire pump dating from 1710.
