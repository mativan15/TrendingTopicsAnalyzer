Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, The "toolkit" is designed to help school nurses with the techniques in dealing with the disordersMental health professionals are to unveil a "toolkit" to help school nurses tackle eating disorders pupils. Bath-based campaigner Hope Virgo developed the strategy with the School and Public Health Nurses Association (Saphna) after rise in cases.Numbers of those waiting for treatment has increased more than three fold since March 2020, one charity says. The toolkit aims to equip school nurses with techniques to discuss eating disorders, and also "what not to say".
