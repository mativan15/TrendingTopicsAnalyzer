By Jake Fenner For Dailymail.Com Published: 23:03 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 23:07 EDT, 20 October 2022 Yankees starter Luis Severino didn't make many mistakes in his outing that saw him strike out six batters and only surrender five hits. But it was a 1-2 pitch with two outs in the bottom of the third to Alex Bregman that saw the third baseman launch a home run into the stands of Minute Maid Park to go up 3-0. That was all that was needed as Astros starter Framber Valdez struck out nine, while only surrendering two runs in his seven innings of pitching.
