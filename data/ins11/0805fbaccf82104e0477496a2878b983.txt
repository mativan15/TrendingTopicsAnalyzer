Getty ImagesIn April, IRDAI chairman Debasish Panda had said the regulator is thinking of allowing micro insurance players with ₹10 crore or ₹15 crore capital which can work in focus areas.New Delhi: The government is in discussion with the insurance regulator on relaxing minimum entry capital requirement for setting up an insurance venture, along with tax sops and lower solvency margin or extra capital requirements. At present a minimum investment of ₹100 crore is required to set up an insurance firm.
