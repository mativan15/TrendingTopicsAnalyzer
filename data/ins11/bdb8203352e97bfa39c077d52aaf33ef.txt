Monsoon rainfall in 2022 to be normal for India, says IMD
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 07:30:00

In 2022, India's state-run meteorological agency predicts that the country will experience normal or above-average monsoon rainfall for the fourth straight year, boosting agricultural and general economic growth in Asia's third-largest economy. Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) director general Mrutyunjay Mohapatra predicted that the country will receive 103 percent of its long-term average rainfall this year.

Also, La Nina conditions to continue during monsoon. MET department had earlier projected southwest monsoon rains at 99% of LPA.
