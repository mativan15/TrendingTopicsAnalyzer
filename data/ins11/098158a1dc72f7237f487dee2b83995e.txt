If you want to know who really controls Dukemount Capital Plc (LON:DKE), then you'll have to look at the makeup of its share registry. The group holding the most number of shares in the company, around 55% to be precise, is individual investors. In other words, the group stands to gain the most (or lose the most) from their investment into the company.

Following a 333% increase in the stock price last week, individual investors profited the most, but institutions who own 35% stock also stood to gain from the increase.
