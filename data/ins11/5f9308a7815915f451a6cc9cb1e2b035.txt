getty Just over 20 years ago, I joined a seven-person Boston-based software startup called Endeca as their first engineer. Those in that scene might remember the company for the role it played in search capabilities. More importantly, though, looking back, Endeca established an outsized legacy in the Boston tech scene, as many company alumni went on to found innovative companies like Toast, Salsify and Jellyfish.
