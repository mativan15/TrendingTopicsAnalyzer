1. What is Advanced Drug Delivery?
Advanced drug delivery refers to the methods, formulations, and procedures (routes of
administration) designed to administer drugs for various applications or therapeutic usage. The
term advanced drug delivery (ADD) refers to technologies that modulate the rate of drug
release. The technology utilized is heavily influenced by the kind of disease, drug, and desired
effect. The objective of advanced drug delivery system is to deliver a therapeutic amount of drug
to the appropriate site in the body in order to attain and then maintain the desired drug
concentration as quickly and efficiently as possible.
