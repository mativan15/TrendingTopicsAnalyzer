Comedy duo Dick and Dom have joined calls for the UK government to call a general election.The Dick & Dom in da Bungalow stars – real names Richard McCourt and Dominic Wood – shared their thoughts on Twitter following a turbulent day in British politics.Wednesday (19 October) saw the sudden dismissal of home secretary Suella Braverman after just weeks in the role.'#GeneralElectionNow you twisted f***s,' tweeted Dick and Dom, from their official shared Twitter account.Since Truss took over as prime minister just weeks ago, calls have been mounting for a general election.
