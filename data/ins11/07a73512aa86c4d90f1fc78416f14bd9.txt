Travis Perkins 3Q Sales Rose; Sees Full-Year Operating Profit Slipping
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 06:30:00

By Joe Hoppe Travis Perkins PLC said Thursday that its third-quarter sales rose and that it expects full-year operating profit to slip, falling around the middle of the current range of market expectations. The U.K. building-materials retailer said for that the third quarter it saw like-for-like sales growth of 7.4%. On a total basis, sales growth was 10.7%. The company's merchanting business delivered 11.5% total sales growth over the quarter, while its Toolstation business grew 6.1%.
