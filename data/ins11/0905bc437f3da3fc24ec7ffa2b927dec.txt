Stock market news live updates: Stock futures inch higher to kick off June
aol.com
news
2022-06-01 11:24:45

U.S stocks edged slightly higher before the open Wednesday as investors look ahead to a fresh month of trading with continued focus on inflation and the Federal Reserve's efforts to rein in rising prices.Futures tied to the S&P 500 climbed 0.3%, while contracts on the Dow Jones Industrial Average gained 130 points, or 0.4%. Nasdaq futures ticked just above breakeven.Shares of Salesforce (CRM) climbed 8.6% in pre-market trading after the software company raised its profit forecast and said it did not see any significant impact on its operations from macroeconomic uncertainty.
