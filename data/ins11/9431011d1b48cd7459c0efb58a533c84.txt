metamorworks/iStock via Getty Images Domo, Inc. (NASDAQ:DOMO) is a software company which offers a leading big data analytics and visualisation platform. The company was founded in 2010 and had their IPO in 2018. Since then the stock had a volatile couple of years before soaring by 158% in 2020, during the growth stock bull market. However, since November 2021 the stock has had a major correction down by 64% and now trades at just 20% higher than its IPO price at the end of the first day of trading, $27 per share.
