Paul Pelosi, the husband of House Speaker Nancy Pelosi, was arrested on suspicion of driving under the influence in Napa County, California, Saturday night. 
The 82-year-old businessman was booked into jail on two misdemeanor counts, driving under the influence and driving with a blood alcohol content level of 0.08 or higher, TMZ reported. Pelosi's bail was set for $5,000, according to records obtained by multiple news outlets. 
Through his various business ventures, Pelosi has accumulated a net worth of $120 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
