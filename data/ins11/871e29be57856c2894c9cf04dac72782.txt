May 31, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO & BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Voltus, Inc., the leading distributed energy resource (DER) software platform, announced it has partnered with SemaConnect, a leader and pioneer in electric vehicle charging services, to offer cash generating opportunities to SemaConnect's workplace charging clients across U.S. and Canadian wholesale power markets.



"Integrating with Voltus is part of our strategic growth strategy allowing our stations to connect to wholesale power markets," said Mahi Reddy, CEO and Founder at SemaConnect.
