Jay Steinback, the longstanding CEO of Rothman Furniture, has been busy since his receiving his 40 under 40 award in 2017. Rothman Furniture evolved into VUE Enterprises, a company dedicated to growing and servicing staff and small businesses alike. Most recently Jay launched yWhales, a Web3 community-based technology firm that builds, advises, and invests in Web3 technologies. Debuting with a strong evaluation, yWhales is first of its kind to provide these extensive Web3 business offerings.
