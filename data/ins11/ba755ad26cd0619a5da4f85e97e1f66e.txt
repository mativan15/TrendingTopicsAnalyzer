Giant alligator wrangled on Florida beach: Video
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 15:16:24

An alligator measuring nearly 12 feet long was spotted in a beach's surf in Delray Beach, Florida, on Wednesday morning.The City of Delray Beach shared a video that featured a slideshow of photos of the incident. According to the video caption, a contracted nuisance alligator trapper with the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission (FWC) was called to help remove the alligator, which was transferred to a farm.Tammy Sapp, a spokesperson with FWC told Newsweek in an email the agency receives calls about nuisance alligators throughout the year because alligators are active and visible whenever the weather is warm.
