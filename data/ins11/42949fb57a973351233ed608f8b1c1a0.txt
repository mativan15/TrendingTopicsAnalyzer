The Vietnam blockchain industry forum co-hosted by Huobi Global and OneBlock Labs drew to a successful close on May 28, with leading experts from Huobi Global and various fields of the Vietnamese blockchain industry having avidly discussed the finer points of the journey toward Web3 and the Metaverse, to the benefit of numerous forum participants.
