Veteran referee, 72, assaulted twice in 18 months says FA must get tough with Jurgen Klopp
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 12:50:00

'For a manager to go at a linesman like that … he's full on shouting. Players see these things. In amateur games, where I am, I do get screamed and shouted at a lot: ‘You should be doing this, you should be doing that'. 
'It's got ridiculous. It's every game. I played myself for 20 years. I was an aggressive little player and got sent off a few times but I knew the difference. I've seen an awful lot of change. There are some very angry people. Sometimes they get pulled away before they get to you.
