Walmart has announced its plans for Black Friday 2022 as the holiday shopping season draws ever closer. The retailer announced in a press release (opens in new tab) that it will bring back its weekly 'Black Friday Deals for Days' events throughout the whole of November featuring significant deals across electronics, home, toys, clothing, and more as well as popular brands such as Apple, Dyson, and Lego.It'll work like this. A new batch of Walmart Black Friday deals will launch every Monday at 7pm ET in November and remain available throughout the week - or until stocks last.
