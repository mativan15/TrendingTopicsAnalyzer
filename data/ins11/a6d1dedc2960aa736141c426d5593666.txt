Published: 19:13 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 19:21 EDT, 18 October 2022 Two weeks ago, Karen Millen's phone pinged with a 'Google alert' to a newspaper story. Not only, she learned, was Kate, the Princess of Wales, wearing a dress from the fashion range that carries her name — but so, too, on the very same day, Prime Minister Liz Truss was out and about in one of the label's frocks.Karen received a flurry of messages on social media from people who'd seen the PM deliver her 'big speech' at the Conservative Party conference in a £225 stylish, red Forever dress, followed by Kate arriving for an official visit at the Royal Surrey County Hospital in a £220 yellow pleated version of the same belted frock — all congratulating her on this design double coup.
