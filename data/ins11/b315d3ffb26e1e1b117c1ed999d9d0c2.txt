Bo Burnham releases unseen scenes from Netflix special Inside
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 15:45:00

Bo Burnham, a comedian, just published an extended video with deleted material from his comedy special "Inside." To commemorate the one-year anniversary of his award-winning Netflix special "Inside," he did so. This almost hour-long film, labeled "The Inside Outtakes," debuted on Burnham's YouTube channel Monday evening. Burnham released the unseen recordings for his special "Inside" In April, Burnham returned to the footage and started editing the supplementary footage into a coherent piece. The comedian and singer-songwriter hinted at the release of this one-hour deleted part on Twitter.
