June 02, 2022 07:53 PM Eastern Daylight Time PITTSBURGH--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Resco Products, Inc. announced a price increase with a range of 12% to 15% on all refractory products. The price change has an effective date for all shipments on or after June 1, 2022. As with most companies, Resco has seen significant cost increases from raw material suppliers and global logistic suppliers, and inflation has also impacted other input costs, including energy, wages, supplies, and consumables.
