China is wedded to a zero-Covid strategy of hard lockdowns, mass testing and long quarantine periods to wipe out clusters as they emerge.







Beijing to reopen schools and workplaces as Covid-19 curbs ease(Bloomberg)


Published on Jun 05, 2022 02:04 PM IST
 

Beijing will gradually lift Covid-19 restrictions this week, city officials said Sunday.After some easing in recent days, the Chinese capital -- which reported 19 new infections Sunday -- announced residents would start returning to work from Monday and schools would reopen from June 13.
