Country witnessing economic, cultural and scientific renaissance under Narendra Modi: Manoj Sinha
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 06:30:00

Lieutenant Governor of Jammu and Kashmir Manoj Sinha on Wednesday said that the country is witnessing an economic, cultural, and scientific renaissance and Jammu and Kashmir is the leading light of the country in culture and literature. Sinha, while addressing an inaugural function of the Kumaon literary festival being held in Srinagar said that J&K is the only Sharda Peeth and abode of Saraswati but unfortunately due to the situation here development and thinking was stalled in this place for several decades.
