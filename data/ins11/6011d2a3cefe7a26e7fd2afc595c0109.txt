 A new Google Drive update should make it easier to find out exactly where your key files are hiding.The cloud storage platform has announced it will be adding a new "Location" column that expands to show the exact full name and other information of your Google Drive files.In a blog post (opens in new tab) announcing the news Google said the new feature will make it easier for users to differentiate between similarly-named files stored in different locations, meaning you should be able to find exactly what you're looking for at a much faster rate.
