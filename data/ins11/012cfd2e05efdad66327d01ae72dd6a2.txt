Suffolk Downs is burning, and firemen are fighting to save the historic building
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 14:30:00

ReutersSuffolk Downs reported a fire late at night yesterday around 11 pm. Firefighters immediately responded to the call and tried to control the fire and save the historic building from burning down completely. Drones were pressed into service by Boston's fire department and last heard a 5th fire truck was deployed to continue the supply of water. There apparently is a shortage of water in the area and the 5th fire truck was deployed as a result. The Boston Fire department tweeted that there was a massive fire on the rooftops and heavy smoke all around the building.
