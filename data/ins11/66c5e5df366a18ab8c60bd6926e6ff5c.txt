Published: 11:23 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 11:25 EDT, 20 October 2022 It was billed, by some, as the best summer of transfer activity in history. Others more conservatively called it the greatest market that Barcelona had ever had. Even the more skeptical could not help be impressed as over €150m were spent on seven new recruits plus the re-signed Dembele. But nine games into the season, and with the first Clasico of the campaign done and dusted, a reassessment might be in order. Sportsmail grades the newcomers at the Camp Nou, and the first-quarterly report doesn't make good reading.
