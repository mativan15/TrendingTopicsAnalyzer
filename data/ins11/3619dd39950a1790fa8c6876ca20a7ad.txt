ReutersOn an immediate basis, 15,770/52,500 and 15,850/52,700 levels would be major hurdles.NEW DELHI: The Nifty Realty index traded positive around 11:00AM(IST)on Monday in an upbeat market. Oberoi Realty(up 6.1 per cent), DLF(up 4.01 per cent), Phoenix Mills(up 3.55 per cent), Macrotech Developers(up 3.0 per cent) and Brigade Enterprises(up 2.94 per cent) were among the top gainers. Indiabulls Real Estate(down 1.09 per cent) were the top losers on the index. The Nifty Realty index was up 3.24 per cent at 400.
