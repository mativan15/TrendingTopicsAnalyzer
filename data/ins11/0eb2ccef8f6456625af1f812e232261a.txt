Paradeep Phosphates lists at a premium
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 05:15:00

ReutersThe price band for the Rs 1,501 crore IPO has been fixed at Rs 39-42 per equity share of face value Rs 10 each. The shares will be listed on BSE as well as NSE.Shares of Paradeep Phosphates listed at ₹43.55 on the BSE on Friday, a 3.7% premium over its IPO price of ₹42. The stock ended 4.6% above its issue price at ₹43.95 after touching a high of ₹47.25 during the day. On the NSE, the stock listed at a premium of 5% at ₹44. Earlier this month, the company had raised ₹1,501.73 crore through its IPO that was priced in the range of ₹39 to ₹42 per share.
