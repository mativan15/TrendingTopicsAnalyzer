Canada GDP Growth Slows to 3.1% in 1Q
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 12:30:00

OTTAWA--Canadian economic growth slowed sharply in the first quarter, missing market expectations by a wide margin, as a decline in trade volumes offset robust advances in household purchases, residential real estate and business investment. The report indicated Canadian household income grew at one of the fastest rates in 40 years, and the savings rate climbed.
