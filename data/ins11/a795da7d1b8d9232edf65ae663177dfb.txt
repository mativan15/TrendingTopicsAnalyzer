Truth prevailed, say ruling parties after NCB clean chit to Aryan Khan
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-27 16:41:43

Soon after the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) on Friday gave a clean chit to Aryan Khan, son of actor Shah Rukh Khan, in the Cordelia drugs bust case, the Maharashtra Vikas Aghadi (MVA) came down on the Bharatiya Janata Party-led Central government for its 'attempts to defame the state and Bollywood'. Shiv Sena MP Priyanka Chaturvedi said 'truth prevails' and slammed BJP for trying to 'demonise' Maharashtra by 'misusing Central agencies.' 'One tight slap on fraud PR created Singham, the news media channels who work in the service of BJP, the BJP actress- spokesperson, those who tried to demonise Maharashtra through misusing central agencies.
