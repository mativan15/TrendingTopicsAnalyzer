What you say and how you say it is critically important in a business setting. I'm certainly an advocate of using trendy and inspiring words, since that helps us all live in the present age and communicate more effectively. A few years ago, writers like me started using the word 'cachet' more and more in online articles and in print, although it has now lost favor (it means 'prestige'). While I'm not saying we should use jargon and catch-phrases, it's okay to communicate like we're living in 2022 and not 2002.
