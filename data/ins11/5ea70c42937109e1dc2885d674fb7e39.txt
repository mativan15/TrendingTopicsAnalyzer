Flight delays and cancellations are becoming a more common part of air travel than ever. Over Memorial Day weekend alone, U.S. carriers canceled more than 2,500 flights. With the world largely moving forward from the pandemic, the airline industry remains stuck behind. In the process, it's leaving many travelers stranded. As business meetings, events and conferences are transitioning back to in-person, the demand for air travel is high--and so are the stakes riding on each flight. Your trip could be the last step in forming a key partnership, or even securing millions in funding.
