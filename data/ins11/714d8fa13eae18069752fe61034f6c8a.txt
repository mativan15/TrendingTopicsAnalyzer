Christopher Ecclestone made a surprise appearance on Tuesday's episode of It Takes Two to wish Tyler West and Dianne Buswell good luck with their upcoming dance to the Doctor Who theme.
Kiss FM presenter Tyler will perform a tango to the sci-fi show's iconic opening soundtrack by Segun Akinola on Saturday night as the celebrity contestants mark the corporation's centenary.
Rylan Clark delighted audiences when he revealed former Time Lord Christopher had a special message for the couple.
