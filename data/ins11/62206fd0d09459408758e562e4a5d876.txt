Taking a side hustle isn't uncommon, but one woman appears to have made it more lucrative than you would imagine. Gemma, 33, from York, UK, claims that she earns £2,000 (Rs 2 lakh) per month from her side hustle alone, which includes taking online surveys, mystery shopping, and cat sitting. Instagram/@helpsavemoneyShe started up her own crafts business, Gembobs Crafts, in 2014. In order to earn an extra bit of cash, she started doing mystery shopping excursions and online surveys for a small fee each time.
