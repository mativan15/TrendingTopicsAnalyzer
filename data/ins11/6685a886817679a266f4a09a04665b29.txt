Noted Sinti Holocaust survivor Zilli Schmidt dies – DW – 10/21/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-21 18:35:33

Zilli Schmidt, who survived Nazi death camps to go on to fight against racism and injustice, has died aged 98. She was an important witness in the trial of a former Auschwitz official.Zilli Schmidt, a Holocaust survivor who was one of the last eyewitnesses of the German Nazi regime's atrocities committed against the Sinti and Roma communities, died on Friday at the age of 98 in the western German city of Mannheim. Among other things, Schmidt suffered internment in the Nazi death camp Auschwitz-Birkenau from 1943 to 1944.
