NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--Colgate-Palmolive (NYSE:CL) Group President, Europe and Developing Markets, Panagiotis Tsourapas, and Chief Investor Relations Officer and SVP, M&A, John Faucher, will participate in a presentation and fireside chat at the dbAccess Global Consumer Conference in Paris on Wednesday, June 15, 2022 at 3:15 am ET.Investors may access a live webcast of this fireside chat on Colgate's website at www.colgatepalmolive.com. For those unable to participate during the live webcast, a recorded version of the webcast will be made available through the Investor Center section of Colgate's website.
