Mullen Shares Climb After 3Q Revenue, Profit Rise
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 13:53:00

By Adriano Marchese Mullen Group Ltd. shares were higher Thursday morning after the company said revenue rose and income more than doubled in the third quarter, though results could moderate in the future due to the macroeconomic climate. At 9:44 a.m. ET, shares were up 1.5% at C$14.89, having risen as high as C$15.48 earlier in the morning. The Canadian logistics providers said revenue rose 20% to 518.4 million Canadian dollars ($376.6 million), driving net income to C$38 million from C$17.5 million a year earlier.
