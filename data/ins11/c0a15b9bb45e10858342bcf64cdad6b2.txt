By CBSBoston.com Staff June 2, 2022 / 9:25 AM / CBS Boston BOSTON -- The matchup between the Celtics and the Warriors in the NBA Finals figures to be a classic. The Warriors are going for their fourth title in the last eight years, while the Celtics are going for their first since 2008.Another championship would further cement Steph Curry, Klay Thompson, and Draymond Green among the greats in NBA history. But Boston's young core of Jayson Tatum, Jaylen Brown, and Marcus Smart are looking to show the basketball world that they've arrived.
