Karnataka Bank Q4 net profit soars
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-28 04:15:00

Mangaluru: Karnataka Bank has posted an annual consolidated net profit of Rs 507 crore for FY 2021-22, an increase of 5% from the previous year's Rs 482 crore. The net profit for the fourth quarter ended March 2022 was Rs 130 crore, a four-fold rise from Rs 31 crore in the corresponding quarter of FY 2020-21. The Board on Friday recommended a dividend of 40%. The business turnover stood at Rs 137,169 crore as on March 31, compared to Rs 127,348 crore a year ago. Mahabaleshwara M S , MD & CEO of the bank, said net profit of Rs 507 crore was an all-time high.
