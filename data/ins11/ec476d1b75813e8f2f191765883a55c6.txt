JD's trainer king, Peter Cowgill, loses his crown – leaving big shoes to fill
theguardian.com
sport
2022-05-28 15:00:34

He was the self-styled king of trainers, leading one of the high street's coolest stores, but a string of missteps has led to Peter Cowgill, 69, being kicked out of the business he built.After a board meeting at the group's Bury headquarters last Wednesday, he exited with immediate effect – and JD Group's shares dived by 18%.Cowgill, the workaholic chair and chief executive of JD since 2004, oversaw a dramatic turnaround in the group's fortunes, building its market value to 60 times what it was when he arrived, and creating a global brand.
