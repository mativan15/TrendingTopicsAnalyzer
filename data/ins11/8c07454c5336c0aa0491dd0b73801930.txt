The partnership adds UserWay accessibility technologies, including its AI-powered solution, to the Tribute Technology website management platform.WILMINGTON, DE, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- UserWay (TASE: UWAY), the leading digital accessibility company, is partnering with Tribute Technology to make funeral home websites more accessible for people with disabilities. The partnership adds UserWay technologies, including its AI-powered accessibility solution, to the Tribute Technology website management platform.
