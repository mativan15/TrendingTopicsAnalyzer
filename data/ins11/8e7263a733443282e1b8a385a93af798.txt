DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc.

CHARLOTTE, N.C., May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- DENTSPLY SIRONA Inc. ('Dentsply Sirona') (Nasdaq: XRAY), announced that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.125 per share of common stock, an indicated annual rate of $0.50 per share. The dividend is payable on July 8, 2022 to holders of record as of June 24, 2022.



About Dentsply Sirona

Dentsply Sirona is the world's largest manufacturer of professional dental products and technologies, with a century of innovation and service to the dental industry and patients worldwide.
