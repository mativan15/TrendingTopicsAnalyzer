Anybody looking for a PS5 restock yesterday got back-to-back events at Walmart and Amazon. Even so, availability for a PS5 restock is looking a bit different these days. Amazon has introduced a new reservation system for products that are difficult to track down, starting today with the latest PS5 restock. But it's not enough to have a subscription and be online when the consoles are available -- you need to wait in a special queue for your turn. While it will take longer to make a purchase with this new procedure, the upside is that it will likely dramatically reduce how many consoles are bought up by bots.
