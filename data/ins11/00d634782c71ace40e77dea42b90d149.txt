Brookfield sells U.K. student housing company Student Roost to institutional investors
theglobeandmail.com
business
2022-05-30 15:22:15

Brookfield Asset Management Inc. BAM-A-T has sold a U.K.-based student-housing company to two institutional investors, the payoff from several years of building the business from scratch.GIC Private Ltd., which is Singapore's sovereign wealth fund, and Greystar Real Estate Partners LLC, a U.S.-based rental-housing company, will acquire Student Roost from one of Brookfield's private real estate funds.The two buyers describe Student Roost as the U.K.'s third-largest purpose-built student accommodation provider.
