AgenciesFormer RBI Governor Raghuram Rajan wants India to focus on services-led growth model.Former RBI Governor Raghuram Rajan has batted for a services-led export model for India instead of following China's manufacturing-led one in order to grow faster. In a lecture on globalisation and climate change, Rajan focused on the positive impact of liberalising services which offers massive potential in reducing inequality. Liberalising trade in services is good for both industrial as well as emerging market economies.
