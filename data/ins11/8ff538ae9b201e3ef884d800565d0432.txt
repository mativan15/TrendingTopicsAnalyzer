3 Ways to Get the Best Deal on a Personal Loan

When you need money, whether to fix up your house, repair your car, or pay off medical bills, you may be inclined to apply for a personal loan. The upside of getting a personal loan is being able to use your loan proceeds for any purpose. And you'll generally pay less interest on a personal loan than you will on a credit card.Plus, personal loans tend to close fairly quickly. When you apply for a mortgage, for example, it can take many weeks from the time you get the ball rolling to close on your home loan.
