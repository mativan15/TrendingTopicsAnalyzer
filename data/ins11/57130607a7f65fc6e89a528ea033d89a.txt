October 19, 2022 04:26 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Reps. John Joyce (R-Pa.), Dan Meuser (R-Pa.), Scott Perry (R-Pa.), and Lloyd Smucker (R-Pa.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

All four representatives scored a perfect 100 percent in 2021, making them all a 'Taxpayer Super Hero.
