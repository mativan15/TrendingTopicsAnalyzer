A woman has been left baffled by her friend's choice of baby name because it sounds just like a common type of infection - but her mate can't seem to see the problemThe mum has been urged to reconsider her baby name choice (stock photo) As parents look for more unique names to give their children, it's more important than ever to make sure the name you've chosen doesn't accidentally take inspiration from something that you really don't want your kid associated with. And after one set of expectant parents recently announced their desire to name their baby Samnella, completely missing the way it sounds just like the bacteria, Salmonella, another woman is now determined to name her baby after an infection - even though her friends have warned her not to.
