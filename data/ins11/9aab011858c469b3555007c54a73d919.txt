Man in dock for extorting money from wife's lover who ended life
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 07:45:00

Vadodara: It may sound strange, but a man has been booked by the City police for blackmailing his wife's lover. Police have registered a case of abetment to suicide and extortion against Darvesh Malek , a resident of Fatehpura , for blackmailing Mayur Patel who committed suicide three days ago.Patel's father told the police that the deceased had complained of uneasiness on October 13 following which they rushed him to the hospital. He died during treatment but during the funeral, his friend Javed Rathod approached his family.
