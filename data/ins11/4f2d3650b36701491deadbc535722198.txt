Published: 10:42 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 10:44 EDT, 2 June 2022 The heartbroken family of a newlywed bride found dead in a Swansea tanning salon is raising cash to fly her home to New Zealand.Piata Tauwhare, 30, collapsed in the tanning booth after booking an appointment for an 11-minute session.The alarm was raised when her worried husband Ifan Jones, 23, was unable to contact her following the visit.He called his mother Emma Collyer-Miles, 42, who then went to try to find Piata at Lextan in Swansea, South Wales, on Saturday.
