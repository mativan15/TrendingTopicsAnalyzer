October 21, 2022 02:18 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KBRA assigns preliminary ratings to four classes of notes issued by Goldman Home Improvement Trust 2022-GRN2 Issuer Trust ('GHIT 2022-GRN2'), a home improvement loan ABS transaction.

GHIT 2022-GRN2 will issue four classes of notes totaling $213.988 million, collateralized by 95% of economic participation interests (the 'Participations') in a pool of approximately $265.0 million consumer loans used to finance home improvement products and services originated by third-party banks under the GreenSky Program.
