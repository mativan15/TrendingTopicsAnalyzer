Moscow in FLAMES: Putin horror as at least 20 trapped after blaze rips through building

2022-06-03 08:37:00

More than 20 people are trapped in a building on fire (Image: TASS)People are understood to be trapped on the sixth and seventh floor of the ten floor Grand Setun Plaza business center in the Russian city.  A source told TASS: "Up to 20 people can be in the building on the sixth and seventh floors. One is blocked on the seventh. The rest may be on the sixth."The cause of the fire is currently unknown. It is unknown whether there are any injuries. It comes amid a spate of mystery blazes across Russia as Vladimir Putin's war on Ukraine continues to rage.
