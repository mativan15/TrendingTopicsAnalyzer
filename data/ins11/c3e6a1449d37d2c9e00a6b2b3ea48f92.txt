How can fraudsters trap you? Beware of Cybercrimes through UPI transactions
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 09:50:52

UPI or Unified Payments Interface has earned gigantic popularity for its user-friendliness interface. It is a single platform that brings a myriad of banking services and features under one universal roof. One needs a UPI ID and generates a PIN to send and receive money. This has proved to be a boon for its efficacy and in providing a hassle-free experience to the end consumers.
But as the famous adage runs 'Every coin has two sides' so has been the case with every development taking place in the internet age.
