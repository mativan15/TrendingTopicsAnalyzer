Texas shooting: Keith Olbermann accuses conservatives of being in the 'School Children Killing business'
foxnews.com
news
2022-05-27 17:23:03

On Wednesday, former MSNBC host Keith Olbermann went low in a Twitter feud with radio host and former NRA spokesperson Dana Loesch about guns and background checks.The vocal liberal commentator accused Loesch and other conservatives of being in the "School Children Killing business" after Loesch criticized the liberal argument that if "Universal Background Checks" had been employed, the Uvalde shooter wouldn't have been able to purchase his murder weapon.Two days after the shooting, Loesch provided her analysis of the major causes behind the Uvalde, Texas school shooting that took the lives of 19 children and two teachers.
