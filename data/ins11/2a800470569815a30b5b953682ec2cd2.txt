Published: 13:07 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 13:10 EDT, 20 October 2022 Stephen A. Smith has been tapped to host an 'alternative broadcast' for some NBA games this season by ESPN, with the first edition of the show scheduled for October 26.The show will be called 'NBA in Stephen A's World,' with four episodes scheduled for the analyst to break down games to fans in a 'very relaxed format.'The inception of the broadcasts comes after the success of Eli and Peyton Manning's 'ManningCast' for Monday Night Football, where the pair have colloquially analyzed games.
