New York, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global gallium market is likely to grow from US$ 1.9 Bn in 2022 to US$ 17 Bn by the end of 2032. Based on product type, opto-semiconductors accounted for 35.1% share of the market revenue in 2021. Gallium is a soft silvery metal and is liquid at room temperature. During bauxite processing, caustic liquor is generated, and gallium is extracted from this as a by-product in impure form. The impure form is further refined to get pure gallium known as 6N Gallium.
