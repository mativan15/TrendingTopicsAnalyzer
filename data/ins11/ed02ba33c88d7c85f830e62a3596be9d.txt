Ukraine Gets Turkish Military Drone Gift After Lithuanian Fundraising Effort
cnet.com
news
2022-06-02 22:18:00

This story is part of War in Ukraine, CNET's coverage of events there and of the wider effects on the world. 


Three days after a Lithuanian crowdfunding effort raised $5.4 million to buy Ukraine a Bayraktar TB2 drone, the Turkish company that makes the unmanned military aircraft said Thursday it's donating one for free instead.The drone maker, Baykar Tech, requested in a tweet that the Lithuanian effort's funds raised be used instead for humanitarian work. But at least some of the money will still go toward the military effort.
