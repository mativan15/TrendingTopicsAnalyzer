Miami, get ready to get down.
The dance-y, annual III Points Festival is back at the Mana Wynwood Convention Center from Oct. 21-22 with some of the biggest names in EDM, reggaeton, electropop, dance-punk and electronic rock.
Just a few of the biggest artists on the bill include crowd favorite LCD Soundsystem, rising star Rosalia, electro-house icon Porter Robinson, genre mixer Flume and post-dubstep pop singer James Blake.
