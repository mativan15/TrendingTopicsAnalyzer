Future Tech: Asus Zenbook 17 Fold OLED Laptop Is All Screen
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-15 14:30:01

While tablets fulfill the role of all-screen computing devices, laptops continue to cling to their physical keyboards, in some shape or form. The Asus Zenbook 17 Fold OLED, however, asks the question, What if your laptop was simply a big folding display? Personal computers are interested in heading in this direction, but few have dared to jump directly to it.The Zenbook 17 Fold OLED can be bent with the display divided and a physical keyboard added, or it can be made to look like a 17-inch monitor with the keyboard in front of it.
