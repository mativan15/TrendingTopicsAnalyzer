 A Toronto man is facing charges after London police seized cash and illegal drugs near downtown over the weekend.
On Saturday, police executed a search warrant at a residence and a vehicle on Woodward Avenue.According to police, officers seized 74 grams of cocaine, a digital scale, two cell phones, and $3,805 in Canadian currency.
