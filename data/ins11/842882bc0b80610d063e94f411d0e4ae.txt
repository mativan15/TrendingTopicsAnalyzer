Space defence tech is future, says Modi
indiatimes.com
science
2022-10-19 19:45:00

IANSHINDIPrime Minister Narendra Modi at defence expo-2022 in Gandhinagar on Wednesday.Announcing India's intent to develop a range of military applications for space warfare, Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched a new initiative that would enable the industry to offer solutions to the armed forces for future offensive and defensive requirements. He also laid the foundation stone for a new airbase at Deesa in Gujarat, close to the border from Pakistan and said the country is ready to respond to any misadventure on the Western border.
