Mumbai. A builder has filed a complaint after two people barged into his room in a hospital in Bandra, West, and threatened to kill him if he failed to pay them extortion money. He is undergoing treatment in that hospital.The suspect has been identified as Ganesh Shinde, who was earlier arrested by the police in a firing case at Sion in 2011.
