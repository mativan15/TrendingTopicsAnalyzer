The Bermuda Triangle Cruise will cost around 1,450 pounds for a cabin on the ship. (Representational)New York: In a bizarre offer, a cruise bound for the mysterious Bermuda Triangle has promised passengers that they will receive a full refund if the ship disappears, UK media reported.The infamous Bermuda Triangle, also known as the Devil's Triangle, has remained a mystery as dozens of ships and flights have mysteriously disappeared in this region, stated the media.The reason behind the disappearances has remained unexplained and has been attributed to the outcome of weather and human error.
