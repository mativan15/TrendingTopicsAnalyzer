Sensex jumps 600 points amid gains in IT stocks; Nifty above 16,750
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 05:15:00

Benchmark indices on Friday opened on a strong footing after weak US employment data raised the possibility of the US central bank turning less aggressive on its policy tightening stance in near future. Data released overnight show private employment in the US increased by 128,000 positions last month, less than economist expectations and below April's tally. Investors globally were keenly awaiting the US Labour Department's comprehensive jobs report, due later in the day for confirmation of a slowdown in the employment market.
