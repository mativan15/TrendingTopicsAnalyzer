Roche Pharma has set up a Global Analytics and Technology Centre of Excellence (GATE) in Hyderabad, its second such in the country. The facility will work with the global affiliates of Roche to advance knowledge and understanding of healthcare space, thereby helping improve customer engagement, patient experience and business outcomes, the company said in an announcement after Roche Pharma India MD and CEO V.Simpson Emmanuel met Industries and IT Minister K.T.Rama Rao.
With the expansion, GATE will have a total head count of 100 by 2022 and pursue expansion plans, the company said.
