NEWARK, Del, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global pyrolytic boron nitride market stands at US$ 201.8 Mn as of now and is expected to reach US$ 333.0 Mn by the year 2032 at a CAGR of 5.2% between 2022 and 2032. Pyrolytic boron nitride is being increasingly used to produce plates, crucibles, boats, and likewise due to its specific properties like thermal stability and exceptional mechanical strength. These factors are expected to take the pyrolytic boron nitride market by storm in the forecast period.
