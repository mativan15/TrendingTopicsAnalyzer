The Tennessee Titans (3-2) are well rested and ready to host the Indianapolis Colts (3-2-1) in a crucial AFC South showdown between the division's top two teams.

Sunday's looming matchup has massive implications on the line for both AFC South squads. The winner will end the weekend in sole possession of first place with no more head-to-head matchups on the schedule between the two teams.

According to Tipico Sportsbook, the Titans are opening as 2.5-point favorites, with the over/under set at 42.
