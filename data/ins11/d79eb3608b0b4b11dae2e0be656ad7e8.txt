Enabling Organizations to Meet and Exceed FDA and Recall Requirements under the Food Traceability and Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA) 
DALLAS, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- One Network Enterprises (ONE), the leading global provider of intelligent control towers and the Digital Supply Chain Network™, is pleased to announce the availability of a fully compliant FSMA 204 solution configuration. The FDA's proposed FSMA 204 rule establishes requirements for end-to-end food traceability across the trading partner ecosystem.
