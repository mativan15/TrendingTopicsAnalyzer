By Fan Feifei | China Daily | Updated: 2022-05-30 09:12 A woman exercises at home with online workout short videos in Beijing, on April 28, 2022. [Photo/VCG]  Home is where the heart is-don't mean to dispute that. Only, I'd like to add COVID outbreaks and related stay-at-home, work-from-home and workout-at-home routines have added a new twist-and shapes, if you will-to home sweet home, turbocharging sales of fitness products, services, content and tech. These days, a typical urban home in China, such as mine, is, well, home to workout equipment, yoga mats, yoga balls and dance-related products.
