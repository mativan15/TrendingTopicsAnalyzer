Pakistan: World Bank estimates floods caused $40B in damages

Pakistan's government says the World Bank estimates that this summer's record-breaking floods have caused $40 billion in damages in this impoverished countryISLAMABAD -- Pakistan said Wednesday that the World Bank estimates this summer's record-breaking floods have caused $40 billion in damages in this impoverished South Asian nation. The figure is $10 billion more than an earlier estimate by the Pakistani government. Cash-strapped Pakistan was already facing a serious financial crisis before the heavy monsoon rains hit in mid-June.
