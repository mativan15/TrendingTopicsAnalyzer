A North-South rail superhighway may make Iowa "Roll-Over" CountryBNSF
A $31 billion merger between Canadian Pacific and the Kansas Southern Railways is roiling Iowa's hotly contested U.S. Senate race, where just three percentage points separate Iowa Senate candidate Admiral Mike Franken from the long-time incumbent, Senator Chuck Grassley. Franken, who worked on railroad issues while a military officer, is concerned that the merger risks making his oft-characterized 'fly-over' state into a 'roll-over' state.
