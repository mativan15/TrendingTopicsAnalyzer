PUMA India Launch First Store in the Maldives – Retail & Leisure International

2022-06-01 11:50:18

Puma's first store in Republic of Maldives is a two-storey outlet located in Majeedhee Magu in Male. The store is the first step in Puma India's plan to expand into the territory.  'Maldives is coming up strongly as a travel destination, especially post-Covid,' said Puma India's executive director of sales and operations Atul Bajaj. 'The number of resorts has swelled up over the years and the tourist-friendly economy has prompted good demand and consumption of global brands. We are excited to expand our presence in this country.
