BEVERLY HILLS, Calif., May 31, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Endeavor Group Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: EDR), a global sports and entertainment company, today announced that President Mark Shapiro will participate in a virtual fireside chat at the Credit Suisse 24th Annual Communications Conference Tuesday, June 14, 2022 at 4 p.m. ET.

A link to the live session, as well as a replay available for 30 days, will be accessible from the "News / Events" section of Endeavor's investor relations website at investor.
