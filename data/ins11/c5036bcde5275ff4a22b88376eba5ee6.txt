 Noting that the number of unicorns in India has reached the 100-mark, Prime Minister Narendra Modi on May 29 said that even during the COVID pandemic, India's startups continued to create wealth and value.In his monthly 'Mann ki Baat' radio broadcast, he said that on the fifth of this month the number of unicorns in India reached the 100-mark."One unicorn means a startup of at least ₹7,500 crore turnover. The total valuation of these unicorns is $330 billion, that is over ₹25 lakh crore.
