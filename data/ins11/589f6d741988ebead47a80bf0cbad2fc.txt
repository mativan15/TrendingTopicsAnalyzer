 Extending their rally to a third straight day, benchmark indices Sensex and Nifty jumped nearly 2 per cent on Monday on heavy buying in Reliance Industries and Infosys amid a firm trend in global equities. The 30-share BSE Sensex rallied 1,041.08 points or 1.90% to settle at 55,925.74. During the day, it gained 1,197.99 points or 2.18% to 56,082.65. The broader NSE Nifty jumped 308.95 points or 1.89% to settle at 16,661.
