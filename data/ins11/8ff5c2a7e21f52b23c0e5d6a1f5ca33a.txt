ANIED leaving with Delhi health minister Satyendar Jain after Rouse Avenue Court sent him in custody in connection with the money laundering case. (ANI file photo)New Delhi: Delhi minister Satyendar Jain on Monday withdrew from the Supreme Court the plea challenging the Delhi High Court verdict dismissing his petition against the transfer of the money laundering case against him from one trial court to another court. On October 11, a bench of Justices M R Shah and Krishna Murari had issued notice to the ED on Jain's plea and posted the case for hearing on October 31.
