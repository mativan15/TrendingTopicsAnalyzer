Gartner Surveys Show Changing CEO and Board Concerns are Driving a Different CIO Agenda for 2023
pcmag.com
news
2022-10-20 19:00:00

One of the things that I always find interesting from Gartner Symposium is the firms' annual surveys of CEOs and CIOs, in which they list each group's priorities for the upcoming year based on the response. Analysts then suggest what CIOs and other senior IT executives should do to meet the needs of their organizations.

Gartner Analyst Daniel Sanchez Reina introduced the firm's agenda for CIOs and Technology Executives for 2023 by noting all the upheaval in the world with economic and social uncertainty.
