 A record number of Winnipeggers aren't waiting until Wednesday to cast their ballot in this year's municipal election.
The city says 41,895 people took part in advance voting, which wrapped up Thursday, a 5.1 per cent increase from the 39,840 electors in 2018, which was the previous record.City of Winnipeg Senior Election Official Marc Lemoine says it's a combination of a number of options to vote early, plus a number of exciting races.
