Boy band WEi poses during a press conference for its fifth EP, 'Love Pt.2: Passion,' held in Seoul on Wednesday. (Oui Entertainment)When you're young, developing a crush can happen in an instant. But in many ways, falling in love is quite difficult. With that knowledge, boy band WEi has made a return to show off a more mature approach to love with its fifth EP, "Love Pt.2: Passion," which dropped Wednesday evening.'We're back with a whole new concept, and our band is excited to showcase this change to people,' the band's leader Dae-hyeon said during Wednesday's media press conference held in Mokdong, western Seoul.
