Photo: https://twitter.com/CleHotDogsCLEVELAND - The Guardians players may set the tone for the game. But the Hot Dogs set the tone for the breaks in between innings. The Hot Dog Derby has become one of the highlights of going to Guardians games for fans of any age. The Hot Dog Derby has been a part of the games at Progressive field since 2005. This year, the Guardians Hot Dogs have a chance to make Mascot History with induction into the Mascot Hall Of Fame in the Major League Best In-Game Routine or Skit category.
