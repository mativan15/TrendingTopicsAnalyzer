Through a tweet, Singh congratulated the party (BJP) nominees for the Rajya Sabha, "Wishes and Congratulations to BJP candidates for the Rajya Sabha polls."Former Congress Minister, R.P.N. Singh, who left the Congress to join the BJP ahead of the Uttar Pradesh elections, has not been adjusted in the list for the Rajya Sabha polls. Singh before leaving the Congress was Jharkhand in-charge and was considered a probable for the Upper House in the party, but left the Congress after differences over the selection of candidates in his hometurf Padrauna.
