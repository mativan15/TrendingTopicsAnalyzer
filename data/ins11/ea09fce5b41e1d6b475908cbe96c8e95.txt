TCS COO says action for moonlighting can ruin a career, company will show empathy
indiatimes.com
business
2022-10-17 14:00:00

MUMBAI: Action against moonlighting can ruin a person's career and hence, it is important to show empathy while dealing with the issue, a top official from TCS has said.Nothing prevents the country's biggest IT services exporter from taking action against employees for moonlighting when it has evidence because it is a part of the service agreement, but young kids will have to be dissuaded, the company's chief operating officer N Ganapathy Subramaniam told PTI."The consequences (of taking action) will be that the person's career will be ruined.
