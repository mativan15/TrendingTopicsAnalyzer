AMC CEO Adam Aron joins 'Squawk Box' to react to the historic turnout for Tom Cruise's 'Top Gun' over the Memorial Day weekend. Aron also breaks down how the theater chain plans to diversify its business and explains his own stake in the company. "Even though we were diversifying, we were never taking our eye off the ball of our core business,' Aron tells CNBC.
