Terms of UsePrivacy PolicyDo Not Sell My InfoHelp CenterAboutUSA TODAY Sports Media Group Krystian Williams is set to take an official visit to Rutgers football in June, part of a busy month for the three-star athlete from Virginia. Williams is a standout wide receiver and defensive back at The Collegiate School (Richmond, VA). Rutgers is recruiting Williams as a safety. The class of 2023 recruit is ranked the No. 13 player in Virginia and the No. 75 athlete in the nation according to 247Sports .
