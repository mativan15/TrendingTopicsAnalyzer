By Yang Wenjing | China Daily | Updated: 2022-05-31 07:34 Staff members chat behind Chinese and US flags displayed at the 2021 China International Fair for Trade in Services in Beijing, on Sept 4, 2021. [Photo/Agencies] The Western media have been playing up China's new security agreement with the Solomon Islands, the first of its kind signed by China with a Pacific island nation. In particular, the agreement has evoked a lot of criticism and hostility from the US, which immediately sent a delegation led by White House Indo-Pacific coordinator Kurt Campbell to the country and claimed there is "lack of transparency" in the agreement.
