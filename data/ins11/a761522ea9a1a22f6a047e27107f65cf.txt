mikroman6 The moment of reckoning VCs have been dreading might finally be here. After investors poured record-breaking amounts of venture money into startups this past year, a spate of disappointing performances from tech companies has investors anticipating a slump, with startup valuations beginning to drop. Data from CB Insights pointed to an expected 19% decline in global-venture funding for the second quarter. Sectors that typically excite VCs could also see less funding. CB Insights said fintech funding would see a 28% drop, digital health would see a 25% drop, and retail tech would see a whopping 50% drop.
