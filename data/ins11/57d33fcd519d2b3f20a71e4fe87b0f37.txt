Xi Jinping Expands His Power, Elevating Loyalists, Forcing Out Moderates
nytimes.com
news
2022-10-22 06:06:52

The retirement from the Central Committee of senior leaders with a record of favoring market-oriented policies and a somewhat less confrontational stance toward the West has left Mr. Xi in complete control of the national agenda.

'By pushing moderates and potential rivals out of the leadership, Xi conveys his overall priorities for sociopolitical stability, national security and policy continuity now and in the years to come,' said Cheng Li, a specialist in Chinese politics at the Brookings Institution.
