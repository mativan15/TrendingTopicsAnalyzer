More Video Agatha Cantrill, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- Germany plans to permit adults to purchase and possess as much as 20 grams (0.71 ounces) of cannabis for personal use as part of its plan to legalize weed, according to government proposals quoted in local media.The ruling coalition's plans include a limit of 15% for the amount of THC -- the chemical in weed that makes you high -- allowed in cannabis products, or 10% for 18-21-year-olds, the Redaktionsnetzwerk Deutschland media group reported Wednesday, citing a government document.
