This Toronto ice cream truck driver is fulfilling his father's wish
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-01 20:02:00

Having a father who drove an ice cream truck around Toronto was the ultimate envy of other kids. 'It was amazing,' Michael Mavrogiannis told CTV News Toronto. 'All my friends envied me. They would hang around and say, ‘When's your father gonna come get us ice cream?'' His father's circa 1977 truck, animated by illustrations of vanilla soft serve swirls and banana split boats, still makes its rounds in the city. But now, it's Mavrogiannis behind the wheel. 'That was his dream – to leave the truck for me when he passed away.
