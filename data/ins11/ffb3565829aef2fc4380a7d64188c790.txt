Ben Shephard welcomed Laura, Tare, Sophie and Ian during a brand new episode of Tipping Point. The ITV quiz show returned to its previous format of four contestants but unlucky player Ian was left apologising to host Ben when he made a blunder in the second round of the popular quiz show. Laura, Sophie and Ian got off to a great start but unfortunately, Tare struggled with the Tipping Point machine.He was eliminated at the end of the first round after failing to add any money to his jackpot.Heading into the second round, Laura had £700, Sophie £350 and Ian £300.
