Angus youth charged with arson after series of fires
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-05-31 15:55:00

A young individual faces serious charges in connection with a series of fires in Angus Monday afternoon. Police say officers responded to a call shortly before 4 p.m. about a fire inside a business on Roth Street. Less than 10 minutes later, officers got a call about another fire inside a McDonald's on Mill Street. And a short time later, officers received a third fire call, this time in a portable toilet on Greenwood Drive. Angus suspicious package investigation a 'misunderstanding:' OPP They say no one was injured in any of the fires.
