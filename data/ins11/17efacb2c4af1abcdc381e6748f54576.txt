Kerala: 8 students hospitalised after suspected food poisoning in govt school
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 12:45:00

AgenciesState Health Minister Veena George has asked the Food Safety Commissioner to look into both the incidents and file a report.Eight students of a government upper primary school in Alappuzha district were admitted to a hospital nearby on Saturday after they complained of uneasiness due to a suspected case of food poisoning. Police said a few students went to the government hospital on Friday evening itself while others reached today. "They are all under treatment. The doctors said their condition is stable," police told PTI.
