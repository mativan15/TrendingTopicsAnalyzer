Centre's Orders To Block Accounts Against Supreme Court Guidelines: Twitter
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-17 20:45:00

Twitter challenged the confidentiality clause invoked by the centreBengaluru: The Union government issued blocking orders in respect of tweets, content and accounts without following the relevant Supreme Court guideline, social media giant Twitter argued on Monday in the High Court of Karnataka and said its rights were also affected and challenged the confidentiality clause invoked by the Centre.The High Court rejected an impleading application filed on behalf of senior advocate Sanjay Hegde whose Twitter account was blocked.
