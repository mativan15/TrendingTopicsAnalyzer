NSW government takes first step to returning Me-Mel/Goat Island to Indigenous community
abc.net.au
news
2022-05-29 04:18:41

An iconic island in Sydney Harbour will be returned to the Indigenous community, with the NSW government allocating $43 million towards its restoration.The island is one of 13 within the Sydney Harbour National ParkThe $43 million will pay for projects such as wharf upgrades and asbestos removalMetropolitan Local Aboriginal Land Council deputy chair says the return is long overdueMe-Mel, also known as Goat Island, is one of 13 islands within the Sydney Harbour National Park and was once inhabited by Bennelong and Barangaroo.
