Ukraine war backlash: Pro-Russian media savages Putin forcing troops to fight unprepared
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-26 08:18:27

The atrocities of the war in Ukraine are constantly buried by Russian state media, but the fallout has begun as Putin's weaknesses are becoming evident even to his close supporters. Pro-Russian milbloggers (military sources documenting the operations) are using their Telegram channels to criticise the forceful mobilisation of troops against Ukraine.It comes as the morale of Putin's army has eroded, with multiple reports of surrendering soldiers coming to light.The milbloggers criticized the Kremlin for appalling treatment of servicemen, contradicting Russian information campaigns about progress of the Russian special military operation.
