Oath Keeper wanted to stop Biden win ‘one way or the other'
fltimes.com
news
2022-10-19 19:14:00

WASHINGTON — A military veteran who stormed the U.S. Capitol with the Oath Keepers testified that members of the far-right militia group were set on stopping the certification of the 2020 election on Jan. 6, 2021, with violence, if necessary.'It seemed to me that a lot of us were prepared, I was prepared, to stop the certification process one way or the other,' said Jason Dolan, who participated in Oath Keeper chats leading up to the attack on the Capitol.Dolan testified for the prosecution in the seditious conspiracy trial of Oath Keepers' founder Stewart Rhodes and four others.
