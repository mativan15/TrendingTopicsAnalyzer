Lin-Manuel Miranda stood in a Coral Gables courtyard. The walls behind him read 'Censorship leaves us in the dark,' with a list of banned books printed in black paint.

'You may be wondering, why is Lin-Manuel Miranda in front of a bookstore in Florida on a Thursday?' he quipped, his tone shifting seconds later. 'Well, the reality is what happens in Florida affects the rest of the United States.'

On Thursday morning, Books & Books hosted a Latino Victory voting rally featuring Miranda, the songwriter, actor, producer and director best known for the Broadway musicals 'Hamilton' and 'In the Heights.
