The Role of the Private Sector in Advancing Self-Care
who.int
news
2022-10-20 15:30:00

By Self-Care Trailblazer GroupJoin us for a captivating panel discussion on the important role of the private sector in developing, manufacturing, and delivering self-care products. While most of the attention, focus, and policy efforts have been on public health systems, the private sector is often left out of discussions on improving access to self-care approaches. This panel aims to understand the different perspectives of private sector intervention – manufacturer, implementer, provider, and government partnerships – in advancing self-care on a grand scale.
