KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- KONE, a global leader in the elevator and escalator industry, today announces the hire of veteran technology leader Teoh Tze Ping as Managing Director to scale the business in Malaysia. KONE supports the nation's smart city goals through the use of technology in buildings to enhance the quality of life and create a more sustainable living environment.

"KONE is the leader in smart and sustainable building flow, with the most energy efficient elevators in the industry.
