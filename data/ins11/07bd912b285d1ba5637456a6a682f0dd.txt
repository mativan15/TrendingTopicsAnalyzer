Lakers will wait a few weeks before looking at trade market
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-19 21:07:10

The past several months have been filled with seemingly endless trade rumors for the Los Angeles Lakers. Most of them have centered around Russell Westbrook, who has been made into the scapegoat for all that has gone wrong with them over the past calendar year.
There have been a few specific rumors that seemed to have legs, but thus far, nothing has materialized.
No matter how much Lakers fans may want Westbrook off the team, it looks like any potential trade won't happen for a while.
Via Lakers Daily:
'The Los Angeles Lakers reportedly are expected to wait until after Thanksgiving in the 2022-23 NBA season to gauge the trade market.
