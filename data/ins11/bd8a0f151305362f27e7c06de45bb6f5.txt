Image source, ReutersImage caption, Liz Truss announced her resignation in a statement outside 10 Downing StreetScotland's first minister has said a UK general election is a "democratic imperative" following the resignation of Liz Truss as prime minister.Ms Truss resigned after just 45 days in the job as her government became engulfed in political turmoil.Nicola Sturgeon said the situation was an "utter shambles", with ordinary people paying the price.Ms Truss will remain in post until a successor formally takes over as Conservative Party leader.
