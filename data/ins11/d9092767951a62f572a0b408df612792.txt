LONDON, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to The Business Research Company's research report on the digestive health supplements market, the rising prevalence of digestive diseases is expected to propel the growth of the digestive health supplement market going forward. A digestive disease is any health problem that affects the digestive tract. These problems can range from mild to severe. Digestive health supplement like probiotics, prebiotics, and enzymes, helps in digestive problems like diarrhea, constipation, inflammatory bowel disease, irritable bowel disease, urinary tract infection, and other gastrointestinal-related digestive problems.
