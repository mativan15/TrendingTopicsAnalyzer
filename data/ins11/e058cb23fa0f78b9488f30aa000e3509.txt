October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time PORTLAND, Ore.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Vacasa (Nasdaq: VCSA), North America's leading vacation rental management platform, today announced that Rachel Gonzalez, former Starbucks Corporation (Nasdaq: SBUX) General Counsel, has been engaged by the Company to serve as an observer on its Board of Directors. The Company intends to seek stockholder approval to expand the size of its board and to elect Gonzalez as an independent director at the 2023 annual meeting of stockholders.
