BOSTON, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Plymouth Industrial REIT, Inc. (NYSE: PLYM) today announced that the Company's board of directors has declared a regular quarterly cash dividend of $0.46875 per share, or an annualized dividend of $1.875 per share, for the Company's 7.50% Series A Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock (PLYM-PrA) for the second quarter of 2022. The dividend is payable on June 30, 2022, to stockholders of record on June 15, 2022.
