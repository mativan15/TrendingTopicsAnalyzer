The Four Seasons Hotel At The Surf Club is widely considered one of the most luxurious properties in the world, and the arrival of Susanne Kaufmann's new spa treatments this month further solidifies that status. As a fifth-generation Austrian hotelier—her family owns the wellness retreat Hotel Post Bezau—and holistic beauty entrepreneur, Kaufmann's keenly understood the importance of incorporating natural, efficacious ingredients in hotel spa treatments long before it became the norm.
