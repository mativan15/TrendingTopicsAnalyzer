MANCHESTER, England, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SmartKem, Inc. (OTCQB: SMTK), a company seeking to reshape the world of electronics with a revolutionary semiconductor platform that enables a new generation of displays, sensors and logic, announced today its CEO, Ian Jenks, will be presenting at the LD Micro Invitational XII investor conference on Tuesday, June 7th at 4:30pm PDT. The event will be held in-person at the Four Seasons Westlake Village in Los Angeles and available to live stream virtually.
