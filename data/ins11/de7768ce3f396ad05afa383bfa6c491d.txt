YouTuber Earning Over Rs 300 Crore A Year Feels Earning 'Too Much Money' Is Unfair & Like Cheating
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 06:21:16

The last decade or so has seen how becoming a YouTuber is no more just a hobby for people, and has gone on to become no less than a full-fledged profession for many. Youtube stars are nowadays earning crazy money with the amounts going into millions or even billions of dollars! After all, who doesn't love earning money, right? But one immensely popular YouTuber who earns a whopping $38 million (nearly Rs 315 crore) a year has reportedly gone on to say that he feels like he's cheating the system by bringing in so much money.
