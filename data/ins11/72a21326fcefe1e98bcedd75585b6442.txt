Today in Entertainment History: 'My Ding-A-Ling" hit #1

On Oct. 21, 1958, Buddy Holly held what ended up being his last recording session. 'It Doesn't Matter Anymore,' which he recorded in that session in one take, became a hit after he died.In 1961, Bob Dylan recorded his first album for Columbia Records. The self-titled album was recorded in one day and cost $400.In 1971, Mick and Bianca Jagger's daughter, Jade, was born. In 1972, after 17 years of recording rock classics, Chuck Berry finally got a No. 1 hit with 'My Ding-A-Ling.'In 1992, Elton John filed a $35 million lawsuit in which he claimed a reporter for the TV show 'Hard Copy' falsely stated John moved to Atlanta to be near an AIDS treatment center.
