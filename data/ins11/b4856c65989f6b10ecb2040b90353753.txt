17 October 2022 by Patrick Frater 
Variety - Film News While the Busan International Film Festival was busily hailing a return to normality the slump at South Korea's commercial box office deepened dramatically over the most recent weekend.Cinemagoing nationwide was worth only 4.20 million between Friday and Sunday, the lowest weekend total since April and a time before Korean cinemas shed their Covid restrictions.The weekend's top film 'Life Is Beautiful' managed the lowest first place score of any film this year, with a weekend haul of just 777,000, according to data from Kobiz, the tracking service operated by the Korean Film Council (Kofic).
