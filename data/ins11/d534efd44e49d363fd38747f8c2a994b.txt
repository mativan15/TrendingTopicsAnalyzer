More Video Rita Nazareth, Bloomberg News Stocks rallied after the S&P 500 breached a technical level that has triggered rallies in the past, with risk appetite improving as more of U.K. Prime Minister Liz Truss's unfunded tax cuts were reversed. About 99 per cent of the U.S. equity benchmark's companies rose Monday, with the measure bouncing from a close below its 200-week moving average. The tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 outperformed.
