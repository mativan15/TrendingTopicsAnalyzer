Korea grapples with U.S. push against 'made in China'
naver.com
news
2022-10-16 18:00:00

Flags of Korea, the U.S., Taiwan and China are shown on a printed circuit board. High-tech products like semiconductors are a focus for the United States as it seeks to protect its economy from China by shifting the manufacturing of these products to the home market. Companies in Korea are finding that their dependence on China for high-tech manufacturing and materials has become a liability. Korea Inc. faces an uphill battle as a push in the United States for economic security is taking its toll on companies dependent on China for manufacturing or for the supply of materials and components.
