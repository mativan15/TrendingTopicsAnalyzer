TORONTO, June 1, 2022 /CNW/ - Manulife today announced the launch of its global Impact Agenda, which articulates the company's commitment to building a better business to better the world, inspired by its mission: Decisions made easier. Lives made better.

Manulife (CNW Group/Manulife Financial Corporation)

Manulife's Impact Agenda is centred around three interconnected pillars:

Empowering sustained health and well-being to support the journey towards a better life

Driving inclusive economic opportunities to create a more even playing field for all

Accelerating a sustainable future to preserve the planet we all share

"I'm pleased to announce the launch of our Impact Agenda, as we deepen our focus on sustainability.
