Medical Tourism: Global TrendsMedical tourism is a $78 billion industry globally. Patients travel from developed countries to lower-priced countries for certain dental and cosmetic treatments. For example, American and European patients are travelling to countries like Turkey, the UAE, and Thailand to get cosmetic treatments.
