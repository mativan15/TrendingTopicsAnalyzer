According to Better Marketing, content writing is a quick and lucrative way to make money in the digital marketing industry. Content writing involves planning and putting thoughts into words for online channels, and Africans can benefit. This idea is for Africans with analytical, keyword research, writing, and search engine optimization (SEO) skills. Skilled Africans can make money writing content like blog posts, video scripts, social media posts, articles, etc. So whether you're in Nigeria, Cameroon, South Africa, Ghana, or Kenya, starting a career in content writing is lucrative.
