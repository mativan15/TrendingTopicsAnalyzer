​An energy saving toolkit will help you reduce energy consumption – and your energy bills, ahead of the heating season while contributing to the EU's independence from Russian fossil fuels in solidarity with Ukraine

​ On 19 May, the European Commission, the Covenant of Mayors – Europe and the European Committee of the Regions launched The Cities Energy Saving Sprint , a joint initiative for cities to immediately reduce energy consumption and be better prepared for next winter by using a simple energy saving toolkit.
