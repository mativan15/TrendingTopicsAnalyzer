BOSTON, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Boston Beer Company, Inc. (NYSE: SAM), today reported financial results for the third quarter 2022 (13 weeks) and year to date 2022 (39 weeks) ended September 24, 2022.
Third Quarter 2022 Summary:

Depletions decreased 6% and shipments increased 1.4% compared to the third quarter of 2021
Net revenue of $596.5 million increased 6.2% compared to net revenue realized in the third quarter of 2021
Gross margin of 43.2%
Net income of $27.3 million, which includes a non-cash impairment charge of $27.
