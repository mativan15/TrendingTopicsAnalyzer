Jessica Jeffers (University of Chicago - Booth School of Business) & Anne M. Tucker (Georgia State University College of Law) have posted Shadow Contracts (University of Chicago Business Law Review, Vol. 1, No. 1, Spring 2022) on SSRN.  Here is the abstract: This project explores side letters in private market funds. Side letters, separate agreements between a fund and an investor, act as an invisible amendment to the main contract. This article introduces a new use case for side letters: impact investments, where funds target social, as well as financial returns.
