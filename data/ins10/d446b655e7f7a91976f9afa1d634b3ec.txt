New Bicknell-owned firm CopperPoint plans to roll up plumbing, electrical and HVAC companies
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 18:50:00

A new Kansas City-based investment company called CopperPoint plans to start acquiring plumbing, heating, air-conditioning and electrical companies throughout the United States.

CopperPoint is co-owned and backed with capital from 1248 Holdings, the Bicknell family office. Its leadership includes CEO Bobby Hamilton, who is Bob Hamilton 's son; CFO Wesley Brown, who previously was director of Dallas-based Cresta Fund Management; founding partner Christian Scharosch, who is managing director of 1248 Holdings; and founding partners Marty and Cheryl Bicknell.
