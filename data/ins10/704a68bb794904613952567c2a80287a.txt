Arlington Heights pandemic-born ‘Alfresco' dining spot opens for season in village's downtown: ‘It's ... a block party everyday'
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-05-30 19:28:10

Arlington Alfresco, the popular outdoor dining venue in downtown Arlington Heights, has opened for its third season and again to the welcoming embrace of crowds and restaurateurs.The streets of downtown Arlington Heights have been transformed and filled with diners enjoying food and drinks outdoors. The main intersection of the venue is Harmony Park at the corner of Campbell Street and Vail Avenue, which will feature live music every Thursday and Friday starting June 16, according to village information about the venue.
