Soil Power Propels Hamden Public Works Nominee
patch.com
news
2022-10-18 04:06:55

(Image via Google Maps)By Nora Grace-Flood, New Haven IndependentHAMDEN, CT — A veteran Hamden public works employee is slated to become the town department's next director — with plans to bring sustainability, including town-made potting soil, to the top of his agenda as well as to community members' gardens. That Hamden public works director to-be is Joe Colello, who currently runs the town's transfer station. Mayor Lauren Garrett tapped him for the top public works job in early October. The town's Legislative Council was slated to vote Monday evening on his appointment.
