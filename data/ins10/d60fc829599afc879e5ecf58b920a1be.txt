Acute stress may make it hard to fight against COVID-19
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 17:15:00

Getty ImagesAcute Stress is likely to cause impairment of the human body's ability to battle against infections. It may affect the immune system's fight against COVID-19. According to a recent study on mice, the strength of the immune system is directly related to acute stress. The research published in the journal Nature is the first-ever report to successfully display how certain regions of the human brain control the body's immune response when under acute stress and affected by influenza or COVID-19.
