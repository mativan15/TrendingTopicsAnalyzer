THE self-cleaning option on your oven might seem like a neat feature to have at first, but a repairs expert warns that you might want to think twice before using it.
Scott the Fix-It Guy, a YouTuber who shares tips on how to repair and maintain your appliances, says using the self-cleaning feature is akin to swimming with sharks.
A repairs expert is warning people to not use the self-cleaning features on their ovens as it can cause more harm than goodCredit: Getty
'There's no guarantee that you'll be bitten by a shark, but it's highly likely,' he said.
