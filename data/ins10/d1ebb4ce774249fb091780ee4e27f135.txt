VXI Global Solutions 'Reuniting with Bain Capital will accelerate our obsessive pursuit of creating legendary and transformational experiences for our employees, clients, and customers as we enter the next chapter of CX and CX Advisory services,' said David Zhou, Co-Founder and Co-CEO of VXI. LOS ANGELES (PRWEB) June 03, 2022 VXI Global Solutions ('VXI'), a leading provider of Business Process Outsourcing ('BPO') services to businesses around the world, announced last May 24, 2022, its acquisition by Bain Capital Private Equity ('Bain Capital') from The Carlyle Group.
