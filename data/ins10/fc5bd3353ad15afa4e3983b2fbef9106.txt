We are very excited to partner with Magnacharge and leverage their distribution expertise and established service network to meet the increasing aftermarket demands for high-quality, high-performance Li-ion batteries,' said Bill Scheich, Director, Business Development at Inventus Power. WOODRIDGE, Ill. (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Inventus Power, a global leader in the design and manufacture of advanced battery systems for commercial, industrial, medical and military markets, today announced it has entered into a new agreement with Magnacharge Battery Corporation, a leading wholesale and battery distribution company serving Canada, the United States and Central America.
