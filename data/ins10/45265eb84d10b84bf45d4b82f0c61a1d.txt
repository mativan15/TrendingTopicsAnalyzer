AHMEDABAD: The city police investigating the abduction and attempted murder of a six-year-old boy in Thaltej said on Friday that the accused, Rahul Patel, 39, the victim's neighbour, committed the crime so that he could provide for his triplets and pay off a loan of around Rs 25 lakh with the ransom money.

A city police officer said that Patel and his wife were childless for 12 years and decided to opt for IVF procedure. "Patel's wife delivered triplets last year through IVF which cost the couple Rs 10 lakh.
