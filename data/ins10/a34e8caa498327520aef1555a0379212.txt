Evening Edition: Kanye West, Or Ye, To Acquire Conservative Social Media Platform
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-17 19:01:11

Kanye West, or Ye as he wants to be known as now, has reached a deal to buy conservative social media platform ‘Parler' after being banned from Instagram and Twitter. ‘Parler' is a right-wing-friendly platform that was started after many conservatives feel their opinions face unfair treatment by Twitter and other apps. FOX's Jeff Monosso speaks speaks with Susan Li, FOX Business Network correspondent, about what we know so far about the deal and how this could possibly cross paths with Elon Musk's bid to buy Twitter.
