Quality Checklist I will run Zebra Technologies through my quality checklist to find out if it is a high-quality company worth investing in. My checklist will run through the following criteria: Are they a market leader in a growing market? Is Free Cash Flow per share growing? What do the margins look like? Do they have a wide economic moat and is it widening? Is the company led by an owner-operator? How is the capital allocation strategy?
