Actor Akshay Kumar on Thursday launched the song 'Jai Shri Ram' from his upcoming film Ram Setu in Mumbai. Director Abhishek Sharma was also present at the song launch. Abhishek spoke about how he came up with the idea of making Ram Setu during the Covid 19-induced lockdown.
The director shared, 'During the pandemic in 2020, a thought came to me and I began to work on it. I called up Akshay Kumar and he asked me if I had written a screenplay. I wrote it and sent it to him. Akshay sir and Vikram sir (Vikram Sharma of Abundantia Entertainment) jammed on it.
