TOP Ships stock sinks premarket on $7.2M registered direct offering
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-03 12:42:47

TOP Ships (NASDAQ:TOPS) stock sank 23.2% premarket on Friday after the firm announced a ~$7.2M registered direct offering and a concurrent private placement. Under a securities purchase deal with an institutional investor, TOPS will sell 14.3M shares (or pre-funded warrants in lieu thereof). In the private placement, TOPS will issue warrants to purchase up to 14.3M shares. The combined effective purchase price for 1 share (or pre-funded warrant in lieu thereof) and 1 warrant to purchase 1 share will be $0.
