June 01, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, Justia announced the launch of Justia Connect Pro, an upgrade for members of the free Justia Connect (connect.justia.com) membership program. This next generation of Justia Connect equips lawyers with additional benefits and resources that help them expand their practices and achieve their professional goals.



Justia Connect Pro members receive enhancements to their professional profile in the Justia Lawyer Directory, including the removal of competitor ads and an integrated contact form.
