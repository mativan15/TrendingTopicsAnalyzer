Oat_Phawat Introduction Vancouver-based gold miner B2Gold Corp. (NYSE:BTG) released its third-quarter of 2022 gold production results on October 17, 2022. Note: I have been regularly covering B2Gold on Seeking Alpha since 2019. This article is an update of my preceding article, published on August 22, 2022. 1 - 3Q22 Gold Production Snapshot B2Gold posted a solid gold production of 227,016 Au ounces (including 12,113 Au ounces for its 30.8% Calibre Mine) in the third quarter of 2022. The company reaffirmed that it was on track to meet annual guidance for 2022 with a total of 990K-1,050K.
