Duxbury gets back to the basics in Patriot League win over Hingham
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-10-22 03:10:46

DUXBURY — A strong first half kept Hingham within striking distance Friday night, but an overpowering second-half surge from rival Duxbury proved to be insurmountable.The sixth-ranked Dragons scored 21 unanswered, running away with a 35-9 Patriot League victory at Duxbury High.Duxbury (6-0) built a 14-0 lead through one, then Hingham (5-2) responded with a promising second quarter to slice the deficit to 14-9 at halftime.Duxbury was relatively quiet early — by its standards, at least — but the Dragons turned it on when they had to and won convincingly.
