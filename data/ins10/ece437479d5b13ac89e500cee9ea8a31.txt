Indianapolis police arrest man for alleged murder on east side on Friday night
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-04 16:57:11

Indianapolis police detectives arrested at 32-year-old man on Saturday as a suspect in an overnight homicide the night before, the Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department said in a statement.

Officers responded to in the 600 block of N. Dearborn Street on the east side just before10:30 p.m. for report of a person shot. The adult man, located inside the house there, was pronounced dead at the scene.

Detectives later arrested David Ice for alleged murder. Preliminary information leads investigators to believe the shooting involved a dispute over money for narcotics, according to police.
