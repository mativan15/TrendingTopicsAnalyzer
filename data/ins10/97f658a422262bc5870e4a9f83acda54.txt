Nets season preview: A Kevin Durant-led title contender or an implosion waiting to happen?
nypost.com
news
2022-10-18 16:00:00

After Brooklyn looked to be on the brink of a breakup — and one of the greatest failures in NBA history — the Nets will run it back this season with Kevin Durant and Kyrie Irving.
Durant rescinded his offseason trade request, and Irving opted into the final year of his contract. Now finally getting a healthy Ben Simmons — or any Simmons at all, for that matter — on the court should make the Nets a legitimate title contender on paper.
But the Nets' failures in recent seasons are proof that titles are won on the court, not on parchment.
