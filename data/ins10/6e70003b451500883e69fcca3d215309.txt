Top crypto firms go on the hunt for acquisition targets as market crashes

Two top cryptocurrency companies, Ripple and FTX, told CNBC that they're on the lookout for acquisitions as the industry hopes to drive growth through buying other firms.It is a sign that some crypto firms feel that they're large enough and well capitalized to splash the cash on acquisitions.Brett Harrison, president of cryptocurrency exchange FTX U.S., said in an interview last week that the company is in "a very good spot in terms of our capital and cash" and will "look around the market for potential merger and acquisition opportunities.
