With the bear market going strong, it is a terrible time for companies to go public, which is why initial public offerings are down nearly 80% at this point in 2022 compared to the same time a year ago. Nevertheless, Intel Corp. (NASDAQ:INTC) is planning to go ahead with the spinoff of its self-driving subsidiary Mobileye, sending its stock down more than 3%.

Even though the Mobileye IPO will not raise much money in the current market compared to what it might have fetched in the quantitative easing bubble of late 2020 and early 2021, Intel is in desperate need of cash to fund the buildout of its capital-intensive semiconductor production business.
