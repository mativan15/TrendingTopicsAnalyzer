The two have shared screen space in numerous films including 'Razia Sultan', 'Sholay', 'Seeta Aur Geeta', 'Chacha Bhatija' and more.Bollywood veteran actress Hema Malini will turn 74 on October 16. The Padma Shri-winner has starred in numerous iconic films in her long career in Indian cinema such as 'Sholay', 'Kinara', 'Seeta Aur Geeta' and many more. Right before her birthday, Malini spoke about different roles she plays as a woman, politician and a mother, why she is no longer in films and sharing screen space with another veteran, Dharmendra.
