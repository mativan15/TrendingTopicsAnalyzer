courtneyk/E+ via Getty Images eXp World Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ:EXPI) is a cloud-based residential real estate brokerage platform. The attraction here is a low-cost technology-based solution that offers an alternative for independent agents and broker teams with a compelling commission draw compared to legacy brick-and-mortar companies. While eXp went through a boom during the pandemic, shares have been highly volatile over the past year against concerns regarding the housing market momentum and the overall economy.
