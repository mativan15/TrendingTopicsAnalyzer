Bridgerton star Ruby Barker has said that she feels 'so happy to be alive' after being discharged from hospital for mental health struggles.The actor is best known for her role as Marina Thompson in Netflix's romantic period drama series. She was a lead character in the show's first season and made a brief appearance in the recently released second season.Last week, Barker posted a six-minute video to Instagram telling fans that she'd been admitted to hospital to focus on her mental health recovery, having been 'really unwell for a really long time'.
