What Bayonetta 3's drama tells us about the market for video game voice work

Enlarge / If you want me to talk, you need to provide a much more valuable bauble... Voice actress Hellena Taylor caused a bit of a social media firestorm over the weekend, calling for players to boycott the upcoming Bayonetta 3 over what she called an "insulting" and "immoral" low payment offer to reprise her role as the title character. And while reports have disputed some of the specifics of Taylor's allegations, that he-said-she-said drama obscures just how little power video game voice actors have to demand a higher share of the revenues from bestselling titles.
