Startup launches 10-min liquor delivery service in Kolkata
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 06:00:00

The service was launched in the eastern metropolis after approval from the West Bengal State Excise Department, the statement said.A Hyderabad-based startup has launched its services in Kolkata of delivering liquor in 10 minutes, the firm said in a statement. Booozie, the flagship brand of Innovent Technologies Private Limited, claimed it is India's first 10-minute liquor delivery platform. Online liquor delivery is already offered by several companies but none have the 10-minute service so far, it said.
