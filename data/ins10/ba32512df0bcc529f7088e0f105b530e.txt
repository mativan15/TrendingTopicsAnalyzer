Wall Street ends down with strong jobs data keeping the pressure on for rate hikes
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 05:15:00

AgenciesShawn Snyder, head of investment strategy at Citi Personal Wealth Management, saw the solid report as a double-edged sword.Wall Street's three major stock indexes ended lower on Friday after a solid jobs report ate in to hopes for a pause in the Federal Reserve's aggressive policy-tightening which is needed to cool decades-high inflation. The technology-heavy Nasdaq led the declines, falling 2.5% as shares of market heavyweights Apple Inc and Tesla Inc were the biggest drags on the market.
