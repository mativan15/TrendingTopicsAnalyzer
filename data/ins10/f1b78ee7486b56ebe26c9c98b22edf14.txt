The Premier League returns for its second round of midweek fixtures this week as fans prepare to be treated to live coverage of every single game on Amazon's streaming serviceThe midweek Premier League games will be shown on Amazon PrimeThe Premier League is back for another special midweek round of fixtures as October's hectic schedule moves up another gear ahead of November's World Cup. And that also means the return of Amazon Prime's coverage, in which football fans will be treated with the luxury of having every single game televised on the streaming service this week, with 18 of the 20 top flight clubs in action over the space of three days.
