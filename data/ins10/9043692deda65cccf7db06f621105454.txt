REUTERS/Dado Ruvic/IllustrationLONDON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - ASOS (ASOS.L) shares fell sharply on Monday after the British online fashion retailer said it was in talks with lenders to change the terms of a 350 million pound ($394 million) borrowing facility to provide more flexibility in tough economic times.The stock was down 8.6% at 0723 GMT, extending losses so far this year to 80%.
