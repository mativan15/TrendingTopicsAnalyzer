An investor monitors a screen displaying stock information at the Abu Dhabi Securities Exchange June 25, 2014.Oct 18 (Reuters) - Most stock markets in the Gulf rose in early trade on Tuesday, tracking strength in Asian shares and oil prices, with the Abu Dhabi index outperforming the region led by a surge in International Holding Co (IHC) (IHC.AD).The Abu Dhabi index (.FTFADGI) jumped about 3%, its biggest intraday gain since May 2021, buoyed by a 7.8% surge in conglomerate International Holding Co.
