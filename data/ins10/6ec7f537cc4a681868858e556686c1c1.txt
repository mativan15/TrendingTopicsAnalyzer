By Reuters Published: 09:44 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 09:44 EDT, 19 October 2022 Oct 19 (Reuters) - Super Apps Holdings will go public in the United States through a merger with a special purpose acquisition company in a deal that values the Malaysian payments technology firm at $1.1 billion, the companies said on Wednesday. Before the deal with Technology & Telecommunication Acquisition Corporation closes, Super Apps will buy 60% of fintech firm MobilityOne's stake in OneShop Retail. Proceeds from the merger will be used to expand headcount, Super Apps said.
