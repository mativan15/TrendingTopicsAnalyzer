It has been over 75 days since media veteran Zulfiqar Ahmad Khan (Former COO of Balaji Telefilms) went missing in Kenya, and now actor and friend Karan Kundrra is putting all his energy in pulling the attention on the case, and finding answers. In fact, he has also joined the petition requesting Prime Minister Narendra Modi to intervene with the Government of Kenya and help find him. 'After Raj Nayak (Former COO of Viacom 18) sir, someone who has worked with him, called me and informed me that it is not a normal disappearance, and that something is fishy.
