Membership-Based Franchise Taps into Growth Potential of $123 Billion Pet Industry 
JUPITER, Fla., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Scenthound, the nation's first and only wellness-focused dog care concept, announces the signing of its 200th location, marking future entry into Arizona and the brand's 23rd state overall. The news comes following an extremely successful quarter for the brand, with a 120% increase year-over-year in membership when compared to Q3 of 2021 and a 264% overall revenue increase.
