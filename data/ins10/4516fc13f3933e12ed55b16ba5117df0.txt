20 October 2022 by Natalie Oganesyan 
The Wrap Thanks to its 'FBI' franchise, CBS has once again locked down a Tuesday night win in both the advertiser-coveted 18-49 demographic and total average viewership, according to Nielsen's live plus same-day figures. The network pulled back up in ratings after last week's slight dip, to 0.5 in the demo, and saw similar total average viewership with 6.2 million.Kicking off the night at 8 p.m., CBS' flagship 'FBI' drew in 0.53 in ratings and 7.1 million total average viewers — the highest of the night across all networks and primetime hours.
