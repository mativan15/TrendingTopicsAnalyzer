Charles and Camilla ‘Tampongate' will be depicted in ‘The Crown' season 5
nypost.com
news
2022-10-20 14:58:25

The juicy scandals of the royal family will surely be featured in the upcoming season of 'The Crown,' including one very tawdry moment.
The notorious phone call between the now-King Charles and his future Queen Consort-wife, Camilla Parker Bowles, involving one delicately placed tampon, will be presented in seasons five of the hit Netflix show after all.
Dominic West — who is taking over actor Josh O'Connor's role in 'The Crown' as Charles — spilled the tea that the incident will indeed be shown on camera.
