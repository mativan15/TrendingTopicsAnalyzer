June 02, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time WESTLAKE VILLAGE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PennyMac Mortgage Investment Trust (NYSE: PMT) announced today that its Board of Trustees declared a cash dividend of $0.47 per common share of beneficial interest for the second quarter of 2022. This dividend will be paid on July 28, 2022, to common shareholders of record as of July 14, 2022.

About PennyMac Mortgage Investment Trust

PennyMac Mortgage Investment Trust is a mortgage real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests primarily in residential mortgage loans and mortgage-related assets.
