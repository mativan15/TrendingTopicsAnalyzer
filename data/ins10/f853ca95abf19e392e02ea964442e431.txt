Alcoa Swings to 3Q Loss on Lower Prices, Higher Costs
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 20:48:00

By Jeffrey T. Lewis Alcoa Corp. swung to a third-quarter loss due to lower aluminum and alumina prices, higher energy and raw material costs and restructuring related charges. Alcoa stock is trading down 8% at $34.52 after hours. The stock closed the regular session down about 5% at $37.62. The Pittsburgh-based aluminum producer had a loss of $746 million, compared with a profit of $337 million in the same period a year earlier. The loss per share was $4.17, down from earnings per share of $1.76 in the year-earlier period.
