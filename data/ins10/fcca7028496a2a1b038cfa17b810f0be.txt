KUWAIT CITY, Kuwait - (NewMediaWire) - October 20, 2022 - Baker Tilly Kuwait has recently earned the well-deserved "Great Place To Work" Certification awarded by the US-based Great Place To Work(R) to companies and organizations that exert remarkable efforts to create a comfortable, positive and motivating work environment for employees.

Creating a healthy productive work environment undoubtedly requires effort by the business entity's management and staff members as they are the stakeholders and owners of a work environment.
