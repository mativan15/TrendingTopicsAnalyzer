Retailers report change in shopping habits since since start of pandemic
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-06-02 05:52:26

Retailers in Timaru's main street say people are still shopping, but they've changed the way the shop since Covid-19 hit. Mac Shaw menswear owner Grant Shaw said he had noticed an 'erractic' flow of customers but said business was 'great'. 'Impulse buying doesn't exist at the moment. 'Because of Omicron, they [shoppers] plan beforehand, hit the shops 1, 2, 3 bang and then go home 'People aren't roaming the streets for no reason,' Shaw said.
