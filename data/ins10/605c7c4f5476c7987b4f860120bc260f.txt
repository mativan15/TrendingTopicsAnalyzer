Injury crisis begins to ease for Liverpool as Klopp's options grow

2022-10-20 14:47:34

Seemingly back on track after two consecutive Premier League wins, Jurgen Klopp's side can move level on points with Manchester United in fifth with a victory at the City Ground.After such a difficult start and for all the talk of this perhaps being the end of a cycle at Liverpool, moving into touching distance of the top four would represent real progress, following up the morale-boosting win over Manchester City with something tangible.In a further boost for Klopp, the injury crisis to have derailed the opening few weeks of the season appears to be subsiding.
