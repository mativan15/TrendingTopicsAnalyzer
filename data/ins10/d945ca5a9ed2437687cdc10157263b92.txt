Warning: This story contains details and language some readers may find disturbingA member of Samwel Uko's family erupted in anger and pain Wednesday at the third day of the coroner's inquiry into Uko's death.Uko, a 20-year-old football player from Abbotsford, B.C., died at Wascana Lake on May 21, 2020, while in Regina visiting an aunt. His family said it was a suicide.Leading up to his death, Uko had sought medical help twice for mental health issues, and was forcibly removed from the Regina General Hospital.
