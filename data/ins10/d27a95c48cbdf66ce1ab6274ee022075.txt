It's crunch time. How quickly will you be able to get at your cash?
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

Concerns are mounting that investors' money could be trapped for months if funds that hold hard-to-sell assets move to halt withdrawals.A net £11.25 billion was pulled from funds in September, according to the data firm Morningstar, the biggest monthly total for more than ten years. In the past year equity funds have shed £25.7 billion.Share prices are falling because of the war in Ukraine, inflation is running at a 40-year high and interest rates are soaring. Central banks have hit pause on the massive bond-buying programmes, known as quantitative easing, which had pushed up asset prices and fuelled the bull market of the past decade.
