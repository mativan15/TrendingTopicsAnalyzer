Allegedly, the company is operating in violation of the state's Capture or Use of Biometric Identifier Act.A. Tarantola|10.20.22Edgar Su / reutersThe office of Texas State Attorney General Ken Paxton announced on Thursday that it has filed a lawsuit against Google over the company's alleged years-long practices to capture and use of biometric data from, "millions of Texans without properly obtaining their informed consent to do so." This is allegedly a violation of the state's Capture or Use of Biometric Identifier Act of 2009.
