October 18, 2022 08:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Logility, Inc., a leader in supply chain innovation powering the sustainable and resilient enterprise, today announced an addition to its growing client community. Workwear Outfitters—the world's leading supplier of innovative work apparel and footwear to workers who dedicate their lives to making our world work better--selected the Logility® Digital Supply Chain Platform to modernize its technology stack and transform its end-to-end supply chain.
