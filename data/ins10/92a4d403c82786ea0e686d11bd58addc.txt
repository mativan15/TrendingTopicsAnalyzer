Photo: Getty ImagesSheryl Sandberg announced that she is stepping down from her role as the chief operating officer at Meta, the parent company of Facebook. Sandberg, who has worked for the company for the last 14 years, said she plans to focus her time on philanthropic work. "When I took this job in 2008, I hoped I would be in this role for five years. Fourteen years later, it is time for me to write the next chapter of my life. I am not entirely sure what the future will bring – I have learned no one ever is.
