LeBron James in Tuesday's loss to the Warriors (Image: GETTY)LeBron James has been told that this season will only be about personal records, with the Los Angeles Lakers predicted to struggle once again after suffering an opening day loss. The Lakers were second best in all facets against the Golden State Warriors on Tuesday night, with Steph Curry and company dominating offensively.Coming off the back of a hugely disappointed year in which they only won 33 games, the Lakers know that they cannot go through the same type of season and expect the team to be together, with many questioning whether they should trade their assets and rebuild the team.
