X5 Retail Sales Rose in 3Q, Boosted by Pyaterochka Business -- Update
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 08:32:00

By Michael Susin X5 Retail Group NV said Monday that total retail sales for the third quarter increased, driven by a robust performance from its Pyaterochka retail chain and amid higher online sales. The Russian food retailer, which is listed in Moscow and London, said total net sales for the third quarter ended Sept. 30 were 647.39 billion rubles ($10.33 billion) from RUB541.95 billion in the same period a year ago. Digital-business net sales increased to RUB14.75 billion from RUB10.66 billion, while offline net sales rose to RUB632.
