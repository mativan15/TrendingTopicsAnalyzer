IRCTC Tatkal ticket booking: Steps to ensure a confirmed ticket during festivals
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 12:15:00

With the festivals around the corner, many people who work far from home will be making the journey back to celebrate with their loved ones. The Indian Railways has predicted that there will be a surge of travelers in the coming weeks and has started the tatkal system for passengers who want to get Tatkal tickets for immediate travel. Those who are attempting to get a tatkal ticket will only be book train tickets just one day prior to their journey. The word ‘Tatkal' means ‘immediately'. The tatkal boking for 3SC Class will begin at 10:00 am and the tatkal booking for sleeper will begin at 11:00 am.
