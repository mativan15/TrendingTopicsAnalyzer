Image source, GoogleImage caption, The new homes made up of houses and flats could be built on Bertie Place recreation groundPeople are being asked for their views on plans to build affordable homes on a recreation ground.Oxford City Council's housing company, OX Place, wants to build 31 new homes on Bertie Place off Abingdon Road.The proposals are for nine, two-bedroom homes sold for shared ownership the remaining 22 will be council homes - 10 houses and 12 flats.If planning permission is given the council said work could start on the development in late 2023.
