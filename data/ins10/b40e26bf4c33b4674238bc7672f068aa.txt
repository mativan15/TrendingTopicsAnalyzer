New York, NY, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Global Tech Industries Group, Inc. (OTCQB: GTII) ('GTII' or the 'Company'), www.gtii-us.com, a Nevada corporation, announced today that it is continuing its count of shareholder requests for the Shibu Inu tokens dividend previously announced, which have been coming into the Company's transfer agent, Liberty Stock Transfer, Inc. ('Liberty') since May 9, 2022. Although Liberty has not completed the count, several questions have been raised about anomalies it has found between what shareholders of record have reported and what is actually being held in brokerage accounts, or recorded on the NOBO list, and at DTCC.
