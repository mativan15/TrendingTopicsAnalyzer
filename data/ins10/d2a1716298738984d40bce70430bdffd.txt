Funding opens for major events in the Timaru District
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-06-01 05:42:13

Extra funding has been made available for major events in the Timaru District, with between $10,000 and $50,000 available to applicants able to demonstrate the economic and social benefits of hosting that event. Applications for Venture Tourism's Major Events Support Fund Applications are now open for major events taking place in the Timaru District before June 30, 2023. The applications are open to event organisers who aim to 'attract visitors from the wider region and beyond ensuring high levels of community engagement''.
