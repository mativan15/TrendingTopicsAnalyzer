Visualise a former decathlete chairing the meeting of the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) selection meeting. Now, imagine a former secretary of the Badminton Association of India attending this meeting as an observer. If it appears contrary to reason or common sense, it indeed is.Quite shockingly, all the above is true in the case of the Table Tennis Federation of India's new selection committee that meets to choose the Indian squad for the upcoming Commonwealth Games on Monday.
Delhi-based former decathlete Surinder Dev Mudgil is the chairman and ex-shuttler Anup Narang, the observer.
