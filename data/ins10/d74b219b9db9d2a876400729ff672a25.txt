Variety - Film News 'Call It!,' an app that empowers the U.K. film and TV workforce to call bad behavior out, has entered into a partnership with ITV Studios.The app enables the workforce to record incidents of bullying, harassment and discrimination quickly and anonymously, without fear of reprisal or repercussion. It also provides easy access to employers' ‘Dignity at Work' policies and procedures for formal reporting, and signposting to third party support and resources.
