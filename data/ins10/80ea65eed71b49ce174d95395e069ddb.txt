U.S. government 3-month rates rose above the yield on 10-year bonds according to U.S. Treasury data for October 18, 2022. This is a fairly unusual and researchers regard this as among the best indicators that a U.S. recession is coming on a 6-18 month view. This indicator has among the best track records in forecasting recessions across a range of economic variables.
