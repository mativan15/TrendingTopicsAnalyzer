Nasser in Africa
theatlantic.com
news
2022-05-28 21:30:00

GAMAL ABDEL NASSER'S actual objectives in Africa become clearer as the pattern of events there unfolds. He wants to enlist the new African governments as members of the neutralist bloc. He wants to acquire influence through common bonds of religion and of aversion to 'imperialism.' He wants to be the leading Arab spokesman in Africa.The disastrous polarization resulting from the struggle in the Congo has forced on Nasser a choice in favor of Lumumba's heirs and against the moderates still under Western influence.
