By Reuters Published: 09:48 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 09:48 EDT, 2 June 2022 WASHINGTON, June 2 (Reuters) - White House interns will be paid for the first time, in a change designed to make its internship program more accessible to lower-income students, the Biden administration said on Thursday. "Too often, unpaid federal internships have been a barrier to hardworking and talented students and professionals, preventing them from contributing their talents and skills to the country and holding them back from federal career advancement opportunities," the White House said in a statement.
