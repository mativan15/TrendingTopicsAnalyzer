Two private security guards of a housing complex here were taken into police custody over a clash between two groups of residents, officials said on Friday. Two groups of the residents of Hyde Park in Sector 78, located within the Sector 113 police station limits, had got into a fight on Thursday night, resulting in minor injuries to two women, they added. "The clash broke out between the residents over the post of president of the society's Apartment Owners' Association (AOA). The guards also got involved in the fight, leading to injuries to two women," a police officer said.
