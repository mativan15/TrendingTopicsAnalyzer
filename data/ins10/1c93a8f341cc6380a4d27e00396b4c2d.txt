Tamil Nadu chief minister M K Stalin came to the rescue of an accident victim, who was lying on the Anna Salai in Chennai after falling off a two-wheeler. The accident victim Arulraj sustained injuries on his head after he lost control of his two-wheeler near DMS and fell down. CM Stalin stopped his convoy and sent the accident victim to Royapettah Government Hospital. The Tamil Nadu CM also directed MLA Dr Ezhilan to take care of the patient.
