Philadelphia 76ers games among priciest in NBA this season for a family of four
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 01:05:00

The average family of four will pay $378.48 to watch the Philadelphia 76ers play a game at Wells Fargo Center this season, according to a new report from Bookies.com.

The 76ers are the eighth most expensive NBA franchise when it comes to attending games in person, the report found. The NBA average is $288.38.

Bookies.com's list measures the cost of four of the cheapest available tickets, a parking spot, two beers, two sodas and four hot dogs. By comparison, the website says the average for an NFL game is $536.
