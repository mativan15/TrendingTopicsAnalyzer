President at CRMDialer and IRIS CRM. getty Not all departments within a company are created equal. While marketing, design and customer support are there to help out, the main driving force of a company is its sales department. And even though it is crucial to any business's success, many sales teams make the same mistakes of focusing on the pitch rather than doing one thing that really moves the needle: getting more technical with the product they sell. Here's why in-depth knowledge of what you're selling matters for your business, sales team and customers.
