getty If you've opened a small business within the past few years, you can be forgiven for thinking that the interest rate market in 2022 is bonkers. After all, before 2022, it seemed as if interest rates would always be stuck at all-time lows, thanks to an extremely accommodative Federal Reserve policy in response to Covid-19. But over the past year, SBA 504 loan interest rates have nearly doubled, jumping from 2.97% in Sept. 2021 to 5.44% in Sept. 2022. For small businesses living on the margin, this massive jump in rates can translate to some serious cash flow problems.
