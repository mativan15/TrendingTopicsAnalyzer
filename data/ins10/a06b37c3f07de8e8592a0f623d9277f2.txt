Blink Charging to Find New Manufacturing Site, Add 300 Jobs
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 20:38:00

By Kathryn Hardison Blink Charging Co. plans to ramp up its manufacturing capabilities in a move that would create roughly 300 additional jobs. The electric-vehicle charging equipment company said Tuesday that it has launched a search to locate a new U.S. manufacturing facility with up to 200,000 square feet of space to manufacture its Direct Current and Buy America compliant AC Level 2 chargers. The facility will have initial production capacity of 10,000 Direct Current chargers and 20,000 to 40,000 Level 2 chargers, it said.
