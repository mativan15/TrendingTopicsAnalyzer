October 14, 2022 09:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bragar Eagel & Squire, P.C., a nationally recognized stockholder rights law firm, reminds investors that a class action lawsuit has been filed against Ampio Pharmaceuticals, Inc. ('Ampio' or the 'Company') (NYSEAmerican: AMPE, OTCMKTS: AMPE) in the United States District Court for the District of Colorado on behalf of all persons and entities who purchased or otherwise acquired Ampio securities between December 29, 2020 and August 3, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period').
