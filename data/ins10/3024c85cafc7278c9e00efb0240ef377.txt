Ko te kaiārahi tūhono o Te Pāti Māori Debbie Ngarewa-Packer te aronga o ngā pānui kaikiri 'mōrihariha'

Hei tā te kaiārahi tūhono o Te Pāti Māori Debbie Ngarewa-Packer, ahakoa ngā pānui kaikiri 'mōrihariha' he rite tonu nei te tae atu ki a ia, ka tū rangatira tonu ia. Te Pāti Māori co-leader Debbie Ngarewa-Packer says 'disgusting' racist mail she receives regularly won't hold her party back from standing up for what's right. Read this story in English here. Ki tētahi pōhi pae pāpori i te Tūrei, i tuku atu a Ngarewa-Packer i ngā whakaahua o tētahi reta tūmataiti i tae atu ki a ia, e kī pai ana i ngā kangakanga, ngā whakaaro hōrapa, me ngā karangatanga kaikiri mō te āhuatanga mahi o te Māori, me te tūnga hei tangata whenua.
