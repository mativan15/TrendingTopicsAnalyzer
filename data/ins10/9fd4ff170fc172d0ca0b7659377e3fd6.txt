Children play video games on their phones at a market in Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines, on July 21, 2020.   © Reuters MICHAEL BELTRAN, Contributing writerOctober 19, 2022 14:42 JST | PhilippinesMANILA -- Philippine President Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos Jr. last week signed his first piece of legislation since entering office -- a law that forces public telecommunications entities to create a database of mobile phone SIM cards and their users.The law means that Filipinos will need to register their identities with the entities or have their cards deactivated.
