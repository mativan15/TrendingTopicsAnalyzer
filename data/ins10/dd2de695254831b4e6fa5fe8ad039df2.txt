NEW DELHI: The battle between two of India's richest businessmen for Asia's richest spot keeps getting interesting with one pitting the other continuously.On Friday, Reliance Industries chairman Mukesh Ambani regained the tag of being Asia's richest person with a net worth of $99.7 billion. He surpassed Adani Group chief Gautam Adani who held the position since the past few months as shares of his company soared.
