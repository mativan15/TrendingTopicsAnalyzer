The Playlist Few recent sci-fi programs have premiered with the same fanfare and anticipation as Prime Video's 'The Peripheral,' dropping its first two episodes on the Amazon streamer on October 21st with a weekly rollout to follow. It's got that Bezos money, the two main voices behind 'Westworld,' and source material by the beloved William Gibson (it's loosely based on his 2014 novel of the same name). And yet, almost from the very beginning, it's a clunky disaster.
