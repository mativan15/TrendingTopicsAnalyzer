Former Adelaide hospital sold to Hong Kong group
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-30 23:01:00

A Hong Kong-based property investment company has bought Adelaide Chambers, a modern office building built at the former Adelaide hospital, in Dublin for €13.5 million.IBI Group Holdings, which is listed on the Hong Kong stock exchange, bought the building from a company associated with Joe O'Reilly's Chartered Land, which paid more than €7 million for the property in 2018.On Peter Street in Dublin 8, the four-storey-over-basement building dates from the 18th century and housed the Adelaide hospital until it moved to Tallaght in 1998.
