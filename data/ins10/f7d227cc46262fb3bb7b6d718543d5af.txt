Filling 75,000 vacant posts: Exams outsourced to TCS, IBPS for transparency
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-20 20:14:22

The Maharashtra government will now fill approximately 75,000 vacant positions in the grades of Class B, C and D by outsourcing the entrance exams to Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) and Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) to bring transparency in the process.
