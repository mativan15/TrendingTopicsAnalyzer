Evening Update: Facebook warns it could block Canadians' access to news over Ottawa's online news bill
theglobeandmail.com
news
2022-10-21 21:07:30

Good evening, let's start with today's top stories:Facebook says it is considering blocking Canadians' access to news sites on its platform, in response to a proposed federal law that would force it to compensate media outlets for carrying links to their articles.The tech giant upped the stakes in the battle over the online news bill, warning it may be forced to block Canadians from sharing or viewing news content out of frustration over the government's 'misguided' approach.Facebook issued its warning after Liberal MPs prevented Facebook from giving evidence to a Commons committee considering Bill C-18 by refusing a Conservative demand to extend the number of sessions to allow the social-media giant to voice its concerns.
