By John Dias October 17, 2022 / 8:14 AM / CBS New York RONKONKOMA, N.Y. -- There's more fallout after the Suffolk County government website was hacked. CBS2's Jennifer McLogan first told you about the story last week. Then, we checked, and it turns out many of the county services are still not working properly. The county has not paid the ransom, and as CBS2's John Dias reports, it's drastically hurting the real estate market."It's definitely creating a level of frustration," Amy Pfister, a real estate agent with Signature Premier Properties, told Dias.
