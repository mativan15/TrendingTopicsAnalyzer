A MUM has revealed how she will save thousands of pounds on Christmas this year - by not buying anyone gifts.
Heidi Ondrak, 51, from the UK, used to spend thousands of Christmas but amid the cost of living crisis has decided to ditch shopping all together.
Heidi Ondrak, 51, refuses to buy Christmas presents this yearCredit: Jam Press/@duchessofthrift
Any she does pick up will be from charity shops or car boot salesCredit: Jam Press/@duchessofthrift
Branding Christmas as a 'big marketing construct', the mum will "never again" spend her hard-earned cash on gifts for family members.
