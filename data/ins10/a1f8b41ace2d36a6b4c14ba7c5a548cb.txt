India's Energy Needs: Goals And ObjectivesFrom the generation of electricity to transportation, energy is used for a multitude of things. With India witnessing rapid urbanization and industrialization and marching towards becoming a $5 trillion economy, its energy needs are growing enormously. In terms of global carbon emissions, India is ranked third. To cope with the increasing energy needs in India, Indian Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, revised the NDC (Nationally Determined Contribution) in 2021, originally announced at the Conference of the Parties (COP21) in 2015.
