By Reuters Published: 22:04 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 22:04 EDT, 18 October 2022 Noah Cates notched the game-winning goal off a turnover as the visiting Philadelphia Flyers won their third straight game to open the season, rallying to beat the Tampa Bay Lightning 3-2 on Tuesday. With the game tied at 2 in the third period, Lightning defenseman Erik Cernak turned the puck over in front of his crease. Cates found the puck in the slot, spun quickly and whipped in his first goal at 12:44 for the game-winning marker.
