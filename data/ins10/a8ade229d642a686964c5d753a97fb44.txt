The Valley gives back: Business Journal honors metro's biggest corporate philanthropists
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 17:08:00

The Business Journal honored Valley companies that go above and beyond to give back to the communities they serve at the annual Corporate Philanthropy awards. Twelve awards were handed out at the event.

This year's in-person event, held Oct. 20 at Scottsdale Center for the Performing Arts, recognized Valley firms for innovation in the ways they give back, for volunteering the most hours and for big-time projects earning philanthropist of the year honors.
