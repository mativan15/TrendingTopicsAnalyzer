Bakelite Synthetics Features BREAKTHRU™ Technology that Significantly Reduces Free Formaldehyde Content in Phenolic Resins and its Environmental Impact

Atlanta, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Bakelite Synthetics, a leading global integrated producer of thermoset specialty resins, systems and engineered thermoset molding compounds, is featuring an innovative technology that reduces residual formaldehyde content in phenolic resins. BREAKTHRU™ technology will be showcased at the Composites and Advanced Materials Expo (CAMX), October 17 through 20, in Anaheim, California. Testing of BREAKTHRU™ has demonstrated reductions of 97 percent in emissions when compared with current MIL-SPEC resin systems.
