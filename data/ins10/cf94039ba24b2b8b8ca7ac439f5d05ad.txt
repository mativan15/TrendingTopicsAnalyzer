Global Female Media Company Recognizes 100 Women with Prestige AwardPHOENIX , May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- KNOW Women Founder Sarah Benken announced this year's honorees of the 100 Women to KNOW, an annual award recognizing the top female leaders across America. The 2022 Honorees were recognized at the Annual KNOW Women Summit held earlier this month at The Saguaro Scottsdale in Scottsdale, Arizona.
