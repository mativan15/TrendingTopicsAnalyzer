Skip Jean-Michel Ménard, seen during the 2017 Brier, scored three in the opening end and pulled away with five points in the third en route to a 12-2 win over Slovenia's Stefan Sever on Thursday in their round-robin finale at the world mixed curling championship in Aberdeen, Scotland. (Andrew Vaughan/The Canadian Press)Canada's Jean-Michel Ménard dumped Slovenia's Stefan Sever 12-2 in the round-robin finale for both teams Thursday at the world mixed curling championship in Aberdeen, Scotland.Ménard, from Gatineau, Que.
