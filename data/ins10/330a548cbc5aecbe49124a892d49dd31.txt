CHANDLER, Ariz.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Insight Enterprises (NASDAQ:NSIT), a Fortune 500 solutions integrator helping organizations accelerate their digital journey, honored Global Diversity Awareness Month Wednesday by hosting a third annual Global Harmony Day, a celebration of the diverse backgrounds and insights shared by the company's 12,000+ teammates worldwide.

This year's Global Harmony Day theme focused on Be Ambitious by embracing the power of culture and inspiring a work environment of belonging.
