I spent some time on the tech job site Blind, and what I learned about the anxiety in Silicon Valley is really, really scary
businessinsider.com
tech
2022-06-01 09:00:00

If you want to get a core sample of what Silicon Valley engineers are feeling these days, as the tech industry implodes around them, spend some time on Blind. The site — part job board, part social network — allows its users to remain anonymous, except for two salient facts: where they work (and for how long) and how much money they make. The employer info is a requirement for entry and is verified by Blind on the back end. The money thing, though, is enforced at the peer level. Fail to disclose it, and the first comment you receive is likely to be "TC or GTFO" — i.
