October 20, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time WALNUT CREEK, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BayCom Corp ('BayCom' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: BCML), the holding company for United Business Bank (the 'Bank' or 'UBB'), announced earnings of $7.2 million, or $0.54 per diluted common share, for the third quarter of 2022, compared to earnings of $5.2 million, or $0.38 per diluted common share, for the second quarter of 2022 and $5.4 million, or $0.51 per diluted common share, for the third quarter of 2021. For the nine months ended September 30, 2022 the Company announced earnings of $18.
