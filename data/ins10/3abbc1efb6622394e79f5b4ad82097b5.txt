Indian envoys to seven countries meet Haryana CM Khattar
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 22:10:44

Haryana chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar on Tuesday apprised the seven heads of mission of India in foreign countries about the swift progress the state has made in agriculture, industries and the business sector.'Haryana has a huge potential for industrial investment and major industries from within the country and abroad are continuously investing in the state,' Khattar said, adding the state government has been formulating new schemes offering favourable business environment in the state.The six Ambassadors of India are Pradip Kumar Yadav (Liberia), Ram Karan Verma (Congo), Pradeep Kumar Rawat (China) Neena Malhotra (Italy), Yogeshwar Sangwan (Paraguay) and Pooja Kapur (Denmark).
