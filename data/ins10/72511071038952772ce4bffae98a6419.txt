Hughley cited Mo'Nique's past show business feuds in an Instagram post Sunday after she ripped him onstage during a show they both performed in at the Fox Theater in Detroit on Saturday.In her set, Mo'Nique, 54, called Hughley, 59, out by name, as she felt slighted she wasn't the clear headlining act for The Comedy Explosion show, which also featured comic Aida Rodriguez and was hosted by Josh Adams.Hughley, in response, shared a frame of Mo'Nique from the 2009 movie Precious in an Instagram post addressing her tirade.
