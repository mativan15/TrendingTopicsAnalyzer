Santos ends sponsorship of popular Darwin Festival after lengthy campaign by environmentalists opposing fossil fuels
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-18 20:33:04

Gas giant Santos has ended its nearly three decade sponsorship of the popular Darwin Festival, following a lengthy campaign by environmentalists and traditional owners opposed to fossil fuels and fracking. Santos funding of the Darwin Festival will end in DecemberThe energy company cites "alternative" funding to the festival as the reason for the decisionCoalition of fossil fuel opponents says it's providing $200,000 to the festival over two years Santos has been a major financial supporter of the well known arts and cultural event in the Northern Territory, holding the naming rights for the opening concert each year.
