Attorney general Online forums like 4chan are being partly blamed for radicalizing the man who carried out a mass shooting in Buffalo, New York, earlier in the year.On Tuesday, New York Attorney General Letitia James announced on Twitter several key findings of her office's investigation into the deadly incident. On May 14, 18-year-old Payton S. Gendron opened fire at a Tops Friendly Supermarket in a predominantly Black neighborhood of Buffalo, killing 10 people and injuring 3 more.
