Far too much leadership and management theory is just that: theory. Intellectually interesting but often not much use when you're under pressure on the front lines. If nothing else, the last few years have been a stark reminder that ideas in business journals can come up short when you're dealing with rapidly changing realities, unexpected curve balls, and human beings whose emotions and anxieties don't always conform to what we read about in case studies. Whether or not you believe in 'The Great Resignation,' and however employee retention may be affecting your business, the reality is that organization effectiveness is about people, and the most successful leaders and managers are the ones who generate followership.
