ARTPARK says incubating six pre-ventures in new age technologies, sets up testbed with Nokia and IISc
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 12:45:00

Umakant Soni, Cofounder & CEO, ARTPARK. NEW DELHI: The AI & Robotics Technology Park (ARTPARK), a non-profit foundation, said it is incubating six pre-ventures in artificial intelligence (AI), robotics, and 5G technology. It has set up a testbed with Finnish telecom gear maker Nokia and the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) in Bengaluru for drones, autonomous vehicles, and other use cases. 'We have incubated around six pre-ventures as part of ARTPARK and supported another 15 startups through our in house program.
