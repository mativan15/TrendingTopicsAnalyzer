Prospect League Commissioner and Deputy Commissioner Stepping Down
iheart.com
news
2022-10-19 21:15:00

(Elkville, Illinois) -- Prospect League Commissioner Dennis Bastien and Deputy Commissioner Lisa Bastien are stepping down from their roles in the league office, effective December 31, 2022, after six years. The Chillicothe Paints are members of the Prospect League. "We have been honored and proud to represent this glorious summer collegiate league for six years," said Commissioner Dennis Bastien. "We feel we have been instrumental in its growth and elevation in status among collegiate summer leagues.
