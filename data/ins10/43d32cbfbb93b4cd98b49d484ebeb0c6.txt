Post the pandemic, the box-office has been highly volatile with Bollywood films not performing as expected. While many have shared their thoughts on the scenario, now Rakul Preet Singh too has shared a piece of her mind.She says, 'I feel that after every few years, there will be this changing phase that people will witness. I just feel that it is not that people's tastes have changed. We have just come out of a pandemic. So, of course, the kind of films people want to watch possibly are just entertaining films or something where they can leave the worries and come to theatre and enjoy.
