Twenty-four FBS teams went into the virus-disrupted 2020 season with new head coaches. Of those, 12 assumed the lead role for the first time in their careers. Ten of those 12 coaches remain in charge of the team that they took over nearly three years ago. The situation each coach stepped into was extremely challenging with spring practice, if not entirely wiped away, then close to it.
