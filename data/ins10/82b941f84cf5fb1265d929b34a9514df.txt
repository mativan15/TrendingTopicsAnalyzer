Many players from Bengal including former India skipper Sourav Ganguly have represented two-time winners Kolkata Knight Riders since the inception of the IPL in 2008. Manoj Tiwary, Wriddhiman Saha, Shreevats Goswami and Mohammed Shami are also a part of the list but the franchise hasn't seen a homegrown talent in the recent past.
