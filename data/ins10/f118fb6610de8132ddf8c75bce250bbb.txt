America's big formula makers to meet with Biden as top adviser signals moves on industry consolidation
aol.com
news
2022-06-01 10:52:40

President Joe Biden is set to meet with top baby formula executives at the White House on Wednesday, focusing on his administration's recent actions to increase production, according to a White House official.But another issue may also be on the table: whether to break up or at least dilute the power of some of these big manufacturers.'Over the medium-term, we have to tackle the fact that this industry got so consolidated,' said Brian Deese, Biden's top economic adviser, in a Yahoo Finance Live interview on Tuesday.
