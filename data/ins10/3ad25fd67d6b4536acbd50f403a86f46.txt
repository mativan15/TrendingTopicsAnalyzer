18 October 2022 by Shakiel Mahjouri 
ET Canada Alan Moore wants nothing to do with the various adaptations of his DC Comics maxiseries, Watchmen — Primetime Emmy awards or not.Moore has made it clear both personally and professionally that he wants no affiliation with the critically adored HBO limited series 'Watchmen'. In a new interview, Moore recalled a tense letter exchange with the TV series' showrunner. Moore did not name the showrunner; however, Damon Lindelof was the one slotted in that role.
