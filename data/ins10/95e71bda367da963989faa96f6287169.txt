Published: 20:50 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 20:50 EDT, 15 October 2022 Legislation introducing minimum service levels during strikes by transport workers is being prepared by Ministers.It comes after months of industrial action by railway workers in disputes over pay, jobs and conditions that have caused travel chaos across the country.Unions criticised the move, with many believing it would be unworkable.Legislation will be introduced following Prime Minister Liz Truss's commitment to bring in such a Bill within her first 30 days of Parliament sitting.
