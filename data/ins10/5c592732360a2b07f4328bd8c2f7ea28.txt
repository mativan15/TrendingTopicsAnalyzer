N.S. family suits up to rescue unwanted bees
cbc.ca
news
2022-06-01 20:29:00

The yard beside the Dugas family home is buzzing with activity.Amanda and Lonnie Dugas, and their kids Gregory, 17, and Sarah, 14, have around half a dozen beehives on their Brentwood, N.S., property.Amanda Dugas, a Mi'kmaw student support worker at a local high school, never saw herself becoming a beekeeper. When an opportunity came up for her to take a summer course in beekeeping along with her daughter a few years ago, they went for it. Soon, the whole family was donning their bee suits to care for hives.
