October 20, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time BLOOMFIELD, Connecticut--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kaman Corp. (NYSE:KAMN) announced today that it will report its third quarter 2022 results after the stock market closes on Tuesday, November 1, 2022, and host a live webcast and conference call at 8:30 am ET on Wednesday, November 2, 2022. A supplemental presentation relating to the third quarter 2022 results will be posted to the Company's website prior to the earnings call at http://www.kaman.com/investors/quarterly-earnings-calls.
