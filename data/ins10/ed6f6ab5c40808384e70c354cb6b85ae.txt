Assam: Daily wage of home guards hiked to Rs 767
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 06:30:00

GUWAHATI: The Assam government on Wednesday decided to fulfill the long-standing demand of 24,000 home guards in the state by approving a hike in their daily wage to Rs 767 from the existing Rs 300.With this, the home guards will now draw around Rs 23,010 as monthly wages.A notification issued by the the home and political department said, "The governor of Assam is pleased to enhance the duty allowances of home guardswith immediate effect."
