Megan Hilty raises money to help recover bodies of family members after plane crash

2022-10-20 18:11:30

Smash actor Megan Hilty has asked fans to consider donating to a GoFundMe that would help her pay a private company to recover the remains of her family members after they died in a plane crash last month.On 4 September, a floatplane carrying 10 people crashed in Mutiny Bay near Seattle, killing everyone on board. Hilty later revealed that she lost four family members in the crash. Her sister Lauren Hilty, who was eight months pregnant, her brother-in-law Ross Mickel, and their son Remy, were all on board.
