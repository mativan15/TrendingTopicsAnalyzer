By Australian Associated Press Published: 21:35 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 21:35 EDT, 2 June 2022 A West Australian man has been stripped of about $4 million in assets, including cash in bank accounts, an apartment and other property, after an international money laundering and tax fraud investigation by the Australian Federal Police. The WA Supreme Court recently ordered more than $2.25 million and a Perth apartment valued at about $585,000 be forfeited to the commonwealth under the Proceeds of Crime Act.
