Vodafone Idea wants more postpaid subscribers as a fix to its financial problems
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-06 07:00:00

ETTelecomVodafone Idea's Ravinder Takkar future planning of the company.In its bid to fix its finances, has come up with a plan to target its focus on postpaid customers. The telco wants to add more postpaid consumers and high paying customers to its user base so as to increase the average revenue per user or ARPU, according to a Mint report. The move comes at a time when it is finalising a plan to raise Rs 20,000 crore to compete with its rivals - and Reliance Jio. Vodafone Idea is "closer than it's ever been" to securing the roughly Rs 20,000 crore in additional funding it needs from lenders and outside investors to more successfully compete with Jio and in the market, according to a report.
