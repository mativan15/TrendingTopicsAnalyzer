Frictionless data: Escaping the gravity of regulation
techradar.com
tech
2022-10-17 14:00:40

If the big trend since the Snowden NSA data collection controversy has been the regulation of data, 2021 may see how big tech could try to set it free again.Rules regulating data proliferated globally after the Snowden revelations, and to increasing fanfare. If the philosopher Jean-Jacques Rousseau were alive today, he may as well write, 'data is born free, but everywhere it is localized, surveilled, regulated, breached, censored, biased and taxed'.Data localization rules are increasingly widespread.
