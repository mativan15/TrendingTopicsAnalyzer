Novak Djokovic has qualified for the ATP Finals and could win a record prize money cheque (Image: Getty)The ATP Finals has confirmed a record-breaking prize pot for the season-ending championships this year. Five players have already qualified for the prestigious tournament including Rafael Nadal and Novak Djokovic, and they will now be battling it out for the biggest winner's cheque in tennis history as well as 1,500 ranking points and the trophy.The winner of the ATP Finals is set to receive the biggest monetary prize in the history of tennis after the tournament announced the money on offer for each win at the year-end tournament.
