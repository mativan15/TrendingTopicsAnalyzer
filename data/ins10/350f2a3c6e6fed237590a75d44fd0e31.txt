Livspace announces C-suite elevations and appointments
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 01:45:00

Saurabh Jain, Co-Founder and CEO, India, LivspaceHome interiors and renovation platform Livspace has announced top-level C-Suite appointments. While Saurabh Jain, chief business officer, has been elevated as co-founder and chief executive officer, India operations, Lalit Mittal has been appointed as chief business officer, India. Furthermore, Ankit Shah has been roped in as the new group chief strategy officer.Jain has been instrumental in driving the company's India operations by launching solutions across regions.
