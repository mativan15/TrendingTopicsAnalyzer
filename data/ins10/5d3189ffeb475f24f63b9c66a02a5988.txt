October 19, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LAKE FOREST, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--STAAR Surgical Company (NASDAQ: STAA), a leading developer, manufacturer and marketer of implantable lenses and companion delivery systems for the eye, today announced that it will release financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2022 on Wednesday, November 2, 2022 after the market close.

STAAR will host a conference call and webcast on Wednesday, November 2 at 4:30 p.m. Eastern / 1:30 p.m. Pacific to discuss its financial results and operational progress.
