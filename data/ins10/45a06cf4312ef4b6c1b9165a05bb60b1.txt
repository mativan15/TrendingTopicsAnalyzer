Dallas/Texas, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Rising consumer desire for targeted advertising, the need to keep customers on board for the sake of business expansion and the necessity for a smart advertising strategy are some of the main factors anticipated to propel the development of the global Marketing Automation Market. Marketers use marketing automation as a platform or piece of technology to organize, plan, carry out, and control all of their offline and online marketing activities. Any business that wants to generate profits must employ a variety of platforms and technologies for automation in the marketing stages.
