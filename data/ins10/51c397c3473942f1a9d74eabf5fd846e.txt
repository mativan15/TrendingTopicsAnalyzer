CAA's Joanna Popper Shares About Anthony Hopkins' First NFT Drop and Opportunities in the Metaverse
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-10-19 21:13:23

CAA chief metaverse officer Joanna Popper knew Anthony Hopkins' first non-fungible token drop would be popular but didn't anticipate it selling out in seven minutes. Popper joined The Hollywood Reporter senior business editor Ashley Cullins on Wednesday in a keynote Q&A at THR's annual Power Business Managers event. She discussed topics ranging from the evolution of the internet to capitalizing on opportunities in the metaverse to the Bored Ape Yacht Club. Popper explained that the metaverse is the 'next wave of the internet.
