Kwasi Kwarteng will confirm this week that reforms to Britain's corporate governance regime have been watered down after a backlash from business leaders and lobby groups over proposals for tougher controls.The business secretary will announce that he is dropping proposed reforms, modelled on America's Sarbanes-Oxley Act, under which directors would have faced bans or fines for signing off inaccurate accounts.
