The Chinese tech market will welcome iQOO's new smartphone series 'iQOO 10' in the third quarter of this year. The Company is loved for producing performance-oriented smartphones at affordable prices. Primarily, there will be two lineups for the developing iQOO 10 series - 'iQOO 10', which will carry model no. V2217A and 'iQOO 10 Pro', which will feature model no. V2218A, as reported by GizmoChina. Probably, the new iQOO 10 series will feature the Snapdragon 8 Gen 1+ SoC, which was recently launched.
