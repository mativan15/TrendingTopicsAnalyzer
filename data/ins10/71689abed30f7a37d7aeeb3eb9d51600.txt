 Drones can be a useful tool for photographers, real estate agents, contractors and other types of small businesses. The Federal Aviation Administration allows for you to fly a drone for work or business as long as it's under 55 pounds and you meet certain requirements. Your drone can generally fly at altitudes up to 400 feet and speeds up to 100 mph.
But at those speeds and heights, a lot can go wrong.
