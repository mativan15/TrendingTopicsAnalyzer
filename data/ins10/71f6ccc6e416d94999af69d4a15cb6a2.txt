More than 1,400 Milwaukee-area business and community leaders packed Tempo Milwaukee's leadership event Thursday to hear from Olympic superstar Simone Biles, who talked about her successes and challenges both on and off the gymnastics mat. Check out the attached slideshow to see photos from the popular event.

The event, which was held at the Miller High Life Theatre, drew one of the biggest crowds for a local community event since the pandemic started in 2020.

They were there to hear from Biles, one of the greatest female athletes of all time who is the most decorated gymnast in World Championships history with 25 medals, including 19 gold.
