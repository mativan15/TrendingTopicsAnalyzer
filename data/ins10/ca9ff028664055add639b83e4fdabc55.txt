Lil Xan's Opening Acts Put Rapper On Blast After He Pulled Out Of Tour

Photo: Getty ImagesLil Xan shocked his fans when he recently pulled out of his upcoming tour. After he put the blame on the management team who represent his opening acts, one of the artists who was scheduled to open for Xan is defending his group. According to a report HipHopDX published on Wednesday, June 1, former opening act Dropout Kings claim that Lil Xan dropped out of the tour last minute and immediately placed blame on their management team. The band claims that Xan even contacted them a week before the tour began and told them how excited he was to meet them.
