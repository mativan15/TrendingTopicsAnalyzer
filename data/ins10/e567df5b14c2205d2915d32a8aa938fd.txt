India's Prime Minister Narendra Modi said Tuesday the cabinet 's approval to raise the minimum support price (MSP) for all mandated rabi crops for marketing season 2023-24 will make the agriculture sector more 'energetic'.Our farmer brothers and sisters are important partners in the development of the nation. To further empower them, the government today approved increase in MSP of all essential Rabi crops including wheat, lentils, gram and barley.
