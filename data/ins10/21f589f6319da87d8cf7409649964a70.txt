Andhra Pradesh: Woman immolates self at police station, dies
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 11:45:00

VISAKHAPATNAM: A 22-year-old woman allegedly set herself on fire at a police station in Visakhapatnam where she had gone for counselling over the harassment by her husband.The woman was taken to a hospital where she died during treatment.The woman brought a bottle filled with petrol and set herself on fire in the cellar of the police station following an argument with her husband.The victim was identified as Vamala Sravani of Nakkavanipalem near the Satyam Junction area.Police said that the woman was an LLB student while her husband is her senior in the college.
