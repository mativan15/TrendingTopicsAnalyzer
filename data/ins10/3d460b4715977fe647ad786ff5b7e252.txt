Pagero Won't Further Increase Bid for Tungsten Corp
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 16:37:00

By Joe Hoppe Pagero Group AB said Wednesday that it won't increase its cash offer for London-listed digital financial-management company Tungsten Corp. PLC, and thatits offer is now final. The Swedish business network provider said it considers its 48 pence a share bid to be sufficient, but that it reserves the right to revise its financial terms. On May 20, Kofax Holdings International Ltd. increased its own offer for Tungsten from 42 pence to 55 pence a share, bringing its total offer for Tungsten to around 70.
