LUCKNOW: Lucknow police on Sunday arrested a man and his minor aide who first made a 14-year-old boy addicted with an online age-restricted game and then also made him steal cash from his house for the game's advanced stages in Wazirganj area.

The minor victim was also made to sell his valuables and steal Rs 4.5 lakh from the locker of his house over a period of one month.
