Michael Vi/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Okta (NASDAQ:OKTA) is scheduled to announce Q1 results on Thursday, Jun. 2, after market close. Consensus EPS estimate is -$0.34 (-240% Y/Y) and consensus revenue estimate is $388.77M (+54.9% Y/Y). Over the last 2 years, OKTA has beaten EPS and revenue estimates 100% of the time. Over the last 3 months, EPS estimates have seen 1 upward revision and 24 downward. Revenue estimates have seen 21 upward revisions and 1 downward. Palo Alto Networks (PANW) issued strong guidance in its Q3 earnings report last month, which analysts said boded well for the entire sector.
