CA Foundation 2022: The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) conducts examinations for CA aspirants twice every year - May/June and November/December. Each candidate aims to clear CA Foundation in their first attempt and make the cut. However, multiple distractions and poor time management can make it challenging for students to clear in one go. Nevertheless, with the right guidance, the right attitude, and relevant study material, one can easily fulfil their dream of becoming a CA.
