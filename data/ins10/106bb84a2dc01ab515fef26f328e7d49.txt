Citizens Financial Group Announces Pricing of $600 Million of Citizens Bank, N.A. Senior Notes

October 20, 2022 04:34 PM Eastern Daylight Time PROVIDENCE, R.I.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Citizens Financial Group, Inc. (NYSE: CFG) today announced the pricing of $600 million of Citizens Bank, N.A. ('CBNA') 6.064% fixed/floating rate senior notes due 2025 (the 'Notes').

CBNA intends to use the net proceeds of the notes offering for general corporate purposes. The notes offering is expected to close on October 25, 2022, subject to customary closing conditions.
