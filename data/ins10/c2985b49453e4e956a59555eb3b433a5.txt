CALGARY, Alberta, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Quorum Information Technologies Inc. (QIS: TSXV) ('Quorum' or the 'Company'), a SaaS software and services company that provides essential software that vehicle dealerships rely on for operations throughout North America, is pleased to announce the appointment of Bruce Atkinson as Vice President Operations, starting June 6th, 2022. Quorum is also announcing the resignation of Mike Herenberg, Chief Operating Officer, effective June 30th, 2022. Bruce will be replacing Mike and will join the executive leadership team, reporting to the President and CEO.
