U.S. rent price growth is showing signs of leveling off, as the median rent price in September decreased month-over-month for the first time this year, by 2.48%. After soaring by nearly 20% earlier in 2022, year-over-year price growth has dropped to 8.79% in September, a Rent.com analysis found. Rents are still high, though: The national median rent price is now $2,002, compared to $1,840 at the same time last year. While prices trended down overall, some metropolitan markets saw bigger drops in than others.
