Published: 21:03 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 21:23 EDT, 5 June 2022 Former NSW deputy premier John Barilaro has been awarded $715,000 in defamation damages from Google and his YouTube nemesis Jordan Shanks faces possible prosecution for contempt of court.The now retired MP sued Google, the owner of YouTube, and commentator Jordan Shanks over videos titled 'Bruz' and 'Secret Dictatorship' published on the friendlyjordies channel in 2020.The videos included claims that Mr Barilaro was a corrupt conman who should be jailed, who committed perjury nine times and engaged in blackmail.
