Coalition MK: Transport Ministry 'stealing from the periphary'
jpost.com
news
2022-05-31 09:45:00

Rebel coalition MK Michael Biton of Blue and White used strong language when criticizing Transportation Minister Merav Michaeli's public transportation reform plan on Tuesday at a meeting of his Knesset Economics Committee.

"The Transportation Ministry is stealing money from the periphery to give to the rich," Biton said. "They are displaying wickedness, not justice."
