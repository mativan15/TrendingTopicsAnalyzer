If you have some savings, you might be wondering whether it's worth using them to overpay your mortgage. Paying down your mortgage will save you money in the long run and shorten the amount of time it takes to pay the loan off. It also means you can own your home outright sooner.But while overpaying might seem like a good idea, there are lots of things to consider first. This article covers: Should I overpay my mortgage? Overpaying your mortgage means you pay off some of your loan on top of your monthly repayments.
