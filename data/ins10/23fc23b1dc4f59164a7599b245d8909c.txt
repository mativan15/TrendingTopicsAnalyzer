CHICAGO, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sprout Social, Inc. ('Sprout Social', the 'Company') (Nasdaq: SPT), an industry-leading provider of cloud-based social media management software, today announced that members of senior management will participate in the following upcoming in-person investor events: the 42nd Annual William Blair Growth Stock Conference on Tuesday, June 7th at 2:00pm CT, the Baird 2022 Global Consumer, Technology & Services Conference on Wednesday, June 8th at 12:25pm CT and the Stifel 2022 Cross Sector Insight Conference on Thursday, June 9th at 2:00pm CT.
