PTIPrime Minister Narendra Modi made a stop to accept a painting from a girl depicting his mother in Shimla.Prime Minister Narendra Modi made a stop at the Mall Road of Shimla to accept a painting from a girl depicting his mother on Tuesday. The incident took place during PM Modi's visit to Himachal Pradesh. PM Modi's visit to Himachal Pradesh was aimed to mark the eighth anniversary of the Modi Government. The PM was accompanied by chief minister of Himachal Pradesh, Jai Ram Thakur.
 While addressing a public rally in the state, Modi said that after the BJP came to power, his government removed nine crore unentitled names from various lists of government-scheme beneficiaries.
