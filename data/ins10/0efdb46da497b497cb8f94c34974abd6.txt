Shark Tank's Daymond John says Kim Kardashian 'works hard' amid backlash
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-21 15:55:31

Shark Tank star Daymond John praised the Kardashians work ethic and said they would do well on the reality series.John, 53, cheered on family matriarch Kris Jenner and daughter Kim Kardashian as "real operators" who "work hard."The FUBU founder stars on Shark Tank, a show where budding entrepreneurs pitch their business ideas to a panel of investors with the hopes they will give them money to propel their companies to success.He spoke of how focused Jenner was about building a strong business foundation for her children during the early seasons of their reality show Keeping Up With the Kardashians so they would have something to fall back on when their 15 minutes of fame was up.
