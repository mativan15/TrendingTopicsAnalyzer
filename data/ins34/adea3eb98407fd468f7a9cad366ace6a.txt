AAIB, DGCA teams to probe Uttarakhand helicopter crash
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 15:45:00

IANSA helicopter carrying 7 pilgrims of Kedarnath crashed in Phata, UttarakhandTeams of the Aircraft Accident Investigation Bureau (AAIB) and aviation regulator DGCA will be probing the helicopter crash near Kedarnath in Uttarakhand that killed seven people on Tuesday, according to a senior official. The operator of the ill-fated helicopter - Aryan Aviation - had come under the regulatory scanner and was fined Rs 5 lakh recently by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) for certain violations.
