October 18, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time HANOVER, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Utz Brands, Inc. (NYSE: UTZ) ("Utz" or the "Company"), a leading U.S. manufacturer of branded salty snacks, including Utz, On The Border Chips & Dips, Zapp's, and other Utz brands, is pleased to expand its portfolio of snack foods to all 112 Schnucks stores. Schnucks, a leading grocery retailer, has stores conveniently located throughout Missouri, Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin.
