Six weeks ago, grinning at the victorious Tory leadership candidate as Liz Truss learned of her imminent accession to No 10, was her husband, who has largely stayed out of the spotlight.Just 45 days later, as he stepped Downing Street with his wife to support her in announcing her resignation to the country, Hugh O'Leary cemented himself in the history books as the most short-lived No 10 spouse. While he has only had the briefest of forays inside the walls of No 10, the accountant and outgoing first man has his own political story to tell, though his career with the Conservatives took quite a different trajectory to his wife's.
