Gerrard leaves Aston Villa after Fulham defeat
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 21:56:04

Aston Villa manager Steven Gerrard has left the club following Thursday's 3-0 Premier League defeat at Fulham.A club spokesman said: "We would like to thank Steven for his hard work and commitment and wish him well for the future."Villa have won just twice in the league this season and only sit above the relegation zone on goals scored.Gerrard succeeded Dean Smith as Villa manager in November 2021 after leaving his role at Rangers.But the former England and Liverpool midfielder, 42, led Villa to only 13 wins from 40 games during his 11 months in charge.
