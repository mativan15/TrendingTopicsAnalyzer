As New Zealand media companies organise to start collective bargaining with Google and Facebook over payment for news content carried by the big platforms, Rod Sims offers some insights into the pioneering efforts across the Tasman. Rod Sims was chairperson of the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission from 2011 until March 2022.
