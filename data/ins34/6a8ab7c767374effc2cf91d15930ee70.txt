MCMURRAY, Pa., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--In the latest Healthcare Business Strategy report, Mark Farrah Associates (MFA), www.markfarrah.com, reported Medicare Supplement plans, also known as Medigap or Med Supp plans, covered over 13.9 million seniors as of December 31, 2021. Many leading managed care organizations, Blues plans, regional plans, and multiline carriers compete in the Medicare Supplement space.

Medicare Supplement plans collectively earned approximately $34.3 billion in premiums and paid out $26.
