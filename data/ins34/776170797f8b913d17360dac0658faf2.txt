Deontay Wilder announced his much-anticipated return to the heavyweight scene with a first-round destruction of Robert Helenius in New York and now appears to be targeting a massive showdown with Oleksandr Usyk.Fighting for the first time since his epic trilogy defeat by Tyson Fury at Barclays Center on Saturday night, the ‘Bronze Bomber' dropped his Finnish opponent - the former European champion - with a massive right hand to end a short, one-sided contest with three seconds left in the opener.
