Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce stock rises Thursday, still underperforms market
marketwatch.com
news

Shares of Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce CM, +1.50% rose 1.50% to C$70.97 Thursday, in what proved to be an all-around favorable trading session for the Canadian market, with the S&P/TSX Composite Index GSPTSE, +1.54% rising 1.54% to 21,031.81. This was the stock's second consecutive day of gains. Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce closed C$12.78 below its 52-week high (C$83.75), which the company reached on February 8th. Trading volume of 2.7 M shares remained below its 50-day average volume of 3.
