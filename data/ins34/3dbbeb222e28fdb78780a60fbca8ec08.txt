 The Ontario Lottery and Gaming Corporation says more than $903,000 in second-quarter gaming revenue payments were recently made in the Peterborough area.
The payments encompass gaming revenue from July 1 to Sept. 30.According to the OLG, the City of Peterborough received $791,037 in second-quarter gaming revenue for hosting Shorelines Casino Peterborough, up from $719,930 reported in the first quarter.
