DUBAI, UAE, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- IBS Software, a leading SaaS solutions provider to the travel industry globally, has appointed Dr. Theresa Wise to its Board of Directors. Dr. Wise has 15 years' experience as Chief Information Officer (CIO) having served with Delta Airlines, where she was responsible for the renewal of Delta's complex and critical technology systems, and previously as CIO of Northwest Airlines. The IBS Software Board now comprises eight Directors, of which six are Independent Directors.
