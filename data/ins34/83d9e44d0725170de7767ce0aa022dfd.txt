WhatsApp to introduce ‘Undo' button for deleted messages SOON!
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-05 05:19:54

Have you ever accidentally deleted a message and once gone, rued the fact that you can do nothing about it now? Well, WhatsApp will soon let you retrieve deleted messages with an 'Undo' button. WhatsApp introduced the ‘delete message' feature quite some time ago, and definitely, it was a big relief for users. It provided a great opportunity to escape making mistakes! But what if you want to retrieve those deleted messages? That is not there and it is one of the most sought after features on WhatsApp users' wish list.
