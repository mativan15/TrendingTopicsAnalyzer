WASHINGTON, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to a report by Vantage Market Research, the Global Electric Vehicle Market was worth USD 165.1 Billion in 2021 and is expected to be worth USD 434.4 Billion by 2028, with a CAGR of 17.5% between 2022 and 2028. The report gives an in-depth look at the top segments, changing market trends, value chain, key investment pockets, competitive scenario, and regional landscape. The report is an important and helpful source of information for leading market players, investors, new entrants, and other stakeholders who want to make new plans for the future and take steps to strengthen their position in the market.
