CEO of Bidease, an AdTech startup on a mission to bring fully transparent, value-driven advertising to the mobile world. getty Year after year, advertisers are investing more in mobile marketing. This is true for both mobile-first and the more conservative mobile-forced brands. According to the latest report by data.ai, the 2021 global mobile ad spend amounted to $295 billion (up 23% year over year) and is forecast to hit $350 billion in 2022. So, while advertisers allocate huge budgets for mobile ads, fraudsters seek to profit from this by all means possible.
