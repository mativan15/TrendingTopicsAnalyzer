Tellurian Shares Drop 8% After Agreement to Sell $500 Million in Senior Notes
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 14:04:00

By Chris Wack Tellurian Inc. shares fell 8% to $4.39 after the company said it agreed to sell $500 million of senior secured convertible notes. The company said the notes will bear interest at 6% per annum, expiring May 1, 2025, and will be convertible into shares of Tellurian common stock at an initial conversion price of $5.724, subject to customary adjustments. Roth Capital Partners and Citigroup served as placement agents for the offering. The stock hit its 52-week high of $6.54 on April 1. Write to Chris Wack at chris.
