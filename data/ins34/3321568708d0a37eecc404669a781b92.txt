TAMPA, FL, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The following is a statement issued by Dave Kanen, who serves as Managing Director at Kanen Wealth Management: Dear Lazydays Inc. Board, Approximately ten weeks have passed since your ill-advised, reflex rejection of B. Riley's $25 per share bid for our company. Since that time, you have not articulated a value creation plan for our enterprise as a continuing entity. You have not repurchased a sufficient quantity of shares to meaningfully grow EPS. You have not run a formal strategic alternatives process.
