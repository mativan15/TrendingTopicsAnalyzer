Roshud Pree, 26, a resident at the Motel 6 on Main Street in Barstow was arrested on suspicion of committing an armed robbery at the pictured Motel 6 across town on Yucca Avenue.

A 26-year-old man living at Motel 6 in Barstow was arrested on suspicion of committing an armed robbery at another Motel 6 across town.

Barstow Police officials reported that at approximately 8:52 a.m. on Monday, officers responded to the suspected robbery at the motel on Yucca Avenue.

Officers and detectives responded to the scene and learned that a man had entered the motel, pointed a handgun at the clerk, and demanded money.
