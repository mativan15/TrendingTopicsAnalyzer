Leicestershire A5 delays cost £17,000 a day, transport body claims

Image source, Sweet TreatsImage caption, The road is home to one of the country's most bashed bridgesDelays on a Leicestershire road are costing the economy more than £17,000 every day, a transport body has said.Figures from Midlands Connect suggest hold-ups on the A5 are costing commuters and HGV drivers time that equates - using a government formula - to thousands of pounds.The body said the cost related to all drivers on the road, each day.The borough council said it had been campaigning for more than a decade for improvements.
