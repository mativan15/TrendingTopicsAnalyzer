Pacific's delicate dance with China is fraught with danger. Australia must reclaim the floor
brisbanetimes.com.au
politics
2022-05-29 19:00:00

Our nation became a federation in 1901 partly as strategic strengthening at a time France and Germany were claiming islands in the south Pacific. Feeling secure in the southwest Pacific from Timor to Fiji – the Melanesian arc – has always been our first-order strategic necessity. Yet the concept seems forgotten. The word 'Melanesia' does not appear once in any White Papers.There is a danger that in our grand narrative sweep of 'Indo-Pacific', we may not see the wood for the trees; that is, the need to zero in on the core region to our immediate north at a time when our strategic choices are now very consequential.
