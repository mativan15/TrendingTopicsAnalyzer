BENSALEM, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--Law Offices of Howard G. Smith announces an investigation on behalf of Novartis AG ('Novartis' or the 'Company') (NYSE: NVS ) investors concerning the Company's possible violations of federal securities laws.On May 5, 2022, Novartis 'announced a temporary, voluntary suspension of production at its radioligand therapy production sites in Ivrea, Italy and Millburn, New Jersey.' The production halt was to 'address potential quality issues identified in its manufacturing process.
