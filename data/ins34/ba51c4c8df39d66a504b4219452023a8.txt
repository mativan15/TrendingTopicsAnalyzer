Readers' poll: Is it time for a general election?
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

The government was in disarray last week after the prime minister dismissed her chancellor, Kwasi Kwarteng. Critics, including some within her own party, say that Liz Truss - who has been in No 10 for just 36 days - no longer has the political and economic credibility required to lead the country.Many argue that only a general election can now provide a PM who has the mandate and authority to lead the UK through difficult times, and say the only honourable course for Truss is to go to the country.
