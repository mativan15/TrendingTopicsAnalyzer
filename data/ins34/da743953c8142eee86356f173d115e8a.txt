They were literally threatened with death by chocolate. 
An Australian family is on high alert after an anonymous neighbor threatened to poison the family dogs with chocolate. A video detailing the creepy correspondence is going viral as the petrified fam waits for the pooch-hating terrorist to strike. 
Brisbane couple Anthony and Jessica Tuite had reportedly received an anonymous note threatening to kill their two dogs — a black Great Dane called Barney and a brown Dane-Ridgeback crossbreed named Donnie — because they were too noisy, 7News reported.
