The AP news staff was not involved in its creation.KANSAS CITY, Mo., May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Fifteen ambitious high school students from across the country will each receive a $10,000 scholarship as the 2022 winners of H&R Block's Children of Associates Premier Scholarship (CAPS) program. These young adults' accomplishments extend beyond academic excellence, as they have each served their communities by solving relevant and timely problems in innovative ways.
