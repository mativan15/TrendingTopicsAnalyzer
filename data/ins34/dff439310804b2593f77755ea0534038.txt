Opportunities for freshers rise 3x in HY2 as India Inc looks to hire new blood
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 16:00:00

India Inc's latest hires are predominantly freshers, according to new report. The study goes on to point out that there has been a threefold increase in employment opportunities for freshers in HY2. According to the TeamLease EdTech Career Outlook Report, freshers are being increasingly hired in a bid to keep up with technological and digital advancements. In addition, attracting and retaining talent has emerged as a top priority for many leading companies. 
 "There is a substantial increase in job openings for freshers, but many demand updated skills and experience due to the accelerated rate of tech and digital advancements globally.
