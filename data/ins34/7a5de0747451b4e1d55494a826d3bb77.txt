[Photo/Xinhua] The report delivered at the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China in Beijing on Sunday points out that the nation must fully and faithfully apply the new development philosophy on all fronts, continue reforms to develop the socialist market economy, promote high-standard opening-up, and accelerate efforts to foster a new pattern of development that is focused on the domestic economy and features positive interplay between domestic and international economic flows.
