Treasurer Jim Chalmers says the forecast cost of the stage 3 tax cuts has risen and will now cost the nation around $254 billion over 10 years.The stage 3 tax cuts were supposed to cost around $243 billionTreasurer Jim Chalmers says the tax cuts will not come into effect for a few years and the budget has other prioritiesDespite a worsening economic outlook, the government appears set to leave these tax cuts unchangedFrom July 2024 anyone earning between $45,000 and $200,000 will be taxed at 30 per cent, while those earning over the highest bracket will have their tax rate set at 45 per cent.
