AgenciesRepresentative ImageJawaharlal Nehru Port Authority (JNPA) is expecting Rs 8,000-10,000 crore investment from private and public entities in its multi-product Special Economic Zone project, its chairman Sanjay Sethi on Friday said. The SEZ is an initiative under the Sagarmala national flagship policy of the shipping ministry, aimed at boosting exports, generating employment, investment and captive cargo by enabling port-led industrialisation. The Special Economic Zone (SEZ), the country's first port-based and port-led facility, has been operational since July last year.
