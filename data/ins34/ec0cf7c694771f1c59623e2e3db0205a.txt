James Parr: the Kiwi CEO searching the universe for signs of life
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-10-15 16:00:00

As a Lower Hutt schoolboy James Parr won a scholarship to Nasa summer camp. Worried he wouldn't fit in, he turned it down. Three decades later, he works alongside the space agency as chief executive of his own space-tech company, which spans the UK, US and Australia. A warm-voiced 'super space geek', whose company is trying to help save the planet using artificial intelligence to scan space for water, asteroids and extraterrestrial life, Parr now knows his schoolboy assessment was adrift of reality.
