Shah Rukh Khan on Friday unveiled the first look teaser of his upcoming action entertainer with director Atlee, titled 'Jawan'. "An action-packed 2023! Bringing #Jawan to you, an explosive entertainer in cinemas 2nd June 2023," the superstar said as he shared the teaser on his social media handles. The 1 minute 30 seconds clip featured SRK with his head and hands in bandages, in a secret hideout full of machine guns, explosive ammo and some outdated tech to keep him in the loop with the outside world.
