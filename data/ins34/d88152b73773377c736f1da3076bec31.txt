Delhi and Gurugram have evolved audiences: Suchitra Krishnamoorthi
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-04 06:33:51

It's not the first time that singer-actor Suchitra Krishnamoorthi will take to the stage to showcase her theatrical prowess. But during the time that she couldn't perform on the stage — like other artistes, who were forced to stay away from live shows due to the pandemic — she only thought how to come back with a bang! 'The pandemic was specifically a hard time for live performers,' recalls Krishnamoorthi, who is till date popular among Bollywood lovers for playing the female lead in Kabhi Haan Kabhi Naa (1994).
