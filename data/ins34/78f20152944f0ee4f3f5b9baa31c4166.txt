There are plenty of tough lineup decisions for fantasy football managers to make in a given week. Now, there are likely even more considering we've reached the point in the NFL season in which some teams will be out of action.

The pool of available players has gotten smaller for Week 6, as the Detroit Lions, Houston Texans, Las Vegas Raiders and Tennessee Titans all have byes this week. If you've been relying on a player or two (or even more) from these teams, you may be contemplating several others as potential fill-ins.
