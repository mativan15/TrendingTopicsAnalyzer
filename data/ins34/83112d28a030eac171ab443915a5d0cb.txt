IL&FS House in Mumbai's Chandivali suburb, owned by IL&FS Securities Services, is spread over 0.4 acre with total built up office space of 28,734 sq ft comprising of a basement, ground floor and 6 upper floors.After selling its iconic headquarters in Mumbai's business district, crisis-hit non-banking finance company Infrastructure Leasing & Financial Services (IL&FS) has put more commercial properties including two standalone prime properties Business Arcade and IL&FS House in Mumbai on the block.
