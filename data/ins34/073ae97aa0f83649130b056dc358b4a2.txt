From fishermen to pensioners, food clubs to families - Sky News brings you a series of reports this week from the maritime city of Plymouth as the cost of living continues to rise.In our second report, we investigate how increasing prices are impacting business.
It is a city built on its maritime fortune.Plymouth accounts for nearly 10% of England's entire marine industry and is home to the largest naval base in western Europe. Some 13.2% of England's fish caught are landed here.But it is clear that as the cost of living continues to rise, those working on the harbourside are feeling the increase just as much as those at home.
