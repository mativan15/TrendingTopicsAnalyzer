By Todd Feurer October 18, 2022 / 12:31 PM / CBS Chicago CHICAGO (CBS) -- One month after he was acquitted of all charges accusing him of helping R. Kelly rig his 2008 child pornography trial, attorneys for the singer's former business manager are asking a judge to award Derrel McDavid $850,000 in legal fees.McDavid's attorney filed a motion Tuesday asking U.S. District Judge Harry Leinenweber to order the federal government to foot the bill for McDavid's legal fees, arguing the decision to prosecute him in the first place "was vexatious, frivolous, and in bad faith.
