Gorman-Rupp completes acquisition of Fill-Rite

The Gorman-Rupp Company, a leading designer, manufacturer, and international marketer of pumps and pump systems, has completed its previously announced acquisition of Fill-Rite and Sotera ('Fill-Rite'), a division of the Tuthill Corporation, for $525 million. When adjusted for approximately $80 million in expected tax benefits, the net transaction value is approximately $445 million. Gorman-Rupp funded the transaction with cash-on-hand and new debt.
