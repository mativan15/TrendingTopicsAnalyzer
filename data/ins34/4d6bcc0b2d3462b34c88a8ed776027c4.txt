October 19, 2022 06:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time GUANGZHOU, China--(BUSINESS WIRE)--XPeng Inc. ("XPENG" or the "Company," NYSE: XPEV and HKEX: 9868), a leading Chinese smart EV company, will host its 2022 TECH DAY event on Monday, October 24, 2022. An English-language replay will be available on Tuesday, October 25, 2022.

At this year's 1024 TECH DAY, XPENG will present its latest developments in autonomous driving technology and mobility ecosystem, including its intelligent EVs, flying vehicles and robotics.
