Cardinal Capital invests €30m in Northern Ireland kitchens company
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-17 00:00:00

Dublin investment group Cardinal Capital has taken a majority stake in Uform, a Northern Ireland-based manufacturer of kitchen doors and furniture, for about €30 million, it is understood.The investment by Cardinal Ireland Partners, a fund managed by Cardinal, will be used to boost capacity at Uform's facilities in Toomebridge Antrim and also at a plant in Donegal run by its sister company Andoras, which trades as Aishling Furniture. The group employs 415 staff.Eamon and Paul Donnelly, who cofounded the business in 1993 with their late father Eddie Donnelly, will remain onboard as 'significant' shareholders.
