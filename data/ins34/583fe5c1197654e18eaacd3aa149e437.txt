Berlusconi says he 'reconnected' with Putin – DW – 10/19/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-19 09:37:24

In a secretly taped speech, the former Italian premier said he exchanged alcoholic gifts and "sweet" letters with the Russian leader. Berlusconi's Forza Italia party was one of the key winners in the recent election.Former Italian Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi has said he "reconnected" with Russian President Vladimir Putin, according to a secretly taped speech obtained by Italy's LaPresse news agency. Berlusconi's comments have caused a stir among the Italian media and public, raising concerns that Italy's possible new governing coalition could grow closer to Moscow.
