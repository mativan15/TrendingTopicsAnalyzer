Morning Drive with Christie LiveJun 1, 2022Johnny Depp is the clear winner in his defamation trial against ex-wife Amber Heard. A Virginia jury agreed with Depp that Heard acted with actual malice and agreed with every one of Depp's defamation counts against Heard. He was awarded 15-million dollars. The jury also found in favor of Heard in some parts of her 100-million-dollar-countersuit against Depp, but only awarded her two-million dollars. Depp sued Heard for 50-million over an op-ed piece she wrote in The Washington Post about domestic violence.
