FY22 trade gap with China swells to $72.9 billion
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-29 20:00:00

iStockRepresentative ImageIndia's trade deficit with China widened to $72.9 billion in FY22 as imports surged to $94.16 billion, 44.3% more than the previous fiscal year as well as the pre-pandemic year of FY20, official data showed. India imported $29.89 billion of electrical machinery, equipment and sound recorders, $12.49 billion of organic chemicals, and $19.83 billion of boilers and mechanical appliances in the last fiscal year, with these imports up a total 40% on-year. In FY21, India's imports from China amounted to $65.
