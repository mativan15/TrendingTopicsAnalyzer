NI technology sector 'thriving' despite pandemic

2022-05-29 05:28:41

By Ross McKeeBBC News NIImage caption, Catalyst has three innovation hubs across Northern Ireland, including one in Belfast's Titanic Quarter (above)Technology leaders in Northern Ireland believe there is a "huge opportunity" for the development of the industry as it emerges from the pandemic.New share investment in companies based in Northern Ireland was more than £100m in 2021, according to Catalyst.This compares with about £5m of venture capital investment in 2014, and just over £44m in 2020.Catalyst says that despite the challenges of the past two years, the tech industry is "thriving".
