CPI-M was instrumental in driving out Tata Motors from Bengal's Singur: Mamata Banerjee
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 08:45:00

Ruling out her role in driving out Tata Motors from Singur, West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee said on Wednesday CPI-M was instrumental in driving out Tata Motors from Bengal's Singur and she had no role in it, creating political debate among the opposition."CPI-M had forcefully acquired land for the Singur project which led Tatas to leave Bengal," Banerjee claimed, while speaking in a programme in Siliguri. "People are spreading wrong words about me that I have driven out Tata Motors from Bengal.
