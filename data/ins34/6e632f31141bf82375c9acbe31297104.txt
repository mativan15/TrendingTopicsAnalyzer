South Bengaluru BJP head demands Steel flyover to be named after Puneeth Rajkumar
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-17 11:26:00

Even after his demise, the legacy of Puneeth Rajkumar remains peerless. To honor Puneeth Rajkumar, the government named the Nayandahalli 100 ft road in Puneeth's name. In the same way, many places in Karnataka have named the roads in the name of Puneeth. Now, according to the latest reports, in Bengaluru Shivananda Circle, the newly constructed Steel Bridge flyover is in the process of being named after Puneeth. The request is being submitted to BBMP by South Bengaluru BJP Head NR Ramesh.Around Shivanand Circle, the head office of Karnataka Film Chamber is situated.
