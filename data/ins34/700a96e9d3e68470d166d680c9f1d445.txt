This week luxury global fashion marketplace Farfetch and leading global Web3 accelerator Outlier Ventures announced the first cohort of startups in their 12 week Dream Assembly Base Camp accelerator program. The eight companies—altr, Curie, iiNDYVERSE, METAV.RS, Mintouge, Reblium, SKNUPS and WearNFT—are focused on defining the future of Web3 luxury commerce. The program taking in luxury commerce, token design & economics, product roadmap, NFT & community strategy will be supported by leading figures in the gaming, retail, marketing, talent management and venture capital.
