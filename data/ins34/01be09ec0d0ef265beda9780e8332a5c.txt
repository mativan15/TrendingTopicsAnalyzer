Apple, Spotify should 'stop working' with Kanye West, says super-agent Ari Emanuel
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 16:38:00

Entertainment industry super-agent Ari Emanuel is asking businesses to stop working with Kanye West after the artist's recent, controversial comments about Jewish people. 'Apple AAPL, +0.37% and Spotify SPOT, -0.34%, which host West's music, whoever organizes West's tours, and Adidas ADS, -0.76%, which collaborates with West on his fashion line, should all stop working with him,' Emanuel, co-CEO of the media and talent agency Endeavor, wrote in a Wednesday op-ed for the Financial Times. ''Those who continue to do business with West are giving his misguided hate an audience.
