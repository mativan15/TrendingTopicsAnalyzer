EU nation's Facebook page likens sanctions to bomb
russiaherald.com
news
2022-10-17 11:45:00

The Hungarian government posted the message against the restrictions on Moscow after polling citizens on their viewsBudapest has issued a condemnation of the EU's sanctions on Russia, posting an image on its official government Facebook page likening the economic measures to a bomb and warning that Brussels' financial warfare against Moscow will "destroy" Hungary.The graphic, which features a warhead emblazoned with the Hungarian word for 'sanctions' and the message "Brussels sanctions will destroy us," was added on Saturday to accompany the government's latest national consultation survey, which asked citizens whether they agreed with Budapest's stated opposition to EU sanctions on Russia.
