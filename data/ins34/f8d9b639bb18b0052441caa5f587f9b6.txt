Twelve people killed in gun attack on bar in central Mexico
indiatimes.com
world
2022-10-16 06:45:00

GUANAJUATO , Mexico : Twelve people were killed Saturday in a shooting attack in a bar in the city of Irapuato , in the central Mexican state of Guanajuato, the municipal government said.Local authorities "confirmed the death of six men and six women" as well as three injured people in a statement, which did not specify the identity of attackers or their motives.
