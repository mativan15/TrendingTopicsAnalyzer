Access Development Teams with LuckyDiem to Reward Cash-Loving Travelers "With Access, we now can surprise and delight our audience not just where they shop but where they travel as well," said Andrew Landis, CEO and founder of LuckyDiem. SALT LAKE CITY (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 Loyalty discount leader Access Development today had three words for the users of LuckyDiem's consumer rewards app: pack your bags. The two companies have signed an agreement making Access Development's cash-back travel platform available to all LuckyDiem shoppers, extending the company's expansive domestic presence.
