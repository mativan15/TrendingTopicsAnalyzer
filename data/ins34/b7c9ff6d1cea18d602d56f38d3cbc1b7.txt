TULSA, Okla., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- AAON, Inc. (NASDAQ: AAON) ('AAON' or the 'Company'), a leader in innovation and production of premium-quality, high-performance, and energy-efficient HVAC products for commercial, and industrial buildings, releases its 2021 Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Report. In this report, the company highlights its commitment to uphold and continually improve overall sustainability practices, further the Company's research and development to lead the industry in innovative solutions, and achieve its long-term environmental targets.
