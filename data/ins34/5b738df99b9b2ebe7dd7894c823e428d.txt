These restaurateurs bravely opened new eateries amid COVID pandemic: 'Determined' to succeed
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-10-19 02:31:54

The COVID-19 pandemic — so deadly for so many — proved a perfect time to open a new restaurant for a select group of entrepreneurs who bravely chose "hope" over fear.  "One way or the other, we were determined to succeed," celebrated Boston-area restaurateur Leo Keka told FOX Business.He audaciously opened a sprawling 7,500-square-foot Mediterranean steakhouse, Alba on 53, in Hanover, Massachusetts, in August 2020, while the state was crippled by executive mandates and many consumers were gripped by fear.
