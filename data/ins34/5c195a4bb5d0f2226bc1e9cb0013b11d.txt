Foodbank charity launches urgent winter appeal as it faces "tsunami of need"

Fears are growing that stock could run low as the Trussell Trust braces for its "worst winter yet"The Trussell Trust appealed for donations amid a spike in demandDesperate foodbanks appealed for donations tonight as demand from hungry families outstripped supply for the first time. Britain's biggest foodbank network, the Trussell Trust, warned of a 'tsunami of need' this winter - sparking fears volunteers could run out of food to hand to hard-up households. The charity gave away 46% more emergency parcels in August and September than the same months last year.
