ET Canada Megan Thee Stallion is safe after her home was broken into. After TMZ reported that two men broke into the 27-year-old rapper's Los Angeles home on Thursday night, Megan took to Twitter to address the situation.'Material things can be replaced but I'm glad everyone is safe,' Megan tweeted.Megan's social media post came after law enforcement sources told the outlet that two men broke in to the rapper's home and took more than 300,000 in jewellery, cash and electronics.
