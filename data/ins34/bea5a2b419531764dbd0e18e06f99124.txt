October 20, 2022 02:33 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Rep. Dan Newhouse (R-Wash.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Rep. Newhouse has a lifetime rating of 82 percent, making him a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero.'
