SoundOut Launches OnBrand

LONDON, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- SoundOut, the world leader in sonic testing, has launched a revolutionary AI-powered music search and testing SaaS platform named OnBrand. It enables marketers to build certainty into every music choice for campaigns. OnBrand is powered by AI algorithms that predict the granular emotional impact of music, trained on feedback from half a million people. OnBrand enables marketers to search across any number of music catalogues to identify campaign music that is both on-brand and campaign appropriate, using a combination of over 200 brand attributes, plus self-defined brand personality and brand archetypes.
