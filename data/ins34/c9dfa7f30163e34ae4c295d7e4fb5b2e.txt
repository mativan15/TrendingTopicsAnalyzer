For many entrepreneurs, experience can have a major impact on the way they conduct business. Whether it's changing a marketing strategy after an unexpected failure or pivoting a product after getting more acquainted with their target audience's needs, leaders must constantly evolve alongside their businesses if they're to succeed long term. This can also mean changing the way they interact with their customers, their employees and their peers on a regular basis. As business leaders themselves, these 10 members of Young Entrepreneur Council have evolved in their own ways over the course of their careers.
