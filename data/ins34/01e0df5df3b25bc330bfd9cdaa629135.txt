Published: 11:15 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 11:15 EDT, 19 October 2022 Wake Forest coach Steve Forbes quickly built success for a long-struggling program. The test now is sustaining that in a time of transfers and roster turnover. Forbes led his second team to a 25-win season and nearly secured only the program's second NCAA Tournament bid since 2010. But that success leaned heavily on a pair of transfers - one who unexpectedly blossomed into the Atlantic Coast Conference player of the year - who have departed.
