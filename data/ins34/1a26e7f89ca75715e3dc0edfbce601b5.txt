Image source, Anthony O'Neil/GeographImage caption, Claridge's Hotel in London's Mayfair said the candle firm had infringed its copyrightThe owner of a candle firm has agreed to change its name after a Mayfair hotel asked a judge to hand her a jail sentence for infringing its trademark.Claridge's Hotel Ltd said Denise Shepherd, who ran a business called Claridge Candles Ltd, had breached a judge's order not to infringe a trademark and was in contempt of court. The High Court was told on Friday Ms Shepherd had agreed to rename the firm.
