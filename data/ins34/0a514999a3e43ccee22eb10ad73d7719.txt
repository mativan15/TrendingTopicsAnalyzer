Plenty of people have complained that this Toronto election campaign is boring.

Friends, I'm here to tell you there are worse things that can happen to a place than boring politics. Like entertaining politics, for one.

I just came from three years living in and writing about the U.S., where the body politic has been amusing itself to death over the past decade, leading to literal battles in the streets and to a situation where the very continued existence of a functioning democratic system is now in serious doubt.
