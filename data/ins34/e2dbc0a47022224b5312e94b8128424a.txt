The past year for Knights Group Holdings (LON:KGH) investors has not been profitable
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 05:14:41

As every investor would know, you don't hit a homerun every time you swing. But serious investors should think long and hard about avoiding extreme losses. So spare a thought for the long term shareholders of Knights Group Holdings plc (LON:KGH); the share price is down a whopping 84% in the last twelve months. While some investors are willing to stomach this sort of loss, they are usually professionals who spread their bets thinly. Notably, shareholders had a tough run over the longer term, too, with a drop of 79% in the last three years.
