NEW YORK, May 27, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ewout Steenbergen, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of S&P Global (NYSE: SPGI), will participate in the 42nd Annual William Blair Growth Stock Conference on June 7, 2022. Mr. Steenbergen is scheduled to speak from 1:40 p.m. to 2:10 p.m. (Eastern Daylight Time). The "fireside chat" will be webcast and may include forward-looking information.
Webcast Instructions:  Live and Replay The webcast will be available live and in replay through the Company's Investor Relations website http://investor.
