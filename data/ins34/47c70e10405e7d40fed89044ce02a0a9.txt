Dharavi redevelopment project: Maharashtra govt, railways ink pact
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 10:30:00

After much-delay, the Dharavi Redevelopment Authority (DRA) and the Indian Railways signed a "definitive agreement" for the redevelopment of Asia's biggest slum colony in Mumbai, reported TOI. With this agreement, the DRA handed 47.5 acres of railway land in Dadar for the Dharavi redevelopment project.

After a meeting with Union Railway Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw, Deputy Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis tweeted that the development would pave the way for fast -tracking the mega project.
