Newton Little League to host first annual Corsi Day honoring late Red Sox pitcher Jim Corsi

news
2022-06-02 16:23:29

Newton Little League will present Family FunFest and the First Annual 'Corsi Day' on Saturday, June 4, at Albemarle Field from 2:30 to 4:30 p.m.  Honoring the late Newton native and Red Sox pitcher Jim Corsi, the event aims to raise money for the Dana Farber Cancer Institute. It will feature baseball and softball games, a dunk tank, and a home run derby.  Wally and Tessy the Red Sox 'Green Monsters' will be on hand for photographs, and free food and ice cream will be provided to any player in uniform.
