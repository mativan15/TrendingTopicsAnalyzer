If you're a bargain-minded investor, there's certainly no shortage of stocks priced at a discount right now. Last month was more or less a breakeven for the broad market thanks to last week's big bounce. For more than a few tickers though, the selling didn't ease.
Before simply plowing into even the best of these beaten-down names from the S&P 500 index just because they're beaten down, take a moment, take a step back, and take a breath. This is no longer the simple "buy-on-the-dlp" environment we all came to know and love for the better part of 2021.
