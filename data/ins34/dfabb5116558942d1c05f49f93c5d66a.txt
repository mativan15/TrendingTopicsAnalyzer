16 October 2022 by Rafaela Sales Ross 
The Playlist It's been quite the year for films that look for inspiration in the autobiographical. James Gray returned to his childhood in Queens for 'Armageddon Time,' Steven Spielberg reflected on the origins of his love for cinema in 'The Fabelmans,' and Alejandro González Iñárritu investigated the pained ripples of diaspora and guilt in 'Bardo: False Chronicle of a Handful of Truths.' In 2022, one more semi-autobiographical film will grace the screens, Gary Oldman's BAFTA-winning directorial debut 'Nil By Mouth.
