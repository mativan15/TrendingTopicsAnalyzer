Iranian prison burns amid protests (VIDEOS)
RT
news
2022-10-15 19:58:10

Evin Prison in the Iranian capital Tehran caught fire on Saturday night, with video footage showing smoke and flames rising from the detention center. The blaze broke out amid widespread anti-government protests, which have been ongoing for several weeks.Videos shared on social media showed the prison compound burning, as an alarm droned and what sounded like gunshots rang out. Several loud explosions were heard as the prison burned.Iran's IRNA news agency said that the fire broke out following a brawl between inmates and guards, Al Arabiya reported.
