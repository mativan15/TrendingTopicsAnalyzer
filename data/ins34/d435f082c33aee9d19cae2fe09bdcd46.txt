Fast food restaurant Burger King is giving out Royale sandwiches for better than half price to mark the Platinum Jubilee of Queen Elizabeth II, and all you have to do is use an appThe Royale normally costs around £5 - but this week it's just £1.99 Burger King is selling its Chicken and Vegan Royales for £1.99 all week - as a cheeky nod to the Queen's Platinum Jubilee . The Burger King sandwiches contain breaded chicken or vegan chicken with lettuce and mayo in a sesame seed bun, and normally cost around £4.
