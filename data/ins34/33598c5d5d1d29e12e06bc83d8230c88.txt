World Bank to disburse USD 700 million to crisis-hit Sri Lanka: Report
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 19:30:00

AFPSri Lanka is nearing bankruptcy and has severe shortages of essentials from food, fuel, medicines and cooking gas.The World Bank is planning to disburse USD 700 million to crisis-ridden Sri Lanka by re-purposing its existing loans, a move that will help the island nation grappling with an unprecedented economic crisis much-needed breathing space till a bailout package with the IMF is worked out, media reports said on Monday. Sri Lanka is nearing bankruptcy and has severe shortages of essentials from food, fuel, medicines and cooking gas.
