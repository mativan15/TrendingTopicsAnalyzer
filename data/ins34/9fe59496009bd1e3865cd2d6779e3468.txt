Chad police fire tear gas at pro-democracy protests
reuters.com
news
2022-10-20 08:57:42

N'DJAMENA, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Security forces in Chad on Thursday used force to disperse a banned protest calling for a quicker transition to democratic rule, firing tear gas at crowds and causing the death of a journalist, a Reuters reporter said.The vast, military-led Central African nation has been on edge since the sudden death of President Idriss Deby while visiting troops fighting rebels in April 2021.There has been resistance to a transitional military council headed by Deby's son, who took power after the president's death and pushed back elections to October 2024.
