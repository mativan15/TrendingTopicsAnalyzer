Orange County romance scammer to plead guilty

By CBSLA Staff October 17, 2022 / 5:30 AM / CBS Los Angeles An Irvine man is set to plead guilty Monday to federal charges for conning more than $1 million from former romantic partners and others and using their personal information to obtain credit.Ze'Shawn Stanley Campbell, 35, has agreed to plead guilty to one federal count each of wire fraud and unlawful monetary transactions, according to the U.S. Attorney's Office.Campbell, who in recent years has lived in several cities in Los Angeles and Orange counties, will face up to 30 years in federal prison at sentencing, prosecutors noted.
