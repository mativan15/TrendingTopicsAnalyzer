Published: 03:58 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 03:58 EDT, 30 May 2022 Manchester United target Pau Torres has distanced himself from a possible move to the Premier League - after claiming 'there is nothing' agreed over a summer deal.After several reports last season that Torres was on his way out of the club, the defender chose to stay in Spain to play Champions League football - something United couldn't offer.Despite Erik ten Hag's interest in the defender, Torres has been quick to refute any suggestions he's agreed a deal in Manchester.
