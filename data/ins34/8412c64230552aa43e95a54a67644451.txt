Sunday, 3:25 p.m. CT, CBS
Everyone's had this Bills-Chiefs game circled on their calendar in a rematch of last year's AFC divisional thriller, where Kansas City emerged victorious 42-36 in overtime. Now, Buffalo will have a chance to exact revenge as two of the league's best quarterbacks in Josh Allen and Patrick Mahomes go head-to-head. This game also provides a chance to see another Bears opponent, as Chicago will host the Bills on Christmas Eve.
