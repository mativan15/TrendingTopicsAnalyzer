Embassy REIT delivers another strong quarter with 1.6 million square feet total lease in Q2FY23

2022-10-20 12:30:00

Getty ImagesRepresentative imageEmbassy Office Parks REIT has leased 1.6 million sq ft across 27 deals, including 5 lakh sq ft pre-commitment from ANZ at Embassy TechVillage in June quarter. The REIT has added 15 new high-growth occupiers in sectors like cloud, cybersecurity, renewables, and healthcare tech and has also increased customer base.

It has achieved 22% positive leasing spreads on 1.6 million sq ft and 14% rent escalations on 2.7 million sq ft. During the quarter, the REIT grew net operating income by13% from a year ago to Rs 703.
