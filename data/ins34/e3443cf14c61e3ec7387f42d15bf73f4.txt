Indiana backup QB Jack Tuttle plans to transfer after season
espn.com
news
2022-10-17 16:53:00

12:53 PM ETBLOOMINGTON, Ind. -- Indiana backup quarterback Jack Tuttle announced Monday that he will transfer at the end of this season.Coach Tom Allen confirmed the development at the start of his weekly news conference, explaining that the two spoke last week and that Tuttle, a team captain, would remain No. 2 on the depth chart.The 23-year-old Tuttle has one year of eligibility remaining."We had an open, honest talk about his time here and he has a desire to want to play his final year," Allen said.
