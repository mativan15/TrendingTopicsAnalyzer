Labour blocks former Kensington MP Emma Dent Coad from running next election
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-17 14:13:18

Labour has blocked the former Kensington MP Emma Dent Coad from the shortlist to fight the seat at the next election, a move which has sparked outrage among local campaigners.Dent Coad, who was elected in 2017 in a shock victory over the Conservatives by just 20 votes, is the leader of the Labour group on the council, and lost her seat in 2019 to the Conservatives.The former MP, who was a member of the Socialist Campaign Group, the Corbyn-loyal wing of the parliamentary Labour party, was the target of a number of stories in the tabloids during her time as an MP.
