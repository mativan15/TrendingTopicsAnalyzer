What degree program did you complete at Lone Star College?

I graduated with an Associate of Applied Science in Instrumentation Technology degree from Lone Star College. The degree covers the ins and outs of DC and AC electricity and the particulars of various devices used in oil & gas and chemical production, including valves, pressure sensors, pressure transducers, temperature sensors, etc.
