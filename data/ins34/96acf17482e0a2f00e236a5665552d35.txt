By Reuters Published: 22:45 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 22:45 EDT, 16 October 2022 BENGALURU, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Indian shares may open lower on Monday, taking cues from weak Asian peers and losses on Wall Street amid heightened nervousness over the U.S. Federal Reserve's aggressive rate hike path, while the domestic earnings season kicks off in earnest. India's NSE stock futures, listed on the Singapore exchange , were down 0.80%, as of 0200 GMT, while MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan fell 1.
