PAWS offers $25 cat adoptions this weekend, highlights 2 looking for homes
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-04 12:00:36

Due to an influx of feline rescues, the Panama Animal Welfare Society (PAWS) is offering $25 cat adoptions through Sunday. This fee includes spaying or neutering, microchipping, vaccines and preventative medications.

The shelter is highlighting two of the cats available for adoption.

Azalea, 1-year-old domestic short-haired cat

Azalea, the PAWS cat of the week

Meet Azalea, a spayed female cat looking for a forever home. A resident at the PAWS shelter since Jan. 29, Azalea is described as sweet and calm.
