NDP leader Wab Kinew speaks after introducing many of the candidates his party will be running in the next provincial election, slated for October 2023. (Ian Froese/CBC)Manitoba NDP Leader Wab Kinew said his party is ready for the next provincial election — and he has the candidates to prove it.During his speech to party faithful on Friday, Kinew brought on stage most of the 36 candidates the NDP has already nominated for the election scheduled for Oct. 2023."There's a lot of speculation about a potential early election.
