 The rupee depreciated 12 paise to close at 77.66 (provisional) against the U.S. dollar on May 31, tracking a negative trend in domestic equities and surging crude oil prices.At the interbank foreign exchange market, the rupee opened lower at 77.65 against the greenback and finally settled at 77.66, down 12 paise over its previous close.During the session, the rupee touched an intra-day low of 77.70 and a high of 77.62.On May 30, the rupee settled at 77.
