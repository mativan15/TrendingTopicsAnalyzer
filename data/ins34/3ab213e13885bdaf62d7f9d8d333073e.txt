As people comes to grips with climate change imperatives, the central question is how we can transition fast enough to zero waste. Corinne Sawers and Eric Lonergan have some pragmatic answers, which they've laid out in their co-written book, Supercharge Me, a manual on how to speed up the transition to net zero. Here are some highlights from their insightful conversation with Tom Raftery, global vice president, futurist, and innovation evangelist at SAP during a recent Climate 21 podcast. To make achieving zero waste less daunting, experts suggest breaking it down into problems that existing affordable technology can solve, those that require further technology development, and mass behavioral change.
