Pierce Brosnan is honoring his wife Keely Shaye Smith with a simple yet powerful statement.The actor revealed that he wore his real wedding ring while filming his upcoming movie "Black Adam" as a tribute to his wife of 21 years. Brosnan plays Dr. Fate in the superhero flick."This is our wedding ring and this is a watch that Keely bought me many years ago with an inscription that [reads], 'Time flies on love's wings,'" he told Entertainment Tonight. "I decided to wear it for this character. It seemed appropriate.
