Internet City, Dubai--(Newsfile Corp. - October 20, 2022) - LBank Exchange, a global digital asset trading platform, will list ONDA Token (ONDA) on October 21, 2022. For all users of LBank Exchange, the ONDA/USDT trading pair will be officially available for trading at 5:00 UTC on October 21, 2022.
