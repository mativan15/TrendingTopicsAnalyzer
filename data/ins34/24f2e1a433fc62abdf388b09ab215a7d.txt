Info Edge shares gain 3.51% as Sensex rises
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 06:45:00

Shares of the Info Edge (India) Ltd. traded at Rs 3823.15 on BSE at 11:27AM (IST) on Monday, up 3.51 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 3314.0 and a high of Rs 7462.95. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 3.82 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 990.1 per share and 3.37 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 17,272 shares changed hands on the counter till 11:27AM (IST). The stock commands a market value of Rs 48702.
