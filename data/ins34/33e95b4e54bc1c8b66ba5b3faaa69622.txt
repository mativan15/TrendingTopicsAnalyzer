RBI launches annual survey on foreign liabilities, assets of mutual funds, AMCs
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 05:15:00

The Reserve Bank of India has launched the 2021-22 round of its annual survey on "Foreign Liabilities and Assets of Mutual Funds and Asset Management Companies", the central bank said in a statement on Thursday. The survey collects information from mutual fund companies and asset management companies on their external financial liabilities and assets as at the end of March of the latest financial year. The survey results are released in the public domain besides being used for compilation of balance of payments statistics and other uses.
