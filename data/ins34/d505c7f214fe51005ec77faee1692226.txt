Several NVIDIA Corporation (NASDAQ:NVDA) reacted to the chipmaker's first-quarter report with lower price targets. Here's what they had to say. The NVIDIA Analysts: BMO analyst Ambrish Srivastava has an Outperform rating and lowered the price target from $375 to $300. Wells Fargo analyst Aaron Rakers has an Overweight rating and $250 price target. Mizuho Securities analyst Vijay Rakesh has a Buy rating and lowered the price target from $345 to $290. Raymond James analyst Chris Caso has a Strong Buy rating and lowered the price target from $365 to $250.
