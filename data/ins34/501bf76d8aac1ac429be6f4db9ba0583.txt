Nestle India retains Zenith for its ₹700-cr media account
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 19:30:00

AgenciesIn April, Nestle had called for a pitch from other agencies, and the two-month process ended with the packaged goods company deciding to retain Zenith. has retained Publicis Groupe-owned media company as its agency of record following a multi-agency pitch that included rivals Mindshare, Dentsu and IPG Mediabrands, according to people in the know. The local unit of Swiss FMCG major Nestle is one of India's largest advertisers, with a media account pegged at over ₹700 crore. It posted a revenue of over ₹15,000 crore in FY22.
