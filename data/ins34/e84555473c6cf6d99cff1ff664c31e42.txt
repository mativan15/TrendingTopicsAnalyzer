October 19, 2022 03:17 PM Eastern Daylight Time IN THE METAVERSE & Wyoming--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Josh Ostrovsky, better known by his Instagram handle @thefatjewish, will host the inaugural Crypties Awards on November 30th at Art Basel Miami, Decrypt Studios, the Web3 production studio, announced today. The Crypties, the first awards ceremony of its kind for the crypto community, honors creative and technical trailblazers who are advancing the industry and bringing Web3 to the mainstream.



The Fat Jewish, an American entrepreneur, radio host, social media personality, author, and plus-size model, burst into the mainstream with his humorous Instagram account in 2013.
