What happened The price of cryptocurrency Waves (CRYPTO: WAVES) has had an exciting week. At the time of writing, the price per token is only up 5.73% over the previous 24 hours. But the price has more than doubled over the past week. And it's likely due to increasing optimism for the Waves ecosystem, which includes stablecoin Neutrino USD (CRYPTO: USDN).
