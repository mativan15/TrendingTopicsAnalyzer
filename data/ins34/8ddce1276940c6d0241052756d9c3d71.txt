Image source, GoogleImage caption, The strike action will affect the Co-op's coffin production facility in GlasgowStaff based at the Co-op's only UK coffin manufacturing factory are set to launch strike action in a pay dispute.Unite said about 50 craft workers at the Co-op Funeralcare facility in Bogmoor Place, Glasgow, had overwhelmingly rejected a pay offer.The strike action is due to run between 31 October and 7 November. Co-op Funeralcare, one of the UK's biggest funeral directors, said the strike would have "no impact on our ability to support bereaved families".
