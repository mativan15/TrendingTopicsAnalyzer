Firework, a startup whose software lets retailers host livestreaming and shoppable videos on their websites, raised a $150 million Series B round led by SoftBank earlier this week.CEO and cofounder Vincent Yang said his tools help retailers use live video to grab sales. Live video will account for $35 billion in US sales by 2024, according to retail analyst firm Coresight Research.Firework's 900 clients, including grocers like Albertsons and Fresh Market, want to build live commerce tools to make their websites more engaging so more people will visit and so they'll be to collect more first-party data, Yang said.
