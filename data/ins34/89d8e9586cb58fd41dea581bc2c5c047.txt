MUMBAI: The Economic Offences Wing of Mumbai police probing a Rs 300 crore foreign remittance by a diamond based company by forging bills of entries, on Tuesday arrested a director of a Hong Kong-based company which received a beneficiary of Rs 19 crores. The banking unit of EOW arrested Harakchand Shah (65) under the IPC sections of cheating, forgery, breach of trust and 120 B of criminal conspiracy.The accused was produced before the 47th Metropolitan Magistrate court which remanded the accused to judicial custody after the defence counsel submitted that the offence is of 2015 and remaining two main accused, Anil Chohara and Suil Chokhara , have been released on anticipatory bail and alleged that there is no justifiable reason for police custody.
