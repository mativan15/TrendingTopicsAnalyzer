TVLine.com Uzo Aduba has lined up her next TV role: The three-time Emmy winner (Orange Is the New Black) is set to star in the Showtime limited series All Her Little Secrets, according to our sister site Deadline.Based on the bestselling novel by Wanda M. Norris, All Her Little Secrets is billed as a fast-paced thriller that stars Aduba as corporate lawyer Ellice Littlejohn. She finds herself embroiled in an affair as well as a shadowy conspiracy that settles on her as its next target.
