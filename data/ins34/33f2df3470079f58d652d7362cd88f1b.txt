Patti LuPone announced her retirement from Broadway and the Actors' Equity Association this week, much to the shock and surprise of everyone.The Broadway legend wrote on Twitter on Monday, 'Quite a week on Broadway, seeing my name being bandied about. Gave up my Equity card; no longer part of that circus. Figure it out.' While her fans were left stumped over the announcement, LuPone later explained to People what prompted her abrupt decision to leave what she's dubbed the 'worst union' after five decades as a due-paying member.
