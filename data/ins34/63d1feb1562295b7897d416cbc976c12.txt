REUTERS/Philippe Wojazer/File PhotoPARIS, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Faurecia (EPED.PA) does not intend to let go of its pollution control business despite the scheduled end of combustion engine sales, as the business will remain profitable for a long time and help reduce debt, the automotive equipment supplier's chief executive told Reuters.
