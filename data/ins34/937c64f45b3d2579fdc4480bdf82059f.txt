MAUMEE, Ohio, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Andersons, Inc. (Nasdaq: ANDE) has been named on Newsweek's list of America's Most Trusted Companies 2022. This prestigious award is presented by Newsweek and Statista Inc., the world-leading statistics portal and industry ranking provider, with the full list available on Newsweek's website.America's Most Trusted Companies 2022 were identified in an independent survey based on a sample of approximately 50,000 U.S. residents who rated companies they know on three key points: customer trust, investor trust, and employee trust.
