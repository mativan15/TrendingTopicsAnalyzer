Vow ASA: Invitation to Vow ASA Q3 2022 trading update
globenewswire.com
business
2022-10-20 06:00:00

Vow ASA will release its trading update for the third quarter of 2022 on Thursday 27 October 2022 at around 07:00 CEST. The update will be published on https://newsweb.oslobors.no/ and on the company's web site www.vowasa.com. At 09:00 CEST on the same day, the company's CEO Henrik Badin will present the results live at Haakon VII's gate 2, 0161 Oslo. Participants are welcome to join in person or via livestream. The streaming link will also be available for replay after the event. The session will be held in English.
