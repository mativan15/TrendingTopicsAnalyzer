India recorded 2,401 fresh COVID-19 cases taking the tally to 4,46,28,828, while the number of active cases of the infection has gone up to 26,625, the Union health ministry revealed on Sunday. As per ministry's data updated at 8 am, the death toll due to the viral disease has risen to 5,28,895 with 21 more fatalities (16 reconciled by Kerala). 
"The active cases account for 0.06 per cent of the total caseload, while the national COVID-19 recovery rate has gone up to 98.76 per cent," the ministry said.
