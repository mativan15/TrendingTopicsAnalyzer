Union Tourism Minister G Kishan Reddy on Tuesday alleged that the TRS government in Telangana has not built double bed room houses for poor as it had promised. He said the Centre had built some four crore houses across the country while in Telangana, houses have not been built for the poor as promised though eight years have passed and added that the centre was ready to give its share. "The Centre has built approximately four crore houses across the country. In neighbouring Andhra Pradesh, 20 lakh houses have been built.
