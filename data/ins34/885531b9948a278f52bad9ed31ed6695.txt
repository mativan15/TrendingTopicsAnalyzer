October 19, 2022 12:01 PM Eastern Daylight Time SANTA MONICA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cornerstone OnDemand, Inc., a leader in talent experience solutions, today announced it has expanded its content portfolio by adding innovative technologies designed to collapse boundaries between content producers and learners, content and skill building. With new AI-powered solutions for content discovery, content curation and integration, Cornerstone is advancing the power of content to drive people growth and support organizations as they develop the skills of the future and drive business transformation.
