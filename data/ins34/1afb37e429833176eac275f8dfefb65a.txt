UnitedHealth Group puts Minnetonka office space up for sublease
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 21:24:00

UnitedHealth Group Inc. (NYSE: UNH) has put one of its Minnetonka offices up for sublease.

The listing is for up to 150,000 square feet in an office building at 12700 Whitewater Drive. The listing was made public earlier this month and is the third large office property occupied by UnitedHealth Group or a subsidiary that's recently hit the leasing market.

The Minnetonka office building, which reaches over 152,000 square feet, was constructed in 1997 and is located on more than 6 acres that are directly east of I-494.
