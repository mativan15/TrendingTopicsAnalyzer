Bluefield Solar Income Raises GBP150 Mln via Share Placing
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 07:40:00

By Ian Walker Bluefield Solar Income Fund Ltd. said Wednesday that it has raised 150 million pounds ($189 million) via a placing and offer for subscription and will use the money to pay down its revolving credit facility, as previously flagged. The renewable-energy and storage-focused fund said it has issued 115.4 million new ordinary shares at 130 pence each, a 5.8% discount to its closing price on May 10, the day before the company first announced the fund-raising plan.
