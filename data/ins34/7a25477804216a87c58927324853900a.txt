Bicyclist helps save Wasaga Beach restaurant from fire
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-03 11:33:00

Fire crews in Wasaga Beach responded to an early morning fire at a restaurant. Fire Chief Mike McWilliam tells CTV News a bicyclist passing by the restaurant at the corner of Main Street and River Avenue Crescent noticed the flames from the window and called 9-1-1. The fire at the Sierra's Mexican Cabana started just after four Friday morning. Four fire units responded to the restaurant, where they discovered a fire in the kitchen. McWilliam blamed an overheated commercial cooking device for the fire.
