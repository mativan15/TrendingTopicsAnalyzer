Published: 12:07 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 12:08 EDT, 27 May 2022 A famous British artist who recently recovered from multiple cancer surgeries has today unveiled a 20ft bronze sculpture near Edinburgh.Tracey Emin, CBE, was given the all-clear from bladder cancer last year after having surgery to remove her bladder, womb, urethra, parts of her intestines and lymph nodes as well as half of her vagina.The 58-year-old artist said her latest works, produced over the last two years in her studios in Margate and London, were an 'expression of love, hope and the fragility of the human form'.
