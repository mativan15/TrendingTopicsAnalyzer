Minneapolis residents can provide feedback Tuesday on a proposal aimed at refining how the city's new system of government should work.

Many of the city's elected leaders have said they believe this will be one of the most important issues they vote on this term, noting that it has the potential to dramatically impact the quality of services residents receive.

Here's a refresher on the proposal and how you can share your thoughts on it.

What does the proposal do?

This proposal creates a cabinet of four people tasked with helping the mayor supervise the leaders of various city departments.
