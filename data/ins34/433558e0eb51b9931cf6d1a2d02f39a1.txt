By Dafna Goor, Assistant Professor of Marketing, London Business School Leaders must command us through geopolitical tensions that affect supply chains, natural disasters that pose a threat to human life or property, and medical emergencies like the Coronavirus pandemic that have a serious economic impact. Each issue offers a specific set of challenges that need to be understood, managed and learned from, particularly if you are trying to organise people to work together.
