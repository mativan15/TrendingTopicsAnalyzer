Tim Cook, Mindy Kaling, and Bill Gates to Headline the 2022 TIME100 Summit
time.com
news
2022-06-03 20:19:03

TIME is hosting its first in-person TIME100 Summit since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic. The summit, the live event extension of the annual TIME100 list of the most influential people in the world, will convene leaders from around the world on Tuesday, June 7. This year's summit draws from the global TIME100 community to spotlight solutions and encourage action toward a better world. Speakers come from the fields of politics, business, health and science, culture, and more. Following an opening performance by five-time-Grammy-winning musician Jon Batiste, Apple CEO Tim Cook will kick things off with a panel on the future of business.
