Diebold Nixdorf Shares Slip 9%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 18:20:00

By Josh Beckerman Diebold Nixdorf Inc. shares were recently down 9%, to $2.44. The stock opened at $3.05 after the company announced a refinancing agreement and lowered 2022 revenue guidance. The maker of ATMs and self-checkout machines said the transactions with holders of term loans and notes would refinance certain near-term debt and provide $400 million in new capital. Diebold Nixdorf expects 2022 revenue of about $3.5 billion, compared with prior guidance of $3.55 billion to $3.75 billion. Write to Josh Beckerman at josh.
