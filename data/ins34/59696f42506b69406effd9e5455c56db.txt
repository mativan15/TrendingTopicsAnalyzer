LITTLE ROCK, Ark., Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Bank OZK (the 'Bank') (Nasdaq: OZK) today announced that net income available to common stockholders for the third quarter of 2022 was $128.3 million, a 1.5% decrease from $130.3 million for the third quarter of 2021. Diluted earnings per common share for the third quarter of 2022 were $1.08, an 8.0% increase from $1.00 for the third quarter of 2021. For the nine months ended September 30, 2022, net income available to common stockholders was $388.
