Florida NewsBy Rob GarguiloOct 19, 2022Marion County, FL - A Florida man faced a federal judge on Tuesday after being accused of sexually exploiting children online and threatening to kill one of the children while live streaming. The FBI says David Patrick Sheffield, who has ties to Seattle, Washington, Nevada, Alaska, Florida and California, was arrested for producing and transmitting sexually explicit videos and photos. Federal agents say Sheffield, an Ocala resident, was using social media apps to solicit sexually explicit photographs and videos from minors in exchange for money.
