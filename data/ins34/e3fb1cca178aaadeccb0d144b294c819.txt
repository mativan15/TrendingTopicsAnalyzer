The past three years for Urstadt Biddle Properties (NYSE:UBA) investors has not been profitable

Many investors define successful investing as beating the market average over the long term. But the risk of stock picking is that you will likely buy under-performing companies. Unfortunately, that's been the case for longer term Urstadt Biddle Properties Inc. (NYSE:UBA) shareholders, since the share price is down 21% in the last three years, falling well short of the market return of around 45%. Furthermore, it's down 13% in about a quarter. That's not much fun for holders. But this could be related to the weak market, which is down 6.
