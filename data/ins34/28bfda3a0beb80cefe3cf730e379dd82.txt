Cardless and Simon® Launch Premium Retail Credit Card on American Express Network

news
2022-10-18 12:00:00

SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- San Francisco-based financial technology firm, Cardless, Inc., and Simon, a real estate investment trust engaged in the ownership of premier shopping, dining, entertainment, and mixed-use destinations in the United States, have agreed to launch the Simon® American Express® Credit Card from Cardless.
Once launched, Cardmembers will receive up to 3 percent back on all eligible purchases made at Simon destinations and up to 1.5 percent back on all other purchases.
