Scott StevensBy Scott StevensOct 19, 2022Thieves Blow Up ATM But Get No Cash: Someone blew apart an ATM in Palmdale, California at a Bank of America branch on Sunday with an explosive device, which sounds like a great way to grab the money. One problem; even though police found pieces of the machine and debris scattered around the ATM, the bomber wasn't able to get at the cash inside. No injuries were reported, and no suspect has been identified so far. Scott's Thought: Doesn't it make you smile
