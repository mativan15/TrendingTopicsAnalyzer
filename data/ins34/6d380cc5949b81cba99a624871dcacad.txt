Published: 07:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 07:40 EDT, 20 October 2022 MDLBEAST has unveiled their headline acts set to perform at the SOUNDSTORM 2022 music festival taking place in Saudi Arabia over the first weekend in December.This year's SOUNDSTORM is the third edition of the flagship event and is anticipated to bring some of the biggest artists to Riyadh - including Bruno Mars, DJ Khaled and Post Malone.Here's everything you need to know about the Middle East's largest music festival. Line-up: MDLBEAST has unveiled their headline acts set to perform at the SOUNDSTORM 2022 music festival taking place in Saudi Arabia over the first weekend in December (Bruno Mars pictured in 2017)When is SOUNDSTORM 2022? The music festival, taking place in Binban, Riyadh, will run over the course of three days, kicking off on December 1 and ending on December 3.
