Collierville Board of Mayor and Aldermen will begin voting in June on the town's $72.9 million operating budget for fiscal year 2023.

Town Administrator James Lewellen presented the budget May 23 during a public hearing and highlighted money focused on public safety, infrastructure and town employee raises.

The budget will go before the board three times in June before the final vote.

No tax rate increase

After lowering the tax rate in fiscal year 2022, town officials plan to keep it the same for fiscal year 2023, which begins July 1.
