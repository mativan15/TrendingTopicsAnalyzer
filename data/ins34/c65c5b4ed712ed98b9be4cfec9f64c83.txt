Published: 03:54 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 03:54 EDT, 20 October 2022 An Australian health insurer's customer data – including diagnoses and treatments – is being held for ransom by a cyber criminal in the nation's second major privacy breach in a month, officials have said. Trade in Medibank shares has been halted on the Australian Securities Exchange since Wednesday when police were alerted that the company had been contacted by what it described as a 'criminal' who wanted to negotiate over the stolen personal data of customers.
