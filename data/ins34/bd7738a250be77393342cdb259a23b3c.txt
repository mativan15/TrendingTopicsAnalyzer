Florida NewsBy Rob GarguiloOct 20, 2022Fort Myers, FL - Police have arrested a man who pepper sprayed a clerk in the face while robbing a Southwest Florida smoke shop. Fort Myers Police arrested 25-year-old Braxton Ryan after Ryan sprayed a clerk in the face while robbing the Holy Smokes and Vape shop on Dani Drive. Authorities say Ryan asked to buy $700 worth of items before spraying the clerk in the face, grabbing the items, and fleeing. Despite being pepper sprayed, the employee chased Ryan outside, where the two got into an altercation.
