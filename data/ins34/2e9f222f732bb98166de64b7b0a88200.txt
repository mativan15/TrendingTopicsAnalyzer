MULLICA HILL, N.J. (CBS) – Nine months after the remnants of Ida caused tornados in South Jersey, you can still see some of the aftermath today. Nearly a year later, some homeowners are still struggling to rebuild their lives.Meanwhile, some of those homeowners are demanding the federal government release some long-awaited money to help them rebuild. Survivors across the state say the federal government has allocated money, but they haven't received it.
