India is prepared for Monkeypox, no cases in country yet: ICMR
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 12:00:00

With monkeypox cases being identified across the world, the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) official on Friday said that India is prepared in view of increasing infections in the non-endemic countries, however, no cases have been reported in the country so far. In an interview with ANI, Dr Aparna Mukherjee, Scientist E, ICMR said, "India is prepared for the infections as it is rapidly spreading in non-endemic countries like Europe, USA and others. However, no cases have been reported in India so far.
