TV Insider Royal drama's afoot in the first trailer for The Crown‘s highly-anticipated fifth season which is set to arrive Wednesday, November 9 on Netflix. Created by Peter Morgan, the Emmy-winning series brings forward a new set of stars to take on roles based on real-life figures. Imelda Staunton steps into the shoes of Queen Elizabeth II in this first look which teases turmoil and division amid the rocky relationship between Princess Diana (Elizabeth Debicki) and Prince Charles (Dominic West).
