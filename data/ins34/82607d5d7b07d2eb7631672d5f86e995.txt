Overwhelming majority of pensioners fear they will be unable to pay energy bills
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-10-18 20:00:00

Exclusive: A poll by the Silver Voices campaign group revealed the extent of worry among older peopleThe £400 energy bill rebate scheme starts this month for everyone with a domestic electricity account.Seven out of 10 pensioners fear they will be unable to pay their energy bills this winter - even after the Government's support package. Ministers are pressing ahead with a guarantee that average dual fuel bills will not rise beyond £2,500. But the pledge, which was due to last until October 2024, will end in April after a series of humiliating U-turns by Prime Minister Liz Truss.
