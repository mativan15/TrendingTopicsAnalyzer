Simplicity Technology Announces Partnership With City of New York

October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Simplicity, the technology company behind the city super-app used by city governments globally to communicate more effectively with their residents, today announced that it has partnered with New York City. Over eight million New York City residents will now be able to receive important updates, city news, emergency alerts and more straight to their smartphones via a single app.



'New York City is the capital of innovation, and, in partnership with Simplicity, we are bringing our city together like never before and delivering information directly to our city's residents,' said New York City Mayor Eric Adams.
