Road Near PM Truss's Office Closed Over Suspicious Package
2022 USNews.com
news
2022-10-18 10:56:00

LONDON (Reuters) -A road in the centre of London's government district, near Prime Minister Liz Truss's Downing Street office and residence, was closed on Tuesday due to a suspicious package, police said on Tuesday.

Whitehall, between the Houses of Parliament and Trafalgar Square, is also home to several government departments including the foreign ministry, the Cabinet Office and the Ministry of Defence.

London police said on Twitter they had been called at 11.42 a.m. (1042 GMT) to reports of a suspicious package.
