Eloisa Sanchez, Duthie Power's Service Operations Manager Duthie Power Services has always said, 'Service is our business,' and they focus on the customer experience every day. LONG BEACH, Calif. (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Demand for emergency power systems, fueled by unpredictable weather and more frequent planned power outages from local utilities, continues to grow in Southern California and beyond. Duthie Power is growing alongside this demand, and as the leading generator service company in Southern California, the company is expanding the leadership in its service delivery team in order to maintain the service excellence that their growing list of customers expect.
