How Martinez mayor, council candidates stand on future of housing, downtown and marina
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-10-16 13:00:29

While neighboring East Bay cities are trying to build thousands of new homes or boast high-end commercial offerings, Martinez has remained a relatively quiet suburb of single-family neighborhoods and more traditional attractions.
But the waterfront town and county seat of Contra Costa has potential to grow and change, with a marina that for years has been ripe for new development, a downtown with a fledgling outdoor-dining scene and 300 acres of freshly acquired open space that will become a public park.
