By Casey Albritton Updated on: October 17, 2022 / 11:49 PM / CW44 Tampa Bay ST. PETERSBURG, Fla. (Tampa Bay Now) - A local organization is collecting donations specifically for babies impacted by Hurricane Ian.Right now there's a baby formula shortage surrounding the Fort Myers area.At the BabyCycle donation warehouse some of the most important things they are collecting are formula and diapers, but organization leaders say any donation will make a big difference in a baby's life."It was supposed to come here, we could have been in that position, now we have to go help the others who are in need," said Tiernan Jasuwan, a volunteer with the organization.
