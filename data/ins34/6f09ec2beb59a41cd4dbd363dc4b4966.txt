Published: 02:31 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 02:31 EDT, 18 October 2022 A Brisbane real estate agency has urged its landlords to hike up rental prices by 20 per cent in a leaked memo to clients.The email, sent by an employee at Ray White West End, recommended homeowners could push their rent up by more than double the current rate of inflation despite the country's ongoing affordability crisis.The agency believed it wasn't providing landlords with the 'information to make an informed decision', and was seeing hikes of just $5 to $20 per week.
