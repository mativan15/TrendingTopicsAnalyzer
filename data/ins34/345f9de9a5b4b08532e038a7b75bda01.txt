Why Is Verizon Selling Personal Massagers?
pcmag.com
news
2022-06-03 18:30:00

Mobile carriers make a lot of money from accessories. Often, that's why they really want to get you into a store to buy your phone—not for the phone, but for the cases and batteries and PopSockets and whatever.But ... how about a personal massager? What is this, Hammacher Schlemmer? Verizon pointed out to us today—very weirdly, I must add—that it is now selling a line of Therabody massagers(Opens in a new window) ranging from $99 to $599. These are mostly intense, percussive massagers, not "massagers," although I guess any massager can be a "massager" if you put your mind to it.
