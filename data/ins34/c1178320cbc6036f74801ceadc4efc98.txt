Grantham Margaret Thatcher statue 'unlikely to be removed'
bbc.com
news
2022-05-30 16:49:33

Image caption, The statue was spattered with red paint at the weekend, just weeks after it was eggedThere is "no prospect" of removing a statue of Margaret Thatcher that has been repeatedly vandalised since it was installed, a councillor has said.The sculpture depicting the former prime minister was put up in her hometown of Grantham on 15 May.Within hours it was egged and on Saturday it was spattered with paint.But a member of South Kesteven District Council said the statue was unlikely to be taken down unless the vandalism became intolerable.
