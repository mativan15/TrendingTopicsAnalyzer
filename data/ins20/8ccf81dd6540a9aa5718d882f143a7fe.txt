NCAA Tournament loss still haunts Cliff Omoruyi: 'I process it every day'
usatoday.com

2022-10-15 05:50:08

Sometimes, a devastating loss can haunt an athlete or a team. But for Rutgers basketball center Cliff Omoruyi and his teammates, last season's NCAA Tournament is something that is going to be used as a learning tool.
An 89-87 overtime loss to Notre Dame in Dayton, OH proved to be one of the iconic games of the NCAA Tournament last year. And while Rutgers was on the wrong end of that game, the result is something that they carried into the offseason as a lesson learned.
