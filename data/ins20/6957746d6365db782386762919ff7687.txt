Johnson & Johnson sells its largest India plant as demand weakens
indiatimes.com
science
2022-10-17 19:29:00

AgenciesJ&J did not give any reasons for idling its plant, but people aware of the matter said the company was grappling with reduced demand for its consumer health products, especially in the babycare segment.Johnson & Johnson (J&J), which is struggling with slowdown of demand for its consumer products in India, sold its largest manufacturing plant in the country - at Penjerla in Telangana - to Hetero on a slump sale basis. The financial details of the sale were not disclosed. A J&J spokesperson did not comment on the sale.
