SILENT Hill's Transmission showcase revealed four new games coming to Silent Hill, reviving the long-dormant franchise.
The last Silent Hill game to be released was P.T., a playable teaser directed by Hideo Kojima and Guillermo del Toro in 2014.
Silent Hill: Townfall was one of the games announced.Credit: Konami
This game was later cancelled, and we didn't hear of a new Silent Hill game in development for the next eight years.
Here is the rundown of all four games announced in the Silent Hill showcase:
Silent Hill 2 remake confirmed
Rumours have surrounded a Silent Hill 2 remake for years, but this is the first time it's been officially announced.
