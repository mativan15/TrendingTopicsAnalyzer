Tottenham have made an impressive start to the season (Image: Getty)Afternoon naps are the key to Tottenham's tireless pursuit of the top two teams this season. A penalty from Harry Kane - his ninth goal in 10 games this season - and Pierre-Emile Hojbjerg's late second were enough to extend Spurs' run to 10 successive wins at White Hart Lane.And Eric Dier revealed he and his team-mates are getting the absolute most out of 'home comforts' with regular power-sleeps during the current relentless run of fixtures that are being squeezed in before next month's World Cup.
