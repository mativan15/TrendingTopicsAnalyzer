Nanoco Proposes GBP2.3 Mln Fundraise; To Beat Full-Year Revenue Expectations
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-06 07:20:00

By Kyle Morris Nanoco Group PLC said Monday that it is proposing to raise 2.3 million pounds ($2.9 million) to provide cash to the business and that it now expects 2022 revenue to be ahead of its previous expectations after it secured a new contract. The U.K. nanomaterials group said it plans to conduct a placing and subscription for 6.1 million new ordinary shares at a price of 37 pence. A broker option will also take place to allow qualifying new and existing shareholders to participate on the same terms, it said.
