Published on Oct 15, 2022 10:26 PM IST Modi government has dismissed the Global Hunger Index 2022 report which ranked India 107 out of 121 countries with its child-wasting rate at 19.3 per cent, being the highest in the world. The ministry of women and child development, in a statement, said the index is an 'erroneous measure of hunger' and suffers from 'serious methodological issues'. Watch this video for more.
