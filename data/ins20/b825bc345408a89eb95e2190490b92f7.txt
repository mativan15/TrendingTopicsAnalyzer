Court Hears Oscar-Winner Paul Haggis Used His Fame to Prey on Women

Embattled Oscar-winning director Paul Haggis was painted by prosecutors as a serial predator who used his power to 'manipulate' vulnerable young women.Haggis' sexual assault trial got underway at a New York City courthouse this morning. The filmmaker appeared in court alongside his defense attorneys in a black suit and gray tie as opening statements began shortly after 10 a.m.Attorneys for Haleigh Breest, the former publicist suing Haggis for allegedly raping her in 2013, opened the trial by framing the Canadian film mogul as a 'powerful' and manipulative' serial predator who 'doesn't stop when women say no.
