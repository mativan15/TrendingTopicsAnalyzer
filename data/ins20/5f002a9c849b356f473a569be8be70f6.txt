The University of Nebraska–Lincoln's College of Journalism and Mass Communications will host the 'MobileMe&You' conference Oct. 20-22 in the Nebraska Union. One of the largest gatherings of digital and mobile media experts is bringing notable speakers to campus from organizations such as CNN, The New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Washington Post and USA TODAY. Mobile Me & You is the conference about mobile and emerging media. It educates and engages industry professionals, students, professors and communications staff in the exploration of mobile media best practices and innovative techniques.
