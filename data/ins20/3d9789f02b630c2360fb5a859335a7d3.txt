AgenciesThe Nagaland Board of School Education (NBSE) will declare the HSLC and HSSLC, that is, the Class 10 and Class 12 results, along with the HSLC compartment result today. Students can find their results on nbsenl.edu.in, the NBSE's official website. Provisional results, along with pass certificates and mark sheets, will be accessible at schools registered with the Board on result day, according to the NBSE. Also, the soft copies will be made available to the students on nbsenl.edu.in. On June 2, centre superintendents will be given access to the results records.
