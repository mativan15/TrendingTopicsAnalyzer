Welcome to your five-minute recap of the trading day, and how the experts saw it.The numbers: The ASX 200 dropped more than 1 per cent to 7211.1 points after rising oil prices highlighted recession fears across the globe and pushed the local share market into posting its worst monthly performance since January.Every sector finished the day lower with tech, telco and banking stocks the worst performers. The finance sector dropped 2 per cent, with the Commonwealth Bank and ANZ both down more than 2 per cent.
