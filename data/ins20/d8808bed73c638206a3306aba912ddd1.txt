These 8 stocks touch their new 52-week highs on Thursday
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 13:15:00

The benchmark index Sensex traded on a volatile note on Thursday, but ended in the green. It closed positive for the fifth consecutive session, gaining about 95 points to 59,202. During the session, eight stocks from the BSE 500 index managed to touch new 52-week highs.The 52-week high is the highest price at which a stock has traded during the last year. Some traders and investors use this technical indicator as an important factor for analysing a stock's current value and for predicting its future price movement.
