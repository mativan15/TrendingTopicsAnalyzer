May 27, 2022 02:18 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The law firm of Kirby McInerney LLP is investigating potential claims against GoodRx Holdings, Inc. ('GoodRx' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: GDRX). The investigation concerns whether GoodRx has violated the federal securities laws and/or engaged in other unlawful business practices.



GoodRx's core business is a healthcare technology platform that provides consumers with a prescription discount card to get discounted pricing on prescription drugs.
