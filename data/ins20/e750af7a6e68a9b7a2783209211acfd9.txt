REUTERS/Brendan McDermidOct 19 (Reuters) - Consumer goods giant Procter & Gamble Co (PG.N) cut its full-year sales forecast on Wednesday, blaming a hit from of a stronger U.S. dollar.A strengthening greenback typically eats into the earnings of companies such as P&G that have sprawling global operations and convert foreign currencies into dollars.Over half of P&G's revenue comes from outside the United States.
