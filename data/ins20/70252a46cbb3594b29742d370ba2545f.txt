This past week, Sony laid out a plan that seems like a pretty large departure from its usual strategy the past few generations, one in which they are moving beyond PlayStation boxes and into the mobile and PC markets. So much so, they plan to have half their game sales on PC and mobile by 2025. We have seen a rapid increase in PC ports from Sony for their exclusives, and their recent acquisitions have come with stipulations that the acquired games don't have to appear on just PlayStation alone (Bungie's Destiny, for one).
