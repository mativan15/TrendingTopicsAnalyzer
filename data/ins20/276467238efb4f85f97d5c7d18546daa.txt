VANCOUVER, British Columbia, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Amcomri Entertainment Inc. ('Amcomri' or the 'Company') (NEO: AMEN) (Frankfurt: 25Y0) (OTC: AMNNF), a global producer and distributor of independent movies, TV series and documentaries, has today provided a corporate update highlighting recent positive developments at its wholly-owned subsidiary, Abacus Media Rights ('Abacus'). "Our TV and documentary distribution business, Abacus, was one of the key performance drivers for Amcomri in the first and second quarters of 2022,' said Robert Price, Chief Executive Officer of Amcomri.
