Pro Kabaddi 2022: U Mumba vs Haryana Steelers

U Mumba lost their last match against Puneri Paltan and have a record of two losses and just as many wins from their four games so far. The Season 2 champions need to improve their overall game, which is highlighted by the fact that their best raider Guman Singh has 28 raid points after four games. Ashish and Jai Bhagwan have been the next best raiders for them this season with 20 points each. U Mumba need to step up defensively as well which is emphasized by the fact that Rinku with 12 tackle points is their top tackler.
