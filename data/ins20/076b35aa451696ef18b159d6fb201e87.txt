Four of gang stealing costly gadgets arrested in Vadodara
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 06:15:00

VADODARA: The prevention of crime branch ( PCB ) has busted a gang of thieves and arrested four persons from the city. The police said that members of the gang are the natives of Karnataka and Tamil Nadu and they frequented Gujarat to commit crime.According to police, the gang members used to travel to Vadodara frequently and stayed in hotels. They used to do recce in the residential colonies and enter the houses either in the evenings or early mornings. 'They used to steal mobile phones, laptops or cash lying in the room and disappear within minutes,' said a police official.
