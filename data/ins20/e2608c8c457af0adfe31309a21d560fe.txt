Published: 10:12 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 10:12 EDT, 20 October 2022 The owner of the art-deco lighthouse which featured in the 'saddest ever' episode of TV's Grand Designs says he's had offers two firm offers from wealthy would-be purchasers from as far afield as New Zealand and Canada - as well as a mystery cash buyer from Australia who is interested.Edward Short, whose desperate battle to build and then sell his Devon seafront house was the subject of a one-hour special Channel Four show last night, says the offers are in excess of £10million.
