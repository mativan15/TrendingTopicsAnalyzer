[Photo/Xinhua] The Chinese scientific and engineering community will continue to help promote the nation's socioeconomic development efforts and improve self-sufficiency in science and technology, Wan Gang, president of the China Association for Science and Technology, said on Monday. China celebrated its sixth annual National Science and Technology Workers' Day on Monday.
