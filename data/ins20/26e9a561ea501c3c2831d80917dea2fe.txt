October 20, 2022 09:06 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--GroupBy, a SaaS-based eCommerce product discovery technology leader, announces their upcoming panel discussion, '2022 eCommerce Holiday Season Trends, Challenges and Solutions.' The session, hosted by Amy Eschliman, Managing Director, Cloud Retail Industry Solutions at Google, will take place on Wednesday, October 26, at 1 p.m. ET/10 a.m. PT.



During this panel session, Eschliman, along with Chris Hiatt, Supply Chain & Manufacturing Industry Advisor, SADA; Roland Gossage, CEO, GroupBy; and guest speaker Sucharita Kodali, VP, Principal Analyst, Forrester, will share insights on retail and online consumer market trends and challenges and how to best tackle the upcoming holiday season and beyond.
