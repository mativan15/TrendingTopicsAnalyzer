MONTREAL, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TOMAGOLD CORPORATION (TSXV: LOT) (OTCQB: TOGOF) ('TomaGold' or the 'Corporation') is pleased to announce that it has completed the induced polarization (IP) survey announced on May 13, 2022, over the southern portion of its 100%-owned Obalski property, located 2 km south of Chibougamau, Quebec. The Corporation expects to receive the results integrating the December 2021 and May 2022 surveys from Abitibi Geophysics in the coming weeks.
