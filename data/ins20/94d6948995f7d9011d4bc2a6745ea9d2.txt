Brooklyn Nets outside top 10 in Bleacher Report's power rankings
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-18 14:32:41

Even though the Brooklyn Nets struggled to start the NBA preseason the team ended up winning its final two games, ending the preseason even. Bleacher Report re-shifted their preseason power rankings, listing the Nets just outside of the top ten.
Basketball insider Andy Bailey of Bleacher Report ranked the Nets at 11th, even after a mediocre NBA preseason. Bailey reports, 'If everything goes right for the Brooklyn Nets, they can win the championship. The upside of this team is 60-plus wins and a relatively comfortable cruise through the playoffs.
