CYPRIOT minnows Omonia Nicosia have given Neil Lennon the chop - just days after his side almost stunned Old Trafford.
Lennon and Co were minutes away from earning a point against Manchester United in the Europa League.

That was until an injury-time goal from Scott McTominay broke Omonia hearts.
The club also put in a good shift in their 3-2 home loss to the Red Devils the week before.
