Published: 11:57 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 11:59 EDT, 18 October 2022 Questions over whether the Government will scrap a disastrous project to build the British Army's new £5.5bn fleet of armoured vehicles will be answered by this week, MailOnline can reveal. Defence Secretary Ben Wallace is set to make a statement about the ill-fated Ajax programme, which has faced years in limbo due to technical faults.The deadly new armoured vehicle was intended to give the Army a new lethal punch on the battlefield.
