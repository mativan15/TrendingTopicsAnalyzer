Rod Stewart rents home to Ukrainian family-of-seven and pays all bills
express.co.uk
entertainment
2022-10-18 07:40:58

Sir Rod Stewart, 77, admitted he prefers to keep his "charitable efforts quiet" after the Maggie May legend opened up about his good deed in a bid to inspire others. The singer explained he has rented a Berkshire home for Ukrainian couple Rostylsav and Olena and their five children for at least a year.The dad-of-eight opened up about his efforts in a new interview, where the music legend revealed he had found the family a new home, had it furnished and pledged to pay the rent and all of their bills.
