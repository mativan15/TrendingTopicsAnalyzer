A Nottingham professor has turned his passion for physics and science communication into his first book, dedicated to a friend who inspired him. "Fantastic Numbers and Where to Find Them" is written by leading theoretical physicist and YouTube star, Professor Antonio (Tony) Padilla and takes readers on an irreverent cosmic tour of nine of the most extraordinary numbers in physics. Tony explains where he got the inspiration to write his first book: "When my best friend became ill with cancer, we started giving public lectures about fantastic numbers and cutting-edge physics to raise money for him to get treatment abroad.
