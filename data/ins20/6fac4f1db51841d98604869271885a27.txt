Thane: Wooed by dating site, engineer loses Rs 6 lakh
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 03:45:00

THANE: A 48-year-old married software engineer from Thane lost Rs 6.3 lakh after he fell prey to scammers while looking for a match on an online dating website, the police said on Sunday.The engineer had got a text message from an unknown number, offering online dating services. He called on the number, after which he was told to pay Rs 38,200 as entry fee.The caller did not register him and kept demanding money for registration. The police said that the engineer suspected the genuineness of the caller and demanded a refund but the accused lured him into paying more.
