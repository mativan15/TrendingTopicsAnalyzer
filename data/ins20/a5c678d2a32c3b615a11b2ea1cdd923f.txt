Published: 03:42 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 03:42 EDT, 3 June 2022 Barcelona are considering asking their captains to take a 50 per cent salary reduction to help ease their financial turmoil, according to reports, with the club once again set to take drastic steps to ease the pressure on their coffers.The club are still reeling from years of financial mismanagement under their former president Josep Bartomeu and the effects of the Covid-19 pandemic, and have already been forced to implement swinging cuts across their playing squad.
