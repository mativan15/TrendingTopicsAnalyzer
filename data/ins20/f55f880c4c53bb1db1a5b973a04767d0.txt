June 03, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time REDWOOD CITY, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Zuora, Inc. (NYSE: ZUO), the leading cloud-based subscription management platform provider, today announced that its Founder & Chief Executive Officer, Tien Tzuo, and Chief Financial Officer, Todd McElhatton, will present at Baird's 2022 Global Consumer, Technology & Services Conference in New York City on Wednesday, June 8, 2022 at 2:00 p.m. Eastern Time (11:00 a.m. Pacific Time).

The presentation will be webcast live and available to replay on Zuora's investor relations website at https://investor.
