Liz Truss and Sir Keir Starmer have led tributes to Sir David Amess MP on the first anniversary of his murder.Sir David was stabbed during a constituency surgery at Belfairs Methodist Church in Leigh-on-Sea, Essex, last October.Ms Truss tweeted a statement along with Sir David's parliamentary portrait on Saturday morning.She wrote: 'On the anniversary of Sir David Amess' death, we cherish his memory and remember his enormous contribution to politics, to the people of Southend and to the country.'My thoughts today are with his wife Julia, the Amess family and to all those who knew and loved him.
