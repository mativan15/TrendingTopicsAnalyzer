UK's Lloyds ditches project finance for new oil and gas fields

REUTERS/Tom NicholsonLONDON, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Britain's biggest domestic bank Lloyds (LLOY.L) said on Thursday it would not support direct financing to develop new oil and gas fields, joining a small number of lenders to push back on funding expansion of the industry.Lloyds updated its climate policy to make the change, which bars project financing or reserve-based lending to greenfield oil and gas projects, although the policy would still mean it could provide general lending to companies in the industry.
