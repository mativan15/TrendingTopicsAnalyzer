Published: 07:48 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 07:54 EDT, 20 October 2022 Tyson Fury has been seen flaunting his money around after winning a £10,000 bet with his promoter Frank Warren over the proposed Battle of Britain clash with Anthony Joshua.Warren revealed earlier in that day that he owes Fury £10,000 after he bet the Gypsy King that the proposed bout with AJ 'would be finalised' during their ongoing negotiations. Fury and AJ had been in negotiations for months over a long-awaited heavyweight clash in Cardiff.
