QuoteToMe Welcomes BuildingConnected Founder Dustin DeVan to Its Board of Directors

2022-05-31 13:32:47

EDMONTON, Alberta--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--QuoteToMe, a digital leader in construction procurement workflow automation, today announced that Dustin DeVan has joined the Board of Directors. In this role, DeVan will leverage his experience as a construction-technology entrepreneur and engineer to enhance QuoteToMe's mission of reimagining the construction industry's procure-to-pay workflow, by creating a network that digitally connects transaction stakeholders.DeVan brings more than nine years of experience as a construction technology leader at BuildingConnected, followed by his experience at AutoDesk as the Vice President of Industry Strategy for Construction.
