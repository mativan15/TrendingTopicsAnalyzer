Bihar hands over another 24 acres of land to upgrade Darbhanga airport
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 16:12:04

The process of acquisition of 24 acres of land sought by the Airport Authority of India (AAI) from the state government for expansion of runway and establishment of night landing facility at Darbhanga airport has been completed in just 10 months, according to the district administration. The process to acquire another 54 acres of land for construction of permanent terminal building will be completed by February next year, officials said.'The district land acquisition officer (DLAO) handed over the charge of the 24 acres of land parcel to the joint secretary (cabinet secretariat) on Monday (October 17) so that it can now be transferred to AAI on behalf of the state government,' said district public relation officer (DPRO) N K Gupta.
