Herbert, Wilson take center stage when Chargers host Broncos

Justin Herbert has shown he has been able to bounce back from a serious injury.Whether Russell Wilson can do the same has turned into the leading storyline for Monday night's game.Herbert's Los Angeles Chargers will host Wilson and the Denver Broncos in prime time in an AFC West matchup. Even though there remains nearly three months of the regular season to be played, both quarterbacks have already experienced their share of adversity.
