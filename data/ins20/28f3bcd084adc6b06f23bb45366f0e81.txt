Ultraviolette F77 bike to cover 300 km on single charge; pre-bookings from Sunday
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 14:30:00

Rigorous in-house testing has been a constant throughout F77's R&D journey, including extensive simulation, on-road testing, power cycling, and extreme off-road testing.New Delhi: Ultraviolette, an innovator in sustainable mobility, today hosted its first Battery Day event, where it announced an industry-leading Indian Driving Cycle (IDC) range of 300 km on a single charge on the top-end variant of its motorcycle F77 to be launched shortly. Customers in India can pre-book F77 from October 23, 2022.
