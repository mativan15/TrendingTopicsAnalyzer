Shares of Bajaj Holdings & Investment Ltd. traded at Rs 6804.6 on BSE at 10:20AM (IST) on Wednesday, up 1.38 per cent from previous close. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 4299.8 and a high of Rs 7377.6. Earlier, shares of the company saw a gap down opening in the morning. A total of 1,005 lakh shares changed hands on the counter till 10:20AM (IST). The stock of & Investment Ltd. commanded a market value of Rs 74299.55 crore, according to BSE. The stock traded at a price-to-earning (P/E) multiple of 17.
