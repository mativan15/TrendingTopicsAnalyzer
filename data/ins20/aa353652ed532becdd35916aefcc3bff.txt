 A Brampton man already facing charges in relation to the attempted theft of a catalytic converter in Guelph is facing additional charges for previous thefts.
The 24-year-old was arrested by Guelph police back on Oct. 5th.That was when investigators say some neighbours spotted a man trying to remove the exhaust component of a vehicle that was parked in the driveway of a Victoria Rd. N. home.A struggle ensued after neighbours confronted the individual.
