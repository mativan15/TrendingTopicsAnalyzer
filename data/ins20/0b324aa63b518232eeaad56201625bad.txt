A woman has been blasted online after she admitted she will be attending her stepdaughter's upcoming wedding - even though the bride doesn't actually want her thereThe bride invited her stepparents to her wedding just to keep the peace (stock photo) Who you invite to your wedding should be completely up to you and your partner, but sometimes you might feel as though you have an obligation to invite certain people you'd rather not see on your big day. That's the case with one bride who has invited her parents to her wedding alongside both of their new spouses - even though she'd rather not face her stepmum or stepdad.
