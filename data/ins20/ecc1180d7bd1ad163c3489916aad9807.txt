Published: 04:11 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 04:13 EDT, 21 October 2022 Brian Cox has spoken about the challenges of Hollywood, describing it as a 'difficult place to live' and 'the worst place in the world to raise female children'.The Succession star, 76, commented on the MeToo movement and how wealth can make you 'guilty' in the limelight in a new wide-ranging interview.He said he believes Hollywood 'exacerbates' the many pressures already on young women as he described it as the 'worst place in the world to raise female children'.
