Berlusconi said he received vodka shipment from Putin, reports say
theguardian.com
sport
2022-10-18 18:34:48

Silvio Berlusconi has allegedly said Vladimir Putin gave him 20 bottles of vodka for his birthday after he 're-established' relations with the Russian president.Berlusconi turned 86 on 29 September, four days after a coalition including his Forza Italia party won general elections.Italy's three-time former prime minister, an old friend of Putin's, is reported to have told a meeting of Forza Italia parliamentarians in Rome on Tuesday that the shipment of vodka was accompanied by a 'very sweet letter'.
