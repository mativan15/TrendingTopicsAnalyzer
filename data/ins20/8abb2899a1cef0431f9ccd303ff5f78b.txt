Liz Truss is a multi-millionaire as she resigns as PM
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 13:10:00

Liz Truss has announced her resignation as Prime Minister today, October 20. She was appointed Prime Minister of the United Kingdom on September 5, replacing Boris Johnson. Despite now being out of a job, Ms Truss has a multi-million pound fortune in the bank.According to Money Transfers, the former Prime Minister has a net worth of £8.4million.This is a huge fortune Ms Truss has been able to accumulate over the years thanks to various jobs in politics. Before becoming Prime Minister, she was serving as Secretary of State for Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Affairs, from 2021.
