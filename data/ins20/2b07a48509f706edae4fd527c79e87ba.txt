It's official. Breakdancing is coming to the Olympics. Officially named 'breaking' by the International Olympic Committee, the sport will be part of the 2024 Summer Games in Paris. And whether or not it has been on your sports radar up to now, breaking competitions have been held all over the world since the 1990s, popularizing the dance form far beyond urban hip-hop communities to cross over to the general public along the way.
