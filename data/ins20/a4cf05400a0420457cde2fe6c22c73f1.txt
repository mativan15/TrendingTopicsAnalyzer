Credo Technology Shares Jump 21% on Big Rise in 4Q Revenue
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 16:27:00

By Chris Wack Credo Technology Group shares were up 21% to $12.44 after the company reported fourth-quarter revenue that was above analysts' expectations. The company said it had quarterly revenue of $37.5 million, up 90% compared to $19.8 million in the same quarter last year. Analysts polled by FactSet were looking for revenue of $36.4 million. Credo reported a loss of $5.4 million, or 4 cents a share, compared to last year's loss of $144,000, or 0 cents a share. Analysts were expecting a loss of $1 million, or 3 cents a share.
