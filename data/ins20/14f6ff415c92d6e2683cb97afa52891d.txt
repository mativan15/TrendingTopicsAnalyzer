Approximately 20 protesters from Just Stop Oil glued themselves to the tarmac in Shoreditch High Street today as part of the group's month-long disruption around LondonThe motorists dragged protesters out of the way of trafficClimate change protesters have been physically dragged off London roads by fuming members of the public to allow traffic through, as one driver shouted his wife 'needed to get to hospital'. Around 20 supporters of Just Stop Oil created a roadblock on Shoreditch High Street at around noon today, delaying hundreds going about their day.
