June 01, 2022 10:48 AM Eastern Daylight Time BURLINGTON, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--HealthEdge announced two strategic additions to its sales leadership: Matt Francis, Senior Vice President, HealthRules Payor Sales, and Jeff Jacobs, Senior Vice President, Source Sales. They will be responsible for driving new customer growth and adoption of the HealthEdge Digital Platform, a suite of cloud-based SaaS applications for core administration (HealthRules Payor), payment integrity (Source), care management (GuidingCare), and digital health management (Wellframe).
