German Book Award at Frankfurt International Book Fair 2022German Book Award winner 2022, Swiss non-binary author Kim de l?Horizon poses before the award ceremony at the Frankfurt International Book Fair 2022, in Frankfurt am Main, Germany, 17 October 2022. Kim de l?Horizon won the prize for the book 'Blutbuch' (lit. blood book). Since 2005, the Stock Exchange Association of the German Publishers and Booksellers Association has awarded the prize for the best German-language novel of the year at the start of the Frankfurt Book Fair.
