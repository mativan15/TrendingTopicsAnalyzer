Indian real estate developers have increased their focus on the environment by saving energy and water, as demand for buildings compliant to environmental, social and governance (ESG) standards is growing, according to property consultants. The buildings and construction sectors combined are responsible for 36% of the global final energy consumption and nearly 40% of total direct and indirect carbon-dioxide emissions. The real estate sector is responsible for 24% of India's annual CO2 emissions. Developers like , , , Macrotech and are taking initiatives to reduce energy and water consumption.
