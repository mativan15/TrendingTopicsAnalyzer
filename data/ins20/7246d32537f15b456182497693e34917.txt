Given the country's economic outlook, Indian equities should have a lion's share of the asset allocation in any investor's portfolio, Trideep Bhattacharya, CIO-Equities, Edelweiss AMC tells ET Wealth. 
What do you make of the market's big swings in recent months?
India appears an oasis in a volatile global market. Global central banks are grappling with the quandary of having to choose between higher interest rates, lower inflation and managing currency in a calibrated manner. This is leading to unprecedented actions.
