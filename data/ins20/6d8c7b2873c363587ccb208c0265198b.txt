On Nov. 19, 1996, the Green Bay Packers went to Dallas, couldn't find the endzone and dropped a 21-6 decision to the defending Super Bowl champion Cowboys. Green Bay's preferred starters at wide receiver — Robert Brooks and Antonio Freeman — were both injured at the time. So the following day, general manager Ron Wolf signed former Pro Bowler Antonio Freeman off the street.
