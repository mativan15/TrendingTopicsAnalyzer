(Shutterstock)HAZEL PARK, MI — A person was found shot to death Friday morning outside a business in Hazel Park, according to reports.Officers found the body in the parking lot of the LG Manufacturing building in the area of 10 Mile Road and Vance Avenue, just south of Interstate 696, according to reports.One person was arrested in connection to the shooting, according to reports.Police were still investigating the shooting to determine a motive.
