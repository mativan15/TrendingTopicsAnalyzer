German Chancellor Olaf Scholz set to become first western leader to visit China in 3 years

AgenciesHis predecessor also advocated a strong business partnership with China.German Chancellor Olaf Scholz is planning to travel to China on November 3-4, making him the first G-7 leader to visit Beijing since the start of the Covid and notwithstanding Western efforts to call out efforts by China to establish hegemony, following strong push by the German businesses. While Germany has recently taken a stereotypical approach on Kashmir calling for UN intervention echoing Pakistani sentiments, Scholz is not keen for "decoupling from China" as suggested by the US.
