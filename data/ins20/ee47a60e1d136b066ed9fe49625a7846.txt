Cosmos Shares Drop 47% After Equity Offering Prices
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 17:37:00

By Josh Beckerman Cosmos Holdings Inc. shares were down 47% to 8 cents Tuesday after the pricing of an upsized $7.5 million public offering. Trading volume was more than 48.3 million shares, compared with a 65-day average of 1.4 million. Cosmos, which produces nutraceuticals and distributes drugs and medical devices, said the offering of 62.5 million shares, plus series A and series B warrants, had a combined price of 12 cents per share and warrant. Cosmos said Oct. 11 that it planned to offer up to $7 million of stock.
