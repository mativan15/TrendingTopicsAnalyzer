Melissa Holler, President Hexagon Digital Wave

June 1, 2022 - Hexagon Digital Wave is pleased to announce the appointment of Melissa D. Holler as its new President, effective May 23rd, 2022. A highly experienced senior leader with more than 25 years' experience from both mature and startup companies, Ms. Holler has a proven track record in strategy development and execution, as well as significant commercial experience within digital transformation solutions.
