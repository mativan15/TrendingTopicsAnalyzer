Tracy Chamoun, the British-educated former ambassador who believes she can change Lebanon
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

She is a half-Australian former yoga teacher who has never held a job in politics. But few know the dangers of Lebanon's treacherous power battles better than Tracy Chamoun.Her father Dany Chamoun, a politician and militia leader, was assassinated during the country's civil war, along with her two brothers. While she was still a teenager, she was bombed out of four homes in Beirut, almost lost an eye in a rocket-propelled grenade attack, and briefly trained in a militia camp. Her grandfather, the former president Camille Chamoun, known as the 'Tiger', narrowly survived repeated attempts on his life.
