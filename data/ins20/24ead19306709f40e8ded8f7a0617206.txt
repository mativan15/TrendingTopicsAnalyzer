Airport boss used private check-in to travel over weekend
irishtimes.com
business
2022-06-01 00:00:00

Dalton Philips, chief executive of Dublin Airport owner DAA, used its private security lane when he travelled for business at the weekend.Mr Philips flew to Saudi Arabia, where DAA has an airport management operation, on Saturday, but cut the trip short when chaos struck security queues in Dublin.He told the Oireachtas Joint Committee on Transport on Wednesday that he travelled through Dublin's private Platinum Services when he flew on Saturday.Responding to questions, he said the process took an hour.
