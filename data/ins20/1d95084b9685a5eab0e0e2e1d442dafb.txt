Christchurch opera singer heads to Europe with $50,000 prize money
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-15 01:42:11

Christchurch opera singer Oliver Sewell says winning the prestigious $50,000 Dame Malvina Major Award is a 'game-changer'' for his career. The 33-year-old tenor said the prize money will support his time in Europe attending auditions and entering competitions to break into the 'big world of opera'. 'It is a game-changer,'' he said. 'There is nothing else that would make a difference like that to get me back into the greater opera world.' 'The biggest thing stopping me is that it is a massive financial risk.
