95.7 The Jet Mornings with Jodi & BenderBy Zuri AndersonOct 14, 2022Wildfire smoke keeps plaguing air quality in Western Washington, and the situation will continue for days, according to KING 5. The National Weather Service says strengthening offshore winds will push the thick haze into the region, sticking around for a while. Reporters say the Chilliwack Area and Bolt Creek fires are driving the heavy wildfire smoke. Most of the region will be under an Air Quality Alert heading into the weekend, according to NWS's website.
