LMF Acquisition Opportunities Down 12%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 16:31:00

By Josh Beckerman Shares of blank-check company LMF Acquisition Opportunities Inc. declined again Friday, recently trading down 12%, to $6. The stock gained 23% on Wednesday after stockholders approved the company's merger with SeaStar Medical Inc. and fell 35% on Thursday. According to a securities filing Wednesday, holders of 10.26 million Class A shares exercised their right to redeem shares as of Monday. SeaStar is developing technology intended to reduce the consequences of excessive inflammation on vital organs.
