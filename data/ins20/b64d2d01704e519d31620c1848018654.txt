It's time to take stock of another wild week in college football, one in which Ohio State was idle. Just like almost every Saturday, we had some pretty amazing games and some pretty epic upsets to throw this whole College Football Playoff race into turmoil.
Alabama went down to Tennessee, and USC couldn't quite finish the deal on the road vs. Utah. Those two games alone took two more unbeaten teams off the board. It didn't eliminate either in the race for the trophies and confetti, but it did drastically reset everyone's expectations and mindset about which teams are the best in the land.
