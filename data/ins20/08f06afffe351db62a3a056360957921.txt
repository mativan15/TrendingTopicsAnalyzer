Two Republicans are vying for Thibodaux mayor in the Nov. 8 election.

The winner will succeed Tommy Eschete, who will step down after his third term expires on Dec. 31.

Kevin Clement, the city's Parks and Recreation director, will face Sandy Holloway, a current member of the Louisiana state school board.

Early voting for all of the Nov. 8 elections runs 8:30 a.m. to 6 p.m. Oct. 25 through Nov. 1, excluding Sunday, Oct. 30, at parish registrar of voters' offices. In Thibodaux, the office is at 307 W.
