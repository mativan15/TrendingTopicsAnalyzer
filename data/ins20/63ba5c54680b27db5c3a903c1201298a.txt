Published: 21:06 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 21:06 EDT, 19 October 2022 When Melbourne Victory host Melbourne City in a tantalising A-League Men derby, a packed-out AAMI Park is on the cards. Victory attracted more than 18,000 fans to their opening home game against Western Sydney, while City have won two games on the bounce heading into Saturday night. But just 24 hours before the derby, Western United are expected to draw one of the season's lowest crowds against fellow expansion club Macarthur FC at the same venue.
