Yaya MartinezOct 17, 2022Photo: Getty Images Have you ever broken the law by throwing something away? Here are five common things people throw out, even though you shouldn't . . .  1.  Batteries. Most states still let you toss standard alkaline batteries, like double-A's. California is an exception. Other types can have things like mercury in them, so they're illegal to throw out. Drop them at a place like Staples instead. 2.  Old phones, computers, and other e-waste. More states are making it illegal to throw out your old tech, especially if they have batteries that can't be removed.
