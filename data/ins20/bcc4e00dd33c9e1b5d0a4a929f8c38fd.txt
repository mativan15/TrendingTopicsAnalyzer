Smart meters: How do they work and will they save you on electricity costs?
irishtimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-22 04:00:00

Smart meters are a hot topic these days. While ESB Networks has been rolling out the meter-replacement project throughout the country in recent months, the public debate over whether the meters are a help or a hindrance to consumers looking to reduce their electricity costs has been raging. Add in spiraling electricity costs, and you can see why people are examining the new technology to see if it actually benefits them in the ways they might have expected.ESB Networks is installing 500,000 smart meters per year as part of a long-term programme, with the goal of replacing every electricity meter in the country with a next-generation smart meter.
