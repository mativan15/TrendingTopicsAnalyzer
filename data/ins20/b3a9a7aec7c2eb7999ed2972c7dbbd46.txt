Another pothole death: Bengaluru woman dies after being hit by bus
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 11:45:00

A woman who met with an accident while trying to avoid a pothole on a road in the Rajajinagar area of Bengaluru succumbed to her wounds on Tuesday. Umadevi and her daughter Sunitha, who were travelling on a two-wheeler on Magadi Road, were hit by a speeding bus as they tried to avoid the pothole . Umadevi sustained serious injuries and died in hospital a day later. "My mother's death happened due to potholes on the roads and we are asking for justice and compensation from the government. We are from a very backward class my mother was the only woman to take care of us," Sunitha told ANI.
