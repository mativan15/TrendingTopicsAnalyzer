Waterloo Regional Police say a 35-year-old man was taken into custody over the weekend after 'indecent acts' were performed on the ION LRT and in downtown Kitchener.
Officers were called to downtown Kitchener near Charles and Gaukel streets after receiving reports of a man committing an indecent act on the ION LRT on Saturday at around 7:45 p.m.The man was also allegedly seen committing another indecent act in front of a business in the area. Police say officers tracked down the man and arrested him.
