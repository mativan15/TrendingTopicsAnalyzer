Tiger Global drops 14% in May during the tech sell-off, pushing hedge fund's 2022 losses to over 50%

2022-06-02 13:27:56

Chase Coleman, founder of Tiger Global Management LLCChase Coleman's Tiger Global Management suffered huge losses in May amid a tech-driven sell-off, making the hedge fund's tough 2022 even worse.The growth-focused flagship fund at Tiger Global tumbled 14.3% in May, bringing its 2022 losses to over 50%, a source familiar with the return told CNBC's David Faber.In the first quarter, Tiger Global doubled down on a number of tech holdings, including Snowflake, Carvana and Sea, before the market decline got uglier, according to a regulatory filing.
