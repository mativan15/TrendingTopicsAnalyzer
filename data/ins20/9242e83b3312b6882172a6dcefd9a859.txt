The daughter of the dreaded 'Happy Face' serial killer has revealed on TikTok the 'cruel' letters her father writes her from prison. Between 1990 and 1995, Keith Hunter Jesperson murdered at least eight women. However, he claims to have murdered as many as 185 people. Melissa Moore disclosed that she had received a letter from her incarcerated father that read, "I never stopped loving you".

She uploaded the video to her TikTok account, @lifeafterhappyface, where it has received eight million views.
