Rockville, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to Fact.MR, a market research and competitive intelligence provider, the global sodium trimetaphosphate market is estimated at US$ 38.9 million in 2022 and is expected to expand at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast years of 2022-2032. Modified starch possesses numerous functional properties that are suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. As a result of its ability to bulk up after absorbing water, it renders the essential mouthful feel to the food products in which it is used.
