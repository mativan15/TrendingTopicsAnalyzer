A new Bath & Body Works store is now open in The Quarry shopping center in Hodgkins.The new store at 9430 Joliet Road held its grand opening May 20. The store is next to the Walmart.Hours are 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. Monday to Thursday, 9 a.m. to 8 p.m. on Friday and Saturday, and from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. on Sunday.The retail chain sells soaps, lotions, fragrances and candles. It was founded in 1990 in New Albany, Ohio.Specialty Physicians of Illinois closed the SPI ExpressCare location at 20180 LaGrange Road in Frankfort on May 27.
