The Kannada dubbed version of Mahabaratha, which garnered the attention of many viewers across the state during the nationwide lockdown in 2020, is set to be back on the small screens. Mahabharatha will be airing its first episode today (October 17). Unlike the previous time, the mythological show will be airing at the noon timeslot and not at primetime.Mahabharat, which was aired from 2013 to 2014 on a Hindi general entertainment channel, had been dubbed in Kannada. The serial, which is based on Mahabharat by Vyasa, was the second dubbed Kannada television series to go on air after Malgudi Days.
