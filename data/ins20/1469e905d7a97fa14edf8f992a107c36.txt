Brand recognition does something for a company that marketers aspire to. It automatically links a product, solution or business with consumers' desires or needs. Thinking of kid-friendly movies and vacations will bring Disney to mind. Or you instantly mention Subway when you and your co-workers need a quick sandwich for lunch. With brand recognition also comes trust and credibility. Your target audience has great confidence that they'll get the product or service they expect.
