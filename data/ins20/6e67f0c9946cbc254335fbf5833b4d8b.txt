Chief minister Uddhav Thackeray on Saturday showered Nationalist Congress Party chief Sharad Pawar with praise, saying like sugar he had brought sweetness to politics. 'Pawarsaheb has brought the sweetness of sugar to politics and all leaders, keeping aside their political affiliations, have come together for the benefit of the state and the country,' Thackeray said.
