Published: 17:09 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 17:09 EDT, 29 May 2022 Wayne Hemingway MBE, 61, co-founded fashion label Red or Dead with wife Gerardine. They sold it in 1998 and a year later set up Hemingway Design. He is curating part of the Platinum Jubilee pageant. Wayne lives in West Sussex with his wife and has four grown-up children.I was born into a working-class household and Mum was forever making clothes. The tipping point was going to see David Bowie in Blackburn in 1973 when I was 12 — I was blown away by his style and wanted to look like him.
