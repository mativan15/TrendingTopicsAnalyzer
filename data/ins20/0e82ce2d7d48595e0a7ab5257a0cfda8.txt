Varanasi: Initiating action under Gangster Act, Bhadohi police attached the bank accounts containing Rs 47.79 lakh of jailed muscleman politician and former MLA Vijay Mishra's family, on Thursday . Superintendent of police Bhadohi Dr Anil Kumar said that illegally earned Rs 47,79,589 were deposited in these bank accounts of Mishra's wife and daughter-in-law.According to SP Bhadohi, under the on-going drive to destroy the economic empire of the mafia elements and gangsters, the police had detected the bank accounts of Mishra's family members in which money earned through illegal means was deposited.
