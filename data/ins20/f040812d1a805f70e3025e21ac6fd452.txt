Top Tennessee Republicans are vowing they'll push to enact some of the strictest anti-transgender policies in the United StatesNASHVILLE, Tenn. -- Top Tennessee Republicans on Friday vowed to push for some of the strictest anti-transgender policies in the United States at a rally Friday, where hundreds of people cheered in support as LGBTQ rights activists yelled back in protest. The rally comes nearly a month after video surfaced of a doctor at Vanderbilt University Medical Center stating that gender-affirming procedures are 'huge money makers' for hospitals.
