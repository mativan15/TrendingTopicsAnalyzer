AgenciesThe Karachi-born billionaire had also been attempting to mobilize funds and help evacuate refugees from Ukraine to the United Kingdom and other parts of Europe, according to a report in UAE-based Khaleej Times. In a significant development, Ukraine's Pakistani origin billionaire Mohammad Zahoor has jumped into the conflict and reportedly purchased two fighter jets to aid Kiev's battle against Russia. ET has learnt that Zahoor has allegedly purchased two Soviet origin jets reportedly from an Eastern European country (Several Eastern European states received jets from the Soviet Union during the Cold War period) with assistance of his business partners.
