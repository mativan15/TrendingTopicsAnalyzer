It can be hard to understand what the hell is going on with the global energy market. Since Russia's invasion of Ukraine in March, the news has been a non-stop churn of confusing updates about oil prices, gas supply, and a stew of acronyms (OPEC? SPR?). The whole thing can be intimidating to think about, even to energy reporters and folks who think about this issue professionally. 'It's one of the most confusing and hard-to-analyze situations that I've been in,' said Clark Williams-Derry, an energy analyst at the Institute for Energy Economics and Financial Analysis.
