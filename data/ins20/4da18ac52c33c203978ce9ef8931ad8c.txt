Matthew Perry reveals ‘dark side' of addiction journey in new memoir, opens up about near-death experience
foxnews.com

Published October 19, 2022 9:17am EDT Matthew Perry is speaking out about his addiction journey.In his new memoir entitled "Friends, Lovers, and the Big Terrible Thing," the "Friends" actor revealed the challenges he faced when he hit rock bottom. "I wanted to share when I was safe from going into the dark side of everything again," Perry shared, according to People."I had to wait until I was pretty safely sober — and away from the active disease of alcoholism and addiction — to write it all down.
