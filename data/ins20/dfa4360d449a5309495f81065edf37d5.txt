RT.com 3rd June 2022, 19:19 GMT+10Hundreds of rescue workers are the scene, authorities have not ruled out arsonTwo people have been injured following a large blaze at the Grand Setun Plaza office building in Moscow on Friday morning.Between 15 to 20 people, according to various local news outlets, could be trapped inside. 125 people have been rescued from the building by emergency services, according to the Moscow Operational Headquarters.The Ministry of Emergency Situations has announced that two people have been taken to hospital with injuries.
