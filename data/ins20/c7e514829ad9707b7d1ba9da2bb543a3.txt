Penny Mordaunt would keep Jeremy Hunt as Chancellor
telegraph.co.uk

2022-10-21 20:00:00

She would make better use of the government residences of Chequers and Chevening for policy-making gatherings.
Ms Mordaunt's team said she would improve 'horizon-scanning capability' when it comes to national security, including putting a greater focus on cyber-security and nuclear.
Other pushes would include seconding civil servants to unblock stalling local projects, bringing in experienced staff into Number 10 and beefing up Tory Party headquarters.
However critics are sure to zone in on political weaknesses they claimed existed during the leadership race earlier this summer.
