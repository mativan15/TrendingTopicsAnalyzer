SHENZHEN, China, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Aurora Mobile Limited (NASDAQ: JG) ('Aurora Mobile' or the 'Company'), a leading provider of customer engagement and marketing technology services in China, today announced that it will participate in the joint development of a public service platform for the testing and certification of mobile apps (the 'Platform') initiated by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology ('MIIT'). The development of the Platform, which officially started recently, is undertaken by the China Academy of Information and Communications Technology ('CAICT').
