NSW Labor pledges to boost transport and teaching staff ahead of 2023 election bid
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-16 02:33:59

New South Wales Labor leader Chris Minns has looked to galvanise party faithful 160 days out from the state election in March 2023.He used a speech to a NSW Labor conference to promise a locally built fleet of trains to replace NSW's ageing rail stock and 10,000 extra teachers.'We are ready for the challenge, we want the responsibility,' Minns said on Sunday.The opposition leader said he couldn't fault everything the Coalition had done.'But does anyone truly believe this government's best days are in front of them?' Minns said.
