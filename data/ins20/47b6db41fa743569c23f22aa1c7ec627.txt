In the 89th episode of Mann Ki Baat, Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday highlighted India's achievement of touching 100 unicorns mark in startups earlier this month and said that India's startup ecosystem has penetrated to rural and grassroot levels. Growth rate of Indian unicorns ahead of that of US and UK, he added. "The number of unicorns in country has reached the figure of 100 and a unicorn is a start-up worth at least Rs 7,500 crore. The total valuation of these Unicorns is more than 330 billion dollars, that is, more than Rs 25 lakh crore," said PM Modi.
