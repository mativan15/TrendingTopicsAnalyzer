Redfin, Zillow stocks jump after existing-home sales data top expectations
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 14:18:15

Shares of real estate services companies rallied Thursday, after data showing that existing-home sales fell again in September, but managed to beat expectations. Zillow Group Inc.'s stock Z, +1.97% ZG, +2.43% surged 1.9% in morning trading, reversing an earlier decline of as much as 0.8%, while Redfin Corp. shares RDFN, +4.80% jumped 4.6% after being down 0.3% at its intraday low. Elsewhere, shares of Anywhere Real Estate Inc. HOUS, +2.48% gained 1.5%, RE/MAX Holdings Inc. RMAX, +1.59% tacked on 1.
