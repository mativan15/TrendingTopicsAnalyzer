Upskilling as the need of the hour to keep afloat during the transitions in the work environment
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 08:31:09

The pandemic has triggered, among other things, the rise of the digital economy. Under the circumstances created by the pandemic and the subsequent lockdowns, even companies that were resistant to reforming traditional systems had no choice but to go digital. This development necessitated training in using new tools and reskilling to remain proficient and relevant in the job markets. This need for re-training was further fuelled by the skill crisis that took hold of the global market. In light of these developments, various ed-tech platforms proved to be indispensable in the training landscape.
