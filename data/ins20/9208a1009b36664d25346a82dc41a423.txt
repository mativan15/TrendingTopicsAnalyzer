The 41st annual Fall for Greenville festival continues through Sunday with plenty of food, music, games and more.

People have traveled from Tennessee, Pennsylvania and as far away as Connecticut to enjoy the taste and tunes of some of Greenville's best.

There is still plenty of time to head downtown to enjoy the festivities. Fall for Greenville continues Saturday night until 9 p.m. and concludes Sunday (noon to 7 p.m.)
