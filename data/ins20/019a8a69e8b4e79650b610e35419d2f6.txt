Ban on US Persons at China Chip Firms Thwarts China Prez Xi Jinping's Key Ambition

tech
2022-10-17 16:00:46

China will speed its efforts to build talent and win the battle to develop homegrown tech, Xi Jinping pledged.
 China will speed its efforts to build a legion of talent and win the battle to develop homegrown technologies, President Xi Jinping pledged at the Communist Party's twice-a-decade congress on Sunday. But new US restrictions issued a week earlier are already undercutting those plans.The Biden administration's latest salvo of sanctions includes restrictions on so-called US persons supporting the development, production or use of integrated circuits at some chip plants located in China.
