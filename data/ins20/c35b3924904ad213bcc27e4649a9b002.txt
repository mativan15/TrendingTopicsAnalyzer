Nigel Slater's recipe for grilled chicken and curry sauce
theguardian.com
news
2022-05-31 11:00:39

A chicken supper inspired by a classic Japanese curry.Make the sauce: roughly chop 2 spring onions and put them in the bowl of a food processor. Peel 40g of ginger and 3 cloves of garlic, then add to the onions.Peel and roughly chop 250g of carrots, then add them to the other ingredients together with 350g of tomatoes.Pour in 3 tbsp of runny honey together with 3 tbsp of light soy sauce. Add 2 tbsp of garam masala and 2 of mild curry powder.Halve and seed 1 small red chilli and add that, too. Process to a thick paste, then transfer to a wide, shallow saucepan.
