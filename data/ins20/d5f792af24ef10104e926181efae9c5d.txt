7 Maryland billionaires among the 400 richest in the nation, Forbes says

2022-10-18 15:36:00

By Rohan Mattu October 18, 2022 / 11:36 AM / CBS Baltimore BALTIMORE -- Seven Marylanders landed on Forbes ranking of the 400 wealthiest people in America, with two sports team owners leading the pack. The locals on the list are all white men who are over 60 years old, with the oldest of them nearing 100. With net worths of $6.4 billion Baltimore Ravens owner Steve Bisciotti and Washington Nationals owner Ted Lerner are tied at #143 on the list. Bisciotti co-founded Allegis Group in 1983 with his cousin.
