Partnership with Leading International Payment Provider DeliversVariety of New Payment Options for Operators and Guests
ORLANDO, Fla., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- accesso Technology Group plc (AIM: ACSO), the premier technology solutions provider for attractions and venues worldwide, has established a new integration with PayPal to offer additional online payment flexibility for clients of its award-winning accesso Passport® eCommerce ticketing solution.
accesso first integrated PayPal into the checkout process for accesso Passport online ticketing system users worldwide in 2014.
