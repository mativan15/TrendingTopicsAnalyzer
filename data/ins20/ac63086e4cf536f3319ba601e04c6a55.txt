Portland, OR, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- As per the report published by Allied Market Research, the global smart bathroom market generated $3.77 billion in 2020, and is expected to reach $10.88 billion by 2030, registering a CAGR of 11.2% from 2021 to 2030. Increase in consumer awareness regarding health hygiene, energy conservation, and water conservation along with use of touchless faucets in non-residential facilities drives the growth of the global smart bathroom market. However, high cost of installation hinders the market growth.
