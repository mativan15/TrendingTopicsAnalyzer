UTA Promotes 26 Staffers to Partner, Its Largest Class
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-05-31 17:30:35

The new partners hail from such divisions as motion picture literary and talent, unscripted TV, Ventures, sports, media rights, speakers, digital talent, UTA IQ, music, legal and human resources. Less than two years after announcing its largest partner class in agency history with the elevation of 19 agents to partner status, UTA is back at it again, this time promoting 26 staffers across 15 divisions.
The new partners, now the largest set in UTA's 31-year-history, include agents and execs from motion picture literary and talent groups, unscripted television, ventures, production arts, sports, MediaLink, media rights, UTA Speakers, digital talent, UTA IQ, and music, as well as corporate departments legal and human resources.
