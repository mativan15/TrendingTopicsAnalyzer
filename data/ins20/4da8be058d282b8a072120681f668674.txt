'The need to take care of mental health is more than ever now,' says actor Aniruddh Dave, who adds that nowadays there is a lot of stress that one has to go through. The actor says that it's important to be aware of how one is feeling and concentrate on feeling better.'I think that in the last few years people must have understood very well that mental health is very important. Because, day by day, whether it is personal life or professional life, stress is increasing. Even in other occupations, everywhere there are targets, there is competition.
