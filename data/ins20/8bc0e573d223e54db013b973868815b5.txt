October 19, 2022 11:46 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ICR, a leading strategic communications and advisory firm, is pleased to announce that Nitin Chexal, CEO of Palladius Capital Management, recently appeared on Episode 48 of ICR's 'Welcome to the Arena' podcast.

Welcome to the Arena is hosted by Tom Ryan, Co-Founder & CEO of ICR. Tom spoke with Nitin about Palladius Capital Management's bold approach to acquisitions, the ways in which technology is being incorporated into the firm's portfolio and operations, what trends he expects in real estate over the next few years, and more.
