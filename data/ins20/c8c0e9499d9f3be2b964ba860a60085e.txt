If you're one of those folks who has been grinding Overwatch 2 to level up the Battle Pass as swiftly as possible, you might want to get in as many games as you can this weekend. Overwatch 2 is running its first double XP event and it's live now. The event will run until 2PM ET on Monday. That's 72 hours during which you'll get twice as much XP as usual. If you have the 20% boost from the premium Battle Pass and play a bunch of games this weekend, you'll be be able to make a lot of progress toward that rad Genji skin at level 80.
