Crypto scammers stole nearly $1m a day from Aussie investors in the first four months of the year, prompting the consumer watchdog to sound the alarm.The Australian Competition and Consumer Commission on Monday announced cryptocurrency fraudsters had been busy since January and were the main reason Australians had more than doubled their total scam losses compared to the same time in 2021.Total scam losses between January 1 and the end of April amounted to $205m – up 166 per cent – and were dominated by investment scams.
