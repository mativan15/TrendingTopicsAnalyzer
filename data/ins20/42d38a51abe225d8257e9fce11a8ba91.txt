Naked Juice's 'Goodness Outside' Commitment Provides Another $50,000 to Alleviate Poverty Through Whole Planet Foundation
yahoo.com
finance
2022-05-31 18:36:06

Northampton, MA --News Direct-- Whole Foods Market Foundations

Naked Juice, a Tropicana Brands Group product, is a supporter of Whole Planet Foundation since 2007, and it is renewing its $50,000 per year commitment to the Foundation's Supplier Alliance for Microcredit. This year, Naked Juice is funding essential microbusinesses to alleviate global poverty through the Foundation's microlending programs. Naked Juice, which can be found in select stores and online on Whole Foods Market on Amazon, is committed to giving opportunities through microcredit to the world's impoverished entrepreneurs for the chance to create or expand a business, often home-based, to generate income for themselves and their families.
