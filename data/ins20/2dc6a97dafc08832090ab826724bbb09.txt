Image caption, Gordon Sparks commentated on Plymouth Argyle's games for 32 yearsThe BBC's director general has paid tribute to BBC Radio Devon presenter and former Plymouth Argyle commentator Gordon Sparks.Sparksy, as he was known, presented the breakfast show at the station for many years and commentated on Plymouth Argyle games for 32 years.The 61-year-old was being treated for cancer at a hospice in Plymouth.Tim Davie said he was "deeply saddened" to hear the news, adding he was "the gold standard in radio broadcasting".
