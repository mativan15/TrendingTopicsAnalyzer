Advertisers' budget cuts hurt Snap
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

Snapchat's sales growth has slowed to its weakest level yet as a public company as advertisers continue to cut spending in the face of growing fears about the strength of the economy.Shares in Snap, the social media platform's owner, fell by as much as a quarter last night after it warned that it was not out of the woods.'We expect that the operating environment will continue to be challenging in the months ahead,' the business said after narrowly falling short of Wall Street's revenue expectations.
