A startup that enables companies to optimize their marketing and revenue streams through partner channels has raised a $6 million seed round from Insight Partners.Relevize, which was launched in Boston in 2020, has designed a platform that allows companies to simplify the way they use channels such as LinkedIn, Facebook, and Hubspot, among others, to distribute digital campaigns.The startup's cofounder and CEO Michael Nardella said it enabled marketing teams to "maximize the return on channel marketing spend.
