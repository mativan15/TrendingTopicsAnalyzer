June 02, 2022 03:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--To continue driving the innovation behind its industry-leading platforms and solutions, Calix, Inc. (NYSE: CALX) announced the grand opening of the Calix India Development Center (CIDC) in Bengaluru, India. The 21,000-square-foot facility will expand research and development (R&D), Commercial Operations, and Marketing to support the next phase of hypergrowth for Calix. Calix chose to invest in Bengaluru, 'India's Silicon Valley,' for its new development center because of its position at the forefront of information technology in India.
