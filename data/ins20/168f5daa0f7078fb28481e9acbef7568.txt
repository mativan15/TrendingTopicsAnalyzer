ED attaches properties of Surya Pharmaceuticals in Rs 828 crore bank fraud case

LUDHIANA: In a latest action by the Chandigarh Zonal office of the Directorate of Enforcement (ED), properties worth Rs 185.1 crore belonging to Surya Pharmaceuticals Limited and others have been provisionally attached by the agency.The properties include building, plant and machinery located in several places in Haryana, Chandigarh & Mohali.The development is fall out of the FIRs registered by CBI against the company and its owners for defrauding several banks of Rs 828 crore.According to a statement issued by ED on the development , 'ED has provisionally attached properties worth Rs 185.
