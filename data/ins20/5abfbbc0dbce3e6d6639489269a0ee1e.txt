There's big news for Guardant Health (GH -5.61%) shareholders today -- but disappointing news also. This morning, analysts at investment bank Craig-Hallum announced a switch in their bets on cancer-screening biopsy companies, downgrading shares of Exact Sciences (EXAS -6.69%) from buy to hold -- and replacing them with a bet on Guardant Health -- initiated at buy.
Responding to the news, investors are selling off Exact Sciences stock by 6.5% today, as of 10:45 a.m. But here's the bad news: they're selling Guardant Health too -- it's down 5.
