Over the last decade, the large casino companies in Australia have been masters at rebranding their businesses as 'integrated casinos' – holistic entertainment establishments with hotels, restaurants, theatres, retail shops and convention centres – rather than enormous barns full of gaming machines for mass-market players and a series of suites for VIP players.The regulator has been disingenuous in mislabelling big casinos as part of the state's tourism infrastructure.Casinos are not roads, schools or hospitals.
