AFPIn the fiscal year ending December 2021, Puma India cemented its leadership position in India's sports and lifestyle market, earning a revenue of Rs 2044 crore. Sportswear brand Puma India has opened its first store in the Republic of Maldives, entering the popular holiday destination that has witnessed a triple-digit surge in inbound tourism over the last two years. With this, Maldives becomes the latest market to join Puma India's syndicate which includes 19 other countries such as Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam and Philippines.
