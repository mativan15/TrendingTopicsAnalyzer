Image source, Met PoliceImage caption, The body of Londoner Mee Kuen Chong was found more than 200 miles away in DevonA devout Christian urged her friend to sell her home for £4m before being decapitated by her, a court heard. Jemma Mitchell, 38, is accused of murdering Mee Kuen Chong after the 67-year-old changed her mind about helping to fund the renovation of her home.The prosecution claim Ms Chong was killed at her home in Wembley before her headless body was dumped in woods, near Salcombe, Devon, in June 2021.
