San Diego, CA, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- via  NewMediaWire --  Forza Innovations Inc.  (OTC Pink: FORZ) (the 'Company' or 'Forza') a health-tech wearable company based out of the Innovation Lab in San Diego, is pleased to provide a company update for Sustainable Origins Inc. ('Sustainable'), our first acquisition under the Forza umbrella.  Based in Charlotte, North Carolina, Sustainable Origins is in the business of collections and refinement of renewable fuel inputs. Sustainable continues to expand steadily since the last update provided in a press release dated April 19, 2022.
