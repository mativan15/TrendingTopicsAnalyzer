Five neighbourhoods in the UK known for their vibrancy, culture and history have been rated among the 51 coolest neighbourhoods in the world in a survey released by Time OutA panoramic city centre view over Scotland's largest city, GlasgowFive spots in the UK have been rated among the world's 51 coolest neighbourhoods. Cliftonville in Margate, Shawlands in Glasgow, Walthamstow in London, the Northern Quarter in Manchester and Kelham Island in Sheffield all managed to snag a place on the list. The survey was compiled and released by Time Out after speaking with experts and conducting polls.
