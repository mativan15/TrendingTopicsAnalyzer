Custom-casket maker Trey Ganem creates and donates coffins for the 19 kids massacred in Texas
nydailynews.com
news
2022-05-31 22:04:12

No sooner did Trey Ganem hear about the elementary school massacre in Uvalde, Texas, than he was on the road.The custom-casket maker posted a quick prayer on Facebook and was on his way.'Please pray for Uvalde!!' wrote the owner of SoulShine Industries, based in Edna, Texas, east of San Antonio. 'I'm on my way to help families in this tragic time. I was asked to help with the caskets and talk with families. This is something no family should ever have to deal with. My love and emotions are already there.
