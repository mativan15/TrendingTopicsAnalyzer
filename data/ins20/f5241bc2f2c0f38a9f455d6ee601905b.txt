Variety - TV News Audiences have embraced all of Oprah Winfrey's signature looks from the fluffed pixie to the long under bob, and now her signature curls. But they may not have known about Winfrey's lifelong hair journey that led to her current look or even what drove Issa Rae to appreciate and love her natural hair after going completely bald. Now both stories are being told in Hulu's upcoming docuseries, 'The Hair Tales,' which premieres on Oct. 22 on Hulu, as well as OWN.
