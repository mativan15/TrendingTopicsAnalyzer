The Federal Reserve's interest-rate hikes to bring inflation under control are starting work, while President Joe Biden is doing everything possible to support the US central bank, two administration economic advisers said.

'What we can see in the economic data is the Fed is focused on bringing down inflation,' Cecilia Rouse, chair of Biden's council of economic advisers, said on CNN's 'State of the Union' on Sunday.
