CALGARY, AB, Oct. 19, 2022 /CNW/ - Tuktu Resources Ltd. (formerly Jasper Mining Corporation) ("Tuktu" or the "Company") (TSXV: JSP) is pleased to announce that all matters put before its shareholders were approved at its annual and special meeting of shareholders held today (the "Meeting").

Approvals included changing the Company's name from "Jasper Mining Corporation" to "Tuktu Resources Ltd.
