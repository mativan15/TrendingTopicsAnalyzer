Kansys picks its new CEO from a firm under its parent company
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 12:45:00

Kansys Inc., an Olathe-based software and managed services provider, named Kurt Daniel as its new CEO.

Daniel comes to Kansys from software company Ubersmith. Ubersmith and Kansys share parent company Lumine Group, which announced it would acquire Kansys in October 2021 for an undisclosed amount.

Daniel has more than 20 years of experience in software management and business development, both at startups and large companies, including Microsoft, Parallels and Worklight, which was acquired by IBM.
