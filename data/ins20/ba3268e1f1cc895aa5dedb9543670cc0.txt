Elastic stock jumps on Q4 results beat; analysts note strong growth in cloud business

Simonkolton/iStock via Getty Images Elastic (NYSE:ESTC) stock is trading 16.5% higher at $72.14 in Thursday afternoon trading, after the application software company reported Q4 fiscal 2022 earnings that beat estimates and provided guidance for Q1 and FY 2023. The Mountain View, Calif.-based firm on Wednesday posted Q4 non-GAAP EPS of -$0.16 which beat expectations by $0.05. Q4 revenue was $239.36M, which beat by $6.98M. For Q1, the company expects revenue to be between $244M-$246M vs. consensus revenue estimates of $245.
