After the disastrous effect of vaccine nationalism on access in Africa, boosting local production is key to preventing a repeat in future pandemics. WHO's new mRNA vaccine hub is at the forefront, report Emma Bryce and Sandy Ong

In June 2021, the World Health Organization selected South African biotech company Afrigen to be part of the 'hub' where mRNA technology—which underpins the most effective covid-19 vaccines—would be developed and shared with other lower and middle income countries.
