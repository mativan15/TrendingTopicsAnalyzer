Auckland business grows veges and honey to help workers with cost of living
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-10-19 16:00:00

Auckland wine and spirit distributor, Hancock's, has begun harvesting fruit, vegetables, and honey on its warehouse forecourt to help its workers manage the cost of living. The initiative has seen 20 planter boxes, seven beehives, and 30 fruit trees moved on to the site to help feed the 200 staff. Finance manager, and garden volunteer, Dave Gollop​ estimated the initiative saved him at least $20 to $30 a week on groceries.
