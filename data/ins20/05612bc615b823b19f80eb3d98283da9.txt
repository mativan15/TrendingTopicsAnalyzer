'One sub sector which I want to highly focus on is probably iron ore. Iron ore prices have bottomed in India and are largely decoupled from global markets,' says Rakesh Arora, Managing Partner, Go India Advisors. 
One section of the market is very cautious and is saying that India cannot outperform and consumption here would also be hit. The other section of the market is looking at indicators and consumption, quality of earnings, management commentary and taking a lot of heart from the fact that India is now emerging as a separate asset class in its own way on a global scale.
