Easy hack shows how to clean jewellery with baking soda
metro.co.uk
news
2022-05-29 08:39:49

We show our beloved jewellery collections a lot of love – so it's only a matter of time before some pieces (especially rings) start to lose their shine.
But that doesn't mean it's time to replace them.
A woman on TikTok has revealed how to give old jewellery a new lease of life – without the need for professionals. 
Australian retail worker Emma has shared how she transformed her silver jewellery using a common household staple.
In a video posted on TikTok, she explains that all you need is hot water and some baking soda.
