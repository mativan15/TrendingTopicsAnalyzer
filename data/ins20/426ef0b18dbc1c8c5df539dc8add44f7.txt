Who and what you missed at (Em)Power Women: Find Your Fit
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 16:00:00

More than 100 people gathered Thursday morning for (Em)Power Women: Find Your Fit, the latest in a Business Review series that's highlighting real-life stories from women in all stages of their career.

The event at The Century House in Latham was an interactive discussion in which the panelists talked about their own career experiences while offering advice based on audience questions. The conversation touched on topics such as confidence when you're first starting out, the role of mentors and sponsors, and the importance of sometimes saying "no.
