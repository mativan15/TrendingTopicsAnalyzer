Image: Getty Images/iStockphotoWhen it comes to remote working, employees say they can be relied upon to get the job done – but don't necessarily say the same about their colleagues.A report by Cisco on the impact of hybrid working found that workers view themselves as more dependable than their co-workers in being productive while working from home.In a survey of 1,050 UK employees, 75% of respondents said their manager trusted them to be productive while working remotely, yet a lower proportion (61%) felt their colleagues could be trusted to do the same.
