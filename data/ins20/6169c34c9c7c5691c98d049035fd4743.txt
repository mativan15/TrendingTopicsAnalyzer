Taxes will go up and departments including defence face spending cuts, the new Chancellor warned this morning as the Government changes tack on its economic policy.Liz Truss said on Wednesday she would "absolutely" not cut public spending and made pledges to cut tax a major part of her Conservative leadership campaign this summer.But Jeremy Hunt, who the Prime Minister appointed yesterday afternoon, warned nine times in one interview that "difficult decisions" would be needed in the coming weeks and months.
