Cook Islands Prime Minister Mark Brown has revealed his hopes for financial support to tackle climate change following the signing of a new partnership deal with Australia.Foreign Affairs Minister Penny Wong has signed the Oa Tumanava partnership deal with the Pacific nation as a pledge of Australia's support towards its neighbour.'This partnership is a really important step, we have never had this sort of agreement before,' Ms Wong said.'Oa Tumanava' roughly translates to friendship in times of peace and in times of adversity.
