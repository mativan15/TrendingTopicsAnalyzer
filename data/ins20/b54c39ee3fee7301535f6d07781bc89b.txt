Gogglebox star Mica thanks fans for support after family tragedy as she takes break from social media
thesun.co.uk

2022-10-17 13:55:09

GOGGLEBOX star Mica Ven has thanked fans for their support after a death in the family.
The Channel 4 star, who appears on Gogglebox with long-term partner Marcus Luther, updated fans on Instagram in an emotional post.
Marcus Luther and Mica Ven are regulars on Gogglebox
She said: "To everyone that has sent love, prayers, concern, well wishes and condolences to our family, on behalf of Marky & I we thank you.
"I Hope you all have a blessed Day and remember to enjoy each other, blow the dust off them board games, catch some joke, life and memories are so precious.
