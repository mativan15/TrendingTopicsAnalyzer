Delete statements on CM Eknath Shinde, his deputy chief minister Devendra Fadnavis from PIL, orders Bombay HC
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 02:00:00

MUMBAI: The Bombay high court on Thursday directed the deletion of statements in a PIL that questioned the integrity of CM Eknath Shinde and his deputy Devendra Fadnavis to run the government after it halted all tribal development works and schemes approved by the MVA ."Respect has to be given to every constitutional functionary," CJ Dipankar Datta and Justice Madhav Jamdar said. They directed the petitioner, tribal activist Gopal Lahange , to delete particulars of the CM, deputy CM and ministers of tribal development, social welfare and justice, rural development, and PWD from the list of respondents.
