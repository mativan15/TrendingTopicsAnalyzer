Oct. 16—Is such a thing possible in Northeast Ohio? Is a fan base that has bled brown and orange since the days of Paul Brown and Otto Graham beginning to bleed Guardians blue and red instead?

The contrast between the mood at Progressive Field on Oct. 15 and the mood at FirstEnergy Stadium less than 24 hours later was like the difference between the temperature at the equator and the temperature in Antarctica.

Joy, screaming, back slapping, hugs all around and fireworks — that was Progressive Field after Oscar Gonzalez singled to drive in two runs with two out in the bottom of the ninth to lift the Guardians to a dramatic 6-5 thriller over the Yankees in Game 3 of the ALDS.
