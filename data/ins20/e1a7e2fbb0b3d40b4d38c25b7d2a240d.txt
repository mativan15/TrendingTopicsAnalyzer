If we want to play the growth game, one sector deserves our support
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

Asked to name a British business sector in which employment has grown by nearly 15 per cent during the past year, you might plump for a one getting back on its feet after the pandemic, such as hospitality. Told that sector had attracted at least £7.7 billion of investment in the past five years, you might suggest one renowned for innovation, such as financial technology. Or on learning that the average annual salary in this sector is £45,000, you might opt for one with high entry qualifications, like biotechnology.
