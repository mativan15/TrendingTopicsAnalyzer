Ambarella: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
sfgate.com
business
2022-05-31 20:32:17

SANTA CLARA, Calif. (AP) _ Ambarella Inc. (AMBA) on Tuesday reported a loss of $10.8 million in its fiscal first quarter.On a per-share basis, the Santa Clara, California-based company said it had a loss of 29 cents. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to 44 cents per share. The results beat Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of nine analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 37 cents per share. The video-compression chipmaker posted revenue of $90.
