SBA's Minnesota chief: Have a plan when you apply for a government loan
startribune.com
news
2022-05-30 19:00:00

Brian McDonald worked at the headquarters of the Small Business Administration in Washington, D.C., before moving to the Minneapolis district office in 2017 and being named district director in May 2020. "Our team is very mission oriented, and it's a great mission," McDonald said. "Meeting with small-business owners directly and seeing the impact that they're having, they really keep our communities livable and vibrant; they're 50 percent of the workforce." In an interview, he offered a few ideas for entrepreneurs seeking SBA loans.
