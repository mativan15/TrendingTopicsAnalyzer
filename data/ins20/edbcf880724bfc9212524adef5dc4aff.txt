October 19, 2022 08:24 AM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best will participate at the Federation of Defense and Corporate Counsel's (FDCC) Insurance Industry Institute (I-3) event, which takes place Nov. 10-11, 2022, in New York.



The I-3 program brings together leading industry experts to discuss the state of the insurance marketplace following the pandemic and examine current insurance coverage issues and line of business opportunities to address current and future risk exposures.
