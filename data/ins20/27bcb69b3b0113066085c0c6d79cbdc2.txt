Goat Island in Sydney Harbour will be handed back to Aboriginal locals The island, known as Me-Mel, in the indigenous language is a heritage-listed siteOne colonial building 'The Queen's Magazine' was used as a gunpowder store The island is off-limits except for guided tours by the National Parks Service  By Nick Gibbs For Australian Associated Press Published: 02:13 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 02:13 EDT, 29 May 2022 A Sydney Harbour island is returning to Aboriginal hands with the NSW government committing $43 million to its clean-up and repair.
