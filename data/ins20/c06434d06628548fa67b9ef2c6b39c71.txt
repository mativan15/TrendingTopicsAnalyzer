An interactive, D&D-inspired theater show is coming to Chicago
timeout.com
news
2022-10-21 17:08:42

Dungeons & Dragons and other fantasy-inspired TTRPGs (tabletop roleplaying games) are enjoying a burst of popularity that's represented in everything from online streaming series to appearances on Stranger Things. Now, a new interactive show coming to Chicago is giving seasoned tabletop players—and curious newcomers—an opportunity to step outside theatre of the mind and explore this fantasy world in, well, a literal theater. The Twenty-Sided Tavern, which begins its residency at the Broadway Playhouse on October 27, is an immersive theatrical experience inspired by games that 'might involve dungeons and might involve dragons.
