We are pleased to offer this brilliant Band H opportunity supporting Adult Social Care until March '23.



This Business Support Administrator role will be assisting the Essex Safeguarding Adults Board (ESAB). The board is a statutory organisation that is committed to protecting an adult's right to live in safety, free from abuse and neglect. It is committed to ensuring that it has access to the view of adults and care and support needs as well as those that work with them.
