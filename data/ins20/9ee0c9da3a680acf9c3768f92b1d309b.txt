Berger Paints shares drop 0.48% as Sensex falls
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 08:15:00

Shares of Berger Paints (India) Ltd. traded 0.48 per cent lower in Tuesday's session at 01:14PM (IST). The stock opened at Rs 609.0 and has touched an intraday high and low of Rs 609.65 and Rs 593.3, respectively, during the session so far. The stock quoted a 52-week high of Rs 872.0 and a 52-week low of 543.85. About 86,007 shares changed hands on the counter so far. Benchmark Nifty50 was down 56.35 points at 16605.05, while the BSE Sensex traded 259.76 points lower at 55665.98 at the time of writing of this report.
