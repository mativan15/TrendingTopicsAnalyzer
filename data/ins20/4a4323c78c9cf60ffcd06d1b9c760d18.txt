Published: 17:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 17:43 EDT, 20 October 2022 President Joe Biden on Thursday downplayed concerns the economic turmoil in the United Kingdom will spill over into the U.S. amid reports his administration has been warned a similar meltdown could happen. Biden was asked if he was worried about the political and economic turmoil in the UK coming his way after Prime Minister Liz Truss resigned after her fiscal agenda was dismantled.  'No, I don't think they're that consequential,' he said.
