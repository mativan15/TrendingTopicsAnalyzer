REUTERS/Dado Ruvic/IllustrationCHISINAU, Oct 20 (Reuters) - The head of Moldova's natural gas company said it had paid $53.5 million to Russian state gas firm Gazprom (GAZP.MM) on Thursday to settle its bill for September gas deliveries, and sent it a $27.4 million advance payment for October deliveries.Moldovagaz director Vadim Ceban wrote on the Telegram messaging app that the company had received a loan from the government so that it could pay for supplies.
