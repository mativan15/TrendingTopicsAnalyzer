Unison chief warns NHS faces threat of mass walkouts over pay this winter
mirror.co.uk
politics
2022-10-16 23:01:00

Exclusive: Unison General Secretary Christina McAnea said health unions were planning to team up to pile pressure on the Government - and coordination could spread to other sectors like railThe NHS could be hit by mass strike action this winter if the Tories refuse to give workers decent pay rises. Unison General Secretary Christina McAnea said health unions were planning to team up to pile pressure on the Government - and coordination could spread to other sectors like rail. Nearly 1 million workers across the NHS could be involved in industrial action in the coming months if members of health unions representing nurses, midwives, ambulance crews and physiotherapists vote for walkouts.
