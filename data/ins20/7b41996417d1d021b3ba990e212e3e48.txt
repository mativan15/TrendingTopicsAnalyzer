Gang jailed after being found guilty of modern slavery offences
sky.com
news
2022-05-27 09:36:00

Five members of a prostitution and modern slavery gang have been jailed for a number of years.They were convicted at Isleworth Crown Court in March of conspiracy to arrange or facilitate the travel of another person with a view of exploitation and conspiracy to control prostitution for gain after a 10-week trial.
Gregaor Borowka, 44; Sebastian Zimoch, 48; Michael Lozinksi, 52; Rafal Lacki, 41, and Anna Zimoch, 45 have now been sentenced.Micheal Lozinski got seven years in jail, Sebastian Zimoch was sentenced to eight years, Gregaor Borowka was given three years and nine months, Rafal Lacki was sentenced to 18 months (time already served), and Anna Zimoch got two years, suspended for two years and 150 hours unpaid work.
