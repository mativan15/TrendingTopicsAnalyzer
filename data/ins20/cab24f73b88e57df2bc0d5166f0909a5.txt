Back in the Switch's inaugural year of 2017, most early adopters showed up for two massive titles: The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild and Super Mario Odyssey. Both were major reinventions of their respective franchises and their one-two punch in the console's launch year functioned as a mea culpa for Nintendo's mishandling of the Wii U. In one year, the company had effectively launched two killer apps that were better than the majority of the games released over the course of an entire generation.
