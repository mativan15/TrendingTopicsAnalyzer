Karnataka CM Basavaraj Bommai announces Puneeth Rajkumar to get 'Karnataka Ratna' posthumously on November 1
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 07:00:00

Puneeth Rajkumar, who passed away on Oct 29 last year, will be conferred with the 'Karnataka Ratna' award posthumously on November 1 for his contributions to the Kannada film industry. Karnataka CM Basavaraj Bommai announced the news. 'Karnataka Ratna' is the highest civilian award of the Government of Karnataka. The presentation ceremony will be held in front of the Vidhana Soudha (legislative assembly) in Bengaluru on November 1. This was decided at a meeting with the ministers, senior government officials and family members of the late actor.
