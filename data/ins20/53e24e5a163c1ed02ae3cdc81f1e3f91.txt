Anthony Castrovince@castrovinceWhen baseball fans salute former Major League reliever Jim Poole during a pregame Lou Gehrig Day presentation at Camden Yards on Thursday, they'll see him restricted to his wheelchair as his son, Hayden, throws out a ceremonial first pitch on his behalf. They'll see the physical toll that amyotrophic lateral sclerosis, or ALS, has taken on Jim's body.
What they won't see is the emotional and financial toll this terrible illness has taken on Jim and his wife, Kim, in the one year since Jim's diagnosis.
