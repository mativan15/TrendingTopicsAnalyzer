Published: 09:23 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 09:24 EDT, 5 June 2022 Martin Lewis, Sir Cliff Richard, Gary Lineker, Dame Shirley Bassey and Idris Elba are among the 150 celebrities set to travel in double decker buses to pay homage to the Queen's final day of her Platinum Jubilee celebrations.The colourful open top buses will each mark the seven decades of Her Majesty's reign, which will each be adorned with images and famous slogans from the time, including 'Groovy' from the 1970s and 'Girl Power' - brought into the mainstream by British pop icons The Spice Girls in the 1990s.
