 A man and woman were killed in a two-car crash in the Rural Municipality of Ste. Anne Friday, police say.
Emergency crews were called to crash on Highway 12 at Two Mile Road around 10:10 p.m.Investigators say a car was heading south on Highway 12 when it hit an SUV that was crossing the highway at Two Mile Road.
