Broe Real Estate Group, Alvarez & Marsal Capital Real Estate Partner to Attract Fortune 500 Company

DENVER, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Broe Real Estate Group (BREG), the wholly-owned subsidiary of the multi-billion-dollar, Colorado private investment firm, The Broe Group, in conjunction with Alvarez & Marsal Capital Real Estate (A&M CapRE), the real estate investment management arm of Alvarez & Marsal, has signed Micron Technologies, Inc. as the lead tenant for their new joint venture, MAX Technology Center (MTC).
