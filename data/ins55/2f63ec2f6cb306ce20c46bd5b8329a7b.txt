A Glasgow mum has revealed how she manages to feed a family of four for a week for just £25.

Eilidh Wells has scoured the aisles at Aldi stores to find the best deals and purchased products that can be stretched across seven days while not impacting the weekly food budget.

The cost-of-living crisis has impacted food prices, and the school holidays leave parents with more meals to provide during the week.
