Amy JamesOct 21, 2022With prices sky high these days, we're all looking to save money where we can and more buyers are turning to online marketplaces for bargains. While peer-to-peer marketplaces like OfferUp and Facebook Marketplace can be great for finding deals, authorities are warning they're also home to scammers. And police departments across the country are investigating robberies and fraud related to these kinds of online purchases. Commander Rodney Hill of the Chicago Police Department says their force is investigating dozens of robberies over the last five months where suspects have allegedly used online listings to entice victims.
