Published: 05:54 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 05:59 EDT, 20 October 2022 Australia's T20 World Cup preparations have been dealt a major blow after Josh Inglis was ruled out of the tournament following an 'incredibly freak' golfing accident.The wicketkeeper-batter cut his right hand when the metal handle of his 6-iron snapped on impact as Inglis was playing at the New South Wales Golf Club on Wednesday.The 27-year-old was subsequently taken to hospital and Australia coach Andrew McDonald confirmed on Thursday the injury had ruled Inglis out of the World Cup.
