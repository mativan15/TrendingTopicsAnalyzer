Doberman's sweet reaction to pregnant owner's baby kicking melts hearts
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-20 10:00:07

A dog's adorable reaction to feeling his pregnant owner's baby kick is melting hearts online.The cute clip was posted to TikTok on October 6 by user Sherrell Rell Kelly (@sherrellmichelle), who was 35 weeks pregnant at the time. Resting his head on her stomach, Doberman DeMar displays a look of surprise and excitement as her baby bump moves.Since being shared, the video has received almost 25 million views and nearly 6 million likes.
