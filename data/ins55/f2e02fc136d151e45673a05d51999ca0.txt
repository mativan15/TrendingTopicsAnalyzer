The Delhi High Court agreed to hear on Friday a plea by the Enforcement Directorate (ED) challenging a trial court's order allowing the presence of a lawyer during the interrogation of Health Minister Satyendar Jain, arrested in a money laundering case.The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader was arrested by the ED on May 30 under the provisions of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA).
