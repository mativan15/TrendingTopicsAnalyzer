Stocks Rise As Investors Await U.S. Jobs Data For Fed Cues
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-03 02:38:27

Asian shares rose broadly on Friday morning after softer-than-expected U.S. employment data raised the possibility of the Federal Reserve turning less aggressive on its policy tightening stance in coming months.MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan was up 0.45%, riding on a strong Wall Street close overnight. Japan's Nikkei was up 0.96%, and shares in Seoul opened up 0.77%, while Australia's resource-heavy index was up 0.81%.Overnight, tech stocks led a rally on Wall Street, lifting the S&P500 1.
