Published: 19:59 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 19:59 EDT, 31 May 2022 Aussie shoppers can now save time at the Woolworths checkout by using a new way to pay for groceries that few are aware of. Using the Everyday Rewards app, customers can scan their card and pay in one seamless transaction using a stored card. To pay simply scan the QR code at the self-serve checkout, scan the items then select 'Pay Now' to instantly pay. The technology will save customers time shuffling through cards while standing at the checkout.
