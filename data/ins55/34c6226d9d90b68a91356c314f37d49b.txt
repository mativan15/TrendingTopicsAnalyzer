Latino Media Network's Stephanie Valencia and Jess Morales Rocketto. Latino Media Network/TelevisaUnivision 
Two Latina social entrepreneurs today officially launched the Latino Media Network (LMN), a new media content creation, talent incubation and distribution company, aimed at reaching the Hispanic market, initially focused on audio. Stephanie Valencia and Jess Morales Rocketto raised $80 million - one of the largest capital raises of a Latina owned and operated startup in the U.S - from a diverse set of investors.
