The Bolingbrook location will be open from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. on weekdays, 8 a.m. to midnight on Fridays and Saturdays, and is closed on Sundays. ​ (Shutterstock)BOLINGBROOK, IL — If you have a sweet tooth, you'll be happy to hear about Bolingbrook's newest business. Crumbl Cookies, known for its pink boxes full of huge cookies, opened Friday in Bolingbrook at 763 E. Boughton Road. Store owners Traci and Frankie Pretzel have spent months working on the new Crumbl location, according to a news release.
