Flames, Calgary city officials to resume talks on new arena
espn.com
news
2022-10-19 20:19:08

4:19 PM ETCALGARY, Alberta -- The Calgary Flames and city officials said Wednesday they will restart negotiations for a new arena.Their previous deal, agreed upon in 2019, collapsed late last year when the estimated cost of the project rose and the Flames withdrew. In January, the city hired third-party negotiators to resurrect the project, and those efforts led to Wednesday's announcement.The Flames have played for 39 years in their current home, the Saddledome, the second-oldest NHL arena behind New York's Madison Square Garden.
