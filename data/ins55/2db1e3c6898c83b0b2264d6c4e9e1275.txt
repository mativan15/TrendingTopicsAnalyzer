Miami, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sky Pegasus has announced its launch as the first design and web development company for cryptocurrency projects. With an amazing team of talented artists and developers, Sky Pegasus is sure to make a mark in the crypto industry. They are focusing on Branding, NFTS, and Web Development, however, they will also start to make Web3 applications for clients soon. This makes Sky Pegasus the only one-stop-shop studio in the market of crypto.
