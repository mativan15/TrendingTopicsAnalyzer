Who is Tom Allen?
Tom Allen was born in Bromley, Greater London on June 14, 1983.
When he was just 22, Tom won the UK's most prestigious comedy newcomer award So You Think You're Funny in 2005 - he also won the BBC New Comedy Awards in the same year.
The comic supported Sarah Millican on her sold out 2016 tour of Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.
