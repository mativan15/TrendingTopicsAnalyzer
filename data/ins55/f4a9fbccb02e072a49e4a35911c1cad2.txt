Kolkata: Life sentence for Musa in 2014 Burdwan bomb blast case
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 19:30:00

A special NIA court in Kolkata on Friday awarded life sentence to Mohd Mosiuddin, alias Musa, in connection with Burdwan bomb blast in 2014. Musa was arrested from a passenger train in West Bengal's Burdwan in July 2016 by the state CID, following which the NIA had taken over the probe. A .38 bore six-chamber revolver, three rounds of ammunition, a knife, a mobile phone and other incriminating articles were seized from him. A laptop and a sword were seized from his house in Tamil Nadu.

Musa was arrested while he was planning to carry out attacks on foreign tourists in West Bengal and Srinagar.
