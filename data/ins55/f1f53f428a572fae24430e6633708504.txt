Milk delivery firm Country Delight plans to foray into kitchen and staples category; become a tech-driven FMCG brand
indiatimes.com
food
2022-10-15 11:00:00

Chakradhar Gade, Co-founder & CEO, Country DelightNew Delhi: Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) milk delivery startup, Country Delight plans to expand its product offerings to the entire kitchen and staples segment in 12 months, thus positioning itself as a tech-driven FMCG firm, Chakradhar Gade, CEO and co-founder of the company shared with ETRetail in an interaction. 'Our vision is to cover everything in a kitchen,' said Gade. The company presently delivers fresh milk, fruits, vegetables, bread, and eggs directly from farmers to customers within 24-36 hours.
