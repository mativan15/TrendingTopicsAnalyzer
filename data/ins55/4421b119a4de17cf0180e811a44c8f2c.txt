(Yonhap)President Yoon Suk-yeol is likely to move into the new presidential residence in Hannam-dong, Yongsan-gu, Seoul, within this month after a monthslong delay.Yoon has been commuting to work from his home in Seocho-gu, southern Seoul since his inauguration in May.An official from the presidential office said at a briefing on Monday, "We are currently conducting a final inspection of the security and safety (for the relocation).
