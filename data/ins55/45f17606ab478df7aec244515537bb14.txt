 Police are seeking the public's assistance in identifying three suspects wanted in connection with a shooting investigation in Toronto earlier this year.
Toronto police said on June 19 at around 2:40 p.m., officers received a report of a shooting in the Humber Boulevard and Alliance Avenue area.
