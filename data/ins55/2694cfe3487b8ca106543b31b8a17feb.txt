After ensuring PAN and eKYC , NRIs can easily invest in the Indian capital markets. They can invest in equity shares, mutual funds, national pension schemes, etc. Apart from this, they invest in the real estate sector and fixed deposits linked through NRE, NRO or FCNR accounts.
