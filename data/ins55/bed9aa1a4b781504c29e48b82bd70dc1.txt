iStockThe tourism sector in the state, like everywhere else in the country and the world, was severely affected during the coronavirus pandemic.The Rajasthan government Saturday accorded industry status to the tourism and hospitality sector in the state. According to an official statement, Urban Housing Development and Local Self Department Minister Shanti Dhariwal issued a notification to this effect on Saturday. In the budget announcement in February, Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot had said the tourism and hospitality sector will be granted industry status.
