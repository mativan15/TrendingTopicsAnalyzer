LAS VEGAS, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- For mobile games enjoyer, this more than a fun game – Swipe, an atypical casino game developed by Expanse Studios, comes with very simple rules. It will appeal to all players who like unconventional games, with a touch of adrenaline, but lots and lots of fun! The goal of the game is to make a perfect combination of numbers that will correspond to randomly selected digits of the game – the closer you get to it, the more money you win!
