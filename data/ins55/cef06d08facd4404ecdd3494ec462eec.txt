Image source, Russell CarterImage caption, Dennis Carter (left) with his son-in-law Mark following a ParkrunAn 83-year-old man who only started running regularly four years ago is hoping to raise money for a sports charity for disadvantaged people.Dennis Carter, of Norfolk, is raising money for the Norwich City Community Sports Foundation. Mr Carter, who joined Wymondham Athletics Club last year, said "it's a well-being thing with me - if I'm not fit I don't feel half as good".He runs twice a week, plays golf once and practices yoga.
