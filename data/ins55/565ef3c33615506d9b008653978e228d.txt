What's driving the resurgence in demand for managed offices and flexible workspaces?
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 11:16:34

After subsequent lockdowns, the managed office and flexible workspace industry has been more resilient than ever before. The sentiments are fairly inclined towards the entrepreneurship wave fuelled by the emergence of game-changing startups. From recording its first ever unicorn in 2011, the Indian startup ecosystem has witnessed its 100 th unicorn in 2022. This has opened new avenues for co-working spaces to expand their business and meet the resurging demand for a flexible workplace for corporates and startups.
