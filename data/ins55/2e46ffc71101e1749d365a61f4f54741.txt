CLARKSTON, Mich. (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 AtomTech, a leading provider of controls and SCADA integration, is excited to announce that it is scaling the company's presence in the North American market. Through the launch of AtomTech Canada, the growing team will meet the widening demand for automation, cyber, SCADA, and IIoT 4.0 assistance across industries such as automotive, medical equipment and warehouse logistics. Overseeing this expansion is newly appointed Manager of Canada at AtomTech, Christopher Turner, joined by Adam Phillips.
