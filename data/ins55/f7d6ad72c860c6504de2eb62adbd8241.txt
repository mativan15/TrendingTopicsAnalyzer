7 takeaways from the 2022 primary season so far

2022-05-30 10:02:03

(CNN)After the first month of multiple major primaries in the 2022 election season, both parties are facing uncertainty over their direction -- and whose influence will dominate the next wave of nominating contests.For Republicans, the question of former President Donald Trump's power over the party has only partly been answered. His endorsements have proved vital to some successful candidates but done little to boost others. Much clearer is the Republican embrace of his style of politics -- whether they won his support or were the target of his recriminations, nearly all the most successful GOP candidates are reading from his political playbook.
