Odisha abolishes contractual recruitment system
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 15:30:00

BHUBANESWAR: Diwali has come early for all those who are aspiring for a government job in Odisha as chief minister Naveen Patnaik on Saturday announced abolition of contractual recruitment system forever besides regularizing services of over 57,000 contractual employees .Naveen, who will turn 77 on Sunday, addressed the people of the state following a meeting of the state cabinet that gave its nod to the decision, which is likely to have long-term socio-political consequences.The decision, which the chief minister termed 'a golden moment in the history of Odisha', will have an additional annual burden of Rs 1,300 crore on the state exchequer.
