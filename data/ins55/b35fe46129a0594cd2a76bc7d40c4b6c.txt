Supreme Court affirmative action cases spark heated debate over meaning of Brown v. Board
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-19 09:03:41

WASHINGTON – Virtually any high school student in America, if asked to name a Supreme Court decision, could probably point to Brown v. Board of Education and explain that it was the landmark ruling that found public school segregation unconstitutional.Now, 68 years after the nation's highest court handed down one of its most recognized and consequential opinions, its meaning is being debated in two closely watched cases in which the justices must decide if colleges may consider race in admissions.
