June 01, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Angel Oak Dynamic Financial Strategies Income Term Trust (the 'Fund'), a closed-end fund traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol DYFN, today declared a distribution of $0.1228 per share for the month of June 2022. The record date for the distribution is June 16, 2022, and the payable date is June 30, 2022. The Fund will trade ex-distribution on June 15, 2022.

The Fund seeks to pay a distribution at a rate that reflects net investment income actually earned.
