New twist to amp up the drama quotient in ‘Dhulokona'
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-18 10:39:00

TV show ‘Dhulokona' starring Manali Manisha Dey and Indrasish Roy as the lead pair, has kept the audience glued to the screen with its story. The frequent twists and introduction of new characters in the latest episodes have made the storyline quite interesting.Now, the makers are ready to take the entertainment quotient a notch higher with the upcoming twist. Lalan, who was apparently killed following a conspiracy, isn't really dead. But he has lost his memory. This causes great pain to Phuljhuri, who had always faith that Lalan will return one day.
