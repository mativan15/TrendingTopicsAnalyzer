Birsa Dasgupta's 2019 comedy caper ‘Bibaho Obhijaan' had all those entertainment elements you would expect in commercial potboilers. It was a fun and light-hearted rom-com sure to entertain those looking to spend two hours forgetting about their lives outside. Now, after much delay, the sequel to the comedy flick is finally set to hit the shooting floors from next month. But this time, Birsa won't sit on the director's chair, it will mark ace cinematographer Soumik Haldar's directorial debut.
