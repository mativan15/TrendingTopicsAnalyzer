Study shows there is no woman CFO in Nifty 50
Copyright 2022 The Hindu
news
2022-05-30 16:35:45

In financial year 2022, there was no women CFO in the list of companies comprising stock index Nifty 50. Also, women held the position of chief financial officer (CFO) in less than 5% of the Nifty 250 companies, according to a study by the IFMR Graduate School of Business at Krea University and Dun & Bradstreet (D&B). The study quoted S. Mahalingam, Fomer CFO of Tata Consultancy Services as saying, ' We usually do not see women in many top positions, and this is a major problem. It is not that women lag in finance.
