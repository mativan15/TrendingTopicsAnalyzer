Simply Good Foods Stock Shares Up as It Raises FY23 Sales Guidance
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 14:23:00

By Sabela Ojea Shares of Simply Good Foods Co. on Friday rose after the company raised its fiscal-year 2023 sales guidance as its fiscal-fourth-quarter earnings beat market expectations. "The company is uniquely positioned within the nutritional snacking category to succeed in a challenging economic environment," Simply Good Foods's President and Chief Executive Joseph E. Scalzo said. The company--which makes snack bars, protein shakes and other products under the Atkins and Quest brands--said sales for the year ending August 2023 are now expected to increase slightly ahead of its long-term algorithm of growth between 4% to 6%.
