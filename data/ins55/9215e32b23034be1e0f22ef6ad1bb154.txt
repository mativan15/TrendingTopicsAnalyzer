Gama Aviation's Helicopter JV Wins Contract Worth GBP130 Mln
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 16:40:00

By Anthony O. Goriainoff Gama Aviation PLC said late Wednesday that its new helicopter joint venture has been awarded a five-year offshore contract expected to deliver overall revenue of around 130 million pounds ($147.2 million). The London-listed business-aviation services group said it will invest around GBP3.5 million in the second half of 2023 to ensure the contract is delivered in January 2024. It added that as part of the contract the joint venture, known as Bond Helicopters Ltd., will supply, operate and upkeep five helicopters which will support the transportation of personnel and equipment to the customer's offshore installations.
