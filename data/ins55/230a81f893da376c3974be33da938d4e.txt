ONGC posts 32% jump in Q4 profit
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-29 20:15:00

Oil and Natural Gas Corporation () reported a 32% year-on-year increase in quarterly profit to ₹8,860 crore on booming oil prices. Revenue for the January-March quarter jumped 63% from a year earlier to ₹34,497 crore. Net profit for 2021-22 climbed 258% to ₹40,306 crore, its highest-ever annual profit. Annual revenue rose 62% to ₹1,10,345 crore in 2021-22. ONGC realised an average oil price of $95 a barrel during the quarter, up 64% from the year before. Natural gas price was 62% higher at $2.9 per mmBtu.
