Elephant filmed giving birth in wild in incredibly rare footage
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 15:06:21

An elephant has been filmed giving birth in the wild in South Africa in incredibly rare footage.The footage taken by Adam Fillmore and posted on Facebook by South African National Parks shows a pregnant elephant giving birth to her calf while surrounded by her herd."Amazing sighting of an elephant giving birth this morning. We were privileged to witness the birth and interactions with the rest of the herd (around 60 elephants), and the electric atmosphere they created with their vocalizing and stomach rumbling.
