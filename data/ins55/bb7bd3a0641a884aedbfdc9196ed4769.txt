Elon Musk Tells Tesla Workers to Return to the Office Full Time Or Resign
nbcdfw.com
business
2022-06-01 14:11:10

Elon Musk has reportedly told Tesla employees to come back into their respective offices full time or leave the company. In two emails obtained by Electrek, Musk said that people must show up for at least 40 hours per week in a main Tesla office. Musk appeared to confirm the validity of the emails on Twitter: "They should pretend to work somewhere else."
