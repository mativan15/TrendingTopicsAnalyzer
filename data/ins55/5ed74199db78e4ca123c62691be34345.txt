Bangalore Mirror Bureau / Updated: May 27, 2022, 06:00 ISTLaurels apart, Asha workers ‘not provided smartphones and data packs', even as their salaries depend on uploading stuff‘Failure to upload on time could mean no incentives'By Melvin Mathew
The World Health Organisation recently recognised ASHA workers' contribution to healthcare, especially during the pandemic. Of late, a lot of their work has to be done digitally. However, several Ashas (Accredited Social Health Activists) were not even provided a phone, we are told, though in some cases tablets were given sans data packs.
