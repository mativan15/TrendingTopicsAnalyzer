Okta stock rally shifts into overdrive following hiked outlook as other software companies trim theirs
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 20:18:00

Okta Inc. shares rallied in the extended session Thursday, adding to gains in the regular session after the identity-management services company reported better-than-expected results and, unlike some larger software companies, hiked its outlook for the year. The company, which makes software that helps authorized employees access applications on their corporate networks, broke with larger software companies and raised its outlook for the year, instead of clawing it back because of a stronger dollar.
