"Life, Liberty & Levin" host Mark Levin ripped those in power for trying to take away individual liberties when tragedy strikes instead of evaluating their own policy failures.On his Sunday night show, Levin illustrated how the "ruling class," the government, the media, big tech companies and academia, routinely attack the rights of the individual in the First and Second Amendments, so they can grab more power for themselves."We have a ruling class that is narcissistic, that is incompetent and in many respects, anti-American.
