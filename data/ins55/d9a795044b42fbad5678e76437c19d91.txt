Chewy deepens pet insurance offering using Lemonade plans in new pact (NYSE:LMND)
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-20 16:19:17

urbazon In an effort to make pet healthcare more simple and accessible, Chewy (NYSE:CHWY) is expanding its insurance and wellness offerings, CarePlus, using new plans provided by Lemonade's (NYSE:LMND) pet health insurance product, the pet-focused ecommerce platform said Thursday. Investors cheered the new alliance, with both Chewy (CHWY) stock, +2.7%, and multi-line insurer Lemonade (LMND) stock, +1.9%, in early afternoon trading. Through the deal, Chewy (CHWY) customers will be able to pick and develop a variety of customized plans to meet their specific needs.
