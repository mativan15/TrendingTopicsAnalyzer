May 29, 2022 09:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Virtual Enterprises International (VE) announced the election of Musa Ali Shama as President effective April 27.



Mr. Shama, a seasoned educator, has served as superintendent of New Visions for Public Schools, deputy superintendent for high schools in the New York City Department of Education, and principal of Francis Lewis HS in Queens, New York where he implemented three successful VE programs. Most recently, he served as VE's National Program Director, responsible for curriculum and resource development, research and professional development, and assessment.
