The Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce has been able to do a lot with a little.

The chamber says it has relatively fewer resources compared to some of its peer markets. Despite that, the organization is as busy as it's ever been.

The Fort Worth Chamber has about 165 projects in the pipeline currently. The Chamber has seen some notable wins this year, including MP Materials' new facility at AllianceTexas.

Going forward, the chamber is looking to be more proactive.

Part of being proactive means going out to the marketplace and finding opportunities, said Chris Strayer, executive vice president of Economic Development for the Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce.
