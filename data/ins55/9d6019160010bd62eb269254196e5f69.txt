Alec Baldwin shares tribute to Halyna Hutchins one year after 'Rust' shooting
foxnews.com

2022-10-21 22:06:34

It's been one year since Halyna Hutchins was fatally shot on the set of "Rust" in New Mexico and Alec Baldwin is sharing a post on Instagram to remember the late cinematographer."One year ago today…" Baldwin, 64, captioned the image of Hutchins. The actor limited the comments on the Instagram post, but fans were able to write a few thoughts. "Such a sad day for you all," one person wrote. "My deepest sympathies to her family and to you," added another person. Others slammed Baldwin for sharing the post, with one person writing: "Inappropriate.
