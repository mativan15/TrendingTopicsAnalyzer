It was during Shereen Fayers's second trip in two days to her local medical centre that her doctor checked her heart and immediately called an ambulance. The contrast dye used in critical medical scans and X-rays is in short supply across Australia and the world Australia's main supplier of the dye had to temporarily close its factory in Shanghai Doctors want the government to look into introducing measures to avoid future shortages  The paramedic arriving at the scene did similar checks and told her: "You're having a heart attack, right now.
