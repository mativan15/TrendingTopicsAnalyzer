First Internet Bank Takes Action to Lessen Inflationary Impact for Employees

June 01, 2022 09:03 AM Eastern Daylight Time FISHERS, Ind.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--First Internet Bank announced today that it has raised its minimum hourly wage for full-time employees to $20.00. The move is in response to rising inflationary economic trends and reflects the Bank's overall commitment to attracting and retaining talented employees.



'This is an important show of support for our teams' standard of living in the face of rising transportation, housing and food costs,' noted Nicole Lorch, President and Chief Operating Officer.
