Aurum PropTech's revenue up 93.3% to Rs 28.3 crore in July-September 2022
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 04:30:00

Aurum PropTech Ltd, formerly known as Majesco Ltd, has reported consolidated revenue from operations worth Rs 28.3 crore for the quarter ended September, up 179% over the last year's total revenue and growth of 93.3% over the previous quarter.Total income for the quarter also rose 96.2% sequentially to Rs 30.84 crore.Total income for the first half of 2022-23 rose to Rs 46.56 crore as against Rs 2.68 crore from a year ago.'The revenue this quarter saw a healthy growth of 90% over last quarter. We once again delivered as per our philosophy of chasing growth without impacting profitability.
