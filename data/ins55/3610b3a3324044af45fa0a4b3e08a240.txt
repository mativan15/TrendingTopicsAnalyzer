Visuals shared by news agency ANI showed the Bharatiya Kisan Union (BKU) leader's face smeared in ink. People around him were seen throwing chairs at each other as ruckus erupted. Some other farm leaders were also present during the event.

ByHarshit Sabarwal | Edited by Swati Bhasin, New Delhi




In Bengaluru, scenes of chaos emerged on Monday when black ink was thrown at farmer leader Rakesh Tikait at a press conference. Visuals shared by news agency ANI showed the Bharatiya Kisan Union (BKU) leader's face smeared in ink.
