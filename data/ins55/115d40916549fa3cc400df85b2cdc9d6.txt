June 3, 2022 Editor's note: This story is part of a series of profiles of notable spring 2022 graduates.Although Lauren Hardy was almost always the top math student every year in her elementary, junior high and high schools, mathematics was not the first subject she considered to major in during college. While attending high school in Mesa, Arizona, she exceeded the amount of math classes offered by the district and alternatively attended Mesa Community College to continue taking math classes during her senior year.
