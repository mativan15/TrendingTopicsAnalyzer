Rajya Sabha member from Kurnool T.G. Venkatesh on Saturday laid the foundation for works related to permanently illuminating the Konda Reddy Buruju, a fort built during Vijayanagara empire in Kurnool. The fort, believed to be built by Achyuta Devarayalu, is one of the key historical attractions of the district and is maintained by the Archaeological Survey of India.
