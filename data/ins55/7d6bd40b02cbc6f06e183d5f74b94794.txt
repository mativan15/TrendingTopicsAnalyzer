Asian Paints rolls out their festive film with an emotional message

­­National, October 21, 2022: People are celebrating Diwali with pomp and gaiety this year. Tuning into that joyous fervour, Asian Paints aims to connect with their consumers through a heart-warming film that carries forward their brand ethos, ‘Har Ghar Kuch Kehta Hai'. The film conveys a heart-filled message about bringing families together while also highlighting the joy-filled equity a home holds.In the film, Asian Paints emphasizes togetherness and celebrations that are at the heart of festivals.
