UPSTACK Forms Its First, Next-Gen Partnership with Lumen Technologies "(UPSTACK is) looking forward to working with Lumen to help our clients leverage more of the Lumen Platform and its next-generation cloud and edge technologies." NEW YORK (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 UPSTACK, a fast-growing platform that transforms the way businesses design and select digital infrastructure solutions, announced today that it has entered into a strategic partnership with Lumen Technologies (NYSE: LUMN). This is the largest direct agreement between UPSTACK and a technology provider.
