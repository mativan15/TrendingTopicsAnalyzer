The Punjab government on Tuesday sought support from heads of Indian Missions abroad for attracting inward investments from their respective country into the state. The heads of Indian Missions abroad visited the Startup Punjab Hub (Neuron) at Software Technology Park of India (STPI) Mohali and interacted with the Invest Punjab team.
