Mercedes-Benz Signs Battery-Grade Lithium Supply Agreement for EV Ramp Up
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 06:51:00

By Pierre Bertrand Mercedes-Benz Group AG said Thursday that it has signed a supply agreement for raw electric-battery materials with Rock Tech Lithium Inc, which is worth about $1.47 billion in sales according to the Canadian-based lithium company. The deal is for the annual supply of 10,000 tons on average of battery-grade lithium hydroxide, enough for approximately 150,000 electric vehicles, the German auto-maker said. The agreement, starting in 2026 including a qualification period, enables the supply of raw materials to its battery partners allowing for rapid scaling of electric vehicle production, Mercedes-Benz said.
