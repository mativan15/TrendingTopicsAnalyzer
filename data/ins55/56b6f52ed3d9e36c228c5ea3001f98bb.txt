Assam govt to provide certificates to 6 minority communities
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 03:15:00

PTIAssam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma Assam government has decided to provide minority certificate to six communities including muslim, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains, Parsis. Assam health minister and government spokesperson Keshab Mahanta said, 'The cabinet meeting has decided that there is scheduled caste certificate and likewise there is scheduled caste certificate or other backward caste certificate however there is no certificate for minorities.' 
He added, 'By minorities a community is generally identified.
