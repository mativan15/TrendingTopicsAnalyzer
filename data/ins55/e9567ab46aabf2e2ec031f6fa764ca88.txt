Published: 14:43 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 14:43 EDT, 19 October 2022 The Kansas City Chiefs have announced that the contract of one of its tight ends, Travis Kelce, has been restructured in an attempt to free up cap space to land new signings, one of them rumored to be free agent, Odell Beckham Jr.ESPN's Field Yates made the front office move public on Tuesday, tweeting that the three-time All-Pro's base salary has been converted into 'a signing bonus to clear $3.44M in cap space.'Beforehand, Kansas City had just over $200,000 in salary cap space to work with, but now, general manager Brett Veach can operate more freely to potentially make a marquee signing, or two.
