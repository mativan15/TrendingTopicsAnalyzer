Futures tracking the Dow and S&P 500 gained on Thursday as positive forecasts from IBM and AT&T helped offset declines from Tesla after it warned of missing its vehicle delivery target this year.IBM Corp rose 3.3% in premarket trading, leading the advance among Dow components after the software and IT services company beat quarterly earnings estimates on Wednesday and said it expects to exceed full-year revenue growth targets.
