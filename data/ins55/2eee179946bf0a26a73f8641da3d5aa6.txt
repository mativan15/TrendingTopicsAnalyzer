The humble press release is one of our most reliable tools in public relations. It is the key vehicle by which practitioners deliver news, at scale, to the media.It was invented out of unfortunate necessity by public relations pioneer (and later, controversial character) Ivy Ledbetter Lee. Credited with opening the nation's first public relations firm, Lee represented the Pennsylvania Railroad. In October 1906, the railroad company experienced an accident that cost the lives of more than 50 people.
