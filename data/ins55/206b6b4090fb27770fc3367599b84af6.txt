Emmy winner to speak at conference

news
2022-10-20 00:07:00

Oct. 19—Melissa Gilbert, an Emmy-winning, Golden Globe-nominated actor, director, producer and New York Times best-selling author, will serve as the keynote speaker during the Greater Scranton Chamber of Commerce's 2023 EMPOWER: NEPA Women's Leadership Conference.

The event will be held 8 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. April 13 at Kalahari Resorts and Conventions, 250 Kalahari Blvd.

The conference highlights women's ability to create positive change in their communities and their own lives — both professionally and personally — while recognizing the power that exists when women support each other.
