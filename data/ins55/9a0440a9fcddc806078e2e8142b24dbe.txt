AgenciesThe event will give developers an opportunity to interact with Apple designers and engineers. (Image: Twitter/@EdwardFerguson_)Apple's gearing up Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC). The much-awaited tech conference will be held between June 6 -10 and will give developers from around the world to engage with designers, engineers and executives at Apple Inc. The event is expected to unveil a host of software updates and even some hardware by the Cupertino-based tech giant. Among the big launches, Apple is expected to launch a new Mac hardware along with a redesigned MacBook Air.
