ScreenDaily The Irish feature has been picked up for France, the Middle East, Benelux, China and more.Colm Bairéad'sThe Quiet Girl has sold across the world for UK sales agent Bankside Films, with theatrical deals in territories including ASC Distribution for France, Front Row in the Middle East, Cinéart for Benelux and DDDream for China.The Irish-language feature, which is Bairéad's debut, has also sold in Eastern Europe (HBO pay-tv); Hungary (Mozinet); Indonesia (Pt Falcon); Taiwan (Hooray Films); South Korea (Choix Pictures); Spain: (La Aventura Cine); and Turkey (Mars Productions).
