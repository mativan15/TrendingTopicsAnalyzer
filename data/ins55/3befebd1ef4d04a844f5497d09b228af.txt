Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivan surrogacy controversy: The couple registered their marriage 6-years ago and the surrogate is a relative
indiatimes.com

2022-10-16 11:58:07

Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivan recently embraced parenthood as they welcomed twin sons via surrogacy. However, it sparked controversy as the couple became parents within four months of their wedding, which raised several eyebrows, questioning if the couple abided by the laws of surrogacy. Now, as per the affidavit submitted by the couple to the Tamil Nadu health department, it has been revealed that the surrogate through which the twins were born is a relative of the 'GodFather' actress, which is by the law.
