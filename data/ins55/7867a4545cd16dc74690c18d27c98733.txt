Photo: Getty ImagesBOSTON (WBZNewsRadio) - The Boston Division of the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) is warning the public of tech support scammers who are targeting financial accounts using remote desktop software as the number of people falling victim to such scams, is on the rise. Across the United States, 23,903 people fell victim to scammers in 2021, losing more than $347 million. That's a 137 percent increase in losses from the year before. The FBI said more than half of those victims were over 60 years old.
