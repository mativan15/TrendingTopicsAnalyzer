Michael Patrick, COO of The Property Advocates 'I am excited to join such an important group of leaders,' said Michael Patrick, COO of The Property Advocates. CORAL GABLES, Fla. (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Chief Operating Officer of The Property Advocates, Michael Patrick, has been accepted into the Forbes Business Council, the foremost growth and networking organization for successful business owners and leaders worldwide. Michael Patrick was vetted and selected by a review committee based on the depth and diversity of his experience.
