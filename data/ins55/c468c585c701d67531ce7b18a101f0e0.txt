Assam govt inks 7 MoUs with industry partners

news
2022-10-18 10:24:16

Oct 18, 2022, 03:54PM IST

Source: ANI

In a move to draw a roadmap for a broad-based development of the state, Assam government inked seven memoranda of understanding and agreement with different industry players on Oct 17. The MoUs and MoAs were signed in presence of CM Himanta Biswa Sarma at Janata Bhawan in Guwahati. Power Minister Nandita Gorlosa, Minister of Labour Welfare Sanjay Kisan and a host of other dignitaries were present on the occasion.
