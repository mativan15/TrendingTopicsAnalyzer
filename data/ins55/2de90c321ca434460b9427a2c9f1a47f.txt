(Shutterstock)PALOS HEIGHTS An Orland Park man is accused of vandalizing a Bitcoin ATM in Palos Heights. Adham Issa, 23, is facing charges of criminal damage to property and possession of a controlled substance, both felonies. Officers took a report Sept. 23 from a vendor that had a Bitcoin ATM damaged in the 13400 block of Ridgeland Avenue. Security video captured two men entering the business.
