Nadiad railway station to be revamped as world-class facility, says Vaishnaw
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-20 19:19:54

Union Minister for Railways, Communications, Electronics & Information Technology Ashwini Vaishnaw Thursday announced the Central government has decided to develop Nadiad Railway Station as a world-class facility in the league of other cities such as Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Surat and Rajkot. Vaishnaw added that the decision was taken within two hours after some residents from the region made a demand to him Thursday.
The minister made the announcement while addressing a public gathering at Fagvel in Kheda organised to mark the conclusion of a leg of Gujarat Gaurav Yatra that started from Unai in Navsari on October 13.
