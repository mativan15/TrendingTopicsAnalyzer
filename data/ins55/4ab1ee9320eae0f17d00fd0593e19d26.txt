'Triple lock betrayal would be slap in the face for loyal voters'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 21:01:00

The final straw would be a cut in their income caused by the Government breaking their triple-lock pledge, which means their state pension would not keep up with the rate of inflation. New Chancellor Jeremy Hunt has not ruled this out as he tore up Liz Truss's tax-cutting budget on Monday.Nothing is off the table to placate the markets. Government spending must be reined in, say international bankers and investors, and older generations are in their sights. This is an easy target for the government.
