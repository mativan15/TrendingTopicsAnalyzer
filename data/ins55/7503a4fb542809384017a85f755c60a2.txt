Corus Entertainment 3Q Swings to Loss as Demand for Advertising Dwindles
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 10:26:00

By Adriano Marchese Corus Entertainment Inc. on Friday reported a loss in its fiscal fourth quarter, missing expectations, as customers were less willing to part with their advertising dollars, lowering revenue in the period. For the period ended Aug. 31, the Canadian mass media company reported a loss of 367.1 million Canadian dollars (US$266.7 million), or C$1.82 a share, compared with a profit of C$19.9 million. or C$0.10 a share a year ago. On an adjusted basis, losses were C$0.08 a share, while analyst polled on FactSet were expecting a profit of C$0.
