Six months jail for sale and bursting of firecrackers in Delhi

Source: Mirror Now

The Delhi government has banned sale and purchase of firecrackers on Diwali. Violators will face up to six months in jail and a Rs 200 fine will also be imposed on them. The curb has been imposed due to rising pollution in the national capital.
