Tiruchi City Police have issued licence to 71 persons for running temporary cracker shops ahead of Deepavali. Commissioner of Police G. Karthikeyan convened a meeting with the applicants, who had sought permission to set up the temporary cracker shops, at the City Police Office on Saturday. He explained the stipulated conditions to run the shops.
