Exclusive: Love Island's star Tasha Ghouri has vowed to give half of her winnings on the ITV2 show to the deaf charity which is close to her heart, as she plans to raise awareness with villa appearanceLove Island: Meet the 2022 IslandersTasha Ghouri will give half of her Love Island winnings to a deaf charity close to her heart. The model and dancer - who hails from Thirsk in Yorkshire - has a cochlear implant, after being born completely deaf. She has had the device in since she was five-years-old, where she underwent an eight hour surgery to have it fitted.
