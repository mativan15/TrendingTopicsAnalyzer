Man uses bear spray to ward off "curious" black bear in gut-wrenching video
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-02 18:28:59

A man showed himself using bear spray in a now-viral Reddit video after he said a black bear followed him for more than five minutes.Posted to the Reddit forum "That's Insane" by u/BertieTheBeaver, the heart-pounding clip amassed more than 65,000 votes since it was shared on Wednesday.For most of the three-minute video, u/BertieTheBeaver filmed himself walking backward while holding out the container of bear spray pointed at the bear.According to the National Park Service, there are a few steps one can take to prevent a situation from escalating after coming into contact with a bear.
