StrideOne raises Rs 250 crore to offer debt to Indian startups

ETtechAbhinav Suri, cofounder, StrideOne (left) and Ishpreet Singh Gandhi, founder, StrideOne (right)New Delhi: StrideOne, a financial services platform for startups, announced on Tuesday that it has secured Rs 250 crore via a mix of equity (led by Elevar Equity) and debt from banks. StrideOne, which was started by venture debt fund Stride Ventures, introduced a startup-led loan distribution mechanism for their supply chains and crossed the Rs 200 crore milestone in AUM (assets under management) in six months since its inception.
