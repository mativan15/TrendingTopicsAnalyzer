The Queensland man has been charged with two counts of sexual assaultThe 28-year-old reportedly posed as a yoga teacher in MackayPolice fear there could be more alleged victims as they issue appeal  Anyone with information should contact Crime Stoppers on 1800 333 000 A Queensland man will face court after he posed as a yoga instructor to allegedly sexually assault women as police launch a public appeal for more potential victims. Police allege the 28-year-old man entered a yoga and pilates studio on Carl Court in the Mackay suburb of Rural View on April 21 and claimed to be an instructor.
