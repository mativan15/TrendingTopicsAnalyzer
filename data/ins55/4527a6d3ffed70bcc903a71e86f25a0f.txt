Bregman homers, Valdez pitches 7 strong innings as Astros edge Yankees for 2-0 ALCS lead
cbc.ca
sport
2022-10-21 02:50:00

Alex Bregman, Framber Valdez and the Houston Astros are off to a perfect start this post-season.Taking the formula that won them 106 games in the regular season — strong pitching and hitting home runs — the Astros kept rolling against the New York Yankees.Bregman connected for an early three-run homer, Valdez threw seven strong innings and the Astros edged the Yankees 3-2 Thursday night in Houston to take a 2-0 lead in the AL Championship Series.The Astros improved to 5-0 this post-season after a sweep to begin this best-of-seven matchup.
