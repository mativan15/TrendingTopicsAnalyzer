LG Group chief visits joint EV battery plant in Ohio SEOUL, Oct. 18 (Yonhap) -- LG Group Chairman Koo Kwang-mo visited a joint venture between the group's battery making affiliate and U.S. carmaker General Motors Co. in Ohio, industrial sources said Tuesday. Koo has recently toured the No. 1 plant of Ultium Cells LLC, a joint venture between GM and LG Energy Solution Ltd. that hived off from LG Chem last year, according to the sources. Ultium Cells' No. 1 plant is set to produce EV batteries during the second half of the year, with two more plants under construction in Tennessee and Michigan.
