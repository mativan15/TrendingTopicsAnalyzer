Using Zoom on your devices? Beware of this new threat which can install spyware and malware on your iPhone, Android or Windows devices without letting you know! When it comes to video calling, Zoom is one of the top-most meeting platforms that people use. And this wide popularity is one of the reasons that hackers have chosen it to seed malicious malware and spyware in users' devices via Zoom! Now, users have been alerted to this new threat which begins with a simple message and it puts your device at risk.
