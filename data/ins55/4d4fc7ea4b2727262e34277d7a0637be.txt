Springfield senator tells Chamber that business priorities depend on GOP split in Senate
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-02 10:45:04

This year's legislative session was "ultimately successful" for business interests in Springfield, members and legislators said at a Springfield Area Chamber of Commerce event Tuesday, but tumultuous politics in Jefferson City continue to instill uncertainty moving forward.

Chamber leaders heaped praise on Sen. Lincoln Hough, the Springfield Republican who as vice chair of the budget committee plays a key role in allocating state — and this year, federal — money. Hough joked to a crowd of about 75 that it's "funny how many people will show up when you give them 100 million dollars.
