ENGLAND stars Ben Currie and Mike Cooper waded into the stands following a heated fan row after Warrington's heavy defeat to Leeds.
Tempers were high among Warrington fans on Friday night as Leeds ran riot at the Halliwell Jones Stadium, hammering the hosts 40-4.
