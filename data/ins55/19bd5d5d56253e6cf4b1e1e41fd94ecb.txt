Many Tory MPs horrified at prospect of Boris Johnson's return
irishtimes.com
world
2022-10-21 00:00:00

Conservative MPs were on Friday deeply divided by the prospect of Boris Johnson returning as party leader and British prime minister – just three months after he quit following a string of scandals.Contenders to replace Liz Truss, who dramatically resigned on Thursday after only six weeks in power, were racing to secure the required support of 100 MPs to stand in the contest.House of Commons leader Penny Mordaunt on Friday announced that she was standing for the Conservative leadership – the first Tory MP to do so.
