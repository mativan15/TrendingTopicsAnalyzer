'Alarm bells should have rung louder in the leadership campaign'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 07:44:44

The final phase of Liz Truss's brutally truncated premiership was all of a piece with the rest - unsustainable positions followed by predictable, chaotic and humiliating U-turns.Not only will she go down in history as the shortest-serving PM, but also its most inept. The rapid unravelling is a personal tragedy for the most assiduous climber of the greasy pole of politics in the party.Alarm bells should have rung louder in the leadership campaign, when she floated various half-baked ideas. One that nearly sank her was reducing pay rates for public sector jobs outside the South-east.
