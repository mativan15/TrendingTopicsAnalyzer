Banks and tech weigh ASX down

2022-05-31 07:53:34

Big-name banking and technology companies hit reverse on Tuesday as the Australian sharemarket registered its worst session in nearly two weeks and capped its worst month since January.ASX traders handed back a hefty slice of their recent gains as US bond yields leapt on sizzling German inflation print and hawkish rhetoric from US Federal Reserve Governor Christopher Waller.Waller's comments on inflation prompted a swift retreat for rate-sensitive tech and payment stocks, while underwhelming building approvals data also knocked the wind out of local lenders Commonwealth Bank, Westpac, NAB and ANZ.
