Image source, Brian Lawless/PAImage caption, Lisa Smith was found guilty of being a member of the unlawful terrorist group but acquitted of financing terrorismA former Irish Defence Forces soldier has been found guilty of Islamic State (IS) membership.Lisa Smith, 40, had pleaded not guilty to being a member of the group between 28 October 2015 and 1 December 2019.Smith was, however, found not guilty of financing terrorism by sending money to a man for the benefit the terrorist group.She had been accused of sending €800 (£680) to a man to fund IS in 2015.
