ASX down 0.3pct at noon but energy gains
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-06 02:14:57

The local bourse is trending lower ahead of a big decision on interest rates, but a number of oil producers and coalminers this morning hit their highest level in years as the war in Ukraine continues to wreak havoc on energy markets.

The benchmark S&P/ASX200 index was down 23.6 points, or 0.33 per cent, to 7,214.7 at noon on Monday. The broader All Ordinaries was down 30.7 points to 7,441.7, a drop of 0.41 per cent.

But the energy sector was up 1.7 per cent after oil prices jumped.

Woodside Energy was up 2.
