For many folks, Walmart is the main way they do most of their shopping. What a lot of people may not realize, though, is that Walmart has a membership program akin to Best Buy Total Tech, although a bit more encompassing.While Total Tech mostly covers electronics, Walmart+ covers the majority of its services. For example, with Walmart+, you can waive the $35 order minimum for delivery fees, allowing you to get your items delivered for free regardless of price, with a couple of exceptions. Similarly, Walmart+ offers a more streamlined shopping experience with a contact-free checkout, which is pretty convenient.
