Nikki Finke, who has died after a long illness aged 68, was a tenacious and fearless journalist whose stream of scoops on her website Deadline Hollywood Daily made her the scourge of producers and studio heads; though her stories did not always stand up, when they were proved to be true she would unleash her trademark headline: 'Toldja!'
In 2007, when she won an award from the Los Angeles Press Club, the judges noted: 'Reading Nikki Finke's salaciously candid coverage of Hollywood and its inhabitants almost feels like a guilty pleasure.
