AJ Bell (LON:AJB) Is Increasing Its Dividend To UK£0.028
yahoo.com

2022-05-29 07:20:48

The board of AJ Bell plc (LON:AJB) has announced that it will be increasing its dividend on the 1st of July to UK£0.028. This will take the dividend yield from 2.6% to 4.5%, providing a nice boost to shareholder returns.

AJ Bell Is Paying Out More Than It Is Earning

Impressive dividend yields are good, but this doesn't matter much if the payments can't be sustained. Before this announcement, AJ Bell was paying out 76% of earnings, but a comparatively small 66% of free cash flows.
