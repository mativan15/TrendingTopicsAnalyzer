New Survey: Vows To Tip Better Fall Flat
Copyright 2022 Forbes Media LLC
business
2022-06-06 04:15:00

Restaurant goers, hotel guests and taxi riders have failed to become better tippers despite their vows to increase support for service industry employees who have been financially impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. That's the conclusion of an online survey of 2,610 adults released Monday by CreditCards.com. The results of the survey, which was conducted May 11-13 by YouGov Plc, are representative of all U.S. adults at least 18 years old and have a margin of error of plus or minus 2%, CreditCards.
