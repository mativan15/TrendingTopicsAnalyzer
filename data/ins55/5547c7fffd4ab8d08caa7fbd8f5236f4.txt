46-year-old Bobby Martin, a native of Pala, closed down his business and jumped into full-fledged rubber farming about two decades ago when the latex prices were skyrocketing. But with the input costs at current highs, the nine acre plantation that he inherited hardly serves his family.And it isn't just the material prices that are rising. The shortage of labour and the resultant increase in wages have all conspired to stock a crisis.
