BEST ELECTRONIC The Best Electronic Music on Bandcamp: May 2022 By Joe Muggs · June 01, 2022 'Electronic music' doesn't always have to be strictly 'electronic.' This month, we've got spoken word, psychedelic folk, Brazilian jazz, black metal, and smooth soul—not simply as sample material, or used to embellish otherwise electronic tracks, but as powerful elements in their own right. They are as intrinsic to the releases they feature on as the grime, electro, gabber, trance, and dubstep with which they intersect.
