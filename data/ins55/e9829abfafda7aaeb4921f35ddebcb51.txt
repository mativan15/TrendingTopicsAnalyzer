Director Amit Dasgupta's next ‘Phire Aye' is a twisting tale of three journalists. With an ensemble cast of Biswajit Chakraborty, Anamika Saha, Bodhisatta Majumdar, Soma Chatterjee, Rish Biswas, Biplob, and others the film is set to release on December 2.The plot revolves around three journalists Tiasa, Ayush, and Jinia. Tiasa's main purpose in life is to deliver accurate news to people, whereas Jinia wants fame and money. Her only concern is to fulfill her family responsibilities. Meanwhile, Ayush has come to Kolkata to fulfill his dreams.
