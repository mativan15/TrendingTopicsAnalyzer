Paul Merson admits he has been surprised by Kepa Arrizabalaga's resurgence at Stamford Bridge and revealed his prediction for Chelsea's upcoming Premier League clash against Brentford.

Arrizabalaga has started Chelsea's last seven games and impressed between the sticks, having initially come into the side to replace the injured Edouard Mendy.
Mendy is back available for selection following a brief spell on the sidelines but new Blues boss Graham Potter has continued to pick Arrizabalaga, who became the most expensive goalkeeper in history when he moved from Athletic Bilbao in 2018.
