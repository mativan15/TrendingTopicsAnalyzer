Valentine In The MorningBy Valerie HernandezOct 17, 2022Photo: Getty Images North AmericaThe United States Postal Service just announced that the price of Forever stamps and other postage will go up again on January 22, 2023. The price increase will go up by 4.2% making the cost of a Forever stamp go up 3 cents, from 60 cents to 63 cents. See more details on the 2023 price changes below: Letters (1 oz.) will now be 63 cents
Letters (metered 1 oz.) will now be 60 cents
Domestic Postcards will now be 48 cents
International Postcards will now be $1.
