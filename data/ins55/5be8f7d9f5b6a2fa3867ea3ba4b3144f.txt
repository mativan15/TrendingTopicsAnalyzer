Here's What Dave Ramsey Recommends Instead of Buying a Rent-to-Own Property
fool.com
news
2022-10-16 17:00:30

If you're looking into different ways to buy a home, rent-to-own properties may be on your radar. With this kind of property, you don't just get a mortgage, purchase a home, and move into it right away. Instead, you lease a property under an arrangement that allows you to buy it at the end of a set time. When you move into the property and begin paying rent, some of that money goes toward your purchase so you acquire equity that can be used as a down payment to qualify for a home loan. This might seem like a wise approach to saving up for a down payment while testing out living in the home you'll someday buy.
