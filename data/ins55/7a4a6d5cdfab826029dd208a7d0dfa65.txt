TAMPA, Fla., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Avalon Health Services, LLC, DBA Avalon Healthcare Solutions, the world's first Lab Insights company, today released its annual Lab Trend Report. Avalon's complimentary report provides insights into the lab ecosystem, including the growing demand for lab testing, regulatory and legislative changes, the digitization of lab results, and the ongoing impact of COVID-19 testing.
