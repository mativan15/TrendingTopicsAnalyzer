The fatality is the second in Kerala since 2019 when the infection claimed the life of a six-year-old boy in Malappuram. Here's what you should know about the deadly virus: 
What is the West Nile Virus?
The West Nile Virus is a single-stranded RNA virus caused due to the bites of the Culex species of mosquitoes.
