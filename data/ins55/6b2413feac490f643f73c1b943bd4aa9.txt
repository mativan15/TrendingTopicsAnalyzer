Photos: Courtesy of Lauren PartridgeWhen 22-year-old Lauren Partridge spotted an advert for an internship at her favourite business and lifestyle podcast, her heart began to race. 'It was my dream opportunity, so I couldn't let it pass me by,' the third-year psychology student says. Well into an eight-month search for a post-uni marketing job, she was well aware that competition for the internship would be fierce.In an attempt to stand out amidst the legions of podcast host, influencer and CEO Grace Beverley's fans, Partridge spent two days creating a hand-decorated postal application which she later shared on TikTok.
