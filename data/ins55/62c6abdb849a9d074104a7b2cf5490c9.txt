The decision of the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries and its allies to cut oil production is a mistake that has favoured the Russians, the White House has said. "The decisions that OPEC+ made last week, we believe, sided with the Russians and were against the interests of the American people and the families around the world," White House Press Secretary Karine Jean-Pierre told reporters at her daily news conference on Tuesday. "We believe that the decision is going to hurt and harm lower-income economies.
