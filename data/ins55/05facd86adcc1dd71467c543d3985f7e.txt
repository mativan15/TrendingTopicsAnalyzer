Chief Minister K.. Chandrasekhar Rao with SATS chairman A. Venkateshwar Reddy
V V SUBRAHMANYAMHYDERABAD
Chairman of Sports Authority of Telangana State A. Venkateshwar Reddy informed the media here on Friday that Chief Minister K. Chandrasekhar Rao is determined to make the State a sports hub and also to create international standard infrastructure in the districts.'We should not be far behind States like Haryana and Odisha which are known to promote sports in a big way'' was the Chief Minister's opinion Mr Reddy said after a review meeting.
