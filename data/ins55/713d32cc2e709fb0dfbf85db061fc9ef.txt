ITC Q2 preview: Cigarettes to light up the show, other businesses to add flavour
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 09:45:00

Mumbai: is seen reporting a strong set of numbers for the quarter ended September on the back of continued growth in cigarette volumes and steady performance across businesses. The company's revenue-net of excise duty-is seen rising 26% year-on-year to Rs.15,989 crores in the quarter gone by, showed the average of estimates by six brokerages. Cigarette sales volumes are seen rising 13-15% on year, compared with a 9% growth seen in the same period last year. The diversified conglomerate will release its earnings on Thursday.
