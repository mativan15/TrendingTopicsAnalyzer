Terra's swift collapse triggered a crypto market sell-off, billions in lost fortunes and a rumored class-action lawsuit.

But now, Terra's most valuable asset – the loyalty of their developers – is being challenged.

With the launch of Terra 2.0, projects must decide whether or not they will stay loyal to the Terra ecosystem or choose another path. Competing blockchains are offering up major perks for them to develop on greener pastures.
