Exxon Mobil stock rises toward 8-year high after analyst says bullish 're-rating' story still has legs
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 18:34:06

Shares of Exxon Mobil Corp. XOM, +1.29% climbed 1.5% toward an 8-year high in afternoon trading Friday, boosted by rising oil and gas prices and an upbeat analyst comment. Crude oil futures CL00, +1.68% rose 1.4% in afternoon trading while natural gas futures NG00, +0.45% advanced 0.5%. Analyst Phil Gresh at J.P. Morgan reiterated the overweight rating he's had on the oil and gas giant since January 2021, and kept his stock price target at $108, saying the company's "re-rating story still has legs.
