The number of petrol pumps selling biofuels in the country has increased threefold from 29,897 in 2016-17 to 67,641 in 2021-22, Union Petroleum Minister Hardeep S Puri has said. The country's ethanol demand is poised to grow to 10.16 billion litres by 2025, the minister noted. He also said that the country is ready from the supply side to launch E20, and has advanced the target by five years from 2030 under the ethanol production goal of 1,000 crore litres to April 2023.
