EV battery swapping provider Gogoro (GGR) stock shot up 26% in afternoon trading Tuesday. Shares of Taiwan company, which also produces electric scooters, opened at $4.20, hitting a peak of $6.12 in early afternoon trading. The stock recently changed hands at $5.31, up 26%, at approximately 2:40 p.m. ET. Gogoro went public on April 5 following a merger with SPAC Poema Global Holdings, with shares climbing 10% during their debut session to close at $14.
