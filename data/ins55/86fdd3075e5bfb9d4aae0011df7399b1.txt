Thai Stock Market May Reclaim 1,600-Point Level
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-19 04:30:06

(RTTNews) - The Thai stock market has tracked higher in back-to-back sessions, gathering almost 30 points or 2 percent along the way. The Stock Exchange of Thailand now rests just above the 1,590-point plateau and it may add to its winnings on Wednesday. The global forecast for the Asian markets is upbeat on bargain hunting and renewed optimism over earnings. The European and U.S. markets were firmly higher and the Asian bourses are expected to open in similar fashion. The SET finished sharply higher on Tuesday following gains from the financial shares, cement companies and energy stocks.
