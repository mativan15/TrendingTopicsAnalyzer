Heavy rain in Bengaluru leads to waterlogging, infrastructural damage and power outage

PTIImage used for representative purposeBengaluru on Wednesday night witnessed heavy showers leading to waterlogging, incidents of tree fall, damage to certain infrastructure and power outage in several parts. The central, southern and eastern parts of the city faced the brunt of the thunderstorm Wednesday night. Metro's retaining wall collapsed near Seshadripuram, leading to several cars and bikes being damaged. 
There were reports of waterlogging in several low-lying areas and underpasses in various parts of the city, leading to traffic jams and affecting normal life.
