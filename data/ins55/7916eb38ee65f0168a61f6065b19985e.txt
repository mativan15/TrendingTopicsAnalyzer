Clix Group partners with Tezzract to lend Rs 1000 crore to MSMEs
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 09:15:00

NEW DELHI: Clix Group , a non-banking financial services group with a pan-India presence, has entered into a strategic partnership with Tezzract Fintech Private Limited, to innovate and bring new age solutions for lending to MSMEs in India. Under this partnership Clix aims to lend an additional Rs 1000 crore to MSMEs by the end of this year.Through this partnership, both companies will build solutions to plug the significant challenges that MSMEs face in raising funding to grow and compete in an already difficult post-Covid environment.
