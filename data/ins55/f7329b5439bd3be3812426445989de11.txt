Find out how to post announcements or events to your local Patch site.The Essex County Prosecutor's Office is offering a free training program focused on helping victims of domestic violence find the resources they need to become healthy and safe. Called 'Sanctuary,' the program will teach students how to provide crisis intervention and outreach services to victims of domestic violence.
