 A 17-year-old boy has been charged with manslaughter in the death of a 20-year-old man in northeast Edmonton last week.
At about 7:40 p.m. Monday, Oct. 10, police were called to an altercation in the area of 120 Avenue and 82 Street.A man was found by police suffering from a stab wound.
