The Outlaws, series 2, review: Stephen Merchant's repeat offenders are criminally underrated
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 21:00:00

There is definitely such a thing as difficult second album syndrome in TV drama. After scoring a hit, the tricky obligation is to repeat it. So it's understandable to fret about quality control as The Outlaws (BBC One) returns a mere eight months on.
The sizzling idea of Stephen Merchant, the first series assembled a motley crew of not-very-ne'er-do-wells who, on community service in Bristol, somehow contrived to thwart a drug gang and trouser their cash. The denouement may have left a few ends loose, but the story concluded with a satisfying smack.
