Jordan Larmour and Dan Sheehan shine as Leinster power past abject Glasgow
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-04 23:01:00

Seven days after Marseille, there was always the probability of a Leinster backlash but we never expected it to be quite as emphatic as this, or quite so humiliating for Glasgow's so-called Warriors.Leinster's determination to send out a statement was summed up five minutes from the end by the attitude of Ciaran Frawley, who could so easily have allowed Glasgow's Jack Dempsey to jog over for a consolation try. But Frawley stretched every sinew to haul the No 8 down and win the turnover.This may not have been Europe and the RDS may not have been packed — four-figure crowds are rare in these parts — but Leinster meant business.
