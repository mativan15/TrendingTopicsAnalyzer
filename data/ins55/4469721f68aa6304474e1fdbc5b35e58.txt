Modern-day decision-making is heavily driven by data analysis. Therefore, the role of statistics is amplified many times and plays a critical role in planning and development across all sectors. To commemorate the role of statistics on society and human life, the United Nations Statistical Commission, at its session in 2010, decided to observe October 20 as World Statistics Day. 
Objectives of the dayAmong the primary objectives of the day, the UN feels that statistics play a crucial role in timely assessing a nation's progress through a series of indicators.
