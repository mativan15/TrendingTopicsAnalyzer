Drake gives away $1,000,000 in Bitcoin during casino live stream
metro.co.uk

2022-05-27 11:18:20

Rapper and noted cryptocurrency fan Drake was feeling generous when he gave away 35 Bitcoins during a live stream.
If you're not up to speed with your crypto conversion rates, that's a cool $1 million – or £800,000.
Drake joined Stake.com's live Twitch stream to give the Bitcoin away to fans around the world.The rapper broadcast his night at the online casino, sharing live action across a number of games with the highlight being an $18million win on his lucky number 11 on the roulette table.
It was Drake's first official live stream collaboration with the company.
