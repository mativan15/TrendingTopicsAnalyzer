Published on Oct 21, 2022 09:56 PM IST Boris Johnson, the man ousted as UK prime minister by his own government three months ago, has emerged as one of the front-runners to replace Liz Truss. At Least three cabinet ministers have openly backed the former prime minister to take reins of 10 Downing street once again. Jacob Rees-Mogg has launched a #BorisorBust campaign on social media. Johnson, who is currently on holiday, may return to the UK as the party races to choose a leader soon.
