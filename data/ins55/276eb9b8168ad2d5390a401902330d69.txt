June 01, 2022 04:10 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--TPG RE Finance Trust, Inc. (NYSE: TRTX) ('TRTX' or the 'Company') today announced that senior members of its management team are scheduled to host investor meetings at Nareit's REITweek: 2022 Investor Conference on June 7, 2022. In addition to the Company's investor meetings, Bob Foley, Chief Financial Officer, will participate in a moderated discussion about TRTX at 11:45am ET.

A live audio webcast of the moderated discussion will be available by visiting the Company's investor relations website at http://investors.
