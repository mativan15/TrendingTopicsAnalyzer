Italian far-right leader formally asks for mandate to govern

Italian far-right leader Giorgia Meloni has said she and her allies have asked the nation's President to give her the mandate to assemble a new government.Ms Meloni and her allies met briefly with President Sergio Mattarella on Friday.Some observers had expected that she would then announce that he had given her the mandate to try to form a government.Instead, she said only that the coalition had proposed her as the next premier.
