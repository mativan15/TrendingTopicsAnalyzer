Tiger Global, known for minting unicorns like Brex, Coinbase and Peloton, saw its losses for the year hit 52% due to the massive tech selloff the market is going through, Bloomberg reported.
Tiger's hedge fund dropped 14.2% last month, after the firm reportedly invested heavily in tech giants such as Snowflake and Carvana just before the downturn started in the first quarter of the year.
