jetcityimage Published on the Value Lab 10/13/22 The iShares Convertible Bond ETF (BATS:ICVT) contains, as you'd expect, convertible bonds, which means bonds that have an option to convert into a certain amount of equity, usually at a price that implies a premium to whatever the equity price traded at the bond's issuance. ICVT is lots of tech bonds, and whatever price they were trading at when the bonds were issued are quite likely to be a lot higher than where they're trading now, meaning the convertibles are far away from being economically convertible into equity.
