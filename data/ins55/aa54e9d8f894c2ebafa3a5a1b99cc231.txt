Committed to completing NDTV open offer, announces Adani Group
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 14:00:00

Adani group had contested the founders' claim, asserting that the deal would not require any clearance from I-T.Adani Group is committed to completing the open offer to buy an extra 26 per cent stake in NDTV, the company said on Wednesday. It may be noted that the conglomerate led by India's richest man Gautam Adani had missed the date for the open offer launch. The offer was to be launched on October 17.

The group has now asked regulator SEBI for comments on its draft open offer letter, it said.
