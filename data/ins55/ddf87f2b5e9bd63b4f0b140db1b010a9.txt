With Patriots' win over Browns, Bill Belichick ties George Halas for second-most overall victories

2022-10-16 21:20:04

On a day where Bill Belichick tied George Halas on the list of all-time wins with 324, the Patriots coach took a moment to acknowledge the impact of Halas, Paul Brown, and those who helped 'lay the building blocks' of the modern NFL.'Coach Halas was a friend of my dad,' Belichick said. 'I have a ton of respect for [him] and the McCaskey Family, and for what he did for pro football.'[Former Cleveland coach] Paul Brown and others like him paved the way for us as coaches,' he added. 'They laid down a lot of the building blocks.
