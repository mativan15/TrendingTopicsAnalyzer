Grainger Engineering Announces CS Alumni to 2022 Hall of Fame
illinois.edu
news
2022-10-18 15:43:22

The Grainger College of Engineering Hall of Fame commemorates the most significant accomplishments throughout the history of Grainger Engineering—accomplishments that have a great impact on society and that represent excellence in engineering leadership, entrepreneurship, and innovation. This year's Hall of Fame class recognizes three Illinois Computer Science alumni whose contributions impact our lives every day. Grainger Engineering empowers tomorrow's leaders, equipping them for a lifetime of collaboration, innovation, and discovery.
