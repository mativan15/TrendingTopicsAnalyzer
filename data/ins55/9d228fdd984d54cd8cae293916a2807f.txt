Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Dame Judi said "the time has come for Netflix to reconsider" suggestions to add a disclaimerDame Judi Dench is the latest high-profile figure to call for a disclaimer to be added to The Crown to make clear the series is not necessarily true.The actress said Netflix "seems willing to blur the lines between historical accuracy and crude sensationalism".Dame Judi said there was a risk that "a significant number of viewers" would take its events as historical truth.
