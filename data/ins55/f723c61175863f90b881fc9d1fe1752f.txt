Arnav Bathla in San Francisco, California Arnav Bathla 
21-year-old Arnav Bathla had dropped out of college, made a bold move of moving into the United States with a one-way ticket, and founded Coinbooks, a San Francisco-based Web3 startup, building an accounting software designed for crypto-native organizations. The company, which Arnav calls 'Quickbooks for crypto', is only 6 months old has now raised a total of $3.2 million with support from world-class investors like Lattice Capital, Founders, Inc.
