NEW DELHI: The Nifty IT index traded negative around 11:21AM(IST)on Wednesday in an upbeat market.Tech Mahindra(up 0.93 per cent), Wipro(up 0.25 per cent) and MPhasis(up 0.2 per cent) were among the top gainers.L&T Technology(down 3.84 per cent), Infosys(down 0.82 per cent), HCL Technologies(down 0.48 per cent), Larsen & Toubro Infotech(down 0.41 per cent) and Tata Consultancy(down 0.38 per cent) were the top losers on the index.The Nifty IT index was down 0.38 per cent at 28206.55 at the time of writing this report.
