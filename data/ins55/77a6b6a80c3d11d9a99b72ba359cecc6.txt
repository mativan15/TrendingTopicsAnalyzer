Tech, talent and startups to help India's Web3 rise: Nasscom report
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 02:15:00

India's rapid adoption of new-age technologies, its growing startup ecosystem, and the large-scale digitally skilled talent potential are the right building blocks for it to emerge as a key player in the global Web3 landscape, according to a report by IT industry association Nasscom. There were over 450 Web3 startups in India as of the first half of the calendar year, with over $1.3 billion invested in Web3 since 2020, the report - The India Web3 Startup Landscape: An Emerging Technology Leadership Frontier - showed.
