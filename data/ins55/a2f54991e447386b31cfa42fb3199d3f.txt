Published: 01:07 EDT, 28 May 2022 | Updated: 01:08 EDT, 28 May 2022 He counts Justin Bieber as a close friend and has an estimated net worth of $4million. But despite his star status, rapper The Kid Laroi mingled with the locals at a pub in Sydney's Newtown on Wednesday. The 18-year-old, who is in Australia for a stadium tour and to launch a collaboration with McDonald's, was pictured playing the pokies and drinking Corona beer.
