Starmer demands general election as he slams 'delusional' Truss
express.co.uk
politics
2022-10-20 09:28:00

(Image: SKY)Sir Starmer has demanded a General Election now, hitting out at "delusional" and "insulting" Prime Minister Liz Truss.Speaking at the Trade Unions Congress Conference, the Labour leader said: "Britain cannot afford the chaos of the Conservatives anymore."We need a general election now."Speaking about Ms Truss' Government, he added: "It's delusional, it's insulting".He continued: "She doesn't care about the distribution of wealth in Britain, she hasn't U-turned on that."Working people will not be better off if we make the rich richer.
