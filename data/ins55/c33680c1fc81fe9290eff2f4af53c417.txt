New York, NY, May 26, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Decentralised finance (DeFi), also known as open finance innovation, was a watershed point in the history of finance. With the advent of tokens like Compound and Sushiswap promising significant payouts to crypto traders, it grew to prominence in the summer of 2020. According to DeFi Pulse, the total value locked in DeFi protocols has increased by tenfold since May 2020, standing at a whopping amount of $78 billion. This is the current value of all cryptocurrency deposits used for lending, staking, liquidity pools, and other purposes.
