The Indian stock market is enormous and highly volatile. It is not that easy for beginners or new investors to keep a track on each and every stock of the entire stock market universe. Here, market indices such as Sensex come into picture which comprises the best stocks which represent the whole or a part of a specific sector of the Indian stock market. It also assists the investors to easily make coherent and articulate investment decisions timely. Here's a detailed guide on what Sensex is, how it works, what it comprises and how one can invest in Sensex.
