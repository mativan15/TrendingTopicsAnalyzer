Collider.com It's that time of year again. The month of screams and scares is upon us, and we're all looking to get our fix of horror. Now, you might be getting your dose from movies and TV shows. That's understandable. Who doesn't love a good slasher, or a suspenseful tale of the supernatural? However, fictional horror can only get you so far. After a while, it starts to lose its edge; there's no serial killer in a hockey mask coming for you, there aren't ghosts hiding in the corners of your house, there's not a boogeyman under your bed.
