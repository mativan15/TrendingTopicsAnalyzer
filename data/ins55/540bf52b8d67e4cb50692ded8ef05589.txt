Lance Brings 30 Years of Industry Experience to the Growing OrganizationCINCINNATI, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- On the cusp of the company's 75th anniversary, HORAN announces a new addition to the organization's leadership team. Nicolas E. Lance joins HORAN as its new Chief Operating Officer (COO), effective immediately. Lance will lead company growth, overseeing corporate functions and partnering with President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Terence L. Horan to strategically position HORAN for continued success.
