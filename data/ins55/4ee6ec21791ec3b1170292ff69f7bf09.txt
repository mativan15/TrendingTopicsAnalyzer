What has the Government decided in relation to evictions?At its Cabinet meeting on Wednesday, Ministers approved a memo from Minister for Housing Darragh O'Brien to ban all termination notices for rented homes between now and April 1?But has the Government not opposed calls from the Opposition to impose such a ban for many months? What's changed?It has not been in favour of a ban until now. What has changed has been the dramatically changing landscape in the rented market sector. The number of summary notices of termination for rented properties has ballooned over the past two years.
