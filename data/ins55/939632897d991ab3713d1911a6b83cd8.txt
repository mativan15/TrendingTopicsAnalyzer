With salary cap space, Leafs summon forwards Robertson, Simmonds from AHL

The Maple Leafs on Sunday recalled forwards Nick Robertson, left, and Wayne Simmonds, right, along with defenceman Victor Mete from the AHL's Toronto Marlies after they were provided salary cap space with goalie Matt Murray on long-term injured reserve. (Getty Images/File)The Toronto Maple Leafs recalled forwards Nick Robertson and Wayne Simmonds along with defenceman Victor Mete from the American Hockey League's Toronto Marlies on Sunday.Toronto was provided some cap space to make the moves after goaltender Matt Murray was placed on long-term injured reserve on Saturday.
