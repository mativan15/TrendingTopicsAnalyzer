iStockLIC is now the seventh most valuable company in the country.NEW DELHI: Bears pushed the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) shares lower on Monday to all-time low levels, with the stock sliding below the Rs 800 mark. The stock traded down 1.64 per cent at Rs 787.10. During the day, it hit the low of Rs 786.05. From its IPO issue price of Rs 949, the prices are down over 17 per cent. With the fall in share prices, the market capitalisation of the company also fell below Rs 5 lakh crore to Rs 4.
