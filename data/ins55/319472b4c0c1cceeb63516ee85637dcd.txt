After you've figured out how to start an LLC in Connecticut and you've filed your LLC formation documents, you'll need to make sure you keep your LLC in compliance by filing taxes and renewing licensure. Next, there are a few tasks that aren't necessarily required to form a Connecticut LLC, but if completed, will help your company stay strong and productive for years to come.
Get Registered and Pay Taxes
If your LLC has employees or collects sales tax on goods, then you'll need to file CT business registration documents.
