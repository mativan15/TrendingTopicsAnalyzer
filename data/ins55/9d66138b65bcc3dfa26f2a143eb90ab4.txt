 The death of a 24-year-old man found lying outside in a Red Deer neighbourhood this week is being investigated by the RCMP's major crimes unit.
In a news release issued Friday, the RCMP said an autopsy performed earlier in the day concluded the man was the victim of a homicide. Police did not reveal how he is believed to have been killed.Police have identified him as Brandon Robert Loughlin of Red Deer.
