Hacked car dealer Pendragon defies $60m ransom demand
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 09:20:00

A luxury motor retailer is defying an extortion plot by revealing that it is refusing to pay a $60 million ransom demand from dark web hackers.Pendragon, which owns dealerships including Stratstone luxury car showrooms, told The Times it was under a 'live cyberattack and is being held to ransom by a gang connected to a sophisticated group known as Lockbit 3.0'.The Stock Exchange-listed company said its servers had been hacked but it believed the attackers had stolen only 5 per cent of its database.
