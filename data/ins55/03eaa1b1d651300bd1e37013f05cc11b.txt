Nathan Parker is the new finance chief at Coda Octopus
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-31 10:45:18

Coda Octopus (NASDAQ:CODA) appoints Nathan Parker as the CFO succeeding Mr. Mike Midgley effective June 13, 2022.Nathan Parker joins the company as a well-qualified and experienced financial professional who will bring a number of additional skills to this role and most recently he served as CFO at the Water Treatment business of Franklin Electric.
