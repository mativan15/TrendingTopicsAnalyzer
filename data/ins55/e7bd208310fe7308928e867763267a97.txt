US business travel revenue back at pre-pandemic levels, says IHG
2022 Google Inc.
news
2022-10-21 11:05:23

The owner of the Crowne Plaza and Holiday Inn hotel chains has said US business travel revenue was now back at pre-pandemic levels, in a hopeful sign for the industry that remote working will not permanently cut demand.InterContinental Hotels Group reported revenue per available room, a key industry metric, for the US hotels that make up more than half of its estate was up 6.2 per cent in the three months to September 30, compared with 2019. Group revpar was up 2.7 per cent compared with 2019.Demand for business travel in the US was equal with pre-pandemic levels, while revenue from leisure travellers was up 13 per cent compared with the same period in 2019 'boosted by a strong summer vacation period and Labor Day', the company said.
