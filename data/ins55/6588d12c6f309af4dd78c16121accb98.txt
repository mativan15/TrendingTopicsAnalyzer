The story has been told countless times regarding the Kansas City Chiefs' comeback victory over the Buffalo Bills in last season's divisional-round playoff. Patrick Mahomes orchestrated the thirteen-second drive to set up the game-tying field goal and secure the victory in overtime.

Mahomes and Bills pro bowl quarterback Josh Allen were teammates for Wednesday's much anticipated 'The Match' golf event. Following the showdown with Tom Brady and Aaron Rodgers, the quarterbacks sat down with TNT's Ernie Johnson for a special roundtable discussion on their football careers.
