DUBLIN, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The "Nitrogen Oxide Control Systems Market: Global Opportunity Analysis and Industry Forecast, 2020-2030" report has been added to  ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.
The global nitrogen oxide control systems market size was valued at $4.3 billion in 2020, and is projected to reach $7.3 billion by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 5.4% from 2020 to 2030. Nitrogen oxides (NOx) is a mixture of nitric oxide (NO) and nitrogen dioxide (NO2), which are gases produced from natural sources, motor vehicles, and other fuel burning processes.
