The prolonged downturn in cryptocurrency prices, along with broader tech sector woes, has led to a growing number of layoffs at crypto firms as participants brace for a bumpier ride ahead.

On Thursday, the Winklevoss-led crypto exchange and custodian Gemini was one of the latest to announce job cuts of about 10% of its workforce, with the firm citing 'turbulent market conditions that are likely to persist for some time.
