As the cold, dark days of autumn and winter draw in, Britons' concerns over the rising cost of energy, fuel, food, and other goods are at an all-time high. While the government has announced a package of support to limit how much households will pay per unit of gas and electricity from October, many are fearful of getting overwhelmed by soaring costs.A recent poll of more than 2,000 UK adults found that nearly one in four people plan never to turn on their heating this winter in a bid to save money.
