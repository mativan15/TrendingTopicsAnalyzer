Cash in the cupboard: 24 million old smartphones, worth £1bn, kept as 'spares' at home, study finds
sky.com
news
2022-10-19 03:06:13

An estimated 24 million old smartphones, worth £1 billion, were kept as 'spares' by UK householders over the past three years, a new study has revealed.The survey, by Deloitte's Digital Consumer Trends, found two in five buyers are holding on to their unused phones after they swap or upgrade.
In 2022 alone, seven million smartphones worth more than £500 million will be left in cupboards or drawers, it suggests.A total of 4,160 UK consumers aged 16-75 took part in the study and revealed that of those who bought or received a new phone in the past year, 38% held on to their old device.
