Electric Car Subsidies Are A Bad Investment
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 10:00:01

Climate activists and politicians constantly tell us electric cars are cleaner, cheaper, and better. California and many countries, including the U.K., Germany, and Japan, will even prohibit the sale of new gas and diesel cars within a decade or two. But if electric cars are really so good, why do we need to ban the alternatives? And why do we need to subsidize electric cars to the tune of $30 billion per year?The reality is far more muddled than the boosters of electric cars would have you believe.
