NEW YORK, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Attorney Advertising -- Bronstein, Gewirtz & Grossman, LLC notifies investors that a class action lawsuit has been filed against LMP Automotive Holdings, Inc. ("LMP" or the "Company) (NASDAQ: LMPX) and certain of its officers, on behalf of shareholders who purchased or otherwise acquired LMP securities between June 29, 2021 and May 19, 2022, inclusive (the "Class Period"). Such investors are encouraged to join this case by visiting the firm's site: www.bgandg.
