15 October 2022 by Melissa Romualdi 
ET Canada 'Feud', Ryan Murphy's historical anthology, is finally back with season 2. After a five-year gap, the FX series returns with an all-new, star-studded installment called 'Capote's Women', with the focus on the falling out between notorious writer Truman Capote and several of his female friends, including Ann Woodward, Babe Paley, Cz Guest, Gloria Guinness, Joanne Carson and Slim Keith.'It's such a great lineup of extraordinary women [and] great roles,' Naomi Watts tells Et about the series, in which she'll portray Paley.
