Liverpool were toppled in the Champions League final by Real Madrid and their success has benefited Chelsea, who saw former star Eden Hazard pick up a winners medalEden Hazard's Champions League triumph has benefitted ChelseaChelsea watched on as Liverpool were beaten in the Champions League final which landed them millions. The Blues were unable to defend their European crown as they were knocked out by eventual winners Real Madrid in the last eight. Los Blancos claimed a fourteenth success in the competition thanks to Vinicius Jnr's second-half strike at the Stade de France.
