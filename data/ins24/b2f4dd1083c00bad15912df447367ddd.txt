October 20, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHARLOTTE, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Armstrong Transport Group ('Armstrong,' or the 'Company'), a leading non-asset-based logistics provider, today announced the grand opening of its new Charlotte headquarters. Located on the fifth floor of the Vantage building, a five-acre mixed-use development in Charlotte's vibrant South End, Armstrong's new, state-of-the-art office space will help foster collaboration across Armstrong's workforce, in addition to increasing opportunities to recruit local talent and give back to the local Charlotte community.
