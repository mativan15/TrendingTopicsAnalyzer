Published: 09:31 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 09:34 EDT, 27 May 2022 Boris Johnson today hailed the Government's energy bills support as a 'big bazooka' - although the Prime Minister admitted the payments won't 'necessarily' protect struggling Britons from the full impact of the cost-of-living crisis.On a visit to Stockton-on-Tees, Mr Johnson said the £21billion package of support announced by Chancellor Rishi Sunak yesterday was a 'very, very substantial' commitment' to help families through 'a bumpy time' as household bills soar.
