Terra Fiasco Propels South Korea To Launch A New Crypto Oversight Committee
ibtimes.com.au
news
2022-06-02 08:57:34

Terra's LUNA and UST's meteoric collapse has many ramifications in the cryptocurrency industry, including a much stricter policy and more tedious supervision, particularly in South Korea, where an oversight committee set to launch in July was created to do precisely that.
The worldwide ruckus Terra created hastened the process of creating the Digital Asset Committee in South Korea -- an oversight team tasked to supervise and control digital assets in the country.
