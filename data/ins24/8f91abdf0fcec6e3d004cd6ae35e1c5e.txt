After another unprecedented year, Turtle & Hughes anchors its ESG strategy with a new report outlining its commitments to sustainable business services, growing philanthropic donations, and achieving an Ecovadis gold rating, among others.
LINDEN, N.J., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Turtle & Hughes, one of the nation's largest independent electrical and industrial distribution companies and a recognized leader in integrated supply, published today its second annual ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) Report.
