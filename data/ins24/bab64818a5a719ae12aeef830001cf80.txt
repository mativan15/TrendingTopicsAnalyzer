How to watch England at World Cup 2022: FREE live stream and TV channel for every match

The UK television schedule for the Qatar World Cup has been confirmed, with the BBC set to show England's opening fixture.

It's a quick start for the Three Lions, who take on Iran in Group B at 1pm on Monday, November 21, which is the third game of the tournament on its second day. The Khalifa International Stadium in Qatar's capital Doha will host the game. It will be the Three Lions' second visit to the stadium, having lost 1-0 to Brazil in 2009.
