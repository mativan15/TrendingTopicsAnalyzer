Friend who "blocked' woman from life after 15 years backed for drastic plan
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 12:17:08

The internet has urged a woman to block her "self-centered" friend after revealing that she felt like she was being used for emotional support, and her friend never really cared about her.In a post shared on Mumsnet on Sunday, under the username Friendblocker1234, she explained that her friend of 15 years, Mary, has had a hard life, going through an abusive relationship, and she's always been there to offer comfort and support.But Mary never has bothered to ask her how she's feeling or even send birthday wishes, and after putting up with her behavior for many years she's now decided to cut her out of her life.
