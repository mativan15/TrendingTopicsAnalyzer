Gavin Macomber is the CEO of Cloudli. getty Instinctively, we know that productive businesses are successful businesses. Data from Harvard Business Review supports this, showing that companies that are 40% more productive have operating margins 30-50% higher than others in their industry. If you're a small business owner, you're probably wondering how you can enhance your productivity. The good news? There are a few tech-enabled hacks that can help. First things first: What is productivity? Productivity is the cousin of efficiency; the two often get confused at family reunions! This article by Michael Mankins does a great job differentiating the concepts: Efficiency is doing the same things with fewer inputs; productivity, on the other hand, is doing more with what you've got.
