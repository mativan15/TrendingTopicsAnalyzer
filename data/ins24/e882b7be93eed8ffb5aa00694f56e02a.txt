Zenith Minerals Ltd.

Zenith Minerals Ltd. is engaged in the identification, exploration and development of base, industrial and precious metal deposits. Its projects includes Develin Creek VMS, Copper Project, Red Mountain Gold Project, Split Rocks Gold Project, Earaheedy and Mount Alexander. The company was founded on April 24, 2006 and is headquartered in West Perth, Australia.
