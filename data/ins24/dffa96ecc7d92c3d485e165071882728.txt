The Flash 2 Already Has A Script
ign.com
entertainment
2022-10-17 23:46:17

The Flash really is the fastest man alive as the sequel to his upcoming solo movie has already been written. According to The Hollywood Reporter, a sequel has been penned by Aquaman writer David Leslie Johnson-McGoldrick. The Flash 2's script was reportedly put together just in case The Flash does well when it's released on June 23, 2023.The Flash stars Ezra Miller as Barry Allen in the character's first solo film since he was introduced to the DC movie universe in Batman V Superman: Dawn of Justice.
