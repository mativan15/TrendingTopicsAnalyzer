Published: 15:30 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 15:30 EDT, 27 May 2022 Lionel Messi and his father have told Barcelona president Joan Laporta to stop talking of a return to the club for the Argentine superstar after growing irritated by his continued comments.Messi joined Paris St Germain last summer after Barcelona failed to fall in line with La Liga financial regulations making it impossible to reach a contract agreement.Laporta has continued to speculate on Messi coming back and said this week that if the Camp Nou idol one day wanted to return it would have to be for free, even indicating the motivation behind his departure was based on money.
