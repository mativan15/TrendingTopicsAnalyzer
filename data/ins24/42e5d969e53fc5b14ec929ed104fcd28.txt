May 31, 2022 01:38 PM Eastern Daylight Time OAKLAND, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--As part of National Safety Month in June, PG&E will host two free 811 safe digging webinars to help customers 'dig safe' and avoid expensive repairs, PG&E will host two free 811 safe digging webinars for homeowners and contractors. The webinar sessions will provide an overview of the 811 process and guidance for digging safely once underground utilities have been marked. Attendees will also have an opportunity to ask questions of PG&E 811 Damage Prevention experts in each session.
