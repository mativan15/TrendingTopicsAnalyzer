By Sakura Gray October 16, 2022 / 10:45 PM / CBS Sacramento SACRAMENTO — Sunday was a busy day for Country Music and basketball fans in the capital city.The GoldenSky County Music Festival wrapped up its debut in Sacramento after bringing in 25,000 Country Music fans. In addition, the 2-day festival brought a welcome boom to the local economy.Mike Testa, CEO of Visit Sacramento, says that roughly 65% of attendees came from outside of Sacramento."So, they're staying in hotels, and they're flying into the market, they're getting here in different ways and renting cars, so the spend is in the millions for sure," he continued.
