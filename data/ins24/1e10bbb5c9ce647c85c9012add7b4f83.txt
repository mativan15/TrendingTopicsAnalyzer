October 19, 2022 10:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--DataCore Software announced today the new Perifery Panel for Adobe Premiere Pro. The Perifery Panel streamlines access to content for DataCore Swarm and Perifery users on Adobe Premiere Pro, significantly improving the efficiency of media production workflows. The new panel allows users to enrich the metadata of searched objects and speeds up the search for media assets, optimizing content browsing on one of the most popular video editing software applications.
