Entrepreneur, philanthropist and international speaker. Regan Hillyer is here to help you make a bigger impact.

getty

The recent global pandemic forced many companies to rethink how they could re-offer and hold the multitude of live events that were canceled. We saw the rise of a plethora of online meetings, webinars, product launches, concerts, readings, social events, games, competitions... the list was virtually endless. And with this massive swing to creatively hosting events and engendering engagement from audiences came the increase of freely available, user-friendly online video platforms and a concentration on presentations especially tailored for the effective virtual get-together.
