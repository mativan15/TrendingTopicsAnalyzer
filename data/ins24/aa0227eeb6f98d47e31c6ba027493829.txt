Published: 4:30 pm, October 20, 2022Updated: 4:30 pm, October 20, 2022Hit BBC Two factual entertainment show Celebrity Antiques Road Trip returns on Sunday for its eleventh series, and the line-up of stars heading out on the road includes husband-and-wife Strictly Come Dancing professionals Janette Manrara and Aljaž Škorjanec.
The couple, who used to compete against each other every Saturday night on Strictly before leaving the show in recent years, will be going head-to-head once again to see who can uncover the most interesting antiques and make the most money at auction.
