Indian stock indices extended their gains for the sixth straight session on Friday, led by strength in banking and financial stocks coupled with good second-quarter corporate earnings. At 9.45 am, Sensex traded at 59,475.40 points, up 272.50 points or 0.46 per cent, whereas Nifty traded at 17,635.75 points, up 71.80 points or 0.41 per cent. Among the Nifty 50 stocks, 32 advanced and the rest 18 slipped this morning, National Stock Exchange data showed. "IT and banks account for the major chunk of India Inc's profits.
