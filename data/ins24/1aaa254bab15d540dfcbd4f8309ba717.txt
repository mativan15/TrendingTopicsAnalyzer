The Curse of Our Culture of Overpromising
chron.com
news
2022-06-03 16:00:00

"We are going to change the world" is a slogan of many startups. Every product starts with an idea, and some ideas become so powerful that they conquer the minds of millions of people, including the most wealthy people in the world. Investors are willing to spend millions of dollars to be a part of something big. However, the most promising concepts often break into pieces when they reach the market, and even more, ideas forever stay on the paper or drawing desks. Why does it happen? The root of this problem in our modern business culture — the culture of overpromising.
