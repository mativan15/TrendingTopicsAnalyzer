Amazon CEO of Worldwide Consumer Business Resigns After 23 Years: 'It's Time For Me To Build Again.'
chron.com
news
2022-06-03 19:30:00

After 23 years with Amazon, Dave Clark, who most recently served as CEO of its worldwide consumer business and as a top lieutenant to CEO Andy Jassy, announced he'll be leaving the company effective July 1. Clark's position made him one of the highest-ranking executives at Amazon, and the company had yet to name a replacement when news of his departure broke. In a post announcing the news, Jassy stated 'we still have more work in front of us to get to where we ultimately want to be in our consumer business.
