Why big investors are pressing companies for data like where their warehouses and factories are
businessinsider.com
news
2022-05-29 12:47:04

Investors and lawmakers are increasingly scrutinizing aspects of the complex, fast-growing multi-trillion-dollar ESG- and impact-investing universes. The Securities and Exchange Commission has started cracking down on the ways investment firms label their funds as environmentally sound, and lawmakers and executives are sounding off about their concerns over investing using environmental, social, and governance criteria. Companies' policies, a key part of the corporate sustainability web, are under a microscope this shareholder meeting season.
