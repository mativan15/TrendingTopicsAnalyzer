By Reuters Published: 16:34 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 16:34 EDT, 19 October 2022 BOURNEMOUTH, England, Oct 19 (Reuters) - Che Adams´s first-half header earned Southampton a 1-0 victory at local rivals Bournemouth on Wednesday as they won for the first time in six games to move out of the Premier League relegation zone. The Scotland international found space in between the Bournemouth defence to nod home a ninth-minute goal, expertly placing a cross from Romain Perraud into the home team´s net.
