Are you a New State Mobile player? You have a chance to win a share of 1000 Chicken Medals. Check details. In some very good news, it has been announced that New State Mobile players have a chance to win 1000 Chicken Medals. Want to know how? As per the information provided by the game, players whose team are victorious in the new Underbridge Round Deathmatch RDM map have the chance to win a share of 1000 Chicken Medals. New State Mobile in a statement said, "Our first place in the RDM challenge is finally here! Players whose team are Victorious in the new Underbridge Round Deathmatch RDM map have the chance to win a share of 1000 Chicken Medals! Jump in and participate in the NEW STATE MOBILE Discord!"It can be noted that the contest starts from today, May 27, 2022 (10AM KST) and will conclude on May 30, 2022 (10 AM KST).
