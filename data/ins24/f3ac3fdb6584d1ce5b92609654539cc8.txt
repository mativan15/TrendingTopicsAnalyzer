PITTSFORD, NY / ACCESSWIRE / October 18, 2022 / Infinite Group, Inc. (OTCQB:IMCI) (the "Company" or "IGI"), a developer of cybersecurity software and related cybersecurity consulting, advisory, and managed information security services, announced today that the Company plans to implement a 75-to-1 reverse split of its common stock in preparation for the planned listing of its securities on The Nasdaq Capital Market ("Nasdaq"). The Company's common stock will begin trading on a split-adjusted basis on the OTC Markets system effective with the open of the market on Wednesday, October 19, 2022 under the temporary ticker symbol "IMCID.
