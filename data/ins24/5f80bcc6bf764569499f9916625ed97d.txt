Appoints Ultano Kindelan as the Country Manager for Spain to expand rapidly across the region

Pixis' AI Infrastructure products now available to businesses in Spain

MADRID, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pixis, the Californian headquartered codeless AI infrastructure company, today announced the onboarding of Ultano Kindelan as the Country Manager of Spain citing its expansion into the market. Currently, the Series C funded company provides codeless AI technology for demand generation and marketing optimization to over 200 businesses across the American, MENA and APAC regions.
