Coronation bank holiday faces local elections clash in England

Image source, Andrew MilliganImage caption, The coronation of King Charles will be the same week as local electionsElection officials have warned about a clash with next year's local elections on 4 May if a bank holiday is added to the coronation weekend.The coronation of King Charles III will be on Saturday 6 May.But there are concerns that if Friday 5 May becomes a bank holiday, that will fall on the day planned for counting votes from local elections in England.The Association of Electoral Administrators has asked the government for clarification.
