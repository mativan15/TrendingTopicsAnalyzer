Ethical business requires us to focus on ESGgetty
Today is Global Ethics Day, a day recognizing the fundamental role ethics plays in the proper functioning of business and society. This year it is an especially poignant day of commemoration for me as I am choosing to retire from my role as CEO of the Institute of Management Accountants, a position I have held for 15 years. As head of the leading global organization for accounting and finance professionals, knowing as I do how ethics affects the way business is conducted and how business in turn impacts society, I have emphasized the importance of ethics in business decision making.
