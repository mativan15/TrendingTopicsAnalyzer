How long is New Tales from the Borderlands?
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-22 03:30:34

Take a trip back to the world of Borderlands with New Tales from the Borderlands, an episodic game series spun off from the original Tales from the Borderlands from Telltale Games. This is a brand new adventure with new characters, a new story and new mechanics just waiting for you to jump in. It's a choose-your-own-adventure narrative that has a little something for everyone.Most of your time will be spent watching stories unfold and participating in them with quick-time events, making conversational choices and occasionally controlling a character as part of an exploration segment.
