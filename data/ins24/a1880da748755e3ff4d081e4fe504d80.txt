Amit Mitra, the principal chief advisor to the West Bengal chief minister and the state finance department Thursday said the Centre has not released Rs 27,000 crore Integrated Goods and Services Tax dues to the states, which if cleared would have bolstered the country's economic growth. Mitra, who was the former finance minister of the state, asserted that Bengal's growth story has remained intact and its GST growth in 2021-22 was 23 per cent.
