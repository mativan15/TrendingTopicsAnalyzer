Election SpotlightBy Joel MalkinOct 18, 2022Photo: Getty Images North AmericaIn our Election Spotlight this time around is the Palm Beach County Schools referendum that is on your ballot. Schools Superintendent Mike Burke says it asks voters to continue funding things like art, music PE, health, choice and career education teachers. "About 25 percent of the money goes first to those 750 teachers, another 25 percent of the money goes to safety and mental health enhancements. We added mental health professionals to all of our 180 schools and we have more counselors, psychologists, therapists that can provide an array of services to those kids in need on our campuses.
