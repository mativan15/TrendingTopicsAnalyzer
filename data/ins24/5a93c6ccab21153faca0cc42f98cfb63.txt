NASHVILLE, Tenn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nissan today announced the launch of its 'Kickin' It with Kicks' campaign, a grassroots endeavor to help music students in Nashville hoping to break into the music industry and advance their careers. 'Nissan moved to Music City in 2006 and has been closely tied-in with the city's vibrant music scene ever since, and we try to bring some of that emotion into every product we produce,' said Nick Reese, Director, Chief Marketing Manager for Nissan's CUV/SUVs.
