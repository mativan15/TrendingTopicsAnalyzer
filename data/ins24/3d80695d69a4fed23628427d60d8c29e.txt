Can You Actually Fix Your Tech By Smacking It?
lifehacker.com
news
2022-05-27 13:00:00

Tech 'emergencies' are often much simpler than they look. And sometimes, the simplest solution of them all (or the one that feels most instinctual) is to go 'dad mode' on it, by which I mean you simply and elegantly smack the shit out your remote until it works again.We here at Lifehacker are mere mortals, and we get fed up with tech just like anyone else. Take Senior Health Editor Beth Skwarecki, who shared with me the time she learned how to fix her old car's fuse box with a well-paced kick. Or there's Staff Writer Stephen Johnson, who once had a beater car that wouldn't start until he opened up the hood and gave it a crack with a hammer.
