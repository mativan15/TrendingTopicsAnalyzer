FORT COLLINS, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--Coastal Beverage Company, a leading beverage distributor in North Carolina, has seen marked business growth over the last 18 months since implementing Encompass Cloud, an ERP system with eCommerce, data sharing capabilities, and inherent connections with manufacturers and retailers. In addition, the company has met customer needs with superior execution and connected the three-tier BevAlc supply chain at unprecedented levels.Encompass Technologies is a comprehensive cloud-based ERP, CRM, eCommerce, and data solutions provider for the food and beverage industry with a vision of minimizing empty shelves and product waste.
