Yotpo is a Business Reporter clientLoyalty programmes benefit shoppers and businesses alike, especially as customer acquisition costs continue to skyrocketIncreased paid advertising costs, more competitors on the market and the devaluation of third-party cookies are all pointing businesses towards revamping their retention efforts to improve overall business performance. For many forward-thinking brands, this means creating a strategic loyalty programme.According to Yotpo's State of Brand Loyalty Survey 2022, loyalty programmes make the majority (60 per cent) of global shoppers more brand loyal.
