Key Takeaways Chevron paused operations in 2019 due to sanctions placed by the US aimed at restoring free elections in the country. Venezuela has the world's largest oil reserves. It will take time to get production back up to full capacity, so investors will have to be patient.
 Oil giant Chevron has been denied oil production in Venezuela since sanctions were imposed in 2019, continuing roughly five years of political pressure on Venezuela to be more democratic. But it now looks like those sanctions will be lifted, freeing Chevron to resume its operations.
