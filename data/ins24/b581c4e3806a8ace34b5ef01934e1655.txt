A new system for online passport renewal is here and this is how it works
sfgate.com
travel
2022-10-19 20:14:30

The timely and laborious undertaking to renew a passport — often requiring months to process and printing out physical paperwork — is facing disruptive innovation from President Joe Biden.

The president signed an executive order in Dec. 2021 aimed at modernizing tech in government and demanded the federal government 'design and deliver services in a manner that people of all abilities can navigate.'

Among the various directives laid out for improving customer experiences and service delivery, was a call to streamline online passport renewal.
