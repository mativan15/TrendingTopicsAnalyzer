A file photo shows a woman receiving a massage. Steinbach RCMP say a woman reported being sexually assaulted during a massage by a 51-year-old man in Blumenort, Man. Winnipeg police say they also received a report this week of a sexual assault during a massage. (Robert Short/CBC)A woman in southern Manitoba says she was sexually assaulted during a massage by a man she believed was a registered therapist, RCMP say — and police believe there may be other victims.The woman filed a report with Steinbach RCMP on Friday, police said in a Saturday news release.
