Evonik Sells Unit of Specialty-Additives Business
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 10:24:00

By Pierre Bertrand Evonik Industries AG on Friday said that it has sold its TAA derivatives unit, part of its specialty-additives division, to Italian chemical company SABO SpA. As part of the divestment, the Italian company bought two production sites, one in Marl, Germany, the other in Liaoyang, China, for an undisclosed price, Evonik said. "We want to continue our profitable growth in specialty chemicals," Evonik said in a statement. "That includes divesting businesses that do not longer fit to our strategic focus.
