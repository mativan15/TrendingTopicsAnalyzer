Fidelity Investments to create 300 jobs in Ireland
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-17 08:56:11

Boston headquartered Fidelity Investments is to create 300 jobs in the next two years as the company continues to expand.The new full time roles will be split between Galway and Dublin, with 200 of the roles linked to the Galway hub. Staff will be able to work flexibly, but must be resident in Ireland due to taxation and employment regulations.Among the new roles being filled are software engineering, cloud, and cyber security. There will also be a number of business operations roles in Dublin.The expansion will grow the total number of people employed by the Irish operation to 1800.
