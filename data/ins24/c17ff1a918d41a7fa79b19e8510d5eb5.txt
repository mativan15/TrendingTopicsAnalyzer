Energy bills aid helps power up government's approval score
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

The coalition government has enjoyed a post-budget bounce, with its approval rating increasing six points to 42 per cent in the latest poll.The Sunday Times/Behaviour & Attidues poll's fieldwork began two days after the announcement of the 2023 budget, when the government announced a number of one-off and long-term measures to tackle the energy and cost of living crisis. The survey was conducted from September 29 to October 11.Just over half of the public (51 per cent) are dissatisfied with the current government, down six percentage points from last month.
