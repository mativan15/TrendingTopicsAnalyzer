There's a saying in Dutch, 'Je kan het dak op,' which, translated literally, means 'you can go up on the roof.' But what it really means is 'get lost.'The origins of the saying are difficult to trace, but for Paul van Roosmalen, it probably has to do with the fact that no one thinks of a flat, grey roof as somewhere you want to be. Van Roosmalen works in the Department of Sustainability for the municipality of Rotterdam, where he leads a program called Multifunctional Rooftops. And, in many ways, his job is to change that preconception.
