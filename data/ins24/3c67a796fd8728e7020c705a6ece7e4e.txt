Ørsted's Offshore Wind Industrial Development Fund sees productive results in nurturing talent, technologies to grow local supply chains

TAIPEI, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ørsted and Metal Industries Research & Development Centre (MIRDC) announced today (19) the results of the Industrial Development Fund which provided resources and knowledge-sharing to help enhance local sub-suppliers' technological capabilities, close the skills gap and nurture talent to sustain the healthy development of offshore wind supply chain in Taiwan.

The initiative, which was backed by a NTD 60 million fund fully sponsored by Ørsted, enabled more than 60 companies to ramp up capabilities required to tap into the offshore wind supply chain.
