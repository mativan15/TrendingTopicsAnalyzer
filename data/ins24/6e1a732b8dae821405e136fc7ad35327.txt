Variety - TV News At September's San Sebastian Festival, Movistar+, Spain's biggest content investor, hosted a press conference for the world premiere of its newest original, 'Offworld' ('Apagón'), featuring five stories, all set in a Spain without electricity, the result of a massive solar storm.On-stage, fielding questions, were not 'Offworld's' actors, however, though they included Goya actress winner Patricia López Arnaiz, but the series' screenwriters and directors.
