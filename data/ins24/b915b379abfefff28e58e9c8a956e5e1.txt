HANOI, VIETNAM - Media OutReach - 2 June 2022 - Recently, Piggy Go, a popular casual & social mobile game under Forever9 Games' was launched in Vietnam, and attracted well-known YouTube influencers such as Lâm Vlog , Gãy Media , Nguyễn Minh Trường and Thien Ph to strongly recommend it.









Since its launch, Piggy Go has triggered a download boom in Europe, Malaysia, Thailand, and other regions, ranking first on the Google Play free app download list and game download list in many countries. So far, its total registered users worldwide exceed 12 million.
