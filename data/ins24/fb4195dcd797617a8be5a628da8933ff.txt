BRANFORD, Conn., June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sachem Capital Corp. (NYSE American: SACH) has sold an additional $2.5 million original principal amount of its 7.125% unsecured, unsubordinated notes due 2027 ('Notes') pursuant to the exercise, in part, of the underwriters' over-allotment option from the previously announced registered public offering, bringing the total gross proceeds of the offering to $30.0 million, and net proceeds to $28.8 million after payment of underwriting discounts and commissions and estimated offering expenses payable by the company.
