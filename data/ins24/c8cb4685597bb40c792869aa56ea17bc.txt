Herbein adds organizational psychology firm, enters Ohio
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 15:02:00

Herbein+Co., a Reading-based accounting and advisory firm with a Pittsburgh office, on Wednesday said it has merged with Watterson & Associates, an organizational psychology firm near Cleveland.

Terms of the deal were not disclosed.

Watterson, which partners with companies to manage employee development needs, is initially operating as Herbein | Watterson Talent Solutions until January 2024, when it will become Herbein Talent Solutions. It helps organizations with hiring, employee development and succession planning.
