The cost of borrowing: What happens when the debtor is disciplined by a creditor?
thestar.com
business
2022-10-17 10:02:00

Will financial markets discipline governments or will governments discipline financial markets? Events in the United Kingdom recently seem to provide a clear answer to this question. What clearer evidence of the power of markets do we need than the recent dismissal of the UK's Chancellor of The Exchequer in response to financial market chaos.

On Sept. 23, the U.K. government's new chief finance minister announced his mini-budget. The following week, the cost of government borrowing soared as investors ditched British government bonds.
