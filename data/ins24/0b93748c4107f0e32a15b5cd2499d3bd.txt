By Dominic Chopping STOCKHOLM--Tele2 AB on Thursday backed its guidance for 2022 after reporting a rise in third-quarter underlying earnings amid a strong performance in the Baltics and its Swedish business-to-business offering while benefiting from a slight tailwind in roaming revenues. The Sweden-based telecom company posted a net profit of 1 billion kronor ($89.1 million), from SEK1.04 billion. Analysts polled by FactSet had expected SEK795 million. Underlying earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, amortization and the cost of leased assets, or Ebitdaal--the company's preferred metric--rose 2.
