Fans celebrate, mourn last scheduled harness racing card in Saint John
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-16 17:17:00

It came down to the final stretch, but American Risk from Truro, N.S., pulled away to win what may be the last race at Saint John's Exhibition Raceway.Dozens turned out to witness what may be the end of an era in the city as the last harness races scheduled for the track kicked off Sunday afternoon.Courtney McKay has been coming to the track for years and considers harness racing her favourite sport.She said knowing that Sunday's card would likely be the track's last breaks her heart."This sport has been going on for so many years," McKay said.
