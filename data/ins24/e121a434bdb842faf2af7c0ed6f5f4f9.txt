MOUNT PLEASANT, S.C., May 27, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- A leading global recovery auditing firm is launching a revitalized brand to better communicate the company's broad range of services and enhanced business intelligence solutions. The new name is Illumis."The firm we have been building for the last twenty-eight years has evolved considerably, to a point where we have refreshed the company name." says Anthony Brush, President and CEO of Illumis. "Our new brand identity supports this evolution and ongoing journey to expand our offerings and solutions.
