PTIUnion Finance Minister Nirmala SitharamanIndia is aiming at developing standard operating procedures for cryptocurrency during its G20 presidency next year, Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman has said, underlining that all countries want the technology to survive but not be misutilised. "That (crypto) will also be part of India's thing (agenda during G-20 presidency)," Sitharaman told a group of Indian reporters on Saturday before concluding her trip to Washington DC to attend the annual meetings of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank.
