Kerala Engineering Students Develop Electric Car; Win Award At International Competition
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-18 10:55:37

Thiruvananthapuram: An electric car, designed by students of the Government Engineering College Barton Hill here, has bagged an award and an honourable mention at the international energy efficiency competition, Shell Eco-Marathon (SEM) 2022, held at Pertamina Mandalika circuit in Indonesia recently. 'Vandy,' the electric car designed by Pravega, a team of 19 students from the mechanical stream of the college, with the mentorship of Acsia Technologies emerged as the best from multiple entries across the world, a statement said here.
