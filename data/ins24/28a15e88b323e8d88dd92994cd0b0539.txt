WhatsApp in collaboration with Netflix has launched the Stranger Things sticker. Check the step-by-step process to download here. As Volume 1 of the sci-fi horror drama series 'Stranger Things 4' premiered today on video-streaming platform Netflix, WhatsApp has introduced a new customised sticker pack in collaboration with Netflix. The new Stranger Things WhatsApp stickers have been developed by an LA-based design studio- STATE Design. It is rolling out to all users on WhatsApp for Android, iOS and desktop as well.
