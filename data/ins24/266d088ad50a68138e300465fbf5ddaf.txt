Harold Weilbrenner, a Republican from Iowa City, will run against Democrat Janice Weiner of Iowa City for the open Iowa Senate seat that encompasses much of the city and all of University Heights.

Weilbrenner, 73, is a U.S. Army veteran and retired employee at Iowa Workforce Development who was nominated by a convention of Republicans in Johnson County to run for Iowa Senate District 45 this November.
