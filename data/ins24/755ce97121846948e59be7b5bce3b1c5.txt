Pushkar Sinha quits Atul Auto as VP Sales, Business Development
indiatimes.com

2022-06-03 14:15:00

Pushkar Sinha, Vice President (Sales, Business Development and Customer care), at Atul AutoNew Delhi: Pushkar Sinha, Vice President (Sales, Business Development and Customer care), at Atul Auto Limited (AAL) has recently resigned from his position, a source aware of the matter told ETAuto. ETAuto has learned that he stepped down from the position after a stint of almost a year and a half due to personal reasons. Sinha having over two decades of experience in the automobile industry started his career in Tata Motors in 1997 and worked there for over a decade from starting as a Graduate Engineer Trainee to Commercial Manager, East, looking after sales planning, RSO management, TBEM/ ISO process owner, CRM -Siebel Implementation for the entire East Sales.
