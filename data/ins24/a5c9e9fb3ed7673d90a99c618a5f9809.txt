The debate is worth having. Or has immaturity overtaken spirited debate in this country? Many were saying that the Coalition had run out of puff. Who could argue with that assessment when we saw, on the Sunday before the election, the so-called 'super-for-housing' policy – a policy which would increase house prices.This is the opposite of dealing with the housing affordability issue. The praise of this thought bubble by some in the media reeked of desperation, clearly upset that their relentless attacks on Anthony Albanese's character were not resonating.
