What qualifies you to hold this position? I grew up in Burnsville, graduated from Burnsville High School and am now raising my family in Burnsville. I have also been an active volunteer in Burnsville.

What would your top priorities be if elected? My top priorities if elected are largely focused on right-sizing our organization and making sure our staff has all the tools they need to succeed, and in turn provide better service to our community.
