Globe-trotting, Iowa-based photographer John Gaps III dies at 63
siouxcityjournal.com
news
2022-10-19 19:15:00

MARGERY A. BECK
Associated Press DES MOINES -- John Gaps III, an award-winning former Associated Press photographer who documented everything from war zones to the NCAA College World Series, was found dead at his home in Iowa, his family confirmed Tuesday. He was 63.Gaps was found by police Monday inside his home in Des Moines when his son, Ethan Gaps, requested a welfare check after not hearing from his father for several days. Ethan Gaps said the cause of his father's death was not immediately clear.
