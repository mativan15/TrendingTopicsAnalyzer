Wolterk/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Introduction: Target Corporation (NYSE:TGT) and Walmart Inc. (NYSE:WMT) are both retail giants with more than 12,000 stores between them. In the days since their earnings announcements, both stocks have gone down in price Target (-31%) significantly more than Walmart (-18%).
