Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday launched Rozgar Mela, which aims to recruit 10 lakh personnel for new jobs. To mark the beginning, on the very first day, 75,000 employees will be handed over the appointment letter, a release from the Prime minister‘s office stated.Jobs offeredThe new recruits, selected from all over India, will join 38 Ministries/Departments of the Government of India. Recruitment under ‘Rozgar Mela' will take place in almost all ministries, departments, PSUs, autonomous bodies, education and health Institutions, etc.
