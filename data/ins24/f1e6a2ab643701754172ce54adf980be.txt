PITTSBURGH, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PPG (NYSE:PPG) today announced that Tim Knavish, chief operating officer, has been named president and chief executive officer (CEO), effective Jan. 1, 2023. Knavish will join the company's Board of Directors, effective October 20, 2022.
