Toronto man, 29, arrested in connection to shooting at Mississauga nightclub HER
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-17 21:00:00

Peel police say they've arrested a 29-year-old Toronto man Sunday in relation to a June shooting at the HER Nightclub in Mississauga. (Christopher Mulligan/CBC)A Toronto man has been arrested in relation to a June shooting at a Mississauga nightclub that has been associated with a series of shootings throughout the past year. Peel police say in a news release that they were called to the club near Eglinton Avenue and Creditview Road on June 5 at about 3 a.m., for reports of a shooting that followed a "dispute" between multiple people.
