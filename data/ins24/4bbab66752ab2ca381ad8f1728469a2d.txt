Editor's Note: This is one of the 11 winners of this year's Deals of the Year Awards, honoring the most influential mergers and acquisitions for the region. We're rolling out one deal a day until the Oct. 26 event celebrating the deals. For details on the other winners, click here.

The deal: Global investment firm Blackstone Inc. completes majority investment in Atlanta-based womenswear brand Spanx Inc. in November 2021.

Purchase price: $1.2 billion

How we got here: Capped the remarkable journey for Sara Blakely, who founded the womenswear brand in 2000 with $5,000 in savings, eventually catapulting herself into an entrepreneurial powerhouse, being featured by Forbes magazine as the youngest self-made female billionaire.
