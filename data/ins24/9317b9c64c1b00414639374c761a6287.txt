By Reuters Published: 15:19 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 15:19 EDT, 18 October 2022 Tampa Bay Buccaneers defensive back Logan Ryan will undergo surgery to repair a Jones fracture in his foot but is expected to return late this season, NFL Network reported Tuesday. A Jones fracture is a break on the outside of the foot. Ryan will be placed on injured reserve. He's set for the surgery on Wednesday, per the report. He has missed the past two games with the injury. He suffered the injury in the Bucs' Week 4 loss to the Kansas City Chiefs.
