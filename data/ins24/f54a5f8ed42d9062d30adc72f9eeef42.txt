Good evening, let's start with today's top stories:B.C. to decriminalize possession of small amounts of ‘hard' drugsIn a Canadian first, British Columbia will decriminalize possession of 'hard' drugs such as illicit fentanyl, heroin, cocaine and methamphetamine, announced Carolyn Bennett, federal Minister of Mental Health and Addictions, alongside her B.C. counterpart, Sheila Malcolmson.Effective Jan. 31, 2023, British Columbians 18 and older will be able to carry up to a cumulative total of 2.5 grams of these illicit substances without the risk of arrest or criminal charges.
