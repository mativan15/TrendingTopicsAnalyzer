F.N.B. Hires Cassandra Cooper as Manager of Diversity and Inclusion
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 13:57:00

By Chris Wack F.N.B. Corp. and its First National Bank banking subsidiary have hired Cassandra Cooper as manager of diversity and inclusion. The company said Ms. Cooper will work closely with FNB's leadership team, Diversity Council and lines of business on the ongoing strategic development and execution of the company's internal and customer- and community-facing D&I efforts. Ms. Cooper will also serve as an adviser to ensure D&I is a key consideration in the development of bank products, services, initiatives and programs.
