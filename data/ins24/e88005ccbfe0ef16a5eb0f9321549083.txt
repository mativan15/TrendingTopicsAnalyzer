BABYLON, N.Y., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- For the residents of Patchogue and West Babylon, going to the dentist is still a friendly, personal experience, thanks to the efforts of Dr. Clifford Brown and the Babylon Dental Care team.

Dr. Brown started his practice nearly 40 years ago with two team members and a single office. Today the practice has grown to 85 members and two locations, bringing quality dental care to the people of the communities he loves. "Our mission is to treat patients like family," he said.
