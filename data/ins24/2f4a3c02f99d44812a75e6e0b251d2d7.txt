Deepak Nitrite shares slip 5% after fire breaks out at Vadodara plant
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 10:30:00

NEW DELHI: Shares of Deepak Nitrite on Friday took a beating, falling 5 per cent in trade after the company reported a fire incident at its Vadodara manufacturing site. In an update on stock exchanges, the company post market hours on Thursday said the fire broke out around the warehouse section of its manufacturing site located at Nandesari, District of Vadodara, Gujarat. The incident occurred at around 6 pm and the company's emergency response team, with the support of local authorities, brought the fire under control within a couple of hours.
