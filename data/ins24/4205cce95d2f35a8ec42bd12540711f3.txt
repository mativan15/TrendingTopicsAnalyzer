Honey Dew hold-up: String of shops robbed on National Doughnut Day
boston.com
news
2022-06-04 00:07:43

Multiple Massachusetts and New Hampshire doughnut shops were robbed on National Doughnut Day, and the culprit is still at large. Police are investigating several robberies that happened early Friday morning at doughnut shops and a liquor store along the Mammoth Road 128 corridor, according to Boston 25. The robbery streak started around 5 a.m. Friday, National Doughnut Day, at a liquor store in Dracut, according to NBC Boston. In that incident, the suspect allegedly smashed a glass door and took off with lottery tickets.
