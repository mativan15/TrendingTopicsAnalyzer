REUTERS/Hannah McKayRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comLONDON, June 1 (Reuters) - A central bank digital currency would not pose an excessive challenge for the Bank of England's operations, Andrew Hauser, the BoE's executive director for markets, said on Wednesday."By themselves, balance sheet considerations do not obviously present any 'redline' arguments against CBDC adoption, if that is the chosen way forward," Hauser said in remarks ahead of a discussion hosted by the Federal Reserve Bank of New York.
