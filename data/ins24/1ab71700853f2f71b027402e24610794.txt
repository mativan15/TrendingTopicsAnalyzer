Govt hosps have drugs in stock for 3 months: Min
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 08:15:00

Salem: Health minister Ma Subramanian on Sunday refuted the allegations of drug shortage in government hospitals and clarified that all the state health facilities had medicines in stock for three months.Earlier, opposition leader Edappadi K Palaniswami had accused the state government of creating an artificial scarcity of drugs in government hospitals and trying to close down the Tamil Nadu Medical Services Corporation ( TNMSC ).Subramanian, who inspected the pharmaceutical godown at the Salem Government Mohan Kumaramangalam Medical College and Hospital on the day, said the government didn't have any intention to shut down TNMSC.
