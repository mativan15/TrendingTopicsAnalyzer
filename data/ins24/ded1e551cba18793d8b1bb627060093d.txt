Manish Sisodia will soon be arrested in 'fake' case: Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 07:00:00

With inputs from PTI

NEW DELHI: Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal on Thursday alleged that just like health minister Satyendar Jain , deputy chief minister Manish Sisodia will soon be arrested in a 'fake' case.Jain was on Monday arrested by the Enforcement Directorate in a case related to money laundering."I had learnt from reliable sources a few months back that Satyendar Jain was going to be arrested in a fake case and now I have learnt from the same sources that Manish Sisodia is going to be arrested in the next few days in another fake case," Kejriwal said at a media briefing.
