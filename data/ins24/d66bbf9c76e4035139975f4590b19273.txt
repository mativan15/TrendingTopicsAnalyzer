Netflix NFLX finally unveiled its new ad-supported plan in 12 countries including the U.S. The new tier, called 'Basic with Ads' will be launched on November 3. The success of the new plan will be important for Netflix, as it needs to bolster subscriber additions and monetization in order to fund its massive content investments and shore up its stock price, which has declined by over 60% year to date.
