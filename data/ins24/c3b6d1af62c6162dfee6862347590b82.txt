DSW parent Designer Brands beats profit and net sales expectations, but misses on same-store sales
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 11:04:15

Designer Brands Inc. DBI, -0.06% reported Thursday fiscal first-quarte profit and net sales that rose above expectations, and gross margin that improved, while same-store sales came up short. The footwear and accessories retailer, and parent of the DSW Designer Shoe Warehouse chain of stores, said net income for the quarter to April 30 rose to $26.2 million, or 34 cents a share, from $17.0 million, or 22 cents a share, in the year-ago period. Excluding nonrecurring items, adjusted earnings per share of 48 cents was double the FactSet consensus of 24 cents.
