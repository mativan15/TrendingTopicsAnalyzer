AgenciesThe Nikkei share average ended 0.33% lower at 27,279.80, after swinging between small gains and losses.Japan's Nikkei index closed lower in see-saw trade on Tuesday, with technology stocks leading the losses amid caution over the global economic outlook. The Nikkei share average ended 0.33% lower at 27,279.80, after swinging between small gains and losses.

"Japanese market rose on Monday following U.S. market's rally late on Friday. But, investors were not aggressive enough to make further bets today," said Jun Morita, general manager of the research department at Chibagin Asset Management.
