October 18, 2022 06:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Rep. Kelly Armstrong (R-N.D.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.
