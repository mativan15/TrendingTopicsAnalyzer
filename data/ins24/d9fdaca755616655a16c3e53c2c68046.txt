Elon Musk's will-he-or-won't-he Twitter purchase may have descended into the Wall Street version of reality television, and it was recently eclipsed by the news that he'd reportedly paid a flight attendant $250,000 in hush money after she'd accused him of sexual misconduct, but his concerns about free speech on social media have sparked a valuable debate. Critics argue he's setting the stage for a troll free-for-all, while others are celebrating Musk for potentially ushering in a new golden age of free speech.
