Fashion is one of the world's largest consumer industries, estimated to be worth over $3 trillion by 2030. But with 4.7 million metric tons of CO2 emitted annually stemming from 17M+ tons of textile waste in the U.S. alone, the fashion industry is also the second largest global pollutant, according to retail tech experts Jessica Couch and Brittany Hicks. With the rise of e-commerce and the incorporation of social media usage, consumer influence is higher than ever before.
