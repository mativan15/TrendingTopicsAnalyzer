The Peripheral is the latest sci-fi drama to be gripping fans as they follow Chloë Grace Moretz's character descending into an online game that puts her family in grave danger.
The Amazon Prime series, which landed on Friday, see Flynne Fisher (Chloë) and her brother Burton (Jack Reynor) earning some extra cash as avatars to pay for their mother's medical bills.
During the challenging game, which is set in London, Flynne is tasked with breaking into a corporation known as the Research Institute to steal a valuable secret.
