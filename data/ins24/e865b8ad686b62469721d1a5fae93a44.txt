The new Yankees pitching forecast for decisive Game 5 after rainout fiasco
nypost.com
news
2022-10-18 11:40:00

Major League Baseball disregarded its paying customers Monday night in attempting to wait out the rain and play Game 5 of the AL Division Series, but the eventual postponement could help the Yankees' taxed pitching situation for Tuesday's decisive makeup.
Aaron Boone now can turn to All-Star lefty Nestor Cortes on three days' rest (in lieu of Monday's scheduled starter Jameson Taillon), and the Yankees' beat-up relief corps should be fresher as the team looks to book a trip to Houston to face the nemesis Astros in Game 1 of the ALCS the following night.
