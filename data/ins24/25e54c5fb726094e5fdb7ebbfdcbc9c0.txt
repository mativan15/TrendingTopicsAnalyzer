Asian Paints to invest over Rs 2,650 crore in manufacturing multiple key inputs
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 13:30:00

Asian Paints on Thursday announced investments in a greenfield vinyl acetate plant, white cement joint venture in the UAE and a nano-technology company for a cumulative investment of over Rs 2,650 crore. The investments will help the paints maker with backward integration, it said. The company will be investing Rs 2,100 to set up a manufacturing facility for VAE (Vinyl Acetate Ethylene Emulsion) and VAM (Vinyl Acetate Monomer) in India. The plant will have an installed capacity of 100,000 tons per annum for VAM and 150,000 tons per annum for VAE.
