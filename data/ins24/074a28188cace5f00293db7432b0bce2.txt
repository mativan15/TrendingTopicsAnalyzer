3 ways to recruit top tech talent in a tight labor market
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 10:00:00

The tech talent market is tighter than ever, as companies keep up with new technologies and IT infrastructure.

So, how do you land top talent? Here are some considerations that could give companies an edge in recruitment, according to Claire Stroh Petrie, director of technology talent management for Lighthouse Technology Services, a tech staffing company in Buffalo.

Remote work flexibility

'A lot of our clients here don't do fully remote,' Stroh Petrie said. 'That's OK, but if you're going to require five days on site, then do you offer parking or a stipend for that?'

When almost everyone worked remotely during the pandemic, many people made life decisions that require them to have more life/work balance and flexible time at home, like starting a family or adopting a pet.
