Winter weather predictions abound, but which should you trust?
sfgate.com
news
2022-10-19 17:26:01

Throughout history, people have sought signs of the winter to come, turning to the skies, the land and the water. Will it be mild or cold? Snowy, rainy or dry? People watch the behaviors of animals and plants, clouds, oceans, and in recent decades, computer models to drop hints about the winter to come. After all, knowledge is power, and any advance warning of severe winter conditions or mild ones can let people prepare in advance.

But who can you trust when it comes to winter look-aheads, and which ones are best left to the entertainment department? If you are making decisions based on winter outlook information, you might want to consider relying on tested methods from experts - not a farmer's almanac or caterpillar.
