Published: 15:16 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 15:16 EDT, 16 October 2022 Statuesque Gwendoline Christie towered over director Rian Johnson as she graced the red carpet the premiere of his latest movie Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery in London on Sunday.The Game of Thrones actress, 43, commanded attention in a voluptuous beige gown on the closing night of the city's annual film festival. Gwendoline appeared in her element as she joked around with the filmmaker, 48, who directed her in 2017's The Last Jedi, before heading inside to enjoy the movie.
