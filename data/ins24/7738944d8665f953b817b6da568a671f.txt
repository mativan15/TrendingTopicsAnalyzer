Britain's Failed Tax Experiment Will Leave Economic Scars and Credibility Issues, Analysts Say
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-18 07:48:15

On Monday, Finance Minister Jeremy Hunt threw out almost all of the package of tax cuts that was announced by his predecessor less than a month ago. Markets cheered the news, but analysts said the market chaos sparked by the plan had dented the U.K.'s credibility and raised questions over the predictability of fiscal policy. Gloom also still surrounds the gilt market and pound, while the public faces high inflation and potential departmental spending cuts.
