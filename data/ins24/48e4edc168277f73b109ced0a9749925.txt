Farmers are going to extraordinary lengths to get through the flooding that is devastating large parts of the country.Dave and Yvonne Poole have walked their dairy cows 20km to get clear of approaching floodwaterTheir property was extensively flooded in 2011. They are now preparing for the same againThey expect to be cut off from each other for some timeAmong them is Dave and Yvonne Poole whose dairy cows have completed a 20-kilometre walk to a friend's property to ensure they are clear of the water.
