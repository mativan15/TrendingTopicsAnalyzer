Tesco customers have just hours left to use Clubcard vouchers before they expire

TESCO shoppers have until tomorrow to use up £17million worth of Clubcard vouchers.
Coupons first issued in May 2020 need to be spent or will be invalid.
Use your vouchers by tomorrow or risk losing themCredit: Getty
Tesco customers who are signed up to the Clubcard scheme get given vouchers to spend four times a year, but they expire after two years.
There's a whopping £17million worth of vouchers that need to be spent by May 31.
Meaning now is the perfect time to grab a bargain in store or online using the vouchers.
