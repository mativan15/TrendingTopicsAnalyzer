ATLANTA, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Verusen , the global supply chain intelligence innovator, today announced it has expanded its leadership team with the appointment of Ross Sonnabend as Senior Vice President, Head of Product.



Reporting directly to Paul Noble, Verusen Founder and CEO, Ross will direct the Verusen Product team on product design and innovation, strategy, and overall product vision.
