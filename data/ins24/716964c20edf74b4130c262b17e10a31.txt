Getty Images/iStockphotoOnline associate degrees provide learners with a strong academic foundation on which to build. These typically two-year degrees include general education and your chosen major's fundamentals. Associate programs can also be more affordable and easier to access than bachelor's programs. Many schools offer open admission policies. Opting for an online associate degree adds another layer of convenience. You'll enjoy more flexibility than on-campus programs offer. Online learning features fewer travel requirements and more scheduling options.
