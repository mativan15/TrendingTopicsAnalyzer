Let's talk about some incendiary topics: politics, religion and electric cars.

Politics and religion don't have much place in an automotive review column, but everyone's interested in the state of the electric car industry, and it seems everyone has an opinion about electric cars.

Still, politics does play a part in an electric car discussion. Earlier this year, President Biden's Infrastructure Law earmarked $5 billion to build electric vehicle (EV) charging stations across the country over the next five years.
