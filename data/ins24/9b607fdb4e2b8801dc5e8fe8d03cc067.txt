Southeast Ohio food pantries feeling the pinch of inflation
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-05 09:15:01

Rocky Black and Gina Bichard unload food from the Mid-Ohio Food Bank at the Cambridge Salvation Army.

The Mid-Ohio Food Collective, a food bank that sells directly to pantries across 20 central Ohio counties, is reporting an uptick in need.

And there's a new challenge for the food pantries. Inflation is causing grocery prices to rise to new levels, just as working people are getting back on their feet following two years of economic instability.

There are also far fewer options for those seeking the pantries' services.
