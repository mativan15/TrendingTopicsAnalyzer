AgenciesRepresentative ImageA woman Naxalite, carrying a reward of Rs five lakh on her head and wanted in several cases of attack on police, has surrendered in Chhattisgarh's Bijapur district to reunite with her daughter back home, an official said on Sunday. The rebel, identified as Somli Sodi alias Vanita (32), was working with the banned organisation from 2003 and was the commander of its Nagaram local organisation squad since 2018. "She surrendered before police on Saturday for the sake of her daughter and family," Bijapur Superintendent of Police Anjneya Varshney said.
