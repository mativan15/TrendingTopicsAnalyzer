NEW ORLEANS, June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Former Attorney General of Louisiana, Charles C. Foti, Jr., Esq., a partner at the law firm of Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ("KSF"), announces that KSF has commenced an investigation into Lightning eMotors, Inc. (NYSE: ZEV).
On August 16, 2021, the Company announced its financial results for 2Q2021, including a $46.1 million net loss, or 79 cents per share, for the quarter, up more than 1,500% from the $2.8 million loss, or 10 cents per share, that it reported in the second quarter of 2020.
