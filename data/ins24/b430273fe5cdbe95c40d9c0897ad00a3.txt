Continuous Government Spending and Rising Urbanization to Act as Key Drivers for Architectural Services Market, Says Fact.MR

United States, Rockville MD, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global architectural services market is worth US$ 302.2 billion in 2022 and is predicted to expand at a CAGR of 3.5% during the forecast years of 2022-2032. The market is expected to grow due to increased government spending on infrastructure and the rising urban population across the world. Rising urban population has put the real estate industry under pressure to come up with a sustainable solution to the rising demand for residential buildings.
