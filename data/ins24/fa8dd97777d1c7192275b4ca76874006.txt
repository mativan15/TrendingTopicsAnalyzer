Ford government wants to double fines for developers that rip off Ontario homebuyers
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-20 17:30:00

The Ontario government wants to boost fines for developers who are found to have unjustifiably cancelled contracts or asked for additional money. (Jane Robertson/CBC)The Ford government says it wants to double the maximum fine for developers of new homes who unjustifiably cancel building projects, terminate purchase agreements or demand more money from buyers.Minister of Public and Business Service Delivery Kaleed Rasheed and Minister of Municipal Affairs and Housing Steve Clark made the announcement in Windsor, Ont.
