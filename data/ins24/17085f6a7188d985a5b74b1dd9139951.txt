Raids unearth expired medicines, shortage of IV fluids in PHCs
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-18 18:06:36

Sleuths of Vigilance and Enforcement (V&E) conducted raids on Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and Community Health Centres (CHCs) in the State on Tuesday and reportedly found many irregularities at the health centres.In some PHCs, there were allegedly no medicines which were shown in the stock register, and in some centres, medicines that were past their expiry date (outdated) were found in the drug stores, said Vigilance and Enforcement Director General Shankha Brata Bagchi.Ambulances were in damaged condition, and variations were noticed between the stock register and the availability of medicines in the hospitals.
