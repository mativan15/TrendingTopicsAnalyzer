We are implementing a new IT solution for calculation of Indian embedded value and we need to cross-check all the data," he told reportersState-owned Life Insurance Corporation of India () on Tuesday said the exercise of determining its embedded value as on March 2022 is a "work in progress" and is likely to be completed by the end of next month. The Embedded Value (EV) is a measure of the consolidated value of shareholders' interest in the life insurance business.
