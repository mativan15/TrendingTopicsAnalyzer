Sidus Space Executes Launch Agreement with Vaya Space

CAPE CANAVERAL, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Oct 20, 2022--Sidus Space, Inc. (NASDAQ:SIDU), a Space-as-a-Service company, focused on mission critical hardware manufacturing combined with commercial satellite design, manufacture, launch, and data collection, today announced that it has signed a launch agreement with Vaya Space for four launches over multiple years. Vaya Space is an emerging leader in sustainable space access and this agreement expands Sidus Space's ability to deliver satellite services.
