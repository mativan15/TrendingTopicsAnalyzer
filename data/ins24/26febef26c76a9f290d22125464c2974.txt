iStockThe rate tightening that has already been implemented would be adequate to bring inflation down to around 5%, Varma said.RBI should put a pause to rate hikes despite high inflation levels to avoid hurting recovery in growth, MPC member Jayanth Varma said. "There is no denying that inflation is unacceptably high but the thing is that we have given a strong dose of medicine and the time has come to wait and see if that medicine works or if we need something even more potent," according to Varma.
