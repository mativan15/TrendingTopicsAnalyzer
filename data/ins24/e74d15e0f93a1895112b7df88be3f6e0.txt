Published: 00:44 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 00:45 EDT, 18 October 2022 A mum of eight has faced a magistrate after she left five of her children home alone for 12 hours while she went out to a nightclub. The 34-year-old woman fronted Townsville Magistrates' Court on Monday after she tried to organise a babysitter for the night last February.The 17-year-old 'sitter never showed up but the mum went out anyway, leaving the kids - including a nine-month-old baby - to fend for themselves. The alarm was sounded after a neighbour found a toddler on her own outside in the morning.
