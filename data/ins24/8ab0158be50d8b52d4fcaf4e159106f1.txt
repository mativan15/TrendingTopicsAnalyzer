There's a national Adderall shortage, and some parents are worried
today.com
news
2022-10-17 22:17:54

A nationwide shortage of Adderall is making it harder for some parents to help their children manage the symptoms of attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD).Kandi and Lion Fludd had no reason to believe they'd have issues filling their 8-year-old son's Adderall prescription when running errands in their hometown of Las Vegas, Nevada two weeks ago. "We had no notice," Kandi, 44, told TODAY Parents. "When we went to the pharmacy, they told us it was nationwide, they didn't know when they'd get (another shipment) and that we needed to call our doctor's office.
