DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Oct 20, 2022--Apex Fintech Solutions ('Apex'), the fintech for fintechs powering innovation and the future of digital wealth management, today released its Third Quarter 2022 Apex Next Investor Outlook ('Q3 ANIO Report' or the 'report'). The report, which analyzes proprietary data of U.S.-based investors who trade through introducing brokers on the Apex Clearing platform as of September 30, 2022, sheds light on the top 100 stocks [1] held by investors across four generations, with a special focus on the millennial and rising Gen Z [2] demographics.
