Jeff Nelson Joins Company as Divisional Manager for East Coast

CHARLOTTE, N.C., June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Leading full-service mortgage lender Nations Lending is continuing to expand its sales leadership team by adding Jeff Nelson as a Divisional Sales Manager. Nelson will be directly responsible for building company sales and supporting various branches' growth across the East Coast.

This press release features multimedia. View the full release here: https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220602005092/en/

Jeff Nelson (Photo: Business Wire)

Nelson comes after serving nearly five years as a Divisional Manager at Guaranteed Rate and previously serving 17 years at Wells Fargo in various roles.
