(2nd LD) Seoul shares sharply rebound on U.S. gains, hope for China stimulus(ATTN: ADDS weekly index changes in 3rd to last para) SEOUL, May 27 (Yonhap) -- The South Korean stock market sharply rebounded Friday as investor sentiment was boosted by upbeat earnings forecast from major U.S. retailers and overnight gains on Wall Street, as well as China's pledge to take measures to spur growth. The local currency steeply rose against the U.S. dollar. The Korea Composite Stock Price Index (KOSPI) added 25.
