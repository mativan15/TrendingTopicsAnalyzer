India fast bowler Mohammed Shami bowled the final over of India's practice match against Australia in Brisbane on Monday.
Mohammed Shami defended 10 off the last over as India beat Australia by six runs in the practice match against Australia in Brisbane on Monday.Shami, who bowled just one over in the game - the 20th over - took three wickets off the last four balls, with one being a run-out, as Australia folded for 180.
