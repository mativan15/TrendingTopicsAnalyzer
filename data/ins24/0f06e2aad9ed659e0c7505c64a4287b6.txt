January 6 Committee Issues Subpoena for Trump Testimony, Documents
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 18:10:45

The House select committee investigating the January 6 U.S. Capitol riot issued a subpoena on Friday to Donald Trump, asking that the former president testify before the committee and provide records related to his actions during the riot.

The subpoena comes one week after the committee voted unanimously to subpoena Trump and requires the former president to turn over documents by November 4 and to appear for a deposition on November 14.
