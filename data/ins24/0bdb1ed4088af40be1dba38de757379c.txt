Uber India hitches a ride on strong air travel resurgence

ETtechUber India has recently tied-up with Adani Airports at Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Guwahati, Lucknow and Jaipur for airport operations (Illustration: Rahul Awasthi)Strong resurgence in air travel in Indian market is rubbing off on ride-hailing services as well, which were hit hard the past two years due to the pandemic. With the daily number of air passengers nearing pre-Covid levels, Uber India has seen increased traction in airport operations last quarter. The number of airport trips are set to rise further as the festive season gathers steam, spurring the ride-hailing firm to work on expanding its footprint in both geographical reach and services.
