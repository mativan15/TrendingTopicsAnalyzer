Waterfield Advisors appoints Nakul Beri as senior managing director
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 08:15:00

Getty Images(Representative image)Multi-family office and wealth advisory firm Waterfield Advisors on Tuesday announced the appointment of Nakul Beri as senior managing director, global client origination and coverage. In this role, Nakul will lead and build the crucial mandate of the firm's global wealth segment. His vast experience in onshore and offshore wealth management, deep promoter relationships, and leadership prowess will be valuable fillip for Waterfield's strategic expansion plans. Nakul brings in over 26 years of substantive experience across the wealth management spectrum.
