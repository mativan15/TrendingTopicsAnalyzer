June 01, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time SANTA ANA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Iteris, Inc. (NASDAQ: ITI), the world's trusted technology ecosystem for smart mobility infrastructure management, today reported financial results for its fiscal fourth quarter and full year ended March 31, 2022. During the fiscal first quarter of 2021, the company completed the sale of its Agriculture and Weather Analytics segment to DTN, LLC. The results of the Agriculture and Weather Analytics segment are reported as discontinued operations for all periods presented in this release.
