The Mike Taylor ShowOct 17, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesThe Eagles remain the only undefeated team in the NFL after a 26-17 win over the rival Cowboys on Sunday Night Football in Philadelphia. Jalen Hurts threw for 155 yards and two touchdowns as the Eagles moved two games ahead of Dallas in the NFC East. The Giants are second at 5-and-1. Miles Sanders ran for 71 yards and a score in the victory. Cooper Rush finished just 18-of-38 for 181 yards, one TD, and three interceptions for Dallas, which dropped to 4-and-2.
