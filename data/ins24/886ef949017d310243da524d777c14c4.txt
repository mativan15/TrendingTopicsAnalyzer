DENVER , May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Today, Homebot, an award-winning, client engagement and retention portal that empowers consumers to build wealth through homeownership, announced it has integrated with Surefire, the industry-leading customer relationship management (CRM) and mortgage marketing automation software from Black Knight, a premier provider of software, data and analytics that drives innovation in the mortgage lending, servicing and real estate industries, as well as the capital and secondary markets.
