Arsenal Reach Europa League Knockouts Victory Over PSV Eindhoven
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-21 03:22:56

Granit Xhaka's second-half winner secured Arsenal a 1-0 victory over PSV Eindhoven at the Emirates Stadium and a place in the Europa League knockout phase on Thursday. Mikel Arteta's men moved five points clear of PSV at the top of Group A in a rescheduled game which was postponed last month following the death of Queen Elizabeth II.
