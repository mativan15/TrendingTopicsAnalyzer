We are told that the Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) has been spending not lakhs but crores of rupees every year towards procuring personal equipment, bleaching powder, sweeping equipment, gang work tools and such, to create a conducive working atmosphere for the pourakarmikas. The average annual budget for procurement expenditure amounts to roughly Rs 15 crore. In the latest of such steps, BBMP executive engineer for solid waste management, Dasappa Hospital Building, has issued a tender inviting bidders to supply personal protection equipment at an estimated cost of Rs 423.
