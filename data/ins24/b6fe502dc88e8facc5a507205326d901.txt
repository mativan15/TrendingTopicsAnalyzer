REUTERS/Andrew KellyRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comJune 2 (Reuters) - Russia's National Settlement Depository will service the country's Eurobonds after Citibank stopped acting as a fiscal, transfer and payment agent, the finance ministry said on Thursday.The finance ministry also said that a delay in payments on its Russia-2022 Eurobond that were due on April 4 was caused by "unlawful actions" of foreign financial intermediaries.
