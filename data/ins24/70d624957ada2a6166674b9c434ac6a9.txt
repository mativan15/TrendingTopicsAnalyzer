 Salem Aavin has set a sales target of ₹3.12 crore for the Deepavali festival and aimed to sell 69 tonnes of sweets this year.The Salem District Cooperative Milk Producers Federation Limited (Aavin) was formed in 1978. Per day, the Salem Aavin procures 6.03 lakh litres of milk from 49,000 milk producers through 791 milk producers' cooperative societies.Salem Aavin produces 42 milk byproducts, including butter, ghee, Khoa, chocolate, and ice cream, which is the highest by an Aavin company in the State.
