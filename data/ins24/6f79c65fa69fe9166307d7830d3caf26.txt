Realty boom at Kanakapura Road
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-28 00:30:00

By Prakash VL


Demand for sites in Kaggalipura | Customers are benefitting from the affordable prices Kanakapura Road is one of the hotspots in the city for site buyers as is not only known for sites of different sizes, but also for budget sites. The development activities on the Kanakapura Road has been a boost to the demand for real estate here. The multi-storied buildings on this road attract buyers. Realty experts said that the presence of Jain College and Dayanand Sagar Institutions is an added advantage for this road.
