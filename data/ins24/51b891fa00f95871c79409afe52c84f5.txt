8,300 kilos of banned firecrackers seized from warehouse near Gurugram
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 17:47:02

Officials from the Chief Minister's flying squad seized 8,343 kilograms of traditional firecrackers from a warehouse in Dabodha village in Farrukhnagar, in what is described by them as the largest consignment of banned crackers ever to be seized in HaryanaPolice officers privy to the details said on Saturday that the warehouse, located nearly 42 kilometres from Gurugram, was first raided on October 12, and over three days, officials from the flying squad and the local police seized the consignment of crackers that was stashed in five separate rooms at the facility.
