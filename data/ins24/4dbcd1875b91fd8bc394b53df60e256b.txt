A retired deputy director of security at the Lok Sabha was allegedly duped for crores (Representational)Gurugram: A retired deputy director of security at the Lok Sabha was allegedly duped for crores on the pretext of investments in mutual funds and shares, police said.BL Ahuja, 83, a resident of Gurugram's Sector 43, retired as deputy director (security) at the Lok Sabha in November 2000.According to Press Trust of India, he was allegedly cheated by an Axis Bank employee, identified as Abhishek Maheshwari, and his wife.
