Drunkard kidnaps sister, demands ransom; arrested
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 13:31:11

Mumbai: Unable to get a job and in need of money to fuel his alcoholism, a 25-year-old man allegedly kidnapped his five-year-old cousin sister and demanded ransom from her family in Palghar on Wednesday. The police, however, traced and apprehended the accused within two hours of receiving the complaint, and rescued the girl.According to the police, the victim stays with her parents in Vishnu Nagar in Palghar and the accused, Pritam Kamble, is her cousin. The police said that he had come to Palghar in search of a job a month ago and was staying with the victim's family in their residence since then.
