Avian flu detected on 17 Manitoba farms
ctvnews.ca
science
2022-10-19 14:23:34

The highly-infectious avian influenza, also known as bird flu, has been detected in farms across Canada, including several in Manitoba. According to the Canadian Food Inspection Agency, bird flu has been detected on 17 farms in Manitoba over the last few months, most of which are commercial poultry operations. These farms are located in the RM of Bifrost-Riverton, the RM of Ste. Anne, the RM of De Salaberry, the RM of Rockwood, as well as a few other communities. The government agency reports that 289,000 birds in Manitoba have been impacted by the virus.
