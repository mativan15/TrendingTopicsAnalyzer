Here are five things in technology that happened this past week and how they affect your business. Did you miss them? 1 – Apple is reporting significant MacBook shortages. Thanks to supply chain issues, war in Ukraine and lockdowns in China, Apple is reporting shortages across their distribution chain. Only three MacBooks are immediately available in the U.S. at this time - the 13-in. MacBook Pro, Mac mini and iMac 24 - and they're all older models.
