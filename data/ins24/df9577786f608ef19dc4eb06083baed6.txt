Published: 15:56 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 16:31 EDT, 3 June 2022 Homes and businesses have been left damaged after more than 9cm of rain fell on a Welsh seaside village in just six hours. Criccieth bore the brunt of the 92mm downpour as thunderstorms hit north west Wales on Friday, while many were celebrating the Platinum Jubilee.
