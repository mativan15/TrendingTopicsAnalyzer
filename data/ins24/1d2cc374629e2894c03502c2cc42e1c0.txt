Her last film, Dasvi, saw Nimrat Kaur playing a character that was different from her other outings. That's something the actress loves doing. She says, 'I love roles where I have the opportunity to colour outside the lines. I have been brought up in a certain way and to play something opposite that is scary yet thrilling and challenging at the same time. I thoroughly enjoyed my role in Dasvi.' In a chat with us, the actress speaks about her next project, the changes in the film industry and why she loves being in Pune.
