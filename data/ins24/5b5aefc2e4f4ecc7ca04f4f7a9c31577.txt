Almost every company in America has been complaining about inflationary pressures, particularly supply chain and shipping costs. But what if a company is part of the supply chain?

FedEx Corporation (NYSE:FDX) is one such company. It provides transportation, e-commerce and business services on a global basis. The FedEx Express segment offers express transportation, small-package ground delivery, freight transportation services, time-critical transportation services and cross-border e-commerce related solutions.
