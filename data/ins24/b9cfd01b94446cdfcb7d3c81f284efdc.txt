REUTERS/Kim Hong-Ji/File PhotoHyundai Motor India on Friday said it has commenced bookings for the new version of its compact SUV Venue which is scheduled to hit the market later this month. Customers can book the new model at the company's dealerships by paying Rs 21,000. It can also be booked online. "Venue has had a stellar success in India ever since its launch in 2019. Customers from across the country have been thrilled by its futuristic design, advanced technology and powerful performance.
