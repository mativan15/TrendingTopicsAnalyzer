TORONTO, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Loopio and nFold are proud to announce the launch of their strategic partnership. Loopio is the leader in Request for Proposal (RFP) response software and nFold is a leading proposal consultancy in South Africa. The partnership allows Loopio to expand into South Africa and nFold to offer clients industry-leading technology that improves their RFP and proposal process.
