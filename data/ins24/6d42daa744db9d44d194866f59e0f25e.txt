Variety - TV News Cannes — The four-part drama 'Litvinenko,' starring Scottish actor David Tennant, has sold to more than 80 international markets, ahead of its debut on the new ITV streaming service, Itvx in the U.K., and on Nent Group's Viaplay streaming service in the Nordics, Netherlands, Poland and the Baltics.AMC Networks' Sundance Now and AMC+ have acquired the exclusive U.S. rights to the drama, and Prime Video has taken the series in Canada.In Europe, 'Litvinenko' will air on M6 in France, ProSiebenSat.
