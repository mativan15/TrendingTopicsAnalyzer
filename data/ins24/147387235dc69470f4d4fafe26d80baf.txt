October 20, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time CUPERTINO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Intero, a Berkshire Hathaway affiliate and wholly owned subsidiary of HomeServices of America, Inc., is proud to celebrate 20 years of helping people achieve their real estate dreams.



Intero, which means 'whole and complete' in Italian, was founded on delivering an innovative and unique experience for real estate agents, homebuyers, sellers, and property investors. Intero opened the doors of its first office in 2002 when founding members Gino Blefari, Kevin Moles, Tom Tognoli, John Thompson, Jerry Kiss, Terry Meyer and Brian Crane came together to combine their experiences under a single vision for an 'ideal' brokerage.
