Patriots yet to declare QB Mac Jones out vs. Browns
espn.com
news
2022-10-15 17:44:00

1:44 PM ETCLEVELAND -- The New England Patriots are keeping hopes alive that injured quarterback Mac Jones could play in Sunday's game against the Cleveland Browns.The team declared Saturday afternoon that two injured players would be out for the game -- starting cornerback Jonathan Jones and No. 3 receiver Nelson Agholor -- but it was notable that Jones wasn't part of the club's announcement.NFL teams are required, by league rule, to declare injured players who don't travel for road games as out.
