David Boyle is head of sales and marketing at Mint Asset Management Limited. OPINION: When I was at school, woodwork was not my forte. What made things worse, my father was a highly skilled cabinetmaker and carpenter. He spent many hours reviewing my work and offering help to improve my future projects. One of his biggest bugbears was the gaps I left in my joins. He would say: 'If a job is worth doing, it's worth doing right.' In other words, there are no shortcuts if you want to have a great outcome.
