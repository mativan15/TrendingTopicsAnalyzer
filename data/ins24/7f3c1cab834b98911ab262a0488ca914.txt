 Winnipeg police say two people were rushed to hospital after a crash Tuesday in Old St. Vital.
The single-car crash happened just before 9 p.m. at St. Mary's Road and Vivian Avenue.A mangled black vehicle could be seen surrounded by evidence markers.There is a serious MVC on N/B St. Mary's Road at Vivian.
