Imelda Staunton addresses The Crown accuracy amid backlash
express.co.uk
entertainment
2022-10-19 12:01:59

Season five of The Crown is set to drop on Netflix on November 9 and has had fans eagerly waiting to see what happens in the next instalment. However, the arrival of the series has been met with some backlash from the Royal Family and people associated with them over claims some of the events didn't take place. Harry Potter actress Imelda Staunton who plays The Queen in the upcoming series has opened up about the inspiration for the series and the scrutiny it has been facing, insisting that the "turmoil" is real.
