Two Colorado prep volleyball players selected to U19 U.S. national training team
denverpost.com
news
2022-06-02 22:04:23

Rampart High's Izzy Starck and Brighton High's Andi Jackson were among a group of 20 players selected this week to join USA Volleyball's Girls U19 National Training Team.
Starck is coming off an all-state sophomore year as a setter/outside hitter that saw her total 410 assists, 392 kills, 85 aces and 50 blocks for the Rams. Jackson, a 6-foot-1 middle blocker, is committed to play for Nebraska. She amassed 143 kills and 64 blocks as a junior in the fall of 2021.
The training team will practice July 6-15 in Tulsa, Okla.
