Something wicked this way comes! 
'American Horror Story,' created by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk for the FX cable network, has been bringing the screams since the haunting horror anthology television series debuted in 2011. 
With the release of a new theme every year, featuring major actors like Lady Gaga, Sarah Paulson, Evan Peters, Jessica Lange, Kathy Bates, Angela Bassett, and more, fans are always curious about what scares lie ahead. So far, the franchise has ventured into all sorts of terror genres, ranging from demons to witches to ghosts and more!
To understand the 'American Horror Story' franchise, the New York Post spoke exclusively with two of the rising voices in American geekdom: Paul Florez-Taylor—known as the Comic King—and Luke James—known as Pop Media Maven.
