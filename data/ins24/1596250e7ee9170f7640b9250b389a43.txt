NDIS: Trio accused of defrauding scheme of $2 million
smh.com.au
news
2022-06-01 14:01:00

Two women and a man allegedly linked to Middle Eastern organised crime and posing as disability support providers have been charged with defrauding the NDIS of more than $2 million.The NDIS Fraud Taskforce raided four properties in western Sydney on Wednesday morning, arresting Wafaa Al Shamari, 32, at Liverpool, Muna El Shreffy, 37, at Bonnyrigg and Adel Al-Shamare, 27, at Wentworthville.The AFP arrested two women and a man they allege are connected to Middle Eastern organised crime. Credit:Alex EllinghausenThe taskforce, which is a partnership between the National Disability Insurance Agency (NDIA), the Australian Federal Police and Services Australia, alleges the trio were operating as disability support providers and were fraudulently claiming payments for services not actually received.
