SINGAPORE, June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Coins.ph (the "Company"), the Philippines' leading fully-regulated fiat and crypto wallet services provider, today announced a partnership with the Philippine Basketball Association ("PBA") that makes Coins.ph the official crypto partner for the PBA's 47th season.

The deal marks the first crypto partnership for the PBA. Coins.ph will have brand presence featured throughout the Philippine Cup, the PBA's premier tournament, where it will offer interactive experiences to engage fans and promote awareness of all things crypto.
