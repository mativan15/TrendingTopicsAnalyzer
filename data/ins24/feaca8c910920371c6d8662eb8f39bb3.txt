Jun. 5—As this year's graduates look forward, we also reflect on some noteworthy events that occurred during their lives. Most of this year's grads were born in 2003 and 2004.

2003

Ohio State upsets the University of Miami in double-overtime to win the national championship in college football. The Space Shuttle Columbia explodes on re-entry, killing seven crew members. The U.S. invades Iraq and deposes Saddam Hussein. Bodybuilder/actor Arnold Schwarzenegger is elected governor of California.

2004

The final episode of "Friends" airs.
