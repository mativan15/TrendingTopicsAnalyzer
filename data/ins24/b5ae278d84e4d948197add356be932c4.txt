Inflation hits home: 34 fixed-term home loans increase
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-21 16:00:00

Thirty-four home loan rates were increased in the past week, data from Canstar shows, as banks respond to the prospect of inflation that is higher for longer than previously expected. The data shows that four providers increased five variable rates by an average of 50 basis points. They increased 34 fixed rates by an average of 26 basis points. Two banks increased two savings account interest rates by an average of 45 basis points, and one lifted its bonus account rate by 40 basis points. Six term deposit providers increased 40 interest rates by an average of 21 basis points.
