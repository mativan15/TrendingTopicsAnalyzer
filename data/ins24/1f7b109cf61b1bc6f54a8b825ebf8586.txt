SA opposition concerned by hospital project delays, nurses union warns budget does not go far enough
abc.net.au
news
2022-06-03 04:02:51

The state opposition has raised its concern over the future of the new Women's and Children's Hospital project, with budget papers revealing the timeline for its completion has been pushed back at least a year.Thursday's SA state budget included more funding for hospital beds and nurses The nurses' union says more nurses will be needed to work on the bedsTwo hospital projects appear to have been delayedThe hospital is now listed for 'estimated completion' in the September quarter of 2027.Previous budgets had the date listed as June 2026.
