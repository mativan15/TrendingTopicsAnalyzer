AsiaVision Investment Thesis I came across IDT Corporation (NYSE:IDT) and thought it was an intriguing company. The company has a low-margin, mature, and declining traditional communication business that is masking the high-growth, high-margin businesses, which are primarily (1) the National Retail Solutions (NRS), its cloud-based POS company, and (2) net2phone, its cloud-based communication business. As IDT has a few business segments, I aimed to cover only 2 businesses in this article - NRS and net2phone.
