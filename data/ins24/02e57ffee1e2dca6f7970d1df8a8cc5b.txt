Billionaire Mukesh Ambani's Reliance Industries Ltd on Friday reported a flattish net profit for the September quarter after a newly introduced windfall profit tax dented mainstay oil earnings.The oil-to-retail-to-telecom conglomerate's consolidated profit of ₹13,656 crore during July-September - the second quarter of the 2022-23 fiscal year - compared to ₹13,680 crore in the year-ago period, it said in a stock exchange filing.
