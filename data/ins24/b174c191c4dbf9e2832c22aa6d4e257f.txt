AgenciesUP's Yogi Adityanath government has set a target of attracting '10 lakh crore of private investment in the state over the next five years, and its decision is expected to make it easier for factories looking to employ women on night shifts.The Uttar Pradesh government has allowed factories to employ women on night shifts between 7 pm and 6 am, if the employers fulfil certain conditions to ensure the safety and security of the female workers and make certain provisions at the workplace. Officials said this was done by relaxing a rule under the Factories Act, 1948 to promote ease of doing business in the state as well as to provide equal opportunity of work to women.
