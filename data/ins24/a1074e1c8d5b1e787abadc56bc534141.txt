Published: 08:25 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 08:30 EDT, 21 October 2022 Taylor Swift sent fans wild on Friday as she released the music video for her new single, Anti-Hero, after releasing her tenth studio album Midnights just hours before. The third track on the album sees Taylor admit she's never delved 'this far into my insecurities', adding, 'It's me, I'm the problem' in the deep-thinking song.In the five-minute long video, the 32-year-old singer doesn't hold back exploring the themes of 'self-hatred', depicting herself as two separate entities, one who she sees as her own worst enemy and one that is too afraid to let loose and have fun.
