As the days grow shorter and we head into winter, could the crypto winter be thawing? Judging by the latest price moves for many cryptocurrencies, at least some investors have such a hope.
On Monday, optimism about this and an upcoming quarterly-earnings release boosted the share price of crypto-leaning bank Silvergate Capital (SI 5.39%). The stock rose by over 5% on the day, eclipsing even the frothy 2.7% gain of the S&P 500 index.
