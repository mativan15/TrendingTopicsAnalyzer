By George HerdBBC NewsMedia caption, Music Venue Trust wants to buy live music venues across Britain to keep them open.A bid to buy grassroots music venues will help protect the Welsh music scene for generations, those backing the plan have said.Among them are The Bunkhouse in Swansea and Newport's Le Pub.Project leaders said they wanted to become a "National Trust" but buying music venues instead of stately homes.MVT warned that more than a third of grassroots music venues in the UK have closed in the past 20 years.
