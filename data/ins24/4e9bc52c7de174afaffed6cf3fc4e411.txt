India continues to amplify struggles outside Europe but is the West listening?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 18:30:00

No joint communique came at the end of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) annual meetings because Russia blocked it. China didn't even bother to attend in person, and has refused to discuss debt relief for countries hit hardest by the spate of economic crises. The US and Europe, while finding endless streams of money for Ukraine, offered words of support to vulnerable countries, but not much else. 'The war is the single-most important element slowing down growth and generating inflation, volatility, energy and food insecurity and uncertainty,' Nadia Calvino, Spain's economy minister, said as the chair of IMF's financial committee.
