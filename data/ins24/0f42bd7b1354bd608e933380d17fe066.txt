Bob HauerOct 20, 2022Believe it or not, it's almost the holiday season, which means plenty of folks are making their travel plans, if they haven't done so already. Well, if you're one of those people who still need to book your visit home for the holidays, Expedia is offering up some travel hacks that may help save you some dough and avoid delays. Hacks include: Book Airfare on Sunday – The site reports that booking flights on a Sunday compared to a Friday, will save travelers 5% on domestic flights and 15% on International travel.
