Binance.US vs. Binance [2022]: Are They the Same Exchange?
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-05 14:40:46

One of the easiest ways to buy, sell, and hold cryptocurrencies is to use an exchange. You can quickly and easily buy crypto with an exchange, particularly if you choose an exchange that lets you buy crypto using a fiat currency like the U.S. dollar.

Binance is the biggest cryptocurrency exchange in the world by daily volume. However, Binance isn't available in the United States. Instead, crypto investors based in the U.S. who want to use Binance instead need to turn to Binance.US, the sister company to Binance.
