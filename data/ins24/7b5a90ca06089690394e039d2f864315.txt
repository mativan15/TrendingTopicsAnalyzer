Dennis Diatel Photography/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Asoka Woehrmann has resigned as CEO of Deutsche Bank's (NYSE:DB) DWS Group after authorities raided its Frankfurt offices on Tuesday to investigate allegations that the asset management business was misleading investors with its ESG claims. Stefan Hoops will take over as CEO of DWS Group, effective June 10, 2022, and will also take responsibility for asset management at the Deutsche Bank (DB) Group level, another role that had been held by Woehrmann.
