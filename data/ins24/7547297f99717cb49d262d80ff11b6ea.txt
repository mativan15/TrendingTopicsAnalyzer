NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Digiday announced Social Native as a finalist for the "Best Influencer Marketing Platform" category in the 2022 Digiday Technology Awards.The Digiday Technology Awards honors leading technology providers who are moving the needle to modernize media and marketing. The customer-focused awards cover all intersections of tech and digital marketing, with this year's focus on ecommerce, sustainability, and influencer marketing.
