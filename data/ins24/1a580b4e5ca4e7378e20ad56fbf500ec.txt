Photo: Getty ImagesLOS ANGELES (CNS) - The Los Angeles Lakers have a new coach, reaching a deal to hire Milwaukee Bucks assistant coach Darvin Ham to replace the fired Frank Vogel. ESPN first reported the hire Friday, which was subsequently confirmed by the Los Angeles Times. The team did not respond to a request for comment from City News Service, but star LeBron James confirmed the news on Twitter, writing, "So damn EXCITED!!!!!!! Congrats and welcome Coach DHam!!" The Times reported that the deal is for four years, and said Ham will be allowed to pick his own assistants.
