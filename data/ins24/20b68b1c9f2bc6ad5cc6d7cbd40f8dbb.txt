October 20, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time VANCOUVER, British Columbia--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Penny AI, a global leader in data-driven social sales enablement solutions, is excited to announce that Mannatech, a global health and wellness company, has partnered with Penny to deliver a digital-first platform for social sales enablement and business intelligence across the entire global sales force.

A top direct sales company operating in 25 global markets1, Mannatech produces innovative, leading-edge products that support health, beauty and wellness.
