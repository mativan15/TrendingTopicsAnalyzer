Carnegie Mellon University has been ranked as the top university in the United States for technology transfer by a new report from Heartland Forward, a nonprofit " think and do tank' headquartered in Bentonville, Arkansas, that focuses on economic renewal and improving economic performance in 20 states located in the middle of the country. The University of Florida took second, making it the highest ranked public university.
