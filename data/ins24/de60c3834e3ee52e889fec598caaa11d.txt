20 October 2022 by Nick Vivarelli 
Variety - Film News Ten years ago, Lucca Comics & Games expanded its scope with a dedicated film and TV section run by Milan-based entertainment marketing company Qmi, headed by Giovanni Cova, who has been working in tandem on this strand with Lucca chief Emanuele Vietina.During the course of this decade, Hollywood has become a more integral part of this unique geek meet. Initially with studio premieres, followed by streamers seeking to tap into the proselytizing power of thousands of cosplayers and fan-based communities that descend upon the medieval Tuscan town.
