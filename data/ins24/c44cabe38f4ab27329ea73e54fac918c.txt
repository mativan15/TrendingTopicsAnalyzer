Photography News: FUJIFILM's New Video-Heavy X-H2S, 18-120mm, and 150-600mm LensesNEW YORK, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- B&H is excited to announce the FUJIFILM X-H2S Mirrorless Camera, a massive upgrade that revives the flagship X Series line and makes tons of improvements, especially to video. Also announced were a pair of zoom lenses for the APS-C system: the XF 18-120mm f/4 R LM PZ WR and the XF 150-600mm f/5.6-8 R LM OIS WR.The X-H2S's major improvements are thanks to use of a 26.1MP stacked BSI APS-C sensor and 64-bit X-Processor 5, both of which are brand-new to the X Series.
