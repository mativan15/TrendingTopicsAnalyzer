Hy-Vee to Donate $100,000 to Families in Uvalde, Texas
iheart.com
news
2022-05-27 20:58:00

Photo: Hy-Vee Twitter(West Des Moines, IA) -- Hy-Vee is donating $100,000 to the victim's families and others affected by the mass shooting in Uvalde, Texas. Hy-Vee is donating the funds through their 'Spirit of Giving Fund', a 501 (c)3 organization set up by Texas retailer H-E-B. "Our heartfelt sympathies go out to the Uvalde community. When discussing how we could best support those affected, we knew our friends at H-E-B were already on the ground helping families," said Randy Edeker, chairman and CEO of Hy-Vee, in a press release.
