Cyient to acquire Portugal-based Celfinet to boost wireless communications offerings
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-06 06:45:00

New Delhi: Global technology solutions company on Monday announced that it had signed definitive agreements to acquire Portugal-based wireless engineering services firm Celfinet. Cyient will acquire Celfinet for 41 million euros, the company said in a statement. With this acquisition, Cyient will strengthen its wireless engineering practice to support communication service providers (CSPs) and enterprises in deploying their pervasive connectivity networks at scale.

This will also enable enterprises to build networks to harness the power of 5G and drive their own digital transformation journey.
