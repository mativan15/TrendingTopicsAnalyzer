The Diwali 2022 box office clash will leave everyone spellbound
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-22 06:45:00

With the festival of lights just around the corner, Bollywood is all geared up with the new lineup of the year's biggest releases. Even though the movies are of different genres, the ringing of box office registers will determine which one succeeds in catching the attention of film enthusiasts the most. The Box Office Battle Diwali 2022 box-office clash will see not one, not two, but three highly anticipated movies releasing this weekend. This time, superhit stars like Akshay Kumar, Sidharth Malhotra, Ajay Devgan, and Sharad Kelkar will be clashing against each other with their movies Ram Setu, Thank God and Har Har Har Mahadev.
