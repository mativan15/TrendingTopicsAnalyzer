Getty Images This is the season to hit the road, hop on a flight or perhaps set sail. While your summer getaway is meant to give you joy, travel can also be stressful, with long lines at airports, congestion on busy freeways and pandemic-related ship restrictions. Then add in managing an itinerary, possibly adjusting to new time zones and dealing with currency exchanges — especially for overseas excursions. The good news: You likely carry a smartphone with you, and with the right apps it could make your trip much smoother.
