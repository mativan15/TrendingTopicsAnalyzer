TikTok to Launch Songwriting Platform for Creators With Simon Cowell, UMG & Samsung

TikTok creators will soon be handed a fresh set of tools to soundtrack their videos and potentially make hit songs when a new in-app platform created by Simon Cowell's Syco Entertainment company, in partnership with Universal Music Group and Samsung, launches on the platform later this month.
