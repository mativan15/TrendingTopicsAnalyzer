Shetland cable damage sparks alarm over Russia's 'grey zone ops'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-22 06:01:00

Russian submarine activity has been placed under the spotlight once again after underwater cables supplying critical communications to the Shetland islands were severed on Thursday. While the culprit for this week's incident is suspected to have been a bungling fishing trawler, security analysts have pointed to the mass disruption on the islands as evidence of the vulnerability of key UK subsea infrastructure to hostile actions.  Alexander Lord, a Europe and Eurasia analyst at Sibylline told Express.
