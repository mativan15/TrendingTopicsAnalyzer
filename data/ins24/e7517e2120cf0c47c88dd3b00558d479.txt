Eaton Vance Tax-Managed Buy-Write Income Fund (NYSE:ETB) has been holding up reasonably well on a YTD basis. One of the problems with this is that the fund's share price has been holding up better than its NAV per share. That has resulted in the fund's premium being extended even further since we last covered the fund.
