Published: 09:04 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 09:04 EDT, 29 May 2022 The 85ft superyacht worth £6million that sank after a blazing inferno in a harbour yesterday was a luxury ship that could house eight passengers and had an al-fresco dining room, bar, sunpad, and a 55-inch TV.The race is on to stop a devastating oil spill after the Princess Y85 superyacht, which was called the Rendezvous, went up in flames at 11.57am on Saturday in Torquay Marina, Devon. The ship had 2,000 gallons of fuel on board when it caught fire.
