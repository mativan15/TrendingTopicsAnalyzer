The free trade agreement signed by Israel and the United Arab Emirates (UAE) on Tuesday and an earlier trade pact inked by India and the UAE have the potential for 'extensive trilateral cooperation', Israeli ambassador Naor Gilon said.Israel's landmark free trade deal with the UAE was the first such agreement with an Arab country and followed the establishment of diplomatic ties in 2020 after the signing of the Abraham Accords. The pact was signed in Dubai by Israel's economy and industry minister Orna Barbivay and her UAE counterpart Abdulla bin Touq al-Marri.
