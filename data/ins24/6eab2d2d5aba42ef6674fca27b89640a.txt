 Waterloo Regional Police are looking for a suspect in a robbery investigation in Kitchener.
Investigators say a man arranged a meeting with another man through a buy and sell website.The man was in the area of Westmount Road East and Ottawa Street South to meet the person Friday around 6:30 p.m. That was when investigators say someone sprayed the 26-year-old with a noxious substance and stole his cell phone.The victim suffered minor injuries as a result.
