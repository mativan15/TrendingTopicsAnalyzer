In 2012, Cartwright defeated 10-term incumbent Rep. Tim Holden — Pennsylvania's longest-serving congressman — in the Democrat primary election. Prior to his time in Congress, the now-61-year-old congressman worked as an attorney for 25 years. In 2020, Cartwright won a 5th term, defeating Bognet by a narrow margin of 3.
