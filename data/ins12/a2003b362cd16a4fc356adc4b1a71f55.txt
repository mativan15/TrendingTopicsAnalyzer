A beekeeper's petition to stop the New South Wales government eradicating hives in response to the varroa mite outbreak has amassed nearly 25,000 signatures.A central coast apiarist said beekeepers should be learning to live with varroa mite like other countriesHis petition to stop the NSW government's eradication strategy has attracted nearly 25,000 signaturesThe government said it was following the advice from industry and experts to control the outbreakDolfi Benesh saw 51 hives on his Jilliby property in the state's central coast destroyed last week because they sat inside an eradication zone.
