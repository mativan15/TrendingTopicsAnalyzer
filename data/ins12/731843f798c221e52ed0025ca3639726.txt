Everywhere you look, it seems like there's a new headline warning of a recession . The stock prices of major retailers like Target, Walmart, and Lowe's have declined substantially after earnings stumbles. Layoff announcements, hiring freezes, and slowdowns at big tech companies like Facebook, Snap, and Uber are on the rise, and the venture-capital community is souring on the outlook for once high-flying growth companies. But for all the doom and gloom, I don't think we're headed for a recession. The pandemic was an economic shock that produced clear winners and losers.
