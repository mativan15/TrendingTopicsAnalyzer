4 Little-Known Perks of a Sam's Club Plus Membership

In October, Sam's Club will raise its membership fees for the first time in nine years. The standard annual club membership will increase from $45 to $50, while the Sam's Club Plus membership will jump from $100 to $110. Before you make a final decision about upgrading, though, take a look at some of the lesser-known perks of a Plus membership.1. ConvenienceLet's say Sam's Club doesn't have something you want in stock, but it's there in the savings event catalog. Being a Plus member means Sam's will ship it to you for free.
