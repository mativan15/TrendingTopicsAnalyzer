by Sheldon Rocha LealHaving travelled internationally and interacted with my fellow citizens here in South Africa, the narrative surrounding the country and its output is generally negative. Some of this is self-inflicted and some is just pure ignorance. The truth is that some of the problems faced in South Africa are issues with which many countries grapple and are, therefore, not unique to the country. Maybe I'm an internal optimist, but I see a lot of good in our nation and have written various articles highlighting some of South Africa(n)'s creative victories, mainly in the music space.
