industryview/iStock via Getty ImagesRubicon Technologies Inc (NYSE:RBT) operates a digital marketplace for waste and recycling services. The software platform connects businesses and government agencies with a network of waste management haulers and landfill operators to streamline the industry supply chain. Recycling is big business, and the attraction here is a disruptive model bringing a high-tech solution to a critical service that has historically been performed offline. The company recently became publicly traded through a SPAC merger on the heels of impressive growth trends, counting on major corporations and municipalities as current customers.
