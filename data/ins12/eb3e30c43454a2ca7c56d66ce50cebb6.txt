Chanel Celebrates New High Jewelry Collection with Star-Studded Soirée
vanityfair.com
beauty
2022-10-21 20:07:10

Old Hollywood was made new last night, thanks to Chanel, who assembled the buzziest young names in entertainment for a Parisian-themed fete on one of tinseltown's oldest back lots. The dinner party was held in celebration of the 90th anniversary of Gabrielle Chanel's first — and only — high jewelry collection, Bijoux de Diamants, designed in 1932. The pop-up, planetarium-like exhibition included the archive pieces, as well as the new anniversary collection in a glimmering display.Maggie Rogers and Phoebe Bridgers.
