India go down 0-5 to Brazil in last group game
hindustantimes.com
sport
2022-10-17 17:37:56

In bright orange shirts they emerged for the last time in a tournament once cancelled and for a country once suspended. Determination writ large, hair pulled back or worn in a close crop, India took on Brazil hoping they could salvage something from the under-17 women's World Cup. It was not to be.As Brazil, 5-0 winners on the night – the last three from range by Aline (51st minute) and two from Lara (86th and 90+3) so good that they could be contenders for the goal of the tournament − go to the quarter-final against Group B toppers Germany, India go home.
