Father who ‘forcefully' shook crying baby son to death jailed for manslaughter
msn.com
news
2022-10-21 15:59:43

A father who admitted 'forcefully' shaking his baby son, causing injuries which later resulted in the boy's death, has been jailed. 
 

 © Social Media matthew banks fb.jpeg


Matthew Banks was sentenced at Winchester Crown Court after pleading guilty to the manslaughter of two-year-old Leo Banks, who died more than two years after suffering a 'catastrophic' brain injury.Banks, from Chichester, said his son had been crying hysterically and seemed stressed during a feed - leading the father to become frustrated and tired.
