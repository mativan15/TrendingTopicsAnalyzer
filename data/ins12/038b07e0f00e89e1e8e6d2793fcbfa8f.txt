A world leader in RF semiconductor manufacturing, GF's Vermont Fab moves closer to large-scale production of next-generation gallium nitride chipsESSEX JUNCTION, Vt., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- U.S. Senator Patrick Leahy and GlobalFoundries (Nasdaq: GFS) (GF), a global leader in feature-rich semiconductor manufacturing, today announced the award of $30 million in federal funding to advance the development and production of next-generation gallium nitride (GaN) on silicon semiconductors at GF's facility in Essex Junction, Vermont.
