Week 7 of the NFL regular season kicks off tonight as the Arizona Cardinals battle the New Orleans Saints in Glendale, Arizona.

The Cardinals are looking to put an end to a number of bad streaks with a win in this contest. Entering this game, Arizona has lost two straight games to fall to 2-4 on the season and has lost eight consecutive games at home. On the flip side, the Saints have lost four of their past five games, including a home loss last week against the Bengals, to also find themselves at 2-4.
