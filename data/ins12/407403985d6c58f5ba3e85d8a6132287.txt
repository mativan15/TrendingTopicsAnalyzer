If someone told you that eating or drinking from a bottle or jar for breakfast isn't the right way to begin your day, then they have not tried cold-pressed juices, overnight oats jars (ONO jars) or smoothies made with seasonal fresh fruits, berries and nuts.To simplify the process of making a healthy breakfast without having to worry about sourcing all ingredients at a time, Dileep Allamsetty started the Breakfast in a Bottle initiative, a subscription-based diet-meal plan.
