Sebi to auction properties of MPS group firms, four others to recover investors' money
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 13:45:00

NEW DELHI: To recover investors' money, capital markets regulator Sebi on Monday said it will auction properties belonging to companies of MPS Group, Vibgyor Group, Prayag Group, Pailan Group and Multipurpose BIOS India Group on June 30 for a reserve price of nearly Rs 64 crore. These companies had raised funds from investors without complying with regulatory norms. The 20 properties put on the block, which include land parcels, storied buildings, an office space, a commercial space and a flat, are situated across West Bengal, the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) said in a notice.
