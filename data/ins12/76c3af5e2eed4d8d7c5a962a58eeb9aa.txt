October 17, 2022 07:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time ST. JOHN'S, Newfoundland and Labrador--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Altius Renewable Royalties Corp. (TSX: ARR) (OTCQX: ATRWF) ('ARR', the 'Company', or the 'Corporation'), expects to report Q3 2022 attributable royalty revenue of $1.6 million, which compares to $0.03 million of attributable royalty revenue recorded in Q3 2021 and $0.6 million in Q2 2022. Revenue in the third quarter exceeded the total revenue for the first half of the year, driven primarily by strong summer merchant power prices.
