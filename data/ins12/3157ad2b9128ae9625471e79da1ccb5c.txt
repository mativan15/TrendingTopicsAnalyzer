REUTERS/Evgenia NovozheninaMOSCOW, Oct 17 (Reuters) - The rouble steadied near 62 against the dollar on Monday, supported by rising oil prices and the start of a favourable month-end tax period, as the spectre of geopolitical risk continued to lurk over Russian markets.At 0730 GMT, the rouble was 0.1% weaker against the dollar at 62.15 , close to a one-week high. It had lost 0.2% to trade at 60.
