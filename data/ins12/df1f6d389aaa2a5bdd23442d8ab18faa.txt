A recent Congress workers' march in MysuruThe Congress party has planned a week-long padayatra from August 9 as Karnataka is headed to assembly polls in less than a year and the ruling BJP too is pulling out all stops to save its face in the upcoming local body polls. The padayatra, that will take place in all districts, will highlight corruption under the BJP regime, governance failure and price rise, among other things, Karnataka Pradesh Congress President (KPCC) president DK Shivakumar said at the two-day Nava Sankalp Shibira he kickstarted at a private hotel on Benglauru outskirts along with AICC general secretary RS Surjewala and Opposition leader Siddaramaiah.
