 The rupee slipped 12 paise to 77.62 against the U.S. dollar in early trade on Thursday, tracking the strength of the American currency in the overseas market. At the interbank foreign exchange, the rupee opened at 77.61 against the American dollar, then lost ground to quote at 77.62, registering a fall of 12 paise from the last close. On Wednesday, the rupee recovered from its record low to close 21 paise higher at 77.
