BMC slashes access charges for laying OFC to ₹1,000 from ₹1 lakh
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-03 17:27:19

Mumbai: In a significant development, the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has revised the access charges for laying telecom Optical Fiber Cables (OFC) in Mumbai to ₹1,000 per kilometre from ₹1,00,000 kilometre. An OFC is a cable made using fibre that is mainly used to transfer digital data signals and these cables are mainly laid by telecom companies to expand and strengthen their network across the city. The BMC has been imposing an access charge of ₹1,00,000 per kilometre since January 2014.
