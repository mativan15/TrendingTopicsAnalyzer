June 01, 2022 05:33 PM Eastern Daylight Time LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BitNile Holdings, Inc. (NYSE American: NILE), a diversified holding company ('BitNile' or the 'Company'), announces today that the pricing of its public offering has been revised so that a total of 144,000 shares of its 13.00% Series D Cumulative Redeemable Perpetual Preferred Stock (the "Series D Preferred Stock") will be offered at a price to the public of $25.00 per share. Gross proceeds from the offering are expected to be approximately $3.
