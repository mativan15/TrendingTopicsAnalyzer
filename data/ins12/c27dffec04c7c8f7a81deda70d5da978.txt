Punjab to increase milk supply to Delhi by 2 lakh litre: Bhagwant Mann
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 13:15:00

LUDHIANA: Punjab chief minister Bhagwant Mann said on Wednesday, Milkfed - the leading milk cooperative society of the state - will increase its supply to Delhi from 30,000 litres to 2 lakh litres which will benefit the farmers and the milk producers of the state.'This is a revolutionary step aimed at supplementing the income of the farmers to bail them out from the agrarian crisis," he said after inaugurating the newly-built milk processing and butter plant Verka in Ludhiana on Wednesday.The chief minister said that the state government has inked an agreement with the Delhi government in this regard.
