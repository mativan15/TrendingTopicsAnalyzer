October 18, 2022 06:03 AM Eastern Daylight Time PALO ALTO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hercules Capital, Inc. (NYSE: HTGC) ('Hercules' or the 'Company'), the largest and leading specialty financing provider to innovative venture, growth and established stage companies backed by some of the leading and top-tier venture capital and select private equity firms, is pleased to announce that its Board of Directors has declared a supplemental cash distribution of $0.15 per share.

This supplemental cash distribution will be paid out of Hercules Capital's undistributed taxable income (taxable income in excess of distributions paid) as of September 30, 2022.
