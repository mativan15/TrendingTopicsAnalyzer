There's perhaps been no time more challenging than now in the field of human resources. In the midst of a job exodus so widespread that it's become known as the "Great Resignation" and on the heels of a global pandemic, HR departments have had to think about everything from work-from-home policies and concerns about health and privacy to strategies around employee retention and recruitment.

The Charlotte Business Journal' s Best in HR Awards program highlights the important work being done in this area.
