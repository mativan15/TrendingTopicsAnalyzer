MPs allege bullying during chaotic fracking vote

news
2022-10-19 20:57:07

Media caption, WATCH: 'That looked like bullying to me' - Chris BryantConservative MPs were bullied and manhandled into backing Liz Truss in a vote on fracking, according to MPs who witnessed the scenes. Ministers denied claims physical force had been used to persuade colleagues to vote with the government.But Labour MP Chris Bryant called for an inquiry into what "looked like bullying".There had been suggestions earlier that Chief Whip Wendy Morton and her deputy had left their roles. However, the government has confirmed that both MPs are staying in post.
