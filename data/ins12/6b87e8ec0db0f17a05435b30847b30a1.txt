[Photo/IC] The China International Import Expo provides tremendous opportunities for US businesses to learn about the Chinese market and improves existing trade ties with China, Californian officials and trade experts say. More than 80 participants, including Chinese and US officials, diplomats, businesses and think tank leaders, attended an event on Thursday hosted by the Shanghai Municipal Commission of Commerce to discuss trade exchanges at the expo.
