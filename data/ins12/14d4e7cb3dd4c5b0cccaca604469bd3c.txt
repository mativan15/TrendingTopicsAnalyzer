Roblox Shares Jump Following September Metrics
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 14:04:00

By Will Feuer Shares of videogame platform Roblox Corp. jumped 15% after the company released key metrics for September. Daily active users on the company's platform came in at 57.8 million, up 23% from a year earlier but down from the 59.9 million users reported for August, the company said. Roblox reported 4 billion hours engaged for September, up 16% from a year earlier but down from 4.7 billion reported in August. The return to school from summer vacation may have driven some of the month-over-month declines.
