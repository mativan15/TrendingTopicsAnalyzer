THE 99 BOYFRIENDS OF MICAH SUMMERS, by Adam Sass. (Viking Books for Young Readers, $18.99.) A young adult romance about a teenage artist on the quest to find his dream boyfriend after their meet-cute on the train is abruptly cut short.

A SCATTER OF LIGHT, by Malinda Lo. (Dutton Books for Young Readers, $18.99.) Lo follows up her National Book Award-winning young adult novel 'Last Night at the Telegraph Club' with a new L.G.B.T.Q. coming-of-age story, this one about a teenager discovering love during a fateful summer before she goes off to college.
