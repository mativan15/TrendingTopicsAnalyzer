whitebalance.oatt/iStock via Getty Images One of the most prominent payment processors is Global Payments (NYSE:GPN), a Fortune 500 firm that is well-positioned to meet the expanding demand for digital payment services. As a result of reaching an agreement to acquire EVO Payments (NASDAQ:EVOP), one of the leading players in the market, for an all-cash deal, GPN was able to maintain its rising top line and revenue despite the news of departing operations in Russia and its consumer business, Netspend.
