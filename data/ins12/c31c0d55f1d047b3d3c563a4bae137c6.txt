City Hospital nursing assistant and mother jailed for stealing from elderly woman
bbc.com
news
2022-06-01 16:28:19

A nursing assistant and her mother have been sent to prison for "mean and despicable" offences against an elderly hospital patient.Julie Anne Lemon, 45, and her mother Claire Clarina Lemon, 65, were each sentenced to eight months.A third woman - Julie Anne's sister Michelle Seenan, 41, - received a six-month sentence suspended for a year.Belfast Crown Court heard that the 72-year-old victim was a "vulnerable and elderly" widow.Julie Anne Lemon, from Altnagarron Mews in Belfast, stole the woman's bank cards and house keys in May 2019 while the woman was a patient at Belfast City Hospital.
