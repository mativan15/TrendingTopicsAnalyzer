October 17, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time HARTFORD, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Travelers Institute, the public policy division of The Travelers Companies, Inc. (NYSE: TRV), will host a webinar Wednesday, Oct. 19, titled 'Inflation, Interest Rates and the State of the U.S. Economy: A Conversation with Neel Kashkari, President and CEO of the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis.'

'Businesses and consumers are facing economic pressures driven by multiple factors, making this conversation with Neel very timely,' said Joan Woodward, President of the Travelers Institute and Executive Vice President of Public Policy at Travelers.
