CVS rose 1.7%.Cano has decided to walk away at this time, according to a Dealreporter item, which cited sources familiar. CVS is expected to look to return capital to holders, potential through an accelerated repurchase plan, which may be announced next month. The news comes after Bloomberg earlier this month reported that CVS (CVS) was said in exclusive talks to purchase the health-care provider.
