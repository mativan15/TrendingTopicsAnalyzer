TVLine.com Dead to Me is exhuming one of ER‘s darkest chapters, tapping Garret Dillahunt to appear in the final season opposite his onetime TV ex Linda Cardellini.Dillahunt — who recurred on ER as Steve Curtis, the the homicidal, abusive former husband of Cardellini's nurse Samantha Taggart — joins Season 3 of the Netflix dramedy as Agent Glenn Moranis, a top-notch detective tasked with getting to the bottom of Steve's (James Marsden) murder.
