The Bombay High Court recently rejected the bail of a man who took undue advantage of the loneliness of a married woman he met on Facebook, took her nude photographs and videos, and shared them via WhatsApp.A single Bench of Justice Nitin Sambre was hearing the bail application of Saurabh Sukhija, who was arrested in July 2021.According to the prosecution, Mr. Sukhija claimed to a single person who met the complainant in June 2019 on Facebook and took undue advantage of her as her husband lives in a foreign country.
