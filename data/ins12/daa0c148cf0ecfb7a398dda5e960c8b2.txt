A visitor (top) at the Industrial-Grade 5G Innovation Application (Dali) Research Institute experiences remote driving in Dali, Southwest China's Yunnan province, on May 26, 2022. A vending cart is driven remotely in Dali. [Photo/Xinhua] 5G technology has expanded industrial-grade innovative application in Dali, Southwest China's Yunnan province. From remote driving vending carts and robot dogs to special vehicles, flexible robots and driverless sightseeing vehicles, 5G technology can be applied in various industries to facilitate and promote economic development.
