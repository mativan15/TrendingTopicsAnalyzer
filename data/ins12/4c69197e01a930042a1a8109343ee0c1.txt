Everest Group names Virtusa a Major Contender across the three biggest hyperscalers for unmatched cloud transformation experiences 
SOUTHBOROUGH, Mass., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Virtusa Corporation, a global provider of digital strategy, digital engineering and IT services and solutions, today announced it has been recognized as a "Major Contender" in Everest Group's System Integration (SI) in Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Microsoft Azure Services PEAK Matrix® Assessments in 2022.
