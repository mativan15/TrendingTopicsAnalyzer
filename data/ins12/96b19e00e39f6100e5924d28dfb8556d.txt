Hollywood star Jon Hamm-led animated comedy 'Grimsburg' has given out an early season 2 renewal by Fox way before the show even premiered.During a MIPCOM panel discussion led by Cynthia Littleton, co-editor-in-chief of Variety, Michael Thorn, head of entertainment for Fox Entertainment, made the announcement.According to Variety, "Grimsburg is yet another clear example of our strong commitment to, and intense focus on, upholding the high bar of standards any show must exceed in order to reside under the Animation Domination banner," Thorn said.
