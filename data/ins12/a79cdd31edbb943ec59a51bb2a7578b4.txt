 Work is now underway to improve a pedestrian path damaged by erosion on Osoyoos Lake.
The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen (RDOS) will be working on the project just south of the Village of Osoyoos boundaries.Floodwaters in 2018 overtopped the road and pathway in this area, causing damage to the retaining wall.
