Jeffrey Katzenberg's WndrCo is betting big on cybersecurity tech and consumer VPNs as 'concerns around privacy have exploded'
businessinsider.com
news
2022-10-18 16:53:48

Like everyone else in the global workforce, Jeffrey Katzenberg and Sujay Jaswa, his investing partner at venture firm WndrCo, use Zoom to connect. But Katzenberg believes that in the not too distant future Zoom will look like a Xerox photocopier, an anachronism quickly replaced by better technology.Katzenberg is placing his bets on new ways that technology will revolutionize the hybrid workplace and screen-based two-way communications. The co-founder of DreamWorks Animation and onetime Disney studios chief, together with Jaswa, the former CFO of Dropbox, are focused in particular on communication tools that can help consumers figure out how to protect their online privacy and cybersecurity along with enterprise technology.
