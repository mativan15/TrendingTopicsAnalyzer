EASYJET and Tui customers are the latest to suffer from last-minute cancelled flights during the start of the school holidays.
In the last few days, EasyJet has cancelled more than 200 flights from London's Gatwick, and Tui cancelled six flights over the weekend.
Martin Lewis' MoneySavingExpert has issued guidance following hundreds of cancelled flights out of the UKCredit: PA
Thousands of holidaymakers have been affected.
A Tui spokesperson apologised to customers, adding cancellations had been caused due to "various operational and supply chain issues".
