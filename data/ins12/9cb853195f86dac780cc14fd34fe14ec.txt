Civility and humility are key for avoiding contractual disputes.getty
The International Institute for Conflict Prevention and Resolution — CPR is a world-wide provider of mediation and arbitration services. But CPR's CEO Allen Waxman says the best way to ensure your company stays out of court is to deploy dispute prevention practices and avoid disputes with business partners in the first instance. Amgen's Executive Vice President, General Counsel, and Secretary Jonathan Graham agrees — and adds civility and humility as two key ways to avoid value-depleting disputes.
