Workers in an Oklahoma City retail store of tech giant Apple voted to unionize on Friday, according to the Communications Workers of America Union (CWA), making them the second Apple store in the United States to do so. Videos shared on social media showed jubilant scenes of employees cheering at the store, and in a mission statement ahead of the vote, the Oklahoma staff said they were fighting for better pay, career development and improved heath and well-being conditions in their work environment.
