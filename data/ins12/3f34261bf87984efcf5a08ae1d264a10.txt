20 grams of cocaine recovered, African national held
thehindu.com
news
2022-06-01 16:59:39

The Halasuru police on Sunday arrested a 32-year-old African national and recovered 20.8 grams of cocaine from him.The accused identified as Camara Charales from Guinea was caught red-handed on Kensington road while he was waiting for his clients to deliver the drugs. Based on a tip off, the police arrested the accused.The accused had come to city on student visa and had been peddling drugs for the past one year, a police officer said adding that the police are now trying to track the source of drugs and his clients.
