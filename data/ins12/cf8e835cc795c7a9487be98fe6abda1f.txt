Punjab: Fake DSP arrested for duping money in Ludhiana
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 18:45:00

LUDHIANA: The Ludhiana police on Tuesday arrested a fraudster who impersonating as deputy superintendent of police and inspector used dupe people of money on the pretext of setting their kin involved in criminal cases free.The city police have also recovered an inspector ranked uniform from his possession, with badges on it, besides one scooter.The arrested accused has been identified as Sukhmanjeet Singh, 28, of Manohar Nagar , Dhuri Line . His accomplice Makhan of Jagraon has yet to be arrested.
