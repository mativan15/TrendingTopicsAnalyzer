How retail brands are helping the economy grow in a better way
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-04 13:53:33

The retail brands yield a powerful impact on the economy by attracting customers to continue with their purchase of innovative and new-age products even during the downward phase of the economy.
The importance of the retail industry in terms of its contribution to the economy couldn't be overemphasised. There are multiple ways through which retail brands help build the economy and take the GDP to the desired level of market capitalisation and growth rate.
On the manufacturing front, retail brands contribute to the entire cycle of sourcing, production, and dispatching the products to customers through intermediaries such as distributors, wholesalers, and retailers.
