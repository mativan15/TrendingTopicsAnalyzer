Toronto Stocks Slide; Yamana Gold Rises on Gold Fields' $6.7B Deal
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 16:00:00

By Adriano Marchese Canadian stocks were lower on Tuesday at midday after closing higher on Monday while U.S. markets were closed for Memorial Day. In the session, retail was the only sector posting gains while tech, health tech and tech services were the main laggards. Yamana Gold Inc.'s Toronto-listed shares rose after South Africa-based mining company Gold Fields Ltd. said it agreed to buy it in an all-stock deal that values the Canadian gold producer at $6.7 billion. Mid-trading, Canada's S&P/TSX Composite Index was down 0.
