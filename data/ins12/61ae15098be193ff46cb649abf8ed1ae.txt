October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Socure, the leading provider of digital identity verification and fraud solutions, will demonstrate the vital importance of trusted identity in the financial services and government sectors through a range of activities at this year's Money20/20 conference, which will be held October 23-26 in Las Vegas, NV.

Featured at the event, Jordan Burris, Vice President, Public Sector Strategy at Socure, and other notable experts, will discuss 'The State of Identity in Government,' in which they will outline initiatives around identity verification and fraud prevention within federal and state governments.
