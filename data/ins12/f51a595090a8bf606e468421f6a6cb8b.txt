REUTERS/Henry Nicholls/File PhotoLONDON, Oct 21 (Reuters) - British finance minister Jeremy Hunt reiterated on Friday that the government will do "whatever is necessary to get drive down debt in the medium term," responding to the latest data on public sector finances."We will do whatever is necessary to get drive down debt in the medium term and to ensure that taxpayers' money is well spent, putting the public finances on a sustainable path as we grow the economy," Hunt said in a statement.
