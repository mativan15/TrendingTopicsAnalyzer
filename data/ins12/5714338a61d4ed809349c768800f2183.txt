Duterte spent P4.5 billion on confidential, intel funds in 2021
blogspot.com
news
2022-10-16 06:53:00

DAVAO CITY - Former
President Rodrigo Duterte spent a total of P4.5 billion in confidential and
intelligence funds for 2021, according to the Annual Financial Report of the
Commission on Audit on national government agencies. The report noted
that the Office of the President under Duterte spent P2.25 billion on
confidential expenses while another P2.25 billion was spent on intelligence
expenses. The report is
prepared annually by the commission, as mandated by the Constitution and the
Government Auditing Code of the Philippines.
