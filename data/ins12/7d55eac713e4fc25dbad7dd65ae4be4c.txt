'Mediterranean Fever': Film Review
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-05-31 16:05:37

Winner of the best screenplay award in Cannes' Un Certain Regard competition, Palestinian-Israeli writer-director Maha Haj's serio-comic Mediterranean Fever explores a complicated friendship-enmity — call it a 'frenmity' — between two middle-aged neighbors, adeptly played by Amer Hlehel and Ashraf Farah. Concerned mostly with domestic life, mental health, crime and class, this is one of those relatively rare features from either Palestine or Israel that's not completely marinated in politics, history and religion, only lightly spiced.
