No. 1 Tennessee, 4 other teams lock up NCAA regional titles
stcatharinesstandard.ca
news
2022-06-06 00:27:05

Tennessee was right where it was expected to be Sunday night, celebrating an NCAA regional title on its home field and eager to continue one of the most dominant runs in college baseball history.The No. 1 national seed Volunteers, No. 4 Virginia Tech, No. 5 Texas A&M, Texas and Notre Dame closed out their regionals and advanced to super regionals beginning Friday.No. 6 Miami was the first top-eight seed eliminated, losing 4-3 to Arizona in Coral Gables, Florida. Tennessee (56-7) beat Georgia Tech 9-6, never leading until it scored six runs in the ninth inning.
