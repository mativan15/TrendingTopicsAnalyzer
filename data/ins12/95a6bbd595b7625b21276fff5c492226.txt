Internet City, Dubai--(Newsfile Corp. - June 1, 2022) - LBank Exchange, a global digital asset trading platform, will list Infinity Rocket Token (IRT) on June 1, 2022. For all users of LBank Exchange, the IRT/USDT trading pair will be officially available for trading at 16:00 (UTC+8) on June 1, 2022.
