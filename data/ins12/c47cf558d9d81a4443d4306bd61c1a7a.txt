4 PFI activists held: Assam police
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 19:15:00

The Assam Police said on Friday that they arrested four activists of the Popular Front of India (PFI), which was banned by the central government last month. Special director-general of police (law & order) GP Singh told the media that the police would continue to take action against jihadi activities and the PFI in the state. These PFI leaders were arrested from different places of Nagerbera. They are identified as Nojrul Islam from Gogolmari, Rafikul Islam from Tupamari, Moninoor Islam from Khemeri and Hafijur Rahman from Polahorhati.
