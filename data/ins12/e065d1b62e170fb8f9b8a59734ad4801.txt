Where L.A. mayoral candidates raise the most money ahead of the June 7 primary

2022-06-01 13:45:00

June 1, 2022By Iris Lee and Sandhya KambhampatiWith less than a week left until the primary, Los Angeles residents and businesses have contributed at least $8.7 million to mayoral candidates, nearly doubling in a month, according to new data released from the Los Angeles Ethics Commission.Roughly $49 million has poured in from contributions and loans, the most of any Los Angeles mayoral race to date. Of that, $33.5 million is money loaned from billionaire developer Rick Caruso to himself, who has spent more than any other candidate in the race.
