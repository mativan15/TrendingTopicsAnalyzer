Oct. 19—CLYDE — The Clyde governing board met with a skeleton crew Thursday, Oct. 13, with Mayor Jim Trantham, Vice Mayor Frank Lay and Alderman Dann Jesse. Alderman John Hemingway was in a mandatory training for his job at the Sheriff's Office while Alderwoman Diane Fore was out of town.

Here are a few meeting highlights:

Audit

Certified public accountant Sheila Gahagan presented the town's annual audit, telling the board she detected no deficiencies in internal controls nor material weaknesses.
