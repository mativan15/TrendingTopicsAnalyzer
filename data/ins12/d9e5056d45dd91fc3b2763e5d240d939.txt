When can I play Gotham Knights?

Gotham Knights is set to release on October 21, and gamers looking forward to jumping into the newest Batman-connected game don't have long to wait. But when exactly can you start playing? Here's what you need to know.Preloading for the upcoming game is already live on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and PC. Reviews and previews for Gotham Knights are looking good, especially for those chomping at the bit for a new game set within the Batman universe. With the Gotham Knights release date pushed up four days from its originally scheduled release, fans can get their hands on the newest crime-fighting adventure as soon as midnight local time on October 21.
