Gail India to invest ₹6,000 cr. in renewables over next 3 years

2022-05-30 14:42:54

State-owned Gail India will invest ₹6,000 crore in the next three years in renewables, a top official said on Monday.The investment can go up by an additional Rs 20,000 crore by 2030, GAIL India chairman and managing director Manoj Jain told reporters here.GAIL India Director (Finance) Rakesh Kumar Jain said the company, which reported a 112% jump in FY22 post-tax net profit to ₹10,364 crore, has outlined an overall capital expenditure plan of up to ₹40,000 crore in the next five years, which will cover a wide array of areas.
