Importance of creating sleep awareness: Expert shares insights

news
2022-10-20 09:58:40

Sleep is the third pillar of good health along with exercise and nutrition. It is necessary for the rejuvenation of the body and the mind. In today's fast-paced our sleep is often compromised for work and entertainment. The connection between sleep and health is real. Sleep awareness is a concept that seeks to promote better sleep as a way to increase overall health and well-being. it also provides us with an opportunity to look at our own sleep habits and search for ways to improve on it and aims to lessen the burden of sleep problems.
