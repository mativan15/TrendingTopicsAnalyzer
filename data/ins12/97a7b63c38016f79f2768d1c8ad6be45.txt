The business community of County Durham has come together to support an appeal to fight poverty in the area ahead of its 2022 launch.

County Durham Community Foundation's Poverty Hurts campaign for 2022, which will officially get underway at an event in Durham city on November 4th, has so far attracted support from organisations including members of Durham Business Group, insurance provider NFU Mutual, estate agency Urban Base, pensions specialist Sandringham Financial Partners, and Stowe Family Law.
