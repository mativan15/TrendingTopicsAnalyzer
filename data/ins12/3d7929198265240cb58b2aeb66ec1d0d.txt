June 02, 2022 06:45 AM Eastern Daylight Time SCOTTSDALE, Ariz.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--STORE Capital Corporation (NYSE: STOR), an internally managed net-lease real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests in Single Tenant Operational Real Estate, today announced that its management will attend Nareit's REITweek: 2022 Investor Conference being held in New York, New York June 7-9. Management will deliver a presentation on Wednesday, June 8, 2022 from 10:15 a.m. to 10:45 a.m. Eastern Time and will host meetings with investors and analysts Tuesday, June 7 through Thursday, June 9.
