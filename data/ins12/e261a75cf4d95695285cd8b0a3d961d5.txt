Nextech AR Solutions (OTCQB:NEXCF) has won its largest purchaser order to date.
The order, valued at $6.7M, came from a NASDAQ 100 Technology Company and pertains to Nextech's (OTCQB:NEXCF) 3D model services. The 3D models are to be delivered in the calendar year 2023.
The company is experiencing accelerated growth in orders for its 3D model business for e-commerce. With the previously announced $700K 3D model order to be delivered in Q4/2022, the total new orders recently announced equals $7.4M.
