Wife Of Civil Deputy Who Shot Her, Killed His Son And Himself, Arrested
iheart.com
news
2022-05-27 01:45:00

Syracuse, N.Y. - Back in February off duty Civil Deputy Isaac Eames shot his wife, Karen, and his son Troy before shooting himself inside their Brewerton home. Eames and his son both died. Today Onondaga County Sheriffs announced they arrested Karen. Onondaga County Sheriff Gene Conway says Eames ands his wife were stealing money through his position as a civil deputy and overseeing a civil account . Conway said of Eames, " He's a murderer. He's a thief and he's a selfish person." Onondaga County District Attorney William Fitzpatrick said, there were about 13 transfers to a personal account totaling about $529,165.
