A group of university friends have broken the record for cycling the length of the UK on a conference bike and raised thousands of pounds for a cancer charity.The team, nicknamed 'Magnificent Seven', rode from John O'Groats to Land's End on a seven-seater bike and completed the challenge in just five days and 21 hours.A group of university friends will have broken the record for cycling the length of the UK on a conference bike. (Exposure Photo Agency Ltd/PA)Two core teams of riders set off on Sunday May 22 and cycled in shifts throughout the day and night on the 200kg (441lb) seven-seater bike, finishing the challenge on May 28.
