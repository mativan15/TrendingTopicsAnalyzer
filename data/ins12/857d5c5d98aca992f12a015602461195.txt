16 October 2022 by Leo Barraclough 
Variety - TV News Limonero Films, an independent boutique distributor of factual programming and documentaries, is launching a selection of documentaries and factual entertainment series at the Mipcom TV market.The titles are 'Women Behind the Wheel,' produced by Dartmouth Films in the U.K., the wildlife titles 'Wild Icons of Iberia' and 'Heroines of the Savannah,' both by Azor Productions in Spain, 'The Baby Makers,' from Mediacorp in Singapore, and 'Shepherds of the Earth,' by Guerrilla Films in Finland.
