New sustainability initiative will drive adoption and growth of clean crypto-carbon ecosystemNEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Community Gaming, the premier all-in-one esports competition platform, has announced today that it has, through a collaboration with the Play2Learn Foundation and KlimaDAO, become climate positive by completely offsetting its on-chain carbon emissions, particularly through the preservation of the rainforest. With the adoption and development of initiatives focused on creating a sustainable Web3 ecosystem, this partnership has helped mitigate the carbon footprint of Community Gaming's on-chain transactions.
