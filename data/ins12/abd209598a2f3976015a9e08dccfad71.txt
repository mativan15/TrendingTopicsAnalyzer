Three cities in Andhra Pradesh prepare for energy saving project

VISAKHAPATNAM: The Andhra Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (APERC) has advised the discoms and Andhra Pradesh State Energy Efficiency Development Corporation Limited (APSEEDCO) to prepare modalities to launch the pilot project on the promotion and sale of energy efficient home appliances.The pilot project would be implemented initially in Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam and Tirupati. The energy department has approved the on-bill financing model and upfront model to launch the pilot project.During a virtual meeting with the discoms and APSEEDCO, a joint venture of power utilities, the APERC chairman, Justice CV Nagarjuna Reddy said that the domestic sector consumes about 25 per cent of total energy demand of the state.
