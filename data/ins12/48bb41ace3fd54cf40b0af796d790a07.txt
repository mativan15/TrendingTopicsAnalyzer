Conover, NC, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- via NewMediaWire -- Fortran Corporation (OTC: FRTN), an emerging telecommunications technology systems integrator, is pleased to announce that our subsidiary, B and L Telephone, has been busy this summer upgrading the capabilities of twenty-one (21) schools in three Western North Carolina counties. 'These upgrades will greatly enhance the abilities of these 21 schools to provide services to their students and faculty,' stated Fortran President & CEO Glenn Withers.
