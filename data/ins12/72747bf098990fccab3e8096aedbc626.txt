Author of two novels, Rogue Male and The Third Hour, GEOFFREY HOUSEHOLD is a talented English writer whose first short story appeared in the Atlantic in 1936. His gift as a linguist made him of immense value to British Intelligence during the war, and later he served with distinction as a major in the British Army of the Middle East.
