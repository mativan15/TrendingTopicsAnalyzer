Our country is known for its rich heritage, vivid colour palettes, intricate embroideries and unique weaves. In keeping with the theme of this year's World Interiors Day, 'Pride of the Past, An incentive for the Future', add a dash of autochthonal flavour to your modern desi home with the new Taana-Baana collection of wall textures developed by Asian Paints Royale Play.This year's theme for International Federation of Interior Architects/ Designers (IFI) World Interiors Day 2022 was chosen to celebrate the innovation and future-forward look at the profession of home designing, while honouring the past and learning from its experiences.
