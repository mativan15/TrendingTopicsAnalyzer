Olaplex Cuts FY22 Sales Guidance, Shares Decline 37%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 21:34:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Olaplex Holdings Inc. were trading lower in Tuesday's after-hours session, following the cutting of sales guidance for fiscal year 2022. At 5:18 p.m. ET, the stock had fallen 37.2% to trade at $6.15. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 1.98% gain, closing at $9.79. The stock touched a 52-week low of $8.66 on Oct. 13. After the bell, the maker of beauty products said it is expecting fiscal year 2022 sales of $704 million to $711 million. Previously, it expected sales of $796 million to $826 million.
