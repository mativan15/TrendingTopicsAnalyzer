Ranvir Shorey on anti-Bollywood sentiment and why boycott calls won't work: ‘People sense there's injustice here but…'
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-19 11:33:00

It is hard to tell if Ranvir Shorey is making a statement in anger, for it is usually followed by a laugh. When he talks about his role being reduced in projects because of insecurity, he says it with a smile; when he insists he can't play politics in the industry, he jokes that even his team can't do that on his behalf.
But beyond the humour, Ranvir Shorey means business. On the sidelines of the premiere of his latest release Midday Meeal, the actor — son of filmmaker KD Shorey — spoke to indianexpress.
