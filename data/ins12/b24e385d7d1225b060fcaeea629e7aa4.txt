Classes will be held in person for the first time since the pandemic began. (Shutterstock)FRAMINGHAM, MA - Registration currently is open for the Framingham High School Summer Institute educational program beginning on July 5.The Summer Institute, which runs from July 5-29, offers both enrichment and recovery classes for students seeking to advance their learning over the summer break, according to Mike Tarlin, the program's administrator. This will be the first time the program is being held in person since the pandemic began.
