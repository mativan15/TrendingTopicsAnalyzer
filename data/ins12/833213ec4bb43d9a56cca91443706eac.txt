It's just over seven months since Mark Zuckerberg declared Facebook's parent company would be renamed Meta. For many people, the whole concept of the metaverse is still a bit hazy. But as companies and early adopters take part in virtual land grabs, and virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) companies rush to grow their customer base, the virtual world already appears to be adopting many of the worst behaviors of the real one.A report from corporate accountability group SumOfUs says Meta's VR platforms (Horizon Worlds and Horizon Venues) are rife with many of the same problems facing the more traditional social media outlets.
