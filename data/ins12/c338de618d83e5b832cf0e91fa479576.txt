Ladakh plans to use fences around Hunder sand dunes, develop Sumoor desert

ANIBorder Roads Organisation (BRO) use a machine to clear a snow-covered passes of Ladakh during snow clearing operations by BRO. The Ladakh administration is likely to use fences around Hunder sand dunes and ensure development of Sumoor desert to upgrade Nubra valley as a multi-destination area with improved facilities for tourists, officials said on Sunday. Chairing a high-level meeting at the Raj Niwas here on Saturday, Lt Governor R K Mathur Mathur asked officials to ensure the welfare of the double-hump Bactrian camels in Hunder and suggested that fitness certificates for the animals may be issued, they said.
