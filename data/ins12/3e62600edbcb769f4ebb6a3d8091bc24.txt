Latest Investment includes partnership with Thompson Medical Group and Disney Investment Vehicle, Shamrock Holdings, Inc.

NEW YORK, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Recharge Capital, a leading thematic-first private investment firm, is announcing a ~$3 million incubation investment into Generation Prime. The digital healthcare platform will be the first full-stack, closed-loop IVF and related health services provider with IVF clinic roll-ups serving Southeast Asia, a region of over 600 million residents.
