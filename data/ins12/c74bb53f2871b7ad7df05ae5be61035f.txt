By Louise DewastBBC Africa Image source, Fatou JallowImage caption, Fatou "Toufah" Jallow says she fled The Gambia after she was raped and now lives in CanadaA 23-year-old former beauty queen in The Gambia, Fatou "Toufah" Jallow, has said she was raped by ex-President Yahya Jammeh when he was in office.Her testimony is part of a Human Rights Watch and Trial International report that details another alleged rape and sexual assault by Mr Jammeh.The BBC tried to contact Mr Jammeh, who now lives in exile in Equatorial Guinea, about the allegations.
