SHELTON, Conn., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Acme United Corporation (NYSE American: ACU) today announced that it has acquired the assets of Live Safely Products, LLC. (d/b/a 'Safety Made'), a leading manufacturer of first aid kits for the promotional products industry. Safety Made had revenues in 2021 of approximately $4.9 million and EBITDA of approximately $1.2 million. The purchase price was not disclosed. The acquisition is expected to be accretive immediately. The company is located in Keene, New Hampshire and employs 24 people.
