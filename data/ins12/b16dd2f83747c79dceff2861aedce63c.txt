In order to hold a convention in Gondwana Bhavan to mark the birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, the tribal communities of Chattisgarh had arrived in the state's capital Raipur. Later, hordes of tribals foot-marched through Raipur to incorporate their demands and protested against the PESA act. Twitter/@ashubhAccording to tribal leader Suraj Tekam, over 5,000 people from various sections of the state attended the protest gathering. Why are tribal communities protesting in the state? Through their protests, the tribal communities are trying to persuade all constitutional authorities to repeal the new PESA rules.
