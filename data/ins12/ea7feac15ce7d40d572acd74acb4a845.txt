JEREMY Vine viewers have clashed with one another on social media over a debate on whether dogs should be allowed in restaurants. 
Britain is fondly known as a nation of dog lovers, with many of us going the extra mile to make our four-legged friends part of the family.
A debate on whether dogs should be allowed in restaurants took a vicious turnCredit: Getty
The Jeremy Vine Show's official Twitter account posed the question to fansCredit: Twitter
But at what point do we draw the line and argue that some places, including restaurants, really are just for people and not for pets?
Taking to Twitter on Friday morning, the official Jeremy Vine account posed that exact question to its followers - but things soon took a vicious turn.
