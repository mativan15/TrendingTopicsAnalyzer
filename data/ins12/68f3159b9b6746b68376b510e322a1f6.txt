October 20, 2022 08:25 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Heritage Global Inc. (NASDAQ: HGBL) ('Heritage Global,' 'HG' or 'the Company'), an asset services company specializing in financial and industrial asset transactions, today announced that the Company will release its third quarter 2022 financial results after the market closes on Thursday, November 10, 2022.

Webcast and Earnings Conference Call

Management will host a webcast and conference call on Thursday, November 10, 2022, at 5:00 pm ET to discuss financial results for third quarter 2022.
