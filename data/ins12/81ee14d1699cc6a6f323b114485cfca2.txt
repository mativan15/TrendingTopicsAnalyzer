Prime Minister Narendra Modi said that he has not done anything that would make Indians hang their head in shame. "I have spared no effort in my service to the nation in the last eight years. I have neither allowed, nor personally done any such work that will make you or even a single person of India hang head in shame," Modi said.

The Prime Minister was addressing a gathering after inaugurating a 200-bed multi-specialty hospital at Atkot town in Gujarat's Rajkot district.
