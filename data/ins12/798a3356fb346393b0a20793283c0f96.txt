October 18, 2022 08:57 AM Eastern Daylight Time MONMOUTH JUNCTION, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CosmoLex, a leading practice management software provider for the legal industry, today announced its new CosmoLex CRM, legal-specific client relationship management tools built for modern law firms.



The addition of the CRM tools to CosmoLex's already robust suite of solutions for law firms creates a powerful all-in-one application designed to help lawyers both manage and scale their practice. The easy-to-use interface functions as a centralized database to track clients and prospects, makes messaging easy for any legal staff, and eliminates hours of manual data entry and recordkeeping.
