Steph Curry Consensus Favorite To Win Finals MVP
Jun 2, 2022 1:25 AM

Steph Curry is the consensus favorite to win his first NBA Finals MVP at sportsbooks across the country. Curry is currently even money at Caesars Sportsbook, with Jayson Tatum coming in with the second best odds at +180. Jaylen Brown has the third best odds at +1000. Klay Thompson at +1500 and Draymond Green at +1800 round out the top five. Curry has attracted 44% of the total money wagered at Caesars. Curry was most recently named the MVP of the Western Conference finals.
