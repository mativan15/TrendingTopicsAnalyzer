June 03, 2022 08:55 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW ORLEANS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ('KSF') and KSF partner, former Attorney General of Louisiana, Charles C. Foti, Jr., remind investors that they have until August 1, 2022 to file lead plaintiff applications in a securities class action lawsuit against Dentsply Sirona, Inc. (NasdaqGS: XRAY), if they purchased the Company's shares between June 9, 2021 and May 9, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'). This action is pending in the United States District Court for the Southern District of Ohio.
