My aim is to provide one lakh jobs: Tamil Nadu Chief Minister M K Stalin
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 15:15:00

Agencies Chief minister (CM) M K StalinChennai: Tamil Nadu Chief Minister M K Stalin set a target to provide one lakh jobs to youngsters every year and said on Saturday that ensuring employment delights a family. Also, he urged his Cabinet colleagues to organise job fairs in all the 234 Assembly constituencies of the State to overcome unemployment. Speaking at a fair here, he said, "Normally Ministers place a request to the Chief Minister. But, this time I would like to urge the Ministers to organise job fairs in Kolathur constituency from where I was elected and in all the Assembly constituencies in the State.
