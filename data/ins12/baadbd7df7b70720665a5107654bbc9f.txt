By Reuters Published: 22:49 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 22:49 EDT, 21 October 2022 Brayden Point scored twice, including a power-play goal with 2:52 left in overtime, leading the Tampa Bay Lightning to a 3-2 win over the Florida Panthers on Friday night in Sunrise, Fla. Steven Stamkos, who had Tampa Bay's other goal, got the primary assist on the game-winner, setting Point up in the slot. Nikita Kucherov added two primary assists for Tampa Bay, and Andrei Vasilevskiy made 35 saves for the win.
