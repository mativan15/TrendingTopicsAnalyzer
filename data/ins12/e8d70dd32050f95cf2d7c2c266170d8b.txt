Published: 22:14 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 22:14 EDT, 2 June 2022 Jennifer Lopez showed off her gym-honed frame in a pair of bright orange leggings, which clung to her every curve, while arriving to a dance studio on Thursday.As she put her hard work at the gym on full display, the 52-year-old singer sported a tie-dye hoodie, gold aviator sunglasses and her hair clipped up.While preparing to work up a sweat, the Love Don't Cost a Thing hitmaker carried a bedazzled, crystal-embellished reusable tumbler to stay hydrated.
