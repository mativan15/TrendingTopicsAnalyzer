New Delhi: Cloud kitchen unicorn Rebel Foods is planning to expand to 100 cities across India by the end of 2022-23, and increasing the brand's global reach, Karan Singla, VP and Head of operations (India Business) told ETRetail in an exclusive interview.On the company's global expansion plans, Singla said the brand currently operates in 10 countries in over 70 cities and aims to reach almost 20 countries in the coming year.
