LUBBOCK, Texas, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- South Plains Financial, Inc. (NASDAQ:SPFI) ('South Plains'), the parent company of City Bank, today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.12 per share of common stock. The dividend is payable on November 15, 2022 to shareholders of record as of the close of business on October 31, 2022.



About South Plains Financial, Inc.

South Plains is the bank holding company for City Bank, a Texas state-chartered bank headquartered in Lubbock, Texas.
