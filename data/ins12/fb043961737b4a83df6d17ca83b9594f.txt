It's possible to challenge your council tax if you think you're in the wrong band (Image: Getty Images/iStockphoto)A savvy dad has explained how he won back an impressive £910 in council tax by challenging his banding.It turns out that Ryan, 34, who lives in Manchester, had been paying too much council tax for over four years. His property was classed as Band B but after he challenged this, it was moved down to Band A.Speaking to the Mirror, Ryan shared that he used to pay roughly £1,400 a year for his council tax.
