Goldman Sachs Restructures Its Divisions, Elevating Tech Offerings
nytimes.com
news
2022-10-18 13:28:13

Goldman Sachs is taking another step away from its past as a clubby Wall Street partnership, adopting a new three-pronged structure that combines investment banking and trading into one unit, asset and wealth management into another and elevates its digital offerings into a third unit, the bank announced on Tuesday.

'Today, we enter the next phase of our growth, introducing a realignment of our businesses that will enable us to further capitalize on the predominant operating model of One Goldman Sachs as we better serve our clients,' Goldman's chief executive, David Solomon, said in a news release accompanying the bank's latest earnings report, referencing an internal initiative that began in 2020 to create a 'client-centric organizational structure.
