Bangalore Mirror Bureau / Updated: May 29, 2022, 06:00 ISTIn the wake of app services constantly cancelling rides, Bengalureans have to resort to taking autos (File)By Shreyashi Mukherjee




From rigged meters to shelling out extra money, citizens are left at the mercy of auto drivers The story of auto drivers in Namme Bengaluru harassing passengers by demanding extra money is a tale as old as time. While many choose to rely on cab app services, those who do dare to take an auto, are left at their mercy, and end up shelling extra for distances as short as two kilometres.
