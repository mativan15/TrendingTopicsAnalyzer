By CHEN BOWEN | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-20 21:01 Share - WeChat Zheng Li, head of the Publicity Department of CPC Sichuan provincial committee. [Photo/cpc.people.com] Sichuan has advanced high-quality development and has made headway in transferring itself into a hub of science and technology and a center for innovation factors, said Zheng Li, head of the Publicity Department of CPC Sichuan provincial committee, at a news conference on Wednesday afternoon. Zheng, also a member of the Sichuan provincial committee of the CPC, made the remarks at the conference during the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China.
