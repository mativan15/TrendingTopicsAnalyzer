Saudi Arabia Is Planning To Build The World's Largest Buildings In A New $500 Billion Project
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 13:10:13

In a new initiative, Saudi Arabia has revealed its plan to build the world's largest buildings in a mostly unpopulated part of the country as part of an entirely new $500 billion development called NEOM. According to a report by Bloomberg, NEOM is the brainchild of Saudi Crown Prince and de facto ruler Mohammed bin Salman who is aiming to build twin skyscrapers about 500 meters (1,640 feet) tall that stretch horizontally for dozens of miles, said people close to the project.  Middle East Eye (Mohammed bin Salman)The report adds that skyscrapers would house a mix of residential, retail and office space running from the Red Sea coast into the desert.
