SCOTTSDALE, Ariz., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Resideo Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: REZI), a leading global provider of home comfort and security solutions and distributor of commercial and residential security and audio-visual products, will release third quarter 2022 financial results after the close of the New York Stock Exchange on Tuesday, November 1, 2022. A conference call and simultaneous webcast to discuss the results will be held on Tuesday, November 1 at 5:00 p.m. ET.
Interested investors and other parties can listen to a webcast of the conference call by logging onto the Investor Relations section of Resideo's website at https://investor.
