Concentrix (NASDAQ:CNXC) Is Increasing Its Dividend To $0.275
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-16 12:12:20

The board of Concentrix Corporation (NASDAQ:CNXC) has announced that it will be paying its dividend of $0.275 on the 8th of November, an increased payment from last year's comparable dividend. This takes the annual payment to 0.9% of the current stock price, which unfortunately is below what the industry is paying.

Concentrix's Earnings Easily Cover The Distributions

If it is predictable over a long period, even low dividend yields can be attractive. Before making this announcement, Concentrix was easily earning enough to cover the dividend.
