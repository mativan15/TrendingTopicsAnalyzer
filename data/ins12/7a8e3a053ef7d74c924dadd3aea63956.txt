Records: San Jose State (4-1, 2-0 Mountain West); Fresno State (1-4, 0-1 MW)
Kickoff: 7:45 p.m., Valley Children's Stadium, Fresno
TV: Fox Sports 2
Radio: KTRB (860 AM) Series history: San Jose State trails 38-43-3 all-time, but has won five of the past nine meetings, most recently in 2019. Before this stretch, FSU had dominated the series, going 18-2, including 12 in a row from 1991-2005. SJSU lost at home to the Bulldogs in the final game of the 2021 season, costing the Spartans a chance to become bowl eligible in back-to-back years for the first time since 1986-87.
