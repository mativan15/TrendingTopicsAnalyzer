SABJ honors three CEOs with Lifetime Achievement Awards for their decades of philanthropic work
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 19:47:00

The San Antonio Business Journal has bestowed lifetime achievement awards to three local business leaders for their philanthropic work with local nonprofits and organizations.

The three honorees — Russ Bookbinder, Mike Gilliam and Harvey Najim — will be among 20 other companies and business leaders to be formally recognized by the Business Journals for their charitable efforts during a Nov. 17 Nonprofit & Corporate Philanthropy Awards program at Morgan's Wonderland.

The program's three lifetime achievement winners come from diverse business backgrounds.
