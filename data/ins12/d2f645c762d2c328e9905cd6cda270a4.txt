Boxing's inability to serve up the best fight is infuriating
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 09:00:00

Between Frank Warren's Queensberry Promotions, Eddie Hearn's Matchroom Boxing outfit and the Joshua's own management team 258Management, there was the opportunity to create a mega-event for British sports fans. Instead, Joshua's handlers have put business concerns over risk-taking, fearing that the needs of his commercial sponsors would not be adequately met.
And here is where boxing once again manages to shoot itself in the foot. The optics were bad enough two weeks ago when the sport would have acted sooner to call off the Conor Benn-Chris Eubank event after the former tested positive for a banned performance-enhancing drug but instead tried to push on with the event.
