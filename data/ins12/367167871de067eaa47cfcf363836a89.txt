Sensex takes a breather after 3-day rally, falls 400 points; Nifty below 16,600
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 04:28:00

Benchmark indices opened lower on Tuesday, as oil prices jumped following EU's decision to ban most Russian crude imports. US markets were shut overnight on account of Memorial Day and Chinese PMI reading released earlier today suggested factory activity there fell at a slower pace in May as Covid curbs in major manufacturing hubs, which restricted the market losses. At 9.25 am, the BSE Sensex was trading 410 points or 0.73 per cent lower at 55,515.45. Nifty50 was trading at 16,569.55, down 91.85 points or 0.
