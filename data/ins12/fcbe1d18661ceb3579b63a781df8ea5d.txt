Iran sanctions British organisations and individuals
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 20:15:00

AgenciesSanctions include a visa ban as well as the seizure of any "property and assets" in Iran.Tehran on Wednesday blacklisted several British institutions and individuals after London sanctioned Iran's morality police amid protests over the death of Mahsa Amini, a foreign ministry statement said. Among the seven institutions listed are Britain's National Cyber Security Centre and the Government Communications Headquarters intelligence organisation, known as GCHQ.
 Tehran said their actions have "led to riots, violence and terrorist acts against the Iranian nation".
