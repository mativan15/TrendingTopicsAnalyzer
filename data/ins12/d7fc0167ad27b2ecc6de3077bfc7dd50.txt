Oct. 15 officially marks "I Love Lucy Day" in honor of America's favorite redhead.The iconic sitcom premiered Oct. 15, 1951, and aired Monday nights until its 179th episode, which aired on May 6, 1957. According to the Lucy Desi Museum, there are 181 episodes including the pilot and a Christmas special. It was nominated for 21 Emmy Awards and won five times.The original cast went on to reprise their roles in "The Lucy-Desi Comedy Hour." A total of 13 episodes aired from 1957 until 1960.The series told the tale of a housewife who constantly tried to become a star in her bandleader husband's show.
