Family demanding man sell them home for what he paid 12 years ago bashed
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-22 03:03:09

A man was supported online when he said he wasn't willing to sell his mother and sister the house he bought when they offered him the same price he paid 12 years ago.The man, u/Puzzleheaded-Rush413, shared his side of the story to the popular Reddit forum r/AmITheA**hole, earning over 6,600 upvotes and 1,100 comments for his post, "[Am I the A**hole] for refusing to sell my starter home for under market value to my mother & sister?"The original poster (OP), 34, says he bought his 1,000 square-foot townhome when he was in his early 20s for $120,000.
