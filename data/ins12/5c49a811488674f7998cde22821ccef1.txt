October 17, 2022 11:04 PM Eastern Daylight Time RICHARDSON, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--FPT USA Corporation proudly announced its achievement as a Great Place To Work certified company amongst other distinguished workplaces. The news came in as it celebrated the 14th anniversary.



FPT USA Corporation (FPT USA), the IT company focusing on digital transformation and a subsidiary of the global IT company FPT Software, announced it has obtained the Great Place To Work certification in early October.

As a global authority on workplace culture and employee experience with trust leadership, Great Place To Work assessment is based on independent employees' survey answers about their company.
