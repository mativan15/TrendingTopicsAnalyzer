Southington Library Will Host Improv Lessons, Performance This Summer

Southington residents can try out improv comedy during workshops held at the town's library this summer. (Shutterstock)SOUTHINGTON, CT — The Southington Public Library has been awarded grant money to host improv comedy workshops for adults, teens and children this summer.The workshop series is called "The Literacy of Laughter: A Community Gets Schooled in the Art of Comedy," officials said, and the library is contracting with Sea Tea Improv to present it.In the sessions, participants will practice unscripted comedy — a skill library officials said can be used in everyday life, as it encourages listening and cooperation and fosters a sense of trust and community.
