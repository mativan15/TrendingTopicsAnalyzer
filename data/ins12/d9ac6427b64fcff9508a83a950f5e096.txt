Privatized medical labs coming to more Alberta communities

news
2022-06-02 19:40:00

DynaLife Medical Labs already operates in Edmonton and some northern communities and will upgrade its facilities and open more service centres. (Submitted by Biohubx)The Alberta government says a private laboratory service that collects blood and other samples for medical testing will be expanding its operations in the province.Health Minister Jason Copping says DynaLife Medical Labs, which already operates in Edmonton and some northern communities, will upgrade its facilities and open more service centres.
