Midyear raises to be paid in order to keep existing Naperville Park District staff, attract new employees
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-05-31 20:32:42

Naperville Park District employees will be getting 3% midyear raises to address a problem with staffing levels.One of the greatest challenges facing the district is filling vacant positions and retaining existing personnel, Executive Director Brad Wilson told the park board when introducing the district's three-year financial plan.Wilson said maintaining adequate staffing is a priority so the district can provide service levels the community expects at prices residents can afford.One area that has received the notice of residents is the appearance of the parks.
