October 17, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Teradata Corporation (NYSE: TDC) today announced that it will release its 2022 third quarter financial results after the market closes on Monday, November 7, 2022. Teradata will host a conference call and live webcast at 2 p.m. PT the same day to discuss the results and provide a business and financial update.

The live webcast, as well as a replay, will be available on the Investor Relations page of the Teradata website at investor.
