The Brazil international has endured a mixed start to the new season and although he has formed an impressive looking partnership with William Saliba, he has made several costly errors that have come close to undermining his side's near flawless star to the season.
As well as the mistakes against Fulham, Tottenham and Liverpool, Gabriel nearly cost his team maximum points against Leeds last weekend when he lashed out at Patrick Bamford in injury-time.
