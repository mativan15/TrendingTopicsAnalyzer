Community AccessBy Lynnsey SpaderOct 21, 2022On Sunday, October 30th there will be two large events to honor the fallen Bristol officers, Lt. Dustin DeMonte and Sgt. Alex Hamzy. Join us to help raise money for our heroic Bristol police officers and their families. FOOD, MUSIC, AND RAFFLES! Prize for best poker hand! Registration starts at 9:00 am at Yankee Harley-Davidson. Kickstands up at 10:30am. The last card is pulled by 3:00 pm at the Lincoln Room on Barlow Street. Additional cards will be pulled at Cadillac Ranch, Johnny B's, and Crabby Al's.
