October 18, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time COS COB, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Chicken Soup for the Soul Entertainment, Inc. (Nasdaq: CSSE, CSSEP, CSSEL, CSSEN), one of the largest operators of advertising-supported video-on-demand (AVOD) streaming services, today announced the timing for the payment of its declared regular monthly dividend of $0.2031 per share of its 9.75% Series A Cumulative Redeemable Perpetual Preferred Stock for November 2022. The dividend will be payable on or around November 15, 2022 to holders of record as of October 31, 2022.
