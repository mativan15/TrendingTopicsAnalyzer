Guardant Health (GH 8.20%), a diagnostic precision oncology testing company, saw its shares plummet 33.6% in May, according to data from S&P Global Market Intelligence. The stock closed at $61.70 a share on April 29, the last trading day of the month. It opened at $61.98 on Monday, May 2, then fell to a 52-week low of $27.65 on May 12, before responding a bit, closing out May at $40.98. For the year, the stock is down more than 61%.
