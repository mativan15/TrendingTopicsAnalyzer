Former OpenSea employee arrested for insider trading of NFTs
mashable.com
tech
2022-06-01 23:20:55

NFTs just made history, although perhaps not in the way their advocates would have liked.Disgraced former OpenSea product manager Nathaniel Chastain was arrested in New York City on Wednesday for insider trading of NFTs. In its statement, the Department of Justice called it the "first ever digital asset insider trading scheme."Chastain has been charged with one count of wire fraud and one count of money laundering as a result of his NFT insider trading scheme.NFTs, or non-fungible tokens, are digital assets on a blockchain that represent artwork or other forms of media.
