New Delhi: Despite a recovery in the equity markets, cryptos were ailing with altcoins hit even more badly. The negative news flow on the global stage and Terra's massacre are still haunting crypto investors. Bitcoin, which is considered the least risky of digital assets, held some ground, amid ongoing concerns about high inflation and economic downturn. Bitcoin's market cap has recently spiked relative to other cryptocurrencies. Barring the stablecoin USD Coin, all crypto tokens were trading sharply lower on Friday.
