Panthers' Robbie Anderson 'confused' about sideline ejection

Carolina Panthers wide receiver Robbie Anderson stuck around after the game to address the incidents he had with the coaching staff that led to him being kicked off the sidelines on Sunday.Anderson was seen getting into a few heated conversations with wide receivers coach Joe Dailey and interim head coach Steve Wilks as the team played the Los Angeles Rams. In the second half, Anderson was told to hit the showers, and he did not return. In the first half, he was spotted sitting away from his team.
