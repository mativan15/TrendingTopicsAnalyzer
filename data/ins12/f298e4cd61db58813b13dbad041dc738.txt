Spencer Platt / Staff via GettyMoveable explores the future of transportation, infrastructure, energy, and cities.Three big names in the nascent ultra-fast grocery delivery industry, which promises an array of items available at your doorstep in around 15 minutes or less, announced mass layoffs last week, putting the skids on an industry that just last year was being hyped as the next big thing in urban tech startups. Berlin-based Gorillas announced it laid off 300 office employees—about half the workforce—and shut down in four European countries.
