May 27, 2022 01:40 PM Eastern Daylight Time FORT WORTH, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ADM Endeavors, Inc. announced today that Irving ISD has awarded Just Right Products RFP #22-60-881 for the Purchase of Student School Uniforms. The start date of this award is May 25th, 2022 and stays in effect until May 24th 2023. The district has an optional annual renewal that could extend the award through May 24th, 2026.

About ADMQ: Since 2010, our wholly owned subsidiary, Just Right Products, Inc., has been consistently increasing sales, with sales topping $6.
