While many investors in the United States may not be immediately familiar with the name Stellantis (STLA 0.07%), they are almost certainly familiar with some of the companies within in its portfolio, Jeep, Dodge, and Ram, which sell some of the most popular vehicles in the United States. Stellantis is also home to some of Europe's largest automakers, such as Peugeot, Opel, and Fiat, and the luxurious Maserati. Stellantis came into being in early 2021 when Fiat Chrysler officially completed its merger with PSA Group (the parent company of Peugeot, Opel, and others).
