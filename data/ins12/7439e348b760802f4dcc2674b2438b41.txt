World Environment Day 2022: Punjab, Himachal to ban single-use plastic from July
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 12:15:00

Punjab and Himachal Pradesh governments on Sunday announced to impose a ban on single-use plastic from July. During a virtual state-level function to mark World Environment Day 2022, Secretary, Science, Technology and Environment Rahul Tewari announced the decision of the Punjab government for environment protection. He said the ban on single-use plastic will be imposed from July to make Punjab greener and healthier. Tewari said the state "government led by Chief Minister Bhagwant Mann is committed to make Punjab clean, green and pollution-free by walking on the path shown by our revered Gurus".
