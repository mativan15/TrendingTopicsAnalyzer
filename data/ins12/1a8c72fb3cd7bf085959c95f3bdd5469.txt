Mireya Acierto/Getty Images Entertainment Wheels Up Experience Inc. (NYSE:UP) stock notched its largest single-day gain since June after announcing a new financing arrangement on Monday. The private aviation company announced that is had issued equipment notes in the aggregate principal amount of $270M via an Enhanced Equipment Trust Certificates loan structure. The equipment notes are secured by a primary lien on selected company-owned aircraft and have a maturity of up to seven years and a coupon of 12%.
