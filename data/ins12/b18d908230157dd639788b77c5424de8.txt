'For my players, the expectation is that we're going to be there at the end,' said O'Brien, whose program has advanced to state final every year since 2004.'It's exciting. I know that my seniors are incredibly happy to be holding the sectional trophy.'Weston junior Zach Pelzar paced the Wildcats with a 1-under-par 71, winning the individual title by five strokes. Georgetown senior Logan Corriveau (76) finished second and Triton senior Ricky Gardella (78) placed third.Pelzar netted five birdies, including four on the back nine, highlighted by a daring approach on the 509-yard, par-4 18th.
