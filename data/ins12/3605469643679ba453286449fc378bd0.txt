Warning: The following article contains mild spoilers from 'Top Gun: Maverick.'The mission for Tom Cruise's Pete 'Maverick' Mitchell and his ace team of young Navy fighter pilots is clear: to take out a nuclear enrichment facility. The risks are even clearer: perilous terrain, anti-aircraft systems, adversaries flying cutting-edge stealth jets.There's just one thing that is not clear in 'Top Gun: Maverick' — and it happens to be a pretty important thing: Who exactly is the enemy? In 'Top Gun: Maverick,' that question is left intentionally fuzzy, with the film offering vague and contradictory hints about the antagonist that don't quite add up.
