AgenciesDr Trehan also said if the heart disease is already in the family then one must get tests done timely.With singer KK's death due to a cardiac arrest leaving the country in a shock, top experts warned about India's predisposition to heart diseases which is 2-3 times higher in percentage in comparison to western countries.
