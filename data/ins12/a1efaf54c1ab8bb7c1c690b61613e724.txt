Americans are still saying 'I quit' in near-record numbers, showing that the Forever Resignation is sticking around
Insider
business
2022-06-01 18:36:01

The economy has taken a host of twists and turns over the past year, but one trend has consistently stuck around: Everyone is quitting their jobs.In April 2022, 4.4 million Americans put in their resignation letters, according to the latest data release from the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Preliminary data for April shows there were about 25,000 fewer quits in April than in March. Job openings also declined in April 2022, falling from 11.9 million in March to 11.4 million in April, bringing them still close to record highs.
