Licence fee cheque bounces, Chandigarh police book former parking contractor

The contractor was formerly managing 25 paid parking lots and the multi-level parking in Sector 17 in Chandigarh. (HT Photo)
 

Police have booked a former parking contractor, who was managing city's 25 paid parking lots and Sector-17 multi-level parking, after a cheque issued by him to MC for payment of pending licence fee bounced.According to Jagdeep Singh, sub-divisonal engineer (SDE), municipal corporation (MC), the civic body had allotted 25 paid parking lots and the multi-level parking in Sector 17 to Sunil Chander Lal Purswani of Arya Toll Infra limited, Sector 8, on June 16, 2017.
