This story is part of Focal Point iPhone 2022, CNET's collection of news, tips and advice around Apple's most popular product. 


Apple's iPhone 14, iPhone 14 Plus and iPhone 14 Pro phones are all now available. With that in mind, you may be wondering: Should you upgrade to the iPhone 14? Above all else, the answer depends on which phone you currently own. If you have a recent model like the iPhone 13 or 12, you should wait. If your phone is older though, it's worth figuring out how much you'll gain by jumping to a newer iPhone.
