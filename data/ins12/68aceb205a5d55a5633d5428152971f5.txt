Delhi CM to Union minister Shah: How much funds did Centre give corporations?

NEW DELHI: Reacting to Union home minister Amit Shah's comments on Thursday that the state government had withheld Rs 40,000 crore from the municipal corporations of Delhi to stymie development work, chief minister Arvind Kejriwal retorted that BJP should not make excuses for the failure of the civic bodies and wondered how much money the central government had given to the corporations for development of the city."These people (BJP) couldn't do anything in 15 years. They should step aside; they are incompetent.
