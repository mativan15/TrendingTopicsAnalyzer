Revamp Moto raises over $1 million USD in pre-seed round of funding
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-01 04:30:18

Revamp Moto is developing new electric two-wheelers for India's small scale businesses.
 








Revamp Moto informed that the new funds will be used to boost its product development program.
 

Revamp Moto has announced that it has raised over $1 Million USD in a pre-seed found round. The company added that the funding round was led by Veda VC and Venture Catalysts and also witnessed participation from IDBI Capital along with a range of angel investors. 
The EV startup communicated that it is developing new electric two-wheelers especially for India's small scale businesses and semi-urban markets.
