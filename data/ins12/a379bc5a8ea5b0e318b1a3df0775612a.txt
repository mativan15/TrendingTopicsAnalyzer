Hospitals Erase $8 Billion as Tenet, HCA Results Disappoint

2022-10-21 15:57:19

(Bloomberg) -- Stocks of hospital operators are plunging on Friday after earnings reports from Tenet Healthcare Corp. and industry-giant HCA Healthcare Inc. underwhelmed investors, wiping out more than $8 billion in market value across the group.

Most Read from Bloomberg

Tenet plunged by as much as 32%, the most intraday since November 2008, after its net operating revenue guidance for the year was trimmed, taking the outlook below the average view on Wall Street. HCA fell as much as 15% after its third-quarter results included revenue that narrowly missed consensus expectations.
