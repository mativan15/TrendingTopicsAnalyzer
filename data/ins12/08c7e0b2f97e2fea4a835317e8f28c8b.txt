Published: 17:43 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 18:52 EDT, 15 October 2022 Frenchman Antoine Griezmann struck early in the second half to earn Atletico Madrid a 1-0 win at Athletic Bilbao, moving them above their opponents into third place in LaLiga on Saturday.Atletico now have 19 points from nine games, overhauling Bilbao who slipped to fourth on 17 points.Leaders Barcelona and Real Madrid, both on 22 points, face each other in the Clasico at the Bernabeu on Sunday. Antoine Griezmann helped Atletico Madrid earn a 1-0 win against Athletic Bilbao on SaturdayAfter a first half of very few chances at San Mames, Atletico took the lead through Griezmann in the 47th minute when the forward received a neat pass from Alvaro Morata and sent a low first-time shot past goalkeeper Unai Simon.
