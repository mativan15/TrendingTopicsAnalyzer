Minneapolis to pay $600K to journalist blinded in one eye by police projectile after George Floyd's death
startribune.com
news
2022-05-27 21:04:19

The Minneapolis City Council this week approved a $600,000 payout to a freelance journalist who was blinded in one eye by a police projectile while covering protests after the killing of George Floyd. Photojournalist Linda Tirado filed a federal lawsuit nearly two years ago accusing the city and police officials of conspiring to deprive journalists of their constitutional rights during the unrest. Tirado's case is the second such settlement approved by the council this month. Earlier in May, the City Council approved the payment of $1.
