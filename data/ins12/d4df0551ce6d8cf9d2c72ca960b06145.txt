October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--IRONSCALES, the leader in AI-powered email security and the fastest growing email security company in the world, today announced the results of a new study conducted by Osterman Research to quantify the direct costs borne by organizations in mitigating phishing threats, and to explore expectations about how phishing will change over the next 12 months. The report includes survey responses from more than 250 IT and security practitioners.
