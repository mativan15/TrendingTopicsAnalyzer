By Zhao Shiyue | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-06-01 17:38 Diane Wang, founder, chairperson and CEO of DHgate Group, attends a panel discussion at the 2022 BRICS Women's Leadership Forum during the BRICS Women Innovation Contest in Beijing on May 31, 2022. [Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn] Digital technology and services have brought unprecedented opportunities for women in employment and significantly lowered the threshold of starting a business and acquiring resources, with "she-power" gaining broader room for growth in the era of the digital economy, said Diane Wang, a member of the China chapter of the BRICS Women's Business Alliance, in an exclusive interview with China Daily.
