Ukraine war: Why Putin wants help from India, China on oil purchase
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-31 17:30:00

(This story originally appeared in on May 31, 2022)President Vladimir Putin may need to count even more on India and China after the European Union agreed to ban Russian oil in the bloc's toughest move against Moscow since the February 24 invasion. From the moment Russia invaded Ukraine, the West has sought to hit Moscow's lucrative energy sector to cut off funding for its war.

However, any such move was a double-edged sword, especially in Europe, which relies on Russia for 25% of its oil and 40% of its natural gas.
