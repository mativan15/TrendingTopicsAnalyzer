Mark Zuckerberg fires another salvo at Apple, says WhatsApp more secure than iMessage
indiatimes.com

2022-10-19 06:00:00

AFPZuckerberg said that WhatsApp has numerous features that are still to feature on Apple's iMessage.A week after an all-out attack on Apple, Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg is back in the news. The billionaire has fired a fresh salvo at the Cupertino-based tech giant. This time the 38-year-old technocrat took to his Facebook account to target Apple over iMessage. In his latest attack, Zuckerberg said that WhatsApp owned by Meta is more private and secure than iMessage and that it comes with end-to-end encryption that works across iOS and Android devices including group chat.
