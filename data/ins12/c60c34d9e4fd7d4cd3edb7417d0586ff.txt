CHALLENGE: Mark 'Billy' Billingham (Image: PA Images)Some may also know that the highly decorated former special forces soldier worked as a bodyguard for Hollywood stars including Tom Cruise, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, after leaving the SAS, which he refers to as The Regiment.
