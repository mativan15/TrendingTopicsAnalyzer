MUMBAI, India , Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ajanta Pharma, a specialty pharmaceutical formulation company, has been awarded as "Best Managed Companies" in India by Deloitte for 2022.The selection process for the award had rigorous evaluation of management abilities for Strategy; Capabilities and Innovation; Governance and Financials; Culture and Commitment. It once again confirms Ajanta's agile management, resilient strategy and superior performance.
