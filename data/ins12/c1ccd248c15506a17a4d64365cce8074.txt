Many cannabis-sector stocks are trading down Friday. But Aurora Cannabis (ACB -35.90%) is leading the way after it plunged as much as 41%. As of 12:24 p.m. ET, Aurora shares were still down 38.8%. At the same time, Tilray (TLRY -0.40%) shares were down 3.6%, and Hexo (HEXO -3.08%) stock was down 5.3%.
So what
The plunge in Aurora shares comes as existing shareholders react to a new round of financing that the company said will bring $150 million to the company. The funding comes from a bought deal financing agreement with underwriters for 61.
