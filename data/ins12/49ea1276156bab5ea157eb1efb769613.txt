By Reuters Published: 16:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 16:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 MADRID, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Spanish swimming pool equipment maker Fluidra said on Tuesday it was downgrading its financial guidance for 2022 after a weaker-than-anticipated third quarter. Fluidra now expects full-year sales for be around 2.4 billion euros ($2.4 billion), down from a previous guidance of between 2.5 billion and 2.6 billion euros. The expected earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (EBITDA) for the full year is expected to be over 500 million euros, compared with a previous expectation of between 600 million and 630 million euros.
