Somerville has been ranked one of the top 50 Best Places to Live in the U.S., according to a new ranking by the online financial magazine 'Money.'​ (Jenna Fisher/Patch)SOMERVILLE, MA — Somerville has been ranked one of the top 50 Best Places to Live in the U.S., according to a new ranking by the online financial magazine 'Money.' The city ranked seventh on Money's 2022-2023 list and is just one of two Massachusetts communities that made the list. The other is Milton, ranking at number 17. Money cites Somerville's music and culture, proximity to Boston and great public transportation, low crime rates, and well-rated school district and higher education institutions as the reasons for the high rating.
