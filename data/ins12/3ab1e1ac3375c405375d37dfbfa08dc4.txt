2022 Colorado Inno Awards winner Pie Insurance doubles venture capital in 2022
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 21:57:00

Pie Insurance is the winner of the 2022 Colorado Inno award in the fintech category.

Pie Insurance raised $315 million in September, more than doubling its total capital and snagging one of the largest financing rounds this year among all U.S.-based property and casualty insurtech companies. Now, it's looking to expand into new lines of business and become a full-stack carrier.
