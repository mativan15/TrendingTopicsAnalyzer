October 18, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Solonis, a leading provider of modern property management solutions, today released its 2022 State of the Industry Report. The report offers insight into guest expectations, travel sentiment, and how hotels can adapt to cater to guests into 2023.



Travel experienced a considerable upswing in 2022 as guests became more comfortable staying in hotels again. However, this increase in travel is viewed with caution as rising inflation increases prices.
