Garcetti signs Declaration of Local Emergency over baby formula shortage
cbsnews.com
news
2022-06-03 22:49:00

By CBSLA Staff June 3, 2022 / 3:49 PM / CBS Los Angeles On Friday, Los Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti signed a Declaration of Local Emergency regarding the baby formula shortage. According to a press release, the declaration was made as a precautionary measure to ensure the city can respond to any future supply shortages and address potential predatory business activity. A number of other city officials, including City Attorney and candidate for mayor of LA Mike Feuer and Council President Nury Martinez, also joined in the signing.
