Karnataka Power Transmission Corporation Limited recruitment scam: 1 more held
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 07:15:00

BELAGAVI: Police have arrested another accused in the Karnataka Power Transmission Corporation Limited ( KPTCL ) recruitment scam and produced him before the JMFC court in Gokak . With this the number of arrested accused reached 23.Bhimappa Siddappa Gudigoppa, 30, of Upparatti village in Gokak taluk is the accused. According to police sources, Bhimappa is the one who supplied money to purchase smart electronic devices including smart watches and Bluetooth devices from a Delhi-based vendor and provided them to candidates writing examinations.
