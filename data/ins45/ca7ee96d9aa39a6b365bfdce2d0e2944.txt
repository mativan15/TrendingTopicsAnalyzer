BENTONVILLE, Ark., October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Back by customer demand, Walmart is announcing the return of its month-long savings event, "Black Friday Deals for Days." Like the past two years, Walmart will spread the excitement of Black Friday savings over three events throughout November and cap off a month of deals with Cyber Monday, delivering customers great prices on the must-have gifts of the season.
