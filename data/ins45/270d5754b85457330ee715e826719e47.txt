Sen. Dick Durbin says federal gun legislation could be approved after Texas school shooting
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-06-02 18:58:33

U.S. Sen. Dick Durbin told an Elgin gathering Wednesday he was cautiously optimistic some sort of federal gun legislation could happen in the aftermath of last week's school shooting in Uvalde, Texas.'What happened was heartbreaking and outrageous,' Durbin said of the deaths of 19 students and two teachers at the hands of an 18-year-old man.Durbin made his comments while in Elgin to announce $250,000 in federal funding for an environmental restoration study of the Fox River.The longtime senator said he has a long list of things he'd like to see done.
