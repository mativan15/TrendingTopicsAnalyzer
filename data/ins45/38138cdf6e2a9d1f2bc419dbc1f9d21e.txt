M'sian news portal partners with international artists to boost art tourism via NFTs
mashable.com
news
2022-06-03 04:45:00

> Life Malaysian news portal Free Malaysia Today (FMT) is on a mission to boost art tourism in the Southeast Asian nation. To turn this into reality, FMT collaborated with Mart Expo – a collective of artists worldwide – to provide opportunities to artists to showcase their works via Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs). The FMT-Mart Expo will promote over 500 artworks – mostly consisting of paintings and calligraphy – produced online and offline by some 100 artists. 50 percent of the artists selected will be Malaysians.
