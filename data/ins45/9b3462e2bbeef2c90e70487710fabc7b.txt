CALGARY, Alberta (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 ZS2 Technologies achieves 70% reduction in carbon footprint after full Lifecycle Analysis SCS Global Services completes Environmental Product Declaration for ZS2 TechPanels ZS2 Technologies, Canada's leading sustainable building materials developer and manufacturer, achieved an Environmental Products Declaration with a 70 per cent reduction in CO2 when compared to traditional tilt up concrete construction. SCS Global Services,—a global leader in independent, third party environmental and sustainability verification—provided the full Lifecycle Analysis for ZS2 TechPanels, and they are now included in their Certified Green Products Guide.
