SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 17, 2022/PRNewswire/ -- As contract lifecycle management (CLM) continues to play a vital role in the digital transformation of agreements, DocuSign (NASDAQ:DOCU) was today named a Leader in the 2022 Magic Quadrant for CLM report by Gartner, Inc.DocuSign named a Leader in the 2022 Magic Quadrant for CLM for the third year in a row!This is the third year in a row that DocuSign has been recognized as a Leader, this year placing highest among the 18 vendors evaluated on the "ability to execute" axis, and second furthest on the "completeness of vision" axis.
