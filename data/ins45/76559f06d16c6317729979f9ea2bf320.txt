Manchester United manager Erik ten Hag has said that his side's busy fixture list is the reason why he substituted Cristiano Ronaldo off against Newcastle. The forward cut a frustrated figure as he left the pitch, with Marcus Rashford coming on in his place.Asked why he made the substitution after the game, Ten Hag said: 'We have to cover four games in ten days. I want to keep the strikers fresh. We have to rotate there. Rashy didn't have energy for the whole game so we have to plan it.'We'll be bringing you the very latest updates, pictures and video on this breaking news story.
