Here's why it may be time to break up with your bank
washingtonpost.com
business
2022-06-01 11:00:19

The Federal Reserve has signaled that more interest rate hikes are coming this summer. That's worrisome if you're borrowing money. But if you're a saver, it's welcome news because it means earning more on the money you have parked at a bank.Since the Great Recession, stockpiling money at a bank or credit union has almost felt like depositing your money in your mattress, given the minuscule interest rates financial institutions have been paying for funds in a checking or savings account.Then high inflation hit.
