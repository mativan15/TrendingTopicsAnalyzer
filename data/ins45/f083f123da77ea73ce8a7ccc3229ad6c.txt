Third Point Takes Stake In Colgate-Palmolive, Urges Pet Food Spinoff
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-18 18:48:30

Colgate toothpaste is pictured on sale at a grocery store in Pasadena, California January 30, 2014. 
Daniel Loeb's Third Point has built a significant position in toothpaste maker Colgate-Palmolive Co and sees value in a potential spinoff of its Hill's Pet Nutrition business and other brands, the activist investor said in a letter seen by Reuters.The investor letter did not disclose the size of Third Point's stake, but cited several reasons for investing in the consumer goods company, including its pricing power in inflationary conditions and the strength in its pet food business.
