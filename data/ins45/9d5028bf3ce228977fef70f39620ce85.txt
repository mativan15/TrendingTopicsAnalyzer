For the second year in a row, the Jeep® brand-supported team of Nena Barlow and Teralin Petereit piloted their Jeep Wrangler 4xe to the overall title, bone stock and electrified class wins at the 2022 Rebelle Rally. The team's Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 4xe was also the top-scoring electrified vehicle.
