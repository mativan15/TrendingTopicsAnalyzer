Hasbro: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-18 10:39:50

PAWTUCKET, R.I. (AP) _ Hasbro Inc. (HAS) on Tuesday reported third-quarter profit of $129.2 million.

On a per-share basis, the Pawtucket, Rhode Island-based company said it had profit of 93 cents. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs and amortization costs, were $1.42 per share.

The results missed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of six analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.53 per share.

The toy maker posted revenue of $1.68 billion in the period, which met Street forecasts.
