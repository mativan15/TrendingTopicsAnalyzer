Amer Movil: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-18 23:32:57

MEXICO CITY (AP) _ Amer Movil (AMX) on Tuesday reported third-quarter earnings of $888.4 million.

On a per-share basis, the Mexico City-based company said it had net income of 28 cents. Earnings, adjusted to account for discontinued operations, came to 23 cents per share.

The telecommunications company posted revenue of $10.6 billion in the period.

Amer Movil shares have declined 23% since the beginning of the year. In the final minutes of trading on Tuesday, shares hit $16.35, a drop of slightly more than 9% in the last 12 months.
