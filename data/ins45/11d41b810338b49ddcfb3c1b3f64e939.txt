Followed categories will be added to My News.A new funding boost of $130 million into the Queensland housing sector will create better stability for vulnerable tenants, according to the state government. Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk announced on Thursday at the Queensland Housing Summit her government will double the size of its Housing Investment Fund by injecting $130 million each year into the state's housing sector. The Queensland Housing Summit hosted in Brisbane on Thursday welcomed government, private and public sector stakeholders to discuss the housing crisis facing the state.
