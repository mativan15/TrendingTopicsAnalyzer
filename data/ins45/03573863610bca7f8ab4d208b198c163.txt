Tesla is set to report earnings after the bell
cnbc.com
news
2022-10-19 19:21:30

Key Points

Tesla reports third-quarter earnings after the bell on Wednesday.
Wall Street was expecting earnings per share of $0.99 (adjusted) and revenue of $21.96 billion, according to a consensus of analyst estimates compiled by Refinitiv.
CEO Elon Musk is in the midst of a distracting, $44 billion acquisition of Twitter, the social network that is an important promotional tool for Tesla.

Tesla is expected to report third-quarter earnings for 2022 after the bell on Wednesday.

CEO Elon Musk does not speak on every earnings call, but plans to do so on Wednesday he said on Twitter.
