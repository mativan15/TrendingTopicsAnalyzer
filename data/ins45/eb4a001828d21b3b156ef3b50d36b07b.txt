Stoking yet another controversy, yoga guru Ramdev on Sunday alleged that Bollywood superstar Salman Khan consumes drugs and also recalled the drug-related incident involving Shah Rukh Khan's son Aryan Khan. While launching a movement against drugs, he went on to claim that drugs are "all around the film industry" and "in politics too". Video clips showing Ramdev make the explosive comments have gone viral on social media. While speaking in the Aryaveer and Veerangana conference in Moradabad on Saturday, he said, "Salman Khan takes drugs, I do not know about Aamir Khan.
