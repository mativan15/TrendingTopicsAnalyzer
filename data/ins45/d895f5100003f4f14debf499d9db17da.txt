12 minutes ago12 minutes agoGerman Chancellor Olaf Scholz has reportedly backed a deal that would see Chinese shipping company Cosco acquire a stake in the Port of Hamburg. The opposition Christian Democrats has labeled the move a "fatal mistake." German Chancellor Olaf Scholz came under fire from both allies and the opposition on Thursday over a reported plan for Chinese investment in the Port of Hamburg.  The deal, which public broadcasters NDR and WDR say is being promoted by Scholz, will allow Chinese shipping firm Cosco to acquire a major stake in a container terminal at the port.
