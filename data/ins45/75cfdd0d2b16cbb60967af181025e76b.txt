Perquisites of modular kitchens in remote area living: Challenges and solutions
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 11:15:26

With the evolving consumer preferences and increasing awareness of modernised living, today the demand for the modular kitchen concept is not limited to the urban areas but has successfully penetrated the remote areas of India involving tier II and III cities. These markets are unlocking enormous growth opportunities for the modular kitchen market which is expected to grow further with the customer's rising aspirational inclination towards home aesthetics inspired by global home trends, higher purchasing power and growing real estate development in smaller cities.
