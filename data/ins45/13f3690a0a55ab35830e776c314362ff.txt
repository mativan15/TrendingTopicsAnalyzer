Each of us must make the decision either to self-insure for our long-term care or get long-term care insurance. Periodically, insurance salespeople have set up billboards suggesting that if your financial advisor does not recommend long-term care insurance you should sue them. However, we are not fans of long-term care insurance. In the fantasy of long-term care insurance, for a small premium now you can insure against high medical costs later.
