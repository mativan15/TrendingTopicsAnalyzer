Financial confidence declines amid increased inflation, recession concerns: survey
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-10-18 23:48:51

Financial confidence among U.S. adults declined this quarter amid increased concerns about inflation and a possible recession, according to a survey recently put out by BMO Financial Group. BMO Financial Group said in late September that its Real Financial Progress Index found just over half of Americans (54%) reported making financial progress in the third quarter, an 8% drop from a year ago.
