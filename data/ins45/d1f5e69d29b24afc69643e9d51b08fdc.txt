Bengaluru: ISRO chairman inaugurates new spacecraft manufacturing facility
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-01 15:54:55

Dr S Somanath, the chairman of Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), Wednesday inaugurated ANANTH Technologies' spacecraft manufacturing unit at Karnataka Industrial Area Development Board (KIADB) Aerospace Park.
The new state-of-the-art spacecraft manufacturing facility can conduct assembly integration and testing of four large spacecraft simultaneously.
