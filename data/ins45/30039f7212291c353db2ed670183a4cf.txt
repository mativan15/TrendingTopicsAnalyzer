Texas Tech was given a No. 3 seed and will play No. 2 seed Notre Dame in the first round of the NCA A baseball tournament's Statesboro Regional.

The Red Raiders (37-20) and the Fighting Irish (35-14) will play Friday at a time to be determined. Georgia Southern (40-18) is the No. 1 seed and the regional host, doing so for the first time, and will play UNC Greensboro (34-28) in the other first-round game.
