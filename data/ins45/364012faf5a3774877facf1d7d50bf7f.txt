Published: 13:13 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 13:16 EDT, 30 May 2022 William and Kate will visit Cardiff on Saturday as part of the Platinum Jubilee celebrations, while Harry and Meghan are to 'meet the Queen as they celebrate Lilibet's first birthday' more than 130 miles away.The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge will meet performers and crew involved in a special Platinum Jubilee Celebration Concert taking place within the grounds of Cardiff Castle, Buckingham Palace has confirmed.The couple will view rehearsals and meet some of the acts taking part in the celebrations, including Bonnie Tyler and Owain Wynn Evans.
