Published: 08:35 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 08:36 EDT, 17 October 2022 Nicola Sturgeon today claimed the UK is 'fundamentally on the wrong path' as the SNP leader continued her fresh push for Scottish independence.The Scottish First Minister insisted an independent Scotland would be able to have its own Scottish pound and dismissed suggestions that Scots would be required to use passports to get into England as 'utter nonsense'.But Ms Sturgeon admitted there would be other border checks between Scotland and England.
