Munster v Bulls, Thomond Park, Saturday, 7.35pm – Live on: TG4 & Premier SportsWhen the Munster coaches, players and supporters cast a glance over the first five rounds of this season's URC they could hardly have envisaged this game would have left them in such desperate need for a win. However, that is the consequence of three defeats in their opening four matches.As a result, Munster sit 12th on six points. To put this in context, in the corresponding four games last season – ie at home to the Dragons and Cardiff, away to Zebre and away to Connacht – Munster picked up 16 points in ultimately finishing sixth.
