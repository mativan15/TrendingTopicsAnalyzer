Stock futures rise following last week's bounce
cnbc.com
news
2022-05-30 22:15:39

Traders on the floor of the NYSE, May 23, 2022.U.S. stock futures rose on Monday night, as Wall Street tried to build on last week's momentum.Dow Jones Industrial Average futures rose by 59 points, or 0.2%. S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 futures climbed 0.4% and 0.8%, respectively. The U.S. stock market didn't open Monday due to the Memorial Day holiday.Those moves follow the best weekly gains for the Dow and S&P 500 since November 2020.The Dow closed up 6.2% for the week, ending an eight-week losing streak.
