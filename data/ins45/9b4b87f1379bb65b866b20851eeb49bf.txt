June 02, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TROY, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--J.D. Power, a global leader in data analytics and consumer intelligence, today announced it has acquired the finance and insurance (F&I) menu and reporting software business of Tail Light, a leading automotive software company. Tail Light's F&I menu and reporting software is used by automotive dealerships and insurance providers to streamline vehicle financing and service contract configuration and reporting. The acquisition follows the 2021 acquisition of Superior Integrated Solutions, Inc.
