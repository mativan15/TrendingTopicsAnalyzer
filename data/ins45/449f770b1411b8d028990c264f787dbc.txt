China is about to hold the most important meeting in its political calendar, the five-yearly Communist Party congress, where President Xi Jinping is expected to gain an unprecedented third term as the country's leader.

Closely watched across the world to gauge the direction Beijing will take in the next half-decade, this year's congress, the 20th in its more than 100-year history, opens on Sunday.
