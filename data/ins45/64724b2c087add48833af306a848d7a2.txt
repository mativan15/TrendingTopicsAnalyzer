Malabar Gold & Diamonds to invest Rs 750 crore to set up manufacturing unit in Telangana; expects to generate 2750 jobs
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 15:45:00

New Delhi: Malabar Gold & Diamonds, one of the largest jewellery retailers in the country, on Saturday said it laid the foundation of its largest manufacturing unit in Telangana and will invest Rs 750 crore to set up this facility and a refinery.It will be built on 2.3 lakh square feet area at General Park, Maheshwaram in Rangareddy District.According to the company, it will have the capacity to manufacture 10 tonnes gold jewellery and 1.5 lakh carats of diamond jewellery annually. Its refinery will have an annual gold refining capacity of 180 tonnes.
