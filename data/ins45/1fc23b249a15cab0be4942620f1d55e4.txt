October 21, 2022 04:16 PM Eastern Daylight Time WILMINGTON, Del.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Global Indemnity Group, LLC (NYSE:GBLI) ('GBLI'), today announced that David S. Charlton, Chief Executive Officer, and Reiner R. Mauer, Chief Operations Officer, are no longer officers or directors of GBLI (including its subsidiaries).

GBLI's board of directors appointed Joseph W. Brown, 73, as its Chief Executive Officer. Mr. Brown has served as a GBLI director since December 2015 and will remain on GBLI's board of directors.
