COIMBATORE: Swathi Sree T , 25, of Coimbatore, who got the 42nd all-India rank in the UPSC civil services exams, is the state topper in Tamil Nadu . The results were announced on Monday.Swathi Sree cleared the exam for the second time out of her three attempts. While she could not clear the main in the first attempt, she secured the 126th rank in the second attempt. She was allotted IRS cadre then.Hailing from Ooty, her family has settled in Thoppampatti Pirivu near Thudiyalur in Coimbatore.
