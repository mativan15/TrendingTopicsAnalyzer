Parsec Capital Acquisitions to Merge With Enteractive Media
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 12:44:00

By Chris Wack Parsec Capital Acquisitions Corp. said it entered into a merger agreement with Enteractive Media Inc. of Calgary, Alberta, last Thursday. Parsec, a special purpose acquisition corporation, said the deal is expected to be consummated in the fourth quarter. Enteractive Media is a business that acts as the gateway between consumers and gambling operators. Enteractive Media's wholly owned PlayerVision subsidiary provides consumers with gambling-themed television broadcasts, on-demand video, and synchronous live video-streamed sports-wagering programming.
