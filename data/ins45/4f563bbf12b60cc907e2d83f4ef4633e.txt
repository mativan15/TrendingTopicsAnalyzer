Anna May Wong Will Be 1st Asian American Featured on US Currency
nbcmiami.com
news
2022-10-18 18:36:28

The trailblazing Chinese American actress Anna May Wong will be the first Asian American featured on U.S. currency as part of a program that includes notable women on American quarters. Wong, who is considered the first Chinese American film star in Hollywood, is the fifth and final woman to be individually featured on a quarter this year. The quarter, which will enter general circulation on Monday, will feature President George Washington on one side and Wong on the other.  United States Mint A quarter featuring Anna May Wong.
