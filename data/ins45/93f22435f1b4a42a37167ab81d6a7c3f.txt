Vidyut Jammwal, Arjun Rampal and Jacqueline Fernandez will be starring in extreme sports action movie 'Crakk'. Directed by Aditya Datt, the film is scheduled to hit the screens in 2023.Presented by Reliance Entertainment and produced by Action Hero Films & PZ Pictures, 'Crakk' will be jointly produced by Vidyut, Parag Sanghvi, and Action Hero Films.Actor-producer Vidyut said: "Looking at the current scenario and the way the audiences have changed, I have realised that everyone puts limits on everything they do, and it spreads in work, and the environment.
