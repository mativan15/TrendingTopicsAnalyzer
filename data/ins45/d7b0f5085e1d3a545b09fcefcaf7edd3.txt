A parking ticket is seen under the windshield wiper of a vehicle. HRM is launching a new program that will waive municipal parking tickets for people who spend $35 at a local business. (Eric Woolliscroft/CBC)People who get a parking ticket in the Halifax Regional Municipality can spend $35 at a local business to have the ticket waived, officials said Wednesday.The program runs June 1 to Sept. 30 and is designed to help businesses recover from pandemic closures, HRM spokesperson Laura Wright said.
