SJVN inks pact to supply 200 MW solar power to discoms in Bihar
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-27 13:30:00

ReutersThe SJVN is adding significant renewable projects to its portfolio to pursue this vision. State-run on Friday said it has signed a power purchase agreement (PPA) for supply of 200 MW from its solar power projects in Bihar to state discoms. Nand Lal Sharma, Chairman & Managing Director, SJVN informed in a statement that SJVN has signed a long-term Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) for 200 MW Grid Connected Solar Power Project in Bihar. According to the statement, the Power Purchase Agreement has been signed between SJVN & Bihar State Power Holding Company Ltd (BSPHCL) along with its two DISCOMs -- North Bihar Power Distribution Company Ltd (NBPDCL) and South Bihar Power Distribution Company Ltd (SBPDCL).
