One basis point is 0.01 percentage point.Mumbai: Hinduja-promoted IndusInd Bank's second-quarter net profit increased 57% due to strong growth in both interest and fee incomes even as provisions declined, reflecting an improvement in asset quality. Net profit increased to ₹1,805 crore in the quarter ended September from ₹1,147 crore a year ago, on the back of an 18% loan growth.
