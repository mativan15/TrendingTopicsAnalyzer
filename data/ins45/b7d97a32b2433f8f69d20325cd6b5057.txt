US Senator Lindsey Graham must testify before a special grand jury investigating whether then-president Donald Trump and others illegally tried to influence the 2020 election in Georgia, a federal appeals court has ruled. A district attorney wants to ask Senator Graham about phone calls he made to Georgia Secretary of State Brad Raffensperger in the weeks after the electionSenator Lindsey Graham has denied any wrongdoing and challenged his subpoenaThe investigation comes after a recording of a phone call between Donald Trump and Mr Raffensperger was made publicThe ruling by a three-judge panel of the 11th US Circuit Court of Appeals paves the way for Fulton County District Attorney Fani Willis to bring Senator Graham in for questioning.
