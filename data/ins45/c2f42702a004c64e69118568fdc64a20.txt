 Waterloo Regional Police are looking for three suspects following a home invasion in Waterloo, Ont.
Officers were called to a home in the are of Keats Way and Leighland Drive at around 6 p.m. Wednesday for a report of a robbery.They found one occupant of the home with non-life-threatening injuries.Investigators say three men forced their way into the home brandishing handguns and stole personal property before fleeing. No descriptions of the suspects were provided.
