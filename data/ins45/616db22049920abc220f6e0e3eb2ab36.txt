Published: 07:38 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 07:41 EDT, 19 October 2022 Over the years, stars such as Hailey Bieber, Judd Apatow, Leslie Mann and Kylie Jenner have been called out publicly for their poor treatment of serving staff at bars and restaurants.And Monday saw James Corden join the list after he was lambasted online by the owner of NYC restaurant Balthazar, Keith McNally, for being the 'most abusive customer' ever.The restaurateur shamed the Late Late Show host over two incidents - one in June and another in October - in which he yelled 'like crazy' at staff over an omelette and fries - and demanded free drinks 'this second'.
