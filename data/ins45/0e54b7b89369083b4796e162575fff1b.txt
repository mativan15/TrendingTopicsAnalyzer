The Worldwide Developers Conference has been touted to be the next major event by Apple. While Apple fans are keenly looking forward to it, tech enthusiasts are also excited about it. The event, which has created a lot of curiosity among fans and the media, will begin on June 6 and go on till June 11. Though WWDC is designed as a conference for developers, what everybody is looking forward to is the launch of new operating system updates that will be rolled out to Apple devices in the near future.
