Published: 23:51 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 00:02 EDT, 17 October 2022 Former prime minister Scott Morrison has been dubbed a 'globalisation mastermind with boundless influence' as he exclusively offers his speaking services on a global networking platform. On Monday, Worldwide Speakers Group (WWSG) announced Mr Morrison was exclusively joining its agency, made up of mostly conservative voices including former US vice president Mike Pence and former US speaker of the House of Representatives Newt Gingrich.
