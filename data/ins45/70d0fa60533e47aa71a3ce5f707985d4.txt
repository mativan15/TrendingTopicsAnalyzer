(Submitted by Winnipeg Police)A 63-year-old woman with dementia was abducted early Saturday morning in Winnipeg's Unicity area, police say.Sandra McLaughlin was taken in her blue 2012 Jeep Patriot with Manitoba license plate KXR 852 very early in the morning, police said in a missing person's alert.The Jeep has a dent in the driver's side door by the handle and rust on the tire rims.
