ALGONQUIN, Ill., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Rendever, a company pioneering the future of aging, partners with The Oaks at Algonquin Senior Living to boost health and wellness, increase engagement, and create positive shared experiences among senior residents. The senior living community launched the partnership with an interactive, month-long event called 'World Explorers of Algonquin'. During this program, 45 residents are signed on to travel to new destinations around the world three times a day through Rendever's virtual reality sessions.
