The Congress government in Rajasthan has launched an exercise for removal of unnecessary punitive provisions and criminal penalties to facilitate hassle-free business transactions and industrial activities. The heads of all the departments concerned have been asked to review the provisions and make the required changes.Chief Secretary Usha Sharma said here that the decriminalisation drive would be completed through a time-bound action plan.
