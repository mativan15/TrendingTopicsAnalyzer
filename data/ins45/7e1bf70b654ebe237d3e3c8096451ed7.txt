OFS Credit estimated net asset value falls in September
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 21:09:12

OFS Credit Company (NASDAQ:OCCI) on Wednesday said it estimated net asset value (NAV) per share of $9.94 to $10.04 as of Sept. 30, 2022.
The company had estimated NAV per share of $11.09 to $11.19 as of Aug. 31, 2022.
The estimated NAV per share range as of Sept. 30 "is not a comprehensive statement of our financial condition or results for the month ended September 30, 2022," OCCI said in a statement.
OCCI stock earlier closed -1.4% at $8.04
