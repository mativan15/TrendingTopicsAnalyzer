AgenciesRepresentative ImageApple is said to be working on the fourth-generation iPhone SE model. If launched, it will be the fourth model in the SE series after Apple iPhone SE, Apple iPhone SE 2, and Apple iPhone SE 3. According to news reports, the iPhone SE 4 will be donning the design of the iPhone XR, which was launched way back in 2018. According to popular tech news breaker Jon Prosser, the 4th-gen iPhone SE will have the iPhone XR's body, which is in line with the previous generations.

The first iPhone SE, which was launched in 2016, had an iPhone 5S-like design.
