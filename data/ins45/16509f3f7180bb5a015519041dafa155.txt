Gluware expands Board of Directors and Advisory Council to lead the industry in pioneering new use cases and delivering the full potential of next-gen intelligent network automation at scale

SACRAMENTO, Calif., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Gluware , the leader in intelligent network automation, today announced the appointment of Michael J. Donahue and John Frazer to the Board of Directors and Joe Skorupa, William Barnes, Kevin Carney, Rajeev Khanolkar, Paul Melchiorre, and Brad Peters to the company's Advisory Council.
