Ramnaresh Sarwan, born in Antigua of Indo Guyanese origin cited personal reasons for resigning from the panel of the senior and junior men's selection panel. He was just in his 5th month on the job when this happened. Robert Haynes, the erstwhile West Indies cricket team member, who is the selector of the junior side will be handling Sarwan's absence for the time being till a replacement is selected. Ramnaresh Sarwan played for the West Indies from 2000 to 2016. His highest score was 291 against England in a test match and he has also contributed with the ball as a leg-break bowler.
