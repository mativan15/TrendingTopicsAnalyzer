Indian Oil Corp. shares gain 0.52% as Sensex falls
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 06:45:00

Shares of the Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. traded at Rs 116.7 on BSE at 11:42AM (IST) on Tuesday, up 0.52 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 101.9 and a high of Rs 141.75. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap down opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 4.37 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 26.66 per share and 0.82 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 286,546 shares changed hands on the counter till 11:42AM (IST). The stock commands a market value of Rs 109722.
