REUTERS/Issei Kato/File PhotoTOKYO, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Orix Corp plans to invest about 300 billion yen ($2 billion) in Toshiba, Nikkei business reported on Friday.A consortium led by private equity firm Japan Industrial Partners (JIP) and including Orix and Chubu Electric Power Co Inc was granted preferred bidder status by Toshiba in a second round of bidding on Oct. 7, though the conglomerate is still open to proposals from others, people familiar with the matter have said.
