Bradford named as the UK city of culture 2025
theguardian.com
sport
2022-05-31 18:40:44

Bradford, one of the most diverse places in the UK, has been named the 2025 city of culture, a prestigious accolade that attracts thousands of tourists and guarantees millions of pounds of funding and investment.Nadine Dorries, the culture secretary, announced the winner live on BBC One's The One Show on Tuesday evening, after months of intense competition for the title.A record 20 initial bids from places across the UK were whittled down to eight and then four, with the final shortlist comprised of Bradford, County Durham, Southampton and Wrexham County Borough.
