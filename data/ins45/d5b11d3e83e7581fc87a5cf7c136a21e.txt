Foresight Receives Nasdaq Notification Regarding Minimum Bid Requirements



The issuer is solely responsible for the content of this announcement.

Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. (Nasdaq and TASE: FRSX) ('Foresight' or the 'Company'), an innovator in automotive vision systems, announced today that on May 23, 2022, the Company received a written notice (the 'Notice') from the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC indicating that the Company was not in compliance with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5550(a)(2), as the Company's closing bid price for its American Depositary Shares ('ADSs') was below $1.
