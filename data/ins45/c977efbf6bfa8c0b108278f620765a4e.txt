 Those travelling to and from Kingston's east end should take note of a potential traffic snag expected next week.
One lane of the LaSalle Causeway will be closed for maintenance on Thursday Oct. 27, from 9:30 a.m. to 3 p.m.The city says two flag people will be directing traffic during those times.
