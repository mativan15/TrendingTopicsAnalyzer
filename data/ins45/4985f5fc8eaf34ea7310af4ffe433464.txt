SuperATV, LLC Acquires Keller Performance Products

MADISON, Ind., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- SuperATV, the industry leader in aftermarket powersports parts and accessories, has acquired the premier ball joint manufacturer, Keller Performance Products, LLC, founded and led by Todd Keller. The acquisition closed on May 25, 2022. "We design and build the best ball joint in the market, and I was looking for the best way for me to plan the future for my business and me personally. It only made sense for me to join the leading parts supplier in the industry," said owner Todd Keller.
