Photo: Getty Images/YouTube Rapper 23 Rackz was shot and killed during Memorial Day Weekend in Washington, D.C.. The 16-year-old rapper's family and fellow D.C. native Wale recently spoke up about their thoughts on his untimely death. According to a report FOX 5 published on Monday, May 31, Justin Johnson aka 23 Rackz was fatally shot last week after he posted photos of himself to Instagram and tagged his location. His family said that Johnson was shot not long afterward. Johnson's father, Michael, asserted that his son was an intelligent student who got A's in school prior to his rap career.
