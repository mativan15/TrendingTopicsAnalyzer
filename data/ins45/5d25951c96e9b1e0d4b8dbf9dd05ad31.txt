English French MONTREAL, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Osisko Development Corp. ('Osisko Development' or the 'Company') (ODV: TSX-V & NYSE) is pleased to announce the completion of its previously announced acquisition of Tintic Consolidated Metals LLC ('Tintic'), which owns the producing Trixie test mine ('Trixie'), as well as mineral claims covering more than 17,000 acres (including over 14,200 acres of which are patented) in Central Utah's historic Tintic Mining District (the 'Transaction').
