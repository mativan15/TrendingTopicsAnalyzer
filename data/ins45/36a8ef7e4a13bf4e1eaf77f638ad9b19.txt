By Reuters Published: 08:59 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 08:59 EDT, 20 October 2022 Oct 20 (Reuters) - Shares of Tesla Inc fell about 5% in trading before the bell on Thursday, with Wall Street analysts worrying that the electric-car maker's growth momentum may have hit a speed bump due to rising inflation and logistics challenges. At least five brokerages cut their price target on the stock, citing softer delivery in 2022, with Wedbush Securities making the biggest cut of $60 to bring its target to $300.
