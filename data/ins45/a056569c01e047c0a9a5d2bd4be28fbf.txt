But it is relatively easy to reduce your fuel consumption with a few easy – and free – steps. Here are five ways to put cash back in your pocket:PUMP UP YOUR TYRESEver pedalled a pushbike with flat tyres? It's not a fun experience. The same goes for cars – there's more rolling resistance in an underinflated tyre, which means your motor has to work harder to push the car along. This translates to increased fuel use.
