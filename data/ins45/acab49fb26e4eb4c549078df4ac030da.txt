A 72-year-old US citizen was tortured and sentenced to 16 years in prison in Saudi Arabia over tweets criticizing the kingdom, his family says
insider.com
news
2022-10-19 06:14:45

A retired project manager from Florida was tortured and sentenced to 16 years in prison by Saudi Arabia over tweets he made in the US that criticized the Saudi government, his son says.Saad Ibrahim Almadi, 72, holds dual US-Saudi citizenship. He was arrested in November while visiting family in Saudi Arabia and was sentenced earlier this month, according to his son Ibrahim, who first spoke to The Washington Post columnist Josh Rogin in an opinion piece published Sunday.Almadi was arrested for making 14 tweets over the last seven years, including some criticizing corruption and policy decisions in the Saudi government, his son said.
