 Halifax Regional Police say a pedestrian was taken to hospital for injuries after a collision in the city's downtown area Friday afternoon.
In a 2 p.m. release, police said Hollis Street was closed to vehicle traffic between Morris and South Streets as an investigation took place. Police said the collision involved a vehicle and pedestrian, but no further details were provided.
