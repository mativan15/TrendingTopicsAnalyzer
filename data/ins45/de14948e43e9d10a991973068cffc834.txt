Published: 13:06 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 13:06 EDT, 30 May 2022 Former Irish soldier Lisa Smith was found guilty of joining the so-called Islamic State group but acquitted of funding terrorism Three judges at the Special Criminal Court in Dublin on Monday found former soldier Lisa Smith guilty of joining the so-called Islamic State group in Syria. Smith, 40, wept in the dock as judge Tony Hunt read the panel's decision, which was delivered after a nine-week trial. The Muslim convert, who wore a hijab to court, pleaded not guilty to membership of an unlawful terrorist group between October 28, 2015 and December 1, 2019.
