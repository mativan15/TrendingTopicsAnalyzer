MonkeyPoxInu Scam: Crypto Coin Developers Vanish With $400 Million In 3 Days
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-26 09:43:12

Amid the recent resurgence of the Monkeypox virus, someone created a cryptocurrency token coin named  MonkeyPoxlnu (MPOX) last week. It was launched on May 21 and reportedly listed on the decentralized exchange (DEX) PancakeSwap by the creator. Soon after listing, the cryptocurrency started gaining traction and reached a significant market cap in the past few days.The MPOX coin was launched on May 21 but lost 99% of its value three days later on May 23rd, as per Cryptoslate.This has happened after the developers of the MonkeyPoxInu cryptocurrency have allegedly run off with investors' money in another rug pull crypto scam.
