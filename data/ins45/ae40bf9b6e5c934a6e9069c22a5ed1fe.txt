Pinterest is bringing popular music to Idea Pins, its TikTok-like feature
theverge.com
news
2022-10-19 18:53:13

Pinterest is doubling down on its TikTok-style video feature by adding more music options through deals with record labels.The company announced deals today that will allow Pinterest users to add popular music to their video posts, called Idea Pins. The record labels participating are Warner Music Group, Warner Chappell Music, Merlin, and BMG, according to a Pinterest blog post, and the available catalog includes music by artists like Ed Sheeran, Paramore, and Twenty One Pilots. Pinterest, mostly known as a platform to create mood boards and organize images, introduced Idea Pins in 2021.
