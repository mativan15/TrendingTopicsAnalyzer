Bluesky Digital Assets (OTCQB:BTCWF) has announced the formal launch of its Blockchain Engagement Platform.
Bluesky has continued to diversify and grow its business focus with the aim of being more than just a crypto currency miner. This production launch of BlueskyINTEL's Web Engagement Platform represents the second release stage of a dedicated business roadmap for the diversification of Bluesky's business.
"This in the middle positioning creates a lot of potential business opportunities that we can explore and convert to value for our business, stakeholders, and shareholders.
