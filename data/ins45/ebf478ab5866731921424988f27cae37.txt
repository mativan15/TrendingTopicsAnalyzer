Bison Capital and Angeles Equity Partners Acquire Precision Surfacing Solutions

Manufacturer of High Precision Equipment and Surface Finishing Machines LOS ANGELES–(BUSINESS WIRE)–October 18, 2022–
Bison Capital Partners VI, LP ('Bison Capital') and an affiliate of Angeles Equity Partners, LLC ('Angeles') have acquired Lapmaster Parent, LLC ('Precision Surfacing Solutions' or 'PSS'), a manufacturer of specialized precision machinery for lapping, grinding, cutting, and polishing substrates used in the manufacture of precision parts in semiconductor, aerospace, and general industrial markets.
