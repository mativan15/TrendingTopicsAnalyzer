Khargone riots: 12-year-old boy handed Rs 2.9 lakh recovery notice
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 11:15:00

BARWANI: A 12-year-old boy has been issued a notice by the Khargone riots claims tribunal to pay Rs 2.9 lakh as compensation for loss of property in the April 10 violence. He was 11 at the time of the riots.The tribunal, set up under ' Madhya Pradesh Prevention and Recovery of Damages to Public Property Act', had issued notices to the boy and seven others in August 2022 on a woman's complaint that her property was damaged when a mob went on the rampage during a Ram Navami procession in Khargone on April 10 this year.
