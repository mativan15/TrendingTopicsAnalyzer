'Pond man' Kamegowda, who earned praise of PM for water conservation, dies
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:00:00

Kalmane Kamegowda, the octogenarian shepherd who had earned praise from Prime Minister Narendra Modi for engaging in water conservation works, has passed away. Kamegowda, 82, died due to age-related ailments in Karnataka's Mandya, PTI reported. Popularly known as "Pond Man" and "water warrior, Kamegowda was credited with developing 16 lakes on a barren hillock at his village in Malavalli taluk of Mandya district.

Over the years these ponds caught water during the monsoons, and now there is water in them even during peak summers.
