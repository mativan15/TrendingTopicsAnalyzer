Meta Pays For NYC Billboard Attacking Apple's iMessage With WhatsApp
cnet.com
news
2022-10-18 10:39:00

The "anything you can do, I can do better" back and forth between Meta and Apple has made its way to a New York City billboard just outside the famous "crossroads of the world" in Times Square. This time, it's about Apple's iMessage.Facebook co-founder and Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg announced a new billboard above Pennsylvania Station, just south of Times Square and near the famous Broadway area theaters, on Monday to attack Apple. This time, his company was taking on the iPhone maker's popular iMessage texting service, claiming Meta's WhatsApp was more trustworthy.
