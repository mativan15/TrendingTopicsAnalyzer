Man who stole PF money held
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 07:45:00

Vadodara: Eight years after he went absconding in a cheating case, the crime branch has tracked down Kiran Patel from Gandhinagar . Patel was nabbed from a residential apartment where he was in hiding for years. The police said that they had been looking for Patel who had duped his employees of provident fund (PF) money.Patel was working in a firm in the city and he had the responsibility of depositing the PF amount of the employees in the PF office. But Patel did not deposit Rs 4.85 lakh in the PF office and went missing.
