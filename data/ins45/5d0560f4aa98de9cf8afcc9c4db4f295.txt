 We've been hearing for a while that Apple might change up the design for the iPhone SE 4, and now unofficial renders have shown us how that design might look.You can see some of these renders below, created by Ian Zelbo and based on information from an anonymous source; provided to famed leaker Jon Prosser, of Front Page Tech (opens in new tab).They show a phone with a notch (the wider notch iPhones featured before the 20% reduction introduced on the iPhone 13 series), no home button, and small bezels.
