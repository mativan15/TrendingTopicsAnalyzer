By Ashley Cox October 20, 2022 / 6:17 PM / CNN Abbott Laboratories was widely criticized over problems that led to the shutdown of one of its infant formula-producing plants. (Stacey Wescott/Chicago Tribune/Tribune News Service via Getty Images) Chicago Tribune STURGIS, Mich. (CNN) -- Abbott Nutrition plans to build a $500 million nutrition facility for specialty and metabolic infant formulas, Chairman and CEO Robert Ford said Wednesday. The announcement comes as the US formula shortage continues and experts have called for more domestic production and diversity among suppliers.
