Biggest threats facing small business owners over the next year

A poll of 1,000 SME bosses found two thirds of those who have been running their business since before the pandemic admit the last two years have been the most challenging since they started their ventureSmall business owners have revealed their fears for the upcoming yearThe biggest threats facing small business owners over the next year include paying back Government COVID loans, lack of consumer spending, and hefty fuel price increases. A poll of 1,000 SME bosses found two thirds of those who have been running their business since before the pandemic admit the last two years have been the most challenging since they started their venture.
