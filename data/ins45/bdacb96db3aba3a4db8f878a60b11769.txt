Park Kyung-il, left, SK ecoplant CEO, and HTS CEO Jeong Yeong-il pose for a photo during a signing ceremony held Tuesday at SK ecoplant headquarters in Jongno District, central Seoul. [SK ECOPLANT] SK ecoplant on Wednesday said that it will enter the ultra-pure water business with HTS, a local engineering company. The two companies signed a memorandum of understanding on Tuesday at SK ecoplant headquarters in Jongno District, central Seoul. Ultra-pure water is highly purified water used in manufacturing semiconductors, solar panels, batteries and others.
