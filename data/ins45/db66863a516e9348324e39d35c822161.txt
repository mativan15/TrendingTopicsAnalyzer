Suella Braverman gone as Home Secretary as chaos in Liz Truss's Government continues

The Home Office confirmed Ms Braverman had left after just 43 days in the job.It come just days after Kwasi Kwarteng was sacked as Chancellor by the Prime Minister and replaced by Jeremy Hunt following the economic fallout of the mini-budget. Grant Shapps, the former transport secretary who strongly backed Rishi Sunak in the Tory leadership race, may replace Ms Braverman, the Guardian reports. It comes after Ms Truss pulled out of a scheduled visit on Wednesday to a business in Hertfordshire with No10 not giving any reason for the last-minute cancellation of the trip.
