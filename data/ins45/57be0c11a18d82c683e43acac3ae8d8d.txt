Andhra Pradesh: Employer ‘abducts' worker's wife after he fails to pay debt, arrested
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-21 20:10:18

An employer of a horticultural nursery in Mydukur mandal was arrested on Friday on charge of abducting a worker's wife for his failure to repay an amount of ₹1 lakh taken as loan from him.The complainant, Chadamala Subbarayudu, works as a labourer in the nursery of the accused, Peddireddy Sudhakar Reddy, at Uppuguntapalli of the mandal in Kadapa district. Upon developing illness, Subbarayudu reportedly left for his home in Jandlavaram ST colony three months back, without repaying the ₹1 lakh due to his employer.
