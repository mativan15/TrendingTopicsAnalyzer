June 03, 2022 01:45 PM Eastern Daylight Time DENVER--(BUSINESS WIRE)--(NYSE: AIV) - Apartment Investment and Management Company, known as Aimco, has been selected as a 2022 Healthiest Employer by both The Denver and South Florida Business Journals. Aimco was ranked first in its category for South Florida and was a top scorer in Denver's medium sized employer category, receiving special recognition given the challenge of supporting employees with limited resources.



This special recognition as a Healthiest Employer highlights Aimco's continued efforts to positively impact the health and wellness of its teammates.
