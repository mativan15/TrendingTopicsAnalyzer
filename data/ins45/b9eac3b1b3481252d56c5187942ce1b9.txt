TechCrunch Disrupt 2022 kicks off today in San Francisco. The event provides a forum for select startups to demonstrate innovative technologies and solutions—while competing for a $100,000 cash prize—and offers individuals a glimpse at emerging technologies and future trendsetters. The true focus of TechCrunch Disrupt, however, is founders and this year's event underscores that commitment.
