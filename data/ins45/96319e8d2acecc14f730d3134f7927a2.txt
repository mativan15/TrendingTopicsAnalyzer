Australia casino gets record fine for money laundering

world
2022-10-17 04:15:00

Australian gambling giant Star Entertainment Group has been fined A$100m ($62m, £55m) for failing to stop money laundering at its Sydney casino. 
 

 © Getty Images The record fine is the maximum regulators can give


The group's licence to operate the casino has also been suspended. The record penalties were announced in response to a damning inquiry in New South Wales (NSW) earlier this year.Casino operators in Australia have been under great pressure to reform their gambling operations following reports of widespread criminal activity.
