ETtechDelhivery (FILE PHOTO)Shares of logistics and supply chain company Delhivery plunged 15.24% to Rs 473.25 in Thursday's intraday trade on BSE after the company said that it expects moderate growth in shipment volumes through the rest of the financial year 2023. 'While the festive season sale surge in shipment volumes will spill over to Q3FY22 as well, we anticipate moderate growth in shipment volumes through the rest of the financial year," Delhivery said in a BSE filing. 'Our Part Truckload (PTL) business faced operational challenges in Q1FY23 due to the integration of Delhivery and SpotOn networks.
