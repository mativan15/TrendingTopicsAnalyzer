As we continue to evolve as an organization my goal is to have a sales structure to support our current and future business. ST. HELENA, Calif. (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 C. Mondavi & Family (CMF), one of the country's most iconic wine names, announces today the promotion of Greg Gauci to Senior Vice President of National Sales and a participating member of the Executive Leadership Team, overseeing the entire C. Mondavi & Family portfolio. Gauci will also represent the US Wholesale team to the company's board of directors.
