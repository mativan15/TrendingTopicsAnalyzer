Assam police arrests two operatives of Ansarullah Bangla Team
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 16:15:00

iStockRepresentational ImageAssam police have arrested two operatives of Ansarullah Bangla Team (ABT), an affiliate of Al Qaeda in the Indian Subcontinent (AQIS). Assam chief minister, Himanta Biswa Sarma tweeted, 'Arrest of Sadeq Ali is a significant achievement since he had been radicalising youth including Jakibul Ali for past 2 years. He was also associated with JMB & Neo-JMB. Meanwhile, Ghagrapar Police (Nalbari Dist) too arrested 2 persons — Habel Ali & Abu Raihan — in similar a case'. He added, 'We're determined to root out jihadi elements from Assam.
