Australia's house prices fall for first time since September 2020
theguardian.com
news
2022-06-01 01:40:24

House prices nationally recorded their first decline since September 2020 in May, led by monthly losses in Sydney and Melbourne, the country's two largest real estate markets.The CoreLogic home value index declined 0.1% in May, with Sydney dropping 1.0% and Melbourne off 0.7% .Canberra, Australia's second-most expensive property market behind Sydney, also eased 0.1%, its first monthly decline since July 2019.CoreLogic research director Tim Lawless said there had been significant speculation around the impact of rising interest rates on the property market.
