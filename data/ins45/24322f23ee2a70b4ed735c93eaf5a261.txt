Fears of a leadership stitch-up to stop Boris are well founded
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 18:05:00

Moments after Sir Graham Brady, the chairman of the 1922 Committee, and party chairman Jake Berry announced the rules for the leadership contest a senior Tory Brexiteer backbencher called me with the simple but sarcastic message: "Not a stith-up at all!" It was followed by what can only be described as a disgruntled laugh. With every passing Tory leadership election (and we are on the fourth since 2016), the one thing that can be guaranteed is that the Conservative MPs will play stupid games with it.
