Click here for a complete list of our election recommendations. Santa Clara needs a clean break from the divisive politics of Mayor Lisa Gillmor.
When we encouraged voters to approve Measure J in 2010 to bring the 49ers to Santa Clara, we argued that the city had a 'highly respected city staff' that could deal with the stadium. Anthony Becker
But since becoming mayor in 2016, Gillmor has engaged in 'my way or the highway' politics that has divided the city, created a dysfunctional council atmosphere, caused constant staff turnover and led to costly lawsuits that contributed to the current $27 million budget deficit.
