Over the past 24 hours, the Solana (SOL) coin has fallen from $45.55 to $38.36 across cryptocurrency exchanges, which represents a 16% decline, according to CoinMarketCap. This latest fall is a direct result of yet another network outage that occurred yesterday across the "celebrated" Solana blockchain.
