June 01, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Accel Entertainment, Inc. (NYSE: ACEL) ('Accel'), a leading distributed gaming operator in the U.S., today announced the completion of its previously announced transaction to acquire Century Gaming, Inc. ('Century'), one of the leading distributed gaming operators in the Western United States, for $164.2 million in cash and stock, including Century's working capital.

'We could not be more excited to further expand our operations in the Western United States with the Century team.
