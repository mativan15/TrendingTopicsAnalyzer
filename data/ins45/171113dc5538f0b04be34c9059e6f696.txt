Minutes before he passed away, he sang, "Hum, rahein ya na rahein kal... Yaad ayenge ye pal..." Who knew his words would be prophetic.  The man who made our childhood awesome with his melodious voice and beautiful songs, Krishnakumar Kunnath, popularly known as KK, passed away on May 31 at the age of 53. Most integral part of 90's kids' lives, his loss felt like a physical pain to many.  AFPKK's last concert - He was sweating profusely. He went away too soon, but in his last moments, he did what he loved the most - sang for a live audience.
