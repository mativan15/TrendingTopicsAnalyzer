Saint-Gobain Sells Glass Processing Businesses in Germany, Austria
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-27 06:25:00

By Cecilia Butini Compagnie de Saint-Gobain SA said Friday that it has entered into agreements to divest its glass-procesing businesses Eckelt Glas and Glas Ziegler in Austria, and its holding in Glaskontor Erfurt in Germany. The Austrian businesses are being sold to privately-owned German group Aequita, while the German business is being sold to the Caleoglas Group, according to Saint-Gobain. Closing of the transaction is expected by the end of summer, the French construction-materials company said.
