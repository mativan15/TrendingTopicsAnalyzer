Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, York is among six locations shortlisted to become home to the headquarters of Great British RailwaysA delay to the planned major overhaul of the way Britain's railways are managed is "incredibly disappointing", York's council leader has said.Plans for Great British Railways (GBR) will not now go before Parliament until at least May, Transport Secretary Anne-Marie Trevelyan has confirmed.Six cities and towns, including York and Doncaster, have been shortlisted as the site for GBR's headquarters.
