Property Vista, an all-in-one property management solution, announces the launch of Vista Pay's outbound payments functionality, in collaboration with Dream Payments

TORONTO, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ - Property Vista is excited to announce the launch of outbound payments for Vista Pay across Canada in Q4 2022 and across the US in Q1 2023, in collaboration with Dream Payments ("Dream") and Dream's connectivity to J.P. Morgan's digital payment services. Property management companies can now rely on Vista Pay to service their end-to-end cycle of payments.
