HeartCore Enterprises, a leading software development company based in Japan, is best known for its almost-perfect customer satisfaction rates and commitment to scale 'joy and happiness in the digital experience for enterprise organizations.'And 2022 has thus far been a strong calendar year for the Software as a Service solutions provider. 'We've begun generating momentum across all fronts of new and existing lines of business as we look to expand through organic and inorganic methods,' said CEO Sumitaka Kanno, a Japanese Air Force veteran who founded HeartCore in 2009 after 20 years in the information tech sector.
