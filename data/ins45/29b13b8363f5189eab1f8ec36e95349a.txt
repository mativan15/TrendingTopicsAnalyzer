Cushman & Wakefield to promote Dallas-based Woody Stone to president of US multifamily asset services
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 19:12:00

Chicago-based real estate firm Cushman & Wakefield (NYSE: CWK) will promote Dallas-based Woody Stone to serve as president of U.S. multifamily asset services following the retirement of Rick Graf on Dec. 31, the company announced. Following the appointment, Stone will remain based in Dallas. Graf is retiring after 48 years with the firm.

In his new position, Stone will oversee company growth and manage assets for institutional clients across multiple states. He will report to Marla Maloney, president of Americas Services at the firm.
