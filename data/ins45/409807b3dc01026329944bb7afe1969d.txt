Non-food bank credit grows 11.3% in April, according to RBI data

Banks' non-food credit grew at 11.3 per cent in April 2022 as compared to 4.7 per cent a year ago, RBI data showed on Tuesday. Loans to agriculture and allied activities continued to grow at 10.6 per cent in April as against a rise of 10.7 per cent in the year-ago month, according to the RBI's Sectoral Deployment of Bank Credit - April 2022. Growth in advances to industry stood at 8.1 per cent in the month from a contraction of 0.4 per cent in April 2021. Size-wise, credit to medium industries registered a growth of 53.
