ED starts probe into RERA scam involving 65 developers in Kalyan-Dombivli
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 04:30:00

THANE: The Enforcement Directorate started its own investigation into the "65 builders Maha RERA certificate scam" probed by Special Investigation Team of Thane police this month.The ED has collected documents related to case from SIT in Thane and on Monday they have also written to KDMC commissioner Bhausaheb Dangade asking him to provide details related to case saying money laundering is suspected in case and they need a full-fledged investigation.Atleast 66 developers in Kalyan-Dombivli constructed illegal buildings in twin city and to sell flats prepared fake documents of building permission and using same documents obtained RERA certificates and later sold flats to buyers.
