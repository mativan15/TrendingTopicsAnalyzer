HC seeks CBI stand on Subramanian bail plea
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 02:30:00

New Delhi: The Delhi High Court on Tuesday sought the CBI 's stand on a bail plea filed by National Stock Exchange (NSE) former group operating officer Anand Subramanian in the co-location scam case. Representing the accused, senior advocate Vikas Pahwa argued that an incomplete chargesheet has been filed in the case and sought a mandatory bail under the Criminal Procedure Code as well as on merits.Justice Sudhir Kumar Jain then issued a notice seeking the CBI's response.The former GOO was arrested by the CBI on February 24 and was remanded to custodial interrogation.
