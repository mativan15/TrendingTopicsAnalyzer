How Granite Properties is preparing for the hybrid office reality
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 12:50:00

A company's relationship to office needs may still be one of the most pressing quandaries for developers, but for Granite Properties, it's an equation to be solved.

While the Plano-based developer forges ahead with five projects across the country – three in North Texas, one in Atlanta and another in Cambridge, Massachusetts – Granite's team has embraced the reality of hybrid work.

'You could say, ‘Well, you guys have $2 billion of office under development, so you obviously believe in office,' said Michael Dardick, Granite's founding partner and chief executive, in a recent interview.
