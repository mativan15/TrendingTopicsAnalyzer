By Afp Published: 13:14 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 13:14 EDT, 21 October 2022 Classified documents seized in the August US Justice Department raid on former president Donald Trump's home in Palm Beach, Florida. Documents seized from former president Donald Trump's Florida home in August included highly sensitive intelligence on Iran and China that risked exposure of US spying methods, the Washington Post reported Friday. Among the documents retrieved by Justice Department investigators included one that described Iran's missile program, and another that "described highly sensitive intelligence work aimed at China," the Post said, citing people familiar with the case.
