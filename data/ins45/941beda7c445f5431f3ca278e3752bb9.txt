Florida projected to play in December bowl games, per ESPN
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-17 20:27:30

ESPN's latest bowl projections have the Florida Gators playing in two different December games. Kyle Bonagura has the Orange and Blue playing in the AutoZone Liberty Bowl in Memphis, Tennessee on Wednesday, Dec. 28 against the Oklahoma Sooners. Mark Schlabach has the Gators playing in the TaxSlayer Gator Bowl in Jacksonville, Florida on Friday, Dec. 30 against the North Carolina Tar Heels.

Oklahoma joins Florida in having a first-year head coach. Brent Venables took over in Norman after Lincoln Riley left to take the head coaching job at USC.
