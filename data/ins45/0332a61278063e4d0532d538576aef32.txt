Company Announces Strategic Hires Across Payments, Vehicle and Sustainability Functions

BOSTON, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Motus, the definitive leader in mobile workforce solutions, today announced three new appointments that will help the company advance and accelerate innovation across its line of market-leading reimbursement solutions. Jon Bernstein joins Motus as Vice President, Product Management for Payments; Candice Helm joins as Vice President, Product Management for Vehicle; John Petrucelli is promoted to Vice President of Product Line Management for Sustainability, all effective immediately.
