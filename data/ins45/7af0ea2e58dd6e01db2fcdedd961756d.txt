NZ Breakers thump Illawarra Hawks to make it three straight in Australian NBL

news
2022-10-17 10:28:46

At WIN Entertainment Centre, Wollongong: NZ Breakers 88 (Will McDowell-White 17, Cam Gliddon 12, Rayan Rupert 11, Tom Vodanovich 10, Jarrell Brantley 10, Rob Loe 10), Illawarra Hawks 62 (Tyler Harvey 12). 1Q: 21-16; HT: 47-26; 3Q: 60-49. The New Zealand Breakers have gone back to back on the road to catapult into the Australian NBL contender conversation just three rounds into the new season. The Kiwi club survived a case of the third-quarter staggers to power away to a 26-point victory over the Illawarra Hawks on Monday night at the WIN Entertainment Centre to extend their win streak to three and their record to 3-1 for the campaign.
