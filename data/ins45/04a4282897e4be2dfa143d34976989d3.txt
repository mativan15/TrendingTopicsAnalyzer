Tory MPs urge Truss to keep Brexit pledges as Farage poses 'danger'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 05:18:00

Liz Truss could 'risk voter desertion' if she drops her plans for post-Brexit reforms, senior Conservative MPs have warned. A news report by The Telegraph explained how Eurosceptic backbenchers rallied behind Ms Truss in the leadership election after she promised to pursue wider deregulation and turbocharge trade with Commonwealth countries.One of the Conservative MPs told The Telegraph: 'The polling suggests that, at least temporarily, we've lost a lot of people already.'Liz will lose a lot of the others if she doesn't deliver a lot of these things we've been talking about [during the leadership contest].
