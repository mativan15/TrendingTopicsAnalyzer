Democratic Party A video of Representative Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, a New York Democrat, dancing while protesters chant at her during a campaign event has gone viral.The video shows the protesters saying "AOC has got to go" while the congresswoman sits on a stage, briefly dancing to the chants, which were accompanied by a drumbeat.One version of the video, which was posted on Twitter by user Danny De Urbina, has been viewed 1.6 million times as of Friday morning.
