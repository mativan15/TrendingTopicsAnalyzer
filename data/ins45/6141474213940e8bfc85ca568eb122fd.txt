Getty ImagesWritten and directed by Anil Ravipudi, 'F3: Fun & Frustration' the Venkatesh and Varun Tej starrer, featuring Mehreen Pirzada and Tamannaah Bhatia, hit the screens on Friday, gathering several positive reviews from everywhere. The same was evident in the box office collection of the movie on its very first day. It collected over 10.26 Crore on the first day, receiving a solid opening. 
What Is F3?
'F3: Fun & Frustration is a hilarious movie starring Victory Venkatesh and Varun Tej. Directed by Anil Ravipudi, F3 is a family entertainer filled with humour.
