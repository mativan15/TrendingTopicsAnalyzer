Jury Rules Mostly In Favor Of Depp In Amber Heard Defamation Trial (Fairfax, VA) -- Johnny Depp is the clear winner in his defamation trial against ex-wife Amber Heard. A Virginia jury agreed with Depp that Heard acted with actual malice and agreed with every one of Depp's defamation counts against Heard. He was awarded 15-million dollars. The jury also found in favor of Heard in some parts of her 100-million-dollar-countersuit against Depp, but only awarded her two-million dollars.
