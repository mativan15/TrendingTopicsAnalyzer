Tomato prices reach Rs100/kg in Mumbai; likely to come down in 2 weeks
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 12:45:00

PTIRetail prices of tomato skyrocket up to Rs 100 per kg in metro cities.The prices of tomatoes have surged in southern states and reached Rs 100 per kg in Mumbai. Consumers in Mumbai's Dadar vegetable market have to shell out Rs 80 per kg for tomatoes while in most other markets it sells at rs 100, according to reports. Heat waves in March-April in key tomato-growing states Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh badly affected production, further driving up prices across the country. The average retail price of tomatoes in India has risen 70 % in the last month and 168 % in the last year, according to figures published by the Food Ministry.
