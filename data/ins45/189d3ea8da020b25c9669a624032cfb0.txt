No one could have foreseen the seismic shifts that last year brought to business and wider life. 2020 redefined how we live and work, and much of the year was spent by businesses focusing on quick fixes to enable remote working and ensure business continuity amidst an uncertain and ever-changing landscape. A year on, and businesses need to be looking into long term, stable investments in technologies and solutions to not only meet challenges, but turn them into business strengths and competitive differentiators.
