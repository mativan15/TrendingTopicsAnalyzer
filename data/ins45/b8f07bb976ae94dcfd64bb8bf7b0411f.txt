Gambia cough syrup scandal: Child deaths risen to 70
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 16:03:54

Oct 15, 2022, 09:33PM IST

Source: Mirror Now

The government of Gambia reported that the previous figure of 69 child deaths from acute kidney injury, which is likely to be related to cough syrups made in India, has increased to 70.The syrups were discovered to be produced by New Delhi based Maiden Pharmaceuticals Ltd., which has now closed one of its operations in northern India while health officials look into the matter.
