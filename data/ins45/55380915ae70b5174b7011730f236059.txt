Punjab vigilance bureau arrests ex-minister Sunder Sham Arora for bribing officials
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 05:15:00

CHANDIGARH: The Punjab vigilance bureau has arrested former industries and commerce minister Sunder Sham Arora on the intervening night of Saturday and Sunday from Zirakpur in Mohali district while offering Rs 50 lakh to vigilance officials to settle his disproportionate assets case.Former Congress leader Arora had joined the BJP in June this year along with other former cabinet ministers. The vigilance bureau had questioned Arora about his properties on September 21 and then on October 12.Chief director of state vigilance bureau ADGP Varinder Kumar said, 'Arora has been arrested from the parking of Cosmo Mall Zirakpur in the night when he handed over a bag containing Rs 50 lakh to a vigilance officer in a vehicle.
