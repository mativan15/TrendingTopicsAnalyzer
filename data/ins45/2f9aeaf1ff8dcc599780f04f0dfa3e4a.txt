June 01, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Elastic (NYSE: ESTC) ('Elastic'), the company behind Elasticsearch and the Elastic Stack, announced strong results for its fourth quarter and full fiscal year (ended April 30, 2022).

Fourth Quarter Fiscal 2022 Financial Highlights


Total revenue was $239.4 million, an increase of 35% year-over-year, or 37% on a constant currency basis


Elastic Cloud revenue was $87.7 million, an increase of 71% year-over-year, or 72% on a constant currency basis


GAAP operating loss was $59.
