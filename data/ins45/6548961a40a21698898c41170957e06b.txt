Motown Legends Berry Gordy and Smokey Robinson to Be Honored as MusiCares Persons of the Year During Grammy Week
variety.com
news
2022-10-20 16:00:00

MusiCares has named Motown founder Berry Gordy and legendary singer-songwriter Smokey Robinson as MusiCares Persons of the Year for 2023. The longtime friends will be honored at the 32nd annual Persons of the Year benefit gala at the Los Angeles Convention Center on Feb. 3, 2023, two nights before the 65th annual Grammy Awards. Proceeds from the event will provide essential support for MusiCares, the charitable wing of the Recording Academy, which has given dozens of millions of dollars in relief money to music professionals in need.
