DENVER, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Assure Holdings Corp. (the 'Company' or 'Assure') (NASDAQ: IONM), a provider of intraoperative neuromonitoring ('IONM') and remote neurology services, announced that it is scheduled to present at the LD Micro Invitational on June 8, 2022. John Farlinger, executive chairman and CEO will present to investors at 7:30 p.m. ET. The investor presentation will be webcast live and is available via the Assure investor relations site. Additionally, Assure management will participate in one-on-one meetings with investors on June 7th-8th, 2022.
