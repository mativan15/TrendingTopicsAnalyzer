Published October 17, 2022 4:17pm EDT Roswell officials say the city's UFO Festival had an economic impact of more than $2 million. The Roswell Daily Record reports the Roswell City Council's finance committee looked earlier this month at an economic report for the event.It indicated more than 40,000 visitors came to the four-day festival, which ran June 30-July 3.
