Rep. Elissa Slotkin is running against Republican state Sen. Tom Barrett in Michigan's 7th Congressional District.

The newly drawn 7th District excludes the city of Jackson, often touted as one of the birthplaces of the Republican Party.

In 2018, Slotkin became one of 31 Democrats to flip Republican seats in congressional districts that Trump won in 2016.
