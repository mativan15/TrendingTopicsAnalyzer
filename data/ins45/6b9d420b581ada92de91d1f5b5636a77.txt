These are Wall Street's favorite Dow stocks as it attempts to rebound
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-02 14:45:57

As the stock market tries to find its footing after a nearly two-month slide, Wall Street analysts are bullish on some of biggest blue-chip names. The Dow Jones Industrial Average has been the best performer this year among the major market averages, but is still down about 10% in 2022 thanks in large part to an eight-week losing streak. That slide finally came to an end last week, and now some investors are turning their focus to what can rebound the most. The stocks below are Dow components that have buy ratings from more than half of Wall Street analysts and upside of at least 10% to their average price targets.
