A sign of Foxconn is seen inside its office building in Taipei, Taiwan November 12, 2020. REUTERS/Ann WangTAIPEI, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Taiwan's Foxconn (2317.TW), the world's largest contract electronics maker, said on Tuesday it was not in the business of selling its own electric vehicle brand but wanted its customers to sell a lot of EVs.Chairman Liu Young-way said at the company's annual Tech Day that from design to build, the company has the capability in EVs and its global footprint gave it a "huge advantage" to meet the EV industry's demands.
