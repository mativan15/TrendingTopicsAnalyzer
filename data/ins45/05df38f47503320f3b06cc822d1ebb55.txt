Hagersville resident loses $400,000 to cryptocurrency scheme
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-03 14:59:11

A Hagersville resident has lost $400,000 through a cryptocurrency scheme. Haldimand County OPP said in a media release that they invested with an online cryptocurrency brokerage, starting in September 2021. They added that the victim is not able to recover the money they invested. The Haldimand Crime Unit is now investigating the alleged theft. OPP shared the following tips to avoid similar scams: Keep a close eye on bank accounts and credit cards Never give out personal information to anyone you don't know Verify all contact information Do not trust phone numbers and email addresses that an unknown person provides you Names can be faked on caller displays Research a company you plan on doing business with If a caller says they're a bank representative, hang up and call your local branch to verify it's legitimate OPP also provided an online link with more advice on fraud prevention.
