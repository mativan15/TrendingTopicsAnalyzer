Sunil BaghelTIMESOFINDIA.COMUpdated: Oct 21, 2022, 12:36 ISTThe affidavit filed by the Gujarat government in the premature release of 11 convicts in the Bilkis Bano rape-murder case was called 'bulky' by the Supreme Court. A brief perusal of the 478-page document reveals that its size should be anybody's last concern. The affidavit, which claims that every official – from the Union government to the state government – consented to the early release of the prisoners, does not say a word about whether the opinion of two officials, who opposed the remission of the sentence, was even discussed.
