 Police are seeking the public's assistance in locating an elopee reported missing in Toronto.
An elopee is a person who is subject to detention at a psychiatric facility and is absent without leave.Police are searching for 56-year-old David Uhlemann, who was last seen on Saturday at around 4:15 p.m., in the Queen Street West and Shaw Street area.
