Ryder System raises Q2, full-year 2022 outlooks (NYSE:R)
seekingalpha.com

2022-06-03 12:12:04

Sundry Photography/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Ryder System (NYSE:R) has raised its Q2 and full-year 2022 forecasts ahead of its Investor Day to be held today. Q2 GAAP EPS is now seen at $4.37 - $4.62 from $3.97 - $4.22 previously, while comparable EPS forecast (non-GAAP) is estimated to be between $3.90 - $4.15 (consensus estimate: $3.71) from $3.50 - $3.75 previously. The logistics and transportation company also raised its forecast for 2022 adjusted ROE to a range of 24% - 26% from 23% - 25%.
