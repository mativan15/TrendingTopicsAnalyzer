Source: ANI

Prime Minister Narendra Modi dedicated 75 digital banking units across 75 districts of the nation via video conferencing on October 16 and said that the government has given the highest priority to taking banks to remote areas. He said, 'We have simultaneously worked on two things. First, improving the banking system, strengthening it and bringing transparency. Second, we did financial inclusion.' He further added, 'We decided that banks themselves will go to the doorsteps of the poor.
