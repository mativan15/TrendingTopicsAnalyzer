The Canadiens won 6-2 on Oct. 20, 2022.Paul Chiasson/The Canadian PressIt took five games and a little more tape on his stick but Juraj Slafkovsky finally scored his first National Hockey League goal.The Canadiens used a three-goal first period to cruise to a 6-2 win over the Arizona Coyotes on Thursday. The Bell Centre crowd gave the first overall pick a standing ovation as the PA announcer called his name and kept it going by chanting Slafkovsky's name after the second-period marker.
