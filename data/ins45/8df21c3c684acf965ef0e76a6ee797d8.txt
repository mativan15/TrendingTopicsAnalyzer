Toby + Chilli MorningsBy @TobyChilliShowMay 31, 2022Inflation is at an all-time high. Life is expensive. We have lots of bills. Saving money isn't just nice, it's a necessity. But how can you make it happen? Here are some quick and easy things you can do now to start saving money: Fill up at the cheapest gas station. Either search online or check gas prices as you're driving around to see who has the cheapest – or who charges the same for cash and credit.
Create a monthly budget. There's are tons of spreadsheets you can find online to help.
