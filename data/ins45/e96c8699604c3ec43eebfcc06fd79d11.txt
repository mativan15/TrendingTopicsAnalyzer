Arrest all of us together, not one by one: Arvind Kejriwal
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 06:15:00

PTIDelhi CM Arvind KejriwalArvind Kejriwal in a press conference on Thursday requested to arrest all AAP ministers together instead of arresting them one-by-one and disturbing the work being done for public welfare. Kejriwal came out in support of Satyendar Jain and revealed that Delhi's education minister Manish Sisodia could be the next minister to be arrested. He added that CBI has already investigated the case where Satyendra Jain came out clean therefore, the false accusations are being made to disrupt the smooth functioning of the state.
