Maks_Lab/iStock via Getty Images The lack of meaningful synergies coupled with more qualified suitors in the market makes Broadcom (NASDAQ:AVGO) an ill fit for VMware (NYSE:VMW). Deal Offer Summary VMware and Broadcom have entered into an agreement to merge, with the combined entity getting rebranded as VMware. The cash and stock deal values VMware at $61 billion, with VMware shareholders getting the choice to receive $142.
