Reliance Retail Q2 Results: Profit drops 7% YoY to Rs 586 crore; revenues rise slightly
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 15:00:00

Reliance Retail on Friday reported a 6.7% YoY drop in its Q2 profit at Rs 586 crore, while revenues increased marginally by 1.2% on year at Rs 1,172 crore. The retailer's net profit margin decreased to 42.4% in the September quarter from 47.97% in the corresponding quarter of last year. Retail opened 795 stores during the quarter and had a total of 16,617 physical stores operational by the end of September.
