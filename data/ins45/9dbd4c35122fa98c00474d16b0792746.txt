James MacDougall, 37, has an incurable genetic condition that leads to low IQ and developmental delay which could affect the children he fatheredJames MacDougall, 37, went ahead with private sperm donations despite knowing that he suffered from an incurable genetic conditionThe family of an online sperm donor who fathered 15 kids despite not telling the mums he had a genetic condition which causes learning disabilities have defended him, labelling him "kind-hearted". James MacDougall, 37, advertised on a social media page for lesbian women seeking sperm donors, even though he knew he had Fragile X syndrome which leads to low IQ and developmental delay.
