Hatton fights for the first time in almost a decade in July when he faces Marco Antonio Barrera and the Brit has lost a huge amount of weight ahead of his returnRicky Hatton previews Marco Antonio Barrera exhibition boutRicky Hatton has been taking his ring return extremely seriously as the former world champion has shed 40lb in just two months ahead of his comeback fight. Hatton, who was nicknamed 'Fatton' during his career, fights for the first time in almost a decade against fellow boxing veteran Marco Antonio Barrera on July 2.
