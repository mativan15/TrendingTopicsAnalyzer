Collider.com The topic of addiction is one that's been explored frequently throughout film history. It's a difficult subject that can be captured in many art forms, sure, but there's something about the visual medium that makes the topic more visceral, personal, and powerful. Film offers a way to get inside a character's mind, or observe a character from a distance, depending on the approach taken. Sometimes, films depicting addiction will do a bit of both.
