Tears as rescue dog is reunited with her sister from Romania in viral clip
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 14:54:11

A dog's reaction after reuniting with her long-lost sister has the internet "crying huge tears," after the pups' owner posted a video of the two of them sharing a very special moment.The viral clip, shared on TikTok last Thursday by Mei_the_puppy, shows Luna, a rescue dog from Romania joining her new forever home where her sister, who was adopted a few weeks prior, was waiting for her to finally settle for good.The poster explained that before entering the country both dogs were tested at the borders and were totally healthy.
