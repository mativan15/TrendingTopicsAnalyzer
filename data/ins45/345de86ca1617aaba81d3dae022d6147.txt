gahsoon/E+ via Getty Images Pinduoduo (NASDAQ:PDD) has borne much of the brunt of China's extended bear market over the last 18 months. As a result, the company's share price and underlying fundamentals have diverged considerably. On paper, the company is killing the game with its unique crowd-sourced e-commerce business model, disrupting large Chinese e-commerce incumbents Alibaba (BABA) and JD.com (JD). Digging a bit deeper, however, several risk factors cloud the business's future. In saying that, I believe the upside potential outweighs the risk factors at today's price.
