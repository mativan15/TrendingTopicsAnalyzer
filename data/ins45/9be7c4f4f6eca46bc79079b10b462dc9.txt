Ailing (Eileen) Gu and Hwang Dae-heon crowned best athletes of Beijing 2022 at ANOC Awards
olympics.com
business
2022-10-19 11:34:13

Months after her history-making performance, freestyle skier Ailing (Eileen) Gu continues to grab headlines.Gu, who became the first action-sports athlete to win three medals at the same Olympics at Beijing 2022 was on Wednesday, October 19, named the best performing female athlete of the Games at the 2022 Association of National Olympic Committees, ANOC, Awards in Seoul.The Chinese freestyle skier was one of the most dominant athletes, winning a gold medal in the Big Air competition, another gold medal in the Halfpipe event, and taking silver in Slopestyle.
