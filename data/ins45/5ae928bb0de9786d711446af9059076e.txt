Appalachian Regional Commission gives $6.9M for coal communities
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 20:13:00

Catalyst Connection and other manufacturing extension partnerships in Ohio and West Virginia will share in $1.37 million in Appalachian Regional Commission funding, and a Fayette County careers institute will receive $1.5 million to train licensed practice nurses as part of $6.9 million in funding for Pennsylvania's coal communities.

ARC announced its Partnerships for Opportunity and Workforce and Economic Revitalization (POWER) Initiative during a news conference Monday in Frostburg, Maryland.

Among the projects are the Tri-State Net Zero Manufacturing Initiative involving Catalyst Connection and the Health Sciences Workforce Development Center at the Fayette County Career & Technical Institute.
