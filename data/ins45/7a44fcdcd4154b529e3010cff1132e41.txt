Truss Fights On Against Backdrop of Glum Tory Faces in Commons
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-19 13:13:47

(Bloomberg) -- Many in Westminster didn't think Liz Truss would make it to Prime Minister's Questions on Wednesday -- including plenty of lawmakers in her own ruling Conservative Party. Since last week's session, she has fired her finance minister and ditched most of her economic program after the financial markets she has long championed turned against her.Yet mainly because angry Tories can't agree on who they want to replace her, Truss was allowed to bound into a packed House of Commons chamber, smile fixed in place.
