EMILY Atack has revealed she likes a drink so much that she had trouble working out whether she had Covid or a hangover after her birthday.
So the Celebrity Juice panellist and actress was delighted when both she and her tamer sister Martha eventually tested positive and cleared up the confusion.
Emily Atack says she cannot tell the difference between hangovers or Covid
Chatting on the CASTaway podcast, Emily said: 'I had my birthday and I sort of felt myself getting a bit ill.
'But when you're a big drinker like me it's hard to tell what's Covid and what's a hangover.
