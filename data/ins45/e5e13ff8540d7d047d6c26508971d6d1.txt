NEW DELHI: With Rs 1.44 lakh crore of mutual fund money riding on it, India's second-largest private lender is now clearly the hottest stock on Dalal Street for fund managers. Data shows that the bank stock is the biggest bet of at least 32 mutual fund houses in India, including the top 3 giants - SBI MF, AMC and . Even at the cumulative level of the entire industry, ICICI Bank is the largest holding at Rs 1,44,132 crore which is equivalent to 6.65% of the equity mutual fund industry's assets under management (AUM) as of September-end, shows data from Prime Database.
