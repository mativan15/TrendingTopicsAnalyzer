 Image: Stock.adobe.com In the last year, the nation's largest home builders acquired a large share of lots either owned or 'controlled' through land purchasing options or contracts, reports Bill McBride in the CalculatedRisk Newsletter. A surge in land purchases could benefit the housing market if demand remains high, but if the market cools, land/lot markets may not see the kind of growth they were expecting.
