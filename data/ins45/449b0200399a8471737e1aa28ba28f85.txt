Image caption, The authority has said it will need to sell off most of its buildings to cut its debtA council that declared bankruptcy last year earlier spent £28,000 on indoor plants which a councillor said are now "dying".Slough Borough Council bought the 200 plants for its £41m Observatory House HQ when it moved in 2019.Its council leader has said the authority will "almost certainly" move out of it as a result of its £760m borrowing debt. James Swindlehurst said he has been watering the plants himself.
