REUTERS/Alwyn ScottCHICAGO, Oct 18 (Reuters) - General Electric Co (GE.N) plans to wind down physical office space in Boston and some other corporate sites as it prepares to split into three independent businesses, a company spokesperson said on Tuesday.The industrial conglomerate plans to spin off its healthcare business next year, and combine its power and renewable energy units to be spun off in 2024.
