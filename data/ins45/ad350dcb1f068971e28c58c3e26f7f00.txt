Steel Dynamics 3Q Sales Lifted by Steel Fabrication Operations
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 20:48:00

By Denny Jacob Steel Dynamics Inc. logged better-than-expected sales in the latest quarter as its steel fabrication operations offset results from its flat rolled steel business. The steel company posted net income of $914.3 million, or $5.03 a share, for the third quarter ended Sept. 30, compared to net income of $990.8 million, or $4.85 a share, a year earlier.
