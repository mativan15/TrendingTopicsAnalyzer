SYDNEY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--Lifestyle Clotheslines has launched a brand-new range of Hills Hoist clothesline products into the Australian market, it was announced today.Based in Sydney, Lifestyle Clotheslines is the leading supplier and installer of clothesline and washing line products across Australia, with the Hills product range making up a large part of the company's offering.Over the last 16 years, the Hills clothesline and laundry product range has evolved through products that are now found throughout most Australian homes.
