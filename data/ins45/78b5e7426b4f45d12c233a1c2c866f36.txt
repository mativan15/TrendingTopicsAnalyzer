Biden administration lifting Trump-era restrictions on Cuba flights
foxnews.com
news
2022-06-02 04:46:45

Published June 2, 2022 12:46am EDT The Biden administration lifted Trump-era flight restrictions that prevented U.S. airlines and chartered flights from going to other cities in Cuba besides Havana on Wednesday. U.S. Secretary of State Antony Blinken said revoking the prohibition was in support of the Cuban people and in the "foreign policy interests of the United States."He had made a written request with the U.S. Transportation Department on Tuesday and the department made the change on Wednesday.
