Martin Lewis urges households to ration energy use amid warnings of £5,000 bills

Money Saving Expert founder Martin Lewis has warned households to ration their energy usage after Jeremy Hunt announced the price cap freeze will end after six months.The personal finance guru said middle earners could be among those unable to pay their bills as average energy costs could hit £5,000.Jeremy Hunt said the government would 'review' the two-year cap on the unit price of energy to keep annual household gas and electricity bills at no more than £2,500.In a shock move aimed at reassuring markets the government can balance the books, the new chancellor said he had find a 'new approach' to energy bills that will 'cost the taxpayer significantly less'.
