ISB, IIM-B figure in FT EMBA 2022 rankings
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:00:00

The Indian School of Business (ISB) was ranked number one in India and 44 globally in the top 100 Financial Times (FT) Executive MBA 2022 ranking. After ISB, Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIM-B) figured in the FT list at 67th position globally. Alumni from the Post Graduate Programme in Management for Senior Executives (PGPMAX) class of 2019 have been surveyed for this year's ranking and ISB has significantly ranked higher in various parameters of this year's rankings than the previous year, the premier B-School said in a statement.
