EA Is Developing The Sims 5, Working Title 'Project Rene'
pcmag.com
news
2022-10-19 11:55:38

The Sims 4 became free to play across PC and consoles this week, and now EA is turning its attention to a brand new game in the series.

Yesterday, EA held a Behind The Sims Summit, which you can watch below. As well as updates on what to expect from the existing games, the next generation of The Sims was teased along with its working title of "Project Rene."

Lyndsay Pearson, vice president of Franchise Creative for The Sims, explained how Project Rene is still in the very early stages of development but wanted to share some details.
