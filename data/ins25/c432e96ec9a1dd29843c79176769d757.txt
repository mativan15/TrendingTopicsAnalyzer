‘The lettuce has won': U.K. PM Liz Truss steps down after short-lived term — and Britain will get a new head
thestar.com
news
2022-10-20 15:13:00

After a tumultuous month and a half, Britain's prime minister Liz Truss has succumbed to the mounting political pressure and has resigned.

Or, based on how you've been following the chaos the past week across the pond, the lettuce has won.

Her quick term ended Thursday after weeks of unrest at Westminster and within her own cabinet over her controversial financial policies that created a steady flow of exits from her top ministers. Truss took the reins following another messy exit by a British leader, PM Boris Johnson, who left unceremoniously earlier in the summer.
