October 19, 2022 08:03 AM Eastern Daylight Time PITTSBURGH--(BUSINESS WIRE)--United States Steel Corporation (NYSE: X) ('U. S. Steel') today announced that it will release financial results for the third quarter 2022 on Thursday, October 27, 2022, following the close of trading on the New York Stock Exchange. Interested stockholders, investors, and others may listen to the company's webcast on Friday, October 28, 2022, at 8:30 a.m. ET. The webcast will discuss the third quarter 2022 financial results and provide a company update which may include forward-looking information.
