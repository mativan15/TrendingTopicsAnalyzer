Seems like Apple is upping the ante in the mixed reality competition by offering iris-scanning technology as the tech giant's forthcoming XR device will include sensors that work like Face ID in iPhones and iPads, as per Mashable. The outlet reported that the Information has shared the news regarding the new technology called 'Iris ID', which would enable users to log in their accounts and make payments biometrically. The headset also reportedly has more than 10 cameras and might have the same M2 chip as the one powering the latest MacBook Air, according to Mashable.
