Published: 15:04 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 15:05 EDT, 27 May 2022 Two members of a nationwide criminal network have been jailed for life for an EncroChat murder plot involving a James Bond-style gun.Jurors heard how fixer Paul Fontaine had supplied a 9mm Makarov self-loading pistol used to murder Abdullahi Mahamoud in a bagel shop in Enfield, north London, on March 19 2020.Weeks later, he helped arrange to supply a new Walther PPK handgun to Cardiff career criminal Frankie Sinclair to murder Keiron Hassan, and others in a rival group.
