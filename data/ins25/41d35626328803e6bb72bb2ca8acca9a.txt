[Paris, France, 18 October 2022] Joy Huang, President of Huawei Cloud Strategy and Industry Development, delivered a keynote speech themed "Huawei Cloud: Unleash Digital with Everything as a Service" on the second day of HUAWEI CONNECT 2022 in Paris, where he introduced how Huawei Cloud is devoted to a green, innovative, and secure digital future in Europe.
Global Market Progress and Everything as a Service
Huawei Cloud has progressed rapidly over the past year. According to a Gartner report, Huawei Cloud is ranked 5th in the IaaS market for 2021.
