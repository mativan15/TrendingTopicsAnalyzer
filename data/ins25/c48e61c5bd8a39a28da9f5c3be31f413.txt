Brown's governing body elected each new member based on their commitment to the University and its mission of education and research. PROVIDENCE, R.I. [Brown University] — During its annual spring meeting in May, the Corporation of Brown University elected two new members to its Board of Fellows and eight new members to its Board of Trustees. Each was invited to serve on Brown's governing body based on their commitment to the University and its mission of education and research. Robert Goodman, a 1982 Brown graduate and trustee from 2013 to 2019 and 2020 to 2022, and Alison Ressler, a Brown graduate and trustee since 2004 who is ending her second three-year term as the Corporation's vice chancellor, were elected to the Board of Fellows.
