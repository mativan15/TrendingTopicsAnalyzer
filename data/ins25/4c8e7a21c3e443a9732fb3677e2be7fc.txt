Matternet Takes Over Drone Business From Swiss Post, Announces Plans for First City-Wide Network in Switzerland

2022-06-01 08:05:40

Matternet will leverage its extensive experience from operations in Zurich and Lugano to build-out the first European city-wide drone delivery network in SwitzerlandMOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Today, Matternet announced it is embarking on the next chapter of its drone operations in Switzerland. Starting January 1, 2023, Matternet will take over drone business activities from Swiss Post, in addition to providing its technology platform and operating customer drone delivery networks.
