Crypto community high on hopes as govt finalises consultation paper

Agencies "The entire ecosystem is evolving rapidly and there is a need for a multi-country consensus for the ecosystem to flourish while keeping in mind the interests of individual countriesNew Delhi: The government will finalise a consultation paper on cryptocurrencies soon. However, the law and regulatory framework may take more time to be put in place. According to Economic Affairs Secretary Ajay Seth, the government is taking inputs from various stakeholders, including the World Bank and International Monetary Fund (IMF).
