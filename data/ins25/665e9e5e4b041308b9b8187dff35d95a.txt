Midland States Bancorp: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

news
2022-10-20 20:26:48

EFFINGHAM, Ill.

Midland States Bancorp, Inc. (MSBI) on Thursday reported third-quarter profit of $23.5 million.

The Effingham, Illinois-based bank said it had earnings of $1.04 per share.

The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of three analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.01 per share.

The company posted revenue of $95.4 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $79.9 million, also surpassing Street forecasts.
