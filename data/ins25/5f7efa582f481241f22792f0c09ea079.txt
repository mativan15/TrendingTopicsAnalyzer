By Reuters Published: 00:04 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 00:04 EDT, 21 October 2022 Ryan Nugent-Hopkins and Connor McDavid both had a goal and three assists as the host Edmonton Oilers cranked out a steady flow of offensive production to hand the Carolina Hurricanes their first loss of the season, 6-4 on Thursday night. Zach Hyman, Ryan McLeod, Evander Kane and Leon Draisaitl scored the other Edmonton goals. McDavid's goal was an empty-net tally with 10 seconds to play. Hyman and Draisaitl each contributed an assist, and Jack Campbell made 36 saves for the win.
