After Nissan, Hyundai too plans to shut Russia business: Reports
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 04:42:58

Several carmakers, including Nissan Motor and Renault, have already shut down operations in Russia amid the country's war against Ukraine this year.
 








Korean carmaker Hyundai had suspended operations at its Russian factory in March. The carmaker did not sell any cars in the last two months. (AP)
 
 Korean carmaker Hyundai Motor may soon exit Russia amid continuous war against Russia that has hit hard the automotive business in the country this year. According to a report by news agency Reuters, Hyundai is likely to shut down its operations completely in Russia and also sell its manufacturing facility to the state.
