Labour's Rachel Reeves has warned the 'damage has been done' despite the Government's 'humiliating U-turns'.The shadow chancellor called on the Government to 'extend a windfall tax on energy producers' adding that Prime Minister Liz Truss has 'no authority, no credibility, no plan for growth'.Ms Reeves cautioned 'every single public service is again at risk from the Conservatives' as she warned of 'austerity season two'.Addressing Chancellor Jeremy Hunt in the Commons, she said: 'The fourth in four months of chaos and fiasco as this Conservative Government spirals down the political plughole.
