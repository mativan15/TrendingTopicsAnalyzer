October 19, 2022 04:13 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW BRUNSWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Johnson & Johnson today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend for the fourth quarter of 2022 of $1.13 per share on the company's common stock. The dividend is payable on December 6, 2022 to shareholders of record at the close of business on November 22, 2022. The ex-dividend date is November 21, 2022.
