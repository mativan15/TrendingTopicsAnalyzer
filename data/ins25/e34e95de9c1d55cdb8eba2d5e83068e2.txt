Natalie CashJun 1, 2022 If you asked a guy who dates much-younger women WHY he does it, he'd say "looks" or he'd be lying. But maybe that's not the case for women who date younger men. A dating site called CougarLife.com polled its members and asked them to name the top benefits of dating a guy who's 10-or-more years younger than them. And the most popular answer was guys like that are more OPEN-MINDED.  (Sure, ladies . . . sure.) Physical attractiveness ranked second, and having a "free spirit" ranked third.
