What's in it for interims? Carolina's Steve Wilks latest Black coach thrust into challenging role
jacksonville.com
news
2022-10-15 14:10:46

When the Atlanta Falcons fired Dan Quinn five games into the 2020 season and promoted Raheem Morris to interim head coach, a reporter asked owner Arthur Blank a straightforward question: Would Morris be a candidate for the team's full-time job?'Absolutely,' Blank said. 'If Raheem ends up 11-0, he's going to be, certainly, a candidate.'Almost two years later, Carolina Panthers owner David Tepper fielded a similar question about his new interim head coach, Steve Wilks. And his answer, while more vague, conveyed the same message.
