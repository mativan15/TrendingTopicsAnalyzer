Lululemon's Strong Brand Beats Inflation
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-03 14:29:46

Lululemon's strong brand has helped the athletic apparel company shake off the many challenges the retail sector faces these days, including food and energy inflation, which has left little room for discretionary items in the stretched family budgets.On Thursday, Lululemon reported strong Q1 2022 financial results that beat analyst estimates. Net revenue increased 32% to $1.6 billion. In addition, total comparable sales increased 29% on a constant dollar basis, with similar store sales up 24%. Meanwhile, direct-to-consumer net revenue increased 33%, with direct-to-consumer net revenue accounting for 45% of total net income, up 44% from the first quarter of 2021.
