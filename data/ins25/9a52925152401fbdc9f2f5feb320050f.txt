Published: 16:54 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 16:54 EDT, 1 June 2022 Spanish police are investigating the death of a former princess of Qatar who claimed her ex-husband 'inappropriately' touched one their daughters.Princess Kasia Gallanio, 45, the third wife of billionaire Abdelaziz bin Khalifa Al Thani, 73, was found dead from a suspected drug overdose in Marbella weeks after describing her royal life as a 'nightmare'. She had also just lost a toxic custody battle over their daughters in the French courts just two weeks earlier - one of whom accused their father of sexual abuse - a claim he denies.
