Game recap: Miami Hurricanes hold off Virginia Tech Hokies in key ACC road win
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-15 20:24:10

The Miami Hurricanes ended a three-game losing streak on Saturday, downing the Virginia Tech Hokies 20-14 in Blacksburg, Va., in an ACC football game.

Tyler Van Dyke threw for 288 yards and two touchdowns as the 'Canes (3-3 overall, 1-2 ACC) got out to a 20-0 lead and held on. The Hokies fell to 2-5, 1-3.

Previously: The Miami Hurricanes are hoping to snap their losing streak on the road.

Mario Cristobal's first season at the U hasn't gone as planned. After starting the season with two convincing wins, the 'Canes have dropped three in a row, including last week's 27-24 loss to North Carolina.
