Pound rallies as Truss resigns
RT
news
2022-10-20 15:29:23

The resignation of the UK Prime Minister Liz Truss after just six weeks in office has bolstered the national currency, the pound.Sterling was up 0.5% touching $1.13 against the US dollar before slipping slightly back.British stocks also moved upwards initially on the FTSE 100 before retreating, with FTSE 250 still in the green.One analyst told the BBC that the markets were 'relieved' by Truss' departure despite there still being a lot of uncertainty.Announcing her resignation, Liz Truss admitted she couldn't deliver her mandate.
