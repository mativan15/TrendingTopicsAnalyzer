European auto industry in trouble S&P
russiaherald.com
news
2022-10-15 10:30:00

Inflation, supply chain issues and the energy crisis are weighing on output, according to a market data providerWith energy prices in EU skyrocketing, a harsh winter could shut down production in parts of the automotive sector, S&P Global Mobility reported this week.According to a report titled "Winter is Coming," the "combined black swan events" of the Covid-19 pandemic and the war in Ukraine have stretched automotive supply lines, especially with regard to semiconductors.As a result, the industry "may face extensive pressure" from energy costs in the coming months.
