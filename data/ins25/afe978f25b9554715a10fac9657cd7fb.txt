BROOKLYN, N.Y., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Etsy, Inc. (Nasdaq: ETSY), which operates two-sided online marketplaces that connect millions of passionate and creative buyers and sellers around the world, today announced virtual participation in the below investor events.Etsy will participate in a webcasted fireside chat at the Stifel 2022 Cross Sector Insight Conference on June 8, 2022 at 10:55 a.m. ET. The live webcast and replay of this session will be featured on our IR website at investors.etsy.
