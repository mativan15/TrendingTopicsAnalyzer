Three fourth-year students selected as 2022 Class Day speakers
uchicago.edu
news
2022-06-01 21:28:00

On June 3, University of Chicago fourth-year students David Liang, Zara Malik and Allison Mattessich will deliver remarks to their fellow graduates as part of the College's fifth annual Class Day ceremony. 
Each year, three students are selected to speak at Class Day, a tradition that celebrates the accomplishments of the College's graduating class. Class Day 2022 will take place this year as an in-person event for the first time since 2019, and will be webcast on the Convocation website starting this Friday at 2 p.
