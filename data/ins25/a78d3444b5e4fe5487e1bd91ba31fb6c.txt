Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal and his Punjab counterpart Bhagwant Mann will address public meetings in Gujarat today Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal and his Punjab counterpart Bhagwant Mann will address public meetings in Unjha town of Mehsana district and Deesa town in Banaskantha district of Gujarat on Monday, an Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader said. AAP national convener Kejriwal and Mann are on a two-day visit to poll-bound Gujarat from Sunday.

The election commission has not yet announced the poll programme for the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)-ruled state.
