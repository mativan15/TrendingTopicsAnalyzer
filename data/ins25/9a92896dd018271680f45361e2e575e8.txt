PARIS, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Akur8 and Sura are pleased to announce their collaboration to enhance Seguros Sura's pricing capabilities on personal auto lines across Argentina, Chile and Colombia. Through this new partnership, Akur8 reinforces its presence in Latin America, where the company has been present since its second year of operations, and further accelerates its global expansion.
