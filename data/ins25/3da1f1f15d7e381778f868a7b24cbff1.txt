SANTA MONICA, Calif. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 For some time now, Comrad socks have been synonymous with comfortable, stylish, and wearable compression socks. This is largely due to the way Andrew Ferenci approached the development of Comrad socks. After going through a medical experience that led him to compression socks as a solution for swelling and discomfort in his lower-legs, Andrew was relieved, but annoyed. While he finally seemed to find a viable solution for his discomfort, the compression socks available at that time were far from aesthetically pleasing.
