Andreas Nesslinger/iStock via Getty Images Have you ever invested in CLO-related investment vehicles? CLOs, Collateralized Loan Obligations, are securitizations of a portfolio of Senior Secured Loans. Funds like Eagle Point Credit Company LLC (NYSE:ECC) invest in CLOs. The US Leveraged Loan market returned to growth in 2021, up 12% in 2021, after a flat year in lockdown-challenged 2020. CLOs have 63% of this market, the largest market share, distantly followed by Loan Mutual funds, at 13%, and Banks, at 12%.
