Roadside cameras stolen in Sweden may end up in Russian drones media
europesun.com
news
2022-10-20 22:30:00

A wave of speed-camera thefts in the country is allegedly linked to the ongoing conflict between Moscow and KievSweden has been hit recently by a wave of roadside camera thefts, with up to 100 units reportedly missing already. Cameras of the same type have been discovered in Russian drones operating in Ukraine, local media reports, suggesting the occurrences might be linked.At least three more camera units had gone missing from the E16 highway between the towns of Hofors and Falun over the previous 24-hour period, daily newspaper Aftonbladet reported Wednesday.
