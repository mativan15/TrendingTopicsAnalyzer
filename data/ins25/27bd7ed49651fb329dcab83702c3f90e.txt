Suzuki Motorcycle sells 71,526 units in May
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 15:15:00

Suzuki Motorcycle India on Thursday said it sold 71,526 units in May. The company dispatched 60,518 units in the domestic market and exported 11,008 units last month. "The two-wheeler industry continues to maneuver through the challenges presented by the pandemic and supply-chain crisis. Despite these testing circumstances, the company has witnessed a satisfactory demand, both from the domestic and overseas markets," Suzuki Motorcycle India MD Satoshi Uchida said in a statement. 
 Last month, the company also commenced the deliveries of 250cc sports adventure tourer V-Strom SX, he added.
