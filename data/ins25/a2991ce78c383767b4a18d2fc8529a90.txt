Members of the Central team check the moisture content in paddy stocked at the Direct Purchase Centre in Manalmedu - 1 village in Mayiladuthurai district on Sunday.

A Central team led by Deputy Director of Quality Control Cell, Food Corporation of India, Hyderabad, M.Z. Khan visited Direct Purchase Centres in Mayiladuthurai and Nagapattinam districts on Sunday to examine moisture content in harvested paddy, in view of the latest spells of rainfall.In the backdrop of the start of paddy procurement from the start of this month, the visit was necessitated by the latest spells of rainfall that had increased the moisture content in the harvested paddy, according to officials.
