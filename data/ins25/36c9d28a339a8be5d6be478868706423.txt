October 17, 2022 03:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time PITTSBURGH--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PPG (NYSE:PPG) today announced its inclusion in the inaugural American Opportunity Index, which measures efforts to create opportunities for employees. PPG ranks #44 among the largest 250 U.S. public companies. The focus of the assessment is on workers' access to role, wage and career advancement.



'At PPG, we know our greatest strength is our people, who are focused on fulfilling our purpose and protecting and beautifying the world every day.
