Government deficit and debt decreased in 2021
europa.eu
news
2022-10-21 13:15:00

In 2021, the government deficit of the EU decreased compared with 2020, as did the government debt. In the EU, the government deficit to GDP ratio shrunk from 6.7% in 2020 to 4.6% in 2021 (-2.1 percentage points, pp). Meanwhile, the government debt to GDP ratio decreased from 89.8% at the end of 2020 to 87.9% at the end of 2021 (-1.9 pp).Â  This information comes from data on government deficit and debt for the years 2018-2021, based on figures reported by EU Member States in the second notification in 2022 for the application of the excessive deficit procedure (EDP).
