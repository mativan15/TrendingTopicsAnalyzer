Dune's sell-out Chanel-dupe sandals are back in stock, but are they worth the hype?

2022-06-01 11:41:31

The fateful combination of being an editor on a shopping section and being easily influenced by fashion trends is bad news for my bank balance. Zara welly boots taking Instagram by storm? In my basket. Blush is the new bronzer? Sign me up. So when Dune's dupe of the coveted Chanel dad sandals returnedlast year, I was ready to invest. Regrettably, the more popular something is, the stronger my desire is to snap it up, and boy do these high street lockstockk sandals fit the bill. They initially launched in May 2020 and sold out within a week, before being restocked (with an 800-strong waitlist of eager shoppers) and selling out once more.
