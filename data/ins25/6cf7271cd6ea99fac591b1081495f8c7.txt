 Police are seeking the public's assistance identifying a suspect wanted in connection with a mischief investigation in Toronto.
Toronto police said on Oct. 18 at around 5:45 p.m., officers received a report that approximately 21 vehicles had been vandalized in the Markham and Robinson streets area.Officers said swastikas and 'various other markings' were etched onto the paint of the vehicles.
