Joe Raedle/Getty Images News Costco Wholesale Corporation (NASDAQ:COST) is being defended vigorously by Wall Street bulls after delivering a double beat with its FQ3 earnings report. Oppenheimer noted better than expected expense controls helped to offset weaker gross margins during the quarter. The firm sees some headwinds to the COST bull case in the near term on the margin front, but advises investors to take advantage of any dips with the retail stock that it has slotted as one of its top picks.
