Fourteen years into the Marvel Cinematic Universe — practically the very last shared experience in pop culture — we all know the beats of a superhero narrative. A person (or alien, or god) who has uncanny abilities uses them to help solve problems that plague less powerful people. This causes difficulties in this person/alien/god's private life, because exercising their powers can have unintended consequences, and maintaining the secrecy required for their good works isolates them from the people they love.
