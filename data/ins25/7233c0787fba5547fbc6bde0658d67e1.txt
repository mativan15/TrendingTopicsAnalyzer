NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--Scott+Scott Attorneys at Law LLP ('Scott+Scott'), an international securities and consumer rights litigation firm, continues investigating certain directors and officers of Guess?, Inc. ('Guess') (NYSE: GES) for breaching their fiduciary duties to Guess and its shareholders. If you are a Guess shareholder, you may contact attorney Joe Pettigrew for additional information toll-free at 844-818-6982 orjpettigrew@scott-scott.com.Scott+Scott is investigating whether Guess's board of directors or senior management failed to manage Guess in an acceptable manner, in breach of their fiduciary duties to Guess, and whether Guess has suffered damages as a result.
