This insurance policy could protect your retirement portfolio -- but is it worth it?
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 11:05:00

It might not qualify as a brand-new hot-off-the-press idea. Academics, actuaries, regulators and others have been talking about contingent deferred annuities (CDAs) for more than a decade.But CDAs are enjoying a resurgence of late, in large part because this product can help retirees and would-be retirees manage two of retirement's biggest risks: longevity risk (the risk of outliving one's assets) and sequence-of-returns risk (the risk of withdrawing assets in down markets early in retirement, which can ravage a portfolio).
