Assume you are the parent or grandparent of one of the 19 precious elementary school students slaughtered in Uvalde, Texas, on Tuesday. Take on their grief by looking into the eyes of your own loved ones and realize that they are now prey.Assume you are the husband or a relative of one of the two teachers who bravely tried to defend their classes from the shooter only to be gunned down.Assume you knew the troubled 18-year-old who committed this unspeakable act, but you did not try to help him with his demons because you did not know how to reach him.
