Cryptocurrencies and crypto-related stocks took a breather today after rallying over the last several days, and as the market continued to have concerns about the future of the economy.
Over the last 24 hours, the price of the world's largest cryptocurrency, Bitcoin (BTC -5.96%), had fallen roughly 5% as of 12:55 p.m. ET today. Meanwhile, shares of the large crypto exchange Coinbase Global (COIN -12.55%) traded more than 12% lower, while crypto bank Silvergate Capital (SI -8.04%) was down nearly 10%.
