Last month viewers of the ITV soap watched in horror as Stephen Reid (Todd Boyce) threw Leo Thompkins (Joe Frost) over the edge of a two-story building to his death after threatening to expose his lies. With Stephen disposing of the body afterwards, the residents of Weatherfield are left believing he's simply skipped town. Knowing that there's no one to expose the truth behind him wanting Audrey's money, the Canadian businessman puts his final plot to con his mother Audrey into motion.The Coronation Street resident became the latest villain of the ITV soap after he killed Leo for threatening to expose him to the family.
