Jonathan Kitchen/DigitalVision via Getty Images This year, the iShares Russell Top 200 Growth ETF (NYSEARCA:IWY) has been one of the most afflicted tech-heavy exchange-traded funds on the Street, losing around 21%. Some of its constituents are much deeper in the red, like Netflix (NFLX) which lost ~68% YTD, tumbling by ~35% on April 20 as investors were solidly dissatisfied with the company losing subscribers, which casts a shadow on future growth and begs a question whether its premium multiples make sense.
