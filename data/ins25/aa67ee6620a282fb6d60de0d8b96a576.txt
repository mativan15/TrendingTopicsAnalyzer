Adani arm bags Rs 1,300 cr smart meters installation deal from Mumbai's BEST
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-10-20 07:00:00

AgenciesAdani TransmissionAdani Transmission on Thursday announced that it has bagged a deal to install and maintain 10.80 lakh smart meters for BEST Undertaking. Sources pegged the deal value for the multi-year-old engagement by Adani Transmission's distribution platform at Rs 1,300 crore. The Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport Undertaking is a state-run utility that serves consumers in the island city exclusively. Adani Transmission's subsidiary Adani Electricity Mumbai distributes power in the suburbs, where it competes with Tata Power.
