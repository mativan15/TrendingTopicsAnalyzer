College student falls prey to online scam, loses 7.8 lakh
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 06:30:00

Coimbatore: A 22-year-old college student lost 7.84 lakh after he fell prey to a scam on Locanto , a user-to-user classifieds platform.The parents of the student, residents of Ashok Street at Udyampalayam , had deposited 7.84 lakh in a bank for his use, the cybercrime police said. 'He was looking to date someone on Locanto on Saturday around 3.30pm and registered using his mobile number. A person, who introduced himself as Kumar, contacted him over phone and promised to arrange escort service. The student was told to visit a hotel in Coimbatore city.
