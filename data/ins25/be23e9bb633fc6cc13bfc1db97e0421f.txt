Rush of preorders for this autonomous lawnmower
zdnet.com
news
2022-05-31 11:00:10

Scythe RoboticsAutonomous lawnmowers are coming to the U.S. in a big way. The latest proof: thousands of reservations for an autonomous, all-electric powerhouse designed for the commercial landscaping sector, as well as key talent acquisitions by the company that makes the mower from the tech giants SpaceX Starlink and Magic Leap.The company, Scythe Robotics, believes the market conditions are primed to help push a robotic overhaul to a sector that hasn't had a substantial technology upgrade in the better part of a century.
