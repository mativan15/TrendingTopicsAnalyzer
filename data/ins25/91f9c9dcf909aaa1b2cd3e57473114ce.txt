Good morning,Ottawa police should have heeded OPP intelligence reports on convoy's plans, inquiry hearsA senior commander from the Ottawa police says the force should have given more weight to intelligence reports that suggested convoy protesters intended to remain in the capital for a long period of time.Acting Deputy Chief Patricia Ferguson testified before the Emergencies Act inquiry that the service planned for a standard protest, even as they were aware large numbers of trucks and other vehicles were bound for Ottawa at the end of January.
