The Chairman and Chief Purpose Officer of co:collective to talk about how purpose-led companies are leading the way

New York, NY --News Direct-- 1BusinessWorld

Renowned author, speaker, entrepreneur and agent of change Ty Montague, the Chairman and Chief Purpose Officer of co:collective, joins the 2022 Leading Entrepreneurs of the World Conference to talk about how purpose-led companies are the ones leading the way.

In a world where customer and employee expectations have never been higher, and businesses are defined by the value they create and the values they demonstrate, Ty and his team at co:collective work with leadership teams to help them align behind a core story and lead stronger and more successful businesses.
