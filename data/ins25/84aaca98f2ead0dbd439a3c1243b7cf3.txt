 The rupee inched 4 paise higher to close at 77.54 (provisional) against the United States dollar on Monday, supported by positive domestic equities and a weak American currency overseas.Forex traders said the rupee consolidated in a narrow range as elevated crude oil prices and persistent FII outflows weighed on the local unit.At the interbank forex market, the rupee opened at 77.53 against the greenback and moved in a range of 77.
