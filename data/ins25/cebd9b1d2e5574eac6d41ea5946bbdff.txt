Image source, GoogleImage caption, Slough Borough Council has been limited to spending on essential services only sinceA cash-strapped council has revealed it needs to find an extra £6m of cuts. The government appointed commissioners to address Labour-run Slough Borough Council's money problems in 2021. It had been tasked with finding £20m of savings in the next financial year but officers have now been told this figure has been revised to £26m, according to the Local Democracy Reporting Service.
