By Reuters Published: 03:34 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 03:34 EDT, 21 October 2022 FTSE 100 down 0.6%, FTSE 250 off 0.7% Oct 21 (Reuters) - UK's main equity indexes fell on Friday, as lower-than-expected retail sales figures and a jump in bond yields added to the market's weak sentiment following a tumultuous week for politics in the country. The blue-chip FTSE 100 index fell 0.6% by 0707 GMT, while the midcap FTSE 250 slipped 0.7%. Liz Truss quit on Thursday after the shortest and most chaotic tenure of any British prime minister, forced out after her unfunded tax cut proposals crashed the pound and sent British borrowing costs soaring.
