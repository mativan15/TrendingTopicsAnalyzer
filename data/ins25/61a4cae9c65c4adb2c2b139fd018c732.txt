Published: 01:06 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 01:06 EDT, 20 October 2022 Anne Hathaway is a mother of two and a world-renowned actress, and she revealed the former aspect of her life has certainly influenced the latter.The 39-year-old Hathaway shares two sons - six-year-old Jonathan and two-year-old Jack - with her husband of a decade, Adam Shulman.While discussing her new role in the upcoming drama Armageddon Time, arriving in theaters November 4, she opened up about how having kids has made her 'choosier' in picking what roles to pursue.
