MINNEAPOLIS, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nuwellis, Inc. (Nasdaq: NUWE) ("Nuwellis" or the "Company"), a commercial-stage company focused on transforming the lives of people with fluid overload, today announced the closing of an underwritten public offering of units with gross proceeds of approximately $11.04 million, which includes the full exercise of the underwriter's over-allotment option to purchase additional shares and warrants, prior to deducting underwriting discounts and commissions and offering expenses payable by Nuwellis.
