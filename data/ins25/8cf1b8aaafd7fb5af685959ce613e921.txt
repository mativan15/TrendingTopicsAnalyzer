June 3, 2022 / 11:00 PM / CBS Miami MIAMI (CBSMiami) - A spokesman for the American Automobile Association is offering some money-saving tips for consumers as gas prices continue to climb.AAA spokesman Mark Jenkins says you can save on fuel by reducing your speed on the highway, leaving for work earlier when there is less congestion and shopping around as prices vary from one area to the next.Whether you have a Toyota Prius or a Cadillac Escalade, it is advisable not to wait until your gas tank is empty.
