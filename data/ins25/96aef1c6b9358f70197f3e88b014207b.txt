Suspension of senior doctor by Bihar deputy CM Tejashwi Prasad Yadav evokes outcry
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 15:45:00

PATNA: The Bihar government appeared to be on a collision course with the medical fraternity on Sunday over the recent suspension of a doctor upon the order of deputy chief inister Tejashwi Prasad Yadav , who holds the health portfolio.Yadav had, on Friday night, carried out surprise inspections of a number of government hospitals to take stock of preparedness for the outbreak of dengue.He seemed displeased at the state of affairs at NMCH, the second largest public health care facility in the city and issued the order for suspension of its superintendent Dr Vinod Kumar Singh, who cried foul.
