AudioEye Shares Rise 22% After $3M Buyback Plan
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 16:29:00

By Chris Wack AudioEye Inc. shares were up 22% to $4.11 after the company said its board of directors has authorized the repurchase of up to $3 million of common stock. The buyback plan has an expiration date of June 30, 2024. The company said it intends to fund the stock repurchase program with working capital and cash from operations. "We remain committed to strategically deploying capital that will drive the greatest value for stockholders," said David Moradi, chief executive of AudioEye. "This stock repurchase program reflects the strength of our business and our confidence in our future cash flow.
