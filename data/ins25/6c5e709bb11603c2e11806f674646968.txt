Tottenham can achieve new record they've never managed before

Tottenham will enjoy their best ever start to a Premier League season if they beat Everton on Saturday. The Lilywhites have been in fine form this campaign and are third in the table after winning six of their first nine matches.Tottenham appointed Antonio Conte as their manager last November following the sacking of Nuno Espirito Santo. And the Italian led the side to a top-four Premier League finish.Conte bolstered his team with a whole host of signings over the summer as he prepared Spurs to go in search of Premier League silverware this time around.
