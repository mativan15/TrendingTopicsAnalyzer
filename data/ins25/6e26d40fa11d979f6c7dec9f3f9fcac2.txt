Results of NEET MDS 2022: The National Board of Medical Sciences (NBEMS) has officially announced the results of the NEET MDS tests conducted on 2 May 2022. Students can view their results on the official website, nbe.edu.in, and natbord.edu.in. The long-awaited results of The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Master of Dental Surgery (NEET-MDS 2022 test) were announced by the National Board Examination in Medical Science (NBEMS) on 27 May 2022. Along with the results, the NEET MDS 2022 school cut-off percentage for each phase has been released.
