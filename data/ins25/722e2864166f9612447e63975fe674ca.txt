Maala pushes Israeli companies to make impact on society, environment
jpost.com
news
2022-05-29 10:57:00

Momo Mahadav is the CEO of Maala, a non-profit corporate membership organization promoting Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) in Israel. Under his leadership, the organization boasts a roster of 115 companies, all of which are committed to going beyond business as usual and making an effort to give back to society and the planet's health.

Mahadav started his career as a community organizer, working on projects such as urban regeneration in Florentine, and urban sustainability for the Society for the Protection of Nature.
