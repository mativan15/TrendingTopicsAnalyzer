Man stabs brother to death with sword on family's front porch: police
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 11:01:11

A man allegedly stabbed his brother to death on his family's front porch, according to authorities in Missouri.Kansas City police were alerted to reports that someone had been cut and raced to the 6200 block of Tracy St on Thursday.When police arrived, they found the victim, Karl Winn, on the front porch and determined a sword had been put through his chest.According to a homicide tracker by NBC affiliate KSHB, there have been 219 homicides in the greater Kansas City metropolitan area as of October 9.
