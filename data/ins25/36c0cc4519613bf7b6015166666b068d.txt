Published: 18:17 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 18:17 EDT, 19 October 2022 The daughters of the man behind Grand Designs' infamous lighthouse have revealed how the 'overambitious' project impacted their lives. Lauren, 24, and Nicole Short, 22, grew up as their parents Edward and Hazel Short tried to build their ambitious lighthouse in North Devon and discuss how it has affected them in a special airing tonight at 9pm on Channel 4. Short, 52, spent a decade building the extraordinary Chesil Cliff House, in Croyde, Devon, with the ambitious project seeing him transform his family's 1950s home into an art-deco white lighthouse.
