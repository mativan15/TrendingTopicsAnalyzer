Published: 21:03 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 21:03 EDT, 17 October 2022 The controversial fly-on-the-wall documentary series featuring the Duke and Duchess of Sussex for Netflix has been postponed until next year, following the widespread backlash over The Crown.Harry and Meghan had been working on the series as part of their rumoured $100 million (£88million) deal with the streaming giant.But with The Crown accused of fabricating a ‘hurtful' smear against King Charles by depicting him secretly plotting to oust the Queen, Netflix has now pushed it back.
