Energy crisis POLL: Will you 'spend to save' by replacing gas boiler with heat pump?
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 08:27:00

With a goal to help households amid the worsening energy crisis, as well as hit net-zero targets, ministers now want 600,000 heat pumps installed every year until 2028. However, with the average heat pump installation costing between £5,000 and £15,000 – up to 10 times the average £1,500 gas boiler – few households are able to make the change, despite assurances that the initial cost would be offset by lower bills going forward.Utilita Energy's inaugural 'Household Energy Behaviour Index' investigated the nation's attitudes towards home energy.
