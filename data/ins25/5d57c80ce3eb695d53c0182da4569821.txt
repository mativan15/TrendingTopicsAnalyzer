Retail investors unload over 1 trillion won of Kospi stocks in May

2022-06-01 14:50:00

A foreign exchange dealer reads news covering the announcements made by the Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell at Hana Bank in central Seoul on May 6. [YONHAP] Retail investors unloaded more than 1 trillion won ($795 million) worth of Kospi stocks in May as the market continued to stay sluggish and central banks around the world push monetary tightening. Retail investors net sold 1.003 trillion won worth of Kospi stocks in May, according to data from Korea Exchange. It was the first month this year in which they net sold Kospi stocks.
