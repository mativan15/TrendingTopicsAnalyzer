Rafael Nadal was frustrated by how late the match rumbled on (Image: GETT)Rafael Nadal was in agreement with Novak Djokovic that their French Open quarter-final clash started and finished "too late" after the Spaniard finally booked his place in the semi-finals after 1.15am local time. The seasoned 35-year-old, who is now hunting his record 14th title at Roland-Garros, had asked officials not to play the night session - a request that was snubbed before he battled past the resilient Serbian to win 6-2 4-6 6-2 7-6 (7-4).
