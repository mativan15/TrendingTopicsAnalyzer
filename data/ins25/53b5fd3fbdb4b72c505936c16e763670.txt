Danielle Cohen-Shohet had a lifelong interest in beauty. But when she started a side hustle in college as a makeup artist, she realized the difficulties that came with managing a business, scheduling her services, and maintaining relationships with clients. So she set out to create a solution to those problems.In 2015, she launched GlossGenius, a technology company that sells software to beauty salons to make their business systems more efficient. For example, the software-as-a-service platform helps salons manage client payments, keep track of inventory, and schedule appointments.
