Anthony Albanese announces flood support payments for parts of Victoria and Tasmania
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-16 04:16:31

Federal government support payments are now available to victims of flooding in a number of areas along the east coast.People in five Victorian and three Tasmanian local government areas are eligible for paymentsThe government will provide $1,000 per adult and $400 per childThe money comes on top of other payments available to those affectedThose in the Victorian local government areas (LGAs) of Campaspe, Greater Shepparton, Maribyrnong, Mitchell and Strathbogie along with the Tasmanian LGAs of Central Coast, Kentish and Meander Valley are now eligible for the one-off, non-means-tested payments.
