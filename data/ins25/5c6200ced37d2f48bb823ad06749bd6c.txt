Khargone violence: Tribunal orders 50 accused to pay over ₹7L in damages
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 18:20:37

The claim tribunal set up in connection with the Ram Navami violence in Madhya Pradesh's Khargone in April ordered the recovery of ₹7.46 lakh from 50 people accused by six complainants of causing damages to their property, officials said on Saturday.Communal clashes erupted in Khargone city on Ram Navami on April 10, resulting in one death. During the violence, houses and shops were damaged and vehicles torched. A total of 220 people were arrested, of which 200 are still in prison.To recover the losses incurred by the victims, chief minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan-led state government set up its first claim tribunal within 48 hours of the incident.
