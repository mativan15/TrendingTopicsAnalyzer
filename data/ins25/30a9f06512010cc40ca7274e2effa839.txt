By David Lawder and Andrea Shalal

WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Global financial leaders are digging into a long to-do list after International Monetary Fund and World Bank member countries sent them a clear message on what is needed to tackle overlapping crises: vast amounts of additional money.

It's a big ask, and one that may prove too tall an order to fulfill, at least in the near term.
