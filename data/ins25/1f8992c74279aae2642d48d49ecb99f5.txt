Houston Texans head coach Lovie Smith wears a 'We are Texans. We stand with Uvalde' t-shirt during OTAs Wednesday, June 1, 2022, at Houston Methodist Training Center in Houston. Smith and the staff, along with many of the players wore the shirt supporting the victims and their families of the Uvalde school shooting.Brett Coomer/Staff photographerThe Houston Texans are donating $400,000 to the Robb School Memorial Fund, which will provide financial assistance to the Uvalde community after 19 elementary students and two teachers were shot and killed last week.
