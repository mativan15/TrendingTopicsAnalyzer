India, Israel adopt 'vision statement' to boost defence ties
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 09:30:00

PTI Israeli Defence Minister Benny Gantz shakes hands with Indian Navy chief Admiral R Hari Kumar as Defence Minister Rajnath Singh looks on, in New Delhi.Marking 30 years of diplomatic ties between the two countries, India and Israel adopted a "vision statement" which will pave the way for strengthening defence cooperation in future. Union Defence Minister Rajnath Singh and his Israeli counterpart Benny Gantz on Thursday held bilateral talks in New Delhi. The two leaders discussed defence cooperation and the prevailing global and regional scenario.
