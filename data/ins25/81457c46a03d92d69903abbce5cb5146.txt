REUTERS/Brendan McDermid/FilesBENGALURU, Oct 17 (Reuters) - JPMorgan Chase & Co (JPM.N) said on Monday it named former Reliance Industries (RELI.NS) executive Navin Wadhwani as the head of its Indian investment banking business, under a slew of management changes for the Asia-Pacific region.Wadhwani has headed M&A for oil-to-retail conglomerate Reliance for the past 10 years and was previously a senior managing director at Rothschild India.
