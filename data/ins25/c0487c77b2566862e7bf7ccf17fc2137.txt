AgenciesThe issue was overall subscribed 1.04 times, with quota for QIB investors getting 1.06 times subscription whereas HNI portion fetched 1.48 times bids. Retail portion was booked only 84 per cent.New Delhi: Ethos made a weak debut on the Dalal street as the luxury watch retailer was listed at Rs 830, a discount of 5 per cent over its issue price of Rs 878 on BSE. On National Stock Exchange (NSE), the counter was listed at a discount of 6 per cent at Rs 825 as against the given issue price. A day before listing, shares of Ethos were changing hands at a marginal premium in the grey market, signaling towards the muted listing of the company.
