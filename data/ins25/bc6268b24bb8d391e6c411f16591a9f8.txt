It is sad that neither of the two big Diwali bashes of Bollywood at the Bachchans and Aamir Khan will be held this year.There was a time when guests would have a tough time deciding how to attend both the parties.In 2013, Aamir Khan's residence on Carter Road in Bandra was the venue for his historic marathon night-long Diwali party. The cards-boozing-eating marathon session kick-started at 8 pm and continued till 11 am the next morning when the last of the guests left after a late breakfast.
