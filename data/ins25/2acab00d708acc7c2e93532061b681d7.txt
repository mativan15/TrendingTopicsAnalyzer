Campbell Wilson, the first CEO & MD of privatised Air India probably has the toughest yet most coveted job in aviation. The 52 year old has to turn around Air India- an airline the Tata group bought back home after six decades.In his first media interaction since taking over in May, Wilson charts out the path that the salt-to-software conglomerate is following to make one of their most coveted acquisitions a success.
