United Spirits, India's largest liquor company, said it will sell about 32 mass-priced brands, including Haywards, Old Tavern, White-Mischief, Honey Bee, Green Label and Romanov, to Singapore headquartered Inbrew for a total cash consideration of Rs 820 crore.
