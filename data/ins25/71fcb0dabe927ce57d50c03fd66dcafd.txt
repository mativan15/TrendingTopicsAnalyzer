IANSRahul Gandhi during the Bharat jodo yatra in KarnatakaCongress General Secretary in-charge of Communications Jairam Ramesh on Sunday said that Rahul Gandhi, who is currently leading the Bharat Jodo Yatra in Karnataka, will cast his vote for the party's Presidential election at the Yatra's campsite in Sanganakallu. Polls to the party's highest post is scheduled for October 17, and the result will be declared on October 19. There've been queries on where @RahulGandhi will cast his vote tomorrow for Congress Presidential election.
