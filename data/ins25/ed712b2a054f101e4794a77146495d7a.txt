'Finding Food': Russia Severs Link To Ukraine's Besieged East
ibtimes.com
news
2022-05-27 08:09:42

A Ukrainian coal miner, completely starved of information, thought he might be able to reason with Russian forces blocking the last good road running out of his war-scarred city on the eastern front.Artyom Ivasenko was nearly killed the last time he drove south to stock up on food and medicine for his father and the other people living in the basement of his home in Lysychansk, the target of ceaseless shelling.And that was when the last paved route to places under slightly less fire was still technically under the control of Ukrainian troops.
