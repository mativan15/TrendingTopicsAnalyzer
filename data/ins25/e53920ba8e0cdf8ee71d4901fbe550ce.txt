Organisers reflect on Coventry's year as UK City of Culture

2022-05-27 13:45:57

Image caption, One thousand people took part in the This is the City parade to mark Coventry's final month as the UK City of CultureCoventry's term as UK City of Culture has generated more than £170m, its chief executive said as the event ends.Chief executive Martin Sutherland said despite Covid-19 restrictions affecting many events, the city had "really responded to it".The City of Culture team received £18m from government and other funders and said it had delivered value for money.Mr Sutherland said the year had seen people create art and culture in their own neighbourhoods.
