As per reports, the tech company will be launching Realme GT Neo 3T with Qualcomm's Snapdragon 870.Realme is all set to launch its new smartphone, GT Neo 3T in June. While the fans in India will have to wait a little longer to get their hands on the new smartphone, the tech company will be launching GT Neo 3T in Indonesia on June 7. The much awaited launch is all set to take place at 8.30 AM Jakarta time, which is 7 am IST. The launch event will be live-streamed on the official YouTube and Twitter handle of Realme Indonesia so fans across the world can attend it.
