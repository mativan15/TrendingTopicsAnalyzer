The chairman of the House of Commons Privileges Committee has confirmed when he will stand down to allow an inquiry to go ahead into whether the Prime Minister lied to Parliament over partygate.Labour MP Chris Bryant said in a statement on Friday that he has summoned the committee to a meeting on June 7 to finalise business before a new head is elected.It follows the publication on Wednesday of the Sue Gray report into Downing Street gatherings, which said 'senior leadership' at No 10 must 'bear responsibility' for the culture that led to lockdown rules being broken at a series of events in 2020 and 2021.
