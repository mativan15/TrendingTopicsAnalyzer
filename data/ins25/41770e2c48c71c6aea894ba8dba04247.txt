alvarez It is certainly not news to anyone reading this that one of the biggest challenges facing the market and the American economy today is the extremely high inflation that is straining the budgets of many families. This inflation has naturally been causing the market to decline as fears of an economic slowdown intensify and the Federal Reserve raises interest rates in an effort to contain the inflation. The combination of rising prices and shrinking portfolios has likely left many retirees wondering what they can do to preserve and even grow their wealth in the face of this inflation.
