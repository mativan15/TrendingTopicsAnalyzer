AIIMS withdraws SOP order
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-21 16:58:50

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi, on Friday withdrew with immediate effect an order which it claimed was brought in, earlier this month, to streamline treatment facilities for all sitting parliamentarians coming to the health facility.The order, issued by newly-appointed AIIMS director Dr. M. Srinivas, faced immediate and strong disapproval from doctors within and outside the institution. They called it a move to encourage "VIP culture" at the government hospital.The director had written to the joint secretary in the Lok Sabha secretariat Y.
