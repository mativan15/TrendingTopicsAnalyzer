Bank transfer

The big news in real estate Wednesday was another downtown employer moving offices. But in this case, JPMorgan Chase is not going far, trading one office tower for another. In case you missed it Tuesday, the same can't be said for another business in the city center.

Paulson poll

Merritt Paulson has been gradually giving up control of the Portland Thorns and Timbers soccer teams since news broke this month of his mishandling of abuse reports against a former coach.
