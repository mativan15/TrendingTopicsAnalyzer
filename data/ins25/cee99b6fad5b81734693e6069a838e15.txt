TIE SURPRISE According to Mr. Doonan, there is nothing you can't do with an old, obsolete necktie.Illustration: Raquel AparicioBy Simon DoonanUpdated June 4, 2022 12:01 am ETListen to article(3 minutes)I RECENTLY accompanied a friend on an upstate New York house-hunting expedition that catapulted us both into the bowel-curdling extremes of the paranormal. In a single day we repeatedly encountered the same uncanny phenomenon. During the course of our house tours we kept stumbling upon spooky attics and garages stuffed to the rafters with business suits, shirts, Larry King suspenders, lightly scuffed wingtips, low-rise loafers and garbage bags full of neckties, neckties and more neckties.
