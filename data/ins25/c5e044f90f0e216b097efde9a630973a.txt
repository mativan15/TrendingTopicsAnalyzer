Now's your chance to recognize Central Florida's top businesswomen for Orlando Business Journal 's 2023 Women Who Mean Business Awards.

Go here to nominate before the Dec. 2, 2022, deadline.

Nominees must be based in Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Volusia or Brevard counties to be eligible.

Nominations will be judged based primarily on business accomplishments, but community service and personal accomplishments also will have some bearing on the decisions.
