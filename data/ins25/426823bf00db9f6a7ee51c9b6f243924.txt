The son of a lifelong educator, Derrick Gragg is determined to stay connected to the classroom while serving as Northwestern's Combe Family Vice President for Athletics & Recreation.'I've always wanted to be fully integrated into campus life,' Gragg said. 'I've been an adjunct faculty member for nearly 18 years at different institutions, and it's just a part of me.
