PM Narendra Modi to visit Ayodhya on Diwali eve
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 06:45:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Ayodhya in Uttar Pradesh to have "darshan" and perform a "puja" at the Ram temple on the eve of Diwali on Sunday. He will also inspect the construction site of the grand Ram temple and later, perform a symbolic coronation of Lord Ram, reported PTI citing a statement issued by the Prime Minister's Office (PMO). 
At around 6.30 pm, the prime minister will witness the "aarti" on the banks of the Saryu river, which will be followed by the launch of the grand "Deepotsav" celebrations by him, the statement said.
