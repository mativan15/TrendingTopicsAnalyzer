It's officially tick season, and depending on where you live, that means it's more important to check yourself, your family members and your pets for those pesky bloodsuckers after you've spent time outdoors. Ticks add an extra layer of anxiety, too, because they sometimes carry illness, such as Lyme disease -- so not finding them can have serious consequences. And if you accidentally break off part of the tick while trying to remove it, it can still transmit its illness and the bite can also get infected.
