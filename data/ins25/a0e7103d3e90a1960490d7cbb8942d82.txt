LeBron James Becomes First Active NBA Player To Become A Billionaire
iheart.com
news
2022-06-02 14:34:13

By Jason HallJune 2, 2022Los Angeles Lakers forward LeBron James has officially a billionaire, becoming the first NBA player to do so during his active playing career, Forbes.com reports. The four-time NBA champion netted $121.2 million in total earnings last year, increasing his official net work to $1 billion, joining the likes of Michael Jordan and the late Kobe Bryant, both of whom exceeded their respective $1 billion net worths after retirement from their Hall of Fame playing careers. "It's my biggest milestone,' James told GQ in 2014 about the potential of one day becoming a billionaire.
