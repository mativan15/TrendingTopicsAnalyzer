Rhythmic event omission shocks Welsh Gymnastics
bbc.com
energy
2022-10-17 16:30:39

Welsh Gymnastics (WG) is working with its counterparts to "understand the impact" on the sport of the decision to omit Rhythmic Gymnastics from the 2026 Commonwealth Games.The Welsh governing body called the decision "extremely disappointing".WG said there is "a desire" to explore the possibility of a Commonwealth Championship with British Gymnastics and Scottish Gymnastics.Golf, BMX and coastal rowing will make debuts at the 2026 Games in Australia.Rhythmic Gymnastics is among the sports making way.
