NEWARK, Del, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The plant-based meat market size is set to be valued at US$ 4,784.2 Mn in 2022 and is expected to reach US$ 7,429.7 Mn by 2032. The market is projected to exhibit growth at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period (2022-2032). Plant-based products are both healthier and safer than animal-based products. The plant-based meat market is being driven by the growing popularity of veganism around the world. Increasing adoption of plant-based meat is primarily due to its rising popularity among consumers, as well as the product's versatility in terms of application.
