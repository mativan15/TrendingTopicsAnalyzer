Bad blood and bad history: Everything you need to know for Yankees-Astros ALCSOur MLB insiders Bob Nightengale and Steve Gardner get you ready for the ALCS between the Yankees and Astros.HOUSTON - A selfie-seeking fan ran onto the Minute Maid Park field moments before the top of the ninth inning during Game 2 of the American League Championship Series, spending several moments with All-Star second baseman Jose Altuve before he was accosted by security personnel. With closer Ryan Pressly poised to record the final three outs of an eventual 3-2 victory that gave Houston a 2-0 ALCS lead over the New York Yankees, the game was delayed about three minutes as the fan, clad in a Craig Biggio jersey, hopped onto the field and escaped notice as the Astros concluded between-innings warm-ups.
