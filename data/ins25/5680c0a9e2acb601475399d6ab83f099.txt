Tottenham's problem solving pleases Conte in win over Everton
reuters.com
news
2022-10-16 02:10:00

LONDON, Oct 15 (Reuters) - Tottenham Hotspur manager Antonio Conte praised his side's ability to "find solutions" as they overcame a resolute Everton for a 2-0 win in the Premier League on Saturday.A second-half penalty by Harry Kane and Pierre-Emile Hojbjerg's late effort sealed the points that took Tottenham to 23 points from their opening 10 league games.It is Tottenham's best ever points haul after 10 games of a Premier League campaign and they moved to within a point of leaders Arsenal, who play on Sunday.
