Plastic bags and straws to be banned on the Isle of Man in 2023
bbc.com
world
2022-10-20 10:39:38

Image source, PA MediaImage caption, Single-use plastic carrier bags are among the items set to be bannedA range of common single-use plastics like straws and carrier bags will be banned on the Isle of Man next year.Tynwald members voted unanimously to replace the sale and supply of 10 items with more sustainable alternatives.Businesses have 12 months to make changes before regulations make it illegal to offer them.It makes the island one of the first places in the British Isles to commit to reducing plastic use in this way, a government spokesman said.
