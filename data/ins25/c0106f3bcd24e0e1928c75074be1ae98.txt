Venomous sea snakes surround photographer in amazing serpent 'siesta" photo
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 14:37:58

An incredible image showing a photographer's point of view as he was surrounded by venomous sea snakes is among the pictures chosen for the 58th Wildlife Photographer of the Year exhibition.The exhibition, which opened Friday at the Natural History Museum, London (NHM), will tour across the United Kingdom before heading to venues across the world, including Texas Tech University and the Detroit Zoological Society in the United States.The exhibition includes 100 photographs that were selected from more than 38,000 entries across 93 countries.
