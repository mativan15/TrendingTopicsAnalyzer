Key Takeaways Existing home sales dropped 0.4% from July, with an overall drop of 19.9% from one year ago. This is attributed to higher mortgage rates that made monthly payments more expensive than ever. Google Trends shows that search queries for 'real estate market crash' have skyrocketed in the last month. Mortgage rates have hit a 20-year high as the Fed tries to fight inflation and cool down the economy. 
 Many hopeful homeowners have been waiting for a housing market crash so that they can finally enter the market.
