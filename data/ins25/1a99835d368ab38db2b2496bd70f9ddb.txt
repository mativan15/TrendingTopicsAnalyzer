As the old saying goes, 'You have to spend money to make money.' However, it's critical for a startup business to stay lean if it wants to ensure healthy growth. There are multiple potential financial pitfalls that could set a business back during a growth period, from hiring employees too soon to making risky investments. While the ultimate goal is to get into an optimal rhythm in terms of cash flow, saving wherever you can is important at this time. Below, 15 members of Forbes Coaches Council offer their top tips on how to save money when a business is in a growth stage.
