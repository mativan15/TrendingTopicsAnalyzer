Tech in Motion announces top regional companies across North America to work at in tech

SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- Tech in Motion is proud to announce Avenue Code as the San Francisco Bay Area's Best Tech Work Culture in the 8th Annual Timmy Awards, celebrating the best in tech workplaces across North America. Voted on by their peers in the tech industry, Best Tech Work Culture Regional Winners like Avenue Code have shown how to create a rewarding team environment by bringing tech employees together over a shared mission and fostering a community that values innovation, learning, and technical creativity.
