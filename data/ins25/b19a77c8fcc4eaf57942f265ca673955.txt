According to Allure, rapper Megan Thee Stallion may be getting ready to launch a line of her very own sex toys. On May 28, 2022, an application to trademark the name 'GOOD SEXXX' was filed by Thee Stallion's Hot Girl Trademark Holdings, LLC with the United States Patent and Trademark Office. The good and services associated with the trademark include 'Sex toys'; 'Sex toys in the nature of edible underwear'; 'Adult sexual stimulation aids, namely, artificial penises, artificial vaginas, vibrators, and anal beads'; 'Electric massage appliances, namely, electric vibrating massager'; and 'Love dolls.
