Howard University's Leadership Program Aspires To Diversify C-suites In The Hospitality Industry

2022-10-17 22:20:47

An experiential learning program designed to build pathways, prepare students for future executive-level positions and improve diversity in the hospitality industry.

Howard University, a prominent Historically Black College and University (HBCU), prides itself as one of the world's leading institutions for professionals in business, health, science, engineering, the arts, law and education. For more than 150 years, the university has a history of advocating for social justice and architecting social change.
