Delhi logs 635 dengue cases in first 12 days of Oct, takes the tally to 1,572
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:00:00

A civic authority report indicated on Monday that the number of dengue cases in Delhi has increased, with 635 new infections registered in the first 12 days of October bringing the total for this year to 1,572. There have been 1,572 cases documented so far this year, and 693 of those cases were reported in September alone. In the report issued by the Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD), the city on Monday had logged 937 cases of dengue this year through the end of September, and 635 additional cases were reported in the first 12 days of October, bringing the total number of the vector-borne disease in the city to 1,572.
