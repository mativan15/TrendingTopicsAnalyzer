Canadian GDP growth hits 3.1% in first quarter, slower than expected
einnews.com
world
2022-05-31 08:17:01

OTTAWA - Statistics Canada says the economy grew at an annualized rate of 3.1 per cent in the first quarter, helped by business investment and household spending.The result was down from an annualized rate of 6.6 per cent in the fourth quarter of 2021.The growth for the first quarter came as the economy grew 0.7 per cent in March.Statistics Canada says its preliminary reading for April indicates the economy grew 0.2 per cent for the month, but cautioned the figure would be revised when it releases its official figure on June 30.
