NEW YORK, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Scott+Scott Attorneys at Law LLP ('Scott+Scott'), an international securities and consumer rights litigation firm, is investigating whether certain directors and officers of Dentsply Sirona Inc. ('Dentsply') (NASDAQ: XRAY) breached their fiduciary duties to Dentsply and its shareholders. If you are a Dentsply shareholder, you may contact attorney Joe Pettigrew for additional information toll-free at 844-818-6982 or jpettigrew@scott-scott.com. Scott+Scott is investigating whether members of the Dentsply Board of Directors (the 'Board') made, or caused Dentsply to make, false and/or misleading statements, as well as failed to disclose material adverse facts, about Dentsply's business, operations, prospects, and financial health.
