Karim Benzema wins Ballon d'Or after firing Real Madrid to Champions League win

KARIM BENZEMA has sealed his first ever Ballon d'Or award after a stunning year when he dragged Real Madrid to Champions League glory. 
The Real Madrid star was instrumental to their European triumph and was handed the annual prize at a swanky ceremony, ahead of Player 1 and Player 2 who finished 2nd and 3rd.
A victorious Karim Benzema arrived looking dapper ahead of his awardCredit: EPA
The 34-year-old has won the Champions League five times as a Real Madrid playerCredit: Getty
Karim Benzema has joined this list of football legends after his Ballon D'Or win
The award is based on a player's individual achievements over a calendar year and comes after his series of outstanding performances in the knockout stages of the Champions League.
