Top badminton stars set to launch Grand Prix Badminton League
thehindu.com
news
2022-06-03 13:12:54

A plethora of Indian badminton stars including P V Sindhu, Kidambi Srikanth, Sai Praneeth, Jwala Gutta, Ashwini Ponnappa and Chirag Shetty will take part in the launch of the Grand Prix Badminton League, Karnataka's own version of Premier Badminton League, on Saturday.The mega event has the blessings of the Karnataka Badminton Association, a statement said on Friday.The league will see eight teams battle for supremacy and it is scheduled to be held from July 1 to 10.The eight teams are -- Bengaluru Lions, Mangalore Sharks, Mandya Bulls, Mysore Panthers, Malnad Falcons, Bandipur Tuskers, KGF Wolves and Kodagu Tigers -- with each team comprising 10 players including a minimum of five Karnataka players, two foreign players and three female shuttlers.
