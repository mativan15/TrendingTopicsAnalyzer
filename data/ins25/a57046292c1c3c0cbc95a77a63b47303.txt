Warning for Android users over malware which could add huge costs to phone bill
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 22:03:52

MILLIONS of Android users are being warned about new malware that could add huge costs to victims' phone bills.
Security experts discovered the trojan malware on multiple applications available in Android app stores and they say it has already tried to infect thousands of users.
The SMSFactory malware was detected in applications available for download in Android app stores and can trigger huge phone billsCredit: Getty
Experts say the malware has been detected in countries all over the world and cybersecurity software has protected at least 165,000 people from the threatCredit: Getty
The newly detected malware, known as SMSFactory, was revealed on Wednesday, after it was uncovered by the cybersecurity company Avast Software.
