The 49ers are trading for Panthers running back Christian McCaffrey. ESPN's Adam Schefter was first with the report.
NFL Media's Ian Rapoport reported the Panthers were in talks with the Rams and 49ers about a trade involving multiple Day 2 picks.

The final deal sent McCaffrey to the 49ers in exchange for San Francisco's 2023 second-round pick, a 2023 third-round pick and a 2023 fourth-round selection. The 49ers will also unload a fifth-round choice in 2024 per Rapoport.
McCaffrey this season has 118 touches for 670 yards and three touchdowns.
