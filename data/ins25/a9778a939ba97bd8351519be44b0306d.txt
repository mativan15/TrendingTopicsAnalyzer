Wheat rises over 4% on Black Sea supply woes; corn, soybeans firm
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-06 04:15:00

India has no plans to curb food exports for now, a minister said on Friday, weeks after New Delhi banned private wheat exports.SINGAPORE: Chicago wheat futures climbed more than 4% on Monday, rising for a second session in three as worries over supplies from the Black Sea region and strong demand underpinned prices. Corn gained over 1% while soybeans advanced more than half a percent. "Russia is trying to put more pressure on the world to lift sanctions by sabotaging world food supply," said Ole Houe, director of advisory services at agriculture brokerage IKON Commodities in Sydney.
