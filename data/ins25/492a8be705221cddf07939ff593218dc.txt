How to make £440 from the banks in three months
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-03 23:01:00

The energy price cap is set to go up another £829 in October — but you can get more than half that back simply by moving your money around.You can get bonuses of between £100 and £170 if you switch your main account to HSBC, First Direct, Lloyds and Nationwide Building Society.If you switch three times you could make £440 before the energy price cap rise kicks in, taking the average household bill for a dual-fuel customer on their supplier's variable tariff to £2,800 a year.Add the switching saving to the £400 handed out by the chancellor, Rishi Sunak, last week and you could cushion yourself from soaring energy bills.
