Now that your part-time side hustle is growing by leaps and bounds, it may seem reasonable to walk away from a full-time paid opportunity in order to focus on what truly fuels your passion and gets you up in the morning. But before you go all-in on the uncertain world of entrepreneurship, you must plan wisely to avoid setting the business (and yourself) up for failure and disappointment. Launching and maintaining a company is no easy feat, especially if you're not willing to take every single aspect of what it takes to get everything done seriously.
