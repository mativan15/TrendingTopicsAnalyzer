Alabama jailbreak suspect Casey White gets new defense team ahead of murder trial in death of Connie Ridgeway
foxnews.com
news
2022-06-01 22:38:09

Casey White, the former Alabama jail escapee whom U.S. Marshals recaptured in Indiana after 11 days on the run last month, will have a new-look defense after his family retained a team of private attorneys as he awaits the start of his murder trial in the death of Connie Ridgeway.They will also be representing him in connection with the jailbreak."There's a preliminary hearing coming up on June the 20th, and that's dealing with the newest charges," Robert Tuten, one of White's new attorneys, told Fox News Digital Wednesday.
