YGG's Beryl Li Joins Fashion League as a Co-Founder; Donald Schneider and Angelic Vendette Join Advisory Board
prweb.com
news
2022-10-19 14:30:00

ZURICH, Switzerland (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Fashion League, the first Web3 create-to-own and free-to-play mobile game to run a fashion empire, is today announcing that Beryl Li, Co-founder, Yield Guild Games (YGG), will be joining Fashion League as a Co-Founder. In addition to Ms. Li, Donald Schneider, Creative Director at Donald Schneider Studio, and Angelic Vendette, Vice President and Global Head of Marketing & Web3 at Alo Yoga, are announced as the newest members of Fashion League's board of advisors.
