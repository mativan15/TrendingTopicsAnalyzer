Alex Wong/Getty Images News 'Don't Get Married For The Sex' I recently took a long position in Stellantis N.V. (NYSE:STLA), although it is admittedly a very small amount of capital. Probably like a lot of investors jumping on the stock over the past few weeks, I was attracted to the firm's superficially low valuation; and I simply couldn't resist the 7+% dividend yield despite the voice in my head reminding me of the (generalized) adage that buying a security for its yield is like getting married only for the sex.
