Laurieanne DeLitta, disbarred Westchester lawyer, admits bilking clients out of millions
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-02 12:15:45

A disbarred Westchester lawyer will spend time in state prison after admitting she bilked eight clients out of millions of dollars when she kept the proceeds of their home sales.

Laurieanne DeLitta was promised a sentence of one to three years last week when she pleaded guilty to second-degree grand larceny in Westchester County Court.

DeLitta was a friend of some of the victims and represented others after handling legal matters for their relatives.
