MIAMI, May 26, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) --  Pordes Residential today announces that The Penthouse at Regalia is now featured on RealOpen, the platform founded by ‘Selling Sunset' star Christine Quinn and her tech entrepreneur husband, Christian Dumontet. Following multi-million-dollar listings in New York City and Los Angeles, this $33.9 million ultra-luxury penthouse is the first South Florida property to be featured on the platform and offered directly to the crypto community. RealOpen offers a real estate express lane for crypto asset holders, merging the traditional real estate process with the digital future of global finance.
