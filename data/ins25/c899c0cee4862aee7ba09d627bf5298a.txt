The field of 64 has been set for the 2022 National Collegiate Athletic Association's (NCAA) Division I baseball championship. The 16 four-team regional sites represent eight different conferences with the Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC) and Southeastern Conference (SEC) both having four schools serving as hosts.
