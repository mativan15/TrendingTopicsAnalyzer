Oslo\ Cape Town, 2 June 2022: Scatec ASA, a leading renewable energy solutions provider, today signed the power purchase agreements for the three Kenhardt projects in the Northern Cape Province of South Africa, under the Risk Mitigation Independent Power Producer Procurement Programme (RMIPPPP) alongside the South African government.
