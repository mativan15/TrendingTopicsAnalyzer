By Garima Prasher 

Displaced of May 3 flash flood still await relief as monsoon looms over Bengaluru skyline

The fate of hundreds of flood-hit slum dwellers in the city still hangs in balance. One month later, the majority of the residents from various slum colonies are still waiting for financial relief from the BBMP's revenue department.

The residents said while the water receded long ago, they are still living with the awful stench of sewage, cracked walls, and little money to feed their children and elderly.
