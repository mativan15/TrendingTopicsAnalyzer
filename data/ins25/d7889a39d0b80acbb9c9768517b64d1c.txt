Fifth Third's third-quarter earnings fall short of analysts' predictions
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 12:23:00

Fifth Third Bancorp posted adjusted earnings of 93 cents per share for the third quarter that slid by a penny from a year ago and fell short of analysts' estimates.

The results missed analysts' consensus predictions downtown Cincinnati-based Fifth Third (Nasdaq: FITB) would earn 98 cents per share, according to Thomson Reuters and Zacks Investment Research compilations.

Revenue of $2.17 billion rose 7% from a year ago but also fell shy of expectations, missing Thomson Reuters estimates by $40 million and Zacks' consensus predictions by $20 million.
