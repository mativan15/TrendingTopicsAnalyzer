Orange Health raises $25 million
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 05:30:00

Orange Health, a startup offering at-home diagnostic services, has raised $25 million in a Series B funding round led by General Catalyst and Bertelsmann India Investments. The money will be used to improve Orange Health's tech stack and for its expansion and product development, a statement from the company's founders, Dhruv Gupta and Tarun Bhambra, said.

The round also saw participation from existing investors Y Combinator, and Uncorrelated Ventures. Orange Health, founded in 2020, offers over 2,000 health test services across Bengaluru and the national capital region (NCR).
