Nanalysis Scientific Sees Stronger Growth in 3Q with Nearly Doubled Revenue
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 12:41:00

By Adriano Marchese Nanalysis Scientific Corp. said Thursday that it expects to report doubled revenue for the third quarter after having overcome challenges to its growth in the previous period. The Canadian developer of portable NMR machines and MRI technology said that it expects to report revenue of 6.8 million Canadian dollars, the equivalent of US$4.9 million, which is an increase of more than double, year-over-year. Supporting its growth, the company said it also ha been awarded a C$1.1 million contract to deliver a custom MRI system for a customer based in France.
