Published: 01:48 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 01:48 EDT, 29 May 2022 After winning just three times in their first 25 games, the Cincinnati Reds are suddenly playing with confidence as they host the San Francisco Giants in the finale of the three-game series Sunday. Aristides Aquino recorded his fifth outfield assist to end Saturday's 3-2 win over the Giants, Cincinnati's fourth straight. After a 5-4 road trip through Pittsburgh, Cleveland and Toronto, the Reds secured a winning record on their seven-game homestand.
