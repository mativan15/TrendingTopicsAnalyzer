Alistair Berg What Is Snail? Culver City, California-based Snail, Inc. (SNAL) was founded as a subsidiary of Suzhou Snail Digital Technology Co. to publish the parent firm's games in the United States. SNAL expanded to include independently-sourced content aimed at premium gamers, then began developing its own games through partner relationships and became an ostensibly independent entity in 2022 that is still controlled by the founder of the parent firm.
