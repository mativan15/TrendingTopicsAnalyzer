Supply Chain Innovator Verusen Achieves Milestones with over $2B in Managed Inventory

ATLANTA, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Verusen, a materials intelligence leader, announced it has reached significant milestones in serving the global supply chain and procurement industry, having helped customers manage more than $2 billion of inventory and nearly 3 million separate SKUs. Verusen will be at ProcureCon in Atlanta from October 16 to 18, in booth #103, where it will showcase its purpose-built materials intelligence platform. Verusen leverages artificial intelligence (AI), deep learning, data harmonization, and decision support to help global brands control risk, attain supply chain resiliency, and improve economies for their operations.
