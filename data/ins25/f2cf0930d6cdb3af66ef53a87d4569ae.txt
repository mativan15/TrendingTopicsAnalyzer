Pavel_Chag/iStock via Getty Images In past articles on Tesla, Inc. (NASDAQ:TSLA), I have emphasized my like for the cars and my admiration for what Elon Musk has achieved, proving that a visionary can also put visions into practice. Others have turned their admiration into near worship, and that has inflated the stock market valuation to unrealistic highs. At the time of my first article Tesla's Ticking Time Bomb on November 16, 2021, the share price was $1,054.73. The last closing price as I write was $740.
