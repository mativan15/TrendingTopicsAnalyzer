[Album Review] For (G)I-dle, self-love is not silly. It's how to feel like the otherworldly person you are

(G)I-dle (Cube Entertainment)People love to talk about the things they love. For some, it could be their friends and family, while for others its a pet or hobby.Girl group (G)I-dle approached the word 'love' in a way that the five members have never experienced before. On its latest project, the group ponders a different way to approach love: How you love yourself is how you teach others to love you. And to do that, the band offers a look into pompous love.With So-yeon at the helm of producing the group's 'I love' album, she once again showed her knack for the sonic.
