This week, Australian furniture brand Coco Republic officially debuted what may just be the largest new retail commitment in Union Square since the start of the pandemic.

Taking 53,000 square feet at 55 Stockton St. with customer access across three levels, the store is aimed as a kind of experience-oriented first foothold in the U.S. on the heels of major investment from Story3 Capital Partners.
