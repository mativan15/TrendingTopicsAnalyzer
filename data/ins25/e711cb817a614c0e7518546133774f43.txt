SAN FRANCISCO, May 30, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--S-Alpha Therapeutics, Inc. ("S-Alpha"), a company that advocates digital solutions and develops digital treatment devices for digital health, will be presenting at Biotechnology Innovation Organization (BIO) International Convention 2022. BIO 2022 is the world's largest and most influential event for the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industry. This year's meeting will be held in San Diego, California from June 13 to 16, 2022, as a fully in-person meeting for the first time in three years.
