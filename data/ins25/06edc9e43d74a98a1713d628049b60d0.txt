Discoms can now settle dues through EMIs within four years

energy
2022-06-06 03:00:00

AgenciesThe rules are applicable to discoms' outstanding dues to gencos, inter-state transmission licensees, and electricity trading licensees.Mumbai: The government on Friday allowed electricity distribution companies to settle their dues to power generating companies and transmission companies in equated monthly instalments (EMIs). As per the Electricity (Late Payment Surcharge and Related Matters) Rules, 2022 notified on Friday, total dues including late payment surcharge up to the date of the notification of these rules shall be rescheduled and the due dates redetermined for payment by a distribution licensee in equated monthly instalments.
