Shanthakumar V Patil, who owns a hardware shop and real estate business in Raichur, has come forward to make a Kannada movie - Kaveripura. Nirnay DK, who has been dialogue writer and assistant, debuts as an independent director with this film. 'The movie is about incidents at a North Karnataka village in a year. The movie will showcase customs and beautiful locations of the region,'' said Nirnay. The director also revealed one unique point of the film. 'The hero exposes the so-called leaders of the village without any fisticuffs,'' he said.
