How to Sell Your Business and Actually Make a Profit

According to M&A Source, 80 % of businesses on the market will never sell. This should be a huge wakeup call for all business owners, as this statistic means you have less than a 20% chance of success when you go to sell your company.One of the chief reasons businesses don't sell is that owners don't think about selling until a catastrophic event occurs. This could be an internal factor--partner disputes, divorce, health issue or even death--or an external one, like the Covid pandemic, or fires, hurricane, tornadoes, government shutdowns, etc.
