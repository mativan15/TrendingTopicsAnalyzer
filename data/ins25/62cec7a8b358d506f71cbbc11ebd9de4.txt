Liquid cats explained: Why your cat can squeeze into impossible spaces
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-31 10:27:08

If you've ever noticed that your cat is so flexible that it almost seems to have transformed into another state of matter entirely, fear not, you are not alone.Liquid cats are a well-known internet phenomenon with countless social media posts highlighting it. Whether its cats folded over a corrugated metal roof or squeezing into a tiny glass bowl, posts have gotten tens of thousands of likes on Twitter.Thankfully the phenomenon can be explained by science, as Vancouver veterinarian Uri Burstyn—whom many will recognize as the vet in the viral "squish that cat" YouTube video—explains.
