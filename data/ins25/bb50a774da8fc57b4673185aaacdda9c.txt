Mollitiam Holdings Invests in Extreme Steel Crane & Rigging to Increase Access to Capital for Growth

2022-10-15 19:30:00

MANASSES, Va. (PRWEB) October 15, 2022 Extreme Crane & Rigging, Inc. (Extreme Crane), one of the fastest growing providers of crane and rigging services in the Mid-Atlantic United States, is pleased to announce that Mollitiam Holdings (Mollitiam) has made a strategic investment in the company acquiring a majority ownership stake. "The decision to recapitalize and partner with a new investor group follows a thorough review of options available to us to enhance our financial strength and ensure Extreme Crane's ability to continue to pursue our goal of becoming a leading provider of crane and rigging services in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States,' stated Kevin Rodney, CEO of Extreme Crane & Rigging.
