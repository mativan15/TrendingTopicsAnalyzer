3 Tips for Starting a Small Business During a Recession

2022-10-17 15:16:44

Starting a business is like jumping off a cliff without knowing if there is a safety net to catch you. It's scary! It can feel overwhelming to strike out on your own, and many of us get stuck waiting for the 'right moment' where favorable market conditions make it worth the risk.

Today, there are lots of external risks to consider, including sky-high inflation, staffing and logistical challenges and a possible economic recession on the horizon. I wouldn't blame you if you felt like now is a terrible time to start a business -; but the current economic climate shouldn't deter you from pursuing yourdream of owning your own business.
