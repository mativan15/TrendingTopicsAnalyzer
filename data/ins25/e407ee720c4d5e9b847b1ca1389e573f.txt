Published: 11:51 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 11:51 EDT, 21 October 2022 American Airlines will soon be giving its customers ‘a suite new ride'.The world's largest carrier by fleet size has unveiled images of brand-new business class seats and reimagined interiors set for introduction on Boeing Dreamliners, Boeing 777s and long-range single-aisle Airbus A321 planes.The new ‘Flagship Suite' business-class seats will offer customers a private ‘premium' experience with a privacy door, a chaise lounge seating option and ‘more personal storage space'.
