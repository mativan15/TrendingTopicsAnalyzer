(PRWEB) June 03, 2022 Engel & Völkers Florida today announced Patti Burch has left Amelia Island Plantation Real Estate to join Engel & Völkers Amelia Island as a real estate advisor. Burch brings with her more than 10 years of experience in real estate, specializing in oceanfront condominiums and luxury homes throughout Nassau County.
