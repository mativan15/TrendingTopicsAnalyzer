THE world's biggest cruise ship - Icon of the Seas - has been unveiled by Royal Caribbean, capable of carrying 7,600 passengers and costing more than £1.2BILLION.
Dubbed the ultimate family vacation, the mega ship will feature the largest waterpark at sea, an 155ft waterfall inside a vast geodesic dome, the world's first swim-up bar at sea and an infinity pool suspended over the ocean.
