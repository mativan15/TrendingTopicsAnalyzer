Insurers win key aspects of Stonegate COVID-19 lockdown case- MS Amlin
reuters.com
news
2022-10-17 11:40:18

LONDON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Insurers have won key parts of a complex legal battle with British pubs group Stonegate, MS Amlin said after Monday's judgment on the 1 billion pound ($1.13 billion) lawsuit against three top insurers over coronavirus-related lockdown losses.Stonegate sued MS Amlin (MITSID.UL), Zurich and Liberty Mutual (LBRTML.UL), alleging that government-ordered closures and restrictions relating to the pandemic triggered business interruption cover multiple times and that the interruption and interference would continue until April 2023.
