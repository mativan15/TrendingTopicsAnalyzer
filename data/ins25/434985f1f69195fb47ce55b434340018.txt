Plug Power, Olin JV to build 15 ton-per-day hydrogen plant in Louisiana
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 12:37:31

Hydrogen and fuel cell systems company Plug Power Inc. PLUG, +2.63% and chlor alkali producer Olin Corp. OLN, +2.91% announced Wednesday a joint venture, Hidrogenii, that will kick off with the building of a 15 ton-per-day hydrogen plant in St. Gabriel, Louisiana. The company's said construction will create 160 jobs in 2022 and 215 jobs in 2023, and when completed, the plant is then expected to create more than 25 full-time jobs. The companies said the plant will benefit from state and local tax subsidies.
