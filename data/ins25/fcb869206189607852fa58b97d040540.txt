Luke and Jamie-Lee Anderson opened their shop in July 2021 (Image: Cravin' / Beth Wood Photography)The owners of a bakery have shared their shock after receiving their latest eye-watering electricity bill, saying they thought it must be a mistake. Husband and wife Luke and Jamie-Lee Anderson made their dreams a reality in July 2021 by opening up their new bakery Cravin', in Mold, North Wales. However, alongside many businesses, they're feeling the pinch when it comes to rising energy bills.Dad-of-two Luke, who won Big Brother in 2012, shared his shock on social media after receiving their latest energy bill, reports North Wales Live.
