66% of American Workers Are Worse Off Financially Than a Year Ago Due to Inflation, Report Finds
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-19 06:49:38

As the cost of living keeps rising, more Americans are struggling financially. Now, two-thirds of adults say they are worse off than they were just one year ago, according to a recent report. Nearly 1 in 3 workers, including those earning more than $100,000, run out of money before payday. Rising costs have chipped away at most Americans' standard of living. As inflation pressures continue, two-thirds of working adults said they are worse off financially than they were a year ago, according to a recent report by Salary Finance.
