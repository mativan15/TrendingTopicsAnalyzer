Published: 05:53 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 05:57 EDT, 18 October 2022 Social media users are mercilessly mocking Liz Truss amid speculation her days as Prime Minister are numbered.Twitter was awash with memes Tuesday morning after Ms Truss's economic policies were brutally torched by her new Chancellor Jeremy Hunt overnight.Britons mocked the Prime Minister for 'hiding' from the public while the UK economy continues to tank. Ms Truss apologised to the nation for her arguably catastrophic mini-budget, but social media users are still calling for her removal.
