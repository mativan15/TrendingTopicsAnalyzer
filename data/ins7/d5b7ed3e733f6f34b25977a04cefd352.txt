Russian Nationals Arrested Over Alleged Scheme to Evade U.S. Sanctions
wsj.com
news
2022-10-19 23:31:00

Photo: angela weiss/Agence France-Presse/Getty Images Two Russian nationals were arrested for allegedly using their Germany-based company as a front to move black market oil and sensitive equipment with military uses in defiance of U.S. sanctions. Yury Orekhov and Artem Uss, both associated with the Hamburg-based industrial equipment and commodity trading business Nord-Deutsche Industrieanlagenbau GmbH, have been apprehended, federal prosecutors said Wednesday.
