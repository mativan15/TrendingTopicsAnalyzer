S&P 500 bounces off make-or-break technical level
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-17 20:11:00

Stocks saw big gains Monday, with the S&P 500 finding support at a key technical level and another giant bank coming out with solid results. A reversal of the U.K.'s vast fiscal stimulus also bolstered trader sentiment. The breadth of the rally was so strong that at one point over 99 per cent of the companies in the U.S. equity benchmark were up, with the gauge pushing away from its 200-week moving average. The tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 outperformed, with its biggest gain since July.
