stockcam/iStock Unreleased via Getty Images Pinterest (NYSE:PINS) was on the rise Monday, climbing more than 6% as the social information and sharing site continued to rally ahead of its upcoming third-quarter results. Since falling to a two-year low of $16.14 a share in May, Pinterest (PINS) has risen 40%, and the company's shares advanced in a broad market rally. Among other social and Internet companies, Facebook parent Meta Platforms (META) rose 6%, Snap (SNAP) climbed nearly 8% and Google parent Alphabet (GOOG) was up by 4.
