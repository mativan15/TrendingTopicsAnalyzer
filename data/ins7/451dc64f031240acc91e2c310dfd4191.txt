₹5 lakh worth illegal crackers seized in Pathankot; three held

A tip-off was received by police that some wholesale cracker traders had illegally stored a huge quantity of crackers at village Gusaipur, Pathankot. (HT file photo)
 

 Launching a crackdown against sale of illegal fire crackers ahead of Diwali, Pathankot police on Tuesday seized a huge cache of crackers valuing ₹5 lakh in Gosaipur village. Three accused who were involved in the illegal business have also been arrested. They are identified as Vinod Kumar of Mohalla Sahibzada, Ashish Kumar alias Ashu of Mohalla Rajgarhia and Bunty of Sarai in Pathankot.
