Santa Clara, CA and Kyoto, Japan, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- ROHM Group company LAPIS Technology Co., Ltd., and global antenna manufacturer Shanghai Amphenol Airwave Communication Electronics Co., Ltd. (a subsidiary of Amphenol Corporation), today announced they have established a cooperative framework to provide wireless charging solutions for hearable and wearable devices that are trending towards greater compactness and sophistication.Advanced antenna customization technology allows Shanghai Amphenol Airwave to provide compact antennas that meet the needs for smaller, thinner housings in the hearable and wearable device markets.
