BYD Co. Shares Rise Sharply After Strong Third-Quarter Guidance
marketwatch.com

2022-10-18 02:12:00

By Yi Wei Wong Shares of BYD Co. jump in early Asian trade after the electric-vehicle company issued better-than-expected guidance for third-quarter earnings. The Shenzhen-based company's Hong Kong-listed shares rose as much as 7.3% to 207.00 Hong Kong dollars (US$26.37) in morning trade. They were last 6.4% higher at HK$205.40. In a stock-exchange release late on Monday, BYD said it expects third-quarter net profit to more than quadruple to between 5.50 billion yuan and 5.90 billion yuan (US$764.
