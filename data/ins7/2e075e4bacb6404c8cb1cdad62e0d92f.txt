Kusama Has A Few Choice Words For People Spreading 'FUD' Within The Community
ibtimes.com.au
news
2022-06-01 10:18:36

Shytoshi Kusama, the lead developer of Shiba Inu, has a few choice words for people in the community who would rather dwell on the negative reports instead of spreading positivity.
Following news that Shiba Inu creator Ryoshi has deleted all his blogs and tweets, many SHIB holders were alarmed, thinking the mysterious figure behind the popular token would no longer be around for the community. Despite Kusama's post on Medium, some people on Twitter seemed committed to their narrative that something was going on within the Shiba Inu dev team.
