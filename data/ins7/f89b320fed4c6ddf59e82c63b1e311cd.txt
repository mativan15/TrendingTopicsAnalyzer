California's first commercial robotaxi is approved for the streets of San Francisco

Robotaxis are now a real thing in California.On Thursday, state officials green-flagged the launch of a fare-based ride-hailing business featuring cars with no human driver at the wheel. Robot-operated Chevy Bolt EVs will be rolled out over the next few weeks by autonomous vehicle maker Cruise.
