NEW YORK, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- FINDINGS, the supply chain automation company, announced today it is an official member of the London Stock Exchange's Issuer Services Marketplace. Through Issuer Services, London Stock Exchange traded companies can now access services provided by Findings, including a specifically designed ESG solution for self, and supply-chain compliance aimed to align with the rapidly growing adoption of ESG requirements affecting businesses worldwide. The FINDINGS ESG solution is jointly created with ESG experts and leaders and aims to help traded, and all other companies to easily adopt ESG standards within their business ecosystem as well as report their sustainability performance to their stakeholders.
