 Nova Scotia RCMP have sent out a local Vulnerable Missing Person Alert to the southwestern shore of the province.
Police said 72-year-old Sharon Titus was last seen at 4:40 a.m. in Mavillette, in the Yarmouth region.Titus is described as being five feet and three inches tall, weighing about 150 pounds with short grey hair.
