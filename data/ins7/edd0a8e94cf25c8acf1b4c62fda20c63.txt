(courtesy: sunnyleone)New Delhi: Sunny Leone, to wish her husband Daniel Weber on his 44th birthday, posted a postcard-worthy picture on her Instagram profile on Thursday. The picture happens to be from an undisclosed beach destination. Both Sunny and Daniel Weber can be seen happily posing for the camera. "Happy Birthday to my darling husband! May we always be so in love and enjoying life together in every way always! Your an amazing father and husband. Always selflessly caring about us over yourself! I love you so much and hope your birthday is amazing," wrote Sunny Leone.
