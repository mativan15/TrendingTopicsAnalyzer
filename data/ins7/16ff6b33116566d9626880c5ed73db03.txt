Nestle announces 2nd interim dividend of Rs 120 per share
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 06:30:00

FMCG major Nestle on Wednesday declared an interim dividend of Rs 120 per share for the year 2022. "The Board of Directors have declared second interim dividend for 2022 of Rs 120 per equity share (Face value Rs 10 per equity share) amounting to Rs 1,157, which will be paid on and from 16 November 2022," said in an exchanging filing to announce its September quarter results. This is in addition to the first interim dividend of Rs 25 per equity share paid on 6 May 2022. The record date for determining the eligible shareholders is November 1 and the ex-date is October 31.
