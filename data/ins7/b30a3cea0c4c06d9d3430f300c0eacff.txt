By Tauren Dyson From connected car solutions that can monitor a driver's mental and physical state to VR headsets that allow sports fans to experience live games without leaving their living rooms, technology has created new and immersive ways for businesses and brands to engage with consumers. As those innovations continue to evolve by the day, so are consumers' expectations, as they look for deeper one-on-one relationships with brands. Business leaders must anticipate consumers' evolving needs and meet them with immersive technologies that yield personalized experiences.
