SADA Partners with Google Cloud to Deliver Contact Center AI Platform

LOS ANGELES, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- SADA, a leading business and technology consultancy and award-winning Google Cloud Partner, today announced it is officially a delivery partner for Google Cloud's Contact Center AI Platform. As a delivery partner, SADA will offer and deploy Google Cloud's enterprise-grade, AI-powered cloud contact center solution globally. Google Cloud's Contact Center AI (CCAI) solution suite provides a unified and intelligent technology experience for contact centers.
