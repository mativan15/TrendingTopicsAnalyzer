The huge wildcard in the 2022 elections is abortion. What I mean is that a recent poll shows something like half of the country being highly motivated to vote this year based on the issue. Unsurprisingly, a lot more Democrats than Republicans are motivated that way. The real question is how many people will vote who otherwise wouldn't vote. That's always the question because if we're talking about a voter who would vote anyway, and vote for a specific party no matter what, it's irrelevant if he or she is particularly interested in an issue.
