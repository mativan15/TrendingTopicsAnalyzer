Gurugram: Two people were arrested on Saturday for allegedly killing a man over an argument involving stock market losses, police informed.Police received a call on May 26 about a man who succumbed to injuries sustained from a fall in a private hospital.The deceased, Shubhankar Bhadra, lived with his wife and children in Nathupur, DLF Phase 3.
