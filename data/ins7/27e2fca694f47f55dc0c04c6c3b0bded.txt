Chaos in Chad as security forces violently put down protests

world
2022-10-20 10:10:15

Chad has been plunged into chaos after police in the capital N'Djamena violently dispersed protests calling for a quicker transition to democratic rule, as demonstrators were accused of looting and setting fire to the newly-appointed prime minister's party offices.'Our headquarters were ransacked and then set on fire this morning,' National Union for Democracy and Renewal (UNDR) vice-president, Celestin Topona, said via telephone. 'The guard was almost lynched by violent protesters,' he said.Saleh Kebzabo, the UNDR president, was named prime minister last week after his predecessor resigned to pave the way for a new administration.
