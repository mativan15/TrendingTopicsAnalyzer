Govt forms exclusive benches at three DRTs to resolve cases above Rs 100 crore
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 02:15:00

AgenciesThe latest available data from the Reserve Bank of India show that DRTs had ₹2.25 lakh crore of debt pending under litigation as of March 2021, higher than the ₹1.35 lakh crore pending in NCLTs across India in that period.Mumbai: The government has created exclusive benches to deal with cases above ₹100 crore at debts recovery tribunals (DRTs) in Chennai, Mumbai and Delhi, agreeing to a long-standing demand by banks to get more focus on high-value cases. The fresh push by the government comes even as bankruptcy cases in the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) are languishing due to delays.
