What benefits may conglomerates derive from working with hospitals to expand their business models?

2022-10-16 07:48:16

Since the pandemic, disruptions have become an unavoidable aspect of our lives and working environment. Businesses across the globe have experienced considerable challenges due to the induced restriction, and the healthcare sector is no exception to it. Healthcare is one of the industries that was intensely impacted by Covid-19. Not only that, many individuals across the industries witnessed significant changes. Hence, to cope up with the scenario, industries have embraced new trends and strategies by partnering with other sectors to boost their business models.
