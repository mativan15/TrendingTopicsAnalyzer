The interventions include setting up of common facility centres and infrastructure development.The government has approved new guidelines of Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) for implementation during the 15th Finance Commission cycle - 2021-26, the MSME ministry said on Friday. The scheme aims at enhancing the competitiveness and productivity of micro and small enterprises by undertaking certain interventions. The interventions include setting up of common facility centres and infrastructure development.
