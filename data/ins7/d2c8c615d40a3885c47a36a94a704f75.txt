I think this post was long overdue, wasn't it? For those who don't know me, my name's Crystelle and I'm a cook and a baker with a miso obsession. Before I continue, I should probably explain what miso is, to those who may not be familiar with it. Without getting all science-y, miso is a Japanese fermented soybean paste, often mixed with salt and koji and sometimes rice or barley. It has such a wonderful depth of rich, salty and umami flavors that just lifts almost any dish you add it to, whether sweet or savory.
