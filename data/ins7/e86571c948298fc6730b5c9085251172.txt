Yang Jeong-woong, executive producer of the PyeongChang 2018 Winter Games` opening ceremony, speaks during The Korea Herald`s Global Business Forum on Wednesday. (Jenny Jung)When the grand opening of the PyeongChang 2018 Winter Games unfolded, it wowed viewers with various spectacular scenes, including 1,217 drones in the air and hundreds of South Korean snowboarders in light-emitting jumpsuits who slid down the slopes with the drones flashing above them. There was also a controversial feature: Inmyeonjo, a mythological bird with a human face that often appears in ancient tomb murals of the Three Kingdoms of Korea.
