Equity Lifestyle Properties: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

news
2022-10-17 20:27:11

CHICAGO

Equity Lifestyle Properties Inc. (ELS) on Monday reported a key measure of profitability in its third quarter.

The Chicago-based real estate investment trust said it had funds from operations of $136.8 million, or 70 cents per share, in the period.

Funds from operations is a closely watched measure in the REIT industry. It takes net income and adds back items such as depreciation and amortization.

The company said it had net income of $67.2 million, or 36 cents per share.

The resort community operator posted revenue of $381 million in the period.
