The Diwali season brings in cheer for all. Be it among family, friends or co-workers, people across the world celebrate the festival of lights with pomp and fervour. Each year, Diwali is marked by lights, fireworks, delicious foods, and most importantly gifts.The idea of gifting loved ones or friends during Diwali has become a universal sentiment. It is not longer limited to friends and family, corporate offices and various establishments and institutions mark the occasion by distributing gifts and hampers to co-workers and colleagues.
