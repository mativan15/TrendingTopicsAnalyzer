Pacific Premier Bancorp: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

business
2022-10-20 10:08:11

IRVINE, Calif.

Pacific Premier Bancorp Inc. (PPBI) on Thursday reported third-quarter earnings of $73.4 million.

On a per-share basis, the Irvine, California-based company said it had profit of 77 cents.

The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of five analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 76 cents per share.

The holding company for Pacific Premier Bank posted revenue of $219.2 million in the period. Its adjusted revenue was $201.3 million, falling short of Street forecasts.
