Yankees Favored To Beat Guardians In Game 5
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 14:35:13

The ALDS between the New York Yankees and Cleveland Guardians comes down to a deciding Game 5 Monday night in the Bronx. After staving off elimination from the 2022 MLB playoffs Sunday in Cleveland, New York is favored to win the series at home and punch its ticket to the ALCS.Game 5 starts at 7:07 p.m. ET on TBS at Yankee Stadium. The Yankees have -162 odds at FanDuel Sportsbook to beat the Guardians, which are +136 underdogs.Neither Game 5 starting pitcher has ever made a playoff start. Aaron Civale will get the ball for the Guardians.
