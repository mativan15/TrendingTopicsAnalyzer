SAN FRANCISCO , Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Blockchain Capital, LLC announced the appointment of Alan Curtis as Head of Platform and Caleb Tebbe as Principal Research Engineer. Alan will be responsible for supporting founders and portfolio companies post-investment and Caleb will be responsible for supporting portfolio company engineering teams, research, and technical due diligence.

Together, Alan and Caleb have a combined 13 years of experience building, supporting, and advising companies at the absolute frontier of crypto.
