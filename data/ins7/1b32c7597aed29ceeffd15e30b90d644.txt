GREENWICH, CT / ACCESSWIRE / October 17, 2022 / The Mark Mariani Scholarship for Entrepreneurs is a scholarship available for students who are studying to become entrepreneurs in the future and those with a dream to study a business course and build a career in entrepreneurship. The scholarship is open for students currently enrolled in university or college studying to become future entrepreneurs. Those in high school who will be attending a university or college to study a business course and later start a career in entrepreneurship can also apply for the scholarship.
