The Allahabad high court on Monday disposed of a petition filed by a homebuyer in Gautam Budh Nagar for recovery of dues from a private builder who failed to give him possession of a flat he booked in 2015
Published on May 31, 2022 12:24 AM IST
 

The Allahabad high court on Monday disposed of a petition filed by a homebuyer in Gautam Budh Nagar for recovery of dues from a private builder who failed to give him possession of a flat he booked in 2015.The bench comprising justices Pritinker Diwaker and Ashutosh Srivastava disposed of the petition filed by one Ankit Gera after the counsel for the state government submitted that the money had been recovered in compliance with orders of the Uttar Pradesh Real Estate Regulatory Authority (UP-Rera) and would be transmitted to the Rera after clearance of the cheque.
