Variety - Film News Jack Black has gone viral on social media thanks to a clip of him serenading a young 'School of Rock' fan diagnosed with a rare illness. The two met at the Layla Paige and Friends Walk for TrinityKids Care, which Black hosts each year to raise funds for child hospice programs in Southern California. A clip shared by Upworthy shows Black singing a 'School of Rock' song to Abraham, a 15-year-old with a rare mitochondrial disease called Pearson syndrome.
