POTOMAC, Md., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Castellum, Inc. (NYSE: CTM) ('Castellum', or the 'Company'), a cybersecurity, electronic warfare, and IT services company, today announced the closing of its public offering of 1,500,000 shares of common stock consisting of 1,350,000 shares sold by the Company and 150,000 shares sold by certain selling stockholders, at a public offering price of $2.00 per share. In addition, Castellum has granted the underwriters a 45-day option to purchase an additional 225,000 shares of common stock.
