Nantucket is a tiny island located a few miles away from Cape Cod, Massachusetts. It can be reached via a plane or a ferry. The island has several historic buildings, such as Jethro Coffin House and Nantucket Windmill, built during the 18th century. The town has cobblestone streets lined up with churches, boutiques, and elegant restaurants offering a variety of seafood, especially clams, oysters, and lobsters. The island has fine sandy shores with historic lighthouses. The beaches are ideal for swimming, surfing, and sunbathing.
