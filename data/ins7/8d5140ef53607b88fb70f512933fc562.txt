AgenciesWell-known South Korean actors Park Shin Hye and Choi Tae Joon welcomed their first child on Tuesday. The actress Park Shin Hye gave birth to a healthy baby boy at a hospital in Seoul. The newborn and mother are in good condition. Park's agency Salt Entertainment gave this good news to her fans in a statement. The famous star is recognised for shows like The Doctors, The Heirs and films The Royal Tailor and The Call. The talented star couple Shin Hye and Tae Joon fell for each other in late 2017 and since then have been dating.
