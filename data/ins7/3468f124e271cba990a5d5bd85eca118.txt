Aryna Sabalenka and Daria Kasatkina qualify for WTA Finals 2022
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 19:15:00

AgenciesAryna Sabalenka and Daria Kasatkina joined World No. 1 player Iga Swiatek, along with Ons Jabeur, Coco Gauff, Jessica Pegula, and Caroline Garcia in the singles draw in the Women's Tennis Association (WTA) Finals 2022. Sabalenka will be back for the second time at the season-ending tournament (in singles). She competed in the group stages in the year 2021. She, along with Elise Mertens, qualified in doubles in 2019.
 On the other hand, Kasatkina made her debut by qualifying for the WTA Finals this season.
