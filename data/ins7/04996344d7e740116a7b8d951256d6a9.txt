getty A non-fungible token (NFT) is a form of digital asset comprising computerized data piled up in a decentralized public ledger known as the blockchain. NFTs are created with the same programming technique used to create cryptocurrencies. NFTs cannot be traded or exchanged for another NFT that's the same. On the other hand, other digital currencies are fungible, which means they can be traded or exchanged.
