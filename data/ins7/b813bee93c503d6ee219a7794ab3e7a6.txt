Bernd Leno has been linked with a move away from Arsenal this summer with Benfica reportedly keen on signing him, while a number of German clubs are interestedBernd Leno Benfica are interested in signing Bernd Leno from Arsenal this summer but the deal may have hit a stumbling block with the Portuguese side reluctant to pay the Gunners' asking price. The 30-year-old has one year left on his current Arsenal contract and will be allowed to leave the club in search of regular first-team football. Leno made just four Premier League appearances last term and only one since September - keeping a clean sheet in a 1-0 win over Aston Villa.
