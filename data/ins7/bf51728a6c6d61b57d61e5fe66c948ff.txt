Liverpool can help Arsenal with two major boosts against Man City
express.co.uk
sport
2022-10-15 07:16:13

Liverpool can help Arsenal in more ways than one against Manchester City on Sunday. Jurgen Klopp's side can boost the Gunners' title chances this season and also preserve an historic record.City head to Anfield as favourites due to being 13 points clear of their rivals. They are second in the table as they look to win a fifth title in six seasons.Many, including Klopp, have already ruled Liverpool out of the title race this season. But Arsenal have been tipped by some to be the nearest challengers to the Etihad club.
