Raju Srivastav's wife Shikha posts old video of him singing Kishore Kumar song, pens note a month after comedian's death

A month after his death, Raju Srivastav's wife Shikha Srivastav has shared an unseen video of the late comedian singing a song. Taking to Instagram on Friday, Shikha also penned a note for her late husband. In the old video, Raju sat on a bed inside a room and sang Kishore Kumar's Yaadon Main Woh Sapnon Main Hai from Swami (1977).
