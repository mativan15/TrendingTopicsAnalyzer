U.K. Prime Minister Liz Truss is out. Here's what happens next.
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 14:18:00

As Liz Truss stepped down from a turbulent and record-short stay as U.K. Prime Minister on Thursday, the question is what's next from the country whose politics have taken investors on a roller-coaster ride over the last several weeks. Truss has said she would exit the top job as soon as the Conservatives hold a contest to replace her, a rush job likely by next week. The rules governing that contest have yet to be announced, but key lawmaker Graham Brady said a new prime minister will be in place ahead of the fiscal statement due on Oct.
