Little Simz has continued an impressive 2022 by scooping the 2022 Mercury Prize best British album of the year with Sometimes I Might Be Introvert.
Accepting her prize onstage, Little Simz said she was ‘very overwhelmed and grateful' to receive the accolade.
‘To my family over here, my loved ones right here, I want to say a huge thank you to the Mercury for this incredible prize,' she said as she received the award.
‘I want to say thank you to my brother and close collaborator Inflo… he's known me since I was so young, he's stuck by me, we created this album together.
