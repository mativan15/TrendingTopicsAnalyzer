Getty ImagesRetail and banking stocks rose 0.5 per cent and 0.7 per cent, respectively.Shares of Max Financial Services Ltd. traded 0.67 per cent down at Rs 801.0 on Thursday at around 12:53PM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex advanced 254.94 points to 55636.11. As many as 4,650 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 0.37 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 1147.9 and a 52-week low of Rs 697.05. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 911.
