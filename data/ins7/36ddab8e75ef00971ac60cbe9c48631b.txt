University of Kansas picks Multistudio and HNTB for football complex renovations

The University of Kansas recently announced a transformational project, including renovations of the David Booth Kansas Memorial Stadium and other football facilities, and two Kansas City-based design firms will work with the university on the development.

KU selected Multistudio and HNTB Corp. to transform the 11 th and Mississippi streets corridor with new facilities, including conference and entertainment space, retail, renovations to the Anderson Family Football Complex and major upgrades to Memorial Stadium, one of the nation's oldest football venues, according to KU Athletics.
