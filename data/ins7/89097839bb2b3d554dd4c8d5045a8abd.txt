("High Tide" or the "Company") (Nasdaq: HITI) (TSXV: HITI) (FSE: 2LYA), a leading retail-focused cannabis company with bricks-and-mortar as well as global e-commerce assets, is pleased to announce that further to its press release dated March 30, 2022, the Company has completed its acquisition (the "Acquisition") of 100% of the equity interest of Livonit Foods Inc.
