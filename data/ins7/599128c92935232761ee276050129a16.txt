FOUNTAIN HILLS, Ariz., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Arizona-based software company, Wise Agent, has been named the Best Real Estate CRM of 2022 by Forbes Advisor. Forbes Advisor chose Wise Agent as the Best Real Estate CRM because of its plethora of integration options, value for money, and 24/7 comprehensive customer support.Wise Agent was given the highest-ranking out of several other comparable Real Estate CRMs based on factors such as pricing, ease of use, and specific real estate industry features available.
