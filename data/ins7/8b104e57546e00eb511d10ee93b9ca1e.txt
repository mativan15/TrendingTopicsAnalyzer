PRINCETON, N.J., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Bank of Princeton (the "Bank") (NASDAQ: BPRN) today reported its unaudited financial condition and results of operations at and for the quarter ended September 30, 2022.  The Bank reported net income of $7.0 million, or $1.09 per diluted common share, for the third quarter of 2022, compared to net income of $6.3 million, or $0.98 per diluted common share, for the second quarter of 2022, and net income of $5.9 million, or $0.88 per diluted common share, for the third quarter of 2021.
