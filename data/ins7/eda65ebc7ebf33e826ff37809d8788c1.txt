Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 raids are beginning soon. Know the date and all the details.
 Infinity Ward and Activision Blizzard have announced that Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 will be released on October 28, 2022. Ever since September 17, the developers have made the game open for beta testing and through that we have seen multiple new features that will be making its way into the game. The most anticipated among them are Spec Ops and the mysterious Raid mode. And while three Spec Ops will be available to play at launch, it is the Raid mode whose details were previously not known.
