Textron Expands Parts Distribution Operations In Kansas
yahoo.com
finance
2022-10-17 18:30:52

Textron Inc (NYSE: TXT) company Textron Aviation plans to add 180,000 square feet of space to its parts distribution operations for better customer support.

Located at the company's headquarters in Wichita, Kansas, the expansion supports parts availability for the aircraft designed and manufactured by Textron Aviation.

The Wichita location houses more than 120,000 parts and employs a team of more than 350 people to support distribution operations.

The new facility is expected to serve customers who own and operate Cessna, Beechcraft, and Hawker products.
