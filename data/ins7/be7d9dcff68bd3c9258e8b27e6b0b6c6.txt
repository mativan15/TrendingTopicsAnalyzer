JHVEPhoto/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Introduction The best thing about market turmoil is that it often causes investors to look for bargains. While the Arconic Corporation (NYSE:ARNC) has done rather well during the most recent market sell-off, advancing 12% since my most recent article in March, it is down 15% year-to-date and roughly 27% below its 2021 all-time high. The company is suffering from sky-high input prices of i.e., aluminum, which makes it hard to grow free cash flow and margins.
