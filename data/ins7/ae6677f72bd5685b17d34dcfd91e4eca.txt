2:48 PM ETMany fantasy football fans watched over the weekend as the Panthers sent Robbie Anderson to the locker room following multiple verbal altercations with his coaches. Before the incident, he had not received a single target against the Rams and was spotted sitting alone on the sideline. Entering Sunday's game, Anderson had reeled in 13 receptions for 206 yards in five games.The Panthers were already having a tough week, with QB Baker Mayfield's ankle injury and the firing of coach Matt Rhule.
