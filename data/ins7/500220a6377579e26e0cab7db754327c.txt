ATLANTA (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 Stratus Building Solutions, the nation's leading green commercial cleaning and janitorial services franchise, is exited to announce it will be in attendance for The Franchise Show in Atlanta from October 22-23. The Franchise Show is Atlanta's largest 'own your own business' event and features franchises from across the country. Stratus Building Solutions is an industry-leading franchise in green commercial cleaning that uses state-of-the-art cleaning equipment, sustainable processes, and biodegradable and non-toxic branded cleaning chemicals.
