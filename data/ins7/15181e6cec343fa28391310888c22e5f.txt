COVID-19 tracker: Tokyo logs 1,588 new cases
japantimes.co.jp
news
2022-10-17 08:19:59

The Tokyo Metropolitan Government said Monday that 1,588 people have been newly confirmed with COVID-19 in the capital, with its daily count rising by 216 from a week earlier.
The metropolitan government also reported six new deaths among COVID-19 patients, while the number of patients with severe symptoms under its criteria stood at nine, unchanged from the previous day.
