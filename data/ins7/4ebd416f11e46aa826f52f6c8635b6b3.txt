Published: 10:02 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 10:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 Steve Cooper says Saturday's game with Liverpool is a reminder of why Nottingham Forest were so desperate to get back in the big time.Forest host Liverpool at the City Ground in a repeat of their first home fixture in the Premier League in 1992, when Teddy Sheringham's goal gave them a 1-0 win.It has been 23 long years since the Anfield club have visited in the league as Forest meandered in the second and third tier. Steve Cooper has discussed his Nottingham Forest side's clash with Liverpool on Saturday It is 30 years after a Teddy Sheringham goal saw Forest beat Liverpool 1-0 at the City GroundBoss Cooper was the man to bring them back up last season and says Saturday lunchtime's game is their reward.
