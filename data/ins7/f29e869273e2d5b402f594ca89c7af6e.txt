Collectively, the Triangle's 50 largest private companies employ nearly 180,000 people and are responsible for tens of billions of dollars in revenue.

Triangle Business Journal 's latest list of largest private companies is ranked by total employees and includes revenue or a revenue range for each.

Here's a breakdown of the information provided in The List.

33 companies on The List employ at least 1,000 workers companywide.
Nine companies on The List have revenue of more than $1 billion.
Six women are mentioned on this year's list.
