 Interpol Secretary-General Jürgen Stock on October 17 said the international criminal police organisation was not playing any role in curbing state-sponsored terrorism and that it focussed primarily on ordinary law crimes. 'We are playing no role to be very specific and concrete. If there is any state activity, Interpol is not conducting any activity.
