By Australian Associated Press Published: 21:30 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 21:30 EDT, 17 October 2022 Premiership-winning coach Michael Maguire will join Canberra as Ricky Stuart's right-hand man for at least the next two seasons. The Raiders officially appointed Maguire senior NRL consultant on Tuesday and will welcome him in after New Zealand's Rugby League World Cup campaign. Working with the club full-time, Maguire will have similar duties to an assistant coach and the Green Machine told AAP they expected he would be Stuart's first port of call for football-related matters.
