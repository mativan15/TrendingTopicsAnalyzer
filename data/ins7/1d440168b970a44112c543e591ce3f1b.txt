 Waterloo Regional Police are looking for three men who they say were involved in a buy-and-sell robbery in Cambridge on Wednesday night.
The victim had arranged to meet someone near Ballantyne and McDonald avenues in the Galt area at around 7 p.m. in order to sell an item the victim had posted on a buy-and-sell website, according to police.Once the seller arrived at the location to complete the sale, police say three men approached the victim and stole the item.
