On ED notice to Sonia-Rahul, BJP chief Nadda says 'criminals deny' their crimes
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-01 11:17:32

Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) president JP Nadda took a swipe at Congress interim president Sonia Gandhi and party leader Rahul after they were summoned by the Enforcement Directorate's (ED) in connection with an alleged money laundering case. Nadda declared that a criminal will always deny their crimes and was quoted by news agency ANI as asking: "Have you ever seen a criminal say I'm (a) criminal? They will, (Sonia and Rahul) of course, deny it."Nadda further said the probe agency's documents were 'proof' of the Gandhis' crimes.
