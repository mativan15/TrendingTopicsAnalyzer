October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CINCINNATI--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CinRx Pharma, a mosaic of biotechnology companies accelerating transformational new medicines to patients, announced today that Gavin Samuels, M.D., M.B.A has assumed the role of Chief Business Officer. In his new role, Gavin will be responsible for leading corporate, strategic and business development and transactions for the CinRx portfolio.
