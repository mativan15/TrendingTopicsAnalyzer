Published: 14:29 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 14:36 EDT, 16 October 2022 Kate Hudson sent temperatures soaring upon her arrival at the BFI London Film Festival closing night gala for her new film Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery on Sunday evening. The braless actress, 43, flashed her sideboob in a plunging brown maxi dress while wearing a faux fur shawl across her shoulders.Meanwhile her dapper fiancé Danny Fujikawa put on a suave display in a black suit and bow-tie as they walked the red carpet at The Royal Festival Hall.
