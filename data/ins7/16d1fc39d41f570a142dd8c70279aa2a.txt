Crypto scams conned thousands of people out of over $1 billion since 2021, the most of any type of currency, according to new FTC report

DeFi scams make up a majority of the fraud in cryptocurrency. Vladimir Kazakov/Getty Images

More than 46,000 people fell prey to crypto scams since the beginning of 2021, according to the Federal Trade Commission.

They collectively lost more than $1 billion, the FTC said in a new report.

About half the scams started on social media sites like Instagram and Facebook.
