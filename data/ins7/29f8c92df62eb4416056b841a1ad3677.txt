Releases of Salman Khan 's films are nothing less than a festival for his fans. The superstar has a line-up of some very interesting projects in his pipeline and one of them was ‘No Entry' sequel.However, if the reports doing the rounds are anything to go by then it is being stated that Salman has decided to shelve ‘No Entry 2' owing to legal and financial complications. According to a report in a news portal, Salman had his heart set on the film and wanted to make it. The report added that the actor had himself sat on the script with Anees Bazmee and felt it was amongst the funniest scripts he read in the last decade.
