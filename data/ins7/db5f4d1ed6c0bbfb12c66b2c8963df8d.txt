Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Police say the animal which attacked is described as an English bull terrier (Stock image)A woman was dragged to the floor and bitten on the leg in a dog attack in North Yorkshire. The woman and her friend were walking their dogs in Holbeck Hill, Scarborough, on Monday morning when they were approached, police said.A dog, thought to be an English bull terrier, attacked their pets and bit one woman's leg, leaving her wounded on the floor. Officers want to speak to the dog owner, said to be a man aged 40 to 50.
