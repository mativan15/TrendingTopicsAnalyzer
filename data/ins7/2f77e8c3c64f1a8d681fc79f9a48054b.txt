ED moves HC against order allowing presence of lawyer during Satyendar Jain's interrogation
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 08:15:00

AgenciesThe trial court had remanded Jain to ED's custody till June 9, saying that his interrogation was required to unearth the alleged larger conspiracy.The Enforcement Directorate on Thursday approached the Delhi High Court challenging a trial court's order allowing presence of a lawyer during the interrogation of Delhi Health Minister Satyendar Jain, arrested in a money laundering case. A bench of Acting Chief Justice Vipin Sanghi and Justice Sachin Datta allowed the request of the ED's counsel for an urgent listing of its plea and fixed the matter for hearing on Friday.
