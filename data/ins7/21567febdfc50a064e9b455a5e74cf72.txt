May 31, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time GRAND RAPIDS, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Acrisure, a fast-growing Fintech leader that operates a top-10 global insurance broker, today announced it has secured additional funding to fuel its ongoing global expansion further. Acrisure closed $725 million in Series B-2 Preferred Equity and received a $23 billion valuation, representing a 31% increase from its last preferred equity raise in March 2021.



A wholly owned subsidiary of the Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA) led the round, with participation from Guggenheim Investments on behalf of certain clients and Oak Hill Advisors ('OHA').
