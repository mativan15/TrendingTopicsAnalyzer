Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Auto index falls 1.82%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 11:15:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Auto index closed on a negative note on Friday.Shares of MRF(up 0.77 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack.On the other hand, Bharat Forge(down 3.77 per cent), Hero MotoCorp(down 2.98 per cent), Bosch(down 2.83 per cent), Ashok Leyland(down 2.79 per cent) and Maruti Suzuki(down 2.78 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.The Nifty Auto index closed 1.82 per cent down at 11282.15.Benchmark NSE Nifty50 index ended down 43.7 points at 16584.3, while the BSE Sensex stood down 48.
