 Businesses that need to make a large purchase to expand operations or create jobs may benefit from a U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) 504 loan. Applicants must meet certain eligibility requirements and comply with a lengthy application process but can access low, capped interest rates and flexible loan amounts.
What Is an SBA 504 Loan?
An SBA 504 loan—or CDC/504 loan—is a financing program the SBA offers to promote business growth and job creation through the purchase or improvement of real estate, equipment and other fixed assets.
