The Spanish transcript is available here.The Economist: How do you plan to finance all your proposals? You want to expand the size of the state while prohibiting new oil and gas exploration, though hydrocarbons make up half of Colombia's exports. Gustavo Petro: Firstly, revenues from oil and coal have been declining. And in reality we are subsidising coal—it's not a source of revenue for Colombia, it's an expense.
