October 18, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time ALAMEDA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Exelixis, Inc. (Nasdaq: EXEL) announced today that its third quarter 2022 financial results will be released on Tuesday, November 1, 2022 after the markets close. At 5:00 p.m. ET / 2:00 p.m. PT, Exelixis management will host a conference call and webcast to discuss the results and provide a general business update. Access to the event is available via the Internet from the company's website.
