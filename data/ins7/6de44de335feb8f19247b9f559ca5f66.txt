Centre short of IT network switches for key projects
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 08:30:00

Shortage of multi-port computer network switches and long lead-time to get them have become a fresh cause of concern for the government when it is racing against time to complete several mega projects, including the New Parliament Building. This is also impacting the shifting of several government offices to new premises.Network switches are the backbone of the entire IT infrastructure of a building project, including the security system, as CCTV cameras are linked to these devices. Multiple data cables are plugged into a switch to enable communication between different networked devices.
