Tesla, the world's most valuable electric carmaker, will cut 10 percent of its workforce and pause hiring because CEO Elon Musk has a 'super bad feeling' about the economy, he said in an internal email to Tesla executives, Reuters reported today (June 3).
The email was sent on June 2 and titled 'pause all hiring worldwide.' In addition to a hiring freeze, Musk said Tesla needs to fire 10 percent of it existing employees to cope with a looming business slowdown, according to Reuters.
