Successful e-commerce stores share numerous must-have features, and arguably one of the most vital is a payment method. Without a seamless and effective way to get paid, your online store cannot deliver value to customers, and you can't make money either. Consequently, payment must be a priority whether you're just starting your B2B e-commerce business or have a thriving setup. But picking a payment method can be harder than it appears. There are many things to think about, including the available payment options in your location and potential fit with your business.
