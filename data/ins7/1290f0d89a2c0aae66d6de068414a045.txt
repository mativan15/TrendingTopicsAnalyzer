By Reuters Published: 06:14 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 06:39 EDT, 19 October 2022 Oct 19 (Reuters) - Health insurer Elevance Health Inc on Wednesday raised its annual profit forecast after beating estimates for quarterly earnings on lower-than-expected medical costs and strong performance of its Medicaid business. Last week, larger rival UnitedHealth Group Inc raised its annual profit forecast for the third straight quarter and said the direct impact of COVID-19 was expected to ease next year.
