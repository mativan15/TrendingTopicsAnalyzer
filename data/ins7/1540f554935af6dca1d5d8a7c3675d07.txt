Published: 13:44 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 16:20 EDT, 17 October 2022 NEW YORK (AP) - Leaning into education aggressively and uncommonly so for a media organization, NBC News is making its leaders available to students this week for a one-day digital seminar on how to succeed in the news business. The second Next Level Summit being held Tuesday is part of NBCU Academy, a nearly two-year-old initiative that also includes jobs, journalism training videos and partnerships with some 45 colleges and universities.
