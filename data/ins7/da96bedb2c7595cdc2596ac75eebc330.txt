A TIPPING Point contestant was forced to apologise to host Ben Shephard after making a huge mistake on the show.
Ben welcomed Laura, Tare, Sophie and Ian during a brand new episode of the ITV quiz show.

The show returned to its previous format of four contestants, but Ian was left apologising to host Ben when he made a catastrophic blunder.
Laura, Sophie and Ian got off to a great start, however, Tare struggled with the Tipping Point machine.
