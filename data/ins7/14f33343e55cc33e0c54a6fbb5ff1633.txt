The Wrap (Spoiler Alert: This article reveals the team that was sent home on Monday, Oct. 17)On Monday, the first night of Week 5's two-part 'Dancing With the Stars' event, social media personality Charli D'Amelio not only held onto the top slot on the leaderboard but also earned the first 10s of the season for her moving contemporary dance about her struggle with anxiety — with dance pro Mark Ballas as a very scary embodiment of that emotion.Judge Carrie Ann Inaba proclaimed the dance 'perfection' and admitted that at one point Ballas — looking every inch like Russian villain Rasputin — scared her as he menacingly approached a vulnerable D'Amelio.
