Location Sciences CEO, CFO to Leave; Considering Strategic Options
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 07:29:00

By Kyle Morris Location Sciences Group PLC said Wednesday that Chief Executive Officer Mark Slade and Chief Financial Officer David Rae will leave the company on June 22, and that the board continues to assess strategic options for the Verify business. The U.K. location-verification company added that the board continues to explore various options for acquisition candidates, in line with the strategy to increase shareholder value.
