It has been not even a month since the American superstar Loretta Lynn, known for her gold albums, left us. And the world has already started to miss her. An unexpected act in tribute to veteran songwriter Lynn by Kelly Clarkson, along with Dwayne Johnson, 'The Rock', is going viral. Clarkson and Loretta Lynn are from Texas.
