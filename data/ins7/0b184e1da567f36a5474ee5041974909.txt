REUTERS/James Akena/File PhotoKAMPALA, Oct 21 (Reuters) - An Ebola outbreak in Uganda that has infected 65 people and killed 27 is coming under control and should be over by the end of the year, the country's information ministry said on Friday.Africa's top public health body said on Thursday there was a risk the outbreak, which was declared a month ago, could spread but that it was manageable.
