KELLOGG, Idaho--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Oct 17, 2022--On or about May 17, NASCO Industrial Services and Supply, LLC ('NISS'), a premier supplier of mining goods and services headquartered in Kellogg, Idaho, entered into an underground mining service contract with CRG Mining, LLC ('CRG') headquartered in Gunnison, Colorado, under the terms of which NISS was to provide certain goods and services at the Gold Links Mine located in Gunnison, Colorado that was operated by CRG. After NISS provided certain goods and services to CRG, the latter ceased payment on outstanding invoices.
