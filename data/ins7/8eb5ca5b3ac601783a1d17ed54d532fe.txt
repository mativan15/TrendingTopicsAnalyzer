June 02, 2022 12:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PPM America, Inc. ('PPM') today welcomes Melissa FitzGerald as the new Vice President, Private Equity Investor Relations in the firm's Business Development and Client Relationship Management Team. With 13 years in the investment services industry, Melissa joins the Chicago office from UBS where she was a client relationship manager for the institutional asset management business. Melissa's role will primarily focus on leading all aspects of client relations in support of PPM America Capital Partners ('PPMACP'), the private equity arm of PPM, as well as other parts of PPM's alternative asset classes including real estate and private credit.
