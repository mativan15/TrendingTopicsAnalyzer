Fifth Third CEO Spence discusses Southeast pulling even with Midwest on loans

Fifth Third Bank's loan makeup hit a major milestone in the third quarter, CEO Tim Spence told me Thursday.

'I think geographically for the first time we've achieved the balance that we've wanted,' Spence told me in a one-on-one interview after downtown Cincinnati-based Fifth Third (Nasdaq: FITB), the largest locally based bank and the ninth-largest U.S.-based consumer bank, posted third-quarter results that fell short of estimates. 'The expansion markets and the Southeast roughly equaled the Midwest markets in terms of loan production for the quarter.
