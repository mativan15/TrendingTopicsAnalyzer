Published: 02:54 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 02:54 EDT, 21 October 2022 Shocking images over the last days have emerged of the arrest of freedom of expression activist Hossein Ronaghi when he presented himself at a prosecutors' office last month Iranian campaigners arrested in a crackdown over protests sparked by the death of Mahsa Amini are at risk of being tortured or even dying behind bars, rights groups warn. Amini, 22, died in September three days after her arrest by the morality police in Tehran for allegedly violating the Islamic republic's strict dress code, triggering protests that have been running for more than a month.
