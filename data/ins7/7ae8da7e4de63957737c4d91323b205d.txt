Washington, DC, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The 2022 United States Mint (Mint) Limited Edition Silver Proof Set will be available for purchase beginning on October 26 at noon EDT.  Production is limited to 50,000 sets, with orders limited to one set per household for the first 24 hours of sales. Produced at the San Francisco Mint, this set includes the following proof coins struck in 99.9 percent fine silver: One American Eagle One Ounce Silver Proof Coin – 'S' mint mark Five American Women Quarters™ Program Coins with reverse designs honoring Maya Angelou, Dr.
