Germany denies VW China investment guarantees over human rights concerns
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-28 08:15:00

Volkswagen said it follows the United Nations' guiding principles on business and human rights and that it was an integral part of the company's code of conduct. BERLIN - Germany's Economy Ministry has refused to provide carmaker Volkswagen with guarantees to cover new investments in China because of concerns over human rights violations in the Xinjiang region, Der Spiegel reported on Friday.The ministry confirmed it had turned down four applications from a company over human rights concerns in Xinjiang but declined to name the company.
