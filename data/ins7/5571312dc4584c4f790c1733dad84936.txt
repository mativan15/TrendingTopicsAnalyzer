Cait has over a decade of experience in the field of re-imaging capitalism. getty The world has seen an extraordinary rise in interest in and funding for Web 3.0 applications over the past two years. Funding across blockchain and cryptocurrency skyrocketed, tripling from under $10 billion in 2020 to more than $30 billion in 2021, according to Pitchbook data, fueled by the rising number of investors in this sector. Not surprisingly, this funding fury has driven up valuations, resulting in nearly 40 new crypto unicorns in 2021 alone.
