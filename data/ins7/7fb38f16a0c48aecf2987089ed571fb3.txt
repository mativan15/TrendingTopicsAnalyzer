October 18, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time INDIANAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Allison Transmission, a leading designer and manufacturer of conventional, electric hybrid and fully electric vehicle propulsion solutions, is pleased to announce that as of Oct. 17, Jackie Bolles has assumed a new role as Executive Director, Treasury and Investor Relations at the company.



In her new role, Bolles will lead Allison's investor relations program and serve as a corporate spokesperson within the investment community, managing relationships with sell-side analysts, institutional investors and individual shareholders.
