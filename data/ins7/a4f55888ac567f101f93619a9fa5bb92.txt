Shares of department store owner Macy's (M 2.42%) popped 27.3% this week, according to S&P Global Market Intelligence. The retailer posted solid first-quarter results that were above analyst expectations and reiterated its full-year guidance. At one point, the stock was up 30% this week. 
So what
On May 26, Macy's reported its quarterly earnings for the first three months of 2022. Revenue was $5.35 billion in the period, up from $4.71 billion a year ago, driven by comparable-store sales of 12.8%.
