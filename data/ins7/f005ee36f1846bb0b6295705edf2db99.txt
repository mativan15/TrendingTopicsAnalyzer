Is Canada facing recession? Freeland says fall fiscal update will reflect gathering storm
q107.com
economics
2022-10-19 18:45:12

Canada is facing some 'difficult days ahead,' but whether the government expects the economy to tip into recession will be reflected in the feds' upcoming fall forecast, Chrystia Freeland says.
Speaking in Windsor, Ont., on Wednesday afternoon, the finance minister and deputy prime minister told reporters that Ottawa will share with 'some precision' its projections for the economy in its fall economic statement. Freeland did not specify when the statement will be released, but that Ottawa will be sharing a date for it in the 'days to come.
