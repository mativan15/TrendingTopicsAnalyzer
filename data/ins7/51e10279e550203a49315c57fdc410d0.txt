Published: 11:26 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:37 EDT, 17 October 2022 A husband and his six-months-pregnant wife are among four people killed by Iranian-made kamikaze drones with 'For Balgorod' scrawled on them, which were launched by Russia in an attack on Kyiv this morning.  The drones, which were made by Iran and launched by Russia, attacked the Ukrainian capital, blowing up people's homes as they slept and taking out key energy facilities. At least five explosions rocked Kyiv starting around 3.
