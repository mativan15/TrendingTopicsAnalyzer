WASHINGTON – As gas prices remain stubbornly high before the midterms, President Joe Biden called on American companies to increase domestic oil production as he unveiled a series of measures aimed at bringing down costs at the pump.'They're not falling fast enough. Families are hurting,' Biden said in remarks at the White House. 'You've heard me say it before, but I get it."Biden said "we need to responsibly increase American oil production without delay or deferring our transition to clean energy.
