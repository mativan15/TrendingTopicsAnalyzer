Shark Tank India's rejected pitcher Rohit Warrier showcases his product in Dubai; shares ‘Glad glass ka mask could make it'
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-05-28 06:15:00

Rohit Warrier, founder of Sippline, who was rejected and mocked for his products on Shark Tank India, got to showcase his line in a Hotel Show in Dubai. Rohit, through the page of his company, gave a glimpse of the same on social media. The trade show is one of the premiere events in the hotel and hospitality sector in the Middle East that hosts global and regional entrepreneurs, agencies and suppliers.

In one of the tweets, Rohit took a dig at Ashneer Grover, who had mocked him for the ‘glass ka mask' products.
