3 men wanted after ATM stolen from Toronto business
globalnews.ca
news
2022-06-01 21:17:32

Three men are wanted after an ATM was stolen from a Toronto business last week, police say.
Toronto police said that at around 1:36 a.m. on May 26, three suspects in a vehicle arrived at a business in the area of Jane Street and Lambton Avenue, just south of Eglinton Avenue, and broke inside.Police said two of the suspects went inside and stole an ATM machine. The trio then allegedly loaded it into the back of their vehicle before fleeing the scene.Investigators released images of the suspects on Wednesday and said they're looking for three men in their 20s.
