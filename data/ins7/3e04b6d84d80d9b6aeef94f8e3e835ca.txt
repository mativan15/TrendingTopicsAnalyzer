A cooked turkey is served on a decorated table (file image) (Image: Getty)The price of a Christmas turkey could rise to more than £100 this festive season, according to reports.A UK-wide bird flu prevention zone was this week declared for the whole of the UK, as the disease has hit "unprecedented levels".Tom Copas, a farmer who works for family firm Copas Traditional Turkeys, said today his industry has had to adapt hugely to cope with the outbreak, which was last found on a farm in Anglesey, North Wales.
