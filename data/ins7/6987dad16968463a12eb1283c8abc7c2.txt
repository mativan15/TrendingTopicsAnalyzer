AgenciesHowever, the kit is now available only for research-use (RUO) and based on literature search and in-silico design, it added.Medical devices company Trivitron Healthcare on Friday announced it has developed the real-time PCR-based kit for the detection of monkeypox virus in India. Monkeypox, once endemic to countries in West and Central Africa, is spreading at a fast pace globally -- with about 200 confirmed and more than 100 suspected cases in over 20 countries. "India has always been at the forefront of extending help to the world, especially during the Covid-19 pandemic, and this time also the world needs assistance," said Chandra Ganjoo, Group Chief Executive Officer, Trivitron Healthcare, in the statement.
