Published: 15:09 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 15:09 EDT, 19 October 2022 PISCATAWAY, N.J. (AP) - Rutgers is no longer wondering whether this will be the year it gets back to the NCAA Tournament. After 30 years of wondering, the Scarlet Knights ended that skid in 2021. Coach Steve Pikiell and his players followed that up with another bid last season and now seek their third straight. Pikiell still has a team capable of making it to March Madness but he will have to do it without his two most reliable players.
