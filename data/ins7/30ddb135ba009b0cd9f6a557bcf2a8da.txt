Gap Home Kids line launches at Walmart
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 14:51:28

Gap Inc. GPS, -3.63% has launched a kids home line through its expanding partnership with Walmart Inc. WMT, -1.62% Gap Home Kids includes 200 items including bedding, rugs and window treatments priced from $20 to $79. Walmart and Gap first launched a home collection in June 2021 comprised of bedding and bath items, and have since expanded it to furniture and now kids. Gap stock is down nearly 40% for the year to date. Walmart shares are down 12.5%.
