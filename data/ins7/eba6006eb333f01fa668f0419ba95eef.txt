October 17, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SANTA CLARA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Stellar Cyber, the leading Open XDR provider for MSSPs, has earned a spot in the 2022 MSSP Alert list of the Top Cybersecurity Vendors Assisting MSSPs. Although it has been offering its innovative Open XDR platform to MSSPs for less than five years, Stellar Cyber garnered a spot among long-standing industry participants like Microsoft and SentinelOne.



'Stellar Cyber has rapidly gained mind share and market share with MSSPs worldwide, according to our annual readership research,' said Joe Panettieri, editorial director of MSSP Alert, a CyberRisk Alliance resource.
