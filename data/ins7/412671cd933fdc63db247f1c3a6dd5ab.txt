 A fire in the Discovery Ridge neighbourhood Thursday morning landed one person in hospital with serious injuries.
Calgary firefighters were called to the 0 to 100 block of Discovery Ridge Park just before 9 a.m.The Calgary Fire Department said the fire appeared to have been sparked outside, behind a home in the southwest community.
