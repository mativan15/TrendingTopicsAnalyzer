Battlegrounds Mobile India, more popularly known as BGMI was pulled from the Google Play store and Apple's App Store in India in July. The Indian government issued an order to ban the game. This was followed Krafton's head of corporate development Anuj Tandon's exit from the company this month. Although these came as a shock to the BGMI community, fans of the game can heave a sigh of relief if what AKOP from Orangutan Gaming has said turns out to be true.
