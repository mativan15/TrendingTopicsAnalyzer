asbe/iStock via Getty Images If you listen to economists and those watching the wave of inflation that is sweeping over us, you will hear many of these people predicting it will soon abate. A huge part of their argument being the best cure for high prices is high prices. This is because high prices bring about demand destruction.Taking this to the next level, some are looking towards an economic slowdown, a recession, or worse. Still, with so much money stashed away and so few real tangible assets, there is no guarantee inflation will slow or that we will see deflation.
