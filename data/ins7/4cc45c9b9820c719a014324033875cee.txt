Saudi Arabia: Retal Urban Development prices IPO at top of range
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-06 07:15:00

DUBAI: Saudi Arabia property developer Retal Urban Development said on Sunday it had priced its initial public offering at 120 riyals a share, the top of the indicated range, implying a market capitalisation of 4.8 billion riyals ($1.28 billion).The book-building process generated orders of 90.3 billion riyals, in a sign of strong momentum for Saudi Arabia's IPO market that has defied the global trend.The company will raise 1.44 billion riyals in the IPO, selling 30% of existing shares.Gulf issuers have raised $8.
