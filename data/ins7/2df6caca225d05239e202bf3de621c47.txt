Capri stock jumps toward 5th-straight gain after big earnings beat, $1 billion buyback program
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 11:15:01

Shares of Capri Holdings Ltd. CPRI, +2.46% hiked up 2.6% toward a fifth-straight gain in premarket trading Wednesday, after the luxury fashion company reported a big earnings beat for the fiscal fourth quarter and announced a new $1 billion stock buyback program. For the quarter to April 2, Capri swung to net income of $81 million, or 54 cents a share, from a loss of $183 million, or $1.21 a share, in the year-ago period. Excluding nonrecurring items, adjusted earnings per share of $1.02 was well above the FactSet consensus of 82 cents.
