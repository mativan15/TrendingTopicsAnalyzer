Around October 2021, my attention got caught by what was happening in the NFT space. If you were there, you certainly felt intrigued at how "pictures of animals" were being sold for hundreds of thousands of dollars.

While my first gut reaction was skepticism at the general sentiment, my curiosity propelled me to learn more. As I dug more into it, all the signs of an unhealthy and unsustainable market were there: the frenetic FOMO, greed, toxic positivity, scarcity and fake urgency.

If you experienced being a part of the NFT space during this time, I'm sure you recognized these patterns on many of these projects' Discord, Twitter and Telegram communities.
