(Oct. 14)LOS ANGELES — A jury of nine men and three women was seated Thursday in the Los Angeles rape and sexual assault trial of Harvey Weinstein.They were chosen in a process of about six days from a pool of 225 potential jurors who were summoned last week.The judge and attorneys still need to seat 10 alternate jurors, and the process will resume Thursday afternoon.
