Armando Bacot named ACC Preseason Player of the Year
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-18 18:29:14

UNC basketball senior Armando Bacot has picked up another honor ahead of the 2022-23 season.
On Tuesday, Bacot was named the preseason player of the year in the Atlantic Coast Conference, voted on by the media. Bacot had 82 first-place votes, beating out Miami's Isiah Wong who had five votes, teammate Caleb Love with four votes, and Duke's Jeremy Roach with three votes among others.
