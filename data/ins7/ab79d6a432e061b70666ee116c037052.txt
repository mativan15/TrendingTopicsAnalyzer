uskarp/iStock Editorial via Getty Images A joint venture-driven expansion plan is now jamming up Burger King's exit from Russia. The Restaurant Brands International (NYSE:QSR) owned burger chain has long-predicated its growth in European markets on partnerships in the region. These partnerships, including master franchisee agreements, allowed the chain to expand rapidly and address differing regulations across markets in a flexible manner. However, now as it seeks to exit the Russian market in a fashion similar to McDonald's (MCD) before it, the operator is appearing far less nimble.
