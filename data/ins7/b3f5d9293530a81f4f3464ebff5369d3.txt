How to go viral and stay viral with Boston's favorite TikTok baker
boston.com
news
2022-06-05 14:25:15

Several users on the viral video app TikTok have one viral video, but to go viral, stay viral, and build a following is a different story. Morgan Knight did just that. Morgan Knight, a 21-year-old recent Northeastern University grad, is using frosting, piping bags, and popular audio clips on TikTok to make a name for her cake company, Saint Street Cakes. The launching point for her viral, and sweet, side hustle was a video starring two frosting frogs and a short audio clip about the video game Fortnite in early 2021.
