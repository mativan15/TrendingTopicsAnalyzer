In an effort to improve Public Safety and end Gun & Gang violence, for an entire decade the Hip-Hop Summit Youth Council, Power 105.1, 'The People's Shark' Daymond John and 105 Days of Summer, along with a host of co-sponsors throughout the years including: Chick-fil-A, 24 Hour Fitness, NYCHA, C-COP, Guns Down Knuckle Up Campaign, Stiff Jab To The Face, Hip-Hop Roc The Rim Project, Time 2 Shine, 7 E's 4 Power, Brooklyn Nets, Big Panda Records, and the P.O.G. Music celebrated Hip-Hop Against Gun & Gang Violence Week.
