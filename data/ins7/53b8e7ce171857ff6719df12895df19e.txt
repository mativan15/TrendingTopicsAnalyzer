Published: 22:39 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 22:39 EDT, 17 October 2022 A sadistic horror film about a deranged killer clown is becoming one of the year's most surprising box office hits. Indie slasher Terrifier 2 has already grossed almost ten times its budget off a limited release in the States and has gone viral on social media following reports that movie-goers have been vomiting in theatres due to the film's extreme violence. While the movie has yet to see an international release, director Damien Leone is already looking forward to making another blood-soaked sequel.
