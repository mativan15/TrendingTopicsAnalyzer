Aftermath Islands Metaverse launched its Liquid Avatar app to replace usernames and passwords with proof of humanity. That means that you will be able to sign into its metaverse apps by using verified face recognition on your smartphone instead of using a normal login. If it sounds familiar, it's not so different from Apple using FaceID on an iPhone to recognize your face and log you into your iPhone or your apps.
