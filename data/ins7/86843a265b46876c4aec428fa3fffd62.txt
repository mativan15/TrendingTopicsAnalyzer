Walmart isn't letting Amazon have all the fun anymore and is hosting its own sales event to compete with Amazon Prime Day. Dubbed Walmart+ Weekend, this online-only sales event runs from 3 p.m. EST on June 2 through 7 p.m. EST on June 5 and comes with significant discounts on tech, fashion, home products, and more.Let's take a look at some of the best deals that you'll find once Walmart+ Weekend goes live. These days, vacuums are straight up expensive. That's because compared to vacuums of the past, modern-day sweepers are way more technologically advanced (some can even clean your home on their own!).
