Pitbull, rottweiler banned: See Ghaziabad's new rules for pet owners
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 10:00:00

AgenciesAuthorities in Ghaziabad have banned residents from keeping the ferocious Pitbull, Rottweiler and Dogo Argentino breeds as pets. The Ghaziabad Municipal Corporation has also issued a host of other guidelines for pet-owners in the city amid a series of pet dog attacks. These, in brief, are the new rules to be followed by pet-owners in Ghaziabad Pet-owners will have to get a licence for their dogs, which will be issued from November 1 The registration should be obtained within two months. No keeping ferocious breeds like Pitbull, Rottweiler and Dogo Argentino as pets.
