When it comes to investing in the cannabis space, there are a few directions investors can go. For those who are interested in the companies growing and selling cannabis, there are plenty of options, and much of the buzz is around those names.
However, another way to get exposure to the space is to invest in the businesses that support the cannabis space. This is where Innovative Industrial Properties (IIPR -3.33%) comes into play. By buying land that it leases to cannabis operators, IIP offers a unique opportunity for investors who are smart enough to know these three things.
