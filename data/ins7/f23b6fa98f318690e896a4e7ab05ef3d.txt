(Image via Sisk Brothers Funeral Home)HAMDEN, CT - (From Sisk Brothers Funeral Home) Raymond F. Hartmann III, 79, of Guilford, CT entered into eternal rest on May 18, 2022 at Yale New Haven Hospital after a long illness. Raymond was born May 12, 1943, to Raymond and Marguerite (Pendergast) Hartmann Jr. Mr. Hartmann grew up in Hamden and graduated from Hamden High School where he was a stellar athlete. He played baseball and basketball for the University of Connecticut. He was an Army veteran and in 1973 he graduated from the University of New Haven with a master's degree in business.
