Punjab Agricultural University employees, students come out in support of VC Satbir Singh Gosal
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 04:30:00

LUDHIANA: The executive councils of the Punjab Agricultural University (PAU) Employees Union, PAU Fourth Class Workers' Union and PAU Students' Union have appealed to governor of Punjab not to be hasty with his decision on PAU's vice-chancellor.Governor Banwarilal Purohit had asked chief minister Bhagwant Singh Mann to remove VC Satbir Singh Gosal stating his appointment was illegal.A renowned biotechnologist, Gosal was appointed as the PAU VC on August 19. Though his predecessor B S Dhillon retired on June 30, 2021, the government did not appoint a regular VC until two months ago.
