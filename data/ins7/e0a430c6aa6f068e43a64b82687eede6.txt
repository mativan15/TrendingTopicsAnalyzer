La. Lawmakers Try To Bring Down Cost Of Insurance Without Killing Insurers
iheart.com
news
2022-10-20 00:15:00

Photo: Getty ImagesLouisiana lawmakers are trying to figure out how to bring down the price of insurance without putting insurance companies out of business Hurricanes Laura, Ida, Delta and Zeta bankrupted a number of insurers and ran some others out of the state. The ones that remained are offering coverage at much higher prices than before. Republican State Rep. Gabe Firment is a board member of the Joint Committee on Insurance, and he says it's tough to make insurance affordable without making it hard for companies to do business.
