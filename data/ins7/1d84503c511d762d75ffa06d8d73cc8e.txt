Won't be cowed down by CBI, ED actions, says TMC's Abhishek Banerjee
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-29 06:00:00

AgenciesTMC leader Abhishek BanerjeeTrinamool Congress party national general secretary Abhishek Banerjee on Saturday said despite the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) and Enforcement Directorate (ED) continuously targeting him, he won't be cowed down. 'I am not afraid of any agency. They tried to harass me by calling me to Delhi twice. I have never run away from any agency. And now I took two BJP MPs who have joined AITC,' Banerjee said while addressing a public rally in East Midnapore's Haldia, the industrial hub in Bengal.
