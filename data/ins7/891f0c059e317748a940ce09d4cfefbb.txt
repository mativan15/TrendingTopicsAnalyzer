May 27, 2022 11:58 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP announces that purchasers or acquirers of Natera, Inc. (NASDAQ: NTRA) common stock between February 26, 2020 and April 19, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period') have until June 27, 2022 to seek appointment as lead plaintiff in Schneider v. Natera, Inc., No. 22-cv-00398 (W.D. Tex.). The Natera class action lawsuit charges Natera and certain of its top executive officers with violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
