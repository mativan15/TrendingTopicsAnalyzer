Russia 'throwing all its power' at Severodonetsk, says Ukraine governor

2022-06-04 13:38:52

Ukraine said Saturday that Russia was using all its available forces to capture the industrial city of Severodonetsk in a bid to control the entire eastern Donbas region. "The situation in the region as a whole is extremely difficult. Fighting is now concentrated in Severodonetsk because, as we understand it, the Russian army is throwing all its power, all its reserves in this direction," Lugansk regional governor Sergiy Gaiday said in an interview posted on his official social media.
