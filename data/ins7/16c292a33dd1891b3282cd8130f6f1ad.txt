SK Telecom CEO touts UAM as ‘game changer'
naver.com
news
2022-06-02 14:36:00

SKT CEO Ryu Young-sang (SKT)SK Telecom CEO Ryu Young-sang on Thursday showed confidence in debuting the telecom giant's own urban air mobility (UAM) service by 2025, touting UAM or flying vehicles as a 'game changer' that will offer solutions to resolving social costs of traffic-related issues. 'Today's business environment is being reshaped at an unprecedented speed since COVID-19,' Ryu said in a column released via the company's website. 'UAM used to be considered a technology that could be commercialized in 2030, but global companies are seeking to push the timeframe forward.
