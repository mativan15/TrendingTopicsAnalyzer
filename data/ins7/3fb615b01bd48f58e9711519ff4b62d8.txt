Published: 08:06 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 08:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 Screaming passengers onboard a United Airlines flight to Newark Airport were left in shock on Monday after a garden snake slithered underneath their seats. The unexpected appearance of the live reptile caused some turbulence among business-class flyers at the end of their journey from Florida to New Jersey. The United Airlines flight 2038 had just arrived at Newark after a two-hour flight from Tampa International when the reptile was suddenly spotted snaking through the seats onboard.
