Published: 13:33 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 13:35 EDT, 27 May 2022 Rishi Sunak was roundly mocked online after pledging to donate his estimated £1,200 bills handout to charity - despite being worth £730million.The super-wealthy Chancellor of the Exchequer urged other well-heeled Brits to hand the £400 state payment per property to charity.Asked about his intentions on ITV's Good Morning Britain earlier today, Mr Sunak said: 'I'm sure you will join me in giving that money to charity.'Mr Sunak is believed to own three homes in the UK, including a house and a flat in central London and a sprawling estate in his Yorkshire constituency.
