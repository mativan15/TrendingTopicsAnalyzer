The new iPad marks the end for Lightning and the Home Button, and I won't miss them
techradar.com
news
2022-10-18 17:18:49

Apple's iPad just turned a major port corner. For the first time in a decade, the base iPad, the one most people probably buy, will not feature Apple's proprietary lightning port. The iPad 10th Generation will feature a USB-C port. It's also, no less notably, dispensing with the Home Button in favor of a power-button housed Touch ID.Still, it's the port switch that got my attention. With this change, Apple's iPhone line stands almost entirely alone as the last vestige of Apple's decade-plus-old proprietary power and connectivity technology.
