If you're financially well, you'll feel secure about your financial future. You won't be overly stressed about money and will be able to enjoy life comfortably.To be financially well, it's important that you have financial literacy. This is the knowledge and training that help you control your finances. Financial literacy includes the ability to invest money for retirement, create and use a household budget, or speak with an expert about large purchases.
