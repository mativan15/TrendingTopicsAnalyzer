Image source, Peacocks of Pittencrieff ParkImage caption, Malcolm the peacock was killed during a break-inPolice have identified two boys aged 13 and 11 after a peacock was tortured and killed during a break-in at an aviary in a Fife park.The bird, known as Malcolm, was found dead at Pittencrieff Park in Dunfermline.Another bird in the enclosure, Louis, was also badly injured in the attack on Monday night.Peacocks of Pittencrieff Park, the group which looks after the birds, said Malcolm was "horribly tortured".
