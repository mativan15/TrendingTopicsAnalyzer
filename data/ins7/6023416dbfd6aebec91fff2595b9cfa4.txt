RFK grandson Conor Kennedy reveals he secretly fought on frontline in Ukraine

news
2022-10-20 16:02:24

Conor Kennedy, grandson of Robert F Kennedy, has revealed on Instagram that he secretly fought in Ukraine earlier this year.The 28-year-old said he was 'moved' to enlist, despite having no military experience, and joined the Ukrainian International Legion.Kennedy did not disclose when he fought nor how long he was in Ukraine.'I wanted to help. When I heard about Ukraine's International Legion, I knew I was going, and I went to the embassy to enlist the next day,' he writes in a lengthy post on Instagram accompanied by a photo of a friend named Nick in military garb.
