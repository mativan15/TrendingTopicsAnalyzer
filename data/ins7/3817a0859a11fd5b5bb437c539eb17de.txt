Woman snapping at sister over her "attitude" toward her dog praised
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 15:44:18

A woman has been praised online for snapping at her sister over her "attitude" toward her dog.More than half of 178 Mumsnet users have voted "you are not being unreasonable" on a poll relating to a dog mom's dilemma.In the post, the user DogMama93, explained she and her sister got sibling puppies around 12 months ago. But they have decided to give their furry companions different diets. The original poster explains she feeds her dog fresh food ranging from chicken, duck and lamb, whereas her sister buys pre-packaged food from the grocery store.
