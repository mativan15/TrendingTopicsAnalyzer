NEW YORK, Oct. 14, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Pomerantz LLP is investigating claims on behalf of investors of Cantaloupe, Inc. ('Cantaloupe' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: CTLP).   Such investors are advised to contact Robert S. Willoughby at newaction@pomlaw.com or 888-476-6529, ext. 7980. The investigation concerns whether Cantaloupe and certain of its officers and/or directors have engaged in securities fraud or other unlawful business practices.  [Click here for information about joining the class action] On September 28, 2022, after the market closed, Cantaloupe announced that it would not timely file its annual report on Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended June 30, 2022 due to 'management finalizing its assessment of the effectiveness of its internal controls over financial reporting.
