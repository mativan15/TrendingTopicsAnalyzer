British Prime Minister Liz Truss resigns after less than two monthsBritish Prime Minister Liz Truss has resigned after 44 days in office, the shortest in British history.LONDON – Liz Truss became the shortest-serving leader in British political history after she resigned Thursday less than two months into the job.Her announcement came after her attempt to roll out aggressive tax cuts aimed at spurring economic growth but instead dramatically roiled financial markets, led to unprecedented central bank intervention and drove her poll ratings to the lowest ever recorded for a prime minister.
