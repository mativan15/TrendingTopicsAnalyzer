How to market a small business? Be as boring as possible
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-06-01 17:00:00

When Morgan Maw​ began marketing her brand of New Zealand made oat milk, she only wanted to be one thing – as boring as possible. Maw​ is the founder of Boring Oat Milk. She came up with the name after she saw other local food products were colourful, eye-catching and constantly competing for a shopper's attention. Instead of trying to beat the other brands at their own game, she decided to create a product that stood out because it had little colour, minimalistic design, and basically was a little boring, she said.
