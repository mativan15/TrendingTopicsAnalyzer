Bangalore Mirror Bureau / Updated: Oct 15, 2022, 06:00 ISTTraffic on Outer Ring Road came to a stand still in September due to heavy flooding in front of Eco space tech park after rainsAfter the deluge in Mahadevapura, a 65-km-long stretch of rajakaluve is being recovered and restored with newly allocated funds. Under the CM Special Grants, Mahadevapura has received Rs 329 crore, and work will be taken up to make the zone flood-proof. Following the flood, BBMP has been taking up demolition drives to remove encroachments on stormwater drains.
