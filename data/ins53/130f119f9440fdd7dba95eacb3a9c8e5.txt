By Melvin Mathew 
Sampangi Rama Nagar government school gets CCTV cameras after frequent incidents of drunks trashing up campus 

When students of this government school would walk in for classes in the mornings, alcohol sachets and cigarette butts littered around were the first thing they saw. Tired of miscreants' making a party of it at the school campus, now the school has installed CCTV cameras to keep any eye on the culprits.

The government school at Sampangi Rama Nagar has got nine CCTV cameras surveilling its premises.
