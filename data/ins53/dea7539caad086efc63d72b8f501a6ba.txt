NEW YORK, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Franklin BSP Realty Trust, Inc. (NYSE: FBRT) ("FBRT" or the "Company") today announced that management will present at Nareit's REITweek 2022 Investor Conference on Tuesday, June 7, 2022 at 2:15 p.m. Eastern Time. A live audio webcast of the presentation will be available on the "News & Events" tab of FBRT's website at www.fbrtreit.com/events-and-presentations. The Company will also host one-on-one meetings during the conference.
