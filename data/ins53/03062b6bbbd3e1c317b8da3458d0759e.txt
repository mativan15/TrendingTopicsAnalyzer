Glasgow Warriors have what it takes to battle the best, says Ryan Wilson
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

Nine league games have now passed since Glasgow Warriors last tasted victory away from home and this miserable run needs to be brought to an end soon if the team's potential is to be reflected in their league standing at the business end of the campaign.Saturday's loss leaves them twelfth in the United Rugby Championship table, having finished eighth last season. Progress needs to be tangibly measurable otherwise it is just an abstract concept. The Franco Smith era is still in its infancy, but a record of two wins from five games played is not good enough.
