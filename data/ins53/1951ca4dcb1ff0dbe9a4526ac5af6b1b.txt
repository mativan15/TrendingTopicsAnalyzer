Wall Street opens lower after strong jobs data; Apple, Tesla weigh
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 14:15:00

U.S. stock indexes opened lower on Friday, dragged down by shares of Tesla and Apple, while a solid jobs report fanned worries over tighter monetary policy and soaring inflation. The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 261.96 points, or 0.79%, at the open to 32,986.32. The S&P 500 opened lower by 39.25 points, or 0.94%, at 4,137.57, while the Nasdaq Composite dropped 219.78 points, or 1.78%, to 12,097.12 at the opening bell.
