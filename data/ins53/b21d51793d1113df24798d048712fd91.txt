NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Regulatory News:

Philip Morris International Inc. (NYSE: PM) today announces its 2022 third-quarter and September year-to-date results. Growth rates presented in this press release on an organic basis reflect adjusted results, excluding currency, acquisitions and disposals. Given the uncertainty and volatility regarding the company's operations in Russia and Ukraine, PMI is also providing figures and comparisons on a pro forma basis, which exclude the company's operations in these two markets for all periods.
