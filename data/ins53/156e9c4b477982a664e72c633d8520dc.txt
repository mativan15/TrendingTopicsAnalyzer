ETtechBrokerages said Infosys emphasised that the demand outlook is strong despite potential macro headwinds, which was a positive factor.Mumbai: Brokerages have reaffirmed their confidence in Bengaluru-based IT major after the company's bullish commentary at its analyst meeting. CLSA has maintained a buy rating on Infosys with a target price of ₹2,040. Emkay Global has maintained a buy with a target price of ₹1,970 considering the strength in demand, steady gain in market share and strong cash generation.
