Union Minister Smriti Irani questioned the silence of Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal.New Delhi: Union Minister Smriti Irani on Thursday questioned the silence of Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal over Delhi Health Minister Satyendar Jain's arrest in the money laundering case.Speaking to the press, Smriti Irani said, "Delhi HC said in its order that Rs 16.39 crore black money was moved to four shell companies through hawala operators by Satyendra Jain, still CM Arvind Kejriwal is silent on it and is still blaming the investigating agencies.
