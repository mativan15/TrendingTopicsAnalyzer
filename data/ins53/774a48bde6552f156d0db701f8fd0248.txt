Comedians Comedian David Cross is mixing humor with politics in a new parody ad that trolls the GOP.The video by liberal PAC MeidasTouch and partners Bold Progressives is aimed at "saving democracy." It features a bearded Cross getting "interviewed" by Fox News host Tucker Carlson.The Arrested Development actor pretends to be a conservative candidate named Mark Finchem, endorsed by former President Donald Trump. As Cross speaks with Carlson, "Faux News" is seen in the bottom-left corner of the screen.
