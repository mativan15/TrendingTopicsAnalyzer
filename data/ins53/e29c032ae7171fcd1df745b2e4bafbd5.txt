Rs 25 lakh crore deposited in Jan Dhan accounts: Government
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 13:30:00

AgenciesAs many as four-crore fake ration cards were cancelled after the implementation of direct benefit transfer (DBT) and the same number of false LPG cylinder accounts, Reddy said.Highlighting the big achievement of the government in terms of digitisation and eliminating corruption in public schemes, Union Minister of Tourism G Kishan Reddy today said that about Rs 25 lakh crore has been distributed so far to several beneficiaries through Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana bank accounts. After inauguration and dedication of 75 digital banking units to the nation by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Reddy said half of the 50 crore Jan Dhan accounts belong to women.
