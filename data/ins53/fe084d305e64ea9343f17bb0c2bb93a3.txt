Written by Harshit Sabarwal | Edited by Aryan Prakash, New Delhi





Swedish luxury vehicle manufacturer Volvo on Wednesday rolled out the first locally assembled unit of its electric sport utility vehicle (SUV) the XC40 Recharge. According to a report by HT Auto, the XC40 Recharge was rolled out from Volvo Car India's manufacturing facility in Hoskote near Bangalore Rural.
