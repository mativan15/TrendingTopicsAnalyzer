Who is Markiplier?
Markiplier is an American Youtuber and podcaster born on June 28, 1989, in Honolulu, Hawaii.
His real name is Mark Edward Fischbach and when he isn't uploading videos, he is taking care of the clothing company Cloak, which he co-founded with fellow Youtuber Jacksepticeye.
