Traders hail Punjab govt's decision to empower director of factories for approval of building plans
indiatimes.com
business
2022-10-21 10:45:00

LUDHIANA: In a relief for the industrialists, the Punjab government has now empowered director of factories across the state to give approvals to building plans and completion certificates for standalone industries.Earlier, approval was required from Housing and Urban Development (H&UD) and Factories Department.Decision in this regard was announced by minister for housing and urban development (H&UD) Aman Arora . City businessmen are upbeat over the development and they are of the view that state government should review and relax the existing policies related to other permissions as well.
