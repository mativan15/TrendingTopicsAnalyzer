IndiaFirst Life Insurance may file DRHP for Rs 2,000-cr IPO by October- end

iStockFollowing Go Digit Insurance and LIC, IndiaFirst Life Insurance is set to be the third insurance player to file for IPO this yearBank of Baroda-backed IndiaFirst Life Insurance Company (IndiaFirst Life) is likely to file its draft red herring prospectus (DRHP) soon to launch its initial public offering (IPO), said two sources in the know. According to them, the Mumbai-based private insurer is likely to tap capital markets to raise funds worth Rs 2,000-2,500 crore at a valuation of Rs 9,000-10,000 crore for the company.
