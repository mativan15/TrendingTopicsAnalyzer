Development of big State building projects ‘outrageously slow'
irishtimes.com
politics
2022-10-20 14:34:09

The development of major State construction projects is 'outrageously slow' a meeting of the Dáil's public spending watchdog has heard.Fianna Fáil Cork East TD James O'Connor outlined planning issues and bureaucratic hurdles he believes are occurring on road and rail projects at the Public Accounts Committee (PAC).Secretary general of the Department of Public Expenditure and Reform (DPER) David Moloney responded, defending efforts to ensure value for money in such projects. But Mr Moloney expressed concern for delivery of the Government's National Development Plan (NDP).
