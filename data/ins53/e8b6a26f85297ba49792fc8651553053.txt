Do Kwon, the founder of failed crypto project Terra, the leader of failing crypto project Luna 2.0, and man hiding from his native country's prosecutors for alleged violations of financial securities law, is—all in all—'a little disappointed' in the way things are going for him. On the latest episode of the Unchained Podcast released Tuesday, Laura Shin talked to Kwon extensively about allegations of fraud against his company as well as what the Terra Labs founder has been up to since South Korean law enforcement put a warrant out for his arrest.
