By Reuters Published: 01:09 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 01:09 EDT, 20 October 2022 By Dharamraj Dhutia MUMBAI, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Indian government bond yields climbed on Thursday, tracking a sharp spike in U.S. yields, while the rupee teetering at record lows also weighed on sentiment. The benchmark 10-year government bond yield was at 7.5076% as of 0500 GMT, after ending at 7.4510% on Wednesday.
