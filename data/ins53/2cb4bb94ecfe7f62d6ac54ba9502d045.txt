Marco Bello Cathie Wood's popular ARK Innovation ETF (NYSEARCA:ARKK) ended Friday's trading session at its lowest point in five years, falling more than the Nasdaq Composite did during the dot-com bubble. ARKK's latest selloff now brings the innovation-based fund down 78.7% from its $159.70 record high, which came back in February of 2021. Despite ARKK's plunge, the ETF still is able to attract investors as the fund has accumulated $869.32M since the fund peaked. Moreover, since September 1, of 2022 ARKK has garnered $267.
