Liz Truss battles on but poll suggests Tory members have lost faith

Liz Truss has survived a meeting of the Cabinet without any ministers calling for her to quit, but Tory members and voters appear to be turning on her.A snap poll of party members released on Tuesday saw more than half respond that she should resign and 83% say she was doing a bad job.Downing Street insisted the Cabinet fully supported the Prime Minister and stressed that Ms Truss was focused on the challenges facing the country rather than party infighting.Asked whether Ms Truss was concerned about ministers discussing replacing her, the Prime Minister's official spokesman said: 'Her view is she needs to be focused on what is right for the country rather than on any internal discussions among the party at the moment.
