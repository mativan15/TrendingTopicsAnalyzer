(Shutterstock)PHILADELPHIA — A Philadelphia man associated with the Philadelphia mafia has been sentenced to prison on federal racketeering and related crimes, authorities said.United States Attorney Jennifer Arbittier Williams said Daniel Castelli, 68, of Philadelphia, was sentenced to five years in prison and four years of supervised release for racketeering conspiracy and conspiracy to distribute controlled substances.
