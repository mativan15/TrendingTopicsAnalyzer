Revfin raises USD 10 m to expand EV financing presence to 25 States
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 15:00:00

Revfin will use the funds from this round to expand its geographical footprint to 25 States and capture over 10% national market share of financed electric three-wheelers. New Delhi: Revfin, a leading EV financing platform for individual drivers, has closed its Series A round of funding worth USD 10 million in a combination of equity and debt. The funding round was led by Green Frontier Capital (GFC), a VC fund focusing on climate investments. Revfin will use the funds from this round to expand its geographical footprint to 25 States and capture over 10% national market share of financed electric three-wheelers.
