By Reuters Published: 16:29 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 16:29 EDT, 18 October 2022 South Florida quarterback Gerry Bohanon will undergo season-ending surgery on his passing shoulder, coach Jeff Scott said Tuesday. Bohanon injured the right shoulder during the second quarter of South Florida's 45-31 loss to Tulane last Saturday. Bohanon was hurt during a running play. Bohanon passed for 1,070 yards, six touchdowns and six interceptions in seven games this season. He also rushed for 386 yards and three scores.
