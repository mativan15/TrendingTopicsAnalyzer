(Google Maps )PORT CHESTER, NY — A Westchester institution is closing the doors of their lone remaining outpost after nearly four decades in business. The news came in the form of a somewhat dispiriting social media post. "To our loyal guests, we wanted you to hear it from us first," the owners told loyal customers Wednesday morning. "This will be Mary Ann's Mexican Restaurant's last two weeks of business as we will not be renewing our lease at the end of the month.
