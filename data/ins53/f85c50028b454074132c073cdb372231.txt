October 20, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SANTA CLARA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tavant, a Silicon Valley-based provider of industry-leading digital lending solutions, and Experian, the world's leading global information services company, are working together to minimize friction in the mortgage application process and improve the borrower's loan application experience. By integrating Experian's Enhanced Instant Form Fill for Mortgage with FinXperience and FinConnect, components of Tavant's VΞLOX digital lending platform, lenders, with a consumer‘s permission, can automate data entry and prefill borrower information on the Uniform Residential Loan Application (URLA) – providing a more simple and seamless experience for borrowers and loan officers.
