THE unsolved mystery surrounding the disappearance of a teenage girl has taken a dramatic twist after it was claimed she was sexually assaulted by a senior Holy See figure.
Emanuela Orlandi, 15, the daughter of a Vatican office clerk, vanished as she returned home from a flute lesson in Rome in 1983.
