'This year, competition for tech talent has grown fiercer across the country,' said Penny Crosman, executive editor, technology at American Banker NEW YORK (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Arizent and Best Companies Group have announced the 2022 Best Places to Work in Financial Technology. This annual survey and awards program was created in 2017 and is designed to identify, recognize and honor the best employers in the financial technology industry. Companies recognized on this year's list operate in and serve companies and consumers in a wide range of financial services including banking and mortgages, insurance, payments and financial advisory.
