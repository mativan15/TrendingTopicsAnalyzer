October 19, 2022 12:53 PM Eastern Daylight Time IRVINE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Arbonne UK, Arbonne International's subsidiary in the United Kingdom, was honored by the UK Direct Selling Association (UK DSA) with its Sustainability Award for redefining what it means to be a conscious corporation. The award was presented during the UK DSA Conference 2022 in Oxford, England.



As a Certified B CorporationTM, one of only 5,800 in the world, Arbonne continues to push boundaries in the beauty and wellness industry with rigorous goals, benchmarking and auditing against the highest standards of social and environmental performance, transparency, and accountability.
