People urged not to set off fireworks during Jubilee weekend over noise fears
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-06-01 18:05:28

People online have issued a plea to others to consider not setting off fireworks during the extended Jubilee weekend - as the noise scares both animals and humansPeople have been urged to be mindful with their fireworks (stock photo) People considering setting off fireworks to celebrate the Queen's Platinum Jubilee this weekend have been urged to reconsider by some online who are concerned the noise could cause distress to both animals and humans. Fireworks are commonly used to celebrate special occasions, most notably Guy Fawkes Night on November 5, and light up the sky with bright colours accompanied by a loud bang - but an image circulating on Twitter implores people to consider "silent fireworks" instead.
