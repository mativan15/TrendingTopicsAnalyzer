One firefighter injured, 33 people displaced after fire rips through building in Lowell
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-04 19:15:40

One firefighter was injured and 33 people were displaced after a 4-alarm fire burned through a building on Westford Street in Lowell Saturday afternoon.

Lowell Fire Chief Philip Charron said the call came in around 10:45 a.m. of reports of heavy smoke coming from the first floor of this 6 business, 12 unit building.

Everyone was evacuated safely, chief Charron said.

State Fire Marshall was on scene assisting with the investigation.

No word on the cause, but chief Charron said there is 'significant damage to the building.
