U.S. stocks edge lower after Microsoft cuts guidance
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 11:24:00

Stocks were slightly lower in early trade Thursday after Microsoft Corp. cut its fourth-quarter guidance ahead of the opening bell, citing the toll taken by a surging U.S. dollar.Investors were also digesting data on weekly jobless benefit claims and private sector employment for May. The Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +0.29% fell 86 points, or 0.3%, to 32,728. The S&P 500 SPX, +0.72% was down 14 points, or 0.3%, at 4,087. The Nasdaq Composite COMP, +1.37% shed 55 points, or 0.5%, to trade at 11,939.
