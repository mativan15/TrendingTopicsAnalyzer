Uncle Jack Charles, 'king of theatre', farewelled in Melbourne state funeral
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-18 04:20:49

Hundreds of mourners have gathered in Melbourne to farewell legendary Aboriginal actor Uncle Jack Charles, who has been remembered for his strength, wisdom and cheeky sense of humour.Note: This story uses the name and image of Uncle Jack Charles with the permission of his family.A packed Hamer Hall in the heart of Melbourne was told the 79-year-old Boon Wurrung, Dja Dja Wurrung, Woiwurrung and Taungurung man was the "finest storyteller", who used his voice to create change. Dancers performed and a smoking ceremony was held before the state funeral.
