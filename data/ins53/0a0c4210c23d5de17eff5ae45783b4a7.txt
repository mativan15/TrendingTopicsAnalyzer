WASHINGTON, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Danaher Corporation (NYSE: DHR) (the "Company") today announced results for the third quarter 2022.  All results in this release reflect only continuing operations unless otherwise noted.
For the quarter ended September 30, 2022, net earnings were $1.6 billion, or $2.10 per diluted common share which represents a 36.0% year-over-year increase from the comparable 2021 period.  Non-GAAP adjusted diluted net earnings per common share for the quarter ended September 30, 2022 were $2.
