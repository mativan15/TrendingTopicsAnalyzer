Punjab government gives nod to regularisation of 3.5k Ludhiana MC's sanitation workers
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 01:04:26

Fulfilling the longstanding demand of the municipal corporation's (MC) contractual sweepers and sewermen, the state government regularised as many as 3,542 employees. State's local bodies minister Inderbir Singh Nijjar, as a symbolic gesture, on Tuesday handed over appointment letters to over 140 employees at an event organised at Guru Nanak Dev Bhawan.Hailing the role of sanitation workers, especially during Covid-19 pandemic, the minister said they put their lives at risk to keep the city clean.
