October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nylas, the state-of-the-art CPaaS platform powering frictionless digital experiences, workflow automation, and personalized engagement through universal APIs, today announced the debut of its channel partner program through a strategic partnership with UpStack, the premium staffing solution to fast-track the tech hiring process with personalized matching. Under the terms of the partnership, UpStack's extensive network of 2,000 vetted developers can now leverage Nylas via the UpStack Marketplace to further assist innovative companies and teams to quickly and efficiently connect and harness communications insights to build better products, reduce costs, and scale quickly.
