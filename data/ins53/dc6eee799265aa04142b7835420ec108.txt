PANAMA, CITY, Panama, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- MetaMate is a pioneering project in MetaFi and SocialFi - the first to combine a digital dating platform and an NFT Metaverse SocialFi. Realizing the shortcomings of many current GameFi that solely focus on Play-to-earn, the development team of MetaMate has used the Dating-to-earn feature as a breakthrough and innovative progress. Following the success of IDOs on five major launchpads, MetaMate had officially launched its Mainnet version by the end of April, in which the earning feature was fully developed and deployed for users.
