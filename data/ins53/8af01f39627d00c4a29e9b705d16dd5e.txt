Stephen Brashear/Getty Images News U.S. flight-safety officials asked Boeing (NYSE:BA) to review its safety documents for the 737 MAX 7 in another possible obstacle to receiving approval for the airplane before a federal deadline expires this year, The Wall Street Journal reported.
