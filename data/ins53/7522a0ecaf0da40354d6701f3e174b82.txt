iPhone SE 2022 price hike alert! Apple's most 'affordable' smartphone now costs Rs 6K more
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 11:00:00

The iPhone SE, which was released in March 2022, runs on Apple's most powerful A15 Bionic chip, which is also in the iPhone 13 series.Apple has been steadily increasing prices on its products with each launch every year. The tech giant, which released its budget smartphone, the iPhone SE, in 2022 at a starting range of Rs 43,900, has hiked the price of the product. According to multiple reports, users recently started noticing the price difference in Apple's iPhone SE from the price tag it was originally launched at Rs 43,900 in India.
