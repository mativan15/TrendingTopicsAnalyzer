alengo/E+ via Getty Images Albemarle (NYSE:ALB) said Wednesday it was awarded a nearly $150M grant from the U.S. Department of Energy as part of the first set of projects funded by the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law to expand manufacturing of batteries for electric vehicles and the electrical grid and for materials currently imported from other countries.
