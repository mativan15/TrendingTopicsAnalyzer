Lamb Weston acquires full stakes in European Meijer frozen potato JV for €700M (NYSE:LW)

news
2022-10-20 13:21:17

Vladimir Popovich/iStock via Getty Images Lamb Weston Holdings (NYSE:LW) has signed deal to acquire the remaining 50% interest in its European frozen potato business with Meijer Frozen Foods, the Idaho-based company stated Thursday. The deal requires Lamb Weston to pay Meijer Frozen Foods €525 million cash and €175 million worth of shares, which is to see the funding coming in through the cash in hand and new borrowings. 'Lamb-Weston/Meijer was built over the last 28 years, and we're grateful to Meijer Frozen Foods for their longstanding partnership,' said Tom Werner, President and CEO of Lamb Weston.
