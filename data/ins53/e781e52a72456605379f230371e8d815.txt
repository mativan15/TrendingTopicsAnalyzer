Relationship tips: 6 ways to become an inter-dependent couple
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 12:35:12

Every relationship is founded on a solid foundation of communication, respect, and trust. Most significantly, neither partner unnecessarily relies on the other; they both understand that they are their own people within the relationship. Despite maintaining mutual respect for each other's limits, they have a deep emotional bond. In a relationship, interdependence is striking a balance between oneself and others, acknowledging that both partners are striving to be present and attend to each other's physical and emotional needs in ways that are appropriate and significant.
