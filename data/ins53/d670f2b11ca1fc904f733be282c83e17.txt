Published: 19:32 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 19:35 EDT, 21 October 2022 The billionaire owner of craft chain Hobby Lobby has declared that he has decided 'to give away ownership of Hobby Lobby' - adding: 'I chose God'.David Green, 80, founded the company in his native Oklahoma in 1972, and has presided over the growth of the family firm into a nationwide chain with annual revenues of $6.4 billion.On Friday, Green wrote an op ed in Fox News saying that he understood the decisions made by Patagonia's founder Yvon Chouinard and Alan Barnhart of Barnhart Crane.
