Vertiv CEO stepping down for health, successor promoted from within
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 18:10:00

Vertiv Holdings Co. plans a change at the top with the upcoming new year.

Giordano Albertazzi will be promoted to CEO and join the board on Jan. 1, succeeding Rob Johnson, who is retiring for health reasons, according to a release.

Albertazzi was appointed COO when the company made the announcement, adding to his title as president for the Americas.

"Retiring is one of the hardest decisions I have ever made,' Johnson said in the release. 'The decision was made easier though, knowing that Giordano is fully prepared to take the helm at Vertiv.
