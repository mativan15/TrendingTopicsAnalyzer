If the Tories don't stand for low taxes, what is the point in voting for them?
telegraph.co.uk

2022-10-17 08:53:00

'In office but not in power' is the description currently being bandied around about Ms Truss - but the rather more serious issue for the Conservatives is this could also be applied to a party whose reputation for fiscal competence now lies in tatters.
For most Tories, it was the poor execution of the mini-Budget that caused it to unravel - not the contents.
As economist Liam Halligan pointed out in this newspaper on Sunday, the policies which cost Kwasi Kwarteng the Treasury were 'mainstream and entirely reasonable' - and largely supported by Labour.
