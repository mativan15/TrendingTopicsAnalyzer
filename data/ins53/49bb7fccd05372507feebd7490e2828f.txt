DCX Systems' Rs 500-cr IPO to open on Oct 31
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 16:15:00

New Delhi, The Rs 500-crore initial share-sale of DCX Systems , manufacturer of cables and wire harness assemblies, will open for public subscription on October 31. The three-day Initial Public Offering (IPO) would close on November 2, according to the Red Herring Prospectus (RHP).The company has cut the size of its fresh issue of equity shares to Rs 400 crore from Rs 500 crore planned earlier.Apart from fresh issue, the IPO comprises an offer for sale of equity shares to the tune of up to Rs 100 crore by promoters -- NCBG Holdings Inc and VNG Technology.
