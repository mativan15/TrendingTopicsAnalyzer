Ensurge Delivers Unit-Cell Microbattery Samples to Lead Production Customer
globenewswire.com
news
2022-10-17 00:00:00

SAN JOSE, California, October 17 2022 -- Ensurge Micropower (OSE: ENSU, and OTCIQ: ENMPY), today announced it has shipped samples of its microbattery unit cells per the terms of a previously announcement agreement with a customer that is a leader and innovator in the digital health market. The sample shipments are the first step toward taking a customized solid-state microbattery into full production for the customer, who is making several milestone-based payments to Ensurge.  "We are pleased to have achieved this major milestone in our customer engagement, and especially that our microbattery has met the company's fast-charging requirements for its next-generation products,' said Kevin Barber, Ensurge Chief Executive Officer.
