TerryJ/E+ via Getty Images Badger Meter (NYSE:BMI) is an experienced competitor in the water measurement and quality industry. They have been providing solutions and expanding capabilities for over 100 years. Badger Meter primarily sells various types of water meters that are focused on serving various water utilities. Badger Meter is also largely aimed at the United States market which represented around 86% of sales through the first two quarters of 2022. Management has targeted international expansion by expanding into the Middle East, Mexico, Europe, Canada, and Asia.
