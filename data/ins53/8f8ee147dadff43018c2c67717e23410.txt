Kendrick Lamar's ‘Good Kid, M.A.A.D. City' Spends 10 Consecutive Years on Billboard Album Chart
variety.com
music
2022-10-20 22:18:02

Kendrick Lamar's 'Good Kid, M.A.A.D. City' celebrates its 10th year anniversary this week, and with it a huge achievement — the album has remained on the Billboard 200 chart since its release. According to chart history, 'Good Kid, M.A.A.D. City' has spent 520 weeks on the Billboard 200, making an even 10 years. The album peaked at No. 2 on Nov. 10, 2012, about three weeks after its debut. Lamar's two followup records, 'To Pimp a Butterfly' and 'Damn.,' have also spent triple-digit weeks on the Billboard album chart, with the former racking up 135 weeks and the latter at 287.
