Opinion: I'm a teacher – I know how important free school meals are

news
2022-06-01 11:23:54

Every teacher has seen it countless times. It's 2pm and a student in your class is visibly dropping off. Maybe they've forgotten their lunch, or decided to blow their money on sweets before school (unbeknownst to Mum). But maybe the problem is this child's family are simply unable to put enough money towards school lunches today. This means that the student is showing up to school with a serious disadvantage – something that's a reality for too many kids in Britain.The free school meals system is an absolute necessity, and a lifeline for many children.
