NYSE to Suspend Trading Immediately in Warrants of G Squared Ascend I Inc. (GSQD.W) and Commence Delisting Proceedings
businesswire.com
news
2022-10-17 00:00:00

October 17, 2022 11:52 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The New York Stock Exchange ('NYSE', the 'Exchange') announced today that the staff of NYSE Regulation has determined to commence proceedings to delist the warrants of G Squared Ascend I Inc. (the 'Company'), each whole warrant exercisable for one Class A ordinary share — ticker symbol GSQD.W — from the NYSE. Trading in the Company's warrants will be suspended immediately. Trading in the Company's Class A ordinary shares — ticker symbol GSQD — and Units — ticker symbol GSQD.
