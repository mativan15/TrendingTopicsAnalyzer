Rangers Marcus Semien Finalist for Gold Glove Award

news
2022-10-21 01:56:02

Marcus Semien is the only Texas player who is a finalist, doing so after his first full season with the Rangers.

Texas Rangers second baseman Marcus Semien is the only Rangers player to be named a finalist for a Rawlings Gold Glove Award.

Semien's name was released as part of the finalists for the American League and National League awards fore each position group. Semien was one of three finalists at second base in the American League.

Semien won the award in 2021 while with Toronto. This year's Gold Glove Award winners will be unveiled in a special edition of 'Baseball Tonight' broadcast on ESPN on Nov.
