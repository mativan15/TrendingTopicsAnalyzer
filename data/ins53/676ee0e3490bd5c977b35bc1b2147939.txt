October 18, 2022 10:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time PORTLAND, Ore. & ST. PAUL, Minn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Standard Insurance Company ('The Standard') and Securian Financial Group, Inc. ('Securian Financial') today announced a definitive agreement under which The Standard will acquire Securian Financial's retirement recordkeeping business. The acquisition is subject to customary closing conditions and is expected to close this year.



The Standard and Securian Financial are leaders in customer-centric retirement recordkeeping administration, compliance and fiduciary services.
