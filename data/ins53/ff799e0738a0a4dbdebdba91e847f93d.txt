ZENTALIS PHARMACEUTICALS

NEW YORK and SAN DIEGO, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Zentalis Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (Nasdaq: ZNTL), a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on discovering and developing small molecule therapeutics targeting fundamental biological pathways of cancers, today announced the promotion of Cofounder Cam Gallagher, MBA, to President. Mr. Gallagher, who previously served as an Executive Director of Zentalis, will remain a member of the Board of Directors, which he has served on since the Company's founding.
