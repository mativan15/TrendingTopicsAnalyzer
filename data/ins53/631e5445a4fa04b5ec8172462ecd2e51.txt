Odisha: Bhubaneswar police launches Swaagat to improve services
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-31 16:15:00

BHUBANESWAR: As part of the government's 5T (transparency, technology, teamwork, timeframe and transformation) charter, the commissionerate police launched Swaagat , an IT-based initiative to further improve its citizen-centric services at police stations In the first phase, the initiative was rolled out at Capital police station as a pilot project. Police commissioner Saumendra Priyadarshi inaugurated the project at the Capital police station in the presence of additional commissioner of police Uma Shankar Dash and deputy commissioner of police Prateek Singh.
