This government can still forge a legacy – here's how
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 23:01:00

Whitehall has, once more, ground to a halt as departments grapple with spending cuts for which there was no public anticipation or mandate. The usual games will begin — ministers will first offer up politically impossible reductions (not that there are many politically good ones) before the Treasury whittles down the budgets. Many of the small budgets — often the most interesting programmes, which could have become significant over time — will go. Not that the small budgets will be enough.All eyes are on Jeremy Hunt and this process.
