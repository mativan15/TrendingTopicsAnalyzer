Pakistan has sided with Saudi Arabia after OPEC+ snubbed the U.S government by announcing crude oil output cuts. "Pakistan appreciates the concerns of Saudi Arabia in avoiding market volatility and ensuring global economic stability," read a statement by Pakistan Foreign office.
