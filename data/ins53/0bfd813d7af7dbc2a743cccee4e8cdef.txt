Truck enthusiast company challenges people to get out 'In The Real' and earn a digital code to spend at realtruck.comOCALA, Fla., June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Preeminent online retailer of truck parts and accessories, RealTruck is celebrating National Get Outdoors Day on June 11 with a unique outdoor experience in which one lucky off-road enthusiast will win $10,000 in "Off-The-Grid Coin"* to spend on RealTruck products at realtruck.com. Ten additional winners will receive a digital code redeemable for $500 in RealTruck product.
