By Afp Published: 07:19 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 07:19 EDT, 20 October 2022 Hong Kong will outlaw cannabidiol (CBD) by February 2023 Hong Kong will outlaw cannabidiol (CBD) by February, the government announced Thursday, placing it in the same category as heroin, cocaine and methamphetamine with users facing hefty jail time. The move is expected to wipe out businesses in the Chinese finance hub that had, until now, been able to sell CBD-infused products such as beers, coffee and health supplements.
