US seeks 6-month sentence for Steve Bannon in contempt case
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-10-17 13:19:22

(Bloomberg) -- Steve Bannon, a longtime adviser to former President Donald Trump, should go to prison for six months and pay a $200,000 fine after a jury found him guilty of contempt of Congress for defying a subpoena, according to a government sentencing memorandum.Bannon's refusal to cooperate with the House Jan. 6 committee 'has exacerbated the assault on the Capitol,' the government said in a harshly worded document filed in federal court in Washington on Monday.The Justice Department said it was recommending the most severe sentence allowed under guidelines for the two counts of contempt.
