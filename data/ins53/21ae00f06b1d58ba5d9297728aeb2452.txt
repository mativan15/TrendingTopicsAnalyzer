(Representational image) The annual passenger capacity of this NITB will be around 2.5 million passengers.Construction of a New Integrated Terminal Building (NITB) with Technical Block cum Air Traffic Control tower and Apron Linked Taxiway and Associated City side works will be taken up at Imphal International Airport, Manipur. Sanmukh Jugani, Regional Executive Director, Airports Authority of India said that a new airport terminal building will be constructed with all modern facilities at Imphal International airport.
