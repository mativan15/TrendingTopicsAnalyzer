Radhika Madan-starrer 'Sanaa' to get a world premiere at Tallinn Black Nights Film Festival

'Sanaa' is the only Indian film competing for The Grand Prix for Best Film award at the festival this year.MUMBAI: Actor Radhika Madan-starrer "Sanaa" will have its world premiere at the 26th Tallinn Black Nights Film Festival, the makers announced Friday. Described as a social-drama, the movie is written and directed by Sudhanshu Saria of "Loev" fame. He has also produced the film through his banner Four Line Entertainment. "Sanaa" is the only Indian film competing for The Grand Prix for Best Film award at the festival this year, as per a press release from the makers.
