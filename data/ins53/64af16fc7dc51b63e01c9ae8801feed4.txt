An Albany restaurant plans to install two entrance gates to stop homeless people living on the premises.An Albany restaurant will install gates at its entrance to stop homeless people from entering after hoursPeople have left possessions and sleeping bags in the restaurant's outside areaPolice say a move-on notice can be issued for people refusing to leave a premises Liberte restaurant and bar includes an outdoor area accessible from the street even when the business is closed.Manager Hester van der Straaten said homeless people had set up in the undercover area during the past two years but the issue was escalating.
