This Frank Lloyd Wright house is Airbnb CEO Brian Chesky's favorite rental on the site
cnbc.com
news
2022-05-28 13:00:01

There are millions of listings on Airbnb's website, ranging from treehouses in Brazil to castles in France — but of all the Airbnbs Brian Chesky, the company's CEO, has stayed in, one home stands apart from the rest. It's the Palmer House in Ann Arbor, Michigan designed by Frank Lloyd Wright. "I'm a designer, but growing up, I always thought I wanted to be an architect," Chesky tells CNBC Make It. "If there's one listing I had to pick, I'd say the Palmer House designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, who is my favorite architect.
