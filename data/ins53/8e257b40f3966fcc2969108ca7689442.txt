'Give them credit!' Harry and Meghan praised for making William and Kate into stars
express.co.uk

2022-05-27 13:36:00

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry should be praised for forcing Kate, Duchess of Cambridge, and Prince William to "kickstart their star quality". Royal author and historian Tessa Dunlop told True Royalty TV's The Royal Beat that Kate and William's "monumental transition" was largely down to Harry and Meghan. She had responded to royal biographer and journalist Duncan Larcombe's claim that "now we are seeing the Prince and Princess of Wales in waiting".Mr Larcombe said: "Since they celebrated their tenth wedding anniversary last year, we have seen a monumental transition of this couple.
