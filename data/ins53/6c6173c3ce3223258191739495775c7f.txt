Dear Readers,It's been 14 years since the crash of Lehman Brothers… I was in my first job and have vivid memories of the havoc in the newsroom: speechless bankers and financial analysts, comments of policymakers assuring that Indian banks are safe. The Lehman crisis overnight bankrupted several big players on Wall Street, while Dalal Street was also hit badly. The Mint Street became very very cautious after the gigantic fall… I am sure, many of you will have memories of those scary days. The world's financial system came to a halt and policymakers came with many restrictions and stringent rules, including Basel-III norms….
