By Australian Associated Press Published: 21:56 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 21:56 EDT, 19 October 2022 Australia's COVID-19 response failed the nation's most vulnerable people and in many cases amounted to overreach, according to a new report. The report - Fault lines: An independent review into Australia's response to COVID-19 - led by former public servant and Western Sydney University Chancellor Peter Shergold, had over 200 consultations with health experts, epidemiologists, business leaders and other community groups with over 160 submissions received.
