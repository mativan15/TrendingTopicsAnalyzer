Russian lawmaker demands WhatsApp ban for state employees as Moscow moves to cut off Western technology
Insider
business
2022-10-20 10:30:47

A Russian lawmaker has said the use of WhatsApp should be banned for state employees as the Kremlin seeks to move away from Western technology during the Ukraine war.Anton Gorelkin, deputy of Russia's committee for information policy, wrote in a Telegram post that he didn't advise anyone to trust messaging platform WhatsApp, owned by Meta, with their correspondence. Gorelkin said Meta worked closely with US security forces and warned that people's conversations on WhatsApp could be interesting to them.
