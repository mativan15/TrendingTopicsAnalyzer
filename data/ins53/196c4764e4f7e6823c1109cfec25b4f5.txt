Obi Toppin, Quentin Grimes expected to be ready for Knicks' opener
nypost.com
news
2022-10-17 01:39:54

It appears Obi Toppin's turned ankle isn't serious, although he was held out of practice on Sunday.
Knicks coach Tom Thibodeau said the decision to rest Toppin was precautionary, and he may return to practice on Monday.
'We wanted to play it safe with him,' Thibodeau said.
Quentin Grimes (sore left foot) also didn't do much at practice. But Thibodeau doesn't think either player is in jeopardy of missing the opener against the Grizzlies on Wednesday.
'I think they'll be fine,' the coach said. 'This is part of the program.
