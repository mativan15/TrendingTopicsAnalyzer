June 02, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time RESEARCH TRIANGLE PARK, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--JAGGAER, the global leader in Autonomous Commerce, today announced it has been named a Value Leader, the highest designation, in Spend Matters' Spring 2022 SolutionMap rankings. JAGGAER was evaluated against 67 other procurement technology vendors and achieved above average analyst and customer scores across major product categories.



JAGGAER ONE secured the Value Leader ranking for all buying personas in the source-to-pay (S2P) and source-to-contract (S2C) categories.
