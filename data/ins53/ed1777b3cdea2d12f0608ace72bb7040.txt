Paul Neave has been jailed for 21 years (Image: Derbyshire Police)Paul Neave, 51, transferred the videos from a camera on to harddrives after he had used it to capture himself having sex with the children. Derby Crown Court heard the professional session musician kept the harddrives in a storage unit at a farm and, for more than one decade, thought he'd got away with the vile offences.But last year, one of the brave victims plucked up the courage to come forward, confided in her sister and a police investigation began.
