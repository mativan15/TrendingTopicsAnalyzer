Ex-girlfriend worried about being implicated in freezer murder
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-05-30 22:19:58

Startling evidence was heard Monday in a St. Thomas, Ont. courtroom from the ex-girlfriend of a man on trial for first degree murder. The woman — who cannot be identified — told the jury about an admission made to her by the accused, Chad Reu-Waters, 48, surrounding the death of a Mississauga man who was found in a freezer years after he vanished. Testifying from a separate room she told the court while out on a drive with the accused in his Corvette in 2018, 'He told me he had ties to Hells Angels and the mob.
