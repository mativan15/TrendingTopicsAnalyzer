India to showcase arms to Africa, Indian Ocean Region nations

2022-10-17 19:00:00

ANIDefence Minister Rajnath Singh speaks at the Def Expo 2022 Curtain Raiser event, in Gandhinagar.India will showcase its indigenously developed weapon systems and research in emerging technologies like swarm drones and artificial intelligence at its flagship DefExpo exhibition as it looks to focus on exports to friendly nations in Africa and the Indian Ocean Region. Defence Minister Rajnath Singh, who is holding bilateral talks with his counterparts from several African nations including Mauritania, Gambia and the Central African Republic (CAR) at the sidelines of the show, said India's target is to achieve exports worth '35,000 crore by 2025, up from last year's Rs 13,000 crore.
