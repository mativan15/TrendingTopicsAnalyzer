In an effort to start and continually run a business, there is a tendency for both leaders and employees to work themselves to the bone. And while these efforts can and often do lead to success, they also put unnecessary pressure on people to give their all for the business, even at the expense of their mental health, free time and quality time with loved ones. Maintaining a successful business may require sacrifices, but there are steps leaders can take to reduce the number of concessions the team has to make for the sake of the business.
