Verizon earnings show third-straight quarter of subscriber losses on a key metric
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 11:47:00

Verizon Communications Inc. just posted its third straight quarter of subscriber losses in its retail postpaid phone business as the company adjusts to the new economic reality as well as evolving competitive dynamics in the wireless market. Chief Executive Hans Vestberg had already cautioned prior to the report that Verizon VZ, +1.18% expected to see a third-quarter 'churn bubble' as a result of recent price increases, and Verizon in its Friday morning earnings release mentioned 'elevated churn partially as a result of recent pricing actions.
