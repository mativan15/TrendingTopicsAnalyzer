By Reuters Published: 14:35 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 14:35 EDT, 15 October 2022 LONDON, Oct 15 (Reuters) - Harry Kane's second-half penalty on his 400th appearance for Tottenham Hotspur helped them to a 2-0 win over Everton to cement their place in the Premier League top four on Saturday. Everton had defended resolutely and wasted a couple of gilt-edged chances but when their goalkeeper Jordan Pickford brought down England team mate Kane just before the hour mark the striker made no mistake from the spot.
