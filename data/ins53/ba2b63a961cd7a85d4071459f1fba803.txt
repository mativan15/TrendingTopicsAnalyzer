Cyberthreats are increasing. It was recently reported that risk has soared 235 per cent in the last year, with small businesses most vulnerable to cyber-related attacks. Experts have been raising their voices to urge all businesses to ramp up their security defences as the overarching financial cost of breaches has been seen to grow in line with increasing threats.A recent Hiscox study involving more than 5,400 small, medium and large businesses across seven countries found that 55 per cent of UK firms had faced an attack in the past 12 months - up from 40 per cent last year.
