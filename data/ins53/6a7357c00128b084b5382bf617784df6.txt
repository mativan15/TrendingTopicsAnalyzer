Compensation owed to home-buyers: Rs 250 cr
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 00:30:00

Home buyers wait as revenue officials drag feet in recovering money As per Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) Act, the orders passed by the RERA or Adjudicating Officer need to be executed by the Revenue Department and compensation; delay interest or deposit money needs to be recovered as arrears of land revenue to compensate for the loss incurred by homebuyers. However, inefficient functioning of revenue officials has become a cause of concern for hundreds of homebuyers, who are compelled to wait for years to get the compensation.
