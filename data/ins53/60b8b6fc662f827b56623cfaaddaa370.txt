October 18, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Andersen Global establishes legal capabilities and strengthens overall coverage in Australia through a Collaboration Agreement with leading law firm Cornwalls.



Established in 1891, Cornwalls offers a full range of legal services, including banking and finance, corporate and commercial, employment and industrial relations, fintech, intellectual property, litigation and dispute resolution, property, restructuring and insolvency, tax, migration and telecommunications.
