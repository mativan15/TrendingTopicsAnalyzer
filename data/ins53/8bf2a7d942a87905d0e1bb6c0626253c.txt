LIFE is certainly not cheap when you have 22 kids to feed - like Sue and Noel Radford, who are parents to Britain's biggest family. 
But some people may wonder how the whopping family from Morecombe, Lancashire, can afford to raise so many children, and how they make their money.
Sue and Noel Radford are parents to 22 kids and are Britain's biggest family
How do the Radfords afford to live?  
The Radfords support themselves with the proceeds of Noel's bakery, The Radford Pie company, which is located near their home.
