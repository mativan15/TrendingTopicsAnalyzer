FPIs' highest selling between October 1 and October 15 was in banking and financial stocks worth ₹4,081 crore.Mumbai: Overseas fund managers nibbled carefully at shares of construction material, power, capital goods, and healthcare companies in the first fortnight of October. They pruned their holdings in financials, IT, and FMCG stocks.
According to NSDL data, foreign portfolio investors (FPIs) bought shares worth ₹565 crore in construction material stocks, ₹323 crore in power stocks, and ₹309 crore in capital goods stocks.
