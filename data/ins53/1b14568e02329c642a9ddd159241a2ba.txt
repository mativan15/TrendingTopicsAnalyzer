Significant surge in the commercial constructions activities across the globe, extensive use of use of submersible electric conduits, liquid tight conduits, and conduits providing protection against fire, rapid urbanization around the world, and increase in demand for highly secure & safe wiring system among people are expected to drive the growth of the global cable conduit systems market.
