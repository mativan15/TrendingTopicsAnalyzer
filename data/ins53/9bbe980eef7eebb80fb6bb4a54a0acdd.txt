 Equity markets extended their rally on Friday, with the Sensex and Nifty jumping over 1% each, in tandem with a positive trend in global equities. Buying in Infosys and banking counters helped markets maintain their winning run. The 30-share BSE Sensex rallied 632.13 points or 1.17% to settle at 54,884.66. During the day, the benchmark zoomed 684.1 points or 1.26% to 54,936.63. The broader NSE Nifty jumped 182.30 points or 1.
