Kelly Rizzo honours late husband Bob Saget with martini tattoo
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 17:59:51

Bob Saget's widow, Kelly Rizzo, has honored her late husband in a very unique way, unveiling a tattoo tribute of his favorite drink.
The Full House actor died on January 9 at the age of 65, after suffering a head injury at a hotel in Florida.
His full autopsy was permanently sealed at the request of his family, with the results described as ‘graphic'.
Months after his death, Rizzo has chosen to mark his life with a permanent inking on her arm, commemorating his love of cocktails.
Taking to her Instagram story, the 43-year-old gave fans a glimpse into the poignant design, showing a martini glass with one olive on a stick.
