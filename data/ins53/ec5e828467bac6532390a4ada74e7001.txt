 Statistics Canada says the economy grew at an annualized rate of 3.1 per cent in the first quarter, helped by business investment and household spending.
The result was down from an annualized rate of 6.6 per cent in the fourth quarter of 2021.The growth for the first quarter came as the economy grew 0.7 per cent in March.
