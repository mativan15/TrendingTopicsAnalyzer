Agencies"Commex along and its directors (noticees) have misled the Indian investors by concealing the information of entering into pledge and loan agreement to facilitate and finance the issue of its own GDRs and not informing the same to the stock exchanges," Sebi said in its order.New Delhi, May 31 (PTI) Capital markets regulator Sebi on Tuesday imposed a penalty of Rs 10.25 crore on Commex Technology in a case related to manipulation of GDR (Global Depository Receipts) issued by the company. In addition, Sebi levied a fine of Rs 5 lakh each on Adi Cooper and Kishor Hegde in the matter.
