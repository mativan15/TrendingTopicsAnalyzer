June 03, 2022 10:00 ET | Source: American Airlines, Inc. American Airlines, Inc. Fort Worth, Texas, UNITED STATES FORT WORTH, Texas, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- American Airlines Group Inc. (NASDAQ: AAL) will conduct a live audio webcast of the formal business of its virtual annual meeting of stockholders on Wednesday, June 8, at 9 a.m. CT. The webcast will be available to the public on a listen-only basis at aa.com/investorrelations, and an archive of the webcast will be available afterward.
