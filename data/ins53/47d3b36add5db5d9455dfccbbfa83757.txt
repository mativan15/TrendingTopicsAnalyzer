 U.S. Bancorp (NYSE:USB) has received all required regulatory approvals for the acquisition of MUFG Union Bank's core regional banking franchise from Mitsubishi UFJ Financial Group (OTCPK:MBFJF) (MUFG).
The acquisition is now expected to close on Dec. 1, subject to the satisfaction of closing conditions. Through the acquisition, USB is expected to gain more than 1M consumer customers and about 190K small business customers on the West Coast, in addition to loans and deposits.
