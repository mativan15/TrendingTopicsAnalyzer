By Reuters Published: 12:44 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:44 EDT, 21 October 2022 New York Jets wide receiver Elijah Moore will be inactive for this weekend's game against the Denver Broncos, coach Robert Saleh said Friday. Saleh's decision comes one day after Moore was sent home following a reported heated exchange with offensive coordinator Mike LaFleur. The second-year wideout also asked to be traded. "We've had our discussions with Elijah. Trading him is not an option," Saleh said. "Elijah will not play this week.
