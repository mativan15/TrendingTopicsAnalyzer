Adelaide Hills locals have voiced their anger over the decision to scrap a proposed heavy vehicle bypass at Hahndorf. The former Liberal state government proposed a freeway interchange that would have diverted trucks through the Beerenberg strawberry farmConsultation is underway on a new proposal to upgrade two existing interchangesResidents say it will not fix the traffic issue in HahndorfA bypass and interchange onto the South Eastern Freeway had been proposed just east of Hahndorf to reduce the number of large trucks travelling through the busy tourism town, renowned for its German heritage.
