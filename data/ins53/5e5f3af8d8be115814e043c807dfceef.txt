Sir Keir Starmer has called on Liz Truss to decline an annual allowance of up to £115,00 given to former prime ministers to help them fund their public duties.Despite resigning after 44 days in office and becoming the shortest-serving prime minister in British history, Ms Truss is still eligible to claim the entitlement.
