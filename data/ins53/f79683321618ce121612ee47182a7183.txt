Nothing Ear(Stick) launching in India on October 26, to be available on Myntra
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 09:00:00

Smartphone and accessory maker, Nothing will launch a True Wireless Earbuds called Nothing Ear (Stick) on October 26. Meanwhile, the company has joined hands with Myntra for the release of the new item in India. The company hopes to reach out to a section of fashion-conscious customers by utilising the customer base of the e-marketplace, Myntra. 
Product details The specifications of the new Nothing-Sticks are yet to be disclosed. The company has indicated a design change, though it retains the previous dual-tone finish.
