Tottenham boss Antonio Conte admits Dejan Kulusevski World Cup fear
express.co.uk
sport
2022-10-21 13:12:00

Tottenham Hotspur head coach Antonio Conte has admitted he fears Dejan Kulusevski could be out injured until the World Cup break in a significant blow. Kulusevski was initially expected to return against Everton last Saturday but now faces a prolonged spell on the sidelines.Tottenham host Newcastle United in north London on Sunday as they look to bounce back from an uninspiring 2-0 defeat at Manchester United in midweek. And when asked for an injury update, Conte provided worrying news about Kulusevski, suggesting he could be unavailable for Spurs' remaining seven games this month and next.
