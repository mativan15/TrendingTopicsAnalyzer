By Reuters Published: 17:15 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 17:15 EDT, 17 October 2022 Los Angeles Lakers guard Dennis Schroder underwent surgery on his thumb Monday and will miss the first 3 to 4 weeks of the regular season. Lakers coach Darvin Ham told reporters that Schroder needed the procedure to repair a ligament in his thumb. Schroder sustained the injury in the team's preseason game against Minnesota last Wednesday. He averaged 13.5 points, 4.6 assists and 3.3 rebounds in 64 games (29 starts) last season with the Boston Celtics and Houston Rockets.
