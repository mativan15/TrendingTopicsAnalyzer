Apple has now started broadly classifying its base variants versus the Pro models starting with the iPhone series 14. The U.S. headquartered company launched the 14th gen of iPhones last month keeping the base variant almost the same as its predecessor but on the contrary, the Pro versions came with some fundamental changes. Today, we talk about the Apple iPhone 14 Pro and find out if it makes sense to go with the dynamic island and 4x jump in camera resolution. The device we tested was running iOS 16.
