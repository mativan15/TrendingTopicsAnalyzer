LUSAKA, Oct. 20 (Xinhua) -- Zambia on Thursday launched an initiative aimed at providing a conducive environment for boosting the manufacturing of pharmaceutical products. Launched under the theme "Strengthening Healthcare through local pharmaceutical manufacturing", the Zambia Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Initiative was meant to reduce the country's dependence on imported pharmaceutical products. Sylvia Masebo, the Minister of Health, said during the launch that the main objective of the initiative was to create an enabling environment aimed at revamping the local pharmaceutical manufacturing industry.
