Editor's Note: Gotham Knights is a game from WB Games Montreal, whose parent company is Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment, which is part of the Global Streaming and Interactive Entertainment unit of Warner Bros. Discovery. CNN Underscored's parent company is Warner Bros. Discovery.

From Warner Bros. Games Montreal comes Gotham Knights, a brand-new, open-world, third-person action role-playing game featuring the Batman Family. Players step into the roles of Batgirl, Nightwing, Red Hood and Robin, a new guard of trained DC Super Heroes who must rise up as the protectors of Gotham City in the wake of Batman's death.
