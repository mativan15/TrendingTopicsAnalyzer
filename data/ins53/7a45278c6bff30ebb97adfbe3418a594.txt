Arsenal fans may have new worry for Leeds clash due to 'Drake Curse'
express.co.uk
sport
2022-10-16 11:08:00

Arsenal may be fearing the worst after receiving heavy backing from rapper Drake ahead of their Premier League clash with Leeds United. Mikel Arteta's men will be hoping to extend their lead at the top of the table with a win at Elland Road, but the mysterious 'Drake Curse' could intervene to ruin their chances after he placed a mammoth bet backing them to win.The Gunners have won eight of their nine games this season as they sit on top of the Premier League, with Manchester City and Tottenham in hot pursuit only a point adrift.
