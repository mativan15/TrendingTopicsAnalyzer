Updated: Oct 17, 2022, 14:20 ISTIn her starched purple uniform, oiled hair tied up in pigtails with purple ribbons, Meghna looks like any other government schoolgirl in Andhra Pradesh's Kakinada district. But the moment she says, 'Hi, guys,' into the camera to test her English, you wonder if she's from Boston, not Bendapudi. It's a transformation trolls have jeered, only to be shut down by wider appreciation and cheers from all over the world.
