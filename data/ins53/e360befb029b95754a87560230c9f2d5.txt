Valorant Episode 5 Act III Release Date and Battlepass Detailed
ign.com
gaming
2022-10-15 12:05:00

Riot Games today announced that Valorant's Episode 5 Act 3 will release on October 18, and will bring a slew of new free and Battlepass content, as well as a new agent called Harbor. Harbor is of Indian origin, and wieldings water-based abilities that allows him to unleash waves to either shield allies or destory enemies. You can see the introductory trailer for the character below. As for the new Battlepass, which will cost 1,000 VP as usual, it will bring a slew of new cosmetics, including Starlit Odyssey Vandal w/ Variants, Iridian Thorn Operator, Rune Stone Odin, Breach's Approval Spray, Grand Designs Card, Shell Song Buddy, and Iridian Thorn Blade.
