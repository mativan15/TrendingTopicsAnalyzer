May 31, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time FORT LEE, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cross River Bank (Cross River), a technology infrastructure provider that offers embedded financial solutions, convened a robust week of important conversations at Fintech Nexus 2022, to bolster the fintech industry.



'Together with our partners, Cross River is reshaping and powering the future of global finance by creating access to safe and affordable financial services to communities historically left out,' said Phil Goldfeder, SVP and Head of Global Public Affairs at Cross River.
