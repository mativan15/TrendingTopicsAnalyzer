October 20, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--VideaHealth, the leading dental diagnostic AI solution, today announced the appointment of dental technology veteran Tennli Nelson as vice president of business development. Nelson brings nearly two decades of dental industry experience in business development, sales, product and customer experience, including successful roles at SolutionReach and Dental Intelligence Inc., to VideaHealth's team.



In this role, Nelson will manage and grow VideaHealth's strategic partnerships, which includes Heartland Dental, 42 North and more.
