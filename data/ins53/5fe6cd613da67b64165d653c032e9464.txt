After a frosty month of trading, almost the entirety of NFT Twitter found itself virtually huddled around a Twitter Space on Thursday, May 26. Deep into the first "NFT Winter" -- a month of trading activity that was consistently lower than anything seen before the NFT boom of August 2021 -- the live-audio Twitter Space had a big audience. It included thousands of everyday traders, as well as influencers and prominent investors. What brought the whole NFT world to this one digital spot?It was a group of about a dozen grown men making barely comprehensible goblin noises.
