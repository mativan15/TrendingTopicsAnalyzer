Lawrence Bishnoi (file photo)In a Facebook post on Sunday evening, Canadian mobster Goldy Brar claimed responsibility for the murder of Punjabi artist and Congress politician Sidhu Moose Wala. Goldy Brar is a close ally of gang leader Lawrence Bishnoi, who is also suspected for murdering the singer. Bishnoi, who is currently detained in Delhi's high-security Tihar jail, is suspected of ordering the murder. 
Who is Lawrence Bishnoi?
Lawrence Bishnoi, a 29-year-old Punjab-based gangster, has disturbed the calm in Delhi-NCR, Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana, and Himachal Pradesh on numerous occasions.
