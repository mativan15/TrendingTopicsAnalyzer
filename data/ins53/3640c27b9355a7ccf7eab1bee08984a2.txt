Japan's Kishida to boost military, energy ties in Australia – DW – 10/21/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-21 06:48:50

China's growing military strength has spurred Japan and Australia to step up security cooperation. Prime Minister Fumio Kishida will also seek to shore up supplies of natural gas and coal on a two-day trip to Perth.Japanese Prime Minister Fumio Kishida was headed to Australia Friday to boost military and energy cooperation between the two countries in response to China's growing assertiveness. Before leaving, Kishida said he hoped to update a 2007 bilateral security pact during talks with Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese in the western city of Perth.
