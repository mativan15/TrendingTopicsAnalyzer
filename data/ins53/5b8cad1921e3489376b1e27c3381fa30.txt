Toronto city councillor Cynthia Lai has died, re-election campaign says
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-21 21:51:33

Toronto city councillor Cynthia Lai has died, her re-election campaign said Friday.
'It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Councillor Cynthia Lai, Ward 23, Scarborough North this afternoon,' a statement emailed to Global News read. 'She was surrounded by family at the hospital.'Lai was seeking to secure her seat again in Monday's municipal election.'Councillor Lai was elected in 2014, representing one of the most diverse multicultural wards in the city,' her campaign said in the statement.
