On 23 October, the 2022 Afghan national road cycling championships for women will take place.Afghan women's sport, under the Taliban?Things are not quite that straightforward and there are a few caveats. The race won't be taking place in Afghanistan, for obvious reasons. It will instead be held over a 28.5-km loop in Aigle, Switzerland, organised by cycling's global body the UCI.Among those taking part will be Masomah Ali Zada, the Tokyo 2020 Refugee Olympic Team member who became the first Afghan woman cyclist to compete at an Olympic Games.
