'Organizations from Atlanta and beyond have stepped up to show their excitement for the Showcase and help us celebrate African film projects. We want to thank Greenspoon Marder, Delta, Fulton Films, Ripple Videos and the many others that have come on board.' ATLANTA (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 RoadShow Africa announced today sponsors and partners for their event, The Africa Film Showcase. RoadShow Africa, a subsidiary of RoadShow Equity Partners, is the first platform to offer investors an opportunity to participate in a diverse portfolio of risk-mitigated media projects involving filmmakers from Africa engaging with Georgia.
