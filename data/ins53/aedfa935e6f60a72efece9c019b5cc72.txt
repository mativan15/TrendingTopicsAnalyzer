Car crashes into business in Pittsburgh's Squirrel Hill neighborhood
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-29 14:36:48

A car smashed into the front of a business in Pittsburgh's Squirrel Hill neighborhood.

According to Allegheny County 911, the call came in at 9:48 a.m. for an incident on Forbes Avenue.

Pictures from the scene sent in by viewers showed a white Chevy with damage to its right side.

Glass was splattered across the hood. The front of the Massage Envy is pushed in from the impact and a window is shattered.

Dispatch said no one was hurt in the crash.
