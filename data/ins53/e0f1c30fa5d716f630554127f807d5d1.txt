RT.com 17th October 2022, 21:13 GMT+11The country intends to produce over 600,000 improved Kalashnikov assault rifles on its soilProduction of Russia's new Kalashnikov AK-203 assault rifles in India may be launched before year's end, a senior Russian official has said.A plant in the town of Korwa in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh is ready to start manufacturing the new Russian AK-203 before 2023, according to Aleksandr Mikheev, the head of Rosoboronexport, the Russian state agency for the international arms trade.
