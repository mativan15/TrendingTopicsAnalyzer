 Opposition leader Edappadi K. Palaniswami can inspect Salem government hospital and the drug warehouse and find out for himself the availability of essential drugs, Health Minister Ma. Subramanian said here on Sunday.The Minister inspected the government drug warehouse at the Steel Plant and the Government Mohan Kumaramangalam Medical College Hospital.
