SAN JOSE, Calif., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Tredence Inc., a leading data science and AI solutions provider, recently launched the Prescriptive Revenue Growth Management (RGM) platform to help consumer packaged goods companies (CPGs) proactively spot growth opportunities and monetize them through prescriptive analytics.
The platform encompasses customizable accelerators (product assortment, pricing, promotion, performance and executive review), frameworks to assess the maturity of the practice, reusable toolkits and customer success stories.
