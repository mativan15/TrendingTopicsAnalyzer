By Lucy Raitano

LONDON, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Sterling edged up against the dollar on Thursday, reversing earlier losses as political chaos unfolded in the UK and as a Bank of England official's comments prompted a further scaling back of bets on a full percentage-point rate hike in November .

British Prime Minister Liz Truss is meeting Graham Brady, the head of the 1922 Committee of Conservative lawmakers, a source in Truss's Downing Street office said.
