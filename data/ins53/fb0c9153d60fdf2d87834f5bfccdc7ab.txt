Korea's state audit agency requests investigation of 20 officials in fisheries official case
naver.com
news
2022-10-16 18:57:00

A Democratic Party (DP) committee against political oppression holds a press conference on Friday at the National Assembly, slamming the the Board of Audit and Inspection for becoming a "pawn" of the Yoon Suk-yeol government a day after the institution referred former officials of the previous administration to prosecutors over the killing of a South Korean fisheries official by North Korea in 2020. [YONHAP] The state audit agency requested the prosecution investigate 20 people, including top security officials of the previous Moon Jae-in administration, on charges of covering up and distorting facts in the killing of a South Korean fisheries official by North Korean soldiers in 2020.
