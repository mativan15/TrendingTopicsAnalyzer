Len Bergstein, a Portland business policy force, dies at age 76
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 17:40:00

One of the city's best-known political operatives has died at the age of 76.

Len Bergstein long represented both candidates and business interests in his 50 years in Oregon. .

"He was a dear and valued man whose insight, intellect and counsel provided so much good to this community that he cared about deeply," said KGW news director Greg Retsinas.

His Northwest Strategies clients included TMT Development, the Portland Trail Blazers, Greenbrier, Sokol-Blosser Winery, Columbia Forest Products and the Confederated Tribes of Grand Ronde.
