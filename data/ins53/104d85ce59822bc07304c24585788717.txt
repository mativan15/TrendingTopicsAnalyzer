Riteish Deshmukh and Genelia DeshmukhBollywood actor Riteish Deshmukh and Genelia Deshmukh have landed in a controversy over the allotment of a plot to their agro-processing company in the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation (MIDC) area in Latur. The Maharashtra government has reportedly ordered an investigation into an alleged out-of-turn government plot allotment to the actor couple. According to reports, BJP's Latur city vice president Pradeep More alleged that during the tenure of the former Maha Vikas Aghadi( MVA) government, a plot of Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation( MIDC) in Latur was given out of turn to the Bollywood couple.
