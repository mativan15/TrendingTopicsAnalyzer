Popular Detroit judge remains driven and strong in retirement

2022-06-04 11:00:22

Retired judge Craig Strong stands in the atrium of the Charles H. Wright Museum of African American History in Detroit on Thursday, June 2, 2022. Strong's wardrobe is known across the city and country, as he has been photographed with many celebrities throughout the year. However, the charismatic Judge Strong has always had a serious side rooted in charity and service. In retirement, he remains committed to uplifting Detroit and one of his lifelong causes is the Charles H. Wright Museum.

Craig Strong takes pride in being 'a man of the people' and he says when people see him they are looking at a reflection of his Detroit roots.
