Police have arrested two doctors posted at the government district hospital in Sonbhadra for allegedly preparing fake medical reports in lieu of money.
Those arrested were identified as Dr Purendu Shekhar Singh and Dr Daya Shankar Singh, both in their 50s. They were booked Sections 420 (cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property), 468 (forgery for purpose of cheating) and 471 (using as genuine a forged document or electronic record) of the Indian Penal Code and provisions of the Prevention of Corruption Act.
