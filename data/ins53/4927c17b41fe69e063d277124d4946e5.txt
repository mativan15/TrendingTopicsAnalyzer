KATY, Texas, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Academy Sports and Outdoors, Inc. (the 'Company' or 'Academy') (Nasdaq: ASO) announced today that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend with respect to the fiscal quarter ended April 30, 2022, of $0.075 per share of the Company's common stock. The dividend is payable on July 14, 2022, to stockholders of record as of the close of business on June 16, 2022.About Academy Sports + Outdoors Academy is a leading full-line sporting goods and outdoor recreation retailer in the United States.
