Strictly's Gorka Marquez urges Helen Skelton to have more self-belief
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 22:03:07

Helen Skelton is definitely partnered with a good egg on Strictly Come Dancing this year, as Gorka Marquez is always there to be her cheerleader.
The former Blue Peter presenter scored 29 points for her paso doble in week four, with the judges comments urging Helen to have some more self-belief.
She well and truly gave the Spanish-themed performance to Tamacun by Rodrigo y Gabriela her all, but Shirley Ballas reckons the Countryfile star is ‘better' than she realises.
Luckily, Gorka – whose previous partners included Katie McGlynn and Alexandra Burke – is on hand for moral support, noting that Helen has had a ‘difficult few weeks.
