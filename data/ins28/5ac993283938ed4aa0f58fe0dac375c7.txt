getty The inception of the cloud dates back to the launch of Salesforce's 'zero software' in 2000. There were very few competitors at the time, and while the benefits of using it (such as elasticity and scalability) were known about in theory, potential users still had to be convinced they could be realized in practice. Now, two decades later, the cloud is no longer up for debate.
