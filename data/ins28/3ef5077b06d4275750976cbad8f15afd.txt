Worker shortage forces summer camps to trim — or cancel — all programs
cnn.com
economics
2022-06-04 11:16:48

New York (CNNBusiness)Many working parents who rely on local summer camps to keep their kids busy when school's out for the summer won't have that option this year because of a serious staffing shortage.The start of summer vacation has arrived, but instead of preparing to welcome campers, several camps have had to disappoint families by shrinking the variety of programs they can offer this year. Some are canceling overnight programs, or worse, camp altogether for the 2022 season.It's a nightmare situation for families who now have to scramble for a plan B.
