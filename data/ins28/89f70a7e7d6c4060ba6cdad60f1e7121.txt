16 October 2022 by Pablo Sandoval 
Variety - Film News Jalmari Helander's WWII action thriller 'Sisu' has made good on its upbeat reception at Toronto Midnight Madness – Variety called it 'outrageously entertaining' – winning the top award at this year's Sitges – International Fantastic Film Festival of Catalonia which is showing, like genre itself, clear signs of expansion.The over 70,000 tickets sold at 2022's Sitges were 10 up on 2019, and an all-time record. Sitges guests stood at 610, also the biggest figure ever, announced Mònica García Massagué, director of the Sitges Foundation.
