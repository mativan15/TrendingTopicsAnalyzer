Igor Kutyaev/iStock via Getty Images International Continental Exchange (NYSE:ICE) is looking to expand its customers' exposure to U.K. markets by rolling out a FTSE 100 Index Total Return Future on November 14, it said Tuesday. The ICE FTSE 100 Index TRF aims to replicate the theoretical returns on a FTSE 100 index total return swap in a less costly way. That allows participants to gain exposure to the FTSE 100 index, a basket of 100 firms listed on the London Stock Exchange with the highest market cap, without owning the cash underlying.
