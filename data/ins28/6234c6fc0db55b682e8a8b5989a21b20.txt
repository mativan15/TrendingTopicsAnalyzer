New York (CNN Business)So much for Amazon eating Chewy's kibble. The pet supply e-tailer reported surprise profit and sales numbers that topped forecasts after the closing bell Wednesday. Shares of Chewy soared almost 20% on the news early Thursday.Chewy (CHWY) is bucking what has been a downward trend for online retail stocks as of late. Amazon (AMZN) disappointed investors last month when it reported an unexpected loss and weak outlook. Other top e-commerce firms, including eBay (EBAY), Wayfair (W), Shopify (SHOP) and Etsy (ETSY), underwhelmed Wall Street with their latest results, too.
