Biden, Israel Are Silent on Settler Violence. Their Apathy Is Fueling It.
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-21 19:27:54

In 2014, Israel's then-Prime Minister, Benjamin Netanyahu, made an unusual call to a Palestinian family to express condolences for the death of their son. Mohammed Abu Khdeir was just 16 when he was kidnapped and brutally murdered by three Israeli settlers, who burned him alive. Khdeir's murder evoked a storm of condemnations from the U.S., the European Union, and the United Nations, as well as from Israel's government. Even the right-wing mayor of Jerusalem said the killing was "a horrible and barbaric act which I strongly condemn.
