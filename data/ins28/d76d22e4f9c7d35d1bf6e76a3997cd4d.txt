Down 55% from recent high, can this Jhunjhunwala stock stage recovery?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 05:15:00

NEW DELHI: , whose shares have fallen 55 per cent from their 52-week high levels, reported a weak set of quarterly numbers, with margins taking a big hit due to pricing pressures in the US market, declining covid contract revenue and import alert at Roorkee site. The numbers though beat analysts' muted projections. Analysts said near to medium term growth would remain under pressure due to regulatory issues, slow recovery in the API business and inflated costs, but felt the recent correction on the counter has made valuations reasonable.
