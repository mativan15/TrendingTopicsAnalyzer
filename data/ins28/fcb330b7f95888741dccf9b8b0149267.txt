Hit of Shanghai Lockdown Endures as Business Surveys the Damage
bloomberg.com
business
2022-06-01 20:00:12

It took only a tiny detail to disrupt William Cao's exports when Shanghai locked down its 25 million residents: a missing chop, as the official seal used by Chinese companies on legal documents is called.As chief executive officer of Gracell Biotechnologies Inc.—a Chinese drugmaker listed on the Nasdaq and backed by foreign investors including Eli Lilly & Co. and Singapore state investment firm Temasek Holdings Pte Ltd.—Cao had kept the seal secure in the company safe at its headquarters in Shanghai.
