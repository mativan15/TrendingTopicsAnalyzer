Nelly Korda returns to golf not a moment too early
thebrunswicknews.com
news
2022-05-31 19:54:00

Nelly Korda has a coach who knows all about top golfers returning from an injury, how patience and belief can be just as critical as any physical therapy.Among the messages Jamie Mulligan offered the 23-year-old Korda is that no one ever returned from an injury too late, even if that means starting on the biggest stage in women's golf.'I didn't want my first event back being the U.S. Women's Open,' Korda said with a smile after playing nine holes at Pine Needles. 'I was just more thinking like I want to be 100%, and that's all I was thinking.
