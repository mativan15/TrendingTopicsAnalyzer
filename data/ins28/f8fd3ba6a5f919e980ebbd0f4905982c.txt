New Research Shows Our Smartphone Use Has Skyrocketed to 352 Times Per Day 
NASHVILLE, Tenn., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Mask mandates may be lifting, but some of our pandemic-fueled habits are here to stay, including our "new normal" for screen time. In fact, even with the return of travel and in-person gatherings, most Americans can't seem to put their phones down and are reaching for their device an average of 352 times a day – once every two minutes and 43 seconds – according to new research by tech care company Asurion1.
