Santiago Guzman is co-founder, chief investment officer and head of research at Boston-based financial and technology firm Cap8.
getty Since exchange-traded funds (ETFs) have become a relevant player in global markets, the debate between active and passive management has been among the most contentious topics. However, the discussion has usually been circumscribed to the opportunity cost investors have between a product that follows an index, usually through an ETF structure, or an actively managed fund that chooses, through an allegedly thorough analysis, which stocks will outperform the broader market.
