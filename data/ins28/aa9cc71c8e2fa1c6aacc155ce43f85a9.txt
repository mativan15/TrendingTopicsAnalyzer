Summary

St. Louis-based Ameren Corp.is a public utility holding company that serves 2.4 million electric customers and 900,000 natural gas customers through its Ameren Missouri and Ameren Illinois regulated subsidiaries. Customers are located in central and eastern Missouri and southern Illinois. Ameren Missouri provides vertically integrated electric service, with a generating capacity of 10,200 megawatts, and natural gas delivery. Ameren Illinois provides electric delivery and transmission service as well as natural gas delivery.
