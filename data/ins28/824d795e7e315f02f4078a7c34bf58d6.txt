BEIJING, June 6, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Gaotu Techedu Inc. (NYSE: GOTU) ("Gaotu" or the "Company"), a technology-driven education company and online large-class tutoring service provider in China, today announced its unaudited financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2022.First Quarter 2022 Highlights[1]Net revenues were RMB724.6 million, compared with net revenues of RMB1,940.3 million in the same period of 2021.Gross billings[2]were RMB318.1 million, compared with gross billings of RMB1,181.
