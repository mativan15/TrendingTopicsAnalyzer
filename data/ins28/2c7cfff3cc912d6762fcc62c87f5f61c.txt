Realme GT Neo 3T to launch on June 7
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 16:00:00

AgenciesAfter a lot of speculation, those who were keenly looking forward to the launch of the Realme GT Neo 3T have finally something to feel cheerful about. The company has made an official announcement about the release date of the much-awaited smartphone. Realme GT Neo 3T will be launched on June 7. This news was shared by Realme Indonesia's official Twitter handle.
