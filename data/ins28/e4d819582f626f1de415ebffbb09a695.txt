Published: 12:10 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:10 EDT, 21 October 2022 Manchester City boss Gareth Taylor feels describing Saturday's match at Tottenham as must-win would be 'too extreme' but has stressed the importance of avoiding another defeat. City's 4-0 victory over Leicester last Sunday saw them register their first Women's Super League points of the season, having previously lost their first two matches 4-3 at Aston Villa and then 2-0 at Chelsea. Taylor, whose team are six points behind the top three of Manchester United, Arsenal and Chelsea, told a press conference: 'Everyone's capable of beating each other on any day.
