FBI Media Alert: Man Wearing 'Gas Monkey Garage' Jacket Robs Uptown Albuquerque Bank
albuquerquenews.net
news
2022-10-21 19:19:14

The FBI and Albuquerque Police Department are seeking the public's assistance to identify the individual responsible for a bank robbery on Friday, February 4, 2022.
At approximately 2:14 p.m., an unknown male entered U.S. Bank, located at 2300 Louisiana Boulevard NE. The suspect displayed a handgun and demanded money from a teller. The robber received an undisclosed sum of money and left the bank.
Suspect's Description:

Sex: Male
Race: White
Age: 30s
Height: Approximately 6'0"
Build: Medium
Clothing: Black and white baseball cap, dark jacket with 'Gas Monkey Garage' on the back, a light-colored scarf covering his face, and dark gloves.
