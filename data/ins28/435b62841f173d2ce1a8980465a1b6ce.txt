Variety - TV News Cannes — Taking place in the Grand Auditorium in Cannes, the crowd that arrived to cheerlead winners at Mipcom's Cannes TV Diversify Awards, was compact but buoyant as winners took to the stage.Some of the TV industry's only awards to celebrate diversity and inclusion, a record 190 submissions were received this year from 27 countries.Hosted by the international anchor and diversity advocate, Femi Oke, the Awards saw 10 winners announced on Wednesday night with many present to receive awards.
