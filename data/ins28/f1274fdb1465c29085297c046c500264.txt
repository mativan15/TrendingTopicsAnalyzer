To the editor: Letters editor Paul Thornton effectively articulated the dread and apprehension — not the joyous anticipation — with which so many Dodger fans greet October now that the team has reached the playoffs 10 consecutive years and left without a World Series title nine times.

I no longer enjoy April through September, and I don't sleep in October.
