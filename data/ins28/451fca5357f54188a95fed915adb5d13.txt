DXM Announces Addition of Retail Veteran Jeff Evans to Board of Directors

2022-10-19 15:04:27

NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- DXM, a technology (SaaS) and manufacturing (MaaS) platform for apparel and footwear industries, today announced that Jeff Evans has been added to the DXM Board of Directors. A strategic and adept senior executive with over 30 years of big box, department store and omnichannel merchandising experience, Jeff has a strong track record identifying business opportunities, and aligning and empowering teams to execute and deliver results.'The DXM team couldn't be more pleased to welcome Jeff to our board of directors,' said Matthew Wallace, CEO of DXM.
