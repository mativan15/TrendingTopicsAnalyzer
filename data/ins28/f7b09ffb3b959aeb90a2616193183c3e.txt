$19 million in loans approved for Black entrepreneurs in Canada along with access to a wealth of resources, masterclasses and financial guidance

TORONTO, May 31, 2022 /CNW/ - The Federation of African Canadian Economics ( FACE ), a nonprofit organization, which in partnership with the Government of Canada, created the first-of-its-kind Black Entrepreneurship Loan Fund to help Black business owners gain access to loans, capital investments and additional business resources, is celebrating its first anniversary.
