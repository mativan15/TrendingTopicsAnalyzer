REUTERS/Amit Dave/FilesBENGALURU, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Indian conglomerate Adani Group-backed Ambuja Cements on Friday reported a 68.75% slump in September-quarter profit, hit by ballooning expenses.Standalone profit for the three months ended Sept.30 was 1.38 billion Indian rupees($16.68 million), compared with a profit of 4.41 billion rupees a year ago. Revenue rose to 13.38% to 36.70 billion rupees.
