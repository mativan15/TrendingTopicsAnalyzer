Chewy CEO Sumit Singh began his discussion of the online retailer's first quarter results by saying the company has been engaged in a tug-of-war between strong consumer demand for pet goods, and an operating environment filled with challenges like inflation, shortages, and supply chain costs. But the more critical tug-of-war Chewy needs to win is the fight to hold on to its customers, and to fight off the competitors who want to steal them.
