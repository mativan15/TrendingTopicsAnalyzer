Empire co-stars Grace and Trai Byers expecting first baby together
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 09:17:28

Empire co-stars Grace and Trai Byers have announced they are expecting their first baby together, in an adorable social media post.
The Anika Calhoun actress shared a sweet Instagram picture announcing the happy news.
The couple, who married in 2016, posed on the red carpet of Trai's Broadway show The Piano Lesson as Grace cradled her bump.
‘Happy Opening Night on your Broadway Debut, my beloved King,' she penned.
‘Words absolutely fail when it comes to describing how deeply and overwhelmingly proud we are of you, @traibyers.
