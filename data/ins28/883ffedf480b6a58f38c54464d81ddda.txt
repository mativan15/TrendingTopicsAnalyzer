Pakistan delegation to visit India next week for dialogue on water issues
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 19:00:00

iStockUnder the relevant provisions of the Indus Waters Treaty, the meeting takes place alternatively in Pakistan and India annually.A five-member Pakistani delegation will visit India next week for talks on the water dispute between the two countries, according to a media report on Saturday. The talks will be held in New Delhi from May 30-31, the Dawn newspaper quoted Pakistan Commissioner for Indus Waters Syed Muhammad Mehr Ali Shah as saying.

The delegation would make the trip through the Wagah Border.
