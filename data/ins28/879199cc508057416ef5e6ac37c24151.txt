Afghan refugees create a new living by selling handicraft products
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 13:59:52

Building a new life in India, the women Afghan refugees living in the national capital have established a full-time career making handmade products and teaching handicraft to other refugee women.Farida Khair Khwa, 46, an Afghan refugee who arrived in India in 2016, teaches art and craft to at least 60 refugee women and asylum seekers, free of cost in South Delhi's Jangpura.
