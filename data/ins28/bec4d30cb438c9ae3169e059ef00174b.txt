Radha (name changed) went to Gani Market in Erode on Friday to buy a few essential items of clothing for her family. While the shopkeeper quoted ₹140 for a product, she wanted it for ₹90. Unable to get it at such a low price, she went to another shop. 'The wholesale price of basic garments, like banian, has increased from ₹90 to ₹140 a piece in the last four months. But customers ask for the old rate.
