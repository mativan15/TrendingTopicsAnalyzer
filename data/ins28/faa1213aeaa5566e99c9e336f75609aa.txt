Rising energy prices: Can AGL resolve its boardroom drama to cash in on rising power prices?

AGL's decline over the past five years is partly due to the impact of plummeting wholesale energy prices on the company's underlying earnings. A wave of new renewable energy flooding the market has largely been credited with driving down wholesale energy prices to the point where coal and gas generators are struggling to compete.LoadingWhile bottom-line earnings increased to $860 million for the year, AGL announced in August that underlying profit – the figure most closely watched by the market – plunged 58.
