Live: Germany sacks cybersecurity chief over suspected Russia ties

The head of Germany's national cybersecurity agency has been dismissed following reports of possible ties to Russian intelligence, the Interior Ministry announced on Tuesday. His dismissal came as several regions of Ukraine and the capital Kyiv were experiencing power cuts after multiple Russian air strikes targeted energy facilities across the country.
