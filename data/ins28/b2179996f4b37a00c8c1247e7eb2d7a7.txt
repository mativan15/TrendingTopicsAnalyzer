In a WBA (super), IBF, WBO and The Ring lightweight championship bout, Devin Haney and George Kambosos Jr. meet Saturday at Marvel Stadium in Melbourne, Australia. The fight can be viewed on ESPN/ESPN+, and will start at approximately 9 p.m. ET. Below, we analyze the Haney vs. Kambosos Jr. odds and lines, with picks and predictions.
Haney vies for the unified title. He has never lost in 27 career pro bouts with 15 knockouts. His past 3 fights are particularly impressive, stepping up in competition with 3 unanimous-decision wins over Joseph Diaz, Jorge Linares and Yuriorkis Gamboa.
