The Hollywood Reporter - Movie News Click here to read the full article.ABC is rounding out the cast of its live Beauty and the Beast special.The Dec. 15 special, celebrating the 30th anniversary of the film's best picture nomination — the first animated feature to earn that honor — has added Martin Short, David Alan Grier, Shania Twain, Rizwan Manji, Jon Jon Briones and Leo Abelo Perry to its cast. They join an ensemble led by Grammy winner H.E.R. as Belle and Josh Groban as the Beast; Joshua Henry also stars, and Rita Moreno will narrate the special.
