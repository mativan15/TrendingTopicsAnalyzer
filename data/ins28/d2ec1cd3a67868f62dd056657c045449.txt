WUHAN, China, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Cubic Sensor and Instrument Co., Ltd. ("Cubic"), a global leading manufacturer of smart gas sensors and advanced gas analyzers, held the prestigious Jaguar Land Rover Quality (JLRQ) award ceremony this month at its Wuhan, China headquarters.Mr. Garrick Wang, Jaguar Land Rover supplier technical assistance vice president, granted the JLRQ award to Dr. Youhui Xiong, chairman and founder of Cubic.
