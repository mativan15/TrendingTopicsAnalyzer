Delhi News Today Live Updates, October 20:   Inaugurating MCD's Tehkhand Waste to Energy Plant in Tughlakabad, Union Minister Amit Shah said Delhi residents have to decide if they want to be AAP nirbhar or Atmanirbhar in the next MCD polls. He said AAP wants to make Delhi AAP-nirbhar, adding that Arvind Kejriwal hold press conferences everyday to misguide people. He said there will be 100 per cent processing of daily waste in Delhi by 2025 and plans to make the national capital will be ready by by the time BJP comes to power in MCD.
