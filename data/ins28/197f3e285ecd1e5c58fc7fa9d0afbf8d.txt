'Sectors, where earnings visibility is relatively better, include banking, capital goods, energy, utilities and autos,' says Ashutosh Bhargava, Head- Equity Research and Fund Manager at Nippon India Mutual Fund.In an interview with ETMarkets, Bhargava said: 'Domestic oriented sectors are looking more attractive at this point; sectors and stocks where earnings meet expectations and margins are intact would command performance premium versus rest of the market.'
