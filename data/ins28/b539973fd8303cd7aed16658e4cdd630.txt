[Photo by WU YIBIN/FOR CHINA DAILY] The 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China convened in Beijing on Sunday. In the report Xi Jinping delivered at the opening session, he called for building cultural confidence and strength and securing new successes in developing socialist culture. Cultural confidence refers to a country's confidence in its history, civilization and values.
