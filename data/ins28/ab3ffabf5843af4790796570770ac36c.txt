TORONTO, May 27, 2022 /CNW/ - Equitable Bank (the "Bank"), is pleased to announce that it has completed an offering of EUR300 million of legislative covered bonds due May 27, 2025 (the "Covered Bonds"). The bonds were issued in accordance with the Canadian Registered Covered Bond Programs Guide, published by Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation ("CMHC").

Equitable Bank Logo (CNW Group/Equitable Bank)

The 3-year EUR300 million Covered Bonds were issued with an AA rating at a spread of 20 basis points over EUR mid swaps and are listed on the Irish Stock Exchange (Euronext Dublin).
