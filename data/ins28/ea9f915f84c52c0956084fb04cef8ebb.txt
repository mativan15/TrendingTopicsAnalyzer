EU not ready to agree on Russian oil ban, leaders say
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
world
2022-05-30 10:47:16

European Union leaders will fail to agree on a Russian oil ban at a summit on Monday and Tuesday in Brussels, leaders said as they arrived, adding that weeks of haggling over the matter were not over even if they were hopeful for a deal later.For sure, the leaders are set to agree in principle over a ban, a draft text showed, but they will leave all the details and hard decisions for later.'We're not there yet,' European Commission President Ursula von der Leyen said.Estonian Prime Minister Kaja Kallas said it was more realistic to expect an agreement next month.
