Feel-good clip shows golden retriever who loves handyman: "Favorite person"
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 16:04:03

A golden retriever has boosted the internet's serotonin levels as he gets excited every time he sees the maintenance man.In a series of videos, Gordy, who lives in Tampa, Florida, is walking down the corridor when he hears the man's keys jangle. He then runs down to be greeted by his "favorite person."His owner has added text to the story, and she points out that Gordy can barely "contain his excitement" as he can be seen jumping up the man until he patiently sits for a treat.The video, which can be seen here, was shared by TikTok user @goldengordy and has been watched by over 428,000 people.
