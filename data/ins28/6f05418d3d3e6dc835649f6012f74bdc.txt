Published: 11:09 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 11:09 EDT, 15 October 2022 Liverpool defender Virgil van Dijk has admitted that he has no idea why his side are underperforming this season.The Reds have seen a massive drop-off in form this campaign compared to the side that pushed on all fronts last season.Jurgen Klopp's side host Manchester City - who pipped them to the title last season by one point - on Sunday, and the Reds could be as far back as 17 points off the top of the table if results go against them this weekend.
