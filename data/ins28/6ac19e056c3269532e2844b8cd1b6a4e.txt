Nottingham Contemporary: Candlelit concerts to take place

Image source, FeverImage caption, The concerts will feature local musicians, Fever saidCandlelit classical music concerts are to take place at a city art gallery.The Nottingham Contemporary will host performances of Vivaldi's Four Seasons on 12 November and Mozart's Requiem on 17 November.Fever, the company behind the event, said they were aimed at helping people enjoy live performances played by local musicians.Niki Harman, events manager at the gallery, said they were delighted to have been chosen as a venue.
