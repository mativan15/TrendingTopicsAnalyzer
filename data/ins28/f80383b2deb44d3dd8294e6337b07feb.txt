OppFi (NYSE:OPFI) shareholders have endured a 70% loss from investing in the stock a year ago
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-18 17:18:59

As every investor would know, you don't hit a homerun every time you swing. But it should be a priority to avoid stomach churning catastrophes, wherever possible. So spare a thought for the long term shareholders of OppFi Inc. (NYSE:OPFI); the share price is down a whopping 70% in the last twelve months. That'd be enough to make even the strongest stomachs churn. Because OppFi hasn't been listed for many years, the market is still learning about how the business performs. The falls have accelerated recently, with the share price down 35% in the last three months.
