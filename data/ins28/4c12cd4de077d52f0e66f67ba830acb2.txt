October 18, 2022 10:18 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Zenoss Inc., the leader in AI-driven full-stack monitoring, today announced that it will be hosting its seventh annual event for customers, partners and industry experts at the sold-out Formula 1 Aramco United States Grand Prix Oct. 21-23 at Circuit of the Americas (COTA) in Austin, Texas.



'Formula 1 weekend has always been an opportunity for our team to recognize our customers and partners in a unique way,' said Greg Stock, chairman and CEO of Zenoss.
