Pic credit: Gamerwk Three indie video games were picked as the winners of the inaugural Indie Rise - Indonesia Edition 2022 last weekend. Indie Rise is a pitch competition that aims to discover, support and highlight indie development studios from emerging countries. A total of US$10,000 in cash prizes will be awarded to the winners in order to help them kick start their project and fund a prototype.The award ceremony was held in conjunction with the recently concluded Indonesian Game Developers Exchange (IGDX) 2022 in Bali, Indonesia.
