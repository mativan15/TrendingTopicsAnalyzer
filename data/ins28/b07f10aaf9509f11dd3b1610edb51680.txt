 An 83-year-old pilot crashed his plane in the MD of Opportunity in northern Alberta on Monday.
Athabasca RCMP received a call from the Joint Rescue Coordination Centre (JRCC) 'of a possible downed aircraft' just after 4:30 p.m., according to a news release issued Tuesday afternoon.The JRCC sent out a helicopter from the Canadian Forces Base Cold Lake to help guide RCMP, EMS and fire crews to the crash site.
