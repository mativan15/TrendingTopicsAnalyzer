Who is Greta Thunberg?
Greta Thunberg, 19, is the Swedish youngster who skipped school to protest and sparked an international climate change revolution in the process.
Greta rose to global notoriety in May 2018, when she began protesting outside Swedish parliament demanding that they take action to meet the carbon emissions target agreed by world leaders in 2015.
