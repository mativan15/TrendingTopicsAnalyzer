 Police have booked five members of a Ludhiana family for allegedly duping an industrialist and his kin of ₹4.33 crore, officials said on Sunday. The accused have been identified as Sudesh Kumar Gupta, his sons Harish Kumar Gupta and Kapil Gupta, Kapil's wife Poonam Gupta, and Harish's wife Anju Gupta — all residents of Bagh Nauhria Mal Jain, Bharat Nagar.
