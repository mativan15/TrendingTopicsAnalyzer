Trump hotels charged Secret Service exorbitant rates, inquiry finds
nzherald.co.nz
news
2022-10-17 21:09:40

Secret Service agents at the White House. Photo / Tom Brenner, The New York Times The Trump Organisation charged the Secret Service up to US$1,185 ($2,100) per night for hotel rooms used by agents protecting former President Donald Trump and his family, according to documents released Monday by the House Oversight Committee, forcing a federal agency to pay well above government rates. The committee released Secret Service records showing more than US$1.4 million ($2.5 million) in payments by the department to Trump properties since Trump took office in 2017.
