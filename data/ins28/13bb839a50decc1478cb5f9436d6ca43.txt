For anyone working as a contractor, it can often make sense to set up a limited company rather than continue as a sole trader. It means more hassle — including the need to file returns — but can also be more tax efficient.However, should you later decide to wind up that company, it is crucial that you first deal with all company assets — including cash sitting in bank accounts. Neglect to do so, and you will find yourself unable to access the money later without jumping through expensive hoops.N
