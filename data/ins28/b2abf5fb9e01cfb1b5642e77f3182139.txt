Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday virtually interacted with a beneficiary of the Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana hailing from Mehsana in Gujarat and appreciated him for choosing to be a 'job creator' instead of becoming a 'job seeker'.The beneficiary, Arvind Patel, told the Prime minister that he had availed a loan of ₹7.20 lakh to expand his business of setting up decorative pavilions for marriages and other events in and around Mehsana in north Gujarat.
