PlayStation Plus free games for November 2022 (Image: SONY)The next batch of PlayStation Plus games are about to be revealed by Sony. The November 2022 free PS4 and PS5 games will be announced at 4.30pm UK time on the afternoon of October 26. The games will be available to download less than a week later on the morning of November 1. The November line-up will replace the current selection, which is headlined by entertaining arcade racer Hot Wheels Unleashed.The current PS Plus games will be available to download until the morning of November 1, so there's still time to redeem them.
