We are pleased to recognize the winners and all the companies considered at the first in-person Universe of Pre-Filled Syringes and Injection Devices Conference since 2019. SILVER SPRING, Md. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 The Parenteral Drug Association (PDA) today announced the winners of the 2022 PDA Drug Delivery Innovation Awards during the opening plenary session of the 2022 PDA Universe of Pre-Filled Syringes and Injection Devices in Palm Springs, CA. Awardees were recognized for their technical innovations in advancing the field of bio/pharmaceutical manufacturing.
