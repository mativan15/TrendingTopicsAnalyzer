The Uttar Pradesh government has cancelled the leave of all doctors and paramedical staff, with dengue cases rising in the state.Deputy chief minister Brajesh Pathak on Wednesday said that dengue cases are less than last year. He said that increasing dengue cases are being controlled in Uttar Pradesh and all measures are being taken to avoid the spread.'Beds are reserved in hospitals and preparations for enough platelets are being made.
