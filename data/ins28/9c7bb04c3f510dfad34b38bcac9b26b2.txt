Southern States Bancshares, Inc

ANNISTON, Ala., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Southern States Bancshares, Inc. (NASDAQ: SSBK) ('Southern States'), the holding company for Southern States Bank, an Alabama state-chartered commercial bank (the 'Bank'), today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend on its common stock of $0.09 per share. The dividend is payable on November 16, 2022 to shareholders of record as of November 3, 2022.
