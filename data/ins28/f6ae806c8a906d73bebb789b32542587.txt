Man arrested in Groton for allegedly carrying pipe bombs near vape shop
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 13:56:00

A 32-year-old man was arrested for carrying pipe bombs in a backpack near a vape shop in Groton, according to the Groton Police Department.

Police responded to the area of 516 Gold Star Highway, a drive-through vape shop location, for a medical call around 5 p.m. Thursday. While on the scene, officers found suspicious items in the backpack of a man identified as Benjamin Dahm.

The backpack contained what appeared to be improvised explosive devices or metal pipe bombs, police said.

The police moved the backpack to a more secure location due to heavy pedestrian and motor vehicle traffic and the area around Gold Star Highway near Tollgate Rd.
