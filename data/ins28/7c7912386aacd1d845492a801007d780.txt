Thunder Bay introduced drive-through voting last week in a way to increase voter participation as advance polling for the 2022 municipal election gets underway ahead of election day on Oct. 24. (Sean Kilpatrick/The Canadian Press)Election day is coming up on Monday Oct. 24, but many municipalities have their online voting portals open now.The Association of Municipalities of Ontario says there will be 417 municipal elections held in the province on Oct. 24, and more than half — 217 — have decided to use online or phone voting.
