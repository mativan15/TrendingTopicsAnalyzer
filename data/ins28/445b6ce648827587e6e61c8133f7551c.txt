54% of teenagers feel unprepared to finance their futures, survey shows
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-01 18:40:36

A college graduate calls their family on video to celebrateTeenagers are looking at the costs of higher education and fear they won't be able to keep up.Some 54% of teens say they are worried about financing their futures, according to a survey from Junior Achievement USA and Citizens Bank of 1,000 children aged 13 to 18 between Feb. 18 and 24.What to do after high school is the biggest stressor around money, the survey found. Nearly 70% of the teens said that rising higher education costs have affected their post-graduation plans.
