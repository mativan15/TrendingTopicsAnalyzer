The Ukraine war poses a serious security threat to East European countries and the Baltic States but it is business as usual for western European nations like France, Portugal and Spain. New Delhi: External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar leaves for eastern and central Europe on Wednesday night for assessment of Ukraine war on ground and its fall-out in the region as European Union after much vacillation has partially banned Russian oil imports.
