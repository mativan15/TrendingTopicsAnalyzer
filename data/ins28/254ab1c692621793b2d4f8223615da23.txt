Last Thursday, the company said it has received Sebi's go-ahead to list its equity shares on domestic stock exchanges. Following NCLT approval, Piramal Pharma has been demerged from in a bid to simplify the corporate structure. According to the notice, the security shall be listed and admitted to dealings on the exchange on the list of T Group of Securities.
