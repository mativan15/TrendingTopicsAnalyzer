AgenciesRepresentative ImageIndia on Saturday logged saw 2,112 new Covid-19 cases taking the tally of infections to 4,46,40,748, while the number of active cases declined to 24,043, as per the Union Health Ministry data. With four fatalities, which includes three deaths reconciled by Kerala, the death toll climbed to 5,28,957. One death has been reported from West Bengal in a span of 24 hours. 
 The active cases comprise 0.05 per cent of the total infections, while the national Covid-19 recovery rate has increased to 98.
