Texas healthcare in-plant expands direct mail, reduces production costs for ADA-compliant signage, and optimizes mail and workflow with Ricoh services

EXTON, Pa., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ricoh USA, Inc. today announced CHRISTUS Health is optimizing and expanding its direct mail services to reach more customers and increase patient engagement with Ricoh. Together with Ricoh, CHRISTUS Health Print Services is already providing 26-31% cost savings to their interdepartmental customers compared to external vendors, equivalent to $685,000, and combined print and mail processing cost savings are more than 35%.
