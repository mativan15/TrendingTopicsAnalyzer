'Where there is a will, there is a way.' This motivational quote stands perfectly well for the young entrepreneurs, Satvir Singh and Nitin Tomar who turned their vision into a reality, setting up INR 24.5 Cr. turnover tea cafe brand, Chaiops. The café provides numerous varieties of teablended with exotic flavours and exquisite taste, providing tea drinkers a warm and blissful experience with each sip. Since establishing the first Chaiops café in Gurugram in 2017, Satvir Singh and Nitin Tomar have successfully expanded up to 50+ tea cafes pan India.
