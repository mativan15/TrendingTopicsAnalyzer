Nepal Army physically locates site of crashed aircraft
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 03:15:00

ReutersRepresentative ImageThe Nepal Army on Monday physically located the site where a Nepalese private airlines plane crashed on Sunday. "Search and rescue troops have physically located the plane crash site. Details will be followed," the Nepal Army Spokesperson said in a tweet.

 Tara Air's 9 NAET twin-engine aircraft with 22 people on board, including four Indians, was found crashed in Kowang village of the Mustang district hours after it went missing in the mountainous district on Sunday morning.
