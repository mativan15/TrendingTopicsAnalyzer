Image source, PA MediaTory Senedd members have written to the chancellor urging him to protect benefits from surging inflation.It was widely reported the prime minister favoured increasing some, including universal credit, according to average earnings, next April.Welsh Tory finance spokesman Peter Fox told the Welsh Parliament that his group had called on Mr Hunt to base the rise on prices.On Monday, Jeremy Hunt appeared to hint he would maintain the link to prices.Certain benefits, including disability benefits and carer's allowance, must increase in line with inflation by law.
