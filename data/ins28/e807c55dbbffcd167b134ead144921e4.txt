Greater Noida: Homebuyers not refunded, residential project sealed
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 03:15:00

GREATER NOIDA: Authorities have sealed a residential project of realty firm Rudra Buildwell in Greater Noida 's Sector 16C for its failure to refund Rs 25 crore to aggrieved homebuyers despite repeated notices.Over 100 construction workers and employees were moved out of the Aqua Casa residential project site by the district administration on Wednesday before it was sealed.Dadri 's sub-divisional magistrate Alok Kumar Gupta said that several homebuyers in the project had approached the UP Real Estate Regulatory Authority (UP-Rera) seeking refunds from the developer for the delay in possession of their flats.
