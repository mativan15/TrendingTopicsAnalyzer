The firm found MSPs see tremendous growth in managed security services with a hybrid MDR solution.

OTTAWA, ON, June 3, 2022 /CNW/ - Global advisory firm Aite-Novarica Group has published a white paper exploring how MSPs are differentiating their managed security services with the right cyber security solutions and partners. The report, Hands-Free Cybersecurity for SMEs: A Roadmap to MSP Growth, found that hybrid MDR—a solution that protects the entire IT environment—is the best go-to-market strategy for small and mid-sized MSPs building a managed security service.
