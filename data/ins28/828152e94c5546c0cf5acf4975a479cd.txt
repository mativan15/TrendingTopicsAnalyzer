June 03, 2022 06:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BitNile Holdings, Inc. (NYSE American: NILE), a diversified holding company ('BitNile' or the 'Company'), announced today that its Executive Chairman, Mr. Milton 'Todd' Ault, III, will be presenting at the 12th annual LD Micro Invitational that will be held next week from Tuesday, June 7 through Thursday, June 9, 2022. This investor conference is being held at the new Four Seasons Hotel and Resort in Westlake Village in Southern California and features 185 companies presenting during the three-day event.
