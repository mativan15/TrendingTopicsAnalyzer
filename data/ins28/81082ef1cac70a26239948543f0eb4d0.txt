Khaosai Wongnatthakan/iStock via Getty Images Medallion Bank, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Medallion Financial (NASDAQ:MFIN), has partnered with Covered Holdings, a fintech firm specializing in "Buy Now, Pay Later" financing services. Shares of MFIN are climbing nearly 5% in Thursday premarket trading. The partnership will allow Medallion Bank to provide loans through Covered Care, a BNPL financing solution for healthcare practices enabling high approval rates, affordable terms, and flexible pricing and integration options.
