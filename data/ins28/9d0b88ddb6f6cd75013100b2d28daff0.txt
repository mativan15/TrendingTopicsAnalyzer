WCLA II exceeds standards

Oct. 21—LIMA — The West Central Learning Academy II is a public community school sponsored by the Ohio Department of Education. Located at 522 West North Street in Lima, students in grade 9-12 use a blended format for their education.

Students spend time in direct instruction in addition to time spent online.

Students' residencies range from Findlay to Wapakoneta. A total of 76 students are enrolled.

The latest Ohio school report card revealed that WCLA II exceeded standards in progress, gap closing and graduation rate.
