NEW YORK (PRWEB) May 25, 2022 NFT-VIP announced today that the four day event will kick off on Sunday June 19th in Margaritaville Resort Times Square to celebrate Culture and Heritage. It will be presented by Coachera powered by Dr Sarah Haynes & Coach Naja Hall - Blended and Black & VIP Step Mom. A panel discussion detailing NFTs for impact and the importance of expanding the Web3 niche to underserved communities will follow and will feature artist Nicole Buffett, Jesse Dylan (Founder and CEO of Wondros), Ben Pousty (Chief Operating Officer, The Giving Block) and Sara Porter (Director of Business Development and Strategic Partnerships with Hope for Haiti).
