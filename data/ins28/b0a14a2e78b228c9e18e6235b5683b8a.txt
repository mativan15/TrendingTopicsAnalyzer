The board of Turning Point Brands, Inc. (NYSE:TPB) has announced that it will pay a dividend of US$0.06 per share on the 8th of July. Including this payment, the dividend yield on the stock will be 0.8%, which is a modest boost for shareholders' returns.

Turning Point Brands' Earnings Easily Cover the Distributions

Even a low dividend yield can be attractive if it is sustained for years on end. However, prior to this announcement, Turning Point Brands' dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings.
