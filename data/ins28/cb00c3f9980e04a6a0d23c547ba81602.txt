OWINGS MILLS, Md. — It's no secret that the Ravens have to play better in the fourth quarter.

These late-game collapses are a big reason the team is 3-3.

The goal this week heading into the crucial AFC North matchup with the Cleveland Browns is finishing the game strongly.

"My message to the guys is, ‘Hey, we're in the fight, guys – we're in it," Ravens defensive coordinator Mike Macdonald said. "Let's just keep battling, keep grinding it out and take it one play at a time.
