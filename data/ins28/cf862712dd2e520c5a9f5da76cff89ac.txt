Goa town planning minister promises a list of violators on June 6
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-05 08:15:00

PANAJI: The town and country planning department's scrutiny committee has prepared a 240-page preliminary report identifying all the violations in ODPs and who violated the norms. TCP minister Vishwajit Rane said on Saturday. I am releasing a list of leaders on Monday who have converted tenanted land into commercial and residential zones, he said.Rane also said that the TCP board will take a decision at its next board meeting to revert all illegally converted eco-sensitive land to its earlier status.
