Experts laud Party report's emphasis on industrial and supply chains

By MA SI | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-18 09:17 An employee works on the production line of solar panels at a company in Lianyungang, Jiangsu province. [SI WEI/FOR CHINA DAILY] Economic observers on Monday lauded a report, delivered on Sunday at the opening of the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, for its emphasis on boosting the security and resilience of industrial and supply chains. The report's emphasis, they said, will help accelerate the nation's high-quality economic development, which will in turn contribute to global economic recovery.
