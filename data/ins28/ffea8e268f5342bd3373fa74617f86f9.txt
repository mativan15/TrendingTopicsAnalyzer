Summer Vacationers Want to Kick It Old School
wsj.com
news
2022-05-29 13:00:00

Photo: Booking.com The new and the high tech are suddenly out of fashion, with the Nasdaq down around 23% this year. That sentiment change seems to have bled into the online travel sector, where the uncool is making a comeback. There is no denying that longer-term trips in the most remote geographies are still seeing a lot of traction, but as even Airbnb pointed out in its first-quarter earnings report earlier this month, bookings to urban destinations are back to growth relative to prepandemic levels, and short-term stays are swiftly regaining favor.
