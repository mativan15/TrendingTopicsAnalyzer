Nutritional beverage maker Glucose Health files for $30M IPO, uplisting to NYSE American
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-01 20:59:23

ismagilov/iStock via Getty Images Nutritional beverage maker Glucose Health (OTCPK:GLUC) has filed to raise up to $30M through an initial public offering and uplist its shares to NYSE American. The company said in a filing that it plans to offer units consisting of shares and warrants, but didn't disclose the number or pricing. A filing fee schedule indicated the company may seek to raise up to $30M. EF Hutton is serving as lead bookrunner. Glucose Health hopes to list its shares on NYSE American under the symbol GLUC.
