Spirit stock falls after Glass Lewis urges shareholders to approve Frontier merger deal
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 12:07:50

Shares of Spirit Airlines Inc. SAVE, +3.99% fell 1.2% in premarket trading Friday, after the air carrier said independent proxy advisor Glass, Lewis & Co. recommended that Spirit shareholders vote for the Frontier Group Holdings Inc. ULCC, +0.59% buyout deal. Frontier's stock rose 0.7% and JetBlue Airways Corp. shares JBLU, +2.83% eased 0.1%. "Glass Lewis shares our Board's view that the Frontier transaction has an easier path to close and provides third party validation that JetBlue may have ulterior motives behind its offer," said Spirit Chief Executive Ted Christie.
