October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BURLINGTON, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--HealthEdge, a next-generation healthcare SaaS company that provides a digital foundation for healthcare payers, today announced the expansion of Dr. Sandhya Gardner's role from Chief Medical Officer, Wellframe to Chief Medical Officer, HealthEdge Clinical Solutions.



'Sandhya will enable us to reimagine and expand our clinical focus through our two market-leading solutions, GuidingCare and Wellframe,' said Steve Krupa, CEO of HealthEdge.
