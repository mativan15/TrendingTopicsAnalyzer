October 17, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Stonehill's commercial real estate group, Stonehill CRE, has deployed approximately $200 million of capital through the origination and purchase of first mortgage loans since being launched in the second quarter of 2022. The company expects its commercial real estate group to deploy an additional $100 million in loan originations and other transactions by year's end based upon deals in its pipeline. Stonehill CRE is targeting over $500 million in transaction for 2023.
