LINCOLNSHIRE, Ill., May 31, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Alight (NYSE: ALIT) (the "Company") today announced that Chief Financial Officer Katie Rooney will present at the Baird 2022 Global Consumer, Technology & Services Conference on Monday, June 6, 2022 at 3:45 PM ET. Additionally, the Company will participate in one-on-one meetings with investors at the conference.

A live webcast of the event will be made available in the Investor Relations section of the Company's website at alight.com. A replay of the webcast will be available until September 4, 2022.
