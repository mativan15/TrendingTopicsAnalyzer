"We designed Prosh IQ to help startups affordably access a platform they can trust, to create their own marketing plan with no marketing background, based on templates and processes I've built from years of marketing planning with startups,' said Roshni Wijayasinha, CEO of Prosh Marketing. TORONTO, Ontario (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 Prosh Marketing is proud to announce the launch of its DIY marketing plan course,Prosh IQ. The course is intended to help startups and entrepreneurs create their marketing plans through self-guided learning modules created by a seasoned CMO, with 1-1 coaching sessions available for purchase as needed.
