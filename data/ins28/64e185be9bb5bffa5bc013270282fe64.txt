Texas Man Indicted for Hate Crime Shooting
justice.gov
news
2022-10-19 18:43:11

A federal grand jury returned an indictment yesterday charging Anthony Paz Torres, 37, with federal hate crimes, resulting in the death of one person and including an attempt to kill four others during a shooting at Omar's Wheels and Tires. Torres is also charged with using a firearm to commit the murder. The indictment alleges that Torres went to Omar's Wheels and Tires on December 24, 2015, and shot at employees and customers at the business. Torres had gone to the business a few days earlier, but was told by law enforcement officers to not return.
