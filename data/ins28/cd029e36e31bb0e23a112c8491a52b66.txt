Discussions around entrepreneurship in Europe often revolve around the creation of unicorns, which are businesses valued at $1 billion or more. There has been a longstanding concern across the continent that the lack of tech companies to rival those emerging from Silicon Valley is undermining the vibrancy of the European economy. These concerns persist despite a report from i5invest that Europe actually created twice as many unicorns as the United States in 2021.
