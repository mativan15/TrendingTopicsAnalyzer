Capcom revealed the first footage of the long-rumored Resident Evil 4 at the June State of Play. It's launching the game on March 23, 2023. The trailer showed off a few of the familiar elements from the original, including Leon's iconic outfit, Ashley Graham, and the castle enemies. It also confirmed the game is coming for PS5, though this doesn't necessarily rule out that it's coming to other consoles.
