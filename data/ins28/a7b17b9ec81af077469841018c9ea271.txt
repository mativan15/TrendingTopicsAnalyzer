(VIEW)By: Preeti Singh, SA News Editor Diego Thomazini/iStock via Getty Images View (NASDAQ:VIEW) shares have surged after the firm reported its full-year 2021 results Tuesday (May 31) and said that it expects to file its delinquent 10-K and 10-Q on or before June 30. Shares are currently up 15% pre-market but had slid earlier in the month on late Q1 filing announcement. It had also noted at the time that expected to disclose substantial doubt about its ability to continue as a going concern.
