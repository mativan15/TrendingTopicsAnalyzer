St. Louis-based events software firm fills newly created chief marketing post
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 19:34:00

Ungerboeck Systems International LLC, an O'Fallon, Missouri-based provider of event and venue management software, has hired a tech marketing veteran to fill a newly created marketing role.

The company said this week that Laurie McGrath has joined Ungerboeck as its chief marketing officer. McGrath, who has more than 25 years of marketing, branding and communication experience, most recently was CMO of Montreal, Canada-based Tecsys, a provider of cloud-based supply chain software and systems that she joined in 2018.
