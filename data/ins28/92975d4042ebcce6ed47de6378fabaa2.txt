CCI fines Google ₹1.3k-cr over unfair business practices
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 18:13:02

New Delhi: The Competition Commission of India (CCI) on Thursday imposed a penalty of ₹1,337.76 crore on technology major Google for abusing its dominant position as the developer of the backbone operating system (OS) software that a wide variety of mobile phone makers deploy on their products.The fine covers what CCI said were anti-competitive practices by Google in the markets for mobile operating system (OS), app stores, web search services, web browsers, and online video hosting services. This includes Android OS, Google Play Store, Google Search, Chrome browser, and YouTube.
