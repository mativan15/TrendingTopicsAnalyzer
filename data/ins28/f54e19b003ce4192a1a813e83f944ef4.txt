Six Month Net Sales of $232.6 millionSix Month Net Income of $0.5 million BOCA RATON, Fla., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Q.E.P. CO., INC. (OTCQX: QEPC) (the 'Company' or 'QEP') today reported its consolidated results of operations for the first six months and second quarter of its fiscal year 2023, which ended on August 31, 2022. QEP reported net sales of $232.6 million for the six months ended August 31, 2022, an increase of $5.3 million or 2.3% from the $227.3 million reported in the same period of fiscal 2022.
