(2nd LD) Kakao on track to fully restore services after fire(ATTN: UPDATES with details throughout; ADDS 1st photo) SEOUL, Oct. 16 (Yonhap) -- Kakao Corp., the operator of South Korea's dominant messaging app KakaoTalk, has restored 40 percent of its data center's servers, an executive said Sunday, as it is working to fully repair its services disrupted by a fire. The tech giant has restored 12,000 servers out of 32,000 at its data center in the SK C&C building located in Pangyo, just south of Seoul, said Yang Hyun-seo, Kakao's vice president handling the company's relationship with the government.
