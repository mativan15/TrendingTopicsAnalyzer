Hubbell Increases Dividend 7%, Begins $300M Stock Buyback
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 17:48:00

By Chris Wack Hubbell Inc. said Friday that its board of directors has increased its stock dividend rate by 7%. The new annual payment is $4.48 a share, or $1.12 a quarter, compared to the former rate of $4.20 a share, or $1.05 a quarter. The dividend will be paid Dec. 15 to shareholders of record as of Nov. 30. Hubbell's board also approved a new share-repurchase authorization of up to $300 million. The new program is set to expire in October 2025. Write to Chris Wack at chris.wack@wsj.com
