Netflix Launching New Gaming Studio Led by Former Overwatch Producer
ign.com
gaming
2022-10-19 08:57:18

Netflix is continuing its expansion into gaming by opening a new development studio in Southern California.According to TechCrunch, Netflix's VP of gaming, Mike Verdu, said this new studio will include former Blizzard vice president and Overwatch boss Chako Sonny at the helm.'He could have done anything, but he chose to come here,' said Verdu. 'You don't get people like that coming to your organization to build the next big thing in gaming unless there's a sense that we're really in it for the long haul and in it for the right reasons.
