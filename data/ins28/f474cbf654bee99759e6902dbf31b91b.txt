June 01, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hewlett Packard Enterprise (NYSE: HPE) today announced financial results for the second quarter, ended April 30, 2022.



'Persistent demand led to another quarter of significant order growth and higher revenue for HPE, underscoring the accelerating interest customers have in our unique edge-to-cloud portfolio and our HPE GreenLake platform,' said Antonio Neri, president, and CEO of Hewlett Packard Enterprise. 'I am optimistic that demand will continue to be strong, given our customers' needs to accelerate their business resilience and competitiveness.
