Instacart pulls plans to go public this year
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 03:30:00

ETtechInstacart filed papers this year for a so-called confidential filing, which meant it did not yet have to disclose certain data about its business (Illustration: Rahul Awasthi)Instacart, a food-delivery company, is pulling its plans to go public this year, in the latest sign of turmoil in the public markets, three people with knowledge of the situation said. The company had been one of the few tech companies seeking to go public this year, as investors all but shut their doors to putting their cash in initial public offerings of unprofitable entities.
