Budget 2023: Indian Finance Ministry Invites Suggestions For Tax Proposals

Nearly a week after kickstarting the Union Budget 2023's preparations on October 10th, the Finance Ministry has now sought suggestions from trade and industry on tax proposals for the Budget of Fiscal Year 2023–24. The budget for the next financial year is likely to be presented on February 1st, 2023.The month-long budget related meetings amidst the Finance Ministry are expected to end next month on November 10th. shutterstockSuggestions For Tax ProposalsSuggestions can be given for Direct Taxes (Personal Income Tax and Corporate Income Tax), Central Excise and Customs Duty.
