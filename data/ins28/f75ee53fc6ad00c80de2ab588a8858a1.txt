The federal government re-introduced legislation on Thursday to create a monthly benefit for working-age Canadians with disabilities. (CBC)Islanders with disabilities say a new monthly benefit from the federal government could go a long way to help them cover the cost of living.The legislation to create the benefit was re-introduced on Thursday after being delayed when the 2021 federal election was called. It will target working-age Canadians with disabilities, though the bill itself does not set the monetary value of the payments or the qualification criteria.
