Prime Minister Narendra Modi said that world leaders are amazed by the success of India in ensuring social security through digitisation. 'World Bank says that India has become a leader in ensuring social security through digitisation. Even the most successful people in the field of technology, the experts of the tech world are appreciating this system in India. They too are amazed by its success,' said the PM.
