Credit Suisse's struggles ‘underline banking woes'
businesslive.co.za
news
2022-10-18 08:47:47

Credit Suisse's struggles with its business model underscore the broader issue facing banks across Europe, a top executive at major shareholder BlackRock says.'If you want to attract investors, you need to have the right business model. And this review in my mind is all about clarity,' Philipp Hildebrand, vice-chair at the world's largest asset manager, told Bloomberg Television on Tuesday. 'This is what has been in many ways haunting the European banking sector for quite some time.' There are notable exceptions, the former Swiss central banker said, adding French lender BNP Paribas is one that stands out.
