October 20, 2022 01:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time RICHMOND, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bowlero Corp. (NYSE: BOWL), the world's leader in bowling entertainment, announced today that Thomas Shannon, Founder & Chief Executive Officer of Bowlero Corp., will be interviewed by Jim Cramer on tonight's edition of Mad Money with Jim Cramer on CNBC.

The interview is scheduled to air tonight during the 6:00 PM ET showing of Mad Money. To view the interview, please visit CNBC's website at www.cnbc.com/live-tv/ or visit the CNBC channel anywhere you get live TV.
