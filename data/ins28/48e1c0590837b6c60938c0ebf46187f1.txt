Published: 01:39 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 01:39 EDT, 18 October 2022 The fast start that the Ottawa Senators were hoping for has eluded them through two games, but Tuesday night's first opportunity of the season to skate on home ice could provide the energy boost they need. Coming off a two-day break, the Senators will take on the undefeated Boston Bruins, who will serve as their opponents after starting a back-to-back by beating Florida 5-3 on Monday. Ottawa was 1:55 away from forcing overtime in Saturday's 3-2 loss to Toronto, but surrendered a late goal to take its second straight setback after opening the season with a loss to the Sabres.
