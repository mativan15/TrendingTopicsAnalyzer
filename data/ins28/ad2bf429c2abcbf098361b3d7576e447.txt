Great expectations can be a killer. The sequel to Rian Johnson's bold and widely adored whodunnit, Knives Out, has Daniel Craig's Benoit Blanc solving a different crime, in a different location, amongst a different set of A-list faces. But now that audiences are expecting the unexpected, is Glass Onion doomed to disappoint? The rumour that's been doing the rounds, ever since the Toronto premiere, is that it's not as good as the first one. Which is cobblers. Glass Onion is fantastic: complex and intelligent, outrageously funny and a visual feast.
