Crushed by Crypto Losses? Here Are Some Tax Tips
wealthmanagement.com
news
2022-05-31 15:23:55

(Bloomberg Opinion) -- Investors in Bitcoin and other digital assets have been pummeled recently by the longest losing streak since 2011. If that's you, you might be glad to hear that there are ways to ease some of the sting of those losses: Act promptly and you can cut your tax bill for next April and beyond. The Internal Revenue Service allows taxpayers to use losses in stocks and other investments, including crypto, to offset gains. If your losses exceed your total gains for the year, you can deduct up to $3,000 against your taxable income.
