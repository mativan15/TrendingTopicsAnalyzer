Troubled UK firm Missguided causing problems for Irish consumers
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-02 23:01:00

Irish consumers are facing delays in deliveries and refunds from Missguided after the UK retailer went into administration on Monday.The online fashion company, which sells clothes aimed at women aged 16 to 35, was purchased on Wednesday by billionaire Mike Ashley's Frasers Group — which also owns Sports Direct — in a £20 million deal. Missguided's suppliers had accused the company of millions of pounds worth of outstanding payments, leading to its collapse.Teneo, the advisory firm, was appointed as administrator for Missguided on Monday and will oversee its operations for the next eight weeks.
