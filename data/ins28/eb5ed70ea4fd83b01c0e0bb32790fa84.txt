October 18, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MONTREAL--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Valnet Inc. (or 'Valnet') has successfully completed the acquisition of SN&CK Media Ltd. (or 'Snack Media') a leading digital sports publishing network based out of the UK. With this acquisition, Valnet establishes a firm foothold in the Sports Category of owned and operated websites with flagship brands such as GiveMeSport.com, FootballFanCast.com and FootballLeagueWorld.com. In addition to the publishing operation, Snack Media operates a specialized Ad Network that serves over 400 publishing properties and has one of the largest sports Facebook pages with 25M+ followers.
