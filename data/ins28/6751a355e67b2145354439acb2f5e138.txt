When will Dhanteras be celebrated?

Dhanteras will be celebrated on October 23 in India. What does Dhanteras signify?

Dhanteras is the festival of wealth and prosperity. It is a very auspicious festival and is celebrated by everyone. What is buying gold considered to be lucky on Dhanteras?

Buying gold is considered to be very lucky on Dhanteras as it signifies wealth and prosperity. When you buy gold, silver or diamond on Dhanteras, good vibes and luck will enter your home. What is Dhanteras also known as?

Dhanteras is also known as Dhanatrayodashi.
