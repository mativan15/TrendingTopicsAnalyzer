M&M looks for global play with revised EV business plan
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 16:30:00

With a realigned strategy now under a different leadership team, a new test awaits M&M in a landscape tipped to be the future of the automobile industry.SUV maker Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) harbours ambitious plans of becoming a global player in the electric vehicle (EV) space, even though it has lost a few opportunities. A fresh start in four-wheeler EVs, detailed plans for which is expected to be announced in the UK on August 15, 2022, is being fuelled by plans to play in the global EV market. A global play is also being planned for its ICE vehicle business.
