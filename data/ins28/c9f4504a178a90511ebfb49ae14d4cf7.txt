Rick Whitmore, from Rochdale, said he was 'disgusted' with Oldham Council's decision to partially shut Tommyfield Market in the Greater Manchester area to mark the Queen's Platinum Jubilee. The market in Oldham will close its doors to the public on Thursday and Friday, before reopening on Saturday.But Mr Whitmore, 52, who owns a butcher shop within the complex, said the council did not consult with the business owners like himself and his partner, Nicola.He told The Oldham Times: 'The council didn't ask us anything, all they've done is they've just gone and taken it upon themselves and shut the whole market for two days.
