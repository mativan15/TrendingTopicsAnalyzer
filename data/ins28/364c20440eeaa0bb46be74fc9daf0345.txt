Variety - Film News Former reality star Holly Madison won't be called to testify during Harvey Weinstein's Los Angeles rape trial, a judge ruled on Tuesday.The defense wanted Madison to take the stand in order to undermine testimony from actress Ashley Matthau, one of the uncharged supporting witnesses. Matthau, who's accusing Weinstein of sexual battery, claims the former mogul masturbated on her at his hotel in 2003 in Puerto Rico, where they were shooting Miramax's 'Dirty Dancing' sequel, 'Havana Nights.
