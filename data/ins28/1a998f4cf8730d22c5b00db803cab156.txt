"We are excited about our acceptance into the 8(a) program as it opens up new avenues of collaboration,' says Kollabio's CEO, Ashok Nare. "We look forward to building new partnerships that bring expanded digital transformation and human centered design solutions to our federal government clients." LEESBURG, Va. (PRWEB) May 31, 2022 Kollabio Inc. is proud to announce its acceptance into the United States Small Business Administration's (SBA) 8(a) Business Development Program. Kollabio is a boutique digital transformation and technology consulting firm that combines expertise in Experience Design, Agile delivery, and Cloud/DevSecOps to create human-centered digital experiences for both public and private sector organizations.
