Who is Eduardo Valseca?
Eduardo Garcia Valseca is known as a Mexican businessman who was kidnapped in June 2007 by a gang of heavily armed men after dropping his kids off at school. 
He was ultimately held captive for more than seven months and was not released until an undisclosed amount of money had been paid.
