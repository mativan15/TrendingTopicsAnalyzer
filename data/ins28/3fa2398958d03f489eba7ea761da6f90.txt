Jeremy and Jazzy Holiday Song "I Want To Wish" to be Released November 1

TORONTO, Oct. 21, 2022 /CNW/ - Multi-platform children's brand Jeremy and Jazzy is thrilled to release the first Jeremy and Jazzy Book - Turtle and Guy: A Jeremy and Jazzy Adventure on November 15, 2022 via Mango Publishing . The book is a collaboration among Vérité Films , Jeremy Fisher Music , and Hidden Pony Records , the team behind the Jeremy and Jazzy children's animated television series, music, and multi-platform brand.
