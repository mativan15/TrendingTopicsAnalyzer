In a new TV spot featuring her dog copping a squat, Marie Newman dismisses Sean Casten's attacks as "sh*t" in IL-06 Democratic Primary. (Newman Campaign/YouTube)IL-06 — The sh*t is flying – both literally and figuratively — in the heated Democratic primary where two incumbent congresspeople — Sean Casten and Marie Newman – are battling it out to retain their seat in the remapped 6th Congressional District.Newman's cable TV spot — titled "Sh*t" — debuted Wednesday on cable TV. It slams attacks from Casten, whom she claims is using money from the SuperPac Democratic Majority for Israel for a major ad buy "to erase a lifelong Democrat and progressive woman from Congress.
