National Herald case: Enforcement Directorate issues fresh summons to Rahul Gandhi
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 06:15:00

NEW DELHI: The Enforcement Directorate ( ED ) on Friday issued fresh summons to Congress leader Rahul Gandhi to appear before investigators on June 13 in National Herald case The probe agency had earlier asked the Congress leader to appear for questioning on Thursday (June 2). However, he had written to ED to postpone the date to after June 5 as he is currently abroad.The Enforcement Directorate had on Wednesday summoned Congress president Sonia Gandhi and her MP son for questioning in a money laundering case linked to the National Herald newspaper.
