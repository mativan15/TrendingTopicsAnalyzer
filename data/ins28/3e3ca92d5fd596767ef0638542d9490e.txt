October 20, 2022 03:02 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Taulia, a leading working capital management solutions provider, and Henkel, the German adhesives and consumer goods giant, have today announced the successful integration of sustainability criteria into the largest segment of Henkel's supply chain finance program.

The Supply Chain Finance program, which the two companies have been running since 2015, will now provide flexible financing options to Henkel's suppliers with preferential rates depending on suppliers' ESG ratings.
