Published: 07:53 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 09:18 EDT, 3 June 2022 By Yoruk Bahceli June 3 (Reuters) - Investors ramped up their bets on Friday on ECB interest rate rises this year, and priced in a bigger, 50 basis-point hike at one of the bank's policy meetings by October. May's record 8.1% euro zone inflation print and broadening price pressures have added to the case for big European Central Bank rate hikes, with several policymakers saying they are open to a 50 basis-point (bps) move instead of the 25 bps increments that are widely expected.
