Realme Pad X Tablet launched; check out price and more
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 17:15:00

Realme launched Pad X in China and has confirmed that it will be launching its first premium tablet in India soon. However, the date of launch is not set yet. Currently, it is seen in three variants- Star Grey, Sea Salt Blue and Bright Chessboard Green. It is rumoured to be capable of taking on the recently launched Pad 5 by Xiaomi. However, we are yet to see how much of it stands true. Realme Pad X tablet price is set at CNY 1,299 in China, approximately Rs. 15,000 in India. However, there might be some additional costs as well that will be added to the price, like the cost of importing it to India.
