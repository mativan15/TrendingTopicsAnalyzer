The Silver Lake voting quirk: 'It might be the great unanswered rugby mystery of all time'
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-06-02 03:37:28

As NZ Rugby chairman Stewart Mitchell made the pronouncement that his organisation had finally agreed to essentially sell off a chunk of its business to Silver Lake, with '89 votes for, one vote against', so was launched one of the mysteries of the game that may never have an answer. Because the votes were cast via a secret ballot, overseen by the great Sir Bryan Williams and former NZR chair Rob Fisher, the identity of the no vote will remain undisclosed, unless the union concerned chooses to out themselves.
