18 October 2022 by EJ Panaligan 
Variety - Film News As part of a new initiative, the United States Mint will honor Anna May Wong, star of movies such as 'Shanghai Express,' by making her the first Asian American featured on U.S. currency, placing her likeness on quarters with production starting Oct. 18.The printed quarter shows an image of Wong resting on her hand, serving as a tribute to what most consider the first Chinese American movie star. She was born in 1905 in Chinatown, Los Angeles and died in 1961 of a heart attack in her Santa Monica home.
