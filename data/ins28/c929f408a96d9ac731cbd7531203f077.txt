As China's Xi Jinping tightens grip on power, a faceless protest grows
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 14:40:31

President Xi Jinping cut a universally admired figure as he opened a major political event to thunderous applause on Sunday. But the widely expected extension of his rule over China belies a faceless protest emerging from Beijing.On October 13, three days before the Chinese Communist Party's twice-a-decade national congress, images of a single man demonstrating against Xi's right to rule began circulating on Twitter. The lone protester—still unidentified—hung banners from an overpass in Beijing's Haidian district to denounce China's leader.
