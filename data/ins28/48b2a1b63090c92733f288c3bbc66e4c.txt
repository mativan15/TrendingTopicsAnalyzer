Supermarket Tesco says millions of pounds of Clubcard vouchers run out today, but these can be extended for another two years if consumers follow expert adviceMillions of pounds of Clubcard vouchers run out after todayTesco shoppers have just hours to go before £17million worth of Clubcard vouchers expire and become useless. The vouchers were issued in May 2020 and must be used by the end of today, May 31. However, there is a way to extend the life of these vouchers. The supermarket previously issued a warning to customers to use their vouchers before they're no longer valid.
