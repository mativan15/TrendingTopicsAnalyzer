October 18, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Vista Equity Partners ('Vista'), a leading global investment firm focused exclusively on enterprise software, data and technology-enabled businesses, today announced it has exited its investment in Ping Identity, the intelligent identity solution for the enterprise and a Vista portfolio company since 2016, following the completion of the previously announced acquisition of the company for $2.8 billion total enterprise value.
