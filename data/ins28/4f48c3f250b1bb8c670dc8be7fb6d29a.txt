Just in time for Stranger Things, season 4, an article published by Bloomberg Wednesday morning announced the arrival of ... "zombie firms" in America. And, to the consternation of investors in cruise line stocks, the reporter named Carnival (CCL -2.95%) as one of them.
Carnival stock closed the session down by 3%. Rival operators Royal Caribbean (RCL -4.30%) and Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings (NCLH -4.50%), which are arguably in the same boat as Carnival, were down by 4.3% and 4.5%, respectively.
