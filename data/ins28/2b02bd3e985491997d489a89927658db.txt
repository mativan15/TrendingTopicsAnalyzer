TN panel indicts Sasikala over Jayalalithaa's death, govt says action after getting legal opinion
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 15:00:00

Sasikala is indicted considering several aspects, the panel said.A panel that probed the circumstances leading to former Chief Minister J Jayalalithaa's death in 2016 indicted V K Sasikala, the confidante of the late leader, and the Tamil Nadu government on Tuesday said it would initiate action after getting legal opinion. The panel's report, that was tabled by the state government in the Assembly on Tuesday also indicted former Health Minister C Vijayabaskar, medical doctor K S Sivakumar (Sasikala's relative) and the then Health Secretary J Radhakrishnan and recommended a probe against all the four persons.
