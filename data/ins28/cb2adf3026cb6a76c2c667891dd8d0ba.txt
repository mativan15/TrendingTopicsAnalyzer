Huntingdon Valley Bank parent agrees to $67M sale to Pennsylvania bank
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 14:37:00

Fast-growing HV Bancorp, parent to Huntingdon Valley Bank, has agreed to be sold for $67.4 million to Citizens Financial Services, a larger community bank headquartered in Mansfield, Tioga County.

Under the terms of the agreement, HV shareholders can elect to receive for each share of the company's common stock either $30.50 in cash or 0.4 shares of Citizens Financial Services common stock. Elections will be subject to proration procedures whereby 80% of the shares of HV Bancorp (NASDAQ: HVBC) common stock will be exchanged for Citizens Financial Services (NASDAQ: CZFS) common stock and 20% of the shares of HV common stock will be exchanged for cash.
