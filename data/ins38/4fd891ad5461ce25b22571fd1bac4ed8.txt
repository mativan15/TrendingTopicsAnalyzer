Published: 08:49 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 08:49 EDT, 27 May 2022 A couple who splashed out £700 for a hot tub but claimed to be struggling financially have been slammed as 'rich idiots' by viewers of Secret Spenders.The programme, which aired last night, claims to help cash-strapped British families cut back on their spending, but was slammed for choosing couples who were spending hundreds a month on unnecessary luxuries. Dan and Cheri, from Chesterfield, appeared on the show as they described struggling to save a deposit for their home but overspending on luxury products, including a outdoor jacuzzi bought during Covid.
