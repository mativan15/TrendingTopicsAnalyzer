Some Victorians are still bracing themselves for worsening conditions while others are in clean-up mode more than a week after widespread heavy rainfall triggered damaging floods.Both the state and federal governments have made emergency payments available to those affected by the floods.As of Thursday afternoon, details released by Emergency Services Minister Jaclyn Symes showed the scale of those needing a helping hand.She said 18,000 applications for support had already been submitted to the Victorian government, with a third of those processed.
