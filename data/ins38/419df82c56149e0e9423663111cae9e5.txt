May 27, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MINNEAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--U.S. Bancorp (NYSE: USB) announced today that Chairman, President and CEO Andy Cecere and Vice Chair and Chief Financial Officer Terry Dolan will present at the Bernstein Annual Strategic Decisions Conference in New York.

The presentation will begin at 10 a.m. ET on Friday, June 3.

A live video webcast will be available on the day of the conference, at the 'Webcasts and Presentations' section of the U.S. Bank Investor Relations website.
