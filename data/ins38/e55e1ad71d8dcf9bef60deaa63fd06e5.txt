October 19, 2022 04:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time EL SEGUNDO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Beachbody Company, Inc. (NYSE: BODY) ('Beachbody' or the 'Company'), a leading subscription health and wellness company, will release its third quarter fiscal 2022 results on Wednesday, November 9, 2022, after the U.S. stock market closes. The Company will host a conference call at 5:00 p.m. (Eastern Time) that day to discuss the results.

The toll-free dial-in for the conference call is (844) 200-6205 (U.S. & Canada), or (646) 904-5544 (all other locations).
