IANSShashi Tharoor and Mallikarjun KhargeVoting to elect the next Congress president drew to a close on Monday evening, with 9,000 delegates splitting their votes between Congress leaders Mallikarjun Kharge and Shashi Tharoor. Former Prime Minister Manmohan Singh and veteran leader P Chidambaram also cast their votes in the race along with many other party bigwigs. 
"I have been waiting for a long time for this day," said Sonia Gandhi, who was present at the AICC headquarters along with Congress General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi.
