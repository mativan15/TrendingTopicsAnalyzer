Asos plunged to a loss of nearly £10 million for the year as the online fashion retailer struggled with a tough consumer climate and troubles with its own finances.The fast fashion firm has been in talks with lenders over a £350 million loan deal as it strives for more flexibility in uncertain financial times.Today it said sales for the year to August had held up well, up slightly at £3.93 billion,, suggesting the business retains its core popularity among young internet shoppers.It made profit before tax of £22 million, about what the City was expecting.
