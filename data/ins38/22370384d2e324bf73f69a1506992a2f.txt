Published: 07:22 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 07:22 EDT, 22 October 2022 Giorgia Meloni has today been sworn in as Italy's first far-right premier since the end of the Second World War as well as the first woman to take the role of Italian Prime Minister.Ms Meloni, 45, whose political party has neo-fascist roots, took the oath of office before the Italian president at the Quirinale Presidential Palace in Rome.Her Brothers of Italy party received the most votes Italy during last month's Italian general election, winning 26 per cent of the vote, 119 seats in the Chamber of Deputies and 65 in the Senate.
