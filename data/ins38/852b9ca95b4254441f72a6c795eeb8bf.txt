(AP Photo/Ashley Landis)SAN DIEGO (CNS) - The San Diego Padres will face the Philadelphia Phillies on Tuesday at Petco Park in their first National League Championship Series game since Oct. 14, 1998, after completing one of the greatest postseason upsets in baseball historyThe Padres defeated the Los Angeles Dodgers, 5-3, Saturday night, at Petco Park to win a National League Division Series three games to one. The Dodgers won a franchise-record 111 games in the regular season, 22 more than the Padres.
