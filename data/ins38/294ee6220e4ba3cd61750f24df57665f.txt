Yahoo News reports that 70% of Americans are seeking additional work in order to combat inflation, and 6 out of 10 Americans are looking for short-term gig work. With inflation near a 40-year high in the United States, everyone is looking for ways to make more money. But having more than one job isn't necessarily a good thing - just ask Tesla shareholders about Elon Musk's multiple interests. Overemployment - where higher-income workers secretly work more than one full-time job - is a trend born out of necessity and driven by today's raging inflation.
