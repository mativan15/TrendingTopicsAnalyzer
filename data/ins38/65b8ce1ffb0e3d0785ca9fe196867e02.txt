Bank Holidays: Banks closed for 6 days from tomorrow due to Dhanteras, Diwali
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:28:46

Several banks across the country will remain shut for six consecutive days starting Saturday, i.e October 22 due to Dhanteras and Diwali. The festival of lights is celebrated across the country with great pomp and joy. Ahead of Diwali, Dhanteras is another festival in which people buy gold and silver to bring prosperity to their homes. While Dhanteras marks the beginning of the five-day festival, Bhai Dooj is the last day. Banks will remain shut across the country on Saturday due to Dhanteras. It is also the fourth Saturday of the month.
