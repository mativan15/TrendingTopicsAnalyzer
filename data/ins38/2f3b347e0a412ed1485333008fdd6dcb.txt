Travellers stuck in queues at Edinburgh and Glasgow airports
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 23:01:00

Travellers at Edinburgh and Glasgow airports have been stuck in winding queues outside the terminals in scenes that one passenger called 'an absolute disgrace'.More than 30 flights were delayed at Glasgow and 25 were behind schedule in Edinburgh yesterday morning.John Morrison, 60, arrived at 5.45am to fly to Gatwick in London at 6.45am for a day trip without luggage — but on arrival had to join passengers in queues that were 'snaking into the car park'.• Q&A: Why are flights being cancelled in the UK and what are your rights?The business-class traveller said he was shocked to see that one queue stretched around the first floor of the building into the parking area.
