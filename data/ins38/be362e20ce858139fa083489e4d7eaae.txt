Stock market tumble booted workers from the 401(k) millionaire's club
washingtonpost.com
business
2022-05-27 11:00:17

For an increasing number of employees, it has been like achieving titanium status in a loyalty program. They had joined an elite group of workers who had more than a million dollars in their workplace retirement plans.The number of 401(k) millionaires in the fourth quarter of 2021 jumped 32 percent compared to a year earlier, according to Fidelity Investments, one of the largest managers of workplace plans.But high inflation has caused consumer prices to spike, while the war in Ukraine and supply chain issues have rattled the stock market.
