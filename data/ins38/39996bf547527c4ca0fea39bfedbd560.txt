ALISO VIEJO, Calif., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Ringler, the nation's largest settlement planning company in the nation, is pleased to announce that Andrew Peach of Scottsdale, Arizona, has joined Ringler as a National Director of Strategic Planning.

Mr. Peach comes to Ringler with 20 years of experience in the insurance industry. His strengths include growing successful agency storefronts and developing and supporting agents in sales growth and development.
