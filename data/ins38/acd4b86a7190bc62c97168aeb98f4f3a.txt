Maksim Safaniuk/iStock via Getty Images MP Materials (NYSE:MP) is initiated on Wednesday with an Outperform rating and $50 price target at BMO Capital, which expects the company will "benefit from persistent tightness and robust pricing for magnet rare earth products fueled by the energy transition." BMO's Robin Fiedler sees the stock as attractive currently as MP (MP) "expands downstream for long-term growth and Chinese supply chain circumvention," and believes the company "can overcome non-trivial technical hurdles ahead given its strong balance sheet and government/auto-OEM support.
