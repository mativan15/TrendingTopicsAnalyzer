With millions set to join the Platinum Jubilee festivities, Lidl says its stores will be open as normal on June 4 and 5 - but hours may vary on June 2 and 3Lidl has more than 900 stores across the UK Millions of people across Britain are expected to join in the celebrations for the Queen's Platinum Jubilee. There's an extended four-day Bank Holiday weekend to mark the occasion, which marks 70 years since the monarch first became Queen. There'll be thousands of events taking place across the country over the Bank Holiday weekend from June 2nd to June 5th.
