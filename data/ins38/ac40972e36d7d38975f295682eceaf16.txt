Jamie Foxx remembers sister DeOndra Dixon on anniversary of her death
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 17:18:38

Jamie Foxx has shared an emotional message on the second anniversary of his sister's death.
The Day Shift actor announced the sad news in October 2020, confirming that his sibling, DeOndra Dixon, had died at the age of 36.
Taking to Instagram, he marked the poignant day with a string of photos of the pair together, and opened up about his grief.
In a heartfelt caption, the 54-year-old penned: ‘Deondra I know ur in heaven making everyone laugh… and have everyone dancing to your songs.
‘I miss you terribly, but I know that your soul is shining bright.
