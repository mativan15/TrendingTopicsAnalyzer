Reopening of office has hit Agarbatti sales. Here's why
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 06:45:00

AgenciesRepresentative imageThere is an unlikely casualty of reopening of offices in the country. The agarbatti sales have gone down to 5% in 2022, from 30% during the pandemic. Covid-19 led panic buying resulted in increased sales of incense sticks taking the sales figures to double digits in this largely unorganised sector. Owing to lockdowns in the nation, people could not even visit temples and ended up lighting agarbattis at their homes. However, with restoration of normal supplies and easing restrictions, the numbers have come to single-digits.
