Tropical system struggling to organize, still expected to bring heavy rain to Florida
sfgate.com
news
2022-06-04 19:22:00

Potential Tropical Cyclone One had reached Florida early Saturday afternoon and was expected to bring heavy rains to some areas.The National Hurricane Center reported the system had maximum sustained winds of 40 mph and was moving northeast at 18 mph. The latest NHC track has the storm off of the east coast of Florida by Saturday afternoon. Being on the left side of the storm, or the dry side, Central Florida should see the rain out of the way by this evening. Models still want to keep some rain around through the afternoon.
