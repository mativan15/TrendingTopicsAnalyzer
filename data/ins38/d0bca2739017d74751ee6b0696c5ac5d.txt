The 'Paramount Global Summit on Disability' will be held virtually and in-person at the company's Hollywood Gower office in Los Angeles on October 31, 2022.

	Presented by the Paramount Office of Global Inclusion, the day-long summit for employees and external partners will feature discussions with performers from various series and films, company leaders, and philanthropic and nonprofit workers to call attention to the need for greater disability inclusion in media and entertainment. The goal is to engage the entire industry in their learnings and to share next and best practices.
