Corporate Cybersecurity Teams Struggle to Fill Jobs
wsj.com
news
2022-10-20 13:00:00

An economic downturn and the Covid-19 pandemic are adding new challenges to hiring for crucial cybersecurity roles, exacerbating an already difficult job market.The cybersecurity talent gap grew by 26.2% over the past year, with around 3.4 million unfilled jobs worldwide, according to a new study from (ISC)2, a nonprofit professional organization that offers cybersecurity certifications.
