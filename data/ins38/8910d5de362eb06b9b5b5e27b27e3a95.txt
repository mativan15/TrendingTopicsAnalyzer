Richard Fielding, 89: Fearless rider and City entrepreneur who rated straight-talkers
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 23:01:00

When Lloyd's of London experienced seismic changes in the 1970s and 1980s as old-school practices gave way to international markets and a new style of doing business, Richard Fielding was at its heart. Fresh from forming a Lloyd's broker, Fielding & Partners, he embraced the modern world of finance and with a strong entrepreneurial sense and clear negotiating skills, turned a beady eye on strategic opportunities. He was known for his probity and could be brusque with what he saw as outmoded practices.
