US First lady Jill Biden who was an honorary captain for the American football team Philadelphia Eagles on Sunday was met with loud boos by several people in attendance, reports said.Several people who attended the game tweeted about the apparent loud boos Jill Biden faced as she was accompanied by US President Joe Biden.
