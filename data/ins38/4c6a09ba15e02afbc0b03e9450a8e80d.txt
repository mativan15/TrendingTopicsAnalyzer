Amit Gautam, Co-Founder & CEO, Innover said, "We expect our business from India market to grow 300% over the next 3 years."NEW DELHI: Innover is all set to launch its digital experiences & analytics studio services in India to propel businesses to deliver superior, personalized customer experiences. Through the studio, Innover aims to unlock cutting edge solutions for Indian enterprises, including Metaverse-powered experience lounges, customer journey optimization, hyper-personalization and experience-driven commerce solutions to advance innovation, maximize performance, and drive overall business growth.
