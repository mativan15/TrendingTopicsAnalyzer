By CBS New York Team October 21, 2022 / 7:24 PM / CBS New York Major improvement project for Jones Beach theater begins Major improvement project for Jones Beach theater begins 00:38 WANTAGH, N.Y. -- The Jones Beach summer concert venue is getting a major makeover.A groundbreaking ceremony was held Friday for a series of improvements to the theater.Live Nation Entertainment is making a multimillion dollar investment to improve the concert-going experience.A new entrance will be installed to allow for seamless entry to the venue, several thousand upper-level seats will be replaced and more restrooms will be added.
