Shares of Netflix Inc. NFLX, +6.31% rose 6.31% to $205.09 Thursday, on what proved to be an all-around favorable trading session for the stock market, with the S&P 500 Index SPX, +1.84% rising 1.84% to 4,176.82 and the Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +1.33% rising 1.33% to 33,248.28. Netflix Inc. closed $495.90 short of its 52-week high ($700.99), which the company reached on November 17th. The stock outperformed some of its competitors Thursday, as Amazon.com Inc. AMZN, +3.15% rose 3.15% to $2,510.
