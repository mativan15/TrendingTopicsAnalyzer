OLD GREENWICH, Conn., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Hudson Global, Inc. (Nasdaq: HSON), a leading global talent solutions company, announced today that its management is scheduled to present at the LD Micro Invitational Conference to be held at the Four Seasons Westlake Village, California.



Hudson Global's main presentation is scheduled for Tuesday, June 7, 2022, at 5:00 pm PT. Investors will be able to access the presentation live over the Internet via the weblink: https://ldinv12.
