Sheryl Crow, Karen Fairchild of Little Big Town and Alternate Routes will headline a concert hosted by Artists for the Prevention of School Shootings and Youth Violence on November 30 at City Winery in Nashville. Produced by In Plain View Entertainment, the event's proceeds will benefit Sandy Hook Promise, a national non-profit organization with a mission to educate and empower youth and adults to prevent violence in schools, homes and communities.
