Uma Bharti tears banner of liquor shop near temple in Bhopal
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 02:00:00

BHOPAL: Former chief minister Uma Bharti on Monday reached Ayodhya Bypass area to protest the location of a liquor shop in front of a temple. The senior BJP leader reached there with a large crowd of men and women who agitated with her. Bharti removed the poster of the liquor shop. She called the manager and asked if he also consumed liquor or just sold liquor to customers. Bharti also warned the administration and police that if the liquor outlet was not removed from near the temple, then she would see to it that the law and order situation was disrupted.
