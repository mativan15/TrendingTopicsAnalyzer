Listening to Gov. Jared Polis and his Republican opponent, Heidi Ganahl, speaking about the current state of Colorado is like looking at an eye chart through entirely different lenses. And the solutions they propose on business issues are just as different as the views.

The Democratic Polis, sworn into office in January 2019, speaks of promises kept on establishing universal kindergarten and preschool and on bringing down the highest health care costs through efforts like his reinsurance program for the individually insured.
