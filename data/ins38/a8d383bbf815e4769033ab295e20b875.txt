De Novo EntreBank Selects CSI's NuPoint® Core as Foundation for Integrated Business Banking Services

2022-06-02 11:32:25

PADUCAH, Ky.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--EntreBank, the first Minnesota-based de novo bank in 15 years, has selected Computer Services, Inc.'s (CSI) (OTCQX: CSVI) NuPoint® core platform as the foundation of its integrated banking services. The bank partnered with CSI, a provider of end-to-end fintech and regtech solutions, to meet the unique needs of entrepreneurs and small businesses in Minnesota by providing modern digital banking services that help the bank work at the fast pace their customers expect.
