(Adrian Wyld/The Canadian Press)A judge has ruled that a class action lawsuit alleging Veteran Affairs Canada failed to inform former Canadian Armed Forces members of benefits to which they were entitled can proceed.At the heart of the case is the department's handling of the former Supplementary Retirement Benefit, a program meant to compensate for lower pension and benefits earnings for veterans unable find gainful employment due to service-related injuries.
