Stocks Fall Despite Strong Jobs Report As Rates Surge On Recession Fears
forbes.com
news
2022-06-03 14:03:14

The stock market moved lower on Friday and government bond yields surged after the U.S. economy added a more-than-expected 390,000 jobs in May, with the strong labor market signaling to investors that the Federal Reserve will continue with its path of aggressively tightening monetary policy. The labor market recovery makes it unlikely that the Fed will halt its rate-hiking campaign anytime ... [+] soon. Richard Drew/ASSOCIATED PRESS The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 0.5%, almost 200 points, while the S&P 500 lost nearly 1% and the tech-heavy Nasdaq Composite 1.
