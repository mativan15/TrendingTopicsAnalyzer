NITI Aayog member V. K. Saraswat addressing the valedictory session of the Engineers' Conclave - 2022 at the LPSC, Valiyamala, on Saturday:
Universities should become centres for value creation for the sustainable development of the country and progress in manufacturing, NITI Aayog member V. K. Saraswat said on October 15, 2022. Dr. Saraswat, the chief guest on the closing day of the Engineers' Conclave at the Liquid Propulsion Systems Centre (LPSC), Valiyamala, underlined the need for traditional concepts on engineering to be replaced with sustainable perspectives for achieving our targets for a better tomorrow.
