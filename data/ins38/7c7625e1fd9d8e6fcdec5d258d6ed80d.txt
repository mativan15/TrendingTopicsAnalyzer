Why Vancouver-based Bosa Properties chose Nashville over Atlanta, Austin
bizjournals.com

When David McCutcheon and his company, Bosa Properties, decided it was time to search for real estate projects outside of Vancouver and Seattle, three markets were on the table: Atlanta, Austin and Nashville.

The trio of Sun Belt cities were all impressive, McCutcheon told the Business Journal. Atlanta was massive and well-established. Austin was a tech hub with years of investment behind it. But Nashville had something different.
