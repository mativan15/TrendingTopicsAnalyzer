The world of cryptocurrency is vast, with various products and services popping up every day to solve one problem and make life easier. Decentralised Finance (De-Fi) provides ways for the world to transition from traditional finance systems to truly decentralised platforms that offer anonymity, peer-to-peer interaction and, most importantly, eradicate the need for intermediaries. As a result, De-Fi coins are some of the hottest cryptos on the market, and understandably so.Then we have DEX coins. DEX coins are the native cryptocurrency of decentralised exchange platforms, a product of the De-Fi ecosystem.
