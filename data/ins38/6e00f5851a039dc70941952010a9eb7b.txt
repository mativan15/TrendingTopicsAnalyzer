BMW To Invest $1.7 Bn In Electric Car Production In US

2022-10-19 15:30:32

A BMW i4 electric car
German carmaker BMW said Wednesday it will invest $1.7 billion in the production of electric vehicles in the United States.The plan includes $1 billion towards expanding the company's Spartanburg US manufacturing plant and $700 million for a new battery assembly facility, BMW said in a statement."The BMW group continues the roll-out of its electromobility plan with a new investment in the US," the company said.The Spartanburg plant in South Carolina is already BMW's biggest factory, producing 1,500 BMW X vehicles a day.
