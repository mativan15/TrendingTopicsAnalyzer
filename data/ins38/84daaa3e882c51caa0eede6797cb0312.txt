Getty ImagesMontgomery plays Jack, who travels to New Zealand to attend the funeral of his long-estranged mother, who abandoned him as a child.Hollywood actors Vicky Krieps and Dacre Montgomery will topline the upcoming ghost story "Went Up The Hill". An Australia-New Zealand co-production, the film is directed by Samuel Van Grinsven from a script he co-wrote with Jory Anast, reported entertainment news outlet Deadline. "Stranger Things" star Montgomery plays Jack, who travels to New Zealand to attend the funeral of his long-estranged mother, who abandoned him as a child.
