Gurugram police to probe: ED seal, letterhead forged; case filed
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-31 18:26:07

The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Monday submitted a police complaint against unidentified suspects for forging its seal and letterhead and sending a summons to the residents' welfare association (RWA) president of a condominium in Sector-67A about four months ago, the police said on Tuesday.According to the police, 'the summons was allegedly served' in connection with a case of money laundering by a group of companies, which the central agency is currently probing. Police said the real estate wing of the same group of companies had constructed the condominium as well.
