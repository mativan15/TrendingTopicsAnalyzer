Third Age Health Services Limited (NZSE:TAH) will increase its dividend on the 20th of June to NZ$0.04. This will take the dividend yield from 3.1% to 3.1%, providing a nice boost to shareholder returns.

Third Age Health Services' Payment Has Solid Earnings Coverage

If the payments aren't sustainable, a high yield for a few years won't matter that much. Prior to this announcement, Third Age Health Services was paying out 71% of earnings and more than 75% of free cash flows.
