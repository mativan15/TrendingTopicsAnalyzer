The decision to return the 206 acres of land to the landowners or to the legal heirs was taken at a cabinet meeting presided over by Odisha chief minister Naveen Patnaik. (PTI)

 


ByDebabrata Mohanty, Bhubaneswar





 The Odisha government on Friday decided to return 206 acres of land, earlier acquired for a Tata Steel plant near Gopalpur coast of Ganjam district, to the original landowners, officials said.The government acquired over 6,900 acres of land in Kalipalli and dozen other villages for the proposed plant in 1996.
