IANSThe first batch of Agniveers will be joining Army training Centres by January next year.New Delhi: The Indian Army has said it has signed agreements with 11 banks to provide banking facilities to Agniveers after their enrolment, the defence ministry said on Saturday. In June, the government announced the 'Agnipath' scheme for the three services to recruit youths between the age bracket of 17-and-half years and 21 for only four years with a provision to retain 25 per cent of them for 15 more years.
