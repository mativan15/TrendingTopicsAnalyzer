Actors Yogi, Ajay Rao, Dharma Keerthi, Milana Nagaraj, and Darling Krishna are all set to play soccer and cricket in Sri Lanka and Maldives soon. An event was organised recently at a five-star hotel in Bengaluru to announce the tour of actors to Sri Lanka and Maldives. The event was organised by Tournival and Anyelp Entertainment. 'Sri Lanka has been my favourite destination. I agreed to attend the event without hesitation when the organisers approached me,'' said Priyanka Upendra. Zakir Hussain, CEO of Anyelp, said talks are on with Sandalwood and Bollywood actors.
