SHANGHAI and HONG KONG and SINGAPORE, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Prophet, the global growth and transformation consultancy, continues to expand its leadership team and capabilities in Asia, appointing three new senior hires: Samuel Levy-Basse, Jolin Guan and Daniel Hu. The new leaders' extensive expertise will help Prophet's clients across Asia unlock uncommon growth and support the firm's growth trajectory in the region.

Samuel Levy-Basse joins as a partner in Prophet's Singapore office and will take on the key role of driving Prophet's organization, innovation and digital transformation disciplines across Asia.
