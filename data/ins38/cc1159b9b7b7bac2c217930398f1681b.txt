Amazon opens Seaport office tower
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-06-03 21:58:41

Amazon is about halfway into its plan to bring 4,500 technology and corporate employees to its 'Boston Tech Hub' in the Seaport District.With much fanfare on Friday, company leaders, state officials, and developer WS Development held a ribbon-cutting ceremony outside the office, where Amazon employs 2,000 people. The 17-story building opened in phases starting in February.Rohit Prasad, a senior vice president and head scientist for Amazon Alexa, said it is betting on Boston because of the city's talent, diversity, and culture of building technology that 'changes lives of everyone.
