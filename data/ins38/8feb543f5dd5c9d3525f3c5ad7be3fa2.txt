TVs, ovens and vacuum cleaners in demand this festive season, says new report
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:45:00

The demand for consumer electronics this festive season will be driven by televisions, microwave ovens, vacuum cleaners and washing machines with consumers prefering premium products, as per a latest report by market researcher and intelligence firm GfK. The report said the pandemic effect appears to be waning as buying momentum for home appliances and electronics has improved in 2022, more so for premium products. Anant Jain, head of market intelligence- India, GfK said as a result of the epidemic outbreak, consumer goods sales have been facing challenges for the past two years, but now things appear to be returning to normal.
