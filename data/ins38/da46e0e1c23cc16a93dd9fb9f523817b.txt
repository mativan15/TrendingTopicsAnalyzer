By Reuters Published: 22:33 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 22:33 EDT, 2 June 2022 By Rory Carroll SAN FRANCISCO, June 2 (Reuters) - NBA Commissioner Adam Silver said the league's return to China's CCTV after an 18-month blackout was a positive development and that engagement through sports is culturally beneficial. The NBA saw its decades-long partnership with CCTV rocked in October 2019 when Daryl Morey, general manager of the Houston Rockets at the time, tweeted in support of anti-government protests in Hong Kong over a controversial extradition bill.
