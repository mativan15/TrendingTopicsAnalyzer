SPRINGDALE, Ark., Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Explore Scientific, LLC has reached an agreement to become a non-exclusive distributor for Unistellar digital smart telescopes and related accessories.

With their proprietary live light accumulation technology and autonomous field detection capability that makes it easy to identify and track observing targets, Unistellar's smart eVscopes, the eQuinox and the eVscope 2, have emerged as game changers in the world of consumer telescopes.
