Verne Global and Landsvirkjun Team Up to Fuel Icelandic Data Center's Back-up Power With Green Hydrogen
apnews.com
business
2022-05-30 09:03:27

LONDON & REYKJAVIK, Iceland--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 30, 2022--Verne Global, provider of sustainable data center solutions for high intensity computing, and Landsvirkjun, the national power company of Iceland, today announced a collaboration which will see Verne Global trial and deploy hydrogen fuel cells to produce reliable and sustainable back-up power for its Icelandic data center campus. The two companies will work together to enable the transition to green hydrogen power produced with Iceland's renewable energy, taking Verne Global even further with its industry-leading sustainability position.
