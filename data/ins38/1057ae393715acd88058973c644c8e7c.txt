Michael Loccisano/Getty Images Entertainment Readers of a certain age will remember Carnac the Magnificent, Johnny Carson's recurring alter ego. As Carnac, the late-night host would list off three seemingly unrelated words, all of which answered a question that was sealed in an envelope that he held to his forehead. Today we're going to play the same game, with the answers being PayPal (PYPL), Kanye (or Ye, as he's now known) and central bank digital currencies (CBDCs). And the question: What are the consequences of financial hyper-centralization? Some of you will make the connections immediately.
