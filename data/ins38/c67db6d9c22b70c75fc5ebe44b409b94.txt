Biden evacuated to a secure location
RT
news
2022-06-04 20:36:59

US President Joe Biden has been evacuated from his Delaware beach house after an unauthorized aircraft crossed into the restricted airspace above the property, triggering security protocols.The president was rushed to a secure location around 1pm on Saturday after a privately owned plane 'mistakenly' entered a secure area near Biden's vacation home in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware, the US Secret Service said in a statement. 'The aircraft was immediately escorted out of the restricted airspace,' the agency added.
