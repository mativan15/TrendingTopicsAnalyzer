BJP chief J P Nadda on Sunday lashed out at opposition parties as he suggested that ideologies of rivals have changed over time but the ruling party has held its ground. Addressing the "Panch Parmeshwar Sammelan", held at the Ramlila Maidan ahead of the Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) polls which are likely to be held in December, BJP chief hit out at the Congress.
