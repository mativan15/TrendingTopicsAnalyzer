Evil Wordle Knockoff Dordle Makes You Tackle Two Words at Once
cnet.com
news
2022-10-17 16:41:00

First came Wordle. Then came the endless Wordle knockoffs. Among them is Dordle, a devious word game that doesn't let you off easy. You have to guess two five-letter words at the same time, but you can only input one word guess at a time. Play it and you'll get it.Wordle fever has calmed down a bit since the game exploded earlier this year, but I'm still a dedicated daily player. At one point, I was also playing four- and eight-word variations every day. Now I'm down to just two: Wordle and Dordle.
