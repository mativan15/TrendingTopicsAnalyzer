By Jason HallOctober 17, 2022The North Carolina Tar Heels will begin the 2022-23 Men's college basketball season as the top-ranked team in the nation. The Tar Heels ranked No. 1 overall in the Associated Press Top 25 College Basketball Preseason Poll released on Monday (October 17), ahead of No. 2 Gonzaga, No. 3 Houston, No. 4 Kentucky, as well as Baylor and Kansas -- who defeated North Carolina in the national championship -- with both tied at No. 5 overall.
