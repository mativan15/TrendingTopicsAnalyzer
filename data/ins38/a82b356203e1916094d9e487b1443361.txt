Published: 00:07 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 00:07 EDT, 30 May 2022 Business owners have slammed the NSW government over its single-use bag ban which will see consumers forking out more money to carry purchased goods.The ban, which comes into effect on Tuesday, could see businesses fined up to $250,000 for giving out single-use bags.Business owners have criticised the government for what they say was short notice, with many set to lose thousands by having to ditch the bags they already have in stock and revert to paper bags that'll cost consumers more money.
