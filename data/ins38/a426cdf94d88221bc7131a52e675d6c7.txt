Open Book Extracts Partners with Day Three Labs to Commercialize Unlokt™ Delivery Technology for Superior Edible Products

ROXBORO, N.C., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Open Book Extracts (OBX), a leading global ingredient manufacturer and product development house for the industry's most innovative and highest quality cannabinoid-enabled products, today announces the immediate availability of new edible products powered by Unlokt™ from Day Three Labs (DTL).
Unlokt™ is a novel, protein-based infusion technology that uses a first-of-its-kind, fully water soluble powder that contains clean, all natural food grade ingredients.
