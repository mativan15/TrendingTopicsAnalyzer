A startup that has a resilient business model, built with an extremely long-term mindset that will see it become a corporate giant in the next decade or so, will be a clear winner, say the jury members. Shailendra Singh, Sriharhsa Majety, Sahil Barua share their views on what they expect from the winners of the ET Startup Awards 2022. 
Shailendra Singh, MD, Sequoia India & Southeast Asia
Our mindset is extremely long-term. For me, point-in-time momentum in a company is less important compared to the potential for enduring success.
