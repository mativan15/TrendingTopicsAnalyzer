PTIKarnataka Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai.Karnataka Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai on Friday said the State government has succeeded in drawing investment commitment worth about Rs 65,000 crore with its participation at the World Economic Forum Meet in Davos, Switzerland. Karnataka is at the forefront in attracting investments and the WEF Meet has proved to be a "success", he said, as he asserted that the State has "not lost or is not going to lose" in attracting investments in the backdrop of such concerns citing certain developments locally.
