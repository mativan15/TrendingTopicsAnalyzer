Binance Labs, the venture capital arm of cryptocurrency exchange Binance, has closed a $500 million fund that will invest in Web3 and blockchain startups. According to a Wednesday blog post, DST Global Partners and Breyer Capital were among the investors who have participated in the raise. The fund will invest across three different stages: incubation, early-stage venture, and late-stage growth.
