A special court in Chhattisgarh on Friday extended the Enforcement Directorate (ED) custody of an Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer and two others in a money laundering case linked to the alleged extortion of money from coal and mining transporters in the state.Additional district and sessions judge Ajay Singh Rajput extended the remand of IAS officer Sameer Vishnoi, businessman Sunil Agrawal and Laxmikant Tiwari, the uncle of absconding coal businessman Suryakant Tiwari.
