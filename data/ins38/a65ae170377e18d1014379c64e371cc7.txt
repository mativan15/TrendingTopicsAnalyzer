James Corden has restaurant ban lifted after 'apologising for abusive behaviour'
sky.com
news
2022-10-18 12:23:53

The manager of a New York restaurant who banned James Corden for "abusive" behaviour has said "all is forgiven" after the British TV host reportedly apologised.Keith McNally, manager of the Balthazar in Manhattan, said Corden was a "hugely gifted comedian" but a "tiny cretin of a man" as he posted details on Instagram of two occasions he claims the the actor was rude to staff.
He added The Late Late Show host was the most "abusive customer to my Balthazar servers since the restaurant opened 25 years ago".
