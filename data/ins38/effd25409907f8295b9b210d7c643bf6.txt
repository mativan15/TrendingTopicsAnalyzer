Indigo trims Q4 loss and squeaks out a $3.3-million profit for the full year
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-06-02 22:43:38

Indigo Books and Music Inc. trimmed its net loss to end its fiscal year that saw it swing to a small profit.The Toronto-based retailer says it lost $23.4-million in the fourth quarter, compared with a loss of $39.5-million a year earlier.Indigo did not provide per share earnings numbers for the quarter.Revenues for the three months ended April 2 were $220.7-million, up 10.9 per cent from $199-million in the fourth quarter of 2021.For the full-year, Indigo earned $3.27-million on $1.06-billion of revenues, compared with a loss of $57.
