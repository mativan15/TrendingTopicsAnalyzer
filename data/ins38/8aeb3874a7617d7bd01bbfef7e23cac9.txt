BAYONNE, N.J., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- BCB Bancorp, Inc. (the 'Company'), (NASDAQ: BCBP), the holding company for BCB Community Bank, today announced that its Board of Directors has amended its current stock repurchase program to increase the number of shares to be repurchased. Under the stock repurchase program as adopted on December 11, 2020, the Company was authorized to repurchase up to 500,000 shares of the Company's common stock. As of September 30, 2022, 417,650 of those 500,000 shares had been repurchased under the program.
