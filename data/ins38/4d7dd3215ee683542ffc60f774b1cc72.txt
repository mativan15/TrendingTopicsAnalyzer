Brought up in India, educated at Oxford and Harvard, Mr. MacFarquhar has traveled extensively in Asia and for several years has written books and articles on China and Asian Communism. Living in London, he is editor of the CHINA QUARTERLY and a commentator for the B.B.C.AFTER seven years of natural calamities, economic dislocation, and slow, painful recovery, China is moving again. Premier Chou En-lai has announced that a third Five-Year Plan will start in 1966. Three years late though it is, the new plan is a sign that the long period of 'readjustment and consolidation' of the economy is almost over.
