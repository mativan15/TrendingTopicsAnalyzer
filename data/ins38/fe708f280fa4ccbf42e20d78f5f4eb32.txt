Problems continue to mount up for senior executives at Scandinavian Airlines (SAS). Thousands of cancelled flights, angry pilots unions, delays to new aircraft and now another quarter of heavy losses have left many in the industry questioning the airline's future. Recent reports in the Swedish media suggest foreign investors are now interested in taking over the airline, albeit with significant financial conditions attached.
