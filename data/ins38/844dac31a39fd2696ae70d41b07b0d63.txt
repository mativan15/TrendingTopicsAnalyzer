Deutsche Bank HQ raided by police in greenwashing probe
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-31 10:08:08

Police in Germany have raided Deutsche Bank's offices in Frankfurt as part of an investigation into greenwashing.Police conducted the raid at the bank's headquarters as well as the nearby offices of the asset manager group, DWS Group. According to Bloomberg, who first reported on the raids, both offices were searched by police as part of greenwashing allegations against DWS."We have continuously cooperated fully with all relevant regulators and authorities on this matter and will continue to do so," a spokesperson for DWS told Bloomberg.
