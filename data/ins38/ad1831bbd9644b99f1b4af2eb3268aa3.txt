EU says Croatia ready to join eurozone in 2023
dw.com
news
2022-06-01 15:17:00

The European Commission says Croatia has met all the conditions to adopt the euro common currency starting in 2023. The planned switch will come less than a decade after the Balkan country joined the EU. 

Croatia has unveiled the new one-euro coin as it prepares to become the 20th member of the currency union in Europe
Croatia is set to adopt the euro currency from the start of 2023 after meeting all the criteria to join the eurozone, the European Commission announced Wednesday.
The switch will make Croatia the 20th European country to use the single currency.
