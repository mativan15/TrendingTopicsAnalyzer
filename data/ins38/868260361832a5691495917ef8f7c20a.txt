The Weekend Ka Vaar episode of Bigg Boss 16 will see Karan Johar taking Salman Khan's place this time. And he makes sure to show contestants their reality.Karan Johar is in a fierce mood and schools all contestants for going against Archana Gautam, who is the house captain now. He then calls out Gori Nagori for trying to steal in front of her. In the promo it is shown, Gori telling Archana, 'Bigg Boss iska sar phodungi main.'Karan asks everyone, 'Were Gori's words intended to hurt Archana or not?' All the housemates respond saying, ‘Yes'.
