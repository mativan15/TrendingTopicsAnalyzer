Bollywood actor Gulshan Grover recently named casing director Mukesh Chhabra is blacklisting veterans from being part in films. The actor who was recently seen in a short film Baj Gai Seeti, opened up on facing a similar problem and there aren't many jobs to be found for the older generation.In an interview with Hindustan Times, Gulshan spoke about he came on board for the film and drew parallels between his reel and real life characters. He shared, when the short film was offered to him he realised it was not about the ageing thief, but as something with a larger connect with life.
