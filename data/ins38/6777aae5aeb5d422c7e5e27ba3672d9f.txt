The Philadelphia Business Journal is pleased to announce our sixth annual class of Best of the Bar: Philadelphia's Top Lawyers. Below is the honoree in the bankruptcy category. Use the navigation at the bottom to see honorees in all of this year's categories.

Regina Stango Kelbon

Law School: Temple University (1985)

Kelbon, head of Blank Rome's bankruptcy and restructuring practice, successfully navigated the bankruptcy of Borden Dairy Co., one of the nation's oldest and largest dairy companies.
