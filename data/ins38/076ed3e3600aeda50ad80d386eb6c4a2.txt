Market veteran Prashant Jain, in his first interview after quitting HDFC AMC, has said that the value and growth debate is misplaced. He advised investors to invest in a growth sustainable business but not at an unreasonable price. He also gave his investment essence - 3Ps - Prudence, patience and performance.
