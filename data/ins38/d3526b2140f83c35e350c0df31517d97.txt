 Calgary firefighters worked throughout Monday morning to get control of a multi-home fire in the city's northeast.
Calgary Fire Department was called to an active fire on Cornerstone Boulevard NE at just after 3:30 a.m. Monday. Global News has learned that approximately three homes were destroyed with more expected to be affected by the fire damage.
