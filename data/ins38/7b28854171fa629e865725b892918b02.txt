PM Modi launches Rozgar Mela, 75,000 people to get appointment letters today
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-22 04:11:43

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday launched Rozgar Mela, the recruitment drive for 10 lakh personnel. In a significant step forward to provide job opportunities for the youth, 75,000 employees will be handed over the appointment letter during the occasion, according to a release.



Addressing the event, Prime Minister Modi said that India has become the 5th biggest economy in the world because of the reforms undertaken in the last 8 years. He asserted that the efficiency of government departments has increased due to the efforts of "our Karmayogis.
