British Prime Minister Liz Truss announces her resignation outside 10 Downing Street in London on October 20, 2022. HENRY NICHOLLS / REUTERS The setup has become familiar to the British public: technicians quickly erect a light wooden desk in front of 10 Downing Street, a wall of cameras lines up facing the famous black varnished door as it opens on a serious-faced leader who announces their departure. In June 2016, a few days after the vote on Brexit, David Cameron bowed out without a word of apology.
