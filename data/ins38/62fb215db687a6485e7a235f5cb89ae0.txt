Elon Musk promises to fund Starlink in Ukraine ‘indefinitely'
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-16 11:54:38

Elon Musk has said he will continue to fund the Starlink mobile internet system in Ukraine for free 'indefinitely' following discussions with the US defence department.The billionaire entrepreneur had complained last week that the service was costing his private space company SpaceX 'approaching $20 million (€20.6 million) a month' and had written to the Pentagon requesting financial support.The Pentagon on Friday confirmed that it had been discussing payments with Mr Musk's company to ensure connectivity for Ukrainian forces, adding that it was also exploring alternatives.
