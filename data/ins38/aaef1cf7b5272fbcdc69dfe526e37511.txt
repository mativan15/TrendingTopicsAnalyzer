AgenciesSenior executives in India exude more confidence about the resilience of the global economy over the next six months (82% CEOs in India compared to 73% CEOs globally) than they did in February 2022 (40% CEOs in India as against 60% CEOs globally), when KPMG surveyed 500 CEOs for its CEO Outlook Pulse survey.(This story originally appeared in on Oct 17, 2022)MUMBAI: A large majority of CEOs in India - 86% - predict that over the next year, recession will impact company earnings by up to 10%.
