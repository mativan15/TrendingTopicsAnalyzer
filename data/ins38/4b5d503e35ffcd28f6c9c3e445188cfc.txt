IANSUN Secretary General Antonio Guterres United Nations Secretary General Antonio Guterres on Thursday said overconsumption has resulted in a three-fold planetary emergency of climate change, biodiversity loss and pollution, and called for the need to treat the earth's resources judiciously and respectfully. Speaking about subsidies for fossil fuels, he also said it is better to subsidize "people" instead of "things". Guterres jointly launched Mission LiFE (Lifestyle for Environment) with Prime Minister Narendra Modi at Kevadia in Gujarat on the second day of his three-day visit to India, and also held a bilateral meeting with Modi.
