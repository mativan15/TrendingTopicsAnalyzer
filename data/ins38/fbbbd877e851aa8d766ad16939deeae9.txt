Mohamed Salah 'made Barcelona promise' as 2023 plan emerges for Liverpool superstar
express.co.uk
sport
2022-06-05 09:46:00

Mohamed Salah has confirmed he'll be playing his football at Liverpool next season, but Barcelona are playing a significant role in the superstar's hesitance to commit his future to the club. Salah has been given assurances of a 2023 switch to Camp Nou if he becomes a free agent, throwing his Liverpool career into doubt.Barcelona's interest is a primary factor behind the 29-year-old's reluctance to extend his current deal at Anfield, Mirror Sport report. The Blaugrana have promised Salah that there will be a place for him in Xavi Hernandez's squad once his Liverpool contract expires.
