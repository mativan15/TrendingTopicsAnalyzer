(Image: WIKIMEDIA COMMONS)The small plane departed from the Adriatic city of Split, Croatia, on Sunday and was heading for Germany. While in the air, the plane went off the radar and could not be detected by authorities. It is reported to have crashed near Brocanac, central Croatia.Onboard the plane were two Germans, one Croat and a Swiss pilot, according to Reuters.These have all now been found dead.Damir Trut, General Director at the National Protection and Rescue Directorate, said on day after the plane went missing: 'Unfortunately, it has been confirmed that all passengers who were on board the Cessna aircraft were killed.
