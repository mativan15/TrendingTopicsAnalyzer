CRISTIANO RONALDO was delighted to catch up with old mentor Sir Alex Ferguson at the weekend.
The Portuguese star was presented with a special award by the legendary Scot ahead of Manchester United's 0-0 draw with Newcastle yesterday.

Ferguson, 80, appeared pitchside to congratulate Ronaldo on scoring his 700th club career goal last week.
Both men beamed as they hugged and posed for pictures together.
