The cheat-reveal is always shocking and delicious, but rarely quite as shocking and delicious as the footage from the Lake Erie Walleye Trail, a fishing tournament held in Ohio at the beginning of October. The video shows a judge weighing the fish that contestants have presented, before deciding to cut the fish caught by two men called Runyan and Cominsky open. He stands up, with a certain amount of piratical fury, and shouts, 'We've got weights in fish!' The crowd erupts, a sea of branded sweatshirts and waterproof trousers.
