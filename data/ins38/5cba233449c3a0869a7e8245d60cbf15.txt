A man is pulled at the gate of the Chinese consulate after a demonstration against China's President Xi Jinping, in Manchester, Britain on October 16, 2022. Photo: Handout via Reuters
 China's Foreign Ministry said on October 18 that its diplomatic missions abroad have the right to 'take necessary measures' to maintain security after British police opened an investigation into the assault of a Hong Kong protester who alleged he was dragged into the Chinese Consulate in Manchester and beaten up during a demonstration.
