Two officers suspended for shady implementation of Swachh scheme
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-15 14:04:39

Following the detection of serious financial irregularities in the implementation of Centre's flagship programme, Swachh Survekshan Gramin, the territorial administration has placed two of its officers — A. Vivekanandan and S. Prema — under suspension. While Vivekanandan was currently serving as Junior Engineer, Irrigation, Public Health Division of the Public Works Department, Karaikal, Prema was functioning as Director (Administration), Perunthaliavar Kamarajar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Karaikal.
