The United Kingdom has warned its former military pilots against working for the Chinese military. According to a BBC report, up to 30 former British fighter pilots are believed to be training People's Liberation Army personnel. It is claimed that the former military pilots are being lured to China with massive amounts of money to pass on their expertise to the PLA.
