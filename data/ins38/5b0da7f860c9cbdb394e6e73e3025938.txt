BUENOS AIRES, Argentina & MEXICO CITY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--Mavenir, the Network Software Provider building the future of networks with cloud-native software that runs on any cloud and transforms the way the world connects, today confirms its selection by Telefónica Hispam (Hispanoamérica) to supply cloud-native IMS Core for fixed and mobile networks across Mexico, Colombia, and Chile.As mobile networks continue to evolve, and to handle growing data and device needs, Mavenir's cloud-native IMS platform serves as the foundational technology for 5G networks.
