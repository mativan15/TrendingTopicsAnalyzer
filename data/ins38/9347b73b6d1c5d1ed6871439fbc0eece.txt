Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, The Wire said it would withhold the four articles from public views for an internal reviewAn Indian news website has removed four investigative reports it published on Meta after a controversy. The reports, published by The Wire, said a member of the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) had privileges on Instagram to have posts removed.Meta - which owns Facebook and Instagram - denied the allegations and said that the documents the website gave as proof were "fabricated".
