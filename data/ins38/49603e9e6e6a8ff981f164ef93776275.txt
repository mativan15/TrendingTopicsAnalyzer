Liverpool's thrilling 1-0 victory over Manchester City was marred by a number of incidents, as Pep Guardiola alleged that coins were thrown at him, while "vile" chants were heard in the away endOff-field incidents marred Liverpool and Man City's fiery clashMerseyside Police will work with both Liverpool and Manchester City to address a string of concerning incidents which are alleged to have occurred during Sunday's fiery clash between the two teams at Anfield. After losing the pulsating Premier League match 1-0, City boss Pep Guardiola accused home supporters near the dugout of throwing coins at him, while the Manchester club also alleged that their team bus was attacked when leaving the stadium.
