July 22nd Options Now Available For Deutsche Bank (DB)
nasdaq.com

2022-06-02 11:58:00

Investors in Deutsche Bank AG (Symbol: DB) saw new options become available today, for the July 22nd expiration. At Stock Options Channel, our YieldBoost formula has looked up and down the DB options chain for the new July 22nd contracts and identified one put and one call contract of particular interest.

The put contract at the $10.50 strike price has a current bid of 40 cents. If an investor was to sell-to-open that put contract, they are committing to purchase the stock at $10.50, but will also collect the premium, putting the cost basis of the shares at $10.
