Getty ImagesIts AUM rose about 5% quarter-on-quarter to Rs 5,782 croreMicro lender Spandana Sphoorty Financial reported a consolidated net profit of Rs 55.2 crore for the September quarter against a Rs 60 crore loss in the year-ago period, even as its total income dipped. Interest income for the lender was 21% lower at Rs 276.5 crore against Rs 350.5 crore while total income was Rs 310 crore against Rs 396 crore. Gross non-performing assets ratio at the end of September was 7.47% while net NPA was 3.
