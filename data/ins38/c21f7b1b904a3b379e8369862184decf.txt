As Part Of Europe Expansion, Binance Registers With Italy's Regulator
ndtv.com
news
2022-05-28 10:28:11

Binance seeks to expand operations in EuropeBinance said on Friday its legal entity in Italy had registered with the regulator in the country, as the major cryptocurrency exchange seeks to gain traction in Europe.The registration of Binance Italy, which was established in recent months, could potentially make the company more accountable and reduce the prospects for money laundering.Binance said it could now open offices in Italy and expand the local team. The company is one of the 14 virtual asset operators to be registered with the Organismo degli Agenti e dei Mediatori (OAM), which regulates the crypto industry in Italy.
