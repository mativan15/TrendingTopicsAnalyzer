June 02, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time DENVER--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Modivcare Inc. ('Modivcare' or the 'Company') (Nasdaq: MODV), a technology-enabled healthcare services company that provides a platform of integrated supportive care solutions focused on improving patient outcomes, announced the Company will host its inaugural Investor Day in-person in Denver, Colorado on Thursday, June 23, 2022, starting at 1:00 PM (MT). The Company will also host a reception with members of the Executive Leadership Team at the conclusion of the Investor Day presentations.
