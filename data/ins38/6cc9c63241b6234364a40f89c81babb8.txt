Let's Break Down the Easter Eggs and Influences of Taylor Swift's Midnights
time.com
news
2022-10-21 14:51:56

When Taylor Swift announced the release of her highly anticipated tenth album, Midnights, in August, she called it the story of '13 sleepless nights scattered throughout my life.' The songs, written during those wee hours in the night when Swift should have been asleep, resulted in a collection that she says is 'a journey through terrors and sweet dreams.' For the past month, she has hinted that her latest record—her fourth in as many years—would be her most self-reflective. Midnights, she promised, would delve deep into her insecurities, guilt, shame, and revenge fantasies, the kinds of things that would keep anyone up way past their bedtime.
