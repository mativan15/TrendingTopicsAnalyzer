Epcot needs a revamp. Marvel's Guardians of the Galaxy are here to save the day
cnn.com
news
2022-05-28 12:52:35

New York (CNN Business)Disney World is the crown jewel of the company's Parks business, but even crown jewels need to be gussied up now and again. Epcot, one of the resort's most beloved parks since its 1982 opening, is in need of an update to attract a new generation of consumers. The company is counting on a group of intergalactic scoundrels to help. "Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind," a state-of-the-art indoor roller coaster featuring the popular superheroes who debuted in the 2014 film, opened at the Florida theme park on Friday.
