STUTTGART, Germany, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Makersite, a leader in delivering supply chain digital twins for product sustainability, risk, and cost optimization for the world's leading brands, announces the completion of an 18M USD funding round. The German startup uses AI, data, and apps to power sustainable product and supply chain decisions at scale, helping manufacturing enterprises solve complex challenges across the entire value chain. Hitachi Ventures, the global venture capital arm of Hitachi, Ltd.
