Treasury management startup Payflows raises $5.5 million in round co-led by Coinbase-backer Ribbit Capital
businessinsider.com
news
2022-10-18 13:40:47

Payflows, a fintech startup focused on treasury management, has raised $5.5 million in a round co-led by US investing giant Ribbit Capital, Insider understands.The French firm, which remains in stealth mode, was established earlier this year by former staffers at Parisian insurance startup Luko. Pauline Glikman, once Luko's vice president of operations, cofounded Payflows alongside Joseph Assouline, who headed up Luko's machine learning division.Coinbase and Plaid-backer Ribbit co-led the round, four sources familiar with the deal told Insider.
