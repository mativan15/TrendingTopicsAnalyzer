Mohamed Salah shows why he has to be Liverpool's central figure
telegraph.co.uk

2022-10-16 00:00:00

The Liverpool manager has belatedly reached the conclusion the solution to his early-season problems has been staring him in the face.
Salah's central-striker role against City was due to circumstances as much as design. Only Klopp will know how influential his six-minute hat-trick against Rangers in midweek was in his planning for Sunday. Maybe switching Salah's position was always the idea, but his spectacular cameo certainly focused Klopp's mind on where he must operate to inflict most damage.
