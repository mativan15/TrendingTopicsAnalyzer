Cindy Ord/Getty Images Entertainment It's fascinating how much can change over the course of a year or so. While some companies in some industries may exhibit slow rates of change, that's not true of all companies or of all industries. A great example of this can be seen by looking at The Boston Beer Company (NYSE:SAM), a producer of alcoholic beverages such as Samuel Adams, Angry Orchard, and Twisted Tea. Despite finishing off its 2021 fiscal year with strong revenue growth compared to the year prior, financial performance in 2022 has been quite disappointing.
