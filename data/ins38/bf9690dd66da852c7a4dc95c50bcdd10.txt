New-Construction Projects California is struggling to navigate a severe drought, but that doesn't seem to be slowing the pace of new home construction
 June 2, 2022 
 Image: Stock.adobe.com California officials have enacted strict regulations on water usage as the state battles another devastating drought, but that doesn't mean a slowdown in construction is underway, according to the Los Angeles Times. Roughly 80% of water statewide is used for agriculture and the remaining 20% is used for houses and businesses, but rather than using that remaining water efficiently, much is wasted to water lawns, fill pools, and wash sidewalks.
