Demon Dentist review — toothsome entertainment in need of a little polish

2022-10-21 11:00:00

★★★☆☆The conspiracy-minded might wonder if this is all part of some cunning plan to relieve pressure on our overstretched health services. Dentists already have a dubious reputation among children. It might be even harder for them to find customers in the wake of Neal Foster's adaptation of the comedian-turned-author David Walliams's tale — full of penny-dreadful horrors — of a drill-wielding harpie who muscles into the tooth fairy business.This production from the Birmingham Stage Company — the same people behind the cheerfully gruesome Horrible Histories series — arrives in time to tap into the festive market.
