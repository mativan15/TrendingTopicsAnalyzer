All the stars seem aligned for India to drive growth, says fund manager Pawan Parakh
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 04:45:00

There is a reasonably high probability that the Nifty50 and Sensex would make new highs by the next Diwali, said Pawan Parakh, smallcase manager and fund manager, Alpha Portfolios at Renaissance Investment Managers, in an interview with ETMarkets. While worsening global macros do pose a challenge to growth, Parakh expressed confidence in the domestic economy, and highlighted the factors which will make India a far more compelling investment destination.
