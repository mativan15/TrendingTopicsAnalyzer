M & M and Statiq join to provide E4W charging solutions across the country

2022-10-19 09:45:00

Statiq said it aims to provide an end-to-end ecosystem for EV chargers and to assure everybody that whether it's within their town or city, or on long drives over weekends and holidays, a Statiq EV charging station will always be at hand.New Delhi: EV charging network provider Statiq on Wednesday said it has partnered with the auto major Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) to provide charging solutions across the country. The two companies said in a media release that they will also collaborate on various e-mobility tech integration projects going forward.
