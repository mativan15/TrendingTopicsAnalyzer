Dallas ranks fifth among nation's fastest-growing economies, Kenan Institute study finds
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 03:24:00

Dallas has been named one of the fastest-growing economies in the U.S., according to a study by the Kenan Institute of Private Enterprise at the University of North Carolina-Chapel Hill.

According to the report, Dallas ranks fifth among the 50 most populous cities in the country with gross domestic product growth of 3.1%. During the pandemic, Dallas-Fort Worth saw 97,390 people move into the area between June 2020 and July 2021.

According to the study, migration to Dallas is due to the diversity in industries with 19 Fortune 500 companies based in the area.
