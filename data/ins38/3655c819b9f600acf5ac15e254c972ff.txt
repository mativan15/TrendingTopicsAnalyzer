FTX US, Billionaire Sam Bankman-Fried Under Investigation By Texas Regulators
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-18 02:51:10

The U.S. arm of crypto exchange FTX and its co-founder Sam Bankman-Fried are being investigated by the Texas State Securities Board and the Texas Department of Banking for potential violations of securities laws.The regulators are investigating if the yield-bearing accounts provided by the crypto exchange fall under the category of unregistered securities, according to a court filing."The Enforcement Division is now investigating FTX Trading, FTX US, and their principals, including Sam Bankman-Fried," wrote Joseph Jason Rotunda, director of enforcement for the Texas State Securities Board.
