Maybe there's a silver lining to inflation being at the highest levels we've seen in decades. Hear me out. At the least, the challenge is a shared one. We are all adjusting to the trial of higher prices--for  raw materials, for subcontractors, for shipping, and so on. The labor shortage compounds supply-chain disruptions. Chances are you're seeing higher costs for your own employees, as demand for talent continues to grow. Everybody's looking for an end in sight. But what to do while we search the horizon?Companies have control over a couple of key things.
