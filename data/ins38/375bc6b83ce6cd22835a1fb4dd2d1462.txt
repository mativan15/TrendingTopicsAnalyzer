Plan in works to improve LWE areas' communication network
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 19:30:00

The government is putting together an action plan to expand telecom connectivity, intelligence sharing and local language outreach to people in areas affected by Left Wing Extremism, ET has learnt. The Ministry of Home Affairs has specifically asked the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting as well as national broadcaster Prasar Bharati to finalise the contours of the plan, people in the know of the matter told ET. In a meeting with I&B officials last month, senior home ministry officials took stock of the existing telecom infrastructure to decide the way forward.
