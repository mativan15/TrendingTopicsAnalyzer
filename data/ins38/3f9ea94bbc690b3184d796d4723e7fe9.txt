June 02, 2022 04:20 PM Eastern Daylight Time OAK BROOK, Ill.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--InPoint Commercial Real Estate Income, Inc. (NYSE: ICR PRA), a Maryland corporation (the 'Company'), announced today that its board of directors has declared a dividend on its 6.75% Series A Cumulative Redeemable Preferred Stock. A quarterly dividend of $0.421875 per share will be paid on June 30, 2022 to holders of record on June 15, 2022.
