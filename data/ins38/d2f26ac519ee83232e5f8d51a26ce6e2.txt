Schellman in Tampa appoints a new CFO
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-17 10:44:00

Andy Goldstein is stepping in as Schellman's new CFO with more than two decades of financial management experience.

Goldstein previously held executive finance roles in several private-equity-sponsored portfolio companies in broadcast technology, digital media, and the sports and entertainment industries.

Tampa-based Schellman specializes in information technology compliance services and is the 58th largest private company in Tampa Bay, with $101.9 million in revenue in 2021.

Goldstein's 'deep knowledge of the technology industry combined with his experience in scaling high-growth companies will be a huge asset to Schellman as we enter our next phase of expansion,' Schellman President and CEO Avani Desai said in a statement.
