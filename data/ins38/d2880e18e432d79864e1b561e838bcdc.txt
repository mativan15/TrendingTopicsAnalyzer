Published: 13:50 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 13:55 EDT, 29 May 2022 A teenager has been charged with the murder of an 18-year-old boy who was fatally attacked while walking home from his school prom.Kajetan Migdal, 18, was found with life-threatening injuries in Cutty's Lane, Stevenage, Hertfordshire, by police and paramedics at around 11.20pm on Friday.The schoolboy was rushed 12.4 miles away to the nearby Lister Hospital where despite the efforts of emergency doctors, he was pronounced dead in the early hours of Saturday morning.
