East West Bancorp, PacWest and Preferred Bank results boosted by rate hikes
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-20 23:26:00

Three banks based in Southern California posted their third-quarter earnings this week.

Pasadena, California-based East West Bancorp Inc. (Nasdaq: EWBC), parent company of East West Bank, reported third-quarter net income rose 31% to $295.3 million, or $2.08 per share, from $225.4 million, or $1.57 per share, in the year-earlier period.

Revenue rose 34% to $627 million, while total loans grew 8% to $47.5 billion as of Sept. 30 and deposits increased 1% to $53.9 million.

'This was another quarter of outstanding results.
