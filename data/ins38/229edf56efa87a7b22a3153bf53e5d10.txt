May 27, 2022 03:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BELLEVUE, Wash.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tech Mahindra, a leading provider of digital transformation, consulting, and business re-engineering services and solutions, today announced that it has inaugurated a 5G Lab as an innovation centre to help customers co-create and co-innovate 5G powered solutions. The innovation lab in Bellevue, WA will curate vertical solutions in the telco space to help customers achieve their future business ambitions through 5G and related technology.
