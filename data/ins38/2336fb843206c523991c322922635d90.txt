HCA Healthcare stock falls after profit tops expectations but revenue falls shy, as admissions declined
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 10:42:05

Shares of HCA Healthcare Inc. HCA, -0.28% fell 1.7% in premarket trading Friday, after the hospital operator reported third-quarter profit that topped expectations but revenue that fell shy, as admissions declined. Net income fell $1.34 billion, or $3.91 a share, from $2.27 billion, or $7.00 a share, in the year-ago period. The latest quarter's results include losses on sales of facilities reduced earnings per share by 2 cents. The FactSet EPS consensus was $3.88. Revenue declined 2.0% to $14.97 billion, just below the FactSet consensus of $15.
