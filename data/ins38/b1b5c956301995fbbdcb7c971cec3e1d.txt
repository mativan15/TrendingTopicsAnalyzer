Sands China Falls After Third-Quarter Loss Widened
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 04:36:00

By Clarence Leong Shares of Sands China Ltd. fall in Hong Kong trading after the Macau casino operator reported a wider net loss for the third quarter weighed by strict Covid-19 rules. Sands China was down 4.1% to 14.68 Hong Kong dollars (US$1.87) at mid-day trade Thursday, extending year-to-date losses to 19%. Other casino stocks were also under pressure, with Galaxy Entertainment Group Ltd. down 2.4% and Wynn Macau Ltd. sliding 6.3%. Sands China posted a US$472 million net loss for the third quarter, compared with US$423 million in the year-earlier period, it said in an early Thursday filing to the Hong Kong exchange.
