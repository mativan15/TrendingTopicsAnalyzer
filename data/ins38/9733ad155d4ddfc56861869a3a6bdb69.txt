Why November's starting to look like a ‘red tsunami': Dems have nothing on voters' top concerns
nypost.com
news
2022-10-21 22:24:12

You could sense the giddiness in the press coverage over the summer about the Democrats' chances in the midterms. President Joe Biden will upend historic norms, the cheerleaders said. Pop the champagne and spend some more government money!
The devastating Jan. 6 testimony and the Supreme Court's decision on Roe v. Wade seemed to cement the narrative — Democrats would keep Congress or at least the Senate.
But as Election Day has gotten closer, voters started paying attention and realized that's all Dems had.
