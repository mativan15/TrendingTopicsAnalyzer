OntheRunPhoto/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Investment Thesis ChargePoint Holdings, Inc. (NYSE:CHPT) reported mixed results for FQ1'23, as it posted a solid quarter of revenue beat. However, its adjusted gross margins came under significant pressure due to supply chain disruptions, higher costs, and logistics snarls. Still, the company reiterated its full-year gross margins guidance, as it believes it can execute much better in H2'22 to compensate for Q1's miss. We suggested in our pre-earnings article that we were awaiting a double bottom bear trap market structure in its price action.
