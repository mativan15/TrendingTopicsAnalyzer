Top-seeded Hokies blast six homers, rout Clemson in ACC tourney
timesnews.net
news
2022-05-27 05:15:00

CHARLOTTE, N.C. — Nick Biddison and Carson Jones each hit two of Virginia Tech's six home runs, combining for nine runs batted in and powering the top-seeded Hokies to an 18-6 romp over No. 12 seed Clemson during pool play of the Atlantic Coast Conference baseball tournament Thursday night.Second-ranked Virginia Tech (41-11) will close out pool play against No. 8 seed North Carolina (35-19) in the nightcap on Friday. The winner will earn a spot opposite Notre Dame in Saturday's 1 p.m. semifinal, with that winner advancing to Sunday's noon championship game against either 10 seed North Carolina State (35-20) or 11 seed Pittsburgh (29-26).
