After months of out-of-stock supplies and a generous free attachment offer, Dyson has finally given everyone what they want: A brand new Airwrap. The Dyson Airwrap multi-styler utilizes powerful and precise airflow to deliver wet to dry styling, styling hair from damp (80% dry).Dyson

 The most notable upgrade for the next generation Airwrap is that you can use one tool to curl hair in different directions. Previously, that required two tools that had to be interchanged during styling.
