Rachel Klaver is a marketing strategist, specialising in lead generation and content marketing. OPINION: One of the most common fears I see in my clients and community when it comes to social media for business is this fear of being attacked online. For the most part, it's a fear that will never be realised. The vast majority of social media users will just scroll on by if they see something they don't like. Of course there are particular hot topics that are likely to raise the ire of people who don't know you.
