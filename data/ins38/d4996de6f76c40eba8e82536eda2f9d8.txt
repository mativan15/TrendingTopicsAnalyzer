LIC Q4 net drops 18%, announces its first dividend
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 01:45:00

Life Insurance Corporation of India () reported an 18% drop in standalone net profit for the fiscal fourth quarter, weighed by an increase in paid claims even as net premium income after adjusting for reinsurance costs rose. Standalone net profit at the nation's largest insurer fell to ₹2,372 crore in the quarter ended March 31 from ₹2,893 crore a year earlier. LIC said total claims it paid during the quarter increased 19% to ₹1.21 lakh crore. For the fiscal year ended March 31, net profit increased 39% to ₹4,043 crore from ₹2,901 crore the year before, helped by a ₹1,299 crore provision write-back.
