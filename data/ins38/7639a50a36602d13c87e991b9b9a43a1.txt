Microsoft's willingness to work with others cements its dominance

Mark Zuckerberg, drumming up interest for his digital world in the real one, introduced a special guest during Meta's Connect broadcast last week. 'Hi Mark, it's great to be here with you,' Satya Nadella, Microsoft's chairman and chief executive, said as he appeared on-screen for an unannounced, yet unsurprising, cameo.By enabling users of Meta's Quest virtual reality headsets to access its software — from Teams video calls to Excel spreadsheets, and ultimately, Windows cloud systems and Xbox games — Microsoft placed itself at the heart of one of the biggest sagas rippling through the technology sector.
