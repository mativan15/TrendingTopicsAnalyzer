REUTERS/Francis MascarenhasMUMBAI, Oct 22 (Reuters) - India's Bombay Dyeing and Manufacturing Company Ltd (BDYN.NS) has been barred by the country's capital market regulator from the securities markets for two years.The company, which is part of the Wadia Group and is engaged in the business of real estate, polyester and textiles, is among 10 entities facing penalties for allegedly misrepresenting financial statements.
