Office development is flourishing, especially in East Austin
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 18:45:00

Austin's office market is the envy of much of the nation.

New towers and campuses continue to sprout across the city. The Texas capital remains No. 1 in the nation on the popular "back to work barometer" by Kastle Systems, which tracks office use among the building access company's customers.

Austin Business Journal's latest lists of the metro's largest office buildings and parks reflects the boom. The list for multi-tenant towers added two members this year: the 350,611-square-foot RiverSouth and the 324,510-square-foot Innovation Tower next to Dell Medical School.
