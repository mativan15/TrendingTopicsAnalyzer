COLEEN Rooney is celebrating her bumper payout from Rebekah Vardy by spending a fortune at one of the world's most luxurious hotels.
Coleen, 36, and her family — including football ace husband Wayne — have flown to Dubai to holiday at the five-star Mandarin Oriental Jumeira.
