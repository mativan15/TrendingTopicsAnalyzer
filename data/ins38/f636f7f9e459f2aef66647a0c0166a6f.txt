Donald Trump calls for teachers to be armed (Image: Getty)The former president said the shooting in Uvalde, which also claimed the lives of two teachers, was no reason for stricter gun laws.'The existence of evil in our world is not a reason to disarm law-abiding citizens,' he told the National Rifle Association's annual convention in Houston. Instead, he called for at least one armed police officer on every American playground, with fortified, single points of entry incorporating metal detectors.
