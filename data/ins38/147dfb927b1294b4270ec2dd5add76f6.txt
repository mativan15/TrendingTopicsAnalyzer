nespix Dear Friends and Investors, The Massif Capital Real Assets strategy returned -2.9% net of fees in the third quarter of 2022. Year-to-date, the strategy has returned -4.7%. Returns for our long book fell just under 4.0% for the quarter, while our short book delivered a gain of just over 1.0%. We added short exposure worth 28.0% of the portfolio to the book or 34.0% on a notional basis, inclusive of a synthetic short via options. Two short positions in North American fertilizer businesses were exited for a position-level return of 22.
