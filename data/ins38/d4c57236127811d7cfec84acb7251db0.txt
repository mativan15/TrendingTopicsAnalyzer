Former President Barack Obama said on a recent podcast that he sometimes got into "trouble" for being "too professorial" during his days in the White House.During an episode of "Pod Save America" released on Saturday, the former president said while it was important to explore policy matters, it was critical to do so in a way that would connect with ordinary people."Look, I used to get into trouble whenever I got a little too professorial and started ... when I was behind a podium as opposed to when I was in a crowd.
