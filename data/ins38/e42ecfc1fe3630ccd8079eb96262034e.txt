SC junks Trinamool leader's plea challenging arrest

PTIBhattacharya, a former chairman of the West Bengal Board of Primary Education, was arrested by the federal agency earlier this month. His counsel argued that not a penny was recovered from Bhattacharya, but it was still argued that huge amounts were recovered.The Supreme Court on Thursday dismissed a plea filed by Trinamool Congress leader Manik Bhattacharya, challenging his arrest by the Enforcement Directorate in a money-laundering case linked to the scandal over the recruitment of primary-school teachers in West Bengal.
