TORRANCE, Calif., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Arriving at dealers today, the 2023 Acura RDX has been updated with new standard premium services to augment its position as the most premium, performance-focused and technologically sophisticated 5-passenger SUV in Acura history.For the 2023 model year, the award-winning RDX comes standard with three years of AcuraLink™ services, including Security and Remote packages, at no extra cost.
