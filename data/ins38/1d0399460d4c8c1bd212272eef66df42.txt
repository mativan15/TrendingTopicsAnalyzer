Saipem Shares Soar on $4.5 Bln Contract From Qatargas
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 10:13:00

By Giulia Petroni Saipem SpA's shares soared on Thursday after the company won a contract worth around $4.5 billion from Qatargas Operating Company Limited to support the expansion of natural gas production off the northeast coast of Qatar. At 0958GMT, shares trade 13% higher at EUR0.73. The Milan-based energy services group said late Wednesday that the contract encompasses the engineering, procurement, fabrication and installation of two offshore natural gas compression complexes in the North Field.
