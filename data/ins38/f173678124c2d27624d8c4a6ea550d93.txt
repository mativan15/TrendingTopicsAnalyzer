(Bloomberg) -- Bond investors still reeling from recent turbulence in the UK market are bracing for the next potential flash point: a return to double-digit inflation.

Most Read from Bloomberg

Turmoil over the government's plans for unfunded tax cuts, now abandoned, appears to be subsiding. But gilts are still vulnerable to the prospect of tighter monetary policy if inflation shows no signs of cooling. Economists expect data Wednesday to show annual consumer prices accelerated 10% in September after slowing marginally a month earlier.
