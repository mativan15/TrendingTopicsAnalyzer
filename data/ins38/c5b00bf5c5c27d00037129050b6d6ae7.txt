Money IQ: Picking the winners in the NZ and Australian markets
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-29 17:00:00

In a global environment of high and rising interest rates, alongside a large-scale conflict unfolding in Europe, it is difficult to envy those trying to pick the winners in tumultuous financial markets. But fund managers need to find good investments in both good and volatile markets. We asked three fund managers for their outlook on New Zealand and Australian markets over the next two years. They say there are industries strong enough to push through global volatility, and companies lurking in the NZX that may be diamonds in the rough.
