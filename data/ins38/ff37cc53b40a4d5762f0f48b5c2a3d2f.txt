Most engineers have to do a lot of repetitive manual work. Enterprises frequently create their own programs, tailored to their specific use cases. These programs complement out-of-the-box technology solutions, which generally require custom programming to integrate into the existing system. The result is a cumbersome hodgepodge of apps and tools.
