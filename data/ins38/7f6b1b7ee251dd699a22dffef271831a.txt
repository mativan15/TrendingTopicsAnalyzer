Rugby League World Cup has kickd off with England facing Samoa in Newcastle. Samoa captain Junior Paulo, also called the Parramatta powerhouse, leads his side against England. It is expected that Samoa will win against Shaun Wane's team after an infusion of top New Zealand and Australian talent. Aucklander Paulo says they will not stop at beating England and will win the whole thing. He believes his team has the quality to win the World Cup. Aucklander Paulo says they need to be in it to win it. They want to increase Tonga's lead as they became better with the presence of legendary stars that beat Australis and Great Britain three years back.
