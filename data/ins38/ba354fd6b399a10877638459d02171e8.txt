October 19, 2022 06:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time CINCINNATI--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Procter & Gamble Company (NYSE:PG) reported first quarter fiscal year 2023 net sales of $20.6 billion, an increase of one percent versus the prior year. Excluding the impacts of foreign exchange, acquisitions and divestitures, organic sales increased seven percent. Diluted net earnings per share were $1.57, a decrease of two percent versus prior year EPS.

Operating cash flow was $4.1 billion for the quarter. Adjusted free cash flow productivity was 86%.
