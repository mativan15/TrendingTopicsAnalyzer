Published: 17:01 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 17:01 EDT, 15 October 2022 Pregnant women should avoid any food, drink, face cream or even toothpaste that comes in plastic packaging. That was the alarming advice issued by Italian researchers last week after they found traces of plastic in human breast milk.So-called microplastics, minuscule shreds of the man-made material, were identified in the milk of three-quarters of the mothers studied. In the latest shocking finding concerning microplastics in the human body, the scientists said the women had unknowingly consumed them.
