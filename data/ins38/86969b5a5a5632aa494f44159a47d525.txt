Princess Anne steps up to award Daniel Craig with same honour 007 has
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 10:40:00

Princess Anne awarded Daniel Craig the same honour bestowed upon iconic, and fictional, British spy James Bond, who the actor has played in five instalments of the 007 series. Mr Craig smiled with pride as the Princess Royal placed around his neck the medal, officially making him Companion of the Order of St Michael and St George (CMG) for services to film and theatre. Princess Anne awarded Daniel Craig a CMG this morning (Image: PA) Princess Anne stepped in for King Charles this morning (Image: PA)The ceremony took place this morning at Windsor Castle, during which Princess Anne, representing King Charles III, donned her military uniform.
