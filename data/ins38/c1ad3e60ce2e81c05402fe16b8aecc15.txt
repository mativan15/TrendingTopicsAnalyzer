REUTERS/Christopher PikeOct 20 (Reuters) - Saudi Arabia's stock market ended higher on Thursday, posting its biggest weekly gain since July helped by rising oil prices, although Qatari shares bucked the trend to close lower.Crude prices, a key catalyst for the Gulf's financial markets, rose by more than $1 in response to tighter supplies and on news that China is considering a cut in the duration of quarantine for inbound visitors.
