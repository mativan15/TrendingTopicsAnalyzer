June 02, 2022 08:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cubic Corporation today announced that it had been awarded a subcontract by Carley Corporation under their prime contract for the U.S. Navy's Sailor 2025 Ready, Relevant Learning (RRL) Fiscal Year 2022 (FY22) Content Conversion Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract.



As part of the agreement, with support from Cubic, Carley will deliver training content to include designing, developing, and delivering modernized training content, including courseware training products for multiple Navy ratings.
