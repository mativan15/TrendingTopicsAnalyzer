AgenciesNetflix series 'The Watcher' has just been released. The series was going No.1 till it was realized that the ending is terrible.However, It was later condemned by Netflix viewers. The seven-episode series is based on a true story. The story follows a married couple who find themselves being harassed when they move into a house situated in Westfield, New Jersey. It's a sinister figure known as ‘The Watcher' that causes trouble for the family. At the end of the series, the couple seems to have solved the mystery when they discover that ‘The Watcher' is no one else but Theodora, played by Noma Dumezweni.
