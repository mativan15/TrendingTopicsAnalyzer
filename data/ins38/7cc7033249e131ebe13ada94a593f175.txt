SEOUL, South Korea, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Aqua Farm, a Full 3D P2E game project, announced that the 2nd CBT (Closed Beta Test) held for two weeks (Aug 29 - Sep 9) ended successfully. Aqua Farm is a gaming project published by The Mars Corp., a fast-evolving metaverse platform development and publishing company in the blockchain industry.The Company's core developers are veteran game developers with over 10 years of experience in developing open-world MMORPG games.
