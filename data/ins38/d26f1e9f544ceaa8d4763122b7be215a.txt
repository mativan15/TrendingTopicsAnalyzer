Mom slammed for telling frustrated Americans to emigrate: "Just do it"
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-27 16:09:07

A former U.S. resident has been labeled "tone-deaf" after telling Americans concerned about the direction of the country to "just do it" and emigrate.The woman, who now lives in Norway, offered her advice on Reddit on Wednesday. Her viral post explained that her grandparents were Holocaust survivors who had moved to the U.S. for a better life. She grew up in America but moved to Europe at 21, when she married a Norwegian man."It opened my eyes to how balanced life could be, and what a society is like when equality is taken seriously," she wrote.
