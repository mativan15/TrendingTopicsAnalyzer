 A 32-year-old man has been charged with multiple offences after a homemade explosive device was found in a vehicle in west Edmonton on Sunday.
Police said they were originally called to an address in west Edmonton at around 12:30 p.m. Sunday for reports of an assault.Police said a man was threatening two people at the home, 'demanding that they drive him to purchase drugs.
