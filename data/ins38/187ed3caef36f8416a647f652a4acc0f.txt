 Police are seeking the public's assistance in locating a man wanted in connection with a sexual assault and criminal harassment investigation in Toronto.
Toronto police said on Oct. 17, a 16-year-old girl boarded a bus at Kennedy Subway Station.Officers said while she was on the bus, an unknown man allegedly sexually assaulted her.Police said the girl exited the bus at Brimley Road and Lawrence Avenue East.Officers said the man exited the bus at the same stop and allegedly 'followed her and continued to harass her.
