CSU hauls in 10 awards for writing, CSU Spur marketing and STATE Magazine

Colorado State University and the CSU System cleaned up in this year's awards from the Council for Advancement and Support of Education, collecting 10 district honors for writing, marketing for the CSU Spur campus and STATE Magazine.
'These awards are significant in three ways,' said Greg Luft, interim vice president for marketing and communications, said of the writing awards. 'First, they confirm the great skill and dedication of the writers and producers who dug into these stories, and I can't emphasize that enough.
