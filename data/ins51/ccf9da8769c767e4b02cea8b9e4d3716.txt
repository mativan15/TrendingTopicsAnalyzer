PE Services Appoints Jennifer Hildebrand as the New President/CEO
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 17:03:46

In 2022, Jennifer Hildebrand was appointed the new President/CEO of Professional Engineering Services, Ltd. An experienced business leader, Jennifer will succeed Ann Johnson, company founder. She will continue to guide the company in the direction of serving the needs of construction inspection and materials testing services. Ann Johnson serves as a MN State Senator in District 44 and will maintain her efforts to educate and support legislators in her endeavors as she continues to grow her career in politics.
