HONG KONG, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- FIT Hon Teng (6088.HK) ("FIT" or the "Group") is delighted to have participated in Hon Hai Tech Day 2022 (HHTD22) held in Taipei today. FIT is fully supporting Hon Hai's strategic direction by developing EV products based on its three core pillars of "Power Management, Automotive Networking and Human-Machine Interface" by offering interconnectors and system module components that extend successively across six product categories.

FIT's 'Smart Access' product is amongst the most eye-catching new offering being previewed by exhibitors.
