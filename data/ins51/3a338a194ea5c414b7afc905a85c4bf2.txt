Published: 09:10 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 09:10 EDT, 30 May 2022 Nancy Pelosi's husband Paul was driving his Porsche drunk and pulled out onto the  highway into the path of an 48-year-old man driving a Jeep in Napa, his arrest report reveals. Pelosi, 82, was arrested for the incident after taking a breathalyzer test. DailyMail.com obtained his arrest report which reveals he was driving eastbound on Walnut Lane, a quiet tree-lined street in Oakville, six miles south of the Pelosi's vineyard in St Helena, on Saturday at 10.
