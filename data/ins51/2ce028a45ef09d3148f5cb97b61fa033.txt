Image caption, The firm, formerly known as First Derivatives, has focused on growing subscription sales of its KX software productsNewry-based FD Technologies made a pre-tax profit of £1.1m in the first half of 2022 on sales of £147m.Chief executive Seamus Keating said a strategy of investment to accelerate growth was paying off.The firm, formerly known as First Derivatives, has focused on growing subscription sales of its KX software products.That involved significantly increasing sales and marketing spend which reduced profits.
