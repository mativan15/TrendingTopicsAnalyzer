Baker Mayfield still with the Cleveland Browns, but for how long?
usatoday.com
news
2022-05-27 17:00:20

It wasn't long ago when NFL Network's Ian Rapoport reported that former Oklahoma Sooner Baker Mayfield would be traded the week of the NFL draft.
Here we are a month later, and Mayfield is still, for all intents and purposes, a member of the Cleveland Browns. With more and more details emerging from the Deshaun Watson lawsuits, a suspension from the NFL seems more and more likely.
Of all the teams that needed a long-term answer at QB, the Seattle Seahawks were the only one who didn't bite on any of the prospects that were available in the 2022 draft class.
