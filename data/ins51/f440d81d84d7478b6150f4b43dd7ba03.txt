By Reuters Published: 23:37 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 23:37 EDT, 27 May 2022 Matthew Mayer, who won an NCAA championship during his four seasons at Baylor, is transferring to Illinois. The 6-foot-9 wing announced his new destination Friday on Instagram, writing below a picture of him in a Fighting Illini uniform, "New fit, what y'all think? #committed @illinimbb" Mayer appeared in 126 games for Baylor, all of them as a reserve until he started 33 games last season. He averaged 9.8 points and five rebounds in 2021-22 as the Bears went 27-7 and lost in the second round of the NCAA Tournament to North Carolina.
