By Australian Associated Press Published: 23:45 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 23:45 EDT, 16 October 2022 Wunderkind Garang Kuol has scored a cracking goal for the Young Socceroos as he attempts to secure a spot in Australia's World Cup squad for Qatar. The 18-year-old, who moves to cashed-up Premier League club Newcastle United in January, is currently in Kuwait for the U20 Asian Cup qualifiers. Kuol gave the Young Socceroos a 1-0 lead over India on Tuesday morning (AEDT) when he took possession and unleashed a brilliant finish from outside the box to send the ball flying into the top corner.
