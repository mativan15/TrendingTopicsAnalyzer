Ryanair Says Boeing Delays Threaten Plan to Expand During Slump
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-18 12:28:50

(Bloomberg) -- Ryanair Holdings Plc Chief Executive Officer Michael O'Leary said the discount carrier's ability to gain market share from rivals in a looming economic slowdown is at risk from delays to Boeing Co. jetliner deliveries.O'Leary said Tuesday he's concerned the US planemaker may ship only 12 or 13 of the 21 Max aircraft that Ryanair is due to get before Christmas, leaving close to 40 to be delivered by the start of the summer peak in late May.'Boeing is a challenge,' the CEO said at a briefing in Rome.
