Cost of living: Residents in Plymouth 'constantly worrying' amid rocketing bills and food prices

2022-05-29 11:56:00

From fishermen to pensioners, food clubs to families - Sky News brings you a series of reports this week from the maritime city of Plymouth as the cost of living continues to rise.In the first of three reports, we investigate how increasing bills are impacting some of the 262,000 people that call Plymouth home.
"How often is money on your mind?" I ask a full-time working mum of three, Lara Thomas.She replies, instantly: "All the time. I worry about money morning, noon and night."Ms Thomas and her children live in a flat in one of poorest parts of Plymouth, Devonport.
