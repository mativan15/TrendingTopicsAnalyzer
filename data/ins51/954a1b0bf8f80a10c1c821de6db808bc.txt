Sinch aims to offer locally-developed capabilities in global markets from FY23: India MD
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 03:00:00

NEW DELHI: Cloud communications provider Sinch is bullish on the India market on the back of increased demand for digital communications and 5G that will support more two-way conversational channels. It is also looking to offer locally developed capabilities to companies globally working in verticals such as BFSI, retail, and e-commerce, starting from FY23. 'I would certainly say that some of the capabilities that we have brought or developed in India will be exported or will be given out to other Sinch mobile companies.
