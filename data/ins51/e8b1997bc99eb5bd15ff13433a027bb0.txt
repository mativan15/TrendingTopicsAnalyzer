ETMarkets.comEconomic reforms that could drive up the per capita income, social development parameters, and resilience in trade relationships will be essential to sustain this premium, says Pawaskar.The aggressive policy measures by global central banks to tame inflation and concerns of recession in developed economies have roiled equities globally and prompted FIIs to exit Indian markets in droves, marking record outflows in 2022. Despite this, Indian equities have fared far better than their peers and remained a premium market.
