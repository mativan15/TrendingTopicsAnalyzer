After keeping a low profile for almost a year, Bollywood actor of Sri Lankan origin, Jacqueline Fernandez, on Friday, made a rare public appearance at an event in Mumbai. The Ram Setu actor was recently granted interim bail in the Rs 200 crore money laundering case against conman Sukesh Chandrashekar.
Jacqueline showed up in a radiant turmeric yellow saree and let her hair down. The diva opted for light rosy nude make-up and minimal jewellery for the event.
