Image source, Everton Football ClubImage caption, Work is under way to build the new stadium at Bramley Moore DockLiverpool City Council is to lose more than £300,000 in fees over a proposed loan to Everton for a new stadium.Everton will pay £502,000 of the £841,000 incurred by the council in due diligence while drawing up a funding plan for the Bramley Moore project.The club eventually secured funding from alternative sources.In a joint statement, the club and council said an "amicable settlement" had been reached, which was "reasonable and proportionate".
