Cong losing, BJP is growing: Singh
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 07:15:00

Hubballi: The Congress was losing ground because of Rahul Gandhi , while the BJP, led by Narendra Modi , was growing massively in the country, said Arun Singh, the BJP national general secretary and Karnataka in-charge.Singh was speaking after inaugurating the Sankalpa Yatra organised by the party at Gurusiddeshwar Kalyan Mantap Bommapur Oni in Hubballi on Sunday.He said the BJP government had come to power three times in Goa. The Congress, on the other hand, had lost in Manipur. Now, the BJP will succeed in Himachal Pradesh and Gujarat.
