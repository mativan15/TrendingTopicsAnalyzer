Two dead & 19 hurt after cars slam into crowds at Memorial Day weekend rally
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-05-30 07:54:33

AT least two people have died while 19 have been injured after several cars slammed into crowds at a Memorial Day weekend rally in Nebraska, according to reports.
Cops rushed to the scene following the collision that took place near the Americruise event in Lincoln on Sunday.
At least two people have died after cars slammed into a crowd in Lincoln, Nebraska (stock pic)
Officials at the Lincoln Police Department said two women have died.
They were in one of the vehicles involved in the crash, police said.
