(LEAD) Ex-presidential chief of staff questioned in N. Korean fishermen repatriation case(ATTN: UPDATES with more details from para 5) SEOUL, Oct. 19 (Yonhap) -- Prosecutors on Wednesday questioned former presidential chief of staff Noh Young-min as part of an investigation into the previous Moon Jae-in government's repatriation of two North Korean fishermen in 2019. Prosecutors are looking into the case on suspicions that the Moon government deported the two North Korean fishermen back home despite their wishes to defect to South Korea.
