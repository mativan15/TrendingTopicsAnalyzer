October 18, 2022 08:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--2bcloud, a leading multi-cloud cost optimization managed service provider for cloud natives from early stage to unicorns, today announces it significantly expanded U.S. operations with new teams in California and New York. Founded in 2018, 2bcloud is an award-winning Amazon Web Services (AWS) Advanced Consulting Partner and a Microsoft Azure MSP Expert Cloud Partner that helps startups achieve their cloud vision and business value.
