Electrify Expo, North America's largest electric-vehicle festival, kicks off the first of five electric vehicle events of 2022 beginning in Long Beach, CA, to showcase the best in EV technology. From e-bikes, e-motorcycles, e-skateboards, and e-scooters to the latest in Electric Cars, consumers will be able to ride, drive and demo the newest tech in electric mobility. As the first stop on the festival tour, Long Beach provides a first look at exciting new electric tech from the world's leading brands.
