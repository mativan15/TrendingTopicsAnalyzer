October 21, 2022 12:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time WAKEFIELD, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Franklin Street Properties Corp. (the 'Company' or 'FSP') (NYSE American: FSP), a real estate investment trust (REIT), announced today that it expects to release its results for the third quarter 2022 after the market closes on Tuesday, November 1, 2022. The Company will hold a conference call/webcast with the investment community to discuss the results at 11:00 AM ET on Wednesday morning, November 2, 2022.
