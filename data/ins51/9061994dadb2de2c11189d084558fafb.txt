Fans have been anticipating God of War: Ragnarok since the release of the last game of the franchise, God of War, landed on consoles in 2018. There's good reason for this: It's fun as hell, and everyone wants to visit grumpy Kratos and his band of friends again.Without question, I am one of those fans. There's just one problem: I never finished God of War.For a lot of people, this might not be an issue. If you're screaming 'Just dive in and experience the new one!' at your screen right now, I get it.
