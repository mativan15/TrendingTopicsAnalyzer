Russia restricts exports of Helium & Neon sourced by Western & E Asian microchip firms
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 12:00:00

ReutersThe restrictions were a response to the ban on supplying Russia with semiconductors necessary for the production of microchips.Russia has restricted the export of gases (Helium, Neon etc) necessary for the production of microchips and this could adversely impact firms in USA, Japan, South Korea and Netherlands among others. The deliveries will now be carried out only by the decision of the Government of the Russian Federation, according to a resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers. The restrictions were a response to the ban on supplying Russia with semiconductors necessary for the production of microchips.
