Some financial advisers grow up in the business. They see a parent succeed in the financial industry and follow a well-marked path. That isn't Matthew McKay's story.Raised in a family beset by financial insecurity, McKay experienced his share of obstacles. His father battled drug addiction and ran afoul of the law. McKay's parents divorced when he was six years old. His mother worked long hours in a factory job. Putting food on the table for McKay and his brother was a constant struggle. 'She did the best she could with the resources she had,' McKay said.
