IT services fresher hiring in H1 exceeds same period last year
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 17:45:00

Getty ImagesHiring intent refers to hiring plans a company/sector has for a certain time period. So when one says 65% hiring intent, it means 65% companies in that sector have conveyed that they plan to hire people in a particular quarter.India's top four IT service providers have onboarded over 105,000 freshers during the first half of the fiscal year despite forecasting lower than last year's numbers earlier. This is higher than the 97,000 freshers onboarded during the same period last year. As existing junior employees become billable at a time of macroeconomic uncertainty, companies are looking to improve utilization levels and doubling down on training programmes while reducing dependency on lateral hiring.
