Two-wheeler knocks down pedestrian in Gummidipoondi
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-16 14:03:06

A 55-year-old man was killed in a hit-and-run involving a two-wheeler near Gummidipoondi SIPCOT Industrial Complex.  The police said Radhay Mochi of Sindhala Kuppam, who works in a private factory at the SIPCOT industrial complex, was returning home from work on the service road when a two-wheeler hit him on Friday night. He was taken to the Gummidipoondi Government Hospital where he died on Saturday. The SIPCOT police of Tiruvallur district have launched a search for the two-wheeler.
