Oil companies should raise dealers' commission: Petrol pump association
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-27 09:00:00

Oil companies should raise dealers' commission on the sale of petrol and diesel to 5% of retail price as operating costs have risen, All India Petrol Pump Dealers Association (AIPDA) has said. Dealers' commission is decided by a five-year-old formula that has fixed as well as variable components.On average, dealers are currently getting a commission of Rs 3.78 per litre of petrol in Delhi.
