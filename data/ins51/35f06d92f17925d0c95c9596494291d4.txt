Continue reading the main storyAs stocks have tumbled this year, predictions that the selling is over have been wrong time and again.Credit...Angelica AlzonaMay 31, 2022, 3:00 a.m. ETThe stock market's staggering run of losses came to an end last week, with the S&P 500 snapping a seven-week losing streak and pulling away from the brink of a bear market with a 6.6 percent bounce through Friday.But the concerns that drove Wall Street's panic this year remain unresolved. It's far too soon to know if skyrocketing consumer prices have peaked, if the Federal Reserve has charted the right path for interest rates, or how well the economy will be able to hold up in the face of fast inflation and rising borrowing costs.
