Film students at the Academy of Interactive Education (AIE) in Canberra are now able to use technology seen on productions such as The Mandalorian and Thor: Love and Thunder.The AIE's StageCraft course is the first to exist in the worldThe technology displays backgrounds that move as the camera doesTeacher Dan Sanguineti says the course focus is hands-on experienceThe non-profit vocational education provider has begun offering a world first course using an immersive visual effects technology called StageCraft, an alternative to a green screen.
