Dallas, Texas, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Market experts and latest studies anticipate that the global Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality products and services market revenue may reach $80.60 billion by 2029 and grow at an annualized rate of 31.7% through the forecast period 2022-2029. Rising interest in augmented and virtual reality devices and applications in healthcare is driving market growth. Augmented reality and virtual reality participates a vital role in the healthcare industry.
