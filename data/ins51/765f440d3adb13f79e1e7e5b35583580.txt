City economist Simon French has given it a name: the dullness dividend. That is the value that investors would place on a vaguely competent, boring even, Prime Minister leading a stable Government with economic policies that add up.We are so far from that currently it is painful. Meanwhile, as we reveal in these pages today, the pound is now seen as so flaky that investors prefer to flip into volatile Bitcoin as a 'safe haven.' This is a pattern that has only been seen before with some of the world's most fragile currencies such as the Turkish lira.
