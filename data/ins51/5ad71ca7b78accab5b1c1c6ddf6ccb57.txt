TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--Oragin Foods Inc. (' ORAGIN ' or the ' Company ') ( TSXV: OG ) ( OTCQX: OGGFF ) ( FRA: 9CW ) is a Canadian multi-faceted food company operating a Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) Division where new and innovative food and beverage brands are developed, acquired, grown and commercialized as well as a Retail Division that operates one of Canada's leading natural and organic food retailers, Organic Garage. The Company is pleased to announce its financial results for the quarter and year-ended January 31, 2022 ('Q4').
