Meta Platforms CEO Mark Zuckerberg acknowledges that he 'sort of missed' some things in the social media sphere and calls TikTok 'a very effective competitor.'

Mark Zuckerberg is beginning to see the light.

The Meta Platforms (META) - Get Meta Platforms Inc. Report CEO has been having a rough time since he turned his attention toward the metaverse, a network of 3D virtual worlds.

He went so far as to change his parent company's name to Meta Platforms from Facebook. He then bought the Oculus Quest VR headset, rebranded it Meta Quest, and formed Reality Labs solely to work on all projects related to the metaverse.
