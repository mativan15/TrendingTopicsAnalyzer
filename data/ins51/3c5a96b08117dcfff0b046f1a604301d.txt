India's gross domestic product (GDP) grew 8.7% in 2021-22, a tad lower than the 8.9% officially estimated earlier, with growth slipping to 4.1% in the last quarter from 5.4% in the third quarter, as per provisional national income estimates released on May 31, 2022.Output from India's eight core sectors grew at 8.4% in April 2022, the fastest pace in six months, with crude oil and steel the only sectors to record a marginal decline in production compared to April 2021.
