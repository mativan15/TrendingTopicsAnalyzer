As Mercedes-Benz has steadily expanded its EQ range of electric cars, the lineup has become a bit like the late stages of a Tetris game. It's mostly complete, but with a few gaps still left. Mercedes recently launched two other electric SUVs at opposite ends of the price spectrum. The EQS SUV is positioned as the lineup's flagship, while the EQB is the entry-level model. The EQE SUV slots between those two in size and, presumably, price.
