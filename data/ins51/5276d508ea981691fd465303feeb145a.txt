AM Best to Participate in Society of Actuaries' 2022 Health Meeting

OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best will join a session on navigating the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic at the Society of Actuaries' Health Meeting, which will be held June 12-15, 2022, in Philadelphia, PA.

AM Best Director Doniella Pliss will review how companies are evolving their financial and capital management practices amid the pandemic to achieve strategic goals during the panel discussion, which is titled, 'COVID-19: Financial Impacts and Re-shaping Risk and Capital Management Practices.
