Apple iPhone 14 Pro and iPhone 14 Pro Max beat Google Pixel 7, Pixel 7 Pro and others in battery test.
 Good news for Apple fans! Apple's newest iPhone 14 Pro Max has set a benchmark in a battery test by outperforming the Android flagship phones. The battery comparison has been conducted between iPhone 14 Pro Max and some of the popular flagship phones like Google Pixel 7, Pixel 7 Pro, Pixel 6 Pro and Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra. The battery test was conducted at PhoneArena where the phone was put through a simulator.
