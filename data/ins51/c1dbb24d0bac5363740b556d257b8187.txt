By Australian Associated Press Published: 23:05 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 23:05 EDT, 14 October 2022 Luke Keary would have been filled with regret had he opted to stay home in Sydney instead of scratching an itch to spearhead Ireland's Rugby League World Cup campaign. The Sydney Roosters five-eighth is the star attraction in the Ireland side, who open their campaign against Jamaica in Leeds on Sunday (Monday AEDT) before further group games against Lebanon and New Zealand. The Roosters five-eighth had a turbulent 2022 season after returning from an ACL injury.
