Variety - TV News Asacha Media Group, the expanding pan-European company, is partnering up with Spain's Plano a Plano and Mexico's Dopamine on 'Cicatriz,' a high-end action thriller based on Juan Gómez-Jurado's bestselling novel.'Cicatriz' is developed by a pair of established writers, Pablo Roa and Fernando Sancristóbal, whose credits include 'Toy Boy,' 'La Casa de Papel' and 'Vis a Vis.'The high-concept series revolves around a brilliant but lonely entrepreneur on the verge of making his millions, who meets a young woman hell-bent on a long-planned revenge mission.
