The real-life family who inspired Netflix's "The Watcher" said they won't be viewing the show.

In comments to several outlets, the Broaddus family said that even watching the trailer was stressful.

Naomi Watts and Bobby Cannavale play fictionalized versions of Maria and Derek Broaddus on the show.

The family that inspired Netflix's chilling new series "The Watcher" revealed in comments to several publications that they won't be bingeing the drama.

The Broadduses declined to comment when reached by ABC News for a segment on a New York affiliate's Tuesday afternoon broadcast, but according to Entertainment Weekly, ABC correspondent Eva Pilgrim noted: "We've been told they have no plans to watch the show; the trailer was traumatizing enough.
