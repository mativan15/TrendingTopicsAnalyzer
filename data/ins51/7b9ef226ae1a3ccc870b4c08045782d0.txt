A testy courtroom exchange between rapper Cardi B and the lawyer suing her for copyright infringement became so contentious that the judge threatened a mistrial.U.S. District Judge Cormac Carney sent jurors out of his Santa Ana, Calif. courtroom Wednesday, announced he was considering a mistrial and said the lawyer's cross-examination of the rapper "totally crossed the line."He said the bickering wasn't "productive. It's unprofessional."
