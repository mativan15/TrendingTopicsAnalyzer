New York deli sign warning impatient customers praised online: "Respect"
newsweek.com
entertainment
2022-06-02 11:00:01

A deli that is telling customers to be patient or leave has earned praise on Reddit for its frank policy.In a viral post shared in the 550,000-strong group r/WorkReform, which can be seen here, Reddit user u/yhl-cis included a photo of the deli's frank message to potential customers.The sign at Dante's Deli in White Plains, New York, read: "To all new customers: I take pride in making a sandwich. My staff and I do not rush. This concept has been working for over 60 years at Dante's. If you have no patience, please leave.
