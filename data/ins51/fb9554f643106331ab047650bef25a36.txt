Published: 03:23 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 03:23 EDT, 2 June 2022 By Yousef Saba DUBAI, June 2 (Reuters) - Starzplay Arabia, an Abu Dhabi-based streaming platform, expects to go public in the next two or three years, its chief executive said on Thursday. Starzplay is among the global and regional streaming players vying for a share of the burgeoning Middle East market, hoping to tap into the region's large youth population and take advantage of low penetration. Netflix leads in the region, having had a head start of more than six years.
