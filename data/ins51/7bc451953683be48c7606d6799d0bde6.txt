Should You Buy or Build Your Gaming PC? - Budget to Best

It's a question brought up every year when shiny new PC parts come through the pipeline: "Should I buy a prebuilt PC or should I build my own?" Once upon a time, the answer was easy! Nowadays, however, prebuilt PC manufacturers have stepped up their game, offering very powerful, and very beautiful PC builds for only a bit more of your money and almost none of your time. The rise of global chip supply shortages in the last couple of years, it's made that decision even tougher.
