Caleb Richter, CEO of MyEListing.com getty War is complicated, messy and multilayered. Even when investors attempt to focus on cold, hard facts, it's not easy when you have to digest the intricacies and tragedies that it brings. With all eyes on Ukraine, it makes sense to probe deeper into how war affects commercial real estate (CRE), both here and far away, and what decisions CRE investors can make during times of turmoil. Overall, armed, global conflict tends to redirect economic output to wartime efforts instead of everyday consumer goods and services.
