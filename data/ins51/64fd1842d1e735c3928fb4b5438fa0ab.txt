krblokhin/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Executive Summary Investors looking to shelter from inflationary pressures, while still enjoying the benefits of safe long-term compounding, should consider Linde plc (NYSE:NYSE:LIN). This industrial-gases multinational enjoys an undisputedly rich corporate history and a market-leading core return on capital that will take its growth investments to the next level. The merger between Linde AG and Praxair has left the new Linde with pretty much all of the latter's assets, a move which the management team hasn't so far regretted.
