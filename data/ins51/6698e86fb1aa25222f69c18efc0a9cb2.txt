GILBERT, Ariz., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Footprint, a global materials science technology company focused on creating a healthier planet through the design, development and advanced manufacturing of plant-based biodegradable, compostable and recyclable solutions to replace single- and short-term use plastics, today reported a strong start to 2022 with record revenue growth and expanded capacity.
