New Jersey high school football coach suspended after appeal hearing over recruiting
Copyright 2022, GANNETT
news
2022-10-19 17:50:33

ROBBINSVILLE, N.J. – The New Jersey State Interscholastic Athletic Association executive committee amended the controversies committee's punishments against Paramus Catholic for recruiting, suspending Paladins head coach Greg Russo for the first four games of next season and suspending assistant coach Christian Maldanado until the end of the school's probationary period.Last month, the controversies committee suspended the Paramus Catholic (New Jersey) football program from this year's postseason and placed the program on probation for two years.
