Chairman of Karnataka Fisheries Development Corporation (KFDC) A.V. Theertarama said here on Wednesday that a feasibility survey has been taken up on a proposal to construct a fisheries harbour in Ankola of Uttara Kannada.Addressing presspersons, he said that the construction work might require ₹6 crore. The fisheries port in Karwar is being re-developed at a cost of ₹250 crore, he added.
