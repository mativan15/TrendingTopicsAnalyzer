Major stock indexes rose for the week for the first time in months
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-05-27 20:23:14

Stocks rose on Wall Street Friday and closed higher for the week, breaking a seven-week losing streak, the longest such stretch since 2001.The S&P 500 rose and notched the biggest weekly gain for the benchmark index since November 2020. Technology stocks were a big factor pushing the market higher. That sent the Nasdaq composite higher, while the Dow Jones Industrial Average also rose significantly. Retailers also made solid gains.On Friday:The S&P 500 rose 100.40 points, or 2.5%, to 4,158.24.The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 575.
