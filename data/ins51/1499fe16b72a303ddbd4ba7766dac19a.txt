Image source, ReutersImage caption, Detectives have warned some of the fireworks could be 'extremely dangerous'A large quantity of fireworks have been stolen from a business in Leicestershire, prompting a police warning.Detectives say the burglary took place at a locked storage unit in Newton Linford Lane, Groby, between 07:00 and 17:00 BST on 13 October.Police said some of the fireworks were "extremely dangerous" and can only be used safely with electronic devices.The force is appealing for anyone with information to come forward.
