NewSpace New Mexico program to help aerospace companies blast off
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 14:00:00

See Correction/Clarification at end of article

Aerospace is a burgeoning industry in New Mexico, and a new development program hopes to propel companies even higher.

Eighteen companies are set to receive technical support, capital resources, mentoring and networking through the NewSpace New Mexico Ignitor program, which launched today. The companies will have access to different services depending on their "readiness levels," said Casey DeRaad, NewSpace New Mexico founder and CEO.
