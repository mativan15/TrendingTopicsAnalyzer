By Jessica Grobstick October 19, 2022 / 2:50 PM / AP TAMPA, Fla. - Tampa Bay Lightning lost 3-2 Tuesday night against the Philadelphia Flyers, Carter Hart stopped 37 shots and Noah Cates scored a tiebreaking goal in the third period.Hart made a pad save on a breakaway by Brayden Point in the first period and improved to 1-5-1 against Tampa Bay.Cates completed the rally from a two-goal deficit by scoring from the slot after a turnover at 12:44 of the third."Carter was outstanding," Flyers coach John Tortorella said.
