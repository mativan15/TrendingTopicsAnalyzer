Winnebago CEO Michael Happe named Minneapolis/St. Paul Business Journal's 2023 Executive of the Year
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-17 16:34:00

Michael Happe, president and CEO of Winnebago Industries Inc., is the Minneapolis/St. Paul Business Journal's 2023 Executive of the Year.

Happe

Business Journal editors selected Happe as the 2023 honoree, in part, because of his transformative leadership at the manufacturing company.

Happe joined Winnebago (NYSE: WGO) as president and CEO in January 2016. At the time, the company was headquartered in Forest City, Iowa, with manufacturing facilities and warehouses in Iowa and Indiana. Its annual revenue was $976.
