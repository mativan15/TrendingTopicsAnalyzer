CCAGW PAC Endorses Oklahoma Sen. James Lankford and Rep. Markwayne Mullin for Senate and Two House Candidates
yahoo.com

2022-10-19 20:36:00

WASHINGTON, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Sen. James Lankford (R-Okla.) for re-election to the U.S. Senate and Rep. Markwayne Mullin (R-Okla.) for election to the U.S. Senate, and Reps. Stephanie Bice (R-Okla.) and Kevin Hern (R-Okla.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.

CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.
