Bollywood actor Saif Ali Khan is a no-filter person. What he feels is mostly on his lips and maybe that's why he avoids being on social media. Because it is known to the birds that if one is on social media, is a celebrity, and has an opinion, shall surely call for a controversy. In a recent interaction with Kapil Sharma, Saif revealed what he thinks of social media and what is the dilemma that stops him from being there.
