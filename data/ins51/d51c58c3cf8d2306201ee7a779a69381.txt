Multistudio and HNTB chosen for KU's Memorial Stadium renovations
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 11:51:00

The University of Kansas recently announced a transformational project, including renovations of the David Booth Kansas Memorial Stadium and other football facilities, and two Kansas City-based design firms will partner with the university on the development.

KU selected Multistudio and HNTB Corp. to transform the 11 th and Mississippi corridor with a number of new facilities, including conference and entertainment space, retail, renovations to the Anderson Family Football Complex and major upgrades to Memorial Stadium, one of the nation's oldest football venues, according to KU Athletics, bringing Kansas Football facilities up to par to compete at the highest level.
