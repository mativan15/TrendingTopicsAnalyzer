From Sylvester to Siouxsie: Director Gwen Isaac gets ready to launch new doco
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-15 16:00:00

Gazing through a viewfinder as Sylvester Stallone set fire to a tract of Thai jungle was not exactly where Gwen Isaac expected to find herself as a first-time mother. Nor was breastfeeding her 6-month-old inches from a baby cobra nest (not deliberately of course - a crew member told her later). Dauntless as any good documentary-maker must be, this was a stretch even for Isaac. "One bite from them, well, no antidote would have saved me." Sly Stallone in the final throes of Rambo, wrangling a wobbly-throwing Paris Hilton in Guatemala, Rod Stewart insisting she looked just like his former wife Rachel Hunter; Isaac had her share of celebrity face-time.
