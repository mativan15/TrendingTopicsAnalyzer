SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The global healthcare supply chain management market size is expected to reach USD 5.2 billion by 2030 and is expected to expand at a CAGR of 9.2% from 2022 to 2030, according to a new report by Grand View Research, Inc. The major factors contributing to the industry growth are the growing adoption of such platforms by healthcare providers to improve efficiency and enhance operational affordability. There is a growing demand for RFID systems by medical device manufacturers, pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical companies, hospitals, and other life sciences companies to control inventory costs in developed countries, which further fuels market growth.
