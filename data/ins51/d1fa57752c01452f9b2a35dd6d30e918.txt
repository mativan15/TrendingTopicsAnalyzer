Kashmir joins the Indian Dominion

2022-10-21 17:50:17

The Maharaja of Kashmir acceded to the Indian Dominion on October 27, 1947, declaring to Lord Mountbatten in a letter that the other alternative, in the face of Pakistan's clandestine actions, 'is to leave my state and my people to freebooters'. Accepting the accession, Lord Mountbatten stated that 'the question should be settled by a reference to the people', adding that Indian troops had been sent to defend the state.Edited excerpts of the correspondence:From the Maharaja of Kashmir:My dear Lord Mountbatten,I have to inform your Excellency that a grave emergency has arisen in my state and request the immediate assistance of your government.
