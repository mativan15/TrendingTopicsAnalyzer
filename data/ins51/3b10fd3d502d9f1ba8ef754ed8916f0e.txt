RH Raises Share Repurchase Authorization by $2B
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 20:27:00

By Stephen Nakrosis RH on Thursday said its board authorized an additional $2 billion for its common share repurchase program. The authorization is effective immediately and is in addition to a prior share repurchase plan which has $450 million remaining, RH said. Gary Friedman, the company's chairman and chief executive, said "we continue to believe our shares are undervalued and this new repurchase authorization reflects our confidence in the outlook for our business over time." RH has a market cap of $7.
