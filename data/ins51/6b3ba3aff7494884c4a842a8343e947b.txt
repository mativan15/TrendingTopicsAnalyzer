Kate Garraway gives her candid verdict on political 'muppet show'

Kate Garraway, 55, has battled the trauma of her long Covid suffering husband Derek Draper's health setbacks, and on top of that has been aghast at the current state of British politics. However the Good Morning Britain star could scarcely resist a smile when she saw Liz Truss and ex-Chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng presented as Muppet Show characters, joking: "Laugh, or you'd cry!" It's like a kind of torture, to have to watch the show...The Muppets Show theme tuneThe video, posted by @Exiled_Sinners on Twitter, featured Kermit the Frog as a politician giving a speech at Number 10 Downing Street.
