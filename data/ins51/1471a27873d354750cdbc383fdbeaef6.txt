On Aug. 30, Prime Minister Justin Trudeau publicly condemned Laith Marouf's tweets and said the government had acted swiftly.Adrian Wyld/The Canadian PressThe Prime Minister's Office knew about an anti-racism consultant's derogatory tweets about 'Jewish white supremacists' a month before the government cancelled a $133,000 contract in which he played a key role.The consultant, Laith Marouf, is an expert for the Community Media Advocacy Centre (CMAC), a non-profit that was awarded the contract earlier this year.
