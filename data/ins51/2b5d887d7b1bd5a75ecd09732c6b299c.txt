October 19, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--WD-40 Company (NASDAQ:WDFC), a global marketing organization dedicated to creating positive lasting memories by developing and selling products that solve problems in workshops, factories and homes around the world, today reported financial results for its fourth quarter and fiscal year ended August 31, 2022.



Financial Highlights and Summary


Total net sales for the fourth quarter were $130.4 million, an increase of 13 percent compared to the prior year fiscal quarter.
