Toronto Stocks Fall; Talon Metals Surges 19% As Infrastructure Project Recipient -- Market Talk
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 22:20:00

18:20 ET--Canadian stocks are slightly lower, with the benchmark S&P/TSX Composite Index edging down 0.7% to 18674 and the blue-chip S&P TSX 60 Index slipping 0.5% to 1131. The S&P/TSX Index is lifted by companies in the energy sector. Talon Metals surges 19% to 59 Canadian cents after the company said its subsidiary Talon Nickel was selected as a recipient of the first set of projects funded by President Biden's Bipartisan Infrastructure Law to expand domestic manufacturing of batteries for electric vehicles and the electrical grid as well as for materials and components currently imported from other countries.
