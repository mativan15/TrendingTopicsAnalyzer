June 01, 2022 03:54 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bitwise Index Services, the indexing subsidiary of Bitwise Asset Management, today announced the results of the monthly reconstitution of the Bitwise Crypto Indexes, which took place on May 31, 2022, at 4pm ET.

There was one change to the constituents of the Bitwise 10 Large Cap Crypto Index as a result of the May 31, 2022 reconstitution: Bitcoin Cash re-entered the index, replacing Cosmos. As of May 31, 2022, at 4pm ET, the Bitwise 10 Large Cap Crypto Index held the following constituents: 66.
