Image source, Martin Giles/BBCImage caption, Before the war Viktoriia (pictured left, with her son and host) lived in a community near the Ukrainian capitalA businesswoman who has hosted a Ukrainian family that fled the war-torn country said the experience had "enriched" her life.Catherine Tompkins, of Whittlesford, near Cambridge, is one of thousands of people who have agreed to host refugees.Viktoriia, and her daughter Ewelina and son Dmytro, will live with Ms Tompkins for at least six months.Ms Tompkins said the three Ukrainians were now "part of the family".
