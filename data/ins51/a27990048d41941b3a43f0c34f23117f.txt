Independent Senate candidate Evan McMullin on Sunday criticized his opponent, GOP Sen. Mike Lee, over his voting record during his two terms in the upper chamber — while also reaffirming his commitment not to caucus with either major political party if he wins the general election.During an interview with Chuck Todd on NBC's "Meet the Press," McMullin — an ex-CIA officer and former 2016 presidential candidate — said that he would retain his independence if chosen by Utah voters to serve in the Senate.
