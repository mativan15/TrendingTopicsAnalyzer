Video: Russian soldier shows 'awful' camp condition, says half of men sick
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-16 22:30:32

A video shared to social media on Sunday purports to show the "grim" conditions that newly drafted Russian soldiers are facing.The video was shared on Twitter Sunday afternoon by Julia Davis, the creator of the media watchdog group, Russian Media Monitor. In it, an unknown man who was recently drafted into the Russian military claims to face extremely poor conditions at a camp, with theft and sickness rampant."Meanwhile in Russia: take a look at the way the newly-mobilized recruits are being treated," Davis wrote alongside the video.
