 Emergency crews responded Thursday to what police said was a fatal collision on a major south Edmonton road.
The crash happened mid-afternoon on Gateway Boulevard northbound, just south of the Ellerslie Road overpass.Alberta Health Services said a man in his 60s was pronounced dead at the scene.
