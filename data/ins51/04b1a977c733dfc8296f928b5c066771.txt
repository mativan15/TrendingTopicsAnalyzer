Florida man found dead in gator-ridden lake after searching for Frisbee
foxnews.com
news
2022-06-01 16:03:55

Published June 1, 2022 12:03pm EDT A Florida man was fatally attacked by an alligator Tuesday while looking for a Frisbee in a gator-filled lake near a disc golf course at John S. Taylor Park.The unidentified victim, 47, went on a hunt for Frisbees where signs reportedly warn people not to swim in the lake.Ken Hostnick, 56, who visits the course often, says it is common for people to dive in the lake to find lost discs in order to make some money, despite the warning signs."These are people that are down on their luck," Hostnick 56, told the Tampa Bay Times.
