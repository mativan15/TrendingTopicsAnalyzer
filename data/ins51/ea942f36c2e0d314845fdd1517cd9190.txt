Smartphone owners downloaded a record-high 38.7 billion apps in the third quarter of 2022, according to mobile intelligence firm Data.ai, while spending cooled a bit. In-app purchases totaled $32.4 billion last quarter, down from the all-time high of $34 billion a year ago. But that's still up 40%, Data.ai says, from pre-pandemic levels. Instagram beat out TikTok to snatch the title of most-downloaded-app, while the-company-formerly-known-as-Facebook captured four of the top six spots and five of the top ten.
