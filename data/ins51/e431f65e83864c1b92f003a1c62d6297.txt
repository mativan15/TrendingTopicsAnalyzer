Copenhagen, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Announcement no. 8-2022

Inside Information





The SaaS company Hypefactors, which delivers a unified solution for media intelligence, reputation and trust tracking, will launch its Names of Interest (NOI) technology on 16th of June 2022. NOI tech is an implementation of Hypefactors' proprietary AI-based Named Entity Recognition (NER), which is now taken to a new level of precision and scale with NOI. NOIs are the media equivalent of ‘points of interests' used in global navigation tech.
