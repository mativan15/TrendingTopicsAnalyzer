Oct. 15—Dance with who brung you.

That's Cal Quantrill's mindset as he prepares to pitch in an elimination game in Game 4 of the American League Divisional Series on Oct. 16 at Progressive Field.

At first glance, one might think Quantrill would be up for a little tinkering after he was the losing pitcher in the Guardians' 4-1 loss to the Yankees in Game 1 of the series earlier this week. But as a creature of habit — he'd prefer not to consider himself superstitious — Quantrill isn't about to deviate much from the game plan that allowed him to go 15-5 with a 3.
