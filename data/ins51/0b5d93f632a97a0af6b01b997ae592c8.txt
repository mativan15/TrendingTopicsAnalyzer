May 31, 2022 08:08 AM Eastern Daylight Time EL SEGUNDO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Impulse Space – a Space 2.0 pioneer providing agile, economical last-mile space payload delivery capabilities – is pleased to announce its appointment of Aaron Mitchell to director of product management. Previously the chief of product at Momentus, Mitchell brings years of experience revolutionizing in-space transportation and will be pivotal to Impulse's goal of accelerating product planning for the upcoming years.



'Aaron is a tremendous addition to the expanding Impulse team,' said Impulse Founder Tom Mueller, a founding member of SpaceX and renowned industry visionary.
