Watch girl with alopecia's moving reaction to being gifted her own wig

2022-10-19 16:51:39

The emotional moment a young girl with alopecia saw herself transformed after being fitted with a wig has been captured in a video widely shared across social media.Tiffani Calix, the owner of Wigs by Tiffani, shared the heartwarming footage to her TikTok account last month, where it quickly gained traction.At the time of writing, the footage has been watched over 19 million times with a second version amassing a further 116,000 upvotes after being posted to Reddit.Alopecia areata is an autoimmune skin disease that results in hair loss on the scalp, face and, in some cases, other areas of the body.
