Dunkin Donuts Park in Hartford will host a benefit softball tournament featuring area fire and police departments on Saturday. (Courtesy of the Hartford Yard Goats)HARTFORD, CT — The Hartford Yard Goats may have packed up for the fall, but their home this Saturday, Oct. 22, will host a very special softball tournament featuring the area's bravest and finest — all for a good cause.Hartford Firefighters Local 760 is hosting its annual Julio Lozada Softball Classic at Dunkin Donuts Park, 1214 Main St.
