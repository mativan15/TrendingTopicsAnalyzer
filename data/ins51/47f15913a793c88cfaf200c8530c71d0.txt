By Reuters Published: 14:25 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 14:25 EDT, 15 October 2022 Oct 15 (Reuters) - Elon Musk said on Saturday that his rocket company SpaceX would continue to fund its Starlink internet service in Ukraine, a day after he said it could no longer afford to do so. Musk tweeted: "the hell with it ... even though starlink is still losing money & other companies are getting billions of taxpayer $, we'll just keep funding ukraine govt for free". It was not immediately clear whether Musk's offer was genuine or if he was expressing sarcasm.
