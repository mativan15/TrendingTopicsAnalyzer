Mom shares "summer clock" hack to keep kids on schedule in divisive video
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-01 21:27:46

Viewers' opinions were split when they saw a mom's "summer clock" that she made to keep her children on a schedule in a viral TikTok video.McKinnley Matson posted the video to her account @mkmatson15 where it garnered nearly 4 million views. While many viewers shared their appreciation for the hack, others questioned whether such a schedule was necessary for the summer months.Parenting website Empowering Parents noted that having some type of structure during the summer is helpful.
A mother's DIY clock to establish a schedule for her children during the summer has gone viral on TikTok.
