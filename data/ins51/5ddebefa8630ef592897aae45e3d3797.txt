One of the airline industry's best-known entrepreneurs says he doubts that antitrust regulators will approve the proposed JetBlue/Spirit merger. 'In evaluating the proposed merger, the justice department may well consider what it does to Spirit as a low-cost carrier, raising its costs,' said Frank Lorenzo, former CEO and controlling shareholder of Continental Airlines, in an interview.
