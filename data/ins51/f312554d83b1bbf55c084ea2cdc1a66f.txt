BJP to play big role in Punjab politics, will emerge as largest party in state in 2024 Lok Sabha polls: Amit Shah
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 18:15:00

Amit Shah also recalled the historic sacrifices of the people of Punjab for the protection of religion and rights of the people.Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Saturday said his Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) will play a big role in the politics of Punjab and will emerge as the largest party in the state in the 2024 Lok Sabha polls. He was addressing state BJP leaders, including core group members, office-bearers and district presidents. "BJP will play a big role in the state politics...Whosoever wants to join hands with us will have to be a small partner and we will also welcome those who want to come into the BJP fold," he said.
