APSince the trilogy encounters with Tyson Fury about a year back, the American heavyweight Deontay Wilder, a.k.a the Bronze Bomber, makes a long-awaited appearance in the ring on Saturday, October 15, to face Robert Helenius, the Nordic Nightmare. The venue of the 12-round explosive battle will be Barclays Center in New York. ‘‘I've always tried to fight the best, and I'm picking up where I left off, and I give nothing but the best'', Wilder told a media person ahead of Saturday evening bout. Wilder added that, at best, he might be active in the ring for another three years.
