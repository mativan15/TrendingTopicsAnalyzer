Xbox's Streaming Stick Goes Back to the Drawing Board
gizmodo.com
gaming
2022-05-27 15:50:00

Xbox confirmed that it's still developing a low-cost streaming stick designed to bring Game Pass to the masses by cutting out the console. However, the company appears to have hit a snag with the most recent under-development hardware and is now pivoting to a new approach.An xCloud streaming device was first announced in June 2021 alongside plans to bring a no-hardware-required Xbox Game Pass app to supported TVs. The company had previously teased an Xbox streaming stick, stating a device you could plug into a TV or monitor to stream games was under development.
