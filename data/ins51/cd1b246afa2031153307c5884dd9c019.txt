Throughout history, major crises have acted as catalysts for change, both socially and technologically, and it seems like this Pandemic is no different. The various lockdowns forced millions of us into remote working quite literally overnight, accelerating changes towards flexible working and new technology investments.Ultimately, the transition towards remote workforces has been a lot smoother than expected for businesses, and it is plausible that working from home will eventually become the new normal, with just a couple of days a week spent in office spaces.
