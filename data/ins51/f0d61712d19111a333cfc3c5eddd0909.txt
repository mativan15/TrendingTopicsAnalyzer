Wall St Slides 1% As Strong Factory Data Fans Fears Of Aggressive Rate Hikes

2022-06-01 10:57:04

U.S. stocks fell in volatile trade on Wednesday after strong factory activity and job openings data stoked concerns that the Federal Reserve would keep raising interest rates sharply this year.Ten of the 11 major S&P sectors declined, with financial and healthcare stocks down 2.1% and 2% respectively. Energy stocks gained 1% as Brent Crude rose to $117 a barrel. [O/R]U.S. manufacturing activity unexpectedly picked up pace in May as demand for goods remains strong, easing concerns about an imminent recession.
