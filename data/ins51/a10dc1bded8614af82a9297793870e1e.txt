October 17, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LAKE FOREST, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Affirming its commitment to providing a stellar user experience, Toshiba America Business Solutions' e‑BRIDGE® Global Print software earns the 2022 Gold Merit Award for Technology within the Cloud Services category.



Merit Awards recognize global industries and the market they serve. Merit Awards for Technology were judged based on submissions addressing technologies that touch every part of our lives ranging from consumer and business-to-business products, solutions leadership and innovations.
