Louise Osborne, of Stamford, died May 19, 2022. She was 74. (Shutterstock)Louise (Buzzeo) Osborne, 74, of Stamford, CT passed away on May 19, 2022 in Largo, Florida. Born in Stamford on July 18, 1947, she was the daughter of the late Anthony and Lena Buzzeo. Louise graduated from JM Wright Tech School. She worked as a social worker in middle schools with children having difficulty socializing and behavioral problems. She also chaired a mental health support group for the State of Connecticut in Hartford.
