Klopp said he deserved his red card for the way he confronted the assistant refereeLiverpool manager Jurgen Klopp has been charged by the Football Association following his sending-off during his side's 1-0 win against Manchester City.Klopp was dismissed after berating a referee's assistant when a foul was not given for a challenge on Mohamed Salah.The incident came on a weekend where Merseyside Youth League games were postponed amid ongoing issues with referee abuse.Klopp apologised for his reaction in his post-match comments.
