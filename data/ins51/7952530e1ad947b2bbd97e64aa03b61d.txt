Amazon Launches Weekly, Post-Thursday Night Football Livestream Concert Series
pcmag.com
news
2022-10-18 14:29:01

Amazon Prime Video has a new weekly livestream concert series featuring big-name musicians performing their latest hits.

Hosted by rapper 2 Chainz, Amazon Music Live airs live from Los Angeles on Thursday, Oct. 27 at 9 p.m. PT (12 midnight ET) following Prime Video's Thursday Night Football Thursday Night Football.

"Whether you're a sports fan waiting for the first football game of the week, or a music fan staying up late to hear the latest drop from your favorite artist, Thursday is the biggest night for entertainment," says Kirdis Postelle, global head of artist marketing of Amazon Music.
