Shooting at Roseville's Macomb Mall, police search for suspect
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 17:00:00

A suspect is wanted after shoppers heard gunfire at Roseville's Macomb Mall as a fight broke out in front of Dick's Sporting Goods. The cops said that two groups of people got into an altercation. The argument between the two groups intensified as they were leaving the mall. One male suspect pulled out his revolver in the parking lot and began firing. According to the police, three shots were fired before they all left. Chief Ryan Monroe stated that one male fired a few bullets into the air as they left the mall outside Dick's Sporting Goods.
