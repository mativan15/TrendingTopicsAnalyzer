gremlin/E+ via Getty Images Aircraft, especially aircraft engines, are incredibly complicated pieces of machinery. They undergo significant amounts of stress and, as a result, need to be repaired from time to time. Although many customers might gravitate to the original equipment manufacturers, or OEMs, that were responsible for the creation of the equipment originally, there are some customers that would opt to purchase parts from another party. For those who fit in this category, one valid company is HEICO (NYSE:HEI).
