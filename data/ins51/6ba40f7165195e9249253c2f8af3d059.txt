Published: 06:19 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 06:28 EDT, 19 October 2022 A shocked carer spotted the face of Boris Johnson staring back at him from a chicken korma —  and suggests it could be a 'premonition' that the former prime minister is on his way back to Number 10.Jonathan Hoitinga was making a chicken korma ready meal for a client when he noticed the politician glaring back at him.The father-of-one said that he found it hilarious that eight pieces of chicken had fallen to make the shape of two eyes, a nose and mouth, with the yellow rice resembling Boris Johnson's 'dishevelled' blonde hair.
