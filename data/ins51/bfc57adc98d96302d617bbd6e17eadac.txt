Tern to Buy Pires Investments for GBP14.9 Mln in All Stock Deal
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 07:01:00

By Ian Walker Tern PLC said Wednesday that it has agreed to buy Pires Investments PLC for 14.9 million pounds ($18.8 million) in shares. Under the deal, accepting shareholders of the next generation technology-focused investment company will get 0.51613 new Tern shares for each share held. The deal values each Pires share at 8.0 pence and is a 54% premium to its closing price of 5.20 pence on Tuesday. Upon completion of the deal, Pires shareholders will own 21.51% of the combined company. Tern's chief executive Al Sisto said the merger will create a group "with larger scale and a more diverse, but synergistic, network of exciting technology companies.
