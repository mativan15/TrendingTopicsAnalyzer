Union ministers to visit J&K in June-July as part of BJP outreach programme
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-02 09:31:47

In order to take stock of the development and conduct an outreach programme, several union ministers will visit the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir in the month of June and July.According to sources, discussions on assembly elections may also be held during these proposed visits. The outreach programme started after the abrogation of Articles 370 and 35A in the year 2019."District Development Council elections have been done and the Council has been formed. The process of delimitation has been completed and the delimitation of 90 assembly segments has been framed.
