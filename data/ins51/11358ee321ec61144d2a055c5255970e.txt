The Wrap What would you do if you were trying to stop your daughter from moving across the world and marrying someone she just met? Team up with your ex-spouse to ruin the wedding, of course!Rom-com veterans Julia Roberts and George Clooney reunite in 'Ticket to Paradise,' as the family of our dreams, complete with 'Rosaline's' Kaitlyn Dever. Though Clooney's character calls their marriage 'the worst 19 years of his life,' when the pair first see each other on the plane to the Bali wedding — to which Roberts retorts 'we were only married for five' — the collective scheming to change their daughter's mind might end up changing their hearts.
