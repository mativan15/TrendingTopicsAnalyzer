KPMG censured, fined US$150,000 by U.S. accounting regulator in latest stumble for Canadian audit firm
theglobeandmail.com
business
2022-10-20 21:12:25

The U.S. accounting regulator has censured KPMG Canada LLP and fined it US$150,000 for filing inaccurate reports about which other affiliates of the firm did audit work for a Canadian client.The Public Company Accounting Oversight Board, or PCAOB, which regulates firms that audit publicly traded companies, entered into the enforcement agreement with KPMG for violations in three countries, including Italy and the Netherlands.It's the latest stumble for one of the Big Four auditing firms with North American regulators, with each having entered into settlements in just over a year.
