The Seahawks have gotten off to a solid 3-3 start, allowing only nine points in last week's victory over the Cardinals.

One of their young defensive players has now been rewarded for his part in the performance.

Cornerback Tariq Woolen has been named NFC defensive player of the week.

A rookie fifth-round pick out of Texas-San Antonio, Woolen had five tackles with an interception and a fumble recovery in Sunday's win.
