Four missing men found dismembered in Oklahoma river, say police
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 14:45:00

A probe into finding missing persons in Oklahoma has turned into a murder investigation after the detection of four mutilated bodies in a river. Police claimed that dismembered bodies discovered in an Oklahoma river were recognized as four men who went missing last week. The four men, Mark Chastain, Billy Chastain, Mike Sparks, and Alex Stevens, left Billy Chastain's Okmulgee, Oklahoma, left house on bicycles around 9 p.m. On Monday, they were reported missing by their family on Monday night or early Tuesday.
