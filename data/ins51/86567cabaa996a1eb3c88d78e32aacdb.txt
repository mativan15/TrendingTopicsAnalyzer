Brazilian digital lender Nubank to launch its own cryptocurrency (NYSE:NU)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 15:02:17

BlackJack3D Despite a bleak environment for the cryptocurrency market, Nubank, the Brazilian digital banking startup owned by Nu Holdings (NYSE:NU), is planning to launch its own digital token next year in the country, it said Wednesday in a blog post. The lender's token, called Nucoin, is currently in development phase and is expected to be rolled out in the first half of 2023 to its 70M users. Around 2K customers will be invited to participate in an exclusive group to contribute their ideas regarding the Nucoin development, NuBank said, adding that Nucoins will be distributed to customers at no cost and used to offer discounts and perks.
