Dublin businesses to call for sharp cut to capital gains tax
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-20 04:00:12

Dublin businesses will call for a big cut in the rate of capital gains tax for indigenous firms due to surging inflation.Dublin Chamber president Vincent Harrison, who is also managing director of Dublin Airport, will tell an audience of 1,200 business representatives at the group's annual dinner in the Convention Centre tonight that the current rate of 33 per cent is 'punitively high'.'Government needs to reduce the rate of capital gains tax for indigenous firms to a simple 20 per cent rate,' he will say.
