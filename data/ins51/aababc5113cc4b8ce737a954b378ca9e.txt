Solving D2C brand's challenges with logistics technologies
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-06 07:45:00

As online shopping continues to gain momentum in the Indian retail settings, the expectations of customers - especially in terms of experience - have evolved dramatically. Amidst this major upheaval in the retail industry, customer experience (CX) has become a centerpiece and D2C brands must focus on offering a lot more than just products in order to ensure a premium experience. Ensuring free and faster shipping, communication of exact date of delivery, and prompt processing of returns are some elements that enable creating a better CX for D2C brands.
