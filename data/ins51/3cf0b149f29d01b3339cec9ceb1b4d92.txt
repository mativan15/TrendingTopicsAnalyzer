High Inflation Causes 25% Of Americans To Delay Retirement Plans
iheart.com
news
2022-06-01 15:30:00

Photo: Getty ImagesAs inflation continues to soar, many workers are being forced to delay their retirement plans, according to NBC News. The quarterly BMO Real Financial Progress Index found that 25% of workers said they will have to keep working as high inflation has put a dent in their retirement savings. The survey, which was conducted between March 30 and April 25, noted that 36% of Americans have reduced their savings, while 21% reduced the amount of money they were putting away for retirement due to rising costs.
