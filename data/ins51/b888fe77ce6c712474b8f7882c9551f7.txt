Establishment Labs stock drops 18% after short report from Hindenburg Research

YakubovAlim/iStock via Getty Images Establishment Labs (NASDAQ:ESTA) fell 18% in premarket trading after a new short report from Hindenburg Research. Hindenburg highlighted that the breast implant maker has had losses every year as a public company and that investors have given the company a "nosebleed" ~9.5x sales multiple. The report also takes issue with safety studies for the products. Establishment Labs (ESTA) didn't immediately return Seeking Alpha email request for comment. Hindenburg has a short position in ESTA.
