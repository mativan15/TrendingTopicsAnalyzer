Jenny Doman and her family stood beside a highway exit ramp watching helplessly as bright orange flames engulfed their brand-new RV.The fire quickly transformed their dreams of a carefree life in their new home-away-from-home to a nightmare.The Oregon family had purchased the 40-foot-long Heartland Road Warrior, a fifth-wheel trailer and 'toy hauler' made by a subsidiary of Indiana-based Thor Industries, just one day earlier. The price tag was more than $100,000.Excited to enjoy the new luxury RV, they hit the road for a trip to visit family in Utah, their RV in tow.
