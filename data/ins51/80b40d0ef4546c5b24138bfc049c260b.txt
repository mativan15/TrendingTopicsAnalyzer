In front of the BBC headquarters in London (UK) on October 6, 2022. JUSTIN TALLIS / AFP At first, it was mostly about selling radio sets. On October 18, 1922, electricians Marconi, GEC and a few others joined forces with the Post Office to create a radio station covering the United Kingdom. Since the idea was not to compete with the all-powerful newspapers, no information; only culture and entertainment. The great press strike of 1926 authorized the British Broadcasting Company to broadcast news all day.
