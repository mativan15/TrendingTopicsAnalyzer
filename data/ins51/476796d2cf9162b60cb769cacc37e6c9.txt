Atlantic mackerel moratorium jeopardizes rod-and-reel bluefin tuna fishery: fishermen
cbc.ca
news
2022-06-02 22:26:00

The moratorium on commercial mackerel fishing in Atlantic Canada is threatening to take a big bite out of the Maritimes' lucrative rod-and-reel bluefin tuna fishery.With the season in Nova Scotia set to open in less than a month, fishermen in the province say their fleet risks becoming collateral damage in Canada's effort to preserve the depleted Atlantic mackerel stock."The amount of mackerel that I'll put on a hook is truly insignificant in the overall grand scheme of … rebuilding the mackerel stock," said Kevin MacDougall, who runs a tuna sportfishing charter from Ballantynes Cove, outside Antigonish, N.
