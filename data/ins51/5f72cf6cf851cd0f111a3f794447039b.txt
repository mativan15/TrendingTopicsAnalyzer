New Vision Printing & Publishing Co. Ltd.

The New Vision Printing & Publishing Co. Ltd. engages in the multimedia business, which includes housing newspapers, magazines, Internet publishing, televisions, radios, commercial printing, advertising, and distribution services. It operates through the following segments: Print Media, Electronic Media, Commercial Printing, and Others. The Print Media segment produces newspapers and magazines generating circulation and advertising revenue. The Electronic Media segment deals with the radio and television stations generating advertising revenue from the electronic media platforms.
