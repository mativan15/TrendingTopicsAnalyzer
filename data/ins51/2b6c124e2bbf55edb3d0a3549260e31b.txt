Ben Wallace: I'm staying defence secretary
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 19:40:00

Ben Wallace has said that he wants to remain defence secretary amid speculation he could be a unity candidate if Liz Truss departs.Wallace said he believed Truss should carry on to ensure stability at a time of heightened threats and economic turmoil.He did not stand for prime minister in the summer but has been touted in recent days as a candidate should Truss be forced out. His handling of Ukraine has driven up his popularity ratings.Wallace, a former Scots Guard, told The Times that he wanted to keep his job and rebuked his Conservative colleagues for playing 'political parlour games'.
