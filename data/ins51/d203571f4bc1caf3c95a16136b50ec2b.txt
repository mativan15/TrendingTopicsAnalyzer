Just Stop Oil activists arrested after blocking Dartford Bridge for 36 hours

It's understood a raised platform may have been used by Essex Police to bring an end to the protest which saw thousands of commuters disrupted as the bridge closedA banner reading Just Stop Oil hangs from the top of the QEII bridge Two Just Stop Oil activists who blocked the Dartford Crossing for the past two days have been arrested. The pair climbed the Queen Elizabeth bridge in Dartford in the early hours of Monday morning, bringing M25 traffic and drivers on surrounding roads to a halt. Essex Police have now confirmed they have been detained with witnesses reporting an elevated platform was used.
