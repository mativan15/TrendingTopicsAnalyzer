October 21, 2022 09:19 PM Eastern Daylight Time MOUNT PROSPECT, Ill.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bison Capital Partners VI, LP ('Bison Capital'), Angeles Equity Partners, LLC ('Angeles'), and Brian Nelson have acquired Lapmaster Parent, LLC ('Precision Surfacing Solutions' (or 'PSS'), a leading producer of high precision equipment and accessories for slicing, lapping, grinding, and polishing a engineered materials including metals, silicon, glass, ceramics, and composites. Bison Capital and Angeles will partner with Brian Nelson, who founded PSS and will resume his role as group CEO, to drive the next stage of the company's global growth.
