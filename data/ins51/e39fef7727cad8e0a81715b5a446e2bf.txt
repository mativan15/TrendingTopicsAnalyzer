October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN MATEO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--With leaking underground storage tanks (USTs) presenting serious risk to public health, safety, and the environment, Booster® and Ambipar today announce a strategic partnership to help customers seamlessly remove and remediate USTs while ensuring continuity in their fueling operations.



Long considered a necessary staple of conventional fueling infrastructure, USTs store fuel at gas stations, fleet yards, businesses, government facilities, and more.
