We're paying the price for the new cult of idleness
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 17:00:00

Airports may be jammed, but there is one form of transport where, as ever, there are no delays: Remainers leaping aboard the bandwagon to blame Brexit for absolutely everything. Never mind that airports on the Continent and in the US are experiencing similar problems; the slightest disruption in Britain is always deemed to come down to our decision to leave the EU. Brexit has, apparently, deprived us of all the eager EU workers who used to check our tickets and load our luggage, or it has caused confusion over the expiry dates on our passports.
