By Associated Press Published: 00:05 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 00:05 EDT, 17 October 2022 HOBART, Australia (AP) - West Indies skipper Nicholas Pooran won the toss Monday and sent Scotland in to bat in the Group B opener at the Twenty20 World Cup. The two-time champion West Indies are playing in the preliminary round and need to place first or second in the group to advance to the Super 12 round. Ireland and Zimbabwe were playing the other Group B game later Monday. There was an upset in the tournament opener on Sunday, with former champion and three-time finalist Sri Lanka upset by Namibia in Group A.
