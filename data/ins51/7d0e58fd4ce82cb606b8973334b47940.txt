BEIJING, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Xinyuan Real Estate Co., Ltd. ("Xinyuan" or "the Company") (NYSE: XIN), a NYSE-listed real estate developer operating primarily in China with assets in other countries, today announced that Xinchuang Technology's Intelligent Manufacturing Industrial Park of Xinchuang The Makers' Town of Central China ("Park Town") was delivered 45 days in advance on May 17. Xinchuang Tech is a wholly owned subsidiary and the unique tech platform of Xinyuan Group for urban operation.
