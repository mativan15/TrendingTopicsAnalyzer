2022-10-20 23:02:00

By The Justice Department's bankruptcy watchdog wants Kirkland & Ellis LLP removed as counsel to 3M Co.'s bankrupt earplug manufacturing unit, saying the law firm has a conflict because it also defends the parent company in mass earplug lawsuits. Kirkland & Ellis doesn't possess 'undivided loyalty' to its client, 3M subsidiary Aearo Technologies LLC, according to court papers filed by the Office of the U.S. Trustee on Thursday objecting to the firm's retention.
