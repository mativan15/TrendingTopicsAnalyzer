ultramarine5 Banking and broad spectrum financial-based exchange traded funds showed strength in Monday's early trading after they received a boost from Q3 earning figures delivered by Bank of America (BAC). There are 241 ETFs that have ownership in BAC but the Invesco KBW Bank ETF (NASDAQ:KBWB), iShares U.S. Financial Services ETF (NYSEARCA:IYG), Financial Select Sector SPDR Fund (NYSEARCA:XLF), and the Vanguard Financials ETF (NYSEARCA:VFH) are the four largest holders. KBWB has a 7.91% stake in the financial firm, while IYG, XLF, and VFH have 6.
