Domestic appliances devour energy, but some are more voracious than others when it comes to monstering your meter. As we all battle rising energy bills – now running at roughly twice what they were this time last year* – it's well worth exploring whether using your appliances differently could trim pennies and pounds off your costs. A quick word about those bills. Last year – indeed, until 30 September this year – energy prices were subject to a price cap set by Ofgem, the energy market regulator.
