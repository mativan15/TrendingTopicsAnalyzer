Te Pāti Māori co-leader Debbie Ngarewa-Packer targeted by 'disgusting' racist mail

2022-10-19 04:00:00

Te Pāti Māori co-leader Debbie Ngarewa-Packer says 'disgusting' racist mail she receives regularly won't hold her party back from standing up for what's right. Read this story in te reo Māori and English here. / Pānuitia tēnei i te reo Māori me te reo Pākehā ki konei. In a social media post on Tuesday, Ngarewa-Packer uploaded photos of an expletive-laden anonymous letter she'd received which had a screed of stereotypes and racist terms undermining the work ethic of Māori and their status as tangata whenua.
