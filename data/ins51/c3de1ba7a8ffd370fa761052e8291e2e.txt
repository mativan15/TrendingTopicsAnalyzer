Ludhiana: Fraudsters dupe chemist, his relative of ₹3 lakh, booked
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-16 22:44:05

Three persons have been booked for duping a Ludhiana chemist and his friend of ₹3 lakh on the pretext of exchanging cash with US dollars, police said on Sunday. After taking the cash from the victim, the accused handed them a bag containing a soap bar covered with pieces of papers, the police added. A first information report (FIR) has been registered at the Moti Nagar police station against the three accused, one of whom was identified as Imran, a resident of Moti Nagar. The other accused haven't been identified yet.
