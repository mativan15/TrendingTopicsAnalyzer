Published: 21:44 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 21:44 EDT, 19 October 2022 SAN DIEGO -- Down by four runs and on the verge of a second straight home loss, the San Diego Padres rediscovered their offense on Wednesday in time to level the National League Championship Series. Brandon Drury and Josh Bell homered on back-to-back, second-inning pitches to cut the Padres' deficit in half, and San Diego came up with a five-run fifth inning to beat the Philadelphia Phillies 8-5. The comeback leaves the series tied 1-1 before the next three games are played in Philadelphia.
