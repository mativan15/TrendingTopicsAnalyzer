Travel chaos: your rights and how to claim back your cash
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-03 11:00:00

Tens of thousands of air travellers are struggling to get their money back after having their half-term plans wrecked by last-minute cancellations.Up to 30,000 passengers have had flights cancelled or rearranged that would have departed this week because of acute staff shortages at airlines and baggage handling companies.Travellers have reported airlines not rebooking their journeys or offering them refunds after cancellations, which they are obliged to do by law, and say they are struggling to claim money they are owed.
