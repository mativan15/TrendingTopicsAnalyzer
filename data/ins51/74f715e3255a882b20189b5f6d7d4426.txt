Former Progressive Conservative MLA Carl Benito has been sentenced to two years of house arrest for immigration fraud. (CBC News)A former Progressive Conservative Edmonton MLA has been sentenced to two years of house arrest for immigration fraud. Carl Benito, 68, will also have to pay a $75,000 fine over the two years. Following the conditional sentence order, he'll be on probation for another two years and must perform 100 hours of community service. Benito's 28-year-old son, Charles, was also sentenced in Court of King's Bench late Friday afternoon.
