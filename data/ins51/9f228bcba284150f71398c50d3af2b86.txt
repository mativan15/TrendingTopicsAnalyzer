Johnny Depp was awarded $10 million in compensatory damages, plus $5 million in punitive damages, after the jury found that Amber Heard defamed the Hollywood star when she wrote a 2018 'Washington Post' op-ed alluding to her past claims of domestic violence.Scarlett Johansson sued Disney for releasing her movie 'Black Widow' on their streaming platform Disney at the same time as the theatrical release, causing a loss of $50 million in bonuses. The two reportedly reached a settlement of around $40 million.
