SecureWorks: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-06-02 11:12:56

ATLANTA (AP) _ SecureWorks Corp. (SCWX) on Thursday reported a loss of $21.6 million in its fiscal first quarter.On a per-share basis, the Atlanta-based company said it had a loss of 26 cents. Losses, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to 9 cents per share. The information security services provider posted revenue of $121 million in the period. For the current quarter ending in August, SecureWorks expects its results to range from a loss of 17 cents per share to a loss of 15 cents per share.
