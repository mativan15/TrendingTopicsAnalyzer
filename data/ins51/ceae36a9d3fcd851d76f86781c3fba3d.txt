In the financial year 2021-22, the primary markets mobilized funds worth Rs 1.39 lakh crore, 26.1 per cent higher than Rs 1.1 lakh crore in the previous fiscal year, said the annual reports from the Reserve Bank of India. The central bank, in its report, said that this includes all the primary market activities, including initial public offering (IPOs), follow-on public offerings (FPOs) and rights issues. This also includes all issues by mainboard and SME players.
