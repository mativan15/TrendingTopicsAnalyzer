Justin Sullivan This story was originally published for subscribers of Reading The Markets, an SA Marketplace service on October 11. The story has been updated on the afternoon of October 18. AMD's (NASDAQ:AMD) shares have slid more than 60% since peaking nearly a year ago, and the outlook doesn't appear to have improved. The company recently slashed its revenue guidance for the third quarter to $5.6 billion from $6.7 billion. Additionally, the company now sees non-GAAP gross margins of 50%, down from a prior view of 54%.
