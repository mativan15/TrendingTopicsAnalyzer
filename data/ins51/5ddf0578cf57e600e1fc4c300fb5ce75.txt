Apple Spatial Audio comes to Mercedes drivers with the latest Apple Music update. Check out the supported cars.
 If you love Apple's Spatial Audio on your iPhone and supported audio gear, there's good news for you, if you are driving a Mercedes Benz. Apple is bringing its Spatial Audio tech to vehicles now, starting with Mercedes Benz cars. Spatial Audio along with support for Dolby Atmos audio will be available natively to Mercedes Benz vehicles for the first time. The Spatial Audio support will be a part of the car's MBUX platform.
