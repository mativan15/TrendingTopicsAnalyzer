Nazara Technologies CEO Manish Agarwal to step down
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 14:45:00

ETtechNazara Technologies CEO Manish AgarwalNazara Technologies on Thursday announced that Chief Executive Officer Manish Agarwal is leaving the firm to pursue entrepreneurship. Agarwal's resignation will be effective December 1, 2022, the company said. "He will continue to be associated with the company as a Nazara nominee on the boards of material subsidiaries of Nazara," Nazara Technologies said in an exchange filing. Nitish Mittersain, joint managing director of Nazara Technologies will return to the role of the Chief Executive Officer following Agarwal's resignation.
