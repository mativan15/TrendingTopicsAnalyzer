The celebrations for the Diwali festival have begun. People of all ages are excited about the festival, and greetings for Diwali 2022 have started floating throughout all social media platforms. Everyone is trying to meet and greet their dear ones on this auspicious occasion. However, a few people may not be able to meet and greet in person and will have to rely on messaging services. However, there is something interesting for them as well. WhatsApp has introduced colourful stickers specially designed for the festival.
