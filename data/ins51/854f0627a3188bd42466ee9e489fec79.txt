Indiewire 'Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness' put Benedict Cumberbatch's magic-wielding Marvel hero back in the superhero spotlight when it opened in theaters this past spring. After the universe-jumping success of 'Spider-Man: No Way Home,' fans were eager to see the Marvel Cinematic Universe keep expanding beyond the boundaries of our reality. 'Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness' delivered that in a big way, taking fans on a visually stimulating journey through the multiverse (with plenty of cameos thrown in for good measure).
