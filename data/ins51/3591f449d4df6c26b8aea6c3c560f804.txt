Golden State Warriors head coach Steve Kerr admits that sometimes the team's string of four NBA championships in eight years — in addition to his five other rings with the Chicago Bulls and San Antonio Spurs — seems a little surreal for a guy just hoping to make the Phoenix Suns team and log a couple of seasons.

"The journey I've been on has been far-fetched and unlikely," Kerr said ahead of the Warriors' defense of the franchise's seventh National Basketball Association title overall.

"Sometimes I feel like I'm going to wake up and it's not going to be real.
