October 21, 2022 | 8:06 PM GMTMayor Eric Adams (D-NYC) announced a new plan during a press conference October 17 stating that NYC residents will now only be able to put out trash at 8 p.m., as opposed t 4 p.m. During the conference, he constantly overemphasized that the initiative is an effort to reduce the amount of rats roaming the city, which he "hates" and considers a "plague." Enter "A Plague Tale: Requiem," a survival horror stealth game whose gigantic wave of rats might give him the cathartic release he needs.
