[YONHAP] Stocks opened nearly flat Wednesday as investors took to the sidelines amid earnings reports and woes over aggressive rate hikes. The benchmark Kospi had inched up 0.49 points, or 0.02 percent, to 2,250.44 points as of 9:20 a.m. Auto and steel companies advanced, while chemical and financial shares declined. Samsung Electronics traded flat, and SK hynix lost 1.67 percent. Hyundai Motor added 0.
