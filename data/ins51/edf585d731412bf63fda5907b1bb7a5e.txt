AMC DINE-IN Topanga 12 features all recliner seating, IMAX with Laser at AMC, Dolby Cinema at AMC, as well as many other popular amenities like all laser projection and the Dine-In Delivery to Seat service

In conjunction with the opening of AMC DINE-IN Topanga 12, nearby AMC Promenade 16 will close at the end of business on Wednesday, June 1

LEAWOOD, Kan., May 31, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AMC Theatres® (NYSE:AMC), the largest theatrical exhibitor in the United States and the world, and Unibail-Rodamco-Westfield (URW), global developer and operator of flagship destinations, today announced that AMC's first newly-built theatre of 2022, AMC DINE-IN Topanga 12, will open to the public this Thursday, June 2.
