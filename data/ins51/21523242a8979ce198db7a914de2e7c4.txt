It was the total opposite result of a UK case decided in 2020, which Depp resoundingly lost.

The result of Johnny Depp's libel lawsuit against Amber Heard in Virginia, in which he won a comprehensive victory and was awarded $15 million in damages, was surprising for a whole host of reasons.
