Trevor Noah slams Democrats meddling in the midterms: 'Gross negligence'
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-20 18:00:14

"The Daily Show" host Trevor Noah called out Democrats interfering in the midterm elections during an appearance on MSNBC Wednesday night.The left-wing comedian scolded Democrats for spending millions in Republican primaries to boost "extreme" candidates that they felt they could beat in the general election."The fact that there are still Democratic machines that are funding extreme Republicans, basically putting them forward. I think that it is gross negligence," he told host Alex Wagner. "Daily Show" host Trevor Noah tells MSNBC host Alex Wagner that it was "grossly negligent" for Democrats to meddle in the 2022 election.
