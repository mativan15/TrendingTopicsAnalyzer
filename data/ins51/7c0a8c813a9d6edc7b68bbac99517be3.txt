Why are so many high-income Americans living paycheck to paycheck?
fastcompany.com
business
2022-06-03 12:10:04

What would you do with $250,000 per year?The typical American family, which earns a median household income of $67,000 per year, would likely be able to put that type of earning potential to serious use by paying down debt, padding their savings and investments, and maybe taking a vacation. But for millions of high-earning households, the answer is evidently 'simply make ends meet.'As many as 61% of U.S. consumers were living paycheck to paycheck as of April, according to a joint study from PYMNTS and LendingClub (the latest in the monthly 'Paycheck-To-Paycheck' series), which surveyed more than 4,000 people in the United States in early April.
