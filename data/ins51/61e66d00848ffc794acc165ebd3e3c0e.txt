NEWARK, DE / ACCESSWIRE / October 20, 2022 / As per Future Market Insights' latest industry analysis, the global XRF Analyzer market is bound to witness a CAGR of 5.7% between 2019 and 2027. It stood at US$ 2.2 Bn in the year 2018.

Adopting XRF analyzers for mining industry to analyse ceramics, glass, metals, and likewise is on a substantial rise. On-site geochemical analysis of mine face, drill core, or for preparing samples could be performed through handheld XRF analyzers. Technologically advanced XRF analyzers are being made available, which come with radiation safety, proximity sensors, enhanced detection parameters, high-count, and better resolution rate.
