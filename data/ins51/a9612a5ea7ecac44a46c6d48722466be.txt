2023 Lexus UX: Now Hybrid-only, Gains Performance and Tech Enhancements
forbes.com
news
2022-06-03 13:00:00

For 2023 the Lexus UX ditches its non-hybrid drivetrain and gets a visual and structural makeover, complete with a sharper-handling F Sport trim. Lexus Lexus has only just begun tiptoeing into fully-electric vehicles, but among premium brands its been the king of gasoline-electric hybrids since it introduced the RX hybrid in 2005. Parent company Toyota's hybrid tech has helped the luxury automaker create a portfolio of fuel-sipping crossovers as buyers have gravitated away from sedans, and now the 2023 Lexus UX will become its first hybrid-only SUV.
