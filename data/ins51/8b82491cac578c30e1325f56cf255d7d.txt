REUTERS/Brendan McDermid/File PhotoOct 16 (Reuters) - Goldman Sachs Group Inc (GS.N) plans to fold its biggest businesses into three divisions, the Wall Street Journal reported on Sunday, citing people familiar with the matter.Undertaking one of biggest reshuffles in the Wall Street firm's history, Goldman will combine its investment banking and trading businesses into one unit, while merging asset and wealth management into another, the report said.
