N26 Crypto will let users buy and sell 100 tokens including bitcoin and ether.N26European digital bank N26 announced Thursday it is launching a crypto trading service, starting with Austria as the first market for the product.The service, called N26 Crypto, is set to become available to N26's Austrian clients in the coming weeks and will initially include 100 tokens including bitcoin and ether. N26 plans to roll out the feature to users in other markets over the next six months and eventually expand its token offering to include a total 194 coins.
