Geneva, Switzerland--(Newsfile Corp. - June 2, 2022) - TRON celebrated a record month of May, achieving multiple massive milestones in a few short weeks. Including USDD, the collateralized decentralized stablecoin on TRON, making it onto CoinMarketCap's top 100 only 16 days after its May 5 launch. As of Wednesday, June 1, USDD has reached a new milestone with over $667 million in circulating supply burning over 8 billion TRX along the way.
