PARAMUS, N.J., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Alexander's, Inc. (NYSE: ALX) today announced that it will file its quarterly report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended September 30, 2022 with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission and issue its third quarter earnings release on Monday, October 31, 2022, before the New York Stock Exchange opens. Vornado Realty Trust (NYSE: VNO), the manager which conducts Alexander's operations, announced it will host its quarterly earnings conference call and an audio webcast on Tuesday, November 1, 2022 at 10:00 a.
