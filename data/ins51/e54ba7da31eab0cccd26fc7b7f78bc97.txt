Sprague Resources Agrees to Acquisition by Hartree Partners, Shares up 18%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 20:40:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Sprague Resources LP were higher in Thursday's late-trading market, following news the company agreed to be acquired by Hartree Partners LP. The all-cash deal will see an affiliate of Hartree acquire all outstanding common units of Sprague not already owned by Hartree and its affiliates, Sprague said.
