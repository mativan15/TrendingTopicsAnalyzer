Triveni Engineering to ramp up spirit output by 210 million litres
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 03:00:00

AgenciesNoida-based TEIL will set up two new distilleries and increase the capacity at its existing two distilleries, eventually raising its alcohol manufacturing capacity from 320 kilolitres per day (klpd) to 660 klpd.New Delhi: Sugar and engineering major Triveni Engineering & Industries (TEIL) aims to add 200-210 million litres of spirit production to its capacity by next financial year, according to a top executive, as it seeks to gain from the government's push for ethanol blended petrol. The target for this year is 160-170 million litres, said Tarun Sawhney, the company's vice chairman.
