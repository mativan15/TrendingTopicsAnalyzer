Appointment: eXo Platform announced the appointment of Mrs Mazour-Mestrallet as Chief Executive Officer. She is expected to begin her role in June 2022.

PARIS, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--eXo Platform, the leading open-source provider of intranet and digital workplace solutions, announces the appointment of New CEO, Veronika Mazour-Mestrallet.

About her appointment, Veronika Mazour-Mestrallet declared: "I thank the members of the Board for their trust. In my previous roles as CFO and then COO of eXo Platform, I have successfully led eXo's business operations and development, focusing on our customers needs of security, employee engagement and productivity.
