U.S. stocks are rallying hard on Friday thanks to suggestions the Federal Reserve is contemplating shifting back to smaller interest-rate hikes after the November meeting, according a report in the Wall Street Journal claiming and comments by San Francisco Fed president Mary Daly. But the fact that $2 trillion in U.S. equity options linked to stocks, indexes and exchange-traded funds are expiring — or have already expired — on Friday is also helping to influence trading on what's shaping up to be the best week for the S&P 500 index since at least July.
