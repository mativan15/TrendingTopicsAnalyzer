A mum has been left 'hurt' after her friend told her that her party to celebrate the Queen's Platinum Jubilee had been cancelled - as she was actually just uninvitedThe mum feels 'hurt' at being uninvited from the party (stock photo) With the Queen's Platinum Jubilee now in full swing, many of us will be attending or hosting parties both today and tomorrow to celebrate the monarch's 70 years of service. But one mum has been left feeling "hurt" after she was lied to about her friend's Jubilee party - as she was told the bash had been cancelled, only for it to go ahead without her.
