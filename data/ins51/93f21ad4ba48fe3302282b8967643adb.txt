Sensory feast June 18-July 24 to feature culinary delights from Chef Neal Fraser and Restaurateur Amy Knoll Fraser (Redbird | Vibiana) and circus and musical entertainment from Vau de Vire SocietyLOS ANGELES, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Redbird | Vibiana and San Francisco-based circus Vau de Vire are excited to announce Revive, a deeply unique and immersive dining and performance experience featuring an all-star cast comprised of 40+ circus performers, musicians, dancers, and characters, with shows twice daily Thursday Sunday, June 18-July 24, 2022, at Vibiana in downtown Los Angeles.
