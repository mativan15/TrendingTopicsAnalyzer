It's not easy being a part of the telly industry and Mohit Dagga is well aware of the ups and downs that TV actors face in their careers. Mohit, in fact, even left Mumbai and went back to his hometown in Madhya Pradesh in 2020, but now he is back and enjoying doing what he does best. Talking about the difficult time that he went through, he says, 'After my show Ek Mutthi Aasmaan got over in 2014, I didn't get any work between 2016 and 2020. I tried auditioning, but no good roles were coming my way.
