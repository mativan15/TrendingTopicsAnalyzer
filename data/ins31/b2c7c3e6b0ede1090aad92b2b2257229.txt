Hyderabad minor gang-raped in car; cops probe role of kin of two politicians
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 12:45:00

A minor girl was allegedly gang-raped by five teenagers in a car in Hyderabad's posh Jubilee Hills area on last Saturday. The shocking incident came to light on Friday. Police have detained two suspects and are questioning them. Class 11 and 12 students belonging to politically influential families are allegedly involved in the incident. Son of a legislator is also believed to be part of the group. However, police said he may not have been involved in the crime as he reportedly got off the car and ran away before the gang-rape.
