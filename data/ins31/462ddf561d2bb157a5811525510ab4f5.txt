The Steelers have a knack for finding the perfect fit at wide receiver on Day 2, and they've done it yet again with Pickens. Once considered a first-round lock, and perhaps the top pass-catcher in this year's class, an offseason knee injury impacted Pickens' 2021 campaign, and also his draft stock.
A well-rounded receiver with the size, athleticism and physicality to be a true No. 1 target, Pickens plays with an edge that should immediately make him a fan favorite in Pittsburgh.
