AgenciesRepresentative imageYet another model was found dead in her residence, the fourth such incident in Kolkata in a fortnight involving a player in the showbiz industry, the police said on Monday. The body of 18-year-old Saraswati Das, who was also a make-up artist, was found hanging in the room of her residence at Bediadanga in Kasba area on Sunday, the police said. Initial probe revealed that Saraswati, who modelled for small ventures and had received a number of offers, had allegedly hung herself in her room on Saturday night using a dupatta, a senior police official said.
