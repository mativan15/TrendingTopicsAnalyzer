Image source, LDRSImage caption, The social media accounts of children with disabilities were monitored by council officersCouncillors want an independent inquiry after council staff monitored parents of children with special educational needs on social media.It emerged over the summer Bristol City Council officers watched the accounts of parents for posts critical of the authority. A report published in September cleared the Labour-controlled council of wrongdoing.But on Tuesday councillors voted for an independent inquiry to be launched.
