Published: 17:26 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 17:26 EDT, 21 October 2022 Home goods conglomerate Unilever has recalled millions of its dry shampoo products after determining that they contained the human carcinogen benzene. The recall affected both Canadian and US markets. The company announced the recalls in both countries earlier this week. The Food and Drug Administration said this week that Unilever's move to pull millions of dry shampoos from the US market was made 'out of an abundance of caution.
