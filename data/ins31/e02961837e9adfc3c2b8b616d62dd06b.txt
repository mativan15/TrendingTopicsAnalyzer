Times Brand Icons Bihar 2022 - a premium initiative from Times Group recognised some of the top brands in the region. The initiative honoured and applauded around 30 brands and individuals combined. These young people have taken the challenges of a relentless year head-on. They represent the opportunity for growth in Bihar and the country as a whole. The list is very dynamic and includes various brands and people from all walks of life who have created something new and different and, at the same time, redefined how things are done.
