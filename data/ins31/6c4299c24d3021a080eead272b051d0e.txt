We are in The Biggest Transformative Shift of Our Times
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 09:45:00

On earth, we have a limited amount of time to make things right. The expanding global economy must do its part to improve our biosphere, and this goes beyond just fostering a "green economy." Now is the moment for reflection and quick action. Fresh new ideas are required to reconcile technical advancement, societal welfare, environmental protection, and economic opportunity. The first step in the transformation is to ensure development without carbon emissions, products that can be recycled and reused, green jobs, and an inclusive economy.
