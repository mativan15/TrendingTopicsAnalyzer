October 18, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time OTTAWA, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Best You® ('TBY'), a long-standing group of medical aesthetic clinics in Ontario, today announced that it has completed the acquisition of the Bay Street Salon and Spa in Embrun, Ontario ('Bay Street'). The Bay Street Salon and Spa, located 20 minutes from the outskirts of Ottawa, complements TBY's current offerings. Bay Street is a family-owned and operated salon and spa, offering massage therapy, hair styling services as well as laser treatments for acne, onychomycosis and wart removal, vascular treatments, laser hair removal, and CoolSculpting.
