Hawkish Fed puts tech stocks and cryptocurrencies at risk, poll shows
businesslive.co.za
news
2022-06-06 07:12:13

The speculative darlings of the easy-money era — technology stocks and cryptocurrencies — are acutely vulnerable now that the Federal Reserve is shrinking its nearly $9-trillion balance sheet. At the same time, central bankers from Canada to Europe are about to test the resilience of global markets as they follow hawkish US policymakers on a liquidity-sapping mission to unwind the pandemic bond-buying spree.That's the broad outlook for Wall Street and beyond, according to the most-popular responses from 687 contributors to the latest MLIV Pulse survey, as the Fed this month starts reducing its asset holdings in a process known as quantitative tightening.
