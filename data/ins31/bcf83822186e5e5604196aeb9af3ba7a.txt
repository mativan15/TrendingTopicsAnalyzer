Zee Entertainment rallies 6% after bulk deal on the counter
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 06:00:00

New Delhi: Zee Entertainment Enterprises (ZEEL) hogged the spotlight in Tuesday's trade following bulk deals on the counter, wherein Invesco likely offloaded more than half of its stake. US investment firm Invesco Developing Markets Fund was looking to offload 52.93 million shares (5.23 crore shares), or around 5.51% stake, in the media major, which is bound for a merger with Sony Pictures Network (SPN). Invesco owns a 10.2% stake in the ZEEL with more than half of its stake in the range of Rs 250-263.
