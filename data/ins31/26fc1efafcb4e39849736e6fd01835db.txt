Pittman to sign her novel at Tybrisa Books
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 03:59:00

Oct. 20—LENOIR — Join Tybrisa Books in welcoming local author Phyllis Pittman, who will be signing copies of her debut novel, "The Trouble with Grits" on Saturday, Oct. 22 from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.

Pittman is originally from Hattiesburg, Mississippi, where she worked as a freelance journalist for nearly 25 years.

"I love feature writing," she said. "I like to meet people and tell their stories. Sometimes it's happy stories and happy endings, and sometimes it's really hard stories, but they all deserve to be told and shared.
