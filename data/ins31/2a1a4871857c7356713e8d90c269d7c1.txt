AgenciesMutual interest was expressed in further expanding contacts and exchanges between the relevant Foreign Ministries' divisions.Foreign Ministries' Spokespersons of the BRICS countries have held a virtual meeting to discuss pressing issues in the sphere of information. The spokespersons examined a range of issues pertaining to interaction between the relevant Foreign Ministries' divisions of the five countries in the media sphere, according to a statement issued by the Russian Embassy in India.
