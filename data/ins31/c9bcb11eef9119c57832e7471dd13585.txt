Interactive Brokers' Thomas Peterffy says the S&P 500 may bottom around 3,000
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 16:07:00

''I generally believe that rates are going to continue to go higher and inflation is not going to come down as much as expected,'' — Thomas Peterffy, chairman and founder of Interactive Brokers Thomas Peterffy, the chairman and founder of Interactive Brokers, thinks the S&P 500 index could decline another 20% from Wednesday's level to bottom at around 3,000.  The S&P 500 SPX, -0.59% is down 22.2% year to date. The large-cap index closed at its lowest level since November 2020 on Oct.12 this year.
