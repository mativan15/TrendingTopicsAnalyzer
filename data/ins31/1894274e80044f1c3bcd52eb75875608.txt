Balogun has been a revelation for Reims, scoring six goals in his first 11 games to help them steer clear of the Ligue 1 relegation zone, but the striker's eyes remain on parent club ArsenalFolarin Balogun has dropped a major hint over his long-term future Folarin Balogun has dropped a huge hint that he sees his long-term future as being at Arsenal as he continues to star on loan at Stade de Reims. He moved to the Ligue 1 side on a season-long deal in the summer transfer window, having spent the second-half of last year on loan at Middlesbrough.
