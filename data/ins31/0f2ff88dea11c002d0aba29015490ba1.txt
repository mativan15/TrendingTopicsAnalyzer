These new board members will provide additional guidance and insight for continuing to sustain and grow Westat as an industry leader whose greatest asset is its employee stockholders. ROCKVILLE, Md. (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 Westat is pleased to announce the election of 3 new members to its Board of Directors. This expansion will facilitate the company's focus on business strategy and growth in new markets. Each of these new members brings a wide range of perspectives and diversity of backgrounds for addressing the complex and competitive business environment we face.
