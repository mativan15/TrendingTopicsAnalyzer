President at CRG, A Real Estate Development and Investment Firm. getty As containers continue to pile up on docks and ships idle offshore for long periods of time, the impact of the pandemic on the global supply chain has put the industrial real estate sector at the center of a critical question: How can we remedy supply chain issues? I believe the answer lies in increased warehouse capacity. In my over ten years in the industry, and from my vantage point as the president of a vertically integrated real estate development and investment firm, I'm seeing the demand for industrial space rise more than ever before.
