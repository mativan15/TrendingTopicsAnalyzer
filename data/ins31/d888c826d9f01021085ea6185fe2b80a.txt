BATHINDA: The Ecological Threat Report (ETR), prepared by the Institute for Economics and Peace ( IEP ), featuring research from the Lloyd's Register Foundation World Risk Poll, found Ecological threats, such as rapid population growth, water risk, and food insecurity will be exacerbated by climate change, causing mass migration and conflict.IEP is an independent think tank focussing to peace as a positive, achievable, and tangible measure of human well-being and progress.The report, released on Wednesday, finds 56% of 228 countries and territories are facing extreme ecological threat.
