Jake Pitre is a PhD candidate at Concordia University in Montreal.What does the future hold?We are obsessed with the possible answers to that question, from visions of postcapitalist utopias to virtual realities that take place in the metaverse. Our movies and TV shows depict some possibilities, from the moralizing dystopias of Black Mirror to David Cronenberg's latest, Crimes of the Future, which depicts humans evolved to feel no pain and digest plastics.The main purveyors of the future in our age, though, come from Silicon Valley.
