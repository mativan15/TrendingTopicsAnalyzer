Sir Rod Stewart 'saved my family from danger' says Ukrainian father
express.co.uk
entertainment
2022-10-19 07:00:00

Sir Rod Stewart, 77, has been hailed a "hero" by many after he agreed to help a Ukrainian family flee war-torn Ukraine, agreeing to pay their rent and utility bills for the next 12 months as well as furnishing the Berkshire property. The father, Rostyslav Kurtiak, 49, has now spoken out about the kind deed, admitting he owes his life to the rocker as his children are "safe" and can now "learn like normal children". Sir Rod is a very great man.Rostyslav KurtiakThe family are settling into their four-bedroomed home in Bracknell, Berks, after moving there in the summer.
