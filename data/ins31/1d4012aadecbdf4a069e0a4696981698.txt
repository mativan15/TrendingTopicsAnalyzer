Casting off the grim spectre of the COVID-19 pandemic that disrupted business for the last two years, firecracker merchants in the city are gearing up to welcome the festival of lights in a grand manner, hopeful that the lifting of restrictions will usher in brisk sales.Though the police are yet to release detailed guidelines regarding the sale of firecrackers, shopkeepers are busy in securing all the necessary permissions and are planning to commence sales from Saturday.
