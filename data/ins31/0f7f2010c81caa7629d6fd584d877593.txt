ORRVILLE, Ohio, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The J.M. Smucker Co. (NYSE: SJM) today announced its Board of Directors approved a $1.02 per share dividend on the common shares of the Company. The dividend will be paid on Thursday, December 1, 2022, to shareholders of record at the close of business on Friday, November 11, 2022.
