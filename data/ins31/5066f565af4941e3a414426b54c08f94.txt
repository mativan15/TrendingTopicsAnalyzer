COLMAR, Pa., May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Dorman Products, Inc. (NASDAQ:DORM) won three awards for its best-in-class website and product content at the recent Automotive Content Professionals Network (ACPN) Knowledge Exchange Conference, recognizing the company for delivering significant value to its partners and a great experience to its customers.



Dorman won top honors in the Web-Based Catalog category for DormanProducts.com, where users can easily search Dorman's extensive catalog of products and find rich, detailed data and content to select the product they need.
