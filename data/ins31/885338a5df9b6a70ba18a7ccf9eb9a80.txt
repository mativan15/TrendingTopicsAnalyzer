NEW YORK (PRWEB) June 03, 2022 Fr. Greg. Udo Njoku CSSp, who was ordained as a priest in 1982 by Pope John Paul II in Nigeria, has completed his new book 'Children's Upbringing in Today's Modern Society (Parenting Now in Crisis): (An Ar-se-nal for Mass Construction and Repairs of Diverse Issues in Human Life Today)': a guide to navigating the ever-changing landscape of rearing children. Tackling modern issues head on, Njoku raises solutions for problems that he finds most common amongst the youth of today.
