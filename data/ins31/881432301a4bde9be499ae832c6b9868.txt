Mark Reason: No one likes England at this Rugby World Cup and that should make them care
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-18 16:00:00

Mark Reason is a sports columnist for Stuff OPINION: 'No one likes us and we don't care.' The infamous chant of the Millwall football fans could appropriately be taken up by the followers of England at this World Cup. They are privileged by money and they have used that advantage to crush the life out of the opposition with a style of rugby that is stultifying. England's captain Sarah Hunter said after her team's 13-7 victory over France: 'Hopefully it was a great showcase for women's rugby.' At that point the yoghurt pot splattered the TV screen.
