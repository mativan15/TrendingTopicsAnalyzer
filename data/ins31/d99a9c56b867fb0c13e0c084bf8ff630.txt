Utah-based financial firm Scalar acquires valuation division of Dallas-based GuideCap Partners
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 19:18:00

Draper, Utah-based financial firm Scalar LLC has acquired the valuation division of Dallas-based investment bank GuideCap Partners, according to a news release.

With the acquisition, Scalar will establish an office in Dallas, keep current GuideCap employees and service its existing valuation customers.
