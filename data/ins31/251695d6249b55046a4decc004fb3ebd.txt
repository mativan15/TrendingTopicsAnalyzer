CG Power mulls expansion of LT Motor units for ₹230 crore
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-19 13:18:55

CG Power and Industrial Solutions Ltd. (CG Power) has proposed to more than double the manufacturing capacity of Low-Tension Motor plants at Ahmednagar and Goa in a phased manner at an outlay of ₹230 crore, considering the future demand for AC motors. Currently, CG Power has a capacity to manufacture 9.93 lakh LT Motors per annum. Post expansion, it would increase to 19.92 lakh units, the TII subsidiary said in a regulatory filing.The current capacity utilisation is about 80%. The expansion would be implemented in two phases spread over four years and funded through internal accruals.
