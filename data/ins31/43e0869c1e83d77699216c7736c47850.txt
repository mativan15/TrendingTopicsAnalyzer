Big Mad Morning ShowBy GimpyJun 2, 2022In late April, Phoenix police raided the home of 34 year old Adrian Valladares. During the search of his home police found 15 firearms, 855 grams of coke, 193 fentanyl pills, nearly $65k, and a Nile crocodile. Nile crocodiles are native to Africa, and this one is believed to be a pet of Adrian's. Adrian was taken in for drug possession, firearms violations, and money laundering. The croc was taken to the Phoenix Herpetology Society.
