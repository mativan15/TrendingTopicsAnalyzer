Published: 19:23 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 19:33 EDT, 5 June 2022 An ex McDonald's employee has shared her favourite secret menu item from the fast food franchise which satisfies her sweet tooth.Marketing manager Queenie Tan, who lives in Sydney, first learned about ordering a mini sundae with a topping of your choice when she worked at Maccas as a teenager.'You can get a soft serve in a lid with chocolate or caramel sauce. It's just like a mini sundae but it only costs $1.30. Did you know about this?' She said in a recent TikTok video.
