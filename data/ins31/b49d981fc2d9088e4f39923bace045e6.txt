Lululemon Beats Wall Street Expectations While Navigating Retail Headwinds

2022-06-02 22:44:00

Lululemon's winning streak continues both on the court and on Wall Street.

The Vancouver, B.C.-based athletic apparel, accessories and footwear retailer revealed earnings after the market closed Thursday, improving on top and bottom lines and updating its guidance. Company shares rose nearly 4 percent in after-hours trading as a result.

'In the first quarter of 2022, continued momentum in the business enabled us to achieve a strong start to the year,' Calvin McDonald, Lululemon's chief executive officer, said in a statement.
