J&K Bank Q2 Results: Profit doubles to Rs 243 crore
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 11:00:00

New Delhi: Jammu & Kashmir Bank on Friday reported over two-fold jump in net profit at Rs 243.49 crore for September quarter 2022-23 on a healthy growth in interest income and fall in bad loans. The bank had posted a net profit of Rs 111.09 crore for the year-ago period.Total income during the quarter rose to Rs 2,444.59 crore from Rs 2,184.20 crore a year ago, J&K Bank said in a regulatory filing.Interest income grew 15.5 per cent to Rs 2,298.85 crore during the quarter.Gross non-performing assets (NPAs) were reduced to 7.
