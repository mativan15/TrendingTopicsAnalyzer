The Great Resignation. Pivoting. Career transition. We've all known people, both inside the wine industry and outside of it, at various points along the trajectory of these phases. Recently I've been drawn into conversations with women in the wine industry who are experienced in their careers, who have also made significant transitions in their professional roles. The transitions are influenced by COVID, not surprisingly, and there are also motivations (internal and external) that speak to a recalibration of the women's position in the industry.
