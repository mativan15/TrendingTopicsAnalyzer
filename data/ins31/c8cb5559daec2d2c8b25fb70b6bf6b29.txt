Canada qualified for their first World Cup since 1986 when they beat Jamaica in MarchThe Canadian men's national team went on strike on Sunday, forcing a friendly with Panama to be called off. The team's refusal to play revolves around a dispute with the country's football association, Canada Soccer.Canada have qualified for the 2022 World Cup for only the second time.The players released a letter on Sunday accusing Canada Soccer of "disrespecting" the team in relation to negotiations around World Cup prize money.
