AgenciesAditya Birla Group has appointed former Facebook and Bain executive Prashanth Aluru as its CEO & co-founder to lead this new venture.Aditya Birla Group on Wednesday announced the launch of its new 'house of brands' entity TMRW which will have a portfolio of disruptor brands in the fashion and lifestyle space. TMRW will create India's largest portfolio of disruptor brands in the fashion & lifestyle space and enable the next phase of direct to consumer (D2C) growth in the country which is poised to be a USD 100-billion market by 2025, Aditya Birla Group said in a statement.
