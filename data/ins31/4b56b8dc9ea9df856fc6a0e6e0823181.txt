Talks Break off Between Long-Term Care Workers and Alderwood Rest Home

June 02, 2022 11:41 AM Eastern Daylight Time SYDNEY, Nova Scotia--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Talks have broken off between long-term care workers, represented by the Canadian Union of Public Employees (CUPE) Local 1635, and their employer, the Alderwood Rest Home in Baddeck, NS.



CUPE National Representative Tammy Martin says, 'After three days of bargaining, we are a long way from reaching a deal. Yet to be resolved are issues around time off, wages, and benefits. The collective bargaining only works if both parties respect the process.
