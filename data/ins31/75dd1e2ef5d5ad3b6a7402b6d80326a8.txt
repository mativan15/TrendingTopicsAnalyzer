The owners of Bradenton's Mixon Fruit Farms, a local icon, is considering selling the business, reports said. (Courtesy of Google Maps)BRADENTON, FL — After 84 years in the same family, the owners of Mixon Fruit Farms in Bradenton — a Manatee County icon — is considering selling the business, according to reports.Owners Dean and Janet Mixon, addressing social media rumors about the farm being sold, told the Bradenton Herald that they have a prospective buyer for the family business, though no contract has been signed yet.
