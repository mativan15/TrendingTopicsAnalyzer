Lakers' LeBron James is the first active NBA player to become a billionaire: report

2022-06-02 18:56:53

Los Angeles Lakers star LeBron James is now a billionaire, becoming the first active NBA player to achieve the milestone, according to Forbes.    The only other NBA player to become a billionaire is Michael Jordan, who did not become one until after his playing days.  LeBron James looks on during the UEFA Champions League final match between Liverpool FC and Real Madrid on May 28, 2022, in Paris.
