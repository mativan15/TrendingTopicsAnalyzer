Levin: Action feels right at a moment of economic uncertainty, but it may not be
startribune.com
news
2022-05-28 13:00:00

When we sold our cabin through an unsolicited offer many years ago at the top of the market, it was a Pyrrhic victory. My wife grew up with a cabin and used ours to create a lifetime of memories for our then-young children. The price we received for the sale of the cabin didn't come close to the experiences we were creating. It was my mistake (and we paid for it by immediately replacing the cabin with a different one). In this real estate market, Pyrrhic victories on both the buy and the sell abound.
