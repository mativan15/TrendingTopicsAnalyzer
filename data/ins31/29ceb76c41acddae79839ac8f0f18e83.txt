Gotham Knights: Batman's Legacy Deserves Better
cnet.com
news
2022-10-21 13:45:56

Gotham Knights does the unprecedented move by killing off Batman. As a follow-up to the popular Arkham series, this game brings in the Bat Family -- Nightwing, Batgirl, Red Hood and Robin -- to investigate the death of the Dark Knight. While the game itself has some bright spots, its lack of polish makes the experience much less of a thrilling Bat adventure.Developer Warner Bros Games Montreal released a game in 2022 that looks like it could have come out in 2012.It's especially frustrating when you remember that the Arkham series includes some of the greatest comic-inspired games ever made.
