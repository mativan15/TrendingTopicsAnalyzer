Dayton-area nonprofit secures new D.C. office
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 13:28:00

A local nonprofit organization dedicated to applied scientific research and development has announced that the organization will be expanding to a new, out of state location. The new location will help the organization meet its growing needs and give it a local presence close to key Department of Defense offices.

Parallax Advanced Research, headquartered in Beavercreek, has scouted Arlington, Virginia for its second office location. The 2,400-square-foot D.C. office, located at 4040 Wilson Blvd.
