Netflix co-founder Marc Randolph: ‘Follow your dreams' is ‘terrible advice'
cnbc.com
news
2022-06-01 13:07:21

Netflix co-founder Marc Randolph says the "most dispensed advice in the history of mankind" might also be the worst.At Cape Cod Community College's recent commencement ceremony, Randolph told the class of 2022 that three common graduation speech words — "follow your dreams" — constitute "terrible advice.""It's not the advice itself that's bad," Randolph, 64, said. "It's bad because all the well-meaning people telling you to follow your dreams leave out something important. They never tell you how.
