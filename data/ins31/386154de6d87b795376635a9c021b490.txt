High-flying tech hardware and software maker Nvidia (NVDA 0.70%) was a standout stock on Wednesday. In contrast to the gloom of the overall market, its shares were a bright light, rising nearly 1% on the day against the S&P 500 index's 0.7% drop. A new tie-up with a storied tech industry titan was at the root of the optimism. So what
Tuesday afternoon, following a "fireside chat" involving their respective CEOs at a tech industry conference, Nvidia and Oracle (ORCL -1.09%) announced that they are deepening their existing collaboration.
