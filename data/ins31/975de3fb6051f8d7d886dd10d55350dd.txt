As a wealth management professional in the great city of Los Angeles, I have sat next to countless divorcing spouses facing significant financial challenges during an overwhelmingly emotional time. Emotional turmoil clouds decision-making, so my aim here is to provide guidance on some of the prep-work you can do before you get to the tougher stages of your dissolution – the filing, the negotiations and of course the ultimate settlement. The financial goals of your divorce should be simple: Unwinding your partnership with as little cost, complexity, and collateral damage as possible.
