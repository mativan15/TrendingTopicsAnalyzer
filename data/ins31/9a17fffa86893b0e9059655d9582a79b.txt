Hyundai Motor And Kia Warn $2 Billion Engine Provision To Hit Q3 Earnings

2022-10-18 11:06:32

A sign shows directions to the Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama automobile plant in Montgomery, Alabama, U.S. July 15, 2022. 
Hyundai Motor and affiliate Kia Corp will book a combined 2.9 trillion won ($2 billion) provision in their third-quarter results as they continue to suffer the effects of engine recalls from years ago, they said on Tuesday.The costs, of which Hyundai accounted for 1.36 trillion won and Kia for 1.54 trillion won, amount to more than half of Hyundai's estimated third-quarter net profit and 77% of Kia's profit, Refinitiv data shows.
