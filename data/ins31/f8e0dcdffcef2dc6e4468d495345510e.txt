Man City tipped to 'spite Liverpool' by moving for Jude Bellingham
express.co.uk
sport
2022-10-19 09:03:00

Manchester City have been tipped to beat Liverpool to a transfer for Borussia Dortmund and England star Jude Bellingham 'out of spite', with tensions between the two clubs at a high after Jurgen Klopp's side's 1-0 win over City at Anfield on Sunday. Mohamed Salah scored the only goal of the game but members of both teams have been controversially speaking after the result.Before Sunday's game, Klopp gave his verdict on teams such as City and Newcastle who, after changing ownership, 'nobody can compete' with, according to the German coach.
