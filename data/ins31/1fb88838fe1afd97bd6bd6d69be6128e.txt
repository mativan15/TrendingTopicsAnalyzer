AgenciesIt has raised its capex for the automotive business by ₹2,900 crore and another ₹400 crore for the tractor business.Fueled by a significant spike in demand for its SUVs and strong electrification push, Mahindra & Mahindra has increased its mid-term capital expenditure for automotive and farm equipment business by 27% or ₹3,400 crore to ₹15,300 crore till FY24. It has raised its capex for the automotive business by ₹2,900 crore and another ₹400 crore for the tractor business. The incremental investment into the auto business will be to increase capacity for its much sought-after SUV XUV 700 and new models in the pipeline including new Scorpio N and also its EV business.
