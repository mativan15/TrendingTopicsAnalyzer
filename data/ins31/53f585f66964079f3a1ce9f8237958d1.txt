Binance Raises $500 Million Fund for Crypto Investments
wsj.com
news
2022-06-01 18:16:00

Photo: MARCO BELLO/REUTERS Investment capital continues to pour into the crypto space even as the prices of most cryptocurrencies are at half their highs. On Wednesday, Binance Labs, the investment arm of the crypto exchange Binance, announced it closed on a $500 million investment fund with capital from outside firms including DST Global Partners and Breyer Capital.
