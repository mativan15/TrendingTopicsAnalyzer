MOSCOW, Oct 19 (Reuters) - The Russian rouble edged lower in a relatively narrow range in early trade on Wednesday after weeks of volatility, with domestic investors focused on two OFZ treasury bond auctions the finance ministry is scheduled to hold later in the day.

At 0709 GMT, the rouble was 0.3% weaker against the dollar at 61.76 and was unchanged at 60.75 versus the euro .
