Most big cryptocurrencies climb on Cardano, Dogecoin increases
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 14:00:00

Most of the largest cryptocurrencies were up during morning trading on Thursday, with Cardano ADAUSD, +3.83% seeing the biggest move, rising 2.94% to 56 cents.Six additional currencies posted rises Thursday. Dogecoin DOGEUSD, +1.14% rose 1.07% to 8 cents, and Bitcoin BTCUSD, +0.87% rose 0.83% to $29,891.22. Ethereum ETHUSD, +0.44% rose 0.27% to $1,799.00, while Litecoin LTCUSD, +0.59% rose 0.26% to $62.82. Ripple XRPUSD, +0.32% climbed 0.06% to 40 cents.
