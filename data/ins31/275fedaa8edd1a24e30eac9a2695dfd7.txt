Astros announce ALCS roster for showdown with Yankees
chron.com
news
2022-10-19 17:06:23

The Houston Astros unveiled their roster for their ALCS showdown with the New York Yankees on Wednesday, making one slight adjustment from their recent series with the Seattle Mariners.

Houston added right-handed reliever Seth Martinez to the roster, while dropping outfielder Jake Meyers.
