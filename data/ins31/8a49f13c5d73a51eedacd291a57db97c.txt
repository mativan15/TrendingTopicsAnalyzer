Funny Car leader Matt Hagan beats Robert Hight in NHRA Funny Car final in New England
espn.com
news
2022-06-06 02:00:00

6:24 PM ETEPPING, N.H. -- Funny Car points leader Matt Hagan raced to his third victory of the season Sunday, beating Robert Hight in the final round of the NHRA New England Nationals at New England Dragway.Hagan had a 3.922-second run at 327.98 mph in a Dodge Charger SRT Hellcat for Tony Stewart Racing in the championship round to beat Hight for the first time in three finals this year. Hagan has 42 career victories, winning four of the past five events at New England Dragway."It was just a great weekend, and I can't say enough about [crew chief] Dickie Venables," Hagan said.
