ReutersOn an immediate basis, 15,770/52,500 and 15,850/52,700 levels would be major hurdles.NEW DELHI: The Nifty Realty index traded negative around 10:50AM(IST)on Thursday in a weak market. Brigade Enterprises(up 0.5 per cent) and DLF(up 0.01 per cent) were among the top gainers. Macrotech Developers(down 1.78 per cent), Godrej Properties(down 1.68 per cent), Oberoi Realty(down 0.65 per cent), Phoenix Mills(down 0.57 per cent) and Indiabulls Real Estate(down 0.53 per cent) were the top losers on the index.
