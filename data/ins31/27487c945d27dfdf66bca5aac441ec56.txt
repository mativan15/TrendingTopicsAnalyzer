Analysts estimate adjusted EPS of $0.47 vs. $0.57 in Q3 FY 2021.Aviation revenue is expected to grow at the second-fastest place in at least four years.Aviation will be GE's sole business by 2024 after the conglomerate spins off healthcare, power, renewable energy and other units.



General Electric Co. (GE), the once-dominant industrial company that's been selling units in recent years to stay afloat, will probably report declining profits and flat revenue in the third quarter due to supply chain bottlenecks, falling demand, and squeezed margins.
