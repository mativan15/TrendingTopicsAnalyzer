Facebook's Transformation to Meta Will Be Complete With New Ticker Symbol on June 9

Meta announced Tuesday that it will change its stock ticker symbol to "META" prior to market open on June 9. Facebook rebranded to Meta in October, saying it was part of its "growing ambitions beyond social media." At the time, Meta said it would change its stock ticker to "MVRS," effective Dec. 1.
