 Dropshipping is an online retail business model where the retailer does not need to maintain inventory of the items they are selling. If you love selling but are put off by the thought of maintaining and paying for a storefront, warehouse and other overheads, dropshipping could be the solution for you.
In this article, we will talk about what dropshipping is, how it works, its advantages and limitations.
