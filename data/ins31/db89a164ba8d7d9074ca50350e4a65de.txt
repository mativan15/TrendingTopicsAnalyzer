A Singapore exchange-traded fund will give domestic investors exposure to one of China's main tech bourses.   © Reuters ECHO WONG, Nikkei staff writerOctober 21, 2022 20:08 JST | SingaporeHONG KONG -- Singapore is to get a first exchange-traded fund giving investors exposure to Chinese tech stocks via a similar vehicle in Shenzhen.The ETF created by Singapore's UOB bank will list in the city-state on November 14. It will invest in the Shenzhen stock exchange's ChiNext market for high-growth tech companies.
