Crypto sponsorships boom in NBA, up 6,400% this season: IEG
aol.com
news
2022-06-01 18:08:34

Crypto brands led the NBA's latest leg up in sponsorship revenue, according to new data from IEG's Sponsorship Intelligence Database.The consulting firm found that cryptocurrency sponsorships increased from $2 million during the 2020-21 season to $130 million this season, marking a 6,400% increase year-over-year. NBA sponsorships as a whole were up 12% during the 2021-22 season, totaling a record $1.64 billion.'The cryptocurrency category's sponsorship sending spree is like nothing we have ever seen before, representing 1/3 of all new NBA sponsorship deals,' Peter Laatz, IEG Global Managing Director, stated in a press release.
