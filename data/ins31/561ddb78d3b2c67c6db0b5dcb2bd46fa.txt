'That goes a long way in building stronger students, as well as a stronger, safer, more productive society.' ROCHESTER, N.Y. (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Greece Athena High School and SpeakWell Youth Leadership - an elite youth leadership development program, have teamed up to host the John Wallace SpeakWell Basketball Camp, where Greece Athena graduate, Syracuse University legend, and first-round draft pick of the NY Knicks, John Wallace, will teach students basketball and leadership skills on and off the court.
