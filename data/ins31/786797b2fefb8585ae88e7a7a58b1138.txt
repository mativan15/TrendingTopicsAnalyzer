New Apple Museum Opens In Former Warsaw Factory
ibtimes.com
news
2022-05-31 13:56:59

A new museum opening in a former metalworking factory in Warsaw brings together 1,600 exhibits linked to Apple -- the result of years of painstaking efforts by a determined Polish collector."It is the biggest and most complete Apple collection in the world," boasted Jacek Lupina, a 56-year-old architect, who amassed the collection spanning the entire history of the US tech giant. The collection spans the entire history of the US tech giant Photo: AFP / Wojtek RADWANSKI The museum is housed in Fabryka Norblina, a red-brick factory from the early 19th century in central Warsaw which has been turned into a retail and entertainment space.
