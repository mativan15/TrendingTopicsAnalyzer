Chip Somodevilla Thesis By Buffett's $1 test, a CEO should be able to grow a company's market capitalization ("MC") by at least $1 for every dollar of earnings retained. It sounds like an easy test to pass. After all, by simply hoarding cash on your balance sheet, your company's net worth should go up in proportion. However, surprisingly, many companies (or their CEOs) do not pass this simple test. Plenty of them actually fails miserably for various reasons, some of their own making and some out of their control.
