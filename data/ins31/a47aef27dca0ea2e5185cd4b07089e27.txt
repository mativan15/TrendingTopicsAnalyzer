Kevin Frayer/Getty Images News The electric vehicles sector got jolts of positive news on Monday and broke higher along with the rest of the stock market. Canoo (GOEV) soared 15.12% after update on a new vehicle order. Proterra (PTRA) gained 7.27% after BTIG issued a strong bull call. BYD Company Limited (OTCPK:BYDDF) also issued an encouraging deliveries and profit forecast. Chinese electric vehicle stocks are higher after President Xi Jinping's comments at the Chinese Communist Party meeting are being interpreted by analysts as neutral to positive for the sector.
