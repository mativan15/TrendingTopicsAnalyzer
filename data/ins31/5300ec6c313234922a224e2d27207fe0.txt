WILMINGTON, Del.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 27, 2022--The Chemours Company ('Chemours') (NYSE: CC), a global chemistry company with leading market positions in Titanium Technologies, Thermal & Specialized Solutions, and Advanced Performance Materials, today announced it is investigating capacity investments to support its growing Hydrogen Economy business. This increased capacity in ionomers would support the demand growth within water electrolysis and fuel cell technology.The company is currently considering potential locations in the United States and Europe, with an eye to local community engagement and collaboration.
