Tamil Nadu: BJP flays CM Stalin for political speech, says GST Council example of cooperative federalism
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 14:15:00

Tamil Nadu BJP president K Annamalai on Friday slammed Chief Minister M K Stalin for delivering an allegedly political speech during Prime Minister Narendra Modi's visit to the city for launching developmental projects and claimed that the goods and services tax (GST) council is a "shining example of cooperative federalism." The Prime Minister did not visit Chennai for a BJP programme but to launch projects and lay foundation stones for over Rs 31,000 crore, he said. "The official function was about the developmental initiatives for Tamil Nadu.
