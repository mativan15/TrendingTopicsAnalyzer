Joel Carillet/iStock Unreleased via Getty Images During the recession of 2001, Leonard Lauder, the then Chairman of cosmetic giant Estee Lauder (EL) introduced the 'Lipstick Index' as a pulse for the economy. Lipstick sales were on the rise despite the slowdown, and it was believed that at such times customers spend on small luxuries in lieu of making big purchases. And that's where the French cosmetics company L'Oreal (OTCPK:LRLCY) comes in. Its sales continue to grow even as the economic gloom gets bigger.
