DC Movies' Black Adam released, but fans have mixed reactions
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 11:00:00

DC Universe' much anticipated Black Adam movie has hit the theaters, but early reviews are not that encouraging. The superhero movie has Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson in the lead role as Black Adam, a powerful being on par with Superman and who has similar powers as Shazam.

The movie also features renowned actor Pierce Brosnan as Doctor Fate, and Aldis Hodge as Hawkman.

The movie was to be released in 2021 but got delayed due to the COVID pandemic. Being the first superhero movie by Johnson, it was one of the highly anticipated movies of the year.
