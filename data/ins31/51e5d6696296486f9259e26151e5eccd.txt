Cross has five hits, drives in five runs in Hokies' regional win over Raiders
timesnews.net
news
2022-06-04 06:30:00

BLACKSBURG — Gavin Cross went 5-for-6 with a double, a home run and five RBIs for Virginia Tech, which beat Wright State 15-9 on Friday night in the Blacksburg Regional.Virginia Tech, the regional's No. 1 seed, plays No. 3 seed Columbia in the semifinals on Saturday after fourth-seeded Wright State meets second-seeded Gonzaga in an elimination game at English Field.The Hokies (42-12) — the fourth overall seed in the NCAA baseball tournament — never trailed.Nick Biddison was hit by a pitch to lead off the bottom of the first inning and Cross followed with a home run to right field to make it 2-0.
