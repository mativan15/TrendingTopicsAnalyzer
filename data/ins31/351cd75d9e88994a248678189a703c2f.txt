LUCKNOW: Gautam Buddh Nagar district in UP is set to become one of India's biggest data centre hubs with at least seven data centre projects coming up in Noida and Greater Noida. This was indicated during the third groundbreaking ceremony held here on Friday.

'World's top companies are looking to store their data in Uttar Pradesh. About 25% of the total investment received on the occasion were for data centres alone,' an official statement from the UP government said, adding that the total worth of investment for data centres is Rs 19,928 crore.
