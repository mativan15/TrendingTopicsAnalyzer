Titan managing director CK Venkataraman (right) and (Fragrance & Fashion Accessories) Manish Gupta with IRTH brand of women bagsTitan Company will launch IRTH, its latest brand of women handbags, at an event in Mumbai on Monday, eying a business of about Rs 1000 crore over the next five years in the affordable premium segment. Titan managing director CK Venkataraman said his company saw a huge potential in the women's bags category and hence was exploring the opportunity. The brand, he said, represented his company's understanding of women consumers and their evolving needs.
