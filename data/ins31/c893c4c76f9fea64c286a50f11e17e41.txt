Zara joins the fashion for pre-loved clothes
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 23:01:00

Customers of Zara will be able to sell, donate and repair their clothes as the high street fashion retailer moves into the second-hand clothing business in Britain.The Zara Pre-owned platform will allow customers to donate clothing in-store or via home collection, to book repairs and to sell second-hand Zara purchases. The launch of the service on November 3 marks Zara's emergence into the resale and refurbishment market.It is the latest clothing retailer to take a more sustainable approach. Fast-fashion brands have come under fire for the levels of waste and pollution they generate, with the textile sector estimated to be responsible for up to 10 per cent of carbon dioxide emissions globally.
