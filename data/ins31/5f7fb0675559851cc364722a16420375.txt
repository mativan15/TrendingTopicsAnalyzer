Australia is on the "precipice" of a UK-style energy crisis that could send many of its power retailers broke and fuel a surge of households unable to pay their bills, a leading expert has warned.There are warnings Australia could slide into a UK-style energy crisis where retailers go broke and energy poverty soarsNew Zealand-based provider ReAmped yesterday became the latest to pull back from the national electricity marketWholesale power costs have more than doubled in a year, fuelling concerns household bills will rocket from JulyThe soaring cost of wholesale energy has triggered widespread alarm among observers and there are fears a significant chunk of Australia's retail electricity market will be wiped out in the crunch.
