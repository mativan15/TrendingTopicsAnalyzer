Fenimore Asset Management names new president
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 21:13:00

Fenimore Asset Management has hired Christian Snyder as the Cobleskill investment advisory firm's new president.

Snyder succeeds Debra Pollard, who is retiring after 30 years with the company.

In his last role, Snyder spent five years as chief operating officer of the Wealth Strategies Group at Goldman Sachs Ayco Personal Financial Management. Before that he was associate counsel and deputy general counsel for the company.

Snyder has a J.D. from Suffolk University Law School and a bachelor's degree in mathematical economics from Colgate University.
