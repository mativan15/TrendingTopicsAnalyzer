The Hollywood Reporter - Movie News Click here to read the full article.Two years after shock revelations about the secret Nazi past of Berlin International Film Festival founder Alfred Bauer, the Berlinale will publish the findings of an independent study on Bauer the festival commissioned with the Leibniz Institute for Contemporary History (IfZ).The Berlinale will also hold a public panel discussion on the study and its revelations, on Nov. 2 in Berlin.Alfred Bauer helped found the Berlin festival, one of the world's top-tier film events, and was director of the Berlinale from 1951 to 1976.
