Nordstrom CFO Anne Bramman to Leave the Company
wsj.com
news
2022-10-18 13:23:00

Photo: brendan mcdermid/Reuters Nordstrom Inc. said late Monday that Chief Financial Officer Anne Bramman will be leaving the company on Dec. 2. Ms. Bramman has served as Nordstrom's finance chief since June 2017. She will remain with the company until after it reports third-quarter results in November. Michael Maher, Nordstrom's senior vice president and chief accounting officer, will assume the role of interim CFO while Nordstrom searches for a permanent replacement. Mr. Maher joined Nordstrom in 2009 as controller.
