Published: 00:10 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 00:10 EDT, 15 October 2022 With matching eight-year, $65 million contract extensions in hand, forwards Jordan Kyrou and Robert Thomas are ready to lead a balanced St. Louis Blues attack into the 2022-23 season. They will open their campaign Saturday by hosting the Columbus Blue Jackets, who are playing their third game but looking for their first win, following a 4-1 loss at Carolina on Wednesday and a 5-2 defeat on home ice to the Tampa Bay Lightning on Friday.
