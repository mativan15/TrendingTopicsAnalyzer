MAUMEE, Ohio, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Huttig Building Products, a Woodgrain Company, one of the largest domestic distributors of millwork, building materials and wood products, has entered into a new distribution partnership with Fiberon, a leading manufacturer of composite wood-alternative decking and railing products. The partnership supports Fiberon's continued growth in the outdoor living space, and significantly expands the company's distribution through Huttig in the Southeast region of the United States.
