Freddie Mac Multifamily Announces Promotions of Corey Aber and Erlita Shively

MCLEAN, Va., May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Freddie Mac (OTCQB: FMCC) Multifamily today announced that it has named two new vice presidents that will better help the company serve its mission and manage its risks. Corey Aber is now vice president of Multifamily Mission, Policy & Strategy and Erlita Shively is now vice president of Multifamily Counterparty Risk Management.



'Corey Aber and Erlita Shively are both veterans of Freddie Mac Multifamily and their promotions reflect our commitment to grow talent and provide stability and affordability to the multifamily market,' said Kevin Palmer, head of Multifamily business at Freddie Mac.
