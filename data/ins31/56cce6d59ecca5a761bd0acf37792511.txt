Image source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Freedom House says the Philippines is one of the most dangerous places in the world for journalistsPowerful commercial interests control or influence much of the media. The lively TV scene is dominated by free-to-air networks ABS-CBN and GMA. Some Manila-based networks broadcast in local languages. Cable TV has extensive reach. Films, comedies and entertainment shows attract the largest audiences. There are hundreds of radio stations. Manila Broadcasting Company is a leading network.
