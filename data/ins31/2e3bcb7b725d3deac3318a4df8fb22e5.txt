Degnan's appointment comes amid cybersecurity leader's 132% ARR growthNEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Axonius, the leader in cybersecurity asset management and SaaS management, today announced the appointment of Nick Degnan as Senior Vice President of Sales. Degnan, who has spent the past decade building and rapidly scaling global sales and channel teams, joins Axonius to oversee the global sales organization."The Axonius platform is well known for solving critical security and infrastructure problems for businesses of all sizes and I am thrilled to join an organization that stands out for its culture, technology and vision," said Nick Degnan, SVP of Global Sales, Axonius.
