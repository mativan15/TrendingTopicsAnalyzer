ETtechIn the case of Zomato, retail investors held 3.52 per cent stake as of March quarter compared with 1.6 per cent stake at the end of December quarter and 1.4 per cent stake as of September 30, 2021, data available with corporate database AceEquity suggests.Small investors, as suggested by individuals holding up to Rs 2 lakh worth of share, were seen raising stake in five new age companies in March quarter, even as institutional investors cut their stake in them amid a sharp fall in value of such stocks.
