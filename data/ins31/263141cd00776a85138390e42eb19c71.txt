Pakistan sends two-member delegation for 90th Interpol meeting in India
indiatimes.com
world
2022-10-18 10:30:00

NEW DELHI: Pakistan has sent a two-member delegation to attend the 90th Interpol General Assembly meeting, which began in New Delhi today.The participation of the Pakistani delegation comes despite high tensions between the two countries. The General Assembly is Interpol's supreme governing body and meets once a year to take key decisions related to its functioning.Prime Minister Narendra Modi will address the 90th Interpol General Assembly in New Delhi's Pragati Maidan, the Prime Minister's Office (PMO) said.
