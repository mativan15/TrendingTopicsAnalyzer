Crowell & Moring Honored as International Law Firm of the Year at the Qatar Business Law Forum 2022

2022-10-18 18:04:00

DOHA, Qatar, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Crowell & Moring, one of the largest international firms operating in Qatar, was named International Law Firm of the Year at the LexisNexis Middle East Qatar Business Law Forum dinner and awards ceremony (7th edition) in Doha on 12 October. Crowell was also shortlisted for the Corporate & Commercial Team of the Year award.
The Qatar Business Law Forum Gala Dinner & Awards Ceremony recognizes exceptional achievement within Qatar's legal community. The awards are judged by an independent panel composed of industry leaders, academic practitioners, and government officials.
