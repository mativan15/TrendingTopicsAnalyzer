Kent & Essex Mutual Insurance Company chooses Guidewire for process automation
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 13:05:27

Kent & Essex Mutual Insurance Company selected Guidewire (NYSE:GWRE) to automate and streamline business processes to drive its growth strategy across all lines of business.
Kent & Essex Mutual will be accessing Guidewire in a private cloud through EY Nexus for Insurance.
'We selected Guidewire because it is deeply entrenched in the P&C insurance industry and is a proven, flexible platform that enables straight-through processing, no-touch underwriting, and renewals,' said Kent & Essex Mutual President and CEO Kevin Konecny.
