REUTERS/StaffOct 17 (Reuters) - European shares edged up on Monday, driven by gains in energy shares as investors awaited clarity on UK's fiscal plan amid jitters about the growing likelihood of a recession and corporate profits coming under pressure from rising interest rates.The region-wide STOXX 600 index (.STOXX) was up 0.2% at 7:02 a.m. GMT.Markets saw volatility last week, with investors fretting about the prospects of a recession from aggressive central bank actions to tame inflation and UK's political turmoil adding to worries.
