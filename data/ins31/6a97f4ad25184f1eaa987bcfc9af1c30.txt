RAF jets blast 53 drones in biggest ever missile drill as Russia tensions rage
thescottishsun.co.uk
news
2022-10-22 08:46:48

RAF warplanes shot down 53 drones in their biggest ever missile war games as tensions mount with Russia. 
Striking footage of the mass firing over the sea at the Hebrides Air Weapon Ranges in Scotland last month shows missiles being shot from the jets and rocketing through the sky.
A missile rockets past an RAF pilot during the 10-day exercise over the sea in ScotlandCredit: RAF
The pilots were aiming at Banshee target drones - designed for training exercisesCredit: RAF
The war games come as tensions rage between Britain and RussiaCredit: RAF
Pilots from eight different squadrons downed dozens of dronesCredit: RAF
The missiles can be seen shooting past the planes heading for the drone targets.
