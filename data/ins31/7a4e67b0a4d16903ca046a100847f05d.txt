Plano's Improving is expanding Canadian market share by acquiring Bit Quill Technologies
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 19:45:00

Plano-based tech firm Improving has acquired Vancouver software consulting company Bit Quill Technologies, according to a news release. The transaction is expected to increase Improving's annual revenue to over $250 million and expand the company into the Canadian market.

"There is apparent alignment between our two companies," Curtis Hite, CEO of Improving, said in the release. "From the beginning, we have been impressed with the Bit Quill team and their commitment to building strong relationships with customers as well as cultivating a strong company culture.
