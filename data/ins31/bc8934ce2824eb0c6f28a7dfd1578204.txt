Senior Congress leaders on Sunday started planning for the "Bharat Jodo Yatra" announced by the party at the Udaipur "Nav Sankalp Shivir". Former Congress chief Rahul Gandhi was also present at the first meeting of the the party's central planning group for the "yatra". The "Bharat Jodo Yatra" will begin from Kanyakumari to Kashmir on October 2, coinciding with Gandhi Jayanti. 
"The first meeting of the Central Planning Group for Bharat Jodo Yatra took place today. The Kanyakumari to Kashmir yatra will start on October 2nd and planning for it began in right earnest.
