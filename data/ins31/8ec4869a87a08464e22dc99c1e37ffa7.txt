Edelman CEO advice to others: Beware of the 'pushback against wokeness'
aol.com
news
2022-06-04 12:12:27

With an ongoing war in Europe, an ongoing public health crisis, and various social issues gripping America, corporate executives increasingly find themselves facing questions from employees about whether or not they plan to take a stand.But if CEOs were to take a stand against this backdrop, according to Edelman CEO Richard Edelman, it shouldn't be representing the company.'They should speak out as citizens, but they shouldn't speak out as CEOs,' Edelman recently told Yahoo Finance at the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland (video above).
