The coveted Nobel Prizes are announced annually, and the most recent installment is here.This year's economics award goes to former U.S. Federal Reserve Chair Ben Bernanke along with fellow U.S. economists, Douglas W. Diamond and Philip H. Dybvig. John F. Clauser was also among the American recipients as one of three Nobel Prize in Physics winners, and American chemists Carolyn Bertozzi and K. Barry Sharpless are this year's winners for the prize in chemistry.With 400 winners, the United States is the country with the most Nobel Prize winners.
