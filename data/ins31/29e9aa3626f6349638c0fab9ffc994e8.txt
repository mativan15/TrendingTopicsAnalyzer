Droneacharya Aerial Innovations files DRHP for SME IPO
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 11:15:00

Droneacharya Aerial Innovations filed DRHP with market regulator Sebi for their IPO on Thursday. The company will be issuing a total of 62.90 lakh equity shares of Rs 10 apiece via the book-building route. The equity shares of the company are proposed to be listed on BSE's SME Platform (BSE SME). The proceeds from the proposed offering will be used by the company to purchase drones and other accessories, and for other general corporate expenses. For the year ended June 2022, the company reported a total income of Rs 3.
