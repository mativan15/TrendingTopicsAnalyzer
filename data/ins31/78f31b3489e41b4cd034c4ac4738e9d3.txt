GSK India appoints Bhushan Akshikar as managing director
indiatimes.com
science
2022-10-17 11:00:00

The Board of GlaxoSmithKline Pharmaceuticals GSK India ) on Monday appointed Bhushan Akshikar as managing director of the company for a period of four years effective from December 1, 2022.Akshikar succeeds Sridhar Venkatesh who is moving to a new senior role within GSK Group from November 30.The company places on record its appreciation for the guidance and support provided by Sridhar Venkatesh during his tenure.Akshikar with over 26 years experience within the pharmaceutical industry, joined GSK India in September 2011 to lead the specialty and commercial excellence business units.
