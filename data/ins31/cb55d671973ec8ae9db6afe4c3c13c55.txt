June 02, 2022 09:03 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KKR Real Estate Finance Trust Inc. ('KREF') (NYSE: KREF) today announced the pricing of a public offering of 7,000,000 shares of KREF common stock (the 'Offering'), consisting of 2,750,000 primary shares to be issued and sold by KREF and 4,250,000 secondary shares to be sold by KKR REFT Holdings L.P., a subsidiary of KKR & Co. Inc. (the 'KKR Stockholder'). In addition, KREF has agreed to grant the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase from KREF up to an additional 1,050,000 primary shares of KREF common stock.
