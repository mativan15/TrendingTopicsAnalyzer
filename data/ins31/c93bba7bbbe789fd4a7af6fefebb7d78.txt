Getty ImagesThe funds will primarily be used to grow the technical team and launch the protocol which requires comprehensive legal diligence and technical audits.New Delhi: Carapace, a protocol to connect buyers and sellers of protection against default risk for under-collateralized DeFi (decentralized finance) loans, today announced raising $2.5M in pre-seed funding for its development company Starlings Lab and the launch of its test version. The funds will primarily be used to grow the technical team and launch the protocol which requires comprehensive legal diligence and technical audits.
