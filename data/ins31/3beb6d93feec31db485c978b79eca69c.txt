Mystery of bodies found in seaside resort as deaths 'unexplained'

Police are investigating after the bodies of a man and woman were found inside (Image: GETTY STOCK)Police have launched an investigation into the "unexplained" deaths of a man and a woman found inside a home in the popular UK seaside resort of Skegness in Lincolnshire. Lincolnshire Police officers were called to a house on Grosvenor Road at 3.26pm on Thursday and found the two people dead at the property. The deaths of the man and woman are currently being treated as unexplained and the bodies have not yet been formally identified.
