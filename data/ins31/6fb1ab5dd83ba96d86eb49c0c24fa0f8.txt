IF you struggle to get to sleep at night, it may be that you need to invest in a new mattress that's better suited to you.
But why does the key to a good night's sleep make such a big dent in our bank accounts?
NASA were the first to develop memory-foam technology for use by their astronautsCredit: Getty
Why are Tempur mattresses so expensive?
Tempur was the first mattress brand to bring memory foam mattresses out for us to buy and has built up an amazing reputation over the past decades... but what makes them cost so much?
Tempur uses NASA-developed memory foam technologies and are the only brand certified by the United States Space Foundation which they say is proof that their mattress quality is superior to other brands.
