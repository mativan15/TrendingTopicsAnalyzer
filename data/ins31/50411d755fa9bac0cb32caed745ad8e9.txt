'Losing $20 Million A Month On Starlink Internet Service In Ukraine,' Tweets Elon Musk
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 11:34:03

Billionaire Elon Musk said yesterday that SpaceX's Starlink services have not received any funding from the U.S. Department of Defense, a day after reports said that the Pentagon is considering paying for the Starlink satellite network in war-torn Ukraine, as per Reuters.For the uninitiated, Pentagon is the headquarters of the United States Department of Defense, and Elon Musk's Starlink is the world's first and largest satellite constellation using a low Earth orbit to deliver broadband internet capable of supporting streaming, online gaming, video calls etc.
