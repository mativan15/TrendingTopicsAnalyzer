Elon Musk says "bankruptcies need to happen" as he predicts recession
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-27 09:52:20

Billionaire Elon Musk said "bankruptcies need to happen" as he predicted there will be a short recession in the U.S. that will ultimately be a good thing.The Tesla CEO was responding to a question on Twitter from a user who asked him if he still believed there would be a recession following remarks he'd made earlier this month."Yes, but this is actually a good thing. It has been raining money on fools for too long. Some bankruptcies need to happen," Musk wrote.He also appeared to take aim those who have been working from home due to the pandemic, adding: "Also, all the Covid stay-at-home stuff has tricked people into thinking that you don't actually need to work hard.
