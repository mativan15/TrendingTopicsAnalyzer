AGARTALA: Communist Party of India (Marxist) [CPM] general secretary Sitaram Yechury on Friday said that all secular forces should unite to fight violence and communal politics and to protect the country and its democracy.'Our party wants a government of secular democratic forces to save the Constitution and build a better India,' Yechury told the reporters during his visit to Agartala.
