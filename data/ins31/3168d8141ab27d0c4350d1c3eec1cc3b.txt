The flipside of rising interest rates: Signs of hope for those with money in the bank
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-19 16:00:00

ANALYSIS: People with money in the bank have had a tough time in recent years. And while interest rates have been rising fast recently, some depositors might think they are not feeling the benefit to the extent that borrowers are feeling the pain. On Wednesday, someone with $10,000 in a 90-day term deposit with ANZ was getting just 1.95% interest. Someone with the same sum in a one-year term deposit was getting 4.1% interest.
