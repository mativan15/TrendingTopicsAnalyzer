FORTNITE fans are gushing online over the prospect that they may be able to play as a legendary Star Wars character in the game.
Gamers can download Fortnite Season 3 Chapter 3 from today and it features Darth Vader.
Gamers can download Fortnite Season 3 Chapter 3 from today (stock pic)Credit: Epic Games
Earlier this month, gameplay starring the legendary Star Wars villain leaked online, fueling speculation that the antagonist could be a playable character.
Darth Vader was once a heroic Jedi Knight but later turned to the dark side of the Force after a battle with Obi-Wan Kenobi on Mustafar nearly killed him.
