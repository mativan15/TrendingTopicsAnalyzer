Remains found on Saint John waterfront identified as missing student
cbc.ca
news
2022-10-19 15:15:00

Chi (John) Thien Tran, a 2021 graduate of Saint John High School, was last seen the night of May 3 in the Parkwood Avenue area in the city's north end. (Saint John Police Force)Remains found at Long Wharf last month have been identified as those of a missing 20-year-old student from Saint John. Police say they were able to identify Chi (John) Thien Tran through DNA, according to a news release from the Saint John Police Force. Tran was last seen on May 3 in the area of Parkwood Avenue in the north end of Saint John.
