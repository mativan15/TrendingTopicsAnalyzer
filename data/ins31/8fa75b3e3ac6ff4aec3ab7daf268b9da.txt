Emma Cui is a founding partner of web3 accelerator and Singapore based-VC fund LongHash Ventures. She pivoted to crypto after starting her career in banking, later moving to a role at McKinsey, which is where she met her co-founder. Cui is now on a mission to catalyze the growth of web3 with a $100 Million fund II that intends to invest in building better web3 infrastructure. With the market down, Cui believes that now is an optimal time for VCs to invest in companies building the foundation that will shape the future of decentralized finance (DeFi.
