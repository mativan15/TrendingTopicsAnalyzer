By Erin Jones October 19, 2022 / 10:18 PM / CBS DFW DALLAS (CBSDFW.COM) - Whether you love them or hate them, rental bikes and scooters are coming back to Dallas. However, there will be new guidelines.  From now until Nov. 4, companies can apply for a permit. Then the city will choose three businesses that can each put out 500 rental bikes or e-scooters for the 2022-2023 season.They'll have to comply with "no ride zones" in places like parks, plazas, trails and "slow ride zones" in busy entertainment districts like Deep Ellum.
