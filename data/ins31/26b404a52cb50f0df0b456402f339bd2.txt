Helix partnered with Visa DPS to enable fintechs and brands to use Visa DPS' suite of data and fraud solutions to build more profitable businesses through personalization

AUSTIN, Texas, October 21, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Helix by Q2 (NYSE: QTWO), a cloud-native core purpose-built for embedded finance, today announced it has embedded its finance platform with Visa Debit Processing Service's data and fraud solutions. This collaboration will provide a personalized and cardholder-centric approach to fraud management, allowing fintechs and consumer brands access to PCI-compliant fraud and risk management solutions through the Helix Admin Console.
