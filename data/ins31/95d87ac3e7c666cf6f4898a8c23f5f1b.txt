We work to see a cleaner, healthier world. This means working with and seeking out partnerships and organizations that share our pursuit to operate in responsible and sustainable ways.
PARSIPPANY, N.J., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- In an effort to make operations more sustainable and protect our planet, Reckitt, a global consumer products company, today signed a pledge with Ad Net Zero to further industry alignment in decarbonising ad operations. This pledge is the advertising industry's drive to reduce the carbon impact of developing, producing and running advertising to real net zero.
