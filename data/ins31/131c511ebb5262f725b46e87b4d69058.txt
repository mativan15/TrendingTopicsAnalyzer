L'Oreal's 3Q Sales Beat Analyst Estimates -- Earnings Review
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 16:44:00

By Kim Richters L'Oreal SA reported sales for the third quarter on Thursday after market close. Here's what you need to know: SALES: The French cosmetics and consumer-products company's sales came in 9.58 billion euros ($9.36 billion). Analysts expected L'Oreal to report sales of EUR9.05 billion for the three-month period, according to consensus estimates compiled by Factset. Quarterly sales a year prior were EUR8 billion. WHAT WE WATCHED: --SALES GROWTH: Sales in the quarter grew 9.1% on like-for-like basis, the company said.
