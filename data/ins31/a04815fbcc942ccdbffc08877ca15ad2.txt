The city of Clermont's average annual pay for employees is $56,849, according to the most recent data available.

In total, the city has 364 employees on its compensation list, including a wide range of positions, as of July. The list includes varying schedules of pay, contracts and other differences between departments and positions.

Why this matters: Salary information can be important for both employers and employees. Both parties are able to compare the value of certain positions and how experience is valued, which can help during the salary negotiation process.
