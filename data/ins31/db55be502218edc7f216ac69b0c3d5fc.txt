REUTERS/Amit DaveAs much as 8 billion rubles (about Rs 1,000 crore) of dividend income belonging to Indian oil firms is stuck in Russia after the Putin administration clamped down on dollar repatriation, officials said on Friday. Indian state oil firms have invested USD 5.46 billion in buying stakes in four different assets in Russia.
