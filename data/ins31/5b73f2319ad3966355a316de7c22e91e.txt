The real estate tech platform helps agents provide an efficient experience for buyers and sellers alike, with fair competition top of mind

WASHINGTON, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Final Offer, an online real estate platform that empowers top real estate agents to facilitate a more transparent offer and negotiation experience to their clients, went live in the Washington, D.C., Maryland and Virginia region today. More than 50 residential real estate listings will be published on the platform through this weekend, all of which will accept offers exclusively through finaloffer.
