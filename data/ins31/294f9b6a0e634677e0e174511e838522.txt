June 01, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time GLENDALE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Public Storage (NYSE:PSA) announced today that the Company will make a virtual presentation at REITweek 2022 on Wednesday, June 8, 2022, from 9:30 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. (ET). The presentation will be webcast and available to registered REITweek participants. Registration is complimentary and accessible through Nareit's website.

Company Information

Public Storage, a member of the S&P 500 and FT Global 500, is a REIT that primarily acquires, develops, owns, and operates self-storage facilities.
