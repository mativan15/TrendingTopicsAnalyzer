Internet praises cat making perfect square in blinds to peep through window
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-30 15:47:01

A cat went viral for breaking her owner's bedroom blinds so she could make a perfect tiny square window for herself, even though all other windows in the house had their blinds up.In a picture shared on Reddit, by the cat's owner, Chrisizeful, which also features a caption saying: "she broke my blinds to make herself a window." The cat can be seen lying by the window ledge, looking outside through the tiny window she made, satisfied with her work.
A stock image shows a cat bending some blinds to look outside the window.
