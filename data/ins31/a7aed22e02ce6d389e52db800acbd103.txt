Chandigarh imposes blanket ban on Chinese kite string
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 20:12:45

The Chandigarh administration has completely banned the procurement, manufacture and sale of Chinese kite string in the city in order to prevent harm to humans, birds, animals and the environment. The direction came following an appeal from People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) India to address bird and human deaths caused by kite string coated with glass and other synthetic material like nylon.In the notification, Nitin Kumar Yadav, UT environment secretary , said, 'Chinese kite string is a serious threat to human health, animals, birds and also disadvantageous to the environment.
