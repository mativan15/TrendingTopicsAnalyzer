Punjab Vigilance Bureau questioned SRG Group director about his relationship with Sunder Sham Arora, the key accused in a isproportionate assets case. (HT File)

 

Vigilance Bureau (VB) on Friday questioned Deepak Garg, the director of realty firm SRG Group, in a disproportionate assets case pertaining to the arrest of former cabinet minister Sunder Sham Arora.The vigilance questioned Garg on the nature of his relationship with the former minister in view of the fact it was his car that brought in the money to pay off some officials in exchange of assurance to settle the case.
