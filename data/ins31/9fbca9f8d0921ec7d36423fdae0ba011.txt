Akhilesh Meets 10-year-old Msy Fan Navratan, To Bear His Education Cost
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 06:30:00

KANPUR: Samajwadi Party (SP) chief Akhilesh Yadav on Saturday met Navratan Yadav, a 10-year-old boy who had run away from his house in Maharajganj on Tuesday to attend the cremation of SP patriarch Mulayam Singh Yadav in Saifai .The boy had managed to reach Etawah when someone helped him board a train to Kanpur instead. At Kanpur the GRP saw him alone and handed him over to the family on Thursday.After coming to know about the incident the next day, Akhilesh had asked former MLA of Nautanwa in Maharajganj, Kunwar Kaushal to escort the boy and his family to Saifai.
