With eight days to go before their first match of the T20 World Cup against Pakistan, the Indian team management and think tank is currently mulling the best possible team combinations. While the top four picks for themselves, the middle order is where it becomes tricky. Especially the No. 6-7 spot. Hardik Pandya will surely be India's No. 5, but the toss-up between Dinesh Karthik and Rishabh Pant at 6 has become the hottest debate surrounding Indian cricket.It is highly unlikely that India will go ahead with both, with Axar Patel knocking on the doors as well.
