SYRACUSE, N.Y., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- NAS Investment Solutions (NASIS), a national sponsor of high-quality real estate investment properties known for delivering reliable yield performance, has acquired a Class-A manufacturing and office facility in Skaneateles, NY. NASIS acquired the 55,000 square-foot property along with 10 investment clients structured as a Delaware Statutory Trust (DST).
