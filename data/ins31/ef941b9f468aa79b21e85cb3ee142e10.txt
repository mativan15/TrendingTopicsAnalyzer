June 02, 2022 09:53 AM Eastern Daylight Time DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The "UCC Investment Priorities for 2022 and Beyond" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.

We surveyed thousands of ITDMs about their current unified communications and collaboration technology adoption status, business goals, challenges and future investment plans.
