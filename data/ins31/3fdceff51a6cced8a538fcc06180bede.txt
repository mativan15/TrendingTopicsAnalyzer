Tesla and SpaceX chief executive Elon Musk this week delivered an ultimatum to the two companies' white-collar workers: get back in the corporate office or face firing.The demand to work at least 40 hours in the office was originally made known through leaked employee emails. Later on Wednesday, Musk appeared to confirm his edict on Twitter: When asked to comment on the email for 'people who think coming in to work is an antiquated concept,' he responded, 'They should pretend to work somewhere else.
