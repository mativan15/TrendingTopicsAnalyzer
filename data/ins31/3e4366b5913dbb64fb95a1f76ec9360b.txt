The US Mint gave Hollywood legend Anna May Wong long overdue recognition
qz.com
news
2022-10-21 19:12:00

Hollywood icon Anna May Wong is about to become the first Asian American to be featured on US currency. The US Mint has announced the pioneering actor will feature on 25 cent coins due to roll into general circulation on Monday (Oct. 24).Wong, considered the first Chinese American movie star, had a decades-long career that spanned across theater, television, radio, and over 60 films. The new quarter is one of five being released in 2022 to recognize the achievements of American women in various fields.
