Nick Train warns against any sale of Burberry on the cheap
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

Any offer for Burberry that was 'remotely' near the British luxury fashion brand's present valuation should be resisted, according to its second largest shareholder in response to takeover speculation.Investors have valued the FTSE 100 constituent far more cheaply than luxury goods brands listed on international stock exchanges, such as the Italian fashion house Prada and LVMH of France.Nick Train, 63, the star stockpicker who runs and part-owns Lindsell Train, the investment house, said he would be 'adamantly opposed' to any bid for Burberry 'at anything remotely like the current share price or valuation'.
