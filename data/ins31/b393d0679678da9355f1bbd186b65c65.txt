The aesthetics of a game of rugby league remains paramount to the fabric of the game in and around Cherbourg, the largely Aboriginal community in Queensland's South Burnett, around three hours northwest of Brisbane.In the cities and suburbs of the larger metro regions, the Pasifika revolution has changed the dynamics of the sport forever. At a certain stage, power and size become premium, to the point where the best age-group teams are locked in a physical arms race in which you either bulk up or get bulldozed.
