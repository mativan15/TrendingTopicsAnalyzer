GOGGLEBOX fans are not happy after tonight's instalment of the popular show was axed from the schedule. 
In a big shake up, C4 bosses switched Friday night's episode of Gogglebox to Saturday night instead.
Gogglebox fans are not happy that Friday's show was axed
It was replaced by Friday Night Live hosted by Ben EltonCredit: C4
But viewers who had not got the memo were left fuming after tuning in to find Friday Night Live on their screens instead of their favourite armchair telly critics.
Hosted by Ben Elton, the one-off rival of the stand-up and entertainment show was being aired to celebrate C4's 40th anniversary.
