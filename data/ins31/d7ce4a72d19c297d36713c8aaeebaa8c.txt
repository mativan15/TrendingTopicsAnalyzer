October 20, 2022 03:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Wells Fargo announced its participation today at the Association for Financial Professionals (AFP) Conference in Philadelphia from October 23-26.

In July, Wells Fargo joined AFP's Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Awareness Initiative for Treasury and Finance. As part of the company's support of the initiative, Troy Keen, Corporate & Investment Banking's Chief Development Officer will participate in a DEI roundtable, The Role of Upskilling in Achieving a More Diverse Workforce at the conference on Sunday, October 23 at 2:00 p.
