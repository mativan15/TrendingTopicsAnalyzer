6:51 AM ETAt a glance, Week 7 appears to be one of mismatches, as nine of the 14 games (64.3%) posted an opening line north of five points. That's a big number, but with how bad the bottom rung of NFL teams is, your gut reaction may be to back the favorites.Be careful. Very careful.It's been no secret that underdogs have been profitable on the whole this season, but with a week like the one we have coming, it's important to add context. The cover (and outright win) rates for underdogs of five or fewer points this season essentially matchup with the rates from 2016-21, but once you cross that 5-point spread, the dogs are barkin'.
