An Australian company will ship more than 1.25 million cans of baby formula to the United States to help a nationwide shortage in the states. The United States has been grappling with shortages of baby formulaThose shortages were brought on by the pandemic and exacerbated by a recall by a leading supplierAn Australian company is sending 1.25 million cans to the US to help alleviate the shortfallSome of the baby formula is currently in stock for transport and more will be produced by Sydney-based manufacturer Bubs Australia in the coming weeks and months.
