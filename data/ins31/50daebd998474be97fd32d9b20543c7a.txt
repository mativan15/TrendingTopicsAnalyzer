EXCLUSIVE: The fourth annual Macro HBCU Summit is underway Tuesday in-person again this year at Howard University in Washington, DC. The microfestival is hosting panels, food trucks, giveaways and a film screening. 

	The inaugural summit debuted at Howard in 2019 with the mission to engage and educate students on careers, opportunities and paths in the media and entertainment fields.  

	Confirmed to appear this year are Naturi Naughton, Oscar-winning production designer Hannah Beachler, M88 partner/talent manager Oronde Garrett, UTA talent agent Yasmine Pearl, journalist/TV host Gia Peppers, managing director and Chase Sapphire GM Marleta Ross and Macro founder and CEO Charles D.
