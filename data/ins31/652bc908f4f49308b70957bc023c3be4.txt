 The Durham District School Board (DDSB) says it is investigating allegations that a teacher used a racial slur at one of its schools in Ajax, Ont.
In a statement emailed to Global News Friday, the board said it is 'investigating reports that a teacher used a racial slur in the classroom' at Viola Desmond public school.'We are taking this matter very seriously and an occasional teacher is currently teaching this class while we conduct an investigation,' the statement read.
