[Photo provided to China Daily] Stock Connect scheme assists Chinese firms in financing overseas listings, expansion China's efforts to link its capital market with the rest of the world have not been held up by geopolitical tensions or market volatility, with global depository receipts (GDRs), which are gaining increasing preference from Chinese companies, serving as solid proof.
