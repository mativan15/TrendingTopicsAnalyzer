The AES Corporation Roughly 11% of the portfolio is allocated to U.S. utility AES (NYSE:AES) and Canadian independent power producer Polaris Renewable Energy (PIF). Both positions have had modest performance this year. AES has been far more stable and traded in a tighter range but is also down for the year by 5.4%, inclusive of reinvesting the dividends into treasury bills. AES has continued to build its robust pipeline of energy projects and its strong backlog of signed power purchase agreements (PPAs).
