The Delhi-Jaipur Highway, which is part of the National Highway 48, is strewn with bad stretches full of potholes and issues with waterlogging during heavy rainfall.
 








File photo of vehicles moving through waterlogged road after incessant rainfall at Delhi-Jaipur highway last month. (ANI)
 

Delhi-Jaipur Highway, which was one of India's top roads, has turned into a nightmare for commuters due to poor maintenance. After stretches of the highway got waterlogged recently due to heavy rains, top officials from National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) and Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) visited to inspect the problematic areas on the NH48 section of the highway.
