Amazon consumer chief Dave Clark resigns after 23 years

June 3, 2022 / 6:12 PM / CBS/AP The Amazon executive overseeing the company's worldwide consumer business is resigning after 23 years as the e-commerce giant deals with its overabundance of warehouse space.Dave Clark's last day at the Seattle-based company will be July 1, Amazon CEO Andy Jassy wrote Friday in a company blog post. Amazon did not name a replacement for Clark but Jassy wrote that he expects to have "an update" over the next few weeks. "As much as I have loved the ride, it is time for me to say goodbye to start a new journey," Clark wrote in an email to his team that he posted on Twitter.
