Corporate Philanthropy 2022: Philanthropist of the Year
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 17:04:00

From the site plan approval to the designing of the project, Ryan Companies has worked with the Arizona Humane Society nearly every step of the way on the development of the nonprofit's new facility.

The facility, known as the Rob and Melani Walton Papago Park Campus, broke ground in November 2021. But Ryan Companies had been involved in the project since 2019, said Chuck Carefoot, Southwest region president at Ryan Companies.

'We've been very fortunate that we have been involved with the Humane Society since the absolute early stages of site selection,' Carefoot said.
