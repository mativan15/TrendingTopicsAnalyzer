‘Largest employer in region with over 7,000 employees'DAVAO CITY - VXI
Global Solutions, a leading BPO and CX provider, unveiled its fifth site in
Davao City, cementing its current legacy as the largest employer in the region.VXI
SVP and Country Manager Eppie Titong with Davao
City Councilor Bernie Al-ag representing Davao City Mayor Sebastian Duterte.The company
announced the new location at SP Dacudao Loop, Agdao, Davao Park District,
which will add to VXI's over 40 global service locations and generate new job
opportunities for citizens in the region.
