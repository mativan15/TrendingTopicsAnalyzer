Jackson, WY, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- CurPay, a leader in AI powered technologies, is proud to announce a trading partnership with BlockFills. The addition of BlockFills's amazing multi-asset technology platform to the CurPay ecosystem will bolster their ever-growing product line. CurPay's mission to help with the world's adoption of Crypto has taken the next step, entering the Merchant Processing space. CurPay's technology provides seamless acceptance for day-to-day retail Merchants to receive hassle free Crypto transactions from their consumers.
