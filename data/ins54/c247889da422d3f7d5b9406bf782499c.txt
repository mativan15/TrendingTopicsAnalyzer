The owner of a diamond factory and four police personnel attached to Mahidharpura station were booked after the factory manager allegedly took his life in Surat's Katargam after being tortured in police custody, his brother said in a complaint to the Surat police Friday night.
Junagadh native Mukesh Sojitra, who resides in Katargam, allegedly took his life on Friday afternoon after his employer Vipul Moradiya and a few police personnel tortured him over an alleged case of theft at his workplace, his brother Kishorbhai said.
