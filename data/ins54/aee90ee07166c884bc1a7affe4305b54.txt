Autistic teen wanting to splurge $900 inheritance on concert splits views
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 08:52:03

A mom who doesn't want her autistic teen to "waste" her inheritance money has divided the internet.In a post shared to UK-based popular discussion site, Mumsnet, the user 11112222 explained her 17-year-old daughter had inherited £1,800 (about $2,000) from her late granddad.She points out her daughter's particular interests change after a "year or so" but she currently likes Taylor Swift and hopes to buy a ticket for her coming 2023 'Midnights' concert. But, the mom is concerned as her teenager wants to fork out £800 ($900) on a pit seat.
