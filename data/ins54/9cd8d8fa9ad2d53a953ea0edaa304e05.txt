Astros game times announced for ALCS
chron.com
news
2022-10-16 22:03:23

The Houston Astros announced game times for the ALCS on Sunday, as they wait to see who they will face out of the Cleveland Guardians and New York Yankees.

Houston will host the first game of the series on Wednesday and Game 2 on Thursday, with both games scheduled to start at 6:37 p.m. The Astros will then head on the road for at least two games and possibly three, before returning home for Games 6 and 7, if necessary.
