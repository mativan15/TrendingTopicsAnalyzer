Mumbai to Bengaluru in five hours? Nitin Gadkari promises new expressway
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 03:54:10

Currently it takes at least thrice the time to cover the distance between India's business capital and IT capital if one chooses to travel in a car.
 








Union Minister Nitin Gadkari has announced that the NHAI will build a green express highway between Mumbai and Bengaluru soon which will run via Pune.
 

You may soon prefer travelling in a car from Mumbai to Bengaluru than take a train or a flight. Union Minister Nitin Gadkari has promised that it will not take more than five hours to cover the distance between the two cities once a green express highway comes up.
