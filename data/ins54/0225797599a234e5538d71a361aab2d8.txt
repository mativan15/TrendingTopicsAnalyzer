DOWNERS GROVE, Ill., October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sevan Multi-Site Solutions, Inc. (Sevan) provides customized site refreshes along an entire program's lifecycle for retail and restaurant clients.

Sevan, a global leader in innovative program management for clients with multiple sites, reached a significant milestone—refreshing over 28,000 retail sites and 21,000 restaurant sites.

Sevan partners with the world's biggest brands to manage multi-site program rollouts across the United States, Canada and the United Kingdom—resulting in fast, data-driven and customized site solutions.
