June 03, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BitNile Holdings, Inc. (NYSE American: NILE), a diversified holding company ('BitNile' or the 'Company'), announces today the closing of its public offering of 144,000 shares of its 13.00% Series D Cumulative Redeemable Perpetual Preferred Stock (the "Series D Preferred Stock") at a price to the public of $25.00 per share. Gross proceeds from the offering were approximately $3.6 million, before deducting offering expenses. Net proceeds to the Company, after payment of commissions, non-accountable fees and offering expenses, are expected to be approximately $3.
