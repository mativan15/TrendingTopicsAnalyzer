UK's Queen Consort Camilla to present Booker Prize for 2022
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 16:15:00

AgenciesOn Monday, the Booker Prize Foundation will conduct its first significant event since 2019 and the Queen Consort Camilla will deliver the prize to one of six hopefuls.Alan Garner, 88, who is the oldest novelist ever nominated, or Claire Keegan, who wrote the shortest novel in the history of the Booker Prize may win this year. On the occasion, the singer-songwriter Dua Lipa will speak about her passion for reading at London's Roundhouse. Late Hilary Mantel, a two-time Booker Prize winner, will also receive a tribute.
