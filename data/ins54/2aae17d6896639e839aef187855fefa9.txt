Fox Corporation Taps Magnite to Drive Programmatic Monetization Across OneFOX

NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Fox Corporation (Nasdaq: FOXA, FOX; 'FOX' or the 'Company') today announced a partnership with Magnite (Nasdaq: MGNI), the world's largest independent omnichannel sell-side advertising platform (SSP), to serve as the launch partner to power programmatic campaigns for OneFOX video inventory across the company's leading entertainment, sports, streaming and news portfolio. Today's announcement is part of FOX's continuing strategy of providing more innovative advertising tools and resources to further deliver integration and alignment with its industry-leading brands, as well as enhancing an advertiser's return on investment through effective innovative solutions.
