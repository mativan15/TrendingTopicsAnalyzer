Published: 12:42 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 12:42 EDT, 19 October 2022 A devout Christian urged her friend to sell her dilapidated home for £4 million and enjoy the money - before being allegedly killed and decapitated by her, a court has been told.Mee Kuen Chong, 67, who was known as Deborah, was allegedly beaten to death by Jemma Mitchell, 38, after changing her mind about bankrolling her £200,000 renovation project.Ms Chong has been described as 'vulnerable' during proceedings and had schizophrenia, but believed Mitchell, of Brondesbury Park, Willesden, was 'healing' her.
