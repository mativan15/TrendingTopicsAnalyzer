LOS ANGELES and DENVER, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nextbite, a leader and innovator in virtual restaurant solutions, today announced that producer and tech entrepreneur will.i.am has joined the fast-growing company as Advisor. He will guide Nextbite regarding strategic initiatives in both brand-building and creative go-to-marketing strategies for its delivery-only restaurant concepts.The music producer and Black Eyed Peas frontman, whose 25-year music career necessitates ordering delivery on the road and while in-studio, was frustrated by the lack of flavorful and nutritious meals available for delivery.
