Chhattisgarh CM accuses SG of levelling baseless allegations against him in SC
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 02:30:00

NEW DELHI: Chhattisgarh chief minister Bhupesh Baghel Friday accused solicitor general Tushar Mehta of levelling baseless allegations against him in the Supreme Court to sully his image, saying the senior official should desist from such 'politically motivated' actions.Speaking to reporters after the inauguration of three-day National Tribal Festival, Baghel said such 'mischief' will be countered appropriately.During a hearing in the apex court, the SG had alleged that the CM met a judge before bail was granted to some accused in a case, suggesting the meeting was to influence the judgement.
