Joe Du Bey is co-founder and CEO of Eden, an all-in-one people operations platform for employee-centric companies.
getty In June 2022, Yelp cofounder and CEO Jeremy Stoppelman announced the closure of Yelp's offices in New York, Chicago and Washington, D.C. While solidifying Yelp's commitment to remote work, Stoppelman also called hybrid work policies 'the worst of three options' and 'the hell of half measures.' To that I say: If hybrid is 'hell,' you're doing it wrong. The data is clear—a survey conducted by Eden found that 82% of tech workers want access to an office at least part time.
