City to get cardiology institute
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 07:45:00

Vadodara: Vadodara will have a state-of-the-art cardiology institute and multi-speciality hospital.The state government on Saturday announced that foundation stone for setting up the new cardiology institute will be laid soon.The institute will come up at Ansuya Leprosy campus on Ajwa Road. It will be managed by state-run SSG Hospital .The state government has allotted Rs 22.50 crore for the initial construction.The overall provision is said to be Rs 150 crore.When made operational, the institute will have a 238-bed cardiac unit with facilities for angiography, angioplasty and other cardiac procedures.
