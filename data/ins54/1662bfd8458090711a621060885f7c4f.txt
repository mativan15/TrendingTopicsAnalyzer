Marc Piasecki/Getty Images Entertainment The Walt Disney Company (NYSE:DIS) had its price target cut by Truist Securities early Thursday morning as the theme park and entertainment company has struggled over the past year. Truist Securities has kept their Buy rating on DIS but slashed their price target to $135 a share, down from $160. Moving forward, Truist is focused on the impacts of COVID-19 and how it will affect Disney's related Shanghai and Hong Kong parks.
