Omnicom's global content and production arm, eg+ Worldwide, has appointed Eva Neveau as global chief creative officer, a new role for the company. Neveau joins to help unify the group's three brands—eg+, Designory and Mother Tongue—from specialist offerings to a holistic content solution.
