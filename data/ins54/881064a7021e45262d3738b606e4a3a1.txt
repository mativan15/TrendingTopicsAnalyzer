Putin's Confederacy playbook: Biden can turn it around on him by unleashing US energy
nypost.com
news
2022-10-19 01:05:43

As the weather turns cold, energy prices rise, German factories grind to a halt and the Eiffel Tower's lights go out, Vladimir Putin is ensuring there will be no respite for the Europeans. After spending much of the summer fiddling with the gas taps to Europe, the Russian president and his fellow energy producers in OPEC+ announced this month they'll remove 2 million barrels of oil a day from global markets. Saudi Aramco has reassured its customers not to worry: Oil supplies will remain steady — for Asia.
