Tesla, Inc. (NASDAQ:TSLA) has been facing production disruption since the start of the year amid the Ukraine-Russia war and China's COVID-19 lockdowns. What Happened: Tuesday, Piper Sandler analyst Alexander Potter noted that German Model Y buyers now have to wait for 23.5 weeks, or about 5.5 months, to take delivery of their vehicles.
