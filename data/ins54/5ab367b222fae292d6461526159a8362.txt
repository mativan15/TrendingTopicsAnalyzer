State Senate Approves Medicaid Expansion (Raleigh, NC) -- Medicaid expansion is back on the table in North Carolina. The state Senate voted yesterday to expand the program, which would make healthcare more accessible for around half-a-million residents. The maximum annual income for an individual to qualify would be raised to 20-thousand-dollars. The bill reportedly faces some opposition in the state House. Recounts Underway In Buncombe County (Buncombe County, NC) -- A couple of primary election recounts are underway in Asheville.
