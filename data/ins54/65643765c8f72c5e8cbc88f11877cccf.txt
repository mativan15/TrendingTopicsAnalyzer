World Food Program USA taking new headquarters

The World Food Program USA has picked a new headquarters location, signing a full-floor lease for 13,277 square feet at The RMR Group's 1750 H St. NW. This marks a 54% expansion of its space from its current home at 1725 Eye St. NW. The lease begins in May 2023.

The nonprofit supports the United Nations World Food Programme through fundraising and programming around issues related to world hunger.
