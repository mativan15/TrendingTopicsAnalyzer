How Quarantines Have Driven Consumers Back To Stores getty 
With the void of human interaction that the pandemic has brought on for the past two years, the desire for people to experience in-person events has increased. As the saying goes, absence makes the heart grow fonder. Compared to pre-pandemic times when consumers were prioritizing convenience over heading out of their homes and into shopping malls, we can now see the pendulum swing in the other direction. Let's look at how consumer behavior has shifted.
