Assam CM Himanta Biswa Sarma may not drop any minister during Cabinet expansion
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 07:45:00

In the first expansion of Himanta Biswa Sarma-led government in Assam no minister is likely to be dropped. The first expansion of the one-year-old government will take place on June 9. Sarma has hinted that one minister will be inducted in the council of ministers. Four berths are vacant in the council of ministers. Sarma on Friday said, 'Nobody will be dropped and there is no question of dropping anyone.' Sarma said on Thursday that he will expand his council of ministers on June 9. Sarma will visit New Delhi on June 6 and finalise the expansion.
