ICICI Pru Life shares drop 0.12% as Sensex rises

2022-10-19 06:45:00

Analysts see 15,900 to continue to pose as the immediate resistance. Shares of the ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Ltd. traded at Rs 512.9 on BSE at 11:47AM (IST) on Wednesday, down 0.12 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 430.0 and a high of Rs 682.15. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap down opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 86.34 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 5.95 per share and 7.87 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 12,883 shares changed hands on the counter till 11:47AM (IST).
