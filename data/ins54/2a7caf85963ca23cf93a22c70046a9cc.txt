Ameesh Divatia is Co-founder & CEO of Baffle, Inc., with a proven track record of turning innovative ideas into successful businesses. getty Few executive positions are as pressure-packed as the chief information security officer (CISO). In one study, 21% of CISOs said their companies offered no support to help alleviate the stresses of their job. It is no surprise that the same report found that the average tenure for CISOs was just 26 months. CISOs are expected to possess the necessary IT background to see where potential security threats exist and the business expertise to know how these threats could impact every line of business.
