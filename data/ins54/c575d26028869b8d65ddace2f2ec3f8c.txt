Published: 09:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 09:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 Eight months after Haas F1 hastily pulled Russian sponsorship off its cars following Russia's invasion of Ukraine, the team has a new title sponsor. Even better for Haas, the only American-owned team in Formula One will now be backed by a global company headquartered in Dallas, Texas. MoneyGram was introduced as the new title sponsor for the Haas race team on Thursday ahead of the United States Grand Prix in Austin, Texas.
