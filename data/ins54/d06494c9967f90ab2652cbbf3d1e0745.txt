October 17, 2022 Produced by Christine Hatfield and Aden Choate Illinois Public Media Election Day is November 8, 2022. Many young people are headed to the polls for the first time and they are plenty more who aren't old enough to vote and who are finding ways to engage with our democratic system. Over the last couple of months, Illinois Public Media Director of Community Content and Engagement Kimberlie Kranich visited multiple high schools and the campus of the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
