Joe Biden heads to Uvalde to comfort grieving families of school shooting

2022-05-29 14:33:22

Joe Biden is visiting Uvalde in Texas to comfort grieving families following a mass school shooting which left 21 people dead. Along with the first lady Jill Biden, the US President will visit a memorial erected at the school and meet with victims' families, survivors and responders. Last Tuesday, a teenage gunman stormed Robb Elementary School in Uvalde killing 19 children and two teachers in the shooting.Mr Biden's visit comes amid mounting criticism over the police response to the shooting, the worst in the US in a decade.
