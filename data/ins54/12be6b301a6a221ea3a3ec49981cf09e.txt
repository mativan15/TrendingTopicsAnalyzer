Riot Games Acquires Wargaming Sydney Studio
ign.com
news
2022-10-18 07:04:22

Riot Games, the video game developer and publisher best known for League of Legends, has fully acquired Wargaming Sydney studio today. The studio, which will now go by the name Riot Sydney, is a worldwide development powerhouse with a track record of producing games that are among the best in the world, as well as prototypes for upcoming game features, add-ons, and console ports. 'We are really excited to bring these talented developers and teams to Riot,' said Riot Games co-founder and president Marc Merrill.
