PARIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Shippeo, a global leader in real-time multimodal transportation visibility, today announced it has been positioned by Gartner, Inc. in the 'Challengers' quadrant of the Gartner® Magic Quadrant for Real-time Transportation Visibility Platforms1. Shippeo is positioned furthest and highest on Ability to Execute and Completeness of Vision in the Challengers quadrant. The ‘ability to execute' decision criteria take into consideration companies' products and services, sales execution and pricing, customer experience and operations, as well as overall viability, market responsiveness and marketing execution.
