Google search introduces suicide helpline for the people of Pakistan
mashable.com
news
2022-06-03 08:00:00

> Social Good According to a statement from Google, the internet giant has developed a suicide hotline one box for Pakistan, which allows users to connect immediately with a suicide helpline at the top of the search results page. The "Umang Pakistan's" helpline will now be displayed to anyone in Pakistan seeking suicide-related queries such as "suicide support" and other searches. Google will now present the hotline at the top of the page on any searches related to self-harm. Umang is a mental health helpline recognized by the World Health Organization (WHO) that provides help to Pakistanis who are resorting to harming themselves.
