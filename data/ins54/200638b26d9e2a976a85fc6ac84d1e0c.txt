Podcast and video content will introduce influential Hispanic and Latino personalities while expanding JefeBet's footprint as the premier Spanish-first sports and entertainment site

LAS VEGAS, NV --News Direct-- JefeBet

JefeBet, the premier gambling and multimedia company for bilingual and Spanish-speaking Americans, operated by FSG Digital (FSG), announced today the launch of the JefeBet Podcast Network. The new network of live and pre-recorded podcast and vodcast shows will expand JefeBet's existing creator network of hosts, creators and correspondents from five to 15.
