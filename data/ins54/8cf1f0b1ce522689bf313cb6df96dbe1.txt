 The Chandigarh administration on Wednesday received an award for being the best performing state/UT in implementation of affordable rental housing scheme. UT adviser Dharam Pal received the award from the Union minister of urban development, during the India Urban Conclave at Rajkot, inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
