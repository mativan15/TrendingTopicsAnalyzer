Reporter's Notebook: Will push for gun control be different this time?
foxnews.com
news
2022-06-03 16:58:06

It is said that for every action there is a reaction.But historically, that hasn't been the case when Congress tried to advance legislation in the aftermath of mass shootings.We have heard for decades now that after a particular mass shooting "things will be different."They rarely are.But, this time may be a little different.This doesn't mean Congress will get something done. But the machinations in the wake of the shootings in Buffalo, New York, Uvalde, Texas, and Tulsa, Oklahoma, are different.
