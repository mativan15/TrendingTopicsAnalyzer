How the cost of going to Sacramento-area colleges and universities stacks up against others in California
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 13:46:00

California has some of most highly regarded colleges and universities in the nation, and those schools attract thousands of students from out of state.

But what those students pay for college depends a lot on where they go.

The Business Journal looked at colleges and universities in California with at least 1,500 students for which the U.S. Department of Education had expense data covering the past three school years. The Business Journal only looked at degree-granting four-year institutions that enroll first-year students.
