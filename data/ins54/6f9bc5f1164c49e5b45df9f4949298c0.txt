ATHENS, Greece, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Performance Shipping Inc. (NASDAQ: PSHG), ('we' or the 'Company'), a global shipping company specializing in the ownership of tanker vessels, today announced that it has completed a restructuring of the debt held by Mango Shipping Corp. ('Mango Shipping'), an affiliate of Aliki Paliou, the Company's Chairperson. In the transaction, the Company issued Series C Preferred Shares in a private placement in exchange for all outstanding Series B Preferred Shares held by Mango Shipping and the forgiveness of principal in the amount of approximately $4.
