HMRC issues warning to Britons on key July tax deadline
express.co.uk
finance
2022-06-04 19:21:05

HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) oversees numerous issues when it comes to tax, with tax credits a key priority. Those who are claiming tax credits will need to renew these by the date shown on their renewal pack.For those people, this date is July 31, 2022, leaving just over a month to sort out the matter.HMRC has warned Britons who miss the deadline that their 'tax credit payments will stop'.Its website adds: 'You'll be sent a statement and will have to pay back the tax credits you've received since April 6, 2022.
