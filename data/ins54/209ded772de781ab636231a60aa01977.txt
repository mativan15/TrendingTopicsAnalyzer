The Inside of a Gnome Home. (image: author)ne of the happiness factors important to any good story is having a home base. This is the place that you return to again and again on your Storyspinner journey. It can be problematic if you don't have a supportive place to return to after all your adventuring.If a home base is lacking, likely it is because you have neglected your Gnome.The Gnome in your Home is the spirit of place. There are countless folktales that speak of this often mischievous spirit that resides in our homes and businesses.
