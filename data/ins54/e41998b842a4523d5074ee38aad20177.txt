For 2022-23, the targeted revenue from the sale of scrap has been set at Rs 4,400 crore, Railways said.The Indian Railways registered record scrap sales during the first six months (as of September 2022) of financial year 2022-23, new data showed. The Railways sells scrap through e-auction.

Going by the data, as much as 3,93,421 metric ton of iron scrap has been sold off so far in 2022-23. The amount sold during the same six months of 2021-22 was 3,60,732 metric ton. Railways posted earnings of Rs 2,582 crore from scrap sales during the period.
