VIENNA, Va., Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- MicroHealth LLC, a leading Health Information Technology company, announced today that it ranked 35th on the Washington Business Journal's list of fastest-growing private companies in the Washington region. Despite the unprecedented times, the company has seen exponential revenue growth over the past two years.  The Washington Business Journal's list of the 50 fastest-growing private companies in the greater Washington area is ranked by average percent change in revenue.
