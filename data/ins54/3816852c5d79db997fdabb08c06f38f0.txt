The biggest race in motorsports, the 2022 U.S. Grand Prix at Circuit of The Americas (COTA), will feature brands from Austin-based companies such as Cadence Design Systems, Dell, and AMD. The world of Formula 1 (F1) is coming to Austin; with it, many tech companies are getting in on the action. F1 is known for its cutting-edge technology and precision engineering, so it's no surprise that some of Austin's top tech firms want to get involved.
