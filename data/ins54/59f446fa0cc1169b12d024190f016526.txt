October 20, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time DENVER--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Western Union Company (NYSE: WU) (the 'Company') will host a meeting with investors and analysts today where it will detail the Company's Evolve 2025 strategy that connects to its purpose by bringing high-value, accessible financial services to the world's aspiring populations.

'We're excited to introduce our new strategy for this iconic 171-year-old company, which builds upon our existing set of assets including our global and trusted brand, our retail network of nearly 600,000 agent locations, and our strong compliance capabilities,' said Devin McGranahan, President and Chief Executive Officer.
