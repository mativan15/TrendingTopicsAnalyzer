TendedBar to Bring Additional Speed to Formula 1's Aramco United States Grand Prix with Industry's Most Innovative Automated Bar Service

2022-10-19 12:31:00

AUSTIN, Texas, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- TendedBar, the most innovative cocktail dispensing experience in the industry, will build on the success of its debut at Circuit of The Americas (COTA) by bringing 10 TendedBars to serve fans at this week's Formula 1 Aramco United States Grand Prix. TendedBar is working with Levy, the hospitality partner of COTA, and DBK Studio, Levy's innovation studio, to launch these new beverage experiences that safely serve fans as fast as one drink every 10 seconds.
