AgenciesShares of digital signature certificate provider eMudhra listed at ₹270, about 5.5% above its IPO price of ₹256 apiece.Shares of digital signature certificate provider eMudhra listed at ₹270, about 5.5% above its IPO price of ₹256 apiece. After rising as much as 9% to ₹279 earlier in the day, the stock gave up early gains and ended at ₹258.85, which is 1.1% over the issue price. The initial public offering of eMudhra was subscribed 2.72 times. The ₹412.79-crore public issue was priced in a band of ₹243 to ₹256 per share.
