Robot competition inspires young people's interest in tech
chinadaily.com.cn
tech
2022-05-30 06:20:00

A young competitor adjusts his robot in Loudi, Central China's Hunan province, on May 28, 2022. [Photo/VCG] Robot competition inspires young people's interest in science and technology, a primary productive force, in Loudi, Central China's Hunan province. More than 500 people competed in eight project categories in intelligent relay, robot control and smart movement.The entries showcased young people's enthusiasm in science and technology and even the hope of rejuvenating the country through science and technology.
