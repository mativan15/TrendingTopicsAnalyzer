Justin Sullivan/Getty Images News While the market has definitely soured in the roughly two months since my last write-up on Broadcom (NASDAQ:AVGO), not all that much has really changed in terms of performance trends or the long-term investment thesis for this leading semiconductor and enterprise software company. The acquisition of VMware (VMW) is certainly material, but not any sort of departure from the company's stated M&A or capital allocation strategy, and I think VMware will prove to be another worthwhile "solid but not spectacular" type of deal for the company.
