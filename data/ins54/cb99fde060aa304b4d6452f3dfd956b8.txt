June 03, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATHOL, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--At the Board of Directors' meeting held on June 1, 2022, the Directors of the L. S. Starrett Company set the record date for those entitled to vote at the annual meeting of stockholders, to be held on October 19, 2022, as at the close of business on August 22, 2022.
