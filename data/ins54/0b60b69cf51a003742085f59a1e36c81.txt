Explainer: CCI draws red line for tech firms with fines on Google, booking sites
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 09:15:00

A Rs 1,337.76-crore penalty on Google, and a Rs 392-crore fine on travel and hospitality sites MakeMyTrip, GoIbibo and Oyo are the latest moves in the Competition Commission of India's (CCI) crackdown on tech companies as the antitrust watchdog looks to rein in anti-competitive practices in the sector. The two decisions, announced in a span of two days, have come in the run-up to the CCI setting up a dedicated Digital Markets and Data Unit (DMDU).What has the CCI said in its orders?In its order against Google on Thursday, the competition watchdog noted that the company was found 'abusing its market dominant position' across multiple categories related to the Android mobile device ecosystem in the country.
