Have you ever thought about how incredibly difficult it is to be an athlete? So many factors affect how well you can perform on the field, court, course, trail, hill, or what-have-you, whether in practice or competitions. And one small mistake or failure in any one of those many areas may result in a disappointing performance with little to no opportunity to turn the day around. I actually see sports in three ways: as always complex, sometimes complicated, and, yes, in the end, quite simple.
