Pollution Control Board cracks down on an illegal unit manufacturing single-use plastics seizing several tens of tonnes of banned plastic in Upkar Layout in Hosur on Tuesday.
| Photo Credit: N. BASHKARAN
 The Pollution Control Board seized over 10 tonnes of banned single-use plastic from an illegal manufacturing unit in Hosur on Tuesday. The seizure was made during a raid at Upkar Layout along the inter-state border.The industrial unit operated by a Rajasthan man had three units within the compound, where banned single-use plastic carry bags were found stacked in rolls freshly manufactured.
