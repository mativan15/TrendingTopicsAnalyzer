CCAGW PAC Endorses Nebraska Rep. Adrian Smith for Re-election to the House of Representatives
businesswire.com
news
2022-10-17 00:00:00

WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Rep. Adrian Smith (R-Neb.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.

CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Rep. Smith has an exemplary lifetime rating of 88 percent, making him a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero.' In 2021, Rep. Smith scored a perfect 100 percent, earning him a 'Taxpayer Super Hero' award.
