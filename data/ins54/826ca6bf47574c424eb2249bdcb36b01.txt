October 21, 2022 08:03 AM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Innovation Policy Solutions LLC (iPolicy), a leading government relations firm that promotes biomedical innovation through policy change, today announced the promotion of Megan Anderson Brooks, PhD, to President and the addition of Lindsey Trischler as Principal. In these new roles, Dr. Anderson Brooks and Ms. Trischler will look to build on the firm's recent success, expand its client base, and enable broader patient access to innovative technologies.
