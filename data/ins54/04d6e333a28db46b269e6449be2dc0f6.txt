Hyderabad restaurant Green Bawarchi catches fire, no casualty till now
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 12:15:00

On Saturday morning, the people of Raidurgam experienced a devastating fire. The fire broke out in a restaurant named Green Bawarchi in Hyderabad at around 11.30 AM. The primary reason for the fire is suspected to be an electrical short circuit. Although around 20-30 customers were present on the rooftop with 10 workers of the restaurant, no casualties have been reported till now. The fire first broke out on the restaurant's first floor and massively spread to the entire building. The two floors of the restaurant were completely engulfed in flames.
