Three dead, 11 wounded in Philadelphia mass shooting
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-05 11:18:37

Three people have died and at least 11 others were injured in a mass shooting in Philadelphia late on Saturday night, authorities said.The Philadelphia Police Department responded to reports of gunfire in a large crowd on South Street at around 11.30 p.m.Police Inspector D. F. Pace said officers deployed to patrol the busy area heard numerous gunshots near Second and South Streets and saw multiple shooters firing into the crowd.An officer fired at a shooter while the person was firing a gun, Pace said.
