Kitron to host Capital Markets Day
globenewswire.com
news
2022-10-17 00:00:00

(2022-10-17) Kitron ASA plans to host a Capital Markets Day in Oslo, Norway, on 13 December 2022 to provide an update on strategic and financial ambitions. Senior management and board members will be present, and there will be a question and answer session. The event will start at 09:00 CET and last for approximately two hours. It will take place at a central location in Oslo. There will also be a live webcast. Details on registration for the physical event and the link to the webcast will be announced at a later date.
