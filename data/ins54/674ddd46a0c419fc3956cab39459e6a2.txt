Published: 13:09 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 13:09 EDT, 20 October 2022 Former Apprentice winner Sian Gabbidon has revealed she is 'ready to have fun' after she parted ways with Lord Alan Sugar earlier this year. The business owner, 29, who was hired as the business mogul's partner in 2018, told FUBAR Radio on Thursday that she was now 'ready to do her own thing.'Speaking to the show's hosts Bobby Norris and Stephen Leng, Sian explained: 'We parted ways, I decided that I wanted to do my own thing.
