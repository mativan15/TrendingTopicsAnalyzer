Image source, ReutersImage caption, It's not just the UK's media that has been following the political chaosThe UK's economic and political turmoil over the past few weeks - culminating in nearly all of Liz Truss's original finance plans now being axed - has been watched around the world.It is rare for close allies to comment on each other's key policies at home - and if they do, it's unlikely to be an outright criticism.But at the weekend US President Joe Biden weighed in, saying Ms Truss's original plan was a "mistake" and it was "predictable" that she would have to backtrack.
