Many have called the current era the Information Age, but it is easy to see that we are living through a more specific moment dedicated to data, data and…. Oh yeah, data.  As Clive Humby, chief data scientist of Starcount, once said, 'Data is the new oil.' Data is worth real dollars. According to a study done at The University of Texas at Austin, a 10% increase in data usability can lead to $2 billion in revenue for a large company.  With that kind of money on the line, it is no wonder that at least 20% of the Fortune 500 use smart tools to verify their location database.
