opinionIn 2016, I thought Hillary Clinton would win the presidency until late on election night. I was on live TV when Michigan, Pennsylvania and Wisconsin began to tilt red, and the shock became visible on the faces of the anchors and the crew in the studio. A Donald Trump victory wasn't in the script.In my defense, few in the chattering classes foresaw as Election Day approached what voters were about to wrought.Ever since, I've declined to make predictions about who will or won't win an election.
