RADNOR, Pa., June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The law firm of Kessler Topaz Meltzer & Check, LLP (www.ktmc.com) informs investors that a securities class action lawsuit has been filed in the United States District Court for the Northern District of California against PLAYSTUDIOS, Inc. ('PLAYSTUDIOS') (NASDAQ: MYPS; MYPSW) f/k/a Acies Acquisition Corp. ('Acies') (NASDAQ: ACAC; ACACW). The action charges PLAYSTUDIOS with violations of the federal securities laws, including omissions and fraudulent misrepresentations relating to the company's business, operations, and prospects.
