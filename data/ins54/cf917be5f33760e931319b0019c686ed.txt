Diwali 2022: It is the season of festivities. Diwali is around the corner and people are waiting to celebrate the festival of lights. Every year, Diwali is celebrated with a whole lot of grandeur and pomp all across the country. From cleaning the homes to decorating every corner of it with lights, colours and lanterns, to Diwali shopping for family and friends, people start prepping for the festive season in advance. Diwali brings with itself prosperity and happiness. Diwali, also known as the festival of lights, will be celebrated this year on October 24.
