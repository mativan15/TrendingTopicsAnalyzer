If you want to save your money, you should avoid using your washing machine or dishwasher during 'peak times' as it could increase your energy bill, one consumer expert saysThe time you choose to use your appliances is important, says one expert An expert has warned that washing machines should not be used during ‘peak hours' of the day for those wanting to save on their gas and electricity bills.
