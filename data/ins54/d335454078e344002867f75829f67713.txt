October 19, 2022 02:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Flowserve Corporation (NYSE: FLS), a leading provider of flow control products and services for the global infrastructure markets, today announced that it plans to release its results for the third quarter 2022 after the close of the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) on Monday, October 31.

The following morning, on Tuesday, November 1, the company will hold its conference call with the financial community at 11 a.m. Eastern time.
