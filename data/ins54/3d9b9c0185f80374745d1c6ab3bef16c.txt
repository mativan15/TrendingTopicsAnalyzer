Jio launches its game controller for gaming enthusiasts
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 13:30:00

Following the launch of its new recharge plans for the JioFi dongle, Jio has come up with yet another product, this time for the gaming market. Jio has launched its game controller that allows gamers to plan games on their Android , Android tablets, Jio set-top boxes and other devices. The controller comes with two primary triggers and eight arrow and direction buttons. On the pricing front, the controller is priced at Rs.3499, with an affordable EMI facility starting at Rs.164.72 per month.

You can buy the controller from the official website of Jio.
