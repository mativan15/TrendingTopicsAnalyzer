Popeyes Cajun-Style Thanksgiving Turkeys Are Now Available for Delivery
cnet.com
news
2022-10-19 17:08:32

The chicken sandwich wars may still be raging, but Popeyes is taking a respite in time for the holidays: For the first time, the Louisiana-based chain is offering to deliver its Cajun-style Thanksgiving turkeys to your house.Customers can head to thecajunfix.popeyes.com to place an order, with deliveries beginning Oct. 24. According to Popeyes, the frozen turkeys should arrive in one to three business days. (Delivery is only available in the continental US.) The holiday birds retail for $94.99 including delivery.
