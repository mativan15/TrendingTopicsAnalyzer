By Keith Russell October 17, 2022 / 7:53 AM / CBS DFW DALLAS (CBSDFW.COM) — The Hail Mary still ranks as, arguably, the greatest play in NFL history, but it couldn't have been done without the blocking from Rayfield Wright. Also known as the Big Cat, Wright was always ready to pounce in protection of his teammates. "He had this big hand and said, 'Rook, it's gonna be alright here,'" Cowboys Hall of Famer Tony Dorsett recalled. "When I had my first game, he said it's gonna be alright. Big Cat said, 'Follow me,' and guess what I did.
