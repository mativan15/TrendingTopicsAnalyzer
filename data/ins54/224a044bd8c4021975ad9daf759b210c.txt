A winning $494 million Mega Millions ticket sold in Fort Myers weeks after Hurricane Ian battered the area
aol.com
news
2022-10-18 15:40:59

A winning Mega Millions ticket sold in Fort Myers, Florida, is offering a glimmer of hope to an area still struggling to rebuild weeks after Hurricane Ian unleashed a fury of destruction and devastation.Two lucky Mega Millions tickets matched all six numbers in Friday's drawing — 9, 22, 26, 41, 44 and the Mega Ball 19 — for a prize totaling $494 million.One ticket was sold in California and the other at a 7-Eleven convenience store on Colonial Boulevard in Fort Myers, the Florida Lottery announced Monday.
