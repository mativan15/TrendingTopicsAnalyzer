Korean Coach Ends Winning Stint at Vietnam Nat'l Football Team
naver.com
news
2022-10-18 12:00:00

Korean football coach Park Hang-seo will end his successful stint training Vietnam's national squad. "I think the best way to describe it is a 'beautiful farewell,'" Park told the Chosun Ilbo on Monday. Both sides announced Monday that they will not extend his contract when it expires on Jan. 31 next year. The Vietnam Football Federation in a statement thanked Park for devoting himself to the sport in the country for the last five years and pledged to keep working closely with him. Park said, "The five years I spent with Vietnam's national team was truly valuable.
