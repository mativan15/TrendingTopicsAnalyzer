Rishi Sunak's energy bills support will hand wealthy pensioners £850 that they may not need while low-income families miss out, new analysis has shown.A £15bn package of grants unveiled by the chancellor on Thursday will deal out 'rough justice' to some groups, the Resolution Foundation said.The think tank said that the biggest winners are wealthy pensioners, while larger families will see their bills rise most steeply but still receive the same flat-rate payment.Mr Sunak announced a £400 energy bill discount for every household in the UK, as well as targeted help for pensioners, who will receive an additional £300.
