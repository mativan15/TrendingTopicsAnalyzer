Edward O'Reilly's three daughters were born prematurely on September 1 in Nairobi, Kenya - he says he's been hit with extra costs despite already paying €50,000 (£43,570) to cover all expensesEdward O'Reilly at his home in Dublin A dad whose triplets were born via surrogacy in Kenya faces paying an extra €39,000 (£34,000) to bring them home. Edward O'Reilly's three daughters were born prematurely on September 1 and he raced to Nairobi to see his newborns. The surrogacy service he used then demanded a further €16,000 (£14,000) when he arrived, despite already paying €50,000 (£43,570) and being told that covered all the expenses.
