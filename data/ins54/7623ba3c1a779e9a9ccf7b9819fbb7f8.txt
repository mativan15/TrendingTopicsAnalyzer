Kharge is second leader from State to become AICC president
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-19 15:32:54

After a gap of nearly 55 years, Congress leader from Karnataka became the president of the All India Congress Committee (AICC). He is occupying the coveted post with just a few months left for elections in his home State Karnataka.A staunch loyalist of the Nehru-Gandhi family and veteran leader, Mr. Kharge is the second politician from Karnataka to occupy the post after Lingayat leader the late S. Nijalingappa, who led the party in 1968-69. Mr. Kharge is also the second Dalit leader to hold the post after Jagjivan Ram (1970-1971).
