Public health insurance is the arrangement by which the government undertakes to provide a guarantee of relief against illness, disability, or death in return for payment of a specified premium. It is imperative to understand who pays the premium and how the services are purchased; this distinction is vital to envision creating a system that can be hassle-free and convenient for beneficiaries.
