Ashok Leyland produces 1 lakh trucks on its modular AVTR platform in Uttarakhand plant
indiatimes.com
business
2022-10-21 09:30:00

The AVTR platform allows truck customisation to suit the needs of the customers.New Delhi: Hinduja group flagship Ashok Leyland has announced that it has rolled out 1,00,000th unit of the truck built on its indigenous AVTR platform at its manufacturing facility in Uttarakhand. The company had launched its modular platform AVTR in 2019, enabling a variety of axle configurations, loading spans, cabins, suspensions and drivetrain options on a single platform.The AVTR platform allows truck customisation to suit the needs of the customers.
