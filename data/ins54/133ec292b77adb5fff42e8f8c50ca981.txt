Government failing disabled people over cost-of-living crisis, charity warns

2022-10-17 23:01:00

The Government is failing disabled people over the cost-of-living and energy crisis, a charity has warned.Disability equality charity Scope said that lives are at risk as disabled people fear they will be unable to afford to heat their homes this winter.It comes amid uncertainty over whether the Government will commit to its promise to increase benefits in line with inflation.Scope said it is calling for the Government to offer direct financial support immediately as it released the results of a recent poll of 1,000 disabled adults, which found that 63% were worried about being able to pay their heating bills.
