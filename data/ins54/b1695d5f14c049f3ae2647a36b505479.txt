Published: 09:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 09:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 Meghan Markle has revealed she'd 'never say never' to an acting return and wants any actresses to portray her as 'soft and playful' in any future adaptations of her life.In a new interview with Variety magazine, the Duchess of Sussex, 41, was promoting her new Spotify podcast Archetypes when talk turned to her early career.When she met Prince Harry in 2016, the mother-of-two was most famous for her role as Rachel Zane in hit legal drama Suits - but quit the show shortly before the couple's April 2018 wedding.
