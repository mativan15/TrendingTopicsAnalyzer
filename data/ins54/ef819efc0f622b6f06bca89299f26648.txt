Hamm Boosts Offer to Take Shale Driller Continental Private
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-17 11:10:47

(Bloomberg) -- Billionaire wildcatter Harold Hamm boosted his offer to $4.3 billion for the remaining stock his family doesn't already own in Continental Resources Inc. as he plans to take private the shale giant he founded decades ago.The Oklahoma City-based company said in a statement Monday it has entered into a merger agreement with Hamm, who is now offering $74.28 a share in cash. That represents a 15% premium to the closing price on June 13, the day before Hamm's family disclosed its initial $70-per-share proposal.
