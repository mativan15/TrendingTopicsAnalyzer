Superstar Shah Rukh Khan recently appeared as the chief guest at a Taekwondo tournament in Mumbai. Saif Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan's son Taimur Ali Khan was also a contestant who took part in the competition along with other kids. While performing his duties as a chief guest, Shah Rukh Khan awarded medals to the winners and kissed Taimur Ali Khan, whose pictures have been doing rounds on social media.  web screen grabPhotos and videos from the Taekwondo tournament surfaced on the internet and it shows Saif's son Taimur in some action and delivering kicks and punches while fighting his opponent during a match.
