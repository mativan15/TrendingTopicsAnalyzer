October 21, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time OAKVILLE, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Harvest Portfolios Group Inc. ('Harvest') declares the monthly cash distribution payable for Big Pharma Split Corp. of $0.1031 for each class A share (PRM:TSX) for the month ending October 31, 2022. The distribution is payable November 9, 2022 to class A shareholders of record at the close of business October 31, 2022.
