Hans Niemann sues Magnus Carlsen, Chess.com for $100M
hindustantimes.com
sport
2022-10-21 15:00:08

Hans Niemann, the American teen Grandmaster sensationally accused of cheating by Magnus Carlsen, has filed a defamation lawsuit against the Norwegian world champion and other parties, adding another twist to the 'single biggest chess scandal in history', as the lawsuit terms it.Filed in the United States District Court of Missouri, the lawsuit lists Carlsen, Play Magnus—the online platform founded by Carlsen—, online platform Chess.com, its Chief Chess Officer Danny Rensch and popular streamer Hikaru Nakamura as defendants.
