Samvat 2078 recap: The good, bad & ugly for D-Street traders
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 07:00:00

Mumbai: Samvat 2078 had started on a good note for Indian equities, but the yearbrought heavy bouts of volatility, resulting in negative returns for Dalal Street investors.Benchmark indices Nifty50 and Sensex have fallen by more than 1% in this period.Concerns over a slowdown in growth globally due to aggressive rate hikes by the US Federal Reserve and other central banks, Omicron, and Russia's invasion of Ukraine have been the major spoilers of bulls' party.The invasion by Russia in February sent prices of crude oil and other commodities through the roof and further stoked inflation.
