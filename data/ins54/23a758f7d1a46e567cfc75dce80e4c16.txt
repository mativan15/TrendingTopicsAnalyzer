Xi Jinping's Congress Unlikely To Mean Progress For China's Gender Equality
ndtv.com
world
2022-10-18 04:00:00

Xi Jinping government has emphasised the value of traditional roles for women as mothers and carersHong Kong: As Xi Jinping consolidates power at China's 20th Communist Party Congress this week, Chinese women are not holding their breath for progress in gender equality.Xi's decade as the party's general secretary has seen the number of women in politics and elite government roles decline and gender gaps in the workforce widen, academics and activists say. Feminist voices have also been muzzled and in recent years, the government has increasingly emphasised the value of traditional roles for women as mothers and carers, they add.
