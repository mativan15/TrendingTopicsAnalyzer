Tokio Marine, alongside other shareholders, will lead bolttech's Series B funding round that follows its largest ever Series A raised by an insurtech

SINGAPORE, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- bolttech, one of the world's fastest growing international insurtech companies, today announced Tokio Marine, alongside other shareholders, will lead bolttech's Series B funding round. The investment values bolttech, Singapore's only homegrown insurtech unicorn, at an up-round valuation of approximately US$1.
