June 01, 2022 06:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOULDER, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Biodesix, Inc. (Nasdaq: BDSX), a leading data-driven diagnostic solutions company with a focus in lung disease, today announced Scott Hutton, Chief Executive Officer of Biodesix, will present at the 42nd Annual William Blair Growth Stock Conference being held June 6-9, 2022 at the Lowes Chicago Hotel in Chicago, IL.

42nd Annual William Blair Growth Stock Conference
Date: Wednesday, June 8, 2022
Time: 9:20 a.m. CT

In addition to the presentation, the management team will host investor meetings.
