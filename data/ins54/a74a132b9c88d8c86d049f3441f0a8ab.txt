Pence says of a Trump run in 2024: 'There might be somebody else I'd prefer more'
nbcnews.com
politics
2022-10-20 13:30:00

Former Vice President Mike Pence on Wednesday appeared to hold back from supporting another presidential bid by Donald Trump in 2024.'Well, there might be somebody else I'd prefer more,' Pence said at a Georgetown University event late Wednesday when asked if he would support the former president if he runs again.'What I can tell you is, I have every confidence that the Republican Party is going to sort out leadership,' Pence continued. 'All my focus has been on the midterm elections, and it'll stay that way for the next 20 days.
