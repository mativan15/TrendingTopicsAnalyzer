Sensex jumps 250 points, Nifty above 17,550 on firm global cues, oil price fall
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 05:00:00

Tracking firm global cues and falling crude oil prices, key equity indices opened higher on Wednesday, led by the banking and financial stocks. At 9.17 am, the BSE Sensex was trading 268 points or 0.45 per cent higher at 59,228. Nifty50 was trading at 17,557, up 70 points or 0.40 per cent.

'There are two factors supporting the ongoing rally in the market: One, support from the mother market US aided by some excellent quarterly results and two, FII selling getting completely overwhelmed by DII buying,' V K Vijayakumar, Chief Investment Strategist at Geojit Financial Services said.
