Netflix could ask customers to scan their fingerprint or use face recognition to clamp down on password sharing, an analyst has predicted. In its annual round of tech predictions, CCS Insight says that by 2023, 'streaming services such as Netflix [will] turn to these processes to validate users'. 'There's only so much you can do with passwords,' said Ben Wood, chief analyst at CCS Insight. 'We may see one of the big streaming providers saying ‘we want some biometric proof that you're there'.
