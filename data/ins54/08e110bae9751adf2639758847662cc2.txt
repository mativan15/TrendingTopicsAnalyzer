Community crowdfunding campaigns save two restaurants in Sierra Madre, Historic Filipinotown

By CBSLA Staff October 18, 2022 / 4:48 PM / CBS Los Angeles Facing one hardship after another, two different restaurants in two different cities were on the verge of closing. But thanks to a crowdfunding campaign they were saved just in time."I put the bat signal up and asked for a GoFundMe if folks can spot us for just a second," said owner John-Eric Concordia. Concordia is the owner of the Park's Finest — a Filipino-inspired barbecue joint near Angelino Heights and Historic Filipinotown. He started it 10 years ago and it flourished into a successful catering company — but then the pandemic hit.
