BBC Breakfast presenter Naga Munchetty today clashed with Rishi Sunak over his £15bn cost-of-living package.
The Chancellor yesterday revealed his monster £21billion cost of living bailout.

Under the scheme, EVERY Brit household will get a £400 grant to help cover the spiralling cost of energy bills.
But presenter Munchetty accused the package of helping the rich and claimed some Brits - including high earners with two properties - may not need the cash at all.
