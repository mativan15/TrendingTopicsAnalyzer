Director-General of Police (DGP) C. Sylendra Babu on Saturday visited a head constable, who, along with his colleague, was attacked by a wanted criminal in Anna Nagar on Friday night. Accompanied by Deputy Commissioner, Anna Nagar, C. Vijaykumar, Mr. Babu visited head constable Nandagopal at a private hospital and enquired about his health. He instructed the officials to arrange all medical assistance to the injured for speedy recovery.
