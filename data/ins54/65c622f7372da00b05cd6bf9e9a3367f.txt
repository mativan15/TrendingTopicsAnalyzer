Manesar cracker manufacturer succumbs to burn injuries at Safdarjung Hospital
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-16 21:40:11

A 40-year-old man from Nakhrola village in Manesar, who was injured in an explosion on October 12, died at Delhi's Safdarjung Hospital late on Saturday night, police said. At least five members of his family, including him and a minor girl and boy, sustained severe burn injuries from the incident which took place in their home. The family was allegedly manufacturing and storing banned firecrackers which caused the explosion, police said. Jai Bhagwan was manufacturing crackers for the last 15 years and picked up fights with people who asked him to stop doing so.
