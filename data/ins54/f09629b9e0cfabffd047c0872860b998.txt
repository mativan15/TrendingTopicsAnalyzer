Weatherford Announces Redemption of $125 Million Principal Amount of its 11.0% Senior Notes due December 1, 2024
globenewswire.com
news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

HOUSTON, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Weatherford International plc (NASDAQ: WFRD) ('Weatherford' or the 'Company') today announced it intends to use cash on hand to redeem $125 million principal amount of its 11.0% senior notes due December 1, 2024 on November 17, 2022 (the "Unsecured Notes"). Girish Saligram, President and Chief Executive Officer, commented, 'I continue to be extremely proud of the progress we have made to strengthen our Company and our balance sheet. Our focus on free cash flow generation, coupled with the announcement of our Revolving Credit Facility, allows us to take another important and significant step towards improving our capital structure efficiency.
