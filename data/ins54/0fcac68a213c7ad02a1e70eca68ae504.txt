Terrorist hideout busted in J-K's Poonch, arms, ammo seized
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:00:00

Security forces on Monday busted a terrorist hideout in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch district, and seized arms and ammunition from it, officials said. The hideout was uncovered during a joint search operation by police and security personnel at Sangla village in the Surankote area of the border district, they said. The officials said a pistol, four hand grenades, four Under Barrel Grenade Launcher (UBGL) cartridges, a wireless set, 16 improvised explosive device (IED) batteries and three AK magazines with rounds were seized from the hideout.
