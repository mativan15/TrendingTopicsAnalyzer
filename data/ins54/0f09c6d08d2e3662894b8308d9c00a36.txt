REUTERS/Vladislav CuliomzaCHISINAU, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Moldova's pro-Western government on Friday complained Russia's Gazprom GAZP.MM was not behaving like a serious partner, given its refusal to tell Chisinau how much natural gas it would provide in November.One of Europe's poorest countries, Moldova is reliant on Russian gas and has been hit hard by a surge in prices since Russia's invasion of Ukraine.
