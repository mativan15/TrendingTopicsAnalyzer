Battery cell makers Britishvolt have partnered up with real estate operator Prologis to set up the new battery cell scale-up facility (gigafactory), in the West Midlands. The Ham Hall site will create new cell formats and formulations to help make higher performance, production-ready, cheaper batteries for EVs. This will let the company ramp up the manufacturing of battery cells at larger volumes at its Cambois Gigaplant in Northumberland, which should begin operating in 2024.Memorandum of Understandings have already been signed to supply Lotus Cars and Aston Martin Lagonda.
