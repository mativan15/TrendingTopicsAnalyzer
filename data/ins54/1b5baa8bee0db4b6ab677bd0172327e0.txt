Okta is not showing effects from breach, and the stock is rallying toward best week in more than two years
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 19:46:00

Okta Inc. shares were ripping toward their best week in more than two years Friday, after the identity-management software company impressed analysts with strong results despite a potential security breach earlier in the year. Okta OKTA, +5.08% shares surged nearly 19% Friday to an intraday high of $111.35 before pulling back to a 5.5% gain at last check, adding to Thursday's 11% regular session gain prior to the release of quarterly results. In comparison, the S&P 500 index SPX, -1.63% was down 1.
