Work to start on £40m entertainment complex in Preston

2022-10-21 06:57:02

Image source, Preston City CouncilImage caption, The new complex includes a cinema, bowling alley, street food hub, parking and a new public squareBuilding work is due to start on a new £40m leisure and entertainment development in Preston city centre.The new complex, called Animate, will be located at the city's former indoor market area, in the Harris Quarter.It includes an eight-screen cinema, bowling alley, street food hub, parking and a new public square.Preparation work at the site will start next month after permission was granted by the council, before construction starts in early 2023.
