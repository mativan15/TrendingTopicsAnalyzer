 A man masquerading as a massage therapist has been charged with sexual assault, Steinbach RCMP says.
On Friday, a woman reported she was touched in an unwanted sexual way during a massage by a man she believed was a massage therapist.She says the man, who is not actually a massage therapist, was providing services out of his basement in Blumenort.
