NEW YORK, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Jakubowitz Law announces that a securities fraud class action lawsuit has commenced on behalf of shareholders of PayPal Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: PYPL).
To receive updates on the lawsuit, fill out the form:https://claimyourloss.com/securities/paypal-holdings-inc-loss-submission-form/?id=32791&from=4
This lawsuit is on behalf of all persons or entities who purchased PayPal common stock between February 3, 2021, and February 1, 2022, inclusive.
Shareholders interested in acting as a lead plaintiff representing the class of wronged shareholders have until December 5, 2022 to petition the court.
