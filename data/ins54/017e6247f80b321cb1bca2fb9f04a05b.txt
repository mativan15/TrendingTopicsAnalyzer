AFPUnion Minister for agriculture and farmer welfare Narendra Singh Tomar will be the chief guest for the event. Most of the global challenges are inextricably intertwined and while we make efforts to reap positive synergies, the current challenges demand a nexus approach. To bring leaders on one platform to engage and deliberate on global issues like climate change, 'India Sustainability Conclave' will be held on June 24th in New Delhi. The annual conclave, organised by Socio Story, brings leaders from different sectors together to discuss on transforming business.
