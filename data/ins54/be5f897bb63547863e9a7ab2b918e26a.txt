Author Pamela Bond emphasizes the importance of a balanced mind, body and soul in 'The Authenticity Compass'

BOSTON, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- "The Authenticity Compass: Essential Guidance for Sustainable Success" by Pamela Bond is a life-navigational tool the author developed to support the self-awareness required to be authentic. Bond wrote this self-assessment guidance to help individuals be true to themselves and those with whom they interact. It is based on her own journey of self-awareness and her business experience.
