Published: 12:00 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:00 EDT, 21 October 2022 Jermaine Franklin says he is excited about the prospect of fighting in the UK and insists Dillian Whyte's home advantage will not affect the result as long as the judges are 'fair'.The American boxer – who has never travelled abroad before – will face Whyte in a heavyweight showdown at Wembley Arena on November 26.Franklin says the rivalry between American and British boxers will 'add another layer' to the fight but he refuses to believe there is any additional 'pressure'.
