Photo: U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention Country Director Dr. Tony Ao (center) commissions the Volta Regional Public Health Emergency Operations Center with partners from the Republic of Korea and Ghana Accra, Ghana – The U.S. Government, through the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), has commissioned the Volta Regional Public Health Emergency Operations Center (PHEOC) in Ho.  This is the fourth such center opened in Ghana since 2021.
The operations center represents a collaboration between the governments of the United States, South Korea, and Ghana.
