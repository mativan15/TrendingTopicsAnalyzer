e55evu/iStock via Getty Images The iShares MSCI Italy ETF (NYSEARCA:EWI) is getting pretty low in multiple, but there are some genuine concerns about the Italian economy that render the ETF rather uninteresting. The proximity to the energy crisis but also an especially high reliance on gas is going to put pressure on a lot of stocks in the ETF directly but also on their end-markets by straining households. Overall, the downside in terms of earnings and the impact that it might have on earnings growth makes the multiple actually a little rich.
