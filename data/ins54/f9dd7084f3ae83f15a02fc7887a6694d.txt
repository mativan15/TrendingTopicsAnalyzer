The OPP, CAFC, and RCMP raise awareness about money mules in Canada

Canada NewsWire

OTTAWA, ON, June 1, 2022

OTTAWA, ON, June 1, 2022 /CNW/ - The Ontario Provincial Police (OPP), the Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre (CAFC) and the Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP) are launching a campaign from June 6th to 10th to raise awareness about how fraudsters are using unsuspecting victims as money mules to launder funds and proceeds of crime.
