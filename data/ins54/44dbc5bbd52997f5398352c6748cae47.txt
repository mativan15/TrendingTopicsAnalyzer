October 19, 2022 09:44 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Halper Sadeh LLC, an investor rights law firm, is investigating whether the sale of HV Bancorp, Inc. (NASDAQ: HVBC) to Citizens Financial Services, Inc. is fair to HV Bancorp shareholders. Under the terms of the agreement, HV Bancorp shareholders will have the right to elect to receive for each share of HV Bancorp common stock either $30.50 in cash or 0.400 shares of Citizens common stock.

Halper Sadeh encourages HV Bancorp shareholders to click here to learn more about their legal rights and options or contact Daniel Sadeh or Zachary Halper at (212) 763-0060 or sadeh@halpersadeh.
