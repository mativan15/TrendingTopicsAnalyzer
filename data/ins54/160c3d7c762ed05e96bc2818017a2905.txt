Toronto Stocks Climb; Greenlane Renewables Rises on $6.8M Contract in US
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-30 16:08:00

By Adriano Marchese Stocks in Toronto were higher on Monday mid-trading while U.S. markets remained closed for Memorial Day. In the session, most Canadian sectors were higher, led by consumer durables, health services and tech services, while the utilities sector lagged. Shares in Greenlane Renewables Inc. were higher after the company said that it won a $6.8 million contract for a dairy-manure-to-renewable-natural-gas project in the U.S. At midday, Canada's S&P/TSX Composite Index was up 0.73% to 20899.
