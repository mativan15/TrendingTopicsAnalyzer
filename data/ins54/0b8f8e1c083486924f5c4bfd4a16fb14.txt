Mario Tama My thesis is that Netflix (NASDAQ:NFLX) has opportunities during these difficult times. They have options to bolster US film efforts with acquisitions seeing as bidding wars are less likely now that the market is not frothy. In addition, they can widen the audience with affordable ad plans and develop new revenue streams with paid sharing and gaming. Film Netflix has opportunities to do more with respect to US films but it is important to keep things in perspective and be mindful of the success they have experienced in other areas.
