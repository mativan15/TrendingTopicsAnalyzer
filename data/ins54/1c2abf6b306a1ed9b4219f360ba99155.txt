People with type 2 diabetes are still going without their essential medicine as a TikTok trend promoting its off-label use as miracle weight-loss solution continues to grow.Australia has been hit with a nationwide shortage of Ozempic (semaglutide), a once-a-week injectable drug that is only approved for people with type 2 diabetes in conjunction with dieting and exercising.But GPs and health professionals have been prescribing the medicine off label to thousands of weight-loss hopefuls across the country.
