First-year Las Vegas Aces guard Aisha Sheppard has a serious rookie moment on Thursday.

The 23rd pick in the 2022 WNBA Draft accidentally checked into the game wearing her warm-up shirt.

An opponent tipped Sheppard off to the mistake, and Aces head coach Becky Hammon had a nice laugh.

A first-year WNBA guard had a hilarious rookie moment during the Las Vegas Aces' blockbuster matchup against the Connecticut Sun Thursday night.
