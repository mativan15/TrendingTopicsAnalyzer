Breaking News AAP Accuses Assam's Himanta Biswa Sarma Of Corruption In PPE Kit DealAssam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma awarded a contract for Covid Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kits to a company linked to his family and overpaid for the gear, Delhi Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia alleged on Saturday.The "big expose" by the Aam Aadmi Party leader followed a teaser by chief Arvind Kejriwal and was pitched as a response to the arrest of Delhi's Health Minister Satyendar Jain, who has been arrested in an alleged money laundering case.
