Shares of Berger Paints (India) Ltd. traded 0.02 per cent up at Rs 613.1 at 01:34PM (IST) on Monday, even as BSE benchmark Sensex gained 472.74 points to 58392.71.The stock had closed at Rs 612.95 in the previous session. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 847.35 and 52-week low of Rs 543.85, respectively. As per BSE data, total traded volume on the counter till 01:34PM (IST) stood at 428882 shares with a turnover of Rs 26.25 crore.At the prevailing price, shares of the company traded at 63.
