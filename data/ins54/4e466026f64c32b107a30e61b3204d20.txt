Fremantle, Samsung Create Jamie Oliver FAST Channel- Mipcom Global Bulletin
variety.com
news
2022-10-17 19:26:12

Fremantle is partnering with electronics giant Samsung to bring a dedicated FAST channel, 'The Jamie Oliver Channel' to audiences in the U.K. Available from 12th October, the channel is dedicated to the most well-known titles from the British chef, catering to Samsung TV Plus watchers with cooking content for all occasions. Samsung TV Plus delivers over 1,600 channels across 24 countries, spanning news, sports, and entertainment. London-based Future Studios has launched factual series 'Unbelievable Me' (10 x 45') at Mipcom.
