ExecOnline and Thinkers50 Partner to Prepare Leaders to Meet New Business Challenges

NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- ExecOnline, the pioneer of online leadership development, and Thinkers50, a global platform for management thinking, have entered into an exclusive partnership to create new online learning experiences to help leaders navigate economic volatility and an increasingly complex business environment. The partnership brings together the strength of ExecOnline's best-in-class 'Learn by Doing with Impact' instructional design process and online learning platform with the unique insights and expertise of Thinkers50 thought leaders.
