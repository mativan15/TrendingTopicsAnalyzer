feedough/iStock via Getty Images Thesis The Dogs of the Dow strategy (detailed in the second section) refers to the buying and holding of the 10 stocks that have the highest dividend yield from the Dow Jones Industrial Index. Banks typically do not make it to the Dogs of the Dow list. The list is frequented more often by stocks in sectors that historically feature higher payouts, such as telecom (such as Verizon), healthcare (such as Walgreens and Amgen), or commodity-oriented stocks (such as Chevron and Dow).
