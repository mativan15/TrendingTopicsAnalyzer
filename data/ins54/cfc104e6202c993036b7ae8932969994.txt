Getty ImagesTotal income for the quarter also rose 96.2% sequentially to Rs 30.84 crore.Aurum PropTech Ltd, formerly known as Majesco Ltd, has reported consolidated revenue from operations worth Rs 28.3 crore for the quarter ended September, up 179% over the last year's total revenue and growth of 93.3% over the previous quarter. Total income for the quarter also rose 96.2% sequentially to Rs 30.84 crore. Total income for the first half of 2022-23 rose to Rs 46.56 crore as against Rs 2.68 crore from a year ago.
