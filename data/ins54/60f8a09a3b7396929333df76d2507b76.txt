June 02, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time MIAMI--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The public relations industry continues to undergo rapid change, with PR pros facing challenges when it comes to long hours, securing coverage, getting bigger budgets and measuring the value of their work, according to Muck Rack's 2022 State of PR survey.



More than half of PR pros (53%) work 40+ hours/week and work after hours or on weekends at least once a week. Brands pay better, with 52 percent of PR pros at agencies earning less than $100K annually (39% of PR pros at brands earn less than $100k annually).
