Published: 17:51 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 17:55 EDT, 20 October 2022 Four weight-loss drugs that cut bodyweight by up to 10 per cent in a year were recommended for moderately overweight and obese adults who did not have luck with diet and exercise changes by a leading panel of doctors.Doctors at the American Gastroenterological Association (AGA) — a top group of clinicians — are now recommending Wegovy, Saxenda, Qsymia and Contrave for the patients.The treatments are administered either as injections — from daily to weekly — or as pills to be taken once a day or twice with meals.
