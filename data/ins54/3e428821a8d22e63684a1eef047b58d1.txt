Cowboys, NFL Foundation Donating $400K Total For Uvalde
iheart.com
news
2022-06-01 17:05:00

Photo: Getty ImagesThe Dallas Cowboys and NFL Foundation are giving $400,000 total to help families affected by the Uvalde school shooting. Team Executive Vice President Charlotte Jones says last week's attack on Robb Elementary broke everyone's heart and promises to keep victims in her thoughts and prayers. Half the money will go to the Robb School Memorial Fund while the other half will go to the Uvalde Strong Fund.
