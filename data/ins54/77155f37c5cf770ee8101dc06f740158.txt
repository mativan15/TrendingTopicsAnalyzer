This week, Orlando Business Journal is honoring the 2022 C-Suite Awards honorees in Central Florida, winners in a new awards program this year.

These 20 individuals are being recognized as the region's top leaders and outstanding performers in their areas of expertise, as well as for their contributions and commitment to their professions and the local community.

The honorees are based in Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Volusia or Brevard counties, and hold the following titles or equivalent positions in the local market:

Chief executive officer (CEO), founder, president or other top executive (managing principal, executive director, etc.
