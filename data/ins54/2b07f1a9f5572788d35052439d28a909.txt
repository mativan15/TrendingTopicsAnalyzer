LSU coach Brian Kelly sounds off on players leaving early to prepare for the draft
usatoday.com
news
2022-05-30 12:00:28

Over the last few seasons, we've seen a growing trend in college football of players on teams in non-playoff bowl games leaving early to prepare for the draft.
Given the relatively short turnaround between bowl season and the combine (and the amount of money players stand to lose if they suffered an injury in what is a mostly meaningless exhibition), it's certainly understandable, but some have voiced concerns about the changing priorities of student-athletes.
LSU coach Brian Kelly is not among that group, though.
