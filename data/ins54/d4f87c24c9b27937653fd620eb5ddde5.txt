Goodbye social distancing, hello labor shortages
naver.com

2022-05-30 19:20:00

Customers wait in line at a restaurant in Jongno District, central Seoul at lunchtime on April 20, two days after most social distancing restrictions were lifted in the country. [YONHAP] Restaurants and coffee shops are returning to normal as social distancing restrictions have been relaxed and customers return. These shops are struggling to staff their available positions, especially as it becomes more difficult for migrant workers to enter the country due to Covid-19 restrictions. A restaurant owner in Gangnam, Seoul, had no choice but to limit business hours despite the surge in customers after the government lifted the business hour restrictions last month.
