Rep. Chris Jacobs said he was ending his reelection bid after backlash over his support for gun control.

"If you stray from a party position, you are annihilated," he said during a Friday press conference.

The Republican lawmaker represents one of the most conservative House districts in New York State.

Rep. Chris Jacobs of New York on Friday announced he was ending his reelection bid after receiving a stream of backlash from conservatives over his support for gun control legislation.
