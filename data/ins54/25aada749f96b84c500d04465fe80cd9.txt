Jun. 4—June 4, 2012 — Northwoods Habitat for Humanity has begun its Blitz Build project, which aims to build a house in just seven days. Located in Bemidji, the house will be for a family with three children and will be finished on June 10. The Blitz Build projects occur every four years, this year saw assistance from Headwaters Building Association.

June 4, 1997 — The Bemidji City Council has approved a street dance to raise money for flood relief and agreed to waive the city's usual cleaning fee.
