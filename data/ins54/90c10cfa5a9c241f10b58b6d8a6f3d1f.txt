'It's bonkers!' Coventry chip shop swamped by fans after becoming viral star

2022-06-01 07:41:00

A viral video thrust the Binley Mega Chippy into the spotlight (Image: SWNS)But the normally quiet former mining village has been suddenly inundated with huge crowds of fans, after a viral video thrust the Binley Mega Chippy into the spotlight. The shop first started to gain attention on social media when a TikTok food reviewer posted a slideshow of the UK's best takeaways, leading with the Mega Chippy. One customer then posted a catchy tune about the shop to the social media app.It has since made its way into a series of viral videos and internet memes, many of them featuring customers travelling from across the world to visit the shop.
