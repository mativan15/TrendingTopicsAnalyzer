TXMD stock soars on acquisition offer from EW Healthcare Partners (NASDAQ:TXMD)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-31 10:36:57

Maks_Lab/iStock via Getty Images Women's healthcare company TherapeuticsMD (NASDAQ:TXMD) announced Tuesday that it reached an agreement to be acquired by an affiliate of private equity firm EW Healthcare Partners in an all-cash transaction for $10 per share. TherapeuticsMD (TXMD) shares have added ~351% in the pre-market on the news. Per the terms, EW Healthcare Partners is set to start a tender offer to acquire all outstanding shares of TXMD common stock at the agreed purchase price, followed immediately by a merger.
