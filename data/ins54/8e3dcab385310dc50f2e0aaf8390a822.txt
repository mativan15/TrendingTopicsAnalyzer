Big Lots: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-05-27 10:11:32

COLUMBUS, Ohio (AP) _ Big Lots Inc. (BIG) on Friday reported a fiscal first-quarter loss of $11.1 million, after reporting a profit in the same period a year earlier.On a per-share basis, the Columbus, Ohio-based company said it had a loss of 39 cents. The results missed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of six analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 89 cents per share. The discount retailer posted revenue of $1.37 billion in the period, which also fell short of Street forecasts.
