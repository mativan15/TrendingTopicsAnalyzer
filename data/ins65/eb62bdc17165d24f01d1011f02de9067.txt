June 03, 2022 07:53 AM Eastern Daylight Time MIRAMAR, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Spirit Airlines, Inc. ('Spirit' or the 'Company') (NYSE: SAVE) today announced that leading independent proxy advisory firm Glass, Lewis & Co. ('Glass Lewis') has recommended that Spirit stockholders vote FOR the proposed merger with Frontier Group Holdings, Inc. ("Frontier") (NASDAQ: ULCC), parent company of Frontier Airlines, Inc.



'We are pleased that Glass Lewis supports our Board's unanimous recommendation to vote FOR the merger with Frontier,' said Ted Christie, President and CEO of Spirit.
