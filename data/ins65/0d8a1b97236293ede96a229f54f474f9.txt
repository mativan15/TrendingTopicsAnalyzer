Russia in "failure to pay" credit event, investor committee says
reuters.com
news
2022-06-01 15:29:59

NEW YORK/LONDON, June 1 (Reuters) - A panel of investors on Wednesday determined Russia had triggered a credit event after it failed to pay nearly $1.9 million in interest on a sovereign bond, taking the nation a step closer to its first major external debt default in over a century.The EMEA Credit Derivatives Determinations Committee (CDDC) said on its website that the committee voted 'yes' to a question to determine whether a "Failure to Pay Credit Event" occurred with respect to Russia.Russia's international 2022 bond matured on April 4 and payment of principal and interest due at maturity was not made until May 2.
