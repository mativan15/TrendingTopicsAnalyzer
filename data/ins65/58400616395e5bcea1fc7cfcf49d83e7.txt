TV show ‘Pherari Mon' to launch on November 7
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-19 08:57:00

Bengali television is ready to welcome a series of new shows. Family-drama, love story, to non-fiction; a variety of platters is here to spice up the entertainment quotient of the Bengali audience. While Indrani Halder hosted game show, Pallavi Sharma-Rubel Das starrer ‘Nim Phuler Modhu' and Neel Bhattacharjee, Tiyasha Lepcha and Sampurna Lahiri's ‘Bangla Medium' are among the much-awaited projects which are set to premiere on two different channels, another General Entertainment Channel is ready with its new offering ‘Pherari Mon'.
