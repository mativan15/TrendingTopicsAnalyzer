NEW YORK, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Brooge Energy Ltd, ('Brooge Energy' or the 'Company'), is a Cayman Islands-based infrastructure provider which is engaged in Clean Petroleum Products and Biofuels and Crude Oil storage and related services. The Company conducts the business and operations through its subsidiary Brooge Petroleum and Gas Investment Company FZE (BPGIC). BPGIC is strategically located outside the Strait of Hormuz at the Port of Fujairah in the Emirate of Fujairah in the UAE.
