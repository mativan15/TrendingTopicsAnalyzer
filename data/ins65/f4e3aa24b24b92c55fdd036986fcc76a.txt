Former Conception Harbour town manager facing 31 charges of fraud, theft, breaching trust

The scenic Town of Conception Harbour is 70 kilometres west of St. John's, between the south and north sides of Conception Bay. (Michael Winsor Photography)Bonnie Lynn Wade, former clerk and town manager of Conception Harbour, is set to appear in court Thursday on allegations she stole money from people and used the town's credit card for her own benefit.A scroll of the court docket reveals a long list of charges under Wade's name — 20 for theft, 10 for fraud and one for breach of trust.Court documents show the fraud charges stem from allegations Wade used the town's Visa card 10 times, for a total of $3,600.
