May 31, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time BENTONVILLE, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Walmart Inc. (NYSE: WMT) announced today, Judith McKenna, president and chief executive officer, Walmart International, will participate in the Baird 2022 Global Consumer, Technology & Services Conference Tuesday, June 7, at 10:15 a.m. EDT.

The session will be webcast live through the 'Events' link at www.stock.walmart.com.

A transcript of the session will be available after the event and will be archived on the company's website.
