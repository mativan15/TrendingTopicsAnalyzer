For Cee, cooking became an escape for her during a particularly sad period of her life.
She knew she was struggling with depression from quite early on. She didn't know the word for it but she would describe it to her parents as a void that sucked away her joy. She learnt to occupy her mind to cope with this and cooking was something that provided some light in what seemed like a never-ending darkness.
