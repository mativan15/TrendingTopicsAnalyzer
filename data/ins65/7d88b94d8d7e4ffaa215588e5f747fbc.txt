Cork, IrelandA Michelin-star restaurant has issued a response to a complaint after would-be diners were slapped with a €250 (£215) for cancelling their meal at 30 minutes' notice - when their child needed to be taken to hospital in an emergency. The owner of Bastion, located in Kinsale, County Cork, Ireland, said they wished to clarify the matter after it was accused of taking the sum from the customer's card, CorkBeo reports.
