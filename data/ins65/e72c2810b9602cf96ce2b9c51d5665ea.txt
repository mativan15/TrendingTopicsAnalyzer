Renault SA's chairman said he's bullish about the carmaker's alliance with Japan's Nissan Motor.
 Renault SA's chairman said he's bullish about the carmaker's alliance with Japan's Nissan Motor Co. as negotiations intensify on reshaping the two-decade partnership that's been strained by an imbalanced ownership structure. 'We talk every day,' Jean-Dominique Senard said at the Paris car show on Monday, adding that representatives from Nissan and smaller partner Mitsubishi Motors Corp. currently are in France.
