June 01, 2022 09:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bragar Eagel & Squire, P.C., a nationally recognized stockholder rights law firm, announces that a class action lawsuit has been filed against Spero Therapeutics, Inc. ('Spero' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: SPRO) in the United States District Court for the Eastern District of New York on behalf of all persons and entities who purchased or otherwise acquired Spero securities between October 28, 2021 and May 2, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period').
