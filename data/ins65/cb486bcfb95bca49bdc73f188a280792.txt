'Unacceptable!' Kyiv fears France and Germany could sell out Ukraine in Putin peace talks
express.co.uk
world
2022-05-27 16:01:00

France and Germany have been put on notice amid concerns in Ukraine that the two leading European Union member-states could strike a peace deal which rewards Russia. An advisor to the Ukrainian Parliament, Mykola Volkivskyi, revealed officials from Germany, Italy, and France are going to meet with Vladimir Putin "in the next few weeks". However, he said any attempt to surrender Ukrainian territory without Ukrainian consent would be "unacceptable".Speaking to GB News, Mr Volkivskyi said: "It is understood that they will meet with Putin in the next few weeks.
