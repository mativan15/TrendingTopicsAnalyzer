Darden Executive Education & Lifelong Learning Unveils Fall 2022 Schedule, Certificates and New Leading Diversity Program
virginia.edu
news
2022-06-02 13:42:44

Darden Executive Education & Lifelong Learning (EELL) has announced its fall 2022 schedule of open enrollment programs, offering a mix of in-person and self-paced online options. Led by Darden's top-ranked faculty, programs offer learners the chance to develop professional skills that are in demand in today's job market, including leadership, business strategy, change management, negotiation and digital transformation.
Additionally, EELL will launch a new program, Leading Diversity, led by Professors Melissa Thomas-Hunt, former head of global diversity and belonging at AirBnB, and Laura Morgan Roberts, a leading thought leader and researcher in the area of diversity, equity and inclusion (DEI).
