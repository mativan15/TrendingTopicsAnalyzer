City of Calgary enters formal discussions on new event centre project
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-19 18:26:28

The City of Calgary and the Calgary Sports and Entertainment Corporation (CSEC) have restarted formal discussions on a new event centre in the city.
The announcement was made at a city council event centre committee meeting Wednesday morning after a lengthy closed-door session. It comes after a deal between the city and CSEC for a new arena to replace the ageing Saddledome ended in January this year after failing to reach an agreement on a construction timeline.The city said it will be represented by CAA ICON, a management consulting firm focused on sports venues, throughout the negotiations.
