Police searching for 2 people charged with stealing $171K in jewelry from Williamsburg business
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-15 16:30:00

A man and a woman walked off with about $171,000 worth of jewelry from a store in the Colonial Williamsburg area last month, according to police, and detectives are asking for the public's help in locating them.

The theft occurred about 3:20 p.m. Sept. 16 at a business in the 400 block of W. Duke of Gloucester Street, according to a news release.

The release does not name the business but there's a custom jewelry shop called The Precious Gem in that block, which is part of Merchant's Square.

The suspects have been identified as Andreia Anghel, 29, of Queens, New York, and Sandor Anghel, 33 of Hemet, Calif.
