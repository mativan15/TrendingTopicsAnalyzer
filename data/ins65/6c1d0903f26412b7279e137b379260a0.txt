Scott Morrison, a 'globalisation mastermind with boundless influence': Former PM takes on a new job
msn.com
science
2022-10-17 03:51:20

Former prime minister Scott Morrison has been dubbed a 'globalisation mastermind with boundless influence' as he exclusively offers his speaking services on a global networking platform.  On Monday, Worldwide Speakers Group (WWSG) announced Mr Morrison was exclusively joining its agency, made up of mostly conservative voices including former US vice president Mike Pence and former US speaker of the House of Representatives Newt Gingrich.For an undisclosed fee, the ex-PM will be available to speak at functions on a range of topics including religion in Liberal democracies, lessons from Covid and the approach to net-zero.
