Shinsei Bank Shares Jump After Report That SBI Considering Delisting Banking Unit
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 02:22:00

By Kosaku Narioka Shinsei Bank Ltd. shares rose sharply Thursday morning following a report that SBI Holdings Inc. is considering delisting the banking unit. The shares were recently 8.2% higher at 2,322 yen after surging as much as 16% earlier. SBI shares were down 0.7%. The Topix subindex for banks was recently 0.8% higher and the Nikkei Stock Average was down 1.3%. SBI would be expected to start a tender offer to boost its stake in Shinsei Bank and gain support from the Japanese government in order to achieve the delisting, Kyodo News reported Wednesday evening, without citing sources.
