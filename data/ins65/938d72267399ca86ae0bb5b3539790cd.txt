Brokerage firm Prabhudas Lilladher is bullish on private sector lender and sees the stock as a re-rating candidate given the consistent increase in core profitability. On the stock, the brokerage has suggested a price target of Rs 165, which at the current price signals a potential upside of 27%. The bank in the Q2 quarter emerged strong on all fronts. NII at the bank grew 19% YoY given the 20% YoY growth in loan. Also, NIM stood at a multi-quarter high of 3.6%. The brokerage report said that the bank's loan growth guidance for FY23 has been at 18-20%, while NIM target is raised to 3.
