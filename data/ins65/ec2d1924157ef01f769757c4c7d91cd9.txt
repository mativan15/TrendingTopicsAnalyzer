Two smartphones namely Moto G82 5G, Oppo K10 5G are all set to launch in India in the coming week. Check the latest details here. The coming week will see launches of two new smartphones in the Indian market, that is Moto G82 5G and Oppo K10 5G. Motorola is all set to launch its latest addition to the G series- Moto G82 5G on June 7 on Flipkart, while Oppo will be expanding the lineup of its K series with the launch of Oppo K10 5G on June 8, 2022. Both Motorola and Oppo have been informing about the insights of the new smartphones to be launched on Flipkart over their official Twitter handle.
