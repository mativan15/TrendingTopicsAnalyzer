DALLAS, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Lennox International Inc. (NYSE: LII) today announced that CEO Alok Maskara and CFO Joe Reitmeier are scheduled to host investor meetings at the KeyBanc Capital Markets 22nd Annual Industrials & Basic Materials Conference in Boston on Thursday, June 2. Presentation materials can be accessed that day on the company's website at www.lennoxinternational.com.
About Lennox International Lennox International Inc. is a global leader in energy-efficient climate-control solutions.
