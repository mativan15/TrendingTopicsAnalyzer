Greens threaten to withhold Senate support over $1.5 billion Darwin petrochemicals hub budget commitment

news
2022-10-20 20:36:23

The Greens are hoping to use their Senate numbers to pressure the Albanese government to drop its $1.5 billion commitment for a petrochemicals and gas carbon capture and storage hub planned for Darwin Harbour from next week's budget. The Greens want to use their balance of power in the Senate to stop the Darwin project going aheadDavid Pocock says $1.5 for fossil fuel projects isn't justified from squeezed budgetThe gas industry says the government should keep its contribution to tax revenue in mindThe Northern Territory government is spearheading plans to develop the manufacturing and minerals precinct in Middle Arm, south of Darwin, which would include a facility to convert natural gas into products like plastics and paint.
