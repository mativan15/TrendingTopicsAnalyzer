Rayonier Advanced Materials Appoints De Lyle W. Bloomquist as President and Chief Executive Officer

May 31, 2022 06:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time JACKSONVILLE, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Rayonier Advanced Materials Inc. (NYSE:RYAM) (the 'Company') today announced that De Lyle W. Bloomquist has been appointed President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company, effective immediately. Mr. Bloomquist succeeds Vito J. Consiglio, who has stepped down as President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company, and as a member of the Company's Board of Directors (the 'Board'), with the mutual agreement of the Board.
