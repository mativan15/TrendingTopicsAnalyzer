Alert at Delhi airport after bomb threat on flight from Moscow

2022-10-15 00:00:00

Image used for representative purpose only.NEW DELHI: Security agencies are on high alert at IGI Airport after a bomb threat on a flight arriving from Moscow was received on Thursday night. The flight safely landed around 3am on Friday and around 400 passengers and crew members were evacuated. Nothing has been discovered so far. According to airport sources, a threat message was received at the airport operations control centre (AOCC)'s social media site that bombs have been planted on board Russian flight SU-272.
