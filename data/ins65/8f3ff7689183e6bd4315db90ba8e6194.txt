October 20, 2022 06:45 AM Eastern Daylight Time MIAMI LAKES, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BankUnited, Inc. (the 'Company') (NYSE: BKU) today announced financial results for the quarter ended September 30, 2022.



"We are happy to announce a very strong earnings quarter with double digit growth in EPS. We're optimistic about the opportunities we see in front of us," said Rajinder Singh, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer.

For the quarter ended September 30, 2022, the Company reported net income of $87.
