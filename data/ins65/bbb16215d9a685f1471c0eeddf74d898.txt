Published: October 18, 20221. Beginnings: The BBC began life as the British Broadcasting Company, set up by the UK's major radio manufacturing companies to promote the sale of radio sets to the burgeoning audio market.
2. Start date: The BBC was officially created on 18 October 1922.
3. 1st transmission: It hit the airwaves on 14 November 1922, launched with its soon-to-be famous call sign '2LO Calling'. 2LO was the code name given to the BBC's London transmitter.
4. 1st programme: Its first programme was a news bulletin, consisting of an odd assortment of items (a robbery, a Shakespeare folio and fog), and read twice – once at normal speed, once twice as slowly – and listeners were asked to say which speed they preferred.
