PM Modi, Yogi and top business tycoons attend UP's third groundbreaking event
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 17:00:00

LUCKNOW: Calling it a testimony of their commitment to bring about an enduring and positive change in the life of citizens in the state, the Yogi Adityanath government would be holding the state's third groundbreaking ceremony on Friday. Foundation stone of projects to the tune of Rs 80,000 crore would be laid by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in the presence of top business tycoons of the country.Industry bigwigs, including Gautam Adani , chairman and managing director Adani Group, Kumar Mangalam Birla, chairman of the Aditya Birla Group, Niranjan Hiranandani, chairman of the Hiranandani group, Matthieu Eyries, vice-president Air Liquide Limited, and Anant Ambani , director, Reliance New Energy Solar Limited, will be sharing their vision for UP and also talk about their future plans.
