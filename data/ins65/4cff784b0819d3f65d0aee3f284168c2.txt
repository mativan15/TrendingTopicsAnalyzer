2d illustrations and photos Commercial property fintech Janover (JNRV) has filed for a proposed $16M initial public offering. Janover said in a filing that it plans to offer 1.7M shares, priced between $5 and $7 per share, which would raise the company around $10M if priced at the midpoint. Selling stockholders would be offering an additional 1M shares at the same price. Underwriters would be granted a 45-day option to buy up to 255K additional shares at the IPO price. EF Hutton is serving as lead bookrunner.
