RALEIGH, N.C., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Veranex, the only truly comprehensive, global, tech-enabled service provider dedicated to the MedTech industry, announced today the appointment of industry veteran Tom Daulton as chief executive officer (CEO). David Dockhorn, who previously served in this role, is assuming the newly created role of chief operating officer (COO).
