EDISON, N.J. (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Zebra Pen Corp., a global leader in the writing instrument space, is celebrating its 40th anniversary for Zebra Pen in the United States. Zebra Pen has grown from a small pen manufacturing company to an internationally recognized authority in writing instruments. To highlight the 40th anniversary of Zebra Pen, CEO Clem Restaino recently sat down in a one-on-one interview to discuss all things, Zebra. In this discussion, Restaino provided valuable insight into the history of the US division of Zebra Pen, the introduction of innovative new products, the challenges, and the achievements over the past 40 years, and so much more.
