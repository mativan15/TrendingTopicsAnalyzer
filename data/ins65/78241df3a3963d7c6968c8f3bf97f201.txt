Top-seeded Vols outlast Camels to reach regional finals
timesnews.net
news
2022-06-05 06:00:00

KNOXVILLE — Jorel Ortega hit two home runs, Cortland Lawson blasted a three-run shot in a four-run ninth inning and No. 1 overall seed Tennessee outlasted Campbell 12-7 on Saturday in the Knoxville Regional.Tennessee (55-7) — which broke the program record for wins in a season — advanced to Sunday's title game. The Vols will play the winner of an elimination game between Campbell (41-18) and Georgia Tech on Sunday. A UT loss would force a Monday finale.A two-run homer by Jarrod Belbin ignited a four-run third and the Fighting Camels grabbed 4-0 lead, chasing Vols starter Chase Dollander.
