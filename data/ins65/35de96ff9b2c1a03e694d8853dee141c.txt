They Accepted Thousands of Dollars to Move to a New Town. Was It Worth It?
cnet.com
news
2022-10-21 14:13:00

Would you move to Topeka, Kansas for $10,000?  Erik Bovell and his family took the offer, and it's been everything they could've hoped for.  Their experience highlights a unique quirk that came with the pandemic: When the shift to remote work turned the American office experience upside down, small and medium towns throughout the country saw an opportunity. 
No longer bound to the physical office in big, expensive cities, remote workers fanned out across the US in search of more space and more affordable accommodations.
