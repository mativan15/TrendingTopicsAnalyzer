October 17, 2022 12:29 PM Eastern Daylight Time MIRAMAR, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CCP Capital Strategies LLC ('CCP'), a private equity investment firm, today announced that a newly formed CCP affiliate has acquired 100 percent of the stock of Wireless Maritime Services, LLC ('WMS') from AT&T and Anuvu. Formed in 2005 and headquartered in Miramar, Florida, WMS provides maritime cellular networks enabling secure, high-performance connectivity at sea. WMS' existing management team will continue to lead to the business, which will now operate as an independent platform.
