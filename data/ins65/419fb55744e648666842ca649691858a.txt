There isn't much that Steve Kerr hasn't done in his three-and-a-half decades in the NBA.He won five rings as a player, four more as a coach, and was a general manager as well as a commentator.He played alongside Michael Jordan and played for Gregg Popovich and Phil Jackson. He currently coaches Steph Curry, the best shooter of all-time who has transformed the modern game.But as if he had to tick off another box or add another line to an already more than impressive CV, Kerr succeeded his mentor Popovich as head coach of USA Basketball for next year's FIBA Basketball World Cup and the Paris 2024 Olympic Games.
