MARTIN Lewis' wife Lara Lewington has revealed her bloodied face and stitches after suffering a horrific accident. 
The TV presenter took to her Instagram page with two selfies showing the gashes across her forehead and under her nose. 
Martin and Lara got married in 2009 and share a daughterCredit: Getty
Lara revealed her bloodied face on InstagramCredit: Instagram
Lara, 43, told her fans that she had fallen while riding an electric scooter and had to have stitches across her face. 
She posted two photos of her face, on the day of the accident and then six days into the painful healing process, on Instagram.
