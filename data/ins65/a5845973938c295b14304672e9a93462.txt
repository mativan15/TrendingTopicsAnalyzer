 A natural gas leak at a vacant duplex on Alexander Avenue sent five people to hospital.
Just after 4 p.m. on Tuesday, WFPS crews arrived at the home in the 500 block and confirmed there were high levels of gas in the atmosphere.While nobody was in the house, five people from a neighbouring home self-evacuated and taken to hospital in stable condition.
