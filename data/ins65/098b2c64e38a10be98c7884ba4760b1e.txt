Indian Bank declares 3 accounts with exposure worth Rs 362 cr as fraud
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 16:00:00

AgenciesThe bank said it has made provisions of up to 100 per cent in all these three fraud accounts as of March 31, 2022. Indian Bank on Friday said it has reported three non-performing accounts, including IL&FS Tamil Nadu , as fraud to the RBI and the public sector lender has a combined exposure of over Rs 362 crore to these companies. Three non-performing accounts have been declared as fraud and have been reported to the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) as per regulatory requirement, Indian Bank said in a BSE filing.
