BILBAO, Spain, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- David Moreno, Chief Marketing Officer of Europe's Virtualware, has been nominated for the Raising Star Award at this year's CMO Awards.
The CMO Awards, established in 2013, is the first and only CMO awards program designed and driven by an elite group of peers. The CMO Awards celebrate the marketing industry's best and brightest leaders.
Moreno is in charge of conceptualizing and leading the execution of the company's marketing, digital communications, and public relations.
