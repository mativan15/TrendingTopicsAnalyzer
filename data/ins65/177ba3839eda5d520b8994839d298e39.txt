More schools for growing North-West Sydney
nsw.gov.au
news
2022-10-18 03:42:36

North-West Sydney has seen more than $500 million invested in 24 new and upgraded schools thanks to the NSW Government's historic commitment to public education infrastructure. Minister for Education and Early Learning Sarah Mitchell joined Parliamentary Secretary for Education and Member for Riverstone Kevin Conolly today to announce the site for the new primary school in Nirimba Fields.Ms Mitchell said the new primary school site was part of the NSW Government's continuing commitment to deliver great public infrastructure for these growing communities.
