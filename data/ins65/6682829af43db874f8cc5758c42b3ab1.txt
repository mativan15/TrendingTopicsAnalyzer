Littleton, CO - (NewMediaWire) - October 17, 2022 - Littleton School of Music offers music lessons for children and adults in Littleton and surrounding areas. The music school has a wide portfolio of services, including vocals and singing, piano, guitar, drums, bass, violin, cello, ukulele, saxophone, trumpet, banjo, songwriting, and more. All these services are offered under one roof with flexible schedules throughout the week. The school opens every day of the week, including weekends, and students have the option to select either online or physical lessons.
