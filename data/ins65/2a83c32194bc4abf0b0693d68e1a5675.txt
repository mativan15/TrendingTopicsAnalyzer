Govt weighs up to 10% stake sale in Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 18:45:00

AgenciesMazagon Dock shares closed 3.8% lower at ₹285.70 on the BSE.The government is planning to sell up to 10% stake in Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Ltd. A proposal to this effect may soon be put in place, said a government official. The government holds 84.83% in the firm. At current valuations, the sale of a 10% stake may fetch the government around ₹570 crore. "The stake sale could be done through the offer for sale (OFS) route. Discussions are on between the administrative ministry and disinvestment department," the official said.
