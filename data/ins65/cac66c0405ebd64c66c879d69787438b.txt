WhatsApp users are being warned of a scam that locks them out of their accounts (Image: GETTY WHATSAPP)WhatsApp users have been put on alert about a new hacking attack which can lock you out of your account after picking up a single phone call. Besides hijacking an account, the attack also lets bad actors access a victim's contact list and read through their WhatsApp messages. And this nasty attack can be carried out on an unsuspecting WhatsApp user in a matter of minutes.The threat was highlighted by Rahul Sasi, the founder and CEO of digital risk protection company CloudSEK who outlined it in a LinkedIn post.
