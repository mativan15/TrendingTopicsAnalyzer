Happy shoppers hit San Jose's Santana Row on holiday afternoon

By Len Ramirez Updated on: May 30, 2022 / 4:04 PM / CBS San Francisco SAN JOSE -- Perfect weather and no COVID health restrictions seemed to put shoppers in a buying mood on the Memorial Day holiday as they filled the sidewalks at Santana Row. They were easing up on the purse strings and filling up their shopping bags."Now we can come out and have a little bit more freedom. Spend the money we didn't get to spend during the pandemic," said Kimmel Rodrigues, a shopper in San Jose.Nationwide, retail sales rose one percent in April according to the National Retail Federation.
