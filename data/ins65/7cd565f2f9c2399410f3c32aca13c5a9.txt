The right wing slot on the Rangers' first line has been a revolving door in recent years. Last year alone, the Blueshirts had stretches where Alexis Lafreniere, Barclay Goodrow, Artemi Panarin and Kaapo Kakko all played there before settling on Frank Vatrano down the stretch, a combination they kept into the postseason.
