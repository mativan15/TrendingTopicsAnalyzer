Princess Diana once "confronted" Queen Elizabeth The Queen Mother over showing favoritism for Prince William over his younger brother Prince Harry, according to a new biography.In an advance copy seen by Newsweek, the royals' relationship has been examined by author and historian Gareth Russell for his new book Do Let's Have Another Drink! The Dry Wit and Fizzy Life of Queen Elizabeth the Queen Mother.When Diana married Prince Charles in 1981, she became the Queen Mother's granddaughter-in-law, and despite the widowed queen being a close family friend, relations between her and the young princess were not always cordial.
