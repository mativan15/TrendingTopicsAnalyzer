Scharfsinn86 With climate change creating chaos all over the world this year, there is beginning to be serious attention paid to exit from burning fossil fuels. The "low hanging fruit" for emissions reductions is the power and transport industries. In 2021 global emissions from the transport sector accounted for an astonishing 37% of CO2 emissions from end-use sectors. By far the biggest global transport emissions (~76%) came from wheeled transport (in comparison with shipping ~11% and aviation ~9%).
