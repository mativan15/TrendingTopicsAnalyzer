Una Mullally: Money talks with Dublin City Council when it comes to spaces for artists
irishtimes.com
news
2022-06-06 06:14:44

At this stage, one should never be shocked by the communication skills of Dublin City Council, but nevertheless, they still keep some surprises in the tank. Recently, they rattled the arts community in Dublin (again), with some correspondence from Richard Shakespeare, the assistant chief executive of the council.On Richmond Road in Dublin 3, a group of artists have been struggling with uncertainty over their studio space. It looks like the building will be sold, so they approached the council for help.
