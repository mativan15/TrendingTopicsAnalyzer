The pound is at its lowest level in nine days, reversing moderate gains from Thursday. Analysts said little has materially changed for investors; and sterling is likely to remain weak. It comes as the U.K. faces recession warnings and inflation challenges.  LONDON — The British pound fell 1% against the dollar Friday morning, more than wiping out the moderate gains it made following the resignation of Prime Minister Liz Truss.
