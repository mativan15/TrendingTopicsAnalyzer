First Trust plans launch of new Aerospace & Defense ETF

2022-10-20 12:07:49

bfk92/E+ via Getty Images First Trust Exchange Traded Fund intends to launch a new fund focused on high-tech military investments. According to the U.S. Securities and Exchange filing the ETF, called the First Trust Indxx Aerospace & Defense ETF, will consist of a universe of U.S. companies engaged in business activities associated with hypersonic warfare weapons technology, high power microwaves, laser technology products and electromagnetic weapons. Additionally, the fund also will engulf organizations that develop rockets, satellites, military aircrafts and cybersecurity systems.
