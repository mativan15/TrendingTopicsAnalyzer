October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time GLENDALE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Dine Brands Global, Inc. (NYSE: DIN), the parent company of Applebee's Neighborhood Grill & Bar® and IHOP® restaurants, will announce its financial results for the third quarter of 2022 on November 2, 2022, before the stock market opens.

In conjunction with this announcement, Dine Brands will also host a conference call on November 2, 2022, at 9:00 a.m. (Eastern Time) to discuss the Company's financial results and business outlook.
