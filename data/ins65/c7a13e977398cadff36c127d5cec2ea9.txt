Volta decreases 54% of U.S. staff through furloughs, other workforce reductions
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-21 21:43:57

Volta (NYSE:VLTA) announced a decrease of 54% of U.S. full-time employees through this and other workforce reductions that began in Q2 2022.
Inclusive of these latest efforts, this continued strategic focus will result in 43% reduction in cash SG&A, improved efficiency, and greater market alignment.
"Volta's management team is positioning the business to capture this dynamic market opportunity while simultaneously reducing costs and growing revenue," said Vince Cubbage, Interim CEO of Volta.
