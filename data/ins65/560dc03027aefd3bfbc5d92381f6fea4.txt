Kim Kardashian has shared a glittering clip of her dressed up and ready to party as news breaks that she's 'not talking' to ex husband Kanye West. Taking to Instagram, Kim, 41, shared a short clip of her wearing a glistening silver dress with her 331M followers. This glistening dress featured laced up ribbons connecting the front and sides, Kim paired the look with a chic up-do and silver jewellery. Over the clip is the Armani White song Billie Eilish as the camera person uses flash feature to emphasise the sparkles on the dress.
