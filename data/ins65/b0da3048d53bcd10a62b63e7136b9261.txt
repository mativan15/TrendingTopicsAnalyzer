Luis D. Ortiz was a luxury realtor who starred in seasons 2-5 of Bravo's Million Dollar Listing New York and made some appearances in season 8. He also hosted Netflix' The World's Most Amazing Vacation Rentals in 2021.In March 2019, baby Leela was born. Her mother Nikita Singh and Luis were not together anymore, but would coparent Leela. In June 2021, Nikita accused him of domestic abuse. She had fled to the UK and created a GoFundMe campaign to help raise money for her and Leela.
