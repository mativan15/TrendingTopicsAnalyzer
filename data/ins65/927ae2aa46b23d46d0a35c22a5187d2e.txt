Oct. 20, 2022 8:37 AM ETBy: Niloofer Shaikh, SA News Editor

October Philly Fed Business Outlook: -8.7 vs. -5.0 consensus, -9.9 prior (unrevised).
The diffusion index for future general activity fell from -3.9 to -14.9.
The employment index rose from 12.0 to 28.5 this month, more than offsetting its decline from last month. 
The future employment index declined 10 points to 12.2. The future capital expenditures index was essentially unchanged at 4.4
