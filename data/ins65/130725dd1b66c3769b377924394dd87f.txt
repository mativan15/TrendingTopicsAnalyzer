The Playlist Spanish auteur Fernando Trueba has wowed the cinema world over the years, and he has the accolades to prove it. He's won three Goya Awards for Best Director, a Silver Bear for 1986's 'Year Of Enlightenment,' and the Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film in 1994 with 'Belle Époque.'
