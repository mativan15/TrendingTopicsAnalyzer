Published: 01:43 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 01:43 EDT, 18 October 2022 The Labor government's first budget could push up the cost of living and increase inflation, Anthony Albanese's treasurer has admitted.   Jim Chalmers revealed that the government's priority would be to provide funding and support to the victims of floods in NSW, Victoria and Tasmania.However, he admitted the move would have a 'substantial impact' on the cost of living. It could lead to another surge in grocery prices and it's unclear just how high the cost to Aussies will be.
