Cannabis startup Eaze quietly laid off as many as 25 employees as pressure mounts for the once high-flying company
businessinsider.com
news
2022-06-02 19:56:42

Cannabis tech startup Eaze quietly laid off as many as 25 employees, Insider has learned.The layoffs took place on Wednesday, and the cuts included members of Eaze's engineering team and live operations team, which handles its delivery service. Two former employees described the job cuts to Insider. They asked not to be identified to preserve relationships and protect future job opportunities in the industry.Eaze didn't respond to requests for comment sent to its press line and to an outside PR firm.
