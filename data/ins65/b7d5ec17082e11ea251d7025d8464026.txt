October 17, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time HAMILTON, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Dodge Construction Network, in partnership with Procore Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: PCOR), today released its most recent study on how economic, workforce and technology trends are transforming the global construction industry.



The report, 'Top Business Issues for Specialty Contractors,' reveals that 99% of these companies experience erosion of their profit margin during construction, one third of which is tied to unplanned rework.
