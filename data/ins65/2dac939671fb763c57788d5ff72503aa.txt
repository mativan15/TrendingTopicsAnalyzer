How do you begin? What do you allow? Will they turn their brains to mush?

One of my internal parent debates has been the introduction of video games. My debate ended, however, when a friend introduced them for me, during a sleepover.

Their love for gaming didn't really come as a surprise, as their father was what would be referred to as a 'gamer.' I, too, played a Super Nintendo around their age but left it back in my teenage years.

As with each parenting decision, I started with a list of non-negotiables and strict limits.
