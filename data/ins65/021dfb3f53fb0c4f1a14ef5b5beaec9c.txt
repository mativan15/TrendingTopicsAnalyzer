Published: 16:35 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 16:39 EDT, 19 October 2022 NEW YORK (AP) - Andrew Cuomo isn´t begging for forgiveness. Fourteen months after resigning from office in a sexual harassment scandal, the former New York governor is elbowing his way back into the public eye, launching a podcast and a political action committee in what could be the first steps toward a comeback bid for political office. But he isn´t following the usual playbook for misbehaving politicians seeking redemption.
