A lingering pandemic. Geopolitical instability. Rising inflation around the world. Executives and business leaders are gathering at the Bloomberg Equality Summit on Tuesday to discuss how to stem rising inequality amid unrelenting global economic crises. What does a more inclusive post-Covid model of development look like? Can governments address ballooning inflation without harming those at the bottom of the economic ladder? The day kicks off with Anne Richards, the chief executive officer of Fidelity International.
