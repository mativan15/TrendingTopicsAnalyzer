Matthew Davis is President of GDI Insurance Agency, Inc. getty Do businesses really need to purchase cyber insurance? The answer to this question goes beyond the typical, "it depends," and more into whether or not the business has exposure to risks associated with what cyber insurance covers. Many business owners believe that cyber insurance only protects them from events such as hacks and theft of customer data; however, that is just one small part of what cyber insurance is all about. In today's ever-changing technological climate, business owners must constantly adapt every part of their businesses in order to stay ahead of the curves in terms of sales, marketing, communication, security and technology.
