Published: 17:02 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 17:12 EDT, 21 October 2022 As the cost of living spirals, employing a cleaner is more of a luxury than ever. Around my area, some people are cutting back on outside help and others have cut down on the hours their cleaner does to save money.Those who do all the chores themselves, meanwhile, are on the hunt for the cheapest, most efficient ways to keep their homes spick and span without spending money on branded cleaning products. So with all this in mind, I've had a chat with my friend Luke Murfitt, founder of cleaning company Integrity Cleaning, to get money-saving tips.
