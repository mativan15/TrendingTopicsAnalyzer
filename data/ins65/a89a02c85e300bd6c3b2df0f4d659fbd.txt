Wendy Morton departed as Chief Whip tonight, the Telegraph understands, amid chaos over a vote on fracking.Craig Whittaker, her deputy, is also understood to have quit.It came as the Prime Minister appointed Grant Shapps, a prominent backer of Rishi Sunak, as Home Secretary after Ms Braverman was sacked for sharing confidential information from her personal email account.The move will be seen as an olive branch to her opponents on the Conservative backbenches, who have called for her to appoint a Cabinet that is more representative of the wider Tory party.
