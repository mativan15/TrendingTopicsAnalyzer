Marlborough's John Baird likes getting things done and that's what he worked on doing for the last 45 years. Baird's services to business and governance were acknowledged as he was made a member of the New Zealand Order of Merit in the Queen's Birthday honours. Former managing director of Ceramco Corp Ltd and Goodman Fielder Wattie, Baird has an extensive marketing and business management background, especially in the food industry.
