SINGAPORE, Oct. 14, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- XT.COM has listed QI Blockchain(QIE) in the Innovation Zone and the QIE/USDT trading pair has opened to trading at 2022-9-22 13:00 (UTC).

About QIE Coin

QIE Coin is the native cryptocurrency of Qi Blockchain and is designed for Finance with low transaction fees and real-time execution. Two QIE coins are minted per block as the QI Blockchain uses the PoW consensus. PoW mining difficulty for QIE coins will increase over time until the mining reward reaches as low as 0.
