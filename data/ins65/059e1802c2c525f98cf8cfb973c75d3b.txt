CUSO acknowledged for flexible culture, people-first philosophy

FARMINGTON HILLS, Mich., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Member Driven Technologies (MDT), a CUSO that hosts the Episys® core platform from Symitar® to provide a private cloud alternative for core processing and IT needs, has been named a Best Place to Work in Financial Technology by Arizent and Best Companies Group for the third year in a row. MDT ranked 18 out of 50 companies.

This annual survey and awards program is designed to identify and honor the best employers in the financial technology industry.
