Visier's people analytics technology will be embedded in TriNet Zenefits solutions to offer small, and medium sized organizations deeper insights into their people

VANCOUVER, BC, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Visier , the globally recognized leader in people analytics and workforce solutions for people-powered business, today announced an embedded partnership with TriNet Zenefits, a wholly owned subsidiary of TriNet (NYSE: TNET) and leader in HR and payroll software and services purpose-built for small and mid-size companies.
