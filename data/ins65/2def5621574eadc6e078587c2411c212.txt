Scholarships of up to $3,000 are available to students transferring into the University

PHOENIX, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--University of Phoenix offers scholarships for transfer students and is highlighting the scholarship availability during National Transfer Student Week as part of a broader effort to raise awareness and understanding of the practical ways that transferring credit can save students time and money. Scholarships of up to $3,000 are available throughout the year and are awarded to prospective students for the opportunity to pursue a degree at the University of Phoenix.
