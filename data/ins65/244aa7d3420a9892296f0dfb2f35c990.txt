ETMarkets Smart Talk: Invest in small & midcaps only in a staggered manner for next 3-5 years: Unmesh Sharma
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 04:30:00

'Investing in small & midcaps should be done in a staggered manner and the horizon should be 3-5 years as longer horizon of investing and staggered buying act as adequate hedges against fluctuation associated with small & midcap stocks,' says Unmesh Sharma, Head of Institutional Equities, Securities.In an interview with ETMarkets, Sharma said: 'During this turbulent socio-economic time, it is best to deploy cash in high conviction stock ideas where near term events do not permanently damage long term prospects.
