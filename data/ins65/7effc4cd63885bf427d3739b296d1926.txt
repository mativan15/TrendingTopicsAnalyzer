October 20, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LITTLE ROCK, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Private investment firm The Stephens Group, LLC ('The Stephens Group') announced that it has made a minority investment in the formation of Exigent (or the 'Company'), which will provide a variety of mission-critical repair, maintenance, and replacement services to large facility operations for education, healthcare, industrial, government, and commercial customers. The investment was made in support of Huron Capital's ('Huron') controlling investment in the company.
