Nicola Sturgeon published her economic case for independence this afternoon (Image: SKY)Nicola Sturgeon today unveiled her latest dodgy dossier aimed at convincing Scots to vote for independence.On the face of it, the document sets out a dreamy vision of life outside the UK.But dig a little deeper and the sheer desperation of the plan is clear.The "Building a New Scotland: A stronger economy with independence" publication was the last of three reports from the Scottish Government making the case for going it alone.
