October 17, 2022 01:02 PM Eastern Daylight Time MINNEAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--For the eighth year in a row, Barron's has named JNBA Financial Advisors' CEO Richard S. Brown to its list of the Top 100 Independent Advisors. Brown is the top-ranked Minnesota financial advisor on the list and ranked 51st in the country.



'Our independence is such an important part of who we are, what we do, and the commitment we make to our clients every day,' says Kim Brown, president of JNBA Financial Advisors. 'JNBA's fiercely independent philosophy was what allowed our firm to break away from the pack over 40 years ago and help pioneer a client-centric approach and business model.
