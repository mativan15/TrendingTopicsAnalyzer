Dragons' Den pitch labelled ‘ridiculous' is now the 'top ever brand' to come from the show
express.co.uk
finance
2022-06-05 15:57:00

Magic Whiteboard has now become an office staple, sold all across the world. Husband and wife duo, Neil and Laura Westwood, entered into the Den in 2008 seeking an investment for £100,000 for a 15 percent equity stake in the company.The duo outranked other famous Dragon Den products like Trunki in analysis by marketing platform experts Affise.They compiled a list of the six most lucrative companies to come out of the Den over 16 series, since it started in 2005.Magic Whiteboard was one of the early pitches in 2006.
