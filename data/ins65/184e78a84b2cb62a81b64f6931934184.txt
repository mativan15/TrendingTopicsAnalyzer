Browns QB Deshaun Watson sued again; 23rd lawsuit alleges sexual misconduct

Another woman has filed a lawsuit against Cleveland Browns quarterback Deshaun Watson, becoming the 23rd civil case to accuse him of sexual misconduct during massage sessions in 2020.It is the first lawsuit filed against him since the 22 others were filed in March and April of 2021 and potentially could complicate any plan for the NFL to resolve questions about Watson's playing status before the season.In this case, the lawsuit filed Tuesday states the woman had three encounters with Watson in Houston that got progressively worse.
