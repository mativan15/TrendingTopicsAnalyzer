Global equity funds see first weekly inflows in seven weeks
reuters.com
news
2022-05-27 10:26:33

May 27 (Reuters) - Global equity funds saw money inflows in the week to May 25 as some investors took heart from a rebound in markets this week, after sharp declines in stock markets in the previous seven weeks.According to Refinitiv Lipper, investors bought a net $6.16 billion worth of global equity funds, marking their first weekly net buying since April 6.Fund flows: Global equities, bonds and money marketThe MSCI all country world index (.MIWD00000PUS) has gained over 3% this week, after slumping about 12.
