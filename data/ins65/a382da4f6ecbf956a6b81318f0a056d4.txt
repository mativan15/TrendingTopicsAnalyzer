AgenciesAdmit card for the West Bengal Public Service Commission (WBPSC) prelims examination for 2022 will be released on Tuesday, May 31, 2022. The WBPSC prelims examination will be held on June 19, 2022. As per the official notification, carrying the admit card to the exam hall is mandatory for all candidates. Apart from the admit card, the official WBPSC notice also mentions that candidates must carry two Stamp-sized photographs to the exam hall.
