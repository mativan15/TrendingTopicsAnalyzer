October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK & PHILADELPHIA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Odyssey Investment Partners, LLC ('Odyssey'), a growth-oriented middle-market private equity firm, today announced that it has signed an agreement to acquire Magna Legal Services ('Magna'), a litigation support services provider, from CIVC Partners, LP ('CIVC'). Financial terms of the transaction, which is expected to close in the fourth quarter, were not disclosed.



Magna provides litigation support services to over 13,000 law firms, corporations, insurance carriers, and government agencies.
