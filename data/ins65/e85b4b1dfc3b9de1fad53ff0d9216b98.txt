Columnist Saira Khan says it was an inspiration the way the country came together to mark the Platinum Jubilee and adds that the Queen must be admired for a lifelong commitment to her roleHer Majesty rules from a gilded cage, says our columnist Whether you are a royalist or not, you have to admit that this bank holiday weekend is exactly the tonic we needed. After years of misery – what with Brexit, the pandemic, war in Ukraine and the cost-of-living crisis – most of us are exhausted. Yet there is always something very special in how our country comes together, celebrating, united, proud and inclusive.
