The Gujarat government has cut VAT from 15% to 5% on gas fuel.
Ahead of the announcement of the Assembly poll dates in Gujarat, the State government on Monday, October 17, 2022, slashed Value Added Tax by 10% on Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Piped Natural Gas (PNG).
