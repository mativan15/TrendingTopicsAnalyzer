Net Revenues Increased by 11% to U$ 86 million, with Adjusted EBITDA Up 15% Year-Over-Year to $9 Million

MIAMI and BARRANQUILLA, Colombia, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Procaps Group S.A. (NASDAQ: PROC) ('Procaps'), a leading integrated LatAm healthcare and pharmaceutical conglomerate, today announced its preliminary financial results for the quarter ended March 31, 2022 ('Q1-22').



Preliminary Q1-22 Results Highlights

Net revenues for Q1-22 totaled $86 million, an increase of 11% when compared with the quarter ended March 31, 2021 ('Q1-21'), primarily driven by strong demand across Procaps Colombia, CAN and CASAND, as well as from our continued rollout of new product launches.
