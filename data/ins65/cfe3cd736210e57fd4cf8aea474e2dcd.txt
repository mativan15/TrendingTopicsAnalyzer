Critical Craven County waterway has been named River of the Year
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 09:14:37

The Neuse River has been named "River of the Year" by a national organization that advocates for the conservation of waterways across the country.

American Rivers, a national organization that advocates for the conservation of waterways across the country, has named this Craven County river as its 2022 River of the Year.

The Neuse River, a critical waterway that flows from Durham County into the Pamlico Sound, the river is one of the widest rivers in the U.S., according to U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary.
