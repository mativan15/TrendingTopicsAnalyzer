LIC market capitalisation falls below ICICI Bank to 7th place
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 02:15:00

(This story originally appeared in on Jun 01, 2022)MUMBAI: LIC's market capitalisation dropped below that of ICICI Bank (Rs 5.2 lakh crore) on Tuesday to the 7th position as Dalal Street expressed its disappointment over its results declared on Monday. LIC's shares fell 3% or by over Rs 25 to Rs 811, bringing down its market capitalisation to Rs 5.1 lakh crore. On May 17, when LIC got listed on stock exchanges, the corporation was the fifth biggest company with a market cap of over Rs 5.5 lakh crore.
