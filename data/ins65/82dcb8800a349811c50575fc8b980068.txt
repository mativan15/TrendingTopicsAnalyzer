Glida46/iStock via Getty Images Vale (NYSE:VALE) is a Brazilian mining company that produces a well-diversified set of metals, including nickel, copper, cobalt, platinum, rhodium, ruthenium, iridium, gold, and silver. It is a company that proves to have slow and steady growth leading into 2026. As they must reduce debt when needed, this is a profitable company in the long run, no matter when a global slowdown occurs.
