AgenciesRepresentative ImageSundaram Home Finance has revised the interest rates on its deposits with effect from June 1, for various tenures offered to individuals, trusts and senior citizens. Accordingly, the rate on two year deposits for individuals has been hiked to 5.90 per cent as against 5.65 per cent earlier while for deposits of three to five years it has been increased to 6.05 per cent per annum as against 5.80 per cent, the company said in a statement on Tuesday.
