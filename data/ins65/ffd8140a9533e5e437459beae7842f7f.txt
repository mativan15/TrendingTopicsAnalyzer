Liz Truss premiership hanging by a thread after bruising day in Westminster

news
2022-10-19 21:35:00

Liz Truss's premiership is hanging by a thread after an extraordinary day in Westminster that saw a cabinet minister resign and a Commons motion descend into chaos with allegations of "manhandling and bullying". The unprecedented events have led to some Tory MPs declaring the Conservative party is "finished", with one hitting out at the "talentless people" who backed Ms Truss "for a seat around the cabinet table".
