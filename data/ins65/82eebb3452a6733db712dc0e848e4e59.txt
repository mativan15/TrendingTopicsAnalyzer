Using firecrackers on Diwali in Delhi may land you in jail for 6 months
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 18:28:18

New Delhi Delhi environment minister Gopal Rai on Wednesday said the state government was prepared to enforce the ban on firecrackers across the Capital this Diwali, adding that teams have been set up to keep a tab on violators. He warned that purchasing and bursting of firecrackers in the Capital is punishable with a ₹200 fine and 6 months of jail time, while those involved in manufacturing, storing and selling firecrackers in the city could be fined up to ₹5,000 and be jailed for 3 years.'The Delhi Police has formed 210 teams.
