October 18, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time ALMATY, Kazakhstan--(BUSINESS WIRE)--In an 8-K filing today, Freedom Holding Corp. (Nasdaq: FRHC) announced that it had appointed the independent registered public accounting firm Deloitte LLP in Kazakhstan ('Deloitte'), a member of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu Limited, a UK private company limited by guarantee, for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2023.
