Oct. 19—COLUMBIA — The Town of Columbia Parks and Recreation Department will hold a Recreation Park Celebration on Saturday.

The event will be held from noon to 2 p. m. at Columbia Recreation Park, 60 Hennequin Road in Columbia.

The event is free to attend and will feature free games, food, live music and disc golf.

There will be pizza and games such as four square, volleyball and Jenga.

Columbia Parks and Recreation Director Debbie Fiske said that her department is holding the celebration to promote the park as well as its newly completed pavilion.
