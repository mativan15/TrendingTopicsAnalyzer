Published: 05:07 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 05:07 EDT, 30 May 2022 By Sophie Yu and Brenda Goh BEIJING, May 30 (Reuters) - A headache-prone Pokemon character given away in KFC children's meals has become China's most wanted toy, selling for up to $200, as people seek ways to express their frustrations over COVID-19 lockdowns. KFC began handing out Psyduck in China on May 21, ahead of a promotion for Children's Day on June 1, and the toy has become a focal point for social media users angered by weeks of lockdown.
