Ameet Naik, managing partner, Naik Naik & Co.Even though pandemic Covid-19 and subsequent lockdowns are histories, its ripples in terms of litigation and disputes will start appearing. Some of the sectors including retail, real estate and financial services are expected to see a rise in the disputes in coming days, feels Ameet Naik, managing partner of law firm Naik Naik & Co.
