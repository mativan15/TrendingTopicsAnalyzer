Raaj Kumar Anand to be new Delhi minister
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 18:36:39

New Delhi Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal on Wednesday announced that he has recommended Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) legislator Raaj Kumar Anand's name for induction into the state government cabinet.'I have recommended his name for induction into the cabinet to the LG (lieutenant governor Vinai Kumar Saaxena),' Kejriwal said at a press conference. It is not yet clear which portfolios Anand will be assigned.The date for the oath-taking ceremony is yet to be announced. No reaction was available from the LG's office.
