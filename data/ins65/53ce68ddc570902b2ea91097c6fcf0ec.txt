OverActive Media and Bell Take Their Partnership to the Next Level with Multi-Year Extension

TORONTO, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- OverActive Media (TSXV: OAM) (OTCQB: OAMCF) ('OverActive' or the 'Company'), a global sports, media and entertainment company for today's generation of fans, and Bell, Canada's largest communications company and a key strategic investor in OverActive Media, today announced the renewal and expansion of their partnership, first established in 2019, for an additional three years. The multi-year extension further connects Bell to esports fans and reinforces its engagement with the gaming community in Canada.
