WhatsApp may soon allow users to keep important messages intact even in the disappearing chats. Know how WhatsApp disappearing messages feature will work. WhatsApp disappearing messages feature was rolled out not too long ago. It allows users to make their WhatsApp messages disappear for any particular contact after a specific time. This is an automatic feature which ensures it removes chats as per the requirement of the user. If your contact has turned on the WhatsApp disappearing chat option on, then there is no other way but to let the chat go.
