'Will-o'-the-Wisp' ('Fogo-Fatuo'): Film Review
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-05-31 16:49:39

Portuguese writer-director João Pedro Rodrigues' queer fantasy sci-fi musical stars Mauro Costa as a royal prince who learns the ways of firefighting from Andre Cabral's hunky hose jockey. Queer, inclusive, eco-conscious and frankly pretty pornographic, Portuguese director Joao Pedro Rodrigues' musical fantasy Will-o'-the-Wisp (Fogo-Fátuo) was arguably the most fun anyone could have had for 67 minutes in a cinema during the 2022 Cannes Film Festival. Which is not to say the film doesn't also have its serious moments, given that it touches on environmental catastrophe, lost love and even death.
