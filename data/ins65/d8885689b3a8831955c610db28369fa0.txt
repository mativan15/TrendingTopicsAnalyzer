LOS ANGELES, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- NuCalm CEO Jim Poole will be this year's featured presenter at the 2022 Neuroscience Summit. The brain health and wellness event features a stellar lineup of New York Times Bestselling authors, Harvard scientists and mental health experts. Complete details are on the event's website: www.nsciencesummit.com.
