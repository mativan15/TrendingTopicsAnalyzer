GREGGS fans looking to bag themselves a free sausage roll are in luck this Jubilee bank holiday weekend - but there's a catch.
Delivery giant Just Eat has partnered with the popular pastry chain to treat customers to a free flaky favourite. 
Greggs sausage rolls are a fan favouriteCredit: Alamy
Just Eat customers can celebrate the Queen's Platinum Jubilee with a freebie that is quintessentially British - a Greggs sausage roll.
From Thursday June 2 to Sunday June 5, the delivery giant will be rolling out the offer - but the freebie isn't available to everyone.
