STAFF at 14 MORE train companies will walk out over the bonfire night weekend in a fresh wave of strike misery.
Britain's rail network will grind to a halt again on November 3 and 5 in the RMT union announced. 
Staff at 14 MORE train companies will walk out over the bonfire night weekend in a fresh wave of strike miseryCredit: Getty
It coincides with separate walkouts at Network Rail on November 3, 5 and 7 - and London Underground and Overground on November 3.
And pubs risk running out of beer as 1,000 delivery drivers stage walkouts which will run into the same weekend as the rail disruption.
