NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Revlon, Inc. (NYSE: REV) ('Revlon' or the 'Company') today announced that on October 20, 2022, the NYSE Regulatory Oversight Committee's Committee for Review rejected the Company's appeal and affirmed the New York Stock Exchange Staff's ('NYSE Staff') decision to delist the Company's Class A common stock following the June 15, 2022 Chapter 11 filing by the Company and certain of its subsidiaries in the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of New York.
