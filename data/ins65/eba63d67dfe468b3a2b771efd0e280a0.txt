High fuel costs drag down UltraTech's Q2 net despite higher sales
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 14:07:11

High power and fuel expenses weighed heavily on the margins of India's largest cement makerin line with other cement players, and its profit for the September quarter nearly halved.The company reported a consolidated top line of Rs 13,893 crore, up 16% year on year on higher cement sales and better realisation. In fact, capacity utilisation went up by 5 percentage points on-year to 76%.However, the increase in expenses far outpaced revenue growth. The company's total expenses went up by 27% year on year to Rs 12,934 crore, led primarily by a 71% increase in power and fuel expenses.
