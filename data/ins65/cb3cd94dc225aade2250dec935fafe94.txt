Imagine Dragons postpone South American tour over singer Dan Reynolds' vocal, knee injuries
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 18:30:00

Pop band Imagine Dragons has postponed its South America tour. The music band conveyed this message to their fans on October 17, Monday. This decision to postpone the South American leg of their Mercury World Tour was taken considering extreme health conditions of Dan. They said that Daniel Coulter Reynolds, the lead vocalist of the music band, is facing leg ligament issues. The band shared the message with the world by leveraging the Twitter platform. 
They further continued that in their tenure of 12 years, it is the first time they have postponed a tour.
