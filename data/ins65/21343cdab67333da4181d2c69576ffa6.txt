Huda Kattan on NFT investing: I've made money and 'I've lost a lot of money'

As the founder and CEO of Huda Beauty — with more than 50 million Instagram followers, and a net worth of $500 million — Huda Kattan is one of the most influential people on the planet. Kattan is now shifting gears and bringing her success to the NFT realm.Kattan already owns hundreds of NFT's, including the projects from World of Women, most of which she purchased on the marketplace OpenSea."As much money as I've made, I've lost a lot of money," said Kattan in an interview with Yahoo Live (video above).
