Other Types of LLCs
The steps outlined above describe how to register a domestic LLC. 'Domestic' means the LLC is formed under the laws of South Dakota and operates for profit in an industry that doesn't require special licensure. There are special types of LLCs which may be formed in another state, provide specialized services or operate under a larger LLC or business parent company.
