Rain, rain and more rain is not the kind of weather condition that many fruit and vegetables lap up and love.But, in between the ridiculously expensive greens like lettuce and the out of season favourites like stone fruit, you can still find some delicious produce to spruce up weekly meals.For adventurous cooks, there are a few rarer offerings entering the market this month too.'Generally speaking, it's a great time for winter veggies,' says Todd Irvine, owner of Ziggy's Fresh in Fyshwick, Canberra, voted 2021 greengrocer of the year in ACT and New South Wales in the Sydney markets fresh awards.
