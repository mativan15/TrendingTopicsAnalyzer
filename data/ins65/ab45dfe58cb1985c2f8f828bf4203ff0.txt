Why sustainable tech is becoming a top initiative for execs
computerworld.com
news
2022-10-17 18:59:00

Sustainable technologies can increase the efficiency of IT services (and boost overall enterprise efficiency) using  technologies such as traceability, analytics, artificial intelligence and renewable energy — and the issue is now a top 10 initiative for CEOs, CIOs, and other top executives, according to a new report by research firm Gartner.Gartnes found that the issue of sustainability traverses all of the strategic technology trends for 2023. Amd by 2025, 50% of CIOs will have performance metrics tied to the sustainability of the IT organization.
