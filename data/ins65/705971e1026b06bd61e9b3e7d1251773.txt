October 20, 2022 02:28 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Reps. Ben Cline (R-Va.), Bob Good (R-Va.), Morgan Griffith (R-Va.), and Rob Wittman (R-Va.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.
