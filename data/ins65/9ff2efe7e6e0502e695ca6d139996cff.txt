Gujarat: Now, test to tell if meat has beef in one hour flat!
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 03:45:00

AHMEDABAD: With stringent laws in place related to cow progeny protection in Gujarat, a new method is introduced in the state to ascertain whether the seized stock of meat contains beef. The LAMP DNA method, currently introduced in limited capacity in Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar , is claimed to provide results within an hour. Conventional methods including serological analysis and DNA analysis take more than a day, said experts.Nikunj Brahmbhatt , a senior faculty at National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU), said that his doctoral thesis 'Development of Loop-mediated Isothermal Amplification (LAMP) for forensic investigation of closed inter-species animals' got completed in 2020 from Kadi Sarva Vishwavidyalaya under guideship of Prof Vivek Upasani .
