In the latest trading session, Nike (NKE) closed at $120.95, marking a -1.99% move from the previous day. This change lagged the S&P 500's 1.64% loss on the day. At the same time, the Dow lost 1.05%, and the tech-heavy Nasdaq lost 0.41%.

Coming into today, shares of the athletic apparel maker had gained 4.03% in the past month. In that same time, the Consumer Discretionary sector lost 1.09%, while the S&P 500 gained 0.73%.

Nike will be looking to display strength as it nears its next earnings release, which is expected to be June 27, 2022.
