Higher education in Canada is attracting a growing number of young French people
einnews.com
world
2022-06-03 02:18:34

The University of Montreal. When she was in high school, Axelle Berthelot was thinking of enrolling to a business school after high school. At a student fair in Lyon, she met with representents from Canadian universities who convinced them the education they were providing was the one for her. Convinced, she immediately plead her case with her parents: a change of scenery, professional opportunities after graduation, moderate fees compared to a business school in France, courses in French or in English.
