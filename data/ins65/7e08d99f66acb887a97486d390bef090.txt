Industry Specialty Services Partner at BDO USA, LLP
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 14:27:30

BDO USA, LLP has named Kasra Mojtahedi a Partner in the firm's Industry Specialty Services practice. Mojtahedi leads BDO's strategy, enterprise transformation and technology enablement solutions for people and project centric organizations. His team focuses on corporate and digital strategy development, mergers and acquisitions integration, enterprise resource planning system selection and implementation, business process improvement, and organizational change management.
