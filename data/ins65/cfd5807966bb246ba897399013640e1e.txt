RIL to rejig EPC resources

MUMBAIReliance Industries (RIL) chairman Mukesh Ambani plans to separate the financial services business from his flagship and list it on the exchanges under the name Jio Financial Services (JFS). RIL shareholders will get one share of JFS for every share held in the company.Financial services, which had a turnover of Rs 1,387 crore in fiscal 2022, is the smallest business segment within RIL. RIL will house the financial services in Reliance Strategic Investments (to be renamed JFS), a 100% arm of the flagship and an RBI-registered non-banking financial company.
