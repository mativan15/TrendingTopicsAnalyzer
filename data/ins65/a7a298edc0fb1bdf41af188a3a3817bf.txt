May 31, 2022 06:56 PM Eastern Daylight Time SYDNEY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--OpenSolar Pty Ltd, a software company focused on empowering solar installers with the world's most accurate and entirely free solar design and sales platform, and Global Sustainable Energy Solutions (GSES), the internationally recognised renewable energy engineering consultancy, education and training provider, today announced a first-of-its-kind platform integration partnership that offers Australian solar installers a one stop shop for running their solar business while securing and maintaining Australian Clean Energy Council accreditations.
