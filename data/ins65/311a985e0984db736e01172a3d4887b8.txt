Rupert Murdoch considering merging Fox and News Corp once again
theguardian.com
sport
2022-10-15 01:01:11

The two parts of Rupert Murdoch's media empire are discussing a merger nearly a decade after they split.The merger would combine Murdoch's Fox News and TMZ assets with News Corp's newspaper and online news operations, including the Times, Wall Street Journal, New York Post and the Australian.In a press release, News Corp confirmed that following instructions from Murdoch and the Murdoch Family Trust, the companies have formed a special committee 'composed of independent and disinterested members of the board' to begin exploring a potential combination.
