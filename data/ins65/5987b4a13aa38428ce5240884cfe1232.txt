Strictly stars to perform to iconic BBC theme tunes
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 10:21:50

Strictly Come Dancing's stars will take on beloved BBC theme tunes this weekend, from The Apprentice to Doctor Who.
The couples will be part of the broadcaster's centenary celebrations, jiving, tangoing and cha cha-ing to the well-known tunes.
It'll be the first time the pieces of music have featured on Strictly – so fans are definitely in for a treat.
The full details of the dances are below!
 
Ellie S and Nikita – Paso Doble to Montagues and Capulets (The Apprentice)
Ellie T and Johannes – Tang
