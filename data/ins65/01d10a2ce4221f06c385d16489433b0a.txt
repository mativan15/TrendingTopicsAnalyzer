Indiewire Television Last year PBS gave audiences the charming Victorian-era mystery series 'Miss Scarlet and the Duke.' Though akin to other PBS and BBC shows like 'Ripper Street,' creator Rachael New's mystery series feels so charming 'because Being Feminist (capitalization intended) isn't what it sets its focus on.' Season 1 concluded with Eliza Scarlet (Kate Phillips) and William 'the Duke' Wellington (Stuart Martin) possibly embarking on a romance — which, for a television show, can be a blessing or a curse.
