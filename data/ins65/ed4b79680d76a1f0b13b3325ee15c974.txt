Scrap putting clocks back in October to save households £400, expert says

news
2022-10-21 15:49:19

Households in the UK could save more than £400 a year on energy bills if putting the clocks back in October is scrapped, an expert has said.Professor Aoife Foley from the School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Queen's University Belfast said the country is in an 'energy war' at the moment.Britobs are preparing themselves for sky-rocketing energy bills this winter after Russia's war in Ukraine caused a spike in oil and gas prices.Professor Foley, who specialises in clean energy research, says that by getting rid of daylight savings, consumers could save hundreds of pounds.
