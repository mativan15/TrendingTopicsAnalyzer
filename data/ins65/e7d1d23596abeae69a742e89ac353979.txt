Variety - TV News Paris-based sales and co-production banner Have A Good One (Hago), has boarded two new series, 'Sex (re) Education' ('Septième Ciel') and 'Caro Nostra,' in the run up to Mipcom, the Cannes TV market.'Sex (re) Education' is a 10-part half-hour comedy series directed by Alice Vial, who won the Cesar Award in 2018 with her short 'The Winkles.'The series, penned by Clémence Azincourt, Clement Marchand and Vial, follows Jacques, an pensioner who has been placed by his daughter in a retirement home.
