Former Arizona insurance agent faces lengthy prison term in fraud scheme
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 12:15:00

Koreasa Maria Williams, a former insurance agent in Tucson, faces more than 11 years in federal prison after pleading guilty to defrauding an elderly client out of more than $1.3 million.

Williams was sentenced to 136 months in prison and was ordered to pay restitution to her victims. Sentencing was handed down Sept. 30, 2022, by U.S. District Judge Jennifer Zipps. Williams had previously pleaded guilty i n May to two counts of wire fraud.

Her sentence was ordered to run concurrent to a prior 51-month sentence for a separate annuity fraud scheme.
