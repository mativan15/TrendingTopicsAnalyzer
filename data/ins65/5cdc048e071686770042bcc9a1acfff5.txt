The board of PriceSmart, Inc. (NASDAQ:PSMT) has announced that it will be increasing its dividend on the 31st of August to US$0.43. Although the dividend is now higher, the yield is only 1.1%, which is below the industry average.

PriceSmart's Dividend Is Well Covered By Earnings

It would be nice for the yield to be higher, but we should also check if higher levels of dividend payment would be sustainable. PriceSmart is quite easily earning enough to cover the dividend, however it is being let down by weak cash flows.
