Published: 11:51 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 12:03 EDT, 3 June 2022 Prince Harry today sported his ceremonial medals as he appeared for the Service of Thanksgiving during the second day of the Queen's Platinum Jubilee celebrations.Arriving at St Paul's Cathedral in London today, the Duke of Sussex, 37, who is currently living in his $14 million mansion in the US having stepped back from royal duty, wore his special Platinum Jubilee medal, which he was awarded by the Queen, proudly pinned to his chest.
