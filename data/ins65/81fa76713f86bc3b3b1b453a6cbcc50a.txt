Scott Olson/Getty Images News Background It has been long recognized that, influenced by a trip to Italy, Howard Schultz had a vision of opening tens of thousands of Italian-style cafes where people could linger over coffee drinks, a 'third place' between home and work. In creating its now massive store footprint, Starbucks (NASDAQ:SBUX) invested in new unit development to ensure the stores had comfortable seating and a welcoming ambiance. There were no drive-thrus, Schultz did not want them because they did not reflect the Italian cafes he sought to emulate.
