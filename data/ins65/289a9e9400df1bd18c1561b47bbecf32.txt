Putting the country first is the only hope Tories have of survival
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 20:25:00

We are, of course, in the middle of an economic crisis. That is what Jeremy Hunt's statement yesterday abolishing almost all of Kwasi Kwarteng's mini-Budget was designed to start tackling.But the crisis is as much political as economic. Tory MPs are well aware that Liz Truss may not only have hurt the economy – she could well have damaged the Conservative Party.If Labour was to win the next election by 25 percent – who is to say that things will necessarily get better for the Tories over the next two years? – then they could well be reduced to around just 50 seats.
