Putin's last stand: Russia warned it will be crushed by UK in revenge for nuclear strike
express.co.uk
world
2022-05-28 10:00:00

Russia stormed into Ukraine on February 24 after more than 100,000 troops spent weeks massing at the border with its neighbour - despite Vladimir Putin lying about their true intentions. A path of destruction has been left throughout Ukraine, with major cities reduced to rubble after heavy military bombardment and reports of mass graves containing thousands of bodies. But Putin's war plan has been declared a failure by many - including many in Russia - who have lamented his troops' inability to overcome a courageous Ukrainian fightback.
