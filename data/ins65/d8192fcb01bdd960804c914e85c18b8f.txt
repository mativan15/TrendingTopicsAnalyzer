There are 3.76L PFRDA subscribers in Northeast India, says fund Chairman
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 13:30:00

Supratim Bandyopadhyay, Chairman Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA) said that there are 3.76 lakh subscribers in Northeast India. Bandyopadhyay who was in Guwahati on Tuesday said that Assets under Management (AUM) in the region which was Rs 17000 Crore has increased to Rs 22,000 Crore. 'Roughly Northeast India account for six percent of the total subscriber's base and five percent of AUM.' 
 Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA) organized a Conference on the Implementation of the National Pension System (NPS) in State Governments and State Autonomous Bodies of North East Region in Guwahati with the objective to oversee efficient and effective implementation of the scheme.
