Kenya Wants to Renegotiate Loans for Chinese-Built Railway

Voice of America 20th October 2022, 05:35 GMT+11Nairobi - Kenya wants to renegotiate a multibillion-dollar loan from China that was used to build a major railway.The country's incoming transport minister, Kipchumba Murkomen, told lawmakers that Kenya is up to date on its payments but cannot continue with the current 20-year schedule.Answering questions from a parliamentary committee, Murkomen said the country will have to ask for an extended period to pay, especially in regard to the loans used to build what is known as the Standard Gauge Railway.
