June 01, 2022 12:47 PM Eastern Daylight Time GREENWICH, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Interactive Brokers Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: IBKR) an automated global electronic broker, today reported its Electronic Brokerage monthly performance metrics for May.

Brokerage highlights for the month included:


2.297 million Daily Average Revenue Trades (DARTs)1, 2% higher than prior year and 4% higher than prior month.


Ending client equity of $314.6 billion, 10% lower than prior year and 3% lower than prior month, primarily due to an omnibus broker that is now using a trust company for custody.
