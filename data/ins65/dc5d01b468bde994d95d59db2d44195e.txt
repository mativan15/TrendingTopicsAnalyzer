2 UP labourers killed in J&K grenade attack; 2 suspects held
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 19:15:00

Two non-local labourers from Uttar Pradesh were killed in a grenade attack in the wee hours of Tuesday in Harmain village of Shopian in southern Kashmir. Police have arrested two suspects allegedly involved in the attack. The two labourers who worked in apple orchards during the harvest season were identified as Ram Sagar and Monish Kumar, both residents of Kanauj of UP. 
Police claimed the person who allegedly threw the grenade, Imran Bashir Ganei, who lives near the metal and wood shack where the incident took place, has been arrested along with his associate.
