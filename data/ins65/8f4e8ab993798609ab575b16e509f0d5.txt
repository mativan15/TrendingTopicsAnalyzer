Canada Post hiring for hundreds of postal clerk jobs this holiday season
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-21 15:13:49

Canada Post is looking to fill hundreds of postal clerk positions over the holiday season in multiple provinces. The Canada Post website has job postings in a number of cities in Ontario, B.C., Saskatchewan, Alberta and Quebec. The positions would last between Nov. 15, 2022, and Jan. 15, 2023.
