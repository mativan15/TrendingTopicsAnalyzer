By ZHANG YUNBI | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-20 14:50 Share - WeChat Over the past years, Xi Jinping, general secretary of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, has advanced head-of-state diplomacy and charted course for major-country diplomacy with Chinese characteristics, a senior Chinese diplomat said.
Xi also offered navigation for the world facing changes and turmoil, Vice-Foreign Minister Ma Zhaoxu made the comments at a news conference on Thursday about China's diplomacy since the 18th National Congress of the Communist Party of China.
