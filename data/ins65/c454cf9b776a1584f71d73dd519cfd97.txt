October 18, 2022 02:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time SACRAMENTO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Inszone Insurance Services: a rapidly growing, national provider of benefits, personal and commercial lines insurance, today announced that Rich Lemon has been appointed Chief Integration Officer (CIO).



Mr. Lemon begun his insurance career in 2005 starting in sales, moving into sales management, and ultimately going into operations. He joined Inszone Insurance in 2010 eventually becoming VP of Operations and has been involved in all aspects of the rapid growth of the agency.
