June 01, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Grandstream, connecting the world since 2002 with award-winning unified communication solutions, today announced the release of the newest addition to their WP series of Cordless Wi-Fi IP Phones. The new WP822 is a business-grade portable Wi-Fi IP phone that offers seamless roaming and advanced features to suit a variety of businesses, enterprises and vertical market applications including retail, logistics, healthcare, education and security.
