'To be or not to be, that is no longer the question,' declared ABBA co-founder and musical mastermind Benny Andersson at the start of 'ABBA Voyage,' the Swedish quartet's first 'concert' in over 40 years. And if that sounds like a curiously existential way to begin a pop concert, well, this is no ordinary live show.For a start, despite Andersson's insistence that 'This is really me, I just look very good for my age,' it's actually his de-aged, computer-generated avatar — or 'ABBA-tar,' if you must — that is speaking his pre-recorded words.
