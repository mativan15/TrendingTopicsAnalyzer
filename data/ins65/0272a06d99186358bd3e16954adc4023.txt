Vivo aims to take total flagship store count to 650 in India by end of 2022
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 11:00:00

NEW DELHI: Vivo on Friday said that it has opened a new flagship store in Indiranagar, Bengaluru and added that it aims to take the total number of exclusive stores to around 650 by the end of 2022.The new 5000-square-foot flagship store has dedicated product experience zones on the ground floor and a premium service centre on the first floor.Vivo said the store will allow customers to experience product features. This new store features a dedicated consumer activity zone where regular Vivo Fan Club activities will be held and the brand will try to connect with buyers effectively.
