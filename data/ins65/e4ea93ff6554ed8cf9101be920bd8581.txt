[Photo/IC] For Michael Borchmann, a former senior official in the German state of Hessen, it's clear that any company wanting to sell its products to the vast Chinese market can't afford to ignore the China International Import Expo, or CIIE. The 5th CIIE will be held in Shanghai from Nov 5-10 at the National Exhibition and Convention Center.
