Diwali celebrations in India and abroad!

Diwali, the Festival of Light, is one of the most-awaited Hindu festivals. In North India, people celebrate Diwali to commemorate the return of Rama and Sita in Ayodhya after 14 years of exile. West Bengal, Diwali is associated with Goddess Durga. People worship the Goddess, who is supposed to be the destroyer of evil forces on Diwali. Diwali is known as Narak Chaturdashi in Goa, and effigies of demon Narkasura are made, filled with crackers and then burnt later.In Fiji, Diwali is a public holiday! The incredible island country celebrates the festival of light with the same passion and enthusiasm as it is celebrated in India.
