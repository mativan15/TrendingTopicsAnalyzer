Nike follows in the footsteps of H&M & Adidas, will not renew franchise agreements in Russia
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 06:15:00

AFPNike's move comes just two days after Starbucks said it was shuttering its 130 cafes in the country.U.S. sportswear maker Nike has not renewed agreements with its largest franchisee in Russia, the Vedomosti daily reported on Wednesday, marking the latest exit by a major U.S. brand since Russian forces entered Ukraine in February. On Monday, Starbucks Corp said it was pulling out of Russia and McDonald's trademark "Golden Arches" were lowered near Moscow, following the sale of its burger chain to a licensee.
