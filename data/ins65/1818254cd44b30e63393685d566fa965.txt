SAN JOSE, Calif., October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SJW Group (NYSE: SJW) today announced that the company will release its third quarter 2022 financial results after the close of the market on Thursday, October 27, 2022. Eric Thornburg, president, chief executive officer and board chair, James P. Lynch, chief accounting officer and Andrew F. Walters, chief financial officer, will review the results in a webcast presentation at 10 a.m. (PT), 1:00 p.m. (ET) on Friday, October 28, 2022.
