By partnering with The Big Table Group, the Blue Light Card community will now be offering a range of discounts for a number of big-name restaurants across the UK, including Bella Italia, Café Rouge and Las Iguanas. With more than 2.4 million members across the country, Blue Light Card offers thousands of discounts and benefits exclusively to its front line cardholders.With more than 14,500 partner retailers within its portfolio, Blue Light Card is the UK's leading specialist discount service for the blue light community, providing exclusive offers across hospitality, retail, travel, electronics and more.
