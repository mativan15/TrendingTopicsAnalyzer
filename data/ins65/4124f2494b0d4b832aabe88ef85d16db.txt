Three quarters of voters in 'Red Wall' seats are in favour of substantial tax hikes for the richest to help to tackle the climate crisis, according to a new poll.

The areas are viewed as a battle ground for votes for the two major parties, having traditionally been Labour seats but which switched to the Tories at the last general election.
