By Reuters Published: 12:30 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 12:30 EDT, 16 October 2022 The Washington Capitals signed forward Sonny Milano to a one-year, $750,000 contract on Sunday. Per the Capitals, Milano will be placed on waivers for the purpose of a loan to the Hershey Bears of the American Hockey League. Milano, 26, recorded career-high totals in assists (20), points (34) and games played (66) last season with the Anaheim Ducks. His 14 goals tied a personal best, set during the 2017-18 season with the Columbus Blue Jackets.
