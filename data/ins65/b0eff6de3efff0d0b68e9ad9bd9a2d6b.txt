4-star DL Monteque Rhames names top schools
usatoday.com
news
2022-06-03 17:51:11

Georgia football is among the top eight schools for elite class of 2023 recruit Monteque Rhames. The four-star defensive lineman plays high school football for Sumter High School in Sumter, South Carolina.
Rhames, the fourth-ranked recruit in South Carolina, considers South Carolina, Coastal Carolina, NC State, Georgia, Florida State, Michigan, UCF, and Virginia Tech to be his top eight schools. The Sumter star is the No. 47 defensive line recruit in the country and the No. 318 overall recruit.
Monteque Rhames is 6 feet, 6 inches and 240 pounds and has received a scholarship offer from Alabama since naming his top eight schools.
