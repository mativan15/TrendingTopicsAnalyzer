The Chief Minister also called Thousand Lights MLA N. Ezhilan and instructed him to ensure that the man was given medical treatment.
Chief Minister M.K. Stalin on Friday stopped his convoy to help a man who had been injured in a road accident near the D.M.S. Metro Station on Anna Salai in Chennai. While he was on his way to the Secretariat, the Chief Minister noticed that Arulraj, of Choolaimedu, had sustained injuries to the head. Mr. Stalin, along with police personnel, helped him into an autorickshaw and sent him to the Government Royapettah Hospital, an official press release said.
