Forcyd Expands its UK Footprint with the hiring of a Partner to oversee its London office
apnews.com
news
2022-06-01 11:40:42

AMSTERDAM, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Forcyd, a Dutch-headquartered international provider of eDiscovery, Cyber Forensics and Document Review Consulting services, has hired James MacGregor to lead their UK practice.'The London eDiscovery and Document Review market is underserved by boutique service providers who can tailor service delivery to their client's needs,' says MacGregor. 'In the wake of vast market consolidation in recent years, less than a handful of specialist eDiscovery providers remain in the UK whose owners are intimately involved in the day-to-day running of the business.
