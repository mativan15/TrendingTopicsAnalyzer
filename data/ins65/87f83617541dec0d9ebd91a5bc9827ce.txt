By XING YI in London | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-15 03:31 Share - WeChat Lujiazui, the financial center in Shanghai. [Photo by Wang Gang/For China Daily] With the 5th China International Import Expo set to be held in Shanghai in less than a month, around 50 business representatives gathered in London on Thursday to learn about investment opportunities in the East China's biggest metropolis. Xue Feng, president of Invest Shanghai, the foreign investment development agency of the city, spoke to the audience via video link, giving insight on a wide range of business opportunities on offer.
