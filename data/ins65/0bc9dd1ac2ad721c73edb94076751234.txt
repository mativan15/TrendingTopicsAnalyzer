Here's How Much Your Social-Media Account Costs on the Dark Web
pcmag.com
news
2022-10-21 09:00:00

The dark web predominantly peddles stolen identities and credit cards, but it's also becoming increasingly common to find hacked social-media accounts available for sale on darknet marketplaces. How much does it cost? Far less than you would think.

According to data extracted by Whizcase(Opens in a new window) between January and September 2022, the cheapest accounts belong to Reddit, which go for as low as $6. TikTok, Pinterest, and Twitter accounts all cost $10 or less, while Snapchat, Instagram, Discord, and Facebook are about $15 a pop.
