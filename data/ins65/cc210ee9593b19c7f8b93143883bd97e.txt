Delhi govt launches 'tactical urbanism' road safety trial at Signature Bridge
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 08:34:26

The Delhi government has launched a 'tactical urbanism trial' for road safety at the Signature Bridge with certain changes in road markings, additional speed calming measures etc, which will be made permanent if proved successful in reducing the number of accidents, said officials.The new measures also include better signages, narrowing of merging and diverging angles and approach lanes for the reduction of conflict points at the site, installation of cones, removal of vendors etc. to check cases of accidents.
