RICHMOND, VA, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kevin S. Elliker, an experienced appellate, complex litigation and government investigations attorney, has returned to Hunton Andrews Kurth LLP as a counsel in the firm's Richmond office.

Elliker, who was an associate with the firm from 2016 to 2019, most recently served as Investigative Counsel to the U.S. House of Representatives Select Committee to Investigate the January 6th Attack on the United States Capitol.
