HV Bancorp Stock Climbs on Merger Deal With Citizens Financial
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 14:22:00

By Sabela Ojea Shares of HV Bancorp Inc. on Thursday rose after the holding company for Huntingdon Valley Bank announced a merger deal with Citizens Financial Services Inc.'s First Citizens Community Bank. At 10:16 EDT, the shares were up 29%, at $25.75 a share. Under the terms of the definitive agreement, HV Bancorp's shareholders would be entitled to receive for each share either $30.50 in cash or 400 shares of Citizens Financial's common stock, or around $67.4 million in the aggregate. The deal, which has been approved by the board of directors of both companies and is expected to complete in the first half of 2023, is aimed at expanding Citizens Financial's presence in Pennsylvania, Delaware and New Jersey, the company said.
