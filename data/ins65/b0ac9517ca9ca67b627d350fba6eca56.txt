Ahammed MP, Chairman, Malabar Gold & Diamonds New Delhi: M. P. Ahammed, Chairman, Malabar Group expects strong growth in consumer demand this festive and wedding season. He also plans to invest Rs 750 crore in company's upcoming operational manufacturing unit in Telangana.In conversation with ETRetail, Ahammed shares his insights on jewellery market and company's plans for this festive season. Edited Excerpts:What has been the experience in the last two festive seasons in 2020 and 2021 (Aug/Sep - Nov/Dec)? How do you foresee consumer sentiment and spending in this festive season?The overall buying power and consumer sentiment took a hit due to pandemic waves during the festive seasons in 2020 and 2021.
