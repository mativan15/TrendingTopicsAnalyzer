7 Army soldiers die in Ladakh after vehicle falls in river
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 12:00:00

AgenciesRepresentative imageSeven Indian Army soldiers were reported dead after a bus carrying 26 army personnel skidded off the road (to a depth of approx 50-60ft), and fell in Shyok river in Ladakh. All 26 individuals have been evacuated and taken to 403 Field Hospital at Partapur. Surgical teams from Leh have moved to Partapur to provide the necessary care. Dedicated efforts are underway to ensure that the best medical care is provided to the injured, including requisition of air effort from the Air Force to shift the more serious casualties to Western Command.
