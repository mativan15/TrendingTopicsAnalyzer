NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- SL Green Realty Corp. ("SL Green") and Caesars Entertainment ("Caesars") announced a partnership to redevelop 1515 Broadway as a premiere entertainment and gaming destination in the heart of Times Square, one of the most visited destinations in the world. Caesars Palace Times Square will not only provide billions in tax revenue to New York City and State, it will also accelerate New York's economic recovery through strategic partnerships with area businesses utilizing its industry-leading loyalty program Caesars Rewards, home to more than 65 million members.
