June 01, 2022 04:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time SEATTLE--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Impinj, Inc. (NASDAQ: PI), a leading RAIN RFID provider and Internet of Things pioneer, today announced that Cary Baker, Impinj CFO, will participate in a fireside chat at the Baird 2022 Global Consumer, Technology and Services Conference on Tuesday, June 7, 2022, at 4:20 p.m. ET / 1:20 p.m. PT.

A live audio webcast and replay of the presentation will be available on the Impinj website at investor.impinj.com.

About Impinj

Impinj (NASDAQ: PI) helps businesses and people analyze, optimize, and innovate by wirelessly connecting billions of everyday things — such as apparel, automobile parts, luggage, and shipments — to the Internet.
