Published: 03:18 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 07:10 EDT, 3 June 2022 Tesla Chief Executive Elon Musk told top managers he had a 'super bad feeling' about the economy and that the electric carmaker needed to cut staff by about 10 per cent, according to an internal email.The email, titled 'pause all hiring worldwide', was sent to Tesla executives on Thursday, and underscored an increasingly gloomy economic outlook for the globe, as prices soar and war in Ukraine passes its 100th day.Musk earlier this week asked Tesla employees to return to the office or leave the company.
