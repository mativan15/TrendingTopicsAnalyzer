Getty ImagesLive-streaming esports tournaments attract viewers in much the same way as soccer, cricket, and tennis matches.India represents just 1% of the global gaming market currently, lagging far behind the United States and China. But rapid growth within the sector is shifting the balance. The Indian esports market alone is expected to hit the Rs 11-billion mark by 2025. 
Growth path
One could say that esports is in the right place at the right time. Cheap data services, high-speed 4G connectivity, and the availability of affordable smartphones are nudging the segment onto the next stage.
