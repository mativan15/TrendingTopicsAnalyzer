Many Seacoast towns have set their hours for trick-or-treating for this Halloween.

Here is a roundup of trick-or-treat dates and times for cities and towns around the Seacoast New Hampshire and southern York County, Maine. If you'd like to add your town, email news@seacoastonline.com.

Trick-or-treaters should use flashlights, walk on sidewalks where available, travel in familiar, well-lighted areas, have parents check candy, and only go to homes with outdoor lights on, according to safety experts.
