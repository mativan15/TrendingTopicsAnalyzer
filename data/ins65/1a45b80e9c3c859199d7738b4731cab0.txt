Published: 12:54 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 12:54 EDT, 18 October 2022 By Allison Lampert ORLANDO, Fla., Oct 18 (Reuters) - Executives who sell or use business jets expressed concern that a French proposal to tax corporate aircraft over greenhouse gas emissions is making their industry a "scapegoat," following protests by climate activists in Europe. At the world's largest business jet show which opened in Orlando, Florida on Tuesday, industry executives noted that corporate aircraft only generate a fraction of aviation's total emissions.
