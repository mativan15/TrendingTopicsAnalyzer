Restaurants Are Adding Extra Charges to Combat Inflation. Why That's a Recipe for Disaster
inc.com
business
2022-06-03 00:48:00

As inflation continues to break 40-year records month after month, you may be tempted to pass on your cost increases to customers in the form of fees. Tread lightly.While tacking an upcharge to your customers' bills can keep you in the black, doing so can also rub some people the wrong way--particularly if those fees are unexpected.The fees can show up as just about anything, from "noncash adjustments," "fuel surcharges," or something as vague as "kitchen appreciation" and are a way for the businesses to combat increased food prices without raising menu prices, as The Wall Street Journal reports.
