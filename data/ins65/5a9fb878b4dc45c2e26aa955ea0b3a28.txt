Ovo revives interest in buying nationalised energy supplier Bulb
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-20 15:45:22

The gas and electricity supplier Ovo has revived its interest in snapping up nationalised rival Bulb, potentially gazumping a bid by Octopus Energy.Ovo has written to Bulb's special administrator to inform it that it is again considering a purchase. Ovo originally submitted an offer to buy Bulb shortly after it collapsed in November last year, but later pulled out of the auction, during which several bidders have fallen away.Discussions between government officials and Octopus have dragged on for months.
