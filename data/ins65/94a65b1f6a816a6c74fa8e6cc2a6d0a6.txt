Published: 12:11 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:14 EDT, 21 October 2022 Far-right politician Giorgia Meloni has been elected as Italy's leader after officially asking the President for the right to form a government. Meloni and her post-fascist Brothers of Italy party emerged the winners of last month's election, but short of an overall majority.She has now formed Italy's most right-wing government since Benito Mussolini after negotiating a coalition with allies including Silvio Berlusconi's Forza Italia.
