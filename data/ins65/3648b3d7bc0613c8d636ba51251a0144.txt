BANGALORE, India, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- As previously announced last week, Zoomcar, Inc. ('Zoomcar'), the world's largest emerging market focused car sharing platform, and Innovative International Acquisition Corp. ("Innovative") (NASDAQ: IOAC), a publicly traded special purpose acquisition company, entered into a definitive merger agreement (the 'Merger Agreement') that will result in Zoomcar becoming a publicly listed company. The transaction values the combined company (the 'Combined Company') at an implied pro forma enterprise value of approximately $456 million.
