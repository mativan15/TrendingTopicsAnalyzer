Nykaa shares hit record low, inch closer to IPO issue price. Will it go the Paytm way?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 10:15:00

Regarded by many investors as the only "white sheep" among all recently-listed new-age stocks, shares of have lost over 55% of their value and are now inching closer to its IPO issue price of Rs 1,125. After a star-studded listing in the presence of Bollywood diva Katrina Kaif on November 10 last year, the stock of the beauty startup nearly doubled to Rs 2,248.10 on listing day. On Tuesday, the stock hit a fresh 52-week low of Rs 1,143.75, down over 55% from its record high price of Rs 2,574. In the pack of new-age stocks, shares have been among the worst performers as it is down around 55% from its 52-week high.
