Vitality Bowls to Open at Arsenal Yards in Watertown
blogspot.com
news
2022-05-31 15:11:00

A chain of "superfood" cafes is getting ready to open within a growing development by the Charles River.According to a press release, Vitality Bowls is planning to open at Arsenal Yards in Watertown on June 10, with the outlet being the first in Massachusetts for the California-based business. Once it opens, expect to see açaí bowls that are topped with a variety of superfoods along with salads, toasts, wraps, smoothies, and more.The press release mentions that there are currently more than 140 locations of Vitality Bowls either open or in development across the country.
