Albanese and his new government should examine some low-hanging fruit that could improve relations with our near neighbour and ally that won't cost the earth.Simplifying and streamlining the visa process for Indonesians to visit Australia would be a good start and makes good sense. Australians enjoy visa-free entry as tourists to Indonesia but we don't offer the same in return.LoadingIf universities are to diversify away from China, making it easier for would-be students to come from Indonesia is a no brainer, while simplifying things for business people looking to travel to Australia to more easily to capitalise on the free trade deal is also an obvious move.
