October 16, 2022 13:41 IST Prime Minister Narendra Modi addresses during the dedication of 75 digital banking units across 75 district through video conference, on October 16, 2022. | Photo Credit: Twitter/@narendramodi Prime Minister Narendra Modi on October 16, 2022 attributed India's sustained economic growth to the efforts of the BJP government to replace pre-2014 'phone banking' with 'digital banking'.
Referring to the previous UPA regime, he said under 'phone banking', instructions were given on phone to banks regarding whom they had to lend to and at what terms and conditions.
