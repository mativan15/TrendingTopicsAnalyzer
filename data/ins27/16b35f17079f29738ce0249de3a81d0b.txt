TORONTO, ON and NEW YORK, NY / ACCESSWIRE / June 1, 2022 / Sekur Private Data Ltd. (OTCQX:SWISF) (CSE:SKUR) (FRA:GDT0) ("Sekur" or the "Company"), the leader in Swiss hosted secure communications and secure data management, is pleased announce that, as part of its US mass marketing strategy to bring awareness to Sekur Private Data's Sekur solution, it has signed a contract with The Epoch Times to become the exclusive sponsor of Roman Balmakov's episodes on EpochTV for all of his shows every Wednesday and every Friday for the remainder of 2022 and all of 2023.
