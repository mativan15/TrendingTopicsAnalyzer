AgenciesApple iPad 10 is the improved version of the A13 Bionic chip-powered iPad 9. The newest iPad, which replaces the Lighting Adapter, has an A14 Bionic CPU and a USB-C charging connector. On October 18, 2022, Apple formally announced the iPad 10 (2022), which is now available for pre-order on the Apple website. The devices will start to arrive in the UK on October 26. Apple has set the prices at £499 for the 64GB model and £679 for the 256GB model. Users who want the cellular variant will pay £679 for the 64GB model and £859 for the 256GB device.
