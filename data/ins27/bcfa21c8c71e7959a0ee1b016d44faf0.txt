By Reuters Published: 08:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 08:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 Oct 20 (Reuters) - AS Roma host seemingly invincible Serie A leaders Napoli on Sunday and the odds seem to be stacked against Jose Mourinho's side as they look to end their opponents' unbeaten league run since the start of the season. Napoli, first in the table with 26 points after 10 games, are on a 10-game winning streak in all competitions. Roma on the other hand are fourth with 22 points and have won just one of their last eight league matches against the table toppers.
