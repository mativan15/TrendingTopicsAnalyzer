23 Gadsden area players named to 2022 All-State high school softball teams
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-05 10:01:13

In this article:

Twenty-three Gadsden-area softball players were named to All-State teams in their respective classifications by the Alabama Sports Writers Association, with six earning spots on the first team.

Fort Payne's AJ Kramer; Boaz's Emmorie Buke; Geraldine's Lydia West; Spring Garden's Neely Welsh; Sand Rock's Jayden Foster; and Cedar Bluff's Jordyn Walker are first-team selections.

Second team from the Gadsden-area include: Southside's Macey Herren, Etowah's Abbi Martin, Hokes Bluff's Abby Payne and Kileigh Blackwell; Glencoe's Abi Bunt and Kaylee Higdon; Piedmont's Savannah Smith; Spring Garden's Abbey Stewart; Sand Rock's EllaGrace Hood; and Cedar Bluff's Mattie Payne, Natalie Bray and Reece Patterson.
