Tencent Music Entertainment Group to Report Third Quarter 2022 Financial Results on November 15, 2022 Eastern Time

2022-10-17 10:00:00

SHENZHEN, China, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Tencent Music Entertainment Group ("TME", or the "Company") (NYSE: TME and HKEX: 1698), the leading online music and audio entertainment platform in China, today announced that it will report its unaudited financial results for the third quarter of 2022 before the U.S. market opens on Tuesday, November 15, 2022.
TME's management will hold a conference call on Tuesday, November 15, 2022, at 6:00 A.M. Eastern Time or 7:00 P.M. Beijing Time on Tuesday, November 15, 2022, to discuss the financial results.
