Pittsburgh snack manufacturer TruFood acquired by UAE-based firm
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 21:49:00

TruFood Manufacturing, a contract snack food manufacturer that is headquartered in O'Hara Township, is being acquired.

The company has been purchased by Mubadala Capital, a subsidiary of Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates-based Mubadala Investment Co. Financial terms of the deal were not disclosed, but the purchase was made with funds "affiliated with AUA Private Equity Partners," a firm based in West Palm Beach, Florida.

TruFood is a manufacturer of products that include nutrition bars and chocolate-molded products for a variety of packaged goods companies and retailers.
