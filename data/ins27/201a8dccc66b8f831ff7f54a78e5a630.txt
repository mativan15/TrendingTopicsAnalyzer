KT&G foundation to hold social innovation contest

A promotional poster for the "social innovation idea contest," held by KT&G Scholarship Foundation (KT&G)The KT&G Scholarship Foundation said Monday it is hosting a social innovation idea contest for college students to showcase creative business ideas that can tackle social challenges.Students can participate in groups of two to four, and may submit business proposals that create products or services to combat a wide range of social issues including climate change and education.Proposals are being received through the foundation's website until Nov.
