Man arrested for chit fund fraud
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-17 16:10:00

The Central Crime Branch of Avadi Police Commissionerate on Monday arrested a 31-year-old man for allegedly cheating many to the tune of ₹61 lakh after receiving money for Deepavali chit fund. According to the police, S. Alamelu, 46, of Maulivakkam, lodged a complaint stating that in 2021, she joined the scheme run by Karthi alias Karthikeyan, 31. She introduced her relatives to the scheme. But Karthikeyan cheated them of ₹61,19,000. She alleged that more than 30 such persons were cheated by him.
