Kristen Snedeker, Founder of Lonestar Homes & Land Our newest brand, Lonestar Homes & Land, brings our unique brand of client service and legacy building expertise to the Austin real estate market AUSTIN, Texas (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 Lonestar Homes & Land today announced its partnership with Side, the only real estate technology company that exclusively partners with high-performing agents, teams, and independent brokerages to transform them into market-leading boutique brands and businesses. The alliance will ensure that Lonestar Homes & Land, a company that specializes in investment properties, land, and commercial real estate as well as traditional home purchases and sales, is powered by the industry's most advanced platform.
