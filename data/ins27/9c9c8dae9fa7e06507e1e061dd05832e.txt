Ashwani Bhatia takes charge as Sebi whole-time member
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 03:00:00

Ashwani Bhatia , a former banker, has taken charge as a whole-time member (WTM) of the Securities and Exchange Board India ( Sebi ).Bhatia, who was earlier a managing director at the, will be in charge of departments including the alternative investment fund and foreign portfolio investors, corporate finance and investigation, among others, said a release on Wednesday.He will also be heading the market intermediaries regulation and supervision department, which regulates the brokerage firms.Bhatia will be the third whole-time member on the Sebi board along with SK Mohanty and Ananta Barua .
