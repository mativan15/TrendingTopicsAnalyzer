June 01, 2022 12:42 PM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hack/Reduce, a non-profit focused on creating talent and technologies for Boston's big data-driven economy, today announced tickets are now on sale for the Tech Tackles Cancer (TTC) charity event being held on June 21, 2022, at the Sinclair in Cambridge from 6-10 pm ET. The fundraiser has raised over $200K with three weeks to go.



TTC is harnessing the power of the Boston technology community to raise awareness and funds supporting pediatric cancer causes.
