Pakistan Likely To Exit Anti-Terror "FATF Grey List" After 4 Years
ndtv.com
world
2022-10-15 05:45:00

Pakistan has been on the grey list of the FATF since June 2018.Four years after it was placed on the "grey list" of the Financial Action Task Force (FATF) for financing of terrorism and money laundering, Pakistan is finally set to see its name off the list after it was found to be compliant in the action plan set out by the global financial watchdog.The decision to strike off the South Asian nation from the list is likely to be taken when the financial crime watchdog meets in Paris from October 18 to 21.
