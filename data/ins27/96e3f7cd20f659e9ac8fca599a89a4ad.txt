‘Why I'm ditching the UK this winter? It's cheaper abroad'
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 16:00:00

As Britain prepares to batten down the hatches in preparation for a winter of discontent amid rising rents, rocketing energy bills and soaring interest rates, a growing cohort are migrating to sunnier climes.So-called digital nomads, who are able to work fully remotely, are ditching their rental properties or subletting their flats and using their flexibility to travel to warmer spots over the colder months — all while saving money.Sound good? There are some things to consider. Remote workers must navigate new Brexit rules that state that you can't spend more than 90 days in any 180-day period in the EU, for example.
