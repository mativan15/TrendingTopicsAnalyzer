TORONTO, May 26, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Fountain Asset Corp. (TSXV:FA) ('Fountain' or the 'Company') would like to announce its financial results for the three months ended March 31, 2022 ('Q1/22'). Highlights from Q1/22: Adjusted net asset value ('ANAV') of $22.42 million ($0.36 per share) at March 31, 2022 compared to $24.44 million ($0.40 per share) at December 31, 2021, representing a 8.2% decrease quarter over quarter on an ANAV basis and a 10.0% decrease on a per share basis. ANAV reflects the net asset value plus the amount of available tax loss pools available;Net comprehensive loss of $2.
