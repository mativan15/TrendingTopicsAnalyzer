DENYS KARLOVSKY — SUNDAY, MAY 29, 2022, 16:59

Russian troops in the occupied Vasylivka periodically block the movement of cars trying to reach Ukrainian-controlled territory.

Source: Oleksander Starukh, Chairman of the Zaporizhia regional military administration, 24/7 national joint newscast – Local Edition "061"

Starukh's quote: "They periodically close the exit through the only corridor, which is on the Kharkiv-Simferopol highway, through the occupied Vasylivka. This happens frequently. Large convoys of 300-400 vehicles are stopped for 4-5 days.
