Avoid unidimensional view of subsidies: Sitharaman to World Bank
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 18:17:18

Washington: India has told the World Bank that it must avoid a 'unidimensional' view of subsidies, asked it to distinguish between 'distortive subsidies' and 'targeted support' to vulnerable households, and said that India's supportive policies have helped the country move towards meeting the sustainable development goals.Speaking at the development committee meeting of the bank on Friday, finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman said that India agreed that making public expenditure efficient must be a priority for all countries and acknowledged the bank's appreciation of India's digitalisation efforts.
