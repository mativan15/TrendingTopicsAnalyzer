Image caption, Residents such as Gareth Cemlyn Jones are helping to generate their own powerEnergy bills are a worry right now, but people in one village are slashing their bills by using green technology. Some residents in the Ogwen Valley, in Gwynedd, joined an energy club which uses a hydro-electric scheme.As a result, they get electricity for 8p a unit at certain times of day - instead of 34p a unit. A separate scheme sells excess power back to the grid, funding grants for local charities and fitting solar panels in the area.
