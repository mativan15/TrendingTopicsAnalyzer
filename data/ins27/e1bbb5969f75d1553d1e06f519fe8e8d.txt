The last few years have brought major upheaval to the business world, impacting employers and employees alike. The strains of the COVID-19 pandemic, economic shifts and global conflicts have caused mental health concerns on a broad scale at home, with those concerns leaking into the workplace. In turn, poor mental health costs the global economy $1 trillion each year in lost productivity, according to the World Health Organization.
