"We're effin' doing this."

That was Jeff Ruby's response after his first visit to the Foundry, Cincinnati Center City Development Corp.'s (3CDC) $51 million overhaul of the former Fountain Place Mall, which had housed a downtown Macy's department store, among other things.

Jeff Ruby's Steakhouse opened in the Foundry on Oct. 8, but that wasn't always the plan. His daughter Britney Ruby Miller, CEO of Jeff Ruby Culinary Entertainment, is our guest on this week's "Above the Fold," a podcast by the Cincinnati Business Courier.
