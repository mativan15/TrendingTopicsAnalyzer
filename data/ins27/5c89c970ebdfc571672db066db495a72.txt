IBM says its technology is helping businesses navigate a wobbly global economy, as stock rallies
msn.com
news
2022-10-20 11:38:00

Shares of International Business Machines Corp. rallied in extended trading Wednesday, after the tech software, consulting and infrastructure giant reported third-quarter results that beat expectations and offered up a more upbeat full-year sales forecast.During IBM's earnings call, Chief Executive Arvind Krishna said customers were turning to its technology and the guidance of its consultants to help steer through rising prices, supply-chain snarls and demographic shifts. 'When I talk with clients it's clear there's a real opportunity to help businesses leverage technology in today's environment,' Krishna said during the call.
