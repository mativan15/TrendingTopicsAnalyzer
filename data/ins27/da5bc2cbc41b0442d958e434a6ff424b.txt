As snow falls across Victoria's alpine regions, businesses that haven't had a good season in two years are finally seeing a glimmer of hope. Good snowfalls have been recorded in recent days, marking a strong start to the 2022 seasonA sled-dog business is busy training dogs ahead of the official season start on June 11It's hoped a strong season will help get businesses back on track after a difficult two yearsIt's been a tough time for many operators, including Jake Greaves, who runs Howling Husky Sled Dog Tours at Mt Hotham, Dinner Plain and Mt Baw Baw.
