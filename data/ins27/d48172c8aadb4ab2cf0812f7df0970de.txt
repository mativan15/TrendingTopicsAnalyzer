India, the one of the most promising economies of the world has achieved satiating numbers in the field of development of transportation facilities especially road construction. Over the last two decades, the nation has focused on the development of infrastructure to pacify road transport. The Golden-Quadrilateral project, envisioned by former prime minister, Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee, gave a kickstart to the construction of highways. On the other hand, Bharatmala project pacified the daunting task of developing an  interstate highways system, similar to that of developed countries.
