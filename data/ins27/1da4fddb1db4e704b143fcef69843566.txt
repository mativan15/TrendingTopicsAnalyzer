Bill Catania, founder and CEO of Orlando-based OneRail, was chosen as one of Orlando Business Journal 's 2022 C-Suite Awards honorees in the CEOs category. Read more about the honorees in OBJ 's Oct. 21-26, 2022, weekly edition.

On Demand Technologies Inc., which does business as OneRail, is a last-mile transportation visibility platform providing shippers with Amazon-level dependability and speed through a real-time connected network of 10 million drivers.

Here, Catania shares more:

What was your proudest business accomplishment in the last 12 months? Expanding our services to 330-plus cities across North America.
