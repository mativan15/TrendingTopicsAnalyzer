Salem Goes Live To Connect Residents Directly To City Services

"I'm grateful to the city staff who have worked diligently over the past several months to get this new program into place and I hope residents will make use of this new option for connecting with their city government." - Salem Mayor Kim Driscoll (Dave Copeland/Patch)SALEM, MA — Help with Salem municipal services is now just a phone call away — at one number, with a real, live operator — during regular business hours.Salem on Wednesday launched Salem 311, which connects anyone in the city to a live operator during business hours by dialing 3-1-1.
