NORFOLK, Va., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- While measures of consumer sentiment have been declining recently, optimism about starting a business remains solid. Despite war headlines, stock market declines and inflation woes, 78% of aspiring business owners surveyed in May were resolute about starting their businesses within the coming six months.
