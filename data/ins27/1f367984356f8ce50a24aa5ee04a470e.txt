Published: 19:21 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 05:14 EDT, 28 May 2022 A Tory MP's son who is fighting in Ukraine helped a fellow British volunteer soldier escape Russian fire after his leg was blown up by a mine.Former Royal Marine Ben Grant, 30, dragged his comrade to safety as mortars and artillery were fired towards them in woodland north of Kharkiv earlier in May, with the rescue filmed on a helmet-mounted camera.The former Royal Marine, who is the son of Conservative MP Helen Grant, said he was 'terrified' but focused on getting former Grenadier Guardsman Dean Arthur out of danger.
