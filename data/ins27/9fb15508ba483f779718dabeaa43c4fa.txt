14 BSE500 stocks rally up to 30% during the week; PSBs lead the party
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-22 05:45:00

New Delhi: Unperturbed by high volatility in global markets, domestic benchmark indices ended the week sharply higher, proving their mettle during the festive season. Benchmark indices - BSE Sensex and Nifty50 - index rallied over 2%, whereas midcap and smallcap indices gained a% each. The fear gauge index -- India VIX tanked more than 5% to below 18 levels. On the sectoral front, PSU Banks stole the show with a whopping 11% rally during the week. Energy, IT and FMCG indices gained 2-3% each. On the contrary, BSE metal and media indices settled in the red.
