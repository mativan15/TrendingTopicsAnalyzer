Varo Bank may run out of funds by year's end—and additional funding may not be an option
Insider
business
2022-06-02 14:18:15

The news: Varo Bank risks running out of funds before the end of the year, according to a regulatory filing, per Banking Dive. Insider Intelligence Funding and burn rate: Varo was the first US neobank to obtain a banking charter from the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC).It raised $510 million last year and was valued at $2.5 billion.But according to the regulatory filing, by the end of March this year, Varo had $263 million in equity and a burn rate of $84 million in the Q1 2022. At that rate, Banking Dive estimated the bank could run out of money by the end of the year.
