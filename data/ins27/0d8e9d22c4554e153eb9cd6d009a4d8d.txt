Over-the-counter hearing aids are only for adults with mild to moderate hearing loss. To ensure safety, the FDA rule has limited the peak sound output of over-the-counter hearing aids.A good rule of thumb is that if you have the technical expertise to figure out an update to your phone's software settings, then you are tech savvy enough to manage a self-fitting hearing aid, according to Nicholas Reed, an audiologist and assistant professor in epidemiology at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health in Baltimore.
