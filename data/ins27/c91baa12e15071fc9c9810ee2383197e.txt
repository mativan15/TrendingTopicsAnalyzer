Cryptocurrency Scams Cost Consumers Over $1 Billion Since 2021, Says FTC
pcmag.com
news
2022-06-03 22:45:00

The amount of money flowing into cryptocurrency scams continues to skyrocket. The US Federal Trade Commission today said it estimates(Opens in a new window) consumers lost over $1 billion to the scams between January 2021 and March 2022.The estimate is based on fraud reports the FTC received from 46,000 victims. The numbers are a huge increase from the six-month period between October 2020 and March 2021, when the agency logged(Opens in a new window) $80 million in cryptocurrency-related losses from 7,000 victims.
