UCO Bank opens special rupee account with Russia's Gazprombank
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 18:30:00

Experts say UCO, which has little exposure to the US market, is the suitable candidate for bilateral trade payments between India and Russia.Russia's Gazprombank opened a special rupee account with state-owned UCO Bank, paving the way for bilateral trades between Moscow and New Delhi, sources told ET. The move would help increase imports of cheaper oil from Russia. Earlier, the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) came out with a bespoke special rupee account for bilateral trades between rupee and any other currencies besides the US dollar.
