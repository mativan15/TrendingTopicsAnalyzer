(CNN)A week ago, Coco Gauff celebrated her high school graduation under the Eiffel Tower. Now, the 18-year-old will compete in her first-ever grand slam singles semifinal following a 7-5 6-2 victory against compatriot Sloane Stephens in an all-American quarterfinal at the French Open.Gauff's extraordinary talent has been evident since she became the youngest player in history to reach the main draw at Wimbledon three years ago -- defeating Venus Williams and reaching the fourth round.In the years since, she has grappled with the pressure that arose from that feat.
