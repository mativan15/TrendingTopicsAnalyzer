Vancouver's mayor-elect Ken Sim set to provide update on transition and policies
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-17 18:02:38

Vancouver's mayor-elect Ken Sim is expected to provide an update Monday morning on the mayoral transition and the plans for the incoming administration.
Sim beat out incumbent mayor Kennedy Stewart for the city's top job in the election Saturday.Sim is expected to outline and address some of the key promises made during his campaign.Describing his election as the 'longest job interview ever,' Sim told cheering supporters they would see a difference in city hall.'ABC Vancouver was born out of a desire for change, a desire to do politics differently,' he said.
