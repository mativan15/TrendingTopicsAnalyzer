Alexander Sanchez/iStock via Getty Images In response to the energy and cost of living crisis in the UK, the Government lifted the moratorium on fracking in England during September 2022, but the moratorium remains in Scotland and Wales. Domestic gas production has been in decline since 2000 with the UK becoming a net importer of gas in 2004 (see Figure 1). The government hopes shale gas can accelerate domestic energy supply with the aim of the UK becoming a net energy exporter by 2040. The government believes that shale gas has the potential to reduce dependency on imports, improve energy security, deliver tax revenues, and benefit communities through job creation.
