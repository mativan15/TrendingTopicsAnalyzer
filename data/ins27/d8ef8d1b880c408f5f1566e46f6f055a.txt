A Disney fan has been slammed for paying over £4,000 for Mickey Mouse to appear at her wedding - as she used her catering budget to fund it, so guests went hungryThe bride spent £4,000 on booking Mickey and Minnie Mouse (stock photo) A bride who had her "dream" Disney wedding earlier this year has been slammed by people online after she admitted to paying over £4,000 to have Mickey and Minnie Mouse make guest appearances at the ceremony - because she used her catering budget to pay for it. The woman, 28, said on Reddit that she thought her wedding was "perfect", but she began to suspect her guests didn't feel the same way when her aunt posted on Facebook about how "disappointed" she was with the event.
