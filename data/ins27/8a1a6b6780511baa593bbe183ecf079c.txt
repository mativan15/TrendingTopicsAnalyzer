ZK international receives nasdaq notice regarding minimum bid requirements (NASDAQ:ZKIN)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 21:43:38

ZK International Group Co (NASDAQ:ZKIN) has announced that the Company received a notice from the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC dated Oct. 13, 2022, The bid price of the Company's ordinary shares fail to maintain a minimum bid price of US$1.00 per share for the last 30 consecutive business days from August 31, 2022 to October 12, 2022. The Company's ordinary share will continue to be listed and trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market during this period, unaffected by the receipt of the written notice from Nasdaq.
