Pet care brand Heads Up For Tails to treble network; will open 150 experience centers by 2024
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 09:15:00

AgenciesThe company said it serves more than 19,000 pin codes across India and in the next two years, will add 5000 more pin codes.Pet care brand Heads Up For Tails said it will open 150 experience centers by 2024, trebling the store count by entering newer markets. At present, it has a current footprint of over 50 experience centers across 12 cities. Apart from existing markets, it will also focus on satellite cities with a high pet population owing to increased demand from pet parents and lack of availability of pet products and services.
