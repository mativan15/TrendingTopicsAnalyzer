All You Need To Know About The Upcoming PUBG Nations Cup 2022

The PUBG Nations Cup (PNC) 2022 is a four-day event taking place in Bangkok, Thailand. It serves as the final stretch to decide the ultimate squad from around the world. Fans all over the globe will be excited to hear the return of PNC as it took a two-year hiatus likely due to the Covid-19 pandemic. Schedule
PNC 2022 will be held at the TRUE ICON Hall in ICONSIAM Mall, Bangkok, Thailand, from June 16 to June 19. Each match will begin at 1 PM CEST each day. This is the battle royale's first live event since the PUBG GLOBAL Championship in 2019.
