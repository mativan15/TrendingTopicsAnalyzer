Variety - Film News Talent agent Brandon Liebman has parted ways with longtime employer WME, multiple sources tell Variety.A partner at the power agency since 2014, Liebman's exit was announced in a brief companywide memo circulated on Monday. WME declined to comment on the matter. Liebman did not immediately respond to a request for comment.Liebman's contract was recently up for renegotiation, one source familiar with his deal said, and neither party could reach terms on a new agreement.
