REUTERS/Tingshu WangRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comSHANGHAI, May 28 (Reuters) - Mainland China reported 362 new coronavirus cases on May 27, of which 96 were symptomatic and 266 were asymptomatic, the National Health Commission said on Saturday.That compares with 444 new cases a day earlier - 102 symptomatic and 342 asymptomatic infections, which China counts separately.There were no new deaths, leaving the death toll at 5,226.
