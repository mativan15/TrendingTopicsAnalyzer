U.S. factory orders rise modest 0.3% in May
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 14:13:00

U.S. factory orders rose a modest 0.3% in April after a much larger gain in the prior month, perhaps a sign of some slight softening in the manufacturing sector. Economists polled by The Wall Street Journal had forecast a 0.6% gain. American factories have been very busy over the past year because of strong demand for machines, electronics, cars, and other big-ticket items designed to last at least three years.
