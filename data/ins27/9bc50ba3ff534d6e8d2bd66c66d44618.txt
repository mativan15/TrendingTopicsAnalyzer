Getty ImagesMalayali actor and producer Joju George appeared before Regional Transport Officer (RTO), Idukki, along with Actor Binu Pappu. They both had received show cause notices from the Motor Vehicles Department (MVD) for the off-road race. The show cause notice was issued on May 10, 2022, that demanded the actor-producer to report to officials at Idukki RTO. He was also asked to produce his driving licence along with the vehicle papers to the MVD officials that he drove in the off-road race that took place on May 7 in Vagamon in Idukki.
