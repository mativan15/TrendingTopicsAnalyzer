PTIKolkata: Preparation underway at Eden Garden Cricket Stadium for IPL 2022 playoff match, in Kolkata.(PTI Photo/Swapan Mahapatra)(Rajasthan Royals sit atop the Orange and Purple Cap tables after a convincing seven-wicket win against Royal Challengers Bangalore in the second qualifier, which sent them to their first final since the season began. Jos Buttler's outstanding brilliance in the first part of the league stage tailed down in the second half, but he seemed to bury those ghosts as he bludgeoned Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) into oblivion and led Rajasthan Royals to their first final since 2008.
