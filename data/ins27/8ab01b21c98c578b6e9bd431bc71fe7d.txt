Used two-wheeler e-marketplace Beepkart raises $9 million in round led by Vertex Ventures
indiatimes.com
business
2022-10-19 10:25:00

ETtechL-R: Nikhil Vaidya (CTO), Hemir Doshi, Abhishek Saraf (co-founder & COO).Online two-wheeler marketplace Beepkart said it has raised $9 million in a funding round led by Vertex Ventures South East Asia & India. The round also saw participation from existing investors Stellaris Venture Partners and Chiratae Ventures, which had led the company's $3 million seed round in September 2021. Beepkart said that the money will be used to scale up the business, customer experience, internal products and systems, as well as branding and operations.
