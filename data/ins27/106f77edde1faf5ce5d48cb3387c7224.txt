Markets: Bitcoin rises as Ether leads gains in crypto top 10
yahoo.com

2022-10-17 00:56:10

Bitcoin and Ether were trading higher along with top 10 tokens by market capitalization excluding stablecoins in Monday morning trading in Asia, as the world's largest cryptocurrency remained above US$19,000. XRP was the only token on that list to fall.

Fast facts

Bitcoin gained 1% in the past 24 hours to trade at US$19,268 as of 8 a.m. in Hong Kong. It had fallen as low as US$19,053 on Sunday morning. Ether led gains on the top 10 list, rising 2.
