Calling them 'unsung heroes', the BCCI on Monday announced a prize money of ₹1.25 crore for the curators and groundsmen, who worked during the just-concluded Indian Premier League.New entrants Gujarat Titans lifted the winners' trophy after thrashing Rajasthan Royals by seven wickets in the summit clash on Sunday night.
