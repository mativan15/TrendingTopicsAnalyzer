Congress of the Alliance for Ibero-America Business CouncilThe president of the Alliance for Ibero-America Business Council (CEAPI), Nuria Vilanova (R), and the Ibero-American secretary general, Andres Allamand (L), participate in the V CEAPI congress in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic, 01 June 2022. Some 300 businessmen and experts meet between 01 and 02 June in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic, at the 5th Congress of the Alliance for Ibero-America Business Council (Ceapi) to address the challenges facing the economies of the region.
