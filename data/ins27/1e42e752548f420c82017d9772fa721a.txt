The Federal Government will unveil a further boost to foreign aid to the Pacific in next week's budget, promising to lift development spending by a total of almost $1.4 billion over the next four years.Funding boost is aimed at increasing Australian presence and influence abroadAustralian Federal Police operations in Solomon Islands will receive $46 millionLabor aims to "build a stronger and more united Pacific family" with increased fundingLabor pledged ahead of the election that, if it won power, it would ratchet up development assistance to South-East Asia by $475 million in coming years, while lifting spending in the Pacific by $525 million over the same period.
