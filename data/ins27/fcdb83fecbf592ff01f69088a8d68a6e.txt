The AA will now help cyclists who have broken down
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-19 23:39:31

The Automobile Association is offering roadside assistance to cyclist who have a flat tyre or whose ebike battery has run flat. The service will be automatically available to all AA member, with any road-side assists included as one of the six free call-outs members were entitled to each year. AA club developments general manager Dougal Swift said the added benefit would be included at no extra cost or sign up for members. The service included tyre tube repair or replacement and a bike recovery service, allowing cyclists to have their bike transported back to their home or a repair shop, Swift said.
