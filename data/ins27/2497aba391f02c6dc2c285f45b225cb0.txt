 It was a close call for one driver in Barrie, Ont., who walked away with minor injuries after a crash involving a car and a GO train.
Barrie police said the crash was reported around 11:15 a.m. on Sunday at the corner of Little Avenue and Hurst Drive.'It would appear as if the train was approaching, and the motor vehicle may have been crossing over the track,' said Peter Leon, Barrie police corporate communications coordinator.
