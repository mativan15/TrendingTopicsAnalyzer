As great as you think a renovation may be, it may not always add value to your home. While an extension or loft conversion will nearly always boost the price of your property, other improvements may be anything but an improvement to a potential buyer. Estate agents from Fine & Country and members of their interior design team have shared six renovations that can devalue your home.Personal items like photos, ornaments and trinkets turn a house into a home. But adding more touches, especially ones that are hard to reverse, can make it hard for a buyer to imagine living there, and therefore devalue your home.
