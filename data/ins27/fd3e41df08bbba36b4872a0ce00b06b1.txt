Centre simplifies nearly 500 rules, earns over Rs 250 cr from scrap disposal under spl Swachhta campaign
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 08:00:00

iStockREPRESENTATIONAL IMAGEThe Centre has simplified nearly 500 rules and processes to ensure "ease of living" besides earning a revenue of more than Rs 250 crore from disposal of scrap under the ongoing special Swachhta campaign, senior bureaucrat V Srinivas said on Friday. He said around three lakh public grievances have been redressed in the campaign. "Of these, 4,500 public grievance cases pertain to pensioners," said Srinivas, Secretary, Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG).
