Sport experience event held to mark one year since Tokyo Games
nhk.or.jp
news
2022-10-16 05:30:00

Sport fans in Japan had a chance to enjoy Olympic and Paralympic sports at an event to mark a year since the Tokyo Games. The event took place at the National Stadium in Tokyo on Sunday. Areas were set up where visitors could experience eight different sports. In the skateboard area, teenage professional skateboarders taught participants basic skills on how to move and stop. The sport has been popular in Japan, with Japanese athletes winning gold medals at the Tokyo Games. There was also an area for wheelchair rugby.
