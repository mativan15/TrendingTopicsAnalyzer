Hokies uncork 14-run inning, pummel Lions 24-4 to make regional title game
timesnews.net
news
2022-06-05 06:30:00

BLACKSBURG  — Tanner Schobel had three hits, scored three times and drove in three runs and No. 4 overall seed Virginia Tech used a 14-run inning to coast to a 24-4 win over Columbia on Saturday night, landing a spot in the championship game of the Blacksburg Regional.The Hokies (43-12) were trailing 2-1 heading into the fourth inning when they sent 17 batters to the plate, scoring 14 runs on 10 hits, four walks and a hit batter. That was their most runs in an inning since joining the Atlantic Coast Conference; their all-time record is 17 set in 1989.
