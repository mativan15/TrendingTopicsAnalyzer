This scenario checked all the boxes in terms of complexity and the number of moving parts needed to get this done. GARDEN CITY, N.Y. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 A New Jersey-based engineering firm recently secured combination financing totaling $6.9 million through United Capital Source, Inc. UCS aided the firm in receiving a $4.8 million SBA 7(a) loan combined with a $2.1 million term loan. It took 12 weeks to complete the lending process. The funds enable the engineering firm to acquire a new business to expand its established patent.
