TODD MISSION, Texas, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Pirates Adventures returns October 22nd and 23rd at the Texas Renaissance Festival! "Gather your crew and set sail for fun during Pirate Adventures this weekend" says Carl Foy, Marketing Director for Texas Renaissance Festival. "Pirate Adventures is the largest pirate party, north of the Gulf of Mexico! Pirate garb, sea shanties, and costume contests; plus over 400 shops, restaurants, and pubs at the Texas Renaissance Festival! You'll leave singing "yo ho, yo ho…!"
