Facebook parent Meta to sell Giphy, giving up battle with UK regulators
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-10-18 15:27:15

Facebook owner Meta has given up its fight against the U.K. government's insistence that it sell animated picture platform Giphy, agreeing Tuesday to unwind its acquisition of the New York-based firm on the orders of the foreign regulator. The U.K.'s antitrust watchdog, the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA), this week re-affirmed its ruling from last year that Meta's purchase of Giphy would stifle competition and innovation.
