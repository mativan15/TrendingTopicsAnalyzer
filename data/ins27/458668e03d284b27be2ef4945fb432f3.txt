Three of the four demands raised by social activist Daya Bai will be implemented. The one related to AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences) cannot be accepted, Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan has said.At a press conference here on Tuesday evening, Mr. Vijayan said the government was considerate of the agitation by Ms. Daya Bai who had been on a hunger strike in the State capital for endosulfan victims. Two Ministers had met and held discussions with the 81-year-old activist and given the government assurances in writing.
