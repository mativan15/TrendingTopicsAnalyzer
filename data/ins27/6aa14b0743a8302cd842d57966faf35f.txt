Offshore Guyana could contain billions more oil barrels, Hess CEO says (NYSE:HES)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-02 22:55:14

Alexmumu/iStock via Getty Images New oil findings at a deeper layer offshore Guyana could add billions of barrels to what is already the world's largest oil discovery in 20 years, Hess (NYSE:HES) CEO John Hess said Thursday, according to Reuters. "There are multi-billion barrels remaining on top of" the 11B barrels of oil and gas found so far in the offshore Stabroek block, the CEO said, noting the Exxon-led (NYSE:XOM) consortium, which made most of its 30-plus oil discoveries at depths of ~15K feet, has started to drill at 18K feet.
