Published: 11:27 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:37 EDT, 17 October 2022 Two thirds of voters say crime is rising and blame 'woke politicians' for the phenomena, including a majority of Democrats, says a poll that suggests law and order will benefit Republicans in next month's midterms.Some 66 percent of voters said they thought crime was rising, while 64 per cent said the apparent increase was the 'fault of woke politicians', according to a Harvard-Harris poll released just weeks before the November 8 midterm elections.
