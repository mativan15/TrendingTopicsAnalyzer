Capital Strategies Group is pleased to announce the launch of a specialized personal insurance business to serve the unique needs of high-net-worth individuals and families. Founded in 1989 in Birmingham Alabama, Capital Strategies Group, Inc. is a wealth preservation-focused insurance firm serving ultra-high net-worth families and their companies Griff Israel, Managing Partner of Capital Strategies Private Client Insurance, is responsible for leading this effort. 'The insurance brokerage industry is experiencing unprecedented consolidation leading to opportunities in certain underserved segments of the business.
