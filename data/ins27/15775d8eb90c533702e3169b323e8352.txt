Raj Samani, Chief Scientist and Fellow at McAfee said ‘The pandemic has completely changed how we live, from the way we socialize to the way we work. With many of us spending far more time online from home, criminals have been quick to adapt their tactics – creating a whole host of new scams which businesses must be more aware of given the large numbers of staff still working remotely. The threat for businesses is also intensified by the fact that many employees are accessing work files and information from home across both corporate and personal devices.
