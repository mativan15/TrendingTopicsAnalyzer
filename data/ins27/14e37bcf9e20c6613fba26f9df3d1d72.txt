June 02, 2022 06:35 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--American Tower Corporation (NYSE: AMT) today announced that, in connection with the funding of its acquisition of CoreSite Realty Corporation ('CoreSite'), which closed in December 2021, American Tower intends to offer 8,350,000 shares of its common stock in a registered public offering, subject to market and other conditions. American Tower intends to grant the underwriters the option to purchase up to an additional 835,000 shares of common stock to cover over-allotments, if any.
