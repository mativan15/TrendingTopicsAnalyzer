It has not been a kind year for U.S. cannabis stocks. After starting 2021 on a tear, most leading cannabis names have sold off hard, as legislative action on legalization has dragged on and the prospects for reform have been thrown into question.
As a result, U.S. cannabis companies continue to bear high costs, and investors have lost enthusiasm for stocks without near-term profits. The Advisorshares Pure US Cannabis ETF (MSOS -2.49%) is down a whopping 75% from its all-time highs as investors have fled for safer pastures.
