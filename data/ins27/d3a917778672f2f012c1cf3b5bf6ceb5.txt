Ambuja Cements' net profit dips 94% in Q2 FY23
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 17:15:00

NEW DELHI: Ambuja Cements, the building materials arm of Adani Group, has reported 94.24 per cent declined in its net consolidated profit during the quarter ended September 30, 2022. Its profit after tax (PAT) stood at Rs 51.30 crore in Q2 FY23 as against Rs 890.67 crore it recorded in the corresponding quarter of the previous fiscal, the company said in a BSE filing.The company's total consolidated income stood at Rs 7,244.78 crore in Q2 FY23, a growth of 7.48 per cent from Rs 6,740.57 crore it recorded in the similar quarter last year.
