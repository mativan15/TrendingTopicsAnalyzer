Your Guide To AfroTech Conference 2022, Held In Austin, Texas For The First Time Ever
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 16:50:32

AfroTech Conference is one of the largest multicultural tech conferences in the United States, and this year, it will be an even more immersive event as, after two years of being hosted virtually due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the conference returns to its in-person roots.

Taking place Sunday, Nov. 13 through Thursday, Nov. 17 at the Austin Convention Center and Hilton Austin, 20,000+ Black tech innovators, founders, entrepreneurs, and more will gather for events filled with dynamic programming on emerging trends, networking opportunities with industry leaders, and conversations with top tech recruiters.
