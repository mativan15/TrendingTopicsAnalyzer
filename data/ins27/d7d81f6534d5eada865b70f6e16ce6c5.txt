YOAN VALAT / EPA Whether they're scrutinized, mocked or fantasized about, the students of Sciences Po – the prestigious, Paris-based international research university – rarely leave anyone indifferent. The majority of them will work in government or in the private sector – more than 70% – and as such, they are among the most recognizable students in the vast community of higher education.
