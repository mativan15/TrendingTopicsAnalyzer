Lynn King, Experienced Clinical Operations Executive, Tapped to Lead M3 Wake Research after CEO Retires

RALEIGH, N.C., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- M3 Wake Research, a leading fully integrated network of clinical research sites and one of North America's largest providers of Phase I-IV clinical trials, welcomes a new Chief Operations Officer, Lynn King.

(PRNewsfoto/M3 Wake Research)

"I'm pleased to announce that Lynn King has joined M3 Wake Research as our new COO," said Aki Tomaru, CEO of M3 USA, the parent company of M3 Wake Research.
