June 02, 2022 04:10 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pinterest, Inc. (NYSE: PINS) - Today, Pinterest is announcing that it's signed a definitive agreement to acquire THE YES, an AI powered shopping platform for fashion that enables users to shop a personalized feed based on the user's active input on brand, style, and size. Pinterest believes that THE YES's leadership, innovative technology and talented team, combining shopping expertise with fashion industry credibility, will help accelerate Pinterest's vision for it to be the home of taste-driven shopping.
