October 20, 2022 06:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time JACKSONVILLE, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Rayonier Advance Materials Inc. (NYSE:RYAM) (the 'Company') announced today that Charles (Chas) R. Eggert has been elected to its Board of Directors, effective October 19th, 2022.



'We're pleased to welcome Chas to our Board of Directors and look forward to benefitting from his more than 40 years of industry experience and proven expertise,' stated Lisa M. Palumbo, Non-Executive Chair of the Board of Directors of the Company.
