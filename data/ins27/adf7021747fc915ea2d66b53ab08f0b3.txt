BENTONVILLE, Ark. and SAN DIEGO, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ - Sam's Club®, a division of Walmart Inc. and a leading membership warehouse club, today announced the completion of a national, chain-wide roll out of 'Inventory Scan' towers that have been added to their existing fleet of robotic scrubbers in partnership with Brain Corp, an artificial intelligence (AI) company creating transformative core technology in robotics.
