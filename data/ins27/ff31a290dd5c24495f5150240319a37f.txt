Biden's attack on gig work is an Uber mistake
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-10-19 16:55:18

If we thought the latest inflation numbers were bad, the Biden administration is about to make things worse. Last week, the Department of Labor announced a new rule that would curtail the use of contractors and freelancers – the heart of the "gig economy."  Framed as "helping" these workers by forcing them onto payrolls, this rule will certainly be another example of "when help hurts" to come out of the Biden administration. The reality is this regulation will increase prices further at a time when inflation on necessities is crushing Americans.
