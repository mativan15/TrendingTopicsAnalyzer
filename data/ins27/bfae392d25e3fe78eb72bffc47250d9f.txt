ServiceNow on the move as it eyes room for expansion in Dublin
irishtimes.com
business
2022-06-02 14:16:36

Digital workflow company ServiceNow is to expand its offices in Ireland after agreeing a 12-year lease for four floors of a new office development on Dawson St.The US software company will take 90,000 sq ft in 60 Dawson St, a new 145,000 sq ft Grade A office development that is due to be completed early next year. The new building will serve as the company's Irish headquarters.Although ServiceNow, did not say how many jobs in plans to create in the coming years, the new offices could comfortably accommodate more than double the number of new jobs it committed to creating last year.
