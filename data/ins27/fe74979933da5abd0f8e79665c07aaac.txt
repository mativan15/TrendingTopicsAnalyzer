AAA: Texas gas prices break new records, keep climbing
iheart.com
news
2022-06-02 19:30:00

The average gas price in Texas set a new record overnight, standing at $4.34 per gallon of regular unleaded according to the AAA Texas Weekend Gas Watch. That's eight cents more than Thursday last week and $1.63 more per gallon than this day last year. Drivers in El Paso are paying the most on average at $4.54 per gallon while those in Lubbock are paying the least at $4.16. The average Houston price today was $4.31.
