IKEA to indulge you with Ethiopian coffee and Indonesian premium chocolates
ikea.com
news
2022-06-01 18:15:00

Coffee is believed to be the common man's gold, and chocolates can fix everything – from broken hearts to hungry tummies. It's no wonder that IKEA turned its lens on these two when it came to bringing home the finest. But IKEA also wants to create a better everyday life through employment opportunities and livelihood for those who need it the most. So, IKEA searched continents and found two such social business partners – an Ethiopian coffee maker and an Indonesian chocolate brand – with great products and a significant social impact.
