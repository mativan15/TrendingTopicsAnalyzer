Forget the beach body, this summer you've got to be housing market ready. After two years of stratospheric property price increases, it appears the market is finally about to come back down to earth.'It feels like we are at a crossroads,' says James Cleland, head of country business at the estate agency Knight Frank. 'The number of buyers is still very high and now vendors are sensing that the market may be at its peak, hence taking offers and coming to the market. It is a sudden recognition that now is the time to act, brought on by all of the obvious factors coalescing at the same time.
