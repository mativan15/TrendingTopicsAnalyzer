REUTERS/Dado Ruvic/Illustration/File PhotoZURICH, Oct 19 (Reuters) - Switzerland will destroy 9 million doses of Moderna (MRNA.O) COVID-19 vaccine that have reached their expiry date, with another 5.1 million vaccine jabs set to meet the same fate by February, the government said on Wednesday.The wastage reflects the Swiss strategy of ordering more vaccines than it needed to ensure its population of around 8.
