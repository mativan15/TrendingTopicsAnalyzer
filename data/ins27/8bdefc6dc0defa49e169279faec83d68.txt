CCI fines Google Rs 1,338 crore for anti-competitive practices
indiatimes.com
tech
2022-10-20 16:15:00

NEW DELHI: The Competition Commission of India (CCI) said on Thursday it has fined Alphabet Inc's Google Rs 1,338 crore ($161.95 million) for anti-competitive practices related to Android mobile devices .CCI also ordered Google not to offer any incentives to smartphone makers for exclusively carrying its search services.Google did not immediately respond to a request for comment.Google is facing a series of antitrust cases in India and stricter tech-sector regulations. The competition watchdog is also looking into Google's business conduct in the smart TVs market and its in-app payments system.
