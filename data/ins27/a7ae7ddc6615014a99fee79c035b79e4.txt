Business activity rose at the quickest pace since April 2011, with new orders rising at the highest rate since July 2011. | Photo Credit: NAGARA GOPAL Updated: June 03, 2022 12:05 ISTIndia's services sector recorded the best expansion in activity this May in over 11 years, as per the S&P Global Services Purchasing Managers' Index (PMI) which moved up from 57.9 in April to 58.9 in May. A PMI reading of over 50 indicates growth in activity levels.Business activity rose at the quickest pace since April 2011, with new orders rising at the highest rate since July 2011, even as input cost pressures surged at the fastest pace ever since the survey's commencement in December 2005.
