October 19, 2022 11:37 AM Eastern Daylight Time ANAHEIM, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The nation's leading advanced design and manufacturing event, featuring Medical Design and Manufacturing (MD&M) West, WestPack, Automation Technology (ATX) West, Design and Manufacturing (D&M) West, and Plastec West today announces registration is live for attendees and press. The event will take place Feb. 7-9, 2023, at the Anaheim Convention Center, housing all five expo brands for guests and exhibitors to connect with every facet of the manufacturing industry.
