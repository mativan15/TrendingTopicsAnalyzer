Published: 19:47 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 20:00 EDT, 4 June 2022 As one of the most celebrated footballers of his generation, Steven Gerrard amassed a fortune of more than £60 million – setting an example that appears to have rubbed off on his daughter, Lilly-Ella.The 18-year-old is already one of the country's most sought-after social-media influencers, earning tens of thousands of pounds thanks to the dedication of her 175,000 Instagram followers and her famous surname.The eldest child of Liverpool legend Gerrard has at least ten deals with lifestyle brands, including the fast-fashion firm Boohoo.
