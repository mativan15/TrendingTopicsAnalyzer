Arappor Iyakkam, a non-governmental organisation espousing transparency in governance, has urged Tiruchi Corporation to create a transparent e-tender system.The organisation has raised apprehensions of favouritism in the e-tender system of the Corporation, citing the physical point of contact between the civic body and bidders. It has exhorted the Corporation to streamline the lapses in the online tender system to ensure accountability in awarding work orders for infrastructure projects.
