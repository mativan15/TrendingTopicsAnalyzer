Apple, AMD's Taiwan exposure may still stay above 40% by 2030

Apple, Advanced Micro Devices and other chipmakers that don't own foundries may only be able to reduce their exposure to China and Taiwan to about 40% by 2030, according to our calculations. Taiwan will still hold the largest share of advanced-node semiconductor manufacturing capacity, while mainland China will dominate mature-node capacity.
