How 100-goal Forrest continues to deliver for Celtic

Twenty trophies, 10 league titles, five League Cups, five Scottish Cups, and now after a hat-trick in Saturday's demolition of Hibernian, 100 goals for Celtic. One hundred not out.He is one of the most decorated players in Scottish football history, never mind Celtic's. Yet somehow, James Forrest doesn't seem to get the recognition a record like that deserves. It's a curious thing. There's no Forrest song sung by Celtic fans. There is undeniably a clear respect, but does that reflect what he has delivered?The Scotland international has had intermittent fights to remind people of his worth, but BBC Scotland has looked at how he continues to defy the doubters.
