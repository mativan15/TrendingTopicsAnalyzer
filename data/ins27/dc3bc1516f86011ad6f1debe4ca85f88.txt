Here's how you can spot the 'cruel' scam and avoid losing money...Scammers targeted 76 year old Christopher Short, and spent more than £1,000 on his card A major bank has warned Brits to keep their eyes peeled for an NHS text scam after a man lost more than £1,000. Barclays UK urged customers to "stay alert" for the Coronavirus-related text message posing as NHS Track and Trace. Christopher Short, 76, was informed by scammers that he'd been in close contact with someone who had tested positive for Covid.
