Kerry Wood named CEO of Sacramento Region Community Foundation
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-21 01:10:00

The Sacramento Region Community Foundation didn't have to look far to find its new CEO.

The local nonprofit announced Thursday that Kerry Wood will take the top spot at the foundation, as outgoing CEO Linda Beech Cutler prepares to retire. Wood has been the foundation's chief marketing and donor engagement officer since 2018, according to her LinkedIn profile.

Wood will take the CEO role on Oct. 31, according to a news release from the nonprofit. Cutler will be an an adviser to Wood and the foundation through the end of the year.
