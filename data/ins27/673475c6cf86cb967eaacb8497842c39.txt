Shares of Colgate-Palmolive (India) Ltd. traded 0.31 per cent up at Rs 1558.45 at 11:01AM (IST) on Monday, even as BSE benchmark Sensex gained 249.05 points to 58169.02.The stock had settled at Rs 1553.7 in the previous session. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 1695.0 and 52-week low of Rs 1375.55, respectively. As per BSE data, total traded volume on the counter till 11:01AM (IST) stood at 165797 shares with a turnover of Rs 25.74 crore.At the current price, the stock traded at 40.18 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 38.
