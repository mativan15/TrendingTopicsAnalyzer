TALLIN, Estonia, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ --  CoinsPaid , the largest crypto payment ecosystem, has processed over €16 billion worth of crypto* in total and continues growing by nearly €1 billion per month in the declining market.
In Q3 2022, CoinsPaid completed 4.9 million transactions worth approximately €3 billion compared to Q3 2021, whereby it handled close to 2.5 million transactions with a volume of about €1.5 billion. Q3 2022 figures show 2X the volume of transactions and 1.94X the number of transactions of the same period in 2021.
