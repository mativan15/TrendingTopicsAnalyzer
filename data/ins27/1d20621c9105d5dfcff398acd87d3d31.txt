Wi-LAN Units, Micron Tech Enter Into License Agreement

By Adriano Marchese Quarterhill Inc.'s Wi-LAN Inc. said Friday that it has entered into a license and settlement agreement with Micron Technology Inc. that also includes the dismissal of patent litigation in the U.S. and China. The Canadian public technology holding company said the agreement is between Micron and Wi-LAN's subsidiaries Innovative Memory Solutions Inc., North Star Innovations Inc. and Cetus Technologies Inc.
