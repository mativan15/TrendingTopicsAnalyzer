Despite the fact that the deadline to keep your PAN active without linking it to your Aadhaar is extended to March 31, 2023, linking your PAN to your Aadhaar will cost you money from April 1, 2022. The Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) made the announcement via a notification dated March 29, 2022, as well as a press release on March 30, 2022. The deadline for linking a PAN to an Aadhaar number has been pushed up from March 31, 2022 to March 31, 2023.
