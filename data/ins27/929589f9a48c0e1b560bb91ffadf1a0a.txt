First Community: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

business
2022-10-19 13:13:33

LEXINGTON, S.C.

First Community Corp. (FCCO) on Wednesday reported third-quarter net income of $4 million.

The bank, based in Lexington, South Carolina, said it had earnings of 52 cents per share.

The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of three analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 46 cents per share.

The holding company for First Community Bank posted revenue of $16 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $15.5 million, also beating Street forecasts.
