ED issues fresh summons to Rahul Gandhi on June 13
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 06:15:00

Rahul Gandhi was summoned by the Enforcement Directorate on June 13 in the National Herald case after requesting a fresh date. Sonia Gandhi was summoned before the ED on June 8. Rahul was scheduled to appear on Thursday, but he requested a postponement and now he will be called on June 13 since he will be out of the country until June 5. 
The ED will record both statements under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act. The issue involves an equity transaction in which Sonia and her son Rahul are accused of "misappropriating" Associated Journals' assets "worth over Rs 2,000 crore" for "only Rs 50 lakh.
