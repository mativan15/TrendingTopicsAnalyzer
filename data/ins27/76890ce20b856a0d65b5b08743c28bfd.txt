PM Modi to dedicate 75 Digital Banking Units to the nation. Details
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-16 04:45:39

In a bid to deepen financial inclusion in the country, Prime Minister Narendra Modi will today dedicate 75 Digital Banking Units (DBUs) to the nation via video conferencing today. He will also address a gathering on the occasion, the Prime Minister's Office said.As part of the Union budget 2022-23, finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman had announced the setting up of the 75 DBUs in 75 districts to commemorate 75 years of India's independence from British rule.What is the objective of the DBUs?The Digital Banking Units are being set up to ensure that the benefits of digital banking reach every nook and corner of the country.
