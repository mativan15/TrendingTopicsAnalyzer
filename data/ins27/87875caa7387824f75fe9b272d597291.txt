A Sydney cleaner who murdered her elderly client by beating and stabbing her has been sentenced to 22 years in prison. The judge found the cleaner's attack was financially motivatedMarjorie Welsh identified her attacker in her hospital bed, before she died from her injuries Daughters of Ms Welsh attended court and were happy with the sentence outcomeHanny Papanicolaou will not be eligible for parole until January 2034. Papanicolaou broke into the Ashbury home of Marjorie Welsh in January 2019 with the intention to steal money from the 92-year-old.
