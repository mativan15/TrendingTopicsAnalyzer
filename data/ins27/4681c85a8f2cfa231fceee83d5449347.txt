Un-Unicornization- Startups losing their billion-dollar value status
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-29 08:15:02

Earlier this month, the Indian startup ecosystem celebrated its 100th entry into the coveted billion-dollar-valuation Unicorn club. With this, India stands amongst the four largest countries in the world to have 100 or more unicorns. It follows US, China and just about rubs shoulders with UK in terms of number of unicorns.
As the Indian entrepreneurship continues to thrive and churn out brilliant businesses to create these unicorns and soonicorns (soon to be a unicorn), the startup ecosystem has also been a beneficiary of the global liquidity surge, thus far, in terms of pumping up of the valuations of these startups.
