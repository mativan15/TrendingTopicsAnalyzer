Fundraiser exceeded its goal, raising $12,000 to serve the children of Southwest Washington and Oregon through the iQ for Kids FoundationVANCOUVER, Wash., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- iQ Credit Union (iQ), a member-owned, modern-day financial institution based in the Pacific Northwest, today announced the success of its iQ Cares Week fundraiser, exceeding its goal of $10,000 by raising $12,000 to support the company's nonprofit, iQ for Kids Foundation. The foundation will distribute the funds in the form of grants and donations to local organizations that serve children in the community.
