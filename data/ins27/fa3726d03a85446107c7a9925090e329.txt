NEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The "Corrugated Box Market in US by End-user and Material - Forecast and Analysis 2022-2026" report has been added to Technavio's offering. The report expects the market size to grow by USD 1.85 billion between 2021 and 2026. Also, the market is anticipated to observe a YOY growth of 2.44% in 2022 and accelerate at a CAGR of 3.32% during the forecast period. The report offers an up-to-date analysis regarding the current market scenario, the latest trends and drivers, and the overall market environment.
