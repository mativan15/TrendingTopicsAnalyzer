Representative ImageNew Delhi: Edible oil major Ruchi Soya Industries Ltd on Friday reported a 25 per cent decline in its standalone net profit to Rs 234.33 crore for the March 2022 quarter, mainly due to higher tax expenses. Its net profit stood at Rs 314.33 crore in the year-ago period. The company's total income rose to Rs 6,676.19 crore in the fourth quarter from Rs 4,859.5 crore in the corresponding period of the preceding fiscal. Net profit increased to Rs 806.3 crore in 2021-22 from Rs 680.
