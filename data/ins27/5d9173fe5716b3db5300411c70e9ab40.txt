Intuitive Surgical Shares Rise 7% After 3Q Results
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 20:33:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of medical equipment maker Intuitive Surgical Inc. were trading higher in Tuesday's after-hours session, following the release of third-quarter results with higher revenue. The company said during the third quarter it placed 305 da Vinci Surgical Systems, adding worldwide da Vinci procedures rose by about 20% compared with the third quarter of last year. At 4:20 p.m. ET, shares had risen 7.51% to trade at $208.28. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 1.9% gain, closing at $193.
