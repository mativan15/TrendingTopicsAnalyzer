June 02, 2022 07:54 PM Eastern Daylight Time OAKLAND, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--A Mullen Automotive class action lawsuit has been filed on behalf of investors who lost money in Mullen (NASDAQ: MULN). Shares of Mullen Automotive dropped 10% on April 6, 2022, after Hindenburg Research published a report alleging that the company misrepresented test results for its solid-state battery technology, exaggerated its business relationships, and overstated its ability to manufacture and sell its branded products.
