New Delhi: Norway's state-owned Statkraft announced it has commissioned its first large-scale solar power plant in India, marking a milestone in its ambition to ramp up as a solar and wind power developer in the country.The 76 Megawatt (MW) Nellai solar power plant is located in Tamil Nadu and will generate 120 GWh of renewable energy per year, equal to the annual power consumption of more than 500,000 Indian homes. 'Statkraft is currently focusing on the development of greenfield solar projects at utility scale in India.
