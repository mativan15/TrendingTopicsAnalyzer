CHENGDU, China, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The local government of Qingyang district in Chengdu, capital of Southwest China's Sichuan province, is striving to revitalize the district through urban renewal.Many old residential communities have had their appearance and functions overhauled. One community, known as Huanhua community, was built in 1998 and began renovations in 2021.
