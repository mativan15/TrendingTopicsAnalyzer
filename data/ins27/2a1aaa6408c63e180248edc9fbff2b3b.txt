'I will tell you what I do': Jaishankar on duty of a foreign minister; 'compare 26/11 with Pulwama and Uri'
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 01:37:27

External affairs minister S Jaishankar on Tuesday addressed a public event in Ahmedabad and said he wants people to understand what he, as a foreign minister, does. Talking to the youth, he said, "You must have read foreign minister is travelling to the US, Australia etc. But what do I do? I want people to understand what I do or a foreign minister does. There are two aspects: first is to introduce India to the world and also to bring the world to India."The world has now become ready for India, Jaishankar said adding that the Modi government has foreign policies for 10 days, 10 months and 10 years.
