Report: US Considers Security Reviews of Musk's Twitter Deal, Starlink
pcmag.com
news
2022-10-21 13:47:40

The Biden administration may be preparing to scrutinize Elon Musk's deal to buy Twitter, along with SpaceX's satellite internet system Starlink.

The White House is considering subjecting Musk's business ventures to national security reviews, Bloomberg reports(Opens in a new window), citing unnamed sources familiar with the matter.

According to Bloomberg, US officials were alarmed by a Musk tweet that threatened to pull Starlink access in Ukraine, which came after SpaceX told the Pentagon it could no longer afford to fund the service in the country.
