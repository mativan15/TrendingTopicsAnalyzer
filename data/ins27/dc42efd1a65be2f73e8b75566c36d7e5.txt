Ethan Miller Netflix's (NASDAQ:NFLX) plan to launch a version of its service supported by advertising represents something of a high-stakes move for a company that set the standard in streaming success - until it finally seemed to run into a growth wall. Netflix stock (NFLX) wrapped up 2021 at a $602 price point (not so far below its all-time high of $700.99 touched last Nov. 17); but two rough earnings reports this year showed subscriber growth was no longer a given (or that the company could actually shed subs) and took the shine off the former new-media darling.
