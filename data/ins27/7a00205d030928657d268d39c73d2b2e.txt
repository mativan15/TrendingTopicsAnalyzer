Variety - TV News Finland unveiled six upcoming shows at Mipcom, with thrillers continuing to be relevant but Nordic Noir taking a back seat.The showcase, organized by Audiovisual Producers Finland – Apfi partnering up with Yle, Finnish Film Foundation, Elisa Viihde and Film in Finland, spotlighted a thriller about a data leak, 'cosy' crime series and even animation inspired by unboxing videos.Aurora Studios is developing 'Sunset Grove' with Elisa Viihde, described as a 'feel-good crime story.
