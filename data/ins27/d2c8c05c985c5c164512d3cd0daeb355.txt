ORLANDO, FL / ACCESSWIRE / October 17, 2022 / Laser Photonics Corporation (NASDAQ:LASE), ("LPC"), a leading global industrial developer of Cleantech laser systems for laser cleaning and other materials applications, today announced that Detroit Diesel recently completed the implementation of its first CleanTech Laser Cleaning Robot system in its manufacturing facilities.

Wayne Tupuola, chief executive officer of Laser Photonics, commented: "Detroit Diesel's use of our CleanTech Laser Blasting system is one of the first of its kind to be installed in a manufacturing facility and is yet another proven use case for our technology.
