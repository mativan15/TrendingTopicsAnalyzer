scaliger/iStock via Getty Images [The war in Ukraine has affected the ESG/Impact investing community in a number of major ways. First of all, through reduced investment inflows. The Institute of International Finance (IIF) reported that ESG funds saw inflows halve in Q1 2022 amidst Russia's war in Ukraine, volatility in ESG-heavy tech shares, and higher oil prices diverting funds into non-ESG energy stocks. ESG Q1 fund flows of $75 billion were at their lowest level in seven quarters. IIF data also showed that flows into ESG bonds were also down sharply in Q1, dropping to $14 billion from $27 billion.
