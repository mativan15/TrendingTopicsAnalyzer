June 01, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Workato, the leading enterprise automation platform, announced today that workforce management platform Deputy will begin utilizing Workato to help scale and transform its current business processes as well as solve data discrepancies. With the help of Workato's integration-led automation approach, tasks that used to take up to 30-minutes to complete now take seconds.



Deputy is a platform that helps millions of hourly-paid workers solve common problems they face daily such as tracking their time and attendance, clocking in and out, and making team communication simple.
