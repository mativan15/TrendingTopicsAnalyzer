June 02, 2022 04:29 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Forge Global Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: FRGE) ('Forge'), a global private securities marketplace, announced today the appointment of Asiff Hirji to its Board of Directors, effective as of June 1. Mr. Hirji was also appointed to the Risk Committee.



'Asiff's experience at the intersection of information technology, investment and financial services, and his deep operating expertise, has already proven valuable to Forge. We look forward to his continued contributions as we continue to grow and scale the private market asset class,' said Kelly Rodriques, CEO of Forge.
