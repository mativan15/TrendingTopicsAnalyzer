AgenciesSiddarth Sharma with his family. (Image: LinkedIn)It is well known that kids get a self-esteem boost each time an adult rewards them or pays compliments on their small achievements. Every parent wants the best for their children which essentially means ensuring that they are safe, healthy, happy and thriving. Children face all kinds of challenges, and when they overcome them, it is usually a sign that they are evolving into strong individuals. Hence, as parents, it is important to acknowledge their achievements and progress.
