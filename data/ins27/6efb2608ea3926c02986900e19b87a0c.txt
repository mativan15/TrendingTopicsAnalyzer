(West Suburban YMCA)NEWTON, MA — The West Suburban YMCA is welcoming the community to its Annual Fall Festival – a free and fun family-oriented event – on Saturday, Oct. 22 from 2-4 p.m., followed by Open House Week when all are invited to 'try the Y' for free.Children are encouraged to wear a Halloween costume to the Fall Festival, an afternoon of autumn-themed activities and entertainment.
