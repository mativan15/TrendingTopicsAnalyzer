October 18, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time PHILADELPHIA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Comcast Business today announced that Comcast Business Mobile customers are now able to mix and match both unlimited and an expanded range of shared data options for up to twenty total lines per account. Comcast Business Mobile is designed to help small businesses be ready for today and whatever comes next with flexible data options, nationwide 5G coverage, millions of secure WiFi hotspots, and increased savings.



Now more than ever, small businesses are looking for ways to increase productivity and reduce costs.
