AFPHere are some tips to help you resist tobacco cravings.If you are among those who have finally decided to quit smoking, World No-Tobacco Day could be the best time to start. Tobacco addiction is associated with both mental and physical habits. You have to address both in order to control your urge, as for most people, tobacco or smoking urges are the first hurdle. When you feel an urge to use tobacco, keep in mind that it will likely pass within the next few minutes. Each time you resist a tobacco craving, you are one step closer to stopping tobacco use for good.
