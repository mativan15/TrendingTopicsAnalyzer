Argos feel they have something to sell after two years affected by COVID
thestar.com
news
2022-05-31 00:00:00

Argos general manager Mike Clemons and his staff could have the beginnings of a championship level team in training camp at the University of Guelph. But what gets Clemons as excited as having at least seven quality players added to this season's roster is the fact the franchise should see growth at the turnstiles.Clemons sees the additions of Hall of Fame-calibre players like Brandon Banks and Andrew Harris as the first real marketing opportunity for the Argos since COVID began shutting down the sports world in March 2020.
