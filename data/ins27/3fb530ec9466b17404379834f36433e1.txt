June 01, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Thryv Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ:THRY) ('Thryv' or the 'Company'), the small business platform for growing small- to medium-sized businesses (SMBs), today announced it has hired Tami Cannizzaro as Chief Marketing Officer. With more than 20 years of experience building tech companies into category leaders, Cannizzaro will play a key role in Thryv's growth strategy.



'Tami has a proven track record of driving new customers, increased engagement, and revenue growth for major global technology brands as well as emerging leaders in the category,' said Joe Walsh, Chairman and CEO of Thryv.
