LYKKE Li's career in music has spanned over 15 years, with other ventures including modelling under her belt.
The Swedish singer's music has featured in popular TV shows and she has even tried her hand at acting.
Lykke Li has been in the limelight since 2007Credit: Corbis - Getty Lykke Li, whose full name is Li Lykke Timotej Zachrisson, is a Swedish singer and model.
She was born March 18, 1986, in Ystad, Sweden.
Her music career kicked off in 2007 when she released her first EP, Little Bit.
Lykke, 36, has since gone on to release five studio albums including Youth Novels and Wounded Rhymes.
