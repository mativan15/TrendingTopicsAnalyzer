Published: 11:23 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 11:23 EDT, 30 May 2022 Top Gun: Maverick has risked angering China by restoring the Taiwanese flag on the back of Tom Cruise's jacket in the new blockbuster.In the original 1986 classic, Lieutenant Pete 'Maverick' Mitchell wears a leather bomber jacket featuring patches on the back, commemorating his father's battleship tours to Japan and Taiwan in 1963 to 1964.But when the trailer for the highly-anticipated sequel was released in 2019, the flags had been mysteriously removed.
