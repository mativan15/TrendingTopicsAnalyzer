Cooper says Forest tweet before loss 'not helpful'
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 11:08:14

Cooper's side were beaten by Wolves following a penalty by Ruben NevesNottingham Forest manager Steve Cooper says a social media post by his club before Saturday's 1-0 defeat at Wolves "was not helpful".Forest put out a picture of striker Emmanuel Dennis on the Molineux pitch surrounded by wolf cubs with the caption "playtime".The post was deleted but not before it was shared by Wolves players.After the game, Wolves responded with a post depicting a chopped down tree with the caption "playtime's over".
