Published: 22:33 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 22:35 EDT, 17 October 2022 Slumdog Millionaire alum Freida Pinto suited up to attend the 16th Annual Golden Heart Awards, which was held at The Glasshouse in Manhattan's Chelsea neighborhood on Monday night.The SAG Award winner - turning 39 this Tuesday - looked chic in a white blazer over a matching top, wide-leg pants, and gold accessories selected by stylist Erin Walsh.Hairstylist Joseph Chase coiffed Freida's signature raven locks as she posed up a storm on the black carpet.
