More Video Moses Mozart Dzawu, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- Ghana's cedi extended the world's biggest loss versus the dollar this year amid uncertainty about talks with domestic bondholders.The currency depreciated as much 9.6% on Thursday, heading for its biggest decline in 22 years. That took losses in 2022 to nearly 52%, the worst performance among 148 currencies tracked by Bloomberg.Ghana formed a committee this month to hold talks with domestic bond investors to collect views for a debt management strategy it is fashioning to guide ongoing negotiations with the International Monetary Fund.
