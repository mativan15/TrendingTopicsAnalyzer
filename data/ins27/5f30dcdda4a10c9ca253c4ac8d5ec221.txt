Oct. 19—MITCHELL — Area voters will get a chance to hear from candidates for the

Davison County Commission

as well as supporters and opponents of Initiated Measure 27 and Constitutional Amendment D Thursday evening.

The public forum, which will be held at the Mitchell Technical College Muth Electric Technology Center Amphitheater at 1800 E. Spruce St. in Mitchell, is scheduled to begin at 6 p.m. The forum is free and open to the public.

The Mitchell Chamber of Commerce Government Affairs Committee, which is organizing the event, has invited the three candidates who will appear on the ballot for the District 3 seat on the commission.
