HDFC Bank leases 2.5 lakh sq ft office space in Mindspace REIT's business park

has leased over 250,000 square feet of office space at K Raheja Corp-backed Mindspace Business Parks REIT's business park in Navi Mumbai's Airoli locality for 10 years, multiple people with direct knowledge of the development said. The bank is planning to set up its technology and processing unit at the new office that will service its operations across the country. The office tower, which is part of Mindspace business park's first phase, is currently under development and is expected to be completed in the next 3-4 months when the bank will start its fit-out work.
