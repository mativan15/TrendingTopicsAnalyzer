Qraft Technologies Chief Product Officer Alan Jo (Im Se-jun/The Korea Herald)SoftBank-backed South Korean financial technology startup Qraft Technologies highlighted the role of artificial intelligence in equity asset allocation, as AI plays a complementary role to boost productivity and efficiency of human's tasks.At The Korea Herald Biz Forum 2022, held at the Four Seasons Hotel Seoul on Wednesday, a senior official of Qraft Technologies said the AI engine can pursue above-average returns with the capability to analyze massive amounts of financial data and discover relationships that humans hardly find.
