ymgerman/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Citigroup Inc. (NYSE:C) stock is being bought by many value investing whales, even including Warren Buffett. Although the bank has historically been at the bottom of the Big Four, Citigroup has the most attractive valuation multiples and dividend. Furthermore, the company is exiting operations in many foreign countries, which will help the bank improve U.S. operations and return on tangible equity. While the company's investor day and exposure to Russia disappointed investors, CEO Jane Fraser is implementing promising strategies to fix both issues.
