MONTEVIDEO, Uruguay, May 31, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Arcos Dorados Holdings Inc. (NYSE: ARCO) ("Arcos Dorados" or the "Company"), Latin America's largest restaurant chain and the world's largest independent McDonald's franchisee, today announced its participation in the following investor events:

BofA Securities 2022 Emerging Markets Debt and Equity Conference. This in-person event will be held in Miami Beach, FL, from Wednesday, June 1 to Friday, June 3, 2022, and the Company will participate on June 1 and 2.
