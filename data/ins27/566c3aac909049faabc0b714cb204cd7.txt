Nazanin Zaghari-Ratcliffe to publish memoir about her years in jail in Iran
theguardian.com
sport
2022-10-18 09:45:58

Nazanin Zaghari-Ratcliffe, who spent six years in jail in Iran, is to write a memoir with her husband Richard Ratcliffe.The book, which is currently untitled, will tell the full story of Zaghari-Ratcliffe's imprisonment in Iran and her husband's campaign for her release.Zaghari-Ratcliffe was arrested in 2016 and separated from her daughter as she was about to board a flight home from Iran. She was in Tehran working as a charity project manager. She was accused of spying by Iranian authorities, an accusation she denied.
