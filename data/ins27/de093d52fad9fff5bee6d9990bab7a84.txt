Ankit Vengurlekar, the Director and Editor-in-Chief of CoinSwitch Kuber, a cryptocurrency exchange platform founded in 2017 in Bengaluru, joined the online rant on the city's ongoing ride-hailing problems, and the post is going viral.Sharing a message on Twitter on Wednesday, he said, "60 minutes of cab hunting and NO CAB! Absolutely terrible state of affairs in Bangalore. Hitched a ride with colleague. Got off on the main road. Now paying Rs. 500 for 9 kms in a riksha. What an awful city for commuting Bangalore is! Ghanta India ka silicon valley.
