How Elon Musk Could Change Twitter
cnet.com
news
2022-05-27 16:19:00

Tesla CEO Elon Musk wants the world to know change is coming to Twitter.In April, Twitter said it reached a deal with Musk, who will buy Twitter for $54.20 a share in cash, valuing the influential social media site at about $44 billion. The announcement came after Musk rejected a seat on Twitter's board. With a 9.2% stake in Twitter, Musk is one of the company's largest shareholders and will reportedly become temporary CEO once the deal closes, CNBC reported this month, citing anonymous sources.
