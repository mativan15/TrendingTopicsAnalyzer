A tablet is one of the coolest inventions of recent years – a slab of glass and aluminium with a display that responds to the touch.At its best, a tablet is intuitive to use, works as a useful second screen or, if it's good enough, the perfect portable display. It can be useful for everything from composing an email to playing games. Oh, and it's great for creativity, too.The Samsung Galaxy S7 Tablet FE 5G is powerful and effective for all of the above. To start with, there's a spectacular 12.4in display, bigger than many other tablet screens – and you can see the difference.
