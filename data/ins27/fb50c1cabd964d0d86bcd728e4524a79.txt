XPeng stock gains after May deliveries jump 78%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 10:30:25

The U.S.-listed shares of XPeng Inc. XPEV, +4.44% tacked on 1.4% in premarket trading Wednesday, after the China-based electric vehicle maker said it delivered 10,125 EVs in May, up 78% from a year ago. The deliveries included 4,224 P& sports sedans, 3,686 P5 family sedans and 2,215 G3 compact SUVs. The monthly deliveries increase follows a 75% jump in April. "The company resumed double-shift production at its Zhaoqing plant beginning in mid-May as supply chains and key manufacturing areas in China started to gradually recover," the company said in a statement.
