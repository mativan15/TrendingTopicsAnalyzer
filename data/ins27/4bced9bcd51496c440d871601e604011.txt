SOUTH COAST METRO, Calif., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Golden State Wealth Management is excited to announce the addition of Justin Peek, who operates under the DBA, PEEK WEALTHTM, and is the seventh advisor coming from Edward Jones. Justin serves as President of PEEK WEALTH and is a Certified Financial Planner®. The office is located in Carlsbad, CA and Justin has been providing the financial planning and investment needs of client families for nearly 20 years. He began his career in 2004 with Edward Jones.
