Glenmark Q4 Results: Net dips 26% to Rs 173 cr
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 06:15:00

ReutersThe company's consolidated revenue for last fiscal rose to Rs 12,305 crore from Rs 10,944 crore in 2020-21. on Saturday said its consolidated profit after tax declined 26 per cent to Rs 173 crore for the fourth quarter ended March 31, 2022. The company had reported a profit after tax (PAT) of Rs 234 crore in the January-March period of 2020-21 fiscal. Consolidated revenues of the drug firm, however, rose to Rs 3,019 crore in the quarter under review as compared to Rs 2,860 crore in the year-ago period, recording an increase of 6 per cent year-on-year.
