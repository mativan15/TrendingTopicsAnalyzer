NEW DELHI: There's a split emerging in India's refining sector as private refiners tap cheap Russian crude and boost profits from exports just as domestically focused state refiners get squeezed by high oil costs and government-capped domestic fuel prices.While many Western buyers are avoiding Russian crude in response to its invasion of Ukraine, private refiners such as Reliance and Nayara have been among the biggest buyers this year of discounted Russian supplies.
