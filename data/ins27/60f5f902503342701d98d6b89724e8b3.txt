India records 2,338 Covid cases, 19 more deaths in a day
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 05:15:00

India recorded 2,338 new Covid infections in a day that took its case tally to 4,31,58,087 while the number of active cases rose to 17,883, according to Union Health Ministry data updated on Tuesday. The death toll climbed to 5,24,630 with 19 more people succumbing to the viral disease, the data updated at 8 am stated. According to the ministry, active cases comprise 0.04 per cent of the total infections and the national COVID-19 recovery rate was 98.74 per cent. India's active caseload increased by 185 in 24 hours.
