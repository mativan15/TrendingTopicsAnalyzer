Shares of IT service provider have dropped as much as 8.7% to Rs 372.40 in the last three trading sessions after the company on Wednesday reported a 9.3% year-on-year (YoY) decline in its profit after tax (PAT) for the second quarter that ended September 30, 2022. The profit figure was lower than the Rs 2,820 crore anticipated in an ET NOW poll. Meanwhile, IT major's Q2 revenue rose 14.6% YoY to Rs 22,540 crore.
