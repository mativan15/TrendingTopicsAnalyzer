SAN JOSE, Calif., May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Firsthand Technology Value Fund, Inc. (NASDAQ: SVVC) (the 'Fund'), a publicly traded venture capital fund that invests in technology and cleantech companies, disclosed today that its top five holdings as of April 30, 2022, were IntraOp Medical, Revasum, Wrightspeed, Hera Systems, and Pivotal Systems.



1. IntraOp Medical Corp. is the manufacturer of the Mobetron, a medical device that is used to deliver intra-operative radiation to cancer patients.
