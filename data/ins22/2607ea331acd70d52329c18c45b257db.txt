BEIJING, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- RISE Education Cayman Ltd ("RISE" or the "Company") (NASDAQ: REDU) today announced its plan to change the number of the Company's shares represented by each American Depositary Share ("ADS") from two (2) shares per ADS to ten (10) shares per ADS (the "Ratio Change"). The effective date of the Ratio Change is expected to be June 10, 2022.
To effect this Ratio Change, ADS holders will be required to exchange their existing ADSs for new ADSs on the basis of one (1) new ADS for every five (5) existing ADSs surrendered.
