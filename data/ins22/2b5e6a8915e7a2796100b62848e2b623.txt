Food safety watchdog warns don't eat food past use-by date or turn off fridge

news
2022-10-20 11:00:00

The food safety watchdog has warned consumers against eating food past its use-by date or switching off the fridge and freezer after finding significant numbers are doing both to save money.The Food Standards Agency (FSA) said some consumers were taking risks with food safety because of money pressures and rising energy costs.A survey by the FSA found that 40% of consumers are worried about being able to afford food in the next month.Some 30% said they had skipped a meal or cut down the size of their meals over the past month because they did not have enough money to buy food.
