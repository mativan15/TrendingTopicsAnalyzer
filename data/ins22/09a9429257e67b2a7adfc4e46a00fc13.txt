Celtics tickets will be among the NBA's priciest this season
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 17:11:00

The Celtics' run to the NBA Finals last season may be making securing tickets this year even harder — and it's going to cost fans a lot of money.

A typical outing for four people to a Celtics game — even for TD Garden's cheapest seats, bought directly and not on the secondary market — will cost an average of $472 when other aspects are added in, including parking, a hot dog for each and a couple of beers and soda.
