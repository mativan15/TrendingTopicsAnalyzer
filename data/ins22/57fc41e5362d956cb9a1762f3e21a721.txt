New York, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The year 2022 is critical for furthering capital market reform. AMCAP Group Wealth Management Agency announced a mid-year meeting in mid-July to release the group's comprehensive investment plan. AMCAP stated that it will actively pursue market opportunities created by fundamental changes such as regional financial opening policies, as well as increase investment in fintech at the business and management levels. AMCAP Group believes that business level, wealth management, and institutional services will be the focus of future financial industry competition.
