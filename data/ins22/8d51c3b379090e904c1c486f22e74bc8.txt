Morning Brief Podcast (ET Bureau)

Kiran Kabtta Somvanshi | 30:59 Min | October 20, 2022, 8:00 AM IST

Can India develop a charity cult replicating the equity cult? That's what the Social Stock Exchange aims to achieve, listing social sector organizations and hoping for fundraising in an organized and transparent manner. But will charity really begin at home and via a Demat account, how will it work and what are the pitfalls? Host Kiran Somvanshi talks to Hemant Gupta, Managing Director - BIL Ryerson Technology Startup Incubator Foundation, Priya Naik, Founder and CEO at Samhita Social Ventures Pvt Ltd and Sanjeev Singhal, Chairman Auditing & Assurance Standards Board, ICAI.
