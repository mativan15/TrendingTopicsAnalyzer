SOUTH BEND, Ind., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Family Entertainment Television, Inc. announced today it has launched FETV and FMC on Philo's entertainment-focused streaming service."We value the relationship we have built with Philo and are excited to bring our family-friendly content to their subscribers," said Cara Conte, SVP of Affiliate Sales for FETV and FMC. "With our continued expansion of FETV and FMC among virtual providers, we look forward to building on our ratings momentum for both networks.
