Inside Chinese consulate that casts a shadow of fear across suburban street
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-10-21 21:23:42

Perhaps the first sign the gated townhouse isn't the same as the others lining this quiet street is the way that people noticeably quicken their pace as they walk past it. Then you see the electric fence sitting atop a tall perimeter wall, and the cameras – at least 30 – dotted at every angle along the entire boundary. Dare to peer through the tall conifers and trellis erected to stop prying eyes and you'll see a huge red Chinese flag flying at the top of a tall pole. In fact 71 Denison Road, an otherwise normal suburban street in Manchester's pleasant Rusholme district, is, as China's consulate general, officially Chinese territory – and that, for many people living nearby, is both disconcerting and frightening.
