Brisbane physio who sexually assaulted two women on same day sent to jail
smh.com.au
news
2022-06-02 05:14:25

A physiotherapist who sexually assaulted two clients on the same day in separate consultations at his practice in Brisbane's inner north has been jailed.

Scott Alexander Stuart Mackay caused 'caused irreparable damage to the trust of those two patients', the judge found. Credit:Facebook

Scott Alexander Stuart Mackay, 48, was found guilty by jury of two counts of sexual assault after a trial in Brisbane District Court this week.

Mackay was found not guilty of a count of rape.

The offences occurred in January 2019 at his physiotherapy practice in Windsor.
