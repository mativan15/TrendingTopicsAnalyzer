Pound, London stocks climb after Truss resignation
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 14:51:02

The London stock market and the pound bounced on Thursday after British Prime Minister Liz Truss announced her resignation following disastrous policies that rocked the markets for weeks.The pound traded around 0.4 percent higher against the dollar to $1.1273 after Truss ended six tumultuous weeks in power -- but analysts said gains were pared by the ongoing uncertainty.The FTSE 100 index was up 0.1 percent while the country's borrowing costs eased on the news, as the yield on 30-year government bonds, known as gilts, fell to 3.
