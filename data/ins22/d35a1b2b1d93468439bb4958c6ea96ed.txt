By Reuters Published: 21:35 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 21:35 EDT, 14 October 2022 By Doyinsola Oladipo NEW YORK, Oct 14 (Reuters) - Apple Inc retail workers voted in favor of forming a union at an Oklahoma location, the U.S. National Labor Relations Board (NLRB) said, making it the tech giant's second U.S. store to organize. Ninety-five employees at the Apple Penn Square store in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma secured a majority, with 56 votes in support of joining the Communications Workers of America Union (CWA), according to a tally by the NLRB.
