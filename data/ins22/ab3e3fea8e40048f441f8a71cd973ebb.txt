AgenciesRinkoo Singh Rahee, who was shot seven times for exposing corruption cases has cleared the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) 2021 exam in his last attempt.A government officer, named Rinkoo Singh Rahee, who was shot seven times for exposing corruption cases has cleared the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) 2021 exam in his last attempt. Rahee was posted in Muzaffarnagar as a Social Welfare Officer after clearing the Provincial Civil Service Examination () in 2009 where he exposed a corruption case.
