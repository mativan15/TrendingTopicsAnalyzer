CHENNAI: Allegedly harassed by three drunk men who demanded money, a truck driver from Uttar Pradesh mowed them down at a private truck parking yard in Red Hills, killing two and critically injuring another on Wednesday night.

Police investigations revealed that the deceased Kumaran, 36, and Kamala Kannan, 35, both from Red Hills, and the injured man, Naveen, 26, were consuming liquor at the truck yard at Vadaperumbakkam in Red Hills.
