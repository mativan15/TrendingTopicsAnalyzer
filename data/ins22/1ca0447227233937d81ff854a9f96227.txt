Investors in Ava Risk Group (ASX:AVA) have unfortunately lost 17% over the last year
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-27 20:13:10

Passive investing in an index fund is a good way to ensure your own returns roughly match the overall market. Active investors aim to buy stocks that vastly outperform the market - but in the process, they risk under-performance. Investors in Ava Risk Group Limited (ASX:AVA) have tasted that bitter downside in the last year, as the share price dropped 53%. That falls noticeably short of the market decline of around 5.2%. Longer term investors have fared much better, since the share price is up 9.
