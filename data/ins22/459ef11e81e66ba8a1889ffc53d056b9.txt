Three legends of reggae and ska music will kick off the 2022 season of First Tech Music in the Park in downtown San Jose's Plaza de César Chávez on June 17. The triple bill features three of Jamaica's most influential bands.
The Legendary Wailers headline, sharing the bill with Jamaican reggae-fusion band Third Worldand ska pioneers The Skatalites. Local reggae and soul DJ Marcus 'Nappy' Adams will mix it up between sets.
Fronted by Julian Junior Marvin, who toured worldwide with Bob Marley at the height of his success, the Legendary Wailers play and sing hit songs by Bob Marley and the Wailers in the traditional style.
