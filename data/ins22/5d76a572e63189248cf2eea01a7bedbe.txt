AgenciesThe MPC is set to announce its decision Wednesday.Mumbai: The Reserve Bank of India, being part of a global central bank chorus to rein in inflation, is certain to raise the policy interest rate this week, according to an ET poll. But the amount of increase is uncertain because it factors in moving parts like crude oil, government action on taxes and the impact of monsoon on food-grain output to predict future prices. The policy repo rate could be increased by 25-50 basis points, taking the rate at which the RBI lends to banks to as high as 4.
