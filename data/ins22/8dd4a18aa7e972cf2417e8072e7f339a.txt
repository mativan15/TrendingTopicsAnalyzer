Published: 08:04 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 08:06 EDT, 2 June 2022 With their crumbly texture and smeared with clotted cream and jam, scones are a favourite treat with Brits across the UK. But despite dating back to the early 1500s, one question remains – how do you pronounce the word 'scone'?Now, Amazon's smart assistant, Alexa, claims to have settled the debate, just in time for the Queen's Platinum Jubilee celebrations.   Alexa claims 'scone' should rhyme with 'gone' rather than 'own' when speaking the Queen's English.
