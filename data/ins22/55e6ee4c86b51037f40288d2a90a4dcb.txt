Dramatic pictures in the seaside village of Mevagissey, Cornwall show groups of unfazed punters continuing to eat and drink as if it is a regular night down the localShocking images show the extent of the flooding inside The Ship Inn, Mevagissey, CornwallA group of hardy pubgoers have been pictured eating their dinner in knee-deep water after their local was flooded following heavy rainfall. The Ship Inn in the seaside village of Mevagissey, Cornwall, has been dubbed 'Britain's Most Flooded Pub' due to its susceptibility to water damage, caused by a combination of heavy rains and high tides.
