Published: 07:03 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 09:28 EDT, 30 May 2022 MOSCOW (AP) - Russia said Monday it may use an arrangement similar to that used for payments for its gas supplies to pay its dollar-denominated foreign debts. The Vedomosti business daily quoted Finance Minister Anton Siluanov as saying that Russia will offer the holders of its Eurobond obligations to accept a payment system bypassing Western financial infrastructure.
