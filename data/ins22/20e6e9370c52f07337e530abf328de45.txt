October 20, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Carlin Consumer Health ('Carlin'), a preeminent consumer health business established by Hildred Capital Management, LLC, Bourne Partners Strategic Capital and The Emerson Group, today announced that Scott Chapman has been appointed Chief Executive Officer. Mr. Chapman joins Carlin with over 30 years of consumer products experience and a strong track record for successfully renovating existing brands, driving margin expansion and accelerating value creation in the over-the-counter ('OTC') space.
