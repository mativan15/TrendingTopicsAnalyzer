BMW Invests $1.7 Billion to Build EVs, Batteries in the US
cnet.com
news
2022-10-20 15:12:07

The revised rebate structure for electric-vehicle purchases places a big emphasis on manufacturing in the US -- and not just the cars themselves, but the batteries that power them, as well. To that end, BMW just announced a major investment that seeks to bring the EV assembly process as close together as possible.BMW this week announced a $1.7 billion investment in US electric vehicle manufacturing. Of that total, $1 billion will be dedicated to EV production through its existing Spartanburg, South Carolina assembly facility, which will get the upgrades it needs to build a whole lot of EVs; BMW hopes to build six fully electric BMW models in the US by 2030.
