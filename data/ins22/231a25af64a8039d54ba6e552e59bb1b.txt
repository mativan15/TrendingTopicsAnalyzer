In January, Audie Cornish announced she was leaving her 17-year career at NPR, including 10 years as the host of All Things Considered, to start anew as anchor and correspondent at CNN, where next month she will launch her new podcast, The Assignment with Audie Cornish. Here, she reflects on navigating newsrooms, the power of making space, and the joy of switching gears. Audie Cornish, CNN anchor and correspondent. CNN Jessica Pliska: At a pivotal moment in your career—having taken a big leap to CNN from NPR and launching your new podcast on November 17—can you reflect on your first job in journalism? 

Audie Cornish: It was at the Associated Press during 9/11.
