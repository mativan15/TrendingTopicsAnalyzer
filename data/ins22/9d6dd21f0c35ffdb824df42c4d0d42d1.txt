According to research by Vanda, retail investors have poured $400 billion into the stock market since 2020. This represents twice the number of equities they purchased in all recent years combined. Traditionally, retail investors who are financially vulnerable and risk-averse steered clear of risky asset classes and stuck to the 60/40 investment strategy. However, the scenario has now changed.
