Dave Clark, CEO of Amazon's worldwide consumer business and a top lieutenant of CEO Andy Jassy, will resign July 1 after 23 years at the company, Amazon announced in a regulatory filing Friday. Amazon did not name a replacement for Clark. In a blog post announcing his exit, Jassy said Amazon is in the process of firming up a succession plan for Clark, and will announce an update "over the next few weeks.
