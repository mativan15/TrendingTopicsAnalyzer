Euronet Worldwide: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
news
2022-10-21 09:02:36

LEAWOOD, Kan. (AP) _ Euronet Worldwide Inc. (EEFT) on Thursday reported third-quarter net income of $97.7 million.

On a per-share basis, the Leawood, Kansas-based company said it had net income of $1.87. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs and stock option expense, came to $2.74 per share.

The results beat Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of four analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.47 per share.

The electronic payments and transactions processor posted revenue of $931.
