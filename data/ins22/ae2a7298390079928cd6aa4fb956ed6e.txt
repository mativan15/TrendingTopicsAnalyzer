Kentucky EV battery manufacturer gets $480M in federal funding
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 10:53:00

Ascend Elements Inc. has been approved for $480.5 million in federal funding.

The producer of advanced, sustainable battery materials made from recycled lithium-ion batteries previously announced its intent to invest up to $1 billion in Southwest Kentucky, where it is breaking ground on a facility in Hopkinsville. The federal funding, announced Wednesday, comes through two federal grants from the U.S. Department of Energy, according to a news release.
