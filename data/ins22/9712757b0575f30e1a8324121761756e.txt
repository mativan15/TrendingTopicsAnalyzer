May 31, 2022 12:06 PM Eastern Daylight Time FORT WORTH, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--We are pleased to announce the City of Arlington Public works will become a new customer. They have chosen to participate through the Tarrant County COOP program. This program allows cities and other government entities to secure requisitions through the Tarrant County bid and participate at counties contracted prices.
