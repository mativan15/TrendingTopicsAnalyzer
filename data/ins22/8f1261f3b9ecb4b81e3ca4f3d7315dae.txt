IRVINE, Calif. (AP) _ Tilly's Inc. (TLYS) on Thursday reported fiscal first-quarter earnings of $813,000.On a per-share basis, the Irvine, California-based company said it had profit of 3 cents. The results met Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of four analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was also for earnings of 3 cents per share. The clothing and accessories retailer posted revenue of $145.8 million in the period, which topped Street forecasts. Four analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $145.
