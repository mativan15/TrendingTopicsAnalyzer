Published: 23:51 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 23:51 EDT, 20 October 2022 Rachel Zegler teased fans with a preview of the new Hunger Games movie set on Thursday.The West Side Story star, 21, was featured in a TikTok that showed the action behind the scenes of the upcoming prequel to the popular Hunger Games franchise, titled The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes.The clip shows Rachel - who plays the lead character Lucy Gray Baird, a tribute from District 12 - walking through the set and showing off some of the cast, including Peter Dinklage, 53, and Tom Blyth, 27, in character.
