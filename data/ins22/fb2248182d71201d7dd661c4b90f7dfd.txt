For businessman, learning is a lifelong pursuit that chases depression away

By HU MEIDONG in Fuzhou and ZHANG XIAOMIN | CHINA DAILY | Updated: 2022-06-04 10:20 Wu Chia-ying, president of the Taiwan Businessmen Association in Xiamen, Fujian province. [Photo provided to China Daily] For Wu Chia-ying, president of the Taiwan Businessmen Association in Xiamen, Fujian province, learning is a lifelong endeavor and has helped him recover from depression. The 62-year-old has earned five master's degrees and two doctorates from six well-known universities, including Fudan University, Xiamen University and the University of Hong Kong.
