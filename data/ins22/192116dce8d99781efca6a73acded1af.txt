Athletics - 2022 European Championships - Olympiastadion, Munich, Germany - August 18, 2022 Britain's Eilish McColgan celebrate after winning bronze in the women's 5000m final REUTERS/Kai PfaffenbachOct 19 (Reuters) - Eilish McColgan's latest European and British 10,000m records set at the Great Scottish Run earlier this month have been invalidated after the Glasgow course was found to be 150m short, organisers said on Wednesday.Commonwealth Games champion McColgan had finished the Oct. 2 race in 30 minutes and 18 seconds but organisers said the course was "not laid out in line with the previously agreed plans" and apologised to the Scottish runner as well as the participants.
