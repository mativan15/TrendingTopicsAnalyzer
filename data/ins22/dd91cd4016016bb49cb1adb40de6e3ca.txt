NBA Seeks to Curb Fouls in Transition After 1,700 Hacks Last year
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 18:01:37

An NBA game has 48 minutes of live action basketball, but from tipoff to the final buzzer, games average 133 minutes. Most additional time is necessary for player rest, business purposes and the like, but not all. Last season, the NBA eliminated automatic out-of-bounds replays in the final two minutes, resulting in slightly faster endings to games on average.

'We're not targeting a specific game length, but there's always an opportunity to increase that ratio of action to dead time by decreasing the frequency and length of stoppages,' NBA executive vice president of basketball strategy and analytics Evan Wasch said.
