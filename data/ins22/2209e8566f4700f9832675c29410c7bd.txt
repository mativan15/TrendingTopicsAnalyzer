[Photo/Xinhua] The General Office of the State Council, China's Cabinet, recently promulgated a guideline aimed at further activating stock assets to expand effective investment. China has accumulated a large amount of stock assets in transportation, water conservancy, urban construction, warehousing and logistics after years of investment and construction.
