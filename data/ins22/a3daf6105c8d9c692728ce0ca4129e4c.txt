CINCINNATI, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- LuckyTruck, the tech-enabled retail insurance agent focused exclusively on trucking, announced today it raised a $2.4m seed extension. Led by Candid Insurance Investors, an angel network of top P&C insurance industry CEO's, this round brings LuckyTruck's total funding to $6.5m.Parker Beauchamp of Markd, an insurance focused venture capital firm, made a significant contribution to the round as well.
