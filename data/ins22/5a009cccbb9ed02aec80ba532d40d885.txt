 Shares of Axis Bank rallied over 9 per cent on Friday after the firm reported a 66.29 per cent jump in consolidated net profit for the September quarter. The stock jumped 8.96% to settle at ₹900.25 apiece on the BSE. During the day, it advanced 9.53 to its 52-week high of ₹905. On the NSE, it rallied 9.46% to end at ₹904 apiece. Its market capitalisation (mcap) also jumped ₹22,757.
