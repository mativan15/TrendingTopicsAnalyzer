The love for SS Rajamouli's RRR is pouring out from all corners of the world, and the latest one to join the bandwagon is Daniel Kwan, the Asian American filmmaker known for co-directing and writing  Everything Everywhere All at Once. Not only did he show his love for the magnum opus, but he also showered praises upon the star of the film Ram Charan. TwitterDaniel Kwan Praises RRRDaniel Kwan watched the movie only recently and he said it was worth the hype. "HOLY COW ya'll weren't kidding. Anytime I watch an Indian action film I feel like I'm working in the wrong country," he wrote.
