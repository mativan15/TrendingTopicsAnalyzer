BBMP for data sharing, drone survey to boost property tax collection; to stop drum beating If you have not paid property tax, then beware, the Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) is now verifying it from Bangalore Electricity Supply Company (BESCOM).
 In a first, the BBMP has tied up with BESCOM to share data on residential properties. Recently, during data verification, BBMP officials found that a plot in Koramangala was shown as vacant land and hence the owner was paying a nominal property tax.
