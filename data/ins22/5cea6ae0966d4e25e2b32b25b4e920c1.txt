PALO ALTO, Calif., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- HP Inc. (NYSE: HPQ) ('HP' or the 'Company') announced today that it commenced a change of control repurchase offer to holders (the 'Holders') for any and all outstanding 4.750% notes due 2029 (the 'Notes') of the Company for cash (such offer to purchase, the 'Post-Acquisition Change of Control Repurchase Offer'). As previously announced, on August 29, 2022, HP completed its acquisition (the 'Acquisition') of Plantronics, Inc. ('Poly'). In connection with the Acquisition, HP completed its previously announced offer to exchange (the 'Exchange Offer') any and all outstanding notes of Poly (the 'Poly Notes') for up to $500,000,000 aggregate principal amount of the Notes.
