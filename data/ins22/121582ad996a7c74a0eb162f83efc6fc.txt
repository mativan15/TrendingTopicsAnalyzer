Pass the…cash? Over half of Gen Z and Millennials say they're financially stressed by Thanksgiving; nearly as many plan to ask guests to help cover costs of this year's feast.
GREENWOOD VILLAGE, Colo., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ --  Americans are preparing for a costlier holiday season as inflation continues to rise at historic rates. A new survey by Personal Capital reveals that 1-in-4 Americans plan to skip Thanksgiving this year to save money, and 42% plan to ask guests to contribute cash to help cover costs.
