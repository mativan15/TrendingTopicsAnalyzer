18 October 2022 by Tom Brueggemann 
Indiewire 'Decision to Kill' (Muni) grossed an impressive 96,000 in only three theaters this weekend. 'Terrifier 2' (Cinedigm/Iconic) went up 28 percent in its second weekend to take eighth place among all films, despite only being in 700 theaters. If you've heard about either film, part of the reason might be because of box office reporting.At an October 12 New York Film festival screening of 'Personality Crisis: One Night Only,' co-director Martin Scorsese decried box office coverage in the media.
