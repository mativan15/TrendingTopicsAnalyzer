Wilmington, Delaware, United States, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Transparency Market Research Inc. - TAED has emerged as an excellent active oxygen agent and has emerged as key ingredient in production of detergents and laundry bleaches. TAED can be degraded by deploying wastewater treatment process since product density is lower than water, a key advantage for businesses in end-use industries. Some of the end-use industries that use TAED include paper and pulp, meat, agriculture, and industrial cleaners.
