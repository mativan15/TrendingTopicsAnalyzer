October 20, 2022 08:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Ladder Capital Corp (NYSE: LADR) ('we,' 'Ladder,' or the 'Company') will release its third quarter 2022 results on October 27, 2022 after the close of markets that day. The Company will host a conference call and webcast for investors at 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time that day to discuss the financial results.

The conference call can be accessed by dialing (877) 407-4018 domestic or (201) 689-8471 international. Individuals who dial in will be asked to identify themselves and their affiliations.
