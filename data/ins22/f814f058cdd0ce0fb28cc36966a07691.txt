The Directorate of Enforcement (ED) on October 19 carried out search operations under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA), 2002 at five premises in Bengaluru in an investigation relating to the Chinese Loan App Case. The premises of Razorpay Pvt Ltd and the bank's compliance offices related to said entities were covered in the search operation. 'An amount of Rs 78 Crore has been seized u/s 17(1) in merchant IDs and bank accounts of these Chinese persons-controlled entities. Total seizure, in this case, now stands is Rs 95 crore,' ED said.
