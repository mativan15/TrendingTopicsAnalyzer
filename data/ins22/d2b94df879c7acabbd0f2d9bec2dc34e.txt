Kantara Becomes The Third Highest-Grossing Kannada Film; Surpasses Sudeep Kiccha's Vikrant Rona
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 05:18:47

The box office is enjoying its best days and better footfalls as Kantara is reaching heights. Unexpectedly, Rishab Shetty's latest Kannada film has broken numerous records and has entered the list of the top 3 highest-grossing films ever made in Kannada. The way Kantara is doing within just three weeks of its release, no doubt the top 2 spots are shaken. Deccan HeraldIf trade sources are to be believed, till October 19th, Kantara saw a worldwide gross collection of over ₹170 crore that includes ₹150 crore plus from India alone.
