Albuquerque, NM, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- In its ongoing efforts to attract top tier businesses to the Albuquereque region, AREA is celebrating the decision by Manna Capital Partners to invest more than $2 billion in the construction of an aluminum can sheet rolling mill and recycling center in Valencia County. The group has signed a letter of intent to purchase a 1,300-acre parcel in Los Lunas with construction set to start by late 2023 and manufacturing in 2026.'AREA received the initial inquiry from the firm, and subsequently played a critical role in the site selection process and provided key assistance to the investors.
