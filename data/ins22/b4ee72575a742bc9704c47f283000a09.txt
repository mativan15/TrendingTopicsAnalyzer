Central Pacific Bank reports Q3 earnings
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-22 00:56:00

Central Pacific Financial Corp., parent company of Central Pacific Bank, announced its third quarter earnings Friday.

The financial institution reported a net income for the third quarter of 2022 of $16.7 million, or fully diluted earnings per share of $0.61. This is compared with the previous quarter's net income of $17.6 million, or fully diluted earnings per share of $0.64, as previously reported by Pacific Business News.

"It was a good strong quarter," Central Pacific Bank Chairman and CEO Paul Yonamine told PBN.
