Credit Suisse Investment Bank Attracts Abu Dhabi, Saudi Interest
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-17 14:46:14

Bloomberg News

Abu Dhabi and Saudi Arabia are weighing whether to put money into Credit Suisse Group AG's investment bank and other businesses to take advantage of depressed values, according to people with knowledge of the matter, Bloomberg News reports.

The oil-rich emirate and its Gulf neighbor are separately exploring potential investments through sovereign wealth funds such as Abu Dhabi's Mubadala Investment Co. and Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund, the people said, asking not to be identified as talks are private.
