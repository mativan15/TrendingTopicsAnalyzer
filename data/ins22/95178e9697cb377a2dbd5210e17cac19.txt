CALGARY, ALBERTA, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Marksmen Energy Inc. ('Marksmen' or the 'Company') (TSXV: MAH) announces the granting of stock options to purchase 1,760,000 common shares of the Company to a consultant subject to regulatory and TSX Venture Exchange approval. The options were issued with an exercise price of $0.085 per share, vest immediately and have a one-year term from the date of issuance. For additional information regarding this news release please contact Archie Nesbitt, Director, and CEO of the Company at (403) 265-7270 or e-mail ajnesbitt@marksmenenergy.
