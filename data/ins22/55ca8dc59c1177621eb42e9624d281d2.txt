A devout Christian urged her friend to sell her dilapidated home for £4 million and enjoy the money – before being allegedly killed and decapitated by her, a court has been told.Mee Kuen Chong, 67, who was known as Deborah, was allegedly murdered by Jemma Mitchell after changing her mind about helping to bankroll her renovation project.Prosecutors say she was killed at her home in Wembley, north-west London, with her headless body dumped in woods near Salcombe in Devon last June.Police later recovered a forged will leaving the bulk of Ms Chong's estate to 38-year-old Mitchell, who she met through church, jurors at the Old Bailey previously heard.
