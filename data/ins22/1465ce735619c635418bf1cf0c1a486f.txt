Genuine Parts: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

news
2022-10-20 11:03:28

ATLANTA

Genuine Parts Co. (GPC) on Thursday reported third-quarter earnings of $312.4 million.

On a per-share basis, the Atlanta-based company said it had net income of $2.20. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs and pretax expenses, came to $2.23 per share.

The results exceeded Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of six analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.03 per share.

The auto and industrial parts distributor posted revenue of $5.68 billion in the period, also surpassing Street forecasts.
