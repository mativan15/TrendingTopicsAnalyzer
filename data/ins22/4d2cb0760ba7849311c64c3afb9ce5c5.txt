FORMER Reading youth star Kynan Isaac has been handed a TEN-YEAR ban by the FA.
The 31-year-old has reportedly been found guilty of spot-fixing and placing illegal bets on an FA Cup game that he played in last season.

Isaac plays for seventh-tier side Stratford Town but after being informed of his ban today, according to the Daily Mail, his career is effectively over.
