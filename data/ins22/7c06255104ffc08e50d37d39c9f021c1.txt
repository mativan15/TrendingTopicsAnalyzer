In Her Own Words: Paula Blankenship paints customer loyalty

2022-10-21 12:53:00

Even businesses that faced growing demand during the pandemic had to deal with financial challenges and supply chain obstacles. A shortage of quality jars was a near disaster that turned into a lesson on customer loyalty for Paula Blankenship and her colleagues at Heirloom Traditions Paint.

"I'm sure you were like me when you first heard about Covid, it can't be that bad. Our business, Heirloom Traditions Paint, was in a serious period of growth in 2019 and we moved the business to a small town outside of the city where we had land for needed expansion in February 2020.
