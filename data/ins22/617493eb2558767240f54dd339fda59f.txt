VC investing is at a two-year low—but there are a few bright spots
fortune.com
business
2022-10-20 10:45:42

Venture hit a two-year low at the end of September as the third quarter rolled to a halt. But the slowdown isn't hitting everyone the same. For one, fundraising is on track to hit a new record by the end of 2022—meaning limited partners don't seem too dissuaded by valuations coming down (whether it be by formal downrounds or pro-active portfolio markdowns). Sectors like energy and health care are offering some resiliency (woohoo!). But many later-stage, unprofitable companies are struggling to adjust to the market's new priorities.
