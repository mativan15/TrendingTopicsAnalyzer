June 03, 2022 01:02 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--YuJa, Inc., a leader in educational technology solutions for enterprises of all sizes, announces a 'Price-Match Promise,' a new offer to meet or beat the value and price of other video and media content management solutions.



YuJa provides a suite of ed-tech solutions ranging from lecture capture to media management, live streaming, video conferencing, digital asset management, video test proctoring, digital media accessibility, digital archiving and compliance and real-time audience response systems.
