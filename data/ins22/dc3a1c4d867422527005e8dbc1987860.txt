May 31, 2022 11:58 AM Eastern Daylight Time MONTRÉAL--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Emilio B. Imbriglio, MBA, member of HGreg's Board of Directors, was awarded the Prix MBA Hommage from the Association des MBA du Québec during the latter's 40th annual gala, held on May 11. Mr. Imbriglio was the first to receive this honour as a tribute to his overall career, his involvement with the Association and his years spent teaching.



Emilio B. Imbriglio is recognized for his success in the business world, his expert advice on governance and strategy, his long list of accomplishments and awards, and his service on the boards of multinational corporations, innovative companies, and non-profit organizations.
