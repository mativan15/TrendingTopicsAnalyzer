Co Kildare referee jailed for abusing teenage boy
irishtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

A sports referee from Co Kildare has been jailed for three years for sexually abusing a boy.Robert Kennedy (45), Allenview Heights, Newbridge, pleaded guilty at Naas Circuit Court to seven charges including possession of child pornography, use of information technology to facilitate sexual exploitation and meeting a child for the purpose of sexual exploitation.At a previous court hearing, Det Garda Shane Donnelly said the victim was aged between 15 and 17 years at the time of the offences, which were alleged to have taken place on dates between March 2018 and April 2019.
