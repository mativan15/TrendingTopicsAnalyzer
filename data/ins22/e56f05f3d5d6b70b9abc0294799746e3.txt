"There are some very positive changes in the proposed charter, and I encourage residents to read the explanatory text and understand what they are." (Patch Graphics)I think most residents zone out when they hear the words "charter reform". It's not a very exciting topic, but one I believe is critical for residents to pay attention to. Fairfield's Town Charter has changed a great deal over the last few decades and our charter has not. As our governing document, it's important that it be updated to reflect our community.
