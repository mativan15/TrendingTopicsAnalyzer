How expensive is it to be a medical aspirant? Parents of medical aspirants are shelling out up to Rs. 80,000 for their children to study at elite coaching institutes backed by multi-billion dollar brands. But do these high prices guarantee students receive a quality education? Or are parents and students being deceived by branding? NEET UG is a highly lucrative business attracting more and more entrepreneurs and with over 18 lakh students applying for the competitive exam each year, the market is vastly expanding.
