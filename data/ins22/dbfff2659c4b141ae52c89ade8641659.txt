Stanford Law School Launches Neukom Center for the Rule of Law
stanford.edu
news
2022-10-21 20:00:00

Gift from Sally and SLS Alum Bill Neukom includes support for an endowed chair and faculty fellows; Center to collaborate with World Justice Project and the Wright Center for the Study of Computation and Just Communities at Dartmouth 
Sally and Bill Neukom at the dedication of the William H. Neukom Building at Stanford Law School
October 20, 2022 – Stanford Law School (SLS) announced the launch of the Sally B. and William H. Neukom Center for the Rule of Law, thanks to a significant gift from the Neukom family.
