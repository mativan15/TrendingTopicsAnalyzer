An inquest in Plymouth heard how Ava-Rose Dean died at just 51 days old while she was sleeping in her mother's arms having been suffering with a coldAn inquest in Plymouth her baby Ava-Rose's death was unexplainedA grieving mother has described the heartbreaking moment her baby daughter died in her arms at just 51 days old. Ava-Rose Dean's death remains unexplained after her parents found her unresponsive while she was sleeping in Sara Collington's arms in September 2020. An inquest held in Plymouth heard the youngster suffered no physical injuries and there were no suspicious circumstances following her death which was put down to natural causes, Plymouth Live reports.
