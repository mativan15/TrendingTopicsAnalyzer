New variant of TVS Raider with SmartXonnect launched at Rs 99,990 in India
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 11:00:00

Two-wheeler maker, TVS Motor has announced the launch of a new top-of-the-line varant of Raider motorcycle. Priced at Rs 99,990, the new TVS Raider 125 comes with SmartXonnectTM technology and a segment-first 5-inch TFT console. The 5-inch TFT console employs an app to connect the user to the motorcycle. This Bluetooth-enabled gadget displays various riding statistics to assist riders in analysing their riding and riding style. The motorcycle also features an incoming call feature, voice and navigation assistance, image transfer choices, and ride reports.
