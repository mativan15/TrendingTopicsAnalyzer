June 01, 2022 12:59 PM Eastern Daylight Time PITTSBURGH--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PPG (NYSE: PPG) today announced that Joe Gette, currently assistant general counsel, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), and securities, will become vice president, deputy general counsel and secretary, effective June 1. Gette will continue to report to Anne M. Foulkes, PPG senior vice president, general counsel and current secretary.



In this new role, Gette will assume corporate secretary responsibilities while continuing to provide oversight and support for the company's global M&A activities.
