SEOUL, South Korea, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- AUTOCRYPT, a world-leading provider of cutting-edge automotive cybersecurity and mobility solutions, draws industry recognition for its inclusive mobility services solution – AutoCrypt EQ – as it was announced as "Ride Hailing Innovation of the Year" at the 2022 AutoTech Breakthrough Awards, held by AutoTech Breakthrough, a leading independent market intelligence organization specializing in the automotive tech industry. In both 2020 and 2021, AUTOCRYPT was AutoTech Breakthrough's choice for "Automotive Cybersecurity Company of the Year".
