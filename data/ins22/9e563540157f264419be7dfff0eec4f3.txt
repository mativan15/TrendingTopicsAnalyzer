NEW YORK, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Center Coast Brookfield MLP & Energy Infrastructure Fund (NYSE: CEN) (the 'Fund') today announced the amount of the June 2022 quarterly distribution. Based upon the recommendation of Brookfield Public Securities Group LLC ('PSG'), the Fund's adviser, and its portfolio management team, the Board of Trustees declared the Fund's quarterly distribution for June 2022 at a rate of $0.225 per share.
