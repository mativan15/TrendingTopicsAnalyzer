Getting employees back to work needs creative thinking, not enforcement
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 19:30:00

Workplaces are morphing. Digital Workplace CEO Paul Miller spoke about digital workplaces in 2019 as an evolution of the ecosystem from the industrial age mentality of work being suffering, to one that's more fluid and flexible, built around a work ethic encouraging passion, fulfilment and enjoyment. The pandemic accelerated digital workplace transformation, led by the belief that aggregations of people would rapidly increase its spread. With work from anywhere (WFA) becoming the norm, companies significantly trimmed their office footprint, presuming lower occupancies at any given time.
