Omar Ayyash

President, World Trade Center-Kentucky

Years in current job: Less than one

Years in the nonprofit sector: 27

Briefly describe what your organization does.

World Trade Center-Kentucky (WTC-KY) was founded and headquartered in Lexington, Fayette County, in 1988, over 30 years ago. Our mission is to accelerate international trade and enhance economic development. The WTC-KY serves as the regions' primary resource for international trade education, in addition to providing technical and general training on importing and exporting from small to large Kentucky companies.
