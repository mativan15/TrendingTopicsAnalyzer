Blue Apron Shares Up Almost 10% as Meal Kits Made Available on Walmart.com
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 15:18:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Blue Apron Holdings Inc. were trading higher on the New York Stock Exchange Thursday, following news its meal kits will be available for sale on Walmart.com. Blue Apron said it is the first and only meal-kit provider on the Walmart marketplace, adding customers will be able to buy meal kits without a subscription. At 11:12 a.m. ET, Blue Apron shares were trading 9.74% higher at $3.36. Year to date, Blue Apron's shares are down over 50%. Write to Stephen Nakrosis at stephen.
