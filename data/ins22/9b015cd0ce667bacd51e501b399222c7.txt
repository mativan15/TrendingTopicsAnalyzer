U.S. Manufacturing Growth Hits 2-Year Low: Is a Recession Imminent?
fool.com
news
2022-10-19 14:00:24

At this point, consumers are increasingly coming to terms with the idea of a recession battering the economy in 2023. Not only is soaring inflation draining consumers' finances, but the Fed's interest rate hikes to cool inflation have the potential to lead to a widespread slowdown in spending. And in another sign that a recession could be imminent, U.S. manufacturing growth recently reached a two-year low, according to the The Institute for Supply Management.If you're worried about a recession, it pays to do what you can to prepare for one.
