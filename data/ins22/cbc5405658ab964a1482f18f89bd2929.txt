Agrify Announces 1-For-10 Reverse Stock Split
wdrb.com
news
2022-10-17 16:34:00

BILLERICA, Mass., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Agrify Corporation (Nasdaq:AGFY) ('Agrify' or the 'Company'), a leading provider of innovative cultivation and extraction solutions for the cannabis industry, today announced that the Company's Board of Directors approved a 1-for-10 reverse stock split, to be effective as of 12:01 a.m. on Tuesday, October 18, 2022. The Company's common stock will open for trading on the Nasdaq Capital Market on Tuesday, October 18, 2022 on a split-adjusted basis under the current trading symbol "AGFY.
