K-pop boy band BTS members will serve mandatory military service, starting with its oldest member Jin, their agency said on Monday.BTS's oldest member Jin will turn 30 in December, making him the first to face military service Globally recognised celebrities in South Korea are already allowed to delay their service until they turn 30The group announced a hiatus in June "All other members will also serve mandatory military duty according to their plans," their management group HYBE said in a regulatory filing.
