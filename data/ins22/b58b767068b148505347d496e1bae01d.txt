Alpha FX Group Appoints Tim Powell as New CFO From Dec. 1
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 08:10:00

By Ian Walker Alpha FX Group PLC said Wednesday that it has appointed Tim Powell as chief financial officer, replacing Tim Kidd who is retiring as previously announced. The provider of foreign-exchange risk-management solutions said Mr. Powell will take up the role on Dec. 1, when Mr. Kidd will step down, but remain with the company until March as part of a transition agreement. Mr. Powell has more than 20 years of experience working in public companies, of which 17 years were spent at the London Stock Exchange Group PLC, the company said.
