JamesBrey/E+ via Getty Images One of the more intriguing REITs on the market today is a company called Equity LifeStyle Properties (NYSE:ELS). With a focus on what management calls lifestyle-oriented properties, the business represents a different kind of asset compared to what many REIT investors might be accustomed to. With a large nationwide network of properties spanning 35 states, as well as British Columbia in Canada, and an attractive record for growing both its top and bottom lines, this is truly a high-quality operator.
