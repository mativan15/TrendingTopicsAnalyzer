Ban on U.S. Talent at China Chip Firms Thwarts Xi's Key Ambition
bqprime.com
business
2022-10-17 11:13:24

(Bloomberg) -- China will speed its efforts to build a legion of talent and win the battle to develop homegrown technologies, President Xi Jinping pledged at the Communist Party's twice-a-decade congress on Sunday. But new U.S. restrictions issued a week earlier are already undercutting those plans.The Biden administration's latest salvo of sanctions includes restrictions on so-called U.S. persons supporting the development, production or use of integrated circuits at some chip plants located in China.
