Stock Futures Edge Up to Start the Month

2022-06-01 07:40:00

Photo: Michael Nagle/Bloomberg News June 1, 2022 3:40 am ET 
 U.S. stock futures edged up on the first trading day of June, ahead of a raft of data including job openings and the Federal Reserve's latest Beige Book on economic conditions. Futures on the S&P 500 were up 0.3% and futures tied to the Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 0.5%. The contracts don't necessarily predict movements after the markets open.
