Victoria has its own site run by the state government called compare.energy.vic.gov.au. There, you can check prices and also register for a reminder on July 1 to apply for a $250 power-saving bonus, which every household in the state is eligible to receive. Or, you can just set your own calendar reminder to apply. Really; do it now.For people in every other state and territory, there is the federal government's website energymadeeasy.gov.au.Perhaps it could be more aptly called 'energy made easier' because it does take a little getting used to.
