Gabriel Zarate crosses the border to work in San Diego as an English teacher during the day and returns home in the evening to Mexico
While many migrants risk their lives chasing the American dream, Gabriel Zarate fled the rising cost of living in California and moved to the Mexican border city of Tijuana.The 38-year-old Chilean American now crosses over to work in San Diego as an English teacher during the day and returns home in the evening to Mexico."One of the biggest reasons is the cost of living in Tijuana.
