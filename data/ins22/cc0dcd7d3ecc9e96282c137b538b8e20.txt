Imran Khan's party Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) caused a major upset to the ruling coalition in the country led by Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif by winning six National Assembly (NA), and two Punjab Assembly (PA) seats in by-elections. The PTI was contesting from as many as seven constituencies in the Pakistan NA, and three in the PA. It won the seats in Mardan, Charsadda, Faisalabad, Nankana Sahib and Peshawar in the NA after defeating the ruling coalition's candidates. In Punjab, the party won in Khanewal, Sheikhupura and Bahawalnagar, Geo News reported.
