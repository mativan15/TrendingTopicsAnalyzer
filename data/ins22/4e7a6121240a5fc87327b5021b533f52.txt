Kenmare Resources plc ('Kenmare' or 'the Company') 26 May 2022 Report on payments to governments 2021 Introduction This report details payments to governments made by Kenmare Resources plc (the 'Company') and its subsidiary undertakings (the 'Group') for the financial year ended 31 December 2021. This report has been prepared in accordance with the requirements of Part 2A of the Transparency (Directive 2004/109/EC) Regulations, 2007 (as amended) ('Part 2A'), Part 26 of the Companies Act 2014 ('Part 26') and rule DTR 4.
