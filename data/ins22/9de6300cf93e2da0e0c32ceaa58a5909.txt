With tough schedule to start NBA season, Raptors hope roster continuity will pay off
thestar.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

The Raptors stumbled out of the gate to begin the 2021-22 NBA season — 'we didn't know who we were,' has been coach Nick Nurse's near constant pre-season refrain — so they hope continuity and the hard lessons learned allows them to avoid another slow start.

It had better because the schedule is not kind.

Cleveland to open the season, three road games in four nights (Brooklyn and two in Miami), two against Philadelphia and then Atlanta; it's an Eastern Conference gauntlet they must run.

'We saw that last year,' Pascal Siakam said.
