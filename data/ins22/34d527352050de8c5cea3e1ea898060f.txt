May 27, 2022 08:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time REDWOOD CITY, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Zuora, Inc. (NYSE: ZUO), the leading cloud-based subscription management platform provider, today announced that its Chief Financial Officer, Todd McElhatton, will participate in a fireside chat during the Jefferies Software Conference on Thursday, June 2, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. Pacific Time (12:00 p.m. Eastern Time).

The fireside chat will be webcast live and available to replay on Zuora's investor relations website at https://investor.
