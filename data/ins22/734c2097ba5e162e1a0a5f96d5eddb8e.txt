Plant-based salmon fillet produced with 3D Technology

The Austrian 3D- and Food-Technology Startup, Revo Foods has presented an ultrarealistic plant-based salmon fillet for the first time in a public tasting event at Rooftop Bar ZOKU Vienna

VIENNA, Austria, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The Austrian 3D- and Food-Technology Startup, Revo Foods has presented an ultrarealistic plant-based salmon fillet for the first time in a public tasting event at Rooftop Bar ZOKU Vienna on the 30th of May, 2022.
