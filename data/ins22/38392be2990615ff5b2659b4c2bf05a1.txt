THE Canary Islands are your safest - and cheapest - option if you're looking for some winter sunshine. 
TUI has released hundreds of holidays to the Canaries for less than £60pp a night this winter. 
TUI has released hundreds of holidays to the Canaries for less than £60pp a night this winterCredit: Getty
The Canary Islands are made up of eight main islands, including Tenerife, Fuerteventura, Gran Canaria, Lanzarote, La Palma, La Gomera, El Hierro, and La Graciosa.
Even during winter, you can expect long days of sunshine and high temperatures of 23-25°C.
