Co-founder and CEO of the Copenhagen-based company Contractbook, supported by Gradient Ventures, Bessemer Venter Partners and Tiger Global. getty The funnel is broken. Maybe that's an over-exaggeration, but I got your attention. In the past months, I've noticed that at least something seems off with the traditional way SaaS companies get leads and expand their user base. For years, tech companies have relied on digital ads to acquire customers at a low cost. Their effect was easy to measure; they offered revenue teams the opportunity to be extremely data-driven; you could target very specific target audiences with tailored messages through microtargeted ads.
