Spice Girls' Geri Horner to publish new children's book series in 2023
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 15:25:30

Former Spice Girls star Geri Horner is set to publish a children's adventure book series in the UK and the US.
The pop icon, 50, revealed her hopes that the first book in the franchise, Rosie Frost and the Falcon Queen, can inspire young readers to find ‘strength and power' in the face of adversity.
The book, scheduled for publication in 2023, follows orphan Rosie Frost on a journey to a mystery island home to extraordinary teenagers and also a sanctuary for endangered species.
Discussing the plot, the Say You'll Be There singer said: ‘Rosie Frost has lived in my heart for a long time and this feels just the right moment to introduce her to the world.
