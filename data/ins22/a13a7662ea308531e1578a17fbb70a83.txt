Michael Vi/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Investment Thesis Zscaler, Inc. (NASDAQ:ZS) is the leader in zero-trust security. The company reported a robust Q3 card and raised guidance. However, its premium multiple has been digested significantly by the market. Coupled with its lack of GAAP profitability, the market reaction seems justified, despite its market leadership. We learned never to argue with the market, no matter your conviction level. One of the key learnings we have had with high conviction high-growth stocks is to set a reasonable stop loss level if the position becomes untenable.
