Many states now recognize the importance of apprenticeship programs in furthering educational and workforce training for the future growth of their communities. The California Department of Industrial Relations (DIR) and its Division of Apprenticeship Standards (DAS) recently awarded $7 million in state apprenticeship expansion and other states have already stepped up efforts. Last spring, Colorado's Governor Polis signed bills totalling $75 million for workforce training and a state apprenticeship agency.
