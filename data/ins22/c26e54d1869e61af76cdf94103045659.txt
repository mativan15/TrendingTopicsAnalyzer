Chelsea are already playing catch-up in terms of their summer business after shelving their recruitment plans because of the sanctions placed on Roman Abramovich. Todd Boehly's takeover means they can finally get to work on the rebuild Thomas Tuchel believes is necessary to challenge for the title next season. He has the backing of the new owner – and all eyes will be on the American in his first summer window in charge. Boehly could make up to £200million available – but he is new to the world of football and he would not be the first to be caught by surprise in his first foray into the market.
