When a masked person threatened millionaire real estate magnate Sylvester Zottola at gunpoint in November 2017, he survived.When three men broke into his residence almost a month later, stabbed him multiple times and slashed his throat, Zottola clung to life but made it.The attacks didn't end. In October 2018, Zottola was fatally shot at a McDonald's drive-through in the Bronx while waiting for his cup of coffee.Zottola, who was 71, owned multifamily rental properties valued at tens of millions of dollars at the time of his death.
