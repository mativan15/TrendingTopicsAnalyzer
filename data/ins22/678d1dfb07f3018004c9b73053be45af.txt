Stockpile Launches Kids Cryptocurrency Trading with 30 Coins
prweb.com
news
2022-10-20 13:45:00

SAN FRANCISCO (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Stockpile, the simplest way to invest in stocks, today announced kids crypto trading on its platform, making it the first and only place where kids can invest in 30 different cryptocurrencies. Stockpile is designed to help bring the future of finance to kids by providing an opportunity to make investing in crypto as easy to invest in as traditional stocks. 'We believe crypto is the inevitable future of currency,' said Stockpile CEO Victor Wang. 'It's important to us that we make it easy and accessible to everyone, especially kids.
