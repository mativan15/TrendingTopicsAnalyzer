A 22-year-old West Bengal native has been arrested on charges of cheating people of large sums of money through an online racket using the names of online shopping websites. The Thiruvananthapuram Rural police arrested Bicky Das of North 24 Parganas in West Bengal. He reportedly is part of a racket specialising in tricking people into giving money by promising them gifts using fake scratch-and-win cards issued in the names of companies such as Naaptol Online Shopping Pvt Ltd and Snapdeal, the police said.
