Infosys to sharpen focus on cloud, digital business: CEO Salil Parekh
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 09:00:00

Bengaluru: Infosys sees a huge opportunity in the cloud and digital business and will continue to focus on expanding its capabilities in these areas, chief executive Salil Parekh said on Tuesday. Parekh said the total market size of cloud and digital services globally is expected to jump to $830-$890 billion by 2027 from $410-$450 billion currently, clocking 14-16% annualised growth, despite ongoing concerns around inflation and the geopolitical tensions in Europe.'We will fine-tune some of that focus (on cloud and digital).
