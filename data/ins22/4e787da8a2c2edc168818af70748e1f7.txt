Singapore's Regulator To Explore Potential Of DeFi And Asset Tokenization
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-01 02:49:56

The Monetary Authority of Singapore launched "Project Guardian"MAS will test potential applications of asset tokenization and DeFi The first pilot will evaluate the wholesale funding marketsSingapore's financial regulator has collaborated with the finance industry to launch "Project Guardian," under which the city state looks to establish itself as a hub for decentralized finance (DeFi).According to Bloomberg, Deputy Prime Minister Heng Swee Keat said Tuesday that Singapore will test the potential applications of asset tokenization and DeFi and simultaneously work on managing risks to financial stability and integrity.
