Women gaining traction in far-right politics – DW – 10/22/2022
dw.com
news
2022-10-22 10:26:00

"I am a woman, I am a mother, I am a Christian!" Giorgia Meloni has repeated these words in countless public appearances — and they seem to be the essence of her political success. Following in the footsteps of men such as conservative politician Silvio Berlusconi, who attracted voters and dominated Italian politics with a decidedly macho attitude, Meloni's electoral success can also be attributed to her emphasis on her femininity. And now she has become the leader of Italy's new coalition government — even if the honeymoon between its partners already seems to be over.
