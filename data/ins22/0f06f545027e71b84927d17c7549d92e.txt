Treasury winds up £100m Covid fraud-busting squad
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

A Treasury anti-fraud squad set up to recover billions of pounds stolen from Covid-19 government support schemes is to be wound up having recovered hundreds of millions less than intended.The Taxpayer Protection Taskforce was set up with a £100 million budget from the Treasury in former chancellor Rishi Sunak's budget in March 2021. It involved 1,265 experts moving in with HM Revenue & Customs to try to get back cash stolen or wrongly claimed from the Treasury's emergency schemes.The Treasury was initially criticised by tax experts for setting expectations low.
