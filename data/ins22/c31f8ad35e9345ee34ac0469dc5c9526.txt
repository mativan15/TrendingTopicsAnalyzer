The KSRTC Staff and Workers' Federation and the All India Union of Trade Union Congress (AIUTC) have urged the State government to revise the payscale of the KSRTC employees by increasing the basic pay and merging Basic Dearness Allowance (BDA).Addressing presspersons in Kalaburagi on Friday, president of KSRTC staff and workers' federation Ananth Subbarao said that the federation along with AIUTUC and Karnataka Rajya Raste Sarige Naukarara Maha Mandali had passed 13 resolutions during its conference held in Kalaburagi on Thursday.
