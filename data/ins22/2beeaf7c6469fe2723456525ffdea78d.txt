Black Wall Street Black Business Expo kicks off this weekend

2022-06-04 16:14:26

The organization behind one of Atlanta's events is highlighting black-owned businesses and African American excellence.

[DOWNLOAD: Free WSB-TV News app for alerts as news breaks]

The Financial Literacy Institute will kick off the second annual Black Wall Street Expo Saturday, June 4 at 12 p.m. at the Westin Peachtree Plaza.

The expo allowed attendees to shop at Black-owned businesses, and enjoy food and entertainment for all ages.

The expo will also include a panel discussion of high-profile guests who will share ways of building financial literacy with steps to help circulate the Black dollar.
