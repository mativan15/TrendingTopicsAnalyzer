Peter Thiel is in the process of acquiring Maltese citizenship, The New York Times reported Saturday.

The Silicon Valley investor and Republican donor has American, German, and New Zealand passports.

The EU is trying to ban Malta's "golden passport" scheme, which grants recipients access to the EU.
