The data as of May 1 shows 826,000 people had been experiencing symptoms for at least a year. The most common symptoms include fatigue, shortness of breath, cough and muscle acheThe ONS said that the rates of long Covid were highest among women (Stock photo) A record two million Brits now have Long Covid as experts warn catching the virus carries a one in ten risk of developing it. The Office for National Statistics found 3.1% of the British population are suffering symptoms persisting for at least four weeks after catching Covid-19.
