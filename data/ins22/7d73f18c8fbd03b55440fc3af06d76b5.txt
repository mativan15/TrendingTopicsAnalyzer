Rail Vikas Nigam soars 5% as JV Bhartia receives LoA for Rs 560 crore project
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 06:00:00

iStockShares of Rail Vikas Nigam rose to a high of Rs 34 as against Rs 32.40 at the previous close on the BSEShares of shot up 4.9 per cent in early trade on Wednesday after the joint venture of the railway firm, Bhartia, received a Letter of Acceptance (LoA) from the Northeast Frontier Railway for the construction of single line BG tunnel. The total estimated cost of the project is Rs 560.26 crore. The LoA is regarding the construction of single line BG Tunnel No. 06 including escape Tunnel, cut and cover, earthwork in the bank and cutting to make formation, security barrack and other protection and ancillary works in connection with DMV-Kohima New BG Line Project by Northeast Frontier Railway.
