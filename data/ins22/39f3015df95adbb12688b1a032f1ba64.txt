Published: 12:04 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 15:27 EDT, 17 October 2022 Tory MP Johnny Mercer said Liz Truss laughed at him when she sacked him as Veterans Minister, adding that the move was a 'gut punch' that sent him into depression.He also said the Prime Minister had lost authority by turning the Conservatives into a 'libertarian' party and handing Labour a massive poll lead.The Plymouth Moor View MP said that in dismissing him, Ms Truss reneged on the promise she made during her Tory leadership campaign that veterans' affairs would not be downgraded under her Government.
