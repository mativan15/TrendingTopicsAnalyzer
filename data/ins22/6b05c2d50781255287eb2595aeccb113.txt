Vice President, Southeast Group at Harkins Builders, Inc.
bizjournals.com

2022-10-21 12:37:45

Harkins is pleased to announce the promotion of Josh Zilke to the position of Vice President of the Southeast Group.
Josh joined Harkins as a Project Executive 2020, bringing with him a significant amount of experience and local knowledge of the Carolina multifamily markets. Under his leadership, the Harkins Southeast team has grown to 30+ people, with a construction backlog built for substantial growth. Josh's ability to bring in work and build the Harkins team, as well as cultivate client and trade partner relationships is admirable.
