At least 50 people killed and 300 injured in Chad as protests for democratic change turn violent
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-21 05:40:36

At least 50 people have been killed and nearly 300 injured in protests in Chad as hundreds took to the streets to demand a quicker transition to democratic rule.The prime minister says it was an "uprising to seize power by force" and those responsible will face justice Human rights groups counter that unarmed civilians were massacred as security forces brutally cracked down on demonstrationsIt is the bloodiest riot since Mahamat Idriss Deby seized power after his father's death last yearPrime Minister Saleh Kebzabo said the government was compiling casualties from what he described as an armed insurrection.
