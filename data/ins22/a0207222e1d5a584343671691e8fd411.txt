Sep 26, 2022Football is funny sometimes. Two years ago -- to the week -- Garrett Shrader entered the transfer portal listed as a Mississippi State wide receiver.But that's not who Shrader is. He's a quarterback, going back to elementary school, when he told his dad that was the position for him. He grew into an elite recruit, winning two state championships in North Carolina, and yes, starting at quarterback in the SEC for the Bulldogs.Following a coaching change after the 2019 season, Shrader was asked to switch to receiver and play special teams in 2020.
