Even though shares have been hitting new 52-week lows every other day, brokerage firm has given a buy call on the stock for a target price of Rs 1,780, suggesting a potential upside of 57% as it believes the risk-reward ratio for the company would turn favourable for the medium-to-long term. On the beauty startup, anchor investors' lock-in period shall expire on November 10. The Street will watch whether the pre-IPO investors offload their positions or continue to hold. The brokerage is of the view that 67% of share capital or around 319 million shares of Nykaa are likely to open for trade on the lock-in expiry day.
