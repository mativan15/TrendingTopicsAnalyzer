Published: 18:23 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 18:28 EDT, 20 October 2022 There has been a mass exodus at Nova radio, with Chrissie Swan, Sam Pang and Jonathan Brown the latest to quit the station. The trio announced they were leaving their top-rating breakfast show Chrissie, Sam & Browny on Friday, after seven years on-air.'We have relied on each other in this studio and it's a good idea to put a bow on what has been this entire experience for all of us and bow out gracefully,' Chrissie said in an emotional statement.
