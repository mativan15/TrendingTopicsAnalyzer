Erode Textile Mall eyes B2B, B2C segments through large format stores: Official
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-04 14:15:00

Representative ImageChennai: Texvalley, owned by Erode Textile Mall Pvt Ltd and promoted by Lotus Group and construction firm URC Group, would be setting up its first 'mall' in Erode to serve business-to-business and business-to-consumer segments through large format stores, the company said on Friday. Texvalley has earmarked about 4 lakh sq ft of the total land measuring 20 lakh sq ft in Erode, to serve the business to customer segments in business and lifestyle in the region, a company statement said.
