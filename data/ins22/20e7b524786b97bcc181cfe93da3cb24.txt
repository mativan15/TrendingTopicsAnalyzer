DALLAS, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TreeNewal, a leading tree service company based in Dallas/Fort Worth, announced the rebranding of its logo along with an updated website effective July 31, 2022. Prompted by the commitment to being leaders in the tree service industry, the new website aims to provide the most up-to-date information and technology to better serve its customers and enable further business growth. The new company logo features the northern star, representing the faith of the leadership team at TreeNewal.
