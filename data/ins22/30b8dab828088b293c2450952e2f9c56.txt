Executive Insights: MSA and Mascaro Construction on raising junior achievers
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 16:43:00

Facing unprecedented labor challenges, fueled in part by escalating higher-education costs, a pandemic that choked supply chains, and the retirement of Baby Boomers, Pittsburgh companies such as MSA-The Safety Company and Mascaro Construction Company are turning to a less traditional approach to workforce development.

Partnering with the nonprofit Junior Achievement of Western Pennsylvania, these companies are helping to prepare young people early with educational programs aimed at teaching the value of — and opportunities in — pursuing hands-on jobs in advanced manufacturing and traditional building trades.
