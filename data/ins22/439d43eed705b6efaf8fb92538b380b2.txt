EV fire incidents: Probe panel to submit report on May 30
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-27 11:30:00

An expert committee set up by the road transport ministry to investigate the cases of electric two-wheelers catching fire and suggest remedial measures will submit its report next week, a senior government official said on Friday. Recently, there have been multiple incidents of electric vehicles (EVs) catching fire and resulting in deaths and severe injuries to people. "The expert committee (formed to enquire into the incidents of electric two-wheelers catching fire) will submit it's report on May 30," the official said.
