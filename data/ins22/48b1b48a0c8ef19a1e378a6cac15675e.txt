Ukraine to introduce electricity restrictions starting from Thursday: presidency
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 19:45:00

ReutersUkraine's President Volodymyr ZelenskiyUkraine will impose restrictions on electricity starting from Thursday, the country's presidency said on Wednesday after Russian missile and drone strikes demolished large parts of the country's power grid. "Today the enemy again destroyed energy generating facilities... it is necessary to minimise the use of electricity," said Kyrylo Tymoshenko, deputy head of the Ukrainian president's office.
