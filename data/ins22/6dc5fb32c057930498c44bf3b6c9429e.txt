By Reuters Published: 20:16 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 20:16 EDT, 17 October 2022 SEOUL, Oct 18 (Reuters) - South Korea's Hyundai Motor is considering a decision on its Russia operations that could include selling its manufacturing plant there, a local media reported on Tuesday. Hyundai Motor recently submitted to management a report analysing the situation and future prospects in Russia, Dong-a Ilbo newspaper said, citing an unidentified auto industry source. The newspaper said Hyundai's report includes the company's analyses of the scenarios and impact of the sale of the Russia plant, citing the difficult environment to conduct normal financial activities.
