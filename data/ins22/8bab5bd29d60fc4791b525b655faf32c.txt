Indiewire Television In its campaign against password sharing, Netflix has begun the global rollout of a feature that allows users to transfer profile data to a new account. In other words, if you're using a shared account that contains your profile with its rich viewing history and saved settings, Profile Transfer will let you move that data to a new account — one that you pay for.The Profile Transfer rollout begins on Monday and account owners will receive an email notifying them when the feature is available.
