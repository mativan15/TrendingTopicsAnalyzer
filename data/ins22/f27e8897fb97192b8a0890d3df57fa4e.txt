Gareth Farr is an award-winning composer and percussionist who was awarded an ONZM in 2006 for his services to music and entertainment. The 54-year-old is one of New Zealand's top composers, who has composed and performed here and internationally. Farr is the composer-in-residence at the World's Edge Festival in Central Otago; he is having seven works performed. (October 8-21). Farr also had a career as a drag queen, performing as the character Lilith LaCroix. He lives in Wellington with his partner, actor Salesi Le'ota.
