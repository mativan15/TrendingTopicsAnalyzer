U.S. stock futures dip as Treasury yields hit fresh 14-year highs, Tesla stock tumbles post results

U.S. stock futures pulled back on Thursday as investors warily eyed Treasury yields climbing to fresh 14-year highs. Also dragging tech stocks was a sharp drop in Tesla Inc. shares after disappointment over results.On Wednesday, the Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, -0.33% fell 100 points, or 0.33%, to 30424, the S&P 500 SPX, -0.67% declined 25 points, or 0.67%, to 3695, and the Nasdaq Composite COMP, -0.85% dropped 92 points, or 0.85%, to 10681. The Nasdaq Composite COMP, -0.85% is up 3.8% from its 2022 closing low but remains down 31.
