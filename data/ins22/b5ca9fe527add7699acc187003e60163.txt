Cleveland Guardians announce Division Series Game 4 sold out
mlb.com
news
2022-10-16 16:13:44

Cleveland, OH – The Cleveland Guardians today announced that American League Division Series Game 4 against the New York Yankees on October 16 is sold out*.
*Additional inventory including Standing Room Only tickets may be released for Game 4.
Game 4 of the ALDS is scheduled for Sunday, October 16 at 7:07 PM ET. Fans attending the game are encouraged to ‘Rock Your Red' at Progressive Field.
Postseason tickets are available online only. Ticket purchasers will be limited to six (6) tickets per game and all tickets will be delivered in digital form.
