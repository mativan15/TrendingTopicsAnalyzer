No Name vs brand name: How much do you actually save?
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-18 21:25:00

With Loblaws freezing prices on 1,500 of its no name products until the end of January, CTV News Ottawa wanted to see if it actually saves you money shopping no name over brand name. So we headed to the store to see if we can save money buying no name versus brand name with a grocery list with 10 generic foods that most families buy each week, including things like butter, pasta and waffles. After purchasing and loading the 10 no name items into the trunk, we went back into the store to buy the exact same items, but brand name versions.
