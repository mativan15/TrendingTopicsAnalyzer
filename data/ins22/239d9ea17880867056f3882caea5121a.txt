TBILISI, Georgia, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- According to developers, WEIcrypto is a complex system products, based on blockchain technologies and focused on the technological changes in the global financial system. The Georgian team has developed fundamentally new solutions which simplify access to the world of cryptocurrencies for individual customers and corporates. WEIcrypto combines several subbrands, including cryptocurrency WEI, online wallet and payment service WEIpay as well as the system of interest-free loans for business WEIpartner.
