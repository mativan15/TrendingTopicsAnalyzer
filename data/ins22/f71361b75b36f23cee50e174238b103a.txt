If you have sold a business for a significant financial profit, inherited a large sum of money or recently won a very large lottery, you might want to consider the merits of a 'family office' in order to manage your financial affairs. A reader, C.P., wants to know how to get started. While definitions and responsibilities can vary, as every family is unique, in general a family office is a firm designed to handle all things financial for the ultra-wealthy and their families – everything from investment management to bill paying, tax planning and funding charitable causes and educating multiple generations.
