Top 4 mistakes which investors should avoid in a rising stock marketThere is no better sight for stock market investors than seeing markets go high every day. A market rally augments wealth creation and propels returns. However, in the euphoria, most investors get carried away and end up committing mistakes that can hamper wealth creation. Just like it is crucial to take guard in a bear market, it is equally vital to avoid these common mistakes when markets are on the rise.
