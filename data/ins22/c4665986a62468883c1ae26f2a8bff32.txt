The Best Small Business CRM Software for 2022

Customer relationship management (CRM) platforms are powerful tools for contemporary sales forces, no matter the business size. Most large companies use CRM software, but the applications are less common in small businesses, especially microbusinesses and startups. If your organization fits this description, you might be able to get away with a contact management app for a while, but you needn't miss out on full CRM capabilities in the long term. There is no shortage of CRMs built specifically for small businesses that can help you unlock your full sales potential without bewildering you with enterprise features or sticker shock.
