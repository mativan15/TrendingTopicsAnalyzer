American Tower plans to shop for minority investor for U.S. data center business
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 11:15:55

American Tower REIT AMT, -1.39% said Thursday one of its subsidiaries entered into a non-binding term sheet that considers a third-party minority investment in its U.S. data center business of about $2.5 billion, according to a filing. The REIT also filed to sell 8.35 million shares to pay down debt after its $10.4 billion acquisition of data center company CoreSite which closed in December. Based on a closing price of $252.56 a share on Wednesday, the offering will raise about $2.1 billion. Shares of American Tower are down 13.
