getty Introducing new products and services is indispensable for an organization's growth and one of the main ways to enhance financial health and overall profitability. An innovative product, though, like any product, needs to have a target market and be aligned with the dynamics of that market. When it comes time to promote a new product, how will the consumer view its value? Does it check the latent demands of the consumer? Consumers, for example, did not show any need or desire for portable personal music devices until the first ones were produced, but the product's utility created the consumer's need and want for the item.
