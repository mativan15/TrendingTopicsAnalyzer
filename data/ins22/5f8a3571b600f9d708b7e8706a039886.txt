[Photo/Xinhua] Xi Jinping reiterated China's commitment to opening to the outside world in his report to the 20th CPC National Congress. It is of vital importance not only for attaining CPC's goal of China great rejuvenation, but also for upholding globalization and multilateralism under the current mounting geopolitical tensions and de-globalization trends.
