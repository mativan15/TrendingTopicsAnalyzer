UK to invest £25bn in own 'world-class research' after being banned from EU project
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 09:28:25

Business Secretary Kwasi Kwarteng has confirmed UK Research and Innovation's (UKRI) detailed three-year budget allocations. The UKRI is a collection of nine councils that direct research and innovation funding through the science budget of the Department for the Government. The UKRI board advised the Government on how the £25billion budget should be allocated to promote scientific research and innovation in the UK.UKRI's overall budget was set as part of the 2021 Spending Review and rises from £7,904million in 2022 to 2023, to £8,874million in 2024 to 2025.
