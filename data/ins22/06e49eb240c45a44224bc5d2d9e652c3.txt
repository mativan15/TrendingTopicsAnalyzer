Mumbai court lets Sachin Vaze turn approver in CBI's case against Anil Deshmukh
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-01 12:02:09

MUMBAI: A special Mumbai court on Wednesday agreed to let dismissed Mumbai police officer Sachin Vaze become an approver in the corruption case registered against former Maharashtra home minister Anil Deshmukh by the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI).The court, however, ruled that Vaze will be granted a pardon in the corruption case only after he makes a true and full disclosure of the entire circumstances and events without concealing any evidence in the case against the 72-year-old Nationalist Congress Party (NCP) leader.
