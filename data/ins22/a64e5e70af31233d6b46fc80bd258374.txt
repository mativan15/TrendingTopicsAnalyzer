Shaming people for their money mistakes is rampant. It needs to stop
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-05-27 14:06:01

The culture of personal finance is one of personal responsibility. Any mistake with your money is your fault. Any success is the result of intelligence, discipline and hard work.The narrative is meant to empower people to take control of their financial future, but in many ways it does the opposite. Instead of feeling inspired and confident to manage their money better, people feel embarrassed and despair at their financial circumstances.The tool wielded by the personal responsibility crowd to keep people in line is shame.
