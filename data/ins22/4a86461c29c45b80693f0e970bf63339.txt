ANAHEIM, Calif., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Targus®, the industry leader in laptop cases and tech accessory products, announced today that it has been acquired by B. Riley Financial, a diversified financial services platform that delivers tailored solutions to meet the strategic, operational, and capital needs of its clients and partners.
