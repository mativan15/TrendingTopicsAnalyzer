Northern communities encourage participating in Commuter Challenge
cbc.ca
news
2022-06-03 18:58:00

Individuals or businesses can participate in the Commuter Challenge this week by leaving their vehicles at home and finding ways to get to and from work, to help reduce carbon emissions. (IStock)Several communities in northeastern Ontario are participating in the Commuter Challenge.That's a nationwide event where participants leave their cars at home and find alternate ways to get to and from work. They then log those kilometres from their commute toward their community's overall total.Transportation could include walking, cycling, taking transit, carpooling and even telecommuting.
