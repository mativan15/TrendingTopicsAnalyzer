Image source, Getty ImagesThe UK government has been urged to clarify its commitment to fund an addiction treatment centre in Londonderry.Funding was outlined in the New Decade, New Approach deal in 2020.On Tuesday, the NI Affairs Committee was told the government is waiting on a business case from Stormont's Department of Health.Tommy Canning, who runs Northlands treatment centre in Derry, said there was urgent need for progress.Mr Canning said it seemed there was "some uncertainty" over the plans for the new centre.
