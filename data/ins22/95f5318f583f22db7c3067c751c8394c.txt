Council says it was not warned of land resumption for Bruce Highway when it approved new estate
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-19 19:54:47

A Queensland council says it was not warned homes in a new housing development could be resumed for Bruce Highway upgrades, when plans were assessed by the state government two years ago.The owners of 24 residential lots on Venture Drive, which runs along the Bruce Highway, were contacted about the plansThe developer says it was not aware of possible land resumptions when planning the estateResidents have been left devastated over the news, questioning why they were not told of the plansHomeowners in the new Aspire development in Griffin, north of Brisbane, this week got an email from the Department of Transport and Main Roads (TMR) saying planning had "identified a future land requirement".
