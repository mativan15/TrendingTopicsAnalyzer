Every investor in Vicarious Surgical Inc. (NYSE:RBOT) should be aware of the most powerful shareholder groups. The group holding the most number of shares in the company, around 28% to be precise, is private equity firms. In other words, the group stands to gain the most (or lose the most) from their investment into the company.

Meanwhile, individual insiders make up 21% of the company's shareholders. Insiders often own a large chunk of younger, smaller, companies while huge companies tend to have institutions as shareholders.
