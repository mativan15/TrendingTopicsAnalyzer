This story is part of CNBC Make It's Millennial Money series, which details how people around the world earn, spend and save their money.In late 2018, Brian Jung, then 21, decided to drop out of college to pursue entrepreneurship full-time. His South Korean immigrant parents were taken aback: Jung had some part-time e-commerce side hustles that earned about $200 a day, but they didn't offer the long-term financial security of a college degree. Jung also had a YouTube channel, which was more of a hobby than a full-fledged business.
