A 35-year-old who's earning $8,400 a month in passive income from an Airbnb learned everything he knows about real estate from YouTube

2022-05-27 16:45:47

In August 2020, 35-year-old Adam Masato stumbled upon a video on YouTube by creator Robuilt outlining how he makes money renting out tiny homes on Airbnb in Joshua Tree, California.At the time, Masato and his wife, Diana Hernandez, had been living rent-free in a spare bedroom of his in-laws' house for almost a year. The millennial couple saved almost 100% of their combined income of $150,000 per year; Masato is a manager at a healthcare IT company, while Hernandez is a therapist with her own private practice.
