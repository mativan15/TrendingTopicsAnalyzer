On Sunday, May 29, the University will confer a total of 2,788 degrees in all categories — undergraduate, graduate, medical and honorary. PROVIDENCE, R.I. [Brown University] — Brown University will confer a total of 2,788 degrees at its 254th Commencement, with undergraduate, graduate and medical school ceremonies taking place on Sunday, May 29, 2022. On Sunday, Class of 2022 degrees will be conferred on Pembroke Field and at Meehan Auditorium during the Graduate School's respective doctoral and master's ceremonies; at the First Baptist Church in America during the College ceremony; and at the First Unitarian Church during the Warren Alpert Medical School ceremony.
