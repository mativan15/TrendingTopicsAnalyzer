Guild Esports' new look is a key part of its most ambitious year yet. Guild Esports 
Ahead of its most aspirational year yet, and to reflect its ambitions for the coming year, young competitive gaming outfit Guild Esports–the London-based organization that's part-owned by global superstar David Beckham–has unveiled its 2022 competitive jersey, which its elite teams have debuted on the international stage. The new shirt builds on Guild's star iconography and colours, initially designed by Fergus Purcell, the man behind the Palace streetwear label.
