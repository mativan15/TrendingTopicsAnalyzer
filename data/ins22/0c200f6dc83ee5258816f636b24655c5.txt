FTI Consulting Announces the Resignation of Nicole S. Jones from Its Board of Directors

WASHINGTON, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- FTI Consulting, Inc. (NYSE: FCN) today announced that on October 14, 2022, Nicole S. Jones tendered her resignation as a director and member of the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors (the 'Board') of FTI Consulting, effective immediately. Ms. Jones resigned due to obligations arising from her position as Executive Vice President and General Counsel of Cigna Corporation, which resulted in scheduling conflicts and limited the time she was able to devote to her position as a director of FTI Consulting.
