The HC verdict was challenged by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights in SC.The Supreme Court has decided to examine a judgment of the Punjab and Haryana High Court which had in a verdict in June held that a Muslim girl over 15 years was competent to marry a man of her choice. A division bench comprising justices SK Kaul and AS Oka appointed senior advocate Rajshekhar Rao as amicus curiae in the case. The HC verdict was challenged by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights in SC.
