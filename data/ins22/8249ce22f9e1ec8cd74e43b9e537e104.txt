Published: 19:29 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 02:20 EDT, 20 October 2022 Arizona Gov Doug Ducey is refusing to remove 60 shipping containers they stacked along the southern border after the Biden administration ordered the state to take them down.The Arizona Department of Emergency and Military Affairs was defiant in its letter to the federal Department of Interior's Bureau of Reclamation saying 'the containers will remain in place until specific details regarding construction are provided.Allen Clark, the director of the Department of Emergency and Military Affairs, then went on to clap back at the federal government for failing to protect southern states from the onslaught of illegal immigrants.
