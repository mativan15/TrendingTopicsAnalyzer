Meghan Markle has revealed she felt 'judgement and envy' towards Paris Hilton when she was young because she was always 'the smart one' growing up while Hilton was 'the pretty one'.The Duchess of Sussex, 41, sat down with the American socialite, 41, for the latest episode of her podcast, Archetypes. During the episode, Markle and Hilton discussed the term 'bimbo' – used to describe women as attractive but unintelligent – and how Hilton played into her media narrative as a 'dumb blonde'.
