The writer is author of ‘The Reset: Ideas to Change How We Work and Live'When I entered the workplace, there weren't too many events I could go to as a black woman to network and attend talks designed with me in mind, and also some of the challenges I was experiencing in the world of work. However, that was 10 years ago. Times have shifted since then, and the UK Black Business Show has become a staple in thousands of people's calendars in October as just that. The show is the biggest business event of its kind in the UK and Europe.
