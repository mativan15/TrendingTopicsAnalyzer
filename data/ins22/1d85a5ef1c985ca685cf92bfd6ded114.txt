Pittsburgh Penguins captain Sidney Crosby named NHL's first star of opening week
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-17 17:52:18

The NHL has released the first set of stars for the 2022-2023 regular season, and Penguins captain Sidney Crosby took the top spot.

Crosby is currently leading the NHL in points with two goals and four assists. Crosby recorded three points in each of the first two games of the season.

According to the Penguins, this is the second time that Crosby has recorded three points in each of the Penguins' first two games of the season. The first time was in 2014-2015.

The Penguins also said that in the Penguins' home opener, Crosby scored the third-fastest goal by a Penguin in a season-opening game, hitting the back of the net 1 minute and 22 seconds into the game.
