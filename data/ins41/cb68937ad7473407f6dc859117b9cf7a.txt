Published: 07:37 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 07:37 EDT, 20 October 2022 An heiress to the billionaire Getty Oil fortune has donated $1million to eco-zealots who have caused chaos and infuriated the public with their disruptive protests in the UK.Aileen Getty, who lives in the United States, is bankrolling an organisation that has given money to Just Stop Oil as it creates havoc with its demonstrations demanding an end to the issuing of new oil and gas exploration licences by the British Government.
