The Hollywood Reporter - Movie News Click here to read the full article.The particular limbo of grief, perennial fertile ground for drama, receives straightforward treatment in Narcosis, but with a poetic twist. Tracing the effect of a deep-sea diver's death on his wife and children, writer-helmer Martijn de Jong infuses a standard template of family loss with a sense of mystery. Ocean exploration and the psychic abilities of the explorer's widow are the story's most unusual angles, and the restraint with which de Jong treats them is one of the film's most effective qualities.
