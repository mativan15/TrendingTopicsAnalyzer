Unsold housing stock rise marginally across top eight cities in Jan-March quarter: Report
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 16:45:00

NEW DELHI: Unsold housing stock rose marginally by 1 per cent to 9.01 lakh units during January-March 2022 across eight major cities as compared to the previous quarter on increased new launches of residential properties, according to a report by Credai, Colliers India and Liases Foras. As per the data, the unsold inventories increased to 9,01,967 units during January-March period of this year from 8,94,100 units in the previous quarter. Out of the total unsold housing stocks across eight cities during the first quarter of this calendar year, Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR) and Delhi-NCR, the two biggest property markets in India, contribute 50 per cent.
