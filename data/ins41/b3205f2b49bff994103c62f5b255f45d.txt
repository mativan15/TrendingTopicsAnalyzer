New Delhi: Even as reported its highest-ever quarterly earnings in the September 2022 quarter, analysts on Dalal Street were divided over the counter. Those suggesting buying the non-banking financial lender see an up to 22% upside in the stock from its last close, whereas those who suggest selling the stock are expecting a downfall of similar magnitude in the counter. A few analysts also remain neutral. Bajaj Finance reported an 88% jump in its consolidated net profit at Rs 2,781 crore for the quarter ended September 2022.
