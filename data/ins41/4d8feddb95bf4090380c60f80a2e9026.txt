Big FIIs lap up Zee Ent shares, holding faith on growth prospects
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 17:45:00

Mumbai: Big foreign funds lapped up shares of media major Zee Entertainment Enterprises through the open market on Tuesday, indicating confidence they have on the company's growth prospects with the merger of Sony Pictures India. The foreign funds include BNP Paribas, BofA Securities, Citigroup Global Markets, Copthall Mauritius, Franklin Templeton, Goldman Sachs Investments, and Morgan Stanley.
 Among domestic funds, Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund and Life Insurance have increased their stake in the media company by purchasing additional shares in the open market.
