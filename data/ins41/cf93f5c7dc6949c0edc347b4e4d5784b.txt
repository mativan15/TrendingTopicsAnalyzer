UPSC Civil Services 2021 Results out, girls bag top four positions
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 09:45:00

UPSC announced the Civil Services Examination (CSE) 2021 results on May 30, 2022. As per results released, Shruti Sharma has claimed the top position, while Ankita Agarwal, Gamini Singla, and Aishwarya Verma bagged the second, third, and fourth ranks, respectively. The AIR 1st rank holder is a former student of St. Stephens College and the Jawaharlal Nehru University. However, Shruti prepared for the elite examination at the Residential Coaching Academy, Jamia Millia Islamia. The UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) prelim was conducted on October 10, 2021, and its results were declared on October 29.
