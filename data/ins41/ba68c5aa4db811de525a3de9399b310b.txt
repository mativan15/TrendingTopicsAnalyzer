Saving money around the house doesn't have to be difficult -- you can try simple tricks like unplugging appliances, taking shorter showers and turning off your lights. But the most consistent method for me has come in an unexpected countertop device: the rice cooker.In 2017, I went to grad school. My whole family -- including my wife, my 18-month-old and my newborn -- had to survive on a meager stipend and whatever I could scrape together by freelancing on the side. To make it work, we rented a tiny apartment, got on SNAP, hustled and ate rice.
