Scary Stock Market: What Should Docs Do With Their Money?
medscape.com
news
2022-06-02 13:03:08

Recently I touched base with some of my physician clients' children who are medical students themselves now. It's a pleasure for me to congratulate them on their hard work as they're being matched with their residency programs. In between these calls, I heard from one of my clients who is an active physician. He had been reading financial articles over the past few months and was convinced that the market was in a continued downswing. Because of this, he felt that the best option was to sell everything, then wait a year and buy it all back when the market hit bottom.
