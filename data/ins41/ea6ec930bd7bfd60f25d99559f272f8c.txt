Dzmitry Dzemidovich/iStock via Getty Images A Quick Take On DUET DUET Acquisition Corp. (NASDAQ:DUET) has raised $86.25 million from an IPO at a price of $10.00 per unit, according to the terms of its most recent S-1/A regulatory filing. The SPAC (Special Purpose Acquisition Company) intends to pursue a merger with an 'enabling technology' company which can 'encompass a wide spectrum of capabilities from holistic e-commerce, fintech and big data analytics to robotic process automation' (Ex-China).
