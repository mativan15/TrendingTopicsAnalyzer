REUTERS/Umit Bektas/File PhotoORLANDO, Fla., Oct 17 (Reuters) - General Dynamics Corp's (GD.N) Gulfstream Aerospace is weighing one of its business jets to replace its popular G550 aircraft which is now out of production for special mission purposes, President Mark Burns said on Monday.Governments are eying "special mission" business jets capable of looking or listening at potentially lower running costs than converted passenger or military planes.
