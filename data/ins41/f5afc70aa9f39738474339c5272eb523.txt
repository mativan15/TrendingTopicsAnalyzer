June 02, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tradeweb Markets Inc. (Nasdaq: TW), a leading, global operator of electronic marketplaces for rates, credit, equities and money markets, today announced it will participate in the Piper Sandler Global Exchange & FinTech Conference on Wednesday, June 8, 2022.

Tradeweb Chairman and CEO Lee Olesky will participate in a fireside chat at 10:30am EDT and Tradeweb President and CEO-Elect Billy Hult will participate in The Changing Fixed Income Electronic Trading Environment Panel at 11:30am EDT.
