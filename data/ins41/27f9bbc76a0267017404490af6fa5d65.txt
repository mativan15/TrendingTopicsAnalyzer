Friday the 13th: The Final Chapter actor Ted White has died aged 96.

According to horror historian and convention runner Sean Clark, White died peacefully in his sleep at his home. 'I just got hit with the news that my dear friend Ted White has passed away,' he said. 'I was told he passed away peacefully in his sleep at his home.'
