25 free things to do over the Jubilee bank holiday weekend
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 13:15:00

When it comes to families, it is almost always the case that the best things in life are free. With living costs on the rise and even the most easily-pleased children liable to be occasionally but exasperatingly fickle, a free day trip or activity makes a stress-free option.
So, in preparation for the four-day weekend in June to mark the Queen's Platinum Jubilee, we have scoured the UK for some of the best adventures for families, which don't cost a penny. Be it fun-filled walks, fairytale-inspired sightseeing trips, history-themed treasure hunts, coastal cycle rides or art appreciation, there should be something to keep children of all ages amused (and educated), both indoors and out.
