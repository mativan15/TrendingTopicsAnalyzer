Jose Pedro Bastos Neves (The New School - Department of Economics) & Willi Semmler (The New School - Department of Economics; Universitaet Bielefeld; IIASA) have posted A Proposal for a Carbon Wealth Tax: Modelling, Empirics, and Policy on SSRN.  Here is the abstract: Although economists widely advocate carbon pricing as an effective solution to reduce carbon emissions, this mechanism has had so far limited effects. This paper proposes a new type of tax to help finance (and accelerate) the green transition.
