LA Local NewsBy City News ServiceJun 2, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesLOS ANGELES (CNS) - Los Angeles ranked at the top of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's list of cities with the highest number of ENERGY STAR-certified commercial and multifamily buildings in 2021, officials announced Wednesday. Such buildings meet criteria for saving energy and money and produce fewer greenhouse gas emissions than typical buildings, according to the EPA. The city of Los Angeles has 648 commercial and multifamily buildings that meet the criteria and are more efficient than 75% of similar buildings nationwide.
