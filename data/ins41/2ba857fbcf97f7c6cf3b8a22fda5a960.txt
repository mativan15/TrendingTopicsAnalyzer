L-R - DFC CEO, Scott Nathan and Soothe Healthcare CEO & Founder, Sahil Dharia signing the letter of agreementNew Delhi: Personal hygiene startup Soothe Healthcare has raised Rs 175 crore in its latest funding round, the company announced in a press release on Friday. In the fresh round of funding, the brand raised Rs 120 crore from its existing investors including A91, Symphony, Sixth Sense, and GII, and Rs 55 crore from the U.S. International Development Finance Corporation (DFC), it said. 'We continue to be believers in the business opportunity in women's hygiene and Soothe's relentless focus on building a high-quality Indian brand in the space,' commented Abhay Panday, general partner, A91 Partners on the investment.
