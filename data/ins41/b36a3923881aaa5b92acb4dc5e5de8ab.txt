Used EVs are now selling as fast as petrol and diesel cars for the first time
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-29 09:25:53

The sales of used electric cars are now matching diesel and petrol vehicles for the first time ever, according to the latest report carried out by Indicata Market Watch. According to the most recent figures, the sales of used EVs were up by 68 percent in April.However, the data also showed that the overall prices for used cars have started to fall.The report showed that from April to the beginning of May 2022 prices fell by 1.4 percent and are now 0.1 percentage points lower than at the start of January.
