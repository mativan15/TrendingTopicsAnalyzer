Parents have been left furious after it was alleged some school children were made to eat separately during a Jubilee picnic - leaving them to feel 'like outcasts'The primary school has been blasted by parents online Furious parents have hit out at a primary school after it hosted a Jubilee picnic for pupils last week, as it has been alleged that some children were made to eat their lunch in a separate field from others. Students at West Hill Primary School in Cannock, Staffordshire, were treated to a picnic on Friday, May 27, ahead of the Queen's Platinum Jubilee and were allowed to bring their parents to enjoy the outdoor eating experience with them.
