As part of the Carrier Partner Program, the partnership streamlines insurance plan offerings on one simple platform.

PORTLAND, Ore., October 21, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Standard Insurance Company (The Standard), a leading provider of financial protection products and services for employers and individuals, is pleased to announce its partnership as part of the Carrier Partner Program with Alight, Inc. (NYSE: ALIT), a leading cloud-based provider of human capital technology and business solutions. The Standard is excited to bring our mutual clients our easy-to-use insurance plans for life, disability, dental, vision and supplemental health in one convenient location on the Alight Worklife platform.
