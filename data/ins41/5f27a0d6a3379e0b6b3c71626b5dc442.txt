Thousands of Vodafone customers face extra charges from TODAY

2022-05-27 06:58:22

THOUSANDS of VOXI customers will see a major shake up to their mobile plans from TODAY following an announcement by Vodafone.
The network provider has introduced new charges affecting how customers use their phones abroad.
From today customers using Vodafone will have to start using a European Roaming Pass if they want to use their phone abroad (Photo by DANIEL LEAL/AFP via Getty Images)Credit: AFP
VOXI is a Vodafone brand, and its customer will now be affected by changes to roaming charges that are coming in to play.
