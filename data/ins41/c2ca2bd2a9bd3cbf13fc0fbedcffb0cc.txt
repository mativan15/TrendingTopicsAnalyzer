Transparency is a word that gets thrown around a lot these days—one that many leaders find mystifying. How can you even tell whether you're being transparent enough? Why exactly is it so important right now?I'd like you to imagine this scenario: You're seated comfortably on a plane, ready to ascend. Only, you're not given any information about safety protocols or how long the trip will take. Then upon departure, things start getting a bit turbulent, the plane begins to shake—and yet, there's no reassuring words from the pilot about whether it's just a momentary agitation or something major to worry about.
