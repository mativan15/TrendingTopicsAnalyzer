FAIRFAX, VA, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Futuris Company (OTC Pink: FTRS) a Human Capital Management (HCM) company focused on areas such as Staffing, Consulting, and Tech Services, today announces the appointment of Charles 'Charlie' LeVoir as President of Cadan Technologies. For over twenty years, Charlie has enjoyed aiding organizations of all shapes and sizes utilize technology to achieve their strategic goals. Charlie is adept at understanding the specific goals that they have and delivering solutions to achieve them, loves exploring new technology and applications and is never happier than when he discovers a way to simplify.
