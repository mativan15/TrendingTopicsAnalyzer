EA Play's October rewards are now available and include a variety of bonuses for FIFA 23, Madden NFL 23, GRID Legends, and NHL 23.First up, EA Play subscribers can claim a tifo of English player Joe Cole in FIFA Ultimate Team, which is displayable in his stadium.
