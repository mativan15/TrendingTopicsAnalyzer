Blue Origin launches 6 people on company's 5th space tourism flight
space.com
news
2022-06-04 13:44:55

Blue Origin now has five crewed spaceflights under its belt.Jeff Bezos' company sent six people to suborbital space today (June 4), acing its fifth human spaceflight mission and notching several milestones in the process.The action started at about 9:25 a.m. EDT (1325 GMT), when Blue Origin's New Shepard vehicle lifted off from Launch Site One, the company's facility in West Texas, about 25 miles (40 kilometers) from the town of Van Horn. And it was all over just 10 minutes later, when the New Shepard capsule and its six passengers touched down under parachutes, sending up a plume of desert dust when it hit the ground.
