By Reuters Published: 14:42 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 14:43 EDT, 31 May 2022 MADRID, May 31 (Reuters) - Barcelona's board of directors has called an extraordinary assembly of its members on June 16 to vote on whether to approve selling 25% of the club's LaLiga broadcasting rights to one or more investors, the club said on Tuesday. In December, the club opted out of a deal with the equity fund CVC Capital Partners, the first of its kind in Europe, that was agreed by 38 of LaLiga's 42 member clubs.
