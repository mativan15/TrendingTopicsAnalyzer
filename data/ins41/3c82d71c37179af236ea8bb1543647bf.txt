(Jason Franson/The Canadian Press)Alberta's New Democrats are meeting this weekend in Calgary — a location picked as a nod to where the next provincial election will likely be decided. The NDP is hosting its annual convention on Friday, Saturday and Sunday to debate policy proposals, hear from leader Rachel Notley and begin gearing up in earnest for a campaign next spring.
