REDMOND, Wash., October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Versium, a leading data technology company, today announced it has achieved record subscription revenue growth for its suite of marketing solutions built on its best-in-class identity resolution and insights engine. As marketers and advertisers rush to find cookieless solutions to reach their target audiences, Versium is uniquely positioned to accelerate growth across B2C and B2B marketing teams alike who are emphasizing first-party data.
