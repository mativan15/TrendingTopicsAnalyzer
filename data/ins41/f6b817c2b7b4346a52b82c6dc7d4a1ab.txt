Johnny Depp fans buy, wear Dior fragrance in celebration
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-06-03 01:08:15

Johnny Depp's win in the defamation trial against ex-wife Amber Heard is only accelerating the popularity of his Dior cologne. Giving French luxury fashion house owned by LVMH Moët Hennessy Louis Vuitton, Ian Wishingrad another win, founder of marketing firm BigEyedWish, told FOX Business. Droves of Twitter users announced on the social media platform that they were either buying or wearing Dior Sauvage, which Depp had been the face of since 2015, in celebration of his win. Earlier this week, the jury awarded the actor more than $10 million and vindicated him of allegations that he abused Heard.
