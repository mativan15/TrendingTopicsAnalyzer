HYDERABAD: A man killed his wife along with their two children before ending his life at their house in Chandanagar where three semi-decomposed bodies were found on Monday after neighbours alerted cops about a foul smell.The man was found hanging from the ceiling fan, while the wife and children were found with multiple stab wounds lying on a bed nearby. Cops suspect the four had died on Friday when they were last seen by neighbours.There was no suicide note but based on neighbours' testimony police suspect that Nagaraju (40), a petty vendor, killed his wife Sujatha (38) as he suspected her of having an affair.
