Carer stole £9K from man aged 95 to fund online gambling addiction
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 10:14:00

Alison Hunter (Image: Derby Telegraph)A carer who a 95-year-old pensioner considered 'a friend' stole £9,000 of his savings to fund her gambling addiction, a court has heard. Heartless Alison Hunter used the victim's bank account to spend on online flutters without his knowledge.The 47-year-old then told the police he had full knowledge of what she was spending his money on when she was arrested and interviewed.Now she has been spared an immediate prison sentence, the Derby Telegraph reports.Handing her a nine-month jail sentence, suspended for 18 months, Judge Shaun Smith KC said at Derby Crown Court: "What you did was despicable, there's no doubt about that and you are the second person to fleece vulnerable people that I have dealt with in court today.
