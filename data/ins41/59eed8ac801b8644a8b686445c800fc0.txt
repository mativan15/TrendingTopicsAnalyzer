It is crucial that you begin managing your finances wisely and compensate for the expenditure.New Delhi: From elaborate feasts, gifts for family and friends, and boxes of sweets for neighbours to a new vehicle or property, there is always a slight uptick in our expenditure during the festive season every year. This increased spending is usually driven by the jubilation that these auspicious festivals bring with them while a host of exciting festive discounts and deals by many brands also prompt us to shell out significant sums.
