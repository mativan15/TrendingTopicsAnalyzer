© Paris Gore/Red Bull Content PoolThe venue isn't the only thing that's changed over the years of competition at Red Bull Rampage. The awe-inspiring mountain bike downhill event has an illustrious reputation and has been taken on by riders and their machines since 2001.The event has evolved from a ragtag group of freeriders gathering in the desert with customised bikes to a world-class event that hosts athletes who spend the entire year training for this one competition. Although the crowds and coverage have grown like the features that riders take on, much has remained the same here in the Utah desert.
