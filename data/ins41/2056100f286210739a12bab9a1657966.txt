'Nothing funny about Britain being an international laughing stock'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 09:09:17

The pioneer of Parliamentary democracy, Britain is in danger of becoming an international laughing stock, though there is nothing funny for the public about the consequences of the economic turmoil, including soaring inflation and mortgage costs. Amid the wreckage of last month's botched mini- Budget, Ministers have now been forced to face up to financial realities rather than indulge in fantasies about growth through borrowing.As Jim Callaghan, the shrewd Labour Prime Minister, said in a speech in 1976, "we used to think that you could spend your way out of a recession and increase employment by cutting taxes and boosting government spending.
