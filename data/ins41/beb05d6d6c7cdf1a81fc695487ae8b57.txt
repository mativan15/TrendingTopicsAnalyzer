Universe Holdings makes second Ventura apartment acquisition this year
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 21:00:00

In its second Ventura acquisition in the past five months, Universe Holdings has bought multifamily community Hacienda Villas for $12.6 million.

Built in 1977, Hacienda Villas features 34 recently renovated two- and three-bedroom townhome-style apartments with spacious floor plans. Universe will continue renovations with upgrades to the building exterior and common areas.

NorthMarq represented the seller in the transaction.

With the purchase, the Los Angeles-based real estate investment firm increased its holdings in Ventura to 500 units.
