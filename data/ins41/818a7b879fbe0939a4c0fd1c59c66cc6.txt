The 2022 Major League Baseball season is getting one step closer to the World Series with the postseason in full swing. Eight teams remain in contention for the 2022 Commissioner's Trophy (the official name given to the World Series trophy) with the League Division Series happening now. In the American League (AL), Aaron Judge and the New York Yankees are facing off against the Cleveland Guardians, while the Houston Astros take on the Seattle Mariners.
