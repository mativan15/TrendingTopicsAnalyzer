Reliance Industries rallies 14% in 2 weeks, market cap tops Rs 19 lakh cr
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 06:15:00

New Delhi: Mukesh Ambani-led once again hit Rs 19 lakh crore market capitalization landmark on Friday as the scrip extended its gaining streak. RIL shares zoomed more than 3 per cent to Rs 2,816.35 level on Friday, just a couple of percentage points below its 52-week high of Rs 2,855 scaled on April 24, 2022. In the last two weeks, shares of Industries have zoomed about 14 per cent, whereas the largest Indian company has surged about 20 per cent in the year 2022 so far. According to some media reports, the Ambani-led company is likely to invest in ed-tech startup Lido Learning despite the mass layoff of 1,200 employees.
