Telangana Govt sets up Basti Dawakhanas, giving free treatment to poor people

Source: ANI

In order to provide Health care services to urban poor Telangana government has established Basti Dawakhanas in urban slums. These establishments give free treatment to poor people. Dr Syeda working as a doctor at the basti dawakhana in Hyderabad said, 'It was inaugurated in October 2019 by MLA Kowsar Moinuddin. Daily OPs are above 110. We have all the facilities of NCD drugs. We also conduct BP sugar tests and provide medicine. We also maintain a book and provide Monthly medicines to patients.
