Bicester's new Arrival heads for America
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

One of Britain's most exciting automotive zero-emission start-ups is to quit the country for the United States, where it says incentives are better for industry.Arrival has scrapped plans to build electric vans for groups including UPS and electric buses for FirstGroup at its Oxfordshire microfactory. Instead, it is to divert assembly to America.The abrupt change came three weeks after the first electric delivery van, which was four years in development, came out of production at its factory in Bicester.
