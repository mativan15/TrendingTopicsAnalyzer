Topping the list, Bengaluru east accounted for most of the fraudulent credit and debit card transactions in the city. An analysis of 300 cases of such fraud over the last five years from across Bengaluru showed that it is mostly the residents from the eastern part of Bengaluru that are falling prey to this crime. As many as 20% of the victims of credit and debit card frauds turned out to be from Bengaluru east, 17.7% were from Bengaluru south, 14.7% were from the Bengaluru central, 13.7% from Bengaluru north and around 13% of the victims were from Bengaluru west.
