October 21, 2022 / 2:13 PM / AP WASHINGTON (AP) - The House committee investigating the Jan. 6 attack on the U.S. Capitol issued a subpoena Friday to Donald Trump, exercising its subpoena power against the former president who lawmakers say is the "central cause" of a coordinated, multi-part effort to overturn the results of the 2020 election.
