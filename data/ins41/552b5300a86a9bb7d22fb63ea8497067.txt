As interest rates and cost of borrowing heads up, yields on government bonds follow suit.For traders and investors in debt assets, the current environment is a challenging one. Soaring global inflationary pressures have prompted central banks, including the US Federal Reserve and the Reserve Bank of India to announce rate hike plans. The domestic central bank is also looking to reduce the massive liquidity surplus in the banking system as it seeks to battle major upside risks to inflation.
