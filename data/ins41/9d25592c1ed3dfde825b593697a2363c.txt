Elon Musk may face a federal investigation in the US, after making officials 'uncomfortable' with his remarks about Ukraine, Bloomberg has claimed, citing anonymous sources in the government.Persons within the US government and intelligence community are 'weighing what tools, if any' are available to target the billionaire, the Friday report said.
