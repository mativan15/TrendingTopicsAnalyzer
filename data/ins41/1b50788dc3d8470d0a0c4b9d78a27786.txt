June 02, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN MATEO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Guidewire (NYSE: GWRE) today announced that management is scheduled to present at the 42nd Annual William Blair Growth Stock Conference. The Guidewire presentation is scheduled for Thursday, June 9, 2022 at 2:40 p.m. CT (12:40 p.m. PT) in Chicago, Illinois.

A live webcast, as well as a replay, of the presentation will be available under the "Webcasts and Presentations" section on the Company's investor relations website at https://ir.
