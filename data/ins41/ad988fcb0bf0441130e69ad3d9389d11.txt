Geetanjali Shree's Ret Samadhi Wins International Booker Prize, The First For A Hindi Novel
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-27 06:10:38

'Tomb of Sand', the English translation of Geetanjali Shree's Hindi novel, 'Ret Samadhi' has won the prestigious International Booker Prize, becoming the first by an Indian language.The 50,000-pound ($63,000) prize money will be split between New Delhi-based Shree and Rockwell, who translated it into English. APSet in 80's northern IndiaThe novel is a family saga set in northern India about an 80-year-old woman who travels to Pakistan to confront the unresolved trauma of her teenage experiences of Partition and re-evaluates what it means to be a mother, a daughter, a woman and a feminist.
