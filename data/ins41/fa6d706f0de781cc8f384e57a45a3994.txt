IPG Joins Ad Holding Companies in Raising 2022 Organic-Growth Forecast
wsj.com
news
2022-10-21 18:15:00

Interpublic Group of Cos. on Friday raised its organic-growth guidance for 2022 to 7% from over 6.5%, citing increased revenues across all markets despite macroeconomic uncertainty that continues to threaten the advertising industry.The New York-based company posted net revenue of $2.3 billion for the quarter ended Sep. 30, an increase of 1.5% from the same period last year. Organic revenue growth, which removes the effects of currency fluctuations, acquisitions and disposals, was at 5.6%. IPG, which owns agency groups such as McCann Worldgroup and IPG Mediabrands, joined holding companies Publicis Groupe SA and Omnicom Group Inc.
