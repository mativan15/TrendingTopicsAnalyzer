 Strength in the base metals sector helped lead Canada's main stock index higher in late-morning trading, while U.S. stock markets also climbed higher.
The S&P/TSX composite index was up 83.54 points at 18,662.83.In New York, the Dow Jones industrial average was up 193.24 points at 30,526.83. The S&P 500 index was up 14.
