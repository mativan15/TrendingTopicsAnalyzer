Here's how to wear a see-through skirt
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

If how much leg we have on show is theoretically an indication of stock price, what does fashion's latest favourite skirt say about where we are? It is both long and short; cloaking but not completely. It can be sleek or frothy; sexy or elegant. You might wear it with chunky boots and a jumper or a silky shirt and heels.It is a midi, but not as you know it — because it isn't a bit modest. Confused? I can relate, but here's one thing I can bank on being transparent this autumn: your skirt. Sheer versions are back in fashion — and yes, grown-ups can wear them too.
