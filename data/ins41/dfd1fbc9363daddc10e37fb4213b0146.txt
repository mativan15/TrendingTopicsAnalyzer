Recently, Migsun group had taken over Parsvnath Palacia in Greater Noida, under PMC model. (Image: Company site)Realty developer Migsun group has acquired a stuck project - The Hemisphere in Greater Noida under the PMC model, a senior executive said. Spread over 100-acre, the group will develop 252 villas and 12 towers in the project. The project that has 400+ units will also have a range of luxury amenities within the vicinity. 'The Hemisphere takeover is a crucial step in our plan to provide homes to the buyers of the project.
