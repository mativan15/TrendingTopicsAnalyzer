Can adverts turn around the fortunes of Netflix?
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

Natasha Lyonne has won acclaim for roles in not one but two Netflix hits. The first, Orange Is the New Black, was released almost a decade ago in the early days of the streaming giant's extraordinary march to dominance.Things had changed markedly by the time the actress was promoting the second, Russian Doll, this spring. During an appearance on the comedy show Saturday Night Live, Lyonne deadpanned: 'Two things you definitely want to be associated with right now are Russia and Netflix.'The joke bluntly summarised the stunning reversal in fortune of a business that months previously had still been hailed as the future of television and cinema.
