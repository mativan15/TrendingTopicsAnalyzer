ReNew Power to invest Rs 30,000 cr to scale up green capacities: Chairman Sumant Sinha
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-10-16 06:30:00

Green energy company ReNew Power plans to invest Rs 30,000 crore over the next two years to scale up its solar and wind energy capacities, its Chairman Sumant Sinha said. "We have 5,000 megawatts (MW) of projects that we are building right now. We have won various auctions... have PPAs (power purchase agreements)," Sinha, who is also the founder and CEO of the company, said. The company plans to spend almost Rs 30,000 crore over the next two years to scale up solar and wind energy capacities, he said.
