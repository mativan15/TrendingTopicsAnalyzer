Elon Musk teases massive Tesla stock buyback as CFO trims forecast for annual deliveries and stock falls
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 20:14:00

Tesla Inc. Chief Executive Elon Musk suggested the electric-vehicle maker could repurchase up to $10 billion worth of its stock Wednesday, as shares declined following a third-quarter revenue miss and his CFO brought down delivery expectations for the full year.Some Tesla TSLA, +0.84% investors have been agitating for a stock buyback after multiple stock splits and the company losing more than a third of its market capitalization in 2022, and Musk said in an earnings conference call that Tesla's board has discussed a buyback in the range of $5 billion to $10 billion.
