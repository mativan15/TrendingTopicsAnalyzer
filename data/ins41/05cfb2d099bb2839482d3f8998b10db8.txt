UConn hit four home runs to quiet a sellout crowd and stun top-seeded Maryland, 10-5, in the NCAA baseball regional Saturday night at College Park, Md.

The Huskies, now 2-0 in the regional. are one win away from reaching the Super Regional, a best-of-three series that serves as the doorstep to the College World Series. UConn last reached the Super Regional in 2011.
