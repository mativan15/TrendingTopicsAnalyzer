Sundry Photography/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Pure Storage (NYSE:PSTG) shares have rallied 10% after the data storage technology provider delivered better-than-expected Q1 results and raised full-year outlook. The company generated adj. EPS of $0.25 on revenue of $620.4M that grew 50.3% Y/Y. Subscription services grew 35% Y/Y to $219.2M, while subscription annual recurring revenue rose 29% Y/Y to $899.8M. Pure Storage (PSTG) raised its full-year 2023 guidance, now expecting revenue of ~$2.
