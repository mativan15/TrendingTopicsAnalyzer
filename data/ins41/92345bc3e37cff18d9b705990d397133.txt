ELKRIDGE, Md., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Cyber response firm Surefire Cyber announces today the appointment of industry leader and entrepreneur, Cyndi Gula, to the company's Board of Directors.

Cyndi Gula has an outstanding record of founding, supporting, and growing cyber companies. Cyndi was co-founder and President of Network Security Wizards and was instrumental in starting Tenable Network Security, helping establish and grow its operations until it eventually went IPO.
