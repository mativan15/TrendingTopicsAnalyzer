UP Dy CM Brajesh Pathak terms killing of non-local labourers in J&K ‘very unfortunate'

Source: ANI

Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak terms killing of non-local labourers in J&K ‘very unfortunate' and assured of befitting reply to the perpetrators. Speaking to media persons, Pathak said, 'Very unfortunate. Our Government stands with bereaved families, we'll make everything available to them. Government working to eliminate terrorists. We'll give them befitting reply. Won't tolerate this cowardly act.' 'Under the leadership of PM Modi, work to uproot Pakistan-sponsored terrorism from India is being done.
