Nigeria LNG outage threatens Europe, traders with higher costs
reuters.com
news
2022-10-18 15:36:35

LAGOS/LONDON, Oct 18 (Reuters) - European countries and gas traders are reeling from the latest LNG supply shock - a force majeure from Nigeria LNG - that threatens nearly 4% of global supply and further squeezes the continent's resources in absence of Russian pipeline gas.Nigeria LNG declared force majeure this week due to heavy flooding in that it said impacted virtually all of its gas suppliers.NLNG, with 22 million tonnes per year of capacity, said it is working to mitigate the impact, but flood waters are still rising.
