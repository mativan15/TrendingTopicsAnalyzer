June 01, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LANGFORD, British Columbia--(BUSINESS WIRE)--As a first adopter of carbon mineralization technology provided by CarbonCure Technologies (CarbonCure), Butler Concrete and Aggregate (Butler Concrete), based out of Victoria, British Columbia, is proud to announce that it exclusively offers low-carbon concrete to all customers. All commercial and residential projects are supplied with low-carbon concrete, in alignment with the City of Langford's Low Carbon Concrete Policy effective June 1, 2022.
