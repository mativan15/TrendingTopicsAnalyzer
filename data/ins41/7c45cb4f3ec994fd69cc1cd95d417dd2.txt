Dorsett Controls Acquires Sunapsys

YADKINVILLE, N.C., Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Dorsett Controls, an industrial technology company and industry leader headquartered in North Carolina that manufactures advanced supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) systems software and hardware, is pleased to announce that it has acquired Sunapsys, Inc., a Vinton, Virginia based system integrator who specializes in the design and implementation of controls and information systems in the water and wastewater market for municipalities, manufacturers, and OEMs.
