The London Stock Exchange Group (LSEG.L) reported a 16.2% rise in third-quarter income, with growth across all divisions.

The stock exchange and financial information company said it was "well positioned" for further growth, and that there was no change to guidance or targets, after third-quarter income rose by 16.2% — or by 5.9% on a constant currency basis — to £1.91bn ($2.13bn). The figure was ahead of forecasts for around £1.
