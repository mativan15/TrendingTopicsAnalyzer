Portland, OR, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to the report published by Allied Market Research, the global prebiotics ingredients market generated $7.19 billion in 2020, and is projected to reach $14.31 billion by 2031, witnessing a CAGR of 6.3% from 2022 to 2031. The report provides a detailed analysis of changing market dynamics, top segments, value chain, key investment pockets, regional scenario, and competitive landscape. Increase in consumer awareness related to health benefits of prebiotics, multifunctional nature of prebiotic ingredients, and ease of incorporation in a wide range of food & beverages drive the growth of the global prebiotics ingredients market.
