WHAT IS NASDAQ 100 INDEX FUND? The Nasdaq 100 Index is a basket of the 100 largest, most actively traded US companies listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange. Known to be a tech-heavy index, it includes 100 of the largest non-financial companies based on market capitalisation. The index also consists of companies across major industry groups like industrials, consumer goods, healthcare, consumer services, telecommunications and utilities apart from technology companies. It does not include stocks of banks and financial companies, including investment companies.
