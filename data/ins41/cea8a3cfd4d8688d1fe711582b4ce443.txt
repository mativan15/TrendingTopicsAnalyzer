People looking for work and companies seeking workers are brought together by Express Employment Professionals of Howell. (Shutterstock)HOWELL, N.J.— A celebration of helping workers find jobs — and employers find workers —is happening today, Thursday, at Express Employment Professionals, which is doubling its space at the Howell Plaza shopping center.Since 2015, more than 2,000 people have landed jobs at 350 local companies through the company, whose franchise is owned and managed by Anna Santucci-Braun.
