Pakistan provides key support to boost Myanmar Air Force
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 20:00:00

Pakistan's military partnership with Myanmar is set to gather momentum, with a team of 15 technical experts of the Pakistan Air Force scheduled to visit Mandalay air force station soon to provide training support for Chinese-made JF-17 fighter jets. The team is expected to visit Meiktila Air Force Station in Mandalay regarding the setting up of a JF-17 simulator for training pilots of the Myanmar Air Force, ET has reliably learnt.

The team is also expected to visit Mingaladon Air Force Station in Yangon to address technical issues relating to JF-17s that the ground maintenance staff is facing, ET has further learnt.
