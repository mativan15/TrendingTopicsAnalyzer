Scottish minister welcomes interim blood scandal compensation payments

2022-10-21 23:01:00

Thousands of Scots who fell victim to the infected blood scandal will receive £100,000 by the end of the month, it has been announced.Patients who were infected with Hepatitis C or HIV in the 1970s and 80s – or if they have died, then their partners – will get the interim compensation payment next week, the UK Government has announced.Maree Todd, Scotland's public health minister, welcomed the Cabinet Office announcement which came after a report published in July by infected blood inquiry chairman Sir Brian Langstaff said the payments should be made 'without delay'.
