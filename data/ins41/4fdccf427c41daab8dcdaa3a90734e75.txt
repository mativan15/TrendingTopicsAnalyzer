Bitcoin has grown in value from around £300 in May 2016 to almost £30,000 in May 2022, and become a part of the zeitgeist along the way. As a result, interest in the cryptocurrency is high.If you're thinking about investing in Bitcoin, you should know there's no guarantee you'll see a return or break even. Between 2017 and 2018 the cryptocurrency's value plummeted from almost £16,000 to £2,400. It's record high is over £60,000, meaning its current value is half what it once was.Such volatility has led the UK's financial watchdog, the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), to repeatedly warn that cryptocurrency buyers should be prepared to lose their entire investments.
