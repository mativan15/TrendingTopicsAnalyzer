Elizabeth Zhong trial: 'Bitter dispute' over money led to murder, Crown says
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-30 01:38:01

An 'embittered' man had a strong motive to kill his business partner after a dispute over millions of dollars led to animosity and hatred, a jury has heard. Fang Sun is on trial at the High Court at Auckland charged with murdering Elizabeth Zhong at her Sunnyhills home in November 2020. He stabbed her multiple times, then put her body in a suitcase and placed it in the boot of her car, it is alleged.Elizabeth (Ying) Zhong was found stabbed to death in the boot of her car on November 28. Sun denies the killing, saying someone else is responsible.
