Leicestershire County Council left visually-impaired woman without help
bbc.com
news
2022-10-20 05:07:38

Image caption, The council said the woman had declined support but the investigation found this was not the caseA council has been criticised after a severely visually-impaired woman was left for 21 months without day-to-day support she was entitled to.The Local Government and Social Care Ombudsman said Leicestershire County Council failed to properly help.It said this led to the woman "frequently burning herself" when trying to cook and several falls. The council apologised and agreed to improve how it prepares care and support plans.
