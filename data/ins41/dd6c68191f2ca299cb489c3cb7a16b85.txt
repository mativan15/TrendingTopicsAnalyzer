His holding in the company declined to 6.2 per cent from 7.5 per cent earlier.New Delhi: Ace investor Rakesh Jhunjhunwala has trimmed his stake in the casino and recreation company , suggest media reports. According to ET Now, Jhunjhunwala, often called Big Bull, sold 25 lakh shares of Delta Corp between May 27-31. His holding in the company declined to 6.2 per cent from 7.5 per cent earlier.
