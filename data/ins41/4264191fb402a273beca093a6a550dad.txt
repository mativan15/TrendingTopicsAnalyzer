6 Metropolitan Police officers caught for being high on narcotics while working in UK
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 13:45:00

AgenciesAt least six officers from the Metropolitan Police were caught for being high while working after consumption of drugs, such as cocaine, benzodiazepines and opiates.According to reports, six Metropolitan Police officers were high on narcotics while working recently. Last year, two cops tested positive for cannabis while on duty, while two more failed tests for ingesting class-A cocaine. As per reports, another Met officer had evidence of opiates in the system, including morphine, and the sixth officer had residues of benzodiazepines and amphetamines in the system.
