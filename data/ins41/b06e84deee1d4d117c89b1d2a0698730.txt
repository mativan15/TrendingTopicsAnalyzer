Passive investing in an index fund is a good way to ensure your own returns roughly match the overall market. Active investors aim to buy stocks that vastly outperform the market - but in the process, they risk under-performance. Investors in Flywire Corporation (NASDAQ:FLYW) have tasted that bitter downside in the last year, as the share price dropped 39%. That contrasts poorly with the market decline of 11%. We wouldn't rush to judgement on Flywire because we don't have a long term history to look at.
