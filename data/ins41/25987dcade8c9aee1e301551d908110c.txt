Glatfelter Shares Rise 7% After Carlson Capital Reports Stake
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 21:42:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Glatfelter Corp. were trading higher after hours Monday, following news that Carlson Capital L.P. reported a 12.1% stake in the supplier of engineered materials. In a filing with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, Carlson Capital said it intended to engage in discussions with Glatfelter's management and board about issues including "business and operations, management and strategic alternatives and direction, including how to enhance stockholder value."
