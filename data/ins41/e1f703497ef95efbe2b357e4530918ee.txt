Shareholders might have noticed that Target Healthcare REIT PLC (LON:THRL) filed its yearly result this time last week. The early response was not positive, with shares down 9.5% to UK£0.81 in the past week. Target Healthcare REIT beat revenue forecasts by a solid 12% to hit UK£64m. Statutory earnings per share fell 15% short of expectations, at UK£0.082.
