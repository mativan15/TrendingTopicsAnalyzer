Friends of Australian Sean Turnell concerned after fatal bomb blast at Myanmar prison where he is being held
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-20 08:58:38

Friends of Australian economist Sean Turnell have expressed concerns after a bomb attack at the Myanmar prison where he is being held.Eight people were killed after a bomb blast and subsequent gunfire at Insein prison entranceSean Turnell was transferred there, according to reports, after being sentenced to three years in prison The Australian government is not aware of any Australians who were impacted by the violence in the vicinityFrench news wire Agence France-Presse, citing an anonymous source familiar with the case, reported earlier this week that the Macquarie University academic had been relocated to Insein prison in Myanmar's biggest city, Yangon.
