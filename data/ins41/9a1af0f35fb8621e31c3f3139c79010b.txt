Published: 20:36 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 22:39 EDT, 17 October 2022 The decisive Game 5 of the AL Division Series between the Cleveland Guardians and New York Yankees has been postponed due to rain Monday night. The game was scheduled to begin at 7:07pm EDT on Monday night but due to the continuing wet weather Major League Baseball announced at 9:36pm that it had been rescheduled for 4:07pm Tuesday, when shadows at Yankee Stadium will likely impact hitters.MLB had earlier announced at about 6:20pm that the start was being delayed, over three hours later it confirmed that the game was postponed.
