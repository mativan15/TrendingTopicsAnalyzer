Automakers, banks knock Indian shares off four-week highs

2022-06-04 04:45:00

The Nifty Auto index led losses among sub-indexes with a 1.8% drop. BENGALURU - A bout of late selling in automakers and banks knocked India's blue-chip stock indexes off four-week highs and into negative territory on Friday, although strength in information technology shares and Reliance Industries helped limit the decline.The NSE Nifty 50 index closed 0.26% lower at 16,584.3, while the S&P BSE Sensex slipped 0.09% to 55,769.23. The indexes still clocked their third straight week of advances with gains of around 1.
