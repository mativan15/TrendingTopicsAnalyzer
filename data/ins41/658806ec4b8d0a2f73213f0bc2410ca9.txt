NDIS forecast to cost extra $8.8bn over estimates as Shorten announces independent review
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-18 07:52:07

The Albanese government has revealed the National Disability Insurance Scheme will cost an extra $8.8bn,as it brings forward an independent review into the scheme.Ahead of next Tuesday's budget, the minister for the NDIS, Bill Shorten, confirmed on Tuesday the scheme was now forecast to cost more than $50bn a year by 2025-26.Shorten said the landmark NDIS was 'arguably Australia's best addition to the social framework of Australian society in the 21st century'. However, he said nine years of Coalition neglect had left it 'not in the position which I think Australians would like to see it in'.
