Knoxville Regional: Tennessee-Alabama State score predictions

2022-06-02 19:47:28

The NCAA Tournament Knoxville Regional will take place June 3-6 at Lindsey Nelson Stadium.
Tennessee (53-7) is the No. 1 overall seed in the NCAA Tournament. The Vols will play Knoxville Regional fourth-seed Alabama State (34-23) Friday.
First pitch is slated for 6 p.m. EDT. SEC Network will televise the matchup with Mike Monaco (PxP) and Ben McDonald (analyst) on the call.
No. 2 seed Georgia Tech and No. 3 seed Campbell will play the first game of the Knoxville Regional. First pitch is slated for noon EDT (ESPN+) Friday.
