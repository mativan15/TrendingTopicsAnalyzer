Introduction The question before us is do we buy ARC Resources (OTCPK:AETUF) before earnings, or wait a bit for a slightly better price. In our last article on the company, just over a year ago we correctly identified the company as a likely winner thanks to crazy high gas prices extant then. ARC doubled as gas prices soared last winter. Gas prices have since cooled about 30% following a peak in the high $9s last summer, and have taken the price of ARC shares down with them.
