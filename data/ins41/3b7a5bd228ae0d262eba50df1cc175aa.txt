The Very Good Food Company Receives Nasdaq Notification Regarding Late Filing of its Form 20-F

VANCOUVER, BC, May 26, 2022 /CNW/ - The Very Good Food Company Inc. (NASDAQ: VGFC) (TSXV: VERY.V) (FSE: OSI) ("VERY GOOD" or the "Company"), was notified on May 20, 2022, by The NASDAQ Stock Market LLC ("NASDAQ") that since the Company had not yet filed its Annual Report on Form 20-F (the "Form 20-F") for the year ended December 31, 2021 (the "Filing"), it was no longer in compliance with NASDAQ's Listing Rules (the "Rules") for continued listing.
