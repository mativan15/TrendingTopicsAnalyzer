Published on May 27, 2022 02:18 PM IST PM Narendra Modi launched 150 remote pilot certificates at India's biggest Drone Festival - Bharat Drone Mahotsav 2022 in Delhi's Pragati Maidan. At the event, PM Modi detailed how technology and drones were helping him keep a track of development across India. He noted that the enthusiasm being seen in India regarding drone technology is amazing and said that this will emerge as a sector of employment generation in India. PM Modi interacted with kisan drone pilots and witnessed open-air drone demonstrations in the drone exhibition centre.
