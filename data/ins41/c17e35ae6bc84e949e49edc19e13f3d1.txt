The two back-to-back collapse of landmark structures this week due to incessant rains has brought to fore poor maintenance of heritage buildings in the city and the lurking threat to public safety. While a portion of the Maharani's Science College collapsed on Friday, a stretch of the Mysuru palace Fort had collapsed on Monday ostensibly due to incessant rains lashing the region.
