Geordie Shore star Charlotte Crosby has reportedly given birth to a baby girl.
The 32-year-old announced the exciting news that she was expecting her first child with boyfriend Jake Ankers in April.
Since then, she's spoken openly about her pregnancy journey, sharing her hopes, joy and excitement – as well as her fears and worries – as she gets ready for their new arrival.
Sharing the happy news on Twitter, Charlotte's dad Gary penned: ‘So, officially a grand father! Little girl, all is well.
‘Excited for the new chapter in our little family.
