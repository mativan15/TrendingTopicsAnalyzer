NEWPORT BEACH, Calif., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Verb Technology Company, Inc. (Nasdaq: VERB) ("VERB" or the "Company"), the leader in interactive video-based sales-enablement applications, including shoppable livestream video, announces that on, June 2 and June 3, three of its newest brand partners will go live on the MARKET platform from the West Coast. The brands feature a variety of products from jewelry to sporting goods.
