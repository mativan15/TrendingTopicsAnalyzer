No mountain too high for vegan mountaineer Prakriti Varshney
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-02 10:29:14

Prakriti Varshney is the first Indian female vegan to ascend the highest peak in the world, Mount Everest, on May 12. Only having returned to Manali, India a few days back this ardent mountaineer is also already planning her next climb.Varshney is a solo traveller and passionate about mountain climbing and going on treks. She blogs about her experiences and shares it with her audience on social media as well. 'I didn't like city life. I left my job to travel. And eventually, I ended up finding a place (Manali) where felt like I belonged,' she says.
