Coinbase extends hiring freeze, rescinds 'a number' of accepted job offers
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 21:56:00

Cryptocurrency marketplace Coinbase Global Inc. said Thursday it will extend its hiring freeze indefinitely, and rescind 'a number' of already accepted job offers. 'We will extend our hiring pause for the foreseeable future,' Coinbase COIN, +7.56% said in a blog post, citing current market conditions and efforts to revamp its business. 'We will also rescind a number of outstanding offers for people who have not started yet.' The company said incoming hires will be notified of the rescinded job offers by email, and indicated they would be paid a severance.
