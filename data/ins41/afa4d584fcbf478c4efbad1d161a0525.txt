Published: 02:34 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 02:34 EDT, 22 October 2022 There are many ways to make money from your house — taking in a lodger, using it as a filming location, holiday letting. But with most of these methods, there is a substantial amount of work and loss of privacy. If you have a period property, however, there's one way that can raise cash: sell it. But don't sell all of it: sell the bits that you don't want to an architectural salvage yard. Salvage — or 'reclamation' — yards buy everything from 1970s taps to Elizabethan floorboards and sell the stock on to others who repair or renovate their homes.
