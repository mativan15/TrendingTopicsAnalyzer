Square Announces Plans to Bring Tap to Pay on iPhone to Square Sellers Later This Year, Launches Early Access Program Today
businesswire.com
news
2022-06-02 00:00:00

June 02, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Square today announced that it is working with Apple to enable Tap to Pay on iPhone, Apple's contactless payment acceptance capability, within the Square Point of Sale app. The new offering enables sellers of all sizes to seamlessly and securely accept in-person contactless payments with only an iPhone and Square's industry leading POS app. Ahead of publicly launching the service later this year, Square also announced an Early Access Program for Tap to Pay on iPhone, allowing select sellers to begin testing the new technology.
