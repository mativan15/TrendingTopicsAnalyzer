Valsoft Acquires Maintenance & Manufacturing Software Developer, Idhammar Systems

MONTREAL, QC / ACCESSWIRE / May 31, 2022 / Valsoft Corporation  Inc. ('Valsoft'), a Montreal-based company specialising in the acquisition and development of vertical market software businesses, is pleased to announce the acquisition of Idhammar Systems Limited, a leader in maintenance and manufacturing software, headquartered in Bristol, United Kingdom.'We spent a long time deliberating on the sale of the business and are confident that, in Valsoft, we have found a shareholder to take the company to the next phase of its evolution,' said John Roberts, exiting founder and Director of Idhammar.
