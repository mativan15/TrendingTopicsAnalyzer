Australian Govt Downgrades Growth Prospects for Major Economies
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 03:57:00

By James Glynn SYDNEY--After talks in the U.S. with government and market officials over the past week, Australian Treasurer Jim Chalmers on Monday warned of a coming global downturn, while announcing sharp downgrades to the growth prospects of major economies. "The world is tiptoeing a narrowing and more perilous path when it comes to the prospect of another global downturn," he told reporters in Canberra. The Australian government now expects the U.S. economy to grow by just 1% over the next year, down from an earlier expectation of 2.
