Newcastle United striker Alexander Isak will not play again until after the World Cup finals after suffering an injury setback, manager Eddie Howe said on Tuesday.The Swedish forward, signed in a deal reported to be worth up to 60 million pounds ($67.82 million) from Real Sociedad in August, has made only three appearances because of a thigh injury."We don't think we will see him again before the World Cup.
