SC refuses to shift conman Sukesh Chandrashekhar from Mandoli prison
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 07:02:44

NEW DELHI: The Supreme Court on Tuesday refused to entertain a fresh plea by alleged 'conman' Sukesh Chandrashekhar seeking transfer from Mandoli jail in the city to any other jail which is not under control of the Director General of Prisons, Delhi.A bench of Justices Ajay Rastogi and C T Ravikumar declined to interfere in the matter."We find no reason to entertain the petition. We don't appreciate this kind of behaviour," the bench said.The counsel appearing for the alleged conman submitted before the bench that in spite of several assurances Chandrashekhar was being allegedly assaulted in jail.
