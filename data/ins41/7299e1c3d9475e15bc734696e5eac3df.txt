By Emily Prescott For The Mail On Sunday Published: 18:45 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 18:45 EDT, 15 October 2022 She may be one of the world's most famous supermodels, but this didn't get Naomi Campbell off the hook when she turned up nearly an hour late to a fashion show in Paris.Despite the Louis Vuitton show at the Louvre starting 45 minutes late, Campbell was still turned away from her seat when she arrived 15 minutes later.But rather than throwing a strop, the 52-year-old, who's well versed in Fashion Week etiquette after her long career on the catwalk, patiently watched the show from the sides before posing happily for photographs in her two-tone metallic trench coat, right.
