Champions League prize money: How much can Liverpool and Real Madrid earn by winning final
express.co.uk

2022-05-28 09:00:00

Liverpool could pocket a cool £93million if they beat Real Madrid in the Champions League final on Saturday, with the losing side only missing out on an extra £3.8m. But due to their domination over the past decade, Los Blancos are set to earn more than Liverpool even if they lose.Due to the prize money that Liverpool have accumulated over this season's Champions League, they are set to receive £93m with a win over Madrid, although Los Blancos can expect £102m should they be successful in Paris, according to The Mirror.
