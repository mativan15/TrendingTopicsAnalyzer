News Corp. and Fox Corp. announced Friday they were exploring whether to merge again nearly a decade after they split. The move would combine Fox news, sports and business channels with News Corp.'s properties such as the Wall Street Journal and the New York Post. Shares of News Corp. rose while Fox shares fell Monday as investors weighed the possibility of Rupert Murdoch's media companies recombining nearly decade after they split.
