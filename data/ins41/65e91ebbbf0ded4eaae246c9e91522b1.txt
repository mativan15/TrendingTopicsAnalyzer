KENNETT SQUARE, Pa., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Genesis Rehab Services, one of the nation's largest rehabilitation therapy providers, and its sister company Respiratory Health Services, today announced a corporate rebrand and launch. At the heart of these rebranding activities is a change of each company's name to Powerback - as described below - along with updated corporate logos, and a reimagined mission, vision, and values.



Genesis Rehab Services is now Powerback Rehabilitation.
