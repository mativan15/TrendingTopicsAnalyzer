Interpol meet: Pakistan non-committal on whether it'll hand over Dawood Ibrahim and Hafiz Saeed to India
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 13:15:00

ANIButt put his finger on his lips in response to reporters' question about if India would get Dawood and Saeed.At the Interpol meet in Delhi today, media asked a top Pakistani delegate whether underworld don Dawood Ibrahim would be handed over to India. Questions were also asked about handing over Hafiz Saeed, the mastermind of 26/11 Mumbai terror attacks. Saeed and Dawood are among terrorists most wanted by Indian security agencies. These two are widely believed to be being provided safe asylum by Pakistan.
