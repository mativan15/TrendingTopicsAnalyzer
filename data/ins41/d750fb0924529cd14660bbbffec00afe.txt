NEW ORLEANS, May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ('KSF') and KSF partner, former Attorney General of Louisiana, Charles C. Foti, Jr., remind investors that they have until July 22, 2022 to file lead plaintiff applications in a securities class action lawsuit against CareDx, Inc. ('CareDx' or the 'Company') (NasdaqGM: CDNA), if they purchased the Company's shares between February 24, 2021 and May 5, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'). This action is pending in the United States District Court for the Northern District of California.
