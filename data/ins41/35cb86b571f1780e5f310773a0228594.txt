Getty ImagesOn National Stock Exchange (NSE), the counter was listed at a premium of 5 per cent at Rs 270 against the issue price.New Delhi: eMudhra made a positive debut on Dalal Street by listing at Rs 271 per share, a premium of 6 per cent over its issue price of Rs 255 on BSE. On National Stock Exchange (NSE), the counter was listed at a premium of 5 per cent at Rs 270 against the issue price. A day before listing, shares of eMudhra were exchanging hands at a marginal discount in the grey market, signaling towards the muted or at par listing of the company.
