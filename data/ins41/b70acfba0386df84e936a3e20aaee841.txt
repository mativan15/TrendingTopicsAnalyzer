Activists of the Bharatiya Janata Party(BJP) staged a demonstration in front of the Prakasam Bhavan here on Wednesday in protest against desecration of a 10th century idol of Nandikeswara at the Siva temple at Kanaparthi village, near N.G. Padu, in Prakasam district.Leading the protest, BJP district president S. Srinivasulu demanded that the police nab miscreants and take stern action against them and book cases under the Ancient Monuments Preservation Act.
