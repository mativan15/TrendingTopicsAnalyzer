PTIFrance's Leolia Jeanjean plays a shot against Romania's Irina-Camelia Begu during their third round match at the French Open tennis tournament in Roland Garros stadium in Paris, France. AP/PTI(Leolia Jeanjean is a French tennis player. On 25 April 2022, Jeanjean reached a career-high ranking of World No. 222. She achieved her career-high World No. 248 ranking in doubles on 18 April 2022. Until knee injuries derailed her career, Jeanjean was one of France's greatest hopes. However, she became the first wild card in 34 years to reach the last 32, losing to former world number one Karolina Pliskova.
