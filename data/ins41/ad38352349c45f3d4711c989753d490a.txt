Boris Johnson urged to make comeback after Liz Truss resigns
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-21 09:48:56

Good morning.Britain's Liz Truss has resigned as prime minister and will step down after a week-long emergency contest to find her successor, she announced outside Downing Street, leaving the possibility of a return of Boris Johnson.Her resignation follows a turbulent 45 days in office during which Truss's mini-budget crashed the markets, making her the UK's shortest-serving prime minister.The prospect of Boris Johnson returning to Downing Street is dominating debate amid fevered speculation the former premier is plotting a comeback.
