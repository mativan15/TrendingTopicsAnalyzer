October 20, 2022 06:32 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KBRA assigns a preliminary rating to one class of notes issued by Gracie Point International Funding 2022-3, Series 2022-3 ('Gracie Point').

Gracie Point formed in February 2010 is in the business of financing life insurance policy premium payments. Gracie Point's clients are high net worth individuals, procuring or having procured life insurance for estate planning or business purposes.

This transaction is secured by a special unit of beneficial ownership interest ('SUBI') of Gracie Point Collateral Trust (the 'Collateral Trust').
