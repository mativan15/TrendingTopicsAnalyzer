Non-binary runners in NYC Marathon to get cash prize for 1st time
espn.com
news
2022-10-17 20:14:00

4:14 PM ETFor the first time in the history of the New York City Marathon, prize money will be awarded to non-binary runners during next month's race. The New York Road Runners announced on Monday that the top five finishers in the category will receive a cash prize."We are so proud that the TCS New York City Marathon attracts such a diverse and global community of runners, and we are deeply focused on ensuring all of our athletes feel welcome and included at NYRR," Kerin Hempel, the NYRR Runners CEO, said in a press release.
