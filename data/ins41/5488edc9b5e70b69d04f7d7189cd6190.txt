If you're a fan of buying blue-chip stocks while they're on sale, now certainly seems like the time to go shopping. Although the Dow Jones Industrial Average (^DJI -1.05%) essentially finished last month where it started, Dow constituents Cisco Systems (CSCO -1.18%), Boeing (BA -0.89%), and Walmart (WMT -1.72%) lost 8%, 12%, and nearly 16%, respectively, of their value in May.
However, before plowing into these well-known names just because they've been up-ended, you might want to take a step back and look at the bigger picture.
