Record attempt for continuous paving the longest stretch of road to begin today
indiatimes.com
gaming
2022-06-03 00:19:00

Amravati: A team of 728 men, armed with high-tech machines, will start an attempt – reportedly first time by any Indian firm on national highway – to enter the Guinness Book of World Records for continuous paving the longest stretch of road to commemorate the ‘Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav' at 7am on Friday at Loni village between Amravati and Akola.Pune-based Raj Path Infracon Pvt Ltd aims to achieve this feat in 108 hours, roughly by Tuesday, June 7.NHAI officials will be monitoring this process and ensure the quality and safety.
