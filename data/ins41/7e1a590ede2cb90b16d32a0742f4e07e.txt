Ibstock Sees 2022 Performance Ahead of Views After Strong 3Q
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 06:44:00

By Kyle Morris Ibstock PLC said Tuesday that it expects performance for 2022 to be above its previous expectations after the third quarter was ahead amid robust demand patterns and strong operational performance. The U.K. manufacturer of clay bricks and concrete products said effective cost management, including energy costs, and the resilience of the business model have supported performance. "Whilst we remain mindful of the elevated macroeconomic uncertainty, we now anticipate performance for the 2022 year to be above our previous expectations," Chief Executive Joe Hudson said.
