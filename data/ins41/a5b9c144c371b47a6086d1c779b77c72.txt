Asian equities were largely higher overnight though Japan, Taiwan, and Australia were down. The key news was President Xi's two-hour Sunday speech, which kicked off the week-long Party Congress. Check out our own Dr. Xiaolin Chen's analysis of the opening speech here. The key takeaway for investors was the emphasis on economic development. The seeds of policy change may have been planted as we've seen a number of pro-economic policy tweaks in the past few weeks. Yes, the speech included the success of zero covid in curtailing deaths, though we've seen a clear change since the Shanghai lockdown.
