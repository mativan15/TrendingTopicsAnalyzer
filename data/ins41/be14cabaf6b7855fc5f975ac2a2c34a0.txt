pp76/iStock via Getty Images 1Q22 Summary Nvidia (NASDAQ:NVDA) reported 1Q22 results (FY1Q23 ended in April) with record revenue of $8.3B (+46% YoY) and adjusted EPS of $1.36 (+48% YoY) that both beat consensus estimates. Non-GAAP gross margin of 67.1% saw a 90 bp expansion from 66.2% in 1Q21. Given a $1.35B termination charge related to the ARM acquisition, GAAP operating margin was 22.5% vs. 47.7% non-GAAP. The company ended the quarter with a $9.4B net cash position. On 5/23, the board of directors increased the buyback program by $15B through the end of 2023.
