ET Canada Awkward…Khloé Kardashian took a trip down memory lane on Thursday's episode of 'The Kardashians' on Hulu. Deciding she needs a mom's night out (but don't call it that!) with her older sister, Kim Kardashian, amid the ongoing drama with her ex Tristan Thompson, Khloé and Kim take Miami, Florida, for a Skims swimwear launch and a night of drunken fun.When they touch down in Miami — on Kim Air, obvi — Khloé immediately calls up her former partner in crime, Kourtney Kardashian, to reminisce about their days on the spinoff series, 'Kourtney and Khloé Take Miami', which ran for three seasons in 2009.
