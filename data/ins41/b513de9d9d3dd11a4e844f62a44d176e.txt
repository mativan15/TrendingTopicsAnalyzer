 An 83-year-old US Army veteran died while riding the PeopleMover at Disney World in Orlando. Joseph Masters, who served in the Army National Guard at Camp Smith Military Base for 23 years, died on September 25 after riding the slow-moving train that runs through Tomorrowland. Masters' wife of 49 years, Alice, told police that Joseph went unconscious while they were on the ride.
