Opinions expressed by Entrepreneur contributors are their own. People often react negatively to change. They fear change, because they fear the unknown. However, when the winds of change blow, a few manage to build windmills and come out on top. One such change the business world has seen recently is the rise in hiring challenges (or competitions) among recruiters.Having been in the recruiting landscape for more than a decade now, I have seen waxes and wanes in the hiring methods from year to year.
