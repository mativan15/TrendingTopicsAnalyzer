Every dog has its day, and now it seems, every virus has its day. From the viruses that have caused the Spanish flu to the latest tomato flu, these agents come and go, but the king of the ring is undoubtedly SARS-CoV-2, the novel coronavirus, so much for what this 0.1-micron thing has done to this world. Apart from the medical aspects, there are some interesting para-science facets of this virus. It did turn the world upside down; well, I mean the social paradoxes that surfaced during the pandemic.
