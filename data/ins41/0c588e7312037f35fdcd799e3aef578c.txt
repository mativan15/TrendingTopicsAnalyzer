Israel refuses Ukrainian demand for weapons

2022-10-19 15:45:00

West Jerusalem has shut down discussions on arms transfers to Kiev after a member of the government broke ranksIsraeli Justice Minister Gideon Saar declared on Tuesday that his country will not send weapons to Ukraine, despite Diaspora Affairs Minister Nachman Shai previously suggesting otherwise. Kiev has repeatedly pressed West Jerusalem for arms."Our support for Ukraine does not include weapons systems and weaponry - and there is no change to that position," Saar told Israeli broadcaster Army Radio on Tuesday, Haaretz reported.
