Extends Revolving and Term Loan Facilities, Increases Term Loan With no Change to Terms and Conditions of Agreements
KINGSEY FALLS, QC, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ - Cascades Inc. (TSX: CAS) announces that it has amended its existing credit agreement to increase its authorized term loan to US$260 million from US$160 million and extend the maturity from December 2025 to December 2027. The Term loan, which can be repaid at any time, will be used to repay certain of the Company's outstanding borrowings related to the strategic Bear Island converting project under its existing revolving credit facility.
