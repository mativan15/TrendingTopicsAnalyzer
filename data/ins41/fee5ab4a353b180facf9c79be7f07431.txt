US stocks open lower as Microsoft offsets oil relief
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 14:15:00

US stock indexes opened lower on Thursday as shares of Microsoft fell after the company lowered its profit forecast, offsetting relief from a pullback in oil prices that eased worries about rising inflation. The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 4.22 points, or 0.01%, at the open to 32,809.01.

 The S&P 500 opened lower by 5.82 points, or 0.14%, at 4,095.41, while the Nasdaq Composite dropped 48.89 points, or 0.41%, to 11,945.57 at the opening bell.
