UK Inflation Returns to Double Digits as Food Prices Soar
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-19 06:04:29

Soaring food prices drove UK inflation back into double digits in September, intensifying pressure on the government and central bank to act.

The Consumer Prices Index rose 10.1% last month from 9.9% the month before, the Office for National Statistics said Wednesday. That matched a 40-year high reached in July and exceeded economists expectations for 10%.
