Ukraine war: FATF imposes additional restrictions on Russia
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 17:57:36

The Financial Action Task Force (FATF) has decided to impose additional restrictions, including barring Russia from current and future projects, FATF President T Raja Kumar said in a presser on Friday.Due to the destruction caused by Russia's invasion of Ukraine and the needless loss of lives in the country, Russia has been restricted to participate in meetings of FATF's regional partner bodies as the task force's members."FATF repeatedly condemned Russia's invasion of Ukraine. Following plenary discussions this week, FATF decided to impose additional restrictions, incl bar Russia from current and future projects and participating in meetings of FATF's regional partner bodies as a FATF member," FATF President T Raja Kumar said.
