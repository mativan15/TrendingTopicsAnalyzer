Shares of several consumer-focused fintech stocks rose today, as investors digested new economic data and thoughts on the economy from several prominent experts.
Shares of the artificial intelligence lender Upstart Holdings (UPST 12.46%) traded more than 12% higher as of 1:17 p.m. ET today. Meanwhile, shares of the Brazilian digital bank Nu Holdings (NU 15.45%) also traded roughly 12% higher, and shares of the Brazilian payments company StoneCo (STNE 7.72%) were up 7%.
