F3 movie review: Venkatesh, Varun Tej film is less fun, more frustration
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-27 10:24:37

When you pay for a seat in a cinema hall to watch a movie, Venkatesh and Varun Tej-starrer F3, that has been marketed as the ‘biggest Telugu comedy entertainer of the year', you come prepared to laugh. You go in with a lot of enthusiasm, putting complete faith in the hands of the filmmakers and actors. You are psyched to be entertained, popcorn is in the bucket and all's right with the world. When things start slow, you choose to stay with the filmmaker — the film will will soon pick up tempo, it has to.
