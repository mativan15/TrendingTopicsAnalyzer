Hiring Event Attendees Can Interview for Jobs, May Receive Offers on Site

EIGHTY FOUR, Pa., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- 84 Lumber, the nation's largest privately held building materials supplier, is recruiting for six immediate openings in greater Buffalo, NY, as it seeks to fill job openings across its enterprise. Job seekers can interview for open positions at a hiring event on October 25, 2022, from 7 a.m. to 5 p.m. at the company's Depew location (3317 Walden Avenue, Depew, NY). The company operates five locations in western New York and employs nearly 40 people in the region.
