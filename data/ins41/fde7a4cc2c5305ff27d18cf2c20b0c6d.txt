Caesars and SL Green want to open a casino in Times Square
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 13:47:00

Gambling giant Caesars Entertainment Inc. and New York developer SL Green Realty Corp. on Thursday said they want to develop a casino in New York City's Times Square.

The Reno, Nevada-based gambling company (Nasdaq: CZR) and the New York real estate investment trust said they want to redevelop 1515 Broadway in the heart of Times Square into a casino.

The companies said the casino will pump "billions in tax revenue to New York City and state" and "will also accelerate New York's economic recovery through strategic partnerships with area businesses.
