lucky-photographer/iStock via Getty Images Biotech Pulse The stock market has retreated sharply this year and quite relentlessly over the past few weeks. Biotechs have been in a retreat since late last year and the industry group is well into a deep bear market with valuations on the S&P Biotechnology Index (XBI) slashed by over 60% from its highs. In the Biotech Outlook, we discussed the uphill task that investors face this year. It has been very difficult thus far, but there will be selective opportunities to recover lost ground and perhaps record some gains this year.
