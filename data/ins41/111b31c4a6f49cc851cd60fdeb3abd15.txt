NOVA Jury Rules Mostly In Favor Of Depp In Amber Heard Defamation Trial
iheart.com
news
2022-06-01 20:45:00

Depp found guilty of one count of defamation Heard found guilty on all three counts of defamation Depp – Awarded $15 million - $10 million in compensatory (money he would have made if not for the defamation) and $5 million punitive (meant to punish Amber Heard.) VA has a punitive damages cap of $350,000, so technically Depp gets $10,350,000. Heard – Awarded $2 million in compensatory (money she would have earned if not for the defamation.) She did not receive any punitive damages from Depp. There will be appeals and the possibility they could still settle out of court.
