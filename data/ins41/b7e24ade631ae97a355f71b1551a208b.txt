Corporation, participates in a Business Roundtable discussion on the"Future of Work in an Era of Automation and Artificial Intelligence", during a CEO Innovation Summit, on December 6, 2018 in Washington, DC.FAYETTEVILLE, Ark. -- Thousands of Walmart employees gathered at a huge arena on Friday, dancing as the Jonas Brothers headlined the return of the annual event that acts as a company pep rally. Yet despite the festive backdrop, CEO Doug McMillon acknowledged the new challenge facing the company: inflation.
