Almost 100,000 people were employed at Tesla and its subsidiaries at the end of 2021 (Image: Getty)The Tesla chief executive has imposed a 10 per cent cut of jobs at the electric carmaker and ordered a ‘pause all hiring worldwide'. The email to executives on Thursday – seen by the Reuters News Agency - came two days after the billionaire told staff to return to the workplace or leave.It adds to a growing chorus of warnings from business leaders about the risks of recession.Almost 100,000 people were employed at Tesla and its subsidiaries at the end of 2021, latest records show.
