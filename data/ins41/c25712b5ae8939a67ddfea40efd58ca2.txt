5:00 PM ETWelcome to Week 6 of the 2022 NFL season, where no top-10 team is safe.The Green Bay Packers' offense was silent in a loss to the New York Jets -- just one week after losing to the New York Giants. Speaking of the Giants, they handed Lamar Jackson and the Baltimore Ravens their third loss of the season. Meanwhile, the San Francisco 49ers (at No. 6 in our NFL Power Rankings) and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers (No. 9) suffered losses at the hands of the Atlanta Falcons and Pittsburgh Steelers, respectively.
