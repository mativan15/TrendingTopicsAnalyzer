By Alexis Wainwright May 31, 2022 / 10:45 PM / CBS DFW DALLAS (CBSDFW.COM) - There are still concerns about safety in Deep Ellum and there was a conversation to address what's being done during Deep Ellum's monthly public safety meeting.All the businesses and people who work and live in the area told CBS 11 there's still a lot of fear because of the recent gun violence that's happened."This is the third watch activity, this is the nighttime that drives perception, and this is what makes the news, we are down 21% for violent crimes," Deep Ellum Public Safety Director, Adam Holden said.
