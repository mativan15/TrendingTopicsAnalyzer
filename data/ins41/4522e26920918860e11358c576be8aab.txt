Byron Trott's BDT merging with Michael Dell's MSD in billionaire tie-up
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 13:44:46

BDT & Company Holdings LLC and MSD Partners LP said they would combine in a merger of two billionaire-controlled investment firms. The name of the combined firm had not yet been determined. Billionaire Byron Trott of BDT, an investment firm founded by Trott after he became known as the banker for Warren Buffett, will be co-CEO of the combined company, along with Gregg Lemkau, who is CEO of MSD Partners, the investment firm founded by Dell Technologies Inc. DELL, +2.00% founder and billionaire Michael Dell.
