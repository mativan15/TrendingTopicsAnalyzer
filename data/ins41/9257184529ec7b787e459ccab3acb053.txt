Samsung app confirms Galaxy Watch 5, Galaxy Watch 5 Pro smartwatches
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 08:11:00

NEW DELHI: Rumours are rife that Samsung is working on its next-generation Galaxy smartwatches likely to be called Galaxy Watch 5 and Galaxy Watch 5 Pro. The company has now released a One UI 4.5 Beta for users to test out on the Samsung Galaxy Watch 4. The beta version of the Samsung Health app reveals a list of compatible devices. In this list, the company has accidentally confirmed the two yet-to-launch Galaxy smartwatches -- Galaxy Watch 5 and the Galaxy Watch 5 Pro.

On the basis of the new revelation, Samsung is said to launch two models of its upcoming smartwatch and there will be no Classic model this time with the popular rotating bezel.
