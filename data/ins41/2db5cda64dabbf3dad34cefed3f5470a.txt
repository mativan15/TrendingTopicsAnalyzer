People are raving over an alternative to an air fryer which can be bought for a fraction of the cost (Image: Birmingham Mail)Thousands of people across the UK have ditched their ovens in a bid to save money during the cost of living crisis. Opting for cooking meals in smaller appliances, people are raving over an alternative to an air fryer which can be bought for just a fraction of the price.In recent months, shoppers have sworn by air fryers - small cooking appliances designed to simulate deep frying without submerging the food in oil.
