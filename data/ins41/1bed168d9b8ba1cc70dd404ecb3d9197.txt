Check out the companies making headlines in midday trading. Salesforce — Shares of the enterprise-software maker jumped nearly 10% after the company's stronger-than-expected quarterly earnings report. Salesforce also lifted its full-year earnings guidance, but reduced its guidance for revenue. The company said it's slowing down in hiring and isn't looking to make another big purchase at this point after its acquisition of Slack. Delta — The stock fell 5% after the airline said it expects sales in the current quarter to return to pre-pandemic levels.
