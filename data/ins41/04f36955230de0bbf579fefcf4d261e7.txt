Supply woes hit uber-luxury residential projects
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 04:45:00

AgenciesThe disruption in supply started with the Covid-induced lockdowns, and with China shutting down intermittently, things never got back to normal. This was followed by the Russia-Ukraine war, which aggravated the supply problem.Unfinished bathrooms are holding up delivery of uber-luxury homes in several real estate projects in India as suppliers of stylish, imported faucets, commodes and wash basins struggle to get hold of wares stuck at the ports. Real estate builders use imported faucets, wash basins, western commodes, bidets, shower enclosures and bathtubs in the uber-luxury homes they sell.
