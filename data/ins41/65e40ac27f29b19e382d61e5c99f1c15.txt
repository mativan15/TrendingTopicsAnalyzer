Adobe shares edge upward as analysts assess 'conservative' 2023 outlook

hapabapa/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Adobe (NASDAQ:ADBE) shares rose by as much as 3% Wednesday as Wall Street began to assess the media-software company's outlook for next year, and its $20B acquisition of online design company Figma. Late Tuesday, Adobe (ADBE) used its Adobe Max Creativity Conference to say that for 2023, it expects to report earnings, excluding one-time items, of $15.15 to $15.45 a share, on revenue in a range of $19.1B to $19.3B. Adobe also reiterated its fourth-quarter earnings and revenue outlook.
