Ghaziabad bans pit bull, Rottweiler, Dogo Argentino as pets
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 05:15:00

GHAZIABAD: The Ghaziabad Municipal Corporation (GMC) on Saturday banned the domestication of three breeds of dogs - pit bull, Rottweiler and Dogo Argentino - in the city.The decision comes in the wake of recent dog attacks in the city and was taken at the GMC board meeting, probably the last before the announcement of municipal polls in the state."Our guidelines mandate the registration of pet dogs as well as compulsory training for these three breeds," said a GMC official. "But in the wake of the recent dog attacks, the GMC board has passed a proposal banning the domestication of these three breeds.
