REUTERS/Carla Carniel/File PhotoBRASILIA, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Brazil's nomination to head the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB) is facing resistance among allies of leftist presidential frontrunner Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva, who want him to have a formal say if he wins office this month.
