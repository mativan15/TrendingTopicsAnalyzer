Kerala students develop electric car; win laurels at global competition
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-10-18 10:45:00

An electric car, designed by students of the Government Engineering College Barton Hill here, has bagged an award and an honorable mention at the international energy efficiency competition, Shell Eco-Marathon (SEM) 2022, held at Pertamina Mandalika circuit in Indonesia recently. 'Vandy,' the electric car designed by Pravega, a team of 19 students from the mechanical stream of the college, with the mentorship of Acsia Technologies emerged as the best from multiple entries across the world, a statement said.
