'The work of our winners protects our schools, workplaces, and national infrastructures against cyber vulnerabilities and attacks,' said Jennifer Taylor, NVTC President & CEO. MCLEAN, Va. (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Northern Virginia Technology Council (NVTC), the trade association representing the National Capital Region's technology community, today announced the winners of the fourth annual NVTC Capital Cyber Awards. Presented during this year's NVTC Capital Cybersecurity Summit held on Wednesday, October 19, 2022, the honorees represent the brightest and most cutting-edge organizations and government contractors that lead the region's thriving cybersecurity industry.
