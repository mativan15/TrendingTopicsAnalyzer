By Australian Associated Press Published: 23:11 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 23:11 EDT, 18 October 2022 Western United will make their A-League Women debut with a touch of star power up their sleeve after signing World Cup-winning striker Jessica McDonald on a guest contract. The veteran American will arrive in November on a three-month deal, meaning she will be available for the opening nine matches of the season. McDonald won the World Cup with the United States in 2019, and has scored four goals from 19 games for her country.
