Published: 10:54 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 10:58 EDT, 20 October 2022 Sir Keir Starmer today stepped up his demand for a general election in the wake of Liz Truss's resignation as the Labour leader called for an end to the Tory 'soap opera'.Soon after Ms Truss announced she was ending her historically short spell as Prime Minister, Sir Keir insisted that voters must have 'their say' at the ballot box.The departing PM intends to hand over to a new Tory leader and PM following a week-long Conservative leadership contest - which will be the second to be held within three months.
