Zscaler leads cyber security stocks higher as analysts call results 'pillar of strength'

Sundry Photography/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Zscaler (NASDAQ:ZS) shares surged 12% on Friday, leading the sector high, after the cloud-based cyber security firm posted third-quarter results that topped expectations and raised its full-year guidance. For the period ending April 30, Zscaler (ZS) said it earned an adjusted $0.17 per share on $286.81M in revenue, up 62.6% year-over-year. Looking ahead, it expects fourth-quarter revenue to be between $304M and $306M, compared to estimates of $291.
