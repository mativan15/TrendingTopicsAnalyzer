getty No one needs to stress the significance of the change from an industrial to an information model in our society. We spend, on average, over half of our waking hours using digital media and apps. We do business over email and Slack, Discord, Telegram and Whatsapp. We elect our officials (arguably) on Facebook and Twitter. We text more than we talk, touch less physical money than ever and sometimes even exercise along with screens.
