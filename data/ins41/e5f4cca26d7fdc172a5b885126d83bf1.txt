TN YouTuber raises funds for temple renovation without permission, arrested
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-30 14:25:04

A YouTuber popular among right-wing Tamil viewers was arrested in Chennai on Monday for allegedly raising funds online purportedly for renovation of temples controlled by the Tamil Nadu government without permission from the authorities concerned, according to the police.
According to police, preliminary investigation showed that besides money collected online, accused Karthick Gopinath, 32, had also received nearly Rs 6 lakh in his personal bank account.
Police said Gopinath has claimed that he was collecting funds for repair and renovation of certain temples 'damaged by miscreants'.
