VICTORIA Coren Mitchell is a British television presenter, author, and international poker player.
She started writing stories and columns at a young age before branching out to broadcasting and becoming the well-known star she is today.

She writes a weekly column for The Observer and hosts the BBC television quiz show Only Connect – a role she has held since 2008.
