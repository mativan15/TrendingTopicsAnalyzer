Boost the Economy by Boosting Child Care

news
2022-10-19 18:22:00

The nation's economic challenges include decidedly mixed indicators: Yes, unemployment sits at 3.5%, matching a 50-year low. But the percentage of the population which is employed still has not returned to pre-pandemic levels: lower shares of both men and women were employed in September 2022 than February 2020.

This problem will be front and center for the new Congress elected in just a few short weeks. But how can policymakers and business leaders fix this labor lag?

They can start by closing the child care gap.
