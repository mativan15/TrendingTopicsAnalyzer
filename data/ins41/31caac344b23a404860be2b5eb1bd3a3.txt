 Police in Kingston, Ont., are looking for suspect who left a hate flyer on a person's car in the west end.
Police say the flyer was left on an unattended car at a shopping plaza on Gardiners Road.'The flyer contained not only anti-Semitic language but also anti-2SLGBTQIA+ rhetoric,' said Kingston police in a press release.
