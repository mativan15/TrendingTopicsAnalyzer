Indian Oil Corp. shares drop 0.09% as Sensex rises
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 07:30:00

Shares of the Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. traded at Rs 114.2 on BSE at 12:22PM (IST) on Friday, down 0.09 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 101.9 and a high of Rs 141.75. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 4.25 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 26.66 per share and 0.82 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 388,922 shares changed hands on the counter till 12:22PM (IST). The stock commands a market value of Rs 106803.
