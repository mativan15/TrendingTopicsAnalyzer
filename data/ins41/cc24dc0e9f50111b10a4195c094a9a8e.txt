Microsoft Corp plans hyperscale data centre in Pune, acquires 25 acres in Pimpri
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 14:45:00

Getty ImagesWhile the total investment in the company's proposed data centre in Pune could not be ascertained, it is expected to be developed in the next 24-36 months.US-based software major Microsoft Corporation is planning to develop a large hyperscale data centre in Pune as part of its multi-city data centre strategy for India, said two persons with direct knowledge of the development. The company's India arm has acquired a 25-acre land parcel in the Pimpri-Waghere locality of the city through an agreement with Finolex Industries to transfer the long-term lease for nearly Rs 329 crore and is planning to develop the data centre on this plot.
