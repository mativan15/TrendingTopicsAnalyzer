Future Ready: The Four Pathways to Capturing Digital Value, authored by MIT CISR research scientists, reveals four pathways to help C-suites and Boards create and capture new value from digital initiativesCAMBRIDGE, Mass., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- A new book released today by research scientists at MIT Center for Information Systems Research (CISR) spotlights a study of 1,311 global firms that finds only 22% are "future ready," having gone through digital business transformation and developed the capabilities that enable them to innovate, engage, and satisfy customers while reducing costs.
