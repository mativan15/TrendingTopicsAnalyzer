ugurhan/iStock via Getty Images I have been investing for over 50 years, and if there is one thing I have learned about forecasting the markets, it is that you can't forecast the markets. So, what is an investor to do?? My investment discipline is straightforward: Try to beat the Cap Weighted Indices like the S&P 500 or the Nasdaq 100 Don't lose money Keep what is working, sell what isn't My market benchmarks are: How many stocks are trading above their 200-Day Moving Averages which I find on Barchart's Market Performance Indicator.
