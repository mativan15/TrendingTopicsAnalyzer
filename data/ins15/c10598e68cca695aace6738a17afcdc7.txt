Company to issue its Fiscal 2022 first quarter results press release on Tuesday, June 14, 2022 at 7:00 a.m. ET with a conference call to follow at 8:00 a.m. ET

TORONTO, May 31, 2022 /CNW/ - Roots ("Roots Canada" or the "Company") (TSX: ROOT), the premium outdoor-lifestyle brand, today announced that the Company will host a conference call to discuss its Fiscal 2022 first quarter results on Tuesday, June 14, 2022. Ms. Meghan Roach, President & Chief Executive Officer, and Ms. Mona Kennedy, Chief Financial Officer, will host the call.
