There's nothing quite like a slab of heavy metal to get the blood pumping. Austrian turntable manufacturer Pro-Ject Audio Systems thinks so too and it's teamed up with the US metal gods to create a Metallica Limited Edition Turntable. Designed at the behest of the earth's mightiest metal band, the new deck delivers spectacular visuals and Pro-Ject's usual impeccable sound quality.
