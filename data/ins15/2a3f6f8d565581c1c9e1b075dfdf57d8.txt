By Zhong Sheng | People's Daily Online | Updated: 2022-06-01 10:42 [China Daily] Thousands of federal, state, local, and tribal systems in the US hold almost 2 million people in 1,566 state prisons, 102 federal prisons, 2,850 local jails, 1,510 juvenile correctional facilities, 186 immigration detention facilities, and 82 Indian country jails, as well as in military prisons, civil commitment centers, state psychiatric hospitals, and prisons in the US territories, said a recent report from American public policy think tank Prison Policy Initiative (PPI).
