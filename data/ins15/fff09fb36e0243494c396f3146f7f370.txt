Hochul needs to galvanize progressives now
nydailynews.com
news
2022-10-20 09:00:00

By any measure, the polls have tightened in the race for New York governor. What was once a blowout has, in some polls, become a single-digit affair. Election denier and Trump acolyte Lee Zeldin has crisscrossed the state fearmongering about crime, with substantial air cover from dark money billionaire backers. Meanwhile, Gov. Hochul has barely engaged in traditional campaigning, while relying on a big-money ad blitz and a 'Rose Garden' strategy of reminding voters of recent governmental accomplishments.
