Published: 20:59 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 21:11 EDT, 17 October 2022 Liz Truss was last night warned she was ‘in office but not in power' amid reports Rishi Sunak met with a senior Penny Mordaunt ally as speculation grows the two could move to oust the Prime Minister after she made a grovelling apology for her failed economic plan.  Mr Sunak, who came second to Ms Truss in the Conservative leadership contest this summer, is said to have spoken to a key Mordaunt backer with a 'tacit' suggestion he could serve as her Chancellor.
