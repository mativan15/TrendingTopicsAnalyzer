Herringbone Highlights Are the Chic New Way of Embracing Your Gray Hair
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-27 22:53:12

Gone are the days of obsessing over every gray hair. Now, we're calling those natural grays highlights. OK, not exactly but the herringbone highlights trend has colorists using each gray strand just like another one of the many highlights in your head of hair. We've seen this trend toward embracing gray hair in the past few years, especially during the pandemic lockdowns when it was harder and harder to book an appointment for gray coverage.

Colorist Tom Smith took to his Instagram to give us the scoop on herringbone highlights.
