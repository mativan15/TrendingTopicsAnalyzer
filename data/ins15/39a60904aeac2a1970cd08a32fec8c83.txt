Published: 23:55 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 23:55 EDT, 17 October 2022 GEELONG, Australia (AP) - Namibia captain Gerhard Erasmus has won the toss and decided to bat against Netherlands on Tuesday in the wake of an upset win over former champion Sri Lanka in the Group A opener at the Twenty20 World Cup. Namibia beat Sri Lanka by 55 runs before Netherlands edged United Arab Emirates with a ball to spare in a low-scoring game on Sunday. Netherlands reached the victory target of 112 in 19.5 overs in the only successful chase in the first four games of the tournament.
