 Halifax Regional Police are looking for suspect with a knife in the Lacewood Drive area, prompting five schools to go into a hold and secure procedure.
Police said they received a report of an assault involving a knife at 2:15 p.m.'Officers arrived on scene and located a man who had been injured. The suspect had fled the scene prior to officers' arrival,' HRP said in a news release.
