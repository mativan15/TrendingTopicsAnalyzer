Super Apps Holdings and Technology & Telecommunication Acquisition Corporation Enter into a Merger Agreement  ●Transaction values Super Apps at an estimated pro forma enterprise value of $1.1 billion upon completion ●With operations based in Malaysia, Super Apps will have a geographic advantage for expanding into the ASEAN market  Super Apps will partner with MobilityOne Sdn. Bhd., a fintech technology company in the payment systems market in Malaysia. Together with OneShop Retail, the combined company is position to be a leading payments systems provider in the ASEAN market ●The combined company will be named TETE Technologies Inc.
