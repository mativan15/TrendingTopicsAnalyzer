The board of InterDigital, Inc. (NASDAQ:IDCC) has announced that it will pay a dividend of US$0.35 per share on the 27th of July. This makes the dividend yield 2.1%, which will augment investor returns quite nicely.

InterDigital's Dividend Is Well Covered By Earnings

Impressive dividend yields are good, but this doesn't matter much if the payments can't be sustained. The last dividend was quite easily covered by InterDigital's earnings. This indicates that quite a large proportion of earnings is being invested back into the business.
