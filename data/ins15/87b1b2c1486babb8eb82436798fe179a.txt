June 02, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SCHAUMBURG, Ill.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Through the first three months of 2022, there were some shifts in the automotive finance market, most notably, significant growth for credit unions. According to Experian's State of the Automotive Finance Market: Q1 2022 report, credit unions amassed 22.06% of the total automotive finance market, up from 18.55% a year prior.
