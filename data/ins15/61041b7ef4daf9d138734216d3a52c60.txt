October 17, 2022 04:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CS Disco, Inc. ('DISCO') (NYSE: LAW), a leader in AI-enabled legal technology, today announced that it will report its financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2022 after market close on Thursday, November 10, 2022.

The financial results and business highlights will be discussed on a conference call and webcast scheduled at 4:00 p.m. Central Time (5:00 p.m. Eastern Time) on Thursday, November 10, 2022.
