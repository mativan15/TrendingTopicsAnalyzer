The results for the tier 2 examination have been declared by the Staff Selection Commission and the candidates can download them from the official website at ssc.nic.in. The examination for the same was conducted in computer based mode on August 8 and 10. The candidates can get the SSC CGL results for Tier 2 in 2021–22 in pdf format with their names and roll numbers. Moreover, the candidates can also check out the SSC CGL cut off 2020–21 along with the tier-II results.

Here's how to check the results:

 Go to the official website of SSC at ssc.
