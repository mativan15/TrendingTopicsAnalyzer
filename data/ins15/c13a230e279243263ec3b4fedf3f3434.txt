With a student population of less than 8%, the Latino community at Penn State has been a small, yet important group on campus, according to many students within that community.Representatives of the Latino Caucus in the University Park Undergraduate Association have continued to navigate problems Latinos face, making changes with their platforms — starting with the lack of representation within Penn State's student government.Refugio Lara first got involved in UPUA during his freshman year after being involved with his student government in high school.
