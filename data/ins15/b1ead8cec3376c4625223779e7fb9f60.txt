Published: 06:25 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 06:25 EDT, 20 October 2022 Furious Amanda Holden took to Instagram on Wednesday to slam a  'nut jobs' pretending to be her on social media. The Britain's Got Talent judge, 51, shared a snap of the fake account and revealed this isn't the first time her identity had been stolen on the social media site.The latest fake account named Amanda_Holden_Live used what appeared to be a professional photo of the television star and had amassed almost 2,000 followers.
