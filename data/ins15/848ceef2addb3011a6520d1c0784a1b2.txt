Inflation is a a bear that eats into your purchasing power. But it's not all bad news. Since federal income tax brackets and some exclusions are indexed to inflation, most people will be able to save on taxes. Inflation indexing is designed to avoid 'bracket creep.' That's when making more money pushes you into a higher income tax bracket. The IRS just revised its brackets for inflation. All told, more than 60 tax provisions were improved due to inflation adjusting.
