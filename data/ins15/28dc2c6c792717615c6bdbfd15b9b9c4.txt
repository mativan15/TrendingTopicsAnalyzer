The minority-certified, family-owned company is approaching the one-year mark since being awarded the five-year contract in November 2021 NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- CTK Trucking & Logistics, a New York City area trucking and junk removal company, is making good on its promises to help NYC area residents, businesses, and governmental operations stay junk-free on the heels of a large recycling contract it was awarded last year. In November 2021, the minority-certified business landed NYC's largest-ever recycling contract, totaling over $6 million in recycling services.
