British singer-songwriter Leo Kalyan is taking TikTok by storm with mash-up covers of our favorite pop songs with Bollywood stylings.

Kalyan started posting the videos earlier this year with "Heat Waves" by Glass Animals. Their third video interpreted "sad girls love money" by Amaarae and garnered over 3.5 million views on the app. Afterwards came more covers featuring tracks by LilNas X, Doja Cat, and even Sade.
