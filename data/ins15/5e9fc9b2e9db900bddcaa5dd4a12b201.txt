When Kraken CEO Jesse Powell, the company's pugnacious yet introverted founder, announced his intention to resign as CEO on September 21st and move to the position of Chairman, few should have been surprised. Powell's anti-woke views on things like gun rights and maternity leave (in an internal message Powell once described children as a 'harmful addiction'' for a private company) had already prompted a New York Times expose about a culture war going on inside his 3,000 person crypto exchange. Kraken had emerged as one of the preeminent crypto exchanges, with backers such as Digital Currency Group and Tribe Capital and a reported valuation of $10.
