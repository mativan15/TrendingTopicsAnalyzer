On June 1, 2022, Victoria's Secret (NYSE: VSC SC O, $44.89, Market Capitalization: $3.7 billion) announced 1Q22 results. Net sales declined 4.5% YOY to $1.5 billion (+0.5% vs. consensus), which was at the high end of the management guidance range. Region wise, North America stores sales decreased 0.2%, direct sales declined by 13.6%, while international sales grew by 31.8%. Gross Profit decreased 22.4% to $521.5 million, and the corresponding margin contracted ~810 bps to 35.
