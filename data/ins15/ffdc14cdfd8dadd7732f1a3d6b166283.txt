ERIK TEN HAG will try and get Manchester United to pursue summer transfer target Frenkie de Jong in January, according to reports.
The Red Devils spent the summer chasing the Dutchman, 25, and even agreed a £72million deal with Barcelona.
United could return for De Jong in JanuaryCredit: Rex
However, the player rejected the move and stayed put at the Camp Nou, despite a row with the Catalan club over £17m worth of wages.
But according to Sport, Ten Hag has asked United to make another attempt to sign De Jong in January.
