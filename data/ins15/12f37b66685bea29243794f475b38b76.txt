DBJ Event: Col. Chris Meeker of 88th Air Base Wing outlines his 3 priorities
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 16:35:00

The newest commander of one of the largest installations at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base gave the Dayton business community an inside track at the priorities for his first year.

Col. Christopher Meeker, commander of the 88th Air Base Wing at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base (WPAFB) gave a keynote address at the Dayton Business Journal's 2022 Aerospace & Defense Forum, held Thursday at Holiday Inn Dayton/Fairborn I-675.

Meeker described his personal priorities for his new assignment as 88th Air Base commander: dominate the dirty work, take community partnerships to a new level, and put the mission support on the front edge of technology.
