October Veteran flag Flag to honor Lt. General John 'Skip' B. Hall, Jr.
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-22 09:00:56

During the month of October, the 38th Lights for Peace flag to fly at the Fort Taber - Fort Rodman Military Museum honors the memory of Lt. General John 'Skip' B. Hall, Jr., a highly decorated fighter pilot and commander, who served in the United States Air Force for 32 years.

During his distinguished career, he flew more than 3700 hours, including 990 combat hours and 435 combat missions during the Vietnam War. Hall was proficient in flying the F-16 Fighting Falcon, F-14 Tomcat and F-4 Phantom II.
