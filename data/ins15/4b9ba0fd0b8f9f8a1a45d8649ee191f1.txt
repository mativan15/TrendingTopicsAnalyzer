Rhodes School District 84.5 breaks ground on $14 million addition of eight classrooms
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-05-31 22:55:37

Officials broke ground in May on a $14 million construction project that will add classrooms and a new courtyard at Rhodes School in River Grove.The work follows 2014 and 2018 renovations as well as the construction of a new bus garage, each of which cost about $3 million, according to School District 84.5.The new wing encompasses 21,000 square feet and adds an early education library, a 1,400-square-foot multi-purpose area, seven breakout rooms, two large restrooms and eight new classrooms --each with its own small bathroom and storage closet.
