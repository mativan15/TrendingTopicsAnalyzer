PROD DB/CROSSROADS ENTERTAINMENT At the great popular and heritage celebration of the cinematograph that is Lyon's Lumière Festival, Thierry Frémaux conjures a mix of great living artists presenting their latest creations and departed directors whose reels are stored in the Hangar of the first film, the last remaining vestige of the Lumière factory. From Saturday, October 15 to Sunday, October 23, a program is dedicated to the American Sidney Lumet (1924-2011).
