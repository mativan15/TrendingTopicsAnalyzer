Oct. 14—CHEYENNE — During a debate among candidates for governor, current Gov. Mark Gordon appeared to have subtly pushed back on his recent secretary of state appointee, Karl Allred's criticism of ballot drop boxes.

Gordon said the boxes that collect ballots, which are usually not inside the polling place itself, but nearby, are "under surveillance on a regular basis."
