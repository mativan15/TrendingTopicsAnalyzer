Even her closest supporters are questioning how long Liz Truss can remain as UK prime minister after another day of deep crisis for her fledgling government.

'The prime minister is not under a desk,' Penny Mordaunt, her former leadership rival, announced at one point in response to fierce questioning from opposition parties in parliament yesterday when she was sent to stand in for Truss.
