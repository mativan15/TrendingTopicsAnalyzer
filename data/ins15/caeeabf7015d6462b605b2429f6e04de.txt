One Of Us Is Lying stars beg for former castmate to return

Could Mark McKenna be making a shock return to One Of Us Is Lying as Simon Kelleher? His co-stars are hoping so…
Simon died in the very first episode of the Peacock series, with the remaining instalments focused on finding out who was responsible for his death, after the Bayview four – Addy, Cooper, Nate and Bronwyn – unwittingly found themselves in the frame.
With season two finally premiering this week, the cast revealed their hopes that he could somehow return.
Let's be honest, it wouldn't be the wildest twist that the ‘murder club' have been faced with…
