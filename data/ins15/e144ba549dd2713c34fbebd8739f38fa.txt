Linksys has revealed a new broadband router (Image: LINKSYS)When you sign up for a new broadband plan from firms such as BT, Sky or Virgin Media, you'll be sent a branded router that plugs into the wall and beams the web around your home. Most of these flashing black boxes offer a pretty decent experience but, if your router hasn't been upgraded in a while or you simply want better speeds, it could be worth splashing out and buying something new.A whole host of firms offer powerful routers with many now featuring the latest Wi-Fi 6 technology which can cope with much faster speeds and having more devices connected to them at the same time.
