REUTERS/Eduardo Munoz(Reuters) - Losses from the COVID-19 pandemic and related government shut-down orders don't trigger coverage under the business-income interruption clauses in Erie Insurance's commercial 'all risk' property policies, a federal judge in western Pennsylvania has ruled.
