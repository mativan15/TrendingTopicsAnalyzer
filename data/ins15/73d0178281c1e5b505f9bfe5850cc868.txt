 International stock indexes were broadly higher Monday while U.S. markets were to be closed for the Memorial Day holiday. European stocks climbed for a four-session winning streak. The Stoxx Europe 600 gained 0.7% in morning trade with the industrials and consumer discretionary sectors leading gains.
