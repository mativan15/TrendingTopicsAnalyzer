Pune, India, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global cyber security managed services market size was valued at USD 12.52 billion in 2021. The market is projected to grow from USD 13.52 billion in 2022 to USD 25.77 billion by 2029, exhibiting a CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period. This information is provided by Fortune Business Insights, in its report, titled, 'Cyber Security Managed Services Market Share, 2022-2029.' Managed Services Related to Investment Security are Growing amid COVID-19 Pandemic that Boosted Market Expansion Individuals and companies are required under COVID-19 norms to accept remote working.
