POINTLESS has become a staple of teatime TV over the years.
Since it first hit screens in 2009, the BBC show has seen some HUGE wins.
Alexander Armstrong has been hosting Pointless since 2009Credit: PA:Press Association Although it's possible to walk away from Pointless without becoming a whole lot richer, some big jackpots have been won on the show.
In 2013, father and son team David and Jonathan Hammond Williams took home an impressive £24,750.
They managed to bag the enormous sum after answering questions related to Enrique Iglesias' song Maybe – although the format of the final round has changed since then.
