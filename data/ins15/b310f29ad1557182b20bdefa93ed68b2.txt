(Bloomberg) -- Korea Gas Corp., one of the world's biggest liquefied natural gas importers, expects to win benefits from its current business as it shifts to a future focused on hydrogen.'We'll completely transform our LNG-driven operations into hydrogen by 2050 in line with South Korea's carbon neutrality target,' Lee Jae Hoon, a general manager of the company's hydrogen business development team, said in an interview. 'We have advantages over utilizing our existing gas infrastructure, technology and experience.
