GALILEO, the richest stud horse ever worth £180million, could have an heir to his throne in the shape of Churchill.
Legendary sire Galileo breeds winners at £600,000 a go and his worth is more than the current estimated transfer values of Cristiano Ronaldo, Lionel Messi and Neymar.
Churchill was a champion in his own right and could be heir to £180m Galileo's throne as richest ever stud horseCredit: Rex Features It shows the extraordinary lengths multi-millionaire and even billionaire racehorse owners are prepared to go to in order to own the next big racing star.
