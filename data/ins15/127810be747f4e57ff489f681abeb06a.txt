Joel McHale to headline Fox comedy series 'Animal Control'
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-17 06:07:00

McHale, best known for starring in NBC show 'Community', will essay the role of Frank, an opinionated, eccentric Animal Control officer who may not have gone to college but is still the most well-read person in the room.American actor-comedian Joel McHale will headline 'Animal Control', a comedy series from Fox. According to entertainment news outlet Variety, the network had given a straight-to-series order for a project in July this year. The show follows a group of local Animal Control workers whose lives are complicated by the fact that animals are simple, but humans are not.
