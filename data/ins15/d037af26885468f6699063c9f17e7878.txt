IRVINE, Calif., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- GATC Health ("GATC" or "the Company"), a science and technology company revolutionizing drug discovery and disease prediction using artificial intelligence (AI), today announced that John O'Reilly has joined its Advisory Board. O'Reilly currently serves on the UCLA Health System Board and as Chairman of the University Medical Center of Southern Nevada Governing Board in Las Vegas. He will use his 40-plus years of leadership experience to support the Company in strategic planning and partnership endeavors.
