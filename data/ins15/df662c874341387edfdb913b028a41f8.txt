Japan: Kishida orders probe into Unification Church after Abe killing
dw.com
news
2022-10-17 03:30:00

The man accused of killing former Japanese premier Shinzo Abe blames the Church for bankrupting his mother. Abe had no direct ties to the Church himself. 

Japanese Prime Minister Kishida has ordered a probe into the Unification Church
Japanese Prime Minister Fumio Kishida on Monday ordered a government probe into the Unification Church, a religious group that has been in the spotlight since the assassination of former premier Shinzo Abe.
An investigation into the killing of Abe has revealed close ties between the Unification Church and the ruling party.
