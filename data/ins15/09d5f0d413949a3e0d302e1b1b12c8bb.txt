ANIThe name of former chief minister and veteran party leader Prem Kumar Dhumal did not figure in the list.The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) on Wednesday released its first list of 62 candidates for the Himachal Pradesh assembly election polls which is to be held next month. In total, there are 68 seats in the Himachal Pradesh assembly. As per the list, incumbent Chief Minister Jai Ram Thakur will contest from his current constituency Seraj. However , the name of former chief minister and veteran party leader Prem Kumar Dhumal did not figure in the list.
