June 01, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time KITCHENER, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bonfire Interactive Ltd. ('Bonfire'), a business unit of GTY Technology Holdings Inc. and a leader in strategic sourcing and procurement software, today celebrated the tenth anniversary of its eProcurement platform. Over the last decade, the solution has empowered nearly 700 public sector clients to drive better collaboration, security, and compliance in their procurement processes. Bonfire is a business unit of GTY Technology Holdings Inc.
