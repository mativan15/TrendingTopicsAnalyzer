Indiewire Cinema Eye Honors, the organization that recognizes outstanding artistic achievement in nonfiction and documentary films & series, announced the first round of their 2023 awards and nominations at its annual Cinema Eye Fall Lunch held in Los Angeles.In the five Broadcast categories, HBO film 'Four Hours at the Capitol,' an inside look at the January 6th riot, led with three nominations: Broadcast Film, Broadcast Editing and Broadcast Cinematography. Other projects like the Disney+ docuseries 'The Beatles: Get Back' and Showtime's 'We Need to Talk About Cosby' also received more than one nomination.
