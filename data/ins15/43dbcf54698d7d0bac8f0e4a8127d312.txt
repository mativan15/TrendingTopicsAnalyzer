Last-minute scramble to find space for incoming Ukrainian refugees as Citywest reaches capacity
irishtimes.com
news
2022-10-21 04:00:00

A last-minute scramble was under way on Thursday night to find space for incoming refugees after a major reception facility in Dublin reached capacity.The Department of Children and Equality announced on Thursday that arrivals to Citywest would be paused and that refugees were facing a night in the airport. However, sources at Dublin Airport distanced themselves from this.A source said arrivals overnight on Thursday would continue to go to Citywest and that any use of the airport to accommodate refugees wouldn't commence this week.
