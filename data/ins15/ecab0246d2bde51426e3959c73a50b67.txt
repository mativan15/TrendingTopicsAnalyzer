June 02, 2022 09:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOULDER, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Zayo Group Holdings, Inc. (Zayo) today announced it has completed the acquisition of Education Networks of America (ENA), a leader in providing managed network connectivity, communications and cybersecurity services to K-12 school districts participating in the federally-funded E-Rate program, as well as other public sector customers.



Zayo, known for connecting many of the world's leading technology and financial services firms, has been serving K-12 customers for six years, and is currently powering school districts across several states, including Texas, Washington, Colorado, Oregon, Georgia, California and Arizona.
