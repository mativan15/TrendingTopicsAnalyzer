McCarthy Loves Himself Some Trump. Watch Out.
washingtonpost.com

2022-10-20 12:36:03

House Minority Leader Kevin McCarthy made news this week when he declared that, should he become speaker next year, the House would likely cut off funding for Ukraine and would attempt to use the debt limit to force cuts to popular social programs. His comments tell us quite a bit about what he would be like as speaker if Republicans take back the House.For one, it sounds like he is ready to put the most radical Republicans in charge, even when that means disregarding what the majority of the Republican conference wants.
