Ruchi Soya's stock ended 2.35 per cent higher on Friday at Rs 1120.15 per shareMumbai (Maharashtra): Shares of edible oil major Ruchi Soya Industries Ltd closed on Friday at Rs 1,120.15, up over 2 per cent for the day, as the company reported a dividend of Rs 5 per share, with a Rs 2 face value.Ruchi Soya's stock ended 2.35 per cent higher on Friday at Rs 1120.15 per share, taking its market capitalisation to Rs 40,548.85 crore.While the company reported a 25 per cent decline in its consolidated net profit, mainly on high taxes, the board declared a 250 per cent dividend for 2021-22.
