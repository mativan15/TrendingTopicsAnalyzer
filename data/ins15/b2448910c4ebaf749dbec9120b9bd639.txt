AUSTIN, Texas, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--DentalMonitoring, the industry-leading company that has pioneered remote monitoring and artificial intelligence in the field of orthodontics, is partnering with People + Practice, a full-service digital marketing firm exclusively for dentists and orthodontists. This collaboration will help providers utilize remote monitoring to offer and, importantly, promote the convenience and quality that their evolving patient population desires in the treatment experience.
