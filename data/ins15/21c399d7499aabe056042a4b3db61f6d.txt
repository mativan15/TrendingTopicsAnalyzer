Share price of Crompton Greaves falls as Sensex gains 251.76 points
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 08:45:00

The share price of Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Ltd. dropped 1.68 per cent to Rs 374.9 at 01:39PM (IST) in Friday's trade. The counter has hit a high of Rs 382.25 and low of Rs 374.75 so far during the session. The stock had closed at Rs 381.3 in the previous session. The counter has had a total traded volume of 38598 shares so far with a value of Rs 1.45 crore on the NSE. It traded at a price-to-earnings multiple of 39.46 and a price-to-book ratio of 9.65. A higher price-to-earnings ratio shows investors are willing to pay a higher price for per rupee earnings given by the stock because of better future growth expectations.
