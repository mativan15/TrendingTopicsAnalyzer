Ethos closes 8.6% below IPO on listing day
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 01:45:00

Luxury and premium watch retail company Ethos made a weak debut on the bourses on Monday, ending at ₹802.60, which is 8.6% below its IPO price of ₹878. The stock opened at ₹830 on the BSE, a 5.5% discount to its IPO price. On the NSE, the stock listed at ₹825, down 6% from its IPO price.

Ethos raised ₹472.29 crore from its initial public offering that was open for subscription between May 18 and May 20. The IPO was priced in the ₹836-878 band. The issue was overall subscribed 1.04 times, with the qualified institutional buyers' segment getting subscribed 1.
