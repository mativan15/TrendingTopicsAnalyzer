Published: 09:04 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 09:53 EDT, 17 October 2022 A devout Christian's headless body was found near the Devon coast with a Biblical quote written on a scrap of paper in her jacket pocket, a court has heard.Mee Kuen Chong, 67, was found dead in woodland off Bennett Road in Salcombe, by holidaymakers on June 27 last year.Ms Chong's fractured skull was found in undergrowth a few metres away from the body days later following an extensive search, the Old Bailey has heard.The prosecution claim she had been dumped there by her alleged killer Jemma Mitchell, 38, during a 500-mile round trip from London.
