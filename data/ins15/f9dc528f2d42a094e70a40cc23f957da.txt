Lululemon is the clothing brand gaining the most popularity among wealthy female teens in the US – by far
Insider
business
2022-10-17 14:17:57

Athleisure clothing and yoga-wear brand Lululemon is the fastest-emerging clothing brand among wealthier female teens in the US, per Piper Sandler's semiannual Gen Z survey. The investment bank's "Taking Stock with Teens" report tracks teens' brand preferences, behavior, and habits. In the latest study, 14,500 teens were surveyed. The study found that teen females with higher incomes are spending the most on clothing since 2012.Seventeen percent of teens chose Lululemon as their favorite clothing brand during Piper Sandler's fall 2022 survey, as well as during its spring 2022 and fall 2021 surveys.
