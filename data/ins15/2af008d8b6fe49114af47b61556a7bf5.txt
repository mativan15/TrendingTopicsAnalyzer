October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TULSA, Okla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Empire Petroleum (NYSE American: EP) ('Empire' or the 'Company'), today announced that J. Kevin Vann has joined Empire as an executive officer in the newly created position of Vice President, Finance and Strategic Planning. Mr. Vann has over 25 years of energy industry and public accounting experience in a variety of financial, accounting, and strategic M&A roles.



Mr. Vann will have primary responsibility for the Company's financial planning and analysis efforts, lead Empire's strategic planning, assist with capital markets initiatives, and provide other strategic oversight.
