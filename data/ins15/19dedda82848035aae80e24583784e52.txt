By Zoe KleinmanTechnology editorImage source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Women are not only more likely to be targeted but also suffer more as a consequence, Ofcom's survey foundThe head of media regulator Ofcom says tech firms must do more to protect women online, after its report revealed they were more likely to be victims. Its report on UK media habits says women are more likely than men to face online abuse or see harmful content, and more likely to be distressed by it.Only 42% of the women in its survey said they felt comfortable about speaking freely online.
