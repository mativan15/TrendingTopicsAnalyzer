If you're trying to land a new client, then as you prepare your pitch to be perfect, you'll also want to think outside of the box about how you can best serve their needs. Be clear about expectations on both sides to determine if your missions align and whether you will be a good fit for each other. By demonstrating that you truly understand their current challenges and care about their business, they'll be much more likely to work with you.
