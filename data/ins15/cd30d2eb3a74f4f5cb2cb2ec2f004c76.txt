Bharat Forge shares fall 1.75% as Nifty gains
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 08:15:00

Shares of Bharat Forge Ltd. traded 1.75 per cent down at Rs 672.75 on Friday at around 12:59PM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex advanced 390.29 points to 56208.4. As many as 34,544 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 2.33 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 848.0 and a 52-week low of Rs 595.85. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 725.55, while 50-DMA was at Rs 692.48. If a stock trades above 50 DMA and 200 DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is up.
