getty Few things in sports elicit the passion and drama of great one-on-one rivalries. Bird versus Magic. Nicklaus versus Palmer. Navratilova versus Evert. In Formula One, the rivalry between Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg has gone down as one of the most remarkable, not least because they were teammates. They dominated the sport from 2013 to 2016, bringing glory to Mercedes and stirring up controversy as they went.
