Gunshots reported as huge fire breaks out at Iran's notorious Evin prison
sky.com
news
2022-10-15 20:43:00

A huge fire has broken out at Iran's notorious Evin prison - where political prisoners and anti-government activists are held.Gunshots are reported to have been fired as the blaze took hold at the jail in capital Tehran on Saturday.
An alarm was heard as it was claimed an "armed conflict" was taking place behind the prison walls.Shots are said to have first been heard in Ward 7 of the jail, according to unverified reports.Thick plumes of smoke billowed from the prison, where now freed Nazanin Zahari-Ratcliffe was detained.
