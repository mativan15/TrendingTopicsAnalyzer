Investors are panic-selling their crypto holdings. Whether it's due to LUNA and UST's collapse or inflation challenging the narrative that bitcoin is a safe-haven asset from macro pressures, the market continues to go down and to the right.Sell-offs in the nascent space are nothing new, however, to Konstantin Richter, whose company Blockdaemon has weathered two major bear markets. "We've been here a few times and we've seen it a lot worse," Richter, the founder and chief executive officer, told Insider.
