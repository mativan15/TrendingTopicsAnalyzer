Gujarat Minister of State for Home Harsh Sanghavi announced that people will not be fined for violation of traffic rules during the Diwali festival this year. The announcement was made on Friday. He said that in view of the Diwali festival, traffic police in Gujarat will not collect any fine from the violators till October 27, reported PTI. Sanghavi said the decision to give relief to people during Diwali has been taken by the state home department as per the guidance of Chief Minister Bhupendra Patel.
