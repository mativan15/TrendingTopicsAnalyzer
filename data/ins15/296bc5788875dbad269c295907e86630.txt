Good morning. David Meyer here in Berlin, filling in for Jacob. Ye (the artist formerly known as Kanye West) is a rap icon, a top-flight producer, and a highly successful fashion designer. But a social network owner? Buckle up, for Ye is buying Parler. Parler, you may recall through the overstuffed haze of the past few years, gained a reputation as a haven for far-right extremists such as former Ku Klux Klan leader David Duke. In the wake of the Jan. 6 insurrection last year, Google and Apple both kicked it out of their app stores; Apple let it back in a few months later, after Parler promised to step up its content moderation, and Google relented last month on the same basis.
