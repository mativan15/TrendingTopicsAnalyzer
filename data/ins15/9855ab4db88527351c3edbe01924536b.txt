YOUR Alexa now has a quiz that can reveal your royal credentials.
In celebration of the Queen's Platinum Jubilee this week, you can find out exactly how royal you are.

Sunday, June 5, will mark the Queen's 70 years of service as the British Monarch.
Millions of Brits will enjoy two days off as national holidays on Thursday and Friday.
And you might want to spend a few moments of that leisure time with Alexa.
Starting from today, you can now ask: "Alexa, how royal am I?"

You'll then be asked a series of questions to work out if you could pass for a royal.
