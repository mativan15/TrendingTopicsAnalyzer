Early in-person voting begins in Georgia on Monday, Oct. 17, hours before the candidates for governor meet in the first of two scheduled debates. (AP Photo/John Bazemore, File)ATLANTA, GA — Republican Gov. Brian Kemp is set to go head-to-head with Democratic challenger Stacey Abrams in a gubernatorial debate Monday—the first day of early voting.Shane Hazel, the Libertarian candidate in the race, was also invited to the debate. The Atlanta Press Club will host the debate at 7 p.m. It is closed to the public but will be televised on GPB-TV, according to the Press Club.
