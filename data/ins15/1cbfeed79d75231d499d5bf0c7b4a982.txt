Tesla to Mercedes: World's top EV makers to double investment this decade

Most EV makers have cut out EV targets for themselves to achieve by 2030 as many of them plan to convert their entire lineup to electric models by the end of this decade.
 








As carmakers aim to convert their lineup to electric vehicles by 2030, investment into EV business is expected to rise sharply in coming years. (HT_PRINT)
 
 Electric vehicle sales around the world has seen more than 50 per cent rise in the first nine months of the year. With nearly six million units already sold globally till September, EV makers are planning to increase their investments to manufacture more electric cars in coming years.
