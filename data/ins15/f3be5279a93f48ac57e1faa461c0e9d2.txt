Single-issue candidates for the US Senate aren't the usual fodder for Ars' car coverage, but it seems like an exception might exist for Dan O'Dowd. O'Dowd, an engineer and CEO of a software company, is running for one of California's Senate seats on a platform to ban Tesla's "Full Self-Driving" feature.
Tesla's stratospheric valuation owes a fair amount to the company's futuristic-sounding FSD feature, which promises a truly autonomous car that could even work the streets while its owner sleeps.
