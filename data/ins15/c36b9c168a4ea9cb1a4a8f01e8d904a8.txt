DEATH in Paradise has announced some very familiar faces to join the sun-drenched drama - and fans are in for a treat.
The upcoming run of the BBC favourite confirmed three more actors on Thursday, including Games of Thrones, Sanditon and Nighty Night star Kevin Eldon.
Death in Paradise is returning to BBC for its twelve seasonCredit: BBC
Kevin Eldon will be seen on the shores of Saint MarieCredit: BBC
Also announced were Amit Shah, who has appeared in The Long Call, The Witcher and Last Christmas, and Chelsea Edge, who is specifically announced as playing a guest role.
