Many observers were disappointed with the recent demo of the AI-enabled 'Optimus' robot at Tesla's AI Day. One reviewer cleverly titled his article 'Sub-Optimus.' However, these views actually miss the point. Whatever else may be said of Elon Musk, he is a genius at sensing timing and opportunity, applying technology and providing the necessary resources. The quality and enthusiasm of the engineering team suggest Optimus could succeed, even if it takes longer than the estimate of 3 to 5 years for full production.
