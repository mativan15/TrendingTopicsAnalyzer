Decision of defence forces to buy most of country-made equipment shows capacity of 'Atmanirbhar Bharat': PM Modi
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 14:00:00

AgenciesThe PM also laid emphasis on the importance of maritime security and space technology.Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Wednesday said the decision of the Indian defence forces to buy a majority of the equipment made in the country shows the capacity of the 'Atmanirbhar Bharat' (self-reliant India). India has made its place despite the monopoly of a few manufacturing companies in the defence sector globally, he said. This also symbolises the rising confidence on defence materials made in India, he said after inaugurating the Defence Expo 2022, which is aimed at showcasing the country's defence manufacturing capabilities.
