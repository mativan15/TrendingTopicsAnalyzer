Samsung Electronics Vice Chairman Lee Jae-yong, right, shakes hands with President Yoon Suk-yeol during an event on May 25 at the presidential office in Yongsan, central Seoul. [JOINT PRESS CORPS.] Samsung Electronics Vice Chairman Lee Jae-yong met with Intel CEO Pat Gelsinger in Seoul on Monday to discuss next generation chip development and chipmaking. 'Vice Chairman Lee and Gelsinger had a number of meetings to share views on the collaboration in different areas, such as next-generation memory chips, system-on-chips, foundry, PC and mobile,' Samsung Electronics said in a statement.
