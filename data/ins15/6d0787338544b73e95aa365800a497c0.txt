(Shutterstock / SMReid)COLUMBUS, OH — State police said a man died Sunday evening after a reported shooting on Ohio Statehouse grounds, according to local media. Officers responded to gunshots around 10 p.m. and found a man hurt in the northwest corner of the grounds, a State Highway Patrol sergeant told the Columbus Dispatch. The officers tried to revive the man, but he died shortly after they found him, according to the Dispatch.
