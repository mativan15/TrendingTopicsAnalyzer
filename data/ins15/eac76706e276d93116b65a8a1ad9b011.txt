Freddie Prinze Jr is coming back to our screens with a new Christmas film, and is bringing a sack full of festive joy with him.
The She's All That star took a step back from the limelight recently, but is set to make his grand return next month in a new Netflix movie, Christmas With You.
Santa has indeed come early for us this year as the streaming platform dropped the first trailer earlier today.
