Kakao says 'limited financial impact' expected from weekend outage



Logo for Kakao Corp. (Kakao)The aftermath of Kakao's sudden shutdown continued Monday, as South Korea's largest messenger app operator claimed its service failures due to a fire at SK C&C's data center over the weekend would have limited financial impacts, while analysts predicted Kakao would suffer up to 22 billion won ($15.3 million) in sales loss in the fourth quarter.Kakao has set up an emergency committee, headed by its co-CEO Hong Eun-taek, to investigate the cause of the disastrous shutdown, come up with preemptive measures to prevent similar incidents in the future and establish compensation plans for all stakeholders affected by the service malfunction.
