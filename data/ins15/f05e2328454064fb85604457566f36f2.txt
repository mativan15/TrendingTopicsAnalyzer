Atlantic Union: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-20 11:42:00

RICHMOND, Va. (AP) _ Atlantic Union Bankshares Corporation (AUB) on Thursday reported third-quarter net income of $58.1 million.

The bank, based in Richmond, Virginia, said it had earnings of 74 cents per share.

The holding company for Atlantic Union Bank posted revenue of $196.7 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $180.1 million, surpassing Street forecasts.

Atlantic Union shares have declined 11% since the beginning of the year. The stock has dropped 10% in the last 12 months.
