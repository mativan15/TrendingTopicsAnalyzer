Delhi Starts Action Plan To Tackle Stubble Burning-Triggered Pollution

Strict action will be taken against those bursting crackers before Diwali. (Representational)New Delhi: Delhi Environment Minister Gopal Rai on Saturday said that the Delhi Government has started a 15-point Winter Action Plan in order to reduce the dust pollution owing to stubble burning in the National Capital."A dust campaign is being run to stop the burning of stubble inside Delhi. Bio-de-composer is being used to stop the burning of firecrackers on Diwali in the same episode in Delhi," he said.
