Published: 06:09 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 06:09 EDT, 15 October 2022 A female prison officer who went on to work for the police passed on 'sensitive intelligence and tactics' to inmate she was having an 'intimate' relationship with. Stephanie Heaps passed on 'sensitive police and prison intelligence and tactics' to Leon Ramsden during their relationship.The 30-year-old failed to report criminality, smuggled in illegal items and supplied a colleague's personal details to a serving prisoner while working at HMP Wymott.
