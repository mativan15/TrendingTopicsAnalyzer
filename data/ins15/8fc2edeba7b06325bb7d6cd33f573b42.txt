APThe agriculture, forestry and fishing sector grew 3.3% each in FY21 and FY22 as against a 6.6% contraction in the overall economy in FY21 and an 8.9% rise in FY22.Rainfall during the southwest monsoon will be normal this year at 103% of the longperiod average, the India Meteorological Department (IMD) said on Tuesday. The long-period average (LPA) — currently the average of rains during 1971-2020 — for the JuneSeptember monsoon season is 87 cm. The secondstage long-range forecast has a model error of plus or minus 4%, the weather bureau said.
