One of the simplest ways to earn money from home is by making use of any spare space.Every year, thousands of students arrive from Spain, France and Italy to study an English language course in cities like London, Bristol and Manchester.After lessons they stay with an English family which helps them witness family life and experience British culture first hand.Lots of families enjoy the experience of welcoming foreign language students into their homes with the added bonus of earning up to £175 a week.
