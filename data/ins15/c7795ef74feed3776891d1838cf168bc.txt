Uganda Introduces Raft of Measures to Halt Spread of Ebola, President Says
2022 USNews.com
news
2022-10-15 18:31:00

NAIROBI (Reuters) - Uganda President Yoweri Museveni said on Saturday the government was implementing an overnight curfew, closing places of worship and entertainment, and prohibiting movement into and out of two districts affected by Ebola for 21 days.
