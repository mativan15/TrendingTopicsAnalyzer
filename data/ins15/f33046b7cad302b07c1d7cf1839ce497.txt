HP: Fiscal Q2 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-05-31 20:24:36

PALO ALTO, Calif. (AP) _ HP Inc. (HPQ) on Tuesday reported fiscal second-quarter earnings of $1 billion.The Palo Alto, California-based company said it had net income of 94 cents per share. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to $1.08 per share. The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of six analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.06 per share. The personal computer and printer maker posted revenue of $16.49 billion in the period, also exceeding Street forecasts.
