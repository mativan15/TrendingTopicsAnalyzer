General Motors to launch electric pickup truck Sierra EV in early 2024
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-20 21:45:17

Justin Sullivan General Motors (NYSE:GM) on Thursday introduced its latest electric pickup truck, the Sierra EV, and said it plans to launch the first version called Denali Edition 1 in early 2024 at a price of $107K. The automaker said that lower-priced versions of the Sierra EV at $50K would also be available and would be announced closer to the start of their production. The Sierra EV will give customers another alternative to GM's Hummer EV pickup and SUV. The Denali Edition 1 will be powered by GM's Ultium battery pack and the company estimated a driving range of 400 miles on a full charge.
