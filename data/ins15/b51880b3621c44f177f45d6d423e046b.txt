WWE forced to MOVE Money in the Bank venue to smaller arena 'over ticket sales'

WWE have announced a major venue for the upcoming Money in the Bank pay-per-view.
The wrestling extravaganza was originally scheduled to be emanating from the Las Vegas Raiders' Allegiant Stadium on July 2.
The Allegiant Stadium will no longer be hosting Money in the BankCredit: Getty
This posted was sent to ticket holders announcing the venue changeCredit: Twitter
However, despite tickets having already been sold, the event will now be taking place at the smaller MGM Grand Garden Arena instead on the same day.
