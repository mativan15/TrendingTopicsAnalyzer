October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--DGT Associates, New England's leading surveying firm, announces the hiring of Robert 'Bob' Bielert as their latest Survey Group Manager. Based out of the firm's Preston, Connecticut office, Bielert brings more than 30 years of industry experience to the role and is responsible for providing oversight to all projects throughout the state.



Born and raised in Lebanon, Connecticut, Bielert has spent the past three decades as a professional land surveyor, working both in the field and in-office; most recently as Deputy Director of Survey at AECOM, a well-respected civil engineering consulting firm located in Rocky Hill, Connecticut.
