2022-05-31 21:15:00

NEW DELHI: A special court here sent Delhi cabinet minister Satyendar Jain to the Enforcement Directorate's custody for 10 days on Tuesday after the agency claimed his custodial interrogation was important to verify the source of more than Rs 5 crore he had allegedly received from Kolkata-based shell companies in entities controlled by him.The agency told the court of special CBI judge Geetanjali Goel that Jain had received 'accommodation entries' from 56 shell companies of three hawala operators based in Kolkata and used the 'proceeds of crime' to purchase 200 bighas of land in north and north-west Delhi.
