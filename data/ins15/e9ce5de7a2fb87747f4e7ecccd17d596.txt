When it comes to indie puzzle games, it's hard to avoid comparisons to the giants of the genre. Portal, Limbo, Braid, and so many more have set an almost unclearable bar, so it takes something truly special to break the mold and join that pantheon. Baba is You managed it with unique ideas, and Zachtronics games overwhelm the senses with dense, complex, but satisfying mechanics.
So where does Silt sit in this genre with so many ‘gold-standard' games that you need sunglasses just to look at them all? The short answer is that it's firmly in the middle of the road – far from the greatest, but not even close to the worst.
