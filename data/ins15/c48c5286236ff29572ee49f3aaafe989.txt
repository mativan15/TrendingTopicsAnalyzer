Published: 00:45 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 00:50 EDT, 27 May 2022 A trailblazing microphone entrepreneur who owns Kurt Cobain's guitar has vaulted into the elite club of Australia's richest people - as he's valued at over $701million.Mr Freedman, the fashionable 63-year-old founder of Rode microphones and other audio gear, leaped into the Australian Financial Review's Rich List on Friday.His equipment has become the sound recording equipment of choice for millions who make social media videos and podcasts - and he is likely worth much more due to his US earnings.
