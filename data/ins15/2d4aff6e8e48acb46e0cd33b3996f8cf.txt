Amazon is proving it's a contender to land NBA rights
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 16:54:00

Amazon AMZN, +1.79% is off to a strong start with the NFL's 'Thursday Night Football.' Given its early success, the e-commerce giant is making the case that it can take on the next mega sports rights package: the NBA. The NBA's current U.S. media deals with Disney's DIS, +0.77% ESPN/ABC and Warner Bros. Discovery's WBD, +4.10% TNT expire after the 2024-25 season. The league will likely seek $50 billion to $75 billion for its next multi-year package, said sources. Amazon is paying the NFL around $1 billion a year to exclusively stream TNF through 2033.
