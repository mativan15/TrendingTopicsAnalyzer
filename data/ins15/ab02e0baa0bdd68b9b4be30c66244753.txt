Randolph, MA, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The United Soccer League has announced a multiyear partnership with CURREX, which joins the USL Preferred Supplier Program as the Official Shoe Insole Supplier of the USL. The agreement with the makers of CleatPro™ insoles covers the USL Championship, Super League, League One, W League and League Two. Designed specifically for soccer, CURREX CleatPro™ insoles deliver ultimate comfort, amazing stability and perfect control. CleatPro improves the fit and feel of any cleat brand and model, and thanks to its Super-Grip technology, players can perform at their best every time they take the field regardless of age-group or skill level.
