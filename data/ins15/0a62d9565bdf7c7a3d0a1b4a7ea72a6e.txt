All eyes on Rishi Sunak as pressure mounts on UK PM Liz Truss to resign
indiatimes.com
world
2022-10-20 14:45:00

LONDON: British prime minister Liz Truss is facing an open revolt against her leadership as an increasing number of Conservative Party MPs call for her to resign amid mounting chaos at the heart of the UK government, with all eyes now on former chancellor Rishi Sunak to be parachuted in to take charge.Sir Graham Brady, the senior-most Tory backbencher as the chair of the 1922 Committee, on Thursday met with her for an unscheduled meeting at Downing Street as at least 13 Tory MPs openly called for Truss to go.
