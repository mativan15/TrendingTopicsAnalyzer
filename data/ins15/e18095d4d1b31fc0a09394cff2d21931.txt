IGEA DISCLOSES ITS 2021 ANNUAL REPORT Hoofddorp, the Netherlands, 20 October 2022. IGEA Pharma N.V. (SIX: IGPH) today announced the disclosure of its 2021 Annual Report. Highlights The Group generated marginal revenues of Keur 103,3 for the year ended 31 December 2021 (previous year: Keur 123,7) due to the ongoing efforts to establish market acceptance and position for the health prevention solutions offered.
