Sidhu Moose Wala told Mika Singh about death threats 3 years ago, felt safe visiting Mumbai last week: ‘Paaji, yahan koi ghoorta nahi hai'
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-31 04:28:50

Singer Mika Singh expressed grief over the death of singer-politician Sidhu Moose Wala. Sidhu was shot dead in Punjab on Sunday. Mika called the incident ‘shameful' and also said that Sidhu had told him about receiving death threats three years ago. Not just Sidhu, Mika said that many singers in Punjab feel threatened by gangsters.
During an interaction with News18, Mika revealed that singers who are extorted by gangsters often end up paying them off. Calling Sidhu's death ‘shameful', he said, 'Singers in Punjab often get such threats from gangsters.
