Governor Abbott Appoints Robbins As 1st Judicial District Attorney
texas.gov
science
2022-10-19 15:08:49

Governor Greg Abbott has appointed Paul A. Robbins as District Attorney of the 1st Judicial District in Sabine and San Augustine Counties for a term set to expire December 31, 2024 or until his successor shall be duly elected and qualified. Paul A. Robbins of San Augustine is an attorney in private practice and serves as a Regional Attorney for the Texas Municipal Police Association. Since 2018, Robbins has served as a Captain with the San Augustine Sheriff's Office. He is a member of the State Bar of Texas, Nacogdoches County Bar Association, and the Angelina County Bar Association.
