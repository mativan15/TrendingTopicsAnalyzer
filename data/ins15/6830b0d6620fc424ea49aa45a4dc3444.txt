HDFC Bank Technical Glitch: The issue soon found its way to social media.Questions are now being raised over a technical glitch at HDFC Bank, which reportedly led to several of its customers becoming millionaires – at least for a few hours. The incident happened on Sunday when a few HDFC Bank customers in Chennai surprisingly found lakhs – in some cases crores -- deposited in their bank accounts.The issue soon found its way to social media and people asked how would the affected customers explain to the Income Tax Department about the sudden rise in transactions into their bank accounts.
