What is an insurance rider in a homeowners insurance policy?

Homeowners insurance can give you peace of mind that your home and financial interests are protected. How much coverage you get from homeowners insurance varies by policy, but you generally get some level of protection when it comes to liability, your home's structure, your belongings, and additional living expenses.  That being said, homeowners insurance policies don't cover everything, and you may need to purchase an insurance rider to get the full coverage you want. Here's what you need to know about insurance riders.
