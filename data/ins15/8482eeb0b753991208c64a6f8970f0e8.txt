SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP announces that purchasers or acquirers of Block, Inc. (NYSE: SQ) securities between November 4, 2021 and April 4, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period') have until December 12, 2022 to seek appointment as lead plaintiff in the Block class action lawsuit. Captioned Esposito v. Block, Inc., No. 22-cv-08636 (S.D.N.Y.), the Block class action lawsuit charges Block and certain of its top executives with violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
