Luxury Pickup Wars: 2022 Toyota Tundra Capstone

If you're looking for luxury, your first thought might be a flagship sedan or SUV. But these days, it can also be found in a pickup truck. The newest of which to hit the market is the 2022 Toyota Tundra Capstone, the brand's new flagship pickup currently competing for Luxury Pickup of Texas. (The winner to be named by the Texas Auto Writers Association, of which I'm a board member, on November 1st.)

2022 Toyota Tundra Capstone

The Capstone, equipped with Toyota's new i-FORCE MAX hybrid powertrain, was introduced on the Tundra as the new halo grade for the Toyota brand.
