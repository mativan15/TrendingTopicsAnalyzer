Moose Wala killing: Dump data of 1-km area of crime spot to be probed
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 05:45:00

In a latest development in connection with the sensational killing of singer-turned-actor-politician Sidhu Moose Wala, sources told IANS that the police is collecting the dump data within the 1-km area of the crime spot, which could be of around one lakh mobile phone numbers. This will help the police in identifying the suspects, sources said, adding that six persons have been detained so far. However, no one has been officially arrested till now. Several teams of the Punjab Police have been formed to look into the matter.
