June 02, 2022 11:25 AM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Council for Citizens Against Government Waste (CCAGW) today released its 2021 Congressional Ratings, highlighting the voting records of all 535 voting members of Congress during the first session of the 117th Congress. The report, which CCAGW has issued since 1989, identifies members whose voting records helped protect taxpayer dollars, as well as those who consistently voted against their interests.

CCAGW 2021 Congressional Ratings Facts:

CCAGW rates members on a 0 to 100 percent scale.
