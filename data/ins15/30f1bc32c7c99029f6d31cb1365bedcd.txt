Jharkhand: CM Hemant Soren launches schemes worth Rs 374 crore in Koderma
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 05:45:00

KODERMA: Chief minister Hemant Soren on Tuesday laid the foundation stones of various projects and inaugurated schemes worth Rs 374 crore besides distributing assets worth Rs 23 crore among 3,377 beneficiaries during the ongoing Aapki Yojana Aapki Sarkar Aapke Dwar programme at Bagitand Stadium in Koderma district.Since the launch of the programme's second edition, Soren has been touring the district to connect to people and roll out various welfare schemes.The CM also inaugurated Savitri app through which school girls can apply for the Savitri Bai Phulle Kishori Samriddhi Yojana under which economic assistance is provided for studies.
