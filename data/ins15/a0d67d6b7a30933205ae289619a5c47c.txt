PATNA: Election strategist-turned-political activist Prashant Kishor (PK) on Friday said all political leaders and parties for whom he worked as a poll strategist over the last 10 years are supporting him in his statewide padyatra in Bihar JD(U) national president Rajiv Ranjan @ Lalan Singh had called Kishor a BJP man and questioned about the source of funds for his padyatra.Talking to reporters at Balthar under Sikta block in West Champaran district after completing first 100km of his padyatra, Kishor claimed that crowdfunding will be another big source of financial support to his campaign.
