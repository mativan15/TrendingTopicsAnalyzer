Record price set for working dog in Australia as prized kelpie goes for nearly $50k
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-21 00:45:49

The record price paid for a working dog in Australia has been broken after a sale in the New South Wales Central Tablelands.A kelpie from near Bathurst has secured a record price for a working dog in AustraliaCapree Eve is believed to have also set a world record for the kelpie breedBreeders say a lack of workers is contributing to increased demand for working dogsCapree Stud from Newbridge south of Bathurst sold a 20-month-old kelpie for $49,000 at their sale yesterday.The previous record was set by Hoover, a male kelpie from western Victoria, who sold for $35,000 in 2021.
