Philip Rosenthal's food blogging show "Somebody Feed Phil" reaches Nashville
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 14:30:00

The docuseries named "Somebody Feed Phil" covers the adventures of the renowned creator as he travels the world and tries out varied cuisine of different cities. The episode which released this Tuesday in the sixth iteration of the show that featured Nashville. In the episode, writer and producer Philip Rosenthal visits the city's bustling restaurants and rides along Lower Broadway on a pedal pub. The 47-minute-long episode also starred country music star Brad Paisley, actor Patricia Heaton and producer T Bone Burnett and some other celebrities.
