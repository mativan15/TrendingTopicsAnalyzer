24 Exchange Joins the Talos Partner Network as New Liquidity Venue for Cryptocurrency Spot Trading
prnewswire.com

Through this new partnership, Talos customers can access liquidity via Crypto Spot trades on 24 Exchange's global, multi-asset class platform
HAMILTON, Bermuda, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- 24 Exchange announced today that it has joined the partner network of Talos, the premier provider of institutional digital asset trading technology. Through the addition of 24 Exchange, Talos users can seamlessly access liquidity found on the 24 Exchange global platform via its unique "central dealer" model in Crypto Spot.
