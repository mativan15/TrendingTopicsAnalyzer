Carbon tracking apps and plug-ins can be most useful when they're alerting users to actions that have a high emissions impact.Fred Lum/The Globe and MailFor Canadians who want to reduce their carbon footprint, there are now, as the saying goes, apps for that.A growing number of apps, internet plug-ins and features are promising to help users track, and in some cases offset, the carbon emissions of everything from their daily commute to their travel plans and online shopping habits. Just as many people use digital tools to track their household budgets, these new tools can help them measure a different sort of cost of their consumer behaviour.
