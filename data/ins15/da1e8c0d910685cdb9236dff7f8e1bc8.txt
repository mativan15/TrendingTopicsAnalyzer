World's Largest Society Publisher Leverages Scale and Expertise to Drive Industry Forward

FRANKFURT, Germany, October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--In the run-up to the Frankfurt Book Fair, Wiley (NYSE: WLY), a global leader in research and education, today announced Wiley Partner Solutions, a new division within its Research business. Wiley Partner Solutions serves associations, scientific publishers, societies and corporations as they transform their business strategies and publishing processes in the open research era.
