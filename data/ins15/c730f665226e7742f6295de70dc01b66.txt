16 people at Creamfields South festival were arrested on range of offencesThese include alleged sexual assault, theft, assault and public order violationsForce said drugs was their biggest target during their operation near ChelmsfordBy Charlotte Mclaughlin For Mailonline Published: 14:37 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 14:41 EDT, 5 June 2022 A total of 16 people have been arrested by police for a range of offences at the Creamfields South festival. The dance event in the grounds of Hyland Park in Chelmsford was allegedly host to drugs, theft, offences under the Public Order Act, sexual assault, and assault over the two-day camping festival.
