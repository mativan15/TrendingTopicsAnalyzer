As an investor and a saver, what are you worried about today? Interest rates? The next variant of some disease? The war in Europe? The supposedly coming war in Asia? Climate change? There's a ‘crisis crisis' in the world today and it's affecting the way some people are making decisions. It must be affecting all aspects of their lives but obviously, I see its effect most clearly in business and investment decisions. What is a crisis crisis, you may be wondering. The term describes the overarching sense of crisis that appears to be affecting the world, one after the other, and points to their effect on people as the real crises.
