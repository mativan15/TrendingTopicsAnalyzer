BELLEVUE, Wash., October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Signaling a continued commitment to investing in its retirement distribution channels to meet the needs of a changing market landscape, Symetra Life Insurance Company announced the promotion of Dayna Cerrone to vice president, channel head for the bank and wirehouse channels, a new role. Ms. Cerrone joins Kevin McGarry, vice president, independent broker-dealer (IBD) channel, in leading Symetra's core distribution channels. The executives report to Andrew Farrell, senior vice president, Retirement Sales, Distribution and Marketing.
