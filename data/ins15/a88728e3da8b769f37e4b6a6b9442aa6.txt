By Reuters Published: 00:48 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 00:48 EDT, 1 June 2022 SHANGHAI, June 1 (Reuters) - China stocks were flat on Wednesday as excitement fades about Shanghai coming out of lockdown amid lingering concerns over the economy. Hong Kong shares fell, led by tech stocks. ** Both China's bluechip CSI300 Index and the Shanghai Composite Index were little changed by lunch break. The indices had rebounded over the past weeks. ** Hong Kong's Hang Seng Index lost 0.7%, while the Hang Seng Tech Index fell 1.
