Nathan Chen said the Kamila Valieva doping scandal at the 2022 Olympics was a 'shock' but that 'some sort of a distraction is a given'
insider.com
news
2022-10-18 18:03:34

Olympic figure skater Nathan Chen said that the Kamila Valieva doping scandal at the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics was "a shock" but that he was prepared for the distraction."At the Olympics, everyone is there to do their job and very focused on trying to do the best that they can," Chen told Insider while promoting his new partnership with Invisalign. "Knowing that there's going to be inherently some sort of distraction is a given, and being able to adapt to that is how we've sort of trained for the past many years.
