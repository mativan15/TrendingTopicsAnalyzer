GetWireless added the Chief Marketing Officer role to implement its Multi-Channel Marketing StrategyEDEN PRAIRIE, Minn., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- GetWireless has announced the promotion of Terra Bastolich to the position of Chief Marketing Officer (CMO). In this new capacity, Bastolich will oversee the company's brand, communication, and marketing strategies as well as lead Business Development and Carrier Relations; working cross-functionally with the GetWireless executive team to develop partnerships and sales strategies that enable continued company growth.
