Pensioners may wait longer for free bus pass due to state pension age
express.co.uk
finance
2022-10-16 15:59:16

The state pension age threshold is set to rise in the coming years which means older people will have to wait longer for the benefit. Under Government proposals, the retirement age threshold is set to reach 67 by 2028.If the state pension age does change, many people will be left waiting longer for their retirement benefits.The free bus pass is a benefit which many older people look forward to as it can reduce their travel expenses significantly.Currently, the state pension age is set to increase to 68 between 2044 and 2046.
