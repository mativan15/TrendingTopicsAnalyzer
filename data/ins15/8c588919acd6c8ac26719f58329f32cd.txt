PTIChennai: Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Tamil Nadu Chief Minister MK Stalin during the foundation stone laying .When Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited Tamil Nadu and Telangana last week, the chief ministers of these states dog-whistled opposition through measures ranging from broadsides to nuanced moves amid gathering political currents to support a regionalist alternative to national parties. Modi visited Chennai and Hyderabad last week. In Chennai, he unveiled developmental projects worth over Rs 31,000-crore including affordable homes.
