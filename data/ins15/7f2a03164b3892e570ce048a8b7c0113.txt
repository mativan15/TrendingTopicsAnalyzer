Hubballi: Congress corporators and members of various organisations staged a protest against the officials of Hubballi-Dharwad Smart City Project Limited (HDSCL), alleging the project management splurged taxpayers' money by holding a project consultation meeting at a private hotel.The congress corporators demanded HDSCL's managing director Shakeel Ahmed to come to the protest venue on Wednesday.Later, HDSCL MD arrived at the proest venue and tried to convince the corporators. The protestors demanded an explanation as to why the project consultation meeting was held in the private hotel when it could have been organised in Hubli-Dhaward Municipal Corporation (HDMC) buildings.
