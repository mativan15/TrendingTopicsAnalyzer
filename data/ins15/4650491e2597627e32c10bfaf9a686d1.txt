Rimini Street expands stock repurchase plan to $50M from $15M, prepays $5M in loan

Rimini Street (NASDAQ:RMNI) board authorized an increase to its earlier announced common stock repurchase program from up to $15M over two years to up to $50M over the next four years. The company also prepaid $5M of indebtedness outstanding under its term loan on May 31, 2022 with no prepayment penalty, leaving an approximate loan balance of $81.6M as of June 1, 2022.
