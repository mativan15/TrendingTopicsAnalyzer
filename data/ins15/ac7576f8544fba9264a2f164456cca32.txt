Published: 06:42 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 06:52 EDT, 27 May 2022 Austin Butler may be better known to some for his high profile relationships rather than his acting prowess, but a breakout lead performance in Baz Luhrmann's new film Elvis is set to change everything.The former child star, who was born in Anaheim, California, is the current darling of film reviewers across the world, with his depiction of Elvis being described as the best thing about the movie.With a career dating back to his childhood, boasting roles in Disney and Nickelodeon productions, as well as multiple movie parts, and now the breakout role in Luhrmann's biopic, the 30-year-old seems to be standing on the cusp of a stellar Hollywood career.
