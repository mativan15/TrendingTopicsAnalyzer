Different_Brian/iStock Editorial via Getty Images CSX Corp. (NASDAQ:CSX) shares marked a strong gain in Thursday's extended session after exceeding expectations in the third quarter. The Florida-based railroad operator posted $0.52 in diluted earnings per share, a 21% increase from the prior year quarter, alongside $3.9B in revenue. Consensus EPS and revenue estimates stood at $0.50 and $3.75B, respectively. Management credited higher volumes, fuel surcharges, and an increase in storage and other revenues for the results.
