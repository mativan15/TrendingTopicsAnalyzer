By Reuters Published: 16:18 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 16:18 EDT, 2 June 2022 June 2 (Reuters) - Nikola Corp's founder Trevor Milton voted against the electric vehicle company's proposal to issue new shares at the company's annual general meeting, a Bloomberg News report https://news.bloomberglaw.com/securities-law/nikola-founder-trevor-milton-is-said-to-block-company-share-sale on Thursday, citing sources. Milton's vote, which threw the outcome into doubt, led to the decision to immediately adjourn the meeting, in order to allow the company time to solicit more proxies in favor of the proposal, the report said.
