'It's crazy busy': New Brandon Food Rescue store continuing to meet community need
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-04 18:01:30

The Brandon Food Rescue store has been moving fast ever since it opened back in December 2021. It started out as a small space where people could pay a fraction of the price for food that was never able to make it to the grocery store shelves. Now it has moved to a full-sized location where there is more space for products. Olivia Boyce, chair of the Brandon Food Council and one of the people behind the Food Rescue, said the past several months have been a blur. "So far, we have rescued and distributed 60,000 pounds of food through the store.
