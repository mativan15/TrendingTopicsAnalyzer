Monopoly board game Thimble (Image: GETTY)Hasbro has announced the results of a recent vote to bring back a classic Monopoly token. The toy company recently gave fans the chance to vote for which retired Monopoly token to bring back for the 2023 edition of the board game. Fans who visited the Monopoly website were able to vote to bring back the Wheelbarrow, Iron, Horse and Rider, Thimble, Boot or Money Bag. The result wasn't close.Receiving 30% of the public vote, the Thimble will make its return to the next edition of Monopoly launching in spring, 2023.
