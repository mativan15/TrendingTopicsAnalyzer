Taylor Swift has given fans a first glimpse of the music videos set to accompany her album Midnights, and there are some very recognisable faces involved.
The Wildest Dreams singer's new album is finally here, having been released at midnight on Friday in the US, or 5am in the UK.
As if that wasn't enough content for the fanbase to chew on, Taylor has teased the upcoming music videos to visualise the tracks, as well as the names of people set to be involved.
