In Bengaluru, the cab aggregators on Saturday slashed down the minimum auto rickshaw fares to Rs. 35 and Karnataka's transport department has asked people to report to the authority if they charge more than 10 per cent of framed prices. According to reports, the transport department in the state received more than 100 complaints from passengers who alleged the aggregators were charging more.
