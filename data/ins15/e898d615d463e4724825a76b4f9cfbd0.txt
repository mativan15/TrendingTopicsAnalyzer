Shashi Tharoor and his team brought allegations of severe irregularities in the Congress presidential election as the counting is going on to pick the non-Gandhi president of the party to lead in the 2024 Lok Sabha election. In a letter written by polling agent Salman Soz, Shashi Tharoor submitted four complaints against the polling process: use of unofficial seals for ballot boxes, presence of unofficial persons in the polling booths, malpractices indulged during the voting and the absence of polling sheet.
