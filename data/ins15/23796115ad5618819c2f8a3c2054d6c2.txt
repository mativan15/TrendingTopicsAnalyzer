Govt gets Rs 952 cr dividend from IRFC, NALCO, Concor
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 12:45:00

The company paid final dividend of Rs 1 per equity share amounting to Rs 183.66 crore for 2020-21. The government has received Rs 952 crore as dividend from three public sector companies, DIPAM Secretary Tuhin Kanta Pandey said on Friday. With this, the total dividend receipts of the government from CPSEs so far this fiscal stands at Rs 16,728 crore. "Government has respectively received about Rs 711 crore, Rs 141 crore and Rs 100 crore from IRFC, NALCO, and CONCOR as dividend tranches," Pandey tweeted.
