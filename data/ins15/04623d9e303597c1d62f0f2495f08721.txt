BHUBANESWAR: President Droupadi Murmu and Prime Minister Narendra Modi dialled chief minister Naveen Patnaik , who turned 76 on Sunday, to greet him on his birthday .Naveen took to Twitter to thank both the President and the Prime Minister for calling him to wish on his birthday."Grateful to honourable Prime Minister Shri @narendramodi Ji for calling up and wishing me on my birthday. Heartfelt thanks for your kind gesture. Heartfelt thanks to Hon'ble President Droupadi Murmu Ji (@rashtrapatibhvn) for calling up and wishing me on my birthday," tweeted Naveen.
