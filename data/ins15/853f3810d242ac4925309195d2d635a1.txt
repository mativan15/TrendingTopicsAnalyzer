Image source, Carole Zambonini Image caption, Carole Zambonini receiving her MBE from the Lord Lieutenant of Warwickshire Tim CoxA woman who runs a free dementia support cafe has been recognised with a special award to mark the Queen's Platinum Jubilee. Carole Zambonini set up the Alcester Cafe in Warwickshire in 2012, which is run twice a week by volunteers.She has been awarded the Platinum Champion Award by the Royal Voluntary Service to recognise her work.Ms Zambonini said: "I would like to take this opportunity to emphasise the value of volunteering".
