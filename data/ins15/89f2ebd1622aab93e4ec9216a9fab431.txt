YouTube 'DOWN' – fans moan as Google app 'not working' in mystery outage
thescottishsun.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 08:41:51

YOUTUBE users claim they are experiencing issues accessing the popular video sharing platform.
There was a spike in reports from around 9.30am BST on Friday from annoyed fans.
YouTube is the world's most popular video sharing site
"Why is youtube down now…." one user vented on Twitter.
It's not clear how widespread the problem is at the moment.
But the current volume of reports suggest it may be a small and isolated incident.
According to the Down Detector site, more than 250 claims were detected online.
