October 17, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CI Financial Corp. ('CI') (TSX: CIX, NYSE: CIXX) today reported preliminary total assets of $338.1 billion as at September 30, 2022, consisting of asset management assets of $114.2 billion, Canadian wealth management assets of $74.0 billion, and U.S. wealth management assets of $149.9 billion.
