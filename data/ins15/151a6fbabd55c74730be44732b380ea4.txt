KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy B.D. HobbsOct 19, 2022With the midterm elections now less than 3 weeks away, with so much at stake, many conservatives have had a 1980's flashback. Joe Biden has done unspeakable damage to our nation since taking office, drawing comparisons to Jimmy Carter and his lone term in the late 70's. Conservatives are hoping to counter with another response, similar to Ronald Reagan. But things are different now. "I don't want to trash the 80's, obviously I think Reagan was a great president" said Rachel Bovard, senior policy director for the Conservative Partnership Institute, "But at the end of the day, the challenges that defined the conservatism of the 80's, don't define the conservatism of the 2020's.
