A few days ahead of their opening match against India at the ICC T20 World Cup Pakistan batter Mohammad Rizwan was seen at the nets in Brisbane facing up to a few throwdowns. One of the videos from the Gabba nets uploaded by Pak TV showed Rizwan briefly interacting with an Indian fan who was watching from the sidelines. The fan first asked Rizwan whether he could bowl leg spin to him. 'Mai daalu leg-spin?' he asked. Rizwan first ignored and then turned back and smiled and said, 'Peshawar aa jao (Come to Peshawar).
