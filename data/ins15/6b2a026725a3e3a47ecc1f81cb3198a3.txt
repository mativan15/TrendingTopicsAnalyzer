October 18, 2022 06:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nextdoor Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: KIND), the neighborhood network, will report financial results for the third quarter of 2022 following the close of the U.S. markets on Tuesday, November 8, 2022. Nextdoor will host a conference call at 2:00 p.m. PT/5:00 p.m. ET to discuss the results and outlook.

A live webcast will be available in the Events & Presentations section of the Nextdoor Investor Relations website located at investors.
