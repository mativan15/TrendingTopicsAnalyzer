"Top Gun: Maverick" is trending to become Tom Cruise's most successful movie.

The acclaimed sequel generated $156 million over the Memorial Day weekend.

"These results are ridiculously over-the-top fantastic," a Paramount executive said.

"Top Gun: Maverick" is on its way to becoming the biggest and most successful movie of Tom Cruise's 41-year career in Hollywood.

The movie was released in US and Canadian theaters on Friday, May 27, and smashed Memorial Day box office records with a $156 million domestic weekend opening, according to Los Angeles Times, and brought in a total estimated $252.
