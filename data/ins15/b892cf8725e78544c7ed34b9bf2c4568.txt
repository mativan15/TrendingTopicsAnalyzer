Thousands of Salesforce workers urge software makers to cut ties with NRA
cbsnews.com
news
2022-06-01 21:07:00

By Megan Cerullo June 1, 2022 / 5:07 PM / MoneyWatch Thousands of workers at Salesforce are pressing the software company to cut ties with the National Rifle Association after a mass shooting at an elementary school in Uvalde, Texas, left 21 people dead.At least 4,000 employees at the San Francisco-based company signed an open letter to co-CEOs Marc Benioff and Bret Taylor urging them to cease doing business with the NRA, SFGate first reported. The NRA uses Salesforce products for marketing and fundraising purposes, according to the letter.
