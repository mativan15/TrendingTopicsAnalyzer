Development remains China's top priority, Xi Jinping said on Sunday during a speech at the opening session of the 20th Chinese Communist party's congress adding that reforms will be made in the direction of a socialist market economy. The remarks come following a tough year for China's economy owing to its strict ‘zero Covid' policies that have hit production and supply chains.
