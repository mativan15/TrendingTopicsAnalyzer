A POSH hamper makes the perfect gift or special treat for any big occasion, but shoppers need to know where to get the cheapest in time for the bank holiday weekend.

You can get a fancy hamper for less

The only problem is hamper specialist, Fortnum and Mason, is flogging their own Jubilee special for an eye-watering £150.
