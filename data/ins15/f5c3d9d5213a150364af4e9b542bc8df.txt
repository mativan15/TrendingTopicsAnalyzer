Xi vows to elevate Chinese military to world-class standards
beijingbulletin.com
news
2022-10-16 09:16:11

The task will be achieved by 2027, the president has saidThe Chinese military will be brought to "world-class standards" by 2027, when it marks its 100th anniversary, President Xi Jinping promised in his opening speech at the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party on Sunday.Over the next five years, the People's Liberation Army (PLA) will intensify troop training and enhance combat preparedness across the board, Xi told the audience at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing."We will strengthen Party building across the board in the people's armed forces to ensure that they always obey the Party's command," he said.
