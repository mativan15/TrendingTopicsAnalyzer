Purchase of Wisconsin-Based Distributor Enhances MSC's Leading Position in Metalworking
MELVILLE, N.Y. and DAVIDSON, N.C., June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- MSC INDUSTRIAL SUPPLY CO. (NYSE: MSM), a premier distributor of Metalworking and Maintenance, Repair and Operations supplies to industrial customers throughout North America, today announced that it has acquired Engman-Taylor, a Menomonee Falls, Wisc.-based distributor of metalworking tools and supplies.
Under the agreement, Engman-Taylor will continue to do business under its current name after becoming an MSC company.
