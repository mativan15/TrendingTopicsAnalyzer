AlexSecret/iStock via Getty Images The year 2022 is on track to be remembered for its historic market volatility. While the S&P 500 (SPY) had its worst start in decades, the Nasdaq had its worst start ever. The sell-off initially affected small and mid-caps. However, it has now extended to the "generals," with Apple (AAPL), Amazon (AMZN), Alphabet (GOOG), and Microsoft (MSFT) all trading significantly down from their previous high. The past six months have been catastrophic for many categories, particularly those that benefited from pandemic tailwinds.
