Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Auto index falls 0.09%

economics
2022-10-19 11:06:09

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Auto index closed on a negative note on Wednesday.Shares of Sona BLW Precision Forgings(up 4.53 per cent), TVS Motor Company(up 3.76 per cent), TIINDIA(up 2.6 per cent), Bajaj Auto(up 1.23 per cent) and Escorts Kubota(up 0.75 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack.On the other hand, Ashok Leyland(down 1.97 per cent), Tata Motors(down 1.29 per cent), Maruti Suzuki(down 1.0 per cent), Hero MotoCorp(down 0.81 per cent) and Mahindra & Mahindra(down 0.43 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.
