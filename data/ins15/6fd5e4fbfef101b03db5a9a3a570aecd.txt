From a Korean student abroad to a foreign professor here, the impact of the won's historically weak performance is felt by many



(123rf)Over the past year, the Korean currency has lost nearly 20 percent of its value against the dollar.A dollar is equal to about 1,429 won, according to the currency's closing rate on Wednesday. But a year ago, you needed 19 percent less of the Korean currency, or 1,196.5 won, to buy a dollar.The won's depreciation, driven largely by the dollar's gains worldwide, has sent the exchange rate into a territory trodden only during major financial crises.
