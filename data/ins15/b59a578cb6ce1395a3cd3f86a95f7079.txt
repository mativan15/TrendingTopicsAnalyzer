Image source, Postcode Lottery Image caption, Ted Owen, 76, won a staggering £370,000 after purchasing two tickets, each winning £185,000More than 400 people in a small Welsh town have scooped thousands of pounds in a People's Postcode Lottery win. The biggest payouts in Rhymney in Caerphilly county from the £3.7m pot go to Ted Owen, 76, who picked up £370,000 after playing with two tickets, and eight others who receive £185,000 each.And 426 others in the same postcode sector will get from £3,894 to £11,682.
