bjdlzx/E+ via Getty ImagesNexstar Media Group (NASDAQ:NXST) began as a single broadcast TV station in 1996 and today is the largest broadcast company in the U.S. The company is roughly 50% larger than their nearest competitor, and provides local news and other programming across 200 markets to 68% of U.S. households. Nexstar owns a 31% stake in the Food Network, and has recently launched a nationwide news network, NewsNation. 2021 revenue of $4.6 billion was split, with about 40% coming from television advertising, 8% from digital advertising, and the rest from retransmission consent [retrans].
