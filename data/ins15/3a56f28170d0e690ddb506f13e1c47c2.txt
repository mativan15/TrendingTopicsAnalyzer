AirAsia Philippines resumes flights to Sabah, Indonesia, Hong-Kong, Singapore, Thailand
blogspot.com
news
2022-05-31 15:42:00

MANILA - AirAsia Philippines expressed
optimism of a strong rebound in the Asean region with the resumption of
flights to Malaysia, Indonesia, Hong-Kong, Singapore and Thailand starting June
1.  The low-cost airline returned May 27 to Kota
Kinabalu, the capital city of Sabah, with 2x weekly flights.  A
growing resort destination, Kota Kinabalu is home to an array of tropical
islands, lush rainforests and the famous Unesco site, Mount Kinabalu. It
is also famous as an eco, wellness, health and sports tourism destination.
