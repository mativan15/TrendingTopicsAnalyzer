Bold Metrics Raises $8M for Fit Tech
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-01 14:08:31

Body data is big, and it's going places it hasn't gone before, according to fit technology firm Bold Metrics.

The San Francisco-based outfit believes the metaverse could be the technology's next stop, and apparently venture capitalists agree, backing the business recently in an $8 million Series A.

More from WWD

The fight to give online shoppers the right fit is a key priority for e-commerce, giving investors like Bessemer Venture Partners, which led the round, plenty of motivation. They were joined by Lytical Ventures, ValueStream Ventures and Nanban Ventures, bringing the company's total raised to $12.
