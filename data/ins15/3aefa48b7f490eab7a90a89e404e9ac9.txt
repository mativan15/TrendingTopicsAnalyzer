Variety - TV News When executive producer and Academy Award-winning actress Reese Witherspoon was tasked with casting a performer to play Amy Wheeler in 'From Scratch,' Netflix's limited series adaptation of Tembi Locke's memoir, she looked no further than her dinner table.Witherspoon was dining across from Zoe Saldaña, who was accompanied by her Italian husband and exchanging bits of dialogue in his native tongue, when she realized the 'Avatar' star would be the perfect actor to play Amy.
