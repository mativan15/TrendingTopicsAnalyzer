Karnataka: CM Bommai assures funds for Bidar-Nanded railway line project
indiatimes.com
science
2022-10-19 08:01:00

Karnataka chief minister Bosavraj Bommai on Tuesday assured that funds will be released for the Bidar-Nanded Railway project. "The Government of India will soon approve the project", CM said. Speaking at the Jan Sankalp Yatra in Aurad, Bommai said that the Government of India will soon approve the Bidar-Nanded Railway project and the state government will provide land and its share of the project cost.While emphasizing upon the developmental promises to be commenced soon in the Aurad of Bidar district, CM Bommai said that the filling up of 36 tanks in Aurad taluk will be taken up at a cost of Rs 698 crore and it will benefit 40 villages.
