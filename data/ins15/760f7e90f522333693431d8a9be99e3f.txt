Kylie Jenner said she cried 'nonstop' with 'baby blues' for 3 weeks after birth of her son
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-20 10:36:22

Kylie Jenner revealed in the latest episode of "The Kardashians" that she "cried nonstop" with the "baby blues" for three weeks after giving birth to her son in February. "It's been really hard for me," she told sister Kendall Jenner in the episode, which was shot in April. "I cried nonstop every day for the first three weeks. It's just the baby blues, and then it kinda goes away." She said she experienced the same emotions after giving birth to her daughter Stormi, 4. Kylie added, "I cried for, like, three weeks.
