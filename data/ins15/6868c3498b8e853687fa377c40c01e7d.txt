Factors driving EdTech in India

Summary: The EdTech sector in India got a shot in the arm during the pandemic as the educational institutions were forced to opt for online classes to ensure the student did not face any disruption in their education.
The education industry witnessed orbital shifts during the pandemic that broke out in December 2019. Education has not been the same from then on. It is no longer associated with the traditional classroom within the four walls.
The trigger for driving the digitalization was the Covid-19 pandemic.
