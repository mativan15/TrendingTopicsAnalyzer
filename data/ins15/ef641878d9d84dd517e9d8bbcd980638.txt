UP madrassa survey: 7,500 'unrecognised' seminaries found on last day of review
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 02:00:00

MUZAFFARNAGAR: On the last day of the ongoing survey of madrassas in Uttar Pradesh, around 7,500 unrecognised seminaries have been found across the state's 75 districts, said Dr Iftikhar Ahmed Javed , chairman of UP Madrassa Board of Education ."Today was the final day of the survey and we can now say that at least 7,500 unrecognised madrassas were found in UP so far. The official figures will follow soon. By November 15, through the district magistrates, a complete survey report will reach the state government for further scrutiny," Javed told TOI on Thursday.
