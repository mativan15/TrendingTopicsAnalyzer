ReutersIndiGo has decided to conduct an internal study on how to better handle specially-abled passengers.IndiGo has decided to conduct an internal study on how to better handle specially-abled passengers after receiving criticism for denying a specially-abled child to board a plane, revealed CEO Ronojoy Dutta. The airline has been slapped with a fine of Rs.5 lakh by DGCA for the act. Aviation minister, Jyotiraditya Scindia, while expressing his concern over the incident said that no human being should have to go through this and he himself is investigating the incident.
