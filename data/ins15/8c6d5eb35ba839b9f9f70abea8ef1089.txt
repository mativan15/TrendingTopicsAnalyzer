October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ENDICOTT, N.Y.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ICS, a portfolio company of ClearLight Partners and leading provider of Information Technology (IT) Managed Services, has acquired TechMD, a provider of Managed IT Services to small to medium businesses, headquartered in Orange County, California.



Under the stewardship of Mark Perez and Sebastian Igreti over its twenty-year history, TechMD has helped small-to-medium sized businesses and organizations in the greater Los Angeles and Orange County metro areas by providing IT managed services, cybersecurity services, technical services, cloud solutions and product procurement.
