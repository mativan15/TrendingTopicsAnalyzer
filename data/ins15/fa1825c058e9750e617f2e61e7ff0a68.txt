NFL Week 8 is nearly upon us, and there's been some huge movement in lines from the preseason, with teams disappointing and impressing in equal measure. The largest adjustment in the NFL Week 8 odds from where we started comes in the final London game of the year where the Broncos went from 6.5-point favorites to 3.5-point underdogs.

The week kicks off with an intriguing matchup in Tampa with the Buccaneers hosting the Ravens on Thursday Night Football as 3-point favorites in a game that had us collectively scratching our heads when we made our NFL Week 8 picks.
