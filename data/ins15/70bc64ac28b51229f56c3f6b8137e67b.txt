Back-to-back visits of central ministers to J&K are aimed at accelerating developmental activities in UT claimed BJP here today.However, the opposition termed it as 'mere photo shoots' that weren't going to change ground realities on the ground.In the past week, around half a dozen Union ministers and MoS visited J&K and sources in the government claim that in the next few weeks around 40 Union ministers and MoS will be visiting different parts of J&K.
