Who is 'The Mole' in Netflix competition? The saboteur is finally revealed
newsweek.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 10:57:42

The identity of the saboteur in Netflix show The Mole has finally been revealed.The game show, which originally aired on ABC from 2001 to 2008, returned for its rebooted edition on October 7.Over the past two weeks, viewers have watched 12 contestants take part in the high-stakes competition series.The contestants have worked together to complete challenges to add money to a pot that only one of them will take home at the end of the competition.
"The Mole" Season 1 cast: Avori Henderson, Sandy Ronquillo, Dom Gabriel, Greg Shapiro, Kesi Neblett, Samara White, Pranav Patel, Will Richardson, Casey Lary, Osei White, Joi Schweitzer and Jacob Hacker.
