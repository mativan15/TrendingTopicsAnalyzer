In today's podcast news roundup, Marc Maron goes into business with Acast; iHeartMedia orders slate of podcasts from Bloomberg Media; Financial Times and Pushkin Industries probe the porn biz; Sony Music goes into 'Binge' mode; American Girl launches podcast network based on its doll franchise; and more.
DEALS
Top podcaster Marc Maron inked a three-year deal with indie podcast platform Acast for his podcast 'WTF With Marc Maron.' The podcast will now be hosted, monetized and distributed via Acast, and Maron's show will also be making use of the platform's paid memberships offering, Acast+.
