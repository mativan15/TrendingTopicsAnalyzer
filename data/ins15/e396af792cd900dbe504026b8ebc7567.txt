RENO, Nev., May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- NuLegacy Gold Corporation announces that as part of its ongoing efforts to streamline operations and reduce corporate costs it has appointed, on a part-time basis, Mr. Xavier Wenzel, CPA, CA, as its new Chief Financial Officer. Mr. Wenzel is a Chartered Professional Accountant with over 25 years experience in public practice (including audit and tax expertise) and financial and regulatory reporting for public companies, including Ely Gold Royalties Inc.
