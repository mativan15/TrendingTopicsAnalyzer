Each year, Atlanta Business Chronicle hosts the Women of Influence Awards, honoring 21 women who are driving business in their industries, leaving an indelible mark on their communities and blazing a trail.

The honorees include C-suite members from Atlanta's largest public companies, leaders in construction and development firms and executives pushing for Atlanta's overall economic growth. Jenny Pruitt, executive chairman and founder of Atlanta Fine Homes Sotheby's International realty received the Lifetime Achievement Award.
