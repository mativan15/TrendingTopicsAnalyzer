The Bobby Bones ShowBy Morgan HuelsmanOct 20, 2022Talking on the radio every day, there's only so many words you can use. Sometimes it's easy to use the same words over and over again. Bobby Bones noticed he was using the same word often whenever he was talking. It's what he refers to as a "crutch" word, meaning he uses it whenever he needs to fill space or when he's thinking while talking. Often times his crutch word changes. The crutch word he's been using lately is "alright," and he wants to get rid of it.
