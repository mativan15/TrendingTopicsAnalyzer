Published: 14:58 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 15:18 EDT, 20 October 2022 Eight months after Haas F1 hastily pulled Russian sponsorship off its cars following Russia's invasion of Ukraine, the team has a new title sponsor.Even better for Haas, the only American-owned team in Formula One will now be backed by a global company headquartered in Dallas, Texas.MoneyGram was introduced as the new title sponsor for the Haas race team on Thursday ahead of the United States Grand Prix in Austin, Texas. The team will be called MoneyGram Haas F1 beginning next season and the two-car livery will change for a look that incorporates the red and white colors favored by both Haas Automation and MoneyGram, a digital money transfer company.
