June 03, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NORWALK, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Xerox Holdings Corporation (NASDAQ: XRX) announced today the promotion of Joanne Collins Smee to Executive Vice President and President, Americas, effective June 3. Collins Smee assumes responsibility for leading all go-to-market operations across the region following the retirement of Mike Feldman.
