October 16, 2022 05:02 PM Eastern Daylight Time --(BUSINESS WIRE)--Photos are available on Business Wire's website and the Associated Press Photo Network of Angus Cloud's attendance at the 2022 Formula Drift Finals with Rockstar Energy Drink. Following the announcement of Rockstar Energy Drink's new partnership with actor and pop culture phenomenon Angus Cloud, the two kicked off the partnership at the racetrack on October 15, 2022 with Angus joining the Rockstar Energy team at Irwindale Speedway as Formula Drift concluded its heart-pounding 2022 season.
