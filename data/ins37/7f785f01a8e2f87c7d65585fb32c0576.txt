S&T Bancorp: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

business
2022-10-20 11:42:56

INDIANA, Pa.

S&T Bancorp Inc. (STBA) on Thursday reported third-quarter profit of $37.2 million.

The Indiana, Pennsylvania-based bank said it had earnings of 95 cents per share.

The results beat Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of three analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 81 cents per share.

The holding company for S&T Bank posted revenue of $104.6 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $98.6 million, also surpassing Street forecasts.
