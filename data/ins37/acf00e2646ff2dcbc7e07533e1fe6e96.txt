Exports need a hand from handsets PLI
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 19:45:00

Mobile handset exports from India crossed $1 billion in September, more than tripling from a year ago and serving as the poster child for the government's much-touted production-linked incentive (PLI) scheme for local manufacturing. Samsung and contract manufacturers for Apple are pushing the surge in exports, having committed to steep increases in production and outbound shipments in the next financial year over this one. They are also required to deepen value-addition that involves enhancing the local vendor pool.
