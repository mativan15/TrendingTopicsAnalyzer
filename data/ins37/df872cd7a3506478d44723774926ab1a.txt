English Dutch PRESS RELEASE Regulated Information 4 June 2022, 07:00 CET, Antwerp, Belgium: VGP NV (‘VGP' or ‘the Group'), a European provider of high-quality logistics and semi-industrial real estate, announced that its share has been admitted to the FTSE EPRA Nareit Developed Europe Index in the FTSE EPRA Nareit Global Real Estate Index Series ('Developed Europe Index'). Its inclusion will be effective from the start of trading on Monday the 20th of June 2022. The admittance to the Developed Europe Index follows VGP's inclusion in the FTSE EPRA Nareit Global Emerging Index on the 23rd of September 2019.
