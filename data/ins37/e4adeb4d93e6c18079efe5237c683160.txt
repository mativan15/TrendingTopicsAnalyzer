AUSTIN, Texas (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Accelerist, a social good SaaS (Software as a Service) platform supporting companies and nonprofits in their partnership efforts, appointed new C-Suite executives - Margaret Bjork as Chief Revenue Officer (formerly of Benevity and Blackbaud) and Robyn Mendez as Chief Operating Officer (formerly of Tiltify and Blackbaud). These new hires, combined with a recent capital raise led by Golden Section Ventures and acquisition of the Giveworx fundraising platform earlier this summer, are part of a larger strategy to launch the industry's first comprehensive CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) platform.
