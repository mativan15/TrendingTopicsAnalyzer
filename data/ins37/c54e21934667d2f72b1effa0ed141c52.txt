Padilla Faces a Familiar Foe in Bid for Full US Senate Term
2022 USNews.com

2022-10-20 13:20:00

By ELLIOT SPAGAT, Associated Press

SAN DIEGO (AP) — Alex Padilla feels comfortable enough in his bid to remain California 's junior senator that he is casting aside his Republican opponent — constitutional lawyer Mark Meuser — to campaign for fellow Democrats.

'We are facing a radical right-wing movement that continues to call into question the democratic process anytime that it doesn't work in their favor,' he wrote supporters in September, asking to split donations with a group representing Democrats running to become the top election official in their states.
