How Trash For The Rich Is Lifeline For 25,000 Poor Recyclers In Colombia's Capital
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 11:29:33

Appearing at nightfall, dragging heavy carts from dustbin to dustbin in the affluent northern suburbs of the Colombian capital Bogota, that's what surrounds the daily task list of around 25,000 rubbish recyclers.The informal recyclers rifle through the trash of the rich, looking for waste plastic, glass bottles and cardboard they can sell for a handful of the country's currency Colombian Pesos.It is back-breaking work for little reward, but salvation for thousands in a country where one in eight city dwellers is unemployed, and the poverty rate approaches 40%.
