Brinc announces partnership with Fusang, Asia's first fully regulated digital asset exchange
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-18 03:00:00

To make startup investment more inclusive through security tokenization and on-chain solutionsBrinc becomes a shareholder in Fusang Corp, furthering a relationship that also sees Fusang coming onboard as a program partner for ZK Advancer (formerly Launchpad Luna), the accelerator launched by Brinc and Animoca BrandsZK Advancer participants will gain access to Fusang's industry expertise, personalized mentorship, and its single platform tokenization solutionParticipants may also list on Fusang's public or private market, which is fully regulated with a digital asset exchange licenced in Labuan, MalaysiaBrinc and Fusang Digital Asset Exchange strive to drive equality and make investment more inclusive by leveraging blockchain technology and unlocking the use of a regulated Exchange.
