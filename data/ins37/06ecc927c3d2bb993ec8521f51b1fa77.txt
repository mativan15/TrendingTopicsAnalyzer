English French MONTREAL, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Osisko Development Corp. ("Osisko Development" or the "Company") (ODV: TSX-V & NYSE) is pleased to announce that its common shares (each, a "Common Share") have commenced trading on the New York Stock Exchange ("NYSE") under the symbol "ODV". Consequently, the Company has satisfied the escrow release condition relating to the 34,093,768 subscription receipts of the Company (the "Subscription Receipts") for gross proceeds (including earned interest) of US$119.
