By Australian Associated Press Published: 20:46 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 20:46 EDT, 20 October 2022 Matildas midfielder Alex Chidiac will return for a second season with Melbourne Victory in a major boost for their A-League Women title defence. Chidiac will head back to Victoria following 18 games for Racing Louisville FC in the US National Women's Soccer League. The former Atletico Madrid player starred in 14 games for Victory last season as they won a second-straight grand final. "We're thrilled to be able to have Alex back for another season at Victory," football director John Didulica said.
