‘What would Diana have expected of you' Meghan pressured to ‘conform' with Royal tradition
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-28 14:08:00

Meghan Markle and Prince Harry are set to visit the UK to attend the Queen's Platinum Jubilee celebrations over the June bank holiday weekend.The couple's plans have attracted much attention as the event will be the country's first public royal appearance made by the couple in over two years.Psychologist, Dr Arthur Cassidy, suggested Meghan Markle will be pressured to align with royal tradition during the ceremonies and claimed her husband, Prince Harry, would expect her to properly represent the Royal Family.
