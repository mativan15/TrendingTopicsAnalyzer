How to change your Fortnite password or reset it

Losing access to your Fortnite account could mean losing years of progress in the game. As Epic Games accounts are typically linked to a credit card, you might also lose actual money.While the Battle Royale mode of Fortnite is free to play, players often buy cosmetic items in the Fortnite Item Shop. This makes Fortnite accounts juicy targets for hackers, some of whom will sell the compromised Fortnite accounts, which may have had hundreds or thousands of dollars spent on them by the original owner.
