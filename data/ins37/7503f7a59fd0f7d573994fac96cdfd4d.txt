[Photo/Xinhua] Humanity has only one home－the Earth. But it isn't ours exclusively. We share our beautiful planet－this stunning blue marble－with millions of other species, and our existence is intertwined with theirs. For the sake of all living things, we must ensure a healthy and thriving planet. This simple yet important truth is reflected in this year's International Day for Biological Diversity theme: "building a shared future for all life".
