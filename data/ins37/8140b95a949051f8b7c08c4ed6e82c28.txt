The Progressive Corporation (NYSE:PGR) has announced that it will pay a dividend of US$0.10 per share on the 15th of July. This means the annual payment will be 1.6% of the current stock price, which is lower than the industry average.

Progressive's Dividend Is Well Covered By Earnings

The dividend yield is a little bit low, but sustainability of the payments is also an important part of evaluating an income stock. Prior to this announcement, Progressive's dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings.
