Liverpool told to make three January signings as 'clear' options named
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 05:30:00

Liverpool have been told that they need to bring in three players in January, with key positions identified and potential targets named. Jurgen Klopp's side have had a disappointing start to the season, as they sit tenth in the Premier League table and next welcome Manchester City to Anfield.Injuries have highlighted problem areas in the squad, with Fabio Carvalho and Harvey Elliott getting key minutes in midfield while Joe Gomez is set to deputise at right-back against Pep Guardiola's side on Sunday, and former Liverpool defender Jose Enrique has urged them to spend in January.
