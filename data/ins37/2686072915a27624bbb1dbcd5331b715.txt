Image source, Ian Warburton / GeographImage caption, The first Menai Bridge was built in the early 19th CenturyOne of two bridges linking Anglesey and the north Wales mainland has shut immediately after engineers identified "serious risks".Menai Bridge was shut at 14:00 BST on Friday on the orders of the Welsh government, which announced it five minutes after it closed.Work to ensure its safety means it may remain closed until next year.Traffic is being diverted to the nearby Britannia Bridge, and the local Senedd Member said he was "very concerned".
