HOLLYWOOD, Calif., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Sycamore Entertainment Group, Inc. (SEGI) is pleased to restate that it was the premier sponsor of the Floyd Mayweather, TMT Racing's #50 car, for the NASCAR Coca-Cola 600 on Sunday, May 29, 2022. Considered one of NASCAR's "Crown Jewel" races, the event had more than 50,000 fans in attendance at Charlotte Motor Speedway while being broadcast live on Fox Sports.
As a boxer, Mayweather competed between 1996 and 2017, retiring with an undefeated record and winning fifteen major world championships from super featherweight to light-middleweight with a record of 50 and 0.
