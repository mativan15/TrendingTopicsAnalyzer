How innovation in embedded insurance is redefining the future of insurance industry
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 08:22:10

The extensive adoption of technology by consumers during the pandemic have led companies to shift their business online. Insurers are integrating technology with their business to deliver hassle-free experience for consumers, and offer insurance solutions that are personalised to customers' needs. With the adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), big data analytics, Open API, and other technological advancements, insurtech is elevating customer convenience, thus transforming the insurance industry.
