REUTERS/Baz Ratner/File PhotoRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comJune 1 (Reuters) - Tullow Oil (TLW.L) will acquire Capricorn Energy (CNE.L) in an all-stock deal valued at 656.9 million pounds ($826.7 million), the energy groups said on Wednesday, as they focus on the African market.Capricorn investors will receive 3.8068 Tullow shares for each share they hold, and will own 47% of the combined group which will be led by Tullow boss Rahul Dhir.
