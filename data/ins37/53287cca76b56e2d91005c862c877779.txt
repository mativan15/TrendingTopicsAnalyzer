Vedanta Aluminium deploys 27 electric forklifts at Odisha plant
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-06-02 01:40:00

New Delhi: Vedanta Aluminium on Wednesday said it has deployed 27 electric forklifts at its smelter at Jharsuguda in Odisha in partnership with GEAR India and the metal producer is recruiting and training women to operate them. The move is in line with the company's plan to decarbonise its industrial vehicle fleet. "Vedanta Aluminium...has commissioned India's largest electric fleet of 27 forklifts, powered by lithium-ion batteries," the company said in a statement. All 27 forklifts arrived last month and the company is recruiting and training women to operate these forklifts, it said.
