The Rod Ryan ShowBy The Rod Ryan ShowOct 19, 2022The NFL found a day when there was no football, and they remedied that situation. Beginning next year, there will be a game on Black Friday . . . the day after Thanksgiving . . . on Amazon Prime. That's good for Amazon since "Thursday Night Football" doesn't happen on Thanksgiving. CBS and Fox have a lock on Turkey Day games.  So Amazon still gets to participate over Thanksgiving weekend . . . which, as a bonus, is also the kickoff to the holiday shopping season.
