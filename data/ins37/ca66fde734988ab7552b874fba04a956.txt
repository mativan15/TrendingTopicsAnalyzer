Image source, ReutersImage caption, Jeremy Hunt arriving at the BBC on SaturdayDifficult decisions will be needed "across the board" on tax and spending, the new chancellor has said. Jeremy Hunt told the BBC that some taxes will go up, while government spending may need to fall.He said two mistakes were made in the mini-budget by Kwasi Kwarteng - cutting the top rate of tax and announcing it without an independent forecast. But he also praised his predecessor for help offered to people struggling with their energy bills.
