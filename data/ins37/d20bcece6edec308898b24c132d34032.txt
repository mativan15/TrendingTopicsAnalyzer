Scott Olson After the bell on Wednesday, we received third quarter results from electric vehicle maker Tesla (NASDAQ:TSLA), seen in this shareholder letter. Analyst estimates had come down a bit recently thanks to deliveries falling short plus some previous production hiccups, and the stock had fallen with the market in recent weeks. Unfortunately, the overall numbers were not great, sending shares lower in the after-hours session. As I discussed in my earnings preview article, expectations were that Tesla would put on a good show.
