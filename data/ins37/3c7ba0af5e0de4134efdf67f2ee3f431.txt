IISc Bangalore to offer sponsored fellowship for MTech students
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-01 10:42:12

The Applied Materials India Private Limited has signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the Centre for Nano Science and Engineering (CeNSE) at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore to offer a sponsored fellowship programme for second-year Master of Technology (MTech) students starting from the academic year 2022-23.
The CeNSE MTech fellowship is designed to provide opportunities for students from traditional engineering and science disciplines to gain expertise in electronics and semiconductor technology.
