THE Chase fans were left open-mouthed as a contestant revealed his age - leaving host Bradley Walsh in stunned silence. 
The TV star was joined by four new hopefuls hoping to take home some cash from the popular ITV game show on Thursday night.

Angela, Gabe, Fiona and Paul were introduced by host Bradley, 61, ahead of their bid to beat the Chaser and win some money. 
But it wasn't the team's quizzing skills that had people in awe - but Birmingham-native Paul's age, which he revealed on the show.
