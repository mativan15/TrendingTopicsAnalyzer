For startup founders looking to scale their startups, a startup accelerator — which offers mentorship, networking opportunities, some funding, and access to venture investors — could be what they need.Founded in 2006, Techstars has become one of biggest and most respected accelerators, responsible for success stories such as ClassPass, DigitalOcean, and PillPack. It has grown from a single accelerator in Boulder, Colorado, into a massive operation, hosting 40 startup accelerators in 15 countries.
