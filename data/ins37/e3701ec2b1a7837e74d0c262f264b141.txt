Royal Caribbean has given a first look at its huge new cruise ship Icon of the Seas with a water park, infinity pools, bars, restaurants and a neighbourhood for familiesRoyal Caribbean unveils new mega cruise ship for 2024Royal Caribbean has unveiled a first look at its brand new cruise ship Icon of the Seas - and judging by the new images and video, it looks absolutely epic. The mega ship will boast the world's largest water park at sea with SIX waterslides, seven pools, a huge swim-up bar, a five-deck high park, the first suspended infinity pool at sea, a theatre and even an ice rink.
