A bride left her wedding guests gobsmacked after she decided to disappear from her own ceremony for more than two hours - only to return with a newly styled haircutThe bride vanished in the middle of her wedding (stock photo) At a wedding, it's not uncommon for the bride to change out of her wedding dress once the main ceremony has finished, so that she can wear something different to the reception. But one bride decided to leave her guests waiting for more than two hours at her wedding because instead of just changing her dress, she decided to switch up her hair too.
