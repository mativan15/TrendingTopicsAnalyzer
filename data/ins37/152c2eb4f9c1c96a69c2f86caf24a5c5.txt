viki2win/iStock via Getty Images When we first covered Broadmark Realty Capital Inc. (NYSE:BRMK), we waxed on poetically about everything we found right with the firm. Here was a company that demonstrated a resilient business model and delivered good returns over the long term. The problem? The bulk of that was in the private markets, and its public career has been far less fantastic. Granted, that it did go public near the COVID-19 pandemic initiation. But even then, it has made a few missteps, and they continue till today.
