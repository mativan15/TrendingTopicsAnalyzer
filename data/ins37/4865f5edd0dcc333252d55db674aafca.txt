The news: Neobanks must focus on customer service to stand out among the crowd and catch up to incumbent banks, per American Banker. Insider Intelligence It's hard to get human help: With approximately 400  neobanks at the start of 2022 serving more than one billion customers, the digital challengers must become fluent in customer service.But less than half (47%) of 3,400 neobank customers surveyed by J.D. Power felt it was convenient to reach a customer service representative, as opposed to 61% of 6,500 direct bank customers.
