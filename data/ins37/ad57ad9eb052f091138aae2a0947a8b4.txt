Poll shows Canadians want more and faster government action on land and ocean protection

2022-10-19 00:00:00

TRADITIONAL UNCEDED ALGONQUIN TERRITORY/OTTAWA, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- A new poll commissioned by the Canadian Parks and Wilderness Society (CPAWS) shows that a strong majority of Canadians support the commitment to protect more land and sea in Canada, and consider land and sea protection to be a critical issue. Key findings of the poll include: A strong majority of Canadians (over 80%) support further government commitments to protecting land and sea in Canada, including increasing protection to at least 30% of land and sea by 2030.
