Shares of . gained 0.06 per cent to Rs 608.5 in Thursday's trade. It hit a high of Rs 610.45 and low of Rs 602.5 during the session. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 851.65, while the 50-DMA was at Rs 717.87. If a stock trades above 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is upward. On the other hand, if the stock trades below 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it is considered a bearish trend and if trades between these averages, then it suggests the stock can go either way.
