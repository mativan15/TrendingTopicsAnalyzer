October 20, 2022 07:50 PM Eastern Daylight Time FREMONT, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Amprius Technologies, Inc. ("Amprius") (NYSE: AMPX), a leader in ultra-high energy density lithium-ion batteries with its Silicon Nanowire Anode Platform, today announced that it will host a demonstration on Wednesday, October 26, 2022 at 1:00 p.m. EDT, of one of its battery's extreme fast charge rate of 0-80% state of charge in less than six minutes.

Amprius has been in commercial production since 2018, and has continued to evolve its batteries in pursuit of game changing performance, offering batteries that provide superior performance compared to conventional graphite lithium-ion batteries.
