[Photo/Sipa] BEIJING -- Despite key indicators suggesting downward pressure on the Chinese economy, new investments continue to flow into the world's second-largest economy, seeking opportunities that businesses believe are irreplaceable for their long-term strategic development. Observers say that China's economic outlook remains robust due to its strong resilience and ample potential despite the Omicron resurgence and external headwinds.
