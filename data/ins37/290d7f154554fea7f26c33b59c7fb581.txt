Farm workers drying and packing paddy at a rice mill in Palakkad on Thursday. Paddy farmers in Kerala are on a warpath as the government has been inordinately delaying its procurement schedule. Rice mill owners in Kerala too are at loggerheads with the government.
| Photo Credit: MUSTAFAH KK
Paddy procurement in the State is set to pick up from Friday following a settlement reached between the government and mill owners at a meeting convened by Food Minister G.R. Anil here on Thursday. The millers who had suspended procurement to press their demands agreed to resume the process and enter into an agreement with Supplyco.
