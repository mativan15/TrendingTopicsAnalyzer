PROPAGANDA. A word more commonly associated with Nazi Germany, Vladimir Putin or the ironically named People's Republic of China. Not TikTok.
Phonetically, 'tick tock' sounds harmless. In reality, the video-sharing platform is a ticking time bomb on the collective mental and physical health of the next generation.
