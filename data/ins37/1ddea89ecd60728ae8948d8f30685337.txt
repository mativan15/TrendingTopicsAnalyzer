Aperam S.A. / Key word(s): Miscellaneous Statement regarding Aperam and Acerinox 03-Jun-2022 / 10:00 CET/CEST Luxembourg, 3 June 2022 (10:00 CET) -  Aperam confirms that very preliminary discussions with Acerinox with respect to a possible  transaction that may result in a business combination have been recently engaged. Those discussions are at an early stage, and no agreement has been reached as to the scope, structure or terms of any possible transaction. There is no certainty that any such agreement will be reached or, if so, on what terms.
