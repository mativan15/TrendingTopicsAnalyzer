Man Utd boss Erik ten Hag has no issue with Bruno Fernandes' performances (Image: GETTY)Manchester United manager Erik ten Hag is adamant he has no problem with the performances of Bruno Fernandes - despite his lack of goals and assists this season. The Portuguese playmaker is usually able to indulge in some eye-catching figures which have often masked some of his poor displays but now his numbers have seemingly dried up since the arrival of his manager.Fernandes has still remained a mainstay in Ten Hag's side, however, yet it's Christian Eriksen that appears to have stolen his thunder following his free transfer in the summer after his release from Brentford.
