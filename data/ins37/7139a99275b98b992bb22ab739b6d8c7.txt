LIVID homeowners have laid into their council for wasting a fortune on painting wiggly white lines on the high street.
The local authority in Worthing, West Sussex, forked out £1,230 on the pedestrian zone paint job - but residents think it's a complete misuse of funds.
White wiggly lines on the street in Worthing, West Sussex, have sparked fury among localsCredit: David Dyson Commisioned by The Sun
Mark and Fiona Goulding described the £1,230 paint job was 'a complete waste of money'Credit: David Dyson Commisioned by The Sun
Mark and Frida Goulding, who live in the area, described the "continental-style" decorative squiggles as "an utter waste of money".
