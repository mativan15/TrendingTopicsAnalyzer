The BJP government in Uttarakhand has increased from ₹16,000 to ₹20,000 the monthly pension given to people who were jailed under two Acts used to quell political dissent during the Emergency.People who were imprisoned under the Maintenance of Internal Security Act and the Defence of India Act during the Emergency from June 25, 1975, to March 21, 1977, were being paid ₹16,000 since January 17, 2018.
