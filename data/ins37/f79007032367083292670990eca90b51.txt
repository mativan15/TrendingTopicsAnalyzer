IANSDengue patient in UP dies after allegedly given Mosambi juice instead of plasmaA dengue patient in Uttar Pradesh passed away after allegedly being transfused fruit juice instead of blood platelets, as per media reports. The police said that the hospital has been sealed. After a video of the alleged incident went viral on social media, the district administration swung into action and the hospital was sealed on Thursday on the direction of Uttar Pradesh Deputy Chief Minister Brajesh Pathak. 
 The patient, Pradeep Pandey, was shifted to another hospital, where he died after his condition deteriorated, officials said.
