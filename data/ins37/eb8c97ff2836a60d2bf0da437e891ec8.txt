SAN JOSE, Calif., October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Ed-tech leader YuJa, Inc. announces a multiyear contract extension with the Dominican University of California for its use of the YuJa Enterprise Video Platform and Zoom Enterprise Connector campuswide. The solution provides instructors and students with a central learning hub for video and media content.

Initially, the institution was seeking an all-in-one Video Content Management System to securely record, edit, manage, and distribute content.
