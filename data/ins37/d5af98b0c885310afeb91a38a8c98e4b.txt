October 19, 2022 17:02 IST Gopal Rai, Environment Minister of Delhi, said a public awareness campaign — Diye Jalao Patakhe Nahi (light diyas, not firecrackers) — will be launched on October 21. File | Photo Credit: Sushil Kumar Verma Bursting of firecrackers on Diwali in Delhi will attract a jail term of up to six months and a fine of ₹200, Environment Minister Gopal Rai said on Wednesday.Production, storage and sale of firecrackers in the capital will be punishable with a fine of up to ₹5,000 and three years jail under Section 9B of the Explosives Act, the minister told a press conference.
