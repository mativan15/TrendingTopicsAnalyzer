Gold prices pared some of the previous gains following stronger dollar and rally in US bond yields he added.May 31: Gold prices dropped on Tuesday, as a higher dollar and rising U.S. Treasury yields weighed on demand for greenback-priced bullion, which is set for a second straight monthly loss for the first time since March 2021. 
FUNDAMENTALS
* Spot gold was down 0.5% at $1,847.50 per ounce, as of 0057 GMT. U.S. gold futures were down 0.4% at $1,850.40. * Gold prices are down about 2.6% so far this month, their most since September.
