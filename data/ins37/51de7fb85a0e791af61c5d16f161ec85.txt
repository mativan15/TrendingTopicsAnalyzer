Shining light: Radiant Cleaners keeps Nottingham's Navigation Inn in top shape (Image: )Founded in Nottingham by former teacher Matt Parfitt in 2017, Grace's award-winning Radiant Cleaners has grown to a team of 30 plus providing services to large corporates and organisations.This year it was joined by Jubilee Events, a marquee hire and hospitality operation with a core of six staff.The outlook is positive for both with Radiant's turnover expected to hit £350,000 in 2023 and Jubilee's £75,000. All profits from the two certified social enterprises are either reinvested or passed to parent Grace, a charity.
