More Than Twenty-Five Million Players Are Playing Overwatch 2
ign.com
news
2022-10-18 06:53:27

Over 25 million heroes have joined the battle in Overwatch 2 in the first ten days since its release on Windows PC, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, and the Nintendo Switch. The game has established itself as a worldwide sensation, with a daily player population that is almost evenly distributed across EMEA, Asia, and the Americas. This figure nearly triples the peak daily player count recorded on the original Overwatch. 'The launch of Overwatch 2 has been such an important moment for Blizzard.
