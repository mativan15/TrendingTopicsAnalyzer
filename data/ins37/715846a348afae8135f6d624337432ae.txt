May 31, 2022 09:53 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Halper Sadeh LLP, an investor rights law firm, is investigating whether the sale of CatchMark Timber Trust, Inc. (NYSE: CTT) to PotlatchDeltic Corporation for 0.23 common shares of PotlatchDeltic stock for each common share of CatchMark is fair to CatchMark Timber shareholders.
