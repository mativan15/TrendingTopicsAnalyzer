SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kyriba's Currency Impact Report (CIR), a comprehensive quarterly report which details the impacts of foreign exchange (FX) exposures among 1,200 multinational companies based in North America and Europe with at least 15 percent of their revenue coming from overseas, sustained $49.09 billion in total impacts to earnings from currency volatility. The combined pool of corporations reported $11.82 billion in tailwinds and $37.27 billion in headwinds in the second quarter
