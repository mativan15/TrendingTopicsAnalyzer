2022-10-20 18:30:00

'We should complete each other's sentences when talking about the region's potential.'

That was Martin Schmidt's message to a group of business and community leaders last week at an event hosted by the United Way.

Schmidt, four months in as the new president of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, challenged the room — and region — to think big and long term. And he called competing against our own cities and towns 'small ball.'

A simple message but one that has proved nearly impossible for the region's stakeholders to overcome.
