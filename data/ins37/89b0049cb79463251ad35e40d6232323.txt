Mindtree and EPOS to develop next-generation audio products, technologies, and experiences COPENHAGEN, Denmark, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Mindtree, a global technology services and digital transformation company, today announced that it has extended its relationship with premium audio and video solutions brand, EPOS as a digital engineering partner to help augment and accelerate the brand's development of audio technologies and solutions.As part of the multiyear engagement, Mindtree will work as an integrated part of EPOS' development organisation, and take part in strengthening its product innovation, time-to-market, and customer satisfaction, especially in EPOS' high-growth enterprise audio and video segment.
