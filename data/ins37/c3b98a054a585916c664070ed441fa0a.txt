Spencer Platt/Getty Images News U.S. stocks had pared some gains but were still comfortably in the green in Tuesday late morning trade, as investor sentiment was buoyed by strong earnings from Goldman Sachs and data that showed a rise in factory output for September. Wall Street's major indices have now gained for two days straight after a major sell-off last week sparked by inflation worries. Earnings season has become the main focus, with market participants hoping for strong quarterly reports by big companies on significantly tempered expectations.
