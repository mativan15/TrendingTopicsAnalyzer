By Australian Associated Press Published: 03:30 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 04:05 EDT, 2 June 2022 Netball Australia CEO Kelly Ryan has defended the decision to sell hosting rights for the Super Netball grand final annually, saying it will help the organisation turn around its dire financial position. The ladder-leading Melbourne Vixens have been dudded after this year's Super Netball grand final was sold to the WA Government. It's believed Netball Australia will receive about $300,000 in cash from the deal.
