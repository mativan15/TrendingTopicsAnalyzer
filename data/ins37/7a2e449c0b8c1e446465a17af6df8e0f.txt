BalkansCat/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Fasten your seatbelts for rough weather ahead. That's the biggest theme in the latest earnings season from China, where companies are warning that a slowdown in the first quarter of the year looks set to accelerate into the current quarter amid growing restrictions to contain the spread of Covid Omicron outbreaks. The latest distress signal is coming from retailer MINISO Group (NYSE:MNSO), whose small, brightly lit shops are being shut down en masse in its home China market during recent lockdowns.
