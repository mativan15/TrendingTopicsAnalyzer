Reliance Jio Q2 Results: Cons PAT rises 27% YoY to Rs 4,729 crore
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 15:30:00

Jio Platforms Ltd. (JPL) posted a 26.9% on-year rise in net profit in the second quarter of the fiscal year 2022-23 (FY23), driven by higher data and voice consumption. It continued to add customers in the second quarter, with 427.6 million users as on September 30, as compared to 419.9 million at the end of the June quarter. For the September quarter, JPL's consolidated net profit stood at Rs 4,729 crore compared to Rs 4, 530 crore in the June quarter and Rs 3,728 crore a year ago, the company said in a media release Friday.
