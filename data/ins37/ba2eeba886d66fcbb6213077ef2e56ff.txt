Liz Truss has lost support says John Redwood (Image: Getty)Conservative MPs who had been strongly supporting Liz Truss's tax cuts for growth are now concerned about what happens next from the new team. Many of them are no longer giving unconditional support. The new Chancellor with the Prime Minister needs to pull together the four strands of a successful growth strategy.They need to rescue as many of the tax cuts as possible. They need to set out decent spending plan. They need to roll out all the many measures needed to grow different sectors of the economy through changed regulations, government purchasing and public/private partnerships.
