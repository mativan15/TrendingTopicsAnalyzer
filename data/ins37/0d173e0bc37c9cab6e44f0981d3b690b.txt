Fuel retailers in 22 states to halt buying on Tuesday to demand higher commission
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-05-30 17:15:00

ANIIndustry officials said that petrol pumps don't need daily refills and their storage tanks store enough supplies to last couple of days.A petrol pump dealers' association will on Tuesday resort to 'no purchase of petrol and diesel' in protest against no increase in their commission by the oil marketing companies, but the protest is unlikely to disrupt supplies as fuel bunks have enough stocks to last couple of days. Petrol Pump Dealers Association said its members in 22 states will join the protest.
