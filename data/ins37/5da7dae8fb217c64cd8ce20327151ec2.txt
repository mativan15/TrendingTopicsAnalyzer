SBI sells Rs 3,815 crore KSK Mahanadi loan to Aditya Birla ARC
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 01:45:00

AgenciesThe RP has received ₹29,501 crore of claims from financial creditors.Mumbai: is set to sell its ₹3,815 crore outstanding loan from KSK Mahanadi Power to Aditya Birla Asset Reconstruction Company, which will help the bank shore up its earnings for the June quarter, said two people aware of the plan. The bank declared Aditya Birla ARC's bid of ₹1,544 crore as the highest at an auction held on Monday, they said.

This will be the single biggest distressed loan sale on an upfront payment basis by any bank, equating to a recovery of 40%.
