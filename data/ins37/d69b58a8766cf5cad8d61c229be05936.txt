UK government bond yields stabilised, even as Liz Truss's premiership moved closer to the cliff edge — a sign that the government's chaos might no longer matter to markets, according to Bloomberg Opinion. On the corporate front, industry bellwether Schroders saw its assets under management drop by £21 billion after the UK pension fund crisis engulfed the sector.
