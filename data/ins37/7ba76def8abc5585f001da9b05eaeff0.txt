Xinhua | Updated: 2022-10-20 18:05 Share - WeChat An undated proof image shows the likeness of Asian American actress Anna May Wong, to be cast on the fifth 25-cent coin in the American Women Quarters (AWQ) Program to be issued by the US Mint. [Photo/Agencies] LOS ANGELES - Anna May Wong, regarded as the first Chinese American film star in Hollywood, is set to become the first Asian American to be featured on US currency, according to the United States Mint, which is responsible for producing coinage for the country.
