Mahindra & Mahindra Fourth-Quarter Profit Rose 48%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-30 01:00:00

By Clarence Leong Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd.'s fourth-quarter net profit rose 48% from a year earlier to 22.37 billion rupees ($288.1 million), thanks to a solid performance from its auto business. Revenue from operations rose 21% from a year earlier to INR259.34 billion, the Indian auto maker said Saturday. Quarterly profit from Mahindra's automotive business more than doubled to INR4.68 billion, compared with INR1.66 billion a year earlier, helped by improving semiconductor supplies. Profit from the company's farm-equipment business declined 34% to INR7.
