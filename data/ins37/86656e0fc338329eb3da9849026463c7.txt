Analyst Profile

Eric Compton Equity Analyst

Eric Compton, CFA, is an equity analyst for Morningstar Research Services LLC, covering the U.S. and Canadian banking sectors, including the U.S. money center banks, U.S. regional banks, and the Big Six Canadian banks.Before joining Morningstar in 2015, Compton was a business analyst for ESIS, a global provider of risk management products and a subsidiary of ACE Group. Compton holds a bachelor's degree in applied health science from Wheaton College. He has passed all three levels of the Chartered Financial Analyst® program and holds the Chartered Financial Analyst® designation.
