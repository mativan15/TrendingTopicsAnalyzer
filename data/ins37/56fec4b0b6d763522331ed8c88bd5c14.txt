SEARCHED FOR:The Uttar Pradesh government has allowed factories to employ women on night shifts between 7 pm and 6 am, if the employers fulfil certain conditions to ensure the safety and security of the female workers and make certain provisions at the workplace...29 May, 2022, 09.04 AM IST​​​The rule is applicable to both the government and private sector. The government has stated that if women are engaged in work or duty between 7 PM to 6 AM, then the company or organisation must have her consent for the same.
