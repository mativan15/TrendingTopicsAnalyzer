Soaring food prices push UK inflation back to 40-year high
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 11:15:00

LONDON: The biggest jump in food prices since 1980 pushed British inflation back into double digits last month, matching a 40-year high hit in July in a new blow for households grappling with a cost-of-living crisis.The Office for National Statistics said the consumer price index (CPI) increased by 10.1% in annual terms in September. A Reuters poll of economists had pointed to a reading of 10.0%, after a 9.9% rise in August.The pound slipped below $1.13 on the news and was last down 0.2% on the day.
