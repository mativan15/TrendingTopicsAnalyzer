By Reuters Published: 21:12 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 21:12 EDT, 27 May 2022 The Los Angeles Lakers filled their head coaching vacancy Friday, signing former Milwaukee Bucks assistant coach Darvin Ham to a four-year deal, multiple outlets reported. Ham, 48, does not have any head coaching experience but has been an NBA assistant coach the past 11 seasons, including two seasons (2011-13) with the Lakers. He has been with the Bucks since 2018. Ham now takes over a storied franchise that has won 17 NBA titles, 19 conference titles and 33 division titles.
