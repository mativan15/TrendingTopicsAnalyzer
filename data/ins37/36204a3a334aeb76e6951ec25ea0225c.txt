Good Morning Britain star Kate Garraway is stunned by reports suggesting Boris Johnson could make a fresh leadership charge to return as Prime Minister weeks after being ousted in favour of Liz Truss.
Ms Truss – who succeeded Boris on September 6 when he was forced out in disgrace – lasted 44 days in office, and quit just 24 hours after telling MPs she was a ‘fighter and not a quitter'.
