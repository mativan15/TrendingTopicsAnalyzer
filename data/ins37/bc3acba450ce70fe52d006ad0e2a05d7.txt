A comprehensive guide on how to preserve your couture pieces

Couture pieces are comparable to high fine jewellery or expensive art. They are labour intensive, emotional, and crafted with the utmost care using time-intensive techniques by most skilled artisans and designers. Hence, it is equally important to know how to store and preserve couture pieces as would fine jewellery or art. And there is no doubt that if you want your couture pieces to last for as long as possible, looking after them is the best way to start. The first tip to taking care of couture garments is to never hang them in your closet for a long period of time.
