Union Commerce & Industry Minister Piyush Goyal Chennai (Tamil Nadu): Commerce Minister Piyush Goyal, who is on his visit to the southern state of the country- Tamil Nadu, on Sunday addressed the students of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. "World today recognizes that India's time has arrived", Piyush Goyal addressed. "Americans and Europeans would also want to engage with sustainability, newer technologies at an affordable price & they would look forward to you for it", Commerce Minister underlined.
