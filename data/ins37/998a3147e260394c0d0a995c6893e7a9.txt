NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Color Star Technology Co., Ltd. (Nasdaq: CSCW) ("Color Star" or the "Company"), an entertainment technology company with a global network that focuses on the application of technology and artificial intelligence in the entertainment industry, recently announced that Color Metaverse Pte. Ltd. ("Color Metaverse"), a subsidiary of Color Star, held a signing ceremony in Dubai to officially sign a strategic cooperation agreement with A-NEX Korea ("A-NEX"), a Korean company.
