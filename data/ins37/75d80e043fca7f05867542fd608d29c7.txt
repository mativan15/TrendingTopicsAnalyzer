Wilberforce, Ohio State students to collaborate on developing electric vehicle systems
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 18:44:00

May 30—WILBERFORCE — A joint team of students from Wilberforce University and the Ohio State University will compete in an challenge to develop electric vehicles over the next four years.

Wilberforce and Ohio State are among 15 North American universities that will compete in the next EcoCar EV challenge, set to begin in this fall. The four-year international competition will challenge students to engineer a next-generation battery electric and autonomous vehicle.

The EcoCAR EV Challenge will place students at the "cutting edge" of automotive engineering education, said Wilberforce advisor and Computer Science Professor Deok Nam.
