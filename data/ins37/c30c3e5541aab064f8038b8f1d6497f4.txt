18 Oct 2022, 07:41 PM ISTThe benchmark index Sensex rallied for the third consecutive session, gaining about 550 points to end at 58,960 on Tuesday. During this upmove, four stocks from the BSE midcap index managed to touch new 52-week highs.The 52-week high is the highest price at which a stock has traded during the last year. Some traders and investors use this technical indicator as an important factor for analysing a stock's current value and for predicting its future price movement.New 52-week high: Rs 133.
