Survey finds 7189 unrecognised madrasas in Uttar Pradesh
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 20:08:25

A statewide survey has found 7189 unrecognised madrasas educating more than 16 lakh students, Uttar Pradesh Madrasa Education Board chairman Iftikhar Ahmed Javed said on Thursday. Describing the findings an eye opener for the authorities, Javed said these madrasas have around 3000 teachers and other staff. There are 16,513 recognised madrasas with about 20 lakh students in the state, according to the Uttar Pradesh Madrasa Education Board. And 560 of them are receiving grants from the Uttar Pradesh government.
