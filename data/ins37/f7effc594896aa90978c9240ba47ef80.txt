Data.ai — the rebranded App Annie — has released its Q3 2022 Games index, revealing the state of the mobile games market. Across the quarter, gamers spent $1.54 billion per week and downloaded over 1.1 billion games on mobile. While both of these figures are down year-on-year, spend still up 25% compared to pre-pandemic levels.
