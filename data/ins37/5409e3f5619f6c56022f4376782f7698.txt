Jury begins deliberations in Sussmann trial; Clinton campaign lawyer accused of lying to FBI
washingtontimes.com
news
2022-05-27 18:00:00

The fate of Hillary Clinton campaign lawyer Michael Sussmann is now in the hands of 12 jurors who will decide if he lied when pitching to the FBI in 2016 a false story about Trump-Russia collusion. The jurors began deliberations Friday and will mull evidence and testimony from more than 20 witnesses presented over the past two weeks in a federal courtroom in Washington. Upon sending the jurors to review the case, U.S. District Judge Christopher Cooper reminded them 'to reach a fair and justice verdict based on the evidence.
