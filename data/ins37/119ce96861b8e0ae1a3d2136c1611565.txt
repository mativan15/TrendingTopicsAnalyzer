Building an Intelligent Life, 132nd Canton Fair Creates 'Virtual Showroom' for Chinese Intelligent Manufacturing

GUANGZHOU, China, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/--The 132nd China Import and Export Fair ("Canton Fair"), which opened on October 15 as a virtual exhibition, is exhibiting over 140,000 intelligent products from top high-tech enterprises. As a trade promotion platform, the Canton Fair has created a "virtual showroom" to showcase the latest achievements of China's intelligent manufacturing and innovative technologies that are building a better, more intelligent life for everyone.Wuhan Linptech Co., Ltd.
