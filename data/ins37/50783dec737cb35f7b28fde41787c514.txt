Mumbai: Tech czars Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, Bill Gates, and Larry Page have collectively lost $140 billion in net worth between January to May this year in the backdrop of a tech rout in the public market, according to Bloomberg Billionaires' Index. According to the report, Tesla's founder and CEO, Musk's net worth fell $46 billion in the past five months, while Amazon's founder Bezos lost more than $53 billion.

Microsoft's founder Gates, the fourth richest person in the world, shed $15 billion in net worth since January while Google cofounder Page lost more than $26 billion of his net worth.
