FARR WEST, Utah (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 A reliable helmet light can be a lifesaver for ATV, snowmobile, and dirt bike riders. Rigel II-H, launching today, is a helmet light kit that magnetically attaches and detaches a powerful flashlight, with directional accuracy, to ATV, snowmobile, and dirt bike helmets. Rigel II (magnetic flashlight) can also be augmented with a magnetic belt clip for everyday carry, or a magnetic wall mount that attaches to any flat surface.
