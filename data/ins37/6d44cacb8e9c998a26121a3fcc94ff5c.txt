Lyndon Pryor

Chief engagement officer, Louisville Urban League

Years in current job: Seven

Years in the nonprofit sector: 17

Briefly describe what your organization does.

The Louisville Urban League is a nonprofit, civil rights, community service organization dedicated to eliminating the adverse impacts of racism on our community. Our mission is to assist Black people and other marginalized populations in attaining social and economic equality through direct services and advocacy.

As part of a broader National Urban League network of historic civil rights organizations throughout the United States, the Louisville Urban League, like all affiliates, is dedicated to fostering economic empowerment in order to elevate the standard of living in historically underserved urban communities, with our focus on jobs, justice, education, health and housing.
