June 01, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Aurea Software announced today that it has been accepted into the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Independent Software Vendor (ISV) Accelerate Program, a co-sell program for AWS Partners who provide software solutions that run on or integrate with AWS. The program helps partners like Aurea drive new business and accelerate sales cycles by connecting the participating ISVs with the AWS sales organization.



This new partnership will accelerate Aurea's ability to bring its cloud cost optimization solution, CloudFix, to AWS customers worldwide.
