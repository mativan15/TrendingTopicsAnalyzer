Robin Williams ‘used to steal jokes from a lot of people', claims actor
Copyright 2022, ESI Media Ltd
news
2022-05-27 14:23:43

Comedian and voice actor Joey Camen has claimed that Robin Williams used to 'steal' jokes from other comedians before paying them off.Camen made the claims during an appearance on Marc Maron's WTF podcast, when he was asked about encounters with the late Good Will Hunting star.'I stayed away from the guy,' said Camen. 'He used to steal material. He stole my material. He stole one of my bits and did it on the Mork & Mindy pilot. And I got really pissed. 'And he gave me a cheque for $300 and told me, ‘Don't cash it til Tuesday'.
