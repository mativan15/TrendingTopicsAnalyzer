Historic deal struck to see Victorian treaty negotiations with First Nations groups begin next year
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-19 19:30:33

Victoria will become the first Australian jurisdiction to begin negotiating treaties with First Nations groups next year, after a historic deal was brokered to allow traditional owners to enter into interim agreements during negotiations.The deal struck between the Andrews government and the First Peoples' Assembly – the body elected to negotiate an overarching framework for treaty – includes an initial investment of $65m over three years into a fund to help traditional owners commence treaty negotiations.
