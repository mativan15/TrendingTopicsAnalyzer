Good morning, Bay Area. The San Francisco 49ers grabbed headlines last night with the news of the team's blockbuster trade for former Stanford star and NFL all-pro running back Christian McCaffrey. The 49ers, who gave up a glut of high draft picks to snag the star from the Carolina Panthers, will pay McCaffrey $690,000 for the rest of the season, but he will earn $11.8 million in 2023 and 2024 and $12 million in 2025. Turning to transportation, San Francisco-based TeraWatt Infrastructure, which provides a network of charging centers, said it's developing the first network of high-powered charging centers for heavy-duty and medium-duty electric trucks along the Interstate 10, stretching from Los Angeles to the El Paso.
