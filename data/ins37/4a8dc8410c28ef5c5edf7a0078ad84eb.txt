HOUSTON, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- To celebrate National Women's Month in March 2022, the City of Houston honored some of Houston's Top Businesswomen. Minuteman Press franchise owner Sharon Rayner was honored with this award. Sharon and Paul Rayner have owned and operated Minuteman Press for over 20 years in the Westchase District of Houston. They have grown their business along with the growth of the Westside of Houston as a vibrant business community. The Rayners came to the forefront as leaders in their business community because of Bounce Back USA, a free initiative launched by Minuteman Press International for local communities in 2020 that was featured in Forbes.
