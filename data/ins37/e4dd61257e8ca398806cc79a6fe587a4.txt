By Thomas Ignudo October 15, 2022 / 9:48 PM / CBS Philadelphia PHILADELPHIA (CBS) -- It's Dallas week. The Eagles will take on the Dallas Cowboys on Sunday night with huge NFC East implications on the line. The Cowboys are 4-1 with backup quarterback Cooper Rush in place of the injured Dak Prescott. Rush is 4-0 in his starts this season. Prescott was limited in practice last week, so it's very unlikely he plays against Philadelphia. Head coach Mike McCarthy told reporters the Cowboys are "preparing for Cooper Rush to start against the Eagles.
