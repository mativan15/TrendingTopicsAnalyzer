October 17, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time LOUISVILLE, Ky.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Turning Point Brands, Inc. ('TPB' or 'the Company') (NYSE: TPB), a manufacturer, marketer and distributor of branded consumer products, including alternative smoking accessories and consumables, announced today the appointment of long-tenured company executive Graham Purdy as CEO and Board Director, effective as of October 16, 2022, following Yavor Efremov's resignation as CEO and Director. Additionally, David Glazek will transition from Non-Executive to Executive Board Chairman, effective January 2023.
