DETROIT, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Five Honigman LLP Michigan practice groups garnered the highest ranking of 'Band 1' in the 2022 edition of Chambers USA, more than any law firm in Michigan.
The prestigious Chambers directory of leading U.S. business law firms and lawyers bestowed the highest ranking 'Band 1,' on the following Honigman practices in Michigan:

Bankruptcy/Restructuring
Corporate/M&A
Intellectual Property
Litigation: General Commercial
Real Estate

Honigman's Labor and Employment and Immigration practices in Michigan were also recognized, as was Intellectual Property and Insurance: Dispute Resolution: Policyholder in Illinois.
