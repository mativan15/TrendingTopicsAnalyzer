REUTERS/Dado RuvicRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comJune 3 (Reuters) - Better regulation of the fast-growing world of crypto assets is needed not to keep rich people from losing money but for the sake of everyone else, Federal Reserve Governor Christopher Waller said on Friday."The main issue in crypto-asset regulation isn't how to protect sophisticated crypto-investors; it's how to protect the rest of us," Waller said in remarks prepared for delivery to the SNB-CIF Conference on Cryptoassets and Financial Innovation in Zurich.
