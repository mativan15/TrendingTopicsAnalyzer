How business can hardwire change to deliver racial equity
weforum.org
business
2022-05-27 13:01:12

Tens of organizations joined the Partnering for Racial Justice in Business initiative helmed by the World Economic Forum following the tragic murder of George Floyd, 25 May 2020. Businesses play a key part in achieving racial equity through inward retrospection, collective actions with communities, government and civil society and by listening to those at the sharp end of harms. Organizations must recognise the systemic nature of racial inequity and where reforms need to take place for meaningful change to occur.
