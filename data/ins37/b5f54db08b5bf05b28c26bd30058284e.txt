October 19, 2022 10:39 AM Eastern Daylight Time FALLS CHURCH, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, BAE Systems, Inc. released Caring for our People, Culture, and Communities, the company's second annual report highlighting the significant progress made in advancing diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI).



'Prioritizing an inclusive workplace isn't just good for our people and communities, it's essential to the health and sustainability of our business,' said Tom Arseneault, president and CEO of BAE Systems, Inc.
