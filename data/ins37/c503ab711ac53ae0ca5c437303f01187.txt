Wall Street breaks 7-week losing streak — longest since 2001
cbsnews.com
news
2022-05-27 20:32:00

May 27, 2022 / 4:32 PM / CBS/AP Stocks rose on Wall Street Friday and closed higher for the week, breaking a seven-week losing streak, the longest such stretch since 2001. The S&P 500 rose 100 points, or 2.5%, to close at 4,158, increasing its gain for the week to 6.6%. That's the biggest weekly gain for the benchmark index since November 2020. Technology stocks were a big factor pushing the market higher. That sent the Nasdaq composite up 3.3%. The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 1.8%.The gains were broad, led by technology stocks.
