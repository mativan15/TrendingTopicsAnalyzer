High data visibility delivers better business outcomes
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 17:59:09

Enterprises pick up data from disparate sources and its visibility is very critical for them.  There is a significant rise in cloud-based workloads across organizations and the top priority for cloud users is to increase cloud visibility to ensure greater operational control.  The extent of ease by which businesses can monitor their data and analyze it indicates data visibility.  The higher the data visibility, the chances of taking informed decisions are greater. Network visibility equips the IT teams to become more aware of the data, components, and other network content within any enterprise computer network.
