Coffee Day Global back in black, logs Rs 65.40 crore PAT in Q4
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 01:30:00

AgenciesCoffee Day Enterprises had reported a consolidated net profit of Rs 18.80 crore for the third quarter ended on December 31, 2021. It had posted a net loss of Rs 110.41 crore in the corresponding period of the preceding fiscal.Coffee Day Global Ltd, which owns and operates popular coffee chain CCD, has posted a net profit of ₹65.40 crore for the fourth quarter ended on March 31, 2022. It had posted a net loss of ₹94.81 crore in the January-March quarter in FY21 and a net loss of ₹94.81 in the October-December quarter of FY22.
