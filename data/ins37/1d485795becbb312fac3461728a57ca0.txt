View: Eight years of technology-led ‘Antyodaya'

Prime Narendra Modi announced the Jan Dhan Yojana, a national mission towards financial inclusion, in hismaiden Independence Day address on August 15, 2014.Jan Dhan accounts were linked to mobile numbers, which then used Aadhaar to enable ‘Direct Benefit Transfer' and empower the marginalised in society.‘Antyodaya' was becoming a reality, and this was just the beginning. Ayushman Bharat, Mudra Yojana, Kisan Samman Nidhi to PM Fasal Bima Yojana — digital technology delivered trust and transparency while governance ensured last-mile delivery.
