Bengaluru The Congress in Karnataka on Tuesday warned the ruling BJP saying it will launch a ‘SayCM' campaign after the ‘PayCM' drive, over the failure to respond to the questions raised by it regarding the government's functioning.The party's warning was over the BJP's alleged silence on the 50 questions posed by it.The Congress launched the ‘Nim Hatra, Idya Uttara' drive asking the BJP government to answer 50 questions.
