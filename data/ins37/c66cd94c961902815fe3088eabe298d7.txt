GEMMA Bird lives mortgage-free in a four-bedroom detached house, drives a Range Rover and jets off on multiple holidays a year to plush resorts in Mexico, Lapland and Dubai. 
But the 40-year-old mum-of-two, who lives in Billericay, Essex, isn't an heiress or a lottery winner - no, she's achieved this comfortable life despite never earning more than £25k a year.
