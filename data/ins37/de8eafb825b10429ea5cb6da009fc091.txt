Wild gesture! Royal fans lay into Femi after throwing shade at Queen's Jubilee
express.co.uk
news
2022-06-02 20:33:00

Writer for the Independent Femi Oluwole caused a storm of angry responses after suggesting the Royal Family should say they would pay for the Jubilee themselves, and then donate the money that would have been spent on the Jubilee to councils in the UK. While thousands have taken to the streets to celebrate the monarch's 70th year on the throne, many others have been left with a bitter taste in their mouths by the amount being spent on it while struggling families are having to choose between eating or heating their homes.
