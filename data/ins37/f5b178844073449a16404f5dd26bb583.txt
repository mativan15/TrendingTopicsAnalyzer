Chelsea ThomasBy Chelsea ThomasMay 31, 2022According to NewsWeek, there are several different outcomes that could come from the Johnny Depp/Amber Heard trial: First, the jury could rule completely in Depp's favor, meaning Depp would be awarded the entire $50 million he sought in his lawsuit. Or the jury could rule in favor of Heard, which could result in Depp having to pay $100 million in damages. However, there is also a chance that Depp could win the lawsuit but through negotiations after the verdict receive a lower amount than the $50 million he sued for.
