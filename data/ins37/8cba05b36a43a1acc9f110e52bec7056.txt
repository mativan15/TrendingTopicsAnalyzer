PLANO, Texas (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 The National Business Research Institute (NBRI) is pleased to welcome Saltchuk Marine to the NBRI Circle of Excellence! This is the first year that Saltchuk Marine has won the prestigious award! The NBRI Circle of Excellence Award recognizes organizations that demonstrate high levels of Employee Experience through rigorous, scientific, psychological research of their employees. Organizations must score at or above Stretch Performance at the 75th percentile of their industry or improve their scores by a statistically significant 5 or more percentiles, which is no small feat.
