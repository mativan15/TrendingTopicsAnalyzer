Hyde Park NY Field of Honor®
iheart.com
news
2022-06-02 19:45:00

The Hyde Park Education Foundation is sponsoring its third Field of Honor® on Memorial Day weekend, May 28-30, 2022, on the lawn in front of the Home of Franklin D. Roosevelt National Historic Site, on Route 9 in Hyde Park. The Field of Honor® is a display of 20+ full-sized American flags, mounted on 8' poles. At the HPEF website, https://www.HydeParkEducationFoundation.org , flags can be purchased or dedicated; click on 'Sponsor a Flag' Each flag purchased can be labeled in memory or in honor of a veteran, a fallen soldier, or anyone the buyer wishes to remember.
