Omnicom Group (NYSE:OMC) Will Pay A Dividend Of US$0.70

The board of Omnicom Group Inc. (NYSE:OMC) has announced that it will pay a dividend on the 8th of July, with investors receiving US$0.70 per share. This means the annual payment is 3.9% of the current stock price, which is above the average for the industry.

Omnicom Group's Dividend Is Well Covered By Earnings

A big dividend yield for a few years doesn't mean much if it can't be sustained. Prior to this announcement, Omnicom Group's dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings.
