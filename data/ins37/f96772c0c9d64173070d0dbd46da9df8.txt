The Plufl, a human-sized dog bed, has been designed by two students at the University of British Columbia. (Plufl/Instagram)Sometimes it takes a Great Dane to spark a great idea.Noah Silverman was in his fourth year of an economics degree at the University of British Columbia while also grinding at a campus coffee shop and he was tired enough to envy the shop owner's large dog lounging in a custom-made dog bed.That's when it dawned on him that there was no reason people couldn't comfortably curl up on a similar design.
