Smartphone cameras will make DSLR cameras obsolete by 2024, Sony boss says
Copyright 2022, ESI Media Ltd
news
2022-06-02 14:29:20

Smartphone cameras will produce better image quality than standalone DSLR cameras by 2024, according to a Sony executive.New technologies like 'quantum saturation' and improved AI processing introduced to smartphones within the next two years will effectively render single-lens set-ups as obsolete.The comments were made during a recent business briefing, Nikkei Japan reported (via TechRadar), alongside slides of expected advancements for smartphones. 'We expect that still images [from smartphones] will exceed the image quality of single-lens reflex cameras within the next few years,' said Terushi Shimizu, president and CEO of Sony Semiconductor Solutions (SSS).
