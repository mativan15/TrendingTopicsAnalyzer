Eric Terashima will challenge Rep. Frank Iler for the N.C. House district 17 seat in November.

Voters in the district will select one of the two candidates for a two-year term ending in December 2024. The district includes Brunswick County west of Bolivia, stretching from Leland to the South Carolina border.

There will be three way for voters to cast their ballots: absentee by mail, one-stop early voting, and in-person on Election Day.

To request a ballot for absentee by mail voting, visit the North Carolina Absentee Ballot Portal (ncsbe.
