Published: 06:47 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 06:47 EDT, 15 October 2022 Kanye West brushed off recent drama and enjoyed an ice cream as he went about his day in Los Angeles on Friday. The rapper, 45, stopped by the shop in Calabasas before heading to his warehouse after it was claimed his ex-wife Kim Kardashian is no longer taking his calls and has disengaged from his seemingly endless feuding.He dressed down from his usual outlandish ensembles and sported a white long sleeve top with a graphic print for his outing.
