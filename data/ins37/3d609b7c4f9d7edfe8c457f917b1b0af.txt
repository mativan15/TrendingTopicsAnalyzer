MADISON HEIGHTS, Mich., May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nano Magic Holdings, Inc. (OTCMKT: NMGX) ('Nano Magic' or the 'Company'), a leader in nanotechnology-powered cleaning, protection and anti-fog solutions announced that effective at the end of the month it is selling a majority interest in Applied Nanotech, Inc. ('Applied Nanotech') to the employees of that subsidiary. Jacque Soptick, an employee for over 20 years, became the majority owner of Applied Nanotech. She stated: 'We are excited for this new chapter of Applied Nanotech as a woman-owned business.
