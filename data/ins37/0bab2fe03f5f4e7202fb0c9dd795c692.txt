Apollo Hospitals ties-up with Bombay Hospital to boost liver transplants in Mumbai
indiatimes.com
science
2022-10-18 13:45:00

Apollo also announced that it will be expanding the scope of liver transplant programmes in Maharashtra and Gujarat.Apollo Hospitals on Tuesday entered into a memorandum of understanding with the Bombay Hospital to boost liver transplants in Mumbai and Western India. The collaboration with Bombay Hospital will increase access to Apollo's advanced liver transplant program for patients across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region. Apollo has already entered into 11 such agreements, where it will provide its expertise, technology, training, post-operative care and guidance on liver transplant.
