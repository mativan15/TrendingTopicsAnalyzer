Both want to deliver high-speed internet services from space. But other players are making moves to strengthen their positions in a market that's expected to quadruple in size by 2030. French satellite group Eutelsat and the scrappy British space-based internet pioneer OneWeb are joining forces with the aim of creating a European champion able to compete with the likes of Musk and Bezos.
