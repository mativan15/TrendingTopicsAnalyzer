BROOKLYN, N.Y., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Etsy, Inc. (Nasdaq: ETSY), which operates two-sided online marketplaces that connect millions of passionate and creative buyers and sellers around the world, plans to release its third quarter financial results on Wednesday, November 2, 2022 in a press release after the market close. The press release can be accessed on the Etsy Investor Relations website ( investors.etsy.com ).

Etsy will also host a video webcast conference call to discuss those results at 5:00 p.
