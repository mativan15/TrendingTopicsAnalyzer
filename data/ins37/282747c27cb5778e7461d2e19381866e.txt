Digital health in India has pockets of excellence and pockets of ignorance: Rajendra Pratap Gupta
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 05:00:00

"There are 34.33 lakh registered nursing personnel, 13 lakh allied and healthcare professionals, 13 lakh allopathic doctors and 5.65 lakh AYUSH doctors in the country," said Rajendra Pratap Gupta.Driven by the pandemic, India's adoption of digital health has grown by leaps and bounds. However, this growth has not been equal and there is a bigger opportunity to make digital health accessible to all. In a conversation with ET Digital, Rajendra Pratap Gupta, the chair of the Global Digital Health Summit and former advisor to the health minister of India, talks about the impediments, the role of entrepreneurs and why digital health today is a necessity.
