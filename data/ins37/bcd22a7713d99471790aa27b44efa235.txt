Melbourne man arrested for allegedly 'threatening to kill' Pauline Hanson  Police allege 27-year-old Ibrahim Koshin used social media to make the threatsHe was released on bail and will face Melbourne Magistrate's court in DecemberBy Antoinette Milienos For Daily Mail Australia Published: 19:30 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 19:30 EDT, 21 October 2022 A 27-year-old man has been arrested for allegedly making online death threats towards Senator Pauline Hanson. Ibrahim Koshin was arrested on Friday after police raided his home in Brunswick West, 6km north of Melbourne's CBD.
