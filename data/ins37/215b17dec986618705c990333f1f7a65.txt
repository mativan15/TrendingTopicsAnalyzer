Ukraine's goal is akin to Pakistan's approach towards Russia: Indian origin lawmaker
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 14:30:00

Ukraine is to Russia what Pakistan is to India, building its identity on rivalry and opposition to a powerful neighbour and ready to militarise, said Abhay Singh, the lone Indian origin lawmaker in Russia. Singh, who created history by becoming the first Indian origin lawmaker in Russia, alleged that Anglo-Saxon powers have instilled hatred among Ukrainians since the 1990s, similar to what was done by the British leading to the partition of India into India and Pakistan. 'Ukrainians have been used by various powers like Pakistan was first used by the British, followed by the USA and then China.
