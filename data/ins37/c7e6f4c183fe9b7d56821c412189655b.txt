Professor Anthony Amoah, an environmental economist, has charged the current administration of Ghana to renew the confidence of its people, owing to the recent economic crises the country is facing. Read Story here. The professor noted that transparency from the government on how it manages the country's resources would be instrumental in restoring the country's economy. He disclosed that not only will people be more reassured, but transparency could attract foreign investors. During the launch of the Ghana Wealth Index Report at the University of Ghana Business School (UGBS) in Accra last Friday, the professor stated; 'We are proposing to the government to work hard to fix the macroeconomic environment.
