Advanced Attractions is now Aegean Med Spa, a medical spa located in the heart of downtown New Bern.

The business will offer customers services such as skincare, Botox, fillers, PRP Facials, RF Micro-needling, facials, float pods, massage therapy, salt therapy, lash and eyebrow care, virtual meditation and salon services.

The spa, at 204 Craven St., C, was celebrated by the New Bern Area Chamber of Commerce with a ribbon-cutting ceremony Thursday,
