Published: 12:36 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:36 EDT, 21 October 2022 At just 44, the politician entrusted with Labor's first budget in almost a decade has already had an industrious career in politics. Australia's 41st treasurer, Jim Chalmers, has written two books, led several different shadow portfolios and produced a doctoral thesis on former Prime Minister Paul Keating's leadership style. The married father-of-three hails from working-class Logan City in Brisbane's south. He was elected to parliament in 2013 as the Member for Rankin, a safe Labor seat.
