Las Vegas Sands (LVS) closed at $34.62 in the latest trading session, marking a +1.32% move from the prior day. This move lagged the S&P 500's daily gain of 2.47%. Elsewhere, the Dow gained 1.76%, while the tech-heavy Nasdaq added 0.71%.Prior to today's trading, shares of the casino operator had lost 4.23% over the past month. This has lagged the Consumer Discretionary sector's loss of 3.32% and the S&P 500's loss of 2.59% in that time.Investors will be hoping for strength from Las Vegas Sands as it approaches its next earnings release.
