Gotham Knights: Batman Will Be Turning in His Grave

Gotham Knights does the unthinkable by killing off Batman. As a follow-up to the popular Arkham series, Gotham Knights brings in the Bat Family -- Nightwing, Red Hood, Batgirl and Robin -- to investigate the death of the Dark Knight. While the game itself has some bright spots, its lack of polish makes the experience much less thrilling: Developer Warner Bros Games Montreal released a game in 2022 that looks like it could have come out in 2012.It's especially frustrating when you remember that the Arkham series includes some of the greatest comic-inspired games ever made.
