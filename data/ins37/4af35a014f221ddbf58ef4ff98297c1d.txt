Published October 21, 2022 2:25pm EDT A middle school student in Florida is accused of stealing $10,000 from their grandparents and giving it to several classmates.The incident happened at Lake Weir Middle School in Summerfield, Florida, where a student apparently was seen passing out a "significant" amount of cash to classmates, according to FOX 35.In a message to parents, Principal Jason Jacobs asked parents for help locating the money."Upon investigation, we uncovered that the student had taken $10,000 from their grandparents and passed it out amongst their peers.
