Nobby Clark: No 'super backers' behind successful Invercargill mayoral bid
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-15 20:00:00

Invercargill's new mayor says there were no 'super backers' behind his election campaign with no contributions passing the $1500 threshold required to be publicly declared. Nobby Clark has replaced Sir Tim Shadbolt as Invercargill's mayor with 7357 votes, 2939 votes ahead of second place Toni Biddle. Clark ran as part of the Let's Go Invercargill ticket which also put up 10 councillor candidates. Four of those councillor candidates, plus Clark, were elected.
