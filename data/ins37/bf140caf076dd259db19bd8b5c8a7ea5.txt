MONEY saving expert Martin Lewis has revealed how to have your direct debit on your energy bills slashed this winter.
On tonight's episode of The Martin Lewis Money Show on ITV, the personal finance guru told viewers that they have a right under energy companies' licensing agreements to have their payment reduced if the increase is too high.
The money saving expert explained how bill payers can have direct debits slashedCredit: ITV
He advised struggling bills payers to take regular meter readings and use an online direct debit calculator to estimate what they should be paying based on usage.
