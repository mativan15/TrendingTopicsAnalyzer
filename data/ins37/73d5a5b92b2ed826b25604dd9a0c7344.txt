Somerset health and social care contract not extended
bbc.com
news
2022-05-30 10:30:37

Image caption, The care service provides district nurses, health visitors and social workers to some of the most vulnerable and houseboundA contract providing 51 health and social care services for residents in Bath and North East Somerset will not be extended, it has been announced.The contract, awarded to Virgin Care Services in April 2017, is due to end on 31 March 2024.The governing body of the Clinical Commissioning Group had recommended an extension of the contract.The decision came as Virgin Care Services was sold to a private investor; T20 Pioneer Holdings Limited.
