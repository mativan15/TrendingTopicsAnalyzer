New Featurespace services will significantly enhance GPS's existing real-time fraud prevention capabilities to reduce fraud throughout the payments journey

LONDON, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Global Processing Services ("GPS"), a leading global payments technology platform, today announced it is partnering with Featurespace, the world's leading provider of adaptive behavioural analytics for fraud detection and risk management, to create a market leading issuer processing fraud mitigation solution.
