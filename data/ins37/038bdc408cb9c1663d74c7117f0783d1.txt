PS5 Sales Hit 20 Million, Console Production to Increase
cnet.com
news
2022-06-03 16:51:00

The PS5 has sold 20 millions units worldwide since its November 2020 launch, the company confirmed to CNET via email on Friday. Sony's next-gen PlayStation gaming console hit the milestone despite a shortage of stock due to the pandemic and global supply chain issues, as previously reported by GamesIndustry.biz.It's currently tracking behind the PS4, but Sony expects the PS5 to ultimately outpace its predecessor. It comes less than a year after sales hit 10 million sales worldwide, a milestone the PS5 reached a month faster than the PS4.
