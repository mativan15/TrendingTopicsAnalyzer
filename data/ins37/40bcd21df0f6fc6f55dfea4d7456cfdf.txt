Hyundai, Kia incur $2bn in quality costs (OTCMKTS:HYMLF)

2022-10-18 10:40:02

z1b/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Hyundai executives (OTCPK:HYMLF) indicated that both the Hyundai and KIA brands will set provisions equivalent to about $2.04B related to recalls. During a presentation on the subject of warranties and recalls on Tuesday, the Korean automaker said that its Hyundai and KIA brands will set aside provisions of 1.36 trillion KRW and 1.54 trillion KRW, respectively related to quality costs. 'We sincerely apologize for repeated quality issues and additional costs related to the Theta II GDI engine recall,' Seog-ju Cha, Head of Quality at Hyundai told analysts.
