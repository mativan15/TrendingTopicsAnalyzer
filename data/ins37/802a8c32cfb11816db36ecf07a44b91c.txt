HC has directed SSP, Mohali, to look into the threat perception to Ajay Pal Singh Middukhera, brother of Vicky Middukhera, who was shot dead in broad daylight in August 2021 in Mohali
Published on Jun 01, 2022 02:18 AM IST
 

The Punjab and Haryana High Court has directed the senior superintendent of police (SSP), Mohali, to look into the threat perception to Ajay Pal Singh Middukhera, brother of Youth Akali Dal leader Vikramjit Singh, alias Vicky Middukhera, who was shot dead in broad daylight in August 2021 in Mohali.
