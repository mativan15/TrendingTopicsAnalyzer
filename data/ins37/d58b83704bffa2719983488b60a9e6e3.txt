'Municipalities are at the forefront of meeting the challenges facing our societies', says President of the Chamber of Local Authorities, Bernd Vöhringer
coe.int
news
2022-10-17 08:39:00

'Municipalities – both urban and rural are at the forefront of meeting the challenges facing our societies over the coming years', stated President of the Chamber of Local Authorities of the Congress, Bernd Vöhringer (Germany, EPP/CCE) during the Round table discussion on 'Current Challenges for Urban and Rural Municipalities: The COVID-19 Pandemic and Beyond', organised by LoGov – Local Government and the Changing Urban-Rural Interplay on 13-14 October 2022 in Munich (Germany). President Vöhringer presented the longstanding commitment by Congress to promote and support sustainable urban development through the European Urban Charter II – Manifesto for a new urbanity adopted in 2008.
