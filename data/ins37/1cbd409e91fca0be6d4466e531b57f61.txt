Variety - TV News 'Band of Brothers' writer Bruce McKenna is teaming with David Broyles ('Six') and Nick Jones Jr. ('Yasuke') to write and produce a limited TV series 'Buffalo Rangers' about the 2nd Ranger Company, the only all-Black special operations combat unit in U.S. history. They were notable for their efforts in the Korean War.The series is being produced by South Korea-based Moving Pictures Company, which has a growing English-language slate. No broadcaster or streamer has been announced.
