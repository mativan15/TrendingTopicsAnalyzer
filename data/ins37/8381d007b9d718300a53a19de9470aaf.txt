Sheryl Sandberg told Fortune that she will not take up CEO roles elsewhere after leaving Meta.

She said her role as Meta's COO took up a lot of time, so she plans to focus on philanthropy next.

She has been a proponent of advancing women's rights in the workplace.

Sheryl Sandberg said that after leaving Meta, she'll focus on philanthropy and women's rights issues amid concerns that women may soon lose their constitutional right to abortion, according to Fortune.
