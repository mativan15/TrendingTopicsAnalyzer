Considering the sharp increase in fuel prices over the past year, many consumers may well have switched to electric vehicles.But new EVs remain expensive relative to the array of petrol-fuelled options in the market.To encourage the uptake of electric vehicles, the Albanese government is pushing ahead with its pre-election promise and has introduced legislation to exempt certain EVs from import tariffs and the fringe benefits tax, a move that would reduce their retail price and, theoretically, encourage more people to include them in salary packaging arrangements.
