Man fights back after being attacked by kangaroo

A man fought back after being attacked by a kangaroo in Australia, footage shows.The footage, which was captured on CCTV and posted to YouTube channel This is Australia, shows the moment Cliff Des tackles the kangaroo to the ground in a New South Wales neighborhood.
A stock photo shows a kangaroo. A video that shows a man being attacked by one in Australia went viral. 
binbeter/Getty Images
Des can be seen frantically running away from a huge kangaroo, which shortly appears behind him.Des then falls over, and gets back up, only for the kangaroo to charge at him.
