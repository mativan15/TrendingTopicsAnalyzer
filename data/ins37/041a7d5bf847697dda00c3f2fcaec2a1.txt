Our Zoom was booked for midnight Moscow time. It's always Moscow time, even if many Russian journalists and activists like Perm native Sergei Ukhov have left the country and are in different time zones. Ukhov was on the road and wouldn't be in a place with internet before 12, so we agreed on that time.Having followed him on Telegram, I was eager to find out more about what the vibe is like in a region far from Moscow. Perm is the gateway to Siberia, a city of about a million in the Ural Mountains – and it's a significant part of the military-industrial complex of Russia.
