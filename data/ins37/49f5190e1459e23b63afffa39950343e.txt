Famed eateries stepping out of bases to go cross-country
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-16 19:45:00

For nearly three decades, Gola Sizzlers didn't venture beyond the National Capital Region. In fact, it clinged on to New Delhi's Connaught Place for the first two decades where it opened its first outlet way back in 1992. Now the owners, part of the third generation, want to serve the hot metal plate dish across India, hoping to piggyback on growing mall culture in the country. Kapoor is just one of a clutch of entrepreneurs who have seen their fathers and grandfathers start a restaurant business, done that too - but are now doing it all over again on a larger scale.
