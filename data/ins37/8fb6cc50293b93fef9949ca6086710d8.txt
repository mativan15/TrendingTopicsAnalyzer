SHENZHEN, China, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The SZSE ChiNext Daily (2x) Leveraged Product recently went public on the HKEX. It is the first 2X EFT listed outside the Chinese mainland that tracks the innovation-oriented enterprises in China A-shares. The product tracks the Hong Kong dollar-denominated index of the ChiNext Index and realizes a return which two times the daily performance of the index through swap investment instruments. Since its listing, the product has been stable and its product units have increased by 83%.
