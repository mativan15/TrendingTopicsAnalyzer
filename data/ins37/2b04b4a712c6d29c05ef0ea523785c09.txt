A military chopper on Friday crashed near Migging village in West Siang district in Arunachal Pradesh claiming the lives of five people. Four out five bodies have been recovered. The place is located 25 kms away from the Tuting headquarters in the Upper Siang district. There were five people on board, four bodies have been recovered from the crash site. The incident took place at 10.43 am on Friday. Arunachal chief minister, Pema Khandu tweeted, 'Shocked to hear about the news of @adgpi Advanced Light Combat Helicopter crash near Tuting area in Upper Siang district of Arunachal Pradesh.
