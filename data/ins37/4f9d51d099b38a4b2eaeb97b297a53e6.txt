Ludhiana: 'We will protest if MLU areas not declared industrial'
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 04:30:00

LUDHIANA: Industrialists having factories in mix land use (MLU) area of Janta Nagar have objected to masterplan 2023. In a meeting conducted at the office of Janta Nagar Small Scale Manufacturers' Association (JSMA), businessmen have demanded that the MLU areas should be declared industrial in the new masterplan.Jaswinder Singh Thukral , president of JSMA, said, "The masterplan 2023 is like a sword hanging on the neck of industrialists having units in MLU areas. Under the provisions of the masterplan, after September 2023 no factory will be allowed to function in any MLU area of Ludhiana.
