SMITHTOWN, N.Y., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- John J. Rush, MD, MBA, MHA, is being recognized by Continental Who's Who as a Top Healthcare Professional in the Medical field and in acknowledgment of his work with Arthritis Knee Pain Centers.
