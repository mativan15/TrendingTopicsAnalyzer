Laurie Foon will be Wellington's deputy mayor
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-21 04:00:00

It will be an all-Green, all-women team leading the Wellington City Council, with Laurie Foon selected as the new deputy mayor. Foon is well known as founder of popular fashion label Starfish in the early 1990s and 'probably the nicest person on council'. She was first elected to council in the Paekawakawa/Southern ward in 2019. She said she was 'incredibly honoured to bring (her) positive mahi to the city' as deputy. 'I see a big part of my role as helping create a unified council to make good decisions on behalf of all Wellingtonians.
