By PA Media Published: 04:46 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 04:46 EDT, 30 May 2022 The takeover of Jus-Rol by a rival pastry manufacturer could lead to higher prices and lower quality, the competition watchdog has warned. The Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) has raised concerns over a potential decrease in competition due to Cerelia's acquisition of Jus-Rol from US food giant General Mills. Cerelia, the largest producer of bake-at-home products in the UK, agreed the deal last year for an undisclosed amount.
