~ TIMIA to pay the cash dividend payment to shareholders of Series A Preferred Shares of record on June 23rd ~

VANCOUVER, BC, June 3, 2022 /CNW/ - TIMIA Capital Corporation ("TIMIA" or the "Company") (TSXV: TCA) (OTCQB: TIMCF), a leading innovator of technology in private credit, today announced that the Company's board of directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.02 per Series A Preferred Shares ("Preferred Shares"), payable on June 30, 2022, to Series A preferred shareholders of record as at June 23, 2022.
