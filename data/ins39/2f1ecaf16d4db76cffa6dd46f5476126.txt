Wimbledon scrap ‘Miss' and ‘Mrs' titles in end of 138-year tradition
Copyright 2022, ESI Media Ltd
news
2022-05-27 08:45:52

Wimbledon is to scrap the use of the titles 'Miss' and 'Mrs' for female champions on its honours boards ahead of this summer's Championships.Martial honourifics have traditionally been placed ahead of the initial and last name of female winners, despite umpires ceasing to use the prefixes when announcing the score during matches back in 2019.The practice, which has been in place since the first tournament in 1884, is not employed for male winners, however, with last year's men's singles victor, Novak Djokovic, displayed on the board as 'N.
