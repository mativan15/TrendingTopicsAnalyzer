PHILADELPHIA (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 Spadea Lignana has brought on five new members to their team of franchise attorneys. The law firm, which has offices in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and Melville, New York and focusing on emerging franchise brands, is thrilled to have the newly promoted partners. These individuals include Stephanie Grobler, Will Jameson, Matt Wizmur, Andrew Matson and Cameron McKinley. 'Our success and growth over the last decade is a direct result of the tremendous team that helps clients win in franchising year after year.
