Football fans still have a chance to see Lionel Messi and his teammates in Abu Dhabi thanks to an open training session three days before the match on November 16. The open training at Al Nahyan Stadium on November 13 has tickets available for sale now. Residents of the UAE and visitors to the nation can view the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 preparations of Messi, the seven-time winner of the Ballon d'Or, and his legendary colleagues, including Angel Di Maria of Juventus, Lautaro Martinez of Inter Milan, and Julian Alvarez of Manchester City.
