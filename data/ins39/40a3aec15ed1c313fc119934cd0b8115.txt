Navi Mumbai: PAPs oppose Garjepoti scheme; 650 applied for regularisation
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 09:30:00

NAVI MUMBAI: Poor response from project affected persons (PAP) for regularization of their constructed houses on need basis (Garjepoti) on encroached land have forced the Cidco to extend the application deadline.About 650 project affected persons (PAP) from 95 villages across Navi Mumbai have applied to Cidco for regularization. The Garjepoti scheme was announced on February 25, 2022. PAPs are opposing the regularization fees and the government not allowing gaonthan extension.The Garjepoti scheme covers PAPs of 95 villages whose land was acquired by Cidco in 1970 to develop Navi Mumbai.
