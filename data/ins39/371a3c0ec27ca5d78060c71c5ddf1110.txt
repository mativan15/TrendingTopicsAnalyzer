Rio Tinto Seeks Pitches From Bankers for Lithium Deals in Battery Metal Foray
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-21 17:13:48

(Bloomberg) -- Rio Tinto Group is asking for pitches from some of the biggest investment banks for lithium companies and projects it could buy as the mining giant looks to expand into the key battery metal.Representatives from leading investment banks visited the miner's offices in Perth, Australia several weeks ago, according to people familiar with the situation who declined to be identified as the meetings were private.Rio Tinto is the only one of the world's biggest diversified miners looking to start mining lithium, a key ingredient to make batteries for electric vehicles.
