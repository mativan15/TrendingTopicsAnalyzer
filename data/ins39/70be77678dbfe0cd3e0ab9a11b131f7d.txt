ELK CITY, Okla., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Dr. Brian Blick scholarship for future medical doctors provides funding for students who are studying on the road to becoming medical doctors. The scholarship is open to all university and college students located in the United States studying to become medical doctors. The scholarship is also open for high school students who will attend a university with plans to pursue a course in medicine. The scholarship will award the chosen individual $1,000.
