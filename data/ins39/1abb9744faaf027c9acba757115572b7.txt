Naga Munchetty weighs in on BBC staffing exodus
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 03:00:00

Throughout the past year, many people have witnessed a roster of presenters leave the BBC. The recent exodus has seen a number of well-known names venture elsewhere to do a series of different projects from podcasts to radio shows. BBC's Naga Munchetty has touched on the tumultuous time that the company is currently facing.Currently, the BBC is facing an extraordinary exodus with many high-profile names deciding to leave the broadcaster to work on ventures elsewhere.With presenters from both TV and radio joining places such as Channel 5, TalkTV and GB News, it's a worrying time for the BBC with the decrease of hosts leaving.
