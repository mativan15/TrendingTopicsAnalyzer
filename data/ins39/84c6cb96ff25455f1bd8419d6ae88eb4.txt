TUC leader warns government over changes to strike laws

2022-10-18 14:58:30

Frances O' Grady said legal advice found plans to change thresholds over strike action breached international law. In her final speech as Trades Union Congress (TUC) leader, she said the government bid was a "cynical move to distract" from the chaos it has caused.
