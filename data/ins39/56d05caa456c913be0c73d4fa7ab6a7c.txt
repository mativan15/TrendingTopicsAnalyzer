"The pandemic is a reminder of the intimate and delicate relationship between people and the planet. Any efforts to make our world safer are doomed to fail unless they address the critical interface between people and pathogens, and the existential threat of climate change, that is making our Earth less habitable."WHO Director-General Dr Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus. Address to the 73rd World Health Assembly. May 18th, 2020.The side event "Together for a healthy, resilient, and green recovery" will be organised by IFMSA, Karolinska Institutet, UNDP, UNFCCC, UN OCHCR and WHO at the margin 
Stockholm+50 international meeting (June 2-3, Stockholm, Sweden).
