Coast to Coast AM with George NooryOct 20, 2022A research group has filed a lawsuit against the federal government in the hopes of forcing the release of a slew of files related to the JFK assassination which have been maddeningly kept secret for decades. Spearheading the legal action, which was reportedly launched on Wednesday, is an organization known as the Mary Ferrell Foundation, which serves as a massive online clearinghouse for materials related to "the assassinations of the 1960s, the Watergate scandal, and post-Watergate intelligence abuse investigations.
