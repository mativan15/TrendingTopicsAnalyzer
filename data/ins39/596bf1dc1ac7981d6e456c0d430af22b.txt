YOUR iPhone can do a magic trick that will wow your friends.
It lets you control the handset without even touching it – just by using your voice.
Your iPhone is packed with clever hidden featuresCredit: TikTok / @letsdodiz / Apple
We're not talking about Siri here, but a little-known Apple feature called Voice Control.
It's tucked away in the Accessibility settings inside your iPhone, and is intended to make the gadget easier to use.
But with the right settings, you can perform a stunning iPhone magic trick that's actually useful: scrolling with your voice.
