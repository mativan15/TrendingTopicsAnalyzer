Tech and fintech industries saw more job cuts in May than rest of year put together: Challenger
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 11:30:00

The technology and fintech industries saw more job cuts in May than in the entire rest of the year put together, according to outplacement firm Challenger Gray & Christmas. There were 4,044 job cuts announced in May for technology, compared to 459 in January through April, and there were 1,619 job cuts announced for fintech, compared to 440 in January through April.
