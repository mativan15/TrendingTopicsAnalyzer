(JFL) on Monday reported an 8.8 per cent decline in its consolidated net profit at Rs 96 crore in the fourth quarter ended March 2022, impacted by higher expenses. The company had posted a consolidated net profit of Rs 105.3 crore in the same period previous fiscal, Jubilant Foodworks Ltd (JFL) said in a regulatory filing. Consolidated revenue from operations during the quarter under review stood at Rs 1,175.
