Singer Neha Kakkar turns 34 on Monday. Once rejected from Indian Idol, Neha became one of the most popular singers in India over the last few years and even went on to judge the same show from which she had been eliminated. Apart from her hits such as Manali Trance, Garmi and Aankh Maarey, Neha also found a large fan-following for herself as a judge on Indian Idol.
