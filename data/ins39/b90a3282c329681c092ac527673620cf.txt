Dutch central bank urges pension funds to review liquidity
reuters.com
news
2022-10-19 11:50:47

AMSTERDAM, Oct 19 (Reuters) - The Dutch central bank (DNB) said on Wednesday it has called on pension funds in the Netherlands to review their readiness to weather a sudden spike in interest rates following turmoil among British funds.A spokesperson for the bank confirmed a Financial Times report that said the funds had been asked to review their holdings of liquid assets.In Britain, the central bank was forced to intervene after a surge in yields on Sept. 28 threatened to overwhelm pension schemes that had loaded up on leveraged derivatives.
