Roseanne Barr says Norm Macdonald 'will redefine death and dying' in 'Nothing Special'
latimes.com
entertainment
2022-05-31 20:48:33

Comedian, actor, writer and legend Norm Macdonald may have left the planet last September, but he left more behind than just his greatest hits. In a 'that's soooo Norm' move, he also gifted us with something so needed, so wanted and at such a perfect time — 'Norm Macdonald: Nothing Special.' Macdonald's special, which premiered Monday on Netflix, was filmed in his living room. Loved and respected by fans and colleagues worldwide, Macdonald formed a unique bond with fellow comedy legend Roseanne Barr back in the early ‘90s when she tapped him to write on her hit show, 'Roseanne.
