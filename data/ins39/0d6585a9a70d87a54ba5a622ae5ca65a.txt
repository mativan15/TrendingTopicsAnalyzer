Elon Musk's attempts at marshalling world peace: Fumbles or gambles?
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 11:06:47

'I prefer to stay out of politics.' That is what Elon Musk said last month in response to Texas state governor Greg Abbott's claim that the Tesla CEO supported the state's anti-abortion laws. Whether the availability of medical care and the option to terminate a pregnancy can be classified as politics, is a separate debate altogether. Yet, Musk has for long, toyed with opinions on topics that would sit quite easily within the realm of politics. Perhaps he should have taken his own tweets seriously.
