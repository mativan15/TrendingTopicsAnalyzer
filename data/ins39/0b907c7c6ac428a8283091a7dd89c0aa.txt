iD Fresh Food opens fourth manufacturing unit with investment of Rs 20 cr
indiatimes.com
food
2022-10-17 11:00:00

PC Musthafa, founder and CEO, iD FreshNew Delhi: Ready-to-cook food brand, iD Fresh Food has launched its fourth manufacturing facility in the country with an investment of Rs 20 crore, the company said in a press release on Monday. Spread across an area of 15,000 square feet, the new production facility is situated at Hind Terminal in Palwal, Haryana. With this new unit, the company aims to cater to the growing customer demand from North India, particularly in the Delhi-NCR region. Additionally, the manufacturing facility in Haryana will help the retailer cater to consumer demand from tier 2 markets, according to the release.
