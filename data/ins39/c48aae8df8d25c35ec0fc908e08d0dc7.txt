Queen Consort Camilla at Ascot in black-and-white outfit
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 16:34:00

Camilla, the Queen Consort, has wowed royal fans with her outfit at Ascot on Saturday. She attended British Champions Day at the Berkshire racetrack in a black-and-white midi dress.Photos from Ascot showed the Queen Consort wearing an A-Line garment featuring a V-neck cut and white bodice.She paired the outfit with a linen-blend matching hat, opaque tights and black suede heels.Camilla also wore a four-stand pearl choker with a round diamond clasp, as she attended the finale of the flat.The Queen Consort was also pictured in the parade ring at Ascot ahead of the Queen Elizabeth II Stakes.
