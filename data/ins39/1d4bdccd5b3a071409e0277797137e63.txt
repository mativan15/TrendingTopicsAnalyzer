New white paper offers insights into the transferable skills companies need to remain competitive in the future

HANOVER, Md., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Aerotek, a leading talent solutions provider specializing in placing light industrial and skilled trades talent in the manufacturing, logistics, construction, aviation and facility management industries, has released the first white paper in a three-part series focused on building a workforce that will stand the test of time.

Aerotek Logo (PRNewsfoto/Aerotek)

The first white paper in the "Workforce 2030: The Skills Companies Will Need To Succeed" series highlights the soft transferable skills companies will need to maintain a versatile and competitive workforce in the future.
