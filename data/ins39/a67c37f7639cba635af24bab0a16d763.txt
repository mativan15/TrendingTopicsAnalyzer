THE TLC reality show 90 Day Fiancé gives viewers a look at long-distance relationships that can extend halfway around the world.
Season 9 premiered in April 2022 and already has fans begging for one couple to call it quits. 
Bilal Hazziez is a Real Estate Agent from MissouriCredit: instagram
Who is Bilal Hazziez?
Bilal Hazziez is known as an entrepreneur who first rose to fame on Season 9 of 90 Day Fiancé alongside Shaeeda Sween.
Outside of the show, he is known for his career as a Real Estate Agent with Compass and as a Firefighter in Kansas City, Missouri.
