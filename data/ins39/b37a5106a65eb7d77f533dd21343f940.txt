Prosecutors raid SBW Group over cash smuggling suspicions
naver.com
news
2022-10-17 17:44:00

A photo of a sign over an SBW Group office in Yongsan District, central Seoul, taken in April. [NEWS1] Prosecutors on Monday raided the headquarters of minor conglomerate SBW Group following evidence that the company's employees were involved in smuggling stacks of U.S. dollars into China, possibly with the final destination of North Korea. Investigators from the Suwon District Prosecutors' Office seized documents and other materials from SBW's headquarters in Jung District, central Seoul, as well as from SBW affiliates Kwanglim and SBW Life Sciences.
