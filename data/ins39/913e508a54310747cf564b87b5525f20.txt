ANIUnion Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman with Vivek Johri, Chairman, Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs and Revenue Secretary Tarun BajajIndia's finance ministry is reportedly working with the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC) to integrate Customs systems to ensure faster clearance. CBIC Chairman Vivek Johri said the department is working on web registration of exports, which would reduce the average release time of consignments/cargoes from ports and airports.
