Yahoo and DIRECTV Advertising Expand Advanced TV Partnership to Include Set-Top Box Data

Partnership bridges the gap between linear and digital campaigns to unlock new value for buyers

NEW YORK, October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Yahoo today announced an expansion of its partnership with DIRECTV Advertising to include set-top box (STB) data, backed by market-leading privacy protection protocols. With the expanded partnership, Yahoo's demand-side platform (DSP) customers can now activate TV usage data from millions of DIRECTV households to optimize planning, activation and measurement of digital campaigns across DIRECTV and non-DIRECTV inventory.
