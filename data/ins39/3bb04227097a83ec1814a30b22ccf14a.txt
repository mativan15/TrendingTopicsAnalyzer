Overcoming Hate in America
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 12:00:01

Hate toward others—be it motivated by politics, race, gender, or sexual orientation—is plaguing and dividing America. President Joe Biden held a summit recently at the White House to unite Americans into action against hate-based violence and white supremacy, and seek solutions.Hate crimes, and violence in general, are on the rise, mostly targeting minorities and women. There's been an unprecedented surge in firearm purchasing, and over 450 mass shootings in 2022 so far. Homicides increased by 27.
