Justin Sullivan/Getty Images News The bar has been set low for nearly all social media companies coming into earnings season because advertising is hit first in challenging economies. Look no further than the steep cuts to earnings (both company guidance and analysts' models) for Snap (NYSE:SNAP) over the last six months, and it's clear the social media industry is struggling for growth. Earnings have only added to the noise, but the investor who can shut out the noise and listen for the real wavelengths will come out on top in the long term.
