Weekly Episodes Drop Every Thursday

NEW YORK, NY – October 19, 2022 – CNN Audio announced today that CNN Anchor and Correspondent Audie Cornish's new podcast, The Assignment with Audie Cornish, will premiere Thursday, November 17.

In this candid weekly show, Cornish will convene conversations with everyday people at the center of debates that are rocking the culture with topics spanning the arts, education, politics, business, and sports – people living behind the headlines in the very heart of the zeitgeist.
