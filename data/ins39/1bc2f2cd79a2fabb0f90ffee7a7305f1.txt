Business leaders of Korea and Japan raise expectations for improved bilateral ties
naver.com
news
2022-05-31 14:14:00

[Photo by Kim Ho-young] South Korean and Japan businessmen turned more hopeful about improvement in bilateral ties under a new conservative government in Korea after stalemated relationship over the last few years due to diplomatic spat over past issues, trade barriers, and pandemic restrictions.Around 260 business leaders of South Korea and Japan vowed economic cooperation for co-prosperity in the 54th South Korea-Japan Business Conference on Monday, hosted by the Korea-Japan Economic Association (KJEA).
