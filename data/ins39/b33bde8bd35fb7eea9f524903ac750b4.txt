Nora Fatehi denied permission to perform in Bangladesh due to foreign currency crunch
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-18 03:28:48

Here's shocking news for the Bollywood fans in Dhaka. The Bangladesh government has decided against letting dancer-actor Nora Fatehi perform in an event reportedly on November 18. The Bangladesh government took this step to avoid further stress on the ongoing foreign currency crunch. According to a notice issued by Bangladesh's ministry of cultural affairs on Monday Nora wasn't granted the permission, 'in view of the global situation and with the aim of maintaining the foreign exchange reserves'.
