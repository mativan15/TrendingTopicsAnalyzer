Billionaire Mike Cannon-Brookes has declared he has big ideas for the future of AGL, as analysts suggest he may seek to steer the energy giant towards aggregating solar power generated on millions of Australian homes following his successful campaign to block its demerger.AGL, the nation's largest carbon emitter, was plunged into crisis this week after a shareholder push, led Cannon-Brookes, scuttled the proposed break-up of its retail and power generation businesses. The company has now launched a 'strategic review' of its future, following the resignations of half of its board of directors.
