BEIJING and SHANGHAI and BOSTON, June 5, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Jacobio Pharma (1167.HK) announced the phase I clinical data of KRAS G12C inhibitor JAB-21822 at the 2022 annual meeting of American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO).

logo (PRNewsfoto/Jacobio)

As of April 1, 2022, the Phase I clinical data of non-small cell lung cancer patients with KRAS G12C mutation shows that the overall response rate (ORR) is 56.3% (18/32) and the disease control rate (DCR) is 90.6% (29/32). In 400mg QD and 800mg QD cohorts, the ORR is 66.
