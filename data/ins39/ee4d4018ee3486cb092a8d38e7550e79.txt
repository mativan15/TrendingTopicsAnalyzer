Shock as sonography student reveals "surprise" baby during lab class
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 16:37:28

A video of a "surprise" pregnancy reveal during a sonography class has gone viral on TikTok, receiving over 36 million views.The video, shared by TikTok user @samantha2074, was overlaid with a message that read: "When you're in lab and you find a surprise." It showed a classroom setting, with a few women lying beside ultrasound machines.The clip later turned towards the machine closest to the camera, which showed live footage of what looked like a fetus on the screen. The video then panned to others in the classroom who appeared shocked by what they saw.
