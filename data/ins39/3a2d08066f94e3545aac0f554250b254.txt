Royal Enfield Pegasus Black StallionThe 650 twins from Royal Enfield's stable came pretty much as a revolution that changed the look, feel, performance, and perspective of what Royal Enfield motorcycles had so far been. The 650cc parallel-twin has also built itself a substantial fondness amongst custom bike builders. Parisian custom shop Bad Winners' take on the Continental GT 650 has resulted in a beast of a drag machine. And yes, it uses a NOS tank on top of a bored-out engine.The custom job was commissioned by Royal Enfield Europe in 2019 for the popular Sultans of Sprint race series.
