LOS ANGELES, CA / ACCESSWIRE / May 27, 2022 / Business owners are experiencing challenges amidst the Covid-19 pandemic, with many businesses facing bankruptcy and even closure. Businesses that continue to thrive throughout this period of uncertainty are a living testament of their determination to continue serving their customers at the highest level, giving them a window of opportunity to stand out above the crowd.

The Australian Exhibitions Group is renowned for pioneering the Australian tattoo expos and the Rites of Passage tattoo expo.
