NITK inks an MoU for EV tech research

energy
2022-06-06 01:56:55

Mangaluru: NITK Surathkal and SEG Automotive India Pvt Ltd, have signed an MoU that aims at research collaboration in electric vehicle technology. The MoU initiated by the impact group 1979, an alumni group, by K Rammourthy and Ajith, was signed recently. Some of the research topics include motor controller development; switched reluctance motor (SRM) design development; system integration in terms of motor, controller, internet of things (IoT) and mobile app design for e-rickshaw to be developed by SEG.
