October 17, 2022 11:43 AM Eastern Daylight Time FRANKLIN, Ind.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--(OTCPINK: TDCB) - Third Century Bancorp ('Company'), the holding company for Mutual Savings Bank ('Bank'), announced it recorded net income of $639,000 for the quarter ended September 30, 2022, or $0.55 per basic and diluted share, compared to net income of $784,000 for the quarter ended September 30, 2021, or $0.66 per basic and diluted share.

David A. Coffey, President and CEO, commented, 'In these challenging economic times, we are pleased to report another solid quarter of performance for our Company.
