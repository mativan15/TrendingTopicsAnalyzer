PTIBihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar with RJD leader Tejashwi YadavBihar will conduct a caste-based census soon, chief minister Nitish Kumar has said. The state Cabinet will meet to approve the decision and draw the contours of the census, Kumar said after an all-party meeting in Patna on Wednesday. There was unanimity on the census at the meeting attended by representatives of BJP, JDU, RJD, Congress, CPIML, CPIM, CPI, AIMIM and HAM. "The all party meeting decided unanimously to conduct a caste-based census in Bihar.
