I'm a battle-hardened retro gamer through and through, so the news of Sony including updated PS1 classics for its upcoming PlayStation Plus Premium tier — some with confirmed trophy support — had me itching to subscribe to the revamped service. However, rumors recently began circulating that, just like with its poorly received PlayStation Classic mini console, the company was again opting to include inferior 50Hz PAL versions of its first-party titles.
