PTIDelhi Health Minister Satyendar Jain.A Delhi court on Tuesday sent Delhi Health Minister Satyendra Jain to Enforcement Directorate's custody till June 9, in connection with a money laundering case. Jain was produced before Special CBI Court Judge Geetanjali Goel in the ED's case, in which he was arrested on Monday evening. The ED had attached the properties of Jain's relatives' worth Rs 4.81 crore in April this year. "Immovable properties worth Rs 4.81 crore belonging to different firms owned by Swati Jain, Sushila Jain, and Indu Jain, the relatives of Jain were attached.
