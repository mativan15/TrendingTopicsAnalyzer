This week's realty hot spot is T Nagar-Nungambakkam in Chenna. It is an established residential and retail destination near central Chennai. It enjoys good connectivity via Arcot Road, Mount Road, GST Road and Blue Line of Chennai Metro. T Nagar-Nungambakkam majorly hosts low to mid-rise apartment projects by developers such as VGN Projects, BBCL and Lifestyle Housing.
