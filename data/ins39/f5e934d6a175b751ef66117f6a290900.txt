On Executive Council, Katherine Harake will support children, families, seniors

Oct. 14 — To the Editor:

The "noise" from various campaigns may seem deafening. But, we cannot let the partisan harangues deflect from what is at stake!

Did you know that New Hampshire's five-person Executive Council largely determines what federal funds reach our communities — including our schools and healthcare agencies? Currently the councilor elected from our region has persistently voted against directing federal dollars to family planning clinics across the Granite State.
