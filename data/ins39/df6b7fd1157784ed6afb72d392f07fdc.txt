Central team visits dengue hot spots in Nalanda
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 15:39:38

PATNA A four-member Central team on Thursday interacted with a few dengue patients and visited a couple of hot spots of the mosquito-borne viral disease in Biharsharif and Rajgir subdivisions of Nalanda district in its endeavour to assist the state government by providing technical guidance, including public health measures, for managing the ongoing upsurge of dengue, said senior health officials.'The team will also visit hospitals and hotspots in Patna, which have been divided into four zones, including areas like Punaichak, Bajrangpuri and Biscomaun Colony,' said Dr Ranjeet Kumar, state surveillance officer.
