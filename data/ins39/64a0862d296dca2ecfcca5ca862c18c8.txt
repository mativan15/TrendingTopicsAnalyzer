Georgia football offers 4-star OL Jordan Seaton
usatoday.com
news
2022-06-01 15:43:33

Jordan Seaton is one of the best interior offensive line recruits in the class of 2024. The rising junior has now has received a scholarship offer from the University of Georgia.
Seaton, a four-star recruit, plays high school football for St. John's in Washington, D.C. and has over 30 scholarship offers. The 6-foot-6, 300-pound offensive line recruit is ranked as the No. 46 recruit in the class of 2024.
The St. John's star is the third-ranked interior offensive line prospect in his class and the top-ranked recruit in Washington, D.
