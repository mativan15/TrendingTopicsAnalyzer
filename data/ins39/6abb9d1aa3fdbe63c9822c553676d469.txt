Indiewire As adults, Tom Felton and Emma Watson are close friends, but the 'Harry Potter' alum revealed it 'did not start well' on set.Felton was 12 years old when he was cast as Draco Malfoy in the franchise, and admitted that his relationship with then 9-year-old Watson at first involved mocking her. As Felton wrote in his memoir 'Beyond the Wand: The Magic and Mayhem of Growing Up a Wizard,' via Insider, Watson 'put together a little dance show' in her dressing room for the cast, while Felton and co-star Josh Herdman teased her.
