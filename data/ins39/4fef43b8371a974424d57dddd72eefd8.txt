Published: 11:27 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 11:51 EDT, 17 October 2022 Beauty junkies have slammed the long awaited UK launch of Sephora today - with many complaining they felt 'underwhelmed' by the brand's website. It was announced earlier this month that the would launch a newly named Sephora.co.uk from today - with a physical store in the works for next year - following its acquisition of the Feelunique website last year.Sephora had promised a 'fully immersive website' which is twinned with its app, and offer brands exclusive to its UK offering, including Tarte Cosmetics, ILIA, Makeup by Mario, ONE/SIZE by Patrick Starrr, GXVE by Gwen Stefani, Skinfix, Vegamour, and Sephora's own Sephora Collection.
