AgenciesRepresentative ImageUnable to lead the life she wanted, a 38-year-old adopted daughter allegedly hacked her mother to death in Bengaluru. The shocking incident came to light on Sunday morning, when police found a 75-year-old woman murdered inside her residence in Sarjapur. The victim is Muniyamma, a resident of Kote Street in Sarjapur and the accused is Chandramma, who has confessed to the crime, as reported by TOI. 
The dead body was first found by Muniyamma's son Srinivas, who came to her residence to take their pet for a morning walk and left shocked.
