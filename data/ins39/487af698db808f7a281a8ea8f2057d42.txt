Apple erases gains as it reportedly cuts iPhone 14 Plus production shortly after debut
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-18 18:55:37

Lobro78 Apple (NASDAQ:AAPL) shares erased earlier gains and turned slightly negative on Tuesday after The Information reported that the tech giant had cut production of its iPhone 14 Plus model less than two weeks after it debuted. The news outlet, citing two people in Apple's (AAPL) supply chain, reported that Apple (AAPL) was reevaluating demand for the product and told a Chinese manufacturer to "immediately halt production" of iPhone 14 plus components. Two Apple (AAPL) suppliers cut their production on the iPhone 14 Plus by 70% and 90% respectively, the person added.
