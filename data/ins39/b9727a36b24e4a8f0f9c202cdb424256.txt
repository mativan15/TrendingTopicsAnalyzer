Manufacturing and Retail Activity Fall Sharply as Global Trade Slowdown Extends to a Third Consecutive Quarter

October 19, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Data from Tradeshift, the global network for digital trade, reveals that trade activity across retail, manufacturing and transport & logistics dropped sharply in Q3, as the volume of transactions between buyers and suppliers globally slowed for the third consecutive quarter.



According to Tradeshift's Index of Global Trade Health, global supply chain activity fell by a further 5 points in Q3 compared to the baseline.
