Image source, ReutersA protester says he was not trying to enter Manchester's Chinese consulate during a pro-democracy demonstration that saw violent scenes on Sunday.Bob Chen told a news conference he was dragged onto the consulate grounds and beaten by men, leaving him with injuries requiring hospital treatment.It comes a day after a British MP accused one of China's most senior UK diplomats of being involved.Amid the growing row, China has claimed there were attempts at illegal entry.Speaking at a news conference organised by several British MPs, Mr Chen, a Hongkonger, said he was left physically and mentally hurt by Sunday's incident.
