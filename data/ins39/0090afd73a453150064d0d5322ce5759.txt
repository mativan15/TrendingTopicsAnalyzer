SVB Financial Releases 3Q Results, Shares Fall 10% After Hours
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 21:14:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of SVB Financial Group were trading lower in Thursday's after-hours session following the release of third-quarter financial results. At 4:58 p.m. EDT, the company's shares had fallen 10.73% to trade at $270 per share. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 4.09% loss at $302.46 per share. Earlier Thursday, the stock touched a 52-week low of $301.88 per share. After the bell, the company reported third-quarter earnings per share of $7.21. In the year-ago period, the company reported EPS of $6.
