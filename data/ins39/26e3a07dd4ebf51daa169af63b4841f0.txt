Construction Is Underway on New Test Complex at NASA's Stennis Space Center to Support Aeon R Engine Testing and Terran R Stage Testing

BAY ST. LOUIS, Miss., October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Relativity Space, the first company to 3D print rockets and build the largest metal 3D printers in the world, today detailed its plans to operate one of the largest rocket engine test facilities in the United States. Through an agreement with NASA, Relativity is significantly expanding its facilities and infrastructure at NASA's historic Stennis Space Center in Hancock County, Mississippi.
