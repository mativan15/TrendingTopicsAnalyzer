NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ --  WHY: New York, N.Y., October 17, 2022. Rosen Law Firm, a global investor rights law firm, reminds purchasers of the stock of Medtronic plc (NYSE: MDT) between June 8, 2019 and May 25, 2022, both dates inclusive (the "Class Period"), of the important November 7, 2022 lead plaintiff deadline.
SO WHAT: If you purchased Medtronic securities during the Class Period you may be entitled to compensation without payment of any out of pocket fees or costs through a contingency fee arrangement.
