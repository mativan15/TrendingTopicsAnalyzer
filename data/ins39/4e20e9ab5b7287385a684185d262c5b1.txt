October 20, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time SEATTLE--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Expedia Group (NASDAQ: EXPE) will report its third quarter 2022 results for the period ended September 30, 2022, on Thursday, November 3, 2022 via an earnings release and accompanying webcast. These items will be available in the Investor Relations section of the company's corporate website at http://ir.expediagroup.com. The earnings release will post after market close and the webcast will begin at 1:30 PM Pacific Time / 4:30 PM Eastern Time.
