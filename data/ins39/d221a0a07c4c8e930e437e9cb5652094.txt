Britain's version of a parliamentary democracy, at present, looks risible. In Liz Truss, it has produced the most inane prime minister in its history.In just seven weeks the prime minister has lost all credibility. She staked her future—and the country's—on an economic plan so ill-conceived that it was virtually dead on arrival. She mindlessly repeated a mantra that she would 'deliver,' but is left with nothing to deliver. Most members of her Tory party believe she has doomed them to an election wipe-out.
