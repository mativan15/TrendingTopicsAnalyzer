"Not haunted": vintage chair for sale goes viral for hilarious advertising

A woman went viral on Twitter for sharing a photo of a vintage chair in England that was being advertised as "not haunted."The woman named Heather Cowan, or @hwardcowan, posted a photo of the "haunted" chair on Twitter where it received 1.2 million likes, 116,000 retweets, and 5,000 quote tweets since Wednesday afternoon. The tweet can be found here.The viral photo showed a vintage, wingback chair that had a sign reading "definitely not haunted!" on it."That's definitely something a haunted chair would say.
