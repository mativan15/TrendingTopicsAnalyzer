JoJo WrightBy Jessica RiveraOct 18, 20221 of 1Photo: Getty Images North AmericaIf you've been to SoFi stadium, the lines can sometimes get a little long especially closer to kickoff time at a football game. Well now it'll be a little bit easier for CLEAR members who will be able to skip the long lines with their own entry lane. 'Partnering with CLEAR is a natural fit for us,' said Jason Gannon, managing director, SoFi Stadium and Hollywood Park. 'With a state-of-the-art building and a fan-first mentality, we are always looking to grow and enhance the guest experience and with the addition of partners like CLEAR, it brings us one step closer to delivering on that goal.
