Philip Morris raises Swedish Match bid
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

The maker of Marlboro cigarettes has sweetened its offer for Swedish Match in a final effort to gain shareholders' support for a $16 billion takeover that is attracting opposition from investors.Philip Morris International struck a deal to buy the Stockholm-based company in May as it seeks to expand its smoke-free business at a time of stricter regulation of combustible tobacco and increased awareness of the health risks.Swedish Match specialises in snus, tobacco placed between the upper lip and gum, which is becoming popular outside Scandinavia.
