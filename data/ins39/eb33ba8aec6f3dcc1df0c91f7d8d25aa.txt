ETMarkets.comThe seasoned investor with nearly three decades of experience predicts that valuations will remain subdued given the uncertainty in the market. 'Whatever capex people are doing, they are doing out of their internal accruals. So the ROE will remain very high, the ROC will remain very high because when I invest my own money, I am not dependent on X, Y, Z, you will see large capex happening even in metal companies after the large capex also they are talking of virtually zero debt or very marginal debt,' says market veteran Madhusudan Kela.
