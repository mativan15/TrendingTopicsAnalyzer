October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ROCKFORD, Ill. & RONKONKOMA, N.Y.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--VAI, a leading Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software developer, and Cleo, the pioneer and global leader of the Ecosystem Integration software category, jointly announced they are expanding their longtime partnership to help mutual customers in key industries migrate B2B integration solutions to the cloud.



The joint announcement was made from VAI Connect 2022, a one-day virtual, instructive event for VAI customers in which Cleo is participating as a Solution Partner.
