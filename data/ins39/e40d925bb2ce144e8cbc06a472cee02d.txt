John Lee is President of Sinobec Trading Inc.SuppliedJohn Lee was working for Quebec-based discount retailer Dollarama 20 years ago when he came up with the business idea for Sinobec Trading Inc.Mr. Lee saw the world-beating advantages Quebec had in the mining and manufacturing of aluminum, and he started the company to export the metal in various forms.China was Sinobec's sole export market at first. Mr. Lee is a native of China, he knew the market well, and demand for metals in the country was booming.
