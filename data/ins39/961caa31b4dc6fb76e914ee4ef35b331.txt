Tiffani Vargas has been appointed as Chief Lending Officer at SAFE Credit Union. This move reflects Tiffani's vast lending know-how, tremendous leadership depth, dedication to her team, and her focus on elevating everything SAFE does to better serve members. Tiffani, who has been with SAFE since 2018, will continue to be responsible for leading the mortgage division, loan servicing and collections, personal lending, auto financing, college funding and insurance products. She will also take on the responsibility of SAFE's commercial, small business lending and account services team and activities.
