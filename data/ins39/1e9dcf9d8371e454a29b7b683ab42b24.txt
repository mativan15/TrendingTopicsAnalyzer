Legendary John Madden returns to cover of 'Madden NFL 23'
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-01 15:00:34

For the first time in 23 years, the late, great John Madden will return to the cover of "NFL Madden 23" in a truly perfect tribute to the legendary Oakland Raiders coach and color commentator.

EA Sports revealed the cover on Wednesday, June 1 — traditionally known as Madden Day, when the new cover of the game is revealed — which honors both Madden and the cover of the very first edition of the game in 1988.
