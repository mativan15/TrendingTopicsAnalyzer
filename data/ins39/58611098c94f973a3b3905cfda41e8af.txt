Published: 13:54 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 13:54 EDT, 21 October 2022 T.J. Otzelberger will be hard-pressed to top what he did in his first season at Iowa State. Otzelberger inherited a two-win team and led it to a 20-win improvement and the Sweet 16, making the Cyclones the first team to go that far in the NCAA Tournament after winning so few games the previous season. "I know there´s going to be the common theme of comparing this year to last year," Otzelberger said. "A lot of gratitude to the team and the group that we had, but we need to focus on this year´s group, what this year´s group can be and will be.
