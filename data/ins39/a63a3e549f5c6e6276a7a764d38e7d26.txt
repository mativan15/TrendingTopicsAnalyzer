May 31, 2022 02:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time KINGSTON, Jamaica--(BUSINESS WIRE)--As Jamaica pursues its Vision 2030 development plan which will transform the island's economy, the country's government continues to develop and seek innovative investments that can accelerate economic and social growth.



These investment projects will not only provide economic rewards, but will also be sustainable, create new avenues for development in communities, towns, and cities, and will lead to significant improvement in Jamaica's overall business environment.
