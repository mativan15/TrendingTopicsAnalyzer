Rhoan Morgan is the CEO and Co-Founder of DemandLab. getty One year ago, in May 2021, Texas A&M University professor Anthony Klotz predicted that the vastly changing post-pandemic workplace would experience a mass volunteer exodus called the Great Resignation. Since then, several variations of Klotz's theory have emerged: The Great Resignation. The Great Reimagination. The Great Reset. And most recently, the Great Regret. No matter which moniker resonates with you, all represent phenomena impacting employees and employers across the United States.
