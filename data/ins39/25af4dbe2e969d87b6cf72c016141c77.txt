UK PM Fights For Survival In First Parliament Questions Since U-turns

YouGov found that within six weeks of taking power Liz Truss has become the most unpopular leader it has ever tracked
British Prime Minister Liz Truss is set to address lawmakers in parliament on Wednesday for the first time since abandoning her tax-slashing economic agenda, as she seeks to reassert her waning political authority.Truss is set to face hostile questions from Tory as well as opposition MPs in a likely febrile House of Commons, less than 48 hours after new finance minister Jeremy Hunt dismembered her flagship tax plans.
