Denis_Dryashkin/iStock via Getty Images As most of you surely know, the current market correction has hit the technology sector especially hard. That being the case, the fact that Lumentum's (NASDAQ:LITE) stock is still up almost 6% over the past year while the tech sector is down nearly 12% - as measured by the Invesco NASDAQ 100 ETF (QQQ) - is quite impressive. That LITE has also outperformed the S&P500 over the past year is even more noteworthy (see graphic below). The reason for the out-performance is two-fold: not only has LITE's operational performance been solid despite many headwinds out of its control, but demand for LITE's optical components has been out-stripping supply.
