Kendrick Lamar's 'The Big Steppers Tour' Will Be Showcased On Prime Video, Amazon Music
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 21:09:14

Kendrick Lamar's The Big Steppers Tour will partner with Amazon Music and Prime Video to present a concert experience for streaming viewers.

The Big Steppers Tour will stream live from the Accor Arena Paris, France Oct. 22, presented by Amazon Music and Prime Video. The show marks the 10-year anniversary of Lamar's album, good kid, m.A.A.d city. The album earned Lamar seven nominations at the 56th Annual Grammy Awards.

The current tour supports Lamar's latest album, Mr. Morale & The Big Steppers, and fans will be able to watch for free on Prime Video in over 240 countries and territories, as well as through the Amazon Music channel on Twitch and on the Amazon Music app.
