October 18, 2022 04:35 AM Eastern Daylight Time HONG KONG--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CSOP Asset Management Limited ('CSOP') is happy to announce the listing of CSOP CSI Brokerage Index Daily (2x) Leveraged Product (ticker: 7252.HK) on Hong Kong Stock Exchange. 7252.HK aims to help investors capture the short-term investment opportunities of China brokerage sector of A Shares. Tracking CSI All Share Investment Banking & Brokerage Index (the 'Index'), 7252.HK intends to adopt a swap-based synthetic replication strategy to achieve the investment objective, which is 2x performance of the index on a daily basis.
