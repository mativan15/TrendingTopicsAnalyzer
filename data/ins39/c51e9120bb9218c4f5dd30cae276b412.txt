EuroMillions boasts £14 million jackpot tonight
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 17:56:07

EuroMillions lottery draws are among several organised by the National Lottery a week that could see ticketholders become multi-millionaires. The May 27 draw is no different, with an eight-figure £14 million jackpot in the balance tonight. But hopefuls don't have much time left to throw their numbers into consideration.EuroMillions organisers release winning numbers for their competition after ticket sales close.The competition closes at 7.30pm, with results usually following within the next hour.
