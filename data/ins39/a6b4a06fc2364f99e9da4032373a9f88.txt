U.S. stocks recover some ground as good earnings offset higher bond yields

U.S. stocks climbed on Thursday, adding to their gains for the week as the third-quarter earnings reporting season got off to a strong start, offsetting the drag from higher bond yields. All three major indexes have recorded robust gains for the week despite finishing lower on Wednesday. The Dow is up 3.6%, while the S&P 500 has gained 3.8%, and the Nasdaq risen 4.8%, leaving all three on track for their best week in more than a month.
