Beyoncé, Mary J. Blige and Chris Brown earned the most nominations at the 2022 Soul Train Awards. Beyonce and Mary J leading with seven nominations each, while Ari Lennox has six, and Chris Brown and Lizzo each have five. Steve Lacy and Burna Boy follow them with four. The show will be hosted by Deon Cole and is set to air on BET November 27 at 8 p.m. ET. Find the complete list of nominations below: Video of the Year 'About Damn Time' – Lizzo 'Bad Habit' – Steve Lacy 'Good Morning Gorgeous' – Mary J.
