background, black, business, button, close-up, communication, computer, concept, connection, copy, data, digital, global, gray, horizontal, (Shutterstock)NEWARK, CA -- The City of Newark is now accepting applications for a Chief Information Officer / Information Technology Director. Applications are due by 5 pm on June 14, 2022. The person hired for the role of CIO/IT Director would lead a team of five full-time staff and supplemental contractors as needed to support all city operations, including a police department with a dispatch center.
