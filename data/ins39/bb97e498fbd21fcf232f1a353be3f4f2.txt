Ottawa police seek help identifying armed robbery suspect
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-02 19:10:42

Ottawa police are asking for the public's help identifying a man accused of an armed robbery in mid-May. Police say an armed man entered a business on Mitch Owens Road near River Road in Manotick at around 6 p.m., May 16. The man left with an undisclosed amount of cash. It's alleged the suspect threatened to shoot the employee if police were called. The suspect is described as a Black man, slim, wearing a black hoodie, black pants, and a black mask. Anyone with information is asked to contact the Ottawa Police Robbery Unit at 613-236-1222 ext.
