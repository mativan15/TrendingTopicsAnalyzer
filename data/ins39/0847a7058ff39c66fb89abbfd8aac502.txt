October 18, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kelly Williams, Director of Business Development for Impact Housing, was elected to serve on the Board of Directors for the Modular Home Builders Association (MHBA) at their annual conference in Harrisburg, PA. He joins modular builders and factory representatives from across the country to guide the industry association.



The mission of the Modular Home Builders Association (formerly the Modular Building Systems Association) is to be the primary organization advancing the legislative and regulatory interests of the modular industry in the United States.
