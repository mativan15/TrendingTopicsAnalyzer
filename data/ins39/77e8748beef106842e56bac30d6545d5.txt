October 17, 2022 06:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time FOOTHILL RANCH, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Abacus Solutions International Group ('Abacus') is pleased to announce a new integration partnership with Fujitsu Frontech North America Inc. providing its Lottery Everywhere service to Fujitsu U-SCAN self-service retail customers.



The Abacus Lottery Service (based on the Abacus Fusion Platform) brings retailers, lottery, and gaming vendors together, ensuring lottery can be played as seamlessly across all channels of a retailer's point of sale solution.
