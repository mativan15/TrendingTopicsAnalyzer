Business leadership during crisis: Here's how to succeed
weforum.org
business
2022-06-02 09:11:12

In an increasingly uncertain world, leaders have to learn how to navigate simultaneous crises. Excelling in turbulent times requires business leaders to prioritize, work at pace and provide a clear and compelling vision. Leaders must be their own communications arm, delivering their message endlessly and consistently to ensure it is heard. 

 In today's world, one thing is certain: uncertainty, a status that has emerged as the defining characteristic of our age.

 For decades, much of the world enjoyed relatively steady economic and social gains.
