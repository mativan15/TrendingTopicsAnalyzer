DWP to introduce new Q&A website as part of £6.4m fund for disabled workers

The new online service will aim to create more inclusive work environments - by giving employers the tools they need to empower and encourage disabled workers and those with health conditionsThe Government is aiming to see one million more in work by 2027 A new £6.4 million online service is being introduced to help thousands of UK businesses support disabled people and those with health conditions in the workplace. The DWP (Department for Work and Pensions) service will give employers the tools they need to empower and encourage workers.
