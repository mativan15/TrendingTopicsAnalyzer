A new report has highlighted the dire state of renting in Australia, with the number of affordable rental properties having halved since the start of the pandemic.The PropTrack Market Insight Report revealed the share of rental properties available for less than $400 a week has dropped from 41.8 per cent at the start of the pandemic to just 19.3 per cent this month.The report analysed the number of rental properties advertised on realestate.com.au and concluded there is a worrying lack of rentals across the country.
