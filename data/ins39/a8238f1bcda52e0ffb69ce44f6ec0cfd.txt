tbradford/iStock Unreleased via Getty Images Toshiba (OTCPK:TOSBF) said on Thursday it received 10 investment proposals, including eight offers to go private and two for capital and business alliances, as the company explores strategic options. Toshiba (OTCPK:TOSBF) said it will evaluate the proposals and choose one or more of them to pursue, as soon as possible after the annual shareholder meeting scheduled for June 28. Private equity investors including CVC Capital Partners, Blackstone and Bain Capital, as well as state-backed investment fund Japan Investment Corp.
