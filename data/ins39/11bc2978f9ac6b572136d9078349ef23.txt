Okta Shares Rise 11% Ahead of 1Q Results Later Thursday
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 17:52:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Okta Inc. were trading higher Thursday, as the company prepared to release its fiscal first-quarter results after the closing bell. At 1:36 p.m. ET, the shares were 11% higher at $93.45. Year to date, the stock is down more than 58%. Analysts are expecting Okta to report a first-quarter loss per share of $1.57 and sales of $389 million, according to FactSet. In the year-earlier period, Okta reported a loss of 83 cents a share. On an adjusted basis, analysts expect a first-quarter loss per share of 34 cents, according to FactSet.
