June 02, 2022 04:51 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CoStar Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: CSGP), a leading provider of commercial and residential real estate information, analytics, and online marketplaces, today announced the appointment of Cyndi Eakin as Senior Vice President of Investor Relations, effective immediately.



As head of Investor Relations, Ms. Eakin will be responsible for maintaining and expanding relationships with institutional investors and analysts and communicating CoStar's strategy and business results to Wall Street.
