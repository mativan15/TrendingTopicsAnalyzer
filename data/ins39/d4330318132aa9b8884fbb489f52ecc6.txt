Prabhas wishes Vishnu Manchu for 'Ginna' release: It's looking like a huge hit
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 06:04:00

Vishnu Manchu, Paayal Rajput and Sunny Leone's much-anticipated 'Ginna' hit the screens today, October 21. Movie buffs are flocking to theatres to watch their favourite actors on screen. On Thursday, pan-India star Prabhas took to his Instagram to wish the 'Ginna' team. Vishnu Manchu and the team were overwhelmed to see Prabhas's tweet. Vishnu Manchu during the promotion of 'Ginna' revealed that the film would definitely be a blockbuster. He also hinted at a sequel to the film. On October 20, Rebel star Prabhas took to Instagram stories and wished 'Ginna' team for the success of the film.
