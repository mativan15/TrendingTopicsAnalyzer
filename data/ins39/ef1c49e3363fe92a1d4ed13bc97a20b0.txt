 London, Ont., police are encouraging the public to be part of the business planning process for the development of the 2024-2027 Strategic Plan.
London police create a new plan every three years, collecting the input from residents in the hopes of addressing their biggest concerns.Londoners have the opportunity to attend any of the three upcoming online virtual community consultations where police are seeking input on crime prevention, law enforcement, victims' assistance, public order maintenance and emergency response.
