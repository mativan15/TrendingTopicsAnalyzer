By CBSLA Staff October 21, 2022 / 10:34 PM / CBS Los Angeles California State Senator Brian Dahle knows that his work is cut out for him as he prepares for the looming November election, when he'll face off against incumbent Governor Gavin Newsom in the polls. While the latest numbers show that Newsom holds a large lead, Dahle said he's in it for the long haul. As he preps for the first and only gubernatorial debate, oddly scheduled for 1 p.m. Sunday afternoon, Dahle noted that regardless of the time he's up for the challenge.
