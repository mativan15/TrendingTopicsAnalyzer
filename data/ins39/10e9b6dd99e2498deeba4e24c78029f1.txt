The 2-3 Rams have lost two games in a row. Many are wondering whether they should be panicking. Cornerback Jalen Ramsey does not agree.

'I mean, if you have two bad weeks of your life at home out of 56 weeks, would you panic?' Ramsey told reporters on Friday. 'Would you? Yeah, we got a long season to go. We got 12 games to go. Hopefully it is, but this might not be the only time that we lose two games in a row.
