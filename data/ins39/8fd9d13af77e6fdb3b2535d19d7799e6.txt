REUTERS/Mike BlakeLONDON, Oct 19 (Reuters) - Global freight volumes have begun to fall as overall consumer and business spending slows and the composition rotates from merchandise back to services after the pandemic.Slower growth in freight and manufacturing will ease pressure on supply chains and commodity markets, taking some heat out of inflation, currently running at the fastest rate for 40 years.
