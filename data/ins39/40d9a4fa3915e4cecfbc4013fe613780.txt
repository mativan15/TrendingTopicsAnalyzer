'Who is really in charge' Kuenssberg confronts Hunt
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 08:32:00

Liz Truss's economic vision appeared doomed on Sunday, as she sought to stay in power despite an increasingly shaky-looking premiership. New Chancellor Jeremy Hunt, brought in to replace the sacked Kwasi Kwarteng and to restore credibility to Downing Street, spent Saturday effectively trashing the mini-budget and the set of policies that brought Ms Truss to power. BBC's Laura Kuenssberg questioned if Mr Hunt is "in charge" after he scrapped almost all of the "mistakes" of Ms Truss' mini budget.She asked: "It's quite staggering really, a few weeks into a new Government to hear you sitting here this morning and basically ditching the entire thrust of what the Prime Minister wanted to do with the economy.
