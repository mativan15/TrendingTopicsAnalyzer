This 19-acre Franklin Park estate is for sale for $5M
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 14:56:47

A 19-acre property is currently for sale in Franklin Park.

The property, located at 1635 Pegher Lane, includes a six-acre private lake in addition to a 10,000-square-foot timber-framed home. The property is listed with Gina Giampietro of RE/MAX Select Realty for $4,999,900.

The home was built in 2003 and has six bedrooms and seven bathrooms. The listing for the property describes it as feeling 'like an expansive Colorado vacation dream home.'
