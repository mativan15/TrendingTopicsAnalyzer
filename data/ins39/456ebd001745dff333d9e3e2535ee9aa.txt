Rudy Giuliani to host son Andrew's NY gov bid fundraiser at Trump golf course
nypost.com
news
2022-06-03 01:13:16

Former president Donald Trump is lending his posh New Jersey golf course for an upcoming fundraiser for his former aide and New York GOP gubernatorial hopeful, Andrew Giuliani.
Rudy Giuliani will co-host the June 15 event for his son with ex-NYPD Commissioner Bernard Kerik at the Trump National Bedminster Golf Club, according to a copy of the invitation obtained by The Post.
