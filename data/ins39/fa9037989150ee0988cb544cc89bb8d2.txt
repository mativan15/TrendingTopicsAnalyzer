Wait, why are there so few dead bugs on my windshield these days?
washingtonpost.com
business
2022-10-21 10:00:00

After a long drive, the only thing that makes our stomachs churn faster than a windshield smeared with bug guts is a windshield bearing no evidence of insect life whatsoever. It feels like a fundamental pillar of the planet's ecology has snapped.You've probably noticed it, too. On long summer road trips, tiny corpses once formed a crust so thick that the reduced visibility posed a legitimate safety risk. Now, many folks we spoke with can't remember the last time they had to scour the bug gore from their RAV4.
