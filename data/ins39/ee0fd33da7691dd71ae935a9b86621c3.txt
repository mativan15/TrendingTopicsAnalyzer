REUTERS/Kaylee Greenlee Beal/File PhotoRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comMay 27 (Reuters) - Bubs Australia Ltd (BUB.AX) plans to ship at least 1.25 million cans of its baby formula to the United States to help ease a nationwide shortage, the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) said on Friday.Some of the baby formula is currently in stock for transport and more will be produced by the Australian company in the coming weeks and months, according to the FDA.
