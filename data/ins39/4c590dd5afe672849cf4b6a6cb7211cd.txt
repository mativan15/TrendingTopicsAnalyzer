Nailers Announce 2022-23 Season-Opening Roster
iheart.com
news
2022-10-22 00:45:00

WHEELING, WV- The Wheeling Nailers, proud ECHL affiliate of the Pittsburgh Penguins, are excited to announce their 2022-23 Season-Opening Roster, as the team prepares to open its 31st season on Saturday night against the Toledo Walleye at WesBanco Arena. Head Coach Derek Army and Assistant Coach Ryan Kuwabara return for their second season together behind the bench, after a 37-31-4 campaign and a trip to the Central Division Final. Joining them on the Hockey Operations Staff are Equipment Manager Bill Higgins (20th season) and Athletic Trainer Lauren Rittle (1st season).
