Prime Minister Anthony Albanese is standing by his decision to reverse a Morrison-era policy recognising West Jerusalem as the capital of Israel, but concedes it could have been handled better.The federal government has reversed a 2018 decision by Scott Morrison to recognise West Jerusalem as the capital of IsraelThe reversal has sparked blowback from the opposition, crossbench and some members of the Labor PartyThe government says its position on the status of Jerusalem has always been clearLabor promised at the election to reverse Australia's recognition of West Jerusalem as Israel's official capital, but the policy change was announced on Tuesday, a holy holiday for Jewish people.
