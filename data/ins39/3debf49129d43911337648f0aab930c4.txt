Watch out for that 59.9% credit rate
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 23:01:00

Credit card borrowing is at its most expensive in 24 years. The average interest rate on credit cards rose to 21.88 per cent in September — the highest since November 1998.Someone with £1,000 debt repaying the minimum amount would take 18 years and 10 months to clear their balance and would incur £1,459 in interest.The cost of borrowing has been increasing ever since the Bank of England started raising interest rates from their record low level of 0.1 per cent in December. The base rate is now 2.
