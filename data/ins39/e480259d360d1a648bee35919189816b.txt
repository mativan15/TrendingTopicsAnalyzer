TORONTO, May 31, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Softchoice Corporation ("Softchoice" or the "Company") (TSX: SFTC) announced today the results of its annual general meeting of shareholders (the "AGM") held on Tuesday, May 31, 2022.

Election of Directors

At the AGM, Softchoice shareholders elected eight directors, comprising seven incumbent directors and one new director, Sylvie Veilleux.

Ms. Veilleux is a global technology leader, board director and former chief information officer with more than 30 years of business experience, including considerable experience as a technology executive in leading companies.
