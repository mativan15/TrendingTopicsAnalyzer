AgenciesRepresentative image India's cumulative Covid-19 vaccination status reached over 193.57 crore on 1 June (as of 7am). So far, more than 3.4 crore first doses have been administered to children between the age group of 12-14 years under the vaccination drive which began in March 2022. The advancement in vaccination drives across the country has led to a surge in recovery rate. It now stands at 98.74%. Meanwhile, the weekly positivity rate is 0.63%.
