High River Levels Disrupt Outdoor Business (Asheville, NC) -- Last week's rains are causing problems for area outdoor adventure businesses. The operations manager at Asheville's Zen Tubing told WLOS-TV yesterday that early rainfall last week caused high levels on the French Broad River through the holiday weekend. Around one-thousand reservations were cancelled as a result. The business is planning to be able to open within the next couple of days. Fire Destroys Small Candler Business (Candler, NC) -- Fire is to blame for the destruction of a small business in Candler.
