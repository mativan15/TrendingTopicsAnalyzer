Whoopi Goldberg has voiced her opinion over Meghan Markle's remarks about her time on Deal or No Deal, when the duchess admitted she felt ‘objectified' on the show.
In an episode of the Duchess of Sussex's Archetype podcast on Spotify, she explained that when she was on the programme, she ‘didn't like feeling forced to be all looks and little substance' as one of the women whose job it was to hold up the suitcases.
