October 18, 2022 10:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time TOKYO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hotel Okura Co., Ltd., is pleased to announce that the Okura Resort & Spa Phu Quoc will open in 2027 at the premier resort of Long Beach on Phu Quoc Island in Vietnam. The company has contracted with Trading Construction Works Organization to manage the new property.



The 20-km long white sandy Long Beach is the only location in Vietnam where people can watch the sun set over the ocean, offering spectacular views as the day comes to an end.
