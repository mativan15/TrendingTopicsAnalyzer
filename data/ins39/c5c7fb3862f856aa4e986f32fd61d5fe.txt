Top cryptocurrency prices today: Avalanche, Shiba Inu, Solana and XRP drop up to 11%
indiatimes.com

2022-06-01 05:30:00

New Delhi: Following two straight sessions of gains, the crypto cart saw some profit booking on Wednesday as investors took advantage of a healthy rise to take some money off the table. A few players might see it as a short term rebound as they are cautious about any long-term bounce in the digital token market amid ongoing inflationary concerns and recession worries. Barring Cardano, all other major crypto tokens were trading with mild cuts. However, the losses were capped in the majority of names.
