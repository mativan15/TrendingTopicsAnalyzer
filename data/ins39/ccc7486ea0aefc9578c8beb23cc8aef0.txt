Ongoing chaos in UK politics

UK Prime Pinister Liz Truss is facing a revolt from senior Conservatives after the disastrous release of her tax-cutting economic package. She's now U-turned on several measures, and on Friday abruptly sacked Chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng, installing former foreign secretary Jeremy Hunt.Now, with her authority and her prime ministership hanging by a thread, conservative MPs are preparing to meet in a reported effort to remove Ms Truss from office.  Guest: Krishnan Guru Murthy, lead presenter of Chan
