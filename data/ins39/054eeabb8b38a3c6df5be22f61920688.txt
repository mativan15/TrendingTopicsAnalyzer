Are Republicans and Big Business Headed for a Breakup?
washingtonpost.com
news
2022-10-20 11:35:51

The alliance between big business and the Republican Party, one of the oldest in US politics, is unusually frayed these days. The question is whether there will be a complete unraveling.There is ample evidence of a strained relationship. Senator Rick Scott of Florida, the current chair of the National Republican Senatorial Committee, introduced his 'Rescue America' policy plan earlier this year with the accusation that 'most corporate boardrooms' are now controlled by the 'militant left.' Florida Governor Ron DeSantis has become engaged in a public battle with the Walt Disney Co.
