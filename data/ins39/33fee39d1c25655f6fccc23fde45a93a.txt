Veritone launches marketplace for sports federations, content producers
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-20 11:48:47

Artificial Intelligence company Veritone (NASDAQ:VERI) has launched a marketplace for sports federations and content producers, SPORT X. The platform will first launch for customers in the EMEA region, and may expand in the future. ATP Media, Euroleague Basketball and Extreme E are set to place their content in the marketplace, while several other federations and sports content owners are in advanced discussions with the company for their content. Additionally, SPORT X plans to host content from the sports news video outlet SNTV.
