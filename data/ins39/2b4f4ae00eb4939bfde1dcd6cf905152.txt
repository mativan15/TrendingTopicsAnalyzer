Variety - TV News Each season, 'Most Memorable Year' night is the most emotional episode of 'Dancing With the Stars.' Monday night's episode was no different when the evening came to an end with Selma Blair and Sasha Farber saying goodbye to the competition.In a pre-recorded packaged, Blair told her partner that she couldn't continue due to health concerns connected to her multiple sclerosis. The duo hugged close while watching the package from inside the ballroom.
