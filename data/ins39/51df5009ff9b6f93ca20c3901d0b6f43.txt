 People are being encouraged to do their part in reducing materials that end up in landfills.
Waste Reduction Week in Canada starts on Monday.The City of Guelph is taking part in the campaign by lighting up City Hall with green and blue lights.Guelph is highlighting the seven themes being promoted on the Waste Reduction Week Canada website. The city is also teaming up with the Guelph Tool Library in the city's large clothing swap and sale on Saturday, Oct.
