Khloe Kardashian ‘has it written in will' to have glam done if she's in coma
metro.co.uk

2022-10-20 09:00:00

Khloe Kardashian has revealed the lengths she is prepared to go to in order to always look good.
The famous family are known for going above and beyond in their bid to appear flawless at all times.
Who could forget when Kim Kardashian announced that she would happily eat poop daily to look younger?
In the latest episode of the Kardashians, Khloe admitted she has certain things written in her will to maintain her glamorous appearance at all times – even when in hospital.
During a catch-up with mom Kris Jenner after her recent hip surgery, their talk turned to their plans for death and whether they would be cremated or buried together as a family.
