Cathay General adopts new buyback program for up to $125M (NASDAQ:CATY)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-27 04:59:14

Cathay General (NASDAQ:CATY) notifies that its board has adopted a new share repurchase program authorizing the company to repurchase up to $125M of the company's common stock. The previous $125M share repurchase program announced on September 2, 2021, was completed on February 18, 2022, with the repurchase of 2.86M shares at an average cost of $43.73.As of the date of this release, the company has ~75.15M shares of common stock outstanding.YTD, the stock lost 8.3%; current share repurchase forms 4.
