Should there be a General Election
express.co.uk
politics
2022-10-21 10:26:00

In scenes reminiscent of her predecessors, Liz Truss delivered a brief speech to confirm her resignation as Prime Minister, a role she had assumed just 45 days ago. Her disastrous spell in office culminated in her earning the title of the UK's shortest-serving Prime Minister in modern history, eclipsing the 119 days served by George Canning, who died while in charge. But while the Conservative Party begin looking for her replacement, swathes of the electorate and rival parties are demanding a general election take place.
