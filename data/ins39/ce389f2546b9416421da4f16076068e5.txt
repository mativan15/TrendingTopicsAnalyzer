Tata Digital joins hands with Mad Street Den to elevate personalisation experience on the super app
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 01:30:00

ETtechIllustration: Rahul AwasthiChennai: Tata Digital has partnered with Chennai-based Artificial Intelligence provider Mad Street Den (MSD) to use the latter's Blox.ai platform for a personalised and connected shopping experience on its Tata Neu app. Tata Neu is a super app that brings together diverse offerings from various consumer brands across the conglomerate.

MSD said Blox.ai had the ability to extract and enrich content from inventory, content, and customer behaviour to deliver insights and personalize customer experiences across channels.
