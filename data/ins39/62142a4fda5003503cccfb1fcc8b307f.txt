Green investments: six tips for making a difference
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 23:01:00

If you feel passionately about combatting climate change, biodiversity loss and the problems caused by plastic, it is possible to invest with the objective of doing good while aiming for a financial return.The amount of money pouring into so-called responsible investments totalled £16 billion last year, up £4.3 billion on 2020. So far this year, a further £5.6 billion has been invested. Such investments typically include ESG funds, which invest in companies with good environmental, social and governance practices.
