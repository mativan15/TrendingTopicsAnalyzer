As companies call staff back to work, Gen Z faces the heat of price rise
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 09:00:00

India's Gen Z started working only around the start of the pandemic when work shifted to the WFH mode. They went back to their homes and saved a lot. As the pandemic recedes and companies call employees back to work, Gen Z is facing the heat as they have to shell out more for almost everything.The discussion has changed from investing in equities and cryptocurrencies to paying more for house rent and groceries A Times of India report quoted Mahima Rawat, a 24-year-old graphic designer, who went to her workplace in Mumbai only to find that she is spending more than what she used to two years back.
