Published: 17:54 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 17:55 EDT, 17 October 2022 NEW YORK (AP) - All the ego-bruising jokes, stinging cheap shots and embarrassing punchlines about New York football are falling flat now. Want to laugh at the Giants and Jets? Fuhgeddaboudit! Football in the Big Apple is rotten no more. But it certainly has been a while. The Giants are 5-1 after an impressive 24-20 win over Lamar Jackson and the Baltimore Ravens on Sunday. The Jets are 4-2 after stunning Aaron Rodgers and the Packers in Green Bay 27-10.
