Ros Wynne Jones revisits the story of 95-year-old WW2 veteran Stan Solomons who was left in a hospital corridor for 26 hours this week and wonders what the plan isStan Solomons was left in a hospital corridor for 26 hours Stan Solomons is a WW2 veteran. He trained with codebreakers at Bletchley Park, the once top-secret base of the Government Code and Cypher School. He served his country in the RAF as a young man, and even worked at a listening post in Hong Kong, close to the frontier with Communist China.
