MongoDB: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-06-01 20:51:49

NEW YORK (AP) _ MongoDB Inc. (MDB) on Wednesday reported a loss of $77.3 million in its fiscal first quarter.On a per-share basis, the New York-based company said it had a loss of $1.14. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to 20 cents per share. The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of five analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for a loss of 10 cents per share. The database platform posted revenue of $285.4 million in the period, also surpassing Street forecasts.
