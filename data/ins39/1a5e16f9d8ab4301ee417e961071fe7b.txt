Gardening: Dahlia plant essential method - how to tie in or stake plants (Image: GETTY)Staking dahlias, or "tying them in", means tying the plants to canes to give them support as they grow in the garden. Dahlias flower in the middle of summer, producing their famously large and lush flowers. However, the weight of these flowers can cause problems for them.Without staking or tying in the dahlias, the plants could break.A gardening influencer who inspired followers yearly with her incredible dahlias.
