naphtalina/iStock via Getty Images Investment Thesis General Motors (NYSE:GM) is clearly learning from its competitor, Tesla (TSLA), in creating a new business unit, GM Energy, with a direct focus on residential and commercial purposes. These include offering a one-stop service for new energy solutions, such as stationary battery packs, solar panels, electric vehicle chargers, and other energy-management products. Though TSLA has had little success and profitability in that segment, we expect things to improve moving forward, given the massive uplifting effect from the solar tariff exemptions and Inflation Reduction Act.
