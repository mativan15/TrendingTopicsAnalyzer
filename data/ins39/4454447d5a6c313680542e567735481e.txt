Police tells Delhi BJP leaders to give more details in plaint against Sisodia
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-02 18:33:18

New Delhi: The Delhi anti-corruption branch (ACB) has sought details from some Bharatiya Janata Party leaders who in 2019 alleged financial irregularities by deputy chief minister Manish Sisodia and health minister Satyendar Jain (who earlier held the PWD portfolio) in the construction of new classrooms and schools in the Capital, according to people aware of the matter.The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) rubbished the allegations, and alleged that the BJP has given a mandate to the investigating agencies to prepare fake cases against Sisodia, days after the Enforcement Directorate (ED) arrested Jain in a money laundering case.
