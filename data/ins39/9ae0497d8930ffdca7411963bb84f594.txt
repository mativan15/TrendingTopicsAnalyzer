Cross-cultural coalitions have ruled Los Angeles politics for decades, helping elect both Black and Latino politicians to top leadership roles in the racially and ethnically diverse second largest city in America. But the year old recording of racist comments by the city's City Council president has laid bare the tensions over political power that have long quietly simmered between the Latino and Black communities.
