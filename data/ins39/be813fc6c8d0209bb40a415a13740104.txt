World's First Authentic Driving Metaverse, Infinite Drive, Set To Release Q4 2022
ign.com
news
2022-06-02 09:37:44

The Tiny Digital Factory announced recently that Infinite Drive will be released sometime at the end of this year. The developers pointed out that the authentic racing game will be powered by web3 technology and will feature real-life cars and authentic driving. Infinite Drive is the first mobile game to offer accessible realistic driving with true-to-life licensed cars to challenge the driving skills of players. The Tiny Digital Factory prides itself on its long-forged relationships with brands such as McLaren, Porsche, Mercedes AMG, Ford, Renault, Alpine, RUF, and W Motors.
