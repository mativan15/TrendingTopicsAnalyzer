Cracks in the Madhya Pradesh unit of the BJP were visible again on Wednesday. Rajendra Dhanora, a leader from Sagar, who was expelled for a controversial social media post against Minister Govind Singh Rajput, accused the party of ignoring its old workers and adopting double standards in dealing with intra-party criticism. Earlier, Mr. Dhanora was expelled for six years, after the party saw his post and his constant demand for a local candidate for the Surkhi seat as an act of rebellion.
