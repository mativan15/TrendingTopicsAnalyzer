A dramatic cat named Pablo has left the internet in stitches after a video of him falling off the table as he was trying on Halloween costumes went viral on social media.The viral clip, shared on TikTok earlier in October by the cat's owner, under the username Lucyshirley_, shows Pablo posing for pictures while trying on new Halloween costumes. All of a sudden, when striking another one of his fancy poses, he lost balance falling behind.It came with a caption that said: "No cat was harmed in the making of this video.
