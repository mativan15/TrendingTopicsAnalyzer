Growing inventories, continued inflation, the rising costs of transportation, delivery, and keeping store shelves fully stocked -- these have been the main stories for retail stocks thus far in the earnings season. A combination of these factors contributed to the shrinking profit margins and massive earnings misses for Target (TGT 4.33%), Walmart (WMT 2.13%), and Abercrombie & Fitch (ANF -1.71%) these past two weeks.
Yet some way, somehow, Best Buy (BBY 2.49%) seems to have largely dodged these bullets.
