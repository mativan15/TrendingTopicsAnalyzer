It can be challenging to know where to begin when it comes to retirement, but many will have big dreams and plans. To help Britons get on the right track, Express.co.uk spoke to Suzanne Williams, chartered financial planner at Old Mill.She offered her top tips on how to prepare for retirement.Ms Williams stressed the more prepared a person is for retirement, the easier it will be - both financially and emotionally.Firstly, Ms Williams said, thinking about when a person wants to retire is key - particularly as there is no fixed age by which to do so.
