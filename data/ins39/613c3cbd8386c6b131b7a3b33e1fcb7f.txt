While the stock market takes a dive, inflation leaps, and supermarkets come under fire, in the middle of Hāwera, a small team of full-timers and volunteers are trying to help those falling through the cracks. Sometimes the Hāwera Budget Advisory Service team are counsellors, sometimes they're organising food, sometimes they're financial mentors, and sometimes they'll even drive clients to the bank, or to their vaccination. When coordinator Julie Gaudin started working at the service in about 2009 she was the only employee and worked one day a week, alongside four volunteers.
