Will County incumbents in District 2 stress experience as they face challenge in Republican primary
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-05-31 10:30:00

Will County incumbent board members Judy Ogalla of Monee and Frankie Pretzel of New Lenox are working as a team to be reelected for the newly drawn 2nd District, which includes all or portions of New Lenox, Frankfort, Manhattan, Beecher, Elwood, Peotone and unincorporated eastern Will County.They are challenged by Frankfort residents Hillary 'Mattsey' Kurzawa and Robert M. Hendrick in the June 28 Republican primary. Voters will select two Republicans to vie against Democrat Bob Howard in November for two four-year seats.
