May 31, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time BERWYN, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Envestnet, Inc. (NYSE: ENV), today announced that the Company will be participating in a fireside chat at the 2022 RBC Capital Markets Financial Technology Conference on Tuesday, June 14, 2022 in New York, NY. The discussion will begin at 10:10AM ET.

Investors and interested parties can access the presentation by visiting the Company's investor relations website at https://investor.envestnet.com/.

About Envestnet

Envestnet refers to the family of operating subsidiaries of the public holding company, Envestnet, Inc.
