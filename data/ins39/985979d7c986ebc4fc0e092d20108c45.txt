Odisha: EOW lens on crores parked in accounts of tribals
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 07:45:00

BHUBANESWAR: Cyber crooks have been using the accounts of unsuspecting tribals from Nabarangpur district to park crores of ill-gotten money, the economic offences wing (EOW) of the state Crime Branch has learnt following an intelligence input, and have launched an investigation.More than 2000 accounts had been opened with India Post Payment Bank (IPPB) in Nabarangpur in the names of tribals, most of whom are daily wage labourers, earning a meagre Rs 300-Rs 400 per day on an average.As per intelligence input, suspicious transactions worth crores were noticed in at least 25 of those accounts.
