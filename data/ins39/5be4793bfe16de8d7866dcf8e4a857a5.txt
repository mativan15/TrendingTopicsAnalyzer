Indian Institute of Technology's are one of the best recognised Indian brand in the world and possibilities in IIT's are going up says Dr S Jaishankar, External Affairs Minister while addressing the students, faculty and staff members of IIT Guwahati. In his address he urged the students to have a problem solving approach in their engineering so that it would benefit the society. He further said that engineers must have a vision for their country. Speaking on the occasion he said that last 8 years have been very important for our country as the present government is trying to approach the longstanding issues which are existed in our society.
