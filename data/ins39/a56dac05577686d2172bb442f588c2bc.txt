NEW YORK, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- VIVA Entertainment Group, Inc. (OTTV) ("VIVA" or "the Company"), a distributor of Over-The-Top (OTT) IPTV content to consumers in a bundled subscription format, today announces a partnership with Alterna, a California based financial services company serving the Latin American and immigrant community in the United States. Together they will launch the VIVALiveTV Alterna® Prepaid Mastercard®, a prepaid card program targeting the U.S. Hispanic and immigrant markets and designed to provide financial inclusion to vulnerable communities and consumers.
