MOVIES

— One of the year's standout documentaries , Margaret Brown's 'Descendant' takes a wide lens to the discovery of the Clotilda, the last known ship to bring African captives to the American South for enslavement. As Brown has said, the discovery of the ship — sunk near Mobile, Alabama, after it brought 100 Africans in the mid-19th century decades after the international slave trade had been outlawed — is 'just the tip of the iceberg.
