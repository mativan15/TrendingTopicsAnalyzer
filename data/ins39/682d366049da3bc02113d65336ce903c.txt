Ithaca's odyssey is set to bring it to the stock market and the FTSE 100
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 14:00:00

A North Sea oil and gas producer is planning to list on the stock market and is set to be an immediate contender to join the FTSE 100 index of Britain's biggest companies.Ithaca Energy has stakes in six of the ten largest fields in UK waters and expects to be producing the equivalent of at least 76,000 barrels of oil a day over the months to come.The Aberdeen-based company's interests include Rosebank and Cambo, two of Britain's largest undeveloped sites. Both have attracted criticism from environmental campaigners.
