Verity Credit Union Announces Participation in Loan Program Providing Small Businesses and Nonprofits in Washington State with Low Interest Business Loans

2022-10-19 17:45:00

'At Verity, we want to ensure that our region's smallest businesses and nonprofits have equitable access to flexible loans to help with their recovery from the pandemic,' said Tina Narron, Chief Lending Officer at Verity. SEATTLE (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Verity Credit Union, a $735 million asset financial institution in Seattle that focuses on socially responsible banking, announced today that it has been selected to participate in the Small Business Flex Fund, providing access to growth capital for the businesses that need it most, ensuring a greater economic recovery for all of Washington.
