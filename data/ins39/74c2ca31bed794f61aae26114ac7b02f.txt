Soccer Football - Champions League - Group F - Shakhtar Donetsk v Real Madrid - Stadion Wojska Polskiego, Warsaw, Poland - October 11, 2022 Real Madrid coach Carlo Ancelotti REUTERS/Kacper Pempel/File PhotoMADRID, Oct 15 (Reuters) - Real Madrid manager Carlo Ancelotti said he plans to keep things simple as he prepares his side ahead of their match at home with Barcelona in the "El Clasico" tie on Sunday.The Italian said he would avoid big changes to his team this time, after he put midfielder Luka Modric up front to replace the then injured striker Karim Benzema in the last El Clasico in March - only to get hammered 4-0 by the Catalans.
