EXPLAINED: Dynamic pricing has been around since people started exchanging stuff, but it looks quite different on your laptop than it did on the Silk Road. With the cost of living now the top national concern, as Consumer NZ has said, and prices rising around us, what we're paying and why is of more interest than ever. Dynamic pricing really hit the consciousness when Uber arrived with its surge pricing, and there have been calls for companies to be more transparent about how they are charging customers.
