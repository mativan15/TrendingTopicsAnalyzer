May 31, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time LARGO, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CONMED Corporation (NYSE: CNMD) today announced that it intends to offer, subject to market conditions and other factors, $600 million aggregate principal amount of its convertible senior notes due 2027 in a private offering to qualified institutional buyers pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the 'Securities Act'). In connection with the offering of the notes, CONMED expects to grant the initial purchasers an option to purchase, for settlement within a 13-day period beginning on, and including, the first day on which the notes are issued, up to an additional $100 million aggregate principal amount of the notes.
