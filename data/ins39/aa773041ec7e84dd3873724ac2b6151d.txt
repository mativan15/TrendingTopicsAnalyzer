Crist campaign manager arrested in apparent domestic incident prior to resignation
aol.com
news
2022-10-21 20:52:41

The manager for Charlie Crist's gubernatorial campaign was arrested shortly before he resigned from the campaign this week, according to court records, in an apparent domestic violence case.Austin Durrer was charged with second-degree assault on Tuesday in Cambridge, Md., according to court records. A woman listing the same address as Durrer, Jackie Whisman, was also criminally charged with second-degree assault for an incident on the same date, and Durrer's name was listed as a 'complainant' in her case in online court records.
