A veteran Philadelphia homicide detective has been convicted of sexually assaulting or exploiting three male witnesses in a case that has led prosecutors to review dozens of homicide convictionsPHILADELPHIA -- A veteran Philadelphia homicide detective was convicted of sexually assaulting or exploiting three male witnesses in a case that has led prosecutors to review dozens of homicide convictions. Former detective Philip Nordo, 56, was taken into custody after the jury verdict Wednesday. A 2019 grand jury report accused him of using his position to intimidate and groom male suspects and witnesses into sexual acts.
