HUL Q2 net profit rises 22.2% to Rs 2,670 cr; revenue up 16.4% at Rs 15,253 cr
indiatimes.com
food
2022-10-22 06:00:00

New Delhi: FMCG major Hindustan Unilever Ltd on Friday reported a 22.19 per cent rise in its consolidated net profit to Rs 2,670 crore for the second quarter ended September 30, mainly helped by volume growth. The company had posted a net profit of Rs 2,185 crore in the July-September quarter of the previous fiscal.Its total income increased 16.44 per cent during the quarter under review to Rs 15,253 crore. It was Rs 13,099 crore in the year-ago period, Hindustan Unilever Ltd (HUL) said in a regulatory filing.
