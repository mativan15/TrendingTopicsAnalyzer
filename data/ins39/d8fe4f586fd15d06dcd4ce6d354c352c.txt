October 18, 2022 01:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time REDWOOD CITY, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Suki, the leader in voice artificial intelligence (AI) technology for healthcare, announced a host of new features and voice-enabled capabilities to its flagship Suki Assistant. The Suki Assistant Fall '22 Release offers functionality that delivers a more flexible, streamlined, and robust experience for tackling tedious tasks, including:



1. 'Mobile as a mic' - Clinicians using Suki Assistant on Windows can now use their phones to dictate and issue commands, releasing them from the constraints of using computers with built-in microphones.
