Santa Clarita apartment community acquired in $313 million 4-property portfolio deal

Apartment investment and management company Bell Partners Inc. acquired a multifamily community in Santa Clarita as part of a four-property portfolio.

Based in North Carolina, Bell Partners acquired the portfolio of four apartment communities, totaling 846 apartment homes across four states, for $313 million.

In addition to Montecito Apartments in Santa Clarita, California, Bell Partners acquired Sidney at Morningside in Atlanta; Residences at Starwood in Frisco, Texas; and Touchstone Apartments in Broomfield, Colorado.
