The Armed Forces minister has warned that he will quit if Jeremy Hunt backs down on the Government's defence spending pledge.

On Monday, the Chancellor said that "eye-wateringly difficult" decisions were needed, as he tore up Liz Truss's economic strategy.Mr Hunt said that there will be more pain to come with a new squeeze on public spending when he delivers his full medium-term fiscal plan, along with updated economic forecasts by the Office for Budget Responsibility, on October 31.
