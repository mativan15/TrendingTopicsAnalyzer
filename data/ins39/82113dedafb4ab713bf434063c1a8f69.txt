The sexual and reproductive health leader expands its most affordable & comprehensive medical abortion service, now available in nine states. Wisp's telehealth medical abortion service is now available in Connecticut and New Mexico, in addition to California, Colorado, Illinois, Maryland, Maine, New York, and Washington, which launched earlier this year. SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Wisp, the leading sexual and reproductive health provider in the US, today became the largest, most accessible medical abortion provider in the country, offering its services in nine states: California, Connecticut, Colorado, Illinois, Maryland, Maine, New Mexico, New York, and Washington.
