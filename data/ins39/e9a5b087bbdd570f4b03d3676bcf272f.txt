Heavy rains lash Pune; city witnesses waterlogging, floating cars
indiatimes.com

2022-10-18 06:45:00

Pune woke up to heavy rains on Tuesday and braced for waterlogging and traffic jams. People staying in some low-lying areas and near the Karha river banks were shifted to safer places, reports said. Areas like Hadapsar, Market Yard, Sinhgad Road, NIBM Road, B T Kawade Road, Katraj, Deccan, Karve Nagar, Kothrud, Kondhwa and Peth witnessed severe waterlogging.
