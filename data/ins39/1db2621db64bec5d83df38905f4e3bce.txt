Updated: 17 Oct 2022, 11:32 AM ISTMoonlighting is not a new trend but has become widespread in the past two years. The work-from-home phase following the outbreak of covid gave people more time at hand. They also picked up new skills and started using technology more effectively. Companies too woke up to the possibilities of remote working.Though moonlighters want to be seen as freelancers, there is a big difference between the two. A freelancer is not an employee so is free to work for different clients.
