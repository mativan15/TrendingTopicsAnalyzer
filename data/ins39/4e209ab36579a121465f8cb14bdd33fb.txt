Rudy Gobert, pictured here against Nic Claxton of the Brooklyn Nets on October 14, 2022 in Mineapolis, is playing this season under new colors, those of the Minnesota Timberwolves. STACY BENGS / AP Here we go again. Like every year in the fall, the North American basketball league is back in action. The new NBA season, which opened on Tuesday, October 18, comprises 82 regular season games per team, followed by the finals for the eight best teams in each conference (plus two, which face obstacles to access the playoffs).
