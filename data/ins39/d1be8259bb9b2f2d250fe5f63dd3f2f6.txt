FBI warns about electronic financial scams in New England; 800 victims reported last year
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-10-19 14:32:13

The FBI is warning the public to look out for electronic financial scams after the number of people who fell victim to such ruses last year in Massachusetts and three other New England states increased by nearly 50 percent from 2020.In a statement, the FBI Boston division said 809 victims from Massachusetts, Maine, New Hampshire, and Rhode Island, last year lost a combined $7.5 million to scammers who used remote desktop software to pilfer victims' funds. That's a 49 percent spike from 2020, the statement said.
