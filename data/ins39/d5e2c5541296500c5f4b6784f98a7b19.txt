High-tech race to map Ukraine's damaged historic buildings
euronews.com
news
2022-05-30 11:24:37

Many of Ukraine's historical monuments were destroyed by the Russian army, but cultural experts are working to preserve their memory using advanced technology and 3D scans.One of them is a volunteer French engineer, Emmanuel Durand, a 3D data acquisition specialist, who is helping a team of architects, engineers, historical building experts and a museum director to record buildings in Kyiv, Lviv, Chernigiv and Kharkiv.He places his laser scanner in a strategic corner of the severely damaged building and records it from all angles.
