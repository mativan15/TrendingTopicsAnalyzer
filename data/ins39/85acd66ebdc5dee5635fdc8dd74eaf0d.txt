Getty ImagesThe calculation ceiling for payments of ad-hoc bonuses will be monthly emoluments of Rs 7,000, the DoE saidThe Union Government has approved the grant of a non-productivity linked bonus (ad-hoc bonus) equivalent to 30 days emoluments for the accounting year 2021-22 for central government employees in Group C and all non-gazetted employees in Group B. The Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, made this announcement via an office memorandum issued on October 6, 2022. The non-productivity-linked bonus is given to those employees who are not covered by any productivity-linked bonus scheme.
