The 13th Global Mobile Broadband Forum will be hosted by Huawei on October 25 and 26, 2022 in Bangkok, Thailand, with support from our industry partners GSMA and GTI. The 2022 forum offers an opportunity for the mobile and adjacent vertical ecosystems to reconnect, rebuild, and reimagine a fully connected, intelligent world. With the strongest speaker line-up and innovative product launches, the mobile ecosystems are back together to explore how mobile broadband technology is shaping the future and continuing to transform people's lives and industries.
