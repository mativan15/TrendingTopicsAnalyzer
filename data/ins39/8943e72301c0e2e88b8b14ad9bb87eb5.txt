Gehlot fires back at Rajyavardhan Rathore over comments on Kharge, says BJP leaders should read history
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 09:30:00

JAIPUR: Rajasthan chief minister Ashok Gehlot on Thursday hit back at BJP MP Rajyavardhan Rathore over his comments on new Congress chief Mallikarjun Kharge and said leaders of the saffron party should read history before speaking.Rathore had on Wednesday called newly elected Congress president Kharge a "rubber stamp" and alleged that the party's internal polls to elect a new chief were a "fraud".In his response, Gehlot told reporters, "These people have no shame. New boys have come... they do not understand that they should read history and then learn to speak so that their image is not spoilt.
