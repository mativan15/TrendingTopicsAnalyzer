It was actually in defeat, in Manchester City's most traumatic loss of last season, that Jack Grealish proved his worth to Pep Guardiola.

Grealish did not even start the second leg of the Champions League semi-final against Real Madrid. He came on in the 78th minute to replace Gabriel Jesus and even then it was at a time when City were still going through to the final.
