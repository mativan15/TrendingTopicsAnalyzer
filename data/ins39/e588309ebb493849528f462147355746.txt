REUTERS/Aly SongBEIJING, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Shanghai reported no new domestically transmitted symptomatic coronavirus cases for Oct. 16, the same as a day earlier, and 32 local asymptomatic cases, up from 28 the previous day, the city government said on Monday.No cases were reported outside quarantined areas, the same as the previous day.
