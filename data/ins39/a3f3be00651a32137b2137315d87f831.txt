10-year Treasury yield pulls back from 14-year high after U.K. tax U-turn
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 12:01:00

Yields on U.S. Treasurys pulled back Monday, as investors expressed relief over the British government's decision to rescind proposed tax cuts that had sparked chaos in U.K. government bonds that spilled over to other financial markets.What yields are doing The yield on the 2-year Treasury note TMUBMUSD02Y, 4.447% fell to 4.443%, down from 4.507% at 3 p.m. Eastern on Friday. The 2-year's Friday finish was the highest since Aug. 8, 2007, based on 3 p.m. figures, according to Dow Jones Market Data.
