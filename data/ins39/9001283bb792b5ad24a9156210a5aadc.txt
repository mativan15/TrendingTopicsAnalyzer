Acme United Acquires First-Aid Kit Manufacturer Safety Made
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 16:39:00

By Kathryn Hardison Acme United Corp. said Wednesday it acquired first-aid kit manufacturer Live Safely Products, which does business as Safety Made, for an undisclosed amount. The acquisition will allow Acme, which supplies safety products for schools, homes, and offices, to expand its sales of personalized products, Chief Executive Walter Johnsen said. Safety Made in 2021 recorded revenue of $4.9 million and earnings of about $1.2 million before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization. Safety Made's team of 24 employees will join Acme in Keene, N.
