Xi Jinping's Taiwan rhetoric raises risk of war
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 18:30:00

ReutersAlthough an invasion, or even a naval blockade, would carry enormous risks for Xi, political risks of inaction are also growing, especially if he secures a precedent-breaking third term as leader this weekend.President Xi Jinping on Sunday proclaimed Taiwan's status should be "settled by Chinese people." Yet he faces a tricky balancing act managing public opinion in China's 1.4-billion population on an issue that looks set to dominate his third term. Xi's vow that China can realise unification with Taiwan "without a doubt" got some of the loudest applause from the 2,400 delegates who watched his speech at the Communist Party congress in Beijing.
