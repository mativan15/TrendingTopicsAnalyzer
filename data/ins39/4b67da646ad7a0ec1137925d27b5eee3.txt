WASHINGTON (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 NDA Partners' General Manager, Eric Fish, announced today that Kevin Clark, a diagnostic device executive, has joined the firm as an Expert Consultant. Mr. Clark has extensive experience in business development and commercialization of diagnostic devices including ELISA and IFA laboratory testing systems. Mr. Clark is founder of ImmunoVision, Inc.
