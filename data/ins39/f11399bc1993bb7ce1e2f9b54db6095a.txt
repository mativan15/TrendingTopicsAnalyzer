ByY Maheswara ReddyY Maheswara Reddy / Updated: Oct 20, 2022, 06:00 ISTTo provide a suitable platform for micro, small and medium enterprises that are still struggling to recover from the losses incurred due to the pandemic, the Karnataka State Small Scale Industries Association (KASSIA) is organising a two-day vendor development programme and industrial exhibition. KASSIA, in association with MSME Development & Facilitation Office, and Department of Industries & Commerce, is organising the vendor development programme and industrial exhibition at its Udyog Bhavan, Vijayanagar on October 21 and 22.
