Is creativity mostly madness or method?Photo by Sharon Pittaway on UnsplashI would argue it is a delightful and chaotic mix of both. Creativity is expansive, energizing, cathartic, and sometimes destructive.When I talk about creativity, I mean the ability to generate novel ideas and abstractions from disparate phenomena. Creativity may feel like an active process of engagement with the outside world to some or a sudden epiphany on a contemplative walk for others.Regardless of how you experience a creative idea or impulse to create, I think you can use systems thinking to make yourself more creative and get through creative blocks with ease.
