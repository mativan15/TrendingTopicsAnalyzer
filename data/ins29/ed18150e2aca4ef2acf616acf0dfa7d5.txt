Pioneering Hollywood actor is first Asian American selected to appear on a US quarter
Copyright 2022, ESI Media Ltd
news
2022-10-19 00:24:45

The actor Anna May Wong will be the first Asian American featured on the US quarter.Wong, born in Los Angeles, was considered the first Chinese-American film star. She is one of five women chosen to appear on the quarter as part of the the US Mint's American Women Quarters Program, which honours American women who have made a contribution to a multitude of different fields.Wong's quarter, which will be released next Tuesday, is historic. Her coin, which like all others will feature George Washington on one side, will show her resting her head on her hand.
