October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SMBC, a member of SMBC Group, announced today the appointment of Robin Milberg as Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO) and Paul Sweeney as Head of Strategy & Transformation. Both report to the SMBC Group in the Americas Chief Operating Officer, Debbie Freer.



'Our company is growing exponentially and having the best leadership is critical. Both Robin and Paul bring decades of experience to their new roles, having finely honed their skills within their respective areas of expertise,' COO Freer said.
