As per Pinpoint Weather predictions, the Tornado Warning in the southwest and central Virginia is gone as of now. However, some areas are still expected to see scattered rains and thundershowers. 
Update 08:23 AM:
The Tornado Warning in southwest and central Virginia has expired. However, Pinpoint Weather still predicts several weather threats in this region until Friday afternoon, and people are warned to stay alert until the storm passes completely. Besides, the National Weather Service (NWS) has issued another Severe Thunderstorm Warning until 09:00 AM in the region of Southside and Central Virginia.
