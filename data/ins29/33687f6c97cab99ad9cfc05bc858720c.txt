Everyone has family issues, even some of the most popular celebrities in the world. But, I'm sure most of them would still like to deal with them in private. In the case of 50 Cent and his son, Marquise Jackson, they are both bringing all of their problems to the forefront for everyone to know.

In an interview with The Breakfast Club on Wednesday, the 'In Da Club' rapper shot down his son's request to sit down for a face-to-face talk so the two can work through their issues.
