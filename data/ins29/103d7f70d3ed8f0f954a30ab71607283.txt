CVR Energy to Release Third Quarter Earnings Results

SUGAR LAND, Texas, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- CVR Energy, Inc. (NYSE: CVI) plans to release its third quarter 2022 earnings results on Monday, Oct. 31, after the close of trading on the New York Stock Exchange. The Company also will host a teleconference call on Tuesday, Nov. 1, at 1 p.m. Eastern to discuss these results. This call, which will contain forward-looking information, will be webcast live and can be accessed on the Investor Relations section of CVR Energy's website at www.CVREnergy.
