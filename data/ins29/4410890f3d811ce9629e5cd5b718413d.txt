Expansion Allows Company To Hire Hundreds

INDIANAPOLIS, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Spot Freight Inc. (Spot), an industry leader in empowering people and technology to optimize freight delivery, announced the official opening of its second Indianapolis office. The expansion will accommodate Spot's exponential growth while helping to strengthen the downtown economy. Spot employs over 350 in-office workers between its two Indianapolis offices. Over the next two years, that number is planned to grow to 600 in-office workers in Indianapolis.
