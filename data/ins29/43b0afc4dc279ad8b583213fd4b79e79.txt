By Reuters Published: 15:29 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 15:29 EDT, 18 October 2022 PARIS, Oct 18 (Reuters) - France international N'Golo Kante has been ruled out of the World Cup finals after undergoing an operation on a hamstring injury, his Premier League club Chelsea said on Tuesday. The midfielder, who played a pivotal role in Les Bleus's winning campaign in 2018, suffered a setback in the final stages of his recovery last week and has now been ruled out for four months after the surgery.
