Drivers warned of dangerous fuel-saving tip that could leave them stranded on motorway

Drivers have been urged to be careful when trying to save fuel as some tips could result in them being stranded on dangerous roads. Despite the cost of living crisis wreaking havoc in many drivers' lives, the last thing motorists need is to find themselves stranded in the middle of a fast-moving motorway.One way of trying to make sure that a car burns as little fuel as possible is by taking unnecessary weight away from it.This can be done by taking out unnecessary items of luggage or even rubbish.
