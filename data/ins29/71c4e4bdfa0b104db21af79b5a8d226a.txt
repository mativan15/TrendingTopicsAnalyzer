NSW communities still facing crisis months after floods, inquiry hears
theguardian.com
news
2022-05-30 08:23:42

Communities hit by this year's devastating floods in northern New South Wales have been left 'exposed' by the disaster that had highlighted a housing crisis in the region, an inquiry has been told.A NSW parliamentary committee is looking into the official response to the floods of February and April that affected the northern rivers region.At least 10 people died in the disaster, which forced thousands to flee their homes and left many towns severely damaged.Federal and state authorities have faced criticism over their handling of the emergency, including over response times, preparedness and recovery.
