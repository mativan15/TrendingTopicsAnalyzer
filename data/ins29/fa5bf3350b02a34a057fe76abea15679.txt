Katherine Griffiths, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- The introduction of a new power giving ministers the right to block or change the actions of regulators would be concerning, according to the Bank of England official responsible for financial stability.Adding a 'call-in' power to the UK's financial services and market bill could undermine regulators' independence and hit the industry's international competitiveness, Jon Cunliffe, deputy governor of the central bank, said to lawmakers scrutinizing the draft legislation.
