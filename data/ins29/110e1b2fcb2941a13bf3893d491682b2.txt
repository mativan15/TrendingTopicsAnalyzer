Okanagan group purchases fully equipped ambulance for Ukraine
globalnews.ca
news
2022-06-05 19:29:38

A fully equipped ambulance has arrived in Ukraine, thanks to the fundraising efforts of Okanagan residents.
The ambulance arrived in Uzshgorod city last week and is filled with medical supplies.'We were able to finalize the purchase and then found a vehicle that was for $18,000 plus extra expenses on transportation,' said Ukrainian Canadian Volunteer Association member Svitlana Shkyn.The Ukrainian Canadian Volunteer Association has been fundraising for Ukrainians since the war first began in February.
