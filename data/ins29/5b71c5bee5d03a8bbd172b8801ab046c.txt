SACRAMENTO, Calif., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Inszone Insurance Services: a rapidly growing, national provider of benefits, personal and commercial lines insurance, today announced the appointment of Kari Thies as Executive Vice President Benefits Department.

A veteran of the employee benefits space, Ms. Thies has over 15 years of experience in the industry. She began her insurance career at a general agency, supporting agents in the education and sale of individual and group health sales.
