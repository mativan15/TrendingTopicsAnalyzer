Wachiwit/iStock Editorial via Getty Images As bitcoin (BTC-USD) and other popular cryptocurrencies fall, so do a number of stocks that rely on the crypto market. Coinbase Global (COIN), the U.S.-listed crypto exchange, is chief among them. Bitcoin, retracing much of the gain it made on Monday and Tuesday, was falling ~4.4% late Wednesday afternoon on the same day that JPMorgan Chase CEO Jamie Dimon warned of an economic "hurricane" approaching and as investors position themselves for the Fed's looming actions to reduce its balance sheet.
