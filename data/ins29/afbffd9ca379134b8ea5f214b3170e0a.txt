Bengaluru: Tata Digital-owned Innovative Retail Concepts, which runs online grocery platform BigBasket, has received Rs 350 crore from holding company Supermarket Grocery Supplies, regulatory filings sourced from business intelligence platform Tofler showed. The latest investment was approved on May 24, according to the filings. ET reported in April that BigBasket had received Rs 1,000 crore from the holding company. 
The additional investment comes at a time when Tata Digital is promoting its super app Tata Neu.
