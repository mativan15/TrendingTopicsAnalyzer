Lockheed (LMT) Wins $98M Deal to Aid F-35 Jet Delivery Schedule
nasdaq.com
news
2022-06-01 08:44:00

Lockheed Martin Corp.'s LMT business unit, Aeronautics, recently secured a modification contract involving the F-35 Lightning II jet program. The Naval Air Systems Command, Patuxent River, MD, has offered the award.

Details of the Deal

Valued at $97.5 million, the contract is expected to get completed by December 2024. Per the terms of the deal, Lockheed will procure Block 4 special tooling and test equipment to aid delivery schedules of non-U.S. Department of Defense (DOD) participants and Foreign Military Sales (FMS) customers' F-35 Lightning II jets.
