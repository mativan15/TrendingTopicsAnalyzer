Queen's official Platinum Jubilee portrait packed with personal symbolism
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-02 09:44:58

The official photograph released to mark Queen Elizabeth II's historic Platinum Jubilee is packed with personal symbolism as well as including some powerful elements of historic royal portraiture.The photograph was released ahead of the 96-year-old queen's central weekend of jubilee celebrations which includes two public holidays and was taken by veteran royal photographer Ranald Mackechnie.Mackechnie has photographed the royals over a number of years including two portraits of the queen and her heirs, Prince Charles, Prince William and Prince George in 2016 and 2020.
