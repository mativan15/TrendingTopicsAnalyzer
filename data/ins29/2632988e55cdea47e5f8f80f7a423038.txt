By Reuters Published: 09:47 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 09:47 EDT, 27 May 2022 MILAN, May 27 (Reuters) - Russia has placed temporary restrictions on the current accounts of Italian individuals and companies that bank with the local units of Intesa Sanpaolo and UniCredit, two people familiar with the matter said. Confirming press reports, the people said the Bank of Russia had told the local arms of Italy's top two lenders that from May 25, Italian residents and companies, as well as the Italian consulates, could not withdraw money from their accounts without approval from the local bank heads.
