Malabar Gold & Diamonds to start construction of manufacturing unit in Telangana
indiatimes.com
beauty
2022-10-15 13:15:00

Malabar Gold & Diamonds will invest Rs.750 crore to set up the manufacturing facility and a refinery in Telangana.Gold and diamond retail chain major Malabar Gold & Diamonds is all set to start construction of its gems & jewellery manufacturing unit in Telangana. The company laid the foundation stone of the new unit on Saturday. Telangana's minister of municipal administration and urban development, IT, Industries, electronics and communication K T Rama Rao, Hon'ble Minister of Municipal Administration and Urban Development, Industries, laid the foundation stone of the manufacturing facility.
