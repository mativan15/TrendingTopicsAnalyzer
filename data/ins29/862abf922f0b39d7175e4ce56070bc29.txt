BOCA RATON, Fla, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Newtek Business Services Corp., (Nasdaq: NEWT), an internally managed business development company ('BDC'), today announced that it will report its third quarter 2022 financial results after the market closes on Monday, November 7, 2022. A conference call to discuss these results will be hosted by Barry Sloane, Chief Executive Officer, and Nicholas Leger, Chief Accounting Officer, the following day, Tuesday, November 8, 2022 at 8:30 am ET. Please note, to attend the conference call or webcast, participants should register online at http://investor.
