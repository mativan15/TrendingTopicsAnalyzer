The convergence of technology and education was always a given. However, none of us imagined that it would be so rapid and all-encompassing simultaneously. The pandemic provided an unprecedented impetus for technologising education. This has resulted in democratisation of quality education like never before. It has brought in flexibility and fluidity which allows students to shift their options and explore newer dynamics without abandoning their core subjects.
