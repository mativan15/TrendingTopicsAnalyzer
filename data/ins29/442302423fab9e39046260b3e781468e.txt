Harry and Meghan face public 'hostility' as some claim couple are 'cashing in' on Jubilee
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-28 10:29:00

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle could face a hostile reaction from public crowds at the Platinum Jubilee next week. The Duke and Duchess of Sussex's visit to the UK marks the first time the pair have been back together for a public event since their departure in 2020. One Briton wrote to GB News claiming that the couple was using the Jubilee to " top up their royal currency".
