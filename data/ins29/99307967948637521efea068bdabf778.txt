2022 South Island Masters Games draws to a close
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-16 01:21:54

More than 2000 athletes participated in the 2022 South Island Masters Games in Timaru, and the organiser says they are already looking forward to next year's event in Marlborough. Simon Carter said it has been a 'hugely enjoyable' experience for participants throughtout the 10-day event, with around 1200 athletes participating in the final weekend alone in sports including football, netball and swimming. 'People have been competing very hard on Saturday and enjoyed a social night out and they're back again for the final day,' Carter said.
