By ZHANG ZHOUXIANG | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-17 08:09 LI MIN/CHINA DAILY To walk across a road is easy for anyone without any vision impairment. However, having interviewed more than 100 people with vision impairments, I know how difficult it can be for them to know when the light turns green signaling pedestrians can cross. Sometimes they cannot even recognize which is the right direction for crossing the road ahead. If you want to experience what it is like for yourself, choose a safe road and try to identify when you can cross with your eyes closed.
