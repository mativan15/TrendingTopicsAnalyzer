Union flags hang above as cavalry horses are walked along Regent Street, in London, on May 30.LEON NEAL/Getty ImagesBritish supermarket groups are set to enjoy a mini-boost to trade as Britons put aside their cost of living worries for a few days and prepare to celebrate Queen Elizabeth's Platinum Jubilee with street parties and barbecues.With two public holidays on Thursday June 2 and Friday June 3 going into the weekend and decent weather forecast, demand for alcohol, soft drinks, barbecue food and ingredients for desserts is expected to soar.
