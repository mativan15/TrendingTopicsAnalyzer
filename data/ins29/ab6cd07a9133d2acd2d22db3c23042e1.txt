If you are an investor, and would like information about our investigation, please complete the Information Request Form or contact Joshua Lifshitz, Esq. by telephone at (516)493-9780 or e-mail at info@jlclasslaw.com. ManTech International Corporation (NASDAQGS: MANT) Lifshitz Law PLLC announces investigation into possible breach of fiduciary duties in connection with the sale of MANT to a private equity consortium led by The Carlyle Group Inc.
