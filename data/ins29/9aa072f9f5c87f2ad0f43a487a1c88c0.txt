October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BENTONVILLE, Ark.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Back by customer demand, Walmart is announcing the return of its month-long savings event, 'Black Friday Deals for Days.' Like the past two years, Walmart will spread the excitement of Black Friday savings over three events throughout November and cap off a month of deals with Cyber Monday, delivering customers great prices on the must-have gifts of the season.



New this year, Walmart's 'Black Friday Deals for Days' events will begin on Walmart.
