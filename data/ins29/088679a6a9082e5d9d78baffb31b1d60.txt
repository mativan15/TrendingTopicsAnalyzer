By CBS3 Staff October 19, 2022 / 7:36 PM / CBS Philadelphia Police investigating robbery at Xfinity store in Wynnefield Police investigating robbery at Xfinity store in Wynnefield 00:18 PHILADELPHIA (CBS) -- Police are investigating the robbery of an Xfinity store in the city's Wynnefield neighborhood. It happened on the morning of Oct. 9 at the store on the 4500 block of City Avenue. Police say the suspect caught on surveillance video demanded money from employees. Several thousands of dollars were handed over before the thief ran off.
