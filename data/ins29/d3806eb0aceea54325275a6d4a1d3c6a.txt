Canara Bank Q2 profit jumps 89 pc to Rs 2,525 cr
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 08:45:00

State-owned on Thursday reported an 89 per cent rise in its net profit to Rs 2,525 crore for the second quarter ended September of this fiscal. The bank had posted a net profit of Rs 1,333 crore in the same period of the previous fiscal. The total income also rose to Rs 24,932.19 crore during July-September this year against Rs 21,331.49 crore in the same period of 2020-21, Canara Bank said in a regulatory filing.
