YORK, Pa. — Get best practices for creating a YouTube Channel and compelling video content that promotes your products and services and drives engagement with your brand during a free Grow with Google session from 10 to 11 a.m. on Oct. 27. The online session, "Use YouTube to Grow Your Business — Let's talk about YouTube," is sponsored by the York LaunchBox and the Great Valley LaunchBox.
Penn State LaunchBoxes from throughout Pennsylvania joined together this fall to offer free online Grow with Google sessions through GoTo Meeting.
