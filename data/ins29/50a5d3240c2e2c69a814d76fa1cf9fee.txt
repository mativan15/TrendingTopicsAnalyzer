ANN ARBOR, Mich.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NSF, a leading public health and safety organization, has certified their first-ever company to the BeVeg Vegan certification standard. Ecuadorian Rainforest, located in Clifton, New Jersey, has received the Vegan certification for its manufacturing facility and 199 products, validating claims that they are Vegan, free from animal ingredients, and cruelty-free.
