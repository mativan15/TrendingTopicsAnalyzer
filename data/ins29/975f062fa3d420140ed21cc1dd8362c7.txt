She-Hulk Writer Explains How the Finale's Extremely Unexpected Cameo Came About

2022-10-17 10:56:14

She-Hulk: Attorney at Law was a show full of quirky Marvel references and comic book deep cuts, but its head writer has now explained how its most surprising cameo came about.Spoiler Warning: This article features spoilers for She-Hulk: Attorney at Law's first season, including its final scene.Speaking to Entertainment Weekly, She-Hulk head writer Jessica Gao explained that the last-minute appearance of Bruce Banner's son Skaar was something of a last-minute decision in itself. Though a final, out-there cameo was planned from the beginning, the writers were unable to lock any one thing in because of real-world restrictions, such as actors' schedules.
