PHILADELPHIA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Grovara, the easy global wholesale experience, was named the winner of the Technology Emerging category at the 29th Annual Enterprise Awards by the Greater Philadelphia Alliance for Capital & Technology (PACT). Grovara co-founders Abu Kamara and Peter Groverman accepted the award on behalf of the company at a ceremony on May 24 at Springfield Country Club in Springfield, Pennsylvania.
