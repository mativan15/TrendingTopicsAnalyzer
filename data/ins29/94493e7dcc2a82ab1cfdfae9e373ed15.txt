Royal-mad nan with £200k of memorabilia has framed napkin used by Prince Charles

A royal-mad grandmother from County Durham reckons she owns more than £200,000 of royal family memorabilia.

Anita Atkins, who lives on her Weardale farm with her husband John, boasts a napkin used by Prince Charles and an oil painting of the Duke of Kent in her 12,000 collection of items - and she has big plans for the upcoming Jubilee weekend.
