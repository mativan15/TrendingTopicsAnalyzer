Thomas S. Murphy attributed his success to a knack for salesmanship and good judgment.Photo: Bloomberg NewsListen to article(2 minutes)Thomas S. Murphy, a jovial Brooklyn native who liked to call people 'pal' or 'kiddo,' had a talent for making and keeping friends. One of them was Warren Buffett.In his late 20s, after he graduated from Harvard Business School, Mr. Murphy's network of pals led him to a job managing a tiny, failing UHF television station in Albany, N.Y., based in a crumbling 19th-century building that had once been a home for retired nuns.
