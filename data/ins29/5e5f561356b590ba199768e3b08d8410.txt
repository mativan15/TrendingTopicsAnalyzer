Game industry investments and acquisitions picked up in the third quarter after a slow second quarter despite economic headwinds, according to a report by Drake Star Partners. That should offer some relief to game companies that are either looking to raise money or get acquired. In Q3, the number of deals was up to 81 valued at $6 billion, compared to 70 valued at $1.
