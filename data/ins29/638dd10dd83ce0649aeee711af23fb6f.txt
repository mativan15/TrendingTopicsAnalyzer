A distraught university student has broken down into tears after being scammed out of all of the money in her bank account.Nineteen-year-old Sarah Towers took to TikTok on Thursday to share her despair after over $3000 was stolen from her last month.As a young person on minimum wage, she said that money is vital for her.'Recently I was scammed out of all my money in my bank account, which is more than $3,000,' Ms Towers said.'This money that was taken was my bills money. All of my savings, which I was saving for the end of the year, and all the money that was on my credit card which was $2000.
