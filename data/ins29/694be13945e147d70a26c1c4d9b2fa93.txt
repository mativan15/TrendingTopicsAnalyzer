Asserting the significance of developing hill states and villages located on the border, Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Friday urged all Indian tourists to set aside 5% of their travel budget to buy local products while visiting various places across the country. "This will give a big push to local products and will give immense satisfaction to you too," he said. Modi visited Uttarakhand on Friday to offer prayers at the Kedarnath and Badrinath shrines.
