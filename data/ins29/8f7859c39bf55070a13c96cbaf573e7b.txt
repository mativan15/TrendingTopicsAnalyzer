If visiting Ibrox has become a little wearisome for clubs of a certain size, somebody forgot to tell Gary Bowyer, the Dundee manager, who cannot disguise his excitement ahead of tomorrow's Premier Sports Cup quarter-final tie against Rangers.Dundee are big enough to have been regulars at Ibrox in recent years, but their last victory there was in 2001, since when they have lost all 14 games and conceded 44 goals in the process. While that kind of record can have a demoralising effect on supporters, Bowyer sees it as a thrilling, career-enhancing prospect.
