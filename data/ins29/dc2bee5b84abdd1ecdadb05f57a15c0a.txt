'IVF for bees' business hoping new owner can help make it profitable
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-10-20 16:00:00

A company that offers an IVF service for bees is hoping to find a new owner to help make it profitable. Bettabees is the country's most advanced queen bee breeder and researcher. It artificially inseminates queen bees in a process similar to in vitro fertilisation in humans. Bettabees chairman Jason Marshall said the business was now for sale and was not breeding any queen bees.
