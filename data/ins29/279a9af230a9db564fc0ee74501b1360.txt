Media caption, Watch: Watch Liz Truss step down as PMAs the UK awaits its new prime minister, a leading think tank has warned failure to take young people seriously could harm future democracy.Hannah White, from the Institute for Government, said losing young voters could risk fuelling "extreme forms of politics".Decision-makers must realise "the voice of young people... is not one voice". Her comments follow a poll for the BBC indicating just 17% of 16 to 24-year-olds felt positive about UK politics.
