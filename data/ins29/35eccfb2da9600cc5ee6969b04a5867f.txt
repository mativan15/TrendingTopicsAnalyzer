Montgomery County Police detectives say they received a tip on May 19 that Phillip Randall​ robbed the Colesville Road McDonald's. Police say they got a warrant for his arrest on May 27. (Michelle-Rotuno Johnson/Patch)GAITHERSBURG, MD — A Capitol Heights man was arrested and charged with armed robbery after Montgomery County Police received a tip that he had robbed a Gaithersburg McDonald's in May, a police news release says. Authorities say that Phillip Randall, 55, was waiting for his order at McDonald's when he went up to the cashier, pulled out a knife and demanded money.
