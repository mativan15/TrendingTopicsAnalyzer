By Reuters Published: 09:24 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 09:24 EDT, 20 October 2022 BEIJING, Oct 20 (Reuters) - China's state-owned margin financing loan provider said on Thursday it will cut brokerages' borrowing costs in a bid to promote stock investment, the latest measure by Beijing to help stabilise markets during the on-going Communist Party Congress. The China Securities Finance Co (CSF), the country's only institution that provides loans to securities firms to fund their margin lending businesses, said the rates would be cut across the board by 40 basis points.
