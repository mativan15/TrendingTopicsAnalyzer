Should You Join Costco in 2023?
fool.com
news
2022-10-20 16:00:24

These days, a lot of consumers are having difficulty keeping up with basic expenses, like housing, transportation, and utility costs. We can thank inflation for that.Worse yet, we don't know if inflation levels will creep downward in 2023 or remain high. And so if you're thinking about ways to ease that burden in the new year, you may want to focus on some of your larger expenses, like groceries.Joining Costco could be a good solution. As of now, a basic Costco membership costs $60 a year, and the warehouse club giant recently announced it has no immediate plans to raise membership fees.
