Published: 17:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 17:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 David de Gea joins Manchester United's exclusive 500 club today with the ambition of becoming one of their longest-serving players.Goalkeeper De Gea, who has been at Old Trafford since 2011, will become only the 11th player in the club's history and the first from outside the British Isles to record 500 appearances when he plays against Newcastle.The 31-year-old Spaniard is in the final year of his contract, though United have an option of an extra 12 months.
