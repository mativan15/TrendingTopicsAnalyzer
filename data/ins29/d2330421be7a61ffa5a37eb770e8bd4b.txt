TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--Titan Medical Inc. (Nasdaq: TMDI; TSX: TMD), a medical device company focused on the development and commercialization of innovative surgical technologies for single access robotic-assisted surgery (RAS), today announced, after careful consideration and a review of the company's future needs, that Perry Genova, Senior Vice President of Research and Product Development, will depart the company effective immediately.'As we transform from a technology research and development company to a company focused on regulatory clearance and commercialization, our needs continue to evolve,' said Paul Cataford, Interim President, CEO and Board Chair.
