"Combining CMS' deep national security expertise with FalconTek's proven ability to deliver highly cleared personnel will provide our combined customers access to an enriched offering of proven national security solutions." HERNDON, Va. (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 FalconTek, a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) that provides Technical and Professional support to Federal Civilian, Defense, and Intel clients, announces the acquisition of Core Mission Solutions LLC (CMS), a provider of language analysis, cultural advisory, intelligence analysis, and training services to the intelligence community.
