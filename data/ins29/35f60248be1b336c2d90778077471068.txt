Former Australian cricketer Andrew Symonds died in a car crash in his home state, northern Queensland, on May 15. Symonds was only 46 and is survived by his wife and two children. The funeral and a private memorial service took place on Friday at Riverway Stadium, which is located on the outskirts of Townsville, North Queensland. Adam Gilchrist, Michael Clarke, Jimmy Maher, Ricky Ponting, Allan Border, Darren Lehmann and Ian Healy from the former cricket fraternity attended the funeral. 
Gilchrist broke into tears as he was paying tributes to the former Australian all-rounder and two-time World Cup winner.
