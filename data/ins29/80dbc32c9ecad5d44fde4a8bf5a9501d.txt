 Wellington County OPP are investigating a fatal single-vehicle crash near Erin.
The crash happened on Winston Churchill Blvd. at Erin-Garfraxa Townline Rd. Friday at around 6:45 a.m.Emergency crews arrived to find a pick-up truck that had left the roadway and struck a tree.Investigators say the two occupants were taken to hospital. The passenger, a 76-year-old from Erin, was pronounced dead in hospital.
