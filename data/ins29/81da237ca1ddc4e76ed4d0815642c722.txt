Not Supplied Credit: istockDaniela PizziraniNCA NewsWireA Queensland man is accused of posing as a yoga teacher before allegedly sexually assaulting two women.Police will allege the 28-year-old man visited a business in Mackay on April 21 and pretended to be a yoga instructor before he sexually assaulted the women.He has since been arrested and charged two counts of sexual assault.Detectives believe there may be other alleged victims and are appealing for people to come forward to police.Police will address the media later on Thursday with more information.
