Tencent Games partners with Globe Telecom, Swarmio to launch gaming tournaments

2022-10-18 11:27:26

David Becker/Getty Images Entertainment Tencent Games (OTCPK:TCEHY) subsidiary Level Infinite has collaborated with Swarmio Media (OTCQB:SWMIF) and Globe Telecom (OTCPK:GTMEF) to launch two exclusive gaming tournaments in the Philippines. Throughout early October, PUBG MOBILE players across the Philippines were invited to register to compete in the PUBG MOBILE Philippine City Tournament and the Gamer Grounds Championship exclusively inside the Globe Gamer Grounds platform, developed by Swarmio. A total of 169 teams registered for the PUBG MOBILE Philippine City Tournament, which kicked off with Open Qualifiers on Oct 15, 2022 and is being live-streamed on YouTube, TikTok and Meta.
