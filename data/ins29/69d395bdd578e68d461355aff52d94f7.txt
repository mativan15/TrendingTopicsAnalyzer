Seven dead in helicopter crash in Kedarnath
sumanasa.com
news
2022-10-18 07:44:14

Seven people, including the pilot, died after a helicopter ferrying pilgrims back from Kedarnath shrine crashed midway, before reaching the base station, near Garud Chatti on October 18.A team of the Aircraft Accident Investigation Bureau (AAIB) will be probing the helicopter crash near Kedarnath in Uttarakhand that killed seven pilgrims on Tuesday, according to a senior official.It is an accident as per the classification of the International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO), and AAIB will be probing the case, the official added.
