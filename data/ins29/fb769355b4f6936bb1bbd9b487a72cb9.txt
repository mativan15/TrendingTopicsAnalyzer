State's unemployment rate steady at 2.6%
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-21 17:22:00

Alabama's seasonally adjusted unemployment rate remained steady at 2.6% in September.

Alabama's seasonally adjusted unemployment rate was 3.2% in September 2021. On a seasonally adjusted basis this September the state's labor force participation rate was also flat at 57.2%. The civilian labor force grew by less than 1,000 to 2,294,506 and total employment remained virtually flat at roughly 2,235,000.

The non-seasonally adjusted unemployment rate in the Birmingham metro fell 0.2% to 2.4% this September.
