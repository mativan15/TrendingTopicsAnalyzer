October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--M-Files, a global leader in information management, today unveiled the company's new partner webinar series to support the fast-growing M-Files partner program. The partner program webinars, which run from October 2022 to May 2023, nurture relationships and offer enhanced technical support and sales enablement for M-Files reseller and solution provider partners. With its software-as-a-service (SaaS) business model, M-Files helps partners drive annual recurring revenue (ARR) and achieve a multiplier effect for partner services revenue.
