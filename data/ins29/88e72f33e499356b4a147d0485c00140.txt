REUTERS/Yves Herman REFILE - CORRECTING DATEPRAGUE, Oct 19 (Reuters) - The Czech government favours applying a planned windfall tax on profits in the energy and banking sectors from 2023, Finance Minister Zbynek Stanjura said on Wednesday, although taxing earnings this year was also under consideration.Czech markets were jolted on Wednesday on news the government could widen its proposed tax on excessive profits to include 2022 earnings, earlier than existing plans applying only to 2023-2025.
