MUMBAI, India, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SVKM's NMIMS Shobhaben Pratapbhai School of Pharmacy & Technology Management (SPPSPTM) has surpassed the campus recruitment record for its current batch of students with several of them securing job offers with leading corporates in India.
SPPSPTM, an arm of the Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS), a Deemed-to-be University with an academic legacy of 41 years, has emerged as one of the country's premier institutes for providing education for integrated Pharm Tech & management programs.
