A week after plastering from the ceiling of classrooms at the Government Middle School in Arakkonam town peeled off, the Arakkonam Municipality has not floated a tender to rebuild the damaged classrooms due to procedural delays.Classes for students of Classes 6 and 7 have been held at a temporary hall near the campus since October 14, when the two classrooms were damaged.
