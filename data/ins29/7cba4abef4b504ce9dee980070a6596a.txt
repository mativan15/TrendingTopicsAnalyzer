New Harry Potter anniversary coins will pay double royal tribute
msn.com
news
2022-10-19 20:43:00

New collectors' coins, marking 25 years since the first Harry Potter novel was published, will also pay a double royal tribute. 
 

 © PA 

The new Harry Potter coin collection from the Royal Mint will feature portraits of both Queen Elizabeth II and King Charles III.It is 25 years since the novel Harry Potter And The Philosopher's Stone by JK Rowling was first published in the UK in 1997 by Bloomsbury Publishing. 
 

 © PA 

The Royal Mint is expecting high demand for the coins and said they would be one of "a small number" that will see a change of royal portrait during the series.
