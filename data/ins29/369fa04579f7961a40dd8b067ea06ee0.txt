Largest cryptocurrencies fall as Litecoin drops
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-26 14:00:00

All of the largest cryptocurrencies were down during morning trading on Thursday, with Litecoin LTCUSD, -7.06% seeing the biggest change, tumbling 8.41% to $62.83. Uniswap UNIUSD, -7.76% declined 7.92% to $5.07, and Ethereum ETHUSD, -6.74% dropped 7.50% to $1,812.20. Polkadot DOTUSD, -6.64% dropped 7.50% to $9.24 on Thursday, while Dogecoin DOGEUSD, -6.13% shed 6.62% to 8 cents and Cardano ADAUSD, -5.96% fell 6.84% to 48 cents. Bitcoin Cash BCHUSD, -4.88% and Ripple XRPUSD, -3.62% fell 6.16% to $180.
