Ghaziabad: The Ghaziabad Development Authority (GDA) will soon finalise a zonal plan for special areas along the Regional Rapid Transit System (RRTS) corridor, sources said on Monday. Officials said that the zonal plan for the RRTS project is getting prepared in line with the new transit-oriented development (TOD) policy, which was recently rolled out by the Uttar Pradesh government. The 82-kilometre-long RRTS project is proposed to link the three cities of Delhi, Ghaziabad and Meerut with a high-speed train network having 25 stations and 22 of these fall under the jurisdiction of the state of UP.
