NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kaplan Fox & Kilsheimer LLP ( www.kaplanfox.com ) is investigating claims on behalf of investors of Opendoor Technologies, Inc. ('Opendoor' or the "Company') (NASDAQ: OPEN). A class action complaint has been filed on behalf of investors that purchased or otherwise acquired Opendoor securities between December 21, 2020 and September 16, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'); and/or Opendoor common stock pursuant and/or traceable to the Offering Documents issued in connection with the business combination between the Company and Opendoor Labs Inc.
