ReutersGmail has a feature known as spam box. As the name suggests, it is a dedicated space in your inbox where unimportant and promotional emails are received. Spam box ensures that you don't have to waste your time going through or managing emails that are of no value. However, there are times when important emails, too, find their way into the spam box. These days, most people face this situation and miss out on receiving important personal or work-related messages because of it. Many individuals have lost business and important opportunities as a result of this.
