 RCMP hit the streets of downtown Penticton over the May long weekend with an 'eagle eye' for possible opportunities for crime.
Penticton RCMP Cst. Dayne Lyons handed out 22 crime prevention notices on vehicle windshields warning drivers to be more aware of what they are leaving behind in their cars.'The biggest thing I saw was bags, money, or phones left in view,' said Cst.
