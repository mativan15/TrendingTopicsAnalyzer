The Maharashtra Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) has arrested four alleged members of the banned Popular Front of India (PFI) from Panvel in the Raigad district for holding meetings for the expansion of organisational committees and appointment of office bearers to replace functionaries held last month.An officer said the local unit secretary of the banned outfit and two other members from Panvel organised the meeting on Wednesday.
