Allegiant World Mastercard® Is Top Ranked For Fourth Consecutive Year LAS VEGAS, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Allegiant Travel Company (NASDAQ: ALGT) today announced its Allegiant World Mastercard® and Allegiant Allways Rewards® were voted as the No. 1 Best Airline Credit Card and Best Frequent Flyer Program in USA Today's 10Best 2022 Loyalty/Rewards Readers' Choice Awards.After four weeks of public voting, Allegiant's credit card and frequent flyer program received the top spots over 20 other airlines.
