A PREM club have axed a long-serving member of their catering staff following a complaint from a WAG.
The popular elderly woman was dismissed after claims she made a comment that was overheard about a player's partner.
The popular catering staff member has been dismissed following the complaintCredit: Getty
Now she has left and was missed at a recent home game when surprised regulars noticed her absence.
The other players at the club are shocked by the development, especially those who have been with the outfit for years.
