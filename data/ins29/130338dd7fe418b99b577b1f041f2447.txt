Boy, 13, who broke arm on bike ride dies after surgery under general anaesthetic

2022-06-02 10:40:10

Jake Wilde, 13, fractured his arm during a bike ride with his siblings and underwent a "simple procedure" but died soon afterwards due to an undiagnosed muscle diseaseJake Wilde underwent surgery after his parents took him to hospital with a fractured arm (stock image) A 13-year-old boy who broke his arm during a bike ride died after undergoing a routine surgical procedure because he was given general anaesthetic. Following his death, Devon teenager Jake Wilde was diagnosed with a muscle disease that increases the risk of danger when given the drug.
