GDS to Hold Annual General Meeting on June 30, 2022

SHANGHAI, China, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- GDS Holdings Limited ('GDS Holdings,' 'GDS' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: GDS; HKEX: 9698), a leading developer and operator of high-performance data centers in China, today announced that it will hold its 2022 Annual General Meeting of Shareholders (the 'AGM') at Beijing Meeting Room, F5, Building C, Sunland International, No. 999 Zhouhai Road, Pudong, Shanghai, P.R.C. at 4:00 p.m. (China Standard Time) on June 30, 2022 (which is 4:00 a.m. (Eastern Daylight Time) on June 30, 2022).
