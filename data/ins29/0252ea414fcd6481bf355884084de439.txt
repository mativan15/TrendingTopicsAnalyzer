The QAnon conspiracy theorist Philip Godlewski began working with the merchandise startup Spring last year. He quickly generated a healthy business and was assigned a dedicated "partner manager."At the time, The Daily Beast had recently reported that Godlewski admitted to corrupting a minor.It didn't take long for employees to start talking. Rather than using the company's Slack account, they sent texts to one another expressing their concerns over his shop."It's bad," one former employee wrote to their colleagues, according to a screenshot viewed by Insider.
