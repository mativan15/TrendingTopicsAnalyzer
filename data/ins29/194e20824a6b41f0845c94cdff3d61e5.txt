SAN JOSE, Calif. (AP) _ NetApp Inc. (NTAP) on Wednesday reported fiscal fourth-quarter net income of $259 million.The San Jose, California-based company said it had net income of $1.14 per share. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, were $1.42 per share.The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of nine analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.27 per share.The data storage company posted revenue of $1.68 billion in the period, which matched Street forecasts.
