By Reuters Published: 15:49 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 15:49 EDT, 20 October 2022 LONDON, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Arsenal qualified for the Europa League knockout stage with two group games to spare after Granit Xhaka's second-half goal secured a 1-0 win over PSV Eindhoven at the Emirates Stadium on Thursday. Victory in the rescheduled A match, postponed last month following the death of Queen Elizabeth, means Arsenal will finish in the top two in Group A having maintained their perfect start after four games.
