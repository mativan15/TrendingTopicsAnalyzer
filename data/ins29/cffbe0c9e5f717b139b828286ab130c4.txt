NEW DELHI: The Special CBI court on Friday dismissed the bail applications of Congress MP Karti Chidambaram and two other, in a money laundering case.The CBI had registered a case against Karti for allegedly facilitating visas of 263 Chinese nationals for a power company after receiving Rs 50 lakh as bribe. According to officials, it was done 11 years ago when his father, P Chidambaram , was the Union home minister.The Special CBI court had earlier reserved its order on the anticipatory bail applications by Karti, S Bhaskar Raman and Vikas Makharia in the case.
