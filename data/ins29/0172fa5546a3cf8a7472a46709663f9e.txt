Jax-based staffing tech firm acquires workforce management company
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 18:42:00

Jacksonville-based Beeline, a vendor management system provider, announced Wednesday it acquired Utmost, a firm that specializes in providing clients with insights into their workforces.

With Utmost, Beeline will enable companies to access talent insights across their workforce of employees and non-employees and will advance its vision of bringing solutions that tailor to the needs of every participant in the extended workforce process under one platform, according to a statement from the company.
