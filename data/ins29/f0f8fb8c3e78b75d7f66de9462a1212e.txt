Prostate Cancer Awareness Month: Supporting Organizations
healthline.com

2022-06-01 00:55:00

More than 3.1 million Americans are living with prostate cancer, a condition that affects more than 1 in 8 males in their lifetime. A cancer diagnosis can be scary, but it's important to remember that you are not alone.September is Prostate Cancer Awareness Month, a time to reflect on the effects of the disease, the progress that has been made in research, and the future of prostate cancer treatment. It's also a time to connect with other people affected by prostate cancer and build a community that celebrates survivors.
