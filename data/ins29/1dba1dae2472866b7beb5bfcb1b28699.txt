(League of Women Voters)MIDDLETOWN, NJ — Aging school buildings, dwindling state aid, academic learning loss due to the pandemic and mental health/bullying were all top topics addressed in a Middletown school board virtual candidates' forum.The virtual forum was held this past Monday night, and was sponsored by the League of Women Voters. Sitting Board member Leonora Lacqua-Caminiti did not attend, and neither did Joseph Fitzgerald, who said he had a prior work obligation.
