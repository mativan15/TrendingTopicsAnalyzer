English singer Sam Fender has scheduled Irish headlining gigs for June 25, 2023 at Malahide Castle in Dublin and June 23, 2023 at Ormeau Park in Belfast. The two outdoor gigs will follow Sam Fender's two sold-out dates at St. James' Park in Newcastle. Sam Fender's, 2022 show was a hit with having sold out 45,000 tickets making it a July 2022 headline at London's 40,000-capacity Finsbury Park. Sam Fender has unwittingly become one of Britain's most recognized and successful artists of the generation.
