For a burglar looking to raise some quick cash, the collection pot of a group of elderly nuns may have seemed an easy target.She did not account, however, for the intervention of an octogenarian sister, who wrestled the thief to the ground and raised the alarm before she could run off with the money.The attempted burglary took place on Monday afternoon at the Convent of Saint Clare in Palencia, a city in northern Spain, as the nuns gathered to pray.The intruder is reportedly known to the nuns of Saint ClareALAMYThe 87-year-old nun, who has not been named, noticed a suspicious outsider, a woman in her forties, entering a separate room.
