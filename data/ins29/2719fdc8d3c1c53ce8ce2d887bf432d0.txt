SINGAPORE, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Bonanza Goldfields Corp. (OTC:BONZ) is pleased to announce that Marvion has entered into a non-binding MOU with Hong Kong listed China Information Technology Development Limited (8178: HK).  
As part of this agreement, Marvion has expressed interest to invest up to 4.9% of CITD by way of a share swap.  In exchange, CITD will provide AI technology services to Marvion with the objective of further enhancing Marvion's product offerings in the media and entertainment space – including Marvion Metaverse and Marvion MetaStudio.
