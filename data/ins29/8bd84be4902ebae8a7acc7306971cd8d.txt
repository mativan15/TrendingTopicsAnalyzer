Amber Heard to appeal against US libel verdict as Johnny Depp celebrates $15m win in Newcastle pub

2022-06-02 09:51:44

Amber Heard has said she will appeal against the verdict in the multimillion-dollar defamation lawsuit brought in the US by her ex-husband Johnny Depp.Heard, 36, said she was "heartbroken" and felt "disappointment beyond words" after Depp was awarded $15m (£11.9m) by a court in Virginia on Wednesday.
Sources close to the actress, who won $2m (£1.6m) in her countersuit against Depp, 58, have told NBC News that she will appeal against the verdict. No additional details were provided.Depp was awarded $10m in compensatory damages and $5m in punitive damages over a first-person article by Heard in The Washington Post in 2018 in which she claimed she was a survivor of domestic abuse.
