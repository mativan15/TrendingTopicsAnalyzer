Rafael Nadal defeats Novak Djokovic in late-night French Open epic

news
2022-06-01 00:05:08

Rafael Nadal battled to a late-night victory over old foe Novak Djokovic to reach the semi-final of the French Open.The Spaniard, looking for a 14th Roland Garros title, hustled his way to a 6-2 4-6 6-2 7-6 (4) win over the defending champion and world number one, in an enthralling showdown lasting four hours and 12 minutes.Epic and entertaining as it was, the match finished at 1.15am local time in temperatures of 12C, again calling into question the wisdom of tournament chiefs starting their night sessions at 8.
