BNP Paribas gets approval for asset management venture in China (OTCMKTS:BNPQF)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-18 13:26:04

Chinese authorities have approved BNP Paribas's (OTCQX:BNPQF) (OTCQX:BNPQY) (OTCPK:BNPZY) request to form an asset management venture with the Agricultural Bank of China (OTCPK:ACGBY) (OTCPK:ACGBF), Reuters reported Tuesday. The China Banking and Insurance Regulatory Commission granted initial approval to allow the two companies to form a joint venture, Reuters said, citing an exchange filing made by AgBank. BNP Paribas (OTCQX:BNPQF) will own 51% of the new venture, while AgBank will hold the rest.
