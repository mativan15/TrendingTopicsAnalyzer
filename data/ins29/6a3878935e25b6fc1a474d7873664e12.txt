Former prime minister Boris Johnson landed back in Britain on Saturday after cutting short a Caribbean holiday to enter the race to replace Liz Truss as Conservative leader, UK media reported.It would mark an audacious attempt to return to power by Johnson, who only left office early last month, after dozens of resignations from his government following a slew of scandals led to his departure.
