A rail freight rolling stock repair facility was damaged yesterday by airstrikes on KyivCHRISTOPHER FURLONG/GETTY IMAGESFor the first time in almost three months of warfare, Russian airstrikes have again targeted Kyiv. The Ukrainian capital was hit yesterday by missiles that destroyed tanks and pounded railway facilities, as President Putin cynically announced retaliation for American proposals to deliver multiple-launch rocket systems to Ukraine. One Russian missile buzzed a nuclear plant to the south of the capital in a reckless attempt at nuclear intimidation.
