Kyle Sinckler has been recalled by England as Eddie Jones named a 36-strong squad for the autumn internationals which also included a first call-up for Quins wing Cadan Murley but no place for playmaker Henry Slade.As well as Sinckler, Eddie Jones has welcomed back scrum-half Raffi Quirke, threequarter Max Malins and prop Val Rapava Ruskin for the autumn campaign.Red Roses hold off France in bruising Rugby World Cup showdownThe England tighthead prop was left behind to recover from a back injury during the summer tour to Australia and was not named in a training squad in September but was rewarded by Jones for his return.
