Second Glance is closed Monday and Tuesday. During that time, staff create themed displays of furniture and accessories and promote them on social media. When the shop opens on Wednesday morning at 9 a.m., customers pour in and scoop up vintage furniture along with fun accessories from lamps to tapestries.All the merchandise is donated and the proceeds support The Open Door community food resource center that operates pantries in Gloucester and Ipswich.'It is a fun place to shop,' said Barbara Kelly of Gloucester, who was excited to be on vacation and able to join the early birds.
