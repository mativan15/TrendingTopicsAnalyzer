ScreenDaily It is the debut feature from Jon Bell.UK sales outfit Bankside Films has acquired Aboriginal Australian filmmaker Jon Bell's debut, psychological horror The Moogai. It is produced by Australian outfit Causeway Films and based on a screenplay by Bell.A young Aboriginal couple brings home their second baby. What should be a joyous time takes a sinister turn, as the baby's mother starts seeing a malevolent spirit she is convinced is trying to take her baby.
