Ghana Traders Protest Inflation by Closing Shops for Three Days
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-18 18:46:47

(Bloomberg) -- Ghanaian shop owners are closing their businesses for three days to protest spiraling inflation.Members of the Ghana Union of Traders Association will suspend commercial activities in the capital, Accra, from Wednesday to show their 'frustration' over the weakening currency, surging inflation and high lending rates, Joseph Obeng, the union's president, said by phone.'We want to give the government the message that, whatever solution they are trying to bring to bear, they should do so with a sense of urgency,' said Obeng, who represents sellers of food, clothes, electrical parts and other goods.
