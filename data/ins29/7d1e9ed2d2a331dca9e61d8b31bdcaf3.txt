June 02, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK & SANTA BARBARA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--LogicMonitor, the leading SaaS-based unified observability platform for enterprises and managed service providers (MSPs), today released a framework that IT teams and CIOs can leverage to evaluate their readiness determining their ability to deliver the necessary resiliency and continuity required for today's digital businesses. The company announced the availability of the framework as part of the presentation at LM Elevate 2022, LogicMonitor's annual in-person conference for IT professionals and technologists, being held at The Glasshouse, June 2 in New York City.
