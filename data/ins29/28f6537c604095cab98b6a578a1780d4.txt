By Reuters Published: 12:00 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 12:00 EDT, 15 October 2022 Tennessee wide receiver Cedric Tillman and safety Jaylen McCollough will miss Saturday's Top 10 showdown with visiting Alabama, ESPN reported. The No. 6 Volunteers (5-0, 2-0 Southeastern Conference) are looking for their first win against the Crimson Tide (6-0, 3-0) since 2006. Tillman practiced on a limited basis this week but hasn't played since injuring his ankle in a Sept. 17 victory over Akron. Tillman has 17 catches for 246 yards and one touchdown this season.
