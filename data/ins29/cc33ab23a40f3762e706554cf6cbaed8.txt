[Photo/Xinhua] BEIJING -- With the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China opening on Sunday, the world is paying close attention to the programs of action and overarching policies that will be formulated during the landmark event. When delivering a key report to the CPC congress, Xi Jinping said China has always been committed to its foreign policy goals of upholding world peace and promoting common development, reiterating the country's dedication to promoting a human community with a shared future.
