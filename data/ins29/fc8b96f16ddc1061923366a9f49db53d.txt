15 U.P. CHCs to run under PPP model
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-17 19:27:45

The state government has decided to run over a dozen community health centres (CHCs) under public-private-partnership (PPP) model.On Monday, deputy chief minister Brajesh Pathak said, 'Fifteen CHCs in the state have been selected first to run on the PPP model. This will be a major step towards improving healthcare services in the state.'This is the initial phase of the scheme and will be expanded in the future.Pathak, who also holds the health portfolio, said, 'Orders have been issued to health officials to execute the process for these CHCs.
