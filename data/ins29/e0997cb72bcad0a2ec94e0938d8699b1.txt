June 01, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Colgate-Palmolive (NYSE:CL) Chief Investor Relations Officer and SVP, M&A, John Faucher, will participate in a virtual fireside chat at the Evercore ISI Consumer & Retail Conference on Tuesday, June 7, 2022 at 11:00 am ET.

Investors may access a live webcast of this fireside chat on Colgate's website at www.colgatepalmolive.com. For those unable to participate during the live webcast, a recorded version of the webcast will be made available through the Investor Center section of Colgate's website.
