NAGPUR: In an attempt to please tourists, a mahout in Kanha Tiger Reserve literally pushed a tiger out of its den and chased the big cat during a Sunday morning safari in Kisli.A video of an annoyed Supkhar male tiger showing its fangs at tourist vehicles standing close-by is in possession of TOI. The video clearly shows a mahout sitting on an elephant chasing the tiger.According to park sources, a racket is being run with the help of a tour operator to have good and regular tiger sightings.'The operator from Pune, who runs a firm, is hand-in-glove with a section of guides, mahouts, and safari vehicle owners and even some staff,' said guides and vehicle owners.
