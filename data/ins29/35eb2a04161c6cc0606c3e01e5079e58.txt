Halsey is launching their own radio show, For The Record With Halsey, as part of Amazon's new Amp radio app. The show, which premieres tomorrow (October 17) will showcase all facets of Halsey, from their interest in politics and cooking to parents and of course music. But as I found when speaking with Halsey this week, they are more excited to show off their interests outside of music.
