(PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Titan CEO and headline sponsor Wipfli LLP are pleased to announce Patrick Booth as a 2023 Wisconsin Titan 100. The Titan 100 program recognizes Wisconsin's Top 100 CEO's & C-level executives. They are the area's most accomplished business leaders in their industry using criteria that includes demonstrating exceptional leadership, vision, and passion.
