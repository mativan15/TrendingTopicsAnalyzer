NewsCity Newsvadodara NewsKids to give neglected statues a spring cleaningVadodara: Finally, the ‘freedom fighters and ilk' languishing in city's prominent circles will get a bath. They badly needed one that has been pending for ages now. But thanks to city's children, Barodians will be able to move around cleaner statues that are veiled not just by dust and pigeon poop, but serious apathy on part of the authorities too.After nearly a month-long exercise, a city-based group of kids — children empowerment forum (chief) — has launched a drive for upkeep of statues by roping in individuals, complexes, business establishments and business associations with the support of Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC).
