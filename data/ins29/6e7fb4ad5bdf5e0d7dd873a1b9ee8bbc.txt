[Note: Walmart's WMT Fiscal Year 2023 ends on January 31, 2023] After a 9% decline year-to-date, at the current price of around $131 per share, we believe Walmart stock (NYSE: WMT), the world's largest retailer by revenue - is fairly priced. WMT stock has declined from around $145 to $131 YTD, outperforming the broader indices, with the S&P falling about 23% over the same period.
