Utah Jazz at Timberwolves

7 p.m. Friday, Target Center

TV, radio: BSN, 830-AM

Game preview: Rookie C Walker Kessler had a double-double (12 points, 10 rebounds) as the Jazz opened with a 123-102 victory over Denver on Wednesday. Kessler was the Wolves' first-round pick (22nd overall) and went to the Jazz in the Rudy Gobert trade. Of the other players in that trade, F Jarred Vanderbilt (12 rebounds) was in the starting lineup and G Malik Beasley (15 points) came off the bench. ... After a 49-33 season, the rebuilding Jazz are 28th in the NBA.
