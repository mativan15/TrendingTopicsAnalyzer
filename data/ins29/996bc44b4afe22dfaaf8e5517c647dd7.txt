About The Author : Lara Korte

Lara Korte covers state politics and is a co-author of POLITICO California Playbook. She previously covered the Capitol for The Sacramento Bee, and prior to moving to California covered higher education in Austin, Texas.
A native of Wichita, Kansas, Lara first got her start in journalism when a high school English teacher told her she was bad at writing fiction and should try non-fiction instead. She is a graduate of the University of Kansas, where she was editor-in-chief of the University Daily Kansan.
