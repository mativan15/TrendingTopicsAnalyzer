REUTERS/Rebecca CookDETROIT, Oct 19 (Reuters) - U.S. legislators need to give automakers operating in the United States more time to achieve the required sourcing levels of battery minerals used in electric vehicles to qualify for federal tax incentives, several industry executives said on Wednesday.
