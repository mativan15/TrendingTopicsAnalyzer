Penguins development team seeking changes for Lower Hill plan reopens some wounds
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 14:50:00

The Pittsburgh Penguins and its development partner, Buccini/Pollin Group faced fresh rancor from members of the Hill District community during a Development Activities Meeting on Monday night and sparred a bit with the leader of the Hill Community Development Corp.

With the new FNB Financial Center now under construction, the Penguins development team is seeking to make some revisions to the Preliminary Land Development Plan for the 28-acre site in the Lower Hill as it works to move forward to build two key projects of Lot E.
