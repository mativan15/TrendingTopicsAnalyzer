Morsa Images/E+ via Getty Images Earlier this month, I wrote about the STEPN app (GMT-USD) and its governance token GMT. The app's core function is advertised as a play-to-earn lifestyle game that pays people for getting outside and exercising either by walking, jogging, or running. On the surface, it's a cool idea. Despite liking the theory behind the app and by extension the potential utility of the token, I was unable to get into the app to try it out myself in time for my Seeking Alpha analysis.
