Cenovus to restart West White Rose oilfield project next year
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-05-31 10:42:05

Cenovus Energy Inc and its partners have agreed to restart the West White Rose project offshore Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada in 2023, the oil and gas producer said on Tuesday.The West White Rose project is expected to add 14 years of production to the White Rose oilfield and is about 65% complete.Cenovus, one of Canada's largest producers, said the first oil from the platform is anticipated in the first half of 2026. Peak production is expected to reach about 80,000 barrels per day (bbls/d), 45,000 bbls/d net to Cenovus, by year-end 2029.
