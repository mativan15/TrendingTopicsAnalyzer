SHAREHOLDER ALERT: Weiss Law Investigates CatchMark Timber Trust, Inc.

2022-05-31 22:11:00

NEW YORK, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Weiss Law is investigating possible breaches of fiduciary duty and other violations of law by the board of directors of CatchMark Timber Trust, Inc. ("CatchMark" or the "Company") (NYSE: CTT) in connection with the proposed acquisition of the Company by PotlatchDeltic ("PotlatchDeltic") Corporation (NASDAQ: PCH). Under the terms of the merger agreement, the Company's shareholders will receive 0.23 common shares of PotlatchDeltic stock for each common share of CatchMark owned, representing implied per-share merger consideration of approximately $12.
