(AP Photo/John Hefti)John Hefti/Associated PressThe Astros didn't wait for Yordan Alvarez to even get to arbitration, they made a move to secure his rights for the long-term. The Astros and Alvarez agreed to a six-year contract extension worth $115 million Friday.The deal, which was first reported by ESPN's Jeff Passan, means Alvarez won't become a free agent until after the 2028 season.
