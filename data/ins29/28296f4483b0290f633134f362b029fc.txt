Published: 06:48 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 06:50 EDT, 21 October 2022 A 70-year-old 'recluse' who was living without electricity or gas has left a surprise windfall of £250,000 to 31 relatives he didn't know about. Richard Ticehurst was sadly found dead in his dilapidated terraced house - where the top floor ceilings had collapsed - in Eastbourne, East Sussex last February without a Will and with little known about his family.The local authority was tasked to arrange his funeral which is when genealogist firm Finders International - stars of BBC's Heir Hunters - was brought in to research Richard's family and background.
