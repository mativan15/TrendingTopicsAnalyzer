Remove archaic laws, ensure speedy justice, says PM Modi
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 17:00:00

ANIModi said that in the last 8 years, India has repealed more than one and a half thousand archaic law.Prime Minister Narendra Modi has emphasised on weeding out archaic, orthodox laws that have become obsolete and ensuring speedy and dependable justice to the common people. Addressing the All-India Conference of Law Ministers and Law Secretaries virtually on Saturday, he also suggested implementation of innovative schemes like evening courts to reduce pendency of cases. "When the delivery of justice is seen, citizen's confidence in the constitutional institutions are strengthened and when justice is delivered, the confidence of the common citizenry goes up equally," Modi said.
