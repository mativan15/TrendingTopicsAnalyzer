Liz Truss' resignation has sparked an Emma Raducanu joke (Image: Getty)Following Liz Truss' resignation, the jokes and comparisons to her 44 days in office have been pouring in and the tennis world has been getting involved. With Emma Raducanu's reputation for axing her coaches in quick succession, one fan has pointed out that Truss' time as Prime Minister was shorter than some of the Brit's coaching relationships.Raducanu parted ways with her fourth coach in the space of 14 months last week as her partnership with Dmitry Tursunov came to an end.
