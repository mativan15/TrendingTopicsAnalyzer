888 Holdings 3Q Revenue Fell Following Netherlands Exit, Increased UK Player Safety Measures
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 06:31:00

By Kyle Morris 888 Holdings PLC said Tuesday that third-quarter revenue fell on a pro forma basis following the acquisition of the international business of William Hill, primarily driven by enhanced U.K. online player safety measures and the closure of the Netherlands business. The London-listed online betting and gaming company said that revenue on a pro forma basis for the quarter was 449 million pounds ($510 million) compared with GBP484 million a year prior. Retail revenue was GBP124 million, stable on year, and online revenue was GBP325 million, down 10%.
