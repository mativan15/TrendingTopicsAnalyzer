Telus Business rolls out online platform to celebrate Small Business Month (NYSE:TU)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-18 12:21:03

Telus Business (NYSE:TU) has introduced a new online platform to mark Small Business Month this October.
ShopWithOwners.ca will help support business owners across Canada with gaining exposure to customers looking for local finds and gifts during the holiday season. Customers will be able to search by location, keywords and categories to discover small businesses they can easily support within their community.
