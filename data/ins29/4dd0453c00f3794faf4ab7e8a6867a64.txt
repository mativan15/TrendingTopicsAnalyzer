Published: 01:42 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 01:42 EDT, 31 May 2022 Nancy Pelosi's husband Paul had been at a dinner party hosted by a friend on Saturday night and was driving a short distance home when he crashed, it emerged on Monday.Paul Pelosi, 82, was driving his Porsche drunk and pulled out onto the  highway into the path of an 48-year-old man driving a Jeep in Napa, his arrest report reveals. Photos from the scene, obtained by ABC News, showed that the vehicles crashed into a wooden fence.
