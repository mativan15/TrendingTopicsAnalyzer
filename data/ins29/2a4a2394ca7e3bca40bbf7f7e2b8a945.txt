Tecum's new TEAM strategy acquires Ohio-based Basement Doctor
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 10:01:00

Tecum Capital Management, one of Pittsburgh's biggest private equity firms on Thursday announced the first platform investment of its equity control strategy.

Tecum Equity Alpha Management (TEAM) has acquired J&D Home Improvement, which does business as the Basement Doctor and is based in Reynoldsburg, Ohio, near Columbus.

Terms were not disclosed.

Tecum, based in Wexford, works with family offices and other institutional investors via TEAM to invest in lower middle market companies, taking a control position of at least 51% ownership.
