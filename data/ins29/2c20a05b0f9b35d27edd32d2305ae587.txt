Republican candidates for Will County District 4 talk of ending gas tax, directing marijuana tax revenue to public safety
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-05-30 10:30:00

Four candidates seeking the Republican nomination for two Will County Board seats say they are interested in lowering taxes, repealing the gas tax and being conservative fiduciaries.Incumbent Stephen Balich along with Anthony Granata, James Richmond and David Molinari will face off in the June 28 GOP primary in the 4th District, which includes all or portions of Homer Glen, Mokena, Lockport and the Will County areas of Orland Park and Tinley Park.Balich has served on nearly all of the county board committees except for the Executive and Diversity and Inclusion committees.
