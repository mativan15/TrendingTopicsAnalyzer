Making it easy to take the first step on business sustainability
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-10-16 16:00:00

Once just a corporate buzzword, sustainability has now cemented its place in the mainstream business agenda. No longer considered a 'nice to have', it has become a critical cornerstone of everyday business operations. Today, the majority of the world's big brands and corporations employ skilled sustainability teams to navigate the journey. But for many of New Zealand's SMEs (small and medium businesses), embracing sustainability is more of a challenge. They know they need to start, but lack the time, knowledge and resources to take the first step.
