Business Aviation Inflight Broadband Service Plans Enhanced by Inmarsat as Part of Jet ConneX Evolution Programme

Existing service plans upgraded at no cost, first new high-end service plan goes live as part of ground-breaking programme to provide even greater performance and value

LONDON, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Inmarsat is transforming the service plans for its Jet ConneX (JX) inflight broadband solution to provide customers with even greater performance, speeds and value for money.

The updates are being introduced as part of Inmarsat's ground-breaking JX Evolution programme, which was first unveiled earlier this year and follows an extensive assessment of passenger usage trends.
