Pakistan: Dozens of rotting corpses found at rooftop of hospital, probe ordered

Oct 15, 2022, 11:11AM IST

Source: TOI.in

Dozens of rotting corpses were found on the roof of a hospital in Multan city of Pakistan. Hundreds of human body parts recovered from the roof of a hospital mortuary. No confirmation or denial from authorities on the number of bodies. The hospital said these bodies were used by the medical students. Vultures and worms were scavenging on the corpses on the roof said a govt official. The government has ordered a probe into the incident.
