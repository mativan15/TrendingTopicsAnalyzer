Twitter reacts to Auburn losing to Ole Miss
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-15 18:15:33

Auburn suffered its third straight and fourth overall loss of the season against Ole Miss Saturday.Auburn fell behind 21-0 but stormed back before ultimately losing 48-34 to the Rebels.However, the Tigers were unable to stop the Rebel's ground game, allowing 448 yards rushing and causing the doubt surrounding Bryan Harsin's future at Auburn to continue to build heading into the bye week.While the Auburn fans are becoming accustomed to losses under Harsin, they still took to Twitter to air out their grievances with the game.
