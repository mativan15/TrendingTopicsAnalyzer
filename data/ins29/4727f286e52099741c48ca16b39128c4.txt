Bronze medal for Doolan at half marathon championships
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-10-19 20:36:06

ATHLETICS: Nelson Doolan won the bronze medal in the men's under-20 grade of the Athletics New Zealand half marathon championships in Cambridge last Sunday. He was 10th overall, crossing the line in 1 hour 15 minutes 46 seconds. David Lovelock was the 32nd finisher in 1hr 24min 3sec, which placed him fifth in the M35 grade. ♦ Congratulation to the Manawatū Secondary Schools Sports Awards finalists and it is great to see athletics so well recognised, with Kimberley Walsh and Emma Ferguson named as female finalists and Braxton Kauri and Forbes Kennedy named in the male awards.
