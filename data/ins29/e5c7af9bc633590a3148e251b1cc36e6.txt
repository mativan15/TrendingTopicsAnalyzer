Johnny Depp, Amber Heard trial going to jury
nydailynews.com
news
2022-05-27 18:09:39

Six weeks of sensational and often graphic accusations end Friday afternoon when lawyers representing Hollywood stars Johnny Depp and Amber Heard gave their closing arguments in a Virginia court, where a jury will now deliberate multimillion-dollar defamation claims.Attorneys for Depp, who claim the 'Pirates of the Caribbean' actor's life was changed when Heard penned a 2018 Washington Post column claiming she'd been a victim of domestic abuse, asked jurors to 'give Mr. Depp his life back' by reaching a verdict in his favor.
