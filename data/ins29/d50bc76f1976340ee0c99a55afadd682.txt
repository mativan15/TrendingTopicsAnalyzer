(PTI Photo/R Senthil Kumar) (The principal opposition AIADMK has convened a meeting of its general council and executive committee here on June 23 to discuss several crucial issues confronting the party and latest political developments. The meet assumes significance as it is being held in the wake of growing resentment among the party members that ally BJP is attempting to overshadow it in playing the role of the principal opposition party in taking on the ruling DMK.
