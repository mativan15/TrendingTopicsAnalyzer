A HIGH GROWTH SAAS BUSINESS, STRONG RECURRING REVENUES OPENS UP NEW GROWTH MARKETS IN EUROPE

LONDON, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Further to the announcement of 10 May 2022, MotorK Plc (AMS: MTRK) ("MotorK" or the "Group") announces the completion of the acquisition of Carflow ("the Company"), a European automotive retail solutions provider that serves approx. 400 car dealers and major automotive OEMs, including Jaguar, Land Rover, Opel, Stellantis and Subaru.

Founded in 2009, Carflow is an established automotive software as a service ("SaaS") player in the Belgian, Dutch and French digital automotive retail markets, expanding MotorK's European presence into these growing markets.
