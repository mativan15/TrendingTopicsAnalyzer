AgenciesThe official teaser of the popular reality show "The Real Housewives of Miami" Season 5 is out.Season 5 of The Real Housewives of Miami appears to meet the high expectations established by Season 4, which redefined reality television. It was confirmed on Sunday at BravoCon that season 5 will begin on Peacock Thursday, December 8. Due to the power of streaming, the first four episodes will be made available on the day of the premiere, and one new episode will be released every Thursday. The returning housewives are Alexia Nepola, Guerdy Abraira, Julia Lemigova, Larsa Pippen, Lisa Hochstein, and Dr Nicole Martin.
