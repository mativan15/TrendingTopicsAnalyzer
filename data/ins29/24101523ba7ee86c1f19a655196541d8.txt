(Renee Schiavone/Patch)CORONA, CA — A lucky lottery player in Riverside County woke up Thursday feeling like a million bucks — $6 million, to be exact.While there was no Powerball jackpot winner in California for the Wednesday night drawing, a ticket worth $6 million was sold at Sunny Donut in Corona. The store is located on Limonite Avenue, near Hamner Avenue, lottery officials said. The winning numbers were 11, 41, 56, 57, 63 and 2.
