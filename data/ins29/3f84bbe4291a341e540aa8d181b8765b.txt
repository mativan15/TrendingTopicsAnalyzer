Cape Veterans To Receive Federal Funding

Federal funding will be going to veterans on Cape Cod to help hem re-enter the workforce and find proper housing. The Massachusetts military support foundation is being awarded almost 185-thousand dollars in the national grant. The money will be used to address homelessness and unemployment among vets on the Cape & Islands.    Photo: Getty Images
