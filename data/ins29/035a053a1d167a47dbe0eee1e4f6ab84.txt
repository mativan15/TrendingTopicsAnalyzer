Variety - TV News Cannes — Ester Expósito, one of the most preeminent of breakout actors from 'Elite,' is set to star in 'La Isla Bonita,' a new series created by Ginesta Guindal, produced by The Immigrant and Sabado Películas with production-distribution superindie Fremantle handling global distribution.Billed as an irreverent dramedy, 'La Isla Bonita' focuses on a group of friends – party, party animal Alfonso, idealist Peter, environmentalist Sol – who share a quaint house on Ibiza, until the owner decides to triple the rent for the summer months, forcing them to find an ingenious solution to keep their lives and home together.
