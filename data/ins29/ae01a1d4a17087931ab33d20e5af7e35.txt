7th Pay Commission: Punjab is the latest to join the list of states that announced the Dearness Allowance (DA) hike for government employees ahead of the festive season of Diwali. The AAP-led Punjab government decided to give a 6 per cent dearness allowance (DA) hike to employees and pensioners, with effect from October 1 this year. The decision expected to benefit lakhs of employees and pensioners was approved by the cabinet. The central government increased dearness allowances for its employees in September.
