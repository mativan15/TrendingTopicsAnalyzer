Havelock residents to get $300,000 boost in cell service

Residents in Havelock will get a boost in cell service.

What does a $300,000 investment into a new cell phone tower get you? Upgraded services.

Havelock residents will soon benefit from the upgrade as UScellular is investing the money into a new cell tower for customers in southern Craven County.

The site enhances UScellular's 5G network in and around the Havelock community and is one of more than 700 sites the wireless carrier has across the state.

5G service provides faster data speeds, seamless video chatting and a more responsive mobile experience, according to a news release from the company.
