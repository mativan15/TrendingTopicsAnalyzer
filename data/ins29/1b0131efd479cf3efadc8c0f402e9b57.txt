da-kuk ~ by Snehasish Chaudhuri, MBA (Finance) The Schwab International Dividend Equity ETF (NYSEARCA:SCHY) is a diversified global exchange traded fund (ETF) offering low but positive yield in a bearish market condition. It selects high dividend paying stocks in various equity markets outside the United States. These stocks are selected through fundamental analysis, which creates multiple filters in order to find out quality companies. Investing internationally allows investors of SCHY to spread their investment base, target the world's best dividend paying stocks, diversify their risks, and reduce home bias.
