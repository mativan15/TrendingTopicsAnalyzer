Hargreaves Lansdown Faces £100 Million Lawsuit From Neil Woodford Fund Investors
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-17 09:10:47

(Bloomberg) -- Hargreaves Lansdown Plc faces a £100 million ($113 million) lawsuit from thousands of investors who suffered losses from the collapse of fallen stock picker Neil Woodford's equity income fund.RGL, the group running the claim, said it had filed the suit in London behalf of 3,200 investors against the investment management service because it recommended the Woodford Equity Income Fund until the day of the fund's collapse despite its 'portfolio diversification and liquidity issues.' This is the latest in a growing number of lawsuits brought by investors of Woodford's funds.
