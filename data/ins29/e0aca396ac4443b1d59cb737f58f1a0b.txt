LOS ANGELES, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Today, Dave Inc. (NASDAQ: DAVE) announced the arrival of cash back rewards for all Dave Spending members with the launch of Dave Rewards. Dave is the banking app on a mission to create products that level the financial playing field. This new offering enables Dave's members to activate card-linked offers from over 100 top brands, including Burger King, Gopuff, Shake Shack, Redbox, and more.
