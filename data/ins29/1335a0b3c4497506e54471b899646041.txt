State-backed BGF invests €22m in two companies
irishtimes.com
business
2022-06-03 14:15:44

Growth fund BGF is investing more than €22 million in two Irish companies in return for minority holdings in each.Supported by the Ireland Strategic Investment Fund and three banks, BGF backs Irish companies with investments normally ranging between €1 million and €10 million.The company confirmed on Friday that it will invest €12.5 million in Galway-based food supplement maker, Revive Active.The deal, subject to the Competition and Consumer Protection Commission's (CCPC) approval, will make BGF minority shareholder in the company.
