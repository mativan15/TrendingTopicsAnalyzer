Collider.com A woman's rage isn't often explored on screen. Since the film's inception, female characters are often written to take up as little space as possible when they enter a frame and stand on the sidelines while the men become the action heroes and hell raisers. So when women explode on screen and speak their minds, commit acts of violence, and soar across rooms with fists raised ready to strike, it's empowering to watch. Seeking vengeance, seeking glory, seeking peace, here are 25 feminist classics streaming right now on HBO Max featuring angry women taking up as much space as they want in pursuit of justice and revenge.
