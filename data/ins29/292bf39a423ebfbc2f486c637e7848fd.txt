Bihar stares at platelets crisis as dengue cases surge
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-22 08:37:20

Bihar stares at a shortage of platelets amid a surge in dengue cases, the highest in recent times, and underutilisation of machines for platelet apheresis, a process to separate blood into different components — platelets, red blood cells (RBCs) and plasma— , said people familiar with the development.Though the state health department has steadfastly denied any platelet shortage, the shortage seemed apparent for negative blood group, as per the government's platelet stock inventory Friday evening.
