Info Edge shares drop 4.03% as Sensex rises
indiatimes.com

AgenciesMazhar Mohammad of Chartviewindia.in said technical indicators are turning mixed as the weekly MACD chart has generated a 'buy' signal, giving an edge to the bulls.Shares of Info Edge (India) Ltd. traded 4.03 per cent lower in Thursday's session at 02:05PM (IST). The stock opened at Rs 4080.0 and has touched an intraday high and low of Rs 4080.0 and Rs 3891.6, respectively, during the session so far. The stock quoted a 52-week high of Rs 7462.95 and a 52-week low of 3314.0. About 25,223 shares changed hands on the counter so far.
