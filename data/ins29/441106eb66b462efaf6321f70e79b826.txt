Jeff Gladney Dies At 25; NFL, Jalen Reagor And Others Mourn Tragic Loss
ibtimes.com
news
2022-05-31 11:40:02

Football cornerback Jeff Gladney died in a car accident in the wee hours of Monday in Dallas, Texas. He was 25.According to the Dallas County Sheriff's office, the accident took place around 2:30 am at Allen Street's Woodall Rogers Freeway. The cause of the accident is still under investigation."We are asking prayers for the family and privacy at this most difficult time," Gladney's agent Brian Overstreet said, Star Telegram reported.The professional player also had a female passenger with him - who also died in the accident.
