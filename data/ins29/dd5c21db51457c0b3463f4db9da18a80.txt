How remote workers are ticking off travel bucket lists

Combining business travels with leisure is nothing new. In the past, many of those fortunate enough to be sent on business trips would often extend their stay in a different city, or a foreign country, to fit in a bit of sightseeing or shopping. And then there are the countless creative freelancers who in recent years packed up their laptops along with some personal belongings and set off for the big wide world, safe in the knowledge that with a stable internet connection, they can be productive and earn an income from literally anywhere.
