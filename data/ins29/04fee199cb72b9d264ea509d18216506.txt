Greenberg Traurig Wins 14 Finalist Spots on the 2022 Euromoney Women in Business Law Awards EMEA Shortlist
prweb.com
news
2022-06-01 23:15:00

NEW YORK (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 Global law firm Greenberg Traurig, LLP and three of its lawyers have 14 spots on the shortlist for Euromoney Legal Media Group's 'Women in Business Law Awards Europe, Middle East, and Africa (EMEA) 2022.' According to Euromoney the award series honors 'women in the forefront of legal work in business law and those firms that have produced initiatives and programmes to support and encourage women and diversity within the industry.' Criteria include the ratio of female-to-male lawyers at all levels of seniority, the retention of women associates to partner level, and relevant diversity initiatives.
