Markets Waver On Inflation Worry; Oil Rebounds From Losses
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-01 03:22:52

Asian and European stock markets wavered on Wednesday, as traders digested Wall Street losses and data showing that runaway inflation shows no sign of easing.Oil however rallied after sinking sharply on reports that OPEC was considering suspending Russia from an output deal, which observers said could allow producers to pump more.Equities have enjoyed a largely healthy run of late on hopes that inflation could be nearing a peak and a sell-off across markets may have run its course.The easing of some lockdown measures in China added to the optimism.
