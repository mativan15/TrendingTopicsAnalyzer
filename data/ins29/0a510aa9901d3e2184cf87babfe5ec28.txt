Emerging trends shaping the consumer goods industry
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 17:30:04

The consumer goods industry has witnessed a dramatic change in recent years. The recent global health crisis has further accelerated a wave of new trends in the consumer goods industry, which is mostly steered by the aspirations of the customers.  
These trends may be driven by a shift in demographics, the constant need for organizations to innovate products that are built on modern technologies, serve value to customers, and most importantly be honest and transparent about the brand vision.     
As digital-first organizations, we must be cognizant that these principal forces will affect the consumer goods industry while helping organizations shape their R&D to meet customer demands.
