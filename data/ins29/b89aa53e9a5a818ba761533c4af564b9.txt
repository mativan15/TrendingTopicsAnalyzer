October 19, 2022 08:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Berkshire Residential Investments announced that John Lamb has joined the company as Managing Director and Head of Portfolio Management. Based in the firm's Boston headquarters, John will be responsible for the execution, management, and investment performance of the firm's equity strategies, which focus on making investments across the U.S. residential housing spectrum. In this position, John will work in close collaboration with portfolio managers, the investments team, asset managers and other functional groups to drive strong investment performance and continue to expand Berkshire's equity platform.
