Temporary licences issued to 1,600 traders in Jaipur to sell firecrackers
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 16:00:00

JAIPUR: After two years of restrictions and ban, markets in Jaipur, especially in the Walled City are flooded with a variety of firecrackers ahead of Diwali. Jaipur police have issued almost double the number of licences for sale of firecrackers as compared to pre-Covid time of 2019. Markets have a mix of firecrackers including those manufactured locally at Kishangarh Bas of Alwar, Bharatpur, other parts of the state and even as far as Kerala.TOI on Saturday spoke to the senior officers of the Jaipur police commissionerate and also visited the city markets to know how the market is geared up for the response on firecrackers.
