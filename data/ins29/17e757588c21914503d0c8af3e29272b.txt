Challenger's SimCorp JV to Deepen Pension Fund Relationship -- Interview

2022-05-27 01:40:00

By Alice Uribe SYDNEY--Challenger Ltd.'s proposed joint venture with Danish software provider SimCorp could see the two companies offer investment operation services to pension funds and investment managers across the Asia-Pacific region, something which Challenger executives see as a yet untapped opportunity. The Australian annuities provider earlier this week entered into a non-binding Memorandum of Understanding with SimCorp to build a cloud-based investment administration as a service platform to be used by Challenger's existing businesses.
