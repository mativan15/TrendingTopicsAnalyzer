October 19, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Clipper Realty Inc. (NYSE: CLPR) (the 'Company'), an owner and operator of multifamily residential and commercial properties in the New York metropolitan area, today announced that it will release financial results for the quarter ended September 30, 2022, after the market closes on Wednesday, November 9, 2022. The Company will host a conference call that same day at 5:00 PM (ET) to discuss the financial results and provide a business update.
