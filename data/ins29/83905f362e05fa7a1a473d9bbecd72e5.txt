Korea's Comeup aims to become world's top startup accelerator program

SMEs and Startups Minister Lee Young (second from left) speaks during a press conference for Comeup 2022 at Maru 100 in Gangnam-gu, Seoul on Tuesday. (The Ministry of SMEs and Startups)SMEs and Startups Minister Lee Young said on Tuesday that it plans to nurture Comeup, the ministry's startup accelerator program, as one of the top five startup events globally in five years.'Ramping up engagement from private sectors, we plan to place Comeup as the world's fifth largest startup event in five years,' said Lee during a press conference at Maru 100 in southern Seoul.
