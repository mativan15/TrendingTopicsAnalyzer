Craig Goodliffe, CEO & Founder of Cyberbacker.
getty Recent data shows that 40% of U.S. workers have a side hustle, according to a 2022 report by Zapier. When you look only at Gen Z, the number increases to 59%, and with millennials, that number increases even more to 61%. There's also been a growing trend of startup founders who work for someone else. This shows me that there could be people working for you who are more than employees; they are also entrepreneurs. So, the question is, how are you as a business leader helping those entrepreneurs? Of course, if your goal is to lose your best employees—those with a strong work ethic, intense passion, creativity, versatility, flexibility and decisiveness—then you don't need to do anything to help them.
