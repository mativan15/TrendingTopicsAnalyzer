Insacog meet on Friday to assess new strains
indiatimes.com
science
2022-10-20 20:45:00

Getty ImagesRepresentative ImageIndian Sars-CoV-2 Consortium on Genomics (Insacog), a network of laboratories monitoring genomic variations of Covid-19 virus in the country, will hold a meeting on Friday in the light of multiple Omicron subvariants being detected in the country. "The meeting will be held to review the situation. We will evaluate whether there's any increase in the number of cases due to the new subvariants," a member of the consortium told ET. Insacog had last week detected the presence of a new Omicron subvariant BQ.
