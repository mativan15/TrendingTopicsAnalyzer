KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy iHeartMedia's Corey OlsonOct 21, 2022When it comes to high gas prices and soaring energy costs, President Biden is running out of fingers to point. Biden has spent months blaming high fuel prices on Russia, Saudi Arabia, oil companies, even gas station owners. Pretty much everyone and everything but himself. Now, with OPEC+ nations cutting global oil output and a potential winter energy crisis looming due to home heating costs, Biden's response is to drain another 15 million barrels of oil from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve, and chastise oil companies for not properly investing their profits.
