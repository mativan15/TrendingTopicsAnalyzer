After the polarization of shootings, Florida Governor Ron DeSantis to veto a $35 mn Tampa Bay Rays facility
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 12:45:00

The governor of Florida has plans that he will veto a $35 million bill that would build a spring training facility meant for the Tampa Bay Rays in Pasco County. He made this decision due to the Rays' politicization of recent shootings that took place in Uvalde and Buffalo a little ahead of May's Yankees game. Following DeSantis' response to Disney earlier in the year, the decision was made. Earlier this year, Florida Governor Ron DeSantis revoked Disney's exclusive tax and also the self-governing privileges after the company improperly intervened in Florida parental rights laws that were dubbed as "Don't Say Gay.
