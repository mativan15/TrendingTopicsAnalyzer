Elon Musk To Layoff 75% Of Twitter Employees In Next Few Months, Twitter Denies Such Plans
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 08:04:06

As the October 28th deadline for Elon Musk to complete the $44 billion Twitter deal draws close, every day is turning out to be eventful. Another turn of events has happened. After Twitter froze its employees' stock accounts in anticipation of deal completion and Elon Musk said that he was excited about the deal despite overpaying.The Washington Post reported yesterday that Elon Musk told prospective investors in his $44 billion deal to buy Twitter that he planned to get rid of nearly 75% of the company's 7,500 workers, citing interviews and documents.
