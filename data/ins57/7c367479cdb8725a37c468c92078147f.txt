Andreas Kluth: If Putin orders a nuke, will his generals obey?
fltimes.com
news
2022-10-20 08:00:00

As military strategists in the West war-game a possible nuclear escalation by Russian President Vladimir Putin, they're also pondering the role disobedience could play. What if Putin gives the order to nuke, but others in the chain of command refuse to execute? Should the U.S. and its allies try to sway those individuals now?Scenarios in which insubordination — from conscientious objection to outright mutiny — come into play aren't far-fetched. If Putin were to escalate, he'd do it with so-called tactical warheads.
