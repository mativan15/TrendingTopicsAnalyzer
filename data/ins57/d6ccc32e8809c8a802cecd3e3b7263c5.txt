13 Injured In Two Separate Lpg Cylinder Blasts In Salem
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 07:45:00

SALEM : As many as 13 people, including an infant, were injured after two LPG cylinders exploded in separate incidents in the city in the past two days. The health condition of the injured is stable, said collector S Karmegam .Rajeswari, a housewife, ignited the gas stove at her house in Ponnammapet around 6am on Monday. 'The cylinder suddenly exploded. While the concrete structure around the cylinder collapsed, a side wall developed cracks. Glass windows of a neighbouring house also shattered,' said a police officer attached with the Ammapet station.
