[Photo/Xinhua] According to data from the General Administration of Customs, China exported 1.91 million automobiles in the first eight months of 2022, 44.5 percent higher than in the same period last year. More importantly, the volume surpassed that of Germany, making China the second-largest automobile exporter behind Japan.
