The New South Wales government has stepped in to ban open-cut mining at an Upper Hunter site, after some multi-million-dollar horse studs looked to move interstate.The NSW Government has moved to ban open-cut mining at Dartbrook Coal MineThe Hunter Thoroughbred Breeders' Association said it was "delighted" after years of lobbyingSome horse studs had threatened to relocate from the Hunter due to the risk of open-cut miningNSW Racing Minister Kevin Anderson said the announcement to "legally rule out" open-cut mining at the Dartbrook Coal Mine, near Aberdeen, was about protecting the country's thoroughbred breeding and racing sectors.
