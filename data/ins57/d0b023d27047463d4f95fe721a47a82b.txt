DETROIT, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Amesite Inc. (NASDAQ: AMST), a leading artificial intelligence software company offering a cloud-based learning platform for business and education markets, released a case study on their partner, EWIE Group of Companies (EGC), highlighting successful results of their training and upskilling initiatives for nearly 1,000 employees in 10 countries. EGC is a supplier group that provides manufacturing, products and supply chain solutions to companies across automotive, aerospace, medical, agriculture and energy industries.
