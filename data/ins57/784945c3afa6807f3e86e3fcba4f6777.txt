Pound and shares fall on political uncertainty and disappointing data
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 11:00:00

The pound and stock markets fell this morning as political uncertainty, together with worse-than-expected borrowing and retail sales data, drained investor confidence.Sterling fell by nearly 1 per cent as the Conservative Party began the process of choosing the third prime minister in two months. It was trading above $1.12 at 7.30am before falling to $1.1131 by late morning.Retail sales data showing that shoppers had cut their spending last month led to a sell-off of retail stocks and weighed on the FTSE 100, which was down 57.
