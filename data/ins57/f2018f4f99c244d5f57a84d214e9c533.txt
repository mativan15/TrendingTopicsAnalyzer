Foxwoods Reports $30.5 Million In Slot Revenue For September
patch.com
news
2022-10-18 18:13:42

(David Allen / Patch)LEDYARD, CT – Foxwoods® Resort Casino is reporting slot revenue of $30.5 million for the month of September 2022. This results in a $7.8 million direct payment to the State of Connecticut in accordance with the Mashantucket Pequot Tribal Nation's long-standing slot revenue contribution agreement. Since 1993, the Mashantucket Pequot Tribal Nation has deposited over $4.5 billion of Foxwoods' slot revenue directly into the state's budget fund. Last month, Foxwoods closed off the summer with their Entertainment Labor Day Sale, where they were able to purchase tickets to see their favorite artists and bands at discounted rates.
