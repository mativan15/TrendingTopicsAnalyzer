Stranger leaves touching gift on dogs' graves in tribute to man's best friend
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-10-22 08:00:00

People have been left in tears after a stranger started leaving small sticks on dog graves at a pet cemetery - they now have sticks to play with in the afterlifePeople have been left in tears over the stranger's actionsWhen grieving a pet, many owners experience similar emotions to mourning the loss of a close friend or family member. While many owners bury their pets in their garden or keep their ashes in an urn on their mantle piece, others choose to lay them to rest in a pet cemetery. A stranger has been leaving sticks on dogs' graves as a way to honour "man's best friend" - and it has brought people to tears.
