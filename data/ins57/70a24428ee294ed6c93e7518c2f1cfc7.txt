Argentina confirms Latin America's first cases of monkeypox
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 01:45:00

AP"The patient is in good general condition, isolated and receiving symptomatic treatment," Argentina's health ministry said.Argentina confirmed the first two cases of monkeypox in Latin America on Friday, the Ministry of Health said in statements. Spain, England and Portugal are the countries with the most cases in the recent outbreak of this usually mild viral disease outside their endemic areas, normally found in parts of West and Central Africa. "The outcome of the PCR result of the case in question is positive," the Health Ministry said about Argentina's first case, adding that the patient is in good health and people who were in close contact with the individual were under clinical and epidemiological control, with no symptoms so far.
