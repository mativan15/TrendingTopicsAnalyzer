Popularity of Whittaker's Hazella chocolate blamed for shortage of other flavours
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-10-18 23:17:08

The popularity of Whittaker's new chocolate block has caused the company's other flavours to run low. The 250g Hazella block was released last month. But a Whittaker's spokesperson said the demand for the chocolate, which was revealed early after Countdown's online shop advertised it before its release date, had been higher than expected.
