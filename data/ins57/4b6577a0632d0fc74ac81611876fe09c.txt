Laura Kuenssberg was back on Sunday with her BBC politics show where she spoke with a panel of experts about Jeremy Hunt being appointed by Liz Truss as the new Chancellor of the Exchequer. The programme also saw Kuenssberg speaking with Hunt about his plans going forward. She also quizzed the MP over whether he was going to be "honest" with the public about his plans.However, just minutes into the programme, fans slammed the host over her chat with Matt Hancock.Some questioned why Kuenssberg had chosen to invite the former Health Secretary onto the show.
