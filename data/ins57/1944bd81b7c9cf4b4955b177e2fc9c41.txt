Owens Corning Completes Acquisition of WearDeck®

June 01, 2022 05:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time TOLEDO, Ohio--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Owens Corning (NYSE: OC) announced that it has completed the acquisition of WearDeck®, a premium composite decking and structural lumber manufacturer based in Ocala, Florida.

'As we begin integrating WearDeck into Owens Corning, I'm excited about the opportunities in front of us,' said Composites President Marcio Sandri. 'WearDeck solutions will advance our growth strategy and help pivot our Composites business to focus on high-value material solutions within the building and construction space.
