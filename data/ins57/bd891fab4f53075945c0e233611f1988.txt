Not foldable iPhones, but Apple may unveil foldable iPad in 2024

AgenciesSo with this iPad, Apple is exploring foldable technology for displays of around 20-inches in size. Ever since Samsung launched foldable smartphones, there have been several rumours about its tech rival company, Apple, entering the game with a similar product. However, amid rumours about a foldable iPhone, a new report suggests that the Cupertino-based tech giant might just launch its first foldable iPad.

According to a report in CNBC, analyst firm CCS Insight forecasted that the tech giant Apple will soon begin experimenting with foldable technology that it plans to launch globally by 2024.
