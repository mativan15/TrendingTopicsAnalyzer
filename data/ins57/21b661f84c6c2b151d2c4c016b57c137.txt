Adobe's (ADBE 1.32%) stock rose slightly on Oct. 19 after the company revealed its guidance for fiscal 2023, which starts in early December. Let's review those new estimates and see if they'll revive its beaten-down shares, which are down more than 50% over the next 12 months from an all-time high of $688.37 last November.
Why did Adobe's stock crash?
Before we review Adobe's outlook, we should look back at the reasons that drove the bulls away. Back in fiscal 2020, Adobe's revenue and adjusted earnings per share (EPS) rose 15% and 28%, respectively.
