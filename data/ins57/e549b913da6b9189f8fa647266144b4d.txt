The mum-of-two asked others if it was wrong to leave her 13-year-old at home (Image: Unsplash (stock photo))A mum has been warned she could "kill any relationship" she has with her daughter after sparking a debate over whether it would be wrong to leave the "rude" teenager behind while she and her other child go on holiday.Many parents have piled into the debate, after she explained her dilema on a popular parenting forum. The mum-of-two was searching for advice after questioning whether leaving her 13-year-old at home with her dad and going on holiday with her older 16-year old daughter would be wrong, reports Hull Live.
