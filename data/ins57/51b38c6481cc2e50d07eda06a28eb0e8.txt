Published: 17:03 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 17:03 EDT, 18 October 2022 Former RAF pilots teaching China how to shoot down Western aircraft were labelled ‘traitors' last night by furious former comrades.Up to 30 Top Guns, said to include former Red Arrows pilots, are passing on vital knowledge and experience to the People's Liberation Army (PLA) in a serious threat to national security, according to defence officials.They are not breaking current laws but came under fire from the UK's armed forces minister, who said legislation will soon be changed to stop veterans aiding a foreign adversary, particularly one labelled our ‘No 1 threat'.
