Supervisors Consider Turning Old West LA Courthouse into Housing

Photo: Getty ImagesLOS ANGELES (CNS) - The old West Los Angeles Courthouse could become affordable and mixed-use housing, after the county Board of Supervisors agreed Tuesday to publish a notice of intention to purchase the property from the Judicial Council. The motion, brought by Supervisor Sheila Kuehl, asks the county to find the money to purchase the property, which is described in the motion as "one of the largest publicly-owned infill sites suitable for affordable housing in this densely-populated western area of the County.
