Bank Of America Spikes, Schwab Slides
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-17 17:52:08

Bank of America Corp. (NYSE:BAC) and The Charles Schwab Corporation (NYSE:SCHW) both printed a third-quarter earnings beat on Monday before the market opened. Bank of America The bank's reported revenue of $24.61 billion beat the $23.6 billion consensus estimate. Earnings per share were 81 cents, beating the 77-cent consensus estimate. The earnings beat caused Bank of America to gap up about 5% higher. JPMorgan Chase (NYSE:JPM) 'set the bar quite high' when it printed a big third-quarter beat on Friday, but Bank of America 'hurdled that high bar today,' UBS analyst Erika Najarian said.
