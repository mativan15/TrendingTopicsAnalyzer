Booker prize winner urges people not to circulate pirated copies of his novel
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-21 13:11:21

Booker prize-winning author Shehan Karunatilaka has asked people not to circulate pirated versions of his novel.Karunatilaka won the prize on Monday for his second novel, The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida. In an Instagram story and a Facebook post two days after his win, Karunatilaka said it had 'come to light that an unofficial and illegal' pdf version of his book was 'doing the rounds on Sri Lankan social media'.Winner of the 2022 Booker prizeIn his post, titled 'Do not steal the moon', the author wrote: 'The book took seven years to write, with countless hours of research, craft and hard work poured into it.
