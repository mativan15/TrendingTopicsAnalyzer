Venus Remedies registers 9.22 pc growth in sales for FY 2021-22
indiatimes.com
science
2022-05-29 06:30:00

Chandigarh: Pharma company Venus Remedies on Saturday said it has registered a 9.22 per cent increase in annual sales in 2021-22 in comparison to sales in 2020-21. "Aided by concerted efforts to build on its export orders, supply chain, distribution network and product portfolio, the annual sales of Venus Remedies grew from Rs 548.12 crore in FY2020-21 to Rs 598.65 crore this fiscal (2021-22)," the company said in a statement here. The Panchkula-based company also reported a 16.81 per cent increase in operating profit, which went up from Rs 53.
