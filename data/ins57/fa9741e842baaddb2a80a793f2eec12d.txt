Ponniyin Selvan will be streamed on Amazon Prime, dates to be announced soon
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 08:30:00

The film Ponniyin Selvan -1 , based on one of the most popular novels in Tamil by Kalki Krishnamurthy, will be streamed on Amazon Prime as the OTT platform has bagged the rights for the film. PS-1 or Ponniyin Selvan-1, released in five languages, has a very popular star cast. Ponniyin Selvan is a Tamil story of 5 volumes, which is about the Chola prince Arulmozhi Varman, who later became Emperor Raja Raja Chozhan. The film is in two parts. The first part directed by the stalwart Mani Ratnam and released recently, has become the highest-grossing creation in 2022.
