Ethos IPO Listing: The stock settled at Rs 802.60, a fall of 8.58 per cent.New Delhi: Shares of luxury and premium watch retail player Ethos Ltd on Monday listed with a discount and settled nearly 9 per cent lower against the issue price of Rs 878.The stock made its debut at Rs 830, registering a decline of 5.46 per cent from the issue price on the BSE. During the day, it tumbled 11.84 per cent to Rs 774. It settled at Rs 802.60, a fall of 8.58 per cent.At the NSE, the stock listed at Rs 825, lower by 6 per cent.
