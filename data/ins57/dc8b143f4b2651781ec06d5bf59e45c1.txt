GOP purity test in DuPage County? Republican candidates for County Board chair question each other's Democratic ties
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-06-04 10:00:01

At a glance, the choice between Pete DiCianni and Greg Hart to become the Republican nominee for DuPage County Board chair later this month might seem like a coin flip.Each is already a member of the County Board. Each says he's tough on crime, fiscally responsible and compassionate toward people afflicted by drug addiction. Each has racked up dozens of endorsements and hundreds of thousands of dollars in campaign cash while expressing similar goals for the office.But their seemingly minimal policy differences haven't blunted the saltiness of the primary race, with each candidate questioning his opponent's Republican bona fides.
