It's been well-documented that the world of investing is a ‘boys' club,' on both sides of the deal. Despite the increasing number of female-led businesses, in 2020, female founders received a mere 2.3% of venture capital funding. Some have argued that women-led businesses are overlooked and underfunded because female investors are also underrepresented; only 22% of angel investors are women, with 65% of American venture capital firms having no female partners.
