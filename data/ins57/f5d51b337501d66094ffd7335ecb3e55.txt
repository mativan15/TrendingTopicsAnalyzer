Mobile technology has spread rapidly around the globe. Today, it is estimated that more than 5 billion people have mobile devices, and over half of these are said to be smartphones.   Minor girl tries to sell her blood to buy a smartphoneAs these devices have become a necessity, everyone wants to own one, if they don't already. That desire to own a smartphone led a 16-year-old girl to approach a blood bank of a district hospital in West Bengal. She wanted to sell her blood in order to buy the latest smartphone.
