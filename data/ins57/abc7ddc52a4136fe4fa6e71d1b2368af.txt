How Much Does it Cost to Start a Rhode Island LLC?
The filing fee for your LLC is $150 for the required articles of organization. You may incur additional fees if you choose to reserve your LLC name before setting up your business, hire a commercial registered agent rather than a noncommercial one or hire a formation company to help you with the initial paperwork.
