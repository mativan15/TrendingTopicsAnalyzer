Vendekin Technologies raises Rs 8.5 crore in funding led by Better Capital
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 12:45:00

Pune: Vendekin Technologies, which makes unattended retail technology solutions said on Monday that it has raised Rs 8.5 crore in a pre-Series A funding round. The round was led by Better Capital with participation from existing investors CP Gurnani's family office and Vineet Nayyar's family office. Gurnani is CEO of while Nayyar is former vice-chairman and MD of Tech Mahindra. The funds will be used towards investing in its R&D and technology teams to improve customer experience at vending machines and micro markets.
