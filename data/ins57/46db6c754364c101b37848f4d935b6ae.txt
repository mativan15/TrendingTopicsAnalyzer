Drivers rage as 'Just Stop Oil' eco mob blocks another major road during peak rush hour (Image: JUST STOP OIL)Just Stop Oil has enraged drivers this morning by blocking a London road, as they continue their campaign across the capital. Activists have halted rush hour traffic on the A4 at Barons Court London Underground station.The movement said 30 supporters have caused traffic to back up from around 7.30am this morning. The group has sent out a demand "that the Government halts all new oil and gas licences and consents".
