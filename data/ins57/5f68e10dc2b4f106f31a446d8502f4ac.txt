HOUSTON (PRWEB) June 03, 2022 Smart and cautious business owners and individuals manage risks on time. This includes having business insurance with liability coverage, and workers' compensation to help protect the company when an accident or mistake related to the business activities harms someone else. These rates are based, in large part, on where the business is located, its age, the type of work done, and the number of workers employed. Some businesses don't fit the traditional model that insurance companies normally insure.
