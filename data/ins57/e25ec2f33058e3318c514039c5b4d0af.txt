Retail sector runs on empty as unsold stock piles up at Adidas
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 19:00:00

More shoppers holding back on their spending and a profit warning from the world's second largest sportswear brand led to a sell-off in the shares of some of Britain's biggest retail groups.The 1.4 per cent fall in retail sales volumes between August and September was more than economists had expected, heightening fears of a looming recession.Adidas proved that the drop-off in demand is not an isolated problem for Britain as it cut its full-year guidance, citing a growing pile of unsold stock as a result of lower demand from shoppers in big western markets.
