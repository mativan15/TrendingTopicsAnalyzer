VETRESKA Singapore-based Subsidiary Raises US$50 Million To Streamline Business
prnewswire.com
news
2022-10-21 02:49:00

SHANGHAI, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- VETRESKA FUTURE PET FOOD, the Singapore-based subsidiary of leading Chinese pet lifestyle brand VETRESKA, recently received a US$50 million strategic investment from Quest FoF, the VCC of a major Pan-Asian Agri-Food conglomerate. Earlier this year, the parent company of VETRESKA also announced that it had completed three rounds of funding, raising an undisclosed eight-figure USD sum over a two-year period. The strategic investment allows the creative and branding nous of VETRESKA to now be complemented by the upstream foundation of its new investor.
