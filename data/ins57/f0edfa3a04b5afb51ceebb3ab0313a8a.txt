Could there be a casino built in Times Square?
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 19:23:30

A battle is heating up to win a New York City casino license, with real estate and gambling giants offering competing proposals.In April, New York state officials voted to issue three new casino permits in the New York City area. The decision has major developers and casino operators competing to secure support from the New York State Gaming Commission and community advisory boards.Possible sites for the new casinos include Times Square and Hudson Yards in Manhattan, as well as Flushing, Queens, and Yonkers in Westchester County.
