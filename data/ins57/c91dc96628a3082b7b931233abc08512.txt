Here's where most of America goes to college
bizjournals.com

2022-10-20 14:59:00

The U.S. higher education sector continues to evolve as a story of haves and have-nots. Twenty-eight colleges posted sticker prices over $80,000 for the current academic year, and despite the ever-increasing price, students continue to clamor for admission to the schools with well-known brands (and typically the largest endowments).

Meanwhile, dozens of campuses struggle to meet enrollment quotas, especially in the Midwest and Northeast where overall populations are declining. In fact, enrollments continue to fall across the higher ed sector.
