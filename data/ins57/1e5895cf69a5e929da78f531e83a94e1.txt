Mueller Industries Posts Lower 3Q Sales
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 11:19:00

By Will Feuer Mueller Industries Inc. posted lower sales for the third quarter as lower copper prices and inventory rebalancing weighed on results, and the company's international business was dented by recessionary pressures. For the quarter ended Sept. 24, the industrial manufacturer of copper, brass, aluminum and plastic products posted a profit of $154.5 million, down from $171.0 million. Earnings were $2.74 a share, down from $3.01 a share. The prior-year quarter included a pre-tax gain of $54.
