NEW YORK, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The "Polymeric Sand Market by Application and Geography - Forecast and Analysis- 2022-2026" report has been added to Technavio's offering. The report expects the market size to grow by USD 31.55 million, accelerating at a CAGR of 7.72% during the forecast period. According to the report, the market will observe significant growth in APAC. The region will account for a 52% market share over the forecast period. The growing construction industry is creating significant opportunities for vendors operating in the region.
