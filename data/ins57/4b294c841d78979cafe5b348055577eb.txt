Insiders of Construction Partners, Inc. (NASDAQ:ROAD) received good value on their investment after their US$628k purchase jumped US$75k in value
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-28 12:38:16

Last week, Construction Partners, Inc. (NASDAQ:ROAD) insiders, who had purchased shares in the previous 12 months were rewarded handsomely. The shares increased by 7.8% last week, resulting in a US$89m increase in the company's market worth. As a result, the stock they originally bought for US$628k is now worth US$704k.

While we would never suggest that investors should base their decisions solely on what the directors of a company have been doing, we do think it is perfectly logical to keep tabs on what insiders are doing.
