Floyd Mayweather's Fitness Franchise Continues Its Strong Franchise ExpansionDALLAS, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- MW Fitness Holdings, LLC, the parent company of Mayweather Boxing + Fitness, today announced that it has acquired KickHouse Fitness, LLC, a leading kickboxing franchise system. The transaction combines the 65 current Mayweather Boxing + Fitness franchise locations with the 26 KickHouse franchise locations into a single group under MW Fitness Holdings, LLC, offering the opportunity to enhance franchisee systems and infrastructure across all areas of both businesses.
