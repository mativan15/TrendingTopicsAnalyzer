This story is part of Recession Help Desk, CNET's coverage of how to make smart money moves in an uncertain economy. 


Lisa Frink knows firsthand how quickly financial stability can devolve into financial disaster.Frink and her husband checked all the boxes: They got their education, worked hard, bought a house and started a family. Then, in June, right as Frink returned to work from maternity leave, her husband lost his job.With a mortgage, medical bills and just one source of income, the Frinks are now one layoff away from collapse.
