iStockThere was no police or any legal action by the complainants in most of the #MeToo cases.NEW DELHI/MUMBAI: The #MeToo debate is back with filmmaker Sajid Khan returning to mainstream entertainment as a participant on "Bigg Boss" and at least two of the women who accused him of sexual harassment publicly expressing their anguish. In 2018, when the movement against sexual harassment at the workplace began in India with several celebrities, including Nana Patekar and Alok Nath, being called out for alleged sexual harassment, Khan was also in the dock.
