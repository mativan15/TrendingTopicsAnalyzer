Meituan Shares Jump After Revenue Growth Surprises
marketwatch.com

2022-06-06 04:39:00

By Yifan Wang Meituan shares rose in Hong Kong on Monday, the first trading day after the Chinese food-delivery giant posted better-than-expected revenue growth for the first quarter. The stock was up 7.1% at 192.90 Hong Kong dollars (US$24.59) at the mid-day break, trimming year-to-date losses to 14%. "The worst likely behind," Citi analysts said in a recent note. "Meituan is well-positioned as reopening play short-term and solid local service ecosystem exposure longer term given proven execution track record.
