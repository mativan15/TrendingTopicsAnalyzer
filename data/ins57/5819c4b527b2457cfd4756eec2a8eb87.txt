Marcos keeps an eye on POGO operations
blogspot.com
news
2022-10-21 04:00:00

PRESIDENT BONGBONG Marcos is closely watching the Philippine Offshore Gaming Operators (POGO) in the country and expects the police to act on untoward incidents involving its operations, according to spokeswoman Undersecretary Cheloy Garafil.During a press briefing in Malacañang, Garafil was asked if there is a directive from Marcos regarding the POGO industry considering the controversies surrounding its operations. 'Of course, the President is closely monitoring this. And as far as the President is concerned, ang PNP po ang in-charge dito sa usapin na ito,' Garafil said.
