Ipsen Initiates a Share Buy-Back Program to Cover Its Employee Free Share-Allocation Plan
businesswire.com
news
2022-06-01 00:00:00

June 01, 2022 12:57 AM Eastern Daylight Time PARIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Regulatory News:

Ipsen (Euronext: IPN; ADR: IPSEY) has appointed an investment-services provider to purchase 125,000 Ipsen S.A. shares, or about 0.15% of the share capital, over a maximum period of three months. The shares purchased under this agreement will be allocated mainly to cover its employee free share-allocation plan.

This program is made pursuant to the authorization granted by the Combined Shareholders' meeting, held on May 24th, 2022.
