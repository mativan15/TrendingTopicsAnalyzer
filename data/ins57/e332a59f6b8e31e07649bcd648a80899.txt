It's no secret that technology stocks haven't been a great place to put your money over the past six months. But stepping away from tech stocks permanently would be a big mistake, considering that they can be some of the best long-term investments in the market.   
For investors who have some time to wait out the market's current downtown, putting $3,000 toward tech stocks like Roku (ROKU -4.35%), Take-Two Interactive (TTWO 0.08%), and Apple (AAPL -0.09%) -- and holding the stocks for five or more years -- could end up being a great decision.
