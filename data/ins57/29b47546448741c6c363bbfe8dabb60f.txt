AMC Says It Drew More Than 5M Guests Globally Over Weekend
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 12:14:00

By Will Feuer AMC Entertainment Holdings Inc. said more than 5 million people visited its movie theaters globally over the weekend, with most visiting to watch 'Top Gun: Maverick.' AMC said its attendance over Memorial Day weekend roughly doubled from almost 2.6 million the same weekend in 2021. "A big salute to our friends at Paramount Pictures. They released a perfect movie and ensured it would be seen the world over in the way movies are meant to be seen: on the big screen with incredible picture quality and extraordinary sound," AMC Chief Executive Adam Aron said.
