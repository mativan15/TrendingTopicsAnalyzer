Snowflake Ventures, the venture capital arm of data cloud company Snowflake, today announced an investment in advanced TV advertising firm OpenAP. The financial terms of the deal, which reportedly gives the data leader a 5% stake, were not disclosed. OpenAP was founded in 2017 by a consortium of television publishers to provide advertisers and agencies with a standard open platform for cross-publisher audience targeting and independent posting.
