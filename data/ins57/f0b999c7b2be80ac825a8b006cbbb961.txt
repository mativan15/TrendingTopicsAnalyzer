The legal fintech platform will use the funds to further expand nationally and dive deeper into the divorce lifecycle, creating tools and services that offer a bridge to financial independence and emotional well-beingOAKLAND, Calif., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Hello Divorce, the leading legal fintech platform for affordable, less-stressful divorces, today announced a seed investment of $3.25 million led by The Artemis Fund, which invests in female founders that are modernizing and diversifying wealth.
