October 19, 2022 07:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time STAMFORD, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NewEdge Wealth, LLC, a registered investment adviser specializing in ultra high net worth, family office and institutional clients, is pleased to announce today that it has opened another office location in Southern Florida. The new location in Miami is the firm's second office in the area and is home to a growing six-person team led by Patrick Dwyer.



Mr. Dwyer joined NewEdge Wealth in 2021 as a Managing Director of the firm.
