It's a four-day weekend for Hawke's Bay with the region's anniversary day on Friday and Labour Day on Monday. The Hawke's Bay Arts Festival has taken full advantage of the holiday days, offering a jam-packed weekend of arts events to suit all budgets and tastes. Here is a taster of what an arts weekend in Hawke's Bay might include. For full details: hbaf.co.nz Jackie Goes Prima Diva, Until Oct 23, 7pm, various locations. Homegrown chanteuse Jackie Clarke (When The Cat's Been Spayed, The Lady Killers) turns her powerhouse vocals, wicked humour and maverick entertainment style to the songs of the legendary divas of the 20th and 21st Centuries.
