Amid an inflationary environment and consequent tightening of monetary policy stances both in India and globally, those buying stocks with a short-term outlook must stay away until there is clarity, says Nikhil Chandak, MD & Head of Investments, . On the other hand, long-term investors should be greedy at the moment. In this interview, he also talks about themes he is bullish on.

We are in the middle of rate hikes, an unending war, soaring inflation and not-so-impressive earnings season. Is it the time to be greedy or fearful?Well, it depends on the investment horizon of an investor, whether one has a short-term investment outlook of a month or a year or a longer term view of 3-5 years.
