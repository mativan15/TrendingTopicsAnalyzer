The Foothills Medical Centre in Calgary on Sept. 23, 2021.Sarah B Groot/The Globe and MailAn Alberta Health Services board member has resigned after Danielle Smith was named United Conservative Party Leader, saying she could not support the new Premier's plans to reform the health system and its organizational structure.Ms. Smith has promised to hire a new chief executive officer for AHS and replace its entire board with an interim health commissioner, in addition to tasking an independent organization with providing recommendations within 90 days on how to decentralize control of health care delivery.
