May 27, 2022 11:56 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP announces that investors that purchased or otherwise acquired Okta, Inc. (NASDAQ: OKTA) securities between March 5, 2021 and March 22, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period') have until July 19, 2022 to seek appointment as lead plaintiff in City of Miami Fire Fighters' and Police Officers' Retirement Trust v. Okta, Inc., No. 22-cv-02990 (N.D. Cal.). The Okta class action lawsuit charges Okta and certain of its top executive officers with violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
