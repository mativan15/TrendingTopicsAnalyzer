Citizens Financial Group: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-19 10:36:09

PROVIDENCE, R.I. (AP) _ Citizens Financial Group Inc. (CFG) on Wednesday reported third-quarter net income of $636 million.

On a per-share basis, the Providence, Rhode Island-based company said it had profit of $1.23. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs, came to $1.30 per share.

The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of six analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.20 per share.

The bank posted revenue of $2.48 billion in the period.
