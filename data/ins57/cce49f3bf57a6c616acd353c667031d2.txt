Conversion therapy stories aren't for the faint of heart. For decades, thousands of LGBTQ teens and adults have shared horrifying tales of so-called experts trying to forcibly "change" their sexual orientation using emotionally and physically traumatizing methods, ranging from electroshock treatments to hypnosis. Organizations like GLAAD have called attention to the harm that this discredited practice has caused, reporting that it contributes to higher rates of suicide and drug abuse among young people who identify as LGBTQ.
