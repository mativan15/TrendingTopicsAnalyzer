Going green can't get around using fossil fuels

Port Jefferson Station, L.I.: 'Hallelujah,' I said out loud on Monday after reading the intelligent and accurate description of New York State's energy future written by Michelle Hook ('New York's risky all-or-nothing energy policy,' op-ed, Oct. 17). Finally, I said to my wife, there are people who do understand the energy problem that this state is facing.

Then on Tuesday morning, I read a totally inaccurate hit piece written by Voicer Laurie Joan Aron saying that Hook is a shill and not properly informed about what New York needs.
