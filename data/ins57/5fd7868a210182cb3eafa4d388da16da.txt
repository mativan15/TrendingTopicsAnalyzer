Fervor and fallout over the release of Netflix's Dahmer—Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story continue—this time with an ultra-spooky twist. As Halloween approaches and Ryan Murphy's limited series breaks *Stranger Things–*level records, people are apparently looking to masquerade as a real-life serial killer.Searches for 'jeffrey dahmer costume' have surged across platforms since the show's release, with that tag earning 8 billion views on TikTok alone. A pair of wire-rimmed glasses with a similar aesthetic to Jeffrey Dahmer's eyewear are branded as 'fashion-inspired glasses' on eBay.
