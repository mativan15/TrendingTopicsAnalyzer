Erling Haaland could surpass Cristiano Ronaldo vs Liverpool
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 06:00:00

Erling Haaland has wasted no time making an impact since arriving in the Premier League this season. He is seemingly on a one-way trajectory to records galore during his stint at Manchester City and could surpass Cristiano Ronaldo in a particular list if he continues in the same ilk this weekend.Man City travel to Anfield to face Liverpool on Sunday, where they have won just one Premier League game since 2003. But there is a justified sense of confidence heading into the two heavyweights' latest battle with the hottest player in Europe leading the line in Pep Guardiola's side.
