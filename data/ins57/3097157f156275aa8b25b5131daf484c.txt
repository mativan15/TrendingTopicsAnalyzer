Published: 05:19 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 05:24 EDT, 3 June 2022 George Kambosos Jr is reportedly set to pocket an eye-watering $10million for his clash with Devin Haney - the most earned from a single bout by any Australian boxer ever.Kambosos Jr fights for all the lightweight belts this weekend at Marvel Stadium when he takes on Haney in front of 40,000 people in Melbourne, having returned to Australia with a glut of belts after beating Teofimo Lopez.The lightweight rivals have captured the public's attention during fight week, with Kambosos Jr labelling Haney a 'rat' for allegedly leaking information about Lopez, while the American claims the unified champion is a 'hypocrite' as he helped Manny Pacquiao prepare for his fight against Australian star Jeff Horn.
