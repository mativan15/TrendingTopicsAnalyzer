70% stubble burning cases in Punjab from Majha alone

Amid the rising cases of stubble burning in Punjab, a sizeable chunk is coming from the Majha region. Of the total 2,625 cases reported so far this year, 1,823 cropped up in Majha region alone, constituting almost 70%. As per government data, Tarn Taran leads with 807 cases so far, followed by Amritsar with 788 and Gurdaspur with 228.
