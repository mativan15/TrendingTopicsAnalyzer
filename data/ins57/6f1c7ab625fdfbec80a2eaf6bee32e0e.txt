Oil Prices Jump, Stock Futures Waver
wsj.com
news
2022-05-31 07:39:00

Photo: Courtney Crow/Zuma Press U.S. stock futures wobbled between small gains and losses while crude prices rose after the European Union committed to a partial ban on Russian oil. Futures for the S&P 500 edged up 0.1% Tuesday, a day after U.S. markets were closed for Memorial Day. The benchmark index had risen 0.6% for the month through Friday, putting it on track to steady after April's 8.8% loss. Futures for the Dow Jones Industrial Average were flat and contracts for the technology-focused Nasdaq-100 gained 0.
