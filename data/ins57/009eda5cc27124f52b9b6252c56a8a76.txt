'In Zimbabwe, money earned is immediately spent, as it will be worthless a few weeks later'
lemonde.fr
news
2022-10-19 05:31:42

If inflation persists, invest in a cow. Not only will it not lose its value, unlike the money in your current account, but it can also offer attractive returns by reproducing. The Economist informed us that Nhaka Life Assurance in Zimbabwe came up with the idea of this inflation-resistant investment. Inflation is so high in Zimbabwe that investments cannot be denominated in local currency, otherwise they would quickly become worthless. So, savers invest in " shares of cows," which are raised a few dozen kilometers from the capital, Harare.
