Customer angry over duck sauce arrested for April shooting death of beloved Queens Chinese restaurant deliveryman
nydailynews.com
news
2022-06-02 10:41:55

An unhinged customer who feuded for months with a Queens Chinese restaurant about not getting enough duck sauce with his order has been arrested for the April shooting death of the eatery's beloved longtime deliveryman. Glenn Hirsch, 51, was arrested at his home Wednesday and charged with murder and gun possession for the April 30 killing of Zhiwen Yan, 45.The victim was riding his scooter near 108th St. and 67th Drive in Forest Hills when he was shot. A witness told cops the driver a of a Lexus SUV sped off from the scene.
