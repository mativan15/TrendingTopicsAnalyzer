Samsung leads India tablet market with 40% share in Q1: IDC
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 13:45:00

NEW DELHI: Korean handset maker Samsung led the tablet market in India in Q1 2022, with a market share of 40% (by shipments), growing by 10% as compared to the year-ago period, according to findings by the International Data Corporation (IDC) released on Monday.With the recently launched Galaxy Tab S8 series, Samsung has gained more than 8.3% in the premium segment during the quarter ended March 31, against the previous quarter (Q4 2021), as per IDC. 'The popularity of the recently-launched Galaxy Tab S8 series and Galaxy Tab A8 series enabled us to further strengthen our leadership in the tablet market,' said Sandeep Poswal, Business Head, New Computing Business, Samsung India.
