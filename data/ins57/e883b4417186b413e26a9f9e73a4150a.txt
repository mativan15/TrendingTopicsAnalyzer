Love it, or hate it, there has been no shortage of drama through the opening two rounds of Major League Baseball's new expanded post-season format.

The National League was littered with upsets after its top four teams were bounced early. The Dodgers and Braves combined to win 212 games this season yet didn't survive a best-of-five division series. The 101-win Mets couldn't even get beyond the wild card, and neither could the Central-winning Cardinals.

The American League has been more predictable.
