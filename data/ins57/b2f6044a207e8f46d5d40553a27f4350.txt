Nervous tension and massive smiles as 104 skydive for a good cause
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-06-01 17:00:00

Pre-jump jitters failed to deter a group of 104 skydivers who leapt out of a plane over the weekend, raising $134,000 for the Graeme Dingle Foundation. The Foundation's Drop for Youth fundraiser saw Skydive Abel Tasman help 74 people – about 14 every hour – make the leap on Saturday, with the rest taking part on Sunday. Graeme Dingle Foundation Marlborough manager Kelvin Watt said the atmosphere at the airfield was 'amazing'. 'There's just a cool buzz, with the variety of people from across the community who have come.
