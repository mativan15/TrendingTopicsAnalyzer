In an interview from Motley Fool Live, recorded on May 18, Fool.com contributor Rachel Warren and ABB (ABB -1.00%) CEO Björn Rosengren discuss the company's new electric car charger and how it could be a gamechanger for ABB and getting more consumers to switch to EVs. How critical is charging infrastructure to meet those goals?
Björn Rosengren: It's of course essential.
