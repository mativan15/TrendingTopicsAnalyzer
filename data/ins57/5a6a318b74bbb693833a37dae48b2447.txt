The Brazilian has been a key part of Mikel Arteta's first-team this season as Arsenal lead the way in the Premier League, starting all ten Premier League games thus far.Brought in from Lille in September 2020, the 24-year-old's contract was not due to expire until 2025 but Gabriel has been rewarded with a new a deal running until 2027. Juventus had been linked with a move for the player though he has now put pen to paper and committed his long-term future to Arsenal.'I'm very happy to sign this new contract with Arsenal, because it's such a big club," he told the club's official website.
