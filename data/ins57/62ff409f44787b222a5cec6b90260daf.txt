Let's use this crisis to bring sanity to our pension savings
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

With all the focus on the immediate aspects of the pension fund crisis, it's easy to overlook the still more devastating long-term consequences of the funds' liability-driven investing (LDI) strategies.In essence, LDI is an alien parasite that has for nearly two decades been consuming the UK's economy and financial system from the inside. It has been doing this through the completely unnatural and wholesale diversion of our nation's savings from living, breathing domestic UK equities into 'dead money' bonds.
