getty Starting with the three bedrock corporate tenets of business execution (CEO), financial oversight (CFO) and operational management (COO), then expanding to marketing (CMO) and human resources (CHRO), executive titles and responsibilities have evolved over time. Today's C-suite has extended across newer functions ranging from technology and cybersecurity to digital transformation and diversity. One of the more recent additions is the chief information security officer (CISO), who works closely with CIOs, CTOs and engineering teams.
