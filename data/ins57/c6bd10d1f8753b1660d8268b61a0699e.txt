Kanye West Reaches Deal To Purchase Conservative Platform Parler
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 14:10:24

Rapper and fashion kingpin Kanye West, who last year changed his legal name to Ye, reached a deal in principle Monday to buy conservative social media platform Parler."In a world where conservative opinions are considered to be controversial we have to make sure we have the right to freely express ourselves," said West, in a statement released by Parler.The announcement comes after West, 45, has faced scrutiny in recent weeks after a series of antisemitic and inflammatory comments through social media.
