Sheryl Sandberg to leave Facebook parent Meta after 14 years
usatoday.com
news
2022-06-01 20:12:32

Sheryl Sandberg, the chief operating officer of Facebook parent company Meta, plans to step down from her role after 14 years.In a statement published to Facebook on Wednesday, Sandberg said she will continue to serve on Meta's board of directors and focus on her foundation and philanthropic work.Sandberg said she will officially leave the company this fall."I am so immensely proud of everything this team has achieved," said Sandberg. "The businesses we've helped and the business we've built. The culture we've nurtured together.
