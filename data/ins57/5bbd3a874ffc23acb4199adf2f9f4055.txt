Family of 14-year-old boy killed after football game says reward for info too small to yield results

By CBS Baltimore Staff October 15, 2022 / 6:47 PM / CBS Baltimore BALTIMORE -- A $2,000 reward is available to anyone who can help find the person who shot and killed a 14-year-old boy in September. Travis Slaughter was one of two teenagers gunned down after a football game at Milford Mill Academy in Baltimore County.The other teenager survived their injuries.Now, Slaughter's family is pleading for the community to help them, but they are worried that the reward amount is not enough. Slaughter was eager to start at Milford Mill Academy this year.
