REUTERS/Heinz-Peter BaderOct 20 (Reuters) - The Haas Formula One team will have payments and money transfer company MoneyGram (MGI.O) as their title sponsor from next season in a multi-year deal that will provide a welcome infusion of cash for the series' only U.S. based outfit.The backing was officially announced at a coffee shop in downtown Austin on Thursday ahead of this weekend's U.S. Grand Prix at the Circuit of the Americas.
