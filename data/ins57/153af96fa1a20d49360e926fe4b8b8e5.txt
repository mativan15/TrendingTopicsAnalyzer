Following Kantara's success, Karnataka govt announces allowances for Daiva Narthakas depicted in the film

2022-10-20 13:16:47

Rishab Shetty's Kannada film Kantara isn't just minting money at the box office, but also making some cultural and socio-political impact in its home state. The film, which revolved around the traditions of Daiva Narthakas in coastal Karnataka, has brought the practice into prominence. On Thursday, the state government announced allowances for all narthakas over the age of 60 in the state.
