Cost of living: Sussex charity runs food market for young people
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 21:00:03

Image caption, Student Mapalo Bray was facing homelessness at 19 during the pandemicA charity has warned the increasing cost of living, a drop in universal credit payments and rising rental costs are leaving more young people at risk of becoming homeless.YMCA DownsLink in Sussex said even those with jobs could not always afford to pay for accommodation.The charity said it had set up a food market for 16 to 25 year olds to ease some of the pressure. It has also helped some young people to find temporary homes.
