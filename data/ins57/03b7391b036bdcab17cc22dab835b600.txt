One night last week, I heard that a right-wing cabal was gathering at the Beach Cafe, the Upper East Side's Republican Cheers. I decided to drop by, because I've been curious how the shadow war between Ron DeSantis and Donald Trump is going among GOP thought leaders. Sure enough, the place was crawling with spidery political operatives and staffers from Newsmax, Breitbart, Fox News, and the New York Post as well as a few red-pilled Park Avenue women with strong opinions and stronger drink orders.
