A plan to tackle surging crime rates in Alice Springs shows a lack of "fresh thinking", according to a sceptic, as others welcome it with cautious optimism.The NT government has released a new plan to address Alice Springs' rising crime rateThe plan aims to boost police foot patrols and respond to youth on the streetsA youth worker says without more resources and funding there is nothing new in the planPolice Minister Kate Worden on Wednesday announced a Social Order Response Team (SORT) would see government, business and community unite to address antisocial behaviour.
