bizjournals.com

2022-10-20 01:50:00

Attendance at Kauffman Stadium for the Kansas City Royals during this 2022 season fell 13.7% from 2019 to 1,277,686 attendees, according to the Kansas City Business Journal 's sister publication Sports Business Journal.

The Royals ranked 26 among Major League Baseball clubs. During its 80 regular season home games, the Royals averaged 15,971 fans per game.

The team's attendance did, however, grow year-over-year because the 2021 season was still affected by Covid-19 restrictions. The Royals' 2021 attendance was 1,159,613.
