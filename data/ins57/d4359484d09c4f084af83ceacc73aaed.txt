Man held for murdering mother in Tiruppur
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-16 13:57:28

The Tiruppur District (Rural) Police on Saturday arrested a man for murdering his mother.According to the police, Sabapathy (60) and his wife Poongodi (55) were living in Vilamarathupatti, near Dhally, in the district. Their son Sathish (35) was living close-by and there was a dispute with his parents related to water supply to his farmland.On Wednesday, Poongodi went missing, and her husband lodged a complaint at the Dhally police station.During investigation, the police found that Sathish assaulted his mother and pushed her into the PAP canal.
