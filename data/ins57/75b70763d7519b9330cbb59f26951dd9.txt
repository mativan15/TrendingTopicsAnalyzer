Bayonetta 3 developer reiterates support for replacement voice actor after controversy
theverge.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 09:04:45

Bayonetta 3 developer PlatinumGames has responded to the controversy that's embroiled its upcoming game after it replaced longtime protagonist voice actor Hellena Taylor with Jennifer Hale. The studio published a statement saying, 'We give our full support to Jennifer Hale as the new Bayonetta,' and asking people to 'please refrain from any further comments that would disrespect Jennifer or any of the other contributors to the series.' The studio also says that it agrees with a statement recently posted by Hale herself.
