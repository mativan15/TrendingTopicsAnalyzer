TORONTO, ON / ACCESSWIRE / May 30, 2022 / GreenBank Capital Inc. (CSE:GBC)(OTC PINK:GRNBF)(FRA:2TL) ("GreenBank" or "the Company") reports, as filed on SEDAR May 26, 2022, that the Company has appointed PKF Littlejohn LLP, hereafter referred to as "PKF" as auditors of the Corporation for the financial year ending July 31, 2022. PKF will fill the vacancy created by the resignation of Dale Matheson Carr-Hilton Labonte LLP as its independent auditor. The Company's decision to change auditors from Dale Matheson Carr-Hilton Labonte LLP was not the result of any disagreement between the Company and Dale Matheson Carr-Hilton Labonte LLP on any matter of accounting principles or practices, financial statement disclosure, or auditing scope or procedure.
