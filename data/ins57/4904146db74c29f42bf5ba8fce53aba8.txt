Kerala Startup Mission-supported fintech startups are set to explore markets for their products and services to establish strong linkage with investors at an online exhibition scheduled on October 21 during the 'Big Demo Day' series of KSUM. Eleven fintech startups will showcase their innovative products and solutions at the expo, which will commence at 10 am on the day, a statement here said on Wednesday. The expo, now into its eighth edition, will help create a platform for industry and investors to leverage products and services from the startup ecosystem.
