Ponte Vedra Beach man pleads guilty to federal charges of distributing child sex abuse videos
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-31 15:44:13

A Ponte Vedra Beach man faces five to 20 years in federal prison after pleading guilty to distributing videos depicting children being sexually abused, according to a press release from the Department of Justice.

George Thomas Griffiths Jr., 42, could also be sentenced to a life term of supervised release, according to the DOJ. A sentencing date has not been set.

Authorities found at least 2,000 images and at least 10 videos on Griffiths' phone depicting children being sexually abused, including infants and toddlers, according to the release.
