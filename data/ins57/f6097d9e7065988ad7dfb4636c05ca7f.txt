Pipedrive Announces Three Executive Appointments to Drive Business Growth
businesswire.com
news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pipedrive, the global sales-first CRM and intelligent revenue platform for small businesses, today announced the appointment of Sean Evers as Vice President of Sales, and the addition of two marketing executives. James Stoker joined Pipedrive as Vice President of Marketing Strategy and Heni Hazbay as Vice President of Demand Generation Marketing.

'I am thrilled to welcome Sean, James and Heni to our team at such a critical point in our journey,' said Dominic Allon, CEO of Pipedrive.
