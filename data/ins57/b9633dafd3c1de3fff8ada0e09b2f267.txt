Actress Geeta Basra, whose last Hindi outing was Second Hand Husband (2015), is all set to make her comeback to Bollywood . Geeta, who got married to former cricketer Harbhajan Singh in 2015, and took a break from films soon thereafter to be with her family, will be returning to the industry with an upcoming legal dramdey film, alongside Parambrata Chatterjee. In an exclusive chat with us, Geeta, who is currently shooting in Bhopal , reveals the changes in the world of entertainment now and how she is balancing her professional and personal life.
