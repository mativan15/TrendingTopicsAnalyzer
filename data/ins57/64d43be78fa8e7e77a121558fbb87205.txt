Egyptian court upholds prison sentence for girl over 'obscene' TikTok videos
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-03 20:45:08

A court in Egypt has upheld a one-year prison sentence for a TikTok girl whose dancing videos were branded obscene by authorities.Her sentence came amid an ongoing crackdown on social media content creators in the country.The Child Juvenile Court in Egypt on Tuesday upheld the sentence for Nancy Ayman, who is known on social media as Mocha Hijazi, on the grounds that her charges pertained to gross indecency, according to local media.Mina Nagy, her lawyer, had appealed the one-year prison sentence.
