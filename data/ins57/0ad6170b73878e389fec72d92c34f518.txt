getty Private 5G and 4G/LTE network deployments are on the rise as enterprises seek to digitize their operations and gain the benefits of automation, including increased efficiency, the increases to data security that come with keeping all data onsite and better-guaranteed coverage and capacity to support a growing number of connected devices. Given all of these potential benefits, having a strong understanding of both the capabilities and deployment considerations is essential for those seeking to take full advantage of their private network opportunities.
