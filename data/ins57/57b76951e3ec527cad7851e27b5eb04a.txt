English French 99 per cent of households in TELUS' territory already have access to the PureFibre network for work, education, entertainment and virtual healthcare TELUS on track to complete its network deployments related to the Operation High Speed program by September 2022 SAINTE-MARIE, Quebec, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Today, TELUS announces a $10 million private investment for its network infrastructure in the Chaudière-Appalaches region this year, rolling out its PureFibre network to 10 communities in Beauce-Sartigan, Bellechasse, Lotbinière, Montmagny and Nouvelle-Beauce RCMs (Regional County Municipality).
