Pro-EU political figureheads have blamed Brexit for the UK's financial troubles while mocking the Government. Veteran leftwing politicians across the bloc have taken pot-shots at the Government as millions of Britons struggle financially. Kwasi Kwarteng's widely ridiculed "mini-budget" - vast tracts of which new Chancellor Jeremy Hunt overturned today - has left the UK with an enduring unfavourable reputation among its peers.EU cheerleader Guy Verhofstadt was among politicians on the bloc who blamed Brexit for the UK's recent troubles.
