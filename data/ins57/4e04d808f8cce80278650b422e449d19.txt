FM Sitharaman said that the Indian government is taking action against activities like spamming and intrusion into privacy data, especially in the banking sector.
| Photo Credit: PTI
The Indian government is taking action against activities like spamming and intrusion into privacy data, especially in the banking sector, Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman has said. Addressing a group of Indian reporters at a news conference here on Saturday, Ms. Sitharaman said the government has withdrawn the old data protection bill and the new one is likely to come sooner.
