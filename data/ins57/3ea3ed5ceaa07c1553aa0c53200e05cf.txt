Delilah May, 27, from Lancashire - who has been given 18 months to live - lost so much weight in the space of a few months a neighbour thought she had an eating disorderDelilah May, 27, is fighting bowel cancer A young mum who has stage 4 bowel cancer initially thought her bloating and abdominal pains were caused by irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
