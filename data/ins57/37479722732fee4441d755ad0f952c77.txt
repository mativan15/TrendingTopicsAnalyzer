Like every other actor, Rangaraj Subbiah, popularly known as Sathyaraj has an astonishing story of achieving his dreams in the film industry. He ruled the South film zone by playing villainous characters and became the trendsetter with his notorious acting but it was only after portraying the famous Kattappa from SS Rajamouli's 'Baahubali' franchise, Sathyaraj marked his impression all over the world. With over 250 films to his credits, the veteran actor is still full of energy and can give any young actor a run for their money.
