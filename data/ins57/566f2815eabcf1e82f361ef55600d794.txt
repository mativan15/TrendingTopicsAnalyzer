DCX Systems sets IPO price band at Rs 197-207/share
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 06:30:00

DCX Systems, a manufacturer of cables and wire harness assemblies, on Friday announced a price band of Rs 197 to Rs 207 per share for its Rs 500-crore initial public offering (IPO). The initial share-sale will open for public subscription on October 31 and conclude on November 2, the company said. The company has cut the size of its fresh issue of equity shares to Rs 400 crore from Rs 500 crore planned earlier. Apart from the fresh issue, the IPO comprises an offer-for-sale of equity shares to the tune of up to Rs 100 crore by promoters -- NCBG Holdings Inc and VNG Technology.
