By Wang Ying in Shanghai | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-20 15:30 Share - WeChat The Antai College of Economics & Management at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, launched a new education platform called Doing Business in China on Thursday during the 9th International Business School Shanghai Conference (IBSSC 2022).
According to Chen Fangruo, dean and professor of the college, the new online platform aims to provide participants with a better understanding of the business environment in China.
