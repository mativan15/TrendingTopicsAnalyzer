Industry Leader Chentelle Lane Joins as COO; Company Partners to Power Leading Cardiology Networks in SouthwestALBUQUERQUE, N.M., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Karoo Health, the leader in end-to-end, tech-enabled, value-based cardiac care, announced today the hiring of industry veteran Chentelle Lane as COO. Lane will help guide the company's business and operating strategy, including broadening its reach to cardiac patients through partnerships with care organizations and health plans. A proven leader in value-based care operations, Lane was most recently COO of Care Services at Cityblock Health, and has also held executive-level positions at Somatus and naviHealth.
