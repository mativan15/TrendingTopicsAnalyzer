June 01, 2022 10:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Forward Risk and Intelligence is proud to announce that legendary corporate investigator Ernest Brod, founder and CEO of Brod Global Intelligence ('BGI'), has joined its Advisory Board. Brod will continue to lead BGI as CEO.



Brod founded BGI after an unparalleled career in the investigations sector. He was most recently a Managing Director at Alvarez & Marsal, leading the firm's business intelligence practice. Prior to that, he held leadership positions at Deloitte FAS, Navigant Consulting, and Kroll.
