Risk or Return? What should you focus on while selecting a PMS
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 12:15:00

Most investors approach equity investing, first thinking about risk, and then about the return. Since PMS or Portfolio Management Services is primarily equity investing instrument, it is also approached generally with a risk-focused approach than a return-focused strategy. This is one of the reasons why smarter PMS companies market their products highlighting the positioning of consistent compounding, wealth preservation and superior risk management. However, ideally speaking, while selecting a PMS, the investor's focus should be on returns more than risk.
