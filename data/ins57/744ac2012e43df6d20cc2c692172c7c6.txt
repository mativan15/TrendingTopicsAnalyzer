(Yonhap)Housing-related expenditures such as rent, water, electricity and gas accounted for the largest part of a single-person households' spending in South Korea, government data showed Wednesday.For the first three months of this year, single-person households spent a monthly average of 300,000 won ($241.74) on housing-related expenditures, accounting for 20.5 percent of their total average monthly spending, a Statistics Korea data showed.
