Banorte Is No Longer Pursuing Citi's Banamex Mexican Unit
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-21 12:37:46

Grupo Financiero Banorte is out of the running for the purchase of Citigroup Inc. 's Banamex Mexican unit.

The bank is no longer pursuing the acquisition as of Thursday, it said in a filing Friday with the Mexican stock exchange. Banorte, Mexico's second-biggest bank by total loans, trailing only Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria SA, was seen as the top contender for Banamex.
