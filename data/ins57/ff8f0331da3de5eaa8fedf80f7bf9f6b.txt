October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CORE Industrial Partners ('CORE'), an industrials-focused private equity firm, announced today the sale of MedLit Solutions ('MedLit' or the 'Company'), a full-service provider of pharmaceutical packaging, labeling and printing solutions, to Resource Label Group, a leading national label company.

Founded in 1986, MedLit offers a broad range of packaging, labeling, printing, design, and fulfillment capabilities, primarily for pharmaceutical and healthcare applications.
