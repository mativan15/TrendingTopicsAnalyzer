NEW MENU 0:01, 6 Jun 2022Updated: 18:25, 5 Jun 2022MCDONALD'S is introducing yet another MAJOR menu shake-up as it launches all new meals this summer.
The chain will bring out not one or two, but FOUR different burgers in the coming weeks. 
McDonald's is bringing out an all new range this summerCredit: Alamy
It's also the first time EVER that popular Cypriot dish, halloumi fries, will grace the fast-food giant's serving platters.
Rivals like Burger King have served up the cheesy side in the past, but the famous golden arches hasn't offered a taste before now.
