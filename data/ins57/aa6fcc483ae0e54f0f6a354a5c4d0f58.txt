Controversy erupts over Bengal BJP chief's comment on 'spies in TMC' ahead of Nadda's visit
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-06 06:00:00

PTIWest Bengal BJP President Sukanta MajumdarAhead of BJP National President J P Nadda's visit to West Bengal in June and amid continuous ramblings inside the party, controversy erupted over state party president Sukanta Majumdar's comments on Sunday, who claimed that BJP has planted 'spies' in Trinamool Congress. Majumdar also mentioned that every party has its people in other parties, who pass on information, and BJP is no exception. Majumdar's comments came at a time when the saffron party is marred with defection and trouble in the state, with possibility of Malda North MP Khagen Murmu's defection making rounds.
