Logistics, shipping, and distribution networks are all changing dramatically in the post-pandemic era. While these differences had been forming during the preceding decade, the pandemic has undoubtedly catalyzed their spread. With the introduction of cutting-edge technology such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, the Internet of Things, blockchain, and cloud computing, modern supply chain management has effortlessly transitioned into a full-stack automated operation. Along with the fourth industrial revolution, the supply chain system employs modernistic future technologies to improve enterprise-wide connectivity, perform real-time analytics, automate tracking operations, and optimize resources with pinpoint accuracy.
