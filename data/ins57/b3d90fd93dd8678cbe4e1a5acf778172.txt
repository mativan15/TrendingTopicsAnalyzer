Macintosh charger for iPhone: This retro fast charger for Apple devices can be bought via Indiegogo. Here are all details. Macintosh charger for iPhone: If you are an Apple fan, this could interest you a lot. It would be then cute to have a charger shaped just like the original Macintosh, right? Thankfully, Shargeek, a kickstarter brand on Indiegogo has come up with just a product like that.
