Published: 05:30 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 05:38 EDT, 21 October 2022 A car filled with bags of concrete has been discovered buried in the backyard of a multimillion-dollar Silicon Valley mansion, baffling police. Landscapers working at the $15million luxury home uncovered the car 5ft deep in the ground yesterday morning in Atherton in Silicon Valley, one of the wealthiest neighborhoods in the country.Police believe the car was buried in the 1990s when it belonged to previous owners, but little else is known about the intriguing mystery.
