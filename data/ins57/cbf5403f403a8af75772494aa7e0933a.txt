The project will include the digitalization of a new series of bonds and its issuance to the system participants. As part of the issuance, the leading banks, both international and local (primary dealers), will participate in a "live test", during which they will be connected to a dedicated Blockchain system that will be developed by TASE and the selected technology vendors - VMware Blockchain and Fireblocks.
