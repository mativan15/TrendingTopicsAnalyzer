Will new design make Yagan Square hip again?
smh.com.au
news
2022-10-16 07:01:37

The owners of Perth venue The Shoe Bar — located in the CBD's struggling Yagan Square — are 'cautiously optimistic' about new plans to revitalise the precinct's market hall.

West Australian hospitality operator Nokturnl has signed a long-term lease with the state government to give the hall a makeover and concept designs for the proposed new hospitality and event space have been released.

Plans for a renewal of Perth's Yagan Square have been revealed. Credit:WA government

The dual spectres of a controversial design of the building and the COVID-19 pandemic have seen visitor numbers decline over the past four years.
