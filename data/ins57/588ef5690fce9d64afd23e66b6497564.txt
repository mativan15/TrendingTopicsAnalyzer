Johnny Knoxville on ‘Reboot,' His ‘Jackass' Future and Love for Bam Margera: ‘I Only Want Him to Get Better'

2022-10-19 16:45:00

In Hulu's 'Reboot,' a meta comedy from 'Modern Family' creator Steven Levitan, Johnny Knoxville plays edgy stand-up comedian Clay Barber. After multiple stints in jail, he gets the chance to reprise his role in a wholesome family sitcom after, yes, Hulu, decides to reboot it 20 years later. Unlike Clay, Knoxville is 'very good' at dubbing lines and is — at least according to his co-star Keegan-Michael Key — 'an absolute gentleman.' But the 51-year-old actor and stuntman admits that there's 'more than a little' of himself in his character.
