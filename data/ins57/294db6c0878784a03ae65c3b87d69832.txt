Shoppers stock up on duvets and blankets to ride out winter
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 16:00:00

Sales of duvets, blankets and warm clothing have increased in Scotland as people 'batten down the hatches' ahead of winter.Retail industry figures suggest that consumers are finding practical ways to save energy going into the colder months. Sales of air fryers, which are considered to be more energy-efficient than traditional ovens, also rose last month.Other trends in September suggest that people are cutting out big-value items, trading down to cheaper brands and seeking products with greater discounts.
