By Patrick Damp June 1, 2022 / 5:08 AM / CBS Pittsburgh Club Cycle raises money for Uvalde, Texas Club Cycle raises money for Uvalde, Texas 00:48 PITTSBURGH (KDKA) - On Tuesday, a Pittsburgh fitness studio and trainer took their exercises outside for an important cause. Club Cycle hosted a charity boot camp at Point State Park to help send support to the Uvalde, Texas community following the deadly school shooting last week. Participants jogged, planked, and did push-ups to raise money for "The Community Foundation of the Texas Hill Country.
