Angry Aston Villa fans get their wish as Steven Gerrard sacked
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 21:45:00

'We want Gerrard out,' the travelling support sang as the Aston Villa manager trudged across the Craven Cottage turf at full-time. About thirty minutes after full-time, they got their wish: Steven Gerrard was sacked by the club after a poor start to the season.He claimed pre-match to be 'a fighter' but there was little evidence of any on the pitch during a chastening defeat by Fulham.Fulham were everything that Villa were not — dynamic and adventurous — as goals from Harrison Reed, Aleksandar Mitrovic and an own goal by Tyrone Mings earned a deserved three points and ended a run of three games without a win.
