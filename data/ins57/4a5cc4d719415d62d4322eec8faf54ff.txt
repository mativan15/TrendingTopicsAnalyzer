A home equity line of credit — more commonly referred to as a HELOC — is one option for homeowners looking to tap the value of their home for cash. Unlike other options, though, HELOCs offer a line of credit — allowing you to withdraw, repay, and then withdraw more funds as needed over an extended period of time.
