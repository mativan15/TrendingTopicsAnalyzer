October 19, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time ALEXANDRIA, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--VSE Corporation (NASDAQ: VSEC, "VSE", or the "Company"), a leading provider of aftermarket distribution and maintenance, repair and overhaul ("MRO") services for land, sea and air transportation assets for government and commercial markets, today announced that its VSE Aviation segment has entered into four distribution agreements with various global business and general aviation ('B&GA') original equipment manufacturers ('OEM') for a combined value of approximately $350 million.
