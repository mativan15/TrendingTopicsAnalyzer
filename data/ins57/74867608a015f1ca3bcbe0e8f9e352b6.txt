Driver faces massive fine after being caught speeding 57 times in 2 months
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 09:17:58

A DRIVER has been fined nearly £50k and banned from the road for three years after speeding a staggering 57 times in just two months. 
John Kelly was fined nearly £47,000 after being found to have driven at more than 60mph in 30mph zones. 
The dangerous driver was caught speeding 57 times in his blue Volkswagen Beetle (stock image)Credit: Getty
The 29-year-old, of Cardiff, was caught out 57 times by a total of four speed cameras, resulting in speeding offences each time and one red light offence.
