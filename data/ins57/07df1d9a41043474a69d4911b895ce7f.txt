Starbucks agrees to divest Seattle's best coffee brand to Nestlé (NASDAQ:SBUX)

lucky-sky/iStock via Getty Images Starbucks (NASDAQ:SBUX) on Wednesday notifies its plans to sell the Seattle's Best Coffee brand to Nestlé (OTCPK:NSRGF). The terms of this deal are kept confidential. The move will further strengthen the Global Coffee Alliance by allowing both companies to focus on their core strengths. Starbucks said that since forming the Global Coffee Alliance in 2018, the companies have introduced a wide range of premium coffee products to new markets. Through the Global Coffee Alliance, the companies will continue to closely work together to develop new, innovative products and go-to market strategies to amplify the reach and expand the unique experience of the Starbucks brand locally and globally.
