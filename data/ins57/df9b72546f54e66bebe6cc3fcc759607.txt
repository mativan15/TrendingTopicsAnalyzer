'Always be thankful:' AHS Class of '22 members reflect on unique paths to graduation

Jun. 5—ANDERSON — As they waited to assemble and form lines to march onto the football field on a cloudless day at Anderson High School, several members of the Class of 2022 were mindful of the hardships they went through in order to don their red robes for graduation.

"These last two years I've been on virtual school," Victoria Bratjan said. "I'm very proud of myself and everybody else that has done this because there were a lot of virtual students that had to teach themselves and they still pushed their way through to graduate today.
