Candidate shortage: Cut-off lowered by 25 percentile to fill vacant PG seats in Punjab medical colleges
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 17:38:29

Facing shortage of eligible candidates to fill about 364 postgraduate medical seats laying vacant in seven medical colleges in the state, the Baba Farid University of Health Sciences (BFUHS) at Faridkot has lowered the minimum qualifying marks and percentile for admission to these seats following directions of the Union Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. The Union Ministry Of Health And Family Welfare has lowered the qualifying cut-off percentile for NEET-PG by 25 percentile for each category (general, SC, ST, OBC and handicapped).
