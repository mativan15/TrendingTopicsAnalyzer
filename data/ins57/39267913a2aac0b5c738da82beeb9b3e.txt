Kamila Valieva's 2022 Winter Olympics ended in tears after she missed out on the podium after days of being embroiled in a doping scadalRussian figure skater Kamila Valieva ends her new routine by covering her head with a black hood. For the previous four minutes, she has been performing an interpretation of her experience from this year's Winter Olympics, where events around her failed drugs test as a 15-year-old shocked the world.She removes the covering to soak up the cheers, while dozens of soft toys and flowers are thrown on to the ice by her fans.
