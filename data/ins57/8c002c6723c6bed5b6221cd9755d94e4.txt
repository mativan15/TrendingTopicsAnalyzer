Relief on Gas Prices as New York suspends gas tax for the rest of the year starting June 1
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-01 15:45:00

ReutersThe world is just coming out of the pandemic mode at the moment, with New York all set to see happy faces this summer. It is estimated that 39.2 million people will be driving around and holidaying the weekend on Memorial Day. This is as per AAA forecast, which categorically states that people are moving around more post the pandemic period. To add to this joy, New York just announced that they would be cutting tax on gas to give more benefits to the drivers. People are ecstatic at this decision by the government as Summer is generally celebrated with a lot of traveling around at weekends coupled with visits to the beach.
