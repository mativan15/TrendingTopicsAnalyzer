REUTERS/Henry NichollsLONDON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Britain's new finance minister Jeremy Hunt said on Monday the government would take decisions of "eye-watering" difficulty to ensure trust and confidence are restored in the country's finances."We are a country that funds our promises and pays our debts and when that is questioned, as it has been, this government will take difficult decisions necessary to ensure there is trust and confidence in our national finances," Hunt told parliament.
