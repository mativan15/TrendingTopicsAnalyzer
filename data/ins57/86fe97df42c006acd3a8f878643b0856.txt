While insiders own 28% of Ferro-Alloy Resources Limited (LON:FAR), individual investors are its largest shareholders with 54% ownership

2022-10-15 07:28:25

A look at the shareholders of Ferro-Alloy Resources Limited (LON:FAR) can tell us which group is most powerful. We can see that individual investors own the lion's share in the company with 54% ownership. In other words, the group stands to gain the most (or lose the most) from their investment into the company.

And individual insiders on the other hand have a 28% ownership in the company. Large companies usually have institutions as shareholders, and we usually see insiders owning shares in smaller companies.
