Jersey promenade barrier built to improve safety at La Haule

news
2022-10-17 15:13:13

Image source, Government of JerseyImage caption, An artist's impression of how the new barrier at La Haule will lookA new barrier will be built along a stretch of St Aubin's Bay, Jersey, aimed at improving safety for pedestrians and cyclists. The 30cm (12in) high wall is due to be built along 200m (650ft) of walkway between La Haule slipway and the public toilet to the east.The decision to build the barrier followed a safety audit.There is currently no raised wall, barrier or railing separating the promenade from the beach below.
