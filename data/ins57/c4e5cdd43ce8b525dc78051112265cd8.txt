By Saumyangi YadavState competitive exam aspirants object to government order to fill 100% KREIS vacancies through promotion, demand provision for direct hiring too Karnataka State Competitive Examination Aspirants Association (KSCEAA) has raised an objection against the recent government order which amends rules to fill 100% of vacancies of principals and other equivalent posts in Karnataka Residential Educational Institutions Society (KREIS) through promotions. The aspirants showed concerns about their future if all the positions are to be filled by promotions only.
