Image source, Newcastle City CouncilImage caption, An eight-lane swimming pool will be among the facilities at the new complexA leisure centre due to be built in Newcastle faces "incredibly challenging" pressures as costs rise and its opening date is pushed back, council bosses have warned.Work is set to begin shortly on the complex in West Denton, which will include swimming pools, exercise facilities and a library.It will replace the defunct West Denton Pool and the existing West Denton library and customer service centre.
