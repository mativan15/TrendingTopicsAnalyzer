Moderate 4.1 magnitude earthquake rocks East Bay near Bay Point, says USGS
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 14:15:00

AgenciesAccording to the United States Geological Survey (USGS), a moderate 4.1 magnitude earthquake struck the East Bay today. According to the USGS, the earthquake struck shortly after 5 a.m., with the epicenter at Bay Point, Contra Costa County. It went down to a depth of 12 miles. Other regions of the Bay Area, notably the North Bay and San Francisco, were affected. As seismologists examine the information and compare their findings, or as some other organizations issue their conclusions, the quake's actual intensity, location, and depth may be altered in the coming hours or minutes.
