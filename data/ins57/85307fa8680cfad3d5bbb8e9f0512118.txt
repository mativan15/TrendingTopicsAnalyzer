Much like the titans of the world who wear his clothes, Brunello Cucinelli – the Italian designer, entrepreneur and renaissance man who helms the brand of the same name – is a company founder. It is a rarity to meet a successful founder in any field, let alone in fashion, an industry dominated by heritage labels helmed by so-called creative directors who change jobs every five years or so. When Cucinelli started his company in 1978 in Solomeo, a hamlet in the Umbria region of Italy where he was born, he wanted to create a brand completely made in Italy and based on the idea of timeless luxury.
