Guest Worker Attacked By 2 Sloth Bears In Tea Estate
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 08:15:00

COIMBATORE: A woman guest worker suffered injuries on her hands after she was attacked by two sloth bears at Injiparai tea estate in Mananbolly forest range of Anamalai Tiger Reserve (ATR) on Sunday.P Sabitha Kumarai , 19, of Jharkhand, had reached the estate with her husband Prakash Kerwar a week ago for work. They were staying in the staff quarters.Sabitha, who came out of the house on Sunday around 5.50am to visit the common bathroom, did not notice the two sloth bears hiding in bushes, which attacked her.
