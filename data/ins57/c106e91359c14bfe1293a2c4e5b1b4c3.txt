DUSSELDORF, Germany, May 30, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--European retailers are some of the world's earliest adopters of electronic shelf labels (ESLs) to reduce operations costs as a foundation for retail digitalization. Now at this week's EuroCIS 2022 trade show, the new IoT platform All-Star is being released and introduced by the digital solutions provider Hanshow (EuroCIS booth - 9 D13), poised to drive Europe's brick‐and‐mortar retail into a new era of efficiency and profitability.
