Two American lawmakers have introduced a resolution in U.S House of Representatives, urging President Joe Biden to recognise Pakistan Army's role in the 1971 Bangladesh 'Genocide'. The resolution introduced by Indian-American Congressman RO Khanna and Steve Chabot also calls upon Pakistan government to apologises to Hindus and people of Bangladesh.
