Silent victims of a multi-billion-dollar gold business
dw.com
news
2022-10-15 13:30:00

In the eastern Democratic Republic of Congo, a powerful armed group, controls lucrative gold mines. DW's Mariel Mueller is the first international journalist who got access. She speaks with miners who are forced to pay 'tax' to the Mai-Mai Yakutumba and with survivors of its brutal attacks.
