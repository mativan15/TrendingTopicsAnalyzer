Published: 01:37 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 01:37 EDT, 30 May 2022 By Wayne Cole SYDNEY, May 30 (Reuters) - Asian stocks tracked Wall Street higher on Monday while the dollar was pinned near five-week lows as investors wagered on an eventual slowdown in U.S. monetary tightening, albeit after sharp hikes in June and July. Helping to mellow the mood was news that Shanghai authorities will cancel many conditions for businesses to resume work from Wednesday, easing a city-wide lockdown that began two months ago.
