ETtech8vdX founder Ravi ChachraBengaluru: Venture debt marketplace, 8vdx has raised $3 million in a funding round from Silicon Valley's famed accelerator Y Combinator. The other investors in this round include Tokyo-based venture capital firm GMO Venture Partners along with global investors like Zillionize; Cathexis; Grant Park; Asymmetry Ventures; Earlsfield Capital Partners and Fox Ventures. The round also saw participation from angel investors Vikram Chachra, founding partner of 8i Ventures and Kevin Moore who has backed the likes of companies like Drip and Robinhood.
