New DelhiThe Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) will hold brainstorming sessions on population control, women empowerment, minority outreach and the use of mother tongue in education, among other issues, during its four-day national executive meeting which begins in Prayagraj on Sunday.The Akhil Bharatiya Karyakari Mandal Baithak (ABKMB) will be headed by RSS chief Mohan Bhagwat and general secretary Dattatreya Hosabale.
