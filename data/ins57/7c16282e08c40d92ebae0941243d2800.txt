Variety - Film News Viola Davis is set to executive produce climate activist Maya Penn's original animated short, 'Asali: Power of the Pollinators.'The action-adventure short film, which marks Penn's directorial debut through her company Upenndo! Productions, features the 22-year-old's own animation.''Asali' made sense as our first major production because it integrates two of my biggest passions — animation and the environment,' Penn said in a statement. 'My goal with Upenndo! is to dive into new worlds that reflect the interconnectedness of people and our planet, from a story-driven and action packed perspective.
