October 18, 2022 02:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time NASHVILLE, Tenn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Demonstrating Nissan's commitment to adventure and vehicles that thrill, Team Wild Grace co-captains Sedona Blinson and Lyn Woodward finished second in the 2022 Rebelle Rally X-CROSS™ class in a stock 2023 Pathfinder Rock Creek.



This year's Rebelle Rally covered more than 1,400 miles of off-road navigation and driving competition from near Lake Tahoe to the famous Glamis Sand Dunes near the border with Mexico in far southern California.
