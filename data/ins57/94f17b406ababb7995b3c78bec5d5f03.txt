AgenciesLTPO displays are key to an efficient AOD mode as they conserve power. (Image: Twitter/@RjeyTech)Apple is synonymous with innovations. While there are many who like Apple devices for their ease of use and clean user interface, many Apple fans feel that Apple could do more to its operating system and hardware capabilities. An always-on display (AOD) is something that Apple has been mulling over in the past few months. In fact, there were reports that the iPhone 13 series may come with a low-temperature polycrystalline oxide (LTPO) display which could enable AOD.
