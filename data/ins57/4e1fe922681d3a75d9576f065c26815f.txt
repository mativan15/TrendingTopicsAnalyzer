May 27, 2022 09:03 AM Eastern Daylight Time PAWTUCKET, R.I.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Hasbro, Inc. (NASDAQ: HAS) ('Hasbro' or the 'Company'), a global play and entertainment company, today announced that in its report issued May 27, 2022 independent proxy advisory firm Glass, Lewis & Co. ('Glass Lewis') has recommended that Hasbro's shareholders vote on the WHITE proxy card FOR ALL of Hasbro's Director Nominees in connection with the Company's 2022 Annual Shareholders Meeting, to be held on June 8, 2022.
