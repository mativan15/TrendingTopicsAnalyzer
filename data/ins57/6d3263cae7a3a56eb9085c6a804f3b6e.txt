Inflation is now at 10.1% putting more pressure on household spending - but how exactly does rising inflation affect your money and will inflation keep going up?Inflation for the 12 months to September has hit 10.1%Inflation has climbed back up to 10.1% with the price of food and energy being blamed for the latest rise. The consumer prices index (CPI) measure of inflation is used to explain how much the prices have increased over time. Inflation is currently at a 40-year high - and is five times higher than the Bank of England target of 2%.
