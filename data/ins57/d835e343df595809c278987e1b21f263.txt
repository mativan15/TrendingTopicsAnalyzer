October 17, 2022 09:12 AM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best will participate in a panel session at the Hawaii Captive Insurance Council's Annual Forum, taking place Oct. 17-20, 2022, in Oahu, HI.

Dan Teclaw, associate director, AM Best, will sit on a panel at the forum on Wednesday, Oct. 19, at 9:30 a.m. (PDT), titled, 'Top 5 Must Know Captive Practices.' Teclaw has been with AM Best since 2013, and manages a team of analysts that cover U.S.- and Caribbean-domiciled captive insurance companies, along with various other commercial specialty and excess and surplus lines carriers.
