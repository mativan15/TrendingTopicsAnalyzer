June 03, 2022 09:57 AM Eastern Daylight Time DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The "RegTech Global Market Report 2022" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.

The global regtech market is expected to grow from $7.9 billion in 2021 to $9.88 billion in 2022 at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 25.1%. The market is expected to grow to $23.76 billion in 2026 at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 24.5%.

Major players in the Regtech market are Abside RegTech, Accuity, ACTICO, Acuant Inc, Ascent Technologies Inc.
