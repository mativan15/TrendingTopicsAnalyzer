Are you thinking about investing in mutual funds in this festive season? Are you confused about which mutual funds to choose or which mutual fund category to bet on? Well, we will help you. First, are you investing for a short period or long period? To be precise, are you investing for at least five to seven years? Then you can consider investing in equity mutual funds. If you are investing for less than five years, you should stick to debt mutual funds. We will concern ourselves with equity mutual funds since most investors think of the stock market when they consider investing in mutual funds.
