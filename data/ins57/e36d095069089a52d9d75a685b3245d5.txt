NEW YORK, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kaltura, Inc. ('Kaltura'), the video experience cloud, today announced, it will release its third quarter financial results for the period ended September 30, 2022 before market open on Thursday, November 10, 2022.

Kaltura will host a conference call to review its third quarter 2022 financial results and discuss its financial outlook.
