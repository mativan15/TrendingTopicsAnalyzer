Dolphins' losing streak should make us appreciate Tua Tagovailoa more
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-16 22:28:41

Fans can be rabid in the way in which they defend their favorite athletes, but the supporters of Tua Tagovailoa have an inarguable point added to their repertoire right now: The Dolphins need Tagovailoa under center.

The franchise's long-term prospects are always going to be up for discussion, but Tagovailoa's current impact cannot be debated. With him in the lineup, the Dolphins had one of the best offenses in the NFL. Without him, they've screeched to a halt and have fielded a unit that struggles to actually put points on the board.
