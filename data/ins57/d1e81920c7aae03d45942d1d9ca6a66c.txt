Netflix aims to attract 4.5 million subscribers by year end and doubles its expected new customer numbers
sky.com
news
2022-10-18 20:40:52

Netflix subscriber numbers increased by 2.4 million from July to September this year, more than double the number than was expected. The world's largest streaming service also saw revenue exceed expectations and top $7.9bn, its third quarter results showed.
It had been hoping to regain the million subscribers it lost in the first half of this year but instead gained more than double that number.Netflix now has a total of 223.1 million subscribers around the world.The company effectively declared itself the winner of the streaming war.
