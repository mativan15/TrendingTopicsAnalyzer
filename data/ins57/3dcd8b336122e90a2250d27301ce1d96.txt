UK M&A Prospects Brighten as Stocks Look Cheap After Pound Slide
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-21 10:34:47

(Bloomberg) -- A flurry of mergers and acquisitions in UK companies may be near as a plunge in the pound has made them more attractive to foreign buyers, according to investors participating in different spheres of the market.Private equity giant Blackstone Inc., $5 billion hedge fund Toscafund Asset Management LLP and strategists at Jefferies are united in calling a possible burst of deals in the UK after a rally in the dollar and economic and political chaos in the country leaves the nation's assets relatively cheap.
