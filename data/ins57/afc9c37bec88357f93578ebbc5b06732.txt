Brooge Energy Ltd Appoints Firoze Kapadia as Independent Board Member and Regains Compliance with NASDAQ Listing Rule 5605(b)(1)

2022-10-21 00:00:00

NEW YORK, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Brooge Energy Ltd, ('Brooge Energy' or the 'Company'), a Cayman Islands-based infrastructure provider, which is currently engaged in clean petroleum products and biofuels and crude oil storage and related services, today announced the appointment of Firoze Kapadia as independent board member of the Company and regaining compliance with NASDAQ Listing Rule 5605(b)(1), which requires the Company's board of directors to be comprised of a majority of independent directors.
