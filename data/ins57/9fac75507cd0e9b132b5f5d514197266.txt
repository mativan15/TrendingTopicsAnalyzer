'Threat to survival' UK rocked by mental health horror
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-28 06:00:00

Last week, it was announced inflation had reached nine percent - its highest level in 40 years. The Governor of the Bank of England, Andrew Bailey stated because of the war in Ukraine, there will be 'apocalyptic' food prices, something which he said he is 'helpless' to stop. Dr Elena Touroni, a consultant psychologist and co-founder of The Chelsea Psychology Clinic, told Express.co.uk that since we need food to survive, hearing terms like 'apocalyptic' food prices, will feel like a 'threat to survival' for struggling Brits.
