Giorgia Meloni has formed Italy's new ruling coalition, assembling the country's first far-right-led government since the end of World War II and becoming its first female prime minister. Giorgia Meloni's Brothers of Italy party scored the most votes at Italy's last electionHer party will form government with the support of other right-wing alliesItaly's president expressed satisfaction that the government was formed in a "brief time"A presidential palace official announced that Ms Meloni and her cabinet would be sworn in on Saturday (local time).
