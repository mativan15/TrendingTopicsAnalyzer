As a result of BBC Radio 2 airplay in 2006, Jack Savoretti's career took off. His most-selling albums 'Written in Scars' (2014) and 'Sleep No More (2016) affirmed his growing popularity, and these were followed by No 1 albums, 'Singing To Strangers' (2019) and 'Europeana' (2021), which spawned the special edition 'Europeana Encore'. Jack commented on the dates that these woodland venues are unlike any other live arenas. With these incredible settings, he is eager to share all of his new music with fans.
