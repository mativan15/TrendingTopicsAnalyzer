David Beckham 'is putting money before morals' says Judge Rinder
express.co.uk
entertainment
2022-10-16 09:47:20

This year, the Fifa World Cup will be hosted in Qatar between November 20 and December 18. David Beckham, 47, has been criticised by Judge Rinder, 44, for accepting the £10million deal despite the country's harsh discrimination laws against women and gay people.The Good Morning Britain presenter hit out at the former England captain for putting 'money before morals'. He also slammed the decision to hold the major tournament in the oppressive Middle East nation as a 'disgrace'.He spoke about the oppression that women and people from the LGBTQ+ community have to endure in Qatar.
