New-type enterprises' role bigger in growth amid woes

By ZHENG YIRAN | China Daily | Updated: 2022-05-31 09:49 An employee of JD, a Chinese tech firm, works at a logistics center in Beijing. [Photo by Wu Xiaohui/China Daily] New-type enterprises, a key component of China's real economy, are ramping up efforts to play a bigger role in better integrating digital and real economies, and safeguarding stable growth amid COVID-19 headwinds. New-type enterprises－companies engaged in emerging technologies, industrial categories and business modes－have been playing an increasingly important role in ensuring stable economic expansion and promoting high-quality growth during the COVID-19 era, said a recent report.
