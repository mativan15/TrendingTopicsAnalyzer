Jindal Steel and Power to set up India's first rail wheelset manufacturing plant
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 16:00:00

AgenciesThe move set up a rail wheelset manufacturing plant in the country is significant since India meets most of its requirement of around 60,000 wheels per annum through imports.Jindal Steel and Power Ltd. (JSPL) will be setting up India's first Rail Wheelset manufacturing plant at its Raigarh facility in Chhattisgarh. A company statement said that JSPL has collaborated with GIFLO Steel - Hungary for this project. The MoU for technology collaboration has been signed between GIFLO Steel -Hungary and Jindal Steel on Friday in New Delhi.
