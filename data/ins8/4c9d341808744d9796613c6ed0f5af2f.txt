The release of Gotham Knights on Friday, Oct. 21 marks an unusual milestone in the Batman franchise: You can't be Batman. DC's Darknight Detective has been the protagonist of more video games than almost any other hero, bolstered by multiple successful movie franchises. Some of those games have been fantastic, while others fell short. Here are our picks for the five best and five worst Bat-vidcons ever made. Let's start with the good ones.
