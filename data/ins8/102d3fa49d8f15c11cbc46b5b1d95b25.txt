Published: 09:56 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 09:56 EDT, 15 October 2022 Former Barcelona midfielder Miralem Pjanic has revealed manager Xavi wanted him to stay at the club before he left for UAE side Sharjah.The Bosnian joined the Catalonian club as part of a swap deal with Juventus, which saw Brazilian midfielder Arthur move to Serie A.After excelling in Italy at Roma and Juventus, expectations were high for Pjanic to succeed at the Camp Nou, but he struggled to find his best form in Spain and, after one season, was loaned out to the Turkish side Besiktas.
