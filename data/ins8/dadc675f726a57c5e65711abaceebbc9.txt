Meet our 2022 conference speakers

We are pleased to announce our lineup for the DealBook Summit, to be held at Lincoln Center in New York City on Nov. 30. Andrew will host a series of conversations with the biggest newsmakers in the world of business, politics and culture.

Among the speakers: President Volodymyr Zelensky of Ukraine; Treasury Secretary Janet Yellen; Mark Zuckerberg, Meta's co-founder, chairman and C.E.O.; Shou Zi Chew, TikTok's C.E.O.; Mike Pence, former vice president of the United States; Andy Jassy, Amazon's C.
