Meet Dayton's Who's Who in Real Estate for 2022

2022-10-17 13:27:00

The Dayton Business Journal is unveiling its fifth annual Who's Who in Real Estate for 2022.

This special report, compiled by the DBJ Editorial Board, includes key business leaders in Commercial Real Estate, Construction and Development industries who play important roles in the success of the Dayton region's real estate industry.

The full coverage will appear in the DBJ online and print edition on Nov. 18.

The 2022 honorees are:

CRE:

Tim Albro, partner, Crest Commercial Realty Inc.
Matt Arnovitz, senior associate, CBRE
Ed Blake, CEO, Culmen Real Estate Services
Chris Conley, president/partner, Oberer Companies
Tim Echemann, owner, Industrial Property Brokers LLC
Mark Fornes, president/founder, Mark Fornes Realty Inc.
