RT.com 19th October 2022, 08:13 GMT+11West Jerusalem has shut down discussions on arms transfers to Kiev after a member of the government suggested sending weaponsIsraeli Justice Minister Gideon Saar stated that Israel would not arm Ukraine, despite Diaspora Affairs Minister Nachman Shai suggesting otherwise. Kiev has repeatedly pressed Israel for weapons, and intends to petition Tel Aviv again in the coming days."Our support for Ukraine does not include weapons systems and weaponry - and there is no change to that position," Saar told Israeli broadcaster Army Radio on Tuesday, Haaretz reported.
