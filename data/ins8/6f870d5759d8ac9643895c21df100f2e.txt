Leonid Sorokin/iStock via Getty Images Most investors have heard the maxim "be greedy when others are fearful". This is, however, easier said than done, as fear is a powerful force that can even scare experienced hedge fund managers and analysts alike. That's when it's important to stick with one's convictions. After all, if one liked a stock when it was expensive, then why should that thesis drastically change when that stock has traded down on short-term fears. This brings me to American Tower (NYSE:AMT), which is now trading well below its near term high of $282 from as recently as August.
