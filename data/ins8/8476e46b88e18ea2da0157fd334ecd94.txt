Published: 16:57 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 16:59 EDT, 16 October 2022 Wasps are destined to go into administration today as English rugby continues to be engulfed by a financial crisis which has been on the cards for several years – long before Covid deepened the clubs' plight. The powers-that-be have been jolted into the realisation that urgent action is needed to revive the sport. This is a full-blown emergency and it is set to trigger a profound overhaul of the whole structure and governance of the game in this country.
