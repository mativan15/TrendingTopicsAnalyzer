Mississauga, ON., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- As the popularity of soccer continues to rise in Canada, Continental Tire Canada is excited to announce that in addition to the continued sponsorship with Major League Soccer (MLS), an onsite activation and VIP customer hospitality opportunities will be present at select Canadian MLS locations to add to the excitement of the 2023 season. The outdoor pod will be a fast-throughput, entertaining, low-touch distribution system that makes receiving a team-branded scarf fun and exciting.
