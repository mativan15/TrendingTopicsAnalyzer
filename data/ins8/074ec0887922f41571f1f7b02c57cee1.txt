Govt will take strict action against wheat exporters who submit back-dated documents: Piyush Goyal
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 13:30:00

AgenciesHe added that the ministry is investigating exporters who have received authorisation to verify if they have filed all the proper documents.The government will take strict action against exporters who are trying to ship or have shipped wheat by submitting back-dated and improper documents following the ban on exports of the crop, Commerce and Industry Minister Piyush Goyal said on Friday. On May 13, the government banned wheat exports to control domestic prices. However, it allowed shipments for exporters who have valid irrevocable letters of credit (LCs) issued on or before May 13.
