Centre clears entire GST compensation dues of states till May 31, 2022
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 12:30:00

The Government of India has released an amount of Rs. 86,912 crores to cover the entire amount of GST compensation due to states until May 31, 2022. This decision was made to aid states in managing their resources and ensuring that their programmes, particularly capital expenditures, are successfully implemented during the fiscal year. The Centre has decided to clear the dues till May 31 in order to ensure that their capex remains on track. The Centre cleared the dues despite only having Rs 25,000 crore in the compensation fund.
