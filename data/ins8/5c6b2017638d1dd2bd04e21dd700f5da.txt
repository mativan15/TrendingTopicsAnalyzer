By Kylie Stevens For Daily Mail Australia Published: 19:38 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 19:38 EDT, 16 October 2022 Sydney's Star Casino has been slapped with a record $100 million fine and had its licence suspended by regulators after a damning report into its operations.The Independent Casino Commission handed down its decision on Monday after a report has found the casino failed to stop the operation of criminal activity on its premises, despite knowing it was ongoing. The Star's licence to operate its Pyrmont casino will be suspended from 9am Friday.
