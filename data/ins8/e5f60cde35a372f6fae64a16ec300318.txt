Peak bitcoin selling may be behind us, but there is no let-up in risk aversion sentiment in the crypto market according to a key gauge of market sentiment. 
The ether-bitcoin (ETH/BTC) ratio, which tracks the amount of ether that can be traded per bitcoin slid to 0.05918, the lowest since July 2021, taking the year-to-date loss to 25%. Ether's dollar-denominated price has dropped by nearly 55%, while bitcoin, the crypto market leader, has shed over 30%.
