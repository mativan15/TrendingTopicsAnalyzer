Published: 22:29 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 22:29 EDT, 14 October 2022 While the likes of Lionel Messi could come under pressure to play for European club teams leading right into the World Cup, players from Argentina´s opening-game opponent Saudi Arabia will have much more time to focus on the global event. The Saudi league finishes Sunday. On Monday, 32 players will head to Abu Dhabi to begin a World Cup training camp. Soccer´s first mid-winter World Cup has squeezed European league schedules.
