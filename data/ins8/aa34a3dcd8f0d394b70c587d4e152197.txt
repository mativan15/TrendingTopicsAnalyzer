Bar Harbor: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-19 20:53:45

BAR HARBOR, Maine (AP) _ Bar Harbor Bankshares Inc. (BHB) on Wednesday reported third-quarter net income of $11.4 million.

The bank, based in Bar Harbor, Maine, said it had earnings of 76 cents per share.

The bank posted revenue of $41.9 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $38.7 million, beating Street forecasts.

Bar Harbor shares have declined almost 6% since the beginning of the year. In the final minutes of trading on Wednesday, shares hit $27.32, a decline of roughly 3% in the last 12 months.
