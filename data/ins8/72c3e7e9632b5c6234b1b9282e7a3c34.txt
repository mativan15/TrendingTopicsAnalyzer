Published: 22:39 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 19:04 EDT, 18 October 2022 Australia's Foreign Minister Penny Wong said Canberra would no longer recognise west Jerusalem as Israel's capital Australia said it would no longer recognise west Jerusalem as Israel's capital Tuesday, a policy reversal that prompted a curt rebuke from the Jewish state but was cheered by Palestinians. Foreign Minister Penny Wong said the city's status should be decided by Israeli-Palestinian peace talks, unwinding a contentious decision by the previous conservative government.
