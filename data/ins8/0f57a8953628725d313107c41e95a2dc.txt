June 02, 2022 11:11 AM Eastern Daylight Time OAKLAND, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Thousands of income-eligible customers could save 18% on their monthly electric bill by enrolling in Pacific Gas and Electric Company's (PG&E) Family Electric Rate Assistance (FERA) Program.



The FERA program gives discounts on electricity bills to income-qualified households of three or more individuals, but thousands of eligible customers have not yet signed up for the program, and are missing out on significant savings.
