Jio launches a new game controller in India without much fanfare

(Image credit: Jio)

 Jio recently announced the postpaid plans that provide JioFi dongle on use and return basis. Now, the company, without much fanfare, has launched the Jio Game Controller in the Indian market. The controller is compatible with Android TV, Jio Set-top-box, Android tablets, and more. It has two triggers along with eight direction and arrow buttons. As for the pricing, the Jio Game Controller is available in the Indian market for Rs 3,499. Furthermore, Jio is also providing EMI service at a starting price of Rs 164.
