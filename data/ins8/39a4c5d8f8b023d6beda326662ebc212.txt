Gurugram: Toll-free number for property ID errors
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-29 08:45:00

GURUGRAM : With an increase in the number of complaints related to property tax, MCG on Saturday launched a toll-free number (1800-180-1061) for residents. The complaints from the call centre will be directed to the officials concerned so that people don't have to run from pillar to post to get their grievances resolved.After new property IDs were generated following a recent survey, it created a lot of confusion among owners who complained of wrong addresses and names.'The new tax ID is not working properly.
