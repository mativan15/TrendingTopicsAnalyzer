Premier League footballer Kurt Zouma has been ordered to do 180 hours of community service and banned from keeping cats for five years after pleading guilty to kicking and slapping his pet cat. Zouma was dumped by apparel sponsor Adidas over the incidentHis club West Ham issued him the maximum fine under their club rulesHe and his brother were both sentenced to communit serviceThe 27-year-old West Ham United defender sparked outrage after a viral video, filmed by his brother Yoan and posted on social media in February, showed him dropping one of his cats and kicking it across the floor.
