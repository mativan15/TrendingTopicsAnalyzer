Acclaimed Japanese Restaurant Nami Nori Expands Into Montclair
patch.com
news
2022-10-19 14:18:20

MONTCLAIR, NJ — An acclaimed Japanese restaurant that specializes in 'open-style temaki' is expanding out of New York City into Montclair, with an opening scheduled for Thursday, Oct. 20.Adding to its presence across the Hudson River, Nami Nori is opening a new restaurant at 2 South Willow Street in Montclair. The restaurant – which has become known for dishes like the Spicy Crab Dynamite and X.O. Scallop, Tobiko, Lemon, and starters like Crispy Calamari with Yuzu Soy and Wasabi Cucumber Tataki – will be pushing the boundaries of its menu with some temaki, starters and desserts 'exclusive to Montclair,' spokespeople said.
