June 02, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cherre, the industry's leading real estate data management and analytics platform, today announced the addition of Julia Liuson, President of Microsoft's Developer Division to its Board of Directors. Julia pioneered Microsoft's journey in embracing the open source ecosystem and reinforced Microsoft as a critical partner to the open source community. Julia received the Asian American Executive of the Year Award in 2013 and was inducted into the Women in Technology International Hall of Fame in 2019.
