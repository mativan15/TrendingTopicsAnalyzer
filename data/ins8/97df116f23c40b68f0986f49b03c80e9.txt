Pimco fund added to Russia credit default swap exposure ahead of Ukraine war
latimes.com
business
2022-05-31 02:38:22

Pacific Investment Management Co.'s largest fund increased its exposure to Russian default swaps in the run-up to the country's invasion of Ukraine by selling more than $100 million of protection to banks that include Barclays and JPMorgan Chase & Co.At the start of the year, Pimco's Income Fund already had almost $1 billion of bets on Russia via credit default swaps, and it added a net $106 million to that in the first quarter of 2022, according to fund documents filed this month with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
