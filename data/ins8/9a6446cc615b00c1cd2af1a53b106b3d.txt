Exclusive! Sharanya Shetty on winning her first silver screen award, says " It was a blessed moment for me"
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-17 10:04:00

Actress Shanranya Shetty has a feather on her hat. The actress recently won the best debutant award for her silver-screen movie '1980'. The movie marked Sharanya's full-fledged silver screen debut after her small screen stint with the daily soap Gattimela. Speaking about the same with ETimes TV, the elated actress said, "It was indeed a blessed moment for me. Receiving the first award in front of stalwarts like Kamal Hassan sir, Yash sir, Darshan Sir, and many others is like a dream come true. This is a lifetime memory for me.
