Draft cabinet note issued for listing of 25% shares of Coal India arm

2022-06-02 17:15:00

AgenciesCIL had last month said that it is planning to divest 25 per cent stake in its unlisted arm BCCL and will go for the subsequent listing of the subsidiary on stock exchanges after obtaining further clearances.New Delhi: The coal ministry has floated a draft cabinet note for seeking inter-ministerial views on the proposal to list 25 per cent shares of Coal India arm BCCL. As a part of (CIL) restructuring, the government has decided for an initial listing of 25 per cent shares of Bharat Coking Coal Ltd (BCCL).
