How to stay in the cottage from ‘The Holiday'
nypost.com
news
2022-10-18 21:28:52

The holidays are just around the corner — and one of America's favorite romcoms, 'The Holiday,' is set to become a reality. 
Beginning in the fall of 2023, you can stay in a nearly exact replica of Cameron Diaz's English getaway, known as the Rosehill Cottage.
Only the exterior of the English cottage was filmed in the 2006 movie. As is typical in the entertainment industry, the interior of the house was actually built in a Hollywood studio soundstage. 
But that doesn't mean you can't get a taste of the cozy idyllic scenery.
