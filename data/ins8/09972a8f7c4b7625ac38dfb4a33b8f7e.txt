From becoming Twitter's largest shareholder, criticising the social media platform over issues like spam accounts, threatening to back out of the deal, to taking a U-turn and reviving the deal ahead of the October 28th deadline set by the court, it's been a busy year so far for Twitter and Musk. The much anticipated $44 billion, finally, seems set to be getting close to being sealed. Ahead of next week's deadline, which could see Elon Musk take over Twitter if everything is completed on time, let's take a look back at what has happened this year surrounding this $44 billion deal.
