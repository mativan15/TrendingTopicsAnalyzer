Friday's game started play in the NCAA Tournament Knoxville Regional. Alabama State, Campbell and Georgia Tech make up the field in the NCAA Tournament Knoxville Regional.The winner of the Knoxville Regional will play the winner of the Statesboro Regional in Super Regional play.Georgia Southern, Notre Dame, Texas Tech and UNC Greensboro are competing in the Statesboro Regional.Redshirt freshman Charlie Taylor replaced graduate senior Evan Russell for Tennessee against Alabama State. Taylor recorded one RBI in the contest.
