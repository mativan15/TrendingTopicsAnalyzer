Blackpink to meet 1.5 million fans on first world tour in four years with 'Born Pink'



Blackpink performs during world tour "Born Pink" in Seoul at the KSPO Dome in Jamsil, Seoul, on Saturday. (YG Entertainment)Blackpink successfully embarked on its first world tour in four years, "Born Pink," kicking it off in Seoul over the weekend.The concerts were held on Saturday and Sunday at the KSPO Dome in Jamsil, Seoul, and the two sold-out shows drew a total of 20,000 people. KSPO Dome is a sports complex with a capacity of some 15,000 seats, but the band's label YG Entertainment said it limited tickets out of safety concerns.
