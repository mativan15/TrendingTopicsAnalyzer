June 02, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time LEHI, Utah--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Weave (NYSE: WEAV), the all-in-one customer communication platform for small business, has won the Dental Product Shopper Readers' Choice Award in the patient communication category for the second consecutive year. Dental Product Shopper readers identified winners of the Reader's Choice Awards as their go-to solutions for their respective categories.



'We are honored to be named a Readers' Choice Award winner for patient communication by the Dental Product Shopper community for a second year,' said Weave Chief Product Officer Branden Neish.
