The General Electric Co. (GE) is a leading U.S. industrial stock that runs global manufacturing operations in the areas of aviation, health care, renewable energy and power generation.

While it has been a leading U.S. corporation for nearly a century, GE has recently announced a new strategy to split into three independent companies: one specializing in health care, another in renewable energy and power, and a separate one in aviation.
