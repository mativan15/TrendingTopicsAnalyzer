Australia has reversed the decision of the former government that recognised West Jerusalem as the capital of Israel, Foreign Minister Penny Wong has announced.Ms Wong said the decision of Scott Morrison's government to recognise West Jerusalem instead of Tel Aviv in 2018 was a 'cynical' and 'unsuccessful' attempt to win the seat of Wentworth in a by-election.'I think we all know the decision … put us out of step with the international community,' Ms Wong said on Tuesday.Foreign Minister Penny Wong blasted former prime minister Scott Morrison over his 2018 decision to formally recognise West Jerusalem as the capital of Israel.
