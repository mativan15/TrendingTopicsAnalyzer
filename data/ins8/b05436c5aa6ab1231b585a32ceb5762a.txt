IT'S fast fashion's biggest success story with 2,000 new items a day and yearly profits of £1.4billion.
But a new documentary on All4 has uncovered the dark side of Shein - with factory staff paid just £16.50 for a 17 hour day and fined three quarters of their wage for the smallest mistake.

After sending an undercover reporter into two of the factories in China, Untold: Inside the Shein Machine also found workers were given just one day off a month and had pay docked if an item was returned by an unhappy customer.
