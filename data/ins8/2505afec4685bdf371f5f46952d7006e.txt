A Hamas delegation will join additional delegations from other Palestinian terrorist groups on a visit to Damascus on Wednesday, Hamas spokesperson Hazem Qassem announced on Tuesday.

The visit comes as Hamas and the government of Syrian President Bashar Assad work to reconcile ties after years of frosty relations.
