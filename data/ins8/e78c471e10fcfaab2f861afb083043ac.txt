Toby + Chilli MorningsBy @TobyChilliShowOct 21, 2022With prices sky high on pretty much everything these days, we're all looking to save money wherever we can and more buyers are turning to online marketplaces for bargains. While peer-to-peer marketplaces like OfferUp and Facebook Marketplace can be great for finding deals, authorities are warning they're also home to scammers. And police departments across the country are investigating robberies and fraud related to these kinds of online purchases.
