Protecting Our Kids Act: House advances gun control bill
csmonitor.com
news
2022-06-03 14:45:30

A House panel advanced legislation Thursday that would raise the age limit for purchasing a semi-automatic rifle from 18 to 21 as Democrats moved quickly to put their stamp on gun legislation in response to mass shootings in Texas and New York by assailants who used such weapons to kill 31 people, including 19 children.The vote came as President Joe Biden gave a prime-time speech about the shootings and told Americans, 'Let's hear the call and the cry, let's meet the moment, let us finally do something.
