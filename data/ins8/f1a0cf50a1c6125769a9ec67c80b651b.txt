Image source, PA MediaAn independent Scotland would build an "economy that works for everyone", according to the first minister.Nicola Sturgeon's pledge came as she prepared to publish a paper outlining the economic argument for leaving the UK.Opposition politicians at Holyrood have criticised the plan to hold a referendum on 19 October next year.The UK government also said now was the wrong time to be talking about a new independence vote.On Sunday it emerged the prospectus, which will be unveiled at a media briefing in Edinburgh, will include details on how an independent Scotland would apply to join the European Union.
