Jets' Elijah Moore expresses frustration over lack of targets: ‘Don't understand'
nypost.com
news
2022-10-17 02:32:08

No one seemed to be looking for Elijah Moore on Sunday and it appeared to upset the Jets receiver.
During the Jets' 27-10 statement win over the Packers at Lambeau Field, the second-year wideout recorded zero targets. Moore took to Twitter to express his frustration after a Jets beat writer brought up the stat.
Elijah Moore was expected to have a breakout season this year but has yet to live up to those standards.Photo by Mitchell Leff/Getty Images
'If I say what I really wanna say… I'll be the selfish guy… we winning,' Moore wrote.
