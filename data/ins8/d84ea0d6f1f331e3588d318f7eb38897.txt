New York (CNN Business)The cover of "Madden" has always held a special place in the world of video games and sports, but this year's cover may stand out most of all.John Madden — the late NFL coach, announcer and namesake of the mega-popular football video game series — will grace the Madden 2023 cover, EA Sports announced on Wednesday. It will be the first time that Madden has been on the cover since 2000.In fact, the former broadcaster will be on three covers that will "pay tribute to a different chapter in Coach Madden's unforgettable life," according to EA.
