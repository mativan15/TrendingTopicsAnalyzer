The ED has arrested a man for allegedly using his crypto account to park illegal funds, in connection with its ongoing money laundering investigation against a Kolkata-based mobile gaming app company that is alleged to have duped many people. Romen Agarwal was arrested under the criminal sections of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) in the case against the app called 'E-nuggets' and its promoter and main accused Aamir Khan. He was sent to ED custody till October 28 by a special court in Kolkata, the Enforcement Directorate said in a statement.
