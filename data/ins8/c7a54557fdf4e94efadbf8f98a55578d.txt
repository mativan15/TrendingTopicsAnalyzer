Jayalalithaa death: Panel holds aide Sasikala guilty, recommends probe
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 06:48:00

The Justice A Arumughaswamy Commission of inquiry, which probed the circumstances leading to the death of former chief minister J Jayalalithaa in 2016, in its concluding remarks said her aide VK Sasikala 'have to be found fault with and investigation is to be ordered'.On Tuesday, the Tamil Nadu government tabled in the assembly the reports of separate commissions of inquiry that looked into the circumstances surrounding the death of Jayalalithaa and the 2018 police firing at Thoothukudi in the state.
