Venture Atlanta announces Siemba as a 2022 Showcase Company "Siemba's selection to Venture Atlanta is a huge validation of the cybersecurity problem statements we are tackling, the solutions and business models we have built and target market we are addressing" said Kannan Udayarajan, Siemba's Founder and CEO. ATLANTA (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Siemba announced today that it has been chosen out of a record-breaking 450-plus applicants as one of the top technology companies in the Southeast to be showcased at Venture Atlanta FORWARD 2022 on October 19-20.
