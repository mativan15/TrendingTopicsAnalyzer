Getty ImagesIndia 10-year bond yield fell 0.41 per cent to 5.89 after trading in 5.87-5.91 rangeNEW DELHI: The Nifty IT index traded positive around 10:29AM(IST)on Thursday in an upbeat market. Larsen & Toubro Infotech(up 2.22 per cent), L&T Technology(up 2.03 per cent), MPhasis(up 1.8 per cent), Mindtree(up 1.35 per cent) and Coforge(up 1.17 per cent) were among the top gainers. Tech Mahindra(down 0.83 per cent) were the top losers on the index. The Nifty IT index was up 0.76 per cent at 29484.5 at the time of writing this report.
