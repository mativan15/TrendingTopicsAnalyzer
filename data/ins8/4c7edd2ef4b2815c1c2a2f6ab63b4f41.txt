Jeremy Hunt is reversing almost all tax measures announced in Kwasi Kwarteng's disastrous growth plan just three weeks ago that have not started parliamentary legislation.In an emergency statement, the new Chancellor of the Exchequer said these measures were intended to provide confidence and stability in the financial markets which have been in turmoil ever since Mr Kwarteng's so-called mini-Budget.Speaking to the nation, Mr Hunt said:A central responsibility for any Government is to do what is necessary for economic stability.
