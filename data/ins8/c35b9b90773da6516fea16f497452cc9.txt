Kings Island announces Adventure Port: themed area with 2 new rides, enhancements to Adventure Express coaster
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 14:53:00

Kings Island on Wednesday announced a new themed area coming to the amusement park in 2023, which will see the addition of two rides and enhancements made to one of its roller coasters.

Kings Island, owned by Ohio amusement park operator Cedar Fair (NYSE: FUN), will debut Adventure Port for its upcoming 2023 season. The new themed area will include two rides – Sol Spin and Cargo Loco – as well as enhancements to the Adventure Express roller coaster, which opened in 1991.

Adventure Port will be located between the Coney Mall and the Action Zone.
