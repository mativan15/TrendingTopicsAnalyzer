Image caption, Rob Funnell busks in a damp Grimsby town centreLike a number of northern towns, Grimsby turned Conservative for the first time in decades at the 2019 election. BBC News' Kevin Shoesmith went to see what people there think of the current political landscape.Busker Rob Funnell is part-way through Oasis hit 'Don't Look Back in Anger' when I arrive in Victoria Street.Fresh from travelling around Australia, he's returned to a country in turmoil and a town in which some feel betrayed.Like many other former Labour strongholds which made up the 'red wall', Grimsby turned blue back in 2019's national vote.
