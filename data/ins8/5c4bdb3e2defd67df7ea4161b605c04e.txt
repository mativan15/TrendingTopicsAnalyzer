Max Verstappen backed to beat Charles Leclerc as Ferrari warned they may let driver down
express.co.uk
sport
2022-05-30 18:01:00

Max Verstappen has been tipped for the Formula One world title this season as Naomi Schiff has pointed out one clear advantage that he has over Charles Leclerc. The Dutchman is the current overall leader and extended his advantage over second-placed Leclerc when he overtook the Monegasque driver following a Ferrari mistake in the pits in the Monaco Grand Prix on Sunday.Naomi Schiff, who co-hosts the Any Driven Monday podcast on Sky Sports, said that Ferrari are making too many mistakes under pressure at present.
