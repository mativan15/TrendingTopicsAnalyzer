Image source, Premier NewsA Rangers fan has been ordered to stay away from his ex-partner for 10 years after repeatedly driving past her home and blasting out flute music. William Gibbs, 40, played traditional marching music outside her house at all hours of the day and night. He admitted subjecting the woman to coercive and controlling behaviour between 1 April 2019 and 14 May 2020.At Dundee Sheriff Court Gibbs was sentenced to 225 hours of unpaid work in the community. Sheriff Paul Brown also placed Gibbs, of Dundee, under social work supervision for three years and imposed a non-harassment order banning him from approaching or contacting his ex-partner for 10 years.
