Who won this year's Celebrity: SAS Who Dares Wins?
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 21:00:00

Maisie Smith, AJ Pritchard, Calum Best, and Ferne McCann have won this year's Celebrity: SAS Who Dares Wins.
During Sunday's final episode, Chief Instructor Rudy Reyes announced that the four famous faces had been successful in the final leg of the course, which saw them captured by the Hunter Force and questioned by a special team of interrogators.
The recruits were forced to maintain their cover story, while also being subjected to punishment such as ice baths and being buried in a box.
Rudy told the winners: ‘From day one, we demanded 100%.
