Mineral output in India grew 4.2% during April-August
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 12:30:00

Mineral output in India cumulatively rose 4.2 per cent in the first five months of the current financial year over the same period a year ago, provisional data from the Indian Bureau of Mines (IBM) showed. The output of key minerals in the month of August included 580 lakh tonnes of coal, 29 lakh tonnes of lignite, 2,829 million cu m of natural gas (utilised), 24 lakh tonnes of petroleum (crude) and 14.5 lakh tonnes of bauxite.

In August, however, the index of mineral production of mining and quarrying sector, at 99.
