October 20, 2022 01:55 PM Eastern Daylight Time QUEBEC--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cloud platform Voilà! announces the closing of its $13.75M CAD Series A funding round, led by Walter Ventures, with participation from Desjardins Capital and Investissement Quebec. This funding round will allow the company to further its mission to help businesses combat labor shortages.



Released in 2016, Voilà! delivers workforce optimization tools to simplify schedule management, staff replacements, as well as time and attendance tracking.
