 The Regina Police Service arrested a 22-year-old man early Saturday morning after a vehicle hit a police car from behind.
Officers said they observed a vehicle behind them driving erratically and without headlights near Dewdney Avenue around 1:45 a.m. and tried to initiate a traffic stop.The vehicle struck the police car, and officers went to assess the occupants, who were not injured.
