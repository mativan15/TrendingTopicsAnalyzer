HOUSTON, TEXAS, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Majic Wheels Corp. (OTC: MJWL), a Wyoming-based holding company priming itself as a mainstay in the disruptive tech space through strategic mergers and acquisitions, has today announced that its board of directors has accepted the resignation of David Chong as CEO, effective immediately. The board has appointed Sathyanandham (‘Sathya') Anguswami as CEO, effective immediately. Sathya is a technocrat and veteran fund manager, with over 30 years' experience in India, UAE, and US equity markets, as well as in Global Currency and Commodities F&O markets.
