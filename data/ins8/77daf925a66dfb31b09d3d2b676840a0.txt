YOUR iPhone has a clever trick that anyone with Instagram should know.

You can change your iPhone camera aspect ratio in secondsCredit: Apple / The Sun
The problem you might usually face is linked to aspect ratios.
This is the ratio that tells you how tall an image is versus its length.
Normally your iPhone shoots in 4:3 aspect ratio, which means for every three pixels horizontally, there are four vertically.
But when upload an image to an Instagram Story, you'll find that it doesn't fill the screen.
