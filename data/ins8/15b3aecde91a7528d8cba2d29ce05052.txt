Eastern chamber of commerce hires manager to oversee future Regional Innovation Center
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 00:35:00

Oct. 19—NEW LONDON — The Chamber of Commerce of Eastern Connecticut has hired city resident Joshua Posey to oversee its planned Regional Innovation Center.

The center on 92 Eugene O'Neill Drive will serve as the chamber's headquarters and create a startup incubator and co-working space.

Posey, former membership development and marketing director for the Ocean Community YMCA, will be the center's business development and operations manager. Posey's role will focus on developing and managing the center as well as supporting economic development for New London and the region.
