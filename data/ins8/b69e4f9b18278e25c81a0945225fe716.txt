Global supply chains are suffering unprecedented disruption in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, with the average large business reporting a loss of $182 million in annual revenue as a result. But many supply chain challenges predate the chaos of the last couple of years. Digital transformation across domestic and global supply chains is long overdue. Improved visibility, increased flexibility and effective communication are crucial for efficient and resilient supply chain operations.
