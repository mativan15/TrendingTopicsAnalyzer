(Left to right top) MBZUAI Provost Fakhri Karray, MBZUAI President Eric Xing, General Manager, IBM Middle East, and Africa Saad Toma, and IBM Senior Vice President and Chief Communications Officer Jonathan Adashek, (left to right bottom) MBZUAI Vice President for Public Affairs and Alumni Relations Sultan Al Hajji, and General Manager IBM Gulf and Levant Wael Abdoush.MBZUAI has announced plans for a strategic collaboration with IBM (NYSE: IBM).
