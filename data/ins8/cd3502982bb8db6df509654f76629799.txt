Xbox Series S could be dropped by big budget games at launch
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 11:09:52

REPORTS claim that a number of developers from big budget games have been petitioning to stop launching titles for Xbox Series S.
Microsoft has two versions in its popular Xbox Series line of modern console releases.
Developers prefer the more powerful Series X.Credit: Reuters
The Xbox Series X comes with a disc drive, and is far more powerful, but also comes with a heftier price tag.
On the other hand, the Xbox Series S is a budget version of the hardware that only runs digital games, and has reduced hardware specifications.
