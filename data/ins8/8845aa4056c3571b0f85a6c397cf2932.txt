Surge Energy (OTCPK:ZPTAF), like so many other junior producers, had a conservative financial record going into the OPEC pricing war followed by the coronavirus demand destruction. But fiscal year 2020 and the recovery in fiscal year 2021 were far more challenging than anyone ever figured. Luckily for shareholders, fiscal year 2022 so far promises a second chance to straighten things out.
