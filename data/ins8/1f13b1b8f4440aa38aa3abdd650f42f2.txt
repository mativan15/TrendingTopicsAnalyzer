Aussies not warming to plug-in hybrids, says Kia
cairnspost.com.au
news
2022-06-02 20:00:00

A leading car maker has raised questions about the long-term viability of an emerging green technology, after customers have failed to warm to it.Kia appears to have pulled the plug on plug-in hybrids, choosing to import only self-charging hybrid and full electric versions of its new Niro SUV.Plug-in hybrids, which can travel roughly 50 to 80km on electric power alone before switching to petrol, were initially thought to be well suited to Australia, where our love of long road trips has created reluctance to adopt EVs because of their limited range.
