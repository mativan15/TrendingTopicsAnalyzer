Getty In the world of startups, things move fast. Recently at unitQ, one of our sales reps overpromised a potential customer that our technology could meet their needs. The problem here was that our product didn't offer a solution to this specific use case. At first, this situation seemed like a big issue, full of potential complications. But it turned into a fruitful opportunity for different teams—that don't usually interact—to build a solution together.
