Investing can be a lot to take in, especially if you're just getting started. While you can hire a financial advisor or broker to help (or invest in specific stocks on your own), a robo-advisor may help grow your money with little time commitment. Robo-advisors put your money to work without your 24/7 attention -- and are typically much cheaper than traditional options. All you need is a little cash and and a few basic financial goals to get started.Robo-advisors are online financial investment platforms that create and manage an investment portfolio based on your financial goals and individual needs.
