IoB to pay ₹1 lakh for not crediting ₹500 to customer
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 07:15:00

Dharwad: The District Consumer Forum imposed a penalty of Rs 1.02 lakh on Indian Overseas Bank , Saptapur branch, Dharwad , for failing to reimburse Rs 500 to a customer.IoB customer Siddesh Hebballi complained that he tried to withdraw Rs 500 from an IoB ATM on November 28, 2020. But the ATM failed to deliver the cash. However, he said Rs 500 was debited from his account though he had not received the Rs 500 . Siddesh told the IoB manager on December 2, 2020, about the issue and requested that the money be credited.
