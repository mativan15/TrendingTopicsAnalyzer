About The Author : Krista Mahr

Krista Mahr is a health care reporter for POLITICO Pro covering public health and the CDC. Krista was also based in New Delhi as a South Asia correspondent for Reuters and Time's South Asia bureau chief. She has an MA from UC Berkeley's journalism school and a BA from UC Santa Cruz.
