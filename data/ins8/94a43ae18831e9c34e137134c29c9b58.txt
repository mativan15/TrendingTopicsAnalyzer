Keeping with the spirit of the festival, a Karwa Chauth event was hosted by Saloni Kesarwani at a hotel in Gomti Nagar. Ladies were seen with henna applied on their hands and all dressed up in vibrant and embellished ethnic wear which added colour and zing to the do.Pratibha (L) and Ashita(L) Khushboo (R) NamrataFor their entertainment, a group of dancers was called from a dance academy to perform along with a musician. Not only these artists but various ladies also enjoyed performing solo as well as with a group on some of their favourite Bollywood songs.
