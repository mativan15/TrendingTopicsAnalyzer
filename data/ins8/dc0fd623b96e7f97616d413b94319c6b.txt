Brexit Britain still owes EU £36billion over May's hated divorce bill
express.co.uk
politics
2022-10-15 07:17:00

According to the EU's court of auditors' annual report, the UK still owes the EU €41.8billion (£36bn). Because of an agreement agreed upon by former UK Prime Minister Theresa May on a so-called "divorce bill", the Brussels executive is still able to demand huge amounts of cash from the UK. Britons voted for Brexit in 2016 but negotiations and a two-year transition period saw the UK fully detached from the bloc only in 2020."At balance sheet date, the EU accounts showed a net receivable due from the UK of €41.
