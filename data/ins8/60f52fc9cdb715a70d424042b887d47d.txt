Published: 14:24 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 14:24 EDT, 21 October 2022 After hitting their offensive stride against the Carolina Hurricanes, the Edmonton Oilers hope to keep rolling when they face the St. Louis Blues Saturday to continue their season-opening six-game homestand. The Oilers rebounded from back-to-back losses to Calgary and Buffalo to skate past the Hurricanes 6-4 on Thursday. "Being 2-2 is nothing to write home about, but we'll take it," said Connor McDavid, who leads the Oilers with 10 points (five goals, five assists) in four games.
