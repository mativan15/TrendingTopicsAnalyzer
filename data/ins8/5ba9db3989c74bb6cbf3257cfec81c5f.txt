Ranchi: Ranchi Municipal Corporation on Monday visited the Enforcement Directorate office for a brief period and submitted documents in a sealed cover. It is learnt that the documents may be related to the Pulse hospital, which is under the ED scanner in connection with the money laundering. Meanwhile, Godda MP Nishikant Dubey said that the central agencies have come across proofs regarding illegal trade with Bangladesh even though he did not elaborate much. TNN
