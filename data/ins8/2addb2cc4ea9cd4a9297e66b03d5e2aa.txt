The Playlist George Clooney and Julia Roberts have been making movies together for more than two decades now, first sharing the screen in 2001's 'Ocean's Eleven,' and re-teaming for 'Ocean's Twelve,' 'Money Monster,' and the Clooney-directed 'Confessions of a Dangerous Mind' (though they shared no scenes). Yet it's not just their shared charisma and charm that accounts for so much of what works in their new movie 'Ticket to Paradise.' It's that we now, as an audience, have a relationship with them, so we're pulling for them in a way we don't when a random Disney personality and Instagram influencer are potentially paired in a new Netflix rom-com.
