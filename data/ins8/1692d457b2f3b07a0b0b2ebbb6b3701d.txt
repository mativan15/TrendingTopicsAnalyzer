Arkansas State transfer Vidal Scott to play for Texas Tech football
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-02 01:11:53

The Texas Tech football team added some depth in the trenches.

On Wednesday, Arkansas State transfer Vidal Scott announced his intentions to play for the Red Raiders football program via social media.

'BEYOND BLESSED! LETS WORK,' he Tweeted.

Scott tallied 24 total tackles with 5.0 TFL last season for the Red Wolves.

In two season at Arkansas State, Scott notched 42 tackles, nine for loss, to go with three sacks.
