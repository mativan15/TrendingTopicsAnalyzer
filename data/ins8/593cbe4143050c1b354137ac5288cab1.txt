UK peer's bid to save refinery from sanctions
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-29 23:01:00

A former British government minister has met the tanaiste and the finance minister to discuss his planned takeover of the Aughinish Alumina refinery in Limerick to protect it against Russian sanctions.Greg Barker, a Conservative life peer with the title Baron Barker of Battle, met Leo Varadkar and Paschal Donohoe at Government Buildings on March 21. Two weeks earlier he had resigned as executive chairman of En+ Group, Aughinish Alumina's Moscow-based parent company.Barker has been negotiating with a consortium of non-Russian investors and American financiers about buying the conglomerate's European operations from UC Rusal, a subsidiary company that controls the group's minerals business.
