The cyber crime team of Gautam Budh Nagar Police arrested a 38-year-old Nigerian national on Friday for allegedly duping over 300 Indian women of crores of rupees on the pretext of marriage after befriending them on social media platforms and matrimonial websites. The accused has been identified as Garuba Galumje, who hails from Lagos in Nigeria, and was staying in south Delhi's Kishangarh area from where he was arrested. Police also seized a forged Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) identity card from his possession.
