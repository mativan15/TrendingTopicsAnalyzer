As World Environment Day is nearing, with the theme of this year being 'Only One Earth", it proposes a critical question - How capable we are in our mitigation measures. Let's take real estate sector, for example.The real estate industry accounts for almost 36% of total energy use and 39% of energy and process-related carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions, with 11% of those emissions coming from the manufacturing of building materials and products such as steel, cement, and glass.The following are the major global trends in the real estate industry:Real estate investors surveyed believe there will be growth in the market in the coming yearsThe respondents forecast that sustainability criteria which include a focus on sustainable energy will continue to play a decisive role in the purchasing decisions of institutional investors in the future.
