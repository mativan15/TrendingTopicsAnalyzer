June 02, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SEATTLE--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Infobip, a global leader in omnichannel communications, has been ranked as a leading CPaaS provider in two prestigious analyst reports. The cloud communications platform has been named Leader in Omdia Universe Report: Selecting a CPaaS Platform Provider 2022, and, with its purchase of Peerless Network, has been hailed by 451 Research as a top CPaaS provider in the U.S. market.



The Omdia Universe report presents an in-depth review of the market to create a forward-looking matrix of capabilities, attributes and features based on vendor briefings and supplementary research.
