Investment Furthers Altamont's Multi-Unit Consumer Sector LeadershipPALO ALTO, Calif., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Altamont Capital Partners ("Altamont") today announced the acquisition of Service Minds, a leading provider of electrical, plumbing and HVAC services based in Sarasota, Florida. Owned and operated by David Connolly since 2014, Service Minds has been a leading franchisee in the Mister Sparky system since its inception and today also operates in the One Hour Heating & AC and Benjamin Franklin Plumbing systems, servicing the Southeastern United States across all three trades.
