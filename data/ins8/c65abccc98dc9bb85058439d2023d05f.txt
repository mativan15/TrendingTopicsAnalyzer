Allison Transmission Recognized as Supplier of the Year by Idealease

October 19, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time INDIANAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Allison Transmission is honored to announce that the company has been named Supplier of the Year by Idealease, a premier commercial truck lease and rental company in North America, with over 45,000 trucks, tractors and trailers in their fleet. The award, which Allison also received in 2018, was recently announced at the Idealease 40th Annual Meeting.



'We're proud to recognize Allison as Supplier of the Year for the second time in four years,' said Dan Murphy, President and CEO of Idealease.
