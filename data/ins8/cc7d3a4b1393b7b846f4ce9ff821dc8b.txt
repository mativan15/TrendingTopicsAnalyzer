Festive cheer alludes Tracxn Tech in grey markets ahead of listing
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 14:00:00

ET SpotlightThe Rs 309.38-crore initial public offering of Tracxn Technologies was open for subscription between October 10 and 12The festive lights have lifted the mood on Dalal Street, but apparently failed to light up Tracxn Technologies' fate on Wednesday, just a day ahead of its listing on the bourses, if one goes by the grey market premium. According to market watchers, the company is witnessing negligible trades in the unofficial market as it is trading at a discount of up to Rs 3 apiece. Even analysts are not very positive for any sort of listing pop at the debut.
