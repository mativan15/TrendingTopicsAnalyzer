 The McMaster Marauders dropped to 2-5 in the Ontario University Athletics football season after a 14-10 loss against the Queen's Gaels in Kingston on Saturday afternoon.
On a windy day at Richardson Stadium, McMaster quarterback Andreas Dueck completed 25 of 41 pass attempts for 256 yards and was intercepted twice.He also ran for Mac's only touchdown of the game, a one-yard score that put the Marauders up 10-5 midway through the third quarter.
