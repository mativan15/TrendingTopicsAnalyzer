The Mike Taylor ShowOct 21, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesDak Prescott makes his return under center when the Dallas Cowboys host the Detroit Lions at AT&T Stadium on Sunday. Prescott has been out since suffering a hand injury in Week One. Dallas has fared well without their starting quarterback thanks to the third-best scoring defense in the NFL. The Cowboys head into Week Seven with a 4-and-2 record. Meanwhile, the Lions should be well-rested coming off of their bye week. Detroit has lost its last three games despite boasting the third-best scoring offense in the league.
