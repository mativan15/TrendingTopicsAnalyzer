Rupee rises by 4 paise to close at 77.54 against US dollar on stocks rally
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 15:00:00

ETBFSIThe week started on a positive note for risk assets as softening of the lockdown in China boosted the sentiments, said Dilip Parmar, Research Analyst, HDFC Securities.The rupee rose further by 4 paise to close at 77.54 against the US dollar on Monday, supported by positive domestic equities and a weak American currency overseas. Forex traders said the rupee consolidated in a narrow range as elevated crude oil prices weighed on the local unit. At the interbank forex market, the rupee opened at 77.
