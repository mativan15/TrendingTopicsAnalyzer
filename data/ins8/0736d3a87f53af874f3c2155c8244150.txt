Variety - TV News During the 2020-21 TV season, a study found that streaming companies put more women in positions of creative power than broadcast networks did, though broadcasters featured slightly more women as major characters in their shows. But a year later, that same study has found that streamers now beat out networks on both fronts by slight margins.The study, titled 'Boxed In,' has been run by Dr. Martha Lauzen, executive director of San Diego State University's Center for the Study of Women in Television and Film, for 25 years now.
