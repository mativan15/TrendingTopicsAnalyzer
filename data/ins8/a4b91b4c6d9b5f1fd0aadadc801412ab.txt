Toronto Stocks Edge Lower; Jamieson Wellness Rises on Acquisition in U.S.
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 16:07:00

By Adriano Marchese Stocks in Toronto pared back some gains of the previous day on Wednesday mid-trading after a rocky morning, with the S&P/TSX Composite Index falling 97.83 points. The Bank of Canada lifted the policy rate to 1.5% from 1.0%, warning that inflation is heading higher and the economy is overheating and that it is willing go further to drive inflation down to its 2% target. In the session, most sectors were lower, led by process industries and consumer durables while commercial services and health tech led the few gainers.
