Principle Construction completes renovation project that added office space to Chicago-area warehouse
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 20:16:00

Principle Construction Corp. has completed a renovation project that has added office space to a warehouse in Elgin, Illinois.

CV-Tek, a food packaging manufacturer, is the sole tenant and Middleby Companies is the landlord.

Total costs for the renovations exceeded $3 million, according to Mark Frane, a superintendent for the project.

The warehouse, located at 440 S. McClean Blvd., previously had 78,000 square feet of warehouse space. Now it has 56,000 square feet of warehouse and 22,000 square feet office space, according to Vicky Dollenmaier, a spokeswoman for Principle Construction general contractor for the project.
