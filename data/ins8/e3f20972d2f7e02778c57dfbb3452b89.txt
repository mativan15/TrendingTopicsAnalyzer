Animal rights group PETA has written to Lego, asking them to rebrand toy farm sets as sanctuaries to avoid misleading kids about the 'filthy, blood-soaked' realities of farmingCampaigners believe kids shouldn't be misled about "the horror and cruelty behind their dairy milkshakes and beef burgers" (Stock Photo)Animal rights campaigners have written to LEGO, blasting the iconic toy manufacturer for misleading children about the '"filthy, blood-soaked" realities of farming In an open letter from PETA, activists slammed LEGO's new farm set, which they argue gives children a rose-tinted view of "the horror and cruelty behind their dairy milkshakes and beef burgers.
