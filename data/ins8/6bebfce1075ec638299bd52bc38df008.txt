Why Student Loan Forgiveness Could Boost Entrepreneurs
inc.com
business
2022-06-02 16:45:00

Small business growth could get a boost if President Joe Biden has his way with student loan forgiveness.Around a third of small business owners say that student loan debt has hampered their ability to grow their companies, according to a new survey from Capital One bank and the NextGen Chamber of Commerce, a business organization that helps entrepreneurs. The survey, completed in partnership with decision intelligence company Morning Consult, polled 1,200 small business owners and 300 auto dealership owners in late March.
