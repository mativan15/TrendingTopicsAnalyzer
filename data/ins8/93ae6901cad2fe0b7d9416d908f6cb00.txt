ONESOLUTION™ and BRUSHEVA™ Engagement
HONG KONG, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- ONESOLUTION INNOTECH LTD, the fully owned subsidiary of King Resources, Inc. (OTC: KRFG), the Brand and Distribution network maker of IOT products and services, today announced the extension of the product line through a new sole distribution agreement with BRUSHEVA™, Brusheva was founded by dentists concerned about the current solutions in the market for dental health. They envisioned developing an electric toothbrush that could bring all the benefits and solutions to the problems they frequently saw with their patients.
