The death toll in the latest suspected hooch tragedy in 'dry' Bihar has shot up to 24 after five more people, undergoing treatment in Gaya, died on Thursday, according to locals and family members of the dead. Of them, four were at Anugrah Narayan Magadh Medical College and Hospital (ANMCH) in Gaya and the sixth at a hospital at Sherghati in the same district.The latest victims were identified as Nanhak Chaudhary of Chaudhary Bigaha, Ashok Paswan of Khiriyawan, Rambhaju Rikiyasan of Noniadih and Sanju Ram of Gunjar Bigaha in Aurangabad and Kailash Yadav of Pathara in Gaya.
