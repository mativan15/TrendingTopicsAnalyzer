Alex Sims is an associate professor at the University of Auckland Business School. Dulani Jayasuriya is a lecturer at the University of Auckland Business School. OPINION: Confidence in stablecoins plummeted this month as the cryptocurrency market took a dive and investors withdrew billions from the most popular stablecoin, Tether. Meanwhile, another stablecoin, Terra, and its sister currency Luna also spiralled, wiping out some investors' life savings. How investors respond will be key to the future of cryptocurrencies.
