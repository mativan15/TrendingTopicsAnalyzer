Marriott Bonvoy offers members chance to meet Mumbai Indians at charity dinner
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 15:45:00

AgenciesWhile initially it was only for Marriott Bonvoy members who could redeem this moment, fans of the team were also given a chance to participate in a contest to be a part of the event.As part of its association with Mumbai Indians, Marriott Bonvoy, the loyalty program of Marriott International's portfolio of 30 hotel brands, has introduced a new experience where its members could bid their points for a chance to meet and dine with the IPL team. When a member redeems this experience, Marriott Bonvoy will also donate equivalent to the number of points raised towards a charity.
