FORT WASHINGTON, Pa., May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Toll Brothers, Inc. (NYSE: TOL) ( TollBrothers.com ), the nation's leading builder of luxury homes, through its Toll Brothers Apartment Living® rental division, and PGIM Real Estate, the real estate investment and financing business of PGIM, the $1.5 trillion global asset management business of Prudential Financial (NYSE: PRU) have announced another new joint venture to develop Lyra, a 34-story, 432-unit multifamily rental community in downtown Boston, MA.
