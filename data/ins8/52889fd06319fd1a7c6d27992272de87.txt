Indo-Chinese cuisine is steeped in history and was created by those who emigrated from Canton and Guangdong in China with their rich gastronomic culture and settled in Kolkata and Mumbai, where they began incorporating Indian spices into their Chinese dishes. Here's four recipes from Soho's Fatt Pundit, which has just launched a new site in Covent Garden, celebrating this unique diaspora cuisine.
