Former Bran Van 3000 singer Stéphane Moraille, says the multiracial band was "a little capsule of what Montreal was," but the record industry struggled to embrace it. (François Nadeau/Audiogram)If you are old enough to remember the year 1997, then you almost certainly remember the song "Drinking in L.A.," the breakout hit by Montreal group Bran Van 3000. It was pretty ubiquitous in the spring and summer of that year, and it didn't sound like anything else on the radio at the time: it was a little bit alt rock, a little bit hip-hop, and a little bit some third thing that you couldn't quite place.
