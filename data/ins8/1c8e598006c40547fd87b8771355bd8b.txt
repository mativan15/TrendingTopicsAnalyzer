ReutersNifty continues to climb a wall of worry as it looks to be in the final leg of its rally before heading for a correction.Shares of Container Corporation of India Ltd. rose 0.22 per cent to Rs 644.95 at 12:28PM hours (IST) on Friday. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up start to the session. As per data available on BSE, total traded quantity on the counter stood at 37,811 shares with a turnover of Rs 2.42 crore till 12:28PM (IST). The stock traded at a price-to-earnings (P/E) multiple of 36.
