Wisconsin's All States Ag Parts acquires Kansas forklift parts company
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 20:02:00

A Kansas business that has been supplying warehouses and manufacturers for more than 50 years has sold to a Wisconsin company.

Hudson-based All States Ag Parts LLC purchased Arrow Acquisition LLC, according to a release. Arrow Acquisition is the holding company for Arrow Material Handling Products and OE Attachments. Terms of the deal were not disclosed. Arrow was a portfolio company of Overland Park, Kansas-based private equity company Konza Valley Capital Inc.

Lenexa, Kansas-based Arrow was founded in 1969 in Kansas City's Blue Valley Industrial Valley, specializing in the sale of parts and attachments for forklifts.
