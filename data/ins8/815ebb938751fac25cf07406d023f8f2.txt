Lulu group may invest Rs 1,500 crore in developing a mall in Noida
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 15:00:00

Image courtesy: Company websiteAbu Dhabi headquartered Lulu Group might invest Rs 1500 crore to develop a mall in Greater Noida in NCR, according to the Greater Noida Authority. The representatives of the company met the CEO of the authority and requested them to identify suitable land. Lulu group, which already has an operational mall in Lucknow, bas committed Rs 2500 crore in Uttar Pradesh in three new projects in the state - one Lulu Mall each in Varanasi and Prayagraj and Lulu Food Proecessing Hub in Greater Noida.
