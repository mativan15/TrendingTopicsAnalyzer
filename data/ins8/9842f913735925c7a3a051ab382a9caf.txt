Prince Albert Grand Council to explore policing options for First Nations through agreement with Sask., Ottawa

The Prince Albert Grand Council, the Government of Saskatchewan and the Government of Canada have signed an agreement to explore new, community-oriented ways to deliver police services and improve safety in First Nations communities. (Don Somers/CBC - image credit)

Grand Chief Brian Hardlotte of the Prince Albert Grand Council joined federal and provincial public safety ministers on Monday in agreement to prioritize community safety.

Hardlotte, alongside the ministers, signed a letter of intent which could pave the path to self-administered policing by PAGC communities.
