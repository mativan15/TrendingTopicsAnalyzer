Email Facebook Twitter LinkedIn Venture capital funding may be slowing, but not for artificial intelligence startups it seems.
A day after London-based Stability AI became Europe's newest unicorn after locking up a $101 million raise, Austin, Texas-based Jasper closed a $125 million Series A funding round at a $1.5 billion valuation.
The round was led by Insight Partners and joined by others including Bessemer Venture Partners, IVP, Foundation Capital, Founders Circle Capital and HubSpot Ventures.
