The summer months are fast approaching, which means we are one step closer to the 2022 college football season. The Auburn Tigers finished the year 6-7 but can they do even better this year? We will soon find out but first, we check in on another set of bowl projections.
According to 247Sports, the Tigers would go bowling in Florida against an opponent with some history.
Brad Crawford has Auburn heading to the Gasparilla Bowl in Tampa, Florida. They would face-off with the Virginia Tech Hokies of the ACC.
