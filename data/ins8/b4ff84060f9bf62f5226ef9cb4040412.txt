WORTH, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Godspeed Capital Management LP ('Godspeed Capital'), a lower middle-market Defense & Government services, solutions, and technology focused private equity firm, today announced the successful acquisition of E4H Environments for Health Architecture ('E4H'), a leading provider of consulting, planning, and facility design architecture services for the healthcare and health science and technology industry. Financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
