Opinion: How the world can foot the bill on urgent climate action
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-10-15 11:40:49

The annual meetings of the World Bank could have important implications for the climate crisis and could help shift the trajectory of global warming. Multilateral development banks can make or break the transition to renewable energy, and governments are their shareholders. It's time for those governments to step up and make sure the banks are prepared to advance the process.
To stop climate chaos from causing enormous human suffering and decimating the global economy, we must bend the emissions curve down, now.
