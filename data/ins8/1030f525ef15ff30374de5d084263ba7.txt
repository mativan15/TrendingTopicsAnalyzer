Sydney, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Sydney, New South Wales - Edith Cowan University (ECU) Online recently announced the launch of its new accelerated online Master of Project Management. This degree is 100% online and is aimed at professionals who want to gain an advanced understanding of project management principles. It covers project leadership, program management, agile methodologies and more. Students understand what makes an effective leader, managing resources and changing expectations in a team environment.
