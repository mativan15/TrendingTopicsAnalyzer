Varanasi court rejects 7 applications for becoming party in Gyanvapi mosque-Shringari Gauri case
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 16:15:00

A Varanasi court on Monday turned down seven applications for becoming a party in the Gyanvapi-Shringar Gauri case. Hearing the case, district judge AK Vishvesha rejected the plea of seven people. It will give its verdict on three other applications, that it heard during the day, on the next date of hearing on October 21, government counsel Mahendra Pandey said. The people who had filed applications for becoming a party include former mahant of Kashi Vishwanath Temple Kulpati Tiwari, Hindu Sena national president Vishnu Gupta, weaver Mukhtar Ahmed Ansari, president of regional Brahmin Mahasabha Ajay Kumar Sharma among others, Pandey said.
