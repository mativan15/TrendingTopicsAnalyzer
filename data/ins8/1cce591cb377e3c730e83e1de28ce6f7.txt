The Masked Dancer: Who was revealed as Odd Socks is eliminated

Odd Socks is the fourth star to be eliminated from The Masked Dancer, and fans were stunned to learn it was really Kimberley Walsh behind the mask. The latest reveal came amid a double unmasking as the competition nears its end, with Sea Slug being revealed as Denise Lewis earlier in the night. 
Last week, Candlestick was revealed to be Bake Off star Liam Charles, with Pillar and Post also being unmasked as David and Frankie Seaman. 
The panel were on the money with Tomato Sauce, who they worked out was Steph McGovern, while the Cactus turned out to be choirmaster Gareth Malone.
