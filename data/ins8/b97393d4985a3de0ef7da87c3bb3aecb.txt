Stanley Black & Decker names CFO to CEO role
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 11:28:04

Stanley Black & Decker Inc. SWK, -0.83% announced its succession plan on Wednesday, naming Chief Financial Officer Donald Allan, Jr. to the chief executive position, effective July 1. He succeeds James Loree, who has been CEO since 2016. Corbin Walburger, the company's vice president of corporate business development, has been named to the CFO position on an interim basis during the company's search for a permanent replacement. Stanley Black & Decker reaffirmed its full-year earnings-per-share guidance of $7.
