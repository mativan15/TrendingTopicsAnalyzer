Scotiabank Gold American Express cardholders earn Scene+ points, which can be redeemed for travel, gift cards, merchandise or a statement credit.
Earning rewards
You can really rake in the rewards with this card. Firstly, if you apply before October 31st, you can take advantage of their current welcome offer. You can get 20,000 bonus Scene+ points by spending at least $1,000 in the first 3 months and another 20,000 bonus Scene+ points for spending at least $7,500 in the first year. In total, that's 40,000 points worth up to $400.
