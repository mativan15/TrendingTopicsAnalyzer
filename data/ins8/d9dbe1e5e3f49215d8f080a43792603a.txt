jetcityimage/iStock Editorial via Getty Images AutoZone (NYSE:AZO) is one of the world's leading companies in the automotive aftermarket. It provides a comprehensive range of new and remanufactured automotive hard parts, maintenance items, accessories, and non-automotive products. Its flagship ALLDATA brand has helped the company establish a solid online retail foundation. FY '22 seems to be strong, with increasing adjusted ROIC and store count, but a closer look at AZO's decreasing margin and ROA on top of its unattractive valuation makes this stock risky as of this writing.
