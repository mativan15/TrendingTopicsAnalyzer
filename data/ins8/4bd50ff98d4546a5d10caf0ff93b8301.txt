Collider.com Editor's Note: The following contains spoilers for all of Season 1 of The Rings of Power.As we learn from The Stranger (Daniel Weyman) in the season finale of The Rings of Power, the word Istar means 'wise one' or 'wizard'. There are many clues indicating who exactly this wizard is. To understand what the identity of The Stranger might be, it's important to look into the deeper lore that Tolkien wrote about their arrival in Middle-earth. Tolkien explains how the Istari were five wizards sent to Middle-earth by the Valar as emissaries to help people in their fight against Sauron.
