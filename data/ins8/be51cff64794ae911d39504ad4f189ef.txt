Music director and singer Anu Malik is distraught that his friend and associate KK is no more. Taking stock of his emotions and feelings, Malik says, "I am shattered. KK and I worked together extensively, he sang so many songs for me. According to me, KK was the new-age Mohammed Rafi . Jitni masumiyat aur tameez Rafi saab mein thi, utni hi KK mein bhi thi (KK had the same innocence and etiquette as Mohd Rafi). He was a great singer and a great human being. He used to love me a lot. He sang for me in Mujhe Kuch Kehna Hai and Main Prem Ki Deewani Hoon,"Revealing KK's simple ways and his affable personality Malik adds, "He was too good a person.
