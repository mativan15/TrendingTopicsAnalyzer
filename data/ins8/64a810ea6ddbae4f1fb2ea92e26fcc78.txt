Chelsea Handler To Host 2023 Critics Choice Awards
deadline.com
news
2022-10-20 17:12:55

Mike Rosenthal




Chelsea Handler has been tapped to host the 2023 Critics Choice Awards. The 28th annual trophy show will be held Sunday, January 15 at the Fairmont Century Plaza in Los Angeles, and will air live from 7-10 PM ET/delayed PT on the CW.

	Handler takes over the reins from Taye Diggs who has hosted the show for the past four years. 

	Handler served as host of E!'s Chelsea Lately for seven years, during which she was the only female late-night talk show host on-air. She went on to launch her documentary series Chelsea Does followed by her talk show Chelsea on Netflix in 2016.
