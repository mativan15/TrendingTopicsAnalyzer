Pune: ED seeks possession of ABIL's plot in money laundering case
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 03:30:00

MUMBAI: Enforcement Directorate (ED) served notice to the company of Pune businessman Avinash Bhosale to handover the possession of the office plot of Avinash Bhosale Infrastructure Ltd or ABIL to them after the adjudication authority had confirmed the attachment of the premises.The ED had attached the plot, worth over Rs 4 crore (current marker worth over Rs 100 crore) last August in a money laundering case they have registered against him and on Tuesday, the adjudication authority had confirmed the attachment.
