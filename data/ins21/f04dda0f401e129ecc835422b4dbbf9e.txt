Grand jury indicts former official on murder charge in stabbing death of Las Vegas journalist
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 23:47:00

A former Nevada county official accused of fatally stabbing a Las Vegas journalist who was investigating his office has been indicted by a grand jury on murder.

Robert Telles, 45, was arrested and charged last month in connection with the death of Las Vegas Review-Journal reporter Jeff German, who police said was found stabbed to death outside his home on Sept. 3.
