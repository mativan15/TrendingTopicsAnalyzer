Arsenal's impressive start to the season has seen them establish themselves as early leaders of the Premier League, but one of their former players doesn't expect it to lastJermaine Pennant has talked down Arsenal's hopes this season Arsenal's Premier League hopes will "cookie crumble" amid early talk of their title chances, according to Jermaine Pennant.
