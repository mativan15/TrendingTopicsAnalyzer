Julia Fox claims her Hermès Birkin bag was ‘attacked by a machete'
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 10:34:28

Julia Fox has revealed one of her Hermes Birkin bags has deep cuts on it, which she claims were from an attack with a machete.
The Uncut Gems star, 32, took to TikTok to share the wild story with her followers, stitching a video to another user who praised the ‘lived-in look' the bags get over time.
Showing off the deep welts in the grey bag, Julia claimed to her followers: ‘I love her, but she's been through a lot.
‘That's a little hard to see there, but she was actually attacked by a machete. I'm not kidding — that actually happened to this bag and me.
