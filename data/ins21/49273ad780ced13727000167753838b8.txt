Below Deck's Captain Lee recalls putting drunk star in hotel before firing them

2022-10-20 22:39:23

Below Deck star Captain Lee Rosbach might be a firm favorite of the Bravo franchise but you really don't want to get on his bad side.
Fans of the show will know that he has very strict rules in place for his crew during every charter season, mainly urging them not to ‘embarrass themselves or the boat' – and is happy to offer a plane ticket home to anyone who doesn't follow his guidelines.
Something Dane Jackson found out the hard way in season three, following an explosive cast night out…
Appearing at BravoCon alongside his fellow captains from the other spin-off programs, he was asked about his least favorite crew member, and did not hold back, recalling the moment he was forced to put the deckhand in a hotel over his antics.
