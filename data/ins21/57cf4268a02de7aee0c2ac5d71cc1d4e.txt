KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy Nik RajkovicOct 21, 2022While President Joe Biden spouts off about nuclear Armageddon, national security experts warn the U.S. is nowhere near ready to defend itself. It's not that we don't have nuclear warheads. Edward Turzanski says our enemies are investing much more on new technology. "China is on a modernization path where they're spending money on space, cyber, navy and nuclear," he says. "We are in a very precarious position." Turzanski says the Biden administration and current Pentagon leaders are too focused on woke policies.
