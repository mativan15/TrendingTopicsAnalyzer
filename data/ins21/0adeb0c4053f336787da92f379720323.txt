Demand for makeup artists booms as Kolkata sees an uptick in weddings, shoots
indiatimes.com
beauty
2022-05-29 20:15:00

The makeup and fashion industry, which was badly hit by COVID-19, is finally on the road to recovery after the pandemic slump. Makeup artists have now set their goals for growth rather than survival. Be it weddings, film assignments, web series, TV serials, ad shoots or ramp shows — every segment is trying to bounce back, providing more work opportunities for them. CT spoke to a few makeup artists to learn how they are getting back to their normal lives. Read on… 
‘Too many talented makeup artists in the industry have made the market competitive'

Makeup artist Aniruddha Chakladar, who has been in the profession for more than two decades, feels that the situation has improved but the competition is getting tougher with each passing day.
