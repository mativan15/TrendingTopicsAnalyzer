NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Juan Monteverde, founder and managing partner of the class action firm Monteverde & Associates PC (the "M&A Class Action Firm"), a national securities firm rated Top 50 in the 2018-2021 ISS Securities Class Action Services Report and headquartered at the Empire State Building in New York City, is investigating KnowBe4, Inc. (KNBE), relating to its proposed acquisition by Vista Equity Partners. Under the terms of the agreement, KNBE shareholders are expected to receive $24.
