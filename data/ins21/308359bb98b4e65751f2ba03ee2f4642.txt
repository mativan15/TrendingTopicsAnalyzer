TV18 Broadcast Q2 Results: Profit falls 95.5% YoY to Rs 10.28 crore, revenue rises 12.6%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 14:00:00

Media firm on Tuesday reported a 95.55 per cent decline in its consolidated net profit to Rs 10.28 crore for the second quarter ended September 30, 2022. The company had posted a net profit of Rs 231.40 crore for the July-September period of the previous fiscal, said in a regulatory filing. However, its consolidated revenue from operations rose by 12.65 per cent to Rs 1,473.43 crore compared to Rs 1,307.90 crore in the corresponding quarter a year ago. Broadcast's total expenses were at Rs 1,485.
