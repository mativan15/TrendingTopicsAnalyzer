Chart Check: This heavy electrical equipment maker hits fresh 52-week high; rallies over 40% in a year
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 06:15:00

ABB India Ltd, part of capital goods industry, rose over 40 per cent in a year compared to over 6 per cent rise seen in Nifty50 in the same period, and the rally is not over yet. The heavy electrical equipment maker hit a fresh 52-week high of Rs 2,488 on 1 June. The stock has been on buyers' radar for the past few weeks. It gave breakout from a falling channel pattern this week on the daily charts which opened room for targets in the range of Rs 2,600-2,700 in the next couple of weeks, suggest experts.
