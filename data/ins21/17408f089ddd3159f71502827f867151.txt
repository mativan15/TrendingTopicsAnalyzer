Daniel Grizelj High Yield Or High Dividend Growth? What qualities should a dividend investor focus on in their stock-buying during the current bear market? As almost the entire market is selling off, dividend investors now have decisions to make with their scarce buying power. Should we prioritize high-yield dividend stocks or lower-yielding stocks with faster dividend growth? It's a question I wrestle with almost daily, as the current bear market has driven some dividend stock yields up into the double digits, but it has also offered rare discounts on some blue-chip dividend growers that almost never go on sale.
