Getty ImagesRetail and banking stocks rose 0.5 per cent and 0.7 per cent, respectively.The share price of ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Ltd. gained 0.09 per cent to Rs 509.95 at 01:49PM (IST) in Tuesday's trade. The scrip has hit a high of Rs 511.45 and low of Rs 501.25 so far during the session. The stock had closed at Rs 509.5 in the previous session. The counter has had a total traded volume of 21190 shares so far with a value of Rs 1.07 crore on the NSE. It traded at a price-to-earnings multiple of 85.
