Published: 02:54 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 02:55 EDT, 2 June 2022 A mother-of-the-bride has sparked a debate about whether it's outdated for parents to pay for weddings, after questioning whether she should be expected to fork out for her daughter's big day. Taking to the British parenting forum Mumsnet, the woman explained that her daughter who is in her 30s is marrying her boyfriend of 10 years, who she's lived with for some time. She explained that she and her husband were able to afford private education for their childre, but there's less money coming in now they're both retired.
