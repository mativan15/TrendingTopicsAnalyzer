Chancellor Rishi Sunak has announced a new package of support worth £15 billion to help the country through the cost of living crisis.Among them, is a one-off payment of £650 for eight million families on benefits to ease the pressure of rising energy bills.The sum will be applied to those on Universal Credit, Tax Credits, Pension Credit, and legacy benefits from July.Those eligible won't need to apply, but will receive two direct payments - the first in July and the second in autumn - from the Department of Work and Pensions straight to their bank accounts.
