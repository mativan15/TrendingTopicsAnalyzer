Gotham Knights review: strong story rescues underpowered Bat adventure
msn.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 07:00:00

As an open-world Batman game that's not connected to the Arkham series and kills off Batman in the first five minutes, Gotham Knights was always going to be a risky endeavor for Warner Bros. Games. Pile on skepticism surrounding the game's multiplayer and RPG systems in the wake of Marvel's Avengers, as well as a frame rate discourse, and Gotham Knights seemed like a game that would either fall flat on its face or soar to greater heights than the Arkham series ever did. After playing through almost everything the game offers, the reality is that Gotham Knights ultimately lands somewhere in the middle.
