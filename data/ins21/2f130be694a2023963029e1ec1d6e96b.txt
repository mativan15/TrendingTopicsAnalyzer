Home Secretary to crack down on disruptive protests with new bill
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:05:38

Image source, Getty ImagesThe home secretary has unveiled plans for a major crackdown on disruptive protests carried out by environmental groups such as Extinction Rebellion. Suella Braverman says the new Public Order Bill will stop demonstrators holding the public "to ransom". Ministers will be empowered to block protests causing "serious disruption" to key infrastructure and goods. Activists Just Stop Oil said they would not be intimidated by law changes aimed at "silencing non-violent people".
