NEW DELHI: Delhi's air quality was in the poor category on Monday morning as thunder and lightning were expected while the mercury was likely to settle around 41 degrees Celsius on Monday amid partly overcast weather.According to the Central Pollution Control Board website, the hourly Air Quality Index (AQI) was 224 at 7 am. The average 24-hour AQI was 273 on Sunday in the poor category.
