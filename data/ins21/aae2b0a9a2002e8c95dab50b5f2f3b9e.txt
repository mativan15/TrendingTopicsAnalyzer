Toronto Raptors' Scottie Barnes during a preseason game against the Boston Celtics, in Montreal, on Oct. 14.Graham Hughes/The Canadian PressThe Toronto Raptors exercised team options for budding sophomore Scottie Barnes, Precious Achiuwa and Malachi Flynn on Wednesday.For Barnes, the reigning NBA rookie of the year, it's a third-year option on his rookie contract that keeps him in Toronto through the 2023-24 season.The six-foot-eight, 232-pound forward averaged 15.3 points and 7.5 rebounds in 74 games as a starter last season.
