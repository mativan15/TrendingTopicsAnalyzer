Leader of Civic Platform Donald Tusk press conferenceLeader of main opposition party Civic Platform (PO) Donald Tusk speaks during a press conference in Warsaw, Poland, 18 October 2022. Donald Tusk has called for an investigation into alleged Russian influence over Poland's energy policy under the present Law and Justice (PiS) government. Tusk's words came in connection with recent media reports that Russia had a hand in a 2014 tape scandal, in which private conversations between leading members of the then government, led by Tusk, were secretly recorded in a restaurant they frequented.
