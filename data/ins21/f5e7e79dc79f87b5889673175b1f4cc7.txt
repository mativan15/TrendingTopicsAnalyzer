AgenciesMakeMyTrip co-founder and group CEO Rajesh MagowSuperapp is the buzz word among conglomerates. From Tatas to Adanis, working to develop a one-stop solution for their customers, country's biggest online travel portal, MakeMyTrip wants to convert itself into a travel super app. With COVID behind and travel showing a rebound, MakeMyTrip is adding multiple products into a single app offering through in-house development and also by acquiring companies.

'The ambition is to become the first port of call for anyone in India thinking about travel.
