October 19, 2022 04:15 PM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The AZEK Company Inc. (NYSE: AZEK) ("AZEK" or the 'Company'), the industry-leading manufacturer of beautiful, low-maintenance and environmentally sustainable outdoor living products, including TimberTech® decking, Versatex® and AZEK® Trim and StruXure™ pergolas, announced today that Jesse Singh, Chief Executive Officer, and Peter Clifford, Chief Financial Officer, will present at the Baird 2022 Global Industrial Conference on Wednesday, November 9, 2022 at 9:05am (CT) in Chicago, Illinois.
