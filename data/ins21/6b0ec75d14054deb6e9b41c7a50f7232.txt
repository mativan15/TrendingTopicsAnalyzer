The hidden message in Rupert Murdoch's latest manoeuvre
brisbanetimes.com.au
news
2022-10-17 08:08:09

Combining a US television company focused on live sport and live news with a digital real estate and news business makes some strategic sense. There is the potential for greater cross promotion across more media platforms, and also cost savings at the corporate level.What's less immediately clear is how the re-unification of the two companies would solve the company's biggest challenge, at least from the perspective of investors. Analysts bemoan that both News Corp and Fox Corp suffer from valuation discounts relative to their media industry peers.
