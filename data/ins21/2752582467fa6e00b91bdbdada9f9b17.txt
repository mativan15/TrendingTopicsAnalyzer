Times letters: Electorate deserves better from politicians
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-17 23:01:00

Sir, Regardless of the personalities and policies involved, the present state of affairs in our country shows how inadequate are our constitutional arrangements. On the resignation of the head of government a small minority of the electorate, based on political party affiliation, decide on who should take over instead of the broad electorate being given that choice. It is high time that this undemocratic procedure was replaced by a clearly set out provision — in a written constitutional form — for the decision on who governs us to be put to the whole electorate when such a situation arises.
