Madison Square Garden Sports (NYSE:MSGS) In May 2019, in an investment presentation, we espoused the virtues of what was then called Madison Square Garden Company. In the intervening three plus years, MSG has split into two pieces - the sport teams (MSGS: NY Knicks and NY Rangers) + MSGS, being the arena itself and associated other assets, Tao Group Hospitality, and the controversial "Spheres" project in Las Vegas. In July 2021, MSGE completed the re-acquisition of MSG Networks, the broadcaster, which had previously been spun out.
