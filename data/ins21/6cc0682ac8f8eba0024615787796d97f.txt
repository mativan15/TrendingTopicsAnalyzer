Xi Policies Hinder China's Economy, Job Market: Analysts

2022-10-22 08:00:00

HONG KONG - China's President Xi Jinping has faced and survived many challenges in life. He spent years as a teenager toiling in the countryside after his father was persecuted. He worked his way up to the top echelons of power and carried out a massive anti-corruption campaign that earned him many enemies.But as he's about to get an unprecedented third term, Xi faces what may perhaps be his toughest challenge - fixing the serious problems that have metastasized in China following four decades of rapid, unbridled and wasteful economic growth, and putting the world's second biggest and soon-to-be-biggest economy in order.
