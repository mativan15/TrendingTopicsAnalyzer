Chicago's Puttshack bringing mini-golf entertainment venue to Reston Station
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 12:00:00

Mini-golf-themed entertainment concept Puttshack is coming to Northern Virginia.

The company, with locations in Atlanta and Chicago, has leased 29,000 square feet at 1850 Reston Row Plaza. The location, slated to open in summer 2025, is part of the second phase of Reston Station, the 80-acre mixed-use project being developed by the Wiehle-Reston East Metro station by Comstock Holding Companies Inc. (NASDAQ: CHCI). Puttshack will join a growing mix of retail tenants at the development including Big Buns Burgers, Matchbox, Scissors & Scotch and Davio's Northern Italian Steakhouse.
