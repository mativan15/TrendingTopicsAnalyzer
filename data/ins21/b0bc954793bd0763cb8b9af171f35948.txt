Inflation Expectations, Business Confidence Soften Among Canada Firms -- Update
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 15:32:00

OTTAWA--Short-term inflation expectations among businesses remain elevated but have softened relative to the prior quarter, according to the latest quarterly survey of companies by the Bank of Canada.The business-outlook survey, released Monday, indicated business confidence has deteriorated but remains in positive territory. Businesses expect sales to expand at a slower pace over the next 12 months, as the combination of higher interest rates and elevated inflation begin to weigh on economic activity.
