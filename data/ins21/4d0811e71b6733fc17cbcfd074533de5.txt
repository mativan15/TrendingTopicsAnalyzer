You likely have one phone number for your mobile phone through which you make and get phone calls, send and receive text messages, and handle other related tasks. But sometimes you could use a second number for personal and professional phone calls and text messages. Or perhaps you'd like a different number to use when you set up accounts at websites.A variety of options are available for snagging a second phone number, some free and some paid. But one option worth trying is Google Voice.With the Google Voice VoIP service, you're assigned a dedicated phone number that can use the same area code as your existing phone number or general location.
