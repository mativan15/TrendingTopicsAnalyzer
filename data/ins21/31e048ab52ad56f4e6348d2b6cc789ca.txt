Published: 10:33 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 10:36 EDT, 15 October 2022 In the latest slice of bizarre from the private life of Elon Musk, the tech titan believes that ex-girlfriend Grimes is so perfect that she must be a simulation he created in his mind, according to a new BBC documentary. The same documentary, called The Elon Musk Show, then goes on to say that Grimes actually agrees with the theory.The Canadian singer was already 31 when she met Musk but those three decades in existence appear not to have swayed her too much when considering the theory.
