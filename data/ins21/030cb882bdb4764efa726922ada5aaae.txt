October 19, 2022 08:31 AM Eastern Daylight Time ORLANDO, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The LGL Group, Inc. (NYSE American: LGL) today announced the adjustment to the terms for its warrants, as a result of its M-tron Industries, Inc. spin-off. The adjustments are as follows:


LGL warrants exercise price is adjusted to $4.75 from $12.50 original before the Mtron spin-off


LGL warrants trigger price for potential acceleration of the exercise date is adjusted to $6.65 versus $17.50 before the spin-off


The LGL warrants are listed and traded under the NYSE American as LGL.
