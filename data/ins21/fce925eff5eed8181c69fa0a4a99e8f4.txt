MADISON – Jim Leonhard's No. 1 and No. 2 goals this week:

Find a way to defeat a Purdue team that is tied for first place in the Big Ten West Division.

Then take advantage of a break in Wisconsin's schedule so many injured players can get healthy and ready for the final four regular-season games, beginning Nov. 5 against Maryland.

'Hopefully, the bye week will help a number of our guys who are dealing with the wear and tear of the first part of this season,' UW's interim head coach said. 'We have to find a way – whatever we have to do – to get to this Saturday with as much health and energy as we can and the best plan to go win.
