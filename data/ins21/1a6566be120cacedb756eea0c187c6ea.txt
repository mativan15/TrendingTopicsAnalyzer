 Police are investigating after a pedestrian was struck by a York Transit bus in Toronto.
Toronto police said on Thursday at around 7:24 a.m., officers received a report that a pedestrian had been struck by a vehicle in the Dufferin Street and Finch Avenue West area.Officers said a 66-year-old woman was crossing Dufferin Street in between stopped vehicles, just north of the intersection.Police said a York Transit bus travelling southbound on Dufferin Street struck the woman.
