‘A certain stigma': Spain's hospitality sector hit by waiter shortage
theguardian.com
news
2022-06-03 09:20:32

After a two-year drought, tourists are flooding back to Spain, but just as the hospitality industry begins to recover from the pandemic, it faces a new crisis – a shortage of waiters.From Mallorca to Madrid, restaurateurs are crying out for waiters with tens of thousands of jobs waiting to be filled. The Hard Rock hotel in Ibiza is so desperate it is offering staff a €200 (£170) bonus to find suitable employees.The paradox is that unemployment in Spain is running at 13.4% – more than double the EU average of 6.
