October 20, 2022 11:32 AM Eastern Daylight Time RALEIGH, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KB Home (NYSE: KBH) today announced the grand opening of Preserve at Jones Dairy, a new, single-family home community in Rolesville. Preserve at Jones Dairy is located off Jones Dairy Road and near Highway 98, Highway 401 and Interstate 540. The Community is also just minutes away from downtown Wake Forest for shopping, dining and entertainment. Residents will enjoy the community's proximity to Main Street Park and Fantasy Lake Adventure Park for outdoor recreation.
