Boris Johnson has arrived back in the UK as speculation mounts that the former prime minister will enter the Tory leadership race.Mr Johnson has been on holiday in the Dominican Republic but flew home after Liz Truss's dramatic resignation on Thursday.
The Conservative Party has a week to pick a new leader and prime minister.'Difficult to see' how Johnson could be PM - politics latestMr Johnson officially resigned six weeks ago after a series of scandals and mass resignations among his ministers but is still popular among some Tory MPs and the party membership.
