Space crystals project aims to put human DNA on the moon in 2023
space.com
science
2022-10-19 15:00:57

Space Crystals LLC plans to deliver crystallized human DNA to the surface of the moon for the first time in 2023. It's unknown which lander will make the delivery. One possibility is Intuitive Machines' spacecraft, seen here in an artist's impression.
(Image credit: Intuitive Machines)


A startup company is betting big on sending human DNA to the surface of the moon.Space Crystals LLC plans to crystallize client DNA in space and put those stable crystals on the surface of the moon via lunar landers.
