Cop's widow paid ₹ 17 lakh in excess; HC quashes Haryana order on recovery
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 19:45:17

: The Punjab and Haryana high court has quashed a Haryana government order for the recovery of ₹ 17.45 lakh paid in excess to a cop's widow from the family pension of the woman. The high court bench of justice Harsimran Singh Sethi said that there is no allegation of any misrepresentation on part of the petitioner woman so as to receive the excess amount which was paid to by the respondents. 'Once, there is no misrepresentation on part of the petitioner at the time of receiving of the benefit and the benefit was received in accordance with the order passed, no recovery can be made,' the bench added.
