Behind Netflix and Disney+'s Dash for Streaming Ad Cash
variety.com
news
2022-10-19 15:30:00

Just over three years ago, Netflix unequivocally shot down the idea that it would ever roll out an ad-supported streaming service. 'When you read speculation that we are moving into selling advertising, be confident that this is false,' the streamer said in its second-quarter 2019 investor letter. 'We believe we will have a more valuable business in the long term by staying out of competing for ad revenue and instead entirely focusing on competing for viewer satisfaction.' Clearly, Netflix's thinking about advertising has changed.
