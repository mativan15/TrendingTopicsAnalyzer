Thanks!During the 2020 election, Disney bought itself the Anaheim City Council, ousting a group of reformers who wanted to cut off the flow of favors running from city hall to Disneyland and its surrounding attractions. At the time, I called Anaheim 'a modern company town,' one where big business doesn't literally own everything, but figuratively owns the mechanisms for decisionmaking and bends policy to its own ends.
