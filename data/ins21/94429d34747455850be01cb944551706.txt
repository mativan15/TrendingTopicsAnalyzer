Sanjay Malhotra to take over as revenue secretary 2 months before Budget
hindustantimes.com
business
2022-10-19 15:05:35

NEW DELHI: The government on Wednesday appointed financial services secretary Sanjay Malhotra, a 1990-batch Indian Administrative Service officer of the Rajasthan cadre as an officer on special duty (OSD) in the revenue department. Malhotra will become the revenue secretary after incumbent Tarun Bajaj retires on November 30, according to an official order.Malhotra, an IIT Kanpur alumnus, will be responsible for giving the final touches to all tax-related matters related to the forthcoming Union Budget for 2023-24 as the top bureaucrat of the revenue department, which is one of the key arms of the Union finance ministry.
