'What are your biggest regrets?' This might be one of the more obvious questions a journalist can ask an actor, but the answer can often be hugely revealing.Many actors have spoken negatively about their time spent as a superhero, playing a teenage heartthrob or starring in films they believed had been written by somebody else.Others, including Charlize Theron, have said they took a job solely due to a director's filmography – only to then be let down by the final results of their collaboration.Below, The Independent looks at 25 actors who have admitted to disliking films they have starred in.
