Gold Futures Puking Stalling
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-15 01:35:34

Aslan Alphan The notorious gold futures speculators just struck again, selling aggressively and pummeling gold sharply lower. Another red-hot inflation report goaded them into puking out more contracts, which is supremely irrational given market history. But their heavy selling since mid-April has exhausted their finite capital firepower. So that gold-distorting spewing is stalling out, and will soon reverse to huge mean-reversion buying. The latest US Consumer Price Index inflation report was released this Thursday, again coming in hotter than expected.
