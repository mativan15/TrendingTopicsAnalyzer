CHICAGO, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- geniant, a next-generation experience consulting group that's taking a radically different approach to traditional interior design and architecture, welcomes Andrew Balster to the key role of President, Physical Space. As a respected figure in the architecture community, Andrew elevates geniant's team through his extensive experience leading complex projects around the globe."I have always been interested in how human-centered design, combined with technology, can improve holistic experiences and, more broadly, enhance people's lives," said Andrew.
