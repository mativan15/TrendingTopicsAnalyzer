Ishaan Khatter and Tara Sutaria are all set to share screen space for the first time in their upcoming project 'Nature 4 Nature'. On Monday, Tara unveiled the teaser on social media.Taking to her Instagram handle, the 'Tadap' actor treated fans with her upcoming movie's teaser video.Sharing the video, she wrote, "Nature is about to unveil its treasured creation..Are you ready to become one with nature? :) Stay tuned to know more! 31.10.22 #ComingSoon."The video starts with two contrasting scenes together.
