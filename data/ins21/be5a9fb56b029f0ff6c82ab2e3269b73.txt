October 21, 2022 12:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--An institutional investor, the Police & Fire Retirement System City of Detroit, has filed a class action lawsuit today against international underwriter Argo Group International Holdings, Ltd. ('Argo' or the 'Company')(NYSE: ARGO), alleging it defrauded investors by issuing false and misleading statements concerning the Company's ability to set appropriate reserves, changing of its underwriting policies, and writing of policies outside of its 'core' business.
