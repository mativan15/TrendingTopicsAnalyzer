Dr. April Willis, Principal Business Consultant & Coach at April Willis Consulting, LLC. getty How familiar are you with the idea of paid corporate roles? Corporate board positions can be incredibly lucrative endeavors that require minimal effort and offer premium payouts. From equity in the company to six-digit stipends, most corporate boards simply require active participation at a few meetings per year, allowing you to supplement your full-time job with this additional stream of revenue. While there is a certain level of responsibility associated with sitting on a board, the experience can elevate your skill set as a leader and increase your exposure in the business world.
