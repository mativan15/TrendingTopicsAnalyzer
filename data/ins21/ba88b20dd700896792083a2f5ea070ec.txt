Party time for those who have invested in initial public offerings of Electronics Mart India as the company on Monday made the stock market debut with a sharp premium of over 50 per cent against its issue price. It listed with 52 per cent and 53 per cent premiums over its issue price on the Bombay Stock Exchange and the National Stock Exchange, respectively, exchange data showed.
