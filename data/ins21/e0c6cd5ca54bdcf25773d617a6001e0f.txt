India will emerge as world's defence manufacturing hub in next 25 years: Rajnath Singh at DefExpo 2022

Source: ANI

Defence Minister Rajnath Singh on October 19 said that the DefExpo-2022 reflects India's strong resolve to safeguard the nation and said that the country would become a defence manufacturing hub for the world in the next 25 years. 'In line with PM Modi's vision to make India a strong, self-reliant nation, the theme of this Expo is 'Path to Pride'. Path to Pride is not just a theme of this Expo, but a new objective of New India,' said Defence Minister Rajnath Singh at DefExpo2022.
