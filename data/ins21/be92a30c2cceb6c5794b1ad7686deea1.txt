Abbott Laboratories Raises Earnings Guidance for 2022
marketwatch.com

2022-10-19 12:09:00

By Dean Seal Abbott Laboratories raised its full-year earnings and adjusted earnings forecasts for 2022. The Abbott Park, Ill.-based healthcare-products company now expects annual earnings of $3.75 to $3.81 a share, compared with its prior forecast of at least $3.50. Adjusted earnings are now projected to be in the range of $5.17 to $5.23 a share for 2022, up from Abbott's previous guidance of at least $4.90 a share.
