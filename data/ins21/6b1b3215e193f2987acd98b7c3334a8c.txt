Published: 00:34 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 00:34 EDT, 3 June 2022 A promising teenage rugby union player faces an uncertain future after an on-field tackle left her in a hospital ICU unit with no feeling from the waist down.Jaida Samuels, 15, who attends Westfields Sports High in Sydney's west, was knocked out in the collision last month and doesn't remember the incident.Days later, the talented rugby union and rugby league player still had no feeling in her right arm or legs. And as the weeks continue, doctors don't know if she will make a full recovery.
