The bye week has been good for Rutgers football quarterback Gavin Wimsatt, who seems to have made some progress in his recovery from injury.
Wimsatt has been out with an undisclosed injury since he was knocked out in a win at Temple. A former four-star recruit, Wimsatt came into the season with high expectations of potentially winning the quarterback battle as a redshirt sophomore.
