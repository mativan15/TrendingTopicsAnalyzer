SINGAPORE, May 30, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Hoe Kian Choon, founder of Cuckoo International, Kossan's Tan Sri Lim Kuang Sia and Puncak Niaga's Tan Sri Rozali Ismail are among 47 winners of the Asia Pacific Enterprise Awards 2022. The Awards were presented by Enterprise Asia, the leading regional non-governmental organization for entrepreneurship.

The Award Recipients of the Asia Pacific Enterprise Awards 2022 Malaysia.

They were chosen from over 200 nominees through a rigorous evaluation process by a jury of prominent judges across four categories: Master Entrepreneur, Inspirational Brand, Fast Enterprise, and Corporate Excellence.
