Sriram, fellow passenger face lesser charges in K.M. Basheer accident death case

news
2022-10-19 15:20:51

A district court here on Wednesday discharged IAS officer Sriram Venkitaraman of the charge of culpable homicide not amounting to murder in the suspected alcohol-impaired motor vehicle accident that resulted in the death of journalist K. M. Basheer in 2019. The court also reprieved a fellow passenger Wafa, alias Najim, of the same charge. However, the court ruled that both stand trial for causing the death of a person due to a rash and negligent act, not amounting to culpable murder. First additional session judge K.
