A hundred years ago, Sadie Alexander became the first African American to receive a PhD in economics and then spent a career fighting racial discrimination. This spring, the American Economic Association made her a distinguished fellow, their first ever posthumous award. We've decided to update this episode we first ran in December 2020 looking at her forgotten contributions to the field.
