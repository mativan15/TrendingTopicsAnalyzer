HC summons home secy over FSL appointments
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 03:45:00

Ranchi: The Jharkhand high court on Friday pulled up the state government for not having completed the appointment process for third and fourth-grade post vacancies in the forensic sciences laboratory.A division bench of Chief Justice Dr Ravi Ranjan and Justice Sujit Narayan Prasad made the observation while hearing a public interest litigation ( PIL ) taken up suo moto. In the course of its hearing, the court ordered the home secretary to appear in person in the case in the next hearing which will be heard again on November 9.
