How the cost of going to Silicon Valley's colleges and universities stacks up against others in California
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 21:38:00

California has some of most highly regarded colleges and universities in the nation, and those schools attract thousands of students from out of state.

But what those students pay for college depends a lot on where they go. Nowhere is that more obvious than in Silicon Valley.

Among the state's largest schools, Stanford University ranks as the fifth priciest, based on last year's costs, according to Business Journal research. Last school year, the total price of attending Standford — including tuition, housing and other expenses — was $78,898.
