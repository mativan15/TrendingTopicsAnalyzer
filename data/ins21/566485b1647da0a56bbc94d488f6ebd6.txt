THE Paris Motorshow is back with the latest and greatest new cars after a four-year pause due to Covid.
The show, which runs every other year, was cancelled in 2020 and some thought it might never return. 
The Paris Motorshow is back on after a four-year pause
But it wasn't to be, and the public can wander around two floors of new metal in the French capital once again between 17-23 October.
Here's a round-up of the cars that stand out, from the weirdest to the most wonderful.
Renault 4EVER Trophy
This EV SUV is a modern-day take on the iconic Renault 4Credit: RENAULT
Rember the quirky Renault 4? It was built for 30 years starting in the early 1960s and is now being 'reborn' with this concept car in 2022.
