Alaska Air: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-20 10:09:52

SEATTLE (AP) _ Alaska Air Group Inc. (ALK) on Thursday reported third-quarter profit of $40 million.

On a per-share basis, the Seattle-based company said it had net income of 31 cents. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs, were $2.53 per share.

The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of eight analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.41 per share.

The airline posted revenue of $2.83 billion in the period, which met Street forecasts.

Alaska Air shares have fallen 20% since the beginning of the year.
