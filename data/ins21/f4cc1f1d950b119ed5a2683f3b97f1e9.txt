FRESHBREW ANNOUNCES $10 MILLION INVESTMENT IN SPECIALTY ROASTING, TEA & READY-TO-DRINK PRODUCTION
prnewswire.com
news
2022-06-01 17:00:00

Company to become one of only a few companies in the country offering total end-to-end beverage solutions
HOUSTON , June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Houston-based FreshBrew, one of the largest private-label coffee and tea producers in the country, announced today the investment of $10 million into its specialty roasting and production divisions. The investment will expand current roasting production and will fund extraction, bottling and canning capabilities, making FreshBrew one of only a few companies in the country to offer total end-to-end beverage solutions.
