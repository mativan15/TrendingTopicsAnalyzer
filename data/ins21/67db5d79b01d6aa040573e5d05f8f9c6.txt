digitalhallway/iStock via Getty Images The official launch of New Jersey recreational cannabis sales should have the market excited about multi-state operators (MSOs). Verano Holdings (OTCQX:VRNOF) is one of those MSOs set to benefit from the unlock of massive cannabis volumes in Northeast markets. My investment thesis remains bullish on the stock with the unlock of value from New Jersey and the New York license in hand. Q1 Struggles The whole cannabis sector struggled during Q1'22 due to the tough comps boosted by stimulus checks last year and the Omicron shutdowns during January and February.
