getty The past two years were fairly easy for fundraising, and by extension, they were like the wild west of spending as well. Startups had the money to spend, so we saw growth inflation across the board as more and more companies benefited. What we're seeing now is a market correction. Business-to-business companies, especially B2B software as a service companies, have been the subject of stories about the negative effects of the 'growth at all costs' growth model.
