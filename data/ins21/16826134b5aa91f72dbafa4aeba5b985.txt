VANCOUVER, BC, May 31, 2022 /CNW/ - NG Energy International Corp. (the "Company" or "NGE") (TSXV: GASX) (OTC: GASXF) is pleased to announce that, further to the Company's news releases dated May 20, 2022 and May 24, 2022, the 8% unsecured convertible debentures (the "Convertible Debentures") and the common share purchase warrants (the "Warrants") of the Company, issued pursuant to the previously announced best efforts, fully marketed offering (the "Offering"), have been approved for listing on the TSX Venture Exchange Inc.
