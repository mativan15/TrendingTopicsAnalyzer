Cloud kitchen startup Curefoods raises $43 million funding led by Winter Capital
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 13:00:00

Mumbai: Cloud kitchen startup Curefoods has raised about $43 million (Rs 331 crore) in a new funding round led by Winter Capital, which contributed about $14.7 million. Three State Capital has joined the company's cap table with a $5.8-million investment. Winter Capital's recent Indian bets include healthcare startup Pristyn Care and fintech firm Money View. Existing investors Chiratae Ventures infused about $1.6 million while India invested $3.9 million and Iron Pillar put in $10 million, according to regulatory filings sourced from business intelligence platform Tofler.
