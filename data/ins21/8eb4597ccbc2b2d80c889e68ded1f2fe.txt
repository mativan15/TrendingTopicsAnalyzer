Textron Aviation boss sees stickiness in aircraft demand
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 16:23:00

Optimism about business aviation is high this week, as the industry has come together amid an upswing at the National Business Aviation Association convention in Orlando.

Demand for business jets has rebounded quickly from the impact of Covid-19, driven in part by new customers coming to the market due to the pandemic.

While concerns about workforce availability, supply chain and macro-economic issues like inflation remain, the belief of Wichita's largest business jet company heading into NBAA was that the demand itself is sustainable.
