Ime Udoka explains why he got T'ed up in Game 2 originally appeared on NBC Sports Boston

Sunday marked a pair of firsts for Boston Celtics head coach Ime Udoka, neither of them anything he'd be looking for entering Game 2 of the NBA Finals.

Udoka suffered the first loss of his head coaching career in the Finals, 107-88 to the Golden State Warriors, in addition to picking up his first technical foul as a head coach in the Finals.
