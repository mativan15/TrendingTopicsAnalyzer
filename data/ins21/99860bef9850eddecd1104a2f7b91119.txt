Published: 09:14 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 10:04 EDT, 21 October 2022 Manchester City manager Pep Guardiola has apologised following chants by the club's fans referencing the Hillsborough and Heysel tragedies during last week's defeat at Liverpool. Liverpool expressed their disappointment over the conduct of City fans after the fiery Premier League clash at Anfield last Sunday. As well as the chants, similarly-themed graffiti was also daubed in a number of places on the concourse of the Anfield Road end stand.
