By Australian Associated Press Published: 23:41 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 23:41 EDT, 19 October 2022 The Socceroos' attacking linchpin Ajdin Hrustic won't play again before the World Cup after suffering an ankle injury in Italy. Hrustic was hurt playing for Hellas Verona with the attacking midfielder damaging ligaments in his left ankle. The 26-year-old, a cornerstone of the Socceroos' qualification campaign for the World Cup in Qatar starting on November 20, won't require surgery, Hellas say.
