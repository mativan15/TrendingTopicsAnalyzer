Published: 17:39 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 17:39 EDT, 21 October 2022 By Sabrina Valle HOUSTON, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Exxon Mobil Corp shares passed their all-time high on Friday as oil prices resumed their climb and analysts raised their long-term views of top U.S. oil producer's cash flow and earnings outlook. Exxon is leading a parade of record profits among oil majors this year after doubling down on oil during the pandemic, when energy prices fell to a two-decade low and European oil majors slashed spending and moved further to renewable projects.
