WHITE PLAINS, N.Y., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Turtle Beach Corporation (Nasdaq: HEAR), a leading gaming accessory business, announced it will report financial results for the third quarter 2022 on Thursday, November 3, 2022.

In conjunction with this announcement, Turtle Beach will host a conference call at 5:00 p.m. ET / 2:00 p.m. PT with the Company's Chairman and CEO, Juergen Stark, and CFO, John Hanson.
