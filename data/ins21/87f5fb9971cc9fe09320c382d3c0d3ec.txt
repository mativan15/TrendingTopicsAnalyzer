TIPPING Point fans have all had the same complaint over the latest episode. 
The ITV quiz show - hosted by Ben Shephard - was on at a slightly later time on Thursday because of bank holiday schedule changes. 
Tipping Point viewers all had the same complaint about Thursdays episode
Four contestants tried to win as many tokens from the machine
It saw four contestants take to the podiums to try and win as many tokens from the machine and build up their cash pots.
Jilly, Connor, Marcus and Dee hoped to win enough money to make it through to the final round, however, the fact there were four players in the starting line up quickly caught viewers' attention.
