By Reuters Published: 12:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 12:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 Golden State Warriors guard Jordan Poole is finalizing a four-year, $140 million contract extension, his representation CAA Sports told ESPN on Saturday. The new deal would keep Poole with the defending NBA champions through the 2026-27 season. Per ESPN, a formal agreement is expected later on Saturday. The reported extension comes ahead of Monday's deadline for members of the 2019 NBA Draft class. It also comes on the heels of teammate Draymond Green's punch of Poole during a recent practice.
