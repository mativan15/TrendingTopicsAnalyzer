China's measures to boost consumer demand are nowhere near enough
qz.com
news
2022-06-01 17:30:00

In a bid to give its struggling economy a boost, numerous Chinese cities and provinces have in recent days taken measures to boost consumer demand with consumption vouchers, spending subsidies, and limited cash handouts.That's a welcome start. Beijing has long been reluctant to use demand-side policies despite prominent economists urging the state to literally hand out money to citizens. Instead, policymakers prefer supply-side measures like tax cuts, interest rate cuts, and infrastructure investment.
