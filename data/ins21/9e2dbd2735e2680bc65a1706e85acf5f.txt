Women visitor in Yellowstone Park dies as bison tosses her 10 feet in the air
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 16:00:00

APFile - A mother bison leads her calf through deep snow toward a road in Yellowstone National Park, Wyo., Feb. 20, 2021. A bison has gored a 25-year-old woman in Yellowstone. The bison was walking near a boardwalk at Black Sand Basin, just north of Old Faithful, when the woman approached it on Monday, May 30, 2022, according to a park statement. She got within 10 feet (3 meters) before the animal gored her and tossed her 10 feet into the air. The woman from Grove City, Ohio, sustained a puncture wound and other injuries.
