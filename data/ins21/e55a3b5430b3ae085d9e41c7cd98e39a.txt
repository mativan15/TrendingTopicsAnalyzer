Hedonistic heaven at the Hard Rock Hotel Tenerife
telegraph.co.uk
travel
2022-10-21 11:10:00

Sandy beaches, calm waters and almost guaranteed sunshine – southern Tenerife is a safe bet for a family holiday. But it doesn't need to be all buckets ‘n' spades and buffets, Tenerife has so much more to offer. And if you're looking for a more glamorous getaway for your crew, head to Hard Rock Hotel Tenerife in Adeje. Right by black-sand cove Playa Paraiso, this playful, music-themed hotel has a cool and contemporary feel that caters for people looking for something a little different.
From the moment you step into its sleek, spacious lobby, with all its music and rock-inspired memorabilia, you can be certain of a fun-packed stay.
