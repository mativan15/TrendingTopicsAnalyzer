WHICH classic tub is choc full of your favourites for Christmas?
The Sun's Hayley Minn had the sweet job of checking out the big four – Quality Street, Roses, Celebrations and Heroes – to find the best value for money.
Which tub is choc full of your favourites - and which is sure to cause a family row this Christmas?Credit: Paul Edwards
With each tub costing £4, she also reveals how many of your top picks you can look forward to.
The 650g box of Celebrations came out on top, with 71 chocs, compared to Roses, 600g, with a measly 56.
