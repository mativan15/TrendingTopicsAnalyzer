Schneider Electric Wins 'Water Technology Company of the Year' in Global Water Awards 2022

MISSISSAUGA, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--Schneider Electric, the leader in digital transformation of energy management and automation, has won the ‘Water Technology Company of the Year' at the Global Water Awards 2022. The award recognizes Schneider Electric's leadership in sustainability and digital transformation within the water, wastewater, and desalination sectors, and its technologies which help customers meet the demand for resilient, efficient, and sustainable operations.Outstanding contribution to digitalization and efficiencyThe ‘Water Technology Company of the Year' category recognizes the company that made the most significant contribution to the field of water and digital technology.
