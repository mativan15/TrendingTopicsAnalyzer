Lori Susi has been appointed as executive director of the Intensive Education Academy of West Hartford. She will begin on July 5. (Contributed photo )WEST HARTFORD, CT — Intensive Education Academy of West Hartford has chosen its next executive director, and she comes with years of experience in the education and business worlds.Lori Susi — currently the assistant director of Enhanced Services Programs at the University of Connecticut's Center for Students with Disabilities — has been appointed to lead the school, which teaches students with special needs using individualized learning.
