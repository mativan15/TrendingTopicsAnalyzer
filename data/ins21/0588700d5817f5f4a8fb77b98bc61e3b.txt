Biden infrastructure czar says photo op by rebuilt bridge shows president 'was actually able to deliver'
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 18:40:00

With just 19 days to go until the midterm elections, President Joe Biden on Thursday visited the battleground state of Pennsylvania and talked up his bipartisan infrastructure package in front of a partially rebuilt bridge in Pittsburgh. Biden also appeared with Democratic Senate candidate John Fetterman in Pittsburgh and was due to raise money for him in Philadelphia, as Fetterman battles Republican Mehmet Oz in a closely watched Pennsylvania race. Oz tweeted on Thursday that Americans 'hold Joe Biden responsible for inflation and John Fetterman will support more reckless spending that worsens this crisis.
