Michigan Board Disqualifies 5 GOP Gov Candidates Over Invalid Signatures
huffpost.com
news
2022-05-27 11:51:08

The bipartisan Michigan Board of State Canvassers ruled Thursday that five Republican candidates for governor, including two front-runners, are ineligible to run because of fraudulent signatures on candidate petitions.The board upheld the state Bureau of Elections' recommendation that five of the 10 candidates in the August Republican primary, including leading contenders Perry Johnson and James Craig, be disqualified because of invalid signatures on their filing papers. The Board of State Canvassers vote deadlocked 2-2, with the two Democratic members in favor of accepting the Bureau of Elections recommendation and the two GOP members against it.
