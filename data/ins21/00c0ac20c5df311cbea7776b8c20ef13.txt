Rúben Neves penalty moves Wolves out of bottom three
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 17:00:00

After over six hours of Premier League football Wolverhampton Wanderers finally scored a goal, it had to come from the penalty spot — but those chanting 'We scored a goal' in the Sir Jack Hayward Stand did not care.Not since a 1-0 win over Southampton at the beginning of September have Wolves managed to find the back of the net and while there are still questions to be asked about their attack, they will happily take the three points that push them out of the relegation zone.The old-fashioned romance of football has diminished over the years but for caretaker-manager Steve Davis it was, to coin a cliche, a moment that dreams are made of.
