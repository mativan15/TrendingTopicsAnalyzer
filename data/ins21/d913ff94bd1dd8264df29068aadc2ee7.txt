David Gasparyan is the founder & president of Phonexa, a turnkey marketing suite for calls, leads, clicks, email, SMS, accounting and more. 
getty As businesses adapt to the evolution of digital technology, the role of chief information officers (CIOs) has also changed. Traditionally, technical expertise has been a hallmark of successful CIOs, but recent changes in consumer spending and operational demands have led companies to seek CIOs with business-related backgrounds. As the masses shift even more toward e-commerce and hybrid or fully remote work environments, companies should certainly rely on business-savvy CIOs to maintain operational continuity.
