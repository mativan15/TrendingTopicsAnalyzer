The old school entertainment venue opens at the newly redesigned SouthSide Works

COLUMBUS, Ohio, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Rise Brands , a brand development company that brings unique brand engagements to life, is set to open its newest Pins Mechanical Co. location in Pittsburgh on October 20. Located in the recently renovated SouthSide Works space, Pins will surround guests with its signature duckpin bowling, old school games and 16-Bit Bar + Arcade which will serve nostalgic cocktails along with local craft beers.
