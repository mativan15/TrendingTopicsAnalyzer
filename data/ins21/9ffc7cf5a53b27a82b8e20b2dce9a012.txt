By Louise CullenBBC NI Agriculture & Environment CorrespondentImage caption, Celia Spouncer wanted to build a home that was as efficient as possibleA County Down woman has said she is "overwhelmingly disappointed" that a connection charge of thousands of pounds has dashed her hopes of installing an environmentally-friendly air source heat pump at her new-build house.After a lifetime working in the environment sector, Celia Spouncer and her partner, David Thompson, wanted to build a home that was as efficient as possible and would allow them to be part of what Celia thought of as the green revolution.
