J. Michael Jones Leading power generator company Generac Holdings Inc. (NYSE:GNRC) recently stunned the market with a prelim Q3 release that came well below the consensus estimates. Consequently, its full-year guidance has also been revised significantly, as the company underestimated the challenges to its backlog and field inventories for H2'22. However, the market had already anticipated significant challenges to GNRC's operating performance in 2022 after a record 2021 that saw its valuation surge to unsustainable levels.
