A seven-year-old schoolgirl, from Worcester, was savagely attacked by a Rottweiler when she went to stroke the dog after its owner said it was friendlyThe schoolgirl was rushed to hospital and left with a gaping hole in her cheekA schoolgirl who was savagely mauled by a Rottweiler was saved by her hoodie which stopped the dog sinking its teeth into her throat. The seven-year-old was left with a gaping bite wound to her cheek after the crazed hound launched at her face outside her home in Worcester.
