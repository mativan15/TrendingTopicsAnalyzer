The former governor, who resigned after accusations of sexual harassment, ushers in his new podcast in the month that marks five years after the #MeToo movement began.

4:55 p.m. ET

This month marks the five-year anniversary of the beginnings of a global outpouring of sexual-assault allegations, the result of which was a movement that left powerful, lecherous men toppled one over the other as if in a junk heap. And this is the moment, however improbably, that former Gov. Andrew Cuomo has chosen to give the world a podcast, to mount his climb back up from the pile.
