Published: 01:16 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 01:28 EDT, 18 October 2022 Greg Norman has mourned his father alongside friends and family after the golfing family patriarch's death aged 95.The golf legend was joined by his son Greg Jr and mother Toini at Moreton Bay Crematorium north of Brisbane on Tuesday where Mervin Norman was laid to rest.Dressed in a black shirt and trousers, Norman gripped his wife Kirsten Kutner's hand as they walked into the funeral to farewell his father.
