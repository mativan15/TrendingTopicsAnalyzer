Company welcomes Alia Kemet as chief marketing officer and promotes Smrutha Ipparthi to chief product officer BIRMINGHAM, Ala., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Today, Shipt announced the hiring of Alia Kemet as chief marketing officer and the promotion of Smrutha Ipparthi to chief product officer. These leadership updates come as Shipt continues to strategically evolve beyond its traditional marketplace offering.
