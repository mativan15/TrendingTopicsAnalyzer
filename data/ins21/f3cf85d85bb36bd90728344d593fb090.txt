EDEN PRAIRIE, Minn., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Miromatrix Medical Inc. (NASDAQ: MIRO), a life sciences company pioneering a novel technology for bioengineering fully transplantable organs to help save and improve patients' lives, today announced that CEO Jeff Ross will be a keynote speaker at BioFabUSA's 2022 Fall Summit from the Advanced Regenerative Manufacturing Institute (ARMI). Mr. Ross will be a keynote speaker at ARMI|BioFab's 2022 Fall Summit: The Meeting in the Millyard on October 26th, 2022.
