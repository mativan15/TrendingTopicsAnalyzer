WB announces exemption of registration fees, motor vehicle and additional taxes
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 14:15:00

AgenciesRepresentative ImageThe West Bengal on Friday announced exemption of registration fees, motor vehicle and additional taxes for two and four wheeled electric vehicles and all categories of CNG-operated vehicles from April 1, 2022 to March 31, 2024, a government official said here.. This will decrease on the road prices of several categories of green energy operated vehicles, he said. For those vehicles which were registered between April 1, 2022 and the date of issuance of the notification on May 25, the financial incentive will be in the form of extension of tax validity for the exact number of days for which tax has been paid between April 1, 2022 to March 31, 2024, the order signed by transport department secretary Rajesh Sinha said.
