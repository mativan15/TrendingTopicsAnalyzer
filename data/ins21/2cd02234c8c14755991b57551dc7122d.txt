P&G stock rises after profit and sales beats, as 9% jump in pricing helped offset volume decline, FX impact
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 11:10:59

Shares of Procter & Gamble Co. PG, +0.81% rose 2.1% in premarket trading Wednesday, after the consumer packaged goods company reported fiscal first-quarter profit and sales that rose above expectations, as higher pricing provided a boost, but lowered its full-year sales outlook. Net income fell to $3.94 billion, or $1.57 a share, from $4.11 billion, or $1.61 a share, in the year-ago period. Excluding nonrecurring items, core earnings per share of $1.57 topped the FactSet consensus of $1.55. Sales grew 1.
