Intel's Mobileye Reportedly Mulling Substantially Lower IPO Value
cnet.com
business
2022-10-17 23:58:08

Intel is considering a significantly lower valuation than previously expected for the initial public offering of its self-driving car unit Mobileye, the Wall Street Journal reported on Monday, citing people familiar with the matter.Mobileye's IPO was originally expected to be worth $50 billion but is now expected to come in at less than $20 billion, with fewer shares being offered than originally planned, sources told the Journal. Mobileye also plans to kick off its roadshow for prospective investors on Tuesday, a day later than anticipated, the Journal reported.
