Shares of Pinduoduo (PDD 15.19%), a Chinese e-commerce platform company, spiked today after the company reported first-quarter earnings and revenue that surpassed Wall Street's average estimates. 
The stock was up 15.2% on Friday. 
So what 
Pinduoduo reported adjusted earnings per American depositary share (ADS) of $0.47, compared to a loss of $1.52 in the year-ago quarter and far ahead of analysts' consensus estimate of $0.25 per share. 

Image source: Getty Images.

The company's sales of $3.7 billion, up 7% from the year-ago quarter, also outpaced analysts' average estimate of about $3.
