iPhone problem? Here is how to put iPhone in Diagnostic Mode before starting Apple Self Service Repair
hindustantimes.com
tech
2022-06-04 05:59:59

If you want to use the Apple Self Service Repair scheme, then your iPhone must be put under the Diagnostic Mode. Here's how to do it. Back in April, Apple had announced a new iPhone Self Service Repair scheme for its devices, starting with select models in the US. The iPhone maker had also mentioned about the expansion of this new self-repair program to Europe later in the coming months. But there is something important that all iPhone users should do before starting the Self Service Repair program and that is kick-start the Diagnostic Mode.
