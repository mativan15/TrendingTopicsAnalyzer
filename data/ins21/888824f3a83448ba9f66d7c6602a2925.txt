Ex-Infosys president Ravi Kumar to join as president for Cognizant Americas
indiatimes.com
business
2022-10-17 14:00:00

Former Infosys president Ravi Kumar is joining as the president for Cognizant Americas effective January 16 next year.Ravi Kumar S has resigned as president effective Tuesday, the company said in a stock exchange filing. Kumar, who is based in New York, was one of the two Infosys presidents appointed in 2015, and served in that role for nearly seven years before he pulled the curtains down on his 20-year career with the company.Kumar's exit did not come as a surprise to many insiders, that there was speculation about this for the last six months.
