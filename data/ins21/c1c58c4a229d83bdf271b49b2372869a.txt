Published: 10:58 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 10:58 EDT, 19 October 2022 A bustling café is preparing to open for its last weekend for the foreseeable future after its desperate search for a new chef ended in failure. Born and raised in Bulli, in the NSW city of Wollongong, Stokes Lane Cafe owner Sam Dodd, 36, told Daily Mail Australia he had made the gut wrenching decision to trade only Monday to Friday after he was unable to find a replacement cook. Mr Dodd said the devastating toll of Covid on the hospitality industry was hitting his business directly and was a large reason he had been unable to replace his weekend chef, who recently left after four years with the business.
