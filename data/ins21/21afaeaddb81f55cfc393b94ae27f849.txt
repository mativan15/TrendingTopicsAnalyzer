ED to investigate financial irregularities in connection with Group-C, D and teacher's recruitment in Bengal
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 17:15:00

AgenciesChatterjee holds two portfolios of Parliamentary Affairs and Commerce and Industries at present.After the Central Bureau of Investigations (CBI) started investigation into irregularities in connection with Group-C, D and teacher's recruitment in Bengal in connection with West Bengal School Service Commission (SSC), the Enforcement Directorate will now investigate the financial irregularities and money trail in regard to the alleged scam. ED will look into the alleged monetary scam in the SSC scam, sources said.
