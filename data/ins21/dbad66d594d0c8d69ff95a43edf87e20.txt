Canadian dollar climbs to five-week high as current account surplus grows
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-05-30 13:55:31

The Canadian dollar CADUSD strengthened against its U.S. counterpart on Monday, adding to recent gains, as easing COVID-19 restrictions in China bolstered investor sentiment and data showed Canada posting the widest current account surplus in nearly 14 years.World share markets rose and the U.S. dollar was anchored at five-week lows on bets of a possible slowdown in U.S. monetary tightening and after news that Shanghai authorities would from Wednesday cancel many restrictions on businesses.Canada is a major producer of commodities, including oil, so the loonie tends to be sensitive to the outlook for the global economy.
