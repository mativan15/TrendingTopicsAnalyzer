Audi introduces warranty coverage for 5 years
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 07:30:00

ReutersLuxury car maker AudiLuxury car manufacturer Audi India on Friday said it has introduced warranty coverage for five years with unlimited mileage for all its cars sold this year, starting June 1. The company has launched the initiative to mark 15 years in the country. "To celebrate 15 glorious years in India, we have announced a segment first warranty coverage for five years with unlimited mileage for our customers this year, starting June 1, 2022," Audi India Head Balbir Singh Dhillon said in a statement.
