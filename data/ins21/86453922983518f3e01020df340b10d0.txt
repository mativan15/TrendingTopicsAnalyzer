REUTERS/Francis MascarenhasRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comBENGALURU, June 3 (Reuters) - The Indian unit of Chinese smartphone maker Xiaomi Corp (1810.HK) said on Friday it has appointed founding member Alvin Tse as its general manager, a change of guard as the company faces government scrutiny over its business practices.Tse, a British national who was also the former general manager of Xiaomi Indonesia, has helped the company expand into many global markets, Xiaomi India said in a tweet.
