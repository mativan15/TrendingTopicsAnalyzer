Indian lenders are facing pressure on their non-funded corporate exposure, as demand for bank guarantees has dried up from telecom as well as engineering and construction companies, bankers ET spoke with said. Non-fund exposures are off-balance sheet items that comprise letters of credit (LC) and bank guarantees (BG). Here, banks don't sanction a loan to the borrower but act as a guarantor.
