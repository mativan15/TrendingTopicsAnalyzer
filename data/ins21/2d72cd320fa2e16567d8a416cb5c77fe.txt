GTAC provides a foundation for boutique and technology specialized investment banks to collaborate to provide more depth and reach across the Canada - USA border than was previously possible. VANCOUVER, British Columbia (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 The Global Technology Advisor Coalition (GTAC) is being unveiled today at the Tech Exit conference in Toronto. Garibaldi Capital Advisors and Allied Advisers being the first partners committing to this strategic alliance. This initial partnership provides enhanced cross-border reach in the Canada-US corridor and greater depth in technology sector specialization for any technology company seeking to raise capital or engage in M&A transactions.
