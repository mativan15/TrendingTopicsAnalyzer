Advisory Member on MetroStar Advisory Board

2022-10-20 12:17:36

MetroStar announces Ryan Lewis as an Advisory Board Member, focusing on pathways to leading AI solutions. Ryan is a Partner at SRI Ventures, the corporate venture arm of SRI International, where he focuses on investing in early-stage deep tech companies. He is also a resident at the Center for Strategic and International Studies. Before joining SRI, he was the Senior Practice Manager of AI for AWS National Security and spent time as the SVP at In-Q-Tel, where he co-founded an AI research lab.
