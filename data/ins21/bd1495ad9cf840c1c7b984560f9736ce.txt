October 19, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas & ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Next Practices Group (NPG) announced today that Content Science is joining its network of companies and Colleen Jones will join NPG's group of firms as founder and leader of the firm. NPG clients will now have access to an expanded team of content analysts, designers, writers, developers, and strategists who are focused on integrating analytic insights and improving the effectiveness of content in all forms.
