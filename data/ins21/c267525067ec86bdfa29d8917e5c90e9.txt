CareMax Shares Rise 15% After Buying Medicare Business from Steward
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 14:52:00

By Chris Wack CareMax Inc. shares were up 15% to $5.37 Wednesday after the company said it is buying the Medicare value-based care business of Steward Health Care System for a combination of cash and stock. Upon closing the transaction, CareMax said it will serve as the exclusive value-based management services organization across Steward's Medicare network. Steward's value-based care network of 170,000 patients includes 50,000 Medicare Advantage patients, 112,000 Medicare Shared Savings Program patients and 9,000 Direct Contracting patients.
