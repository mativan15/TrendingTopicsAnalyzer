(News Focus) Fire at data center causes unprecedented massive service disruptionBy Kim Boram SEOUL, Oct. 16 (Yonhap) -- A fire at a data center near Seoul has disrupted services of South Korea's dominant messenger and internet portal for a day, affecting nearly all sectors of the country, even finance and transportation. The fire broke out at 3:33 p.m. Saturday at the building of SK C&C located in Pangyo, just south of Seoul, which houses the data centers for Naver Corp. and Kakao Corp., South Korea's two major tech giants.
