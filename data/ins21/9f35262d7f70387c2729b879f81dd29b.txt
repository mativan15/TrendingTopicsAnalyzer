By LIU YUKUN | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-18 09:18 Share - WeChat Ma Yushan, entrepreneur and engineer in Ningxia It's hard to refuse a job at a company that offers a salary of over a million yuan ($143,000) and a big mansion, but that's what Ma Yushan did. Ma is a delegate to the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China and chairman of Wuzhong Instrument, a valve manufacturing company in Wuzhong city of the Ningxia Hui autonomous region. In 2004, when Wuzhong Instrument was struggling due to failures in investment, nearly 200 technicians left the company and went to cities in East China for better job opportunities.
