Mikaben passes away after collapsing during concert
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 20:00:00

AgenciesMikaben was a guest of the Haitian group Carimi at the 20,000-seat Accor Arena in Paris, France. According to local media, Mikaben died after suffering a seizure on stage, and despite the efforts of emergency responders, the venue reported the incident on Twitter, according to local media. The singer was 41 when he passed away. Mikaben fainted before he left the stage, and audience members recorded the event on video, which they later posted on social media. The artiste could be seen in the footage playing before abruptly turning and leaving the stage.
