'For Britain, leaving the European Union has been useless'
lemonde.fr
news
2022-10-16 15:00:12

Incredible but true! Since Brexit, British governments are showcasing greater talent every year at demonstrating that leaving the European Union (EU) took the British people a little further away from the promised land of regained sovereignty and free and unfettered independence. If we fully understand what is happening across the Channel, the world has been turned upside down! The British nation has been put under trusteeship, like poor Greece in the 2010s, and its people are being robbed of their right to choose their government, like unfortunate post-Berlusconi Italy in 2011.
