Pac-12 columnist says one scheduling idea deserves serious consideration
usatoday.com
news
2022-06-04 12:58:36

Most Pac-12 fans will tell you that the conference should move to eight conference games, down from nine, in order to maximize the league's chances of making the College Football Playoff and, by extension, the national championship game.
The thought process is simple: Fewer conference games means more chances to schedule cupcake nonconference games and rest a team's starters during the season. Alabama playing The Citadel or Chattanooga in late November offers a perfect example of this.
Yet, the Pac-12 needs to make money.
