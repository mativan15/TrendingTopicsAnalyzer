Banaras Hindu University Denies Fee Hike Claims; Proposed New Fee Is For Academic Session 2022-23
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-15 11:37:32

Banaras Hindu University has denied any fee hike for students as claimed by socaial media posts of few university students. According to BHU, the proposed fee structure is for students taking admission in 2022-23. 'No fee hike has been effected for the students of BHU. The proposed new fee is for candidates taking admission for academic session 2022-23," BHU release stated.
