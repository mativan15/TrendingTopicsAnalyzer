Berlusconi told to keep his mouth shut as Giorgia Meloni forms Italy's new government
telegraph.co.uk

Silvio Berlusconi was reportedly told to 'keep his mouth shut' by Italy's prime minister-in-waiting, Giorgia Meloni, as she prepared to formalise the country's most right-wing government since the Second World War.
Mr Berlusconi, known for his gaffes and unpredictability, leads Forza Italia, one of three parties which will make up the new government, along with the hard-right League and Brothers of Italy, led by Ms Meloni.
