Robbie Coltrane was much more than Harry Potter
cairnspost.com.au
entertainment
2022-10-15 04:33:00

For whole generations of moviegoers, Robbie Coltrane will always be Rubeus Hagrid.As the hulking and kindly gamekeeper of the Harry Potter movies, Coltrane endeared himself to fantasy fans who watched him play Hagrid in eight chapters of the mega-successful franchise.It may be the defining role of his career.Coltrane's death at 72 after two years ill health led to an outpouring of tributes and memories for a versatile and respected thespian.But it may be that his most famous role, which he only took after his kids persuaded him to do so, overshadowed a varied career which took in everything from sketch comedy to serious dramas.
