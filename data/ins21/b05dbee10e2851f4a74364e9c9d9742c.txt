By Associated Press Published: 15:20 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 17:49 EDT, 21 October 2022 SUMMERFIELD, Fla. (AP) - A 14-year-old girl caused a stir at a north Florida middle school this week when she handed out more than $10,000 that officials say she stole from her grandmother's safe. Marion County deputies responded to Lake Weir Middle School in Summerfield on Thursday after reports that a student was giving classmates hundreds of dollars each, according to an arrest report. Summerfield is about 60 miles (100 kilometers) northwest of Orlando.
