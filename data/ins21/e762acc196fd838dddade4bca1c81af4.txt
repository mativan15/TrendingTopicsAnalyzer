June 01, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time LA JOLLA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--DermTech, Inc. (NASDAQ: DMTK) ('DermTech' or the 'Company'), a leader in precision dermatology enabled by a non-invasive skin genomics platform, today announced that management is scheduled to present at the William Blair Growth Stock Conference on Wednesday, June 8, 2022 at 2:00 p.m. CT. A live webcast of the presentation will be available on the investor relations section of DermTech's website at https://investors.dermtech.
