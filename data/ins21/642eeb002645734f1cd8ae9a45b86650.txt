After a few weeks of training in Perth, Team India arrived on Saturday in Brisbane where they will play two warm-up games against Australia and New Zealand, The video of their journey from Perth to Brisbane to Perth was posted by the Board of Control for Cricket in India on its Twitter handle with the caption "Touchdown Brisbane." It shows the players arriving at the Perth airport, some of them waving to the camera. "Bye, bye Perth. We go to Brisbane," allrounder Hardik Pandya said in a piece to camera.
