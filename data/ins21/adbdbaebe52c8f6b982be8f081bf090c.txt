Mixed-use project's office portfolio is over 99% leased as Company continues to see demand and positive releasing activity

VIRGINIA BEACH, Va., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Armada Hoffler (NYSE: AHH) announced that Old Dominion University has agreed to lease 18,000 square feet in the Town Center of Virginia Beach. The new ODU space, previously occupied by Hampton University, will be home to the University's Institute of Data Science and Coastal Virginia Center for Cyber Innovation.
