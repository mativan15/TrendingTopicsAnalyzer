Netflix added a healthy 2.4 million subscribers in third quarter. Copyright 2022 The Associated Press. All rights reserved
After two quarters of not-very-good news, Netflix had something to celebrate during Tuesday's third-quarter earnings call. The big question is whether this good news is the blip or those previous six months of bad news were the blip. Will subscriptions continue to peak or dip back into the valley? Though Wall Street seemed pleased Tuesday evening, the company still faces a lot more questions in the coming months.
