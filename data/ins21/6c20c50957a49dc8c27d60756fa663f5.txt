Work Remotely? Why It May Not Be Worth It to Move to a Cheaper State
fool.com
news
2022-10-19 16:00:20

In the wake of the pandemic, many people are now doing their jobs remotely -- either on a partial or full-time basis. If you're in the latter camp, then you might especially be reaping some cost savings.Working remotely on a full-time basis means not having to spend a dime on commuting. And not only might that arrangement help you save money on things like gas and parking, but it could also lead to lower auto insurance premiums (insurers commonly give discounts for cars that don't get a lot of use).
