There's a new contender for most absurd GOP scapegoat for gun violence.Rep. Billy Long (R-Mo.) was asked during an interview with Missouri radio station The Eagle 93.9 on Wednesday whether there was any appetite among Republicans in Congress to pursue changes to gun laws. It came in the wake of a gunman's massacre of 19 children and two teachers in Uvalde, Texas.Long, who is running for the U.S. Senate, said guns aren't the issue. He criticized proponents of gun control for 'trying to blame an inanimate object for all of these tragedies.
