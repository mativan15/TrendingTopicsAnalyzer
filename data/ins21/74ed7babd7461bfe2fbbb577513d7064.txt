Strictly Come Dancing judge Anton Du Beke has urged the ‘Strictly mole' to stop spoiling the show results before they are broadcast.
Appearing on Loose Women today, Du Beke was asked about the social media account that is dropping information about the show before it airs on Sunday nights, including revealing who is up for elimination. 
‘It gets on my nerves,' Du Beke said.
‘Please stop.
