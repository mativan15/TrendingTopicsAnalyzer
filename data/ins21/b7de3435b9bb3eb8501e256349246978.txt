Wellbeing nurses deliver vital school support
nsw.gov.au
news
2022-10-19 03:27:23

Students across NSW are being provided with critical school-based health and wellbeing support, as part of a $46.8 million NSW government investment.Minister for Mental Health Bronnie Taylor said 87 highly trained School Wellbeing Nurses were now providing care and support to students in approximately 300 rural, regional and metropolitan schools across the state.'School Wellbeing Nurses play a critical role in identifying the health and social needs of students and their families, and then supporting and connecting them with the services they need,' Mrs Taylor said.
