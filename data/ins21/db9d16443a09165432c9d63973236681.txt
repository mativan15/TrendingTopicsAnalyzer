Unopened iPhone From 2007 In Its Original Box Sells For Over ₹32 Lakh—Worth 49 iPhone 14s
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 15:43:40

The 2007 original iPhone model has been sold at auction for more than 60 times its actual cost, illustrating the device's value as a prized collectible. The boxed phone, a "first-release" model that was apparently in excellent condition, with a two-megapixel camera, 4 or 8 GB of storage, a cutting-edge touch screen, and a web browser.
When it was first released publicly, it cost between $499 and $599.
The first-release variant was sold at auction on Sunday by US-based LCG Auctions for $39,339.60.
