Selection will enable accelerated product advancementsDENVER, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Engage Mobilize Inc. (ENGAGE®) was officially selected to receive a $250,000 Advanced Industries Award from the Colorado Office of Economic Development and International Trade (OEDIT). The Advanced Industries Accelerator Grant Program targets funding the development and commercialization of advanced technologies created in Colorado. The program encourages public-private partnerships and is intended to drive innovation, accelerate commercialization, and provide access to capital in the state.
