Max & Amy In The MorningBy MaxwellOct 18, 2022Do you have a loved one - or are you - affected by prostate cancer? Listen to Max & Amy's conversation with Mark Good, from Team Blue Iowa, about how you can join Prostate Cancer Awareness of Central Iowa and The Urology Center of Iowa at our 2nd annual Iowa Prostate Cancer Awareness Dinner at St. Boniface in Waukee. We are hosting this dinner to honor Iowa patients, survivors & families affected by prostate cancer.  Dinner will be provided by Olive Garden and is FREE for the first 100 RSVPs.
