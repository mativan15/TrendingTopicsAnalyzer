EDMONTON, AB, June 1, 2022 /CNW/ - Canadian Western Bank ("CWB") (TSX: CWB) today announced that it has established a new at-the-market equity program (the "ATM Program") that allows CWB to issue up to $150 million worth of common shares from treasury ("Common Shares") to the public from time to time at the prevailing market price through the Toronto Stock Exchange or any other marketplace on which the Common Shares are listed, quoted or otherwise traded.
