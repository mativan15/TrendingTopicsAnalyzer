Toyota Kirloskar sales jump 14-fold to 10,216 units in May
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 07:30:00

The company had dispatched just 707 units to dealerships in May last year amid the second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic.Toyota Kirloskar Motor (TKM) on Wednesday reported an over 14-fold increase in total vehicle sales to 10,216 units in May, as compared to the same month last year. The company had dispatched just 707 units to dealerships in May last year amid the second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic.

"Demand continues to peak as we have very encouraging booking orders and enquiries for all our models," TKM Associate Vice President (Sales, and Strategic Marketing) Atul Sood said in a statement.
