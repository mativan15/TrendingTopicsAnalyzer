More Video Stephen Kirkland, Bloomberg News Stocks traded well off session highs, with Apple Inc. quickly erasing gains and Netflix Inc. slumping ahead of its earnings report. The S&P 500 trimmed most of a rally that topped 2 per cent earlier in the day, while the tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 underperformed. Apple shares turned negative after the Information reported the tech giant is cutting production of its iPhone 14 Plus, citing people familiar with the matter.
