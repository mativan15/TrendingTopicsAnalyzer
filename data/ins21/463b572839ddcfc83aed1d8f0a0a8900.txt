Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Pharma index advances 0.08% in a weak market
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 05:15:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Pharma index traded positive around 10:04AM(IST)on Thursday in a weak market.Aurobindo Pharma(up 1.33 per cent), Cipla(up 1.16 per cent), Torrent Pharmaceuticals(up 1.08 per cent), Zydus Lifesciences(up 0.8 per cent) and Alkem Laboratories(up 0.65 per cent) were among the top gainers.Laurus(down 1.77 per cent), Granules India(down 1.47 per cent), Gland Pharma(down 0.93 per cent), Strides Pharma Science(down 0.82 per cent) and Dr Reddys Laboratories(down 0.65 per cent) were the top losers on the index.
