Jamie Trull, CEO of Balance CFO LLC, helps business owners make & keep more money through her financial fitness programs. getty In 2021 annualized inflation in the U.S. nearly reached 5%, a figure last seen over 30 years ago. Most small business owners have never had to contend with such a significant change in the cost of goods and labor necessary to sustain their businesses. As a result, small business owners are grappling with rapidly rising costs and falling profit margins, in some cases threatening the long-term sustainability of their businesses.
