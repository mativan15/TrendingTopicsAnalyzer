The Influencing Factors of Users' Attitudes and Continuance Intention for Olympic Viewing on Mobile Applications in China
Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY)
science
2022-10-16 00:00:00

Along with the proliferation of the mobile information system, broadcasters depend on multiple channels to distribute the massive amount of Olympic content. Users' viewing habits for the Olympics gradually tend to be diverse, and undergo changes from the outlets of television to mobile broadcasting on smartphones. Through the mobile application of rights holding broadcasters, the users not only watch high-quality live-streaming content via multiple platforms but also enjoy interviews with athletes after the competition.
