10 applicants so far; govt extends PLI scheme for specialty steel for third time till June 30
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 16:00:00

AgenciesOn July 22 last year, the Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi had approved a Rs 6,322-crore PLI scheme to boost the production of speciality steel in the country. The government on Monday extended for the third time the deadline to submit applications under the production-lined incentive scheme for specialty steel till June 30, 2022. Till now only 10 applications have been received from the players looking to invest under the Rs 6,322-crore scheme which was launched in July last year.
