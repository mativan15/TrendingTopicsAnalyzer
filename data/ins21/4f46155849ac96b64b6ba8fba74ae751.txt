These photos and videos of empty baby formula shelves illustrate how bad the crisis really is

news
2022-05-31 15:48:34

Parents are taking to social media to vent their frustration and share images of just how dire the baby formula shortage has become.The shortage — induced by pandemic-related supply chain issues and exacerbated by a recent recall by major formula manufacturer Abbott Nutrition — has left parents scrambling to feed their children. As of the first week of May, 43 percent of the nation's formula supply was out of stock, according to retail data firm Datasembly, and FDA Commissioner Dr. Robert Califf said on May 26 that it will be two months before the formula supply returns to normal and "shelves are replete again.
