Final Week to Buy Record-High 9.62% I Bonds: How to Do It

2022-10-21 15:55:02

I bonds -- US Treasury savings bonds with an interest rate tied to inflation -- have been touted as the best low-risk investment around, ever since their record 9.62% interest rate was announced back in May. You can still lock in that impressive rate for the next six months, but you'll need to act quickly -- the current rate on I bonds expires Oct. 28.Although inflation has eased slightly of late, the projected rate for the next six-month period is still 6.47%, very good on its own for a low-risk investment.
