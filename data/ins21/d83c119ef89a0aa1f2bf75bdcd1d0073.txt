By Reuters Published: 13:00 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 13:00 EDT, 17 October 2022 For a record 10th time, North Carolina is No. 1 in the Associated Press preseason Top 25 poll released Monday. The Tar Heels, who lost to Kansas in the national championship game in April, garnered 47 of 62 first-place votes to beat out Gonzaga for the top spot. Gonzaga was No. 1 in the preseason poll the past two years. The Tar Heels return four starters with Caleb Love, R.J. Davis, Leaky Black and Armando Bacot.
