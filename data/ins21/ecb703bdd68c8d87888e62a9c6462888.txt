16 October 2022 by Shane Stahl 
Collider.com On October 11, Hollywood and the culture at large lost one of the last great icons of stage and screen when Dame Angela Lansbury passed away in her sleep just days short of her 97th birthday. Many of her greatest triumphs were on stage — she won five Tony Awards over a 43-year span, and was honored with the Lifetime Achievement Tony Award just this year — and Broadway paid tribute on Saturday by dimming the marquees in recognition of a landmark career.
