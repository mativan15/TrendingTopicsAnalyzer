By Reuters Published: 16:44 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 16:44 EDT, 18 October 2022 CAIRO, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman on Tuesday launched the National Industrial Strategy, which aims to increase the industrial exports value to 557 billion riyals by 2030 ($148.34 billion), the country's state news agency, SPA, reported. The strategy is a plan to promote industry and attract investment in the kingdom's economy, leading to economic diversification and growth of non-oil exports and GDP.
