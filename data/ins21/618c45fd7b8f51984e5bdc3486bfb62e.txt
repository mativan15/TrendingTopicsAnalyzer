June 02, 2022 11:51 AM Eastern Daylight Time ENGLEWOOD CLIFFS, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--OwnBackup, the leading SaaS data protection platform, today announced that they are a Platinum sponsor of the 2022 Gartner Security & Risk Management Summit in National Harbor, MD, and will be presenting at the event on Friday, June 10, 2022.



Gartner Security & Risk Management conferences are unique destination experiences designed for security leaders to connect face to face with Gartner experts, peers and a wide array of solution providers.
