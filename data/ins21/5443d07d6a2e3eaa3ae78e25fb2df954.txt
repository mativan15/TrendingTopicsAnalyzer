October 17, 2022 02:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Reps. Blaine Luetkemeyer (R-Mo.), Jason Smith (R-Mo.), and Ann Wagner (R-Mo.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

All three candidates have exemplary lifetime ratings that have made them a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero.
