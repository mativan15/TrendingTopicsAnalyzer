October 17, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Progressive Grocer, the only media brand serving the grocery industry for more than 100 years, is honoring Amazon, Publix, Kroger and others as part of its 2022 Retailer of the Century Awards.



Over the past two years, grocery retailers have had to reassess and adapt nearly every facet of their operations. Changes to the grocery landscape will continue, shaped by both macroeconomic factors and those pesky, always-changing consumer preferences.
