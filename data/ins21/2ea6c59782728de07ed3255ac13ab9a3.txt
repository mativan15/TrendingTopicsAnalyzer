Cristi Croitoru/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Uber Technologies, Inc.'s (NYSE:UBER) share price is down about 45% since the beginning of the year and is turning a popular growth stock into a possible value play. However with more and more analysts forecasting a recession in the near future, investors should be careful when deciding to jump into the stock. The company's three business segments do not appear to be resistant to a recession and could be drastically harmed from lower consumer spending.
