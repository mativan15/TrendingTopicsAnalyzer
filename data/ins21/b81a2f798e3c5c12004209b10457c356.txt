Internet City, Dubai--(Newsfile Corp. - October 19, 2022) - LBank Exchange, a global digital asset trading platform, will list Spectre Chain (SPC) on October 20, 2022. For all users of LBank Exchange, the SPC/USDT trading pair will be officially available for trading at 3:00 UTC on October 20, 2022.
