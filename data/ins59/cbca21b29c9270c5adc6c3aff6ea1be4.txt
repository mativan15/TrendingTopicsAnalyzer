The college baseball regular season and conference tournaments have concluded.
The NCAA Tournament begins with regional play Friday.
Regionals take place June 3-6 at 16 campus sites. Each regional features four teams and will be contested in a double-elimination format.
The winner of each regional advances to the NCAA Super Regionals, which are slated to begin June 9 at campus sites of the higher seed. Each Super Regional is a best-of-3 series.
The 16 regional hosts are Tennessee, Georgia Southern, Texas, East Carolina, Texas A&M, Louisville, Florida, Virginia Tech, Stanford, Maryland, North Carolina, Oklahoma State, Miami, Southern Miss, Auburn and Oregon State.
