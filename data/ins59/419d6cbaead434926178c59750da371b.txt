By Reuters Published: 00:24 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 00:24 EDT, 21 October 2022 Alex Tuch produced his first career hat trick to help the visiting Buffalo Sabres earn a 6-3 win against the Calgary Flames on Thursday. Dylan Cozens and Casey Mittelstadt each added a goal and an assist while Rasmus Dahlin also scored for the Sabres. Buffalo's Vinnie Hinostroza had three assists, and Owen Power and Jacob Bryson had two assists apiece. Eric Comrie made 40 saves for the victory. Andrew Mangiapane, Trevor Lewis and Nikita Zadorov scored for Calgary.
