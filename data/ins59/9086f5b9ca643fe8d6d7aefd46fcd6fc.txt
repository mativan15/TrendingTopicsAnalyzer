Texas school shooter Ramos "loved hurting animals": Uvalde classmate
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-29 21:51:23

Teenage gunman Salvador Ramos was known for abusing animals prior to Tuesday's massacre of 19 schoolchildren and two teachers in Uvalde, Texas, according to some of his fellow students.One Ulvade High School classmate said Ramos, 18, was a bully who hurt dogs and provoked other people, despite claims by family and friends that the shooter was a victim teased for a stutter and other reasons."He would go to the park and try to pick on people and he loved hurting animals," Jaime Arellano told the Daily Beast.
