The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur will close the window for applying online for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE 2023) today, October 16. Applicants can register on the official website for GATE 2023 at gate.iitk.ac.in. Those who have not registered yet can apply today by paying an additional late registration fee. A candidate currently studying in the 3rd or higher years of any undergraduate degree program or has already completed any government approved degree program in Engineering/ Technology/ Architecture/ Science/ Commerce/ Arts can apply for GATE 2023.
