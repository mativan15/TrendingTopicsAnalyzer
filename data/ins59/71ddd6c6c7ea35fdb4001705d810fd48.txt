Solar-powered medicine storage facility ready at Kozhikode medical college hospital

news
2022-05-31 13:08:41

The Agency for New and Renewable Energy Research and Technology (ANERT) has successfully completed the trial operation of a hi-tech solar-powered vaccine storage container at Government Medical College Hospital, Kozhikode. Electricity Minister K. Krishnankutty will inaugurate the new project, claimed to be the first such initiative in the State, on Wednesday.The container, fitted with 7-kW solar panels for steady power supply, has a storage capacity of five metric tonnes to meet the additional requirements at the Medical College Hospital.
