'Chaudhary' Singer Mame Khan Shares Heartwarming Reason Why He Wore Rajasthani Outfit At Cannes
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 11:37:24

Rajasthani singer Mame Khan who is known for his distinct, raw and mesmerising voice made history earlier this year by becoming the first folk artiste from India to walk the prestigious Cannes 2022 red carpet. While everyone went suited-booted to the prestigious film festival, Khan walked the red carpet rather rooted in Indian culture. He wore a Rajasthani folk attire.The man behind the Coke Studio song Chaudhary has spilled the beans on why he wore an Indian outfit. Known for crooning for movies like Luck By Chance, No One Killed Jessica, Mirzya and Sonchiriya, Mame Khan said that he wanted to represent the true culture and tradition of India.
