2022-10-17 The Abenaki - all we don't know. Part 1.

In the 1990s Robert Goodby, a NH anthropologist, posed this question to his UNH classes. 'When did the first human beings arrive in New England?' The answers were invariably 1620 or 1492. No, not the Pilgrims or Columbus.

The answer? About 13,000 years ago.
