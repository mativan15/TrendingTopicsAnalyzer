Former Bayern Munich winger Ribery retires

Franck Ribery won a league and cup double in his final year at Bayern MunichFormer Bayern Munich and France winger Franck Ribery has announced his retirement from football.The 39-year-old has terminated his contract with Serie A side Salernitana after struggling with a knee issue.He scored 124 goals and contributed 182 assists in 425 games for Bayern from 2007-2019, winning the Champions League in 2013 and nine Bundesliga titles."The ball stops, the feelings do not. Thanks to everyone for this great adventure," said Ribery on Twitter.
