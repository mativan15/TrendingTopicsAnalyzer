October 19, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sloane & Company ('Sloane'), an industry-leading strategic communications firm, today announced the company will open an office in South Florida, allowing the firm to serve existing and new clients in the region.

South Florida is one of the fastest-growing regions for the financial services, technology, and healthcare industries, among many others, and with this investment, Sloane will be able to better serve clients as they expand their own footprint there.
