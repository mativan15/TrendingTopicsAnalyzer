The Edinburgh Woollen Mill is to reopen its former store at Metrocentre Gateshead this week. Situated on upper Exhibition Square, opposite Primark, the shop will stock some of the retailer's most popular collections for women and men, including Country Rose, Isle, Honor Millburn and James Pringle. To celebrate the opening, the retailer will be hiding golden envelopes around the store for customers to find. The envelopes will contain prizes and money off vouchers to spend in-store. Gail Robinson, area manager for the store, said: 'We are excited to welcome visitors to our new store in Gateshead.
