Japan police warn of cyberattacks by North Korea's infamous Lazarus hackers
japantimes.co.jp
news
2022-10-16 08:40:43

Japanese police and other authorities have warned that a North Korean hacker group known as Lazarus is highly likely targeting Japanese crypto asset-related operators in its cyberattacks.
The National Police Agency, the Financial Services Agency and the National Center of Incident Readiness and Strategy for Cybersecurity issued the warning in a document released Saturday.
