Tokens.com to Host Miami Fashion Week in the Metaverse

June 01, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tokens.com Corp. (NEO Exchange Canada: COIN)(Frankfurt Stock Exchange: 76M) (OTCQB US: SMURF) ('Tokens.com' or the 'Company'), a publicly-traded company that invests in Web3 crypto assets and businesses linked to the Metaverse and NFTs, is pleased to share that its subsidiary, Metaverse Group, is partnering with Miami Fashion Week to host Fashion Week events in the Metaverse on its renown Fashion Street Estate.



'Our Fashion Estate has become a pillar in the virtual fashion community and a popular virtual destination to host Metaverse fashion events,' said Andrew Kiguel, CEO of Tokens.
