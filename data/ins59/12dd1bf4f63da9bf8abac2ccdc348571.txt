US stocks waver as earnings roll in with focus on UK
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 14:45:00

Wall Street's main indexes opened lower on Thursday after the resignation of Liz Truss as prime minister of the UK added a layer of uncertainty to markets struggling to hold on to gains from a set of positive earnings forecasts from U.S. companies. The Dow Jones Industrial Average added 160 points, or 0.5%. The S&P 500 and the Nasdaq Composite gained 0.2% and 0.4%, respectively.
