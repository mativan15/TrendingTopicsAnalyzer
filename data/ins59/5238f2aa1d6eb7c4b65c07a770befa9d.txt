Sasural Simar Ka actress Vaishali Thakkar was found hanging in her Indore apartment, earlier today (October 16). The actress, who reportedly died by suicide, was one of the first people to open up about late friend and famous TV and Bollywood star Sushant Singh Rajput's demise. She believed it was a 'planned murder'.Vaishali revealed how Sushant couldn't be the one to die by suicide as he valued life a lot. At that time, she spoke to an entertainment portal and said, 'Sushant was a person who wanted to save others' lives and that means he used to value life a lot.
