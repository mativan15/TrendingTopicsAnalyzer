Chinese Stocks in US Skid to Lowest in More Than Nine Years

(Bloomberg) -- Chinese stocks listed in the US tumbled Wednesday as concerns over the nation's economic outlook and a rise of Covid cases in the middle of the twice-a-decade party congress weighed on investor sentiment.

Most Read from Bloomberg

The Nasdaq Golden Dragon China Index sank as much as 7.3%, its steepest one-day decline since June 13, putting the gauge on pace for its lowest level in more than nine years. The index has erased all its gains since March, when Beijing announced a strong push to stabilize its markets.
