L&T Technology announces interim dividend of Rs 15 per share
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 13:15:00

ETMarkets.comPreviously, the company had declared a final dividend of Rs 15 per shareL&T Technology Services, in its board meeting on Tuesday, announced an interim dividend of Rs 15 per share. It has fixed October 28 as the record date. 'The interim dividend shall be paid to equity shareholders of the company whose names will appear in the Register of Members or in the records of the depositories as beneficial owners of equity shares as on Friday, October 28, 2022, which is the record date fixed for the aforesaid purpose,' said the company in its filing with the exchanges.
