Omega Flex Chief Operating Officer Retires
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 16:51:00

By Kathryn Hardison Omega Flex Inc. said on Wednesday that Chief Operating Officer Mark Albino has retired. Mr. Albino retired on May 31 after 26 years at the metal hose and accessory manufacturer where he helped expand the company's products and its business in Europe. He will continue to serve as a director. Shares of Omega were trading at $108.97, down 14.2% so far this year and down 27.8% over the past 12 months.
