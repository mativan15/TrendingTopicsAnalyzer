NEW YORK, NY / ACCESSWIRE / October 20, 2022 / AssetTech Pioneer Pepper announces the addition of Romi Mahajan as President. In this role, Romi will partner with CEO Pulak Sinha on all aspects of the business and growth. Pepper is an industry-leading, cloud-native data platform for Asset Management, focused on Alternatives, Illiquid Investments, and Secondaries.

"Romi has a storied background in the technology and Financial Services space. His leadership and network are key to the next phase of Pepper's growth," said CEO Sinha.
