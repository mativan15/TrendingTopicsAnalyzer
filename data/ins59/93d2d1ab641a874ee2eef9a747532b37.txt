WITH mortgage rates rising and the cost of living crisis putting a huge strain on many people, for first-time buyers, the thought of ever being able to own a home may be seeming pretty bleak right now.
But one couple has proved that life doesn't only have to involve moving out and paying a mortgage or renting a property.
Tate, 19, and her husband Ben, 20, decided to buy an old school van, so they could keep their cost of living lowCredit: instagram/olathebus

The couple love to travel around in their home on wheelsCredit: instagram/olathebus
One married couple, known on social media as ‘OlaTheBus', decided to ditch the idea of renting a property and instead live in a school bus full-time.
