Image source, Open NottinghamImage caption, Artist Rachel Carter was joined by the National Justice Museum and the older women's group, WondrousA group of campaigners have held a silent protest calling on more women to be depicted in statue form. Artist and sculptor Rachel Carter took part in the demonstration under the statue of Brian Clough at Nottingham's speakers' corner on Tuesday. She said excluding sculptures of female royals, statues of women currently only make up 5% of monuments across the country.
