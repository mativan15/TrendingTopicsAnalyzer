Atlanta, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Piedmont Office Realty Trust (NYSE: PDM) announced today that the Company will release its third quarter financial results on Wednesday, November 2, 2022, after the close of trading on the New York Stock Exchange. A conference call is scheduled for Thursday, November 3, 2022, at 9:00 a.m. Eastern daylight time and will be broadcast live in listen-only mode on the company's investor relations website at investor.piedmontreit.
