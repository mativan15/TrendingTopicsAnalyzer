The Narendra Modi government on Tuesday approved minimum support prices of six Rabi crops for marketing season 2023-24.Union minister Anurag Thakur announced that the MSP of wheat increased by ₹110, barley by ₹100, gram by ₹105, lentil or masur by ₹500, Rapeseed & Mustard by ₹400 rupees and Safflower by ₹209 .
