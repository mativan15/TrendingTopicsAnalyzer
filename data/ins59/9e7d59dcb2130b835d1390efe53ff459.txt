How your voice can reveal if you're depressed
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-01 15:01:31

More people than ever are suffering from mental health problems, with one in six adults managing symptoms associated with mental ill health at any given time.Depression can manifest itself in many ways, from insomnia to libido loss, tearfulness to anxiety. However, one company has developed an unusual way to tell if someone is depressed: by listening to their voice.Research has shown that people suffering from depression speak with a reduced frequency range in vowel production, which means that they have a flatter-sounding voice.
