Newark, NJ, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- You've heard how the cannabis industry is growing in leaps and bounds. We're poised to witness the largest historical event of this magnitude since prohibition, especially in New Jersey. However with the Green Rush firmly underway, the smoke screen of confusion expands for opportunists looking to get a piece of the pie. That's precisely why NJ based husband & wife team Gary & Marie George created the 2-day event, the 'Real Cannabis Entrepreneur Conference' happening Friday thru Saturday (Oct 21-22nd) @ the Doubletree in Newark NJ.
