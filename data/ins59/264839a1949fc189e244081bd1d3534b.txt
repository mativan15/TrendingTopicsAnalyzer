Thiruvananthapuram: Panchayat buys land for flats, fund crisis forces plot division
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 10:00:00

THIRUVANANTHAPURAM: Soaring construction costs coupled with recurring maintenance expenses for apartment complexes has forced the government to recommend individual housing to local bodies even after purchase of land for LIFE towers.Kilimanoor panchayat is now deliberating on future course of action as the government has directed the local body to divide the plot purchased for apartment complex among the beneficiaries of the scheme based on priority. The panchayat with a lone assistant engineer will in effect have to complete plot subdivision and other technical processes to complete the task.
