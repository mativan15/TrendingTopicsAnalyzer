Vadodara: Three offences lodged against Siddhi Vinayak Developers for cheating buyers
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 09:30:00

Representative imageVADODARA: Three separate offences were registered against a city-based builder after he did not come up with a site and failed to refund money to the complainants who had booked properties there.A series of offences has been registered against the builder Apoorva Patel of Siddhi Vinayak Developers earlier too.The fresh offences were registered by the Manjalpur police when three people filed complaints alleging that Patel had taken advances and part payments of properties booked by them, but did not refund the money when he could not conduct work on the sites.
