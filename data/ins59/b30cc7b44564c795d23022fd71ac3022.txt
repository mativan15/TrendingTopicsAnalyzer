Youngsters get in Halloween spirit at Fall Fest & Safe Trick-or-Treat event in Pottsville
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 23:02:00

Oct. 21—POTTSVILLE — Trick-or-treaters of all ages took part in a festive, fun-filled Halloween event on Thursday at the Fall Fest and Safe Trick or Treat.

Dressed as ghouls, superheroes, Pokémon and other characters, the youngsters made their way around Barefield Outdoor Recreation Complex, hauling in candy and participating in games and activities.

More than 200 people attended the event, which was hosted by Helping Young People Excel (HYPE) Schuylkill, part of the Schuylkill County Chamber of Commerce.
