Significant developments have been made to improve Annapolis Town Center's community gathering areas, parking, and high-end retail shops. ANNAPOLIS, Md., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Annapolis Town Center will host a Ribbon Cutting Ceremony to celebrate the grand opening of the newly built East Village. The event will take place at Annapolis Town Center on Sunday, October 30th at 1:30PM. First Lady of Maryland Yumi Hogan, Mayor Buckley, Senator Sarah Elfreth as well as many other local community leaders and county officials will be in attendance.
