Board to their practice squad in a corresponding move.Gordon was signed to Tennessee's practice squad in September after being released by the Kansas City Chiefs.The veteran receiver was elevated to the Titans' game-day roster from the practice squad twice through five games but didn't record any stats other than 22 snaps in two games.
