The son of murdered New York mob associate Sylvester "Sally Daz" Zottola has been convicted of hiring the hitmen who killed him.According to Sky News partner NBC New York, Anthony Zottola was convicted of plotting to kill his father and brother in an attempt to take over the family's multi-million dollar property empire.
Zottola and Himen Ross, a co-conspirator, were convicted on Wednesday, following a six-week federal trial over the 2018 killing of 71-year-old Sylvester at a McDonald's drive-in in the Bronx area of New York City.
