Michael Andretti pushes to become F1's 11th team

2022-05-27 03:00:21

Michael Andretti is one of the US's most successful IndyCar team owners, and also involved in Formula E, Imsa, Extreme E and the Australian Supercars Championship. His time spent at racetracks is not just for fun.So, while Formula One's A-list celebrities were strutting their stuff for the cameras before the start of this month's Miami Grand Prix, the former grand prix driver was hard at work.Amid a push to get Andretti Global on to the F1 entry list for 2024, he was courting Mohammed Bin Sulayem, president of the sport's governing body, the FIA, to gain support for his plans.
