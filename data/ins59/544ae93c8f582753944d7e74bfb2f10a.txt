Ori Devuda Movie Review: This tastefully made fantasy rom-com explores second chances in life
indiatimes.com
entertainment

Story: Arjun Durga Raj (Vishwak Sen) gets married to his best friend Anu Paul (Mithila Palkar) without second thoughts, but one year into the marriage, he regrets his decision and files for a divorce. A stranger (Rahul Ramakrishna) in the judicial court guides him to a love court, wherein a mysterious man (Venkatesh) offers him a golden ticket. What happens once he accepts the ticket with conditions? How did his life change?Review: In a frame reminiscing the Creation of Adam, a Fresco painting by Michelangelo, a mysterious man gives a golden ticket to our protagonist Arjun and offers to solve his problem.
