Free organ transplants in Karnataka touch century mark

Karnataka's Organ Transplantation scheme reached a milestone on October 13 when the free organ transplants touched the century mark.The 100 patients from below the poverty line (BPL) families, who have undergone free organ transplants, have also got a total of 726 cycles of free immunosuppressive medication under the scheme since its inception in 2019. Health and Medical Education Minister K. Sudhakar, who tweeted about this milestone on Saturday, said the 100 transplants comprised 77 kidney transplants, 15 heart transplants, and eight liver transplants.
