Indiewire Former Walt Disney Studios chairman and DreamWorks Animation co-founder Jeffrey Katzenberg wanted to take 'West Side Story' to the next level.In an oral history of the 2002 Disney film 'Lilo & Stitch,' co-writer and director Christopher Sanders recalled how Katzenberg pitched a failed feline animated reimagining of 'West Side Story' to legendary composer Leonard Bernstein.'Jeffrey Katzenberg once flew me out to New York City because he wanted us to do ‘West Side Story' with cats,' Sanders told Vulture.
