Sebastian Vettel has detailed the ordeal of trying to chase down the thief who stole his bag after the Spanish Grand Prix.The four-time Formula One world champion had left his backpack in his car while checking out of the hotel and emerged from reception to discover it was gone - yet he did have one method of catching the assailant.'I was checking out in the morning from the hotel, and I had my bag in the car,' he told Friday's press conference.'When I quickly went back in and came back out the bag was gone.
