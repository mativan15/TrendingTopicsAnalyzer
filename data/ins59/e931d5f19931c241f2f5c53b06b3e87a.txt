indiatimes.com

iStockFor the day, the index closed at 16,584.55, down 76.85 points or 0.46 per cent.NEW DELHI: Nifty50 took a breather on Tuesday and formed a Long Legged Doji on the daily chart, suggesting indecisiveness among traders. Analysts said the uptrend remains intact even as they believe some sideways move is a possibility after the recent rally. A support for the index is seen at 16,400 while resistance is likely for the index at 16,700 level. The price action for the last couple of sessions is developing as a Flag pattern on the hourly chart, said Gaurav Ratnaparkhi of Sharekhan, who believes the sideways action can continue in the range of 16,500-16,700, before the index prepares for the next leg up.
