Millions of households are skipping meals or finding it hard to put healthy food on the table as they struggle with the cost-of-living crisis, Which? research suggests.Among the 9% who said they are finding it 'very difficult' to get by, half (50%) said their household was skipping meals, as did 26% of those who are finding the current situation 'quite difficult'.Almost half of all consumers (46%) said they were finding it harder to eat healthily compared to before the crisis, rising to 78% of those finding it very difficult financially.
