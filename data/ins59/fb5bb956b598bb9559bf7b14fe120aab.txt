Image source, Press AssociationImage caption, Stansted Airport plans to increase passenger capacity to 43 million per yearA district council has made an improved offer of £2.05m to Stansted Airport to cover its legal costs following a battle in the High Court.The international airport won an appeal against Uttlesford District Council in 2021, allowing it to increase passenger capacity to 43 million per year.Stansted Airport said "we welcome this positive step forward by Uttlesford District Council".
