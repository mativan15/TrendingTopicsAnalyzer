Programming refers to a technological process for telling a computer which tasks to perform in order to solve problems. You can think of programming as a collaboration between humans and computers, in which humans create instructions for a computer to follow (code) in a language computers can understand. Programming enables so many things in our lives. Here are some examples: When you browse a website to find information, contact a service provider, or make a purchase, programming allows you to interact with the site's on-page elements, such as sign-up or purchase buttons, contact forms, and drop-down menus.
