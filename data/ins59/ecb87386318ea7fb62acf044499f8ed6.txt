Morning Update: Bank of Canada says it's ready ‘to act more forcefully' after interest-rate hike
theglobeandmail.com
economics
2022-06-02 10:38:51

Good morning,The Bank of Canada announced another oversized interest-rate hike yesterday and said that it is prepared to 'act more forcefully if needed,' signalling that it intends to keep pushing borrowing costs rapidly higher in an effort to bring inflation back under control.The central bank's governing council voted to raise the policy rate by half a percentage point – its third interest-rate increase this year. That brings the benchmark rate to 1.5 per cent, just a quarter point below the prepandemic level.
