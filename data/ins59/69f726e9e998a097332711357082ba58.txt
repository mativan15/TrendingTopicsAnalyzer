May 31, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOCA RATON, Fla. & VERONA, Italy--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sun Capital Partners, Inc. ('Sun Capital'), a leading private investment firm focused on defensible businesses in growing markets with tangible performance improvement opportunities, today announced that an affiliate has completed the acquisition of Verona, Italy-based TENAX, a world-leading manufacturer of chemical products, abrasives and tooling for the treatment of natural stone, engineered stone and ceramic tiles.
