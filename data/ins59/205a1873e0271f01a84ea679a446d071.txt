 Drivers headed from the Lower Mainland into the B.C. Interior will want to plan an alternate route Friday evening, with the Coquihalla Highway closed to northbound traffic due to a serious crash.
DriveBC said the closure covered the route between Mine Creek and Juliet.Callers to AM730 Traffic reported that the collision involved a motorcycle. The number or severity of injuries was not immediately clear. DriveBC said there was no estimated time for the route to reopen.
