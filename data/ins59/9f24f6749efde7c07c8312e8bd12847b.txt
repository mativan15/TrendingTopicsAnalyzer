Published: 04:19 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 05:09 EDT, 17 October 2022 New Chancellor Jeremy Hunt is to fast-track billions of pounds of savings in an attempt to get the public finances back on track and stabilise financial markets after weeks of turmoil. The Treasury said Mr Hunt will issue an emergency statement at 11am on Monday to rush forward measures that had been due to be announced on October 31 in what is known as the medium-term fiscal plan. He will then address MPs in a statement to the House Of Commons later in the afternoon.
