Published: 07:14 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 07:14 EDT, 17 October 2022 Arsenal goalkeeper Aaron Ramsdale expressed his 'elation' after his side held on to win 1-0 at Leeds and extend their lead at the top of the Premier League.
