The Joint Examination Council Uttar Pradesh(JEECUP) is expected to release the admit card in online mode for the UPJEE exam today. Candidates who are planning to appear for the Uttar Pradesh Joint Entrance Examination can download their hall tickets from the official website of the examination board which is jeecup.admissions.nic.in. The examination is scheduled to be held from the 6th of June to the 10th of June, 2022. The application process for the exam was conducted on the 30th of April of this year.
