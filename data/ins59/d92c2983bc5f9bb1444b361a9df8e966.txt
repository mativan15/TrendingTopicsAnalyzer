ReutersShares of the company have risen about 25 per cent in the year 2021 so far, whereas the counter has more than trebled since its Covid lows.New Delhi: Homegrown auto major Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M) rallied over 3 per cent in the early trade on Tuesday to scale Rs 1,000 mark for the first time ever. The scrip rose more than 5 per cent in the previous session and extended its gains on Tuesday to hit an all time peak of Rs 1,029.90. The company reported a strong performance in the March 2022 quarter, which led to the buoyancy.
