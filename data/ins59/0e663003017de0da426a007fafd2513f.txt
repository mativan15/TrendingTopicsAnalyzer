Webster Bank is the exclusive bank partner for this first-of-its-kind savings program.

HOUSTON, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Save®, a fintech company that helps people earn higher yields on their spending and savings, announced that they have signed an agreement with Webster Bank, N.A., a leading bank in the Northeast that provides a wide range of digital and traditional financial solutions to consumers and commercial clients. Webster Bank will make savings accounts available to Save customers who participate in this product offering on Save's innovative Savetech platform.
