Beekeepers demand end to NSW hive culls in anti-varroa mite campaign
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-20 01:52:00

Beekeepers in New South Wales are demanding the end to the killing of honeybees as part of the state's ongoing battle with the spread of varroa mite.A petition calling on the government to 'stop the killing of honeybees', increase financial support, roll back regulations, and release the modelling underpinning the state's strategy has garnered over 25,000 signatures.Dolfi Benesh, a NSW Central Coast beekeeper who co-authored the petition said he felt bullied by the Department of Primary Industries after it ordered the destruction of his 60 hives.
