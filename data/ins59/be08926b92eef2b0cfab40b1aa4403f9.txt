The Very Good Food Company to raise $6.5M in stock and warrants offering
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-31 12:43:20

The Very Good Food Company (NASDAQ:VGFC) is down 3.03% premarket on Tuesady after the firm has entered into a securities purchase agreement with an institutional investor for aggregate gross proceeds of ~$6.5M.The company will issue 32.5M units and equal no. of warrants at a purchase price of $0.20 per unit.Each warrant entitles the holder to acquire one share at an exercise price of $0.2582/share for a period five years from the issuance date.Net proceeds will be used by the company for general working capital purposes.
