Srinivas Prasad to retire from politics after his term as MP ends

Former Minister and BJP MP representing Chamarajanagar (reserved) Lok Sabha constituency V. Srinivas Prasad on Monday said he will be retire from politics after his present term as MP ends in 2024. Speaking at a function organised to felicitate C. Basavaraju, Vice-Chancellor of Karnataka State Law University, and Kalyanasiri Bhanteji, who obtained a PhD from KSOU in Mysuru on Monday, Mr. Prasad attributed his decision to quit politics to his ill health. He said he would complete 50 years in politics when his present term as MP ends.
