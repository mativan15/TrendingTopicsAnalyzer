By Colin Kellaher Shares of ClearSign Technologies Corp. fell nearly 20% in premarket trading Friday after the developer of industrial combustion and sensing technologies raised about $4 million in a public offering of stock. The Tulsa, Okla., company said it is selling 3.64 million shares at $1.11 apiece, about 15% below Thursday's closing price of $1.31. ClearSign, which had about 32.2 million shares outstanding before the offering, said it expects gross proceeds of about $4 million, or about $4.
