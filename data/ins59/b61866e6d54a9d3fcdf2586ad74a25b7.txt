REUTERS/Mike Blake October 19, 2022 - The popularity of digital assets has enhanced the need for advisors and fiduciaries to quickly become well versed about them in order to assist their clients in protecting and administering this relatively new asset class. A critical step in protecting and administering digital assets is determining which jurisdiction should govern trust planning. Wyoming is often selected due to its favorable tax, trust and privacy laws.But when it comes to assets like cryptocurrency, Wyoming is the only state with a comprehensive legal framework specifically designed for digital assets.
