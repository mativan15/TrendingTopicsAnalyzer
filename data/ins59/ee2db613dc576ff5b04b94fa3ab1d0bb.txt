October 19, 2022 10:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--EVO Payments, Inc. (NASDAQ:EVOP), a leading global provider of payment technology integrations and acquiring solutions, will release its third quarter 2022 financial results after the market closes on Wednesday, November 2, 2022.

EVO Acquisition by Global Payments


On August, 1, 2022, EVO and Global Payments, Inc. ('Global Payments') announced that they have entered into a definitive merger agreement under which Global Payments will acquire EVO for $34.
