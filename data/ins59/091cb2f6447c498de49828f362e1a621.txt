Manasi Parekh Gohil honored at Lieutenant Governor Bethany Hall
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-19 10:12:00

Manasi Parekh Gohil, has time and again won the audience's affection. The 'GolKeri' actress who enjoys a massive fan following has recently posted a video on her Instagram handle. As seen in the video shared by the actress, Manasi is honored for her work. Captioning the post, Manasi wrote, "What an incredible honor it is to be recognized by the Governor's office @johncarneyde in the Presence of the Lieutenant Governor Bethany Hall @ltgovernorde for our work as artists in India and for the vision we have to take our language and culture across America and the world through our combined work as actor, singer and creators! This gives us more confidence and support to stay close to our Indian roots while making our presence felt globally! Thank you so much Palash Gupta and Be United Entertainment for making this happen!"

Many of her industry colleagues, and friends congratulated her for achieving the honor on the global platform.
