In 2020, the Indiana State Police Lowell Regional Laboratory was requested by The FBI's Gang Response Investigative Team (GRIT) to re-examine evidence collected in this case and Smith was developed as a suspect from the analysis of male DNA found in the victim's sexual assault kit collected at her autopsy.
