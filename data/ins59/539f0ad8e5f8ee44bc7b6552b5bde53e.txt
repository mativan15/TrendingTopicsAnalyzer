Guilty plea expected in Michigan school shooting that killed 4
journalgazette.net
news
2022-10-22 00:00:00

DETROIT -- A teenager accused of killing four fellow students and injuring more at a Michigan high school is expected to plead guilty to murder next week, authorities said Friday.Ethan Crumbley had created images of violence during a classroom assignment last November but was not sent home from Oxford High School in southeast Michigan. He pulled out a gun a few hours later and committed a mass shooting.'We can confirm that the shooter is expected to plead guilty to all 24 charges, including terrorism, and the prosecutor has notified the victims,' said David Williams, chief assistant prosecutor in Oakland County.
