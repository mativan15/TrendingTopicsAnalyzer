(KB Financial Group)KB Financial Group Chairman Yoon Jong-kyoo and the UK Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy's Deputy Minister Garry Grimstone discussed partnership in achieving net-zero carbon emissions and green investment, the South Korean bank said Friday.In the meeting held Friday, Grimstone called for KB's active cooperation with UK businesses in investment of renewable energy, according to the firm.
