Dalit Man Allegedly Abducted & Tortured For 31 Hours In Rajasthan: What We Know
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 09:35:32

In yet another incident of atrocities against Dalits, a 35-year-old Dalit labourer was allegedly abducted, held captive in a cattle shed, and tortured over a monetary dispute in Rajasthan's Bundi district last week, according to a report by news agency PTI.The police are yet to arrest the accused.A senior police officer said on Sunday that two teams have been formed to nab the accused.Abducted and tortured for 31 hoursAccording to a complaint filed by Radheyshyam Meghwal on May 24, he was abducted by Paramjit Singh and five others on May 22, taken to his home in Alfa Nagar, chained in a cattle shade and tortured for nearly 31 hours till his younger brother freed him.
