Can we spot the next crypto buzzword within the online game?The founder of the Ethereum network, Vitalik Buterin, made waves in the Web3 space when he released his thesis about a new type of digital asset called Soulbound Tokens, or SBTs, in May 2022. In his paper titled 'Decentralized Society: Finding Web3's Soul', he highlighted that SBTs were key to the next phase of creating a richer Web3 ecosystem known as a Decentralised Society, or DeSoc for short.
