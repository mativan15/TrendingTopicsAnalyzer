The last two years have greatly accelerated the transition into hybrid work, but that's not when remote collaboration started. Nonetheless, many creative professionals still use collaboration methods best suited for in-person offices. That means that if you've been struggling to work together over the internet, it might be high time to upgrade your collaboration methods.
