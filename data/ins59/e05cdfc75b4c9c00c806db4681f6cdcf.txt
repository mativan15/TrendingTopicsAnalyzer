TEL AVIV, Israel , Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SuperCom (NASDAQ: SPCB), a global provider of secured solutions for the e-Government, IoT and Cybersecurity sectors, will hold a conference call on Wednesday, October 26, 2022, at 9:00 a.m. Eastern time (6:00 a.m. Pacific Time / 4:00 p.m. IL time) to discuss its financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2022. Financial results will be issued in a press release prior to the call.

SuperCom management will host the conference call, followed by a question-and-answer period.
