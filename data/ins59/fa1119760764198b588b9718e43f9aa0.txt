SEATTLE (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 PTO Exchange, the leading benefits platform that allows employees to self-direct the value of their unused paid time off (PTO) for other needs and causes, today introduced a new technology partnership with UKG, a leading provider of HR, payroll, and workforce management solutions for all people.
