AgenciesThe event will begin with a keynote address that will introduce the latest innovations.The Cupertino-based tech giant, Apple, is all set for its much-awaited Worldwide Developer Conference (WWDC). The event will be held between June 6 and June 10. It will be introduced with a keynote address that is expected to unveil a slew of developments in terms of Apple's operating systems and to an extent some hardware. According to reports, the tech company will likely introduce OS for iPhone, Apple Watch, iPad Mac, and Apple TV among others.
