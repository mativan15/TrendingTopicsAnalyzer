Published: 01:02 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 01:02 EDT, 30 May 2022 Broncos forward Payne Haas has bid farewell to his partner Leilani Mohenoa and daughter Lalita as he departed Brisbane for the Blues Origin camp, with debate continuing to rage amongst fans and NRL legends on his shock transfer request.Haas shocked the Broncos and the entire footy world last Wednesday when he requested an immediate release from the club due to contract disputes - something which the club denied.He'll need to try and switch off all the noise surrounding his request over the next six weeks, after arriving in Blues camp at Coogee on Monday ahead of Game 1 on June 8.
