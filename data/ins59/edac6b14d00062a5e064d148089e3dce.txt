iStockThe company has a long-term power purchase agreement with UP Discom ending in 2036 for unit 1 and 2037 for unit 2, and both are extendable by five years.Mumbai: Banks led by will soon sell 71% equity stake of thermal power company Lanco Anpara Power to Hyderabad-based Megha Engineering and Infrastructures for ₹855 crore, said two people aware of the development. Although Lanco Anpara Power, a special purpose vehicle (SPV) of bankrupt Lanco Infratech, is a performing asset, lenders decided to monetise 71% equity stake that defaulter Lanco Infratech had pledged as security against ₹12,000-crore loans.
