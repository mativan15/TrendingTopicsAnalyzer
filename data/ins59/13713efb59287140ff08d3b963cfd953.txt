The latest episode of Imlie begins with Atharva expressing his love for Cheeni. Cheeni on the other hand, questions him if he really loves her and wants to get married to her. Atharva agrees. Chinni responds by saying that she has feelings for him as well but cannot do anything about it since he is supposed to get married to her sister Imlie. Atharva gets surprised as years that Cheeni has feelings for him as well. He asks her to support him and they will together lead a happy life.As episode continues, Atharva and Chinni decide to tell Imlie about their feelings.
