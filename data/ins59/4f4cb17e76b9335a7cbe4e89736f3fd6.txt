'The starting question is what do you want to do with the rest of your life,' says retirement expert Liz Koh. After decades as a financial adviser, Koh ushered in her own retirement from the profession by creating an online education business offering courses for retirees on how to manage their finances. But Enrich Retirement is not just about making a retirement nest egg last for the rest of retirees' lives, it's training to live the best retirement possible. Which is why the experts in the subscription education service includes health experts, yoga experts and even a sex therapist.
