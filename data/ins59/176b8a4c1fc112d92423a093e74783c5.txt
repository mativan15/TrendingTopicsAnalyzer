SC status to converted community members will open 'floodgates' for religious conversions: VHP
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 18:30:00

The VHP will soon launch a nationwide public awareness campaign as demands for giving scheduled caste status to converted members of the community has once again gained momentum, he said.Giving scheduled caste status to converted members of the community will open "floodgates" for religious conversions in the country, the Vishva Hindu Parishad (VHP) said on Monday, days after the Centre appointed a commission to examine the issue. The RSS affiliate claimed that demands for giving scheduled caste status to such persons have "once again" gained momentum, and announced that it will soon launch a nationwide campaign against any such move.
