By Reuters Published: 14:35 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 14:35 EDT, 16 October 2022 Golden State Warriors guard Jordan Poole signed a multi-year contract extension on Sunday. The Warriors did not disclose terms of the deal, but Poole's representation, CAA Sports, told ESPN on Saturday that the sides were finalizing a four-year, $140 million contract extension. "We're thrilled to have Jordan in the fold for the next several years," Warriors executive vice president/general manager Bob Myers said.
