As humans, we like to find patterns in events, and thus try to forecast what will come. But allure of prognostications can lead us astray as investors. Jason Lina, founder of Golden Bell Financial Planning in Alpharetta, Ga., tells us just how dangerous this is, and why prediction have so much power.: Larry Light: Just how powerful are predictions? Jason Lina: Very. As a species, we have a demonstrated behavioral aversion to ambiguity and need for control. Resultantly, it is in our nature to look for patterns in rock-paper-scissors or other aspects of life.
