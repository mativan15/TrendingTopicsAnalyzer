HONEYWELL FORECAST SHOWS STRONG GROWTH FOR BUSINESS AVIATION AS PURCHASE PLANS INCREASE SHARPLY

31st annual Global Business Aviation Outlook projects 8,500 new business jet deliveries valued at $274 billion over the next decade.
Projected business jet deliveries, expenditures over the next decade surge 15% from last year's survey. 
Business jet usage in 2022 is expected to be 9% higher than a year ago. 
Operators report far greater interest in reducing their carbon footprints. 
ORLANDO, Fla., Oct. 16, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Honeywell's (NASDAQ: HON) 31st annual Global Business Aviation Outlook forecasts up to 8,500 new business jet deliveries worth $274 billion from 2023 to 2032, which is up 15% in both deliveries and expenditures from the same 10-year forecast a year ago.
