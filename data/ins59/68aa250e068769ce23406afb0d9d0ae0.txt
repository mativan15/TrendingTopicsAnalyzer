Some 8.9 million Canadians received the Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) during the pandemic, and thousands of them are now finding out they didn't qualify for some or all of it. Now, of course, they have to pay it back, with the Canada Revenue Agency sending repayment notices to those it has determined either weren't eligible at all or received too much.If you can park how annoyed you are to find out you have to pay the money back, you really have no choice but to try to be strategic about how you're going to do it, especially if you really, truly can't afford to do so.
