Rancho Cucamonga's newest boutique IV hydration business, Drip Deluxe is re-envisioning Southern California's wellness industry. RANCHO CUCAMONGA, Calif. (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Drip Deluxe ®, an intravenous (IV) hydration and Vitamin therapy business announces the opening of its first luxury wellness studio in Rancho Cucamonga. Becoming the first boutique IV drip studio to open in the area, Drip Deluxe will offer an optimal self-care and wellness experience in the comfort and privacy of their new luxurious suite – located at Aspire Salon Studios.
