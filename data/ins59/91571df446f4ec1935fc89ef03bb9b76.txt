Outcry at man sculpting feminist icon Elsie Inglis
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

For years female activists have campaigned to correct the gender imbalance of statues on Edinburgh's Royal Mile by immortalising the feminist pioneer Elsie Inglis in bronze.However, the decision to commission a renowned male sculptor for the task has provoked a nepotism row amid claims the appointment is yet another example of 'jobs for the boys'.Alexander Stoddart, the King's sculptor in ordinary in Scotland, was chosen on Monday by a charity after a design competition was unexpectedly cancelled.
