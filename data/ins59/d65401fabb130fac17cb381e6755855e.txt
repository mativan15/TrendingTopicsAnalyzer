During a May inspection of a production and manufacturing facility belonging to Best Daze, compliance officers with the Cannabis Control Division discovered 87 violations out of 103 observable benchmarks.

Throughout the three-hour inspection, officers noted open electrical boxes, personal drinks and food items near cannabis products, a prescription bottle in a processing area, toxic cleaning chemicals stored near cannabis products and food in a staff breakroom. Also documented: A box labeled "mold.
