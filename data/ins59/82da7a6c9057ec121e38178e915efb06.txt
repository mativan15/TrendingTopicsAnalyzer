What's the most pressing issue facing your organization?

Employee mental health. That's one of the most common responses the Washington Business Journal gets from local executives to that question.

It's no surprise leadership is worried, given the country's ongoing mental health crisis, relentlessly fueled by the pandemic and social uncertainties of the past two years. And while the pandemic's grip may be loosening on society, businesses must still contend with the steep toll it's taken on the workforce.
