HSINCHU, May 27, 2022 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- ChipMOS TECHNOLOGIES INC. ("ChipMOS" or the "Company") (Taiwan Stock Exchange: 8150 and NASDAQ: IMOS), an industry leading provider of outsourced semiconductor assembly and test services ("OSAT"), announced its shareholders have approved a cash dividend distribution of NT$4.30 per common share or approximately US$2.92 per ADS at the Company's AGM on May 26, 2022. The dividend, which the Company's Board earlier resolved on February 24, 2022, will be distributed from earnings.
