PONTINS released themed adults-only breaks from just £58.50pp - working out at £29pp a night.
They're a great choice for those looking to get away without the kids, couples or mates just wanting a fun getaway.
Escape to the seaside on an adults-only Pontins breakCredit: Alamy
The themed breaks will take place across the parks, including Brean Sands in Somerset, Southport, Camber Sands in Sussex and Prestatyn Sands in North Wales.

Sand Bay is right on the coast in North Somerset and is a great base from which to explore the seaside resort of Weston-super-Mare and the village of Kewstoke.
