TVS Motor strengthens product portfolio in Kenya
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 12:00:00

Two and three-wheeler manufacturer on Friday said it has unveiled limited edition of its TVS HLX125 Gold and TVS HLX150 Gold in Kenyan market, commemorating global sales of TVS HLX series two million unit milestone. HLX series, launched in 2013 and has been highly reliable product across rugged terrains. The motorcycle has provided last mile connectivity for commercial taxis and delivery segments in rural and urban regions across Africa, the Middle East and Latin American markets (LATAM), a company statement here said.
