Search for best prices lifts takings at stores
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 23:01:00

Britons are shopping around for the best prices amid the cost of living crisis according to findings from NielsenIQ, the data firm.With household budgets increasingly squeezed, visits to stores were up 7 per cent on a year ago in the four weeks ending May 21.The trend drove a small rise in takings at the tills, which were up 0.6 per cent over the same time period, the survey found.
