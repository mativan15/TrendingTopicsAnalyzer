Published: 10:42 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 10:42 EDT, 31 May 2022 It's launching on to screens on Wednesday - yet Bravo TV's The Real Housewives of Dubai has already caused a stir online among residents of the emirate long known as a playground for the rich.Much like the Real Housewives series filmed in Beverly Hills, Atlanta and New Jersey, the show will follow the lives of six affluent women in Dubai, offering an inside look into their glamourous - and often dramatic - world.A three-minute trailer revealed that themes like sex, money and fashion will continue on in the series' new iteration in Dubai, where foreigners make up the vast majority of the approximately three-million strong population.
