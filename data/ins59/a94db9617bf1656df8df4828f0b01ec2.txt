Indian Railways runs special trains to clear festive rush

2022-10-18 10:59:12

The Indian Railways is running 2561 trips of special trains till Chhath Puja this year. These trips will be made by 211 pairs of trains. This is being done in light of the on-going festive season, for the convenience of rail travellers and to clear extra rush of passengers. An official statement said that special trains have been planned to connect major destinations across the country on railway routes, like Darbhanga, Azamgarh, Saharsa, Bhagalpur, Muzaffarpur, Firozpur, Patna, Katihar, and Amritsar, among others.
