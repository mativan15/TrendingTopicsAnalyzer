Introducing the TotalRT All in One Solution to Quickly Adapt CIVCO RT's Radiotherapy Solutions
CORALVILLE, Iowa, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- CIVCO Radiotherapy, the leading global provider of high quality, innovative, patient-centric radiotherapy solutions, will feature the TotalRT All in One Solution along with several innovative solutions in booth #3766 at the ASTRO Annual Meeting in San Antonio, Texas, October 23-25, 2022.
The TotalRT Platform is designed to quickly adapt CIVCO RT's positioning and immobilization solutions to radiotherapy clinical setup and treatment needs, providing an all in one solution.
