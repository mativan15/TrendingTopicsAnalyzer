Parkdean Resorts launched a new sale with savings of up to £150 on all 2023 holidays. 
Deals start from £189 for week-long breaks, meaning groups of six can bag a staycation from just £4.50 per person a night.

Parkdean Resorts has more than 60 parks across the UK, including in popular destinations like Cornwall, Norfolk, Scotland, Yorkshire and Devon.
On top of the many location options, the parks offer everything from glamping to caravans, apartments and lodges, so there's something for everyone.
