(Bloomberg) -- Chicago won its first upgrade from Fitch Ratings Inc. in more than a decade, a sign that the Windy City is finally starting to turn its beleaguered finances around.Fitch boosted Chicago's debt ratings to two notches above junk on Friday, citing the city's improved financial position and increased payments into its pensions.
