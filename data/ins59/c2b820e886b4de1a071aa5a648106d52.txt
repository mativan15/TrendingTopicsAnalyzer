By Liu Yukun | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-19 16:07 Share - WeChat Zhuang Zhaolin, head of the publicity department of the Jiangxi Committee of the Communist Party of China, speaks during a news conference, Oct 18, 2022. [Photo/Xinhua] Innovation and opening-up played an important role in boosting economic development in East China's Jiangxi province, said an official on Tuesday. Zhuang Zhaolin, head of the publicity department of the Jiangxi Committee of the Communist Party of China, said during a news conference at the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China that the province has been promoting the development of digital economy, setting up funds to support entrepreneurship and cultivating talent, and accelerating the construction of inland open economy pilot zones.
