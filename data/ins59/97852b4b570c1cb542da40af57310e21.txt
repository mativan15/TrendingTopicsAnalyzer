16 October 2022 by Emilio Mayorga 
ScreenDaily Quentin Dupieux awarded screenplay prize ex-aequo with himself for Smoking Causes Coughing and Incredible But True.Finnish production Sisu directed by Jalmari Helander took the main award at the 55th edition of Sitges, marking the director's second time winning the Catalonian genre event's best feature award after his 2010 selection Rare Exports: A Christmas Tale.Helander's third feature also earned best actor for Jorma Tommila, cinematography for Kjell Lagerroos, and music for Juri Seppä and Tuomas Wäinölä.
