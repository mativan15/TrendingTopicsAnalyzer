Did You Know Director Pan Nalin Had To Sell His Mumbai House To Make 'Chhello Show'?
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 20:27:14

The official Indian entry for the Oscars in 2023, "Chhello Show," premiered on October 14 in Indian theatres. Pan Nalin's directorial debut is the first Gujarati film to be chosen for the Tribeca Film Festival showcase. The movie was shown at the Valladolid International Film Festival in Spain, where it won the Golden Spike for Best Film as the festival's top honor.  The team worked extremely hard to complete the film. webscreengrabIn an interview with India Today, Pan Nalin confessed that he had to sell his house to fund the movie.
