7 Mistakes to Avoid When Hiring IT Talent
chron.com
news
2022-05-31 17:00:47

Currently, the most significant success barrier most sectors face is a stifling lack of IT talent. According to Gartner, a lack of qualified team members directly halted the adoption of 64% of new technologies.With the continuing digital shift in the workforce, competent IT specialists are in higher demand than ever, yet there is an ever-widening skills gap. Korn Ferry found that, by 2030, the U.S. could lose out on at least $162 billion in revenue per year if nothing changes. This means recruiters need to rethink how they scout top talent.
