October 18, 2022 04:37 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--IFF (NYSE: IFF) today announced that Ralf Finzel has been named executive vice president and global operations officer, effective Nov. 1, 2022. He succeeds Francisco Fortanet, who will be leaving the Company following a period of transition. With this appointment, Finzel becomes a member of IFF's Executive Committee and will be based in the Company's New York City headquarters.



Finzel is an award-winning international operations executive with more than three decades leading operations and integrated supply chain management for multinational companies.
