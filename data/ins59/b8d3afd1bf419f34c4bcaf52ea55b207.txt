A justice for some? After Roe overturned, state Supreme Court races turn political
nbcnews.com
news
2022-10-22 11:00:00

In the run-up to Election Day, what should be a nonpartisan race for judge on Kentucky's highest court has become a high-stakes showdown injected with political rhetoric and imagery.Joseph Fischer, a candidate in the 6th Supreme Court District, appears in a recent campaign ad telling voters he's 'committed to defending the rule of law, not radical activist politics,' as photos flash of his opponent, incumbent Justice Michelle Keller, as well as U.S. Supreme Court Justice Elena Kagan and retired Justice Stephen Breyer.
