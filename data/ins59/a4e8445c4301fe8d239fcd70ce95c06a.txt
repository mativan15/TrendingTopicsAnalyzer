State Street Corp. now says its long-delayed purchase of Brown Brothers Harriman and Co.'s investor services business is "increasingly uncertain" due to questions around its regulatory approval.

The Boston bank's latest filing with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission says closure of the deal, already delayed months longer than planned, has been slowed by regulators.
