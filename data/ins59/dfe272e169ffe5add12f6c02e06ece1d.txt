GLENDALE, Calif., Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- LegalZoom.com, Inc. (Nasdaq: LZ), will report its financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2022 on Thursday, November 10, 2022, following the close of market.Dan Wernikoff, Chief Executive Officer, and Noel Watson, Chief Financial Officer, will host a conference call and webcast at 4:30 p.m. EST on Thursday, November 10, 2022 to discuss the company's financial results.
