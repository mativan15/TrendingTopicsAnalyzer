By Reuters Published: 00:58 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 00:58 EDT, 31 May 2022 WELLINGTON, May 31 (Reuters) - Richie Mo'unga is likely to leave New Zealand to play rugby in Japan after next year's World Cup in France, the All Blacks flyhalf told the New Zealand Herald on Tuesday. The 28-year-old, who vies with twice World Player of the Year Beauden Barrett for the black number 10 shirt, signed a one-year contract extension with the Canterbury Crusaders and New Zealand Rugby last week. "I want to experience another culture with my family," the playmaker told the newspaper.
