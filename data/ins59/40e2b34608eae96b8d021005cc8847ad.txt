Revlon in Talks With Lenders Ahead of Debt Deadlines

Photo: Richard B. Levine/Zuma Press Cosmetics maker Revlon Inc. has begun talks with lenders ahead of looming maturities of debt to try to steer the business clear of bankruptcy, according to people familiar with the matter. A group of lenders including Angelo Gordon, Glendon Capital Management LP and King Street Capital Management are in restructuring discussions with Revlon about pushing out the due date on roughly $1.7 billion in debt that matures as early as 2024, people familiar with the matter said.
