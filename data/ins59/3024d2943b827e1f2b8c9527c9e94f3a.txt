Kakao, Naver suffer service outage due to fire at data center
naver.com
news
2022-10-15 21:12:00

A passenger walks at the office of Kakao Bank. [NEWS1] Two major tech giants — Kakao and Naver — suffered a service malfunction on Saturday due to a fire at a data center, the company officials said. Kakao's major services inlcuding instant messenger Kakao Talk, ride-hailing app Kakao T and Kakao Bank all went down. Kakao said around 7 p.m. that the services could resume in two hours. The blaze broke out at around 3:33 p.m. at the building of SK C&C located in Pangyo, just south of Seoul, they said.
