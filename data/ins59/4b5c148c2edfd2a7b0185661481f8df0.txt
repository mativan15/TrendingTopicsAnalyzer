Petroneft Resources Appoints Pavel Tetyakov as CEO, David Sturt as Chairman
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 08:30:00

By Michael Susin Petroneft Resources PLC said Friday that it has appointed Executive Director and Senior Vice President of New Business Pavel Tetyakov as chief executive with immediate effect. The Russia-focused oil-and-gas company also said that former Chief Executive David Sturt will assume the role of chairman. The company added that the decision follows the resignation of Non-executive Chairman Alastair McBain with immediate effect. "Through these challenging times the board has decided to provide continuity to the management of the company," Petroneft Resources said.
