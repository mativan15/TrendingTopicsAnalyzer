Published: 07:59 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 08:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 Liverpool left-back Kostas Tsimikas is keen to feed 'goal machine' Darwin Nunez to increase the striker's output. Nunez could have added to his tally in an impressive 57-minute appearance, having also hit the post, but his header from a left-wing cross is something Tsimikas hopes to be able to replicate regularly.
