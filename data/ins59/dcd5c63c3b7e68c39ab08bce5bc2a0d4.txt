For those old enough to remember, the infamous Cannonball Run was more than just a wacky star-studded Burt Reynolds movie, it was an actual, real-life, coast-to-coast race on public roads started by automotive journalist Brock Yates in the early 1970s. And it is still being run today. Now, four men, three of whom have sons with a rare genetic disorder, are retracing the race route in a thoroughly modern machine: A Tesla Model Y electric car.
