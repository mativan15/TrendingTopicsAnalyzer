Key Takeaways OPEC+ just announced the biggest cut to oil production since the beginning of the pandemic, and this will drive up energy prices during a period when inflation's soaring. The Russian attack on Ukraine has had severe economic impacts on the entire world as energy prices have gone up due to sanctions on Russian oil. The White House has stated the importance of not relying on foreign fuel sources and emphasized the importance of developing clean energy sources. 
 Oil prices have had a turbulent few years thanks to everything that's been happening around the globe.
