Vancouver, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Appnovation, a global digital partner, announced today several appointments amongst the Global Leadership team, as well as a new leader in one of its strategic growth markets. 'This is a really exciting moment in time for Appnovation because the evolution of our Global and Regional Leadership teams signals our forward momentum as a business,' said Arnold Leung, CEO, Appnovation. 'Under these skilled and experienced leaders, Appnovation teams are well positioned to impact and accelerate our clients' digital businesses with creativity, agility and innovation.
