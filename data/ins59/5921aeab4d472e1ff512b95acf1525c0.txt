AgenciesElectrifuel raises Rs 1.8 crore in fundingSeed and early-stage investment firm Indian Angel Network (IAN) has closed its second fund - IAN Alpha Fund - with a corpus of Rs 1,000 crore. This is more than twice the size of its previous fund launched in 2017 with a corpus of Rs 350 crore. Alpha Fund will deploy the capital in sectors including cleantech and environment, health tech, agritech, edtech, fintech, and emerging sectors like web3, space-tech, among others. IAN plans to invest the money over eight years.
