By Li Guoxiang | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-15 08:10 A harvester operates in the highland barley field in Rasog township of Gyangze county, Xigaze, Southwest China's Tibet autonomous region, Sept 19, 2022. [Photo/Xinhua] In 2013, China adopted a national strategy for food security, characterized by self-sufficiency based on domestic grain production, production capacity, moderate imports and technological support. China has also established a strict farmland protection system and adopted a policy to ensure the sustainable use of farmlands, innovative application of agricultural technology, and self-reliance in seeds.
