ETMarkets.comExits, however, make or mar a trader. When to exit is the most important decision you make as a trader.When you buy a share, you are so full of optimism. You picture your stock rallying to new highs and becoming a multi-bagger. So entries are easier decisions to make. Exits, however, are an entirely different ballgame. When you exit a profitable position, you are always worried that the stock could still go up further and you might miss out on all the potential profits by exiting early.
