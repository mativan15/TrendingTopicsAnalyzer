FinTron Secures Series A Funding to Help Expand Opportunities in Connecticut

New funding will help foster innovation and provide jobs and educational opportunities to Connecticut students
STAMFORD, Conn., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- FinTron, a Connecticut-based investment and personal finance application whose mission is to democratize personal finance for the mobile-first generation, today announced the close of its $6.5 million Series A funding round, bringing the company's total funds raised to nearly $10 million. With new funding and the help of key partners, FinTron plans to continue expanding its organization and staff, provide greater financial education and job opportunities to high school and college students, and cultivate new and existing partnerships in Connecticut.
