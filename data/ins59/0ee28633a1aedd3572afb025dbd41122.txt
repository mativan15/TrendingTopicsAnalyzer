Tippie's Erin Johnson wins the Hubbard-Walder Award for Teaching Excellence
uiowa.edu
news
2022-10-17 19:13:50

Associate Professor Erin Johnson has been awarded the 2022 Hubbard-Walder Award at the University of Iowa for her excellence in teaching and contributions to curriculum. The award carries a $1,000 honorarium. Johnson, who teaches within the Department of Management and Entrepreneurship at the Tippie College of Business, is a versatile instructor who teaches in both the undergraduate and MBA programs, in-person and online, and in multiple content areas. She is an innovator who is dedicated to providing students hands-on experiences that extend far beyond traditional classroom teaching.
