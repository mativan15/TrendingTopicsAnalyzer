Silk Road Medical prices 2.33M common shares offering at $43.00/share
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 06:00:09

Silk Road Medical (NASDAQ:SILK) prices an underwritten public offering of 2,325,582 shares of its common stock at a public offering price of $43.00/share. Underwriters are granted a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 348,837 shares of common stock at the public offering price. Gross proceeds from the offering to Silk Road Medical are expected to be ~$100.0M. Net proceeds from the offering will be used to expand its sales force and operations, increase its research and development activities, conduct or sponsor clinical studies and trials, lease new facilities, expand internationally, and to provide for working capital and other general corporate purposes.
