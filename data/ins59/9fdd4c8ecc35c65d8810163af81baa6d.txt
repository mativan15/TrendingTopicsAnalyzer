Sportsman's Warehouse: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
sfgate.com
business
2022-05-31 20:14:36

WEST JORDAN, Utah (AP) _ Sportsman's Warehouse Holdings Inc. (SPWH) on Tuesday reported profit of $2 million in its fiscal first quarter.On a per-share basis, the West Jordan, Utah-based company said it had net income of 5 cents. The outdoor sporting goods specialty retailer posted revenue of $309.5 million in the period. For the current quarter ending in August, Sportsman's Warehouse expects its per-share earnings to range from 22 cents to 30 cents. The company said it expects revenue in the range of $330 million to $350 million for the fiscal second quarter.
