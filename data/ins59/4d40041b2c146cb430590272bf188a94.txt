SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Financial Venture Studio, the country's premier seed-stage fintech venture capital firm, today announced the six companies that will be making up their Fall 2022 cohort. This comes alongside the news that Financial Venture Studio is officially being renamed to Restive Ventures. Restive represents the attitude of the founders that we seek to back: entrepreneurs who will not be still, who relentlessly agitate, iterate, and build. Restive is committed to financially and operationally supporting these founders and visionaries who want to get something done, and won't rest until they do.
