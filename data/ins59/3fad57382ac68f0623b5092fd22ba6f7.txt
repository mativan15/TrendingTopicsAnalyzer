Winners of the previous editions of ET MSME Awards 2022 – which builds on its previous avatar as ET Rise Top MSME Rankings that saw participation from over 18,000 businesses – have emphasized the benefits they've seen from receiving this recognition from ET Digital, including the fact that the programme enabled them to expand their presence in both domestic and international markets as it lent their businesses much credibility and standing, while also serving as strong validation and morale booster for the team.
