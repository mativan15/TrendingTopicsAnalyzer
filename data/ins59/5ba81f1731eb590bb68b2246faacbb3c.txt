Need seen for developing Ballari jeans manufacturing cluster in a holistic way
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-16 17:07:17

Congress leader Rahul Gandhi has stressed the need for developing jeans and ready-made garment cluster in Ballari in a holistic way with proper banking and financial assistance, infrastructure, training and other policy initiatives.'I am awed by the brilliance of the garment workers of Ballari and their enterprising zeal. Considering the historical connections of garment production in the city, I feel that there is a dire need for a marketing strategy to popularise Ballari jeans and garment products.
