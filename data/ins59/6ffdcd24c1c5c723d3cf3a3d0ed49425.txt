Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati Showcases Innovations At Research And Development Fair At IIT Delhi

Guwahati: The Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati (IITG) has showcased its research and development projects and innovations at the ongoing IITs Research and Development (R and D) fair in New Delhi. IIT Delhi is hosting the first of its kind R and D fair 'IInven Tiv' to bring together key stakeholders from the industry, government institutions and academia to collaborate, learn, exchange ideas and innovate.
