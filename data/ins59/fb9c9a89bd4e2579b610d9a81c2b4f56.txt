Bengaluru: ED searches 5 premises in Chinese loan app case
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 06:30:00

BENGALURU: Continuing its investigation into the Chinese loan app case, the Directorate of Enforcement (ED) on Friday said it carried out search operations under the provisions of Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) at five places in Bengaluru.The searches, conducted on Wednesday, were on the premises of payment gateway Razorpay Pvt Ltd and its compliance offices related to these entities.During the search operation, ED seized Rs 78 crore in merchant IDs and bank accounts of these Chinese-controlled entities.
