There was a time when Londoners lured by the sea would head off to Brighton or perhaps Southend. The Kent coast, while lovely, was just that bit too far for a daily commute.But WFH means Kent's sandy beaches, grammar schools and lovely towns are drawing a new generation of Londoners willing to live farther from the office.Another home county packed with commuter-friendly locations is Hampshire, which has everything from charming cities to rural villages, although its hotspots tend not to be shoestring budget options.
