Controversy over crypto continues as some call for countermeasures
naver.com
news
2022-06-02 17:51:00

The crash of Luna in May has reignited the controversy over the worth of cryptocurrencies and the countermeasures aimed to prevent another such meltdown that is being described as a Ponzi scheme. International Monetary Fund Managing Director Kristalina Georgieva compared some cryptocurrencies to pyramid schemes at the World Economic Forum meeting in Davos, Switzerland, in May, while European Central Bank President Christine Lagarde said cryptocurrencies are 'worth nothing' on a Dutch television program in the same month.
