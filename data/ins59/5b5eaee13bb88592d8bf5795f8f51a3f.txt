MySale takeover is on after Frasers builds shareholding
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

Frasers Group is set to go ahead with a full takeover of MySale, the Australian retailer specialising in 'flash sales' of out-of-season stock, after securing more than 50 per cent of its shares.The British company, whose high street brands include Sports Direct, House of Fraser and Evans Cycles, has been building a stake in the ecommerce business since the summer as part of a strategy to increase its international reach.Frasers, which is almost 70 per cent-owned by Mike Ashley, the retail tycoon, said yesterday that it either held or had received acceptances for 50.
