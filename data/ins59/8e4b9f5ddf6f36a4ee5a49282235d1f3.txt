BNP Paribas & Nippon India MF buy stakes in Electronics Mart India

ETMarkets.comShares of the speciality retail company were bought by Nippon India Mutual fundElectronics Mart, which was listed on the bourses today with over 50 per cent premium, saw multiple bulk deals as per NSE bulk deals data. BNP Paribas Arbitrage took positions in the stock and bought 33,31,32 shares at an average traded price of Rs 88.58. In another deal, it sold 9,340 shares in the counter for Rs 85.94. After logging handsome listing gains in trade today, the stock of Electronics Mart ended with gains of over 43% at Rs 84.
