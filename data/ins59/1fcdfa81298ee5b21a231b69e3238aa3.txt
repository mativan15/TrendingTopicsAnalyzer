ATLANTA, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- American Virtual Cloud Technologies, Inc. (the 'Company') (Nasdaq: AVCT), today announced that it has entered into definitive agreements with institutional investors for the purchase and sale of 5,000,000 shares of its common stock (the 'Shares'), pursuant to a registered direct offering priced at-the-market under Nasdaq rules (the 'Offering'), and warrants to purchase up to 10,000,000 Shares (the 'Warrants'), in a concurrent private placement (the 'Private Placement').
