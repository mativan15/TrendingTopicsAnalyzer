Sydney-Based Global Fund Management Firm to Benefit from SaaS-Based Portfolio Reporting and Analytics Platform and Services
BOISE, Idaho, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Clearwater Analytics (NYSE: CWAN), an industry-leading SaaS provider, announced today that Sydney-based, global long/short fund manager Bronte Capital has selected Clearwater to power its investment data management, reporting, and portfolio analytics. Founded in 2009, Bronte Capital focuses on generating alpha from both its long and short portfolio, with its investments consisting principally of listed equity and equity-related securities.
