There is no doubt that AI is progressing at an exponential rate. The question is, how long will it be before machines become as intelligent as humans? Some experts say it could be as soon as next year, while others believe it could take longer. But regardless of when it happens, the impact on society will be huge.The most immediate impact will be job losses. Millions of jobs will be destroyed as machines take over. For example, Current text-to-image AI is almost as good as professional painters, at a fraction of the cost.
