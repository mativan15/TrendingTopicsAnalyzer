What is Regulation Z?
cnet.com
news
2022-10-19 16:00:41

Regulation Z, better known as the Truth in Lending Act, is a federal law designed to ensure that you, the borrower, have access to clear information about the fees you will pay when taking out a loan. Can your interest rate go up? Will you be penalized if you pay off your loan early? What is the annual percentage rate associated with your loan? These are just a few questions you'll have the answers to when you apply for a loan, thanks to Regulation Z.Regulation Z is a set of legal requirements that lenders must follow when offering to consumers such financial products as mortgages, credit cards, car loans and home equity loans.
