The latest series of I'm A Celebrity…Get Me Out Of Here! will be back on our screens in a matter of weeks.
The hit reality show will finally be returning to Australia for the first time in three years after the series was forced to set up a new camp at Gwrych Castle in Wales due to the pandemic.
ITV has confirmed that the hit show will start in November, but an exact date is yet to be announced. 
Though the cast has not yet been confirmed, rumours have been swirling as to who will be appearing in the latest ITV series.
