Golden Globes: HFPA Announces There Will Be No Press Conferences Tied to Awards Show
hollywoodreporter.com
news
2022-10-22 02:12:32

The Hollywood Foreign Press Association, the organization behind the Golden Globes, will not have any press conferences this awards season. 'The HFPA will not be hosting any press conferences during the current awards cycle,' HFPA president Helen Hoehne said in a statement. 'Our commitment remains on recognizing the very best in film and television both in the U.S. and abroad and bringing the Golden Globes to a global television and streaming audience.' She continued, 'With the expansion of the voting body and the increase in eligible content this year, we are focused on the nominations and making the 80th a historic event to remember.
