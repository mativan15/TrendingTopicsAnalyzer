October 19, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time BOCA RATON, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--MiX Telematics Limited (JSE: MIX) (NYSE: MIXT) ('MiX Telematics' or the 'Company'), a leading global SaaS provider of connected fleet management solutions, will hold a conference call on Thursday, October 27, 2022, at 8:00 a.m. Eastern time and 2:00 p.m. South African time to discuss its financial results for the second quarter of fiscal 2023, which ended September 30, 2022. The results will be reported in a press release prior to the conference call.
