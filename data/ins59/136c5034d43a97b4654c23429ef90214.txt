Resentment runs high against sale of liquor at J&K departmental stores
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-15 21:00:31

Condemnations are escalating against the government's decision of allowing the sale of alcoholic drinks like beer in departmental stores of Jammu and Kashmir.People's Democratic Party (PDP) staged a protest outside the party office in Srinagar and also in Jammu district against the order while Srinagar Municipal Corporation (SMC) mayor Junaid Mattu also aired his resentment against the decision.A group of PDP leaders and activists raised slogans against the government while demanding rollback of the liquor order in Srinagar.
