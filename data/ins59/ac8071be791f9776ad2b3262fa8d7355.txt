Published: 13:52 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 13:52 EDT, 31 May 2022 Manchester United are reportedly plotting a move for Benfica striker Darwin Nunez during the summer transfer window. Nunez has been one of Europe's breakout stars of this season, scoring an astonishing 34 goals in 40 appearances for Benfica. However, Benfica have attached a £68million price tag to the 22-year-old striker ahead of the summer window.  Man United are reportedly plotting a move for Benfica striker Darwin Nunez this summer According to TalkSPORT, Nunez is said to excited about the prospect of playing under the club's new United manager Erik ten Hag.
