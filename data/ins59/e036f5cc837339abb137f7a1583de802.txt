The world's most dangerous countries to visit have been revealed in the annual Global Risk Assessment report.Compiled by Global Guardian, a duty of care firm specialising in travel risk, the countries are marked on an interactive map showing risk ratings and geopolitical conflict zones of countries all over the globe.The map highlights areas of geopolitical tension and evaluates the prospects of escalation and their possible impacts, classifying countries under 'extreme', 'high', 'medium', 'moderate' and 'low'.
