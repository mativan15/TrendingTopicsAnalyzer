da-kuk What Is The NFT Gaming Company? Roseland, New Jersey-based The NFT Gaming Company (NFTG) was founded to develop proprietary games and related NFT (Non-Fungible Token) technologies to provide unique experiences to game players worldwide. Management is headed by Chairman and CEO, Vadim Mats, who has been with the firm since 2022 and was previously CFO of DatChat (DATS) and prior to that CFO of Grand Private Equity, a fintech-centric family office. The company's primary offering under development is Gaxos, which will seek to bring together gamers, publishers and developers on a platform with the opportunity to earn rewards and 'participate in other opportunities.
