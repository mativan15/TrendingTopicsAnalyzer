Au Small Fin Bank shares fall 5.44% as Nifty drops
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 09:00:00

AgenciesMazhar Mohammad of Chartviewindia.in said technical indicators are turning mixed as the weekly MACD chart has generated a 'buy' signal, giving an edge to the bulls.Shares of Au Small Finance Bank Ltd. traded 5.44 per cent down at Rs 592.35 on Thursday at around 02:05PM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex declined 136.0 points to 58971.19. As many as 159,477 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 9.56 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 732.9 and a 52-week low of Rs 467.
