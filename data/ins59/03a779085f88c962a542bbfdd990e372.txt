May 31, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Three small businesses have been awarded $20,000 each as part of Intuit QuickBooks and Mailchimp's nationwide Small Business Hero Day contest. The inaugural celebration of this recognition day on May 31 caps off Small Business Success Month by Intuit (NASDAQ: INTU), the global technology platform that makes QuickBooks, TurboTax, Mint, Credit Karma, and Mailchimp.



'The stories of these small businesses and the hundreds more who were nominated are truly inspiring and showcase the best of what small businesses are all about - serving their communities,' said Alex Chriss, EVP and GM, Small Business & Self Employed Group at Intuit.
