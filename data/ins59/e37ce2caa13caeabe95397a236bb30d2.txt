Is ‘Top Gun: Maverick' Completely Electrifying or Totally Embarrassing?
vogue.com
news
2022-05-27 12:25:40

Nostalgia! The culture is drowning in it. And today here comes a tidal wave: Top Gun: Maverick. This movie, which I practically sprinted to go see, is a paradox: shamelessly embarrassing and deliriously exciting, as devoid of new ideas as it is expert at delivering on expectations. My verdict was a fist pump—Maverick's flight scenes and dogfight sequences are undeniable, and the final third is the most fun I've had in a theater since…well, since Mission Impossible: Fallout (and more on that in a minute).
