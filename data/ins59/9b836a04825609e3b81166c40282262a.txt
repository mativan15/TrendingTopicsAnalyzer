U.S. agency asks Tesla for information on Canadian fire incident
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-05-27 12:05:32

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration said on Thursday it has asked Tesla Inc. for information about a recent 2021 Tesla Model U fire in Vancouver, British Columbia, in which a driver reported struggling to exit.The NHTSA told Reuters the agency 'is aware of the incident and has reached out to the manufacturer for information.'Electrek posted a video of the incident in which the owner said he received an error notification and then saw smoke. The driver said that to get out he 'had to smash the window.
