IRVINE, Calif., Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- via InvestorWire — IBN ('InvestorBrandNetwork'), an innovative corporate communications firm and content distributor, is pleased to announce that its InvestorWire ('IW') brand has been engaged by Octane as an Official NewsWire for the upcoming Medical Innovation Forum. IBN will also serve as an Official Media Partner for the event. Slated to be held in a hybrid format on October 27-28, 2022, attendees will be able to participate virtually as well as on-site at the Irvine Marriott, 18000 Von Karman Ave.
