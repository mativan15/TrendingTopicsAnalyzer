To get a sense of who is truly in control of SJW Group (NYSE:SJW), it is important to understand the ownership structure of the business. The group holding the most number of shares in the company, around 73% to be precise, is institutions. In other words, the group stands to gain the most (or lose the most) from their investment into the company.

After a year of 5.3% losses, last week's 4.7% gain would be welcomed by institutional investors as a likely sign that returns might start trending higher.
