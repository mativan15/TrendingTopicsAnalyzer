ByteBrew has raised $4 million for its all-in-one mobile game analytics platform that provides developers with tools they need to grow their games business. It may come as no surprise that former game developers created this game analytics platform. What's pretty amazing about it is that it was built by a team of just two brothers in San Diego, California.
