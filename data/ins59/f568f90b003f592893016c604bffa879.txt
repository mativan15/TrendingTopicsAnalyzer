Musk feels 'super bad' about economy, needs to cut 10% of Tesla jobs
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 19:00:00

Tesla CEO Elon MuskTesla CEO Elon Musk has a "super bad feeling" about the economy and needs to cut about 10% of jobs at the electric carmaker, he said in an email to executives seen by Reuters.The message, sent on Thursday and titled "pause all hiring worldwide", came two days after the billionaire told staff to return to the workplace or leave, and adds to a growing chorus of warnings from business leaders about the risks of recession.Tesla shares fell 9% in U.S. trade on Friday after the Reuters report.
