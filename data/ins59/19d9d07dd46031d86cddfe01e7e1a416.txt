Some Sask. First Nations say they're not being fairly consulted by province
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-19 13:18:00

Some Saskatchewan First Nations feel they are not being adequately consulted by the provincial government on matters affecting them. The province has a consultation policy in place but some Chiefs think it should be written into law. Whether it's the sale of crown land or the recent government constitutional White Paper, some Saskatchewan First Nations don't think they're being adequately consulted on matters that could impact them. 'If status quo continues, and all the land that we had relied on, our ancestors had relied on will be all gone.
