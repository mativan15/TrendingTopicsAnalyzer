More Video Ditas Lopez, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- Philippine police arrested Japanese gaming tycoon Kazuo Okada on Monday for coercion cases related to his group's takeover of a Manila casino.Universal Entertainment Corp., in a statement on Tuesday, said Okada was arrested at the Manila airport after he landed from Japan. A warrant of arrest has been issued against Okada and his group for alleged grave coercion, following their takeover of the Okada Manila resort on May 31.
