This article Is a Critical analysis Of The telecommunication Bill – 2022 .
The Department of Telecommunications published a Draft discussion document in July of this year and invited feedback on it before publishing the draught into the bill .. ie  Telecommunication Bill, 2022. The Indian Telegraph Act of 1885, The Wireless Telegraphy Act of 1933, and the Telegraph Wires (Unlawful Possession) Act of 1950 are just a few instances of the old telecommunications laws that would be repealed by The Telecommunications Bill-2022, according to the Introductory Note too that same Proposal.
