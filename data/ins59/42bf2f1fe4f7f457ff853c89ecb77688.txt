Middlesex Sheriff Warns Of Scam Targeting Medical Professionals
patch.com
news
2022-10-21 18:23:28

The Middlesex Sheriff's Office is warning residents, specifically medical professionals, about a scam in which the caller impersonates a member of the MSO and demands money. There have been three different reports of the scam, the MSO said. (Shutterstock )BILLERICA, MA — The Middlesex Sheriff's Office (MSO) this week warned residents —specifically medical professionals — about an ongoing scam involving a caller claiming to be an employee of the MSO.According to the MSO, there have been three different reports of the scam, though none has resulted in the loss of any money.
