What is Terra 2.0? Crypto exchanges, tech entrepreneurs divided on supporting new Terra-Luna revival plan
indianexpress.com
business
2022-05-27 08:16:06

Terra coin failed miserably, plunging the investors' money once and for all. However, Do Kwon, CEO and co-founder of Terraform Labs, the company behind Terra and Luna has a ‘grand revival plan' to salvage the Terra ecosystem from its current stage. The new plan is called— ‘Terra 2.0'. However, tech entrepreneurs and cryptocurrency exchanges are divided on supporting the CEO's new plan.
Terra 2.0, is the revamped version of Terra coin. The company CEO believes that validating all invalid blocks as well as transactions on the blockchain network will make the coin boom once again.
