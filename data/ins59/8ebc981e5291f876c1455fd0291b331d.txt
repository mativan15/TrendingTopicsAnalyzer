May 31, 2022 03:53 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Class: Shareholder rights law firm Robbins LLP informs investors that a shareholder filed a class action on behalf of all persons and entities that purchased or otherwise acquired LMP Automotive Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: LMPX) securities between June 29, 2021 and May 19, 2022. The complaint alleges violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. LMP, through its subsidiaries, offers customers the ability to buy, sell, rent and subscribe for, and obtain financing for automobiles online and in person.
