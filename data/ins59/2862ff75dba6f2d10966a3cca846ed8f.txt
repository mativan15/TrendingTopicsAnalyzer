PLEASANTON, Calif., Oct. 15, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- October is National Breast Cancer Awareness Month and Service Champions, a leading Home Services Company specializing in heating, air conditioning, electrical, water, and insulation in Northern California, has announced it will contribute $5 of every HVAC tune-up performed in October to the American Cancer Society. The company aims not only to raise awareness of this disease but to give back to a community that's been affected so much by it.

In addition, Service Champions is sponsoring the Bay Area's Making Strides Against Breast Cancer walk.
