(Photo: Paul Archuleta/FilmMagic)Jaime King's estranged husband, Kyle Newman, claims she's not paying her court-mandated child and spousal support leaving him "in dire straits."In court documents obtained by Yahoo Entertainment, the Fanboys director says his actress and model ex, from whom he split in May 2020, is nine months behind in support. Per their agreement established earlier this year, King is to pay at least $429 in child support and $1,000 in spousal support each month.
