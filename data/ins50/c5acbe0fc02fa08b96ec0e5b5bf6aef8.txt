Politics is a game of relativity. There is no need to be great so long as you are merely mildly better, or modestly more palatable, than your opponents. Those people with firm convictions may make the greatest amount of noise but most voters care more for a quieter life. They resent political intrusions and, most of the time, they would very much like to be left alone. There is a little Greta Garbo in most of us.The Conservative Party has forgotten this. The public has been exposed to more politics than it can swallow.
