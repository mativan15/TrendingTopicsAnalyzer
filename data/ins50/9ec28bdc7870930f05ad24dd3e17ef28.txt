October 17, 2022 01:51 PM Eastern Daylight Time PORTLAND, Ore.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Savage is one of the most recent companies to offer medZERO's innovative benefit to its more than 4,000 Team Members. medZERO is an award-winning, employer-sponsored financial wellness platform that gives employees a smarter way to pay for healthcare via on-demand access to funds to pay their out-of-pocket healthcare expenses with repayment automatically deducted from their payroll over time with zero interest and zero fees.
