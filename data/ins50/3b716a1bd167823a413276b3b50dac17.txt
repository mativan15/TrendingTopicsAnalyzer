Cigna received millions of Medicare dollars based on invalid diagnoses, lawsuit claims
go.com
news
2022-10-17 18:00:47

The lawsuit claims Cigna made recipients seem sicker than they actually were.Health care giant Cigna improperly obtained tens of millions of dollars in Medicare funding by making certain Medicare Part C recipients seem sicker than they actually were, the federal government alleged in a civil lawsuit filed Monday.Cigna submitted false and invalid diagnoses to artificially inflate the payments it would receive for providing insurance coverage to its Medicare Advantage plan members, the suit said.The lawsuit resulted from a whistleblower complaint and seeks damages and penalties.
