Getty Nearly every company in today's technology-driven world is on the verge of automation, as technology has advanced to unprecedented levels of sophistication. Intelligent automation enabled with artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) is playing a critical part in transforming modern workspaces to improve efficiency and productivity. According to a 2019 poll, roughly 74% of respondents said that the deployment of intelligent automation in their enterprises would have a substantial impact on 11% to 50% of their present workforce.
