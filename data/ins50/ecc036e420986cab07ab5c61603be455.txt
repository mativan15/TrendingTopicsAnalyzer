A Thai restaurant has been praised online after introducing a no-refund policy for customers who try to send back spicy dishes, but not everyone is so convinced about the ideaSpicy food can be challenging to eat if you aren't used to it (stock photo) Can you handle spicy food? No, but can you really? All too often foodies like to exaggerate how much they can tolerate, before finding their bravado fails after taking one eye-watering mouthful. And one Thai restaurant has had enough. The eatery has gone viral online after displaying a no refunds notice, telling customers they can't get their money back if they order spicy food but "can't handle it.
