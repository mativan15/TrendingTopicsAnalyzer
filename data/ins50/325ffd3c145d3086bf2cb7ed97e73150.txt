In the days that followed, I wrote a piece for the 10/14 edition of 'The Jerusalem Post' expressing my own experiences with growing antisemitism in the U.S. and abroad. 'This isn't a matter of my Jewish faith,' I wrote. 'It's a fundamental breakdown of our shared humanity.
