XPO Logistics Reports Preliminary 3Q Revenue of $3.04 Billion
wsj.com
news
2022-10-17 20:54:00

Photo: Angel Garcia/Bloomberg News XPO Logistics Inc. on Monday issued preliminary third-quarter results ahead of its scheduled release date. The freight transportation services provider said it expects to report revenue of $3.04 billion, operating income between $181 million to $185 million and adjusted earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization between $348 million to $352 million. The company also issued long-term financial targets ahead of its planned spinoff of its freight brokerage business later this year.
