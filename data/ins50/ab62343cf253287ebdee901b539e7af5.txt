NEW YORK, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Perella Weinberg Partners (Nasdaq: PWP), a leading global independent advisory firm, announced today that R.A. McDonough has joined the Firm as a Partner.Based in Los Angeles, Mr. McDonough will provide strategic and financial advice to clients across North America. He will establish and head the Firm's North American Power & Utilities practice in the Industrials group and work closely with Tudor, Pickering, Holt & Co., the Energy business of PWP, across our traditional and energy transition advisory efforts.
