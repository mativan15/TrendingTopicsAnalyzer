getty For much of the previous decade, a long-anticipated digital transformation of healthcare was slowly taking shape. Globally, healthcare systems were busy building infrastructure to enable doctors to securely share patient histories, access pooled services and use field data to expand knowledge and best practice application. From software that informs clinical decisions to applications that use machine learning to spot or diagnose abnormalities, technology was already starting to make health systems more effective, efficient and sustainable.
