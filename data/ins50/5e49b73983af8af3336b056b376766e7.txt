Quantity - as many new-age tech companies that only think of valuation don't seem to know - has a quality of its own. So, despite the job being wholly hereditary and dynastic in nature, 75 years on the throne for Elizabeth Windsor has to count for as much as, if not more than, an elected citizen's eight years as head of government. There may be no MBGA - Make Britain Great Again - campaign or franchise caps and sweatshirts, but Windsor's platinum jubilee celebrations has pipped past l'affaire Johnny Depp-Amber Heard in the headlines-ticker tape stakes.
