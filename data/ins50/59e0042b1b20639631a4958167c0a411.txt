Concrete levy to be cut to 5% and delayed until September next year
irishtimes.com
news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

The Government is to delay the introduction of the concrete levy, and to halve the rate at which it is imposed, under proposals that went to Cabinet on Tuesday.The levy will now not come in until next September 2023, rather than the initially intended date of April 3rd.The rate at which products are levied is also due to come down to 5 per cent, rather than the 10 per cent indicated on budget day.It is understood the full year yield from the levy is going to be around €30 million, down from €80 million.
