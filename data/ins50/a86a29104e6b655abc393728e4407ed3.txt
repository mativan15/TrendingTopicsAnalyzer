Nation set to advance high-level opening-up
chinadaily.com.cn
news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

By Liu Zhihua | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-18 07:12 China will release the sixth list of major foreign investment projects and further expand the 2022 industry catalog of sectors encouraging foreign investment as part of its latest efforts to advance high-level opening-up, a senior government official said on Monday. Zhao Chenxin, deputy head of the National Development and Reform Commission, said at a news conference that the nation will offer policy support at the central government level to foreign investment projects on the upcoming list, in terms of industrial planning, land use, environmental protection assessment and energy consumption.
