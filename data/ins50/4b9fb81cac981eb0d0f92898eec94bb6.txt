Toronto, Ontario--(Newsfile Corp. - October 19, 2022) - Zonetail Inc. (TSXV: ZONE) (OTCQB: ZTLLF) ("Zonetail" or the "Company") is pleased to announce that it has signed an agreement with Osgoode Properties (Osgoode) to launch its marketplace within Osgoode's existing mobile app across 29 locations, with over 5,000 apartment and townhouse units, in Ottawa, Kingston, Calgary, Edmonton and Gatineau, QB.

Osgoode utilizes Yardi's premium platform and mobile application, RENTCafe, as their tenant portal, allowing residents to communicate and interact with their property manager, create service requests, receive updates on community news among other valuable tools all at the touch of a screen.
