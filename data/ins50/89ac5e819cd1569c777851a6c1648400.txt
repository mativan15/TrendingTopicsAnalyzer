The Cabinet has approved minimum support price ( MSP ) hike for all mandated rabi crops for marketing season 2023-24, union minister Anurag Thakur announced.The MSP for lentil ( Masur ) has seen the highest increase of Rs 500 per quintal. Rapeseed and Mustartd has been increased by Rs 400.MSP is the rate at which the government buys grain from farmers.
