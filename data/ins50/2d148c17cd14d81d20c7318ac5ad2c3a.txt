REUTERS/Luisa Gonzalez/File PhotoBOGOTA, May 27 (Reuters) - Colombians will choose among a leftist promising change, a center-right candidate seen as the natural successor to the incumbent leader, and an eccentric business magnate in a presidential vote on Sunday.Gustavo Petro, the leftist, has consistently led opinion polls, with around 40% of voting intentions.
