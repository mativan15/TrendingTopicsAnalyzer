If you thought your air fryer was at the height of high-tech gadgetry, wait ‘til you see what's cooking in 'smart appliances' in the kitchen – and in the laundry room, too.From app-controlled washers and dryers to voice-enabled ranges and A.I. (artificial intelligence)-powered fridges with cameras and touchscreens, many of today's Wi-Fi appliances benefit from high-tech amenities designed to make home life easier for you and your family.While the exact features may vary between brands and models – and some companies call them 'connected appliances' opposed to 'smart appliances' – here's a look at how these modern devices add convenience, peace of mind and can help you get more done in less time.
