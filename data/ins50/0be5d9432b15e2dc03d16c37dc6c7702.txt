Paid demonstration will provide validation of commercial-scale e-Zinc long-duration energy storage system

TORONTO, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--e-Zinc, the company enabling sustainable, long-duration energy storage with its zinc-air battery, announced it has entered into a collaboration for a pilot project with Toyota Tsusho Canada Inc. (TTCI) to further test and validate how commercial scale e-Zinc energy storage systems can reliably provide long-duration energy storage. This project will validate e-Zinc's capability to store 24 hours of energy, which is approximately 10x that of traditional batteries.
