On October 23, India and Pakistan will face off in the T20 World Cup. Both countries shall be starting their respective campaigns in the tournament. Almost a year earlier, on October 24, 2021, Pakistan had crushed India by 10 wickets in the last edition and also ended a jinx in the process.  Track recordBut when Rohit Sharma's Men in Blue take on Babar Azam's team in less than a week, history will be on India's side. While last year's loss was unbearable, it was the first ever for India. ICCBetween 2007 and 2021, India and Pakistan have met six times.
