Balasubramaniam Ramesh Appointed to George E. Smith Eminent Scholar's Chair

Atlanta, Georgia, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Distinguished University Professor Balasubramaniam (Bala) Ramesh has been appointed to the George E. Smith Eminent Scholar's Chair in Computer Information Systems (CIS) at Georgia State University's J. Mack Robinson College of Business. The Board of Regents of the University System of Georgia approved the appointment on May 18. The George E. Smith Eminent Scholar's Chair was established in 1987 to advance Robinson's global reputation in computer information systems.
