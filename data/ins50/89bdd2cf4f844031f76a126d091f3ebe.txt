Sustainable fashion of late has become a buzzword. While luxury retail brands and homegrown brands are embarking on the bandwagon and welcoming the trend with open arms, it remains a niche market and has failed to find many takers.However, to say that the consumer adoption is entirely bleak- would be wrong. The market for conscious or sustainable fashion is growing steadily. Many top manufacturers are forced to sit back and take a cue from conversations and awareness revolving around eco-friendly clothing.
