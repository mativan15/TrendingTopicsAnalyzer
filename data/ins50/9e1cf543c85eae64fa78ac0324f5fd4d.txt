Restaurant Industry Veteran Opening First DFW Area Mici Italian in Frisco

2022-05-27 17:45:00

FRISCO, Texas (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 Mici Italian, the fast-casual Italian franchise, is gearing up to open the brand's first Texas location in Frisco. The doors will open early July at 8244 FM 423, Suite 400, Frisco, Texas. This exciting news is just the beginning of the Colorado restaurant chain's expansion plans. Carlos Smith, the franchisee who owns the forthcoming Frisco location, has plans to develop about a dozen locations in the Dallas-Fort Worth metropolitan area. He hopes to get the establishments up and running fairly quickly, as he is excited about being the brand's first Texas franchisee.
