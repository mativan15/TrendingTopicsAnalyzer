When one thinks about nightlife in America, one thinks about models and bottles. Exclusivity and status drive the American nightlife scene from hole-in-the-wall dive bars to glitzy, glamorous clubs. Where there is money, there is power. In the nightlife industry, power is usually found in the hands of men. Women fight for its leftovers, if any at all. Women are commonly seen at these events as venue hosts, bottle girls, dancers, or behind-the-scenes staff. Many enjoy the nightlife, but few exist as owners within it.
