State police warn of scam in Emmet County area
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 16:01:41

The Michigan State Police said on Monday they had received reports of a scam in the Emmet County area.

According to a press release from the state police, a family is begging for money at local gas stations. They say they need money for gasoline and to feed their children. They offer fake jewelry in return for cash donations.

A victim in Petoskey gave the family money and later observed them not buying fuel or food. The victim followed them to another gas station and witnessed them conning another person.
