Holt|10.17.22Behaviour Interactive/KonamiAlmost a decade to the day since it released the last Silent Hill game (as long as you don't include P.T.), Konami is finally ready to reveal what's next for the franchise. The publisher announced the showcase in a tweet that includes a link to a placeholder Silent Hill website.
