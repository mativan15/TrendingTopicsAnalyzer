REUTERS/Neil HallLONDON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Shares in ITV (ITV.L) jumped 7% in early deals on Monday, lifted by a Financial Times report that it is considering selling a stake its production arm ITV Studios.Britain's biggest free-to-air commercial broadcaster has recently fielded expressions of interest in ITV Studios, the report said citing people familiar with the discussions.ITV Studios makes programmes such as "Love Island" and "Coronation Street" and is one of the largest producers in Europe.
