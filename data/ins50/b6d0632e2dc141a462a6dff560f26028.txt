Comstock Holding Companies, Inc. signed a new lease with Puttshack​ to bring four nine-hole mini golf courses to Reston Station. (Michael O'Connell/Patch)RESTON, VA — Residents of Reston Station will soon be able to enjoy an upscale, tech-infused mini golf experience while imbibing on global food and drink. On Monday, Comstock Holding Companies, Inc. announced that it had signed a new lease with Puttshack for 1850 Reston Row Plaza in Reston. With a projected summer 2025 opening date, Puttshack Reston will not only anchor Comstock's 80-acre Reston Station development at the Wiehle-Reston East Metro Station, it will be the first live-entertainment venue there.
