Lucy Dreaming is a quintessentially British pixel art point-and-click adventure that is funny, entertaining, and thought-provoking.Lucy Dreaming: Trailer for point and click adventure gamePoint-and-click adventures first emerged in the 1980s, with studios such as LucasArts and Adventure Soft coming out with generation-defining game franchises like Monkey Island and Simon the Sorcerer. Computers and consoles and the height of the point-and-click era were in their infancy, but point-and-click games require very simple engines, as well as minimal graphics (what we now call " pixel art ") to really work effectively.
