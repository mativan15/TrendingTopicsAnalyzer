Student athletes to breathe new life into Highland Games
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 23:00:00

Student athletes are being lined up to halt the steady decline of competitors competing in Highland Games.Gifted cross-country runners, weightlifters and cyclists are being urged to enter disciplines similar to their sport, such as heavy throwing and grass track running.A social media campaign by Scottish Student Sport and the Royal Scottish Highland Games Association aims to engage a new generation of competitors.Ian Grieve, secretary of the association, called the partnership a 'long-term project' and hopes the promotion will see the number of competitors soar in 2023.
