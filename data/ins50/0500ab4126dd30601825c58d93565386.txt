2022 marks the 29th year The Austin Business Journal has published a list of the fastest growing companies in our area. Companies are grouped according to revenue size: Those that earn those that earn more than $10 million and those that earn less than $10 million.
To be eligible, companies must be headquartered in the Austin area and have annual revenue of at least $500,000 in 2019.
Information supplied by each company through a nomination process. Nomination forms can be accessed from the ABJ the website.
