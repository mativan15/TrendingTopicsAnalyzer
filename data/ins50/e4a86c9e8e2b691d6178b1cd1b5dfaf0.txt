If you're watching Wednesday's Game 1 of the ALCS at Minute Maid Park between the New York Yankees and Houston Astros, you may notice something quirky.
The roof in Houston is closed.
The weather forecast is in the low 60s on Wednesday night. So what's the deal here? Is it because it'll be cold?
We don't know much beyond the decision to close it for Game 1 and we'll see about Game 2. We do know that, per Axios, 'The Astros typically close the roof for night games below 65 degrees, according to MLB.
