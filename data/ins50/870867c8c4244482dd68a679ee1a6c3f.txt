By ZHU LIXIN in Hefei | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-22 10:15 Lu Binghui explains the centennial history of the Communist Party of China using model boats during a lecture at Anhui Normal University in Wuhu, Anhui province, last year. [Photo/China Daily] Teaching a course on history, philosophy and politics at Anhui Normal University, Lu Binghui often finds his lectures crowded with students. "During such a 45-minute class, the students would raise so many good questions that I could not answer well due to the time limit," said Lu, a professor who has worked at the university for 30 years.
