Beijing, China--(Newsfile Corp. - June 6, 2022) - China Whale Capital, one of China's largest crypto communities, recently announced a partnership with VCGamers, a social commerce platform that allows its users to connect with other gamers from all over Indonesia, as they look to further spread cryptocurrency adoption across China and Asia. China Whale Capital's mission is to create a free and equal forum for cryptocurrency investments and blockchain technology.
