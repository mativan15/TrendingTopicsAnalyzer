Anyone would assume that turning off your heating means paying less towards bills - but energy experts have warned that it could in fact do the oppositeThe advice sounds counter-intuitive, but it could reduce your energy bills It's an energy debate that plagues households in the winter months - should you turn radiators that aren't being used off to save money? Generally speaking, the answer is yes. If the radiators aren't being used, then switching them off will save you money as you're using less gas.
