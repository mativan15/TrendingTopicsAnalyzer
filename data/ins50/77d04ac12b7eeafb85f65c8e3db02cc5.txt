Published: 19:29 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 19:33 EDT, 1 June 2022 A new trailer has been released for the Netflix action comedy The Man From Toronto, starring Kevin Hart and Woody Harrelson.In the motion picture, Hart portrays a hapless salesman who enters the wrong Airbnb, where he's mistaken for 'The Man From Toronto,' a feared hitman played by Woody Harrelson.'Airbnbs, you never know what you're gonna get,' Hart's character said, before encountering a basement torture chamber where his identity is mistaken as the titular character.
