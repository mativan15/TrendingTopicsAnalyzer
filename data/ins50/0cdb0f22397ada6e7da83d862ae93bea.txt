Indices: Stock market update: Nifty Realty index advances 1.72%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 12:00:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Realty index closed on a positive note on Tuesday.Shares of Phoenix Mills(up 5.55 per cent), Brigade Enterprises(up 2.25 per cent), DLF(up 1.74 per cent), Godrej Properties(up 1.49 per cent) and Oberoi Realty(up 0.72 per cent) ended the day as top gainers in the pack.On the other hand, Indiabulls Real Estate(down 0.34 per cent) and Macrotech Developers(down 0.11 per cent) finished as the top losers of the day.The Nifty Realty index closed 1.72 per cent up at 425.45.Benchmark NSE Nifty50 index ended up 175.
