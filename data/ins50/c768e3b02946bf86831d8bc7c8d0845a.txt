More than half of UK homeowners claim their property is a 'work in progress', study finds
express.co.uk

2022-06-01 08:00:00

Siobhan Murphy has teamed up with Shawbrook Bank (Image: SWNS)The study of 2,000 property owners found almost half (48 percent) don't think their home will ever be ‘finished' – with some of the bigger tasks still in progress ranging from loft conversions to half-done home offices.Some of the top reasons for these jobs being left unfinished include, not having the skills required to complete them (22 percent), underestimating the time it would take to manage the project (20 percent) and under budgeting (16 percent).
