Flag of Mahagathbandhan will fly at Centre: Tej Pratap Yadav

Source: ANI

Bihar Environment Minister Tej Pratap Yadav on October 15 in Patna, expressed confidence in Mahagathbandhan coming into the power at the Centre. The leader also said that the flag of Mahagathbandhan will fly at the Centre. 'Public has rejected BJP government in Bihar. I can predict the future too. Last time, I had predicted about Chachaji (Nitish Kumar), today he's with us. Today, I predict that the flag of Mahagathbandhan will fly at the Centre,' the RJD leader said.
