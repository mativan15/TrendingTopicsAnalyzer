Nebraska Business receives $1M endowment for building enhancements
unl.edu
science
2022-06-02 05:24:00

University of Nebraska‒Lincoln alumni Paul and Mary Ann Koehler have made a $1 million gift to the University of Nebraska Foundation to establish a permanently endowed fund for the College of Business. The endowment will provide annual support to the college to make enhancements at Howard L. Hawks Hall. 'We are grateful to Paul and Mary Ann Koehler for their generous gift that will allow us to continue to enhance our building,' said Kathy Farrell, James Jr. and Susan Stuart Dean of the College of Business.
