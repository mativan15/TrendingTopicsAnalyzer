NGT orders Karnataka government to pay Rs 500 crore for damaging Chandapura Lake

Changing land-use pattern is worrying: Tribunal

BENGALURU: Coming to the rescue of ailing waterbodies in and around Bengaluru, the principal bench of National Green Tribunal ( NGT ) has cracked the whip on the state government, holding it responsible for causing immense damage to the historic Chandapura Lake , off Hosur Road, near Electronics City.Observing that the lake's worsening condition was due to inaction/complicity of state authorities, NGT directed the government to pay environmental compensation of Rs 500 crore for failing to provide a clean environment to citizens.
