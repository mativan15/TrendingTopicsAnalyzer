ARLINGTON, Va., June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Privia Health Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: PRVA), today announced that its management team plans to participate in a "fireside chat" Q&A session at the following investor conferences: Tuesday, June 7th – 11:00 am ET – William Blair Growth Stock ConferenceThursday, June 9th – 9:30 am ET – Jefferies Healthcare Conference The live webcast and replay of each event, as well as any accompanying slide presentation, will be available at ir.priviahealth.com/news-and-events/events-and-presentations.
