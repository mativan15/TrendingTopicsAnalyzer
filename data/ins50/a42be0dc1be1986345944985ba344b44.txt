Punter wins £25K from free to play game after Liverpool vs Real Madrid final
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 11:30:44

ONE Coral customer certainly enjoyed the Champions League final as he won £25,000 from the bookmaker's free-to-play Football Super Series game.
The game asks football fans to predict what will happen in the big match of each weekend with four questions.
Simon Palmer made 12 out of 16 predictions correctly to win £25,000.
Simon Palmer, an Ipswich fan from Worcester, correctly answered 12 out of 16 across May to finish one correct answer ahead of the next best.
He said: "I knew I was joint top of the table before the last game of the month on Saturday evening, the Champions League final.
