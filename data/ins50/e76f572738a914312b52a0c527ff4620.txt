Photo: Seth Wenig/Associated PressListen to article(2 minutes)A federal judge dismissed a lawsuit in which Donald Trump accused New York Attorney General Letitia James of abusing her authority by investigating the financial affairs of his family business.The suit, filed by Mr. Trump and the Trump Organization in December, alleged Ms. James had a political vendetta against the former president and had opened her civil-fraud investigation without a justifiable legal basis.
