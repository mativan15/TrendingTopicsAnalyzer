Neighbours 'refusing some redundancy payments to crew'
metro.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 09:32:05

Neighbours production crew members are claiming that production company Fremantle are refusing to pay their redundancy.
The Australian soap, which has blessed our screens for 37 years since its 1985 debut, was cancelled in March with the final episodes set to air in August.
It's been confirmed that the likes of Kylie Minogue and co-star Jason Donovan will return to the show to mark the ending
Paul Stanley, an organiser of the Media Entertainment and Arts Alliance union, said: ‘They've had Kylie Minogue and Jason Donovan come over to shoot scenes recently.
