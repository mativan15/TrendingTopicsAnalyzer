Princess Eugenie spotted in Paris wearing chic Rinaland tweed top
express.co.uk
beauty
2022-10-20 10:16:00

Princess Eugenie stepped out in Paris on Wednesday and was photographed at the Hauser & Wirth Booth, the international gallery at which she is a Director, during the press preview for the Paris + Par Art Basel. This took place at the iconic Grand Palais Ephemere.Eugenie looked gorgeous in a chic checked look. The royal sported a tweed crop top by Rinaland, which she layered over a black chiffon dress in a pinafore style.Tweed is a timeless trend and a wardrobe must-have for the autumn fashion season, which Eugenie perfectly exemplified.
