NT government pays $250,000 to private firm to find backer for proposed luxury Darwin hotel
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-20 07:49:38

The Northern Territory government will pay a private company nearly $250,000 to begin "travelling around the world" to try to find someone to build a new Darwin hotel.The NT government says it wants to see the new luxury hotel built at the Darwin WaterfrontThe proposal comes just over a year since plans for a "six-star" hotel nearby were shelvedA former Labor MLA has voiced his concern over the new proposal, saying it shouldn't be the government's focusThe government is opening up a prime piece of Darwin waterfront land for a new high-end hotel to be built on Darwin Harbour, providing it can find someone to back it.
