While social media is often steered through the rat-a-tat-tat of thumbs dancing through phones, more and more professionals want to learn the 'language' of social media to advance and expand the addressable market relative to their company or product. According to Pew Research, most older adult Americans use YouTube and Facebook, while Instagram, Snapchat, and TikTok are more common among those under 30 years old.
