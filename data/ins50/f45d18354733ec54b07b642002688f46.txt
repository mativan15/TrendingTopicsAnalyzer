Published: 01:53 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 01:55 EDT, 16 October 2022 A man has been charged for a second time after cashing in an almost $10,000 winning betting slip he found on the floor of a pub.Jake Linardos, 25, from Sydney's south-east, was charged with obtaining a financial advantage by deception after cashing in a ticket he found at the Doncaster Hotel in Kensington in August, 2020.Mr Linardos found the betting slip, containing a $9,787 winner, and held onto it for a few days before cashing it in at a different pub.
