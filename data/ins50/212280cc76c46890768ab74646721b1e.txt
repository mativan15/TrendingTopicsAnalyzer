Snail to Sell 5 Million Shares at $5-$7 Each in IPO
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 11:37:00

By Colin Kellaher Snail Inc. on Monday said it plans to sell 5 million shares at between $5 and $7 apiece in its initial public offering. At the $6 midpoint of that range, the Culver City, Calif., gaming and digital entertainment company said it expects net proceeds of about $24.8 million, or roughly $28.9 million if the underwriters exercise an option to buy an additional 750,000 shares. Snail said it expects its the shares will trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol SNAL. Write to Colin Kellaher at colin.
