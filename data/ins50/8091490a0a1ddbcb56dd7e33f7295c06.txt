Police have issued an emergency text alert after a 63-year-old woman with dementia was abducted early Saturday morning in Winnipeg's Unicity area.Sandra McLaughlin was in the back of her blue 2012 Jeep Patriot, with Manitoba licence plate KXR 852, when it was stolen at about 1:57 a.m., police said.Const. Jay Murray with the police service's public information office said at a news conference on Saturday the driver of the vehicle, a relative of McLaughlin's, was inside a gas station in the 3600 block of Portage Avenue when it was stolen with McLaughlin inside.
