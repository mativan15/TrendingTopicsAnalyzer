Collider.com Many of Disney's heroes, heroines, and supporting sidekicks wouldn't survive a horror movie. However, some of their villains and supporting creatures would not only survive but thrive as the antagonist. Disney and horror movies are not usually referred to in the same sentence, but here we are. As films like Winnie the Pooh: Blood and Honey begin to take the beloved, sweet characters and turn them into movie monsters, we can't help but wonder which other Disney personalities could hold the backdrop of a horror film.
