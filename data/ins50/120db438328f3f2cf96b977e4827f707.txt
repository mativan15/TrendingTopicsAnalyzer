Published: 04:58 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 04:59 EDT, 21 October 2022 A House Democrat has accused her Republican rival in Virginia of wanting to kiss House Minority Leader Kevin McCarthy's 'feet, ring and every other thing' in one of the tightest races in the US.Democrat Rep. Elaine Luria accused Republican Virginia state Sen. Jen Kiggans of blindly backing McCarthy who viewed a temporary decline of record-breaking gas prices as bad thing for Republicans as it could hurt their chances of winning the midterms.
