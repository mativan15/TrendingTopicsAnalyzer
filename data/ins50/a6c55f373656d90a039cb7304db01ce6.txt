Government has put a renewed thrust on Act East policy: Assam CM
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 20:15:00

AssamChief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma said that Government of India under the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi has put a renewed thrust on Act East policy which has resulted into fast-paced growth and brought about paradigm shift to development narrative in Assam and other Northeastern states. Inaugurating a conclave on Act East through Northeast in presence of the ambassadors and high commissioners of ASEAN countries in Guwahati, Sarma said that development narrative generated by the Act East policy has given a new hope to the people of Northeast to align themselves with the development initiatives of the Government of India.
