Tru Niagen® enters the international market with digital cross-border launch strategy

LOS ANGELES, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ChromaDex Corp. (NASDAQ:CDXC) today announced a partnership with Juvenis to conduct cross-border sales of Tru Niagen® in South Korea. Juvenis is a Korean healthcare marketing company with a focus on the cosmetic and dietary supplement industries. With strong partnerships in a dynamic and trend-setting Korean market, Juvenis emphasizes products with differentiated science and proven benefits.
