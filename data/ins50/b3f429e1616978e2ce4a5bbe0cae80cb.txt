Zerbor/iStock via Getty Images Introduction We're doing things a bit differently in this article. For the first time in a while, I'm going to discuss a high-yield stock. In the past few months, we have covered a lot of lower-yield dividend growth companies and some "high-yield" stocks that a lot of readers believed were far from high-yield. The problem is that dividend yields, in general, are very low. This turns a 3% into a high-ish-yield stock. In this article, we will discuss Hanesbrands Inc. (NYSE:HBI), which combines two things that pull me out of my comfort zone: consumer apparel and a (very) high yield.
