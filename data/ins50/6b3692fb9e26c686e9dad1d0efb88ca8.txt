By PA Media Published: 10:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 10:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 Average petrol prices have risen for the first time since early July, figures show. Statistics from the Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy show the average cost of a litre of the fuel at UK forecourts on Monday was 162.8p. That was up from 162.1p a week ago, and was the first weekly increase since the seven days to July 4. Average price of a litre of petrol (Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy) The price rise comes after several countries cut their output of oil to boost its value.
