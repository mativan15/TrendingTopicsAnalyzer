Jess Lenouvel, real estate marketing expert and founder of The Listings Lab, redefines what success looks like for agents. Have you remembered to set up that new virtual tour? Have you cold-called enough people today?Jess says that the latter is probably the one that gets shunted down to the bottom of the priority list out of all these tasks.
