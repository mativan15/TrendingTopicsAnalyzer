 A 14-year-old boy has been charged in connection with a taxi scam investigation in Toronto, police say.
Toronto police said a vehicle resembling a taxi cab would park in a high traffic area such as a mall parking lot, with one suspect posing as a driver.Another suspect would pose as a customer, officers said.According to police, the suspects would then have an argument over the cab driver not accepting cash.
