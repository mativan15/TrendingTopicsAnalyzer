Meghan says she felt "objectified" as briefcase girl on 'Deal or No Deal'
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 10:16:40

Meghan Markle told her Archetypes podcast she was surrounded by "smart women" as a "Deal or No Deal" briefcase girl but "reduced" to "this specific archetype" in an episode titled "Breaking Down the Bimbo."The Duchess of Sussex interviewed Paris Hilton for her latest episode of her Spotify show, which aired on October 18, 2022.Meghan said: "Like I said, I was thankful for the job but not for how it made me feel which was not smart.
Meghan Markle, seen during The Fashion Awards 2018 at the Royal Albert Hall, in London, on December 10, 2018, was a "Deal or No Deal" briefcase girl in the early stages of her acting career.
