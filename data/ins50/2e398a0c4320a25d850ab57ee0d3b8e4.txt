By PA Media Published: 09:49 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 11:54 EDT, 16 October 2022 Thousands of protesters, including France's newly crowned Nobel literature laureate, piled into the streets of Paris on Sunday, in a show of anger against the bite of rising prices and cranking up pressure on the government of President Emmanuel Macron. The march for wage increases and other demands was organised by left-wing opponents of Mr Macron and lit the fuse on what promises to be an uncomfortable week for his centrist government.
