Hedge fund star David Einhorn expects stocks to tumble
Insider
business
2022-10-21 10:22:06

David Einhorn expects stocks to fall further, fears the Federal Reserve's interest-rate hikes will backfire, and worries stubborn inflation will morph into a market disaster.The Greenlight Capital boss laid out his concerns in a third-quarter letter to clients obtained by ValueWalk. He said the Fed seems intent on lowering stock prices as it works to crush inflation, which surged to a 40-year high of 9.1% in June, and remained above 8% in September.As a result, he sees the current bear market lasting a while.
