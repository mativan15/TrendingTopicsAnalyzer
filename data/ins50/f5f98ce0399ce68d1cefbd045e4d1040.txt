The Wrap 'Halloween Ends' had a healthy — albeit slightly underwhelming — box office performance in its debut weekend, but the 13th film in the popular horror franchise looks to have made up for it with a strong debut on Peacock, according to Whip Media's latest movie ranker — which reflects data from TV time, a TV and movie app that tracks viewer interest from 22 million global registered users.That's because 'Halloween Ends' was the most streamed movie in the U.S.
