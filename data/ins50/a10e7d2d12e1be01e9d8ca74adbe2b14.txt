Public cloud investment has plummeted in 2022

tech
2022-10-21 21:04:24

(Image credit: Future)

 US and European public cloud firms have lost $1.6 trillion from their overall market capitalization over the last 12 months, a new report has warned.Research (opens in new tab) by analyst firm Accel looked at the valuation of 100 publicly-traded US and EU businesses that center their business models around cloud storage services.It concluded that the valuations of these firms have dropped to around pre-pandemic levels, before the sudden switch to digital caused an explosion in the demand for cloud computing.
