Bar Broadway owners cash in on pub property sector boom
brisbanetimes.com.au
business
2022-06-03 09:30:00

One of Sydney's landmark pubs, Bar Broadway, formerly known as Sutherlands Hotel, in the inner-city suburb of Chippendale has come to market for the first time in two decades with price expectations of around $45 million.The long-term private freeholder of the property has joined with the leaseholder to sell the site which comes with development potential and sits in the heart of the Central Park development, Sydney's new 'Silicon Valley', and Chinatown.It has a long history of being a Tooth and Co pub and over the years has been known as the Westminster Hotel, Guys & Dolls Hotel, Sutherlands Hotel and most recently Bar Broadway.
