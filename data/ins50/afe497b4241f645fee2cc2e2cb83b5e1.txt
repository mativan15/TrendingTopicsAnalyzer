Redesigns Platform to Help Artists and Hosts Connect and Create In-person Shows

TORONTO, Oct. 20, 2022 /CNW/ - Canadian tech company, Side Door has launched a redesigned platform that better connects hosts with artists to create incredible in-person concert experiences, and they're entering the DRAGONS' DEN this fall. Tune in on Thursday, November 3rd, 2022, at 8:00 PM (8:30 NT) on CBC and the free CBC Gem streaming service to see Side Door's Laura Simpson (CEO and co-founder) and Dan Mangan (co-founder and JUNO Award-winning artist) pitch the popular show's Dragon investors.
