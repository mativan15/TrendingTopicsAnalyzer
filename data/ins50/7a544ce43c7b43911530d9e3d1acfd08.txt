ReutersTotal income rose to ₹591.16 crore in the last quarter of FY22, from ₹290.08 crore in the corresponding period of the previous fiscal year (FY21).PB Fintech, which operates insurance marketplace PolicyBazaar and credit platform PaisaBazaar, narrowed losses to ₹219.6 crore in the fiscal fourth quarter, from ₹643.8 crore in the year-ago period. Total income rose to ₹591.16 crore in the last quarter of FY22, from ₹290.08 crore in the corresponding period of the previous fiscal year (FY21). On a sequential basis, curbed losses by almost 36% compared to the quarter ended December 31, when it reported total losses of around ₹298 crore.
