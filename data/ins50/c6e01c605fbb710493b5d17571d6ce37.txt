The day was Friday before Diwali. I walked into my familiar retail banking branch. Aghast to find that the Manager (Accounts) was swarmed by a host of clientele. I thought at 3p.m, the Manager would have some leisure. She did not have even her lunch by that hour. The reasons: In a sparsely staffed branch, three employees were on leave; the Branch Manager went on leave for writing an examination. There were at least fifty KYCs of new accounts on her table to key in and approve. Across the counter, the patient customers, understanding her position, exhibited no hurry.
