Ben Stevens, former Alaska Senate president, dies while hiking at age 63

October 17, 2022 / 9:46 AM / CBS/AP Ben Stevens, a former Alaska Senate president and a son of the late U.S. Sen. Ted Stevens, has died while hiking in his home state. He was 63.The Alaska State Troopers said they responded to a report Thursday evening of a hiker having a medical emergency on the Lost Lake Trail near Seward. The hiker was later identified as Stevens, the troopers said. The troopers' statement said a medical service reached the scene at 6:41 p.m. and that "lifesaving measures were unsuccessful.
