Declared and opted-in consumer data made available for targeting across Connected TV and streaming audio platforms

NEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Fluent, Inc. (NASDAQ: FLNT), a leading data-driven performance marketing company, today announced its comprehensive set of audience segments will be available via TransUnion's TruAudience® Data Marketplace. The partnership connects Fluent's database of 280 million US consumer profiles to TransUnion's three-dimensional view of identity — covering people, homes, and devices delivering scale across 80 million US-connected households.
