Dr. Alexander Frech is CEO of the Amiblu Group, the leading GRP pipe producers, focusing on water-management solutions worldwide. getty For hundreds of years, up until the industrial revolution, large areas of land were 'common.' This meant that common citizens could use that land for grazing, growing things or simply being outside. The idea of the common is an intriguing concept and one that goes far beyond the basic provision of common land. In fact, I believe reinstating a sense of the common as a collective will be key to solving big societal problems in the future and a key to combating climate change.
