Image caption, Young peoplewere set the challenge of summing-up the Queen's 70-year reignThe student who designed the Queen's Platinum Jubilee emblem has said he felt "privileged" his work has received a "warm reception" across the UK.Edward Roberts's purple and platinum-coloured logo was selected as the winning entry in a competition.The 19-year-old, from Nottinghamshire, said it was a "pleasure" to see his creation on everything from buses to chocolate bars and even deck chairs.The Queen's 70-year reign will be marked over four days next week.
