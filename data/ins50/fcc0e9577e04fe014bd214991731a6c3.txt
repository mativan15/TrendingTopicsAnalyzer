Wall Street and the Problem of 'Way Too Many Publicly Traded Fintechs'
nbcconnecticut.com

Fintech has racked up some notable wins against the Wall Street incumbents, whether it's in payments, trading or lending, but the recent market has clobbered all of the fintechs that went public. One area where financial services upstarts struggled to achieve scale and go public is in asset management. While the roboadvisor concept is here to stay and is appealing to many investors, it's tough to compete with giants like Schwab and Vanguard as an independent company. Source: Wealthfront
