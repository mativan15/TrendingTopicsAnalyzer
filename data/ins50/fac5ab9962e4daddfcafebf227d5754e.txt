Nora Fatehi's performance in Dhaka cancelled by Bangladesh government 'to save dollars' as part of austerity measures
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 09:54:02

Actor and dancer Nora Fatehi was all set to perform in Dhaka soon. But her performance was ‘disallowed' by the Bangladesh government. The reason is an unusual one. Bangladesh has recently adopted austerity measures and the cancellation of Nora's performance is part of that effort to ‘save dollars'. Also read: Nora Fatehi appears before Delhi Police for questioning in Sukesh extortion caseNora, known for her work in the Indian film industry, wasn't granted permission 'in view of the global situation and with the aim of maintaining the foreign exchange reserves,' according to a notice issued by Bangladesh's Ministry of Cultural Affairs on Monday.
