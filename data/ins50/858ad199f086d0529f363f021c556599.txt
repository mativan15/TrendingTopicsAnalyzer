'Cucuruz Doan's Island' is a genuinely great film. Sunrise 
The original Mobile Suit Gundam is very much of a big deal in Japan. Its enduring cultural impact has defined the last four decades of anime. So to go back and breathe life into that world again would be no trivial matter. On paper, going back to an episode of the original Mobile Suit Gundam series that was effectively struck off the record due to its appalling animation is not the most logical starting point for a new movie. However, over the years, it became clear that the episode called Cucuruz Doan's Island had somewhat of a following.
