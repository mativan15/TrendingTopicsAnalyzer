June 01, 2022 11:22 PM Eastern Daylight Time LARGO, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CONMED Corporation (NYSE: CNMD) today announced the pricing of $700 million aggregate principal amount of 2.25% convertible senior notes due 2027 in a private offering to qualified institutional buyers pursuant to Rule 144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the 'Securities Act'). In connection with the offering of the notes, CONMED has granted the initial purchasers an option to purchase, for settlement within a 13-day period beginning on, and including, the first day on which the notes are issued, up to an additional $100 million aggregate principal amount of the notes.
