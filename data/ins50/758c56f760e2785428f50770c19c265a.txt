Share tip: Renewi may turn muck into gold
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

Renewi turns muck into gold. Well, almost: the firm collects, sorts and treats waste — including contaminated soil and water, oily sludge, hazardous chemicals, discarded electrical appliances and glass — and then sells as much as it can. Most is reused by the construction industry.Operating in Holland, Belgium, France, Portugal and Hungary, as well as the UK, Renewi is the sort of sustainability-focused business that should be flourishing given that using recycled materials cuts corporate carbon footprints.
