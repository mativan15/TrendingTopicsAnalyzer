Ex-rugby player jailed for violent attacks on transgender sex workers
irishtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 00:00:00

A man has been jailed for 10 years for violent attacks on transgender sex workers in their homes.Former schools rugby player Buti Sashi (26) was armed with a large knife when he went to the homes of the sex workers, having arranged to meet them at their city centre apartments.In the first attack on the night of May 26th, 2017, Sashi began hitting the victim on the face and body before cutting her with the knife. He demanded sex and sexually assaulted her before stealing her mobile phone and laptop.
