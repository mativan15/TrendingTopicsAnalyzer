Empowering Communities with Integrated Network Systems (ECINS) is a social enterprise dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable people and empowering the practitioners who serve them. ECINS exists to empower communities and the practitioners who serve their various needs. JERSEY CITY, N.J. (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 ECINS, a social enterprise dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable people and empowering the practitioners who serve them with a highly collaborative case management system, announces its participation in the National Association of Social Workers (NASW) Conference held June 22-25, 2022 at the Omni Shoreham Hotel in Washington, DC.
