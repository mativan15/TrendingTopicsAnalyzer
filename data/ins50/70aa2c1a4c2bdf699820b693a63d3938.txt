Client-focused: IT services agent aligns with ARG due to mutual commitment to prioritizing the client experience; its growth mindset, resources, broad solutions portfolio, and industry-leading consulting methodology 
MCLEAN, Va., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- ARG today announced that it is expanding its geographic reach with the acquisition of Fla.-based Global Network Services (GNS), an IT services agent primarily focused on providing voice and networking services to businesses located throughout North America.
