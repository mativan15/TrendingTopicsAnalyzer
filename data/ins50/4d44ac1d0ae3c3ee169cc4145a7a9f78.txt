CLEVELAND, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Maria Carr has rejoined the Cleveland office of McDonald Hopkins LLC as an Associate in the Strategic Advisory and Restructuring Department.
Carr focuses her practice on corporate restructuring, commercial bankruptcy, business counseling, and creditors' rights matters. She frequently counsels businesses and fiduciaries in chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings, state or federal receiverships, out of court workouts, or other insolvency proceedings and commercial matters.
