The summer solstice marks the beginning of the summer, but the exact dates vary depending on where in the world you are. The UK is in the northern hemisphere, and therefore summer begins in June, according to the astronomical calendar. Summer officially begins in the UK on June 21. (Image: Getty Images)In 2021, summer began on June 21 and ended on September 22, meaning this year we get one extra day of summer, based on the astronomical calendar.
