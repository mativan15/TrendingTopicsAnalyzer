(Patch Graphic)June 1, 2022 The Chamber of Commerce of Eastern CT now officially owns 92 Eugene O'Neill Drive in downtown New London, where it will begin to create a regional Innovation Center which includes a start-up incubator and co-working space.On May 6 the Chamber closed on the building and on May 31, held a formal signing for the Department of Economic and Community Development grant of $1.386 million that will allow renovations to begin. By closing on the grant the Chamber is formally agreeing to all the provisions the state laid out as part of the grant.
