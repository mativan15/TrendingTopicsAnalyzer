jetcityimage/iStock Editorial via Getty Images On Wednesday afternoon, PPG Industries (NYSE:PPG) reported earnings, which sounded the alarm bell for a global recession. The company has made great strides in bringing up prices to negate cost inflations, and supply chain issues are past their worst. However, management is warning about significant demand headwinds. Down over 25%, the stock was already discounting much of this, but the dour outlook pushed shares down another 1% after-hours, and it still does not appear to be time to get into PPG.
