GLENDALE, Ariz. (AP) — The Arizona Cardinals and New Orleans Saints are two teams stuck in the early season doldrums, struggling with injuries and inconsistent play.It's too early to throw out the ‘must-win' label for Thursday night's matchup.But it's also too late to pretend everything is OK.'It's getting to that time where you don't want to press that button, but we've got to win,' Cardinals quarterback Kyler Murray said.The Cardinals (2-4) host the Saints (2-4) on Thursday night in a game that is crucial for these reeling teams that find themselves in remarkably similar situations.
