San Diego's Morning News with Ted and LaDonaOct 20, 2022 The best of seven series is now tied at one win apiece. The Phillies won Game Tuesday night, 2-0, with the Padres getting only one hit. But it was much different Wednesday afternoon as the Padres had 13 hits including three home runs by Brandon Drury, Josh Bell and Manny Machado. Game 3 is scheduled for 4:37 p.m. PT on Friday in Philadelphia. Games 4 and 5 also will be played in Philadelphia. If neccessary, Games 6 and 7 will be back at Petco Park.
