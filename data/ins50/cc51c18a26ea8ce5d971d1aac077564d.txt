Lock Upp's Saisha Shinde keeps her promise of donating half of her earnings from the show to the LGBTQI+ organisations
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-06-01 13:45:00

Ace designer Saisha Shinde, who was seen in the reality show Lock Upp as a contestant, had promised to donate half of her earrings from the show to the LGBTQI+ organisations. The designer has kept her promise after coming out of the show. Saisha shared a few pictures from her recent visit to a foundation to fulfil her promise and shower love to her community. Saisha took to her Instagram and shared with her fans that she visited a Trans led organisation which works with young transpersonal to provide shelter education, employment, skill building, counseling support.
