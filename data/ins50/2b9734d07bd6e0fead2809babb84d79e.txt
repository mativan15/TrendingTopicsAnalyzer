The power of two! Google set to merge Meet and Duo into a single app. Here's a list of upcoming features
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 12:00:00

Google has confirmed that it is merging its two disparate video calling apps - Duo and Meet - into a single application. It's already been a long time since the tech giant last updated both its services. The tech company said that it has made deep investments in both the platforms, Google Duo and Google Meet to support its users who still use the apps. Google said that it will be adding all the Google Meet features to the Duo app, allowing its users to easily schedule their video calls by coordinating with others more conveniently or continue connect instantly with someone or a group via video call option.
