Mexico: 12 people killed in bar shooting
dw.com
news
2022-10-16 10:15:00

An armed group is believed to have opened fire on patrons and staff in a bar in Mexico's state of Guanajuato, killing six men and six women. The latest massacre comes just weeks after a similar mass shooting.

Mexico security forces, including the army and the national guard, were looking for the gunmen (file photo)
Gunmen killed 12 people in a bar in the central Mexican city of Irapuato in Guanajuato state on Saturday evening, Mexican authorities reported.
The shooting left six men and six women dead.
