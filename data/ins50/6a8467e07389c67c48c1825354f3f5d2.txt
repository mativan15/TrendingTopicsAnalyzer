‘I have always been cautious': Sri Lankan writer Shehan Karunatilaka
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-22 08:30:03

Even though the time he gets to spend with his guitar is now decidedly less, Sri Lankan writer Shehan Karunatilaka, 47, has lost none of his rockstar grunge. The black nail paint on his fingers — 'male polish', he calls it — was a hat tip to his 'juvenile rockstar ambitions'. 'My wife took me for a manicure before the award ceremony. She said you are going to the Booker, you need to look nice. Big mistake! I was getting the manicure and I see all these bottles and I told the manicurist, ‘Can you put something black?' and here we are!' he says, with a laugh.
