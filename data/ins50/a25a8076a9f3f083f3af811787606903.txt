Leafs hit the road after rollercoaster start: 'The nature of the market'

2022-10-21 21:04:30

Mark Giordano has seen it before. He'll no doubt see it again. Wins, losses, injuries and controversy are magnified early in every NHL season. In a Canadian market, the spotlight's even brighter. And then there's playing in Toronto. The Maple Leafs suited up for five games in eight days to start the schedule, and from pointed coach comments, sluggish play, and an oft-injured pair already sidelined, there's been plenty of chatter around an organization with sky-high expectations and intense pressure to succeed.
