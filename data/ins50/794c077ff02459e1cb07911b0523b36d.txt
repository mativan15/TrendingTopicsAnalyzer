News that President Biden and his wife Jill 'fext' (fight over text) to avoid bickering in front of the bodyguards is comical but also mildly encouraging. Mrs Biden said that in a recent digital squabble the president griped: 'You realise that's going down in history? There'll be a record of that,' meaning all presidential communication is logged.Finally! A politician gets it. At 79, he grasps that tech-bants are never truly private, which is more than many younger ones do. I'm astonished that so many still blithely send incriminating evidence via texts and email.
