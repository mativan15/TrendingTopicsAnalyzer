NYSE to Suspend Trading Immediately in Zhangmen Education Inc. (ZME) and Commence Delisting Proceedings

2022-06-02 00:00:00

June 02, 2022 10:34 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The New York Stock Exchange ('NYSE', the 'Exchange') announced today that the staff of NYSE Regulation has determined to commence proceedings to delist the American Depositary Shares, each representing 72 Class A Ordinary Shares (the 'ADSs') of Zhangmen Education Inc. (the 'Company') — ticker symbol ZME — from the Exchange. Trading in the Company's ADSs was suspended on the NYSE on June 2, 2022.

NYSE Regulation reached its decision to delist the ADSs pursuant to Section 802.
