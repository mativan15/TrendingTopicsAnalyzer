Flowers Foods' (NYSE:FLO) Upcoming Dividend Will Be Larger Than Last Year's
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-01 13:33:28

Flowers Foods, Inc. (NYSE:FLO) has announced that it will be increasing its dividend on the 23rd of June to US$0.22, which will be 4.8% higher than last year. This takes the dividend yield from 3.0% to 3.1%, which shareholders will be pleased with.

Flowers Foods' Earnings Easily Cover the Distributions

We like to see robust dividend yields, but that doesn't matter if the payment isn't sustainable. Before making this announcement, Flowers Foods' was paying out quite a large proportion of earnings and 88% of free cash flows.
