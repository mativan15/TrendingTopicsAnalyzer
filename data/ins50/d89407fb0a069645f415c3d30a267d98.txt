(Kristin Borden/Patch)SANTA MONICA, CA — Election day may not be until Nov. 8, but Californians are allowed to begin voting almost a month before the general election. In the Golden State, Oct. 10 was the deadline for counties to send out vote-by-mail ballots, which every registered voter will automatically receive, according to the California Secretary of State's office.
