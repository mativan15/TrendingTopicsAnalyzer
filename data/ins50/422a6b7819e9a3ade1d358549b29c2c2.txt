Marcos Silva Introduction As the name suggests, the iShares China Large-Cap ETF (NYSEARCA:FXI) offers investors access to a portfolio of large-cap Chinese stocks; to be more specific, the product focuses on 50 of the largest Chinese stocks that are listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. FXI has continued to bleed for much of this year, and over the last month or so, the losses have only intensified with the product underperforming the popular emerging markets ETF - iShares MSCI Emerging Markets ETF (EEM), by a factor of 1.
