Firecrackers to be sold at seven places in Bhubaneswar
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 15:30:00

BHUBANESWAR: Seven places in the city have been identified for selling firecrackers. A proposed area of more than 1.3 lakh square feet area will be used for erecting shops.General administration and public grievance department on Saturday in a letter to additional commissioner of Bhubaneswar municipal corporation (BMC) said the department has accorded permission for temporary use of government land in respect of seven different patches for selling of firecrackers during Diwali this year.These places are Sabarasahi Melan Padia under Gadakana mouza of ward-9, Rasulgarh Durga Mandap under Bomikhal mouza of ward-10, Palasuni Biju Patnaik Playground under Pandra mouza of ward-18, Unit-1 high school back side in Bapuji Nagar locality of ward-40, Laxmisagar Melan Padia of ward-43, In front of Badagada crematorium of ward-57 and backside of BDA market under Badagada mouza of ward 57.
