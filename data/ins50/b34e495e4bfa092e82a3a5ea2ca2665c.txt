Vendor Landscape
The global digital pathology market is fragmented, and the degree of fragmentation will accelerate during the forecast period. The market is characterized by the presence of a few established vendors. They are identified based on their revenues, market dominance, geographical presence, product portfolio, and financials.The growing adoption of IoT infrastructure among laboratories  has been instrumental in driving the growth of the market. However, issues regarding privacy and safety of a digital database might hamper the market growth.
