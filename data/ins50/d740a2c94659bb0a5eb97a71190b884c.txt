Nets, Nuggets Start Season With Shocking Losses
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 14:09:36

The first full slate of games for the 2022-23 NBA season featured a couple of shocking results. Two of the league's top championship contenders suffered blowout losses, raising early questions about their preseason expectations.The Brooklyn Nets were blown out in their home opener, losing 130-108 against the New Orleans Pelicans Wednesday night. The Utah Jazz defeated the Denver Nuggets 123-102 in their season debut.Brooklyn has championship expectations now that all three of its stars are finally healthy and eligible to play together.
