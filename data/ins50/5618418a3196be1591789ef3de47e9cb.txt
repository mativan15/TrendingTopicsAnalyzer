Harry Styles and Live Nation donate $1 million to Everytown for Gun Safety Support Fund
usatoday.com
news
2022-05-27 23:33:26

Harry Styles isn't just posting on social media about the devastation of the shooting in Uvalde, Texas that left 19 children and two teachers dead earlier this week.He's also putting money behind his sentiments. The singer, who just released "Harry's House," announced that he will partner with Live Nation to donate at least $1 million in proceeds from his latest tour to the nonprofit Everytown for Gun Safety."Along with all of you, I have been absolutely devastated by the recent string of mass shootings in America, culminating at the latest in Robb Elementary School in Uvalde, Texas," Styles posted on his Instagram account Friday.
