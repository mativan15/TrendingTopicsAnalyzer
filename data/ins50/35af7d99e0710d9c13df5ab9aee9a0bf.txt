getty 5G's promise of a faster, bigger, richer data world is finally becoming a global reality. Just last month, India announced its 5G rollout, and now other countries are starting to follow suit. While the initial focus for 5G has been on the consumer, what 5G promises for businesses remains just that: promises. At an enterprise level, actually delivering on the promise of new, game-changing technologies is often painfully slow. It can involve infrastructural revamp, expensive change management initiatives, budget cuts (or expansions), new hires and maybe even layoffs.
