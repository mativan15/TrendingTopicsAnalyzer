2 Youths Missing Near China Border In Arunachal, Families File Complaint
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-15 07:30:00

A search operation has been launched for the two missing Arunachal youths, police saidItanagar: Two youths in Arunachal Pradesh have been reported missing for almost two months after they had ventured near the Line of Actual Control (LAC) with China in search of medicinal plants. The youths, Bateilum Tikro and Bayingso Manyu, who had left their village on August 19, were last spotted on August 24. Their families reported them missing on October 9. A search operation has been launched, police officials said.
