Drive-by shooting at South Carolina graduation party kills mother of five and injures seven others
msn.com
news
2022-06-06 07:30:00

A mother-of-five was shot dead and seven others were wounded during a drive-by shooting in South Carolina at around 11 p.m. Saturday night. The Clarendon County Sheriff's Office said in a statement that around 150 people were attending a party in the backyard of a home when two cars approached and sprayed the crowd with bullets. The shooting occurred in the town of Summerton, 60 miles southeast of Columbia. 'According to her family, 32-year-old Audrionna 'Tutu' Kind was shot dead in the attack. She is survived by her five children, the youngest of whom is just three months old.
