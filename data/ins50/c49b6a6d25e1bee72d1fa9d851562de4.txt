Shutterstock splashes out
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 23:01:00

The American stock photography company Shutterstock has bought Splash News, a British celebrity video and picture agency, for an undisclosed sum.The acquisition gives Shutterstock an archive of more than 27 million images, with 40,000 images uploaded each day from 4,000 photographers.'Splash has been revered for decades as the best in the business for premium celebrity and entertainment content, and with this acquisition, Shutterstock will own the entertainment category,' Jamie Elden, the chief revenue officer at Shutterstock, said.
