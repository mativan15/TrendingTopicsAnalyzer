CB&I and DSME Sign MoU for Feasibility Study of Large Liquid Hydrogen Carrier
yahoo.com

2022-10-18 11:00:00

HOUSTON, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- CB&I, McDermott's storage business line, and Daewoo Shipbuilding & Marine Engineering Co., Ltd. (DSME) have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for a feasibility study of a large liquid hydrogen (LH2) carrier including an LH2 storage tank design.

The ability to ship large quantities of hydrogen across the ocean is an increasing need to help countries, like South Korea, achieve carbon reduction goals in a hydrogen economy. CB&I and DSME bring unique expertise to the study.
