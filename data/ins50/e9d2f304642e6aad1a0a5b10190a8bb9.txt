INSIGHT-Turbulent markets jolt currency hedge funds from decade-long slumber
yahoo.com

2022-10-18 06:00:00

By Nell Mackenzie

LONDON, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Veteran hedge fund manager John Taylor describes one of his favourite Gary Larson cartoons, where one vulture sitting on an animal carcass says to another as more descend: "good friends flying in from all over...this is the best of times".

There is, he says jokingly, a resemblance to the hedge funds currently circling foreign exchange markets, where a sudden rise in volatility offers to boost returns for the few specialist investors who survived the decade-long period of calm that forced many from the sector.
