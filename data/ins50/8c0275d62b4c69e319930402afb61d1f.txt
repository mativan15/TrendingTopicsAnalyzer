Dublin Airport delays: ‘Major catastrophe' or ‘first-world problem'?

2022-06-03 04:00:00

It's just past 7am on Monday, the day after the fiasco at Dublin Airport, and Gavin Jennings sounds like he's champing at the bit as he starts his interview on Morning Ireland (RTÉ Radio 1, weekdays). After curtly introducing Kevin Cullinane, head of communications with Dublin Airport Authority (DAA), Jennings launches into his questions: 'Do you know how many people missed their flight yesterday?' Silence. 'Hello, Kevin?' More silence. 'Is Kevin there?' Admitting defeat, the presenter goes to an ad break.
