(PTI Photo)Having risen from the ashes like a proverbial phoenix, the renewed Indian men's hockey team hopes to improve on previous performances when it plays Japan in the first "Super 4" match of the ongoing Asia Cup. The young team led by Sardar Singh dominated Indonesia despite facing a below-par opponent in their final group game, scoring an amazing 16 goals within an hour.
