Altitude Acquisition Corp. Announces Intent to Adjourn Special Meeting to Approve Extension Proposal

2022-06-03 10:03:10

ATLANTA, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Altitude Acquisition Corp. ('ALTUU')(Nasdaq: ALTUU, ALTU, ALTUW), announced today that it intends to adjourn, without conducting any business, the special meeting of its stockholders to be held with respect to the extension of the time ALTUU has to consummate an initial business combination (the 'Extension Meeting'), which is scheduled to occur at 12:00 p.m., Eastern time, on June 7, 2022, and reconvene at 10:30 a.m., Eastern time, on June 10, 2022, in order to solicit additional proxies for the Extension Meeting.
