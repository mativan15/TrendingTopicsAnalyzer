Electronic Arts (EA 0.86%) reported strong earnings results in early May, and the stock has been on a tear ever since. After underperforming the Nasdaq Composite for most of the past year, shares are up almost 19% over the last month. 
Even after that sharp rise, the stock still looks attractive based on one growth opportunity.
EA is building momentum in mobile gaming
EA has 580 million unique player accounts spread across a strong lineup of titles on PC and console, including FIFA, Apex Legends, and The Sims.
