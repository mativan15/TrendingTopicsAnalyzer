Those who hoped an electric car would be cheap to run will be shocked by recent rises in energy costs. The RAC has revealed that the price of recharging an average family-size car has risen by 42 per cent since the spring. On average, this cost is now more than £32, which is a full £9.60 higher than May 2022.The good news is that there are still various ways to save big money if you are smart about recharging. In this second part of our guide to how to save money charging an electric car, we look at the options for doing so at public points.
