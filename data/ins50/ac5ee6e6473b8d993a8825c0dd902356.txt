Fox Soul Partners With Black Women Talk Tech for the 6th Annual 'Roadmap to Billions' Conference
globenewswire.com

2022-05-31 13:30:00

BROOKLYN, N.Y., May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) --  Today, Black Women Talk Tech announces that FOX SOUL, the Black community's premiere free streaming platform, will serve as an official media partner for their 6th Annual Roadmap to Billions hybrid conference, taking place June 15-17 at the Brooklyn Navy Yard in Brooklyn, New York. The program aims to celebrate Black culture and deal with real topics that impact the everyday lives of the Black community through frank and insightful dialogue with local and national influencers.
