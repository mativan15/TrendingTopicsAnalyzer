Rumors have suggested for months that Bloober Team is working on a Silent Hill 2 remake. Konami has confirmed this today, leading off its Silent Hill Transmission event with the announcement. The remake is coming to PlayStation 5 and PC at an unspecified date. As seen in its reveal, this looks to be a big budget remake on a similar scale with what we've seen from efforts like Capcom's revamps of past hits like Resident Evil 2.
