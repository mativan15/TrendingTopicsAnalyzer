TORONTO, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Immutable Holdings Inc. (NEO:HOLD) ('Immutable Holdings' or the 'Company'), a publicly-traded blockchain holding company, announces that the board of directors of the Company has appointed Richter LLP as the Company's new auditor, replacing BF Borgers CPA PC. The Company has filed a Notice of Change of Auditor in respect of this change under its profile on SEDAR at www.sedar.com. About Immutable Holdings Inc. Immutable Holdings Inc. (NEO:HOLD) is on a mission to democratize access to Web3 and blockchain-based products and services.
