Burton Cummings to perform two shows at namesake venue in December
Copyright Bellmedia
news
2022-10-18 14:00:00

Legendary Manitoba musician Burton Cummings will be playing two shows at his namesake venue in December. On Tuesday, True North Sports and Entertainment announced the Winnipeg-born singer and songwriter will be performing at the Burton Cummings Theatre on Dec. 28 and 31. True North explained that the Dec. 28 show will be an 'up-close and unplugged performance' from Cummings and his band in an acoustic and intimate setting. The Dec. 31 show will a birthday bash for Cummings as it takes place on his 75th birthday.
