CALGARY, Alberta, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- DIRTT Environmental Solutions Ltd. ('DIRTT' or the 'Company') (Nasdaq: DRTT, TSX: DRT) today announced the departure of Jennifer Warawa as Chief Commercial Officer, and Jeffrey Calkins as Chief Operating Officer and Interim Co-CEO. Ken Sanders, Board Chair, commented: 'DIRTT's Board of Directors continues to be impressed with the energy, creativity, teamwork, and passion that exists across the organization. At the same time, we are committed to building a more streamlined, agile, and tightly integrated company that is best positioned for sustainable growth and financial performance in the future.
