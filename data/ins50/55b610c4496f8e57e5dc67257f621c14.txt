May 27, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time RADNOR, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Lincoln Financial Group (NYSE:LNC) announced today that the board of directors of Lincoln National Corporation declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.45 per share on the corporation's common stock. The dividend on the common stock will be payable August 1, 2022 to shareholders of record at the close of business on July 11, 2022.
