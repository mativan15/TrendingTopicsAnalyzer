5 Takeaways From the Utah Senate Debate
nytimes.com
news
2022-10-18 03:01:35

Senator Mike Lee, Republican of Utah, and his independent challenger, Evan McMullin, a former C.I.A. officer, met Monday evening for their only debate, a largely genteel affair that showed flashes of tension mainly around Mr. Lee's role in the effort to overturn the results of the 2020 election and keep Donald J. Trump in the presidency.

Here are five takeaways from an unusual debate in an unusual Senate race.

Jan. 6 remains center stage.

No race in the country has spotlighted the events after the 2020 election quite so much as the Senate contest in Utah.
