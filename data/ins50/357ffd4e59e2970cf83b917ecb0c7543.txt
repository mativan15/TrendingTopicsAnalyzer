Some of Orlando's largest private high schools are among the most expensive.

Eighteen of metro Orlando's 20 most expensive private schools cost at least $10,000 for the 2022-23 academic year. The largest private high schools in the area offer a full pre-K through 12th grade slate, while others offer just grades 9-12.

Why this matters: Some private schools can offer an advanced education or expanded resources when compared to public schools.
