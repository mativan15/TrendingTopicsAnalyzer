Before release, Ram Setu gets high court's piracy shield against rogue websites
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 16:12:49

NEW DELHI: The Delhi high court has restrained 23 'rogue websites' from distributing, hosting, streaming, retransmitting, exhibiting and downloading of the upcoming movie Ram Setu, saying that piracy has to be curbed and needs to be dealt with a heavy hand.In an order of October 18, justice Jyoti Singh said that the producer, Cape of Good Films, and the author of the movie had made out a prima facie case for grant of ex parte ad-interim relief as huge investments have been made by makers.The producer had list 23 video sharing websites that are known to offer visitors download links to movies and web series.
