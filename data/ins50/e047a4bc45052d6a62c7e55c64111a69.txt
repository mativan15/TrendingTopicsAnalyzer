Boris Johnson said the £15bn cost of living payment 'bazooka' will 'get us through until prices start to abate' - but 'the government cannot solve every problem'Boris Johnson during a visit to CityFibre Training Academy in Stockton today Brits could face a long wait for the next round of cost of living help after Boris Johnson and Rishi Sunak suggested what's announced will have to tide people over until Spring. The Prime Minister said he hoped a £15bn package announced yesterday - including £650 for people on benefits from July and £400 off all household electricity bills from October - would 'get us through' until prices 'start to abate'.
