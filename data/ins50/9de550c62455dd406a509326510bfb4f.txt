Share price of ICICI Lombard falls as Nifty weakens

2022-06-06 06:45:00

Getty ImagesNifty moved in a tight range of 82 points, and ended slightly below the psychologically crucial level of 15,700. Shares of ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Ltd. slipped 0.49 per cent to Rs 1212.9 in Monday's trade as of 11:16AM (IST). The stock hit a high price of Rs 1220.4 and low of Rs 1208.0 during the session. The return on equity (ROE) for the stock stood at 13.42 per cent. Traded volume on the counter stood at 11:16AM shares and turnover at Rs 0.42 crore around that time.
