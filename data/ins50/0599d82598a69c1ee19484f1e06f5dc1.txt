A pandemic would seem like the perfect climate to sell insurance to people wanting to protect themselves against major illnesses. But just the opposite seems to the case, with a Chinese economy reeling from the latest Covid-19 outbreaks leaving insurance broker Fanhua Inc. (NASDAQ:FANH) helplessly stuck with dimming prospects. The company's net revenue dropped about 37% to 686.
