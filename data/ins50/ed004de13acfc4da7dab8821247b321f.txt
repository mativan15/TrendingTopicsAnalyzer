AgenciesAPJ Abdul Kalam (File Pic)Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday paid tributes to former president A P J Abdul Kalam on his birth anniversary. "He is greatly admired for his contribution to our nation as a scientist and as a President who struck a chord with every section of society," Modi tweeted on Kalam's 91st birth anniversary. One of India's leading scientists, Kalam wrote some bestselling books and was widely popular, especially among the younger generation, for his efforts to mentor them and hold frequent interactions with them across the country.
