Jose Mourinho to face old club Tottenham for first time since brutal sacking
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 09:54:59

JOSE MOURINHO will face his former club Tottenham for the first time after he was brutally sacked ahead of the Carabao Cup final last season.
The Special One will be taking on Spurs with his Europa Conference League-winning Roma team on July 30 in Haifa, Israel.
