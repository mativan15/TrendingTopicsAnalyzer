SALT LAKE CITY, UT / ACCESSWIRE / June 1, 2022 / The consumer and commercial drone market has experienced exponential growth in recent years, with some projections, such as those from Goldman Sachs Research, estimating a market size surpassing $100 billion in the near future.Tapping into this demand, startup and consumer drone pioneer EXO Drones achieved early success. Lead by an audacious team out of Salt Lake City, Utah, EXO Drones debuted in early 2020 offering highly affordable GPS camera drones that earned praise from both consumers and critics alike.
