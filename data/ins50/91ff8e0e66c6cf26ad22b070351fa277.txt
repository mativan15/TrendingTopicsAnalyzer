When the Dolphins host the Steelers on NBC's Sunday Night Football, the home team will be celebrating the 50th anniversary of the 17-0 team from 1972. They'll be wearing their awesome throwback uniforms.

Unlike former Dolphins coach Brian Flores, who preferred the old-school look to the current base uniform, current Dolphins coach Mike McDaniel doesn't lean one way or the other.

'I like winning jerseys,' McDaniel told reporters on Wednesday.
