ScreenDaily Martyna Szmytkowska, Malaika Bova promoted to artistic director roles.The UK's Raindance Film Festival has promoted senior programmers Martyna Szmytkowska and Malaika Bova to artistic directors, as part of a restructure of the festival's leading team.They join existing artistic director Suzanne Ballantyne who also works as head of programming and co-founded the festival with Elliot Grove.Festival producer David Martinez has been promoted to executive director, ahead of the festival's 30th anniversary edition, which runs from October 26 to November 5 this year.
