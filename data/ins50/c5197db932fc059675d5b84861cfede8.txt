Here's how sports-betting stocks like DraftKings and Caesars performed in May 2022
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 13:49:00

Since the U.S. Supreme Court struck down the Professional and Amateur Sports Protection Act (PASPA) in 2018, sports-betting companies have benefitted.As of the end of May 2022, 30 U.S. states and Washington, D.C. now offer some form of legalized sports wagering, according to the latest tally from the American Gaming Association. In the four years since legalization, Americans have wagered over $125 billion on sports as betting morphed from a taboo topic on sports television to commentators openly discussing gambling odds before and during games.
