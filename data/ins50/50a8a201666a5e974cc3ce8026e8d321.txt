By Reuters Published: 11:53 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 11:53 EDT, 31 May 2022 WASHINGTON, May 31 (Reuters) - South Korean supergroup BTS, a fundraising juggernaut for social justice causes in the United States, will visit the White House on Tuesday to discuss hate crimes targeting Asians with U.S. President Joe Biden. The group will also address the White House press briefing Tuesday afternoon. "Biden and BTS will also discuss the importance of diversity and inclusion and BTS' platform as youth ambassadors who spread a message of hope and positivity across the world," the White House said last week in announcing the visit.
