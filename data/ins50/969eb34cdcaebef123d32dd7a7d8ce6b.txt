Published: 01:31 EDT, 6 June 2022 | Updated: 01:31 EDT, 6 June 2022 Anthony Albanese wobbled as he cycled alongside the Indonesian president during his second overseas trip since taking power.The Prime Minister joined Joko Widodo on a brief cycling tour around the presidential palace in Jakarta after being treated to a ceremonial welcome on Monday. Witnesses said he appeared to struggle with the slow pace of the bike ride, while President Widodo rode confidently without wobbling. The two leaders chatted to one another as they cruised around the palace grounds ahead of their private one-on-one meeting.
