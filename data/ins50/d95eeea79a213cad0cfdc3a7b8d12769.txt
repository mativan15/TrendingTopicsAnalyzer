Southeastern Ontario elects 4 PC MPPs, 1 Liberal
globalnews.ca
news
2022-06-03 03:30:51

The 2022 Ontario election has come and gone, and Doug Ford's majority government is well represented in Southeastern Ontario.
Four ridings in the region elected a Progressive Conservative MPP, while Kingston and the Islands voted in Liberal Ted Hsu.The seat for Lanark-Frontenac-Kingston was up for grabs after previous MPP Randy Hillier did not seek re-election. Taking his place is PC candidate John Jordan.In Hastings-Lennox and Addington, PC MPP Darryl Kramp did not seek re-election, but the riding will remain Tory blue, electing former mayor of Loyalist Township Ric Bresee.
