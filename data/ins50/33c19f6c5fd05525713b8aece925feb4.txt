Gujarat govt announces 2 free cylinders under PMUY, 38 lakh households to benefit
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-17 16:58:25

The Gujarat government Monday announced plans to provide two domestic cylinders to around 38 lakh gas connection holders covered under the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) in the state free of cost. It also announced reducing Value Added Tax (VAT) on Piped Natural Gas (PNG) and Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) by 10 per cent.
The twin decisions are likely to provide financial benefits worth Rs 1,650 crore to the poor and middle-class families in the state, stated government spokesperson and senior minister Jitu Vaghani and Finance Minister Kanu Desai.
