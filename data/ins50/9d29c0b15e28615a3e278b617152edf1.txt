NAI SunVista moving to new office in Uptown Albuquerque
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 12:00:00

NAI SunVista plans to officially open its doors to its new uptown Albuquerque office on Nov. 14.

The move after 14 years is due — in part — to a need for more office space.

' SunVista is a growing company, and our new, state-of-the-art offices will enable us to better serve our expanding client base,' said NAI SunVista CEO Debbie Harms, who runs the company alongside her husband Irwin, who is the company president. "Its central location in the heart of one of Albuquerque's largest trade areas is the perfect site for us.
