Chancellor Rishi Sunak revealed the financial relief as the cost of living crisis threatened to spiral out of control. He said his help 'will make sure the most vulnerable and least well off get the support they need at this time of difficulty'. One of the key points of his support package is a £400 discount on energy bills for all.The Chancellor previously said that from this October, every resident in the UK will automatically receive a £200 loan from the Government, to help cover rising energy bills.
