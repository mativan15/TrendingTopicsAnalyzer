June 01, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--cove, a technology solution that integrates the tenant experience ('TeX') and building operating systems ('building OS') for commercial real estate, today announced the completion of a funding round from Blackstone Innovations Investments and other investors including Kastle Systems. They join existing investor, Second Century Ventures.



cove provides landlords and operators with a suite of products and services, including centralized TeX solutions, integrated building OS and flexible office management, all through a single platform.
