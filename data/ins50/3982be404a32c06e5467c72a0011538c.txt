Green Capital Scores Against Coal in Australia
wsj.com
news
2022-05-30 12:50:00

Photo: JASON REED/REUTERS Environment-conscious investors have scored a victory against dirty power in Down Under, derailing a plan by Australia's largest power company to spin off its coal power assets—which would have helped ringfence them from activist pressure to shut down early. That win shows that investors are buying into the idea that a green agenda can also be a good business strategy—even at companies still heavily reliant on dirty fuels. Whether they are really right will probably take years to prove.
