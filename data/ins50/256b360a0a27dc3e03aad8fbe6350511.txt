Stray Kids Top U.S. Billboard 200 Again
naver.com
news
2022-10-18 11:08:00

Boy band Stray Kids have returned to the top of the U.S. Billboard 200 albums chart with their mini album "Maxident."Billboard said on Sunday that the boys "ring up their second No. 1 album on the Billboard 200 -- and second of 2022 -- as ["Maxident"] debuts atop the list" for the week ending Oct. 22.Stray Kids, which were created by JYP Entertainment and debuted in 2018, topped the chart last March with their previous mini album, "Oddinary."Boy band Bangtan Boys, also known as BTS, are the only K-pop group to top the chart more than twice, doing so eight times in total.
