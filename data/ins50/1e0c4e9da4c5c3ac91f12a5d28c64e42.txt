June 01, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Lazard Ltd (NYSE: LAZ) announced today that Kenneth M. Jacobs, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, is scheduled to participate in a moderated question and answer session at the Morgan Stanley U.S. Financials, Payments & CRE Conference on Tuesday, June 14, at 8:00 a.m. EDT.

A live audio webcast will be available via the Lazard Investor Relations website at www.lazard.com. A replay will be available on the same website, shortly after the conference.
