Gold recovers on technical buying, softer U.S. dollar
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 03:00:00

Gold prices rose on Monday, supported by technical trading after the previous session's steep fall and a pause in the dollar rally, although an impending oversized rate hike by the U.S. Federal Reserve limited bullion's appeal. FUNDAMENTALS
 * Spot gold was up 0.5% at $1,650.49 per ounce, as of 0149 GMT. Prices fell more than 1% on Friday and marked its biggest weekly decline since July. * U.S. gold futures were up 0.4% at $1,656.00. * The dollar index was down 0.1%, easing some pressure on the greenback-priced gold.
