October 20, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time ROCHESTER, Minn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Outdoor Living Supply ('OLS'), a distribution platform for outdoor living products with a focus on hardscapes, announced today it has acquired Bedrock Landscape Supply ('the Company'), an independent distributor of natural stone, pavers, bulk materials, and landscape supplies to the Las Vegas and Henderson, Nevada markets. As OLS continues to expand its network westward, this acquisition gives OLS a foothold in the fast-growing southern Nevada region.
