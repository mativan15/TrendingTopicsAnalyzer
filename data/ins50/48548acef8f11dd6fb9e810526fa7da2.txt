Twelve years ago, my husband Brian and I were raising two toddlers. While we made enough money through our 9-to-5 jobs to support our family, I was itching to start my own business. I brainstormed ideas every day. But one afternoon, during a four-hour drive, something clicked. We had just signed our son up for music classes — and they weren't cheap. We were always looking for incredible experiences for our kids on a budget. "What if there was a deals website that focused specifically on what parents want and need? Think of Groupon, but for parents," I explained to Brian.
