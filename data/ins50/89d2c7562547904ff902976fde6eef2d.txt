PUNE The Anti-Extortion Cell (AEC) of Pune crime branch on Sunday nabbed notorious goon Gajanan alias Gajanan Marne from Wai, Satara in connection with ₹20 crore extortion case.Marne had invested ₹4 crore in shares and had demanded ₹20 crore as extortion money from a businessman.
