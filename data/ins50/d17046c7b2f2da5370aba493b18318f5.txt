Environmental Sustainability as a Business Opportunity and Risk
2022 Google Inc.

2022-05-31 10:45:05

General Earth Institute, Sustainability Consumers and businesses are starting to pay attention to the environmental impact of what they buy and sell. This is a trend responding to the reality of climate change, the loss of biodiversity, and a cultural awareness of the importance of the planet to human well-being. Of course, the definition of human well-being is changing in many parts of the world. In the developing world and places under assault like Ukraine, schools in Texas or supermarkets in Buffalo, the issue is survival.
