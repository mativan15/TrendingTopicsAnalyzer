Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma visits the erosion-affected embankment at Maijan area, which is crucial in keeping the town safe from floods, in Dibrugarh, on October 16, 2022.
| Photo Credit: Twitter/@himantabiswa
 Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma on October 16 inspected the erosion-affected embankment in Maijan area of Dibrugarh district and gave assurance that urgent measures would be taken to address the problem.He also said that his government would provide assistance to the families who have lost their land and houses to erosion in the area.
