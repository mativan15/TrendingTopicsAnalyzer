UCF partners with Universal for new design lab, class
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 21:58:37

The University of Central Florida has partnered with Universal Creative to give students a new hands-on learning experience.

On Wednesday, UCF announced the 'Creative Lab' will begin with a blended modality.

Students will attend classes at Universal Orlando Resort and learn from engineers, creative directors and other professionals in the field.

During the class, students will also go to on-campus design workshops.

The first class using the design lab will be offered to graduate students in the Themed Experience program.
