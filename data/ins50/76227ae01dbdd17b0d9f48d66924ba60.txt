German army needs enemy top MP

Russia's military action in Ukraine has allowed Germany to once again see ?what an enemy could look like,? a senior MP has saidThe proposed complex modernization of the German Armed Forces will require re-inventing "an image of an enemy," Marie-Agnes Strack-Zimmermann, the head of the German Bundestag's Defense Committee, has outlined. Now, as Russia continues its military operation in Ukraine, the Bundeswehr sees once again "what an enemy might look like," the MP told the RND media group on Tuesday.
