Nancy Pelosi's husband sells calls in Micron, Nvidia for loss, exercises Alphabet options
seekingalpha.com

Anna Rose Layden/Getty Images News A new congressional trading form filed by House Speaker Nancy Pelosi disclosed that her husband Paul sold call options in Micron Technology (NASDAQ:MU) and Nvidia (NASDAQ:NVDA) for a loss of less than $1M, while making other trades. The filing, which stems from CongressTrading.com, a database that keeps track of lawmaker's stock trades, shows that Paul Pelosi lost $392,575 on the Micron (MU) call options he purchased in December 2021. The 82-year-old Pelosi also lost $361,476 on Nvidia (NVDA) call options he bought in July 2021.
