Taser Drones Are the School Shooting 'Solution' No One Asked For
gizmodo.com
news
2022-06-03 21:25:00

Axon, the maker of Tasers, police body cameras, and VR training simulations, wants to add a new buzzy product to its tech policing ecosystem: Taser Drones. While the company believes these shoebox-sized shock machines could potentially save lives during school shootings, privacy and civil liberties groups told Gizmodo they believe the drones may do more harm than good and could be used to brutally suppress protestors and other political dissidents.Axon floated its idea in a press release this week announcing it had begun formally developing a 'non-lethal, remotely-operated TASER drone system.
