We've been writing a lot about TV streaming services recently here at IndyBest, from Netflix and Disney+ to Amazon Prime Video and Apple TV+. Now it's time for one more, but with Hulu things are a little different.You see, Hulu is one of those rare things on today's open internet. It can only be accessed in the US.We're used to seeing the content catalogues of Netflix change depending on the country it's viewed in, but with Hulu there is unfortunately a hard and fast rule: It is only available in the US, as well as US territories and on US military bases.
