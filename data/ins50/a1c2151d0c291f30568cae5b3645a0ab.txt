We may soon look back at this week as the decisive moment for Elon Musk's takeover of Twitter. Yes, for real this time, folks. And this is just Round 2 of the Elon Musk Shenanigans Watch! On Thursday night, Bloomberg reported that there's been a vibe shift, if you will, in the talks between the Tesla CEO and the hard-hit social network. Internal sources told Bloomberg that 'talks between the two sides have turned cordial and are focused on closing the transaction rather than litigation.' In other words, Musk is (once again) resolving not to battle the deal, and Twitter is trying to meet his demands in hopes of wrapping this whole thing up by Oct.
