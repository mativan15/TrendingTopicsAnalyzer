Jets exploit ‘specific thing that we saw' for blocked punt against Packers
nypost.com
news
2022-10-17 05:12:57

GREEN BAY, Wis. — A few Jets players saw something on early Packers punts Sunday that they felt like they could exploit.
They were right. The Jets blocked a punt for a touchdown with 5:43 left in the third quarter after coming up with a block on the sideline in the 27-10 Jets win at Lambeau Field.
'That was a specific thing that we saw today, during the game. That was an adjustment,' said Will Parks, who scored the touchdown. 'I came up to [special teams coordinator Brant Boyer] and I said something and he dialed up that play.
