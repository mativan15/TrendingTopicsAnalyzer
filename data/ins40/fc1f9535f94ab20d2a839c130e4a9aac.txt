Duke Energy Corporation (NYSE:DUK) is largely controlled by institutional shareholders who own 64% of the company
yahoo.com

2022-10-15 13:00:24

To get a sense of who is truly in control of Duke Energy Corporation (NYSE:DUK), it is important to understand the ownership structure of the business. The group holding the most number of shares in the company, around 64% to be precise, is institutions. In other words, the group stands to gain the most (or lose the most) from their investment into the company.

Given the vast amount of money and research capacities at their disposal, institutional ownership tends to carry a lot of weight, especially with individual investors.
