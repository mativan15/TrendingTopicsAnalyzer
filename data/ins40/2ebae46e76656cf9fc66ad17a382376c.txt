(PRWEB) May 26, 2022 Virsys12, a leading Salesforce solution integrator in healthcare consulting and AppExchange ISV, today announced it has appointed Kevin Collins as VP of services, technical architecture. Collins is a healthcare technology executive with more than 35 years of experience in product design and development, customer success, cloud computing services and business development.
