PTITelangana BJP President Bandi Sanjay KumarBJP will hold a two-day party national executive meet in Hyderabad from July 2 to take stock of the various programmes in progress under different forums in the organization and the state units. The party is holding this conclave in Telangana where it is fast emerging as the main political challenger to ruling TRS. This will be the first full-fledged post-Covid national executive of the party outside the national capital and will be held ahead of crucial elections in Gujarat and Himachal Pradesh.
