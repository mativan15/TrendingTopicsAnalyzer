Group14 lands $100M from Department of Energy for battery facility
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 21:46:00

Local renewable energy companies continue to land major funding.

Woodinville-based battery technology company Group14 Technologies announced Wednesday it will receive $100 million from the Department of Energy. Group14 said the money will go toward building two manufacturing modules in its planned Moses Lake facility, slated to open in the second half of next year.

The award follows last year's passage of the $1 trillion infrastructure law, which President Joe Biden signed in November.
