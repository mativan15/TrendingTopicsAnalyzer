TaroWorks Offers $20,000 Grant to Help Last Mile Distributors Scale with Mobile Tech

PR Newswire

WASHINGTON, June 1, 2022

WASHINGTON, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- TaroWorks , the offline mobile field service app, is awarding a $20,000 grant to subsidize the digitization of a nonprofit's last mile distribution operations in vulnerable communities. The grant will be funded by Qualcomm Incorporated, through its Qualcomm® Wireless Reach™ initiative.

TaroWorks

TaroWorks provides a mobile app that helps last mile distributors manage and gain greater visibility into sales, inventory, and product maintenance while offline in places like rural India, Kenya, and Cambodia.
