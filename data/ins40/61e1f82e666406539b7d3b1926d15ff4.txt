Published: 05:59 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 06:08 EDT, 31 May 2022 Nottingham Forest owner Evangelos Marinakis has revealed that manager Steve Cooper is set for a new contract, and has vowed to provide him with a sizeable war chest to allow him to splash the cash this summer after their promotion.Forest sealed their spot in the Premier League next season after beating Huddersfield Town in the Championship play-off final at the weekend, a memorable result which sealed their return to the top-flight after 23 years away.
