VoltaGrid closes $150M equity raise to scale microgrid, alternative-fuel platform

Houston-based VoltaGrid LLC raised more money to grow its distributed power generation business.

The microgrid energy company recently announced closing a $150 million equity raise from existing investors, including Canada Pension Plan Investment Board, Pilot Company, Longbow Capital and Walter Ventures.
