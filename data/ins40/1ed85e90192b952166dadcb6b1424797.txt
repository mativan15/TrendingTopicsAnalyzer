More Video Mohamed Sheikh Nor, Bloomberg News (Bloomberg) -- Somalia's government signed an oil-production sharing agreement with US-based Coastline Exploration Ltd., the latest step toward developing the war-torn nation's energy industry.Coastline, based in Houston, Texas, paid $7 million to the government for seven agreements and will now proceed with an exploration program, the government said in a statement on Friday.
