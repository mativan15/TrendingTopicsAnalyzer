Steve Maharey was a former Labour Cabinet Minister and vice-chancellor of Massey University. OPINION: When I was a Cabinet Minister, some 14 years ago now, international travel was part of the job. On the first occasion I led a delegation, a mix of public and private sector people, the senior official in charge of the group sat everyone down at the Auckland airport to explain the rules of the game. The number one rule was, whatever differences we might have as individuals, when we were offshore, we were team New Zealand.
