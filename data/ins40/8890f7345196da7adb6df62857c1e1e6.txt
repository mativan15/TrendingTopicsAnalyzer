Aftermath of floods in HeraklionA street is covered with mud and debris, in the aftermath of a flood in Aghia Pelagia, Heraklion, Crete island, Greece, 16 October 2022. House and shop owners started to remove mud and debris from their properties that were carried by the rushing waters, while a government task force visits the area to inspect the damage caused by flash floods.
