Why Shares of Northern Trust Are Falling Today
fool.com
news
2022-10-19 17:13:12

Shares of the large custodian bank Northern Trust (NTRS -9.75%) had fallen roughly 9.5% as of 12:53 p.m. ET today after the company reported earnings results for the third quarter of the year this morning.
So what
Northern Trust reported diluted earnings per common share of $1.80 on total revenue of roughly $1.77 billion. Earnings missed analyst estimates for the quarter, while revenue came in line with estimates.
"Northern Trust's third-quarter results reflected consistent execution in the face of challenging macroeconomic and market conditions," CEO Michael O'Grady said in an earnings statement.
