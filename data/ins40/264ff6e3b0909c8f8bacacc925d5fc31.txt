Variety - Film News Dollhouse Pictures, production company founded by Krew Boylan, Rose Byrne, Jessica Carrera, Shannon Murphy and Gracie Otto, is to produce 'Devotion,' a book-to-film adaptation of Hannah Kent's bestselling novel of the same title. The production is in partnership with production and finance firm Storyd.The story narrates a love story between two young women in 1836 Prussia. On the verge of womanhood, the two outsiders find a kindred spirit in the other.
