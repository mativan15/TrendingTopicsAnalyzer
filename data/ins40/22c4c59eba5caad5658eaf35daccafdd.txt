Variety - TV News Cannes — Top European powerhouse Studiocanal has renewed its global deal with Nickelodeon which will board Season 3 of CG animated megahit 'The Adventures of Paddington.'Marking most surely one of the biggest single title content deals unveiled at this year's Mipcom, the Nickelodeon renewal for Season 3 comes as Studiocanal has also unveiled a slew of second-window deals on Season 1 after the title's hugely successful global rollout overseen, like Season 2, by Nickelodeon.
