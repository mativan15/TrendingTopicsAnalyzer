CM launches e-operations of agriculture societies
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 05:45:00

Bhubaneswar: Chief minister Naveen Patnaik on Friday launched online operations in primary agriculture cooperative societies (PACS), helping the 2,063 PACS to come under the core banking solution platform.Speaking on the occasion, Naveen said this is the first of its kind initiative in the country. He said it will bring about efficient and transparent service delivery in the field of banking, credit and other operations.As many as 384 banking assistants were inducted on Friday in 15 district central co-operative banks (DCCBs) in the presence of the chief minister.
