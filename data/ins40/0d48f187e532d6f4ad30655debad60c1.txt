GUWAHATI:An expert committee will soon submit an action plan for the restoration of polluted waterbodies in Meghalaya.Unscientific coal and limestone mining, stone quarrying and the release of industrial, commercial and domestic waste are the prime causes of pollution of the State's waterbodies.The Meghalaya government had set up the 10-member expert panel on the protection and restoration of the State's waterbodies on June 23, following an order of the High Court of Meghalaya.
