The fentanyl crisis is killing more people than ever and plaguing communities across America – and it's being fueled by President Biden and his failure to secure the southern border.
In 2021, nearly 108,000 people died of drug overdoses; 71,000 of whom died from fentanyl or fentanyl-related substances. Illicit fentanyl overdose deaths among teens accounted for 77% of adolescent overdose deaths in 2021.
