What if you could earn airline miles every time you went to Starbucks for a caffeine boost? Well, now you can! Starbucks and Delta Air Lines recently launched a loyalty partnership that will allow customers to earn more rewards through both loyalty programs. It's quick and easy to link your accounts, and you could earn free coffee and free flights sooner.Introducing the Starbucks and Delta loyalty partnershipAs of Oct. 12, U.S. customers can link their Delta SkyMiles and Starbucks Rewards accounts to boost their rewards potential.
