October 19, 2022 11:16 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KBRA releases a Day 2 recap of the ABS East 2022 conference held in Miami on October 17-19.

The day began with three plenary sessions focused on policy and regulatory developments as well as assessments of macro and geopolitical risks in the securitized market. The afternoon was once again allocated to several breakout panel sessions focused on specific securitized asset classes.
