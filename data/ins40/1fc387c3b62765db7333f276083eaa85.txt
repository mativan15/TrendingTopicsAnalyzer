Biden goes for the safer play on canceling student loan debt
washingtonpost.com
politics
2022-05-27 16:40:59

We've finally received word on just what President Biden plans to do in the face of long-standing pressure from his left to cancel student loan debt. And he's eschewing bold action in favor of what seems to be about the most broadly acceptable option.As The Post's Tyler Pager, Danielle Douglas-Gabriel and Jeff Stein report, the White House is planning to cancel $10,000 in debt per borrower, while limiting it to individuals who make less than $150,000 per year or couples who make less than $300,000.
