Failing to report child sexual abuse should be prosecuted under new law, inquiry finds

People who are in a position of trust and fail to report a child who is being sexually abused should be prosecuted a new report says, as its authors call for a new law to be introduced.

The sexual abuse of children is an epidemic In England and Wales with tens of thousands of victims, the major inquiry found.

The Independent Inquiry into Child Sexual Abuse (IICSA) described the sexual abuse of children as an "epidemic that leaves tens of thousands of victims in its poisonous wake", as its final report was published today.
