Engineering firms in Kansas City are busier than ever, and their projects are more complicated — and cool — all the time.

The Kansas City Business Journal asked the metro area's top engineering firms to share a recent or upcoming project that readers should know about. These "gee whiz" projects are innovative, sustainable and really, really cool.
