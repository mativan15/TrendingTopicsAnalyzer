By Reuters Published: 04:25 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 04:25 EDT, 17 October 2022 DUBAI, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Abu Dhabi sovereign wealth fund ADQ said on Monday it had made an offer to take a controlling stake in Abu Dhabi Aviation and merge the helicopter operator with ADQ stakes in Etihad Engineering, AMMROC and GAL to create a "globally competitive aviation business" with about 9.4 billion dirhams ($2.56 billion) in assets. ADA said in a stock exchange filing it would hold a board meeting on Wednesday to discuss the offer.
