The unauthorised development must be demolished (Image: Cheshire East Council)A couple has been ordered to tear down three buildings at their home after the council took action.The landowner and his wife built on the Six Acres site in Wirswall, near Whitchurch, Cheshire, without gaining planning permission.Now they must demolish them, reports Cheshire Live.The High Court injunction, obtained by Cheshire East Council, was the culmination of an eight-year planning enforcement saga.The authority first issued an enforcement notice concerning unauthorised building on the land off Wirswall Road back in 2014.
