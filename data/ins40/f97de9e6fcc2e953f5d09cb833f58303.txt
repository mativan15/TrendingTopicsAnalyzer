LONDON, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Today marks the annual launch of the Ecological Threat Report (ETR), produced by the Institute for Economics and Peace (IEP), featuring exclusive research from the Lloyd's Register Foundation World Risk Poll.

Key Findings:

Ecological threats, such as rapid population growth, water risk, and food insecurity will be exacerbated by climate change, causing mass migration and conflict

56% of 228 countries and territories monitored by the ETR are facing extreme ecological threat

The world's 40 least peaceful countries will increase their population by 1.
