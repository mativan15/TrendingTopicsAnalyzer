Pakistan approves 6% hike in defence budget for 'critical shortfalls'
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 11:15:00

AgenciesAccording to the Tribune report, the Pakistan Defence Ministry had demanded an additional PKR 80 billion defence budget for "critical shortfalls". Moreover, additions were made for spending on the Jinnah Naval base, the Naval Base Turbat and the multi-functional office building in the headquarters.The Pakistan government has increased the defence budget for the outgoing fiscal year by nearly 6 per cent to over PKR 1.45 trillion to meet the critical needs of the country's armed forces. This decision to increase the budget was taken by Pakistan's Economic Coordination Committee (ECC) to meet the enhanced salary requirements and other key needs, The Express Tribune newspaper reported.
