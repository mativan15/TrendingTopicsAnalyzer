Platforms are ramping up their social shopping features. TikTok; YouTube; Meta; Amazon; Getty; Sydney Bradley/Insider In 2022, tech giants set to bring social and livestream shopping to US customers. Motivated by the success of these efforts in China and Southeast Asia — according to eMarketer, in China alone, social and livestream shopping accounted for 14% of the country's $2.6 trillion e-commerce market in 2021 — US platforms have built infrastructure to facilitate this new way of purchasing.From TikTok to YouTube, companies introduced features to encourage users to shop in their apps — including native checkout — and shoppable livestreams, photos, and videos.
