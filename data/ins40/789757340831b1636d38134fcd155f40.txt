LIONEL MESSI has admitted he worries about missing this year's World Cup through injury.
The seven-time Ballon d'Or winner confirmed recently the Qatar tournament would be his last World Cup.
Messi has missed PSG's last two games with a calf issueCredit: AFP
The Argentine has already revealed that this will be his final World CupCredit: AFP
And the Paris Saint-Germain star has now confessed that after seeing Argentina teammates Paulo Dybala and Angel Di Maria sidelined, he fears he could be next.
