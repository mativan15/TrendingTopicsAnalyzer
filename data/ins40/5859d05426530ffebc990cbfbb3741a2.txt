VANCOUVER, BC, CANADA, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- TOWER ONE WIRELESS CORP. (CSE: TO) (OTCQB: TOWTF) (Frankfurt: 1P3N) ('Tower One' or the 'Company') is pleased to provide a construction update for the month of September 2022. During the month of September, the Company completed thirteen (13) new towers: nine (9) towers in Colombia, and four (4) towers in Ecuador with a total of forty-three (43) towers under construction in these specific countries, as well. These towers are built to suit for specific clients and will provide a total, non-cancellable rent revenue for 10 years of approximately $2,300,000 (*This figure excludes colocation revenue upside and inflation escalator terms).
