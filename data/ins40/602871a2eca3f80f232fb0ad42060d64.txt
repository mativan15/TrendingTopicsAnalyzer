October 19, 2022 05:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time BOCA RATON, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The ODP Corporation (NASDAQ:ODP), a leading provider of products and services through an integrated business-to-business (B2B) distribution platform and omnichannel presence, will announce third quarter 2022 financial results before the market open and will host a virtual investor day event beginning at 9:00 am Eastern Time on Wednesday, November 2, 2022.

Chief Executive Officer, Gerry Smith, and members of his executive leadership team will provide an in-depth review of the company's four distinct business units, growth trajectory and the company's capital allocation plans.
