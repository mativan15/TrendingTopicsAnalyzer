Once upon a time, way back in the 1970s, a young teenager used to buy hi-fi magazines and pore over their pages lusting after items like Linn Sondek LP12 turntables and the legendary Stax Lambda electrostatic headphones. There was no way that teenager could afford those devices, but they were a dream for him because they were the best. Fast forward 40 years and we find that the Linn Sondek LP12 is still considered the gold standard in turntable design but the Japanese Stax brand no longer exists.
