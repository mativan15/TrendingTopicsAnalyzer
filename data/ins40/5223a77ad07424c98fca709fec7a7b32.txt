With Upgraded Risk Control System, CBiBank, A Licensed Commercial Bank in the U.S., Protects Customers' "pocketbooks"

San Juan, Puerto Rico, June 05, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Recently, CBiBank, a licensed emerging commercial bank in the United States, upgraded its risk control system as a whole in terms of system security and transaction monitoring, in order to ensure the transaction security of customers. It is understood that safety compliance has always been the foundation of CBiBank. As one of the representatives of emerging commercial banks in the global financial market in recent years, CBiBank is a formal licensed entity bank in the United States, which is different from the general Internet payment institutions.
