(AP) -- Every once in a while, Nathan Chen and the rest of the American figure skaters who finished second in the team event at the Beijing Olympics will get on a group chat and catch up on everything happening in their lives.

Things that aren't happening, too, like a medal ceremony for the Winter Games.
