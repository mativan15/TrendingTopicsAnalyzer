The state government is providing three-time meals daily to the attendants of patients by charging a nominal Rs. 5 for each meal. We get one meal at Rs.5. It's a very good initiative for the poor people. We get sufficient, good quality food on time. We are grateful to our MLA of Karwan, Kausar Moinuddin and Telangana CM, KCR.
