Huron Consulting Names J. Ronald Dail as New Operating Chief
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 15:53:00

By Stephen Nakrosis J. Ronald Dail is expected to assume the role of Huron Consulting Group Inc.'s chief operating officer effective July 1, the company said Wednesday. He would succeed C. Mark Hussey, who would remain the company's president until he becomes chief executive and president on Jan. 1, 2023, Huron said. Mr. Dail, leader of Huron's healthcare performance improvement business unit, joined Stockamp & Associates in 2004. That firm was acquired by Huron in 2008, the company said.
