Market volatility is as much of a part of the stock market as the stocks themselves. It's always been that way, and I'm willing to bet my last two quarters that it'll always be that way. For investors, the sooner you get comfortable with volatility, the better, because you learn not to let short-term movements affect your long-term investing strategy and goals. Here's why market volatility should be an afterthought if you're a long-term investor.
