Comcast CMCSA is poised to report its Q3 2022 results on October 27. We expect Comcast's earnings to come in at about $0.91 per share, slightly ahead of consensus estimates and up about 5% compared to last year. We project that revenue will stand at about $29.7 billion, roughly in line with consensus estimates.
