Excavations for the Smart Road projects have been filled ahead of monsoon, although much of the work, including cabling, remains. Motorists are able to use only one half of the road now.
| Photo Credit: S. MAHINSHA
With the ongoing Smart Road works in the city Corporation not getting completed by the scheduled deadline ahead of the monsoon, the contractors have now temporarily filled the excavated areas. But the woes of the motorists are not over, as negotiating these mud-filled roads, which have not been re-tarred, is still tough.
