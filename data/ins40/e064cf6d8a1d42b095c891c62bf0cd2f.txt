Stocks fell early Wednesday as another round of worrisome housing data rolled in and investors turned to safer assets, walking back two days of big gains in the stock market. Major stock indexes opened lower Wednesday.Getty Images The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell 0.1%, or four points, while the S&P 500 dipped 0.3% and the tech-heavy Nasdaq dropped 0.6%. The dive follows yet another rash of poor housing data, as the Census Bureau reported—the number of housing starts, or the number of new homes breaking ground on construction, fell 8.
