‘I considered crashing my car to avoid playing' – Alison Lee reminds us elite sport can be dangerously unhealthy
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 08:00:00

If golf needed a reminder that the professional game is not all prize money, photoshoots and trophies glinting in the sunlight, it came last week when Alison Lee recalled the darkest days of her playing career.In 2020, five years after an impressive rookie season that propelled her up the world rankings and into America's Solheim Cup team, Lee had still not won a tournament. Plagued by frustration and self-doubt, she lost the confidence to make cuts and suddenly found herself back at Q School (qualifying school, where players can earn tour cards for the next season).
