CTC Global Opens New ACCC® Core Manufacturing Facility in South America

IRVINE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--Today, CTC Global Corporation announced the completion and commissioning of its fourth composite fiber core manufacturing and testing facility. The new facility, CTC Global S.A. (CSA), is located in Asuncion, Paraguay and is already serving customers in South America. The CSA facility is a fully qualified ACCC ® Core manufacturing and test facility and joins facilities in California, Indonesia and China in enabling CTC Global to serve the advanced conductor needs of utilities around the world.
