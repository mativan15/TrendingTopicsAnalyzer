MARTA tested on-demand shuttles. How popular were they?
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 19:55:00

Following a six-month pilot, MARTA is exploring how to permanently implement an on-demand service to bridge the last-mile transit gap.

The transit agency is working with Georgia Tech to analyze the results of MARTA Reach, a program tested from March through August that transported passengers in vans to and from bus and rail stations. The service provided rides for more than 8,300 customers in West Atlanta, Decatur, Avondale Estates, Belvedere Park, Forest Park, Morrow, Alpharetta and Roswell.
