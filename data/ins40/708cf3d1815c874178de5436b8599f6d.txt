Home sellers are lowering their prices at the fastest clip since 2019
cnbc.com
news
2022-05-27 13:07:16

The U.S. housing market may finally be cooling off.Nearly one in five home sellers dropped their price during the four-week period ending May 22 — the highest rate since 2019, according to a weekly Redfin study published Thursday. The same four-week period saw 13% fewer "homes for sale" browser searches on Google, and a 12% year-over-year drop in home tours and other related services from Redfin agents — the largest such decline since April 2020.Meanwhile, the Market Composite Index, which tracks mortgage purchase applications, dropped 1.
