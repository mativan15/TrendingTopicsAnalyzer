When you think about investing, your mind may automatically default to investing in stocks.  But stocks are just one of many different asset classes investors have the opportunity to put their money into. Another common type of investment you might consider adding to your portfolio: bonds. And some experts argue that this particular investment class is on the up and up and worth considering ahead of the new year. A bond is essentially a loan from you, the investor, to a corporation, government entity, or other organization.
