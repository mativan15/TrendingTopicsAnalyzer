Published: 17:00 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 17:03 EDT, 21 October 2022 The Tories have a simple question to answer in the next few days: are they still a serious, grown-up party of government? Given its reckless, chaotic, cack-handed handling of the nation's finances in recent weeks, it is by no means certain that it is.The only hope it has of convincing public opinion, at home and abroad, is by choosing Rishi Sunak as its next leader and hence our next prime minister.Any other choice would only confirm what many — from British voters to international investors — already suspect: the Tories are no longer fit to govern.
