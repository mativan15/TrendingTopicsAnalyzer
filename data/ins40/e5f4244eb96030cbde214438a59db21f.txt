Photo: AP / Evan VucciNew Zealand Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern's visit to the U.S. is proving providential in light of recent mass shootings. Following her stirring message on U.S. television the night of the attacks, she's now met with U.S. President Joe Biden, who expressed eagerness to learn from what he called her 'galvanizing' leadership that saw her country ban military-grade guns after twin mass shootings in 2019.
