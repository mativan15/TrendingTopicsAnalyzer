Bollywood actor Suniel Shetty-backed wellness solutions firm Vieroots looks to raise upto $10 million for a Series A round for the expansion of its core businesses. Founded in 2019, Bengaluru-based healthtech startup Vireroots, had got Suniel Shetty as an early stage investor in 2020.

The company uses an advanced technology called EPLIMO and is into personal consumer geonomics and epigenetic management.
