IRVINE, Calif., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Big Purple Dot, provider of an ecosystem of mortgage marketing technologies has tapped CoreLogic ®, a leading global property information, analytics, and data-enabled solutions provider, to launch a mortgage recruiting platform that provides detailed production data on loan originators (LOs) and a customer relationship management (CRM) service to help lenders in their recruiting efforts.
