Central govt sanctioned Rs 1,400 crore as grant to Puducherry, says CM
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 11:15:00

PTIPuducherry CM N RangasamyChief Minister of Puducherry N Rangasamy on Friday said the Centre sanctioned Rs 1,400 crore as against Rs 2,000 crore the Union Territory had sought for implementation of developmental schemes. He claimed the territorial government was meeting the welfare requirements of the people. He said Rs 43.85 crore was being disbursed to ration cardholders. Under the direct benefit transfer (DBT) system, cash equivalent of 10 kg free rice and 2 kg free sugar for Deepavali was being given.
