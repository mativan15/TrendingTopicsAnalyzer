No. 6 Tennessee beats No. 3 Alabama on late FG
tribdem.com
news
2022-10-16 00:15:25

KNOXVILLE, Tenn. – Chase McGrath made a 40-yard field goal as time expired to give No. 6 Tennessee a 52-49 victory over No. 3 Alabama on Saturday.As soon as the kick knuckle-balled through the uprights, some of the more than 100,000 fans stormed the field to celebrate the Volunteers (6-0, 3-0 Southeastern Conference) ending a 15-game losing streak to the Crimson Tide (6-1, 3-1). It didn't take long for the goal posts to go down.Hendon Hooker drove the Volunteers 45 yards in 18 seconds to set up the winner.
