BEVERLY HILLS, Calif., June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SimWin Sports, the premier metaverse fantasy sports league, announced that Backstreet Boys star Nick Carter has acquired a football team on its platform. Named the Tampa Bay Bullsharks, Carter's team will join a lineup of SimWin teams owned by sports and entertainment legends, including Jerry Rice, Marshall Faulk, LaMelo Ball and more.
