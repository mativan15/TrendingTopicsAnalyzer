China set to launch new youth soccer league
reuters.com
news
2022-06-02 09:13:41

June 2 (Reuters) - China is set to launch a new national youth soccer league, the country's soccer association (CFA) said, as the Asian nation looks to develop young talent and establish itself as a force in the global game.The initiative is being jointly led by the CFA, the education ministry and State General Administration of Sport (GAS), who unveiled a workplan for 2022-2024.The China Youth Football League (CYFL) will be categorised into 18 age groups -- nine each for men and women -- and will be open to all youth teams across the country.
