Tango Networks Launches eSIM Support for Mobile Unified Communications Service

October 20, 2022 10:42 AM Eastern Daylight Time FRISCO, Texas & LEEDS, England--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tango Networks today announced general availability of eSIM support for its Tango Extend service, enabling touchless activation of business lines on users' personal mobile phones.



Extend embeds a full-featured, secure and controlled business extension on employees' Bring Your Own Device phones without any apps or special configuration. Users can activate a business line in their personal phones simply by scanning a QR code to download the eSIM.
