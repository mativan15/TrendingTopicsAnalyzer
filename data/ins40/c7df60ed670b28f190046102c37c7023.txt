The story of a woman who disguised herself as a man to become a surgeon in the 1800s and a tale of a baseball player who misses his early shot at success have garnered Niles and Skokie statewide attention in a contest of up-and-coming authors.

Niles resident Alina Rubin and Skokie resident Len Joy have been named two of the three finalists in the statewide Soon to Be Famous Illinois Author Project. Rubin was chosen for her novel, 'A Girl With a Knife,' and Joy for his novel, 'American Past Time.
