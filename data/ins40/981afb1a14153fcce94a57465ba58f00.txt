October 17, 2022 11:19 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--YCharts, a cloud-based investment research platform that enables wealth advisors and asset managers to make smarter investment decisions and to orchestrate better communications with their clients, won 'Best Value' in the second annual Carson Excell Advisor Tech Awards presented by Cetera.



'Having Carson Excell recognize YCharts with this award is amazing validation of our mission,' said Sean Brown, CEO at YCharts. 'YCharts is in the constant pursuit to better support finance professionals.
