"Muted subscription and high valuations may lead to at par listing for eMudhra."New Delhi: After a muted listing of Ethos, the certifying authority player eMudhra is also headed for a soft landing at Dalal Street on Wednesday, if one goes by the initial signals from the grey market. eMudhra is scheduled to get listed at the bourses on June 1, Wednesday and the unofficial market is not much excited about the issue, particularly after the mixed listing of Ethos, Paradeep Phosphates, Delhivery and Venus Pipes.
