Comet achieves an excellent sales performance in Q3

Trading update third quarter 2022     Net sales of CHF 164.8 million in Q3/22, CHF 432.3 million after 9M/22
    Book-to-bill ratio of 1.14 at the end of Q3/22
    Guidance for FY22 confirmed 
Comet achieved strong sales of CHF 164.8 million in Q3/22 driven by continued vigorous demand from the semiconductor equipment industry (15.5% sales growth compared to Q2/22). In the first nine months, the company achieved sales of CHF 432.3 million. This acceleration underlines the continued robust demand for Comets products and the conversion of record high backlogs.
