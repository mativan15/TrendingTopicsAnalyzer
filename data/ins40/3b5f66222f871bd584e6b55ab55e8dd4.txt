How supply chain can help FMCGs solve their inventory problem
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-22 08:14:20

Fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) include food, drinks, and household items. It's a competitive industry, and India has one of the biggest marketplaces in the FMCG sector.
More than any other industry, FMCGs operate on the edge, they must instantly adapt to unanticipated developments and trends. Forecasts are clearly important to achieving this, anticipating what inventory you need, where and in what quantities is key to avoiding stock outs and ensuring optimal service levels. Not surprisingly, FMCGs tend to focus heavily on those forecasts.
