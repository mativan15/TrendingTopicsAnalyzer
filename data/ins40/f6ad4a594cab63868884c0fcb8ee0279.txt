Iranian climber Elnaz Rekabi competes in Seoul without hijab amid protests
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 10:30:00

Popular Iranian climber Elnaz Rekabi competed an international tournament in Seoul without a hijab, amid protests across Iran following the death of Mahsa Amini. Concerns are growing around her safety, and a reputed TV channel said her mobile phone and passport had been confiscated. According to reports, Elnaz Rekabi's life was in danger as she reported back to Tehran. She competed in the Asian Championships without a hijab. Various reports indicate various things, but the most common report doing rounds on the internet is that she is being taken back home secretly to avoid protests and keep her safe.
