Published: 01:28 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 01:29 EDT, 22 October 2022 Ryan Gosling is still busy filming scenes for his upcoming film The Fall Guy in Sydney.On Friday, the Canadian actor was shooting alongside his stunt double near an apartment complex in North Narrabeen.He was surrounded by cameras during the night shoot as he stepped out of a GMC truck, complete with surfboards in the tray, and walked into a beachside garage while sipping on a fast food beverage using a straw.
