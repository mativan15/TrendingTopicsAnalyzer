 Police are seeking the public's assistance in locating a vulnerable man reported missing in Oshawa, Ont.
Durham Regional Police said 33-year-old Chaise Hutchings was last seen on Oct. 14 in Oshawa.'Police and his family have concerns for his well-being and he is overdue for his needed medication,' officers said in a news release.
