Odyssey Interactive — an indie game development studio founded by ex-Riot Games development leads — has raised $19 million in a series A round. The studio released an open beta for Omega Strikers, its debut title, in September. The title is a free-to-play 3v3 competitive knockout striker. The game draws inspiration from Rocket League's format and League of Legend's character-based combat.
