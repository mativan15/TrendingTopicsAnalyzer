Why Chevy Chase's Forbright Bank sees opportunity in the Inflation Reduction Act
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 19:14:00

The bank holding company Forbright Inc. has been growing by leaps and bounds over the past year, and Chairman and CEO John Delaney is counting on renewable energy incentives in the recently passed Inflation Reduction Act to keep the momentum going.

Its subsidiary, Forbright Bank, was once known as Congressional Bank and when it rebranded last year, its stated mission was to accelerate the transition to a clean energy economy by providing financing to companies and households driving that change.
