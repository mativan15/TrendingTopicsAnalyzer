By Reuters Published: 01:08 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 01:08 EDT, 30 May 2022 BENGALURU, May 30 (Reuters) - A rally in technology stocks drove up Indian shares more than 1% on Monday, with the market also benefiting from an improvement in the global mood after China eased its COVID-19 curbs. The NSE Nifty 50 index was up 1.61% at 16,614.60 by 0427 GMT, while the S&P BSE Sensex rose 1.64% to 55,785.70. Both the indexes added more than 1% on Friday.
