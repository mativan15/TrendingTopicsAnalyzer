Harry Maguire ‘surprised' when England fans booed him but says he is his own biggest critic
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 21:30:00

Harry Maguire has opened up on the pressure of being criticised for his Manchester United performances, booed by some England fans and particularly in having to cope with a bomb threat that required the police to do a sweep of his home. Nothing suspicious was found.'I'm in a position where I'm going to be criticised,' Maguire began, talking after England training at St George's Park yesterday. 'Manchester United paid a large sum of money for me (£80 million), it is one of the most loved clubs in the world and also one of the most hated.
