When Russia invaded Ukraine on Feb. 24, the future of WLCM App Studio was plunged into doubt. All but one of the company's 25 employees are from Ukraine. Today only four of them remain in Lviv, the city where WLCM's Ukrainian office was located. The rest are refugees, some in that country, the others spread out across Europe, wondering if they will ever return home.
