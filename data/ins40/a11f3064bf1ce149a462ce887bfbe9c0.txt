Banks to develop two city circles under CSR initiative
indiatimes.com
finance
2022-10-19 05:15:00

Mangaluru: The AB Shetty Circle in the heart of the city will soon be developed through CSR funds by the Bank of Baroda in association with the Mangaluru Smart City Limited (MSCL) at an estimated cost of Rs 40 lakh. Meanwhile, the Mahaveer Circle (Pumpwell Circle) will be developed by Karnataka Bank in association with the MSCL at an estimated cost of Rs 40 lakh.DK MP Nalin Kumar Kateel laid foundation for the development of two circles and other development works worth Rs 8.5 crore to be taken up by the MSCL in the city, including development of TV Ramana Pai Hall Road at an estimated cost of Rs 2.
