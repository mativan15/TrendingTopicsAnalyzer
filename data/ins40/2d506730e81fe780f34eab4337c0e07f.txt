2 L&T firms eye 1.4 m sqft in group's office park in Chennai
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 19:45:00

Technology firms L&T Infotech( LTI) and L&T Technology Services (LTTS) are in talks to pre-lease around 1.4 million sq ft of office space in L&T's upcoming commercial office in Chennai in the largest commercial space leasing transaction this year. The lease will be spread over a term of 10 years at the L&T Innovation Campus, a mixed-use development sprawling over 40 acres. "The facility is scheduled to be delivered and be operational by the end of 2024, and can seat over 15,000 employees for the technology firms," said a person aware of the deal.
