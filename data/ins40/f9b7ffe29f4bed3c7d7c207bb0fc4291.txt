Victoria police offer $500,000 to track down man accused of sexually abusing children
theguardian.com
news
2022-05-31 04:28:33

Victoria police is offering a $500,000 reward for information that can help track down a sexual predator who targeted young girls in high-rise apartments in Melbourne during the 1980s.Police said survivors were still traumatised, nearly four decades after the horrendous assaults, and they believe there may be more victims yet to come forward.Four assaults in high-rise apartments in Melbourne's inner north are believed to be linked, detective inspector Juliann Goldrick alleged on Tuesday.A three-year-old girl was allegedly sexually assaulted in a Richmond building's clothes drying room on 24 October 1982.
