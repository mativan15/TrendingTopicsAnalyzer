Doron Gerstel, CEO, will present at the conference and host one-on-one meetings

TEL AVIV & NEW YORK, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Perion Network Ltd. (NASDAQ:PERI) a global advertising technology company that delivers holistic solutions across the three main pillars of digital advertising – ad search, social media and display / video / CTV advertising – announced today that it will participate in the Stifel 2022 Cross Sector Insight Conference on June 7th in Boston.

Doron Gerstel, Perion's CEO will participate in a fire-side chat and will be available for one-on-one meetings with attending investors on that day.
