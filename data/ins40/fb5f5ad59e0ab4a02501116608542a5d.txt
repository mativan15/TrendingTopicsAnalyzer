Anbumani Ramadoss will be new PMK president
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 09:15:00

Former Union Health Minister Dr Anbumani Ramadoss has been named the new president of the Pattali Makkal Katchi (PMK). Ramadoss will replace GK Mani who helmed the position for the last 25 years.

A special executive committee meeting of the PMK held on Saturday elected Anbumani Ramadoss as the party president and a resolution to this effect was adopted at the party executive meeting at Tiruverkadu in Chennai.

Anbumani Ramadoss is the son of PMK founder leader and ideologue Dr Ramadoss. The incumbent president is a medical doctor just like his father and has been active in politics at the grassroots level for the past twenty-five years.
