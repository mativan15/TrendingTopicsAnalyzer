Photo_Concepts/Getty Images Insider has been analyzing how much CEOs and workers in the drug and biotech industries get paid. Using exclusive data from the company-intelligence firm, MyLogIQ, Insider identified the 21 companies with the highest median employee pay for 2021.These 21 biotech and pharma companies all reported median employee pay of at least $350,000 last year. All the companies on the list were smaller biotechs with less than 600 employees. The vast majority also have a specific research focus, ranging from neuroscience to cancer to eye diseases.
