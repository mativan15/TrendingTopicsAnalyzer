Here's why you should spend less, invest more this festive season
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 05:15:00

What's with the festive season and discounts? It nudges you to buy brand new items which you may or may not need. We do get instant pleasure having such items, but most of these purchases lose value over time. What about buying something that appreciates in value? Yes, that's how you should kick off this festive season. What is it whose value will appreciate over time? Investments. Let's focus on investments over expenses as the festivities begin this year. Just as buying stocks on Muhurat trading on Diwali day is considered a good omen, start investing for your key financial goals from your preferred festival day.
