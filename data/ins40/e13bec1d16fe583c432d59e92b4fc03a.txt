Citi Strategists Say Profit Downgrades Pose Risk to Equity Rally

2022-06-01 09:17:28

(Bloomberg) -- After a difficult first five months of 2022, the pain may not be over yet for global equity markets, according to Citigroup Inc. strategists.The prospect of downward revisions to earnings estimates is the latest headwind to face stock investors, already rattled by runaway inflation and the potential impact of central-bank tightening aimed at controlling it, the strategists led by Jamie Fahy wrote in a note.With quantitative tightening yet to hit full stride, negative economic data and profit revisions will bring increased realized volatility, 'much lower' expected returns, and 'most ominously, much larger left-tail risk' for stocks, they wrote, adding: 'We are sellers of rallies rather than buyers of dips.
