Toronto Stocks Rise; Corus Entertainment Slides as Lower Advertising Revenue Weighs on 4Q
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 16:01:00

By Adriano Marchese Toronto-listed stocks were higher midday Friday after a rocky morning, on track to post a weekly gain despite a pullback earlier in the week. In the session, most sectors were posting gains, led by process industries and materials. The consumer services sector was the outsized laggard, followed by technology services and consumer durables. Shares of Corus Entertainment Inc. fell after the company reported a swing-to-a-loss in its fiscal fourth quarter as revenue fell in the period.
