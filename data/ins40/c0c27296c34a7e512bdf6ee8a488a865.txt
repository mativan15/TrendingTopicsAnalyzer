Techno-horror is a uniquely terrifying genre of film, especially when you're watching it in 2022. It takes the tech we've grown accustomed to, that's permeating our lives -- in how we work, how we communicate, how we have fun -- and melds it with some of our worst fears. What if a demon began wreaking havoc during a Zoom call with friends? What if dying in a video game meant dying in real life?Many of you are already aware of the likes of Black Mirror, the dystopian TV show. Other favorites in the genre include David Cronenberg's Videodrome and even the holiday cult classic Gremlins.
