You'd be forgiven for asking, how relevant now is Data Privacy Day? The online universe definitely looked a lot scarier in 2007 - when Data Privacy Day first launched - than it does today. Our digital lives are more real, more active, and consumers and businesses both understand the need to protect themselves and their customers, now that data is worth its weight in gold. Whether it's our everyday data for online banking or ecommerce, or business-level security around regulations or ethical AI, we're probably safer than we've ever been.
