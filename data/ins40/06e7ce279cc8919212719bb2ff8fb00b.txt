Apple to Launch a Foldable IPad Rather Than iPhone in 2024, Analyst Predicts
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-17 16:04:17

Apple will likely launch an iPad with a folding screen in 2024, analyst firm CCS Insight said on Tuesday. CCS Insight said a foldable iPad would likely come before a folding iPhone, bucking the trend of consumer electronics companies launching folding screen smartphones. The analyst firm also predicts that Apple is likely to integrate its own 5G modem into the A series of processor for a "single-chip" solution for iPhones in 2025.
