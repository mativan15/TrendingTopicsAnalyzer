EDMONTON, AB, May 27, 2022 /CNW/ - Canadian Western Bank (CWB) today announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.31 per common share (TSX: CWB), payable on June 23, 2022 to shareholders of record on June 9, 2022. This quarterly dividend is up one cent, or 3%, from the dividends declared last quarter and one year ago. The Board of Directors also declared quarterly cash dividends for preferred shares of $0.2688125 per Series 5 and $0.375 per Series 9, payable on July 31, 2022 to shareholders of record on July 22, 2022.
