China's Lu Kaiman pulled off a major upset, beating reigning world champion Anna Korakaki of Greece 16-10 in the women's 10m air pistol final at the 2022 ISSF World Championship Rifle/Pistol in Cairo, Egypt, on Saturday.Korakaki was the top shooter in the ranking match to earn her place in the gold-medal contest against Lu.The 22-year-old Lu lay down the early marker taking a four-point lead, with Korakaki making regular fightback attempts to get within two points. While Korakaki seemed out of kilter, the Chinese shooter remained cool, calm and collected, surging to a 14-6 lead.
