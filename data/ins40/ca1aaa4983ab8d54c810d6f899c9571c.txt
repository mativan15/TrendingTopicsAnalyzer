Brandon Bell/Getty Images News Shopify (NYSE:SHOP) has had a very difficult year, to say the least. After the e-commerce giant received a huge growth boost in 2020 and 2021 as businesses went exponentially more digital, shares plummeted nearly 85% this year back to 2019 levels, erasing all gains. That said, we will employ several valuation methods to explain why Shopify may have bottomed out and why we believe it currently seems to have an attractive valuation. There are also numerous factors that could give the stock its well-deserved rebound.
