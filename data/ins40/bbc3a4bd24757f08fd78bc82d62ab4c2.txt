UK battles to keep Jaguar Land Rover's planned EV production
theguardian.com
news
2022-05-27 15:57:25

Britain is locked in a battle to hold on to production of Jaguar Land Rover's future range of electric vehicles, amid concerns the UK is falling behind in the race to build vital large-scale battery factories.The automaker, which is owned by the Indian conglomerate Tata, said it continues to 'explore all options' around battery supply amid reports it could build electric cars in Slovakia.Bloomberg reported JLR is considering buying batteries from Sweden's Northvolt AB or China's SVolt Energy Technology for a range of electric cars that it may assemble in Slovakia.
