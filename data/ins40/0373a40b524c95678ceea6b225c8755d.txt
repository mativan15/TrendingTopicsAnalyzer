Intelligent Healthcare: How AI and Digital Health are accelerating Indian Healthcare System to greater heights
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 09:48:29

In a rapidly transforming healthcare landscape, digital transformation has become crucial to drive clinical care delivery, operational efficiencies and enhance patient care. The pandemic has further pushed digital transformation as a key priority for healthcare providers in India. From virtual care to remote monitoring to the use of AI, the healthcare industry is witnessing a massive shift to tech-enabled care that's allowing personalised diagnosis and treatment, smarter and more informed decisions and bringing patients closer to precision health.
