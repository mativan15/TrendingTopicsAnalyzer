Steelworkers Union Calls for Criminal Investigation, Public Inquiry Into Deadly Come By Chance Refinery Explosion

2022-10-18 00:00:00

October 18, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time COME BY CHANCE, Newfoundland and Labrador--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The United Steelworkers union (USW) is calling on the RCMP's Major Crimes Unit to launch a criminal investigation into the catastrophic explosion on Sept. 2 at the Braya Renewable Fuels refinery in Come By Chance.



The USW, which represents hundreds of workers at the refinery, also is urging the Newfoundland and Labrador government to launch a formal inquiry into the disaster, which killed one worker and injured seven others.
