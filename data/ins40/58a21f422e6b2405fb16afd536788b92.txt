By Reuters Published: 13:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 13:30 EDT, 15 October 2022 Basketball Hall of Famer Dikembe Mutombo is being treated for a brain tumor, the NBA confirmed Saturday. "He is receiving the best care possible from a collaborative team of specialists in Atlanta and is in great spirits as he begins treatment," the league said in a statement. "Dikembe and his family ask for privacy during this time so they can focus on his care. They are grateful for your prayers and good wishes.
