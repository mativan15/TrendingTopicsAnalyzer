AgenciesThe man presented lesions compatible with monkeypox on Thursday, a day after arriving in the country.Argentina reported a case of the monkeypox virus on Friday in a man who recently travelled to Spain. It also reported a suspected case of the rare virus in another person. A man from the province of Buenos Aires has monkeypox, Argentina's health ministry said in a statement. An official in the ministry earlier confirmed the positive case but said health authorities were waiting to finish sequencing the virus before making the official announcement.
