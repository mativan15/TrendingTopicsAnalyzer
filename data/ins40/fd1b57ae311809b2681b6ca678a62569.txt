Published: 05:15 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 05:17 EDT, 19 October 2022 Social media is causing petty playground spats among primary school pupils to escalate into serious violence - and even murder, a major report has found.Arguments are continuing online after the school day has finished and 'amplifying' as children are goaded by their peers watching online.Rather than petering out naturally, researchers said there was an expectation for the threats to be then carried out in the real world.
