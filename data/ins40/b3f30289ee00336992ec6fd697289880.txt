Jira Pliankharom/iStock via Getty Images The article is primarily based on technical analysis, which I use personally to assess if a bounce is likely in a downtrend. In the past 9 weeks, the S&P 500 (NYSEARCA:SPY) has had a handful of positive days. The downtrend might make a temporary bottom, but to have a meaningful turnaround for equities, several headwinds will have to become tailwinds. That is unlikely to happen until inflation comes down meaningfully and sequentially. Once inflation falls for multiple months, the Fed could change forward guidance.
