You don't need bulky gear or a big budget for a big screen. With a small portable projector you can have a movie night inside a pillow fort, in the backyard or even out in the wilderness. The best options can fit easily in any backpack, and some are small enough to fit in your pocket.  Whether you project onto a blank wall or a projector screen, you can capture the picture quality you crave without getting weighed down. They also offer a handful of connectivity options including HDMI and Bluetooth, and they typically run on batteries.
