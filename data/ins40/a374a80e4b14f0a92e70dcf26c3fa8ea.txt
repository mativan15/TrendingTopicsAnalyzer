Renewables company waves goodbye to its tidal business
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 23:01:00

A renewable energy developer has sold a majority stake in its tidal engineering division. Simec Atlantis Energy said that managers of its Advanced Tidal Engineering and Services division were buying it out.The unit is being renamed Proteus Marine Renewables and plans to widen its scope beyond the tidal sector. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed.Simec Atlantis Energy is keeping a minority holding in the business and expects to continue to collaborate on projects including Meygen, the world's largest tidal array, off the north of Scotland.
