Want More Responses to Your Job Ads? Tout Your Company's Diversity, Huge New Stanford Study Suggests
inc.com
business
2022-06-02 08:44:00

With the job market tighter than it's been in years, companies are desperately seeking any edge that will help them compete for talent. Options like higher salaries and more career progression are great, but a new study from researchers at Stanford, Harvard, Cornell, and Southern Methodist University suggest one simple but effective way to be more appealing that many firms could implement at no cost: Simply tout your company's diverse workforce more in your job ads.  There's certainly plenty of chatter about the benefits of working on diverse teams, and plenty of cultural momentum toward prioritizing diversity.
