Delhi air quality worsens, 24-hour average index recorded at 262
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 15:00:00

Air quality in the national capital worsened on Friday with the 24-hour average AQI recorded at 262, the Central Pollution Control Board said. The maximum temperature was recorded at 33.5 degrees Celsius on Friday, according to the India Meteorological Department. The minimum temperature was recorded at 17.3 degrees Celsius, a notch below normal. The weather office has predicted mainly clear skies on Saturday. The Central Pollution Control Board's 24-hour average AQI bulletin on Friday said the index value was in the 'poor' category at 268.
