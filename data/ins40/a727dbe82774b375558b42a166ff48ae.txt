New TV Web Platform, RUN3TV, Adds Server-Side Ad Insertion Functionality and AdTech Platform IntegrationsWASHINGTON, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- At NAB Show New York, Pearl TV, the coalition of U.S. broadcast companies transitioning to NEXTGEN TV, today announced expanded support for digital video advertising technology in its Web TV platform, RUN3TV. It brings digital advertising capabilities equivalent to the web and connected TV, and is a key driver for broadcaster's return on investment in NEXTGEN TV services.
