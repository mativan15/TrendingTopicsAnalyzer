October 19, 2022 04:23 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Rep. Dusty Johnson (R-S.D.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Rep. Johnson has a lifetime rating of 97 percent, making him a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero.' In 2021, Rep. Johnson scored a perfect 100 percent, earning him a 'Taxpayer Super Hero' award.
