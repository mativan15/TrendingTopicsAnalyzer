We're going hands-on with the FiiO K9 Pro ESS version, comparing it side-by-side to its predecessor (Photo: IBTimes / Jeff Li)FiiO shook the audiophile world in 2021 with their release of their first high-end, desktop grade DAC/Amp - the K9 Pro. However due to the global shortage of AKM chips, for a while the K9 Pro was a rare collector's item. However, FiiO very quickly caught up in early 2022, with the re-release of the K9 Pro, this time equipped with dual ES9038PRO chips as well as some cosmetic differences.
