Joe Raedle/Getty Images News Carvana Co. (NYSE:CVNA) is the leading e-commerce platform for trading used cars. In just 8 years, it is clearly a disrupter becoming the second-largest company in the industry. Recently the stock is under tremendous pressure given headwinds of the macroenvironment and the company's logistical issues. The used car market is historically very volatile, CVNA is not immune to it. Future uncertainty still remains but the growth capability and innovative culture of CVNA are undeniable.
