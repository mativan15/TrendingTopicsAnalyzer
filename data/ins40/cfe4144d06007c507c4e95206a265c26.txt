Amazon warehouse workers in NY state vote against union
pressfrom.info
news
2022-10-18 17:14:32

Amazon (AMZN) workers at a warehouse near Albany, New York voted against joining the Amazon Labor Union (ALU), in a blow to a union that had a historic victory at JFK8 in Staten Island earlier this year.The final count was 406 against the union, and 206 for the union, with 31 challenged ballots.
