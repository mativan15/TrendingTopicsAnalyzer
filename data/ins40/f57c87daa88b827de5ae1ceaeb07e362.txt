How the Queen has embraced technology over the years

The Queen's life has been steeped in tradition, but she has kept up with the vast technological advances during her reign of nearly 70 years.The 96-year-old monarch took part in her first official video conference call in 2020 as part of her public duties, following in the footsteps of other royals as engagements went online during the pandemic.In the early years of her reign, she recorded her first televised Christmas broadcast in 1957 and spoke of the 'speed at which things change around us'.
