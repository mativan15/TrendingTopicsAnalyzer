Truss held talks with Graham Brady before appearing stoney faced
express.co.uk
politics
2022-10-17 17:07:00

Liz Truss had crunch talks with Chair of the 1922 Committee Graham Brady, before appearing stoney faced in front of MPs. Penny Mordaunt, answering an urgent question on Ms Truss' behalf, told MPs that Ms Truss had "urgent business" to attend to. While it is claimed the meeting was "pre-planned", a source told Express.co.uk that Ms Truss had been expected to be in the House of Commons Chamber at 3.30pm to hear Jeremy Hunt address MPs.She only became "detained on urgent business" after Labour tabled the urgent question demanding a statement from the Prime Minister.
