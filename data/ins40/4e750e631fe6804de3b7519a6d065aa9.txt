Acquisition adds complex commercial and personal insurance expertise, continuing NFP's growth in the United Kingdom

NEW YORK and LONDON, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- NFP, a leading property and casualty broker, benefits consultant, wealth manager, and retirement plan advisor, today announced it has acquired Mason James Insurance Services Limited (doing business as Bentley Insurance Services), one of the largest brokers in Staffordshire. Bentley has provided complex commercial and personal insurance solutions since 1922.
