ET CONTRIBUTORSAnalysts said the year-on-year comparison of the volumes is not meaningful due to the low-base effect owing to the second Covid wave-induced lockdowns last year.NEW DELHI: Marriage season across the country is likely to bring cheer for auto companies, especially those who make two-wheelers (2Ws) for the second consecutive month in May, said analysts tracking the industry. They also see commercial vehicle (CV) makers reporting strong sales. Passenger vehicle makers are also likely to deliver sequential growth in sales.
