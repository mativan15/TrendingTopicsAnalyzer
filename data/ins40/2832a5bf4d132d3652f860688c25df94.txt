ReutersRamkrishna approached SAT after Sebi, through an order dated February 11, slapped a penalty of Rs 3 crore on her for alleged governance lapses in a case related to the appointment of Anand Subramanian as the Group Operating OfficerThe Securities Appellate Tribunal (SAT) has given more time to former NSE chief Chitra Ramkrishna for depositing an amount of Rs 2 crore in a case pertaining to governance lapses at the bourse. "The period to deposit the money by our order dated 11th April, 2022 is extended for a further period of four weeks," the appellate tribunal said in an order passed on May 31.
