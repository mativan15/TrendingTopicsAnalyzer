'Now is the time': Premiers urge federal government to talk health-care dollars
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-05-27 23:30:21

REGINA - The federal government needs to come to the table to discuss more money for health care, Western Canada's premiers said at their annual meeting. British Columbia Premier John Horgan said Friday at the meeting in Regina that health-care services are not sustainable coming out of the COVID-19 pandemic. 'We're now concerned about accessing the system because of the frailty of the providers and the inability to meet the expectations of the public,' Horgan said. 'Now is the time for a new vision in health care in Canada.
