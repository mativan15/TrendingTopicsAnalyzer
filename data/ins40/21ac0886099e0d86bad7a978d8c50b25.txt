VodaIdea CEO suggests distributing unsold spectrum at certain prices
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 07:30:00

Vodafone Idea, MD and CEO, Ravinder TakkarVodafone Idea CEO Ravinder Takkar suggested that the telecom department should consider distributing the unsold spectrum at certain fixed prices to mobile operators, reported TOI. The suggestion comes ahead of the 5G auctions which are scheduled to take place in June-September in India. The Digital Communications Commission (DCC) has supported the telecom department's plan to auction 5G airwaves at their base price. However, the tenure for spectrum usage is still being deliberated upon.
