Published: 17:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 17:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 New England Patriots rookie Bailey Zappe may start again at quarterback Monday night. Or he might not. As incumbent Mac Jones recovers from a high-ankle sprain that has sidelined him since Week 3, perhaps putting him in line for a return, Zappe is only worried about what he can control: his knowledge of New England's opponent, the visiting Chicago Bears. "I will study the game plan the same way I would whether I was third string or a starter," Zappe said.
