3 Ways Companies Can 'Adapt to Endure' Tough Times, According to Sequoia Capital
inc.com
business
2022-05-27 17:08:00

The world economy is in a "crucible moment"--and businesses need to adapt for more change to come.That's according to a leaked, 52-page memo that the venture capital firm Sequoia Capital presented to its portfolio companies on May 16, The Information first reported. In the presentation--titled "Adapting to Endure"--the VC firm explains that it's not yet time to panic, but businesses should think critically about how they can prepare for economic downturn. Planning for the worst will help companies to "avoid the death spiral.
