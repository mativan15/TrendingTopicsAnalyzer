When President Joe Biden announced in August that his administration would be taking steps to cancel some amount of student-loan debt for millions of Americans, the news was met with a variety of reactions.
There was excitement from some borrowers, frustration from those who felt the plan didn't go far enough, and concerns that the cancellation might add to inflation when prices have already risen significantly.
