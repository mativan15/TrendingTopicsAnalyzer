High street banks like HSBC, Lloyds and Nationwide are offering up to £170 for people switching current account, but beware as some of the deals have strings attachedBanks are giving generous bungs to switchers, Martin Lewis' MoneySavingExpert said Martin Lewis' MoneySavingExpert is telling Brits that switching their bank account can bag them up to £170. The latest MoneySavingExpert newsletter says more than two-thirds (70%) of Brits have never swapped bank account - or at least not in the last five years.
