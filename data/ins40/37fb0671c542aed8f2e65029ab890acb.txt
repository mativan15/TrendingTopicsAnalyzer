The former VP of Product and Engineering at Enjoy Technology joins the proptech startup to transform Stellar's technology-driven marketplace DALLAS–(BUSINESS WIRE)–October 19, 2022–
Stellar, a venture-backed technology-driven marketplace which solves maintenance at scale for the Single-Family Rental (SFR) market, today announced the appointment of Renaud Casanova as Chief Technology Officer and member of Stellar's executive team, effective September 19, 2022. Mr. Casanova is a technology leader with more than 20 years of experience leading high-growth businesses.
