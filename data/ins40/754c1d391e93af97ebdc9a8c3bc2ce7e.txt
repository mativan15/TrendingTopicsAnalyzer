Tim Ryan asks why Democrats ‘don't smell blood' and help him in Ohio
washingtonpost.com
politics
2022-10-19 22:39:42

National Democrats are heaping praise on their nominee for U.S. Senate in Ohio, lauding Rep. Tim Ryan's spirited debate performances, his outside-the box television ads, his aggressive campaign schedule and his robust fundraising.But they're not spending big money in his race — prompting Ryan and his allies to complain that national party strategists involved with funding decisions are falling to adequately fund his unexpectedly competitive campaign against Republican J.D. Vance for a seat the GOP is hoping to hold onto in November.
