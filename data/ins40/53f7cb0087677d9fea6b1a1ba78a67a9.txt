Hedge fund Tiger Global beaten up by tech selloff: CNBC
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 15:59:08

Tiger Global Management, a hedge fund run by Chase Coleman, has lost more than 50% of its value in 2022 including a 14.3% drop in May, according a report by CNBC. The losses came after Tiger Global bet on tech stocks in the first quarter including Snowflake Inc. SNOW, +7.25%, Sea Ltd. SE, +4.71% and Carvana Co. CVNA, +11.10%, CBNC reported, citing regulatory filings. Snowflake has lost 60% of its value in 2022, while Carvana is off by nearly 88% and Sea is down by 63%. The Nasdaq COMP, +1.35% has lost about 22% of its value this year.
