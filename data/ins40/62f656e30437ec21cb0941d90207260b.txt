Wastewater isn't a burden, it's a valuable resource
medium.com
entertainment
2022-10-20 18:14:25

Wastewater can be put to good useAbout 80% of wastewater dumped into the environment globally does not receive the required treatment. This harms our ecosystems and endangers the general public's health. It is wise to use wastewater that has undergone sufficient treatment to help ease the water crisis because water demand is rising across all industries, from agriculture to industry.Although it may be a basic human necessity, more than 785 million people worldwide do not have access to even the most basic water services today.
