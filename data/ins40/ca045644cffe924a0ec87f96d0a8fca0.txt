Avacta Group Raises GBP7 Mln via Share Placing, Management Subscription
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 11:31:00

By Ian Walker Avacta Group PLC said Tuesday that it has now raised 7 million pounds ($8 million) via the share placing and management subscription announced earlier. The oncology drug and diagnostics company said that it has issued 7.4 million new shares under the fund raising at 95 pence each. The price is a 4.0% discount to the company's closing price of 99.0 pence on Monday. Chairman Eliot Forster has subscribed for 15,000 new shares at the offer price. Earlier Tuesday Avacta said that it was buying Launch Diagnostics Holdings Ltd.
