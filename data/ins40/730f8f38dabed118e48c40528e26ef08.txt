7:15 AM ETENGLEWOOD, Colo. -- If there is one signature moment that encompasses the Denver Broncos' frustrating season thus far, it has been authored by wide receiver KJ Hamler.Hamler took off his helmet and slammed it into the turf over and over after the Broncos failed to score a game-winning touchdown in the final seconds of a dismal loss to the Indianapolis Colts on Oct. 6.The Broncos rarely score touchdowns this season -- six through five games -- and the lack of consistency offers little hope for things turning around.
