Agrify Shares Drop 40% After 1-For-10 Reverse Stock Split
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 16:02:00

By Kathryn Hardison Shares of Agrify Corp. plunged 40%, to $6.41, on Tuesday after shares began trading on a split-adjusted basis following a one-for-10 reverse stock split. The company, which provides innovative cultivation and extraction solutions for the cannabis industry, said Monday that the reverse split is intended to increase the per-share trading price to satisfy the minimum bid requirement to remain listed on the Nasdaq Capital Market. The reverse stock split will reduce the number of shares from approximately 26.
