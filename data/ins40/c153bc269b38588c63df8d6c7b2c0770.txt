News Corp. closes $295 million acquisition of Base Chemicals (NASDAQ:NWS)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-01 15:25:29

Kevin Hagen/Getty Images News News Corp. (NASDAQ:NWS) (NASDAQ:NWSA) has closed its $295 million acquisition of the Base Chemicals business from S&P Global Market Intelligence. It will add the business under the name Chemical Market Analytics by OPIS, a Dow Jones company. UK antitrust regulators had required IHS Markit to divest the Oil Price Information Service and Base Chemicals as part of its merger with S&P Global. News Corp. had acquired OPIS in February, when IHS Markit and S&P Global wrapped their merger.
