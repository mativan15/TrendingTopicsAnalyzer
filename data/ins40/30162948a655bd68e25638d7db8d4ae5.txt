Korean dairy industry down in the dumps as Purmil readies to bow out

2022-10-21 13:51:00

A shopper browses the dairy aisle at a large mart in Seoul on Sept. 12. [NEWS1] As Purmil, a dairy company formerly under Lotte, announced that it will officially close business at the end of next month, the dairy industry is on high alert should the situation affect the entire industry. Purmil on Monday announced that it will close its business on Nov. 30, and sent a company-wide email to its approximately 350 employees informing them that they would be let go. Purmil, which stood at No. 10 in the domestic dairy industry in terms of market share in 2020, was known for its popular products such as "Milk with Black Soybean" and "Ganachoco Milk.
