Delhi chief minister and Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) national convenor Arvind Kejriwal on Wednesday urged Prime Minister Narendra Modi to collaborate with his government in developing schools across the nation.He said the authorities managing the Delhi schools have gained experience and if all governments cooperate, all state-run schools can become excellent in just five years.
