Community Capital Bancshares to pay dividends

May 30—ALBANY — The Board of Directors of AB&T's parent company, Community Capital Bancshares Inc., announced payment of a 30-cent dividend for each share of common stock held, following the bank's solid 2021 performance, at the board's annual shareholder meeting at the bank's main office on Meredyth Drive.

"That the company resumes a payment of dividends to its shareholders reinforces the strength, stability and sustainability of the bank," AB&T CEO Perry Revell said. "Our people are a force for good.
