Published: 10:57 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 11:12 EDT, 18 October 2022 Less than 48 hours since the dust settled on last weekend's action, it is already time to gear up for another round of Premier League fixtures with nine games to come over the next three days.Top-flight chiefs have crammed in a set of midweek games for fans to feast on ahead of the Qatar World Cup, which will bring club football to a halt when it gets underway next month, although Arsenal and Manchester City are not involved after their clash was postponed to accommodate the former's rescheduled Europa League tie against PSV Eindhoven.
