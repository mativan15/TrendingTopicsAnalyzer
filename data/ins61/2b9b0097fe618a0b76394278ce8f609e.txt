Union TrustPhiladelphia, Pennsylvania (October 20, 2022) 6:45 P.M. EDT THE PRESIDENT: Hello, everybody. (Applause.) My name is Joe Biden. I'm Jill Biden's husband. (Laughter and applause.) I married a — I married a Philly girl. (Laughter.) And I tell you what — she is, like most of you, a rabid, rabid, rabid Phillies fan. (Laughter and applause.) A rabid Eagles fan. (Applause.) And as Bobby knows, she is also rabid — she says she hates violence, but she loves hockey. (Laughter.) I don't know, man.
