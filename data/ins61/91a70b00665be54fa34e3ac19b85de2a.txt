Grant Shapps to replace Suella Braverman as UK interior minister

Reuters"I have made a mistake, I accept responsibility; I resign," Braverman said in letter to Truss posted on Twitter.Former transport minister Grant Shapps will replace Suella Braverman as Britain's interior minister, the BBC reported on Wednesday. Earlier, adding to the turmoil surrounding Prime Minister Liz Truss's six-week premiership, Suella Braverman left the job as interior minister, saying she had to go after she breached government rules.

She was the second senior minister to leave the government in less than a week, Braverman's departure heaps yet more pressure on Truss as she fights to stay in power just over six weeks after she entered Downing Street.
