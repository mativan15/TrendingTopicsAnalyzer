Toople set to topple after failed plan for takeover
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 23:01:00

A telecoms specialist serving small businesses is set to fall into administration after failing to raise capital for a possible takeover.Toople had hoped to expand its business providing phone lines and broadband services to small companies by acquiring a rival company. It had identified a credible acquisition target, but was unable to raise the funding required to complete the deal and to provide the working capital needed to run a larger group.DMS Holding, Toople's subsidiary business, provides communication services delivered via large providers including BT Business, EE, TalkTalk and O2.
