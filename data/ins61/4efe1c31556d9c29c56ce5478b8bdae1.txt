Image source: Getty Images Gemini and Coinbase are both making major cuts to their budgets. Has crypto crumbled? Key points Many large cryptocurrency-centric organizations believe the crypto market is in a "crypto winter," forecasting low coin prices for an extended period.Gemini is cutting a tenth of its staff, while Coinbase is extending its hiring pause and halting certain yet-to-start prospects.The decisions these two organizations are making do not suggest these companies will go out of business, but it does suggest that crypto prices may not recover for some time.
