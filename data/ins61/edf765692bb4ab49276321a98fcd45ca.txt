According to the airport's online arrivals board the budget operator has cancelled flights from European destinations including Madrid, Palermo, Malta and Milan as well two domestic flights from Scotland. Wizz Air also cancelled three flights from Mikonos, Milan and Catania.The latest cancellations come with thousands of travellers still stranded abroad after Easyjet said it had to cut 80 flights on Sunday and apologised to customers for the disruption.'We are very sorry and fully understand the disruption this will have caused for our customers,' the airline said in a statement, adding it was doing everything possible to get passengers to their destinations.
