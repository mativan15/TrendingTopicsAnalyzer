Graham Potter 'not surprised' by Trevoh Chalobah England links
bbc.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 14:14:59

Graham Potter says he is "not surprised" that defender Trevoh Chalobah is being linked with an England call-up.The 23-year-old has started the past three Premier League games for Chelsea and has also featured for England Under-21s, although he has yet to be capped at senior level."He has been very impressive from day one, as he wasn't starting in the first couple of games but acted perfectly," said Potter. "He trained really well, very professional and determined. He is an impressive character who is very focused on his football.
