Brad Greenwood, George Mason University (THE CONVERSATION) The day after an armed 18-year-old entered the Robb Elementary School in Uvalde, Texas, and shot dead 19 children and two teachers, the share prices of gun and weapons manufacturers jumped. A week on, and the market rally of gun stocks following the latest mass shooting hasn't subsided. As of the close of trading on May 31, 2022, the stock price of weapons maker Sturm Ruger was up more than 6.
