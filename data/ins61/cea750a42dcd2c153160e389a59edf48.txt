REUTERS/Sergio MoraesHOUSTON, Oct 17 (Reuters) - Exxon Mobil Corp (XOM.N) said on Monday that it left Russia completely after President Vladimir Putin expropriated its properties following seven months of discussions over an orderly transfer of its 30% stake in a major oil project.Exxon did not say if it received any compensation for the assets, which it had valued at more than $4 billion.
