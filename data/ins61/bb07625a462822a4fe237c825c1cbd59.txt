YOUR iPhone is packed with clever tricks – including "hidden buttons" lurking on your Home Screen.
Even if you're a long-time iPhone user, you might not be making the best use of your handset.
Have you ever noticed these hidden iPhone menus?Credit: Apple / The Sun
It might surprise you to learn that your Home Screen is littered with hidden buttons.
They unlock secret menus for apps, letting you instantly access some of the best features.
Apple calls the feature Quick Actions, and you may have accidentally found it while moving an app.
