By Associated Press Published: 10:04 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 10:13 EDT, 29 May 2022 PHOENIX (AP) - Arizona Gov. Doug Ducey is in Israel for five days of talks with political and business leaders of the Middle Eastern country. Ducey arrived in Israel on Sunday morning, accompanied by the heads of the Arizona Commerce Authority and the Arizona Chamber of Commerce and Industry. Ducey spokesperson C.J. Karamargin said the Republican governor's meetings will focus on trade, water and border security.
