Tesla 3Q profit more than doubles from a year ago
stuff.co.nz
news
2022-10-20 19:18:21

Tesla reported Wednesday that its third-quarter profit more than doubled from a year ago, fuelled by higher vehicle sales. The Austin, Texas, electric vehicle and solar panel maker posted net income of US$3.29 billion ($NZ5.64 billion) from July through September. CEO Elon Musk said on the company's quarterly conference call that the Tesla is considering a US$5 billion to US$10 billion stock buyback next year, even if the economy stumbles.Tesla released details of its quarterly financial performance in a challenging environment.
