Dog's before and after weight loss photos delight internet: "No treats"

2022-10-17 12:53:39

One dog's weight loss journey has been praised online after his before and after transformation pictures went viral.Chester the dog's owner said on Reddit that they went "overboard" on feeding him when he was first adopted. But after some hard work and lots of love, Chester's back to a healthy size."The veterinarian said he needed to lose weight. I present to you, dechonkified Chester," his owner shared in a post on the subreddit r/aww, where it gained over 25,000 upvotes so far."He didn't like the journey but never takes food for granted after the diet he had to endure," they added.
