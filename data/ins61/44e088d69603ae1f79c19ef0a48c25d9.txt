West Bengal gears up to tackle cyclone ‘Sitrang'; Odisha on high alert as well
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 12:16:00

With the West Bengal coast expected to fall within the trajectory of cyclone ‘Sitrang', the state government has already started preparing for it, with chief secretary HK Dwivedi convening a meeting on Friday with senior officials of south Bengal districts, which may bear the brunt of the cyclone, and top officials of various departments such as irrigation, power, police, health and agriculture among others. 'Preparations have already started. The cyclone and flood shelters are being readied. Essential items such as food, medicines and drinking water are being stored.
