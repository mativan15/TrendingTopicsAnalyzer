October 19, 2022 10:47 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kroo, a national provider of 5G connectivity solutions for commercial real estate (CRE), announces an exclusive multi-year agreement with Panda Express delivering 5G technology and long-term value to locations, guests, and communities through their rooftops.



The high-tech assets allow Panda to be a community hub and deliver connectivity to underserved communities, furthering the restaurant brand's commitment to build positive and meaningful connections with its guests.
