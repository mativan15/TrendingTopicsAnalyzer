Wolfspeed, Applied Materials rise as William Blair analyzes silicon carbide sector
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-17 13:30:09

TomekD76 Wolfspeed (NYSE:WOLF), ON Semiconductor (NASDAQ:ON) and Applied Materials (AMAT) were among the semiconductor companies that rose on Monday as investment firm William Blair initiated coverage on the silicon carbide industry, noting it appears poised to disrupt significant parts of the power analog segment of the chip market. Analyst Jed Dorsheimer started coverage on Wolfspeed (WOLF), On Semiconductor (ON) and Aehr Test Systems (NASDAQ:AEHR) with outperform ratings, while STMicroelectronics (STM), Coherent (NASDAQ:COHR) and Applied Materials (AMAT) received market perform ratings.
