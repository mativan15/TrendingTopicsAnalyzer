Employment Minister Carla Qualtrough says that more than $45-million will go to the Canadian Apprenticeship Forum to help hire 4,000 first-year apprentices in the construction and manufacturing trades.Adrian Wyld/The Canadian PressThe federal government is announcing nearly $247 million to help create more than 25,000 apprenticeship positions in the skilled trades across Canada.Employment Minister Carla Qualtrough says the money will fund 13 programs to help small- and medium-sized employers offer apprenticeship training.
