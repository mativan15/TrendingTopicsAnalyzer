BPSC exam paper leak: Revenue officer arrested from Araria
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-28 16:18:50

A fourth government official has been arrested by Economic Offences Unit (EOU) of Bihar Police in connection with the leak of question papers of state's civil services examination, which was held on May 8 and cancelled hours later as a set of question papers began circulating on social media even as the examination was being held, officials familiar with the matter said. The latest to be arrested is Rahul Kumar (26), who is posted as revenue officer in Bhargama block in Araria district.Rahul, a resident of Gaya's Chiriyawan village, was also an examinee of BPSC's 67th combined examination.
