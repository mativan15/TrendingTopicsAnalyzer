Some of the shops are closed at Public Utility Building. | Photo Credit: SUDHAKARA JAIN Updated: May 30, 2022 00:44 ISTIt might be one of the city's oldest skyscraper, situated in the heart of the Central Business District (CBD), with a prominent view from Namma Metro. But today, it is a story of neglect and few give this iconic building a second look.Traders who have set up shops inside the Public Utility Building hardly receive any new customers. Out of 85 shops in the complex, which occupies the first and second floors of the building, only 30 to 35 are now open for business.
