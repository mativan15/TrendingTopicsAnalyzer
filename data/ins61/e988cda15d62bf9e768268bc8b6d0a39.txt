More Video Rita Nazareth, Bloomberg News U.S. stocks climbed after the S&P 500 breached a technical level that has triggered gains in the past, with risk appetite improving as more of U.K. Prime Minister Liz Truss's unfunded tax cuts were reversed. The breadth of the rally was so strong that at one point over 99 per cent of the US benchmark's companies were rising, with the gauge pushing away from its 200-week moving average.
