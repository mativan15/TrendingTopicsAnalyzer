China Daily via REUTERS MILAN, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Investors raised cash levels further in October as sentiment towards the economic outlook remained close to max bearishness, the latest global fund manager survey (FMS) by BofA showed on Tuesday, although expectations of a policy pivot grew."FMS screams macro capitulation, investor capitulation, start of policy capitulation," BofA said in the survey.
