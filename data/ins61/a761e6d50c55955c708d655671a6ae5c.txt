Pro Kabaddi season 9 got underway at the Shree Kanteerava Indoor Stadium in Bengaluru on October 7.Three lopsided matches were played on the second day of the Pro Kabaddi league's second week. In the first match of the night, Jaipur Pink Panthers, led by its raiders, defeated Gujarat Giants 25-18. The panthers began their season with a loss to UP Yoddhas, but have since turned the tables to win the next three games in a row, vaulting them to second place in the points table with this victory.While, Dabang Delhi had no problems beating Telugu Titans (46-26) convincingly in the second game of the night, who looked way out of form to compete with the defending champions and current table toppers.
