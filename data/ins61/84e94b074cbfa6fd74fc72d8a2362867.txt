Bookclub Millie files application to be listed on Kosdaq
naver.com
news
2022-05-30 16:39:00

Bookclub Millie's logo Bookclub Millie, an e-book subscription service provider in Korea, filed an application on Friday to be listed on the Kosdaq market, its largest shareholder KT said Sunday. The company did not disclose the desired price band, but Bookclub Millie's potential market capitalization is estimated at around 300 billion won ($241 million) by market analysts, according to local press reports. Bookclub Millie plans to offer 2 million shares for public subscription, out of some 8.2 million shares.
