Robert F Kennedy's grandson fought on Ukraine frontline
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 09:50:30

The grandson of assassinated US senator Robert F Kennedy has revealed he secretly fought on the frontline against Putin's Russian forces in Ukraine earlier this year. Conor Kennedy, whose grandfather RFK was assassinated in 1968 while running for President, said he was 'moved' to enlist, despite having no military experience and joined the Ukrainian International Legion.On Instagram, the 28-year-old, who used to date Taylor Swift, told his followers how he hid his famous family connections, which includes President JFK, who was also assassinated in 1963, in order to fight.
