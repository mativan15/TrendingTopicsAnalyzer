Schools Are Getting Hit Hard By Cyberattacks. What Can They Do About It?
forbes.com
news
2022-10-21 10:00:00

Cyberattacks are an increasing threat to businesses, governments, and organizations of all kinds. Schools are no exception. Last month, the Los Angeles Unified School District, the nation's second largest school system, was victimized by a massive ransomware attack. In late September, Michigan's South Redford school district was targeted by a cyberattack that closed schools for two days. These attacks aren't isolated incidents. The explosion in online learning during the pandemic only exacerbated these challenges.
