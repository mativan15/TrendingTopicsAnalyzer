REUTERS/Henry RomeroMEXICO CITY, Oct 21 (Reuters) - Mexico's craft beer industry should grow by over 10% this year, the country's brewing association ACERMEX said on Friday, even as it faces sky-rocketing costs and competition from European-owned heavyweights."The effects of the pandemic, added to the war between Russia and Ukraine ... have caused costs to skyrocket," ACERMEX official Jose Rosas told Reuters.
