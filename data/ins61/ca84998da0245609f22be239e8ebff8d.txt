Local rally for Ukraine calls for more military help
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-15 20:39:00

More than 100 gathered at London's Victoria Park to support Ukraine Saturday. Vorbets and other speakers called for Canadian and western government support for fighters already on the ground in the embattled nation. 'Ukraine is strong. Ukraine will fight. Ukraine will not fall,' said Volodymyr Vorbets, the president of the London Ukrainian Centre. Vorbets and other speakers called for more Canadian and western government military support for the embattled nation. They also demanded an immediate end to the Russian Invasion led by Vladimir Putin.
