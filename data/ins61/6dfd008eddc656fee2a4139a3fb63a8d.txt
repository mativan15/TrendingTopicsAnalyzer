Cleveland Native Dan O'Shannon Speaking At Film Commission Fundraiser
610wtvn.com
news
2022-10-17 08:45:00

(Cleveland) - The Greater Cleveland Film Commission will host award-winning screenwriter and producer Dan O'Shannon for the return of its signature Behind the Camera fundraiser event Friday, October 21st, 2022 at Hilarities 4th Street Theatre at Pickwick & Frolic. Behind the Camera with Dan O'Shannon will be moderated by former CNN Reporter and current WKYC-News Anchor Christi Paul. This look behind-the-scenes will include a wide variety of topics ranging from Dan's experiences in the entertainment business, how growing up in Northeast Ohio impacted his career, and special insight into the creative process behind some of the greatest TV sitcoms of all time.
