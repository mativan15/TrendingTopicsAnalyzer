Nicola Adams and her girlfriend Ella Baig have opened up about the struggles they faced while having IVF treatment.
The boxer and the model welcomed their first child, Taylor Nate Adams, on July 11, with Nicola remarking she was ‘overwhelmed with love and proud of Ella.'
The proud parents had revealed they were expecting a bundle of joy in February after ‘what feels like a lifetime' of IVF.
