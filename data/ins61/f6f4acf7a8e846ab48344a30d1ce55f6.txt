Ryanair passengers spend €500 on new clothes after suitcases 'lost' on flight
express.co.uk

2022-05-31 08:20:00

Emma Hayward with her family in Fuerteventura (Image: Supplied)Emma Hayward, 44, her husband, two young children and elderly mother have been without clothes or essentials since they arrived on the Spanish island from Manchester Airport on Saturday. The mother claims she and her husband have already had to fork out €500 (£425) on new clothes, shoes, and toiletries - and still have no idea where their bags are or when they might arrive.She's made repeated attempts to contact Ryanair but claims she's been told they can't help as it's Manchester Airport's responsibility.
