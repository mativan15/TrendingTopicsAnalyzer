 For the second week in a row, someone in Kelowna won a sizeable chunk of money in this week's LottoMax draw.
The winning numbers in Friday's draw, which featured an estimated jackpot of $70 million, were 02, 24, 27, 31, 40, 43 and 46. The bonus was 13.There were no winners for the massive jackpot, but one person in Ontario matched six of seven numbers, plus the bonus, and won $1.
