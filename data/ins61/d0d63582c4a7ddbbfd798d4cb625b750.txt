Lululemon stock gains on earnings beat, raised guidance (NASDAQ:LULU)

FinkAvenue/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Share of Lululemon Athletica (NASDAQ:LULU) gained sharply in Thursday's extended session after the company beat earnings expectations and elevated expectations for the full year. The Vancouver-based athleisure leader reported $1.6B in net revenue, a 32% jump from the prior year, bolstered by a 32% increase in North American sales and a 29% jump in international sales. Meanwhile, the company reported diluted EPS of $1.48 as compared to $1.16 in the first quarter of 2021.
