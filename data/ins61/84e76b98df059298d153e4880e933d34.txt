Twiggy's big win over Facebook in court

Facebook's parent company has conceded Australia has jurisdiction over a criminal case linked to iron ore billionaire Andrew Forrest, with the matter now likely to go to trial.The mining magnate claims the tech giant has failed to stop clickbait advertising scams, which promote bogus investment schemes, from using his image since March 2019.Facebook, which is owned by Meta Platforms, had previously argued the court had no jurisdiction over the matter but on Tuesday finally conceded that point in Perth Magistrates Court.
