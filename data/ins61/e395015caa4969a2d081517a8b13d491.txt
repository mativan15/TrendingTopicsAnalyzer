Sonarika Bhadoria aka Parvati from Devon Ke Dev Mahadev recently visited Lucknow. Last seen on the TV show Ishq Mein Marjawan, the actress says she has consciously taken a break from TV and is now focusing on films and OTT. Sonarika made her film debut with Saansein in 2016 which didn't do well at the box office, and the actress seemed quite excited about her second film, which was released recently. 'In terms of films, this is definitely a second inning for me because when Saansein happened, at the same time, the announcement of demonetization broke out and our movie lost its significance in the public eye.
