ED quizzes Farooq Abdullah; he says central agencies will trouble NC till polls
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 19:45:00

Enforcement Directorate officers on Tuesday questioned former chief minister Farooq Abdullah in an alleged 'money laundering and J&K Cricket Association fund siphoning' case. Abdullah said that the central agencies would keep troubling leaders of the National Conference till the assembly elections. ED alleges over ₹46 crore funds of the J&K Cricket Association were siphoned off between 2001 and 2021. The funds were part of ₹112 crore grant BCCI had given to J&KCA. ED had summoned Abdullah twice and also attached his property and that of his associate in JKCA worth around ₹12 crore.
