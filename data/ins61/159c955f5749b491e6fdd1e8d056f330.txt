USD 87.27 bn growth expected in Fast Food Market  APAC to occupy 45% market growth

The global fast food market is fragmented. The market is characterized by the presence of several large and medium-sized players and a few well-established vendors. Market players are competing in terms of brand name, new product innovation in their menu, and others. During the forecast period, the market will witness the entry of new players. This is expected to intensify the level of competition among existing players.
The increasing online presence of fast food vendors has been instrumental in driving the growth of the market.
