Man, 52, pistol-whipped during attempted robbery in NYC: cops

A 52-year-old man was pistol-whipped during an attempted robbery in Brooklyn late Thursday, cops said. 
The suspect approached the victim around 9:40 p.m. at 54th Street near Seventh Avenue in Sunset Park and demanded money, authorities said. 
When the victim said he didn't have any, the suspect struck him with the gun, authorities said.
