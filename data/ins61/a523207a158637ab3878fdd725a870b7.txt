Ford India workers waiting for good compensation for loss of jobs
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 07:30:00

APFord India has four plants in the country - vehicle and engine plants in Chennai and Sanand.About 2,000 workers of Ford India Pvt Ltd's plant near here that will be shutdown soon, are hoping for some good news from the management in respect of their severance package, said a union official. "The management said they will come out with their offer on Wednesday afternoon. We are waiting for the same. If the offer is acceptable, then it is fine and if not, we will continue our protest," the union official told IANS, preferring anonymity.
