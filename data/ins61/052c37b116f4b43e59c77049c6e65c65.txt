BengaluruCoinSwitch, a crypto investing app, on Thursday unveiled Crypto Rupee Index (CRE8), the country's first benchmark index to measure the performance of the Indian Rupee-based crypto market.CRE8 is capable of tracking the performance of eight crypto assets that represent more than 85% of the total market capitalisation of cryptos traded in Indian Rupee, according to the company.
