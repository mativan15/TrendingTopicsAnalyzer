PHOENIX (PRWEB) May 31, 2022 Titan CEO and headline sponsors, Class VI Partners and Wipfli LLP, are pleased to announce Geri Wright, Arizona Theatre Company Managing Director, as a 2022 Phoenix Titan 100. The Titan 100 program recognizes Phoenix's Top 100 CEO's & C-level executives. They are the area's most accomplished business leaders in their industry using criteria that includes demonstrating exceptional leadership, vision, and passion. Collectively the 2022 Phoenix Titan 100 and their companies employ upwards of 90,000 individuals and generate over $32 billion dollars in annual revenues.
