Ruud van Nistelrooy: Arsenal can definitely win the title
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

Ruud van Nistelrooy says Arsenal are 'definitely a contender' to win the Premier League after his PSV Eindhoven side were beaten 1-0 at the Emirates Stadium.Granit Xhaka's second-half strike was enough to seal victory for Arsenal, who secured qualification for the Europa League knock-out stages and have now won 13 of their 14 games in all competitions this term. In the Premier League, Arsenal are four points clear of Manchester City and Tottenham Hotspur at the top of the table, having lost only one of their ten games.
