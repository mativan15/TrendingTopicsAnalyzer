Austria's president on Thursday called for wide-ranging reforms of the country's political culture following fresh revelations over a graft scandal, which led then-chancellor Sebastian Kurz to resign last year.Austrian politics has been rocked by a series of corruption scandals with Kurz's resignation in 2021 marking a spectacular fall for a politician who had been hailed as the "Wunderkind" of Europe's conservatives.
