Liz Truss battling open revolt as number of Tory MPs demanding she goes doubles

Liz Truss was battling an open revolt as the number of Tory MPs demanding her resignation swelled after a calamitous 24 hours for her premiership.The number of Conservatives publicly calling for the Prime Minister to quit doubled to a dozen within the space of a couple of hours on Thursday morning.Tory MPs were wondering how long Ms Truss can go on after the chaotic scenes in the Commons that followed the resignation of Suella Braverman as home secretary.But Transport Secretary Anne-Marie Trevelyan insisted that, 'at the moment', she believes Ms Truss will lead the party into the next election.
