Hardayal Prasad resigns as MD, CEO of PNB Housing Finance
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 07:30:00

Representative ImageNEW DELHI: PNB Housing Finance (PNBHF) announced that Hardayal Prasad has resigned from his role as managing director and chief executive officer of the company citing personal reasons, the company said in a BSE filing.Prasad's tenure as company's MD and CEO shall cease on the close of business hours of October 20, 2022. He has agreed to continue as an advisor to the company to ensure a smooth transition.Girish Kousgi will join as the new MD & CEO of the company effective October 21, 2022.
