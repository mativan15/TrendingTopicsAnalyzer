Girl Scouts Receive $84.5 Million Donation From Billionaire MacKenzie Scott
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-19 17:45:10

The Girl Scouts of the USA announced it had received a $84.5 million donation from billionaire philanthropist MacKenzie Scott on Tuesday.The funds will be awarded to 29 of its local organizations chosen by Scott and the national chapter. The donation accounts for the largest donation from a single individual ever given to the Girl Scouts.Executives with Girl Scouts expressed how impactful the donations will be to their small programs."We are so appreciative of MacKenzie Scott's gift to Girl Scouts.
