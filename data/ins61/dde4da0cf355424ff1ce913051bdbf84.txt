EV startup Arrival to focus on U.S. market with restructuring
reuters.com
news
2022-10-20 16:07:00

Oct 20 (Reuters) - British EV startup Arrival SA on Thursday said it would restructure its business to focus on the U.S. market, driven by incentives offered by the country's Inflation Reduction Act signed into law in August.
