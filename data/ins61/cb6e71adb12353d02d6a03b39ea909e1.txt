ED attaches pharmaceuticals firm's Rs 185 crore assets
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 04:30:00

LUDHIANA: In its latest action, the Enforcement Directorate 's (ED) Chandigarh zonal office has provisionally attached properties worth Rs. 185.1 crore belonging to Surya Pharmaceuticals and others.The properties include buildings, plants and machinery located in several places in Haryana, Chandigarh and Mohali in Punjab. The development is the fallout of FIRs (first-information reports) registered by CBI (Central Bureau of Investigation) against the company and its owners for defrauding several banks ofmoney to the tune of Rs 828 crore.
