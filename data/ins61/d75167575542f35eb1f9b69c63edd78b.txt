Published: 09:08 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 09:13 EDT, 27 May 2022 One of the country's leading academy chains has been warned its school could be stripped of funding over rampant homophobic, bullying and derogatory language by students.The Ark Schools academy chain has been ordered to make drastic and urgent improvements to the Ark Kings Academy in Birmingham by the education watchdog.A recent Ofsted inspection found that secondary pupils did not feel safe, while 'bullying, derogatory language and homophobic behaviours are commonplace'.
