Soccer Football - Premier League - Brentford v Brighton & Hove Albion - Brentford Community Stadium, London, Britain - October 14, 2022 Brentford's Ivan Toney celebrates scoring their second goal with team mates Action Images via Reuters/John Sibley Oct 15 (Reuters) - Brentford have condemned racist abuse directed at striker Ivan Toney via a direct message on social media following the side's 2-0 win over Brighton & Hove Albion in the Premier League.Toney shared images of the messages he received on Instagram, with the caption: "I wasn't even going to post this but I woke up angry".
