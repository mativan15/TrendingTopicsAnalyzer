Indian Companies Climate Change Readiness
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 12:15:00

The next 10 years will be an important turning point in India's economic lifecycle. India is already the 5th largest economy in the world, with a burgeoning middle class and increasing aspirations. Sustaining this growth trajectory will mean that Indian companies need to be addressing consumer demands and also moving towards a NetZero world. Climate action is taking centre stage as momentum builds in Indian companies, to limit global warming to 1.5 degrees and harness the opportunities that climate action will bring.
