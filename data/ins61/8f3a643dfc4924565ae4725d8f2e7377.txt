ROCKVILLE, Md., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Xometry, Inc. (NASDAQ: XMTR), the global online marketplace connecting enterprise buyers with suppliers of manufacturing services, today announced it will report its third quarter 2022 financial results before the market opens on Thursday, Nov. 10, 2022. Xometry will host a conference call and webcast to discuss the results at 8:30 a.m. ET (5:30 a.m. PT) on Nov. 10. In addition to issuing a press release, Xometry will post an earnings presentation to its investor website at investors.
