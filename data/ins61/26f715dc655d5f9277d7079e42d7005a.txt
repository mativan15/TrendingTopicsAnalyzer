Xi Warns Against Foreign Interference in Taiwan, Says China Will ‘Never Promise to Renounce' Force
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-15 21:11:28

Chinese President Xi Jinping said China reserves the option of "taking all measures necessary" against "interference by outside forces" on the issue of Taiwan. In a wide-ranging speech Sunday, Xi spoke firmly about China's resolve for reunification with the self-governed island, which Beijing considers part of its territory. Xi was speaking at the opening ceremony of the ruling Communist Party of China's 20th National Congress — held once every five years.
