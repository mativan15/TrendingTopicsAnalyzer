Soccer Football - Serie A - Atalanta v U.S. Sassuolo - Stadio Atleti Azzurri, Bergamo, Italy - October 15, 2022 Atalanta coach Gian Piero Gasperini REUTERS/Alessandro GarofaloOct 17 (Reuters) - Atalanta cannot say they are aiming for the Serie A title as the Bergamo-based side does not have enough resources to compete with bigger clubs, manager Gian Piero Gasperini said on Monday.Atalanta are in second place, two points behind Napoli after 10 games, and extended their unbeaten run from the start of the season to a club record 10 matches with a 2-1 home win over Sassuolo on Saturday.
