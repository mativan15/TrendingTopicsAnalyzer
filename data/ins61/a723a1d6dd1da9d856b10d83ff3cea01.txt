France24 21st October 2022, 05:08 GMT+11British Prime Minister Liz Truss quit on Thursday - bowing to the inevitable after a tumultuous term in which her policies triggered turmoil in financial markets and a rebellion in her party that obliterated her authority.Making a hastily scheduled statement outside her 10 Downing Street office, Truss acknowledged that "I cannot deliver the mandate on which I was elected by the Conservative Party."She is the third Conservative prime minister to resign in as many years and leaves a divided party seeking a leader who can unify its warring factions.
