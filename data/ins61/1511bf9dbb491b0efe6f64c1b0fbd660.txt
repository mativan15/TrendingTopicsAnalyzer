Core sector output in April shows robust growth of 8.4%
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 13:00:00

India's eight core sector industries showed a robust growth of 8.4 per cent in April compared to 4.9 per cent in March. The production of Coal, Electricity, Refinery Products, Fertilizers, Cement and Natural Gas industries increased in April 2022 over the corresponding period of last year. The Eight Core Industries comprise 40.27 percent of the weight of items included in the Index of Industrial Production (IIP). 
Final growth rate of Index of Eight Core Industries for January 2022 is revised to 4.
