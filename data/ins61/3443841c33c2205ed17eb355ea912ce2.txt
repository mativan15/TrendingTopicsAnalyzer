By LI FUSHENG | China Daily | Updated: 2022-05-30 10:55 The Buick GL8, here on display at an auto show held in Xi'an, Shaanxi province, is the best-selling multipurpose vehicle produced by SAIC GM. [Photo provided to CHINA DAILY] A growing number of Chinese and international carmakers are jumping on the bandwagon of premium multipurpose vehicles, or MPVs, eyeing opportunities brought by larger families and more time spent traveling in the world's largest vehicle market. Sino-Japanese joint venture FAW Toyota unveiled the Granvia last week, which is expected to hit the market in the second half of this year.
