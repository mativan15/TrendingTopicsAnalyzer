Veeva Systems Shares Up 15% on Better-Than-Expected 1Q
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 17:02:00

By Kathryn Hardison Shares of Veeva Systems Inc., a cloud-computing company, rose 15% on Thursday following first-quarter earnings in which profit fell, but still beat analyst expectations. Net income for the quarter was $100.1 million, down 13% from a year ago as operating expenses increased quicker than revenue. Earnings per share were 62 cents, down from 71 cents a year ago, and adjusted earnings per share rose to 99 cents from 91 cents. Analysts polled by FactSet expected adjusted earnings of 92 cents.
