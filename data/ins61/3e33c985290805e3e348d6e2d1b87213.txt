Apple's iPhone 14 Plus has been on sale for just one week, but industry reports already claim the release of this much-anticipated smartphone is not going to plan. Released on 7 October, three weeks after the rest of the iPhone 14 range, the iPhone 14 Plus was widely expected to be the most popular device in the lineup. It was seen by many (including myself) as marrying the popularity of big-screen phones with outstanding battery life and — for the first time from Apple — a lower price. But those expectations appear to have been dashed.
