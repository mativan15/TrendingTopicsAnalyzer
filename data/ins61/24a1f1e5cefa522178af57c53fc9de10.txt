PTIGains in metal and banking stocks were offset by losses in auto, healthcare and IT scrips, while data showed that India's economic growth slowed to the lowest in a year in the first three months of 2022.Benchmark indices turned the page to June following a volatile May on a negative note as traders continued to assess new data on inflation and economic activity amid mixed trends from Asian markets. After eking out minor gains in the early minutes of the session, the 30-share pack Sensex turned negative but managed to close off lows and ended 185.
