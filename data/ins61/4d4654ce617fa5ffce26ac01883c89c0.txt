IT'S cold both outside my house and in it - and sooner or later I'm going to give in and put the heating on.
But with fuel prices rapidly rising - as of this month I will pay the average £2,500 a year - I need to find some way of cheating my energy bills down.
Harriet Cooke was left shocked after checking the cost of running one electric radiator
In the corner of my living room is an electric radiator which I have always suspected might save me some money. 
After all, heating just one centrally-heated radiator involves firing up the boiler, which always clanks and rumbles loudly when switched on - suggesting it's working hard.
