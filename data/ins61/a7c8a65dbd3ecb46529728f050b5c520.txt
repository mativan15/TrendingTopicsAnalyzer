Peresmeh/iStock via Getty Images The Global X Social Media ETF (NASDAQ:SOCL) lost ground in Friday's early trading following poorly received financial figures from Snap (SNAP) and more headlines in the ongoing Elon Musk-buyout drama for Twitter (TWTR). SOCL, which is among the most closely watched of the benchmark ETFs for the social media space, dropped 3.6% Friday morning. With the decline, the fund, with its 43 holdings and 0.31% expense ratio, added to weakness it has seen all year. The ETF has cratered 52.
