Unions NSW to campaign against Perrottet government in lead up to March state election
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-15 03:52:03

Unions NSW has publicly declared it will campaign against the Perrottet government to address growing anger amongst workers across the state.Unions NSW say they are on a campaign footing in the lead up to the March electionThe majority of people polled by the unions believe public services are getting worseIndustrial action by nurses, rail workers and teachers has hit the state over the past 12 monthsSecretary of Unions NSW, Mark Morey, told the ALP's state conference in Sydney, union members are now on a campaign footing for the next 160 days in the lead up to the March state election.
