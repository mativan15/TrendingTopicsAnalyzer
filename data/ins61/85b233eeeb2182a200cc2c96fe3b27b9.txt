Tennessee Titans unveil community benefits platform for new stadium
bizjournals.com

2022-10-21 11:28:00

After agreeing to terms for a new $2.1 billion stadium, the Tennessee Titans announced a community benefits platform, ONE Community, at a press conference Oct. 20.

In partnership with 16 local and regional organizations, the platform stands on three pillars: opportunity, neighborhoods and education. Each has its own roster of programs that have either already launched or will do so in the future, as multiple are dependent on stadium construction.
