Over $1 Billion Loss In Crypto Scams Since 2021: Federal Trade Commission

2022-06-04 04:52:27

Crypto scam victims lose more than $1 billion since 2021 - FTCMore than 46,000 people reported losing over $1 billion in cryptocurrency scams since the start of 2021, the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) said in a report on Friday.Nearly half the people who reported losing digital currencies in a scam said it started with an ad, post or a message on a social media platform, according to the FTC.The craze for cryptocurrencies was at a fever pitch last year with bitcoin hitting a record high of $69,000 in November.
