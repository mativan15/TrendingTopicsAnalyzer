Good morning, darling Times reader — and what a morning, in what a week, in what a month this is! Are you, too, now in such a state of disbelief over the burning hayrick that is our government — panicking voles fleeing from the inside, ears on fire — that you have taken to watching the news and simply laughing throughout? Not a joyful laugh, obviously — farewell, pension! Hello, gigantic mortgage bill! No, this is more like the laugh me and my sister did when we were aged four and six respectively and our father came into the room and said, still in shock: 'Your grandad.
