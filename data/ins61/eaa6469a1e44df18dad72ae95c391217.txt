The New Zealand companies still doing business in Russia, and those that have left
stuff.co.nz
economics
2022-06-04 17:00:00

As the war in Ukraine rages on, some New Zealand companies are continuing to do business in Russia, but the list of those turning their backs on the country continues to grow. An expert in Russian politics said that even though the economic impact of New Zealand businesses withdrawing may be small in the scheme of things, it sent a symbolic message to Russia, and showed solidarity with Ukraine. Globally, hundreds of companies, including at least eight from New Zealand, have withdrawn from Russia since its invasion of Ukraine in late February.
