German minister warns Russia could use 'people as weapons'
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 17:00:00

ReutersStressing that there cannot be a situation "where people are being used as weapons", the minister said Germany was in talks with the Czech Republic and Slovakia on the issue.Germany's foreign minister warned Saturday that Russia could seek to spark division in the West through refugees, as Moscow seeks to expand its "hybrid war". "This war is not only waged with weapons, it is also waged with energy and for that, we have found an answer. But it will also be waged with fear and division, and that is precisely what we have to prevent," said Annalena Baerbock at a congress of her Greens' party.
