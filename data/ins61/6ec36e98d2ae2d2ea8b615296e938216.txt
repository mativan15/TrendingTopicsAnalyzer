Seahawks rookie Tariq Woolen named NFC Defensive Player of the Week
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-19 07:00:36

Forget about the Defensive Rookie of the Year conversation. Seahawks rookie Tariq Woolen might just have the stuff of an All-Pro.
Woolen posted his fourth interception in as many games on Sunday and recovered another fumble forced by Coby Bryant. For his efforts, Woolen has been named the NFC Defensive Player of the Week.
