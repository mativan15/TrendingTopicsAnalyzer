Global Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Market: Overview
The solutions offered by the manufacturers and players functional within the global optical coherence tomography (OCT) market are used for various applications in the healthcare domain, including ophthalmology, oncology, cardiovascular, and dermatology, among others.
A few of the major types of products and solutions provided by the players in the global optical coherence tomography market include Doppler OCT devices, table top OCT devices, catheter based OCT devices, and handheld OCT devices.
