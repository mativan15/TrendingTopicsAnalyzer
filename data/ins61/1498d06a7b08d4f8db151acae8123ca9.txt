"Tango Extend makes BYOD phones truly ready for business for the first time" LOS ANGELES (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Mobile Breakthrough, a leading independent market intelligence organization that recognizes the top companies, technologies and products in the global wireless and mobile market, today announced that Tango Networks has been selected as winner of the 'Unified Communications Solution of the Year' award in the 6th annual Mobile Breakthrough Awards program, recognizing the Tango Extend platform.
