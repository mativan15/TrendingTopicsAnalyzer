Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) MP Varun Gandhi on Tuesday shared a video of a girl, who reportedly studies at a private school in Uttar Pradesh's Unnao district, weeping as she was barred from sitting for an examination over non-payment of fee.Taking to Twitter, Gandhi, an MP from Pilibhit, said, 'The tears of this daughter show the pain of lakhs of children who have to face humiliation over non-payment of fees.
