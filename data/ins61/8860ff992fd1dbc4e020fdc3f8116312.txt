With escalating fuel costs, UK consumers have sought out energy efficient devices and gadgets to try to mitigate the rise in their bills. Air fryers, electric heated rails and electric blankets are alternative appliances to energy-hungry tumble dryers and ovens; these have proven so popular that retailers are struggling to meet demand.
