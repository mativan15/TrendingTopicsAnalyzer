Ludhiana: As part of its campaign to get the ban on the manufacturing and sale of plastic carry bag lifted, a delegation of Federation of Industrial & Commercial Organisation ( Fico ) has handed over a memorandum to Punjab Pollution Control Board (PPCB)chief engineer Pardeep Gupta According to Gurmeet Singh Kular , president of FICO, ' Plastic carry bags with thickness of 75 microns and non-woven plastic carry bags made up of 60gm per square meter have been allowed in some states, so similar permission should be given to the local manufacturers here too.
