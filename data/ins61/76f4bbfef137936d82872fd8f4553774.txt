Published: 13:34 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 13:34 EDT, 16 October 2022 'Halloween Ends' with Jamie Lee Curtis, which premiered October 11, 2022 in Hollywood, California, topped the North American box office in its opening weekend Universal scarefest "Halloween Ends" scored a strong start this weekend, topping the North American box office with an estimated take of $41.3 million, industry watcher Exhibitor Relations reported Sunday. The movie, ostensibly the last in a long string of profitable "Halloween" films, again stars Jamie Lee Curtis in a tale replete with throat-slashing, choking, fatal falls and a mysterious masked sewer-dweller.
