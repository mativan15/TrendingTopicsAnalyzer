October 20, 2022 06:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time RALEIGH, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The NovaQuest Private Equity team announced today it has updated its name to be more closely identified to its management company, QHP Capital, L.P. ('QHP' or the 'Firm'). As an operationally focused investor, QHP partners with tech-enabled life sciences and pharmaceutical services companies that help to reduce total cost of care, fulfill unmet medical needs, diminish unnecessary procedures, improve efficiency, and/or elevate patient quality of life.
