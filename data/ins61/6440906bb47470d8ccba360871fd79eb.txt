It's that time of the year when increments and annual bonus add cheer to the salaried individuals. With lump sum cash in hand, many look forward to prepaying a home loan or enjoying little luxuries of life, such as buying a swanky car, the latest mobile phone, a dream holiday, or enrolling in a new skill, etc. However, pandemic-struck past two years have reiterated the significance of financial planning for the future at the same time. Besides our wants and desires, our annual bonus ought to be used for building an emergency fund and saving for future expenses such as Kid's Education, marriage and Retirement.
