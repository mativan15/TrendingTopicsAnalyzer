PonyWang/E+ via Getty Images Introduction ASML Holding N.V. (NASDAQ:ASML), the Dutch lithography juggernaut, has seen its shares rocket up over 300% in the past 5 years. Data by YCharts ASML supplies semi-conductor foundries with the tools that allow them to create some of the most advanced chips ever designed. Their products help Moore's law chug along and advance the creation of breakthrough technologies and new use cases for chips.
