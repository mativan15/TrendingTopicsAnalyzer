Delhi news Live: A fire was reported in an auto parts manufacturing company at Bilaspur industrial area in Gurgaon. Fire tenders are present at the spot.
Meanwhile, a 27-year-old woman has been arrested from Noida in connection with the murder of Delhi Police head constable Ratan Lal during the 2020 Northeast Delhi riots. Police sources said the woman was allegedly part of the mob that attacked Lal and other senior officers in Chand Bagh. The woman had fled her home after being named a suspect in the case.
