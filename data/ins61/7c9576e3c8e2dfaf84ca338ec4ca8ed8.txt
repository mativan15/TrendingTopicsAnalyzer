This is the shortest time anyone has been prime minister for in the history of British politics. For weeks people had been focused on the mistakes they thought she had made, but now everyone's attention has turned to choosing a new leader. The new leader of the Conservative Party, which is the party which is in power in government, will automatically become the prime minister as well.
