REUTERS/Wolfgang RattayBERLIN/FRANKFURT, Oct 20 (Reuters) - The German gas importer Uniper may need up to 40 billion euros ($39 billion) in additional government support, the Handelsblatt newspaper said on Thursday, citing financial and government sources.Uniper declined to comment on the report.
