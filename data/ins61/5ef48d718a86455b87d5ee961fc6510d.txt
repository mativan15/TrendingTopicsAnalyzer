Rosary High School will present 'Fiddler on the Roof' Nov. 3 to 6 at the school, 901 N. Edgelawn Drive in Aurora.

The production team includes Megan Carron, producer, vocal and orchestra director; and director Laura Biancalana, both teachers at Rosary High School.

The cast, crew and pit orchestra includes more than 50 students from Rosary High School and Marmion Academy in Aurora, Rosary officials said in a news release.

Music will be provided by select members of the Rosary Symphony Orchestra and the Marmion-Rosary Band, and some local musicians.
