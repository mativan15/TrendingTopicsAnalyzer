Ukrainians in Manitoba reflect on 100 days of war
globalnews.ca
news
2022-06-03 22:44:10

It's been 100 days since the first Russian bomb dropped in Ukraine, and those who have fled to Manitoba are reflecting on the grim milestone.
Yaryna Chepiha spent 18 days in a bunker in Kyiv before fleeing to Europe en route to Winnipeg.She says being on Canadian soil is a blessing, but it's been an emotional transition as she recalls the pain of the war back home.'My days are filled with sunshine and joy .. .but when I talk about it hurts,' she said.Chepiha says the support she's received so far in Winnipeg is indescribable.
