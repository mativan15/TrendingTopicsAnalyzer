Britons warned of fines, points and a driving ban for being drunk in the passenger seat
express.co.uk
gaming
2022-06-02 05:00:00

As millions look to go out for the Platinum Jubilee bank holiday weekend, drivers are being warned of massive fines if they drive drunk. Anyone supervising a 'designated' learner driver while they are over the legal drinking limit can face massive consequences.If they are found to be doing so, they risk fines of up to £2,500, 10 points on their licence, or even a ban from driving.This can all apply, even if they are not at the wheel.James Armstrong, CEO of Veygo, sent a warning to drivers, urging them to plan ahead if they are looking to drink this weekend and to ensure everyone involved remains safe.
