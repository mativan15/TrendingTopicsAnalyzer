Woman backed for not throwing stepsister a baby shower: "Not my problem"
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-31 21:26:31

Commenters backed a woman who refused to throw her stepsister a baby shower because it was not her issue that no one threw one for her.The anonymous woman, known as u/LifeDiscount1952, posted about the situation in Reddit's popular "Am I The A**hole" forum where it received more than 14,000 upvotes and 1,700 comments.In the post titled "AITA for saying my stepsisters lack of support network for her baby is not my problem" the woman, 24, said she and her stepsister are pregnant and are due around the same time.
