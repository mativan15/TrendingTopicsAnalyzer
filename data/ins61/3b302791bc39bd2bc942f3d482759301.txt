By Reuters Published: 21:15 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 21:15 EDT, 15 October 2022 By Jeff Mason and Mike Stone PORTLAND, Oregon, Oct 15 (Reuters) - U.S. President Joe Biden said testimony and video from a Jan. 6 committee hearing two days ago, featuring congressional leaders in tense phone calls with Pentagon and White House officials during the assault on the U.S. Capitol, was "devastating." Biden was speaking to reporters during a stop at an ice cream shop in Oregon as he campaigned for Tina Kotek, who is running for governor of Oregon.
