'Sturgeon's uncosted Independence plan is truly her Truss moment'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 13:19:51

Yesterday Sturgeon launched her party's third ‘independence paper' – a fantasy document designed, not to deal with the problems faced by Scots in the here and now, but instead to sell them a distant dream of what life could be like in a fantastical independent Scotland of her party's imagination.The paper was paid for by public money, not by the SNP. It was published by the august imprimatur of the Scottish Government. And yet it is a tissue-paper thin series of proposals, an exercise in transparent politics.
