NEW YORK,, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Juan Monteverde, founder and managing partner of the class action firm Monteverde & Associates PC (the "M&A Class Action Firm"), a national securities firm rated Top 50 in the 2018-2021 ISS Securities Class Action Services Report and headquartered at the Empire State Building in New York City, is investigating IMARA Inc. (IMRA), relating to its proposed merger with Enliven Therapeutics, Inc. Under the terms of the agreement, IMRA shareholders are expected to own approximately 16% of the combined company.
