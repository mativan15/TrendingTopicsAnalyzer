Nick Clegg explains his role as president of global affairs at Meta. And he sets out his vision of the metaverse and why we must ensure that people can travel in it freely and not be stuck in a 'splinterverse'. He spoke to the Radio Davos podcast at the World Economic Forum's Annual Meeting 2022. Susbscribe to Radio Davos here. 

 The metaverse was very visible at the World Economic Forum's Annual Meeting in Davos this year, with tech giants Microsoft and Meta both presenting their own demonstrations of what it looks, sounds and feels like to be in a virtual reality or augmented reality world.
