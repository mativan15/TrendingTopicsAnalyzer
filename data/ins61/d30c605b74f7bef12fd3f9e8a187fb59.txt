BI Graphics The past two years have been stressful for so many of us. Whether I'm speaking with friends, colleagues, or clients, I notice that everyone is experiencing some level of burnout. Do you feel like you need a break from the daily grind that a one- or two-week vacation is not enough? Has the pandemic caused you to rethink your priorities and career path? Taking extended time away from work — otherwise known as a sabbatical — comes with many benefits. It can help reduce stress and give you the time needed to relax.
