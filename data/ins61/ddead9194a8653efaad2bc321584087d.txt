From Taranaki to the world: Green Meadows Beef celebrates 10 years
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-17 07:00:00

What started from a conversation around the family dinner table about selling home kill has turned into a successful business venture for a company now exporting to five countries. Green Meadows Beef was started 10 years ago by the Carey family from their Inglewood, Taranaki, home. Co-founder and general manager Nick Carey said he is now the only paid employee left from the family, although his two brothers, Brent and Karl, still sit on the board. 'My parents [Joe and Margy] were dairy farmers and converted to beef finishing as sort of a retirement-type option.
