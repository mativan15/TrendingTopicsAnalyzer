ZUG, Switzerland, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The BSV Blockchain Association is proud to announce that it is sponsoring the upcoming WeAreDevelopers World Congress. The congress will take place on June 14 and 15 in the CityCube in Berlin. With more than 5,000 developers in attendance and over 200 speakers presenting the latest innovation, projects and other topics of interest, this event will be the world's largest developers congress.

Those who will be in Berlin's CityCube on June 14 and 15 can expect two days of discussions and presentations with breaking insights in the field of software development and future tech trends.
