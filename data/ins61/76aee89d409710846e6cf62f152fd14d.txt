Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on June 1 said Satyendar Jain, arrested by the Enforcement Directorate, should be awarded the Padma Vibhushan for giving the 'mohalla clinics' model that treats people free of charge.Mr. Kejriwal defended Mr. Jain as a 'hardcore patriot and honest person' who was being framed in a 'false case' and hoped the Minister will come out clean after the Enforcement Directorate (ED) probe. 'The country should be proud of him as he gave the model of Mohalla Clinic that are being visited by people from the world, including (former) Secretary General of the UN.
