Salesforce stock rallies on earnings beat, raised outlook
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 20:24:00

Salesforce Inc. shares rallied in the extended session Tuesday after the cloud-based customer-relationship-management company topped Wall Street estimates for the quarter and hiked its earnings forecast for the year while lowering its revenue guidance. Salesforce  CRM, -2.94% shares surged 6% after hours, following a 2.9% decline in the regular session to close at $160.24. The company reported fiscal first-quarter net income of $28 million, or 3 cents a share, compared with $469 million, or 50 cents a share, in the year-ago period.
