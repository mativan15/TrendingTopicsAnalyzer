RT.com 20th October 2022, 09:14 GMT+11Angered by their NATO ally's ties to Russia, American officials want Ankara to comply with their sanctions regimeAmerican officials flew to Ankara this week to press Turkish officials and business leaders into compliance with Washington's anti-Russian sanctions, Bloomberg reported on Wednesday. With Türkiye taking a neutral position on the conflict in Ukraine, the US has issued the country several warnings.Led by the Treasury Department's assistant secretary for terrorist financing and financial crimes, Elizabeth Rosenberg, the US delegation met the Turkish central bank governor and business groups in Ankara and Istanbul earlier this week, the report stated.
