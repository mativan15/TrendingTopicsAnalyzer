DALLAS, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AECOM (NYSE: ACM), the world's trusted infrastructure consulting firm, announced today it has been awarded a contract by the Toronto Transit Commission (TTC) to serve as owner's engineer for the Bloor-Yonge Capacity Improvements (BYCI) project. In this role, AECOM is expected to provide consulting services – including the utilization of lean project delivery and building information modeling – through all phases of the project, which aims to expand and modernize the transit hub to accommodate current and future ridership, improve accessibility and safety features, and enhance the customer experience.
