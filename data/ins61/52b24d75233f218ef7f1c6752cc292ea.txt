SHEIN Builds New Community Destination Through SHEIN Exchange Resale Platform
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-17 14:55:00

LOS ANGELES, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SHEIN, an online retailer of fashion, beauty, and lifestyle products, today announced the launch of SHEIN Exchange, an integrated online peer-to-peer resale destination to buy and sell previously owned SHEIN products.The launch of SHEIN Exchange was inspired by SHEIN's community of customers who are passionate about self-expression through fashion. Over the past years, through community-created Facebook groups and social platforms, SHEIN fans have communicated an interest in resale destinations where they can buy and sell previously owned SHEIN products without the barrier of high platform fees that they are usually required to pay.
