Netflix stock surges 14% after its Q3 earnings beat targets and it returns to subscriber growth
Insider
business
2022-10-19 09:53:56

Netflix shares jumped as much as 14% in premarket trading Wednesday, after the streaming giant's third-quarter earnings beat Wall Street's revenue and profit forecasts.The company added 2.4 million subscribers globally, it said in its earnings report published after the market close Tuesday. That growth in its base comes after two straight quarters of declines.Netflix benefited from the release of several popular shows in the period, including "Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story" and the fourth season of "Stranger Things.
