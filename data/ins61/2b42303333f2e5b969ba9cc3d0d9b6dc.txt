Food charities urge Liz Truss to tackle UK hunger as cost of living crisis bites
Reach PLC 2022
news
2022-10-17 15:00:00

Exclusive: The beleaguered Prime Minister has been told to end the need for foodbanks by boosting welfare as prices soar3,000 volunteers have signed a letter to the Prime MinisterFoodbank leaders today urged Liz Truss to end hunger in the UK. In an unprecedented intervention, 3,000 volunteers from organisations feeding Britain's poorest families signed a letter to the Prime Minister calling on her to 'take urgent action to end the need for their services by ensuring everyone has enough income, from work and social security, to buy the essentials'.
