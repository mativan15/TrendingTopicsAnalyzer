In a Monday Wall Street Journal column (paywall), President Biden urged Congress to help fight inflation by raising tax rates on the very wealthy and using the money to reduce the deficit. Does that strategy make sense in today's economic environment? At least in theory, raising taxes could ease inflation. But not necessarily for the reason Biden suggested.
