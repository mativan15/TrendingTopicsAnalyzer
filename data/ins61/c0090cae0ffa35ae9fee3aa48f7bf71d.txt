Ottawa race weekend is back and it could be a record breaking year for a first time participant

Eighty-nine year old Viola Turner has been running for 40 years. "I don't have aches and pains. My knees are good. My back is good," says Turner. "I'm able to put one step ahead of the other. And I don't consider age when I do that." She has been participating in Tamarack Ottawa Race Weekend every year since 2005 and this year is no different. "At 90 if I feel as good as I am today, 91 might be better," says Turner. 'Who knows." Viola Turner, 89, has been running for 40 years. Turner is participating in Tamarack Ottawa Race Weekend.
