(Associated Press ) NEW YORK — Stocks rose in afternoon trading on Wall Street Friday, keeping the market on track for its first weekly gain after seven weeks of losses.
The S&P 500 rose 1.8% as of 12:01 p.m. Eastern and is headed for a 5.9% gain for the week.
The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 356 points, or 1.
