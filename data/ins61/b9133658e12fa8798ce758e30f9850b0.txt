May 31, 2022 12:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time BERLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SIGNA Sports United ('SSU' or the 'Company'), a specialist online sports retail and tech company with a focus on bike, tennis, outdoor and team sports, today announced that its leadership team will participate in the following investor conferences during June 2022.

Barclays European Internet & Emerging Tech Conference, 10 June 2022. Stephan Zoll, Chief Executive Officer, and Alexander Johnstone, Chief Financial Officer, will conduct virtual one-on-one meetings.
