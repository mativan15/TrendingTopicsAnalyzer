We Irish routinely moan about our weather, we grumble about the quality or quantity of food we are served in restaurants and we complain about the absence of entertainment laid on by others to keep us amused. And if giving out about the price of everything was a national sport, this little island of ours would be world-beating.In recent weeks, there has been an outpouring of national outrage about the shambles that Dublin Airport has become at the busiest of times, while Joe Duffy's Liveline has been hopping mad with people infuriated by the spiralling cost of car hire and hotels close to home.
