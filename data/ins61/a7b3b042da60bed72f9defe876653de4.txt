May 31, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--DexCom, Inc. (NASDAQ:DXCM) today issued the following statement in response to recent media and market speculation:

'Like many companies, Dexcom's Board and management team regularly review opportunities to enhance stockholder value and create benefits for customers, including through mergers and acquisitions. It is generally our policy not to comment on rumors or speculation, however, in light of recent sustained media and market speculation as well as the upcoming American Diabetes Association conference, we wish to confirm that Dexcom is not in active discussions regarding a merger transaction at this time.
