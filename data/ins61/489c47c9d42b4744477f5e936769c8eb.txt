By Associated Press Published: 21:19 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 21:19 EDT, 15 October 2022 CEDAR FALLS, Iowa (AP) - Theo Day threw three touchdown passes, ran for another score, and Northern Iowa racked up 548 yards on offense in a 42-14 victory over Utah Tech on Saturday. The Panthers (3-4) struck early, with Day's 44-yard touchdown pass to Sergio Morancy coming on their first possession. Day threw second-quarter TD passes of 32 yards to Desmond Hutson and 70 yards to Sam Schnee for a 34-7 halftime lead.
