When we talk about actresses in the South Indian film industry, Samantha Ruth Prabhu's name emerges as one of the finest. It was the 2010 film ‘Ye Maaya Chesave,' in which the actress made her debut. She was praised for her stellar performance and also received the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut- South for the film.

The actress has carved a niche for herself after starring in several films over the years. Now, she is set to appear in the ‘Yashoda' film, a solo-lead film.
