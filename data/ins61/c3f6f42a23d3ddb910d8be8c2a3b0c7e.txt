Some changes are coming to Hasbro. Well, one of its most popular games. After a month long voting period, the company is announcing that the "thimble" is returning after a 5 year retirement to Monopoly. Hasbro has owned the rights of of the iconic game for more than 30 years. The company gave the choice to voters to bring back one of its tokens. Besides the thimble, the wheelbarrow, iron, boot, horse and rider and money bag was on the ballot. The thimble, which is one of the game's original pieces will come back to production for games in the fall of this year.
