Panuwat Dangsungnoen/iStock via Getty Images Kanzhun took the top spot among gainers, continuing its see-saw trend, while airline stocks almost packed the entire list of decliners on concerns of fuel jet prices, among other things. For the week ending June 3, The SPDR S&P 500 Trust ETF (SPY) was (+1.29%) in the green for the second week straight after a 7-week losing streak. However, YTD, the ETF is still in the red. The Industrial Select Sector SPDR (XLI) also gained (+2.41%) for the second week in a row, which preceded two back-to-back weeks of losses.
