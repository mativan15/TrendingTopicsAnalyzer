AgenciesDuvvuri Subbarao said civil servants, at different rungs of administrative hierarchy, are required to provide effective leadership in order to get the work done by people and in order to become successful leaders, he called upon the officials to earn credibility from their team members, rather than demanding it.Former RBI Governor Duvvuri Subbarao on Thursday said the problems being faced by the Civil Servants in the contemporary society are 'highly complex' in nature and there are no quick fix solutions to them.
