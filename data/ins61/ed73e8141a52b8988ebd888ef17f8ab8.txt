Chegg Adds $1B to Repurchase Program, Shares Rise 12%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 21:56:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Chegg Inc. on Thursday said its board authorized an increase to its repurchase program for up to $2 billion in common stock and convertible notes. The company said it has about $65 million remaining from its previously announced $1 billion securities repurchase program. At 5:31 p.m. ET, the stock had risen 12.6% to trade at $23. The stock finished the days regular session with an 8.9% gain, closing at $20.42. Chegg is an educational technology company which provides textbook rentals, tutoring and services to students.
