ERIK TEN HAG has named 17-year-old starlet Kobbie Mainoo on the bench for today's clash against Newcastle.
The teenage midfielder is in the senior squad for the first time after impressing for Manchester United U21s this season.

Red Devils boss Ten Hag is also a little light in midfield with Scott McTominay suspended and Christian Eriksen out through illness.
