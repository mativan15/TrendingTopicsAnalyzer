Just take a look at the filled-up cycle parking lots at MNCs When Bengaluru wants to cycle, can the city's elevation, badly maintained cycle tracks and poorly designed roads prove to be deterrents? No way. Now, more than ever Bengalureans are cycling to work. Recently, the image of a parking lot at a tech park filled with cycles took the social media by surprise.

Karthik Ranganathan (Test Engineer and Cycling Ambassador) told Bangalore Mirror, 'Close to 100 of the employees ride cycles to office daily.
