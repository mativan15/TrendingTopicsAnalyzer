Another baker has bid an emotional farewell to the Great British Bake Off tent tonight, with Dawn being the sixth contestant out of the competition.
During the latest instalment of the beloved annual series, the competitors did their best to impress judges Paul Hollywood and Prue Leith in Halloween week, as they were tasked to create an apple cake for the spine-tingling signature.
Kevin's apple bobbing was unfortunately branded a ‘bit of a mess,' by Prue, while Paul said he wanted a ‘cake not a pudding.
