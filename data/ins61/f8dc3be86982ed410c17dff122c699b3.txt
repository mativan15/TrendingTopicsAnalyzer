~ PEDMARK is the First and Only FDA-Approved Therapy Indicated to Reduce the Risk of Cisplatin-Induced Hearing Loss in Pediatric Patients ~ ~ Fennec HEARS™ Program Offers Comprehensive Patient Services, Including Access to Care Coordinators, Financial and Prescription Drug Support ~ RESEARCH TRIANGLE PARK, N.C., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Fennec Pharmaceuticals Inc. (NASDAQ: FENC; TSX: FRX), a commercial stage specialty pharmaceutical company, today announced the U.S. commercial launch and availability of PEDMARK® (sodium thiosulfate injection) to reduce the risk of ototoxicity associated with cisplatin in pediatric patients one month of age and older with localized, non-metastatic solid tumors.
