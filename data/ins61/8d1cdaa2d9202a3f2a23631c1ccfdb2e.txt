Why a Botched Story About Facebook Is a Huge Blow to Indian Journalism
slate.com
tech
2022-10-18 23:49:47

The state of journalism in India, the 'world's largest democracy,' has never been so dire. And recently, it just got much worse—this time not because of the government or its acolytes, but because of a once-dependable news website whose mistakes will have ramifications far outside the subcontinent. On Oct. 10, the Wire—one of India's few truly independent publications—put out an explosive report alleging deep ties between the Indian government and employees at Meta, the parent company of Facebook and Instagram.
