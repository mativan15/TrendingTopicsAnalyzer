Home that inspired horror movie sold for more than $1.5M
chron.com
news
2022-05-27 14:28:42

BURRILLVILLE, R.I. (AP) — The Rhode Island farmhouse where the paranormal happenings that inspired the 2013 horror movie 'The Conjuring' occurred has been sold to a Boston developer who plans to keep it open to the public.The 18th century home in Burrillville sold for $1.525 million on Thursday, far higher than the $1.2 million asking price. 'This purchase is personal for me,' buyer Jacqueline Nuñez, owner of WonderGroup LLC told The Boston Globe. 'It's not a real-estate development. It's around my own beliefs.
