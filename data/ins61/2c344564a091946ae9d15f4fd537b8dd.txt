Cirque du Soleil to return to Microsoft Theater with new show 'Corteo'
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 20:54:00

Cirque du Soleil is coming back to Los Angeles with its latest show "Corteo."

After touring in 20 countries across four continents and performing in front of 10 million spectators, "Corteo" will be the second Cirque du Soleil show at to come to the Microsoft Theater.

Tickets for the acrobatic production, directed by Daniele Finzi Pasca, go on sale for the public Oct. 24.

"We are excited to bring Corteo to the Microsoft Theater along with our great partners at AEG," said Mike Newquist, president of the touring shows division at Cirque du Soleil Entertainment Group.
