A vet has been left in tears over a young boy's explanation on why dogs do not live as long as humans - and it has brought people great comfort after recently resurfacing on social mediaThe vet was moved to tears by the child's words (stock photo) A six-year-old boy moved a vet to tears after he explained why he thinks dogs live such short lives compared to humans. The young boy, named Shane, was left emotional after attending a vet appointment with his parents, Ron and Lisa, after the family's 10-year-old Irish wolfhound, Belker, started acting out of sorts.
