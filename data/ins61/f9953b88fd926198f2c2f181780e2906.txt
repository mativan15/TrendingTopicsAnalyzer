AMAZON Prime is offering all of its subscribers 10 rare FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) cards for FIFA 23 players starting today.
EA Sports is teaming up with Amazon to offer FIFA 23 rewards to all Prime subscribers.
Prime Gaming now offers rewards for FIFA 23.Credit: Amazon
The gaming sector of Amazon Prime, known as Prime Gaming, gives away numerous rewards for popular live-service games every month.
This includes in-game loot for games such as Pokémon Go, League of Legends, and Fall Guys.
FIFA 23 released a few weeks ago, but this is the first time Prime Gaming is offering rewards for the latest game.
