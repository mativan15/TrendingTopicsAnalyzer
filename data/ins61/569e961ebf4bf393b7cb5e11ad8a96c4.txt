June 02, 2022 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time OVERLAND PARK, Kan.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--As the journey from education to employment becomes more complex, the challenges of building tomorrow's workforce are intensifying, notably in the STEM education space. To build a foundation for long-term employment solutions for the engineering and construction industry, Black & Veatch has joined the Center for the Advanced Professional Studies (CAPS) Network Council of Champions as the inaugural CAPS Network Engineering Champion.
