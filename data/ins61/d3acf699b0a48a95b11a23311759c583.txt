 Millions of football fans have been waiting for Liverpool's face-off match with Real Madrid at the UEFA Champions League final this weekend. For those not lucky enough to have one of approximately 70,000 tickets to the Stade de France on Saturday, this is how you can watch the game on your TV or online. What time is kick-off for the Champions League final? The two teams will go head-to-head in the final match of the season at 8pm UK time and 9pm local time on Saturday.
