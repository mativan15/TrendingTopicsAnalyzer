CBI Probe has reduced crypto scam in Austria: Andreas Holzer
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 19:00:00

Indian investigations helped to drastically reduce cryptocurrency scam in Austria and neighboring countries, Austria's Federal Police and Director of Criminal Intelligence Service, General Mag Andreas Holzer, has said. CBI had recently cracked a Bitcoin racket after it received information from several countries that call centres run in Delhi and neighboring states were duping foreign nationals. "CBI recently conducted searches on a call centre in Shadi Khampur village in West Delhi where victims from foreign countries were duped by the accused, who impersonated Europol officers.
