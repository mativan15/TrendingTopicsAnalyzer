Agrify (NASDAQ:AGFY) is set to implement a 1-for-10 reverse stock split, effective October 18, 2022. The 1-for-10 reverse stock split will automatically convert 10 current shares of Agrify's (AGFY) common stock into one new share of common stock. No fractional shares will be issued in connection with the reverse stock split.
