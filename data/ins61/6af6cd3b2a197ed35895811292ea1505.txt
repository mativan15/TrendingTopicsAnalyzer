Published: 08:20 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 10:46 EDT, 17 October 2022 Lyft rides are now, on average, 60 cents more expensive in the US due to inflation increasing insurance costs for drivers working on its platform.The company offers its drivers insurance that covers accidents involving bodily injuries and property damage, and third-party auto liability.A 55-cent surcharge was added earlier this year to help drivers deal with the higher gas prices, which means consumers are paying, on average, an extra $1 per ride this year than compared to this time last year.
