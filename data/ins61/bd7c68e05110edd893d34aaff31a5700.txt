Image source, PA MediaImage caption, Tobias Ellwood says he has been readmitted to the Conservative parliamentary partyA senior Tory MP said he had been readmitted to the parliamentary party after he had the whip withdrawn. Tobias Ellwood was suspended in July when he missed a vote of confidence in Boris Johnson's government.At the time the Bournemouth East MP said he was "sorry" to lose the whip but had been unable to get back from Moldova in time.Earlier, he tweeted that he was "off the naughty step" and welcomed the government's "re-set".
