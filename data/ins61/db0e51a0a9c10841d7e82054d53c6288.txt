Shinny Burns, a former professional boxer, runs the free Shine Head Boxing Gym in his garden. Shine Head Boxing Gym may not look like much from the outside, but inside it is the ambitions and goals of young people in the neighbourhood who want to solve their issues. Coach Shinny Burns claims he effectively uses his gym to help his fighters perform at their best. Burns gives free lessons to children of all ages at his house and instructs them inside and outside the ring. Some of his boxers claim that taking up the sport has given them a second chance at life.
