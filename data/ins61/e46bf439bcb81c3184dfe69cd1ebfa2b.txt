Gambling harm will be the cost of row over lottery's future
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 23:01:00

While the government's long-awaited gambling review white paper looks set to finally land at the end of the month, the future of the National Lottery is still up in the air. A few weeks ago, the present operator Camelot appeared in court with IGT, its technology supplier, to oppose the Gambling Commission's decision to give the next ten-year licence to Allwyn. A decision is expected imminently, but in the meantime we've had stasis.As the mess created by Camelot's legal challenge is resolved, it is pushing for an extension to its present licence.
