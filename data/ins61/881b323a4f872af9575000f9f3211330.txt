Modi, UN chief spell out green pledges for Indians
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 18:27:19

Prime Minister Narendra Modi and United Nations secretary-general Antonio Guterres launched on Thursday India's flagship program on climate change to encourage individual and collective action reduce emissions, save energy and cut down on waste. Touting the principle of sustained lifestyles, India's Mission Life, where life is short for 'lifestyle for environment' will encourage people to adopt environmentally friendly behaviour like switching off engines at traffic lights, using reusable carry bags and turning off taps when not in use.
