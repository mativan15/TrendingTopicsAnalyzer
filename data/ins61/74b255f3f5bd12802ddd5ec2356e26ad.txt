The Shenehon Center for Real Estate at the University of St. Thomas recently announced this year's class of inductees for the Minnesota Real Estate Hall of Fame.

The Hall of Fame honors leaders in real estate who show outstanding business performance, high ethical standards and a commitment to the community. The recipients of the honor will be celebrated during a ceremony Nov. 9 at the Golden Valley Country Club.
