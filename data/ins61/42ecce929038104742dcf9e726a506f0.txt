By Jake Nisse For Dailymail.Com Published: 23:47 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 23:52 EDT, 15 October 2022 Matt Ryan said he was 'speechless' Saturday at making the Lakers' roster, as the small forward's winding journey to the NBA reached a new high.The former Notre Dame, Vanderbilt and Chattanooga player went undrafted in 2020, and failed to earn an invite to the G League Bubble in 2021.And until Ryan latched onto the Cavaliers' summer league roster that summer, his life was far from glamorous.
