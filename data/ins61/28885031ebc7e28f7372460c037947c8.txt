*Warning: Contains spoilers for House of the Dragon episode 9*
House of the Dragon episode 9, entitled The Green Council, saw half of the Game of Thrones prequel's cast missing from action.
With Princess Rhaenyra (Emma D'Arcy) and Daemon Targaryen both away on Dragonstone, they were locked out of the events in the latest installment of the Sky Atlantic drama. 
Alicent (Olivia Cooke) and Otto Hightower (Rhys Ifans) both want to keep the news of King Viserys' death under wraps, but for different reasons.
