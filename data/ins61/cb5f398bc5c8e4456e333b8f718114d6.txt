Korean players to be rewarded with record prize money at World CupBy Yoo Jee-ho SEOUL, May 31 (Yonhap) -- Winning matches and reaching the knockout stage at this year's FIFA World Cup won't just mean personal glory for South Korean players; it will also fatten their bank accounts in record-breaking fashion. The Korea Football Association (KFA) on Tuesday announced details on monetary compensation for the national team players at the World Cup in Qatar. Each player on the final 23-man roster will receive 20 million won (US$16,155) for making the team.
