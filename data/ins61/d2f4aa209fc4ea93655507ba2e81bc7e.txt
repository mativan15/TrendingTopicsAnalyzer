CM in Mum for Odisha Bhawan expansion event
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 07:15:00

Bhubaneswar: A day after attending the Odisha investors' meet in Hyderabad, chief minister Naveen Patnaik on Tuesday arrived in Mumbai, where he will lay the foundation stone for expansion of Odisha Bhawan . After the programme, he will return to Bhubaneswar.The existing facility at Vashi in Navi Mumbai will be expanded to cater to the needs of people from Odisha, who visit Mumbai in large numbers for different works, particularly cancer treatment.The home department's plan for expansion of the Odisha Bhawan includes state-of-the-art accommodation facility and funds for the project have been approved by the state government.
