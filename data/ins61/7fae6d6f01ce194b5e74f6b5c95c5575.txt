Hiro Komae/Pool via REUTERS/File PhotoSYDNEY, Oct 22 (Reuters) - Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese and Japanese Prime Minister Fumio Kishida on Saturday said their countries had agreed to deepen and expand their cooperation to respond to pressing security challenges in their region.Kishida also said he wanted to reinforce the Japan's defence capabilities within five years, and would examine all options for national defence, including "counterstrike capabilities".
