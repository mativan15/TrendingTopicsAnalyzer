Australian home prices have fallen nationally for the first time in at least 20 months, according to two leading monthly indices.The two home price indices both show a 0.1 per cent drop nationally in a monthBoth show Sydney, Melbourne and Canberra led the fallsAdelaide, Brisbane and some regional areas continued to post steep price risesBoth indices — from CoreLogic and REA's PropTrack — put the national monthly fall at around 0.1 per cent.CoreLogic records that as the first monthly decline since September 2020, while PropTrack has it slated as the first fall since the COVID pandemic started.
