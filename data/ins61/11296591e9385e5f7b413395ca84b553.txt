Getty Images Microchip manufacturer Broadcom has offered $61 billion to buy cloud computing and virtualization company VMWare The deal would see shareholders receiving cash consideration of $142.50 per share or a swap of 0.252 Broadcom stock per VMWare share The global microchip shortage has been a catalyst for Broadcom CEO Hock Tan to expand the revenue centers for the company beyond hardware One of the biggest ever tech acquisitions is about to go down, and it involves two companies you may have never even heard of.
