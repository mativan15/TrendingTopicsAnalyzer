Iteris stock rises 12% as FY23 revenue outlook seen above estimates
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-02 15:28:32

Zerbor/iStock via Getty Images Iteris (NASDAQ:ITI) stock rose ~12% on June 2 after the company reported its Q4 results a day ago and announced FY23 revenue outlook above estimates. Q4 total revenue grew +7.96% Y/Y to ~$34.24M. The company said the increase was mainly due to sustained strong customer adoption of Iteris' ClearMobility Platform. "We continued to experience strong customer demand for Iteris' ClearMobility Platform, with fourth quarter and full year bookings growing 27% and 28%, respectively, year over year," said Iteris President and CEO Joe Bergera.
