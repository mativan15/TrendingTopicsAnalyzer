Actress Monalisa Pal blessed with a baby boy
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-16 10:43:00

Popular actress Monalisa Pal and hubby Biswajit Sarkar have all the reasons to celebrate. The couple has been blessed with a baby boy. Congratulatory messages have been pouring in for the new parents who are beaming with joy. Colleagues, industry peers, and fans have extended warm wishes to the couple. Darshana Banik, Abhishek Bose and other artists have congratulated Monalisa who has just embraced motherhood.Monalisa is one of the most popular faces on Bengali television. The actress has been keeping low for past few months and wasn't seen on the small screen much, expect for occasional appearances.
