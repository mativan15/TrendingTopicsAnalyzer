GameStop Lost $160M In Past Three Months, Looking To Crypto And NFTs For Growth
gamespot.com
news
2022-06-02 15:35:13

Video game retailer GameStop announced earnings results for the last three months, and the company posted an overall loss of $157.9 million. This compares to a loss of $67 million for the same period last year.While profit declined, GameStop made more in revenue during its Q1, rising 8% to $1.378 billion. Management said on an earnings call that analysts and investors should judge the retailer on revenue first and foremost as a signal of the company's overall health.GameStop had cash and cash equivalents of $1.
