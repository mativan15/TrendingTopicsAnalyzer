JOSEPH & RIVERSIDE, Mo. & OVERLAND PARK, Kan., May 27, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Epiphany Dermatology and the Colorado Melanoma Foundation are hitting the road this spring to provide FREE skin cancer screenings and sun-safety education to individuals throughout the Midwest, stopping in St. Joseph, MO 6/7, Riverside, MO 6/8, and Overland Park, KS 6/9.

This press release features multimedia. View the full release here: https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20220527005319/en/

The Sun Bus, a community project originally launched in Colorado to provide FREE skin screenings, skin cancer awareness, and sun safety education, is now expanding its services to the Midwest through its partnership with Epiphany Dermatology.
