Andrew Kelly/ReutersA sustained stock market rally into 2023 will only happen if investor sentiment remains bearish, according to Ned Davis Research.Investor sentiment has hit bearish extremes in recent weeks as inflation and interest rate concerns linger."Sentiment would need to remain subdued, providing a wall of worry for the market to climb," NDR said.A rally in the stock market heading into 2023 won't be sustainable unless investor sentiment remains in bearish territory, according to a Thursday note from Ned Davis Research.
