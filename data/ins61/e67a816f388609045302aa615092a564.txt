2022-06-02 11:16:46

Kimberly White/Getty Images Entertainment Sheryl Sandberg, Chief Operating Officer of the social media giant Meta Platforms (NASDAQ:FB) has stepped down after nearly a decade and a half worth of service. Sandberg who started with FB back in 2008 helped the organization become profitable, go public, and aided in the development of FB to become one of the ten largest organizations by market cap weight. CEO Mark Zuckerberg stated that product executive, Javier Olivan will replace Sandberg later this fall.
