Being voted by The 20 as the Best Revenue Booster in our first year of partnership is a testament to our efforts and commitment to making a difference in the business and financial well-being of channel partners. SAN FRANCISCO (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Zomentum, creators of the first Revenue Platform built for the partner ecosystem, announced it has received recognition from The 20 and The ASCII Group. The company earned The Best Revenue Booster award from The 20 MSP. It also earned The Best Market Opportunity award, two Best Keynote awards, and recognition as one of the Top 10 Vendors of the year from The ASCII Group.
