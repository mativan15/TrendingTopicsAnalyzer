The Uttar Pradesh government has inked 13 agreements with various firms promising investment worth Rs 564 crore in the state at the Defence Expo 2022 in Gujarat's Gandhinagar, state cabinet minister Nand Gopal Gupta has said. The MoUs have been inked by the Uttar Pradesh Expressways Industrial Development Authority (UPEIDA) and investments will be made in the UP Defence Industrial Corridor being developed by it, he said on Wednesday. 
 Gupta, the industrial development minister, is representing the state at the Defence Expo 2022, which was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Gujarat's capital city on Wednesday.
