If knowledge is power, the absence of knowledge can feel like powerlessness. Not knowing—or uncertainty—can be a source of anxiety, confusion, and overwhelm. But we can't know everything that's coming in our professional or personal lives, so we have a choice. We can live with the excess anxiety that uncertainty creates or take steps to avoid having the uncertainty overwhelm us.
