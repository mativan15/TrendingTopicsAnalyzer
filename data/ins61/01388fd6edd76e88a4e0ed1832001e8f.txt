1:44 Southern Alberta motorcycle community rides together for fatal crash victim A fatal motorcycle accident in Lethbridge claimed the life of 25-year-old old Jake Legge last week. His family and the motorcycle community came together on Saturday for a memorial and a ride in his honour to help promote driver awareness. Jaclyn Kucey has more.  A sea of orange drove through Lethbridge on Saturday to raise awareness for rider safety.
On October 4, 25-year-old Jake Legge was hit by a car while riding his motorcycle.
