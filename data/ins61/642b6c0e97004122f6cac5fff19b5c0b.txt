Geena Davis opens up about not becoming a mother until her 40s. Here's what she says
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 11:45:00

Thelma & Louise and Cutthroat Island actor Geena Davis explained on ITV's Loose Women Wednesday episode, why she is grateful for not embracing motherhood until she reached her 40s. In the interview, Davis revealed that she encourages her children by showcasing behaviours- a trick she learned from her parents. She also added that she wanted to have children late because of how mature she hoped to be to become a mother. She revealed that she was happy that she had her children in her 40s. She also added that she wanted to wait and hoped she could still bear children by that time.
