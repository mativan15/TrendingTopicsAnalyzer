Irans Rekabi latest female athlete at risk in home country
ap.org
news
2022-10-21 22:45:00

MORIOKA, Japan (AP) — Days after Iranian climber Elnaz Rekabi caused an international incident by not wearing a her country's mandatory headscarf while competing abroad, her fate is top of mind for the world's best climbers. 'It has made me ill — nauseous,' said American Brooke Raboutou, speaking to The Associated Press on Friday at a World Cup climbing event in northern Japan. 'I support her 100 percent and I'd like to think I can speak on behalf of most of the athletes,' she added. 'I've reached out to her, just asking if there is anything we can do to help, to support.
