WARNER BROS. DISCOVERY TO REPORT THIRD-QUARTER 2022 RESULTS ON THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 3
prnewswire.com
news
2022-10-20 12:30:00

NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Warner Bros. Discovery, Inc. (the "Company") (Nasdaq: WBD) today announced that it will report its third-quarter 2022 results on Thursday, November 3, 2022, after the market close. The Company will host a conference call at 4:30 p.m. ET that same day to discuss the results. Links to the live webcast of the conference call as well as the earnings materials will be available in the "Investor Relations" section of the Company's website at https://ir.wbd.com/.
A telephone replay of the call will be available approximately two hours after the completion of the call until November 11, 2022.
