AM Best Assigns Credit Ratings to Nacional de Seguros S.A. Compañía de Seguros Generales

2022-05-27 00:00:00

May 27, 2022 11:28 AM Eastern Daylight Time MEXICO CITY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has assigned a Financial Strength Rating of B++ (Good) and a Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of 'bbb' (Good) to Nacional de Seguros S.A. Compañía de Seguros Generales (Nacional de Seguros) (Bogota, Colombia). The outlook assigned to these Credit Ratings (ratings) is stable.

The ratings reflect Nacional de Seguros' balance sheet strength, which AM Best assesses as very strong, as well as its adequate operating performance, limited business profile and appropriate enterprise risk management.
