What if you want to work from home but your employer doesn't agree?
irishtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

The return to the office is happening, but it is all still in a bit of a limbo. Labour shortages in some sectors remain a big factor, meaning managers are treading carefully for fear of losing key staff. Employees, used to the flexibility offered by remote work during Covid-19, are often loath to return to the office routine. The five-day, nine-to-five, office routine will never come back, but some big questions remain.What if you want to continue to work remotely, but your employer wants you back in the office? The legal position relies on your work contract, but normally this specifies the office as your place of work.
