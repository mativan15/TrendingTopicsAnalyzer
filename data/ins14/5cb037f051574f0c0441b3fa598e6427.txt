Netflix landlord Hudson Pacific promotes 2 executives to C-suite
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 21:27:00

Real estate investment trust Hudson Pacific Properties Inc. is promoting two of its executive vice presidents to chief officer roles.

Former Executive Vice President for California Office Operations Drew Gordon will be the company's chief investment officer, while Hudson Pacific (NYSE: HPP) Executive Vice President for Pacific Northwest/Canada Office Operations Andy Wattula will be promoted to chief operating officer.

"Drew and Andy are tenured executives who have demonstrated their ability to strategically lead Hudson Pacific's investments and operations functions, respectively," said Hudson Pacific Chairman and CEO Victor Coleman.
