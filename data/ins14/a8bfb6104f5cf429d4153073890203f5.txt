Ashwin Yardi, CEO – India, Capgemini Women and children are 14 times more likely to die when disasters strike; Bangladesh reports the highest levels of trafficking in women post every cyclone disaster; and the number of child soldiers in Africa's civil wars sees a sharp increase during seasons of drought. The list goes on… Climate change and resultant natural disasters are gender biased; it affects the vulnerable populations more, especially women. And climate change impact is aggravated because of the paucity of women in decision-making positions to shape policy.
