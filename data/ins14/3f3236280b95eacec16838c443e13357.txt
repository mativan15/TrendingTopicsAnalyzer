Delhi court summons Karnataka Cong leader Shivakumar in money laundering case
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-31 10:30:29

A Delhi court Tuesday summoned Karnataka Congress leader DK Shivakumar and others while taking cognizance of a chargesheet filed by the Enforcement Directorate, in a money laundering case. The court has directed him to appear before it on July 1.
