AgenciesThe city-based company had registered standalone net profit at Rs 71.63 crore during corresponding quarter previous year.Cement manufacturer The has reported standalone net loss for the quarter ending March 31, 2021 at Rs 23.71 crore due to 'spiraling' increase in the cost of input materials together with the loss of volume, the company said on Friday. The city-based company had registered standalone net profit at Rs 71.63 crore during corresponding quarter previous year. For the year ending March 31, 2022 standalone profit stood at Rs 38.
