A man has been left mortified after he thought it would be 'funny' to swipe right on his boss on Tinder - but ended up matching with her after she swiped right tooThe man was mortified when he matched with his boss on Tinder (stock photo) If you've ever tried looking for love on a dating app, you'll probably be all too aware of the dilemma that you face if you come across the profile of someone you already know. Do you swipe right and risk subtly confessing to your neighbour that you're into them, or do you brush them off and miss out on what could have been the love story of a lifetime? That's the conundrum one man on Reddit faced when he was browsing Tinder for a potential date and found himself faced with the profile of his manager at work.
