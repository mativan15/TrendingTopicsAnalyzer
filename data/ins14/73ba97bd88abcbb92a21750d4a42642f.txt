Photo by Solen Feyissa on UnsplashStock photos are the bane of many marketers and designers. It is a huge part of the life of content marketers, graphic designers and copywriters. They command high fees for usage and are able to justify them due to the fact that the majority of the users of stock images are enterprises.One of the first examples of a stock photo was circa 1920 when American photographer H. Armstrong Roberts ensured that the people photographed in Group in Front of Tri-Motor Airplane all signed model releases.
