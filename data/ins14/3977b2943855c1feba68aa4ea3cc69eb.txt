The idea of handing over personal data and access to our browsing habits to be able to access online services is something to which we've become so accustomed, we don't even question it anymore. However, the dawn of Web3 is introducing a new paradigm of data ownership, one where we own our personal data and can determine who accesses it and how it's used.
