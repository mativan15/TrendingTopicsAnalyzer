The duo lured the Chandigarh-based businessman into investing in the company, promising him that the invested money would multiply in three years
Published on Jun 02, 2022 02:03 AM IST
 

Police booked a father-son duo for allegedly duping a businessman based in Sector 40 of ₹56.85 lakh in lieu of providing high returns on an investment. In his complaint, Deepak Garg of Sector 40, alleged that the accused Ishwar Garg and his son Shivam Garg, had approached him in January 2016 through a common friend and told him that they have a manufacturing unit of plastic chairs, Sangsharmann Plast private limited Kala Amb, in district Sirmaur in Himachal Pradesh, with office in Sector 29, Chandigarh.
