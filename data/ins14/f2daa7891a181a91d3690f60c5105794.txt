"American Horror Story: NYC" sees a killer hunt the LGBTQ+ community.

Dubbed "Big Daddy," the killer uses the handkerchief code as part of his reign of terror.

The handkerchief code is a color-coded system from the 1970s used by gay and bisexual men.

Ryan Murphy is back for more terrifying delights with "American Horror Story: NYC," which sees a leather-clad serial killer called "Big Daddy" target the LGBTQ+ community.

This occurs against the backdrop of a mysterious virus ravaging Fire Island, as many men report symptoms of a rash that won't go away.
