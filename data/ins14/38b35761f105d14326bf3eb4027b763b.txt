Bonds Will Determine Where Bear Market in Stocks Goes Next
washingtonpost.com
economics
2022-10-21 10:05:51

The bear market in US stocks may be nearing the end of Act I (adjusting to higher interest rates), and many investors are looking ahead to Act II (adjusting to lower earnings.) But the transition is taking longer than anticipated, and some are questioning whether Act II is truly coming at all.That's reasonable, and that uncertainty could make for some compelling short-term rallies in a stock market that's been beaten up.Of course, the stock selloff in the first 10 months of 2022 has been driven mainly by the surge in Treasury yields, with the 10-year note rising to 4.
