iStockTitan, HUL, UltraTech Cement and Sun Pharma were the top gainers, rising about 1-5%. L&T, M&M, Nestle, Asian Paints, Reliance and Bajaj Finserv also opened higher.Led by strong corporate earnings growth by index majors and steady domestic inflows, benchmark indices defied the tepid global market to tread higher on Friday. At 9.19 am, the BSE Sensex was trading 203 points or 0.34% higher at 59,406. Nifty50 was trading at 17,622, up 58 points or 0.33%. Asian equities declined after a slide in US stock markets while US treasury yields surged to the highest since the global financial crisis.
