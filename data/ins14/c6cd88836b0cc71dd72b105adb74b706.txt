Hewlett Packard Enterprise Posts Flat Earnings, Revenue
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 20:20:00

By Dean Seal Hewlett Packard Enterprise Co. reported nearly flat earnings and revenue for its second quarter but said strong customer demand drove orders up 20%. HPE's second-quarter profit was $250 million, compared with $259 million a year earlier. Earnings were flat at 19 cents a share. Stripping out one-time items, earnings were 44 cents a share, just below Wall Street estimates of 45 cents a share, according to FactSet. Net revenue for the quarter ended April 30 was flat at $6.7 billion. Analysts polled by FactSet had expected $6.
