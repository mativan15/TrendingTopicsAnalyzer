Canonical Crypto Closes $20 Million to Back Developers Building infrastructure on the Blockchain

New "picks and shovels" VC fund led by serial entrepreneur and founder of Trusted Anand Iyer will invest in pre-seed and seed-stage start-ups 
SAN FRANCISCO, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Canonical Crypto, a newly launched venture fund focused on developer infrastructure on the blockchain, today announced it has raised $20 million in capital in an oversubscribed round for its inaugural fund. Leading the firm is founder and general partner Anand Iyer, a longtime Silicon Valley operator and investor, who most recently was a visiting partner at Pear VC, where he led their crypto investments.
