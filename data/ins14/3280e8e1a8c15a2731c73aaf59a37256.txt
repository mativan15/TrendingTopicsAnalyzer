Need for Speed Unbound is all set to be a game that fans of the franchise have wanted for a long time, as developers Criterion Games continue to share more details about the upcoming arcade racing title. In a new blog post titled Roll the Dice, the developers revealed the events that players might want to take part in Unbound in order to gain access to The Grand, which is touted to be Lakeshore's ultimate racing challenge, where players will face off against the best in the business. 
Time is money.
