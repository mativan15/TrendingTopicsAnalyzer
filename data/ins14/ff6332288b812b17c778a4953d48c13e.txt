___By: - Rashen SamarawickramaINTRODUCTIONA cryptocurrency or also termed crypto is an accumulation of binary data which is intended to operate as a mechanism of exchange where private coin ownerships are deposited in a computer-aided database utilizing extremely robust cryptography to ensure transaction credentials to guide the creation of supplementary coins and also to validate the transference of coin ownership.The cryptocurrency evolved with the assistance of Satoshi Nakamoto who is the legitimate sovereign architect of the coin we today call BITCOIN, Satoshi Nakamoto's fundamental approach was to design a peer-to-peer computerized cash scheme that succeeding aimed out to be a fabulous contrivance the society today uses usually.
