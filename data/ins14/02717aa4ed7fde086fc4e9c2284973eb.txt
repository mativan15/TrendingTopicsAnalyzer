DENVER, May 31, 2022 /CNW/ -- CryoMass Technologies Inc. ("CryoMass" or the "Company") (OTC: CRYM) is pleased to announce that the Canadian Intellectual Property Office has granted CryoMass a patent (#3064896) for its novel plant material separation process technology. The patent relates in general to separating plant particulates and, in particular, to a system and method for cryogenic separation of plant material. The patent will remain valid until 2039.
