66% CEOs in India see a recession in the next 12 months, survey shows
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 12:00:00

Around 66% of CEOs in India anticipate a recession in the next 12 months, compared to 86% CEOs globally, a private survey shows. Around 58% of Chief Executive Officers (CEOs) in India and globally feel that the recession which the global economy will see over the next twelve months will be mild and short with 55% of CEOs in India having plans in place to deal with it, as per the KPMG 2022 India CEO Outlook. The KPMG 2022 CEO Outlook surveyed CEOs globally as well as in India on their three-year outlook on the business and economic landscapes.
