Black Eyed Peas To Perform At East LA Classic
iheart.com
news
2022-10-18 22:05:00

On Friday, October 21, 2022, the Roosevelt High School Rough Riders will host cross-town rival Garfield High School Bulldogs for the 87th annual meeting of the gridiron clash at the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. This year's game will feature a halftime performance by global music supergoup, Black Eyed Peas. Students, administrators, Coliseum officials and Black Eyed Peas bandmates will come together to preview the big game, which is the homecoming event for both Eastside schools.  The East LA Classic is a celebration of community, tradition and is a source of pride for many families from Boyle Heights and East Los Angeles.
