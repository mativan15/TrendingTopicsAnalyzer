U.S. stocks open modestly lower as Treasury yields hit 14-year high, UK prime minister resigns
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 13:33:00

U.S. stocks opened marginally lower on Thursday as investors reacted to Treasury yields touching 14-year highs, along with Tesla Inc. TSLA, -7.74% earnings that fell short of expectations and the resignation of U.K. Prime Minister Liz Truss. The S&P 500 SPX, -0.17% fell 4.5 points, or 0.1%, to 3,690. The Nasdaq Composite COMP, -0.18% shed 23 points, or 0.2%, to 10,657. The Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +0.19% rose 44 points, or 0.2%, to 30,468, bolstered by International Business Machines Corp.
