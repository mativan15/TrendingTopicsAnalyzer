Lisa Fallon: Saturday's draw turns World Cup dream into a reality for Ireland
irishtimes.com
sport
2022-10-21 00:00:00

When Katie McCabe's team made history last week and qualified for their first major tournament, it was a watershed moment. No two ways about it.We may as well get used to the 7.30am starts because on Saturday morning in Auckland, Ireland will come out of Pot 3 and into a sobering 'Group of Death'.When Ireland's opponents at next year's World Cup are revealed – we could get England and Brazil! – it will feel very real.Not to worry, this is the unattainable dream come to life. This past week I found myself thinking of all the young girls at home watching the Scotland game and seeing Irish women take this giant leap.
