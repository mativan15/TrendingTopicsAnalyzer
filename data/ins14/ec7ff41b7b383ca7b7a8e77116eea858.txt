The only thing more precious than a ring is an NFT. Per Deadline, Warner Bros. Discovery is set to become the first major studio to release film footage as a multimedia NFT, dropping a bundle of The Lord of The Rings: Fellowship of the Ring NFTs tomorrow.The studio is partnering with Content Blockchain pioneer Eluvio to bring footage of Middle Earth and the Shire to life via the Web3 Movie Experience. 'Fans of The Lord of the Rings can now acquire, participate, and trade in an epic living media experience that will undoubtedly surprise and delight them,' said Michelle Munson, CEO and co-founder of Eluvio.
