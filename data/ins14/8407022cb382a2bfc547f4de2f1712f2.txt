NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The "Personal Cooling Device Market by Type and Geography - Forecast and Analysis 2022-2026" report has been added to Technavio's offering. The report expects the market size to grow by USD 1.73 billion, accelerating at a CAGR of 5.28% during the forecast period. To estimate the size of the market, Technavio has tracked the recent trends and developments in the household appliances industry. Some of the factors considered include the consumer base, adoption rate, revenue generated by household appliances companies manufacturing personal cooling devices, and per capita income among others.
