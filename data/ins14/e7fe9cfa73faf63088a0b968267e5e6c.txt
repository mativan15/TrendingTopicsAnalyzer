As Nick Jonas headed over to Sin City to join his brothers Joe and Kevin for the Jonas Brothers: Live in Vegas' residency, the singer got a special surprise from his wife Priyanka Chopra. Right before Nick could hit the stage for his first show, wifey PeeCee made sure to send the hunk a note of love and some balloons and a champagne bottle to celebrate his new stint. Nick took his Instagram stories to share a clip of the goodies and the large note that read, "Vegas Residency baby, crush it. Wish I could be there! Love Pri.
