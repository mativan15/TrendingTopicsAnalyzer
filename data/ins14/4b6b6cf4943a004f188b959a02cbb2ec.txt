Glantus Holdings PLC

Glantus Holdings Plc provides accounts payable automation and analytics solutions. Its products include Active AP Discovery, Intelligent AP Automation and Advanced AP Analytics. The firm has two operating units EMEA and the US, which provide sales, account management, project delivery, and customer support. The company was founded by Maurice Leonard Healy in 2018 and is headquartered in Clontarf, Ireland.
