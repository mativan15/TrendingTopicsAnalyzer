In this study, we use multiple methods to understand water contact and the related risk of acquiring schistosomiasis among school aged children. The study was conducted over two years in communities that have persistent high endemicity (>50% school aged children infected). Suzan Trienekens 3 Jun 2022 Although control measures have successfully reduced the burden of schistosomiasis in some countries in recent years, the complexity of transmission in many settings requires interdisciplinary research and integrated interventions.
