Russia fines Twitch for fake news
russiaherald.com
news
2022-10-18 22:00:00

The streaming platform promoted ?inaccurate information? about Moscow's military campaign in Ukraine, a court has ruledA Russian court has fined Twitch a total of eight million rubles ($130,000) after the US-based live streaming giant failed to delete materials containing what it said were falsehoods related to the Ukraine conflict, local media reported on Tuesday.According to RIA Novosti, the Tagansky Magistrate's Court in Moscow hit the online platform, a subsidiary of tech company Amazon, with two fines, each equivalent to four million rubles, in two separate cases.
