Did Danchenko Get a Polygraph? He Should Have
wsj.com
news
2022-10-19 18:40:00

Though the Journal has published several opinion articles on the conduct of the Federal Bureau of Investigation concerning the Steele dossier, including Kimberley Strassel 's 'Durham's FBI Indictment' (Potomac Watch, Oct. 14), none of them seem to have considered whether the bureau gave polygraph exams to its cooperating sources during the Russiagate investigation.

There are only a few reasons someone becomes a cooperating source for government investigators, and even fewer cases in which a polygraph isn't called for.
