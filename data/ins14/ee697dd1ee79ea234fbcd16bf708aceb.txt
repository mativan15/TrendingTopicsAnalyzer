LUCKNOW: As many as 51 out of 60 sanitation workers were found absent from duty during a surprise inspection conducted by Lucknow's municipal commissioner in the city's Labour Colony ward on Sunday. Subsequently, the commissioner directed Dr SK Rawat, the city's health officer, to serve a show cause notice to the area supervisor, who will have to explain the absence of these many sanitation workers.
