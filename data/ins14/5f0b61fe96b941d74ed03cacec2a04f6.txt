IANSSafety Tips: Do's & Don'ts while bursting crackersThis year, as in years past, there will be a lot of fanfare and celebration surrounding the festival of lights. While the Diwali festival definitely merits a big celebration, it's equally crucial to do it in the utmost safety. The number of eye damage cases that occur during the Diwali celebration is significantly higher than average due to the use of firecrackers. Elder family members should therefore keep an eye on the kids while they participate in the Diwali festivities.
