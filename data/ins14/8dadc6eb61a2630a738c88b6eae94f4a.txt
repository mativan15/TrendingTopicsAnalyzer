October 20, 2022 05:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MONTRÉAL--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nouveau Monde Graphite Inc. ('NMG' or the 'Company') (NYSE: NMG, TSX.V: NOU), Mitsui & Co., LTD ('Mitsui') (TYO: 8031) and Panasonic Energy Co., Ltd. ('Panasonic Energy'), a wholly owned subsidiary of Panasonic Holdings Corporation ('Panasonic') (TYO: 6752), have entered into a Framework Agreement establishing the terms of the commercial relationship between the parties to enable the next development steps of NMG's ore-to-battery-market integrated graphite project in Québec, Canada.
