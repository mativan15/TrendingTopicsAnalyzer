kmatija/iStock via Getty Images Cannabis company Bright Green (BGXX) stock rebounded 20% in late morning trading Thursday after plunging 30% in the previous session. Shares of the marijuana cultivator opened at $3.44. The stock recently changed hands at $4.22, up 20%, at approximately 10:45 a.m. ET. BGXX shares tumbled on Wednesday amid no apparent news, falling 30% to close at $3.54. The stock has been highly volatile since the company went public through a direct listing on May 17 with a reference price of $8 per share.
