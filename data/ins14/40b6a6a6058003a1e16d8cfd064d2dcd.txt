EVLI PLC AND EAB GROUP PLC HAVE SIGNED A COMBINATION AGREEMENT Evli Plc ('Evli') and EAB Group Plc ('EAB') announced on April 22, 2022 that they had signed a letter of intent to look into the possibility of combining their operations and that the intention of the parties is to sign the merger plan concerning the merger of the companies ('Merger Plan'), a combination agreement ('Combination Agreement') and other transaction agreements relating to the combination during May 2022.
