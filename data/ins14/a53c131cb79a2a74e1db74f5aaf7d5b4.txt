October 18, 2022 11:28 AM Eastern Daylight Time GEORGETOWN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AustinPx, Pharmaceutics and Manufacturing, a contract development and manufacturing organization (CDMO), today announced the appointment of Derek Hennecke and Marshall Crew as independent members of its board of directors. Appointments provide additional experienced CDMO leadership to drive growth in the complex formulation and manufacturing market



"I am pleased to welcome Derek and Marshall to the board of AustinPx, two seasoned CDMO leaders with broad expertise,' said Tim Scott, president and chief executive officer and member of the board of AustinPx.
