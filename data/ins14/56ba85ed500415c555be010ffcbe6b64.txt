Adobe India appoints Anindita Das Veluri as marketing head
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-06 07:30:00

Adobe has announced the appointment of Anindita Das Veluri as head of marketing for Adobe India. In this role, Veluri will lead Adobe's marketing strategy across the Indian market, reporting to Adobe Asia Pacific (APAC) marketing vice president Duncan Egan. In a career spanning over two decades, Veluri has held various leadership roles across sales and marketing. She joins Adobe from Deloitte. Prior to that, she was associated with IBM. Duncan Egan, vice president, marketing, Adobe APAC, said, "We're excited to welcome Veluri to drive Adobe's marketing in India.
