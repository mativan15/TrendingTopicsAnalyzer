New crack in Apple's armour as dozens strike at its stores in Australia
nzherald.co.nz
news
2022-10-18 22:30:45

Dozens of Apple workers in Australia have walked off the job. Strikes in the country's retail sector are rare. Photo / AP Dozens of Apple workers in Australia walked off the job Tuesday after negotiations over pay and working conditions stalled, the latest crack in the armour of the tech giant as it contends with a burgeoning unionisation movement in the United States and Europe. The action itself is small. About 150 retail workers, out of the company's nearly 4,000 Australian employees, voted to strike for an hour.
