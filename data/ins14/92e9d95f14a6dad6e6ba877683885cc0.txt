CSM Sport and Entertainment signs Chelsea office lease
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 18:20:00

CSM Sport and Entertainment has inked a deal for office space in Chelsea.

The sports and entertainment marketing company has grabbed 12,000 square feet of space on the fifth floor of 80 Eighth Ave. The office space will be used for administrative services.

CSM Sport and Entertainment was represented by Savills' Sean Hoffman. Building ownership, GFP Real Estate, was represented in-house by Matthew Mandell.

CSM has more than 25 locations worldwide, including in London, Paris, Hong Kong and Dubai.
