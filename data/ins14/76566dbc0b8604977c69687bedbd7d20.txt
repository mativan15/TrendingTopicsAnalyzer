One week, Mississippi zapped Vanderbilt for 448 passing yards. The next, the seventh-ranked Rebels bulldozed Auburn's defense for the same number of yards on the ground. Opponents, including LSU on Saturday, don't always know which facet of the Rebels offense will be featured on that particular day. Advantage, Ole Miss.

'Every game's independent,' Ole Miss coach Lane Kiffin said. 'Every game plan is different every week. I think we're a little unique that way in that you don't ever know what you're going to see when you play us.
