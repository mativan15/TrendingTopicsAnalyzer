Agreements Will Continue to Bring the Most Exciting Live Moments to Next-Gen Basketball Fans on MobileNEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Buzzer, the mobile platform for short-form live sports, today announced renewals of its multi-year partnerships with the NBA and WNBA. The platform will continue to distribute the live out-of-market game subscription services NBA League Pass and WNBA League Pass.
