Neymar arrives in Barcelona to stand trial on charges of fraud and corruption

2022-10-17 07:53:35

NEYMAR has arrived in Barcelona to stand trial on fraud and corruption charges.
Brazilian investment firm DIS has called for the Paris Saint-Germain forward to be sentenced to five years in prison.
Neymar arrived in court on Monday morning to face corruption chargesCredit: Reuters
The Brazilian is being tried over his transfer from Santos to BarcelonaCredit: Reuters
DIS owned 40 per cent of the rights to Neymar at the time of his transfer from Santos to Barcelona in 2013.
The company claim they lost out on a fair share of the fee because Neymar star was sold for below his value.
