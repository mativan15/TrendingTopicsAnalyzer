IRS makes changes for inflation IRS announces adjustments in response to inflation 03:17 Employers typically offer a period of open enrollment in the fall, when their workers are allowed to pick new health plans, enroll in a Flexible Spending Account or make other changes to their benefits. This year, there are some changes ahead that could help  employees, while also potentially opening up some financial pitfalls.  Among the biggest changes for 2023 are with two tax-advantaged health savings accounts — Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) and Health Savings Accounts (HSA).
