LOS ANGELES, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Versus Systems Inc. ('Versus' or the 'Company') (Nasdaq: VS) announced today that its new Click Play Win interactive advertising tool will amplify brand awareness for NBA Champion Matt Barnes' premium men's grooming company HUE for Every Man at the UCLA Anderson Global Sports Business Forum in Los Angeles on October 21, 2022. This pioneering advertising campaign tool from Versus, developed to drive brand engagement through rewarded gameplay, seamlessly aligns with the ethos of the HUE for Every Man brand.
