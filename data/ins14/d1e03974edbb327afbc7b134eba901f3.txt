West Palm Beach, Florida, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- DesCrypto Holdings Inc., ('Descrypto' or the 'Company' OTC: DSRO) is pleased to announce that it has successfully uplisted from the OTC Pink Market to the OTCQB Venture Market. The uplisting has been approved by OTC Markets Group and the Company will commence trading on the OTCQB on October 20, 2022.Mr. Howard Gostfrand, CEO stated, 'Uplisting to the OTCQB Venture Market is yet another important milestone that we set out to achieve. The listing on the OTCQB tier provides much greater visibility and transparency to investors which will allow for us to reach a wider audience.
