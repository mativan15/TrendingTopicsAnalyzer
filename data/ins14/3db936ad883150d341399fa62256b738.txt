Top Venture Funds Launch Multicultural Leadership Coalition to Accelerate Diversity of Board Directors and Advisors

NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Gold House, the leading Asian and Pacific Islander (API) changemaker organization, is partnering with top multicultural funds to launch the Multicultural Leadership Coalition (the "MLC") to accelerate the placements of leaders from underrepresented communities as Board Directors and Advisors of privately held and newly listed companies. MLC co-founders represent more than $1.5 billion in AUM and include BBG Ventures, Gaingels, Gold House, Golden Seeds, Harlem Capital, Halogen Ventures, Kapor Capital, MaC Venture Capital, Multitudes, the NAACP, SoGal Ventures, and VamosVentures.
