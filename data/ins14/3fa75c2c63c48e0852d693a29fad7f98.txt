On October 19, 2012, Karan Johar launched three newcomers with 'Student Of The Year' which was directed by him. Varun Dhawan, Sidharth Malhotra and Alia Bhatt made their movie debuts and as they complete 10 years in the industry, all the three actors have come such a long way!Sidharth Malhotra has reflected on his journey in an interview with News18 and said that he doesn't feel that it's been ten years, and more so because the pandemic took away two years. The actor added that his parents couldn't believe when he said he's about to bag a film.
