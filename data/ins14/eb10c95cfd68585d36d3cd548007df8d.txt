Daily flows down the Murray River are predicted to reach levels not seen in nearly 50 years.The South Australian government says people should be prepared but not alarmed for Murray River flows not seen since 1975A team of engineers and flood planners will check key levees this week to ensure they are ready for the predicted flowsAuthorities say despite the forecast people can still enjoy the Riverland but should be vigilant while in the riverThe updated forecast comes after recent flooding inundated some shacks and low-lying areas in the Riverland this week.
