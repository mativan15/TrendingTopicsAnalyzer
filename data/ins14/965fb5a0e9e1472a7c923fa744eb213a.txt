Industry-trusted analyst firm includes Malbek again as a leader in the CLM industry
PRINCETON, N.J., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Malbek, the most cutting-edge, AI-fueled Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) platform, was recognized as a CLM leader in Spend Matters' Spring SolutionMap. Known as the most rigorous, data-centric functionality assessment of procurement technologies in the industry, SolutionMap contains 500+ RFI requirements across 10 source-to-pay module and suite technology categories to pinpoint the actual capabilities of each participating vendor.
