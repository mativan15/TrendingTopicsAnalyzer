BEVERLY HILLS, Calif., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Genius Brands International, Inc. (NASDAQ: GNUS), today announced that it has repurchased 419,336 shares of common stock in a private transaction from an entity owned by Harold and Jennifer Chizick, former executives of the Company. This share repurchase was made in connection with a comprehensive settlement agreement resolving certain employment-related claims by Harold and Jennifer Chizick against the Company as well as the counterclaim by the Company against the Chizicks.
