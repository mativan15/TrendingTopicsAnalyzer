BASKING RIDGE, N.J., June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- With forecasts of severe weather that may become Tropical Storm Alex and threaten Florida this weekend, Verizon is more prepared than ever to respond and help customers and first responders this hurricane season.While hurricane season officially began earlier this week (June 1), Verizon engineers have been preparing year-round by conducting emergency drills, testing fiber and cell-site readiness, and strategically positioning extra recovery equipment that includes more than 500 portable assets.
