Meghan Markle accused of ‘staggering lack of self-awareness'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 19:40:00

Meghan Markle's latest interview makes the Duchess seem like a Disney character as a result of her ‘staggering lack of self-awareness', a royal commentator has claimed. Jonathan Sacerdoti was reacting to Variety Magazine's glossy profile of the 41-year-old in its latest edition.During the course of the 3,000-word article, Meghan talks about her life since moving to California, her Archetypes podcast, the forthcoming Netflix series featuring her and her husband Prince Harry, and being a mother to Archie, three, and Lilibet, one.
