Olaplex stock tanks 45% as company says salon-goers are spending less on hair-care services
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 11:55:00

Hair-care company Olaplex Holdings Inc. hasn't had an easy go of things on the public market.Shares of the company, which makes products aimed at restoring damaged hair, were already down more than 50% from their September 2021 initial-public-offering price of $21 as of Tuesday's close. The stock is on track for a further steep decline Wednesday, as it's off more than 45% premarket after Olaplex's OLPX, +1.98% management team dramatically reduced its financial outlook for the full year in the face of competitive and economic pressures.
