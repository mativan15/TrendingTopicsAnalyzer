The Yankees' reward for surviving a gritty, little Guardians team and two elimination games in one of the longest ever division series by days is no easy assignment: a trip to Texas and an ALCS date with hated Houston that begins immediately.
The Yankees' overwhelming task does present a delicious opportunity to avenge three upsetting past postseason defeats to the Astros the last seven years, including the galling ALCS encounter in 2017 that apparently does still count since the record book to date lists Astros as official World Series champions* that year.
