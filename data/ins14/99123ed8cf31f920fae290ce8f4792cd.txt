HARRY MAGUIRE says angry football fans overstepped the mark by sending him a bomb threat.
The Manchester United star and his family were forced to flee their home after the email threat in April. Cheshire police searched the premises but no device was found.
Harry Maguire says fans went way overboard with making a bomb threat against him and his familyCredit: PA
Maguire said: 'Obviously I'm going to be criticised. Manchester United paid a large sum of money for me, it is one of the most loved clubs in the world and one of the most hated.
