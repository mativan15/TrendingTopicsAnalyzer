Published: 01:01 EDT, 28 May 2022 | Updated: 01:04 EDT, 28 May 2022 A couple have only just found out they've technically been living in the wrong house because of a 130-year-old bureaucratic blunder, threatening their retirement plans. Peter and Cheryl Plowman bought and fixed up an investment property next door to their Candelo, NSW with plans to sell it to help fund their retirement. But when they got around to selling the property the prospective buyer's lawyer found a problem that meant the sale couldn't proceed, reported A Current Affair.
