Image source, Oxford Half Image caption, Runners including eventual winner Andrew Heyes (left) started the Oxford Half Marathon outside Balliol CollegeAbout 9,000 people have taken part in Oxford's half marathon.The race started outside Balliol and Trinity Colleges in Broad Street at 09:30 BST and finished on Parks Road. The number of competitors was slightly down on last year's, with organisers saying it was affected by the recent London Marathon and the Covid pandemic.Andrew Heyes, a former Oxford student, won the race in a time of 1:05:30.
