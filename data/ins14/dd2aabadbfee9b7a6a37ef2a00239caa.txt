SAN DIEGO, May 28, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The law firm of Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP announces that the Playstudios class action lawsuit seeks to represent investors who: (1) purchased or otherwise acquired Playstudios, Inc. (NASDAQ: MYPS; MYPSW) securities between June 22, 2021 and March 1, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period'), including, but not limited to, those who purchased or acquired Playstudios securities pursuant to the offering of the private investment in public equity ('PIPE' offering); (2) held common stock of Acies Acquisition Corp.
