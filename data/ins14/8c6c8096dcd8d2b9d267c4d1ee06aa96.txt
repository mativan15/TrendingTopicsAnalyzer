Will not providing work-from-home result in more employees quitting their jobs?
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 12:30:00

A recent survey conducted by a top HR firm covered over 700 companies and found that the work-from-home options are declining as the pandemic situation eased. 38% of companies allowed fully remote work in January, and the number stood at just 9% in August, a survey by Aon, an HR solutions company, said. Even as the companies ended remote work, it is apparent from the attrition rate that employees prefer work-from-home. Many huge companies have allowed a hybrid model. A hybrid model provides flexibility to its employees.
