Published: 17:36 EDT, 5 June 2022 | Updated: 17:43 EDT, 5 June 2022 An Aussie influencer couple known for pushing the boundaries of good taste with their raunchy posts have broken up after two years of dating.OnlyFans star Mikaela Testa, 22, and 'crypto bro' Atis Paul, 21, called it quits sometime last month, and confirmed the split on social media on Sunday.Atis was first to address the break-up - which fans had suspected for a week after Mikaela went on holiday without him - during an Instagram Q&A on a flight to Europe.
