By Associated Press Published: 15:29 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 15:29 EDT, 20 October 2022 MERCED, Calif. (AP) - The younger brother of a man charged in the kidnapping and killing of a family in central California pleaded not guilty Thursday to charges he helped his brother. Alberto Salgado was charged with conspiracy to commit robbery, accessory after the fact, and arson of property, the Merced County District Attorney's Office said. Salgado, 41, was arrested days after authorities arrested his older brother, Jesus Salgado, 48.
