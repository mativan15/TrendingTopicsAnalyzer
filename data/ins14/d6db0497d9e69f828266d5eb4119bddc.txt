Shares of Larsen & Toubro Infotech Ltd. rose 4.14 per cent to Rs 4165.6 at 10:10AM hours (IST) on Monday. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up start to the session. As per data available on BSE, total traded quantity on the counter stood at 14,316 shares with a turnover of Rs 5.9 crore till 10:10AM (IST). The stock traded at a price-to-earnings (P/E) multiple of 31.76, while the price-to-book value ratio stood at 12.22. Return on equity (ROE) was at 26.05 per cent, according to exchange data.
