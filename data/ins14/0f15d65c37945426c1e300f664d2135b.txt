Sudha Bharadwaj, a human rights lawyer and trade unionist, spoke about Rhea Chakraborty in a new interview. She said that during her time in jail, she saw Rhea, and recalled how the actor had no airs about herself as she mingled with inmates and even performed for them on her last day in jail. Rhea, who was the girlfriend of late actor Sushant Singh Rajput at the time of his death in 2020, had left Byculla jail after spending 28 days in judicial custody.
