EDEN PRAIRIE, Minn. & REHOVOT, Israel--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--Stratasys Ltd. (NASDAQ: SSYS), a leader in polymer 3D printing solutions, today announced that it has been named an official partner of Toyota Racing Development (TRD). The partnership will make its debut with 3D printed production parts on the forthcoming Toyota GR86 for the GR Cup, a new single-make racing series sanctioned by SRO America.This press release features multimedia.
