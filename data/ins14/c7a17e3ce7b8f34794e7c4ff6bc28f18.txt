Karnataka: Property registrations soar, revenue at all-time high
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 05:00:00

BENGALURU: Despite floods, high interest rates, rising inflation and the withdrawal of 10% rebate on guidance value, property registration in Karnataka has continued its upward trajectory in the six months ending September, reports Manu Aiyappa.However, this year's data shows that while nearly nine lakh property registrations were recorded between June and September, revenue of Rs 5,699cr, the highest in recent times, flowed into the exchequer. This is Rs 935cr higher than last year's revenue for the four months.
