Four arrested in hawala racket, over ₹1 crore seized
.
news
2022-10-21 17:03:29

The Shahinayathgunj police and Commissioner's Task Force (South) on Friday arrested four persons who were allegedly carrying out hawala transactions in the city. The police seized ₹1, 10,73,400 unaccounted cash from their possession. According to the police, Kamlesh Kumar, a resident of Chudibazar and dealing in plastic bags at Begum Bazar, was the main accused. He shared his plan of earning easy money with Rahul Agarwal, and together they employed Ashok Kumar and Ratan Singh for collection and delivery of money to customers.
