Published: 00:37 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 00:37 EDT, 20 October 2022 The Silent Hill franchise is being revived after a decade in limbo. Konami, the Japanese company that owns the rights to the video game series, announced the new projects during a live global streaming event on Thursday.A new film titled Return to Silent Hill is in production with French director Christophe Gans, who directed the first Silent Hill movie in 2006. The Silent Hill franchise is being revived after a decade in limbo with a new movie and three new gamesAccording to Gans, the movie will be based on the game Silent Hill 2, which is arguably the most popular entry in the franchise.
