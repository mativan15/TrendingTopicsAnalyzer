LOS ANGELES, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Norman® USA's award-winning WCMA-certified "Best for Kids™" products will be featured in an episode of Designing Spaces™ airing on Lifetime TV® on October 21, 2022 titled "Hidden Hazards in the Home: Innovations in Peace of Mind for Today's Parents."
For more than 50 years, Norman®'s innovation in the window fashion industry has positioned the brand to be the leader in quality, innovation, safety, and fashion. They have taken an industry stand by discontinuing the manufacturing and selling of corded window blinds in 2022.
