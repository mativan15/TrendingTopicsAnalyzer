Wheels Up Experience Shares Rise 13% on $270M Loan Structure
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 17:42:00

By Denny Jacob Wheels Up Experience Inc. shares rose 13% to $1.16 after the company received $270 million in financing through an enhanced equipment trust certificates loan structure. The on-demand private aviation provider said its subsidiary, Wheels Up Partners LLC, issued equipment notes, the sale and issuance of which provided Wheels Up with roughly $259 million in net proceeds. "These proceeds give us additional financial flexibility to invest in our business as we pursue our goal for positive adjusted Ebitda in 2024," said Chief Financial Officer Todd Smith.
