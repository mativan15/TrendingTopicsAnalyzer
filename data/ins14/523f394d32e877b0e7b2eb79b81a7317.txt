What Is an LLC?
LLC stands for 'limited liability company' and describes a business structure that is easier to create and maintain than a corporation, but more formal than a partnership or sole proprietorship. It provides personal legal protection against liability for the activities of the business, which encourages entrepreneurs to start their businesses since only their investment is at risk — not their personal assets.
