Kansas Overseas Appreciated by the Government Of India (Finance Ministry)
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-18 14:23:08

Kansas Overseas has been acknowledged by the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, for prompt filing of returns and payment of Goods and Services Tax in Financial Year 2021-22. The Indian Government has initiated steps to directly communicate to the GST taxpayers about their contribution.When we spoke to the Kansas Overseas Director, Ms Hima Bindu P., about the acknowledgement from the Government, she said, 'we are glad to receive appreciation from the Government for fulfilling our obligation'.
