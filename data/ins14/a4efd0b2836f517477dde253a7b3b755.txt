Mukesh Ambani replaces Gautam Adani to become India's richest man
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-03 10:00:00

Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) chairman Mukesh Ambani has replaced Gautam Adani to become Asia and India's richest man owing to a surge in the stock prices of RIL.For the past few months, Gautam Adani had been enjoying the position due to accelerated prices of stocks of the Adani group. However, with a total net worth of $99.7B, Mukesh Ambani has pushed Adani (with net worth of $98.7B) to second position as per a report published by Bloomberg.
