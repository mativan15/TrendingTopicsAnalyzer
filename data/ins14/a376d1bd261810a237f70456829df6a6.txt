Summer days are on the horizon, and that means more fun in the sun, long beach days and some of the best sales of the season, including the annual 2022 Nordstrom Anniversary Sale.

This year's sales event officially kicks off on July 15 and will run through July 31. However, if you are a Nordstrom cardmember, you can start shopping as early as July 9.

Whether you're looking for fashion, beauty or home items, the annual sale not only has a wide variety of brands to shop from, but it also features over 100 new brands, including Nike, Charlotte Tilbury, Cult Gaia, Free People and list goes on.
