FREMONT, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Amprius Technologies, Inc. ("Amprius") (NYSE: AMPX), a leader in ultra-high energy density lithium-ion batteries with its Silicon Nanowire Anode Platform, today announced that the company was awarded a $50 million cost sharing grant from the U.S. Department of Energy ('DOE')'s Office of Manufacturing and Energy Supply Chains ('MESC').
