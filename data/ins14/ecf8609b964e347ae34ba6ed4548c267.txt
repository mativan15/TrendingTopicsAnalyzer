Limited use cases a stumbling block to India's 5G buildout
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-06 03:15:00

Globally, 59 countries have already deployed 5G since deployment of commercial 5G networks began in 2020. 5G networks could reach 12% of world mobile connections (1.1 billion) and generate revenues up to $1.3 trillion by 2025 for telecom operators.At a time when the Indian government and telcos are betting on rapid rollout of 5G networks to boost growth in various sectors and help improve governance and ease of doing business, lack of India-specific use cases risks slowing down development of the next-generation technology.
