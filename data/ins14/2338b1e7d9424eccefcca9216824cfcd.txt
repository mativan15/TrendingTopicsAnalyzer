Sam Luu and Hillary Cohen's Every Day Action is working to reallocate food waste on TV and film sets. (Photo: Hillary Cohen/Instagram)Trucks loaded with gourmet meals — from steak to pasta to salmon and filet mignon — are frequently seen driving past homeless encampments in Los Angeles en route to Hollywood film locations.And the worst part is that there would be two or three trays of leftover food headed for the trash bin.This wasteful habit didn't sit well with longtime television and film producer Hillary Cohen, an associate director on NCIS: Los Angeles.
