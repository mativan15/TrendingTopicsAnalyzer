RIO DE JANEIRO, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- Rayssa does it again! Monster Energy congratulates team rider Rayssa Leal on claiming first place in the Women's Skateboard Street competition at STU Open 2022 in Rio de Janeiro this weekend. In a high-stakes final on Sunday, the 14-year-old from Imperatriz, Brazil, took the win and $50,000 cash prize with her signature blend of finesse and technical street skating.
