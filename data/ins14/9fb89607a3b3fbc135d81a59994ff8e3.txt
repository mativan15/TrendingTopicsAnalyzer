London October 10(Reuters) - Sterling weakened on Thursday as political chaos unfolded in the UK and as a Bank of England official's comments prompted a further scaling back of bets on a full percentage-point November rate hike.

British Prime Minister Liz Truss battled to retain her grip on power, a day after a second top minister quit and rowing and jostling broke out among her lawmakers in parliament.
