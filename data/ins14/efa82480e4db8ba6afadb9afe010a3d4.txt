For many business owners, growing your business is a priority. But what business owners often may not consider is that not all growth is created equal. When developing your business, you need to monitor specific key performance indicators to ensure that you grow your business with profit. An excellent way to monitor if you are growing your business profitably is to monitor your profit margin. Your profit margin is the percentage of revenue that your company keeps as profit after accounting for all costs and expenses.
