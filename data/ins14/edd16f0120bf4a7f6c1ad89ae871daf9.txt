By Reuters Published: 09:59 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 09:59 EDT, 19 October 2022 AMSTERDAM, Oct 19 (Reuters) - New restrictions by the United States government on exports of semiconductor technology could affect up to 5% of ASML Holding's order backlog, CEO Peter Wennink said on Wednesday. On a call with analysts, Wennink said ASML is not directly impacted as its products and most of their parts are made in Europe. However, indirect effects could still lead to ASML's Chinese customers cutting orders.
