June 02, 2022 08:45 AM Eastern Daylight Time HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Magnolia Oil & Gas Corporation (NYSE: MGY) ('Magnolia' or the 'Company') today announced the pricing of the previously announced underwritten block trade of 7,500,000 shares of the Company's Class A common stock (the 'Class A Common Stock') by certain affiliates of EnerVest, Ltd. (the 'Selling Stockholders') resulting in total gross proceeds of $203.6 million (the 'Offering'). The Offering is expected to close on or about June 6, 2022, subject to customary closing conditions.
