BOULDER, Colo. and NEW YORK, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- TIFIN, a fintech platform using artificial intelligence (AI) and investment-driven personalization to shape the future of investor experiences, today announced the launch of their Magnifi+ platform. Magnifi + features investment intelligence from leading financial pros through proprietary and engaging video content.
