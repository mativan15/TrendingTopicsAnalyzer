After going through the process of how to start an LLC in Nebraska, you don't want that hard work to go to waste. You should stay on top of annual filings, pay your taxes and comply with any laws that govern your LLC.
Are There Annual Filings for Nebraska LLCs?
All LLCs are required to file a report once every two years on April 1, starting in the next odd year after your LLC is formed. For example, an LLC that's formed in the year 2022 will file its first annual report by April 1 of 2023. An LLC that's formed in 2023 won't file its first report until April 1 of 2025.
