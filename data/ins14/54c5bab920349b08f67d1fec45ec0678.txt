London North Centre is a battleground like few others in Thursday's election

LONDON, Ont.—It's a tight, three-way fight that spotlights key provincewide issues and comes steeped in political drama, from bragging rights to a job windfall that could point the way to reviving some of Ontario's struggling downtowns.Home to the sprawling campus of Western University, two major hospitals, the city core and suburban neighbourhoods, the riding of London North Centre is a battleground like few others in Thursday's election — making it a proving ground for the claims and ambitions of all three major parties.
