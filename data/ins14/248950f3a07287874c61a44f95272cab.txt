Sun Pharmaceutical Industries, part of the pharma sector, which has rallied more than 200% from COVID low of 2020 could well surpass Rs 1,000 levels to hit a fresh multi-year high in the next 2 months, suggest experts. The pharma stock rallied from Rs 315 recorded on 23 March 2020 to Rs 979 as on 17 October 2022 which translates into an upside of over 200% in 2 years Short-term traders who missed the rally can look at buying the stock now or on dips for a possible target above Rs 1,000 in the next 1-2 months, suggest experts.
