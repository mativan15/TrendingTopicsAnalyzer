San Jose State's offense stalls as Spartans fall at Fresno State
santacruzsentinel.com
news
2022-10-16 06:32:52

FRESNO –– San Jose State's hot streak came to an end Saturday night at Valley Children's Stadium as the Spartans fell 17-10 to Fresno State.
SJSU, which had won three straight games, dropped to 4-2 overall and 2-1 in Mountain West. The Spartans remain in first place in the conference's West Division after UNLV's 42-7 loss to Air Force.
Fresno State quarterback Logan Fife had the game-winning play on a seven-yard touchdown pass to Nikko Remigio in the third quarter.
The Spartans, who had outscored opponents 107-29 in their past three games, struggled on offense against the Bulldogs.
