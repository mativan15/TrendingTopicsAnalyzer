Voting by mail is underway in Leamington.CBC News reached out to all the candidates in Leamington, asking the same questions so voters can learn a bit more about who may be on their next council.The following candidates were invited to complete the questionnaire via email but did not respond by deadline:Mayoral candidate Jimmy Simoni.Deputy mayoral candidates Shelly J Quick and John Tofflemire.Councillor candidates Gerald Barros, Bill Dunn, Heather Latam, Christine Lehn, Dave Metcalfe, Christine Montgomery, Oscar Ramirez and Paul Tiessen.
