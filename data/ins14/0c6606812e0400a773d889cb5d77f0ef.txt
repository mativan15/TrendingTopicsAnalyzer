By Reuters Published: 08:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 08:39 EDT, 20 October 2022 By Steven Scheer JERUSALEM, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Alphabet's Google on Thursday activated a local cloud region for Israel, offering cloud services to the country's government and military in a move expected to boost jobs and economic growth. Israel signed a deal with Google and Amazon Web Services (AWS) in May 2021 for the more than $1 billion four-phase "Nimbus" project. As part of the project, billions of shekels will be invested in local infrastructure, Israel's Finance Ministry said in a statement on Thursday.
