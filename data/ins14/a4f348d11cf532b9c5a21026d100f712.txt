Work doesn't look the same as it did 50 years ago. Recent college graduates and young employees are often looking for flexible work environments at companies that share their values.

In this podcast series on embracing a leadership mindset, Jim Wallace, BPM CEO, and John Sensiba, Sensiba San Filippo managing partner, discuss their organizations' missions, and how they focus on the well-being of their employees and their communities.
