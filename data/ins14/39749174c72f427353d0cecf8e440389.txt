The Rajasthan returning officer for the elections of the Congress national president reached here with ballot boxes, a day before over 400 delegates in the state are set to vote in the polls. Senior party leaders Mallikarjun Kharge and Shashi Tharoor are in the fray for the post. As many as 414 delegates from Rajasthan will vote for the national president from 10 am to 4 pm at state Congress headquarters here. State Congress spokesperson Swarnim Chaturvedi said Pradesh Returning Officer Rajendra Singh Kumpawat reached with ballot boxes here.
