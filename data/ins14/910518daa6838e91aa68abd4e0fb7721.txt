Published: 13:01 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 13:01 EDT, 19 October 2022 Meghan Markle decided to re-record a bombshell interview with a pet US publication because it was first done before the Queen died and she wanted to give her tribute, it was revealed today.The Duchess of Sussex praised Her Majesty's 'warmth' and 'leadership' after she passed away on September 8 but did not speak about other members of the Royal Family including her 'in-laws' to avoid any 'distraction' from mourning her death, Variety magazine said.
