The brightly lit Orionid meteor shower is set to peak tomorrow, October 21. Know all about it and how to watch it live online.

But before that, you will also get to experience the spectacle of a meteor shower. Yes, and the peak of the Orionid meteor shower is set to take place tomorrow, October 21. Orionid is a major meteor shower event that takes place in the month of October.
