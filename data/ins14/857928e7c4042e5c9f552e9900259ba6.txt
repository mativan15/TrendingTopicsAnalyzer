 The Punjab government on Monday urged the Centre to resume domestic flights from Adampur, Pathankot, Sahnewal and Bathinda airports in the state.Principal secretary of civil aviation of Punjab Rahul Bhandari made the appeal during an event here.He said these airports were part of the regional connectivity service of the Government of India and services from these airports were stopped during the coronavirus pandemic.
