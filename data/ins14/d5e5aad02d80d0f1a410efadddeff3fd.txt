Variety - TV News Disney+ Swoops On ‘Ralph & Katie'Keshet International (Ki) has closed a weighty multi-territorial deal with Disney+ on 'Ralph & Katie,' the new 6×30' comedy-drama spinoff from 'The A World.' Sale forms part of a wider agreement that includes all three seasons of 'The A Word,' the BBC One drama. Disney+ has licensed multiple rights for both titles across all of Europe, (excluding the U.K.), the Middle East and Africa.'The A Word' will be available on Disney+ across the Nordics and Belgium.
