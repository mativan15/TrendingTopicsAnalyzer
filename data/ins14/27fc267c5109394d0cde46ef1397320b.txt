By CBSLA Staff May 27, 2022 / 11:25 AM / CBS Los Angeles Authorities arrested a trio of suspects wanted in connection with an attempted home invasion in Perris on Wednesday.The scene unfolded Wednesday evening on Avishan Drive, when a homeowner in the area contacted Riverside County Sheriff's Department to report that a man had entered his home and attempted to rob him at gunpoint.At some point, after the suspect pistol-whipped the homeowner and demanded money, he was able to wrestle the firearm away and restrain the suspect at the scene.
