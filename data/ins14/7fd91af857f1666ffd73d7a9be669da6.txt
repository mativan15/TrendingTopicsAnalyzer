Bengaluru: BBMP to share data, conduct drone survey to boost property tax collection
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-04 12:15:00

BENGLAURU: If you have not paid property tax, then beware, the Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) is now verifying it from Bangalore Electricity Supply Company (BESCOM).In a first, the BBMP has tied up with BESCOM to share data on residential properties. Recently, during data verification, BBMP officials found that a plot in Koramangala was shown as vacant land and hence the owner was paying a nominal property tax.However, when the officials checked with BESCOM, the property was found to draw electricity.
