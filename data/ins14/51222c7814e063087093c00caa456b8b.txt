Paradise, lost: Kiwis who returned during the pandemic are leaving again
stuff.co.nz
economics
2022-05-28 17:00:00

Kiwis returned to New Zealand in droves when the Covid pandemic hit, but many of them are now leaving again. Kelly Dennett reports. When Rhys Jarvis returned home before the borders shut in March 2020 he felt like a Covid-19 refugee. Jarvis was setting up his own freelance travel consultancy in Europe when flights were suddenly grounded. 'And then boom, it all collapsed. I was probably more of a desperate case than a few other people would be... I made the call to come back down under. I thought, let's see if I can rejig my life here.
