Image caption, Kristine Green says it is "lovely" to think the work she has done is appreciatedA woman who spearheaded a campaign to help struggling families get to a play-off final has been recognised in the Queen's Jubilee birthday honours.Kristine Green, chair of Grimsby Town supporters' trust, has been awarded a British Empire Medal (BEM) for services to football in North East Lincolnshire.As well as her work with Grimsby Town fans and her fundraising efforts, she also volunteers for a charity.
