GAIL shares rise 0.77% as Nifty drops
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 07:15:00

Shares of GAIL (India) Ltd. traded 0.77 per cent up at Rs 85.4 on Thursday at around 12:12PM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex declined 143.19 points to 58964.0. As many as 334,553 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 2.83 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 115.63 and a 52-week low of Rs 83.0. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 95.57, while 50-DMA was at Rs 88.53. If a stock trades above 50 DMA and 200 DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is up.
