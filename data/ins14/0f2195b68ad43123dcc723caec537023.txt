NHL roundup: Jackets rally in OT as Canucks collapse again
reuters.com
news
2022-10-19 05:48:05

October 19 - Vladislav Gavrikov scored at 3:21 of overtime as the Columbus Blue Jackets rallied for a 4-3 home victory over Vancouver on Tuesday as the Canucks blew a multi-goal lead and lost for a record fourth straight game to open the season.Off a two-on-one, Yegor Chinakhov (two assists) slid a cross-slot pass to Gavrikov, who blew it past Vancouver goaltender Spencer Martin (29 saves) for the Blue Jackets' first win in four tries this season.Gavrikov also added an assist, Johnny Gaudreau registered a goal and an assist, and Justin Danforth and Zach Werenski also scored for Columbus, which got 32 saves from Elvis Merzlikins.
