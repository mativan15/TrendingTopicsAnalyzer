eToro vs. Coinbase [2022]: Where Should You Invest in Crypto?

2022-06-05 16:39:00

Investing in cryptocurrency might help you add diversity to your portfolio. However, learning how to buy and sell digital currencies used to come with a steep learning curve. Exchanges such as eToro and Coinbase aim to change this by offering simple trading platforms that could be easy to use.

eToro offers a unique approach to investing via a social form of trading that allows you to copy other traders. While Coinbase is one of the oldest crypto asset exchanges still in operation, and it offers an intuitive trading interface.
