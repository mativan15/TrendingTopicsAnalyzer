SL Green, Caesars partner to pursue gaming license for Times Square (NYSE:SLG)
seekingalpha.com
business
2022-10-20 12:18:06

Pawel Gaul/iStock via Getty Images SL Green Realty (NYSE:SLG) and Caesars Entertainment (NASDAQ:CZR) have partnered to pursue a gaming license for the planned Caesars Palace Times Square. SL Green and Caesars will redevelop 1515 Broadway in the heart of Times Square into Caesars Palace Times Square, which will be include a Broadway theater for The Lion King. The proposed resort casino will come with significant security and traffic improvements in the Times Square area, including better security infrastructure and 24/7 monitoring.
