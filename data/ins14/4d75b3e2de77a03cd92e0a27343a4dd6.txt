Cumbria hospitals charity left £350,000 in wills

2022-10-16 10:31:33

Image source, UNIVERSITY HOSPITALS OF MORECAMBE BAYImage caption, Furness General Hospital in Barrow is run by UHMBTNearly £350,000 has been left in wills to south Cumbria hospitals this year. The Bay Hospitals Charity said it had received £250,000 from one individual as well as three donations of more than £20,000.It said NHS budgets cover essential services but donations help in providing extras, including specialist equipment. Head of charities and fundraising Suzanne Lofthouse said many people give money "as a way of saying thank you".
