Ruth Taylor started her journey with managing money well while working to pay off £21,000 of debt (Image: LancsLive)A money-conscious mum has shared how she managed to save £10,000 in just seven months by making lifestyle changes. Ruth Taylor, 43, first started her journey through managing money well, whilst working to pay off £21,000 of debt.Ruth managed to be completely debt-free after just eight months.Explaining her situation, Ruth told LancashireLive that the debt began when she moved into her first home with her family, deciding to furnish the house on credit and pay for a new boiler by taking out another credit card.
