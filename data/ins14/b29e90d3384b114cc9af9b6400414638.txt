Published: 11:32 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 11:32 EDT, 1 June 2022 Rory McIlroy insists he is 'indifferent' about the line-up for the first event of the controversial Saudi golf league - after former world No 1 Dustin Johnson surprisingly signed up to the competition. Johnson, a two-time major champion now No 13 in the world, is the highest-ranked player and jewel in the crown of a field that currently includes 26 of the top 150 players in the world for the event at Centurion.  Asked his opinion on the line-up ahead of playing in the Memorial Tournament this week, McIlroy said: 'I'd say indifferent is probably the way I would describe it.
