chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-19 20:25 History shows that cooperating with China has always served the United States well. When the two countries broke ice in 1972 and established diplomatic relations in 1979, the trade volume between them skyrocketed. When China joined the World Trade Organization in 2001, it gave a tremendous boost to globalization, from which the US has been one of the main beneficiaries.
Connected with buyers and sellers around the world, the US has sustained and expanded its leading position in the global economy with US companies focusing on the high end of the value chain and innovation.
