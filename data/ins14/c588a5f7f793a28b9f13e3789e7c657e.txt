PM Modi, CM Yogi Adityanath & top biz tycoons at UP's 3rd groundbreaking event today

LUCKNOW: Calling it a testimony of their commitment to bring about an enduring and positive change in the life of citizens in the state, the Yogi Adityanath government would be holding the state's third groundbreaking ceremony on Friday. Foundation stone of projects worth Rs 80,000 crore would be laid by PM Narendra Modi in presence of top business tycoons.Citing CM, an official spokesperson said: 'UP is onboard a transformational journey to turn PM's dream of Atmanirbhar Bharat into a reality that contributes towards achievement of the one trillion-dollar economy goal.
