Sonia determined to appear before ED on June 8: Surjewala
thehindu.com
news
2022-06-02 15:18:59

Congress president Sonia Gandhi, who tested positive for COVID-19 and isolated herself, is determined to appear before the Enforcement Directorate (ED) on June 8 in connection with a money laundering case, the party General Secretary Randeep Surjewala said here on Thursday.Addressing reporters on the sidelines of the Karnataka Congress' Nava Sankalpa Shivir here, Mr. Surjewala said, "She is perfectly alright and recovering."The ED has summoned Ms. Gandhi and her son Lok Sabha MP Rahul Gandhi to appear before it on June 8 in connection with a money laundering case linked to the National Herald newspaper-AJL issue.
