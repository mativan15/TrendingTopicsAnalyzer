Stevie Winners Will Be Presented Their Awards On June 13 in New York CitySAN DIEGO, June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Netradyne, a leader in artificial intelligence (AI) and edge computing focusing on driver and fleet safety, was named the winner of a Gold Stevie® Award in the New Product & Service category for The 20th Annual American Business Awards® today. The American Business Awards are the U.S.A.'s premier business awards program. All organizations operating in the U.S.A. are eligible to submit nominations – public and private, for-profit and non-profit, large and small.
