Codere Online Announces Changes in the Composition of its Board

Madrid, Spain and Tel Aviv, Israel, October 21, 2022 – (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) Codere Online (Nasdaq: CDRO / CDROW), a leading online gaming operator in Spain and Latin America (the 'Company'), today announced that Alejandro Rodino, who has served in its Board of Directors since the consummation of the business combination between Codere Online1 and DD32 on November 30, 2022, has resigned as Director of the Company and been replaced by Borja Fernández. Mr. Rodino was one of four Directors appointed by Codere Newco S.
