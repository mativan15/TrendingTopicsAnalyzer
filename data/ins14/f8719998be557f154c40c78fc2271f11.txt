Chinese students stage rare COVID protests ahead of Tiananmen anniversary
dw.com
world
2022-06-03 13:23:00

Hundreds of students in Beijing have called for freedom of movement amid concerns over tightening COVID restrictions on campuses. Videos circulated online showing students tearing down a metal wall.

Around 200 students protested against COVID rules at a Peking University campus in May
University students in Beijing have been staging protests against pandemic control measures over the past few weeks, demanding freedom of movement. Meanwhile, Shanghai is easing strict pandemic measures following more than two months of lockdown.
