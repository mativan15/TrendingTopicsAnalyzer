Back on track after two league wins on the bounce, Jurgen Klopp's side could move level on points with Manchester United with a win at the City Ground, even if for just a few hours.Given the pressure Klopp was under (externally at least) following a difficult start to the season, pushing closer to the top four would be a great way of further underlining their recent progress.Forest, meanwhile, stopped the rot at least with a creditable draw at Brighton this week but face a huge task to deny Liverpool.
