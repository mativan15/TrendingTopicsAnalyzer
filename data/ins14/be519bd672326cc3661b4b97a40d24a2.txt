In this blog we will be trying to explain in detail the types of chat bots, their development, and how they're made.Before we start properly, let's get a few basics down pat. A chat bot (Conversational AI) is an automated program that simulates human conversation through text messages, voice chats, or both. It learns to do that based on a lot of inputs, and Natural Language Processing (NLP).Chat bots today are integral in many industries and companies for Social Media, E-Commerce, Customer Services and even Healthcare.
