Altru hires new executive vice president of hospital operations, chief nursing officer

Oct. 14—GRAND FORKS — Altru Health System has hired an Iowa man as its new executive vice president of operations and chief nursing officer.

Cory Geffre comes to Altru with 22 years of experience in health care leadership and business development, according to an Altru release sent to the Herald Friday afternoon. He most recently has served at a medical center in Ames, Iowa, and before that, he was vice president and chief nursing officer at Avera St. Luke's in Aberdeen, S.D.

"I am thrilled to welcome Cory to our executive team and the nursing team at Altru," CEO Todd Forkel said in the release.
