Alithya Agrees to Buy Datum Consulting Group for Up to $45.5M
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 10:53:00

By Adriano Marchese Alithya Group Inc. has agreed to acquire U.S.-based software development and consulting company Datum Consulting Group LLC and its affiliates for up to $45.5 million. The Canadian digital strategy and technologies company said Wednesday that the acquisition, which is immediately accretive, deepens its expertise in the insurance industry with proprietary modernization systems and enhances Alithya's recurring revenue profile. The deal price include an upfront cash consideration of around $13.
