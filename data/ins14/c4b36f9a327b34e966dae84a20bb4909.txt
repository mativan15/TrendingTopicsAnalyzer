Who was Pat Tate?
Pat Tate ran the Essex Boys gang which was involved mainly in drug deals.
He was a bodybuilder who weighed 18 stone and on the side, he was getting convicted of armed robbery and drug offences.
Former football hooligan and bouncer Bernard O'Mahoney told Birmingham Live that Tate suffered from a drug problem.
