Telangana Government Fixes BE, BTech Fees; Asks Institutions Not To Charge Capitation Fee
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-20 10:27:04

New Delhi: Telangana government has fixed the fees for BE and BTech programmes in private-unaided engineering colleges for the academic years between 2022-23 and 2024-25. The government has fixed the fees after approving the fee hike in 159 engineering colleges in the state according to the recommendation by the Telangana Admission and Fee Regulatory Committee (TAFRC). Telangana engineering institutions have also been directed to collect annual tuition fee every year in advance either in lump sum or in installments and not to charge capitation fees.
