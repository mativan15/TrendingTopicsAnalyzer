How Much Small Business Owners Should Pay Themselves
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-20 07:42:28

Up to one-quarter of small business founders, especially new ones, don't pay themselves as they do employees. That's a mistake which can add to financial stress and end up negatively impacting their business decision making. Half of business owners take home less than $100,000 annually, and while there is no one-size-fits-all approach, too many business owners skimp on their own salary. Many small business owners have trouble figuring out an appropriate take-home pay as the company expands, and some don't pay themselves at all.
