Global headwinds are shaking Japan's growth outlook. The fallout from rising geopolitical risk, Omicron's lingering presence and inflation threaten to stall the recovery of the world's third-largest economy. After 2021's post-pandemic bump, real GDP is expected to grow 2.1% this year, then slow to 1.
