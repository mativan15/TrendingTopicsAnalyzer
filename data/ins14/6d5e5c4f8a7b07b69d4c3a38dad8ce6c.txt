Right-wing anger over a defamation judgment reflects disgust at seeing the rules applied to them.Michelle McLoughlin/ReutersOctober 17, 2022, 6 AM ETTo many on the right, Alex Jones is a free-speech martyr. On Wednesday, the conspiracy theorist was hit with a nearly $1 billion defamation judgment, after having smeared the families of the victims of the 2012 Sandy Hook massacre as 'crisis actors' in a government plot to ban guns. Twenty first graders and six teachers were killed in the shooting.In response to the verdict, the second time a court has found Jones liable, prominent conservative-media figures began insisting that Jones's free-speech rights had been violated.
