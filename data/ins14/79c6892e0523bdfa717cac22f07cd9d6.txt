REUTERS/Neil HallLONDON, Oct 18 (Reuters) - British exploration and production company Ithaca Energy is planning a London listing it said on Tuesday, stoking hopes for a fourth quarter recovery in a stock market overshadowed by the dismal economic outlook.Ithaca Energy will use the proceeds to help it become a key player in UK energy security, the firm said in a statement, adding it had delivered a 5.4 times increase in equity value between 2018 and end-June 2022.
