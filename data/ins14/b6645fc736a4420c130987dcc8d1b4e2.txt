'This is the day to shape our future:' Hamiltonians head to the polls on election day

2022-06-02 15:34:00

Hamiltonians are reporting voting processes are going smoothly on election day. (Eva Salinas/CBC)While there have been technical issues at some polling stations across the problems, Doug Flockhart says everything went "smooth" at his polling station near Ottawa Street North.Polls have officially opened in Ontario after a month-long election campaign and will close at 9 p.m. tonight.Flockhart says "It's an easy thing to do."Flockhart, originally from Toronto, said although he wishes his party was "doing better," he is not focused on who will win.
