Spanning 25,000 square feet, the new office at Star Metals is more than 70% larger than its predecessor and will be designed to align with Sovos' future of work philosophy, 'In-Person for a Purpose,' enabling flexibility based on employees' preferences around hybrid work. ATLANTA (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Global tax software provider Sovos today announced that it is moving its office from the outskirts of Atlanta to the heart of the city in order to attract diverse and rich talent that will fill new roles in sales, technology and finance positions as the company expands its local team by 10 to 20 percent in the next year.
