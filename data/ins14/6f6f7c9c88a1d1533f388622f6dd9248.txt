Polls show Republicans flip key metric in 2022 midterm elections, Democrats 'blew their lead'
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-19 15:34:18

In September, some commentators were confident Democrats might be able to avoid a "red wave," but with just three weeks to midterm election day, momentum from the overturn of Roe v. Wade and President Biden's string of summer legislative wins could be waning.Polling for the 2022 generic congressional vote — a closely watched indicator for midterm performance — shows Democrats' average advantage over Republicans peaking in mid-September with a 1.3 percent lead, a decent advantage given that Biden's approval rating consistently hovered in the low 40s.
