Sensex rises! But these stocks fell 5% or more in Friday's session
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 12:00:00

NEW DELHI: Several stocks plunged in excess of 5 per cent in Mumbai trading on Friday even as equity benchmark BSE Sensex traded 632.13 points up at 54884.66 amid brisk buying in frontline bluechip counters. On the BSE, Unique Organics(down 19.95 per cent), Triveni Enterprises(down 19.95 per cent), Ltd.(down 19.58 per cent), Wallfort Fin(down 14.21 per cent) and KIC Metaliks(down 12.16 per cent) stood among the top losers in today's trade. In the Nifty pack, 37 stocks closed in the green, while 13 stocks ended in the red.
