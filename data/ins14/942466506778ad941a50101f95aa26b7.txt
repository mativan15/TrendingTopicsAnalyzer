June 03, 2022 02:46 PM Eastern Daylight Time SEATTLE--(BUSINESS WIRE)--PEMCO Mutual Insurance, the Northwest's largest locally based auto and home insurance carrier, is exploring the sale of its company headquarters building, along with three adjacent parcels of land located in Seattle's South Lake Union neighborhood. The properties were listed for sale on June 2, 2022.



'Over the last two-plus years, the pandemic taught us how to efficiently conduct business in a new and different way that continues to meet and exceed our customers' needs,' said PEMCO President and CEO Stan McNaughton.
