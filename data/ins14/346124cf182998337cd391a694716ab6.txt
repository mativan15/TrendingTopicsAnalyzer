Mayor Dickens announces city's first senior tech advisor
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 11:55:00

Mayor Andre Dickens announced that Donnie Beamer Jr., general manager of LotVision Automotive at Cox 2M, will be Atlanta's first "tech czar."

The announcement was made Oct. 18 at the Engage Executive Reception at the Cox Communications Inc. headquarters in Sandy Springs.

Atlanta has seen an 18% annual increase in tech jobs, according to data from IT trade association CompTIA. Major tech companies including Airbnb Inc., Google LLC and Microsoft Corp. have announced expansions in the city in recent years.
