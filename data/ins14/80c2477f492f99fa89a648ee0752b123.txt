Chandigarh Housing Board puts 31 freehold properties on sale
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-02 21:55:16

The Chandigarh Housing Board (CHB) has invited e-bids for the sale of 27 residential and four commercial units on freehold basis.The latest offering closely follows CHB's previous auction where the response remained tepid even though all properties were freehold. Yet, the board has decided not to decrease the reserve prices.Of the 27 residential properties, 17 are located in Indira Colony, Manimajra, with reserve prices ranging from ₹32 lakh to ₹34 lakh, and sizes varying from 548 square feet to 562 square feet.
