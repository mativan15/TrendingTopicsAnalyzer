Sowmya Jaganmurthy and Pruthvi Ambaar to star in a comedy thriller
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 18:30:00

An upcoming dark comedy thriller, Dollarspete, will bring together Pruthvi Ambaar and Sowmya Jaganmurthy in important roles. The movie is director Mohan's debut project in Sandalwood and is inspired by a real incident that happened in Tamil Nadu . "The entire story is set in a fictional place called Dollarspete. Around `5 lakh gets misplaced in a bank. The story narrates the search for this money. Apart from Pruthvi and Sowmya, there are five major characters in the movie, including newcomers Kushal and Dattu," he said.
