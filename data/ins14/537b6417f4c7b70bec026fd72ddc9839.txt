Published: 14:19 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 14:19 EDT, 17 October 2022 A once safe Senate race in Utah has grown increasingly competitive in the final weeks of the midterm election - giving Democrats fresh hope that a GOP takeover of Congress is not totally inevitable.Independent Evan McMullin, a former CIA officer, is running against Republican Senator Mike Lee in the closest race the state has seen in years - with a new poll showing them less than five points apart.It's gained the attention of state Democrats, who opted to back him in the race rather than a member of their own party for a better chance of unseating Lee on November 8.
