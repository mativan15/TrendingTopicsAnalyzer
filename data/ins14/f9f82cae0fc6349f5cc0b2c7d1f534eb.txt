By Peng Wensheng | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-17 09:31 Share - WeChat CAI MENG/CHINA DAILY Economic figures recorded since August have begun to show signs of recovery, especially upticks seen in infrastructure and manufacturing investment, which are key to hedging the downward pressure on real estate and driving overall investment. Industrial production is also seen moving back on track. But compared with the traditional cyclical recovery, as we see, the latest round of recovery features atypical characteristics amid the ongoing impact of COVID-19.
