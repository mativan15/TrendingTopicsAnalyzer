October 18, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Rush Street Interactive, Inc. (NYSE: RSI) ('RSI') today announced that it will release its third quarter 2022 results after the market close on Wednesday, November 2, 2022, followed by a conference call at 5:00 pm Eastern Time (4:00 pm Central Time) to discuss the results.

RSI's earnings press release and related materials will be available at ir.rushstreetinteractive.com. To listen to the audio webcast and live Q&A, please visit RSI's investor relations website at ir.
