Illinois' governor's race could again be the nation's most expensive. Illustration by Tracie Keeton / USA TODAY; and Getty Images Illinois' 2018 governor's race was the most expensive in American history. This year's may top it. Since January, three billionaires have thrown $140 million into the mix before Illinois' primary June 28. It's a mounting second round in the state's battle of the billionaires, with eye-popping figures. The hundreds of millions of dollars funneling into the race are, in part, thanks to Illinois' unique campaign finance laws that trigger a funding free-for-all once one candidate decides to self-fund.
