Liz Truss has resigned as leader of the Conservative Party and Prime Minister after just 44 days in power.
She has become the shortest-running UK Prime Minister after a tenure fraught with humiliations, including mini budget u-turns, sacking Chancellor of the Exchequer Kwasi Kwarteng after 38 days, and Home Secretary Suella Braverman quitting.
With only six weeks in office, the UK barely got to know the former Tory leader before bidding goodbye.
With that in mind, here is everything you need to know about Ms Truss.
