'Prince' Twitter review: Sivakarthikeyan's Diwali release opens with mixed reviews
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 05:22:00

Sivakarthikeyan starrer 'Prince' has hit the screens today on the occasion of Diwali and the film has opened from early morning shows in several locations. Despite the rains in several parts of Tamil Nadu, fans didn't miss catching the Sivakarthikeyan in theatres, and the theatres tune into festival mode. Social media too is buzzing with reviews from fans and Sivakarthikeyan's Diwali release has opened with mixed reviews by the fans. In the film, Sivakarthikeyan plays a teacher, and it's a hilarious laughter riot from the humorous actor.
