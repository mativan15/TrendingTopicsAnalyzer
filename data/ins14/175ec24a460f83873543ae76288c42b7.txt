Penny Mordaunt has insisted she will 'keep calm and carry on' as she was teased about her leadership ambitions following Liz Truss's resignation.The Conservative minister was in the Commons when Ms Truss appeared in Downing Street to announce her resignation as Conservative Party leader.Ms Mordaunt came third in the leadership contest over the summer and narrowly missed out on a place in the head-to-head phase between Ms Truss and Rishi Sunak.She later backed Ms Truss over the former chancellor Mr Sunak.
