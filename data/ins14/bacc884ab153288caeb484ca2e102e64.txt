CrowdStrike: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot

news
2022-06-02 21:04:58

AUSTIN, Texas (AP) _ CrowdStrike Holdings Inc. (CRWD) on Thursday reported a loss of $31.5 million in its fiscal first quarter.The Austin, Texas-based company said it had a loss of 14 cents per share. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to 31 cents per share.The results topped Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of nine analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 23 cents per share.The cloud-based security company posted revenue of $487.8 million in the period, which also beat Street forecasts.
