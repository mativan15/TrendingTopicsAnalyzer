(CBC News)Tehran may want to talk with Ukraine about claims that it's arming Russia with deadly "kamazaki" drones, but President Voldomyr Zelenskyy said Wednesday he doesn't trust the words of Iran's leaders.The Ukrainian president sat down with both CBC News and CTV News for a roundtable interview at the presidential palace in Kyiv. Zelenskyy told Canadian reporters he doesn't believe Iran's public denials.
