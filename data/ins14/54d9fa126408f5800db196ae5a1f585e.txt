Apple may launch RealityOS for its upcoming AR/VR headset
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 13:45:00

San Francisco: Tech giant Apple is reportedly planning to launch RealityOS for its upcoming augmented reality/virtual reality (AR/VR) headset. According to a report by The Verge, the RealityOS has appeared in a trademark filing, "which hasn't been officially filed by the Apple but it's common practice for large companies to apply for trademarks under one-off company".

The RealityOS powered headsets will be able to offer a combination of VR and AR experiences, immersing users in virtual content as well as layering virtual elements over real world environments.
