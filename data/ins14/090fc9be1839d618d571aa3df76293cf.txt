CANADA STOCKS-TSX opens higher with gains for energy and industrial shares
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 13:44:11

May 30 (Reuters) - Canada's main stock index opened higher on Monday, aided by gains in energy and industrial shares, with trading volumes expected to be subdued because of a U.S. market holiday.

At 9:33 a.m. ET (13:33 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange's S&P/TSX composite index was up 89.45 points, or 0.43%, at 20,838.03.
