ByHT News Desk, New Delhi





Delhi environment minister Gopal Rai on Wednesday said that manufacturing, storing, and selling firecrackers in the national capital is a punishable offence and would attract a fine of up to ₹5,000 and three years in jail.News agency PTI, quoting the minister, said that purchasing and bursting of firecrackers would also be punishable by a ₹200 fine and six-month jail term.
