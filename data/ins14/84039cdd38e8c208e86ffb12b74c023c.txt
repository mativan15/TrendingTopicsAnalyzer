Olive Real Estate Group and Hotel Equities Announce Grand Opening of Dual-Branded SpringHill Suites® and Element® Hotel in Downtown Colorado Springs

Colorado Springs, CO, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Olive Real Estate Group, Hotel Equities ('HE'), Colarelli Construction and Hotel Operations Services, the owners, development and operations partners, are excited to announce the grand opening of the new dual-branded SpringHill Suites by Marriott and Element by Westin in Downtown Colorado Springs on May 26, 2022. The dual-branded property has a combined 261-guestrooms and features panoramic views of Pikes Peak, Garden of the Gods Park, and the Rocky Mountains.
