Delhi HC dismisses real estate firm director's plea challenging one year imprisonment, imposes cost

The Delhi high court has dismissed with costs of Rs 30,000 a petition challenging the state consumer commission's decision of awarding one year imprisonment to a real estate firm's director for not refunding over Rs 20 lakh to a person whose commercial property was not handed over in time.

NEW DELHI: The Delhi high court has dismissed with costs of Rs 30,000 a petition challenging the state consumer commission's decision of awarding one year imprisonment to a real estate firm's director for not refunding over Rs 20 lakh to a person whose commercial property was not handed over in time.
