By Zhong Nan | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-19 09:07 Share - WeChat Zhong Nan It is hard to deny that consumers in many countries had a tough time getting goods, from masks to electronic products, in a timely manner after the COVID-19 pandemic broke out. To resolve the issue, China, led by the Communist Party of China, has not only deployed enormous resources and manpower to sustain the operation of the global supply chain since the outbreak, but also adopted more targeted measures to solve problems and challenges facing foreign companies to help ensure stable growth.
