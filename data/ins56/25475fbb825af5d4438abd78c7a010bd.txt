Acuity Brands to Announce Fiscal 2022 Third Quarter Results on June 30, 2022

Atlanta, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Acuity Brands, Inc. (NYSE: AYI) ('Company') today announced that the Company is planning to release its fiscal 2022 third-quarter earnings results on Thursday, June 30, at 6:00 a.m. (EDT), followed by a conference call at 8:00 a.m. (EDT).  Neil Ashe, Chairman, President, and Chief Executive Officer of Acuity Brands, will lead the call.    On that day, the webcast, earnings release, and supplemental presentation can be accessed via the Investor Relations section of the Company's website at investors.
