Mellow Mushroom Celebrates Pride Month with the Mellow Love Merchandise Collection, Proceeds Benefit the Human Rights Campaign

May 31, 2022 03:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Mellow Mushroom is proud to support Pride Month 2022 and the LGBTQA+ community with 'Mellow Love,' an effort throughout June to support the community by contributing to the Human Rights Campaign (HRC). During Pride Month, Mellow will match any items sold on The Yellow Room -- its retail website -- with a minimum donation of $10,000.



Since Mellow Mushroom opened its doors in 1974, inclusivity has been a core value of its business.
