UP: Probe into man's death after platelet transfusion
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:30:00

PRAYAGRAJ: Senior superintendent of police (Prayagraj) Shailesh Kumar Pandey has ordered a probe into the mysterious death of a 30-year-old man who, according to his family members, allegedly died after wrong platelet transfusion .The SSP told TOI that two teams have been constituted to probe the case. "We would first ascertain the actual cause of the death and then initiate legal action accordingly. We have also consulted the chief medical officer (Prayagraj) Dr. Nanak Saran regarding the incident.
