BSP chief: No recession in PH
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 10:27:00

A TOP official of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) is optimistic that the Philippine economy will sustain its recovery as the country continues to post strong macroeconomic fundamentals.



'I can say with confidence that there will be no recession in the Philippines,' BSP Gov. Benjamin Diokno said when asked about warnings of a recession in the United States and its potential spillover to the rest of the world during a recent interview on BBC Asia Business Report.



'We had been growing at around six to seven percent before the pandemic.
