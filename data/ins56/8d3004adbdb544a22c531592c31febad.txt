'Voice of the Rabbits' reflects on Bowie's 1981 state runner-up baseball team

2022-06-01 02:43:31

Scott Weber doesn't apologize for being a homer.

As the Voice of the Rabbits, Weber has spent the past 22 years providing play-by-play analysis of just about every sport Bowie High School has to offer.

But his relationship with the community goes deeper than that. Weber is a Bowie native and 1981 graduate of Bowie High.

And it was during Weber's senior year that the Bowie baseball team made history, advancing to the program's first and only state tournament.

It's a feat the 2022 Jackrabbits have a chance at duplicating.
