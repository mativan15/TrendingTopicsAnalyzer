Maureen Farrell, Emily Flitter and Joe Rennison'Credit Suisse is probably going bankrupt.'It was Saturday, October 1, and Jim Lewis, who frequently posts on Twitter under the moniker Wall Street Silver, made that assertion to his more than 300,000 followers. 'Markets are saying it's insolvent and probably bust. 2008 moment soon?'Lewis was among hundreds of people — many of them amateur investors — who had been speculating about the fate of Credit Suisse, the Swiss bank. It was in the middle of a restructuring and had become an easy target after decades of scandals, failed attempts at reform and management upheavals.
