Katie Price celebrates mum Amy's 70th birthday amid terminal lung condition

2022-10-18 09:25:38

Katie Price has shared a sweet birthday message in honour of her mother Amy to mark her 70th birthday.
The former glamour model, 44, took to Instagram to share a montage of images of her with her mother and her own children.
She wrote alongside it: ‘No words out there to describe the love I have for my mum . Happy 70th mother.'
Her daughter Princess Andre, 15, commented: ‘Happy birthday nanny', while Katie's sister Sophie added a series of heart emojis.
Sophie had also shared photos of her and her mother from her wedding, adding: ‘Happy Birthday to my beautiful AP…70 today! Love you loads.
