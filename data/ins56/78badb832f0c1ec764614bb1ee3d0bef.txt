KANYE West has insisted he'll "love Kim Kardashian for life" and only considers them divorced "on paper". 
The rapper, 45, made the revelations on Piers Morgan Uncensored in an exclusive interview where he was also grilled about his recent anti-Semitic tweets.

Piers, 57, asked Kanye - now known as Ye - if he's sad he and Kim are no longer together, after the reality star filed for divorce in February 2021 following six years of marriage.
