At Communist Party Congress, Xi Jinping says full control achieved over Hong Kong, focus now on Taiwan

Source: TOI.in

Chinese President Xi Jinping addressed the Communist Party Congress in Beijing. Xi discussed about issues like Taiwan and Hong Kong. He said that China has achieved full control over Hong Kong and in Taiwan, the country is trying to firmly promote 'reunification' efforts. In a speech that lasted over an hour, Xi outlined his vision for the Chinese military, economic growth, innovation in technology, climate change and several other matters. He said that economic development will be top priority for party and his government will aim for high quality growth.
