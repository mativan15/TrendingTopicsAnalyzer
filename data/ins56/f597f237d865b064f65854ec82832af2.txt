Warburg Pincus Leads $250 Million Investment in Vietnamese Developer
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 09:36:00

By Yifan Wang Warburg Pincus L.L.C. led a $250 million investment in No Va Land Investment Group Corp., a Vietnamese property developer listed on the Ho Chi Minh City Stock Exchange. The deal marked the New York-based private-equity firm's first investment in the company, also known as Novaland. Novaland, one of Vietnam's largest public companies, intends to use the proceeds to continue expanding its land bank and complete its ongoing flagship projects, the two companies said in a joint statement on Wednesday.
