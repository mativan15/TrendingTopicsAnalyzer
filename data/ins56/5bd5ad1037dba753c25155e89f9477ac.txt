Local documentary Web of Chaos looks at the internet's dark side
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-19 22:00:00

The internet could be described as a blessing and a curse. While its benefits are numerous, sifting fact from fiction can be challenging or even impossible. 'The internet can be used for good or bad,' says Justin Pemberton, the director of Web Of Chaos, a new local documentary about the online world. 'It can be everything you love and everything you despise. Social media companies learned pretty quickly that we engage more with content that enrages us or shocks us or annoys us or pushes our buttons more in a negative space than in a positive space.
