Long wait for Schengen visas likely to get longer
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 06:00:00

(This story originally appeared in on Jun 02, 2022)NEW DELHI: A Delhi-based doctor who had to go to Paris to attend a medical conference with his team has lost lakhs of rupees in cancellation charges for airline tickets and hotel bookings. The reason -- he and his team did not get their Schengen visa on time that requires confirm bookings to be attached at the time of filing the application. The way for a Schengen visa can extend almost a month. 'This is not an isolated case. Hundreds of people waiting for Schengen visas are facing a similar predicament as the processing time has gone up like never before.
