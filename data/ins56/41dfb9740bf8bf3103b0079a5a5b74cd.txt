Leader in air purification offers improved performance in small particle capture, combining proprietary PECO technology with HEPA and carbon filtration
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Molekule, the leader in reinventing air purification, today introduced a revolutionary new filter for its Air Pro and Air Mini+ air purifiers. The PECO-HEPA Tri-Power filter boasts not one, but three layers of protection. It combines true HEPA 99.97% particle-capture efficiency rate with Molekule's very own patented PECO technology that destroys organic pollutants like viruses, bacteria, mold, allergens, volatile organic compounds, chemicals, and more.
