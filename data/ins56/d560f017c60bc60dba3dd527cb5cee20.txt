October 18, 2022 08:42 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Americans are embracing the holiday spirit and focused on spreading cheer to others, however widespread uncertainty and personal financial strains are prompting consumers to plan for the holidays with greater precision, according to results of the 16th Annual Holiday Shopping Survey from Accenture (NYSE: ACN).



The survey of more than 1,500 U.S. consumers found that the majority (84%) are looking forward to the holidays.
