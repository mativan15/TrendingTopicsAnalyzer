Four tips to help businesses navigate today's threat landscape

Following the outbreak of Covid-19, businesses around the world rapidly implemented new technologies to adapt to remote working and accommodate hybrid workforces.In general, businesses have shown great resilience and agility. But as working from home continues, many organizations are beginning to turn their focus to cyber security.From an attack standpoint, the pandemic has not changed the way cyber criminals operate. The methods they use are much the same, with phishing, vishing, and smishing among their main tactics.
