 On the seventh day of its protest, Bharti Kisan Union (BKU) (Ekta - Ugrahan) on Saturday, organised a rally here, demanding fulfillment of their demands, which include government compensation to farmers for damaged crops and death of cows due to lumpy skin disease. Raising anti-government slogans, thousands of farmers were present in the rally, which was held outside a private colony here, which has a rented accommodation of chief minister Bhagwant Mann.
