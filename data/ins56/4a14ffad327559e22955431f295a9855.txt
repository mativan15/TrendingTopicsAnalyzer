DON'T Forget the Lyrics! is back in 2022 for a revival hosted by Niecy Nash.
The original game show ran from 2007 to 2009 and was hosted by Wayne Brady.
Don't Forget the Lyrics! host Niecy Nash pictured on the showCredit: Hulu
What are the rules of the show?
Don't Forget the Lyrics is a game show hosted on the Fox network.
Contestants have a total of nine categories of songs to sing from, Decider reports.
Each contestant must pick two songs to sing, which they sing karaoke style.
There is a screen helping the contest along before it begins to have blanks.
