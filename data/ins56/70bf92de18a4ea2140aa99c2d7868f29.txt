REUTERS/Reuters photographer/File PhotoRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comMay 31 (Reuters) - Russian company Gazprom (GAZP.MM) said on Tuesday it would cut off gas flows to Denmark's Orsted (ORSTED.CO), as well as to Shell Energy (SHEL.L) for its contract on gas supplies to Germany, after they failed to make payments in roubles.The cuts will be effective from June 1, Gazprom said.
