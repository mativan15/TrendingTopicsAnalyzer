Cannon-Brookes and his team studied the list of seats where climate had clearly been key – such as Kooyong, North Sydney, Wentworth, Warringah, Brisbane – and wondered whether they would be overrepresented in terms of AGL shareholders.LoadingSure enough, cross-checking postcodes on the share register against the seats that had turned ‘teal' or Green soon revealed they were home to an extraordinary 8.5 per cent of shareholders. If they were counted as one single group, they would represent AGL's second-largest investor.
