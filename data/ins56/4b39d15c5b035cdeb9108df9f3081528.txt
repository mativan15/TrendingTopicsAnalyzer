Whenever we talk about technology and automation then why don't we apply it to our decision-making process? This and many other such questions toss the coin 'The Decision-Intelligence'. Addition of the automation and intelligence to decision-making will greatly influence the business outcomes.The decision-making process of businesses is following the same pattern collecting data, visualizing it, finding their insights, and then the output means the decision. The traditional decision-making processes are unpredictable and the complexity is not considered in that process.
