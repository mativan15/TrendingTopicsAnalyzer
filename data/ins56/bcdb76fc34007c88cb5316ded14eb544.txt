Lee Chatfield being investigate for alleged crimes

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. - According to The Detroit News, former Michigan House speaker Lee Chatfield is under investigation over several alleged crimes. According to records obtained by Attorney General Dana Nessel's office, Chatfield's brother is accused of giving Adderall to Chatfield and two lobbyists in Lansing several times, and that the brother got money from his political accounts. There has been no response from the attorney general's office on the investigation.
