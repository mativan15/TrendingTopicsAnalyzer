Taylor Swift ‘mind blown' as Midnights breaks Spotify record
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-22 09:06:04

Taylor Swift has shared her joy after her new album Midnights broke Spotify's record for the most-streamed album in a single day. 
Midnights was released on Friday and Spotify announced less than 24 hours later that it had broken the streaming record on their platform. 
Spotify tweeted: ‘And before the clock could even strike midnight on October 22nd, Taylor Swift broke the record for most-streamed album in a single day in Spotify history. Congratulations, @taylorswift13'.
Taylor was over the moon to amass the most streams an album has had in a single day in Spotify's history, thanking fans for getting her the accolade.
