By Reuters Published: 09:57 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 09:57 EDT, 27 May 2022 By Giulia Segreti ROME, May 27 (Reuters) - Italian design group Pininfarina is looking at M&A to give it skills in new sectors, mainly the digital one, and widen its customer base, Chief Executive Silvio Pietro Angori said on Friday. "So far we have grown organically, our ambition is to now grow externally. We have the ambition of simply becoming the largest design company (in the world)," Angori told reporters at a meeting at the Foreign Press Association in Rome.
