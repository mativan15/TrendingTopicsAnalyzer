If you're someone who has a hard time submitting applications for different jobs when unemployed, then here is a hack for you that will make things super easy. This unemployed man and musician identified as Jarrah Skye, who goes by the handle Mumfighter, filmed himself quickly applying for certain jobs to get his fortnightly benefits payment of $642.70 (£511). UnsplashDespite the fact that the job-seeker service requires people to spend 15 to 29 hours each week searching for jobs, Mumfighter showed how he got done filling out forms at lightning speed.
