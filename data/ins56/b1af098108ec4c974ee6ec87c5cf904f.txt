Doug Ford to end development fees for homeowners who add rental units
thestar.com
news
2022-10-21 00:00:00

In a bid to boost the supply of affordable housing, Premier Doug Ford plans to eliminate development charges for homeowners who want to add rental apartments to existing homes.

Municipal Affairs Minister Steve Clark, who will table sweeping new legislation Tuesday, said the move is part of the government's push to build 1.5 million new homes over the next decade.
