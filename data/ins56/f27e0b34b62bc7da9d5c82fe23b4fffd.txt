Flexible workspace providers are taking up larger spaces to cater to the demand from companies and give a campus-like feeling to the tenants, multiple operators said. Pre-Covid, most of the operators used to pick up less than 50,000 square feet, but in the last one year, a majority of them have taken centres spread over double that area. Some have even leased half a million sq ft. "Corporate occupiers are looking for portfolio re-optimisation and focusing on the concept of new-age real estate trends such as managed office spaces.
