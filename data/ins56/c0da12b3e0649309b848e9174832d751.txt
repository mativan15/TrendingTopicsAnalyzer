Rev. __ (2022): In 2018, Santa Clara Law launched an innovative new certificate, called the 'Tech Edge JD' (TEJD), for JD students who know when they apply to law school that they want to pursue technology law. TEJD students acquire valuable professional skills by completing milestones, not just specific courses, with support from a faculty/staff advisor and two practitioner mentors.
