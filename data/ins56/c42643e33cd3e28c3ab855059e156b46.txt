Piece of cake from King Charles and Princess Diana's 1981 wedding now for sale

A slice of fruit cake from the wedding of Princess Diana and King Charles is now set to be auctioned - 41 years after the royal ceremony which took place in 1981. The piece was given to Nigel Ricketts, then an employee at the Royal household, who kept it safe in its original presentation box till he passed away last year, the Daily Mail reported.

The cake is reportedly still in very good form and shape largely because of all that alcohol which went into making it.
