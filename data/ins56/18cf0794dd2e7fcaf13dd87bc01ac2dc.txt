ATLANTA, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--LeaseQuery, a leading provider of software built to simplify the complexities of accounting, today announced the addition of new AI capabilities to enable lease abstraction and lease entry. Combining accounting technology with the power of artificial intelligence will allow LeaseQuery to initially automate key parts of its implementation process, offering customers a seamless onboarding experience.

LeaseQuery remains committed to efficiently guiding customers through complex accounting processes.
