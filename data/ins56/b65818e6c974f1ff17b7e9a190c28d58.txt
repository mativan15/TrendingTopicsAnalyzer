Black Hair in Hollywood: Many Bad Hair Days (Part 1)Black Hair in Hollywood: Many Bad Hair Days (Part 1)Black hair is complex.It can be politicized. It can be painful. It can be traumatizing. But Black hair is also beautiful. It's joyous, summoning memories, traditions, stories and unique journeys. And that part of the story is what "Hair Tales" (premiering Sunday on OWN, 9 EDT/PDT, and streaming weekly on Hulu) spotlights. The six-part series unravels the humanity of Black women through the metaphor of hair and provides context to those experiences, producer and host Tracee Ellis Ross tells USA TODAY.
