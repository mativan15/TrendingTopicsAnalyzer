Rout those insulting Gujarat with 'canards': Modi tears into AAP
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 23:45:00

RAJKOT: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Wednesday mounted a full-on attack on his political opponents and asked people in his home state to rout those insulting Gujarat with what he called a torrent of canards.At a rally in Junagadh, where he dedicated to the people and laid foundation stones of projects worth Rs 4,155 crore, Modi said some parties have developed a habit of trash-talking Gujarat and people should teach them a lesson."Gujarat has progressed due to the hard work and penance of its people.
