5 Ways to Make Cybersecurity Fun

It's 2022, and we all use the internet for everything. Teaching people, from heads of industry to heads of households, how to browse and interact safely online could save us all a lot of money. A 2019 report from RiskIQ suggests cybercrime costs organizations $2.9 million every minute, and major businesses pay $25 per minute as a result of data breaches. Now, more than ever, cybersecurity training of all kinds is necessary at home and in the office.The Advanced Computing Systems Association (USENIX) conducted a study to observe employees who received security training for identifying phishing attacks.
