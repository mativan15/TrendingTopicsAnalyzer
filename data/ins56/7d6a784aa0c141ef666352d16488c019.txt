Bank of Maharashtra cuts home, personal loan rates
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 01:15:00

Going against the rising interest rate trend, state-owned Bank of Maharashtra has lowered its home loan interest rates by 30 to 70 basis points from Monday. The bank also cut personal loan rates by 245 bps. The bank said it is making retail loans cheaper to bring in cheer among customers during the festive season.

Home loans from the bank will now be available at a minimum 8% per annum rate while the personal loan rate becomes cheaper at 8.9% from earlier 11.35%. Earlier, the lowest home loan rate was available at 8.
