Reliance Brands to acquire 40% stake in Italy's Legno SPA's India toy manufacturing business
thehindu.com
news
2022-06-01 14:47:53

Reliance Brands Ltd. (RBL) on Wednesday announced that it has entered into a JV with Italian toy manufacturing company Plastic Legno SPA by acquiring a 40% stake in its India business for an undisclosed amount.This investment would help RBL in bringing in vertical integration for its toy business and diversify the supply chain with a long-term strategic interest in building toy manufacturing in India, a joint statement said.Plastic Legno SPA is owned by the Sunino group that boasts more than 25 years of toy production experience in Europe.
