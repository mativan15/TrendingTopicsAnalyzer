Published: 08:26 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 08:26 EDT, 21 October 2022 Carol Vorderman has slammed the shambolic government as she said she wants a 'decent' Pride of Britain winner to be the next prime minister.The presenter, 61, appeared on This Morning on Friday and launched an attack on the government and former prime minister Liz Truss as she called for 'kind' and 'brave' people in the House of Parliament.She went on to discuss the Daily Mirror Pride of Britain Awards, which celebrates ordinary people doing extraordinary things.
