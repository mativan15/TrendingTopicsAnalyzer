AgenciesWordle is one of the most popular games that is widely played globally. The game's popularity shot up at the end of last year and is only growing. The rules of this game that is now owned by New York Times are very simple. Players need to guess the five-letter word until they get to the correct answer. They get six chances to guess the correct word. This game is a good and fun way to test one's English vocabulary. One can get major hints from the tiles which change their color in accordance to the correctness of the guesses entered.
