Robert Saleh on win: 'We've got to start expecting stuff like this'
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-16 19:48:27

The Jets have won three straight games after going into Lambeau Field in Green Bay and leaving with a 27-10 win over the Packers. The Jets are certainly excited. But if you ask head coach Robert Saleh, it was just another day at the office that they now expect to be the norm.
'I'll be honest, it doesn't mean anything,' Saleh said when asked what this victory means for the Jets organization. 'Just like a loss wouldn't have meant anything. I've said it before, we've got to start expecting stuff like this.
