Cristiano Ronaldo has been dropped to the bench for Manchester United's clash with Tottenham on Wednesday night.

Marcus Rashford takes the 37-year-old's place up top, in the only change from the goalless draw with Newcastle last Sunday.
It's a big game for United against another top team in Antonio Conte's Tottenham who have serious ambitions about winning the Premier League title.
Having scored 14 goals against Spurs in 20 games, including a hattrick in this fixture last season, it is a surprise to see Ronaldo benched but Erik ten Hag insists it is the right call.
