Humane Long Island says it reached out to the Bethpage Equestrian Center to get an event by Sloth Encounters canceled. People would have been able to take free selfies with baby sloths on Thursday before the cancelation. (Shutterstock)OLD BETHPAGE, NY — An event by Hauppauge's Sloth Encounters where people would have taken free selfies with baby sloths — for a donation of any amount of money — in Old Bethpage was canceled after Humane Long Island President John Di Leonardo says he reached out to the event venue.
