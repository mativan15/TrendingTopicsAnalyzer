Sorare is launching the beta for its NFT-based NBA fantasy basketball game played with digital collectible cards. The Sorare: NBA Beta game is a big addition to Sorare's lineup, which includes a Sorare: Major League Baseball game and soccer game, Sorare: Football, as well. Players can collect free cards or buy NFTs, or non-fungible tokens.
