Crypto Miner Core Scientific Flags Threat From Celsius Chapter 11 Dispute
wsj.com
news
2022-10-20 17:46:00

Bitcoin miner Core Scientific Inc. accused bankrupt crypto lender Celsius Network LLC of refusing to pay its bills since filing for chapter 11, threatening the data hosting company's own financial health. The brewing dispute could have a material impact on Core Scientific, one of the largest publicly traded crypto miners, as the massive data centers that host bitcoin mining operations struggle to weather the crypto-market downturn that dragged Celsius and other crypto firms into bankruptcy.
