Brent Venables is still learning how to be a head coach on the fly. Nobody doubts his understanding of football or defensive football, but f there's one thing we can say he needs to improve, it's managing the clock.
The Sooners wasted a valuable chance to score before the half because of questionable clock management. Oklahoma had more than a minute and multiple timeouts available and slow played on first and second down. A third and eight run by Eric Gray forced the Sooners to begin pushing to score, but by that point, they'd already wasted about 40 seconds of game clock.
