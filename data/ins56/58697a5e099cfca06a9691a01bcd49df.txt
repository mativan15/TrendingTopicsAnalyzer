June 02, 2022 02:33 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Shareholder rights law firm Robbins LLP is investigating Oscar Health, Inc. (NYSE: OSCR) and its officers and directors to determine whether the breached fiduciary duties and violated securities laws in connection with the Company's March 2021 initial public offering ("IPO"). Oscar Health is a health insurance company that claims to be the first company "built around a full stack technology platform."
