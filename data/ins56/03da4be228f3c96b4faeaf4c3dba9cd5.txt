October 18, 2022 08:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time FRANKLIN, Tenn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Resource Label Group, LLC ('Resource Label'), a full-service provider of pressure sensitive labels, shrink sleeve and RFID/NFC technology, announced it has acquired MedLit Solutions, a leader in pharmaceutical packaging and printing solutions. With two locations, in Garner, NC and Winston NJ, MedLit offers end-to-end solutions for the pharmaceutical, biotech, and healthcare industries.



For more than 25 years, MedLit Solutions has been an industry leader in pharmaceutical and biotech packaging.
