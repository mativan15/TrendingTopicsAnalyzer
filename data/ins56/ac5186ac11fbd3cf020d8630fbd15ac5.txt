The best high-waisted jeans come in multiple fits and styles to flatter your body. DL1961 It's easy to associate denim trends with different eras (see: 70's bell-bottoms, 80's acid wash, the early aughts low-rise). Right now, we're in the throws of a high-waisted craze, with styles that hit well above the hip appealing to a wide swath of the denim-wearing population. It's not really a trend, though, since the look has been around for decades. Some of the first Levi's made for women in the 1940s had sky-high waists, which made the jeans practical and easy to tuck a shirt into when working in a factory.
