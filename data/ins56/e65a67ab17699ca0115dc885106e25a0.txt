HOUSTON, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ascend Performance Materials has launched ReDefyne™, a portfolio of sustainable polyamides created with up to 100% pre- and post-consumer recycled polyamide 66 or 6 to provide a low carbon footprint and reliable performance, even in demanding applications.

Ascend Performance Materials has launched a new portfolio of sustainable pre- and post-consumer recycled polyamides under the name ReDefyne.

 



"We are focused on helping our customers meet their long-term sustainability goals with innovative, reliable products," said Steve Manning, Ascend's senior director for engineered materials.
