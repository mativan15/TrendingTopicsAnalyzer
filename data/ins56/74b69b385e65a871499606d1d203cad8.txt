EPA to award up to $1.13 million in sustainability grants
yahoo.com
entertainment
2022-06-02 17:49:46

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency plans to award source reduction grants between $40,000 to $250,000 to sustainability projects across the U.S.

The United States Environmental Protection Agency announced in a press release it is accepting applications for a $1.13 million source reduction grant opportunity on May 27.

The EPA grant opportunity is an effort to proactively reduce greenhouse gas emissions, generation of hazardous materials, conserve natural resources and benefit underserved communities through sustainability projects designed by applicants.
