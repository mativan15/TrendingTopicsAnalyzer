The speed of change and innovation in the blockchain space can be disorienting. New cryptocurrencies, new kinds of assets, even whole new organizational ideas like DAOs are constantly popping up around the world — and constantly flaming out.It's a culture not just comfortable with change and disruption, but obsessed with it.That's one part of what we cover in this newsletter. The other part is the rulemakers in Washington and Brussels, the people who have to worry about this stuff – and who constantly frustrate blockchain businesses with the much slower pace of government.
