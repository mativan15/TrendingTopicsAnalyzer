As we come out of the pandemic, companies want to grow. But how can you grow without great people? If you can help companies attract and retain great employees, then you have an offering that can attract high-paying clients. Inflation, labor and supply upheavals, and a work culture transformation are top of mind for C-suite execs and business owners in 2022, according to C-Suite Outlook, The Conference Board's 23rd annual survey of business leaders around the world.
