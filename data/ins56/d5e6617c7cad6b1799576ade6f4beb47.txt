A film about family, love, authenticity, and loss. DEERFIELD BEACH, Fla., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Maverick Entertainment is proud to present, "Boxed In," a film directed by Ariel Julia Harston and starring Reginae Carter (Lil Wayne's daughter). The premiere will be held outside of Atlanta on November 3rd, attended by Reginae Carter and the rest of the cast. The movie will be available for streaming exclusively on Peacock as of November 1st, and all other streaming platforms on December 1st.
