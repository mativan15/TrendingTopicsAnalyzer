Khosrork Yesterday, I took a quick look back at the monetary aggregates, something that I had neglected during all the turmoil taking place in money and finance over the past three years or so. I must admit, not much attention is paid to the monetary aggregates these days. The times have changed. Economists have adopted new foci. I, too, am guilty of this neglect. Let's take a look at the performance of the M2 money stock over the past few years. M2 Money Stock (Federal Reserve) We can see the results of how the Federal Reserve reacted to the spread of the Covid-19 pandemic with the big jump in the M2 money stock in early 2020.
