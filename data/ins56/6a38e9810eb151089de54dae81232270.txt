Stitch Fix (SFIX 6.84%) investors were in a good mood early Tuesday. The beaten-down e-commerce stock jumped 10% by 10:45 am ET, compared to a 2% spike in the S&P 500. That boost barely made a dent in recent losses for the apparel retailer, though. Shares remain lower by 80% so far in 2022.
The rally came as investors become less pessimistic about the economy as we kick off third-quarter earnings season.
So what
The big banks ushered in the earnings season with generally good news that suggests consumer spending is holding up well as we head into Q4.
