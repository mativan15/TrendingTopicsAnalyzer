WISeKey Announces that in Cooperation with the Chinese Museum of Finance will Mint the World's First Jiaozi NFT Exclusively on WISe.ART ZUG, Switzerland - October 21, 2022: WISeKey International Holding Ltd ('WISeKey ') (SIX: WIHN, NASDAQ: WKEY), a leading global cybersecurity, Blockchain and IoT platform company, today announced that in cooperation with the Chinese Museum of Finance, WISe.ART will mint the world's first Jiaozi NFT. A limited edition of Jiaozi NFTs are available now exclusively on the WISe.
