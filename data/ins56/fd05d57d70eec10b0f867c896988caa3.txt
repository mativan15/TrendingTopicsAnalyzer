Verizon and Razer unveil Razer Edge 5G — the ultimate 5G handheld gaming device
verizon.com
news
2022-10-15 17:53:44

IRVINE, Calif. and NEW YORK, N.Y. - Verizon, the Network America relies on, together with Razer™, the leading global lifestyle brand for gamers, today unveiled the Razer Edge 5G, the ultimate 5G handheld gaming device during the keynote address of RazerCon 2022. This groundbreaking collaboration will bring to market the world's first dedicated 5G handheld console — equipped with the world's most advanced display of any gaming handheld, powered by the latest Snapdragon® G3x Gen 1 Gaming Platform, and running on Verizon 5G Ultra Wideband, the 5G network for gaming.
