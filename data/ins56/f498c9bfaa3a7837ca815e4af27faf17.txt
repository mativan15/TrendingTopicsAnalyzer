AM Best Removes From Under Review With Negative Implications, Assigns Negative Outlook to Best Meridian Insurance Company and Best Meridian International Insurance Company SPC

October 21, 2022 10:06 AM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has removed from under review with negative implications and affirmed the Financial Strength Rating of A- (Excellent) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Ratings of 'a-' (Excellent) of Best Meridian Insurance Company (BMIC) (Miami, FL) and Best Meridian International Insurance Company SPC (BMIIC) (Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands). The outlook assigned to these Credit Ratings (ratings) is negative. These companies are collectively referred to as Best Meridian.
