SANTA BARBARA, Calif., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- H2 Clipper, Inc., an aerospace and alternative energy company developing uniquely capable hydrogen-powered airships and end-to-end hydrogen infrastructure solutions, announces that Martin Whittaker has joined the company's Board of Directors. A veteran of the sustainable finance and impact investing industry, Whittaker is the founding CEO of JUST Capital, a leading nonprofit in the stakeholder capitalism arena. Whittaker brings almost 30 years of experience at the intersection of global business, finance, philanthropy, and social and environmental impact.
