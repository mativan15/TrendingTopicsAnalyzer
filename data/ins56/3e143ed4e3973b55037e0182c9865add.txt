Disgraced former politician Salim Mehajer has been blocked from launching legal action in NSW after his attempt to sue former business partners for $52m backfired.The controversial former developer was on Thursday declared a 'vexatious litigant' by the NSW Supreme Court.Under the orders, the ex-Auburn deputy mayor is prohibited from bringing any new proceedings in NSW unless granted leave by the court.Supreme Court Justice Geoff Lindsay granted the orders following an application by SC Lowy Primary Investments and 16 other parties.
