October 20, 2022 08:12 AM Eastern Daylight Time STAMFORD, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Legal & General Retirement America (LGRA) has hired Londone Moulds as vice president, head of service and operations effective October 3.



Moulds brings deep expertise in the pension risk transfer (PRT) operations space, including implementation, data management, customer relations, benefit calculations, operations, and customer service management. She will be responsible for leading all operational aspects of LGRA's PRT business and the continued delivery of excellent client service to effectively grow and scale the organization.
