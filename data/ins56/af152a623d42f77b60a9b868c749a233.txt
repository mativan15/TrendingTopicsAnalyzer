In Latin America, China steps in where US has stepped out
csmonitor.com
news
2022-06-03 13:56:11

To a large extent, China's rise to economic dominance and deepening political influence in Latin America has been a quiet affair.Even the United States – first preoccupied with wars in the Middle East and then turning inward to the tune of 'America First' – seemed to hardly notice over the past two decades as its chief economic competitor and geopolitical rival dethroned Uncle Sam as top trading partner and go-to investor in much of his own backyard. At least until recently.But for Miriam Arce Pinta, China's arrival in her picturesque fishing village on Peru's Pacific coast has been anything but tranquil.
