Robbie Coltrane's daughter shares picture of them together as Harry Potter co-stars pay tribute

Robbie Coltrane's daughter has shared a touching picture of the pair together, following his death aged 72.Alice McMillan posted the photo of her hugging her father on Instagram on Saturday, accompanied by a black heart emoji.
Coltrane is survived by Alice and her brother Spencer, their mother Rhonda Gemmell and his sister Annie Rae.The actor and comedian's agent of 40 years announced his death on Friday.She did not give details of how he died, but Coltrane's family thanked staff at Forth Valley Royal Hospital in Larbert, Scotland, for their care.
