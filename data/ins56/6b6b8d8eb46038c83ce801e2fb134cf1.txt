NBA Finals tickets go for eye-popping prices at San Francisco Chase Center
cbsnews.com
news
2022-06-02 14:20:00

By Jocelyn Moran June 2, 2022 / 7:20 AM / CBS San Francisco SAN FRANCISCO -- The cost of a ticket to the NBA Finals at the Chase Center in San Francisco is leading hardcore Warriors fans to look for other ways to join in the excitement. According to Logitix, a ticketing tech business that analyzes trends, the average ticket is $1,759. Company president Greg Nortman says that's 67% higher than the average price for a ticket when the Dubs were last in the NBA Finals in 2019. "We're seeing a huge demand for the NBA Finals this year," he said.
