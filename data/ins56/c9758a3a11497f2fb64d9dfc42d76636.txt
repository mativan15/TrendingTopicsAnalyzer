By Australian Associated Press Published: 16:35 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 16:35 EDT, 15 October 2022 A nearly $10 billion infrastructure cash splash will see the construction of road and rail projects across the country. The federal government is pledging $9.6 billion in the October budget as it lays out spending for the 2022-23 year. "It's about making journeys quicker, but also making sure people can get home to their families safely," Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said on Sunday. "Sound and planned infrastructure investment in Australia creates jobs, builds opportunity and unlocks economic growth and productivity for our cities and our regions.
