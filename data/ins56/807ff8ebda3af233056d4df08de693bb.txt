Nissan India today took the wraps off three new SUVs of which two are mild hybrids that include the Nissan Qashqai and Nissan Juke. Plus, the Nissan X-Trail was revealed today as well. For now, there is no word on when or if the Hybrid SUVs will go on sale but the company has stated that it is beginning the testing for the X-Trail in the country and it will soon go on sale here. The Qashqai will also be evaluated for the India market. Until then, here are the specs and details about the three. Nissan QashqaiIn its third generation, the Qashqai gets Nissan's new V-Motion grille and boomerang LED matrix headlamps.
