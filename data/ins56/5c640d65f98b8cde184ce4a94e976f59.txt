Carole Middleton's party decoration business is officially coming stateside.With the help of a cardboard cutout of herself wearing a Canadian tuxedo, the mom of Princess Kate announced this week that her company Party Pieces, which sells various party decorations and paraphernalia, will now be available in the United States at 39 ShopRite grocery store locations. She also visited several of those stores in New Jersey this week to check out the displays of her products, sharing the images on the brand's Instagram account.
