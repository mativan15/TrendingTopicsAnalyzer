A mum fumed as her daughter was deprived of presents on her third birthday when her family failed to send a present - let alone a card - to mark the special occasionThe mum fumed that the family didn't send a birthday gift (stock photo) Children are filled with nothing but utter excitement on their birthdays as they're gifted with an ever-growing supply of presents and often get to enjoy a party and cake. A mum was understandably furious, therefore, when her three-year-old daughter was deprived of extra gifts when members of the family failed to give the little one a present or even a card for her special day.
