Published: 12:33 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 12:33 EDT, 31 May 2022 By Mimosa Spencer PARIS, May 31 (Reuters) - The 30 largest listed fashion firms must do more to hit Paris climate accord targets and U.N. sustainable development goals, although some are improving their social and environmental credentials, The Business of Fashion said in a report on Tuesday. Fashion brands face increasing pressure from consumers, particularly younger ones, and governments to show they are doing better on environmental issues.
