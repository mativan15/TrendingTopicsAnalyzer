Who Lost Saudi Arabia?
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-21 11:11:01

In February 1980, National Security Advisor Zbigniew Brzezinski traveled to Riyadh to request Saudi support in opposing the Soviet invasion of Afghanistan. King Khalid quickly agreed and for the next decade Saudi Arabia matched American support of the Afghan mujahideen dollar of for dollar. In 2022, numerous senior American and European officials asked the Saudis to help blunt the oil price rise following the Russian invasion of Ukraine. This time the Saudis said no. What changed? As with most historical processes, the altered relationship between Saudi Arabia, the West has numerous causes.
