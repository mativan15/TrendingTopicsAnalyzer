By CBSNewYork Team May 27, 2022 / 5:34 PM / CBS New York SEASIDE HEIGHTS, N.J. - New York City and New Jersey beaches open this weekend for the unofficial start of summer. CBS2's Leah Mishkin went to Seaside Heights to see how businesses along the shore are gearing up for the holiday weekend. Last year shore towns were struggling to find seasonal employees and that impacted some of their bottom lines because they had to shorten their days.The owner of Kohr's Original Custard Stores Gregory Kohr says hiring for this summer has been better, but it's still a challenge.
