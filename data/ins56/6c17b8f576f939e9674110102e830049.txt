 Provincial police in Perth County say that a man was pulling people over while pretending to be a cop in North Perth over the weekend.
The OPP says that on Sunday, a man reported being pulled over on Perth Line 88 by the perpetrator.Police say that the man who approached the car was white, heavy-set, in his 50s and was said to be wearing a navy blue outfit with white New Balance sneakers.
