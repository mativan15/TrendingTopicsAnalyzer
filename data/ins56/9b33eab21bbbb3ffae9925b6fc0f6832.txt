SANTA CLARA, Calif., May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Artec 3D, a world-renowned developer and manufacturer of  professional 3D hardware and software, today announces the opening of a  new European office located on the Adriatic coast in Montenegro, Bar, which will serve as a hub for marketing and research & development.Artec 3D continues to grow, and in 2021 alone, its Luxembourg headquarters increased by more than 30% in terms of employee headcount. The new Montenegro office is an addition to the company's current locations in the United States, Luxembourg, and China, as well as its network of global authorized resellers.
