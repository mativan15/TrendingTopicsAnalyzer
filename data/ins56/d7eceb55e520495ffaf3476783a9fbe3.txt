ANIDrugs weighing 6.836 Kgs, valued at Rs 34.18 crore, were seized from a vehicle at Champhai.In a major haul the Mizoram police have rescued 140 exotic animals and seized drugs worth Rs 34.18 crores and arrested four people in connection. The exotic animals and seized drugs were smuggled from neighbouring Myanmar. Officials said that the police in a joint operation with the Excise and Narcotics Department personnel intercepted three vehicles and rescued the 140 wild animals and birds in eastern Mizoram's Champhai district.
