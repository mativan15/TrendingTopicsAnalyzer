Good morning and welcome to the Tuesday edition of the New York & New Jersey Energy newsletter. We'll take a look at the week ahead and look back on what you may have missed last week.FINAL COUNTDOWN: With only three scheduled session days before lawmakers are expected to head home, the chance of major action on climate policy three years after the state passed mandates to slash emissions is fading. Environmental advocates are continuing to press for a ban on fossil fuels in new construction, which would be a significant step to implement the state's draft climate plan, but it faces an uphill battle.
