June 02, 2022 08:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Dr. Kami Hoss, DDS, MS – founder of The Super Dentists and Howard Healthcare Academy, and renowned orthodontist and dentofacial orthopedist – released his highly anticipated book, 'If Your Mouth Could Talk,' which provides a roadmap for reducing the oral health care gap keeping so many from personal and financial success.



The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention reports that nearly 18 percent of all working-age adults, and 29 percent of those with lower incomes, say that the appearance of their mouth and teeth affects their ability to interview for a job.
