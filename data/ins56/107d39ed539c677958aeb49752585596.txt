Need for Speed Unbound: EA Shares Details About New In-Game City Lakeshore; Here Is Everything You Need to Know
ign.com
gaming
2022-10-15 13:11:21

Need for Speed fans are excited for Unbound as the latest entry in the series was revealed on October 6, 2022. The game is developed by Criterion Games and published by Electronic Arts for PC, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S. The game is set in a fictional city called Lakeshore City which is inspired by Chicago. EA revealed more details about Lakeshore City in a new Under the Hood community blog. The developers describe Lakeshore as a canvas, and the players as the artists. Weekly Qualifiers, Takeovers, and Meetups will take place in the world of NFS Unbound, as your goal is to start from the bottom and race to the top.
