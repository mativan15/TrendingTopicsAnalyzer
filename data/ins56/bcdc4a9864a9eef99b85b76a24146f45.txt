ABoR: Housing development fees are contributing to Austin's affordability crisis
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-17 20:34:00

It's no secret that despite economic challenges and shrinking housing inventory the Central Texas housing market experienced significant growth in the last several years, while simultaneously changing how REALTORS® serve potential homebuyers. From the second half of 2020 through the first half of 2022, Central Texas fostered record-breaking sales in our market as the demand to live in Austin soared.

This rapid growth in demand, combined with impacts from the pandemic — including consistent supply chain issues and soaring construction costs — and years of limited housing inventory exacerbated by the city's arduous permitting process, placed enormous pressure on home prices.
