The Delhi High Court on May 31 sought the Central Bureau of Investigation's (CBI) stand on a bail plea filed by National Stock Exchange (NSE) former Group Operating Officer Anand Subramanian in the co-location scam case.Representing the accused, senior advocate Vikas Pahwa argued that an incomplete chargesheet has been filed in the case and sought a mandatory bail under the Criminal Procedure Code as well as on merits.Justice Sudhir Kumar Jain then issued a notice seeking the CBI's response.
