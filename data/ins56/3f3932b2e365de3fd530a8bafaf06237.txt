Imposition of Hindi is divisive in character; detrimental to integrity of India: Stalin writes to Modi

2022-10-16 08:56:12

Reiterating Tamil Nadu government's position against attempts to impose Hindi on the State, Chief Minister M.K. Stalin on Sunday wrote to Prime Minister Narendra Modi contending that the continued efforts to promote Hindi in the name of 'one nation' would destroy the feeling of brotherhood of people of different languages and cultures and is detrimental to the integrity of India.In his letter, Mr. Stalin argued: "The recent attempts to impose Hindi are impractical and divisive in character; puts non-Hindi speaking people in a very disadvantageous position in many respects.
