October 19, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Ouster, Inc. (NYSE: OUST) ('Ouster' or 'the Company'), a leading provider of high-resolution digital lidar sensors for the automotive, industrial, robotics, and smart infrastructure industries, announced the launch of its newest OS series scanning sensors, REV7, powered by its next-generation L3 chip. REV7 features the all-new OSDome sensor, as well as upgraded OS0, OS1, and OS2 sensors that deliver double the range, enhanced object detection, increased precision and accuracy, and greater reliability.
