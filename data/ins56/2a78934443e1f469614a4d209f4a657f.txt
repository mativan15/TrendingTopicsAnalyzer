REUTERS/Brendan McDermidRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comMay 27 (Reuters) - Tech billionaire Mike Cannon-Brookes is seeking two seats on AGL Energy's (AGL.AX) board, if the Australian power producer's plan to split into two companies fail.In the letter addressed to AGL Chair Peter Botten on Friday, Cannon-Brookes reiterated that the demerger was a 'deeply flawed' plan and expressed his intention to appoint two nominees for Grok Ventures - a vehicle he owns - on the board of AGL.
