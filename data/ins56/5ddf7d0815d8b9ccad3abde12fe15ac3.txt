Centre has lost control over fuel and food prices: Kolkata Mayor Firhad Hakim
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 19:30:00

Kolkata: Trinamool Congress leader and West Bengal minister Firhad Hakim alleged that the central government had no control over the prices of fuel and essential commodities, and that its pro-corporate policies were turning the Modi administration into a private company. Such policies aren't viable in a country like India with a huge population and the people are facing the brunt of the deeds of the government, Hakim, who is also the mayor of Kolkata, told ET on Monday.
