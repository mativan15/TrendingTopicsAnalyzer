Tua Tagovailoa clears concussion protocol, set to start next week vs. Steelers
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-15 17:08:15

The Miami Dolphins know when they are getting both of their quarterbacks back, most importantly when Tua Tagovailoa will return to the starting lineup. CBS Sports lead NFL insider Jonathan Jones confirms Tagovailoa has cleared concussion protocol and will start for Miami in its Week 7 matchup against the Pittsburgh Steelers.

Teddy Bridgewater also cleared concussion protocol and will serve as the backup quarterback to Skylar Thompson for Sunday's matchup against the Minnesota Vikings, the NFL Network reported.
