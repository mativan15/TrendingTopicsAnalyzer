How often do you get a job that you didn't even apply for in the first place? Well, to burst your bubble, it doesn't happen. Many scammers fool people into thinking they got a job for them in order to later extort money. The same happened with IFS officer Parveen Kaswan when he received a text about being selected for a job that will pay him Rs. 9,700.
