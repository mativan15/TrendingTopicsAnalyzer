BySingh Rahul Sunilkumar | Edited by Aryan Prakash





Apple upgraded line up of its flagship products including iPhone, Airpods and Apple Watch at the September 7 Far Out event, but stayed away from revealing any models for iPad. Now, the Cupertino-based tech giant is expected to announce two new iPad Pro tablets powered by their latest M2 chip under the hood "in a matter of days," Bloomberg's Mark Gurman tipped.
