Twitter Says Antitrust Waiting Period For Musk Deal Has Closed
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 12:35:00

By Dean Seal Twitter Inc. said Friday the window has closed for federal antitrust regulators to block Elon Musk's proposed $44 billion acquisition of the social media company. The company said the 30-day waiting period established under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976, which gives the Federal Trade Commission and Justice Department time to review a transaction for potential antitrust violations, expired for the deal Thursday night.
