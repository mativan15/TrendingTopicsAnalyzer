Iran prison fire death toll rises
dw.com
news
2022-10-17 10:30:00

The fire at Tehran's Evin prison over the weekend killed eight people, authorities said. Details over the blaze were scarce as officials sought to distance the incident from nationwide protests.

Tehran said the blaze was extinguished after several hours and no detainees escaped
The death toll in the  fire at Tehran's Evin prison over the weekend rose to eight, Iran's judiciary said on Monday.
The deadly blaze came amid nationwide anti-government protests, sparked by the death of 22-year-old Jina Mahsa Amini in police custody.
