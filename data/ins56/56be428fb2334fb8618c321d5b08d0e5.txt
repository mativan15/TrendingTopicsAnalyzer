Child benefit chaos as parents see reduced payments in bank account
thescottishsun.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 08:49:02

CONFUSED parents have complained of receiving reduced child benefit payments on Friday morning.
The benefit is usually paid every four weeks and is worth £21.80 for the eldest child and £14.45 for subsequent children each week.
Parents have been left puzzled over child benefit paymentsCredit: Getty
Parents have taken to social media to share confusion over unexpected payments from HMRC for child benefit - and being underpaid.
They have also been unable to get through to the child benefit help line due to phone issues.
