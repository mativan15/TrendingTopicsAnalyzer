Indian firms' fundraise via public bond sales to double in H2: Analysts
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 08:45:00

Indian corporates are likely to raise double the amount of funds through the public issue of bonds in the second half of this fiscal than they did in the first, as tighter systemic liquidity and lower fixed deposit rates make these instruments attractive, analysts said.
