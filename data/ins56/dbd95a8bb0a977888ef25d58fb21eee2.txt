People throng markets to shop decorative lights ahead of Diwali

Source: ANI

Markets were decked up ahead of Diwali festival in Bhubaneswar. People rushed to the shops to search for handmade decorative lights, due to which demand of such lights has increased. As the festival of lights Diwali is round the corner, Indian handmade rainbow, King Jhalar and metallic lights are in demand. After two years of pandemic, people stepped out from their homes to shop on this Diwali. Speaking to ANI, a light shop owner, Sujit on October 20 said, 'This time the response is good.
