Fire extinguished at Tehran's notorious Evin prison
cbc.ca
world
2022-10-16 08:58:00

Iranian state television aired a video apparently showing that calm had returned to Tehran's Evin prison on Sunday after a fire broke out there overnight, as unrest sparked by Mahsa Amini's death in police custody last month continued.State media said the fire at the prison, notorious for housing political prisoners and anti-government activists, was extinguished after several hours and no detainees escaped.Iranian authorities said a sewing workshop had been set on fire "after a fight among a number of prisoners convicted of financial crimes and theft.
