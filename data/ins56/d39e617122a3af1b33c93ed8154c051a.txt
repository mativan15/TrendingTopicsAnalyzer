National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme 2022: Registration Deadline Extended, Know Last Date To Apply

NMMSS Scholarship 2022: The National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS) 2022 registration deadline has been extended, the candidates can now apply on the official website of National Scholarship Portal (NSP)- scholarships.gov.in till October 31. The NMMSS application process was earlier scheduled to be closed on September 30, but extended till October 15. ALSO READ The NMMSS scholarships are awarded to meritorious students of economically weaker sections to arrest their drop out at Class 8 and encourage them to continue their education at secondary stage.
