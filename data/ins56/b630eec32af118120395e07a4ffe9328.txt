Postcards from the pyramid: Plymouth Argyle consign play-off woe to past... and the goals that were too high
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

The very recent history of Plymouth Argyle includes two pivotal games against Milton Keynes Dons.The first, a 1-1 draw last December, was Steven Schumacher's first match as the manager of Plymouth. The second came on the final day of last season and Argyle, needing a win to make the play-offs, were hammered 5-0 at home.Chapter three, at Stadium MK on Saturday, was equally significant. Plymouth's rollicking performance delivered a 4-1 win that puts them four points clear at the top of Sky Bet League One.
