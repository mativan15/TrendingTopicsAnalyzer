Anarock's FY22 revenue up 32% to Rs 402 crore on sharp recovery in housing demand: Puri
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-05 09:00:00

Agencies(Representative image)Home-grown real estate consultant Anarock has achieved a 32 per cent growth in its revenue at Rs 402 crore during the last fiscal year owing to a sharp recovery in housing demand post second wave of the Covid pandemic, its chairman Anuj Puri said. Anarock, one of the leading housing brokerage firm in India, was established by Anuj Puri in April 2017. Before setting up his own firm, Puri had served as chairman and country head at a global property consultancy firm for 10 years.
