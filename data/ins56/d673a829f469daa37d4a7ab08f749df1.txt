Rep. Castor Celebrated Hispanic Heritage Month with Message of Solutions for Lower Costs, Good-Paying Jobs and Safer Communities
house.gov
news
2022-10-19 17:43:56

U.S. Rep. Kathy Castor (FL14) celebrated Hispanic Heritage Month across the Tampa area to tout solutions for the economic squeeze, particularly the Inflation Reduction Act and H.Res. 1353 relating to Hispanic Heritage Month and celebrating the heritage and culture of Latinos in the United States and the immense contributions of Latinos to the United States. Castor knows District 14 is vibrant due to its diverse culture and strong Hispanic community. Tampa Bay neighbors shared their interest in solutions for more affordable housing, rent, health care and jobs including the American Dream and Promise Act.
