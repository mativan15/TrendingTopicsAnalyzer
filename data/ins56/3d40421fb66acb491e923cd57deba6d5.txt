Saudi Arabian Energy Minister likely to visit India this week
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 19:00:00

ReutersSaudi Arabia's Minister of Energy Prince Abdulaziz bin Salman Al-SaudSaudi Arabia's energy minister Prince Abdulaziz bin Salman is likely to visit India this week amid the Arab kingdom's public spat with the US over the OPEC+ decision to slash production, which has also upset India and other consumers, according to people familiar with the matter. Saudi energy minister's visit to New Delhi is yet to be made public and its details are being finalised. He is likely to meet petroleum minister Hardeep Puri, power minister R K Singh and commerce minister Piyush Goyal during his visit that's likely to start Friday and discuss bilateral issues between the two countries, people familiar with the matter said.
