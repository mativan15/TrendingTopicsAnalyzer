From Guilty Minds and Illegal, to Criminal Justice – courtroom happenings make for gripping drama on OTT. While critically-acclaimed international courtroom dramas such as The Good Wife, How To Get Away With Murder and Suits are quite popular among Indian audiences, desi courtroom shows have also become an OTT favouite. Here's a look at the expanding lineup of courtroom dramas. 
‘COURTROOM DRAMAS ON OTT HAVE MOVED ON FROM MONOLOGUES AND UNREALISTIC DEPICTIONS' Unlike most Indian courtroom dramas, recent web shows have been lauded for their realistic depictions.
