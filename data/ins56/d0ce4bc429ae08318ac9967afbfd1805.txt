Interviewing Eric 'Airrack' Decker Charles Benoit 
Eric Decker, also known as 'Airrack,' has gained nearly 7 million subscribers on his YouTube channel faster than most creators in the platform's 17 year history. His videos range from cycling 38 miles on ice to helping his subscribers start a 5-star restaurant. Some say he's the next Mr.Beast. Others say he's a modern Nathan For You. But after interviewing him and his team, his journey as a creator is surprisingly reminiscent of Elon Musk's journey as an entrepreneur.
