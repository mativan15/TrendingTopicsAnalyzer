Central government committed to compensating losses of farmers: NS Tomar

news
2022-10-15 15:29:21

Oct 15, 2022, 08:59PM IST

Source: ANI

Union Agriculture Minister Narendra Singh Tomar attended two days long farmers' program in Pusa on October 15. Speaking to media persons, Union Agriculture Minister Narendra Singh Tomar said that the Central government is committed to compensating the losses of farmers due to unseasonal rains. 'Unseasonal rains have affected the crops. State governments are aware of this. The survey is being done where the maximum damage has occurred. The central government is committed to compensating the loss of farmers,' said Union Agriculture Minister Narendra Singh Tomar.
