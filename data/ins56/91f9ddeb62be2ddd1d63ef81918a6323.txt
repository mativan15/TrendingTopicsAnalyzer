Published: 19:57 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 19:57 EDT, 30 May 2022 Another dismal round in an already horrorible season will see McLaren get rid of Australian F1 star Daniel Ricciardo, a former F1 champion has declared.F1 legend Jacques Villeneuve won the 1997 Formula One World Championship with Williams and is a current pundit and analyst on all things F1.He didn't mince his words after Ricciardo crashed in practice and could only manage a 13th-place finish in Monaco on the weekend, crossing the line well behind younger teammate Lando Norris who came in sixth despite battling the effects of tonsillitis.
