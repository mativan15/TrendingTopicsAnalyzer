Family of Premium Bakeries to Continue Growing Products and Solutions Under Shared Identity

CHICAGO, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Main Street Gourmet, Biscotti Brothers Bakery, and Meurer Brothers Bakery have launched a new brand identity for their combined business: Sweetmore Bakeries. Collectively, Sweetmore Bakeries focuses on high quality and innovative products to help customers differentiate their sweet baked goods and improve operations.

Sweetmore Bakeries Chief Executive Officer, David Veenstra, says the name helps unify the bakeries internally and externally.
