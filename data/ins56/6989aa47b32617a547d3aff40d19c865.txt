Salesforce.com: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot
sfgate.com
business
2022-05-31 20:16:43

SAN FRANCISCO (AP) _ Salesforce.com Inc. (CRM) on Tuesday reported fiscal first-quarter net income of $28 million.The San Francisco-based company said it had net income of 3 cents per share. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to 98 cents per share. The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of 15 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 93 cents per share. The customer-management software developer posted revenue of $7.41 billion in the period, which also topped Street forecasts.
