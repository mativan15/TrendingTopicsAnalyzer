China's Xi opens Party Congress with speech tackling Taiwan, Hong Kong and zero-Covid
ctvnews.ca
world
2022-10-17 00:04:10

Chinese leader Xi Jinping on Sunday vowed to steer China through grave challenges toward national rejuvenation, advancing a nationalistic vision that has put it on a collision path with the West. Speaking at the opening of the 20th Party Congress, where he is poised to secure a norm-breaking third term in power, Xi struck a confident tone, highlighting China's growing strength and rising influence under his first decade in power. But he also repeatedly underscored the risks and challenges the country faces.
