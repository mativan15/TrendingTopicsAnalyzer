Yorkshire pub crowned best Sunday lunch in the land
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 08:51:00

Queen o' t' owd Thatch, North Yorks (Image: Facebook )A Yorkshire pub has been voted the winner of Best Sunday Lunch in the UK for the second time.The Queen o' t' owd Thatch in South Milford, near Selby, North Yorks, received the Observer Food Monthly gong for Best Sunday Lunch 2022.It's the second time the pub restaurant has scooped the gong as it also won the category in 2018 prior to the pandemic.Owners Kirsty Cheetham, 42, and Annie French, 51, picked up the prestigious trophy at Freemason's Hall in London on October 13.
