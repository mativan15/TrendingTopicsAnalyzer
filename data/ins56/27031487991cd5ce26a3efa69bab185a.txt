Assam CM distributes sanction letters to PMAY, PM Kisan Samman Nidhi beneficiaries
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 21:00:00

Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma on the occasion of Garib Kalyan Sammelan where in Prime Minister Narendra Modi interacted virtually with the beneficiaries of government schemes, distributed sanction letters to the beneficiaries of PMAY and PM Kisan Samman Nidhi at a programme held at Gargaon in Sivasagar district today. It may be noted that synchronising with the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav to commemorate 75 years of India's independence, Prime Minister Modi addressed the beneficiaries at a programme held in Shimla.
