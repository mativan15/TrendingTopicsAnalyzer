October 17, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--U.S. Physical Therapy, Inc. ("USPH" or the 'Company') (NYSE: USPH), a national operator of outpatient physical therapy clinics and provider of industrial injury prevention services, announced today that the operations of 51 of the Company's 614 clinics were affected by Hurricane Ian. Due to the high winds, loss of electrical power, flooding and road closures attributed to the severe storm, U.S. Physical Therapy's business was interrupted in large parts of the states of Florida, Georgia, and South Carolina.
