Meghan Markle has had some iconic fashion moments so far in 2022. These include her stunning, sleek and sophisticated looks from the Queen's Jubilee to the Invictus Games. But what has been her most expensive outfit thus far?Insurance provider, wearesosure.com, analysed social media and Google Trends data to break down Meghan's top outfits from head to toe, to calculate the value of her entire wardrobe.The Duchess' most costly outfit so far in 2022, according to the research, was worn at her first public engagement of the year, back in February, but was made so expensive by one particular jewel.
