New Delhi, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Global Single-Use Assemblies market is growing at a high CAGR due to the rising use of Single-Use Assemblies to lower the danger of cross-contamination and conserve energy and water. Additionally, it is anticipated that Single-Use Assemblies would expand significantly due to the expanding public-private support for these operations and the simple development of bioprocessing equipment. Over the next few years, the market for single-use bodies is anticipated to rise in response to the rising need for generic medications and biosimilars.
