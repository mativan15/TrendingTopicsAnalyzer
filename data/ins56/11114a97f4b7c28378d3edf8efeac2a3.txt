Modern workplace culture is changing: Four ways it's impacting your company's network security
techradar.com
tech
2022-10-17 13:14:57

The way employees work in today's tech-driven office has changed significantly over the last few years. Working from home is becoming increasingly common. Cloud applications – Salesforce, Google Docs, Microsoft Office, etc. – are the gold standard because they allow employees to do their jobs from anywhere. And employees are using their own mobile phones and laptops to access work documents and emails remotely.    The good news in all of this is that employees are now often happier and more productive than they have been in the past.
