Colorado's two most competitive congressional races see glut of money as midterm nears
canoncitydailyrecord.com
news
2022-10-18 12:00:29

The Democratic contenders in Colorado's two most competitive congressional races have raised far more money than their Republican rivals but will that extra firepower pay off at the ballot box amid a stumbling economy, runaway inflation and unabated crime?
It's a question the congressional campaigns in the 7th and 8th congressional districts — stretching from Westcliffe to Greeley and home to nearly 1.5 million Coloradans — are badly trying to decipher as the election looms just three weeks away and ballots hit mailboxes this week.
