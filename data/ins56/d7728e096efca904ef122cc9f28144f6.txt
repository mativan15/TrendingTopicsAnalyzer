Far-right Giorgia Meloni appointed 1st female prime minister – DW – 10/21/2022

2022-10-21 16:03:55

Giorgia Meloni, the leader of the post-fascist Brothers of Italy party, has formed Italy's most right-wing government since Benito Mussolini. Italian politician Giorgia Meloni has been appointed Italy's first female prime minister and formed a right-wing coalition government on Friday. Meloni and her coalition partners, Silvio Berlusconi, leader of the center-right Forza Italia, and Matteo Salvini, leader of anti-migrant League party, met Italian President Sergio Mattarella Friday at the presidential palace.
