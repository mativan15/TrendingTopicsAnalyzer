Council Post: The Secret Struggles Behind Being An Entrepreneur
forbes.com

What do you associate with entrepreneurship? When most people think of entrepreneurs, they think of money, fame and charisma. After all, the most successful entrepreneurs attract the most attention and usually possess these things. However, no matter how much an entrepreneur demonstrates an aura of effortlessness, I've found that there is a secret that ties all entrepreneurs together. As an entrepreneur, your goal may be to earn a lifestyle that's enviable, but the path to that destination is anything but.
