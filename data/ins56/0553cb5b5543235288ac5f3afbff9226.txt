UK stocks and pound rise as PM Liz Truss quits, US stocks hit by rate fears
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-20 20:54:39

The UK's benchmark index has closed higher after the UK Prime Minister Liz Truss stepped down amid political and financial turmoil in that country, and US stocks have ended lower overnight as interest rate fears resurface. In London, the FTSE 100 index rose 0.3 per cent, to 6,944, and the pound jumped to 1.13 US dollarsMeanwhile, the Dow Jones index in the US fell 0.3 per cent, to 30,334, the S&P 500 lost 0.8 per cent, to 3,666, and the Nasdaq Composite rose 0.6 per cent, to 10,615The Australian dollar had risen 0.
