We won't see the first-ever commercial spacewalk this year after all.SpaceX's private Polaris Dawn mission to Earth orbit has been pushed from late 2022 to no earlier than March 2023, according to the website of the Polaris Program (opens in new tab), the organization planning the flight.Polaris Dawn will lift off atop a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket from Launch Complex 39A at NASA's Kennedy Space Center in Florida.
