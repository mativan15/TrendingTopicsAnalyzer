ANIIndore, Dec 25 (ANI): Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan addresses the inauguration of Indore Metro Rail Line and 16 Metro Station, in Indore on Saturday. (ANI Photo)Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan on Tuesday said that Indore will soon become the IT hub of the country. "In the next 10 years, Indore will surpass Bengaluru and Hyderabad. Indore will become the IT hub of the country. Around 100 new startup companies were opened recently. We will take the startup ecosystem here to the international level," Chouhan said while addressing an event in Indore.
