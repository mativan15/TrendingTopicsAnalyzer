Bournemouth v Southampton: Ché Adams' derby winner brings relief to beleaguered Ralph Hasenhüttl
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 20:50:00

The night ended with Ralph Hasenhüttl, hand in hand with his players, basking in the acclaim of the away support. It could have been so different.The Austrian lives to fight another day as Southampton manager. It wasn't pretty and this was far from the complete performance but nobody could accuse his players of not fighting for him as they ground out a precious victory thanks to Ché Adams' early goal.It may prove a temporary reprieve but four points from two games after a dismal run gives Hasenhüttl hope.
