Amitbh Bachchan has been sharing several interesting anecdotes from his films, about his co-stars and his family while hosting the 14th season of television quiz show Kaun Banega Crorepati.
On the latest episode of KBC14, while quizzing contestant Suraj Das, Bachchan revealed that during the filming of Muqaddar Ka Sikandar he accidentally hurt his co-star, the late actor Vinod Khanna and he had to be rushed to the hospital.
 
It was during the game that Bachchan asked Das a question about a song from his film Muqaddar Ka Sikandar.
