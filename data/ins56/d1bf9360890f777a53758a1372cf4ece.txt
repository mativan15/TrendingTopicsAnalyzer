Super Micro Computer raises FQ1 2023 guidance, stock soars ~18% after hours
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-19 21:53:14

Kwarkot/iStock via Getty Images Super Micro Computer (NASDAQ:SMCI) on Wednesday raised its expected net sales and non-GAAP EPS ranges for FQ1 2023. Shares of the information technology company jumped 17.8% to $65.20 in aftermarket trading. The company said it now expects Q1 net sales of $1.78B to $1.82B. It had previously guided Q1 net sales of $1.52B to $1.62B. The consensus revenue estimate is $1.57B. SMCI also raised its non-GAAP EPS forecast to a range of $3.05 to $3.20 from a prior outlook of $2.
