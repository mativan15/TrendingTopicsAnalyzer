October 19, 2022 04:56 PM Eastern Daylight Time CEDAR RAPIDS, Iowa--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AAVIN Equity Advisors, LLC ('AAVIN'), a Cedar Rapids-based private equity firm, announced today the closing of AAVIN Capital III, L.P. ('AAVIN III') with total capital commitments of $63 million and a license to operate as a Small Business Investment Company ('SBIC'). AAVIN III received strong support from its existing investors and expanded its capital base with new institutional investors. AAVIN III continues the strategy of the previous two AAVIN funds and the firm's predecessor organization, Allsop Venture Partners.
