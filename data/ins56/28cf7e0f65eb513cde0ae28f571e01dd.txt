Published: 16:02 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 16:02 EDT, 21 October 2022 Former President Donald Trump suggested it would be traitorous behavior if former Vice President Mike Pence or other Trump Cabinet officials tried to run against him in 2024. 'I think it'd be very disloyal,' the ex-president said Friday in a radio interview with Fox's Brian Kilmeade.Kilmeade brought up the names of Pence, former Secretary of State Mike Pompeo and former U.N. Amb. Nikki Haley, who have long hinted they have future political ambitions.
