Jun. 3—COLUMBUS — Nick Biega knows as well as anyone by now what Day 1 at the state track and field meet is all about.

Handle your business, and get back to your hotel somewhere in the Greater Columbus area.

The Mayfield junior, and Ohio for that matter, were thrown a proverbial curveball as Biega competed in Division I preliminaries, though.

Biega recorded a 10.64 in the 100-meter dash and a 21.50 in 200, two outstanding times to take into Day 2 finals June 4.

But the news wasn't what was on the track, but rather who was not.
