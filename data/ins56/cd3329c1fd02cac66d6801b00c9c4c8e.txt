Cambium enters the audio podcast product market with Audio Array
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-17 13:05:27

India, 17th October 2022: India's podcast market is growing at a brisk pace. The country now has the third-largest listener base globally, just behind the US and China. The listener base is expected to grow to 90 million by this year-end. To provide a great listening experience to listeners, podcasters look for high-quality output podcasting equipment. Recognizing this need for quality podcasting equipment by discerning customers, Cambium Retail Pvt Limited (CRPL) is introducing its own brand of high-quality audio products under Audio Array.
