Australian Economy Under Pressure Amid Strong Inflation, But Signs Of Price Peak Appear
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-18 15:10:00

sasirin pamai The release of the Australia flash PMI on the 24th of October will be eagerly awaited after recent PMI survey data, compiled by S&P Global, signalled that inflationary pressures remained a major risk to the health of Australia's private sector economy. September saw steep price rises and subsequent interest rate hikes acting to subdue demand and business activity. Business confidence meanwhile slumped to the lowest since the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic. The performance of the economy in 2023 will depend in large part on how resilient demand can be in the face of a range of challenges.
