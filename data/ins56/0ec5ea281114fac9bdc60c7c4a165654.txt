Man fatally shoots wife in front of crying daughter: Police
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 09:56:19

A man is alleged to have shot his wife dead in front of their crying daughter, according to Florida police.Oscar Edmundo Marin Martinez, 42, allegedly shot his wife seven times in the head and neck while they were sitting in the front seats of a pickup truck in a parking lot at 12490 Southwest 5th Street, just before midnight on Saturday.The shooting occurred as their 5-year-old daughter sat crying in the back seat, per a Davie police report obtained by NBC 6.
Stock image of a police car. Oscar Edmundo Marin Martinez was arrested in connection with the shooting.
