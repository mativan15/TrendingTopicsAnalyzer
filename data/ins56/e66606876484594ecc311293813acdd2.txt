At 1132, India Has Third Highest Number Of 'Centi-millionaires' In The World: Report
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 10:32:31

On one hand, much of the world is grappling with a cost-of-living crisis amid high inflation and deepening recession fears. On the other hand, a new fast-growing and powerful class of super-rich titans, financiers, CEOs of multinationals and heirs has emerged with a mammoth wealth of over $100 million (approximately Rs 830 crore). They are known as centi-millionaires.According to the world's first-ever global study of centi-millionaires, India currently has 1,132 centi-millionaires (individuals with investable assets of more than $100 million), the third-highest number in the world, beating the UK, Switzerland and Russia.
