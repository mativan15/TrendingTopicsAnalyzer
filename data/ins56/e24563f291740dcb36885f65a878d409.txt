ASX set to drop as Wall Street slides to close a messy May
brisbanetimes.com.au
business
2022-05-31 19:22:38

US stocks are in negative territory in late trade, as Wall Street nears the end of a tumultuous month, bruised by worries about a possible recession, inflation and rising interest rates.The S&P 500 was down 0.6 per cent in late trade, after briefly erasing all of an earlier drop of 1.3 per cent during the morning. The Dow Jones Industrial Average was down 0.6 per cent while the Nasdaq composite has shed 0.4 per cent. Both came back from earlier losses of at least 1.4 per cent. The Australian sharemarket is set to fall, with futures at 5.
