Shoham, Israel, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) — Mobilicom Limited (Mobilicom or the Company, NASDAQ: MOB, MOBBW, ASX: MOB), a provider of cybersecurity and smart solutions for drones, robotics, and autonomous platforms, announces that Mobilicom CEO Oren Elkayam will be presenting at the 2022 ThinkEquity Conference in New York on Wednesday, October 26, 2022.
The ThinkEquity Conference will feature presentations from emerging leaders spanning the technology, artificial intelligence, big data, biotechnology, metals & mining, and oil & gas sectors.
