Earthquake of magnitude 3.5 strikes Gujarat
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 07:30:00

AgenciesRepresentative imageGujarat's Surat on Thursday reported an earthquake of magnitude 3.5 on the Richter Scale, informed the National Center for Seismology (NCS). The movement of tectonic plates under the earth's surface was felt around 10.26 am in 61 kilometres Southeast of Surat, reported ANI citing NCS. Notably, Prime Minister Narendra Modi is also in his hometown and is scheduled to launch Mission LiFE in Kevadia today.
 The LiFE's launch will take place after the Prime Minister's bilateral meeting with UN Secretary-General, Antonio Guterres in Kevadia.
