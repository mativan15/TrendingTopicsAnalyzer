Propofol drug incident: Have no further role in investigation, says Chandigarh health department
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-17 23:01:15

A few days after the Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER) submitted an internal committee's report on the death of five people, suspected to have been caused by the Propofol drug, to the UT administration, health department authorities claimed that they do not have any further role in investigating the matter and the Centre will now take required action.Director health services, Chandigarh, Dr Suman Singh in an official note, said, 'There is no further role of the Drug Control Authority, Chandigarh, and required actions are being taken by the Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation.
