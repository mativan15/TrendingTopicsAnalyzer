In our work at AgingParents.com, we often hear from adult children that the elder in their lives absolutely doesn't want to be 'put in a home'. By that they typically mean that they fear giving up their own living situation and being forced to go elsewhere for some form of care. They extract promises from their loved ones that they'll never do that. There are times when family can't take care of the aging parent at home and a living situation with caregivers in place is a solution. But for some with ruthless adult children, the nightmare comes true.
