Setting up a budget is an important part of managing your money. You can figure out how much you earn, record all your major monthly expenses, and then make a plan for how to allocate your after-tax income to ensure those bills are covered—including an emergency fund.  'A budget ensures you're going to be able to do fun things that you want to do, while working toward more serious financial goals,' says Julie Everett, a certified financial planner with Financial Finesse.  But the hard part might be sticking to the budget you make.
