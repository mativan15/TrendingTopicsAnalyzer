Naveen Patnaik urges biz captains to come and invest in Odisha

BHUBANESWAR: Odisha with its rich natural resource advantage and strategic location is in a take-off stage and will witness high growth in the next 10 years, chief minister Naveen Patnaik told 20 top investors in Hyderabad in a roadshow on Monday.Ahead of the Make in Odisha (MIO) conclave, the state's flagship business summit scheduled from November 30 to December 4 in Bhubaneswar."We are now progressing fast in every sector. On the strength of our abundant natural resources, skilled human resources and stable governance, Odisha is emerging as a leading destination for industries and investment," he said, while addressing the Odisha Investors' Meet in the Telangana capital, which was attended by several top industry captains.
