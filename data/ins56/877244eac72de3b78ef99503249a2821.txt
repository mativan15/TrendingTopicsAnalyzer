AI-powered supplier intelligence provider recognized for its innovative and powerful solutions

ATLANTA, October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Scoutbee, the leading AI-powered supplier intelligence and discovery platform, today announced it has been recognized as one of Spend Matters' "50 Providers to Watch" for 2022.

Each year, the "50 Providers to Watch" list recognizes fast-rising companies in the procurement and supply chain market. These are the up-and-coming solution providers that continue to grow and develop innovative products propelling the market forward.
