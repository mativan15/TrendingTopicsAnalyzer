Email: amichael@forbesadvisor.com 27 May: Luna Relaunches On New Blockchain The Luna cryptocurrency is relaunching on a new blockchain, two weeks after playing a role in the collapse of the Terra blockchain. The original Terra blockchain had two tokens, luna and stablecoin terraUSD (UST). Luna played a part in pegging UST to the US Dollar, but when UST lost its 1:1 pegging with the US fiat currency, the Terra algorithm began issuing more luna coins to rebalance the system.
