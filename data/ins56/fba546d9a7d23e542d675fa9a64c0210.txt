Earlier this year, a horrific fire in the Bronx that killed 17 people once again brought attention to the hazards of home space heaters. Space heaters can be a welcome addition to your central heating system or a handy and inexpensive way to heat a space without fixed heat. But it's also extremely important to exercise great caution and care when using a space heater. According to the US Consumer Product Safety Commission, space heaters are responsible for more than 25,000 house fires every year, in addition to a number of deaths and injuries.
