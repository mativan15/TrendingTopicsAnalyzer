LOS ANGELES, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Valiant Eagle, Inc. (OTC Pink: PSRU) is proud to announce that it has entered into a letter of intent (LOI) with the American Basketball Association (ABA) to granting the company the right to create an officially-licensed ABA video game. The LOI gives VE Gaming the ability to create an 'NBA 2K" styled video game that utilizes ABA teams, players, and other related content. Under the terms of the LOI agreement, VE Gaming and the ABA will utilize a revenue-sharing model, which will be finalized at a later date.
