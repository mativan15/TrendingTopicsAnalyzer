Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers has won back-to-back MVP awards. ASSOCIATED PRESS Green Bay is coming off its third straight 13-win season, making it the first team in NFL history to accomplish that feat. The problem is the Packers are just 2-3 in the playoffs in that time, losing NFC Championship Games in 2019 and 2020, and falling in the divisional playoffs last year. Green Bay had a tumultuous offseason, trading All-Pro wide receiver Davante Adams and losing star pass rusher Za'Darius Smith in free agency.
