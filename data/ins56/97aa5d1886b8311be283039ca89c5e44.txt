A solar plant in Pavagada Tumkur district in Karnataka (AP Photo/Rafiq Maqbool)Infrastructure and energy are important pillars in our journey towards becoming a developed country. The government is emphasizing developing non-fossil fuel energy and solar energy has already taken a quantum jump. We have achieved our target of 40 percent power generation from green sources as per the 2016 Paris Climate Accords well ahead of time, according to Sudhanshu Trivedi, MP Rajya Sabha and Member of the Standing Committee on Energy.
