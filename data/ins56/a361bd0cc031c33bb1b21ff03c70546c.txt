New analysis from the Global CCS Institute finds that there are over 160 commercial CCS projects in development worldwide as the response to climate change advances from ambition to action.
MELBOURNE, Australia, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Carbon capture and storage (CCS) continues to grow worldwide, according to a new report released by climate think tank the Global CCS Institute. The Global Status of CCS 2022 report reveals a record high of 196 commercial CCS facilities in the project pipeline, including 30 projects in operation, 11 under construction, and 153 in development.
