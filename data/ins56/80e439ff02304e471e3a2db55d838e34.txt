Why You Shouldn't Rush to Open a CD, Even Though Interest Rates Are Rising
fool.com
news
2022-10-20 11:00:54

The Federal Reserve is on a mission to slow the pace of inflation. To achieve that goal, it's been aggressively implementing interest rate hikes that are driving borrowing costs up. That's bad news for consumers, because now, it's more expensive to carry a credit card balance or sign an auto loan. The good news, though, is that a rise in interest rates is making savings accounts and CDs a more lucrative prospect.Over the past few months, both savings accounts and CDs have been offering higher interest rates to customers.
