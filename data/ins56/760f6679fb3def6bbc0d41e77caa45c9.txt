Sony Expects PS5 Shortage To Continue Until 2024
ign.com
news
2022-05-27 07:05:40

Gamers will continue to experience difficulty purchasing the PlayStation 5, as Sony expects the ongoing console shortage to continue until 2024. Sony recently released a Business Segment Briefing for 2022, suggesting that PS5 supply concerns will likely persist for a few years, Gamerant reports. On one page of the document, Sony shared a few mitigation efforts in the hopes of resolving the problem, including sourcing from several vendors and negotiating for the best PS5 logistics. The document also said the demand for the PS5 system is at an all-time high.
