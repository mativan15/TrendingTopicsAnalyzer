Published: 15:39 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 15:39 EDT, 15 October 2022 Elon Musk's SpaceX has donated some 25,000 ground terminals to Ukraine for use of its Starlink satellite internet, according to Musk Elon Musk said Saturday that his company SpaceX will continue to pay for Starlink satellite internet in war-torn Ukraine, one day after the tech mogul suggested he could not keep funding the project. Starlink, a constellation of more than 3,000 small satellites in low Earth orbit, has been vital to Ukraine's communications as it battles Russia.
