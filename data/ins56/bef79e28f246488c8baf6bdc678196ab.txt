Kenan Institute study ranks Charlotte, Raleigh among nation's fastest-growing local economies
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 11:50:00

The Charlotte area will finish this year as one of the nation's 10 fastest-growing economies, according to data analysis and projections by the Kenan Institute of Private Enterprise at UNC Chapel Hill.

According to the report, released Tuesday morning, Charlotte ranks 8 th among the 50 largest metro areas in the U.S. for gross domestic product growth at 2.5%.

The study is the first from the American Growth Project, a Kenan Institute research series that will examine so-called microeconomies in cities, towns, suburbs, and rural communities by analyzing how they differ and how they align.
