Storypod Signs Licensing Agreement with Maya the Bee, Expanding Its Audio Library For Children From Infancy to Six Years Old

2022-10-18 19:45:00

Storypod is expanding it's audio library with Maya the Bee content this fall. 'Our team has been fans of the French-German computer-animated series and are honored to be able to bring Maya the Bee to a new generation of eager learners,' said Daniel Buelhoff, founder of Storypod. MIAMI (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Storypod announces its partnership with Studio 100 Media to add the popular Maya the Bee stories to its smart audio system for young learners. Based on the television series teaching kids about bees through its spunky main character Maya, the award winning Storypod allows children to use their imagination while listening to tales of the adventurous bee.
