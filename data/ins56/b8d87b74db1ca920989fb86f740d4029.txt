Embassy REIT awarded 5-star rating by GRESB
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 12:00:00

iStockGRESB has become the leading ESG benchmark for real estate and infrastructure investments across the world.India's first listed REIT and the largest office REIT in Asia by area has been awarded a 5-star rating by GRESB, the leading global ESG standard for real estate and infrastructure investments, for its entire 42.8 msf operational and development portfolio. GRESB has also recognized Embassy REIT as a sector leader for office development in Asia for its sustainability leadership, a milestone for an Indian REIT.
