Max Financial shares gain 0.81% as Sensex rises

Shares of the Max Financial Services Ltd. traded at Rs 719.0 on BSE at 10:51AM (IST) on Monday, up 0.81 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 664.95 and a high of Rs 1081.0. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap up opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 86.12 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 8.36 per share and 6.61 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 370,116 shares changed hands on the counter till 10:51AM (IST). The stock commands a market value of Rs 24848.
