Rules need to be calibrated to address risks from big tech

The Reserve Bank of India and other global financial market regulators need to calibrate the regulatory frameworks with a mix of entity- and activity-based rules to address the risks emanating from the inter-linkages between bigtech companies and their role as critical service providers for financial institutions, a RBI research paper has stated. This is important for protecting data privacy and financial stability, as the regulators face the challenges of keeping up the pace with innovations by bigtech companies.
