Ashish Kacholia increases stake in this smallcap stock
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 07:30:00

Ace investor Ashish Kacholia increased his stake in smallcap stock SJS Enterprises during the September quarter.According to the shareholding pattern filed by the company for the September quarter, Kacholia held 11,69,839 equity shares, equating to a 3.84% stake in the company. In the previous quarter, the veteran investor held 11,48,342 equity shares or a 3.77% stake in SJS Enterprises.The investor holds stake in 39 listed stocks, with an estimated net worth of around Rs 1,776.5 crore, shows Trendlyne data.
