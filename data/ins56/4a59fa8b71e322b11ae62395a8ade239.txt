Navi Mumbai: Airoli woman seeking job through online portal loses Rs 23,000 to cyber fraudster
indiatimes.com
science
2022-06-02 05:12:00

NAVI MUMBAI: A 26-year-old woman residing in Airoli area of Navi Mumbai has become a victim of cyber fraudster, who duped her of Rs 23,000.The fraudster posing as a representative of a job portal and made her transfer the payment as processing fees, to his e-wallet under pretext of offering her a chemist's job in a reputed Pharmaceutical company.Acting on a complaint by the victim woman, Rabale police have registered a cheating case under relevant IPC section and Information Technology Act.Rabale police informed that the complainant woman resides with her parents in sector 19, Airoli and works as a chemist in a pharmaceutical company.
