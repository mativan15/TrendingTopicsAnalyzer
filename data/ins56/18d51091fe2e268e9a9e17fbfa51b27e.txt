By Reuters Published: 09:24 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 09:24 EDT, 19 October 2022 Oct 19 (Reuters) - U.S. equities have priced in the most recession risk compared with other assets, Citigroup researchers said, warning that was not enough as further losses loom in the wake of an "unusual" time for economies. The benchmark S&P 500 index is firmly in bear market, down about 22% year-to-date, as investors worried over rising interest rates, record inflation and the lingering impact of global-supply chain snags turned risk averse.
