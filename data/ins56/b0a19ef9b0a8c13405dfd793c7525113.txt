From welfare to wealth: Why Modi govt needs to leverage its first eight years for a big shift
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 19:30:00

There are a couple of ways to look at Narendra Modi's eight years in power. One would be the matter-of-fact way that it's just a second term of a prime minister. The other, as the party believes, is to see it as the foundational years of a long BJP reign. While the first chooses to ignore changing political realities, the other seems eager to extrapolate them. Realistically, what we are staring at is a possible middle-of-an-era milestone for Modi. In many ways, 2022 could go down as turning point in the Modi era.
