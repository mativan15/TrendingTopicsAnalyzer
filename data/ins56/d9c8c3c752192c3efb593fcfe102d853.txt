Toshiba's shares rise after it reveals buyout bids
businesslive.co.za
news
2022-06-02 07:21:19

Toshiba says it has received eight offers to buy out the conglomerate and two proposals for capital and business alliances as the Japanese industrial giant moves a step closer to a possible privatisation.The Tokyo-based company outlined the number of non-binding offers received in a statement on Thursday, without disclosing the bidders. It will evaluate the proposals and choose one or more of them to pursue, as soon as possible after the annual shareholder meeting scheduled for June 28.Toshiba's shares rose as much as 1.
