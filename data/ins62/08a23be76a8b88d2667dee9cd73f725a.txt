Buying a home is a major financial decision, and interest rate levels have a major impact on how much your mortgage will cost you over the years. When you choose to become a homeowner, you'll want to secure the right type of mortgage at the lowest possible rate, especially since rates are expected to continue rising over the course of this year. If you can manage a higher monthly payment, a15-year mortgage is an attractive option if you're looking to pay off your home loan sooner while saving on interest.
