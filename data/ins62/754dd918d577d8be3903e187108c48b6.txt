How it feels to get ‘dragged' online
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 23:01:00

Last Wednesday morning, at the precise moment an Ofcom report into the UK's social media habits declared tech companies must do more to protect women online, I (a woman) was being trolled on Twitter because of an article I'd written for The Times about the trolling of Amber Heard (another woman).Give or take the one American male lawyer who (according to a friend, I've yet to watch it) 'ripped into' me on YouTube, my trolling was perpetrated by other women — just as the trolling of Heard had been.
