The Central Coast community is being invited to help shape the future of the Gosford waterfront and have its say on the exciting revitalisation project set to boost liveability and economic growth across the entire region.The Central Coast Council has adopted a concept plan for the Gosford waterfront, a starting point for the community's input, with the plan now on public exhibition for community members to submit their ideas, feedback and opinions.Council worked with key stakeholders to develop the concept plan including the Darkinjung Aboriginal Land Council, Greater Cities Commission, Crown Lands, TAFE and The University of Newcastle.
