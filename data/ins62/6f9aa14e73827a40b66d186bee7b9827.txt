Famed 60/40 portfolio is so beaten down it's almost cheap again
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 02:30:00

Blame the Fed, war and fiscal profligacy all you want. But big trouble was lurking in many widely followed portfolio strategies long before those threats took hold. That's the upshot of new research that uses a yield-derived valuation model to show the famous 60/40 allocation reached its most expensive level in almost five decades during the Covid-19 rally. The situation has reversed in 2022, which is now by some definitions the worst year ever for the bond-and-equities cocktail. The data is a harsh reminder of the primacy of valuation in determining returns.
