Emma Hayward and her family had to pay out for new clothes on their first holiday since the pandemic began as their suitcases did not arrive in FuerteventuraEmma and her family have had to buy new clothes A family was forced to spend €500 on new clothes after all of their suitcases were lost on a flight to a Spanish island. Emma Hayward, 44, her husband, children and elderly mother found themselves without any clothes or essentials when they arrived in Fuerteventura. After arriving on Saturday from Manchester Airport they still have no idea where their bags are.
