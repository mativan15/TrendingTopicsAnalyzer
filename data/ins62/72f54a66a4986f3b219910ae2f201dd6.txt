MCLEAN, Va., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Iridium Communications Inc. (Nasdaq:IRDM) ("Iridium") today reported financial results for the third quarter of 2022 and updated its full-year 2022 outlook. Net income was $2.1 million, or $0.02 per diluted share, for the third quarter of 2022, as compared to net loss of $2.1 million, or $0.02 per diluted share, for the third quarter of 2021. This change in net income was primarily the result of growth in total revenue, which was broad based. Operational EBITDA ("OEBITDA")(1) for the third quarter was up 8% to a record $107.
