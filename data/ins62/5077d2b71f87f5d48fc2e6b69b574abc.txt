Help bring home body of Kerala ‘human sacrifice' victim, PMK tells state govt
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-16 04:00:00

Dharmapuri: Rajya Sabha MP and PMK president Dr Anbumani Ramadoss on Saturday urged the state government to help the sons of the Kerala ‘human sacrifice' victim Padma to bring her mutilated body to Errapatty village as early as possible.In a media statement, Anbumani Ramadoss said the Kerala government hadn't handed over Padma's body to her sons Seddu and Selvaraj , even after taking their blood samples for the DNA test.'The officials there even refused to tell the reason for the delay. The state government should initiate talks with its Kerala counterpart and help the Padma's family receive her dead body at the earliest to perform final rites as per the Tamil culture and tradition,' the PMK leader said.
