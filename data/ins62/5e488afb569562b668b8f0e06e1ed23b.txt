The annual report highlights the company's enterprise-wide commitment to caring for people, the planet and communitiesKANSAS CITY, Mo., Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- For the sixth consecutive year, Hallmark Cards, Inc. shared the results of its enterprise-wide corporate social responsibility (CSR) efforts with the release of the Caring in Action report. The report focuses on the company's impact in the areas of diversity, equity and inclusion; community; and sustainability during 2021.
