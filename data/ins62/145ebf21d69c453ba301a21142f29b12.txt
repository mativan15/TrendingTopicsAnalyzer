Hardik Pandya's Gujarat Titans thrashed Rajasthan Royals by seven wickets in the Indian Premier League (IPL) final on Sunday, with the skipper taking three wickets and scoring 34 runs in front of home fans. Gujarat were playing the game at the Narendra Modi Stadium which accommodated over 105,000 fans for the title showdown. Hardik has had his fair share of injuries in the past couple of years. But the flamboyant all-rounder, who previously was associated with Mumbai Indians, shone in his fresh IPL chapter with the home franchise.
