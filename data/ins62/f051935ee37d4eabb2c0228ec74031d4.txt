As someone who is working very closely on some of the behind-the-scenes elements of the metaverse, Riccitiello is in a unique position to see through the (considerable) hype to what's really possible within the next few years.
John Riccitiello sits atop a company, Unity Technologies, that provides perhaps the most widely used game development engine in the world. Unity is used to create games for mobile, consoles, PCs, and AR/VR—the latter of which, you may have heard, is a key component of the so-called metaverse.
