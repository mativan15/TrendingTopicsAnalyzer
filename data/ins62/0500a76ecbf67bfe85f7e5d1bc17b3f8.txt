NYSE American to Suspend Trading Immediately in Warrants of Ault Disruptive Technologies Corporation (ADRTW) and Commence Delisting Proceedings
businesswire.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

October 20, 2022 05:34 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NYSE American LLC ('NYSE American' or the 'Exchange') announced today that the staff of NYSE Regulation has determined to commence proceedings to delist the warrants of Ault Disruptive Technologies Corporation (the 'Company'), each warrant exercisable for one share of Common Stock— ticker symbol ADRTW — from the Exchange. Trading in the Company's warrants will be suspended immediately. Trading in the Company's common stock — ticker symbol ADRT — and units — ticker symbol ADRTU — will continue on the NYSE American.
