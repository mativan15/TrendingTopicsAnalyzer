Himachal Pradesh will also change tradition of voting out govt: Amit Shah
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-15 17:45:00

ANIAmit Shah addresses the 'Hati Aabhar Rally' in SirmaurUnion home minister Amit Shah on Saturday said that after Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand, now Himachal Pradesh will also change the tradition of voting out the existing government and give a second chance to BJP in the state. At a public meeting in Sirmaur district, Shah also launched BJP's election theme song, 'Himachal ki pukar, phir Bajpa sarkar' (Himachal calls for BJP government again.).

The state assembly elections will take place on November 12.
