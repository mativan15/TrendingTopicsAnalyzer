REDONDO BEACH, Calif. (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Sandler Partners, a leading independent technology solutions distributor and one of the nation's fastest-growing private companies, welcomes Michele Johnson as Director, Channel – Southern California. In her new role, she will help build and support the greater Southern California market by assisting Partners in sourcing the ideal technology solutions for their customers. Michele has been in the industry for over 20 years, developing business and making connections within the Channel while representing notable brands Sungard and AT&T.
