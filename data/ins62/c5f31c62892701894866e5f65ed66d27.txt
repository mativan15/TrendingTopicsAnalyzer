Flight test: Flying Auckland to Singapore with Singapore Airlines

2022-06-04 23:15:00

Even if you are a seasoned flyer, long-haul international travel can be a bit daunting now. My advice? Check all your documentation, twice; give yourself plenty of time at the airport; bring several masks; then try to enjoy the journey. The route: Auckland to Singapore with Singapore Airlines. The plane: A350-900 long-haul range. Time in the air: We left Auckland at 2.10pm, and arrived in Singapore at 9pm local time, with a total flying time of 11 hours, 10 minutes.There are 42 business class seats in Singapore Airlines' A350-900.
