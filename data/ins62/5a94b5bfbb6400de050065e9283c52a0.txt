 An animal caused a large power outage in Toronto's east end Monday afternoon, Hydro One says.
In a tweet at around 1:30 p.m. on Monday, Toronto Hydro said it was experiencing a 'large area outage affecting customers in the east end,' adding that it was working with Hydro One to 'address it.'We're currently experiencing a large area outage affecting customers in the east end and are working together with @HydroOne to address it. We appreciate your patience.
