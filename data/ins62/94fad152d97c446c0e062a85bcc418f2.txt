Here are the 50 fastest-growing middle-market companies in North Texas
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 17:58:00

Plano-based LiquidAgents Healthcare earned the top ranking in the 2022 Middle Market 50, the Dallas Business Journal's annual ranking of the fastest-growing middle-market companies.

The health care staffing agency had a three-year revenue increase of 192.2% from 2019 to 2021 with 2021 revenue of $174.4 million.

The rankings were revealed at an event on Thursday. In its eighth year, the award defines the Middle Market 50 as companies with annual revenue between $10 million and $1 billion.

The companies were ranked using the percentage of total annual revenue over the past three years.
