NK may have attempted cyberattack amid Kakao outage: report
naver.com
news
2022-10-18 13:57:00

An attempted cyberattack that occurred during the recent outage of South Korea's biggest messenger service KakaoTalk may have been the work of North Korean hackers, a news outlet has reported.According to Radio Free Asia, various individuals working in services related to the communist country, along with several North Korean defectors received an email claiming to have been sent by the Kakao tech service team on Sunday, as the extended outage that began Saturday took down most of the tech giant's services.
