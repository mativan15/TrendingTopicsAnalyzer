ReutersJio will take the ECB route for the loan. It is in contact with BoA, BNP Paribas, HSBC and MUFG Bank, among others, for the purpose.Reliance Jio Infocomm is planning to raise an additional $1.5 billion (Rs 12,300 crore) via external commercial borrowings (ECBs) to fund its 5G capex, three persons familiar with the matter told ET. It has reached out to foreign banks including BNP Paribas, HSBC and MUFG Bank, they said, adding that India's telecom market leader is seeking to mop up five-year money via the ECB route, with Reserve Bank of India having relaxed norms a few months ago.
