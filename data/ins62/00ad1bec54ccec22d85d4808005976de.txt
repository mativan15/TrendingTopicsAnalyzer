Mica levy on construction set to be reduced from 10%
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:01:00

The government's controversial concrete levy of 10 per cent is to be reduced.Announced as part of the 2023 budget, the levy led to huge outcry from the opposition and government backbenchers as well as the construction industry.The Sunday Times has learnt that the levy will be reduced from the mooted 10 per cent and its planned introduction date of April 3 put back to later in the year.A number of ministers have reiterated their support for a levy on the industry to help pay for the multibillion-euro scheme to rebuild the homes damaged by mica.
