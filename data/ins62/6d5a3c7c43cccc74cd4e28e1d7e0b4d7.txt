Chad Scarah has admitted causing criminal damage after the midnight din when he threatened to attack his neighbour's son and 'smash his face in'Chad Scarah was woken up by his neighbour's loud musicA man hurled a glass through his neighbour's window after storming round to her house enraged that her loud music had woken him up. Chad Scarah ignored attempts to calm him down from the noise maker and threw a drinking glass at a window, smashing it into into pieces. He was woken up by loud music from the next door property and threatened to smash all the windows after the midnight din.
