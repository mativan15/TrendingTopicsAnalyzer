Published: 07:44 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 07:44 EDT, 3 June 2022 While Prince Harry is officially keeping a low profile during the four days of Platinum Jubilee events, the 37-year-old couldn't resist larking around in the pews of St Paul's, sharing a joke with Princess Eugenie's husband Jack Brooksbank on Friday. The 37-year-old Duke, wearing a black suit adorned with military medals and a silver tie, arrived for the 11:30am commemorative service with Meghan Markle, leaving the couple's children Archie, three, and Lilibet, nearly one, behind - and took seats alongside his cousins Princesses Eugenie and Beatrice and their respective husbands, Jack Brooksbank, 36, and Edo Mapelli Mozzi, 38.
