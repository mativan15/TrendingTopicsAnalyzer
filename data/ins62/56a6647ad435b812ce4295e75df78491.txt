REUTERS/Aly Song/File PhotoNew Delhi: Smartphone shipments fell 6% on-year in the third quarter ended September 2022, dragged by a lackluster low end segment, according to a report by Canalys.The third quarter saw smartphone shipments reaching 44.3 million units, which while is around 23% higher sequentially over the previous quarter, has declined on-year due to a strong base in Q3 2021.
