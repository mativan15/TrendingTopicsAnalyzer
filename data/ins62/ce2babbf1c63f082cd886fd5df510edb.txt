When the Toronto Police Services Board embarked two years ago on a process to hire a new police chief, it retained the polling firm Environics to determine the public mood. Environics reported that 'at some point in all the consultations, stakeholders referred to a perceived persistent lack of trust in the service.'

The Toronto Police Service needs significant structural change if it is to reflect the demands for a new and progressive approach to community safety and security, one that de-emphasizes policing and puts more resources into tackling the roots of crime and violence.
