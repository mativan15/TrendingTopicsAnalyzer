Chennai/New Delhi: As EV fires continue to make headlines in the country, a fire broke out at electric scooter manufacturer Ather Energy's experience centre in Chennai. The company said in a tweet that there has been a minor fire incident on its premises in Chennai."While some property and scooters got affected, thankfully all employees are safe and things are under control. The experience centre will be operational shortly," the EV company said late on Friday.It, however, did not reveal what caused the fire incident.
