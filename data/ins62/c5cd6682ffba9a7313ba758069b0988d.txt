A mum has been left baffled by a 'bonkers request' her neighbour made when she demanded that her kids stop riding their scooters on the footpath outside her houseThe neighbour hates hearing the kids ride scooters past her house (stock photo) A confused mum has vented her frustrations online after her neighbour demanded that her two children stop riding their scooters on the public footpath outside of her house - even though she has no claim to the area. In a post on Mumsnet, the woman explained her neighbour first knocked on her door and told her to stop letting her kids ride past her house, before she later approached the woman's daughter directly to demand that she doesn't ride on the footpath that runs past her property.
