By Wu Yong, Chen Xinyu and Song Nan | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-10-21 22:32 Share - WeChat Liu Chuanbo, staff member of State Grid Fushun Electric Power Supply Company, is a delegate from Liaoning province attending the 20th National Congress of Communist Party of China.
As a grassroots power supply worker, whose main job is to serve the people, Liu is committed to providing good technical services to users at home and in enterprises, helping them save on electricity, optimizing the business environment, and improving power accessibility.
