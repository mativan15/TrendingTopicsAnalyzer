Kitty Brown, 42, (Kāi Tahu) is the co-creator of Reo Pēpi, which produces bilingual books in Te Reo and English for children and has just published Matariki. She is married to Dan Parker, 39, a builder/stay-at-home father to the couple's children Tama Taituha, 10, and daughter Kuini, 3. They live in Portobello, Dunedin. Kitty I was 23 when I met Dan. I'd just broken up with the man I thought was the love of my life and had been pretty badly burned. I was broken-hearted and definitely wasn't looking for another relationship.
