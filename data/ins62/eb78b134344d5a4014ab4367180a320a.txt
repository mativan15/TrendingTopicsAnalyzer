Published: 12:10 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 12:34 EDT, 21 October 2022 Giorgia Meloni has formed Italy's new ruling coalition, giving the country its first far right-led government since the end of the Second World War and becoming the first woman to serve as the country's premier. A presidential palace official announced that Ms Meloni and her Cabinet would be sworn in on Saturday. Ms Meloni's Brothers of Italy, a party with neo-fascist roots, secured the most votes in Italy's national election last month.
