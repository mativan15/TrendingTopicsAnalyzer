SANTA CLARA, Calif. and OMAHA, Neb. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Former PayPal and eBay Executives Leverage Tech to Help Seniors Age in Place The HomeCare Advocacy Network (HCAN) and Zemplee, a technology platform that utilizes attentive artificial intelligence (AI) and passive sensors to help seniors age safely in their homes, today announced they are joining forces to deliver a high-tech, hybrid mode of combined home care.
