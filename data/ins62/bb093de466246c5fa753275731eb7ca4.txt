'Selling Sunset' star Maya Vander starts her own real estate group after saying goodbye to the cast in season 5
insider.com
news
2022-05-29 15:27:08

Maya Vander has officially branched out on her own. On Friday, the "Selling Sunset" star shared on Instagram that she launched "The Maya Vander Group" in Miami. "So excited to continue to share this new chapter. Miami, we're taking over!" she captioned the photo. "We are all working hard & pushing every day. We love and respect our clients, which is so important in order to be successful in real estate." A post shared by Maya Vander (SELLING SUNSET) (@themayavander) Vander's "Selling Sunset" costar and The Oppenheim Group boss Jason Oppenheim congratulated her by commenting, "Yes maya!!!!" The 40-year-old real estate agent told Insider that although it's being reported that she started her own real estate firm, that is not the case.
