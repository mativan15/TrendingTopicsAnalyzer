'Return To Work From Office Or Quit' Elon Musk's Warning To Tesla Employees
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-02 06:20:57

Seems like the world's richest person and billionaire Elon Musk has had enough of the 'work-from-home' phase. The Tesla CEO has sent a warning email to Tesla employees on the new work mandate.While the world has been witness to how companies and employees have temporarily or even permanently shifted to the work-from-home module ever since covid hit the world in 2020, the Tesla CEO doesn't seem to be a fan of employees staying away from the office.The Tesla chief has reportedly shot an elaborate email to all the employees, saying that remote work is no longer acceptable, urging staff to get back to the office or quit, as per an ET report.
