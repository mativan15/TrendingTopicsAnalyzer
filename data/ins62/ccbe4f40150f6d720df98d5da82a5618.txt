Crypto Blocks to Kick-Off a Huge Giveaway for one lucky winner

Dover, DE, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- As they prepare to release their latest project, Crypto Blocks is celebrating their recent success with a giveaway as a thanks to their user base. The giveaway will award one participant the grand prize of $50,000 worth of BLOCKS tokens. For those interested in participating, no purchase required, entry is contingent upon becoming a Crypto Blocks follower on social media.





Find the organization on platforms such as Twitter, Telegram, Instagram, and Discord where a simple click of the follow button will submit your entry towards winning their $50,000 worth of BLOCKS token grand prize.
