Carola Nilsson new Managing Director of Ålandsbanken Fondbolag Ab

Carola Nilsson has been appointed as the new Managing Director of the Bank of Åland's wholly owned subsidiary Ålandsbanken Fondbolag Ab. The company manages the Bank's mutual funds and alternative investment funds. Carola Nilsson has extensive experience and has held various leading positions in the banking and finance industry in Finland and Sweden over the past three decades.
