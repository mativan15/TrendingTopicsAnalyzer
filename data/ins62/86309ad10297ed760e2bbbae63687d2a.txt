A guided bike or kayak trip to downtown Little Rock will help you get to know the city. Explore Riverfront Park, which includes the Arkansas River Trail, a sculpture garden, and River Lights in the Rock, which has three lit bridges with free nightly light shows. The Museum of Discovery, as well as a range of culinary and entertainment alternatives, can be found in the famous River Market District. In Little Rock you will find some beautiful attractions that you just cannot miss! Places like Crystal Bridges Museum Of American Art, Big Dam Bridge, and Baum Stadium and many more.
