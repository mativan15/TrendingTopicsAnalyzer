May 31, 2022 09:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Bragar Eagel & Squire, P.C., a nationally recognized stockholder rights law firm, announces that a class action lawsuit has been filed against LMP Automotive Holdings, Inc. ('LMP' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: LMPX) in the United States District Court for the Southern District of Florida on behalf of all persons and entities who purchased or otherwise acquired LMP securities between June 29, 2021 and My 19, 2022, both dates inclusive (the 'Class Period').
