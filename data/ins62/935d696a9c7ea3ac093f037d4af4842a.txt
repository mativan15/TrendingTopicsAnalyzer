China Last Night KraneShares Asian equities had a relatively upbeat week overall as President Biden visited the region and China internet earnings season kicked off with a few surprises to the upside in names including Alibaba, Baidu, Kuaishou, NetEase, and Pinduoduo. However, Snapchat's deeply disappointing earnings release in the US led to a sentiment downturn in Asia on Tuesday. John Tuttle (Vice-Chairman and Chief Commercial Officer, at the New York Stock Exchange) joined KraneShares CEO Jonathan Krane on a panel in Davos this week, where he said he believes NYSE will see more China ADR listings in the future rather than fewer as regulators in the US and China work towards an audit deal.
