ICICI Lombard shares fall 0.23% as Nifty gains
indiatimes.com

2022-10-21 07:15:00

AgenciesMazhar Mohammad of Chartviewindia.in said technical indicators are turning mixed as the weekly MACD chart has generated a 'buy' signal, giving an edge to the bulls.Shares of ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Ltd. slipped 0.23 per cent to Rs 1150.7 in Friday's trade. It hit a high of Rs 1163.25 and low of Rs 1147.35 during the session. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 1258.04, while the 50-DMA was at Rs 1208.49. If a stock trades above 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is upward.
