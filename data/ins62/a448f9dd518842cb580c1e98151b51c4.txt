(LEAD) GM Korea targets turnaround in 2023 with focus on exports(ATTN: UPDATES throughout with details) CHANGWON, South Korea, Oct. 19 (Yonhap) -- General Motors Co. said Wednesday it is targeting to transform its South Korean operations into a "profitable and sustainable business" next year by increasing exports of next-generation vehicles. GM Korea Co., GM's South Korean unit, reported net losses for eight consecutive years through 2021 and expects losses to narrow this year. To help achieve a turnaround, GM Korea plans to produce a next-generation crossover utility vehicle (CUV) model in its Changwon plant, about 300 kilometers south of Seoul, and expand the production of the next-generation Trailblazer SUV in its Bupyeong plant, just west of Seoul, next year.
