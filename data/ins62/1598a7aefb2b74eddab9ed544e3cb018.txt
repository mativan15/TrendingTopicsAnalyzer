Caterpillar CEO doesn't see slowdown in demand despite recession fears
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-06-02 17:42:26

Construction and mining equipment maker Caterpillar Inc. CAT-N said on Wednesday that the company doesn't anticipate a slowdown in demand in its core markets, despite Wall Street fears of a looming recession next year.'The vast majority of the markets we serve are still quite strong,' said Chief Executive Jim Umpleby.
