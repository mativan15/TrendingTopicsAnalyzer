National Conference said Farooq Abdullah would continue to cooperate with the authorities. (File)Srinagar: National Conference president Farooq Abdullah on Tuesday appeared before the Enforcement Directorate (ED) for questioning in connection with a money laundering case linked to alleged financial irregularities in the Jammu and Kashmir Cricket Association.Mr Abdullah, the Lok Sabha MP from Srinagar, arrived at the ED office at Rajbagh at 11 am. Before going in, the former chief minister spoke to reporters and linked his questioning with the forthcoming assembly elections in Jammu and Kashmir.
