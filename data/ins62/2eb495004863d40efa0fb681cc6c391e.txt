Man held in city for extortion
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 04:30:00

Bhubaneswar: The Pahala police here on Sunday arrested a man for allegedly extorting money from a vegetable vendor. The accused was identified as Jitendra Samal (22).Jatindra Khatua was selling vegetables in Pahala area on Saturday when Samal approached him and asked him to pay him dada bati (extortion money). When Khatua expressed his inability to pay, the accused attacked him and snatched Rs 3,000 from his pocket. The accused also threw Khatua's vegetables on the road. 'We have arrested at least 180 people on charges of demanding dada bati from vendors and businessmen in Bhubaneswar this year so far,' a police officer said.
