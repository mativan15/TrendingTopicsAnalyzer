[Photo/Xinhua] BEIJING -- China's warehouse storage sector expanded in May, according to a survey jointly released by the China Federation of Logistics and Purchasing and CMST Development Co, Ltd. The index tracking the sector's development rose 3.7 percentage points from April to 50.2 percent, putting it back in the expansion range, the survey showed. A reading above 50 percent indicates expansion, while one below 50 percent reflects contraction.
