HP Lifts Earnings Outlook Despite Supply Chain Disruptions
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 20:16:00

By Dean Seal HP Inc. offered an upbeat earnings outlook on Tuesday even as ongoing supply chain disruptions and sanctions against Russia affect its laptop and printing business. HP said it is expecting higher adjusted annual earnings after sales climbed 3.9% in the latest quarter, with revenue from personal computers business rising on strength in its commercial segment. Laptop sales to consumers and revenue from HP's print business meanwhile dropped due to industry-wide supply chain issues that are expected to last throughout the year.
