The government has been merrily heaping blame for the chaotic scenes at British airports on the aviation industry. It's all their own fault, so the briefing goes, it should have seen the upsurge in foreign holidays coming and it was perfectly happy to pocket government aid and furlough money while laying off thousands of workers that it now cannot persuade to return.Well hang on a minute. While it's certainly true that airlines and, in particular airports, have been caught on the hop. They did underestimate, perhaps bizarrely, the hunger for Spain and Greece after two years of Wales and Cornwall.
