New Mercedes-Benz GLCThe GLC SUV is one of Mercedes-Benz's top-selling models globally, and now, the car has been updated with a new generation version. The India-bound new-gen GLC has been debuted with a newer design, updated tech, and mild-hybrid tech which is standard across the range this time around.The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLC is ever so slightly bigger in size as compared to the outgoing model. The SUV measures 4,716 mm in length, which is 60 mm longer than the predecessor, whereas the wheelbase has been increased by 15 mm to 2,888 mm.
