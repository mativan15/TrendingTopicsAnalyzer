Capri Global enters the race for twin Srei companies
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 02:15:00

Lenders to twin Srei companies have passed a resolution to include Capri Global Holding Pvt Ltd as a prospective resolution applicant, said two people aware of the development. Following the inclusion of Capri Global Holdings, there will be 15 eligible applicants for Srei Infrastructure Finance and Srei Equipment Finance, the people said.

Srei administrator Rajnish Sharma reinvited expressions of interest by the end of September, while Capri Global submitted EoI on October 14, one of the persons cited above said.
