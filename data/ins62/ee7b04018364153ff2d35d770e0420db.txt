Meet Milwaukee Business Journal's 2022 CMO, CFO of the Year winners
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 16:00:00

The Milwaukee Business Journal has chosen five Milwaukee-area executives as its 2022 CMO of the Year winners, while at the same time selecting five area finance professionals as its CFO of the Year winners.

Anne Ballentine, vice president of marketing and communications at Rogers Behavioral Health; Geraud Blanks, chief innovation officer of Milwaukee Film; and Andrew Flack, chief commercial officer for Marcus Hotels & Resorts, are among the Milwaukee Business Journal's 2022 Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) of the Year winners.
