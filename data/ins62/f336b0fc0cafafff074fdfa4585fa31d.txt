The Uttar Pradesh government has hiked the dearness allowance of state government employees by four per cent to 38 per cent ahead of Diwali. Chief minister Yogi Adityanath announced on Twitter saying, 'The Uttar Pradesh government has decided to increase the dearness allowance and dearness relief for all state government employees and pensions to 38 per cent with effect from July 1, 2022.
