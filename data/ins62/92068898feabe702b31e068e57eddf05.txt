Natural AIGenerally speaking, this is the type of AI that we want to have and that we're most familiar with. It uses rules and patterns to decide how it should act within a situation. For example, if you give it information in paragraphs rather than as single sentences, it can make decisions about what part of a sentence to focus on or which portion of a sentence to emphasize over another.This style of AI is similar to the way humans make decisions. If you were to ask someone why they did something, they would usually tell you what their best understanding was at the time.
