Pakistani drone carrying bombs shot down in Jammu and Kashmir
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-29 18:56:53

A Pakistani drone with a payload attached to it was shot down in Jammu and Kashmir's Kathua district shortly after it crossed into the Indian side from across the International Border on Sunday morning, police said, adding that magnetic bombs and grenades were recovered from the payload.'A police search party picked up the movement of the drone at the border in Talli Hariya Chak area in early morning and fired at it,' said Mukesh Singh, additional director general of police (ADGP), Jammu.'The drone was shot down,' said Singh.
