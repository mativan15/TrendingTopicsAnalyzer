Published: 03:03 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 03:03 EDT, 19 October 2022 Kevin McCloud revisits one of Grand Designs' most infamous builds in a special episode airing tonight.The Channel 4 presenter will travel to North Devon for the show, which will air at 9pm, to revisit the Lighthouse, a £7million build which was the brain child of Edward Short, who appeared on the show in 2019 with his wife Hazel. Short, 52, spent a decade building the extraordinary Chesil Cliff House, in Croyde, Devon, with the ambitious project seeing him transform his family's 1950s home into an art-deco white lighthouse.
