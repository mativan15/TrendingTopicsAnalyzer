Unilever shares rise as billionaire investor Nelson Peltz joins board
theguardian.com
sport
2022-05-31 08:35:04

Unilever's share price has risen after the consumer goods company announced that the billionaire activist investor Nelson Peltz is to become a board member.Peltz, the US founder and chief executive of Trian Fund Management, has been building his Unilever stake since January amid growing speculation that he will push the Dove soap and Marmite manufacturer to shake up its sprawling operations.His $7.4bn (£5.9bn) investment firm has previously mounted activist campaigns at Unilever's rivals, including Procter & Gamble, Heinz and Mondelēz.
