, register or subscribe to save articles for later.Billionaire Bryan Sheffield, a third generation oil and gas executive from Texas, has bet $110 million on the Northern Territory's Beetaloo Basin becoming one of the next big shale gas developments globally.Sheffield, who worked part of his early career as an investment banker in Sydney and whose father Scott Sheffield helms a $US60 billion oil and gas company, has bought stakes in ASX-listed Tamboran Resources and Empire Energy, in the past year.
