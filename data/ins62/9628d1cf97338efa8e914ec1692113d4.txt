COLDWATER, Mich., June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kurt Miller, President of Southern Michigan Bank & Trust recently announced the appointment of John R. Waldron as Jackson Market President. Waldron earned a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in Finance and minor in Accounting from Western Michigan University. He brings nearly thirty years of commercial banking and leadership experience to his new position with Southern. Since 2002, Waldron has worked at County National Bank (CNB) in Hillsdale, where he most recently served as President and CEO.
