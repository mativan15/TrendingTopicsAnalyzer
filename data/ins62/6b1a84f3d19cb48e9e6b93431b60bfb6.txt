Making mobile games is a tricky business, especially if you're a smaller developer. The big game companies take on risk, but they have the tools and staff to handle it. Mad Curve is attempting to fill this niche for developers of any size. Mad Curve is a SaaS (Software as a Service) platform that tracks app data to create helpful analytics without requiring an SDK.
