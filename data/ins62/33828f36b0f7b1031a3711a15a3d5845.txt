A DOLLAR store chain is making a major new change - and the move will affect every customer.
Dollar General, a variety store company that has more than 18,000 locations across the US, will see one huge overhaul take place in 2022.

The alteration comes as the US retailer is opening up approximately three stores a day, resulting in an additional 1,100 stores, according to Forbes.
