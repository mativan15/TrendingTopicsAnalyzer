Published: 21:19 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 21:22 EDT, 16 October 2022 Sky News Australia host Tim Gilbert and his wife Josie are selling their beautiful beachfront home in south-east Sydney.The 54-year-old, who was previously the sports presenter for Channel Nine's Today show, has listed the five-bedroom, three-bathroom pad with a $4million price guide.Bought in 2012 for $1,595,000, the property in La Perouse will go under the hammer on November 5, reports The Daily Telegraph. Sky News Australia host Tim Gilbert and his wife Josie are selling their beautiful beachfront home in south-east Sydney (street view pictured) The house has been approved for extensive renovations, including an additional bedroom, ensuite and basement.
