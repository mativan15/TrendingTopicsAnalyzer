SNP doubles spending on spin doctors to £3.7 million
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

The SNP had been accused of 'immorally' doubling the money spent on spin doctors to try to cover up successive policy failures.The cost of Scottish government communication staff last year reached a record £3.7 million, according to figures obtained by Scottish Labour. It has described the amounts as 'eye-watering'.The figures show that the annual bill has risen by £1.5 million since Nicola Sturgeon became first minister in 2014.The cost of Scottish government press officers has more than doubled since the SNP came to power in 2007.
