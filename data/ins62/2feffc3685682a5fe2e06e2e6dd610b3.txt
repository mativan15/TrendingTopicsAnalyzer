Skip to main contentMemorial Day is behind us. The official start of summer is a few weeks away. But Medford and Somerville residents may be waiting nearly another four months for the second branch of Green Line Extension to open.Slated to start carrying passengers in December, then May, then this summer, the branch and its five new stations are now scheduled to open for business in 'late summer,' said MBTA spokesperson Joe Pesaturo via e-mail. Test trains have been operating along the new branch since May 14, he said.
