Amazon today announced that Dave Clark, CEO of the company's worldwide consumer division, will step down after 23 years in the position. In a note to leadership, Amazon CEO Andy Jassy said that Clark's last day in the office will be July 1.
'[W]e still have more work in front of us to get to where we ultimately want to be in our consumer business. To that end, we're trying to be thoughtful in our plans for Dave's succession and any changes we make. I expect to be ready with an update for you over the next few weeks,' Jassy wrote in the note, which was shared on the public About Amazon blog this morning.
