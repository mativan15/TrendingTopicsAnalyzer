Published: 20:54 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 20:57 EDT, 30 May 2022 For fans it would be the ultimate fast food convenience.But in today's weight conscious times, the idea of a pizza vending machine has given health campaigners severe indigestion.The machine, planned for The D-Day Story museum's seafront car park in Southsea, Portsmouth, provides pizzas 24 hours a day in less than four minutes.Customers can choose from a selection of pizzas including the Notorious PIG, topped with pulled ham hock, cheddar, bacon and fresh pineapple.
