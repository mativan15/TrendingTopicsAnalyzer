HARRISBURG, Pa., Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Ollie's Bargain Outlet Holdings, Inc. (Nasdaq: OLLI) announced the hiring of Rob Helm to the position of Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, effective immediately. Rob will report to John Swygert, President and Chief Executive Officer, and will lead the Accounting, Financial Planning and Analysis, Financial Reporting and Compliance, and Investor Relations teams. 'We are delighted to welcome Rob to the Ollie's family. Rob has a proven track record of success, and brings significant financial leadership experience in the consumer retail sector,' said John Swygert, President and Chief Executive Officer.
