'Jeremy Hunt has stopped Trussonomics in its tracks'
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 09:49:47

To correct the missteps of the ‘mini budget', Chancellor Jeremy Hunt has today rolled back its most controversial features and killed Trussonomics in its tracks. In scrapping almost all of the previous Chancellor's low tax policies, Hunt has seriously undermined the Prime Minister's pro-growth platform.  To the ire of many free marketeers, the focal point of the government's policy is now the untargeted Energy Price Guarantee. By capping the price of energy for households, the policy amounts to the largest welfare handout in recent memory.
