Olivia Wilde speaks about getting through 'burning hellfire' of 'misogyny' in entertainment industry
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-18 21:57:26

Olivia Wilde opened up about how she keeps going through the "burning hellfire of misogyny" in the entertainment industry during an event Monday.The "Don't Worry Darling" actress delivered her speech at the Elle's Women in Hollywood event just hours after Wilde and ex Jason Sudeikis issued a statement denying a former nanny's allegations about their relationship. "Let's face it, it's not always easy to keep going," Wilde said, according to Page Six. "In fact, sometimes it's tempting to excuse ourselves from the burning hellfire of the misogyny that defines this business and say, ‘Good night, good luck, I'd rather eat glass for a living.
