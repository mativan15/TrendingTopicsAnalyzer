If you have a freezer, using it wisely could really help you save money on Christmas and allow you to spread the costs over several months.'If your freezer's small, declutter it now as best as you can so you've only got essentials in there,' says Mitch. 'You can make more room by taking food out of boxes and putting them in freezer bags labelled with the best before date and cooking instructions.'Meanwhile, Lorna Cooper, author of Feed Your Family For £20 A Week is a big fan of freezing leftovers, say, from a weekly roast, to use at a later date – including Christmas.
