October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Blue Vista Capital Management ('Blue Vista') today announced it has acquired Shelby Oaks Industrial Park, a 480,911 square foot portfolio of light industrial and flex properties located in the Northeast industrial submarket of Memphis, Tennessee. In partnership with Westmount Realty Capital, LLC, Blue Vista acquired a majority interest in the property on behalf of its 2018-vintage value-add middle market real estate fund, Blue Vista Real Estate Partners V.
