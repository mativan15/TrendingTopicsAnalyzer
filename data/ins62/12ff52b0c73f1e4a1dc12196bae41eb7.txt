Peacock's Pete Davidson comedy series Bupkis has added 6 guest stars for its premiere season: Charlie Day (It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia), Ray Romano (Get Shorty), Kenan Thompson (Saturday Night Live), Brad Garrett (Everybody Loves Raymond), Chase Sui Wonders (Bodies, Bodies, Bodies), and Simon Rex (Red Rocket) in undisclosed roles.
