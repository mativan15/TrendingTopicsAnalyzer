It is hard to get excited after looking at AvalonBay Communities' (NYSE:AVB) recent performance, when its stock has declined 13% over the past month. To decide if this trend could continue, we decided to look at its weak fundamentals as they shape the long-term market trends. In this article, we decided to focus on AvalonBay Communities' ROE.

Return on Equity or ROE is a test of how effectively a company is growing its value and managing investors' money. In short, ROE shows the profit each dollar generates with respect to its shareholder investments.
