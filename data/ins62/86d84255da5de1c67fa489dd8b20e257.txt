You Can Play Resident Evil Re:Verse Ahead Of Launch
gamespot.com
gaming
2022-10-21 19:41:13

Resident Evil Re:Verse, a multiplayer game free for owners of Resident Evil Village, will enter an early access period ahead of the game's proper launch.The early access event begins on Sunday, October 23 at 7 PM PT / 10 PM ET until October 25 at 11 PM PT, which is 2 AM ET on October 26.. The full game will launch three days later on October 28. The game supports cross-play on all platforms. Re: Verse will see support after launch, with more survivors, creatures, stages, costumes, and challenge missions to come.
