A POPULAR bakery has announced it will be opening a second shop in Christchurch.

M's Bakery, a family-run business that has proven popular in Pokesdown, is due to open a second bakery in the Christchurch town centre.

The new premises, which will be in the Saxon Square, will offer a delicious selection of freshly made artisan bread, pastries, and cakes.

The power pair behind the bakery are Marta and Istvan, and they are excited to bring the family business to Christchurch.
