With all the chaos and tragedies that the pandemic brought to the world in the last two years, it did leave a silver lining in the form of new innovations that changed our way of life. Out of all the brick and mortar retail stores that were hit hard, we have noticed the emergence of a new era of the cyber fashion industry. From suit tailoring via smartphone apps, shirt tailoring via website, to accessing artisan products from rural Italy, we have been intrigued by various new pathways of clothes shopping.
