Opposition parties including the Congress, Raijor Dal and Assam Jatiya Parishad have demanded a probe by the central agency.Bitter war of words has broken out between Assam chief minister Himanta Biswa Sarma and Delhi Deputy chief minister Manish Sisodia. Sarma has threatened to file a criminal defamation suit after Sisodia alleged corruption in the procurement of Covid Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) kits. Sisodia in Delhi has said that PPE kit contracts were awarded to a company linked to Sarma's wife,' "Himanta Biswa Sarma awarded the contract to his wife's company.
