NCC AB Series B

NCC AB operates as a construction and property development company. It operates through the following segments: NCC Building, NCC Infrastructure, NCC Industry, NCC Property Development and Other. The NCC Building segment engages in the construction and refurbishments of housing and offices as well as public and commercial premises. The NCC Infrastructure segment engages in the design, construction and production of infrastructure and road services. The NCC Industry segment offers products and services concerning roads such as stone materials and asphalt products, piling works and paving assignment.
