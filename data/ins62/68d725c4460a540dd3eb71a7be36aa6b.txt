Is the Fern Hollow Bridge open?

Not yet. But it's well on its way.

Less than 10 months after its collapse on a snowy morning drew worldwide attention and a presidential visit, the Fern Hollow Bridge replacement is in the finishing phases and will be done by the end of the year.
