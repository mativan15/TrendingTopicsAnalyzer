THE CRITICALLY-ACCLAIMED NONPROFIT TO RAISE $2.5 MILLION AND CREATE 250 TECHNOLOGISTS FROM ATLANTA, NEWARK, NEW YORK CITY AND LOS ANGELESBRONX, N.Y., Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Knowledge House (TKH) will continue its eight-year journey with its 2022-23 Fellowship Campaign. This year's theme is the "Great Restoration" to answer the call to corporations in need of Black and Brown technologists through its successful nationwide programming at its satellite locations in New York City, Newark, Atlanta, and Los Angeles.
