Elle Mundus Investing can be a fun and intellectually fulfilling endeavor, but like anything else in life, there has to be some sort of balance. For example, if one were to solely buy speculative growth stocks in a portfolio, then one may spend sleepless nights fretting over what they will do in an economic shock. That's why it pays to have what I would call "reduced stress" holdings that have proven business models and durable income streams. This brings me to Iron Mountain (NYSE:IRM), which to me reflects those aforementioned qualities, with a large number of quiet facilities that store vast amounts of documents.
