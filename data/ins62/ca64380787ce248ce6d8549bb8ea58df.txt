Netflix stock could be even more volatile around future earnings, thanks to this change
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 00:57:00

Already a volatile stock during corporate earnings season, Netflix Inc. is likely to be even more so in the future after a decision made by the streaming service's executives. Starting next quarter, executives will no longer give a forecast for new subscriber additions, the component of its financial results that is most closely watched by analysts and investors. Netflix NFLX, -1.73% executives said in their quarterly letter to shareholders Tuesday that they want investors to instead focus on revenue.
