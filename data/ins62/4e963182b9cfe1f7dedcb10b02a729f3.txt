GENASHTIM PTE. LTD. WINS INAUGURAL ESG EXCELLENCE AWARD
yahoo.com
finance
2022-05-31 12:36:00

SINGAPORE, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Genashtim Pte. Ltd., a recruitment and education firm that works to provide sustainable, meaningful and gainful employment for disadvantaged individuals, has become the inaugural recipient of the ESG Excellence Award Singapore.

L-R:Christina Yeoh, Senior Executive,Corporate Services, Genashtim Pte. Ltd.; Callum Laing, CEO, MBH Corporation plc; Wee Ming Lee, Regional Sales & Business Development Manager, Genashtim Pte. Ltd.

Organised by MBH Corporation plc (MBH), a diversified investment holding company, the ESG Excellence Awards Singapore 2022 is a competitive awards initiative intended to showcase exceptional Environmental Social Governance (ESG) efforts by small to medium-sized Singapore businesses.
