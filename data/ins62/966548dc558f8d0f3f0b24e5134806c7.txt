By HU DONGMEI in Yinchuan and YE ZIZHEN | China Daily | Updated: 2022-06-02 10:40 Zhang Jun runs with his students during a PE class at the Minning Central Primary School in the Ningxia Hui autonomous region. [Photo/China Daily] Zhang Jun vividly recalls moving from Xiji county to Minning town with his family in 1996 when he was 10. There wasn't much development back then. Water pipes and buildings were still under construction. There wasn't even a primary school. And whenever it rained or was windy in Minning, which is surrounded by the vast Gobi Desert, everything would get covered in dust.
