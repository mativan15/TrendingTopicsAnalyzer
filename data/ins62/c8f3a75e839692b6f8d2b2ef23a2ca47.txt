jxfzsy Therapeutic and psychedelic mushroom producer First Person (FP) has filed to raise approximately $8M through an initial public offering. The Canadian company said in a filing that it was considering offering 1.1M shares priced between $6.75 and $7.75 per share, which would raise around $8M if priced at the midpoint. Underwriters would be granted a 45-day option to buy up to 166K additional shares at the public price to cover any over-allotments. First Person intends to list its shares on Nasdaq under the symbol FP.
