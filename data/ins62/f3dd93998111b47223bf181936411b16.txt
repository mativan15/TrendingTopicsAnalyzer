'China is probably only halfway through the Xi Jinping years'
lemonde.fr
news
2022-10-17 07:12:04

Within the next five, even ten, years, only one face will be visible on the Chinese political horizon: Xi Jinping's. With his high cheekbones, sad smile, combed hair and heavy stature beginning to bend, the 69-year-old came to power in late 2012 and intends to stay there. Tomorrow's China will be Xi Jinping's China: under close surveillance at home, flaunting its power abroad.
