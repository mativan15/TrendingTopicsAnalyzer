July 2019, as viewed from Choon Guan Street. Oasia Hotel Downtown is now completely covered in plants, creating an almost furry look. Google Maps

Singapore's Oasia Hotel Downtown is famous for its "living facade" of greenery.

The hotel, located in the central business district (CBD), was completed in 2016.

Photos show how plants have taken over the building's facade through the years.

Thanks to its red mesh coating and green "living facade," Oasia Hotel Downtown stands out from its dull-colored neighbors in Singapore's central business district.
