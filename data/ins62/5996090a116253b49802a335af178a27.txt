Exclusive: Decks being cleared for Lumax Group to acquire IAC India biz

Lumax DK Jain Group having a turnover of about INR 3400 crore is a diversified auto parts manufacturer with two listed entities, Lumax Industries, and Lumax Auto Technologies.New Delhi: Lumax DK Jain Group (Lumax), a leading Indian auto component manufacturer, is in an advanced stage of acquiring the local business of International Automotive Components (IAC), multiple sources aware of the matter told ETAuto.'The company has been engaged in due diligence for quite some time and has signed an early document for the deal,' the first person aware of the matter said.
