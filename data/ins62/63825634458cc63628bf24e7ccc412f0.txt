Updated: 18 Oct 2022, 11:46 AM ISTThe National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) has recently launched a feature enabling the linkage of RuPay credit cards to the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). Let's see how this new feature worksCurrently, the RuPay credit card on the UPI facility is available on the BHIM app only for certain bank customers. "Customers of Punjab National Bank, Union Bank of India, and Indian Bank will be the first to be able to use RuPay Credit Card on UPI with BHIM App," according to a circular released by NPCI on September 20, 2022.
