Eurovision Song Contest Winners Release NFT For Ukraine Charity Auction
ibtimes.com.au
news
2022-05-27 09:38:55

Kalush Orchestra, the winners of the Eurovision Song Contest 2022, have organized an auction of an NFT (non-fungible tokens) for charity. The auction started Wednesday and will last till Saturday on MetaHistory, the official Ukrainian charitable NFT museum.
"The symbols of the Kalush Orchestra's victory at Eurovision will now help make Ukraine's victory in the war with the Russian occupiers," the MetaHistory website read. 
Bids will be accepted in both cryptocurrency and fiat currency (via Monobank), creating a friendly competition for what type of currency can bring the most donations.
