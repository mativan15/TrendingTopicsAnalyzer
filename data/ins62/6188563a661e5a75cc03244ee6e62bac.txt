Broadcom will let VMware shop for another buyer, but is any other software company interested?
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-27 00:03:00

Virtualization-software maker VMware Inc. agreed to be acquired by Broadcom Inc. on Thursday in an roughly $61 billion deal that will triple the chip maker's software business, and has an usual provision that lets VMware look for another buyer.The unusual go-shop provision of the deal raises the question of whether any software makers that might be a better fit with VMware would want to top Broadcom's offer. On Thursday, shares of VMware VMW, +3.17% rose 3% but still closed nearly 13% below Broadcom's AVGO, +3.
