This election season has been framed by one major prediction: Maura Healey will be the next governor of Massachusetts.If it comes true, what kind of governor will Healey be? It's hard to say, and she aims to keep it that way.If elected, Healey will break ground as the first woman and first gay person to win the governor's office in Massachusetts. But on matters of policy, she's not campaigning as any kind of groundbreaker. Even with a 26 point lead over Republican Geoff Diehl, she's ever in search of the safe middle.
