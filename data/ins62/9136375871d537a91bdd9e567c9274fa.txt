Image source, PAImage caption, The aftermath of the landslide which engulfed Pantglas Junior SchoolA collection of objects telling the story of the Aberfan disaster has gone on display ahead of the 56th anniversary of the tragedy.The collection is being held at St Fagan's National Museum of Wales in Cardiff.Survivor Gareth Jones hopes they will be looked at for many years to come."We're not going to live forever. Once we're all gone that's it, there's no more survivors, so we can't tell the story," he said.
