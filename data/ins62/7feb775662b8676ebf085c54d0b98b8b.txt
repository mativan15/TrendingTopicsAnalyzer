Rail passengers are being warned of significant disruption to services over the Bank Holiday weekend because of a strike by conductors at a train company.Members of the Rail, Maritime and Transport union at TransPennine Express will walk out on Saturday and Sunday in a long-running dispute over pay.TransPennine Express (TPE) urged people not to travel, saying it will be running an amended timetable on both days, with a very limited service available for those making essential journeys.

With strike action planned for both Saturday and Sunday, coupled with major engineering work, our advice for customers is not to travel by trainPeople heading to events including Jubilee celebrations over the weekend were urged to seek alternative transport.
