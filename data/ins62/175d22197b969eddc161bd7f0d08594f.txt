The 5 new foods you'll want try at a Timberwolves game
startribune.com
news
2022-10-18 13:58:11

There are plenty of reasons to get excited about the Timberwolves 2022-23 season, from the addition of standout center Rudy Gobert to a roster of tasty foods to fuel the fandom.

After taking two years off because of the pandemic, executive chef David Fhima and the Timberwolves leadership unveiled an impressive lineup of food items ahead of Wednesday's season opener, from highbrow carving stations and Wagyu beef sliders to cheffed-up classics like sticky-crispy wings and gravy-topped poutine. Since first partnering with the chef/owner of Fhima's Minneapolis five years ago, the concessions at Target Center have seen a deliberate upgrade.
