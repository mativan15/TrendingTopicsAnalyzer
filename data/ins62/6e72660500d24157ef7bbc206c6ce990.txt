I will never understand the knee-jerk reactions of NFL coaches. RB Jeff Wilson Jr. has been stellar in the backfield for the 49ers. He is 12th in rushing in the NFL, ahead of such luminaries as Christian McCaffrey, Joe Mixon and Jonathan Taylor. 92% of all fantasy owners started Wilson against the Falcons as he was ranked a top-ten RB last week. Yet when he fumbled in the game, Wilson was summarily benched by coaching 'genius' Kyle Shanahan and only touched the ball three more times. The 49ers went pass-heavy after that.
