Photo by Sean Hannon/iStock via Getty Images Global methane emissions, and efforts to reduce them, are attracting significant and increasing private, corporate, and political attention and funding. Satellites will provide ubiquitous high resolution surveillance and compelling images. This attention will increasingly be reflected in both investment preferences and regulatory mandates, creating a demand to detect and reduce methane emissions, and to document this performance. Many businesses will be significantly impacted, for good or ill.
