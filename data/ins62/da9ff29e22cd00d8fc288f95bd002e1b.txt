The Korea Herald to kick off forum on technology reshaping humanity
naver.com
news
2022-10-18 16:00:00

Forum explores evolving interaction between humans and technology, and future dynamics of the two



The poster for The Korea Herald Biz Forum.The Korea Herald, the nation's largest English newspaper by circulation, holds its fifth annual business forum on Wednesday to shed light on the latest technology trends that could change the way people live as well as ways to make the transition smoother and more effective.Under the theme 'Humanity in Tech,' the forum has invited policymakers, industry leaders and academics to explore the growing presence of blockchain and artificial intelligence in the digital age.
