French Open to listen to Novak Djokovic and Rafael Nadal concerns but risk sexism storm (Image: GETTY)French Open boss Amelie Mauresmo has risked accusations of sexism by claiming only one of the 10 night sessions at this year's event had a women's match because men's tennis has more 'appeal'. And she promised to review starting the night sessions earlier after Tuesday night's Rafa Nadal vs Novak Djokovic quarter-final finished at 1.16am local time. Both players complained the match started too late.
