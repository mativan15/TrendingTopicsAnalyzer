As record-high inflation swells, and food and gas prices continue to climb -- many of us are feeling the effects on our wallets. For instance, many are considering a side hustle to add extra padding to their bank accounts. But if you'd rather tackle your budget to unlock savings, consider these road-tested spending strategies that may help offset inflation's toll.
