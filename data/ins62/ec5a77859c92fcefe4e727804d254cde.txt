The board of JOYY Inc. (NASDAQ:YY) has announced that it will pay a dividend of US$0.51 per share on the 6th of July. Based on this payment, the dividend yield on the company's stock will be 5.9%, which is an attractive boost to shareholder returns.

JOYY Might Find It Hard To Continue The Dividend

If the payments aren't sustainable, a high yield for a few years won't matter that much. JOYY isn't generating any profits, and it is paying out a very high proportion of the cash it is earning.
