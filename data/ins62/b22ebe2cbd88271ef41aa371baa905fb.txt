By Reuters Published: 01:03 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 01:03 EDT, 18 October 2022 A look at the day ahead in European and global markets from Ankur Banerjee Three days into the job, Jeremy Hunt has pretty much gutted British Prime Minister Liz Truss' entire economic plan that propelled her to lead the government less than six weeks ago. The new finance minister's policy reversal has lifted investors' mood, leading to a rally across equities, bonds and some currencies. With Truss's spokesman batting down suggestions that Hunt was running the country, it remains to be seen how long the PM is able to survive the political maelstrom, even though she remains defiant.
