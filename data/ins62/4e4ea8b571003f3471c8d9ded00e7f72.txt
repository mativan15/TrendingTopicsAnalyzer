Demand for rental housing rebounds, sees 16% sequential rise, says report
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 12:00:00

The demand for rental housing has started to rebound after witnessing sluggish activity for nearly two years owing to the outbreak of Covid19 pandemic, mobility restrictions and companies offering the work-from-home option. Indian rental housing market demand, as indicated by searches, grew 15.8% sequentially and 6.7% from a year ago in the March quarter, showed a Magicbricks' report. The cumulative rental housing supply, as indicated by listings, increased 30.7% sequentially and 101.5% from a year ago across the 13 Indian cities mapped by the property portal.
