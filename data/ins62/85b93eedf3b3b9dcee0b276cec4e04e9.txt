SAN DIEGO, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The Class: Shareholder rights law firm Robbins LLP informs investors that a shareholder filed a class action on behalf of all persons and entities that purchased or otherwise acquired HUMBL, Inc. (OTC: HMBL) common stock and/or the unregistered HUMBLE TX securities between November 21, 2020 and May 19, 2022. The complaint alleges violations of the Securities Act of 1933 and Securities Exchange Act of 1934. HUMBL is a mobile financial services company that offers: (i) a mobile app that allows peers, consumers, and merchants to connect; (ii) a mobile marketplace that allows consumers and merchants to connect more seamlessly in the digital economy; and (iii) financial products and services, targeted for simplified investing on the blockchain.
