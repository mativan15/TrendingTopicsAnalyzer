Pipehawk's Subsidiary Partner Gets Funding for Modular Heat Pump Facility
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 08:19:00

By Michael Susin Pipehawk PLC said Thursday that a partner of its subsidiary QM Systems Ltd. has secured up to 2.5 million pounds ($2.8 million) in development capital to deliver a modular heat pump production facility in Worcestershire. The U.K.-listed technology company said Ventive Ltd. will have the necessary funding to complete the development of the initial product variants and, in partnership with QM, complete the manufacturing facility ready for product release in fourth quarter of 2023.
