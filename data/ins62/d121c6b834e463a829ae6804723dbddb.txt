HC asks govt to file affidavit in Dumka burn victim case
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 03:45:00

Ranchi: The Jharkhand high court on Friday directed the government to explain under what circumstances the deceased burn injury victim was brought from Dumka to Ranchi. A division bench of Chief Justice Dr Ravi Ranjan and Justice Sujit Narayan Prasad took up the matter and ordered state DGP Neeraj Sinha to explain what was the percentage of the burn injuries sustained by the victim.The bench also directed the Union government to inform what is the status of the AIIMS Hospital in Deoghar and whether it has a burn ward to treat patients with burn injuries.
