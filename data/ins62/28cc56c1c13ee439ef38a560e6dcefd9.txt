Stranger things have happened at Netflix, but its new policy to prevent password sharing is confusing users during its pilot program in Costa Rica, Chile, and Peru according to Rest of World.What is Netflix's new password sharing policy?Netflix's new password sharing policy is simple: it hits users who share passwords with an additional charge. The streaming service is defining a household as the immediate people a specific subscriber lives with. That means you can share your password with your roommates, but not your family member who lives across the country.
