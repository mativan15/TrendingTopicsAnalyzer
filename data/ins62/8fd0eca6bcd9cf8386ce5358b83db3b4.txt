Rising rents and strong warehouse demand keeps Segro growing
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

Europe's biggest warehouse landlord has said that occupier demand for its buildings remains strong, although valuations dropped off sharply over the summer.Segro signed another £20 million of leases at its warehouses between July and September. So far this year it has increased its rent roll by £76 million. For those buildings that came up for renewal or review during that period, Segro was able to increase rents by, on average, 22 per cent.David Sleath, its chief executive, said that 'long-term structural trends' were driving a need for warehouse space.
