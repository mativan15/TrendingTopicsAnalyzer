(Minerva Studio/Shutterstock)A former registered nurse in B.C.'s Interior is facing a disciplinary hearing over comments he allegedly made about COVID-19, Black Lives Matter demonstrations and racism in the province's health-care system.The allegations against Sean Taylor, from Kelowna, are laid out in a citation published Friday by the B.C. College of Nurses and Midwives.It claimed Taylor made five inflammatory comments over a series of months in 2020, during the earliest months of the pandemic and as Black Lives Matter demonstrations swept the United States after the murder of George Floyd.
