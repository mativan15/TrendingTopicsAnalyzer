June 03, 2022 08:23 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN DIEGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP announces that purchasers or acquirers of Energy Transfer LP (NYSE: ET) common shares between April 13, 2017 and December 20, 2021, inclusive (the 'Class Period') have until August 2, 2022 to seek appointment as lead plaintiff in Vega v. Energy Transfer LP, No. 22-cv-04614 (S.D.N.Y.). The Energy Transfer class action lawsuit charges Energy Transfer and certain of its former top executive officers with violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
