Willard/iStock via Getty Images The Russia-Ukraine war is still raging on, which has increased geopolitical uncertainty and been a catalyst for defense spending increases. Lockheed Martin Corporation (NYSE:LMT) is poised to benefit from these tailwinds as a supplier of best-in-class armaments. The company is a military powerhouse that produces battle-tested products for land, air, sea, and even space defense applications. Lockheed Martin announced solid financial results for the third quarter of 2022, as the company beat earnings estimates.
