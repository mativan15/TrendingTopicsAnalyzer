Key points Many Americans received rent relief funds over the past year.New data reveals that the distribution of those funds wasn't as fair as it could have been. When the COVID-19 outbreak erupted in early 2020, it led to an almost immediate uptick in unemployment numbers. Because so many people lost their jobs and didn't have money in savings to fall back on, lawmakers worried that delinquent rent payments would lead to an eviction crisis. As such, they put a ban on evictions that didn't expire until 2021.
