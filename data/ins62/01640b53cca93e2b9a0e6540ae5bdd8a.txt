Risks to small business owners during the cost of living crisis revealed
thescottishsun.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 09:36:10

THE biggest threats facing small business owners over the next year include paying back Government COVID loans, lack of consumer spending, and hefty fuel price increases.
A poll of 1,000 SME bosses found two thirds of those who have been running their business since before the pandemic admit the last two years have been the most challenging since they started their venture.
A poll has found that two thirds of of business owners have admitted the last two years have been the most challengingCredit: Getty
Nearly half of them (47%) fear the next 12 months could prove even more difficult.
