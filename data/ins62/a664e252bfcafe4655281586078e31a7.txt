Coimbatore: The Directorate of Vigilance and Anti-Corruption (DVAC) on Monday searched at the Aavin dairy plant at Perur Pachapalayam and seized Rs 8.4 lakh from the car and cupboard of senior factory assistant A Krishnamurthy. Acting on a tip-off, DVAC additional superintendent of police Dhivya and team searched at the Aavin dairy plant at Pachapalayam on Monday evening. The officials searched a car belonging to Krishnamurthy and seized Rs 5.9 lakh from it. The sleuths also checked his cupboard at his office and found Rs 2.
