Sixteenth overall driver's championship for Subaru Motorsports USA

MARQUETTE, Mich., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Subaru Motorsports USA driver Brandon Semenuk and co-driver Keaton Williams clinched their first-ever American Rally Association (ARA) driver's and co-driver's championships ahead of Hoonigan Racing Division rivals Ken Block and Alex Gelsomino with a victory at Lake Superior Performance Rally (LSPR), the final round of the 2022 ARA National Championship calendar.
