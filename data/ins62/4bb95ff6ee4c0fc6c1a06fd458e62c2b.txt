Gold discounts widen in India as prices rise, wedding demand cools
indiatimes.com
beauty
2022-06-03 12:45:00

Gold discounts widened in India this week as demand faltered due to a rise in prices and a winding up of wedding season, while consumers remained wary of purchasing bullion in top consumer China amid a gradual relaxing of coronavirus restrictions.Indian dealers offered a discount of up to $9 an ounce over official domestic prices this week - inclusive of the 10.75% import and 3% sales levies, up from the last week's $5 discount.Local gold prices were trading near their highest level in a month on Friday.
