Published: 13:45 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 13:54 EDT, 20 October 2022 Former England caps Brad Shields and Dan Robson will play for the Barbarians against Northampton Saints next month after they were made unemployed by the demise of Wasps.Shields and Robson were two of the 167 Wasps employees who immediately lost their jobs on Monday when the Coventry-based side officially entered administration. On Thursday, Josh Bassett became the first former Wasps player to move on when he signed for Harlequins until the end of the season.
