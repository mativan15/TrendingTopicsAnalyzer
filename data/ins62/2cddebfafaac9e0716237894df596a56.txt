Two labourers die while working in septic tank of Pune housing society
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 06:00:00

AgenciesImage used for representative purposeTwo labourers on Friday died of suspected asphyxiation while working in a drainage chamber-cum-septic tank in Wagholi area of Pune city, reported PTI citing the police. The incident took place at a housing society, they said, adding that one more worker is likely to have got trapped inside the tank. 
"The victims were working in an 18-feet deep drainage-cum-septic tank. It seems that they were suffocated and got stuck inside. We were informed about it around 7 am and after reaching the spot, we took the bodies of two workers out," PTI quoted an official from the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority's fire brigade department as saying.
