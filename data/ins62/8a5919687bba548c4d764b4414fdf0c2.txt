Silvio Berlusconi praises Vladimir Putin for birthday gifts that included vodka
stuff.co.nz
world
2022-10-19 06:49:15

Silvio Berlusconi, the Italian ex-premier who has a longtime friendship with Vladimir Putin, has been caught on audiotape boasting that he had recently reconnected with the Russian president and exchanged gifts of vodka, wine and 'sweet' letters over his recent birthday. Italy's LaPresse news agency published what it said were comments by Berlusconi, 86, to his centre-right Forza Italia lawmakers during a meeting this week in the lower Chamber of Deputies. 'I have reconnected with President Putin,' Berlusconi was heard saying.
