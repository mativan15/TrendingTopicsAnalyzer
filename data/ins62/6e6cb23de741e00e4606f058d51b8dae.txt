Published: 14:39 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 14:40 EDT, 21 October 2022 The Property firm Helen Skelton founded with her ex Richie Myler has been threatened with forced closure. The Strictly Come Dancing star, 39, and her recently estranged husband, 32, are facing the forced closure of their business Myler Properties LLP, after it failed to file legally required accounts.The company - which was set up in January 2020, when they were still together - has been warned of an impending 'compulsory strike off' order, which would necessitate it to be shut down.
