Alex Caruso on LeBron: 'He helped me reach new levels of my game'
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-17 10:06:42

Alex Caruso is entering his sixth NBA season and his second as a member of the Chicago Bulls.
Prior to signing with the Bulls last summer, Caruso began his career with the Los Angeles Lakers, where he won an NBA championship in 2020.
Caruso recently spoke with K.C. Johnson of NBCS Chicago about his time in L.A. and his relationship with LeBron James.
'He helped me reach new levels of my game that I maybe didn't have the confidence to reach at a certain point in my career,' Caruso said of James. 'He enabled me to get on the court because we had such good chemistry.
