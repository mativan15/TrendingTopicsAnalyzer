Judgment in breach-of-contract and underpayment of statutory royalties lawsuit bars Slacker, Inc., and parent company LiveOne, Inc., from using statutory license
WASHINGTON, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SoundExchange, the premier music tech organization powering the future of music, today commented on the United States District Court for the Central District of California's decision to enter a judgment against Slacker, Inc., and its parent company LiveOne, Inc., in a suit filed by SoundExchange to recover unpaid royalties owed to performers and rights owners.
