It's been no waltz in the ballroom for Dancing With the Stars judge Carrie Ann Inaba, as she offered an online apology to fans.

Though Dancing With the Stars season 31 is at its height, Carrie Ann took the time off-screen to share a message with fans. In a video posted to her Instagram Story, the judge appeared makeup-free in a car on her way to get ready for the dance competition show's Disney+ Night.
