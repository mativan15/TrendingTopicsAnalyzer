ESD (electrostatic discharge) bags packaging protects various ESD-sensitive products, such as electronics and electrical gadgets and equipment, against static charges in a partial or total manner.

The packaging, which is commonly constructed of polymers or metals, is typically resistant to electrostatic discharge and protects the contents from static charges in a variety of external settings.
