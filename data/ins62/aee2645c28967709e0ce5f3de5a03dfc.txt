Windvane, KuCoin's NFT Marketplace, Launches NFT Launchpad with Zzoopers as its Debut Project

2022-06-02 00:00:00

June 02, 2022 06:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time VICTORIA, Seychelles--(BUSINESS WIRE)--KuCoin, a leading global cryptocurrency trading platform, launched a brand-new NFT Launchpad on its Windvane NFT Marketplace. Zzoopers, an NFT project that enables users to collect and use vivid and adorable animal avatars as digital identities across many different metaverses, will become the first NFT project available for launchpad users. Zzoopers Genesis, an NFT collection of anthropomorphic animal characters, will have their initial sales on the Windvane NFT Launchpad on June 20, 2022.
