[Photo/Agencies] The United Kingdom's new finance minister has called on lawmakers from the ruling Conservative Party to support the nation's embattled prime minister. Chancellor of the Exchequer Jeremy Hunt urged members of Parliament to back Liz Truss, who only came to office 40 days earlier after a political coup ousted Boris Johnson.
