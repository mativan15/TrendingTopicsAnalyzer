Company denying raise to overworked staff while taking a bonus sparks fury
newsweek.com
entertainment
2022-05-27 11:00:01

The internet has slammed managers for refusing to give a hardworking employee a raise, despite them doing the work of two people, while all then time lining their own pockets with a salary bump and bonuses.Reddit user u/ubermick shared a post in the 2.3 million-strong r/ChoosingBeggars group, that contained a glowing performance review that, while acknowledging the employee's hard work, also ruled out any hope of a raise.In the post, the employee also noted their frustration that managers were given a 10 percent raise.
