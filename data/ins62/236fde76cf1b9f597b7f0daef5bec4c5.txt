SSF to look after security of religious sites & vital establishments in UP soon
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 16:20:56

PRAYAGRAJ: The Special Security Force (SSF), recently constituted by the Uttar Pradesh government, will soon look after the security of prominent pilgrimage sites, religious centres, the high court, and other important establishments in the state. At present, paramilitary jawans ensure the security of these places.Ahead of this proposed transition, policemen and PAC jawans are being recruited into the SSF, said officials. The deployment is being done in Lucknow, Prayagraj, Gorakhpur, Ayodhya, and Mathura districts.
