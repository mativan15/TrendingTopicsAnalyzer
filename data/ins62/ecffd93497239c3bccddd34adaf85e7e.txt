The long holiday weekend can be a chance to catch up on reading you've been meaning to do, but haven't quite found the time for. Books can be great educators, teaching us about the history of systemic inequality or immersing us in stories of people who have different backgrounds and experiences than ourselves. Learning, and in some cases, unlearning, is an essential first step in allyship. Allyship shouldn't end with increasing your knowledge, but it is an important place to begin.
