Richarlison facing uncertain transfer future with Everton

EVERTON face the prospect of losing hot-shot striker Richarlison this season, reports suggest.
The Brazilian ace's stunning haul of six goals in Everton's last nine games this season ensured the club's Premier League survival.
Richarlison bagged vital goals for Everton last in the seasonCredit: AFP
But Richarlison might not remain at Goodison Park to reap the benefits of his hard work in the upcoming campaign.
That's because the 25-year-old wants to swap relegation scraps for trophy battles, the Daily Mail claim.
