Ottawa mayoral candidates work to get out the vote
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-17 21:58:32

In the final week of Ottawa's municipal election campaign, there is a push to sway the undecided voter. Algonquin College held an election fair on Monday, where candidates for mayor, councillor, and school board trustee were invited to attend. 'It was an idea that we had for (this election) because we realized municipal elections should be very important to students,' said Amanda Logan, an events programmer for Algonquin's Students' Association. 'A lot of issues that municipal politics cover like transit and housing are very important to students, and we just wanted to make sure that they knew about their options.
