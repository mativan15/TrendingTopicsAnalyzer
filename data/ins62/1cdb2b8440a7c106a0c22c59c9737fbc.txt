Keir Starmer's Labour novices coached in how to govern
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

Labour is training shadow cabinet ministers on how to be in government as the party prepares for power.Sir Keir Starmer's team has arranged the training because the vast majority of the front-bench team have only known opposition. The bespoke 90-minute training sessions begin this week and are being provided by the Institute for Government think tank, The Times has learnt.They comprise three sections: one on the role of cabinet ministers, another on the transition into government and a third on scrutiny and spending.
