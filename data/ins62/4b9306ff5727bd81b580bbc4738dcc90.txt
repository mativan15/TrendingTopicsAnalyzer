Aggregate investments made in Denver have reached $335 million
COMMERCE CITY, Colo., Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Brennan Investment Group ("Brennan"), a private real estate investment firm that acquires, develops, and operates industrial facilities throughout the United States, has acquired a 80,345 square foot manufacturing and industrial building, located at 6195 Clermont Street in Commerce City, Colorado.
The seller, McCallin Real Estate LLC, was an owner-user who will vacate the property in 2023.
