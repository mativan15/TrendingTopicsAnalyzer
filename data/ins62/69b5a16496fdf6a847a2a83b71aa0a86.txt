BRIGHTON, England, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Brighton and Hove Albion failed to score for the third Premier League game in succession as they were held to a 0-0 draw at home by struggling Nottingham Forest on Tuesday.Roberto De Zerbi's side dominated throughout but lacked a cutting edge as Forest hung on for a gritty point.
