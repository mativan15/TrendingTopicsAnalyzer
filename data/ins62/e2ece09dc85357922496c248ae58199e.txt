Matt and AlyMay 27, 2022The MI Department of Transportation aiming to make travel a little easier and a little quicker this Memorial Day weekend. MDOT announced they'll remove most traffic restrictions starting Friday, May 27 at 3pm. Restrictions will be reinstated Tuesday, May 31 at 6am. The state warns some equipment and traffic arrangements will still be present. AAA Michigan estimates more than 1.1M Michiganders will be traveling this weekend 50 miles or more from home - that's a 7.9% increase from last year.
