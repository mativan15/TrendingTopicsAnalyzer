Good morning, Cincinnati! Another Wednesday means another edition of the Courier 's "Above the Fold" podcast is out. But first, here are five of the most important local news stories from the past day.

Covington apartment sale sets record

The developers of a mixed-use apartment building in Covington's Mainstrasse have sold the structure to a Florida-based real estate investment firm for $62 million. While it's not the highest-dollar sale, it does set a record for price per-unit at just under $350,000.
