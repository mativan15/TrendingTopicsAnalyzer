LYNNWOOD, Wash. (AP) _ Zumiez Inc. (ZUMZ) on Thursday reported a loss of $397,000 in its fiscal first quarter.On a per-share basis, the Lynnwood, Washington-based company said it had a loss of 2 cents. The results fell short of Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of four analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 7 cents per share. The clothing retailer posted revenue of $220.7 million in the period, topping Street forecasts. Four analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $217.
