June 01, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time RESEARCH TRIANGLE PARK, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--WellAir, a leading indoor air disinfection and purification solutions provider, today announced that JJ Littrell has been appointed as the Company's Chief Financial Officer (CFO), effective immediately. Ms. Littrell has been working closely with the chief executive officer, Kari Dixon, as vice president of accounting, driving rigor and lean management across the Company.



"Our Company's continued financial strength is essential to WellAir's ability to deliver on our purpose, creating solutions that make the indoor world cleaner and safer," said Kari Dixon, CEO.
