MELBOURNE, Australia, October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Film and game industry trainer CG Spectrum welcomes award-winning leader Anne Bosman as Chief Executive Officer.

Bosman brings 20 years of experience managing small and high-growth startups and new ventures, turning nascent ideas into fully operational multi-million dollar organizations. Prior to joining CG Spectrum, she held leadership positions as the GM of Career Fellowships at startup On Deck, GM of General Assembly's US campus business, and Product Manager of executive education courses at HBX, Harvard Business School's online education initiative.
