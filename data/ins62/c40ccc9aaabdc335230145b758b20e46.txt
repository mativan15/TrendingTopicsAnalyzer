The accountant who drops off Deliveroo orders on his ride home. The teacher who sidelines as a nail artist. The art curator walking dogs in her lunchbreak. These are just some of the thousands of Londoners who've taken up a side hustle to earn some extra cash amid the cost-of-living crisis. According to a recent study, more than 10 million Brits are considering taking a second or even a third job to make ends meet, with Google searches for 'side hustle' rocketing by 58 per cent over the last year.
