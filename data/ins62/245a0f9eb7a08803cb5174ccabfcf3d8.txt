UK asset managers warn of tough times as investors pull or divert cash
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-20 11:16:06

Asset managers have warned they are navigating tough investment conditions in the UK, as economic turmoil reduced the value of their portfolios and persuaded customers to pull and divert their cash.London-based firms including Jupiter, Schroders, and St James's Place all issued trading updates on Thursday, which laid bare the challenges facing fund managers amid surging inflation and continued global uncertainty, including the effects of the war in Ukraine.'A worsening macroencomic backdrop, continued geopolitical challenges and inflationary concerns, particularly in the UK, again weighed upon investor sentiment in the third quarter,' Jupiter Fund Management said in its release.
