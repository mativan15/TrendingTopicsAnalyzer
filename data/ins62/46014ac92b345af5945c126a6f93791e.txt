Diwali gift: Punjab government decides to restore old pension scheme for employees
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 11:00:00

(With inputs from PTI)

CHANDIGARH: Giving a Diwali gift to its employees, the Punjab government on Friday took an in-principle decision to restore the old pension scheme, chief minister Bhagwant Mann announced after a meeting of the state cabinet in Chandigarh."We have taken in-principle decision to this effect in the cabinet meeting. This will benefit lakhs of employees. We are bringing Punjab under the old pension scheme," he said.Finance minister Harpal Singh Cheema said that employees will be given the option to join the old pension scheme.
