SAN FRANCISCO, May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- It has been a big year for Products That Count. Following recognition for the Product Talk podcast by the Webby and Shorty Awards, accolades now belong to Founding CEO SC Moatti. Product analytics behemoth Amplitude today announced that Moatti has been named as a Most Admired Product Leader on the Product 50, the definitive list of the top global product leaders.
Product 50 recognizes the leaders who are developing boundary-breaking, business-making products.
