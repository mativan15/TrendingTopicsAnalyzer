 Three COVID-19 outbreaks have been declared at Guelph General Hospital.
Tthe first occurred on Friday in the 4 West unit where two patients were said to have hospital-acquired COVID.Then on Tuesday, four more patients contracted COVID-19, two each in the 7 East and 6 West units.The hospital said in a news release that all three units are closed to visitors except on compassionate grounds.
