Australians losing superannuation savings to sophisticated new scam
abc.net.au
news
2022-10-18 20:04:02

A sophisticated new scam has been uncovered that targets the retirement nest eggs of Australians.At least 45 self-managed super funds have been made for potential victimsInvestigator Ken Gamble believes there is evidence to link the Invest Quick scam to the Metro Manila call centreIt is understood the Invest Quick scam ran from at least late 2020 until April last yearThe ABC's 7.30 program can reveal the scam used the names Invest Fast or Invest Quick and pretended to be a financial advice firm based in Melbourne.
