Taking It Too Far? AI Mimics Your Dead Loved Ones To Help You Move On
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 07:00:45

Would you talk to virtual forms of your deceased loves ones? Powered by artificial intelligence, "grief tech" is now here. According to MIT Technology Review, this technology works by training an AI on images, recordings, and footage of people who may have died recently.Based on this information, the AI creates a virtual form of the person, allowing those who are grieving to interact with their deceased loved ones. The purpose of these AIs? To help people who're dealing with loss to come to terms with it and to give them the option to preserve memories of their loved ones.
