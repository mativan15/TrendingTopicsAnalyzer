LYNCHBURG, Va., Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Bank of the James Financial Group, Inc. (the 'Company') (NASDAQ:BOTJ), the parent company of Bank of the James (the 'Bank'), a full-service commercial and retail bank, and Pettyjohn, Wood & White, Inc. ('PWW'), an SEC-registered investment advisor, today announced unaudited results of operations for the three month and nine month periods ended September 30, 2022. The Bank serves Region 2000 (the greater Lynchburg MSA), and the Blacksburg, Charlottesville, Harrisonburg, Lexington, Roanoke, and Wytheville, Virginia markets.
