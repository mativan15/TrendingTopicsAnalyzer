Published on Jun 04, 2022 07:49 PM IST One week after a 17-year-old girl was gangraped in a car by five people, Telangana police has arrested 2 juveniles and another accused in connection to the case. The girl was raped while she was returning home in Hyderabad's Jubilee Hills area after a party in a pub. Police have confirmed that one of the accused has VIP links. West Zone DCP Joel Davis said the survivor could not reveal anything about the culprits, barring one name. 'Special teams were immediately formed to nab them.
