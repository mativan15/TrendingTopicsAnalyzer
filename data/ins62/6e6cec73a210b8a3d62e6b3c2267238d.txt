Government officials on hand for sneak peak of Project Arrow
ctvnews.ca
politics
2022-10-19 15:04:00

It's a zero-emission electric vehicle that is being designed, engineered and built by Canadian companies. The Automotive Parts Manufacturers Association (APMA) launched this idea in October 2020, and revealed two small portions of the concept car Wednesday during the APMA's 70th conference held at Caesars Windsor. 'This is really starting to feel real,' Ontario's Minister of Economic Development Vic Fedeli said as he lifted a small portion of the cover off the vehicle. 'I don't know who gets to do the next unveiling of a little more, but I got to show the ankle today,' Fedeli quipped.
