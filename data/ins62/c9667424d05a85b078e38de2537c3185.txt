Liz Truss' resignation gives Mikel Arteta the chance to ensure that Arsenal boast a 100 per cent record during her reign as UK Prime Minister, as they beat PSV Eindhoven hours after her announcementMikel Arteta is on the cusp of making history after Arsenal maintained their perfect record since Liz Truss became UK Prime Minister. The last time that the Gunners didn't win a match was a 3-1 loss Manchester United on September 4, two days before Truss moved into No.10 Downing Street. But not even two months later, the Conservative leader has resigned following a crisis-ridden 44 days as PM.
