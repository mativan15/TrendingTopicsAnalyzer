AgenciesThe BJP government of Yogi Adityanath earlier proposed Rs 75.50 crore for a women empowerment scheme.In a move to ensure the safety of working women, the Yogi Adityanath government has issued new rules. The circular now states that women cannot be forced to work between the hours of 7 pm and 6 am. The BJP government of Yogi Adityanath earlier proposed Rs 75.50 crore for a women empowerment scheme. The state administration has recommended establishing cyber support desks for women at the district level.
