Rents, retirees and rail strikes

Phew, what a week! From stalling railroad union negotiations to record inflation data, the American economy has taken a beating. Our three indicators of the week address three key causes and effects of this week's news. How have skyrocketing rents contributed to an overheating economy? Why is January 1 such an important day for Social Security recipients? And while the White House might have pulled a rabbit out of a hat to prevent a massive rail strike last month, was it enough?
