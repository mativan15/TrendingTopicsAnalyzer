Strengthen Lokayukta, Hegde urges state govt
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 05:30:00

Mysuru: Former Lokayukta and retired judge of the Supreme Court , Justice Santosh Hegde on Monday appealed to the state government to make all efforts to strengthen the anti-graft institution that he previously headed. 'The state government must equip the Lokayukta with the necessary infrastructure, empower police associated with the institution, and depute personnel who can help curb corruption in Karnataka,' Justice Hegde said.Hegde, in Mysuru on Monday, welcomed the order issued by the division bench of the high court, which revoked the powers of the Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB), and called for the revival of the police wing of the Karnataka Lokayukta.
