Government-run dialysis units across Karnataka did not function on Thursday as staff of the units have launched an indefinite strike.
| Photo Credit: Special arrangement
Over 4,000 renal patients undergoing dialysis in 122 government-run units across Karnataka have been hit following a protest by employees of these units on Thursday.Demanding restoration of the salaries paid before the transfer of the dialysis scheme from BRS Health and Research Institute to Kolkata-based ESKAG Sanjeevini Pvt. Ltd.
