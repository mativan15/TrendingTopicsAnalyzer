The AfroTech Conference schedule has been released. And, needless to say, it proves that some things are worth the wait.

After nearly two years of virtual conferences, thanks to the global COVID-19 pandemic, the Blavity family is pleased to announce the return of an in-person gathering for the preeminent Black in tech conference in the United States.

The AfroTech Conference schedule features fireside chats with thought leaders and intimate performances with some of the industry's hottest artists.
