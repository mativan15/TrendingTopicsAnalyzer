Suzlon Energy rises 6% on bagging new project from Adani Green Energy
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 09:45:00

Shares of renewable energy solution provider Suzlon Energy rallied nearly 6 per cent to Rs 8 in Tuesday's intraday trade after the company secured an order for 48.3 MW wind power project from Adani Green Energy Suzlon will install 23 units of wind turbine generators (WTGs) and a Hybrid Lattice Tubular (HLT) tower with a rated capacity of 2.1 MW each, Suzlon said in a BSE filing.The project is located in Kutch, Gujarat and is expected to be commissioned in 2023. This order is a repeat order from Adani Green Energy and is in addition to the existing order of 226.
