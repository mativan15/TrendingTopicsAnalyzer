Kiwi axeman wins Timbersports World Trophy in Austria
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-29 02:30:47

Jack Jordan has cut the competition to pieces in his first international Timbersports World Trophy outing, claiming the world title in Austria. Jordan, a former lock for the Taranaki Bulls now farming near Taumarunui, defeated America's Jason Lentz in the final, with Australian axe-legend and three-time World Trophy champion Brad De Losa finishing third. Also competing in the Timbersports Rookie World Championship over the weekend was Tokoroa's Cleveland Cherry, who came fourth. Jordan set the fastest time of the competition (58.
