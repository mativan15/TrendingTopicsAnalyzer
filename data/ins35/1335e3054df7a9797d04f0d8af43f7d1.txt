October 18, 2022 03:07 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Mitsubishi HC Capital Inc. (Representative Director, President & CEO: Takahiro Yanai, 'Mitsubishi HC Capital'), today announces that its Executive Committee has approved the merger of two group companies, CAI International, Inc. (President & CEO: Timothy Page, 'CAI') and Beacon Intermodal Leasing, LLC (President & CEO: Katherine McCabe, 'Beacon'). The merger is planned to become effective January 1, 2023. The combined company, CAI, will be headquartered in San Francisco, California and led by CEO, Timothy Page, and President, Katherine McCabe.
