TORONTO, June 2, 2022 /CNW/ - Auxly Cannabis Group Inc. (TSX: XLY) ("Auxly" or the "Company"), a leading consumer packaged goods company in the cannabis products market, is thrilled to announce that the Company was named Licensed Producer of the Year at last night's O'Cannabiz Industry Awards Gala. This award recognizes top service, top quality products and LPs that make a true contribution to the future as voted by the cannabis industry itself.
