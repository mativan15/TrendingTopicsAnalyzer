Love Island star Shaughna Phillips has revealed she is pregnant with her first child.
The 28-year-old shared the adorable news in a sweet video on social media, which showed updates sharing the announcement with family and friends as well as the pregnancy test.
The star rose to fame on the winter series of Love Island in 2020, and has kept her boyfriend away from the public eye in recent months.
