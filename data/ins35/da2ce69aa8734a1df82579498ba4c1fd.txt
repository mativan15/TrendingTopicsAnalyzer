MIAMI, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- PennantPark Investment Advisers, LLC announced today the addition of Richard T. Allorto, Jr. as a senior executive on its Finance and Operations team. Mr. Allorto reports into PennantPark group Chief Financial Officer, Richard Cheung. Mr. Allorto will be responsible for financial oversight of and will serve as Chief Financial Officer of PennantPark's two publicly-traded business development companies, PennantPark Floating Rate Capital Ltd. (NYSE: PFLT) (TASE: PFLT) and PennantPark Investment Corporation (NYSE: PNNT).
