Cybercrime fighters raise €10m to out-think thieves
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:01:00

A cybersecurity provider that aims to identify human behaviours that it claims are the source of 91 per cent of company data breaches has raised €10 million.AlbionVC, a specialist in early stage companies, has backed the latest investments in OutThink, which uses machine learning, natural language processing and applied psychology to identify, understand and manage the attitudes, intentions and sentiments of individuals.The London-based OutThink was founded by a team of security officers frustrated at how cybercrime awareness was being taught to employees.
