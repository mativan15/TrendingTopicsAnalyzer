JAKARTA, Indonesia, June 04, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Everscale, the most scalable blockchain network in existence, was a Gold Sponsor for The Capital 2022, an important global online event for the whole blockchain industry. Members of the network governance took an active part in the opening session on day one of the conference and joined the panel discussions on decentralized exchanges and the adoption of cryptocurrencies. The Capital is a global conference organized by CoinMarketCap, the world's most-referenced price-tracking website for cryptoassets.
