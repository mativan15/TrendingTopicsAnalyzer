'Whole Foods Market Research' Secret Shopper Email Is a Scam
snopes.com
news
2022-10-21 19:06:17

A message claims the recipient has been chosen to work for Whole Foods as a 'market research representative' or 'secret shopper' and includes instructions about cashing a check. In October 2022, we received reader mail about a 'Whole Foods Market Research' scam. The scam arrives in the form of an email, text, or mailed letter, and claims that the recipient has been chosen to be a secret shopper for Whole Foods Market. The goal of the scammer is to get the recipient to deposit a check into the recipient's bank account.
