Britain cannot afford chaos of Conservatives any more, says Starmer
standard.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 11:50:48

Labour leader Sir Keir Starmer said the Conservative Party needs to keep the future of the British people out of its 'pathetic squabbles'.He said the Tories have wrecked national and household finances, adding: 'Britain deserves better, Britain cannot afford the chaos of the Conservatives any more; we need a general election now.'He told the TUC in Brighton: 'Never again can Britain take seriously their claim to be a party of aspiration or sound money."
