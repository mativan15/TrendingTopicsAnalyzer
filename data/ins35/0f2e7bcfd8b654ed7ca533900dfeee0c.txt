HONG KONG, May 30, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Yahoo reinforced its commitment to innovation, fueled by immersive adtech and performance driven solutions at its 2022 APAC Growth Summit. Following last year's successful Yahoo APAC Growth Summit live stream, the two-day virtual event was held on May 26 and 27, 2022. With a theme around "Inspire to Win", the showcase brought together 24 industry leaders from around the world to fuel the audience's digital strategies for 2022 and beyond.
