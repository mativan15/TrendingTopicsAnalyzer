21 October 2022 by Steve Pond 
The Wrap In August, Brandi Carlile had a viral moment when footage was released from Joni Mitchell's magical performance at the Newport Folk Festival, which Carlile had organized. As Mitchell sang with an age-defying grace and power, Carlile sat a few feet away, playing guitar and beaming in a mixture of pride and awe.Two months later, you can see a variation on that same look in 'The Return of Tanya Tucker – Featuring Brandi Carlile,' a documentary that shows how Carlile has helped bring back more of her idols than just Mitchell.
