Exclusive: 20 years ago, Karen Betts tattooed her friend's areolas after she underwent a mastectomy to help her regain confidence - now she trains others in permanent make-up to help them empower womenKaren is a specialist in permanent make-up and was one of the pioneers of areola treatments in the UK When Karen Betts found out that her close friend had breast cancer, she knew that she wanted to do everything in her power to help restore her confidence. Like thousands of other women in the UK each year, Karen's friend had a mastectomy following her diagnosis.
