AP A $2 billion investment by a Saudi Arabian wealth fund in Jared Kushner's private equity firm is the subject of an investigation by the House Committee on Oversight and Reform, according to a letter the committee sent to former President Trump's son-in-law and White House adviser. The New York Times reported the investigation on Thursday.
