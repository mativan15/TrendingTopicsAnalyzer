In the lead-up to the release of 'Midnights,' Taylor Swift herself said this 10th record of hers was inspired by haunting late-night thoughts and, believe it or not, a bit of 'self-loathing.' Her first single, 'Anti-Hero,' in particular, she said delves 'far into my insecurities' — and on 'Midnights' release day, we get to see what that means.While the lyrics certainly show a Swift who's hard on herself — 'It's me, hi, I'm the problem, it's me,' she declares — the music video too reflects how a person is often their own toughest critic.
