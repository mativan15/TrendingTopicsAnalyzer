Phillies set NLCS roster, reliever David Robertson is active originally appeared on NBC Sports Philadelphia

SAN DIEGO -- The Phillies have set their 26-man roster for the National League Championship Series.

Reliever David Robertson is on the roster.

He replaces Nick Nelson.

Robertson got the win in the Phillies' victory over the St. Louis Cardinals in Game 1 of the NL wild-card series on October 7.
