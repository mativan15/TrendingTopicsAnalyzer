Let's embrace the chaos and make 23 predictions for the 2022-23 Rockets, starting with a flurry of thoughts on Green and his ascending stardom.

23. Jalen Green will lead all second-year players in scoring

Green finished just a tick below No. 1 pick Cade Cunningham for the rookie scoring title last season, and there's reason to believe he'll hold a healthy lead over his fellow 2021 draftees in scoring this season. Cunningham projects to have a heavy playmaking load this season, and
Green should get off to a hot scoring start compared to his tepid start to last season.
