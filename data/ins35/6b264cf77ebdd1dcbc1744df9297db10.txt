Truss' government dealt another blow after UK home secretary quits

2022-10-19 17:31:21

London: British Prime Minister Liz Truss's floundering leadership has been plunged into a fresh crisis after her home secretary - one of the most senior members of the government - was forced to quit after she admitted a security breach.

Suella Braverman insisted she was resigning after 'a technical breach of the rules' but a statement explaining her actions contained a broadside against Truss and hinted at the bitterness behind her departure.

Suella Braverman hsa resigned as Britain's Home Secretary.
