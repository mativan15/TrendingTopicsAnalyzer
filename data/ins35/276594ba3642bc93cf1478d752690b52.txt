Ryder Raises Earnings Guidance for Second Quarter, Full Year
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 12:41:00

By Michael Dabaie Ryder System Inc. boosted its guidance for the full year for the second time since establishing its outlook in February and increased its second-quarter expectations, citing better-than-expected performance across its businesses. The supply-chain, transportation, and fleet-management-solutions company increased its second-quarter earnings per share forecast to a range of $4.37 to $4.62, from $3.97 to $4.22. Guidance for quarterly non-GAAP comparable EPS was boosted to $3.90 to $4.
