By Chen Weihua | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-21 06:55 Share - WeChat European Union flag flutters outside the EU Commission headquarters in Brussels, Belgium, October 19, 2022. [Photo/Agencies] European Union foreign policy chief Josep Borrell reiterated on Monday the EU's multifaceted approach toward China as a cooperation partner, an economic competitor and a systemic rival, but also stressed that the competition between the two sides is getting tougher. Without naming China, he said the EU should avoid turning dependency on any economy into vulnerability like what the EU has allowed its dependency on Russian for gas to become.
