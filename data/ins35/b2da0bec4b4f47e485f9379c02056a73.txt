Silk Road Medical Shares Fall 5%, Company Commences Public Offering
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 22:21:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Silk Road Medical, Inc. were trading lower in Monday's after-hours market following news the company commenced a proposed underwritten public offering of $100 million of its common shares. At 5:12 p.m. EDT, the company's stock had fallen 5.7% to trade at $40.05 per share. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 4.76% gain at $42.47 per share. The medical device company said it is "focused on reducing the risk of stroke and its devastating impact." The company has a market cap of $1.
