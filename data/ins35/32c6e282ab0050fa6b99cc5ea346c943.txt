The Biden administration is leaning toward forgiving $10,000 in student loans per borrower, according to reports. Students of color typically rack up hundreds of thousands of dollars in student loan debt, meaning they are unable to focus on other financial goals like buying a home, paying off credit card debt, and saving for retirement.
