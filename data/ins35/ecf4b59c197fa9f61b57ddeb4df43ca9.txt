Two days after completing a 4,000-kilometre run from Perth to Sydney, an exhausted Nedd Brockmann says he could run further if he had to.Nedd Brockmann has completed a run from Perth to SydneyThe tradie had to battle a host of physical ailments along the wayHis efforts have raised funds for a charity that helps the homelessThe 23-year-old is recovering after running an average of 100 kilometres a day for 47 days, battling sore knees, severe blisters and tenosynovitis — an inflammation of the tendon — in his shin.
