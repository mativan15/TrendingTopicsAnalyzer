QYOU Media India to acquire majority stake in Maxamtech

(Photo:Unsplash)QYOU Media India has signed a binding termsheet to acquire a majority ownership stake in Pune-based Maxamtech Digital Ventures, a six-year-old mobile gaming venture. Maxamtech, which has its proprietary platform Gaming 360, creates technology and games for the mobile gaming industry. It offers a variety of gaming destinations for companies including Vodafone and Glance.

The acquisition is in line with QYOU Media's strategy to to ramp up its portfolio of direct-to-consumer products in India.
