19 October 2022 by Sharon Knolle 
The Wrap With executives at NBC Universal actively looking to cede the 10 p.m. programming block to affiliates, Hearst TV president Jordan Wertlieb expects ABC and CBS to follow.At a panel of local station heads at Nab Show New York on Wednesday, Wertlieb was asked if the other broadcast networks will also can their in-house 10 p.m. programming if NBCU takes the plunge. 'I expect they will and I hope they will,' he said, according to Deadline. Hearst Television owns and operates 33 local television stations across the country.
