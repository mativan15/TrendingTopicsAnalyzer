Variety - TV News Each weekday on MSNBC, Nicolle Wallace examines the biggest issues in politics. But on Peacock, she's about to get a little personal.In a four-part miniseries now available on the NBCUniversal streaming hub, the one-time White House Communications Director and host of MSNBC's 'Deadline: White House' is launching the first entry in 'Deadline: Special Report,' a series of deep dives into issues she might not typically cover during her late-afternoon cable shift.
