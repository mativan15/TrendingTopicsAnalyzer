The Mile-High City has not been a kind place to the Winnipeg Jets in recent memory. Over their last nine visits to Denver, Winnipeg has won just once and produced a grand total of five of 18 eligible points.So as the Jets prepare for game two of their three-game road trip tonight in a meeting against the Colorado Avalanche, the prospects of success appear to be as thin as the rarified air in the Rocky Mountain region.
