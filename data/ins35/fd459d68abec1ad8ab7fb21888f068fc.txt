Investment banking veteran's experience in financial operations, corporate strategy, and capital market access to serve well in company's next growth phase

SAN DIEGO, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--GoSecure, a leading provider of managed detection and response and expert advisory services, announced the appointment of David DeRuff as Chief Financial Officer. DeRuff brings with him an extensive background of investment banking experience focused on growth companies in industries where technology plays a transformative role.
