porcorex/iStock via Getty Images Connexa Sports Technology (CNXA) (OTCQB:SLBG), formerly known as Slinger Bag, has disclosed more terms for its proposed uplisting to Nasdaq and $12.5M initial public offering. The sports technology and media company said it plans to offer 1.25M shares at the assumed priced of $10 per share, which is subject to change. Underwriters would be granted a 45-day option to buy 15% more additional shares at the IPO price. Connexa has filed to list its shares on Nasdaq under the symbol CNXA.
