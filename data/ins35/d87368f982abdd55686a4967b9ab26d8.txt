Insomnia in Aging Adults: Why It Happens and How to Beat It
cnet.com
news
2022-10-19 23:00:02

Aging typically comes with more nights of poor rest. The National Library of Medicine reports that about 50% of adults aged 60 and older struggle with poor sleep. Our bodies are constantly changing as years pass, and our sleep tends to be an unfortunate casualty of ripened age. The natural processes that occur as you get older combined with common external factors is a recipe for worsened sleep quality, and it's important to understand what's happening so you can get ahead, and try to put poor sleep to rest.
