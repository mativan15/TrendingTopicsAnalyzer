Blue Apron to Offer Meal Kits on Walmart.com, Shares Rise 12% After Hours
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 22:10:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Blue Apron Holdings Inc. were trading higher after hours Wednesday, following news that it will be offering its meal kits on Walmart.com. The company said, beginning Wednesday, customers can buy meal kits without a subscription on Walmart's website. At 6:04 p.m. ET, shares of Blue Apron were trading 12.05% higher at $3.44 per share. Volume at the time topped 709,000 shares. The stock ended the day's regular session with a 6.97% loss, closing at $3.07 per share. Write to Stephen Nakrosis at stephen.
