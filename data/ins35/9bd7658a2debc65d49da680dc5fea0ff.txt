Gujarat government drafts new impact fee ordinance for cities, towns
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 05:00:00

GANDHINAGAR: Ahead of the state assembly election this year, the state urban development department has finalized the draft for an ordinance to regularize illegal buildings across major municipal corporations and nagarpalikas. The law entails regularizing these constructions without building use permissions by charging an "impact fee".This will be the third instance since the early 2000s that the state government will bring out a law to regularize illegal construction.A highly placed source in the state government told TOI, "After many delays, the state government has now approved a draft for the ordinance, which will be made public in a day or two.
