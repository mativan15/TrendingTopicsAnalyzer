(Image via Sisk Brothers Funeral Home)WALLINGFORD, CT - (From Sisk Brothers Funeral Home) Dennie Edward Conway, 88, of Wallingford, died peacefully on May 26, 2022 surrounded by his beloved wife of 58 years, Kathleen, and his children by his side. Besides his wife Kathleen, Dennie is survived by his children and their spouses Moira and Patrick McCullough, Amy Conway-Hatcher and Sid Hatcher, Megan and Joseph Romano, and Chris Conway and Stacie Jeong; and his ten beloved grandchildren Mark, Megan, and James McCullough; Olivia and Jack Hatcher; Will, Nina, and Francesca Romano; Samuel and Maxwell Conway.
