Digital gaming company Snail to offer 5 million shares in IPO priced at $5 to $7 each
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 11:47:26

Snail Inc., a digital entertainment company that offers gaming on a variety of platforms, set terms for its initial public offering on Monday with plans to offer 5 million shares priced at $5 to $7 each. The company would raise $35 million at the top of that range at a valuation of $350 million, based on the 50 million shares to be outstanding once the deal is completed. The company has applied to list on Nasdaq under the ticker 'SNAL.' US Tiger Securities and EF Hutton are underwriters on the deal.
