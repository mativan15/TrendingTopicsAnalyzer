October 19, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time ORLANDO, Fla. & BOCA RATON, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Abacus Settlements, LLC (d/b/a Abacus Life) and Longevity Market Assets, LLC (together 'Abacus' or the 'Company'), a leading buyer of life insurance policies and vertically integrated alternative asset manager specializing in specialty insurance products, today announced that Abacus CEO Jay Jackson will be a keynote speaker at the Fasano Longevity Conference sharing his insights on The State of the Life Settlement Market.
