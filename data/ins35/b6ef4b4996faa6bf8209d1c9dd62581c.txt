Two Scarlet Knights make Top Drawer Midseason Top 100 List
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-21 05:53:23

Earlier this week, Top Drawer Soccer released its mid-season top 100 rankings. The list highlights the top college soccer players in the country. Two Rutgers players made the list in Matthew Myers, ranked 24th, and Jackson Temple, ranked 66th. As the Scarlet Knights have gotten off to a 6-3-5 start, Myers and Temple have been key to their success.
Through 14 games, Myers leads the Scarlet Knights with 28 points and 12 goals. The senior has been Rutgers' primary attacking threat as he has landed 30 shots on net.
