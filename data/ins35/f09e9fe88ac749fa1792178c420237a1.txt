Thomas Barwick/DigitalVision via Getty Images While it would be great to see investments pay off in a very short period of time, the sad truth is that investing often requires a great deal of waiting. This is especially true during these uncertain times. Because although a company might continue to boast strong performance, negative investor sentiment could push shares down further. But for those who are bullish about companies like that, this could represent a good buying opportunity. One great example of this playing out so far has been Caleres (NYSE:CAL), a specialty retailer in the footwear market.
