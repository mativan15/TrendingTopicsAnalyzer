By Reuters Published: 09:57 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 09:57 EDT, 27 May 2022 May 27 (Reuters) - * Russia's Expobank is in negotiations to buy Citibank's Russia retail banking business, Frank Media, a Russian banking focused publication, reported on Friday, citing three industry sources; * Representatives of both Expobank and Citibank declined to comment to Frank Media; * Citigroup Inc was in "active dialog" to carry out the sale of its Russia consumer business that it announced a year ago, Chief Executive Jane Fraser said in early May.
