June 02, 2022 08:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Logility, Inc., a leader in supply chain innovation powering the sustainable and resilient enterprise, today was announced as a winner of Inbound Logistics' 2022 Top 100 Logistics IT Providers award. This marks the 25th consecutive year Logility has received this recognition, which honors IT companies that support and enable logistics excellence.



'Implementing the best logistics technology helps large companies expand markets and drive undiscovered efficiencies.
