Ambassador Speaks at Innovation Roundtable
usembassy.gov
news
2022-06-01 16:33:02

Ambassador Reinke joined the Ministers of Economic Development and Tourism, Finance, and Science and Technological Development in an innovation roundtable hosted on June 1, 2022 by Digital Den.  In her remarks, she underscored that the United States is committed to helping to create a broadly prosperous and economically resilient Montenegro, which includes fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.
          
To that end, the Embassy is proud to support the Montenegro Tech and Startup Bridge which is creating the first pipeline of its kind to bring Montenegrin and other regional startups to the United States.
