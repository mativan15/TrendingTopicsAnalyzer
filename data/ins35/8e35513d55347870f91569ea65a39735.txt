Liberty All-Star Equity Fund (NYSE:USA) certainly catches investors' attention due to its higher yield. This fairly plain equity fund has a managed distribution plan of 10%, which is divided by 2.5% and paid quarterly. This leads to a variable distribution from quarter to quarter but also predictability. If the net asset value per share rises, you'll get a boost and vice versa.
