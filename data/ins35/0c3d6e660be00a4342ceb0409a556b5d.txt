All 30 Dow stocks rally, led by Goldman and Salesforce stocks
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 11:53:12

The Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, +1.86% is headed for another big rally, with all 30 components trading higher in the premarket. The biggest gainers ahead of the open were shares of Goldman Sachs Group Inc. GS, +2.24%, which climbed 2.9% after reported better-than-expected third-quarter results, and of Salesforce Inc. CRM, +3.49%, which also rallied 2.9%. The most active Dow stock was Apple Inc.'s AAPL, +2.91%, which rose 1.7%, while the worst performer was Merck & Co. Inc.'s stock MRK, +2.
