Hitachi Energy India re-appoints N Venu as MD, CEO for 5 years

New Delhi: Hitachi Energy India Limited on Tuesday announced the reappointment of N Venu as its Managing Director and CEO. He has been re-appointed for a term of five years commencing December 2, 2022, Hitachi Energy India Limited said in a statement. "N Venu has been reappointed as MD and CEO for the second term of five years commencing December 2, 2022. The reappointment was on the recommendation of the Nomination and Remuneration Committee at the Board meeting held Monday," it said. The reappointment is subject to the approval of shareholders, the company said.
