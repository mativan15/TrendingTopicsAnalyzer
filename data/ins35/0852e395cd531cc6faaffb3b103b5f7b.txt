Published: 06:30 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 06:44 EDT, 1 June 2022 Footage has emerged of Real Madrid hero Vinicius scoring a goal eerily similar to his Champions League final winner, just hours before the showpiece in Paris.The Brazilian international evaded the clutches of Liverpool's Trent Alexander-Arnold to tap home the winner from a low cross, handing the Spanish giants a 14th success in the competition.But the goal looks to have been constructed on the training ground the day before the final as Madrid were fine-tuning their preparations.
