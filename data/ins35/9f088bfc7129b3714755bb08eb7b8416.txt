Old IITs are considering a fee revision for students joining from next session
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 06:00:00

Old Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) are considering a fee revision for students joining the next session starting 2023. The fee revision will be limited to hostel and mess charges for now and not apply to existing students, directors at multiple top IITs told ET. Fee revision, especially the tuition charges, has always been a pain point for the IITs, with previous such decisions often prompting protests at different campuses. 'Next year, we may look at revising the fee. We will soon hold discussions for which we will be setting up a committee where even the students' views would be included.
