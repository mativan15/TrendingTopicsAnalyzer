Three different ways to enjoy nighttime tour at Changdeokgung
naver.com
news
2022-10-21 17:39:00

Huijeongdang at Changdeokgung (CHA)Changdeokgung is scheduled to offer three different series of nighttime tours at the palace, according to the Cultural Heritage Administration's Royal Palaces and Tombs Center.For 'A Night at Changdeokgung, Huijeongdang and Glass Lanterns With Plum Blossom,' visitors will be guided through the brightly lit pavilion. Glass lanterns with plum blossom seals in gold that decorated both the indoors and the outdoors of the royal palace after the introduction of electricity in 1887 will be lit for the first time in 100 years.
