According to the report, Apple can introduce these smart capabilities into its iPads very soon.Apple is currently busy working on a new M2 Chip powered iPad and as per recent reports, the tech giant has big plans for the product. According to the report, Apple can introduce these smart capabilities into its iPads very soon.
