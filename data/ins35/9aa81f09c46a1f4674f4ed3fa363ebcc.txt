Since Russian President Vladimir Putin launched his full-scale invasion of Ukraine, the West has imposed a wide range of sanctions against Moscow. But support for every penalty has not been unanimous, as seen in Hungary's opposition to a complete EU ban on Russian oil. Fractures within NATO have also thrown up doubts over whether Finland and Sweden will be able to join the military alliance.In recent weeks, the European Union (EU) fought to push through a sixth package of sanctions against Russia.
