ReutersA revival plan for the loss-making carrier, named 'Vihaan.AI', is currently under way.Air India is eying 30 per cent share in domestic and international markets in the next five years, the Tata Group-owned airline's MD and CEO Campbell Wilson said on Tuesday. At present, the airline has a domestic market share of 10 per cent and an international market share of 12 per cent. Air India, which was acquired by Tata group in January, is in the process of expanding its fleet and services.

Wilson described the revival of Air India as not a "T20 match" but a "Test match".
