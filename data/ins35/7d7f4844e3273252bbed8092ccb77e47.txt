'Ginna' Twitter review: Netizens laud film as Vishnu Manchu's perfect comeback
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-21 05:12:00

The much-awaited Vishnu Manchu, Paayal Rajput and Sunny Leone's film 'Ginna' has hit the theatres today. The horror comedy created a huge buzz ever since its inception, and as per reports, it has managed to get a good opening at the box office. Many netizens took to social media to share their views after watching the movie. Fans have gone to length to praise Vishnu's performance. The humour quotient, different storyline, crisp narration, lead actors' performances, music and of course production value are the high points of 'Ginna' that are also being talked about on the internet.
