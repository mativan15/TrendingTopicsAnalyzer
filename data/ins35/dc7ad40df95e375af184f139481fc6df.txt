Engine No. 1 rushes to back ESG disclosures at top companies
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-06-02 12:34:57

Sustainability-focused hedge fund Engine No. 1 has backed 83% of shareholder resolutions on environmental, social and governance (ESG) topics at top U.S. companies so far this year as it looks to arm investors with more information, leaders said.Ballot items, such as several at Exxon Mobil Corp calling for more details around reduced plastic use and emissions-cut scenarios, help investors understand a business, Engine No. 1 founder and executive chairman, Christopher James, said in an interview with Reuters on Wednesday.
