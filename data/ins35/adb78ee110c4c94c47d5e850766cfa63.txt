WorkSafe investigating sudden death incident in rural North Otago
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-21 04:36:14

WorkSafe is investigating the sudden death of a person on a private property in rural North Otago. Emergency services were called to a sudden death at a private property in rural North Otago about 3.30pm on Wednesday, a police media team spokesperson said, on Friday, 'I can confirm it is not a crash and is not suspicious, therefore police will not be providing any further details,' the spokesperson said. Police would not confirm the age of the person who died. A WorkSafe New Zealand spokesperson said they had been notified and are making inquiries.
