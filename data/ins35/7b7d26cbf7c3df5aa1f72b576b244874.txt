TORONTO, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Canada's leading regulated cryptocurrency exchange VirgoCX is set to launch the anticipated OTC Direct overhaul to its over-the-counter (OTC) trading platform, VirgoCX Wealth. As part of their ongoing commitment to providing the best tools and user experience for their clients, the launch intends to provide the resources investors require to make timely, educated trading decisions with utmost ease.
