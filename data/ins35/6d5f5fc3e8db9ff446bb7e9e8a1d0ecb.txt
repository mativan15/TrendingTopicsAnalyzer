How to make a group video call on WhatsApp?
Creating a group call on WhatsApp is simple and is only a few additional clicks than usual.
Here is a three step guide on how to do it:

Open WhatsApp, then tap the CALLS tab
Tap New Call then New Group Call
Find the contacts you want to add to the call, then tap Voice Call

In 2021, WhatsApp added the joinable calls feature.
