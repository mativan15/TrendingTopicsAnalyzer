By Reuters Published: 15:54 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 15:54 EDT, 18 October 2022 Philadelphia Phillies right-hander David Robertson is back on the roster for the National League Championship Series after missing the divisional round with a calf injury. Robertson bumps Nick Nelson, who replaced him on the team's NL Division Series roster. Robertson missed the NLDS against the Atlanta Braves with a right calf strain sustained as he celebrated a home run in the wild-card round.
