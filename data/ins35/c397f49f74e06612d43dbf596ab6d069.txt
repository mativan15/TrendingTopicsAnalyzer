Mumbai: Cache of arms seized in goat farm, 1 arrested
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 12:45:00

AgenciesRepresentative imageMumbai Police on Wednesday recovered a cache of arms from a goat-rearing farm in the Behram Baug area and arrested a man for his alleged connection with the incident, ANI reported citing officials. The accused has been identified as Syed Majeed Alam alias Vicky and the police recovered seven swords and a knife, informed the officials.

The searches were conducted by Unit 9 of the Mumbai Crime Branch, which detained the accused from the Behrambagh area for allegedly keeping illegal arms and ammunition at his goat-rearing place.
