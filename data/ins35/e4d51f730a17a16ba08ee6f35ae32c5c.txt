California former solar company exec sentenced to prison for ‘billion-dollar Ponzi scheme,' prosecutors say
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-06-01 20:29:04

A former executive of a California-based solar power company was sentenced Tuesday to nearly a decade in prison for his role in what prosecutors say was a "billion-dollar Ponzi scheme."  Alan Hansen, 51, of Vacaville, pleaded guilty in 2020 to conspiracy and money laundering charges, the U.S. Attorney's Office said in a statement.  FILE: McGregor Scott, the U.S. Attorney for the Eastern District of California, answers questions during a news conference in Sacramento Calif., Friday, Jan. 24, 2020.
