Techwave to raise Hyderabad global development centre headcount to 8,000
indiatimes.com
tech
2022-05-29 08:30:00

HYDERABAD: Bullish on Telangana, US-based global software solutions provider Techwave has lined up aggressive hiring plans for its sole global development centre (GDC) at Hyderabad. It eyes a quadrupling of global headcount from the existing 2,500 employees to 10,000 by 2025 and a 2.5X growth in topline from $105 million in 2021 to $250 million by 2025 as it charts eyes acquisition of not just customers but companies too.Techwave, which currently has an India headcount of 1,800, plans to house 80% of its employees in state by 2025, which translates into nearly 8,000 techies to be based in the Hyderabad GDC by then, its chairman Damodar Gummadapu told TOI.
