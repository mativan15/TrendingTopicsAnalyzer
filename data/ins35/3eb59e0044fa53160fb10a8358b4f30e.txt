October 16, 2022 11:47 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--With the support of co-hosts and partners like BNB Chain, Quest3, and Yeeha Games, Project Twelve has launched the P12 Arcana game carnival in October 2022. An unprecedented event that explores the synergy between Web2 and Web3, P12 Arcana will offer the ultimate experience for a global audience of gamers, e-sport fans, Web3 enthusiasts, and beyond.

By collaborating with one of the world's largest Dota 2 esports tournaments, The International 2022, P12 Arcana is set to become the greatest Web3 game carnival of 2022.
