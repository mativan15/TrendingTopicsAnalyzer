Published: 08:41 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 08:44 EDT, 18 October 2022 Defiant union leaders are threatening to plunge Britain into a second 'winter of discontent' with mass strike action that could see up to two million workers taking industrial action, bringing the nation to its knees.  Amid the chaos unfurling in Westminster this week, the Prime Minster has been dealt another political broadside, this time by the Trades Union Congress (TUC), in what could be the most militant showdown with the Government since the 1970s.
