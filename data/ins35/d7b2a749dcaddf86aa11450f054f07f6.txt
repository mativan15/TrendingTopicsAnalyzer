Tesco is hiring 15,000 temporary workers this Christmas, with roles on offer in both Tesco superstores and smaller Extra convenience stores, with morning, day and evening shifts availableTesco is hiring 15,000 temporary workers for ChristmasTesco is hiring 15,000 temporary workers this Christmas to help customers during the busy festive season. There are roles on offer in both Tesco superstores and smaller Extra convenience stores, with morning, day and evening shifts available. Tesco said there may be opportunities for Christmas workers to apply for any permanent roles that are available.
