Hyderabad: Man demands cash from cops to buy booze, gets 70-day jail
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-18 11:00:00

HYDERABAD: A local court has sentenced a repeat offender to 70 days imprisonment for calling dial 100 and threatening the blue colts patrolling team that he would cut himself if they do not to give him money to buy liquor.The accused, Naveen from Anna Nagar in Begumpet called dial 100 at 6.05 pm on Sunday asking the police to come to him.The blue colts team personnel reached the spot and Naveen picked up an argument with Home Guard Pasha who was part of the blue colts team, asking him for money to buy liquor.
