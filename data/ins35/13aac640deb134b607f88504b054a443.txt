In late 2018, I finally finished paying off my vehicle by making one last, glorious car payment. I had bought a brand new luxury SUV in 2014 (one of my bigger money mistakes), so the sting of depreciation and a higher-than-necessary auto loan had plagued me for years.

I have thoroughly enjoyed being car payment-free ever since, which has loosened up my monthly budget for other exciting money moves. However, as much as I don't want to take on another car loan, I am now finding myself watching the odometer tick perilously close to the 100,000-mile mark.
