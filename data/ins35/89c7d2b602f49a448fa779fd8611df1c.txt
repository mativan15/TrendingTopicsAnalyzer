Victoria PLC Acquires International Wholesale Tile for $28.5 Mln -- Deal Digest
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 08:00:00

By Joe Hoppe ACQUIRER: Victoria PLC, a U.K.-based maker of flooring products. TARGET: International Wholesale Tile LLC, a Florida-based flooring distributor. PRICE: $28.5 million, with a further contingent payment payable over the next four years, conditional on the business achieving certain targets. STATUS: Complete. RATIONALE: The acquisition is expected to be immediately revenue and earnings enhancing, and further grow Victoria's North American division. Synergies with existing Victoria businesses will drive further benefits to customers and stronger financial results.
