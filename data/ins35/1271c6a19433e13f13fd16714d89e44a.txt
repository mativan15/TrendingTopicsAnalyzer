New Delhi: A special CBI court on Tuesday remanded Delhi health minister Satyendar Kumar Jain to Enforcement Directorate custody till June 9 in a money laundering case. Jain was arrested by the central agency on Monday. The central agency has "recorded an incriminating" statement of an individual linked to one of the alleged shell companies "beneficially owned and controlled" by Jain, said people in the know. In its remand paper, the central agency has referred to the statement recorded under Section 50 of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) which is admissible in court.
