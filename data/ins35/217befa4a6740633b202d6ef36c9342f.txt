Variety - Film News 'Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom' co-stars Ke Huy Quan and Harrison Ford broke the internet in September when they reunited for the first time in over three decades. The two actors snapped a viral photo together at Disney's D23 convention. Now in an interview with The New York Times' Kyle Buchanan, Quan detailed the moments leading up to that beloved reunion photo.'We were at the D23 event and I was told Harrison was gonna be there,' Quan said.
