Dow, S&P 500 Join Nasdaq Firmly In Positive Territory
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-06-02 19:54:09

(RTTNews) - Stocks have moved mostly higher over the course of the trading session, with the Dow and the S&P 500 joining the tech-heavy Nasdaq firmly in positive territory. The upward move on the day comes after the major averages closed lower for two straight sessions. The major averages have seen further upside in recent trading, reaching new highs for the session. The Dow is up 223.38 points or 0.7 percent at 33,036.61, the Nasdaq is up 267.85 points or 2.2 percent at 12,262.31 and the S&P 500 is up 52.
