A New Collection of Design Driven, Sustainable Sunglasses with a Zero Waste Supply Chain NEW YORK, June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- MONTSERRAT New York, the lifestyle brand worn by New York's up and coming "it" crowd is set to unveil their first collection of eyewear on June 3, 2022. Alongside King Children, a sustainable eyewear platform using zero waste 3D printing technology, the two teams worked together to create two debut sunglass designs, each available in three colorways.
