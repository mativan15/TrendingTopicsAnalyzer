Delta Air Lines raises revenue outlook, trims capacity view
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 12:14:50

Shares of Delta Air Lines Inc. DAL, -1.28% climbed 1.5% in premarket trading Wednesday, after the air carrier raised its total and unit revenue guidance for the second quarter, while the capacity outlook was lowered. Delta said it expects "strong" results for the quarter through June, and now expects revenue to be approximately 100% of what it was for the same period of pre-pandemic 2019, up from previous guidance of 93% to 97% of 2019 levels, while unit revenue is now expected to be seven-to-eight percentage points better than previously expected.
