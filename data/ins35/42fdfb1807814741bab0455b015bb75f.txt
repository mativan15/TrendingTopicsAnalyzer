iStockThe two were arrested during the day after the matter was reported to the police.A 22-year-old man was tied to a two-wheeler and made to run for about two kilometres on a busy road in Odisha's Cuttack city allegedly by two others as he failed to repay Rs 1500 to them on time, police said on Monday. The two were arrested during the day after the matter was reported to the police. "The accused were charged with wrongful confinement, kidnapping and attempt to murder," Cuttack city Deputy Commissioner of Police Pinak Mishra said here.
