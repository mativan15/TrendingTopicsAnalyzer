LOS ANGELES, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Pacific Avenue Capital Partners ("Pacific Avenue"), a Los Angeles-based private investment firm, has acquired Sunrich Products ("Sunrich"), the sunflower and roasted snack division of SunOpta (Nasdaq:STKL) (TSX:SOY). The transaction includes three processing facilities in the upper Midwest, hybrid seed development activities, a collaborative program with independent sunflower growers, and relationships with an extensive set of customers in the food ingredients, snack food, and birdseed markets.
