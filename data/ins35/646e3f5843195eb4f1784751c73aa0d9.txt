iStockThe upcoming XR device by Apple will come packed with sensors that work like Face ID in iPhones and iPads.It seems like Apple is on a mission to one-up Meta in the mixed reality competition as it's working on iris-scanning technology for the tech giant's forthcoming XR device. As per a report in The Information, the upcoming XR device by Apple will come packed with sensors that work like Face ID in iPhones and iPads.

As per the report, the 'Iris ID' will help users log in to their accounts and even make payments biometrically.
