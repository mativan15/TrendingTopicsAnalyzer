Motorola has launched Moto e32s in India at an introductory price of Rs. 8,999. The phone gets a 6.5-inch 90Hz IPS LCD display, 5000mAH battery, and much more. Check details here. Motorola on Thursday launched the Moto e32s in India at an introductory price of Rs. 8,999 across more than 60,000 retail stores through JioMart Digital and Reliance Digital, and on Flipkart. The Moto e32s will be available from June 6, 2022 onwards on Flipkart. "Aimed at giving the most premium, contemporary, and durable designs in the budget segment, the Moto e32s comes with a premium PMMA finish, an ultra slim and durable design with segment's first IP52 rating.
