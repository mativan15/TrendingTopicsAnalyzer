‘My five-hour-long hell at the Indian visa office'
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 16:00:00

My upcoming journey to India, meticulously planned over the last six months to start with the lights of Diwali in Delhi, hung in the balance yesterday — over 50p. Such is the comedy of errors the tourist visa process application has become as the tit-for-tat between our respective governments descends into sheer farce.The pre-2020 days of sitting on your phone and applying for an e-visa nearly as quickly as you might sign up for a parking permit, and receiving it within two to four working days, have vanished in a diplomatic fiasco.
