One of the trickiest parts of traveling is maintaining a solid fitness routine. Though hotels can have gyms, it's not always guaranteed that they will have what you need. Plus, if you're following a regimen at home, it can be challenging to translate that into a jet-setting lifestyle. So, one couple decided to change that by creating the LIT AXIS, the world's first smart, portable, fully connected resistance fitness system (no electricity or wifi required).
