KKR Real Estate Finance Trust sells 4.25M shares in secondary stock offering (NYSE:KREF)
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-02 20:28:17

KKR Real Estate Finance Trust (NYSE:KREF) has announced a stock offering of 7M shares that includes 4.25M secondary shares to be sold by KKR REFT Holdings L.P. Underwriters are to be given ana overallotment option to purchase up to an additional 1.05M primary shares of KREF common stock The company intends to use the net proceeds to originate, acquire and finance target assets in a manner consistent with its investment strategies and investment guidelines and for general corporate purposes. After giving effect to the offering, the KKR & Co's stockholder is to own 10M shares down from 14.
