James Corden's Carpool Karaoke confession left 'fans more upset than Cats movie'
irishmirror.ie
news
2022-10-19 11:08:44

James Corden once left fans gutted when they uncovered a secret behind his famous Carpool Karaoke series. The British presenter has hosted the popular segment on The Late, Late Show for several years, with global icons such as Madonna, Britney Spears, Adele, Lady Gaga and Mariah Carey sitting in a car's passenger seat and belting out some of their hit tunes as James seemingly drives around. But during Justin Bieber's stint in the car alongside James, fans discovered Carpool Karaoke wasn't filmed how they imagined it would be - with James going on the admit the shocking revelation upset fans more than his cameo in the ill-fated movie Cats.
