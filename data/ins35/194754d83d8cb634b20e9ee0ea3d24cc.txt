Brings 20 Years of marketing, sales and innovation experience from established publicly-traded companies and high-growth startup brands to Remark.  LAS VEGAS, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Remark Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: MARK), a diversified global leader in AI powered video analytics, authentication technology and risk management solutions today announced that DeMeakey Williams, Sr. joins the organization as Chief Revenue Officer (CRO), where he will oversee Remark's revenue generation, brand awareness, go-to-market strategies, global communications program, demand creation and customer advocacy.
