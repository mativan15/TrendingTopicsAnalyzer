June 01, 2022 04:33 AM Eastern Daylight Time DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The "Augmented Reality (AR) in Construction - Thematic Research" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.



Augmented reality (AR) is a technology that allows the user to see the real world overlaid with digital data. The AR market was worth $9 billion in 2021, and the publisher estimates it will grow to $150 billion by 2030.

By the end of the decade, enterprise AR will account for 65% of global AR revenue, as enterprise AR headsets dominate the market.
