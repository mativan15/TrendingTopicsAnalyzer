KATERYNA TYSHCHENKO – THURSDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2022, 22:47

President Volodymyr Zelenskyy calls on the international community to convey to Russia that a possible terrorist attack on the KHPP [Kakhovka Hydroelectric Power Plant] will be equated to the use of mass destruction weapons, and urged Ukraine's foreign partners to take preventive measures.

Source: the President's evening address

Quote: " All of us – Europeans, world leaders, international organizations – must make it clear to the terrorist state that such a terrorist attack on the KHPP will mean exactly the same as the use of weapons of mass destruction.
