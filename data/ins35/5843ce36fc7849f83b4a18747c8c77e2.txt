Agencies'We expect a tepid listing because of expensive valuations but any positive surprise can't be ruled out amid improved market sentiments.'NEW DELHI: Shares of three companies which launched their initial public offer (IPO) recently will list on the bourses for secondary market trading during the week. Given the market conditions and grey market premium (GMP), a big bang listing gains should not be expected. The first listing will be Ethos on Monday, which is a luxury watch retailer. It will be followed by eMudhra a couple of days later, which is a certifying authority.
