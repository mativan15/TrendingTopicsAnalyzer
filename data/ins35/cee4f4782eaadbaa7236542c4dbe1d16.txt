October 17, 2022 08:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time SCOTTSDALE, Ariz.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--STORE Capital Corporation (NYSE: STOR, 'STORE Capital' or the 'Company'), an internally managed net-lease real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests in Single Tenant Operational Real Estate, announced today the expiration of the 'go-shop' period set forth in the previously announced definitive merger agreement, dated September 15, 2022, with affiliates of GIC, a global institutional investor, and Oak Street, a Division of Blue Owl (the 'merger agreement').
