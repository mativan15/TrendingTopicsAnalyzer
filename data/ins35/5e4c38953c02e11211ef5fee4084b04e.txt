The NFL will play a game on Black Friday streamed on Amazon next year, the league announced Tuesday, expanding the lucrative partnership between the trillion-dollar e-commerce giant and the wealthiest sports league as each looks to extend their dominance around the Thanksgiving holiday. Amazon took over as the broadcaster of Thursday NFL games this year.Getty Images Amazon, the exclusive broadcaster of Thursday Night Football beginning this fall, is 'uniquely positioned' to air the Black Friday game given the significance the day already holds for the company's retail business, Hans Schroeder, NFL Media's chief operating officer, said in a statement.
