Karnal BJP leaders attend dera chief's satsang, spark controversy
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 20:13:45

: BJP leaders from Karnal, including municipal corporation mayor Renu Bala Gupta, attended an online satsang here of rape convict and now on parole dera chief Gurmeet Ram Rahim held in Uttar Pradesh, triggering a controversy on social media with people accusing their participation in the event as a politically motivated move. Gupta, who was accompanied by senior deputy mayor Rajesh Aggi and deputy mayor Naveen Kumar, attended the virtual satsang at Karnal's Kambopura village and arrangements were made so that the followers could join the virtual satsang on Tuesday.
