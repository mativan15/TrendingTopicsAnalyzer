 A 26-year-old man was shot in the neck by an unidentified person outside Modern Complex Society on Old Kalka Road in Zirakpur on Saturday night.Police said the victim, Aditya, lives in Aerocity, Mohali, and works for a private bank in Rajpura. He remains in the ICU at Government Medical College and Hospital, Sector 32, where he underwent a surgery after being referred by a private hospital.
