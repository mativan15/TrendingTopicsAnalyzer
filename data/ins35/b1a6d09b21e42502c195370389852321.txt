The crimes occurred between May 25 and May 29. (Shutterstock)SUMMIT, NJ — Two cars were stolen and one burglarized in Summit — all of which were unlocked, according to Summit Police's latest police blotter.On May 25 at 11:45 a.m., the driver of a black 2018 Mercedes-Benz E300 said they went left the car unlocked with the key fob inside while they went into an Ashwood Avenue business to use the restroom, said Summit Police.When they returned to the parking lot, the car was gone. The car is valued at $35,000.
