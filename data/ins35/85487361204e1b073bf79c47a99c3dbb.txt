KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy Nikki CourtneyJun 5, 2022Data from real estate company Redfin reveals some sellers are dropping home prices in 7 of the top 10 destination cities in a bid to attract buyers as rising interest rates and inflation take a bite out of prospective business. San Antonio is the only Texas city that made the list. In Houston, prices aren't going down. Houston is still hot, though not quite as hot as we've seen over the past two years, says Baker Mortgage's Cathy Baker.
