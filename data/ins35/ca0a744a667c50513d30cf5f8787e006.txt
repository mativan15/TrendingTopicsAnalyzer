IT'S FAIR to say Pierce Brosnan is one of the most famous (and sexiest) men on the planet.
But some fans often wonder about his wife Keely Shaye Smith, the lady who captured the James Bond actor's heart following the heartbreak of losing his first wife.
 Pierce Brosnan and his wife Keely Shaye SmithCredit: GETTY
Who is Pierce Brosnan's wife Keely Shaye Smith?
Born in 1963, Keely Shaye Smith is an American journalist, model TV producer, and the wife of Pierce Brosnan.
She has won numerous awards for her journalism and specialises in gardening and the environment.
