Mastermind of Koth bus robbery held
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 05:45:00

Surat: The city police on Thursday arrested a 35-year-old man from Kapodra area for his alleged involvement in the robbery worth crores from an Amreli-Surat bus of Ramdev Travels. The crime took place in the Koth police station limits of Ahmedabad district in early hours of Wednesday.Accused Hiren Dhiru Aakoliya, a resident of Sarthana and a native of Dhari in Amreli, is the mastermind of the loot. A team of prevention of crime branch arrested Aakoliya based on specific details provided by Ahmedabad rural police.
