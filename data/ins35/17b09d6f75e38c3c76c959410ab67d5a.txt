20 October 2022 by Ed Meza 
Variety Cohen Film Collection is continuing its restorations of classic Merchant Ivory productions, among them 1963's 'The Householder,' the first film collaboration between Ismail Merchant and James Ivory.The classics label of Cohen Media Group, Cohen Film Collection is lining up the restorations of four titles that also include the 1977 episodic romantic drama 'Roseland,' with Teresa Wright and Christopher Walken, and two films directed by Merchant, 'In Custody' (1994), featuring Shashi Kapoor, and 'The Proprietor' (1996), starring Jeanne Moreau.
