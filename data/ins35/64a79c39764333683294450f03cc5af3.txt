Bilateral trade between India and Korea grew 40% to $23.7 billion in 2021

The bilateral trade between India and Korea grew 40 per cent to USD 23.7 billion in 2021, according to Korea Trade-Investment Promotion Agency (KOTRA). In 2020, the value of bilateral trade between the two countries was at USD 16.9 billion. KOTRA is the economic division of the Embassy of the Republic of Korea in India. 
 According to KOTRA, the information on trade was disclosed by the Ambassador of the Republic of Korea to India Chang Jae-bok while inaugurating the 4th edition of Korea Fair in India (KFI) 2022 in New Delhi on Wednesday.
