The School Education Department screened the French movie. The Red Balloon at all of the state's government schools. A number of initiatives for government schools were introduced this academic year, including monthly film screenings. It is to promote art and culture through extra and co-curricular activities. The Red Balloon is a 34 minute award-winning film which was released in 1956. It follows a young boy who discovers a red helium balloon which seemingly follows him everywhere. This is the third film screened in the current academic year after Charlie Chaplin's The Kid and Majid Majidi's Children of Heaven.
