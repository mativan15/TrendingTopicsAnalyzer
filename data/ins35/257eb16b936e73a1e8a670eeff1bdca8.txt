First Stream Latin is a compilation of the best new Latin songs, albums, and videos recommended by the Billboard Latin editors. Check out this week's picks below. 
 Yuridia & Angela Aguilar, 'Qué Agonía' (Sony Music Entertainment México) Yuridia joins forces with Ángela Aguilar for 'Qué Agonía,' the fifth single from her newly released Regional Mexican album, Pa' Luego Es Tarde, produced by Eden Muñoz. Both song and album came out Thursday (October 20). With beautiful orchestration, and sublime harmonies from these two powerhouses, 'Qué Agonía' — written by Aguilar — is a romantic Mexican ballad about being in love long after a breakup.
