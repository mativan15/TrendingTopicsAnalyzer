In this article we are going to estimate the intrinsic value of Devon Energy Corporation (NYSE:DVN) by taking the expected future cash flows and discounting them to their present value. We will take advantage of the Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) model for this purpose. Don't get put off by the jargon, the math behind it is actually quite straightforward.
