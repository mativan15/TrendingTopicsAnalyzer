BONN, Germany & BELLEVUE, Wash.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--T-Mobile (NASDAQ: TMUS) and T-Labs, Deutsche Telekom's research and development unit, today announced the six winners of T-Challenge, a six-month-long worldwide competition for startups, developers, and designers to submit innovative research and solutions aimed at transforming retail with 5G and Extended Reality (XR) technology. The six winners were among 19 finalists who traveled to Bonn, Germany to present their solutions to top leaders at Deutsche Telekom and T-Mobile US.
