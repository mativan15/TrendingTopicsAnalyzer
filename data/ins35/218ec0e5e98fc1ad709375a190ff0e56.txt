The managing members at Vallit Advisors are pleased to announce the addition of Robert W. Carter, MS, CPA/CFF, CVA, CFE, CEPA, to the firm. He joins the firm as a Director focusing on Dispute consulting, business valuation and forensic accounting. Robert brings over 15 years of experience in disputes, business valuation consulting, expert witness services, forensic accounting and fraud investigation services across numerous industries. His previous work includes a wide range of services from financial analysis to the development of internal control procedures.
