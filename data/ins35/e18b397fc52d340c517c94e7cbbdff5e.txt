Iowa Wolves Acquire Returning Rights To Justin Jaworski
nba.com
news
2022-10-21 17:00:00

The Iowa Wolves announced today that the team has acquired the returning player rights to guard Justin Jaworski from the Oklahoma City Blue in exchange for two second-round picks in tomorrow's 2022 NBA G League Draft.

Jaworski, 6-3, played in 37 games with Oklahoma City in 2021-22, averaging 8.0 points and 2.6 rebounds per game while shooting 40.2% from the field and 37.6% from three.

Jaworski played four collegiate seasons for Lafayette University where he scored 1,484 career points and made 247 three-pointers and twice earned All-Patriot League honors.
