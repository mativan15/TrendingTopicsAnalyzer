Shinsei Bank Shares Jump After Report That SBI Is Mulling Delisting Banking Unit -- Update
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 05:37:00

By Kosaku Narioka Shinsei Bank Ltd. shares rose sharply Thursday following a report that SBI Holdings Inc. is considering delisting the banking unit. Shares were recently8.4% higher at 2,326 yen after surging as much as 16% earlier. SBI shares were down 0.7%. The Topix subindex for banks was recently 0.8% higher and the Nikkei Stock Average was down 1.3%. SBI is expected to start a tender offer to boost its stake in Shinsei Bank and gain support from the Japanese government in order to achieve the delisting, Kyodo News reported Wednesday evening, without citing sources.
