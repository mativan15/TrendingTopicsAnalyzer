Vanguard selects Nuvei as payment partner to accelerate their business

Montreal and Atlanta, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nuvei Corporation ('Nuvei' or the 'Company') (Nasdaq: NVEI) (TSX: NVEI), tomorrow's payment platform, announces today that it has been selected by Vanguard Truck Centers as a payment provider to help accelerate the growth of their multi-regional, full-service U.S. commercial truck dealer group. Nuvei's end-to-end payment solutions accessed through a single integration supports Vanguard's entire business-to-business payment processing needs as they grow.
