Religare Enterprises shoots up 10% as lenders to subsidiary to mull loan settlement proposal

2022-06-02 05:30:00

AgenciesReligare has resolved many legacy issues left over by its erstwhile promoters, including settlement with SEBI and becoming debt-free, Shares of skyrocketed 10.2 per cent in early trade on Thursday after the NBFC said that lenders to its debt-ridden subsidiary Religare Finvest have agreed to a consider a one-time settlement. Religare has resolved many legacy issues left over by its erstwhile promoters, including settlement with SEBI and becoming debt-free, PTI reported. RFL has received a communication dated May 31, 2022, from its lead lender informing that in a meeting held on May 20, 2022, lenders have in principle agreed to consider the OTS proposal of RFL and to put up the same to their respective authorities, the report said.
