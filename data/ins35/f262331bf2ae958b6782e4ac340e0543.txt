Business leaders and unions call for permanent holiday
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 23:01:00

Boris Johnson is facing calls from business leaders, leading broadcasters and charity heads to make tomorrow's Platinum Jubilee bank holiday permanent to recognise 'community and service in all its forms'.The signatories of the open letter include Tony Danker, director-general of the CBI, Sir Peter Bazalgette, the chairman of ITV, General Lord Dannatt, a former head of the army, and leading unions.They say in the letter: 'As we start a weekend of unprecedented national celebrations we write as a group of leaders from across businesses, charities, trade unions and the arts to urge the government to help maintain this spirit of community in the years to come.
