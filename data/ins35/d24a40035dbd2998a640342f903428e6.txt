Jurgen Klopp believes he will be on the touchline for Liverpool's match against West Ham on Wednesday night, as he reflected on the fallout to his side's clash with Manchester City.The Liverpool boss was sent off at Anfield against City after a furious outburst at a linesman when Mohamed Salah was not awarded a free-kick following a challenge from Bernardo Silva.Despite that red card, Klopp is not expecting to receive a touchline ban, though he admitted that he lost control of his emotions during the much-needed win over Pep Guardiola's side.
