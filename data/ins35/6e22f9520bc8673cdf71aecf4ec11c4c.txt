Aditya Birla group launches house of brands venture Tmrw; Prashanth Aluru named CEO
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 08:15:00

The Aditya Birla group announced the launch of its new ‘House of Brands' entity– Tmrw. Over the next three years, Tmrw will acquire as well as incubate more than 30 brands in the fashion and lifestyle segment. The group has appointed ex Facebook Bain and Kalaari Capital executive Prashanth Aluru , as the CEO & cofounder to lead the build out of this new venture, a company statement said.Tmrw will enable India's next phase of direct to consumer (D2C) growth, which is expected to reach $100 billion by 2025, the statement said.
