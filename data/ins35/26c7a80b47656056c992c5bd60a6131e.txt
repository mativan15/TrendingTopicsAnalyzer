Leisure Concept Woking Superbowl Has Opened in Woking – Retail & Leisure International
uk.com
news
2022-10-19 14:23:00

Leisure concept Woking Superbowl has opened in Victoria Place, Woking,
the significant town centre regeneration initiative that launched in March this
year. The recently opened 26,500 sq ft space features amusements such as a state-of-the-art
14-lane bowling alley and a ‘space age' Laser Quest arena along with both food
and dining options. Designed by Funk Bowling of Munich, who also fitted out the Superbowl at
Wokingham, the leisure concept promises affordable, family-focused entertainment
and fun.
