Smart Trackers Enable Revenue Teams to Identify Deal Risks and Opportunities with Industry-Leading AccuracyPALO ALTO, Calif., Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Gong, the Revenue Intelligence leader, today launched Smart Trackers™, the first user-trainable AI system for deep understanding of customer conversations. Smart Trackers enable customer-facing teams to identify deal risks and opportunities, understand the effectiveness of strategic initiatives, replicate best practices, and get ahead of emerging market needs.
