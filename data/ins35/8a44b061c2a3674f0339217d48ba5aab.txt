By Amy Phipps & Samantha NobleBBC NewsImage source, Annie AshtonImage caption, Nearly 70,000 people have signed the petitionA widow has handed in a petition calling on the government to publish a review of gambling laws.Annie Ashton, from Leicester, has called for a review of the law since her husband Luke killed himself.She said he developed a gambling disorder while he was furloughed and despite his efforts to stop, the offer of "free bets" kept luring him back.The government said it was "determined to protect those most at risk of gambling-related harm".
