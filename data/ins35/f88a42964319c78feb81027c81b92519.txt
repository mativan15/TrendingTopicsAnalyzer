V-Mart to acquire fashion marketplace Limeroad; invest Rs 150 cr to scale the startup
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 16:00:00

New Delhi: Retail store chain V-Mart to acquire online marketplace Limeroad and invest Rs 150 crore to scale the etailer and achieve sustained profitability, the companies said in a press release on Monday. According to the release, in this partnership, the companies will combine LimeRoad's fashion quotient, logistics efficiency, technology and data with V-Mart's strong retail presence through 410 stores across tier 2, 3, and 4 cities to build a profitable omni-channel value fashion retail platform.
