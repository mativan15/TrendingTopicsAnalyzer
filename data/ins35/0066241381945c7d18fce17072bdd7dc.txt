Oil prices open higher on EU Russian oil ban, end of Shanghai lockdown
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 03:45:00

AgenciesIndia has already mopped up record amounts of Russian oil while Chinese refineries are expected to step up purchases after emerging from a months-long lockdown.Oil prices rose in early Asian trade on Wednesday after European Union leaders agreed to a partial and phased ban on Russian oil and China ended its COVID-19 lockdown in Shanghai. Brent crude for August delivery was up 78 cents, or 0.7%, at $116.38 a barrel at 0037 GMT. The front-month contract for July delivery expired on Tuesday at $122.
