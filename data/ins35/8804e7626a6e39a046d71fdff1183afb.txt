Image source, The Royal Marines CharityImage caption, L/Cpl Grainger has been joined by other fellow marines on each of his marathonsA soldier from Devon is running 60 marathons in 60 days to raise money for The Royal Marines Charity.It is the first time L/Cpl Luke Grainger has ever taken part in any ultra marathons.The Royal Marines Commando has already completed 45 marathons consecutively, in Plymouth, Barnstaple, Land's End, Windsor and Bristol.On Saturday, L/Cpl Grainger takes on the Plym Valley Trail marathon in Plymouth.
