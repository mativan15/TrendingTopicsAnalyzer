Is this the world's best premium economy cabin?
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 10:17:00

Few feelings rival that buzz of turning left on an aeroplane – knowing that rather than having to fold yourself into your seat and fight over an armrest with a too-close-for-comfort neighbour, you'll be able to lie back, sip champagne and generally be cosseted. 
Sadly, with flight prices skyrocketing and the economic crisis beginning to bite, the indulgence of business class has become even more unattainable for all but a privileged few. 
Enter Emirates and its hotly anticipated premium economy cabin, which has been rolled out on selected routes on board the double-decker A380, including London to Dubai.
