GROOVOO launches new event tech to save event producers time and money, and to help prevent the growing issue of ticketing fraud in an age of digital tickets. Ticketing fraud and scams have become increasingly common as the events industry has begun to transition from physical to digital tickets. For example, about 12 percent of U.S. adults buying concert tickets online said they'd been scammed, according to a CNBC report. MIAMI and SAN FRANCISCO (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Following a few years of pandemic-induced lockdowns, there's been a spike of interest to attend live concerts, sporting events, and in-person business conferences again.
