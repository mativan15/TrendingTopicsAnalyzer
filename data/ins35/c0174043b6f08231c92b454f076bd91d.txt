The second wave of Covid-19 is ebbing in India with total active cases at less than 20 per cent of May highs.Shares of Larsen & Toubro Infotech Ltd. rose 1.25 per cent to Rs 3918.6 in Friday's trade as of 10:30AM (IST) even as the benchmark Nifty ruled at 16261.75, up 91.6 points. The scrip had closed at Rs 3870.4 in the previous session. The stock quoted a 52-week low of Rs 3733.25 and a high of Rs 7595.25. The company quoted a market-cap of Rs 68889.
