JD(U) to become strong in J'hand: Party president
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 07:15:00

Ranchi: National president of Janta Dal (U) and MP Lalan Singh on Sunday said his party would emerge as a powerful political organisation in Jharkhand Singh was addressing the state-level meeting of his party here. Former MLA Gautam Sagar Rana merged his party Jharkhand Janta Dal with Janta Dal (U) on the occasion.Singh said the joining of Rana and his supporters would further strengthen the Janta Dal (U) in the state. He said Bihar chief minister Nitish Kumar has a soft spot for Jharkhand and is disappointed over the slow progress of the state, which has vast mineral resources.
