Japan's Nikkei edges back from 6-week high as Astellas, Sony drag
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 09:00:00

Healthcare and tech were the worst-performing Nikkei sectors, sliding 1.51% and 1.14% respectively. Utilities and financials alone eked out gains, but were each up less than 0.1%.Japan's Nikkei share average retreated from a six-week high on Thursday, tracking losses on Wall Street overnight amid simmering worries about global inflation. The Nikkei slipped 0.16% to 27,413.88 at close, but was off the early morning lows at 27,251.24. The benchmark ended at 27,457.89 in the previous session, the highest since April 21.
