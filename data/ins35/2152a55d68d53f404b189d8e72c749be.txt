May 31, 2022 09:43 AM Eastern Daylight Time RESTON, Va.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--NVE Inc., a woman-owned small business specializing in facilities operations and maintenance, construction security monitoring, and program support for federal government programs, today announced it has won a contract from the Internal Revenue Service to provide facility operations and preventive maintenance services for its headquarters in Washington, D.C. The contract was acquired through NVE's seat on the General Services Administration's Building, Maintenance & Operations Phase I governmentwide contract vehicle.
