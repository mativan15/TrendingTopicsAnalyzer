In 1907, Vadilal Gandhi, a resident of Ahmedabad founded the eponymous brand. He started by selling soda and gradually added ice cream as well. It was this small but fast-growing business that Ranchod Lal Gandhi, Vadilal Gandhi's son, inherited.The century-old company Vadilal, foundation of which was laid pre-independence era, has now taken off and made a buzz in the US market. He used to make ice creams from the handmade "Kothi" method, where ice creams were made by mixing sugar, salt, milk and ice in a traditional hand-operated machine.
