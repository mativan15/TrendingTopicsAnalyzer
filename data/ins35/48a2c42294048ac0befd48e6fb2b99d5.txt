Godrej RenTRUST eyes 23% YoY growth

2022-05-27 16:55:26

Godrej RenTRUST, a business of Godrej & Boyce Mfg. Co. Ltd., is targeting 23% growth YOY in its rental solutions service.It said it is the only Indian rental solutions provider that manufactures and provides Indian origin material handling equipment to its clients. In line with the growth of e-commerce sector in Tier 3 and Tier 4 markets, the demand for warehousing equipment was also rising, it added.It said companies across industries, especially project-based firms, were moving towards renting equipment and outsourcing material handling requirements to expand their operations.
