Marcus Sasser addresses the audience during the University of Houston Athletics Department celebration of the men's basketball team's run to the 2021 Final Four at the Fertitta Center in Houston on Wednesday, April 7, 2021.Elizabeth Conley/Staff photographerUniversity of Houston basketball got some excellent news Wednesday night when Marcus Sasser removed his name from the NBA draft and announced he'd return to the Cougars for another season.The 6-foot-2 guard was a key member of the 2021 Final Four team, then looked like the best player on last year's team that went to the Elite Eight before he broke a bone in his left foot that knocked him out of the season after just 12 games.
