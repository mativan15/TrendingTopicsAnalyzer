getty First, let's take a few steps back and look at the web timeline. It all started with static pages, useful for reading and passive experiences, which we called Web 1.0. Then came the Web 2.0 revolution, bringing the boom of communities and collaboration on centralized social media platforms. This has been the era of user-generated content, with billions of users around the world blogging, podcasting and vlogging everywhere.
