Cunaplus_M.Faba/iStock via Getty Images Wex Inc. (NYSE:WEX) rose 2% after investor Impactive Capital pitched the stock at an investor conference. Impactive Capital head Lauren Taylor Wolfe pitched Wex (WEX) as a long idea earlier at the 13D Monitor Active-Passive Summit in New York City. Investors appear to misunderstand the impact that electric vehicles will have on Wex's (WEX) fleet segment, Wolfe told CNBC in an interview after she presented at the conference. "We contend the opposite," Wolfe said.
