October 19, 2022 04:40 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement for Sen. Tim Scott (R-S.C.) for re-election to the U.S. Senate, and Reps. Jeff Duncan (R-S.C.), Nancy Mace (R-S.C.), Ralph Norman (R-S.C.), William Timmons (R-S.C.), and Joe Wilson (R-S.C.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidates' lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.
