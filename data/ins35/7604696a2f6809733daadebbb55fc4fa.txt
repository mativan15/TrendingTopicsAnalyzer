The Indian League or the IPL often serves as a platform for young cricketers to showcase their talent. Ever since its inception in 2008, IPL has made many cricketers, who were playing locally, household names. The most recent success story spawned by the IPL has been that of Rajat Patidar. While the twenty-eight year old cricketer, who hails from Madhya Pradesh, was fortunate enough to get a spot in Royal Challengers Bangalore, Rajat deserves credit for putting in a lot of hard work and making very good use of the platform given to him.
