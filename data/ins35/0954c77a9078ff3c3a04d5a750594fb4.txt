REUTERS/Hannah McKayLONDON, Oct 20 (Reuters) - To some Conservatives, Rishi Sunak is the minister who got Britain through the COVID pandemic, to others the traitor who knifed Boris Johnson or the rival to Liz Truss who presciently warned her tax plans would bring chaos.Now he has to weigh up whether he can convince Britain's governing party he is the man to save it from chaos.
