'We're in a bear market. And I think that's good': Crypto firms hope market slump shakes out bad players
cnbc.com
news
2022-05-27 07:57:26

Executives from the blockchain and cryptocurrency industry told CNBC that the recent crash in the digital coin market should help get rid of "bad actors" in the space.Billions of dollars of value has been wiped off the cryptocurrency market in the last few weeks driven by a sell-off in stocks and the collapse of algorithmic stablecoin terraUSD and its related token luna."We're in a bear market. And I think that's good. It's good, because it's going to clear the people who were there for the bad reasons," Bertrand Perez, CEO of the Web3 Foundation, told CNBC at the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland.
