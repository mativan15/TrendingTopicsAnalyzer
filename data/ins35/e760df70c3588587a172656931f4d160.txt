Published: 10:24 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 10:24 EDT, 20 October 2022 LONDON, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Russia said on Thursday that the West's supplies of advanced weapons to Ukraine were finding their way onto the black market and then into the hands of extremist and criminal groups in the Middle East, central Africa and Asia. Since Russia launched its war with Ukraine on Feb. 24, the biggest land invasion in Europe since World War Two, Western powers have sent Ukraine an array of weapons in an attempt to help forces fighting Russian troops.
