[Photo/Xinhua] Heilongjiang province will continue to strive to make new breakthroughs in the overall revitalization and improve its strength in safeguarding food security, Shen Ying, deputy governor of the province said on Tuesday at a news conference that was held in Beijing during the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China.
