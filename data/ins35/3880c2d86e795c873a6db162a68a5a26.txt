BOSTON, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Cannabis Creative Group, an award-winning full-service cannabis digital marketing agency, and Joint Venture & Co., a strategic growth firm working with companies in the cannabis and wellness spaces, announced the TeeHC Open, a one-of-a-kind, invite-only cannabis networking event taking place at the Highfields Golf & Country Club in Grafton, Massachusetts on Friday, September 9, 2022.
