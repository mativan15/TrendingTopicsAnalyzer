Photo: Getty ImagesThe Tennessee Titans have reportedly reached a deal with Mayor John Cooper to build a new, domed stadium in downtown Nashville, sources with knowledge of the deal told Axios' Nate Rau. An official announcement, which will provide details on a financing plan, is expected to "come as soon as Monday following months of negotiations," Rau reports. The stadium is expected to be located east of Nissan Stadium's current location near Interstate 24 as part of the recently approved redevelopment plan for the East Bank waterfront neighborhood in downtown Nashville.
