David McWilliams: ‘Putinflation' has rewritten the economic rulebook
irishtimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 05:00:00

Recent falls in stock markets are just what we needed. In fact, the falls in all financial markets – bonds, stocks and of course, the plaything de jour, crypto – may just save the world from an economic catastrophe. This may sound counterintuitive. Aren't financial markets supposed to be economic indicators; when they go up, the future is rosy and when they go down, it's time to get out of town? Sometimes this is true but not in 2022. Before we dive into the argument that falls in assets prices could be good for the global – and by extension the Irish – economy, let's examine the market falls.
