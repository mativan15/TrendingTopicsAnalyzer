Balenciaga Has Officially Severed Ties With Kanye West
iheart.com
news
2022-10-21 14:39:00

Photo: Getty ImagesYe's business relationship with Balenciaga has come to an end. According to a report TMZ published on Friday, October, 21, Kering, the French house parent company to Balenciaga, officially severed its ties with the rapper formerly known as Kanye West. The high-end fashion brand did not go into detail about why their ending their collaborative efforts. However, it's the first major statement the brand made since Ye has generated so much negative attention from his controversial 'White Lives Matter' t-shirts, his disparaging anti-Semitic comments and that he's made during several interviews.
