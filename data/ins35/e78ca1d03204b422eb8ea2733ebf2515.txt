SAN DIEGO —

This was the day the Padres and Dodgers truly became rivals, the moment the feisty little brother rose up and embarrassed the glamorous big brother with a display of heart and persistence that no amount of money can buy and no manager can teach.

While thousands of Padres fans screamed themselves hoarse and waved rally towels in a swirl of golden glee at Petco Park, the Padres rallied for five runs in a madcap seventh inning Saturday and held on for a 5-3 victory over the Dodgers to win the National League Division Series in four stunning games.
