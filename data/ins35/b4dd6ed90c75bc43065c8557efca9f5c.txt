Bob DelmontJun 3, 2022Couples feeling a burning love and a desire to get married in Las Vegas by an Elvis impersonator better hurry up and do it because it's now or never. "The King" has been a big part of the Las Vegas wedding industry for decades, but that may all come to an end soon because Authentic Brands Group, the licensing company which controls Elvis Presley's name and image, has sent a cease-and-desist letter to Vegas wedding chapels holding Elvis-themed ceremonies. Owners of these businesses perform thousands of Elvis-officiated weddings each year and are just now seeing business returning to normal after the pandemic.
