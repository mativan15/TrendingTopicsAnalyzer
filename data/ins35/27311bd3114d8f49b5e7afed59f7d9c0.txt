ROSEMONT, Ill., May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Wintrust recently earned seven Greenwich Excellence Awards in U.S. Middle Market and U.S. Small Business Banking categories from Coalition Greenwich for 2021.
