getty A cookie-less world is coming. Tech giant Apple has already phased out third-party cookies, and Google is set to follow. While third-party cookies and behavioral targeting make advertisers' worlds go round, cookies that track users from site to site have long been a privacy gray area. Plenty of users employ cookie blockers, and cookie consent is annoying for everyone. Cookies are also becoming less reliable than they used to be, as the data gained from them is patchy and inconsistent.
