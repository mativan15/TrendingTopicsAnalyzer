AM Best Affirms Credit Ratings of Everen Specialty Ltd.

October 21, 2022 10:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has affirmed the Financial Strength Rating of A- (Excellent) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of 'a-' (Excellent) of Everen Specialty Ltd. (Everen Specialty) (Hamilton, Bermuda) (formally known as Oil Casualty Insurance, Ltd.). AM Best also has affirmed the Long-Term Issue Credit Rating of 'bbb' (Good) on the $200 million, 8.00% deferrable subordinated debentures, due Sept. 15, 2034. The outlook of these Credit Ratings (ratings) is stable.
