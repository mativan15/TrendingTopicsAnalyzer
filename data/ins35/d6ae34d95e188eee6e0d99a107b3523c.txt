Carnival Corporation (CCL -3.91%) saw its stock price sink 19.8% in May, according to S&P Global Market Intelligence.
The cruise line trailed the S&P 500, which was up 0.2% in May, and the Nasdaq Composite, which dropped 1.9% last month. Carnival is down about 32% year to date, trading at around $13 per share. 
Image source: Getty Images.

So what
Carnival Corporation, which runs Carnival Cruise Lines, was hit as hard by the pandemic as just about any company, along with others in the industry. But things have been looking up for Carnival.
