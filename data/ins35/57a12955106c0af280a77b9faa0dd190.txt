Kochi-based co launches e-marine tech to equip boats with electric modular kits
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-06-04 01:14:00

Kochi: Kochi-based Yesen Sustain, a clean-tech company, has launched a marine technology to equip boats and other watercraft with electric modular kits. The company claims the technology to electrify the existing and new marine craft was being used for the first time in the world. "e-marine technology will help electrify existing and new boats at affordable prices, with a fast return on investment. It will help reduce carbon footprint across the world by revolutionising the way fisheries and water-tourism industries work by replacing the usage of fossil fuels," the company said in a release.
