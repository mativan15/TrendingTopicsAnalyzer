Mumbai special court likely to pass order on Anil Deshmukh's bail plea in corruption case on Friday
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 12:30:00

PTIDeshmukh, who was a minister in the previous Maha Vikas Aghadi (MVA) government comprising Shiv Sena, NCP and Congress, is currently lodged in Arthur Road jail in Mumbai.A special court here on Thursday said it would pass an order on Friday on the bail application filed by former Maharashtra home minister Anil Deshmukh in a case of alleged corruption and misuse of official position. The case is being probed by the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI). Deshmukh (71) had sought bail in the case as soon as the Bombay High Court granted him bail on October 4 in a money laundering case registered against him by the Enforcement Directorate (ED).
