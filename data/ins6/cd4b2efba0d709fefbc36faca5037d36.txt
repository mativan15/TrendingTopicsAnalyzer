Val Kilmer used AI technology to recreate his iconic voice in 'Top Gun: Maverick'
insider.com
news
2022-05-29 17:58:53

After first starring as Admiral Tom "Iceman" Kazansky in the 1986 film "Top Gun," Val Kilmer made his triumphant return, 36 years later, in the sequel "Top Gun: Maverick."Kilmer, 62, was diagnosed with throat cancer in 2014 according to The New York Times Magazine. After going through several treatments, including a tracheotomy, he lost his voice, the outlet reports. Kilmer publicly confirmed his diagnosis in 2017 after denying reports of his illness.Last year the actor worked with a London-based tech company, Sonantic, using AI technology to recreate his iconic voice for the sequel.
