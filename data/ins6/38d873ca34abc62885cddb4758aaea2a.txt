Dublin, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Passenger Information System: Global Markets" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering. The report covers the market for PIS by user base across different regions. It also highlights major trends and challenges that affect the market and the vendor landscape. The passenger information system (PIS) is the operating tool that provides passengers with audio and visual information at stations and transfer facilities, either automatically or manually, at any time.
