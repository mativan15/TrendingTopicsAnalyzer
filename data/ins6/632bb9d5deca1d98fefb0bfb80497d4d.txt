Atlanta, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Atlanta, Georgia - Central Atlanta, GA: National franchise Mighty Dog Roofing just announced a free new digital tool to help homeowners, real estate investors, and commercial business owners near Summit Boulevard in Central Atlanta. The new roofing visualizer is available on the Mighty Dog Roofing website and can be used for free by anyone who accesses the website. To use the visualizer, clients can simply choose an option from the photo gallery that resembles their home or upload their own photos.
