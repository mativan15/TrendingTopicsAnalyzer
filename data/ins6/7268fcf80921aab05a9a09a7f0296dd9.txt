TikTok denies it could be used to track US citizens
bbc.com
business
2022-10-21 04:51:15

Image source, Getty ImagesTikTok has denied a report that a China-based team at its parent company ByteDance planned to use the app to track the locations of US citizens.The social media giant said on Twitter that it has never been used to "target" the American government, activists, public figures or journalists.The firm also says it does not collect precise location data from US users.It was responding to a report in Forbes that data would have been accessed without users' knowledge or consent.
