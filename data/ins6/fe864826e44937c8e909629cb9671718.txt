Arsenal on red alert as Fabian Ruiz makes contract decision and rules out Newcastle move
express.co.uk
sport
2022-05-30 10:29:22

Arsenal may have been handed a major boost regarding their interest in negotiating a move for Fabian Ruiz, who has rejected the offer of a new and improved contract at Napoli, according to reports. The Gunners are believed to be in the hunt to land a new central midfielder ahead of next season and have been credited with a long-standing interest in Ruiz, but have failed to launch a concrete move for his services as of yet.Ruiz has registered 38 appearances for Napoli in all competitions over the course of this season, chipping in with seven goals and five assists in the process.
