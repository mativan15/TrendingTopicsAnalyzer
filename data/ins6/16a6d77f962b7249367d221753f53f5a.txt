By MAO WEIHUA in Urumqi and ZHOU HUIYING | CHINA DAILY | Updated: 2022-06-04 09:49 A clerk counts cash at a bank in Nantong, Jiangsu province. [Photo/Sipa] Targeted measures provide training, employment as massage therapists Over the past decade, more than 350 massage institutions employing the visually impaired in the Xinjiang Uygur autonomous region have received special support funding of over 7 million yuan ($1.05 million), which has been earmarked for the promotion of the massage industry and the employment of visually impaired massage therapists.
