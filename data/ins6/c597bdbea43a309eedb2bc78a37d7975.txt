Annual Stiles Farm Field Day to be June 21 in Thrall

The annual Stiles Farm Field Day scheduled from 5:30-8:30 p.m. June 21 will feature strategies on combating high fertilizer and input costs, plus information on new herbicide technology and hemp production in the Blacklands region. The annual Stiles Farm Field Day scheduled from 5:30-8:30 p.m. June 21. (Texas A&M AgriLife photo by Blair Fannin) The event is sponsored by the Texas A&M AgriLife Extension Service. The Stiles Farm is at 5700 Farm-to-Market Road 1063 in Thrall. There is no cost to attend the event.
