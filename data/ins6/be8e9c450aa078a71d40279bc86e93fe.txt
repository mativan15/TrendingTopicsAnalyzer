Lancaster Ave., Wayne, PA (Holly Herman/Patch Staff)MALVERN, PA —Acme Markets, the Malvern-based supermarket chain, owned by Idaho-based Albertsons Cos, is in the process of being purchased by Kroger, based in Cincinnati, for $2o billion in stock, according to the Philadelphia InquirerThe deal combines the second- and third-largest U.S. grocers — behind Walmart — who together employ over 700,000 workers at 5,000 food markets, nearly 4,000 drugstores, and 2,000 gas stations. Kroger, the buyer, said the company hopes to save an eventual $1 billion by combining food processing, operations and sourcing operations, and adding labor saving technology.
