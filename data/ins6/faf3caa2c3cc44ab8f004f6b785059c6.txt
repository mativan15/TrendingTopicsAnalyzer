Collider.com We're six weeks into fall, and all of social media is exploding with 'Nora Ephron Fall' themed videos and pictures. Whether that's outfits inspired by Nora Ephron characters played by Meg Ryan, or scenes from her movies that take place against the backdrop of an Autumnal New York day, Gen Z is obsessed with Ephron. And why shouldn't they be? Ephron wrote and directed movies with characters everyone can relate to. She gave audiences characters that make them laugh, cry, and reassess the relationships they currently hold.
