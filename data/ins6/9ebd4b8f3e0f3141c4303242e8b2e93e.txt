At Rs 5,330 cr, Axis Bank Q2 net profit rises by 70%
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-20 22:15:28

Private sector lender Axis Bank Thursday reported a 70 per cent growth in its standalone net profit at Rs 5,330 crore in the second quarter ended September 2022 compared to Rs 3,133 crore in the year-ago period. Its net interest income (NII) grew by 31 per cent to Rs 10,360 crore and net interest margin (NIM) stood at 3.96 per cent in the reporting quarter.The bank's gross non-performing assets (GNPA) ratio declined to 2.5 per cent from 3.53 per cent and net NPA reduced to 0.51 per cent from 1.08 per cent.
