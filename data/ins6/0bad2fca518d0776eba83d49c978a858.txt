ReutersPakistan Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif Pakistan Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif's agenda for his maiden visit to China in November is to seek new investments worth billions of dollars and urge Beijing for rescheduling its debt, media reports said. In a further expansion of CPEC, Pakistan will seek a multibillion-dollar financing window from China to fund 10,000 megawatts solar-powered electricity generation projects, media reports said. 
 This comes as Sharif chaired a meeting on Wednesday to review and finalise the agenda for his Beijing visit which is expected to be scheduled for November 1st.
