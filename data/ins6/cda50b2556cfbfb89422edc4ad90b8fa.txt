Govt shortlists top 300 drug brands to introduce QR codes

India's drug pricing authority has shortlisted the top 300 brands of drugs including widely used analgesics, pain relievers, anti-platelet, vitamin supplements, blood-sugar lowering medicines and contraceptive tablets which will have to put QR codes to their packages, in a bid to ensure authenticity and enable tracing. The brands identified by the National Pharmaceuticals Pricing Authority (NPPA) include Dolo, Saridon Fabiflu, Ecosprin, Limcee, Sumo, Calpol, Corex syrup, Unwanted 72 and Thyronorm.
