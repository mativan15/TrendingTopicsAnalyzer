By Reuters Published: 14:25 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 14:25 EDT, 15 October 2022 The Charlotte Hornets are set to waive guard/forward LiAngelo Ball, The Athletic reported Saturday. The middle of the three Ball brothers, the former UCLA signee also was waived prior to the 2020-21 season by the Detroit Pistons, and by the Hornets in advance of last season, and has yet to play an NBA game. Younger brother LaMelo Ball is an All-Star guard with the Hornets, who is entering his third season. Older brother Lonzo Ball is a member of the Chicago Bulls who is currently recovering from a knee injury in advance of his sixth NBA season.
