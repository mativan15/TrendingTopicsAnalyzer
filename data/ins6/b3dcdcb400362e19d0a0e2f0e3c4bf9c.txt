Designer Brands: Fiscal Q1 Earnings Snapshot

news
2022-06-02 10:57:09

COLUMBUS, Ohio (AP) _ Designer Brands Inc. (DBI) on Thursday reported fiscal first-quarter net income of $26.2 million.On a per-share basis, the Columbus, Ohio-based company said it had profit of 34 cents. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs, were 48 cents per share.The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of four analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 23 cents per share.The footwear and accessories retailer posted revenue of $830.5 million in the period, also topping Street forecasts.
