Disneyland Paris is set to open Avengers Campus July 20, 2022 The Walt Disney Company 
The highly anticipated Marvel Avengers Campus will open at Disneyland Paris on July 20, 2022. The new land will allow guests at Walt Disney DIS Studios Park to see their favorite Marvel superheroes and jump into the world of Marvel like never before. According to Disney, 'Marvel Avengers Campus is an entirely reimagined universe dedicated to discovering, recruiting and training the next generation of Heroes, right here, right now in Disneyland Paris.
