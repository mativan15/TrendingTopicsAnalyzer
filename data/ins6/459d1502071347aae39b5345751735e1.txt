BT broadband users can save money if they switch to EE (Image: BT)There's a very simple way to save money on your broadband bill and it's especially enticing for anyone who loves BT. UK mobile network EE has just announced a major sale event which, along with the usual iPhone and SIM-only deals, also includes reduced prices for its internet services. You might not be aware that BT now owns EE after buying the firm back in 2016 and the telecoms giant actually powers EE's broadband network.This new sale means you can get that BT-powered broadband for a much cheaper price than BT itself can currently offer.
