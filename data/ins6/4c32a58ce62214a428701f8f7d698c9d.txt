Kerbal Space Program 2 Begins Early Access in February
ign.com
tech
2022-10-21 15:38:52

The Kerbal Space Program sequel is heading to early access in February next year.The much-anticipated Kerbal Space Program 2 will launch into early access on February 24, 2023, available on Steam, Epic Games Store, and other PC storefronts.'We are so excited to release Kerbal Space Program 2 in Early Access because it will allow Kerbal fans to see for themselves all the amazing progress we've made as we reach the final stages of development,' said creative director Nate Simpson. 'At its heart, KSP2 is about exploration, discovery, and conquering the force of gravity by adding more boosters.
