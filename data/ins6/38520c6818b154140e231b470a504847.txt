A COURT in South Africa has heard why a twisted killer allegedly stabbed his mother to death and then DRANK her blood in a crazed meltdown.
'Vampire killer' Thabang Mosoane, 24, from the town of Koster, in North West province, pleaded guilty to the barbaric May 9 slaying of Kedisaletse Mosoane.
Mosoane drank Kedisaletse Mosoane's blood from a stab wound in her neck after slaying herCredit: News24
The court heard how evil Mosoane - who was described by his sickened aunt as "a problematic child" - allegedly killed his mum after she refused to give him money for cannabis.
