ROSEMONT, IL / ACCESSWIRE / October 21, 2022 / With the future of manufacturing focused on Robotics, Autonomous & Electric Mobility, Artificial Intelligence, Automation, and Factory of the Future thousands of manufacturing professionals and engineers will attend the 10th Anniversary of The ASSEMBLY Show taking place next week at the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center in Rosemont, IL. The trade show and conference, produced by BNP Media, will take place Tuesday, October 25 to Thursday, October 27, 2022 and attendees will have the opportunity to meet with hundreds of exhibiting companies, attend dozens of educational sessions on the show floors and network at several special events.
