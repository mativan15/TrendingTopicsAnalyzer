Jordan Poole signed a new deal with the Golden State Warriors after winning the NBA title (Image: GETTY)Jordan Poole signed a monster new deal to keep him tied to the Golden State Warriors for the foreseeable future - but his contract is riddled with unrealistic incentives that cover vast sums of money. Poole starred for the Warriors alongside Steph Curry and Draymond Green as Golden State won the NBA championship last season.The Warriors have created a winning culture under Steve Kerr over the last decade, with much of their success stemming from their ability to have great players while also developing future stars.
