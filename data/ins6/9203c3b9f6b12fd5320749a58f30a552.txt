BOCA RATON, Fla., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Newtek Business Services Corp., (Nasdaq: NEWT), an internally managed business development company ('BDC'), today announced that Newtek's CEO and President, Barry Sloane, will present at the LD Micro by SRAX Invitational XII at the Four Seasons Westlake Village, California on Wednesday, June 8, 2022 at 9:30 AM PDT (12:30 PM EDT), Track 4. Barry Sloane will be available for one-on-one meetings throughout the day.  To schedule a meeting, please contact your LD Micro representative, or Jayne Cavuoto, Director of Investor Relations for Newtek, at 212-273-8179 or  jcavuoto@newtekone.
