Chief Minister M.K. Stalin on Wednesday made major announcements, allocating funds for improving infrastructure in government schools, laying roads in Chennai and purchasing buses for the State Transport Corporation.
He said in the Legislative Assembly that in last two years, 15 lakh more students had joined government schools because of the improvement in the quality of education. 'In the current year, 6,000 classrooms for panchayat union primary and middle schools would be constructed at a cost of ₹800 crore.
