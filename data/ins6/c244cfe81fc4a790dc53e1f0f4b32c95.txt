COCONUT CREEK, Fla., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Willis Lease Finance Corporation (NASDAQ: WLFC) ('WLFC') has announced it will unite its multiple WLFC businesses under new branding that will more clearly demonstrate the extent of its combined offerings. The new imagery and corporate profile will be unveiled at Aviation Week MRO Europe in London on October 18, 2022, and will include a new graphic 'look and feel' along with a revamped website for integrating all WLFC entities.
