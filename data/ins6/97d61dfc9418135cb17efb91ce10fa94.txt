Katie Horvath, Chief Marketing Officer, Aunalytics. getty In my previous column, Market Smarter: How To Personalize Customer Interactions With Data Analytics, I discussed how consumers expect a personalized experience with brands today. But to truly gain the benefits of personalized interactions with customers and prospects, you have to get your data in order, which is not an easy task. However, as the CMO of a data platform company that offers data analytics and management capabilities, I've found that inconsistent data is a huge problem for marketers in particular.
