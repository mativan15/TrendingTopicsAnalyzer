NEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Wheels Up Experience Inc. (NYSE: UP), the leading brand in private aviation, today announced that its subsidiary, Wheels Up Partners LLC, has issued equipment notes in the aggregate principal amount of $270 million through an EETC (Enhanced Equipment Trust Certificates) loan structure. The sale and issuance of the equipment notes provides approximately $259 million of net proceeds (exclusive of transaction expenses) for Wheels Up.
