ConflictsUnited States of America53 minutes ago53 minutes agoUS prosecutors have busted a "sophisticated" network that facilitated the shipment of semiconductors, radars and other dual-use and military technology that was later recovered on the battlefield in Ukraine.US federal prosecutors charged five Russians and two Venezuelans over their alleged roles in a complex scheme to procure American dual-use and military technology ahead of the invasion of Ukraine, officials said on Wednesday. Federal prosecutors in New York accused some of the defendants of using a German company to transport semiconductors, radars and satellites that could be used in fighter aircraft, missile systems, smart munitions, radar, satellites and other space-based military applications.
