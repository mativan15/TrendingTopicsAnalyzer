The cash will likely be a welcome addition (Image: Christopher Bill/Liverpool Echo)From November 6, millions of people will see more money in their bank accounts. The news comes after former Chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng confirmed a planned national insurance increase will be reversed in his mini-budget earlier this month. New Chancellor Jeremy Hunt confirmed that the reverse would still be going ahead, reports the Liverpool Echo, despite him turning on a number of measures put forward by his predecessor, during an emergency statement on Monday.
