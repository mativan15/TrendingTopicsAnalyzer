May 26, 2022 08:33 PM Eastern Daylight Time NEW ORLEANS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Former Attorney General of Louisiana Charles C. Foti, Jr., Esq. and the law firm of Kahn Swick & Foti, LLC ('KSF') are investigating the proposed sale of VMware, Inc. (NYSE: VMW) to Broadcom Inc. (NASDAQ: AVGO). Under the terms of the proposed transaction, shareholders of VMware will elect to receive either $142.50 in cash or 0.2520 shares of Broadcom for each share of VMware that they own, subject to proration. KSF is seeking to determine whether this consideration and the process that led to it are adequate, or whether the consideration undervalues the Company.
