 RCMP in Rocky Mountain House have laid sexual assault charges against a southern Alberta man.
On Oct. 10, RCMP received a report of a recent sexual assault of a youth.After an investigation, three days later, RCMP arrested and charged 42-year-old Tyler Kanten with sexual assault and sexual interference.
