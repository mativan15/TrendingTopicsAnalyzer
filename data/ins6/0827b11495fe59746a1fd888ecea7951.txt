NEW DELHI: Indian billionaire industrialist and founder of HCL Technologies Shiv Nadar has reclaimed the 'India's most generous' title with an annual donation of Rs 1161 crore, while Wipro's Azim Premji has slipped to the second spot with an annual donation of Rs 484 crore, according to the EdelGive Hurun India Philanthropy List for 2022.The third and the fourth spot were occupied by Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani and Kumar Mangalam Birla, chairman of the Aditya Birla Group, respectively.
