[Photo/Envision AESC] Envision AESC, a battery-making arm of China's Envision Group, said on Wednesday that it has inked a new multi-year partnership with BMW to supply battery cells for the German carmaker's next-generation electric vehicles. To support production, the battery maker, which is headquartered in Japan, is planning to build a dedicated 30GWh battery manufacturing plant in South Carolina, the United States.
