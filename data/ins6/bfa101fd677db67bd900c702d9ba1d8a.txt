1. Sasural Simar Ka Star Vaishali Takkar No More; Suicide Note Alleges Harassment By Ex-BoyfriendTelevision actress Vaishali Takkar is no more. The 26-year-old was known for her roles in 'Sasural Simar Ka' and 'Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai'. She was found dead at her Indore residence on Sunday morning. Police suggest that she passed away by suicide and a note was also recovered. Indore Assistant Commissioner of Police M Rahman has said that they recovered a suicide note which suggests that she was stressed and was being harassed by a former boyfriend, news agency ANI reported.
