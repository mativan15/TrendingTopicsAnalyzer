Philip Morris stock rallies after revenue fell but beat expectations, as heated tobacco shipments jumped
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 11:28:09

Shares of Philip Morris International Inc. PM, +0.19% rallied 1.8% in premarket trading Thursday, after the cigarette and heated tobacco units seller reported third-quarter profit that and revenue that fell from a year ago, as rising costs hurt margins, but beat expectations amid increased pricing and a favorable mix shift to smoke-free products. Operating income fell to $2.97 billion from $3.46 billion and earnings per share dropped to $1.34 from $1.55 a share. Excluding nonrecurring items, adjusted EPS fell to $1.
