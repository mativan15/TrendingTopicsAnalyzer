Gaggan AnandIllustration by João FazendaAware that the music lover cannot live on mushrooms alone, the organizers of this year's Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival enlisted a brigade of chefs to prepare haute cuisine in the Palm Springs desert. One of them was Gaggan Anand, formerly of the Michelin two-star restaurant Gaggan, in Bangkok. 'Twenty years back, festivals had no food, only drugs,' Anand said. He wore a black T-shirt and had a halo of salt-and-pepper hair. 'Now they think food is important, too.
