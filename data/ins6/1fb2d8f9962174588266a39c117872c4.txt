Company directors losing faith in economic outlook
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 23:01:00

Business leaders' confidence in the outlook for the economy has fallen to its lowest level since October 2020.The May 2022 Directors' Economic Confidence Index slid to its lowest level since October 2020 of -45, down from -36 in April.The Institute of Directors, which publishes the index, found that of those who said they were pessimistic about the prospects for the UK economy, the overwhelmingly most frequent reason given (41 per cent) was the rate of inflation, followed by difficulties in Britain's trading relationship with the European Union (20 per cent).
