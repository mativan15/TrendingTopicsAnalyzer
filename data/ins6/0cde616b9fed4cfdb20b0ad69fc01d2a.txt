Indus Towers shares up 0.67% as Nifty gains
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 08:45:00

AgenciesMazhar Mohammad of Chartviewindia.in said technical indicators are turning mixed as the weekly MACD chart has generated a 'buy' signal, giving an edge to the bulls.Shares of Indus Towers Ltd. gained 0.67 per cent to Rs 195.3 in Friday's trade. It hit a high of Rs 195.75 and low of Rs 192.2 during the session. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 215.78, while the 50-DMA was at Rs 196.73. If a stock trades above 50-DMA and 200-DMA, it usually means the immediate trend is upward.
