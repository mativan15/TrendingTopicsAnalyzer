A pilot project in the Eastern Himalayas is set to usher in smarter facilities for raising the living standards of troops posted along the border with China.Army engineers used a mix of materials, including bamboo and other locally available resources, to give shape to a 'first-of-its-kind integrated' post beyond 10,000 ft above mean sea level in the Tawang sector of western Arunachal Pradesh in November 2021.
