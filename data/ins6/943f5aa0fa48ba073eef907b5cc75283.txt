Hospitality warning for diners to book ahead as staffing crisis bites
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-21 23:00:00

As summer approaches the days of strolling up to a restaurant and asking for a table are gone. As Nelson-Tasman prepares for an influx of tourists with borders opened after two years of Covid restrictions, restaurants and bars are warning they they can't find enough staff. With hospitality businesses tretched to the limit, many are preparing to limit hours and seating or risk employee burn-out. 'I'm majorly concerned for the welfare of my people,' The Tides managing director Garry Munro said.
