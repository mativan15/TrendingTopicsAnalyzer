MUNICH, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--MYT Netherlands Parent B.V. (NYSE:MYTE) ("Mytheresa"), the parent company of Mytheresa Group GmbH, today announced the date for the release of its first quarter fiscal year 2023 ended September 30, 2022 financial results and its participation at upcoming investor conferences.

First Quarter Fiscal Year 2023 Earnings Call and Webcast

Mytheresa will release first quarter fiscal year 2023 financial results before the U.S. market open on November 8, 2022. A conference call to discuss its results will follow at 8:00am Eastern Time that same day.
