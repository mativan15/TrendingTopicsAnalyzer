Blink Charging announces plan for manufacturing plant in US after meet with labor head

SouthWorks/iStock via Getty Images Blink Charging (NASDAQ:BLNK) said Tuesday it plans to set up a new manufacturing facility in the U.S. to increase charger production capacity amid growing demand. The move follows a roundtable talk with U.S. Secretary of Labor Marty Walsh. Shares of the EV charging equipment maker rose 2.% postmarket after the announcement. Blink (BLNK) launched a search to locate a facility spanning up to 200K sq. ft. - with potential for expansion - and will create ~300 additional jobs.
