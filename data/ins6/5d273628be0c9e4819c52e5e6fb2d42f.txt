Rep. Stefanik On the GOP's "Huge Opportunity" to Make History
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-17 09:00:35

With just over three weeks until Election Day, Republicans have a chance to make history in the House if they pick up 35 seats — which would lead to 'the largest majority since the Great Depression' sitting in Congress. As several close toss-up races take place throughout the country, it's yet to be known how many seats the GOP will be able to win. However, many Republicans like House Republican Conference Chairwoman Elise Stefanik are confident that Americans will ultimately vote red as concerns about inflation, crime, and border patrol remain at the forefront of their minds.
