Appointment demonstrates Group commitment to help clients go global by leveraging its unique blend of people, technology and scale

CHALFONT ST PETER, England, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--RWS, a unique, world-leading provider of technology-enabled language, content and intellectual property services, today announces that Amanda Newton will lead the company's recently formed Strategic Solutions Group (SSG) as Executive Vice President.

Amanda will hold responsibility for the SSG's growth, client relationships and delivery, and for developing propositions and new solutions that enable clients to reach their customers in any language, or device they choose.
