Oral arguments heard in Lake County sheriff's case against commissioners over contracting for jail medical services
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-06-01 21:27:56

Oral arguments were heard Wednesday in the case Lake County Sheriff Oscar Martinez filed against the Lake County Board of Commissioners over contracting for inmate medical services.Superior Court Civil Division Judge Stephen Scheele heard the case, and among those in attendance were Lake County Board of Commissioners President Michael Repay, D-3rd; Commissioner Kyle Allen, D-1st; Commissioner Jerry Tippy, D-2nd; as well as commissioner's attorney Matt Fech and sheriff's department attorney John Kopack.
