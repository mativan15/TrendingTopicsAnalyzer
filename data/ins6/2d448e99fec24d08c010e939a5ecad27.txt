AM Best Revises Under Review Status to Negative for Credit Ratings of Randall & Quilter Investment Holdings Ltd. and Its Rated Subsidiaries

2022-05-27 00:00:00

May 27, 2022 12:40 PM Eastern Daylight Time LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has revised the implications of the under review status to negative from developing for the Financial Strength Rating of A- (Excellent) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Ratings (Long-Term ICR) of 'a-' (Excellent) of Accredited Surety and Casualty Company, Inc. (ASC) (Orlando, FL), Accredited Specialty Insurance Company (ASI) (Phoenix, AZ) and Accredited Insurance (Europe) Limited (AIEL) (Malta). Concurrently, AM Best has revised the implications of the under review status to negative from developing for the Long-Term ICR of 'bbb-' (Good) of Randall & Quilter Investment Holdings Ltd.
