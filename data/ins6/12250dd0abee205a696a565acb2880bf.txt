Flight ‘took off empty' due to delays at Gatwick
theguardian.com
news
2022-05-31 17:14:01

Passengers booked on a flight from Gatwick to Florence were told the plane had departed empty without them because of delays at the West Sussex airport amid the widespread half-term travel chaos hitting hubs across the UK.The Vueling flight VY6209 had been due to take off at 8.20pm on Monday, but the Airbus A319 – which can carry up to 144 passengers – left nearly two hours late with no customers on board.Nisha Gupta was booked on the trip with her husband, Ash. She told the PA news agency they had to queue for more than four hours to check in luggage, but when they arrived at the departure gate they were told no passengers could board the plane due to a staff shortage.
