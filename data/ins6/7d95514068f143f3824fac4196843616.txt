Asia stocks edge higher, US dollar down
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-30 00:43:30

Asian stocks have followed Wall Street higher while the dollar is pinned near five-week lows as investors wager on an eventual slowdown in US monetary tightening, albeit after sharp hikes in June and July.

Helping to mellow the mood on Monday was news that Shanghai authorities will cancel many conditions for businesses to resume work from Wednesday, easing a city-wide lockdown that began two months ago.

The Memorial Day holiday in the United States could make for a thin session before the end of the month and MSCI's broadest index of Asia-Pacific shares outside Japan edged up 0.
