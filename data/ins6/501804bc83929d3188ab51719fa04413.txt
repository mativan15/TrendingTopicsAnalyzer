Is this the first and only Jew to head a Christian University?

The Worcester Massachusetts-based Assumption University board of trustees announced on Monday that Greg Weiner, Ph.D., has been elected the 17th President of the University. This announcement wouldn't make any major headlines if it wasn't for the fact that Weiner, who has successfully served as Assumption University's Interim President since last April, is a proud and observant Jew. Weiner celebrated a bar mitzvah in an orthodox congregation and is nowadays a member of a Conservative synagogue.
