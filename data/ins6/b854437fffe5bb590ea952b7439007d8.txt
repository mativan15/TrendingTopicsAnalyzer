Ontario Premier Doug Ford speaks as Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau listens on after reaching and agreement in $10-a-day child-care program deal in Brampton, Ont., March 28.Nathan Denette/The Canadian PressOntario is making further changes to its $10-a-day child-care program funding in a bid to boost uptake, with nearly a quarter of operators still undecided about opting in less than two weeks before the deadline.The Ministry of Education sent a memo to licensed child-care providers this week saying that it heard their feedback on a need for stability and consistency, and the new guidelines aim to make it easier for operators to participate in the program.
