 Gold prices on October 19 fell by ₹200 to ₹50,214 per 10 grams in futures trade as speculators reduced their positions.On the Multi Commodity Exchange, gold contracts for December delivery traded lower by ₹200 or 0.4% at ₹50,214 per 10 grams in a business turnover of 13,732 lots.Analysts attributed the fall in gold prices to the trimming of positions by participants.Globally, gold was trading 0.49% lower at $1,647.70 per ounce in New York.
