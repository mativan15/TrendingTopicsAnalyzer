Variety - TV News As Gabriel 'Fluffy' Iglesias marks the 25th anniversary of the start of his career, the comedian reminisces about the origins of his act.In the first episode of Variety's new docuseries 'My Comedy Journey,' created by Variety's Alex Castro and Steven Gaydos, Iglesias visits his hometown of Long Beach, Calif., where he recalls his journey from selling cell phones to becoming a renowned stand-up comedian.'I love how I can drive five minutes and see my childhood,' Iglesias says while driving through his old neighborhood.
