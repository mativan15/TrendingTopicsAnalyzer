Netflix Renews Is It Cake? for Season 2
adweek.com
news
2022-06-02 16:58:33

Is it cake? Well, it's definitely renewed whether or not it's a delicious – and edible – confection of dessert.
Netflix has renewed the hit series Is It Cake? for a second season.
In the series, the world's best bakers compete to produce hyper-realistic cakes that resemble everyday objects with the aim of deceiving a panel of judges and winning money.
Mikey Day will return as host for the second season, along with an all-new cast of participants and guest judges.
The first season aired in March 2022 and spent four weeks on Netflix's global Top 10 English TV list, as well as reaching the Top 10 TV list in 75 countries.
