Like a puppy chasing its tail, some new investors often chase 'the next big thing', even if that means buying 'story stocks' without revenue, let alone profit. In the age of tech-stock blue-sky investing, my choice may seem old fashioned; I still prefer profitable companies like Enphase Energy (NASDAQ:ENPH). While profit is not necessarily a social good, it's easy to admire a business that can consistently produce it.
