SVB Financial Shares Fall 19%, Touch 52-Week Low Friday
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 15:25:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of SVB Financial Group were trading lower on Nasdaq Friday, touching 52-week lows a day after the company released its third-quarter financial results. At 11:16 a.m. ET, the company's stock had fallen 19.61% to trade at $243.54 per share, a 52-week low. Volume at the time topped 2.2 million shares, above the stock's 65-day average volume of 585,365 shares. After the bell Thursday, the company reported third-quarter earnings per share of $7.21. In the year-ago period, the company had reported EPS of $6.
