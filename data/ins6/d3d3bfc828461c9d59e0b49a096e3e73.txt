Putin's aggression in Ukraine teaches us in respect of economics what climate change taught us on the environment: that we've turned a blind eye to the long-term costs of our actions. By historical standards we have enjoyed decades of cheap capital but, like the global economy purchasing energy from regimes who weaponise its supply, it is not a sustainable strategy.This is one of the insights behind the government's plan. A large part of which continues to be about protecting households against rising energy bills.
