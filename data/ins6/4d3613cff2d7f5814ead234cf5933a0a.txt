Romanian policemen protest in BucharestA Romanian policewoman holds a placard during a rally held in front of the Interior Ministry headquarters in Bucharest, Romania, 19 October 2022. Europol trade union gathered about one hundred policemen from all over the country to join a protest demanding that the government should comply with the final court decision regarding salary rights of policemen that sued the ministry for non-compliance with the salary law. Although Romania hopes to join the Schengen Area by the end of this year, Europol union leaders claim that Romanian policemen will have the lowest salaries in the European Union.
