Baby's remains found in backyard during sexual assault probe: police
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-01 08:50:06

The remains of a baby were found in the backyard of a Texas home during an investigation into the sexual assault of a child, according to police.A man being sought by police on suspicion of aggravated sexual assault was later found dead, Lubbock Police Department (LPD) said.The LPD said officers received a report of ongoing sexual assault against a minor at 11:25 a.m. on Monday, May 30.In a Tuesday, May 31, Facebook post, the LPD said the alleged abuse was believed to have been going on for about five years.
