Boris Johnson's big problem pinpointed by Ann Widdecombe
express.co.uk

2022-10-20 21:30:00

Boris Johnson's hopes of making a sensational return to No.10 are likely to be scuppered by the ongoing Parliamentary inquiry against him, Ann Widdecombe has warned. And speaking hours after the resignation of Prime Minister, the former Tory minister branded the situation a 'circus' - and blasted the party's MP for ignoring the wishes of rank-and-file members. I don't think any of us have ever seen anything quite like thisAnn WiddecombeMs Widdecombe, who held several roles in the Government of former Prime Minister Sir John Major, including Employment Minister and Prisons Minister, admitted her bewilderment after yet another an extraordinary day at Westminster.
