Midea signs agreement for science and tech industrial park

By Qiu Quanlin in Guangzhou | chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-05-27 20:09 Midea's booth at an expo in Shanghai, March 14, 2019. [Photo/Sipa] Chinese home appliance manufacturer Midea Group signed an agreement with the Shunde district government of Foshan, Guangdong province, on Friday to develop a digital science and technology industrial park, marking a leap forward in its digitalization of the manufacturing sector. The park, carrying an investment of 10 billion yuan ($1.49 billion), is designed to become a development zone for low-carbon and intelligent manufacturing, according to company sources.
