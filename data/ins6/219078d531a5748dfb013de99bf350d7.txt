da-kuk/iStock via Getty Images The NFT Gaming Company (NFTG) is looking to raise around $7M through an initial public offering to help fund development of its new gaming platform Gaxos. The online game developer said in a filing that it is considering offering 1.7M shares at the assumed price of $4.15 per share. Underwriters would be granted a 45-day option to buy up to 15% additional shares beyond those sold in the offering. The company hopes to list its shares on Nasdaq under the symbol NFTG. EF Hutton is serving as lead bookrunner.
