ByPrashant Jha, Washington





 Amid criticism over misuse of government agencies for political purposes, finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman has said the Enforcement Directorate (ED) is 'completely independent in what it does', outlined the process the ED follows on cases, and said there have been some standout cases where the agency has seized wealth and had to act.'The ED is completely independent in what it does.
