The Hollywood Reporter - Movie News Click here to read the full article.Rubicon TV, the Norwegian production house behind such cross-over drama successes as Lilyhammer, and Beforeigners, is getting into the spy business with its new series E14 Unit for Special Intel.The show, which Rubicon is presenting to potential co-production partners at the international TV market MIPCOM in Cannes this week, looks at the untold story of how Norwegian officers from the country's elite E14 unit played a key role as NATO's frontline spies for NATO in the 1990 Yugoslav War.
