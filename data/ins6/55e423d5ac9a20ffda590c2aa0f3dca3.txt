Published: 21:32 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 21:34 EDT, 14 October 2022 The mayor of New York City was facing questions on Friday night about whether he had declared any income from his vegan cookbook - after he said publishers gave him an advance of up to $50,000.Eric Adams on Friday released his long-awaited tax returns, after receiving an extension from the IRS due to his contracting COVID around the filing deadline.The returns are his first as mayor, and the first since he fired his accountant, who was not licensed, made multiple errors, and registered his business to a non-existent office.
