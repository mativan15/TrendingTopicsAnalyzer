Former One Direction star Liam Payne has caused a stir online over his admission about co-parenting his son with his ex-partner left fans 'speechless'.

In a wide-ranging chat on controversial YouTuber Logan Paul's podcast, Impaulsive, the 28-year-old – who shares four-year-old Bear with his ex, fellow British singer Cheryl – said she'd taken the reins on looking after their child.
