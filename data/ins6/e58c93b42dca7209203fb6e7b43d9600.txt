June 01, 2022 06:42 PM Eastern Daylight Time COLORADO SPRINGS, Colo.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--All Pro Capital, a leading Real Estate Investment firm with an impeccable track record for excellence, announced that it has acquired a stake in Advenire Wealth, LLC, a pioneering RIA providing integrated wealth planning and investment solutions.

In recent years, All Pro Capital has experienced robust growth, but its investors were consistently asking for services and solutions outside of their core business including an option for 1031 Exchange transactions.
