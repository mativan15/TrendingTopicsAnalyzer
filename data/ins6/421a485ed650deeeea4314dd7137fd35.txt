Karim Benzema ready to complete journey from blackmail trial to Ballon d'Or
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 23:01:00

When the 66th Ballon d'Or is handed over to the leading male footballer of the past season in Paris this evening, any gasps among the audience will sound artificial. It looks a shoo-in. The voters, mostly journalists, have almost certainly returned a significant majority in favour of Karim Benzema, the captain of the European and Spanish champions Real Madrid. He was the galvaniser of Madrid's edge-of-the seat comebacks en route to the Champions League title, the standard-bearer for epic nights of entertainment.
