JOYY Inc. slumps 13% to lead mixed day for Asian Internet stocks
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-06-01 17:44:41

ronniechua/iStock via Getty Images Asian Internet companies put in a mixed performance, Wednesday, as JOYY Inc. (NASDAQ:YY) took the spotlight with its shares falling more than 13% as investors showed their dissatisfaction with the Singapore-based social-media platform operators quarterly earnings report. Late Tuesday, JOYY (YY) reported first-quarter revenue of $623.8M, which topped analysts' forecasts for sales of $607.4M. However, JOYY's (YY) revenue slipped by 3% from the same period a year ago, and the company also saw global average monthly users of its platforms decline by 19.
