A blaze at a data center disrupted services at two of South Korea's major tech companies -- Kakao Corp. and Naver Corp. -- keeping their servers down for hours on Saturday.The fire broke out at around 3:30 p.m. local time at SK Inc. C&C's building in Pangyo, south of Seoul where several major software companies are based, according to Yonhap News Agency.
