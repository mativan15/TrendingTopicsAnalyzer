Hello players! Are you ready for a spooky Halloween? Dead by Daylight has arrived in PUBG: Battlegrounds to bring you a Halloween treat. The iconic heart-pumping and spine-chilling Dead by Daylight hide-and-seek mode is now available in the game. Make sure to remember the guide below and survive the deadly night. How to Play Once a match starts, there will be a number of broken generators (3 greater than the number of Survivors) scattered throughout the map.Survivors (three teammates) will have to work together to repair 1 more generator than the number of Survivors to activate switches to the exit gates.
