Coffee drinkers still love Starbucks (SBUX -3.36%). But investors haven't been as excited about the coffee shop giant in recent times. The stock has slipped more than 20% so far this year. Its performance is about in line with that of the S&P 500 Index.
Starbucks faces the challenge of inflationary pressures -- like many companies that depend on consumer spending. But, on a brighter note, Starbucks recently set out a new growth plan. So, it's fair to say the company has reached a big inflection point.
