Starting from Malwa, field fires hit new max in Punjab
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 09:30:00

BATHINDA/CHANDIGARH: Punjab 's 436 cases of paddy stubble burning on Wednesday were highest of the harvesting season, taking its tally to 2,625, as Malwa starts its blazes. In just five days, the state has recorded 1,556 field fires, which is more than 59% of the 34-day count. The last maximum was 403 on Monday. Except Pathankot , all other 22 districts recorded stubble burning.The October 19 figure of 436 fires was 698 in 2020 and 496 in 2021. The tally till October 19 is now 2,625 but it was 7,115 in 2020 and 2,942 in 2021.
