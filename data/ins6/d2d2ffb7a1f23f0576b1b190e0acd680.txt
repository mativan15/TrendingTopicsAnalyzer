Shares of surged nearly 10% Friday to a new high as analysts raised their price targets on the private lender on the back of better-than-expected second quarter earnings. The stock closed at '900.25 on the BSE, up 8.96% from Thursday's close, before touching an all-time high of '905 per share. A little more than six crore shares exchanged hands on the BSE and NSE compared with a combined average volume of 1 crore shares on both exchanges in the past month. Most analysts raised their price targets on the stock with a median target of '1,019.
