Alzheimer Society of N.L. ramping up fundraising as demand for services increases

Shirley Lucas is the CEO of the Alzheimer Society in Newfoundland and Labrador. She hopes this weekend's event will help the organization reach their fundraising goal of $60,000. (CBC )The Alzheimer Society of Newfoundland and Labrador says there has been a significant increase in demand for its services since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, and fundraising is now ongoing to help meet that demand.Shirley Lucas, the CEO of the Alzheimer Society of Newfoundland and Labrador, says the number of calls the society receives has increased by 55 per cent over the past few years.
