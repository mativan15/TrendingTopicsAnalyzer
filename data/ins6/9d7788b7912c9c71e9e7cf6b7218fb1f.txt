Misha Kaminsky/E+ via Getty Images Cannabis then and now A year ago the cannabis sector was speeding ahead at full throttle. Sales were up sequentially each quarter, capital was plentiful, expansion was rapid across the country, and it seemed the sky was the limit. Although stock prices had come off their highs, many investors were still way ahead. Market veterans knew that times to test investors were over the horizon, and here we are. Earnings reports from the first quarter of 2022 across the sector revealed a number of headwinds: revenue and customers down, sequential sales declines, margin compression, profits turning into losses, and more.
