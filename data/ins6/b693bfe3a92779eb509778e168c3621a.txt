Getty Images Although it falls right between the big holiday shopping weekends of Memorial Day and the Fourth of July, the month of June has plenty of sales of its own to offer savvy shoppers. Many department and chain stores host 'semiannual sales' in June, offering sizable discounts in an effort to move inventory and as the first half of the year comes to a close. Plus, Father's Day means that stores may offer additional discounts on items traditionally purchased as gifts for dads. However, those price drops typically come very close to the holiday itself, which falls on June 19 this year.
