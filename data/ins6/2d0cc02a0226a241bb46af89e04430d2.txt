Man Dies Of Severe Bleeding After Quack's Wife Extracts His Tooth
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 11:21:01

A businessman in India died of severe bleeding after he attended an illegal health facility for tooth extraction. The man who approached a quack's facility to get his tooth extracted was treated by the latter's wife, reports said.The tragic incident happened in Malkangiri, a city in the eastern Indian state of Odisha, after the victim, Tapan Pal, went to meet the unauthorized practitioner, Rabindranath Biswas, with complaints of a toothache, news outlet The New Indian Express reported Sunday.When Pal reached the facility, Biswas was not available, and he was attended to by the latter's wife, identified by her first name Basanti.
