Makers of the upcoming horror comedy film 'Phone Bhoot' on Tuesday unveiled the second song of the film 'Kaali Teri Gutt'. Taking to Instagram, Katrina dropped the song which she captioned in Hindi, "Kaali teri gutt te paranda tera laal ni, roop di ye raaniye parande nu sambhal ni #KaaliTeriGutt out now."
