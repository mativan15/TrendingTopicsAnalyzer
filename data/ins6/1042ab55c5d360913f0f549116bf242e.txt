NFL Black Friday Game Coming to Amazon in 2023
nbclosangeles.com
news
2022-10-18 11:58:08

Amazon will stream the first ever Black Friday NFL game in 2023. The game will take place the day after Thanksgiving at 3 p.m. ET on the online retailer's Prime Video platform. This places a game with potentially millions of U.S. viewers in the middle of the massive brick-and-mortar retail holiday. Amazon will stream the first ever Black Friday NFL game in November 2023, the company revealed in a joint announcement with the National Football League on Tuesday.
