Southgate in line for £4million bonus if Three Lions win World Cup
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 22:10:30

ENGLAND manager Gareth Southgate is in line for a £4million bonus if he can lead the Three Lions to World Cup glory.
It is almost three times as much as the £1.5million he was offered to win the 2018 World Cup.

Southgate, 52, has already banked £500,000 for qualifying for the tournament, which kicks off five weeks today.
