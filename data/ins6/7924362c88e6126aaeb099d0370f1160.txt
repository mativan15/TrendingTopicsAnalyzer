In The ZoneBy Mike TozziOct 18, 2022In the Orlando area, there is a lot of buzz brewing for the upstart Magic after winning four straight games to finish the preseason, but there are so many storylines throughout the league and you could make a case that it is one of the most intriguing seasons in a long time. You know it's a promising NBA season when even the rebuilding teams excite you, plus you have the likes of Kawhi Leonard and Zion Williamson back on the floor which can make the Clippers and Pelicans big time contenders.
