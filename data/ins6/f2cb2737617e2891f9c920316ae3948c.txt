Costa customers FURIOUS as coffee chain hikes prices AGAIN
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-06-02 13:48:36

CAFFEINE lovers are steaming at Costa Coffee for putting up its drinks prices for the second time in six months - with some rising by a whopping 12 per cent.
Fans of the chain found a flat white increased overnight from £2.95 to £3.30, medium skinny lattes from £3.10 to £3.35 and small cappuccinos from £3.15 to £3.50.
The coffee shop has put up prices againCredit: Rex
The increase varies depending on the drink and size, but the average is 14p, Costa said. Prices vary by branch. 
Coffee machines in branches have gone up by 20p, others reported.
