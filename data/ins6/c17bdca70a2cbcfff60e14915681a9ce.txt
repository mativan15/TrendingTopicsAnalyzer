Dulcie's passion for Mount Druitt drives 50 years of raising money for a hospital cherished by the community
abc.net.au
business
2022-10-16 02:21:19

Queen Elizabeth II opened Mount Druitt hospital in 1982.But for more than a decade beforehand, hardworking female volunteers were raising funds trying to make it a possibility.Forty years later, the group's founding treasurer Dulcie Harrison is still running the fundraising for medical equipment with the ladies committee.Since forming in 1973, the committee has raised more than $1 million through raffles, street stalls and a gift shop.The money has been used to buy equipment such as exercise bikes and lifting devices used in rehabilitation.
