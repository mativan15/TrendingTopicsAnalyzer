Tower block catches fire in Istanbul
calcuttanews.net
news
2022-10-16 00:45:00

The blaze ripped through the 24-story residential buildingAn apartment tower caught fire in the Turkish city of Istanbul on Saturday night, setting all 24 floors ablaze and covering the city in a dense cloud of smoke. The fire occurred in the same district hit by an explosion last week.The fire broke out in a building in the Fikirtepe district of the city, Oda TV reported. After starting on one of the apartment block's lower floors, it quickly climbed to the top of the building, the Turkish network added.
