Mobility tech company ECARX merges with COVA Acquisition
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-29 17:45:00

ECARX develops hardware and software solutions that are essential for the development of connected, automated and electrified mobility.New Delhi: ECARX Holdings, a global mobility tech company, has entered into a merger agreement with COVA Acquisition Corp. Upon completion of the transaction ECARX expects to be listed on Nasdaq under ticker symbol "ECX", the company said in a release. The closing of the transaction is expected to occur in the fourth quarter of 2022.The transaction represents a combined company pro forma equity value of approximately USD 3.
