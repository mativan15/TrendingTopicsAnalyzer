TORONTO, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jamieson Wellness Inc. ("Jamieson Wellness" or the "Company") (TSX: JWEL) today announced it has signed a definitive agreement to acquire Nutrawise Health & Beauty Corporation ("Nutrawise"), a leading innovator, manufacturer and marketer of premium supplements under the youtheory brand in the United States and other international markets, for approximately US$210 million ($265 million) on closing, plus potential additional consideration contingent on achieving pre-determined growth targets post-closing.
