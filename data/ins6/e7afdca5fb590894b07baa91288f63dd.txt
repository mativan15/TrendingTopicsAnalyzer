FT Executive MBA Ranking 2022: methodology

2022-10-16 17:00:05

This is the 22nd edition of the FT's annual ranking of the world's top 100 executive MBA programmes for senior working managers.Participation in the ranking is voluntary and at the business school's request. EMBA programmes must meet certain criteria to be eligible. First, the school must be accredited by either the US's Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business, or Europe's Equis. The EMBA must also be cohort-based, with students enrolling and completing their studies together.A total of 138 programmes took part in the 2022 ranking process, including 13 joint programmes delivered by more than one school.
