June 02, 2022 08:35 AM Eastern Daylight Time OLDWICK, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best will exhibit and host a networking reception at the Florida Association of Insurance Agents' (FAIA) 2022 Convention & Education Symposium, which will take place June 15-17, 2022, in Orlando, FL.



Visitors to the AM Best booth, which is No. 424, can learn about the rating agency, its role in the insurance industry and the resources it offers to insurance professionals, including Best's Credit Ratings, insurance data and analysis resources and financial data products.
