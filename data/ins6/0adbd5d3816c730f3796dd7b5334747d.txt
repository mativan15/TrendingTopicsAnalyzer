Ted Budd, R-N.C., joined 'Mornings with Maria,' Wednesday, to discuss the nationwide struggle with inflation, which recently prompted the IRS to adjust its tax brackets. The GOP congressman told host Maria Bartiromo that Biden's ‘American Rescue Plan' has actually made inflation ‘worse' for the American people, especially working-class families.
