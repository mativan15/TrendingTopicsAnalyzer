AgenciesCurrently, Annapurna Group has three plants, all of which are present in Guwahati and one plant in Meerut. Assam based FMCG major Annapurna Group, plans to tap the national market in a phased manner. It has readied a war chest of Rs 300 crore for making foray in national market. The group has plans to go for IPO. The group has presence in West Bengal. The group deal in Dairy, Health Drinks, Fruit Drinks, Juices & Agro-based products. Subir Ghosh, Managing Director, Annapurna Group said that the Rs 300 Crore Group, which has concentrated big time on Research & Development in the recent past will be investing Rs 300 crore by 2025 for their expansion - in terms of foraying in new markets, new product development and marketing initiatives.
