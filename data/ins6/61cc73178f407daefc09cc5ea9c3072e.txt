Man fights crocodile with knife as it held his friend's head in its jaws
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 13:35:05

A man fought a crocodile with a knife as it held his friend's head in its jaws during an attack in Australia.The men were on a fishing vessel in Cyclone Creek in the Kimberly region of Western Australia on October 17 when the attack occurred, a spokesperson for Horizontal Falls Seaplane Adventures told 7News.The two men were crew members of a tour operator and were not showing guests around the area at the time of the incident. One man was bitten on the head by the predator while the other attempted to rescue him, an eyewitness to the incident told 7News.
