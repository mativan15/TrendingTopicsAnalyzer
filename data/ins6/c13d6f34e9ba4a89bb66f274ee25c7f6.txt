Leisure, business travel to Jacksonville expected to surpass pre-pandemic levels this year
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 05:37:00

Jacksonville is poised to have both business and leisure travel fully rebound by the end of 2022, according to recently released research by the The American Hotel & Lodging Association.

The study, conducted by industry tracking firm Kalibri Labs, compares projected 2022 hotel revenue for both leisure and business travel to revenue in 2019.

In Jacksonville, hotel revenue for business and leisure travel combined is projected to be $622.4 million for 2022, up 9.93% from 2019.
