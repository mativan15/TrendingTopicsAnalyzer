You stop judging all the shortcuts and workarounds that make parenting easier once you actually become one, like Takara Tomy's new AI-powered smart speaker that can imitate a parent's voice at bedtime and read those children's books and fairy tales most parents are sick of reading over and over.For a few years now, we've been aware of the threat that deepfake technology poses, particularly when used to imitate someone in a position of power or influence. In 2019, scammers successfully used a deepfake voice copy of an energy firm's CEO to fool an employee into transferring $243,000 USD to them, and in 2021 the stakes were raised even higher when an AI-cloned voice was used as part of a $35 million heist.
