Increase in quota is to uphold justice: CM
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-17 14:19:17

The government has taken a decision to hike reservation for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes to uphold social justice, said Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai.
Addressing a group of SC/ST Community leaders of Karkala Assembly constituency led by Energy, Kannada and Culture Minister V. Sunil Kumar here on Monday, he said the demand to hike reservation has been pending for the last 50 years. Mr. Bommai said this decision will ensure social justice to oppressed classes in the society, which are denied of education and employment.
