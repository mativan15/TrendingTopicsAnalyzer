Electronics Mart India makes a strong D-Street debut, lists at 53% premium
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 05:15:00

New Delhi: Electronics Mart India (EMIL) made a strong debut on Dalal Street as shares of the consumer durables and electronics retailer got listed at Rs 90 apiece on NSE in Monday's session, a premium of 52.5 per cent over its issue price of Rs 59. The shares debuted at a premium of 51.5 per cent at Rs 89.40 as against the given issue price on BSE. Ahead of its listing, the company's shares were being exchanged at a premium of Rs 30 in the grey market despite high volatility in the secondary market.
