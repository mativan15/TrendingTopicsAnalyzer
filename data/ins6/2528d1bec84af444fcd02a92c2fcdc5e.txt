October 19, 2022 12:01 AM Eastern Daylight Time LONDON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Inmarsat's ground-breaking new SwiftJet inflight connectivity service for the business aviation market has passed an important milestone, after industry heavyweights Satcom Direct and Honeywell were named as the first global distribution partners.



The announcement comes less than five months after SwiftJet was launched as the world's fastest business aviation inflight connectivity service over L-band, receiving an extremely positive response from the market.
