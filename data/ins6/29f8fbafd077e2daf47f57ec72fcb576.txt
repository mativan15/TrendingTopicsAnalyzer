Jyoti Gondek reflects on first year as Calgary's mayor
globalnews.ca
news
2022-10-18 13:20:27

Looking back at the first year of her term as Calgary's mayor, Jyoti Gondek feels there is a growing sense of optimism in the city.
'We've seen so many amazing things happen. We've had festivals return, people are excited about Calgary,' Gondek told Global News. 'We're just seeing a lot of optimism and it's nice to be part of that.'On Oct 18, 2021, more than 393,000 Calgarians lined up to cast their ballot in a municipal election that would see an historic turnover on city council.Gondek emerged victorious 26 other candidates with 45 per cent of the vote, and became the first woman elected to the mayor's office in the city's history.
