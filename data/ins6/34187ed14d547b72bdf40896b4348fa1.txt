Pokemon Scarlet Violet extended gameplay preview

Nintendo invited us to a preview even for their and developer Game Freak's latest endeavor in the widely popular monster-catching universe. Pokemon Scarlet and Violet dive even deeper into an open world design, with new features like autobattling and a cooking minigame designed to provide temporary boosts to stats. But how to these features all look in game? Take a look at some never before seen extended gameplay.
