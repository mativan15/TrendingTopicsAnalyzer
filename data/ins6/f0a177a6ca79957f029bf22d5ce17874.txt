Crompton Greaves share price up 0.67 per cent
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-06 06:45:00

ReutersNifty continues to climb a wall of worry as it looks to be in the final leg of its rally before heading for a correction.Shares of Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Ltd. traded 0.67 per cent up at Rs 366.8 at around 11:27AM (IST) on Monday. The stock had closed at Rs 364.35 in the previous session. Equity benchmark Sensex traded 281.86 points lower at 55487.37 around the same time. The scrip has declined 8.35 per cent in the last one year till date, while the 30-share index has gained 7.
