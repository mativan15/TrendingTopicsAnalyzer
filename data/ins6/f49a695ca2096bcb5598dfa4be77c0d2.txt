American Virtual Cloud Technologies Shares Down 25% on Direct Offering, Private Placement
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 15:56:00

By Kathryn Hardison Shares of American Virtual Cloud Technologies Inc. dropped 25%, to $1.69, on Tuesday after it said it has agreed to a $10 million registered direct offering and concurrent private placement. The company said Tuesday that it has entered into definitive agreements with institutional investors for the purchase and sale of five million shares of its common stock, pursuant to a registered direct offering priced at-the-market, and warrants to purchase up to 10 million shares in a concurrent private placement.
