Prog to expand tree coverage launched
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 07:30:00

Guwahati: The Assam government and the US Agency for International Development ( USAID ) on Tuesday announced the launch of the 'Trees Outside Forests in India ( TOFI )' program in the state, which will bring together farmers, companies and private institutions to rapidly expand tree coverage outside of traditional forests in the state.Announcing the launch, state forest minister Chandra Mohan Patowary , said, 'Agroforestry is intimately associated with the Assamese civilization and we are very delighted to launch the TOFI program in Assam.
