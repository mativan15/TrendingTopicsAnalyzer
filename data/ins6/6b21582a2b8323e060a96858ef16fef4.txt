October 18, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cepton, Inc. ('Cepton') (Nasdaq: CPTN), a Silicon Valley innovator and leader in high performance lidar solutions, announced today it will provide a business update and release its financial results for the third quarter of 2022 following the close of the market on Tuesday, November 8, 2022. Cepton will hold a conference call and webcast at 2:30 p.m. PT (5:30 p.m. ET) that same day.
