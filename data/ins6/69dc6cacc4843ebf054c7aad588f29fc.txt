Nicole Kidman surprises Keith Urban during concert, calls herself 'Nicole Urban'

Keith Urban fans were treated to a sweet onstage interaction between the country singer and Nicole Kidman when the Oscar-winning actress made a guest appearance during one of his recent shows.Urban, 54, shared a video of the aww-worthy moment on Instagram, captioning it "...just a Saturday night in Vegas!" He was performing at The Colosseum at Caesars Palace."What's your name? Where are you from?" the country superstar asked Kidman, 54, when she walked onstage and gave him a kiss. Kidman replied that her name was "Nicole Urban," which appeared to take Urban aback in the best way possible.
