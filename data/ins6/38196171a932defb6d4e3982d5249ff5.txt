Who is Shruti Sharma? UPSC topper who scored 340 out of 400 points
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 12:45:00

On May 30th, UPSC announced the final results of the 2021 Civil Services Examination. Shruti Sharma topped, followed by Ankita Agarwal, Gamini Singla, and Aishwarya Verma. Union Minister, Dr Jitendra Singh, congratulated the candidates, marvelling at the top four, saying, "Women power at its best!" It is a proud feeling to see girls doing so well in all the areas of life and education. The complete list of all the candidates who made the cut can be found on the official website of UPSC. Six hundred eighty-five candidates are shortlisted for various civil services fields under the Central Government of India, like IAS, IPS, and IFS.
