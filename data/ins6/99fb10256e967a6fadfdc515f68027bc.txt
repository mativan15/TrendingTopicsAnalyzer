The weather office said that the southwest monsoon will reach Kerala in next 2-3 days. In a series of tweets, the IMD said that the conditions are becoming favourable for the advancement of monsoons. 
"The conditions are becoming favorable for monsoon onset over Kerala during next 2-­3 days. The conditions are also favourable for further advance of Southwest Monsoon into some more parts of Arabian Sea and Lakshadweep area during the same period," the IMD said.
