About The Author : Daniel Payne

Daniel Payne is a health care reporter at POLITICO. Before becoming a health care reporter, he was a fellow at POLITICO.
Before POLITICO, Daniel worked to push The DeSoto Times-Tribune toward a digital-first approach as the Mississippi newspaper's executive editor. He emphasized aggressive accountability reporting on the pandemic and redesigned the newspaper, newsletters and social media accounts. A graduate of the University of Mississippi, Oxford, Daniel was editor-in-chief of The Daily Mississippian, where he also helped overhaul the newspaper's website and workflow.
