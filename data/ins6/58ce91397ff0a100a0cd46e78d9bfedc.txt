GOOGLE Chrome fans are being urged to protect their personal data by taking the browser's latest crime-busting tool for a spin.
"Privacy Guide" is a simple, step-by-step guided tour of some existing privacy and security controls in Chrome.
Privacy Guard began rolling out to users in recent weeksCredit: Google
It allows you to shore up your account's security without having to trawl through the browser's settings.
Users are guided through Chrome's controls for cookies and browsing history.
Changing those controls restricts the data that Google and the websites you visit store about you.
