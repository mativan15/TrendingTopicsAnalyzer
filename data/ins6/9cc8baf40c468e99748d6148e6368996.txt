October 17, 2022 03:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time CARLSBAD, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Upper Deck, the worldwide leader in entertainment and sports collectibles, today launched its third-annual ‘My MVP' contest to find the most passionate National Hockey League (NHL) fans. For the first time, Upper Deck will identify an MVP for every team in the league.



'An MVP can be the person who hypes up the crowd with a crazy dance on the big screen or a mega collector of team memorabilia and cards,' said Paul Nguyen, Upper Deck's Senior Marketing Manager.
