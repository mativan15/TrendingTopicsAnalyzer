AgenciesIn 2022, the RBI was quoted as saying that more than 101.9 per cent of Rs 500 currency notes and 54.16 per cent of Rs 2,000 notes that reached the bank, are fake, he said.Congress leader Rahul Gandhi on Tuesday cited the demonetisation exercise of 2016 to attack Prime Minister Narendra Modi, saying this "dictatorial decree of a king" caused grave harm to the people and the country will never forget the "pain". In a Facebook post in Hindi, Gandhi said that on November 8, 2016, the people of the country were made to stand in queues in the name of demonetisation.
