HV Bancorp Closes Deal to Merge With Citizens Financial Services
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 13:10:00

By Sabela Ojea Citizens Financial Services Inc. and HV Bancorp, Inc. on Wednesday said they signed a definitive merger agreement aimed at combining First Citizens Community Bank with HV Bancorp's Huntingdon Valley Bank. Under the terms of the agreement, HV Bancorp's shareholders would be entitled to receive for each share either $30.50 in cash or 400 shares of Citizens Financial's common stock, or around $67.4 million in the aggregate. This deal is expected to enhance and expand Citizens Financial's presence in Pennsylvania, Delaware and New Jersey, the company said.
