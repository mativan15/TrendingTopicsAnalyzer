There are some financial gurus who preach asceticism during debt payoff. Eat cheap ramen noodles, sit on the floor of your bare apartment (it's bare because you sold off most of your possessions to scrape together a few hundred dollars to send to your debt) lit by a single 60-watt lightbulb, and feel ashamed of all your financial missteps. You are not allowed to buy yourself nice things.I've gotta say, these are not optimum conditions to make a positive and lasting change to your life. I instead spent $2,555 of my hard-earned dough on several nice purchases to improve my work and my quality of life.
