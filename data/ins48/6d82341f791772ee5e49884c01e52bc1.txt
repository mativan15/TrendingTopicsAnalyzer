GoNetspeed coverage area in Bath, Brunswick and Topsham. 'The importance of high speed, reliable Internet access has never been more evident than it is right now,' commented GoNetspeed CEO Richard Clark. NEW GLOUCESTER, Maine (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 GoNetspeed, a high-speed fiber Internet provider, is actively building in Bath, Brunswick, and Topsham. GoNetspeed is collectively investing over $6.7 Million into these communities, providing a reliable connectivity option for residents and businesses in Cumberland County.
