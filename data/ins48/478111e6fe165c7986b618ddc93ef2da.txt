BEIJING, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Kaixin Auto Holdings ('Kaixin' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: KXIN) announces today that it has hired Mr. Lei Gu as the strategic advisor to lead development of its new energy vehicle business. Before joining Kaixin, Mr. Gu was senior technical expert with Ford Motor and had served as CTO and vice president of BAIC Motor, a Fortune 500 company, president of BAIC Motor Engineering Research Institute, board director and CEO of BAIC and Mercedes Benz Technology Center, president of Chery Automobile Engineering Research Institute, and president of Linktour Motor.
