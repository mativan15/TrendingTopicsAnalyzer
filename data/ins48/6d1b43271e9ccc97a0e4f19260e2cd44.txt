Fangdd received Nasdaq notification regarding minimum market value deficiency
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-21 12:34:22

Fangdd Network (NASDAQ:DUO) has received Nasdaq notification that it is not in compliance with the minimum market value requirement. The company no longer meets the minimum market value requirement based on the market value of for the 30 consecutive business days from September 8, 2022 to October 19, 2022. The company has been provided 180 calendar days, or until April 18, 2023, to regain compliance. In the event that the company does not regain compliance by April 18, 2023, the company may be eligible for additional time to regain compliance, or may face delisting.
