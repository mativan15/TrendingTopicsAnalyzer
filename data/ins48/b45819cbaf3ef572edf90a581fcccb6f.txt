Dublin, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Digital Pen Market by Technology, by Operating System, by End User - Global Opportunity Analysis and Industry Forecast, 2022-2030" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.



The Global Digital Pen Market size valued to USD 569.7 million in 2021, shall garner up-to USD 1.54 billion by 2030, with a growing CAGR of 11.6% from 2022-2030.

Digital Pen is an electronic input device which captures the analog information as well as the brush strokes created by the user into digital data, in conjunction with smart gadgets like smartphones, tablets, digital paper and others.
