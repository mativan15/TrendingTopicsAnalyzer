Veteran actor Bo Hopkins has passed away in hospital in LA, his wife has confirmed. The star suffered a heart attack earlier in the monthBo Hopkins has died aged 80Veteran actor Bo Hopkins has died at the age of 80 after suffering a heart attack. The star passed away in hospital in Van Nuys, Los Angeles with his wife Sian confirming to The Hollywood Reporter he had had a heart attack on May 9. The star had an illustrious career spanning seven decades and was best known for his role in American Graffiti, where he played the role of gang leader Joe.
