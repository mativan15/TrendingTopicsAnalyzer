Published: 17:37 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 17:39 EDT, 31 May 2022 Real Madrid have made an improved £68 million offer for Monaco midfielder and Liverpool transfer target Aurelien Tchouameni.Ligue 1 champions Paris Saint-Germain are also chasing the 22-year-old's signature, with Monaco earmarking Brighton's Yves Bissouma as a potential replacement should he depart.The Frenchman operates predominantly as a defensive-minded central midfielder and could be Real Madrid's long-term plan to replace 37-year-old Luka Modric in the engine room.
