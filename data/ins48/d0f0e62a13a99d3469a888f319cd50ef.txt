ТАMPA, Fla., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- MANTA, the data lineage platform, today announced it has partnered with global SaaS software company MEGA International to deliver comprehensive data lineage and governance solutions to its customers and partner organizations. The complexity of today's ecosystems has left many organizations struggling to navigate the expanding, highly interconnected data environments, resulting in slower data delivery, increased number of incidents, and unreliable business insights.
