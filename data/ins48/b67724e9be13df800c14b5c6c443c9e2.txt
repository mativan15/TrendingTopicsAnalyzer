alvarez/E+ via Getty Images One rule of thumb when it comes to investing that some investors overlook is that it's necessary to distinguish between a good company and a company that is worth investing in. A company might be a decent, good, or even excellent operator, but if shares are trading at levels that don't make sense fundamentally, then investors might just be putting their capital somewhere that will either lose the money or not make them the return that they want. One example of a good company that looks either fairly valued or overpriced today is Waters Corporation (NYSE:WAT).
