Finally, after a month of its premiere in theatres, Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore is available on OTT now. You can start watching it from May 30th on HBO Max exclusively. The movie is the third prequel spinoff of Harry Potter, the best selling book series by J.K Rowling. The spinoff series of Fantastic Beasts is expected to extend to five movies. Jude Law does justice to the character of professor Albus Dumbledore. Dumbledore entrusts Newt Scamander, the magizoologist played by Eddie Redmayne, to assemble a team consisting of witches, wizards and a Muggle, played by Dan Fogler.
