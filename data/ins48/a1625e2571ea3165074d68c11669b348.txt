The past year for Luxfer Holdings (NYSE:LXFR) investors has not been profitable
yahoo.com

2022-10-20 19:19:56

You can invest in an index fund if you want to make sure your returns approximately match the overall market. By comparison, an individual stock is unlikely to match market returns - and could well fall short. For example, that's what happened with Luxfer Holdings PLC (NYSE:LXFR) over the last year - it's share price is down 27% versus a market decline of 24%. However, the longer term returns haven't been so bad, with the stock down 6.4% in the last three years. On the other hand the share price has bounced 5.
