Jun. 03, 2022 8:35 AM ETAllianz SE (ALIZF)By: Max Gottlich, SA News Editor Sean Gallup/Getty Images News Allianz (OTCPK:ALIZF) is expecting to take a hit of EUR 400M ($430M) to profit after the German-based insurer agreed to sell a majority stake in its Russian operations to Interholding amid the war in Ukraine, according to a release. Upon closing of the deal, Allianz (OTCPK:ALIZF) will own 49.9% in the combined company, the company said, adding that the deal won't impact its solvency capitalization and cash position.
