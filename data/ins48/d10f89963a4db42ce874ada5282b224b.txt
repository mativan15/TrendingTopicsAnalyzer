Clean environment and South-South cooperation top the agenda as Antonio Guterres, secretary-general of the United Nations (UNSG), begins his visit to India from Tuesday, the first to the country since the start of his second term in office in January. Guterres had earlier made a trip to the country during his first term in 2018. The UNSG will begin his October 18-20 programme in India by paying tributes to the victims of the 26/11 terror attacks at the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel in Mumbai.
