Lanxess Shares Jump on Advent JV to Buy DSM Unit
marketwatch.com
news
2022-05-31 07:53:00

By Ed Frankl Lanxess AG shares jumped on Tuesday after it said that it would join with private-equity firm Advent International Corp. to buy Royal DSM NV's engineering-materials business for around 3.7 billion euros ($3.99 billion), creating a new venture for high-performance plastics, and said that it would start a new share buyback. At 0745 GMT, Lanxess shares were up 11.5% to EUR43.51. The German specialty-chemical company said it would also transfer its own high-performance polymers business into the joint venture, and receive EUR1.
