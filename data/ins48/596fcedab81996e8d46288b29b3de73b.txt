David Hill assumes Deloitte's Asia Pacific CEO role, succeeding Cindy Hook in overseeing 68,000 professionals in 19 geographies.

HONG KONG, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Today, David Hill assumes the role of Asia Pacific Chief Executive Officer (CEO) for Deloitte, succeeding Cindy Hook who has served in the role since 2018.
