The Election Commission has suspended sheep distribution programme in Munugode Assembly constituency till the counting of votes for by-election.  Neither direct benefit transfer nor physical distribution of sheep to beneficiaries could be taken up till then, sources said.In fact, the second phase distribution of sheep under the programme had commenced on October 3, the day when the notification for poll was issued. About 3.5 lakh units of sheep were distributed to beneficiaries in the first phase and another 3.
