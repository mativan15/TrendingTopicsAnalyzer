Geolocation tech company TomTom is cutting 10% of its global workforce.

The layoffs are concentrated in its maps unit, due to improvements in TomTom's mapmaking automation.

500 employees will be affected by the restructuring, according to a press release.

Geolocation tech firm TomTom is laying off 500 employees in its maps unit after engineering investments improved its automated mapmaking platform, the company said.
