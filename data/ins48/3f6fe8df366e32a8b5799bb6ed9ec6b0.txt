Yerevan, Armenia, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- In the bustling tech hub that is Armenia, the typically male-dominated IT industry is experiencing a shift. Women are climbing the ranks in companies like Synergy Armenia, Renderforest, and Resal Soft — paving the way for young women to break into the IT sector. See how male employers are meeting the moment to help close Armenia's gender gap in this issue of AGBU Magazine.
