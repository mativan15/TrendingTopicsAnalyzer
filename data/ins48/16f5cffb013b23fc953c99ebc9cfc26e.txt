Cognyte Software to Sell Some Assets to Volaris for at Least $47.5 Million
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 12:45:00

By Dean Seal Cognyte Software Ltd. has agreed to sell part of its Threat Intelligence Analytics offering to the Volaris Group for $47.5 million plus up to $35 million in performance-based earn-outs. Security-analytics-software-company Cognyte said Wednesday that it is divesting the assets of its Situational Intelligence Solutions business, which is focused on security implications for physical facilities and employees. The assets include products and intellectual property, along with customer contracts and employees.
