Gama Aviation Acquires US Hangar Facility for $3.5 Mln
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 10:02:00

By Michael Susin Gama Aviation PLC said Thursday that its U.S. subsidiary Jet East acquired a maintenance, repair and overhaul hangar facility in North Carolina, for $3.5 million in cash. The London-listed business-aviation services group said it expects to invest a further $1.5 million in equipping and standing up the 90,000-square-feet facility. The hangar is expected to be operational in the first quarter of 2023 and will be focused on providing major maintenance inspections and aircraft system upgrades, Gama Aviation said.
