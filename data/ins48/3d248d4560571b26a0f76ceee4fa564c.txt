Pakistan pace legend Waqar Younis is displeased with the way India are handling Umran Malik. The veteran feels that the fast bowler from Kashmir should be given more chances and it was not right to send him back to domestic cricket, as per Circle of Cricket.After a good show in the IPL, Umran got a taste of international cricket. But after failing in 3 games, he was shown the door and is now taking part in a domestic competition back home while the Men in Blue prepare to start their campaign in the T20 World Cup.
