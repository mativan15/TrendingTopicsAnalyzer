AAP is BJP's B team in Gujarat: Congress
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 19:15:00

Gujarat Congress leaders alleged on Friday that the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) is working as the "B team of BJP" in the state in the run-up to the assembly election and said the people of the state have understood the AAP plot. They said the BJP government in the state is facing a huge incumbency burden and that this explained why the Prime Minister was making a slew of pre-poll announcements in his home state. "There is an attempt to project a false impression that the Congress party is not in the picture in the Gujarat elections.
