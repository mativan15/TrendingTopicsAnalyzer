Image source, LDRSImage caption, The hotel did not get enough bookingsBlackpool's £1.3m Art B&B has announced it will close at the end of October saying there are not enough future bookings to sustain the business.The Promenade venue will still host workshops and in October and November and will be seeking a new operator.It opened in 2019 after Blackpool Council bought the former Ocean Hotel between Banks Street and Springfield Road for £203,000 in 2016.The B&B, which employed 11 people, was run as a community interest company.
