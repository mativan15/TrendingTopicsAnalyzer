Celeste Ford, Board Chair, Stellar Solutions Celeste Ford is an extraordinary role model who grew a renowned global company, expanded opportunities for women there and throughout her industry, and now is at the forefront of space venture investing. -- Ann Drake, Chair and Founder of AWESOME PALO ALTO, Calif. (PRWEB) June 03, 2022 Celeste Ford, Board Chair of Stellar Solutions, Inc. and General Partner at Stellar Ventures, was bestowed the AWESOME Legendary Leadership (ALL) Award at the 9th Annual AWESOME Symposium gathering last month in Chicago, IL.
