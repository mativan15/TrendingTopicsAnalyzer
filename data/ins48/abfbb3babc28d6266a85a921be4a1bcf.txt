A trio of thieves broke into a high-end New York City jewellery store and stole costly gems and watches, surveillance video showed. The video tweeted by the New York Police Department showed the thieves smash their way into the Cellini jewelers on elite Park Avenue in midtown Manhattan and get away with items worth an estimated $500,000.
