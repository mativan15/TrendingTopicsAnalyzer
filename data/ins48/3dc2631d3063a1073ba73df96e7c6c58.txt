Modi govt abrogated Article 370 brought by Nehru: Amit Shah
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 10:15:00

SHIMLA: Union home minister Amit Shah on Saturday said the Narendra Modi government abrogated Article 370, which was brought by former prime minister Jawaharlal Nehru Shah was addressing an election rally of the ruling BJP in poll-bound Himachal Pradesh 's Sirmaur district."Did you ever think that Article 370 would be abrogated," he asked the gathering."But Modi ji did it," he added.If you talk to Congress leaders and workers about Article 370, they remain mum as it was brought by Nehru, he said.
