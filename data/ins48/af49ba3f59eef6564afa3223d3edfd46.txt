Vodafone Idea to raise Rs 1,600 crore via issue of convertible bonds to ATC, to repay tower firm's dues
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 16:00:00

New Delhi: Vodafone Idea's board on Friday approved issuing Indian Rupee denominated optionally convertible, unsecured, unrated and unlisted debentures to ATC Telecom Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. (ATC) to raise Rs1,600 crore, and will use the fund to repay the dues of the US tower company.The cash-strapped telco will issue the debentures 'having a face value of Rs. 10,00,000 each, in one or more tranches, aggregating upto Rs. 1600 crores, convertible into equity shares at a conversion price of Rs. 10/- per equity share,' Vodafone Idea said in a notice to stock exchanges late Friday.
