Toll gate protest: Mangaluru's Surathkal police register two cases
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 19:15:00

MANGALURU: Mangaluru city police have registered two separate FIRs against more than 25 people, for shouting slogans and disrupting traffic near the toll gate at Surathkal on Tuesday.FIRs were registered based on a complaint filed by the National Highways Authority of India ( NHAI ), as well as another complaint lodged by the manager of the Surathkal toll gate.A large number of protesters, led by the Action Committee Against Surathkal Toll Gate, had marched towards the toll gate at Surathkal on National Highway-66 on Tuesday, demanding that the NHAI stop toll collection at the plaza.
