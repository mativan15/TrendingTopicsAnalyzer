By Reuters Published: 01:04 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 01:04 EDT, 19 October 2022 A look at the day ahead in European and global markets from Ankur Banerjee With investors' mood perking up this week, leading to a buoyant rally, UK inflation data, due on Wednesday, takes the spotlight. The reading will likely determine how hawkish the BoE gets in the near term. The consumer price index, already at a decades high, is expected to jump 10% for the month of September. The cost of living crisis has led to protests and strikes in in European countries, while red-hot inflation has pushed firms eastward in Europe to cut costs.
