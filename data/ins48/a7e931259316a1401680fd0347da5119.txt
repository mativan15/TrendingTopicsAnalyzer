How do I support an employee returning from retirement?
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 07:45:00

Q: I have an employee due to start in two weeks who is coming out of retirement. They previously worked here and I'm glad to have them back, but the business has changed significantly since they retired. I'm not sure what support they will need.A: People choose to return to the workforce after retirement for several reasons, most commonly due to the high cost of living, boredom, or a desire for more social interaction. Since the default retirement ages for both men and women were lowered to 65, the number of people choosing to come out of retirement has increased drastically.
