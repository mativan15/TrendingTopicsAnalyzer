Published: 17:35 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 17:41 EDT, 17 October 2022 Netflix is in crisis over the backlash to the latest series of its mega-budget drama The Crown.The streaming giant is said to be ‘spooked' by the outrage that some storylines have caused – even before it has been broadcast next month.Netflix, which spends £11.5 million per episode on the show, which is one of its biggest global hits, is about to launch series five.It will cover the years from 1991 to 1997, and will include details of Diana's Panorama interview in 1995.
