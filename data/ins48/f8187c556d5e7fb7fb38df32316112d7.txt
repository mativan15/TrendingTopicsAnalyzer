Bob Diamond's Panmure approaches finnCap about takeover
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 23:00:00

A City broker confirmed yesterday that it had received a takeover approach from Panmure Gordon, a rival controlled by Bob Diamond's Atlas Merchant Capital.Shares in finnCap leapt by about a third after it said it had received 'indicative non-binding proposals' that were 'still at an early stage'.Panmure is offering a pure cash purchase or a transaction in which finnCap shareholders receive part of the consideration in Panmure shares.FinnCap, founded in 2007, offers mergers and capital-raising advice to small companies and has an equity sales and trading division.
