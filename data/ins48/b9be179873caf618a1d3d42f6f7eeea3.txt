New York, NY, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- iQSTEL, Inc. (OTCQX: IQST) ("IQST" or the "Company") today announced a forty-two percent (42%) increase in 2022 year-to-date (YTD) revenue compared to the same period in 2021 based on preliminary results.  Still subject to auditor review, the company's 2022 YTD revenue is $66.5 million compared to $46.8 million for the same period in 2021. Notably, the company's 2022 YTD revenue of $66.5 million exceeds the $64.7 million in revenue reported for the entirety of 2021.
