Mom Refuses to Accept Baby's Heart Condition as Death Sentence, Raises Money and Awareness for Others
foxnews.com
news
2022-06-04 17:00:59

'We're Momming Today' with Elizabeth Nichols, a mother of four, about how she managed through receiving a prenatal diagnosis of a very rare heart defect for her third child, Sunley, while maintaining a sense of normalcy and hope for her family. Sunley has had three open heart surgeries and is a cheerful and vibrant four-year-old. Liz raises money to help others through the Write With Light Project.
