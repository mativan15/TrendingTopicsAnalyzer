Person in serious condition after alleged assault in central Christchurch
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-18 06:36:47

A person has been taken to hospital in a serious condition after an alleged assault in the heart of the Christchurch central business district. It is believed the victim, a male, was chased by a group of people before he was allegedly assaulted. Police were notified of an assault on Cashel St at 6.20pm. At least 10 officers could be seen at the scene, and part of Cashel St was cordoned off near the Colombo St intersection. A police spokesperson said one person was taken to hospital in a serious condition.
