This is the latest edition of our series on underrated destinations, It's Still a Big World.Few foreigners realize that Sparta still exists.The ancient city-state, which walked the line between legend and history, is often assumed to have fallen off the map at some point in antiquity. In fact, Sparta–or at least Spartans–have continued to endure until the present day, inhabiting the wild, mountainous province of Laconia in the southern Peloponnese. Formerly an isolated backwater, the blasting through of new highways in 2016 opened up the region like never before, turning this remote corner of Greece into a comfortable two-and-a-half hour drive from Athens.
