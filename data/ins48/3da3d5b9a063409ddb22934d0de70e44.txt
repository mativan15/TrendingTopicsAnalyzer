Macy's is the new home of several Toys 'R' Us locations, including 16 in Georgia. (Karen Wall/Patch)GEORGIA — Ahead of Christmas, Toys "R" Us opens several Georgia locations in Macy's stores. The retail giant announced bankruptcy in 2017, shuttering all of its brick-and-mortar stores in 2018, Fox Business reported.Macy's collaborated in 2021 with WHP Global — owner of the Toys "R" Us, Babies R Us and Geoffrey the Giraffe brands — to restore Toys "R" Us locations. Macy's has begun establishing Toys "R" Us in 451 stores, Fox Business reported.
