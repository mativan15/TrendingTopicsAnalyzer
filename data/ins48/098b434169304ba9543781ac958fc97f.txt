NEW YORK, Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nasdaq, Inc. (Nasdaq: NDAQ) today reported financial results for the third quarter of 2022. Third quarter 2022 net revenues1 increased 6% compared to the third quarter of 2021. Solutions segments2 revenues increased 8%, including 10% organic growth, partially offset by a negative 2% FX impact.Annualized Recurring Revenue (ARR)3 increased 8% compared to the third quarter of 2021. Annualized SaaS revenues increased 13% and represented 35% of ARR.Third quarter 2022 GAAP diluted earnings per share increased 5% compared to the third quarter of 2021.
