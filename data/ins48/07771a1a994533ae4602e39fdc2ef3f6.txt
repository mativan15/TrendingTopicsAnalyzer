The redesigned iPad runs on iPadOS 16, which comes with powerfl features and built-in apps designed to take advantage of the unique capabilities of the Apple device.Apple on Tuesday launched the new iPad, it's first redesigned entry-level iPad in five years, the iPad Pro (2022) and a cheaper third-gen Apple TV 4K streaming device. The redesigned iPad comes with an all-screen design featuring a large 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display. The new iPad is powered by Apple's A14 Bionic chip and is available from October 18 in 28 countries.
