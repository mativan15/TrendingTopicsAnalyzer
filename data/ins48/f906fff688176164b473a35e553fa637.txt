HAIG PARTNERS SERVES AS THE EXCLUSIVE SELL-SIDE ADVISOR FOR WAIKEM AUTO FAMILY ON ITS SALE TO DIEHL AUTOMOTIVE GROUP

FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Haig Partners LLC, the leading buy-sell advisory firm to higher value dealerships and dealership groups, was the exclusive sell-side advisor to Canton, Ohio-based Waikem Auto Family on the sale of its six dealerships to Diehl Automotive Group. The transaction includes Honda, Ford, Kia, Subaru, Hyundai and Mitsubishi dealerships plus a collision center and Quick Lane facility.Haig Partners has been involved in the purchase or sale of 38 dealerships nationwide so far in 2022.
