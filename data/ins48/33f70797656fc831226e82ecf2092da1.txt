A man has been blasted online for telling his fiancée to wear contact lenses instead of glasses on their wedding day because he thinks it will make her 'look nicer'The groom landed himself in hot water with his comments (stock photo) A groom-to-be has landed himself in hot water after he asked his fiancée to ditch her glasses in favour of contact lenses for their upcoming wedding - because he thinks it'll make her "look nicer". The anonymous man posted on Reddit to ask if he was in the wrong for his request, as he explained that when he first met his wife-to-be, she almost always wore contacts instead of glasses.
