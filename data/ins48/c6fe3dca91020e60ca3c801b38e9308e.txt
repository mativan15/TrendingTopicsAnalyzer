Seems like Kollywood is in its best form now, several superhits films are coming back to back from the Kannada film industry this year. They had ‘KGF: Chapter2' and then ‘Vikrant Rona', ‘James', ‘777 Charlie', and now ‘Kantara' all have minted huge money at the Tollywood box office.'Kantara' (The Mystical forest) is a 2022 Kannada action thriller film Written, directed and acted by Rishab Shetty that was funded by Hombale films Vijay Kiragandur who funded ‘KGF: Chapter 2'. The film was released on 30th September in Kannada, and was released on 14th October in Telugu, and is said to be in no mood to slow down at the Tollywood box office.
