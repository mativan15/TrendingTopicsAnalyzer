In the latest episode of House of the Dragon, viewers learned a little more about Ser Larys Strong's personal proclivities, and now the director of the episode has shed new light on the scene in question. This story contains spoilers for House of the Dragon. In Episode 9, Larys (Matthew Needham) and Queen Alicent (Olivia Cooke) share a quiet moment in which Alicent removes her shoes and socks, exposing her feet on a table near Larys as the two discuss how their plans are unfolding. At the end of the scene, Alicent turns away and Larys puts his hand down his pants to seemingly pleasure himself.
