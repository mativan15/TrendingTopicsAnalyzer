October 20, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK & GRANADA, Spain & MONACO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Despite choppy global economic seas, ViewLift, a global leader in end-to-end streaming and OTT solutions, today announced new activities on multiple continents: shortlisting for a prestigious industry award; twin thought-leadership industry keynotes; and two new prestigious sports clients.



ViewLift is shortlisted as a 'Best Emerging Tech Company' for SportsPro OTT Awards. Launched in 2008, the SportsPro OTT Awards recognize organizations driving the growth of the sports OTT industry through technological innovation, as judged by an expert panel.
