House Speaker Nancy Pelosi's Husband Arrested For DUI
ibtimes.com.au
news
2022-05-30 02:38:07

Paul Pelosi, the 82-year-old husband of Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi, was arrested by law enforcement officers this weekend on suspicion of driving under the influence. 
A public booking report from the Napa County Criminal Justice Network showed that Paul was arrested at 11:44 p.m. PT on Saturday and was charged with one count of driving under the influence. Officials also charged him with one count of driving with a blood alcohol content level of 0.08 or higher. 
Paul was booked on two misdemeanor charges and his bail was set for $5,000.
