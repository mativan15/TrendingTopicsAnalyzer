Steven Gerrard shows the harsh reality that star players are rarely as good as managers

news
2022-10-21 23:01:00

The question has been fielded so many times that Steven Gerrard's nascent career in management almost felt preordained. With every step the former Liverpool captain was asked about 'unfinished business at Anfield'. And with every question the 42-year-old pointed out that, were he ever to return as manager to the club where he is so revered, the position would have to be earned.Gerrard's dismissal by Aston Villa on Thursday night, against a backdrop of caustic songs from Villa's travelling support, makes that prospect feel more distant than ever, of course, but also throws the former Rangers manager's future into the unknown.
