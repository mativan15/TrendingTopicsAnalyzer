Tracee Ellis Ross. Actress, Businesswoman, and Executive Producer Maarten de Boer
Tracee Ellis Ross is many things. On and off the screen, she is a leading lady. Not merely as an actress. But now, as an executive producer and host of the unscripted documentary series, The Hair Tales, alongside Michaela angela Davis and Oprah Winfrey. Presented by Onyx Collective, a new content brand on Hulu designed to curate a slate of premium entertainment by creators of color and underrepresented voices, in partnership with OWN and Hulu — The Hair Tales is a highly anticipated six-part honest conversation that explores the identity of Black women through the metaphor of hair featuring Ross, Issa Rae, Chlöe Bailey, Congresswoman Ayanna Pressley, Marsai Martin, and Chika.
