U.S. Rep. John Rutherford faces House Ethics probe over disclosure of stock trades
azcentral.com
news
2022-06-02 13:10:19

JACKSONVILLE, Fla. – The U.S. House Committee on Ethics will investigate Rep. John Rutherford over multiple late disclosures of stock transactions, a matter that Rutherford said he believed he'd already put behind him by paying fines.The House Ethics Committee's leaders announced Tuesday they would open the investigation after receiving a referral from the Office of Congressional Ethics.Rutherford, R-Jacksonville, serves on the bipartisan, 10-member House Committee on Ethics, so the committee is reviewing one of its own members.
