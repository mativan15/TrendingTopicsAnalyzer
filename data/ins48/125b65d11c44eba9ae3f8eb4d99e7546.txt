This Memorial Day weekend, with surging gas prices that are redefining pain at the pump, that is the question for many Americans as a new COVID-19 surge also spreads across the country.Memorial Day is Monday. The holiday that honors soldiers who died during service to the United States is observed on May 30 this year. Though its roots date back to the Civil War, it was declared a national holiday in 1971.  While many people across the country will have the day off work or school on Memorial Day, you might be wondering whether your bank will be open, if you'll get mail and more.
