Published: 07:45 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 12:50 EDT, 17 October 2022 By Arunima Kumar and Ruhi Soni Oct 17 (Reuters) - Continental Resources Inc said on Monday it had agreed to a higher offer from billionaire-founder Harold Hamm to take the U.S. shale producer private at a valuation of about $27 billion. Hamm, a legendary oilman who once called the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries a "toothless tiger," has offered $74.28 per share for the stake not owned by him and the Hamm family trust.
