Violating rights in the name of protecting kids
nydailynews.com
news
2022-10-18 09:00:04

Last week, the Administration for Children's Services (ACS) was confronted with allegations that its agents routinely barge into tens of thousands of homes across the city without a warrant, violating the Constitution and the inner lives of predominantly Black New Yorkers. Remarkably, ACS does not dispute the well-substantiated allegations that they sought warrants in only 0.2% of home investigations. Instead, ACS' responses were technical. These are not home 'searches,' they responded, these are 'home evaluations'; ACS does not require warrants, they said, they require 'entry orders.
