Stock market update: Nifty Realty index advances 0.18% in an upbeat market
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 06:45:00

NEW DELHI: The Nifty Realty index traded positive around 11:25AM(IST)on Thursday in an upbeat market. Macrotech Developers(up 2.68 per cent), Indiabulls Real Estate(up 1.54 per cent), Godrej Properties(up 0.53 per cent) and Phoenix Mills(up 0.2 per cent) were among the top gainers. Prestige Estates Projects(down 1.23 per cent), Oberoi Realty(down 0.73 per cent), Brigade Enterprises(down 0.51 per cent) and DLF(down 0.12 per cent) were the top losers on the index. The Nifty Realty index was up 0.18 per cent at 407.
