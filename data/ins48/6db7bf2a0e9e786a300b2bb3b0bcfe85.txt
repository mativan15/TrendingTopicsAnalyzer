AgenciesMarvel Studios has revealed a huge Diwali treat for Indian fans ahead of the holiday season. Black Panther: Wakanda Forever, the largest action movie from Marvel Studios for 2022, is already accepting advance reservations. The action-adventure film, directed by Ryan Coogler, will see Letitia Wright, Angela Bassett, Tenoch Huerta Mejia, Winston Duke, Danai Gurira, Lupita Nyong'o, Florence Kasumba, and Martin Freeman in action. On November 11, 2022, Black Panther: Wakanda Forever will be released in theatres in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, and English.
