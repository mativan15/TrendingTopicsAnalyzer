The Study Shows Americans Are Redefining Health in the Second Half of Life

WASHINGTON, June 3, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Older Americans are not only the happiest adult Americans as a whole, but also consider themselves healthier and more financially secure than those in their 40s and 50s, reveals a new study published in AARP Bulletin. The "Second Half of Life Study," conducted with National Geographic, surveyed thousands of adults age 18 to 90 to explore how Americans perceive their current life, their expectations for the future and aging in general as we emerge from the COVID pandemic.
