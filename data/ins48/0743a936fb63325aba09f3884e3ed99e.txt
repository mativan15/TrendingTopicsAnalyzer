FuzzMartin Rolls-Royce Holdings (OTCPK:RYCEY) operates internationally as an industrial technology company, offering primarily aircraft and marine engines. To date, the company seems quite distressed, and the market cap halved compared to the beginning of the year is an indication that the market is concerned about its future performance.
