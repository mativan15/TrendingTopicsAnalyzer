Kia's Sohari Plant in Gwangmyeong, Gyeonggi. [YONHAP] An employee was found dead after a fire broke out at a Kia factory in Gwangmyeong, Gyeonggi on Wednesday morning, according to a report released by the Gwangmyeong city government. The fire broke out in a container used as a rest area for employees at 6:05 a.m. The dead employee was found near the fire. According to the city government, the fire was completely extinguished by 6:48 a.m.
