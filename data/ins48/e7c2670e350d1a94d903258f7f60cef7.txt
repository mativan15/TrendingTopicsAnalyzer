Rural vitalization boosted by Taiwan compatriots

By ZHAO YIMENG | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-21 07:58 Share - WeChat Children experience agricultural work in July at a farm opened in 2019 by Kuo Dian-i (left) in Kunshan, Suzhou, Jiangsu province. [Photo/CHINA DAILY] More Taiwan compatriots and enterprises have been devoted to rural vitalization on the Chinese mainland under supportive policies, delegates from Taiwan said in Beijing on the sidelines of the ongoing 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China. Young compatriots learned from the experience of Taiwan's modern agriculture and rural construction and applied this knowledge to the development of agriculture and beautiful countrysides on the mainland, they said.
