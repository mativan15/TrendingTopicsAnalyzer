GoFundMe 'shuts down' fundraiser to help Amber Heard pay Johnny Depp $10.4m damages
express.co.uk
entertainment
2022-06-04 13:00:00

GoFundMe has reportedly removed a $1 million fundraiser entitled 'Justice for Amber Heard' which claimed to be raising money to help Ms Heard pay Johnny Depp $10.4 million in damages following their high-profile libel trial.It is understood that the campaign was set up shortly after the jury's verdict was announced on Wednesday.A user named Kimberly organised the fundraiser and claimed that she had been in touch with Amber Heard's legal team.Kimberly also promised that Amber Heard would receive all of the money raised, as reported by TMZ.
