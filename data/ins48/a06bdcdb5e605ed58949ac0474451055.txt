How much trouble is Mark Zuckerberg in?

2022-10-16 14:23:13

It is night-time at the Soapstone Comedy Club. In fact, it always is. The club is a space in Horizon Worlds, Meta's flagship metaverse app, where users can watch and perform comedy in virtual reality (vr). 'It's hard to do stand-up when you have no legs,' quips one performer, gesturing to his hovering avatar, before accidentally dropping the virtual microphone and floating offstage. A night out in vr lacks some of the atmosphere of a real bar, though it does cause authentic dizziness and nausea.It is almost a year since Mark Zuckerberg announced that his company would change its name from Facebook to Meta, to reflect its commitment to the metaverse and, no doubt, to escape the firm's toxic public image.
