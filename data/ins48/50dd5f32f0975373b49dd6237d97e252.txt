John Mara not taking victory lap after Giants' stunning start
nypost.com
news
2022-10-18 16:26:14

It seems owner John Mara doesn't want to jinx the Giants.
With a rare chance to take a victory lap for making the right hires and relish in a surprising 5-1 start, Mara was brief in 25 seconds of on-the-move comments to reporters camped at the NFL owners meetings Tuesday in lower Manhattan.
'So far, so good,' Mara said. 'We're on to Jacksonville.'
Mara skipped his annual preseason state of the team during training camp and has offered few comments after wins this season, including telling The Post's Ian O'Connor 'What's not to like?' and Steve Serby, 'It's nice for the fans to win a game at home, finally.
