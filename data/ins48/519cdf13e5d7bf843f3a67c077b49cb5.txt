REUTERS/Christopher PikeOct 20 (Reuters) - Saudi Arabia's stock market rose in early trade on Thursday amid rising oil prices, while Qatari shares extended losses from the previous session.Crude oil prices, a key catalyst for the Gulf's financial markets, gained around $1 as investor sentiment improved on news that China was considering a cut in the quarantine duration for inbound visitors.
