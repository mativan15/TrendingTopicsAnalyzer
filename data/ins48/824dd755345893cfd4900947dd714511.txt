VANCOUVER, BC, May 27, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Verisante Technology Inc. (NEX: VRS.H) is pleased to announce that it expects trading on the NEX board of the TSXV to be reinstated shortly following the full revocation of the Failure to File Cease Trade Order ("FFCTO") dated April 6, 2022, from its principal regulator, the British Columbia Securities Commission.

Corporate Update

In early 2018 Verisante Technology Inc. discontinued its medical device business and entered into an RTO transaction with SIM Co.
