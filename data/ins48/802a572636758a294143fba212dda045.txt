Market May-hem: Steel, diagnostics, paints stocks hit most last month
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 11:15:00

NEW DELHI: One of every three BSE500 stocks plunged more than 10 per cent in May which saw the benchmark indices falling nearly 4.48 per cent for the month amid concerns over inflation, tightening monetary policy by central banks globally, rupee weakness and resultant foreign outflows. Data showed 166 stocks from the index fell over 10 per cent; among them, 31 stocks tanked over 20 per cent. The list comprised stocks from a host of sectors, including steel, paints, diagnostics, cement, textiles, midcap IT and financials.
