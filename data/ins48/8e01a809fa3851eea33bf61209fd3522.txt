Chiefs open as road favorites over 49ers in Week 7
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-17 11:45:07

The Kansas City Chiefs have somehow regained the confidence of oddsmakers after the Week 6 loss to the Buffalo Bills.
According to Tipico Sportsbook, Kansas City (4-2) are 3.5-point favorites over San Francisco next Sunday (3-3). The over/under point total is set at 48.5 points scored. The money line is now -165 for the Chiefs, giving K.C. a 62.26% implied win probability. Keep in mind this Kansas City team is now 2-4 against the spread this season and they're also 3-3 on the over/under point total.
