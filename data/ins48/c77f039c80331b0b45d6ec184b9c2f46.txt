By Reuters Published: 10:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 10:04 EDT, 20 October 2022 BERLIN, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Germany's Borgers, a supplier of textile components to the auto industry that filed for insolvency earlier this week, urgently needs funds from its customers in order to continue production, its insolvency administrator said on Thursday. "There can be no turnaround in the supplier industry without the manufacturers. We need their financial help to stabilize the company and bring it into calmer waters," Frank Kebekus told Reuters.
