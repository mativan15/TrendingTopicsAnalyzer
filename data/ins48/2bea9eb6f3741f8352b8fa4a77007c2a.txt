Tenet Calls for Full Redemption of 6.750% Senior Notes due 2023

2022-06-01 00:00:00

June 01, 2022 05:26 PM Eastern Daylight Time DALLAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Tenet Healthcare Corporation (NYSE: THC) today called for the redemption of all of the $1.748 billion aggregate principal amount outstanding of its 6.750% senior notes due June 15, 2023 (the 'Notes'), which were issued pursuant to the Indenture, dated as of June 16, 2015, among THC Escrow Corporation II and The Bank of New York Mellon Trust Company, N.A. (the 'Trustee'), as supplemented by the Supplemental Indenture thereto, dated as of June 16, 2015, among Tenet and the Trustee (collectively, the 'Indenture').
