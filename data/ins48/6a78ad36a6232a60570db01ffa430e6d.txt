Geronay Whitebooi's and Charné Griesel's know a few things about breaking barriers.The South African's own encounters with judo have sent them on an unexpected path of discovery, whether dealing with anxiety or coming to grips with the untold hardship many in the country faced.Olympian and Commonwealth Games gold medallist Geronay Whitebooi initially hoped to use judo to help support her family when she received a scholarship at the University of Pretoria. The financial management graduate has since expanded her network of influence, hoping to inspire the next generation of judokas.
