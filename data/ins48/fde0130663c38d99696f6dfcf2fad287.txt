The top cryptocurrencies were trading lower on Thursday, with the global cryptocurrency market cap dropping 0.48% to $920.23 billion in the last 24 hours. Also, the total trading volume declined 15% to $45.20 billion. Earlier in the day, Bitcoin fell below $19,000, with a cut of 1.5%. Also, Ripple, Solana, Cardano and Terra Classic dropped up to over 10%.

The losses came in as investors were worried over the new housing data as well as ongoing macroeconomic uncertainty.
