October 20, 2022 19:46 IST British Prime Minister Liz Truss announces her resignation, outside 10, Downing Street in London on October 20, 2022. | Photo Credit: Reuters British Prime Minister Liz Truss on October 20 dramatically announced her resignation just six weeks after taking office.Ms. Truss bowed to the inevitable after her right-wing platform of tax cuts disintegrated and as many MPs among the ruling Conservatives revolted.Speaking in Downing Street, Ms. Truss said she would stay on as Prime Minister until a successor is chosen to serve as Tory leader.
