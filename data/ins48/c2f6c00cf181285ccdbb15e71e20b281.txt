Army helicopter crashes in Arunachal Pradesh

2022-10-21 07:04:06

An Army helicopter on Friday crashed at Migging in West Siang district of Arunachal Pradesh, reported PTI citing a defence official. The advanced light helicopter, carrying Army personnel, was on regular sorties, he said. The incident took place at 10.43 am, and search operation is underway, the official added. 
Further details are awaited.
