Manchester United once again fielded a strong XI in the UEFA Europa League as they took on Cypriot minnows Omonia Nicosia at Old Trafford on Thursday night United boss Erik ten Hag has rejected the opportunity to rotate his team in the Europa LeagueBut the Dutchman's very strong team selection for the meeting with the Cypriots - and indeed his unwillingness to make changes generally for the Europa League - also raises concerns.
