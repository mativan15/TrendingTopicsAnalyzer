AgenciesThe Nifty has fallen 10.4% from its all-time high of 18,604.45 in October.Mumbai: Indian equities advanced for the third session in a row on Monday led by gains in beaten-down information technology stocks, with investors taking their cues from Friday's rebound in the US Nasdaq. Sentiment across Asian markets also got a lift from reports that China may ease Covid restrictions in Shanghai, allowing businesses to resume work on Wednesday. The Nifty ended 1.89% up at 16,661.40 points and the Sensex rose 1.
