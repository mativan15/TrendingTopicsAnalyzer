73,035 square feet of four connected buildings provides rare new creative space near Downtown Culver City, home to Apple, Amazon and TikTok

LOS ANGELES, October 19, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Ratkovich Company (TRC), a Los Angeles-based real estate development company specializing in urban infill and historic rehabilitation of landmark properties, and institutional investors advised by J.P. Morgan Global Alternatives have acquired the Bowcroft Collection stretching from 5950-5978 Bowcroft Street, near the intersection of Jefferson and Obama Boulevards in the City of Los Angeles.
