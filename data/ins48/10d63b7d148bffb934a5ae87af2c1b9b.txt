HOUSTON, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Guardion Health Sciences, Inc. ('Guardion' or the 'Company') (Nasdaq: GHSI) announced an agreement with OmegaQuant Laboratories ('OmegaQuant') to provide Guardion with analytic testing and laboratory support services for current and future products. Guardion is a clinical nutrition company that offers a portfolio of science-based, clinically-developed products that are designed to support the health needs of consumers, healthcare professionals and providers and their patients.
