Germany's Olaf Scholz defends record on arms to Ukraine
dw.com
news
2022-06-01 07:23:00

German Chancellor Olaf Scholz has defended Germany's record on delivering arms to Ukraine and promised more — including modern anti-aircraft equipment.

Scholz faces accusations that he has delayed too much in sending weapons to Ukraine
The German chancellor addressed the German parliament's lower chamber on Wednesday and said Berlin would supply state-of-the-art air defense weaponry to Ukrainian forces.
In his speech to lawmakers, Scholz outlined Germany's approach to the Ukraine war and its effects on the wider economy and public spending.
