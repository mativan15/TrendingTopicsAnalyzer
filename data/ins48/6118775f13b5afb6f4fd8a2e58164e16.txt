May 27, 2022 05:25 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Glancy Prongay & Murray LLP ('GPM'), announces that it has filed a class action lawsuit in the United States District Court for the Southern District of Florida, captioned Nguyen v. LMP Automotive Holdings, Inc., et al., Case No. 22-cv-61019, on behalf of persons and entities that purchased or otherwise acquired LMP Automotive Holdings, Inc. ('LMP' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: LMPX) securities between June 29, 2021 and May 19, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period').
