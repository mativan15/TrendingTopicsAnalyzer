Marriott to Suspend Operations in Russia
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-03 15:18:00

By Will Feuer Marriott International Inc. said it is suspending all operations in Russia more than three months after Russia invaded Ukraine, spurring international restrictions on the country and the ability of businesses to do business there. In March, the company said it would close its corporate office in Moscow and pause the opening of upcoming hotels and all future hotel development as well as investment in Russia. The company said Friday that newly announced US, UK and EU restrictions "will make it impossible for Marriott to continue to operate or franchise hotels in the Russian market.
