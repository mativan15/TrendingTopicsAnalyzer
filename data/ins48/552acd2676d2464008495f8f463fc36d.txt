Ramlila performance by artists from Russia, Malaysia, Sri Lanka and Fiji will be the added attraction at the three-day Deepotsav celebrations starting in Ayodhya from October 21. The Ayodhya administration has planned mega cultural activities across the temple town for the Sixth Deepotsav celebrations. The three-day cultural extravaganza will end on October 23 on the eve of Diwali with the Ayodhya administration setting a target of lighting 14 lakh diyas earthen lamps at the majestic Ram Ki Paidi ghat.
