Veteran film producer falls prey to cyber fraud, Rs 3.82 lakh stolen
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-27 08:26:41

A 66-year-old veteran film producer from Mumbai recently fell prey to a cyber crime, where a fraudster managed to leak his credit card details and transfer Rs 3.82 lakh from his bank account. An FIR was registered in the case at the Amboli police station on May 25.
The complaint was filed with the Amboli police by a producer, whose office falls in the jurisdiction of Amboli police. On February 9 this year, the fraudster transferred Rs 3.82 lakh in five transactions. The money went into the account of a company based in Gurgaon, Haryana.
