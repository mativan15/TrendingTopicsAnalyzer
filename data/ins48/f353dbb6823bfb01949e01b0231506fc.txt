Nonprofit MMI Helps Military Service Members and Veterans with Free Debt Counseling

Stafford, Texas, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- 'MMI was a cheerleader, they helped with accountability. They also helped me reduce interest rates on my credit cards that were eating up my paycheck,' said Deborah Wang, a U.S. Army Veteran from Phoenix, who paid off almost $30,000 with the help of an MMI debt management plan. The experts at MMI understand military transition challenges firsthand and are dedicated to helping service members and their families reconnect to the life of financial stability they deserve.
