AgenciesThe 42nd Nationwide Children's Hospital Columbus Marathon is returning. The event follows a cloverleaf pattern and is a Boston Marathon qualifying event. Due to COVID-19, it was cancelled in 2020, but it returned last year with over 8,000 runners and raised more than $1 million for Nationwide Children's Hospital. More than 12,000 runners are scheduled to compete in this year's competition. The corrals will be open at 6 a.m. The wheelchair division begins racing at 7:25 a.m. ET, while the marathon begins at 7:30 a.
