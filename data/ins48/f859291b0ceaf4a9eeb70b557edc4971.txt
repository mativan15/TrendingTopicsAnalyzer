Downing Street set to hold weekly press conferences over cost of living crisis

The news conferences would aim to keep the public informed on what the Government is doing to deal with the squeeze.It is thought they would follow a similar format to the televised Covid-style briefings that took place during the height of the pandemic.Ministers, including Boris Johnson and Chancellor Rishi Sunak, would speak during the proposed events, alongside officials and experts.The weekly press conferences would keep people informed on issues such as soaring energy bills, inflation and support measures to help those struggling.
