The Joule pioneered the rebirth of Downtown Dallas.Headington Companies 
The Downtown Dallas neighborhood struggled for years. However, In the early 2000s, Headington Companies purchased the long-neglected and vacant Neo-Gothic Dallas National Bank Building and embarked on a nearly decade-long preservation and expansion project resulting in The Joule, a luxury boutique hotel designed by the world-renowned designer Adam D. Tihany. In addition to Headington a growing number of savvy developers in places such as Kansas City are revitalizing industrial architecture and creating hubs of artistic talent.
