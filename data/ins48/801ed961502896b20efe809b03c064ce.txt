Businessman takes lift in car, robbed on Pune-Mumbai Expressway; second incident in 3 days
indiatimes.com
business
2022-06-02 01:00:00

PUNE: Barely three days after a software engineer from Khopoli, who took a lift in a car, was threatened, robbed and abandoned at a spot near Talegaon Dabhade along the Pune-Mumbai Expressway, another victim was targeted in a similar manner last week. The police believe both crimes were perpetrated by the same duo.On May 27, the techie from Khopoli in Rajgad district was robbed of cash and valuables when he sought a ride from two men in a sports utility vehicle (SUV) at Bhumkar Chowk. In the latest instance on May 30, a pest control businessman from Bhosari was robbed of his belongings in the same manner.
