As Diwali festivities begin, many Indians are likely to travel to their hometowns to celebrate with their families. This is of particular importance because, after a span of three years, there will be no covid restrictions enforced. The Indian Railways has also put out an advisory to ensure the safety of its passengers keeping in mind the expected increase in travelers during this period. If you are planning on traveling too, there are some of these new rules and regulations that you should know about as well.
