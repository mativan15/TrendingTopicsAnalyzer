Upcoming PMI Data In Focus As U.K. Recession Risks Intensify Amid Market Turmoil
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-15 13:12:00

Maksim Labkouski October's flash PMI data for the UK will be eagerly awaited, given the growing chorus of concerns regarding the country's economic health and financial market stability. A botched 'mini' budget in late September - that turned into one of the most disruptive government-induced economic shocks in recent memory - exacerbated downward pressure on sterling and gilts, leading to unprecedented intervention in the bond market by the Bank of England to help stave off a pension industry crisis.
