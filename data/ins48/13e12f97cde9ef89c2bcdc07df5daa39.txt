The report spotlights growth and rental trends in the current economy, Build-for-Rent opportunities, and the booming Nashville marketBETHESDA, Md., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Walker & Dunlop, Inc. announced today the release of its latest Multifamily Outlook Report—an exclusive look at the apartment market amid last year's historic growth, recent developments from monetary tightening to the war in Ukraine, and more. The report provides the latest market trends and insights backed by proprietary data from Zelman & Associates, the leading housing research firm in the country.
