kali9/E+ via Getty Images On 5/31/22 PotlatchDeltic (NASDAQ:PCH) announced a merger in which it will buy CatchMark Timber Trust (NYSE:CTT) for 0.23 shares of PCH for each share of CTT. This equates to an enterprise value of $919 million for CTT and a converted value of CTT shares of $12.88 using the unaffected price of PCH shares. Mergers are often a source of opportunity so let us dig in to see if there is a play here. I begin by analyzing the merits and demerits of the merger and follow with an analysis of the arbitrage between CTT and PCH shares while they are in this state of limbo.
