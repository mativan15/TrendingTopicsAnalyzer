The Udupi DistrictÂ Child Protection Unit, in association with police and social activist Nityananda Volakad, on Friday, rescued seven children who were found begging near the bus stands in Udupi and Manipal.The police said the rescued children were aged between 10 and 12 years and were students in different government schools. Parents had put them in this task since Dasara holidays.
