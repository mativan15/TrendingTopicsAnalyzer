October 20, 2022 01:10 PM Eastern Daylight Time DARIEN, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SR Alternative Credit, LLC ('SRAC'), formerly Old Hill Partners, today announced a deal that provided $10 million in the form of a senior secured one-year term loan to a company that acquires and leases standard 20- and 40-foot shipping containers, among other transportation equipment. This is the third transaction for SRAC with this borrower. The company's lessees include shippers with trade routes primarily in Southeast Asia and other regional routes.
