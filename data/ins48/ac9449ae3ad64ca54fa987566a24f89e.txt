Global buy now, pay later market to reach a five-fold growth of $656.34 billion by 2026 with omnichannel technology and data collection, says Frost & Sullivan

SAN ANTONIO, May 31, 2022 /CNW/ -- Zero-interest fees and flexible repayment plans are vital in boosting the global buy now, pay later (BNPL) market, according to Frost & Sullivan's latest report. Emerging as an alternative credit payment approach, BNPL aims to meet the need of younger consumers who often have a limited credit history and low credit scores.
