Costa Rica is 'at war' with Russian hackers and other countries will be next, experts warn

2022-06-03 18:30:00

This week, Costa Rica came under attack — again.On Tuesday morning in the Central American country, printers at the national health service abruptly churned out copies of a ransomware note.Hospital record-keeping systems went down, and screens flashed up demands for a digital key needed to unlock compromised files and servers.This was just the latest in a string of cyber attacks that had knocked out basic government services, including the online tax portal and automated system for paying teachers' salaries.
