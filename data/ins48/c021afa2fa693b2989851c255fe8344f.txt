Nucor: Q3 Earnings Snapshot

business
2022-10-20 12:09:08

CHARLOTTE, N.C.

Nucor Corp. (NUE) on Thursday reported third-quarter net income of $1.69 billion.

On a per-share basis, the Charlotte, North Carolina-based company said it had profit of $6.50.

The results beat Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of three analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $6.45 per share.

The steel company posted revenue of $10.5 billion in the period, also exceeding Street forecasts. Three analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $10 billion.
