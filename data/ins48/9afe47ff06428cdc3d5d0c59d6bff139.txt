Strap: Around 600 cops searched every house in Jalandhar's Ganna Pind village which is infamous for drugs.This village is also adopted by Jalandhar LS MP Santokh Singh Chaudhary







Almost 92 kg of narcotic substances and 60 grams of heroin have been recovered from Jalandhar's Ganna Pind village, which is infamous for drugs. Apart from this, 260 litres of illicit liquor have also been seized and many people have been arrested. (HT File Photo/ Representational image)

Infamous for drugs, village Ganna Pind in Jalandhar district was raided by the police on Sunday morning.
