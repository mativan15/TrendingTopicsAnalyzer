2022-10-15 00:40:00

On Thursday, the University of Hawaii Shidler College of Business Alumni Association inducted four alumni executives into its Hall of Honor in an event at the Sheraton Waikiki, the first to be held in person since 2019.

Click on the image above for a slideshow of the evening's event.

This year's inductees are Julie Arigo, BBA '88, general manager, Halepuna Waikiki by Halekulani; Terry George, MBA '09, president and CEO, Harold K.L. Castle Foundation; Shelee Kimura, BBA '95, president and CEO, Hawaiian Electric Co.
