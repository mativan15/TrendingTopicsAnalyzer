Almost by definition in the fashion industry, keeping an eye on changing trends and tastes is de rigueur for Max Mara to stay relevant and stay true to its brand pledge. That's the job of designers, product managers and others whose focus is bringing the right mix of products to market.But there's also another side of Max Mara's business model that's essential to keeping its clientele coming back: a satisfying buying experience, whether it's through one of the company's 10 brand-specific websites or its more than 2,300 brick and mortar stores around the world.
