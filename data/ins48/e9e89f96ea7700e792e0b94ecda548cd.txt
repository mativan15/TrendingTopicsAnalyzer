Andy García looks like he's ready for an Architectural Digest photoshoot. Seated in his studio for our Zoom interview, the actor tells me in that distinct and distinguished murmur, 'This is where I live.' Sunlight bounces around the room, illuminating an appealingly cluttered menagerie—a stack of books on the coffee table here, four conga drums there… and two Panama hats side by side on the back of a tan leather couch.As a devout maximalist, I'm enchanted—and perhaps reminded of my late abuelo's office, which also pulled off a certain organized-but-chock-a-block je ne sais quoi.
