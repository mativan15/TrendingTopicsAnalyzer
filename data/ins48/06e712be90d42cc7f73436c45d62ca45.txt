One dead & another missing after boat capsizes in Memorial Day tragedy
thesun.co.uk
news
2022-05-30 07:10:38

AT least one person has died while a man remains missing after a boat capsized - days after five were killed in a river crash in Georgia.
Crews rushed to the scene at Lake Pueblo, Colorado on May 29 and rescued 11 people, including eight kids from the water.
At least one person has died after a boat capsized at Lake Pueblo, Colorado (stock pic)Credit: Alamy
One of the youngsters was flown to hospital with serious injuries, KRDO reports. 
Three adults were rescued, according to the Colorado Springs-Gazette.
