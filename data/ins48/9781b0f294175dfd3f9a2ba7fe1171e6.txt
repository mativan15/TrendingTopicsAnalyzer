Ollie's Celebrates 40th Anniversary with a Search for "America's Biggest Cheapskate"
Enter Now and Show America How Cheap You Are!
HARRISBURG, Pa., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Ollie's, America's largest retailer of closeout merchandise and excess inventory with 445 stores and growing, today announced its search for "America's Biggest Cheapskate" to help mark the company's 40th anniversary. The nationwide contest is open for submissions until July 3, through an online entry form found at www.americasbiggestcheapskate.
