chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2022-05-27 10:42 Vitesco CEO Andreas Wolf unveils the company's logo on Oct 1, 2019. [Photo provided to chinadaily.com.cn] German auto parts supplier Vitesco Technologies is confident in its business in China despite recent outbreaks of the COVID-19 pandemic in the world's largest vehicle market, said CEO Andreas Wolf. Vitesco's plants in Changchun, Jilin province, and Shanghai has been affected but they are revving up production as the pandemic is gradually put under control, said Wolf earlier this week.
