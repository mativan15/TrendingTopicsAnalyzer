AFPRafael Nadal reaches final after Zverev pulls out due to injury.Rafael Nadal advanced to his 14th French Open final after his opponent Alexander Zverev pulled out of the clash due to an injury. Alexander Zverev had to leave the court in a wheelchair after suffering a horrific right ankle injury during his French Open semi-final against Rafael Nadal. The German third seed was trailing 7-6 (10/8), 6-6 after more than three hours of play when he turned his ankle on the Paris clay.
 After screaming in anguish, a tearful Zverev was helped from the court by medics.
