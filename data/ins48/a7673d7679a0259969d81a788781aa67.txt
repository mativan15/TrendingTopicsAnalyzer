AMC's Subsidiary Odeon Finco PLC Announces Closing of Senior Secured Notes Offering
businesswire.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

LEAWOOD, Kan.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Odeon Finco PLC (the 'Issuer') announced today that it has completed its private offering (the 'Offering') of $400.0 million aggregate principal amount of 12.750% senior secured notes due 2027 (the 'Notes'), at an issue price of 92.00%.

Odeon used the proceeds from the Offering, together with cash on hand, to fund the repayment in full of OCGL's existing term loan facilities which had a principal amount of $506 million at June 30, 2022 (based on the currency exchange rates as of such date) and to pay related fees, costs, premiums and expenses.
