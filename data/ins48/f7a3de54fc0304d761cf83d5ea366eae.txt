Woolworths are dropping their popular green reusable bags for a new lineThe 'Bag for Good' design will be replaced by a new mostly recycled versionThe old bags carried a free-replacement promise, the new bags will not By Max Aldred For Daily Mail Australia Published: 09:44 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 09:44 EDT, 20 October 2022 Woolworths has made a shock decision to scrap their popular, reusable bags – loved by shoppers for their free replacement guarantee.The supermarket giant has confirmed it's now phasing out the 99c green bags, a check-out staple at stores across Australia.
