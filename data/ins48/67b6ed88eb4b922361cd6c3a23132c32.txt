jetcityimage/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Fastenal (NASDAQ:FAST) is integral to enabling manufacturing and construction with their wide array of industrial and construction supplies. Fastenal's operations are primarily tied to the United States although Fastenal does sell and operate in Canada, Mexico as well as other international spots. Fastenal provides things like screws, nuts, bolts, various plumbing fittings, and electrical supplies. Additionally Fastenal provides safety items such as fall protection supplies, safety glasses, safety signage, and specialty gloves.
