By Reuters Published: 21:54 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 21:54 EDT, 17 October 2022 NEW YORK -- Due to off days and weather, the New York Yankees and Cleveland Guardians are engaged in a lengthy postseason series. The American League Division Series was extended another day when the forecast of heavy rain forced a postponement of Game 5 on Monday. After a delay of more than two hours, the game was postponed and moved to Tuesday at 4:07 p.m. EDT when shadows at Yankee Stadium will likely impact hitters.
