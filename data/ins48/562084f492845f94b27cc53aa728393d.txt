Third-party motor insurance rate hike to hit two-wheeler business: TVS Motor
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-05-31 09:35:25

Third-party motor insurance rates are set to go up from June 1 which will make buying of new cars and two-wheelers a bit more expensive.
 TVS Motor has reacted to the third-party motor insurance rate hike. It feels the hike will impact the two-wheeler business in India.



TVS Motor has reacted to the new third-party motor insurance rates, which will be implemented from tomorrow, June 1. One of India's largest two-wheeler manufacturer said that the increase in the third-party motor insurance premium will affect the two-wheeler industry which is already battling slowdown and drop in sales in recent times.
