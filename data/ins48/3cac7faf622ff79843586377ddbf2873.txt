Netflix wows Wall Street
brisbanetimes.com.au
news
2022-10-19 20:11:02

A slew of bullish calls on Netflix from 'back on track' to 'rise of a new version' by Wall Street brokers confirm that a change in tact and rebound in user growth are likely to set the stock off to a sustainable recovery.Shares in the video streaming giant surged 14 per cent on Wednesday, set for its biggest jump since January 2021. JPMorgan Chase & Co., Deutsche Bank and KGI Securities Co upgraded their recommendations on the stock, while at least a dozen other analysts boosted their target price.
