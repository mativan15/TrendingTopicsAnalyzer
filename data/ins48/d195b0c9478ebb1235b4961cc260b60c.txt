Â A two-wheeler rally was taken out by the district administration on Monday to mark the World Trauma Day.It was organised in association with the Krishnagiri Government Medical College Hospital. The rally was taken out highlighting the message of trauma prevention and use of helmets.The aim is to ensure 'accident-free Krishnagiri'. Trauma Day is observed every year on October 17 to create awareness on road safety to prevent accidents leading to death and disability.
