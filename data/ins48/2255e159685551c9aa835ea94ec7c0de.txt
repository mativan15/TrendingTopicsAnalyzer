By Nick Starling June 3, 2022 / 7:09 PM / CBS DFW NORTH TEXAS (CBSDFW.COM) - Commuters are experiencing record gas prices as the summer driving season kicks off. It's the pain at the pump, no one looks forward to."It's brutal," said driver Matt Brady."I think that I'm getting scared to even look at the price now," said driver Crystal Moralez.  "Pretty are shocked that they're pretty expensive right now," said driver Alan Ramirez.  The high prices are forcing drivers to adjust. "I just don't go out as much anymore, when I go to the store nearby I just either walk or ride the bike," added Ramirez.
