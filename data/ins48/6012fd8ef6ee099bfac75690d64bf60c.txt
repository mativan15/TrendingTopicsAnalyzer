(PTI Photo/Kunal Patil)(The bulls made a strong comeback this past week, with the benchmark indices recouping all the losses it made in the first three sessions to end the week on a strong note, thanks to an improvement in global sentiment and F&O related activity. Banking and auto stocks gained while metals and oil & gas stocks fell sharply in the week that saw the BSE Sensex rising 558.27 points or 1.02 per cent to 54,884.
