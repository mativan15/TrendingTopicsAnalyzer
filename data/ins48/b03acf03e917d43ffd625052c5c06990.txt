Wise Upgrades FY 2023 Views After 2Q Revenue Rose 60%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 06:39:00

By Michael Susin Wise PLC said Tuesday that it has again upgraded its fiscal 2023 guidance, citing robust revenue growth during the second quarter. The money transfer company said it expects income growth between 55% and 60% for the year ending March 31. Its adjusted earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization--which strips out exceptional and other one-off items--margin over the medium term is expected to be at or above 20% of total income, it said. Revenue for the second quarter rose to 211.
