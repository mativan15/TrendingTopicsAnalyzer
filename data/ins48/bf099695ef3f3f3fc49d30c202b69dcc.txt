The TAKE with Rick KleinThere was perhaps a touch of strident optimism -- if not some forgetfulness about 2016 -- in former House Speaker Paul Ryan's prediction last week that it doesn't matter if former President Donald Trump runs again because he won't win the GOP nomination.There was also a piece of strategic positioning in Colorado Republican Senate candidate Joe O'Dea saying on CNN over the weekend that he hopes Trump doesn't run -- coupled as it was with a promise to "actively campaign against Donald Trump" if he does.
