An emotional Kevin Spacey says he regrets apologising to actor Anthony Rapp over claims that he made an unwanted sexual advance when Mr Rapp was 14. It is alleged Kevin Spacey tried to seduce Anthony Rapp in a bedroom at a party in 1986 The House of Cards actor said he received pressure from publicists and lawyers to apologise to Mr Rapp and come out as gayMr Spacey told jurors he regrets his entire statement Mr Spacey is facing trial after Mr Rapp made the first in a string of sexual misconduct allegations against him in 2017.
