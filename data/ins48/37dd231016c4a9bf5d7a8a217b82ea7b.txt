Tired Truckers to Get Help Finding Safe, Legal Parking Spots
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-21 13:00:15

Commercial truck drivers are on the road for hours hauling cargo from place to place. During their long journey a safe place to stop and rest are hard to come by.
Truckers are vital to the nation's supply chain and the industry trade group for years has called for the need of more safe and legal parking locations. Overcrowded truck stops force rigs to park on the shoulders of interstates, highways or on/off ramps -- far from ideal options. To help alleviate truckers' woes, a San Diego-based tech company is launching a new offering that will help truckers find nearby safe places to park.
