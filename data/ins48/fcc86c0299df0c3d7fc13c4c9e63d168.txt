Big reshuffle at JP Morgan: India CEO Madhav Kalyan moves to a global role
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 06:30:00

JP Morgan India elevated its India investment banking head Kaustubh Kulkarni as the chief of India operations, replacing Madhav Kalyan who is set to take a bigger role in the Asia Pacific region, the bank said in an internal note of which ET has a copy. While Kalyan will head the US bank's payments business, Kulkarni is the new senior country officer India. Kalyan will succeed Sridhar Kanthadai, who has been appointed co-head of Global Payment Rails, announced last month. The incumbent India CEO has nearly three decades of experience in the banking and financial services sector.
