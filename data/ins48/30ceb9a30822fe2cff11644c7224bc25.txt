Oklahoma State Game Gives Longhorns Chance to Vanquish Road Struggles

news
2022-10-17 23:42:11

College Sports

Steve Sarkisian acknowledged the difficulty of winning on the road and how the Longhorns will handle doing so.

When the Texas Longhorns outlasted the Iowa State Cyclones on Saturday in their 24-21 win, they seemingly got the monkey of not finishing games off of their back. At least for now, that is, as one game is not indicative of an entire shift in culture or composure.

However, it is a step forward for a Longhorns team that under coach Steve Sarkisian struggled to hang on to second half leads in 2021.
