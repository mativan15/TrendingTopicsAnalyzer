YOU might be missing out on better phone calls – check your iPhone settings now.
There's a clever iPhone feature that can significantly boost your call quality.
WiFi Calling can boost your iPhone phone callsCredit: Apple / The Sun
If you've ever made a phone call with bad cell reception, it can be infuriating.
But the iPhone has the ability to make phone calls over WiFi too.
The feature is tucked away in your iPhone settings, and simply needs to be toggled on.
"When your iPhone has a low cellular signal, use WiFi Calling to make and receive calls through a WiFi network," Apple explained.
