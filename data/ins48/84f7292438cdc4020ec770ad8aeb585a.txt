Local job numbers up; manufacturing up 7.8%
yahoo.com
economics
2022-10-21 23:38:00

Oct. 21—MANKATO — Job numbers continued their strong growth in the Mankato region in September, with manufacturing jobs surging nearly 8% year over year.

Blue Earth and Nicollet counties combined increased job numbers by 2.9% in September compared to a year earlier. Manufacturers added 7.8% to their job numbers while the service sector added 1.8%. Government jobs were down 0.6% year over year.

Average hourly earnings in the Mankato area in September were $32.66, up from $28.22 a year ago.

There were 56,906 jobs in the two counties in September, up from 55,319 a year earlier.
