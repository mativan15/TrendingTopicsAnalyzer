A new report reveals the best — and worst — days to book a flight

Crunching the data on millions of airline passengers flying on more than 200,000 daily flights reveals some interesting trends that can save you money the next time you fly.For starters: Never book your airfare on a Friday. That's when prices are at their highest.The new 2023 Air Travel Hacks Report from Airlines Reporting Corporation and Expedia released on Wednesday offers hacks, tips, and tricks on booking your next flight.The eighth annual report leverages ARC's insights from the world's largest air ticketing dataset which includes more than 15 billion passenger flights on 490 airlines.
