F1 Solutions acquired by Houston firm
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-18 02:25:00

Houston, Texas-based managed IT and cybersecurity services company Meriplex has acquired an Alabama-based firm.

Huntsville-based F1 Solutions was acquired on Sept. 29. Financial details were not disclosed.

F1 has managed security and IT services for defense contractors and businesses in North Alabama for 25 years.

"We are excited to welcome F1 Solutions to the Meriplex organization as they will complement our growing cybersecurity and operational teams while adding vertical expertise around government compliance," said Meriplex CEO David Henley.
