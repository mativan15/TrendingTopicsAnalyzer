Netflix Unveils Key Art, New Stories in Junji Ito's 'Maniac: Japanese Tales of the Macabre'

Netflix Anime and Junji Ito have shared the key art, voice cast and four new stories that are joining their upcoming series, Maniac: Japanese Tales of the Macabre.

In addition to adapting the Japanese horror mangaka's Tomie, Souichi and The Hanging Balloons stories, the show will also feature Ito's Ice Cream Truck, Library Vision, Headless Statue and Tomb Town, with Ryotaro Okiayu, Sara Matsumoto, Takatsugu Chikamatsu, Ryohei Kimura, M・A・O, Aya Uchida, Yuki Kaji, Tomoe Hanba, Fumiko Orikasa and Takashi Kondo lending their voices to bring these four stories to life.
