'Stop the vicious PR attack!' Meghan Markle's half-brother erupts over dad's treatment
express.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 09:44:00

Meghan Markle's estranged father Thomas Markle was admitted to hospital last week, following a stroke. Speaking to Dan Wootton on GB News, Meghan's half-brother Thomas Markle Jr discussed how the Duchess attending the scene of the fatal Texas school shooting, but not choosing to visit her father, is the "most insensitive thing" he has seen in his life.In the interview, Thomas Markle Jr said: "I honestly don't know what Meghan's agenda is, I'm just telling you from my standpoint of view."It's totally wrong what she's done".
