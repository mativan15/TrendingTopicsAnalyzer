Zouma given community service after attacking cat
bbc.co.uk
sport
2022-06-01 12:45:00

Zouma is a regular starter for Premier League side West HamWest Ham United defender Kurt Zouma has been ordered to carry out 180 hours of community service after he pleaded guilty to kicking and slapping his cat. The 27-year-old has also been banned from keeping cats for five years.He had admitted two offences under the Animal Welfare Act at a hearing at Thames Magistrates' Court in May.Zouma's brother Yoan, who plays for Dagenham and Redbridge, was ordered to carry out 140 hours of community service at the 15-minute hearing.
