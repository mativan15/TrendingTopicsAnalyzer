Cumberland County Man To Pay $900,000 For Violations Of The False Claims Act
justice.gov
news
2022-06-03 15:46:57

HARRISBURG, PA —The United States Attorney's Office for the Middle District of Pennsylvania announced that Rodney L. Yentzer, of Cumberland County, has agreed to pay the United States $900,000 to resolve civil liability for alleged violations of the False Claims Act. According to United States Attorney, John C. Gurganus, between 2017 and 2019,  Yentzer, through a group of pain clinics he controlled known as Pain Medicine of York (PMY), caused the submission of false claims for payment to Medicare.
