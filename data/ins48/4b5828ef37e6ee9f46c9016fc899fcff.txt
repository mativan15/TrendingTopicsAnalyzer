KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy Nikki CourtneyJun 2, 2022Where is the money that you pay to fill your gas tank going? It's easy to direct anger at the corner gas station, but Patrick DeHaan, head of petroleum analysis for GasBuddy.com, says the corner station is struggling to find someone to run the register inside. 'Who is making the most? Oil companies are doing extremely well, as are refiners, but the closer you get to retail the less money there is to be made. So the big winners, oil companies and refineries,' DeHaan tells KTRH News.
