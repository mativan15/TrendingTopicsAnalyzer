Apple Workers Vote To Unionize Second U.S. Store

The Apple logo is seen at an Apple Store in Brooklyn, New York, U.S. October 23, 2020. 
Apple Inc retail workers voted to form a union at an Oklahoma location, the U.S. National Labor Relations Board (NLRB) said on Friday, making it the tech giant's second U.S. store to organize.Employees at the Apple Penn Square store in Oklahoma City voted 56 to 32 in support of joining the Communications Workers of America Union (CWA), securing the needed majority, according to a tally by the NLRB.The move to organize spread to new industries during the pandemic, sparked by concerns about workplace safety.
