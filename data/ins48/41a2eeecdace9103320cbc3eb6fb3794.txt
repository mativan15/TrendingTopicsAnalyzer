Ryder System Boosts Q2, FY22 Outlook
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-06-03 14:17:27

(RTTNews) - Ahead of its Investor Day meeting in New York City later on Friday, logistics and transportation company Ryder System, Inc. (R) raised its earnings and adjusted earnings guidance for the second quarter and the full-year 2022 to reflect better-than-expected performance across our business. The company said its increased outlook particularly reflects earlier-than-expected realization of benefits from pricing initiatives in all business segments and strong rental and used vehicle sales performance in the second quarter.
