Multiple attacks are alleged to have taken place on Collier Street, to the south-west of Maidstone (Image: Google)According to local reports, multiple attacks are alleged to have taken place on Collier Street, to the south-west of Maidstone. The most recent encounter, on May 2, is said to have led to one dog being killed.One local recounted an alleged attempted mauling, in which four men had to prize the attacking dog off her pet.Kent police said in a statement that they had spoken to the owner, but had not identified any criminal acts.
