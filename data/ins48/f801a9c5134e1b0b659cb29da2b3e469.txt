2022 Wilbur Cross Medals recognize alumni for outstanding work and service
yale.edu
news
2022-10-17 22:00:00

Four alumni of the Yale Graduate School of Arts and Sciences will receive 2022 Wilbur Lucius Cross Medals in honor of their outstanding work and service.
The Graduate School and the Graduate School Alumni Association annually award the Wilbur Cross Medal to alumni to honor exceptional work in scholarship, public service, teaching, or academic administration.
