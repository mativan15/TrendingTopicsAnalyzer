Chegg Announces Increased Authorization of Securities Repurchase Program by $1 Billion

SANTA CLARA, Calif., June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Chegg, Inc. (NYSE: CHGG), the leading student-first connect learning platform, today announced its board of directors has authorized the increase of its previously announced $1 billion securities repurchase program, up to an aggregate of $2 billion of Chegg's common stock and/or convertible notes, through open market purchases, block trades and/or in privately negotiated transactions or pursuant to Rule 10b5-1 plans, in compliance with applicable securities laws and other legal requirements.
