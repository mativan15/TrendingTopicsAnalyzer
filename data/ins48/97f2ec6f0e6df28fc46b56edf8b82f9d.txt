AgenciesChopra said JB plans to have at least 7 brands in top 300 by end of FY23.JB Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals (JBCPL) on Friday unveiled a new logo and identity as the 45-year old company backed by PE firm KKR is trying to evolve as per patient needs with an aggressive India focussed strategy.
JBCPL will now be identified as JB and with a motto of 'Good People for Good Health'. 'In 45 years, we at JB have built a strong foundation of integrity, trust and reliability. Now we are taking the next leap forward towards becoming more agile, lean and simple," said Nikhil Chopra, CEO & whole-time director of JB.
