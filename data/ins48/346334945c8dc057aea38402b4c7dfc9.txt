PALO ALTO, Calif., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Why wait until you're dead to leave a legacy? Working 40 hours a week for 40 years to accumulate wealth to leave behind is an antiquated understanding of "leaving a legacy". What if there were ways to make a lasting impact that go beyond money or property?"It's not enough to think about what we want to leave behind when we're gone," says Rebecca Cafiero, founder of The Pitch Cub. "Asking the question, 'how can I LIVE a legacy' changes everything."
