 The Business Innovation Centre which encourages and helps local businesses to grow through Innovation and invest in new products and services is holding a Business Advice Clinic on 16 November 2022 at the Tamworth Enterprise Centre.
Apart from offering business advice around Innovation the BIC is keen to promote its £9,000 New Product Development Grant which is designed to help Staffordshire small and medium sized companies operating on a Business to Business (B2B) basis pay for external support to help them research, protect, develop and launch to market a new product or service.
