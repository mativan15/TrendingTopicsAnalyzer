Fans of 'Indiana Jones' were thrilled as the protagonist, Harrison Ford, revealed the release date of the next movie in the franchise. According to Ford, his upcoming flick, 'Indiana Jones 5', would hit the screens on 30th June 2023. On Thursday, 26th May 2022, Harrison Ford attended the Star Wars Celebration and made a surprise appearance at the Lucasfilm panel. As it turned out, his appearance was solely to reveal the date for his upcoming and highly awaited movie, 'Indiana Jones 5'.

Aged 79, Ford approached the stage to the 'Indy' theme of Indiana Jones's iconic franchise.
