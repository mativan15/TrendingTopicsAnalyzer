Ibero-American Forum Challenges of Labor FormalizationThe Minister of Inclusion, Social Security and Migration of Spain, Jose Luis Escriva speaks during the Ibero-American Forum Challenges of Labor Formalization in Bogota, Colombia, 20 October 2022. The Minister of Inclusion, Social Security and Migration of Spain, Jose Luis Escriva, defended this Thursday, before Ibero-American ministers, the social management of the Spanish Government in the face of the pandemic, with the Minimum Vital Income and the labor reform as flags, and that it has allowed an unexpected increase in social security contributions.
