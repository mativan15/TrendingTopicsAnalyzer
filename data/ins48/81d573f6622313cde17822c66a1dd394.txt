Published: 07:04 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 09:24 EDT, 21 October 2022 By Liz Hampton and Ruhi Soni Oct 21 (Reuters) - Top oilfield services provider Schlumberger Ltd reported its strongest adjusted profit since 2015 on Friday, with third-quarter earnings that topped Wall Street expectations as a surge in oil and gas prices led to more drilling activity. Oil and gas producers are looking to increase production as crude prices stay near eight-year highs, boosting demand for Schlumberger's equipment, services and technology.
