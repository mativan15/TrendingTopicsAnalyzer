Beyoncé accused of secretly changing songs from 'Renaissance' album by fans
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-19 10:37:18

A fan theory about Beyoncé's latest album, Renaissance, is gaining traction as more people convince themselves she has been tweaking the songs online since its release.Beyoncé dropped her seventh solo studio album at the end of July, and it quickly became a smash with both critics and fans alike.But since then, fans are sure the singer has changed some things including lyrics, background vocals and even pauses between songs. Others claimed the album sounded different between various music streaming platforms.
