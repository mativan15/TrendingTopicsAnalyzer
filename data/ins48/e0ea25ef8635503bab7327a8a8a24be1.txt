By Afp Published: 09:16 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 09:16 EDT, 2 June 2022 South African President Cyril Ramaphosa has denied claims that he organised the kidnapping and bribery of thieves who stole money from one of his properties South African President Cyril Ramaphosa Thursday confirmed a robbery at one of his properties, but denied claims levelled by a former spy chief that he had kidnapped and bribed the thieves. Former intelligence boss Arthur Fraser late Wednesday accused the president of organising the kidnapping, questioning and bribery of thieves who stole $4 million from his farm north of Johannesburg.
