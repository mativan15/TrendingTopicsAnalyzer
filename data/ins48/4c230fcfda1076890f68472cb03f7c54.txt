Passive investing in an index fund is a good way to ensure your own returns roughly match the overall market. When you buy individual stocks, you can make higher profits, but you also face the risk of under-performance. Investors in Elixirr International plc (LON:ELIX) have tasted that bitter downside in the last year, as the share price dropped 23%. That's disappointing when you consider the market declined 14%. Elixirr International may have better days ahead, of course; we've only looked at a one year period.
