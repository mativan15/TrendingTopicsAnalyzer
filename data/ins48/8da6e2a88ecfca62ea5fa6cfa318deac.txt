 Corgis may be the animal most commonly associated with Queen Elizabeth II, but any royalist will tell you that Her Majesty has a special place in her heart for horses. The Queen's official portrait for her 96th birthday is evidence of this, picturing the monarch beaming while holding the reins of two of her own stunningly white fell ponies, Bybeck Nightingale and Bybeck Katie.
