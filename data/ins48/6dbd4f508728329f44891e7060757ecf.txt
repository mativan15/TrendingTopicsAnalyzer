Dogecoin co-creator calls Elon Musk a 'grifter' who had trouble running basic code
businessinsider.com
news
2022-05-31 08:23:30

The co-creator of Dogecoin has made some harsh comments about Elon Musk, calling the Tesla and SpaceX CEO a "grifter" who wasn't capable of running basic code. Jackson Palmer, the co-creator of Dogecoin, spoke to independent Australian news site Crikey on topics ranging from Musk to the future of cryptocurrency.Palmer is best known for being one of the people behind Dogecoin, a cryptocurrency coin based on a meme that launched in 2013. Partly thanks to Musk, Dogecoin is now one of the world's most valuable crypto coins.
