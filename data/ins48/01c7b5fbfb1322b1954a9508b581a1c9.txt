October 17, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kapnick Insurance, a premier independent insurance brokerage, has become the latest intermediary to partner with cyber risk analytics specialist CyberCube.



Kapnick is licensing CyberCube's Broking Manager to help quantify financial exposure for its clients to make better coverage and limit decisions. Kapnick is a Top 100 independent insurance brokerage in the US, offering a comprehensive suite of insurance solutions.
