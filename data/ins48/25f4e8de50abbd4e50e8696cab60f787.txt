Over the past few days, retail stocks as a group have recovered in price, some quite dramatically.
Some, but not all. One instance of the latter is clothier Express (EXPR 0.39%), whose stock according to S&P Global Market Intelligence was down by over 21% week to date as of early Friday morning before market open. Investors remained cautious about the company despite the estimates-beating quarter it reported over the course of the week.
So what
The surprisingly weak first-quarter results of onetime retail investor darling Target, published last Wednesday, sent shock waves throughout the sector.
