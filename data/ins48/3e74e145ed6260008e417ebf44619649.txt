A Sydney, N.S., man is once again setting up a spooky display that may be the scariest property in the city this Halloween 2022. Rikki Tortola has been transforming his front yard, in the city's Kytes Hill subdivision, into a haunted field of horrors for a few years now. Lately, it's been attracting a lot of attention. "Basically, it's an obsession that I have with my daughter," Tortola said. 'Everything in behind the gate there comes to life. So everything either moves, says something, jumps at you.
