Fintech company, IOUX looking to raise $10 mn, to promote ease of doing business

The government has granted patents for a secured system and method for generating and monitoring a smart contract using computer- implemented distributed ledger; and contractual dispute resolution mechanism (CDRM).NEW DELHI: Fintech company IOUX, which specialises in providing a holistic solution to execute legal and financial agreements to promote ease of doing business, is looking to raise $10 million (about Rs 77 crore) in a few months to further strengthen its presence in the sector, its co-founder Vineet K Sachdev said.
