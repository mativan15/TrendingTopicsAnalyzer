In Shanxi, carving tradition set in stone
chinadaily.com.cn
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

A craftsman carves a stone lion in Xizhuang village in Xinjiang county of Yuncheng, Shanxi province. [Photo by Gao Xinsheng/For chinadaily.com.cn] Benefiting from local government policies, residents of Xizhuang village in Xinjiang county of Yuncheng, Shanxi province, have the stone processing business boom in recent years. Jiangzhou stone carving, a traditional skill originating in Xinjiang county, has a history of more than 1,000 years. In 2017, Jiangzhou stone carving was listed as a provincial-level intangible cultural heritage, encouraging more residents of Xizhuang to join the business.
