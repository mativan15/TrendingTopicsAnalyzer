Michael Vi Thesis In August, I highlighted ASML Holding N.V. (NASDAQ:ASML) stock as a 'Sell' idea, arguing that the stock is overvalued with 40% downside. Since my article, ASML stock has lost about 20%, but the valuation is clearly still above my $315/share price target. Although I continue to believe that ASML stock is priced at an excessively rich valuation, I am slowly turning more positive on the Dutch-based semiconductor equipment maker. ASML's Q3 results have clearly highlighted that demand for semiconductor equipment remains strong - despite macroeconomic challenges.
