Published: 07:14 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 07:14 EDT, 30 May 2022 The first banknote to ever feature a picture of the Queen will be auctioned off to mark her Platinum Jubilee this week.A five shilling note issued in Bermuda, dated October 20, 1952 - the year Her Majesty came to the throne - is expected to be bought for up to £500.It is just one of 500 historic banknotes, all featuring a portrait of the monarch, that are set to go under the hammer at Noonan's Auctioneers in Mayfair, central London on Tuesday.
