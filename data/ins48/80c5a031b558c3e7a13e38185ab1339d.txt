CHICAGO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 1, 2022--UBS Private Wealth Management today announced that Coyle, Schmitt & Beaudoin Wealth Management Group have joined the firm in Chicago, Illinois. The seven-person team, co-founded and led by Pat Coyle, John Schmitt and Jean Beaudoin, advises ultra high net worth individuals and families, and manages more than $1.3 billion in client assets.This press release features multimedia.
