Photo: Getty ImagesWill Kelsea Ballerini team up with Carly Pearce and Kelly Clarkson for a live performance of their sassy collaboration? The Country Music Association Awards ceremony is around the corner, and unnamed production sources for the upcoming show have reportedly told TMZ that Ballerini, Pearce and Clarkson are planning 'a very big and powerful performance' of the song on Ballerini's latest album. Ballerini released Subject To Change last month. One of the most highly-anticipated songs on the 15-track collection was the 'sassbomb yeehaw collab moment' between the three artists (all of whom happen to be divorced women), 'You're Drunk, Go Home.
