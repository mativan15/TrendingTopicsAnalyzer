The Hollywood Reporter - Movie News Click here to read the full article.Brandon Kyle Goodman's newly published You Gotta Be You: How to Embrace This Messy Life and Step Into Who You Really Are is much more than an advice book.Goodman's new book is part memoir, following the actor and writer's journey toward fully embracing their many identities, which include being Black, queer and non-binary. It is also an engaging an Ask Me Anything for anyone who may have questions about identifying as non-binary, the distinctions between sex, gender and gender expression, and how all of those things can intersect.
