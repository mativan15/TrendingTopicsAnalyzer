Just Stop Oil protest on Dartford Crossing ends as activists removed from bridge

2022-10-18 16:59:23

The two Just Stop Oil protesters who blocked the Dartford Crossing have been removed from the bridge by police.The pair claimed in a statement they had 'successfully disrupted oil supplies to Kent and the southeast for 36 hours' and said more protesters would be 'stepping up day after day, causing disruption and putting their liberty on the line to demand that the government ends new oil and gas.'Just Stop Oil tweeted: 'Morgan and Marcus have agreed to cooperate with police and leave the bridge. JustStopOil will continue in civil resistance to harmful politics and our criminal government.
