Whatever you're thinking of selling, from candles and cakes to life coaching sessions or spare car parts, if you want your business to succeed, you need to be online.Your business is all about you, to a degree, you've been there from the start and poured your heart and soul into its creation but it's also about your customers. Where they are in the world, what they like to do, what makes them tick and (most importantly) how they want to shop.Growing a successful business is a delicate balancing act.
