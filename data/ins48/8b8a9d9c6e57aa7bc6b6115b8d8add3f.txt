Xinhua | Updated: 2022-10-18 09:55 Iraq's newly-elected President Abdul Latif Rashid attends a ceremony in Baghdad, Iraq, Oct 17, 2022. [Photo/Xinhua] BAGHDAD - Iraq's newly-elected President Abdul Latif Rashid assumed office on Monday, expressing his hope for a quick formation of the new government. "We keep in mind what the Iraqi people are waiting for from the new government, which we hope will be formed quickly, and be strong, efficient, and unified to meet the people's aspirations for security, stability, and services," Rashid said in a televised speech during a ceremony held in the presidential palace in Baghdad.
