New Wauwatosa brewery closes Sunday after running low on beer
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-17 16:41:00

Just two days after opening in Wauwatosa, Neenah-based Lion's Tail Brewing Co. needed to close Sunday because it was running low on beer.

Lion's Tail Brewing opened Oct. 14 at the site of the former Wisconsin Garden & Pet Supply building at 8520 W. North Ave. Wisconsin Garden & Pet Supply closed in July 2021 after 68 years in business.

The brewery announced the Sunday closure on its Facebook page.

"Of all the things that have could have gone wrong on our opening weekend, having too much demand or more demand than we can possibly keep up with.
