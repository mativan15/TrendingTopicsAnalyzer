Rep. Jared Golden is running against Republican Bruce Poliquin in Maine's 2nd Congressional District for the second time.

The 2nd District encompasses nearly 80% of the state, including Augusta, the state capital.

Abortion is expected to be a hot-button topic in the race.

Rep. Jared Golden, a Democratic incumbent, once again faces off against Republican Bruce Poliquin in Maine's 2nd Congressional District. Golden unseated Poliquin in 2018.
