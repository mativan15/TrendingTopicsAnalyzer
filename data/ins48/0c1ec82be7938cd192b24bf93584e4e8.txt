This smallcap company announces 3:2 rights share issue
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 15:45:00

Smallcap company on Wednesday announced a 3:2 rights share issue. The company has fixed Rs 16.12 crore as the issue size. In the filing with the exchanges, the company specified all the details pertaining to the rights issue and mentioned that 64.5 lakh shares will be issued in this regard. For the offer, the firm has fixed price at Rs 25, including a premium of Rs 15 per share. The rights issue price is at a considerable discount from the stock's closing price of Rs 43.05 per share on Wednesday.
