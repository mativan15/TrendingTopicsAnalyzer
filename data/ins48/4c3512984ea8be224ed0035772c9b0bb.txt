Oct. 17—ROCHESTER — For over 20 years, Bertrand Memorials has been a staple in the Rochester community for those who need to bury loved ones who have passed away.

The business, owned by Bill Bertrand, will remain in Rochester, but a change of scenery is happening as Bertrand Memorials is moving a few blocks away from its current location to a new spot basically around the corner at 2125 Third Ave. SE.
