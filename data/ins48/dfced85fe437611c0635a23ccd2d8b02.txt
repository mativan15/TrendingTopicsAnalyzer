Families struggle as lost applications, data entry glitches block Covid compensation
indiatimes.com
science
2022-06-01 19:23:00

Panaji: While the state government compensated a number of families of Covid-19 victims with Rs 2 lakh following the deadly second wave of the pandemic, there are still several reeling under debts taken to foot fat hospital bills who are yet to receive the much-needed assistance.Flavia Lobo applied for the state's compensation scheme for Covid-19 victim's families in August last year, after her husband succumbed to the virus. Nine months on, she is still waiting for the government to fulfil its promise.
