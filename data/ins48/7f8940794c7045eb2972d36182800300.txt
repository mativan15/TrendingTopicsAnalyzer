Arrests made in Beverly hills follow-home robbery from April

2022-05-27 19:15:00

By CBSLA Staff May 27, 2022 / 12:15 PM / CBS Los Angeles Authorities have made a pair of arrests in connection with a late-April follow-home robbery in Beverly Crest, where four people had over $70k in money and jewelry stolen from them. The initial incident occurred on April 20 at around 12:45 a.m., when four people on their way from dining at a restaurant in Beverly Grove were followed back to their short-term rental property located on Coldwater Canyon Drive. As soon as they had exited their vehicle, they were confronted by three suspects, all of which were armed with firearms.
