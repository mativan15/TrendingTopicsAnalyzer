Electrician breaks world record for fastest run across AustraliaNedd Brockmann, an Australian electrician, reacts after crossing the finish line at the end of his charity run from Perth to Sydney, at the North Bondi Surf Life Saving Club in Sydney, Australia, 17 October 2022. Brockmann ran 4,000km to raise money for the homeless, breaking the world record for the fastest ever run across Australia in 43 days. The previous record of 67 days was set in 2002 by Donnie Maclurcan. EPA/STEVEN SAPHORE A
