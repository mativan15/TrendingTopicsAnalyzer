Businesses urged to claim their share of multi-million class-action settlement with Visa, Mastercard
2022 The Globe and Mail Inc.
business
2022-05-30 19:59:48

Small merchants, defined as those with under $5-million in annual revenue, can receive $30 a year for each year they accepted credit-card payments, up to a maximum of $600. Small merchants do not need to provide proof of their claim. Larger merchants can claim more money from the $131-million settlement fund, but are required to submit supporting documentation.Ryan Remiorz/The Canadian PressBusiness groups are urging merchants to sign up for a multimillion-dollar class-action settlement with Visa and MasterCard over credit-card processing fees.
