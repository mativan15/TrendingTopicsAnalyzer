Soluna Holdings Shares Fall 21% After Public Offering News
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-21 21:33:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Shares of Soluna Holdings Inc. were lower in Friday's late-trading session, after the company said it plans to sell shares of its common stock in an underwritten public offering. Soluna also said all of the shares of common stock are to be sold by the company. At 5:17 p.m. ET, the company's stock had fallen 21.11% to trade at $1.42 per share.
