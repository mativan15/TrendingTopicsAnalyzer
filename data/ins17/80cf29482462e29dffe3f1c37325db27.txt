NEW YORK and SAN DIEGO, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Wolf Haldenstein Adler Freeman & Herz LLP announces that a federal securities class action lawsuit has been filed in the United States District Court for the Southern District of California against Humbl LLC ('Humbl' or the 'Company') (OTC: HMBL) common stock and/or the unregistered Humbl ETX securities between November 21, 2020 and May 19, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period'). All investors who purchased the shares of Humbl LLC and incurred losses are advised to contact the firm immediately at classmember@whafh.
