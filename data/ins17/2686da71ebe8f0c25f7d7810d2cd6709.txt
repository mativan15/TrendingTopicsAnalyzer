Exclusive: A union leader fears the contract will be awarded overseas, denying British staff work worth hundreds of millions of poundsThe Government is due to order three Fleet Solid Support shipsCampaigners calling for naval supply ships to be built in Britain today blasted 'secrecy' surrounding the deal. The Government is preparing to place a £1.6billion contract for three Fleet Solid Support vessels which will restock Royal Navy warships at sea. But unions fear much of the work will go overseas - depriving UK staff of hundreds of millions of pounds' worth of contracts.
