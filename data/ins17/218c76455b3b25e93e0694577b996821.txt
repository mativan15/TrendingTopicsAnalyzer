Image source, PA MediaImage caption, The government is encouraging islanders over 50 to get both Covid and flu jabsOnly a third of people in Jersey over 50 who are eligible for a Covid booster have received their autumn jab, officials have confirmed.The government launched its booster scheme in September for islanders aged over 80 and in care homes.It said it hoped to encourage more people to get vaccinations for Covid and flu to ahead of winter.Vaccination bosses said more people were coming forward now than at the start of the rollout.
