IIFL Fin, ZestMoney join hands to build USD 1 bn loan book in 2-3 yrs
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 17:15:00

Non-banking finance company IIFL Finance and digital EMI financing platform ZestMoney on Friday announced a partnership through which they aim to have a loan book of USD 1 billion (about Rs 8,274 crore) over the next 2-3 years. Under the partnership, IIFL Finance will provide the financing to potentially new to credit customers while doing online and offline purchases through ZestMoney platform. As an industry-first association, IIFL Finance will get access to a new customer base on the ZestMoney platform which has disbursed USD 1 billion through their platform so far, a release said.
