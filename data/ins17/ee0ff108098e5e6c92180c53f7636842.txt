NewsIndia NewsWomen sweep top 3 in UPSC exam, Delhi student Shruti Sharma 1stNEW DELHI: Women have aced Civil Services Examination (CSE) 2021 by cornering the top three slots, with history graduate from Delhi's St Stephen's College Shruti Sharma No. 1 in the merit list. This is the fifth time in the past 10 years that a woman has secured the top rank. In CSE 2014, the top four slots had gone to women candidates. As per CSE 2021 results declared by the UPSC on Monday, there are 177 women (25.8%) among the 685 successful candidates recommended to various services.
