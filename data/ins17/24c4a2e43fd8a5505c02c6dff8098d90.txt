The runaway train: Amtrak's bloated $35 billion Gateway boondoggle, far more than a tunnel, cries out for a rethink
yahoo.com
news
2022-05-29 08:00:00

Six years ago today, we published an editorial called 'Tunnel blockheads,' opposing Amtrak's plan to raze a big chunk of Midtown for an unneeded Penn Station annex, called Penn South, part of the larger Gateway boondoggle. It was the first of 90 Gateway editorials and counting (sorry, not sorry).

In every aspect of the scheme, from the Portal North Bridge, to digging new tubes under the Hudson to repairing the existing tubes to improving Penn, we have discovered and documented lies, waste, stupidity and even fraud, gleaned from examining thousands of pages of official records from Amtrak, NJTransit, the Port Authority, the Federal Transit Administration and Federal Railroad Administration.
