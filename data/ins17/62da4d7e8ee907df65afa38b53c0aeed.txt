Hospitality staff lose £200m a year in tips as Tory pledge still not made law
mirror.co.uk
politics
2022-10-16 21:30:00

Exclusive: The Tories pledged to stop restaurants and bars deducting money from tips for workers in their 2019 manifesto, but the promise still has not been enshrined in lawHospitality workers are being cheated out of £200million a year after Government failure to ensure they get tips from customers. The Tories pledged to stop restaurants and bars deducting money from tips in its manifesto at the last election, but it still has not been made law. New figures, released by the Government, estimate that around £200million was deducted in tips from front of house staff by their employers across the hospitality sector in 2021.
