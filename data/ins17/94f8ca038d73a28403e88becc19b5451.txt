ContextLogic (WISH -2.22%), better known as Wish, was once a fast-growing e-commerce platform, but since going public at $24 per share in Dec. 2020, it has become one of the worst-performing tech stocks on the market. Shares are down nearly 95% from the IPO -- as a Wish investor, I have been appalled by its poor performance.
