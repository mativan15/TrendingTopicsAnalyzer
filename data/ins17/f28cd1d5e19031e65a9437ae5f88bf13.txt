Intuitive Surgical stock (NASDAQ NDAQ : ISRG) has seen an 11% rise in a month, while it is up 14% in a week. This compares with a 2% fall for the broader S&P500 index in a month and a 2% rise in a week. The rise for ISRG can be attributed to solid Q3 performance, with revenue falling in line and earnings slightly above our estimates.
