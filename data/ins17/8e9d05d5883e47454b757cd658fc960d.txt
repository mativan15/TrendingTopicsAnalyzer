Goodfellow (TSE:GDL) Is Increasing Its Dividend To CA$0.50
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-21 10:10:20

Goodfellow Inc. (TSE:GDL) will increase its dividend from last year's comparable payment on the 10th of November to CA$0.50. This will take the dividend yield to an attractive 8.4%, providing a nice boost to shareholder returns.

Goodfellow's Payment Has Solid Earnings Coverage

If the payments aren't sustainable, a high yield for a few years won't matter that much. Prior to this announcement, Goodfellow's dividend was only 27% of earnings, however it was paying out 193% of free cash flows.
