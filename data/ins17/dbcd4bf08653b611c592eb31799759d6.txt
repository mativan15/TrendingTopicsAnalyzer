Juan marcos borsatto/iStock via Getty Images GrowGeneration Corp. (NASDAQ:GRWG), once an investment favorite in the cannabis sector, has seen its stock clobbered over the past 18 months. The once high-flying hydroponics company has seen growth turn deeply negative this year as the cannabis sector faces headwinds nationwide. Isn't the cannabis sector supposed to be a high-growth industry as more states come online for legalized sales? The issue is that GRWG is correlated not to the growth of the legal market, but the volatility of the overall cannabis market.
