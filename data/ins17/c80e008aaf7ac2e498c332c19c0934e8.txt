Fighting terrorism must be global priority: UN chief Antonio Guterres
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 12:15:00

United Nations Secretary General Antonio Guterres on Wednesday said fighting terrorism must be a global priority. Speaking after paying floral tributes to martyrs of the 26/11 terror attacks in Mumbai at Taj hotel, Guterres also said that no cause can justify terrorism of any kind. "Fighting terrorism should be the global priority of every country," said Guterres. People need to recognise the diversity and richness of cultures to stay together, Guterres said. "Terrorism is absolute evil and has no place in today's world.
