Lethbridge business blames inflation and pandemic for store closures
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-17 21:45:47

A Lethbridge business, which ran two shops, says the ongoing challenges of inflation and the COVID-19 pandemic are the main reasons it had to close down recently. Officials say Mocha Cabana, along with its sister location Mocal Local, were unable to remain open during the troubling state of the economy. "Our small, local business faced impossible odds over the past two years," a sign posted to Mocha Cabana's front door read. "The pandemic and current economic state has proved to be too much for us to overcome.
