The NSW government has scrapped a proposed $350 million dam in the state's north, which local landholders feared would inundate several properties.The NSW government announced funding for a business case in late 2019The Mole River proposal faced widespread local oppositionThe government says it remains committed to two other regional dam projectsThe government announced in 2019 it would develop a final business case for a proposal to dam the Mole River west of Tenterfield, near the border with Queensland.
