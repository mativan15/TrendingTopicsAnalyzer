Prime Minister and Auckland mayor disagree halting Three Waters work will increase rates
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-19 03:45:54

Auckland mayor-elect Wayne Brown disagrees with Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern's assertion that stopping Three Waters in Auckland will mean rates rises. Ardern's comment on Tuesday came after Brown wrote to the chairperson of Watercare Services Limited, Margaret Devlin, on Monday asking the agency to stop work on the water reforms. Ardern said modelling shows that without the water reforms, Auckland ratepayers will pay more. However, a spokesperson for Brown said the new mayor disagreed. 'The Mayor has no intention of getting into a tit-for-tat with the government, but it is wrong.
