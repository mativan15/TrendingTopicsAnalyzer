Persona 5 Royal is heading to Xbox this week (Image: ATLUS SEGA MICROSOFT)Persona 5 Royal - one of the most celebrated JRPGs ever - is heading to Xbox this week. The beloved SEGA and Atlus game is launching on Friday, October 21 and will be available on Xbox Game Pass. An exact release time for Xbox Game Pass hasn't been confirmed yet but it has been revealed when Persona 5 Royal will be available on Steam.According to the countdown on the Steam page for P5R, the game will unlock on Valve's platform at 4am BST / 5am CEST on Friday, October 21.
