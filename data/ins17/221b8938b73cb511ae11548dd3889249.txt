iPhone 14 models to feature faster & power efficient 6GB RAM, leaks reveal upgraded devices
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-03 06:00:00

AgenciesThe latest development was spotted on the website of a Taiwanese research firm.Only a few days to Apple's WWDC, and more reports are surfacing around the upcoming iPhone 14 line-up. According to the latest reports, the Cupertino-based tech giant is planning to include 6GB RAM on all models in the iPhone 14 line-up. The development has been reported by Taiwanese research firm TrendForce. The next-generation iPhone 14 Pro models will likely come with upgraded, faster and power-efficient RAM known as LPDDR5, while the regular iPhone 14 and iPhone 14 Max models will feature LPDDR4X.
