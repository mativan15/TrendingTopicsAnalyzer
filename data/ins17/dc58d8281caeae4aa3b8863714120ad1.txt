Dy CM takes stock of municipal poll preparations
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 06:45:00

Prayagraj : Deputy Chief Minister Keshav Prasad Maurya held a meeting of Bharatiya Janata Party's ( BJP ) Kashi Pranth at district panchayat auditorium here on Friday to discuss the preparations for the upcoming municipal elections.'Today BJP is strong in the district panchayat cooperative societies because of the strength of its workers. Samajwadi Party (SP), Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) and Congress would be defeated in the upcoming polls,' he said.Maurya added that after winning the municipal body elections, good governance would prevail in the urban areas of the district.
