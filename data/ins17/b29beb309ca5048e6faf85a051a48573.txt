Check out these newly-created trails northwest of Saskatoon
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-03 22:44:04

A Saskatoon man wants more people to experience a new set of trails, about 30 kilometres northwest of the city. River Ridge Trails includes 10 kilometres of walking and biking paths, overlooking the North Saskatchewan River. In the winter, the trails are groomed for cross-country skiing. The property is pet friendly and suitable for kids. 'Everyone can come out and have a good time,' said Ryan Derksen, a cyclist and River Ridge Trails board member. 'We have green trails that you can walk, kids can ride.
