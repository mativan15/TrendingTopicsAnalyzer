Texas' Anti-Business, Pro-Fossil Fuel Law Is Spreading
gizmodo.com
news
2022-06-02 12:00:00

The Texas law that seeks to stop companies from being mean to fossil fuels is getting popular—even if its execution and implications are still murky. States across the country are looking at adopting similar bills to one passed in Texas that forbids the state from doing business with any institutions that 'boycott' fossil fuels.The original bill was passed last year, but its enforcement mechanisms are just now coming into play. The bill instructs the Texas comptroller to create a list of businesses that 'boycott' fossil fuels.
