By Jessica Stevenson October 20, 2022 / 1:34 PM / CBS Detroit (CBS DETROIT) - The City of Detroit continues its tradition of Halloween in the D with a variety of family-friendly activities.Last year, Halloween in the D was visited by about 30,000 children. Organizers said nearly 1 million pieces of candy were handed out, nearly $100,000 in donations were provided and more than 250 volunteers assisted with the Halloween activities.
