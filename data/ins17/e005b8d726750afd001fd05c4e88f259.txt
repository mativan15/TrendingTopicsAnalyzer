Published: 02:21 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 02:21 EDT, 19 October 2022 A disgruntled wedding guest has revealed her surprise after she was asked to pay £100 to contribute towards food and drink at a friend's wedding.Posting on Mumsnet, the British mother said she had been surprised when the wedding invitation arrived with the request for money, saying: 'Is this the new thing?' She took umbrage at the hefty cost, which would see her and her husband shell out £200 for some buffet food as well as drinks and a cash contribution for the honeymoon.
