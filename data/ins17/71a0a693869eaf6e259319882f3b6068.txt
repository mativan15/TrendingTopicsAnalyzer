October 19, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--EngageSmart, Inc. (NYSE: ESMT), a leading provider of vertically tailored customer engagement software and integrated payments solutions, has been named a Top Workplace 2022 for Employee Well-Being. The Employee Well-Being award honors Top Workplaces noted for putting employee health and wellness at the center of their workplace culture. The list is based solely on employee feedback gathered through a third-party survey administered by employee engagement technology provider Energage, LLC.
