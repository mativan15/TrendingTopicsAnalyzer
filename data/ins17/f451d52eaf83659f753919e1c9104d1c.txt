' I think Republicans realize how scared parents and kids are across this country. I think they realize that the answer this time cannot be nothing, that it's frankly a test of democracy, it's a test of the federal government as to whether we can deliver at a moment of just fierce anxiety amongst the American public.' — Sen. Chris Murphy That's Sen. Chris Murphy, a Connecticut Democrat, who said Sunday morning he was 'confident' and 'anxious' about the prospects of bipartisan gun reform in the country, after multiple mass shootings — including one at a Texas school that killed 21.
