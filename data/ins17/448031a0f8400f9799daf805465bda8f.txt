PTIAlia Bhatt gave fans a small glimpse of the much-awaited film Brahmastra, which is all set to release this year. Bhatt took to Instagram to share the teaser alongside the caption, 'In just 100 Days, BRAHMASTRA: Part One will be all yours.' The film stars newlyweds Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor in primary roles while Amitabh Bachchan, Mouni Roy, and Nagarjuna are cast in supporting roles. While fans have already been treated to a glimpse of the first look of Ranbir's character Shiva and Alia's character, Isha, the new teaser also unveiled Amitabh, Mouni, and Nagarjuna's first look from the film.
