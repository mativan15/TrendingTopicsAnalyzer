Housing intensification will change the face of Upper Hutt
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-19 04:00:00

Upper Hutt residents should get used to the site of Brittons house removal trucks around town. On Wednesday evening, Brittons moved a large four-bedroom villa on Fergusson Drive​ to Greytown. The 200Om² site and an adjacent section are part of a 15-lot development by Friday Homes. Government-mandated changes allowing greater intensification were only starting to have an impact but Upper Hutt mayor Wayne Guppy said the face of the city was already changing.
