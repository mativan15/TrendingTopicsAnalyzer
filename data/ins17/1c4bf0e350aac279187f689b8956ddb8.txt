Cyber Cell formed in every police station in Tiruchi district

news
2022-06-05 13:42:32

: Every police station in Tiruchi district now has a Cyber Cell to immediately attend to complaints from the general public relating to cyber offences. Three police personnel have been nominated as Cyber Support Officer in each police station falling under the Tiruchi City Police and Tiruchi Rural Police. The cell has been formed in the All Women Police Station as well.The Cyber Cell in each police station would be headed by an officer in the rank of Special Sub Inspector and assisted by one Grade I police constable and the station receptionist.
