May 27, 2022 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Law Offices of Frank R. Cruz reminds investors of the upcoming May 31, 2022 deadline to file a lead plaintiff motion in the class action filed on behalf of investors who acquired Lucid Group, Inc. ('Lucid' or the 'Company') (NASDAQ: LCID) common stock between November 15, 2021 and February 28, 2022, inclusive (the 'Class Period').



If you are a shareholder who suffered a loss, click here to participate.

On February 28, 2022, Lucid disclosed that it had only delivered approximately 125 EVs in 2021 – 452 less than expected – and would only produce between 12,000 and 14,000 EVs in 2022, despite previous claims that it would produce 20,000.
