In the past two days, the fraudster has sent messages to 32 hospital staffers, including doctors, the GMCH director told the police
Updated on Jun 02, 2022 02:33 AM IST
 

WhatsApp gift voucher scam:In a fresh case of WhatsApp gift voucher scam, a fraudster posed as the director-principal of Government Medical College and Hospital (GMCH), Sector 32, in an attempt to swindle 32 hospital staffers.Demanding immediate investigation into the matter, hospital director Dr Jasbinder Kaur told the police that someone had been seeking Amazon gift vouchers by contacting hospital staffers through WhatsApp number '8480451643' while using her image as the display picture.
