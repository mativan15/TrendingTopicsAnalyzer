Clinics now have a pre-written and completely configurable practice manual at their fingertips!BALLARAT CENTRAL, Australia, May 27, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Power Diary, an online practice management software, announced today the release of an in-platform Health Practice Operations Manual, a new feature available to Power Diary's global customer base of private health practices.This industry-first feature allows practice owners to create, implement and update a set of policies and procedures that support operational consistency, enable sustainable growth, and ensure compliance with their professional, ethical and regulatory obligations.
