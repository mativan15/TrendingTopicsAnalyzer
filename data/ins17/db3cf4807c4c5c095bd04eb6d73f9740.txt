AgenciesMohapatra said there was no clarity yet about under what head the department was going to tax transactions before April 1.New Delhi: US-based electronics retail company GameStop has launched a non-custodial crypto wallet, as a beta version, that will facilitate gamers to buy and sell their crypto-assets and NFTs without having to leave the game. A crypto wallet is where users can store all their virtual digital assets, including crypto tokens, coins and NFTs. Unlike MetaMask, GameStop is a non-custodial wallet, exclusively designed for gamers only.
