Task force moots age check scans for online gaming
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 18:11:57

Online gaming companies should be made to use facial recognition technologies to verify the age of players, an inter-ministerial task force of the Union government has recommended, citing growing concern around addiction to such applications, especially among children.The report of the task force, set up by the ministry of electronics and information technology (Meity) to begin work on new gaming regulations, includes recommendations from the Union ministries of home, sports and youth affairs, information and broadcasting, finance, law and consumer affairs.
