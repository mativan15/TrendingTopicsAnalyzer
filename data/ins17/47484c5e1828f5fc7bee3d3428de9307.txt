Indian shares stay at three-wk highs as oil slips overnight
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-19 02:53:31

BENGALURU (Reuters) -Indian shares on Wednesday hovered at three-week highs hit in the previous session, tracking an overnight fall in oil prices and gains in other Asian equities following the overnight rally in Wall Street on strong corporate reports.

The NSE Nifty 50 index was 0.51% higher at 17,576.50 as of 0432 GMT, while the S&P BSE Sensex climbed 0.54% to 59,281.66. Both indexes were on track to add to three straight session of gains.
