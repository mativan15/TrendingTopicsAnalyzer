Matthew Perry opens up about his battle with addiction: 'Being on 'Friends' probably saved my life'
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-20 22:12:37

Matthew Perry is speaking out on his battle with severe addiction during his years starring as Chandler Bing on "Friends."Perry was 24 when he landed the role and described himself as "just a guy desperate for fame, thinking that fame would fix everything.""I thought being funny all the time was how I would get through," the 53-year-old actor told People magazine. "I thought ['Friends'] was going to fix everything. It didn't."Perry, who detailed his struggles with drugs and alcohol in his upcoming memoir "Friends, Lovers and the Big Terrible Thing," told the outlet he initially thought he could manage his addiction.
