By Bryan GruleyOct. 15, 2022 12:01 am ETListen to article(2 minutes)Forty-five years ago, Notre Dame linebacker Bob Golic bumped into a classmate in a dormitory elevator. The classmate, a cocky, 20-year-old junior named Castellini, said he was smuggling a keg of beer into the stadium at that Saturday's game. He vowed to bring Golic a cold one on the sideline. Beer in all forms, let alone a fat steel barrel, was forbidden at Notre Dame Stadium. But sure, Golic said, he'd take a brewski if Castellini could get him one.
