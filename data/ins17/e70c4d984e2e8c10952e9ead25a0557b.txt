May 27, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time SANTERAMO IN COLLE, Bari, Italy--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Natuzzi S.p.A. (NYSE: NTZ) ('we', 'Natuzzi' or the 'Company' and, together with its subsidiaries, the 'Group') today reported its unaudited financial information for its first quarter ended March 31, 2022.

Pasquale Natuzzi, Chairman of the Group commented: 'The demand of our branded products in the first quarter has been robust. At the same time, the context around us invites to be extremely prudent. Generalized inflation, persisting supply-chain disruptions and the conflict in Ukraine provide multiple challenges to our supply chain.
