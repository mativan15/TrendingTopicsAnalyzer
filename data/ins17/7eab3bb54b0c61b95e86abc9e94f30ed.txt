Construction's only change order communications software to exhibit industry-changing SaaS at leading technology forum

SAN FRANCISCO, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Extracker , the construction industry's only change order communication platform, which supercharges manual workflows and connects companies outside of their internal-facing financial software, will exhibit its innovative COR and digital T&M Tag platform at ENR FutureTech , June 8-10 in San Francisco.

Exhibiting at ENR FutureTech from June 8-10, Extracker, the only change order communication platform for the construction industry, will be demonstrating its advanced cloud-based solutions for managing CORs and T&M Tags.
