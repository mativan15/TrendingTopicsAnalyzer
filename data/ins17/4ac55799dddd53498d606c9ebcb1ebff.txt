The spookiest cities in the US -- and why they still scare us
msn.com
travel
2022-10-16 13:30:00

Several locales across the US claim to be king when it comes to Halloween. But three in particular – Savannah, Georgia; New Orleans and Salem, Massachusetts – have the haunting histories to truly earn the title. The trio of historic cities, each of them at least a couple of centuries old, are charming and welcoming in the light, with their cobblestone streets, well-preserved, centuries-old structures and other nods to days of old. But when night falls and the wind howls through empty streets, these cities cast a darker spell.
