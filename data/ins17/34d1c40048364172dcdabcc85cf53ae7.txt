Getty ImagesIndia 10-year bond yield fell 0.41 per cent to 5.89 after trading in 5.87-5.91 rangeNEW DELHI: The Nifty Realty index traded positive around 10:44AM(IST)on Tuesday in an upbeat market. Brigade Enterprises(up 2.86 per cent), DLF(up 2.13 per cent), Phoenix Mills(up 1.69 per cent), Godrej Properties(up 1.31 per cent) and Oberoi Realty(up 0.11 per cent) were among the top gainers. Macrotech Developers(down 3.03 per cent) and Indiabulls Real Estate(down 0.14 per cent) were the top losers on the index.
