PM CARES For Children: Monthly Stipend, Free Treatment To Kids Who Lost Parents To COVID

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday launched PM CARES for Children scheme for children who lost their parents due to the COVID-19 pandemic.The benefits of PM CARES will be extended to thousands of children who lost their parents during the coronavirus pandemic.PM CARES for childrenPM Modi has promised Rs 4,000 a month for basic needs, financial support for schooling, scholarship for higher education, and free treatment up to Rs 5 lakh.
