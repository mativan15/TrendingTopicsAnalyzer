Nearly half of SME owners in the UK have surplus stock heading into the festive season - and predict this excess totals around £66,000Nearly half of the UK's small business face slashing prices or giving away freebies this ChristmasNearly half of small businesses in the UK have excess goods to the tune of £66,000 – and are faced with having to slash prices or even give away some of this surplus for free over the Christmas season. A poll of 250 SME owners found that, of the 45% who are looking to offload surplus stock, they estimate this to make up a staggering 19% of the current stock holdings in their warehouse.
