Fact Check-Uber is not offering a 'walking buddy' option
reuters.com
news
2022-10-21 19:06:18

By Reuters Fact CheckSocial media posts in October feature a fabricated screenshot claiming that Uber is offering a 'walking buddy option' to its customers.The screenshot depicts different Uber riding options, with 'walking buddy' listed at the bottom. One tweet with the screenshot received over 219,000 likes and over 23,000 retweets at the time of writing (here).Other posts on Facebook sharing the claim can be seen (www.facebook.com/photo/?fbid=10160218586393197&set=a.454760088196), (here).Conor Ferguson, an Uber spokesperson, confirmed via email that the screenshot is fake.
