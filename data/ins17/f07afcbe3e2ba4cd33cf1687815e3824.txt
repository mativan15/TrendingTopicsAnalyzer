BEIJING, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Jinchuan's success comes from an unswerving commitment by generations of people to support the country's development in the nonferrous industry, said Wang Yongqian, chairman of Jinchuan Group.Headquartered in Jinchang, Northwest China's Gansu province, the group was established in 1959 and is a leading nonferrous metal producer.
