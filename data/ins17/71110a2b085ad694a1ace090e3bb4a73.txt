MEADVILLE, Pa. (PRWEB) October 21, 2022 'Not to Worry, Lucille and Wilson (The Sequel to Not to Worry, Lucille)': a heartfelt message of comfort for young readers. 'Not to Worry, Lucille and Wilson (The Sequel to Not to Worry, Lucille)' is the creation of published author Linda Gordon, a general pediatrician for over twenty years. Gordon shares, 'Not to Worry, Lucille is designed to teach young children that humans and all members of the universe rely on God for sustenance and do so on faith. With children increasingly exposed to life's uncertainties and adult concepts of terror, death and fear, it is important to introduce the concept of an all-powerful and benevolent Creator.
