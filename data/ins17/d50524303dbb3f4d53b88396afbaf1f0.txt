Shares of the Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Ltd. traded at Rs 380.5 on BSE at 01:29PM (IST) on Thursday, down 1.08 per cent. The stock quoted a 52-week low price of Rs 312.05 and a high of Rs 493.0. Earlier in the day, the stock saw a gap down opening. At the prevailing price, the stock traded at 39.87 times its trailing 12-month EPS of Rs 9.54 per share and 9.65 times its book value, as per BSE data. A total of 20,742 shares changed hands on the counter till 01:29PM (IST). The stock commands a market value of Rs 24107.
