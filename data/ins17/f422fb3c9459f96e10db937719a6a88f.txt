Elektros files patent pending for EV portable battery tech
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-05 18:30:00

Representative ImageNew Delhi: American electric transport company Elektros has filed a patent pending with the United States Patent and Trademark Office for a portable charger battery that will be compatible with all established electric vehicle companies such as Tesla Motors and Mercedes-Benz EQS.The portable battery will be like an attached case that contains an extension cord and allows consumers to plug in their electric vehicles at any time or place in case they do not have access to a traditional electric vehicle charger.
