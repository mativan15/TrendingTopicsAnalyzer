(Photo: ©Orion Pictures Corp/Courtesy Everett Collection)The first rule of Sylvester Stallone action movies is: Sly always makes it to the end of the picture alive. The Rocky star has a history of dodging death scenes over the course of his career, even when he's scripted them himself. Stallone's original script for Rocky V, for example, ended with the Italian Stallion dying in the arms of his beloved wife, Adrian, until he decided to grant his alter ego a stay of execution.
