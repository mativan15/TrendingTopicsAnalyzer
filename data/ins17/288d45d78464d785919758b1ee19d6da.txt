Behind the Review host and Yelp's Small Business Expert, Emily Washcovick, shares a look at this week's episode of the podcast.Tucked into the peaks and valleys of Rutherford, California, Cakebread Cellars is a magnet for all wine lovers, from the swirling connoisseur to the vino newbie. With its breadth of culinary and wine tasting options, it has something for everyone. Beginning in 1972 as just a 22-acre chunk of land in Napa County, Cakebread Cellars has grown to over 1,600 acres and 16 distinct vineyards.
