Published: 03:25 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 03:27 EDT, 30 May 2022 Liverpool 'were considering a bid for Son Heung-min this summer' had Tottenham not qualified for the Champions League.Jurgen Klopp had reportedly identified Spurs' South Korean forward as a potential replacement for Sadio Mane or Mohamed Salah if they departed Anfield.According to Football.London, Liverpool were prepared to test Spurs' resolve with a substantial bid for Son had they not finished in the top four. Liverpool were reportedly keen to sign Son Heung-min had Tottenham not finished in top four Liverpool manager Jurgen Klopp is a big admirer of the South Korean internationalHowever, after Son's 23 goals won him the Golden Boot - jointly with Salah - and helped Spurs into Europe's top competition, Liverpool ended their interest.
