The state aimed at better nutritional standing of school children through the midday meal programme in Telangana. The students expressed gratitude to the government for providing them with delicious meals. The mid day meal program supports the children to grow in a nutritiously healthy way. Fahed Fathima, a class 9 student of Government High School, 2nd Lancer said, "We always get good, fresh food here from the government. We get Dalcha, white rice and bagara.
