The soprano Claudia Boyle trained at the Royal Irish Academy of Music. In 2012 the Dublin singer won the Maria Callas award, one of the top gongs in opera. Since then she has performed on some of the most prestigious stages in the world, including Rome's Teatro dell'Opera, the Metropolitan Opera House in New York and at Konzerthaus Berlin. She regularly performs at home and will soon take the lead role of Violetta in Verdi's La traviata at the Bord Gais Energy Theatre. Boyle, 39, lives in Dublin with her husband, David, and their two children, Blossom, three, and George, one.
