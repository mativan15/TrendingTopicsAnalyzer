Westend61 | Westend61 | Getty Images In September, Neighbor.com unveiled its list of America's 25 most neighborly cities. The cities on the list were ranked by examining residents' charitable giving, generosity, happiness, and well-being. In addition to determining the most neighborly cities, the study found that 42.5% of people are more willing than before to help a neighbor after the COVID-19 pandemic. And nearly 30% of people say they hang out with their neighbors and local friends more than they used to.
