SHOPPERS have spotted an unusual new Christmas treat at M&S - and they're all raving about it. 
The popular find is under £10 and fetched hundreds of likes when it was shared to social media. 
M&S have a tasty new treat on shelvesCredit: Alamy
The Choc Corn Wreath is on sale for under a tennerCredit: SweetReviewsUK
M&S has come out with a chocolate and popcorn wreath - the perfect thing to commence the Christmas countdown. 
The Choc Corn Wreath costs £8 and consists of salted toffee popcorn covered in milk chocolate with mini green and red sugar beans and Christmas trees scattered throughout.
