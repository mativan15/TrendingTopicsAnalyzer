Amul makes foray into organic food industry
indiatimes.com
food
2022-05-28 12:45:00

In pic: R S Sodhi , MD, Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation LtdDairy major GCMMF, which markets its products under Amul brand, on Saturday announced entry into organic food market with the launch of organic wheat flour. The first product launched in this portfolio is "Amul Organic Whole Wheat Atta", Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation Ltd (GCMMF) said in a statement. Further, Amul will be launching products like moong daal, tur daal, chana daal and basmati rice in its organic portfolio.
