Kapook2981/iStock via Getty Images Steel Dynamics (NASDAQ:STLD) +0.6% post-market Wednesday after reporting better than expected Q3 adjusted earnings and revenues, as its steel fabrication operations offset results from its flat rolled steel business; steel shipments totaled a quarterly record 3.2M tons. Q3 net income totaled $914.3M, or $5.03/share, compared to $990.8M, or $4.85/share, in the year-earlier quarter, while net sales improved 11% Y/Y to $5.65B; cash flow from operations reached a quarterly record $1.
