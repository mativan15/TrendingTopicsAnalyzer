FTSE 100 Live: Sterling higher ahead of Hunt tax and spending plan
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-17 09:27:17

The new chancellor's plan for an emergency fiscal statement today helped to calm markets as sterling rose and gilt yields fell back.

This afternoon's Commons statement by Jeremy Hunt will include details of the medium-term fiscal plan due on 31 October.

The statement, which is expected to include the scrapping of the 1p cut in the base rate of income tax, helped to calm markets in the first session since the Bank of England ended its government bond-buying scheme.
