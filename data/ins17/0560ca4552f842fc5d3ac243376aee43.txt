APThe biggest difference between deleting the facebook account and deactivating it is if you deactivate your Facebook account, you may always reactivate it later, but if you delete your Facebook account, your account will be permanently removed from Facebook including your photos, posts, comments etc.If you're concerned about your online privacy, or just want to cut off yourself from social media, there are two ways to get rid of a Facebook account: You can temporarily deactivate your account or choose to permanently delete it.
