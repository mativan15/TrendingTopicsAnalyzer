Titans' Mike Vrabel calls for more consistency from officials in email to NFL
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-16 12:49:42

NFL officials did not have a good week around the NFL in Week 5 thanks to some questionable calls, one of which occurred in the Tennessee Titans' victory over the Washington Commanders.

Titans defensive lineman Jeffery Simmons was called for a questionable roughing the passer penalty during the game, one of many lackluster roughing the passer calls made this season.

As a whole, we've seen many games impacted by bad officiating in 2022, and the Titans have been no stranger to that, also.

So, when the NFL e-mailed general managers and coaches its weekly video on officiating, Vrabel did something rare: he decided to reply all to the e-mail, sending his response to the league and everyone else in the group.
