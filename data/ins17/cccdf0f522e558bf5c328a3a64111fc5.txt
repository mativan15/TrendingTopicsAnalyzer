There are people in this world who hate fall. They bemoan the end of summer, seeing the season as a time of mourning. But those glass-half-empty people are wrong. Fall is a season for rejoicing, because while the weather cools off in the Northern Hemisphere and birds begin their long slog south, travel to Europe is at its best. Gone are the hordes of tourists in sweat-soaked tank tops. You're looking at sweater weather. Better yet, you're looking at European scarf weather.
