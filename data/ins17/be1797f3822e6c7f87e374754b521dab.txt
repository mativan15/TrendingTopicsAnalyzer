NEW DELHI: Domestic stocks saw their market value rising Rs 4.4 lakh crore in Monday's trade, as investors tracked Friday's surge in US stocks amid hopes of inflation peaking soon. The BSE market capitalisation rose to Rs 257.51 lakh crore from Rs 253.13 lakh crore in morning session. 
The BSE Sensex climbed over 1,000 points while Nifty50 topped 16,200 in a day every three of four BSE stocks were trading higher.
