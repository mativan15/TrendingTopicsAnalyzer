Russian Officials Have Spent Millions on VPNs Since Invading Ukraine
pcmag.com
news
2022-06-01 18:15:00

It's not just Russian civilians turning to VPNs to find out what's really happening in Ukraine, state officials are spending millions on them to circumvent the censorship put in place by their own government and secure communication channels.According to research carried out by Top10VPN(Opens in a new window), the Russian Federal Treasury has published 236 official VPN technology contracts since the war in Ukraine started. 188 of those contracts are for state institutions, while the remaining 48 are for companies regulated by public procurement law.
