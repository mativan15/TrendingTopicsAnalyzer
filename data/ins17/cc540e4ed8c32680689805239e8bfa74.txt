With opening night now on the horizon, let's take a peek at the Rockets' 2022-23 schedule with an eye on the 10 most-intriguing matchups.

10. Oct. 21 vs. Grizzlies

The Rockets' first home game of the season will treat fans to a pair of high flyers as Green squares off against dynamic Memphis point guard Ja Morant. The energy should be high at the Toyota Center given the anticipation of a new season, and Rockets fans looking at things through a long-term lens should pay close attention to the Grizzlies.
