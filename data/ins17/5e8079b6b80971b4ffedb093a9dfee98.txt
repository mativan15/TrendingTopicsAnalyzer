REUTERS/Brendan McDermidNEW YORK, Oct 18 (Reuters) - Goldman Sachs (GS.N) outlined its leadership team for its new core business units on Tuesday as the Wall Street giant undertakes its biggest overhaul since 2020.The bank named Ashok Varadhan, Dan Dees and Jim Esposito as global co-heads of Global Banking & Markets while Marc Nachmann will be global head of the asset and wealth management division.
