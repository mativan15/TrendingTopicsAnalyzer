Dollarama rose just 4.5 per cent this week, but the stock is up more than 30 per cent over the past 12 months.Fred Lum/The Globe and MailInvestors are having a tough time trying to figure out what to do with retailing stocks right now, given soaring inflation and signs of an oncoming recession. But if this week's activity is any indication, low expectations can lead to big returns.That's particularly true among humble dollar stores, which may be turning the tumultuous backdrop to their advantage.
