By Associated Press Published: 01:23 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 01:49 EDT, 4 June 2022 STATESBORO, Ga. (AP) - Catcher David LaManna had a two-run double in the bottom of the first inning before a 5-hour, 59-minute rain delay and Jared Miller scored the go-ahead run on a wild pitch in the eighth after play resumed as Notre Dame held off Texas Tech 3-2 in the Statesboro Regional on Friday. Notre Dames (36-14) will play the winner of Saturday morning's postponed game between tourney host Georgia Southern and UNC Greensboro, later Saturday.
