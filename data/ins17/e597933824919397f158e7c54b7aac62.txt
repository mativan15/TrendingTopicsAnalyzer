A Samsung Electronics manufacturing facility in Pyeongtaek, Gyeonggi[SAMSUNG ELECTRONICS] Samsung Electronics is doubling down on the chip business as its competitors walk away from the table. 'Right now, we aren't having discussions about reducing capacity,' Han Jin-man, executive vice president at the chipmaker's memory chip division, noted earlier this month following a question on possible production cuts. TSMC, Micron, SK hynix and Kioxia have announced capital expenditure or production cuts in the midst of what is shaping up to be a chip glut for the ages.
