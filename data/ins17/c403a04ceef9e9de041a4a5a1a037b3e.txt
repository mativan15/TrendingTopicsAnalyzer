Eilish McColgan's 10,000m new British, European records invalidated due to course error
espn.com
news
2022-10-19 15:04:35

Eilish McColgan's 10,000-metre British and European records set at the 2022 Great Scottish Run have been invalidated due to a human error that meant the course was 150m too short.The 31-year-old, who is reigning Commonwealth Games champion, finished in 30 minutes 18 seconds, but a human error meant the course differed from agreed plans.McColgan said in a Twitter post: "Obviously disappointed with this news, but these things happen! Thankfully my British and European Record still stands from the Great Manchester 10K.
