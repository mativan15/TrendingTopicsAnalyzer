[Photo by Zhao Haijiang/For chinadaily.com.cn] Attending the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China has given Gong Lijiao more determination to continue her sports life. Gong, a shot-putter from Hebei province and a delegate to the congress, said the report delivered on Sunday deepened her sense of responsibility to advance the mission of sports, Hebei Daily reported.
