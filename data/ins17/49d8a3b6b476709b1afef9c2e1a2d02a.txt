SINGAPORE, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Upstairs.io , Asia's first custodial NFT Marketplace has successfully sold out their first curated NFT collection following their grand debut on 10th October, 2022. The NFT Marketplace previously launched a limited-edition commemorative digital collectible offered to their first 888 users to claim which went out of stock within an hour. Following their debut success and a showcase of the platform's smart and user-friendly design, the Marketplace launched their first-ever curated collection on their Marketplace on 13th October, 2022 (UTC+8).
