Virgin Media TV is offering free kids channels for two weeks (Image: VIRGIN MEDIA)Right in time for the half-term holidays, Virgin Media has announced a major boost for millions of users. From today, the telecoms giant is switching on a swathe of extra kids channels and it won't cost a penny extra to tune in and watch.The content-boosting offer lasts until October 31 and includes premium services such as Cartoon Network, Nickelodeon and Boomerang.Virgin says that its free kids TV offering caters for all ages with popular shows including Star Trek Prodigy, Scooby Doo, Teen Titans and many more all featuring on the service.
