By Yuan Shenggao | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-17 08:15 People gather at a 5G pavilion of a tech expo in Zhanjiang.[Photo provided by Liu Jicheng/For China Daily] In recent years, Zhanjiang in Guangdong province has taken digital construction as its key to government operation and services. One example is the in-depth integration of environmental governance and big data, aiming to build a strong governance capacity and create an intelligent system for the ecological environment, local officials said.
