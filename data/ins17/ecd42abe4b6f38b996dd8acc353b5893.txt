By Dave Carlin June 2, 2022 / 6:41 PM / CBS New York NEW YORK -- Every year, the New York City-based business advisory group Out Leadership releases rankings of all the states according to workplace and community climates for LGBTQ+ residents and workers.As CBS2's Dave Carlin reports, the findings show a growing divide across the nation when it comes to legislation, workplace environment and overall culture.Some states are more inclusive and welcoming of the LGBTQ+ community compared to others. It's spelled out in the just-released fourth annual State LGBTQ+ Business Climate Index from Out Leadership.
