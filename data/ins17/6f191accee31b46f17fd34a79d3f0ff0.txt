Bhopal: Madhya Pradesh has done a U-turn on his decision to import coal. Officials say the Centre will provide it and tenders floated by the state to import coal are put on hold.'There have been some changes. Coal India will provide us imported coal, now state Genco will not import it. These are directions from the Union government. Our tenders are on hold, the Union government will import and provide us,' chief engineer (fuel management) MP Genco, Rajeev Shrivastav told TOI.Earlier, Madhya Pradesh Power Generating Company Limited, (MP Genco) had invited tenders to import 7.
