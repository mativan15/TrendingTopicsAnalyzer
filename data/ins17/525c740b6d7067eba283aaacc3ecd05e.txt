Simplilearn makes senior appointments amid push for growth in key industry domains
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 09:00:00

Getty Images(Representative image)Simplilearn, an online bootcamp for digital economy skills, has announced the appointments of Mohit Yadav as VP of growth, and Sujoy Ghosh as VP of product, said a statement issued today. The company has increased its workforce from 1,400 to around 2,200 in the past year. With the appointments of these new executives, the company is looking to drive further growth in key industry domains. Yadav comes with over 12 years of experience in sales and category management, which includes verticals such as FMCG, home and kitchen, toys, sports and luggage.
