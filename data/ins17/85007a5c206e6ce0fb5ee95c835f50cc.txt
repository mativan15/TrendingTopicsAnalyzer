During a Volatile Fund-Flows Week, Equity ETFs Attract a Net $11.3 Billion
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-21 15:57:00

Andranik Hakobyan/iStock via Getty Images Investors were overall net redeemers of fund assets (including those of conventional funds and ETFs) for the fourth week in a row, withdrawing a net $6.8 billion for the Refinitiv Lipper fund-flows week ended Wednesday, October 19. Fund investors were net purchasers of equity funds (+$4.1 billion) while being redeemers of money market funds (-$5.7 billion), taxable bond funds (-$2.6 billion), and tax-exempt fixed income funds (-$2.6 billion) for the week.
