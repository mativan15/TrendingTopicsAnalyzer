ISTANBUL, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Global Systems Integrator and network transformation specialist Odine is pleased to announce a new partnership with Wavecrest that will migrate their international voice services to a managed cloud-based solution.  The transformation will empower Wavecrest to meet evolving market demands, virtualise their core network and launch converged services.
