Dame Judi Dench slams The Crown for 'cruelly unjust' portrayal
express.co.uk

2022-10-19 20:55:00

An Oscar-winning actress has slammed The Crown for "crude sensationalism" as she claims the Netflix show is "cruelly unjust". Dame Judi Dench, who was an actress in the James Bond films, called on Netflix to insert a disclaimer at the start of the programme to say it is a "fictionalised drama" in a letter to The Times.
