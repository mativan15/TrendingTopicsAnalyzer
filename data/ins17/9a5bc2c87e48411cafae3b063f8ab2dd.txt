End of May, may shower us with spectacular meteor showers
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-28 17:15:00

The stargazers and space enthusiasts may have another reason to cheer as May end is expected to have a heavy meteor shower. The meteor shower, called Tau Heculids, is expected to take place on the night of May 30 and last until the early morning of May 31. People are expected to enjoy a spectacular view as the sky brightens up because of the shooting stars. 
The meteor shower would happen due to a disintegrated and bleeding comet named SW3 or Schwassmann-Wachmann. The comet got its name after Arnold Schwassmann and Arno Arthur Wachmann, the two observers from Germany.
