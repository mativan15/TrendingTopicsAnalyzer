Published: 22:07 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 22:07 EDT, 30 May 2022 A player with a famous last name will likely make his major league debut when the Detroit Tigers host the Minnesota Twins in a doubleheader on Tuesday. Infielder Kody Clemens, son of Roger Clemens, was called up by the Tigers from Triple-A Toledo. He replaced Robbie Grossman, who was placed on the 10-day injured list due to a neck injury, on the active roster. The left-handed hitting Clemens was batting .283 with eight home runs and 31 RBIs for Toledo.
