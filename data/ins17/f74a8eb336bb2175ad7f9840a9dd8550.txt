The Security Service of Ukraine has received confirmation that Russian invaders have started eating dogs again due to supply problems.

Source: text messages from a Russian invader intercepted by the Ukrainian Security Service

Quote: "Everything is f*cked up. The Ukes are f*cking us over like we're young kids. We're eating dogs, we have nothing to eat. We ate a Yorkie today. A Yorkshire terrier."

Details: The invader, who is located in the temporarily occupied territory of Kherson region, was texting an acquaintance about the issues with food.
