ANN ARBOR, Mich. — Penn State was off to a hot 5-0 start, just like 2021, and after a big loss to Michigan on Saturday, the Nittany Lions are 5-1, just like 2021.In 2021, and in years past, the blue and white has had one loss derail its season after a hot start. Penn State lost to Iowa on the road and then the following week was upset by Illinois at home.The Nittany Lions started off the 2020 season ranked No. 8 and lost to Indiana. Penn State then lost four straight games to start 0-5, the worst start of James Franklin's tenure.
