AgenciesWatsa hinted at possible investments in railway station modernisation as well but chose not to elaborate on those plans.Billionaire investor Prem Watsa proposes to have $7 billion more worth of investments in India over the next five years where he believes the country is in an "impressive" phase with the goal of 10% economic growth in front of it. Hyderabad-born Watsa, often described as Canada's Warren Buffet, has built a diverse portfolio of investments in India spanning insurance, banking, chemicals, fintech, travel and shipping.
