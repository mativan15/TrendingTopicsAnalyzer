Published: 06:29 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 06:48 EDT, 3 June 2022 Princess Margaret's daughter Lady Sarah Chatto looked elegant in ivory as she arrived at St Paul's Cathedral for today's National Service of Thanksgiving - taking a seat next to the Duke and Duchess of Sussex for the historic service.The 58-year-old royal, who was joined by her artist husband Daniel and two sons Arthur, 23, and Samuel, 25, at the service to mark 70 years of the Queen's reign, appeared deep in chat with Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, who she was seated next to at the commemorative service.
