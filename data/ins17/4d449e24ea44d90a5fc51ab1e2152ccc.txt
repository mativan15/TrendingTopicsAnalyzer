Survey: B2B Construction Ecommerce Missing the Mark with Buyers
globenewswire.com
business
2022-06-02 13:01:00

NEW YORK, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- As the construction sector continues an upward growth trajectory, e-commerce sites are failing to meet critical needs of its B2B buyers. Recent data commissioned by Sana Commerce shows a significant disconnect between buyers' expectations and their actual customer experiences, with over half (51%) of B2B construction buyers on average reporting that suppliers' websites were missing the mark when it came to meeting their needs.    Sapio Research on behalf of Sana Commerce found construction industry buyers are frustrated with a number of e-commerce problems.
