Published: 14:18 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 14:18 EDT, 16 October 2022 The mother of one of Jeffrey Dahmer's victims has said that Halloween costumes impersonating the Milwaukee serial killer are insensitive to the families of his victims.Shirley Hughes, 85, the mother of Dahmer's 12th victim Tony Hughes, has criticized the Netflix show for re-victimizing the family of those he killed and misrepresenting the circumstances around the death of her deaf son.Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story has become the second most viewed English Netflix series of all time, having racked up more than 700 million hours viewed.
