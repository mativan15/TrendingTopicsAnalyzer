James Gunn Is Secretly Working on Another DC Movie
ign.com
entertainment
2022-10-17 23:07:46

The Suicide Squad director James Gunn isn't quite done with DC. According to The Hollywood Reporter, the filmmaker has been secretly working on another DC movie for Warner Bros Discovery.Gunn previously directed The Suicide Squad, essentially a soft reboot of DC's Suicide Squad, and also created the recent smash hit superhero show, Peacemaker.Now, Gunn is reportedly in talks with producer Peter Safran for a new, mystery project. It's said that Gunn will be focussing on the second season of Peacemaker first but will eventually take on another DC property.
