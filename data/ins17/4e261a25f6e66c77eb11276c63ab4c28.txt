Joining the Scaled Agile Partner Network gives Isos Technology yet another avenue to help companies transform their business for the better TEMPE, Ariz. (PRWEB) May 27, 2022 Isos Technology, a leading Atlassian Platinum and Enterprise Solution Partner, announced it has joined the Scaled Agile Partner Network as a Bronze Transformation Partner. This worldwide network includes transformation and platform providers who help enterprises facilitate, improve, and accelerate business results through adoption of the Scaled Agile Framework® (SAFe®).
