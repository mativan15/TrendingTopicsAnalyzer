(PTI Photo/Arun Sharma) (New Delhi: RIL group's drone firm Asteria Aerospace expects to grow around 10 times in the current financial year and cross the Rs 100-crore revenue mark, a senior official of the company said on Friday. Asteria Aerospace co-founder and director Nihar Vartak told PTI that there has been exponential growth in the drone services business after the government came up with liberal policy for the sector in September last year.
