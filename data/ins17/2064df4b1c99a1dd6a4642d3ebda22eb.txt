 A Hamilton man who won $1 million in the lottery says he's spreading his some of his good fortune among family members.
Larry Culp, 67, says a trip to Las Vegas with his siblings and another to Italy with his girlfriend is how he intends to share his new-found wealth with loved ones.'It's a wonderful windfall that I intend to share with my children and grandchildren,' Culp added.
