While Netflix Pushes Quantity, Quality Matters More To Most Viewers, UTA Data Suggests
forbes.com
news
2022-10-19 23:01:38

Future Publishing via Getty Images
The flood of Netflix NFLX shows – a record 1,024 episodes released in Q3, led by big hits such as Stranger Things and Monster - Dahmer: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story – may have helped the stumbling company regain its momentum after a crummy first half of the year. But new data from United Talent Agency's big data unit released Wednesday during Advertising Week presentations suggests that consumers are more in tune with quality than quantity in an era where they have seemingly bottomless choice in what to watch.
