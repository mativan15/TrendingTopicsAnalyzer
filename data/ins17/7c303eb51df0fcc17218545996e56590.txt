AJ Bell FY 2023 Assets Under Administration Fell on Adverse Market Movements
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 06:41:00

By Michael Susin AJ Bell PLC said Thursday that assets under administration and net inflows fell in fiscal 2023 amid adverse market movements, despite a robust inflow from its advised platform business. The U.K. investment platform said total assets under administration as at Sept. 30 came to 69.2 billion pounds ($77.63 billion) compared with GBP72.8 billion a year earlier. Total net inflows in the year fell to GBP3.8 billion from GBP6.4 billion, it added. The company said its platform business's robust net inflows were offset by adverse market movements of 11%.
