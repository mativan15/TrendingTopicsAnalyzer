The Russian invasion of Ukraine has created a 'perfect storm' of risks for businesses from cybersecurity threats to supply chain uncertainties, according to a survey of about 800 audit heads.In a poll by the Chartered Institute of Internal Auditors, companies said the war had magnified dangers across the board. Seventy-nine per cent of respondents said the conflict had driven up macroeconomic and geopolitical uncertainties, while 77 per cent said that it had worsened cybersecurity and data security risks.
