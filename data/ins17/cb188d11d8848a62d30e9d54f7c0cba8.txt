Battlefield 2042 Season 3 With Massive Change To Specialists Coming Late November, Report Says
gamespot.com
news
2022-10-21 15:33:36

Battlefield 2042's next season will kick off in late November, according to a new report. Insider Gaming cites sources familiar with EA's internal release schedule that claim Season 3 will begin on November 22.The report said Battlefield 2042 players can expect at least one new map and Specialist character in Season 3, as well as a new battle pass, and more weapons and vehicles. Earlier this year, EA announced a "massive overhaul" for Battlefield 2042's Specialist characters. "This is a big undertaking for us, and whilst this work won't feature in Season 2, we're planning for it to feature in Season 3 later on this year," the company said.
