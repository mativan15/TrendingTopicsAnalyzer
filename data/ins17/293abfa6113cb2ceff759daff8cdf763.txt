Shares of Amazon (AMZN 3.66%) plunged after the company released its first-quarter earnings report last month. Recently, the company's stock dropped to a 52-week low and lost nearly all the gains accumulated during the pandemic.
While some investors may be worried about the future of Amazon's stock, there are others standing on the sidelines ready to buy. Amazon announced plans for a 20-for-1 stock split in March, and many investors have been waiting to jump in to collect their additional shares.
