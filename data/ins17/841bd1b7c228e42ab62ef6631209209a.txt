 Two police agencies in New Brunswick are investigating fatal crashes.
The Fredericton Police Force said a 42-year-old man died in a single-vehicle crash overnight on Saturday.According to police, the incident occurred at around 1 a.m. near 80 Riverside Drive in the city. The area was temporarily closed to traffic as investigators attended the scene.
