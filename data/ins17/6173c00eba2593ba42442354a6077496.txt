Executive Turntable: SoundCloud Taps New People Head; Symphonic Names Canada Chiefs
einnews.com
world
2022-06-03 14:00:58

Jennifer Caserta was named head of people at SoundCloud, where she will be responsible for developing and executing the company's human resources strategy to support the overall business and direction of the organization. Reporting to CFO and COO Drew Wilson with a dotted line to CEO Michael Weissman, Caserta will promote strategic people practices, leadership and development while overseeing internal communications and managing branding for hundreds of international SoundCloud employees. Symphonic Distribution is expanding its business to Canada as part of a strategic merger with Higher Reign Music Group's recently-launched Higher Reign Music Distribution.
