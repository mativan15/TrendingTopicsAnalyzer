Nicola Sturgeon torn apart over latest Scottish independence push
express.co.uk
politics
2022-10-17 16:36:00

Nicola Sturgeon's latest push towards independence has been torn apart by the Scottish Tories. The First Minister today unveiled her Government's post-independence plans for the economy in a 108-page paper at a press conference that took place at her official residence, Bute House.The paper laid out proposals including an independent Scotland's currency, borders and rejoining the EU.But Scottish Tory leader Douglas Ross accused the SNP leader of "trying to sell Scotland a pig in a poke".Mr Ross said: 'This illustrates just how thin the economic case for independence is.
