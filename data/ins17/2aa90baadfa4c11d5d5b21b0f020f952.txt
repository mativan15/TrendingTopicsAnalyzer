Reach To Teach Foundation Seeks To Make Learning Joyful In Arunachal Pradesh Government Schools
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-19 14:59:35

Itanagar: An organisation aiming at making learning joyful on Wednesday said it has taken up the mission to improve the quality of teaching in government schools in Arunachal Pradesh. The organisation, Reach to Teach (RTT) Foundation, had inked a tripartite agreement with the state government and NITI Aayog in July this year for a large-scale transformation in school education. The three-year partnership (2022-25) focused on the enhancement of the learning outcome of students across 3,000 plus government schools in the state, thereby reaching out to over two lakh children.
