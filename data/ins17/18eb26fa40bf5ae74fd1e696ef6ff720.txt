MGMGH conducts first-ever cardiac bypass surgery
thehindu.com
news
2022-10-15 13:00:11

Doctors at Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Government Hospital (MGMGH) carried out the facility's first-ever coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) operation recently, with the aim of extending cardiac care services to a wide section of the public.The procedure was done by a team of doctors led by A. Kumaravel, professor, Department of Cardio Thoracic Surgery, K.A.P.V. Government Medical College, on a 45-year-old male patient, from Krishnarayapuram town in Karur district, it was announced at a press gathering on Saturday.
