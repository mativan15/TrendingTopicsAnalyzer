Market assessment addresses consumer and industry trends in smart watch market DALLAS, Oct. 21, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- New research from Parks Associates reveals that the top use cases for wearables involve health and activity tracking, while 22% of US internet households (25 million households) using their wearables for safety reasons. The international research firm's study Smart Watches and Wearables Market Assessment reveals adoption of smart watches increased from 16% of US internet households in 2020 to 29% in 2021.
