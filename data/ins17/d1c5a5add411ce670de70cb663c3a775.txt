Thousands of Salesforce workers demand end to NRA relationship
sfgate.com
news
2022-05-27 00:29:40

Employees at Salesforce, San Francisco's largest private employer, are urging executives to cease their working relationship with the National Rifle Association after the Uvalde, Texas, elementary school shooting that left 19 children and two adults dead.An open letter titled, 'Ending our customer relationship with the NRA,' was sent to the tech titan's co-CEOs, Marc Benioff and Bret Taylor; CFO Amy Weaver; and CMO Sarah Franklin on Wednesday evening, according to a current Salesforce employee (who was granted anonymity in accordance with Hearst's ethics policy).
