The private 18-room dog-friendly hotel earns one of the best hotels awards from a leading global travel provider.PALM SPRINGS, Calif., June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Inn at Palm Springs, a boutique, 18-room dog-friendly hotel located in the Palm Springs Uptown Design District, has been recognized by HotelsCombined as one of the best hotels in the United States.HotelsCombined is a hotel price comparison website recognized as the Best Hotel Booking Site two years in a row for 2020 and 2021 by Frommer's.
