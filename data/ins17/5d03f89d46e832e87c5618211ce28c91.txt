REUTERS/Ralph OrlowskiRegister now for FREE unlimited access to Reuters.comBERLIN, June 2 (Reuters) - Healthcare group Fresenius (FREG.DE) plans to cut 2,000 jobs globally in its generic drugs unit Kabi, or around 5% of the unit's workforce, as part of an efficiency programme, business daily Handelsblatt reported on Thursday.In Germany, 250 jobs would be affected, the paper said.Fresenius did not immediately reply to an emailed request for comment.
