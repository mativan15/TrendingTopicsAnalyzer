Google Meet and Duo will soon be one app for video calling
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-02 04:31:18

Google's Duo app will soon merge into the Google Meet, the company announced in a blog post. Reports had indicated this would happen back in 2020, and it is now official. Google Meet is the company's answer to Zoom. It lets G-Suite users (aka the business and enterprise users), as well as personal Gmail users, set up and host their own video calls.
In a blog post, the company said it will bring 'Google Meet features to the Duo app, so users can easily schedule a video meeting at a time that works for everyone, or continue using video calling to instantly connect with a person or group.
