The Maharashtra government led by Chief Minister Eknath Shinde and Deputy CM Devendra Fadnavis on October 20 reversed the erstwhile Maha Vikas Aghadi (MVA) government's decision of withdrawing the general consent to the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) to investigate cases in the State.According to sources, the new BJP-Balasahebanchi Shiv Sena government restored the general consent given to the Central probe agency, with CM Shinde clearing the Home Department's proposal to reverse the previous MVA government's decision to withdraw general consent granted to the CBI.
