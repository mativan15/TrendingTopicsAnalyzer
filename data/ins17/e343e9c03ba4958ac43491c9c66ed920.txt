'Good Grief' director Dan Levy is set to make directorial debut on Netflix
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 13:45:00

Schitt's Creek actor and co-creator Dan Levy is set to make his film directing debut with Netflix's "Good Grief," a bittersweet comedy film. Four-time Emmy Award winner Dan Levy will be seen alongside Passing's Ruth Negga, Beauty and the Beast's Luke Evans, Station Eleven's Himesh Patel, David Bradley, Celia Imrie, Arnaud Valois, and Jamael Westman. Apart from working on his role behind the camera, Dan also wrote the script for "Good Grief".

Dan Levy released a statement, saying that "Good Grief" is an admonitory story about friendship and loss and all the mess that comes when the truth reveals something one has avoided for most of their life.
