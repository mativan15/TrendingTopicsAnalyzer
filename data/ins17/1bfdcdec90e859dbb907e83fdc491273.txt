RICHMOND, Va., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--ASGN Incorporated (NYSE: ASGN), a leading provider of IT services and solutions, including technology and creative digital marketing, across the commercial and government sectors, announced today that it has signed a definitive agreement to acquire GlideFast Consulting LLC ("GlideFast" or "the Company"), an elite ServiceNow Partner and leading IT consulting, implementation and development company, for $350 million in cash.
