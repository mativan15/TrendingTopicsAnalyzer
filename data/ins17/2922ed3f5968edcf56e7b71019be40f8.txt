NEW YORK (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Founder and CEO, Jean Pierre-Louis of capracare was awarded a Proclamation from Queens Borough President Donovan Richards, Jr. at the Caribbean Heritage Celebration. The celebration took place on Tuesday, September 13, 2022, at Queens Museum. Jean was recognized by the Queens Borough President for his work following the 2021 southern Haiti earthquake and for capracare's ongoing efforts to improve the lives of those in Haiti. 'Queens is most grateful for the significant impact capracare has had in Haiti.
