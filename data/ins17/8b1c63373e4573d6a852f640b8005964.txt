JACKSONVILLE, Fla., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Landstar System, Inc. (NASDAQ: LSTR) reported revenue of $1.816 billion in the 2022 third quarter, a 5 percent increase over revenue of $1.734 billion in the 2021 third quarter and quarterly diluted earnings per share ('DEPS') of $2.76, an increase of 7 percent compared to $2.58 in the 2021 third quarter. 2022 third quarter revenue and DEPS each established new all-time third quarter records for Landstar. Gross profit in the 2022 third quarter was $185.
