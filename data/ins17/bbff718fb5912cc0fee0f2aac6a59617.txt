Foreigners turn net buyers of Japanese stocks as risk sentiment improves
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 09:00:00

AgenciesCross-border investors secured Japanese bonds worth 304.5 billion yen, marking a fourth consecutive weekly net buying, finance ministry data showed.Foreign investors were net buyers of Japanese stocks in the week ended May 27 as upbeat performances in Wall Street partially eased concerns over global slowdown and also boosted appetite for local shares. Overseas investors purchased net 254.28 billion yen ($1.96 billion) worth of Japanese stocks last week, marking a first weekly inflow in three weeks, data from exchanges showed.
