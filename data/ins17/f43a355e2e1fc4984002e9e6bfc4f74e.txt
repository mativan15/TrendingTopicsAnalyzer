Impact startups dedicated to tackling issues like global warming raised $458 million in 2021. But even they struggle to balance survival with sustainability, investors say.
businessinsider.com
news
2022-06-01 09:00:00

For most early-stage startups, even the ones dedicated to tackling the ongoing climate emergency, survival comes before their own impact on the world.Investors are keen on so-called impact or purpose-driven startups that were created to address some of humanity's biggest challenges such as global warming, inequality, and the health crisis. Europe's impact startups raised $457.6 million in 2021, according to data from PitchBook.But their output may not reflect how they are run internally as time and resource-constrained founders have to balance their ESG obligations with building a business that can last long enough to make a meaningful dent in the problem they were designed to solve.
