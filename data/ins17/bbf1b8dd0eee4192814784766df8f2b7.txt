Oz slams Fetterman over Democrat policies increasing crime
foxnews.com
news
2022-10-20 10:00:50

Pennsylvania Republican senatorial candidate Mehmet Oz and Republican gubernatorial candidate Doug Mastriano joined Sean Hannity for a Fox News Town Hall in Pennsylvania on Wednesday, where they criticized Democratic politics as the root of most of America's problems.Oz, challenging Lt. Gov. John Fetterman for retiring Sen. Patrick J. Toomey's, R-Pa., seat, told Fox News that Democratic policies are "farming homelessness" in much of Pennsylvania's urban areas.He accused Fetterman of failing to express compassion toward Pennsylvanians living in inner city environments where illegal drug use and crime are spiking.
