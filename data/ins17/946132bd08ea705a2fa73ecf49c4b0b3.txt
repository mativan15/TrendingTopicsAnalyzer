Corus: Fiscal Q4 Earnings Snapshot
sfgate.com
business
2022-10-21 10:21:48

TORONTO (AP) _ Corus Entertainment Inc. (CJREF) on Friday reported a fiscal fourth-quarter loss of $284.8 million, after reporting a profit in the same period a year earlier.

The Toronto-based company said it had a loss of $1.41 per share. Losses, adjusted for asset impairment costs and restructuring costs, came to 6 cents per share.

The media company posted revenue of $263.5 million in the period.

For the year, the company reported a loss of $192.7 million, or 94 cents per share, swinging to a loss in the period.
