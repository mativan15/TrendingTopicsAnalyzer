Taylor Swift's 7 'Midnights' bonus tracks: How to listen to her 'chaotic surprise'
today.com
news
2022-10-21 11:28:35

Taylor Swift's 10th studio album, "Midnights," was slated to have 13 song tracks, each exploring the idea of midnight. Ahead of the release, Swift released each of the song titles on TikTok, sometimes teasing meanings for songs like "Lavender Haze" and "Anti-Hero."But in her "Midnights Manifest," a schedule of releases the days after the album release, Swift made room for a 3 a.m. "chaotic surprise" the day "Midnights" dropped.Well, we know what it is: In the early morning hours, Swift dropped 7 extra tracks, which she's calling "the 3am tracks" (as opposed to the "Midnights" tracks).
