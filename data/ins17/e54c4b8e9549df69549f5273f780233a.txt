Jharkhand HC reserves order on maintainability of PIL against CM Soren
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-01 21:08:31

The High Court of Jharkhand on Wednesday reserved its order on the point of maintainability of the PIL seeking a probe against Chief Minister Hemant Soren on transactions of some shell companies purportedly operated by his family members.
A division bench of Chief Justice Ravi Ranjan and Justice Sujit Narayan Prasad concluded the hearing of the PIL, filed by Shiv Shankar Sharma, and will deliver the order on June 3.
