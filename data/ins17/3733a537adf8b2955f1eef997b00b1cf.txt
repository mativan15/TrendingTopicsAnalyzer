Binance NFT head: ‘Real value creation of the NFT is not really here yet'
aol.com
news
2022-05-31 15:38:43

As the crypto market sells off after last year's go-go years — taking NFTs down with it — one industry insider believes the best is yet to come for non-fungible tokens.'If you ask me, did I really buy a lot of [these]? No, I didn't,' Helen Hai, head of Binance's NFT marketplace, told Yahoo Finance. 'Because I still think the real value creation of the NFT is not really here yet.'Noting that the industry is in the early stages of this technology and its applications, Hai said: 'We need to understand more about the user's perspective, and also the creator's perspective.
