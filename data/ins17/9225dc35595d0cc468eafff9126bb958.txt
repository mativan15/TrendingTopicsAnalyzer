Razing Of Dapoli Resorts To Cost State 1cr: Officials
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 07:15:00

NAVI MUMBAI: The estimated cost to be incurred by the government to demolish and dispose of the debris of the controversial Sai Resort and Sea Conch Resort in Dapoli will be Rs 1 crore, about one-seventh of the current value of Rs 7 crore, officials said. Sai Resort is owned by a Kandivli (East) resident Sadanand Kadam, while the Sea Conch Beach is owned by a Thane resident.Sai Resort was earlier owned by former state minister Anil Parab. Dapoli police had lodged an FIR against the Sea Conch Beach owner for causing damage to the environment.
