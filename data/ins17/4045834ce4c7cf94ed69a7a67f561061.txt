AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Oct 20, 2022--Epicor, a global leader of industry-specific enterprise software to promote business growth, today introduced a variety of features in its Fall 2022 release for Epicor Kinetic for Manufacturing, part of the Epicor Industry ERP Cloud portfolio. The updates will help manufacturers better compete with deep industry-specific functionality, connected services to integrate and optimize workflows, and deliver a smoother user experience journey.'Now more than ever, manufacturers need people-centric, open, and connected technologies to gain visibility across their business and optimize supply chain processes,' said Vaibhav Vohra, Chief Product Officer at Epicor.
