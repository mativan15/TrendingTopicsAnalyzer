Paddy Power Betfair parent CFO to move into new COO role
irishtimes.com
business
2022-10-21 00:00:00

Paddy Power Betfair parent Flutter Entertainment has announced today that group chief financial officer (CFO) Jonathan Hill is to become group chief operating officer (COO) next year.The company said it was establishing the new role to maximise the benefits of Flutter's global scale and support the strategic direction of the business.Paul Edgecliffe-Johnson, currently CFO and group head of strategy at InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG), will join as CFO and executive director of the group in the first half of 2023.
