High court accepts maintainability of pleas against Jharkhand CM Hemant Soren
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-03 18:44:24

Ranchi: The Jharkhand high court on Friday accepted the maintainability of two petitions seeking inquiry by the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) and Enforcement Directorate (ED) against chief minister Hemant Soren for alleged irregularities in grant of mining lease and transactions of some shell companies allegedly operated by his family members.A division bench of chief justice Dr. Ravi Ranjan and justice SN Prasad on Wednesday had reserved its order after concluding the arguments on maintainability of the pleas.
