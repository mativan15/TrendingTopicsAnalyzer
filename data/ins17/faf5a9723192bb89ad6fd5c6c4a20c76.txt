Mortgage lenders no longer provide 80/20 loans. MoMo Productions/Getty Images Saving for a down payment on a home is a huge financial undertaking — one that often takes years. What do you do if you feel ready to buy a home, but you don't have money saved for a down payment ? There was a time that taking out an 80/20 loan could have been your solution — however, lenders are not offering these types of loans right now.What is an 80/20 loan?An 80/20 loan was a type of piggyback loan, which is a home loan that's split into two parts.
