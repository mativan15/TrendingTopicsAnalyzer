Regal Rexnord (NYSE:RRX) Is Paying Out A Larger Dividend Than Last Year

Regal Rexnord Corporation (NYSE:RRX) will increase its dividend on the 14th of July to US$0.35. This makes the dividend yield 6.5%, which is above the industry average.

View our latest analysis for Regal Rexnord

Regal Rexnord Doesn't Earn Enough To Cover Its Payments

If the payments aren't sustainable, a high yield for a few years won't matter that much. However, prior to this announcement, Regal Rexnord's dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings. This means that most of its earnings are being retained to grow the business.
