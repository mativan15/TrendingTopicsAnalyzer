By SHI FUTIAN | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-19 09:07 Share - WeChat Zhu Ting, pictured playing for Team China during the Tokyo Olympics last year, is embarking on a new chapter of her career with an Italian club after recovering from wrist surgery. REUTERS After months away from the spotlight due to wrist injuries, Chinese volleyball ace Zhu Ting is now ready to start another chapter in her glittering career with new club Pallavolo Scandicci, and the superstar says she is determined to return to her best form.
