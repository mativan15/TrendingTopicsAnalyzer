How Big-Name Sneaker Brands Are Racing Into the Metaverse

When first introduced to the concept of Web3 — through NBA Top Shot — which solved the pain points of collecting and trading sports cards, I immediately saw the parallel to my early dotcom days of surfing basketball and design forums where my shared interests (and typing) led to faster connections than my unrefined verbal skills ever could.With the power of decentralization, transparency and ownership, Web3 has the potential to solve pain points across the sneaker industry which, in recent years, has faced growing consumer frustration around issues such as affordability and a lack of accessibility.
