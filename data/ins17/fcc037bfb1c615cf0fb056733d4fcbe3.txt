Capitol Wright Distributing LLC, an Austin-based distributor of both alcoholic and non-alcohol beverages, is being purchased by Reyes Beer Division, which is part of one of the largest privately held companies in the United States.

The deal, announced Oct. 4, is expected to close in December. Financial terms were not disclosed. In a statement, Reyes Beer Division East President Stephen Reyes said the company had been considering a move into Texas "for quite some time" and that "Capitol Wright was the right opportunity for us to enter our 11th market and we're honored to continue the legacy of the great business they've created.
