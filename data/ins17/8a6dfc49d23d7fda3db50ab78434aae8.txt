Jay Kotak, co-head of Kotak 811, left, and Uday Kotak, CEO, Kotak Mahindra BankHarvard alumnus and Uday Kotak's elder son Jay has lately had significant enhancements to his executive role at the Kotak Mahindra Bank, prompting analysts to view the assignments as a start to long-term succession planning at the financial empire bearing the family name of Asia's richest banker. To be sure, the central bank has put a 15-year tenure cap on chief executive roles at lenders, and Uday Kotak has already led the private bank he founded for more than 18 years.
