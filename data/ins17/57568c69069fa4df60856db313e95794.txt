Who will start for the Boston Celtics on opening night vs. the Philadelphia 76ers?
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-17 08:00:26

Boston Celtics reserve guard Derrick White started all four preseason games and posted a solid showing as a 3-point shooter, switchable defender, ball handler, and screener alongside star Celtics forward Jayson Tatum and wing Jaylen Brown.
Despite the success of that unit in preseason play so far, interim Celtics head coach Joe Mazzulla said that he's still undecided about who he will choose to be the starting group ahead of the team's opening night showdown vs. the Philadelphia 76ers on Tue., Oct.
