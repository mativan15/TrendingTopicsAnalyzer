TOKYO, June 2, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- FTX Trading Ltd.("FTX" or "the Company"), a leading regulated global cryptocurrency exchange, today announced that it has launched FTX Japan to service Japanese customers. FTX Japan, operating via FTX Japan K.K. ("FTX Japan"), is a licensed Japanese crypto-asset exchange services provider and Type 1 Financial Instruments Business license holder.
