Good evening, let's start with today's top stories:Bank of Canada eyes benchmark rate of 3 per cent or higherThe Bank of Canada is signalling that it may need to raise its policy interest rate to 3 per cent or higher to head off rising inflation.Bank officials have previously said they intend to get the benchmark rate to a 'neutral' level of between 2 per cent and 3 per cent relatively quickly. In a speech today, deputy governor Paul Beaudry said there is a growing probability that the central bank will need to move to the top end of this range or above.
