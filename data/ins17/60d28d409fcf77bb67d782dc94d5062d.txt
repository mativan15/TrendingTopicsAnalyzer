Telangana flaunts 26.14% growth in IT/ITeS sector in 2021-22
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-01 09:44:54

Telangana has recorded a phenomenal growth of 26.14 per cent in the IT/ITeS sector in 2021-22, with a total of Rs 1,83,569 crore in IT/ITeS exports, IT Minister K T Rama Rao said on Wednesday while unveiling the IT Progress Report 2021-22. In 2020-21, the state had recorded IT/ITeS exports of Rs 1,45,522 crore.
During the period, the sector also added 1,49,506 new jobs, recording an increase in employment by 23.78 per cent to reach 7,78,121 jobs so far.
