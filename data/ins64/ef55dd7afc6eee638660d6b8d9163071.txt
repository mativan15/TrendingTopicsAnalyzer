WEST SACRAMENTO, Calif., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Origin Materials, Inc. ("Origin," "Origin Materials," or "the Company") (Nasdaq: ORGN, ORGNW), today announced that it will release its third quarter 2022 results after market close on Thursday, November 3, 2022, to be followed by a conference call at 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time on the same day.

Interested investors and other parties can listen to a webcast of the live conference call by logging onto the Investor Relations section of the Company's website at https://investors.
