Published: 18:45 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 18:49 EDT, 18 October 2022 Six e-cigarette brands could be pulled from shelves in the US amid the Biden administration's crackdown on the devices. The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) in conjunction with the Department of Justice filed suit against six e-cigarette companies — E-Cig Crib in Minnesota, Soul Vapor LLC in West Virginia, Super Vape'z LLC in Washington, Vapor Craft LLC in Georgia, Lucky's Vape & Smoke Shop in Kansas, and Butt Out in Arizona.
