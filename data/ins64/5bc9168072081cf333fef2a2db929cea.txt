Motor neuron disease symptoms may get detected in gut 14 years before brain symptoms appear. See details

AgenciesAccording to a Scottish study, patients showed signs of motor neuron disease (MND) in their gut and other parts of their body around 14 years ahead of appearance of any symptoms linked with brain.Motor neuron disease (MND) does not have a cure now, and it is terminal. The legendary Stephen Hawking suffered from the same disease. Some researchers at the University of Aberdeen found MND proteins in the gut long before any symptoms linked with the brain appeared in the patients. Apart from the brain, the disease also affects other body parts.
