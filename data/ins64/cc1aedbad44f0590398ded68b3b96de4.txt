> Life The number of times we depend on technology in a day should tell us just how important tech and science will be in the days, weeks, and months to come. Really, STEM Education – once a buzzword mostly uttered by academicians and policymakers – has evolved from being yesterday's fad to becoming today's and tomorrow's necessity. But while the subjects that make up this group are undoubtedly vital, the ways in which they're taught in the classroom can often be a bit of a bore-fest. For the most part, this isn't completely the fault of teachers or lecturers – subjects dealing with science and tech can be at times awfully dry and full of hard-to-follow technicalities.
