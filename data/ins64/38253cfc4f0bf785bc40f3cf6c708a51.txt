Man with 'gun' holds up Hutt Valley gas station, demands cash
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-06-02 20:06:43

Detectives in Lower Hutt are investigating after a man turned up at a petrol station early on Friday demanding money with the threat of a gun. The man came to the High St petrol station at 2am and demanded cash, a statement from police said. Police did not say whether he managed to get any money before he left on foot. He told petrol station staff that he had a gun. Police, using dogs, could not find the man.
