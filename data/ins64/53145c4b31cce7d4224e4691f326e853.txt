GREENVILLE, S.C., October 20, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Regional Management Corp. (NYSE: RM), a diversified consumer finance company, announced today that it has completed a $200 million asset-backed securitization, its ninth securitization. The securitization has a 2-year revolving period, and the Class A notes received an "AAA" rating by Standard & Poor's Ratings Services (S&P), the first time a senior class of notes in a Regional Management securitization has received S&P's top rating.
