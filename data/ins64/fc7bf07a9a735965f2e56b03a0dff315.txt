Why I'm not betting on a Russian market recovery
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-03 23:01:00

Ukraine's 3-1 victory over Scotland on Wednesday leaves the football team one win away from qualifying for the Fifa World Cup this year. It came two and a half weeks after Ukraine's totally expected triumph at the Eurovision Song Contest, where the UK was delighted to finish second.The game was a small victory, considering that it is now 101 days since Russia invaded Ukraine, but one that President Zelensky described as 'two hours of happiness to which we are not accustomed'. The war rages on, but its direct impact on global stock markets has been limited: share prices initially fell but bounced back quickly.
