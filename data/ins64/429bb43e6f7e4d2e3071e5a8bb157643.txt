Travis Perkins sales on shaky ground in face of cost of living storm
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 11:00:00

Business for builders' merchants is continuing to fall as Britain's householders batten down the hatches against the cost of living storm this winter,Travis Perkins, the market leader, reported a 6 per cent fall in volumes in its larger outlets in the third quarter of the year, a worsening picture extending the contraction for the year to date to 2.5 per cent.At its smaller Toolstation outlets, which are more geared to the home improvement market rather than larger construction jobs, third-quarter volumes fell 3.
