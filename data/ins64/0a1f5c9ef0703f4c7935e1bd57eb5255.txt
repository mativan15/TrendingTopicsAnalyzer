Purpose Investments Inc. Announces Redesignation of Canadian Dollar Denominated ETF Non-Currency Hedged Carbon Offset Units of Purpose Ether ETF
globenewswire.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

TORONTO, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Purpose Investments Inc. ('Purpose Investments') today announced that effective as of November 30, 2022, the Canadian dollar denominated ETF non-currency hedged carbon offset units (the 'ETF Carbon Offset units') of Purpose Ether ETF (the 'Purpose Fund') will be redesignated as Canadian dollar denominated ETF non-currency hedged units of the Purpose Fund based on the relative net asset value per unit of each class of the Purpose Fund on November 29, 2022.
