October 17, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time DEERFIELD, Ill.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--CF Industries Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: CF) today announced appointments related to the Company's clean energy initiatives.

Erik Mayer has been appointed vice president, clean ammonia growth. In this newly created role, he will lead a team focused on the evaluation and development of CF Industries' blue ammonia capacity expansion. Mr. Mayer has been with CF Industries for 17 years, most recently serving as vice president, clean energy solutions.
