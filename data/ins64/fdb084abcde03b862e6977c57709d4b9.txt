One cyber fraudster arrested by Delhi Police

Source: ANI

Delhi Police arrested one cyber fraudster namely Zalaluddin who used to cheat innocent people across the country. The accused was the kingpin of the Mewati gang who used to dupe money from people on the pretext of shopping. He was wanted in several cases in Delhi, Kurukshetra, Ambala and other states. Delhi Police had declared a 25,000 rupees reward on the arrest of the accused. Police were receiving many cases of cheating from Ambala. In this regard, the Police registered a case and the investigation was taken up.
