Kantara, a Kannada film starring actor Rishab Shetty that has received praise from critics and audiences, will soon be available on the OTT platform. The film was initially released in Kannada, but due to overwhelming popular demand, it was dubbed in several languages and future release dates were scheduled. Kantara, written and directed by Rishab Shetty, is expected to be one of the highest-grossing Kannada films of all time.
