Published: 22:45 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 04:57 EDT, 17 October 2022 Goldman Sachs is reportedly planning the biggest shake-up in the firm's Wall Street history - in a move that will combine its investment banking and trading business into one unit just one month after a massive workforce cull. The group plans to fold its biggest business into three divisions and the reorganization could be announced within days, the Wall Street Journal reported on Sunday.  Goldman will combine its investment banking and trading businesses into one unit, while merging asset and wealth management into another, the report said.
