Decades-old local company files for Chapter 11 bankruptcy
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 17:04:00

Elevated Construction and Remodeling LLC filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Indiana.

Chapter 11 protection enables a business to restructure its creditor obligations to keep the business alive and pay back its debts over time.

Elevated Construction and Remodeling is a Sellersburg, Indiana-based business that was founded about 25 years ago. It services Jefferson, Oldham and Bullitt counties in Kentucky, and Clark, Floyd, Scott and Harrison counties in Indiana.
