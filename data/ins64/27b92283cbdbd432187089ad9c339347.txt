UP metro to appoint consultant for monetisation of 86-acre land in Lucknow
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 17:15:00

AgenciesThis space has been leased out by UPMRC for commercial exploitation as a retail area.The Uttar Pradesh Metro Rail Corporation Ltd. (UPMRC) is in the process of appointing a consultant for the monetisation of 86 acre of land in Lucknow, its managing director Kumar Keshav told ET. UPMRC is a special purpose vehicle (SPV) set up by the Uttar Pradesh government and the central government as a joint venture. It operates metro trains in Lucknow and Kanpur. The corporation is trying to generate non-fare revenue in different ways, including advertisements on trains, joint branding of stations, and leasing of commercial space at the stations.
