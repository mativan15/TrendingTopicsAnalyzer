October 19, 2022 06:47 PM Eastern Daylight Time TOKYO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Kawasaki Heavy Industries, Ltd. (TOKYO:7012) announced today that it has shipped one KG-18 Kawasaki Green Gas Engine to major Taiwanese chemical company Yee Fong Chemical & Industrial Co., Ltd. (Yee Fong) for use in an expansion project at their Taoyuan Plant.

Yee Fong was founded in 1950 and is a leading manufacturer of acidic and alkaline chemical products in Taiwan. To accommodate the increased production capacity of their Taoyuan Plant, Yee Fong is pursuing an expansion project that involves adding a new 8 MW class gas engine for captive power generation.
