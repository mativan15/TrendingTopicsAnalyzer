(Google Maps)CHICAGO HEIGHTS, IL — Two men with Chicago Heights connections are in the running for a top position at Western Illinois University, school officials told Patch. Gregory Kain — an executive director of information technology at Prairie State — and James Connors — director of information technology and business intelligence at Howard Brown Health — are competing for the executive director of University Technology position at WIU.
