June 02, 2022 09:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--American Express (NYSE: AXP) today announced it is adding three new participating partners, Goldbelly, Wine.com and Milk Bar to the $120 Dining Credit on the Gold Card. These are in addition to existing Dining Credit partners and Card Member favorites, which include Grubhub, The Cheesecake Factory and select Shake Shack locations. Once enrolled, American Express Gold Card Members can earn up to $10 in statement credits monthly when they pay with their Card at these participating partners.
