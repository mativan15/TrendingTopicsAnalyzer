Two Chinese restaurants serving Bay Area for decades shut down
sfgate.com
news
2022-06-02 21:00:11

After four decades of serving hefty portions of fluffy fried rice, crispy orange chicken and savory wonton soup, Little Shin Shin has permanently closed, as first reported by Berkeleyside.The Oakland mainstay, located at 4258 Piedmont Ave., quietly closed its doors on May 10, announcing the closure on its storefront with a goodbye note. The business owners' decision to close came as they sought retirement, the note shared. 'Thank you for the past two decades,' the note read.
