FA launch investigation as violence and poor behaviour mars Liverpool
express.co.uk
sport
2022-10-17 12:21:00

The FA will investigate complaints from both Liverpool and Manchester City after violence and poor behaviour marred the Premier League match at Anfield on Sunday. City's manager Pep Guardiola was the target of coins thrown from home fans behind the dugout and their team coach was once again attacked as it left Anfield.Pictures issued by City yesterday showed a cracked front windscreen although the club stated nobody was injured.City are to present a 'wide-ranging' complaint about their Premier League rivals to the FA.
