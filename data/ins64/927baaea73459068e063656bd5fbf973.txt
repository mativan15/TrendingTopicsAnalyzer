LXRandCo, Inc. engages in the retail of branded vintage luxury handbags and accessories. It sells its products through its physical stores and online shops. The firm offers luxury brands such as Hermès, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, and Chanel. The company was founded by Frédérick Mannella and Kei Izawa in November 2010 and is headquartered in Montreal, Canada.
