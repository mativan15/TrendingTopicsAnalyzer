The Chicago Bulls are already starting the 2022-23 regular season off on the wrong foot as they'll be without one of their All-Star's on Wednesday night.
Per Shams Charania of The Athletic, Zach LaVine will miss Wednesday's opener against the Miami Heat due to left knee management.

Bulls All-Star Zach LaVine (left knee management) will miss tonight's opener vs. Heat.
— Shams Charania (@ShamsCharania) October 19, 2022 LaVine was listed as questionable on Tuesday which came as a surprise. On Friday of last week, LaVine spoke after practice saying that he feels ‘really good' and isn't ‘having any aches and pains' on the knee that he had the procedure on following last season.
