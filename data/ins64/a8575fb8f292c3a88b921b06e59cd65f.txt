Rivian Automotive shakes up its manufacturing leadership team
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-05-31 11:46:02

Michael M. Santiago/Getty Images News Rivian Automotive Inc (NASDAQ:RIVN) announced that manufacturing engineering exec Charly Mwangi will leave the company. Mwangi is a former Tesla exec who has served as Executive Vice President at Rivian since May 2020 and helped bring the R1T pickup truck and R1S SUV to production last year. New Rivian COO Frank Klein is expected to oversee many of the senior manufacturing employees as part of a reorganization. Klein was hired away from Magna International (MGA).
