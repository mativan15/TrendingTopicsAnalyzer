Published: 03:17 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 03:29 EDT, 19 October 2022 Saudi Arabia, Egypt and Greece 'will proceed' with their extraordinary bid to host the 2030 FIFA World Cup.The governments of all three countries have given the green light to push on with the application process for what would be the second winter staging of the World Cup after Qatar this year.Most of the costs will be borne by the Saudis, with the majority of matches staged in their country. Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman has sent the joint nomination file to a company in the United States for 'processing', according to Greek website TO10.
