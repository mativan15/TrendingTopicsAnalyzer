From panoramic sea views to fish market sounds, everywhere and anywhere becomes a drama scene when the camera lens are focused



Merchants and shoppers haggle over prices at Jagalchi Market (Lee Si-jin/The Korea Herald)BUSAN -- Though there are many coastal towns in South Korea, the southern port city of Busan is one of the most treasured sites for renowned television and film directors and content creators. The city mesmerizes them with upscale luxury high-rise apartments, hotel buildings, vast natural landscapes, panoramic seascapes and, of course, the unique Gyeongsang dialect.
