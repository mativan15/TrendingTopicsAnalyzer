Shell Rowe, a film and TV student, took to social media to document her battle with cancer and how she sported whacky hairstyles, from glitter to popcorn, to raise awarenessShell Rowe began documenting her cancer journey on social media One brave cancer survivor has gone viral online, sporting whacky hairstyles to raise awareness. Shell Rowe began documenting her cancer journey on social media, sticking everyday objects like popcorn and glitter, to her head to create crazy hairstyles. After first being diagnosed with non- Hodgkin lymphoma in August 2019 when a tennis ball-sized lump was found in her neck, Shell has now amassed 700,000 followers online wearing the outlandish hairdos.
