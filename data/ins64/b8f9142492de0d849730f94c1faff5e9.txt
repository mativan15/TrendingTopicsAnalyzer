June 01, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The M&A Advisor, recognized as the world's premier leadership organization for mergers & acquisition, restructuring and corporate finance professionals, announced that MACCO Restructuring Group, who served as Debtor's Financial Advisor, in the high profile cryptocurrency bankruptcy case of Cred, Inc. (Case No. 20-12836, in the United States Bankruptcy Court, District of Delaware, Jointly Administered) was named the WINNER of The Chapter 11 Reorganization of The Year (between $250MM AND $500MM).
