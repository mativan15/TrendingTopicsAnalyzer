Published: 02:15 EDT, 30 May 2022 | Updated: 02:17 EDT, 30 May 2022 Aussies looking to head overseas have been warned they'll likely be waiting more than a month to receive a new passport or a renewal. The Australian Passport Office has been grappling with 'unprecedented' demand for the travel documents as Aussies re-embrace travel after more than two years of border closures. The Department of Foreign Affairs and Trading (DFAT), which runs the passport office, has asked for patience from people hoping to head overseas and warned them to apply as early as possible.
