EHang Plans To Establish JV In Thailand
yahoo.com
finance
2022-05-27 16:12:20

In this article:

EHang Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: EH) formed a strategic alliance with Charoen Pokphand Group Co., Ltd. to establish a joint venture in Thailand for AAV sales and urban air mobility operations. The financial terms of the arrangement were not disclosed.

The parties intend to fully leverage their respective expertise and resources in a long-term comprehensive collaboration on AAV product sales and marketing, regulatory compliance, business development, infrastructure deployment, talent cultivation for future UAM operations.
