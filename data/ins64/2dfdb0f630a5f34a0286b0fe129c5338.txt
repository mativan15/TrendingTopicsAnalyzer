Liz Truss's resignation as British Prime Minister on Thursday triggered another leadership race — the second in just four months — for the U.K.'s fractured and demoralized Conservative Party.Truss, who quit after just 45 days in office, said her successor will be chosen in a leadership contest to be completed by the end of next week.
