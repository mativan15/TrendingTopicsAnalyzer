Saving money has always been important to me. But because I have a decent amount of money in my savings account, at this point in my life, I don't go to extremes to eke out a little extra. Sure, I might decide to cut back on takeout meals to spend less on food. And I may decide to road trip it rather than fly to spend less on a vacation. But none of these decisions are really so out there. Back in the day, however, I've done some pretty…let's call them interesting things to save money. And while I'm not necessarily proud of these moves, I'm also not too ashamed to share them.
