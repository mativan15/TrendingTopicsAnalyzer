Current accounts: customers urged to switch to pocket up to £200
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-22 06:00:00

Time is running out to pocket cash freebies worth up to £200 for switching your current account.Several banks and building societies are offering financial incentives that could prove useful but some of the deals are due to end in the next few weeks.Nationwide is tempting new customers with £200 if they switch their current account to it, and those who move to NatWest before 3 November will receive £175. Lloyds is offering switchers £150 if they move before 14 November.TSB will give away up to £180, and First Direct is handing out £175 to new customers.
