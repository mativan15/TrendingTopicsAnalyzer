Published: 02:56 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 02:56 EDT, 3 June 2022 Hay fever season is already here, which means for some of us it's the time of sneezes, itchy eyes and runny noses.From ruining picnics to making every step of a country walk a struggle, symptoms can be irritating - or, at their very worst, unbearable - with some people even finding that their school, work, or sleep is affected.Pollen seasons are also getting longer. A US study published in February 2021* found that the pollen season extended by 30 days between 1990 and 2018.
