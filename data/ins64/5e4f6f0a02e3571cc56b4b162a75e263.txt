Canadian Tire Corporation, Limited (TSE:CTC.A) has announced that it will be increasing its dividend on the 1st of September to CA$1.63. This makes the dividend yield 3.1%, which is above the industry average.

If the payments aren't sustainable, a high yield for a few years won't matter that much. However, prior to this announcement, Canadian Tire Corporation's dividend was comfortably covered by both cash flow and earnings.
