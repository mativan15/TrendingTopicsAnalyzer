The Washington Capitals signed forward Sonny Milano to a one-year, $750,000 contract, the team announced Sunday. Milano was placed on waivers for purpose of loan to the Capitals' American Hockey League affiliate in Hershey, Pa. If he clears waivers, Milano will start his season in Hershey.
