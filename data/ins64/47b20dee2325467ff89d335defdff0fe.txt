Dubai, UAE, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Global Web3 venture capital and one of the largest market makers, DWF Labs has announced a strategic investment in the recent private funding round held by Precog.Finance that raised $3.5 million. DWF Labs joins a team of strategic advisors who are unified in strengthening Precog's ecosystem, focusing on driving the value and utility of the PCOG token, the platform's native digital asset.
