VA loans are mortgages for active or retired members of the military and their spouses, backed by the US Department of Veterans Affairs. They have more favorable terms than traditional mortgages and offer financial benefits to eligible service members, such as lower interest rates and no down payments. If you're a prospective homeowner looking to save money by securing a lower interest rate, especially as rates climb this year, a VA loan could be an appealing option.  Current VA loan rate trends Mortgage rates are increasing at their fastest pace in two decades -- and they're expected to keep going up.
