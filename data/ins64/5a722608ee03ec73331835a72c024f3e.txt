Kroger-Albertsons Tie-up Spurs Union Lobbying To Stop Merger

A screen displays the company logo for Kroger Co on the floor of the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) in New York City, U.S., October 14, 2022. 
Thousands of unionized retail workers from the U.S. Mid-Atlantic to the West Coast are lobbying regulators and lawmakers as part of a broader effort to nix the $25 billion merger between supermarket operators Kroger and Albertsons."There is no way that this is going to be good for workers," said Maggie Breshears, who works in the pickup department at a Kroger-owned Fred Meyer in Seattle.
