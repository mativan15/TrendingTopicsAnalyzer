PALO ALTO, Calif., Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Inpixon® (NASDAQ: INPX), the Indoor Intelligence® company, today announced that it has entered into a securities purchase agreement with a single institutional investor to purchase approximately $15.0 million of its shares of common stock (or pre-funded warrants in lieu thereof) and warrants to purchase common stock in a registered direct offering priced at-the-market under Nasdaq rules. The combined purchase price for one share of common stock and associated warrants will be $5.
