Momentum Grows for 2nd Korean Stock Exchange
naver.com
news
2022-06-03 11:33:00

Momentum is growing for a second stock exchange in Korea. Industry insiders on Thursday said a committee of representatives from the Korea Financial Investment Association and seven brokerages has garnered a generally positive response from 30 securities companies when it asked if they support the idea.The next step is to set the equity participation of each brokerage and apply for authorization, which will take about two years.The Korea Exchange was established in 1956 and has maintained its monopoly since then.
