Delhi to replace word 'Harijan' with 'Dr Ambedkar' in names of colonies, streets: Minister Gautam

APIn April 2018, the ministry wrote to all states and Union territories to avoid using the words 'Dalit' and 'Harijan' when referring to people belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC) communities.Delhi will soon issue a notification to replace the word 'Harijan' with 'Dr Ambedkar' in the names of streets and colonies in the national capital, Social Welfare Minister Rajendra Pal Gautam said on Thursday. Delhi will be one of the first states in the country to curb the use of the word which many people claim is "offensive and derogatory", Gautam told PTI.
