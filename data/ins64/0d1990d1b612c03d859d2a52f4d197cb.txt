October 18, 2022 06:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time LAS VEGAS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--BitNile Holdings, Inc. (NYSE American: NILE), a diversified holding company ('BitNile' or the 'Company'), announced today that its Executive Chairman, Mr. Milton 'Todd' Ault, III, will be presenting at the 15th annual LD Micro Main Event that will be held next week from Tuesday, October 25 through Thursday, October 27, 2022 at the Luxe Sunset Boulevard Hotel in Southern California and features over 200 companies presenting during the three-day event.
