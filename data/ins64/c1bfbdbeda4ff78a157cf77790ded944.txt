A Russian proverb: 'Wherever God has a church, the Devil opens a chapel.'As cops in Uvalde keep hiding behind officious language and their supposed respect for the kids they left to be slaughtered over the course of an hour, I keep thinking about 'The Night of the Hunter,' the haunting 1955 film about children pursued by an evil man while the adults they'd counted on remain preoccupied with their own concerns.That's not just the father gunned down shortly after robbing a bank and delivering the proceeds to his 9-year-old son and with his dying breath swearing the boy and his 4-year-old sister to silence about the stolen money.
