Op shop fashion is the secret of Aotearoa's most stylish toddler
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-18 22:26:34

Halo-Jerrau Wichman must be a contender for Aotearoa's best-dressed toddler. The 2.5-year-old from Palmerston North has a clothing collection that would put a Kardashian to shame, but with a major difference: it's all done on an op-shop budget.A $6 coat from the Red Cross Shop completes Halo-Jerrau Wichman's outfit. A fashion stylist, mum Summer Simon has always loved putting outfits together for friends and relatives' children, and approaches op shopping with her expert eye scanning colours and fabrics.
