Pakistan's flood victims forced to put rebuilding on hold – DW – 10/21/2022
dw.com
world
2022-10-21 11:17:31

Zaman Khan's simple mud and clay home in Pakistan's northwest Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province was washed away by record monsoon flooding in August. He wants to return, but cannot afford to rebuild. Khan is one of many flood victims in the decimated Swat River valley who are still waiting for financial aid promised by the government for repairs and reconstruction. The 38-year-old, who works in a plum orchard, said he is growing desperate. "The little I had saved as a lowly paid worker has been exhausted.
