If you aren't already obsessed with Sadie Sink, you're about to be. The 20-year-old actress plays Max Mayfield in Stranger Things, and with Season 4 of the Netflix show dropping over the weekend, get ready to be hearing about the talented Sadie Sink non-stop.

Or that she's strutted the catwalk as a runway model at Paris Fashion Week? Or that she went vegan at age 14 thanks to Woody Harrelson?
