Co-regulated policy environment enabling growth of start-ups in digital economy sector
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 09:09:31

The government's constant endeavours to transform India into a digitally empowered society and a knowledge economy have strengthened its claim to create USD 1 trillion-dollar economic value from the digital economy sector by 2025-26. Start-ups from the digital economy sector are one of the leading growth engines contributing to this economic value. With a strong foundation of digital infrastructure and expanded digital access created through the Digital India Programme, digital economy start-ups will be witnessing the next phase of growth in the coming five years.
