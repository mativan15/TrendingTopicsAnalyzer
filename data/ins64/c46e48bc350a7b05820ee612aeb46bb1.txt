Co-owner, Abrahams Oriental Rugs

How have you displayed high achievement in your industry? When I arrived in Houston in 1973, I founded Abraham's Oriental Rugs as a way to capitalize on my master's degree in Islamic History & Art and to reach an undeveloped oriental rug market in Houston at the time. Forty-nine years later, that company is the oldest and largest oriental rug business in Houston and one of the oldest and largest in the United States.
