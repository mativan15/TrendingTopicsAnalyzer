PKL 9: Haryana Steelers aim to bounce back to winning ways against U Mumba
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-20 13:43:59

Haryana Steelers will look to get back to winning ways in the Pro Kabaddi League season 9 when they face U Mumba at the Sree Kanteerava Stadium, in Bengaluru, on Friday. The Steelers gave a tough fight against defending champions Dabang Delhi, but lost the contest by a narrow margin of two points.Mumba, like the Steelers, have also won two out of their four games so far and Joginder Narwal, who was one of the key players for Steelers against Delhi, said his team has worked on their shortcomings and are ready for the encounter.
