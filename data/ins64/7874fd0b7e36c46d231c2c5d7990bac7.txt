June 01, 2022 04:01 PM Eastern Daylight Time MINNEAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The ROIG Group, a specialized consultancy with a relentless focus on executable, customer-centered outcomes, is pleased to announce two new clients to its recently launched Payments Practice.



The expansion of The ROIG Group's consulting offerings, led by industry experts Sheree Thornsberry and Shelly Schneekloth, comes at an exciting time for the fintech and payments industries. In fact, the global fintech market is expected to grow at a CAGR of around 20% per year over the next four years and the market value is anticipated to reach around $305 billion by 2025, according to Research and Markets.
