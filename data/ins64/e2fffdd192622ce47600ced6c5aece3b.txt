Year-end is a time when many people review their financial situation. As you look at your finances this year-end, here are five financial planning issues to consider.Portfolio review and rebalancingIt's a good idea to review your investment portfolio at the end of every year, but especially this year thanks to the volatility in the stock market. Is the asset allocation of your portfolio still in line with your risk tolerance and your financial goals? Rebalancing and making adjustments to your portfolio as we move forward into 2023 and beyond can help ensure that your portfolio is aligned with your overall financial plan.
