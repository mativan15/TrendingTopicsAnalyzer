Michigan football has been without one of its top offensive weapons since September.

On Friday night, the world got a bit of a glimpse as to why.

Tight end Erick All posted on Instagram several photos, including one of him in a hospital bed, seemingly preparing for surgery. In the caption, All wrote that he underwent "a life changing surgery" earlier this week in Fort Myers, Florida.
