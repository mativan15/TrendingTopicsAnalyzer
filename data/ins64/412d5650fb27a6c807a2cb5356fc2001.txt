COVID watch: ‘Cult' of Fear Harmed Kids
'There is no shortage of articles and studies documenting the harm caused by COVID-19 school policies,' notes Alex Gutentag at Tablet. One stat: 'The rate of chronically absent students nationwide is now 22%, 2.7 times what it was before school closures.' As the tragedy 'comes to light, some outlets . . . have been eager to suggest that we are now aware of the overwhelmingly negative consequences of these policies thanks to ‘new research' ' and that closure advocates 'can therefore be forgiven.
