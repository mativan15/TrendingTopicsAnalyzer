Police Travel To Taiwan To Arrest Home Invasion Suspect
sfgate.com
news
2022-06-04 07:00:00

San Francisco police this week traveled to Taiwan to arrest a suspect wanted in connection with a March home invasion. Tianze Zhang, 30, of Los Angeles, was arrested on an outstanding warrant on suspicion of felony burglary, felony kidnapping for robbery and felony terrorists threats, according to the San Francisco Police Department. On March 16 at approximately 5:30 p.m., San Francisco police officers responded to a residence in the Bernal Heights neighborhood on a report of a home invasion burglary.
