Lee Jae-seung, president and head of the consumer electronics business at Samsung Electronics [SAMSUNG ELECTRONICS] A home appliance president at Samsung Electronics offered to resign for personal reasons, the company said Tuesday. Lee Jae-seung, president and head of the consumer electronics business at Samsung Electronics, started working at the company in 1986 and is credited for leading the company's development of many innovative home devices, including the Bespoke Edition. He climbed the corporate ladder to become president of the division in December 2020.
