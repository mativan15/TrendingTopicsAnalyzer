October 18, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--First Republic Bank (NYSE:FRC), a leading private bank and wealth management company, today announced that Michael Brodnik has joined First Republic Investment Management.

Brodnik was named Managing Director and Wealth Manager. He will provide portfolio management, retirement planning, investment consulting and other wealth management services to individuals, families, nonprofits and private family foundations.

'Michael Brodnik shares First Republic's commitment to exceptional and differentiated client service,' said Bob Thornton, President of First Republic Private Wealth Management.
