SOME of these ongoing renovations include half painted walls and doors, incomplete fire pits or pizza ovens in the garden - and even entire kitchen overhauls which have fallen by the wayside.
The study of 2,000 property owners found almost half (48 per cent) don't think their home will ever be ‘finished' – with some of the bigger tasks still in progress ranging from loft conversions to half-done home offices.

Some of the top reasons for these jobs being left unfinished include, not having the skills required to complete them (22 per cent), underestimating the time it would take to manage the project (20 per cent) and under budgeting (16 per cent).
