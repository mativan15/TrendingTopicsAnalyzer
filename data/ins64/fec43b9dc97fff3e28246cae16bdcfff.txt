Dublin, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Global Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Distribution Growth Opportunities" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.

This research assesses the global PPE distribution industry's consolidation activity from 2018 to 2021, including distributors' mergers and acquisitions (M&As) in all major industries (manufacturing, construction, mining, oil and gas, transport, utilities, and food and chemical processing).

The product segments analyzed are above-the-neck PPE; respiratory, hand, foot, and fall protection; protective clothing; and gas detection.
