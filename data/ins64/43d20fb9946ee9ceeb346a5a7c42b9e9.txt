SF leader ‘anxious' to assist Government to implement eviction ban this week

Sinn Fein has called on the Government to implement the temporary eviction ban this week. 
 

 © Niall Carson Sinn Fein Leader Mary Lou McDonald speaking to the media as she visited the National Ploughing Championships at Ratheniska Co Laois (Niall Carson/PA)


Party leader Mary Lou McDonald told the Dail she is 'anxious' to assist the Government to ensure there is no delay in the winter ban becoming law.The Government approved a one-off ban on evictions for the winter period during Tuesday's Cabinet meeting.
