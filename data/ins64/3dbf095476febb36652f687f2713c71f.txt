This elevated Metro Rail package is to be constructed in 35 months.NEW DELHI: Larsen & Toubro (L&T) has secured one more ‘significant' package in the Chennai Metro Rail Projects. L&T has been awarded Package C3 (CP08 EV01) of Phase-II Chennai Metro Rail Projects. The scope of CP08 EV01 package involves the construction of an elevated viaduct of approximately 10 km, including an elevated ramp and ten elevated metro stations.
