A Blackpool financial assistant pleaded guilty to stealing £100,000 ($113,254) from her boss to fund an Instagram-worthy lifestyle that she couldn't afford on her own £900 a month salary. Laura Howarth bought a white SUV, hair extensions, and VIP concert tickets — sharing otherwise inexplicably lavish belongings and experiences to social media in the style of other seemingly wealthy women, "including her boss's wife," Lancs Live reported.
