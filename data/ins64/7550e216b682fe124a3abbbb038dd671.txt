reklamlar/iStock via Getty Images About SPLG This monthly article series reports sector metrics in the S&P 500 index. It is also a top-down review of all funds tracking it. Among them, the SPDR Portfolio S&P 500 ETF (NYSEARCA:SPLG), launched in November 2005, is less popular than the SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust (SPY). However, it has a lower expense ratio (0.03% vs. 0.09%) and a lower share price offering more flexibility to investors who can't trade fractional shares. It has $11.7B of assets under management and an average daily volume of about 5.
