Canada to ban replacement workers during strikes and lockouts, labour minister says

Canada's Labour Minister Seamus O'Regan announced Wednesday that the federal government is banning replacement workers in the event of a strike or lockout. The move is being made to "keep parties at the negotiating table," O'Regan said.
