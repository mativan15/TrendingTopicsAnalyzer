ANIDelhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal New Delhi: Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Sunday attacked the BJP government at the Centre for its "inaction" even as Kashmiri Pandits were being attacked and forced to leave the Valley. Addressing a Jan Aakrosh Rally, organised by the Aam Aadmi Party at Jantar Mantar in Delhi, Kejriwal compared the present situation with what happened in the 1990s when Kashmiri Pandits fled from the Valley.
