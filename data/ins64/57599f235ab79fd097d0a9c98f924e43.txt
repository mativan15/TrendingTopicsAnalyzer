TVLine.com Julianne Moore (Lisey's Story) will finagle her way into the royal court in the period drama Mary & George for AMC Networks and Sky.Based on true events, the eight-episode limited series stars Moore as Mary Villiers, Countess of Buckingham, 'who in 17th century England molded her beautiful son, George (casting Tba), to seduce King James I and become his all-powerful lover,' our sister site Deadline reports. The mother and son rose from humble beginnings to achieve great riches and influence through their scheming.
