Carson Wentz is facing a 4-6 week recovery process
usatoday.com
news
2022-10-17 15:10:08

Washington Commanders quarterback Carson Wentz is facing a recovery time of 4-6 weeks after fracturing the ring finger on his throwing hand last week, according to Ian Rapoport and Mike Garofolo of The NFL Network.
Wentz is currently in Los Angeles visiting with a hand specialist and could be a candidate for injured reserve with a designation to return.
