The world's biggest cryptocurrency by market value jumped 7.6% on Monday, registering its biggest single-day percentage rise since March 8. The bounce came after nine straight weekly losses and the recent seller failure to keep prices under the crucial support of $28,800 – the July 2021 low on Coinbase. This jump comes shortly after the the S&P 500 rose 6.
