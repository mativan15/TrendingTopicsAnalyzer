May 27, 2022 07:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Mednow Inc. ('Mednow') (TSXV: MNOW), Canada's on-demand virtual pharmacy, is pleased to announce a strategic partnership with Curv Health, a digital health company that provides low-cost health services to tens of thousands of employees across Canada. Curv Health and Mednow's care teams have partnered on an integrated care program for type 1 and type 2 diabetes patients across Canada. The program consists of a 16-week regime of guided physical and nutritional education and activities to engage patients of all levels - as designed by Curv Health's Certified Diabetes Educators (CDE) with integrated support from the pharmacy care team at Mednow.
