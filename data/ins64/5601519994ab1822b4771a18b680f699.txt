Alcoa rallies despite big Q3 miss, as some analysts see attractive entry point (NYSE:AA)

Jeff Swensen/Getty Images News Alcoa (NYSE:AA) +6.4% in Thursday's trading, a dramatic reversal of its opening 5% loss that followed worse than expected Q3 results, as some analysts come to the stock's defense. Goldman Sachs analyst Emily Chieng reiterated a Buy rating and a spot on the firm's Conviction List for Alcoa (AA), seeing an attractive valuation, "particularly for investors who maintain a positive view on the secular growth story for longer-term aluminum demand." Chieng said the stock's pullback "offers an attractive entry point for those seeking leverage to commodity price upside, once near-term earnings expectations have been recalibrated.
