Ashok Gehlot asks PM Modi to compare Rajasthan model with Gujarat model

Source: ANI

Asking Prime Minister Narendra Modi to compare the Rajasthan model with the Gujarat model, Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot on October 20 in Jaipur, said that the PM should adopt the good things from the Rajasthan model and can share the better features from the Gujarat model with the Rajasthan government. 'PM Modi should study the Rajasthan model and compare it with the Gujarat model. He should adopt good things from our model and if they have anything good to offer from the Gujarat model, they should tell us, we will implement those too,' the Rajasthan CM said.
