Delhi's air quality plunge into 'poor' category, experts warn it to remain same for next few days

The maximum temperature in the national capital reached 32.6 degrees Celsius today, the weather office said.After few days of good and moderate air quality due to recent spell of rains, the air quality in Delhi dropped to "poor" category on Sunday. The Central Pollution Control Board's 24-hour average Air Quality Index (AQI) data showed that at 6.05 pm in the evening , Delhi's air quality plunged to poor category at 232. The experts warn that the air quality is expected to remain 'poor' for the next few days.
