LorenzoT81/iStock Editorial via Getty Images2022 has thus far been a very bearish year for most technology stocks. The Nasdaq 100 has lost around a quarter of its value while 2020's high-flyers such as Cathie Wood's ARKK is down by over 50% this year. A quick look at online retail trading groups such as WallStreetBets shows that once-voracious crowds of young investors have now turned quite bleak and demoralized. Top brokers among these typically younger investors, such as Robinhood (NASDAQ:HOOD), have seen a crash in the brokerage's fundamentals.
