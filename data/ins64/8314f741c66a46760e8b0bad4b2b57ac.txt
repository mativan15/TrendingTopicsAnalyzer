(Reuters-Yonhap)Four people have been arrested for scamming a total 1.7 billion won ($1.3 million) out of investors who transferred money on a fake cryptocurrency exchange, South Korean police said Tuesday.The four, recently taken into police custody, are suspected of operating the fake crypto exchange that tricked investors into thinking they were actually trading coins and gold. More than 20 investors saw losses after the scammers told them they were unable to return their investments due to the coins' and golds' plummeted value, according to the Seoul Seongdong Police Station.
