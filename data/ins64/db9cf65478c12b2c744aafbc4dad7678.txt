KTRH Local Houston and Texas NewsBy Wyatt GoolsbyOct 19, 2022Demand for riskier home loans is way up. Homebuyers trying to cope with rising interest rates are turning to adjustable-rate mortgages. These ARMs can be fixed up for 5 to 10 years, but they are considered riskier loans because the rate can go up or down after that initial period. That description might sound familiar to many Americans. It's the same type of loan that played a big role in the 2008 housing crash. Economics Professor Hank Lewis at Lone Star College says the country is not in the same situation in 2022, but he's still concerned.
