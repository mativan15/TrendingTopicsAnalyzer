The move came after services of 21 assistant project officers (APOs) of MGNREGA were terminated by the state government on Friday.Over 12,000 contractual MGNREGA employees, who were on strike for the last two months for regularization of service, on Saturday resigned en masse. The move came after services of 21 assistant project officers (APOs) of MGNREGA were terminated by the state government on Friday. The employees of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act scheme are protesting since April for various demands, including regularization of service and better pay, said Tikamchand Kaushik, vice president of Chhattisgarh MGNREGA Karamchari Mahasangh.
