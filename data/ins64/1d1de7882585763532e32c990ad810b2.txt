DWAC sister SPAC Benessere Capital to liquidate after eCombustible deal terminated
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-21 21:47:41

Patrick Orlando's SPAC Benessere Capital Acquisition (NASDAQ:BENE) said Friday it will liquidate as a result of the recent termination of its merger with eCombustible, ongoing regulatory investigations and its sponsor not making additional monthly contributions to the trust account. Benessere Capital (BENE) will redeem all outstanding class A shares included in the units issued in its IPO at a per-share redemption price of ~$10.45. Redemption of shares will be effected within 10 business days after Oct.
