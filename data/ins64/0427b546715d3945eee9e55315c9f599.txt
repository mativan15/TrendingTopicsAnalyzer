LOUISVILLE, Ky., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- LogicMark, Inc. (Nasdaq: LGMK) (the 'Company' or 'LogicMark'), a provider of personal emergency response systems (PERS), health communications devices, and technology for the growing care economy, today announced that Chia-Lin Simmons, Chief Executive Officer and Mark Archer, Chief Financial Officer will present a corporate overview at the LD Micro Main Event XV. The conference is being held on October 25 – 27, 2022 at the Luxe Sunset Boulevard Hotel in Los Angeles.
