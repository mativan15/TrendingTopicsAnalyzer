ATLANTA, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Global FinTech Capital on Tap has secured a $110 million corporate revolving credit facility with Atalaya Capital Management to provide small businesses in the US and UK with a central hub to manage all aspects of their business finances.

Since launching in 2012, Capital on Tap has provided over $5 billion of funding to more than 200,000 small businesses. In 2021, Capital on Tap launched their US business, which already has more than 14,000 Capital on Tap Business Credit Card holders in its one year of existence.
