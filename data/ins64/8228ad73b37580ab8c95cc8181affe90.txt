Activist Irenic said to push for News Corp. to split media, real estate listings business
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-17 13:38:01

Activist investor Irenic Capital Management is said to want News Corp. (NASDAQ:NWSA) to split its media and real estate listing business. The activist wants News Corp. (NWSA) to consider splitting its online real estate listings unit from its other businesses, according to a New York Times report from late Sunday, which cited an interview with Adam Katz, one of Irenic's founders. The activist is said to have accumulated a $150 million stake in the owner of the Wall Street Journal and the New York Post.
