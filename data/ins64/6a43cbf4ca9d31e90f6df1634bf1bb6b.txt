REUTERS/Shailesh Andrade/FilesBENGALURU, Oct 21 (Reuters) - India's SBI Life Insurance Co (SBIL.NS) reported a 53% jump in second-quarter profit on Friday, helped by a rise in premiums and as its value of new business grew.Insurance penetration has traditionally been low in India, but that is slowly changing, especially as more people rushed to buy new policies after the pandemic.
