Illinois Governor Election 2022: Know the candidates
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 14:30:00

Two candidates will be competing with J.B. Pritzker to become Illinois Governor in 2022 elections. Scott Schluter, a downstate diesel mechanic and an Air Force member, and Republican State Sen. Darren Bailey are competing with Pritzker, who was first elected in 2018. Businessman and philanthropist J.B. Pritzker is running for re-election in the Illinois state legislature after defeating former governor Bruce Rauner in the general election of 2018.

The Illinois governor has bragged about his track record and performance leading US state through the COVID-19 pandemic while running for office.
