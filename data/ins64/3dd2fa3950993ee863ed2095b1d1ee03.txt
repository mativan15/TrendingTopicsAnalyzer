This year's Fast 50 companies are a testament to fortitude.

Over the past several years, they have thrived in the face of a global pandemic, supply chain constraints, the Great Resignation and, more recently, threats of a potential economic slowdown.

In many industries, growth has slowed compared with the halcyon days of the late 2010s. But these businesses have nevertheless navigated the choppy waters to earn their place among the fastest-growing companies in the metro.

The 2022 Fast 50 chronicles exemplary financial performance, but also contains nuggets of wisdom about company culture, handling challenges and growing sustainably.
