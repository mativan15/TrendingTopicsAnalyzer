Supply Chain Unicorn Flexport's Financing Arm Gets $200M From KKR

2022-10-20 16:58:28

Email Facebook Twitter LinkedIn Just eight months after San Francisco-based Flexport locked up a $935 million round valuing the company at $8 billion, its five-year-old trade financing arm secured its own $200 million investment from PE giant KKR.
The investment comes in the form of a credit facility and will be used to grow the market of the lender, which helps finance importers and exporters along the supply chain which often face choppy cash flow issues.
