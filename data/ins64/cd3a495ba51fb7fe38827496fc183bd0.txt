NewsAuto NewsMost affordable cars in India with an air purifier to fight pollution: Nissan Magnite to Kia SonetAir pollution is one of the major causes of concerns for North Indians during winters, with the air quality even reaching hazardous levels each year. While it is advised to stay home during days with poor air quality, sometime's travel is unpreventable. In light of the situation, a range of manufacturers today offer an inbuilt air purifier with their cars, at least on the higher-end variants.
