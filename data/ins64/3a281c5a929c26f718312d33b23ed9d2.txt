May 31, 2022 04:01 PM Eastern Daylight Time PIOMBINO DESE, Italy--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Stevanato Group S.p.A. (NYSE: STVN) a leading global provider of drug containment, drug delivery, and diagnostic solutions to the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and life sciences industries, today announced the acquisition of a new industrial complex in Cisterna di Latina, Italy. The new facility is expected to strengthen Stevanato Group's presence in Europe and expand the Company's production capabilities to satisfy increased demand for its standard and premium products from high-growth customers.
