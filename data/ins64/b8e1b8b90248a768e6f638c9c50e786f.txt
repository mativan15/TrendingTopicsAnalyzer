Published: 08:43 EDT, 2 June 2022 | Updated: 08:46 EDT, 2 June 2022 A Morris dancer left in charge of her dead parents' £2million fortune been removed as executor of the estate after being accused of having her 'hands in the till'.Dana Conlin and her brother Eugene were appointed to look after Rosina and Charles' multimillion-pound properties in south Wales after they died in 2012.The will instructed them to look after the family wealth until their dead brother Daniel's children were old enough to in inherit their combined third of the estate.
