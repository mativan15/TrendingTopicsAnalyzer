Stablecoin Regulations 'Probably Not Happening In Early 2023': US Congressman
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 06:12:25

Despite the hype among regulators surrounding the creation of the regulatory framework for stablecoins, U.S. Rep. Jim Himes (D-Conn.) has noted that a stablecoin bill may not be on the table in the early part of 2023.Stablecoin is a cryptocurrency designed to have a stable price by being pegged to a commodity or currency"It's probably not happening in early 2023," Himes, who sits on the House Financial Services Committee, said during a financial conference Tuesday. The U.S. congressman shared that the bill is "not happening this Congress" because of the upcoming elections.
