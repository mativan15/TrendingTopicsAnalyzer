Bernard Dunne confirmed as head of Indian Boxing
irishtimes.com
sport
2022-10-17 00:00:00

Bernard Dunne has been officially declared the head of India's High Performance boxing. The former professional world champion, who resigned from a similar post in Irish boxing about six months ago, joins USA's Billy Walsh as one of two former top Irish coaches who will now compete against Ireland at the next Olympic Games in Paris 2024.The Boxing Federation of India (BFI) tweeted the appointment of the 42-year-old on Monday as a replacement for Santiago Nieva citing the emergence of Kellie Harrington as Olympic champion at Tokyo 2020 as well as the world champion in 2018.
