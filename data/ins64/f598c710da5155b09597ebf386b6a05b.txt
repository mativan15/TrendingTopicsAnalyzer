By Reuters Published: 10:07 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 10:07 EDT, 27 May 2022 By Amal S May 27 (Reuters) - Canada's main stock index rose on Friday, putting it on track for its best weekly performance in more than three months, aided by gains in technology and cyclical shares amid an upbeat mood in global equities. At 9:47 a.m. ET (13:47 GMT), the Toronto Stock Exchange's S&P/TSX composite index was up 87.44 points, or 0.43%, at 20,619.62. It appeared set to gain for a sixth consecutive session, its longest winning streak since mid-March.
