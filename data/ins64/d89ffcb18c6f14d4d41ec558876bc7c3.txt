Minimum Support Price Hiked For 6 Crops, Including Wheat
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-18 07:57:56

The government on Tuesday hiked the minimum support price for six crops to boost production and income of the farmers.The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA), which is chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, decided to raise the MSPs (minimum support prices) at a meeting.The MSP is the price the government pays farmers for their grain. The government currently sets MSPs for 23 crops that are farmed through both the rabi and kharif seasons.In October, shortly following the harvest of kharif (summer) crops, rabi (winter) crops are sown.
