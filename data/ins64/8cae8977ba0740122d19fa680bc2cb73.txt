With the growing consensus that Airbnb isn't worth its cost these days, you're probably back to booking hotels as the top choice for your upcoming travel this summer. And if you can afford it, you might automatically assume it's better to opt for the most luxurious accommodations within your budget. But does a higher price tag really lead to a higher quality experience? (And with room service getting axed in many hotels across the country, what even counts as luxury anymore?)When you're budgeting in your pre-travel spreadsheet, your lodging situation shouldn't be based solely on the numbers you can technically afford.
