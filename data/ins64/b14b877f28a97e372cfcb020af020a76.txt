Melissa Carrion, a professor at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, who studies the rhetoric of health and medicine, spoke to 50 mothers who had refused one or more vaccines for their children for a study published in 2017.Loading'Across the board, every single one of them gave some variation of the advice that a mother ‘should do her own research,' ' she said in a phone interview. 'It was this kind of worldview that was less about the result of the research than the individual process of doing it themselves.
