In the Land of Too Much Television, This Nerd Is Queen
nytimes.com
news
2022-10-16 14:00:08

For millions of people who tune in each week, keeping up with HBO's ' House of the Dragon ' is a diverting pastime; for Mallory Rubin, it's more like a professional sport.

Rubin makes two weekly podcasts about 'Dragon,' the 'Game of Thrones' prequel series. To train, she watches each episode a minimum of three times and then prepares a discussion outline, typically running seven or eight pages, cross-referencing the show with the original books by George R.R. Martin and eight seasons of 'Thrones.
