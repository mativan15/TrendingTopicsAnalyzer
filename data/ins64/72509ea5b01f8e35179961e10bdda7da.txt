US baby formula manufacturer Abbott Nutrition says its production plant — closed for months due to contamination — has reopened, taking a step towards alleviating a nationwide shortage that has sent parents scrambling for supplies.Two babies died after consuming contaminated formula produced at the plantUS officials say it could take about two months before formula supplies return to normal levelsAround three-quarters of baby products were out of stock in the US in late MayThe February shutdown of the largest formula factory in the US led to supply problems that have forced some parents to seek formula from food banks, friends and doctors' offices.
