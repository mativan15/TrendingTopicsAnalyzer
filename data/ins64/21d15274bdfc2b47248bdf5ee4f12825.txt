Founder and CEO of Artisan, an innovation consultancy supporting global leaders with technology strategy and solution services. getty Sitting on the tarmac heading home after my company's annual employee retreat, I was reminded that an investment in people and culture is rarely wasted. This year's retreat was special because it marked the first time our employees had come together in over two years due to the pandemic. The last time we all got together, the company was one-quarter of the size, which meant this was the very first time most people had the opportunity to shake their colleagues' hands.
