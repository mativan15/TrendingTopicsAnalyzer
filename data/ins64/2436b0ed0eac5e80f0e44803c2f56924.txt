Only 5 Bay Area companies went public in Q3 — and 1 moved to Texas
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-17 19:58:00

Just five Bay Area companies hit Wall Street in the third quarter — and not one of them through an initial public offering.

None of the companies, which all went public via mergers with blank-check companies, had a particularly auspicious start to life on Wall Street. Each of them raised less money in their combinations than they initially projected. All five are now worth significantly less than when they went public.

And one of them has since moved to Texas.
