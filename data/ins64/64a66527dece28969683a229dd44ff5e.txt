Three MCDs' finances merged as body looks to ensure salary parity
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-06-03 20:15:00

Orders for merging the bank accounts and financial books of the three erstwhile municipal corporations were issued on Friday, and from here on, only common bank accounts of the unified Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) will be operated in the name of the unified body's commissioner, a senior municipal official said.'After the unification of the erstwhile corporations, the finance department has opened new bank accounts for the unified municipal corporation, in the name of municipal commissioner, for various departments and payment gateways.
