Sappington Names Alicia McGarry New Content Strategist Adding Messaging Power

SEATTLE (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 Sappington, a leading technology marketing firm with headquarters in Seattle, WA, today announced the appointment of Alicia McGarry to the position of Content Strategist. McGarry will bring enterprise technology to life for the firm's clients, providing thought leadership and creating powerful content through creative storytelling and messaging frameworks for marketers seeking to position their offerings to win hearts, minds, and market share. McGarry comes to Sappington with a depth and breadth of experience in brand communications and product marketing across a range of enterprise technology companies.
