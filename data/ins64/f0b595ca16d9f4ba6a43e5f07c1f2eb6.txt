Ulta Beauty (ULTA -3.73%) gave shareholders some good reasons to cheer its latest earnings report. The beauty products retailer said in its fiscal 2022 first-quarter update that it is still growing sales at a robust pace as people return to more in-person interactions. Ulta's earnings spiked, too, as it handled rising costs.
CEO Dave Kimbell and his team described a positive environment in this part of the retailing world as they raised their 2022 outlook. Let's take a closer look at why Ulta's business is looking so attractive today.
