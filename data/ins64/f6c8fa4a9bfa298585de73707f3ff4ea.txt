For Rescue Of Man Found Under Car In Milford, First Responders Honored

2022-10-17 12:04:47

Firefighters and police from Milford, Hopkinton and members of the Fire District 14 Tech Rescue were honored by Worcester DA Joseph Early Jr. for a search and rescue effort in July. (Worcester DA Joseph Early Jr. )MILFORD, MA — The large group of local first responders who helped rescue a 60-year-old man found trapped under an SUV off I-495 in July were honored recently by Worcester County District Attorney Joseph Early Jr. for their work.Early presented police and firefighters from Milford and Hopkinton — plus a technical rescue team that includes firefighters from Framingham, Natick, Wayland, Westborough, Ashland and other local towns — with his Team Excellence and Merit (TEAM) award for their multi-day effort.
