Kanye 'Ye' West has inked a deal with Parlement Technologies to buy Parler, the extreme right-wing copycat of Twitter that was started to "fight against Big Tech, Big Government, censorship and cancel culture,".Parler, founded in 2018, and banned by both Apple and Google's play stores after the January 6 Capitol Hill riots in the U.S., has been described as an American alt-tech microblogging and social networking service associated with Donald Trump supporters, conservatives, conspiracy theorists, and far-right extremists.
