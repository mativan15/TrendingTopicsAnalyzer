June 02, 2022 05:18 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--SmartBiz®, the leading AI-powered financing platform for small businesses, has been named one of the 2022 Best Places to Work in Financial Technology. This is the fourth year SmartBiz Loans has earned this distinction.



Companies from across the United States entered a two-part survey process to determine Arizent's Best Places to Work in Financial Technology. The scores determined the top companies and the final ranking.
