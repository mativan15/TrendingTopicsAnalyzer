Fanatics Hires Finance Chief for Its Sports-Betting Division Ahead of Planned Launch
wsj.com
news
2022-10-18 23:43:00

Photo: Fanatics Inc. Sports-merchandising company Fanatics Inc. has picked a finance chief for its sports-betting and gambling division ahead of its planned launch early next year. Jacksonville, Fla.-based Fanatics on Tuesday named Andrea Ellis as chief financial officer of its betting and gambling unit. Ms. Ellis was most recently CFO for about two years at electric-scooter company Lime. Before that, she held senior roles in finance at Restaurant Brands International Inc., which owns brands including Burger King and Tim Hortons.
