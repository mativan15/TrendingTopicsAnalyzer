LMF Acquisition Opportunities Down 27% After Rising 23% Wednesday
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 17:27:00

By Josh Beckerman Shares of blank-check company LMF Acquisition Opportunities Inc., which is merging with SeaStar Medical Inc., fell 27% to $7.61 after a 23% gain Wednesday. According to a securities filing Wednesday, holders of 10.26 million Class A shares exercised their right to redeem shares as of Monday. "Subsequently, LMAO has been made aware that certain holders of Class A common stock may request to change their election to have their shares redeemed, which LMAO may allow," the company said.
