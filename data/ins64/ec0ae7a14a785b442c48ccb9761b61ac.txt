Twitterati gives thumbs up to Tamil movie 'Sardar', call it a 'Diwali Blockbuster'
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 11:15:00

Tamil movie 'Sardar' has been released in theatres on Friday, October 21. The spy thriller, with Karthi in the lead role, has been rated highly by moviegoers. The film also features Raashii Khanna and Chunky Panday. On Twitter, fans have termed it a "Diwali Winner''. The audience was spellbound by the stars' performance and the overall plot. This happens to be the third hit in a row for Karthi, whose two other recent releases include Ponniyin Selvan and Viruman.
 One user claimed it was the best performance by Karthi so far.
