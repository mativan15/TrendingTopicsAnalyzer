Athersys Shares Fall 7% After Company Announces Restructuring, Mgmt Changes
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 21:45:00

By Stephen Nakrosis

Shares of Athersys, Inc. were lower in Thursday's late trading session after the biotechnology company announced a corporate restructuring plan and changes to its executive team.

At 5:24 p.m. EDT, the company's shares had fallen 7.29% to trade at 20 cents each. The stock finished the day's regular session with a 7.71% gain, closing at 22 cents per share.
