Law for population control will be brought soon: Union Minister Prahlad Singh Patel
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-31 16:00:00

Union Minister Prahlad Singh Patel on Tuesday said a law for population control will be brought soon. The Union Minister for food processing industries was in Raipur to attend 'Garib Kalyan Sammelan' at ICAR-National Institute of Biotic Stress Management, Baronda. When asked by reporters about a law on population control, Patel said, "It will be brought soon, don't worry. When such strong and big decisions have been taken then the rest too (will be taken)." 
He also attacked the ruling Congress in Chhattisgarh claiming it has failed to achieve targets under some Central schemes.
