Telangana govt provides auto trollies under Dalit Bandhu Scheme

news
2022-10-18 18:04:16

Oct 18, 2022, 11:34PM IST

Source: ANI

Telangana government is providing auto trollies worth Rs 10 lakhs under Dalit Bandhu Scheme. They aim to empower Dalits by taking them out of poverty. There is no bank linkage and no collateral security required in Dalita Bandhu Yojana. The beneficiary has complete freedom to select any business or activity, an individual is good at. The distribution is done for the fourth time. 10 more vehicles will be distributed soon. Under this, complete financial assistance is in the shape of grants which need not be paid back.
