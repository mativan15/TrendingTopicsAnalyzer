Albany NY, United States, May 27, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The value of the global metallized flexible packaging market was clocked at US$ 4.1 Bn in 2021. The market is estimated to develop at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period, from 2022 to 2032. The global metallized flexible packaging market is anticipated to attain value of US$ 62.8 Bn by 2032. A rise in demand for adequate food packaging, as well as customers' increasingly stressful lifestyles, are likely to drive sales of metallized flexible packaging.
