SAN FRANCISCO (PRWEB) May 31, 2022 AIKON is thrilled to welcome Karan Sachan as AIKON's Head of Product! In the midst of rapid product growth, Karan will oversee product strategy and champion the continued innovation of AIKON's Web3 authentication platform, ORE ID, and B2B crypto wallet, ORE Vault. Deepening AIKON's Leadership Bench with Powerhouse Blockchain Executives With over 8 years of product development, leadership and consulting experience in blockchain, cryptocurrency and fintech, Karan joins AIKON following a four-year tenure at Nasdaq where he led as Director of Product Management.
