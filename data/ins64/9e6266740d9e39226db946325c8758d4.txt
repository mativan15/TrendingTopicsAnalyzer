wine2wine Business Forum 2022 returns to Verona on November 7th-8th 2022 This makes wine2wine Business Forum a must-attend event for professionals who want to develop and expand their trade in the international wine sector. VERONA, Italy (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 The wine2wine Business Forum returns to Verona for its 9th edition on November 7th-8th, presenting a program of more than 50 sessions spread over two days, along with B2B opportunities, a book corner and a podcast marathon. Highlights include emerging digital communicators opening the door to the world of social media, as well as analysis of consolidated and fast-growing markets such as China and Africa.
