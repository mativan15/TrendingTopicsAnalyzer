Low pressure area forms in Bay of Bengal, cyclonic storm likely
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 09:30:00

VISAKHAPATNAM: A low pressure area (LPA) was formed over north Andaman Sea and adjoining areas of south Andaman Sea and southeast Bay of Bengal on Thursday morning.The India Meteorological Department (IMD) has said that it is very likely to move west-northwestwards and concentrate into a depression over east central and adjoining southeast Bay of Bengal around October 22 and into a deep depression on October 23. Subsequently, it is very likely to recurve northwards and intensify into a cyclonic storm over west central and adjoining east central Bay of Bengal by October 24 .
