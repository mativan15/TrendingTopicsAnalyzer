Govt relaxes educational qualification to recruit tribal youths from Chhattisgarh in CRPF
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-01 14:30:00

AgenciesThe decision was taken at a meeting of the Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.The Centre on Wednesday approved a proposal to relax the minimum educational qualification to recruit native tribal youths from interior areas of Bijapur, Dantewada and Sukma in Chhattisgarh as constables in the CRPF. The decision was taken at a meeting of the Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The Union cabinet has approved a proposal of the Ministry of Home Affairs to relax requisite minimum educational qualification from 10th class pass to 8th Class for the recruitment of 400 candidates as constable (general duty) in the CRPF from three districts of south Chattisgarh namely Bijapur, Dantewada and Sukma, an official release said.
