Operation Fox Hunt: Seven charged in alleged plot to repatriate US resident to China
brisbanetimes.com.au
news
2022-10-20 20:15:27

Washington: The United States unsealed criminal charges on Thursday against seven Chinese nationals accused of waging a surveillance and harassment campaign against a US resident and his family, in a bid by the Chinese government to repatriate one of them back to China.The eight-count indictment, unsealed in a US District Court in Brooklyn, New York, is the latest case by the Justice Department targeting China's apparent expatriation campaign, known as 'Operation Fox Hunt.'The FBI has begun investigating more cases of state-directed coercion in the US.
