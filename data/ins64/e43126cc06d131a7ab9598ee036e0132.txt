June 01, 2022 10:48 AM Eastern Daylight Time HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Audubon Engineering Company LP (Audubon), a leading provider of engineering, procurement, construction, and fabrication services, has entered a three-year contract with Shell Offshore Inc., a subsidiary of Shell plc, to provide brownfield engineering and procurement (EP) services support for Shell's Mars Corridor.



The contract, which comes with two one-year options to extend, covers some of Shell's offshore assets in the US Gulf of Mexico, including its Mars, Olympus, Ursa, and Vito tension leg platforms.
