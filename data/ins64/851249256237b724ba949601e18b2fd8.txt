Omnicom: Strong Q3 Results Despite Recessionary Winds (NYSE:OMC)
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-19 16:16:47

We Are Omnicom Group (NYSE:OMC) just delivered Q3 2022 results that exceeded expectations, with organic growth of 7.5%, which was broad-based. Shares are responding positively in after hours, since there was a lot of pessimism given the recessionary winds the industry is facing. Earnings per share for the quarter were $1.77, up 7.3% versus 2021, despite a negative currency impact due to the strong US dollar. On a constant currency basis, earnings per share would have increased approximately 12.3%.
