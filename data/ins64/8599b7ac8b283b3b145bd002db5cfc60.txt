BANGALORE, India , June 1, 2022 /CNW/ -- Sonata Software, a global IT services and technology solutions company, has announced that Balaji Kumar has joined the Company as Chief Human Resources Officer, responsible for HR function globally. Balaji will be based out of the Company's Corporate Office at Bangalore, India.

Sonata Software Logo

In his last stint, Balaji was the Chief Human Resources Officer globally at L&T Infotech.
