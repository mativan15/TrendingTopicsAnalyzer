Found roaming the paths of Vardoran, you will discover several merchants that offer rare and unique items. In this Merchant Guide, you will learn everything you need to know about finding merchants, the requirements needed to trade merchants, and custom maps that track all known routes that merchants can travel.
In V Rising, you will find three merchants roaming the paths of Vardoran, selling unique and rare goods from their traveling stores. Merchants can be found in the following regions:
 Farbane Woods - Gavyn the Shady Dealer Dunley Farmlands - Berk the Traveling Trader Silverlight Hills - Ottar the MerchantThese merchants can stock items that range from rare seeds that can be planted and farmed for resources, gemstones, potions and other consumables, as well as unique cosmetic hats for Silver Coins.
