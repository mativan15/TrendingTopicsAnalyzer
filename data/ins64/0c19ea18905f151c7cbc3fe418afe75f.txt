New Research Reveals Causal Effect Between Brand Social Values and Increased Business Performance

NEW YORK, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- UM, the global media agency network of IPG Mediabrands, today released its groundbreaking "Future Impact" study at Advertising Week New York. The research highlights the importance of socially responsible practices and values -- including integrity, equity and sustainability -- on consumer decision making, with consumer purchase intent more than doubling when socially conscious brands engage advertising media partners that share common values.
