Published: 10:46 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 11:09 EDT, 18 October 2022 Stunning new pictures reveal the incredible features of a £16,745-a-night ($19,000) estate on Richard Branson's latest private paradise - Moskito Island.The nine-bedroom estate - part of the Virgin Limited Edition portfolio - is called The Village and has impressive amenities that include a two-storey infinity pool, a slide and a futuristic, hidden DJ booth that rises from the outdoor deck.It joins three other estates that already call Moskito home - The Branson Estate (owned by Sir Richard Branson), The Oasis Estate and The Point Estate.
