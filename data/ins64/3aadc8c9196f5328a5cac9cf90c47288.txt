People of Crypto Lab (POC) And The Sandbox to Debut the First-Ever Metaverse Diversity, Equity and Inclusivity Hub, The Valley of Belonging, During Pride Month

May 31, 2022 06:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK & PARIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Sandbox, a leading decentralized gaming metaverse and subsidiary of Animoca Brands, will collaborate with newly launched People of Crypto Lab (POC) on the Valley of Belonging, the first-ever diversity, equity, and inclusivity hub in the metaverse. The new hub will launch 24 June 2022 during a special Belonging Week event where The Sandbox will open ahead of its Alpha Season 3 to celebrate our differences, boldy reaffirming the importance of an equitable Web3.
