Los Angeles Angels star Mike Trout mum on fantasy football dispute, undecided on being league commissioner again
espn.com
news
2022-06-01 21:07:00

NEW YORK -- Los Angeles Angels star Mike Trout is trying to steer clear of the fantasy football league dispute between Cincinnati Reds outfielder Tommy Pham and San Francisco Giants outfielder Joc Pederson.After Pham told reporters on Tuesday that Trout was the "worst commissioner in fantasy sports" because he could have prevented the dispute between the two outfielders, Trout was asked on Wednesday to address whether Pederson broke the league's rules.The three-time MVP and nine-time All-Star kept his answer brief.
