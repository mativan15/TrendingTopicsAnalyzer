A roundup of recent banking and finance news from the Philadelphia area:

Citizens adding 200 wealth advisors

Citizens Financial Group, the parent company to Citizens Bank, plans to hire 200 new wealth advisors as part of a broader goal to expand wealth management operations. The bank is also offering a cash incentive to clients that start a relationship with Citizens.

The Providence, Rhode Island-based Citizens (NYSE: CFG) said it will launch Citizens Private Client to provide broader wealth management services to customers with $200,000 or more in deposits or investments.
