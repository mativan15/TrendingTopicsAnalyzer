Continue reading the main storyMay 29, 2022, 7:00 a.m. ETCredit...Giacomo BagnaraOn Wednesday at midnight, the Biden administration let lapse an exemption in the sweeping sanctions against Russia that had allowed the country to continue making payments on its foreign debts during its war on Ukraine. The Treasury Department issued the announcement just hours before the deadline. Russia is now hurtling toward a benchmark it has been intent on avoiding: a default on its foreign debt for the first time in more than a century.
