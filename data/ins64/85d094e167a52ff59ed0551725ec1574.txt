RSS' 4-day meet begins today, to discuss NEP, population
indiatimes.com

2022-10-15 18:15:00

AgenciesThe Sangh leaders will also mull ways to increase the participation of women in RSS-affiliates, sources told ET.The Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh's four-day meeting starting from October 16 in Prayagraj will focus on the need for a national population policy, ways to increase the RSS' outreach among the SC communities and strengthen the implementation of the national education policy, particularly get more institutions to offer courses in regional languages. The Sangh leaders will also mull ways to increase the participation of women in RSS-affiliates, sources told ET.
