Residential to the rescue
bizjournals.com
news
2022-10-20 21:09:00

The pandemic was not kind to downtown Minneapolis. Offices sat empty for months and hybrid work has limited the workforce that has returned, causing property values for the central business district's office buildings to plummet.

However, on the residential side of the market, things are different. The total value for all residentially zoned properties in downtown Minneapolis has risen 47% since tax year 2019, largely driven by new construction that has transformed vacant and underutilized properties into luxury and other market-rate apartments.
