Big 95 Morning Show with Dewayne WellsBy Dewayne WellsOct 21, 2022Elton John's 'Farewell Yellow Brick Road' tour kicked off back in 2018, and even though it was off in 2020 due to COVID, it's still the 3rd highest grossing tour in history.  According to 'Billboard,' the tour has grossed $662.3 million, placing it at #3 on the list. There have been 4.5 million tickets sold to 257 shows. In North America alone, Elton has grossed $133.4 million from 830 million tickets sold. As of today, Ed Sheeran's 'Divide' tour, which ran from 2017-2019, has earned the most money at $776.
