Biohaven prices 25M of stock offering at $10.50/share
seekingalpha.com
science
2022-10-21 07:37:39

Biohaven (NYSE:BHVN) has priced upsized public offering of 25M of its common shares, at a public offering price of $10.50/common share.
Underwriters are granted a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 3.75M common shares.
Offering is expected to close on October 25, 2022.
Biohaven intends to use the net proceeds received from the offering for general corporate purposes.
Earlier, Biohaven stock falls 5% after the bell on 20M share offering.
