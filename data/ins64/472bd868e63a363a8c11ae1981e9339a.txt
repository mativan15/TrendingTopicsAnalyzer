Trail Blazers Tip Off Next Chapter of Rip City History With 2022-23 Home Opener at Moda Center October 21
nba.com
news
2022-10-18 16:38:56

The Trail Blazers will begin their home slate of the 2022-23 regular season on Friday, October 21 vs. Phoenix at Moda Center with tip off scheduled for 7:00 p.m. Tickets to opening night and the remainder of the Trail Blazers season are available now at trailblazers.com/tickets or by calling 844-RIP-CITY. On opening night, fans can expect plenty of giveaways, live music, gameday posters, a first look at the team wearing the brand new 2022-23 Statement Edition jerseys, new food and beverage options and much more.
