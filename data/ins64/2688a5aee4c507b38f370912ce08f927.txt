Big change coming to Woolies and Big W
geelongadvertiser.com.au
news
2022-06-03 00:12:00

Woolworths and Big W will begin permanently phasing out its 15-cent reusable bags across Australia, but the supermarket wants to get rid of all bags eventually.Woolworths has announced it will stop selling its 15-cent reusable plastic shopping bags across Australia.It comes as the major supermarket started phasing out the bags in Western Australia back in March ahead of a statewide ban that is due to come into effect in July.Now Woolworths is making the move to remove the bags from its 1000 stores across Australia, as well as Big W by this time next year.
