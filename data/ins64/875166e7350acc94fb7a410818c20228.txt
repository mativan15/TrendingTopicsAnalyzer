Credit Suisse To Pay $495 Mn In US To Settle Securities Case
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 08:00:33

Subprime mortages were at the heart of the 2008 financial crisis
Credit Suisse said Monday it would pay $495 million to settle a row over mortgage-backed securities dating back to the 2008 financial crisis.Switzerland's second-biggest bank said it had agreed with New Jersey authorities to make the "one-time payment... to fully resolve claims" for compensation, and said it had already provisioned the amount.In the claim filed in 2013, Credit Suisse was criticised for not having provided sufficient information on the risks relating to $10 billion of mortgage-backed securities.
