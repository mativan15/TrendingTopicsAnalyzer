NEYMAR is the first player to headline FIFA 23's Team of the Week, who doesn't play for the English Premier League.
His new card comes with a 90-rating which will make the Paris Saint-Germain forward a popular pick.

Most of the players in this week's pick land in the mid-to-upper 80s, making a number of strong pulls to add to your FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT).
Manchester City took the top spot two weeks in a row, following a Liverpool and Tottenham Hotspurs headliner.
