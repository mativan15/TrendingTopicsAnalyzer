Bulldogs grind up Griswold

2022-10-18 03:59:00

Oct. 17—LEBANON — Leah Comeroski and Avery Volle each scored two goals to lead Lyman Memorial to a 7- 1 Eastern Connecticut Conference inter- division girls soccer victory Saturday over Griswold.

Magda Carpenter added a goal and two assists and Alyssa Judd had a goal and assist for the Bulldogs (10-1-1). Emily McKelvey also scored a goal while Maddie Archer and Becca Ziegler had assists.

Phoebe Carpenter made six saves.

On Friday, Addyson Earl recorded a hat trick and Granby Memorial (6-4-1 overall, 5- 3 league) scored all of its goals in the first half in a 5- 0 North Central Connecticut Conference victory over Coventry.
