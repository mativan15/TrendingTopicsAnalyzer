The stock market has cooled off considerably this year. The S&P 500 is down double digits while many stocks have fallen even further. 
Despite that sell-off, some high-quality stocks still seem relatively expensive. Because of that, they're not screaming bargains yet. They could be if their share prices keep falling, though. Three stocks that would be much better buys if their sell-offs deepen are NextEra Energy (NEE 0.22%), Nucor (NUE -2.22%), and Brookfield Renewable Partners (BEP -0.14%) (BEPC -0.
