 A 65-year-old farmer, protesting outside the residence of chief minister Bhagwant Mann here, died of a suspected cardiac arrest, when he had gone to take bath at a nearby Gurdwara on Friday. The deceased has been identified as Karnail Singh, a resident of Akoi Sahib village. This is the second death of a farmer, during the ongoing protest.
