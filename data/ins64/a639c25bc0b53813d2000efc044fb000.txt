AM Best Affirms Credit Ratings of Tokio Marine Pacific Insurance Limited

October 20, 2022 11:05 AM Eastern Daylight Time HONG KONG--(BUSINESS WIRE)--AM Best has affirmed the Financial Strength Rating of A+ (Superior) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of 'aa-' (Superior) of Tokio Marine Pacific Insurance Limited (TMPI) (Guam). The outlook of these Credit Ratings (ratings) is stable.

The ratings reflect TMPI's balance sheet strength, which AM Best assesses as very strong, as well as its adequate operating performance, neutral business profile and appropriate enterprise risk management.
