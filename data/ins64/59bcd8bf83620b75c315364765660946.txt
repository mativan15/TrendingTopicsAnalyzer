A PLAGUE Tale: Requiem is a new narrative game with story and combat sections broken up into chapters.
Knowing how many chapters you have done, and how many you have left, is a good way to tell just how far though you are.
A Plague Tale: Requiem is out now.Credit: Asobo Studio
The main story is about 12 - 15 hours, with around 20 - 30 hours needed if you are hoping to complete everything.
However, there are a few points in Requiem where you feel the story is wrapping up, only for it to ramp up again.
