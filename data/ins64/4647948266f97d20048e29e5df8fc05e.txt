Variety - TV News Return To SpaceAustralia's Slr Productions has announced production on season two of the award-winning original CGI animated series, 'Space Nova' for ABC Me. The 15 x 22-minute season has received significant production funding from both Screen Australia and the Australian Children's Television Foundation as well as development funding from ABC Me and Create Nsw. 'Space Nova' will be co-produced with Giggle Garage in Malaysia and distributed internationally by Zdf Studios, with Actf representing the series across Australia and New Zealand.
