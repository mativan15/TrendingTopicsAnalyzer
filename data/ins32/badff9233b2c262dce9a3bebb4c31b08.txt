Diwali 2022: Traveling this festive season? Or maybe you are just on the move for the next few weeks. Either way, you may need more data than usual on your mobile plan, along with headroom for a lot of calls and messages ahead of the festivities.
If you're someone who uses one of Airtel, Jio or Vodafone Idea's unlimited calling plans, you're good to go. If not however, here are some value-for-money short term plans that should sort your data, calling and messaging needs over the next few weeks.
Airtel
Airtel offers the Rs 209 plan which offers 1GB per day with unlimited calling and 100 SMS per day for 21 days.
