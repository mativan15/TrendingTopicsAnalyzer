A local truck driver has revealed he spends an eye-watering amount of money on petrol just to keep his freight business alive.Tristan Wernhard, who owns A-A Plus Driver Trainer and operates a fleet of six trucks, said fuel cost him an extra $25,000 per month compared to the start of the year.He also spends about $1000 in tolls each week and told his drivers to try and avoid them.Trucker Tristan Wernhard has revealed he has spent an extra $25,000 per month on petrol to keep his business afloat. NCA NewsWire / Morgan Sette Credit: News Corp AustraliaSpeaking to Nine radio host Jim Wilson, Mr Wernhard said he was almost as breaking point and running his business was almost no longer sustainable because of the costs.
