Could your neighbouring high-rise building double as a battery? Well, scientists think so!A new energy storage concept by researchers wants to turn high-rise buildings in cities around the world into batteries. Researchers at the International Institute for Applied Systems Analysis (IIASA) have devised a new energy storage concept that could turn skyscrapers into batteries. UnsplashTurning buildings into batteriesIn their study published in the journal Energy, IIASA researchers proposed a gravitational-based storage solution that uses lifts and empty apartments in high rises to storage energy.
