Published: 13:29 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 13:29 EDT, 18 October 2022 The words 'Saudi' and 'sportswashing' have become well and truly ingrained into the vocabulary of professional men's golf in the wake of LIV Golf's divisive foray into the sport but the women's game is not without controversy of its own.Last week, in the shadow of the Manhattan skyline the Aramco Team Series held its fourth event of the season at former President Donald Trump's course in the Bronx, where the female professionals were served a big prize purse and even bigger questions.
