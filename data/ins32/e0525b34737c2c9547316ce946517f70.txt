(CNN)When the Department of Education announced massive loan forgiveness for former students of the for-profit Corinthian Colleges system on Wednesday, it was the largest single effort by the Biden administration to help Americans pay down their student debt.The administration's actions so far have largely focused on students who were defrauded by now-shuttered schools, like Corinthian.
