Suspect wanted after break-ins at east Windsor businesses
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-18 14:04:03

Windsor police are asking for the public's help to identify a suspect wanted after two break-ins at businesses on the city's east side. Officers say a man broke into a business in the 11000 block of Tecumseh Rd E. and stole cordless lawn tools on Thursday, Oct. 13, around 2:30 a.m. Later that night, at 4 a.m., police say the same suspect broke into a business in the 3000 block of Seminole Street and stole two lawn mowers and tools. The suspect is described as a white male, 6' 2', and 220 pounds. At the time of the incident, he wore a blue long-sleeve shirt, beige work pants, black work boots, and a black face mask.
