Shift to cloud could result in huge cost savings: Salesforce report
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 15:15:00

Pune: A new report by Salesforce, Trail to Net Zero for India, found that businesses moving to the Cloud could result in cost savings of $2.2 billion in 2022 and $24 billion between 2022-2030. The study was commissioned by Salesforce in collaboration with YouGOv and Access Partnership across over 1,000 business managers in India to assess their readiness to address climate change. 
Arundhati Bhattacharya, CEO & Chairperson, Salesforce India, said, 'The role of technology is paramount and the transformative impact on energy consumption, carbon emissions and waste management will accelerate the journey of businesses to net zero.
