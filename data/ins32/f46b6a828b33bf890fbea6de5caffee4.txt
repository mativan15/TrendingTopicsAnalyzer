American Express: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-21 11:14:25

The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of 11 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $2.42 per share.

The credit card issuer and global payments company posted revenue of $13.56 billion in the period, which also beat Street forecasts. Eleven analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $13.51 billion.

American Express expects full-year earnings to be $9.25 to $9.65 per share.

American Express shares have dropped 13% since the beginning of the year, while the S&P's 500 index has declined 23%.
