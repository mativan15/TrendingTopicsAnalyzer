Naturally, Mark McGowan's never hidden how proud he is of WA's finances.Having inherited a state deeply in the red, he's been able to boast of record surpluses at a time when other states' economies are deeply in deficit — something that's only added to his stratospheric levels of popularity.But what's ahead might be a little harder to swallow.Because after years of bearing the brunt of budget repair, the demands of public sector workers – like nurses and police – for bigger pay rises are growing louder.
