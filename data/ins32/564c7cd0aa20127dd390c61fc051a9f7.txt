Published: 13:50 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 13:50 EDT, 20 October 2022 BATON ROUGE, La. (AP) - It's a new era at LSU. Virtually an entirely new team, for that matter. Not long after Matt McMahon was hired to take over a Tigers program which remains under investigation because of NCAA allegations against former coach Will Wade, every scholarship player on the roster either graduated, left for the NBA draft, or entered the transfer portal. But McMahon managed to fill all 13 scholarships available in time for this season.
