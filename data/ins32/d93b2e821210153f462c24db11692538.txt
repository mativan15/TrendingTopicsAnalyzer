 An Ottawa-area man is dead following a crash on Highway 15 in Leeds County Tuesday night.
According to police, the crash happened at about 6:30 p.m. near Poonamalie Road and involved three vehicles in total.A 52-year-old man was pronounced dead at the scene.Two others were transported by Ornge to a hospital. Police say the occupant of the third vehicle was uninjured.Highway 15 was closed for several hours overnight but has since reopened.
