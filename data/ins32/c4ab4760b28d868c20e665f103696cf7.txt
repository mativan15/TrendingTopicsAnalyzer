Gillette Stadium Awards Janitorial Services Contract to UG2

UG2 has clearly elevated the level of janitorial services at Gillette Stadium, with facilities that are noticeably clean and pristine. BOSTON (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 UG2 today announced that it has been awarded the contract to provide janitorial services at Gillette Stadium, home of the six-time world champion New England Patriots and New England Revolution. The leader in integrated facility services was awarded the engagement based on its innovative solutions and proven ability to deliver a pristine environment with a 5-star level of customer service.
