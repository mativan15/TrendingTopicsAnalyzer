Dublin, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The "Analgesics Market By Type, By Route of Administration: Global Opportunity Analysis and Industry Forecast, 2020-2030" report has been added to ResearchAndMarkets.com's offering.The global analgesics market was valued at $26,654.4 million in 2020, and is projected to reach $50,682.6 million by 2030, registering a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period 2021-2030. Analgesics, also known as painkillers, are used to treat and reduce pain in the body. Typically, they are used when the pain is severe and becomes unbearable for the patient.
