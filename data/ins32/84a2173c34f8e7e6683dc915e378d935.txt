Apprenticeship firm earns MBE for Euan Blair
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 23:01:00

Sir Tony Blair's multimillionaire son Euan has been appointed MBE for services to education.Blair, 38, who spent his teenage years living in Downing Street, is the founder and chief executive of Multiverse, a start-up that provides young people with an alternative to university by matching them with apprenticeships at leading employers.Blair founded the business, previously named WhiteHat, in 2016, and the employers it has worked with include Google, Facebook, BP, Microsoft and Morgan Stanley. It offers events and training to apprentices which it says are intended to 'exceed the best of the university experience'.
