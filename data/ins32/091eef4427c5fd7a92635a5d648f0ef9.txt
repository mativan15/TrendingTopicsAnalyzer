Students graduating this year will enter the workforce in what is one of the strongest job markets in recent years, with employers planning to hire 31.6% more new graduates from the Class of 2022 than they hired from the Class of 2021, according to the National Association of Colleges and Employers Job Outlook 2022 Spring Update survey. In a photo taken before the coronavirus pandemic necessitated wearing masks, social distancing and remote classes and activities, students attend a career and internship fair.
