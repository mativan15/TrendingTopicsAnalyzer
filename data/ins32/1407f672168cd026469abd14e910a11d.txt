Speaking in the corridors of Barcelona's Camp Nou, after a pre-season friendly in August 2019, Arsenal defender Nacho Monreal summarised the squad's excitement following the arrival of Nicolas Pepe at the club.

Pepe had joined from Lille a few days earlier, for a club-record £72 million, and for fans and players alike there was a genuine belief that he would take Arsenal to another level.
