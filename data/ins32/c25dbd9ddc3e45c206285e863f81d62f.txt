katleho Seisa/E+ via Getty Images While we may have hoped that with the end of Covid-related restrictions, 2022 would be a return to economic normalcy, it is now clear that the extreme market volatility that began in 2020 will continue for at least another year. Our strategy composite returned -15.87% net of fees in the first quarter (-15.62% gross) driven primarily by our Russian equity holdings. Excluding our Russian securities, our composite would have had a gain despite the decline in overall equity markets.
