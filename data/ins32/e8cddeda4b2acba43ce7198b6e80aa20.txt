TORONTO, ON / ACCESSWIRE / May 31, 2022 / CCL Industries Inc. (TSX:CCL.A)(TSX:CCL.B)('the Company'), a world leader in specialty label, security and packaging solutions for global corporations, government institutions, small businesses and consumers, announced today that it has acquired another horticultural market specialist for its Avery unit.Floramedia Group B.V. ('Floramedia'), based in Westzaan, in the Netherlands, is the European leader in the horticulture media and consumer communications market, with in-house tag & label creation and production, complemented by sales offices in seven countries.
