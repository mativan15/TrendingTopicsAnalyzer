Uttar Pradesh: Nemo, Avengers, Poppet and Cold Drink crackers new arrivals this Diwali
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-20 15:15:00

PRAYAGRAJ : This year, those who are fond of bursting firecrackers can fulfil their wishes with a wide variety of eco-friendly crackers. Among the new arrivals are special flowerpots (annar) with unique names and functions.Interestingly, this year, the cracker manufacturing firms have also provided QR codes on the main cover so that the customers can themselves check the exact amount of content used to make the cracker, including Bromine Nitrate.According to Mohammad Quadir, a wholesaler of firecrackers, the new arrivals in the market this year have been launched keeping in mind the safety as well as the interest of the kids.
