Rajasthan: Earthen lamp sellers in Jaipur are expecting good business this Diwali

Source: ANI

Earthen lamp sellers are expecting a good business this Diwali. Last two years saw poor business due to COVID 19 pandemic. They are producing earthen lamps in huge quantities this year to meet the rising demand for Diwali this year. The auspicious festival is to be celebrated on October 24.'Work is going good this time. Earlier due to Covid, sales were down but we are hopeful that sales will be good this time," said a seller.
