Bank of America breaks down the 'signs of life' emerging in the struggling M&A space — and lists 23 under-the-radar stocks that could soar as acquisition targets
businessinsider.com
finance
2022-10-18 17:45:49

Higher-than-expected inflation has crushed stocks this year, as central banks aggressively hike rates to rein in rising prices. Core inflation, which removes energy and food costs, notched a 40-year high in September, to a year-over-year rate of 6.6%. As investors wade through the murky waters of a looming global recession, there are "signs of life" in certain market sectors. Traders may want to look to the mergers and acquisitions (M&A) space for their next play, according to a recent Bank of America note.
