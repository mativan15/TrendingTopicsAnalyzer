Energy Storage Canada awards highlight energy storage leaders from across the industry

FRAMINGHAM, Mass., October 17, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Ameresco, Inc., (NYSE: AMRC), a leading cleantech integrator specializing in energy efficiency and renewable energy, today announced that Ameresco Canada is the recipient of the Energy Storage Canada's (ESC) 2022 Landmark Application of Energy Storage Award for its comprehensive microgrid and facility renewal project with the London District Catholic School Board's (LDCSB) John Paul II Catholic Secondary School (JP II).
