Michael Vi/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Shares of Keysight Technologies, Inc. (NYSE:KEYS) have held up relatively well during a period that has been brutal to many technology companies. With its movement toward software from hardware, it has handled supply chain issues well, and it is a secular beneficiary of 5G, defense modernization, and electric and autonomous vehicles. Given it is not focused in AI, it also has less exposure to U.S.-China trade restrictions, though that does remain a risk.
