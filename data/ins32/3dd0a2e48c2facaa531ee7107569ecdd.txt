ATHENS, Greece, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- OceanPal Inc. (NASDAQ: OP) (the 'Company'), a global shipping company specializing in the ownership of vessels, today reported net income of $414 thousand and net loss attributed to common stockholders of $510 thousand for the first quarter of 2022. Time charter revenues for the same period were $3.7 million.

Dividend Declaration

The Company declared a cash dividend on its common stock of $0.01 per share for the first quarter ended March 31, 2022.
