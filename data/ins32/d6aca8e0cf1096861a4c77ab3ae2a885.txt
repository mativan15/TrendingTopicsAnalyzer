AgenciesKharge's public life began as a union leader in his home district of Gulbarga, now Kalaburagi, and he joined the Congress party in 1969 and became the President of the Gulbarga City Congress Committee.Mallikarjun Kharge, hailed by Congress cadres as "Solillada Saradara", a leader who has faced no defeat, rose from humble beginnings as a union leader to become the president of the nation's grand old party. In his political career spanning over five decades, he deftly steered the ministries he held and remained a steadfast Gandhi family loyalist notwithstanding the ebb and flow of politics and power.
