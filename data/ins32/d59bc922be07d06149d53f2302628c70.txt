Also Welcomes Sports & Entertainment Agency, Rubicon Talent, to its Growing Client Roster

WEST CHESTER, Pa., May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- LifeBrand welcomes Austin Ekeler of the Los Angeles Chargers as their newest professional athlete brand ambassador and equity stakeholder and continues to expand its client roster with the addition of Rubicon Talent. Ekeler, a celebrated running back for the Los Angeles Chargers, will hold equity in the tech startup and use his influence to further promote LifeBrand's solutions.
