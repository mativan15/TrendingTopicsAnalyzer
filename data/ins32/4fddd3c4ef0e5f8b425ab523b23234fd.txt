With the new Premier League season already 11 weeks old, pundits are beginning to look back at their predictions and lament their unwise picks - and former Chelsea midfielder Joe Cole is no exceptionJoe Cole has revised his Premier League prediction after the beginning of the seasonJoe Cole now reckons that Arsenal have what it takes to mount a Premier League title challenge - after previously dismissing their chances.
