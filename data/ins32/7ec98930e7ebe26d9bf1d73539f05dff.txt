Tesla Could Adopt Apple AirPlay to Improve its Speaker Systems (Sorry, Still No CarPlay)
gizmodo.com
tech
2022-05-31 18:45:00

Tesla could one day bring Apple technology to the cabin of its electric cars. Before I go any further: CarPlay isn't coming to save you from using Tesla's operating system. Rather, the automaker is discussing ways of improving its already excellent sound system by using AirPlay instead of Bluetooth.Twitter user ShibaDoge proposed last week that Tesla use AirPlay for lossless audio streaming. Tesla CEO Elon Musk responded, stating that he'd discuss the idea (and other improvements) with Tesla's audio engineering team.
