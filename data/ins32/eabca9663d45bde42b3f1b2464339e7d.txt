NOTIFICATION OF TOTAL VOTING RIGHTS AND CAPITAL IN THE COMPANY

WORKINGHAM, UK / ACCESSWIRE / June 1, 2022 / Ferguson PLC (The "Company") In accordance with DTR 5.6.1, the Company hereby notifies the following:

The Company's issued share capital as at May 31, 2022 consisted of 232,171,182 ordinary shares of 10 pence each ("Ordinary Shares"), of which 17,879,291 Ordinary Shares were held in treasury as at the date of this disclosure. The voting rights of treasury shares are automatically suspended.
