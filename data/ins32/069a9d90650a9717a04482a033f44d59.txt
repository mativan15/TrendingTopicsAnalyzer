Deutsche Bank replaces DWS unit chief amid greenwashing probe
businesslive.co.za
news
2022-06-02 14:45:28

Six years ago, Stefan Hoops grabbed the attention of Deutsche Bank management board member Christian Sewing with his cool head and proactive approach during a time of crisis.As the threat of a costly settlement in the US was spooking investors and key clients, the young executive was among Deutsche Bank leaders putting in a barrage of calls to assure them the firm was safe to do business with.Now Sewing, as CEO, is relying on Hoops to help fix another crisis and tidy up the lender's asset management unit as it reels from allegations of overstating its green investment credentials.
