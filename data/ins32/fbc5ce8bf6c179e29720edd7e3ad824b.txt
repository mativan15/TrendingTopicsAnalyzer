New Delhi: Power giant NTPC has come out with its "biodiversity policy 2022" for conservation and restoration of a balanced ecosystem, the power ministry said on Tuesday. "The power major aims to mainstream the concept of biodiversity across its value chain and adopt a precautionary approach for sustainable management of biodiversity in all the decision-making processes to ensure the earth's variety of life in and around the business units of NTPC. The policy also aims to adopt systematic consideration of local threats to biodiversity beyond the company's business activities," the ministry said.
