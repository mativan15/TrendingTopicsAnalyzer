Long ferry queues worry Waiheke businesses as summer tourist season kicks off
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-10-19 19:28:12

Waiheke business owners are worried that long queues for ferries from downtown Auckland will turn off summer visitors, starting with what's expected to be a busy Labour Weekend. Owner-operator of Island Magic Wine Tours Glenn Fowler said customers who had booked with him last Saturday queued for two hours to catch their ferry from the city. 'I have a wine tour company. People normally get the 10 o'clock ferry and go to their first winery at 11am. If they miss that, it has a knock-on effect for the whole day.
