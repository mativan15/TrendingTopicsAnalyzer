FinkAvenue/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Nvidia (NASDAQ:NVDA) and Advanced Micro Devices (NASDAQ:AMD) led the way for semiconductor stocks as the broader tech sector surged in mid-day trading on Thursday, with AMD noting it would hold its investor day next week. Nvidia (NVDA) tacked on more than 6% to $194.50 shortly after 1 p.m. with more than 42 million shares having changed hands. Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) gained nearly 8% to $109.23 after it said on Thursday that it would hold its financial analyst day on June 9.
