Nextdoor Shares Up 12% After Share Repurchase Plan Approval
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 14:09:00

By Kathryn Hardison Nextdoor Holdings Inc. shares were up 12% to $3.14 on Thursday after reporting that it was moving forward with a share repurchase plan. The holding company for a neighborhood network platform said on Wednesday its board approved a $100 million share repurchase plan to take effect through June 20, 2024. Chief Financial Officer Mike Doyle said in prepared remarks that the plan reflects the company's market opportunity.
