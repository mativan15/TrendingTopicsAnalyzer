Members of nationwide criminal network jailed for life over EncroChat murder plot

2022-05-27 12:16:15

Two members of a nationwide criminal network have been jailed for life for an EncroChat murder plot involving a James Bond-style gun.Jurors heard how fixer Paul Fontaine had supplied a 9mm Makarov self-loading pistol used to murder Abdullahi Mahamoud in a bagel shop in Enfield, north London, on March 19 2020.Weeks later, he helped arrange to supply a new Walther PPK handgun to Cardiff career criminal Frankie Sinclair to murder Keiron Hassan, and others in a rival group.In messages on EncroChat, Sinclair referred to the gun – made famous by Sean Connery in the 007 film Dr No – as a 'James Bond ting'.
