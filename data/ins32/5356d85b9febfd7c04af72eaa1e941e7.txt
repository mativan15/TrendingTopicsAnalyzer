It's hard to convince people to change at all, but it's especially difficult to switch to a different web browser. Whether you're team Chrome, Edge, Safari, or one of the other dozens of options, you like what you like. DuckDuckGo has its work cut out for them if they want users to hop aboard its new macOS browser, but they have some clever pro-privacy arguments that might finally make you drop Chrome. The company's Mac browser has been in a closed beta for some time now, but as of today, the beta is open.
