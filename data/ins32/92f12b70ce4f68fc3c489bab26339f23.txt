India-UK trade pact talks moving in right direction: Commerce Secretary
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 08:30:00

Negotiations for the proposed trade pact between India and the UK are moving in the right direction, and both sides are expected to reach an agreement soon, a top government official said on Thursday. Commerce Secretary Sunil Barthwal said negotiators have finalised many things and many aspects are yet to be finalised. "So, we are moving in the right direction...Negotiations are on. We are on track," he told reporters at the sidelines of the CII National Exports Summit. India and the UK are negotiating the pact to boost trade and investments between the countries.
