Union Minister Nitin Gadkari on October 17 attended Phillip Capital Infrastructure meet with key institutional investors in Mumbai. He raised concern about the road safety and stated the measures government is taking in the area. He said, 'Road safety is a big concern...We've taken the decision of 6 airbags in the economic model also. We're trying to change a lot of things...We cannot control two things in India. One is population growth and other is automobile growth.
