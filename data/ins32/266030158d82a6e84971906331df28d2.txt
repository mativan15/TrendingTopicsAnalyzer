We both decided to work together for betterment of people: Kejriwal after meeting Delhi L-G
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 14:15:00

Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal met Lt Governor Vinai Kumar Saxena on Friday and said they both decided to work together for the development of the national capital and the betterment of people in Delhi. This was Kejriwal's first formal meeting with Saxena, who took oath of office on Thursday. Anil Baijal resigned as Delhi L-G on May 18 citing "personal reasons". 
"I extend my best wishes to him for a fruitful tenure. We discussed many issues and decided to work together for the development of Delhi.
