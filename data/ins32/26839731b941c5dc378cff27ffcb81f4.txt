Colorado secretary of state race: Business fees and customer service under debate
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 20:57:00

At a time when the conduct of elections has become a preeminent topic in national politics, it may be easy to forget that business registration and recording is one of the primary roles of the Colorado Secretary of State's office.

And while Democratic Secretary of State Jena Griswold and her Republican challenger, former Jefferson County Clerk and Recorder Pam Anderson, may agree the state's elections are safe, they disagree in several ways about how Griswold is running the office and overseeing business.
