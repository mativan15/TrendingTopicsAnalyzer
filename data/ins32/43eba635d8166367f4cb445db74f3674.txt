By Haley Ott Updated on: October 20, 2022 / 7:39 AM / CBS News U.K. inflation soars as food prices jump U.K. PM Liz Truss contends with soaring inflation, rising food prices 05:57 London — Liz Truss resigned as U.K. Prime Minister on Thursday after a rising chorus of members her own party called for her to step down after support for the premiere collapsed spectacularly not even two months into her tenure. Truss faced a highly uncertain future after a parliamentary vote on Wednesday descended into total chaos on Wednesday night, with members of her Conservative party shouting and swearing at each other, and some claiming they were physically manhandled.
