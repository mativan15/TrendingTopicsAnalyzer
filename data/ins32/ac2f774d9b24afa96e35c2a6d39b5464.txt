Sheryl Sandberg to leave Facebook this fall
aol.com
news
2022-06-01 19:44:14

Sheryl Sandberg, Facebook's chief operating officer who helped transform the company from an upstart social network into a tech behemoth, is stepping down.Sandberg said in a Facebook post on Wednesday that she will be leaving the company this fall.Sandberg joined Facebook in 2008 and helped build the company's online advertising operation into one of the internet's most profitable businesses and expand the company's reach outside the U.S. But her high-profile role alongside co-founder Mark Zuckerberg also made her among the most recognizable figures tied to Facebook's various scandals, including data privacy and misinformation issues that have continued to surround the company.
