Delhi: Pollution certificates not mandatory for refuelling from Oct 25
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-21 18:12:23

A Pollution Under-Control (PUC) certificate will not be mandatory for refuelling vehicles across the Capital from October 25, environment minister Gopal Rai said on Friday, adding that representatives of petroleum and diesel associations informed the government that the move may lead to chaos at petrol pumps.The minister had announced on October 12 that the government will make PUCs mandatory in a move to curb vehicular emissions ahead of winter when air pollution peaks in Delhi.In a press conference, the minister clarified: 'PUC will not be mandatory for refuelling at filling stations from October 25.
