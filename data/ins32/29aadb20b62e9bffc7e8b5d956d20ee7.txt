Published: 11:19 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 11:29 EDT, 27 May 2022 Spanish holiday resorts offer the best value in the world, according to British holidaymakers.In a poll in which they were asked to rate foreign destinations they'd previously visited for value, Spain's Costas on the mainland come first with a 92 per cent rating and Spain's Canary Islands (91 per cent) take the silver medal in the ranking.Almost as many gave good-value approval ratings for Greece (third, 89 per cent), Portugal (joint fourth, 88 per cent) and Turkey (joint fourth, 88 per cent).
