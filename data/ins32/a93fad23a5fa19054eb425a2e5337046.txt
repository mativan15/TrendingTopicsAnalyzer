Preparations are afoot at Duhai Depot in Ghaziabad for the arrival of India's first Regional Rapid Transit System (RRTS) train from Gujarat, an official statement said on Saturday. The first set of RRTS trains was handed over to the National Capital Region (NCRTC) on May 7 at Savli in Gujarat. "First RRTS will soon arrive at Duhai Depot in Ghaziabad. The depot is gearing up for its arrival as tracks have been laid out, and preparations are being made for the testing of the train there," the statement by NCRTC said.
