Kroger (NYSE:KR) is making moves in a merger deal valued near $25B to acquire all of the outstanding shares of Albertsons Companies, Inc. (ACI) for an estimated $34.10 per share. And where Kroger is already the largest supermarket in the nation, technology and the digital landscape have set in motion that it is time for the grocer to evolve, emphasizing an online shopping experience to compete with the likes of Amazon and Walmart's e-commerce.
