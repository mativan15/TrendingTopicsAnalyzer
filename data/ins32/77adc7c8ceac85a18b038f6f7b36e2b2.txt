Weibo Corporation Changes Date to Report First Quarter 2022 Financial Results to June 1, 2022
prnewswire.com
news
2022-05-27 10:00:00

BEIJING, May 27, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Weibo Corporation (NASDAQ: WB and HKEX: 9898), a leading social media for people to create, share and discover content, today announced that it has changed the date to announce its unaudited financial results for the first quarter 2022   from May 30, 2022 to June 1, 2022, due to a public holiday in the United States on May 30, 2022.
Weibo's management team will host a conference call from 7 AM – 8 AM Eastern Time on June 1, 2022 (or 7 PM – 8 PM Beijing Time on June 1, 2022) to present an overview of the Company's financial performance and business operations.
