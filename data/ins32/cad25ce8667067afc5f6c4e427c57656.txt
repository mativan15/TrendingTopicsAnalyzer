 The state of California is home to some of the most picturesque scenery in the USA. Santa Clarita is a unique location set just 35 miles from the LA airport, granting it just the right balance of proximity and remoteness to and from the concrete jungle and bustling life of Los Angeles. The area has a rich history, even richer traditions, and several hot attraction spots for residents and tourists alike. Without further ado, let's take a dive into this amazing city.
