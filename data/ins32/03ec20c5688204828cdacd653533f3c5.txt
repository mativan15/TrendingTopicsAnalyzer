StarSolid StarSolid StarHalf StarOutline ( 2.5 stars ) Does the world need a hero, or someone less afraid to get their hands dirty? That's the (moderately intriguing) question that infuses the otherwise formulaic comic-book movie 'Black Adam' with a measure of … something. Gravitas? Grit? Grandiloquence? Whatever you call it, it does not lie at the heart of the film. What does is the same molten core of rock-'em, sock-'em hokum we've come to expect from almost every other film of this ilk.
