When it comes to horror movies, being immersed in the perspective of the characters on screen can really add to a film's scare factor, and that's something that the found footage style executes masterfully. As the genre's name implies, these horror flicks are often presented as "found" videos that are usually filmed by the characters themselves and then discovered after some kind of horrifying event has occurred.
