bpm: TCS retains 10th spot among global BPM providers in 2021
indiatimes.com
business
2022-06-06 04:45:00

BENGALURU: TCS has retained its 10th position among the top global BPM (business process management) providers in 2021, as per estimates by outsourcing consultancy Everest Group. The ranking is based on a composite score of revenue, which was over $3 billion for TCS, and revenue growth, which grew by 14-16% over 2020. Everest uses the term BPS or business process services to describe the segment. The top 5 BPM players in terms of revenue were ADP, Teleperformance, Accenture, Concentrix and Sitel Group.
