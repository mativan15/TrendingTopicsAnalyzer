Visit our ETF Hub to find out more and to explore our in-depth data and comparison toolsHong Kong and mainland Chinese regulators have announced they finally have come to an agreement for the long-awaited inclusion of exchange traded funds into the Stock Connect programme after years of multiple missed deadlines.Initial eligibility requirements have been outlined, including limiting Hong Kong-listed ETFs to those with at least HK$1.7bn ($217mn) in assets over the past six months, and only including products that track indices that primarily track Hong Kong-listed stocks.
