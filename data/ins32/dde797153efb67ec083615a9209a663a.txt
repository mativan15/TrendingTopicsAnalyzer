New York, NY, Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Zectron Electric Bike is launching on Indiegogo! A dream electric bike that pushes the limits like no other bikes have before, providing a memorable experience for their customers. 60 years ago, Wham-O, a renowned American company with over 75 years of experience in outdoor toys and fun activities, created the Zectron Superball - an innovative toy loved by many. 60 years later, Wham-O's Zectron is spearheading the mobile industry bringing another futuristic product in the form of a dynamic electronic bike.
