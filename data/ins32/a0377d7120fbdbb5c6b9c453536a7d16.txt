 Woodstock, Ont., police have announced multiple road closures across the city due to an ongoing weapons investigation.
On Tuesday, at 9 p.m., police a weapons-related incident was reported in the area of Vansittart Avenue and Devonshire Avenue.Police are conducting an investigation in the area of Vansittart Ave & Devonshire Ave. As a result, the following roads are closed: Oxford Road 17 at Vansittart Ave, Devonshire Ave at Light St, & Vansittart Ave at Vincent St.
