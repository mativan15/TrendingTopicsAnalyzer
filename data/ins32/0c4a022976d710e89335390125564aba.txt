May 31, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time AUSTIN, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--M-Files, a global leader in information management, today announced that the company has been Certified™ by Great Place to Work®. The prestigious award, which is based entirely on what current employees say about their experience working at M-Files, found that 97% of employees believe M-Files is a great place to work – as compared to 57% of employees at a typical U.S.-based company.



M-Files prides itself on having a people-first mindset and strives to integrate employee wellbeing into the fabric of its culture.
