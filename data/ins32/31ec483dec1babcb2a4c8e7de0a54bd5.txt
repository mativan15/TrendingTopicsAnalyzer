Image source, AFPImage caption, India's $42bn drug manufacturing industry is one of the world's largestIn the winter of 2019, a number of children living in India's Jammu region began falling sick with what many thought was a mysterious illness. The children, suffering from cough and cold, had been prescribed a cough syrup by local doctors. Instead of recovering, they fell seriously ill, vomiting, running high fever and kidneys shutting down. By the time the mystery was solved, 11 children, aged between two months and six years, had died.
