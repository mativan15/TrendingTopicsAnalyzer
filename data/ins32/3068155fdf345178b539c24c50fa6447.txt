The Vikings have a new general manager in Kwesi Adofo-Mensah and a new head coach in Kevin O'Connell. In addition to that new leadership, the team is introducing new schemes on both offense and defense in an attempt to jump start the team's performance after two dull-as-dishwater seasons in 2020 and 2021. The new ideas and leadership should have a positive impact, but it all comes down to the performance of the players.
