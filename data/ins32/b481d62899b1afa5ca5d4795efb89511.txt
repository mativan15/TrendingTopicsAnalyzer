Pensions Drama Sparks Call for Rethink of $1.8 Trillion Market
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-15 10:01:49

(Bloomberg) -- Pressure is growing for a sweeping rethink of the UK's pension fund regulation after the sector's near-implosion wreaked havoc across financial markets and helped bring about a humiliating government retreat.Pensions were caught on the hop by rapidly rising gilt rates after the government's mini-budget last month, forcing them into a wave of asset sales. The Bank of England stepped in to contain what it saw as 'fire sale dynamics' -- an emergency measure that's already prompting questions about why a huge but hitherto sleepy corner of the financial sector could spiral in this way.
