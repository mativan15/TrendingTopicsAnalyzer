Axis Bank on Thursday reported a jump of 70% in its standalone net profit to ₹5,329.77 crore in quarter ended September 2022 on healthy core income aided by trimming of bad loans.The country's third largest private sector lender had posted a net profit of ₹3,133.32 crore in the year-earlier quarter.Total income on standalone basis rose to ₹24,180 crore in September quarter of 2022-23 from ₹20,134 crore, Axis Bank said in a regulatory filing.
