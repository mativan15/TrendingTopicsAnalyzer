3 in 10 uninsured Americans with diabetes may ration insulin: Study
go.com
news
2022-10-18 19:13:52

"Universal access to insulin, without cost barriers, is urgently needed."Nearly 30% of Americans living with diabetes who are uninsured may ration their insulin to save money, according to a new study.Researchers at Harvard Medical School, the City University of New York's Hunter College and consumer advocacy organization Public Citizen determined the prevalence of insulin rationing by analyzing the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention's 2021 National Health Interview Survey in the study, published in the Annals of Internal Medicine on Monday.
