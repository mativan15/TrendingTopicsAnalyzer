By Mark Viviano October 19, 2022 / 8:35 PM / CBS Baltimore BALTIMORE -- The Baltimore Ravens are six games into the season and still, the team's roster of players is ever-changing.Players get hurt. Players get healthy. New players are added to the list.Today it's the return of receiver Rashod Bateman. He has been practicing again after missing two games with a foot injury.Also, there was the debut of newly signed receiver DeSean Jackson, a 14-year veteran wearing the number 15.Jackson is 35 years old but he still has enough in the tank to possibly contribute to the team.
