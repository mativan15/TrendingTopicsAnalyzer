Proof of Burn Is Another Way to Validate Crypto
bloomberg.com
news
2022-10-20 20:05:03

'There are other ways to validate cryptocurrencies,' beyond proof of stake and proof of work, says Liz Moran of EarthJustice. Alternatives include proof of burn, which doesn't require miners to solve cryptographic puzzles. She's on Quicktake's Crypto IRL. (Source: Bloomberg)
