Gerrard has won 13 of the 39 matches he has taken charge of as Aston Villa bossAston Villa boss Steven Gerrard says he will not "feel sorry for myself" as he looks to steer the club away from the Premier League relegation zone. Gerrard is under increasing pressure with Villa sitting 16th in the table having won just two games this season.The former Liverpool midfielder was jeered after Villa's defeat by Chelsea on Sunday, but he insists he has "total trust" in himself and his players."The outcome was a defeat but we'll take the positives," said Gerrard.
