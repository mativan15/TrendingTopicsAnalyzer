Gateway Fiber Awarded $10 Million in National Grant to Expand Broadband Internet Coverage in Lincoln and St. Charles Counties

2022-06-02 00:00:00

WRIGHT CITY, Mo. (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Wright City-based Gateway Fiber has been awarded $10 million as part of a $42 million grant to the State of Missouri from the National Telecommunications Information Administration (NTIA) to expand broadband internet coverage in underserved communities. Gateway received the largest share of this funding state-wide. The US Department of Commerce (DOC) announced in late February that Missouri will receive the grant money to expand broadband coverage in several communities where residents do not currently have access to high-speed internet.
