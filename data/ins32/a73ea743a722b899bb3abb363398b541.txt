EQS-News: Nordex SE / Key word(s): Incoming Orders Nordex SE: Landwind Group places 82 MW order in Germany with Nordex Group 19.10.2022 / 07:30 CET/CEST The issuer is solely responsible for the content of this announcement.Hamburg, 19 October 2022. The Nordex Group has received an order for 81.6 MW from the Landwind Group. The Nordex Group is to supply 12 N163/6.X turbines to be distributed among four wind farm sections in eastern Lower Saxony, Germany. The order also includes Premium Service for the turbines covering a period of 20 years.
