Edmonton Launch Party Startups Raise Over $278M USD in Investment Since 2010
globenewswire.com
news
2022-10-17 00:00:00

Edmonton, Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Launch Party is the annual flagship event of Edmonton Startup Week, produced by Edmonton Unlimited, recognizing and showcasing 10 of Edmonton's hottest startups. Since 2010, 115 celebrated companies have launched their businesses and raised more than $278M USD in investment and $22M CDN in grants to grow and scale.   'Innovation is a team game and we're pumped to rally the entire city behind this year's Launch Party honourees,' said Edmonton Unlimited CEO Catherine Warren.
