Dave Armstrong is a playwright and satirist based in Wellington. OPINION/SATIRE: With results finally confirmed for our local body elections, successful candidates are scrambling to thank all those who supported them and set out their plans for the next triennium. It's a busy period, so to save time and money, I have created a form letter for winning mayors and councillors to send out. Simply cross out the options which don't apply. Dear .... I writing to you to thank you for (a) electing me (b) delivering fliers for me (c) destroying my opponents' billboards, in the recent local body elections.
