NEWARK, DE / ACCESSWIRE / October 20, 2022 / As per FMI, the Latin America hand sanitizer market is estimated to reach US$ 709.5 Mn in 2022 and is set to be valued at US$ 1,617.3 Mn by 2032. The market is likely to exhibit steady growth at a CAGR of 8.6% from 2022 to 2032.

Future Market Insights, Inc., Thursday, October 20, 2022, Press release picture

Aloe vera-based natural hand sanitizers are becoming increasingly popular among consumers. These hand sanitizers are simple to make and manufacture as the essential components are widely available in the market and surrounding areas.
