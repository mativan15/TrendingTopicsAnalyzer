‘Gigafactory' firm Britishvolt powers up £200m pilot plant in West Midlands
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-30 23:01:00

The company building Britains first battery 'gigafactory' plans to open a £200 million pilot facility in the West Midlands next year.Britishvolt said that the project was in addition to a factory at Blyth planned for the Northumberland coast that it hopes to be operational by 2024.The government-backed developer of battery cell technologies said that it would work with Prologis, an industrial construction business, on an additional facility that would provide testing and 'validation' as it develops batteries for carmaking customers such as Lotus and Aston Martin.
