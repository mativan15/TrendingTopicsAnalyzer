Popeyes to sell its Cajun turkey for home delivery this Thanksgiving

Published: 10:38 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 10:38 EDT, 20 October 2022 Fast foot restaurant Popeyes will offer home delivery of its pre-cooked Cajun-style turkey for the first time this Thanksgiving, for $95 plus tax. The popular Thanksgiving turkey, which is marinated in Louisiana spices, has been available for purchase from restaurants since 2001 and become a favorite of fans of the New Orleans-style eatery.This year, for the first time, the Turkey can be delivered to your door. But the luxury will come at a price - picking up a turkey from a Popeyes branch will be cheaper, costing almost half the price and starting at $50.
