Industrial robots give China a tech leap

By ZHANG ZHOUXIANG | China Daily | Updated: 2022-10-20 07:28 An employee (left) of Tianjin Allians Technologies Co Ltd, a robot maker, addresses queries on smart robots at a facility in Tianjin in July. [PHOTO/CHINA DAILY] Data show that China manufactured 366,000 industrial robots in 2021, 10 times that in 2015. The prosperity of China's robotic industry was in evidence at the 2022 World Robot Conference in Yizhuang district, Beijing, in September, where domestic robot manufacturers showcased to the world their best products, such as medical robots that can help with surgeries.
