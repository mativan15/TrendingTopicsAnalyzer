Moyo Studio/E+ via Getty Images Introduction 2022 has been a difficult year for investors, particularly long only investors, as fixed income and equity securities have, on balance, fallen materially. It is within this context that I assess how the securities discussed in my 2022 Seeking Alpha articles have performed. Those articles covered the Amplify Transformational Data Sharing ETF (BLOK), Clarus Corporation (CLAR), the Bitwise Crypto Industry Innovators ETF (BITQ), Great Ajax Corporation (AJX) and its publicly traded debt (AJXA), Great Elm Capital Corporation (GECC), and the ProShares UltraShort FTSE Europe ETF (EPV).
