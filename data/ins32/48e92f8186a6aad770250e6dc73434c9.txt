Support will be provided for those affected by the horrific flood disaster across Victoria, as the federal government joins forces with the state government.The governments have prepared the Centre for National Resilience in Mickleham to provide crisis care for those needing accommodation.The Mickleham facility will provide 250 beds from Tuesday next week for people who have lost their homes in the floods.Residents will also receive foot and other support services at the site, as well as remotely.
