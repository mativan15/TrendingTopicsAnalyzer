‘Harsh reality' means Ricciardo's time at McLaren ‘is over', says F1 legend

McLaren must consider terminating Daniel Ricciardo's contract after a torrid start to the season, Jacques Villeneuve has said.The former Formula One world champion believes that the team have almost made their minds up after only one point-scoring race for the Aussie in 2022, leaving him 11th in the championship and well off the pace of teammate Lando Norris.'Daniel Ricciardo's time at McLaren is over,' Villeneuve wrote for Formule1.nl.'CEO Zak Brown is now saying that there are clauses in his contract, and that means that a decision has almost been made.
