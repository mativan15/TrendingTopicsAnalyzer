Collective Artists' Network to scale up creator marketplace with new features
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 10:15:00

AgenciesImage used for representational purpose onCollective Artists Network (earlier Kwan Entertainment) is planning a major upgrade for its organised creator marketplace, Big Bang Social. As a part of the scaling up, new tech features and services will now be made available to meet both creator and brand needs, the company said. The platform will now offer licensed products which brands can subscribe to in line with the upgrade. For the brand's side, this model will enable sustained engagement with a curated list of creators from more than 120 thousand creators on the platform.
