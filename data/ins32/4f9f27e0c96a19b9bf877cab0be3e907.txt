Chelsea's potential lineup vs Aston Villa as Potter left without trio
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-16 04:30:00

Chelsea head coach Graham Potter faces being left without three key players for the trip to Villa Park to face struggling Aston Villa on Sunday. The Blues boss will be forced to change his winning team after being rocked by injuries to key personnel within his squad just as the new boss began to find some momentum after four straight wins.The Blues will be brimming with confidence after showing signs of improvement under Potter, moving up into the top four with their previous victory and topping their Champions League group.
