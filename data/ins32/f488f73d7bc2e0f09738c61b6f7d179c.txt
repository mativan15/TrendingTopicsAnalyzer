Ft. Lauderdale, FL, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The first-ever B2B metaverse conference, Metaverse Spectrum Business Conference & Expo will be held virtually from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. ET on Oct. 25 in the metaverse with 3 unique tracks designed to expand knowledge with insights, best practices and latest trends. The conference will feature a panel discussing, 'Is The Future Of Hip Hop And Music In The Metaverse?,' at 3:30 p.m. ET as part of the Media & Entertainment track, presented by Tech With Soul.
