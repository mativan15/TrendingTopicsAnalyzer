What has happened since the Married At First Sight UK reunion episode?
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-19 17:08:24

The latest installment of Married At First UK has been the most chaotic to date, and honestly, we've loved every minute of it.
Monday's reunion provided us with some pretty juicy revelations as we learned many of this season's couples have already split.
The compelling Channel 4 show sees a group of singletons paired up for a blind first date but there's a twist – this meeting sees them get married.
While some newlyweds did their best to adjust to married life, a fair few ditched the experiment and each other after a string of explosive rows, and teary exchanges.
