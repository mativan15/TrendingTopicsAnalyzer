Hogwarts Legacy release date
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 19:13:41

Fans of the iconic Harry Potter franchise have been waiting years for a game like Hogwarts Legacy to release. The game, which was developed by Avalanche Software, will finally give players the chance to step into Hogwarts as their very own wizard. Those looking forward to this upcoming role-playing game will no doubt want to know when to expect its release date to arrive.The game was originally slated for release back in 2021. However, due to a delay, Hogwarts Legacy was pushed into 2022. Then, later down the line, the upcoming wizarding role-playing game was pushed into 2023.
