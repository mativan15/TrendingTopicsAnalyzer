French Govt Under Pressure On Immigration After Girl's Killing
ibtimes.com
world
2022-10-19 13:21:39

Police have determined that the main suspect had a history of psychiatric disorders
France's government acknowledged Wednesday that it had to "do better" on illegal immigration after it emerged that an Algerian woman charged with the murder of a 12-year-old girl had been ordered to leave the country.The brutalised body of the victim, identified only as "Lola" under French law, was found in a plastic box after going missing in northeast Paris last week.A 24-year-old woman from Algeria was quickly identified as the main suspect and detained.
