Though you probably don't need the reminder, it's been an abysmal year for professional and everyday investors alike. Since hitting their respective all-time highs between mid-November and the first week of January, the iconic Dow Jones Industrial Average (^DJI -1.34%), benchmark S&P 500 (^GSPC -2.37%), and growth-dependent Nasdaq Composite (^IXIC -3.08%) have plunged by as much as 22%, 26%, and 34%, through Oct. 10, 2022, and are firmly in the grips of a bear market.
Regardless of whether you're a tenured or new investor, bear markets can be scary.
