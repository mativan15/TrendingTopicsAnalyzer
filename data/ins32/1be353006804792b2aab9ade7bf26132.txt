Schwab shares rise on record earnings per share
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 13:02:41

Charles Schwab Corp. SCHW, -6.87% stock advanced by 3.3% in premarket trading Monday after the broker said it posted its "strongest quarterly performance in company history" as it topped Wall Street expectations for profit and revenue. The brokerage firm said its third-quarter net income jumped 32% to $2.02 billion, to a record of 99 cents a share, from $1.53 billion, or 74 cents a share, in the year-ago quarter. Adjusted profit increased to $1.10 a share from 84 cents a share. Revenue increased by 20% to $5.
