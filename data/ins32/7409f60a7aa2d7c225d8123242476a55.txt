Gaming industry is winning the battle of VC funding
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 11:51:05

The pandemic waves have transformed the growth curve of the online gaming sector in India and the emerging growth spectrum is shaping up serious business and investment opportunities. According to a report published by All India Gaming Federation (AIGF), the gaming sector in India has attracted investments worth more than $1.6 billion in the first nine months of 2021. What's quite interesting to note here is that the flurry of investment has surpassed the total investment the industry attracted in the last five years.
