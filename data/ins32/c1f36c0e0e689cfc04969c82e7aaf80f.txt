WhatsApp rolling out Avatar feature for profile photos
hindustantimes.com
tech
2022-10-21 07:08:11

WhatsApp is testing Avatar features for Android beta users. It allows users to create a custom avatar for their profile photo.
 WhatsApp is reportedly testing an interesting feature that will let users to get a customised "avatar" for their profile photo. The app is currently rolling out this feature for selected android beta users.As shared by WABetaInfo, the Meta-owned instant messaging app will soon roll out the avatar feature to Android, iOS, and desktop users worldwide. The new Avatar feature is currently available on WhatsApp beta for Android 2.
