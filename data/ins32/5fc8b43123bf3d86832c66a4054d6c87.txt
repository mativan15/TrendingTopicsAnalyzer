Image source, ThE hinduImage caption, Binny was a bowler who troubled the best with his swing and somewhat unorthodox actionRoger Michael Humphrey Binny - many friends like to call him by his full name - is one of the good guys. His most likely elevation as president of the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) is both timely and necessary after years of instability and court cases involving the richest sports governing body in India. The BCCI will announce its formal decision on Wednesday but local media and experts have put him as the frontrunner.
