June 01, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time STAMFORD, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Altus Power, Inc. (NYSE: AMPS) ('Altus Power' or the 'Company'), a leading clean electrification company, today announced that on May 31, 2022 it signed separate, privately negotiated warrant exchange agreements with multiple holders of its public warrants to purchase shares of its Class A common stock (the 'Public Warrants'), which are traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol 'AMPS WS.'



Pursuant to the exchange agreements, the Company will issue an aggregate of 981,113 shares of its Class A common stock in exchange for the surrender and cancellation of an aggregate of 4,087,962 Public Warrants, which were previously issued by the Company as part of its initial public offering in December 2020.
