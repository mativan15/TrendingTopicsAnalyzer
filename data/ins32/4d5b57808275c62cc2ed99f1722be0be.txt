18 October 2022 by Marta Balaga 
Variety - TV News Fifth Season has acquired international distribution rights to Elisa Viihde original series 'The Man Who Died.'The company, formerly known as Endeavor Content, is behind 'The Lost Daughter' or 'Cha Cha Real Smooth.' It also handles global distribution for such hit shows as 'Killing Eve,' 'The Morning Show' or 'Normal People.'Based on the bestselling book by Antti Tuomainen, the six-episode series is led by Jussi Vatanen, who will next be seen in Aki Kaurismäki's upcoming feature 'Dead Leaves.
