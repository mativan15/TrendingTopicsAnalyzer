Since February 2020, so much has changed in the world that one would automatically assume that everything would have changed for investors as well. We have raging global inflation, currency upheavals, falling equity markets, central banks jacking up rates, the western sanctions, China's continuing covid travails, slackening demand and an unpredictable recession in the western countries.
