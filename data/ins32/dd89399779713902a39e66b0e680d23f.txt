June 01, 2022 01:35 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Filip Kaliszan, Founder & CEO of leading physical security technology company Verkada, is a featured guest this week on the Impact Podcast with John Shegerian. The show is hosted by Shegerian, Co-Founder and Executive Chairman of ERI, the nation's leading fully integrated IT and electronics asset disposition provider and cybersecurity-focused hardware destruction company.



Since founding Verkada in 2016, Kaliszan has led the organization's technology growth into five essential security product lines with more than 11,000 customers.
