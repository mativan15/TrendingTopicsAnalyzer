October 17, 2022 02:45 PM Eastern Daylight Time WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Rep. Matt Rosendale (R-Mont.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.



CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Rep. Rosendale has an exemplary lifetime rating of 96 percent, making him a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero.'
