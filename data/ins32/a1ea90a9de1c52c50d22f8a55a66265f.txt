Global undernourishment leaps by 35 per cent in four years as climate crisis worsens

news
2022-10-19 05:32:15

Around 768 million people across 27 countries around the world are at the increasing risk posed by catastrophic ecological threats as the climate crisis worsens, a major international study has warned.Climate-related threats, such as water scarcity, food stress, and natural disasters mean that in 2021 the number of undernourished people around the world rose to 750 million, a rise of 35 per cent since 2017, according to the latest Ecological Threat Report from global think tank the Institute for Economics and Peace.
