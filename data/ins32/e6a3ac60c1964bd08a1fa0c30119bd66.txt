A COUPLE who bought their home for just £1 eight years ago say it's now worth £90k.
Tommy and Carol McMillan paid just a quid to buy a derelict terraced house in Stoke-on-Trent, Staffs.

Now their three-bed home is already worth £79,000 - and they reckon it will top £90,000 when they come to sell up.
