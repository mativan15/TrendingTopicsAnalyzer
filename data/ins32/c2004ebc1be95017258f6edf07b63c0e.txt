Axis Bank hikes loan interest rates by 25 bps; EMIs to increase
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 06:30:00

Axis Bank has increased its marginal cost of funds-based lending rate (MCLR) by 25 basis points (bps) across tenors from October 18, 2022. The marginal cost of lending rate, or MCLR, which is a crucial factor in determining various loan rates, has been raised by up to 25 basis points. According to Axis's website, overnight and one-month MCLR stand at 8.15 percent; three-month MCLR is at 8.25 percent; six-month at 8.30 percent; one-year at 8.35 percent; two-year at 8.45 per cent; and three-year at 8.
