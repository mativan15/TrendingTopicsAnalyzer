(Photo courtesy of the campaign of Dupré Kelly)NEWARK, NJ — There have been 'community boards' in New York City for more than 50 years. And according to Newark Councilman Dupré Kelly, it's time that voters in the Brick City get the same voice as their neighbors in NYC.Earlier this month, Kelly – the rap icon who was elected after a runoff election in June – recorded his inaugural legislative victory when city council approved his first local law.
