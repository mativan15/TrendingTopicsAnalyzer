Online Network Addresses the Urgent Need to Improve the Diagnosis and Treatment of Rare Diseases by Healthcare Professionals SAN DIEGO and MONTGOMERY, Texas, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Rare Expertise, the company founded in 2015 with the mission of shortening the rare disease patient journey for diagnosis and treatment, is pleased to announce the launch of the Rare Medical Network (RMN), a group of 16 specialty-specific rare disease online publications and associated e-newsletters.
