Introduction to Animal Welfare
Animal welfare has become a growing topic in recent years, with individuals of all ages getting involved and trying their best to contribute to the welfare of the animals. Especially in India, a variety of small to large non-governmental organizations (NGOs) have been working tirelessly to raise awareness, support stray animals, and provide them with necessities such as food, shelter, and health care. However, we have not yet arrived at a stage where we can confidently state that we have figured out the necessary solutions to eliminate HUMAN-ANIMAL CONFLICT and Animal cruelty.
