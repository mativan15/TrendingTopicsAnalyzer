Brighton Park Capital Announces Key Senior Hires
businesswire.com
news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

GREENWICH, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Brighton Park Capital ('Brighton Park'), a growth equity investment firm that specializes in partnering with software, healthcare and tech-enabled services businesses, today announced that the firm has made two key senior hires; Calen Angert, who joins the firm as a Partner and Head of the Healthcare Group, and John Cordo, who joins as a Principal specializing in cybersecurity.

'We are delighted to welcome Calen and John to the firm,' said Mark F. Dzialga, Managing Partner at Brighton Park Capital.
