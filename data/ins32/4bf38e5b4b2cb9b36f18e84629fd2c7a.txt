In a turbulent economy, conventional wisdom would call for reducing costs — not accelerating them. However, according to research and consulting company, Gartner, worldwide IT spending will total a whopping $4.6 trillion in 2023, a 5.1% increase from 2022. Also, per Gartner surveys, 69% of CFOs plan to increase spending on digital technologies and CIOs are being directed to accelerate time to value on digital investments.
