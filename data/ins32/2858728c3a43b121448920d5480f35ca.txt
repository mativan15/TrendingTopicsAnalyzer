By Reuters Published: 12:50 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 12:50 EDT, 17 October 2022 Tampa Bay Buccaneers tight end Cameron Brate underwent testing and X-rays on his neck showed no fractures, ESPN reported Monday. Brate, 31, was released from a Pittsburgh hospital late Sunday night following a scary helmet-to-helmet collision. He was carted off the field during the third quarter of the Buccaneers' 20-18 loss to the Pittsburgh Steelers. Brate was unable to get up after taking a hit from Steelers linebacker Myles Jack after a 6-yard reception.
