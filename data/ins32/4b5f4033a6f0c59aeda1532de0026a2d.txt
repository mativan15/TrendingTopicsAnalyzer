You can't get a complete picture of what to expect in this game by looking at last season. These teams split their four games, but each of those games were before James Harden joined the Sixers. He tilts how these teams will look against each other.
However, picking Philadelphia to win isn't simply because of Harden. It's mostly a value play. As the underdog, Philly is getting plus money, and this game could really go either way. And while vibes don't win games, they definitely favor the Sixers right now.
