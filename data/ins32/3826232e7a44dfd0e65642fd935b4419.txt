HT: 17-22 Yellow card: Dane Whale (Wanganui) 52min) The Meads Cup remains with Heartland Championship giant killers South Canterbury. Defending champions South Canterbury defeated Whanganui 47-36 in the Meads Cup final in sun-soaked Pleasant Point on Saturday afternoon, and in doing so recorded their 22nd consecutive official championship victory.
