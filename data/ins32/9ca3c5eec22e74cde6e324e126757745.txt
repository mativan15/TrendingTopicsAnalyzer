By Reuters Published: 13:49 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 13:49 EDT, 19 October 2022 BRUSSELS/KYIV, Oct 19 (Reuters) - The European Parliament on Wednesday awarded the people of Ukraine its annual Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought to honor their fight against Russia's invasion. "They are standing up for what they believe in. Fighting for our values. Protecting democracy, freedom and rule of law. Risking their lives for us," European Parliament President Roberta Metsola said when she announced the winner.
