Mary Berry's easy cupcakes which have a ‘little surprise inside' (Image: BBC)Cupcakes are a perfect sweet treat for the Platinum Jubilee, and look no further than Mary Berry's delicious orange butterfly cakes, or be a little more adventurous and make her lemon meringue cupcakes. Both have easy recipes, with her signature ‘all-in-one' sponge mixture. Mary Berry's 'easy butterfly fairy cakes are filled with orange curd and topped with icing'. They take less than 30 minutes to prepare, and 10 to 30 minutes to cook.
