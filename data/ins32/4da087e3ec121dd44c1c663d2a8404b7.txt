I see a fair amount of news that exposes me to the ideas of people whose business is economics. Here is a recent summary of what I have heard them say about the causes of inflation.

High gasoline prices: Years of underinvestment by the U.S. petroleum industry in production and the war in Ukraine are raising fossil fuel prices. Of course, the Russians and the Saudis, along with the rest of OPEC, just made a deal to keep prices high by cutting production. I found out that Exxon-Mobil's profits for the second quarter of 2022 was $32.
