Published: 19:05 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 19:09 EDT, 20 October 2022 Southern Charm personality Naomie Olindo is facing a lawsuit from her ex-business partner Virginia Cox, who accuses her of fraudulent tactics and breaching a contract they held amid Cox's exit from the fashion line they worked on, L'Abeye.Olindo, 30, was dishonest and manipulative in her maneuvers in the business; misappropriated funds; and sought to sabotage Cox's future career prospects, Cox said in court docs reviewed by Page Six.
