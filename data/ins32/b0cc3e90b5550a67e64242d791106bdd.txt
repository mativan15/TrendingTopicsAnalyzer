Germany reveals massive military expansion

2022-05-31 13:15:00

Berlin to pour 100 billion euro into its military, which would enable it to field the largest NATO army in EuropeGermany's plan to invest a whopping 100 billion euro ($107.35 billion) into its military will allow Berlin to significantly expand its fighting force, Chancellor Olaf Scholz told local media on Monday. This will make the German army the biggest in Europe among NATO members, he said.The pledge, which the Chancellor made in an interview with several domestic newspapers, came after the country's major parliamentary factions agreed, on Sunday night, to a special exemption required to borrow the money and invest it in the military.
