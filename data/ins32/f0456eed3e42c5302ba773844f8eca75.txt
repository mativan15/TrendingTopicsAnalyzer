Several of the world's shrewdest investors, executives, academics, and analysts are sounding the alarm on the global economic outlook. They're warning that countries face a devastating combination of brutal inflation, shrinking output, plunging asset prices, and soaring unemployment.They blame years of debt-fueled buying and borrowing, pandemic disruptions, and Russia's ongoing invasion of Ukraine. They also point the finger at misguided thinking from the central banks and governments in charge of shepherding the global economy.
