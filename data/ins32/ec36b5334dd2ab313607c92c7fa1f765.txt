Largest cryptocurrencies fall as Ripple declines
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 14:00:00

All of the largest cryptocurrencies were down during morning trading on Wednesday, with Ripple XRPUSD, -3.13% seeing the biggest change, falling 3.37% to 45 cents. Cardano ADAUSD, -2.65% shed 2.83% to 35 cents, and Uniswap UNIUSD, -1.95% declined 2.25% to $6.47. Ethereum ETHUSD, -1.48% shed 2.25% to $1,293.21 on Wednesday, while Polkadot DOTUSD, -1.25% shed 1.33% to $6.09 and Dogecoin DOGEUSD, -1.35% declined 1.35% to 6 cents. Bitcoin BTCUSD, -1.19% and Bitcoin Cash BCHUSD, -1.29% fell 1.30% to $19,121.
