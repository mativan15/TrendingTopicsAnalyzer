Pet projects are fetching profit for Irish entrepreneurs

2022-10-15 23:01:00

There's a meme-friendly line from the movie Jaws when the police chief Martin Brody, stunned by the size of the shark he has just seen, tells the captain: 'You're gonna need a bigger boat.'Frances O'Reilly, a Waterford entrepreneur, had a similar experience when she spotted the potential for her new business, DogDry, a maker of drying robes for dogs. She had commissioned a survey from Behaviour & Attitudes to assess the market. It was funded by a feasibility grant from her local enterprise office (LEO).
