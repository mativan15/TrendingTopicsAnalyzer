Suzlon Energy rights issue gets 1.8x bids
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-21 02:15:00

AgenciesThe allotment and listing formalities of the new shares on the BSE & NSE are expected to be completed by the first week of November. Mumbai: The ₹1,200-crore rights issue of Suzlon Energy was subscribed 1.8 times on Thursday, the final day of bidding. The issue was launched on October 11. The issue size totalled 2.4 billion partly paid-up equity shares for cash, at ₹5 per rights share to its eligible equity shareholders in the ratio of 5 rights equity shares for every 21 fully paid-up equity shares held.
