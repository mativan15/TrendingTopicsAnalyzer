MIAMI, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- BNY Mellon Wealth Management named Gonzalo A. Acevedo as senior client strategist. Gonzalo works with ultra-high-net-worth families, business owners, corporate executives, private equity and hedge fund principals and their family offices, as well as foundations and endowments on their comprehensive wealth needs. Gonzalo is based in Miami and reports to Laura Kaplan, market president, South Florida.

Gonzalo A. Acevedo, Senior Client Strategist, BNY Mellon Wealth Management

Gonzalo joins BNY Mellon Wealth Management from Safra National Bank of New York, where he served as a senior vice president.
