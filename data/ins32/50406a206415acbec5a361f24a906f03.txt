Stock Futures Edge Lower With Inflation, Earnings in Focus
wsj.com
news
2022-10-20 10:41:00

U.S. stock futures ticked lower and Treasury yields inched up as investors weighed how aggressively central banks will raise interest rates to moderate inflation and the outlook for corporate earnings.

Futures for the S&P 500 edged down 0.2% Thursday. Contracts for the tech-focused Nasdaq-100 declined 0.5% and futures for the Dow Jones Industrial Average were flat.
