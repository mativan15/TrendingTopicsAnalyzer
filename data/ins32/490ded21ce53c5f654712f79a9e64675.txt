CM Khattar meets envoys of India to 7 countries, showcases Haryana as investment destination
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-18 14:11:19

Seeking to draw investment and explore the possibilities of mutual cooperation in various fields, Haryana Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar and Chief Secretary Sanjeev Kaushal Tuesday separately held meetings with the envoys of India to seven countries and apprised them of the progress the state has made in various fields, including agriculture and industry.
Khattar told the envoys that Haryana has a huge potential for investments and major companies from the country and abroad continue to make investments in the state and set up their units.
