Advance action on bank privatisation underway: DFS secretary

iStockRepresentative ImageAdvance action is underway for privatisation of two public sector banks in pursuance of the announcement made by finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman, Department of Financial Services secretary Sanjay Malhotra said on Monday. In the Union Budget for 2021-22, the government announced its intent to take up the privatisation of two PSBs (Public Sector Banks) in the year and approved a policy of strategic disinvestment of public sector enterprises. "In so far as banking privatisation is concerned, there is already a statement on the floor of the house by the finance minister for making enabling provision.
