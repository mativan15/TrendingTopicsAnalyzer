THE best and worst service stations in Britain have been revealed - is your local on the list?
Motorway services in Britain were scrutinised and ranked by members of the public in a recent survey.
The Hartshead Moor East services on the M62 was ranked the worstCredit: Google
Rugby Motorway services on the M6 was ranked the best in BritainCredit: PA
Transport Focus spoke to over 31,000 visitors across 119 services about the food, toilets, staff, electrical vehicle charging points and whether it had an impact on their mood.
