Published: 21:13 EDT, 14 October 2022 | Updated: 21:13 EDT, 14 October 2022 Kim Kardashian and her ex-husband Kanye West just barely avoided an awkward encounter on Friday when they arrived separately at their oldest daughter North's basketball game in Los Angeles.Although Kim, 41, has previously opted for low-key looks and even gone makeup-free to some of her daughter's games in the past, she was clearly dressed to draw attention this time with her striking casual cool all-black ensemble.The shapewear mogul seemed unbothered by the presence of her 45-year-old rapper ex, following a report that she is no longer taking his calls and has disengaged from his seemingly endless feuding.
