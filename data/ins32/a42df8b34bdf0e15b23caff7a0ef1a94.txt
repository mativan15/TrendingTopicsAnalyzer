Wall St Slides As Solid Jobs Data Supports Rate Hike Bets
ibtimes.com
news
2022-06-03 09:58:04

U.S. stock indexes fell on Friday as a solid jobs report supported the view that the Federal Reserve would continue on its aggressive policy tightening path to cool decades-high inflation, with shares of Apple and Tesla weighing the most.Ten of the 11 major S&P sectors declined, with consumer discretionary losing 2.5% and technology falling 2.2%. The energy sector was an outlier with a gain of 1.2%.The Labor Department's closely watched report showed nonfarm payrolls rose by 390,000 jobs last month and wages grew, while the unemployment rate held steady at 3.
