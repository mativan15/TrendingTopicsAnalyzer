Living up to her promise, Saisha Shinde donated half the money she earned on Kangana Ranaut's reality show, Lock Upp, to an LGBTQ+ organisation.
Published on Jun 02, 2022 02:02 PM IST
 

Saisha Shinde donated money she earned from Kangana Ranaut's reality show, Lock Upp, to an LGBTQ+ organisation. She pledged on the show that she would donate half of her earnings, and now shared that she has fulfilled her promise. She also said she would now make a monthly donation to the organisation.
