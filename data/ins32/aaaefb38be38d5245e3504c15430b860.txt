Rahul Gandhi says Karnataka BJP regime 'anti-SC-ST', alleges it is a 'commission' government
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 10:55:46

BALLARI: Congress leader Rahul Gandhi on Saturday hit out at the BJP-led regime in Karnataka as against the Scheduled castes and tribes and alleged that it is called a "40 per cent commission" government.The BJP government in Karnataka is "anti-SC and ST," and there is a 50 per cent rise in atrocities against these oppressed people, Rahul Gandhi said addressing the Bharat Jodo Yatra here.The BJP-led regime in the state is called a "40 per cent commission" government as any work could be done by paying it, he alleged.
