THE expansion of London's Ultra-Low Emission Zone has seen drivers cough up nearly £100million in less than 12 months. 
And, that figure doesn't include the money collected from the fines people are slapped with when they fail to pay their Ultra-Low Emission Zone (ULEZ) charge.

Late last year the city's ULEZ was extended to cover a much larger area. 
And new figures, analysed by RAC, show between November and June a whopping £93.
