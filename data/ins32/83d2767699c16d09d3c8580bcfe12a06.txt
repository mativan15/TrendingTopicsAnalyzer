Mark Littlewood: It's easy to say the priority has to be growth, but harder to explain why
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 23:00:00

In seeking to explain her priorities at the Conservative Party conference, Liz Truss attempted, not wholly successfully, to channel the spirit of Tony Blair. He said the priority for his government would be 'education, education, education'. For Truss, her key ambition was 'growth, growth, growth'. In her crisis-management press conference on Friday following the sacking of her chancellor and the collapse of his mini-budget, she stuck to the theme that growing the UK's GDP was her guiding principle.
