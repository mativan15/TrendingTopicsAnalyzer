Pandemic Archive Will Collect COVID History In Monmouth County
patch.com
news
2022-10-20 15:10:40

(Shutterstock)MANALAPAN, NJ - A COVID-19 pandemic archive to help preserve related government records for historical purposes is being created by Monmouth County Clerk Christine Giordano Hanlon's archive office.Hanlon is asking municipal and county government officials to submit documents pertaining to the pandemic to be preserved within the County Clerk's Archives, which are based in the headquarters of the Monmouth County Library in Manalapan. "The COVID-19 pandemic is an historic event that will be of great interest to future historians, researchers, scientists, health and public policy experts, as well as government leaders,' said Hanlon.
