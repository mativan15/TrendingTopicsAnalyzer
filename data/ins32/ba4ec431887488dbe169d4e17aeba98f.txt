By Australian Associated Press Published: 01:00 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 01:00 EDT, 19 October 2022 Australian drivers' resistance to buying electric vehicles could be dissipating, with more than half the respondents in a new survey saying they would consider making the switch. The NRMA surveyed the attitudes of more than 1200 NSW and ACT members to buying an electric vehicle and found 57 per cent would consider it.
