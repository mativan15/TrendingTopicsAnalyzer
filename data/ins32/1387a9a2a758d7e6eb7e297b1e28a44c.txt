What started as a simple business idea by a LaSalle, Ont. woman has led to collaboration with a local winery. Her business takes the concept of an outdoor picnic and gives it an upgrade, allowing customers to purchase a high-end picnic at a location of their choosing. But thanks to a recent partnership, Proper Picnics founder Ceana Ussoletti has been able to offer luxury beachside picnic experiences at Sprucewood Shores Estate Winery.
