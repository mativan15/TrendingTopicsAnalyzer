Try as she might to prove her debut film 'Sell/Buy/Date' has no agenda, that's the one place Sarah Jones' performance falters. From the many dynamic characters she plays, it's clear the virtuosic performer and playwright can sell just about anything — except neutrality. A charming onscreen presence whose varied personas bustle with verve, Jones uses the contours of her one-woman show to explore her feelings about the sex industry with a faux-journalistic approach. Though her impressive character work and earnest approach are endearing, the film is a confused jumble of ideas that never comes together.
