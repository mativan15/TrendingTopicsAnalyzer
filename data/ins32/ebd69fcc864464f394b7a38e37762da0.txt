Former MLA and influential leader from western Uttar Pradesh Imran Masood joined the Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP) in Lucknow on Wednesday after quitting the Samajwadi Party (SP). Mr. Masood, who is considered a Muslim face in the Saharanpur region, where the community is in sizeable numbers, has been made the western U.P. coordinator of the BSP by the party's national president, Mayawati.
