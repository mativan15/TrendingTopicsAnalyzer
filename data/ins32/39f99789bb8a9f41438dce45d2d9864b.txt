David Coulthard wants the FIA to put fans first (Image: GETTY)David Coulthard has called for F1's fans to be put first after the explosive fall-out from a controversial season's finale showed no sign of dying down. Reacting to the drama of last weekend's rain-affected race in Japan which ended with Max Verstappen securing his second consecutive world crown, the Scot believes clearer joined-up thinking will help the sport prosper.The conditions in Suzuka dictated events that ended with Red Bull furiously thumbing-through the rule book as they attempted to work out if the Dutchman had won enough points to be crowned champ.
