Musk retracts statement over job cuts, says headcount will increase
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-06-06 01:53:00

San Francisco: Two days after reportedly emailing employees of a 10 per cent job cut at Tesla, CEO Elon Musk on Sunday retracted his statements saying total headcounts will increase.However, Musk also noted that the number of salaried people may not change.An unverified Twitter account by the name Whole Mars Catalog tweeted that headcount in the Tesla company will increase in the coming year."Over the next 12 months Tesla's headcount will increase."To this Musk replied: "Total headcount will increase, but salaried should be fairly flat"A Reuters report said on Friday that Musk has emailed Tesla executives, telling them the electric car-maker needs to pause hiring worldwide and cut its workforce by about 10 per cent.
