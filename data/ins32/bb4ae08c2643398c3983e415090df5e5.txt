FOR as long as we can remember we've lived by strict fashion rules that we haven't wanted to break.
But now a style guru has revealed many of them are long out of date and need to be altered.
These three fashion rules no longer need to be followed, according to one style guruCredit: TikTok/@sistersguidetostyl
Social media star Abby, who runs the popular TikTok account @sisterguidetostyle, shared a video titled: 'Outdated fashion rules you don't need to follow.'
She then went through the top three trends that people tend to think of, including how your bag and shoes no longer need to match.
