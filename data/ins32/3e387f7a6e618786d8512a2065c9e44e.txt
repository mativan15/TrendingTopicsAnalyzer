VANCOUVER, British Columbia, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- DMG Blockchain Solutions Inc. (TSX-V: DMGI) (OTCQB: DMGGF) (FSE: 6AX) ('DMG' or the 'Company'), a vertically integrated blockchain and cryptocurrency technology company, today announced that Sheldon Bennett, Chief Executive Officer and Steven Eliscu, Chief Operating Officer will present a corporate overview at the LD Micro Main Event XV. The conference is being held on October 25-27, 2022 at the Luxe Sunset Boulevard Hotel in Los Angeles.
