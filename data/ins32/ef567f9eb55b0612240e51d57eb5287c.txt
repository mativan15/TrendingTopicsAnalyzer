9 Ways Doctors Let Money Slip Through Their Fingers 'Ultimately, we want doctors to form healthier financial habits, that way, they can remain in private practice, caring for the patients and helping them to achieve better medical outcomes.' FORT WORTH, Texas (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Too many stories of physicians who were dealing with the consequences of financial mistakes inspired an entrepreneur and a physician to write a book showing how doctors can avoid such pitfalls. 9 Ways Doctors Let Money Slip Through Their Fingers is the form Adam Phillips of American Business Systems (ABS) and Vicki Rackner, MD, FACS, chose to express their commitment to helping physicians in private practice thrive.
