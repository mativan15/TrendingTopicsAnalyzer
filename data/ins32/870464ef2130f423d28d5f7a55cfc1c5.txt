May 27, 2022 04:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time MILL VALLEY, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Four Corners Property Trust (NYSE:FCPT), a real estate investment trust primarily engaged in the ownership and acquisition of high-quality, net-leased restaurant and retail properties ('FCPT' or the 'Company'), is pleased to announce the acquisition of a Heartland Dental property for $3.0 million. The property is located in a highly trafficked corridor in Kansas and is occupied under a triple net lease to the corporate entity with approximately eight years of term remaining.
