Published: 01:43 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 01:44 EDT, 27 May 2022 Iris Smit, 27, has been named on the Forbes Asia 30 under 30 listForbes has revealed its 30 under 30 in Asia list - and on it is an Australian beauty entrepreneur who famously declined a $300,000 offer on Shark Tank.Iris Smit, 27, went on to build a $15million dollar beauty empire championing two genius products: The Quick Flick and Beauty Fringe.The young Perth woman told FEMAIL she is honoured to have made the list in what has been a difficult time for businesses in the Asia Pacific region.
