New Business Central Standard Pack Gets SMBs Up to Speed Quickly and Cost-Effectively
NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- SYSCOM GLOBAL SOLUTIONS announced today their new Business Central Standard Pack offering for Microsoft Dynamics 365. Business Central was developed with the speed, resources, and growth potential of Small- to Medium-Sized Businesses (SMBs) in mind, while connecting them with the family of Microsoft business tools and functions. It integrates with Office, updates over the cloud, and boasts robust security.
