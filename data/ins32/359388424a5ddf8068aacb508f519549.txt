How Rishi Sunak became Britain's most powerful tech investor
telegraph.co.uk
news
2022-06-04 09:00:00

One of George Osborne's many jobs since leaving politics has been working as a tech investor at his brother Theo's Silicon Valley firm. Some might say the current chancellor is following in his footsteps despite remaining in Number 11.
Since Boris Johnson's election victory and Rishi Sunak's elevation to the front benches, the Government has become increasingly willing to spend taxpayer money directly on stakes in tech companies. 
Sunak — a former hedge fund manager — has approved billions in public investment in start-ups and cutting edge tech companies.
