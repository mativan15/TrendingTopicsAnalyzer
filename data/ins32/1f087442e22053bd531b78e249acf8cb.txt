The midfielder picked up the blow late on in West Ham's draw against Southampton last weekend, raising fears he would miss several weeks of action with a serious injury.However, Moyes confirmed Paqueta will return sooner than expected after the player named a two-week recovery time, but urged caution nonetheless.'We don't think Lucas Paqueta's injury is as serious as first feared,' he told reporters. 'But the injury is not one that is normal for football players, so we need to treat it accordingly.
