Published: 17:26 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 17:28 EDT, 20 October 2022 Apple is said to have slow production of its new iPhone 14 Plus 'by 40 percent' just two weeks after the September launch due to a lack of demand among consumers.The report from the Information states the Cupertino company told one of its producers in China to stop production altogether, while two others lowered it by 70 percent and 90 percent.The news comes less than one month since Apple halted manufacturing of its iPhone 14 for the same reason, Bloomberg reported in September.
