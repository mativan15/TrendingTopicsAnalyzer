A common mantra in life is that cheap is never normally a good bet for the long term. There is usually a good reason why a particular product or service is cheap. And this philosophy applies in equal measures to VPN services.To the uninitiated looking to understand what all the fuss is about, VPNs are private networks that route your online traffic through secure, remote servers. In other words, if somebody tries to snoop on the encrypted data generated by any browsing that you do with a VPN, then they will be unable to read it.
