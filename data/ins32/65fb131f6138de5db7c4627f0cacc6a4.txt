Selling Sunset is one of Netflix's popular shows that circles around the Los Angeles real estate market and the Oppenheim Group where real estate brokers sell luxurious properties to affluent buyers... plus all the cutthroat drama that comes with the business Jason Oppenheim talked to Ryan Seacrest on-air about the hit show where he has garnered a lot of business as well as media attention from with his very public relationship and breakup with fellow star Chrishell Stause. He also shares his thoughts on marriage, and the new Selling Sunset spinoff based on the Oppenheim Group's OC office called Selling The OC.
