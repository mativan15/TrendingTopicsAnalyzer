Karen SharpBy Karen SharpJun 2, 2022Photo: Getty ImagesA local Colton woman finds a hidden treasure from a free couch she got on craigslist, $36,000 in cash was stashed in one of the cushions. Vicky Umodu was the one who had found the cash, she said she considered keeping her find but she returned it to the family who gave her the couch. "God has been kind to me and my children, they're all alive and well, I have three beautiful grandchildren, so what can I ever ask from God?" She got the couch because she was looking for furniture to fill her new home.
