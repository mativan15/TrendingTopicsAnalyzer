Soldiers embracing the Samoan way of life
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-29 07:00:00

NZ Army soldiers have opened up a free gym for the people in the village they live in. Plans are also under way to open a new primary school in the village In Samoa the village comes first. This was an important lesson a Southlander started to learn and embrace when he and his family settled in the small village of Gataivai, on the island of Savai'i in Samoa. Zane and Lina Langford met as soldiers in 2008, during their time with the New Zealand Army, fell in love and got married. Soon after the move to Samoa, Zane had the idea to open up a gym and make a living for his family with it.
