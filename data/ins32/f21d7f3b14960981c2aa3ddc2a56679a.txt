Jeremy Hunt has announced a string of changes to the mini-budget previously set out by Liz Truss and Kwasi Kwarteng. Here's how the new chancellor's decisions could affect you:Energy bills could rise by £1,000 in six months after the government announced it will review its support for households. Previously the plan was to limit costs for at least two winters.From this month the government limited the cost of energy to about £2,500 for those with average annual use. This will save households about £1,000 a year on average, according to official estimates.
