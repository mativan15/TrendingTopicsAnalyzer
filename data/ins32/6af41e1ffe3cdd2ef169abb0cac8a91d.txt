Russia bets on digital ruble to fend off Western sanctions
euronews.com
news
2022-05-26 12:19:58

A waiver issued by the US Treasury allowing Russia to still transfer payments for its foreign bonds despite Western sanctions expired on Wednesday, leaving Moscow to face an eventual, inevitable default.Russia made its last payment over some dollar-denominated bonds in April, and its last two payments for euro-denominated bonds just a few days ago to avoid defaulting on May 27.As of today, Russia is officially unable to make any payment in US dollars, and it remains blacklisted from the euro market.
