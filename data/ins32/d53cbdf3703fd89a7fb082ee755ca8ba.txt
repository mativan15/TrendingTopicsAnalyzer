HAMILTON, Ohio (PRWEB) June 02, 2022 Steve Mitchell announces the release of his debut publication titled 'Type B' (published by iUniverse), a fictional thriller about how a virus can affect society and how one family tries to survive, because of not only the virus but also how to survive when civilization starts to fall apart. John, a courier for Christ Hospital lab, is given instructions to go pick up two blood specimens and rush them to the hospital for analysis. John discovers information about a deadly blood disease that is fatal to everyone except those who have type B blood and those who have not reached adolescence.
