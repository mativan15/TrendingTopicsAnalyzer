Assam govt deems tea garden workers eligible for 10% compensation

Assam government has decided that tea garden workers (both permanent and temporary) to be eligible for receiving 10% of the compensation amount payable to the tea garden towards acquisition cost of the land. The Assam cabinet has decided on Sunday that in the Interest of Tea Garden Workers, Approval to rationalisation of Zonal valuation and fair compensation of acquisition of tea land.

Rationalisation of Zonal valuation involves, Determination of zonal value for tea land by averaging the sale price of tea land on the basis of registered sale deeds of preceding three years in the district.
