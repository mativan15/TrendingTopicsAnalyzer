Beginning today you can save money on purchases of water and energy efficient items during the Texas Seventh Annual 'Water-Efficient Products Sales Tax Holiday.' The Texas Comptroller's Office provides the following details: 'Texas Comptroller Glenn Hegar is reminding families and businesses that they can save on the purchase of certain water- and energy-efficient products during the state's Water-Efficient Products and ENERGY STAR® sales tax holidays. The holidays, created by the Texas Legislature, both take place Saturday, May 28, through Monday, May 30.
