Rack up demerit points on your licence and you'll lose it. You won't be able to drive.Casinos have a much better system. Rack up the demerits and, even if your licence is suspended, the state will provide you with a driver so you can stay on the road.This week The Star had its licence "suspended". But the doors stay open.After years of scandals, shocks and inquiries that proved them out, the results are in.There appears to be nothing casinos could do that will see them lose their lucrative licences to operate, for even a single hour.
