Home-grown automaker Tata Motors is planning to strengthen its R&D initiatives by hiring fresh staff as well as by upskilling its current employees this fiscal year. The company aims to scale up its capabilities across various business verticals, including the electric vehicle segment. Tata Motors aims to enhance its expertise in the area of battery packs and vehicle architecture as well.
