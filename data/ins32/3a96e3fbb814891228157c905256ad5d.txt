Over the last few years, Island Brands has been making waves across the Southeastern United States beer market with its signature product, Island Coastal Lager. Wrapped in a package designed to resemble the white sandy beaches of Cuba and the Caribbean basin, the beer has quickly established a presence in beer coolers across a wide array of major grocery retailers.
