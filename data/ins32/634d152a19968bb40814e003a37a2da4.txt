Inflation expected to return to double figures
sky.com
news
2022-10-19 01:11:00

New figures are expected to show inflation returned to double figures in September due to still-rising food prices.
The Office for National Statistics will reveal the latest increase in the cost of living for UK households on Wednesday morning.
And economists have predicted it will show Consumer Price Index (CPI) inflation increased to 10% in September, compared with 9.9% the previous month.There is likely to be an increase, despite falls in petrol prices (which dropped by around 4% over the month according to Forex.
