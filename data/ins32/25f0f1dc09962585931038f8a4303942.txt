FTX's Sam Bankman-Fried and founders of 23andMe and Zynga promise to give away most of their money
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-04 11:55:00

A politically active crypto billionaire, the co-founder of a DNA testing company, and the founder of a well-known videogame maker are the latest ultra-wealthy people to publicly promise to give away most of their money. Sam Bankman-Fried, founder of the FTX crypto exchange; Anne Wojcicki, co-founder and CEO of 23andMe ME ; and Mark Pincus, founder and former CEO of Zynga, the maker of FarmVille and other popular games, all signed the Giving Pledge this past week. The pledge was launched in 2010 by Microsoft MSFT co-founder Bill Gates and his then-wife Melinda Gates along with their friend, Berkshire Hathaway BRK CEO Warren Buffett.
