Financial crisis is a massive opportunity
express.co.uk
finance
2022-10-15 06:03:00

This year's stock market crash and the woes afflicting the UK have made investors fearful. It could pay to get greedy instead.Now is probably not the time to panic and dump your investments, unless you urgently need the cash. Investors who do that could be selling at the worst possible time.Brave investors should consider gritting their teeth and buying more shares and bonds instead.That strategy will not be right for everyone, but investing after prices have crashed can be the perfect long-term strategy.
