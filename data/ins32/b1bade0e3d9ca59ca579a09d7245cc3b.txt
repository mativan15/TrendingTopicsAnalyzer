ReutersWith an external public debt of over USD 90 billion, Pakistan is clearly on the brink of an economic disaster.Pakistan is one of the fifty-two countries facing a severe debt crisis and the repayment and servicing of external debt are amongst the most severe issues faced by the country. According to Islam Khabar, borrowing creates a spiral with more borrowing leading to an impending economic crisis. Evidence supports the view that a large portion of the government debt has a negative impact on economic growth potential, and in many cases, the impact gets more pronounced as debt increases.
