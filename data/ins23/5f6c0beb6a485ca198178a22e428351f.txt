Textron Aviation Expands Parts Distribution Operations at Its Headquarters Location to Better Serve Global Customer Base

October 17, 2022 10:15 AM Eastern Daylight Time ORLANDO, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Textron Aviation today announced a significant move to add 180,000 square feet of space to its parts distribution operations to better support customers who own and operate Cessna, Beechcraft and Hawker products. Located at the company's headquarters in Wichita, Kansas, the expansion supports parts availability for the thousands of aircraft operating about the world which were designed and manufactured by Textron Aviation.
