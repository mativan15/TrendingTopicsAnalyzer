TORONTO, ON / ACCESSWIRE / June 2, 2022 / GameSquare Esports Inc. (CSE:GSQ)(OTCQB:GMSQF)(FRA:29Q1) ("GameSquare", or the "Company"), announced today that it will be presenting at the LD Micro Invitational XII being held at the Four Seasons Westlake Village on Tuesday, June 7, 2022, at 4:00 PM PT / 7:00 PM ET. Justin Kenna, CEO of GameSquare, and Kevin Wright, President and Chairman, will be giving a formal presentation and answering questions from investors.
