CCAGW PAC Endorses New York Reps. Chris Jacobs and Nicole Malliotakis

news
2022-10-18 00:00:00

WASHINGTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Today, the Council for Citizens Against Government Waste Political Action Committee (CCAGW PAC) announced its endorsement of Reps. Chris Jacobs (R-N.Y.) and Nicole Malliotakis (R-N.Y.) for re-election to the U.S. House of Representatives.

CCAGW PAC based its endorsements on the candidate's lifetime score in CCAGW's 2021 Congressional Ratings.

Rep. Jacobs has a lifetime rating of 83 percent and Rep. Malliotakis has a lifetime rating of 80 percent, making them both a lifetime 'Taxpayer Hero.
