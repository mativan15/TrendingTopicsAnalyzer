New 'Money Heist Korea — Joint Economic Area' Trailer Readies Top-Class Thieves for the Ultimate Robbery
yahoo.com
news
2022-06-04 21:38:50

Netflix has dropped off the official trailer for Money Heist Korea — Joint Economic Area, the upcoming spinoff of its popular Spanish crime drama series La Casa De Papel (Money Heist).

The 12-episode series will follow a stealthy heist on the Korean Unified Mint in a fictional Joint Economic Area, where 'the Korean Peninsula faces reunification,' according to the streamer. The show will feature an entirely new cast with updated takes on familiar characters, including Professor (Yoo Ji-tae), Berlin (Park Hae-soo), Tokyo (Jun Jong-seo), Moscow (Lee Won-jong), Denver (Kim Ji-hun), Nairobi (Jang Yoon-ju), Rio (Lee Hyun-woo), Helsinki (Kim Ji-hoon) and Oslo (Lee Kyu-ho).
