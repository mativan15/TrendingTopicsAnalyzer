Singapore introduced legislation in Parliament to repeal law criminalising sex between men and to amend the constitution to protect the existing definition of marriageA man and a woman walks with their child in Singapore, 20 October 2022. Singapore introduced legislation in Parliament on 20 October 2022 to repeal a colonial era law criminalising sex between men and to amend the constitution to protect the existing definition of marriage as between a man and woman. Both bills are tabled for their first readings with debate scheduled for next month.
