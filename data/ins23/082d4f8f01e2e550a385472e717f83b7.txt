Published: 03:14 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 03:14 EDT, 4 June 2022 If you think Tim Tams, ciggies and toilet paper are legit work-related deductions in your tax return, be warned: the Australian Taxation Office is ready for you.The ATO will begin scrutinising claims with returns being lodged from July 7 and expects to start paying refunds from July 16.Aussies have a long history of dubious deductions sought at tax time and the ATO is likely to be dusting off its index of illegal and frankly bizarre claims.
