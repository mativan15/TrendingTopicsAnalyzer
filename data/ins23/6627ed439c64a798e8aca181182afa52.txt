May 30, 2022 04:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time VANCOUVER, British Columbia--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Eat Well Investment Group Inc. (CSE: EWG) (US:EWGFF) (FSE: 6BC0) ('Eat Well' or the 'Company') is providing an update with respect to the expected timeline for filing its audited annual financial statements for the year ended November 30, 2021, the related management's discussion and analysis and certificates of its CEO and CFO (collectively, the 'Required Documents') and its previously announced management cease trade order ('MCTO') issued by the Company's principal regulator, the British Columbia Securities Commission (the 'BCSC'), on March 31, 2022.
