Batman's former sidekicks are drafted in to protect Gotham City, in a co-op action role-player that has a lot to live up to.
It's been seven years since Batman: Arkham Knight and while Gotham Knights isn't set in the same continuity, the circumstances are eerily similar. Batman is seemingly killed at the end of Arkham Knight but his death is also the catalyst, by entirely different means, for the plot of Gotham Knights.
