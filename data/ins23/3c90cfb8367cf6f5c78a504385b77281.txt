How global startups tap on early adopter demographic driving the first wave of innovation in India

2022-05-28 14:20:33

India stands as one of the most diverse countries in the world, a subcontinent with an amalgamation of diverse cultures, habits and thoughts. Ever since India welcomed globalization, Indian consumers have been increasingly exposed to global trends that have shaped their sensibilities. As a result, a certain section of the Indian populace has managed to develop a global outlook. This segment of consumers tend to be the early adopters of all kinds of new technologies, almost in sync with the rest of innovation hubs across the world.
