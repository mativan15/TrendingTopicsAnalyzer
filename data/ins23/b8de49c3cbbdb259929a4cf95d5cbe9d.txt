(Photo: Getty Images)Ron Howard knows some of the iconic shows he's been a part of will likely get the reboot treatment. But is there anything that's too sacred to touch? Yahoo Entertainment caught up with the filmmaker at the 2022 Newport Beach Film Festival where Howard said that none of his past projects are off-limits — even Happy Days."I think the things that I care a lot about, there are premises that probably have value today," Howard said.
