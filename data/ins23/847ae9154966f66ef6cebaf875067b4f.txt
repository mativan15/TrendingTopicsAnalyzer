THE family of Dame Deborah James has issued a heartfelt update on her final mission.
Sun writer Deborah has been receiving at-home hospice care since the start of May.
Deborah James has been fund raising for the BowelBabe fund since the start of this monthCredit: supplied
Her family today posted an update on the BowelBabe Fund's Instagram page. Deborah is pictured above with her husband Sebastien, daughter Eloise and son HugoCredit: Refer to Caption
But not one to back down, the 40-year-old has had a rose named after her, launched a fashion collection and even had her very own Lego mini-me sent to her.
