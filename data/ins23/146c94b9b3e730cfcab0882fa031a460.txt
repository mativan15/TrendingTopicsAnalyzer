Job losses as garden furniture firm Screen with Envy goes to the wall
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 16:00:00

A fast-growing home and garden screens designer has ceased trading despite having just embarked on a three-year growth plan backed by Santander.Screen with Envy, based in Aylesbury, had grown its sales almost fourfold in three years to £14.5 million, helping it secure a place on The Sunday Times 100 ranking of Britain's fastest-growing private companies.To help fuel its continued growth, in April Santander agreed to provide it with a £3.75 million funding package.However, the five-year-old company is understood to have found trading challenging in recent months.
