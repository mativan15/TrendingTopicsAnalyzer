This is the stage Harper is meant to be on
mlb.com
news
2022-10-20 14:10:54

Up until now, Bryce Harper had done everything except this. And now that he is here, finally playing games past his Oct. 16 birthday, he is reminding everybody all over again that his full-time job is still being a baseball star -- one of the biggest we have, and for a long time. Somehow, he just turned 30.
Harper was a phenom as a teenager. Eventually he became, along with Manny Machado -- whom he is now going up against in the National League Championship Series -- one of the youngest stars to ever hit the free agent market.
