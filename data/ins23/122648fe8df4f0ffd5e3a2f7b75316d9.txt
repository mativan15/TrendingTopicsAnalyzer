October 17, 2022 05:37 PM Eastern Daylight Time SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Skillz Inc. (NYSE: SKLZ) ('Skillz'), the leading mobile games platform bringing fair and fun competition to players worldwide, today announced that it will release its third quarter 2022 results after the close of the market on Wednesday, November 2, 2022.

Skillz will post an earnings release discussing its third quarter results on its investor relations website at investors.skillz.com. A live overview of the quarter as well as a question and answer (Q&A) conference call and audio webcast with analysts and investors will begin at 5:30pm Eastern Time (ET).
