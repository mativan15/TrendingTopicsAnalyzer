Long-term investor and smallcase portfolio manager Arvind Kothari says a little bit of luck is always helpful and if Diwali Muhurat trading is an opportunity to bring luck in our favour, then why not do it? At the office of his firm Niveshaay, he has put up a board saying: 'God and good luck give extra 5% CAGR returns'.In the last one year, his Green Energy fund has outperformed with nearly 41% return. 'Diwali or no Diwali, the strategy remains really simple. It is to scrub through the business as clean as we can and see if it deems fit for investment.
