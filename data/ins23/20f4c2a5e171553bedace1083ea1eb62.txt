FTSE 100 rises despite UK inflation returning to 40-year high
yahoo.com
economics
2022-10-19 07:49:24

The FTSE 100 opened higher as soaring food prices drive UK inflation back to double-digits. Photo: Reuters/Toby Melville

The FTSE 100 (^FTSE) pushed 0.4% higher at the open in London on Tuesday as UK inflation has returned to double-digits, the latest data from the Office for National Statistics (ONS) shows.

In Paris the CAC 40 (^FCHI) rose 0.4% after the opening bell, while the DAX (^GDAXI) shot up 0.9% in Frankfurt.

According to the ONS consumer prices rose by 10.1% in the 12 months to September.
