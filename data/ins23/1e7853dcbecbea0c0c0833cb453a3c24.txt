JasonDoiy/iStock Unreleased via Getty Images Hewlett Packard Enterprise (NYSE:HPE) shares fell nearly 6% on Thursday after the enterprise IT company posted second-quarter results that were below estimates and lowered its forecast for adjusted 2022 earnings. For the period ending April 30, Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) said it earned an adjusted 44 cents per share on $6.7B in sales, compared to estimates of 45 cents in earnings and $6.8B in sales. Looking ahead to the third-quarter, HP Enterprise (HPE) said it expects adjusted earnings to be between 44 cents and 54 cents per share, compared to estimates of 51 cents per share.
