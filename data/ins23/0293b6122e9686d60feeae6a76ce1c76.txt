Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivan may get a clean chit in surrogacy probe as Tamil Nadu health official says 'no complaint' so far in the matter
indiatimes.com
entertainment
2022-10-15 08:59:21

Nayanthara and Vignesh Shivan recently embraced parenthood as they welcomed twin sons via surrogacy. However, it sparked controversy as the couple became parents within four months of their wedding, which raised several eyebrows, questioning if the couple abided by the laws of surrogacy. However, now it seems the new parents may get a clean chit in the case. The Tamil Nadu government has now shared an update stating that they are taking 'suo moto proceedings' to 'clear off the mystery' behind this episode.
