Published: 04:18 EDT, 29 May 2022 | Updated: 04:18 EDT, 29 May 2022 A young couple who went from rags to riches after launching multimillion-dollar activewear company STAX. have started a new business venture. Co-founder Don Robertson, 33, and wife Matilda, 30, both from Perth, are planning to launch clothing brand Second Left on July 15; a venture that has left their many influencer and celebrity fans delighted. As the stylish couple adore fashion, they wanted to have the opportunity to design everyday attire rather than restricting themselves to activewear.
