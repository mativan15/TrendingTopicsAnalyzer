Abhinav Sinha as new CEO: Sachin Singh to continue as Executive Vice President and Bhushan Tamhankar to lead as COO. These appointments come during significant growth as travel businesses seek new technology amid their pandemic recovery. 
PUNE, India, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- ZentrumHub, established with a vision to be one of the world's first smartest and fastest hotel API, today announced the appointment of Abhinav Sinha as chief executive officer and Bhushan Tamhankar as Chief Operations Officer, effective immediately.
