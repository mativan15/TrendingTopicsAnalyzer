PTIJaipur: Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot speaks at the Invest Rajasthan Summit 2022A new proposal allowing candidates from the reserved category to apply for government jobs even if they do not possess an OBC/MBC/EWS certificate was approved by Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot. The proposal stated that individuals belonging to other backward class (OBC), most backward class (MBC) or economically weaker sections (EWS) can apply for jobs by submitting an affidavit if they do not have a caste certificate for any reason.
