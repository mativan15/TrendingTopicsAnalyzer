Pvt freight terminal: Villagers protest land acquisition bid
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-10-21 06:30:00

Erode: The residents of Thulukkampalayam on Thursday protested the state government's bid to acquire their land for a private freight terminal and warned to take out a series of protests if officials didn't give up the project.Activist B Tamilselvan told TOI that the railway ministry was planning to set up a private freight terminal between Engur and Vijayamangalam railway stations at Thulukkampalayam under the Gati Shakti Multi-modal Cargo Terminal scheme.According to him, the state government is taking steps to acquire a little more than 30 acres for the freight terminal under the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997.
