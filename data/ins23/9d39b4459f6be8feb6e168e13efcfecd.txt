Published: 11:35 EDT, 3 June 2022 | Updated: 11:35 EDT, 3 June 2022 Britain's Got Talent judges revealed their favourites as Simon Cowell confessed that there could be 'two or three' wildcard acts in Sunday's final on Thursday. Speaking with Josie Gibson on This Morning, Simon was joined by Amanda Holden, Alesha Dixon and David Walliams as they discussed the talented hopefuls. The music mogul, 62, divulged that his favourites to win were Maxwell [Thorpe] and Eva [Gale] whilst David, 50, joked that Simon just liked 'making money from them.
