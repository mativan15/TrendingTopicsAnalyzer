What's the Relationship Status of Germany and Russia? It's Complicated
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-20 12:00:01

What is the relationship status of Germany and Russia? It's complicated, and that is an understatement.The two nations famously fought each other to the death before partnering up and seeking friendly relations, only to turn on each other again. Over the past 100 years, there have been several such cycles, including World War I, World War II, and the Cold War. While I was growing up in Berlin during the final years of the Cold War, Germany and Russia were at the beginning of a decades-long warming of relations.
