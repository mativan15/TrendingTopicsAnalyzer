MONTVALE, NJ, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- MICT, Inc. (NASDAQ: MICT) ('MICT') and Tingo, Inc. (OTC Markets: TMNA) ('Tingo' or the 'Company'), today announced that Tingo Mobile PLC ('Tingo Mobile'), which is being wholly acquired under a Merger Agreement with MICT (the 'Business Combination'), has signed a trade agreement with the All Farmers Association of Nigeria (AFAN), the umbrella body of the 56 recognized commodities and agricultural associations in Nigeria.
