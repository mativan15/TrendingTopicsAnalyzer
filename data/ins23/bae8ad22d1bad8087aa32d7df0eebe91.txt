AgenciesNational Road Trip Day is the day when families hit the road and find out the wonders that await them on this journey. Every year on May 27th, National Road Trip Day is observed. First observed in 2019, in the United States, it's celebrated one day before Memorial Day. Road trips are a great way to relax and spend time with your family and friends while travelling long distances. In simple terms, a road trip means a journey or trip on the road, which is often a long one. If you are car and bike frantic, then a road trip can be a choice for you as you could get to view beautiful scenery with a tinge of adventure.
