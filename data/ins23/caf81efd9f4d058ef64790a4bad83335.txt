Sanaby Health Acquisition Plans Liquidation
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 21:39:00

By Kathryn Hardison Sanaby Health Acquisition Corp. I said Monday that it intends to dissolve and liquidate, citing economic uncertainty and market dynamics. The special purpose acquisition company said it was unable to land an initial business combination within the required time period, and its liquidation will be effective at the close of business on Wednesday. It said it will redeem its outstanding shares of Class A common stock that were included in the units issued in its initial public offering.
