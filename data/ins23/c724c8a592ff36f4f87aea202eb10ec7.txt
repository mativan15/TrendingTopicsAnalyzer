Published: 11:30 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 11:33 EDT, 19 October 2022 Arsenal will 'cookie crumble' after Christmas despite their strong start to the season under Mikel Arteta, says former Gunners winger Jermaine Pennant.The north London club sit four points clear at the top of the Premier League after ten games, having claimed nine victories and suffered defeat on just one occasion.Their impressive early form has raised optimism that a title challenge could be on the cards at the Emirates - but Pennant thinks otherwise.
