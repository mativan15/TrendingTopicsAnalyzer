REUTERS/Vladimir SoldatkinMOSCOW, Oct 20 (Reuters) - Russia's central bank said on Thursday that its business climate index slid into negative territory in October, with future expectations becoming markedly less optimistic as production and demand assessments worsened.Businesses' price expectations rose, partly due to a rise in costs, according to a central bank survey of Russian businesses.Russian companies took a hit from unprecedented Western sanctions that followed Russia's move to send tens of thousands of troops into Ukraine on Feb.
