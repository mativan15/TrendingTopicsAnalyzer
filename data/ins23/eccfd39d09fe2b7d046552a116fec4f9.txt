Isro Chief, Rohit Sharma were in 2022 long list but didn't get Padma Awards
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 19:30:00

AgenciesPadma Awards are conferred on the recommendations made by the Padma Awards Committee, which is constituted by the prime minister every year.Indian Space Research Organisation chairman S Somnath, cricket captain Rohit Sharma and former science and technology department secretary Ashutosh Sharma were among 14 persons recommended for Padma awards by a government search panel, but did not make it to the final list announced on the eve of Republic Day 2022. The names were made public as the Union home ministry invited nominations for 2023 Padma awards.
