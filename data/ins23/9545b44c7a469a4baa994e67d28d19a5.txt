Harry should be 'banished' if memoir hurts royals
express.co.uk
news
2022-10-15 21:45:00

Prince Harry should be 'banished' from the Royal Family if his memoir damages the Firm, according to a poll. Carried out between October 12 and 13 with 1,624 respondents, TECHNE UK found almost seven in 10 Brits think the Duke should be banished if his new book contains damaging statements.In the poll, a massive 68 percent of respondents agreed that Harry should be 'banished' if his memoirs have 'damaging statements'.Meanwhile, 22 percent said the Duke should not be banished, and another 10 percent said they don't know.
