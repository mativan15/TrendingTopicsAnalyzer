MILLBROOK, NY / ACCESSWIRE / June 2, 2022 / Bexil Corporation (OTC PINK:BXLC) ("Bexil" or the "Company") today announced the results of its tender offer, which expired at 5:00 p.m. ET on June 1, 2022.

Based on the count by Securities Transfer Corporation, the depositary for the tender offer, Bexil accepted for payment an aggregate of 320 shares at a purchase price of $27.50 per share. The depositary will promptly pay for all the shares accepted for payment in accordance with the terms of the tender offer materials.
