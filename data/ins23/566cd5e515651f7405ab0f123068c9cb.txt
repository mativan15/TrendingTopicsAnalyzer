UP: Government hospital doctor, ward boy booked in gangrape case

(The victim's identity has not been revealed to protect her privacy as per Supreme Court directives on cases related to sexual assault)

LUCKNOW: A government hospital doctor and a ward boy here have been booked for allegedly raping a woman after giving her sedatives, police said on Thursday. The ward boy was arrested on Wednesday, they said.According to the complaint filed by the woman, the incident happened on April 30 when she went to the hospital for some treatment, Deputy Commissioner of Police (North) SM Qasim Abidi.
