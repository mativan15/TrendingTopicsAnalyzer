By Reuters Published: 17:17 EDT, 27 May 2022 | Updated: 17:17 EDT, 27 May 2022 The Cleveland Browns and franchise-tagged tight end David Njoku have agreed on a four-year, $56.75 million contract extension, NFL Network reported Friday. Under the agreement, Njoku will receive $28 million in guaranteed money at signing, per the report. With an annual average value of nearly $14.2 million, he'll be among the top five in pay for the position. Njoku, 25, would have earned around $10.9 million in 2022 under the franchise tag.
