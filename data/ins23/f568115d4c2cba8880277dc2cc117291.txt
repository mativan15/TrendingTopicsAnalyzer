By Tony M. CentenoOctober 21, 2022Marquise Jackson wants to have a real sit down with his dad after the rapper dismissed his recent request for quality time. In a lengthy message he posted to Instagram on Thursday, October 20, 50 Cent's eldest son reacted to the comments his father made while he was on The Breakfast Club earlier this week. He started off the message by claiming his pops blocked him on social media years ago and that he has no real phone number for him. He explained that making viral posts appears to be the only way to reach Fif.
