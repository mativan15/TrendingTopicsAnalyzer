Appoints Dean Vocisano as the Country Manager for ANZ to expand rapidly across the region

WELLINGTON, New Zealand, October 18, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pixis, the California-headquartered codeless AI infrastructure company, today announced the onboarding of Dean Vocisano as the Country Manager of Australia & New Zealand citing its expansion into the market. Currently, the Series C funded company provides codeless AI technology for demand generation and marketing optimization to over 200 businesses across the American, MENA and APAC regions.
