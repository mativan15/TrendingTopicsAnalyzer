Published: 20:30 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 20:30 EDT, 16 October 2022 The celebration on opening night was special for the Colorado Avalanche, but they found out 24 hours later that winning a title means getting every opponent's best game. Colorado won convincingly in its season opener, which included a banner-raising event and the debut of goaltender Alexandar Georgiev, but then host Calgary took it to the defending champs the next night. The Avalanche have had a few days to brush off that 5-3 loss to the Flames and next will take on Central Division rival Minnesota.
