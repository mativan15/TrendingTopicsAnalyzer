Image source, UK ParliamentImage caption, Daniel Kawczynski said the North West Relief Road is "extremely important" for ShrewsburyThe government is being asked for another £30m to fund a ring road.About £54m was promised for the North West Relief Road around Shrewsbury in 2019, but MP Daniel Kawczynski said costs have risen since then.The total cost of the scheme had been £80m, but in recent months Shropshire Council admitted costs would be higher than first anticipated.But the authority said it does not yet have a final estimate for the cost of the road.
