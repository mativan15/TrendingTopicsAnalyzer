Jen Shah revealed that her lawyer advised her to stop getting botox injections ahead of her fraud trial on Wednesday's episode of The Real Housewives Of Salt Lake City.

The 49-year-old reality star during a group trip to Arizona discussed her looming trial while still maintaining her innocence.

'My lead trial attorney Priya [Chaudhry] said, 'You know, Jen, I'm going to need you to not get botox'',' Jen told Meredith Marks, 50; Lisa Barlow, 47, and Heather Gay, 40.

'I'm like what?,' Jen said as the women looked on incredulously.
