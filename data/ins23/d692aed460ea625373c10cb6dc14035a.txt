In Her Own Words: For Verizon's Rose Stuckey Kirk, equity is a way of life
bizjournals.com

2022-10-17 13:20:00

The emphasis on equity throughout our workplaces has been more pronounced since George Floyd's death, but Rose Stuckey Kirk has been practicing what she preaches since she was a child. She shares her learning with all of us.

"Whether during college, in my first job as a journalist, or today as the Chief Corporate Social Responsibility Officer at Verizon, one core belief has held true: the importance of education in helping to create a more unified and equitable society.

When I think about education, deep inequities still exist, including in the access and application of digital tools and resources.
