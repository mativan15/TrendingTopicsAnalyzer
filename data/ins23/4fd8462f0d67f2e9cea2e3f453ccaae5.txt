In the dusty, northern-most sheikhdom of the United Arab Emirates, where labourers cycle by rustic tea shops, one of the world's largest yachts sits in a quiet port — so far avoiding the fate of other luxury vessels linked to sanctioned Russian oligarchs.A 118m superyacht belonging to Russian billionaire Andrey Melnichenko is siting in a UAE waterway to avoid seizureLuxury boats belonging to Russian oligarchs have been seized across the world due to sanctionsThe UAE has offered a refuge for mega-wealthy Russians since the invasion of UkraineThe display of lavish wealth is startling in one of the UAE's poorest emirates, a 90-minute drive from the illuminated high-rises of Dubai.
