2023 Mercedes-Benz GLC Has Mild-Hybrid Power, S-Class Tech
cnet.com
news
2022-06-01 15:00:15

Mercedes-Benz unveiled the new generation of its compact GLC SUV.
It features a standard hybrid powertrain and lots of new tech
It will be followed by multiple AMG variants and a plug-in hybrid.

Mercedes-Benz is wasting no time updating its lineup, following up the recently redesigned C-Class sedan with the new-generation 2023 GLC SUV that has evolutionary styling but major updates on the powertrain and tech fronts. Like the C-Class the new GLC rides on Mercedes' MRA II architecture that also underpins the S-Class and most other upcoming gas-powered models.
