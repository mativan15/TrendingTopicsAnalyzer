What does the future hold for Unilever as Peltz steps in?
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-31 23:01:00

Nelson Peltz's first breakfast meeting with Unilever's Alan Jope was about ice cream, and it's likely to remain a topic of conversation between the two men.Last September, the billionaire founder of Trian Fund Management met with Jope over breakfast at Claridge's in London to tell him in no uncertain terms that its Ben & Jerry's brand was unqualified to be making political statements about business in occupied Palestinian territories. Peltz's swift appointment to Unilever's board has now got the City questioning the future of its ice cream division.
