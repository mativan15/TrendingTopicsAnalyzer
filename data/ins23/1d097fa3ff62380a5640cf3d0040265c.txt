Why Snap Stock Got Crushed Early Friday

2022-10-21 16:15:54

Shares of Snap (SNAP -30.12%) cratered on Friday, falling as much as 32.1%. At 11:00 a.m. ET, the stock was still down 31.4%.
The catalyst that sent the social media company plummeting was disappointing results caused by a slump in its ad tech business. So what
For the third quarter, the Snapchat parent reported revenue of $1.1 billion, up 6% year over year, while its net loss of $360 million plunged 400%. This resulted in a loss of share of $0.22, far worse than its loss of $0.05 in the prior-year quarter.
