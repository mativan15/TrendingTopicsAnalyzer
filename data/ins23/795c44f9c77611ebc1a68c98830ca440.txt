City break meets beachside haven at Sofitel Dubai The Palm

2022-10-21 11:16:00

You don't need to fly long-haul to find winter sun and seclusion. In just seven hours from the UK, you can immerse yourself in luxury in sun-filled Dubai, and without the dreaded jetlag. But deciding where to base yourself in this mega-city can be overwhelming, especially if you're looking for a little coastal calm.
Luxury lovers, gourmands and beach babes all adore Palm Jumeirah Island, Dubai's famous man-made archipelago. Attached to the mainland, and just a 30-minute drive from Dubai International Airport, the island has become the Emirate's ultimate beachside getaway.
