COVID-19 stimulus money served as a lifeline for Americans during the heart of the pandemic. But not everyone received all of the payments that were due.The IRS is now ramping up its outreach efforts to reach the millions of people who didn't get their funds deposited into their bank accounts. Those who are missing payments still have time to act, but there is a November deadline that is fast approaching.IRS letters are coming in the mail to these individualsIn early October of this year, the IRS initiated the process of sending letters to people who they believe missed out on COVID-19 payments they were owed.
