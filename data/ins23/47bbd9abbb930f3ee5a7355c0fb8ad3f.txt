Rockefeller Capital Management Welcomes The Mendoza Group of San Jose, CA, to its Rockefeller Global Family Office

May 31, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Rockefeller Capital Management has announced that The Mendoza Group, a six-member team led by the respected father-son team of David and Matthew Mendoza, both Managing Directors and Private Wealth Advisors, has joined the firm's Rockefeller Global Family Office (RGFO). Based in the Northern California's Bay Area, the group is located in San Jose and becomes part of the Pacific Northwest Division, headed by Brian Riley.



'With more than four decades of experience, David Mendoza has earned the trust of business owners, executives, professionals and multiple generations of his clients' families by guiding them expertly through changing economic conditions and market cycles,' said Christopher Dupuy, RGFO Co-Head and President of Private Wealth Management, and Michael Outlaw, RGFO National Field Director.
