By Reuters Published: 02:45 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 04:15 EDT, 17 October 2022 By Sinchita Mitra Oct 17 (Reuters) - Hargreaves Lansdown said on Monday its Chief Executive Officer Chris Hill would step down after more than five years in the role. The British investment platform and digital wealth manager also issued a trading update two days ahead of schedule and reported assets under administration of 122.7 billion pounds ($138.41 billion) as at Sept. 30, down from 123.8 billion pounds at the end of June.
