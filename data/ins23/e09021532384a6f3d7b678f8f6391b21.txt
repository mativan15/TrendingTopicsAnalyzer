FORT LAUDERDALE, Fla., May 30, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- After two years of virtual events, MWR Life held its annual ELEVATION 2022 Conference in Barcelona, Spain, at the International Barcelona Convention Center on May 6-7, 2022. Lifestyle Ambassadors from around the world joined CEO and Founder Yoni Ashurov, their Corporate Team, and Top Leaders for two days of high-level training, inspiring recognition, and special education on new business tools and techniques. ELEVATION 2022 was a mind-blowing experience for all who attended.
