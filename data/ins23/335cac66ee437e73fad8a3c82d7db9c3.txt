Health insurer Medibank enters trading halt after purported cyber attack
theguardian.com
news
2022-10-19 08:49:55

Private health insurer Medibank has entered a trading halt after telling customers it had received messages from a group claiming to have accessed the data of its customers in a cyber-attack.In a statement to the Australian stock exchange on Wednesday, the company said it had received messages from a group that wished to negotiate regarding the alleged removal of customer data.'I apologise and understand this latest distressing update will concern our customers,' Medibank's chief executive, David Koczkar, said.
