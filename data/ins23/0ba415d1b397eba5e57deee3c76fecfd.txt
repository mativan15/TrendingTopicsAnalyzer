THE WOODLANDS, Texas, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Newpark Resources, Inc. (NYSE: NR) ("Newpark" or the "Company") today announced that it has entered into a definitive agreement to sell substantially all the long-lived assets, inventory and operations of its Excalibar mineral grinding business ("Excalibar") to Cimbar Resources, INC. ("Cimbar"), a mineral-based additive solutions company based in Chatsworth, GA.

As of September 30, 2022, Excalibar had approximately $65 million of net capital employed.
