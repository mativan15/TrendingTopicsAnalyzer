(AP Photo/Mark Humphrey)Popular country music star Wynonna Judd who was part of a singing duo with her mother, Naomi Judd opened up about the death of her mother, a month after her tragic demise. Naomi Judd's death at the age of 76 was announced on the 30th of April. In a statement posted on Instagram, Ashley Judd wrote that she and her sister Wynonna 'lost their beautiful mother to the disease of mental illness.
