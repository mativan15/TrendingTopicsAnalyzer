Social Security Payments in October: When Is Your Money Coming?
cnet.com
news
2022-10-21 12:15:02

All but one of the Social Security payments for October have been sent to beneficiaries. And while you won't see a benefits increase on your check this month or for the rest of 2022, you can expect a higher amount on your checks starting in 2023. Those who receive Supplemental Security Income will see an increase starting in December. We'll explain why below and how the timing of Social Security payments works.Also in December, keep an eye on your mailbox for a letter about your Social Security benefits increase for 2023.
