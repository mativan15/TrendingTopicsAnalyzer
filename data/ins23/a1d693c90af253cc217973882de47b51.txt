People now homeless tops 10,000 for first time since pandemic
irishtimes.com
economics
2022-05-27 15:54:30

The number of people in homeless accommodation has surpassed 10,000 for the first time since the pandemic.Figures published on Friday by the Department of Housing show there were 10,049 people, including 2,944 children, homeless during the week of April 18th to 24th. The figures represent an increase of 224 people since March.In February 2020, the last time numbers were so high, there were 10,198 including 3,584 children homeless.The number of homeless families has gone up by 70 since March, with 55 of these in Dublin.
