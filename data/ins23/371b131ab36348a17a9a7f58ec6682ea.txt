It was a stressful situation that brought with it all sorts of administrative faff, and a real feeling of anger that someone had tried to dupe me for their own illegal financial gain. The con was, thankfully, unsuccessful.But not everyone ends up being as lucky as I was.The threat from cyber criminals attempting to steal away your hard-earned cash is a very real one. Figures show 1.6million people and businesses in the UK were victims of cyber crime over the last year.So for me, in my role as the lead government minister for cyber security, it is people's personal stories of financial ruin that sit within these numbers.
