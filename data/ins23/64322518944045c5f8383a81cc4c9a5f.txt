The global acrylonitrile butadiene styrene (ABS) market size was valued at USD 31.93 billion in 2021 and is projected to reach USD 57.24 billion by 2030 at a CAGR of 6.7% from 2022 to 2030. China is expected to dominate the global market, led by its strong automobile and home appliance industries.

New York, United States, Oct. 20, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Acrylonitrile butadiene styrene resin is considered the best polymer of the styrenic family and is used as an engineering plastic in several industries around the globe.
