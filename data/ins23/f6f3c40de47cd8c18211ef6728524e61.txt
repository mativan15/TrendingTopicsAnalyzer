With Candy Prices Up 13%, Trick-Or-Treaters Get An Economics Lesson
forbes.com
news
2022-10-18 21:15:02

Weighing a Snickers bar in my officeWilly Shih
What we will pay for Halloween candy is up 13.1% over last year, the biggest jump in candy prices ever recorded. While people who buy the candy for trick-or-treaters will no doubt pay the higher prices, the impact most trick-or-treaters will likely notice is the smaller size of chocolate bars in their bags. This can be an early lesson for them on how consumer product companies handle inflation. The problem of course is that the cost of the raw materials used for making candy have gone up.
