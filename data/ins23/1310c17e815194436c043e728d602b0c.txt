J&K election likely next March: Karan Singh
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 19:15:00

Srinagar: Senior Congress leader Karan Singh on Monday said that the assembly elections in Jammu and Kashmir should be held immediately as a democracy cannot function without elections. Speaking to reporters on the sidelines of the AICC presidential voting at the party's Srinagar headquarters, Singh said all parties in the region have been demanding the immediate conduct of elections here. 
"We have been hearing that the elections will be conducted in March next year. But there is no official confirmation yet.
