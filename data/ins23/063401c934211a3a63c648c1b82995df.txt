What the average UK house price was at the Queen's coronation and the figure now

2022-06-01 17:08:39

During the Queen's record-breaking reign the average house price faced by Brits buying their home has increased from less than £2,000 to 2008's highest figure of £300,000The Queen came to the throne in 1952 The average price of a home when The Queen ascended the throne in 1952 was just £1,891. At the time around 32% of people owned their own home - compared to 65% today. At under £2,000 - £62,000 in today's money- the prices sound miniscule compared to today's prices where the standard cost of a home today is £269,914, according to Nationwide.
