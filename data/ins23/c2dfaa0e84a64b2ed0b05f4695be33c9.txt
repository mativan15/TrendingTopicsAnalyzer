Photo: Getty ImagesA lot of us are heading out of town for Memorial Day weekend, and gas prices are sky high. So here are a few tips that might help you save a little money . . . 1. Fuel up today. Gas prices are supposed to peak tomorrow. So you might save a couple bucks if you fill up now. Plus, you don't want to waste gas AND time sitting in line at the pump while everyone's leaving town. 2. Use an app like GasBuddy to find the cheapest gas stations. The ones right next to highways tend to be more expensive.
