Published: 11:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 | Updated: 11:10 EDT, 19 October 2022 Notre Dame coach Mike Brey was out recruiting during the Peach Jam summer tournament when someone cornered him with a question. "They said, `Hey Coach, do you think the two new guys at Duke and Carolina are going to do well?´" Brey recalled. "I said, `I hope not.´ But I think they're pretty good." Rivals North Carolina and Duke have long grabbed the spotlight in the Atlantic Coast Conference, and that continues in a time of drastic change for both programs.
