New Chancellor Jeremy Hunt faced questions about how long he believed Prime Minister Liz Truss would last.  The new Chancellor of the Exchequer told Martha Kearney that Ms Truss will be judged at the next general election in 2025.  He also claimed that what the British public wanted was stability in the Government.
