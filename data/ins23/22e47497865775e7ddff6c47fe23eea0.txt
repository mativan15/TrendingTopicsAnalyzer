Elon Musk Calling Tesla Workers Back To Work
iheart.com
news
2022-06-02 13:30:00

Austin, TX) -- Elon Musk is telling employees of his Austin-based Tesla plant to return to the office or seek opportunities elsewhere. Other tech companies are still allowing employees to work remotely, but the electric carmaker is requiring its workers to get back to the office. Musk, the CEO of Tesla, sent an email to the company's executive team saying all employees must spend at least 40-hours a week in the office or quit.
