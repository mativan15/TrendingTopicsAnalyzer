Middle East's MBC Strikes Wide-Ranging Deal With Turkey's Medyapim & Ay Yapim; ‘Doctor Foster' Remake Deal With The BBC

	Leading Middle Eastern media firm MBC Group has struck wide-ranging content agreements with Turkish production companies Medyapim and Ay Yapim, and is remaking British drama following a deal with BBC Studios. The five-year partnerships – unveiled today at Mipcom Cannes – will see Turkish content offered exclusively on MBC channels and streaming service Shahid through first-look and volume deal agreements, along with original Arabic-language originals produced for the MENA market.
