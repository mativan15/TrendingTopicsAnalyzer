Entegrus Announces Sale of Fibre Optic Business to London's Start.ca Effective July 1, 2022

2022-06-02 13:30:00

ST. THOMAS, ON, June 2, 2022 /CNW/ - Entegrus Services Inc., operating as Entegrus Fibre located in St. Thomas Ontario, announced today that it has entered into an agreement to sell Entegrus Fibre to Start.ca, headquartered in London, ON. Start.ca will take over Fibre operations effective July 1, 2022.

Entegrus Services Inc. Logo (CNW Group/Start.ca)

"We are confident the team at Start.ca will be able to continue to provide the exceptional service that our customers expect," stated Jim Hogan, President & CEO of Entegrus.
