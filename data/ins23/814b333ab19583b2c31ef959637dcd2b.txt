Soccer Football - Premier League - Liverpool v Manchester City - Anfield, Liverpool, Britain - October 16, 2022 Liverpool manager Juergen Klopp remonstrates with the assistant referee REUTERS/Phil NobleOct 18 (Reuters) - Liverpool manager Juergen Klopp has been charged with improper conduct following his outburst at the assistant referee during his side's 1-0 Premier League win against Manchester City at the weekend, the Football Association (FA) said on Tuesday.Klopp was sent to the stands for confronting the official after Liverpool's Mohamed Salah was denied a free kick late in the second half at Anfield.
