Rivian stock falls after only bearish analyst starts coverage, sees more than 20% downside
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-02 12:23:01

Shares of Rivian Automotive Inc. RIVN, -3.66% dropped 0.8% in premaket trading Thursday, after the lone Wall Street bear started coverage of the electric truck and SUV maker by recommending investors sell. Analyst Michael Shlisky at D.A. Davidson initiated Rivian at underperform with a stock price target of $24, which implies about 21% downside from Wednesday's closing price of $30.25. Shlisky is the only one of the 18 analysts surveyed by FactSet to be bearish on Rivian. He said that while Rivian's "high-performance and feature-rich vehicles" may be able to justify their premium pricing, near-term challenges and growing pains are of concern.
