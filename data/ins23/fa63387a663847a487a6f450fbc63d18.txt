RT.com 21st October 2022, 05:14 GMT+11Dr. Akjemal Magtymova is accused of corruption, staffers told the agencyThe head of the World Health Organization's office in Syria, Dr. Akjemal Magtymova, is facing "one of the biggest internal probes" in the history of the UN health body, as the Turkmenistan national stands accused of misuse of funds and abuse, AP reported on Thursday.WHO has looked through more than a hundred documents in which its staffers in the country allegedly described Magtymova's suspected wrongdoings to the investigators.
