The hunt for a man who sexually assaulted young children in the 1980s in Melbourne has ramped up, with police now tabling a $500,000 reward for more information.Detectives believe a series of sexual assaults in the city's inner north between 1982 and 1988 – when children as young as three years old were set upon – are linked, with incidents occurring in high-rise flats from Richmond to Fitzroy.Victoria Police is now offering the monetary reward in a plea for public information, with the identity of the offender remaining unknown.
