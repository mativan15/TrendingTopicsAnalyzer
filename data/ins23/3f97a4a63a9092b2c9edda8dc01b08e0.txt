VANCOUVER, British Columbia, May 31, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Rover Metals Corp. NWT Infrastructure InitiativesThe Government of Canada has moved one step closer to funding the expansion of the Taltson Hydro dam into the city of Yellowknife, NWT. In a recent interview with the CBC, the federal infrastructure minister provides an update on the project.
