Leicester landscaping company owner dies following small construction vehicle accident

The owner of a Leicester landscaping company was killed after he was trapped under a small construction vehicle Friday in a garage that housed the business, officials said.The victim was identified as Robert Stuart Jr., 51, in a statement released by the Worcester district attorney's office Saturday. His death appears to be a tragic accident, according to Leicester Police Chief Kenneth Antanavica.Leicester police, fire, and EMS went to 66 King St. for a report of a man trapped under a piece of equipment at about 11:49 a.
