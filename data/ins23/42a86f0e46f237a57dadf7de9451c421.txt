The Bottom Line: What is a C300? A C300 is a compact 4 door luxury sedan built and sold by Mercedes Benz, and is in the Mercedes Benz lineup of C class cars. The C300 4MATIC models have an AWD all wheel drive system that has a lightweight drivetrain, and provides better traction and smooth handling on all roads, in all climate conditions.
The Mercedes Benz C Class has 3 models, a luxury compact coupe, sedan and cabriolet. The base RWD rear wheel drive C300 Premium Edition sedan starts at $43,550, while the 4MATIC AWD C300 Premium Edition costs $45,550.
