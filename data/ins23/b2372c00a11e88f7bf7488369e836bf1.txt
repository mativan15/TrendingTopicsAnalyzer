Shah Rukh Khan selling bhutta on The Kapil Sharma Show.  (YouTube/SET India)
 

A video of Shah Rukh Khan ‘selling' roasted bhutta (corncob) on The Kapil Sharma Show is doing the rounds on social media, and it has sparked chatter online. The clip is from the comedy show aired on 26 November 2016 on Sony Entertainment Television (SET), where Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt become salespersons to sell miscellaneous items, including a towel, a pair of jeans, and of course, bhutta.
