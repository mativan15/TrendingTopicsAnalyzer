October 21, 2022 03:30 PM Eastern Daylight Time TORONTO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Docebo Inc. (Nasdaq:DCBO; TSX:DCBO) ("Docebo" or the "Company"), a leading artificial intelligence(AI)-powered learning suite provider, announced today that it will hold a conference call to discuss its third quarter fiscal year 2022 results on Thursday, November 10, 2022 at 8:00 a.m. (ET). The call will be hosted by Claudio Erba, Chief Executive Officer, Alessio Artuffo, President and Chief Operating Officer, and Sukaran Mehta, Chief Financial Officer, followed by a question and answer period.
