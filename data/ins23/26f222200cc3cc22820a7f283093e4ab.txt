Nashoba Tech sophomore Anthony LoConte, who is from Chelmsford, was one of two winners of the school's Athlete of the Month award for September. LoConte is the captain of the golf team (Courtesy of Nashoba Tech)WESTFORD, MA — Chelmsford resident Anthony LoConte was one of two students selected as September Athletes of the Month at Nashoba Valley Technical High School in Westford.LoConte, a sophomore, is the captain of the golf team and part of the plumbing and heating program at the school. With a modified Stableford point system used during Commonwealth Athletic Conference (CAC) play —where the highest score wins — LoConte averaged 26 points per match.
