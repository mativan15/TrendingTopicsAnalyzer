ZUG, Switzerland, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Autentica, a Swiss technology startup, announces its official launch and pre-seed investment of $1.2 million, ensuring the company can continue the development of its cross-chain certifying technology for NFTs.

Autentica, a Swiss tech startup developing cross-chain certifying technology for digital assets. (PRNewsfoto/Autentica.io)

The company provides proprietary authentication technology, enabling digital creators to transform their artworks into authentic NFTs without exposing themselves to fraudulent use or duplicates.
