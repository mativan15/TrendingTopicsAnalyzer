[BIGHIT MUSIC] HYBE rose 4.8 percent in trading Tuesday, the day after the company said that a member of BTS would be enlisting in the army soon. 'The announcement has put an end to months of speculation about the group's military service,' said Choi Min-ha, senior analyst at Samsung Securities. 'Now that the BTS-related uncertainty has disappeared, HYBE's share performance should hinge on the results of its business strategy.
