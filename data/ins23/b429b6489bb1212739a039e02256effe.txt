VANCOUVER, Wash., Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Cyber Acoustics, a leading manufacturer of reliable and affordable computer peripherals used in schools, businesses, and homes, today announced the CA Essential Bluetooth Headset HS-2000BT. Compatible with all unified communications (UC) platforms, including Microsoft Teams, Skype for Business, Zoom, Slack, and Google Meet, the CA Essential Bluetooth Headset was designed for today's demanding business environments, delivering high-quality, reliable audio when speaking and listening, no matter where you are.
