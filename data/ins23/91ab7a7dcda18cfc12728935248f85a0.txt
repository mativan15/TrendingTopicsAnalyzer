Govt Sets Oct 2024 Deadline For Maha Kumbh-2025 Work
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-15 06:45:00

PRAYAGRAJ : The state government has fixed October 31, 2024 as the deadline for the completion of all works pertaining to Maha Kumbh 2025 to be held on the banks of Sangam. The Yogi Adityanath government has already declared that the Maha Kumbh would be held on a grand scale.During an online meeting held on Thursday and chaired by principal secretary, urban development, Amrit Abhijat, it was decided that all the works and the departments concerned would follow the deadline and comply with the quality of work.
