Compliance certification strengthens Cribl's role in cybersecurity use cases and expands its footprint in the enterprise
SAN FRANCISCO, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Cribl, the leader in enabling open observability, today announced the completion of its Service Organization Control 2 (SOC 2) Type II security compliance attestation. SOC 2 ensures compliance with worldwide industry standards for data security and enables enterprises to be confident in using Cribl in their observability and cybersecurity environments.
