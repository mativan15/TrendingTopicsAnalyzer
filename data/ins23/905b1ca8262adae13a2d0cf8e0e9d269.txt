Published: 17:16 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 17:16 EDT, 4 June 2022 Believing passionately that bringing new life into the world 'is the greatest gift you could give anyone', Melissa Cook became a surrogate mother.'When I became pregnant with triplets, I was thrilled,' she reflects. 'I believed then that everyone has the right to be a parent.'However, six years on, she thinks very differently. For not only did she never see the three babies after they were born, but she has no idea where they now live.
