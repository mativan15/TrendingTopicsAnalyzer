Several mourners gathered at the residence of slain Punjabi singer Sidhu Moose Wala in Punjab's Mansa district on Tuesday morning as the family prepared to conduct his last . Accompanied by some relatives, Moose Wala's father received his son's body from Mansa Civil hospital, where it was kept for post-mortem, around 8:15 am.

The body has been taken to his residence at Moosa village in Mansa. The last rites of Moose Wala, who was killed on Sunday, will be performed on Tuesday. A heavy police force has been deployed outside the residence of the slain Punjabi singer.
