June 02, 2022 12:16 PM Eastern Daylight Time EWING, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Church & Dwight Co., Inc. (NYSE: CHD) will participate in a fireside chat at the dbAccess Global Consumer Conference on Thursday, June 16 at 9:15 a.m. CEST (3:15 a.m. EDT). A link to the broadcast will be provided through the Investors section of Church & Dwight's website at http://investor.churchdwight.com/investors/news-events.

Church & Dwight Co., Inc. manufactures and markets a wide range of personal care, household and specialty products under the ARM & HAMMER brand name and other well-known trademarks.
