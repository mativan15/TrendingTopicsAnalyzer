WASHINGTON , May 31, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Today, Humanity Forward applauds the introduction of a bipartisan resolution in the US Conference of Mayors recognizing the potential of blockchain technology to create unique opportunities for communities across America to grow their economies and create jobs. The US Conference of Mayors (USCM) is the largest national organization representing mayors of America's largest cities.

Humanity Forward logo (PRNewsfoto/Humanity Forward)

A bipartisan group of mayors call for "direct and coherent guidelines for the testing, adoption" of blockchain tech.
