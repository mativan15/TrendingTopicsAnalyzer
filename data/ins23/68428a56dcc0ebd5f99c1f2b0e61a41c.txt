Colgate-Palmolive (NYSE: CL) stock price rose almost 1.25x from $60 at 2018 end to around $73 currently, primarily due to favorable changes in its sales. During this period, the company witnessed a steady 14% rise in revenues, and combined with a 4.3% rise in its P/S multiple and a 3% drop in the outstanding share count, the company's stock price has rallied.
