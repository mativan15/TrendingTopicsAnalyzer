Billionaire refusing to pay estranged wife £45m so their kids 'learn the value of money'

A New York billionaire has shirked calls from his family to pay his estranged wife a huge settlement so his children can "learn the value of money".

Michael Fuchs, 62 told a judge in London, where divorce papers were filed, "smothering them in luxuries makes them jaded".
