This week, we finally got a bit of good news from Sony, that we appear to be on the back end of the chip shortage for the coming fiscal year, where they're predicting they'll have enough components to increase PS5 sales by 50% over last year. And yet given the state of the market, demand for current consoles and the tech inside them, unlike the PS4/Xbox Series X generation, the idea that we're just a year or two away from a PS5 Pro or an Xbox Series Y/Z/A is a much tougher sell than last time.
