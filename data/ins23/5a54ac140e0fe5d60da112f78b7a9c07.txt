Stray Kids' 'Case 143' Spends Second Week at No. 1 on Hot Trending Songs Chart
billboard.com
news
2022-10-18 19:11:27

Stray Kids‘ 'Case 143' tallies a second week at No. 1 on Billboard‘s Hot Trending Songs chart (dated Oct. 22), powered by Twitter. The song is the lead single off the K-pop group's new set MAXIDENT, which debuts at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 with 117,000 equivalent album units earned in the Oct. 7-13 tracking week, according to Luminate. Album sales comprise 110,000 of the set's sum, marking the fourth-largest sales week of any title this year. 'Case 143' became the Stray Kids' first No. 1 on Hot Trending Songs a week earlier.
