Harsco pursues its goal of global leadership in environmental solutions through innovations in beneficial reuse

CAMP HILL, Pa., June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- In recognition of World Environment Day, Harsco Corporation (NYSE: HSC), a global market leading provider of environmental solutions for industrial and specialty waste streams, today announces its continued commitment to improving sustainability and protecting our environment through beneficial reuse.

Since 1974, the United Nations has designated June 5 as World Environment Day to engage governments, businesses and citizens in an effort to address pressing environmental issues.
