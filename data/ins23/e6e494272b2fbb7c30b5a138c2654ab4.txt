By Garima Prasher City schools are back with a fresh in-person academic calendar after a hiatus of almost two years. Parents, in the meantime, are struggling to afford essential items for their children.
 After textbooks, now school uniforms have become dearer. Not only has the cost gone up by almost 40%, but there is also a shortage of supply.
 Parents BM spoke to said it is weighing on their pockets a bit too much and they have not been able to purchase the uniforms for the children yet. 'We incurred a great loss during the pandemic.
