These two Mass. communities named top places to live by Money magazine
bostonglobe.com
news
2022-10-20 19:08:12

Two Massachusetts communities cracked the top 20 in Money magazine's list of the 50 best places to live in the U.S.Somerville hit No. 7 on the annual list, which looks at quality of life, economic opportunities, and diversity, among other factors, to determine the rankings.Also making list from Massachusetts was the town of Milton, which was ranked No. 17.While the Money authors touted Somerville for its delectable Fluff Festival, profusion of breweries, and schools, the city was also lauded for its Department of Racial and Social Justice.
