Terry Savage: How to do a rollover
chicagotribune.com

2022-05-31 19:03:39

You worked so hard to accumulate all that money in your 401k plan. And a huge bull market helped. But when you retire or move to a new job, you might be much better off doing an IRA Rollover.The best reason to do a rollover is that the investment choices in 401(k) plans — as well as 403(b) and 457 plans — are mostly designed for younger people, who will continue to buy shares through their payroll deductions, even throughout a bear market. Eventually, they will be well-rewarded for their discipline — because 'eventually' the market moves higher.
