DALLAS (PRWEB) June 01, 2022 Long before Layne's Chicken Fingers began expanding within Texas and beyond, now-owner Mike Garratt said he would visit the restaurant in College Station two or three times a week for lunch. His father owned a landscaping business, he owned a lawn mowing business, and the duo just happened to be remodeling a school directly behind Layne's. 'Never in a hundred years did I ever think I was going to be in the restaurant business!' said Garratt. 'It all just kind of fell into place.
