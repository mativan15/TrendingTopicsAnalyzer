Bucks Starts Season As New Title Favorites
ibtimes.com
news
2022-10-17 20:23:13

A new favorite to win the 2023 NBA Finals has emerged as the regular season is set to tip off. The Milwaukee Bucks have the best odds to win the championship, moving ahead of the Boston Celtics on Opening Night.After the Golden State Warriors defeated the Celtics in the 2022 Finals, both teams spent time as the championship favorites during the offseason. Milwaukee now leads the way with +550 odds to win the title, according to FanDuel Sportsbook.The Celtics are just behind the Bucks with +600 odds.
