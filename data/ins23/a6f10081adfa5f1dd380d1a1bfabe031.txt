There is a wide range of options available when it comes to riding your bike indoors. The exercise bike category is a little bit different as there are two - maybe even three - different marketplaces that are currently all within this category.

At Cyclingnews we regularly cover 'smart bikes' which employ the technology developed in the best smart trainers and use it in standalone devices. These devices use ANT+ and Bluetooth technology to connect to your smart device, whether that be a laptop, smartphone or tablet, which you can then use to run indoor cycling apps on.
