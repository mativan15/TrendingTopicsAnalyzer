"We need to look at global regulation of crypto," says European Commission's financial services commissioner Mairead McGuinness
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 19:44:00

'We do need to see other players also legislating […] perhaps differently, but with the same objective . . . We need to look at global regulation of crypto.' — Mairead McGuinness, European Commissioner for Financial Stability, Financial Services  In an interview with the Financial Times, Mairead McGuinness, European Commissioner for Financial Stability, Financial Services  McGuinness said that regulating the crypto industry would need to be a global effort in order for it to be effective. 'There's a lot of concern at a European level as to [what would happen] if crypto were not to be regulated,' said McGuinness to the FT, adding that there 'could be — in time, if it grows — financial stability problems.
