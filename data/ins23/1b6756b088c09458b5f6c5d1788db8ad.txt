October 18, 2022 09:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--National CineMedia (NCM), America's largest cinema advertising network, and Spotlight Cinema Networks, the leading luxury, dine-in and art house cinema advertising network, today announced a strategic sales relationship, the Elevate Cinema Network (Elevate). This collaboration will combine nearly 1,500 premier NCM® screens with Spotlight's existing premier network of over 1,200 screens to bring Elevate's network to 2,700 ‘blue chip' screens available for cinema advertising through Spotlight.
