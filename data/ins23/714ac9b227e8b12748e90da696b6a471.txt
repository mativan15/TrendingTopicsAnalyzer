Karnataka: Two held for trying to extort money by posing as ACB officers
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-28 08:16:52

The Karnataka Police arrested two people who allegedly posed as officers of the Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB) and tried to extort money from government officials in the state, the police said.
The accused have been identified as Murgappa Ningappa Kumbar (56) and Rajani Kanth (46), the police added. According to officers, Kumbar has allegedly been involved in more than 40 similar cases across Karnataka and Rajani Kanth is involved in about six cases. The arrest came after Kumbar recently claimed to be a DySP with the ACB and tried to extort money from M Dhananjaya, under-secretary to the government education department, they said.
