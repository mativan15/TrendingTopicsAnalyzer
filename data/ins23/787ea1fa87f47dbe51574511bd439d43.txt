Published: 11:59 EDT, 20 October 2022 | Updated: 12:01 EDT, 20 October 2022 Selma Blair was spotted enjoying a quiet stroll in Los Angeles earlier on Wednesday along with her adorable service dog named Scout. This is the first time the talented star, 50, has been seen since exiting Dancing With The Stars earlier this week. The Cruel Intentions alum tearfully announced during Monday's episode of Dancing With The Stars that she would make an early departure from the competition show to focus on her health amid her ongoing battle with multiple sclerosis.
