June 01, 2022 11:46 AM Eastern Daylight Time PALO ALTO, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Aeldra, a global neobank launched in 2021 to provide Indians access to U.S. banking and investment, has picked up its second round of investment from the early Twitter Silicon Valley investor Greg Kidd. Kidd was among the first investors in Aeldra when it was launched. Today, close to 50,000 Indians are using Aeldra for their U.S. banking needs with more than $30 million in transactions.
