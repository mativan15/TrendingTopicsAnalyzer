India bond yields at 1-week high tracking uptick in global crude prices
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-05-30 12:00:00

AgenciesThe Reserve Bank of India's primary focus is to bring inflation down closer to target but it cannot disregard the concerns around growth, governor Shaktikanta Das told newspaper Economic Times in an interview published on Friday.India's benchmark 10-year bond yield rose to its highest level in a week on Monday as a rally in global crude oil prices to nearly $120 a barrel added to concerns over imported inflation. India's benchmark 10-year bond yield ended trading at 7.41%, its highest level May 23.
