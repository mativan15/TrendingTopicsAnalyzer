BJP president JP Nadda will address the rally at the Ramlila Maidan. (File)
 

The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) has made elaborate arrangements for the organisational meeting scheduled for Sunday that will be addressed by party president JP Nadda. Over the last two months, the BJP has been carrying out the enrolment of booth-level party workers and has been appointing teams of five people or panch parmeshwar, which comprise a booth president, booth-level officer for election-related work, a woman worker, a youth worker and an experienced party worker.
