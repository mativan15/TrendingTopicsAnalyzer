Despite the massive global investment in cybersecurity in 2021 (totaling nearly $72.5 billion) the year still proved to be one of the most challenging periods for CISOs as high-profile cyberattacks increased significantly. According to the Allianz Risk Barometer, 2022 won't be different, with cyberattacks becoming the number one global business risk for the second time in the survey's history.
