Gotham Knights: How to switch characters
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-20 21:28:13

Batman fans will no doubt be jumping into Gotham Knights when it releases on October 21, 2022. But, when they do, they'll need to pick up on a few things very quickly. First, you can unlock new suits to increase your hero's overall power. This is vital for staying on top of the action in Gotham Knights.Secondly, you can join up with friends at any point during the campaign to explore the city together and take on criminals as a team. And, finally, with four distinctly different characters to choose from, you can always switch characters to take on different types of combat, use different gadgets, and change up the game's cutscenes a little bit.
