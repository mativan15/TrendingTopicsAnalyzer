By Cao Yin | CHINA DAILY | Updated: 2022-06-06 08:47 Cao Yin While some people are leaving Shanghai because of the COVID-19 outbreak, others are doing the opposite and journeying to the city. Leaving or returning, the journey signifies a delayed homecoming for most of the travelers. After Line 10 of the city's metro resumed operations on May 22, I met and spoke with a number of passengers on the train that connects Pudong, a district east of the Huangpu River, with Puxi, on the west, and also provides easy access to the Shanghai Hongqiao Railway Station.
