BEIJING, Oct. 15, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The curtain is about to be raised on the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China (CPC) amid enthusiastic expectations from the public. This will be the first national congress held after the CPC's 100th anniversary, and its significance to the Party and the country is self-evident. From the grand perspective of history and the world, the 20th CPC National Congress also provides the outside world with a window to gain an in-depth understanding of China in the new era.
