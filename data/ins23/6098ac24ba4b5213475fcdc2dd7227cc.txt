Good morning, Bay Area. Starting the day in the U.K., Meta said on Tuesday that it would sell Giphy to settle its dispute with British antitrust regulators, representing the first time the Silicon Valley giant has been forced to sell part of its business. Meta — when it was still Facebook — paid roughly $400 million for Giphy in 2020. Turning to national news, citrus producers in Florida may be reeling in the wake of Hurricane Ian, but Circle Ks in the Sunshine State will soon be on the receiving end of new produce as the global chain signed a deal with Chicago-based Green Thumb Industries Inc.
