Editorial: Free Livermore from grip of manipulative political powerplay
eastbaytimes.com
news
2022-10-20 12:32:20

The obsession of Livermore's wealthy local newspaper publisher with stopping a much-needed, voter-supported affordable housing and commercial development project once again dominates the city's local elections.
During the past four years, Joan Seppala, the octogenarian founder and publisher of The Independent, a local weekly newspaper, her relatives and her allies have spent more than $1.4 million on unsuccessful ballot measures and candidate campaigns to try to stop the project, and unknown sums on a parallel, and thus far equally futile, effort in the courts.
