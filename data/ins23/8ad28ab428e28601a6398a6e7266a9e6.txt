Alsip

Hazelgreen Baptist Church, 5022 W. 115th St.: Harvest Party from 6-8 p.m. Oct. 29. Fire pits will be set up in the parking lots, and s'mores, coffee, cider and cocoa will be served. Games will be available for children. Costumes are optional. Information: 708-389-5941 or www.hazelgreenbaptist.com.

Flossmoor

Flossmoor Community Church, 2218 Hutchison Road: Good Gourd Fall Community Party from 2-5 p.m. Saturday outside at the church's Community House. Fun for all ages includes food and craft vendors, live music, BYOB, pumpkin decorating, games and contests with prizes.
