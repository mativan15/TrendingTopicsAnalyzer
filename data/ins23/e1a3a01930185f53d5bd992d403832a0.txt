Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 is running in its third week and continues to sustain itself at the ticket counters. Starring Kartik Aaryan, Kiara Advani and Tabu, the film had a fantastic first and second week and is on the way to cross the ₹150 crore mark on Sunday. It currently stands at ₹149.11 crore. It is currently competing with Akshay Kumar's new release, Samrat Prithviraj in theatres.
