Avalanche Technology and TMS-Elektronik Announce Representation Agreement for the Republic of Turkey

news
2022-10-17 14:30:00

FREMONT, Calif. (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Avalanche Technology, the leader in next generation MRAM technology, today announced that it has entered into an agreement with TMS-Elektronik, to represent Avalanche in the sales and support of its STT-MRAM-based discrete Persistent SRAM (P-SRAM) non-volatile memory products in the Republic of Turkey. In support of this new relationship, Avalanche Technology will join the TMS-Elektronik team at the upcoming SAHA Expo 2022 in Istanbul, October 25th-28th. 'With benchmark high reliability, high endurance and low power, combined with high access speed, Avalanche's STT-MRAM products will bring significant benefits to our customers who develop boards and systems for aerospace and industrial applications,' said Eren Çağaçan, Co-founder and CEO of TMS Elektronik.
