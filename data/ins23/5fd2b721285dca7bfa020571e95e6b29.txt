Photo: CBS 12A South Florida college student is charged with making threats to commit a massacre and providing advice for future terrorists and school shooters. 24-year old Saul Jean of Miami was arrested over the weekend after an investigation that started when a student at Palm Beach State College in Boca Raton reported online threats linked to him. The profanity-laced rants were found on Change.org and included comments against the federal government, gun control and Governor Ron DeSantis. They included a suggestion that if you're "fed up with how Florida politics are, target DeSantis.
