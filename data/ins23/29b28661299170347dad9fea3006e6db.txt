Company Holds Prime Position in Four of Six Domains to Support Behavioral Health Programs
FAIRFAX, Va., Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) recently selected global consulting and digital services provider ICF (NASDAQ:ICFI) as an awardee under its multiple-award recompete indefinite delivery, indefinite quantity (IDIQ) contract to support the agency's mission of reducing the impact of substance use and mental illness on America's communities.
