Akzo Nobel India's profit at Rs 75 crore in Q4 FY22
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-27 18:15:00

NEW DELHI: Paints and coatings maker Akzo Nobel India on Friday said its consolidated net profit remained flat at Rs 74.7 crore for fourth quarter ended March 2022. The company had logged a net profit of Rs 74.2 crore in January-March period a year ago, Akzo Nobel said in a regulatory filing. Revenue from operations was higher at Rs 867.1 crore during the quarter under review as against Rs 781.4 crore in the year-ago period. Total expenses were at Rs 772.7 crore as against Rs 688 crore earlier. For fiscal year ended March 2022, net profit was up 39.
