This smallcap hits upper circuit again; announces 1:2 stock split
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 10:15:00

Shares of Confidence Futuristic Energetech were locked in the 5% upper circuit on Tuesday at Rs 440.75. The stock has been hitting the upper circuit on a daily basis for the last few weeks now. Only recently on Sunday, the company had announced a 1:2 stock split, wherein the stock's face value will be changed from Rs 10 each to Rs 5 each. Also, the board of the company has intimated the record date for the stock split as November 3, 2022. Confidence Futuristic is a multibagger stock with 1-month and 3-month return of 108% and 152%, respectively.
