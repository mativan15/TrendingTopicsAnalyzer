There can be no safe havens for terrorists, drug cartels, corrupt, organised crime: PM
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 19:30:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday said there can be no safe havens for the corrupt, terrorists, drug cartels, poaching gangs and organised crime, and called for a global response to tackle these dangers. Speaking at the 90th General Assembly of the Interpol, he said a safe and secure world is "our shared responsibility" and when the forces of good cooperate, the forces of crime cannot operate. Modi said corruption and financial crimes have harmed the welfare of citizens of many countries.
