By Marlee Ginter October 14, 2022 / 11:00 PM / CBS Sacramento OLIVEHURST — Yuba County Sheriff's deputies say James Renshaw Senior and Tabitha Heidbreider went to the front office and assaulted the principal of Lindhurst High School after their son was involved in a fight with several students.CBS13 went to their neighborhood, where some are now learning much more about their neighbors.  One man who didn't want his face on camera said he had no idea his neighbors were felons and is now shocked and concerned, considering he has nieces and nephews that go to the same school.
