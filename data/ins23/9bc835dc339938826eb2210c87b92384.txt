RiceBran technologies shares slip on $1.5M registered direct offering
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-18 12:45:15

RiceBran Technologies (NASDAQ:RIBT) to raise $1.5M through a definitive securities purchase agreement with institutional investors for the purchase and sale of 1M shares of the company's common stock at a purchase price of $1.50/share of common stock in a registered direct offering. The closing of the offering is expected to occur on or about October 20, 2022. In addition, in a concurrent private placement, the company will issue to the investors warrants to purchase up to 2M shares of common stock.
