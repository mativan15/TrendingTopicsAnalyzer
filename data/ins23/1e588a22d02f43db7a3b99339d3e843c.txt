Portland, OR , Oct. 17, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- According to the report published by Allied Market Research, the global cloud security posture management market generated $4 billion in 2021, and is projected to reach $15.2 billion by 2031, growing at a CAGR of 14.5% from 2022 to 2031. The report offers a detailed analysis of the top winning strategies, evolving market trends, market size and estimations, value chain, key investment pockets, drivers & opportunities, competitive landscape and regional landscape.
