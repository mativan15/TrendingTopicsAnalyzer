Cap on bankers bonuses will still be lifted

Image source, Getty ImagesA plan to remove a cap on bankers' bonuses is one of the few mini-budget policies to remain after most measures were unwound by the new chancellor.Jeremy Hunt made no mention of a U-turn on removing the cap on banker's bonuses as he reversed many of his predecessor's other policies on Monday.Kwasi Kwarteng said last month the cap would be lifted, as part of a shake-up of rules governing the City of London.A Treasury source told the BBC the plan to remove the cap was going ahead.
