Streaming TV ad spend is expected to surge to $19 billion in 2023 — but it will 'cannibalize' traditional TV ad revenues, a Wells Fargo analyst says
businessinsider.com
news
2022-10-18 13:01:16

Next year will see a huge shift in ad spend from traditional TV to streaming, according to a Wall Street analyst. Steven Cahall, a managing director and senior analyst at Wells Fargo, said at the ad industry conference Programmatic I/O that streaming TV ad spend will be $19 billion by 2023 and will make up over 20% of total TV spend, compared to 6% in 2019.But as marketers brace for a recession, Cahall does not expect them to expand their budgets to buy more streaming ads. Instead, over the next two years, Cahall said, the surging growth of streaming TV advertising is going to "cannibalize real dollars out of linear television.
