Suhail M&T Bank (NYSE:MTB) Q3 earnings increased from both the previous quarter and the year-ago quarter but fell short of the average analyst estimate as the bank continued to integrate People's United into its operations. Loans were stable from Q2 and deposits slipped, according to its results reported Wednesday. MTB stock dropped 3.9% in Wednesday premarket trading. Q3 net operating EPS of $3.83, trailing the $4.26 consensus, rose from $3.10 in Q2 and from $3.76 in Q3 2021. Q3 annualized return on average common equity of 10.
