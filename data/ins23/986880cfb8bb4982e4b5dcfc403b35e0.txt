TORONTO & SEATTLE, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--POSaBIT Systems Corporation (CSE: PBIT, OTC: POSAF), the leading provider of payments infrastructure in the cannabis industry, has announced they will be presenting on the cannabis technology panel at the Jefferies 2022 Cannabis Summit on June 2 in New York City, and participating in one-on-one meetings.

About POSaBIT

POSaBIT (CSE: PBIT) is a financial technology company that delivers unique and innovative payment processing and point-of-sale systems for cash-only businesses.
