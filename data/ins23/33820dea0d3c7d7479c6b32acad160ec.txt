June 02, 2022 12:05 PM Eastern Daylight Time COSMOS, Minn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--The Cosmos Builders Foundation today launches its federation of crypto-native organizations collaborating toward a more vibrant, egalitarian, and resilient Interchain economy. In coordination with the Interchain Foundation, the Cosmos Builders Foundation is focused on supporting the maintenance and development of infrastructural public goods for the long term growth of the Interchain ecosystem.



'The Interchain is rapidly expanding, with over 250 apps and services having launched, largely since the start of last year, and it's vital that the infrastructure supporting interoperability in this ecosystem is well funded for the long-term sustainability of the Cosmos,' said Jack Zampolin, Founder of Strangelove Ventures and Member of the Cosmos Builders Foundation Governing Council.
