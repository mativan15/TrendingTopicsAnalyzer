Published: 08:41 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 08:41 EDT, 1 June 2022 A driver became the laughing stock of rowdy locals online after stopping in a bizarre spot in a suburban carpark.The Honda hatch was seen sticking so far out the front a parking space in Sydney, that many assumed the car had a person in it. Only the back wheels of the car were within the lines of the parking space outside the Bangor shopping centre in Bangor, southern Sydney.  A poorly parked car in Bangor has got locals mystified, with the majority of the hatchback in the middle of the road (pictured)The parking job was so poor, a local woman took to Facebook to share photos of the car, according to Yahoo news.
