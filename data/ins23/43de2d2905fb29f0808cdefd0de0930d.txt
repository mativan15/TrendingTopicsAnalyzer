Winton Shire Council launches legal action over $4m geothermal plant that's never delivered power

In parts of arid outback Queensland, bore water pumped from deep underground is so hot it needs cooling before drinking. An outback Queensland council built what was supposed to be Australia's only operating geothermal power stationThe council is suing the Local Government Association of Queensland's commercial arm over the failed projectPlans to build more geothermal power stations across western Queensland have stalledA $4-million geothermal power plant in Winton was built to use the naturally hot water to create electricity.
