Published: 17:19 EDT, 18 October 2022 | Updated: 17:19 EDT, 18 October 2022 A team reliant on stars not all that long ago now will lean on togetherness when the Toronto Raptors open their season against the visiting Cleveland Cavaliers on Wednesday night. Winners of the 2019 NBA Finals behind players like Kawhi Leonard and Kyle Lowry, the Raptors still have leftover experience from that squad in Pascal Siakam, Fred VanVleet and OG Anunoby as head coach Nick Nurse leans on a team approach. "The ideal, or the premise, is that's who we are and that's the way we've got to play and it probably is," Nurse said.
