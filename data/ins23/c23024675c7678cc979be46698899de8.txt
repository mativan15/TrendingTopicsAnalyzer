Local winery raising money in honor of fallen Clark County Sheriff's Deputies
yahoo.com
news
2022-10-20 06:44:24

A winery in Enon is looking to raise funds for the Clark County Sheriff's Department.

>>RELATED: Sheriff's deputy dies from injuries after being shot at Clark County mobile home park

Brandeberry Winery will be selling a, 'Hero in Blue' wine bottle and portions of the proceeds will be used to purchase safety equipment.

This is being done in honor of Deputy Matthew Yates and Deputy Suzanne Hopper, both lost their lives in the line of duty, according to its Facebook page.

They will be donating $4 per bottle to local Clark County Law Enforcement.
