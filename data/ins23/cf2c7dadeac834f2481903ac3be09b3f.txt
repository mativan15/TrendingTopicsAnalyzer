October 17, 2022 06:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time SAN JOSE, Calif. & NAGOYA, Japan--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Energous Corporation (NASDAQ: WATT), a leading developer of RF-based charging for wireless power networks, and NGK INSULATORS, LTD ('NGK') — one of the world's leading battery manufacturers — today announced a partnership to develop solutions that combine Energous' WattUp technology with NGK's lithium-ion rechargeable EnerCera® battery to enable maintenance-free IoT devices such as sensors and tracking devices supporting the growing IoT ecosystem.
