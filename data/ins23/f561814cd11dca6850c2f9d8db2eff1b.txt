Published: 13:04 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 13:04 EDT, 17 October 2022 Pierce Brosnan has revealed that he wore his real wedding ring in his latest film Black Adam.The actor, 69, told in a new interview that he purposely wore the sentimental jewellery as a tribute to his wife of 21 years Keely Shaye Smith, remarking that: 'It seemed appropriate'.  Pierce appears in Black Adam as Kent Nelson, who goes by Doctor Fate as part of the Justice Society of America, a group of superheroes to try to stop the murderous rampage of the title character, played by Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson.
