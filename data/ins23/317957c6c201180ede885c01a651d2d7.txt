Regulated Australian derivatives exchange market operator to launch institutional digital asset custody services underpinned by Tier 1 bank-grade technology

SYDNEY & SINGAPORE, June 02, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Mercari, which operates a licensed Australian Tier 1 wholesale financial derivatives exchange, has selected METACO, the market-leading provider of digital asset custody orchestration technology to complex, global financial institutions, to launch its institutional digital asset custody operations.
