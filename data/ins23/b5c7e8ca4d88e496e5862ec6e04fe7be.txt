Restaurants participate in Black Restaurant Week Birmingham
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-19 01:52:00

Several metro Birmingham restaurants are participating in this year's Black Restaurant Week.

Black Restaurant Week LLC, the organization celebrating the flavors of African-American, African and Caribbean cuisine with a series of regional cultural events, has brought the event to Birmingham once again.

The event kicked off Oct. 14 and will run through Oct. 23. This year's theme is "More Than Just a Week," which coincides with a nationwide tour.

Local restaurants participating include Kuntri Kitchen, Michael's Restaurant, Deb's Italian Ices, Frozen Rooster and Ivory LeShore's Gourmet Bread Pudding.
