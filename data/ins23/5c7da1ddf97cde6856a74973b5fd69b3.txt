Tom Welling played Clark Kent. Stefanie Keenan/Getty Images for A Sense of Home Tom Welling as Clark Kent. The WB Clark, whose birth name is Kal-El, was found and raised by his adoptive parents Jonathan and Martha Kent after his spaceship crash-landed in Smallville from the planet Krypton.During his teen years, he was made aware of his alien heritage by his adopted father. Throughout the series' ten seasons, a pre-Superman Clark Kent learned to harness his power, befriended Lana Lang and his future nemesis Lex Luthor, and eventually met his comic-book soulmate Lois Lane.
