Puneeth Rajkumar will be the ninth recipient of the Karnataka Ratna, which was last awarded to Dr Virendra Heggade for social service in 2009.BENGALUR
U: Late Kannada film star Puneeth Rajkumar will be posthumously conferred with the 'Karnataka Ratna', the state's highest civilian award, in front of Vidhana Soudha here on November 1, which also happens to be 'Kannada Rajyotsava', the state's formation day. Considered Kannada cinema's reigning star, Puneeth Rajkumar, the youngest of five children of Kannada matinee idol Dr Rajkumar, died of cardiac arrest on October 29 last year at the age of 46.
