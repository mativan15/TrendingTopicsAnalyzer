Published: 01:00 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 01:00 EDT, 16 October 2022 I've found a new way to torture myself during these darker autumn evenings snuggled on the sofa. Worse still, this new addiction is making me want to reposition said sofa and repaint the walls. And it's leaving me even more frustrated that I can't find sleek kitchen bar stools for love nor money. Trouble is, I can't stop watching Designing Miami. It's like catnip to a Virgo like me. This new Netflix show follows the lives of high-end interior designers Eilyn and Ray Jimenez, a 30-something married couple who furnish the homes of Florida's wealthy elite.
