Helvetia presents a gratifying result for occupational benefits



Media release

Basel, 31 May 2022 The collective life business of Helvetia Insurance is on a firm footing. The results for the savings, risk and cost process were positive, and costs per insured person were reduced significantly. The following still applies: a lasting reform of the occupational benefits scheme remains urgent and essential. Helvetia Insurance presents a gratifying result for occupational benefits.
