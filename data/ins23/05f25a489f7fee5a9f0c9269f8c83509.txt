How ETFs can help you support conscious or sustainability causes
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-05-31 20:17:10

If you're looking for a way to direct your investments toward sustainable companies or support socially conscious causes or entities, consider purchasing ETFs to broaden your portfolio. "ETFs are a diversified basket or typically stocks or bonds that can be paired to build a portfolio," said Todd Rosenbluth, head of research at VettiFi. "Some are broadly based and others are more narrowly focused to areas such as smaller companies, dividend payers or technology. They can be purchased or sold intraday as needed and tend to have low management fees.
