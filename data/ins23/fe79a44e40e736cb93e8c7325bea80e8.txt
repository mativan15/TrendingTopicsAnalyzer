"I'm a millionaire. We should be taxed more"
newsweek.com
news
2022-05-28 08:00:01

At the end of May, I was at the World Economic Forum in Davos protesting with Patriotic Millionaires. As an organization, we have a counter intuitive message. We're trying to get across to other millionaires and to politicians that, as millionaires, we are very happy to contribute more to society. We want to pay more tax.A socialist group in Switzerland had organized the Davos protest and invited us along. They were "the 99 percent" and we were "the 1 percent", so to speak, and we had the same message.
