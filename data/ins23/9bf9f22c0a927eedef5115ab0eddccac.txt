Smith announced today that on May 16, 2022, Marvelous Eghage, 39, of Houston, TX, pleaded guilty to one count of wire fraud before Magistrate Judge Ruth Miller. According to court documents, between February 26, 2018, and March 13, 2018, Eghage devised a scheme to fraudulently obtain money and property by means of materially false and fraudulent pretenses and promises directed to third parties. Through her fraudulent scheme, Eghage diverted funds from her victims to a bank account under her controlled by way of electronic wires and later withdrew or transferred the funds for her own personal benefit and the benefit of others.
