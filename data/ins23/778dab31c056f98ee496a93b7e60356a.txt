240,000 apply for costly fast-track service (Image: Getty)The Passport Office expects to receive more than 240,000 fast track applications between this month and October.A standard online application for an adult passport costs £75.50 but the fee for a one week fast track service is £142.The number of fast track applications has more than doubled from 21,124 in December to 44,386 in April.People making standard applications are now encouraged to apply more than 10 weeks before they are due to travel.
