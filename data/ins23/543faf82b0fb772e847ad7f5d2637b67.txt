PALO ALTO, Calif., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Mega Matrix Corp. ('MTMT' or the 'Company') (NYSE American: MTMT), today announced that, on October 18, 2022, the Company has completed its previously announced private placement for the purchase of 4,400,000 shares of the Company's common stock, $0.001 par value per share, at a purchase price of $1.00 per share. The gross proceeds from the private placement were $4.4 million, before deducting estimated offering expenses payable by the Company.
