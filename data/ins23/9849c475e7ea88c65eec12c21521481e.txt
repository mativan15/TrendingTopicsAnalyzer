WILMINGTON, DE / ACCESSWIRE / October 17, 2022 / Transparency Market Research Inc. - In 2021, the value of global cotton picker market stood at US$ 1.20 Bn. The global market is likely to progress at 5.6% CAGR during the forecast period, from 2022 to 2031. By 2031, the global cotton picker market is anticipated to attain value of US$ 1.10 Bn.In order to gain an edge over its competitors, as part of marketing strategy for cotton picker, strong emphasis is placed on developing robotic cotton harvesting devices with features like LCD monitoring systems, sensors, GPS, and so on.
