DAVANAGERE/BENGALURU: Avinash V , a 24-year-old from Davanagere, topped Karnataka with an all-India rank of 31 in the civil services examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC). As many as 685 candidates have cracked the exam this time, of which 25 are from Karnataka.Avinash completed schooling and pre-university from Davan College in Davanagere. Later, he shifted to Bengaluru to pursue a five-year BA LLB course at National Law School of India University (NLSIU), where he bagged a gold medal.
