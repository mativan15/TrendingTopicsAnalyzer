NEW YORK, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- The global advanced ceramics market size is expected to grow by USD 3.29 billion, accelerating at a CAGR of 5.56% between 2021 and 2026. To estimate the size of the market, Technavio has tracked the recent trends and developments in the commodity chemicals industry. Some of the factors considered to estimate the market size include the revenue generated by chemical products companies manufacturing advanced ceramics, demand from end-user industries, construction industry growth, and GDP growth among others.
