The society will soon fully embrace AI children, an expert predicts Dubbed Tamagotchi Generation, it will be consist of AI or digital babiesThis tech revolution could happen within the next 50 yearsSeemingly taken from one of the episodes of the British anthology TV series "Black Mirror," an expert on Artificial Intelligence (AI) in the U.K. has predicted that the world is "already well on [its] way to creating the Tamagotchi Generation."Catriona Campbell, a renowned behavioral psychologist specializing in AI, user experience, and human-machine interaction (HCI) argued in her new book, titled "AI by Design: A Plan For Living With Artificial Intelligence," that overpopulation will propel society to fully accept AI children.
