Toronto Stocks Climb; Tidewater Renewables Rises on New Long-Term Offtake Agreement
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 16:01:00

By Adriano Marchese Toronto-listed stocks were notably higher at midday Monday. Sentiment among investors was more positive in the first trading day of the week as U.K.'s new chancellor announced his plans to scrap most of his predecessors tax cuts and energy price caps in an attempt to calm the markets. In the session, all of Canada's sectors were posting gains, led by technology services, tech and health services. Tidewater Renewables Ltd. shares rose after it announced a new 20-year agreement to supply renewable natural gas to FortisBC Energy Inc.
