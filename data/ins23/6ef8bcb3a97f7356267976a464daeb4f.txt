Report: Apple Union Organizers Cancel Election
pcmag.com
news
2022-05-28 14:30:00

A labor group looking to unionize an Apple Store in Atlanta has reportedly thrown in the towel.Bloomberg reports(Opens in a new window) that the Communications Workers of America (CWA) has canceled a union election scheduled for June 2 "because Apple's repeated violations of the National Labor Relations Act have made a free and fair election impossible." The group reportedly said that it's also concerned about rising COVID-19 infections making an in-person election unsafe.The CWA has accused Apple of conducting a "systematic, sophisticated campaign to intimidate them and interfere with their right to form a union," including the use of "captive audience" meetings that allow managers to espouse the evils of unionizing to employees.
