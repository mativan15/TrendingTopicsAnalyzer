Bengaluru police arrest high-end bicycle thief, recover 54 bicycles worth Rs 6 lakh
indianexpress.com
news
2022-05-29 08:55:12

Bengaluru police on Saturday arrested a high-end bicycle thief in the city and recovered 54 bicycles, worth Rs 6 lakh, from him, officers said.
The police in Sudduguntepalya identified the accused as Balraj (48), a resident of Mylasandra. They had recently received a complaint regarding a bicycle theft following which officers checked the CCTV footage in the area and, upon investigation, found that Balraj was a habitual offender, the police said. Balraj had been out on bail after being arrested in a house theft case.
