Nasdaq, S&P 500, Dow rally after big sell-off as earnings season heats up
seekingalpha.com
finance
2022-10-17 15:58:20

Spencer Platt/Getty Images News U.S. stocks rallied in Monday morning trading, helped by solid results from Bank of America and BNY, with bargain hunters also stepping in to buy equities after a major sell-off last week. By mid-morning, the tech-heavy Nasdaq Composite (COMP.IND) had gained 3.37% to 10,669.26 points, buoyed by a more than 5% jump in shares of Netflix and Amazon. The benchmark S&P 500 (SP500) was up 2.67% to 3,678.69 points. The blue-chip Dow (DJI) added 1.85% to 30,182.80 points, boosted by JPMorgan and Microsoft.
