BRIDGEVILLE, Pa., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Universal Stainless & Alloy Products, Inc. (Nasdaq: USAP) today reported that, based on preliminary results, it expects to report net sales for the third quarter of 2022 of $46.2 million, which is 12% lower than the second quarter of 2022, although 24% higher than the third quarter of 2021. Premium alloy sales are expected to represent 17% of total third quarter 2022 sales.
