ARSENAL could be forced into a legal battle over the £3million deal to sign Marquinhos from Sao Paulo after the striker reportedly signed a pre-contract with Wolves.
Gunners boss Mikel Arteta is desperate to add strikers this summer, with 19-year-old Marquinhos one for the future.
Marquinhos is the subject of a transfer battle between Arsenal and WolvesCredit: Getty
But according to MailOnline, the deal could be in jeopardy.
It has emerged that the teenager "has already inked a pre-contract agreement to join Wolves".
