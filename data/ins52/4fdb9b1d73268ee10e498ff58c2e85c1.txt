Rainfall that brought misery and misfortune
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-21 00:30:00

By Shreyashi MukherjeeIn Shivajinagar, RT Nagar, and other places, people were stranded for hours, and vehicles damaged It was a scene that reminded people of the flood deluge that swept Bengaluru nearly a month back. Roads unmotorable, homes reeking of sewage, and cars and bikes stuck. The heavy rain that started on Wednesday brought with it woes and anger in citizens, who feel authorities are not doing enough.
 In Shivajinagar, several residents almost lost their vehicles as they were on the verge of floating away.
