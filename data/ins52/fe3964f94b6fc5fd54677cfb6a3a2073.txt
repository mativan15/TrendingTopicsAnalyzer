Concorde New Media is a full spectrum marketing company located in the heart of the Chicago Loop at 303 East Wacker Drive. As group of creative agencies, Concorde is driven by a shared goal: advancing business growth for their clients with measurable results. Chicago, Illinois (PRWEB) October 20, 2022 Concorde New Media is delighted that they were able to sponsor such a celebrated event that included mingling, a cocktail hour, dinner, dessert, entertainment, and dancing to name just a few. The fundraising Gala highlights the ability to boost the economy in the Chicago Loop along with sales, businesses, and also offers residents a deep sense of community.
