18 October 2022 by Anne Thompson 
Indiewire Thirteen-year-old Doc NYC, America's largest documentary festival, has revealed its influential 15-film Short List. The festival will run its main lineup of 112 features and 124 short films in-person November 9-17 at the IFC Center, Sva Theatre, and Cinépolis Chelsea and continue online until November 27 with films available to viewers across the U.S. All the films will have theatrical screenings at the festival, often with the directors in person.Historically, most of the Doc NYC shortlist titles overlap with the Academy's official 15-film Oscar Shortlist.
