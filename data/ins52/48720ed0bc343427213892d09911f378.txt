Kotak Mahindra Bank Q2 Results: Profit jumps 21% YoY to Rs 3,608 crore; NII rises 24%

NEW DELHI: Kotak Mahindra Bank on Saturday reported a 21% year-on-year (YoY) rise in the profit after tax (PAT) at Rs 3,608.18 crore for the September 2022 quarter. It reported a PAT of Rs 2,899.74 crore in the same quarter previous year. The private lender's net interest income (NII) stood at Rs 6,620 crore for July-September 2022 period. This was about 24% higher than NIIs of Rs 5,353 crore in the year-ago period.
