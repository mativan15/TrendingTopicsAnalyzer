Sen. Bob Menendez and Cory Booker were joined by students, teachers and gun safety advocates at a rally on Thursday, including Montclair Mayor Sean Spiller. (Shutterstock)MONTCLAIR, NJ — Montclair Mayor Sean Spiller was among the advocates who stood alongside New Jersey's two U.S. senators to push for "strong federal gun legislation" on Thursday.Sen. Bob Menendez and Cory Booker were joined by students, teachers and gun safety advocates at a rally in Hudson County. The gathering was held in the wake of recent mass shootings in New York, Texas and Oklahoma.
