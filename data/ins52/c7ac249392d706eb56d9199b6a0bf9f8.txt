Heartland Financial USA Raises Dividend to 28c/Shr
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-19 18:09:00

By Kathryn Hardison Heartland Financial USA Inc.'s board of directors has approved a quarterly cash dividend of 28 cents per share. The dividend represents an increase of one cent from the prior dividend of 27 cents per share, the company said Wednesday. The dividend is payable on Nov. 29 to stockholders of record at the close of business on Nov. 14. Write to Kathryn Hardison at kathryn.hardison@wsj.com
