Debate Herschel Walker, Georgia's Republican nominee for Senate, sparked backlash on social media after the first half of Friday night's debate with Democratic Senator Raphael Warnock after saying that diabetics needed to be "eating right" in order for insulin to be effective.Walker made the statement after a response from Warnock to a question on whether the senator took some responsibility for record inflation after supporting nearly every piece of legislation that has been pushed by the administration of President Joe Biden.
