House of the Dragon, the prequel series to Game of Thrones, has been a huge hit. The series has a strong fanbase in India, just like GOT. The first season's finale episode is scheduled to premiere on Sunday, October 23, on HBO and HBO Max. In India, the series is available for streaming on Disney+ Hotstar. To commemorate the epic first season, Oppo has partnered with the production and launched a House of the Dragon Edition of the Reno 8 Pro in India. The limited edition Reno 8 Pro comes in two colors - Glazed Black and Glazed Green.
