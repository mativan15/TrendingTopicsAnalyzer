Projects Worth Rs 1,34,200 Crore Being Implemented In North-East: Finance Minister

Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman said North-East to get big ticket infrastructure projectsGuwahati: Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman on Sunday said the Government is implementing various rail, road and air connectivity projects worth Rs 1,34,200 crore in the North-East.Addressing the 'Natural Allies in Development and Interdependence' conclave here, she said the Union government has been pumping in huge money in developing a number of infrastructure projects throughout the region."We are carrying out 20 railway projects worth Rs 74,000 crore for 2,011 km, which are spread across the northeast," Ms Sitharaman said.
