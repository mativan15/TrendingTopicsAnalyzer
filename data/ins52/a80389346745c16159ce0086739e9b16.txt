Myth: Including gelatin in your diet will strengthen weak nails
We all know that gelatin is a protein and nails are also made of keratin(protein), there's no scientific research to prove that gelatin can strengthen the nails. Best tip - Add good nutrients in your daily diet including proteins. Don't wait for the miracle, give your nails a makeover, find a professional nail tech who can give the million dollar look.


Myth: Remove the enhancements occasionally to give your nails a break
If you're addicted to nails, it's unthinkable to remove all enhancements, there's no break required.
