For Jaspinder Singh Bhullar, a Muktsar-based farmer's son, clearing the UPSC civil services exam is a childhood dream come true. The 26-year-old resident of Bhullar village now wants to work for the development of rural areas after joining the bureaucracy. A first generation graduate from his family, Jaspinder says it was consistency and self-confidence that helped him attain the 33rd rank.
