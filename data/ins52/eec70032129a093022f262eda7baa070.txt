Energy and tech stocks help lift S&P/TSX composite which is headed to positive month
ctvnews.ca
business
2022-05-30 09:25:41

TORONTO - A broad rally Monday led by the energy and technology sectors lifted Canada's main stock index, which is on track to end the volatile month of May in positive territory. The S&P/TSX composite index increased 170.82 points to 20,919.40, its highest close since May 4 and a seventh straight day of gains. 'It seems like last week we finally broke this streak of weeks where the stock markets were losing,' said Vincent Tonietto, portfolio manager at Fiduciary Trust Canada.
