NOAA Awards Parsons Team $16 Million Contract For Polar Operational Environmental Satellites Operations

CENTREVILLE, Va., Oct. 19, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) has selected a team led by Parsons Corporation (NYSE: PSN) to provide engineering services, information technology functionality, and flight operations support to assume on-orbit operations for the Polar Operational Environmental Satellites (POES) NOAA-15, NOAA-18, and NOAA-19. The $16 million definitive contract has a one-year performance period and two one-year option periods and is new work for Parsons.
