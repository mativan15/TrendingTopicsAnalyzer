Saudi Arabia Sentences 72-Year Old US Citizen To 16 Years In Prison Over Tweets: Reports
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 18:56:47

A 72-year-old man holding US citizenship has been allegedly sentenced to 16 years of life in prison, as per his son. The son alleges that a retired project manager from Florida was incarcerated in Saudi Arabia for 16 years and was tortured because of tweets he posted from the US criticizing the Saudi government.Saad Ibrahim Almadi, 72, is a dual citizen of the US and Saudi Arabia. According to his son Ibrahim, he was arrested in November while visiting family in Saudi Arabia and was convicted earlier this month.
