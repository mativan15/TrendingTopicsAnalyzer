This part of IGN's Mario Strikers: Battle League wiki guide covers the First Kick Demo, which gives you a chance to try out the online portion of Mario Strikers: Battle League before the game releases on June 10. Below you'll learn how to download the First Kick demo, the Mario Strikers Battle League demo times, what you can do in the demo outside of the online play windows, and more.
To download the Mario Strikers First Kick demo, you just need to head to the Nintendo Switch eShop. It's currently listed under the 'Featured' tab, the first page that the eShop opens up to.
