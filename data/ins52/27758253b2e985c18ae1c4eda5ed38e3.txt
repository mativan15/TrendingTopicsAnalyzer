October 21, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time STAMFORD, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Enterprises of all kinds are considering or using software-defined (SD) networking to improve their operations and competitiveness, according to a new research report published today by Information Services Group (ISG) (Nasdaq: III), a leading global technology research and advisory firm.



The 2022 ISG Provider Lens™ Network — Software Defined Solutions and Services Archetype report finds that organizations around the world see value in SD networking for increasing flexibility, streamlining network management and supporting remote work and business continuity.
