NEW YORK, June 03, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Bragar Eagel & Squire, P.C., a nationally recognized shareholder rights law firm, reminds investors that class actions have been commenced on behalf of stockholders of Twitter, Inc. (NYSE: TWTR), LMP Automotive Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: LMPX), and Spero Therapeutics, Inc. (NASDAQ: SPRO). Stockholders have until the deadlines below to petition the court to serve as lead plaintiff.
