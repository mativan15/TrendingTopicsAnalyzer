EU leaders manage — just about — to avoid commenting on London shambles
irishtimes.com
world
2022-10-21 16:40:19

They spent hours wading through the treacle of EU energy policy before agreeing a mushy compromise in the early hours of the morning. But EU leaders were as agog as everyone else at events in London – even if they couldn't say so.Leader after leader lined up to offer variations of no comment at all to the massed ranks of cameras and microphones and outstretched iPhones that greeted the EU heads of government as they entered the summit venue.Some marched past imperiously; most stopped for a few words with clusters of journalists from their home countries.
