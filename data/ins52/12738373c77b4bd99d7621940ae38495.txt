AAP, BJP exchange words over Delhi deputy CM Manish Sisodia's claims of 'BJP offer'
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-19 05:45:00

NEW DELHI: BJP's challenge to deputy chief minister Manish Sisodia to appear for a narco test over his allegations that the CBI tried to pressure him into quitting AAP and joining BJP evoked a sharp reaction from AAP, which called for the narco test of PM Narendra Modi.Addressing a press conference, West Delhi MP Parvesh Verma on Tuesday said if Sisodia was an honest leader, he should agree to a narco test or step down for 'lying'. 'If any CBI officer has asked Sisodia to join BJP then he should name that officer.
