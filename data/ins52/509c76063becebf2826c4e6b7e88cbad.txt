Collider.com Editor's note: The below contains spoilers for The Watcher.You've probably heard of Netflix and Ryan Murphy's latest collaboration, The Watcher. In the wake of its October 13 release date, the true crime limited series quickly rose to that coveted #1 on Netflix spot, proving that plenty of users were tuning in to see what the latest in a long string of true crime dramas is all about. When you reflect on the true story that The Watcher is based on, it's really no wonder that the tale was adapted into a show or that it's caught a lot of viewers' attention.
