October 18, 2022 12:09 AM Eastern Daylight Time HONG KONG--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Guotai Junan International Holdings Limited ("Guotai Junan International", "GTJAI" or the "Group", Stock Code: 1788.HK) announced that as one of the largest wealth management platforms in Hong Kong, the Company has launched a new H5 online "Speedy Account Opening" function, in which the introduction of face recognition, OCR text recognition and other technologies has greatly optimized the efficiency of clients' account opening and provided safe and convenient services for wealth management and retail clients.
