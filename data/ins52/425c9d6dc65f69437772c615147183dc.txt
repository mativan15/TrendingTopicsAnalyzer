Two locations in hurricane-prone region converted to permanent CAT yards

WESTCHESTER, Ill., June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--IAA, Inc. (NYSE: IAA), a leading global digital marketplace connecting vehicle buyers and sellers, announces the expansions of the Crowley and Scott CAT yards near Lafayette (LA). These full-term conversions of CAT yards into permanent facilities support IAA's long-term capacity strategy in this hurricane-prone region.
