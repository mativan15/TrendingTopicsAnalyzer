Prime Minister Narendra Modi will address the 90th Interpol General Assembly on October 18 in New Delhi's Pragati Maidan, the Prime Minister's Office (PMO) said on Monday."The 90th General Assembly of INTERPOL will be held from 18th to 21st October. The meeting will be attended by delegations from 195 INTERPOL member countries comprising of Ministers, Police Chiefs of countries, Heads of National Central Bureaus and Senior police officers," the PMO said in a statement.
