Women's representation on Indian Boards has tripled in 10 years: EY Report
ey.com
news
2022-10-18 10:39:58

Almost 95% of NIFTY500 companies have one female board member, up from 69% in 2017 With a 24% women representation on board, the life sciences sector has taken the lead, followed by the Media & Entertainment sector. While India has improved gender diversity on boards, it still lags far behind countries like France, Sweden, the US, and the U.K. New Delhi 17 October 2022: EY released its report on 'Diversity in the Boardroom: progress and the way forward' highlighting its findings on the representation of women on Indian boards and emphasizing the actions organizations must take to increase gender diversity.
