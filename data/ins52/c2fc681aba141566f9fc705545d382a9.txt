Matthias Metz, currently a member of the Executive Board of ZEISS Group, will move to Europe's leading home appliance manufacturer as CEO and Chairman of the Board of Management on October 1, 2022Metz will take over the CEO role currently held on an interim basis by BSH CFO Dr. Gerhard Dambach MUNICH, Germany, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Effective October 1, 2022, Dr. Matthias Metz will take over as CEO of BSH Hausgeräte GmbH. In this capacity, he will be responsible for the global business of the home appliance manufacturer with more than 62,000 employees and a global production network of 40 factories worldwide.
