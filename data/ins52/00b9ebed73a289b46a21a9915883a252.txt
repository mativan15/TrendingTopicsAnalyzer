Woman backed for not swapping meal with pregnant cousin: "Quit complaining"
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-01 16:06:57

Commenters praised a woman for not swapping meals with her pregnant cousin after she said the smell of her pasta made her sick.The anonymous woman, known as u/aita_burgers_, posted about the incident in Reddit's popular "Am I The A**hole" forum where it received more than 11,000 upvotes and 1,300 comments.According to Healthline, hyperosmia—a heightened sense of smell—is a common symptom of pregnancy and can cause nausea and migraines. Studies have shown that nearly two-thirds of pregnant individuals have experienced hyperosmia.
