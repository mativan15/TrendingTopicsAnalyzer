Published: 19:41 EDT, 1 June 2022 | Updated: 19:41 EDT, 1 June 2022 First came the huge explosion in a school yard that damaged 2,400 flats in the early hours of the morning. Minutes later, a missile struck the city centre. The following night another huge bomb dropped on the sleepy outskirts.One thousand pounds of explosives falling from the skies left a huge hole in the ground three times my height. The shockwaves and savage shards of shrapnel ripped through another 100 homes.City officials reached the scene within minutes to find a 90-year-old woman clambering from the ruins of her house.
