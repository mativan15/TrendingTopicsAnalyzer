' I couldn't care less' Platinum Jubilee torn apart by apathetic young Brits
express.co.uk
news
2022-06-05 23:33:00

Delirious royal fans have attended a wide range of events including Trooping the Colour, a flypast and a star-studded concert at Buckingham Palace while thousands of street parties have been held across the nation. However a number of young Brits actively avoided the festivities irritated by the pomp and ceremony for a monarch and institution they feel has little place in their lives.Some young Brits cite colonialism or the lack of diversity for their apathy or even hostility to the royal pageantry.
