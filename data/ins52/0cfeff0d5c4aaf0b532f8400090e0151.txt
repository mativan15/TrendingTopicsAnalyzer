SINGAPORE, Oct. 18, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- XT.COM, the world's first socially infused trading platform, is pleased to announce the listing of the RFT token (RTF in its Main Zone. The token is paired with USDT so users can buy, sell and trade RFT token seamlessly on XT.COM.

About the RTF Token

Investors of RTF Token are able to partake in the staking of the sales revenue of rare earth minerals. Capped at 100,000,000, the ERC-20 based token is linked to the discovery, mining, and distribution of rare earth minerals.
