Pokémon Scarlet and Pokémon Violet aren't that far off now, and Pokémon fans everywhere are gearing up for the next installment of the acclaimed franchise. The two newest titles will feature a lot of the same features seen in Pokémon Legends: Arceus, as well as some returns to the normal Pokémon formula. But when exactly can you expect to jump in and explore Paldea? Here's what you need to know.Pokémon Scarlet and Pokémon Violet will release on November 18 on the Nintendo Switch. Like previous Pokémon games, these titles will only be available on the Nintendo console.
