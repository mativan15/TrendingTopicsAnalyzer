AgenciesImage used for representative purpose onlyMumbai Police on Friday issued prohibitory orders under section 37 of the Maharashtra Police Act for 15 days from November 1 to November 15, 2022. Under the prohibitory orders, not more than 5 persons can gather at a place. According to news agency ANI, the police has banned processions as well.
