iStock For the week ending May 31, average personal loan rates for 5-year loans have ticked up to 24.29% from 22.08% a week prior, and average rates for 3-year loans have hit an 8-week high of 23.51%. But fortunately for those with excellent credit, rates are significantly lower, with 5-year loans at 14.87% and 3-year loans at 13.90%, according to Bankrate's latest data. (You can see the lowest rates you may qualify for here.) Personal loans can be used for a variety of reasons, from consolidating high-interest debt to paying for large purchases like a needed home repair.
