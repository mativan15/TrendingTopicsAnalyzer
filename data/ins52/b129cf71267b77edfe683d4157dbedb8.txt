AgenciesHimanta Biswa SarmaChief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma on Saturday threatened to file criminal defamation case against Delhi Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia for alleging that the Assam government had given contracts to his wife's firms and son's business partner to supply PPE kits above market rates when COVID-19 pandemic was raging in the country in 2020. Sarma said in a series of tweets that Assam hardly had any PPE kits then. "My wife gathered the courage to come forward and donated around 1500 of them free of cost to the government to save lives.
