50 smallcap stocks turnaround investors' fortune multifold since last Samvat
indiatimes.com

2022-10-18 07:15:00

Mumbai: Diwali is around the corner, and as D-Street investors are gearing up to pick stocks that could turn multibaggers during the new Samvat, let's do a recap of the market performance since the last Samvat. If one looks at the performance across largecap, midcap, and smallcap segments, then the third segment has by all means outpaced the other two. Data shows that 50 smallcap stocks turned multibaggers since last Diwali. Among these, 31 stocks have actually given more than 100% returns for two straight years.
