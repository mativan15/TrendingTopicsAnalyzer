Citywest reception hub for refugees closed to new arrivals after reaching capacity
irishtimes.com
news
2022-10-20 00:00:00

The Citywest reception hub for refugees has been closed to new arrivals as the centre has reached capacity, the Government has announced.The announcement was made this afternoon as the Government predicts a shortfall of 15,000 places for refugees by December amid continuing pressure on the accommodation system.In a statement this afternoon, the Department of Children said that 'in the interests of the welfare of those presently at Citywest awaiting accommodation, a decision has now been taken to pause arrivals to the transit hub until numbers can reduce.
