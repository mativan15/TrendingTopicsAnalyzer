Cathay General Bancorp Adopts New Share Repurchase Program

May 26, 2022 08:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Cathay General Bancorp (the 'Company', Nasdaq: CATY), the holding company for Cathay Bank, announced that its Board of Directors has adopted a new share repurchase program authorizing the Company to repurchase up to $125,000,000 of the Company's common stock. The previous $125,000,000 share repurchase program announced on September 2, 2021, was completed on February 18, 2022, with the repurchase of 2,858,503 shares at an average cost of $43.
