Millennials and the reasons for growing popularity of luxury housing
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-04 11:34:39

Luxury real estate has emerged as a hot favourite among the new and young home buyers post-pandemic. As per the recent trends discerned in the home buying patterns of property buyers, there is an upward swing in the number of millennials (age bracket of 25 to 35 years) purchasing luxury homes in recent times. The millennials are tech-savvy, appreciative of fine living, high-income individuals who have a predilection for a luxe concoction of grandeur house designs and modern facilities. The market is also witnessing change of mindset from splitting property investment at several locations to opting for a good one time investment that assures lifetime luxury and settlement.
