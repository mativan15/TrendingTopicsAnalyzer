Singer KK's sudden death has left the music fraternity in India shocked. Most have taken to their respective social media platforms to express their sorrow and shared their condolences. According to several reports, KK's preliminary post-mortem results indicates that the singer died of cardiac arrest. The report also stated that he had weak lungs and liver. He was performing live at a concert in Nazrul Mancha, Kolkata, shortly before his death.
Many of those who were present at the concert have pointed out the general mismanagement at the venue.
