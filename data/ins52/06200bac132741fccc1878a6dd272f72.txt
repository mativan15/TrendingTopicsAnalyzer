Leading sportswear brand Li-Ning has started its own freshly-brewed coffee business has made it a new contender to the competitive on-premise coffee sector, but it's also left a question mark on whether such a crossover will work. The sports brand has recently applied to register its brand as 'Ning Coffee' and has already operated coffee services in Beijing, Xiamen of Fujian province and Zhanjiang of Guangdong province. Most of its coffee businesses are expected to be included inside its brick-and-mortar sportswear stores, which are numbered at more than 7,000.
