MEDIROM Healthcare says customers and sales per customer rose in September

knape/E+ via Getty Images MEDIROM Healthcare Technologies (NASDAQ:MRM), a health services provider in Japan, on Thursday said its number of relaxation salons decreased to 312 in Sept., down from 316 a year ago. Apart from the relaxation salons business, which includes leading bran Re.Ra.Ku, MRM also offers products such as a training app called Lav and a smart tracker device called MOTHER Bracelet. MEDIROM (MRM) said total customers served by the company increased to ~71.8K in Sept. from about 65.
