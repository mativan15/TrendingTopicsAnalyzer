Jeweler in San Francisco's Marina closes shop after multiple burglaries

By Shawn Chitnis June 3, 2022 / 5:59 PM / CBS San Francisco SAN FRANCISCO -- A San Francisco jewelry designer and shop owner says she's closing her business after three burglaries in less than a year, with the most recent burglary happening in the last week. Meg Shackleton turned a college hobby into a successful business. For the last decade, she has run Margaret Elizabeth with its own SF store to showcase her jewelry designs on Chestnut Street.  Margaret Elizabeth jewelry shop in SF set to close.
