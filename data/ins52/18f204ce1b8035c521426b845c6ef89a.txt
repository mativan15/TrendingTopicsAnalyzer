Delhi News Live Updates: Don't compare anyone to Bhagat Singh for political gains, Gautam Gambhir tells Kejriwal
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-19 03:02:39

Delhi News Today Live Updates, October 19: East Delhi MP Gautam Gambhir Tuesday hit out at Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal for comparing deputy CM Manish Sisodia with freedom fighter Bhagat Singh and said no one should be compared to Bhagat Singh for political gains. 'One should do politics on one's own potential. Shaheed Bhagat Singh is bigger than any position, any chair. I request everyone whether it is Delhi CM or any politician that Shaheed Bhagat Singh should not be compared for ones political gains,' he said.
