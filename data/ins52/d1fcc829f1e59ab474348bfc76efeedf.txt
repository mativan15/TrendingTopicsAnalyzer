The Drink Champs interview where Kanye West continued to air antisemitic remarks and spread misinformation about George Floyd's death has been pulled from YouTube, Rolling Stone reports.The YouTube show's host, N.O.R.E., apologised for the contents of the interview that he said 'hurt' people during a Monday morning radio show appearance. Hours later, the video had been made private on YouTube while the podcast version of the conversation was removed from both Spotify and Apple Music earlier in the day.
