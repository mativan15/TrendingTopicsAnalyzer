Major Milwaukee-area employer names new CEO
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-20 19:45:00

Financial technology firm FIS announced this week Stephanie Ferris would be taking over the role of CEO from Gary Norcross, who has served as the company's CEO for the last eight years and chairman since 2018.

FIS (NYSE: FIS) is based in Jacksonville, Florida, but has 3,000 Milwaukee-area employees, according to Milwaukee Business Journal research. It has an office in Brown Deer and also occupies space in other Milwaukee office complexes, Business Journal research shows.

Ferris will continue her role as president as well as assume Norcross' role of CEO as he takes on the role of executive chairman of the board.
