By Kathryn KyteBusiness reporterImage source, Getty ImagesImage caption, Most of what we look at on the internet is trackedThe details of what each and all of us look at online is an incredibly valuable resource.This tracked data helps the likes of Google and Facebook earn billions and billions of dollars a year in advertising revenue, as they use the information to target adverts at us.For example, if you are browsing online fashion retailers to potentially buy a new pair jeans, you should very soon see adverts for the denim trousers appearing elsewhere on your computer screen.
