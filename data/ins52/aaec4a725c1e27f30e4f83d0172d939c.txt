New tech should not be only option for farmers to meet their emission targets: Commission
Stuff Limited.
news
2022-05-29 20:44:23

Government's emission reduction plan focussed too much on new technologies to help farmers, Climate Change Commission says There are improvements to on-farm management that could help meet the 2030 biogenic methane target Some feel farmers got off 'scot-free' in the plan New technologies cannot be the country's only hope to reach emission targets from agriculture, the Climate Change Commission says. The commission reacted after Agriculture Minister Damien O'Connor announced $339 million was earmarked to speed up technologies that would reduce agricultural greenhouse gas emissions.
