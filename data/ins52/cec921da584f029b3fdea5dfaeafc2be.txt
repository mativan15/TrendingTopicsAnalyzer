RAJKOT: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday inaugurated a multi-specialty hospital at Aatkot village under Jasdan taluka of Rajkot district and addressed a huge gathering.The K D Paravadiya multi speciality hospital was built by BJP leader Bharat Boghra and run by Patel Samaj in Aatkot which is 55 km far from Rajkot city. According to organisers around 2 lakh people gathered to hear the address of the PM from all the communities and BJP workers from all over the state.
