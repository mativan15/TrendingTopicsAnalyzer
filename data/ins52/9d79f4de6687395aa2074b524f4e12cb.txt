Intervention in Haiti: Can the world respond without interfering?

2022-10-18 17:47:43

The Haitian government is calling for international military intervention to avoid a 'major humanitarian crisis.' Armed gangs closed access to one of the country's main fuel terminals, forcing gas stations to shutter and hindering essential services like banks and hospitals.Does Haiti need intervention? What has gone wrong – and right – with the long history of international intervention there? And is there an international responsibility to intervene? Haiti's government is asking for international military help to regain control from violent gangs that have immobilized the capital city, Port-au-Prince.
