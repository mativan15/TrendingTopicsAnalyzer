British Prime Minister Liz Truss announces her resignation, outside Number 10 Downing Street, London on Oct. 20.TOBY MELVILLE/ReutersAre we not entertained?The resignation on Thursday of British Prime Minister Liz Truss after 45 calamity-ridden days in office was a source of hilarity for many people. A London tabloid, the Daily Star, last week placed a head of iceberg on a table beside a photo of Ms. Truss and asked, 'Which wet lettuce will last longer?' When the unrefrigerated greens handily won the contest, it was salad days for punsters on social media.
