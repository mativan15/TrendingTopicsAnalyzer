POLITICO Journalism Institute Applications Now Open

We are excited to announce that applications for the 2023 session of POLITICO Journalism Institute (PJI) are now open!

PJI is a 10-day program for young professionals who want to improve their skills and learn about the world of journalism in one of the largest politics and policy-focused newsrooms in America. The goal of PJI is to train the next generation of journalists while also promoting diversity throughout newsrooms.
