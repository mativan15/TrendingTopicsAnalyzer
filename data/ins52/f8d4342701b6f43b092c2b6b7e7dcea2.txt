Associate of ex-Rangers owner faces fraud charge
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-20 23:01:00

A business associate of the former Rangers owner Craig Whyte has appeared in court accused of fraudulent trading.Aidan Earley, 55, had a long history of working together with Whyte on various ventures which included looking into establishing a feeder academy in the south of England for the Ibrox club.Whyte bought Rangers from Sir David Murray in 2011 but the business went into administration the following year after failing to pay VAT and other taxes.Yesterday Earley appeared in an English court along with Richard Spurway, 68, Douglas Ware, 71, and Allan Biggar, 62, for allegedly concealing the insolvency of Worthington Group plc from shareholders and the London stock exchange.
