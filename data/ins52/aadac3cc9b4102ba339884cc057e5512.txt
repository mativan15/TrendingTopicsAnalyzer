The picture is looking healthier at Netflix
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 20:55:00

Netflix expects to add 4.5 million subscribers over the coming months as the world's largest streaming service incorporates advertising in an effort to drive growth.The entertainment platform's paying audience rose by 2.41 million in the last quarter, drawing a line under two consecutive quarters of contraction.Shares in the business, which had fallen by 60 per cent since the start of the year, rose by 14.4 per cent, or $34.64, to $275.50 in New York last night during late trading on Wall Street.
