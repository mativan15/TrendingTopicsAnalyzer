October 19, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time GHENT, Belgium--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pozyx, an industry-leading provider of RTLS (real-time location systems) and asset tracking for smart manufacturing, has announced the appointment of Rick Graham as Chief Executive Officer. As the company gains international momentum, Graham and his strong leadership team will elevate the company to new heights. With record-breaking sales in the last quarter, Pozyx is well on its way to closing 2022 with stellar results.
