Assurance Partner at BDO USA, LLP
bizjournals.com
business
2022-10-18 13:05:20

BDO USA, LLP has named Asim Khan a Partner in the firm's Assurance practice. Khan is the Southwest region Information Systems (IS) Assurance practice leader, responsible for business development, talent development, and delivery of IS assurance services. He is also the national IS Assurance Learning & Development initiative champion and works closely with Assurance leadership to drive an integrated approach, resulting in best-in-class learning and development.
