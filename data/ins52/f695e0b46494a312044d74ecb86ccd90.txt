K.J. Duff pulled in his tenth offer on Saturday afternoon, this one from Syracuse.
A consensus three-star recruit, Duff is a 6-foot-5 wide receiver from Holy Trinity (Melville, N.Y.). He counts Power Five offers from Rutgers as well as Maryland, Pittsburgh and West Virginia.

Last month, Duff spoke with RutgersWire, providing an update on his recruitment. He said that a number of programs were showing sustained interest including Syracuse. He also listed programs like Florida State, Kansas, Penn State and Virginia Tech among those interested in him.
