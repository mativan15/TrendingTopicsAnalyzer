'Whatever it takes!' Chancellor urged to help businesses deal with cost of living crisis
express.co.uk
news
2022-06-06 02:48:00

Business leaders are calling for the Chancellor to help firms cope with the cost of living crisis as penny-pinching Brits continue to be hit as prices soar. The fresh calls come as the Chancellor prepares for a grilling from Parliament's Treasury committee later today.Mr Sunak is expected to face MPs at 1.45pm.The committee, chaired by Central Devon MP Mel Stride, includes 11 MPs.Commenting ahead of the session, Mr Stride said: 'It is very welcome to see such a bold package introduced by the Chancellor to support households with the rising cost of living.
