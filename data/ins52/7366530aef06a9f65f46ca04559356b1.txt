Bihar: Government School Question Paper Describes Kashmir As Separate Country; Probe Ordered
ndtv.com
news
2022-10-20 03:34:53

Patna City: A recent Class-7 question paper circulated in Bihar government schools allegedly described Kashmir as a separate country, prompting the Education Department to order a probe, officials said Wednesday. The claim that Kashmir is not a part of India was made in the English mid-term examination paper approved by the Bihar Education Project Council, and distributed among students in Araria, Kishanganj and Katihar districts, they said. Dipak Kumar Singh, Additional Chief Secretary (Education Department), told PTI, 'We have ordered a probe into the matter and strict action will be taken against those who are found guilty.
