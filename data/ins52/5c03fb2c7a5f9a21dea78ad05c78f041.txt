ConocoPhillips stock rises Friday, outperforms market

Shares of ConocoPhillips COP, +2.75% advanced 2.75% to $118.12 Friday, on what proved to be an all-around grim trading session for the stock market, with the S&P 500 Index SPX, -1.63% falling 1.63% to 4,108.54 and Dow Jones Industrial Average DJIA, -1.05% falling 1.05% to 32,899.70. ConocoPhillips hit a new 52-week high, surpassing its previous peak of $117.99, which the company reached on May 31st. The stock outperformed some of its competitors Friday, as Exxon Mobil Corp. XOM, +1.45% rose 1.45% to $99.
