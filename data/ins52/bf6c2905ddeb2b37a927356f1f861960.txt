Opinion: TABOR whiplash is coming once federal dollars run out
denverpost.com
news
2022-05-30 18:38:18

You're right to be confused and angry. Colorado's tax system makes no sense at all.
One second we're celebrating $500 tax rebates on the West Steps, the next we're complaining about our ranking as the 47th worst place for teacher pay. In one room, someone is thrilled about the Lexus lanes on I-70, in another people are complaining about how bad traffic is on I-25. One year, your income taxes are going down, the next year your local sales and property taxes go up. Every year there's another question for an important thing we thought we had funded.
