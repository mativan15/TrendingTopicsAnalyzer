What homeowners should know about consolidating debt into a mortgage
foxbusiness.com
news
2022-10-20 13:11:48

Household debt has increased in recent years, reaching record highs in 2022, according to Federal Reserve data. Not only can these balances be costly, but many forms of consumer debt — like student loans — can also affect your ability to buy a home. Even though mortgage rates have climbed throughout 2022, they're still lower than rates for other types of debt, such as credit cards. Keep reading to find out whether or not it's possible to consolidate your existing debt into a new mortgage, and why you might want to do so.
