Diablo Immortal Blizzard 
If you've been playing Diablo Immortal the last day or two, you've probably noticed that you're positively flying through it. So many levels and skills unlocked! The way it's set up, it's easy to hit level 20, 25 or even 30 in just a handful of play sessions. But eventually…you'll hit the wall. The good old 'mobile games' wall, and something not present in past Diablo games. This is where leveling slows down to a crawl and you have to be a lot more…targeted in terms of how you progress.
