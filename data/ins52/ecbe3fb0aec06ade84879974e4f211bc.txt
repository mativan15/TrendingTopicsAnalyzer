By Reuters Published: 06:05 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 06:05 EDT, 16 October 2022 Oct 16 (Reuters) - British commercial broadcaster ITV is reviewing the future of its production arm ITV studios, including whether to sell a stake, the Financial Times reported on Sunday. Britain's biggest free-to-air commercial broadcaster has recently fielded expressions of interest in ITV Studios, the report said citing people familiar with the discussions. Potential buyers include private equity groups and other large independent producers such as Bertelsmann's Freemantle or FL Entertainment, the parent company of Banijay, the newspaper added.
