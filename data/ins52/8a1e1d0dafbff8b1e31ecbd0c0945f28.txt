A (bus) driving force for school kids
startribune.com
news
2022-06-03 07:30:00

By temperament and training, Hal Barnes was the quintessential advertising guy: creative and competitive, hard-driving and award-winning. He defined himself by what he did. So former colleagues might raise an eyebrow or two at what the long-retired ad executive is doing now. The crusty curmudgeon, who admits he "never particularly liked kids," is now driving them around in four shifts a day as a bus driver for Safe-Way, a Wisconsin-based student transportation company. His riders range in age from 5-year-old kindergartners to high school seniors.
