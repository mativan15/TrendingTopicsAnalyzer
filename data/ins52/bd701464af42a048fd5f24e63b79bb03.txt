BJP leader Manjinder Singh Sirsa on Sunday said the Centre is ready to get the killing of Punjabi singer Sidhu Moosewala probed by a central agency like NIA if the Punjab government recommends it. Sirsa visited Moosewala's village in Mansa and met the singer's family to express his condolences. Asked to comment on the demand of the singer's family to get the death investigated by a central agency, the BJP leader told reporters, "If they want it to be probed by the National Investigation Agency (NIA), we will get it probed.
