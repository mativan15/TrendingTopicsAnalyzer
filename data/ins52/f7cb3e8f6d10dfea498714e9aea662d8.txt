Published: 11:13 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 11:26 EDT, 16 October 2022 Roadworks to repair a slightly raised manhole cover, which should have been 'a three-hour job', have caused fury for business owners and drivers after blocking a busy road for 10 days.A lane of the busy A31 near Wimborne, Dorset, was coned off by engineers on October 6, and is not expected to be reopened until tomorrow evening.But contractors for BT Openreach have failed to turn up to do the job, leaving an empty site being controlled by temporary traffic lights.
