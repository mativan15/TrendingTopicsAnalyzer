Leader of the Opposition in the Rajya Sabha Mallikarjun Kharge, who is contesting the election for the AICC president's post, on Sunday said the RSS-backed BJP government at the Centre was not functioning as per the Constitution and alleged that it was misusing the investigating agencies to destabilise the Congress-ruled governments in States.
