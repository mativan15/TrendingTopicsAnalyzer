Royal Mail could introduce Sunday post delivery in major shake-up
thescottishsun.co.uk
news
2022-05-30 08:30:32

ROYAL Mail is paving the way for a Sunday parcel delivery service in a bid to try and keep pace with online rivals.
Bosses have drawn up a "road map" to put the business ahead of Amazon, DPD and Evri (formerly Hermes) with a Sunday delivery service ten times bigger than it currently is.
Royal Mail are drawing up plans to offer a Sunday parcel delivery serviceCredit: Getty
The 506-year-old business has branded the plan a "revolution" similar to the end of trading restrictions for shops in the 1990s, reports the Daily Mail.
