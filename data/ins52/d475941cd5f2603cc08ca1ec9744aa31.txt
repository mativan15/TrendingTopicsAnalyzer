Variety - TV News 'Cha Cha Real Smooth' and 'Star Trek: Strange New Worlds' are among the recent honorees of the Ruderman Family Foundation's Seal of Authentic Representation, Variety has learned exclusively.The Foundation recognizes films and television series that include authentic representation of people with disabilities. Vanessa Burghardt, an actor with autism, plays Domino's (Dakota Johnson) autistic daughter Lola in the comedy-drama film 'Cha Cha Real Smooth.
