Germany's Scholz outlines Bundeswehr upgrade plans
dw.com
news
2022-06-01 08:15:00

German Chancellor Olaf Scholz is expected to tell the German parliament how the country will go about upgrading and updating its military. 

The German chancellor was addressing the German parliament's lower chamber on Wednesday about his government's spending priorities.
Scholz was expected to outline how Germany is approaching the Ukraine war and its effects on the wider economy and Germany's public spending budget.
He was also set to share details on how a €100 billion fund would be used to bring the German military's weapons and equipment up to scratch.
