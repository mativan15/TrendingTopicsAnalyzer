Paddy Farmers in Tamil Nadu Incur Huge Losses After Heavy Pre-Northeast Monsoon Showers Rattle the South Indian State
weather.com
news
2022-10-15 00:00:00

(Suman Reddy D/TOI,BCCL)Heavy showers in several parts of Tamil Nadu have impacted the Kurvai paddy farmers of the state.With the farmers in harvest mode and storing the grains, heavy rains before the advent of the northeast monsoon season by October-end have led to huge losses to the farmers.Kumaresan, a paddy farmer from Erode district of Tamil Nadu, told IANS: "We are in harvest mode now, and the harvesting machines cannot be used in the fields inundated by rainwater. This is going to be our waterloo as the District Procuring Centres will also not take our products as the moisture content is high due to the rains.
