Flexport Receives $200 Million From KKR to Grow Trade Financing
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-20 11:16:47

(Bloomberg) -- Flexport Inc.'s trade financing arm received $200 million from KKR & Co. to expand the digital freight company's reach into the growing market for supply chain lending.The credit facility from the New York-based investment firm is Flexport Capital's first outside funding since its inception in 2017. The unit has financed more than $1 billion in invoices for hundreds of companies across more than 20 countries, with a portfolio that's up almost 150% year over year, Flexport said in a statement Thursday.
