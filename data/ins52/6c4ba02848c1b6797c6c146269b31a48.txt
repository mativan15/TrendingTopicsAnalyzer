Woman made drugs in her kitchen and sold them from window

Jayne Jones, 56, has been sentenced to imprisonment (Image: South Wales Police)A woman who made crack cocaine in the kitchen and sold it to addicts through her window has been locked up.Jayne Jones, 56, used a number of implements in her home to "wash" cocaine - turning it into crack cocaine - and flogged it from her window as part of her "established business". A court heard Jones kept a diary ledger, which showed amounts of drugs supplied and cash owed.But police raided her property in Swansea, south Wales, and found a stash of high-purity cocaine worth almost £9,000 along with rocks of crack, dealer's lists, and a burner phone.
