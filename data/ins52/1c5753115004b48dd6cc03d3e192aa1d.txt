Women-led Early Stage VC Fund Good Growth Capital Announces Over-subscribed Third Fund
prnewswire.com
news
2022-05-27 12:05:00

Good Growth Capital invests in pre-seed through Series B investments in biomedical and health sciences, green tech, data, and physical and material sciences.  Sixty percent of Good Growth's Managing Partners are female and twenty percent are Latino, and the firm reports more than 75% of Good Growth Capital's portfolio is led by founders from diverse backgrounds. "This is the future of venture capital," says Good Growth Managing Partner David Mendez.  "We don't need to give these founders any preference, just a level playing field.
