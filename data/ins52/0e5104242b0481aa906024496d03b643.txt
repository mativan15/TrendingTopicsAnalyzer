The housemates will experience a major shock after Bigg Boss takes a never-seen-before decision. The latest teaser of the upcoming episode shows Bigg Boss summoning all the housemates in the garden area, opening the main door for them, and asking them to leave if they are not interested in the game. This will leave the housemates shocked. The teaser video shows the contestants dressed according to the theme of the new captaincy contender task and trying to entertain with their respective avatars.
