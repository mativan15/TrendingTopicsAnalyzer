BMW's Smallest SUV Gets Bigger With Introduction of 2023 X1
newsweek.com
news
2022-06-01 02:58:16

The smallest member of the BMW SUV family has undergone a generational redesign that has made it bigger and loaded it up with high-tech features. The 2023 BMW X1 arrives on dealer lots in the fourth quarter of 2022.BMW's design team has given the X1 a body similar to that of the X3 and X5, continuing the tradition of keeping the company's boxier SUV's looks close. It has a pronounced front end, more traditional kidney grille than on the iX.Its closest competitors are now and will continue to be the Volvo XC40, Audi Q3 and Cadillac XT4.
