NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Vornado Realty Trust (NYSE:VNO) announced today that it will file its quarterly report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended September 30, 2022 with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission and issue its third quarter earnings release on Monday, October 31, 2022, after the New York Stock Exchange has closed. The Company will host a quarterly earnings conference call and an audio webcast on Tuesday, November 1, 2022 at 10:00 a.m. Eastern Time (ET).
