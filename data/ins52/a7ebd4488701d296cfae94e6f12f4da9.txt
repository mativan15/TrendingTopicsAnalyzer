As a rule, it's best to approach questions about the cost of Secret Service protections with some caution. Elected leaders and their families often receive very serious threats, and it's important for the U.S. government to ensure their safety with highly trained personnel.There is, however, something qualitatively different about Donald Trump — because he's been in a position to profit from the circumstances.The public has been assured that this should not be an area of concern. In fact, Eric Trump — one of the former president's sons and an executive at the Trump Organization — has claimed that the company provided hotel rooms for Secret Service agents 'at cost,' or in some cases, for free.
