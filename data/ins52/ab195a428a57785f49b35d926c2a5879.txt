Tata Elxsi falls 8% as analysts cut targets on weak Q2 results

AgenciesAt its current price, the stock trades nearly 60 times its one-year forward price-to-earnings (P/E) and a rich premium compared with its average P/E of 41.45 times over the trailing five-year period, Bloomberg data showed.Mumbai: Shares of Tata Elxsi, the design and technology services provider, slumped over 8% on Monday as analysts trimmed price targets after the company reported a decline in quarterly net profit - the first such fall since June last year. Analysts mostly maintained a sell rating on the stock citing rich valuations.
