AM Best Places Credit Ratings of Hotai Insurance Co., Ltd. Under Review With Negative Implications
apnews.com
business
2022-06-02 14:25:02

HONG KONG--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Jun 2, 2022--AM Best has placed under review with negative implications the Financial Strength Rating of A (Excellent) and the Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of 'a' (Excellent) of Hotai Insurance Co., Ltd. (Hotai Insurance) (Taiwan), in view of the company's exposure to potentially significant claims related to pandemic insurance policies amid the deteriorated COVID-19 situation in Taiwan, and uncertainty of the magnitude of negative impact to the company's credit fundamentals.
