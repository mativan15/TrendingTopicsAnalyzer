Ulta, Bath & Body Works, CeraVe, E.l.f. Cosmetics, and SheaMoisture among beauty favorites for female teens, survey finds

Ulta, Bath & Body Works, E.l.f. Cosmetics, SheaMoisture, and CeraVe are the most popular beauty retailers and brands for female teens in the US, as revealed in Piper Sandler's semiannual Gen Z survey. The investment bank's "Taking Stock with Teens" report tracks brand preferences, behavior, and habits – including spending on cosmetics, skincare, fragrance, and hair care – and where teens most like to shop.Of all the beauty categories, the teens spent the most on skincare: $103 a year. Cosmetics followed at $96, but those who report wearing makeup every day spent $155 a year on cosmetics.
