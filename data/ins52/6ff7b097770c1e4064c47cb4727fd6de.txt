"Cut it Out," digital painting by Marco Melgrati. 2022. MARCO MELGRATI Mahsa Amini, a 22-year-old Iranian student who was arrested by the morality police because of her "inappropriate" clothing and fell into a coma three days after being detained, died in a Tehran hospital on September 16. Following her death, the number of demonstrations and rallies against the regime has increased. The Iranian government has responded with a massive crackdown that has already led to at least 200 civilians being killed.
