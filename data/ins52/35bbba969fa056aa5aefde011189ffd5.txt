NEW CITY, N.Y., Oct. 21, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Web3Port's first Demo Day on Oct. 19 was closed successfully in collaboration with Web3Port's co-hosts including Kucoin Ventures, Hashkey Capital, SNZ Holding, Spark Digital Capital, ByteTrade- Sig Crypto Lab, Cabin VC, AC Capital, and Geekcartel. Web3Port's Demo Day series aims to connect Web3 startups with top Web3 VCs to help the early-stage projects gain access to feedback and resources to grow. Seven projects from three major tracks presented their idea and product roadmap to an audience of over 100 VCs.
