Watching an action movie shouldn't feel like a chore, but Black Adam does. Amid a slew of publicly damned decisions, Warner Bros. has released a DC Extended Universe movie that is more exhausting than exciting, spooling out tedious exposition alongside ugly action for a muddled mess of a movie that squanders its big budget and the promising star power of Dwayne Johnson, Aldis Hodge, and Noah Centineo. So, Warner Bros. will serve this up in theaters, but won't give us a streaming Batgirl? What gives? What is Black Adam about?
