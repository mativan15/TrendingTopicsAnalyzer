Propylon named one of the Best Workplaces in Technology by Great Place to Work®
prweb.com
news
2022-06-04 12:00:00

DUBLIN (PRWEB) June 04, 2022 Propylon, a leading software provider at the intersection between laws and regulations, has officially been recognized as a Best Workplace in Technology by Great Place to Work®. To determine the Best Workplaces in Technology list, Great Places to Work utilizes the global standard Best Workplaces methodology as its basis. Propylon features alongside some of the biggest names in technology including, Adobe, Salesforce, Cisco, and others. This news follows hot on the heels of the company's top three ranking in its category at the 20th annual Great Place to Work® Awards.
