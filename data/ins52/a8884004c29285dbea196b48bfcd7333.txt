Ed-tech companies are the prime end-users in the market. The segment is driven by the increased need for personalized learning, especially in the K-12 and higher education sectors. In addition, developments in the education sector such as high broadband penetration, widespread education networks, a large number of technology deployments in schools, adoption of content digitization, shortage of teachers, new government education policies, and high enrollment in online higher education are contributing to the growth of the segment.
