Quebec premier under fire for promising another inflation cheque, if re-elected
globalnews.ca
news
2022-06-01 15:16:41

The province's premier has come under fire for pledging to send out another cheque to Quebecers to help offset the impact of inflation ⁠— but only if his party is re-elected next fall.
François Legault made the comment during question period at the national assembly Tuesday. He said Coalition Avenir Québec members discussed the rising cost of living during its convention over the weekend.'There will be an election campaign,' Legault said in the provincial legislature. 'The Liberal Party is proposing to lift sales taxes on certain products.
