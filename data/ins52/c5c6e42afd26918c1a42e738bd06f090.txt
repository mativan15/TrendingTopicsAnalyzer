Lonestar Land at Circuit of The Americas offers the best local BBQ Texas has to offer to F1 fans.Circuit of The Americas
The Formula 1 Aramco United States Grand Prix brings one of the biggest weekends of entertainment to Austin, Texas. And while race activities and activations dominate the scene, Circuit of The Americas (COTA) isn't just the ultimate destination for racing, it's also a hub for all things Texas cuisine. The race welcomes hundreds of thousands of fans from across the world each year, and is the perfect opportunity to showcase the best eats from across the Lone Star State.
