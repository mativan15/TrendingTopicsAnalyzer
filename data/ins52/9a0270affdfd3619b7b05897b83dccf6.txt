RT.com17 Oct 2022, 06:15 GMT+10 Guatemala's committee was suspended after a key deadline was missedThe International Olympic Committee (IOC) banned Guatemala's Olympic Committee (NOC) on Sunday due to a failure to solve a legal dispute.The IOC warned the Guatemalan Olympic Committee last month that it would be banned if it did not resolve the issue by October 15.The NOC has been in a dispute with the Central American country's Constitutional Court in relation to some provisions in the NOC's regulations.
