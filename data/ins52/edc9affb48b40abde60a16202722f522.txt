Published: 01:54 EDT, 22 October 2022 | Updated: 01:58 EDT, 22 October 2022 A San Francisco tech entrepreneur has shared startling scenes of the deserted downtown of her city, with the previously bustling streets void of people and eerily quiet.Michelle Tandler, founder of professional development firm Growth Path, posted the images to her Twitter account.'This is downtown San Francisco, Monday morning at 9:20am,' she captioned one at the beginning of this week.Her photos showed the streets to be largely empty, with only a scattering of people, and very few cars.
