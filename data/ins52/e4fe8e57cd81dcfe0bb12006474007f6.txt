Cat's response to owner's new baby hailed as relatable: "Won't look at us"
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-17 14:15:55

The internet was left in stitches by a cat who won't look at her owners anymore because they brought home a baby and she's not happy about it.The viral video, shared on TikTok by the cat's owner under the username novels_and_coffee, shows her laying on her bed, facing the wall, refusing to look at her owners after they brought their baby home.Originally shared on the platform on Friday, the video, which has so far reached over 1.8 million views and 208,500 likes, was captioned: "She's mad at us for bringing a baby home and won't look at us.
