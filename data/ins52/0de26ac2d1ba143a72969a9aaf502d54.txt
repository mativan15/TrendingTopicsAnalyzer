(PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Valence Surface Technologies LLC ('Valence') is pleased to announce our Garden Grove, CALIFORNIA (formerly known as Coastline Metal Finishing) gained six new paint approvals from Raytheon Technologies (NYSE:RTX) as well as new coating approval from Boeing Satellite (NYSE: BOE).
