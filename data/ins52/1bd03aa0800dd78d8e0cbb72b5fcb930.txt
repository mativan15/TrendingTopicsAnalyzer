Wells Fargo Told by Senator to Fix Its Risk-Management Practices

Risk & Compliance Journal Photo: Andrew Harrer/Bloomberg News Wells Fargo & Co. must fix its governance and risk-management issues, the chairman of the U.S. Senate Banking Committee said, highlighting what he called a 'laundry list' of consumer abuses and compliance breakdowns. The bank has been plagued by weaknesses in its governance and risk-management practices for nearly a decade, Sherrod Brown (D., Ohio) said in an open letter sent Tuesday to Wells Fargo Chief Executive Charles Scharf.
