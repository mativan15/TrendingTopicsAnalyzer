It seems like Black Friday sales kicked off earlier than ever this year — and if you haven't been keeping an eye out, you may have already missed some of them. Target held its Deal Days event at the beginning of October, and Amazon's Prime Early Access Sale kicked off just a few days later. Now, Walmart joins the list of retailers letting shoppers in on Black Friday savings way ahead of time.Walmart's Black Friday sale, known as its Deals for Days event, will kick off on Monday, Nov. 7 at 12 p.m.
