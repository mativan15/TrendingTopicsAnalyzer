PTIBJP President JP Nadda.Amid the infighting and factionalism, the BJP central leadership has in a strong message to its Chhattisgarh unit asked it to put its house in order before next year's assembly polls. The party is exploring the possibility of new leadership in Chhattisgarh before the assembly polls. The BJP ruled Chhattisgarh for 15 years from 2003 to 2018. Party national vice president Dr Raman Singh was chief minister for 15 years. BJP politics in Chhattisgarh revolved around Singh for almost two decades and he still wields some influence.
