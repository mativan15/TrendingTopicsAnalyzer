Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami Pushkar Singh Dhami, the Chief Minister of Uttarakhand, won the Champawat bypoll by a margin of 55,000 votes. The chief Minister tweeted after his glorious win saying ''My heart is very emotional with the love and blessings showered by you through votes in Champawat by-election, I am silent,'. Pushkar Singh Dhami defeated the competitors Nirmala Gahtori of the Congress Party, Manoj Kumar Bhatt of the Samajwadi Party, and Himanshu Garkoti, an independent candidate.
