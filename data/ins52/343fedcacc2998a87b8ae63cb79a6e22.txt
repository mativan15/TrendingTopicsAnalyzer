The staggering quantity of cash, much of which appeared to be in the form of $100 bills, had been stuffed inside one of the sofa's cushionsIt is unclear why the owner concealed the cash, which totalled around $36,000 (£28,000) A woman found approximately £28,000 stuffed in a sofa she bought off Craigslist. Vicky Umodu from Colton, California, discovered the wads of cash inside one of the cushions after finding the set of sofas during a hunt for furniture to fill her new home, reported ABC7. Umodo was delighted to find them.
