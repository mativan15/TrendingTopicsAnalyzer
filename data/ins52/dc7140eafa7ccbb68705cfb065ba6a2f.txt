Florida-based IMO with winning pedigree further bolsters Integrity's mission to innovate life, health and wealth solutions that help Americans make the most of what life brings

DALLAS, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Integrity Marketing Group, LLC ("Integrity"), a leading distributor of life and health insurance, and provider of wealth management and retirement planning solutions, today announced it has acquired Abernathy Financial Services, an independent marketing organization ("IMO") based in Fort Walton Beach, Florida.
