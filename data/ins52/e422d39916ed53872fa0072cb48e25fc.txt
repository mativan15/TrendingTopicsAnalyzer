Photo by Dragos Gontariu on UnsplashHow many of you feel like you've lost that creative edge you used to have when you were a kid? That feeling of excitement and energy that helped to propel you out of bed in the morning. Whether it was getting immersed in the world and story of a book or video game, or creating scrapbooks or imagining where you would be when you were all grown up and had it all figured out. Or maybe it was just trying a new thing every day without worrying about what it meant or whether it was a waste of time or not.
