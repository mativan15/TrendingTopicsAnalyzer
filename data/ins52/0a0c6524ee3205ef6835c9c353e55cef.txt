'The Department of Time's' Onza Unveils Development and Co-Production Pact with Intrepidus
variety.com
news
2022-10-21 08:24:19

Spain's Onza Entertainment, producer of 'The Department of Time,' 'Little Coincidences' and 'Parot,' is joining forces with outfit Intrépidus to develop and co-produce TV fiction and films for the Spanish and the international markets.  With the deal, Onza continues putting creative talent at its core and strengthening its creators network. Recently launched by Fernando J. Múñez, writer and creator of Atresmedia and Netflix period TV series 'La Cocinera de Castamar,' actor Rodolfo Sancho and executive producer Esteban Zabala, Intrépidus specializes in screenplay creation and audiovisual contents' development and production.
