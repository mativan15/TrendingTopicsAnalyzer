 A former school bus driver in the Halton Region has been charged in connection with a sexual assault investigation, police say.
Halton Regional Police said on Oct. 7, officers arrested 80-year-old Gordon Jack from Aylmer, Ont.Officers said he has been charged with sexual assault and sexual interference.
