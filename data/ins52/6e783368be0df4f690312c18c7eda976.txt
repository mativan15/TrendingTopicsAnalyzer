BYD opens new PV showroom in Indore with Shubh

2022-10-17 10:15:00

BYD India was established in March 2007 in Chennai with an office in New Delhi.New Delhi: Warren Buffett-backed EV maker BYD on Monday inaugurated a new passenger vehicles showroom in Indore, its ninth outlet in India. According to a media release, the showroom managed by Shubh BYD is spread across 3413 square feet, and has service equipment, EV charging stations, a customer lounge and a showroom display floor.BYD India was established in March 2007 in Chennai with an office in New Delhi. The company has two factories, covering over 140,000 sqm, with a cumulative investment of over USD 200 million.
