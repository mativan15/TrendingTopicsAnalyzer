Virtuzone, in collaboration with Wio Bank, offers entrepreneurs seamless access to banking services through Wio's SME-focused digital banking application, Wio Business.
As a result of this alliance, Virtuzone will equip its business owners with the tools and support they need for efficient financial management and enable them to set up their business bank accounts fast, without the need to visit a branch.
DUBAI, UAE, Oct. 20, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Virtuzone, the UAE's leading Company Formation Specialists and corporate services provider, has joined forces with Wio Bank, the region's first platform bank, to revolutionise the banking process for its clients through the next-generation digital banking solution, Wio Business.
