iStockThe latest version of the Vivo Y33 series has officially arrived in China. The model is part of a variant of the Vivo y33s 5G that was released by Vivo earlier this year. The new variant comes under sub 15K with the intention of launching it as an affordable handset. Powered by a MediaTek Dimension 700 SoC powered by 5G and a durable battery and dual rear camera module, this phone is the latest version of Android. Other features include a 6.51-inch LCD screen with HD + resolution (720x1.600 pixels) and a 60Hz resolution.
