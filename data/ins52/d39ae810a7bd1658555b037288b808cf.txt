WSL: Arsenal beats Reading, Man United goes top with Brighton win
thehindu.com
sport
2022-10-17 04:42:49

A first-half goal from Stina Blackstenius gave Arsenal a 1-0 win away to struggling Reading on Sunday but it wasn't enough to dislodge Manchester United, which is top of the Women's Super League table after a 4-0 win over Brighton & Hove Albion.The Red Devils have nine points after three games with 10 goals scored and none conceded, while Arsenal boasts a similar defensive record and the same number of points, but has scored one goal fewer.Blackstenius fired home from just outside the penalty area on the half-hour mark to give her side the lead, and the Swede hit the woodwork twice in quick succession in the second half.
