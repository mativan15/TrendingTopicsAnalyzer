Images By Tang Ming Tung Necessity Retail (NASDAQ:RTL) is a real estate investment trust which operates diversified retail and office real estate; since its inception in 2013, the company has acquired substantial properties over the period. It currently owns 1056 properties, accounting for about 28.9 million rentable square feet, which includes 944 single-tenant net-leased commercial properties and 112 multi-tenant retail properties. Currently, about 90.8% of the company's portfolio is leased. The company is managed by a third-party advisor and over the period, management has taken many vital decisions which have created good value for the shareholders.
