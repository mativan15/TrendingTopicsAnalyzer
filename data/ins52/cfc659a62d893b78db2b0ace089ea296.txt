The Impact the Metaverse Will Have on Reaching Consumers

The development of marketing typically runs alongside technological developments. We've seen this with the printed press, television, radio and, of course, the internet. And by all accounts, we're now on the cusp of the next great marketing leap forward.Much has been said, mostly just speculatively, about the metaverse and its potentially transformative impact. While most things related to the metaverse are still largely at the conceptual stage, there's enough smoke to suggest that it'll become hot property in the not-too-distant future.
