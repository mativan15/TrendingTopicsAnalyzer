Column: Our public servants are getting old. Are toxic politics scaring away young people?
chicagotribune.com
news
2022-10-20 19:54:59

Younger people these days seem less inclined to seek public office amid a toxic political environment that appears to discourage good people from becoming involved in government.

From the halls of Congress to south and southwest suburban schools, municipalities and other taxing bodies governed by locally elected officials, the median age of public servants seems to be increasing.

'We need a new generation,' U.S. Rep. Elissa Slotkin, D-Mich., said on NBC's 'Meet the Press' Oct. 9. 'We need new blood, in the House, the Senate and White House.
