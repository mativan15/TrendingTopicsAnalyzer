PM Modi to launch 'Rozgar Mela' drive to recruit 10 lakh people
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 11:00:00

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will be launching ' Rozgar Mela ' on October 22 to - a drive to recruit 10 lakh people, the PMO said on Thursday.Modi will be attending the event via video-conference and appointment letters will be given to 75,000 candidates during the ceremony.The PMO said this will be a significant step forward towards fulfilling the continuous commitment of the prime minister to providing job opportunities for the youth and ensuring welfare of citizens.All ministries and departments are working towards filling up existing vacancies against sanctioned posts in a "mission mode", the statement from the Prime Minister's Office said.
