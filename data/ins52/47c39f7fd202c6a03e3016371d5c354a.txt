Pellets are an important wood product Credit: Joel Prince NASF Wood products play an important role in our everyday lives. Maybe for you it's a family heirloom, a beautiful table or cabinet, but have you ever considered that piece of furniture might have come from a forest near you? Forest products, including furniture, help keep our forests healthy. With that in mind, DEC's Forest Utilization program promotes markets for forest products as a way of supporting New York forests for generations to come.
