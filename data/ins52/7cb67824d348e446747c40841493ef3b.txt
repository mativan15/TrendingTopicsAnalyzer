The strategic partnership between India and Russia is evolving in various sectors, from economy to culture. The cooperation between the two countries has great potential. deputy chief minister of the Indian state of Maharashtra Devendra Fadnavis met with representatives of the Diaspora as well as the business community of the two states in Moscow. Rahul Narvekar, speaker of the Maharashtra state legislature,also attended the meeting. It began with the traditional custom of lighting the lamp.
