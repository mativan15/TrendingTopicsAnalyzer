Polio may resurge globally, WHO says, as countries pledge funds
cbc.ca
science
2022-10-18 19:07:00

Doctors say as long as the polio virus still exists somewhere in the world, it can spread to people in other countries. (Eloisa Lopez/Reuters)Various governments pledged $2.6 billion US on Tuesday toward a global plan to again try to eradicate polio, following its comeback, the World Health Organization says.New cases of the disease in previously polio-free countries are a "stark reminder" that if polio is not ended "everywhere, it may resurge globally," WHO Director General Tedros Ghebreyesus said in a statement as the UN body wrapped up its World Health Summit in Berlin.
