Another blank-check merger plan and a new round for a Berkeley robotics startup top the Bay Area's startup and venture news at the beginning of a new week.

Here are the details:

Fundings

Ambi Robotics Inc., Berkeley, $32 million: Tiger Global and Bow Capital co-led the round for this developer of package sorting and goods packing robots. Ahren Innovation Capital and Pitney Bowes Inc. also invested.

SPACs

DiaCarta Inc. on Friday said it has agreed to go public in a merger with HH&L Acquisition Co.
