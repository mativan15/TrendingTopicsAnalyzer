FIBRA Macquarie México Leases First Phase of Its Apodaca Industrial Development Project

October 19, 2022 09:52 PM Eastern Daylight Time MEXICO CITY--(BUSINESS WIRE)--FIBRA Macquarie México (FIBRAMQ) (BMV: FIBRAMQ) announced the execution of a 183 thousand square foot lease for the first phase of its recently completed industrial development in Apodaca, Nuevo Leon in the Monterrey region of Mexico. The building will be 100%-occupied by a United States-based manufacturer of electric vehicle components. The triple-net 10-year lease is U.S. dollar denominated and represents an NOI yield of 10.
