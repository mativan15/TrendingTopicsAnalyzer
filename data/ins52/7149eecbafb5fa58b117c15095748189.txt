Industrials Move Higher as Volatility Continues -- Industrials Roundup
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-17 21:28:00

Shares of industrial and transportation companies rose in another day of outsize moves on Wall Street. The New York Fed's Empire State business conditions index, a gauge of manufacturing activity in the state, fell 7.6 points to negative 9.1 in October, the regional Fed bank said. This is the third straight negative reading. Economists had expected a reading of negative 5.0, according to a survey by The Wall Street Journal. China abruptly delayed the publication of its third-quarter gross domestic product data, a day before it was set to be released, an unusual move as the country's ruling Communist Party stages a key political gathering this week.
