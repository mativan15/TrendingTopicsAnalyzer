By CBSLA Staff June 3, 2022 / 6:52 PM / CBS Los Angeles With the rash of violent home invasions robberies in Riverside, local gun shop owners and conceal-carry instructors said they've seen a major uptick in customers. Riverside has seen four home invasion robberies and 10 burglaries in just the last few weeks. "What they're looking for is money," said Officer Ryan Railsback, who is with the Riverside Police Department.He said detectives have started to see patterns and that the home invaders seem to be two different groups of masked men.
