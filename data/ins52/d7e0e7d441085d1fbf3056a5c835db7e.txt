Sensex gains 650 points, Nifty tops 16,500; IT stocks lead gainers

AgenciesAt 9.30 am, the BSE Sensex was trading 14 points or 0.02 per cent lower at 61,208.20. Benchmark indices opened on a higher note on Monday, tracking a rally in the US stocks on Friday. Asian peers hit a three-week high while the dollar fell to a five-week low as data released in the US last week showed the pace of rise in inflation was slowing. Oil climbed to the $120 a barrel mark amid EU talks on embargo on Russian oil. At 9.20 am, the BSE Sensex was trading 666.15 points or 1.21 per cent higher at 55,550.
