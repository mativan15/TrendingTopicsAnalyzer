The Future 5 recognition further substantiates the value of the platform as well as the other innovative companies on the horizon that are making exceptional advances in the industry. ATLANTA (PRWEB) October 17, 2022 Focal Point, provider of an end-to-end enterprise procurement management platform, today announced it has been included in Spend Matters' Future 5 list. The list recognizes procurement tech solution companies that are less than five years old and maintain clear momentum and a demonstrated path of sustainability.
