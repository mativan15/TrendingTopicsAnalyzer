October 21, 2022 07:30 AM Eastern Daylight Time OAKVILLE, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Harvest Portfolios Group Inc. ('Harvest') announces the following distributions for Harvest ETFs for the month ending October 31, 2022. The distribution will be paid on or about November 9, 2022 to unitholders of record on October 31, 2022 with an ex-dividend date of October 28, 2022.

Harvest has established a Distribution Reinvestment Plan ('DRIP') for certain classes of Harvest ETFs, allowing investors to easily benefit from compounding their distributions on a monthly basis.
