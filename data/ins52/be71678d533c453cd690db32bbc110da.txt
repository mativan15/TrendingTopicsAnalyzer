 Police are seeking the public's assistance in identifying a suspect wanted in connection with a mischief investigation in Toronto.
Toronto police said on Oct. 2, at around 9 p.m., officers received a report of mischief in the Yonge Street and Centre Avenue area.Police said a group of protesters had gathered in the area.
