RELX Underlying Revenue Growth Year to Date +9%
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 06:14:00

By Kyle Morris RELX PLC said Thursday that underlying revenue growth for the year to date across the business was 9% and that it continues to see full-year underlying growth in revenue and adjusted operating profit as momentum remains strong. The FTSE 100-listed information-and-analytics group said underlying revenue growth in the year to date in the risk business was 7% and was 4% in the scientific, technical & medical business.
