October 19, 2022 11:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NASHVILLE, Tenn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Melanie McFaddin, representative of Wiley Bros – Aintree Capital, LLC is pleased to announce its representation of EXB Solutions ('EXB') in its acquisition by Quest Global's U.S. defense subsidiary, Quest Defense Systems & Solutions ('Quest Defense'). EXB's addition expands the Quest Defense's capabilities in software and systems engineering with a particular focus on quality in execution of design, development, verification and validation for safety and mission critical applications.
