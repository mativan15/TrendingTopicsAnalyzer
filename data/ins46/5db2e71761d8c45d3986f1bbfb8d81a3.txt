Published: 22:29 EDT, 4 June 2022 | Updated: 22:29 EDT, 4 June 2022 A youngster was caught on surveillance camera video holding up a Michigan gas station as he demanded the money from the cashier. Footage sees the skinny 12-year-old waiting calmly in line in the town of Hartford, with his hand in his backpack as a customer in front of him pay for gas. When he reaches the front of the line he whips out a pistol from his black bag and demands money. 'Put the money in the bag!' the boys states calmly.
