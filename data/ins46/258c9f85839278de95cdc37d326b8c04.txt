Published: 06:09 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 06:09 EDT, 21 October 2022 Brighton head coach Roberto De Zerbi will stay true to his vision as he looks to get the Seagulls back on the goal trail as they prepare to face Manchester City.
