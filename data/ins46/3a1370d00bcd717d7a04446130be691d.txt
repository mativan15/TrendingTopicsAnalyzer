Media caption, Watch: Sir Keir Starmer renews his call for a general electionSir Keir Starmer has called for an immediate general election, accusing the government of losing "control of itself and the economy".The Labour leader says the Conservatives hit "a new chaotic low" after Wednesday's chaos in Parliament.Speaking at the TUC congress, Sir Keir said a Labour government would deliver "fair pay, good work, and prosperity for all".His election challenge came as more Tory MPs called for the PM to quit.
