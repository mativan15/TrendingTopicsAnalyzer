The Panel: 'Excited about a potential housing market crash'
stuff.co.nz
business
2022-06-03 17:00:00

How are you feeling about house prices at present? If you own a house, are you worried about its value? If you don't, do you think prices are falling enough to allow you to get into the market? Charlotte Fielding is a writer who lives in Wellington with her son and dog. She moved into a house bus to save money on rent. The current house price trends are making me feel more optimistic about being able to own a house. I watched prices skyrocket over the last year or so with utter dismay; it seemed like it would be impossible to ever own a house and therefore have some housing security.
