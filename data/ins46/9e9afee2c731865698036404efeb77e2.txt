By Reuters Published: 21:55 EDT, 16 October 2022 | Updated: 21:55 EDT, 16 October 2022 World No. 1 Iga Swiatek of Poland earned her eighth title of the season by outlasting Croatian qualifier Donna Vekic 6-3, 3-6, 6-0 to win the San Diego Open on Sunday. The final was Swiatek's 64th match victory of the season, the most in a single season since Serena Williams finished 2013 with a whopping 78.
