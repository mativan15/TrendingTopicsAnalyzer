'Relentlessly thinks about herself' Meghan warned against 'appalling tactics' at Jubilee
express.co.uk
news
2022-06-01 17:01:00

Commentator Nile Gardiner hit out at the Duchess of Sussex, saying she "relentlessly thinks about herself". He warned the Duchess against using the celebrations to "advance her own personal interests", describing the concept as "appalling". Meghan and Harry have returned to the UK for the Jubilee celebrations, taking place this weekend.They are expected to attend a limited lineup of events, including a Service of Thanksgiving at St Paul's Cathedral on Friday morning.The Duke and Duchess of Sussex are also planning to attend the BBC's Party at the Palace the following evening.
