From Infosys To Tata Sons: Here's How Much Some Top Indian Companies' CEOs Earn

IT giant Infosys has reportedly raised CEO Salil Parekh's salary by a whopping 88%, and he will be paid nearly ₹79.75 crores as salary after his reappointment, effective July 1st 2022.According to the annual report of the company, his salary has been hiked from ₹42.5 crore (in FY 2021-22) to ₹79.75 crore, implying a hike of nearly 88%.For the financial year 2021-22, he was reportedly paid a total of ₹71.02 crore, of which ₹52.33 crore was for exercising 2,29,792 restricted stock units (RSUs) under the 2015 plan and 1,48,434 RSUs under the 2019 plan.
