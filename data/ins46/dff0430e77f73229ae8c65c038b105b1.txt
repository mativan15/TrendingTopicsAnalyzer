20Cube Logistics to go Public in SPAC Deal With Evo Acquisition
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 22:04:00

By Stephen Nakrosis Special purpose acquisition company Evo Acquisition Corp. and 20Cube Logistics, a supply chain orchestrator, announced a deal Tuesday which will see 20Cube become a publicly traded company. The proposed business combination, which is expected to close in the first quarter of next year, will see shares of the combined company list on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol TCUB. 20Cube said it is a "software-enabled international supply chain orchestrator from purchase order to point of delivery.
