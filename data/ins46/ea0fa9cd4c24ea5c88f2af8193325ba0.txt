UiPath stock rallies as results, outlook surpass Wall Street consensus
marketwatch.com
news
2022-06-01 21:25:00

UiPath Inc. shares surged in the extended session Wednesday after the 'software robot' provider reported a narrower-than-expected loss and forecast an outlook just above the Wall Street consensus. UiPath PATH, -1.41% shares rallied 8% after hours, following a 1.4% decline in the regular session to close at $16.83, well below its April 2021 IPO price of $56 a share. The stock price has fallen 79% over the past 12 months, while the S&P 500 index SPX, -0.75% has declined 2.4%. The company reported a first-quarter loss of $122.
