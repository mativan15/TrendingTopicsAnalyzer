Prestige Estates registers sales of Rs 3511 cr, up 66% yoy for quarter ended September 2022
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-19 08:45:00

Prestige Estates has registered sales of Rs 3,511 cr , up by 66% yoy and collections of Rs 2,603, up by 68% yoy, for the quarter ended September 2022. The sales during this period is attributed to4.55 mn sft volume with an average realization of Rs 7711 per sq ft. The first half of the year H1 FY23, the Group registered sales of Rs 6523, up by 129% yoy and collections of Rs 4750 cr, up by 85% yoy. The sales during this period is attributed to 8.18 mn sft volume with an average realization of Rs 7976 per sq ft.
