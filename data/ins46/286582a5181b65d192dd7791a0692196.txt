 One person was hospitalized after a house exploded on Selkirk Avenue on Friday night, the Winnipeg Fire Paramedic Service says.
Crews were called to a bungalow in the 1600 block of Selkirk Avenue at 10:33 p.m. The home was heavily damaged and there was a strong smell of gas coming from it.One person was inside the house at the time of the explosion and suffered significant injuries.
