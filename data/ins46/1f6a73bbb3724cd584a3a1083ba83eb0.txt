Tractor Supply profit, same-store sales top expectations, but total sales comes up a bit shy
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 10:31:22

Tractor Supply Co. TSCO, -3.21% reported Thursday third-quarter profit that topped expectations but sales came up a bit shy, and raised its full-year outlook. The rural lifestyle retailer said net income rose to $234.1 million, or $2.10 a share, from $224.4 million, or $1.95 a share, in the year-ago period. The FactSet consensus for earnings per share was $2.08. Sales increased 8.4% to $3.27 billion, compared with the FactSet consensus of $3.28 billion.
