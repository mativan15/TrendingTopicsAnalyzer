Holcim Unit Pleads Guilty in US Over Payments to Islamic State
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-18 14:43:47

(Bloomberg) -- Holcim Ltd.'s Lafarge unit pleaded guilty in a US courtroom and agreed to pay a $777.8 million fine for conspiring to provide material support to the Islamic State in Syria, as part of an effort to keep its cement factory operating.The charges mark the first time a corporate entity has ever pleaded guilty to aiding terrorists in a US courtroom, prosecutors said.Lafarge has been under investigation for years for accusations it aided the Islamic State and other terrorist groups operating in Syria by funneling money to them to keep the plant running.
