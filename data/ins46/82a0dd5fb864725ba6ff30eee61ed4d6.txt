Street Angels Windsor-Essex hoping 'The Soup Shack' will close gap in food security
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-10-21 18:04:06

Street Angels Windsor-Essex are hoping to fill a void in the community. The outreach program for the homeless says there is a gap in food security between 7p.m. and 9 p.m. each night. Their solution is The Soup Shack. The plan is to renovate a 20-foot trailer and serve hot soup, rolls and warm beverages during that time in the downtown core.The Soup Shack in Windsor, Ont. (Source: Merissa Mills) Street Angels has already purchased the trailer and is now raising money to renovate and operate it during the winter months.
