FATF takes off Pak from its 'grey list' after four years

2022-10-21 18:42:23

After four years, Pakistan has been taken off from the infamous grey list of FATF, the global watchdog on terror financing and money laundering, in the wake of Islamabad's "high level political commitment" in dealing with the menace and carry out reforms in its existing monitoring mechanism.
