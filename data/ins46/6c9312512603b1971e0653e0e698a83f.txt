Mobileye Sees IPO Market With Surprising Clarity

Jan Hetfleisch By Breakingviews Intel (INTC) is taking its autonomous driving technology for a white-knuckle ride. Five years after buying Mobileye (MBLY) for some $15 billion, the chipmaker plans to sell a stake in it again for about the same valuation the chipmaker originally paid. While the Israeli subsidiary's fast growth is attractive, other factors have depressed expectations. Desperation has a way of sharpening focus. When Intel first said it would float Mobileye last December, investors were reveling in post-pandemic exuberance.
