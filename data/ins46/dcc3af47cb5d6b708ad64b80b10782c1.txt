US-based United Parcels Service to invest in India's InterGlobe for logistics business
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-29 17:15:00

(L-R): JB Singh, Director InterGlobe Enterprises and Ufku Akaltan, UPS President Indian subcontinent, Middle East and AfricaNew Delhi: US-based United Parcels Service (UPS), a global leader in logistics and InterGlobe Enterprises, announced a joint venture to launch MOVIN, a new logistics brand to meet the needs and demands of the fast-moving Indian market.This is in line with Prime Minister Narendra Modi's aims to make India a great centre for business. The name MOVIN is a combination of MOVEMENT and INDIA (MOVEMENT+INDIA), Ufku Akaltan, President, Indian Subcontinent, Middle East and Africa, of Atlanta-based UPS, told on the day of the announcement.
