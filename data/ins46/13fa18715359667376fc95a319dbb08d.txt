The Wrap Critics love Martin McDonagh's new film 'The Banshees of Inisherin,' which reunites Colin Farrell and Brendan Gleeson, his two leads from 2008's black crime comedy 'In Bruges' as feuding former friends.The film had its world premiere at the 2022 Venice Film Festival, where Farrell won the Volpi Cup for Best Actor and McDonagh won for Best Screenplay. Five years ago, McDonagh won the same award for 'Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri,' which set the stage for an Oscar in the same category.
