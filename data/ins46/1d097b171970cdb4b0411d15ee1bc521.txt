Published: 05:25 EDT, 17 October 2022 | Updated: 05:27 EDT, 17 October 2022 Liz Truss's premiership appears to be on borrowed time after just 40 astonishing days, but there is no easy, straightforward way for her to leave or be forced out of No10. Due to the astonishingly short term she has so far served, the usual Conservative Party rules don't apply. Normally MPs can submit letters of no confidence and if they reach the threshold - 15 per cent of Tory MPs - then a vote is called. If the PM loses that vote they are out.
