By CBS Miami Team June 3, 2022 / 10:55 AM / CBS Miami Keep your family and home safe during hurricane season At FPL, safety is the cornerstone of our commitment to customers and employees. Thirty years after the season that brought the unprecedented devastation of Hurricane Andrew and five after the destruction caused by Hurricane Irma, it is more important than ever to proactively plan for the upcoming hurricane season. FPL urges Floridians to be prepared and be aware of potential safety hazards at all times, especially before, during and after severe weather.
