Foiye Launches as Real Estate Centric Social Entertainment Platform

October 20, 2022 09:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time MIAMI--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Media executive Melinda C. Witmer and her team today announced the launch of Foiye, a new digital platform bringing real estate together with home and design entertainment to create an online community for home and interior design enthusiasts, real estate professionals and more.



At launch, Foiye will offer free home and real estate-related entertainment content alongside active and sold listings from Multiple Listing Service (MLS) partners.
