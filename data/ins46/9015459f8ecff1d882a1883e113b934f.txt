UNC Baseball selected as a regional host
usatoday.com
news
2022-05-29 23:58:28

On Monday, we will officially know the field for the 2022 NCAA Baseball Tournament and following UNC's win over NC State on Sunday, the Tar Heels were selected as a regional host.
The NCAA announced on Sunday night that UNC is one of 16 teams to host a regional site for the upcoming tournament. It's the 12th time in program history the Diamond Heels have hosted a regional as they push for the goal of reaching Omaha.
