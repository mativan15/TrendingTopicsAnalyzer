The Commons Defence Select Committee issues the plea in a report published todayMPs attack Britain's "unacceptable lack of progress in (the) space domain"A Space Minister should be appointed to boost Britain's galactic ambitions, MPs say today. The UK currently lags behind Italy in terms of 'space power tiers', according to an expert who gave evidence to the cross-party Commons Defence Select Committee. But creating a new post in the Cabinet Office would help convince Earth-based rivals the country takes space seriously, a report urges.
