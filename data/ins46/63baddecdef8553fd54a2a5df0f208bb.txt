Akshay Kumar is a man on a mission as goes to deserts, climbs mountains, takes helicopter rides, and even goes underwater to seek answers in the new song from Ram Setu that was released on Thursday. Titled Jai Shree Ram, the catchy song shows Akshay, who plays the role of an archaeologist in the movie, travelling to different locations solo and with his team on a quest to discover and save Ram Setu. Nushratt Bharuccha, Jacqueline Fernandez, Nassar, and Satya Dev feature alongside Akshay in the song's video.
