DODGEVILLE, Wis. (AP) _ Lands' End Inc. (LE) on Thursday reported a fiscal first-quarter loss of $2.4 million, after reporting a profit in the same period a year earlier.On a per-share basis, the Dodgeville, Wisconsin-based company said it had a loss of 7 cents. The clothing maker posted revenue of $303.7 million in the period. For the current quarter ending in August, Lands' End said it expects revenue in the range of $335 million to $350 million.
