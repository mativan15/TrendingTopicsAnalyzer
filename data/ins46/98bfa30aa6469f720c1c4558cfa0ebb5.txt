Democrats up pressure on Silicon Valley to safeguard abortion data
sfgate.com
news
2022-05-31 20:29:15

Democratic lawmakers are dialing up calls for tech giants like Apple and Google to protect user data related to abortions amid mounting fears that the anticipated reversal of Roe v. Wade could turn that information into a powerful surveillance tool for law enforcement.The trend highlights how - without a federal data privacy law in place - Democrats in Washington have limited tools at their disposal to try to prevent tech companies from scooping up and sharing sensitive health information with authorities in GOP-led states.
