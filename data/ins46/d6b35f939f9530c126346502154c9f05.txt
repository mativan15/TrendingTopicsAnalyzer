Logan Paul trains with WWE legend Shawn Michaels for huge Crown Jewel match
metro.co.uk
news
2022-10-21 09:21:59

Logan Paul has turned to WWE Hall of Famer Shawn Michaels as he prepares for his huge world championship match at Crown Jewel next month.
The YouTuber is making waves in the world of wrestling after his WrestleMania debut in April and a singles win over The Miz at SummerSlam, and now he'll battle for the Undisputed WWE Universal Title in Saudi Arabia on November 5.
The company has shared a behind the scenes look at Logan and the Heartbreak Kid's session, with the social media sensation locking up with WWE star and coach Drew Gulak in a training ring.
