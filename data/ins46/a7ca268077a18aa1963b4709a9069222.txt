Tom Brady's sideline outburst in Sunday's 20-18 loss to the Steelers was admittedly 'not one of [his] better days.'
During the latest episode of his 'Let's Go!' podcast on SiriusXM, the Buccaneers quarterback was asked by co-host Jim Gray about the moment Fox cameras caught Brady yelling at his offensive linemen as Tampa Bay trailed Pittsburgh, 10-6, at Acrisure Stadium. 
'I don't know if it's motivation, but I do think that it's a bad day when there's more F-bombs than touchdowns. So, that was not one of my better days.
