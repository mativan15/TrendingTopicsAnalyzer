Samson et Dalila review — a surging biblical epic with star turns
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-05-27 11:00:00

★★★★☆Money or morality? In Richard Jones's entertaining new Royal Opera staging of Camille Saint-Saëns's biblical opera the divide seems clear. On one side are the Philistines, cavorting frenetically in lashings of bling round an effigy of Dagon, their god, depicted as a clown's head flanked by a fruit machine and casino chips. On the other the Israelites: pious, plainly clad and persecuted, although also fanatically self-righteous.In the middle, however, are two of the most conflicted figures in opera.
