Stock Futures Fall Slightly as Investors Dwell on Health of the Economy
nbclosangeles.com
business
2022-06-01 22:45:00

U.S. stock futures were slightly lower Wednesday night as economic concerns dragged down investor sentiment. Futures tied to the Dow Jones Industrial Average edged lower by 58 points, or 0.18%. S&P 500 futures dipped 0.14%, and Nasdaq 100 futures were lower by 0.15%. Shares of pet retailer Chewy surged after hours by nearly 20% after the company reported strong quarterly results. Apparel retailer PVH also got a lift from earnings, with shares adding more than 4%. Meanwhile, Hewlett Packard Enterprise fell more than 6% following slight misses on both earnings and revenue.
