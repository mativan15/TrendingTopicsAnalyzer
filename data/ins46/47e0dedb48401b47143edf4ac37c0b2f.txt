Lahore, Lyallpur book shops: Favouring doorstep delivery, Ludhiana residents ditch trips to bookstores
hindustantimes.com
news
2022-10-19 23:05:59

Having made the long and arduous journeys from Lahore and Lyallpur — now Faizalabad — to set up successful businesses in the city, two of the oldest book strokes in Ludhiana are suddenly finding it difficult to bridge the gap between their dusty shelves and their customers' doorsteps.'Named after the cities where they were founded, the Lahore Book Shop and Lyall Book Shop are losing favour with customers — who are increasingly opting for one-day doorstep deliveries from online retailers and assorted online collections.
