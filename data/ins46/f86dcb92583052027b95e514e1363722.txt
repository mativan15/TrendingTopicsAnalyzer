These places have some of the best halal food in the GTA
thestar.com
news
2022-06-02 00:00:00

Changing demographics and more interest in halal-friendly options have restaurant owners and cooks across the Greater Toronto Area considering how they design their menus. If you want to explore how diverse the options are, eat through Mississauga where many restaurants, regardless of the type of cuisine, have halal-inclusive menus. Chang Huang (5120 Dixie Rd., Mississauga)Kolkata-born owner/cook Anthony Hsieh took over the Chang Huang space 11 years ago. He left the unique menu of Thai and Chinese dishes mostly intact while infusing it with his own culture.
