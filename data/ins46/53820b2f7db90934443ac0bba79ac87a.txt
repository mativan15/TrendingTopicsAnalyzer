During her remarkable run on Jeopardy! earlier this year, contestant Amy Schneider was unable to come up with the question, 'What is Ottawa?' A couple weekends ago, Schneider visited the capital to see for herself. (Jeopardy Productions, Inc./The Associated Press)Amy Schneider earned $1.3 million in prize money on Jeopardy! by correctly answering trivia questions — or, if you're a stickler, by correctly asking the questions that matched the answers provided.Schneider's 40-game winning streak, which stretched from November until late January, was the second-longest run in the popular game show's history.
