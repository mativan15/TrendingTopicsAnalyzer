Dharma Keerthiraj's fans will see their favourite actor in a different avatar in the upcoming film Vasundara Devi. Dharma plays the lead role and Sonu Gowda is paired opposite him. 'I am grateful to my parents for attending the teaser launch. This film is different from the ones I have acted in so far. I have changed my look also for this movie. A big thanks to director Mahesh. All actors have done a good job,'' said Dharma. Vasundara Devi is Dharma's second film directed by Mahesh Chinmayi. Earlier, he acted in Chanaksha that released in March 2019.
