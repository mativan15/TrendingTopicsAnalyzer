Published: 19:51 EDT, 21 October 2022 | Updated: 19:55 EDT, 21 October 2022 Shops are bringing forward heavy Black Friday and Christmas discounts amid a slump in sales driven by the cost of living squeeze.Families are paying more and getting less according to official figures showing bigger-than-expected falls in retail sales, both on the high street and online.Sales volumes in September were down by 6.2 per cent in a year, however soaring prices meant the value of these sales was up by 3.3 per cent.
