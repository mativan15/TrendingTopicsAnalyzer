 Mounties are looking for witnesses and video after gunfire erupted in a South Surrey neighbourhood Wednesday night.
Surrey RCMP says officers were called to a report of shots fired near 27A Avenue and 127 Street just after midnight.Police say the targeted home had people inside at the time, but that no one was hurt.
