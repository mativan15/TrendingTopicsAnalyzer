October 17, 2022 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Angelo Gordon, a $52 billion alternative investment firm focused on credit and real estate investing, today announced that Matt Heintz, former Head of North America at J.P. Morgan Global Insurance Solutions, has joined the firm as Managing Director and Head of Insurance in the Client Partnership Group. In this new role, Matt will be leading the expansion of Angelo Gordon's focus on investment management solutions for the insurance sector.
