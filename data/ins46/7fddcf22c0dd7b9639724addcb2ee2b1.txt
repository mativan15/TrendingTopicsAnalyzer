(Shutterstock)SOMERVILLE, MA — Somerville's Assembly Row development is expanding to welcome four new businesses in the next year, reported Boston Business Journal. The mixed-use site said Wednesday it will soon be home to the athleisure store Athleta, designer handbag retailer Coach, German kitchenware maker Zwilling J.A. Henckels, and stationery store Paper Source.
