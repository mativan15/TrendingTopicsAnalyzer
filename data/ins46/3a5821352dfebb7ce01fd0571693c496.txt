DUBAI, United Arab Emirates, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The global musical instruments market stands at ~US$ 11.5 Bn as of now and is expected to reach US$ 14.8 Bn by the year 2032 at a CAGR of ~2.6% between 2022 and 2032. The major end-users of musical instruments are students pursuing degrees in or learning music, music composers, and orchestra owners. Besides, there are people who go for musical instruments merely as showpiece or even as a hobby. It's a known fact that musical instruments are pretty expensive, as they undergo several processes for getting the perfect sound/beats.
