The city of Malden is planning to use some of its federal pandemic recovery funding to give $1,000 scholarships to every high school graduate this year who is heading to college.Mayor Gary Christenson said in a telephone interview that students in the Class of 2022 have faced a daunting challenge during the COVID-19 pandemic during the past two years - and they now face rising college costs.'We want to show them that we appreciate all that they have had to deal with during their time in high school.
