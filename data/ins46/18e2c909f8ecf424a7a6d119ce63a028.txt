To reach to 6 lakh villages is ambitious: CSC's Sanjay Kumar Rakesh
indiatimes.com
news
2022-10-17 12:15:00

NEW DELHI: CSC e-Governance Services India is expecting to reach 6 lakh villages in India, which is an ambitious target, a top executive said, adding that the agency is also in talks with multiple states to make government-to-citizen (G2C) services available at doorstep."The government has given us a mandate to have one common service centre (CSC) in every village. There are village-level entrepreneurs (VLE) present in as many as 3.75 lakh villages. To reach 6 lakh villages is an ambitious target," Sanjay Kumar Rakesh, managing director, CSC told ETTelecom.
