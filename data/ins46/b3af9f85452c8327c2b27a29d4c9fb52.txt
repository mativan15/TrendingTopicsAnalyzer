When trying to solve a problem—small or large—many people may tend to rush to a solution in order to get a process in motion or to just get it off their plate so they can focus on other things. However, this method has the potential to create negative consequences, especially if the wrong decision was made or all variables weren't considered. To help prevent this situation, it's vital to slow down your problem-solving process. To do this, consider these tips recommended by the members of Young Entrepreneur Council.
