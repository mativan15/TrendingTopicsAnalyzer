BOCA RATON, Fla.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 31, 2022--MiX Telematics (NYSE: MIXT), a leading global Software-as-a-Service ('SaaS') provider of connected fleet management solutions, announced that its Chief Executive Officer, Stefan Joselowitz, Chief Financial Officer, John Granara, and Chief Accounting Officer, Paul Dell, will present at the William Blair Growth Stock Conference in Chicago, IL.MiX Telematics' presentation is scheduled for Tuesday, June 7, 2021 at 12:00 p.m. Central Time (7:00 p.m. South African Time) and will be webcast live.
