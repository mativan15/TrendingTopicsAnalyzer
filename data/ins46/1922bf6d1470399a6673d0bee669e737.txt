I've had the privilege of living in Florida for my entire life. During my numerous trips abroad, whenever I have mentioned that I was from Florida, the other person's jaw would hit the floor; they couldn't believe that I lived where they vacationed.

A key part of what makes Florida so special is its business community, a fact that was highlighted during the height of the pandemic. When other states were closing their doors, we kept ours open.
