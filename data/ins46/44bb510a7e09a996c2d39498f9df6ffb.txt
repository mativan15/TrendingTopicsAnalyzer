Innovative Sales Leader Brings Over 15 Years of CPG Experience to Growing Mission-Driven Wellness Company in Canada

VANCOUVER, British Columbia, June 01, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Natreve, the mission-driven and ocean-forward premium wellness company dedicated to providing the most innovative, highest-quality products, announced Meredith Harren as Director of Sales, Canada. In her new role, Harren will define and execute sales strategies for the growing Canadian region.

"We're elated to have Meredith join Natreve at this important moment in time for our growing company," said Roland Radu, Natreve CEO.
