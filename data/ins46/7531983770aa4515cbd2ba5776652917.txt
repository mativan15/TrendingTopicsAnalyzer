Tempers flare Parliament as Speaker roars at MPs amid 'bullying' row
express.co.uk
politics
2022-10-19 21:27:00

The Deputy Speaker of the House Eleanor Laing erupt in a furious swipe at MPs around the chair following a statement by Labour's Chris Bryant stating Tories had been "bullied' during Wednesday's tense vote on fracking.Mr Bryant told the Deputy Speaker:  "I  would urge you to launch an investigation into the scenes outside the entrance to the no lobby earlier."As you know members are expected to be able to vote without fear or favour and the behaviour code which is agreed by the whole of the house says there shall never be bullying or harassment.
