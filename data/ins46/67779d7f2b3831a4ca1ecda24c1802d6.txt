Texas Will Send Election Inspectors to Harris County, Citing Trump-Driven Audit
nytimes.com
news
2022-10-20 21:16:26

The State of Texas confirmed this week that it would send a contingent of election inspectors to Harris County during the midterms in November, an additional layer of scrutiny tied to an active examination of vote counts from 2020 that former President Donald J. Trump had sought.

But that step quickly drew criticism from some officials in Harris, Texas' most populous county, which includes Houston. They accused the state of meddling in the county's election activities as early in-person voting is about to begin on Monday in Texas.
