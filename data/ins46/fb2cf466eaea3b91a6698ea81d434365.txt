The Shiv Nadar Foundation announced the launch of its K-12 school chain Shiv Nadar School at Adyar in Chennai. The foundation, which is a philanthropic venture of Shiv Nadar, founder of HCL, runs three schools in Delhi. 'The intention of these schools is to create a stimulating and enriching environment for young children and guide them towards becoming lifelong learners.
