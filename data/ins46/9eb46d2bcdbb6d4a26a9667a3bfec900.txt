Asomiya Pratidin Pratidin Time Presents ‘The Conclave 2022'
indianexpress.com
news
2022-10-15 07:39:41

To bring Northeast India in the spotlight by bringing together stakeholders and policymakers to discuss the various issues for more inclusive development of the region, the Pratidin Media Group, in association with Dalmia Cements is going to organize ‘Asomiya Pratidin and Pratidin Time presents The Conclave 2022' at Manekshaw Centre, Delhi Cantonment in New Delhi on October 15 and 16.
The conclave has been conceptualized to put forward and discuss the issues of Northeast India with the outcome serving as a roadmap towards more inclusive development of the region.
