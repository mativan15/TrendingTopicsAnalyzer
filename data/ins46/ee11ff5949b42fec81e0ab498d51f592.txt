Tourists enjoy visiting Japan after borders reopen
nhk.or.jp
news
2022-10-21 08:15:00

Foreign tourists were visible in shopping and entertainment areas in Japan for the first time since the coronavirus pandemic began as the country eased COVID border controls last week. Tokyo's Akihabara district is well-known for electronics shops and manga culture. Tourists are enjoying shopping with the advantage of a weak Japanese yen. A man from the Philippines said he can buy two bags with one price in his country. A woman from the United States said, "It is even more affordable now because the dollar is stronger against the yen.
