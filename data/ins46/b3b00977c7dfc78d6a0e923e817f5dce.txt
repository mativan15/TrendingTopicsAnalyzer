Dev and Prosenjit Chatterjee joining hands after a long time had already created the hype needed ahead of the release of ‘Kacher Manuch' this pujo. The film saw a decent response from the box office and critical acclaim for the reflection of the times we live in, with rampant corruption, large-scale unemployment, scams and an alarming rise in death by suicide.Dev and Prosenjit both delivered commendable performances to win the audience hearts. And now, the Pathikrit Basu-directed film is all set for its UAE release on October 28.
