Four Bangladeshi women escape from shelter home at Surat police headquarters
indianexpress.com
news
2022-06-04 20:22:57

Four Bangladeshi women lodged in a shelter home at police headquarters in Surat district escaped on Friday evening, police said. A complaint in this connection has been registered with Kamrej police station.
Sources said that the four women, Diya Hasarat Biswas (20), Sumaiya, alias Rupali, Shaikh (21), Lubnibegum Mulla (22), Hasina Mulla (20), were caught by Surat police from a house in Kholwad village in Kamrej taluka, on August 14, 2021. When police asked for their documents, they failed to produce them and were sent to a shelter home at the Police headquarter in Kamrej.
