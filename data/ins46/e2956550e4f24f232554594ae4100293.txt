13 killed after military fighter jet Su-34 crashes into apartment in Russia, say reports
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 13:45:00

Atleast 13 people were killed after a military Russian fighter jet, Su-34 crashed into a residential building apartment in Yeysk, Western Russia. The Ministry of Emergency Situations reported that thirteen bodies, including three children, were removed from the debris as of Tuesday morning. At least twenty-five people were injured, state media reported earlier. It is unclear what happened to the ejected pilots. A small part of the Sea of Azov separates Yeysk from the occupied Russian territories of southern Ukraine in a narrow body of water.
