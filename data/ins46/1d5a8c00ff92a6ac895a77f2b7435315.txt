Community Shield could be played overseas or become all-star match
thetimes.co.uk
news
2022-10-18 16:00:00

Premier League clubs are pushing for the Community Shield to be moved from its traditional spot as the curtain-raiser to the season — with some proposing that it should be played overseas or as an All-Star match.The future of the Community Shield — the match in which the Premier League champions play the FA Cup winners — is discussed in the Premier League's 'New Deal for Football' plan, which cover the post-2024 football calendar.The Community Shield, organised by the FA to raise money for charities and grassroots projects, takes place on the Sunday before the first weekend of the Premier League season.
