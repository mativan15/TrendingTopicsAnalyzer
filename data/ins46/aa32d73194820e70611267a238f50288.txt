PeopleImages/iStock via Getty Images BlackLine (NASDAQ: NASDAQ:BL) recently reported a very strong start to the year with revenue growing 22% to $120 million, beating expectations by ~1%. Despite the difficult macro-economic environment and rising fears around a potential recession, BlackLine continues to prove the significant TAM opportunity ahead of them. Prior to the pandemic, many back office accounting functions were completed using legacy systems that were often dated, or manual processes. BlackLine aims to provide an integrated accounting software platform, making it easier for businesses to perform all of their accounting functions.
