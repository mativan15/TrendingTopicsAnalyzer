Prime Minister signs historic land claim settlement with Siksika First Nation
ctvnews.ca
news
2022-06-02 16:04:00

SIKSIKA, Alta - Prime Minister Justin Trudeau and the chief of the Siksika First Nation have signed a historic land claim settlement, which the federal government says is one of the largest agreements of its kind in Canada. Trudeau and Marc Miller, minister of Crown-Indigenous relations, participated in a signing ceremony Thursday with Chief Ouray Crowfoot, council and community members. "We're gathered today to right a wrong from the past," Trudeau said during the ceremony. "We're gathered to give ourselves a chance to start rebuilding trust between us, nation to nation.
