Legend of Moose Wala: Cremation site turns into a pilgrimage spot
indiatimes.com
news
2022-06-06 05:30:00

MOOSA ( MANSA ) : When Punjabi rapper Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, popularly known as Sidhu Moose Wala, came out with the album, ‘‘Legend' in 2019, he would never have thought that he would become the stuff of legends one day.Today the field where Moose Wala was cremated, is turning into a new pilgrimage spot, with people from far off places coming to pay their respects and to bow before the picture of the Moose Wala placed in front of a small bricked platform where his last rites were performed. It is not just youngsters who come here.
