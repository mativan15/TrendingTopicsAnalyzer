Mastercard to roll out program that will help banks offer crypto trading (NYSE:MA)
seekingalpha.com
news
2022-10-17 12:25:33

yalcinsonat1 Payments titan Mastercard (NYSE:MA) is teaming up with cryptocurrency platform Paxos to introduce a new program on Monday that will help lenders offer crypto trading to their clients. As such, Mastercard (MA) said it will take care of the regulatory compliance and security in an effort to make crypto trading services more attractive for financial institutions to adopt. The move comes on the heels of Mastercard's (MA) plans to launch a tool that will help banks find and block suspicious transactions from crypto exchanges.
