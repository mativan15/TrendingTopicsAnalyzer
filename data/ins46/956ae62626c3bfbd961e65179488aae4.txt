REUTERS/Kai PfaffenbachOct 19 (Reuters) - Germany's finance agency said on Wednesday it had increased the size of 18 outstanding bonds by 3 billion euros each to use in the repo market and provide flexibility to cover financing needs arising from the energy crisis.The increase, 54 billion euros in total, was done for bonds that the agency said were in high demand.
