India has achieved target for ethanol blending 5 months before deadline: PM Modi
indiatimes.com
energy
2022-06-05 07:45:00

ANIPrime Minister Narendra Modi at 'Save Soil Movement' program.Prime Minister Narendra Modi announced on Sunday that India has achieved the target of 10 per cent ethanol blending in petrol five months before deadline. Making the announcement at a programme on the 'Save Soil Movement', which coincided with the World Environment Day, Modi cited a number of measures taken by his government to protect the environment, saying its efforts have been multi-dimensional despite the country having a negligible role in climate change.
