Journey, one of the greatest rock bands ever, has announced the 50th Anniversary Freedom Tour 2023, featuring American rock band Toto as a guest on all dates. Journey, the diamond-selling Rock & Roll Hall of Famers, will perform a selection of their international chart-topping classics. The most popular ones, including "Don't Stop Believin'," "Any Way You Want It," "Faithfully," "Lights," and others, will be performed live in 38 cities across North America. The Journey Freedom Tour 2023, which has been organised by AEG Presents, will kick off on February 4 in Allentown, Pennsylvania and travel to Austin, Montreal, Memphis, and more before ending on April 25 at the brand-new Acrisure Arena in Palm Springs, California.
