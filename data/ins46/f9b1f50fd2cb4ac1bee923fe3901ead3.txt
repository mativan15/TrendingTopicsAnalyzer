The Chicago Bulls will face a familiar nemesis when it visits the Miami Heat in the season opener on Wednesday.Miami has won 11 of its last 13 meetings against Chicago, including a season sweep of all four games last season. The Heat won the last three meetings by an average of 19 points.Both teams enter the new season with several returning stars and a few key question marks.Miami forward Jimmy Butler averaged 21.4 points last year and nearly led his team to the NBA Finals for the second time in three seasons.
