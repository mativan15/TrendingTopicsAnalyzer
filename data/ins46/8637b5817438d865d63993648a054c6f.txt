Table Tennis Federation to spend Rs 66 lakh on CoA, 55 lakh on prize money for 2021-22 season
thehindu.com
news
2022-05-30 09:42:29

The Court-appointed Committee of Administrators (CoA), running the affairs of Table Tennis Federation of India (TTFI), will be entitled to Rs 5 lakh compensation, including Rs 3 lakh to the Chairperson, per month. In addition, the manager's remuneration is fixed at Rs 50,000 per month. This takes the total expenditure to Rs 66 lakh per year. In comparison, the prize-money for the 2021-22 season, spread over four ranking tournaments and three National championships, comes to around Rs 55 lakh.
