Published: 07:43 EDT, 31 May 2022 | Updated: 07:43 EDT, 31 May 2022 LaLiga president Javier Tebas has insisted that Barcelona are currently unable to sign Robert Lewandowski from Bayern Munich.Lewandowski put Europe's top sides on high alert this week when he publicly declared his career at Bayern was 'over'.  The 33-year-old has one year left on his deal at the Bundesliga side and has been widely linked with a move to the Nou Camp, with the striker said to be in contact with Blaugrana boss Xavi.
