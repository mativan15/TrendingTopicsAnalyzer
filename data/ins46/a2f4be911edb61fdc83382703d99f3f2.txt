GOP-Friendly House District Becomes Nail-Biting Race on Abortion
bnnbloomberg.ca
news
2022-10-18 14:37:48

(Bloomberg) -- In any other election year, two Navy veterans vying to represent a Virginia district dominated by military interests would focus on their national security bonafides. Instead, the race between two women on opposite sides of the abortion debate has turned one of the nation's closest House contests into a referendum on Roe v. Wade. The bellwether district, which includes Virginia Beach and its Oceana Naval Base, skewed more Republican after redistricting brought in more GOP-heavy areas, hurting incumbent Representative Elaine Luria's prospects for a third term.
