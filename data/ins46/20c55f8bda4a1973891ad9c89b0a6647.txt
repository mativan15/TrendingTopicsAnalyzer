October 18, 2022 10:27 AM Eastern Daylight Time LOS ANGELES--(BUSINESS WIRE)--This winter season, rising brand Aha Selected is introducing its new 'Puffer' down jacket series designed for a variety of activities including sports, casual, and city life.
