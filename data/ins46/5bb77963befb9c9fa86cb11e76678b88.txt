The CEO of Tecnotree Corporation, Padma Ravichander, acquires 15 million shares from Fitzroy Investments Limited

2022-06-03 00:00:00

June 03, 2022 06:21 AM Eastern Daylight Time ESPOO, Finland--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Padma Ravichander, the CEO of Tecnotree Corporation ('Tecnotree') has today entered an agreement, wherein Fitzroy Investments Limited ('Fitzroy') will sell 15 million shares of Tecnotree to Padma Ravichander for a purchase price equivalent to the 10-day moving-average of the Tecnotree share price on the NASDAQ Helsinki Stock Exchange. The sale of these shares will be conducted in multiple tranches and will be completed by September 2022.
