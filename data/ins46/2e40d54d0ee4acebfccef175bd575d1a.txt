Once across the bridge, we find our way to the Palisades Parkway, a series of sweeping curves bordered by forest running alongside the Hudson River that's a favorite of test drivers. Here I get to switch between the other ride modes—Sport, which dials up the power and dials down the traction and slip controls; Eco, which maximizes smoothness and efficiency; and Road, a balance somewhere in the middle. Switching modes while riding is simple—just press a button within easy reach of your right thumb—and seamless.
