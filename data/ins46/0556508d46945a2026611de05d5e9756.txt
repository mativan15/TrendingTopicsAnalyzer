Here's How Insulating Your Water Pipes Can Save You Money This Winter

This story is part of Home Tips, CNET's collection of practical advice for getting the most out of your home, inside and out. 


About one out of every 60 insured homes requires an insurance claim for water damage and freezing each year, according to the Insurance Information Institute. That's a lot of time and hassle for a lot of people. Properly insulating your pipes can help you remain one of the 59 in 60 who avoid it.Besides avoiding a disruptive disaster, insulating your pipes can actually save you money by reducing your heating costs, which the Environmental Protection Agency says accounts for up to 20% of home energy use.
