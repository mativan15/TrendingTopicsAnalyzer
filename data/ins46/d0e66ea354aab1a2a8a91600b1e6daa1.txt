Apollo Hospital shares down 1.5% as Nifty drops
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 08:00:00

NEW DELHI: Shares of Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Ltd. traded 1.5 per cent down in Thursday's trade at 12:58PM (IST). Around 3,287 shares changed hands on the counter. The counter opened at Rs 4390.0 and touched an intraday high and low of Rs 4390.95 and Rs 4314.0, respectively, in the session so far. Shares of the company of Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Ltd. quoted a 52-week high of Rs 5930.7 and a 52-week low of Rs 3365.9. As per BSE, the total market cap of the Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Ltd. stood at Rs 62091.
