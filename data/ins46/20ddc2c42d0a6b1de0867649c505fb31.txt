EUGENE, Ore. (PRWEB) October 18, 2022 Datalogic, a global leader in the automatic data capture and factory automation markets, brings an expansive array of technology and solutions for traceability, inspection, vision, and safety to PACKEXPO 2022 in Chicago October 23-26. This year Datalogic will be joined by Datasensing and Pekat Vision, both part of the Datalogic Group, enhancing the display of interactive demonstrations in booth S-4266, located in the South Hall. The booth will feature an extensive array of traceability technology for use in manufacturing, packaging, supply chain, and last mile.
