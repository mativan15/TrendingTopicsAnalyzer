Camille's House

Price: $200 a night

In North Texas, Camille's house offers a hot tub and a prime location for restaurants in the Lubbock area. The Airbnb rental in Tech Terrace is is also proximally located to area coffee shops and parks for people seeking a little outdoor activity during their retreat.

Naturalist Boudoir

Location: Southeast Texas

Price: $262 a night

The Naturalist Boudoir in Lumberton is a romantic getaway option in Southeast Texas. Located near the heart of the Big Thicket, this spot is extremely private, according to the Airbnb post, and boasts an outdoor hot tub and shower.
