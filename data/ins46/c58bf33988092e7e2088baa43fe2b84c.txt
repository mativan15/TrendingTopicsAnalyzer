LegalPay funds logistics firm Pidge to pursue its lawsuit against Meatigo
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-17 14:00:00

In the year 2020, Meatigo engaged Pidge to provide logistic services across Delhi-NCR regionLegalPay, a third-party litigation funder and interim financier, said it has financially helped logistics firm Pidge to recover outstanding dues from online non-vegetarian products retailer Meatigo. In the year 2020, Meatigo engaged Pidge to provide logistic services across Delhi-NCR region and despite the delay and default in payment of invoices, Pidge continued to provide its services. Subsequently, Pidge initiated proceedings against Meatigo in lieu of disputes arising out of unpaid invoices after Meatigo defaulted.
