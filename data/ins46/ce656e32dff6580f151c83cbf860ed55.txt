ReutersOn an immediate basis, 15,770/52,500 and 15,850/52,700 levels would be major hurdles.Shares of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. traded 0.28 per cent down at Rs 228.95 on Thursday at around 11:52AM (IST), while the benchmark BSE Sensex advanced 151.7 points to 55532.87. As many as 103,720 shares changed hands on the counter with a total value of Rs 2.38 crore. The stock quoted a 52-week high price of Rs 354.55 and a 52-week low of Rs 225.75. On the technical charts, the 200-DMA of the stock stood at Rs 289.
