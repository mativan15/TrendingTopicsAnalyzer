LaLiga clubs 'consider going on STRIKE' over hated European Super League

LA LIGA clubs are considering going on strike as pressure grows on the Spanish Government to block the European Super League, reports say. 
Real Madrid president Florentino Perez is desperate to revive his hated project, which fell apart within days 18 months ago. 
La Liga fans could be deprived of seeing the league's best stars, like Vinicius Jr, if it goes on strike on October 28Credit: Rex
Barcelona remain one of the clubs in favour of the European Super LeagueCredit: Getty
But La Liga clubs are determined to stop him as political parties become increasingly pulled into the debate.
