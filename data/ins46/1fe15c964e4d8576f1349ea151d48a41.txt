I-T searches premises of restaurants
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-29 06:00:00

Coimbatore: The income tax department on Saturday conducted searches on the premises of Shree Anandhaas chain of restaurants in the city in connection with alleged tax evasions.
The searches were also conducted at the residences of directors and staff of the restaurants.
The searches were conducted in the restaurants located at eight places - Vadavalli, RS Puram, Gandhipuram, Ram Nagar, Lakshmi Mills, Sundarapuram, Avinashi Road and Brookefields Malls.
Twenty-five teams were pressed into action to conduct the searches.
