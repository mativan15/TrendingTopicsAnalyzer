ETtechIllustration: Rahul AwasthiGlobalfair, a technology-first B2B building material supplier, has raised $20 million in series A funding, in a round led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, with existing investors India Quotient and Saama Capital participating. The Gurugram-based startup, founded by IIT alumni Shaily Garg and Ashish Chandra in 2020, has operations in 36 of the 50 states in the USA and supplies building material for projects across India and Vietnam.
