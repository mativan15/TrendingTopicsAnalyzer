Tagboard and Heymarket Partner to Unlock New Level of Real-Time Engagement for Broadcasters

Tagboard's integration with Heymarket allows content creators and producers to feature text messages, images, videos, GIFs, emojis, and other conversational content on-air. "We're excited to partner with Heymarket to empower enterprise media producers around the world to connect with their communities via text message" said Tagboard CEO Nathan Peterson. SEATTLE and SAN FRANCISCO (PRWEB) October 19, 2022 Tagboard, a leading audience engagement platform used by 600 sports, media, and entertainment organizations worldwide, announced a direct integration partnership today with Heymarket, a leading conversational messaging platform.
