Internal transfer to prepare for the potential IPO of Yara Clean Ammonia
globenewswire.com
news
2022-06-02 00:00:00

Oslo, 2 June 2022: The board of directors of Yara International ASA ('Yara') has today signed a demerger and a merger plan for the internal reorganization and transfer of Yara's Clean Ammonia business to a wholly-owned subsidiary of Yara. The internal reorganization is being implemented as a preparatory step for a potential initial public offering ('IPO') of Yara Clean Ammonia ('YCA') on the Oslo Stock Exchange. The demerger and merger follow Yara's announcement 4 May that it is evaluating a potential IPO of YCA and will initiate an internal process to organize YCA assets, contracts etc.
