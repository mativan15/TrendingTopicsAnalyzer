 A Burnaby B.C. RCMP officer has been stabbed and killed in the line of duty Tuesday.
Sources confirm to Global News that the female officer was assisting Burnaby bylaw officers with a call at a homeless encampment in a park.The incident occurred on Canada Way between Willingdon Avenue and Boundary Road.
