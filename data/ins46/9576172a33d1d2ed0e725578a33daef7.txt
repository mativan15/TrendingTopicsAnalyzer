Generac stock nosedives 20% after full-year guidance cut (NYSE:GNRC)
seekingalpha.com
business
2022-10-19 13:51:27

coffeekai Generac (NYSE:GNRC) shares plunged over 20% on Wednesday after the energy tech solutions firm lowered its full-year outlook and guided for third quarter sales below consensus estimates. Preliminary net sales for Q3 grew 15% Y/Y to ~$1.09B, but fell short of company's prior expectations and Wall Street estimates of $1.34B. Preliminary net income reduced from $132M, or $1.93 per share, in the year-ago quarter to ~$58M, or $0.83 per share. Adjusted EBITDA also narrowed from $209M to ~$184M.
