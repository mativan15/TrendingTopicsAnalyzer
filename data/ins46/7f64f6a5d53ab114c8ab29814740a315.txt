Metrio's data collection, analytics and reporting solutions to bolster Nasdaq's portfolio of IR & ESG services

Nasdaq plans to maintain Metrio's headquarters in Montreal

NEW YORK and MONTREAL, June 02, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Nasdaq, Inc. (Nasdaq: NDAQ) announced today that it entered into an agreement to acquire Metrio, a privately-held provider of environmental, social and governance (ESG) data collection, analytics and reporting services based in Montreal.
