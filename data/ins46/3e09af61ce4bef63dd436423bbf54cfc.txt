Samples of 5-year-old UP girl collected for suspected monkeypox
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-04 05:15:00

India may have its first suspected case of monkeypox in Uttar Pradesh, according to reports Samples of a 5-yr-old girl was collected for testing for monkeypox, as a precautionary measure, the CMO of Ghaziabad said in a statement. 
"The girl had complaints of itching & rashes on her body. She has no other health issues & neither she nor any of her close contact travelled abroad in the past 1 month," an official was quoted by ANI. The US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) said Friday it was aware of more than 700 global cases of monkeypox, including 21 in the United States, with investigations now suggesting it is spreading inside the country.
