Manchester United could live to regret their decision to keep Cristiano Ronaldo with Paul Merson explaining why Erik ten Hag faces a gloomy task to turn around the club's fortunes.

The Dutchman, 52, has been brought in on a three-year contract and has an almighty task on his hands. His new charges limped to a sixth-place finish this season with a number of stars badly under-performing. And with money tight, new signings are expected to be light on the ground.
