October 20, 2022 08:55 AM Eastern Daylight Time RALEIGH, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Officials at The Preiss Company (TPCO), one of the nation's largest, privately-held, student housing owner-operators, today announced the acquisition of the 928-bed Valentine Commons for an undisclosed amount. The off-campus student apartment complex will undergo a $10 million renovation to further upgrade certain public and private spaces.



Ben Roelke and Ian Walker with Newmark, a global commercial real estate services provider, arranged $77,300,000 in acquisition financing through a life company lender.
