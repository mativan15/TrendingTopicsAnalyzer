How one sportswear brand elevates sustainable principles to achieve peak performance
2019 Insider Inc.
news
2022-06-03 15:03:31

From making running shoes with bio-materials such as castor beans — a natural raw material that exists outside the food chain — to creating a shoe-recycling program and extracting materials from CO2, On is placing itself at the forefront of innovation. On is a brand built on sports culture, and it collaborates with athletes such as Roger Federer and Hellen Obiri, the two-time Olympic medalist and World Champion Kenyan runner. It's no surprise that one of On's cofounders, Olivier Bernhard, was a professional athlete himself.
