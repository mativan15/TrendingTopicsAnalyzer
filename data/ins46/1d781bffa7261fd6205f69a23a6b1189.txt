Iyad Jabri to Discuss How Personalization Enhances Upskilling and Promotes Skills Mastery; CYPHER to Also Demonstrate its MATRIX Platform at Booth #823PLANO, Texas, Oct. 19, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- CYPHER LEARNING, a leading provider of intelligent learning platforms for schools, universities and organizations around the world, today announced its participation at DevLearn Conference & Expo, Oct. 26-28, in Las Vegas. CYPHER CTO Iyad Jabri will be among the featured speakers at the learning and development (L&D) event, which explores the future of organizational learning and performance, and how technology is shaping L&D.
