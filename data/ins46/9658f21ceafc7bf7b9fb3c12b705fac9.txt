Establishes new POPs in four major data centres in Seoul Metropolitan area

LONDON, May 30, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Enterprises in South Korea now have access to cost effective, low latency connectivity services from Colt Technology Services, with the announcement of the expansion of its network into the market.

The expansion sees Colt establish the network hubs in four key carrier-neutral data centres in the Seoul Metropolitan area and deliver the speeds of up to 40G Ethernet Service, in addition to providing cost-effective local coverage in the South Korean capital and other major cities.
