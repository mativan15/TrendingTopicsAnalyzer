PITTSBURGH, Oct. 17, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- MSA Safety (NYSE: MSA), the global leader in the development and manufacture of safety products that help protect people and facility infrastructures, today announced that Lee McChesney has been named Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer.  Mr. McChesney previously served as Vice President, Corporate Finance and Chief Financial Officer for Stanley Black & Decker's $12.8 billion operating unit, Global Tools and Storage.
