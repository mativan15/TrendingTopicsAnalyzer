Publishers Clearing House Launches 'PCH Audiences' to Help Marketers Reach PCH's Most Engaged Consumers Across the Open Web

NEW YORK, Oct. 18, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Publishers Clearing House (PCH), a leading digital entertainment and commerce destination for millions of U.S. consumers, has announced the launch of PCH Audiences, a new marketing solution from PCH Media. PCH Audiences leverages PCH's nearly 70 years of expertise in data capture and qualification and applies it to their 54M-person audience file to allow brands to reach the most captivated and ready-to-act PCH consumers wherever they are across the open web.
