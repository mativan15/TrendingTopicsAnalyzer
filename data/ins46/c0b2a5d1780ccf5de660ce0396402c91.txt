New Retail Report from Getwizer Reveals Parents' Extreme Frustration With Retailers' One Size Fits All Model

New York, New York, June 01, 2022 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- A new report from consumer insights platform Getwizer reveals the extreme highs and lows that parents and their children experience due to sizing discrepancies from store to store. Despite a long history of shopping being a bonding experience for parents and children, the reality of inconsistent sizing and the difficulty of shopping for kids who are 'out of the norm' can make kids feel isolated and parents feel bad about the entire shopping experience.
