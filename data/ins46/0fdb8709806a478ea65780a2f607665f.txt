Megha Engineering & Infrastructures Ltd (MEIL) group company ICOMM and small arms maker Caracal, which is a part of Edge Group of UAE, have signed a partnership agreement to develop a portfolio of small arms for the Indian defence forces and export.
'The complete line of small arms will be produced in ICOMM's design, development and manufacturing centre in Hyderabad,' Chief Sumanth P said in a release on the agreement signed at the ongoing DEFEXPO 2022 in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. ICOMM is into manufacture of missile and sub-systems, communications and EW system, radars, electro-optics, composites, loitering munitions, shelters, drone and counter drone system.
