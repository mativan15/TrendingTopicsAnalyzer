Uber launches Journey Ads division to drive ad business growth (NYSE:UBER)
seekingalpha.com

2022-10-19 13:44:30

Cristi Croitoru/iStock Editorial via Getty Images Uber (NYSE:UBER) has launched Uber Journey Ads, a dedicated advertising division led by industry veteran Dr Mark Grether. Uber Journey Ads will use first-party data and insights from Uber's mobility and delivery businesses and place brand content on the Uber and Uber Eats apps. Over 40 marquee brands have already partnered with Uber to run Journey Ads. These include NBCUniversal, Heineken, and United Artists Releasing. Journey Ads will include prominently placed sponsored listings across Uber Eats, with Uber claiming clients such as Shake Shack have already seen increased engagement, ROI and customer acquisition.
