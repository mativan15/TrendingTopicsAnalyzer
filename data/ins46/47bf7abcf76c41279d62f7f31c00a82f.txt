The company reported quarterly results Thursday.Justin Sullivan/Getty ImagesGap Inc slashed its annual results forecast on Thursday, sending shares 13% lower after hours as the clothing retailer blamed poor fashion choices at its Old Navy line and weak demand in the face of decades-high inflation.The Banana Republic parent also posted a much wider-than-expected quarterly loss, slammed by surging costs of air freight and deeper discounts.The dismal outlook echoes peers American Eagle Outfitters and Abercrombie & Fitch Co as rising prices of essentials like food force consumers to limit discretionary spending.
