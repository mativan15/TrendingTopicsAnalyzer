Democrats funnel money to North Carolina in hopes of sneaky Senate upset
newsweek.com
news
2022-10-18 14:46:03

With three weeks until the midterm elections, Democrats are funneling money to North Carolina's Senate race to bolster Cheri Beasley, who is locked in a tight race against Donald Trump-backed Representative Ted Budd.Democrats vying to retain control of the Senate see North Carolina as one of their top opportunities to pick up a seat, as GOP Senator Richard Burr is retiring, and Trump only won a narrow victory in North Carolina in 2020.The Democrats are banking on former state Supreme Court Justice Beasley to deliver a victory in the Tar Heel state.
