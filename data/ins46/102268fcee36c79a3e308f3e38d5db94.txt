[Photo/Xinhua] A growing number of multinational companies are establishing R&D centers in China, showing confidence in the "world factory, world market and world lab", China Youth Daily reported. China R&D centers have become an important part of the company's global R&D system, Shi Dongmei, head of Merck R&D China told the newspaper. With the upgrading and developing of China's pharmaceuticals and healthcare industry over the past decade, some global early clinical development programs launched R&D exploration in China, ensuring China becomes one of the first-tier countries to get new drugs into the market.
