Nio stock bounces, even after Tesla CEO Elon Musk says China seeing 'burst of recession'
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-20 17:48:59

The U.S.-listed shares of China-based Nio Inc. NIO, +0.14% gained 0.1% in afternoon trading Thursday, after closing the previous session at the lowest price since July 2020, despite the 6.7% selloff in rival electric vehicle maker Tesla Inc.'s stock TSLA, -7.12% after disappointing third-quarter results. Meanwhile, shares of fellow China-based EV makers XPeng Inc. XPEV, +3.64% climbed 3.9% after closing Wednesday at a record low, and Li Auto Inc. LI, +2.56% hiked up 3.0% after closing Wednesday at a two-year low.
