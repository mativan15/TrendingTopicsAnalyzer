Automotive SoC Market – Find Out What are the Reasons for the Rise of the Industry Growth

Improvements in automobile demand sentiments in the upcoming financial year are expected to propel the global automotive SoC market post the COVID-19 crisis. However, the risk of potential security breach due to the use of communication technologies in vehicles is hindering market growth. Hence, automotive companies should join forces with cyber security experts to prevent incidence of security breaches in autonomous vehicles. Cyber security is gaining increased importance in autonomous vehicles, since infotainment and IoT (Internet of Things)-connected applications are taking their own place in vehicle interior systems that align with changed consumer behavior for handy data and entertainment.
