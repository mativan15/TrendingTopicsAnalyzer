Ally Financial: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
sfgate.com
business
2022-10-19 11:39:44

DETROIT (AP) _ Ally Financial Inc. (ALLY) on Wednesday reported third-quarter net income of $482 million.

The Detroit-based company said it had profit of $1.40 per share. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs, were $1.76 per share.

The results surpassed Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of eight analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of $1.73 per share.

The auto finance company and bank posted revenue of $2.08 billion in the period, which fell short of Street forecasts.
