Bride Refused To Get Married After Groom Failed To Arrange A Photographer For Wedding
indiatimes.com
news
2022-05-30 05:48:31

Recently, there have been many cases where brides are standing up for what they want and refuse to marry if things are not working out well for them. Now, another such case has come to light from Uttar Pradesh. A bride refused to get married because the groom forgot to hire a photographer. The incident was reported from a village in Kanpur Dehat district on Sunday.
