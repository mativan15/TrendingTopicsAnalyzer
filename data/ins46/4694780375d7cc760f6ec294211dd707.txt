It's more than a race. It's an experience.

The inaugural Chicago Street Course event in July 2023 will host NASCAR's first Cup Series and NASCAR Xfinity Series race weekend on a street course, one that twists and turns through downtown Chicago. NASCAR's top two series will run on city streets for the first time in proximity to many Windy City landmarks, including Grant Park, Buckingham Fountain and the edge of Soldier Field.
