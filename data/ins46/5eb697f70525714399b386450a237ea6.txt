By Reuters Published: 16:15 EDT, 15 October 2022 | Updated: 16:15 EDT, 15 October 2022 Tyler Van Dyke threw two touchdown passes and Miami held off a late Virginia Tech rally in a 20-14 win Saturday afternoon in Blacksburg, Va. In snapping its three-game losing streak, the Hurricanes (3-3, 1-1) controlled the Hokies for three quarters, before Tech made a late run behind quarterback Grant Wells. In losing its fourth straight, Tech (2-5, 1-3) gained 232 yards. The Hurricanes registered six sacks, including 3 1/2 from Akheem Mesidor.
