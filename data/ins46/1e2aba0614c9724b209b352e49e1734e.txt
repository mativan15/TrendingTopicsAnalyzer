Kalera Stock Jumps on Plans to Divest Units
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 11:48:00

By Will Feuer Shares of Kalera PLC jumped nearly 40% in premarket trading after the vertical-farming company said it plans to sell its international assets and its Vindara subsidiary, which Kalera bought less than two years ago. The international businesses to be divested include farming assets in Kuwait and Singapore, as well as the management team of &ever GmbH, which Kalera bought a little more than a year ago.
