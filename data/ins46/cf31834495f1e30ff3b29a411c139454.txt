Maruti Suzuki eyes record output of over 2 million

has targeted 25% growth in volumes for the ongoing financial year (FY23) to surpass an output of 2 million, its highest ever in a year. If the company secures semiconductor chips on time and achieves the target, then it will be the highest growth posted by India's largest carmaker in more than a decade. The company will introduce new products across price points, from its entry-level hatchback to a premium crossover and a mid-sized sport utility vehicle (SUV), to win back market share and meet the target.
