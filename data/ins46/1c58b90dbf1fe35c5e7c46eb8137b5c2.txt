Posted 3h ago3 hours agoThu 2 Jun 2022 at 12:38am Police have not been able to recover any of the money.(ABC News)An Adelaide man has been arrested for allegedly deceiving five people to invest large sums of money in properties that never existed. SA Police has charged the 39-year-old Fullarton man with four counts of aggravated deception totalling $5,556,000 and one count of deception worth $500,000.Police say the offences were committed between April 14, 2019, and July 3, 2020, against five people but detectives are trying to work out if there were more victims.
