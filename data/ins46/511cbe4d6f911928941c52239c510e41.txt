BigBasket gets another Rs 350 crore investment from its holding company
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 09:45:00

Bengaluru: Tata Digital-owned Innovative Retail Concepts, which runs online grocery platform BigBasket, has received a new capital infusion of Rs 350 crore from holding company Supermarket Grocery Supplies, regulatory filings sourced from business intelligence platform Tofler showed. The latest investment was approved on May 24, the filings showed. ETtech reported in April that BigBasket had received a Rs 1,000-crore infusion from the holding company. 
The additional investment in BigBasket's comes at a time when Tata Digital is aggressively promoting its super app, Tata Neu.
