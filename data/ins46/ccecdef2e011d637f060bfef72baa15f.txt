BT Group Changes Guidance to Reflect Sports JV With Warner Bros -- Update
marketwatch.com
news
2022-10-18 09:39:00

By Kyle Morris BT Group PLC said Tuesday that it was updating its estimates to reflect the reorganization of the company following the completion of the its sports joint venture with Warner Bros. Discovery. The U.K. telecommunications group said the completion of the sports JV on Sept. 1 will reduce reported BT Group revenue for fiscal 2023 by 300 million pounds to 350 million pounds ($340.8 million and $397.6 million) as a result of seven months of the year's revenue transferring to the JV. BT Group continues to expect underlying on year revenue growth on a pro forma basis.
