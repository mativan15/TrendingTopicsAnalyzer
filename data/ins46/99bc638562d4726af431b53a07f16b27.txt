No matter where you work, if you use your Mac to get work done, you'll be thinking about installing macOS Ventura when it ships Oct. 24. While I always urge caution when upgrading your Mac, here are some of the ways I think Apple's new desktop OS will help you get more work done.Desk View helps you work together betterAt the top of my list is a combination of two great features: If you own an iPhone 13 or 14, you'll not only be able to use it as a FaceTime camera for your Mac, but will also be able to use the all-new Desk View tool to show people your keyboard and objects on your desk.
