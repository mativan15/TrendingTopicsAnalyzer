As business-minded Kiwis connected in the Big Apple, a gloomy economic outlook threatens turbulence. Sunday Star-Times editor Tracy Watkins reports from New York. On the top floor of the art-filled Manhattan penthouse of Kiwi power couple Kirsten and Craig Neville-Manning, the air is filled with Kiwi accents as the wine flows. The Kiwi expat community in New York is famous for opening its doors to fellow New Zealanders and on this particular evening they've turned out to welcome a business delegation.
