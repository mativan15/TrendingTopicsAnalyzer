LGBTQ Rainbow Honours name big winners at star studded bash

news
2022-06-02 10:20:18

STEPHEN Fry, Paul O'Grady and Claire Sweeney were among the big winners at last night's star-studded LGBTQ Rainbow Honours.
Former QI host Stephen was named Celebrity Champion of the Year at the annual ceremony hosted by comedian Julian Clary and RuPaul's Drag Race star Victoria Scone.

Claire Sweeney was rewarded for her allyshipCredit: Getty
ITV favourite Paul received the Choice Award for his championing of LGBTQ rights, while Brookside legend Claire scooped the Celebrity Ally of the Year Award.
