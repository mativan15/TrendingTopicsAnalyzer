Shahpur Begu, Sirsa: Hira Singh and Gurlal Singh, from a prosperous farming family in Sirsa's Begu village are preparing to harvest their 17-18 PUSA Basmati paddy crop towards the end of this month. They will have to then sow wheat during the 15-day window between November 1 and 15. The brothers will set fire to the paddy stubble as soon as the crop is harvested.The brothers are among the majority of farmers in Punjab and Haryana that will set fire to paddy stubble later this month after the late sowing paddy varieties are harvested.
