FOH brings the web3 space and independent restaurants together, giving exclusive benefits to some of New York's hottest spots

NEW YORK, June 1, 2022 /PRNewswire/ -- Front of House (FOH), a curation of exclusive restaurant digital collectibles created for people who love to eat and drink, launches in the New York market. With the first drop scheduled for June 1 at 5:00 PM EST, FOH launches with beloved partners including Wildair and Dame, bringing the hospitality and independent restaurant space into the web3 world.
