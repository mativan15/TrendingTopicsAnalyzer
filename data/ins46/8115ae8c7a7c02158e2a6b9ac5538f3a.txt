REUTERS/Francis Mascarenhas MUMBAI, Oct 19 (Reuters) - The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) may need to revive open market bond purchases to manage banking system liquidity that is likely to slip into deficit over the next two months, Bank of America strategists wrote in a note on Wednesday.India's banking system liquidity was already at a deficit of 100 billion Indian rupees ($1.21 billion) by this week, with more tax payment related outflows likely.
