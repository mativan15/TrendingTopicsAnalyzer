Prime Minister Nehru during his recent visit to Hyderabad to participate in Bharat Sevak Samaj Convention , met Acharya Vinoba Bhave at Madhavaraspalli in Mahbubnagar district on March 6, 1956. He is seen here with the Acharya proceeding to the latter's prayer meeting.

On April 18, 1951, Vinoba Bhave performed a miracle while walking back from Shivarampally near Hyderabad to his ashram in Pavner, Maharashtra. Bhave who espoused Gandhian ideals and led a spartan life, wanted to tour the areas affected by Communist insurgency and halted at Pochampally village in Nalgonda district.
