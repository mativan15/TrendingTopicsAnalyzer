Gujarat-based Indian dairy state government cooperative society, Amul has hiked the price of full cream milk and buffalo milk by ₹2 per litre in all states except Gujarat, news agency ANI reported quoting RS Sodhi, MD, Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation Limited.
