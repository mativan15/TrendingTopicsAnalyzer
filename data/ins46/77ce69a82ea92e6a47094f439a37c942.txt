CHICAGO, June 03, 2022--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Groupon, Inc. (NASDAQ: GRPN) announced that Kedar Deshpande, Chief Executive Officer, and Damien Schmitz, Interim Chief Financial Officer, will participate in the Baird Global Consumer, Technology & Services Conference on Tuesday, June 7, 2022. Kedar Deshpande will participate in a fireside chat at 3:45pm ET. A live audio webcast and replay of the event will be available on the company's investor relations website (investor.groupon.com).
