Bengaluru becomes second city to get 90-metre aerial ladder platform for fire services
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-20 15:30:00

IANSKarnataka Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai inaugurates the 90 meter aerial platform ladder of the Fire and Emergency Services Department, in BengaluruBengaluru has become the second city in India to have a 90-metre ladder platform for fire rescue operations. Mumbai was the first city to have the facility. Dedicated to the city Fire Department by Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai, the 90-metre aerial ladder platform system has been termed the department's "biggest strength" and an essential facility that will work as a "real friend in need" by the Chief Minister.
