The American Express Centurion Black card is an ultra-exclusive, top-tier charge card that is ideal for the biggest of spenders and most frequent of travellers – if you can get your hands on one, that is. The card is only offered by invitation, not application.   American Express is more open about the benefits on offer, should you become one of the rare Centurion Black cardholders. 
Invite only
