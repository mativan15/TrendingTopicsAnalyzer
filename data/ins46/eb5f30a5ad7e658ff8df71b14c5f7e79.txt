Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Chrystia Freeland delivers a speech to business leaders in Gatineau, Que. on Monday, Oct. 17, 2022. (Adrian Wyld/The Canadian Press)Finance Minister Chrystia Freeland warned Monday that Canadians can expect an economic slowdown in the coming months.Speaking to business leaders at a local company in Gatineau, Que., Freeland said that even with inflation beginning to ease off, the road ahead will be difficult."It is important, as deputy prime minister and minister of finance, that I tell people the truth about what awaits us," she said in French.
