Olivia Wilde speaks about enduring 'hellfire' amid nanny crisis
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-10-18 18:30:00

At Elle's Women in Hollywood party on Monday, actor Olivia Wilde remarked, "Let's face it, it's not always easy to keep going". She admitted that there are times when it's easy to turn away from the raging hellfire of misogyny that typifies the industry. However, she went on to clarify that she is still determined to keep battling hellfire and is as energetic as ever. Anne Hathaway, Issa Rae, Michelle Yeoh, Sydney Sweeney, Ariana DeBose, and Sigourney Weaver were also honoured, and the "Don't Worry Darling" director said they needed to "remind the world" that they "aren't so easily manipulated.
