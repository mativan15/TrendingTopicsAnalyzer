By ANGUS McNEICE in London | China Daily Global | Updated: 2022-10-18 09:31 File photo of Bill Gates. [Photo/Agencies] The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation has donated $1.2 billion to efforts to eradicate the polio virus from its last remaining strongholds in central and south Asia. The foundation made the announcement in Berlin, Germany, where health leaders are gathered for the World Health Summit on Sunday. "Polio eradication is within reach. But as far as we have come, the disease remains a threat.
