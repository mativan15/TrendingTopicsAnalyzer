UK is in a mess but it's not just us
express.co.uk
finance
2022-10-22 08:26:00

Politicians and central bankers have been pumping the global economy full of stimulus for more than a decade to prevent an economic and social meltdown. Now the bills are arriving for everyone.Since the 2008 crisis, Western economies have been kept afloat by record-low interest rates, endless money printing and massive government deficits.The UK was hardly the only offender, the US, Europe, Japan and others are also in the dock.Global bond markets singled us out for a punishment beating, as a warning to everybody else.
