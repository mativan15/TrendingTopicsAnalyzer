Ultratech announces Rs 12,886 cr investment towards capacity expansion
indiatimes.com
economics
2022-06-02 12:45:00

UltraTech Cement announces an investment of Rs. 12,886 crores for a 22.6 MTPA capacity increase at a cost of US$ 76 per tonne of cement An increase in capacity of 22.6 million tonnes per year (mtpa) has been approved by the Board of Directors today, with a combination of brown field and green field expansion.

By putting up integrated and grinding equipment and bulk terminals, the goal will be achieved. All around the country, more capacity will be built to meet the increased demand. By the end of FY25, these new capacities are planned to begin commercial production in a staggered way.
