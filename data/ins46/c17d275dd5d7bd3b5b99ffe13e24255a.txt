This work analyzes a novel solid-state manufacturing approach of a friction stir additive manufacturing (FSAM) technique for fabricating multiple layers of alternating gradient composite structure using alternate layers of AA6061-T6 and AA7075-T6 aluminum alloys of 3 mm thickness.
