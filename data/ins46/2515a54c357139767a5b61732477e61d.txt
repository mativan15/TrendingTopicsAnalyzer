Cambridge: Q3 Earnings Snapshot
chron.com
business
2022-10-18 11:11:37

CAMBRIDGE, Mass. (AP) _ Cambridge Bancorp (CATC) on Tuesday reported third-quarter net income of $14.6 million.

The Cambridge, Massachusetts-based bank said it had earnings of $2.07 per share. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, were $2.09 per share.

The bank posted revenue of $50.7 million in the period. Its revenue net of interest expense was $46.7 million, which topped Street forecasts.

Cambridge shares have declined nearly 9% since the beginning of the year. The stock has declined roughly 2% in the last 12 months.
